Yie-li-mi liag ka kaung ma
32
aYam mawh Si-ti-cia simiang pa giex kaing ka piang meung Yo-tax ka daux kao num,
mai mawh nang yam giex simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha kaing ka piang Pa-pi-loeng ka daux kao daix num,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
2 Yam an,
pui naing simiang Pa-pi-loeng jiet taug lai lawm tix puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui laung aux ot ka daux thawng nyiex haw simiang.
3 Simiang Si-ti-cia laung ka aux,
mai ne raw ka lox jhai pa krai aux.
Aux hoik sax krai pao ka lox Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang ap veng in ka daux tawm taix simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
mai kieh nawh pon teei kah.
4 Simiang Si-ti-cia ang yaox tix pheeik luan;
tag sang ap tix tawx nawh ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
kieh nawh si kah ka plak ngai Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha.
5 Pui tag sang viex nawh hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai deuh nawh ot dee an,
krox jun aux ciyang ka nawh.b
Viang nawh hoik yuh naing tau mai Pa-pi-loeng,
nawh kawx ang doh pon pex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
6 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux,
7 keeing iak aux kawn simex Sa-long pa mawh Ha-na-mawx tag sang hoek tix jhak ka aux.
Nawh nhie aux liag ka kaung ma tix pa ot ka daux veng Ya-na-thu haktiex Piien-ya-ming;
kheu hoik mawh aux pui nyiex dex mai nawh,
mai koe akhuang tix deeh taoh mai liag sivoe.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
jiet awm pa krai Simiang Siyiex,
pux aik aux Ha-na-mawx hoek tix kia aux ka daux thawng,
mai nhie aux liag ka kaung ma tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux hoik tawng,
Simiang Siyiex rug hoik krai lox in ka aux.
9 Aux liag kaung ma in ka Ha-na-mawx,
mai jiang tix tawx mau luah ka nawh kao aliah plah.
10 Aux kok pui mawh cao pa kham sakse,
yuh lih mawh ngai lai,
pua mhawm,
mai jiang ka mau luah.
11 Khaix an,
hawt been lox tat jhawm prawng,
aux guah lai liag kaung ma in lih koe ra pheen,
tix pheen pua tix deuh,
tix pheen yah kah.
12 Khaix an,
tawx ka kawn saux Ma-si-yax kawn simex Ni-li-yax pa kaux Pa-lux ka plak sivoe ngai Ha-na-mawx,
mai ka plak sivoe ngai pui pa kham sakse pa tiiem ceu kaux tix ot daux lai in,
khawm ka plak sivoe ngai pui Yo-tax pa ot daux thawng khawm uik.
13 Aux ah nin ka Pa-lux ka plak sivoe ngai kix:
14 “Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex tawx lox nhie maix priex lai in khawm ra pheen,
lai pa pua mai lai pa yah tag caw tix mox ka daux aw tiex,
son sang deuh tix een tom rheeng.
15 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai,
pui tag caw tix deeh cox liag ka nyiex ma,
haktiex,
mai si peem plix siiet heue.”
Lox rawg buan Yie-li-mi
16 Khaix hoik tawx aux lai liag haktiex in ka Pa-lux,
aux rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
17 “Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
maix teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix phan yuh ka rao ma haktiex;
ka daux maix ang koe kaing ceu pa ang maix pon yuh.
18 Maix hoik sax teei krax muh tix pa ang chix tieh loh kia ngien ka pui kaing rheng kaing mheen,
viang mawh nan,
maix kawx hoik sax pawk bix ka kawn simex kawn bun kheu son phi map pa yuh keeing tix.
Maix mawh Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong,
mai pa ting siawp sibrawm;
ceu kaux maix mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh simiang kru naing.
19 Krax pa keud maix tix sang yuh kied ting kied lhaong,
kaing pa yuh maix mawh pa tang siyox;
maix hoik lam lia ka kaing pa yuh pui ku ceu,
mai hawt tix pawk bix awm kaing pa yuh pui kaux mawx kaux an.
20 “Maix hoik sax yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka Ai-cix;
maix kawx hoik ot tix yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex mai ka daux meung piang haktiex khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
daux in singaix maix hoik ting min ka daux kawn pui.
21 Maix teei mhai pa daox pa am kieh pui pa koeng ka yix yaoh yee,
mai baox teei riang rao pa ting siawp sibrawm tix thung pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Ai-cix.
22 Maix hoik giex kaux ka lox pa ap tix ka tax du kix,
hoik tawx haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia ka kix.
23 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoik laig kix,
khaix hoik pon ka haktiex in,
kix tag ang lai nghiet ka lox tawx maix,
ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix.
Gum lox pa tawx maix kix yuh,
kix ang yuh,
mawh kheu nan maix tom kieh kix si pup ka krax laux krax leeing pa ting heue.
24 “Pui Pa-pi-loeng hoik sum ka tab pa sang puing ka veng mhawm mhawm,
mai jauh tix puing kah.
Veng in tag sang laux leeing ka daux tawm taix pui koeng kheu naing,
kheu cai rhawm,
mai kheu jhawm saux preng.
Krax laux leeing pa krai maix hoik lih tix mawh;
maix hoik yaox ka ngai tix.
25 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
viang pui Pa-pi-loeng hoik dex sang puing ka veng in,
maix ca kawn tawx lox nhie aux liag ka kaung ma an ka plak sivoe ngai pui pa kham mhai sakse heue.”
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
27 “Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh Siyiex kawn pui.
Aux koe kaing ceu pa ang pon yuh lie?
28 cAux tag sang tawx veng in ka daux tawm taix simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
mai ka pui naing cie nawh khankix,
mai kieh kix pon tix cie.
29 Pui Pa-pi-loeng pa puing ka veng in tag sang laig daux veng,
ploi ngu gleeing khawm uik,
pie khawm lawg nyiex pa ot ka piang braux nyiex pa gleeing nyawng bhawng kah,
pa tawx krax tan pa jauh ka Pa-lix siyiex tang kaux,
pa hoik cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm khankix,
hoik gleeing ka ngu khawm uik.
30 Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax,
jauh tom yam jauh kix tix sum ka meung,
kix hoik yuh pa caox pa caig cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm ang jud.
31 Jauh tom yam sum ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui haktiex kawx hoik ca cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm.
Aux hoik but rhawm tix yuh laux leeing ka veng in;
32 mawh kheu pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pie khawm simiang kix kawx,
cao kaing kix kawx,
cao giex kaing li kix kawx,
cao jhai kix kawx,
hoik yuh pa caox pa caig khawm uik.
33 “Kix veeh pak ngai tix khaing aux;
aux hoik sidiex gau ka kix ang jud,
kix kawn ca ang cu tix nghiet kah,
mai ang cu tix gau kah.
34 dKix ot ka daux nyiex buan pa krub ka aux an,
mai yuh krax tan lu ka riang rao pa yum koeng,
hoik kieh nyiex buan aux seeing brai.
35 eKix ot ka ngawd Sing-noeng-cee,
yuh khu dee tan son Pa-lix,
mai teei kawn simex kawn bun tix gleeing tix tan ka siyiex pa caox pa caig Maw-law.
Aux ang bax tawx lox nhie kix yuh kaing pa yuh nin,
mai ang doh sang keud kix sang yuh kaing pa yuh nin kieh pui Yo-tax yuh phi map heue.”
Gum lox pa deuh son sang deeh sum ka meung
36 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai ka aux yuh nin:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
pui hoik krai ku kaux son naing,
son sibhawm praix,
mai son jhawm saux preng tag sang kieh veng in laux leeing ka daux tawm taix simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Yam in,
maix bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox krai aux.
37 Aux hoik sax ploi pui cie tix hu ka haktiex meung ku meung ka daux krax pa kied song rhawm tix.
Yam in aux tag sang deeh khrawm kix hoek khaing haktiex an kix,
viex kix deeh hoek,
kieh kix yaox tix gaux rhawm nyawm ot ka haktiex in.
38 Yam an,
kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux,
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix.
39 Aux tag sang tawx kix koe rhawm tix mu khaing,
son sang lhat graung yung yi kix ka aux,
son sang sawg krax nyawm ot nyawm koe son to kaux tix mai kawn praix tix.
40 Aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix leeing ju,
mai sawg krax nyawm ot nyawm koe son kix ang jud ang jai,
son sang kieh kix viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix lhat graung yung yi ka aux,
ang lai baox tieh vheen ka aux.
41 Aux tag sang viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix sawg krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe son kix,
kieh kix koe riah ka daux haktiex in heue.
42 “Aux hoik sax kieh kix si pup krax laux krax leeing,
mai aux kawx hoik hawt tix tawx buan son ka kix awm gum lox pa deuh tix yuh nan.
43 Pui hoik krai sang riem haktiex in ku kaux,
ang koe pui,
mai ang koe nang to tiag;
tag sang ap ka daux tawm taix pui Pa-pi-loeng.
Viang mawh nan,
aux krai ah nin,
kawn pui tag sang deeh liag haktiex ka dee in.
44 Kawn pui tag sang liag haktiex ka baoh haktiex Piien-ya-ming ku dauh,
mai ka veng pa koe ka sivie siveng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka veng Yo-tax ku mu,
mai ka veng iak pa koe piang gawng piang lua,
mai ka yaong pa koe ka gawng,
mai ka haktiex Yo-tax plak seh.
Kix yuh lai liag haktiex,
pua nyip si glaing,
mai kok pui hoek mawh cao kham sakse.
Aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma daux haktiex in.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
