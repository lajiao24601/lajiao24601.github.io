Pui gau Yaw-hang cao cub com hoek ka Ye-sux
(Lai Lu-cia 7.18–35)
11
Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux lox ka pui gau kao ra kaux,
tom lih hu khaing haktiex an,
baox hoek tix sidiex gau ka pui mai ka gum lox buan ka nyiex yaong dex baoh dee an heue.
2 Yaw-hang cao cub com mhong kaing pa dex dawt tau mai Khrit ot daux thawng,
nawh tom ploi pui gau tix hoek ka Ye-sux,
3 mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh maix kaw pa krai Yaw-hang pa tag sang lih hoek tix kaux an,
phan ang mawh nan,
yix kawn sang krox jun tang kaux lie?”
4 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pex deeh ing,
mai krai ka ceu pa hoik mhong pex,
pa hoik yaox pex ka Yaw-hang,
5 apa mawh:
pui dug ngai hoik chix yaox,
pui laux caong hoik chix hu,
pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg hoik pon pa singax,
pui lheet yhaok hoik chix mhong,
pui yum hoik deeh kaoh im,
pui hot hoik pon mhong ka lox buan.
6 Pui pa koe krax yum rhawm ka aux kied ting buan son heue!”
7 Khaix hoik hu pui gau Yaw-hang,
Ye-sux tom krai grawng Yaw-hang ka pui hun ah nin:
“Yam hu pex sawg Yaw-hang ka daux tiag daux tu sivoe an,
hoik simie pex tix yaox ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh jhak gawng priem pa phlu bhaung lie?
8 Mawh sang lih pex yuh ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh sang jhak ka pui pa ceep kraung mhawm lie?
Pui pa ceep kraung mhawm yuh nin mawh pa ot daux nyiex haw simiang heue.
9 Phan mawh nan,
pex lih tix jhak ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh jhak ka cao jhai lie?
Mawh tete,
viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
nawh kawn lheu ting khaing cao jhai.
10 bNawh hoik mawh kaw pa hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri an tix kaux.
Siyiex ah nin:
‘Jhak heue,
aux tag sang ploi kawn cau tix mawh pa hu sivoe maix;
nawh tag sang paoh daux krax son maix.’ ”
11 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
ka daux kawn pui ang lai koe pui pa lheu ting khaing Yaw-hang,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux meung Siyiex,
pa blawn iak khaing paox tix tix kaux hoik mawh pa lheu ting khaing Yaw-hang heue!
12 dJauh ka Yaw-hang cao cub com sidiex gau tom hoek yam in singaix,
meung Siyiex hoik si pup ka naing pa kied ting,c
pui tud pui rhim hoik simie tix pon teei ka nawh.
13 Hoik si dauh mai Yaw-hang,
can phuk lai cao jhai khawm uik mai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si hoik krai lao ka meung Siyiex khawm uik;
14 ephan pex cu tix rieb ka gum lox jhai pa hoik krai kix,
Yaw-hang hoik mawh Yi-li-yax pa tag sang hoek an.
15 Pui pa koe yhaok,
tag caw tix nghiet ka khawm uik heue!
16 “Phan mawh pui singaix baux ju in,
aux tag sang teei ceu pa tix tiag ka kix lie?
Kix hoik awm kawn nyawm pa klieh daux laih yuh nan,
ka daux kix tix cub kok yo ka tang cub tix cub ah nin:
17 ‘Yix hoik phlu pi daux poi ka paox son pex,
pex ang graoh heue!
Yix hoik rhax lai grawng yum pui,
pex kawx ang yiiem heue!’
18 Yaw-hang hoik hoek,
ang ih ang nyaux,
pui hun ah nin:
‘Nawh mawh pui pa laig phi daux heue!’
19 Kawn kawn pui hoek,
ih nang nyaux nang,
viang mawh nan,
pui hun ah nin:
‘Nawh mawh pui yoek ka plai,
pa yuh paox grawm tau mai pui koh keen,
mai pui caox heue!’
Viang mawh nan,
ping nyia Siyiex hoik mawh plix ping nyia pa jhi tix lih khaing nawh heue.”
Veng pa ang koe krax yum rhawm
(Lai Lu-cia 10.13–15)
20 Yam an,
Ye-sux ne raw ka veng pa hoik yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am tom nie,
mawh kheu pui haktiex an kix ang loh rhawm rhi tix.
21 fNawh ah nin:
“Keu-la-shuing eue,
maix tag sang si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!
Paw-sai-tax eue,
maix tag sang si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!
Mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh aux ka daux pex tag sang yuh lih ka daux Thai-eux mai Si-toeng,
pui pa ot dee an hoik dawg ceep ka man keei sum mai lhop nyaox piang tix,
jhi grawng pa hoik tik kix pa caox pa caig mai hoik loh rhawm rhi tix heue!
22 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
ka daux singaix prawng ca,
krax ciyang pa si pup Thai-eux mai Si-toeng kawn lheu chaong khaing pa si pup pex lie!
23 gPhan mawh maix,
Cia-pai-nong eue,
maix tag sang yaog to kaux tix haok ka rao ma lie?
Tag sang si blat maix laig daux Maw-na-rhok heue!
Mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik yuh aux ka pex dee an tag sang yuh ka Saw-to-ma,
nawh kawn klawd ka daux in singaix heue!
24 hAux baox krai ka pex,
ka daux singaix prawng ca,
krax ciyang pa si pup Saw-to-ma kawn lheu chaong khaing pa si pup pex lie!”
Hoek ka aux dee in
(Lai Lu-cia 10.21–22)
25 Yam an,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Keeing Siyiex,
simiang haktiex rao ma,
aux yaog yaw ka maix;
mawh kheu hoik mox maix kaing yuh tix ka pui pa lhak lhiao mai ka pui pa koe ngai lai,
viang mawh nan,
hoik plak jhi ka pui pa ang koe ngai lai heue.
26 Mawh tete,
keeing Siyiex eue,
grawng pa yuh nin hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo pa mhawm maix heue!
27 i“Keeing aux hoik tawx ku ceu khawm uik ka aux.
Lheu khaing keeing aux,
ang koe pui pa tawng ka kawn simex;
lheu khaing kawn simex mai pui pa cu kawn simex tix plak jhi,
ang koe nang pui pa tawng ka keeing heue!
28 “Hoek heue,
can pui pa taug mao pa klawm kraung cien khawm uik hoek ka aux dee in heue!
Aux tag sang kieh pex pon tix laux lee tix.
29 jPex tag caw tix klawm ka khaox glawm aux,
mai hawt tix gau ka aux,
mawh kheu koe aux rhawm pa awn pa tiiem.
Yuh nin,
pex tom tag chix pon tix laux lee tix.
30 Khaox glawm aux mawh pa yie tix klawm;
kraung aux mawh pa chaong heue.”
