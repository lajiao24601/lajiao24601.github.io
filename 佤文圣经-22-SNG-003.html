3
Aux it piang ku leeing sawm,
ca maox tix yaox ka pui pa muh aux;
aux sawg ka nawh,
mai ang yaox.
2 Aux hoik tauh hu ka haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
hoik tiang laig ka daux laih ting laih iak;
aux sawg ka pui pa muh tix,
aux sawg ka nawh,
mai ang yaox ka nawh.
3 Pui pa lai ling tix pau ka daux veng yaox ka aux,
aux chawk ka kix ah nin:
ang pex yaox ka pui pa muh aux lie?
4 Yam lih hu aux khaing kix,
tom yaox ka nawh.
Aux bluh nawh,
mai ang kieh nawh hu,
tom hoik viex aux nawh laig daux nyiex miex tix,
hoek ka lawg nyiex dee keh aux heue.
 
5 Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix eue,
viex pex cu ka lox aux,
tiex tu ka kiah mai cak miex,
pex paw vi so yiex haing,
mai paw vi tuk tiak ka krax muh yiex heue.
Lox rhax ka daux loe ceu
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
6 Pa hoek khaing daux tiag daux tu,
awm pa mawh rong ngu yuh nan,
ceu pa teei sidah bhawng Law-ban mai Muran cie pui pa yuh lai gee khankix siang kah,
mawh ceu pa tix lie?
7 Jhak heue,
an hoik mawh ling law cie Saw-law-meung,
koe pui naing tix gliah kaux pa pau kah,
pui an kix hoik mawh pui naing pa laoh cie Yi-seu-liex ku kaux.
8 Kix hoik mawh pui pa chix mug ka vaig ku kaux;
kix hoik mawh pa meed pon pex tix ka daux naing.
Kix ku kaux hoik viex ka vaig pa lawm daux taix tix,
rien khre tix kuan pau ka pui naing pa jawm tix puing kix yam pon sawm.
9 Simiang Saw-law-meung siao ka khaox Li-pa-noeng,
yuh ling law cie to kaux tix kah.
10 Gawng rong ling law hoik teei mau om kah,
ka piang koe keei khri pa lai tai;
ka dee ngawm hoik teei man lung pliex yuh kah,
hoik mawh pa caing nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung ka krax muh.
11 Nyawm bun Shi-ang khankix eue,
hoek jhak ka simiang Saw-law-meung.
Ka piang kaing nawh ceep ka mhok khri,
hoik mawh singaix yuh nawh ka paox,
ka daux singaix pa kied gaux rhawm nawh an,
hoik mawh miex nawh pa tawx nawh ceep.
