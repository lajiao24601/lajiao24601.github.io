Yuh naing ka Shui-li-yax
20
Piien Ha-tax simiang pui Shui-li-yax khrawm ka can pui naing tix khawm uik,
thung simiang pa ot greem tix tix ngoe ra kaux,
mai ling law kix,
mai pui naing brug brung hu puing ka naing,
lai lawm ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
2 Nawh ploi kawn cau khankix hu krai ka Ya-ha simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ka veng ah nin:
“Simiang Piien Ha-tax nhie
3 maix tawx ka khri,
mau,
moeng maix mai moeng iak maix,
mai kawn simex kawn bun maix pa blawn mhawm kaux hak khankix heue.”
4 Ya-ha pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix krai ka simiang aux Piien Ha-tax,
aux cu lox nawh.
Aux mai can pa koe aux khawm uik,
tag sang tawx mawh cie nawh heue.”
5 Ang koe miex glang tix,
kawn cau khankix baox deeh hoek,
mai viex lox pa mhaing Piien Ha-tax tang ceu ah nin:
“Aux hoik krai ka maix,
maix tag caw tix tawx khri mau,
moeng maix mai moeng iak maix,
mai kawn simex kawn bun maix ka aux.
6 Viang mawh nan,
yam in aux tag sang ploi siliex aux hu ka nyiex haw maix mai ka daux nyiex ma kawn cau pa ting maix,
can ceu pa een kixa koe jum khawm uik,
tag sang teei hu.
Bawg yam in pa sax kix hoek ka dee an heue.”
7 Simiang Ya-ha kok kuad song pa ot daux meung khankix hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex jhak,
pui in hoek tix nyhap yuh ex.
Nawh ploi pui sang hoek teei ka moeng aux mai moeng iak aux,
mai kawn simex kawn bun aux,
khri mau aux,
aux hoik cu ka lox nawh heue.”
8 Can kuad song mai kawn pui khawm uik,
ah nin:
“Paw simi ka nawh!
Paw cu lox nawh heue!”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-ha pawk lox kawn cau Piien Ha-tax khankix ah nin:
“Krai ka simiang aux simiang Piien Ha-tax,
aux cu lox pa mhaing nawh tix ceu,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux ra grawng ang cu heue.”
Kawn cau khankix lih hu,
ang koe miex glang tix baox deeh viex
10 lox pa krai Piien Ha-tax hoek,
mai ah nin:
“Aux tag sang viex pui pa ang uik simin hoek yuh laux ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
Phan nyaox pa koe daux veng,
kub kix jhawp tix kaux tix jhawp,
kieh siyiex tawx krax ciyang pa cien hoek ka piang aux heue!”
11 Simiang Ya-ha ah nin:
“Maix hu krai ka simiang Piien Ha-tax,
pui naing ang mawh pa nieb pon tix krai sivoe sang puing kah,
tag mawh pa yaw rang di khaix hoik puing tix ka heue.”
12 Yam rieb Piien Ha-tax ka lox pa pawk Ya-ha,
nawh mai paox grawm pa hoik yuh kix sijax mukheu tau,
pa mawh simiang tang kaux khankix,
nyaux plai ka daux nyiex bang.
Nawh tawx lox ka pui ot greem tix,
rien khre tix puing ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
Mawh kheu nan kix tom rien khre tix puing ka naing heue.
13 Yam in,
koe cao jhai tix kaux hu ka simiang Ya-ha,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
‘Paw lhat ka pui naing pa ting riang an.
Ka daux in singaix,
aux tag sang kieh maix puing pex ka kix,
maix tom tag tawng aux mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.’ ”
14 Ya-ha chawk ah nin:
“Mawh mawx pa sang thung ex hu puing naing lie?”
Cao jhai ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai,
mawh pui naing pa awn ju pa ot greem simiang haktiex ku dauh pa sang thung pui hu puing ka sivoe heue.”
Simiang baox chawk ah nin:
“Mawh mawx pa sang bung pui naing hun hu lie?”
Cao jhai ah nin:
“Mawh maix heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang khrawm pui naing pa awn ju pa ot greem simiang haktiex ku dauh,
khawm uik koe ra yieh tix ngoe ra kaux,
mai baox khrawm ka pui naing Yi-seu-liex aliah rheng kaux heue.
16 Greung ngaix,
bawg yam nyaux Piien Ha-tax plai tau mai paox grawm pa hoik yuh nawh sijax mukheu tau mai tix ngoe ra kaux ka daux nyiex bang,
kix jauh tix puing.
17 Pui naing pa awn ju ran hu puing.
Pui jiang pa ploi Piien Ha-tax hu,
deeh hoek krai ah nin,
koe pui naing pa koeng lih khaing Sa-ma-li-yax tix cub.
18 Nawh tom tawx lox ah nin:
“Giex im kix!
Kix hoek tix puing naing ang ah,
hoek tix khiox khaw lox ka daux pa nhim ang ah.”
19 Pui naing pa awn ju thung pui hu puing;
pui naing Yi-seu-liex hawt khaix kix.
20 Pui ku kaux hoik pauh ka plak pui koeng.
Pui Shui-li-yax yaog tix to,
pui Yi-seu-liex leue ka kix plak khaix.
Piien Ha-tax brug ka brung,
mai to tau mai pui naing brug brung khankix.
21 Simiang Ya-ha leue kix tom hoek ka praok veng,
pon teei ka ling law mai brung,
mai pon pex ka pui Shui-li-yax heue.
22 Khaix an,
cao jhai hoek ka simiang Ya-ha,
mai ah nin:
“Deeh ing kieh riang pui naing maix yah tix ting,
mai bre tix cox keud pa sang yuh tix,
mawh kheu simiang pui shui-li-yax num khaix,
bawg yam rung praix,
nawh tag sang baox deeh hoek tix puing ka maix heue.”
Pui Shui-li-yax hoek yuh kix bawg ra
23 Siliex Piien Ha-tax ah nin ka nawh:
“Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex mawh Siyiex gawng,
mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex tom pon pex ka ex.
Viang mawh nan,
phan ex puing kix ka haktiex dee lung,
ex tag pon pex ka kix.
24 Khiox khaw simiang teei siawp sibrawm pui naing khaing simiang an kix tix ngoe ra kaux,
mai deeh tawx cao bung kaing naing tang kaux mawh cao bung tang kix,
25 khaix an deeh cox laug ka pui naing pa kua tix cub,
kaux pui kix kieh sidu awm been kaux pui naing pa hoik to sivoe,
khawm ling law naing mai brung kawx kieh awm paox yuh nan nang.
Ex sang puing pui Yi-seu-liex ka haktiex dee lung;
bawg in ex tag sang puing pex ka kix heue.”
Simiang Piien Ha-tax khaw rhawm ka lox pa krai kix.
26 Ka daux num ra yam rung praix,
Piien Ha-tax khrawm ka pui naing tix,
mai hu tau mai kix,
puing pui Yi-seu-liex ka veng Ya-fu.
27 Pui Yi-seu-liex kawx khrawm ka pui naing,
mai rien tix sang puing ka naing,
kix guah tix koe ra cub,
sum bang ot see pui Shui-li-yax.
Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex tiag tix tau mai pui Shui-li-yax pa naog ot ka daux tiag,
hoik awm ngie phung kawn pex iak ra cub khaing heue.
28 Koe pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex tix kaux hoek ka simiang Ya-ha,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Mawh kheu ah pui Shui-li-yax mawh aux Siyiex gawng,
ang mawh Siyiex haktiex dee lung.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh maix puing pex ka pui naing pa haoh hun kix khankix,
maix mai pui cie maix tom tag tawng aux mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.’ ”
29 Pui Shui-li-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex tang ot ka nyiex gox bang cie tix,
veeh tix plak paox been aliah singaix.
Ka daux aliah singaix,
kix jauh tix puing paox tix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pauh ka pui naing Shui-li-yax pa hu ka caong tix yum kao mheen kaux.
30 Pui naing pa hu ka caong tix pa kawn im khankix hoik tang to mox tix laig daux veng Ya-fu;
guh veng ghrup,
mai gut pui yum ra mheen aliah rheng kaux heue.
Piien Ha-tax kawx to laig daux veng,
mai mox tix ot plak khaix nyiex.
31 Siliex nawh ah nin ka nawh:
“Yix mhong krai pui simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix kied koe krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi.
Khiox khaw maix kieh yix fua nyoi tix ka man keei sum,
jhup ngawg tix ka maox,
hu ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nawh rien tix chix deuh ka ju maix.”
32 Mawh kheu nan,
kix teei man keei sum fua ka nyoi tix,
teei maox sau ot ka ngawg tix,
hu ka Ya-ha dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Siliex maix Piien Ha-tax khiox khaw maix deuh ka ju tix heue.”
Ya-ha ah nin:
“Kawn im nawh lie?
Nawh hoik awm pux aik aux!”
33 Siliex Piien Ha-tax ca kawn pau ka bawg yam,
yam mhong kix krai Ya-ha ah nin:
“Pux aik”,
dum mawx dum an pon ka bawg yam,
mai ah nin:
“Awm lox pa hoik krai maix,
Piien Ha-tax mawh pux aik maix heue!”
Ya-ha tawx lox ah nin:
“Hu teei nawh hoek.”
Yam hoek Piien Ha-tax,
Ya-ha nhie nawh brug ling law naing tau mai tix.
34 Piien Ha-tax ah nin:
“Aux tag sang deeh tawx veng pa hoik rhim keeing aux tix teei khaing keeing maix ka maix,
khaix an mai kieh maix yuh dee coh cai ka Ta-ma-see-keux,
awm pa hoik yuh keeing aux ka Sa-ma-li-yax yuh nan heue.”
Ya-ha pawk lox ah nin:
“Chix tix,
kheu lox pa hoik ap maix yuh nin,
aux tag ploi ka maix.”
Ya-ha tom yuh sijax mukheu tau mai nawh,
khaix an ploi nawh hu heue.
Cao jhai ne raw ka Ya-ha
35 Koe cao jhai tix cub,
ka daux kix tix kaux pon lox tawx khaing Simiang Siyiex,
mai krai ka paox tix tang kaux ah nin:
“Lauh ka aux heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
cao jhai an tix kaux ang cu.
36 bMawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka cao jhai an:
“Mawh kheu ang maix nghiet ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
yam lih hu maix khaing aux,
tom koe sivai sang si kiied maix yum.”
Khaix hoik hu cao jhai an,
sivai sang si rug lih hoek,
mai kiied nawh yum heue.
37 Cao jhai pa krai lox sivoe an baox hoek ka pui tix kaux dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Lauh ka aux heue.”
Pui an tom sau riang tix lauh ka nawh,
mai hoik lauh nawh mat.
38 Cao jhai teei man kie ngai nawh kah,
mai yuh tix mawh pui tang kaux,
cung ka praok krax,
krox jun ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex hoek tix lai ling.
39 Yam hoek simiang tix lai ling,
cao jhai kok yo lox tom ting ah nin:
“Vawng,
yam puing aux ka naing,
koe pui naing tix kaux,
tawx pui koeng pa hoik giex kix ka aux,
mai ah nin:
‘Jhak pau ka pui in.
Phan nawh to,
maix tag sang plud ju son ju,
phan ang mawh nan,
tag sang prawng maix tawx mau luah loe rheng plah.’
40 Viang mawh nan,
yam ki yuh aux ka kaing tang ceu khankix,
pui in hoik to tix hu heue.”
Simiang ah nin:
“Maix hoik prawng ca ka phi map to kaux tix;
maix ang ciyang ang chix heue.”
41 Cao jhai dum mawx dum an yah man khaing ngai tix,
simiang tom tawng mawh nawh cao jhai.
42 Mawh kheu nan,
cao jhai ah nin ka simiang:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Mawh kheu ploi maix ka pui pa hoik tawm aux lox nhie maix pauh yum hu,
maix tag caw tix plud ju son ju,
kawn pui cie maix plud ka kawn pui cie nawh heue.’ ”
43 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang ang nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi mai ing tix ka nyiex haw Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
