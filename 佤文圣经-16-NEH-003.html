Deeh cox sum guh veng
3
Guh veng hoik deeh cox sum tix mawh nin:a
Yi-li-ya-seu cao giex kaing li pa ting mai paox nawh pa yuh kaing caox giex kaing li khankix cox sum Siviex Yung,
hoik sau ka siviex mai daux doh,
mai hoik tan lu kah.
Kix baox yuh ka guh veng,
tom hoek ka Pai-lo mai Ha-nang-yiex-lo,
mai hoik tan lu ka heue.
2 Pui Yie-li-keu sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux.
Sa-kheux kawn simex Ying-lix baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux.
3 Ceu cad cub nyiex Ha-si-na sum ka Siviex Kax.
Kix hoik sau ka lat mai plah siviex,
mai baox sau ka jhong siviex mai se,
son sang chix song ka siviex veng kaux.
4 Kawn saux Ha-keu-see pa kaux Mi-li-mawx kawn simex Wu-li-yax cox sum cie tix tang baux tix baux.
Kawn saux Mi-see-sa-piiex pa kaux Mi-su-lang kawn simex Pi-li-cia hoik baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux.
Sa-tu kawn simex Pa-na hoik baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux.
5 Pui veng Thi-keu-yax hoik cox baox sum ka baux khaix tix baux;
viang mawh nan simiang daux veng ang kieh cao kaing ciak ci kaing pa yuh kix heue.
6 Yie-heu-yie-tax kawn simex Pa-si-yax mai Mi-su-lang kawn simex Pi-saw-taix deeh cox sum ka Yie-sa-na-meung.b
Kix hoik sau lat mai pang siviex,
mai baox sau ka jhong siviex mai se,
son sang chix song ka siviex veng kaux heue.
7 Mi-la-thix pui Ci-piien,
Ya-toeng pui Mi-loeng,
khawm pui daux veng Ci-piien mai veng Mi-see-pa hoik cox baox sum cie tix ka baux khaix tix baux,
tom hoek ka dee ot simiang haktiex Heu-si heue.
8 Cang mai mau Wu-shiie kawn simex Ha-hai-ya baox cox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux heue.
Ha-na-ni-ya pa yuh ka sidah bhawng baox cox sum cie tix ka baux khaix tix baux,
tom hoek ka dee vieh ding heue.
9 Li-fa-ya kawn simex Hu-eux pa kuang ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung tix plak,
hoik cox baox sum ka baux khaix tix baux heue.
10 Yie-ta-ya kawn simex Ha-lu-mawx baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux,
dex ka nyiex ma tix heue.
Ha-thux kawn simex Ha-sa-ni baox sum cie tix ka baux khaix tix baux heue.
11 Ma-ci-ya kawn simex Ha-ling mai Ha-sux kawn simex Pa-ha Maw-ya baox sum cie tix ka baux khaix tix baux mai Lui-lo heue.
12 Sa-long kawn simex Ha-law-he pa kuang ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung tang dauh tix plak,
hoik baox cox sum ka baux khaix tix baux.
Kawn bun nawh khankix kawx teem coi ka nawh heue.
13 Pui pa ot daux veng Ha-nuing mai veng Sa-naw-yax hoik cox sum ka Ku-meung.
Kix hoik sau ka pang siviex mai daux doh,
mai hoik sau nang jhong siviex mai se,
son sang chix song siviex veng kaux.
Kix baox cox sum ka guh veng pon yieh tipon kongjhee,
tom hoek ka La-ci-meung.
14 Ma-ci-ya kawn simex Li-cia pa kuang ka haktiex Paw Ha-ci-ling,
hoik cox sum ka La-ci-meung.
Nawh hoik sau ka pang siviex mai khawm jhong siviex mai se,
son sang chix song ka siviex veng kaux heue.
15 Sa-long kawn simex Keu Heu-si pa kuang ka haktiex Mi-see-pa,
hoik cox sum ka Chuan-meung mai khawm dee laig.
Nawh hoik sau ka pang siviex mai daux doh,
mai baox sau ka jhong siviex mai se.
Nawh kawx hoik cox sum ka guh veng baux pa rieb tix hoek ka si peem tai nyiex haw simiang ka dung Si-law-ya dee an,
tom hoek jauh ka veng Ta-wue lih ka glawk bawng heue.
16 Ni-si-mi kawn simex Ya-pu pa kuang ka haktiex Paw Su,
hoik baox cox sum ka baux khaix tix baux,
tom hoek ka si meeig Ta-wue,
ka dung rawm,
mai ka dee bang pui naing heue.
Pui Li-we pa cox sum ka guh veng
17 Plak greem pui Li-we in kix teem coi pui cox sum ka guh veng si doe paox tix ra loe baux:
Li-hong kawn simex Pa-ni cox baox sum cie tix ka baux khaix tix baux.
Ha-sa-pi-ya pa kuang ka haktiex Ci-yi-la tix plak,
baox cox sum cie tix ka baux khaix tix baux.
18 Pa-wa-yi kawn simex Si-na-tax,
pa kuang ka haktiex Ci-yi-la tix plak,
hoik baox cox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux.
19 Yi-shiex kawn simex Yie-su-yax pa kuang ka Mi-see-pa hoik baox cox sum ka baux khaix tix baux,
ka sivoe krao deuh kraung naing,
tom hoek ka dee kawk guh veng heue.
20 Pa-lux kawn simex Sa-paix hoik cox baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux,
tom hoek ka diang siviex nyiex ot Yi-li-ya-seu cao giex kaing li pa ting heue.
21 Mi-li-mawx kawn simex Wu-li-yax,
kawn saux Ha-keu-see,
hoik cox baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux,
tom hoek ka praok nyiex ot Yi-li-ya-seu heue.
Cao giex kaing li pa cox sum ka guh veng
22 Plak greem cao giex kaing in kix teem coi pui cox sum ka guh veng si doe paox tix ra loe baux:
Cao giex kaing li pa ot vung viang Yie-lu-sa-leung baox sum cie tix ka baux khaix tix baux.
23 Piien-ya-ming mai Ha-sux hoik cox baox sum ka baux khaix tix baux,
pa ot sivoe nyiex cie kiex heue.
Kawn saux Ya-nang-ni,
kawn simex Ma-si-ya pa kaux Ya-sa-li-ya baox sum ka baux khaix tix baux,
pa ot sivoe nyiex cie nawh heue.
24 Ping-noex kawn simex Si-na-tax hoik baox cox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux,
jauh ka nyiex cie Ya-sa-li-ya hoek ka dee kawk guh veng heue.
25-26 Pa-la kawn simex Wu-saix hoik cox baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux,
jauh ka dee kawk guh veng mai ka dee dex ka nyiex pau dee lhaong khaing nyiex haw simiang jan plak piang an hoik jauh tix sum heue.
Pi-ta-ya kawn simex Pa-lux hoik cox baox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux,
tom hoek ka dex Siviex Rawm pa ot plak lih singaix tix dauh mai ka nyiex lhaong pa pau ka nyiex buan dee an.
Dee in dex ka veng Eu-fe-leux,
ka dee paung ot pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan heue.
Pui pa cox sum tang kaux khankix
27 Pui veng Thi-keu-yax hoik baox cox sum cie tix baux khaix tix baux;
in mawh pa cox yuh kix ka daux ra baux:
jauh tix ka si tieb dee jauh nyiex pau lhaong nyiex buan tix dauh,
tom hoek dex ka guh veng Eu-fe-leux baux an heue.
28 Koe caox giex kaing li baox cox yuh ka baux khaix tix baux,
jauh ka Siviex Brung cox yuh tix haok plak laong.
Cao giex kaing li ku kaux tang ot tix yuh ka sivoe nyiex tix heue.
29 Sa-tu kawn simex Ying-mai hoik cox baox sum baux khaix tix baux pa ot ka sivoe ngai nyiex nawh heue.
See-ma-ya kawn simex See-cia-ni pa pau ka siviex plak lih singaix hoik baox cox sum baux khaix tix baux heue.
30 Ha-na-ni-ya kawn simex See-li-mi-ya mai Ha-nuing kawn simex Sa-la ka daux liah kaux,
hoik cox baox sum baux khaix tix baux.
In mawh baux pa sum kix ka daux ra baux heue.
Mi-su-lang kawn simex Pi-li-cia hoik cox baox sum baux khaix tix baux,
pa ot ka sivoe ngai nyiex nawh heue.
31 Cang mai mau Ma-ci-ya hoik baox cox sum baux khaix tix baux,
tom hoek ka nyiex ma pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan mai nyiex ma pui yuh lai gee dee an;
nyiex in phlat hoek praok nyiex buan Ha-mi-fu-cia-meung,c
dex ka lawg nyiex pa ot ka piang dee kawk guh veng plak lih singaix mai plak laong heue.
32 Cang mai mau mai pui yuh lai gee khankix cox yuh ka baux nghoik,
jauh ka lawg nyiex ka piang dee kawk guh veng hoek ka Siviex Yung heue.
