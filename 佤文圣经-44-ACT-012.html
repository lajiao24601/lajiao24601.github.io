Ceu cao kham krax pa tuk tiak tuk te tom nie
12
Bawg yam an,
simiang Si-luix tawx taix tix tuk tiak tuk te ka paox grawm ceu cao tix blah.
2 Nawh pauh ka Ya-keux pux ke Yaw-hang.
3 Nawh hoik tawng pui Yo-thaix khaw rhawm ka pa yuh nawh kaing yuh nin,
mai baox hu giex ka Pi-teux.
(Kaing in lih tix mawh yam yuh Poi Tik-su-saix.)
4 aKhaix hoik giex nawh ka Pi-teux,
tom laung nawh daux thawng,
mai tawx pui pau ka daux pon cub pau ka nawh,
tix cub lien koe pon kaux.
Si-luix keud tix sang teei nawh lih yam yuh Poi Luat-luan,
mai prawng nawh ka sivoe pui hun.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-teux ot ka daux thawng,
pui pau ka nawh tom nyawm nyiem.
Pux aik pux o ka daux ceu cao sau riang tix rawg buan ka Siyiex son nawh heue.
Taoh Pi-teux lih khaing daux thawng
6 Ka daux bo sivoe sang teei Si-luix tix prawng Pi-teux ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
Pi-teux it ka si nax pui pau ra kaux,
maox ceng ra nyaing phrawk ka nawh,
ka plak praix siviex mawh pui pau.
7 Dum mawx dum an,
koe kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux cung dee an;
koe pa toh riang jhan ka daux thawng.
Kawn cau Siyiex dieh ka klip Pi-teux,
mai so nawh kaoh ah nin:
“Nied kaoh heue!”
Maox ceng an cawd khaing taix Pi-teux dum mawx dum an.
8 Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Mad maox nyoi maix,
mai ceep kiiep maix heue.”
Pi-teux hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa krai nawh.
Kawn cau Siyiex baox ah nin:
“Ceep sibex dha maix hawt ka aux.”
9 Pi-teux hawt nawh lih khaing daux thawng,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng grawng kaing pa sang yuh kawn cau Siyiex,
nawh ce mawh hoik yaox ka riang su sai.
10 Kix hoik luan hu khaing dee pau pui pau ka siviex tix mai ka sivie ra,
khaix an nghoik tix hu ka plak ot siviex rhiem tix mu ka pa phlat hu daux veng,
siviex lawk baoh tix.
Kiex lih hu ka krax iak tix nyaing;
dum mawx dum an,
kawn cau Siyiex lih khaing Pi-teux heue.
11 Bawg yam an,
Pi-teux tom taug tawng,
mai ah nin:
“Yam in aux hoik tawng in mawh tete;
Simiang Ye-sux ploi kawn cau Siyiex tix hoek taoh aux luat luan khaing daux tawm taix Si-luix mai krax pa sang yuh pui Yo-thaix aux laux leeing heue.”
12 Yam yaox nawh ka grawh grawng in,
nawh tom hu ka nyiex ma Ma-li-yax pa mawh miex Yaw-hang Ma-kheu;
koe pui tom nie khrawm paox tix rawg buan dee an.
13 Pi-teux gleuh siviex plak praix,
tom koe bun siliex pa kaux Law-tax lih tix pawk lox.
14 Bun an hoik tawng nawh mawh lox Pi-teux,
gaux rhawm ka mhong lox nawh,
nawh ang lai tien paoh ka siviex tom to tix ing krai ka pui hun,
krai cung Pi-teux ka diang siviex plak praix.
15 Kix ah nin:
“Yhot maix heue!”
Bun siliex an kawn ca krai mawh tete.
Kix tom ah nin:
“An tee rien mawh kawn cau Siyiex cie nawh heue.”
16 Bawg yam an,
Pi-teux ca gleuh siviex ang jud.
Kix paoh ka siviex,
mai yaox mawh nawh tete,
mai kix kied daox deng kah.
17 Nawh yeeb taix tix nhie kix yien yawg,
nawh sang krai grawng hoik viex Simiang Ye-sux nawh lih khaing daux thawng yuh ka mawx.
Pi-teux baox tawx lox nhie kix krai grawng in ka Ya-keux mai kawn Khrit khankix,
khaix an nawh lih hu,
mai hu ka plak haktiex tang dauh heue.
18 Yam riang praix,
pui pau kied suk sag,
ang tawng Pi-teux hoik lih yuh kaing ceu pa tix.
19 Si-luix tawx lox hu sawg,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox ka dee ot Pi-teux;
nawh tom chawk pui pau,
mai tawx lox prawng kix yum heue.
Khaix kaing in,
Si-luix lih hu khaing Yo-thaix,
mai hu ka Khai-sha-li-yax,
mai ot dee an tix bawg yam.
Grawng yum Si-luix
20 Si-luix song rhawm ka pui Thai-eux mai pui Si-toeng,
mawh kheu nan,
kix tom ploi cub pui tang hu ka Si-luix.
Sivoe an kix hoik pon ah ka cao kuang kaing daux nyiex haw simiang pa kaux Paw-la-see-to,
khaix an tom hu khiox khaw ka Si-luix,
mawh kheu kix sang lo ka nghox koi ka haktiex pa kuang simiang heue.
21 Ka daux singaix pa hoik ciak ci,
Si-luix ceep ka kraung simiang,
mai ngawm ka piang dang ngeung,
krai lox ka piang pui hun.
22 Kix kok yo ah nin:
“In ang mawh lox kawn pui,
mai hoik mawh lox siyiex tang kaux heue!”
23 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex lauh ka Si-luix dum mawx dum an,
mawh kheu ang nawh bax tawx min plak ngai ka Siyiex.
Viag vua tom kiied nawh yum heue.
24 Krax cie Siyiex nied tix haok huan ting lhaong heue.
25 Khaix hoik kaing pa yuh Pa-na-pa mai Sao-law,
kiex deeh ing khaing Yie-lu-sa-leungb;
kiex viex Yaw-hang Ma-kheu hu tau mai tix heue.
