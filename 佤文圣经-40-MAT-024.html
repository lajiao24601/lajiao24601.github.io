Grox krai grawng sang laux leeing nyiex buan
(Lai Ma-kheu 13.1–2; Lai Lu-cia 21.5–6)
24
Ye-sux kaoh khaing daux nyiex buan jiet tag sang lih hu,
pui gau khankix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
mai ci nawh yaox ka nyiex buan pa hoik sum tix.
2 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Ang pex hoik yaox nyiex pa sum tix in kix khawm uik lie?
Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
simaox pa koe dee in ku lon tag sang yieh tix tik khawm uik,
mai ang lai koe simaox tix lon pa deuh ot ka piang paox tix heue.”
Krax laux krax leeing mai krax tuk tiak tuk te
(Lai Ma-kheu 13.3–13; Lai Lu-cia 21.7–19)
3 Ye-sux ngawm ka piang gawng sime;
pui gau khankix briex tix chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Krai ka yix,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh bawg yam mawx lie?
Pa lih hoek maix mai pa si dauh haktiex koe mhai pa yuh ka mawx lie?”
4 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pex tag caw rien rhawm tix,
paw vi kieh pui pie lhien ka tix.
5 Mawh kheu koe pui tom nie tag sang pie tix hoek ka daux ceu kaux aux,
mai ah nin:
‘Aux mawh Khrit’,
mai pie lhien ka pui tom nie.
6 Pex tag sang mhong ka lox puing pui naing ka baoh haktiex dee dex mai ka dee singai.
Pex paw vi lhat,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh,
viang mawh nan,
in ang mawh krai hoik si dauh been.
7 Pui tix ceu mai tix ceu tag sang yuh naing ka paox tix,
tix meung mai tix meung tag sang puing ka paox tix;
sibhawm praix mai kla reem tag sang lih koe ku dauh ku dee.
8 Grawng kaing ceu in kix khawm uik tag sang awm jhawm saux yha bun yam jauh tix keh heue.
9 a“Yam an,
pui tag sang giex pex hu,
kieh pex kha rhawm tix mai pauh ka pex.
Kheu son aux,
pui piang haktiex hoik koeng ka pex khawm uik.
10 Pui tom nie tag sang tik vud ka krax yum rhawm tix,
mai ra lai coh ka paox tix ra lai koeng ka paox tix.
11 Cao jhai pa pie lhien tom nie tag sang lih hoek,
nyaw glaw ka rhawm rhi kawn pui.
12 Mawh kheu hoik naog pa caox pa caig,
krax muh kawn pui tom nie hoik grai si nyek si nyek.
13 bViang mawh nan,
pui pa pon gun yeung tom si dauh tag sang pon krax taoh heue.
14 Lox buan meung rao ma tag sang ran tauh ka piang haktiex,
mai kham sakse ka kawn pui khawm uik,
khaix an dee si dauh been tom taug lih tix hoek heue.”
Krax laux leeing pa ting
(Lai Ma-kheu 13.14–23; Lai Lu-cia 21.20–24)
15 c“Pex jhak tix yaox ka lox pa hoik krai cao jhai Tang-yi-li ‘To sat pa seeing pa yuh krax laux leeing’ an,
yam cung ka dee pa singax singeh (pui pa phat tag caw tix tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox in),
16 pa paung ot ka haktiex Yo-thaix,
caw to mox tix ka gawng.
17 dPa ot ka piang braux nyiex paw vi lih teei kraung khrai ka daux nyiex.
18 Pa ot daux ma kawx,
paw vi ing teei kraung ceep ka daux nyiex.
19 Ka daux singaix yam an,
bun pa nghe mai bun miex pa tawx kawn tix peux tag sang kha rhawm tix heue!
20 Pex tag caw tix khiox khaw Siyiex,
paw kieh kaing in kix lih hoek bawg yam kuat praix mai ka daux singaix tuah.
21 eMawh kheu krax laux leeing ka daux singaix yam an,
hoik mawh ceu pa ang sax lih tix koe tom yam jauh tix phan yuh haktiex,
singaix pa sang hoek kawx tag ang baox lai koe.
22 Phan ang mawh Siyiex pa klat dawt ka singaix pa laux leeing an kix,
ang koe pui pa pon klawd ju.
Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu son pui pa hoik laug nawh,
Siyiex tag klat dawt ka singaix an kix heue.
23 “Phan koe pui pa krai ka pex ah nin:
‘Jhak,
Khrit ot dee in heue!
Jhak,
Khrit ot dee an heue!’
Paw yum rhawm ka nawh.
24 Mawh kheu Khrit pa pie lhien mai cao jhai pa pie lhien tag sang lih hoek,
kix tag sang yuh mhai pa daox pa am pa kied ting hoek nyaw glaw ka pui;
phan mawh pa chix,
pie pui pa laug Siyiex kawx tag sang nyaw glaw kah.
25 Een tix tawng ka heue!
Aux hoik grox krai ka pex heue.
26 f“Phan koe pui pa krai ka pex ah nin:
‘Jhak,
Khrit ot ka daux tiag daux tu heue!’
Pex paw vi lih hu kah;
phan ang mawh nan,
ah nin:
‘Jhak,
Khrit ot ka daux lawg nyiex heue!’
Pex kawx paw vi yum rhawm kah.
27 Mawh kheu yam lih hoek kawn kawn pui tag sang awm yam pu plak praix yuh nan,
tix vud khaing,
jauh ka plak lih singaix hoek ka plak klip singaix si dauh ka rao ma heue.
28 g“Koe to kaux yum dee mawx,
klang preng kawx tag sang khrawm tix ot dee an heue.”
Pa lih hoek kawn kawn pui
(Lai Ma-kheu 13.24–27; Lai Lu-cia 21.25–28)
29 h“Khaix hoik luan hu singaix pa laux leeing an kix,
singaix tag sang tieh tix faik,
khix ang lai baox tawx pa riang,
simuing cawd khaing rao ma,
ka daux rao ma kawx tag sang yung yang khawm uik.
30 iBawg yam an,
mhai sakse lih hoek kawn kawn pui tag lih ka daux rao ma.
Kawn pui piang haktiex tag sang yiiem khawm uik;
kix tag sang yaox kawn kawn pui naog ka siawp sibrawm,
mai brug ka pai awm piang rao ma pa ting min lih hoek.
31 Lox teet tawng tag sang luah tom ting,
nawh tag sang ploi kawn cau Siyiex hu ka ku caong daog haktiex,
jauh ka daog haktiex plak in hoek ka daog haktiex plak an,
mai khrawm ceu pui pa hoik laug nawh heue.”
Pa sidiex ka khaox ruh
(Lai Ma-kheu 13.28–31; Lai Lu-cia 21.29–33)
32 “Pex tag caw gau tix tawng pa sidiex khaing khaox ruh.
Yam awn kak,
lih lhax klai pa khraox,
pex hoik tawng bawg yam rung praix dex sang hoek.
33 Awm an,
yam yaox pex ka sakse in kix khawm uik hoik tawng ka bawg yam dex sang hoek,
j hoik ot ka diang siviex.
34 Pex tag caw priex tix tawng yuh nin:
sivoe sang yum pui baux ju in khaing haktiex,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh heue.
35 Rao ma mai haktiex tag sang grai,
viang mawh nan,
gum lox aux ang grai leeing ju heue.”
Ka daux singaix bawg yam an ang koe pui pa tawng
(Lai Ma-kheu 13.32–37; Lai Lu-cia 17.26–30, 34–36)
36 “Phan mawh singaix mai bawg yam nari pa tag sang lih hoek an,
ang koe pui pa tawng;
kawn cau Siyiex daux rao ma ang tawng,
kawn kawx ang tawng,
k mawh ngie keeing pa tawng khaing.
37 lYam lih hoek kawn kawn pui tag sang awm kaing pa lih tix mawh ka daux singaix baux ju Naw-yax yuh nan.
38 Sivoe sang hoek rawm tix kheem,
kawn pui ih nyaux teei tix koe mex koe moeng,
tom hoek singaix pa laig Naw-yax ka daux reu an;
39 mkix hoik daox hoik aw,
yam hoek rawm tix kheem mai chun kix hu khawm uik.
Yam lih hoek kawn kawn pui kawx yuh nin nang.
40 Yam an koe pui ra kaux yuh kaing ka daux kaung ma,
mai teei hu tix kaux prah ka tix kaux.
41 Sibun ra kaux pa krid mhaw,
tag sang teei hu tix kaux,
mai prah tix kaux heue;
42 mawh kheu nan,
tag rien rhawm tix,
mawh kheu ang pex tawng Simiang Ye-sux tag sang lih hoek ka daux singaix mawx.
43 nTag caw priex tix tawng:
cao nyiex tix nyiex phan tawng pui briex sang hoek bawg yam mawx plak pon bo,
nawh tag rien rhawm tix,
ang kieh pui briex yieh tix laig briex daux nyiex.
44 Mawh kheu nan,
pex kawx rien khre tix ku bawg ku yam,
mawh kheu kawn kawn pui tag sang lih hoek ka daux bawg yam ang pex tawng dum mawx dum an heue.”
Siliex pa kaux sijax mai pa ang kaux sijax
(Lai Lu-cia 12.41–48)
45 “Phan mawh nan,
pui mawx mawh siliex pa kaux sijax mai pa lhak lhiao lie?
Tag sang mawh siliex pa tawx simiang nawh hoek tix kuang pa hawt tix tawx priex preem awm bawg yam yuh nan.
46 Yam hoek simiang nawh,
yaox grawng pa hoik kaux sijax siliex in;
siliex in hoik mawh pa kied koe buan son heue!
47 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
simiang nawh tag sang tawx nawh kuang ka can sin ngao pa koe tix khawm uik.
48 Viang mawh nan,
phan nawh mawh siliex pa caox tix kaux,
nawh tag sang keud sawn ka daux rhawm tix,
‘simiang aux tag ang nyang khran hoek’,
49 mawh kheu nan,
lauh ka paox tix tang kaux,
mai ih nyaux klieh tau mai pui yoek.
50 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux bawg yam ang nawh tawng,
yam ang nawh keud,
simiang nawh deeh hoek.
51 Simiang nawh tag sang lauh nawh tom nyawm nyiem,o
mai kieh nawh kham krax ciyang tau mai pui pa pie lhien;
ka dee an,
nawh tag sang yiiem grai mai greeik rhang braing tix heue.”
