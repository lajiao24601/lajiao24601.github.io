Mhai sijax mukheu
24
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix mai Ya-loeng,
Na-ta,
Ya-pi-hux,
khawm simiang pui Yi-seu-liex atigliah kaux,
haok ka gawng hoek ka aux dee in;
yam kawn ot pex dee singai,
pex kok krub ka aux.
2 Mawh ngie maix tix kaux pa pon hoek dex ka sivoe aux,
pui tang kaux khankix tag ang pon tix dex;
kawn pui tag ang pon tix haok tau mai maix piang gawng heue.”
3 Maw-si lih khaing gawng,
mai krai gum lox pa tawx mai been Simiang Siyiex ka pui khawm uik.
Pui khawm uik rom tix pawk lox tau ah nin:
“Gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex khawm uik,
ex tag hawt tix yuh ka tete.”
4 Maw-si tiiem ka gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ku gum.
Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
nawh sum khu dee tan hawt caong gawng tix mu,
mai jung rong simaox kao ra mu,
tix mu rong simaox mawh son pui tix ceu ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu.
5 Khaix an nawh ploi kawn nyawm si naw pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tix blah tan lu khawng brie ka Simiang Siyiex,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
6 Maw-si teei nham khawng brie tix plak sau ka daux pan,
an tix plak nawh phruah ka piang khu dee tan,
7 khaix an nawh phat tom ting ka gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa hoik tiiem tix deuh ka daux phuk lai sijax mukheu,
mai pui khankix ah nin:
“Can gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex khawm uik,
ex tag cu nghiet mai hawt tix yuh ka heue.”
8 aKheu nan,
Maw-si teei nham ka daux pan mai phruah ka piang pui khankix,
ah nin:
“Nham in hoik jhi mawh pa hawt Simiang Siyiex ka gum lox ping nyap nawh tix yuh mhai sakse sijax mukheu tau mai pex khawm uik heue.”
9 Maw-si,
Ya-loeng,
Na-ta,
Ya-pi-hux,
khawm simiang pui Yi-seu-liex atigliah kaux khankix haok ka piang gawng;
10 kix yaox ka Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Ka greem caong nawh,
awm pa koe daux krax pa tawm tix ka siang simaox klai,
mai klai awm daux rao ma yuh nan.
11 Siyiex ang tawx taix tix yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex laux.
Kix yaog ngai tix jhak ka Siyiex;
mai kix ih tau nyaux tau heue.
Maw-si ot ka piang gawng Si-nai
12 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix haok piang gawng hoek ka aux tin.
Yam ot maix tin aux sang tawx khlawp simaox ka maix ra khlawp,
ka piang khlawp simaox hoik tiiem tix deuh lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap khawm uik,
son sang sidiex gau ka pui cie aux khankix.”
13 Maw-si mai cao pa teem coi nawh Yaw-su-yax hoik rien khre tix mhawm;
Maw-si b jauh tix yaog tix hu ka gawng singax.
14 Maw-si ah nin ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Pex ot daux nyiex bang tin krox ka yiex,
tom deeh hoek yiex.
Ya-loeng mai Hu-eux tag ot tau mai pex;
phan pui koe kaing pa thung thiang ka paox tix,
chix tix hoek sivoe kiex kieh kiex prawng heue.”
15 Maw-si haok ka piang gawng si-nai,
mai lon pai awm ceep ka piang gawng.
16-17 Min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex lih ot ka piang gawng.
Ka plak sivoe ngai pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa riang min plak ngai in,
awm pa cap ngu ting pa proe ka piang gawng.
Lon pai awm ceep ka piang gawng been liah singaix,
ka daux aliah singaix Simiang Siyiex kok Maw-si khaing daux pai awm.
18 cMaw-si haok laig ka daux pai awm;
nawh ot ka piang gawng tan tix pon singaix tipon sawm heue.
