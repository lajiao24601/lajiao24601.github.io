21
Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
2 Viex pex bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox krai aux;
in mawh pa tawx pex krax nyawm rhawm ka aux tix ceu khaing;
3 tawx aux koe bawg yam tix krai ka lox,
krox aux hoik krai,
pex koi baox nyiah khaix ang lha.
 
4 Phan mawh aux,
aux ang siao simah tau mai pui pa chix laux chix leeing;
aux hax rhawm ang mawh ang koe krax.
5 Jhak tix yaox ka aux,
in kawn ang bax kieh pex kub yaoh yee dao aw tix lie?
Pex tag sang yaoh yee tom ang lai koe lox!
6 Yam keud aux krax pa si pup tix,
aux tag sang yaoh yee kah,
ang lai pon kham to kaux aux hoik si yaung si kua heue.
 
7 Yuh ka mawx Siyiex lai kieh pui pa caox pa caig im ju?
Yuh ka mawx lai kieh kix pon krax lang ju,
krax haok huan,
krax mag mi?
8 Kawn praix kix kied haoh hun,
mai ngai kix yaox grawng pa haok huan kawn praix tix.
9 Nyiex ma kix pon krax pa yien pa yawg,
mai ang koe krax lhat krax graung;
khaox gix pa ciyang Siyiex ang lih hoek piang kix.
10 Mawh tete,
moi mhaing kix pon tawx simie tix lih;
moi miex kix keh kawn moi iak,
mai ang ghlua rheem.
11 Kawn simex kawn bun kix awm kawn yung pa hu klieh;
kawn nyawm iak kix yuh graoh.
12 Kix hawt tix rhax lai tau mai lox luah hing,
kraung,
ting jung;
kix hawt tix gaux rhawm tau mai lox shiao.
13 Ka daux ju kix pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem,
mai yaox tix laig daux si meeig ka daux krax pa yien pa yawg heue.
 
14 Pui caox pui caig nhie Siyiex hu singai khaing tix;
ka daux ju kix ang cu tix sawg ka alo Siyiex.
15 Kix ce tix ang siao yuh kaing krub ka cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik.
Rawg buan ka nawh mai ang koe ceu.
16 Kix kieh kaing pa yuh tix lih mawh pa kaoh khaing riang rao tix khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
grawng krax in aux ang doh cu heue.
 
17 Kawk ngu cie pui pa caox pa caig hoik sax yeet lie?
Ka daux kix hoik koe pui pa sax si pup ka krax laux krax leeing lie?
Siyiex hoik sax ciyang ka pui caox ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix lie?
18 Nawh hoik phlu kix rai rhuat tix,
awm nyhee brex nghox ca pa phlu bhaung yuh nan lie?
Nawh hoik pua kix hu,
awm phawng tiex pa phlu bhaung yuh nan lie?
 
19 Pex krai,
Siyiex lai ciyang kawn simex kawn bun ka daux krax pa lud loe keeing;
aux keud tix yaox,
Siyiex khaw tix ciyang ka to kaux pui phi map,
kieh kix tang klawm ka phi map to kaux tix.
20 Pui phi map khaw tix rieb ka krax ciyang pa khaw nawh tix kham;
kix khaw tix cim ka krax pa song rhawm song rhi cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik.
21 Yam hoik dex sang uik krax pa im ju pui ka piang haktiex,
nawh kawn sang chix kia ngien ka krax pa mhawm ot mhawm koe kawn simex kawn bun tix lie?
22 Siyiex tag sang prawng ca ka pui pa mawh simiang,
kawn pui kawn sang chix pon sidiex gau Siyiex lie?
 
23 Koe pui mhawm nex nham ka daux ju tix,
tom hoik yum nawh pon krax pa mhawm ot mhawm koe.
24 Ka nyoi nawh hoik naog ka pa koe riang koe rao;
gua si-ang nawh hoik koe rawm pa caux.
25 Koe pui tom si dauh ju nawh ang koe krax pa gaux rhawm,
ka daux ju hoik naog ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi.
26 Viang mawh nan,
pui in kiex khawm ra ceu hoik yum awm paox tix,
mai si paung nawh ot daux tiex,
mai graik hoik kheem ka awm paox tix heue.
 
27 Aux hoik tawng grawng krax pa keud pex ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
hoik tawng grawng krax keud pex kaing pa ang mhawm son sang kieh aux laux leeing.
28 Pex chawk ah nin:
mawh koe nyiex ma pui cau nyhau an dee mawx?
Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig ot dee mawx?
29 Ang pex bax chawk cheng pui pa lawng krax lie?
Ang pex tawng lox pa viex kix hoek lie?
30 Ka daux singaix krax pa song rhawm mai krax pa ciyang Siyiex,
pui pa caox pa caig hoik yaox tix luat luan.
31 Koe pui mawx pa ne raw pui pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
Koe pui mawx pa pawk bix kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh nawh?
32 Yam grawng nawh hu ka plak gawng si meeig,
si paung nawh ka daux si meeig pa sied ot pui cao pau khankix,
33 pui pa hu si paung kied nie kied hun,
pie tiex glaox kawx hoik yuh kaing mhawm son to kaux pa yum nawh heue.
 
34 Pex lie?
Keud tix sang teei lox giex nyawm yuh rhawm aux!
Lox pa pawk pex hoik mawh lox pie ku gum heue!
