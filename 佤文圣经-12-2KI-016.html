Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ha-see
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 28.1–27)
16
Yam mawh kawn simex Li-ma-lix pa kaux Pi-cia simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux kao aliah num,
kawn simex Yaw-thang pa kaux Ya-ha-see mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
2 Yam an nawh koe ju tix nga num;
nawh giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung kao liah num.
Nawh ang hawt tix yuh ka kaing mhawm pa yuh tax du tix simiang Ta-wue ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
3 amai tieh tix hawt ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
pie kawn tix kawx hoik gleeing tix tan lu ka riang krub,
hawt ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui haktiex sivoe an.
Pui an kix hoik mawh pui pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex lih hu yam jauh pui Yi-seu-liex tix laig daux haktiex in.
4 Ya-ha-see ot ka daux nyiex vad,
ka piang gawng iak,
ka greem veei khaox yuh krax tan lu gleeing ka nyawng bhawng heue.
5 bSimiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Li-shuing mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pi-cia,
puing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai lai lawm ka veng,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix pon pex ka Ya-ha-see.
6 Ka daux bawg yam in,
simiang Yi-tongc deeh teei ka veng Yi-la-tha,
mai grheu ka pui Yo-tax pa paung ot dee an.
Pui Yi-tong tom paung ot ka Yi-la-tha,
mai ca paung ot dee an tom si dauh.
7 Ya-ha-see ploi kawn cau tix hoek ka Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux mawh kawn cau maix pa cu lox;
simiang pui Shui-li-yax mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex hoek tix puing ka aux,
khiox khaw maix taoh teem ka aux.”
8 Ya-ha-see teei khri mau pa ot daux nyiex buan mai pa ot daux nyiex haw simiang,
kieh mawh khawng phak lak sawng,
mai tawx ka Vawng Ya-sux.
9 Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux cu ka lox pa khiox khaw Ya-ha-see,
mai thung pui naing tix hu puing ka veng Ta-ma-see-keux,
mai pon pex ka veng an,
mai pauh ka simiang Li-shuing,
khaix an mai baox giex kawn pui hu ka haktiex Cie-eux heue.
10 Yam hu rieb simiang Ya-ha-see ka Vawng Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux ka veng Ta-ma-see-keux,
nawh yaox khu pa koe dee an,
tom hawt tix yuh awm pak peung an,
mai tawx ka cao giex kaing li Wu-li-yax dee an.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Wu-li-yax tom hawt tix yuh khu tix mu awm pak peung an,
mai yuh hoik sivoe sang hoek Ya-ha.
12 Ya-ha-see kaoh khaing Ta-ma-see-keux,
yaox ka khu hoik yuh tix mhawm,
13 nawh tom yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi ka piang,
khaix an hieh plai mai nham Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka piang.
14 dKhu tawng an pa tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex hoik dawg mawh pa deuh ot si nax khu khraox mai nyiex buan,
Ya-ha-see teei gah ot ka plak laong khu khraox.
15 Khaix an tawx lox ka cao giex kaing li Wu-li-yax ah nin:
“Siao khu pa ting cie aux in,
yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing pon ngawb cao,
mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi pon bo kah,
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi cie simiang,
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi mai Krax-tan plai cie kawn pui,
khaix an mai hieh can nham khawng tan ka piang.
Viang mawh nan,
caw tix deuh khu tawng kieh mawh pa siao chawk ka cao si pawk son aux.”
16 Wu-li-yax tom hawt tix yuh awm lox pa tawx simiang heue.
17 eSimiang Ya-ha-see yieh ka daux jhup jaong dang ngeung tawng pa ot daux nyiex buan,
mai teei tix viex ka pan tawng pa ot ka piang,
mai baox gah ka dung rawm tawng pa ot piang blawg riang moi tawng an kao ra mu,
hu deuh piang jaong simaox nyiex tix mu.
18 Ya-ha-see yuh son sang khaw rhawm Vawng Ya-sux,
mai baox gah ka glawk dang ngeung pa ngawm simiang lih plak praix nyiex buan,
mai nyeep ka siviex dee laig simiang nong nong ka nyiex buan heue.
19 Kaing yuh simiang Ya-ha-see tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
20 fYam hoik yum nawh,
kix si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang ka veng Ta-wue;
kawn simex nawh Si-si-cia plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
