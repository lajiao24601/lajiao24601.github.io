Miex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-tha-li-ya
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 22.10–23.15)
11
Ya-tha-li-ya miex simiang Ya-ha-shiex hoik mhong pauh pui kawn simex tix,
nawh tom tawx lox kieh pui pauh ka cub nyiex simiang Yo-tax khawm uik.
2 Mawh ngie kawn simex Ya-ha-shiex pa kaux Yaw-a-see pa pon to tix kaux khaing.
Nawh deuk ngie ceeix khaing pui pon pauh ka nawh awm pui tang kaux yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
nan keeing nawh pa kaux Yaw-see-pa taoh ka nawh;
Yaw-see-pa mawh kawn bun simiang Yaw-lang,
pa mawh nan pa rom keeing tang miex ka Ya-ha-shiex.
Nawh mox Yaw-a-see mai yiex nawh ot ka daux nyiex buan daux lawg it pui tix lawg,
mai mox tix ka Ya-tha-li-ya,
mawh kheu nan,
pui ang bax pon pauh ka Yaw-a-see.
3 Ka daux baux bawg yam pa giex Ya-tha-li-ya yiex simiang ka kaing,
Yaw-see-pa kia ka kawn nyawm in liah num,
mai mox nawh ot ka daux nyiex buan tom si dauh heue.
4 Hoek ka daux aliah num,
cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax khrawm pui pau nyiex ot simiang mai cao kaing pui pau nyiex haw simiang khankix hoek ka daux nyiex buan,
nhie kix tiex tu cu ka rhawm pa keud tix.
Nawh viex kawn simex simiang Ya-ha-shiex pa kaux Yaw-a-see hoek sivoe ngai kix,
5 mai tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Singaix tuah,
yam pau pex ka nyiex,
pui loe son tix son caw tix pau ka nyiex haw simiang,
6 loe son tix son pau ka siviex Su-eux,
an loe son tix son pau ka siviex veng tau mai pui naing pa pau pa deuk khankix.
7 Cub caw an ra cub pa ang pau nyiex singaix tuah,
tag caw tix pau ka nyiex buan,
kia ngien ka simiang.
8 Pex caw tix viex vaig kia ngien ka simiang Yaw-a-see;
nawh hu dee mawx tom hawt nawh dee an;
pui mawx hoek dex ka nawh pauh pui an yum heue.”
9 Simiang naing khankix hawt tix yuh ka gum lox pa krai Yie-heu-yie-tax cao giex kaing li,
pui naing cie nawh,
pa pau nyiex ang ah,
pa ang pau nyiex ang ah,
viex hoek khawm uik.
10 Yie-heu-yie-tax guah tix tawx pliah mai dieb tie naok pa deuh simiang Ta-wue ot ka daux nyiex buan sivoe an ka simiang,
11 mai kieh pui naing pa pau,
viex vaig pau sivoe ngai nyiex buan,
kia ngien ka simiang,
12 khaix an,
nawh viex Yaw-a-see lih,
ceep mhok khri ka piang kaing nawh,
mai tawx lai grawng pa dex dawt mai simiang ka nawh,
mai ap nawh mawh simiang.
Pui haoh pui hun dieh taix tix yaog yaw ah nin:
“Kieh simiang lang ju pu rhang heue!”
13 Ya-tha-li-ya miex simiang mhong lox pa yaog yaw pui naing pa pau mai lox pa yaog yaw pui hun,
mai nied tix hu ka nyiex buan,
dee khrawm pui hun paox tix.
14 aNawh yaox simiang pa khraox hoik hawt tix yuh awm been pa tawx mawh simiang,
cung ka praok rong dee laig pui ka daux nyiex buan,
simiang naing khankix,
mai pui pa phlu teet tawng vung viang ka nawh,
pui haoh pui hun hoik gaux rhawm khawm uik,
mai phlu teet tawng.
Ya-tha-li-ya laux rhawm mai riah kraung ceep tix,
kok yo ah nin:
“Tieh vheen heue!
Tieh vheen heue!”
15 Cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax ang cu pui pauh Ya-tha-li-ya ka nyiex buan,
mai tawx lox ka simiang naing khankix ah nin:
“Teei nawh lih hu khaing pui pa pau;
pui mawx keud tix sang taoh ka nawh,
tag sang pauh yum heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
kix giex ka Ya-tha-li-ya,
viex nawh hoek ka nyiex haw simiang,
mai pauh nawh yum ka Siviex Brung dee an heue.
Grawng pa loh Yie-heu-yie-tax ka been
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 3.16–21)
17 Cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax kieh simiang Yaw-a-see mai kawn pui yuh sijax mukheu tau maix tix,
son sang mawh pui cie Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh kawx kieh simiang mai kawn pui yuh sijax mukheu.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui hoek ka nyiex vad Pa-lix,
mai yieh nyiex vad laux,
tawk khu mai riang rao khankix max,
mai baox pauh Ma-tang cao giex kaing li Pa-lix yum sivoe khu.
Yie-heu-yie-tax tawx pui hu pau ka nyiex buan,
19 khaix an,
nawh mai simiang naing,
mai pui cao pau nyiex haw simiang,
hu tawx simiang jauh ka nyiex buan hoek ka nyiex haw simiang,
pui haoh hawt khaix kix.
Yaw-a-see jauh tix laig ka diang siviex pa pau pui naing,
mai ngawm ka piang dang ngeung.
20 Pui meung khawm uik gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
daux veng kawx hoik pon krax yien yawg,
mawh kheu singaix yam an,
pui hoik pauh Ya-tha-li-ya yum ka daux nyiex haw simiang heue.
21 Yam mawh Yaw-a-see simiang Yo-tax,
ju nawh taug koe aliah num khaing heue.
