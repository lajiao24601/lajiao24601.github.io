2
Mawh kheu nan aux hoik but rhawm tix,
yam lih aux vhi ka pex mai ang lai baox kieh pex koe krax khi rhawm.
2 Phan aux kieh pex koe krax khi rhawm,
pui mawx sang pon kieh aux koe krax gaux rhawm lie?
Mawh ngie kheu krax khi rhawm pa hoik kieh aux lih khaing,
pex tom pon kieh aux koe krax gaux rhawm.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tiiem lox lai an ka pex,
mawh son ang sang kieh pui pa khaw tix kieh aux koe krax gaux rhawm khankix,
deeh tieh tix kieh aux koe krax khi rhawm yam hoek aux ka pex dee an.
Aux kawx hoik yum rhawm ka tete,
krax pa gaux rhawm aux hoik mawh krax pa gaux rhawm pex pui ku kaux.
4 Aux hoik roik rawm ngai mai tiiem tix tawx lo lai ka pex,
ka daux krax pa khi rhawm laux rhi tix,
ang mawh son sang kieh pex koe krax khi rhawm,
hoik mawh son sang keh pex tawng grawng pa kied muh aux ka pex heue!
Pluat ploi ka pui pa ang su tix phit lud
5 Kheu nan,
phan koe pui pa kieh pui tang kaux koe krax khi rhawm,
nawh ang kieh mawh pa yuh ka aux,
hoik kieh mawh pa yuh ka pex pui ku kaux,
viang mawh nan,
in hoik kieh mawh ka daux pex pui tix blah koe krax khi rhawm.
Aux krai yuh nin,
hoik mawh kheu ang aux rhawm kieh pui pa yuh nin koe krax kaig ngai.
6 Nawh kham krax pa ne raw pui hun hoik kub.
7 Pex caw tix pluat ploi ka nawh,
jheeh nyaw ka nawh,
son ang nawh sang deek rhawm ka pa nie luan laux rhawm tix yuh nan.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
aux khiox khaw pex kieh nawh tawng grawng pa hoik muh pex ka nawh tete.
9 Lox lai pa hoik tiiem aux ka pex sivoe an,
kaing ting mawh son sang kieh pex tawng,
hoik ang pex hoik pon kham ka krax pa cheuk tix yaox,
hoik ang pex hoik cu tix nghiet ka aux khawm uik.
10 Phan pex pluat ploi ka pui mawx,
aux tag sang pluat ploi ka pui an.
Phan can krax pa pluat ploi aux khawm uik,
mawh pa tang son Khrit son sang pluat ploi ka pex,
11 son ang Sa tan sang pon tix laig sivoe,
kheu nan,
ex caw tix tawng sidawng krax keud pa pie lhien Sa tan heue.
Grawng pa ang Pao-law nyawm rhawm
12 aYam hoek aux tix sidiex gum lox buan Khrit ka Theu-law-yax,
Simiang Ye-sux hoik paoh siviex yuh kaing jhaw son aux.
13 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu ang aux bax yaox ka pux aik aux Thi-to,
rhawm rhi aux kied ang nyawm rhawm.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai sang hu tix khaing pui haktiex an,
mai hoek ka Ma-chi-toeng heue.
Pon pex ka daux krax pa een rhawm ka Khrit
14 Viang mawh nan,
yaog yaw Siyiex heue!
Mawh kheu hoik ot nawh maix ex jheeng jheeng,
hoik kieh ex pa mawh pa laung Khrit kah,b
yaox tix laig dia tix tau mai mai cub pui pa pex cie Khrit.
Nawh baox kieh ex rhuat bhawm pa si oe bhawng,
kheu pa tawng yung ka Khrit ku dauh ku dee.
15 Ex hoik awm bhawm pa si oe bhawng pa tan lu Khrit ka Siyiex yuh nan,
hoik rhuat tix tauh hu ka piang to kaux pui pa pon krax taoh mai ka pui pa grai khankix.
16 Phan mawh ka son pui pa grai khankix,
bhawm in hoik lih mawh pa cux pa kieh pui yum;
phan mawh ka pui pa pon krax taoh khankix,
bhawm in hoik lih tix mawh bhawm pa si oe bhawng pa im ju.
Pui mawx sang pon tieb tie ka kaing pa ting ceu yuh nin lie?
17 Ex ang awm pui tom nie khankix,
pa kieh gum lox Siyiex mawh kraung pa coh cai;
mawh kheu hoik ploi Siyiex ka ex,
mai sang kieh ex mawh pui siliex cawng ka plak sivoe ngai Khrit,
viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex heue.
