Dee si dauh krax lawng pui kheem
2
Laux leeing heue,
pui blauh tix piang ku pa keud tix yuh pa caox pa caig heue!
Hoek yam riang praix kix tag yuh kaing ang mhawm pa hoik keud kix tix sang yuh.
2 Phan kix sang cie ka haktiex,
kix tag bleet kah,
phan kix sang cie ka nyiex ma,
kix tag cax kah.
Ang koe nyiex ma pui tix kaux kawx,
phan ang mawh nan sin ngao tix ceu kawx pa pon krax pa kaux cong heue.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Aux keud tix sang kieh krax laux leeing lih hoek ka piang pex;
pex ang pon tix pheeik luan khaing,
pex kawx ang lai pon yaog kaing tix hu krax,
kheu in hoik mawh yam si pup pex ka krax laux leeing.
4 Yam an pui pa chix tiiem lai rhax tag sang nyiah pex,
mai teei grawng pa yaox pex krax kha rhawm tiiem lih mawh lox rhax yiiem doe.
Kix ah nin:
yix hoik laux leeing khawm uik heue!
Simiang Siyiex hoik teei sin ngao yix hu,
mai ap tix tawx kaung ma yix ka pui pa tud ka yix heuea.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
yam teei Simiang Siyiex hu ka haktiex pui cie tix,
mai pex tix kaux kawx ang koe nyum cie heue.
6 Pui hun song rhawm ka aux ah nin:
“Paw vi krai lox pa sidiex ka yix heue!
Paw vi krai pao kaing ceu pa ang mhawm heue!
Siyiex ang chix kieh pex tik pak ngai tix heue!
7 Khex sang pon pui Yi-seu-liex krax jhax lawg lie?
Simiang Siyiex ang lai rhawm si kah tau mai kix lie?
Nawh rug sang yuh kaing pa yuh nin lie?
Nawh b ang krai lox pa caox rhawm ka pui pa yuh kaing pa rauh pa see lie?”
8 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Pex hoik yuh naing ka pui cie aux awm pui koeng yuh nan.
Pui naing kaoh khaing bang naing lih hoek ka nyiex tix,
kix ce tix hoik pon krax pa nyawm ot dee an;
yuh ka mawx pex lai krox jun dee an,
mai hoek tix briex kraung ceep kix khaing plak khaix kix.
9 Pex hoik grheu bun miex pa koe daux pui cie aux lih hu khaing nyiex ma pa muh kix,
khaix an mai teei ka khawng phak buan son pa tawx aux ka kawn simex kawn bun kix leeing ju.
10 Kaoh,
to heue!
Dee in ang lai baox mawh haktiex pa nyawm ot nyawm koe pui heue!
Phi map pex hoik kieh haktiex in ting laux leeing heue.
11 “Phan koe pui pa krai lox pie lhien hu ku dauh ku dee ah nin:
‘Aux hoik krai lox jhai,
pex tag ang uik nyaux ka plai num mai plai khien’,
nawh tag sang lih mawh cao jhai pa muh pui hun khawm uik heue.
12 “Pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix eue,
aux tag sang khrawm pex ot tau;
aux tag sang khrawm pex ot tau,
awm viex cao liang yung phung yung ing daux kawg yung yuh nan.
Haktiex pex tag sang baox deeh pon krax pa haok huan,
awm yung pa haoh hun ka daux bang liang yuh nan heue.”
13 Siyiex tag sang paoh daux krax son pex,
mai viex pex lih hu khaing haktiex pa vuk vied vuk viang pex daux.
Kix tag sang preeng tix lih khaing siviex veng,
mai deeh pon krax pa luat luan.
Simiang kix pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang hoek tix thung ka kix heue.
