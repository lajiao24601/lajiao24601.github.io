Krax ot krax koe Yie-li-mi nong nong
16
Simiang Siyiex baox ah nin ka aux:
2 “Maix ot ka daux haktiex pa yuh nin,
ang pon tix koe moeng,
ang pon tix keh kawn.
3 Aux tag sang krai ka maix kaing ceu pa si pup kawn nyawm pa keh dee in mai miex keeing khankix.
4 Kix tag sang yum kheu jhawm saux;
ang koe pui pa yiiem son kix,
si paung ka kix.
To kaux yum kix tag sang pum tix piang tiex awm phon yuh nan.
Kix ang yum ka cai rhawm,
mai yum ka naing;
to kaux yum kix lih mawh priex preem pa ih sim mai to tiag khankix heue.
5 “Maix ang pon tix laig daux nyiex ma pui yum,
ang laux rhawm yiiem son pui tix kaux kawx.
Aux ang lai tawx pa nhim ka pui cie tix,
mai ang teei krax muh mai krax pa caox rhawm tix kia ngien ka kix.
6 Mawh pui hot pui mi ang ah,
tag sang yum dee in awm paox;
ang koe pui pa si paung ka kix,
ang koe pui pa yiiem son kix.
Ang koe pui pa chix mug ka to kaux tix mat,
phan ang mawh nan khlao ka hauk kaing tix ghle yiiem son kix.
7 Ang koe pui pa ih tau nyaux tau mai nyiex pui yum,
son sang tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka nyiex pui yum yuh nan,
pie pui pa hoik yum miex keeing khankix kawx,
ang koe pui pa tawx krax pa khu rhawm ka heue.
8 “Maix kawx ang pon tix laig daux nyiex ma pui yuh poi krax gaux rhawm,
ih tau nyaux tau mai kix.
9 aAux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex simiang kru naing krai ah yuh nin:
aux tag sang kieh haktiex in naog krax laux rhawm,
ang lai baox koe krax gaux rhawm,
mai ang lai koe nang lox gaux rhawm pa yuh pui poi ka paox.
Ka daux singaix yam im ju kix ka piang haktiex,
tag sang yaox ka kaing in kix heue.
10 “Yam krai lao maix lox in ka kix,
kix tag sang chawk maix ah nin:
‘Yuh ka mawx Simiang Siyiex lai ciyang ka yix kin yuh nin lie?
Hoik yuh yix krax phit lud ceu pa tix lie?
Hoik phit lud yix ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix dee mawx lie?’
11 Maix tag sang krai ka kix,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Tax du pex hoik tieh vheen ka aux,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux tag hak.
Kix hoik tik vud ka aux,
mai ang nghiet ka lox sidiex gau aux.
12 Viang mawh nan,
pex kawn lheu caox caig khaing kix.
Pex ca kawn kieh rhawm rhi pa kiien tix thung ka to kaux tix,
mai ang cu nghiet ka aux.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang teei tix tik pex lih khaing haktiex in,
mai grheu pex hu ka haktiex pa ang pex,
mai tax du pex sax tawng kah.
Pex ot ka dee an,
mai pex tag sang krub ka siyiex tang kaux leeing ngaix leeing sawm;
aux ang lai baox caox rhawm ka pex heue.’ ”
Deeh hoek ka meung tix khaing daux pa vuk vied vuk viang
14 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Koe tix ngaix,
yam tiex tu pui ang pon tix lai baox ah nin:
‘Aux tiex tux ka Simiang Siyiex pa thung pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Ai-cix pa im ju leeing ju an’.
15 Tag sang ah nin:
‘Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju an,
pa thung pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing haktiex plak laong,
mai ka meung haktiex pa grheu nawh kix lih khaing.’
Mawh kheu aux tag sang thung kix deeh ing ka haktiex kix,
deeh hoek ka daux haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka tax du kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Krax ciyang pa tag sang lih hoek
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang ploi pui rug kax tom nie hoek tix giex ka pui in kix,
tag sang baox ploi pui jaw tom nie hu tix giex kix ka gawng ting gawng iak,
mai ka daux dawh rang.
17 Aux hoik yaox ka can kaing pa yuh kix khawm uik;
phi map kix hoik ot ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
ang lai pon mox tix glip.
18 Aux tag sang kieh kix kham krax ciyang ra son kheu krax pa caox pa caig mai krax pa phit lud tix;
mawh kheu krub kix riang rao pa ang koe bhawm,
pa awm to kaux pui yum yuh nan,
hoik seeing brai yuh haktiex cie aux.
Kix hoik teei siyiex pa yum koeng pa pie lhien sau naog ka haktiex in heue.”
Krax yum rhawm Yie-li-mi mai krax rawg nawh buan
19 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh riang rao aux mai tab aux;
maix mawh nyiex dee mox son aux yam yaox aux krax kha rhawm.
Meung khawm uik kaoh khaing piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
mai hoek ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
ah nin:
“Tax du yix krub ngie ka siyiex pa pie lhien,
krub ngie ka riang rao pa ang koe ceu pie ceeix kawx.
20 Kawn pui sang pon chix phan yuh siyiex son to kaux tix lie?
Viang hoik phan yuh kawx,
kix ang tie mawh Siyiex pa mawh pa meh heue.”
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
bawg in aux tag sang kieh meung ka piang haktiex tawng grawng pa ting siawp sibrawm mai pa ting riang ting rao tix;
kix tag sang tawng ceu kaux aux mawh Yie-heu-hua heue.”
