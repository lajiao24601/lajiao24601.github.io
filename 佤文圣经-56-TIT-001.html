Lai Thi-to
1
Aux Pao-law pa mawh siliex Siyiex,
kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit.
Aux pon pa kok Siyiex ploi aux hu teem coi ka krax yum rhawm pui cie nawh,
dung kix tawng yung ka pa mawh pa meh pa sidiex ex ka daux krax yum rhawm;
2 pa mawh pa meh in teei krax keud pot jun pot ju leeing ju mawh riah rawng.
Siyiex pa ang chix pie chix lhien an hoik dawg ap pong tix tawx ju leeing ju in ka ex sivoe sang phan yuh ceu pa koe khawm uik,
3 yam hoik hoek bawg yam pa khaw,
tag sang krai pao jhi lih sidawng khaing gum lox in.
Aux hoik pon beeh glawm khaing Siyiex,
pa mawh gum lox tawm cao taoh ex,
son sang krai pao hu ka gum lox in heue.
4 aThi-to,
yam in aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka maix;
maix hoik mawh kawn simex aux tete ka daux krax pa yum rhawm ex tau heue.
Kieh keeing Siyiex mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit cao taoh tawx buan son mai pa nhim ka maix heue!
Kaing yuh Thi-to ka Kheu-li-theux
5 Aux deuh maix ot ka Kheu-li-theux,
kaing ting mawh son sang kieh maix yuh kaing ceu pa ang bax yuh tix hoik an kix,
khaix an sum kuad song koe ka daux veng ku veng.
Aux hoik sax tawx lox ah nin:
6 cPa mawh kuad song khankix tag caw tix mawh pui pa ang koe dee ne raw.
Nawh tag caw tix mawh pui pa koe moeng tix kaux khaingb;
kawn praix nawh tag caw tix mawh kawn Khrit,
mai ang mawh pui pa lu lhia pa ang cu lox.
7 Kheu hoik mawh nawh simiang ceu caod,
mawh cao pa kia ngien ka nyiex Siyiex,
tag caw tix mawh pui pa ang koe dee ne raw.
Nawh tag ang caw tix vao rhawm,
khluh rhawm,
ang yoek ka plai,
ang brax yuh simah,
ang ghoik ka mau;
8 tag caw tix chix rieb tix kia ngien ka pui,
muh tix yuh ceu pa mhawm,
chix rien khre ka to kaux tix,
tag caw tix rauh see,
singax singeh,
pon priex ka to kaux tix.
9 Nawh tag caw tix giex kaux cong ka krax pa sidiex pa kaux sijax,
pa khaw mai been li.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom taug pon teei been pa grum mhawm in kieh pui tang kaux cu lox,
mai pon cox ka krax pa lud loe pui pa tieb tie khankix heue.
10 Mawh kheu koe pui tom nie pa lieh lox,
kix teei lox pa ang koe ceu cad pie lhien pui tang kaux kah,
nie mawh kawn Khrit pui Yo-thaix an kix pa nhie pui Pat-hak tix.
11 Maix paw kieh pui in kix krai lox;
mawh kheu yuh kix son pa muh ka mau,
hoik teei lox pa ang khaw tix sidiex,
kieh nyiex ma pui laux tom nie.
12 Kix pui Kheu-li-theux koe cao jhai tix kaux pa hoik sax krai lox pa ah nin:
“Pui Kheu-li-theux hoik ca mawh pui pa pie lhien,
mawh to sat pa caox,
simie tix ih mai ang rhawm yuh ka heue.”
13 Lox pa krai nawh in ang lud.
Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix ne raw kix tom nyawm nyiem,
son sang kieh koe krax yum rhawm pa grum mhawm,
14 Paw lai baox nghiet ka lox lao pui Yo-thaix pa ang koe ceu cad an kix,
mai ka been pa kawn ca hawt pui tix tik vud ka pa mawh pa meh an kix.
15 Ka son pui pa singax,
khawm uik hoik mawh pa singax;
viang mawh nan,
ka son pui pa seeing brai mai pa ang koe krax yum rhawm,
ang koe tix ceu kawx mawh ceu pa singax,
mawh kheu rhawm rhi kix mai krax dee chix keud chix tawng kix hoik seeing brai yuh khawm uik.
16 Kix krai tawng yung tix ka Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
kaing pa yuh kix ang tawng ka nawh.
Kix mawh pui pa caox,
mawh pui pa tieh vheen,
mai ang chix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm tid tix lih heue.
