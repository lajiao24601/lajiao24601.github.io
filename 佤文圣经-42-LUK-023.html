Kham krax prawng ka plak sivoe ngai Pi-la-to
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.1–2; 11–14; Lai Ma-kheu 15.1–5; Lai Yaw-hang 18.28–38)
23
Pui hao pui hun yaog tix kaoh khawm uik,
viex Ye-sux hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Pi-la-to,
2 mai deuh phi map ka nawh ah nin:
“Yix hoik yaox grawng pa kieh pui in paox ceu paox cad ex suk sag,
mai ang cu pui tawx keen ka Vawng ex,
mai baox tang kok tix mawh Khrit,
mawh simiang heue.”
3 Pi-la-to chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh maix simiang pui Yo-thaix lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“In hoik mawh maix pa krai heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-la-to krai ka simiang cao giex kaing li mai ka pui hun khankix ah nin:
“Aux ang yaox grawng pa koe phi map pui in heue.”
5 Viang mawh nan,
kix nied sau riang tix ah nin:
“Nawh sidiex ka pui,
mai suk sag yuh pui hun ka daux haktiex Yo-thaix khawm uik,
jauh ka haktiex Cia-li-lix,
yam in hoek ka dee in heue.”
Ka plak sivoe ngai Si-luix
6 Pi-la-to mhong ka lox in nawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Pui in hoik mawh pui Cia-li-lix lie?”
7 Nawh tawng Ye-sux mawh pa hoek khaing baoh haktiex pa kuang Si-luix,
nawh tom tawx Ye-sux hu ka Si-luix dee an.
(Yam an Si-luix kawx hoik ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.)
8 Si-luix yaox ka Ye-sux,
nawh kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
mawh kheu hoik mhong nawh grawh grawng Ye-sux,
mai hoik dawg simie tix yaox ka nawh,
mai simie tix yaox pa yuh Ye-sux ka mhai pa daox pa am.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh chawk Ye-sux ka grawh grawng kaing tom nie ceu,
viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ang pawk tix gum kawx.
10 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri hoek,
mai sau riang tix deuh phi map piang nawh.
11 Si-luix mai pui naing cie nawh nu nai ka Ye-sux,
map nai ka Ye-sux.
Kix ceep nawh ceep kraung dha tix pheen,
mai deeh tawx nawh ka Pi-la-to dee an.
12 Ka daux singaix an,
Si-luix mai Pi-la-to lih mawh paox grawm;
sivoe an kiex hoik mawh pui pa ang khaw rhawm paox tix heue.
Prawng ca nawh yum
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.15, 26; Lai Ma-kheu 15.6–15; Lai Yaw-hang 18.39–19.16)
13 Pi-la-to khrawm ka simiang cao giex kaing li,
ka simiang pui haktiex,
mai ka pui hun khankix,
14 ah nin ka kix:
“Pex giex viex pui in hoek ka aux dee in,
mai deuh phi map ka nawh krai hoik suk sag yuh nawh pui hun.
Aux prawng nawh ka plak sivoe ngai pex,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox grawng krax phit lud nawh pa deuh pex phi map ka nawh tix ceu kawx.
15 Pie Si-luix kawx ang yaox grawng krax pa phit lud nawh,
mai deeh tawx nawh hoek dee in.
Phan ex jhak tix yaox,
nawh ang koe ceu pa yuh pa khaw tix kieh yum.
16 Aux tag sang kieh pui si vied ka nawh,
khaix an deeh ploi ka nawh heue.”a
18 Viang mawh nan,
pui hun rom tix kok yo ah nin:
“Pauh ka nawh heue!
Ploi tix tawx Pa-la-pa ka yix heue!” (
19 Pa-la-pa hoik sax yuh krax pa suk sag ka daux veng,
khaix an mai hoik pauh ka pui,
mawh kheu nan pui giex laung nawh laig daux thawng heue.)
20 Pi-la-to keud tix sang ploi ka Ye-sux,
nawh tom si kah ka pui hun.
21 Viang mawh nan,
kix nied tix kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue!
Tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue!”
22 Pi-la-to krai ka kix ka daux loe bawg ah nin:
“Hoik yuh nawh krax phit lud ceu pa tix lie?
Aux ang yaox ka grawng krax phit lud nawh pa khaw tix kieh yum.
Aux tag sang kieh pui si vied ka nawh,
mai deeh ploi ka nawh heue.”
23 Pui hun kawn ca nied tix kok yo tom ting,
kawn ca tawk Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak;
krax pa kok yo kix dee si dauh hoik pon pex.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-la-to tom hawt tix yuh awm lox pa khiox khaw kix tix prawng ca ka Ye-sux.
25 Nawh ploi ka pui an pa hoik suk sag yuh pui,
pauh ka pui,
pa laung ot daux thawng,
mai baox tawx Ye-sux ka kix,
hawt awm pa hoik simie kix tix yuh ka nawh heue.
Tawk Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.32–44; Lai Ma-kheu 15.21–32; Lai Yaw-hang 19.17–27)
26 Pui naing viex Ye-sux hu,
ka daux lawng krax kix si pup pui Ku-li-naix tix kaux,
pa hoek khaing haktiex yaong iak laig ka daux veng,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Si-meung.
Kix giex ka nawh,
mai deuh khaox lhak piang klip nawh,
nhie nawh klawm hawt plak khaix Ye-sux heue.
27 Cub pui hun hawt ka Ye-sux,
ka daux pui an kix koe bun tix blah hoik laux rhawm yiiem son nawh.
28 Ye-sux veeh to kaux tix,
mai krai ka bun miex an kix ah nin:
“Bun Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
paw vi yiiem son aux,
tag caw tix yiiem son to kaux pex mai son kawn praix pex heue!
29 Mawh kheu singaix bawg yam tag sang hoek,
kawn pui tag sang ah nin:
‘Pui pa ang sax keh kawn,
pa ang sax nghe kawn,
pa ang sax tawx kawn tix peux,
kied koe buan heue!’
30 bYam an,
kawn pui tag sang ah nin ka gawng ting:
‘Ghrup piang to kaux yix heue!’
Tag sang ah nin ka gawng iak:
‘Tawb kheem ka yix heue!’
31 Mawh kheu nan,
phan kix rug hoik yuh kaing pa yuh nin yam mhawm klai khaox,
son khaox pa kroh rham sang yuh kix ka mawx lie?”
32 Yam an kix viex pui lud ra kaux hoek nang,
tag sang pauh yum tau mai Ye-sux.
33 Kix hoek ka haktiex tix dauh,
kok mawh “Gawng si-ang kaing”,
tawk Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak dee an,
baox tawk pui caox tau mai nawh ra kaux,
tix kaux ot plak khawn dawm nawh,
tix kaux ot plak khawn goix nawh.
34 d[Ye-sux ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
pluat ploi ka phi map kix,
mawh kheu ang kix tawng ka kaing ceu pa yuh tix mawh ceu pa tix heue.”]c
Kix yuh krax pa cawk lai guah ka kraung ceep Ye-sux.
35 ePui hun cung tix jhak,
viang mawh nan,
simiang pui Yo-thaix nyiah nawh ah nin:
“Nawh hoik pon taoh ka pui tang kaux,
phan nawh rug mawh Khrit pa hoik laug Siyiex tete,
kieh nawh tang taoh ka to kaux tix heue!”
36 fPui naing kawx hoik nyiah nawh yuh nan nang.
Kix hoek dex nawh,
mai teei plai niex tawx nawh nyaux,
37 ah nin:
“Phan maix mawh simiang pui Yo-thaix,
taoh to kaux maix heue!”
38 Ka piang kaing nawh koe na bat phia pa tiiem ah nin:
“In mawh simiang pui Yo-thaix.”
39 Pui lud pa tawk tau mai nawh ra kaux,
koe tix kaux ang jheeh tix map nai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ang maix mawh Khrit lie?
Taoh ka to kaux maix,
mai taoh ka yiex nang heue!”
40 Viang mawh nan,
an tix kaux ne raw ka paox tix pui lud an ah nin:
“Maix hoik kham krax ciyang nang,
ang maix lhat ka Siyiex lie?
41 Ax kham krax ciyang hoik mawh pa khaw ax tix kham;
pa hoik kham ax ang hoik mawh pa khaw ax tix pon lie?
Viang mawh nan,
pui in ang bax yuh kaing ceu pa lud tix ceu kawx.”
42 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Ye-sux eue,
yam lih hoek maix tix mawh simiang,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka aux liak heue!”
43 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux krai ka maix,
ka daux in singaix maix tag sang ot tau mai aux ka daux meung gaux rhawm heue.”
Grawng yum Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.45–56; Lai Ma-kheu 15.33–41; Lai Yaw-hang 19.28–30)
44-45 gYam hoek greung ngaix,
pa riang hoik grai,
pa faik tawb kheem ka haktiex,
tom hoek greung ngaix loe taix nari;
man tie pa vhak ot ka daux nyiex buan hoik griah koe ra plak.
46 hYe-sux kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
aux ap khuan ju tix ka daux tawm taix maix heue!”
Khaix hoik krai ka lox in,
nawh tom dut rhawm heue.
47 Simiang naing an yaox grawng pa lih kaing in tix mawh yuh nin,
nawh tom rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui in mawh pui rauh see tete heue!”
48 Pui hun pa hoek tix jhak ka grawng in hoik yaox ka khawm uik,
kix hoik laux rhawm mai dieh naok tix deeh ing.
49 iCan pui pa hoik tawng sidawng ka Ye-sux khawm uik,
mai bun pa kaoh khaing haktiex Cia-li-lix pa hawt ka nawh khankix,
hoik kok cung tix jhak grawng kaing pa lih tix mawh in ka dee singai heue.
Grawng si paung ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.57–61; Lai Ma-kheu 15.42–47; Lai Yaw-hang 19.38–42)
50-51 Koe pui pa kaoh khaing veng Ya-li-ma-thaix haktiex Yo-thaix tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-seux.
Pui in hoik mawh pui rauh pui see tix kaux,
nawh hoik dawg simie siawp sibrawm Siyiex lih hoek.
Viang nawh hoik mawh pui ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix tix kaux,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax yuh krax pa tieh vheen,
mai kaing pa ang mhawm tau mai pui tang kaux khankix.
52 Nawh hoek khiox khaw to kaux yum Ye-sux ka plak sivoe ngai Pi-la-to,
53 khaix an hu teei ka to kaux yum Ye-sux lih khaing khaox lhak,
teei man keei sum kie mhawm,
deuh ot ka daux si meeig pa hoik saik tix lih khaing rang simaox,
pa mawh daux si meeig pa ang nyang sax si paung pui ot daux.
54 Singaix an mawh singaix pa rien khre,
singaix tuah hoik dex sang hoek heue.
55 Bun miex an kix pa kaoh khaing haktiex Cia-li-lix pa hawt ka Ye-sux,
hu tau mai Yaw-seux,
kix hoik yaox ka si meeig an,
mai hoik yaox ka to kaux Ye-sux pa si paung tix ot daux.
56 jKix tom deeh ing,
mai rien khre sidah bhawng mai rawm bee kraix son to kaux Ye-sux heue.
Bun miex khankix hawt ka been pa hoik tat jhawm prawng tix deuh,
mai laux lee tix ka daux singaix tuah heue.
