Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
12
Ka daux singaix an,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux yaog yaw ka maix!
Viang maix hoik sax song rhawm ka aux kawx,
viang mawh nan,
maix hoik naox ka krax pa song rhawm tix;
maix hoik kieh aux nyawm rhawm heue.
2 aJhak heue,
Siyiex hoik mawh cao pa taoh ka aux;
aux sang een rhawm tix ka nawh,
mai aux ang koe krax lhat.
Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh riang rao ka aux,
hoik mawh siawp sibrawm ka aux;
nawh hoik mawh cao pa taoh ka aux heue.
3 Hoik awm rawm si ngaong pa kieh pui simie tix nyaux ka rawm pon krax gaux rhawm,
pui cie Siyiex kawx hoik gaux rhawm kheu krax pa taoh nawh heue.
 
4 Ka daux singaix an,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai ah nin:
Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
rhax lai yaog yaw ceu kaux nawh heue!
Tag caw tix krai pao kaing pa hoik yuh nawh hu ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik;
tag caw tix sidiex ceu kaux pa singax singeh nawh hu ka kawn pui piang haktiex heue.
5 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik yuh nawh ka kaing pa ting ceu lih;
tag caw tix kieh haktiex khawm bee ma mhong ka gum lox in.
6 Pui pa ot ka Shi-ang tag caw tix gaux rhawm rhax lai khawm uik;
Siyiex pa singax singeh Yi-seu-liex kied sidaing ting ceu heue!
Nawh hoik ot ka daux si nax ceu pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
