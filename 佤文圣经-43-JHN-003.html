Ye-sux mai Ni-keu-teu-mawx
3
Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ni-keu-teu-mawx,
nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-thaix.
2 Nawh hoek ka Ye-sux yam pon bo,
mai ah nin:
“Sara,
yix hoik tawng maix mawh sara pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an.
Mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh maix,
phan ang mawh Siyiex pa ot tau mai maix,
ang koe pui pa pon yuh heue.”
3 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux krai ka maix sidawng,
phan pui ang deeh baox keh tix bawga,
tag ang pon tix yaox grawng pa lih meung Siyiex heue.”
4 Ni-keu-teu-mawx chawk ah nin:
“Hoik mawh pui pa kuad tix kaux,
kawn sang deeh baox keh yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh sang deeh baox pon laig ka daux vaig miex tix mai baox deeh lih yuh ka mawx lie?”
5 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux krai ka maix sidawng,
phan pui ang deeh baox keh khaing rawm mai khaing Khuan Singax Singeh,
tag ang pon tix lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex heue.
6 Nex nham kawn pui hoik mawh pa keh khaing miex keeing tix,
khuan ju nawh hoik mawh pa keh khaing Khuan Singax Singeh.
7 Paw vi daox deng kheu pa krai aux ‘Pex tag caw tix deeh baox keh tix bawgb’.
8 Bhaung hoik phlu awm pa simie nawh tix phlu,
maix mhong ka lox bhaung,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng dee kaoh khaing dee mawx,
hu ka dee mawx.
Can pa keh khaing Khuan Singax Singeh,
mai awm pa yuh nin heue.”
9 Ni-keu-teu-mawx chawk ah nin:
“Ceu pa yuh nin sang pon lih tix koe yuh ka mawx lie?”
10 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Maix hoik mawh sara pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux,
pie kaing pa yuh nin yuh ka mawx lai ang tawng lie?
11 Aux krai ka maix tete te lai,
yix krai lao ka ceu pa hoik tawng tix sidawng,
yix kham sakse ka ceu pa hoik yaox tix tete;
viang mawh nan,
pex kawn ca ang cu tix rieb ka mhai sakse pa kham yix.
12 Aux krai pex mhong ka kaing pa dex dawt mai haktiex,
pex kawn ang yum rhawm kah,
phan aux krai pex mhong ka kaing piang rao ma,
pex sang chix yum rhawm ka yuh ka mawx lie?
13 Lheu khaing kawn kawn pui pa lih hoek khaing rao ma,
ang koe pui pa sax haok ka rao ma heue.”
14 cAwm hoik yaog Maw-si si uing tawng ka daux tiag daux tu,
kawn kawn pui kawx tag sang yaog yuh nan nang,
15 son sang kieh can pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh khawm uik pon ju leeing ju leeing phan.
16 Siyiex kied muh kawn pui piang haktiex,
nawh tom tawx kawn tix pa koe tix kaux khaing,
son sang kieh can pui pa yum rhawm khawm uik ang hoek krax laux krax leeing,
viang mawh nan,
pon ju leeing ju leeing phan.
17 Mawh kheu Siyiex ploi kawn simex tix hoek ka piang haktiex,
ang mawh son sang prawng ca ka phi map kawn pui piang haktiex,
mai hoik mawh son sang kieh nawh hoek tix taoh kawn pui piang haktiex heue.
18 Pui pa yum rhawm ka kawn simex nawh ang hoek krax prawng krax ca;
pui pa ang yum rhawm hoik hoek krax prawng krax ca,
mawh kheu ang nawh yum rhawm ka kawn simex Siyiex pa koe tix kaux khaing.
19 Pa riang lih hoek ka piang haktiex,
kawn pui piang haktiex ang muh pa riang muh pa faik kheu hoik caox caig kaing pa yuh tix;
grawng hoek kix krax prawng krax ca hoik mawh kheu jao in.
20 Pui pa yuh kaing pa caox pa caig hoik koeng ka pa riang khawm uik,
mai kix ang hoek dex ka pa riang,
mawh kheu lhat kix kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix lih.
21 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa yuh kaing ka daux krax pa mawh pa meh,
kix hoek tix dex ka pa riang,
son sang kieh pa riang sidawng ka daux can kaing pa hoik yuh tix khawm uik,
awm hoik hawt tix yuh ka alo Siyiex yuh nan heue.
Ye-sux mai Yaw-hang
22 Khaix hoik luan kaing in,
Ye-sux mai pui gau hoek ka baoh haktiex Yo-thaix.
Nawh hoik paung ot tau mai kix dee an been tix bawg yam,
mai cub com kix ka rawm.
23 Yaw-hang kawx hoik cub com pui ka rawm ka Ai-noeng pa ang singai khaing Sa-leung miex glang tix;
mawh kheu kied haoh rawm dee an,
pui hoik hu cub com tix khawm uik. (
24 dYam an Yaw-hang ang bax laig tix ot daux thawng heue.)
25 Pui gau Yaw-hang ra loe kaux,
simah ka pui Yo-thaix tix kaux grawh grawng krax pa kieh singax ka daux been.
26 Kix hu ka Yaw-hang,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
maix jhak,
pui an pa hoik ot tau mai maix ka toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
pa hoik kham maix sakse son nawh,
yam in nawh kawx hoik cub com pui ka rawm,
pui hun hoik hu sawg ka nawh khawm uik heue!”
27 Yaw-hang ah nin:
“Lheu khaing khawng phak pa hoik tawx Siyiex,
ang koe pui pa pon tid tix.
28 eAux hoik sax krai,
aux ang mawh Khrit;
aux hoik mawh ngie kaw pa ploi hoek sivoe nawh khaing.
Lox in pex chix tix kieh mawh pa kham sakse son aux.
29 Pa teei tix moeng ka bun pa yuh ka paox,
mawh simex pa yuh ka paox;
paox grawm simex pa yuh ka paox cung tix nghiet ka praok,
yam mhong nawh lox simex pa yuh ka paox,
nawh tag gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
Awm an,
aux hoik pon krax gaux rhawm pa grum naog.
30 Nawh tag sang haok huan,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang iak riang heue.”
Kaw an pa hoek khaing rao ma
31 Kaw an pa hoek khaing rao ma mawh pa ting ka piang khawm uik;
kaw an pa hoek khaing haktiex mawh pa dex mai haktiex,
lox pa hoik krai nawh kawx mawh kaing ceu pa koe ka haktiex.
Kaw an pa hoek khaing rao ma mawh pa ting ka piang khawm uik.
32 Nawh hoik kham sakse ka ceu pa hoik yaox tix,
mai ka ceu pa hoik mhong tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa rieb ka mhai sakse pa kham nawh.
33 Pui pa rieb ka mhai sakse pa kham nawh,
hoik kham sakse grawng mawh Siyiex pa kaux sijax.
34 Kaw pa ploi Siyiex lih hoek an krai lao ka gum lox Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik tawx Siyiex Khuan Singax Singeh pa ang lai chix tiag tix yaox ka nawh.
35 fKeeing muh ka kawn simex tix,
mai hoik ap tix tawx can ceu pa koe khawm uik ka daux tawm taix nawh.
36 Pui pa yum rhawm ka kawn simex nawh,
pon ju leeing ju leeing phan;
pui pa ang yum rhawm ka kawn simex nawh,
tag ang chix pon ka ju pa mawh pa meh,
mawh kheu nan krax ciyang Siyiex ang yoi tix singai khaing nawh leeing ju leeing phan heue.
