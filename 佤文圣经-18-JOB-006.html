6
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
2 Simie tix kieh pui ciang tix yaox ka krax pa laux pa leeing aux,
koe pui teei krax khi rhawm aux deuh ot piang jiang;
3 krax khi rhawm in kawn lheu cien khaing mhaik pa koe praok rawm sigrit sigra;
mawh kheu nan,
paw vi saux rhawm ka lox pa ting aux.
4 Siyiex pa ting piang khawm uik puing te krox ka aux;
jai toh te hoik tauh ka khawm gaw kaux aux.
Siyiex siao krax laux krax leeing pa ting lhat graung pui ka ku ceu puing ka aux heue.
 
5 La tiag yaox tix sawm rib klai hoik naog rhawm;
moi yaox tix sawm nyhee glauh ang lai lih lox.
6 Viang mawh nan,
priex preem pa ang koe kih mawx pa sang ih lie?
Brang tawm ia chix koe jhawm nyawm yuh ka mawx lie?
7 Aux ang rhawm ih priex preem pa yuh nin,
yam ih aux kah,
mai hoik kieh aux deeh hao lih heue.
 
8 Siyiex yuh ka mawx lai ang hawt tix tawx ka aux awm lox pa khiox khaw aux lie?
Nawh yuh ka mawx lai ang nghiet ka lox buan rawg aux?
9 Simie nawh pauh aux yum khaing!
Simie nawh gut aux phawng khaing!
10 Viang aux hoik yaox krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi pa kin singaix,
ca mawh hoik tawng aux nawh sang yuh tix laux leeing,
aux kawx tag gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
Aux hoik tawng Siyiex mawh pa singax singeh;
aux ang sax pon lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx nawh tix bawg kawx.
11 Aux sang koe riang rao yuh ka mawx son sang im ju lie?
Aux ang tie lai koe krax keud pot jun pot,
yuh ka mawx lai ca kawn im ju lie?
12 Khex mawh aux pa yuh lih ka simaox lie?
Khex mawh to kaux aux pa tawk lih khaing rhiem lie?
13 Riang rao pa sang tang taoh aux tix ka ang lai koe pie ceeix;
aux ang lai koe dee pa sang khiox khaw joh jhan tix tix dauh kawx heue.
 
14 Ang kuang aux tik ang tik vud ka Siyiex pa ting ka piang khawm uik,
ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi yuh nin,
aux simie ka paox grawm pa yung yi.
15 Viang mawh nan,
paox grawm aux eue,
pex awm rawm klawng iak yuh nan ang pon tix een rhawm kah,
phan lhiex ang rheeng lih praix klawng tag sang kroh.
16 Rawm klawng iak hoik si ku kheu pa kang si tap,
rha hoik thox kah;
17 yam si aoh praix,
rha tom grai,
dawng ang koe rawm hoik kroh heue.
18 Pui yuh lai gee hoik prung prang tix grai kheu sawg daux lih rawm;
Kix lud krax mai yum ka daux tiag.
19 Cub caw pa yuh lai gee See-pa sawg kah;
Nyo si nie pui Thi-max maong tix jhak.
20 Kaing pa keud kix tix sang yuh hoik grai;
Kix hoik yaox ngie ka klawng kroh an khaing;
21 phan mawh ka aux,
pex jiet awm klawng in;
yam yaox pex ka grawng aux,
pex hoik yaoh yee mai yawn tix deeh.
22 Aux hoik teei khawng phak lak sawng ceu pa tix khaing pex,
kieh pex rieb mau chun praok jao aux yam mawx?
23 Phan ang mawh nan khiox khaw pex taoh aux luat luan khaing tawm taix pui koeng,
taoh aux luat luan khaing tawm taix pui pa cau nyhau?
 
24 Chix tix,
viex pex sidiex gau ka aux,
aux tag sang daong yhaok tix nghiet;
kieh aux tawng ka krax pa lud loe tix.
25 Lox pa khaw caw pon kieh pui yum rhawm kah;
viang mawh nan,
krax pa kum kawng pex hoik mawh kaing pa daox tete.
26 Pex ce lox pa hoik krai aux mawh lox giex lox going;
phan ang mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx pex lai kawn sang pawk lox deek rhawm deek rhi lie?
27 Pex lai kawn sang yuh krax pa cawk lai,
son sang teei kawn doe yuh siliex,
sang keud tix mag mi ka pa coh tix ka paox grawm ting tix.
28 Pex jhak ka aux,
aux ang pie lhien ka plak sivoe ngai pex.
29 Pex baox bre tix keud mhawm mhawm,
ang pon tix yuh pa ang rauh ang see yuh nin;
baox keud tix yaox,
mawh kheu rauh see aux ang pie lhien.
30 Khaix an,
pex ce aux pie lhien,
mai ce aux ang pon guah tix tawng ka ceu pa mawh mai ceu pa ang mawh heue.
