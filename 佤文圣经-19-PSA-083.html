Rawg buan son sang pex ka pui pa koeng ka Yi-seu-liex
83
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi yien yawg tix;
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi nyip jheeh tix ang nye ang nyux.
2 Jhak heue,
pui pa koeng ka maix hoik tieh vheen;
pui pa koeng ka maix hoik yuh krax pa tieh vheen.
3 Kix sang keud tix yuh krax pa tieb tie ka pui cie maix;
kix sang yuh pui pa kia ngien maix laux leeing.
4 Kix ah nin:
hoek heue,
yuh meung kix laux leeing,
kieh pui piawm ka ceu kaux pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju heue.
 
5 Kix rom tix keud sawn tau,
yuh paox pie tau,
son sang tieb tie ka maix.
6 Kix mawh pui Yi-tong,
pui Yi-seu-ma-lix,
pui Maw-ya,
pui Sa-cia,
7 pui Cia-pa-leux,
pui Ya-meung,
pui Ya-ma-lix,
pui Fe-li-see,
pui Thai-eux.
8 Pui Ya-sux kawx rom tix tau mai kix,
rom tix tau mai doeh ceu cad Law-teux pa koe riang,
pui pa koe gleeh ka ex heue.
 
9 aKhiox khaw maix yuh naing ka kix,
awm hoik yuh maix ka pui Mi-tiien yuh nan,
awm hoik yuh maix ka Si-si-la,
mai Yie-ping ka rawm klawng Ci-suing yuh nan.
10 Maix hoik yuh kix laux leeing ka Yin To-eux;
to kaux yum kix hoik laux hoik si um ka daux tiag daux tu.
11 bKhiox khaw maix yuh ka simiang kix,
awm hoik yuh maix ka Eu-lix mai Si-yi-paw;
yuh ka cao kaing kix laux leeing,
awm hoik yuh ka Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na yuh nan;
12 kheu kix ah nin:
ex hoek tix tud,
hoek tix pon ka haktiex cie Siyiex,
kieh mawh cie tix heue.
 
13 Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix prung prang kix tang ot,
awm phlu bhaung phawng tiex yuh nan,
kieh kix awm kam riex pa phlu bhaung hu.
14 Ngu gleeing tiag khaox yuh ka mawx,
cap ngu yuh ka mawx ka gawng lua,
15 khiox khaw maix hawt tix kieh bhaung ting leue kix to;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix kieh lhiex ting ghlat ka kix.
16 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh pak ngai kix naog krax pa kaig pa tua,
son sang sidiex gau kix doh keud ka pa ting riang ting rao maix.
17 Kieh kix yaoh yee ang pon pex leeing ju leeing phan;
kieh kix yum ka daux krax pa kaig pa tua.
18 Kieh kix tawng mawh ngie maix pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tix kaux khaing;
maix mawh cao pa ting pa lhaong ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
