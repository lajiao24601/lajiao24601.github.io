Yaw-seux krai to kaux tix sidawng ka pux aik tix
45
aYaw-seux ang pon gut rhawm tix sivoe pui cau tix khankix,
kheu nan tom tawx lox kieh kix lih hu plak praix.
Yam krai Yaw-seux to kaux tix ka pux aik tix,
pui tang kaux ang koe tan.
2 Viang mawh nan,
lox yiiem riag nawh pui Ai-cix mhong kah,
lox an hoek ka daux nyiex haw simiang.
3 Yaw-seux ah nin ka pux aik tix khankix:
“Aux mawh Yaw-seux!
Kawn koe keeing ex lie?”
Yam mhong pux aik nawh khankix lox an,
kix keud daox aw kah,
ang pon pawk ka lox.
4 Yaw-seux nhie kix hoek,
kix tom haok dex ka nawh.
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux mawh pux pex Yaw-seux pa hoik coh pex ka daux Ai-cix.
5 Viang mawh nan,
pex paw lai khi rhawm ka kaing pa yuh pex in sivoe.
Mawh kheu Siyiex sang taoh ka ju ex,
mai kieh aux hu sivoe pex dee in.
6 Sibhawm praix haktiex dee in tag hoik ngie ra num,
khaix in kawn deuk phuan num pa ang pui nyang pon sum kaung,
mai ang chix vawg nghox heue.
7 Kheu sang taoh ka pex mai kawn praix pex,
Siyiex ploi aux hu sivoe dee in,
siao krax in taoh ka pex.
8 Jhak tix yaox yuh nin,
pa ploi aux hoek mawh Siyiex,
ang mawh pex;
nawh kieh aux mawh simiang pa ting ka piang nyiex haw simiang,
kieh aux mawh simiang pa kuang ka Ai-cix,
mai priex meung kix uik heue.
9 b“Nied pex kaoh ing ka keeing aux dee an,
krai nawh mhong,
kawn simex nawh Yaw-seux ah yuh nin:
‘Siyiex hoik kieh aux mawh simiang,
kuang ka daux Ai-cix khawm uik;
paw lai tuh rhawm tix,
nied tix hoek ka aux dee in.
10 Maix mai kawn simex kawn bun,
kawn saux kawn se,
phung moi phung yung pex,
mai can pa koe pex khawm uik hu ot ka haktiex Keu-sang,
dex mai aux.
11 Yuh nin,
aux tag eei ka maix.
Sibhawm praix kawn koe phuan num,
aux ang rawm yaox yum maix khawm daux nyiex maix mai khawng briex maix kheu cai rhawm tix heue.’ ”
12 Yaw-seux baox ah nin:
“Yam in,
pex hoik yaox tau mai pux aux Piien-ya-ming,
mawh aux,
pa mawh Yaw-seux pa hoik ah lox in ka pex.
13 Pex krai ka keeing,
aux koe siawp sibrawm ka daux Ai-cix mie ding,
mai baox krai ka nawh pa hoik yaox pex ka ngai tix khawm uik.
Nied pex rieb nawh hoek ka aux dee in.”
14 Nawh kawm pux tix Piien-ya-ming mai yiiem,
pux nawh kawx kawm nawh mai yiiem.
15 Khaix an,
nawh ngheet ka pux ke tix ku kaux,
mai yiiem khawm kawm tix kix;
khaix an,
pux ke nawh khankix jauh tix si kah mai nawh heue.
16 Yam mhong pui pa ot daux nyiex haw simiang khankix grawng hoek pux ke Yaw-seux ka daux Ai-cix,
simiang mai siliex nawh khankix kied gaux rhawm ka heue.
17 Nawh ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Krai ka pux aik maix khankix,
tang kraung pex naog piang la mai deeh hoek ka haktiex Cia-nang.
18 Viex keeing pex mai pui daux nyiex ma nawh hoek ka dee in.
Aux tag sang tawx haktiex pa jiet mhawm Ai-cix ka pex,
kieh pex tag sang yaox tix ih ka dee mhawm haktiex heue.
19 Aux kawx tag sang krai ka kix,
kieh kiex viex ling law hu khaing Ai-cix,
son sang rieb ka moeng kix,
kawn simex kawn bun kix mai khawm keeing kix hoek tau.
20 Paw lai lhawk ka sin ngao pex dee an,
mawh kheu can ceu pa nie mhawm daux haktiex Ai-cix hoik mawh son pex heue.”
21 Kawn simex Ya-keux khankix yuh hawt awm lox an.
Yaw-seux hawt ka lox simiang tawx ling law mai priex pa ih kix daux krax.
22 Nawh baox tawx kraung ceep ka pux ke tix tix kaux tix jung,
viang mawh nan,
pa tawx ka Piien-ya-ming mawh phuan jung mai mau luah loe yieh.
23 Nawh tawx keeing tix cie la mhaing kao mu,
tang ka kraung pa kied mhawm pa koe ka daux Ai-cix;
baox tawx la miex kao mu,
tang ka pa sang ih keeing tix ka daux krax,
pa mawh nghox,
khao mun,
mai pa ih tang ceu.
24 Nawh tawx pux ke tix khankix ing,
mai tawx lox ang kieh kix simah paox tix hawt krax heue.
25 Kix lih hu khaing Ai-cix,
dee hoek ka haktiex Cia-nang ka keeing tix dee an.
26 Kix ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Yaw-seux kawn im ju,
mai nawh mawh simiang pa kuang ka piang haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik.”
Ya-keux daox deng kah,
ang pon yum rhawm ka lox kix heue.
27 Viang mawh nan,
yam mhong nawh ah kix lox pa krai Yaw-seux ka nawh,
mai yaox pa kieh Yaw-seux kix rieb nawh hu ka ling law ka daux Ai-cix,
ang lai daox kah.
28 Nawh ah nin:
“Kup phaox!
Kawn simex aux Yaw-seux kawn im ju,
sivoe ang aux nyang yum,
aux khaw tix hu jhak ka nawh.”
