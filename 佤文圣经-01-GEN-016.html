Sa-cia mai Yi-seu-ma-lix
16
Moeng Ya-paw-lang Sa-lai ang nyang chix keh kawn simex kawn bun son nawh,
Sa-lai koe bun siliex tix kaux pa kaux Sa-cia,
mawh pui Ai-cix.
2 Sa-lai ah nin ka Ya-paw-lang:
“Simiang Siyiex ang nyang bax kieh aux keh kawn.
Khiox khaw maix ot tau mai bun siliex aux khaing!
Nawh tee chix keh kawn simex tix kaux son aux heue.”
Ya-paw-lang nghiet ka lox moeng tix Sa-lai.
3 Sa-lai tom teei tix tawx Sa-cia mawh moeng iak Ya-paw-lang;
yam lih kaing in mawh yam ot Ya-paw-lang ka haktiex Cia-nang khaix kao num.
4 Ya-paw-lang ot tau mai Sa-cia,
nawh tom koe kaux.
Nawh tawng hoik koe kaux tix,
tom ting rhawm lhaong rhi mai ang lai yung yi ka Sa-lai heue.
5 Sa-lai tom salao ka Ya-paw-lang:
“Krax pa lud koe piang maix,
bun siliex an tom ang lai yung yi ka aux.
Aux tawx nawh ka maix;
nawh tawng hoik koe kaux tix,
tom ang lai yung yi ka aux.a
Kieh Simiang Siyiex prawng mhawm si nax ax heue.”
6 Ya-paw-lang pawk ah nin:
“Chix tix,
nawh hoik mawh bun siliex maix,
mai ot daux tawm taix maix,
maix nieb yuh ka mawx ka nawh kawx chix heue!”
Khaix an,
Sa-lai yuh krax kha rhawm ka Sa-cia,
nawh ang pon kham mai to hu heue.
7 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex yaox ka Sa-cia hawt rawm maw daux tiag daux tu,
plak krax hu veng Su-eux,
8 mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Sa-cia bun siliex Sa-lai eue,
kaoh maix khaing dee mawx?
Maix sang deeh hu plak mawx?”
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik to khaing bun simiang tix Sa-lai heue.”
9 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Maix deeh ing ka bun simiang tix dee an,
mai nghiet ka lox nawh heue.”
10 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex baox deeh ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh doeh ceu cad maix haoh hun,
mai ang lai koe pui pa uik simin.
11 Maix tag sang keh kawn simex tix kaux;
maix sang sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-seu-ma-lix;b
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik mhong grawng pa kha maix tix.
12 Kawn simex maix kawn sang awm la tiag tix mu,
mai yuh naing ka pui ku si dauh;
pui kawx sang yuh naing ka nawh.
Nawh kawx sang tang ot khaing ceu pui tix,
mai tang ot nong nong heue.”
13 Khaix an,
Sa-cia deeh chawk cheng ka to kaux tix:
“Aux rug hoik yaox ka Siyiex pa kia ngien ka aux,
mai kawn kieh aux im ju tix krai grawng kaing in lie?”
Khaix an,
nawh kok Simiang Siyiex pa krai nawh mhong lox an mawh “Siyiex pa kia ngien.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
pui tom kok dung an mawh Pi-eu La-hai Lai;c
dung in ot ka si nax veng Cia-ti-see mai veng Pa-liex heue.
15 dSa-cia keh kawn simex tix kaux son Ya-paw-lang,
Ya-paw-lang tom sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-seu-ma-lix.
16 Yam keh Sa-cia ka Yi-seu-ma-lix son Ya-paw-lang,
Ya-paw-lang koe ju tix daix liah num heue.
