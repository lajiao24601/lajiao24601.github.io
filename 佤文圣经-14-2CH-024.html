Yaw-a-see simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 12.1–16)
24
Yam mawh Yaw-a-see simiang pui Yo-tax,
ju nawh koe aliah num,
nawh giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tipon num.
Miex nawh Si-pi-yax mawh pui veng Piie-see-pa.
2 Yam kawn im ju cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax ka piang haktiex,
Yaw-a-see hoik yuh kaing pa mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
3 Yie-heu-yie-tax laug tix tawx Yaw-a-see koe moeng ra kaux;
mai kiex khawm ra kaux hoik keh kawn simex kawn bun son simiang heue.
4 Yaw-a-see taug mawh simiang,
nawh tom but rhawm tix cox ka nyiex buan.
5 Nawh tawx lox nhie cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we hu koh keen ka pui hun ka daux veng Yo-tax ku dauh,
son sang kieh mawh mau pa cox nyiex buan ku num.
Nawh nhie kix nied tix yuh,
viang mawh nan,
pui Li-we dha ka bawg yam.
6 aYaw-a-see tom kok Yie-heu-yie-tax simiang kix hoek,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai ang bax kieh pui Li-we hu koh mau keen ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung lie?
Keen in hoik mawh pa nhie siliex Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Maw-si pui hun tawx son sang yuh nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex mhawm ka heue.”
7 Kheu hoik mawh pui pa ot greem Ya-tha-li-ya bun pa caox anb pa hoik yuh nyiex buan laux sivoe an,
khaix an mai teei kraung pa singax tom nie viex hu krub Pa-lix heue.
8 Simiang tawx lox nhie pui Li-we yuh teug tan tix mu,
deuh ot ka diang siviex nyiex buan,
9 mai nhie kix hu krai ka pui hun ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka haktiex Yo-tax ku dauh,
viex mau koh keen been pa hoik tat Maw-si siliex Simiang Siyiex tix deuh yam ot ka daux tiag,
mai hoek tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
10 Kawn pui mai simiang khankix kied gaux rhawm kah,
kix tom viex mau koh keen hoek,
mai deuh ot ka daux teug.
11 Pui Li-we ku singaix grawng teug in hoek ka dee ot simiang pui cao kuang kia teug in dee an.
Yam hoik naog teug,
cao kaing pui tiiem lai ka daux nyiex haw simiang mai pa tang cao giex kaing li pa ting tom teei mau lih,
mai deeh deuh teug ot ka dee ot teug.
Kix hoik yuh mawh nin ku singaix,
mawh kheu nan tom yaox tix pon rieb ka mau tom nie heue.
12 Simiang mai Yie-heu-yie-tax tawx mau ka cao pa thung pui cox ka nyiex buan,
mai kieh kix lo tix siao ka cang mai saik simaox,
cang mai khaox,
cang mai tawk rhiem hoek tix yuh kaing.
13 Can pui khawm uik hoik gaux rhawm tix yuh kaing,
mai deeh cox nyiex buan awm pak peung sivoe an,
kieh kaux cong awm sivoe an yuh nan.
14 Khaix hoik hoik kaing pa cox ka nyiex buan,
kix tawx mau khri pa kawn deuk ka simiang mai ka Yie-heu-yie-tax;
Yie-heu-yie-tax tom yuh klong,
pan mai kraung tang ceu lih kah,
son sang kieh mawh pa lo tix siao yam yuh kaing krub ka nyiex buan mai yam yuh krax tan heue.
Pui tik ka been cie Yie-heu-yie-tax
Yam kawn im ju Yie-heu-yie-tax ka piang haktiex,
kawn pui yie ot ka nyiex buan jheeng jheeng mai hawt tix tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa gleeing awm pa khaw tix tan lu hawt awm bawg yam.
15 Yam koe ju Yie-heu-yie-tax tix yieh tix ngoe num nawh yum.
16 Son sang doh keud ka kaing pa yuh nawh son pui Yi-seu-liex mai pa yuh nawh son Siyiex mai khawm can kaing pa yuh nawh son nyiex buan khawm uik,
kix tom si paung nawh ka veng Ta-wue daux gawng si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang heue.
17 Khaix hoik yum Yie-heu-yie-tax,
simiang pui Yo-tax khankix pon kieh simiang Yaw-a-see nghiet lox krai kix.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui ang lai krub Siyiex tax du kix ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
mai hu krub ka riang bun siyiex Ya-seu-la mai ka riang rong tang ceu khankix.
Phi map pa yuh kix kieh Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yo-tax mai ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung.
19 Simiang Siyiex ploi cao jhai ne raw ka kix,
mai nhie kix deeh hoek ka tix,
viang mawh nan,
kawn pui ang nghiet.
20 cKhaix an,
khuan Siyiex lih laig daux Sa-cia-li-yax kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax cao giex kaing li;
nawh tom cung ka dee kok chix yaox kawn pui tix,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Siyiex chawk:
yuh ka mawx pex lai lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex mai kieh krax laux krax leeing hoek lie?
Pex hoik tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh kawx tag sang tik vud ka pex!”
21 Simiang Yaw-a-see hoik rom tix tau mai krax keud pa tieh vheen ka Sa-cia-li-yax;
nawh tawx lox nhie pui hun teei simaox tawk Sa-cia-li-yax yum ka biang brawk nyiex buan.
22 Simiang hoik piawm grawng kaing mhawm pa yuh Yie-heu-yie-tax keeing Sa-cia-li-yax ka nawh,
mai pauh ka Sa-cia-li-yax yum.
Yam sang yum Sa-cia-li-yax,
mai nawh kok yo lox ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex yaox ka can kaing pa yuh maix,
ciyang ka maix heue!”
Kaing pa giex Yaw-a-see hoik si dauh
23 Ka daux num an bawg yam raux,
pui naing Shui-li-yax puing ka Yo-tax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hoik pauh ka can simiang yum khawm uik,
mai baox tud kraung pa pon daux naing hu tom nie,
viex hoek ka Ta-ma-see-keux.
24 Pui naing Shui-li-yax mawh ngie pui tix cub khaing,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh kix pex ka pui naing Yo-tax pa lheu ting riang khaing kix,
mawh kheu hoik tik vud kawn pui ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix.
Simiang Siyiex teei grawng krax in ciyang ka simiang Yaw-a-see.
25 Nawh kied nie mat.
Yam dak pui naing tix deeh,
cao kaing pui naing ot greem nawh tieh vheen ka nawh,
pauh nawh yum ka piang ku it nawh,
mai pawk bix son pa pauh nawh ka kawn simexd cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax.
Pui si paung Yaw-a-see ot ka veng Ta-wue,
viang mawh nan,
ang ot ka daux gawng si meeig pui nyiex haw simiang.
26 Pa tieh vheen ka nawh mawh Sa-pa kawn simex See-mi-ya pa mawh bun miex Ya-meung mai Yaw-sa-pa kawn simex See-mi-lix pa mawh bun miex Maw-ya.
27 Kaing yuh kawn simex Yaw-a-see khankix,
mai lox jhai pa ang mhawm ka nawh,
khawm kaing pa cox nawh nyiex buan hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang.
Kawn simex nawh Ya-ma-shiex plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
