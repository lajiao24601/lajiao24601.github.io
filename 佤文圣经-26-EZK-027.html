Lox rhax yiiem doe son Thai-eux
27
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
nawh ah nin:
2 Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix rhax lox rhax yiiem doe son Thai-eux,
3 mai yiiem son nyiex bang pa jung tix ot ka diang praok rawm sigrit sigra,
dee yuh pui meung klawn tiex tom nie ka lai gee.
Tag caw tix krai ka Thai-eux,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
Thai-eux eue,
maix yaw rang di to kaux tix
mawh veng pa grum mhawm.
4 Maix kieh rawm sigrit sigra mawh nyiex ma tix;
hoik phan yuh maix mawh pa grum mhawm tete.
5 Kix hoik kieh khaox gix gawng He-meung mawh phia,
hoik kieh khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noeng mawh khaox rong man reu.
6 Kix kieh khaox yang yit Pa-sang mawh khaox vai reu,
sau rang sang ka khaox gix Ci-thix kieh mawh phia plak daux.
7 Man reu maix hoik teei man keei pa breeh mhawm khankix caing kah,
hoik mawh kraung pa kaoh khaing Ai-cix,
hoik sidawng tix yaox yam kok jhak ka dee singai.
Man nyiex bang maix hoik teei man pa blawn mhawm yuh kah,
hoik mawh man lung pliex pa viex hoek khaing klawn tiex Yi-li-sa.
8 Pa vai reu maix mawh pui Si-toeng,
pui Ya-fa;
pa phat reu maix mawh pui pa kied ting chix ka daux haktiex maix.
9 Cang mai pa ot ka daux reu mawh cang mai prim pa hoek khaing Cia-pa-leux;
Reu pa ot ka piang rawm sigrit sigra mai pui pa ot ka daux hoik mawh pui pa yuh lai gee ka nyiex bang maix heue.
10 “Pui naing maix mawh pui naing pa vai khaing Paw-see,
Lui-ti-yax,
mai Fu.
Kix vhak dieb tie naok mai mhok rhiem daux nyiex bang maix.
Kix yuh kaing pa koe min plak ngai son maix.
11 Pui naing pa vai khaing Ya-fa mawh cao pa pau guh veng son maix;
pui naing pa vai khaing Cia-ma-tix mawh cao pa kia ngien ka nyiex lhaong maix.
Kix vhak dieb tie naok ka piang guh ding maix.
Kix kieh maix mawh pui pa grum mhawm heue.
12 “Maix yuh lai gee tau mai pui Tha-see,
mai teei ka kraung pa coh cai maix pa kied nie loh ka mau kix,
rhiem kix,
lied kix,
mai si vun kix.
13 Maix yuh lai gee tau mai pui Si-lax,
pui Thu-pa,
pui Mi-seux,
mai teei kraung khrai maix loh ka siliex cawng kix,
mai ka kraung tawng kix.
14 Maix yuh lai gee tau mai pui Thaw-cia-max,
mai teei kraung khrai maix loh ka brung tang kix,
brung naing kix,
mai law kix.
15 Maix yuh lai gee tau mai pui Law-teux,a
teei kraung khrai maix loh ka rhang sang mai khaox pa ting ngawh pa koe ka haktiex hawt rawm sigrit sigra.
16 Maix yuh lai gee tau mai pui Ya-lang,
teei kraung khrai maix tom nie loh ka siang simaox klai,
man lung pliex,
man pa breeh mhawm,
man keei sum pa blawn mhawm,
siang simaox paing,
mai siang simaox raoh cie kix.
17 Maix yuh lai gee tau mai pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai teei kraung khrai maix loh ka nghox ca,
rawm hia,
bee sime,
mai sidah bhawng cie kix.
18-19 “Maix yuh lai gee tau mai pui Ta-ma-see-keux,
teei kraung khrai maix mai kraung pa coh cai maix loh ka plai He-peung mai hauk yung Sa-ha cie kix.
Kix kawx teei ka rhiem pa mhawm mai sidah bhawng loh ka kraung khrai cie maix.
20 Pui Ti-tang teei mhawn brung loh ka kraung khrai cie maix.
21 Simiang pui A-la-pawx mai pui Ci-tax baoh an teei kawn pex,
yung,
pex loh ka kraung khrai cie maix.
22 Pui See-pa mai pui La-max pa yuh lai gee teei siang,
khri,
mai sidah bhawng pa ting ngawh loh ka kraung khrai maix.
23 Veng Ha-lang,
Kang-ni,
Yi-tiien khankix,
pui See-pa pa yuh lai gee,
veng Ya-sux mai Ci-mawx khankix hoik yuh lai gee mai maix khawm uik.
24 Kix viex kraung ceep pa mhawm,
man lung pliex,
man pa breeh mhawm,
man mhawm pa tom piang tiex,
maox pa kied mhawm,
mai maox khankix coh ka maix.
25 bMaix kieh cub pui pa yuh lai gee ka daux reu pa kied ting mawh cao pa tang kraung khrai khankix heue.
Maix awm reu ting pa hu ka piang rawm sigrit sigra,
mai tang kraung khrai naog ka daux.
26 Pui pa vai reu viex maix hoek ka rawm sigrit sigra tang dauh;
bhaung plak lih singaix phlu maix ka piang rawm sigrit sigra.
27 Yam laux reu com daux rawm,
can kraung khrai pa ting ngawh,
khawm pui pa ot daux reu mai cao phat,
cang mai khaox pa ot daux reu mai pui pa yuh lai gee,
pui naing pa ot daux reu khawm uik,
hoik com greem rawm sigrit sigra khawm uik.
28 Lox pui pa com daux rawm sigrit sigra kok yo pui taoh tix,
ca pawk tix luah ang jud ka praok rawm heue.
 
29 Can reu hoik tik tix khawm uik,
pui yuh kaing daux reu ku kaux mai cao phat hoik pon hoek ka praok rawm khawm uik.
30 Kix yiiem grai laux rhawm son maix,
mai sau phawng tiex piang kaing tix,
lu lieh tix ka daux nyaox.
31 Kix khlao hauk kaing tix ghle,
mai ceep kraung yum yam yum pui;
kix laux rhawm laux rhi son maix.
32 Kix rhax lox yiiem doe in son maix ah nin:
Veng mawx pon tiag tix tau mai Thai-eux lie?
Veng mawx pa lheu khaing Thai-eux pa com greem rawm lie?
33 Yam hu tawx maix kraung khrai ka haktiex tang dauh,
meung ka piang haktiex hoik pon kub ka ceu pa siao pa lo khawm uik,
sin ngao maix mai kraung khrai pa coh cai maix,
hoik kieh simiang mag mi.
34 Yam in,
maix si pup ka krax laux leeing daux rawm sigrit sigra;
maix com ka greem rawm sigrit sigra.
Can kraung khrai pa koe maix mai siliex maix,
hoik grai tau mai maix ka daux rawm sigrit sigra khawm uik heue.
35 “Pui pa paung ot klawn tiex hoik yaoh yee kheu yaox tix grawng pa si pup maix;
simiang kix kawx hoik yaoh yee nang,
pak ngai kix hoik jhi krax pa laux rhawm.
36 Maix hoik laux leeing,
hoik laux leeing ju heue!
Pui pa yuh lai gee ka piang haktiex ku dauh hoik lhat graung khawm uik,
lhat tix si pup ka krax pa laux leeing awm maix yuh nan heue.”
