18
Pui Su-yax pa kaux Pi-leu-ta baox krai lox ah nin:
2 Yaw-paw eue,
maix krai lox pie dawt chix lie?
Maix yien yawg tix keud mhawm mhawm,
yix tom taug yaox tix pon krai lox ka maix.
3 Maix ce yix awm khawng brie lie?
Yix daox awm to sat yuh nan lie?
4 Krax song rhawm maix hoik yuh to kaux maix laux;
khex sang riem haktiex kheu krax pa song rhawm song rhi maix lie
khex sang gah Siyiex gawng lua kieh naog yuh rhawm rhi maix lie?
 
5 aPa riang cie pui pa caox pa caig tag sang yeet;
rao ngu cie nawh ang lai baox deeh cap.
6 Kawk ngu ka daux nyiex bang nawh hoik faik;
kawk ngu pa vhak ot plak piang kawx hoik sang yeet.
7 Diang caong pa kaux cong nawh hoik jauh tix yung yang;
nawh deeh sideeing tix ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix.
8 To kaux nawh hu laig ka daux rub;
caong nawh krix kah.
9 Daux jhawk pon giex ka loek caong nawh;
daux jhawk pon kip ka nawh.
10 Ka piang tiex hoik taung ka daux jhawk;
ka daux krax hu nawh hoik tawm rub ot kah.
11 Krax lhat krax graung hoik sivie tix kuan ka nawh,
tix diang caong hoik tix diang caong krup ka nawh.
12 Singaix pa hoik luan nawh mag mi,
singaix yam in nawh cai rhawm;
krax laux krax leeing ca krox jun ka praok nawh.
13 Jhawm saux pa kieh pui yum lhawn ka to kaux nawh khawm uik;
taix caong nawh hoik laux khawm uik.
14 Tik nawh lih khaing ka daux nyiex gox bang pa ot nawh,
mai viex nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai simiang yum.
15 Ka daux nyiex gox bang nawh,
khaix hoik sau sidah mhatb son sang kieh singax,
deeh kieh pui tang kaux laig paung ot daux.
16 Riah pa koe plak greem nawh hoik kroh;
lhax pa koe plak piang nawh hoik khrum.
17 Ka piang haktiex ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa doh keud ka nawh;
ceu kaux nawh paox ceu paox cad nawh hoik piawm kah.
18 Tag sang teei tix tik nawh khaing daux kawn pui haktiex,
grheu nawh lih khaing daux pa riang,
hu laig daux pa faik.
19 Nawh ang koe kawn praix kawn saux ka daux ceu pui tix;
ka dee ot nawh ang bax deuh pui ot ka tix kaux kawx.
20 Pui plak klip singaix mhong grawng nawh mai daox deng kah;
pui plak lih singaix tawng grawng krax pa si pup nawh mai lhat graung kah.
21 In hoik mawh dee si dauh krax lawng pui pa caox pa caig;
in hoik mawh kaing pa hoek piang pui pa ang lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex heue.
