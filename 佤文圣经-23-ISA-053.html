53
aPui cie nawh pawk lox ah nin:
pui mawx chix yum rhawm ka lox pa sidiex ex lie?
Siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex sang jhi tix ka pui mawx lie?
2 In mawh alo Simiang Siyiex:
siliex nawh tag sang awm laoh ka simie pa taug tawx riah tix yuh nan,
awm rib riem pa kuah ka dee kroh tiex yuh nan.
Nawh ang koe pak peung,
mai ang mhawm grang,
ang kieh ex koe rhawm tix jhak;
pak peung nawh ang mawh pa tang siyox,
ang kieh ex koe rhawm pa simie kah.
3 Pui hoik map nai ka nawh,
tik vid ka nawh;
nawh hoik kham gee krax kha rhawm,
mai hoik yaox krax laux rhawm.
Pui hoik mox plak ngai tix mai ang jhak ka nawh pie tix khiep ngai;
pui hoik map nai ka nawh,
mai ex ang yung yi ka nawh.
4 bViang mawh nan,
nawh hoik klawm ka krax pa khi rhawm ex khawm uik;
nawh hoik klawm ka krax kha rhawm pa caw ex tix sang kham heue.
Ex tieh tix ah nawh khaw tix kham krax ciyang,
khaw tix kham krax pa si vied Siyiex,
mai krax pa kieh nawh kha rhawm tix.
5 cKheu son phi map ex,
pui hoik klauh nawh mat;
kheu son krax phit krax lud ex,
pui hoik lauh ka nawh.
Kheu pa kham nawh krax ciyang,
ex hoik pon tix brah kua.
Kheu pa kham nawh pa si vied,
ex hoik pon tix moi.
6 dEx hoik awm cub yung pa grai khawm uik yuh nan,
hoik tang lawng krax cie tix heue.
Viang mawh nan phi map ex khawm uik,
Simiang Siyiex hoik klawm nawh tang ex khawm uik heue.
 
7 efNawh kham krax pa tuk tiak,
mai krax pa nu nai pui,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang ploi lox tix lih pie tix gum.
Nawh awm kawn yung pa krox jun pui pauh tix yum yuh nan,
awm yung pa ghrip pui hauk yuh nan,
ang nye ang nyux pie tix gum kawx heue.
8 Pui giex laung ka nawh,
prawng ka nawh,
pauh ka nawh,
ang koe pui pa teem coi ka nawh.
Nawh hu khaing haktiex kheu phi map pui cie tix,
pui pauh ka nawh kheu krax phit pui cie tix.
9 gViang nawh ang sax yuh krax pa caox pa caig tix bawg kawx,
ang sax krai lox pie lox lhien tix bawg kawx,
pui hoik si paung nawh ot tau mai pui pa caox pa caig;
nawh rom tix si meeig tau mai cao mi heue.
 
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Nawh kham krax kha rhawm kha rhi hoik mawh alo aux;
grawng yum nawh hoik mawh krax tan lu pa taoh ka phi map.
Nawh tag sang yaox ka doeh ceu cad tix;
nawh hoik koe singaix ju pa lang.
Ka daux nawh,
aux tag sang yuh krax keud krax sawn tix lih.
11 Kheu hoik yaox krax kha rhawm tom si dauh ju tix,
siliex aux tag sang deeh pon ka krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
nawh tag sang tawng krax pa kha rhawm tix ang mawh pa giex pa going.
Siliex pa rauh see aux,
tag sang kieh pui haoh pui hun lih mawh pui rauh see kheu pa hoik tawng ka aux;
kheu son nawh,
aux tag sang pluat ploi ka phi map kix.
12 hMawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh nawh koe min ka daux pui pa ting ceu khankix,
pon min plak ngai ka daux pui pa ting riang ting rao khankix.
Nawh cu tix tawx ju tix,
klawm ka krax lawng pui phi map.
Nawh hoik klawm ka phi map pui haoh pui hun,
hoik rawg hoik mhaing son krax phit krax lud kix heue.i
