Rawg buan son sang yien yawg meung
85
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik dawg chix kia ngien ka haktiex cie tix;
kieh pui Yi-seu-liex pon tix deeh cox sum nyiex ma tix mhawm.
2 Maix hoik pluat ploi ka phi map pui cie tix;
hoik pluat ploi ka krax lud krax loe kix khawm uik.
3 Maix ang lai baox deeh song rhawm ka kix;
hoik deeh teei ka krax pa kied song rhawm tix heue.
 
4 Siyiex pa taoh ka ex eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh kieh ex haok huan;
khiox khaw maix naox ka krax pa song rhawm tix ka ex.
5 Khex sang song rhawm maix ka ex leeing ju lie?
Krax pa song rhawm maix ang chix naox tix leeing ju lie?
6 Khiox khaw maix kieh ex deeh baox haoh huan;
mawh pui cie maix,
mai ex tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix.
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
jhi krax muh maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix ka ex;
khiox khaw maix taoh ka ex heue.
 
8 Aux sang bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex;
phan ex ang lai baox deeh hu krax pa daox aw,
nawh tag sang tawx pa nhim ka pui cie tix.
9 Nawh tag sang taoh ka pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh tete;
min pa riang plak ngai nawh tag sang naog ka daux haktiex ex heue.
 
10 Krax muh mai pa kaux sijax tag sang lai si pup ka paox tix;
pa rauh pa see mai pa nhim tag sang lai kawm paox tix.
11 Pa kaux sijax kawn pui kaoh lih khaing haktiex;
krax pa khaw caw Siyiex kaoh hoek khaing rao ma.
12 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh ex haok huan;
haktiex ex tag sang pon pa plix sok tom nie.
13 Pa rauh see tag sang hu ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
cox khre daux krax son nawh heue.
