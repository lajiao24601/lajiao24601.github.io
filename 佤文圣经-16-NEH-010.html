Lox ka daux lai sijax mukheu
28 Ex pui Yi-seu-liex in kix,
mai cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
pui pau nyiex buan,
pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan,
pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan,
mai can pui pa hoik priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex khawm uik,
hoik khan tix tang ot khaing pui tang ceu pa ot ka daux haktiex ex.
Ex mai moeng ex,
khawm kawn praix ex pa hoik pon tawng praix sidawng khankix,
29 ot dee in hoik rom tix ah lox tiex tu tau mai simiang ex:
phan ex tieh vheen,
tag sang pon krax ciyang kah.
Ex hoik tiex tu tix sang hawt lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx Siyiex siliex nawh pa mawh Maw-si krai.
Ex tag caw tix nghiet lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh lox tawx Simiang Siyiex ka ex;
ex tag caw tix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh mai lox been nawh khawm uik heue.
30 aEx ang mex moeng pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka daux haktiex ex khankix.
31 bPhan pui haktiex an viex nghox koi,
mai kraung tang ceu ang ah,
hoek tix coh tawx ka ex ka daux singaix laux,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix pa singax tang ngaix,
mai ex ang pon tix liag heue.
Yam hoek aliah num ku bawg,
ex ang sum kaung sum ma,
mai ang pon tix sawg ceuk pui ka ex khankix heue.
32 cKu num,
ex ku kaux tag caw tix tan lu mau phuan kongkheu,
teem coi pui tawx ngawh pa siao son nyiex buan heue.
33 Ex tag caw tix tawx khao mun pa siao son pa yuh krax krub daux nyiex buan,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi ku singaix khawng brie,
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ku singaix,
khawng tan pa singax son singaix laux,
khawng tan ku ceu yam haok khix ku khix mai khawng tan pa singax daux poi tang ceu khankix,
khawng tan pa yuh son phi map pui Yi-seu-liex,
khawm can ceu pa sang lo son kaing nyiex buan khawm uik heue.
34 Ex pui hun khawm uik,
mai cao giex kaing li,
mai pui Li-we tag caw tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
ku num yuh krax pa cawk lai,
caw ceu cad cub pui nyiex mawx pa caw tix tawx khix,
son sang gleeing tix tan lu khawng tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex heue.
35 dKu num,
ex tag caw tix teei nghox koi pa liang tix pon,
mai plix khaox pa liang tix plix viex hoek tan lu daux nyiex buan heue.
36 eEx ku kaux tag caw tix hawt awm pa hoik tat lox tat jhawm prawng tix deuh,
teei kawn simex pa liang tix keh viex hoek dee ot cao giex kaing pa yuh kaing krub ka Siyiex dee an,
mai tan lu ka Siyiex;
mai khawm kawn moi pa liang moi miex tix keh,
khawm yung mai pex pa liang tix keh,
viex hu tan lu ka Siyiex heue.
37 fKu num,
ex tag caw tix ran teei glua khao mun pa hoik yuh ka nghox koi pa liang tix pon,
viex hoek ka dee ot cao giex kaing li daux nyiex buan dee an;
mai tag caw tix tan lu nang khawm plai,
bee sime,
mai plix khaox ku ceu khankix heue.
Ex tag caw tix teei pa hoik pon ex kao son tix son lih,
viex hoek tawx ka pui Li-we pa caw tix pon khaing daux nyiex yaong ex kao son tix son.g
38 hYam teei tix tawx kao son tix son,
cao giex kaing li doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng khankix sang ot tau mai pui Li-we,
khaix an,
pui Li-we teei lih kao son tix son khaing daux pa hoik pon kix hoek kao son tix son,
mai ap tix tawx daux krao nyiex buan son sang siao ka nyiex buan.
39 Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Li-we baox viex nghox koi,
plai,
bee sime krax tan khankix ot daux krao kraung khrai nyiex buan,
pa mawh dee pau cao giex kaing li,
pui pau nyiex buan,
mai pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka nyiex buan.
Ex ang doh prah ghram nyiex buan Siyiex ex heue.
