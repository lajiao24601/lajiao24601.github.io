Gum lox deuh Ya-keux sivoe sang yum tix
49
Ya-keux khrawm ka kawn simex tix khankix mai ah nin ka kix:
“Hoek sivoe aux tin,
aux sang krai pex mhong kaing pa sang lih hoek tix mawh ka piang pex:
2 Kawn simex Ya-keux khankix eue,
khrawm paox tix hoek tau,
mai nghiet!
Priex rhawm tix nghiet ka lox keeing pex Yi-seu-liex heue.
 
3 Kawn ai aux Lui-piien eue,
maix mawh riang rao aux;
maix mawh kawn simex pa jauh tix keh yam ting aux,
mawh kawn simex aux pa ting riang pa kied koe min plak ngai tete.
4 Maix awm rawm ting pa pu pak,
mai ang pon tieb tie.
Maix ot tau mai moeng iak keeing tix,
hoik seeing brai yuh ku it keeing maix;
mawh kheu nan,
maix tag ang pon tix mawh pa ot sivoe.
 
5 Si-miien mai Li-we mawh pux aik ka daux pa nu pa khawm;
kiex kum paox tix teei vaig gawn yuh pa caox pa caig.
6 Aux ang yuh tau mai laig daux bang kum kiex,
ang yuh paox pie tau mai kiex;
mawh kheu jao song rhawm kiex,
kiex hoik pauh ka pui,
kiex hawt alo to kaux cao tix,
mai mug ka si niag bax moi dut heue.
7 Kiex kin jhawm song rhawm,
khaw tix pon krax jhax lawg;
kiex kied ting rhawm ting rhi,
khaw tix pon krax ciyang;
aux tag guah kiex khaing haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik;
aux tag kieh kiex prung prang khaing pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
 
8 Yo-tax eue,
pux aik maix khankix tag yaog yaw maix.
Maix tag jhawp ka ngawg pui pa koeng ka maix;
pux aik maix khankix tag hoek tix krub ka sivoe maix heue.
9 aYo-tax awm kawn sivai sang si,
kaoh khaing dee sawg tix ih kuk,
mai deeh hoek daux dawh rang;
nawh nyat to kaux tix it,
pui ang pon hu so ka nawh!
10 Yo-tax ca kawn koe siawp sibrawm tom si dauh;
doeh ceu pui nawh sang kuang leeing ju.
Meung ka piang haktiex sang tawx keen ka nawh;
pui ka piang haktiex ku ceu sang krub ka nawh.
11 Nawh phrawk kawn la ka nhu plix siiet,
phrawk ka nhu plix siiet pa mhawm.
Nawh si daux kraung tix ka daux dung plai raoh awm nham;
12 ngai nawh hoik raoh kheu nyaux tix plai,
nawh hoik paing rhang kheu nyaux tix rhawm teeh heue.
 
13 Si-pu-loeng tag ot praok rhawm sigrit sigra.
Nawh mawh diang praok rawm sigrit sigra dee deuh reu;
mee haktiex nawh hoek tom veng Si-toeng heue.
 
14 Yi-sa-cia mawh pa ting riang awm la,
Tom it si nax kawg yung.
15 Nawh muh dee yien yawg haktiex;
mai muh ka haktiex dee mhawm,
nawh tag klawm beeh glawm kraung cien piang klip tix,
mawh siliex pa yuh kaing taug mao heue.
 
16 Tang tag kuang ka cie pui tix,
awm doeh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
17 Tang tag awm si uing ka praok krax;
si uing pa koe jai pa ot hawt krax iak;
son sang tuh ka loek caong brung,
mai sang kieh cao brug brung raux plak khaix heue.
 
18 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux krox jun ka krax taoh maix heue!
 
19 Cia-teux tag si pup krax tud rhim pui;
Viang mawh nan,
nawh tag deeh veeh tix leue ka kix.
 
20 Haktiex Ya-seux tag mawh pa haoh priex preem pa mhawm khaing;
Nawh koe priex pa kied nyawm pon yuh simiang ih heue.
 
21 Na-fu-tha-lix mawh cak miex pa bhla tix,
Nawh keh kawn cakb pa mhawm heue.
 
22 Yaw-seux awm la tiag praok rawm klawng,
awm kawn law tiagc ka piang gawng.
23 Pui pa koeng sau riang tix yuh naing ka nawh,
siao ak siao te hawt puing ka nawh.
24 Viang mawh nan,
deuh rhawm tix Siyiex Ya-keux pa ting siawp sibrawm,
ak nawh ca kawn koe riang;
deuh rhawm tix cao liang,
pa mawh cao teem coi ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
plawng taix nawh ca kawn koe riangd.
25 Mawh Siyiex keeing maix pa teem coi maix,
Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm tag nawb buan ka maix.
Nawh tawx rawm ciex pa kaoh khaing daux rao ma,
dee keet rawm daux raux pa mox pa koe plak greem ka maix;
moi brung haoh hun,
kawn saux kawn sex haoh hun.
26 Nawh kieh nghox koi maix haoh huan,
tai pruh si jheeing;
koe gawng tom dix dix,
koe gawng ka leeing ju,
kieh buan son in khawm uik hoek ka piang Yaw-seux,
lih ka piang pux aik nawh pa kied mhawm an tix kaux.
27 Piien-ya-ming awm sox la pa kied caox,
nawh ih ka nex to tiag pa pon tix pon ngawb cao cao,
pon bo guah nex to tiag pa pon rhim tix heue.”
 
28 Plak piang in mawh keeing doeh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu,
mai gum lox si kah ka kawn simex tix ku kaux;
nawh nawb buan ka kix ku kaux awm buan son pa khaw kix tix pon heue.
Grawng yum Ya-keux mai si paung nawh
29 Ya-keux tawx lox ka kawn simex tix khankix ah nin:
“Aux sang dex yum,
son sang hu dee ot tax du dee an.
Pex si paung aux mai keeing aux khankix ka daux rang pa koe daux kaung Yi-fu-loeng ceu pui Heux,
30 eDaux rang in ot ka Cia-nang,
ka daux rang Me-pi-la ka plak lih singaix Mang-lix,
mawh Ya-paw-la-hang pa hoik liag ka Yi-fu-loeng ceu pui Heux,
son sang mawh haktiex si meeig cie nawh.
31 fYa-paw-la-hang mai moeng nawh Sa-la kawx si paung tix dee an,
Yi-sha mai moeng nawh Li-pai-cia kawx si paung tix dee an,
aux kawx si paung Li-yax dee an.
32 Haktiex an mai kaung daux rang an mawh pa hoik liag khaing ceu pui Heux.
Pex hu si paung aux dee an.”
33 gKhaix hoik tawm Ya-keux lox ka kawn simex tix khankix,
tom deeh noi tix it,
mai dut rhawm heue.
