Grawng pa phit vao mai Ti-na
34
Koe tix ngaix,
kawn bun pa keh Ya-keux mai Li-yax pa kaux Ti-na hu vhi ka kawn bun haktiex Cia-nang.
2 Simiang ceu pui Si-we pa kaux Ha-mawx,
koe kawn simex tix kaux pa kaux See-ciien;
nawh yaox ka Ti-na,
mai giex tix phit vao mai nawh,
3 See-ciien kheu yaox mhawm grang Ti-na,
kied laig rhawm ka nawh,
mai keud tix sang pon krax muh nawh.a
4 Nawh rawg mhaing lox ka keeing tix ah nin:
“Keud sawn son sang teei aux tix moeng kawn bun in liak.”
5 Ya-keux tawng grawng pa phit vao See-ciien mai kawn bun tix.
Yam an kawn simex nawh khankix kawn ot ka daux tiag pau ka khawng brie,
kheu nan nawh ang nyang nye,
krox kix hoek.
6 Keeing See-ciien Ha-mawx hoek ka Ya-keux,
mai rawg kawn simex tix koe moeng,
7 Kawn simex Ya-keux khankix taug hoek khaing daux tiag.
Yam mhong kix grawng kaing an,
kied sidaing song rhawm kah,
eei grawng pa phit vao See-ciien mai kawn bun nyiex Ya-keux mawh kaing kaig tua pa map nai ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
8 Ha-mawx ah nin ka kix:
“Kawn simex aux See-ciien,
kied muh ka kawn bun nyiex pex,
kieh nawh teei tix moeng liak.
9 Kieh ex pui ra ceu lai mex moeng paox tix:
nyiex yix teei tix moeng kawn bun pex;
nyiex pex kawx teei tix moeng kawn bun yix.
10 Yuh nin,
pex tag pon tix ot ka daux haktiex yix,
chix tang sawg tix ot dee sang ot tix,
yuh lai gee,
liag ka kraung khrai.”
11 Khaix an,
See-ciien rawg mhaing ka keeing Ti-na mai pux aik nawh khankix:
“Rawg pex tawx pak ngai;
pex sang mhaing ceu pa tix,
aux kawx cu kah.
12 Ca mawh cu pex aux moeng ka kawn bun nyiex pex,
pex sang ih khawng phak ceu pa tix ang ah,
aux kawx cu tix tawx uik heue.”
13 Kawn simex Ya-keux khankix kheu phit See-ciien mai pux kix Ti-na,
tom pie lhien lox cu ka lox pa rawg See-ciien mai keeing nawh Ha-mawx.
14 Kix ah nin:
“Yix ang pon tawx nan tix cie pui pa ang nyang Pat-hak,
in mawh krax pa kaig tua ka yix heue.
15 Phan pex rieb ka grawng in tix ceu,
yix tom cu ka lox pa rawg pex:
pex caw tix awm yix ku kaux,
simex ku kaux caw tix Pat-hak tix.
16 Yuh nin,
yix tom cu ex lai mex moeng ka paox tix,
mai ot daux haktiex pex,
lih mawh pui tix ceu khaing.
17 Phan pex ang rieb ka grawng in Pat-hak tix,
yix sang viex nan tix hu,
lih hu singai khaing tin heue.”
18 Ha-mawx mai kawn simex nawh See-ciien khaw rhawm ka grawng in.
19 Pui awn ju an hoik mawh pui pa ting ceu son daux nyiex ma,
kheu kied muh nawh kawn bun Ya-keux,
kheu nan,
nawh tom hawt tix yuh ka grawng in dum mawx dum an heue.
20 Kheu nan,
Ha-mawx mai kawn simex nawh See-ciien khrawm pui hoek ka diang siviex veng,
mai ah nin ka pui haktiex nawh:
21 “Pui in kix hoik yuh paox pie mai ex,
kieh kix ot haktiex in,
yuh lai yuh gee.
Haktiex vieh va,
kub son kix.
Ex chix teei tix moeng kawn bun kix,
mai kix chix teei tix moeng kawn bun ex khankix.
22 Viang mawh nan,
kix mhaing lox tix gum,
ex pa mawh simex caw tix Pat-hak awm simex kix,
kix tom tag chix cu tix ot tau mai ex,
lih mawh pui tix ceu khaing.
23 Ex cu khaing heue,
son sang yaox kix tix ot daux ex.
Yuh nin,
khawng brie kix mai kraung khrai cie kix,
tag yaox tix mawh cie ex ang mawh lie?”
24 Pui pa ot ka daux veng an khawm uik,
hoik khaw rhawm ka lox pa kraix Ha-mawx mai See-ciien;
kheu nan,
simex ka daux veng khawm uik rieb tix Pat-hak heue.
25 Khaix hoik luan loe ngaix,
yam kawn saux nex kawn simex khaix Pat-hak tix,
kawn simex Ya-keux ra kaux,
pux ke lih Ti-na,
Si-miien mai Li-we,
ang nye mai viex vaig gawn laig daux veng an.
Kiex pauh kawn simex ka daux veng khawm uik.
26 Khawm Ha-mawx mai kawn nawh See-ciien kawx hoik pauh kah,
khaix an deeh teei Ti-na hu khaing nyiex See-ciien.
27 Khaix hoik pauh kix kah,
kawn simex Ya-keux tang kaux khankix tud rhim ka veng an,
pawk bix ka son nan tix kheu pon pa kaing pa tuah.
28 Kix rhim tix teei phung yung,
phung moi,
la khawm can pa koe daux veng mai daux kaung.
29 Kix rhim kraung khrai pa ngawh khawm uik,
mai gah hu ka kraung khrai sin ngao nyiex pui,
giex laung ka bun miex mai kawn nyawm khawm uik heue.
30 Ya-keux ah nin ka Si-miien mai Li-we:
“Pex hoik yuh krax laux ka aux.
Jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai,
pui Cia-nang,
pui Pi-li-si mai pui haktiex in ku ceu tag sang koeng ka aux.
Pui ex ang nie,
phan kix khrawm tix puing ka ex,
ex khawm nyiex tag yum uik heue.”
31 Viang mawh nan,
kix pawk lox:
“Ex ang doh kieh pui map nan tix mawh bun pa coh kaux heue.”
