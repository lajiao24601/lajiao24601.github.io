See-thi-fang krai lox sidawng
7
Cao giex kaing li pa ting chawk ka See-thi-fang ah nin:
“Rug koe kaing pa yuh nin lie?”
2 aSee-thi-fang pawk ah nin:
“Tax yiex miex keeing pux aik pux o khankix,
nghiet heue!
Yam kawn ot tax du ex Ya-paw-la-hang ka Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax,
yam ang nawh bax gah tix hu khaing Ha-lang,
min plak ngai Siyiex hoik jhi tix lih ka nawh,
3 mai ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix lih hu khaing haktiex ot maix mai paox ceu paox cad maix,
hoek ka haktiex pa jhi aux maix hu heue.’
4 bMawh kheu nan,
Ya-paw-la-hang tom lih hu khaing haktiex Cia-leu-tix,
mai hu paung ot ka Ha-lang.
Khaix hoik yum keeing nawh,
Siyiex gah nawh hoek ka haktiex ot nawh,
pa mawh haktiex pa paung ot pex yam in.
5 cYam an Siyiex ang bax tawx sin ngao ceu pa tid tix ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
pie haktiex pa sang ot nawh kawx ang bax koe.
Viang mawh nan,
hoik mawh Siyiex pa ap tix tawx haktiex in ka nawh,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie nawh mai doeh ceu cad nawh,
viang nawh ang bax koe kawn simex bawg yam an.
6 dSiyiex ah nin ka nawh:
‘Doeh ceu cad maix tag sang paung ot ka meung tang meung,
yuh siliex cawng dee an,
mai kham krax nu nai pui pon yieh num.
7 eViang mawh nan,
aux tag sang ciyang ka meung an pa kieh pex yuh siliex cawng.
Khaix an kix tag lih hu khaing haktiex dee an,
mai krub aux ka dee in heue.’
8 fMawh kheu nan,
Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai kieh krax pa Pat-hak mawh mhai.
Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-paw-la-hang Pat-hak Yi-sha khaix hoik keh ka daux daix ngaix;
yuh nan nang,
Yi-sha kawx hoik Pat-hak Ya-keux,
Ya-keux hoik Pat-hak cao kaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra kaux heue.
9 g“Cao kaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix boh rhawm ka Yaw-seux,
mai coh nawh hoek haktiex Ai-cix.
Viang mawh nan Siyiex ot mai nawh,
10 htaoh nawh luat luan khaing krax laux leeing khawm uik.
Yam cung nawh ka plak sivoe ngai Pha-raw simiang Ai-cix,
Siyiex hoik tawx nawh koe grang mai koe ping nyia cuyi.
Pha-raw ap nawh mawh simiang meung mai simiang cao kuang daux nyiex haw simiang.
11 iAng koe miex glang tix,
haktiex Ai-cix mai Cia-nang khawm uik,
sibhawm praix pa ting kin lih hoek kah,
mai hoik lih mawh pa kied kieh pui laux leeing;
tax du ex hoik cud ka priex preem.
12 Ya-keux hoik mhong koe priex preem ka Ai-cix,
nawh tom ploi kawn simex tix khankix pa mawh tax du ex,
hoek ka dee an;
in hoik mawh ka daux bawg tix.
13 jYam hu kix ka daux bawg ra,
Yaw-seux mai pux aik nawh khankix ra lai tawng ka paox tix;
jauh yam an Pha-raw tawng ka cub nyiex ma Yaw-seux.
14 kYaw-seux ploi pui hu ka keeing tix Ya-keux,
mai ghaok nawh khawm pui nyiex nawh khawm uik hoek ka haktiex Ai-cix;
ceu pui nyiex nawh pa hoek khawm uik koe atigliah phuan kaux.
15 lYa-keux lih plak seh ka haktiex Ai-cix;
nawh mai tax du nawh yum dee an.
16 mTo kaux yum kix viex hoek ka See-ciien,
mai si paung ka daux si meeig pa hoik liag Ya-paw-la-hang ka kawn praix Ha-mawx heue.
17 n“Singaix yam sang yuh Siyiex lih ka gum lox pa hoik ap tix ka Ya-paw-la-hang dex sang hoek,
kaux pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux haktiex Ai-cix hoik kied thiem thie tix tom nie.
18 Khaix an koe simiang khraox tix kaux pa ang tawng ka Yaw-seux pie ceeix jauh tix giex kaing Ai-cix.
19 oSimiang in teei krax keud pa pie lhien gut kheem ka paox ceu paox cad ex,
yuh kaing pa ang khaw ang jiet ka tax du ex,
khin kix tik ka kawn ngie,
ang kieh kix im ju.
20 pKa daux bawg yam an,
Maw-si lih tix keh;
nawh kied mhawm grang,
mai eei nawh ot daux nyiex loe khix,
21 qhoek yam tik vud ka nawh,
kawn bun Pha-raw teei tix eei ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh awm kawn simex tix yuh nan.
22 Nawh hoik rieb tix gau ka ngai lai pui Ai-cix,
lox ah si kah krai nawh mai kaing pa yuh nawh hoik mawh pa koe siawp sibrawm heue.
23 r“Yam koe Maw-si tix pon num,
nawh but rhawm tix sang kieh tix tawng grawh grawng ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
24 Koe tix bawg,
nawh yaox lauh pui Ai-cix ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
nawh tom hoek tix teem coi ka nawh,
prawng rauh see son nawh,
mai pauh ka pui Ai-cix an.
25 Nawh ce paox ceu paox cad tix sang tawng grawng sang teei Siyiex nawh taoh ka kix,
viang mawh nan kix ang tawng.
26 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
nawh yaox pui Yi-seu-liex ra kaux pa lauh paox tix,
nawh keud tix sang kieh kiex ah nin:
‘Pax hoik mawh paox ceu paox cad,
yuh ka mawx pax lai sang yuh paox tix laux leeing lie?’
27 Pui pa yuh pa ang khaw ka paox ceu paox cad tix an nyheen ka Maw-si yoi,
mai ah nin:
‘Mawx pa kieh maix mawh simiang yix,
mawh simiang cao prawng pa hoek tix kuang ka yix lie?
28 Maix kawx keud tix sang pauh ka aux,
awm hoik pauh tix ka pui Ai-cix kawx kawx yuh nan lie?
29 sYam hoik mhong Maw-si ka lox in,
nawh to lih hu khaing Ai-cix,
mai hoek ka Mi-tiien.
Ka dee an,
nawh keh kawn simex ra kaux heue.
30 t“Khaix hoik luan tix pon num,
daux tiag pa dex ka gawng Si-nai,
koe kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux jhi Maw-si yaox tix ka daux rao ngu pang kat.
31 Maw-si yaox grawng in kied daox deng kah,
nawh hu dex kah,
mai sang jhak tix yaox mawh ceu pa tix.
Yam an,
nawh mhong lox Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
32 ‘Aux mawh Siyiex tax du maix,
pa mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux.’
Maw-si lhat graung yaoh yee,
ang pon dong ngai tix jhak.
33 Simiang Siyiex baox ah nin ka nawh:
‘Peeik kiiep maix!
Mawh kheu dee cung maix mawh dee singax.
34 Krax kha rhawm pa kham pui cie aux ka daux Ai-cix,
aux hoik yaox ka sidawng khawm uik;
aux kawx hoik mhong ka lox pa riag yo kix,
aux son sang lih taoh ka kix.
Maix hoek heue!
Aux sang ploi maix hu ka Ai-cix heue.’
35 u“Maw-si in hoik mawh pui pa tieb tie pui Yi-seu-liex kah,
pa chawk ah nin:
‘Mawx pa kieh maix mawh simiang yix,
mawh simiang pa hoek tix kuang ka yix lie’ pui an.
Kawn cau Siyiex pa jhi tix lih ka daux pang kat,
Siyiex ploi nawh mawh simiang,
mawh cao pa taoh.
36 vNawh thung kawn pui lih khaing daux Ai-cix,
ka daux yam ot nawh ka Ai-cix,
ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
mai ka daux tiag daux tu tix pon num,
nawh hoik yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am tom nie.
37 xHoik mawh nang Maw-si in pa krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
‘Siyiex tag sang yaog cao jhai tix kaux son pex lih khaing ka daux paox ceu paox cad pex,
awm hoik yaog nawh ka aux yuh nan.’w
38 yNawh hoik ot tau mai pui hun Yi-seu-liex ka daux tiag daux tu;
nawh mai tax du ex khawm kawn cau Siyiex pa si kah mai nawh ka gawng Si-nai hoik ot tau mai nawh;
nawh hoik rieb ka gum lox Siyiex pa koe leeing ju,
mai hoik sidiex gum lox in ka yix heue.
39 “Viang mawh nan,
tax du yix ang nghiet ka nawh,
mai ang simi ka nawh,
kix kawn simie tix deeh ing ka Ai-cix.
40 zKix ah nin ka Ya-loeng:
‘Maix yuh riang krub son yix,
son sang thung krax ka plak sivoe ngaix yix.
Yix ang tawng Maw-si pa thung yix lih khaing Ai-cix hoik si pup ka kaing ceu pa tix.’
41 aMawh kheu nan,
kix yuh ka riang kawn moi tix mu,
mai yuh krax pa tan lu kah,
son sang yuh krax gaux rhawm ka ceu pa hoik yuh taix tix lih.
42 bViang mawh nan,
Siyiex veeh ngai tix ang jhak ka kix,
mai ghram kix hu krub ka simuing rao ma,
awm hoik krai phuk lai cao jhai ah nin:
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
ka daux yam ot pex ka daux tiag daux tu tix pon num,
pex ang bax tan tix tawx krax tan lu ka aux heue!
43 Pex hoik grawng ka nyiex gox buan man cie siyiex Maw-law,
mai baox grawng ka riang simuing siyiex Li-fang,
pa mawh riang rao pa yuh pex tix krub ka heue.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux gah pex hu ka praok Pa-pi-loeng heue.
44 c“Tax du ex hoik ot ka daux tiag daux tu pa hoik tiag awm nyiex gox buan man cie Siyiex pa lih tix bleeh,
in hoik mawh pak peung pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa krai Siyiex ka Maw-si.
45 dKhaix an tax du ex plud ka nyiex gox buan man in;
yam mawh cao pa grheu pui tang ceu tang cad hu khaing ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
pon tix ot ka haktiex an,
Yaw-su-yax mai kix tom gah nyiex gox buan man in hu ot dee an,
tom hoek ka baux singaix ju Ta-wue.
46 fTa-wue hoik pon krax pa khaw rhawm Siyiex,
mai khiox khaw tix sum nyiex buan tix lhang son Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Ya-keuxe;
47 gviang mawh nan,
khaix an pa sum nyiex buan son Siyiex mawh Saw-law-meung.
48 “Tete te lai,
Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ang ot daux nyiex pa hoik sum kawn pui ka taix tix,
awm hoik krai lox jhai ah nin:
49 hSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
rao ma mawh dang ngeung aux;
haktiex mawh ngot dee deuh aux caong tix.
Pex tag sang sum nyiex buan pa yuh ka mawx son aux lie?
Dee mawx mawh nyiex dee laux lee aux tix lie?
50 In kix khawm uik ang hoik mawh pa phan yuh aux lie?
51 i“Pex pa mawh pui pa kiien rhawm eue,
rhawm rhi pex hoik thox tix,
daux yhaok pex ang mhong ka gum lox Siyiex heue!
Pex mai tax du pex hoik awm paox tix yuh nan,
ca yuh krax pa tieb tie ka Khuan Singax Singeh heue!
52 Cao jhai mawx pa ang bax kham krax pa tuk tiak tuk te tax du pex lie?
Cao jhai khankix hoik krai pao grawng sang lih hoek siliex pa rauh pa see an,
tax du pex hoik pauh ka kix;
yam in pex lai baox coh ka siliex an,
mai pauh ka nawh.
53 Pex hoik mawh pui pa rieb ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex;
lox tat jhawm prawng in hoik mawh pa sidiex kawn cau Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng heue!”
See-thi-fang yum son gum lox Siyiex
54 Pui ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix mhong ka lox pa krai See-thi-fang in kix,
kied song rhawm kah,
mai greeik rhang braing tix ka nawh.
55 See-thi-fang hoik naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
nawh yaog kaing tix maong ka rao ma,
mai yaox min plak ngai Siyiex,
mai baox yaox cung Ye-sux ot ka plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex,
56 nawh tom ah nin:
“Aux yaox siviex rao ma hoik baoh,
kawn kawn pui cung ka plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex!”
57 Kix tom kok yo tom ting,
mai thox ka yhaok tix,
mai baox su shiet paox tix haok giex ka See-thi-fang,
58 kix nyheen nawh lih khaing daux veng,
mai teei simaox tawk nawh kah.
Pui pa kham sakse khankix teei tix tawx sibex ka Sao-law pui pa nhum ju priex kah.
59 Yam tawk simaox ka See-thi-fang,
nawh khiox khaw ah nin:
“Simiang Ye-sux eue,
khiox khaw maix rieb tix priex ka khuan aux liak!”
60 Nawh baox deeh grux ngawng tix,
mai kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
paw vi kieh phi map in hoek piang kix heue!”
Khaix hoik krai nawh lox in nawh yum heue.
