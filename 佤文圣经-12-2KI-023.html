Yaw-si-yax yieh tix tik ka krax pa krub been li tang ceu
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 34.3–7, 29–33)
23
Simiang Yaw-si-yax khrawm ka can simiang pui Yo-tax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik,
2 hu tau ka nyiex buan.
Kawn koe khawm cao giex kaing li,
cao jhai,
mai kawn pui khawm uik,
hot mi ang ah,
mai hoek ka nyiex buan khawm uik.
Simiang phat lox lai sijax mukheu pa yaox tix ka daux nyiex tom ting ka plak sivoe ngai kix.
3 Simiang cung ka praok rong ting,
mai yuh sijax mukheu ka Simiang Siyiex,
son sang nghiet ka nawh,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai hawt ka gum lox pa hoik koe ka daux lai sijax mukheu an.
Can pui khawm uik cu tix hawt ka sijax mukheu in heue.
4 aMawh kheu nan,
Yaw-si-yax tawx lox ka Si-leu-cia cao giex kaing li pa ting,
cao pa teem coi cao giex kaing li,
mai ka pui pau ka diang siviex nyiex buan,
nhie kix teei gah ka can kraung pa siao krub ka Pa-lix,
pa krub ka siyiex bun Ya-seu-la,
mai simuing khankix lih hu.
Simiang gleeing kraung in kix ka praok ngawd Ci-loeng plak praix veng,
khaix an nhie pui viex nyaox khaix gleeing hoek ka Paw-theu-lix.
5 Nawh tik ka cao giex kaing li pa hoik kieh simiang pui Yo-tax khankix,
kix yuh ka krax tan lu ka daux nyiex vad,
pa dex ka veng Yo-tax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai cao giex kaing li pa yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Pa-lix,
ka riang singaix,
ka riang khix,
mai ka simuing khankix.
6 Nawh baox gah ka riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la lih khaing daux nyiex buan,
mai viex hoek gleeing ka praok ngawd Ci-loeng plak praix veng,
lauh max lih mawh phawng nyaox,
mai rhuat ka gawng si meeig pui khawm uik.
7 Nawh yieh ka nyiex vad simex pa coh kaux tix pa hoik rhim tix ot ka nyiex buan,
pa mawh dee taing bun miex khankix tix ceep ka kraung dai ceu an,
yam krub ka Ya-seu-la.
8 Nawh viex ka cao giex kaing li pa ot ka veng Yo-tax ku dauh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai yuh laux ka nyiex vad pa hoik yuh kix krax tan lu ka piang meung ku dauh,
mai baox yieh ka nyiex vad pa hoik tan lu kawn pex si ngie,
pa ot ka praok diang siviex veng.
Nyiex vad siyiex tang kaux in hoik mawh pa sum Yaw-su-yax cao kaing,
ka plak goix diang siviex ting laig ka daux veng.
9 Cao giex kaing li an kix ang lai pon tix yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex buan,
viang mawh nan,
chix tix ih ka khao mun pa ang sau su saix pa tawx cao giex kaing li tang kaux ih ka heue.
10 bSimiang Yaw-si-yax baox yuh laux ka nyiex vad Thaw-fe-theux pa ot daux ngawd Sing-noeng,
mai ang lai kieh kawn pui gleeing tix tan lu kawn simex kawn bun tix ka siyiex Maw-law.
11 Nawh kawx gah ka riang brung pa hoik yuh simiang pui Yo-tax khankix son sang kieh mawh pa krub ka riang singaix sivoe an khankix,
mai gleeing ka ling law pa siao yam yuh krax krub.
(Kraung ceu in kix hoik dawg mawh kraung pa deuh ot ka biang brawk nyiex buan,
dex ka diang siviex,
ang koe miex glang tix khaing nyiex dee ot kawn cau pa ting Na-tang Mi-leu).
12 cSivoe an,
simiang pui Yo-tax khankix sum khu ot ka piang choek nyiex haw simiang Ya-ha-see,
khawm simiang Ma-na-si deuh khu ot ka si nax biang brawk nyiex buan ra mu,
simiang Yaw-si-yax kawx hoik yieh laux uik.
Nawh lauh khu max,
mai tik daux ngawd Ci-loeng.
13 eYaw-si-yax hoik seeing brai yuh nyiex vad pa hoik sum simiang Saw-law-meung pa koe dee lung.
Pa mawh pa ot ka plak lih singaix ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung ka plak seh ka gawng simed,
kheu son sang krub ka riang pa yum koeng siyiex bun Si-toeng pa kaux Ya-see-tha-lux,
siyiex Maw-ya pa kaux Ci-mawx,
siyiex Ya-meung pa kaux Mi-leu-kong.
14 Simiang Yaw-si-yax pauh ka rong simaox max,
kid ka riang bun siyiex Ya-seu-la goh,
ka dee sum rong ot kah,
mai hoik thom si-ang kaing pui yum naog ka heue.
15 fYaw-si-yax kawx hoik yieh laux ka khu pa ot ka Paw-theu-lix,
pa mawh khu pa sum simiang Yie-law-paw-ang kawn simex Ni-pa,
pa thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map.
Yaw-si-yax yieh ka khu.
Lauh simaox max lih mawh phawng nyaox,
mai baox gleeing riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la.
16 hYaw-si-yax jhak ka plak goix plak dawm tix,
mai yaox si meeig khankix ka piang gawng.
Nawh tom kaong si-ang kaing lih khaing daux si meeig,
deuh ot ka piang khu mai gleeing kah,
kieh khu seeing brai,
awm lox jhai pa krai pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex sivoe an dix,
pa mawh,
yam cung simiang Yie-law-paw-ang tix krai ka daux bawg yam yuh poig.
Yaw-si-yax baox jhak khawm pon daog,
mai yaox ka si meeig pui ah lox jhai pa kaoh khaing Siyiex;
17 inawh chawk ah nin:
“Mawh an si meeig pui mawx?”
Pui Paw-theu-lix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh si meeig pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex an pa hoek khaing Yo-tax.
Pa taug hoik yuh maix ka piang khu in khawm uik,
nawh hoik dawg krai ka lox jhai heue.”
18 Yaw-si-yax tawx lox ah nin:
“Paw vi chuh;
paw vi gah ka si-ang nawh.”
Mawh kheu nin,
si-ang pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex an ang bax chuh tix;
mai si-ang cao jhai pa hoek khaing Sa-ma-li-yax kawx ang bax chuh tix heue.
19 Simiang Yaw-si-yax hoik yieh ka nyiex vad pa cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm,
pa hoik sum simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix sivoe an ka daux veng pui Yi-seu-liex ku veng.
Awm pa hoik yuh nawh ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
nawh yieh tix tik ka can nyiex vad khawm uik.
20 Nawh pauh ka can cao giex kaing li nyiex vad pa ot ka dee lung yum ka piang khu kix,
mai gleeing nang si-ang kaing pui yum ot ka piang,
khaix an deeh ing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Yaw-si-yax yuh Poi Luat-luan
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 35.1–19)
21 Yaw-si-yax tawx lox nhie pui priex tix hawt ka lox pa tiiem ot daux lai yuh Poi Luat-luan,
doh keud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix.
22 Jauh tom bawg yam singaix cao prawng kaing ka daux meung,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ku kaux mai simiang pui Yo-tax ku kaux ang nyang sax yuh Poi Luat-luan pa si num yuh nin.
23 Mawh ngie yam giex Yaw-si-yax kaing ka daux kao daix num,
simiang mai kawn pui yuh Poi Luat-luan ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Grawng pa loh Yaw-si-yax ka been
24 Son sang yuh lih ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik yaox cao giex kaing li pa ting pa kaux Si-leu-cia pa tiiem tix ot ka daux lai an daux nyiex buan.
Simiang Yaw-si-yax tom teei tix tik can pui pa kok khuan,
pui pa yuh si pawk,
riang siyiex pa krub cub nyiex ma pui tang ceu,
riang rao,
khawm kraung ceu pa krub been li tang ceu khankix khawm uik khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung mai khaing daux haktiex Yo-tax pa deuk khankix.
25 Sivoe nawh,
ang nyang sax koe simiang pa awm nawh,
pa hoik yuh kaing krub Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh nan,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik krai Maw-si;
khaix nawh kawx ang lai koe simiang pa awm nawh tix kaux yuh nan heue.
26 Viang mawh nan,
kheu kaing pa yuh simiang Ma-na-si,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka pui Yo-tax hoek tom yam in kawn ang bax chix naox tix.
27 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux sang yuh ka pui Yo-tax awm hoik yuh ka pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nan.
Aux tag sang grheu pui hun Yo-tax lih hu khaing plak sivoe ngaix tix;
aux tag sang tik vud ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa hoik laug tix,
mai ka nyiex buan pa ah aux khaw tix krub ka aux heue.”
Pa hoik si dauh bawg yam giex Yaw-si-yax kaing
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 35.20–36.1)
28 Kaing yuh simiang Yaw-si-yax tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
29 Daux yam giex nawh kaing,
simiang Ai-cix pa kaux Ni-keu ploi pui naing hu ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
mai teem coi ka Vawng Ya-sux.
Simiang Yaw-si-yax hu rieb tix yuh naing ka pui naing Ai-cix ka haktiex Mi-cie-to,
dee si dauh hoik yum.
30 Cao kaing pui naing nawh deuh to kaux yum nawh ot daux ling law,
reu nawh deeh ing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang heue.
Pui Yo-tax khankix laug ka kawn simex Yaw-si-yax pa kaux Yaw-ha-see mai ap nawh plud tix mawh simiang khaix keeing tix heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-ha-see
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 36.2–4)
31 Yam koe ju Yaw-ha-see tix nga loe num,
nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
nawh giex kaing ka piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung loe khix.
Miex nawh mawh Ha-maw-tha,
kawn bun Ye-li-mi pui veng Li-na.
32 Yaw-ha-see hawt ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh tax du tix khankix,
mai hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
33 Simiang Ai-cix pa kaux Ni-keu giex Yaw-ha-see ka veng Li-pi-la haktiex Ha-ma,
mai kieh nawh naox giex tix kaing,
mai baox rhim tix teei mau luah loe rheng pon yieh kongcing,
mai khri tix ngoe pon kongcing.
34 jSimiang Ni-keu yaog kawn simex Yaw-si-yax pa kaux Yi-li-ya-cing plud keeing tix Yaw-si-yax mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai loh ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-ya-cing.
Simiang Ni-keu viex Yaw-ha-see hu ka Ai-cix;
Yaw-ha-see yum dee an heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-ya-cin
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 36.5–8)
35 Son sang khrawm ka mau pa nhie simiang pui Ai-cix kix tawx,
simiang Yaw-ya-cing tom koh hawt krax mi hot kawn pui heue.
36 kYam koe ju Yaw-ya-cing tix nga phuan num,
nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai giex kaing ka piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung kao tix num.
Miex nawh mawh Si-pu-ta,
mawh kawn bun Pi-ta-ya pui veng Lu-max heue.
37 Yaw-ya-cin hawt ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh tax du tix khankix,
mai hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
