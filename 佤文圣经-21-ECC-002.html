2
Aux ah nin ka to kaux tix:
“Hoek heue,
cheuk tix yuh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
Hoek cim tix yaox ka krax mhawm ot mhawm koe.”
Viang mawh nan,
in kawx hoik mawh nang pa giex pa going.
2 Aux hoik tawng krax klieh krax nyiah mawh krax pa cau nyhau,
krax gaux rhawm mawh pa ang koe dee mhawm ceeix kawx.
3 Rhawm rhi pa sawg ka ping nyia cuyi pa viex hu,
aux but rhawm tix teei plai yuh krax gaux rhawm,
sawg krax gaux rhawm.
Aux bre tix keud,
in rien tix mawh krax pa blawn nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux ju kawn pui yam ot ka piang haktiex tix vud tix cu khaing heue.
4 aAux yuh kaing ting sum nyiex ma son to kaux tix,
mai sum khaox plix siiet.
5 Aux cox sum ka nyiex ma si peem plix siiet,
mai khaox plix ku ceu.
6 Aux kaong dung rawm son sang caux khaox plix kah.
7 bAux liag siliex cawng simex mai siliex cawng bun,
khawm pa keh daux nyiex.
Aux hoik koe moi koe yung tom nie,
mai kawn lheu nie khaing pui pa ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
8 cAux hoik khrawm tix deuh mau khri simiang son to kaux tix,
mai siang pa koe ka haktiex ku dauh.
Aux hoik koe simex bun pa chix rhax lai tom hun,
moeng iak tom ang lai uik simin,
yuh ceu pa simie tix yuh heue.
9 dMawh tete,
aux hoik kied ting riang,
mai kawn lheu khaing pui pa ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix;
khaix an aux ca kawn koe ping nyia cuyi tom si dauh.
10 Aux sang lo ceu pa tix,
hoik koe ceu pa tix;
aux sau riang tix yuh krax gaux rhawm,
mai ang lai koe krax pa tuh.
Aux hoik pon krax gaux rhawm khaing daux kaing pa taug pa mao tix;
in hoik mawh ngawh riang pa khaw aux tix pon.
11 Viang mawh nan,
yam deeh doh keud aux ka kaing pa pon yuh tix,
ka krax pa taug pa mao pa hoik yuh tix,
aux deeh pon tix tawng sidawng ka mai hoik mawh pa giex pa going,
hoik mawh pa ang koe ceu;
ka greem riang singaix khawm uik ang koe ceu pa mhawm tix ceu kawx.
12 Mawh kheu kaing ceu pa yuh simiang tix kaux kaux,
mai hoik dawg mawh nang kaing ceu pa yuh simiang pa koe sivoe an heue.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux baox deeh keud ping nyia cuyi mawh ceu pa tix,
pa cau nyhau mawh ceu pa tix,
pa daox aw mawh ceu pa tix.
13 Mawh tete,
aux hoik tawng ping nyia cuyi kawn pex ka pa daox aw,
awm hoik pex pa riang ka pa faik yuh nan.
14 Pui pa lhak lhiao hoik tawng sidawng ka krax pa lawng tix hu sivoe ngai;
viang mawh nan,
pui daox aw pu pi ka daux pa faik.
Viang mawh nan,
aux kawx hoik tawng,
dee si dauh ka daux krax pa im ju kix hoik awm paox tix yuh nan.
15 Aux keud daux rhawm tix ah nin:
“Krax pa si pup pui pa daox aw,
mai hoik mawh nang krax pa si pup aux.
Viang aux hoik koe krax lhak pa lhiao kawx,
mai koe ceu pa mhawm ceu pa tix lie?”
Lox pa pawk aux mawh nin:
“Ang koe,
khawm uik hoik mawh pa giex pa going!”
16 Pui pa lhak lhiao mai pui pa daox aw hoik awm paox tix,
ang koe pui pa doh keud ka kix tom rheeng;
mawh kheu singaix pa sang hoek,
pui hoik piawm ka kix khawm uik.
Mawh pa lhak ang ah,
pa daox ang ah,
tag sang yum uik heue.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
krax pa im ju kawn pui ang koe dee bleeh son aux;
kaing pa koe ka greem singaix khawm uik,
hoik chix kieh ngie aux suk sag rhawm.
Khawm uik hoik mawh pa giex pa going,
hoik mawh pa ang koe ceu heue.
18 Ka greem singaix,
pa hoik pon khaing daux pa taug pa mao tix khawm uik hoik mawh nang pa ang koe dee bleeh ka aux;
mawh kheu aux ang doh ang deuh tix een pa koe khawm uik ka son pui ju phaox khaix,
19 pui an mawh pui lhak mawh pui daox,
pui mawx sang tawng lie?
Kheu nan,
nawh tag pon koe ceu pa mhawm pa koe aux khawm uik yam ot aux ka piang haktiex,
khaing pa teei ka daux krax pa taug pa mao mai ping nyia cuyi tix.
In kawx hoik mawh pa giex pa going.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
aux kied sidaing laux rhawm ka krax pa taug pa mao tix ka greem singaix ka daux singaix pa hoik luan.
21 Ceu pa pon pui khaing ping nyia cuyi,
ngai lig ngai lai,
mai krax lhak krax haing tix khankix hoik deuh tix een khawm uik,
mai kieh pui tang kaux pon yie yie khaing.
In kawx hoik mawh pa giex pa going,
mawh pa kied ang khaw heue!
22 Kawn pui yuh kaing kha rhawm khi rhawm,
taug mao ka greem singaix hoik koe dee mhawm ceu pa tix lie?
23 eIm ju ka piang haktiex tix ngaix,
can kaing pa yuh khawm uik hoik mawh krax pa kha rhawm laux rhawm,
mai ang yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
In kawx hoik mawh pa giex pa going heue.
24 fPui tix kaux kaux phan pon yaox tix ih nyaux khaing daux krax pa taug pa mao tix,
mai in hoik sawn tix mawh krax pa nyawm ot si u koe heue.
Viang mawh nan,
in hoik mawh nang pa kaoh khaing alo Siyiex.
25 Phan ang mawh alo Siyiex,
pui mawx pon tix ih nyaux?
Pui mawx yaox tix gaux rhawm lie?
26 gSiyiex hoik tawx ping nyia cuyi,
ngai lig ngai lai mai krax gaux rhawm ka pui pa kieh nawh koe krax gaux rhawm khankix,
mai hoik kieh pui phi map yuh kaing pa taug mao,
mai teei tix tawx ceu pa hoik khrawm kix tix deuh ka pui pa kieh nawh koe krax gaux rhawm.
In hoik mawh nang pa giex pa going mai pa ang koe ceu heue.
