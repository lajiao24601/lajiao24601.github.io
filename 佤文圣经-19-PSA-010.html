Lox rawg buan krax teem krax coi
10
Simiang Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kied singai hu khaing yix?
Yuh ka mawx lai ang kia ka yix yam ot yix ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi?
2 Pui caox pui caig mai ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm;
kieh kix deeh krix ka daux rub pa taong tix heue.
 
3 Pui caox pui caig yaw rang di alo nex nham to kaux tix;
pui pa koe rhawm ghoing tieb tie mai map nai ka Simiang Siyiex.
4 Pui caox pui caig ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
ang khiox khaw ka Siyiex;
ka daux rhawm rhi kix ang koe Siyiex heue.
 
5 Can kaing pa yuh pui pa caox pa caig hoik pon yaox tix mhawm khawm uik.
Nawh ang tawng grawng krax prawng ca Siyiex;
nawh nyiah ka pui pa koeng ka nawh khankix.
6 Ka daux rhawm rhi nawh keud ah nin:
aux ang chix yaox krax pa som nai leeing ju leeing phan;
ka daux ju aux ang yaox krax laux krax leeing.
7 aKa daux jheeh nawh hoik naog ka pa jhax lawg,
pa pie lhien,
pa ghlat,
can lox pa krai nawh hoik mawh pa koe jai mai pa caox pa caig khawm uik heue.
 
8 Nawh hoik kuan mox tix ot ka daux yaong;
nawh krox jun tix pauh ka pui pa ang koe dee lud.
Nawh jiang ka pui pa ang koe paox teem paox coi,
9 awm sivai sang si pa kuan mox tix ot ka dee glip yuh nan.
Nawh krox jun tix sang giex ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
teei rub rub nawh kah,
giex nawh hu heue.
 
10 Pui hot pui sibhawm pa ang koe paox koe pie,
hoik goh piang tiex;
krax tud krax rhim hoik kieh kix goh.
11 Pui caox pui caig ah nin ka to kaux tix:
Siyiex ang kuang in kix!
Nawh nyip ngai tix mai ang chix yaox ka aux leeing ju!
 
12 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix hoek taoh ka aux liak!
Siyiex eue,
paw vi piawm ka pui pa gut kheem pui kah!
13 Pui caox pui caig yuh ka mawx lai map nai ka Siyiex,
ka daux rhawm rhi kix ah nin:
nawh ang chix ciyang ka aux lie?
 
14 Viang mawh nan,
maix lam lia ka khawm uik,
mai kia ngien ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi kawn pui;
maix hoik cu tix joh jhan teem coi ku bawg ku yam.
Pui hot pui sibhawm pa ang koe paox koe pie hoik deuh rhawm tix ka maix khawm uik;
mawh kheu hoik dawg teem coi maix miex mai kawn doe pa ang koe cao teem krai.
 
15 Khiox khaw maix yuh laux ka sibrawm pui pa caox pa caig;
khiox khaw maix laux leeing ka phi map pa yuh kix,
mai si kaw tom die heue!
 
16 Simiang Siyiex giex simiang leeing ju leeing phan;
pui tang ceu tang cad tag sang laux leeing khaing ka daux haktiex nawh heue.
 
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw pui hot pui sibhawm;
maix tawx kix koe krax kua rhawm kua rhi.
18 Maix prawng rauh see son pui pa gut kheem pui mai kawn doe khankix;
kieh pui pa chix laux leeing ang lai baox tawx krax pa ghlat ka piang kix heue.
