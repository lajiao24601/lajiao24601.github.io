Krax keud pot jun pot pa sang hoek
23
Simiang pa grheu pui cie Simiang Siyiex hu an kix hoik laux leeing heue!
2 Kix hoik dawg mawh pa khaw tix kia ngien ka pui cie Siyiex.
Phan mawh pui simiang in kix,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex krai ah nin:
“Pex ang bax kia ngien ka pui cie aux,
mai tieh tix grheu kix hu.
Jhak heue,
aux tag sang ciyang pex kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pex.
3 Aux tag sang deeh khrawm ka pui pa deuk khankix,
khaing daux meung pa ploi aux kix hu ka ku meung,
mai viex kix deeh hoek ka haktiex tix.
Kix tag sang koe kawn praix tom nie,
kaux pui kix tag haok huan.
4 Aux tag sang ploi simiang kia ngien ka kix.
Pui cie aux ang lai baox yaox krax yaoh yee;a
aux ang lai ciyang ka kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
5 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
aux tag sang yaog pa rauh pa see ‘kak khaox’b son Ta-wue.
Krax pa giex nawh kaing kied mhawm;
nawh tag sang teei pa rauh pa see haktiex in giex ka meung.
6 Ka daux pa giex nawh kaing,
pui Yo-tax tag pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
pui Yi-seu-liex pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem.
Nawh tag sang kok mawh ‘Simiang Siyiex pa mawh pa rauh pa see ex heue’.”
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
yam sang hoek bawg yam,
yam kawn tiex tu pui ang lai baox ah nin:
‘Aux sang tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
pa viex pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Ai-cix’.
8 Ah nin:
‘Aux sang tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
pa viex pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing haktiex plak laong,
lih khaing meung pa hoik ploi kix hu ot ka ku meung.’
Yam an,
kix tag sang paung ot ka daux haktiex tix heue.”
Pa ne raw ka cao jhai khankix
9 Rhawm rhi aux hoik max;
to kaux aux hoik si yaung khawm uik.
Simiang Siyiex mai gum lox nawh pa singax singeh hoik kieh aux yoek,
kieh aux awm pui pleeng tix yoek ka plai yuh nan.
10 Ka piang haktiex in ku dauh hoik mawh pui pa ang cu ang nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex pa ot kah;
kix nieb pon siao ka alo sibrawm tix,
mai ot ka daux krax pa caox pa caig.
Mawh kheu krax jhax lawg Simiang Siyiex,
haktiex hoik ngia,
pak dee eei khawng brie hoik kroh heue.
 
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
cao jhai mai cao giex kaing li ang koe krax nab yam yung yi;
aux hoik yaox yuh kix kaing caox ka daux nyiex buan.
12 Krax pa lawng kix nyu nang faik nang;
aux tag sang kieh kix cang klad tix goh.
Bawg yam kham kix ka krax ciyang hoik hoek;
aux tag sang ploi krax laux krax leeing ka piang to kaux kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
13 Aux yaox phi map cao jhai Sa-ma-li-yax khankix:
kix dha tix krai lox ka daux ceu kaux siyiex Pa-lix,
kieh pui cie aux lud hu.
14 dAux yaox pa nie blawn caox caig cao jhai Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix:
kix phit vao,
pie lhien,
cheeik pui yuh kaing pa caox;
mawh kheu nan,
ang koe pui pa tik vud ka krax pa caox,
mai hu ka daux krax pa rauh pa see.
Ka plak sivoe ngai aux kix hoik caox caig awm pui veng Saw-to-ma yuh nan,
hoik seeing brai awm pui veng Eu-maw-la yuh nan heue.
 
15 Mawh kheu nan,
phan mawh cao jhai Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing krai ah nin:
Aux tag sang kieh kix ih plix song,
nyaux rawm pa koe jai,
mawh kheu hoik rhuat kix ka bhawm ceu pa ang nab yam yung yi ka daux meung khawm uik heue.
16 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
krai ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung yuh nin:
“Paw vi nghiet ka lox cao jhai in kix;
ceu pa tawx kix ka pex mawh krax keud pot jun pot ka daux pa giex pa going.
Kix dha tix krai lox pa giex pa going khaing pa keud to kaux tix,
mai ang sidiex ka lox aux.
17 Kix krai ka pui pa ang cu tix nghiet ka lox aux ang jud ah nin:
‘Kieh pex grum mhawm khawm uik heue!’
Baox krai ka pui pa kiien rhawm an kix ah nin:
‘Pex ang yaox ka krax laux krax leeing heue.’ ”
18 Aux ah nin:
“Tete te lai,
ka daux cao jhai in kix,
ang koe tix kaux kawx pa hoik sax cung tix ot ka daux nyiex Simiang Siyiex,
mai nghiet ka gum lox pa krai nawh,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa nghiet ka gum lox nawh.
19 Jhak heue,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex awm bhaung klia pa kied ting yuh nan,
mai yia tix ka piang kaing pui pa caox pa caig.
20 Krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex tag sang nied tix lih,
tom hoik hoik nawh kaing pa yuh tix khawm uik;
bawg yam an,
pui cie nawh tag sang chix tawng ka kaing in kix heue.”
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux ang bax ploi ka cao jhai in kix hu,
viang mawh nan,
kix hoik to hu ku dauh ku dee;
aux ang bax tawx gum lox tix ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
kix hoik teei ceu kaux aux krai lox jhai.
22 Phan kix hoik sax cung ka daux nyiex rao ma,
mai rieb ka gum lox aux,
kix tom taug chix krai gum lox aux ka pui cie aux,
mai kieh kix tik vud ka pa caox pa caig,
mai ang yuh ka kaing ceu pa caox heue.
23 “Aux mawh Siyiex pa ot ku dauh ku dee.
24 Ang koe pui pa pon mox tix ka aux,
mai kieh aux ang yaox tix.
Khex ang pex tawng ka piang haktiex dee mawx ang ah,
mai aux hoik ot ka lie?
25 Aux mhong ka cao jhai khankix teei ceu kaux aux pie lhien ka pui,
kix krai pon tix gum lox aux khaing daux ca maox.
26 Cao jhai an kix pie lhien,
mai kieh pui cie aux lud krax;
kix pie ka pui tag sang pie hoek yam mawx lie?
27 Kix ce tix sang kieh ca maox pa pie lhien tix kieh pui cie aux piawm ka aux,
awm hoik sax piawm tax du kix ka aux yuh nan,
mai veeh tix ka Pa-lix heue.
28 Cao jhai pa ca maox khaw tix krai mawh ca maox tix;
cao jhai pa mhong ka gum lox aux,
khaw tix kaux sijax ka daux pa krai ka gum lox aux.
Rib mai nghox ca sang chix krai tix tau yuh ka mawx lie?
29 “Gum lox aux awm ngu pa cap,
awm daix pa tawk ka simaox lauh yuh nan.
30 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
nghiet heue,
aux tag sang yuh ka cao jhai in kix.
Kix ra lai siao ka lox paox tix,
mai kieh mawh pa krai ka gum lox aux.
31 Nghiet heue,
aux tag sang yuh ka cao jhai in kix,
kix teei lox to kaux tix mawh gum lox aux.
32 Nghiet heue,
aux tag sang yuh ka cao jhai in kix.
Ca maox kix hoik mawh pa pie lhien.
Kix krai lao ka ca maox in kix,
mai teei lox pa pie lhien mai lox pa ting lhaong kieh pui cie aux lud krax.
Aux ang bax ploi kix hu,
mai ang bax tawm nang lox ka kix.
Kix ang tie koe grawng ceu pa mhawm tid tix mai pui cie aux pie ceeix kawx.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Beeh glawm pa cien cie Simiang Siyiex
33 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
phan pui cie aux,
phan ang mawh nan cao jhai,
phan ang mawh nan cao giex kaing li chawk maix ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex koe gum lox ceu pa tix lie?’
Maix caw tix krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Pex mawh beeh glawm pa ciene Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh tag sang luat luan khaing pex.’
34 Phan koe cao jhai,
cao giex kaing li,
pui cie aux siao ka ‘beeh glawm Simiang Siyiex pa cien’ gum lox in,
mai aux tag sang ciyang ka nawh mai khawm pui nyiex ma nawh.
35 Pui ku kaux caw tix chawk ka paox grawm tix mai ka paox plawg paox nyhawm tix ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex hoik pawk lox yuh ka mawx lie?
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ceu pa tix lie?’
36 Kix ang pon tix baox siao ka ‘Beeh glawm Simiang Siyiex pa cien’ gum lox in;
pui mawx baox siao,
aux tag sang kieh gum lox tix lih mawh beeh glawm pa cien ka nawh.
Kix hoik nieb pon siao ka gum lox Siyiex kix,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju heue.
37 “Yie-li-mi eue,
maix tag caw tix chawk ka cao jhai an kix ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex hoik pawk lox yuh ka mawx ka pex lie?
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai lox ceu pa tix ka pex lie?’
38 Phan kix lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx aux,
mai siao ka ‘beeh glawm Simiang Siyiex pa cien’ gum lox in,
maix tom krai ka kix,
39 aux tag sang kieh kix mawh beeh glawm pa cien,
mai yaog tix jiang ka kix,f
teei ka kix khawm veng pa hoik tawx aux ka kix mai ka tax du kix,
tag sang si vun tix tik ka plak sivoe ngai aux.
40 Aux tag sang kieh kix pon krax kaig krax tua tom rheeng,
mai kham krax pa map nai pui,
pa ang pui chix piawm leeing ju heue.”
