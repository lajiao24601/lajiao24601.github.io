Lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
119
Can pui pa ang yuh krax lud krax loe,
priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex khankix kied koe buan son heue!
2 Can pui pa nghiet ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix hawt ka nawh khankix koe buan son heue!
3 Pui pa yuh nin ang yuh kaing pa caox pa caig;
kix lawng ngie ka daux krax cie Simiang Siyiex khaing.
4 Simiang Siyiex eue, maix tawx lox tat jhawm prawng ka yix,
nhie yix priex tix hawt mhawm mhawm.
5 Aux kied simie tix pon priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx maix tom kaux cong heue!
6 Phan aux kieh gum lox ping nyap maix khawm uik mawh pa ting ceu,
aux tag ang hoek krax kaig krax tua.
7 Yam gau aux pa rauh see lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
aux tag sang teei rhawm pa singax rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix.
8 Aux tag sang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi tik vud ka aux heue!
Priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
9 Pui pa nhum ju khankix sang ot ka daux krax pa singax yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh tag priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
10 Aux tag sang viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix sawg ka maix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux lieh lheu ka gum lox ping nyap maix.
11 Aux priex gum lox maix ot daux rhawm rhi tix,
son ang aux sang lud loe ka maix.
12 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix;
khiox khaw maix teei lox tat jhawm prawng tix sidiex gau ka aux;
13 aux sang krai ka lox pa ting si num hu,
ka can lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik yuh maix khawm uik.
14 Aux kied simie ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
kawn lheu khaing simie aux ka sin ngao pa koe khawm uik.
15 Aux gau ka lox pa sidiex maix;
aux bre tix keud ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix;
16 aux kieh lox tat jhawm prawng maix mawh krax gaux rhawm;
aux ang piawm ka gum lox tawx maix heue.
Krax gaux rhawm ka daux pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
17 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka siliex maix,
kieh aux priex tix hawt ka lox pa sidiex gau maix ka daux pa im ju tix.
18 Khiox khaw maix paoh ka ngai aux,
kieh aux yaox pa mawh pa meh ka daux pa raux pa mox khaing lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
19 Aux mawh ngie nyo si nie ka piang haktiex khaing;
khiox khaw maix paw vi mox gum lox ping nyap maix ka aux.
20 Son pa hoik keud ce ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix ku singaix,
aux ang lai pon kham ka daux rhawm rhi tix hoik hax awm ngu.
21 Maix ne raw ka pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi;
pui pa tik vud ka gum lox tawx maix tag pon krax jhax lawg heue!
22 Khiox khaw maix teei tix tik ka can krax kaig krax tua mai lox jhax lox lawg pa hoek ka piang aux,
mawh kheu hoik priex aux tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
23 Viang koe simiang pa keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing kawx,
aux pa mawh siliex maix ca kawn gau ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
24 Krax pa sidiex gau maix hoik kieh aux gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
hoik mawh sara pa mhawm paox grawm pa muh ka aux heue.
But rhawm tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
25 Aux hoik goh ka piang phawng tiex;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix taoh aux awm lox pa hoik deuh maix.
26 Aux sang krai doh ka kaing pa yuh tix khawm uik, mai maix tom taug cu nghiet ka lox aux;
khiox khaw maix teei lox tat jhawm prawng sidiex gau ka aux.
27 Khiox khaw maix sidiex gau aux tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix;
aux sang gau ka gum lox pa sidiexa maix pa raux pa mox.
28 Krax khi rhawm hoik tuk tiak ka aux;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix kieh aux koe riang awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix.
29 Khiox khaw maix paw kieh aux lawng daux krax pa ang mawh ang meh.
Tawx buan son sidiex gau aux ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
30 Aux laug ka daux krax pa mawh pa meh;
aux kieh lox tat jhawm prawng maix ting ceu.
31 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux hoik priex tix hawt ka krax pa sidiex gau maix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux hoek krax kaig krax tua.
32 Aux tag sang gaux rhawm tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix,
mawh kheu maix nie blawn kieh aux pon tix tawng sidawng ka heue.
Khiox khaw pon tix tawng sidawng ka dee bleeh lox tat jhawm prawng
33 Simiang Siyiex eue, khiox khaw maix sidiex gau dee bleeh lox tat jhawm prawng ka aux,
aux cu tix priex hawt tom si dauh.
34 Khiox khaw maix krai dee bleeh lox tat jhawm prawng maix ka aux;
aux cu priex tix hawt, mai hawt tix yuh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
35 Khiox khaw maix thung aux lawng daux krax gum lox ping nyap maix,
hoik mawh kheu pon aux krax gaux rhawm khaing ka daux nawh.
36 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux gaux rhawm tix priex hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
kieh lheu khaing ghoing ka mau khri.
37 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux een kaing pa pie lhien koe ceu;
khiox khaw maix tawx ju pa khraox ka aux ka daux krax maix.
38 Lox pa ap maix tix deuh ka pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix khankix,
khiox khaw maix tawx lih ka piang to kaux siliex maix.
39 Khiox khaw maix kieh lox jhax lawg pa lhat graung aux ka singai hu khaing aux;
lox tat jhawm prawng maix kied sidaing mhawm heue!
40 Aux tag sang priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix;
khiox khaw maix tawx ju ka aux khaing ka daux krax pa rauh see maix heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex
41 Simiang Siyiex eue, khiox khaw maix jhi krax muh tix ka aux;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix taoh aux awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix.
42 Aux tom taug pon pawk lox pui pa map nai ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm aux ka lox maix.
43 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux pon krai lao ka pa mawh pa meh maix ku jheeng jheeng,
mawh kheu krax keud pot jun pot aux hoik ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
44 Aux sang nied tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue.
45 Aux sang ot ka daux krax pa ang koe ceu pa tieb tie,
mawh kheu hoik priex aux tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix.
46 Aux sang krai pao gum lox tawx maix ka simiang haktiex,
aux ang kieh in mawh krax pa kaig tua.
47 Aux kieh krax pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix mawh krax gaux rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik muh aux ka nawh.
48 Aux yung yi mai muh ka gum lox ping nyap maix;
aux sang keud ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix heue.
Yum rhawm ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex tete
49 Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka lox pa ap maix tix deuh ka siliex maix,
mawh kheu gum lox maix hoik kieh aux koe krax keud pot jun pot.
50 Pie yam ot aux ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi, mai aux kawx hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm,
hoik mawh kheu gum lox pa deuh maix, pa hoik tawx ju pa khraox ka aux.
51 Pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi map nai ka aux ang jud ang jai,
aux kawx ang nyang sax lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
52 aux hoik doh keud ka krax pa sidiex gau maix ka aux singaix pa hoik luan;
Simiang Siyiex eue, krax pa sidiex gau maix ka aux hoik kieh aux pon pa nyawm rhawm.
53 Aux yaox ka pui pa caox pa caig pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
krax pa song rhawm aux hoik kied nu tix tieb tie.
54 Aux paung ot ka piang haktiex;
aux tiiem lai rhax mai yaog yaw ka gum lox tawx maix.
55 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux keud ka maix yam pon sawm;
aux priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
56 Aux priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
Pa mawh krax gaux rhawm ka aux heue.
Kied muh ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
57 Simiang Siyiex eue, maix mawh can pa sang lo aux khawm uik;
aux but rhawm tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
58 Aux khiox khaw ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
khiox khaw maix hawt tix caox rhawm ka aux awm lox pa hoik ap maix tix deuh.
59 Aux deeh bre tix keud ka krax pa hoik lawng tix,
but rhawm tix deeh veeh tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
60 Aux priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap maix,
ang lai kieh tix dah khaix heue.
61 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig teei maox prawk aux kah,
aux ca kawn ang piawm ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
62 Aux kaoh yam pon sawm,
rhax lai yaog yaw lox tat jhawm prawng maix pa khaw caw.
63 Pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix, mai pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix khankix,
hoik mawh paox grawm aux khawm uik.
64 Simiang Siyiex eue, piang haktiex hoik naog ka krax muh maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix;
khiox khaw maix teei gum lox ping nyap maix sidiex aux ka heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex pa kied koe jum
65 Simiang Siyiex eue, hawt tix yuh awm lox pa ap maix tix deuh,
kia ngien mhawm mhawm ka siliex maix.
66 Khiox khaw maix tawx ping nyia cuyi mai ngai lig ngai lai ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm aux ka gum lox tawx maix.
67 Sivoe sang ciyang maix ka aux, aux hoik lud loe jheeng jheeng,
viang mawh nan, yam in aux hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox maix.
68 Maix kied sidaing mhawm, kied sidaing koe krax muh heue!
Khiox khaw maix teei gum lox ping nyap maix sidiex gau aux kah.
69 Pui pa ca nyhau teei lox pa pie lhien simah ka aux,
viang mawh nan, aux viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix priex tix hawt ka lox sidiex lox gau maix.
70 Kix hoik pheem mai ang koe krax lhak lhiao;
aux tieh tix kieh lox tat jhawm prawng maix mawh krax gaux rhawm.
71 Can krax kha rhawm kha rhi pa kham aux hoik mawh pa mhawm son aux,
mawh kheu nawh hoik kieh aux gau tix tawng ka gum lox ping nyap maix.
72 Can lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tawx maix ka aux,
kawn lheu khaing mau khri kaing rheng kaing mheen heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex pa rauh see
73 Maix phan yuh ka aux, mai kia ngien ka aux;
khiox khaw maix tawx aux koe rhawm pa chix doh chix riang, mai kieh aux pon gau ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
74 Pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix yaox ka aux tom koe krax gaux rhawm kah,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm aux ka gum lox maix.
75 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux tawng krax prawng krax ca maix mawh pa rauh see;
maix ne raw ka aux, mawh kheu hoik kaux sijax maix.
76 Khiox khaw maix hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix ka siliex maix,
teei krax muh maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix nyawm yuh rhawm aux.
77 Khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux, mai kieh aux im ju,
mawh kheu hoik kieh aux lox tat jhawm prawng maix mawh krax pa gaux rhawm.
78 Kieh pui an kix pa ca nyhau pa salao ka aux pon krax kaig krax tua;
phan mawh aux, aux tag sang bre rhawm rhi tix keud ka lox tawm jhawm krai maix.
79 Kieh pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix hoek ka aux,
pui pa tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix cu nghiet ka aux.
80 Kieh aux viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix, mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap maix,
paw vi kieh aux yaox krax sum krax nai mai krax kaig krax tua heue.
Khiox khaw krax teem coi Simiang Siyiex
81 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux hoik taug mao kheu pa simie tix ka krax taoh maix;
krax keud pot jun pot aux ot ka daux lox pa ap maix tix deuh.
82 Ngai aux hoik faik kheu pa simie aux ka gum lox pa hoik deuh maix,
aux chawk: sang kieh aux nyawm rhawm yam mawx lie?
83 Aux awm hau plai prim pa hoik grah yuh nan;
viang mawh nan, aux ang bax piawm ka gum lox tawx maix.
84 Aux kawn sang krox jun miex glang lie?
Maix tag sang ciyang ka pui pa tuk tiak ka aux yam mawx lie?
85 Pui ca nyhau pa ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
hoik kaong ka daux sang yuh aux laux leeing.
86 Kix tuk tiak ka aux khawm ang aux tie bax lud, khiox khaw maix teem coi ka aux;
gum lox ping nyap maix hoik mawh pa kaux pa cong.
87 Kix hoik dex sang pauh ka aux,
viang mawh nan, aux ca kawn ang tik vud ka gum lox tawx maix.
88 Khiox khaw maix tawx ju pa khraox ka aux, mai muh ka aux leeing ju,
son sang pon priex aux tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix heue.
Yum rhawm ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
89 Simiang Siyiex eue, gum lox maix koe leeing ju leeing phan,
hoik sum tix kaux cong ot ka rao ma.
90 Pa kaux sijax maix koe kaing mheen ju;
maix sum ka haktiex, mai kieh haktiex kaux cong leeing ju.
91 Can ku ceu khawm uik hoik hawt tix lih koe awm pa tawx maix, tom hoek yam in singaix,
mawh kheu to sat ka piang haktiex khawm uik hoik mawh siliex maix.
92 Phan aux ang kieh lox tat jhawm prawng maix mawh krax gaux rhawm,
aux hoik dawg yum ka daux krax pa kha rhawm.
93 Aux ang tik vud ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
mawh kheu hoik teei maix gum lox in kieh aux im ju,
94 aux mawh cie maix, khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik priex aux tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
95 Pui caox pui caig krox jun tix sang pauh ka aux,
viang mawh nan, aux ca kawn keud ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
96 Aux jhak kaing ka piang haktiex hoik mawh pa ang grum naog;
viang mawh nan, gum lox ping nyap maix kied ting kied vieh.
Kied muh ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
97 Aux kied sidaing muh ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix heue!
Aux keud ka leeing lang singaix ang jud ang jai.
98 Gum lox tawx maix ot daux rhawm aux jheeng jheeng,
kieh aux kawn lheu krax lhak lhiao khaing pui koeng.
99 Aux kawn lheu doh rhawm khaing can sara tix khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik keud ce aux ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix.
100 Aux kawn lheu koe ping nyia cuyi khaing pui pa riex ju,
mawh kheu hoik priex aux tix hawt ka gum lox pa tawx maix.
101 Aux hoik singai hu khaing krax pa caox pa caig khawm uik,
mawh kheu hawt aux ka gum lox maix.
102 Aux ang bax tik vud ka lox sidiex lox gau maix,
mawh kheu pa sidiex gau maix mawh aux.
103 Gum lox pa sidiex gau maix kied sidaing tie heue!
Ka daux jheeh aux kawn lheu tie khaing rawm hia.
104 Aux hoik pon ping nyia cuyi khaing lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
mawh kheu nan aux koeng ka krax pa caox pa caig heue.
Pa riang lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
105 Gum lox maix mawh kawk ngu pa thung ka aux,
mawh pa riang ka piang krax pa im ju aux.
106 Aux sang giex ka lox pa ap maix kaux cong,
priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa khaw caw maix.
107 Simiang Siyiex eue, krax kha rhawm pa kham gee aux kied sidaing ciien;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix tawx ju pa khraox ka aux awm gum lox pa hoik ap maix tix deuh.
108 Simiang Siyiex eue, rieb ka lox rawg buan pa yaog yaw aux maix;
khiox khaw maix teei gum lox tawx tix sidiex gau aux kah.
109 Ju aux ot ka daux krax pa kha rhawm ku bawg ku yam;
viang mawh nan, auxb ang bax piawm ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
110 Pui caox pui caig taong rub sang yuh aux laux leeing,
aux ca kawn ang bax lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx maix.
111 Gum lox tawm ping nyap maix mawh sin ngao aux leeing ju,
mawh krax gaux rhawm ka daux rhawm rhi aux.
112 Aux but rhawm tix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
tom hoek si dauh ju tix heue.
Pon krax nyawm ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
113 Aux koeng ka pui pa ang koe krax yum rhawm ka maix,
viang mawh nan, aux muh ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
114 Maix mawh cao pa kia ngien ka aux, mawh dieb tie naok ka aux;
krax keud pot jun pot aux mawh gum lox pa hoik deuh maix.
115 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig eue, hu singai khaing aux heue;
aux sang priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Siyiex aux.
116 Khiox khaw maix hawt tix joh jhan aux awm lox pa krai maix, mai kieh aux yaox tix im ju;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh krax keud pot jun pot aux lih mawh riang pang peue pa giex pa going.
117 Maix joh jhan ka aux, mai aux tom nyawm ot;
aux sang priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix jheeng jheeng.
118 Maix tik vud ka pui pa lieh lheu lox tat jhawm prawng maix;
can krax pa keud pa sawn kix hoik mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik.
119 Maix hoik kieh can pui pa caox pa caig khawm uik mawh dee tik pa seeing brai,
mawh kheu nan aux muh ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix.
120 To kaux aux khawm uik hoik si yaung si kua kheu lhat graung tix ka maix;
Aux lhat graung yaoh yee kheu krax prawng krax ca maix heue.
Priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
121 Aux prawng ca ka daux pa rauh pa see;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux cawd ka daux tawm taix pui koeng.
122 Khiox khaw maix cu tix teem coi siliex maix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh pui pa ca nyhau nu nai ka aux.
123 Kheu hoik keud aux ka buan son maix, krox jun aux ka krax taoh maix,
ngai aux hoik vu vi khawm uik heue.
124 Khiox khaw maix hawt tix kia ngien aux awm krax muh maix;
khiox khaw maix sidiex gau aux ka lox tawx maix.
125 Aux mawh siliex maix; khiox khaw maix tawx aux koe krax lhak lhiao,
son sang tawng aux ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix.
126 Simiang Siyiex eue, bawg yam chuh maix tix hoik hoek heue,
mawh kheu kawn pui hoik lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
127 Aux kied muh ka gum lox tawx maix kawn lheu khaing mau khri,
kawn lheu khaing gawng khri pa hoik raug daux ngu.
128 Mawh kheu nan, aux priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix;
aux koeng ka kaing ceu pa pie lhien khawm uik heue.
Kied simie tix priex hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
129 Gum lox sidiex maix kied mawh pa daox deng tete!
Kheu nan, aux priex tix hawt khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
130 Gum lox pa sidiex gau maix, phan krai dee bleeh lih hoik mawh riang pa jhan tix lih,
pon kieh pui pa daox aw koe ping nyia cuyi.
131 Aux ang jheeh toek bhawm tix ang jud ang jai,
mawh kheu hoik kied simie aux ka gum lox tawx maix.
132 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka aux, caox rhawm ka aux,
awm hoik rieb maix ka pui pa muh ka maix yuh nan.
133 Khiox khaw maix hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix, mai paw vi kieh aux sideeing tix goh,
kieh aux bheeik luan khaing pa giex gang pui pa caox pa caig.
134 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing krax pa gut kheem pui ka aux,
kieh aux pon priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
135 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien aux ka daux krax pa caox rhawm maix,
teei lox tat jhawm prawng maix sidiex gau aux kah.
136 Rawm ngai aux lih awm rawm klawng,
mawh kheu kawn pui hoik lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex pa rauh see
137 Simiang Siyiex eue, maix mawh pa rauh see;
lox tat jhawm prawng maix rauh see.
138 Lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tawx maix,
hoik mawh pa grum khaw grum caw, mai pa grum rauh grum see.
139 Krax song rhawm aux awm ngu pa cap,
mawh kheu pui pa koeng ka aux, ang een gum lox tawx maix koe ceu.
140 Gum lox pa hoik deuh maix kied sidaing singax tete heue!
Aux kied sidaing muh ka tete heue!
141 Aux mawh pui pa iak pa lied, mai pui map nai ka aux,
viang mawh nan, aux ang bax piawm ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix.
142 Pa rauh see maix mawh pa rauh see leeing ju leeing phan,
lox tat jhawm prawng maix mawh pa mawh pa meh.
143 Aux si pup krax kha rhawm taug mao,
viang mawh nan, gum lox ping nyap maix hoik kieh aux koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
144 Gum lox pa sidiex gau maix rauh see leeing ju,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux tawng, kieh aux im ju heue.
Khiox khaw krax taoh teem
145 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux khiox khaw kok yo ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix, khiox khaw maix pawk ka lox aux;
aux sang priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
146 Aux sang khiox khaw kok yo ka maix, khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux;
aux sang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix
147 Sivoe sang riang praix mai aux khiox khaw kok yo ka maix;
krax keud pot jun pot aux hoik mawh ka daux gum lox pa hoik deuh maix.
148 Aux blah ngai tix leeing lang sawm,
keud ka lox pa sidiex gau maix.
149 Simiang Siyiex eue, khiox khaw maix hawt tix nghiet lox aux awm krax muh maix;
khiox khaw maix jhi krax pa caox rhawm tix, mai tawx ju pa khraox ka aux.
150 Pui pa cau nyhau hoik hoek tix dex ka aux;
kix ang sax priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix tix bawg kawx.
151 Viang mawh nan, Simiang Siyiex eue, maix hoik ot tau mai aux;
lox tat jhawm prawng maix khawm uik hoik mawh pa mawh pa meh.
152 Aux hoik dawg tawng ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix;
maix hoik kieh gum lox pa sidiex gau tix kaux cong leeing ju heue.
Khiox khaw krax teem krax coi
153 Khiox khaw maix kok jhak ka krax pa kha aux rhawm tix, khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux,
mawh kheu aux ang sax prah ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
154 Khiox khaw maix prawng rauh see son aux, mai pluat ploi ka aux;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix taoh ka aux awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix.
155 Pui caox pui caig ang pon krax taoh,
mawh kheu ang kix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
156 Viang mawh nan, Simiang Siyiex eue, krax muh maix kied ting kied lhaong;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix tawx ju pa khraox ka aux awm pa rauh see maix.
157 Pui koeng mai pui pa map nai ka aux kied haoh hun yuh nan,
viang mawh nan, aux ang sax tieh vheen ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
158 Yam yaox aux ka pui pa tieh pa vheen khankix, mai aux kied yum koeng kah,
mawh kheu ang kix priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
159 Simiang Siyiex eue, jhak grawng pa kied muh aux ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix heue!
Khiox khaw maix hawt tix taoh ka aux awm krax muh maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix.
160 Num lox tat jhawm prawng maix hoik mawh pa mawh pa meh;
lox tat jhawm prawng pa khaw caw maix khawm uik hoik koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Tan to kaux tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
161 Pui pa koe siawp sibrawm tuk tiak aux khawm ang aux tie bax lud,
viang mawh nan, aux nab yam yung yi ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
162 Aux gaux rhawm kheu gum lox pa hoik deuh maix,
hoik awm yaox pui ka siang pa koe dee glip yuh nan.
163 Aux kied koeng ka lox pa pie lhien khawm uik,
viang mawh nan, aux kied muh ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
164 Tix ngaix aliah bawg aux tan lu krax yaog yaw ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik khaw caw krax pa prawng ca maix.
165 Pui pa kied muh ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix tag sang pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem,
tang ceu tix ceu kawx ang pon kieh kix goh.
166 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux krox jun ka krax taoh maix;
aux priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
167 Aux nghiet ka gum lox pa sidiex gau maix;
aux muh lox tat jhawm prawng maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
168 Aux priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix mai gum lox pa sidiex gau maix;
can kaing pa yuh aux khawm uik maix hoik yaox ka heue.
Lox rawg buan son krax teem coi
169 Simiang Siyiex eue, kieh lox pa kok yo krax khiox khaw aux hoek ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix tawx krax lhak lhiao ka aux awm lox pa hoik deuh maix.
170 Kieh lox rawg aux buan hoek ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix taoh aux awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix.
171 Aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix ku bawg ku yam,
mawh kheu hoik sidiex gau maix lox tat jhawm prawng tix ka aux.
172 Aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
mawh kheu hoik rauh see gum lox tawx maix.
173 Khiox khaw maix teem coi ka aux jheeng jheeng,
mawh kheu hoik priex aux tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
174 Simiang Siyiex eue, aux kied muh ka krax taoh maix tete;
lox tat jhawm prawng maix mawh krax gaux rhawm aux.
175 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux im ju, son sang yaox aux tix yaog yaw ka maix;
Kieh krax pa sidiex gau maix teem coi ka aux.
176 Aux hoik deek rhawm keud, awm kawn yung pa hoik grai yuh nan;
khiox khaw maix hoek sawg ka siliex tix,
mawh kheu ang aux bax piawm ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix heue.
