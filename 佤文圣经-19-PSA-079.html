Rawg buan son piang meung
79
aSiyiex eue,
pui tang ceu tang cad laig ka daux haktiex cie maix.
Kix hoik seeing brai yuh nyiex buan maix;
kix kieh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung lih mawh dee riem.
2 Kix teei tix tawx klang preng ih ka to kaux yum pui cie maix;
kix tik tix tawx to tiag ih ka to kaux yum siliex maix.
3 Kix kieh nham pui cie maix jauh tix lih mawh rawm klawng,
hoik kheem ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ang lai deuh pui tix kaux kawx si paung ka to kaux yum.
4 Meung pa koe ka sivie siveng yix hoik map nai ka yix;
kix nyiah ka yix,
mai map nai ka yix heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix sang song rhawm ka yix leeing ju lie?
Krax pa song rhawm maix awm cap ngu,
sang hoik si dauh yam mawx lie?
6 Khiox khaw maix kling krax pa song rhawm maix ka meung pa ang koe krax lhat graung yung yi ka maix,
klup ka piang to kaux pui pa ang koe krax khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka maix.
7 Mawh kheu pauh kix ka pui cie maix,
kieh dee ot maix lih nhik khawm uik heue.
 
8 Khiox khaw maix paw vi ciyang yix kheu phi map tax du yix liak.
Khiox khaw maix nied tix caox rhawm ka yix liak,
mawh kheu hoik som nai krax keud pot jun pot yix khawm uik.
9 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix teem coi ka yix,
taoh ka yix liak;
son sang mawh min plak ngai to kaux maix,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka yix,
pluat ploi ka phi map yix.
10 Yuh ka mawx meung ka piang haktiex chawk cheng ah nin:
Siyiex pex ot dee mawx lie?
Khiox khaw maix kieh yix yaox pa ciyang maix ka meung ka piang haktiex,
mawh kheu pauh kix ka siliex maix heue.
 
11 Kieh maix nghiet ka lox pa riag yo pui pa laung khankix;
kieh maix teei taix pa ting riang rao tix yah ploi ka pui laung pa sang pauh pui yum.
12 Simiang eue,
krax kaig tua pa yuh meung pa koe sivie siveng yix ka maix,
khiox khaw maix deeh pawk piang kix aliah son.
13 Yuh nin,
pa mawh pui cie maix mai phung yung cie maix,
yix tag sang yaog yaw ka maix leeing ju leeing phan,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix tom hoik kaing mheen ju heue.
