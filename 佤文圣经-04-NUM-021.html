Puing pex ka pui Cia-nang
21
aYam mhong simiang Ya-la-teu pa ot ka Cia-nang plak seh,
via pui Yi-seu-liex tix hu krax Ya-tha-ling,
nawh tom hu puing ka kix,
mai pon giex ka kix tix blah.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ap pong tix tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Phan maix kieh yix puing pex ka pui in kix,
yix tag tan kix mai veng ot kix ka maix giex goingb,
mai baox pauh kix laux leeing.”
3 Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox rawg kix,
tawx pui Cia-nang daux tawm taix kix.
Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex pauh kix mai veng ot kix laux leeing uik,
dee an tom kok mawh Heu-eu-mac heue.
Riang si uing pa yuh ka tawng
4 dPui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing gawng Heu-eu,
mai hu ka krax plak Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
son sang via ka baoh haktiex Yi-tong.
Ka daux krax,
kawn pui ang lai rhawm hu;
5 ekix simah ka Siyiex mai Maw-si ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx lai viex yix lih hu khaing Ai-cix,
son sang kieh yix yum ka daux tiag dee ang koe priex preem mai dee ang rawm koe lie?
Yix ang lai pon bleet ka ceu pa ang nyawm in kix!”
6 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ploi si uing pa koe jai laig ka si nax pui;
koe pui Yi-seu-liex tom nie hoik yum ka tuh si uing pa koe jai tix.
7 Kawn pui hoek ka Maw-si mai ah nin:
“Yix hoik ah lox caox ka Simiang Siyiex mai maix,
yix hoik phit lud.
Yam in khiox khaw maix rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
kieh si uing in kix hu singai khaing yix liak.”
Maw-si tom rawg buan son kawn pui.
8 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
nhie nawh yuh si uing tawng tix mu,
vhak ka rong khaox,
son sang kieh pui pa tuh si uing pa koe jai im kheu yaog ngai tix jhak si uing tawng.
9 fKhaix an,
Maw-si yuh ka si uing tawng tix mu,
mai vhak ka piang rong khaox.
Yam yaog pui pa tuh si uing pa koe jai kaing tix jhak si uing tawng an,
tag yaox tix im heue.
Jauh ka gawng Heu-eu tom hoek ka ngawd Maw-ya
10 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix baox nied tix hu sivoe,
mai sum nyiex bang ka A-pawx.
11 Khaix hoik gah kix tix hu khaing A-pawx,
kix sum nyiex bang ot haktiex Maw-ya plak lih singaix,
ka daux dee riem tiag Ya-pa-ling,
12 khaix an mai dee hoek ot ka ngawd Sa-liiex.
13 Kix deeh gah tix hu khaing tan,
mai sum nyiex bang ot ka daux tiag ka praok plak laong;
daux tiag an phlat hoek tom mee haktiex pui Ya-maw-lix.
(Rawm klawng Ya-noeng mawh si nax mee haktiex pui Maw-ya mai pui Ya-maw-lix.)
14 Mawh kheu nan,
hoik tiiem tix deuh Ka Daux Phuk Lai Naing Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Veng Wa-ha-pawx daux ngawd klawng Ya baoh haktiex Su-fa,
15 mai lih laig daux veng Ya-eux,
tom hoek ka dee kawk ka praok haktiex Maw-ya heue.”
16 Kix lih khaing tan mai hu ka dee kok mawh Pi-eux (dee bleeh mawh “rawm maw”).
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si dee an:
“Maix hu khrawm pui khankix,
mai aux tag sang tawx kix nyaux ka rawm.”
17 Yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex khankix rhax ka lai ah nin:
Maw eue,
rawm hoik buing tix lih khaing;
yix sang rhax lai yuh krax gaux rhawm.
18 In mawh rawm maw pa kaong pui pa koe min khankix,
mai pa kaong simiang pui haktiex khankix ka khaox gix tix.
Kix lih khaing daux tiag,
mai gah tix hu ka Ma-tha-na,
19 mai baox dee hu khaing dee an hoek ka Na-ha-liex,
mai baox deeh hoek ka Pa-mawx,
20 Jauh ka Pa-mawx hu ka daux ngawd mee haktiex Maw-ya,
mai hoik kok chix yaox ka dee pa mawh daux tiag choek gawng Pi-see-cia heue.
Puing pex ka simiang Ya-maw-lix mai simiang Pa-sang
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 2.26–3.11)
21 Pui Yi-seu-liex ploi kawn cau khankix hu ka Si-hong simiang Ya-maw-lix,
mai ah nin:
22 “Khiox khaw maix kieh yix laig daux haktiex cie maix liak.
Yix mai khawng brie cie yix,
ang chix lih hu khaing daux krax ting,
hu laig ka daux kaung,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka daux si peem plix siiet;
yix ang nyaux ka rawm maw pex.
Yix tag sang lawng ngie ka daux krax ting,
tom phlat laig ka daux haktiex cie pex.”
23 Viang mawh nan,
simiang Si-hong ang cu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hu laig ka daux haktiex tix.
Nawh khrawm pui naing tix hu ka daux tiag,
son sang kuan tix puing pui Yi-seu-liex ka haktiex Ya-cax.
24 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex pon pauh ka pui koeng tom nie,
mai pon cax ka haktiex kix,
jauh ka rawm klawng Ya-noeng tom hoek ka haktiex Ya-paw,
pa mawh daog haktiex Ya-meung,
mawh kheu mee haktiex ceu pui Ya-meung hoik mawh pa kaux pa cong.
25 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix pon cax ka veng pui Ya-maw-lix khawm uik,
khawm veng Si-seu-peung mai nyiex yaong pa koe sivie siveng dee an;
pui Yi-seu-liex tom ot dee an.
26 Si-seu-peung mawh veng ting daux tix mu pa ot simiang cie simiang Si-hong ceu pui Ya-maw-lix;
Si-hong hoik puing ka simiang haktiex Maw-ya sivoe an,
mai pon cax ka haktiex nawh khawm uik,
tom hoek ka rawm klawng Ya-noeng.
27 Mawh kheu nan,
can pui pa ah lox leng khankix ah nin:
Hoek ka veng Si-seu-peung,
deeh ot simiang Si-hong;
ex sang jhak grawng pa deeh cox sum nawh tix.
28 gMawh kheu ngu lih khaing veng Si-seu-peung,
koe rao ngu pa lih khaing veng Si-hong,
gleeing veng Ya-eux haktiex Maw-ya,
bleet ka gawng hawt rawm klawng Ya-noeng.
29 Pui haktiex Maw-ya eue,
pex tag kha rhawm kha rhi tix!
Ceu pui Ci-mawx khankix eue,
pex tag laux leeing!
Siyiex in hoik kieh kawn simex pex tang to mox tix khaing krax yum,
kieh kawn bun pex khankix mawh pa giex pa laung simiang Ya-maw-lix.
30 Yam in doeh ceu cad kix hoik laux leeing,
jauh ka veng Si-seu-peung tom hoek ka veng Ti-peung,
jauh ka Na-sing hoek ka Naw-fax baoh dee dex ka Mi-ti-pa.
31 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix yaox tix ot ka daux haktiex ceu pui Ya-maw-lix khankix.
32 Maw-si ploi pui jiang hu ka veng Ya-shiex;
pui Yi-seu-liex khankix pon cax ka Ya-shiex mai nyiex yaong sivie siveng kix,
mai grheu ka ceu pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot tan to heue.
33 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex deeh veeh tix ing,
mai hu krax plak haktiex Pa-sang.
Eux simiang haktiex Pa-sang thung pui naing tix hoek puing kix ka Yi-teu-lai.
34 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Paw lhat ka nawh.
Aux sang ap nawh mai pui cie nawh mai haktiex nawh ka daux tawm taix maix.
Maix hoik yuh yuh ka mawx ka Si-hong simiang pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot Si-seu-peung,
kawx yuh nang nan ka simiang Pa-sang.”
35 Kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex pauh Eux mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
khawm pui cie nawh khawm uik,
ang lai deuk tix kaux kawx,
mai pon cax ka haktiex nawh khankix heue.
