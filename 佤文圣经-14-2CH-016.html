Yo-tax mai Yi-seu-liex puing paox tix
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 15.17–22)
16
Yam mawh simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa cao pa giex kaing ka daux tix ngoe liah num,
simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pa-sa puing ka pui Yo-tax,
mai yuh tab ot ka La-max,
kaing ting mawh son sang tat dut ka krax pa laig pa lih ka Yo-tax khawm uik.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-sa viex khri mau pa ot ka daux nyiex buan mai ka daux nyiex haw simiang hoek ka Ta-ma-see-keux,
ka dee ot simiang Shui-li-yax pa kaux Piien Ha-tax dee an,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
3 “Tax du ex hoik mawh pui pa yuh paox pie tau,
kieh ex yuh tau nang heue!
Khri mau in kix mawh khawng phak pa tawx aux ka maix.
Khiox khaw maix deeh tik ka krax pa hoik yuh paox pie tau mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pa-sa,
son sang kieh nawh viex pui naing tix lih hu khaing haktiex cie aux heue.”
4 Piien Ha-tax cu ka lox krai Ya-sa,
nawh tom ploi cao kaing pa bung pui naing pa ting tix hu puing ka veng Yi-seu-liex.
Kix puing ka Yi-yuing,
Tang,
Ya-paw Paw Ma-cia,
khawm veng Na-fu-tha-lix pa hoik deuh kraung khrai ot daux.
5 Simiang Pa-sa mhong kaing pa lih tix mawh,
dum mawx dum an ang lai sum ka tab pa sum tix ka La-max,
mai prah ghram ka kaing dee an.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-sa khrawm ka pui Yo-tax pa ot ku dauh,
mai kieh kix gah simaox khaox ox pa siao Pa-sa ka La-max,
viex hu sum veng Cia-pa mai veng Mi-see-pa heue.
Ha-na-ni cao pa chix yaox sivoe
7 Yam an,
cao pa chix yaox sivoe Ha-na-ni hu ka simiang Ya-sa,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix een rhawm tix ka simiang Shui-li-yax,
ang een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix,
mawh kheu nan pui naing simiang Yi-seu-liexa tom yaog tix to heue.
8 Singaix pa hoik luan,
ang Ku-seu mai Li-pi-yax hoik koe pui naing pa ting riang,
mai baox koe nang ling law mai pui naing brug brung tom nie lie?
Viang mawh nan,
maix hoik een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai nawh tag kieh maix pex ka kix.
9 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka haktiex khawm uik,
mai tawx riang rao ka pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh.
Kaing in maix hoik kieh mawh pa daox pheem tete,
mawh kheu nan jauh yam in,
maix tag sang si pup ka krax naing ang jud ang jai.”
10 Yam mhong Ya-sa ka lox in nawh kied saux rhawm kah,
nawh tom laung ka cao pa chix yaox sivoe.
Khaix an kawx,
Ya-sa jauh tix nu nai ka kawn pui tix blah heue.
Kaing giex Ya-sa hoik si dauh
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 15.23–24)
11 Kaing pa lih tix mawh yam giex Ya-sa kaing,
hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Yo-tax mai Simiang Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
12 Yam mawh Ya-sa simiang ka daux tix ngoe dim num,
mawh kheu koe jhawm saux caong nawh,
nawh tom chawh;
viang nawh yuh nan ka daux bawg yam an kawx,
nawh ang khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex teem coi tix,
mai sawg ngie ka cao sidah khaing.
13 Khaix hoik luan daux ra num nawh yum,
14 mai si paung nawh ot ka veng Ta-wue,
pa mawh daux si meeig simaox pa hoik grox saik nawh son to kaux tix.
Pui khankix teei sidah bhawng mai bee bhawng kraix ka to kaux yum nawh,
mai baox phlu ngu tom ting,
laux rhawm son nawh heue.
