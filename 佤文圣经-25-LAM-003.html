Krax ciyang, krax loh rhawm, krax keud pot jun pot
3
Siyiex ciyang ka aux,
aux hoik cim krax kha rhawm tete.
2 Nawh grheu aux laig ka daux pa faik,
kieh aux nied ot nied glip.
3 Nawh baox lauh ka aux,
ang lai koe krax pa caox rhawm ka aux pie tix ceeix.
4 Nawh hoik kieh nex nham aux mat;
hoik raig ka si-ang aux pot.
5 Nawh sum guh veng ka sivie siveng aux,
teei krax kha rhawm tuk tiak lai lawm ka aux.
6 Nawh khin aux paung ot ka daux pa faik,
hoik awm pui yum tom rheeng yuh nan heue.
 
7 Nawh teei maox ceng phrawk ka aux;
aux lih mawh pa laung pui,
ang pon yaox tix luat luan.
8 Aux kok yo lox si num krax taoh teem,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex ang nghiet.
9 Nawh teei simaox ting thox daux krax hu aux,
kieh aux chawh mai lawng krax heue.
 
10 Nawh awm krih pa krox jun tix kuan ka aux yuh nan,
awm sivai sang si pa plab tix ka aux yuh nan.
11 Nawh grheu aux lih hu khaing krax ting,
riah ka aux,
tik aux ot praok krax.
12 Nawh vaik ak puh sau te ka aux,
kieh aux mawh mhai tang pa puing ka te heue.
 
13 Nawh puing aux ka te,
cieh ka to kaux aux.
14 Pui hoik nyiah ka aux leeing ngaix;
aux lih tix mawh krax pa nyiah pui.
15 Nawh kieh aux sak ka cim krax laux leeing,
khin aux nyaux ka plai pa song heue.
 
16 Nawh yeen tix ku kried ngai aux ka tiex;
nawh teei simaox su si rhang aux pot kah.
17 Krax ot krax koe aux ang yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe;
aux hoik piawm ceu tix mawh krax gaux rhawm.
18 Singaix bawg yam pa im ju aux ka piang haktiex ang lai koe miex glang tix;
krax keud pot jun pot aux ka Simiang Siyiex kawx hoik uik heue.
 
19 Yam keud aux ka krax kha rhawm mai krax vu vie tix,
hoik awm mawh sidah awp pa song.
20 Aux keud kaing in kix ang jud ang jai,
ka daux rhawm rhi aux hoik cien tom ang lai chix krai lox.
21 Viang mawh nan,
yam keud aux ka kaing tang ceu,
krax keud pot jun pot aux baox cox tix rug heue.
 
22 Krax muh Simiang Siyiex ang chix grai leeing ju;
krax pa caox rhawm nawh ang chix naox tix leeing ju;
23 mai mhawm singax awm pon ngawb cao yuh nan,
awm yam jauh singaix tix lih tete yuh nan.
24 Simiang Siyiex mawh can pa koe aux khawm uik;
aux tawx krax keud pot jun pot tix ka nawh heue.
 
25 Pui pa sawg ka nawh,
mai pa yum rhawm ka nawh,
Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka kix.
26 Mawh kheu nan ex caw een rhawm tix ka nawh,
viex rhawm gun krox jun ka krax taoh nawh.
27 Ex caw gau tix klawng khaox glawm yam nhum ju tix heue.
 
28 Yam si pup ex ka krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi,
ex caw ngawm tix gun yeung.
29 Ex caw num kaing tix cu nghiet,
ex tee kawn koe krax keud pot jun pot.
30 Viang ex hoik yaox krax pa si vied pa nu nai,
ex caw tix gun yeung heue.
 
31 Simiang Siyiex koe krax pa caox rhawm tom nie,
nawh ang chix tieb tie ka ex.
32 Viang nawh hoik kieh aux laux rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
krax muh nawh ka ex hoik kaux cong ang yung yang.
33 Nawh ang su tix kieh ex kha rhawm tix,
ang su tix kieh ex khi rhawm heue.
 
34 Yam kha rhawm tix ka daux thawng,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka heue!
35 Siawp sibrawm pa tawx rao ma pui hoik dug dig kah,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka heue!
36 Pui nieb pon ah ka daux nyiex prawng see,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka heue!
 
37 Lheu khaing gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
ang koe pui pa pon yuh lih.
38 Mawh krax laux krax leeing,
mawh buan son,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing lox tawx cao pa ting pa lhaong.
39 Hoik kham krax ciyang kheu phi map,
kawn sang chix salao yuh ka mawx lie?
 
40 Yix caw tix chix tang deeh doh ka kaing ceu pa yuh tix,
deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex.
41 Yix caw tix paoh siviex rhawm rhi tix,
khiox khaw ka Siyiex piang rao ma ah nin:
42 yix hoik tieh vheen phit lud,
maix ang bax pluat ploi ka yix heue.
 
43 Maix hoik leue yix ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix;
maix ang lai koe krax pa caox rhawm mai hoik pauh ka yix.
44 Maix teei pai awm tawb,
ang pon kieh krax rawg yix buan phlat tix hu heue.
45 Maix hoik kieh yix lih mawh kram nyiex ka daux kawn pui piang haktiex,
pui ang rhawm yaox ka yix heue.
 
46 Pui pa koeng ka yix nyiah ka yix,
ca nu nai ka yix ang jud ang jai.
47 Yix hoik kham krax kha rhawm mai laux leeing,
mai ot ka daux krax kha rhawm mai ka daux krax lhat graung.
48 Son laux rhawm ka laux leeing pa si pup paox ceu paox cad tix,
rawm ngai aux hoik buing tix lih awm rawm klawng yuh nan heue.
 
49 Rawm ngai aux lih ang jud,
hoik buing tix lih ang jud ang jai,
50 krox jun Simiang Siyiex kia ngien tix,
mai kok simi tix jhak ka rao ma.
51 Yam yaox aux krax pa si pup bun miex pa ot daux veng,
rhawm rhi aux hoik saux heue.
 
52 Pui koeng hoik koeng ka aux giex going,
mai leue tix giex ka aux,
awm leue ka kawn sim yuh nan.
53 Kix si paung im aux ka daux o,
teei simaox thox daux moing.
54 Rawm hoik dex sang kheem ka aux;
aux keud:
ka daux singaix yum aux hoik hoek heue.
 
55 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux ot ka daux dee raux,
khiox khaw ka ceu kaux maix.
56 Aux khiox khaw ka maix,
maix hoik nghiet ka aux.
57 Maix hoik pawk ka lox aux,
maix ang cu aux lhat heue.
 
58 Simiang eue,
maix prawng rauh see son aux;
maix hoik taoh ka ju aux.
59 Maix hoik tawng grawng pa kha aux rhawm tix;
khiox khaw maix prawng aux ang koe krax lud.
60 Maix hoik tawng grawng pa koeng pui koeng ka aux,
hoik tawng grawng sang yuh kix aux laux leeing heue.
 
61 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik mhong lox pa jhax lawg kix ka aux;
maix hoik tawng grawng pa sang keud kix tix yuh pui laux.
62 Kix hoik kum kawng son aux leeing lang singaix
hoik keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing.
63 Jauh tom pon ngawb hoek tom pon sawm,
kix hoik nyiah ka aux heue.
 
64 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix ciyang ka kix;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix pawk bix awm kaing pa yuh kix yuh nan;
65 Khiox khaw maix jhax lawg ka kix,
kieh kix deek rhawm.
66 Khiox khaw maix leue kix ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix;
kieh kix grai grup khaing daux haktiex heue.
