Lai Pi-teux Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Lai Pi-teux Phuk Tix mawh pa tiiem tix tawx ka kawn Khrit pa rai rhuat tix ot ka baoh haktiex Ya-si-yax pa iak plak laong, lox lai in kok kix mawh “pui cie Siyiex”. Phuk lai kaing ting tawx krax pa joh jhan ka kawn Khrit pa si pup krax kha rhawm mai krax tuk tiak tuk te son pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex. Cao tiiem baox teei ka gum lox buan Ye-sux Khrit pa mawh pa yum nawh, pa deeh kaoh im nawh, mai gum lox pa deuh son pa sang baox deeh hoek nawh sidiex gau ka kix, tawx kix koe krax keud pot jun pot. Mawh kheu nan, kix tag caw tix kham gee gun yeung krax kha rhawm, yum rhawm grawng krax kha rhawm mawh pa cheuk tix yaox ka krax yum rhawm kix, khaix an tag chix kieh kix pon khawng phak buan son “ka daux singaix bawg yam lih hoek Ye-sux Khrit” heue.
Cao tiiem ang joeng tawx lox pa joh jhan ka kawn Khrit yam si pup kix ka krax kha rhawm yuh ka mawx, mai sidiex gau nang grawng krax ot krax koe pa sang yuh kix pa mawh pui pa dex dawt tau mai Khrit heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–2)
2. Krai grawh grawng krax taoh Siyiex (1.3–12)
3. Sidiex gau grawng sang ot ka daux krax pa singax singeh (1.13–2.10)
4. Beeh glawm pa caw kawn Khrit tix yuh (2.11–4.19)
5. Krax pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi kawn Khrit mai kaing krub (5.1–11)
6. Lox pa nghoik tix krai (5.12–14)
