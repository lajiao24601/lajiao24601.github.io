10
Lox tat jhawm prawng ang mawh ceu pa grum mhawm mai pa koe pak peung tete,
mawh ngie riang pang peue ceu pa mhawm pa sang lih tix mawh singaix pa sang hoek.
Krax tan lu pa yuh nan nang hoik tan tix ku num,
lox tat jhawm prawng kawn sang chix kieh hoek diag pa grum mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex kheu ka daux krax tan lu in kix yuh ka mawx lie?
2 Phan phi map pui pa krub ka Siyiex pon pa singax tete,
kix tag ang lai baox koe rhawm rhi pa keud ka phi map,
kaing ceu pa tan lu khawm uik kawx tag sang naox tix.
3 Tete te lai,
pa tan lu ku num mawh ngie son sang kieh doh keud ka phi map to kaux tix,
4 mawh kheu nham moi mhaing mai nham kawn pex ang chix teei tix tik ka phi map kawn pui heue.
5 aMawh kheu nan,
yam sang hoek Khrit ka piang haktiex,
ah nin ka Siyiex:
maix ang lo ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai khawng tan tang ceu;
maix hoik rien khre to kaux tix son aux.
6 Maix ang lo ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing pa koe piang khu dee tan,
ang lo ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
7 Mawh kheu nan aux ah nin:
Siyiex eue,
aux hoik hoek,
mawh son sang hawt ka alo maix,
awm pa tiiem daux phuk lai lox tat jhawm prawng,
pa koe grawh grawng mai aux heue.
8 Nawh ran ah nin sivoe:
“Maix ang lo,
mai ang muh ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai krax pa tan lu tang ceu,
mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing pa koe piang khu dee tan,
mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.”
Viang krax pa tan lu in kix hoik hawt tix tan awm lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai nawh ca kawn krai yuh nin.
9 Khaix an nawh baox ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
aux hoik hoek,
mawh son sang priex tix hawt ka alo maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex hoik teei tix tik ka krax tan lu pa koe sivoe khawm uik,
son sang kieh pa yum Khrit tang.
10 Ye-sux Khrit hoik priex tix hawt ka alo Siyiex,
tan lu ka to kaux tix,
ka daux tix bawg khaing hoik mawh ku jheeng jheeng,
hoik kieh ex singax singeh tix mawh pui cie Simiang Ye-sux heue.
11 bCao giex kaing li Yo-thaix cung tix yuh kaing krub ka Siyiex ku ngaix,
tan lu ka khawng tan pa awm paox tom nie bawg;
viang mawh nan,
khawng tan in kix ang pon teei tix tik ka phi map kawn pui leeing ju.
12 cViang mawh nan,
Khrit tan lu to kaux tix tix bawg khaing mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap pa koe ceu leeing ju,
khaix an ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex,
13 krox jun Siyiex kieh pui pa koeng ka nawh kreet tix ot greem caong nawh.
14 Krax pa yum nawh tix bawg khaing,
hoik kieh pui pa singax singeh cie Simiang Siyiex lih tix mawh pa grum mhawm heue.
15 Khuan Singax Singeh kawx hoik sax kham sakse ka ex.
Nawh ran tix ah nin sivoe:
16 dSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu pa yuh nin tau mai kix:
aux tag sang deuh lox tat jhawm prawng tix ot ka piang sok rhawm kix,
tiiem ot ka daux dieb dong kix heue.
17 eKhaix an nawh baox ah nin:
“Aux ang lai tiiem tix deuh ka phi map kix mai krax pa lud loe kix.”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
kheu hoik pon phi map in kix krax pa pluat ploi,
mai tag ang lai baox lo ka khawng tan pa pluat ploi phi map heue.
Kieh ex hoik dex ka Siyiex
19 Phan mawh nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
ka daux pa yum Ye-sux ex chix tix viex rhawm pa ang lhat ang graung laig ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh.
20 Nawh hoik paih krax pa khraox tix nyaing son ex,
pa phlat hoek ka man tie,
pa mawh krax im ju pa phlat hoek ka to kaux nawh.
21 Kheu hoik koe ex cao giex kaing li pa ting tix kaux pa giex kaing ka nyiex Siyiex,
22 fPhan mawh nan,
ex caw tix siao ka rhawm pa rauh pa see mai krax yum rhawm pa kaux pa cong,
siao ka rhawm rhi pa hoik pon krax pa singax singeh,
mai ka to kaux pa hoik khoek singax ka rawm hoek dex ka Siyiex.
23 Kieh ex kaux cong ang yung yang ka krax keud pot jun pot pa hoik krai pao ex,
mawh kheu hoik mawh gum lox pa deuh Siyiex pa kaux sijax.
24 Kieh ex ra lai chix jhak ka paox tix,
ra lai muh ka paox tix,
ra lai joh jhan paox tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm.
25 Paw vi awm pui tix blah pa hoik tik vud ka been pa khrawm paox tix tau,
tag caw tix ra lai sidiex gau ka paox tix;
kheu hoik tawng singaix bawg yam cie Simiang Ye-sux dex sang hoek,
pex nie caw tix yuh nin heue.
26 Khaix hoik tawng ex ka pa mawh pa meh,
phan ex ca kawn su tix yuh phi map,
taug ang lai baox koe khawng tan pa chix pluat ploi ka phi map tix ceu kawx.
27 gEx si yaung si kua mai krox jun ka krax prawng ca,
mai ka ngu ting pa tag sang gleeing ka pui pa tieb tie ka Siyiex heue!
28 hPui pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
ca mawh koe pui ra loe kaux pa kham sakse pa lud nawh,
tag caw tix pauh yum,
ang lai pluat ploi ka nawh.
29 iPhan mawh nan,
ka pui pa nu nai ka kawn simex Siyiex,
pa ang een nham Siyiex koe ceu,
pa mawh nham pa hoik singax singeh ka phi map nawh,
kheu nan ka pui an pa map nai ka buan son khuan tag sang deeh yuh ka mawx lie?
Keud tix yaox,
sang caw ang nawh caw tix kham krax ciyang pa nie kin lie?
30 jEx hoik tawng pui mawx pa ah nin:
“Krax prawng rauh see mawh cie aux,
aux tag sang deeh vied kah”;
mai baox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex tag sang prawng ca ka pui cie tix.”
31 Pui pa cawd ka daux tawm taix Siyiex kied mawh krax pa ting lhat graung heue!
32 Tag caw tix doh keud ka singaix pa hoik luan.
Khaix hoik kia ngien Siyiex ka pex,
krax kha rhawm pa si pup pex tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang bax som nai laux leeing ka naing.
33 Bawg tix blah,
pex kham krax pa map nai pui mai krax pa nieb pui yuh ka piang pex;
bawg tix blah,
pex cung tau mai pui pa si pup krax pa kha rhawm in kix.
34 Pex kham krax kha rhawm tau mai pui lud.
Yam tud pui ka sin ngao pa koe pex khawm uik,
pex pon gun yeung;
mawh kheu pex hoik tawng,
pex kawn priex tix deuh ka sin ngao pa nie blawn mhawm pa koe leeing ju.
35 Mawh kheu nan,
pex paw vi kieh tix grai riang rao pa kua;
riang rao pa kua in tag sang viex tix tawx khawng phak buan son pa ting ka pex.
36 Son sang yuh lih ka alo Siyiex mai ka pa pon ka gum lox pa deuh nawh,
pex tag caw tix gun yeung.
37 kMawh kheu hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
baox jun tix vud,
pa tag sang hoek an sang hoek,
ang lhax hoek.
38 Pui pa rauh see cie aux tag sang pon ju kheu krax yum rhawm tix heue.
Viang mawh nan,
phan koe pui pa yawn tix deeh,
aux tag ang khaw rhawm ka nawh heue.
39 Ex ang mawh pui pa yawn tix pa laux leeing;
ex hoik mawh pui pa koe krax yum rhawm pa pon krax taoh heue.
