Lai Pi-teux Phuk Ra
Krai dawt
Lai Pi-teux Phuk Ra mawh pa tiiem tix tawx ka kawn Khrit pa koe sivoe, kaing ting mawh pa cox rauh see ka lox krai sara cao gau pa pie lhien khankix, mai ka grawng pa yuh kix krax pa laux leeing lih ka daux ceu cao. Phan sang cox kaing in kix mawh pa mhawm, tag caw kieh tix tawng ka Siyiex mai ka Ye-sux Khrit tete; ngai lai pa yuh nin mai hoik mawh pa sidiex gau pui pa hoik sax yaox Ye-sux ka ngai tix, pa hoik mhong lox sidiex gau nawh ka yhaok tix. Cao tiiem nie krai sidawng yuh nin: Lox krai pui pa een Khrit ang lai deeh baox hoek, taug mawh pa lud; grawng ang Khrit bax lih hoek, hoik mawh kheu Siyiex “ang rhawm kieh pui tix kaux laux leeing, viang mawh nan, tag simie pui ku kaux loh ka rhawm rhi tix heue”.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–2)
2. Pa kok Siyiex (1.3–21)
3. Sara pa pie lhien (2.1–22)
4. Pa deeh hoek Khrit (3.1–18)
