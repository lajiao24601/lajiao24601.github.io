Pui Yo-thaix deuh phi map piang Pao-law
24
Khaix hoik luan ka daux phuan singaix,
Ya-na-ni-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting mai kuad song ra loe kaux,
khawm sara cao prawng tix kaux pa kaux Thie-thu-law,
hoek ka Khai-sha-li-yax.
Kix deuh phi map piang Pao-law ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Fe-li-see.
2 Kix kok Sao-law laig daux nyiex prawng;
Thie-thu-law deuh phi map piang nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Fe-li-see pa ting heue!
Mawh kheu kuang kia maix ka daux krax pa lhak lhiao tix,
yix tom pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe tom rheeng,
been ceu pa ang mhawm ka daux meung hoik pon tix tieh loh tix.
3 Kheu daux krax pa mhawm giex maix ka been,
yix hoik yaog yaw maix ang jud ang jai.
4 Aux ang pon lai hoek tix nie luan nyhap ka maix,
kieh maix suk sag,
mai khiox khaw ngie maix nghiet ka lox dawt pa krai yix ah nin:
5 yix hoik yaox sidawng,
pui in mawh pui pa chix kieh pui laux leeing tix kaux.
Nawh mawh pui pa yuh krax pa suk sag ka daux pui Yo-thaix piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai baox mawh cub caw cao bung kaing pui Na-sha-leux tix kaux,
6 pie nyiex buan kawx hoik seeing brai kah,
yix tom giex ka nawh.a
8 Phan maix chawk cheng tix yaox ka pui in,
maix tag sang tawng grawng pa deuh yix phi map ka piang nawh khawm uik.”
9 Pui Yo-thaix pa ot dee an kawx hoik rom tix deuh phi map piang nawh,
mai hoik een lox pa krai yix mawh tete heue.
Pao-law krai lox son to kaux tix
10 Yam an,
simiang an khiep ngai tix,
nhie Pao-law si kah.
Pao-law tom ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawng grawng pa giex maix been ka daux meung tix tom nie num,
mawh kheu nan,
aux gaux rhawm tix krai lox son tix ka plak sivoe ngai maix.
11 Ang nu maix si kaw,
jauh yam haok aux yuh krax krub ka Yie-lu-sa-leung hoek yam in taug mawh kao ra ngaix khaing.
12 Kix ang bax yaox yam thung thiang aux pui tix kaux kawx ka daux nyiex buan,
mai ang bax yaox nang yam yuh aux pui hun suk sag ka nyiex sidiex,
mai ka haktiex tang dauh pa koe daux veng.
13 Kix ang koe ceu pa sang deuh phi map ka piang aux.
14 Kheu nan,
aux sang krai doh ka kaing tix ceu yuh nin:
aux hoik hawt ka daux krax pa een kix mawh krax pa ang khaw an,
mai hoek tix krub ka Siyiex tax du yix.
Aux kawx yum rhawm ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
mai ka gum lox pa hoik tiiem cao jhai daux phuk lai khawm uik.
15 Aux kied koe krax keud pot jun pot ka Siyiex awm pa koe kix yuh nang,
pa mawh simie pui ku kaux,
pui caox ang ah,
pui mhawm ang ah,
tag sang deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum khawm uik.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
aux sidiex ka to kaux tix jheeng jheeng,
ka plak sivoe ngai pui mai Siyiex,
tag caw tix kieh rhawm rhi tix singax jheeng jheeng heue.
17 b“Aux lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik koe miex num mawx.
Bawg in aux haok hoik viex mau pa sang teem coi tix ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
khaix an kawn sang baox tan lu ka krax tan.
18 Yam yaox kix ot aux ka daux nyiex buan,
aux hoik yuh ka been pa singax,
mai tan lu ka krax tan;
aux ang bax khrawm ka pui hun,
mai ang bax yuh krax pa suk sag.
19 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui Yo-thaix pa hoek khaing haktiex Ya-si-yax ot dee an,
pui in kix phan kix koe kaing ceu pa deuh phi map piang aux,
kix khaw tix hoek krai lao lox ka plak sivoe maix.
20 Phan ang mawh nan,
tag caw tix kieh pui in kix pa ot dee in krai lih,
yam krai aux lox ka daux bang kum,
mawh hoik yuh aux krax phit lud ceu pa tix.
21 cYam cung aux ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
aux kok yo lox tix gum khaing ah nin:
‘Ka daux in singaix aux hoik kham krax pa prawng pex,
khex ang mawh son pa yum rhawm ka grawng krax pa deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum heue.’ ”
22 Fe-li-see hoik dawg sidawng ka daux krax in tete,
mawh kheu nan naox nghiet tix ka lox see,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Krox jun simiang naing Lui-si-yax hoek ha,
aux tom tag sang prawng son maix.”
23 Nawh tawx lox nhie pui naing pau ka Pao-law,
kia nawh mhawm mhawm,
mai cu paox grawm nawh tawx pa sang lo nawh ku singaix heue.
Pao-law ot plak sivoe ngai Fe-li-see mai Thu-si-la
24 Khaix hoik luan ra loe singaix,
Fe-li-see mai moeng nawh pa mawh Thu-si-la kawn bun Yo-thaix hoek tau.
Nawh kok Pao-law hoek,
mai nghiet lox pa krai nawh son grawng pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux Khrit.
25 Pao-law baox krai lox sidiex grawh grawng pa dex mai ceu pa mhawm pa mhiem,
ceu pa chix priex rhawm tix,
mai krax prawng ca pa tag sang hoek singaix khaix khankix.
Fe-li-see kied lhat graung kah,
mai krai ka Pao-law ah nin:
“Yam in maix hu heue,
krox jun aux koe bawg yam ha,
aux koi baox kok maix hoek.”
26 Nawh baox simie Pao-law tawx mau ka tix,
mawh kheu nan,
rien kok nawh hoek krai lao lox tau mai tix jheeng jheeng heue.
27 Khaix hoik luan ra num,
Paw-chio Fe-see-to plud son Fe-li-see,
lih mawh simiang.
Fe-li-see yuh son sang khaw rhawm pui Yo-thaix,
mai ca kieh Pao-law ot ka daux thawng heue.
