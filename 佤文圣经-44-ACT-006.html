Pui pa teem coi aliah kaux
6
Bawg yam in,
kaux pui gau tix thiem thie tix ku singaix;
pui Yo-thaix pa si kah ka lox Si-lax khankix,
mai ka si nax pui Yo-thaix pa si kah ka lox Si-paw-lai simah paox tix.
Pui Yo-thaix pa si kah ka lox Si-lax salao kawn cau ka son mau sawm pa guah tix tawx ku ngaix ka daux kaing in,
ah kawn cau hoik piawm cie miex mai ka daux kix.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau kao ra kaux tom khrawm pui gau hoek khawm uik,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex nhie yix ploi kaing jhaw Siyiex,
hu yuh kaing pa kuang ka priex preem in ang khaw.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
paox grawm ka daux Simiang Ye-sux khankix,
tag caw tix laug pui pa koe min,
pa naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
pa baox koe ka ping nyia khankix lih khaing daux pex aliah kaux,
mai kieh kix hoek tix kuang kaing in.
4 Phan mawh son yix,
yix tag sang yuh kaing pa rawg buan mai kaing pa sidiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix heue.”
5 Lox krai kawn cau pui hun hoik khaw rhawm kah.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui hun laug ka See-thi-fang;
nawh hoik mawh pui pa kaux krax yum rhawm tix kaux,
pa naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh;
mai baox laug ka Fe-lix,
Paw-law-keu-lawx,
Ni-cia-naw,
Thi-meung,
Pa-mi-na,
Ni-keu-la pui Ang-thi-a pa hoik loh tix laig li Yo-thaix.
6 Pui hun nhie kix cung ka plak sivoe ngai kawn cau;
khaix hoik rawg kawn cau buan,
kix tom deuh taix tix piang kaing kix heue.
7 Gum lox Siyiex nied tix sidiex tix hu;
kaux pui gau ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik thiem thie tix tom nie,
cao giex kaing li tom nie kawx hoik rieb krax yum rhawm heue.
Giex ka See-thi-fang
8 See-thi-fang hoik naog ka khawng phak buan son mai ka riang rao Siyiex,
mai hoik yuh mhai pa daox deng pa ting mai mhai pa daox pa am ka daux pui haktiex.
9 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui tix blah ang khaw rhawm ka nawh;
pui in kix hoik mawh pa krai mawh tix “pui pa bheeik luan” ka daux nyiex sidiex,
pa mawh pui khawm pui Yo-thaix pa kaoh khaing Ku-li-naix mai Ya-li-sang-tax.
Kix mai pui Yo-thaix pa hoek khaing Ci-li-cia mai Ya-si-yax,
thung thiang ka See-thi-fang.
10 Khuan Singax Singeh tawx ping nyia ka See-thi-fang,
mawh kheu nan,
kix ang tawng ka sang pawk lox pa hoik krai nawh.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
kix yuh krax pa khaw rhawm ka pui tix blah,
mai hoek tix deuh phi map piang nawh ah nin:
“Yix hoik mhong lox pa map nai pui in ka Maw-si,
mai ka Siyiex heue!”
12 Kix teei grawng in yuh krax pa suk sag ka pui hun,
ka kuad song,
mai ka sara pa chix kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix.
Kix hoek sawg ka See-thi-fang,
mai giex nawh viex nawh hoek ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix.
13 Pui pa kham mhai sakse pa pie lhien pa yuh kix krax pa khaw rhawm kah lih ah nin:
“Pui in hoik krai ka lox pa ang khaw ka nyiex buan ex,
mai ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si jheeng jheeng.
14 Yix hoik mhong lox pa krai nawh Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux in sang yieh ka nyiex buan,
mai sang tieh loh ka been pa hoik tawx Maw-si ka ex khawm uik heue!”
15 Can pui pa ngawm ka daux bang kum khawm uik,
hoik dong ngai tix jhak ka See-thi-fang,
kix yaox pak ngai nawh hoik awm pak ngai kawn cau Siyiex heue.
