Num Laux-lee
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 15.1–11)
25
aSimiang Siyiex si kah ka Maw-si ka piang gawng Si-nai,
nhie nawh
2 tawx lox krai pui Yi-seu-liex mhong ka lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ah nin:
“Khaix hoik laig pex ka daux haktiex pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pex,
aliah num tix bawg,
pex kieh haktiex laux lee tix,
son sang mawh krax pa nab yam ka Simiang Siyiex.
3 Ka daux liah num,
pex chix tix sum ma sum kaung,
mai cox pawt ka si peem plix siiet tix,
mai khrawm plix nghox plix koi khankix.
4 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux num aliah,
pex tag caw tix kieh haktiex laux lee tix khawm uik;
ka daux num in hoik mawh pa tan ka son Simiang Siyiex,
pex ang pon sum kaung sum ma,
ang pon tix pawt ka si peem plix siiet.
5 Khawm can nghox koi pa tu khankix ang pon tix vawg;
mai khawm plix siiet pa plix pa ang pex baox cox pawt khankix ang pon pieh.
Ka daux num in,
haktiex tag sang laux lee tix.
6 “Ka daux num in,
haktiex viang laux lee tix kawx,
viang mawh nan,
can to ma pa lawk kuah khankix,
tag mawh priex preem son pex.
Siliex pex,
pui cang pex,
mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax pex,
7 khawng brie,
mai to sat pa ot ka daux tiag daux tu khankix,
tag yaox tix ih ka ceu priex preem khawm uik.
Can to ma pa lawk kuah ka daux haktiex khankix,
pex tag chix tix ih uik heue.”
Num Gaux-rhawm
8 “Pex simin Num Laux Lee aliah bawg,
khawm uik tag mawh tipon dim num.
9 Ka daux num an khix aliah kao ngaix,
mawh singaix pa pluat ploi phi map,
pex tag caw tix ploi pui hu phlu teet tawng ka meung haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tom tag pon guah ka daux tix phuan num lih,
mai krai pao krax luat luan ka daux num in son pui pa ot ka daux haktiex an khawm uik,
mawh Num Gaux-rhawm.
Ka daux num in,
can sin ngao gawng ma nhu ke pa coh hu,
tag sang deeh ka cao,
mai ka doeh ceu cad nawh khankix;
can pui pa hoik coh mawh siliex khankix sivoe an,
tag sang ploi kah,
mai kieh nawh deeh ing ka nyiex cao tix.
11 Daux tix phuan num hoik mawh Num Gaux-rhawm pex.
Pex ang pon tix sum kaung ma,
ang pon tix vawg nghox koi pa lawk kuah khankix,
ang pon tix pieh plix siiet pa ang pex nyang baox cox pawt pa koe daux ma khankix.
12 Ka daux num in mawh Num Gaux-rhawm,
mawh num pa singax singeh ka pex;
pex chix ngie tix ih ka to ma pa lawk kuah khaing ka daux tiex khankix heue.
13 “Ka daux Num Gaux-rhawm in,
can sin ngao gawng ma nhu ke pa hoik coh khankix,
tag sang deeh ka cao tix sivoe an.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
yam yuh pex krax coh cai lai gee haktiex ka paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
pex caw tix kieh mawh ka daux pa rauh see.
15 Ngawh jum haktiex caw tix hawt ka to ma pa hoik pon yam jauh tix coh liag,
tom bleeh ka si nax num pa pluat ploi.
16 Phan num lang,
jum tag lhaong,
phan num dawt,
jum tag tiiem,
mawh kheu haktiex pa coh hu,
hoik yaox tix vawg ra loe bawg.
17 Pex ang pon tix ih pa yie cie paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yi-seu-liex khankix;
pex caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex pex,
mawh kheu aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex heue.”
Grawng Num Laux Lee
18 “Pex priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex mai ka gum lox ping nyap nawh khankix,
mai son sang yaox pex tix nyawm ot nyawm koe ka haktiex in.
19 Haktiex sang lih to ma to koi,
mai kieh pex yaox tix ih tom sak;
pex tag nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.
20 “Ka daux si nax pex tee koe nang pui pa sang chawk,
ka daux aliah num ex sang kieh kaung ma laux lee tix mai ang lai pon ka to ma,
sang ih ex ka ceu pa tix lie?
21 Simiang Siyiex tag tawx buan son ka pex,
kieh plix nghox plix koi pa pon pex ka daux liah num kub pex ih son ka daux loe num.
22 Ka daux num daix,
pex tag caw tix sum ka kaung ma,
viang mawh nan,
pex ca kawn ih ka nghox koi pa kawn deuk khankix,
tom hoek ka daux num dim yam hoik yaox pex tix khrawm ka nghox koi pa khraox heue.”
Been deeh taoh ka haktiex
23 “Pex ang pon tix coh dut ka haktiex,
mawh kheu haktiex mawh cie aux;
pex awm ngie pui tang ceu tang cad pa vai tix yuh ka haktiex khaing heue.
24 “Yam coh liag pex ka haktiex,
pex tag caw tix doh keud ka cao haktiex,
nawh koe akhuang tix deeh taoh kah.
25 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux kheu hot sibhawm nawh,
mai coh ka haktiex cie tix,
paox nyiex paox ma nawh tag caw tix deeh taoh hoek.
26 Pui pa ang koe paox nyiex paox ma tix deeh taoh ka haktiex an,
phan nawh lih mawh pui mi tix ngaix sax,
nawh tag koe mau tix chix deeh taoh ka haktiex.
27 Ka daux grawng kaing in,
nawh tag caw tix simin tix yaox ka num pa hoik yuh tix kah,
dee plud mau ngawh haktiex ka pui pa hoik liag son pa kawn deuk sivoe sang naog ka daux Num Gaux-rhawm,
yuh nin,
nawh tom tag yaox tix deeh taoh ka haktiex pa sang deeh plud nawh tix yuh khankix.
28 Phan nawh ang koe mau tix kub deeh taoh ka haktiex an,
haktiex an tag sang mawh cie pui cao pa hoik liag,
tom hoik hoek ka Num Gaux-rhawm phaox khaix.
Yam hoik hoek ka daux num an,
haktiex an tag sang deeh ka cao nyiex sivoe an heue.
29 “Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa coh nyiex tix,
pa koe ka daux veng khankix,
ka daux tix num nawh tag koe akhuang tix deeh taoh ka nyiex an hoek.
30 Phan nawh ang lai pon taoh ka daux tix num,
nawh tag ang lai pon koe ka akhuang an,
nyiex an tag mawh sin cie pui cao liag khawm doeh ceu cad nawh khankix leeing ju;
ka daux Num Gaux-rhawm kawx,
ang lai yaox tix deeh ka cao nyiex sivoe an khankix.
31 Viang mawh nan,
phan mawh nyiex pa koe ka plak praix veng,
tag sang hawt been awm yuh ka kaung ma khankix;
cao nyiex sivoe an tag koe akhuang tix deeh taoh kah,
yam hoik hoek ka daux Num Gaux-rhawm,
nyiex an tag sang deeh ka cao nyiex sivoe an.
32 Viang mawh nan,
ceu pui Li-we khankix tag chix tix deeh taoh ka nyiex pa ot ka daux veng Li-we ku jheeng jheeng.
33 Phan ceu pui Li-we coh ka nyiex pa ot ka daux veng Li-we,
mai ang baox yaox tix deeh taoh kah,
hoek ka daux Num Gaux-rhawm,
nyiex an tag sang deeh ka nawh,
mawh kheu nyiex ceu pui Li-we pa koe ka daux veng Li-we khankix,
tag mawh sin ngao cie kix ka daux si nax ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix leeing ju leeing phan.
34 Viang mawh nan,
haktiex dee liang ka khawng brie,
vung viang veng Li-we khankix,
tag ang pon tix coh;
an hoik mawh sin ngao cie kix leeing ju heue.”
Been tawx pui hot pui shiah yaog ka mau
35 b“Phan koe paox ceu paox cad maix tix kaux kaux,
pa hot pa shiah tom ang lai pon eei tix,
maix tag caw tix tawx nawh sawm,
awm tawx maix ka pui tang kaux mai pui tang ceu pa ot si nax pex yuh nan,
son sang yaox nawh tix ot tau mai maix tan heue.
36 Maix ang pon tix teei rao ka nawh;
caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
mai kieh nawh mawh paox plawg paox nyhawm ka maix.
37 cMaix tawx nawh yaog ka mau,
ang pon tix teei ka rao,
maix coh nawh ih priex preem,
ang pon tix kieh lih rao.
38 In mawh gum lox pa hoik tawm aux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Aux hoik viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
mawh son sang tawx haktiex Cia-nang ka pex,
mai mawh Siyiex pex heue.”
Been deeh taoh ka siliex cawng
39 d“Phan koe paox ceu paox cad maix tix kaux kaux,
hot shiah mai coh to kaux tix mawh siliex cawng ka maix,
maix ang pon tix kieh nawh awm siliex.
40 Caw tix kieh nawh ot tau mai maix awm pui cang,
yuh kaing jhaw maix tom hoek bawg yam Num Gaux-rhawm gaux rhi.
41 Yam hoek ka daux Num Gaux-rhawm,
nawh mai kawn simex kawn bun nawh khankix tag sang lih hu khaing maix,
mai deeh ing ka dee ot ceu pui nyiex tix,
pa mawh haktiex tax du nawh khankix.
42 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix mawh siliex aux.
Aux hoik viex kix lih khaing Ai-cix;
tag ang lai pon tix baox coh kix mawh siliex cawng.
43 Maix ang pon tix yuh kaing pa khin ka kix,
caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex maix.
44 Phan maix sang lo ka pa mawh siliex,
chix tix hu liag ka meung pui pa ot hawt vung viang maix.
45 Phan ang mawh nan,
maix kawx chix tix liang ka kawn simex kawn bun pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax pex khankix,
pui nyiex pui ma kix pa keh ka daux haktiex pex khankix.
Pui in kix chix tix mawh sin ngao cie pex.
46 Pex tag chix tix deuh kix son kawn praix tix,
kieh kix yuh kaing jhaw son kawn praix pex tom si dauh ju tix.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang pon tix yuh kaing pa khin ka paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
47 “Phan pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax pex khankix pon tix mag mi,
mai paox ceu paox cad pex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hot shiah,
mai coh to kaux tix ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai ka pui yiex pui ma tix,
48 khaix hoik coh nawh to kaux tix,
nawh ca kawn koe akhuang tix pon deeh taoh ka to kaux tix.
Pux aik nawh khankix,
49 keeing iak nawh khankix,
pux aik nawh tang kaux khankix,
mai can pui nyiex pa dex ka nawh khankix,
tag pon tix deeh taoh ka nawh.
Phan nawh deeh koe mau,
nawh kawx tag chix tix deeh taoh ka to kaux tix.
50 Nawh tag caw tix ah jum tau mai pui cao pa liag ka nawh.
Kix tag sang jauh tix simin tom yam jauh tix coh nawh mawh siliex,
tom hoek ka Num Gaux-rhawm,
tag sang sawn ngawh riang nawh awm tawx bix pui cang yuh nan,
51-52 viang mawh nan,
nawh tag caw tix hawt ka jum pa kawn deuk khankix,
mai plud ka ngawh pa deeh taoh tix.
53 Cao pa liag tag caw tix kieh nawh awm siao tix ka pui cang khankix yuh nan,
ang pon tix yuh kaing pa khin ka nawh.
54 Phan nawh kawn ang nyang baox pon krax pa luat luan ka grawng daux krax in,
yam hoik hoek ka daux Num Gaux-rhawm,
nawh mai kawn simex kawn bun nawh tag caw tix ploi kah,
mai kieh pon tix luat luan.
55 Mawh kheu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix mawh siliex aux;
aux hoik viex kix lih khaing Ai-cix.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix heue.”
