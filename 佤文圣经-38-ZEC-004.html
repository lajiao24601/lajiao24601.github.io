Riang su sai jawk kawk ngu
4
Kawn cau Siyiex pa si kah ka aux sivoe an baox deeh hoek ka aux dee in,
awm so aux haing yam laig it tix.
2 Nawh chawk aux ah nin:
“Yaox maix ceu pa tix?”
Aux pawk ah nin:
“Jawk kawk ngu khri pa mhawm tix mu,
ka piang koe klong pa sau bee ka tix mu.
Ka piang jawk kawk ngu koe kawk ngu ka aliah mu,
kawk in kix ku mu hoik koe ngu aliah mu.
3 aKa praok klong khawm ra plak koe khaox sime ka ra gawng,
tix plak tix gawng.”
4 Aux chawk kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Sara,
ceu in kix mawh tang son ceu pa tix lie?”
5 Nawh ah nin:
“Maix ang tawng lie?”
Aux pawk ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng heue.”
10b cKawn cau Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Kawk ngu an aliah mu mawh son tawm ngai Simiang Siyiex aliah mu,
pa simi tix jhak ka haktiex khawm uik b heue.”
11 dAux baox chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Khaox sime an ra gawng pa koe ka praok jawk kawk ngu khri khawm ra plak mawh dee bleeh ceu pa tix lie?
12 Kak khaox sime an ra gawng pa koe ka praok gawng maox khri an ra mu ka dee hieh ka bee sime,
mawh dee bleeh ceu pa tix lie?”
13 Nawh chawk aux ah nin:
“Ang maix tawng lie?”
Aux ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng heue.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin:
“In mawh pa hoik laug lih yuh kaing krub simiang ka piang haktiex ra kaux heue.”
Gum lox pa hoik krai Siyiex tix deuh ka Saw-law-pa-paw
6 eKawn cau Siyiex kieh aux krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka Saw-law-pa-paw.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Ang een rhawm tix ka kraung naing,
ang een rhawm tix ka krax pa lhak lhiao,
kheu nan een rhawm tix ka khuan aux tom taug chix pon krax pa pex.
7 Viang krax pa tieb tie hoik lhaong awm gawng yuh nan kawx,
tag sang chix grai ka plak sivoe ngai maix.
Maix tag sang deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan;
yam nghoik maix tix teei simaox tix lon deuh ot ka piang,
kawn pui tag sang kok yo ah nin:
‘Kied sidaing mhawm grang,
kied sidaing mhawm grang!’ ”
8 Simiang Siyiex baox tawx gum lox tang ceu ka aux tix ceu,
9 nawh ah nin:
“Saw-law-pa-paw hoik sum ka riah nyiex buan,
mai nawh tag sang pon yuh lih mawh tete.
Yam lih kaing in tix mawh,
pui cie aux tag sang tawng aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing ploi maix hu ka kix dee an.
10a Kix laux rhawm,
mawh kheu ang kaing in pon yuh tix phai phai,
viang mawh nan yam yaox kix viex Saw-law-pa-paw ka daix,
mai nied tix sum ka nyiex buan,
kix tag gaux rhawm heue.”
