6
Aux hoik yaox ka grawng koe ceu pa laux rhawm tix ceu ka piang haktiex,
pa gut ka to kaux kawn pui.
2 Siyiex hoik tawx pui tix kaux kaux koe min plak ngai,
pon krax mag mi,
koe sin ngao,
mai ceu pa sang lo nawh khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
ang kieh nawh yaox tix ih,
mai tieh tix kieh pui tang kaux pa ang nawh tawng yaox tix ih.
In hoik mawh pa giex pa going,
mai pa kied laux rhawm nang.
3 Pui tix kaux kaux viang nawh hoik koe kawn praix kaing yieh kaux,
mai yaox tix lang ju ka piang haktiex,
phan nawh ang bax yaox tix gaux rhawm ka daux buan son pa khaw nawh tix pon,
yam yum nawh mai ang yaox tix si paung mhawm mhawm,
kheu nan,
viang nawh hoik lang ju kawx,
mai koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux keud tix yaox,
kawn ngie pa yum daux viag miex tix kawn lheu mhawm khaing nawh heue.
4 Kawn ngie pa yum daux vaig miex tix ang mawh ceu pa mhawm;
nawh hoik grai ka daux pa faik,
mai pui ang lai tawng kah.
5 Nawh ang sax yaox ka pa riang,
mai ang bax tawng tid tix;
viang mawh nan,
nawh kawn yaox tix lau lee heue.
6 In kawn lheu mhawm khaing pui pa im ju ra rheng num,
pa ang bax yaox tix ih nyaux ka daux krax pa im ju kawn pui.
Dee si dauh,
kiex khawm ra kaux khex ang sang hoek ka haktiex tix mu khaing lie?
7 Kawn pui taug mao son vaig tu tix,
viang mawh nan,
kawn ang chix sak chix naog leeing ju heue.
8 Pui pa lhak lhiao lheu mhawm khaing pui pa daox aw miex ding lie?
Nhie pui hot pui sibhawm tawng tix yuh paox yuh pie tau mai pui khankix,
mai hoik koe dee mhawm ceu pa tix lie?
9 In hoik mawh pa giex pa going,
mai pa ang koe ceu.
Pa chix naog rhawm ka can pa koe tix,
mai kawn lheu mhawm khaing rhawm ghoing heue.
10 Kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh khankix hoik dawg mawh pa tat tix deuh;
ex hoik tawnga,
kawn pui ang pon ghrang tix tawng khaing pui pa ting riang khaing tix.
11 Maix nied ghrang tix tawng,
mai nied tix ang koe ceu,
ka son to kaux maix kawx ang koe dee mhawm.
12 Ka daux krax im ju kawn pui pa kied dawt,
pa giex pa going,
awm riang pang peue pui yuh nan,
pui mawx pon tawng mawh ceu pa tix pa blawn koe ngawh koe jum ka nawh lie?
Pui mawx pon krai ka nawh,
khaix hoik yum nawh mawh ceu pa tix pa sang lih tix mawh ka piang haktiex lie?
