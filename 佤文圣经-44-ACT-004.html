Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang kham krax prawng pui ka bang kum pui Yo-thaix
4
Yam kawn krai Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang lox ka pui hun,
koe cao giex kaing lia tix blah,
pui pau nyiex buan,
mai pui Sa-tu-kai lih hoek dum mawx dum an.
2 Mawh kheu kawn cau in kiex sidiex grawng pa kaoh im,
krai pao Ye-sux hoik kaoh im khaing krax yum,
pui in kix kied song rhawm ka tete,
3 mawh kheu nan giex ka kiex,
mai laung kiex daux thawng tom hoek singaix khaix,
mawh kheu hoik faik praix ka daux singaix an.
4 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa mhong ka gum lox in hoik yum rhawm ka tom nie,
simex khaing koe dex phuan rheng kaux heue.
5 Ka daux singaix khaix,
simiang pui Yo-thaix,
kuad song,
mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khrawm tix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
6 Ya-na cao giex kaing li pa ting mai Kai-ya-fa,
Yaw-hang,
Ya-li-sang-tax,
khawm pui nyiex ma cao giex kaing li pa ting hoik ngawm tix ot dee an khawm uik.
7 Kix nhie kawn cau cung ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
mai chawk ah nin:
“Mawh een pex rhawm tix ka riang rao ceu pa tix,
yuh pex kaing in ka daux ceu kaux pui mawx lie?”
8 Pi-teux hoik naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
nawh pawk ah nin:
“Simiang ka daux pui haktiex mai kuad song khankix,
9 phan pex chawk yiex grawh grawng pui chawh in,
hoik moi yuh ka mawx ka daux kaing ceu pa mhawm in.
10 Phan mawh nan,
pex pui hun mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
khaw hoik tawng mawh nin:
pui in cung ka plak sivoe ngai pex,
hoik pon krax pa moi khawm uik.
In hoik mawh riang rao lih hoek khaing ceu kaux Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux.
Pex hoik tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak,
mai Siyiex hoik kieh nawh dee kaoh im khaing krax yum.
11 bPhan mawh Ye-sux kaw in,
phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ah nin:
simaox pa hoik tik cang mai yuh nyiex pex
hoik lih mawh simaox jaong nyiex pa blawn ting ceu.
12 Krax taoh hoek ngie khaing nawh tix kaux khaing;
mawh kheu greem rao ma si nax kawn pui,
Siyiex ang lai tawx ceu kaux pui tix kaux kawx,
pa sang kieh ex pon krax taoh heue.”
13 Pui ka daux bang kum khankix yaox grawng pa kied kua rhawm Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang yuh nan,
mai baox tawng grawng mawh kix pui pa ang sax gau ka ngai lai,
mai kied daox deng ka tete,
kix tom tawng grawng mawh pui in kiex pa hoik hawt ka Ye-sux.
14 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang lai koe lox pa sang krai,
mawh kheu hoik yaox kix pui chawh an cung tau mai Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang.
15 Pui ka daux bang kum tom grheu ngie kiex lih khaing bang kum pui Yo-thaix,
khaix an kum paox tix,
16 ah nin:
“Sang yuh ex kiex yuh ka mawx lie?
Pui pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik tawng grawng pa hoik yuh kiex mhai pa daox pa am in,
ex kawx ang lai tawng ka sang thiang kah.
17 Viang mawh nan,
son ang ex sang lai kieh kaing in nied ot nied lao tix ting hu,
ex hoek tix ne raw ka kiex,
paw lai baox cu kiex krai lao ceu pa tid tix ka pui hun ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux heue.”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
kix kok kiex hoek,
mai ne raw kiex yuh ka mawx ang lai cu kiex krai lox,
phan ang mawh nan,
sidiex gau ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux.
19 Viang mawh nan,
Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang pawk ka kix ah nin:
“Ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
mawh khaw tix nghiet lox pex,
phan ang mawh nan,
mawh khaw tix nghiet lox Siyiex lie?
Pex keud tix yaox heue!
20 Ceu pa hoik yaox yiex mai pa hoik mhong yiex,
ang krai ang chix.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
pui ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix baox sau riang tix ne raw ka kiex,
khaix an ploi kiex,
mawh kheu ang kix yaox grawng sang ciyang ka kiex.
Pui hun hoik yaog yaw Siyiex son kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh khawm uik.
22 Pui pa moi an ka daux mhai pa daox pa am in,
hoik koe tix pon num haoh heue.
Kawn Khrit ih rhawm paox tix rawg buan tau
23 Yam hoik ploi kix ka Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang,
kiex deeh ing ka dee ot pui tix dee an dum mawx dum an,
mai krai lox pa hoik krai simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song khankix ka pui hun.
24 cYam hoik mhong pui hun kah,
kix tom ih rhawm paox tix rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
“Cao pa phan yuh ka rao ma,
haktiex,
rawm sigrit sigra,
mai can pa koe ka daux khawm uik!
25 dMaix hoik tawx Khuan Singaix Singeh,
kieh jheeh siliex maix pa mawh tax du ex Ta-wue krai ah nin:
pui tang ceu tang cad yuh ka mawx lai grao lie?
Kawn pui piang haktiex yuh ka mawx lai sawg ka kaing ceu pa giex going lie?
26 Simiang piang haktiex rien tix sang hu yuh naing;
cao giex kaing kawx hoik khrawm tix ot tau,
son sang puing ka Simiang Siyiex mai Khrit pa groi rawm bee cie nawh heue.
27 eMawh tete,
Si-luix mai Phang-shio Pi-la-to si doe paox tix tau mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot daux veng,
mai pui Yi-seu-liex,
son sang puing ka Ye-sux siliex pa singax maix,
pa mawh Khrit pa hoik yaog maix.
28 Kix si doe paox tix tau,
son sang yuh ka siawp sibrawm maix,
mai ka alo kaing pa hoik dawg tat tix sang yuh lix khawm uik.
29 Simiang Siyiex eue,
krax pa ghlat kix,
yam in khiox khaw maix lam lia tix yaox,
khaix an kieh yix pa mawh siliex maix,
pon kua rhawm tix sidiex ka gum lox maix.
30 Khiox khaw maix nyeu taix tix pai ka jhawm saux,
mai baox kieh yix yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux siliex pa singax maix heue.”
31 Khaix hoik rawg kix buan,
dee khrawm kix tix ot yung yang.
Kix hoik naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh khawm uik,
mai jauh tix koe rhawm pa kua tix sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex heue.
Rom tix siao sin ngao tau
32 fCub kawn Khrit in kix hoik ih rhawm paox tix grum rhawm tau,
mai ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa krai mawh sin ngao cie tix;
can kraung khrai pa koe khawm uik kix hoik rom tix siao tau.
33 Kawn cau khankix hoik ting siawp sibrawm tix kham sakse grawng pa kaoh im Ye-sux;
Siyiex hoik ploi buan son pa ting lih hoek ka piang kix ku kaux.
34 Ka si nax kix ang lai koe pui pa cud cha ka ceu pa tid tix.
Pui pa koe kaung ma nyiex ma an kix,
hoik coh khawm uik,
35 teei mau pa hoik coh haktiex tawx ka kawn cau,
mai hawt tix guah awm pa sang lo kaux pui mawx kaux pui an heue.
36 Koe pui Li-we tix kaux pa keh ka haktiex Sai-phu-lu-see,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-seux,
kawn cau khankix kok nawh mawh Pa-na-pa (dee bleeh Pa-na-pa mawh cao pa joh jhan);
37 Nawh hoik coh ka kaung ma tix tix dauh,
mai teei mau in tawx ka kawn cau heue.
