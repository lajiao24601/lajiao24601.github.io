Phuk lai cie kawn yung
5
aAux yaox kaw an pa ngawm ka piang dang ngeung,
taix khawn dawm nawh giex ka phuk lai;
phuk lai in khawm ra plak hoik tiiem naog ka ngai lai,
mai teei cum aliah mu tawk nyeep kah.
2 Aux baox yaox kawn cau Siyiex pa ting riang tix kaux,
kok yo krai pao ah nin:
“Pui mawx sang keung tix paoh ka cum in kix,
plak ka phuk lai in lie?”
3 Viang mawh nan,
piang rao ma ang ah,
piang haktiex ang ah,
greem haktiex ang ah,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon plak pon phat ka phuk lai in.
4 Aux ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting,
mawh kheu ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa keung tix pon plak pon phat ka phuk lai in.
5 bMawh kheu nan,
ka daux kuad song tix kaux krai ka aux ah nin:
“Paw vi yiiem!
Jhak heue,
sivai sang si pa lih hoek khaing daux ceu pui Yo-tax,
pa mawh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue hoik pex;
nawh hoik pon paoh ka cum aliah mu,
plak ka phuk lai an heue.”
6 cKhaix an,
aux yaox ka dang ngeung pa lai lawm to sat pa im ju an pon mu mai kuad song khankix,
koe kawn yung cung dee an.
Kawn yung awm pa hoik pauh tix yuh nan,
koe rung aliah mu,
tawm ngai aliah mu,
pa mawh khuan Siyiex aliah mu,
ploi kix hu ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
7 Kawn yung an hoek ka plak sivoe ngai,
rieb ka phuk lai khaing daux tawm taix khawn dawm kaw pa ngawm piang dang ngeung an.
8 dYam yuh nawh nan,
to sat pa im ju pon mu an kix mai kuad song tix nga pon kaux,
tom gup tix krub ka plak sivoe ngai kawn yung.
Kuad song ku kaux hoik viex ka ting jung lah,
mai mawk ngu khri pa sau nyawm bhawm naog kah;
nyawng bhawm in hoik mawh krax pa rawg buan kawn Khrit khankix.
9 eKix rhax lox lai rhax pa khraox tix ceu ah nin:
Mawh ngie maix pa keung tix rieb ka phuk lai in,
paoh ka cum pa tawk piang;
mawh kheu pui hoik sax pauh ka maix!
Mawh kheu pa jauh nham yum maix,
maix hoik taoh pui deeh hoek khaing pui ku cub,
khaing lox si kah ku ceu,
khaing pui ku ceu,
khaing meung ku meung
mai kieh mawh cie Siyiex heue.
10 fMaix kieh kix lih mawh meung cao giex kaing li,
pa hoek tix yuh kaing krub ka Siyiex ex;
kix tag sang giex kaing ka piang haktiex heue.
11 gAux baox jhak kah;
aux mhong lox kawn cau Siyiex kaing rheng kaing mheen.
Kix sivie ka dang ngeung,
to sat pa im ju pon mu,
kuad song khankix khawm pon daog,
12 rhax lai yaog yaw ah nin:
kawn yung pa hoik pauh an,
keung tix pon rieb ka siawp sibrawm,
ka ceu pa grum naog,
ka ping nyia cuyi,
ka pa ting riang ting rao,
ka pa ting min ceu kaux,
ka min plak ngai,
mai krax pa yaog yaw heue!
13 Aux baox mhong ka to sat pa koe ka piang rao ma,
ka piang haktiex,
mai ka greem haktiex,
pa koe ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
can ceu pa koe ka daux rao ma.
Hoik rhax lai khawm uik ah nin:
kieh krax pa yaog yaw,
pa ting min ceu kaux,
min plak ngai,
mai akhuang,
mawh cie kaw pa ngawm ka piang dang ngeung an,
mai cie kawn yung,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue!
14 To sat pa im ju pon mu an kix tom pawk ah nin:
“A-meung!”
Kuad song hoik gup tix krub khawm uik heue.
