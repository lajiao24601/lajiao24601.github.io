Dee si dauh ju pui mhawm mai pui caox
37
Paw vi kieh tix suk sag kheu pa yuh pui pa caox pa caig;
paw vi ghoing ka pui pa ang rauh ang see.
2 Kix tag sang kroh vhit awm rib klai yuh nan;
kix tag sang khrum awm tai khaox yuh nan heue.
 
3 Tag caw tix yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai sau riang tix yuh kaing pa mhawm;
ot ka piang haktiex in,
mai pon yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe.
4 Tag caw tix sawg krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi ka Simiang Siyiex;
nawh tag sang yuh lih mawh awm pa simie maix tix yuh heue.
 
5 Tag caw tix tawx to kaux tix ka Simiang Siyiex;
yum rhawm ka nawh,
mai nawh tag chix teem coi ka maix.
6 Nawh tag sang kieh pa rauh see maix jhan awm pa riang ngu;
nawh tag sang kieh pa rauh see maix awm greung ngaix heue.
 
7 Tag caw tix viex rhawm gun rhawm yeung krox jun ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex;
paw vi kieh tix suk sag kheu pa haok huan pui,
paw vi kieh rhawm rhi tix ang nyawm ot kheu pa lih krax keud krax sawn pa caox tix mawh heue.
 
8 Paw vi saux rhawm saux rhi,
mai paw vi song rhawm song rhi;
paw vi kieh rhawm rhi tix suk sag,
kheu in mawh pa ang mhawm ka maix.
9 Pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex tag sang yaox tix paung ot ka haktiex in;
Viang mawh nan,
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig tag sang grheu tix hu heue.
 
10 Mawh ngie tix khiep ngai khaing,
pui pa caox pa caig tag sang laux leeing;
maix sawg ka kix,
maix kawx ang yaox.
11 aViang mawh nan,
pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
tag sang yaox tix ot ka haktiex in;
kix tag sang pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ang jud ang jai heue.
 
12 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig keud sawn tix sang yuh pui rauh see laux,
mai greeik rhang braing tix ka nawh,
koeng khoi ka nawh.
13 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex nyiah ka pui pa caox pa caig,
mawh kheu hoik tawng nawh dex sang laux leeing kix heue.
 
14 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig,
lawd vaig lih khaing phak,
mai vaik ak sau te,
puing ka pui hot pui sibhawm pa ang koe cao teem cao coi,
pauh ka pui pa yuh pa rauh pa see.
15 Viang mawh nan,
vaig viex kix deeh klauh ka rhawm kix phlat;
ak viex kix tag sang raig pot heue.
 
16 Viang pa koe pui rauh pui see iak kawx,
kawn lheu khaing sin ngao cie pui pa caox pa caig khawm uik;
17 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tag sang raig pot ka plawng taix pui pa caox pa caig;
nawh tag sang kia ngien ka pui rauh pui see heue.
 
18 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh khankix;
haktiex in tag sang mawh cie kix leeing ju leeing phan.
19 Yam ot kix ka daux krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi,
mai kix ang yaox tix taug mao,
yam sibhawm praix,
pon yaox tix sawm sak.
20 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig tag sang laux leeing;
pui pa tieb tie ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang khrum awm tai daux tiag daux tu yuh nan;
kix tag sang grai awm taox ngu yuh nan heue.
 
21 Pui caox pui caig yaog yaing mai ang plud kah,
pui rauh pui see si pieh rhawm tix tawx.
22 Pui pa tawx Simiang Siyiex buan tag sang yaox tix ot ka haktiex in;
pui pa jhax pa lawg nawh kah,
tag sang grheu kix lih hu heue.
 
23 Simiang Siyiex duk thung pui hu ka krax pa khaw tix lawng,
mai kia ngien ka diang caong pui pa nghiet ka nawh.
24 Yam sideeing nawh caong tix,
mai nawh kawx deeh pon yaog tix kaoh,
mawh kheu sang yaog Simiang Siyiex nawh kaoh heue.
 
25 Aux hoik kuad,
mai ngai aux hoik yaox ka tom nie,
viang mawh nan kawx,
ang nyang sax yaox ka pa hoik tik vud Simiang Siyiex pui rauh pui see tix bawg kawx,
ang nyang sax yaox ka pa sawg kawn praix nawh tix caung.
26 Nawh hoik ca mawh pui pa si pieh rhawm tix tawx,
mai gaux rhawm tix tawx pui yaog yaing;
kawn praix nawh kawx hoik pon ka buan son heue.
 
27 Tag caw tix tik pa caox hawt pa mhawm,
kawn praix nawh tom tag yaox tix kaux cong leeing ju.
28 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex muh ka pa khaw caw,
ang tik ang vud ka pui singax singeh cie tix.
Nawh kia ngien kix leeing ju leeing phan;
viang mawh nan,
kawn praix pui pa caox pa caig,
tag sang grheu kix hu.
29 Pui rauh pui see tag sang pon tix cie ka haktiex,
tag sang yaox tix ot ka haktiex in tix ju hoek tix ju heue.
 
30 Jheeh pui pa rauh see krai ka lox pa koe ping nyia cuyi;
dak nawh krai lao ka kaing pa khaw caw.
31 Ka daux rhawm rhi nawh koe ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex;
caong nawh ang yaox tix cang klad heue.
 
32 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig jiang ka pui rauh pui see,
sawg ka bawg yam sang yuh nawh laux;
33 viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang kieh nawh cawd ka daux tawm taix pui koeng,
ang kieh nawh hoek krax prawng ca singaix yam prawng ca heue.
 
34 Tag een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tawx nawh,
nawh tom tawx riang rao ka maix,
mai kieh maix pon tix cie ka haktiex an tom kaux tom cong.
Maix tag sang yaox ka pui pa caox pa caig,
mai hoik grheu kix hu;
 
35 aux hoik sax yaox ka pui pa caox pa caig,
pa meung tix nieb pon yuh ka pui,
awm khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noengb pa lhaong khaing can khaox khawm uik.
36 Viang mawh nan,
yam baox laig preeng aux ka dee an,
mai nawh hoik grai;
auxc sawg ka nawh,
mai ang yaox heue.
 
37 Bre tix lia ka pui mhawm,
mai jhak tix yaox ka pui rauh see,
pui muh ka pa nhim pa tiiem,
kawn praix nawh yaox tix haoh hun;
38 pui phi map tag sang laux lak pak leeing,
kawn praix kix kawx tag sang teei tix tik.
39 Simiang Siyiex taoh ka pui rauh see,
yam ot kix ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
tag sang kia ngien ka kix.
40 Simiang Siyiex teem coi ka kix,
taoh ka kix;
nawh taoh kix luat luan khaing pui pa caox pa caig,
mawh kheu hoik sawg kix krax kia ngien nawh heue.
