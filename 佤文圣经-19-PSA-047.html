Simiang pa ting pa lhaong
47
Kawn pui ka piang haktiex eue,
tag caw tix dieh taix tix khawm uik heue!
Tag caw tix rhax lai tom ting,
mai rhax laix yaog yaw ka Siyiex heue!
2 Tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex pa ting ka piang khawm uik;
nawh mawh simiang ting,
giex kaing ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
3 Nawh kieh ex pon kuang ka kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik;
nawh kieh ex pon giex kaing ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik.
4 Nawh hoik laug ka haktiex dee ot son ex,
mai hoik kieh mawh sin ngao pa koe min plak ngai cie pui pa muh tix heue.
 
5 Siyiex ngawm ka piang dang ngeung,
ka daux lox pa yaog yaw pui;
Simiang Siyiex haok ngawm ka piang dee ngawm tix ka daux lox luah teet tawng.
6 Hoek heue,
ex hoek rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex heue!
Hoek heue,
hoek ex rhax lai yaog yaw ka simiang heue!
7 Siyiex mawh simiang ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
ex tag caw tix teei lox rhax yaog yaw ka nawh heue!
 
8 Siyiex ngawm ka piang dang ngeung ka dee pa kied singax singeh;
nawh giex kaing ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik.
9 Simiang meung ka piang haktiex khrawm paox tix,
mai ot tau mai pui cie Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex cie Ya-paw-la-hanga.
Can dieb tie naok pui naing hoik mawh cie Siyiex khawm uik;
mawh ngie nawh tix kaux khaing pa ting ka piang khawm uik heue.
