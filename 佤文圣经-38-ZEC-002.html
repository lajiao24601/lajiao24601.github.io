Riang su sai maox diag
2
Ka daux riang su sai tang mu an,
aux yaox pui tix kaux,
daux taix nawh giex ka maox diag tix nyaing.
2 Aux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Hu maix dee mawx?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux hu tiag ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
sang jhak tix yaox viex va koe miex glang heue.”
3 Khaix an aux yaox kawn cau Siyiex pa si kah ka aux sivoe an hu plak sivoe,
kawn cau Siyiex tang kaux an tix kaux rieb ka nawh.
4 Kawn cau Siyiex pa hoek sivoe an krai ka kawn cau Siyiex hoek khaix an ah nin:
“Nied to hu krai ka pui nhum ju pa viex maox diag an ah nin:
‘Veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang lih koe pui mai khawng brie tom nie;
tag sang lih mawh veng pa ang koe guh.
5 Kheu nan Simiang Siyiex hoik krai tix deuh lox,
nawh tag sang lih mawh guh veng ngu,
pa lai lawm ka veng,
pa kia ngien ka veng,
pa lih tix mawh min plak ngai heue.’ ”
Kok pui pa vuk vied vuk viang deeh hoek ka haktiex tix
6-7 Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui cie tix ah nin:
“Aux hoik sax prung prang pex hu ka haktiex khawm pon praok si daix daog.
Viang mawh nan pex pa mawh pui pa vuk vied vuk viang in kix eue,
tag caw tix to lih hu khaing Pa-pi-loeng,
tag caw tix deeh hoek ka haktiex tix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
8 Pui mawx lauh ka pex,
mai hoik awm lauh ka kawn pa muh aux heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm,
pa mawh simiang kru naing,
ploi aux hu krai ka gum lox in plak greem ka meung pa hoik tud rhim ka pui cie nawh ah nin:
9 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang hoek tix lauh ka pex;
pui pa yuh kaing son pex sivoe an tag sang tud rhim ka pex heue.”
Yam lih kaing in tix mawh,
pui ku kaux tom taug tawng Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik ploi aux hu heue.
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm rhax lai heue!
Mawh kheu tag sang lih hoek aux,
tag sang paung ot ka daux pex heue.”
11 Yam an,
meung tom nie tag sang hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai lih mawh pui cie nawh.
Nawh tag sang paung ot ka daux pex,
pex tom taug tawng mawh nawh pa ploi aux hoek ka pex dee an.
12 Pui Yo-tax tag sang baox lih mawh sin ngao pa blawk ka haktiex pa singax cie Simiang Siyiex;
Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang lih mawh veng pa kied muh nawh heue.
13 Kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik eue,
tag caw tix yien yawg ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik lih hoek Simiang Siyiex khaing dee paung ot tix pa singax singeh heue.
