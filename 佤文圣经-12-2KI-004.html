Yi-li-sa teem coi ka miex mai hot
4
Koe miex mai tix kaux,
mex nawh mawh ka daux cub cao jhai tix kaux;
nawh hu ka Yi-li-sa,
mai ah nin:
“Sara,
mex aux hoik yum.
Maix hoik tawng nawh mawh pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex tix kaux.
Yam in koe pui pa ceuk nawh ka tix kaux hoek,
sang teei kawn simex aux ra kaux hu mawh siliex cawng tix,
son sang plud ka ceuk mex aux heue.”
2 Yi-li-sa chawk ah nin:
“Aux sang yuh pa tix son maix lie?
Krai ka aux,
ka daux nyiex maix koe ceu pa tix lie?”
Miex mai an ah nin:
“Koe ngie bee sime tix kawng iak khaing,
lheu khaing an ang lai koe tang ceu heue.”
3 Yi-li-sa krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix hu vai aw khankix pa ang tid tix koe daux ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
nied nie nied mhawm.
4 Khaix an,
maix mai kawn simex maix khawm ra kaux laig daux nyiex,
nyeep ka siviex,
mai hieh bee ot daux aw an kix,
khaix hoik sau naog tom deuh tang ot tix plak heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
miex mai an mai kawn simex nawh ra kaux laig daux nyiex,
nyeep ka siviex,
mai hieh bee pa ot daux kawng iak an ka daux aw pa hoik vai kawn simex nawh khankix.
6 Khaix hoik sau kix bee naog ka daux aw khawm uik,
miex mai an chawk aw kawn koe ang koe,
kawn simex tix kaux pawk ah nin:
“Ang lai koe.”
Mawh kheu nan,
bee sime ang lai jauh lih.
7 Miex mai deeh ing ka Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an,
Yi-li-sa ah nin ka nawh:
“Coh bee sime in,
hu plud ceuk maix kah.
Mau pa deuk khankix hoik kub pax miex kawn eei tix ka heue.”
Yi-li-sa mai pui Su-niang bun miex pa koe mau
8 Koe tix ngaix,
Yi-li-sa hu ka Su-niang.
Dee an koe bun miex pa koe mau ka tix kaux,
nawh ghaok Yi-li-sa sawm.
Jauh yam an,
Yi-li-sa hu ka Su-niang miex bawg ang ah,
tom hoek tix sawm ka nyiex bun miex an.
9 Bun miex an ah nin ka mex tix:
“Aux hoik tawng pui pa rien hoek ka ex dee in,
nawh mawh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex pa singax singeh.
10 Ax sum nyiex iak tix mu ka piang choek nyiex,
ka daux nyiex deuh ku,
pheen,
khaox gaong,
mai jawk kawk ngu koe kah.
Yam hoek nawh ka daux nyiex ax,
kieh nawh paung ot dee an heue.”
11 Koe tix ngaix,
Yi-li-sa hoek ka Su-niang,
mai nawh haok laux lee tix ka nyiex cie tix.
12 Nawh nhie siliex tix Ci-ha-si kok ka bun miex an hoek.
Bun miex an hoek,
13 Yi-li-sa ah nin ka Ci-ha-si:
“Maix chawk ka nawh,
sang yuh aux pa tix son nawh.
Nawh hoik koe rhawm tix chix keud sawn ka priex pa sang lo ex ku si jheeing jheeng,
aux sang pawk buan son ka nawh.
Nawh tee rien tix simie aux hu ka simiang dee an,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka simiang naing dee an,
mai krai lox mhawm son nawh ra loe gum heue.”
Bun miex an ah nin:
“Aux paung ot ka daux pui cie tix,
mai ang cud cha ka ceu tid tix heue.”
14 Yi-li-sa chawk Ci-ha-si ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
aux sang yuh ceu pa tix son nawh lie?”
Ci-ha-si ah nin:
“Nawh ang koe kawn simex,
mex nawh hoik kuad ju heue.”
15 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Kok nawh hoek!”
Bun miex an hoek,
cung ka diang siviex.
16 aYi-li-sa ah nin ka nawh:
“Num khaix bawg yam in,
maix tag sang klien ka kawn simex tix kaux heue.”
Bun miex an ah nin:
“Sara,
paw vi pie lhien ka aux.
Maix mawh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex heue.”
17 Viang mawh nan,
awm lox pa hoik krai Yi-li-sa,
ka daux num khaix bawg yam in,
bun miex an rug keh kawn simex tix kaux heue.
18 Khaix hoik koe ra loe num,
daux ngaix yam khrawm ka nghox koi plak pon ngawb,
kawn nyawm in hu sawg ka keeing tix;
keeing nawh khrawm nghox daux kaung tau mai pui yuh kaing khankix.
19 Kawn nyawm an tix vud khaing kok yo ka keeing tix ah nin:
“Aux saux kaing heue!
Aux saux kaing heue!”
Keeing nawh ah nin ka siliex tix tix kaux:
“Klien hu tawx kawn nyawm in ka miex nawh dee an heue!”
20 Siliex tom viex ing tawx kawn nyawm an ka miex nawh dee an.
Miex kawn nyawm an klien kawn nyawm an ot piang ngawng tix,
tom hoek greung ngaix,
khaix an kawn nyawm an yum heue.
21 Miex kawn nyawm an klien kawn nyawm an hu ka nyiex ot Yi-li-sa,
deuh nawh ot piang ku,
nyeep siviex mai lih hu khaix heue.
22 Bun miex an kok mex tix hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix ploi siliex tix kaux hu tuk la hoek dee in tix mu;
aux sang hu ka Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an.
Aux sang nied deeh hoek heue.”
23 Mex nawh ah nin:
“Maix tag sang hu tete daux in singaix lie?
Singaix in ang tie mawh singaix tuah,
mai ang tie mawh singaix jauhb khix tix haok eue!”
Bun miex an ah nin:
“Ang yuh ka mawx tix.”
24 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh nhie pui rien khre ka la,
mai tawx lox ka siliex ah nin:
“Nied grheu la hu,
nied brax nied mhawm;
phan aux ang bax si kah,
maix paw vi nyang koi hu.”
25 Mawh kheu nan,
bun miex an yaog tix hu plak gawng Cia-mix heue.
Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex ot dee kied singai kok yaox hoek bun miex an,
nawh tom ah nin ka siliex tix Ci-ha-si:
“Maix jhak,
bun miex Su-niang hoek heue!
26 Nied hu rieb ka nawh,
mai chawk nawh mhawm ang mhawm ot lie?
Mex nawh mai kawn nawh mhawm ang mhawm ot lie?”
Bun miex krai mhawm ot tix ka Ci-ha-si.
27 Viang mawh nan,
yam haok nawh ka piang gawng,
hoek dex ka sivoe ngai Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
nawh tom kawm ka caong Yi-li-sa.
Yam taug sang nyheen Ci-ha-si bun miex yoi,
viang mawh nan,
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Ghram heue!
Nawh kied laux rhawm laux rhi.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang bax krai aux mhong kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh heue.”
28 Bun miex an ah nin ka nawh:
“Sara,
aux hoik khiox khaw kawn simex ka maix lie?
Ang aux hoik krai ka maix,
paw vi kieh aux koe krax gaux rhawm grawng in ang mawh lie?”
29 Yi-li-sa veeh to kaux tix ah nin ka Ci-ha-si:
“Nied rien khre tix mhawm!
Teei ka khaox gix aux!
Ka daux krax,
phan si pup ka pui,
paw vi si kah tau mai nawh.
Phan pui chawk ka maix,
paw vi pawk.
Yam hoik hoek maix,
deuh khaox gix aux ka piang ngai kawn nyawm an heue.”
30 Bun miex an ah nin ka Yi-li-sa:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju mai ka maix,
aux ang doh lih hu khaing maix!”
Mawh kheu nan,
kiex ra kaux hu krax tau.
31 Ci-ha-si ran ing sivoe,
mai deuh khaox gix Yi-li-sa ka piang ngai kawn nyawm an,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox chuh pie ceeix kawx.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh deeh ing ka Yi-li-sa,
mai ah nin:
“Kawn nyawm an ang bax haing heue.”
32 Yam hoik hoek Yi-li-sa,
nawh laig daux nyiex nong nong,
mai yaox kawn nyawm an hoik yum,
noi nawh it ka piang ku.
33 Nawh nyeep siviex,
mawh ngie nawh mai kawn nyawm pa ot daux nyiex khaing;
nawh rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex.
34 cKhaix an nawh haok ka piang ku,
mai gup tix ka piang to kaux kawn nyawm an,
jheeh see jheeh,
ngai see ngai,
taix see taix.
Mawh kheu gup nawh tix ka piang to kaux kawn nyawm an,
to kaux kawn nyawm jauh tix si u.
35 Yi-li-sa yaog tix kaoh cung,
mai lai ling hu deeh ka daux nyiex,
khaix an baox deeh haok piang ku,
gup tix ka piang to kaux kawn nyawm an.
Kawn nyawm tom miah aliah bawg,
ngai nawh tom blah.
36 Yi-li-sa kok Ci-ha-si ah nin:
“Hu kok bun miex pui Su-niang hoek heue!”
Bun miex hoek,
Yi-li-sa ah nin ka nawh:
“Teei klien ka kawn simex maix heue.”
37 Bun miex an grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai Yi-li-sa,
mai gup pak ngai tix piang tiex,
khaix an klien ka kawn simex tix hu heue.
Mhai pa daox pa am ra ceu
38 Koe tix bawg,
yam si pup piang meung khawm uik ka krax sibhawm praix,
Yi-li-sa deeh hoek ka Cie-cia.
Yam kawn sidiex gau nawh ka cub cao jhai khankix,
nawh nhie siliex tix deuh aw ting ot ka piang ngu,
mai ngaik kix nyaux ka rawm rheep.
39 Koe pui hu pon taux tiag ka daux kaung,
mai yaox ka nhu ke tiag tix nhu,
tom pieh ka plix ke tiag hoek tom nie.
Nawh sit phawng plix ke tiag,
mai sau daux aw,
mai ang tawng an mawh ke ceu pa tix.
40 Yam hoik sin rawm taux,
kawn pui pug tix rheep.
Viang mawh nan,
yam ceem kix tix yaox ka rawm taux an,
kix tang riag yo,
mai ah nin ka Yi-li-sa:
“In mawh pa koe jai eue!”
Pui hun ang lai pon rheep.
41 Yi-li-sa teei phawng mien nghox ca hoek ceeix sau ka daux aw,
mai ah nin:
“Pug lih tawx pui hun rheep heue!”
Rawm rheep ka daux aw tom ang lai koe jai heue.
42 Baox koe tix bawg,
koe pui tix kaux kaoh khaing Pa-lix Sa-li-sa,
mai viex hoek tawx khao mun tix nga lon ka Yi-li-sa,
khao mun an hoik mawh pa yuh ka nghox ca pa lang mu pa liang tix pon khaing daux num an.
Nawh kawx viex nang si gleem nghox ca pa taug khraox teei tix khankix.
Yi-li-sa tawx lox ka siliex tix,
nhie nawh guah tix tawx priex preem an kix ka cao jhai khankix ih.
43 Viang mawh nan,
siliex chawk ah nin:
“In kix kub pui tix yieh kaux ih lie?”
Yi-li-sa pawk ah nin:
“Tawng tawx kix ih khaing heue,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik krai,
kix tag sang ih sak,
khaix an mai kawn sang haoh heue.”
44 Mawh kheu nan,
siliex guah tix tawx kix ih priex preem.
Awm gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex yuh nan,
pui haoh pui hun hoik yaox tix ih tom sak,
khaix an mai kawn haoh heue.
