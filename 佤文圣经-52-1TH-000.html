Lai Thie-sa-law-ni-cia Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Thie-sa-law-ni-cia mawh nyiex dee ot Simiang ting ka haktiex Ma-chi-toeng greem pa giex Law-ma kaing. Khaix hoik lih hu Pao-law khaing Fe-li-pix, nawh sum ceu cao dee an. Viang mawh kawx ang koe miex glang tix, pui haktiex Yo-thaix boh rhawm kheu pa sidiex Pao-law ka grawng Khrit ka daux pa ang mawh pui Yo-thaix, mai tieh pon teei tix siao ka been li Yo-thaix mawh pa tieb tie ka Pao-law. Pui khin Pao-law lih hu khaing Thie-sa-law-ni-cia, mai hu ka Pi-li-yax. Khaix hoik hoek nawh ka Keu-ling-to, Thi-maw-thaix paox pa yuh nawh kaing tau hoek tix jhak ka nawh, mai krai nawh mhong ka grawh grawng ceu cao Thie-sa-law-ni-cia heue.
Pao-law tiiem lox lai in, kaing ting mawh son sang joh jhan ka kawn Khrit Thie-sa-law-ni-cia, mai kieh krax yum rhawm kix kaux cong. Nawh hoik yaog yaw buan son Siyiex ka pa mhong tix ka grawh grawng krax muh kix, mai krax yum rhawm kix. Nawh krai grawh grawng yam ot tix tau mai kix, khaix an pawk lox pa chawk kix tix blah ka grawh grawng pa sang deeh hoek Khrit yuh nin: pui pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux, phan hoik yum sivoe sang lih hoek Khrit, sang pon ang pon ka ju leeing ju pa viex Khrit ka daux singaix yam lih hoek nawh lie? Khrit sang lih hoek yam mawx lie? Pao-law teei bawg yam in sidiex gau ka kix, tag caw nied tix yuh kaing mhawm mhawm, mai krox jun pa sang lih hoek Khrit ka daux krax keud pot jun pot heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–1)
2. Lox pa ting buan son mai pa yaog yaw (1.2–3.13)
3. Kaing ceu pa caw kawn Khrit tix yuh (4.1–12)
4. Pa krai ka grawh grawng pa sang lih hoek Khrit (5.13–5.11)
5. Lox pa nghoik pa sidiex gau (5.12–22)
6. Lox pa nghoik (5.23–28)
