Lai Yi-see-thie
Krai dawt
Grawng kaing ka daux Lai Yi-see-thie mawh pa lih tix mawh ka daux nyiex haw Vawng Paw-see. Ka daux kaing in mawh kawn bun Yo-thaix pa kaux Yi-see-thie pa mawh cao grawng kaing. Mawh kheu kua rhawm nawh mai muh ka pao ceu tix, Yi-see-thie taoh ka pui meung tix luan khaing krax pa pauh pui koeng. Daux lai in krai grawng pui Yo-thaix ka daux been mai grawng kaoh Poi Phu-eu.
Phuk lai pa mawh pa kied ting ceu ka daux lai in yaox ka daux simin ting pon simin iak kao pon: “Maix ang krai lox ka daux bawg yam in, pui Yo-thaix rien tix sang pon krax taoh teem tang krax, phan mawh maix mai pui nyiex ma maix tag yaox krax pa laux leeing. Pui ang tawng, hoik mawh maix moeng simiang mawh kheu kaing in heue!”
Krai kaing
1. Yi-see-thie pon tix mawh moeng simiang (1.1–2.23)
2. Kaing pa yuh Ha-mang (3.1–5.14)
3. Ha-mang yaox krax ciyang pa yum (6.1–7.10)
4. Pui Yo-thaix pex ka pui pa koeng (8.1–10.3)
