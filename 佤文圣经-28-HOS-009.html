Heu-si-ax krai pao grawng sang caw pui Yi-seu-liex tix kham krax ciyang
9
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
paw vi gaux rhawm si lao,
paw vi yuh poi krax gaux rhawm awm pui laig li tang ceu.
Pex hoik tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang lai cu nghiet ka nawh.
Pex hoik cu tix coh to kaux tix ka Pa-lix awm sibun coh kaux tix yuh nan,
mai teei nghox koi kieh mawh pa coh mawh jum to kaux tix.
2 Viang mawh nan,
ang koe miex glang tix,
pex tag sang jud cha ka nghox koi mai ka bee sime,
mai plai pa nyawm kawx ang lai baox koe.
3 Pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai baox pon yaox tix ot ka haktiex pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kix;
kix tag sang deeh ing ka haktiex Ai-cix,
tag sang ih ka priex preem pa ang singax singeha ka haktiex Ya-sux.
4 Kix kawx ang lai baox pon yaox tix tan lu plai nyawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang lai pon yaox tix tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka nawh nang.
Ceu pa ih kix hoik awm priex preem pa ih nyiex pui pa yum,
mai tag chix kieh pui seeing brai.
Priex pa ih kix chix kieh kix sak vaig naog tu ngie khaing,
mai ang pon viex laig ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex son sang mawh krax tan.
5 Kheu nan,
ka daux poi pa ciak ci krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
sang pon yuh kix ceu pa tix lie?
6 Yam hoek krax laux krax leeing,
kix yaog tix to hu ku dauh ku dee;
viang mawh nan,
pui Ai-cix tag sang khrawm ka kix,
mai si paung kix ka Maw-fux.
Nyiex ma kix dee pa hoik deuh sin ngao ot kah,
tag sang kuah naog pang kat yeem yaw ka heue.
7 bSingaix pa ciyang hoik hoek heue!
Singaix pawk bix hoik hoek heue!
Yam lih kaing in tix mawh,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tawng kah.
Pex ah nin:
“Cao jhai in mawh pa daox aw;
pui pa laig khuan daux in mawh pui yhot.”
Mawh kheu kied ting phi map pex,
pex tom koeng ka aux yuh nin.
8 Siyiex hoik tawx aux mawh cao pa toe tix krai ka lox nawh,
mai nhie aux ne raw ka pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex.
Viang mawh nan,
aux hu dee mawx ang ah,
mai kix hoik keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing,
awm giex ka kawn sim yuh nan.
Ka daux nyiex buan Siyiex,
cao jhai hoik si pup ka pui koeng khawm uik.
9 dPui in kix,
phi map kix hoik cux rao ma,
awm yam ot kix ka daux haktiex Ci-pi-yaxc sivoe an yuh nan.
Siyiex tag sang tiiem tix deuh ka phi map kix tete,
mai ciyang ka kix heue.
Phi map pui Yi-seu-liex mai dee si dauh krax lawng kix
10 eSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yam jauh aux tix si pup ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
hoik awm yaox aux plix siiet ka daux tiag daux tu yuh nan;
yam jauh aux tix si pup ka tax du pex bawg tix,
hoik awm yaox aux plix ruh pa liang tix pon ka piang khaox ruh yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
yam hoek kix ka gawng Pi-eu,
mai kix jauh tix krub ka Pa-lix,
ang koe miex glang tix kix tag tieh tix lih caox caig awm siyiex pa muh kix yuh nan.
11 Min plak ngai pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang pu hu awm sim yuh nan.
Ka daux haktiex kix,
bun ang lai baox chix keh kawn,
ang lai baox chix nghe,
ang lai chix koe kaux.
12 Phan kix keh ka kawn simex kawn bun,
mai aux tag sang teei hu,
ang deuh koe tix kaux kawx.
Yam tik vud aux ka kix,
kix tag sang laux leeing heue!”
13 Simiang Siyiex eue!
Aux yaox ka leue pui tix pauh kawn simex kawn bun Yi-seu-liex yum.
14 Sang khiox khaw aux maix kia ngien ka pui in kix yuh ka mawx lie?
Sang kieh bun kix ang lai pon keh kawn,
ang lai pon eei heue!
Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka pui Yi-seu-liex
15 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Krax pa caox pa caig pa jauh pui Yi-seu-liex tix yuh ka daux haktiex Cie-cia khawm uik,
tag kieh aux jauh tix koeng ka kix ka daux haktiex an.
Mawh kheu kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix,
mai aux tag sang grheu kix lih hu khaing haktiex aux.
Aux ang lai baox muh ka kix,
mawh kheu simiang kix hoik tieh vheen ka aux khawm uik.
16 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik awm riah khaox kroh yuh nan,
ang lai pon plix plix.
Kix ang lai chix koe doeh ceu cad,
phan koe,
aux tag sang pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun pa muh kix yum heue.”
Cao jhai krai grawng pui Yi-seu-liex
17 Siyiex pa krub aux kah,
tag sang tik vud ka pui cie tix.
Mawh kheu ang kix nghiet lox,
kix tag sang vuk vied vuk viang ka meung piang haktiex heue.
