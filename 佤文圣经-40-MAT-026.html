Pa keud sawn grawng sang pauh ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.1–2; Lai Lu-cia 22.1–2; Lai Yaw-hang 11.45–53)
26
Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux gum lox in,
nawh krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
2 a“Pex hoik tawng,
baox koe ra loe ngaix mawh Poi Luat-luan,
pui tag sang coh ka kawn kawn pui,
mai tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue.”
3 Yam an,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song ka daux kawn pui khankix khrawm tix ot ka daux biang brawk nyiex Kai-ya-fa cao giex kaing li pa ting,
4 rom tix keud sawn grawng sang giex ka Ye-sux,
mai pauh nawh yum.
5 Viang mawh nan,
kix ah nin:
“Ex ang pon tix yuh kaing in ka daux yam yuh poi,
son ang suk sag yuh pui hun heue.”
Pon pa groi rawm bee ka haktiex Paw-ta-ni
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.3–9; Lai Yaw-hang 12.1–8)
6 Ye-sux ot ka daux nyiex ma Si-meung kaw pa koe jhawm saux si pawg ka haktiex Paw-ta-ni;
7 bkoe bun tix kaux viex kawng hoek tix mu,
mai hoik sau naog ka rawm bee pa ngawh.
Yam sawm Ye-sux,
bun an hieh bee bhawng an ka piang kaing Ye-sux.
8 Pui gau yaox ka grawng kaing in,
mai kied ang naog rhawm kah,
ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx lai kieh nai yuh nin?
9 Bee in chix tix coh mawh mau mai teem pui hot pui sibhawm ka lie!”
10 Ye-sux hoik tawng kah,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh pa tix lai tieb tie ka bun in lie?
Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa mhawm son aux tix ceu.
11 cMawh kheu hoik ot pui hot pui sibhawm tau mai pex jheeng jheeng,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang yaox tix ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng.
12 Nawh hieh rawm bee pa bhawng in ka to kaux aux,
mawh son sang rien khre tix sang si paung ka aux.
13 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
gum lox buan in sidiex tix hoek dee mawx ang ah,
pui ku kaux tag caw tix krai lao ka kaing pa hoik yuh nawh khawm uik,
son sang doh keud ka nawh heue.”
Yo-tax cu tix coh ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.10–11; Lai Lu-cia 22.3–6)
14 Ka daux kawn cau Ye-sux kao ra kaux,
koe Yo-tax pa mawh pui Cia-liiex tix kaux;
nawh hu ka simiang cao giex kaing li,
15 dmai ah nin:
“Phan aux teei tix tawx Ye-sux ka pex,
sang tawx pex aux cie ceu pa tix lie?”
Kix teei mau luah tix ngoe plak tawx ka nawh.
16 Jauh yam an,
Yo-tax sawg bawg yam pa sang coh ka Ye-sux heue.
Laig sawm priex bo tau mai pui gau ka daux Poi Luat-luan
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.12–21; Lai Lu-cia 22.7–13, 21–23; Lai Yaw-hang 13.21–30)
17 Poi Tik-su-saix ka daux tix ngaix,
pui gau hoek tix chawk ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sang kieh maix ex rien khre priex bo Poi Luat-luan son maix dee mawx lie?”
18 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pex laig ka daux veng yam yaox ka pui tix kaux kaux,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
‘Sara ah nin:
bawg yam cie aux hoik dex sang hoek;
aux mai pui gau tag sang yuh Poi Luat-luan ka daux nyiex maix heue.’ ”
19 Pui gau tom hawt awm gum lox pa tawm Ye-sux rien khre ka priex bo Poi Luat-luan heue.
20 Pon bo,
Ye-sux sawm tau mai kawn cau tix kao ra kaux.
21 Yam sawm kix Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex,
ka daux pex koe pui tix kaux tag sang coh ka aux heue.”
22 Kix kied laux rhawm tete,
tix kaux lai tix kaux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
mawh aux lie?”
23 eYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pui an pa cux khao mun tau mai aux ka daux pan tag sang coh ka aux.
24 Awm pa hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri,
kawn kawn pui tag sang kham krax pa kha rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa coh kawn kawn pui an hoek krax laux krax leeing heue!
Pui in phan ang lih keh,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing heue!”
25 Yo-tax pa coh Ye-sux kawx ang jheeh tix chawk ah nin:
“Sara,
mawh aux lie?”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix hoik tang krai to kaux tix heue.”
Priex bo cie Simiang Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.22–26; Lai Lu-cia 2214–20; Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk Tix 11.23–25)
26 Yam sawm kix,
Ye-sux teei ka khao mun,
ran rawg buan ka daux krax pa yaog yaw,
khaix an si vit kah,
mai guah tix tawx ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Pex teei tix ih;
in mawh to kaux aux heue.”
27 Khaix an,
nawh teei ka kawk,
khaix hoik yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
mai tawx ka kix ah nin:
“Pex teei tix nyaux heue;
28 fin mawh nham aux,
mawh pa jhi grawng pa yuh Siyiex sijax mukheu mai kawn pui,
mawh pa jauh son sang kieh phi map pui hun pon krax pa pluat ploi.
29 Aux krai ka pex,
aux ang doh lai baox nyaux ka plai in,
tom hoek yaox aux tix nyaux ka plai pa khraox ka daux meung keeing aux tau mai pex ka daux singaix an heue.”
30 Kix rhax ka lai tix ceu,
tom lih,
mai hu ka gawng sime heue.
Ye-sux grox krai ang Pi-teux tawng ka tix
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.27–31; Lai Lu-cia 22.31–34; Lai Yaw-hang 13.36–38)
31 gKhaix an,
Ye-sux krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Ka daux bo in,
pex tag sang tik vud ka aux khawm uik kheu son aux;
mawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Siyiex tag sang pauh ka cao liang yung,
cub yung tag sang prung prang tix tang hu heue.’
32 hViang mawh nan,
khaix hoik kaoh im aux,
aux tag sang ran hoek sivoe pex ka haktiex Cia-li-lix heue.”
33 Pi-teux ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Viang pui tang kaux hoik tik vud ka maix,
aux ang doh tik vud ka maix heue!”
34 Ye-sux krai ka Pi-teux ah nin:
“Aux krai ka maix,
ka daux bo in sivoe sang ux ia,
maix tag sang thiang tix ang tawng ka aux loe bawg heue.”
35 Pi-teux pawk ah nin:
“Viang aux caw tix yum tau mai maix,
mai aux ang doh thiang tix ang tawng ka maix heue.”
Pui gau tang kaux kawx hoik ah nin khawm uik heue.
Rawg buan ka daux si peem Kheu-si-ma-ni
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.32–42; Lai Lu-cia 22.39–46)
36 Ye-sux mai pui gau hoek ka haktiex tix dauh,
ceu kaux mawh Kheu-si-ma-ni;
nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Pex ngawm dee in,
aux hu rawg buan plak an.”
37 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh viex Pi-teux mai kawn simex Si-pi-thaix ra kaux hu tau maix tix.
Nawh jauh tix laux rhawm laux rhi,
38 mai ah nin ka kix:
“Aux kied laux rhawm tete,
hoik dex sang yum.
Pex ot dee in,
rien rhawm tix tau mai aux heue!”
39 Nawh koi hu ka plak sivoe,
mai gup tix piang tiex rawg buan ah nin:
“Keeing aux eue,
phan chix,
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux nyaux ka kawk pa song in heue!
Viang mawh nan,
paw vi kieh mawh alo aux,
kieh mawh pa hawt tix yuh ka alo maix heue.”
40 Nawh deeh hoek ka dee ot pui gau loe kaux dee an,
mai yaox hoik laig it kix,
nawh tom ah nin ka Pi-teux:
“Ang pex pon rien rhawm tix tau mai aux tix nari lie?
41 Tag rien rhawm tix rawg buan,
son ang pui sang pon nyaw glaw ka pex.
Rhawm rhi pex hoik cu tix yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
nex nham ca mawh pa iak riang heue.”
42 Ka daux ra bawg Ye-sux baox rawg buan ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
phan kawk pa song in ang pon lih hu khaing aux,
tag sang kieh aux nyaux ka tete,
kieh mawh alo maix pa yuh heue!”
43 Nawh baox deeh hoek ka pui gau tix dee an,
mai yaox kix hoik laig it;
pie ngai kix kawx ang lai pon blah tix heue.
44 Ye-sux baox lih hu khaing kix,
rawg buan ka daux loe bawg,
lox pa krai nawh hoik awm pa hoik krai nawh sivoe yuh nan.
45 Khaix an nawh baox deeh hoek ka pui gau tix dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Pex ca kawn laig it,
kawn laux lee tix lie?
Jhak heue,
bawg yam pa coh kawn kawn pui ka daux tawm taix pui phi map hoik hoek.
46 Kaoh,
hu ex heue!
Jhak heue,
pui pa coh aux hoik hoek heue!”
Giex ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.43–50; Lai Lu-cia 22.47–53; Lai Yaw-hang 18.3–12)
47 Yam kawn si kah Ye-sux,
Yo-tax pa mawh ka daux kawn cau kao ra kaux tix kaux hoek.
Koe cub pui hun viex vaig khaox hoek tau mai nawh;
kix mawh simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song ka daux kawn pui pa ploi hoek.
48 Pui pa coh ka Ye-sux an ran yuh mhai klip ka kix,
ah nin:
“Hu ngheet aux pui mawx,
pui an tag mawh pui pa sang giex pex,
pex giex ka nawh heue.”
49 Yo-tax hu ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux dum mawx dum an ah nin:
“Sara,
maix mhawm heue!”
Khaix an ngheet ka nawh.
50 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Paox grawm,
pa keud maix tix yuh nied tix yuh heue!i”
Mawh kheu nan,
pui an kix hu ka plak sivoe ngai giex ka Ye-sux,
mai phrawk ka nawh.
51 Ka daux pui pa ot tau mai Ye-sux tix kaux,
leed vaig lih mug ka siliex cao giex kaing li pa ting,
vaig thak ka yhaok nawh cawd tix khawn.
52 Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Sau vaig ka daux phak heue!
Mawh kheu pa yaog ka vaig tag sang som ju greem pa lawm vaig.
53 Khex ang pex tawng aux chix tix khiox khaw keeing aux teem coi tix,
mai kieh nawh ploi kawn cau Siyiex hoek kaing kao ra cub haoh dum mawx dum an lie?
54 Phan aux yuh nin,
grawng kaing pa hoik krai ka daux phuk lai sigang khri tag sang caw tix lih mawh,
sang chix pon lih yuh ka mawx lie?”
55 jKhaix an,
Ye-sux ah nin ka cub pui an kix:
“Pex viex vaig khaox hoek giex ka aux,
mai kieh aux awm pui tud pui rhim lie?
Aux ot tix sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan ku singaix,
pex ang bax tawx taix tix ka aux.
56 Viang mawh nan,
grawng pa lih kaing in kix tix mawh khawm uik,
tag sang kieh gum lox ka daux phuk lai sigang khri lih tix mawh heue.”
Bawg yam in,
can pui gau khawm uik hoik tik vud ka nawh,
mai hoik yaog tix to heue.
Kham krax prawng ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.53–65; Lai Lu-cia 22.54–55, 63–71; Lai Yaw-hang 18.13–14, 19–24)
57 Pui pa giex Ye-sux khankix viex Ye-sux hu ka daux nyiex cao giex kaing li pa ting Kai-ya-fa.
Ka daux an,
sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai kuad song khrawm paox tix dee an.
58 Pi-teux ot dee singai hawt khaix Ye-sux,
tom hoek ka biang brawk nyiex cao giex kaing li pa ting.
Pi-teux laig ka biang brawk,
ngawm tau mai pui pau,
sang jhak tix yaox ka kaing pa yuh kix yuh ka mawx.
59 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai bang kum pui Yo-thaix sawg grawng krax yuh sakse pa pie lhien son sang deuh phi map ka Ye-sux,
son sang prawng nawh yum,
60 viang koe pui tom nie deuh phi map ka piang nawh,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon teei mhai sakse lih.
Dee nghoik,
koe pui ra kaux hoek tix ci ka nawh ah nin:
61 k“Pui in hoik krai ah nin:
‘Aux pon yieh ka nyiex buan Siyiex laux,
ka daux loe singaix aux deeh pon cox sum ka mhawm heue.’ ”
62 Cao giex kaing li pa ting kaoh cung,
chawk ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui in kix deuh phi map piang maix,
maix ang koe lox sang pawk lie?”
63 Ye-sux ang nye.
Cao giex kaing li pa ting baox krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
nhie maix tiex tu krai ka yix,
mawh ang maix mawh Khrit,
kawn simex Siyiex lie?”
64 lYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“In hoik mawh maix pa krai.
Viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
khaix in,
pex tag sang yaox kawn kawn pui ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm cao pa ting siawp sibrawm,
mai brug pai awm lih hoek khaing rao ma heue!”
65 mCao giex kaing li pa ting mhong ka lox in,
riah ka kraung ceep tix ah nin:
“Nawh map nai ka Siyiex!
Ex ang lai lo ka pui pa kham sakse heue.
Pex hoik mhong ka lox pa map nai nawh ka Siyiex;
66 pex krai sang khaw tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Nawh khaw tix yum heue!”
67 nKix baig rawm mia tix ka ngai nawh,
mai lua taix tix tawk ka nawh.
Pui an kix pa si mang ka yhaok nawh
68 ah nin:
“Khrit eue,
maix mawh cao jhai heue!
Maix krai tix yaox,
mawh pui mawx pa lauh ka maix lie?”
Pi-teux ang tawng ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 14.66–72; Lai Lu-cia 22.56–62; Lai Yaw-hang 18.15–18)
69 Pi-teux ngawm ka biang brawk plak praix;
koe bun siliex cao giex kaing li pa ting tix kaux hoek ah nin:
“Maix kawx mawh paox Ye-sux pui Cia-li-lix heue.”
70 Pi-teux krai ang tix tawng Ye-sux ka plak sivoe pui hun.
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng krai maix ceu pa tix”,
71 khaix an nawh lih hu,
mai hoek ka diang siviex biang brawk.
Baox koe bun siliex tix kaux baox yaox ka nawh,
ah nin ka pui pa cung dee an:
“Pui in mawh paox Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux heue.”
72 Pi-teux baox krai ang tix tawng,
mai tiex tu ah nin:
“Aux ang doh tawng ka pui an heue!”
73 Baox koe tix vud,
pui pa cung ka hawt praok nawh,
mai krai ka Pi-teux ah nin:
“Maix mawh paox kix tete;
lox si kah maix hoik plih maix lih heue.”
74 Pi-teux baox tiex tu tix bawg ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng ka pui an.
Phan lox pa krai aux ang mawh tete,
Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka aux heue!”
Ka daux bawg yam in,
ia tom ux.
75 Pi-teux tom taug doh keud ka lox pa krai Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sivoe sang ux ia maix tag ang tawng ka aux loe bawg.”
Nawh tom lih hu mai yiiem heue.
