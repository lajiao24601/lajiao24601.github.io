Ya-na-ni-yax mai Sa-fe-la
5
Koe pui tang kaux tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-na-ni-yax;
nawh mai moeng nawh Sa-fe-la coh ka kaung ma tix tix blah.
2 Nawh deuh mau tix blah,
mai teei tix tawx pa deuk ka kawn cau;
kaing pa yuh nawh in moeng nawh kawx hoik cu kah.
3 Pi-teux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ya-na-ni-yax,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh Sa tan giex gang ka rhawm rhi tix,
kieh maix pie lhien ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
teei tix deuh mau pa coh tix kaung ma tix blah lie?
4 Yam ang kaung ma bax coh tix hoik mawh cie maix,
khaix hoik coh tix,
mau an kawx hoik mawh cie maix;
yuh ka mawx maix lai koe rhawm pa yuh nin lie?
Ang mawh pie lhien maix ka pui,
mai hoik mawh pie lhien maix ka Siyiex heue!”
5 Ya-na-ni-yax mhong ka lox in,
nawh tom goh yum;
pui pa mhong ka kaing in kied lhat graung ka tete.
6 Koe pui pa nhum nu laig daux nyiex,
mai kie to kaux yum pui in,
grawng hu si paung ka heue.
7 Ka daux bawg yam loe taix nari,
moeng nawh laig daux nyiex,
mai ang bax tawng grawng kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh.
8 Pi-teux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Krai aux mhong,
ot mau pa coh pex kaung ma dee in khawm uik lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
ot dee in khawm uik.”
9 Pi-teux tom ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pax lai rom tix nyaw glaw ka khuan Simiang Siyiex lie?
Maix jhak,
pui pa si paung mex maix kawn ot ka diang siviex,
kix kawx tag sang grawng tix hu si paung ka maix heue!”
10 Nawh goh dum mawx dum an ka plak sivoe caong Pi-teux,
mai yum.
Pui pa nhum ju an laig daux nyiex,
mai yaox grawng pa hoik yum bun an,
nawh tom grawng bun an lih hu,
mai si paung ot ka praok mex nawh.
11 Pui ceu cao khawm uik mai pui tang kaux pa mhong ka grawng in hoik kied lhat graung khawm uik heue.
Mhai pa daox pa am
12 Kawn cau khankix hoik yuh mhai pa daox pa am tom nie ka daux pui haktiex;
can kawn Khrit ih rhawm rhi khri mau paox tix khrawm paox tix ka greem grah Saw-law-meung.
13 Viang pui hun hoik nab yam yung yi kix,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa ang mawh kawn Khrit khankix ang pon hoek dex kix.
14 Yam an pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux nied ot nied hun,
khawm simex khawm bun.
15 Mawh kheu hoik yuh kawn cau khankix ka mhai pa daox pa am tom nie,
koe pui tom klien ka pui pa koe jhawm saux hoek ka daux laih,
deuh nawh ot piang ku,
ka piang prux it nawh,
mai simie yam hu Pi-teux dee an,
pang peue nawh tag sang veei ka piang to kaux pui an.
16 Kawn koe cub pui hun pa viex pui pa koe jhawm saux mai pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai hoek khaing veng pa dex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
pui pa koe jhawm saux in kix hoik pon tix moi khawm uik heue.
Kawn cau kham krax pa tuk tiak tuk te
17 Cao giex kaing li pa ting mai cub caw nawh khankix,
pa mawh pui Sa-tu-kai pui haktiex,
kied boh rhawm ka kawn cau,
mawh kheu nan kix but rhawm tix sang tawx taix tix.
18 Kix tawx taix tix giex kawn cau,
mai laung kix ka daux thawng.
19 Viang mawh nan,
kawn cau Simiang Ye-sux paoh ka siviex daux thawng,
mai thung kawn cau lih,
ah nin ka kix:
20 “Pex hu cung ka daux nyiex buan,
krai pao daux krax pa dex dawt mai ju pa khraox ka kawn pui.”
21 Kawn cau nghiet ka lox in,
laig daux nyiex buan sivoe sang bax riang praix,
mai jauh tix sidiex gau ka pui heue.
Cao giex kaing li pa ting mai cub caw cie nawh khankix,
khrawm can kuad song Yo-thaix ka daux bang kum,
khaix an tawx lox ka daux thawng teei kawn cau lih.
22 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoek pui pau,
kix yaox ang kawn cau khankix lai ot daux thawng,
kix tom deeh ing krai ka bang kum pui Yo-thaix ah nin:
23 “Yix hoek ka daux thawng,
yaox siviex daux thawng hoik song tix kaux,
pui naing pa pau daux thawng hoik pau diang siviex khawm uik.
Viang mawh nan,
yix paoh ka siviex daux thawng,
mai ang yaox pui pie tix kaux kawx pa ot dee an heue!”
24 Simiang pui pau nyiex buan mai simiang cao giex kaing li khankix mhong ka lox in,
mai kied daox deng kah,
ang tawng kawn cau hoik si pup ka kaing ceu pa tix.
25 Bawg yam an koe pui tix kaux hoek tix krai ah nin:
“Pui pa laug pex daux thawng hoik ot daux nyiex buan sidiex gau ka pui hun lie!”
26 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui pau thung pui cau tix hu,
mai viex ka kawn cau hoek.
Kix ang bax tawx taix tix tawk ka kix,
mawh kheu lhat kix pui hun teei simaox tawk kix ka heue.
27 Khaix hoik viex kix kawn cau khankix hoek,
kix nhie kawn cau khankix cung ka plak sivoe ngai bang kum.
Cao giex kaing li pa ting jauh tix prawng ka kix.
28 aNawh ah nin:
“Yix hoik ang cu pex yuh nin tom kin,
ang cu pex sidiex gau ka daux ceu kaux pui in,
mai pex hoik tieh tix sidiex ka daux krax cie pex tauh hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
khaix an tag sang kieh nham ceuk pui in hoek ka piang to kaux yix heue!”
29 Pi-teux mai pui gau tang kaux pawk ah nin:
“Yix tag sang nghiet ka Siyiex,
ang nghiet ka kawn pui.
30 Ye-sux pa hoik tawk pex piang khaox lhak,
Siyiex tax du yix hoik kieh nawh deeh kaoh im.
31 Siyiex hoik yaog ka nawh,
mai hoik kieh nawh mawh simiang ka plak taix khawn dawm tix,
mawh cao taoh,
son sang tawx krax pa sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex loh rhawm rhi tix,
kieh phi map kix pon krax pa pluat ploi.
32 Yix hoik mawh pui pa kham sakse kaing in kix;
Khuan Singax Singeh pa tawx Siyiex ka pui pa nghiet ka nawh kawx,
hoik rom tix kham sakse tau mai yix heue.”
33 Pui ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix mhong ka lox in,
kied song rhawm kah,
kix tom but rhawm tix sang pauh ka kawn cau khankix.
34 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux kix koe pui Fa-li-saix tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Cia-ma-liex,
mawh sara lox tat jhawm prawng pa nab yam yung yi pui tix kaux.
Nawh kaoh cung ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix,
mai tawx lox nhie kix viex kawn cau hu,
35 khaix an krai ka daux bang kum ah nin:
“Paox ceu paox cad pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
pex pa yuh ka kaing ceu in kix,
tag caw tix rien rhawm tix heue!”
36 “Sivoe an,
Du-tax yaog tix kaoh,
tang yo rang di to kaux tix,
koe pui been pon yieh kaux pa nghiet ka nawh.
Dee si dauh pui kawx hoik pauh nawh yum,
pui pa nghiet ka nawh hoik pung prang tang hu,
mai hoik laux leeing ka daux kaing pa ang khaw.
37 Khaix an baox koe Yo-tax pui Cia-li-lix,
yaog tix kaoh cung yam si kaw pui ka ca rang,
nawh kawx hoik nyaw glaw pui tom nie hawt ka tix;
pui kawx hoik pauh ka nawh,
pui pa nghiet ka nawh kawx,
pui hoik grheu hu.
38 Phan mawh son kaing in yam in,
aux krai ka pex,
paw vi yuh krax pa tieb tie ka kix,
ghram kix yuh heue!
Phan pa keud kix pa yuh kix mawh pa kaoh khaing kawn pui,
tag sang laux leeing;
39 phan mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
pex ang pon tix pex ka kix.
Pa yuh pex aux lhat mawh pa tieb tie ka Siyiex heue!”
Ka daux bang kum pui Yo-thaix cu ka lox pa krai Cia-ma-liex,
40 mawh kheu nan,
kok kawn cau hoek,
si vied ka kix,
mai baox tawx lox paw lai cu kix sidiex gau ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux,
khaix an ploi ka kix.
41 Kawn cau lih hu khaing bang kum pui Yo-thaix,
kheu kix hoik keung tix kham krax pa kaig tua son ceu kaux Ye-sux,
rhawm rhi kix kied gaux rhawm tete.
42 Kix ca ot ka daux nyiex buan ku singaix,
mai sidiex gau pui ang jud ka daux nyiex,
sidiex gau grawh grawng pa dex mai gum lox buan cie Ye-sux Khrit heue.
