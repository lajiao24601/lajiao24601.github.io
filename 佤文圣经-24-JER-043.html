Viex Yie-li-mi hu ka Ai-cix
43
Khaix hoik uik krai aux gum lox pa kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix aux krai lox ka kix ku gum.
2 Kawn simex Heu-sa-ya pa kaux Ya-sa-li-ya,
kawn simex Cia-li-yax pa kaux Yaw-ha-nang,
khawm can pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi khankix taug krai ka aux ah nin:
“Maix hoik pie lhien heue!
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex ang bax tawx lox nhie maix tieb tie yix ang hu paung ot haktiex Ai-cix.
3 In hoik mawh krax pa cheeik kawn simex Ni-li-yax pa kaux Pa-lux maix lih tieh vheen ka yix,
sang ap yix ka pui Pa-pi-loeng,
mai kieh kix pauh ka yix,
phan ang mawh nan giex yix hu haktiex Pa-pi-loeng.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
can simiang naing Yaw-ha-nang khawm uik mai pui tang kaux ang cu tix nghiet ka gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex kix paung ot ka daux Yo-tax.
5 aYaw-ha-nang mai can cao kaing khawm uik viex pui pa deuk pa ot haktiex Yo-tax,
mai pui pa ang lai koe nyiex koe ma pa kaoh khaing meung ku meung hoek ka haktiex Ai-cix,
6 ka daux kix mawh khawm simex bun,
kawn nyawm,
mai kawn bun simiang khankix.
Kix teei tix tawx Ni-pu-sha-la-tang cao kaing pui pa pau ka Ci-ta-lix cao kuang pui,
mawh khawm Pa-lux mai aux,
hoik viex hu khawm uik.
7 Kix ang nghiet ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai hu ka Ta-pi-nix haktiex Ai-cix heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux dee an ah nin:
9 “Maix tag caw tix ot ka plak sivoe ngai pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai gah tix si paung simaox tix blah ot ka greem daux glaox ka daux bang pa koe sivoe diang siviex simiang Ta-pi-nix.
10 Khaix an krai ka pui hun ah nin:
‘Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
tag sang viex siliex aux simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoek dee in;
nawh tag sang deuh dang ngeung tix ot ka piang dee si paung auxb simaox an,c
mai nih ka nyiex gox bang pui nyiex simiang dee an.
11 Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha tag sang hoek puing ka Ai-cix.
Pui an kix pa khaw tix yum tag sang yum ka jhawm saux;
pa khaw tix giex tag sang giex;
pa khaw tix yum ka naing tag sang yum.
12 Aux tag sang ploi ngu lih gleeing laux leeing ka nyiex vad pui Ai-cix.
Simiang Pa-pi-loeng tag sang gleeing laux leeing ka siyiex tang kaux kix,
mai giex kix hu.
Kix tag sang yuh laux ka pui Ai-cix,
mai deeh hoek khaing pon krax pa puing pex,
awm hoik tik cao liang khawng briex tix breeing khaing kraung ceep tix yuh nan.
13 Nawh tag sang yuh laux leeing ka rong simaox riang siyiex singaix pui Ai-cix,
mai gleeing laux leeing ka nyiex vad pui Ai-cix heue.’ ”
