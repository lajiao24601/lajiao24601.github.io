Yum rhawm ka Siyiex
62
Aux yien yawg tix krox jun ka Siyiex;
nawh mawh cao pa taoh ka aux.
2 Mawh ngie nawh pa kia ngien ka aux,
mai pa taoh ka aux,
nawh mawh tab aux,
mai aux ang yaox tix laux leeing som nai heue.
 
3 Pex puing ka pui tix kaux,
sang hoek yam mawx lie?
Nawh awm ngie guh ding pa vawk,
mai die pa sang ghrup.
4 Pex sang bluh nawh lih khaing dee lhaong khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix;
pex muh krax pa pie lhien.
Jheeh pex krai ka lox pa nawb buan,
rhawm rhi pex jhax lawg ka nawh heue.
 
5 Aux yien yawg tix krox jun ka Siyiex;
nawh mawh ngie krax keud pot jun pot aux nong nong khaing.
6 Mawh ngie nawh pa kia ngien ka aux,
mai pa taoh ka aux;
nawh mawh tab ka aux,
mai aux ang yaox krax laux leeing som nai.
7 Cao pa taoh ka aux mai riang min plak ngai hoik koe ka Siyiex;
nawh mawh tab pa kaux pa cong ka aux,
mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux heue.
 
8 Pui cie aux eue,
pex tag caw tix yum rhawm ka Siyiex jheeng jheeng.
Pex tag caw tix krai ka nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
kheu hoik mawh nawh nyiex dee mox ka ex heue.
 
9 Pui hot pui sibhawm awm ngie tix toek bhawm khaing yuh nan;
pui mi pui koe kawx hoik mawh ceu pa giex pa going khawm uik.
Phan deuh kix ot piang jiang,
mai ang yaox grawng pa cien pie ceeix kawx,
kawn lheu chaong khaing tix toek bhawm.
10 Pex paw vi een rhawm tix ka daux krax pa cau nyhau tix;
paw vi keud tix sang mag mi ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka krax pa tud pa rhim;
viang hoik koe sin ngao tom nie kawx,
mai paw vi een rhawm tix ka heue.
 
11 Aux baox mhong lox pa hoik krai Siyiex tix bawg,
siawp sibrawm hoik mawh cie nawh khawm uik;
12 aNawh hoik koe krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix.
Simiang eue,
maix tag sang hawt tix tawx buan son ka kaing pa yuh pui ku kaux heue.
