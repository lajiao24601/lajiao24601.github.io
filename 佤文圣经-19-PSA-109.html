Krai lao grawng ot daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi
109
Siyiex pa hoik yaog yaw aux eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi yien yawg tix ang nye.
2 Pui pa caox pa caig an kix hoek tix puing ka aux.
Kix pie lhien salao ka aux,
3 teei lox pa koe jai ah ka aux.
Puing ka aux khawm ang aux tie bax yuh tid tix.
4 Viang aux muh ka kix,
rawg buan son kix,
kix ca kawn tieb tie ka aux.
5 Kix pawk pa caox son pa mhawm,
pawk pa koeng son pa muh heue.
 
6 Khiox khaw maix teei cao prawng see pa ang mhawm hoek tix prawng ca ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
teei pui pa koeng ka nawh deuh phi map ka nawh.
7 Kieh nawh pon krax prawng,
mai prawng nawh koe phi map;
kieh lox pa rawg nawh buan kawx sawn tix mawh phi map.
8 aKieh ju nawh dawt;
kieh pui tang kaux plud tix yuh kaing pa yuh nawh.
9 Kieh kawn simex kawn bun nawh lih mawh kawn doe,
kieh moeng nawh lih mawh miex mai.
10 Kieh kawn simex kawn bun nawh vu vie tix ang koe nyiex ma,
mai hawt tix caung ka diang siviex pui;
kieh pui grheu kix lih hub khaing nyiex ma pa laux.
11 Kieh pui pa ceuk kix mau teei ka sin ngao can pa koe kix khawm uik;
kieh pui pa ang kix sax tawng sax yung tud rhim ka pa pon kix ka daux pa taug mao tix khawm uik.
12 Kieh nawh ang pon ka krax muh pui tang kaux;
kieh pa mawh kawn doe nawh ang pon krax pa caox rhawm.
13 Kieh doeh ceu cad nawh grai grup laux leeing;
kieh ru ceu cad nawh ang lai yaox tix hoek ka daux ra ju.
14 Kieh Simiang Siyiex doh keud ka phi map tax du nawh,
ang chix piawm ka krax pa lud loe pa yuh miex nawh.
15 Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawng ka phi map kix leeing ju;
kieh pui piawm ka kix khawm uik heue.
 
16 Mawh kheu ang nawh sax koe nang krax muh krax rhawm,
ca tawng tix gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm khaing,
phan ang mawh nan pauh ka pui pa koe krax laux rhawm,
pa ang koe cao teem cao coi.
17 Nawh muh tix jhax lawg,
kieh krax pa jhax lawg deeh hoek piang nawh;
nawh ang muh krax nawb buan,
kieh krax pa nawb buan singai hu khaing nawh.
18 Nawh jhax lawg,
awm yie tix ceep kraung yuh nan;
kieh krax pa jhax lawg in awm rawm pa laig ka nawh yuh nan,
awm bee pa hoik laig ka daux si-ang nawh yuh nan.
19 Kieh krax pa jhax lawg in awm kraung ceep pa fua tix ka nawh,
awm maox rawd nyoi pa hoik mad ka nawh jheeng jheeng heue.
 
20 Kieh Siyiex ciyang ka pui pa koeng ka aux yuh nin,
pawk bix ka pui an kix pa ah lox caox ka aux.
21 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
khiox khaw maix hawt tix teem coi aux awm lox pa hoik ap maix tix deuh,
mawh kheu krax muh pa kied mhawm kied mhiem maix hoik taoh ka aux.
22 Mawh kheu aux hot sibhawm mai kha rhawm rhi tix;
aux kied sidaing laux rhawm laux rhi tete.
23 Aux awm pang peue pa grai hu si nyek si nyek yam faik boi praix yuh nan,
awm sido tix mu pa grheu pui kah.
24 Ngawng aux hoik awn kheu tuah ka priex preem;
to kaux aux hoik krawx ang lai koe riang.
25 cPui pa yaox ka aux hoik nu nai ka aux khawm uik;
kix vuk vied kaing tix nyiah ka aux.
 
26 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix teem coi ka aux;
mawh kheu koe maix krax muh pa ang chix tieh chix loh,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux.
27 Kieh pui pa koeng ka aux tawng,
maix hoik mawh pui pa taoh ka aux an.
28 Viang kix hoik jhax lawg ka aux kawx,
maix ca kawn sang tawx buan ka aux.
Kieh pui pa tuk tiak ka aux ang pon krax pa pon pexd,
siliex maix tag sang pon krax pa gaux rhawm.
29 Kieh pui pa koeng ka aux pon krax pa kaig tua,
kieh awm ceep ka kraung dha pa kaig pa tua yuh nan heue.
 
30 Aux sang ah lox pa ting si num yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh ka daux bang kum pui hun paox tix,
31 mawh kheu nawh prawng rauh see son pui hot pui sibhawm,
taoh nawh luat luan khaing daux pa prawng ca pui nawh yum heue.
