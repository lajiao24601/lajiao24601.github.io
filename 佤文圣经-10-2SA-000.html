Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra
Krai dawt
Phuk lai in hoik mawh pa toe tix tiiem hawt phuk Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Tix, mai krai grawng bawg yam yuh Ta-wue simiang, jauh tom yam kuang nawh ka Yo-tax plak seh simin ting tix hoek pon, tom hoek kuang nawh ka meung haktiex kix uik, khawm Yi-seu-liex plak laong Simin ting phuan hoek tix nga pon. Phuk lai in hoik krai sidawng Ta-wue hoik pon vih vieh ka meung haktiex tix, giex kaux cong ka dang ngeung tix, mai yuh naing ka pui koeng pa ot daux haktiex kix mai pa ka sivie siveng kix. Ta-wue hoik mawh pui pa koe krax yum rhawm, pa chix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex tix kaux. Nawh hoik pon krax pa kieh pui khaw rhawm ka tix khawm uik. Khaix an, son sang simie nawh tix pon naog krax gaux rhawm ka daux pa si lao mai rhawm pa vao, mai bawg tix blah nawh hoik plih lih ka rhawm keud tix pa caox pa caig, yuh ka phi map pa ting. Viang mawh nan, yam krai Na-tang cao jhai Simiang Siyiex ka krax pa lud loe yuh nawh, mai nawh hoik doh keud ka phi map tix, mai cu tix rieb krax ciyang Siyiex ka tix heue.
Ka daux ju Ta-wue mai kaing pa hoik pon yuh nawh khankix, tag mawh kaing pa mhawm pa kieh pui Yi-seu-liex ang chix piawm tix bawg kawx. Mawh kheu nan, ka daux ju khaix, yam si pup meung haktiex ka krax laux krax leeing, kawn pui kied simie simiang hoek tix taoh ka kix, mai kix kied simie simiang tix kaux lih koe khaing ka daux doeh ceu cad Ta-wue, awm Ta-wue yuh nan heue.
