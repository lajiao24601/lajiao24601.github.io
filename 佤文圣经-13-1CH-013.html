Gah teug sijax mukheu hu khaing Ci-liex Yie-ling
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 6.1–11)
13
Simiang Ta-wue kum kawng tau mai can simiang naing pa thung ka pui naing kaing rheng kaux mai kaing yieh kaux khankix.
2 Khaix an nawh krai pao ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Phan pex cu,
mai in hoik mawh alo pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
kieh ex ploi pui hu krai ka paox ceu paox cad ex pa kawn deuk khankix,
mai cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we pa ot ku veng,
nhie kix hoek khrawm tau tix mai ex khawm uik dee in.
3 Khaix an,
ex teei ka teug sijax mukheu Siyiex hoek,
mawh kheu yam mawh Sao-law simiang,
ex ang bax kieh teug sijax mukheu in koe ceu.”
4 Kawn pui khaw rhawm ka lox in,
mai cu ka khawm uik heue.
5 aMawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue khrawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm meung,
jauh ka mee haktiex Ai-cix pa ot ka plak caong ma seh tom hoek ka pui pa ot ka haktiex diang moing gawng Ha-ma ku dauh ka plak caong ma laong,
son sang gah teug sijax mukheu hu khaing Ci-liex Yie-ling hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
6 bTa-wue mai kawn pui hoek ka daux haktiex Yo-tax pa mawh veng Pa-la (pa mawh Ci-liex Yie-ling),
son sang teei teug sijax mukheu Siyiex hoek;
teug sijax mukheu in hoik hawt ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex pa ngawm ka piang Ci-lu-paw ra mu.
7 Kix grawng teug sijax mukheu lih khaing daux nyiex Ya-pi-na-ta,
deuh ot ka piang ling law khraox tix lhang,
mai kieh Wu-sha mai Ya-si-yaw mawh cao bung krax hu sivoe.
8 Ta-wue mai can kawn pui khawm uik graoh,
yaog yaw Siyiex.
Kix rhax lai,
mai tauh ting ku ceu,
koe ting jung,
ting pawd aliah lah,
kraung,
mai chiang,
teet tawng khankix heue.
9 Yam hoek kix ka Ci-toeng dee pauh nghox ca,
moi pa bluh ling law dex sang goh,
Wu-sha tawx taix tix giex ka teug sijax mukheu.
10 Simiang Siyiex dum mawx dum an song rhawm ka nawh,
mai pauh ka nawh,
mawh kheu giex nawh ka teug sijax mukheu.
Wu-sha ot ka dee an,
mai yum ka sivoe Siyiex.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
haktiex an tom kok mawh Pi-lie-see Wu-shac.
Kheu jao song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka Wu-sha,
Ta-wue kied ang gaux rhawm ka heue.
12 Ta-wue lhat ka Simiang Siyiex,
tom ah nin:
“Yam in aux sang pon teei teug sijax mukheu hoek yuh ka mawx lie?”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ang viex teug sijax mukheu hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai deuh ot daux veng Cia-theux nyiex ma Eu-piie Yi-tong.
14 dTeug sijax mukheu deuh tix ot tan loe khix;
Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka nyiex ma Eu-piie Yi-tong,
khawm can pa mawh cie nawh khawm uik heue.
