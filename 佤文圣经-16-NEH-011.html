Pui pa paung ot daux Yie-lu-sa-leung
11
Simiang pui hun khankix hoik ot daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik;
pui hun pa kawn deuk khankix cawk lai,
kao nyiex tix nyiex laug lih tix nyiex,
mai kieh hu ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa singax,
pui pa kawn deuk khankix tag ot ka daux veng tang dauh.
2 Pui pa gaux rhawm tix hu ot daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui haoh pui hun tag yaog yaw nawh.
3 aPui hun Yi-seu-liex,
cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan,
mai doeh ceu cad siliex Saw-law-meung,
paung ot daux veng tang dauh,
pa mawh dee koe sin ngao tax du pui nyiex tix heue.
Plak greem mawh ca rang cao kaing pa ot daux haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung haktiex Yo-tax:
4 Ceu pui Yo-tax mawh:
Kawn simex Wu-si-ya pa kaux Ya-tha-ya,
kawn saux Sa-cia-li-ya.
Tax du nawh kawn koe Ya-ma-li-ya,
See-fa-thi-ya,
mai Ma-leu-liex;
kix hoik mawh kawn simex Yo-tax doeh ceu cad Fa-leu-see khawm uik heue.
5 Ma-si-ya,
nawh mawh kawn simex Pa-lux,
kawn saux Kux Heu-si.
Tax du nawh kawn koe Ha-sai-yax,
Ya-ta-ya,
Yaw-ya-lix,
mai Sa-cia-li-ya;
kix hoik mawh doeh ceu cad See-law-ni kawn simex Yo-tax khawm uik heue.
6 Doeh ceu cad Fa-leu-see koe pui naing pa kua pon yieh tix gliah daix kaux pa paung ot daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
7 Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming mawh:
Sa-lux,
mawh kawn simex Mi-su-lang,
kawn saux Yaw-yex.
Tax du nawh kawn koe Pi-ta-ya,
Keu-lai-ya,
Ma-si-ya,
Yi-thie,
mai Yie-sai-yax heue.
8 Cia-paix mai Sa-lai,
kix mawh pui nyiex pui ma pa dexb ka Sa-lux heue.
Pui Piien-ya-ming khawm uik pa ot daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung koe dim yieh tix nga daix kaux.
9 Kawn simex Si-ci-lix pa kaux Yaw-eux mawh cao pa kuang ka piang kaing pa yuh kix.
Kawn simex Ha-si-nu-yax pa kaux Yo-tax mawh cao pa ot greem pa kuang piang kaing ka daux veng in heue.
10 Cao giex kaing li mawh:
Kawn simex Yaw-ya-lix pa kaux Yie-ta-ya,
kawn koe Ya-cing heue.
11 Si-lai-ya,
pa mawh kawn simex Si-leu-cia,
kawn saux Mi-su-lang.
Tax du nawh kawn koe Sa-tu,
Mi-la-yaw,
mai Ya-si-thu;
kix hoik mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting khawm uik.
12 Ceu cad cub pui nyiex in khawm uik koe daix yieh tix nga ra kaux pa yuh kaing nyiex buan.
Ya-ta-ya,
nawh mawh kawn simex Yie-law-hang,
kawn saux Pi-la-lix.
Tax du nawh kawn koe Ang-si,
Sa-cia-li-yax,
Pa-see-hu-eux,
mai Ma-ci-ya.
13 Ceu cad cub pui nyiex in khawm uik koe ra yieh tipon ra kaux pa mawh cao kaing ceu pui kix heue.
Ya-ma-soix nawh mawh kawn simex Ya-sa-liex,
kawn saux Ya-ha-saix.
Tax du nawh kawn koe Mi-seu-li-mawx mai Ying-mai.
14 Ceu cad cub pui nyiex in koe tix yieh tix nga daix kaux pa mhawm yuh naing.
Cao pa kuang ka piang kaing yuh kix mawh Sa-pa-ti-yiex,
pui pa kaoh khaing pui nyiex pa ao min ceu kaux khankixc heue.
15 Pui Li-we mawh:
See-ma-ya nawh mawh kawn simex Ha-sux,
kawn saux Ya-li-kang.
Tax du nawh kawn koe Ha-sa-pi-ya mai Pu-ni heue.
16 Sa-pi-thaix mai Yaw-sa-pa,
kiex mawh pui Li-we,
pa mhawm rhawm keud rhawm sawn,
pa yuh kaing pa kuang plak praix nyiex buan heue.
17 Ma-tha-ni,
nawh mawh kawn simex Mi-cia,
kawn saux Sa-ti,
doeh ceu cad Ya-sa.
Nawh duk thung nyawm cawng nyiex buan yaog yaw rawg buan heue.
Pa-pu-cia,
nawh mawh pui pa teem coi Ma-tha-ni heue.
Ya-thaix,
nawh mawh kawn simex Sa-mu-yax,
kawn saux Cia-la,
doeh ceu cad Yie-tu-toeng heue.
18 Pui Li-we khawm uik koe ra yieh tix daix pon kaux,
pa ot ka daux veng pa singax Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
19 Pui pa pau siviex nyiex buan mawh:
Ya-kux,
Ta-meung,
mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh khawm uik koe tix yieh atigliah ra kaux heue.
20 Pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix mai cao giex kaing li pa kawn deuk khankix,
pui Li-we paung ot ka daux veng Yo-tax pa koe tang dauh dee koe sin ngao tax du kix.
21 Pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan Eu-fe-leux ot daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
kix yuh kaing ka greem Si-ha mai Ci-see-thiex heue.
22 Cao kuang ka piang kaing pui Li-we pa ot daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mawh Wu-si kawn saux Ha-sa-pi-ya,
kawn simex Pa-ni.
Tax du nawh mawh khawm pa kaux Ma-tha-ni mai Mi-cia,
ceu cad cub pui nyiex Ya-sa,
pa mawh ceu cad cub pui nyiex an pa kuang lox ting lox siao ka daux nyiex buan.
23 Simiang hoik koe been pa tat tix deuh,
mai krai sidawng grawng sang khaw ceu cad cub pui nyiex in tix sang lai loh tix thung gau lox ting lox siao ka daux nyiex buan heue.
24 Pi-tha-si-ya kawn simex Mi-see-sa-piiex,
mawh ceu cad cub pui nyiex Shie-la,
ceu pui Yo-tax;
nawh mawh pa tang son pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa paung ot meung ting haktiex Paw-see heue.
Pui tang veng
25 Pui tom nie paung ot daux yaong pui yuh kaing haktiex pa koe sivie siveng kix.
Ceu pui Yo-tax paung ot daux Ci-lie Ya-pa,
Ti-peung,
Ye-cia-shiie,
mai nyiex yaong pa koe sivie siveng veng in.
26 Kix kawx kieh pui tix blah paung ot ka Yie-su-yax,
Maw-la-tax,
Paw Pha-liex,
27 Ha-sa Su-yax,
Piie-see-pa mai nyiex yaong pa ot sivie siveng kix.
28 Kix paung ot ka veng Si-keu-la,
Mi-keu-na mai nyiex yaong pui yuh kaing haktiex pa ot siviex veng kix,
29 paung ot ka siviex Ying Ling-meung,
Saw-la,
Yie-maw,
30 Sa-naw-yax,
Ya-tu-lang,
mai nyiex yaong pui yuh kaing haktiex pa ot dex veng in kix.
Kix paung ot ka La-ciex mai nyiex yaong pa yuh kaing haktiex pa ot dex kix,
Ya-si-cia mai nyiex yaong pa ot sivie siveng kix.
Pa hoik mawh haktiex dee paung ot pui Yo-tax ngawd Sing-noeng si nax Piie-see-pa heue.
31 Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming paung ot ka Cia-pa,
Mi-mawx,
Ai,
Paw-theu-lix mai nyiex yaong pa ot sivie siveng kix,
32 Ya-na-thu,
Naw-pawx,
Ya-nang-ya,
33 Sha-saw,
La-max,
Ci-tha-ying,
34 Ha-tiiex,
Si-piien,
Ni-pa-la,
35 Law-teux,
A-naw,
mai ngawd Ciang-reng.
36 Koe pui Li-we tix blah pa paung ot haktiex Yo-tax,
mai hoik ciak ci kix ot tau mai pui Piien-ya-ming heue.
