Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Yi-fa-liien mai ceu pui Ma-na-si
16
Haktiex pa guah ka doeh ceu cad Yaw-seux:
Mee haktiex plak seh jauh ka rawm keet Yaw-tang pa dex mai Yie-li-keu,
pa mawh haktiex dee lih rawm keet Yie-li-keu plak lih singaix,
laig ka daux tiag.
Baox jauh ka Yie-li-keu plak laong tom hoek ka gawng Paw-theu-lix,
2 baox jauh ka Paw-theu-lix tom hoek ka Lu-see,
hoek tom dee ot pui Ya-ci pa mawh veng Ya-tha-lux Ya-ta,
3 hu plak klip singaix hoek tom ka haktiex pui Ya-li-thi,
tom hoek ka Paw Heu-loeng,
mai baox jauh dee an hu plak Ci-seux,
tom si dauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong heue.
4 Haktiex pa guah ka doeh ceu cad Yaw-seux pa mawh Yi-fa-liien mai Ma-na-si pa ot plak klip singaix,
kieh mawh sin ngao kix heue.
Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien
5 Mee haktiex cie doeh nyiex ma pui Yi-fa-liien ku nyiex mawh:
jauh ka Ya-tha-lu Ya-ta hu ka plak lih singaix tom hoek ka Paw Heu-loeng,
6 baox hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong;
plak laong mawh Mi-mi-tha.
Mee plak lih singaix jauh ka Tha-na See-law tom hoek ka Ya-naw-ha plak lih singaix,
7 khaix an hoek plak seh,
jauh ka Ya-naw-ha tom hoek Ya-tha-lux mai Na-la,
mai baox tiang ka Yie-li-keu,
nghoik tix hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue.
8 Mee haktiex plak klip singaix jauh ka Tha-pu-yax tiang ka rawm klawng Cia-na,
nghoik tix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
In plak piang mawh haktiex pa guah ka ceu pui Yi-fa-liien ku nyiex pon,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix heue.
9 Khaix an,
ka daux haktiex pui Ma-na-si koe veng yaong tix blah,
khawm yaong pa ot hawt veng kawx guah tix tawx ka pui Yi-fa-liien heue.
10 aPui Yi-fa-liien ang bax grheu pui Cia-nang pa ot ka haktiex Ci-seux to,
mawh kheu nan,
pui Cia-nang kawn ot si nax pui Yi-fa-liien,
tom hoek in singaix;
viang mawh nan,
kix hoik lih mawh siliex cawng pa yuh kaing son pui Yi-fa-liien heue.
