Krax muh Simiang Siyiex ka Yi-seu-liex
54
aYie-lu-sa-leung eue,
maix hoik awm bun pa ang chix keh kawn,
viang mawh nan,
yam in maix pon rhax ka lai,
ah lox pa ting pa si num.
Bun pa hoik tik vud pui an tag sang chix koe kawn praix tom nie,
lheu keh kawn tom nie khaing bun pa hoik koe mex khankix heue!
2 Tag caw tix vih va ka nyiex gox bang pa paung maix tix ot daux,
bluh ka maox nyiex gox bang tom lang,
sum kaux cong ka jim.
3 Tag caw tix vih va ka haktiex maix hu plak goix plak dawm.
Viang pui tang ceu tang cad hoik pon tix ot ka daux haktiex in,
pui cie maix ca kawn sang deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix;
veng pa hoik riem khankix,
pui tag sang hoek tix ot naog.
4 Paw vi lhat,
maix ang lai hoek krax kaig krax tua;
maix ang lai baox yaox ka krax pa kha.
Maix tag caw tix piawm ka baux bawg yam seeing brai bun khankix,
ang lai baox deeh doh keud ka krax pa ot miex mai nong nong.
5 Cao pa phan pa yuh maix hoik mawh mex maix;
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh simiang kru naing.
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh mawh cao pa taoh maix;
nawh hoik giex kaing ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
 
6 Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix hoik awm moeng pui nhum ju tix kaux,
mex nawh tik vud ka nawh,
mai nawh laux rhawm laux rhi.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex rieb nawh deeh hoek.
Nawh ah nin ka maix:
7 Aux hu khaing maix tix vud tix cu khaing;
viang mawh nan,
aux kied muh ka maix tete,
tag sang deeh rieb maix hoek.
8 Aux song rhawm ang simi ka maix mawh ngie tix vud tix cu khaing;
krax muh aux ka maix hoik mawh leeing ju leeing phan.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao taoh maix hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
9 bKa daux bawg yam singaix Naw-yax,
aux hoik sax ah tix deuh,
ang lai baox deeh kieh rawm kheem ka haktiex.
Yam in aux hoik ah tix deuh,
mai ang lai baox deeh song rhawm ka maix,
ang lai baox deeh ciyang ka maix.
10 Gawng ting khankix chix gah tix hu,
gawng iak khankix chix gah tix;
viang mawh nan,
krax muh aux ka maix ang chix tieh loh tix leeing ju leeing phan,
sijax mukheu pa nhim kawx ang chix tieh loh tix leeing ju.
Simiang Siyiex pa muh ka maix hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Kaing pa sang hoek ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung
11 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
Yie-lu-sa-leung,
maix pa mawh veng pa laux leeing pa ang koe cao teem eue,
ang koe pui pa hoek tix tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka maix.
Aux tag sang teei simaox pa koe rawm siang sum mawh riah maix,
teei siang simaox cox sum ka riah maix.
12 Teei ka siang simaox raoh sum ka nyiex lhaong maix,
teei simaox pa toh riang yuh siviex veng kah;
guh veng khawm pon daog hoik sau naog ka siang simaox khawm uik heue.
 
13 dAux tag sang hoek tix sidiex gau ka pui cie maix,
tawx krax pa haok huan mai pa nhim ka kix.
14 Pa rauh pa see hoik kieh maix ting lhaong,
ang lai baox koe pui pa hoek tix con cam ka maix,
kieh maix lhat graung;
maix ang lai baox yaox krax pa map nai pui,
ang yaox krax laux krax leeing.
15 Aux ang joh jhan ka pui pa puing ka maix;
pui pa yuh naing ka maix tag sang hoek krax sum krax nai heue.
 
16 Aux phan yuh cang mai pa yuh rhiem;
kix yuh glah ngu,
mai raug ka kraung naing.
Aux phan yuh ka pui naing;
kix viex kraung naing hu pauh ka pui.
17 Viang mawh nan,
ang koe kraung naing tix ceu kawx pa pon yuh maix laux leeing;
pui pa sang deuh phi map ka maix ku kaux,
maix hoik pon pawk ka lox kix.
Aux tag sang kia ngien ka siliex tix,
kieh kix pon pex ka daux lox prawng.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai yuh nin heue.
