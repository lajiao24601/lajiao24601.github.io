Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra
Simiang Saw-law-meung khiox khaw ka ping nyia cuyi
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 3.1–15)
1
Kawn simex simiang Ta-wue pa kaux Saw-law-meung kieh meung Yi-seu-liex pa giex tix kaux cong.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex nawh tawx buan ka nawh,
kieh nawh haok huan ting lhaong heue.
2 Simiang Saw-law-meung tawx lox ka can cao kaing pui naing kaing rheng kaux mai ka simiang naing kaing yieh kaux,
simiang ting haktiex,
simiang ceu pui,
khawm can kawn pui khawm uik
3 hoik hawt nawh ka dee yuh krax krub ka haktiex Ci-piien.
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka dee ot nyiex gox buan man dee an;
nyiex gox buan man in hoik mawh pa sum siliex Simiang Siyiex pa kaux Maw-si ka daux tiag daux tu. (
4 aTeug sijax mukheu ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
yam viex simiang Ta-wue teug sijax mukheu in hoek khaing Ci-liex Yie-ling,
nawh sum nyiex gox buan man,
son sang deuh teug sijax mukheu in ot daux.)
5 bKhu tawng pa hoik sum kawn saux Hu-eux kawn simex Wu-lix pa kaux Pi-sha-liex kawx ot ka Ci-piien,
sivoe ngai nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Saw-law-meung mai can kawn pui khawm uik ot tix krub Simiang Siyiex dee an.
6 Simiang krub ka Simiang Siyiex ka sivoe ngai nyiex gox buan man,
mai tan lu khawng brie tix rheng mu ka piang khu tawng,
son sang kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing heue.
7 Ka daux bo an,
Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka Saw-law-meung,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix sang kieh aux tawx ceu pa tix ka maix?”
8 Saw-law-meung pawk ah nin:
“Maix hoik chix kia ngien keeing aux Ta-wue ka daux krax muh pa ting tix,
yam in mai baox kieh aux plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh.
9 cSimiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix tawx lox pa ap maix tix deuh ka keeing aux.
Maix yaog aux mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing ka piang pui pa nie awm phawng tiex ka piang tiex yuh nan,
10 khiox khaw maix tawx aux koe ping nyia cuyi mai krax lhak lhiao,
son sang pon kuang mhawm aux kix.
Phan ang mawh nan,
aux doh sang pon kuang ka kawn pui cie maix tom nie yuh ka mawx lie?”
11 Siyiex pawk lox Saw-law-meung ah nin:
“In hoik mawh pa simie maix.
Maix ang bax khiox khaw tix mag mi,
koe sin ngao,
pa ao min,
mai ang bax khiox khaw tix yuh pui koeng yum,
phan ang mawh nan,
khiox khaw to kaux tix lang ju,
khiox khaw ngie ping nyia cuyi mai krax lhak lhiao khaing,
son sang pon giex maix kaing piang pui ceu aux,
pa mawh pa hoik yaog aux maix mawh simiang son sang kuang ka kawn pui.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang tawx krax lhak lhiao mai ping nyia cuyi ka maix.
Lheu khaing an,
aux kawx tag sang baox tawx sin ngao,
krax mag mi,
pa ao min,
kawn lheu khaing simiang pa koe sivoe an ku kaux,
khaix in kawx ang lai koe simiang pa lheu khaing maix tix kaux kawx heue.”
Siawp sibrawm krax naing mai sin ngao simiang Saw-law-meung
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 10.26–29)
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Saw-law-meung lih hu khaingd dee krub ka Ci-piien,
jauh ka sivoe nyiex gox buan man deeh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kuang kia ka pui Yi-seu-liex dee an.
14 eSaw-law-meung sum pui naing brug brung koe tix rheng pon yieh lhang mai pui naing brug brung tix mheen ra rheng kaux.
Nawh deuh kix tix blah ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa deuk an kix tix blah guah kix ot ka veng tang dauh.
15 Baux bawg yam giex nawh kaing,
mau khri Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik haoh nie awm simaox yuh nan,
mai khaox si oe bhawng kawx hoik haoh nie awm khaox liah khankix ka piang gawng iak yuh nan.
16 hPui pa yuh lai gee cie simiang pon giex gang ka brung pa viex laig ka Maw-cee-lif mai Ci-li-cia,g
17 khawm ling law pa hoek khaing Ai-cix.
Kix deeh coh brung mai ling law ka simiang pui Heux mai simiang pui Shui-li-yax khankix:
Ling law tix lhang coh liah yieh plah mau luah,
brung tix mu coh tix yieh tix phuan plah mau luah heue.
