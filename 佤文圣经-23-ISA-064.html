64
Simiang Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang riah ka rao ma,
mai hoek ka daux kawn pui lie?
Gawng lhaong yaox ka maix taug si yaung.
2 Kix si yaung si kua,
awm rawm rheep pa lok ka piang khix kroh ngu hax.
Khiox khaw maix jhi pa ting siawp sibrawm tix ka pui pa koeng,
mai kieh meung ka piang haktiex si yaung si kua ka plak sivoe ngai maix.
3 Maix hoik sax lih hoek ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa kieh pui lhat graung kah,
pa mawh ceu pa ang yix nyang sax yaox sax tawng;
gawng lhaong yaox ka maix tag si yaung kah.
4 aAng koe pui pa yaox,
phan ang mawh nan mhong ka Siyiex pa awm maix;
ang sax koe Siyiex tang kaux pa hoik sax yuh kaing ceu pa ting pa lhaong son pui pa yuh kaing tix yuh nin.
5 Maix khaw rhawm ka pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm,
pa ang piawm ka lox pa sidiex gau maix.
Maix song rhawm song rhi ka yix,
mai yix ca kawn yuh phi map;
viang maix hoik song rhawm,
mai yix ca kawn yuh krax phit krax lud pa hoik yuh pui tom dix dix heue.
6 Yix hoik koe phi map khawm uik,
pie kaing ceu pa mhawm pa yuh yix kawx hoik awm kraung ceep pa ang singax singeh yuh nan.
Mawh kheu phi map yix,
yix hoik awm lhax khaox kroh pa hoik phlu bhaung yuh nan.
7 Ang koe pui pa rawg buan son yix;
ang koe pui pa khiox khaw maix teem coi tix.
Mawh kheu phi map yix,
maix ang simi ka yix;
maix hoik tik vudb ka yix heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik mawh keeing ka yix.
Yix awm tiex glaox,
maix awm cang mai cao yuh;
yix hoik mawh pa teei maix taix tix phan yuh ka heue.
9 Khiox khaw maix paw vi song rhawm ka yix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi tiiem tix deuh ka phi map yix tom rheeng.
Yix hoik mawh pui cie maix khawm uik;
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka yix.
10 Veng pa singax singeh maix pa mawh veng Shi-ang hoik lih mawh dee riem;
Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik lih mawh dee riem.
11 Nyiex buan pa kied singax singeh,
pa kied mhawm an pa mawh haktiex dee hoik sax yaog yaw tax du yix maix dee an,
hoik gleeing laux leeing khawm uik.
Haktiex dee muh yix hoik laux leeing khawm uik heue.
12 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kaing in kix khawm uik ang bax chuh yuh rhawm rhi maix lie?
Simiang Siyiex eue,
ang maix hoek tix teem coi ka yix lie?
Yix ang lai pon kham gee ka krax kha rhawm in heue.
