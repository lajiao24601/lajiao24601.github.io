Ye-theu-law hoek tix jhak ka Maw-si
18
Paox Maw-si,
pa mawh Ye-theu-law cao giex kaing li Mi-tiien,
hoik mhong ka yam viex Siyiex kawn pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Ai-cix,
son can kaing pa yuh ka Maw-si mai ka kawn pui cie nawh,
2 atom hoek ka Maw-si,
viex khawm moeng nawh Si-phaw-la pa hoik deuh ot nyiex miex moeng nawh,
3 cmai kawn simex nawh ra kaux.
Ceu kaux kawn simex an kiex ra kaux tix kaux mawh Keu-soeng,b
mawh kheu hoik ah Maw-si nin:
“Aux hoik mawh nyo si nie daux haktiex pui tang ceu pa ang tix tawng;”
4 mai ceu kaux an tix kaux mawh Yi-li-yi-shiexd,
mawh kheu hoik ah Maw-si nin:
“Siyiex keeing aux teem coi ka aux,
taoh ka aux,
mai kieh simiang Ai-cix ang pauh ka aux.”
5 Ye-theu-law viex moeng Maw-si mai kawn simex nawh ra kaux hoek ka gawng singax,
pa mawh dee sum Maw-si nyiex bang ka daux tiag tan.
6 Ye-theu-law ran tawx lox ka Maw-si,
krai nawh mhong,
kix sang hoek.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si hu rieb ka nawh,
mai ngeuh kaing tix ka nawh,
ngheet nawh.
Khaix hoik lai chawk kix paox tix mhawm ot,
kix tom laig daux nyiex gox man Maw-si.
8 Maw-si krai paox tix Ye-theu-law mhong grawng pa taoh Simiang Siyiex ka kawn pui Yi-seu-liex,
grawng kaing pa hoik yuh ka piang simiang Ai-cix mai ka pui cie nawh khankix khawm uik.
Nawh dee baox krai ka paox tix grawng krax pa nu pa khawm pa si pup pui Yi-seu-liex hawt krax,
mai ka grawng pa taoh Simiang Siyiex ka kix yuh ka mawx.
9 Ye-theu-law mhong ka kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
10 Nawh ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
nawh hoik taoh pex lih khaing tawm taix simiang Ai-cix mai kawn pui cie nawh khankix!
11 Yam in aux hoik tawng Simiang Siyiex mawh pa ting khaing siyiex;
mawh kheu yam nu nai pui Ai-cix ka pex yuh nan,
nawh yuh kaing pa ting lih mawh heue.”
12 Khaix an paox Maw-si Ye-theu-law yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai khawng phak krax tan tang ceu tan lu ka Siyiex;
Ya-loeng mai kuad song simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
hoek ih priex preem pa singax singeh tau mai paox Maw-si,
mai kieh mawh kaing krax krub tix ceu ceu heue.
Laug ka cao prawng see
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 1.9–18)
13 Daux singaix khaix,
Maw-si ot tix prawng see simah kawn pui,
jauh pon ngawb tom hoek pon bo nawh kied ki.
14 Ye-theu-law yaox ka grawng an,
nawh tom ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Pa yuh maix son kawn pui mawh ceu pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh mawh tix tix kaux pa yuh kaing,
mai kieh pui hun in kix cung krox tix nghiet ka lox maix tin,
jauh tom pon ngawb tom hoek pon bo lie?”
15 Maw-si pawk ah nin:
“Aux tag caw tix yuh yuh nin,
kheu kawn pui hoek ka aux tin,
mawh son sang chawk cheng kix ka alo Siyiex.
16 Yam simah pui paox tix,
kix hoek sawg ka aux,
mai aux tag prawng son kix,
mawh cie mawx pa khaw,
mawh cie mawx pa ang khaw,
mai krai lox tat jhawm prawng mai alo Siyiex ka kix heue.”
17 Ye-theu-law ah nin:
“Ceu pa yuh maix ang mhawm;
18 maix khawm pui ceu cad maix tag taug mao kah.
Maix ang pon tix yuh kaing tom nie yuh nin nong nong.
19 Nghiet ka lox aux yam in,
aux sang tawx rhawm keud dong sawn ka maix,
kieh Siyiex ot tau mai maix.
Maix mawh pa yuh kaing son kawn pui,
mai viex see cawng kix hoek ka sivoe Siyiex,
mai krai ka nawh mawh pa khaw.
20 Viang mawh nan,
maix tag caw tix gau kix ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex mai lox tat jhawm prawng nawh,
mai krai kix mhong dee bleeh,
mai kieh kix tawng krax pa sang lawng tix mai kaing pa khaw kix tix yuh heue.
21 “Lheu khaing an,
maix laug ka pui pa kied chix tix blah yuh simiang piang pui Yi-seu-liex;
tix rheng kaux pui kieh lih koe simiang tix kaux,
khaix an tix yiex kaux,
tix phuan kaux,
kao kaux tang kieh lih koe simiang.
Kix tag mawh pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
pui pa kaux sijax,
pui pa ang rieb ka mau pa ang rauh ang see.
22 Maix kieh kix tang prawng ka kawn pui ku bawg ku yam.
See pa ting ceu khankix kix chix tix viex hoek ka sivoe maix,
phan mawh see pa iak pa lied khankix,
kieh kix tang prawng.
Yuh nin,
kix tom chix teem maix klawm beeh glawm,
mai kieh khaox glawm maix chaong.
23 Phan maix yuh mawh nin,
Siyiex kawx hoik tawx lox nhie maix yuh mawh nin,
mai maix tag ang yaox tix taug mao,
kheu nan see kawn pui tag pon prawng tix phai heue.”
24 Maw-si nghiet ka lox paox tix Ye-theu-law,
mai yuh awm pa ah nawh,
25 mai laug ka pui pa kied chix khaing ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
kieh kix yuh simiang kuang pui tix rheng kaux,
kuang pui tix yieh kaux,
kuang pui tix phuan kaux,
kuang pui kao kaux.
26 Kix tag sang prawng ka see kawn pui ku bawg ku yam;
see pa nu tix prawng kix tag sang viex hoek ka Maw-si,
see pa iak pa lied ku ceu kix tang prawng ka heue.
27 Khaix an,
Maw-si mai paox nawh Ye-theu-law guah ka paox tix,
mai Ye-theu-law deeh ing ka nyiex ma tix heue.
