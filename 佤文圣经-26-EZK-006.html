Simiang Siyiex ne raw pa krub ka riang rao
6
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix veeh ngai tix ka plak gawng Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai krai ka gum lox aux.
3 Tag caw tix kok yo gawng Yi-seu-liex bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka si niag gawng,
gawng iak pa koe dee lung,
gawng lhaong,
daux ngawd ah nin:
aux tag sang teei vaig gawn mai mug laux ka dee krub pui riang rao.
4 Tag sang yieh laux ka khu dee tan,
tag sang pauh max ka khu bhawng tan.
Pui pa ot ka sivoe khu dee tan khankix,
tag sang pauh kix yum ka sivoe riang rao an.
5 Aux tag sang kieh to kaux yum pui Yi-seu-liex prung prang tix,
mai prung prang si-ang kix ka praok khu dee tan khawm pon daog.
6 Can veng Yi-seu-liex pa koe khawm uik tag sang yuh laux leeing.
Yuh nin,
khu dee tan kix mai riang rao krub kix tag sang yuh laux leeing,
tag sang pauh max ka khu bhawng tan.
Can pa yuh kix hoik yuh laux khawm uik.
7 Pui hoik pauh pui yum ku dauh ku dee,
kheu nan pui pa kawn deuk khankix tag sang krai doh grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
8 “Aux tag sang kieh pui tix blah pon to tix klawd ju khaing daux krax pa pauh yum,
mai prung prang tix hoek ka meung haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
9 krax ot krax koe mawh pa vuk vied vuk viang.
Ka dee an,
kix tag sang doh keud ka aux,
mai tawng sidawng ka krax pa ciyang aux ka kix,
pa kieh kix hoek krax kaig krax tua hoik mawh kheu tik vud kix aux khaing daux rhawm tix,
ang cu lox aux,
mai muh riang rao krub an lheu khaing muh ka aux.
Kix ang rhawm yaox to kaux tix,
kheu hoik yuh tix pa caox pa caig,
mai kaing pa ang mhawm.
10 Kix tag sang tawng sidawng ka grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
lox pa hoik ne raw aux ka kix ang mawh lox pa giex pa going heue.”
11 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Maix tag caw tix su si tawm taix tix,
blauh caong tix,
mai yiiem son kaing pa caox pa yuh pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kaing pa seeing pa brai yuh kix.
Kix tag sang yum ka naing,
yum ka cai rhawm,
yum ka jhawm saux.
12 Pui pa vu vie ka dee singaix khankix tag sang yum ka jhawm saux;
pui pa ot dee dex khankix tag sang yum ka naing.
Pui pa kawn deuk khankix tag sang yum ka cai rhawm.
Aux tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix uik ka piang kix.
13 To kaux yum kix tag sang rhuat tix ot ka si nax riang rao krub an,
ka khu dee tan khawm pon daog,
ka gawng ting ku gawng,
ka piang choek gawng ku gawng,
ka greem khaox klai mai khaox yang yit ku gawng,
ka dee pa hoik tan lu kix ka riang rao krub an ku dauh.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
14 Aux tag sang nyeu taix tix yuh laux leeing ka meung haktiex kix.
Aux tag sang kieh haktiex an riem,
jauh ka daux tiag haktiex plak seh tom hoek ka veng Ti-paw-la-tha ka haktiex plak laong;
haktiex dee ot pui Yi-seu-liex ang koe dee ang riem tix dauh kawx.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
