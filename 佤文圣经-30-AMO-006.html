Pa laux leeing Yi-seu-liex
6
Pui pa ot ka Shi-ang pa yaox krax gaux rhawm,
pex laux leeing heue!
Pui pa ot ka gawng Sa-ma-li-yax pa ce tix nyawm ot,
pex hoik laux leeing heue!
Pex hoik ce tix mawh pa koe min ka daux meung ting Yi-seu-liex,
pon krax yung yi pui tang kaux heue!
2 Pex hu jhak tix yaox ka veng Cia-ni,
mai baox hoek ka veng ting Ha-ma,
khaix an hu lam lia ka veng Cia-theux pa mawh veng pui Fe-li-see.
Ka daux meung in kix,
koe veng mawx pa ang lheu ting riang khaing pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex lie?
Haktiex kix mawh dee mawx pa ang vieh va khaing haktiex pex lie?
3 Pex ce bawg yam singaix pa lih hoek krax laux leeing pa ting kawn ang bax hoek;
viang mawh nan,
kaing pa yuh pex deeh kieh pex laux leeing.
4 Hoik laux leeing heue!
Pex pui in kix it ka piang ku rhang sang,
neung tix nyawm ot ka piang dang ngeung pa awn,
mai ih ka nex moi awn mai ka nex yung kluing.
5 Pex rhax ka lai rhax pa khraox tiiem,
mai teei ting jung tauh mai lox rhax,
awm Ta-wue yuh nan.
6 Pex nyaux ka plai nyawm tix klong,
mai kraix sidah bhawng pa blawn ngawh ka to kaux tix,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang laux rhawm son laux leeing pui Yi-seu-liex.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
tag sang ran grheu pex hu;
krax gaux rhawm pex ka daux kaing pa ang khaw caw kawx tag sang uik phaox heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
pa mawh simiang kru naing tiex tu ah nin:
“Aux kied koeng ka krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi pui Yi-seu-liex;
aux ang rhawm yaox ka nyiex mhawm pa ot kix.
Aux tag sang ap tix tawx veng ting ot kix mai can ceu pa koe daux khawm uik ka pui pa koeng ka kix heue.”
9 Phan koe nyiex tix lhang pa deuk pui kao kaux,
pui kao kaux in kix kawx tag sang yum khawm uik.
10 Yam hoek paox nyiex pui pa yum an tix reu tix gleeing to kaux yum,
nawh tag sang krai ka pui pa mox tix daux nyiex an ah nin:
“Lheu khaing maix kawn koe pui mawx lie?”
Pui an pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang lai koe heue.”
Paox nyiex nawh ah nin:
“Si,
paw vi nye,
ang pon tix krai ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
11 Yam tawx Simiang Siyiex lox tix,
nyiex iak nyiex ting tag sang ghrup khawm uik,
mai lih mawh dee riem.
12 Brung sang chix to piang rang lie?
Moi sang chix pon thai daux rawm sigrit sigra lie?
Viang mawh nan,
pex hoik kieh pa khaw caw tieh tix lih mawh sidah awp,
mai kieh pa rauh pa see tieh tix lih mawh sidah song.
13 Pex yaw rang di ka daux pa pon pex tix ka Law Ti-paa.
Pex ah nin:
“Yix hoik pon kuang ka Cia-lingb ka daux pa een rhawm ka riang rao tix heue.”
14 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux tag sang kieh pui naing meung tang meung pon teei ka meung pex;
plak laong jauh ka diang moing gawng Ha-ma,
plak seh hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ya-la-pa,
kix tag sang hoek tix dug dig ka pex heue.”
