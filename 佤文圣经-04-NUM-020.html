Kaing pa lih ka daux haktiex Cia-ti-see
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 17.1–7)
20
Ka daux khix tix,
pui haoh hun Yi-seu-liex hoek ka daux tiag Shing,
mai sum nyiex bang ot ka haktiex Cia-ti-see.
Me-li-ang yum tan,
mai kix tom si paung nawh tan heue.
2 Dee sum kix nyiex bang ot,
rawm ang koe kah,
mai pui haoh pui hun khrawm paox tix hoek ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng dee an,
3 salao ah nin:
“Sivoe an,
paox ceu paox cad yix pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yum ka sivoe nyiex gox buan man;
yix cu tix yum tau mai kix.
4 Yuh ka mawx maix lai viex yix hoek ka daux tiag in lie?
Khex sang kieh maix yix yum tau mai khawng brie dee in lie?
5 Yuh ka mawx maix lai viex yix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
hoek ka haktiex ang tie mhawm?
Dee in ang koe nghox koi,
ang koe plix ruh,
plix siiet,
plix mak cang,
pie rawm kawx ang yaox tix nyaux!”
6 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng lih hu khaing pui hun,
mai cung ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man.
Kiex gup tix piang tiex;
pa riang min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka kiex heue.
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
8 “Teei khaox gix pa ot sivoe teug sijax mukheu an hoek.
Maix mai Ya-loeng khrawm pui hun,
si kah mai rang simaox sivoe kix,
rawm tag buing lih khaing rang simaox an.
Yuh nin,
maix chix tix kieh pui hun mai khawng brie nyaux ka rawm pa kaoh khaing rang simaox in.”
9 Maw-si hawt ka gum lox Simiang Siyiex teei khaox gix an hoek.
10 Nawh mai Ya-loeng khrawm pui hun hoek ka sivoe rang simaox.
Maw-si ah nin:
“Pex pa tieh vheen in kix nghiet kah!
Yiex khaw tix kieh rawm lih khaing daux rang simaox son sang tawx pex nyaux ka lie?”
11 Kheu nan,
Maw-si yaog ka khaox gix,
mai lauh ka rang simaox ra bawg,
koe rawm pa buing lih tom nie;
pui hun mai khawng brie nyaux ka heue.
12 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ne ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng ah nin:
“Mawh kheu ang pax yum rhawm ka aux,
ang yung yi ka siawp sibrawm pa ting aux ka plak sivoe ngai pui Yi-seu-liex,
pax tag ang pon tix viex pui hun laig ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap aux tix tawx ka kix.”
13 Kaing in lih mawh ka daux haktiex Mi-li-paa;
pui Yi-seu-liex salao ka Simiang Siyiex dee an,
Simiang Siyiex jhi kix yaox pa ting siawp sibrawm tix dee an heue.
Simiang Yi-tong ang cu pui Yi-seu-liex tiang ka haktiex tix
14 Maw-si ploi pui cau tix khaing Cia-ti-see hu ka simiang Yi-tong,
kix ah nin ka simiang Yi-tong:
“Paox nyiex dex maix pui Yi-seu-liex koe lox krai ka maix.
Maix hoik tawng kaing nu pa si pup yix.
15 Tax du yix hu yuh ka mawx ka Ai-cix;
yix hoik ot dee an tom rheung num.
Pui Ai-cix gut kheem ka tax du yix mai yix,
16 Yix riag yo Simiang Siyiex taoh teem ka yix.
Simiang Siyiex hoik mhong ka lox riag yix,
ploi kawn cau Siyiex viex yix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix.
Yam in yix hoik hoek ka praok haktiex maix pa mawh veng Cia-ti-see,
17 khiox khaw maix cu yix tiang ka haktiex maix.
Yix mai khawng brie yix ang chix lih hu khaing krax ting,
hu ka daux kaung ma mai si peem plix siiet;
yix kawx ang nyaux rawm ka daux dung rawm pex.
Yix hu ngie daux krax ting,
tom hoik luan khaing haktiex maix.”
18 Viang mawh nan,
simiang Yi-tong ah nin:
“Aux ang cu pex tiang ka haktiex cie tix!
Phan pex hoek,
aux tag kieh pui naing tieb tie ka pex.”
19 Pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Yix lawng ngie daux krax ting.
Phan yix mai khawng brie yix nyaux ka rawm maix,
yix chix tix tawx mau.
Yix lawng ngie daux krax khaing.”
20 Simiang Yi-tong baox ah nin:
“Ang chix!”
Kheu nan,
nawh kieh kru naing tix hoek puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
21 Mawh kheu simiang Yi-tong thiang pui Yi-seu-liex laig ka haktiex tix,
pui Yi-seu-liex veeh tix hu tang krax khaing heue.
Grawng yum Ya-loeng
22 Cub pui hun Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing Cia-ti-see,
mai hoek ka gawng Heu-eu;
23 gawng in ot ka praok Yi-tong.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng dee an:
24 “Ya-loeng tag ang pon tix hu ka haktiex pa ap aux tix tawx ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh tag yum,
mawh kheu pax ra kaux lieh lheu gum lox aux ka Mi-li-pa.
25 Maix tag viex Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh Yi-li-ya-sa haok ka gawng Heu-eu,
26 peeik kraung ceep Ya-loeng pa mawh kraung ceep cao giex kaing li dee an,
tawx Yi-li-ya-sa ceep.
Ya-loeng tag yum ka dee an.”
27 Maw-si yuh awm lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex.
Kix haok ka piang gawng Heu-eu plak sivoe ngai cub pui hun.
28 bMaw-si peeik kraung ceep Ya-loeng pa mawh kraung ceep cao giex kaing li,
tawx Yi-li-ya-sa ceep.
Ya-loeng tom yum ka piang choek gawng dee an;
Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa deeh lih khaing piang gawng.
29 Pui hun khankix hoik tawng yum Ya-loeng,
tom yiiem son nawh tix ngoe singaix heue.
