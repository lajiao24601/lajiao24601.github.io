Ye-sux kham krax nyaw krax glaw
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.1–13; Lai Lu-cia 4.11–13)
4
aKhaix an,
Khuan Singax Singeh viex Ye-sux hu daux tiag daux tu,
kham krax nyaw glaw Phi-caox.
2 Khaix hoik tuah ka priex preem tipon singaix tipon sawm,
Ye-sux cai rhawm.
3 Kaw pa nyaw glaw an hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh ah nin:
“Phan maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex,
tawx lox kieh simaox in tieh tix lih mawh khao mun heue!”
4 bYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Krax pa im ju pui ang joeng im ka priex preem nong nong,
mai hoik im ju kheu gum lox pa kaoh khaing daux jheeh Siyiex ku gum heue.’ ”
5 Phi-caox baox viex Ye-sux hoek ka veng pa singax singeh,
mai kieh nawh cung ka piang choek nyiex buan pa blawn lhaong,
6 cah nin ka nawh:
“Phan maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex,
taing maix lih khaing dee in heue.
Mawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Siyiex tag sang tawx lox ka kawn cau Siyiex son maix;
kix tag sang teei taix tix bluh ka maix,
kieh caong maix ang yaox tix sideeing mat ka piang simaox.”
7 dYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Viang mawh nan,
ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Ang pon tix nyaw glaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix heue.’ ”
8 Dee nghoik,
Phi-caox viex Ye-sux haok ka gawng lhaong tix mu,
mai jhi nawh yaox meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik mai ka min plak ngai pa mhawm meung an.
9 Phi-caox ah nin:
“Phan maix krub ka aux,
aux tag sang tawx in kix khawm uik ka maix heue.”
10 eYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Sa tan,
yoi heue!
Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Tag krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix,
tag krub ka nawh khaing.’ ”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Phi-caox lih hu khaing Ye-sux,
kawn cau Siyiex tom hoek tix khan kheu ka nawh heue.
Ye-sux jauh tix sidiex lox buan ka Cia-li-lix
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.14–15; Lai Lu-cia 4.14–15)
12 fYe-sux mhong grawng pa laung pui Yaw-hang ka daux thawng,
nawh tom hu khaing dee an,
mai hoek ka Cia-li-lix.
13 gNawh ang bax paung ot ka Na-sha-leux,
viang mawh nan,
paung ot ka veng Cia-pai-nong.
Veng an ot ka baoh haktiex Si-pu-loeng mai Na-fu-tha-lix,
pa dex ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix.
14 In hoik kieh lih tix mawh awm gum lox pa krai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax ah nin:
15 hHaktiex Si-pu-loeng mai Na-fu-tha-lix,
krax hawt rawm sigrit sigra,
ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak an,
Cia-li-lix pui tang ceu tang cad heue!
16 Pui pa ot ka daux pa faik
tag sang yaox ka pa riang ting;
pui pa ot ka greem veei krax yum,
pa riang tag sang jhan riang ka kix heue.
17 iJauh yam an,
Ye-sux sidiex gum lox ah nin:
“Loh rhawm rhi tix heue,
mawh kheu meung rao ma hoik dex sang hoek heue!”
Ye-sux kok pui rug kax pon kaux
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.16–20; Lai Lu-cia 5.1–11)
18 Ye-sux hu hawt praok rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix,
mai yaox ka pux aik ra kaux pa rug kax,
Si-meung (ceu kaux tang ceu Pi-teux) mai pux nawh Ang-teu-liex,
jiet kawn rug kax ka daux rawm nhawng.
19 Ye-sux ah nin ka kiex:
“Hawt khaix aux heue!
Aux tag sang kieh pax lih mawh pui rug kax pa pon ka pui heue.”
20 Kiex tik rub dum mawx dum an,
mai hawt khaix Ye-sux heue.
21 Ye-sux baox hu ka plak sivoe ngai,
mai baox yaox ka pux aik ra kaux pa mawh Ya-keux kawn simex Si-pi-thaix mai Yaw-hang;
kiex cox rug tau mai keeing tix ka piang reu.
Ye-sux kok kiex,
22 mai kiex tom prah reu dum mawx dum an,
mai krai lox pa tang hu khaing keeing tix,
hawt khaix Ye-sux heue.
Pa sidiex gau Ye-sux mai pa pai jhawm saux
(Lai Lu-cia 6.17–19)
23 jYe-sux tauh hu ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix khawm uik,
sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex sidiex ku dauh,
krai pao ka gum lox buan pa mhawm meung rao ma,
pai ka jhawm saux ku ceu moi ka daux kawn pui.
24 Ceu kaux nawh hoik tauh ka haktiex Shui-li-yax,
mawh kheu nan pui haktiex an viex pui pa koe jhawm saux ku ceu,
pa kham krax pa kha rhawm yuh nin:
pa mawh pui pa laig phi daux,
pui saux laung,
pui yum taix caong,
hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh khawm uik,
mai nawh pai kix moi ku kaux.
25 Pui hun kaoh khaing Cia-li-lix,
khaing See-yix,
khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
khaing Yo-thaix,
mai khaing baoh toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang hawt khaix nawh heue.
