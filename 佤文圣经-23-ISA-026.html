Siyiex kieh pui cie tix puing pex
26
Ka daux singaix an,
pui hun tag sang rhax lox rhax in ka haktiex Yo-tax ah nin:
guh veng ex kied sidaing kaux cong heue!
To kaux Siyiex hoik pau ka veng in.
2 Paoh ka siviex veng heue,
kieh meung pa rauh pa see laig heue!
Kieh pui pa kaux sijax laig heue!
3 Simiang Siyiex eue,
tawx krax pa nhim pa tiiem maix ka
pui pa viex rhawm cong,
tawx ka pui pa yum rhawm ka maix.
4 Tag caw yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex leeing ju;
nawh hoik kia ngien ka ex ku bawg ku yam.
5 Nawh hoik kieh pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi;
nawh hoik yuh veng pa kaux pa cong laux leeing,
hoik kieh guh veng pa kaux pa cong lih mawh haktiex dee riem.
6 Pui pa gut kheem pui tag sang hu ka piang haktiex dee riem.
Pui hot pui sibhawm tag sang dig ka guh veng pa hoik ghrup an heue.
 
7 Simiang Siyiex pa rauh see eue,
maix hoik kieh pui rauh pui see lawng daux krax pa si dawm;
maix hoik kieh pui rauh pui see lawng daux krax pa vieh pa va.
8 Yix hoik krox jun ka maix,
mai hawt ka alo maix;
ceu kaux maix mai siawp sibrawm maix mawh ceu pa simie rhawm rhi yix.
9 Yam pon sawm,
aux hoik sawg ka maix,
aux hoik simie ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
Yam ciyang maix ka haktiex,
kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
tom taug tawng ceu pa tix pa mawh pa rauh pa see.
10 Viang maix hoik koe krax caox rhawm ka pui pa caox,
kix kawx ang sax gau tix yuh ka kaing ceu pa mhawm.
Viang haktiex in hoik mawh cie pa khaw caw kawx,
kix kawn ca yuh ka kaing ceu pa ang mhawm;
kix ang doh keud ka pa hoik mawh maix pa ting pa lhaong heue.
11 aPui pa koeng ka maix ang tawng maix sang ciyang ka kix.
Kieh kix kaig tua kheu yaox ka grawng pa kied muh maix ka pui cie tix;
kieh kix yaox krax ciyang pui hoik dawg rien maix son kix heue.
 
12 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh yix haok huan;
can kaing pa pon yuh yix lih khawm uik hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing maix.b
13 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
yix hoik sax kham krax pa giex gang pui;
viang mawh nan,
mawh ngie maix pa mawh simiang yix tix kaux khaing.
14 Kix hoik yum,
mai ang lai chix deeh kaoh im,
mawh kheu hoik ciyang maix ka kix,
mai yuh kix laux leeing.
15 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik kieh meung yix pon krax pa haok huan,
hoik kieh haktiex yix vieh va;
ceu kaux maix mawh pa ting min,
tom si dauh sidu.
16 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik sax ciyang ka pui cie tix sivoe an;
kix hoik khiox khaw ka maix ka daux krax pa kha rhawm.
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix kieh yix kok yo,
hoik saux awm yam sang keh bun miex,
riag yo lu lax,
18 yix kawx hoik sax saux,
hoik sax lu lax,
viang mawh nan,
mai ang tie bax lih keh heue.
Yix ang nyang sax puing pex naing son meung tix bawg kawx;
yix ang nyang sax yuh buan son son pui haktiex tix bawg kawx.
19 cViang mawh nan,
paox ceu paox cad yix pa hoik yum tag sang kaoh im;
to kaux kix baox deeh koe ju.
Pui pa it ka daux si meeig tag sang haing,
mai rhax lox pa ting si num;
jiet awm rawm cie pa viex ka bhawm pa im hoek ka piang tiex yuh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pui pa hoik yum deeh kaoh im heue.
Krax prawng ca mai krax haok huan
20 Paox ceu paox cad aux eue,
laig ka daux nyiex pex heue!
Nyeep siviex,
mox tix tix vud tix cu ha,
krox tom grai krax pa song rhawm Siyiex.
21 Simiang Siyiex tag sang lih khaing nyiex dee ot tix piang rao ma,
mai ciyang ka phi map pui haktiex.
Krax pa pauh pui paox tix ka piang haktiex tag sang jhi lih khawm uik;
ka piang haktiex mai ang lai baox pon kheem tix mox ka to kaux pui yum heue.
