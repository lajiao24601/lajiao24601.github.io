Lox rawg buan krax teem krax coi
35
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix tieb tie ka pui pa tieb tie ka aux khankix;
khiox khaw maix puing ka pui ka puing ka aux khankix!
2 Khiox khaw maix teei dieb tie naok pa iak pa ting,
hoek tix teem coi ka aux.
3 Khiox khaw maix yaog ka gawng pliah mai lawm moe maix,
rieb tix puing ka pui koeng pa leue ka aux.
Khiox khaw maix krai ka aux ah nin:
maix sang taoh ka aux heue.
 
4 Kieh pui pa sang pauh ka aux laux rhawm kaig tua!
Kieh pui pa keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing laux rhawm som nai!
5 Yam leue kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ka kix,
kieh kix awm kam riex pa phlu bhaung.
6 Yam pauh kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ka kix,
kieh krax hu kix faik nang nyu nang heue.
 
7 Kix ang koe krax koe grawng mai nih tix taong rub ka aux;
kix kaong daux o sang giex ka aux.
8 Kieh krax laux krax leeing hoek piang kix dum mawx dum an;
kieh kix deeh krix ka rub pa taong tix,
mai cawd ka daux o pa kaong tix heue.
 
9 Yam an,
aux sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu hoik taoh nawh ka aux,
mai aux tom gaux rhawm.
10 Aux sang ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
pui mawx pon tiag tix mai maix?
Maix hoik kia ngien ka pui pa iak riang luat luan khaing krax pa map nai pui;
maix hoik kia ngien ka pui hot pui sibhawm luat luan khaing krax pa tud rhim pui heue.
 
11 Pui pa caox pa caig yuh ka mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh,
ne raw ka phi map pa ang aux sax yuh.
12 Kix pawk pa caox son pa mhawm,
kieh aux kha rhawm tix ang koe lox sang krai.
13 Viang mawh nan,
yam kham kix ka jhawm saux,
aux ceep ka kraung man keei sum laux rhawm son kix,
aux tuah priex preem,
mai num kaing tix rawg buan son kix;
14 aux kia ngien ka kix,
awm kia ngien ka paox grawm tix,
pux aik tix yuh nan,
aux kieh to kaux tix laux rhawm,
awm yam laux rhawm pui son miex tix heue.
 
15 Kheu nan,
yam si pup aux ka krax pa kha rhawm,
kix khrawm tix ot tau,
mai gaux rhawm ka si pup aux krax laux krax leeing,
mai nyiah ka aux;
pui pa ang aux tawng lauh ka aux,
mai puing ka aux ang jud ang jai.
16 Kix awm pui pa lang teung caox mai map nyiah ka pui chawh,
kix greeik rhang braing tix ka aux,
mai koeng khoi ka aux heue.
 
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
sang kawn ngawm maix tix jhak mai ang simi ka aux hoek yam mawx lie?
Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing krax pa tuk tiak tuk te kix,
taoh ju aux khaing daux jheeh phung sivai sang si in kix heue!
18 Yuh nin,
aux sang yaog yaw maix ka daux pa khrawm pui hun tix;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix plak sivoe ngai pui haoh pui hun heue.
 
19 aPaw vi kieh pui koeng pa pie lhien khankix gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu krax pa laux rhawm som nai aux.
Paw vi kieh pui pa ang koe krax koe grawng tix koeng ka aux khankix khiep ngai tix ka aux.
 
20 Kix ang krai lox pa kieh pui khaw rhawm paox,
kieh pui pa muh krax pa nhim pa tiiem laux rhawm ka lox pa pie lhien tix.
21 Kix ang jheeh tix krai lox pa ang mawh ang meh ah nin:
A ha,
ngai ex hoik pon tix yaox heue!
22 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik yaox kah,
khiox khaw maix paw vi yien yawg tix ang nye;
cao aux eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi cung tix jhak aux ka dee singai,
mai ang simi ka aux.
23 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kaoh haing krai lox son aux;
cao aux eue,
khiox khaw maix kaoh tix prawng rauh see son aux.
24 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix hawt awm pa khaw caw maix prawng aux ang koe phi map;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh pui pa koeng ka aux yaw rang di ka aux.
25 Paw vi kieh kix keud ka daux rhawm tix ah nin:
a ha,
in hoik mawh pa simie ex tix cie heue!
Paw vi kieh kix ah nin:
ex hoik bleet ka nawh heue!
 
26 Kieh pui pa gaux rhawm ka si pup aux ka krax laux krax leeing laux rhawm som nai heue!
Kieh pui pa ce tix ting riang khaing aux an kix laux rhawm kaig tua heue!
 
27 Kieh pui pa nyawm rhawm kheu yaox grawng pa prawng aux ang koe phi map an kix
gaux rhawm tix rhax lai khawm uik;
Kieh kix ah nin ang jud ang jai:
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex siliex pui an pa mhawm kaing yuh,
pa pon tix yaox ka krax gaux rhawm,
tag sang khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw.
28 Yuh nin,
aux tom krai pao krax pa khaw caw maix;
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix leeing lang singaix heue.
