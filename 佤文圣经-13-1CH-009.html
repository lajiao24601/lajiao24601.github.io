Pui pa deeh hoek khaing vu vie tix
9
Can pui Yi-seu-liex khawn uik hoik hawt awm cub pui nyiex tix,
mai tiiem tix deuh ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Pui Yo-tax kheu yuh phi map hoek krax ciyang,
mai giex kix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng heue.
2 aPa jauh tix ran deeh ing ka veng tix,
pa ot ka haktiex brex brung sin ngao tix mawh pui Yi-seu-liex,
cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
mai pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan.
3 Ceu pui Yo-tax,
Piien-ya-ming,
Yi-fa-liien,
mai Ma-na-si khankix,
ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
4-6 Ceu pui Yo-tax koe liah yieh tix dim nyiex pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Ka daux doeh ceu cad kawn simex Yo-tax pa kaux Fa-leu-see,
lih koe Wu-thaix pa mawh simiang kix.
Wu-thaix mawh kawn simex Ya-mi-fu,
kawn saux Ang-lix;
tax du nawh kawn koe mawh Ying-lix mai Pa-ni heue.
Ka daux doeh ceu cad kawn simex Yo-tax pa kaux See-law,
lih koe Ya-soi-ya pa mawh simiang kix.
Ya-soi-ya mawh simiang cub nyiex kix heue.
Ka daux doeh ceu cad kawn simex Yo-tax pa kaux Shie-la,
lih koe Yie-wu-lix pa mawh simiang kix heue.
7-8 Ceu kaux ceu pui Piien-ya-ming pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung tiiem tix plak greem:
Sa-lux,
pa mawh kawn simex Mi-su-lang,
kawn saux Heu-ta-we-yax,
kawn sex Ha-si-nux.
Yi-pi-ni-ya,
pa mawh kawn simex Yie-law-hang.
Yi-la,
pa mawh kawn simex Wu-si,
kawn saux Mi-ci-lix.
Mi-su-lang,
pa mawh kawn simex See-fa-thi-ya,
kawn saux Lio-eu,
kawn sex Yi-pi-ni-ya.
9 Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming koe dim yieh tix gliah nyiex pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Pui pa tiiem ceu kaux ot plak piang hoik mawh cao kaing cub nyiex kix heue.
Cao giex kaing li pa ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
10-12 Plak greem mawh cao giex kaing li pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung:
Yie-ta-ya,
Yie-heu-ya-lix,
Ya-cing,
Ya-sa-li-ya (kawn simex Si-leu-cia cao kaing pa kuang nyiex buan;
tax du nawh mawh Mi-su-lang,
Sa-tu,
Mi-la-yaw,
Ya-si-thu),
Ya-ta-ya (kawn simex Yie-law-hang.
Tax du nawh mawh Pa-see-hu-eux,
Ma-ci-ya),
Ma-sai (kawn simex Ya-ti-yiex;
tax du nawh mawh Ya-si-si-la,
Mi-su-lang,
Mi-seu-li-mix,
Ying-mai heue).
13 Pa mawh cao giex kaing li cub nyiex cao kaing koe tix rheng aliah yieh tix gliah kaux;
kix hoik mawh pui pa mhawm yuh kaing ka daux nyiex buan khawm uik heue.
Pui Li-we pa ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
14-16 Plak greem mawh pui Li-we pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung:
See-ma-ya (kawn simex Ha-sux;
tax du nawh mawh Ya-li-kang,
Ha-sa-pi-ya,
mawh ceu pui cub nyiex Mi-la-lix),
Pa-pa-cia,
He-leu-see,
Cia-la,
Ma-thang-ya (kawn simex Mi-cia;
tax du nawh mawh Si-ci-lix,
Ya-sa),
Eu-pa-tix (kawn simex See-ma-ya.
Tax du nawh mawh Cia-la,
Yie-tu-toeng),
Pi-li-cia (kawn simex Ya-sa;
kawn saux Yi-li-cia-na;
kix ot ka haktiex veng Ni-thaw-fax heue).
Pui cao pau nyiex buan pa ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
17 Plak greem mawh pui cao pau nyiex buan pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung:
Sa-long,
Ya-kux,
Ta-meung,
Ya-si-mang.
Sa-long mawh simiang kix.
18 Ceu pui cub nyiex in kix ka daux bawg yam an,
kix yie sum tix ot ka dee laig diang siviex simiangb plak lih singaix jheeng jheeng,
son sang pau kah;
sivoe an,
kix hoik mawh pui pa pau ka diang siviex nyiex bang Li-we heue.
19 Kawn saux Yi-pi-ya-sha,
kawn simex Kheu-lix pa kaux Sa-long,
tau mai pux aik tix ceu pui Kheu-la khankix,
mawh cao pa pau ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man;
tax du kix hoik mawh cao pa pau ka nyiex gox bang man Simiang Siyiex.
20 Kawn simex Yi-li-ya-sa pa kaux Fe-ni-ha,
hoik mawh cao kaing kix sivoe an.
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai nawh heue.
21 Kawn simex Mi-see-li-mi-ya pa kaux Sa-cia-li-ya kawx hoik mawh nang cao pa pau ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex heue.
22 Pui pa hoik laug lih mawh cao pau diang siviex khankix,
khawm uik koe ra yieh kao ra kaux.
Kix hoik hawt awm nyiex yaong pa ot tix ka daux,
mai tiiem lih ka ca rang cub nyiex kix.
Simiang Ta-wue mai cao pa chix yaox sivoe Sa-mu-eu hoik tawx kaing pa ting ceu in ka kix.
23 Kix mai doeh ceu cad kix,
mai plud tix mawh cao pa pau ka diang siviex nyiex buan.
24 Koe siviex plak lih singaix,
siviex plak klip singaix,
siviex plak seh,
siviex plak laong,
tix mu siviex tix kaux cao kaing pui pau.
25 Pui cao pa pau in kix,
hoik kieh mawh pui nyiex kix pa ot ka daux nyiex yaong kix pa teem coi kix,
mai lai loh tix pau,
tix bawg pau aliah singaix.
26 Cao kaing pui pau pon kaux hoik mawh pui Li-we khawm uik,
mai kix koe beeh glawm ting son kaing an.
Kix kawx koe beeh glawm tix mawh cao pa kuang kia ka daux nyiex buan mai nyiex krao.
27 Kix paung ot ka sivie siveng nyiex buan;
mawh kheu hoik koe beeh glawm kix tix mawh cao pa pau ka nyiex buan,
ku ngaix pon ngawb cao,
kix mawh cao pa paoh ka siviex nyiex buan heue.
Pui Li-we tang kaux khankix
28 Pui Li-we tang kaux khankix,
mawh cao pa koe beeh glawm tix kuang kia ka kraung siao yam yuh ka krax krub.
Yam siao ka kraung in kix ku bawg,
teei lih ang ah,
deeh deuh ang ah,
kix tag caw tix simin sidawng.
29 Pui Li-we pa deuk khankix mawh cao pa kuang kia ka kraung daux nyiex buan mai phawng mien nghox ca,
plai,
bee sime,
nyawng bhawng,
sidah bhawng khankix.
30 Viang mawh nan,
cao pa koe beeh glawm tix yuh ka kaing pa klao sidah bhawng mawh cao giex kaing li heue.
31 Pui Li-we kawn ai Sa-long ceu pui Kheu-la pa kaux Ma-tha-thi-ya,
koe beeh glawm tix mawh cao pa rien khre tix tong ka khawng tanc.
32 Ceu pui Keu-siax khankix koe beeh glawm tix mawh cao pa rien khre ka khao mun pa sang lod nyiex buan daux singaix tuah heue.
33 Pui Li-we tix blah koe beeh glawm tix mawh cao pa kuang ka lox rhax daux nyiex buan.
Cao kaing cub nyiex pui in kix,
paung ot ka daux nyiex pa koe daux nyiex buan tix blah;
kix ang yuh kaing tang ceu,
mawh kheu kix tag caw tix yuh ka kaing daux nyiex buan leeing ngaix leeing sawm heue.
34 Pui pa hoik tiiem ot plak piang khankix,
hawt ka cub nyiex kix,
hoik mawh cao kaing ka daux nyiex pui Li-we ku nyiex;
kix paung ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik heue.
Tax du simiang Saw-law-meung mai doeh ceu cad nawh
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 8.29–38)
35 Yie-lix pang ka veng Ci-piien,
mai paung ot tan.
Ceu kaux moeng nawh mawh Ma-cia.
36 Ceu kaux kawn ai nawh mawh Ya-paw-toeng;
ceu kaux kawn simex nawh tang kaux mawh Su-eux,
Ci-see,
Pa-lix,
Ni-eu,
Na-ta,
37 Ci-to,
Ya-si-yaw,
Sa-cia-li-ya,
Mi-ci-lawx.
38 Mi-ci-lawx mawh keeing See-mi-ang.
Doeh ceu pui kix paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ang lai singai ot khaing cub nyiex kix,
pa mawh pui tang nyiex khankix heue.
39 Ni-eu keh Ci-see;
Ci-see keh Sao-law.
Sao-law koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Yaw-na-tang,
Me-ji Su-yax,
Ya-pi-na-ta,
Yi-see Pa-lixe.
40 Yaw-na-tang keh Mi-li Pa-lix;
Mi-li Pa-lixf keh Mi-cia.
41 Mi-cia koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Pi-toeng,
Mi-leux,
Tha-li-yax,
Ya-ha-seeg.
42 Ya-ha-see keh Ya-la.
Ya-la koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Ya-la-miiex,
Ya-see-ma-we,
Sing-lix.
Sing-lix keh Maw-sha;
43 Maw-sha keh Pi-ni-yax;
Pi-ni-yax keh La-fax;
La-fax keh Yi-li-ya-sa;
Yi-li-ya-sa keh Ya-si heue.
44 Ya-si koe kawn simex liah kaux,
pa mawh Ya-see-li-kang,
Paw-ci-lux,
Yi-seu-ma-lix,
See-ya-li-ya,
Eu-pa-ti-ya,
Ha-nang heue.
