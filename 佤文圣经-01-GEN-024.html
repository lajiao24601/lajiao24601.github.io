Yi-sha teei tix koe moeng
24
Yam in,
Ya-paw-la-hang hoik kuad,
Simiang Siyiex nawb buan son ka kaing yuh nawh ku ceu heue.
2 Ya-paw-la-hang kok ka siliex pa riex ju daux nyiex tix,
pa kuang ka can pa koe nawh khawm uik mai ah nin:
“Deuh taix maix si nax ba aux.a
3 Aux tag kieh maix tiex tu ka sivoe ngai Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex cao rao ma mai haktiex,
maix ang pon tix teei kawn bun ceu pui Cia-nang ka dee ot ex tin,
son sang mawh moeng kawn simex aux heue;
4 yuh ka mawx maix deeh ing ka haktiex ot aux,
deeh ot paox ceu paox cad aux,
mai teei kawn simex aux Yi-sha koe moeng tix kaux heue.”
5 Viang mawh nan,
siliex an ah nin:
“Phan kawn bun ang cu tix hawt khaix aux hoek daux meung in,
aux sang yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh sang kieh maix aux viex kawn simex tix deeh ing khaing dee kaoh tix lie?”
6 Ya-paw-la-hang pawk ah nin:
“Paw doh viex kawn simex aux hu tan!
7 Simiang Siyiex rao ma hoik viex aux lih khaing nyiex ma keeing aux,
mai khaing haktiex dee ot ceu pui aux,
nawh hoik tiex tu son sang tawx haktiex in ka doeh ceu cad aux.
Nawh tag ploi kawn cau Siyiex hu sivoe maix,
maix tag yaox tix viex bun tix kaux son kawn simex aux khaing tan heue.
8 Phan bun an ang cu tix hawt khaix maix,
maix hoik pon luat luan khaing lox pa tiex tu tix.
Viang mawh nan,
paw doh viex kawn simex aux hu ka dee an heue.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
siliex nawh deuh taix tix si nax ba simiang tix Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai tiex tu hawt tix yuh awm lox tawm Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
10 Siliex an laug ka-la-uk cie simiang tix khaing daux cub kao mu,
mai viex ka kraung khrai mau khri cie simiang tix ku ceu hu ka meung Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax plak caong ma laong ka veng dee ot Na-heux heue.
11 Nawh hoek ka haktiex an mai kieh ka-la-uk khankix grux ngawng tix plak praix veng dex rawm maw tan,
yam an praix hoik boi faik,
jiet mawh bawg yam hoek kin bun miex khankix rawm.
12 Nawh rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex cao aux Ya-paw-la-hang eue,
kieh krax pa keud aux grum naog daux in singaix;
khiox khaw maix giex kaux cong ka lox pa ap maix ka cao simiang aux Ya-paw-la-hang liak.
13 Aux cung dex rawm maw yam in,
mai cub kawn bun krih pui veng an hoek tix kin rawm.
14 Aux tag sang ah nin ka kawn bun pa koe daux kix tix kaux:
‘Khiox khaw tah si ngien maix mai tawx aux nyaux rawm liak.’
Phan nawh ah nin:
‘Nyaux heue!
Aux tag baox tawx ka-la-uk maix nyaux!’
Bun an tag mawh pa hoik tawx maix ka siliex tix Yi-sha.
Yuh nin,
aux tag tawng maix giex kaux cong ka lox pa ap maix ka cao simiang aux heue.”
15 Yam ang nawh nyang tien hoik rawg buan,
Li-pai-cia hoik hoek ka praok rawm maw,
piang klip nawh klawm ka si ngien rawm.
Nawh mawh kawn bun Pi-thu-lix;
Pi-thu-lix mawh kawn simex Mi-cia mai Na-heux,
pux Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
16 Li-pai-cia mawh kawn bun kied sidaing mhawm grang tix kaux,
mai kawn mawh kawn bun krih,
pa ang sax piet sax pha simex tix bawg kawx;
nawh lih hu ka praok rawm maw,
mai kin rawm daux si ngien naog mai deeh viex hoek heue.
17 Siliex an nied tix to hu ka nawh,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Khiox khaw tawx aux nyaux rawm kin maix ceeix liak!”
18 Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
nyaux heue”.
Khaix an nawh nied tix tah si ngien rawm khaing klip tix,
mai tawx siliex an nyaux heue.
19 Khaix hoik tawx nawh siliex an nyaux rawm,
Li-pai-cia ah nin:
“Aux kawx kin tix tawx ka-la-uk maix khankix nyaux rawm,
mai tawx kix nyaux sak.”
20 Khaix an,
nawh nied tix hu sau rawm ka daux dawng mai deeh to tix kin rawm,
son sang yaox ka-la-uk khankix tix nyaux sak khawm uik.
21 Pui an bre tix jhak ka nawh,
mai keud tix yaox,
sang mawh ang mawh kaing pa kieh Simiang Siyiex nawh yuh hoik.
22 Khaix hoik nyaux ka-la-uk rawm,
pui an teei krim khri meeh tix mu pa kied ngawh,
cieh ka meeh kawn bun an,
mai baox teei ble khri ra mu,
jhup ka plawng taix nawh khawm ra khawn.
23 Pui an chawk:
“Mawh maix kawn bun nyiex mawx,
keeing maix mawh mawx?
Krai aux mhong liak,
koe lawg dee sang paung yix daux nyiex keeing maix lie?”
24 Nawh deeh pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mawh kawn bun Pi-thu-lix kawn simex Mi-cia,
pa keh khaing Na-heux.
25 Nyiex yix hoik koe brex nghox mai priex preem tom nie,
mai lawg sang paung pex kawx koe.”
26 Khaix an,
pui an grux ngawng tix mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
27 Nawh ah nin:
“Yaog yaw buan son Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex simiang aux Ya-paw-la-hang.
Nawh hawt tix yuh ka lox pa ap tix ka simiang aux.
Nawh hoik thung aux hu ka paox nyiex paox ma simiang aux heue.”
28 Khaix an kawn bun an to tix ing krai grawng in kix ka miex tix mai pui daux nyiex tix.
29 Yam an pux ke Li-pai-cia pa mawh La-pang to hu ka rawm maw dee ot siliex Ya-paw-la-hang dee an.
30 Yam yaox nawh krim khri mau mai ble khri khankix ka taix nan tix,
hoik mhong khaing nan tix lox pa krai pui an khawm uik.
Yam an,
siliex Ya-paw-la-hang ca cung ka praok dung rawm,
cung ka praok ka-la-uk.
31 Nawh ah nin:
“Hoek heue,
maix mawh pui pa pon buan son khaing Simiang Siyiex;
maix lai cung plak praix yuh ka mawx?
Mawh kheu aux hoik khre rien nyiex son pex,
khawm dee sang ot ka-la-uk khankix heue.”
32 Khaix an,
kawn cau an laig daux nyiex,
La-pang tah an ka-la-uk,
mai tawx ka-la-uk sawm brex.
Khaix an,
nawh teei tix tawx rawm ka siliex Ya-paw-la-hang mai pui pa hu tau mai nawh khoek caong tix.
33 Kix hoek khan khre priex preem,
pui an ah nin:
“Krox aux krai lox pa tawx simiang aux,
aux tom taug sawm!”
La-pang ah nin:
“Krai heue.”
34 Pui an tom ah nin:
“Aux mawh siliex Ya-paw-la-hang.
35 Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx buan son pa kied ting ka cao simiang aux tom nie,
kieh nawh hoik mawh pui pa ting min mag mi;
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx phung moi,
phung yung,
mau khri,
simex siliex,
bun siliex,
ka-la-uk,
mai la khankix ka nawh.
36 Moeng cao simiang aux Sa-la,
keh kawn simex tix kaux son nawh yam riex ju tix;
mai simiang aux hoik tawx can ceu pa koe tix khawm uik ka kawn simex tix.
37 Simiang aux kieh aux tiex tu mai ah nin:
‘Maix ang pon tix teei kawn simex aux moeng ka bun pui haktiex Cia-nang.
38 Maix caw tix hu ka nyiex ma keeing aux,
ka ceu pui aux,
mai teei tix viex moeng tix kaux,
son kawn simex aux.’
39 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ah nin ka simiang tix:
‘Phan kawn bun an ang cu tix hawt khaix aux,
aux sang yuh ka mawx lie?’
40 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
‘Simiang Siyiex pa hoik hawt aux grawng tag ploi kawn cau tix hu tau mai maix,
tom hoik pon yuh maix kaing yuh tix.
Maix tag yaox tix viex moeng tix kaux,
son kawn simex aux,
khaing daux doeh ceu cad aux,
mai khaing daux nyiex ma keeing aux hoek.
41 Ca mawh hoek maix ka doeh ceu cad aux,
in hoik mawh hawt maix tix yuh lox pa tiex tu tix,
phan kix ang tawx kawn bun an ka maix,
maix tag luat luan khaing lox pa tiex tu tix heue.’
42 “Daux in singaix,
yam hoek aux ka praok rawm maw,
aux rawg buan ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang simiang aux eue!
Rawg maix cox daux krax pa hu aux grum mhawm liak.
43 Aux cung praok rawm maw,
bawg yam hoek bun krih tix kin rawm,
aux khiox khaw nawh tawx aux nyaux rawm khaing daux si ngien cie nawh.
44 Phan nawh cu,
mai baox cu tix kin rawm tawx Ka-la-uk aux nyaux,
aux simie nawh mawh moeng kawn simex simiang aux pa laug maix son nawh.’
45 Sivoe ang aux nyang tien hoik keud daux rhawm tix grawng in,
Li-pai-cia hoek mai klawm si ngien rawm piang klip tix,
mai lih hoek ka praok rawm maw kin rawm.
Aux tom ah nin ka nawh:
‘Tawx aux nyaux rawm ceeix.’
46 Nawh tah si ngien rawm tix phai phai khaing klip tix,
mai ah nin:
‘Nyaux heue;
aux kawx tag baox kin rawm mai tawx ka-la-uk maix nyaux.’
Aux tom nyaux,
nawh kawx kin rawm tawx ka-la-uk aux nyaux.
47 “Aux chawk ka nawh:
‘Keeing maix mawh pui mawx?’
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
‘Keeing aux mawh Pi-thu-lix kawn simex Na-heux mai Mi-cia.’
Aux tom sau krim khri meeh mai ble khankix ka taix nawh.
48 Aux grux ngawng tix mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai tawx krax yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang aux Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang;
pa hoik jhi aux yaox daux krax pa rauh pa see,
son sang teei kawn saux bun pux aik simiang cao aux son kawn simex nawh koe moeng.
49 Yam in,
phan pex viex rhawm rauh see ka cao simiang aux,
tom krai ka aux;
ang pon yuh,
mai krai ka aux nang,
son sang but aux rhawm tix heue.”
50 La-pang mai Pi-thu-lix pawk lox mai ah nin:
“Kaing in mawh pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex,
ex ang chix krai ka maix mhawm ang mhawm.
51 Li-pai-cia hoik ot tin,
maix chix tix viex nawh hu,
kieh nawh mawh moeng kawn simex cao simiang tix,
awm lox pa hoik ah Simiang Siyiex.”
52 Yam hoik mhong siliex Ya-paw-la-hang lox krai kix,
nawh tom grux ngawng tix ka piang tiex mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
53 Kawn cau an teei kraung mau kraung khri khawm kraung ceep khankix mai tawx ka Li-pai-cia,
tawx khawng phak pa ngawh pa mhawm ka prie nawh mai miex nawh.
54 Yuh nin,
siliex Ya-paw-la-hang mai pui pa hu tau mai nawh,
ih tau nyaux tau mai paung tan daux sawm an.
Yam kaoh kix pon ngawb cao cao ngaix khaix mai nawh ah nin:
“Kieh aux deeh ing ka cao simiang tix liak.”
55 Prie nawh mai miex nawh ah nin:
“Kieh kawn bun in ot mai yix kao ngaix ha,
khaix an nawh pon tix hu heue.”
56 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Paw nyang lai kieh aux glah bawg yam liak,
awm hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex krax hu aux grum mhawm;
kieh aux deeh ing ka cao simiang tix heue.”
57 Kix baox pawk lox ah nin:
“Yix sang chawk tix yaox ka rhawm kawn bun in ha,
sang ah nawh ka mawx!”
58 Khaix an,
kix kok Li-pai-cia mai chawk ah nin ka nawh:
“Cu maix tix hu mai pui in lie?”
Nawh deeh ah nin:
“Aux cu.”
59 Mawh kheu nan,
kix ploi Li-pai-cia mai miex eei nawh,
hu tau mai siliex Ya-paw-la-hang mai pui pa hoek tau mai nawh heue.
60 Kix nawb buan ka Li-pai-cia mai ah nin ka nawh:
Kieh nan ex mawh miex ka pui kaing rheng mai kaing mheen!
Kieh simie ceu pui maix pon kuang ka siviex veng pui pa koeng ka kix heue!
61 Li-pai-cia mai bun siliex pa priex nawh khankix rien khre tix mhawm,
mai brug ka-la-uk.
Siliex Ya-paw-la-hang tom viex Li-pai-cia yaog tix hu heue.
62 Yam in,
Yi-sha gah tix hoek ka daux tiag Pi-eu La-hai Laib mai ot ka Cia-nang plak seh.
63 Yam sang boi faik praix,
nawh hu lai ling daux kaung,
mai maong tix jhak,
nawh yaox ka cub ka-la-uk pa hoek khankix heue.
64 Li-pai-cia maong tix jhak mai yaox ka Yi-sha,
nawh lih khaing ka-la-uk brug tix.
65 Chawk siliex Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Mawh pui mawx pa lai ling daux kaung son sang rieb ka ex lie?”
Siliex pawk ah nin:
“Nawh mawh kawn simiang aux.”
Li-pai-cia tom teei man tawb mai tawb pak ngai tix ka heue.
66 Siliex an krai grawng ku ceu pa hoik yuh tix ka Yi-sha heue.
67 Yi-sha rieb Li-pai-cia laig daux nyiex gox man miex tix Sa-la,
mai teei Li-pai-cia mawh moeng tix.
Yi-sha kied muh nawh,
jauh khaix hoik yum miex nawh,
yam in Yi-sha tag pon pa nyawm rhawm heue.
