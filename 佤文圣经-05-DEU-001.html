Lai Been-ra-bawg
Gum lox pa jauh tix krai
1
Phuk lai in mawh lox pa hoik tiiem Maw-si.
Yam ot pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux tiag hawt praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
ka dee si nax veng Pa-lang mai veng Thaw-fu,
La-pang,
Ha-si-lux,
Ti-sha-ha khankix,
praok Su-fux dee ngawd rawm klawng Yaw-tang khankix,
Maw-si krai lox in plak greem ka kix.
2 Jauh ka gawng Heu-liex preeng laig ka gawng haktiex Yi-tong hoek ka Cia-ti-see,
Pa-ni-yax,
koe kao tix ngaix krax.
3 Khaix hoik lih pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Ai-cix,
ka daux tipon num,
khix kao tix tix ngaix,
Maw-si krai lox pa tawx Siyiex ka nawh khawm uik ka kix.
4 bYam an,
Si-hong simiang Ya-maw-lix,
pa ot ka veng ting daux tix mu simiang Si-seu-peung,
mai pa ot ka veng ting Ya-see-tha-lux,
Yi-teu-lai simiang Pa-sang,
Eux khankix,
ang pex ka nainga.
5 Maw-si kah ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang haktiex Maw-ya heue.
Nawh ah nin:
6 “Yam kawn ot ex ka gawng Heu-liex,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex hoik si kah ka ex ah nin:
‘Pex hoik ot ka piang gawng in tom rheung bawg yam heue.
7 Yam in pex yieh ka bang,
mai gah hu sivoe ngai.
Pex hu laig ka piang gawng Ya-maw-lix,
mai haktiex ka dee dex baoh an,
pa mawh ka daux ngawd Yaw-tang,
dee tiiem haktiex,
dee lhaong gawng,
ka haktiex mee plak seh,
mai ka hawt Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong,
mai pa ah nin,
pex tag laig ka meung Cia-nang,
ka Li-pa-noeng,
jauh dee an tom hoek ka baoh haktiex rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
8 Haktiex an hoik mawh aux,
Simiang Siyiex pa hoik ap tix tawx ka tax du pex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux khawm doeh doe pex.
Pex tag caw tix laig ka daux,
mai kuang ka haktiex an heue.’ ”
Maw-si laug ka cao prawng see
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 18.13–27)
9 Maw-si ah nin ka pui haoh pui hun:
“Yam kawn ot ex ka gawng Heu-liex,
aux hoik krai ka pex ah nin:
‘Aux ang pon klawm beeh glawm son pa mawh cao kaing pex nong nong.
10 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
hoik kieh kaux pui pex haoh hun,
haoh hun awm simuing ka rao ma yuh nan.
11 Kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex,
hawt awm lox pa ap tix mai kieh kaux pui pex nie haoh hun kaing rheng son,
son sang kieh pex ting lhaong haok huan.
12 Viang mawh nan,
aux sang pon klawm kaing pa nyap pa mao pex nong nong,
mai glawm ka beeh glawm pa thung thiang pex paox tix yuh ka mawx lie?
13 Pex tag caw tix laug ka pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi,
pui pa chix kaing ku ceu,
mai pui pa kub rhawm keud khankix ka daux ku ceu pui hoek,
aux sang tawx kix kuang ka piang pex.’
14 Pex hoik dawg sax cu,
mai khaw tix yuh nin.
15 Mawh kheu nin,
aux tom tawx pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi,
mai pa kub rhawm keud khankix pa laug pex khaing daux ceu pui ku ceu hoek kuang ka piang pex:
tix blah kuang pui tix rheng kaux,
tix blah kuang pui tix yieh kaux,
tix blah kuang pui tix phuan kaux,
tix blah kuang pui kao kaux.
Aux baox laug cao kaing tang kaux son piang doeh ceu cad pui ku cub khankix heue.
16 “Yam an,
aux tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
‘Pex tag caw tix mawh cao prawng see si nax pui khankix.
Mawh see ka daux paox ceu paox cad tix ang ah,
phan ang mawh see pa hoek ka piang pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax pex khankix ang ah,
pex tag caw tix prawng ca khaw caw heue.
17 Pex tag caw tix prawng ca khaw caw,
mai ang pon tix jhak ka pak ngai pui tix kaux kawx,
viang mawh simiang kawx ang pon,
mai tag caw tix jhak pui kieh khaw paox.
Ang pon tix lhat ka pui tix kaux kawx,
kheu mawh pex cao pa prawng ca tang Siyiex.
Phan pex koe see pa nu tix prawng khankix,
mai tag chix tix viex hoek ka aux tin,
aux tag sang mawh cao pa prawng.’
18 Yam an,
aux kawx tag tawx lox ka pex,
son kaing tang ceu pa khaw pex tix yuh khankix heue.”
Ploi pui hu jiang ka haktiex Cia-nang
(Lai Simin-pui 13.1–33)
19 “Ex hawt ka lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
lih hu khaing gawng Heu-liex,
mai tiang ka daux tiag ting pa koe krax lhat kah,
mai laig ka gawng haktiex pui Ya-maw-lix.
Yam hoek ex ka haktiex Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax,
20 mai aux hoik ah nin ka pex:
‘Yam in,
pex hoik hoek ka gawng pui Ya-maw-lix,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex ka ex.
21 Jhak heue,
haktiex an ot sivoe ngai pex.
Pex tag caw tix hawt ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex,
haok cax ka haktiex an,
paw vi lhat,
paw vi si yaung heue.’
22 “Viang mawh nan,
pex hoek ka aux,
mai mhaing lox pa ah nin:
‘Kieh ex ran ploi pui hu jiang ka haktiex an,
mai kieh kix deeh viex lox hoek krai ka son krax pa sang khaw ex tix hu,
mai veng yaong pa sang khaw ex tix hu puing heue.’
23 “Aux keud kaing in mawh pa khaw,
aux tom laug pui lih khaing ka daux ceu pui ku cub,
tix cub tix kaux,
mai hoik laug lih khawm uik kao ra kaux.
24 Kix haok ka piang gawng,
tom hoek ka daux ngawd Yi-seu-keux,
mai jiang ka dee an.
25 Kix viex plix khaox pa koe ka daux haktiex an hoek,
mai krai ah nin,
haktiex pa tawx Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
kied sidaing mhawm heue.
26 c“Viang mawh nan,
pex lieh lheu ka lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
ang cu tix laig ka daux haktiex an.
27 Pex si briem tix ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex koeng ka ex!
Nawh viex ex lih khaing daux Ai-cix,
mawh son sang ap ex ka daux tawm taix pui Ya-maw-lix,
mai kieh kix yuh ex laux leeing.
28 Ex sang laig yuh pa tix lie?
Lox krai pui jiang khankix ex kied lhat kah.
Kix hoik krai,
pui pa ot ka daux haktiex an,
kied ting lhaong khaing ex,
koe riang khaing ex;
mai veng pa ot kix kied kaux kied cong,
guh veng mai cux pai awm.
Kix kawn yaox ka pui Ya-na pa kied ting khankix ot tan heue.’
29 “Viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex:
‘Paw vi lhat ka kix.
30 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
duk thung ka pex,
mai yuh naing son pex,
awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan.
Pex hoik yaox ka can kaing pa yuh nawh son pex,
yam ot nawh ka daux Ai-cix,
31 dmai ka daux tiag ka ngai tix khawm uik.
Nawh hoik viex pex lih hu mhawm mhawm hoek ka dee in,
awm keeing pui viex kawn tix yuh nan.’
32 eViang mawh nan,
die aux krai ah ka mawh kawx,
pex ang chix cu tix yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
33 Viang mawh nan kawx,
Simiang Siyiex in ca kawn hu sivoe pex tom si dauh sidu,
mai sawg pex koe bang dee ot.
Yam pon sawn,
nawh teei rong ngu,
mai yam pon ngaix,
nawh teei rong pai awm,
dug thung ka daux krax hu pex heue.”
Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex
(Lai Simin-pui 14.20–45)
34 f“Simiang Siyiex mhong ka lox pa salao pex,
mai kied song rhawm kah,
tom tiex tu ah nin:
35 ‘Daux baux ju pex pa caox pa caig in,
mai tix kaux kawx tag ang pon yaox tix pon laig ka daux haktiex pa mhawm pa hoik ap aux tix tawx ka tax du pex khankix;
36 mai mawh ngie Cia-leux kawn simex Yie-fu-ni,
pa pon yaox tix laig ka khaing.
Mawh kheu nawh hoik hawt grawng lawng khaix aux khawm rhawm tix;
mai aux tag sang tawx haktiex pa hoik hu nawh mawh cie nawh mai cie kawn praix nawh khankix.’
37 Mawh kheu jao pex,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka aux.
Nawh ah nin:
‘Maw-si eue,
khawm maix kawx tag ang pon tix laig daux haktiex an.
38 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-su-yax cao teem coi maix,
kawn simex Noeng,
tag yaox tix laig tan;
maix teem coi nawh kaux cong.
Nawh tag sang viex pui Yi-seu-liex hu cax ka haktiex an heue.’
39 “Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka ex:
‘Kawn nyawm iak pa ang nyang pon guah pa khaw mai pa ang khaw khankix ka daux in singaix,
pex tee ce pui koeng khankix,
sang chix pon teei kix hu,
viang mawh nan mai tieh tix mawh kix pa pon laig ka daux haktiex an heue.
Kix tag sang cax ka haktiex an;
mai aux tag sang tawx haktiex an ka kix.
40 Phan mawh pex pui in kix,
deeh veeh pex hu ka daux tiag,
ka krax hu plak Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh heue.’
41 “Kix deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘Maw-si eue,
yix hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex!
Yam in yix tag sang hawt ka lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
haok puing heue.’
Khaix an,
pex ku kaux nhawt ka kraung naing tix,
mai rien khre tix yuh naing,
phan ang mawh nan,
pex ang pon cax ka haktiex an yie yie heue.
42 “Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
‘Maix krai ka kix,
paw haok puing ka kix,
lhat pex ang pex ka pui koeng,
mawh kheu ang aux ot tau mai pex.’
43 Aux hoik krai lox Simiang Siyiex ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
pex kawn ang nghiet.
Pex tieh vheen ka nawh,
mai viex rhawm pa ting pa lhaong haok ka piang gawng.
44 Pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot ka piang gawng an,
kuan rieb tix puing ka pex awm phung hia yuh nan.
Kix leue si gheet ka pex,
tom hoek ka Heu-eu-ma haktiex Yi-tong,
mai yuh pex laux leeing dee an heue.
45 Yam an pex yiiem tix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai mhaing nawh teem coi tix;
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang cu tix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw pex,
ang simi ang jhak ka pex heue.”
Vung viang ka daux tiag
46 “Khaix an,
ex ot ka haktiex Cia-ti-see tom rheeng num heue.”
