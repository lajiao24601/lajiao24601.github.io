Pon ka veng Ai
8
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Maix tag thung pui pa pon yuh naing hu ka veng Ai;
paw lhat paw graung.
Paw koe krax laux rhawm.
Aux tag kieh maix puing pex ka simiang veng Ai;
kawn pui cie nawh,
veng nawh,
haktiex nawh tag mawh cie maix.
2 Sivoe an maix yuh ka mawx ka veng Yie-li-keu mai simiang nawh,
maix kawx yuh nang nan ka veng Ai mai simiang nawh;
viang mawh nan,
bawg in pex chix tix deuh kraung khrai mai phung yung son to kaux tix.
Maix sang puing plak khaix veng Ai.”
3 Kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax mai pui naing rien tix hu puing ka veng Ai.
Nawh laug ka pui naing ting riang loe mheen kaux,
kieh kix hu tom pon sawm.
4 Nawh tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Pex mox tix ka veng plak an,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix singai,
rien rhawm tix hu pheet.
5 Pui pa thung aux sang puing ka veng Ai;
yam lih puing pui pa koeng pa ot daux veng Ai ka yix,
yix sang awm bawg sivoe mai veeh tix to yuh nan.
6 Kix tag chix leue ka yix,
yix sang glaw kix singai khaing veng Ai.
Kix keud yix awm bawg ka yuh nan to sivoe kix.
7 Yam an,
pex tag lih pheet ka kix,
pon ka veng an.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex sang tawx veng in ka pex.
8 Khaix hoik pon pex veng in,
tag hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
ploi ngu gleeing ka veng.
In mawh lox pa tawx aux ka pex.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax ploi kix hu,
kix hoek ka plak klip singaix veng Ai,
si nax Paw-theu-lix mai veng Ai,
mai mox tix dee an.
Yaw-su-yax it tau mai pui khankix daux bang.
10 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
Yaw-su-yax kaoh,
khrawm ka pui naing.
Nawh mai kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex khankix thung pui naing hu puing ka veng Ai.
11 Yaw-su-yax thung pui naing laig hu plak krax ting daux veng,
sum bang ka plak laong veng;
kix mai veng Ai si nax ka ngawd gawng tix mu.
12 Yaw-su-yax kieh pui phuan rheng kaux mox tix ka plak klip singaix veng,
si nax veng Ai mai Paw-theu-lix.
13 Pui naing caw tix ot yuh nin:
pui naing pa ting ot plak laong,
pui pa mox ot ka plak klip singaix veng.
Sawm an,
Yaw-su-yax it ka daux ngawd piang gawng.
14 Simiang veng Ai yaox ka pui naing Yaw-su-yax,
mai lih rieb tix puing ka kix.
Nawh khrawm ka can pui naing khawm uik,
hu puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux ngawd Yaw-tang dee puing kix naing bawg sivoe,
ang tawng ka plak khaix koe pui naing pa mox.
15 Yaw-su-yax mai pui naing yuh tix to sivoe kix,
to plak daux tiag.
16 Pui naing daux veng Ai lih khawm uik leue ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
kix nied pheet,
nied singai khaing veng.
17 Pui daux veng Aia lih khawm uik pheet ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
deuh ka veng yi,
ang koe pui cao pau.
18 Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Veeh kaing pliah maix tawx hu plak veng Ai;
aux sang tawx veng in ka maix.”
Yaw-su-yax hawt tix yuh heue.
19 Nawh yaog ka khaox pliah,
pui naing pa mox yaog tix kaoh cung,
mai laig ka daux veng Ai,
pon ka veng,
mai ploi ngu gleeing ka veng laux.
20 Pui veng Ai veeh ngai tix jhak,
yaox ngie taoh ngu haok ka rao ma.
Yam in,
pui Yi-seu-liex pa to daux tiag veeh tix puing ka kix;
kix ang koe dee mox tix.
21 Yaw-su-yax mai pui naing nawh yaox ka pui naing pa mox tix hoik pon ka veng Ai,
veng kawx jauh tix gleeing,
tom veeh tix hu puing ka pui veng Ai.
22 Yam in,
pui Yi-seu-liex pa pon ka veng kawx lih rieb ka kix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex lai lawm ka pui veng Ai,
pauh ka khawm uik,
ang koe pui tix kaux pon to.
23 Lheu khaing simiang veng Ai,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx klawd ju.
Kix pon giex im simiang veng Ai,
viex nawh hoek sivoe Yaw-su-yax.
24 Pui veng Ai leue pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka daux tiag daux tu;
ka dee an,
pui Yi-seu-liex pauh ka kix khawm uik,
khaix an veeh tix in ka veng Ai,
mai baox pauh pui pa ot daux veng khawm uik.
25-26 Yaw-su-yax ci khaox pliah tix ka plak veng Ai,
tom hoik yum pui pa ot daux veng khawm uik,
nawh tom taug ploi ka khaox pliah tix.
Ngaix an,
pui veng Ai hoik yum ka khawm uik,
simex bun khawm uik koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux heue.
27 Pui Yi-seu-liex hawt ka lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Yaw-su-yax,
mai deuh moi yung mai kraung tang ceu,
kieh mawh cie to kaux tix.
28 Yaw-su-yax gleeing ka veng Ai;
tom in singaix veng Ai kawn ca mawh dee riem.
29 Nawh teuk teue simiang veng Ai ka piang khaox,
kieh to kaux pa yum ot ka dee an,
tom hoek pon bo yam klip singaix,
Yaw-su-yax kieh pui teei to kaux yum nawh lih,
tik ka diang siviex veng.
Kix pum simaox ka piang to kaux yum nawh;
simaox in kawn koe tom in singaix heue.
Phat lox tat jhawm prawng ka piang gawng Yi-pa-lux
30 bYaw-su-yax sum khu dee tan tix mu son Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka piang gawng Yi-pa-lux,
31 chawt yuh awm lox pa tawm Maw-si pa mawh siliex Simiang Siyiex ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
lox tat jhawm prawng cie Maw-si tiiem tix deuh:
“Siao simaox ang saik ka rhiem yuh khu dee tan kah.”
Kix sang tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai baox tan Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
32 Ka dee an,
sivoe ngai pui Yi-seu-liex,
Yaw-su-yax baox tiiem lox tat jhawm prawng pa tiiem Maw-si ka piang simaox.
33 dPui Yi-seu-liex mai kuad song kix,
cao kaing kix,
cao prawng see kix,
mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka daux kix guah tix ra cub,
cung ka praok teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex ra plak.
Sivoe kix koe cao giex kaing li pui Li-we cao glawm teug sijax mukheu,
pui tix plak si tiep ka sivoe gawng Ci-li-sing,
mai an tix plak si tiep ka gawng Yi-pa-lux,
siliex Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Maw-si hoik tawx lox ka kix,
yam rieb kix krax nawb buan,
tag caw tix yuh nin.
34 Kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax hawt ka pa tiiem lox tat jhawm prawng,
ah lox si num phat ka lox tat jhawm prawng in khawm uik,
mawh khawm krax pon buan son mai krax jhax lawg.
35 Ka daux gum lox sijax mukheu cie Maw-si ku gum,
Yaw-su-yax hoik phat ka daux yam khrawm pui paox tix;
ka daux pui in kix,
mawh khawm bun miex,
kawn nyawm mai nyo si nie pa ot ka daux kix heue.
