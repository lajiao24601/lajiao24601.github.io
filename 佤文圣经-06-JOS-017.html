Ceu pui Ma-na-si
17
Kawn ai Yaw-seux doeh ceu cad Ma-na-si pon ka haktiex pa ot ka plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang tix blah.
Ma-ciex mawh kawn ai Ma-na-si,
pa mawh keeing Ci-liex,
nawh mawh pui pa chix yuh naing,
mawh kheu nan,
Ci-liex mai Pa-sang pa ot ka plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang tom tawx ka nawh heue.
2 Haktiex plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang tawx ka kawn praix pa nghoik cub Ma-na-si,
pa mawh Ya-pi-yi-shiex,
Si-leux,
Ya-see-liex,
See-ciien,
Si-fu,
mai See-mi-tax.
Kix mawh kawn simex Yaw-seux simex daux nyiex Ma-na-si,
mawh cao kaing daux nyiex.
3 Si-law-fe-ha mawh kawn simex Si-fu,
kawn saux Ci-liex,
kawn se Ma-ciex,
kawn sawk Ma-na-si;
nawh ang koe kawn simex,
koe ngie kawn bun.
Ceu kaux kix mawh Ma-lax,
Naw-a,
Heu-la,
Mi-cia,
mai Teu-sa heue.
4 aKix hoek ka cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa,
kawn simex Noeng pa mawh Yaw-su-yax,
khawm sivoe ngai simiang khankix ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si kieh nawh tawx haktiex ka yix,
kieh mawh sin ngao,
awm pa tawx ka paox nyiex simex yix yuh nan.”
Mawh kheu nan,
hawt awm lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
kix kawx hawt awm paox nyiex simex mai pon ka haktiex heue.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Ma-na-si,
lheu khaing Ci-liex mai Pa-sang pa ot ka plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
khaix an baox pon ka kao nyum,
6 Mawh kheu doeh ceu cad kawn simex kawn bun nawh hoik pon ka haktiex.
Doeh ceu cad tang kaux Ma-na-si hoik pon haktiex Ci-liex heue.
7 Mee haktiex ceu pui Ma-na-si jauh ka Ya-seux,
tom hoek ka veng Mi-mi-tha,
pa koe plak lih singaix See-ciien.
Plak seh hoek ka dee ot pui Yin Tha-phu-yax heue.
8 Haktiex hawt praok Tha-pu-yax mawh cie Ma-na-si.
Viang mawh nan,
haktiex pa ot ka mee veng yaong Tha-pu-yax,
mawh cie doeh ceu cad cub Yi-fa-liien heue.
9 Mee haktiex ceu pui Ma-na-si jauh ka dee an hu plak seh hoek rawm klawng Cia-na,
veng yaong pa ot plak seh rawm klawng viang ot ka daux haktiex Ma-na-si kawx,
kawn mawh cie Yi-fa-liien.
Mee haktiex Ma-na-si hawt ka rawm plak laong hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
10 Yi-fa-liien ot ka plak seh.
Ma-na-si ot ka plak laong;
Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong mawh mee haktiex kix ka plak klip singaix.
Ya-seux ot ka daog plak laong mai plak klip singaix;
Yi-sa-cia ot ka daog plak lih singaix mai plak laong.
11 Ceu pui Ma-na-si koe Paw Sang mai Yi-paw-liien ka daux haktiex Yi-sa-cia mai Ya-seux,
mai yaong pa ot hawt praok,
kawn koe To-eux (pa mawh veng ot ka praok rawm sigrit sigra),
Yin To-eux,
Tha-na,
mai Mi-cie-to,
khawm yaong pa ot hawt praok veng in kix.
12 bViang mawh nan,
Ma-na-si ang lai pon grheu pui pa ot ka daux veng an kix hu.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Cia-nang kawn ot ka dee an.
13 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoik koe riang koe rao pui Yi-seu-liex,
kix kawx ang grheu ka pui Cia-nang lih hu,
mawh ngie khin kix pui Cia-nang yuh kaing son kix heue.
Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien mai ceu pui Ma-na-si mhaing tix pon haktiex tom nie
14 Doeh ceu pui Yaw-seux ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Yuh pa tix maix lai guah ngie haktiex tix nyum ka yix mai kieh mawh sin ngao yix lie?
Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka yix,
yix kied nie pui heue.”
15 Yaw-su-yax ah nin:
“Phan pui pex kied haoh kied hun,
mai keud haktiex Yi-fa-liien kied iak,
pex hu ka haktiex daux nong khaox cie pui Pi-li-si mai pui Li-fa-ying hu paih ka haktiex heue.”
16 Kix ah nin:
“Son yix,
haktiex an ang nyang kub,
viang mawh nan,
pui Cia-nang pa ot daux lung,
pa mawh Paw Sang mai yaong pa ot hawt veng,
khawm pui pa ot daux ngawd Yie-see-liex,
kix kawx koe brung naing uik heue.”
17 Yaw-su-yax ah nin ka ceu pui Yi-fa-liien mai ceu pui Ma-na-si pa ot plak klip singaix:
“Pui pex kied nie tete,
mai kied koe riang rao nang,
ang khaw tix pon ngie haktiex in tix nyum.
18 Haktiex an tag mawh cie pex,
viang mawh daux tiag daux nong khaox,
pex hu kid kah,
mai pon ka haktiex khawm uik.
Viang pui Cia-nang koe brung naing,
ting riang ting rao kawx,
pex kawn pon grheu kix lih hu heue.”
