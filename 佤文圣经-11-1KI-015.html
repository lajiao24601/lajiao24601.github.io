Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-pi-yax
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 13.1–14.1)
15
Yam giex simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang kaing ka daux kao daix num,
Ya-pi-yax yaog tix mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
2 Nawh giex kaing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung loe num.
Miex nawh kaux Ma-cia,
mawh kawn bun Ya-sa-long.
3 Nawh yuh phi map pa hoik yuh keeing tix,
mai ang awm keeing tax du tix Ta-wue hawt Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh nan.
4 aViang mawh nan,
mawh kheu jao Ta-wue,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex nawh tawx Ya-pi-yax koe kawn simex tix kaux,
son sang plud tix kuang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung khaix nawh,
khaix an mai nyawm ot yien yawg yuh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
5 bSimiang Siyiex yuh nin,
hoik mawh kheu jao Ta-wue yuh kaing pa khaw ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex;
lheu khaing kaing an pa yuh nawh ka Wu-li-yax pui Heux,
Ta-wue ang sax lheu ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex tix bawg kawx.
6 Yam ot Ya-pi-yax ka piang haktiex,
si nax Law-paw-ang mai Yie-law-paw-ang rien yuh naing ka paox tix jheeng jheeng.
7 Kaing yuh Ya-pi-yax tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng khawm uik ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khankix.
Si nax Ya-pi-yax mai Yie-law-paw-ang rien yuh naing ka paox tix jheeng jheeng heue.
8 Yam yum Ya-pi-yax,
kix si paung nawh ka veng Ta-wue.
Kawn simex nawh Ya-sa plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh.
Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 15.16–16.6)
9 Yam giex simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ka kaing ka daux tix nga num,
Ya-sa mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
10 Nawh kuang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tipon tix num.
Yiex nawh kaux Ma-cia,
mawh kawn bun Ya-sa-long.
11 Ya-sa awm tax du tix Ta-wue yuh nan,
yuh kaing pa khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
12 Nawh hoik grheu ka can simex pa coh kaux tix ka daux nyiex vad lih hu ka meung tang meung,
khaix an mai yieh tix tik ka can riang krub pa yuh tax du tix khankix.
13 Nawh teei tix tik ka miex simiang pa mawh yiex tix Ma-cia,
mawh kheu hoik yuh miex simiang riang rong Ya-seu-la bun siyiex pa phit vao ka daux pa seeing pa brai.
Ya-sa kid ka riang bun siyiex an goh,
mai gleeing ka daux rawm klawng iak Ci-loeng.
14 Viang Ya-sa ang bax yuh laux khawm uik ka can nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak khankix,
mawh kheu nan,
ka daux ju nawh,
nawh hoik hawt Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
15 Nawh hoik teei ka kraung pa tan lu keeing tix ka Siyiex,
khawm khri mau pa tan lu tix khawm uik,
mai deuh ot ka daux nyiex buan.
16 Yam kawn mawh Ya-sa simiang pui Yo-tax mai Pa-sa simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
kix ra plak rien puing ka paox tix jheeng jheeng.
17 Pa-sa puing ka pui Yo-tax,
mai yuh tab ot ka La-max,
kaing ting mawh son sang tat dut ka krax pa laig pa lih ka Yo-tax khawm uik.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-sa viex khri mau pa kawn deuk ka daux nyiex buan mai nyiex haw khankix,
mai tawx ka kawn cau nawh viex hoek ka Ta-ma-see-keux,
hoek ka kawn simex Tha-paw-li-meung,
kawn saux Si-shuing,
simiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Piien Ha-tax dee an.
Kawn cau pa ting Ya-sa viex lox hoek,
mai ah nin:
19 “Tax du ex hoik mawh pui pa yuh paox pie tau,
kieh ex yuh tau nang heue!
Khri mau in kix mawh khawng phak pa tawx aux ka maix.
Khiox khaw maix deeh tik ka krax pa hoik yuh paox pie tau mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pa-sa,
nawh tom lih hu khaing haktiex aux khaix heue.”
20 Piien Ha-tax cu ka lox krai Ya-sa,
nawh tom ploi cao kaing pa bung pui naing pa ting tix hu puing ka veng Yi-seu-liex.
Kix puing ka Yi-yuing,
Tang,
Ya-paw Paw Ma-cia,
rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix pa dex ka kix,
mai haktiex Na-fu-tha-lix khawm uik.
21 Simiang Pa-sa mhong kaing pa lih tix mawh,
dum mawx dum an ang lai sum ka tab pa sum tix ka La-max,
mai deeh tix hu ka Teu-sa heue.
22 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-sa tawx lox ka pui Yo-tax khawm uik,
pui ku kaux,
ang pon tix kieh koe pa lheu tix kaux kawx,
tag caw tix hoek teem viex simaox mai khaox rong khankix pa sum Pa-sa ka La-max hu.
Ya-sa siao kraung krai in kix,
sum tab ka daux haktiex Mi-see-pa mai Cia-pa (veng pa koe ka daux haktiex Piien-ya-ming) heue.
23 Kaing yuh simiang Ya-sa tang ceu,
awm grawng pa kua nawh mai grawng pa mhawm kaing yuh nawh ka daux pa sum ka veng khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khankix khawm uik.
Yam kuad ju nawh,
nawh chawh kheu koe jhawm saux caong tix.
24 Yam yum Ya-sa,
kix si paung nawh ot tau mai si meeig pui daux nyiex simiang ka veng Ta-wue.
Kawn simex nawh Yaw-sa-fa plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Na-ta
25 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa ka kaing ka daux ra num,
kawn simex Yie-law-paw-ang pa kaux Na-ta mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh giex kaing ra num.
26 Nawh yuh ka phi map awm keeing tix,
yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
mai kieh pui Yi-seu-liex krix ka daux phi map heue.
27 Kawn simex Ya-si-ya pui Yi-sa-cia pa kaux Pa-sa,
keud sawn tix tieh vheen ka Na-ta,
yam lai lawm Na-ta mai pui naing cie nawh ka pui Fe-li-see veng Ci-pi-toeng,
Pa-sa pauh ka nawh.
28 Kaing in lih tix mawh yam giex simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa ka kaing ka daux loe num.
Pa-sa plud tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khaix Na-ta;
29 cYam yaog nawh tix mawh simiang,
mai pauh ka pui nyiex ma Yie-law-paw-ang yum pak lawg.
Hawt awm gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka siliex tix cao jhai Ya-si-ya pui See-law,
pui nyiex ma Yie-law-paw-ang hoik yum khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh klawd tix kaux kawx.
30 In hoik mawh kheu jao phi map pa yuh Yie-law-paw-ang,
mai baox kieh pui Yi-seu-liex krix ka daux phi map,
mai hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex song rhawm song rhi heue.
31 Kaing pa yuh Na-ta tang ceu,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix khawm uik.
32 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pa-sa ka kaing,
kix khawm ra plak rien yuh naing ka paox tix jheeng jheeng heue.
Simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pa-sa
33 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa kaing ka daux loe num,
kawn simex Ya-si-ya pa kaux Pa-sa mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh giex kaing ka Teu-sa tix nga pon num.
34 Nawh yuh phi map awm simiang Yie-law-paw-ang yuh nan,
hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
mai hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex krix ka daux phi map heue.
