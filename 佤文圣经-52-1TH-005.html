Rien khre tix rieb pa deeh baox hoek Simiang Ye-sux
5
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
phan mawh singaix bawg yam pa sang lih kaing in tix mawh,
ang siao lai kieh pui tiiem lox lai krai ka pex.
2 aMawh kheu hoik tawng pex,
singaix yam lih baox hoek Simiang Ye-sux tag sang lih hoek awm lih hoek pui briex yam pon sawm dum mawx dum an yuh nan.
3 Yam jiet taug ah pui “hoik pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe khawm uik” yuh nin,
krax laux leeing tag sang lih hoek dum mawx dum an,
awm yam saux yha bun yam sang keh yuh nan.
Kix tag ang pon tix bheeik luan khaing.
4 Viang mawh nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex ang mawh pa ot ka daux pa faik;
singaix yam an tag ang lai awm yam hoek pui briex dum mawx dum an.
5 Pex hoik mawh pui pa ot ka daux pa riang khawm uik,
mawh pa dex dawt tau mai pon singaix.
Ex ang mawh pa dex dawt tau mai pon sawm,
ang ot ka daux pa faik.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
ex ang caw tix laig it awm pui tang kaux yuh nan;
ex tag rien rhawm tix rien khre tix.
7 Pui pa laig it,
laig it yam pon sawm;
pa yoek ka plai,
yoek yam pon sawm.
8 bKheu hoik mawh ex pa dex dawt tau mai pon singaix,
ex tag caw tix rien khre tix.
Ex tag caw tix kieh krax yum rhawm mai krax muh mawh dieb tie naok ka naok tix,
kieh krax keud pot jun pot krax taoh mawh mhok rhiem ka kaing tix.
9 Siyiex ang laug ex son sang hoek tix kham krax ciyang,
mai mawh son sang kieh ex pon krax taoh ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
10 Khrit hoik yum son ex,
mai mawh son sang kieh ex yum ang ah,
im ang ah,
tag sang pon tix kaoh im tau mai nawh singaix yam deeh baox hoek nawh.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix ra lai joh jhan ka paox tix,
ra lai teem coi ka paox tix,
awm pa hoik yuh pex yam in yuh nin heue.
Lox pa nghoik tix krai mai lox chawk mhawm ot
12 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
ex khiox khaw ka pex,
tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka pui pa yuh kaing pa taug mao ka daux pex an kix,
pa mawh pui pa hoik laug Simiang Ye-sux hoek tix sidiex gau ka pex an kix.
13 Son kaing pa yuh kix,
pex caw tix teei krax nab yam pa ting mai krax muh kia ngien ka kix.
Pex kawx tag caw tix yuh krax pa khaw caw rhawm paox tix tau heue.
14 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
ex sidiex gau ka pex,
tag caw tix ne raw ka pui pa nieh khankix,
joh jhan ka pui pa laux rhawm khankix,
teem coi ka pui iak riang iak rao khankix,
gun yeung ka pui ku kaux.
15 Tag caw rien rhawm tix,
pui mawx ang ah paw vi pawk pa caox son pa caox,
tag caw tix ra lai chix kia ngien ka paox tix jheeng jheeng,
mai yuh ceu pa mhawm son paox tix heue.
16 Tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi jheeng jheeng,
17 rawg buan jheeng jheeng,
18 yaog yaw ah lox pa ting buan son ka daux kaing ku ceu.
In hoik mawh alo pa ciak ci Siyiex ka son ex pui in kix pa dex dawt tau mai Ye-sux Khrit heue.
19 Paw vi tieb tie ka kaing pa yuh Khuan Singax Singeh;
20 paw vi een gum lox pa krai ang koe ceu.
21 Tag caw lam lia tix yaox ka kaing ku ceu sidawng,
giex kaux cong ka ceu pa mhawm pa mhiem,
22 mai tik ka ceu pa caox pa caig khawm uik heue.
23 Kieh Siyiex tawx krax pa nhim ka ex,
kieh nawh kia ngie ka khuan,
ju,
mai to kaux pex ang koe dee cud cha mai grum mhawm singaix deeh baox hoek Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit!
24 Siyiex pa hoik laug ka pex an mawh pa kaux sijax;
nawh tag sang yuh kaing in grum naog heue.
25 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
khiox khaw pex rawg buan son ex liak.
26 Tag caw tix teei jheeh pa singax tix ngheet ka pux aik pux o tix chawk mhawm ot mhawm koe heue.
27 Aux tawx lox ka pex ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Ye-sux,
tag caw tix phat lox lai in ka can kawn Khrit pa koe khawm uik.
28 Kieh Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son ka pex liak!
