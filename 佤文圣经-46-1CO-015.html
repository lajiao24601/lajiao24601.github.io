Pa kaoh im Khrit
15
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux nhie pex doh keud ka gum lox buan pa hoik sidiex aux ka pex sivoe an;
gum lox buan in pex hoik rieb kah,
khaix an teei kieh mawh riah krax yum rhawm pex.
2 Phan pex giex kaux cong ka gum lox buan in,
paw vi kieh krax yum rhawm tix mawh pa giex going,
mai pex tag sang pon krax taoh kheu gum lox buan in heue.
3 aAux hoik sax sidiex tix tawx gum lox buan pa hoik rieb aux pa blawn ting ceu ka pex,
pa mawh:
phan hawt awm pa krai phuk lai sigang khri,
Khrit hoik yum son phi map ex.
4 bBaox hawt awm pa krai phuk lai sigang khri,
pui hoik si paung ka nawh,
ka daux loe singaix nawh hoik deeh kaoh im.
5 cNawh jhi Pi-teux yaox tix,
mai baox jhi kawn cau kao ra kaux yaox tix.
6 Khaix an nawh baox jhi pui pa hawt tix phuan yieh kaux yaox tix;
pui in kix plak nie kawn im ju,
viang mawh nan,
tix blah hoik yum.
7 Khaix an,
nawh jhi Ya-keux yaox tix,
mai baox jhi kawn cau yaox tix khawm uik heue.
8 eDee si dauh,
nawh kawx jhi aux yaox tix;
aux awm pa mawh pui pa keh ka daux bawg yam pa ang khaw cawd yuh nan
9 fAux hoik dawg mawh pa iak khaing paox tix ka daux kawn cau tix kaux,
mai ang keung tix mawh kawn cau,
mawh kheu hoik tuk tiak aux ka ceu cao Siyiex.
10 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu buan son Siyiex aux tom taug lih tix mawh aux singaix,
buan son pa hoik tawx nawh ka aux ang bax mawh pa giex going.
Aux tieh tix kawn nie yuh kaing pa taug mao khaing kawn cau tang kaux khawm uik;
deeh tieh tix,
ang mawh to kaux aux pa yuh kaing,
hoik mawh buan son Siyiex pa yuh kaing tau mai aux.
11 Kheu nan,
mawh aux ang ah,
mawh kawn cau tang kaux ang ah,
ex hoik sidiex yuh nin ka khawm uik,
pex kawx hoik yum rhawm ka yuh nin heue.
Pa kaoh im kawn Khrit
12 Kheu hoik mawh gum lox pa sidiex ex grawng pa kaoh im Khrit khaing krax yum,
yuh ka mawx ka daux pex lai koe pui krai ang koe grawng ceu pa kaoh im khaing krax yum lie?
13 Phan ang koe grawng ceu pa kaoh im,
kheu nan awm ang Khrit bax kaoh im;
14 Phan Khrit ang bax kaoh im khaing krax yum,
ex tag ang koe ceu pa khaw tix sidiex kah,
pex kawx ang koe gum lox pa khaw tix yum rhawm kah.
15 Yuh nin,
ex tag sang jhi grawng pa kham sakse pa pie lhien son Siyiex;
mawh kheu sakse pa kham ex,
krai grawng hoik kieh nawh Khrit kaoh im khaing krax yum.
Phan ang koe kaing pui pa yum dee kaoh im tete,
Siyiex tag ang lai kieh Khrit deeh kaoh im heue.
16 Phan pui yum ang bax kaoh im,
awm hoik krai ex ang Khrit bax kaoh im.
17 Phan Khrit ang bax kaoh im,
krax yum rhawm pex tag sang mawh krax giex krax going,
mai pex ca kawn grai ka daux phi map.
18 Phan yuh nin,
kawn Khrit pa hoik yum khankix tag sang sawn mawh pa laux leeing khawm uik.
19 Phan ex pui pa yum rhawm ka Khrit koe ngie krax keud pot jun potg ka daux singaix yam in khaing,
ex tag lheu kha tix khaing pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
20 Kheu nan,
tete te lai Khrit hoik kaoh im khaing krax yum;
in tag sang mawh krax pa kaux cong ka son pui pa hoik yum kawx tag sang kaoh im.
21 Mawh kheu krax yum hoik hoek khaing pui tix kaux,
mai krax pa deeh kaoh im pui yum kawx hoik hoek khaing pui tix kaux nang.
22 Awm hoik yum pui khawm uik kheu pa kleuk tix tau mai Ya-tang,
awm an,
krax pa deeh kaoh im pui khawm uik kheu pa kleuk tix tau mai Khrit.
23 Viang kawx nan,
pui ku kaux tag sang hawt paox tix awm pa khaw maix bawg yam deeh kaoh im yuh nin:
ran mawh Khrit sivoe,
khaix an yam deen hoek nawh,
mawh pui cie nawh.
24 Khaix an bawg yam tom taug si dauh;
bawg yam an,
Khrit tag sang yuh laux leeing ka simiang,
ka cao giex kaing mai ka pui pa koe siawp sibrawm ka daux mee haktiex khuan khawm uik,
mai teei tix tawx siawp sibrawm simiang ka keeing Siyiex.
25 hMawh kheu Khrit tag sang giex kaing,
tom hoik si dauh pon pex Siyiex ka pui koeng khawm uik,
mai kieh kix kreet tix ot greem caong nawh.
26 Pa sang yuh nawh pui koeng laux leeing dee si dauh mawh krax yum.
27 iMawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik kieh ku ceu khawm uik kreet tix ot greem caong nawh heue.”
Hoik kied sidawng pa krai dee in “ku ceu khawm uik” ang mawh khawm to kaux Siyiex,
kheu hoik mawh nawh kaw pa kieh ku ceu khawm uik kreet tix ot greem caong Khrit.
28 Yam hoek kreet ku ceu khawm uik tix ot greem Khrit,
to kaux nawh pa mawh kawn simex kawx tag sang kreet tix ot greem Siyiex kaw pa kieh ku ceu khawm uik cu nghiet ka nawh,
son sang kieh Siyiex giex kaing ka piang ku ceu khawm uik heue.
29 Phan ang yuh nin,
pa cub com son pui pa yum khankix,
sang koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
Krax keud pot jun pot kix mawh ceu pa tix lie?
Phan ang koe grawng ceu pa deeh kaoh im pui khaing krax yum,
yuh ka mawx kix lai kawn cub com son pui yum lie?
30 Ex kawx lai kawn siao yuh tix kha rhawm rhi tix ang jud ang jai lie?
31 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux hoik si pup krax yum ku ngaix heue!
Aux pon krai yuh nin,
hoik mawh kheu pa ot ex ka daux pa im ju Ye-sux Khrit,
aux yaw rang di son pex.
32 jPhan pa yuh aux krax pa lauh ka to sat an kix daux Yi-fu-saw,
mawh pa kaoh khaing rhawm keud kawn pui in,
sang koe dee mhawm ka aux yuh ka mawx lie?
Phan pui yum ang deeh kaoh im,
kheu nan,
tag sang awm lox been kawn pui pa ah nin:
“Kieh ex ih nyaux heue!
Pa sax ex tag sang yum heue.”
33 Paw vi kieh tix daox aw.
“Paox grawm pa caox chix kieh ceu pa mhawm laux heue!”
34 Tag caw rien rhawm tix,
paw vi lai baox yuh phi map.
Aux krai lox in mawh son sang kieh pex tawng tix kaig tua,
mawh kheu ka daux pex koe pui pa ang tawng yung ka Siyiex heue.
To kaux pa kaoh im
35 Koe pui pa tag sang chawk ah nin:
“Pui yum sang pon deeh kaoh im yuh ka mawx lie?
Pak peung kix sang yuh ka mawx lie?”
36 Pui pa daox aw eue,
simie pa sum maix ka daux tiex,
phan ang yum taug ang chix pon kuah tix lih.
37 Pa sum pex mawh nghox ca,
phan ang mawh nan mawh simie tang ceu,
pa mawh simie tix mu khaing yuh nan,
kheu nan ang mawh pak peung pa tag sang kuah tix lih.
38 Siyiex hawt ka alo tix tawx simie pa koe pak peung tix mu;
nawh kieh simie ku ceu tang koe pa khaw mai pak peung tix heue.
39 Nex nham to tiag ku ceu kawx,
ang awm paox tix khawm uik yuh nin:
kawn pui tang koe pak peung,
to sat tang koe pak peung,
sim tang koe pak peung,
kax tang koe pak peung heue.
40 Kawn koe pak peung rao ma,
mai pak peung haktiex;
pak peung rao ma koe ceu pa mhawm tix ceu ceu yuh nin,
pak peung haktiex koe ceu pa mhawm tix ceu ceu yuh nan.
41 Singaix koe pa riang singaix,
khix koe pa riang khix,
simuing koe pa riang simuing;
pie simuing in mai simuing an,
pa jhan riang ang awm paox tix heue.
42 Pa deeh kaoh im pui yum kawx hoik yuh nin.
Khaix hoik si paung ka pui yum,
chix laux chix si um;
khaix hoik deeh kaoh im,
mai ang lai chix laux chix si um.
43 Pa hoik si paung an,
mawh pa iak riang pa ang mhawm grang;
pa deeh kaoh im an,
mawh pa koe riang mhawm grang.
44 Pa hoik si paung an mawh to kaux ka daux nex nham;
pa deeh kaoh im an mawh to kaux pa dex dawt mai khuan.
Viang hoik koe to kaux daux nex nham,
mai tag koe to kaux daux khuan.
45 kMawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ah nin:
“Ya-tang pui pa liang tix phan yuh,
lih mawh pui pa im ju”;
viang mawh nan,
Ya-tang pa nghoik khaix,
mawh pa tawx khuan pa im ju.
46 Pa koe sivoe ang mawh pa dex mai cie khuan;
hoik mawh pa koe nex nham,
khaix an tom taug mawh pa dex mai cie khuan.
47 Ya-tang pa liang tix koe sivoe,
mawh pa phan tix yuh lih khaing phawng tiex;
Ya-tang pa hoek khaix,
mawh pa lih hoek khaing rao ma.
48 Pui pa mawh cie phawng tiex,
mawh pui pa phan yuh lih khaing phawng tiex;
pui pa mawh cie rao ma,
hoik mawh awm pui pa lih koek khaing rao ma.
49 Ex hoik koe greeh grang pak peung pa mawh pa dex mai cie phawng tiex,
singaix pa sang hoek sax ex kawx,l
tag sang koe greeh grang pak peung pa lih hoek khaing rao ma heue.
50 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pa tag sang krai aux mawh nin:
to kaux pa phan yuh ka nex nham ang pon tix cie ka meung Siyiex,
ceu pa chix laux chix si um an ang pon tix cie ka ceu pa ang chix laux chix si um heue.
51-52 mPex tag caw rien tix ka kaing ceu pa raux pa mox in yuh nin:
ex ang dak yaox tix yum khawm uik,
ka daux tix vud tix cu,
tix khiep ngai khaing,
yam nghoik teet tawng tix luah,
tag sang tieh loh tix khawm uik.
Yam nghoik lox teet tawng tix luah,
pui yum tag sang deeh kaoh im,
mai lih tix mawh ceu pa ang chix laux chix si um;
ex kawx tag sang tieh loh tix.
53 Mawh kheu ceu pa chix laux chix si um an,
tag sang tieh tix lih mawh ceu pa ang chix laux chix si um,
ceu pa chix yum tag sang tieh tix lih mawh ceu pa ang chix yum.
54 nYam hoik hoek bawg yam pa tieh loh ceu pa chix laux tix lih mawh ceu ang chix laux,
ceu pa chix yum hoik tieh loh tix lih mawh ceu pa ang chix yum,
gum lox ka daux phuk lai sigang khri hoik lih tix mawh yuh nin:
krax yum hoik laux leeing;
ceu pa pon pex hoik lih tix mawh heue!
55 oKrax yum eue!
Pa pon pex maix koe dee mawx?
Krax yum eue!
Nyeeig maix koe dee mawx?
56 Nyeeig krax yum mawh pa hoek khaing phi map,
siawp sibrawm phi map mawh pa hoek khaing lox tat jhawm prawng.
57 Viang mawh nan,
aux yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
nawh hoik kieh ex pon pex ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue!
58 Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik pux o pa muh khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix cung kaux cong,
ang pon tix yung yang.
Sau riang tix yuh kaing son Simiang Ye-sux jheeng jheeng;
mawh kheu pex hoik tawng,
pa yuh kaing son Simiang Ye-sux ang doh mawh krax pa giex going heue.
