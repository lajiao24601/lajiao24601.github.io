Yaw-su-yax pex ka Yie-ping mai meung pa yuh paox pie mai nawh
11
Grawng deeh hoek pui Yi-seu-liex hoik hoek ka daux yhaok simiang Sha-saw Yie-ping,
nawh tom tawx lox ka simiang Ma-toeng Yaw-pa,
simiang Seung-loeng,
simiang Ya-sa,
2 mai ka simiang khankix pa ot plak laong piang gawng piang lua,
daux ngawd daux lung Yaw-tang ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix plak seh,
mai praok To-eux khankix.
3 Nawh kawx kieh pui tawx lox ka pui Cia-nang pa ot khawm ra plak rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai baox tawx ka pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Heux,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Yie-pu-see pa ot piang gawng,
mai pui Si-we pa ot greem caong gawng He-meung ka daux haktiex Mi-see-pa heue.
4 Simiang khankix viex pui naing tix khawm uik hoek,
pui kix nie awm mhai praok rawm.
Kix koe brung mai ling law naing tom nie.
5 Simiang khankix khrawm paox tix hoek ka rawm klawng iak Mi-loeng,
son sang yuh naing mai pui Yi-seu-liex.
6 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Paw lhat ka kix,
pa sax bawg yam in,
aux tag pauh ka kix son pui Yi-seu-liex.
Maix tag mug dut ka caong brung brug kix,
mai gleeing ka ling law naing kix.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax viex can pui naing tix,
mai pheet kix daux rawm klawng iak Mi-loeng heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex hoik ap kix ka daux tawm taix pui Yi-seu-liex,
pui Yi-seu-liex hawt pheet ka kix,
plak laong hoek tom Mi-see-li-fu Ma-ying mai Si-toeng,
plak lih singaix hoek tom ngawd Mi-see-pa,
tom hoik pon pauh kix pui pa koeng khawm uik.
9 Yaw-su-yax hawt awm lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex yuh ka kix,
mai mug dut ka caong brung naing kix,
mai gleeing ka ling law brung naing kix ka ngu heue.
10 Khaix an,
Yaw-su-yax deeh ing plak khaix,
mai puing ka Sha-saw,
mai pauh simiang nawh.
(Yam an Sha-saw mawh pa kied ting riang ka daux can meung khawm uik.)
11 Pui Yi-seu-liex pauh ka can pui pa ot dee an,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx,
mai ploi ngu gleeing ka veng an heue.
12 Yaw-su-yax puing pex ka veng in kix,
mai pon giex im ka simiang kix,
pauh ka can pui khawm uik,
awm lox pa tawx siliex Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si.
13 Pui Yi-seu-liex ang bax gleeing ka can veng pa ot ka piang gawng khankix;
mawh ngie Sha-saw pa hoik gleeing Yaw-su-yax kah.
14 Pui Yi-seu-liex tud rhim ka kraung daux veng in kix,
teei can kraung pa koe jum mai moi yung khankix,
mai pauh ka pui khawm uik,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
15 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka siliex tix Maw-si;
Maw-si kawx awm an tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax.
Can kaing pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si ku ceu,
Yaw-su-yax hoik hawt tix yuh uik heue.
Haktiex pa pon Yaw-su-yax
16 Yaw-su-yax hoik pon rhim ka haktiex baoh an khawm uik,
pa mawh khawm haktiex gawng lua pa koe plak seh mai plak laong,
haktiex Keu-sang khawm uik mai haktiex kroh plak seh,
khawm rawm klawng ngawd Yaw-tang.
17-18 Mee haktiex khawm uik,
plak seh jauh ka gawng Ha-la praok Yi-tong,
plak laong hoek ka plak seh gawng He-meung,
tom hoek ka Pa-lix Cia-teux pa koe daux ngawd Li-pa-noeng.
Yaw-su-yax hoik yuh naing mai simiang baoh in tom nie num,
mai pon giex ka kix,
mai pauh ka kix.
19 Lheu khaing pui Si-we pa ot ka daux Ci-piien;
pa yuh pa nhim tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex;
tang kaux hoik pon kuang ka khawm uik heue.
20 aSimiang Siyiex kieh pui veng an kix koe rhawm tix yuh pa ang khaw mai Yi-seu-liex,
son sang kieh kix laux leeing khawm uik,
pui pauh ka kix khawm uik,
ang koe krax caox rhawm,
awm lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si.
21 Yam an,
Yaw-su-yax hoik pauh ka ceu pui ting laong Ya-na pa ot ka piang gawng.
Ceu pui in ot ka Si-paw-loeng,
Ti-pix,
Ya-na-paw,
khawm Yo-tax mai ka piang gawng Yi-seu-liex.
Yaw-su-yax yuh kix laux lak pak leeing tau mai veng pa ot kix khawm uik heue.
22 Ka daux haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex ang koe pui Ya-na tix kaux kawx,
mawh ngie ka Cia-sa,
Cia-theux,
mai Ya-seu-thu pa koe pa deuk heue.
23 Yaw-su-yax hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si mai pon kuang ka haktiex khawm uik,
nawh guah haktiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
tix ceu pui tix nyum.
Mawh kheu nan,
haktiex khawm uik hoik mhawm,
ang lih koe naing.
