Lai Si-paw-lai
Krai dawt
Lai Si-paw-lai mawh cao tiiem pa tiiem tix tawx ka cub kawn Khrit pa si pup krax pa kha rhawm. Kix kham krax pa tawx pui pa tieb tie ka kix ku singaix, pa sang kieh kix tik vud ka krax yum rhawm pa dawg koe sivoe an. Son sang kieh krax yum rhawm kix kaux cong, cao tiiem bre tix ah nin ka kix: Ye-sux Khrit mawh pa plak jhi Siyiex pa blawn khaw caw, pa blawn grum naog tete. Cao tiiem lai nie blawn krai in plak greem loe grawng:
1. Ye-sux mawh kawn simex pa im ju Siyiex leeing ju; nawh hoik kham gee krax kha rhawm khawm uik, nawh hawt ka alo Siyiex tom si dauh. Ka daux pa mawh kawn simex Siyiex, Ye-sux kawn lheu ting lhaong khaing cao jhai pa koe ka daux phuk lai sigang Khrit pa prim, kawn lheu ting lhaong khaing kawn cau Siyiex mai Maw-si heue.
2. Siyiex yaog Ye-sux mawh cao giex kaing li son ju leeing ju, nawh kawn lheu ting lhaong khaing cao giex kaing li ka sijax mukheu pa prim khankix heue.
3. Ka daux Ye-sux, kawn Khrit hoik pon taoh lih khaing daux phi map, khaing daux krax lhat krax graung, mai khaing daux krax yum. Ka daux pa mawh Ye-sux cao giex kaing li, nawh hoik kieh pui pon krax taoh tete; krax tan lu pa yuh nawh ka piang rao ma pa singax, kawn lheu khaing Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai been li pa yuh pui Yo-thaix heue.
Cao tiiem krai lao ka krax yum rhawm pa mhawm pa koe pui Yi-seu-liex pa koe min khankix tom dix dix (simin ting kao tix mu), sidiex gau pui pa phat tag caw tix kieh krax yum rhawm tix kaux cong. Simin ting kao ra mu, nawh khiox khaw pui pa phat khankix giex krax yum rhawm tix kaux cong tom si dauh, yum rhawm ka Ye-sux tom si dauh, gun yeung ka krax kha rhawm mai krax pa tuk tie ku ceu pa tee sang lih hoek ka piang to kaux kix. Dee nghoik, nawh teei gum lox pa sidiex gau mai pa ne raw kieh mawh gum lox pa nghoik tix krai heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai: Khrit mawh pa plak jhi Siyiex pa grum naog (1.1–13)
2. Grawng pa nie mhawm Khrit (1.4–10.39)
(1) Khrit kawn lheu ting lhaong khaing kawn cau Siyiex (1.4–2.18)
(1) Khrit kawn lheu ting lhaong khaing Maw-si mai Yaw-su-yax (3.1–4.13)
(3) Kaing yuh cie Khrit (4.14–7.28)
(4) Sijax mukheu cie Khrit (8.1–9.22)
(5) Cao giex kaing li cie Khrit (9.23–10.39)
3. Krax yum rhawm pa koe sivoe (11.1–12.29)
4. Lox sidiex mai lox pa nghoik tix krai (13.1–25)
