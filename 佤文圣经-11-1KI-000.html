Lai Simiang Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Lai Simiang Phuk Tix mai phuk ra hoik dawg mawh lai tix pheen, phuk tix si toe tix krai ka Lai Sa-mu-eu grawng been simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix, mai chix tix guah lih mawh loe grawng:
1. Saw-law-meung plud tix yuh ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex mai simiang pui Yo-tax, khawm grawng pa yum keeing nawh Ta-wue.
2. Ka baux yam pon simiang Saw-law-meung tix giex ka kaing khankix; mai nie blawn krai sidawng ka pa sum ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung.
3. Simiang meung guah paox lih mawh meung plak laong mai meung plak seh, mai baox bleeh ka sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num ka daux dim yieh num son kaing pa yuh simiang khankix.
Ka daux Lai Simiang Phuk Tix mai phuk ra, mai hoik tiag tix yaox ka simiang ku kaux, yum ang kix yum rhawm ka Siyiex. Pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex, meung cie nawh tag pon krax pa haok huan; phan krub riang krub rao, lieh lheu lox tawm jhawm krai Siyiex, meung cie nawh tag hoek krax laux krax leeing. Kaing pa yuh simiang meung plak laong khankix ang mawh pa mhawm; kaing pa yuh simiang plak seh khankix koe pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm khankix heue.
Ka daux Lai Simiang Phuk Tix, cao jhai Simiang Siyiex khankix mawh pui pa kied ting ceu tete. Kix kua rhawm mai pon si kah tang Siyiex, ang cu kawn pui krub ka riang rao, lieh lheu ka lox tawm jhawm krai Siyiex. Pa blawn yaox tix krai tom nie mawh grawng pa lah pa cha Yi-li-yax mai cao jhai pa pie lhien khankix paox tix. (Simin ting kao daix).
Krai kaing
1. Grawng pa si dauh yam giex Ta-wue ka kaing (1.1–2.12)
2. Saw-law-meung yuh simiang (2.13–46)
3. Grawng pa giex Saw-law-meung ka kaing (3.1–11.43)
(1) Yam jauh tix giex ka kaing baux num sivoe an (3.1–4.34)
(2) Sum ka nyiex buan (5.1–18.66)
(3) Yam giex ka kaing bawg nghoik (9.1–11.43)
4. Simiang meung guah paox tix (12.1–22.53)
(1) Ceu pui plak laong tieh vheen (12.1–14.20)
(2) Simiang pui Yo-tax mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix (14.21–16.34)
(3) Cao jai Yi-li-yax (17.1–19.21)
(4) Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ya-ha (20.1–22.40)
(5) Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa mai simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex (22.41–53)
