Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha tawx lox nhie pui krub riang khri
3
Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha yuh riang khri tix mu,
krax lhaong koe tix nga aliah kongjhee,
krax vieh koe loe kongjhee,
mai sum riang an ot ka dee lung Tu-la haktiex Pa-pi-loeng.
2 Simiang khrawm can kawn cau pa ting tix khawm uik,
khawm cao kuang kaing pa ting,
simiang haktiex,
simiang haktiex ka daux na bat ra,
simiang naing,
kawn cau pa ting pa priex mau,
simiang cao prawng,
simiang haktiex ku dauh,
mai can simiang tang kaux haktiex ku dauh khankix,
nhie kix hoek tix yuh poi pa sum Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka riang khri in.
3 Kawn cau pa ting mai simiang kaing yieh kaux khrawm tix hoek yuh poi in khawm uik.
Yam cung kix ka plak sivoe ngai riang khri an,
4 simiang cao pa tawx lox krai pao lox pa ting si num ah nin:
“Pui ku meung,
ku ceu,
pa si kah ka lox ku ceu heue,
5 yam mhong pex lox teet tawng,
lox siao,
lox ting aliah lah,
lox kraung,
lox ting jung lah,
mai can lox ting tang ceu khankix,
mai tag caw tix krub ka riang khri pa sum simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha khawm uik.
6 Pui mawx ang krub,
tag sang taing tix sau nawh ka daux glah ngu pa kied hax dum mawx dum an.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
yam hoik luah lox ting,
pui ku meung,
ku ceu,
pa si kah ka lox ku ceu,
tag krub ka riang khri pa sum Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha khawm uik heue.
Pui krai lieh lheu paox grawm Tang-yi-li loe kaux ka lox tawx simiang
8 Yam an,
koe pui Pa-pi-loeng tix blah sawg bawg yam deuh phi map ka pui Yo-thaix.
9 Kix krai ah nin ka simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha:
“Vawng lang ju lang phan heue!
10 Vawng hoik tawx lox,
yam hoik luah lox ting,
can pui khawm uik tag caw tix krub ka riang khri;
11 pui mawx ang krub kah,
tag sang taing tix sau ka daux glah ngu pa kied hax.
12 Koe pui Yo-thaix ra loe kaux,
pa mawh pui pa tawx Vawng kuang ka haktiex Pa-pi-loeng,
pa kaux Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx simiang.
Kix ang krub ka siyiex simiang,
mai ang krub nang riang khri pa sum simiang heue.”
13 Simiang mhong ka grawng in,
nawh kied song rhawm tete,
nawh tom tawx lox nhie pui viex pui loe kaux in kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix.
14 Simiang ah nin ka kix:
“Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu,
rug ang pex krub ka siyiex aux,
ang krub ka riang khri pa sum aux lie?
15 Yam in,
yam mhong pex lox teet tawng,
lox siao,
lox ting aliah lah,
lox kraung,
lox ting jung lah,
mai can lox ting tang ceu khankix,
tag caw tix krub ka riang khri;
pui pa ang krub tag sang taing tix sau ka daux glah ngu pa kied hax dum mawx dum an.
Pex ce siyiex sang pon hoek taoh tix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix aux lie?”
16 Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng,
yix ang lai rhawm krai lox son to kaux tix.
17 Phan Siyiex pa krub yix pon taoh yix luat luan khaing daux glah ngu pa kied hax mai ka daux tawm taix maix,
nawh tag sang pon taoh ka yix tete.a
18 Vawng eue,
viang nawh ang taoh ka yix kawx,
maix tag caw tix tawng,
yix ang doh krub ka siyiex maix,
ang krub ka riang khri pa sum maix heue.”
Prawng paox grawm Tang-yi-li loe kaux yum
19 Yam mhong simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka lox pa krai kix,
pak ngai nawh hoik tieh loh tix,
nawh kied song rhawm ka Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu.
Mawh kheu nan simiang tawx lox nhie pui pa ot plak goix plak dawm tix baox kieh glah ngu hax khaing sivoe an aliah son,
20 mai baox tawx lox nhie pui naing pa blawn ting riang khaing daux cub pui naing phrawk ka pui in kix loe kaux,
mai taing ka daux glah ngu.
21 Pui naing khankix tom phrawk ka kix loe kaux,
mai taing tix sau kix daux ngu.
Yam an pui loe kaux in jiet ceep kraung mhawm,
ceep kraung plak daux,
kraung dha plak praix,
mai mhok heue.
22 Kheu lox tawx simiang pa kied kin,
glah ngu kied hax,
rao ngu pa cap lih khaing glah ngu hoik gleeing ka pui naing pa phrawk kix yum.
23 Yam an,
Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu mai ca kawn phrawk tix,
laig ka daux glah ngu heue.
24 Dum mawx dum an,
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha daox deng cang luh tix kaoh cung,
mai chawk kawn cau pa ting tix ah nin:
“Ang ex hoik phrawk pui loe kaux,
mai taing tix sau kix daux glah ngu lie?”
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
Vawng.”
25 Simiang chawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx aux lai yaox pui pon kaux pa lai ling ka daux ngu lie?
Kix ang bax phrawk tix khawm uik,
mai awm pa ang bax hax gleed pie ceeix kawx yuh nan,
pui an kix pon kaux awm pa mawh Siyiexb yuh nan heue.”
Ploi pui loe kaux an kix mai yaog kix ting
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hu dex ka moeng glah ngu pa si aoh,
mai kok yo lox pa ting si num ah nin:
“Sa-teu-la eue!
Mi-sa!
Ya-paw-ni-keu!
Siliex Siyiex pa kied ting min,
aux khiox khaw pex lih heue!”
Kix tom lih khaing daux ngu dum mawx dum an.
27 Can simiang ting pa kuang kaing,
simiang haktiex,
simiang haktiex ka daux na bat ra,
mai simiang tang kaux khawm uik hoik khrawm tix ot sivie siveng pui loe kaux in kix,
mai yaox grawng ang ngu bax hax gleed kix pie ceeix kawx;
hauk kaing kix ang bax gleeing,
kraung ceep kix ang bax proi,
mai to kaux kix kawx ang mhong si oe taox ngu ka heue.
28 Simiang ah nin:
“Kieh Siyiex Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu pon krax pa yaog yaw heue!
Nawh ploi kawn cau Siyiex hoek tix taoh ka siliex pa yum rhawm ka nawh.
Kix cu tix tawx ju tix yum ka daux pa lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx aux,
mai ang cu tix krub ka siyiex tix kaux kawx;
kix krub ngie ka Siyiex kix tix kaux khaing heue.
29 “Yam in aux tawx lox yuh nin:
meung mawx ang ah,
pui ceu tix ang ah,
pui pa si kah lox ceu pa tix ang ah,
phan salao ka Siyiex Sa-teu-la,
Siyiex Mi-sa,
Siyiex Ya-paw-ni-keu,
tag sang tat taix caong dut,
ka daux nyiex ma nawh tag sang lih mawh dee riem;
mawh kheu ang lai koe siyiex pa pon yuh krax pa taoh yuh nin heue.”
30 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang yaog Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu loe kaux mawh simiang pa blawn ting ka daux haktiex Pa-pi-loeng heue.
