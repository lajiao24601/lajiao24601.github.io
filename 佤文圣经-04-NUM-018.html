Beeh glawm cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we
18
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-loeng:
“Maix mai kawn simex maix khankix,
khawm pui Li-we tag caw tix klawm ka phi map pa lud loe pa yuh pui ka daux nyiex singax singeh;
viang mawh nan,
mawh ngie maix mai kawn simex maix khankix pa sang klawm ka phi map pa yuh cao giex kaing li.
2 Maix viex paox nyiex dex maix ceu pui Li-we hoek,
kieh kix yuh kaing tau mai maix.
Yam yuh maix mai kawn praix maix khankix kaing krub ka daux nyiex gox buan,
kix tag teem ka maix.
3 Kix tag kham sakse son maix,
yuh kaing ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix teeh ka nyiex dee pa singax singeh mai kraung pa singax singeh pa koe ka piang khu dee tan,
son ang pex mai kix sang yum uik.
4 Kix tag yuh kaing tau mai maix,
kuang ka kaing daux nyiex gox buan man;
viang mawh nan,
pui tang kaux pa ang kub rhawm keud ang chix tix yuh kaing tau mai maix.
5 Mawh ngie maix mai kawn simex maix khankix pa caw tix kuang ka kaing daux nyiex gox buan man mai kaing khu dee tan.
Yuh nin,
aux tom ang lai baox song rhawm ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
6 “Aux laug pui dex mai nyiex ma maix pui Li-we khaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tawx ka pex.
Kix tag tawx mawh cie aux,
son sang yuh kaing ka daux nyiex gox buan.
7 Mawh ngie maix mai kawn simex maix khankix pa mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing khu dee tan mai nyiex pa singax singeh.
In mawh beeh glawm pex,
mawh kheu hoik tawx aux beeh glawm cao giex kaing li ka pex.
Pui tang kaux phan hu dex ka kraung pa singax singeh,
tag kieh yum heue.”
Khawng tan pa khaw cao giex kaing li tix pon
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-loeng:
“Aux sang tawx khawng phak pa blawk tix tan ka aux khawm uik,
pa mawh khawng phak pa ang pui gleeing tix tan ka piang khu dee tan,
tawx ka maix uik.
Aux tawx khawng tan in ka maix mai doeh ceu cad maix,
kieh mawh nyum pa khaw maix tix pon leeing ju.
9 Phan mawh ka daux khawng tan pa singax singeh an,
pa ang gleeing piang khu dee tan,
pa mawh Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud hoik tawx mawh cie pex.
Can khawng tan pa singax singeh tix tawx ka aux,
tag mawh cie maix mai kawn praix maix.
10 Pex caw tix ih in kix ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
mawh ngie simex pa pon tix ih.
In kix mawh pa kied singax.
11 “Khaix an,
khawng tan pa blawk tang ceu pa tawx pui Yi-seu-liex ka aux kawx tawx ka pex.
Aux sang tawx in kix ka maix mai kawn simex kawn bun maix,
kieh mawh nyum pa khaw maix tix pon tix ju hoik tix ju.
Pa singax singeh daux been ka daux nyiex pex ku kaux tag caw tix ih heue.
12 “Aux sang tawx khawng phak pa blawn mhawm pa jauh pui Yi-seu-liex tix tan ka aux ku num ka pex,
koe bee sime,
plai,
mai nghox koi.
13 In kix caw cie pex khawm uik.
Ka daux nyiex pex pa singax singeh ka daux been,
pui ku kaux tag caw tix ih heue.
14 a“Can khawng tan pa tawx pui Yi-seu-liex ka aux giex giex going going tag mawh cie pex uik heue.
15 “Can kawn ai mai khawng brie pa liang tix keh pa tan pui Yi-seu-liex ka aux,
tag mawh cie pex uik;
viang mawh nan,
pex caw tix rieb tix yuh krax taoh kawn ai ku kaux,
mai baox rieb tix yuh krax taoh khawng brie pa liang tix keh pa ang singax singeh.
16 Yam hoik naog kawn nyawm tix khix tag caw tix deeh taoh,
hawt awm diag pa tat nyiex pa singax singeh tawx phuan plah mau luah.
17 Viang mawh nan,
kawn moi pa liang tix keh,
yung pa liang tix keh,
mai kawn pex pa liang tix keh tag ang pon tix deeh taoh;
kix tag mawh cie aux khawm uik,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie.
Maix tag phruah nham kix ka piang khu dee tan,
mai gleeing khlawp bee kix,
kieh mawh son Krax-tan Ngu pa si oe bhawng pa tan ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex.
18 Nex kix tawx mawh cie pex,
awm pa blawk tix tan nex eek mai ba khawn dawm plak khaix tawx mawh cie pex yuh nan heue.
19 “Aux tag tawx krax tan pa blawk pui Yi-seu-liex tix tan ka aux khawm uik,
tawx ka maix mai kawn simex kawn bun maix,
kieh mawh nyum pa khaw tix pon tix ju hoek tix ju.
In mawh sijax mukheu pa yuh aux tau mai maix mai kawn praix maix pa ang chix grai.”
20 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-loeng:
“Maix tag ang pon rieb tix koe sin ngao khaing ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
can pa chix tix tawx ka kawn praix tix,
ang pon tix koe haktiex tau mai kix.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux mawh nyum cie maix,
sin ngao maix heue.”
Khawng tan pa khaw pui Li-we tix pon
21 bSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawx sin ngao pa tan pui Yi-seu-liex kao son tix son ka aux mawh cie pui Li-we.
In mawh ngawh riang pa pon kix kheu yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
22 Pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux ang pon tix dex ka nyiex gox buan man,
lhat kix yuh phi map tom pauh yum heue.
23 Jauh yam in,
mawh ngie pui Li-we pa chix tix kuang ka nyiex gox buan man,
mai yuh kaing khawm uik ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
In mawh been pa hawt pex tix yuh tix ju hoek tix ju.
Pui Li-we ang pon tix koe sin ngao si nax pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju,
24 Mawh kheu aux hoik tawx krax tan pui Yi-seu-liex kao son tix son ka kix.
Kheu nan,
aux tom ah nin ka kix,
kix ang pon tix koe sin ngao si nax pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju.”
Kao son tix son cie pui Li-we
25 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si
26 krai ka ceu pui Li-we ah nin:
“Yam rieb pex krax tan kao son tix son ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pex,
teei daux kao son tix son kieh mawh pa blawk tix tan,
tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
27 Pa blawk pex tix tan khawng phak in awm tan pui yuh kaing ka nghox koi mai plai pa khraox pa liang tix pon.
28 Yuh nin,
pex kawx teei nang yuh nan ka daux pa tan pui Yi-seu-liex kao son tix son,
pa mawh ka daux khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex baox tan lih.
Pex tag tawx khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex in ka cao giex kaing li Ya-loeng,
29 teei pa kied mhawm ka daux cie pex lih.
30 Khaix hoik tan pex pa kied mhawm tix blah,
chix tix deuh pa deuk,
awm pui yuh kaing deuh pa deuk khaix hoik tan tix yuh nan.
31 Pex mai pui nyiex pex chix tix ih pa deuk an kix ku dauh ku dee,
mawh kheu an hoik mawh riang rao pa pon pex son pa yuh pex kaing ka daux nyiex gox buan.
32 Khaix hoik tan pex pa blawn mhawm an tix blah ka Simiang Siyiex,
pex ih pa deuk an ang koe phi map;
viang mawh nan phan ih sivoe sang tan pa blawn mhawm an,
mawh pa seeing brai yuh khawng tan pa singax singeh cie pui Yi-seu-liex,
tag kieh yum heue.”
