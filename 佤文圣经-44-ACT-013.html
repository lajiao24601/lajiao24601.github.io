Khuan Singax Singeh ploi Pa-na-pa mai Sao-law hu
13
Ka daux ceu cao Ang-thi-a,
koe cao jhai mai sara ra loe kaux pa mawh Pa-na-pa,
Si-miien (ceu kaux tang ceu mawh “simex mhaing lung”),
Lu-chiox pui Ku-li-naix,
Ma-niang pa ting tau mai simiang Si-luix,
kawn koe Sao-law.
2 Yam krub kix ka Simiang Siyiex,
tuah ka priex preem,
Khuan Singax Singeh ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw tix ploi Pa-na-pa mai Sao-law hu yuh kaing pa hoik kok aux kix yuh heue.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
kix rawg buan tuah ka priex preem,
deuh taix tix piang kaing kix,
mai ploi kix hu heue.
Ka daux krax Sai-phu-lu-see
4 Pa-na-pa mai Sao-law hoik pon pa ploi Khuan Singax Singeh hu,
kiex tom lih ka Si-lio-ci,
mai brug reu dee an hoek ka Sai-phu-lu-see.
5 Yam hoek kix ka Sa-la-mi,
kix tom sidiex gum lox Siyiex ka nyiex sidiex ka haktiex pui Yo-thaix ku dauh ku dee.
Yaw-hang Ma-kheu ot tix teem coi kiex ka dee an heue.
6 Kix tauh hu ka klawn tiex khawm uik,
tom hoek ka Pha-fux,
kix si pup pui dee an tix kaux ceu kaux mawh Pa Ye-sux pui pa chix yuh si meed,
nawh mawh pui Yo-thaix,
mai tang kok tix mawh cao jhai.
7 Nawh mai See-chio Pao-law cao giex kaing ka klawn tiex haktiex an kied ting meed paox tix.
Cao giex kaing an mawh pui pa lhak tix kaux;
nawh kok Pa-na-pa mai Sao-law hoek,
son sang nghiet ka gum lox Siyiex.
8 Viang mawh nan,
pa chix yuh si meed an Yi-lui-ma (Yi-lui-ma mawh ceu kaux Si-lax) tieb tie;
nawh keud tix sang tieb tie ka cao kuang kaing in paw rieb krax yum rhawm in.
9 Bawg yam in,
Sao-law kawx pa mawh Pao-law hoik naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
dong ngai tix jhak ka pui pa chix yuh si meed an,
10 mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh kawn simex phi caox in!
Maix mawh pui koeng ka pa rauh pa see,
pa naog ka krax pa caox pa caig pa pie lhien,
pa su tix vawk yuh krax pa mawh pa meh cie Simiang Siyiex heue!
11 Yam in,
krax ciyang cie Simiang Siyiex tag sang lih hoek ka piang maix;
maix tag sang dug ngai,
mai ang lai chix yaox ka pa riang tix vud tix cu heue.”
Dum mawx dum an,
Yi-lui-ma hoik mhong tawb rawm si mawg pa faik ka ngai tix;
nawh pu pi tix hu,
mai khiox khaw pui tuk taix tix thung nawh ka krax.
12 Simiang an hoik yaox ka kaing pa lih tix mawh,
nawh tom lih tix mawh kawn Khrit;
nawh hoik kied daox deng ka krax pa sidiex pa dex dawt mai Simiang Siyiex heue.
Ot ka Ang-thi-a haktiex Pi-si-tix
13 Pao-law mai paox pa hu nawh tau jauh tix phat reu ka Pha-fux,
hoek ka Piie-cia haktiex Phang-fe-li-yax;
Yaw-hang Ma-kheu lih hu khaing kix dee an,
mai deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
14 Kix lih khaing Piie-cia mai nied tix hu krax pa hu kix,
hoek ka Ang-thi-a haktiex Pi-si-tix.
Ka daux singaix tuah kix hoek tix ngawm ka nyiex sidiex pui Yo-thaix.
15 Khaix hoik phat ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si mai lox lai cao jhai,
cao nyiex sidiex khankix ploi pui hu ah nin ka kix:
“Pux aik khankix eue,
phan pex koe lox pa sang sidiex ka pui hun,
pex krai heue.”
16 Pao-law tom kaoh cung,
mai yeeb taix tix ah nin:
“Paox ceu paox cad pui Yi-seu-liex mai can pui tang ceu tang cad pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex eue,
pex nghiet heue!
17 aSiyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik laug ka tax tu ex.
Yam paung ot kix ka daux haktiex Ai-cix,
Siyiex hoik kieh kix lih mawh pui ting tix ceu,
mai teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix,
18 cnawh ot ka daux tiag daux tu mai hoik va rhawmb ka kix been tix pon num.
19 dNawh hoik yuh laux leeing ka ceu pui haktiex Cia-nang aliah ceu,
mai hoik ap tix tawx haktiex kix ka pui cie tix;
20 ekaing in kix hoik mawh pa lih tix mawh ka daux bawg yam tix pon yieh tix phuan num heue.
“Khaix an,
Siyiex baox tawx simiang cao prawng see ka kix,
tom hoek singaix yam cao jhai Sa-mu-eu.
21 fKhaix an,
kix khiox khaw tix koe simiang tix kaux,
Siyiex tom laug Sao-law kawn simex Ci-see khaing daux ceu pui Piien-ya-ming lih,
mai ap nawh mawh simiang kix,
been tix pon num.
22 gKhaix hoik tik ka Sao-law,
Siyiex baox yaog Ta-wue mawh simiang son kix.
Phan mawh Ta-wue,
Siyiex ah nin:
‘Aux hoik yaox ka Ta-wue kawn simex Yie-si;
nawh hoik mawh pui pa khaw ka rhawm aux,
pa priex tix hawt ka alo aux ka daux kaing ku ceu.’
23 Ka daux doeh ceu cad nawh,
Siyiex hoik hawt ka lox pa ap tix,
mai tawx cao taoh ka Yi-seu-liex tix kaux,
pa mawh Ye-sux.
24 hSivoe sang jauh Ye-sux tix yuh kaing,
Yaw-hang tawx lox buan ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai kieh kix loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai rieb ka krax cub com.
25 iKaing yuh Yaw-hang dex sang hoik,
nawh ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
‘Pex keud aux mawh pui mawx?
Aux ang tie bax mawh pui sang krox pex an.
Viang mawh nan,
pui an pa hoek khaix aux,
pie teem aux nawh kah maox kiiep aux ang keung ka heue.’
26 “Paox grawm ku kaux,
kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex eue,
gum lox pa taoh in mawh pa sidiex tix ka ex heue!
27 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung mai simiang kix ang tawng nawh mawh cao pa taoh,
mai ang tawng nang phuk lai sigang khri pa phat cao jhai ka daux singaix tuah.
Kix hoik prawng pa Ye-sux koe phi map,
in tieh tix kieh lox grox krai cao jhai lih tix mawh.
28 jViang kix ang yaox ka grawng deuh phi map prawng yum ka piang nawh,
mai kawn ca kieh Pi-la-to prawng nawh yum.
29 kKhaix hoik yuh kix kaing pa dex dawt mai Ye-sux pa tiiem tix deuh ka daux lai cao jhai khawm uik,
mai tom teei nawh lih khaing ka piang khaox lhak,
mai deuh ka daux si meeig.
30 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik kieh nawh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
31 lmai ka daux bawg yam tom nie,
nawh jhi tix lih tom mie bawg ka pui pa kaoh tau mai nawh ka Cia-li-lix mai hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Pui in kix tom in singaix lih tix mawh cao pa kham sakse son nawh ka daux si nax pui Yi-seu-liex.
32-33 mYam in,
ex tawx gum lox buan in ka pex:
teei krax pa kaoh im Ye-sux,
Siyiex hoik ap tix kieh lox pa deuh tix ka tax du ex lih tix mawh,
mai hoik lih tix mawh ka kawn praix ex.
Awm lox pa ah Lai Yaog Yaw ra mu:
maix mawh kawn simex aux;
in singaix,
aux mawh keeing maix.
34 nPhan mawh grawng pa kieh nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
ang lai chix yum,
Siyiex hoik krai ah nin:
aux tag sang tawx gum lox pa deuh tix ka Ta-wue
mai tawx buan son pa kaux pa cong pa singax an ka pex.
35 oLai Yaog Yaw tang dauh tix dauh baox ah nin:
maix ang doh kieh siliex pa kaux sijax tix cux si um heue.
36 “Yam kawn ot Ta-wue ka piang haktiex,
nawh hoik yuh lih ka krax keud Siyiex,
khaix hoik yum nawh pui si paung nawh ka praok tax du tix,
nawh hoik cux si um.
37 Viang mawh nan,
yam kieh Siyiex nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum ka daux singaix an,
nawh ang lai chix cux si um.
38-39 Paox ceu paox cad khankix eue,
pex hoik khaw tix tawng khawm uik,
gum lox an pa pluat ploi ka phi map pui,
pa mawh gum lox pa hoik sidiex Ye-sux ka pex.
Pex tag caw tix tawng,
lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si ang pon kieh pex pheeik luan khaing phi map khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
pui pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux ku kaux,
hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi phi map.
40 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
son ang gum lox pa hoik krai cao jhai sang lih hoek ka piang to kaux pex ah nin:
41 pPui pa map nai ka Siyiex eue,
jhak heue,
pex tag sang yaoh yee,
tag sang yum heue!
Mawh kheu kaing pa hoik yuh aux ka singaix bawg yam cie pex,
viang hoik koe pui pa krai sidawng ka pex kawx,
pex ca ang chix yum rhawm ka heue!”
42 Yam taug sang yaog Pao-law mai Pa-na-pa tix lih hu khaing nyiex sidiex,
pui hun ghaok kiex krai in kix ka kix ka daux singaix tuah bawg khaix.
43 Khaix hoik tang hu pui hun,
koe pui Yo-thaix tom nie mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa hoik laig been li pui Yo-thaix hawt ka Pao-law mai Pa-na-pa.
Kiex kawn cau ra kaux tawx lox kaux cong nhie kix nied tix een rhawm ka buan son Siyiex heue.
44 Ka daux singaix tuah bawg khaix,
pui ka daux veng hoik dex sang hoek khawm uik,
son sang nghiet ka gum lox Simiang Ye-sux.
45 Pui Yo-thaix yaox cub pui hun in kix,
rhawm rhi kix hoik naog ka krax boh rhawm;
kix thung thiang ka gum lox pa krai Pao-law,
khaix an map nai ka nawh.
46 Viang mawh nan,
Pao-law mai Pa-na-pa nied tix kua rhawm tix krai pao ah nin:
“Gum lox Siyiex tag caw tix ran sidiex ka pex.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang rieb kah,
mai een tix ang keung tix pon ka ju leeing ju.
Mawh kheu nan,
yiex sang lih hu khaing pex,
mai hu ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad;
47 qmawh kheu hoik tawm Simiang Ye-sux lox ka yiex ah nin:
Aux hoik ciak ci pax mawh pa riang ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai sang kieh pax viex krax taoh hu ka ku caong daog haktiex heue.”
48 Pui tang ceu tang cad mhong ka lox in kied gaux rhawm ka tete,
mai kix yaog yaw ka gum lox cie Simiang Ye-sux;
pui pa hoik laug Simiang Ye-sux hoek tix rieb ka ju leeing ju khankix,
kix hoik lih tix mawh kawn Khrit khawm uik heue.
49 Gum lox cie Simiang Ye-sux hoik tauh hu ka daux haktiex an ku dauh.
50 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix suk sag yuh pui haktiex an pa koe min khankix,
mai ka bun miex pui tang ceu tang cad,
pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex pa hoik koe min ka daux haktiex khankix,
jauh tix tuk tiak ka Pao-law mai ka Pa-na-pa,
grheu kiex lih hu khaing haktiex kix.
51 rKiex khruah cawd ka phawng tiex pa kawb caong tix,
son sang mawh pa ne raw ka kix,
khaix an kiex hu ka Yi-keu-nien.
52 Rhawm rhi pui gau Ang-thi-a hoik naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm mai ka Khuan Singax Singeh heue.
