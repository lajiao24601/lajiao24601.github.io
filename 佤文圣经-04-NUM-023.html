23
Pa-lang ah nin ka Pa-leux:
“Maix sum khu dee tan son aux aliah mu dee in,
mai tawx moi mhaing aliah mu mai yung mhaing aliah mu ka aux.”
2 Pa-leux hawt tix yuh;
nawh mai Pa-lang tan moi mhaing tix mu mai yung mhaing tix mu,
ka piang khu dee tan ku mu.
3 Pa-lang ah nin ka Pa-leux:
“Maix cung ka praok Krax-tan Pa-gleeing maix;
aux tag lih hu jhak ka Simiang Siyiex,
sang hoek ang nawh hoek tix yaox ka aux.
Nawh jhi aux yaox pa tix,
mai aux tag krai ka maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Pa-lang haok ka gawng nong nong.
4 Siyiex lih hoek ka Pa-lang;
mai Pa-lang ah nin:
“Aux hoik sum khu dee tan aliah mu,
mai hoik tan moi mhaing tix mu,
mai yung mhaing tix mu ka piang khu dee tan ku mu.”
5 Simiang Siyiex krai lox pa sang caw Pa-lang tix sang ah,
khaix an nhie nawh deeh ing ka Pa-leux dee an,
mai krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka nawh.
6 Pa-lang deeh ing,
mai yaox Pa-leux mai can simiang pui Maw-ya khawm uik,
kawn cung ka praok Krax-tan Pa-gleeing heue.
7 Pa-lang tom krai lox jhai lih
Pa-leux simiang Maw-ya viex aux lih khaing haktiex Shui-li-yax,
viex aux lih khaing si niag gawng plak lih singaix.
Nawh ah nin:
hoek heue,
jhax lawg ka Ya-keux son aux;
hoek heue,
jhax lawg ka Yi-seu-liex khankix son aux.
8 Simiang Siyiex ang bax jhax lawg,
aux sang jhax lawg yuh ka mawx?
Simiang Siyiex ang bax ah lox caox,
aux sang ah lox caox yuh ka mawx?
9 Aux kok yaox kix khaing ka piang choek gawng lhaong;
aux kok jhak kix khaing ka piang gawng iak.
Kix hoik mawh meung pa tang pa siyox tix mu;
kix hoik pon buan son tom nie khaing meung tang meung.
10 Doeh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex haoh hun awm phawng tiex;
kix haoh hun tom ang lai pon uik simin.
Kieh aux awm kawn praix Siyiex yuh nan tom si dauh ju tix;
aux cu tix awm pui rauh see hu khaing haktiex ka daux pa nhim yuh nan.
11 Pa-leux ah nin ka Pa-lang:
“Maix hoik yuh pa tix ka aux?
Aux kok maix hoek tin,
mawh son sang nhie maix jhax lawg ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
maix lai nawb buan son ka piang kix.”
12 Pa-lang pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux tag ah ngie lox pa nhie Simiang Siyiex aux ah khaing heue.”
Lox jhai Pa-lang bawg ra
13 Pa-leux ah nin ka Pa-lang:
“Maix hu tau mai aux ka haktiex tix dauh,
khaing dee an mai chix yaox ka pui Yi-seu-liex tix blah.
Viex maix jhax lawg kix son aux dee an.”
14 Nawh viex Pa-lang hoek daux kaung Saw-fe-thiien,
ka piang choek gawng Pi-see-cia,
mai sum khu dee tan aliah mu dee an,
mai tan moi mhaing tix mu,
mai yung mhaing tix mu,
ka piang khu dee tan ku mu heue.
15 Pa-lang ah nin ka Pa-leux:
“Maix cung praok Krax-tan Pa-gleeing maix tix tan,
aux hu kia ka Siyiex plak an.”
16 Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka Pa-lang,
mai krai nawh mhong lox pa sang krai nawh khankix,
khaix an nhie nawh deeh ing ka Pa-leux dee an,
mai krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka nawh.
17 Pa-lang deeh ing,
mai yaox kawn cung Pa-leux tau mai cub simiang pui Maw-ya khankix ka praok Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
Pa-leux chawk ka nawh,
Simiang Siyiex krai lox pa tix,
18 Pa-lang tom krai lox jhai in plak greem:
Hoek heue,
Pa-leux kawn simex Si-paw;
maix hoek nghiet ka lox pa sang krai aux.
19 Siyiex ang awm kawn pui,
ang pie ang lhien,
nawh ang awm kawn pui,
ang chix tieh loh ka lox pa krai tix.
Gum lox pa hoik deuh nawh,
mai nawh tag yuh lih mawh tete;
gum lox pa lih khaing ka daux jheeh nawh,
tag sang lih tix mawh kaing.
20 Aux nawb buan khaing gum lox pa tawx nawh;
Buan son pa tawx Siyiex,
aux ang pon tix teei deeh.
21 Aux hoik tawng sivoe ka kaing pa sang hoek tix lih ka piang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
ang koe krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
ang koe krax laux krax leeing.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix ot tau mai kix;
kix yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex mawh simiang kix.
22 Siyiex hoik viex kix lih khaing Ai-cix;
nawh awm moi tiag yuh nan,
pa yuh naing son kix.
23 Si meed sat ceu pa tix ang ah,
ang pon yuh Ya-keux laux;
ang koe pui pa yuh si meed pon yuh Yi-seu-liex laux.
Yam in,
pui sang krai lao ka Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
Jhak heue,
Siyiex tag sang yuh ka kaing pa ting ceu lih!
24 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang awm sivai sang si siang pa yaog tix kaoh,
awm sivai sang si mhaing pa ting riang ting rao;
nawh ang bleet ka to tiag pa hoik kied tix khankix,
phan ang nyaux ka nham to sat pa kua khankix,
mai ang nyang vhah rhawm ka heue.
25 Pa-leux ah nin ka Pa-lang:
“Maix ang jhax lawg ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
viang mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx kawx paw nawb buan ka piang kix khaing!”
26 Pa-lang pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang aux hoik krai ka maix,
aux tag sang yuh awm gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux ang mawh lie?”
Lox jhai Pa-lang bawg loe
27 Pa-leux ah nin ka Pa-lang:
“Hawt khaix aux,
aux sang viex maix hu ka haktiex tix dauh;
aux keud Siyiex kieh maix jhax lawg kix son aux ka dee an heue.”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh viex Pa-lang haok ka dee kok chix yaox,
ka daux tiag piang choek gawng Phi-eu.
29 Pa-lang ah nin:
“Maix sum khu dee tan son aux aliah mu dee in,
mai viex moi mhaing aliah mu mai yung mhaing aliah mu hoek.”
30 Pa-leux hawt tix yuh,
mai tan moi mhaing tix mu mai yung mhaing tix mu ka piang khu dee tan ku mu heue.
