Keu-keu pa mawh kraung siao Simiang Siyiex
38
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 anawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka Keu-keu simiang piang meung Mi-seux mai Thu-pa ra meung ka haktiex Ma-keux.
Maix tag caw tix ne raw ka nawh,
3 krai ka nawh,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
aux tag sang tieb tie ka maix.
4 Aux tag sang kling maix deeh hoek,
tag sang teei goik goik kap maix,
mai reu maix mai puing naing pa thung maix hu tau.
Pui naing maix hoik koe brung naing tom nie,
mai pui naing brug brung pa ceep kraung tix ceu khaing.
Pui naing ku kaux hoik viex ka dieb tie naok mai vaig.
5 Ka daux pui naing maix kawn koe pui naing pa vai khaing Paw-see,
Ku-seu,
mai Fu;
kix hoik viex ka dieb tie naok,
mai hoik ceep ka mhok rhiem khawm uik.
6 “Khaix an kawn koe pui naing pa hoek khaing Keu-miiex,
mai Thaw-cia-max pa ot ka haktiex plak laong,
kawn koe pui naing pa hoek khaing meung tang meung khankix.
7 Maix tag caw tix gau ka pui naing mhawm mhawm,
to kaux maix kawx tag caw tix rien khre tix mawh cao pa bung kaing naing ku bawg ku yam.
8 Baox luan ra loe num,
aux tag sang kieh maix hu pon kuang ka meung haktiex tix meung;
pui meung haktiex an mawh pa deeh hoek khaing meung haktiex ku meung,
yam in hoik mawh bawg yam pa ot ka daux pa nhim pa tiiem.
Maix tag caw tix puing tix laig ka gawng cie pui Yi-seu-liex khankix;
haktiex in hoik mawh pa tik vud pui sivoe an,
pa kied riem tete,
viang mawh nan,
pui hoik paung ot ka yam in tom nie.
9 Pui naing pa thung maix,
mai pui naing meung ku meung pa hoek tix yuh naing tau mai maix,
tag sang awm lon pai awm pa kheem ka haktiex in,
awm bhaung ting pa phlu klauh ka haktiex in khawm uik heue.”
10 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka Keu-keu ah nin:
“Hoek yam an,
maix tag sang chix koe krax keud pa pie lhien,
11 mai puing ka veng an kix pa ang kaux cong,
pa mawh pa ang koe guh,
ang koe krax pa tieb tie tang ceu,
veng pa nyawm ot nyawm koe pui daux.
12 Maix tag caw tix tud rhim ka veng pa riem sivoe an,
yam in pui hoik deeh naog kah.
Pui pa ot daux hoik mawh pa khrawm tix hoek khaing meung ku meung,
mai hoek paung ot ka daux dee greung haktiex in.
Kix hoik koe khawng brie mai sin ngao tom nie.
13 Pui See-pa,
pui Ti-tang,
mai pui yuh lai gee pa hoek khaing veng Tha-see ku veng tag sang chawk maix ah nin:
‘maix khrawm pui naing tom nie hoek tix puing mawh son sang tud sang rhim lie?
Maix keud tix sang teei ka mau khri,
ka khawng brie,
ka sin ngao son sang kieh mawh kraung khrai pa pon tix khaing daux naing lie?’
14 “Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix deeh krai ka Keu-keu,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
jawm yam pon pui cie aux krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
maix tag caw tix hu.b
15 Maix tag caw tix lih hu khaing haktiex tix pa ot plak laong,
mai thung pui naing pa brug brung pa khrawm hoek khaing ku meung pa kied kua khankix,
16 puing ka pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex;
pui naing cie maix tag sang awm lon pai awm kheem ka haktiex khawm uik.
Yam an,
aux tag sang ploi maix hu puing ka haktiex cie aux,
son sang kieh meung ka piang haktiex tawng yung ka aux;
aux kawx tag sang kieh can pa yuh maix,
mai kieh kix tawng sidawng grawng mawh aux pa singax singeh.
17 Maix hoik mawh pui pa krai aux sivoe an,
pa tag sang lih hoek tix kaux.
Yam an,
aux tag sang kieh siliex tix pa mawh cao jhai pui Yi-seu-liex khankix krai pao lox jhai,
singaix pa sang hoek aux tag sang kok pui tix kaux hoek puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka Keu-keu
18 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Yam puing Keu-keu ka pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang song rhawm tete.
19 Aux krai pao ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix yuh nin:
ka daux singaix an,
ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang lih koe kla reem pa ting.
20 Kax pa koe daux rawm,
sim pa pu daux rao ma,
to tiag pa ting pa iak pa koe piang tiex,
khawm kawn pui khankix hoik si yaung si kua kheu lhat graung tix ka aux.
Gawng lhaong gawng iak tag sang bih,
guh veng ghrup.
21 Aux tag sang teei krax kha rhawm pa koe ku ceu kieh Keu-keu lhat graung,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin.
Pui naing cie nawh tag sang simah simaing ka paox tix,
mai ra lai pauh paox tix yum.
22 Aux tag sang kieh jhawm saux preng mai krax pa jauh nham ciyang ka nawh.
Aux tag sang kieh lhiex ting,
mu pre,
ngu pa ting,
mhat lih ka piang to kaux pui naing pa thung maix mai ka piang to kaux cao bung pui naing khankix.
23 Aux tag sang yuh nin,
mai kieh meung ka piang haktiex tawng grawng mawh tix Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong,
pa singax singeh.
Yam an,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
