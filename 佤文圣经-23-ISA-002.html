Ka daux pa nhim leeing ju leeing phan
(Lai Mi-cia 4.1–3)
2
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa tawx Siyiex ka Yi-sai-yax kawn simex Ya-maw-see,
grawh grawng meung Yo-tax mai veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
2 Ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
nyiex buan pa ot piang gawng tag sang jung tix ka piang gawng lua khawm uik,
tag yaog lhaong khaing gawng pa koe khawm uik.
Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang preeng tix hoek.
3 Ceu pui tom nie tag sang ghrang paox tix hoek sivoe ah nin:
hoek heue,
hu ex tau ka gawng Simiang Siyiex,a
hu ex tau ka plak nyiex buan Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh tag sang chix jhi ka krax pa sang khaw ex tix lawng;
ex tag caw tix hu ka daux krax in.
Krax pa sidiex Simiang Siyiex hoik jauh tix lih khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung;
gum lox nawh hoik jauh tix sidiex tix hu khaing gawng Shi-ang.
 
4 bSimiang Siyiex tag sang kieh raw ka simah pa koe daux pui ku ceu,
tieb tie ka krax pa simah pa ting.
Kix tag sang raug vaig gawn lih mawh to thai,
tawk pliah lih mawh si vawg.
Ka piang haktiex khawm bee ma ang lai baox lih koe naing,
ang lai baox rien khre son sang puing naing heue.
5 Doeh ceu cad Ya-keux eue,
hoek heue,
kieh ex hawt tix hu awm pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex pa riang heue.
Pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tag ang pon pex
6 Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tik vud ka pui cie tix pa mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-keux.
Ka daux meung hoik lih koe pui pa yuh si meed yuh sat ku daux ku dee,
pa hoek khaing plak lih singaix mai khaing daux pui Fe-li-see dee an;
kawn pui hoik hawt ka been pui tang ceu tang cad.
7 Ka daux meung kix hoik naog ka mau khri,
sin ngao ang dak lai uik simin;
ka daux haktiex kix hoik naog ka brung law,
mai kawn koe ling law naing pa ang uik simin.
8 Haktiex kix hoik naog ka riang rao pa krub pui;
kix krub ka kraung ceu pa teei taix tix yuh ka heue.
9 Kix tang dee yuh ka kaing ceu pa ang khaw,
tang dee laux leeing yuh to kaux tix.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi pluat ploi ka phi map kix heue!
10 cKix tag sang mox tix ka daux dawh rang,
mox tix ka daux rawng greem tiex,
mai ce tix sang mox tix khaing krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
pon tix luat luan khaing siawp sibrawm Siyiex mai min plak ngaix nawh.
11 Bawg yam sang hoek,
krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi kawn pui tag sang naox tix,
krax pa lhaong rhi kawn pui tag sang grai grup;
mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex pa pon krax pa yaog yaw khaing.
12 Ka daux singai an,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
tag sang yeen tiiem ka pui pa koe siawp sibrawm,
mai pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
13 Nawh tag sang reeig tix tik ka khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noeng pa ting pa lhaong,
mai yuh laux ka khaox yang yit Pa-sang.
14 Nawh tag yuh choek gawng ting gawng iak si dawm,
15 mai cot ka gawng mu lhaong mai ka guh veng pa kaux pa cong goh.
16 Nawh tag sang kieh reu pa ting pa mhawm Tha-see com.
17-18 Yam hoik hoek singaix an,
krax pa ting rhawm kawn pui tag sang naox tix,
krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi kawn pui tag sang grai grup;
riang rao pa krub pui tag sang yuh laux leeing,
mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex pa pon krax pa yaog yaw khaing heue.
19 Yam yung yang Simiang Siyiex ka riah haktiex,
kawn pui tag sang mox tix ka daux dawh rang,
mox tix ka daux rawng greem tiex,
mai ce tix sang mox tix khaing krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
pon mox tix khaing siawp sibrawm Siyiex mai min plak ngai nawh.
20 Yam hoik hoek singaix an,
kix tag sang tik ka can riang rao mau khri pa hoik raug tix,
mai tik tix tawx ka kiang mai blak.
21 Yam yung yang Simiang Siyiex ka riah haktiex,
kawn pui tag sang mox tix ka daux dawh rang,
mai ka daux rhoi khaix bih rang,
kix ce tix sang mox tix khaing krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
pon mox tix khaing siawp sibrawm Siyiex mai min plak ngai nawh heue.
22 Paw lai baox yum rhawm ka lox pui sang yum,
kheu koe kix ngawh jun ceu pa tix lie?
