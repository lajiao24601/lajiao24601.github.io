2
a“Khaix an,
ex hawt ka gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
deeh gah plak hu tix,
mai hu krax plak daux tiag Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
Yix hoik vung viang ka gawng haktiex Yi-tong tom rheeng bawg yam heue.
2-3 “Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux,
yix hoik kub vung viang ot ka piang gawng daux tiag,
mai khaw tix hu plak laong.
4 bNawh nhie aux krai gum lox in plak greem ka pex ah nin:
‘Pex tag tiang ka gawng Yi-tong,
pa mawh dee ot doeh ceu cad paox nyiex pa singai khaing pex Yi-sao khankix.
Viang kix lhat ka pex kawx,
mai pex kawn caw rien to kaux tix mhawm mhawm;
5 ang pon tix cheeik ka kix;
mawh kheu ang aux tawx haktiex in ka pex,
khawm haktiex pa dig tiag caong pex kawx aux ang tawx ka pex.
Aux hoik tawx haktiex Yi-tong mawh cie doeh ceu cad Yi-sao.
6 Pex tag chix tix liag priex preem,
mai rawm nyaux khankix ka kix heue.’
7 “Pex hoik tawng,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex hoik tawx buan yuh ka mawx ka piang kaing jhaw pa yuh pex khawm uik.
Yam vung viang pex ka haktiex pa vieh pa va an,
nawh hoik kia ngien ka pex.
Ka daux tipon num,
nawh hoik ot tau mai pex,
mai pex ang lai koe ceu pa cud pa cha tid tix heue.
8 “Khaix an,
ex via ka haktiex Yi-tong,
pa mawh haktiex dee ot doeh ceu cad pui nyiex pui ma pa singai khaing ex Yi-sao khankix,
jauh ka Yi-la-tha mai Yi-shuing Cia-piie,
bleeh ka krax hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
mai hu ka plak lih singaix,
plak laong ka daux tiag Maw-ya.
9 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
‘Pex ang pon tix yuh krax tuk tiak tuk te ka pui Maw-ya,
phan ang mawh nan,
cheeik ka kix.
Aux hoik tawx veng Ya-eux mawh cie doeh ceu cad Law-teux;
mai aux ang tawx haktiex cie kix ka pex heue.’ ”
10 Sivoe an dix,
koe ceu pui pa kied ting lhaong tix ceu pa ot ka daux veng Ya-eux;
kaux pui kix kied hun,
mai pak peung to kaux kix kied ting lhaong awm ceu pui Ya-na.
11 Kix awm ceu pui Ya-na,
mai kok ceu kaux kix mawh pui Li-fa-ying,
viang mawh nan,
pui Maw-ya kok kix mawh ceu pui Yi-mi.
12 Pui Heu-lix khankix hoik dawg ot ka haktiex Yi-tong;
mawh doeh ceu cad Yi-sao khankix pa grheu kix hu,
mai yuh meung kix laux leeing,
mai ot tan,
awm hoik grheu pui Yi-seu-liex ka pui pa koeng lih khaing haktiex pa tawx Siyiex ka kix yuh nan.
13 Khaix an,
ex hawt ka gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
mai tiang ka rawm klawng iak Sa-liex;
14 dan mawh bawg yam khaix hoik lih hu yix khaing Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax ka daux tix ngoe daix num.
Hawt awm lox pa hoik tiex tu Simiang Siyiex,
pui naing ka daux bawg yam an hoik nyao yum pak lawg.
15 Simiang Siyiex ca yuh kaing pa ang mhawm ka kix ang jud,
tom hoik laux leeing kix khawm uik heue.
16 “Khaix hoik yum kix khawm uik,
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka yix:
18 ‘Daux in singaix,
pex tag sang kaoh khaing veng Ya-eux,
mai tiang hu phlat ka haktiex Maw-ya.
19 eKhaix an pex tag sang yaox tix hoek dex ka haktiex doeh ceu cad Law-teux pui Ya-meung;
viang mawh nan,
pex ang pon tix tuk tiak ka pui Ya-meung,
phan ang mawh nan yuh naing ka kix.
Aux hoik tawx haktiex in mawh cie doeh ceu cad Law-teux;
mai aux ang tawx haktiex in ka pex heue.’ ”
20 Haktiex in deeh baox kok mawh haktiex Li-fa-ying,
mawh kheu pui pa ot dee an sivoe hoik mawh pui Li-fa-ying.
Pui Ya-meung kok kix mawh Sang-cu-ming.
21 Kaux hak kix ting lhaong awm ceu pui Ya-na yuh nan,
kaux pui kix haoh hun,
mawh ceu pui pa kied ting lhaong heue.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex yuh kix laux leeing;
pui Ya-meung tom yaox tix pon cax ka haktiex kix,
mai ot tan heue.
22 Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh ka kaing pa awm paox,
son doeh ceu cad Yi-sao pa mawh pui Yi-tong.
Nawh hoik yuh laux ka pui Heu-lix pa ot ka haktiex Yi-tong;
mai pui Yi-tong tom pon cax ka haktiex an,
mai ot ka dee an tom yam in.
23 Pui pa kaoh khaing klawn tiex Kheu-li-theu hoik ot ka hawt Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong;
kix yuh laux ka pui Ya-wue pa ot ka sivoe an,
mai pon ka haktiex cie kix,
tom hoek ka veng Cia-sa plak seh heue.
24 “Khaix hoik tiang ex ka daux Maw-ya,
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ka ex ah nin:
‘Yam in pex lih hu,
mai tiang ka rawm klawng Ya-noeng.
Aux hoik ap Si-hong simiang Ya-maw-lix,
pa yuh veng ting ka Si-seu-peung,
mai haktiex cie nawh ka daux tawm taix pex khawm uik.
Pex hu yuh naing ka nawh,
mai pon tix cax ka haktiex cie nawh.
25 Jauh ka daux in singaix,
aux tag kieh pui ku ceu piang haktiex lhat graung ka pex.
Yam mhong kix ka ceu kaux pex,
kix tag sang yaoh yee mai si yaung si kua heue.’ ”
Pui Yi-seu-liex pex ka simiang Si-seu-peung
(Lai Simin-pui 21.21–30)
26 “Khaix an,
aux ploi kawn cau khaing daux tiag Ci-ti-maw hu ka Si-hong simiang Si-seu-peung,
mai siao lox pa mhawm ah nin ka nawh:
27 ‘Khiox khaw maix kieh yix laig daux haktiex cie maix liak.
Yix tag sang lawng ngie ka daux krax ting,
tom hoek luan ka plak an,
ang lih hu khaing daux krax ting.
28 Yix cu tix lih mau son sang liag priex preem,
mai rawm nyaux ka pex.
Yix sang lawng ngie daux haktiex pex khaing,
29 tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai laig ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap Siyiex tix tawx ka yix.
Doeh ceu pui Yi-sao pa ot ka Yi-tong mai pui Maw-ya pa ot ka veng Ya-eux khankix,
hoik cu yix tiang ka daux haktiex kix heue.’
30 “Viang mawh nan,
simiang Si-hong ang cu yix laig ka daux haktiex tix.
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex kieh nawh kiien rhawm,
son sang pex ex ka nawh,
mai pon tix cax ka haktiex cie nawh;
hoek tom yam in,
mai yix ca kawn pon ka haktiex in heue.
31 “Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
‘Jhak heue,
aux hoik ap tix tawx simiang Si-hong mai haktiex cie nawh ka pex.
Pex tag sang haok cax ka heue.’
32 Si-hong thung kru naing tix kuan rieb pex ka veng Ya-cax.
33 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex ap kix khawm uik ka ex,
mai ex pauh nawh,
mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
khawm pui naing nawh khawm uik yum.
34 Ex baox pon cax,
pon yuh ka veng ot kix laux ku veng,
khawm pui pa ot ka daux veng,
simex ang ah,
bun ang ah,
kawn nyawm ang ah,
pui kuad ju ang ah,
tag pauh yum khawm uik,
mai deuk tix kaux kawx.
35 Ex tud ka khawng brie kix hu,
mai rhim ka veng yaong ot kix uik.
36 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex pon cax ka veng ka yaong khawm uik,
jauh ka praok daux ngawd Ya-noeng hoek ka Ya-law-eu,
hoek ka yaong ka veng sigreung ngawd,
tom si dauh hoek ka Ci-liex khawm uik.
37 Viang mawh nan,
hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
ex ang baox yaox tix hoek dex ka haktiex pui Ya-meung,
mai ka hawt rawm klawng Ya-paw khawm ra plak,
mai ang baox yaox tix hu ka veng ka yaong pa koe piang gawng khankix ku dauh heue.”
