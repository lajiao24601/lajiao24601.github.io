27
bKa daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang teei vaig pa lawm ciyang si yawng,
pa mawh si uing prim pa yie tieh loh tix,
khaix an mai pauh ka to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui ka heue.a
2 Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang krai grawng si peem plix siiet pa hoik khaw rhawm nawh kah,
ah nin:
3 “Aux pau ka si peem in,
mai jauh ka rawm ang jud;
aux hoik pau ka leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
mai ang koe pui pa yuh laux tix kaux kawx.
4 Aux ang lai baox deeh song rhawm ka si peem plix siiet.
Phan ka daux si peem koe pang kat mai yeem kat,
mai aux tag sang gleeing ka khawm uik.
5 Phan pui pa koeng ka pui cie aux keud tix sang kia ngien ka aux,
mai tag caw ran kieh tix khaw lox mai aux sivoe.
Khaw heue,
mai kix tag caw ran kieh tix khaw lox mai aux heue.”
6 Ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
pui Yi-seu-liex,
kawn praix Ya-keux,
tag sang kaux riah awm khaox yuh nan;
kix tag sang plaung,
pruh tai,
plix ka daux haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
7 Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ang ciyang kin awm ciyang ka pui pa koeng yuh nan.
Nawh pauh ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ang nie pauh awm hoik pauh nawh ka pui pa koeng yuh nang.
8 Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka pui cie tix tom cien,
mai grheu kix hu;
nawh kieh bhaung ting phlu hoek khaing plak lih singaix,
mai klia kix hu heue.
9 Lheu khaing yieh pui Yi-seu-liex tix tik khu dee tan pa hoik sum kix son siyiex pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai cot ghrup ka riang rong siyiex bun Ya-seu-la mai ka khu dee tan nyawng phawng,
phi map kix tag ang doh pon krax pa pluat ploi heue.
10 Veng pa kaux pa cong hoik lih mawh haktiex pa riem,
ang koe tid tix ka daux,
mai hoik awm daux tiag yuh na;
moi it tix sawm rib dee an.
11 Kak khaox hoik pot kroh khawm uik,
mai bun miex khankix hu sut tix kieh mawh khix taung.
Mawh kheu kawn pui hoik daox aw,
mai Siyiex kix ang lai baox deeh priex kia ka kix,
cao pa phan pa yuh kix ang lai baox deeh caox rhawm ka kix heue.
12 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
hoek yam an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang hoek tix kok pex ku kaux,
jauh ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti hoek ka daog haktiex Ai-cix,
mai hoik awm guah pui nghox ca mai kam tang ot yuh nan heue.
13 Yam hoek singaix an,
lox pa phlu teet tawng luah tag sang kok ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa hoik vuk vied vuk viang tix ot ka haktiex Ya-sux mai Ai-cix khawm uik.
Kix tag sang krub nawh ka gawng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa mawh gawng Simiang Siyiex pa singax singeh heue.
