Kraung ceep cie cao giex kaing li
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 39.1–7)
28
“Maix laug tix teei pux ke tix Ya-loeng,
khawm kawn simex nawh Na-ta,
Ya-pi-hux,
Yi-li-ya-sa,
Yi-tha-ma ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
ap kix mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux.
2 Yuh kraung ceep pa singax singeh son pux ke maix Ya-loeng pa mawh cao giex kaing li,
mai kieh nawh mawh pa koe min plak ngai mai pa mhawm grang.
3 Maix kok ka pui pa hoik tawx aux koe ping nyia khankix hoek,
mai tawx lox nhie kix caing kraung son Ya-loeng,
kieh nawh tan to kaux tix mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux.
4 Tawx lox nhie kix yuh na bat naok,
sibex singax singeh Yi-fu-teu,
sibex dha,
sibex plak daux pa koe lai tai,
lawg kaing,
mai maox rawd nyoi.
Kix yuh pux ke maix Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix ceep ka kraung pa singax singeh in kix,
son sang mawh kix cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux.
5 Cang mai khankix tag teei keei khri,
keei pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt mai man keei sum caing ka heue.
6 “Kix tag sang yuh sibex singax singeh Yi-fu-teu ka keei khri,
keei pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt mai man keei sum,
mai sau kieh koe lai tai.
7 Ka piang klip Yi-fu-teu khawm ra plak,
tag caing maox klip ka ra nyaing.
8 Siao man pa awm paox yuh maox tix nyaing,
mai caing kleuk tix ka Yi-fu-teu,
lih mawh tix nyaing khaing.
9 Teei siang simaox bawd raoh ra mu,
mai saik tix deuh ceu kaux kawn simex Ya-keux khankix ka piang,
10 mai hawt awm pa keh kix hawt paox tix yuh nan,
tix khlawp simaox khri an tiiem ceu kaux kix ot ka piang liah kaux.
11 Sawg cang mai pa blawn chix taix saik tix deuh ka ceu kaux kawn simex Ya-keux khankix ka piang siang simaox an ra mu,
mai sau siang simaox an ot ka daux jhawk khri pa singax singeh,
12 khaix an kawb ot ka piang maox klip Yi-fu-teu khawm ra plak,
son sang mawh son min pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu.
Yuh nin,
Ya-loeng tag yaox tix viex ceu kaux kix kao ra ceu ka piang klip tix,
son sang kieh aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex doh keud ka pui cie tix leeing ju leeing phan.
13 Yuh daux jhawk khri an ra mu
14 mai maox khri mhawm ra gawng;
pa duing tix awm maox yuh nan,
mai vien ka piang daux jhawk an heue.”
Dieb naok
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 39.8–21)
15 “Caw tix yuh na bat naok son cao giex kaing li pa ting mai siao hawt prawng tud krax keud Siyiex,
yuh awm Yi-fu-teu,
caing ka keei khri,
pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
mai pa raoh rawt,
mai kieh koe lai tai.
16 Pak peung na bat naok an mawh pon pak,
mai niep tix koe ra jan,
plak lang tix nga ra kongfeeng,
plak vieh tix nga ra kongfeeng.
17 “Ka piang na bat naok sau siang simaox ka piang pon rhoi,
rhoi tix sau siang simaox raoh,
siang simaox lheung mai siang simaox pa raoh.
18 Rhoi ra sau siang simaox pa klai,
siang simaox pa klai rao ma mai siang simaox pa toh riang;
19 rhoi loe sau siang simaox pa klai si ngi,
siang simaox bawd paing mai siang simaox pa raoh pliex;
20 rhoi pon sau siang simaox pa klai,
siang simaox pa raoh mai siang simaox pa paing pau.
Siang in kix tag sau ka piang jaong khri khawm uik.
21 Ka piang siang simaox kao mu ra an kix,
tag saik ceu kaux kawn simex Ya-keux ot ka tix kaux tix khlawp,
son sang mawh pa tang min ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ceu ra.
22 Yuh maox ceng na bat naok ka khri mhawm,
greeh grang kieh awm maox pa duing tix tau.
23 Yuh daux jhawk khri ra mu,
mai kieh toe kleuk paox tix ka piang na bat naok khawm ra plak,
24 mai phrawk maox khri pa hoik duing tix tau ra nyaing kaux ka daux jhawk toh dawb tie naok khawm ra toh.
25 “Toh maox khri plak an khawm ra toh plak an,
phrawk tix kaux ka piang daux jhawk khri an ra mu,
yuh nin kix tom vien ka piang maox rhawt klip pa koe plak sivoe ngai Yi-fu-teu.
26 Yuh daux jhawk khri ra mu,
mai kleuk ot ka greem toh na bat naok khawm ra toh,
dex ka daog plak daux Yi-fu-teu.
27 Baox yuh daux jhawk ra mu,
mai kleuk tix ka piang maox rawd klip ra nyaing ka plak greem sivoe ngai Yi-fu-teu,
dex ka gawng caing,
ka piang maox pa hoik jawg bre tix taing mhawm.
28 Teei maox pa klai rao ma,
phrawk daux jhawk na bat naok mai daux jhawk Yi-fu-teu ot tau,
kieh na bat naok ot ka piang maox Yi-fu-teu,
son ang sang chix ghah.
29 “Yam laig Ya-loeng ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
caw tix viex ka na bat naok in pa hoik tiiem ceu kaux pui Yi-seu-liex kao ceu ra ot kah;
yuh nin,
aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tom tag chix doh keud ka pui cie tix leeing ju leeing phan.
30 bSau ‘Wu-ling’ mai ‘Thu-ming’ a ot ka daux na bat naok an,
son sang kieh Ya-loeng viex laig yam hoek ka plak sivoe ngai aux.
Yuh nin,
yam ot nawh ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
nawh tag yaox tix viex ka dieb naok jheeng jheeng,
mai tag chix prawng ka pui cie aux,
awm pa khaw mai rhawm keud aux heue.”
Sibex ceep cao giex kaing li tang kaux
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 39.22–31)
31 “Sibex dha pa ceep piang Yi-fu-teu,
sang mawh pa caing tix ka keei pa klai rao ma khawm uik.
32 Kieh plak kaing ka dee sigreung kraung dha koe daux ngawg tix mu;
vung viang ngawg kraung dha an baox caing ka daog,
son ang sang griah.
33-34 Vung viang jaong sibex dha caing tix ka keei pa klai rao ma,
keei pa lung pliex,
keei pa raoh rawt yuh ka riang plix mak cang,
mai sau kawn hing pa yuh ka khri mhawm khan ka si nax.
35 Yam yuh Ya-loeng ka kaing cao giex kaing li,
tag sang ceep ka sibex dha in.
Yam laig nawh ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
mai hoek ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
phan ang mawh nan yam deeh lih,
aux tag sang chix mhong ka lox pa luah hing an;
yuh nin,
nawh tom tag ang yaox tix yum heue.
36 “Teei khri mhawm yuh khlawp na bat tix khlawp,
mai saik ka piang mawh nin:
‘Kieh pa singax singeh mawh cie Simiang Siyiex’.
37 Teei maox pa klai rao ma phrawk nawh ka piang lawk kaing plak sivoe ngai.
38 Ya-loeng caw tix vhak khlawp na bat in ot ka dong re tix.
Yuh nin,
aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tom tag chix rieb ka khawng phak pa tan lu pui Yi-seu-liex ka aux khawm uik;
viang pui lud loe kawx yam tan lu tix,
aux ca kawn rieb ka heue.
39 “Teei man keei sum caing sibex dha plak daux Ya-loeng,
mai baox yuh lawk kaing ka man keei sum,
teei man taing pa koe lai tai yuh maox rawd nyoi ka heue.
40 “Yuh sibex dha plak daux,
maox rawd nyoi,
mai mhok son kawn simex Ya-loeng khankix,
kieh kix koe min plak ngai mai pa mhawm grang.
41 Ceep kraung an kix ka to kaux pux ke maix Ya-loeng mai ka piang kawn simex nawh khankix,
khaix an kraix bee sime ka kix,
ap pong ara pa singax singeh piang kix,
ap kix mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux.
42 Teei man keei sum yuh kix ceep khlax kah;
khlax an caw tix jauh ka nyoi tom hoek ka ba,
son sang kheem ka nex nham dee kaig tix.
43 Yam laig Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix daux nyiex gox buan man ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
phan ang mawh nan,
yam ot tix yuh kaing cao giex kaing li ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
tag caw tix ceep kah,
son ang to kaux kix sang lih mai pauh yum.
In mawh grawng been pa yuh son Ya-loeng khawm doeh doe ceu pui nawh khankix leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
