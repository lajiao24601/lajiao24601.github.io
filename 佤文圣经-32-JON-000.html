Lai Yaw-na
Krai dawt
Lai Yaw-na ang awm lai cao jhai tang mu, mawh pa tiiem lih kaing ceu pa hoik yaox tix; ka daux lai in tiiem ka kaing ceu pa hoik cim cao jhai ka krax pa keud tix sang lieh lheu ka gum lox tawm Siyiex. Siyiex tawx lox nhie Yaw-na hu ka pui pa koeng Yi-seu-liex, pa mawh veng ting ka daux meung Ya-sux, pa mawh veng Ni-ni-we, viang mawh nan, nawh ang cu tix viex gum lox Siyiex hu ka dee an, mawh kheu keud nawh Siyiex ang tau tix yuh veng an laux leeing awm lox pa ap tix. Khaix hoik lih kaing in tix mawh, Yaw-na tom taug but rhawm tix nghiet ka gum lox tawx in, viang mawh nan, dee si dauh mawh kheu ca kawn ang gum lox pa krai pao nawh lih tix mawh, nawh kied ang naog rhawm ka heue.
Lai in krai sidawng Siyiex hoik koe siawp sibrawm ka ceu pa phan pa yuh tix. Nie blawn krai sidawng ka Siyiex mawh Siyiex pa muh mai krax pa caox rhawm; viang mawh nan, nawh cu tix pluat ploi mai taoh ka pui pa koeng ka pui cie tix, mai ang rhawm ciyang yuh kix laux leeing. Nawh kok yo pui Yi-seu-liex loh rhawm rhi tix, khaix an tawx lox ka kix tag caw tix kieh kawn pui piang haktiex rieb ka krax muh Siyiex mai krax pa pluat ploi phi map heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pa kok ka Yaw-na mai pa lieh lheu nawh lox tawm (1.1–17)
2. Pa loh Yaw-na ka rhawm rhi tix mai pa pon krax taoh (2.1–10)
3. Yaw-na ne raw ka pui veng Ni-ni-we (3.1–10)
4. Krax pa caox rhawm Siyiex ka pui veng Ni-ni-we (4.1–11)
