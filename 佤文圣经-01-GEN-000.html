Lai Yam-jauh
Krai dawt
Dee bleeh Lai Yam-jauh mawh “yam jauh tix phan yuh”. Nawh krai grawng yam phan yuh haktiex mai rao ma tix, yam jauh kawn pui tix koe phi map mai yam jauh kawn pui tix kham krax kha rhawm; mai baox tiiem yam jauh Siyiex tix yuh paox grawm mai kawn pui. Lai in guah tix koe ra baux ting:
1. Simin ting 1 hoek simin ting 11 krai yam jauh rao ma mai haktiex tix phan yuh mai grawng yam jauh kawn pui tix koe, khawm Ya-tang mai Sha-wa, Kai-yin mai Ya-paw, Naw-yax mai pa kheem rawm ting, mai gawng mu Pa-piie heue.
2. Simin ting 12 hoek simin ting 50, krai grawng tax du ceu pui Yi-seu-liex, pa jauh tix koe krax yum rhawm mai tawng ka Siyiex mawh pa kaux Ya-paw-la-hang, khaix an kawn simex nawh Yi-sha, kawn saux nawh Ya-keux kaux Yi-seu-liex, mai kawn simex Ya-keux kao ra kaux pa lih tix mawh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ceu ra, mai grawng Yaw-seux mai Ya-keux khawm cub kawn simex nawh khankix, kix viex yuh ka mawx ka kawn praix tix hoek ot ka Ai-cix heue.
Phuk lai in ang joeng krai ngie ka kawn pui, kaing ting krai grawng pa phan yuh Siyiex. Jauh yam phan yuh Siyiex ka rao ma mai haktiex, mawh kheu gum lox Siyiex, nawh viex kawn praix tix hu khawm uik. Phuk lai in kieh Siyiex mawh kaing ting. Nawh prawng ca, ciyang ka pui pa koe phi map, mai duk thung, teem coi ka kawn praix tix. Nawh mawh cao ka piang khawm uik. Grawng lai in krai ka cub pui pa koe krax yum rhawm khankix, mai teem coi ka cub doeh ceu cad kix mai kieh kix kaux krax yum rhawm heue.
Krai kaing
1. Grawng phan yuh rao ma haktiex mai kawn pui (1.1–2.25)
2. Yam jauh phi map mai krax pa kha rhawm tix (3.1–24)
3. Jauh ka Ya-tang tom hoek ka Naw-yax (4.1–5.32)
4. Grawng Naw-yax mai pa kheem rawm ting (4.1–5.32)
5. Gawng mu Pa-piie (11.1–9)
6. Jauh ka Sang tom hoek ka Ya-paw-lang (11.10–32)
7. Grawng cao kaing khankix: Ya-paw-la-hang, Yi-sha, Ya-keux (12.1–35.29)
8. Doeh ceu cad Yi-sao (36.1–43)
9. Yaw-seux mai cub pux aik nawh khankix (37.1–45.28)
10. Pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ot ka Ai-cix (46.1–50.26)
