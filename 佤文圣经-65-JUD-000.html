Lai Yo-tax
Krai dawt
Lai Yo-tax kaing ting mawh pa ne raw ka sara cao gau pa pie lhien pa tang kok tix mawh kawn Khrit khankix. Pa tiiem ka daux lox lai in dex sang awm lox pa tiiem ot ka daux Lai Pi-teux Phuk Ra yuh nan, cao tiiem tawx krax pa joh jhan ka pui cao phat khankix “nhie kix niied tix ca yuh son Siyiex tix bawg khaing, tag sang pon yaox tix grum tawx ka kawn Khrit ka daux naing krax yum rhawm heue” (3).
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1-2)
2. Krax si daux, krax rhawm keud, mai krax si kah sara pa pie (3-16)
3. Kieh krax yum rhawm kaux cong mai lox (17-23)
4. Lox nawb buan (24-25)
