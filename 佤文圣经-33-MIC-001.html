Lai Mi-cia
1
aKa daux singaix bawg yam pa lai loh simiang Yaw-thang,
Ya-ha-see,
Si-si-cia khankix tix giex ka kaing meung Yo-tax,
Simiang Siyiex plak jhi kaing pa dex ka Sa-ma-li-yax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung ka cao jhai Mi-cia pui Maw-li-seux.
Pa tiiem plak greem mawh lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka cao jhai Mi-cia heue.
Lox rhax yiiem doe son pui Sa-ma-li-yax mai son pui Yie-lu-sa-leung
2 Meung ka piang haktiex eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet heue!
Kawn pui piang haktiex eue,
pex tag caw tix daong yhaok tix nghiet heue!
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang deuh phi map ka pui cie tix;
Simiang Siyiex tag sang mawh cao pa kham sakse khaing rao ma.
3 Jhak heue,
Simiang Siyiex lih khaing daux nyiex buan nawh;
nawh tag sang lih hoek ka piang haktiex,
mai dug dig ka gawng lua.
4 Gawng lua khankix tag sang bih ka greem caong nawh,
awm hoik ceh ngu tien yuh nan;
rang simaox klup ka daux ngawd,
awm chun rawm ting tix lih daux klawng yuh nan heue.
 
5 Kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh tete,
mawh kheu hoik tieh vheen pui Yi-seu-liex ka Siyiex,
hoik phit lud ka nawh.
Pui mawx khaw tix kham krax ciyang son pa lieh lheu pui Yi-seu-liex lie?
Veng ting Sa-ma-li-yax khaw tix klawm heue!
Pui mawx khaw tix peeh ka phi map pa krub pui Yo-tax ka riang rao lie?
Veng ting Yie-lu-sa-leung khaw tix peeh heue!
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh veng Sa-ma-li-yax lih tix mawh daux tiag pa riem,
lih mawh dee sum pui plix siiet kah.
Aux tag sang nyheen guh veng an lih ka daux ngawd,
mai kieh riah jhi tix lih.
7 Can riang rao pa koe daux veng khawm uik,
tag sang yuh laux leeing;
sin ngao pa pon khaing pa coh kaux,
tag sang gleeing laux;
aux tag sang kieh riang rao pa krub pui lih mawh kram nyiex.
Pui Sa-ma-li-yax khrawm riang rao in kix kieh mawh Siyiex pa lih tix keh hao hun,
pui koeng tag sang tud rhim kix hu,
mai viex hoek tang dauh kieh bun coh kaux haktiex an krub heue.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Mi-cia ah nin:
“Aux tag sang laux rhawm voi rhi son kaing in;
aux tag sang hu ka caong giex tix,
mai tauh hu ku dauh ku dee.
Aux tag sang riag yo awm sox tiag,
yiiem doe awm sim ka-la-uk.
9 Rhoi khaix saux pui Sa-ma-li-yax ang lai pon chix pai moi;
rhoi khaix mat pui Yo-tax kawx ang lai pon chix moi.
Krax laux leeing yuh siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung laux,
nied tix dex ka nyiex ma paox ceu paox cad aux heue.”
Pui koeng nied tix tuk tiak dex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
10 Paw vi krai pao hoik som nai pex ka haktiex Cia-theux,
paw vi kieh pui koeng yaox pa lih rawm ngai pex.
Pui haktiex Paw Ya-fu-la eue,
laux rhawm lu lieh pex piang tiex heue!
11 Pui haktiex Sa-fe eue,
pex tag sang kham krax kaig krax tua,
ot ka kaong,
vu vie tix hoek ka meung tang meung heue!
Pui haktiex Sha-nang ang doh pon yaox tix luat luan.
Yam mhong pex ka lox yiiem pui haktiex Paw Yi-shiie,
pex taug tawng daux veng ang koe dee mox tix.
12 Pui haktiex Ma-luix simie krax laux rhawm pon grai hu,
mawh kheu hoik viex Simiang Siyiex krax laux rhawm hoek ka siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue!
13 Pui haktiex La-ciex eue,
pex tag caw tix sau ling law ka brung naing,
mai rien tix to heue!
Mawh kheu hawt pex ka phi map pa yuh pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai gau pui Yie-lu-sa-leung yuh phi map.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yo-tax eue,
pex tag caw tix krai lox sang tang hu tix khaing veng Maw-li-seu Cia-theux;
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ang pon krax teem coi khaing haktiex Ya-keu-si heue.
15 Pui haktiex Ma-li-sa eue,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ap pex daux tawm taix pui koeng;
kix tag sang puing teei ka veng pex.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pa koe min khankix tag sang to mox tix hoek ka daux dawh rang Ya-tu-lang.
16 Pui Yo-tax eue,
tag caw tix khlao hauk kaing tix,
mai laux rhawm son kawn simex kawn bun pa hoik muh tix;
tag caw tix khlao ghle awm klang preng yuh nan,
mawh kheu pui tag sang grheu kix hu heue.
