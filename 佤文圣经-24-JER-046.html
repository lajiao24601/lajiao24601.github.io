Pui Ai-cix yaox krax kha rhawm ka haktiex Cia-ci-mi-see
46
Simiang Siyiex krai aux mhong grawng kaing pa dex pa dawt mai meung in kix.
2 aPhan mawh Ai-cix mawh nin:
nawh krai son pui naing simiang Ai-cix pa kaux Ni-keu,
yam mawh Yaw-ya-cing simiang Yo-tax ka daux pon num,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
pon pex nawh ka Cia-ci-mi-see dee dex ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
Nawh ah nin:
3 Simiang naing Ai-cix tawx lox ah nin:
rien khre ka dieb tie naok heue!
Ceep kraung naing laig puing naing heue!
4 Sau an brung,
mai brug brung naing heue!
Dia tix hawt paox,
mai ceep mhok rhiem heue!
Klaing pliah klaing vaig tom lawm,
mai ceep mhok kraung rhiem heue!
 
5 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex chawk ah nin:
aux yaox ceu pa tix lie?
Kix yaoh yee mai deeh plak khaix tix;
pui naing kix laux leeing,
mai sau riang tix to,
ang pon veeh kaing tix deeh.
Sivie siveng kix hoik naog ka krax lhat krax graung heue!
6 Pui pa brax to kawx ang lai pon to;
pie pui naing kawx ang lai pon to mox tix.
Ka haktiex plak laong,
ka praok rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
kix hoik laux leeing khawm uik.
7 Pa haok huan awm rawm klawng Ni-law mawh mawx lie?
Pa ting awm rawm klawng khankix mawh mawx lie?
8 Mawh Ai-cix pa haok huan awm rawm klawng Ni-law,
pa ting awm rawm klawng yuh nan.
Ai-cix ah nin:
aux tag sang haok huan,
mai kheem ka haktiex;
Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka veng,
ka pui pa ot daux veng.
9 Pui naing brug brung,
mai ling law preeng tix hu heue!
Ploi pui naing hu puing ka naing yuh nin:
pui Ku-see mai pui Fu viex dieb tie naok;
pui Lui-ti-yax viex ka ak puh heue.
 
10 In hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
pa mawh singaix bawg yam simiang kru naing yuh nin:
in singaix,
nawh tag sang pawk bix;
in singaix,
nawh tag sang ciyang ka pui koeng.
Vaig cie nawh tag sang sak ka ih nex pui koeng,
mai vhah rhawm ka nyaux tix nham pui koeng.
In singaix,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong simiang kru naing,
tag sang ot ka praok rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti ka plak laong,
kieh pui koeng mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa tan lu heue.
11 Pui Ai-cix eue,
haok ka Ci-liex heue,
hoek tix sawg sidah kraix dee an heue!
Can sidah pa koe maix ang lai koe ceu;
ang koe sidah pa pon pai maix moi.
12 Meung ka piang haktiex hoik mhong ka kaing pa ang mhawm maix;
haktiex deeh pawk ka lox yiiem doe maix.
Pui naing ra lai si kleeing ka paox tix;
kix hoik mat khawm ra plak heue.
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoek tix yuh naing
13 bYam laig simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha tix puing ka Ai-cix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
Nawh ah nin:
14 tag caw tix krai pao ka veng Ai-cix ku mu,
mai krai pao ka Mi-tawx,
ka Naw-fu,
ka Ta-pi-nix ah nin:
kaoh,
rien khre tix yuh naing heue!
Krax naing tag sang bleet laux leeing ka pa koe maix khawm uik.
15 Yuh ka mawx siyiex moi ting Ya-pi-see lai goh lie?
Hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex pa yuh goh heue!
16 Pui naing pex hoik laux leeing khawm uik.
Kix ra lai ah nin ka paox tix:
kaoh heue!
Deeh ing ka nyiex heue!
Deeh ing ka paox ceu paox cad tix dee an,
mai to mox tix khaing ka vaig gawn pui koeng heue!
 
17 Tawx simiang Ai-cix koe ceu kaux tix ceu heue!
Kok nawh ah nin:
simiang pa krai lox giex pa hoik nai ka bawg yam.
18 Aux mawh simiang;
ceu kaux aux mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh simiang kru naing.
Aux hoik tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux pa im ju tix leeing ju ah nin:
pui naing pa ting cie Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha pa hoek tix con,
tag sang awm gawng Tha-paw pa lhaong khaing gawng khawm uik,
pa sum tix ot ka piang gawng Cia-mix yuh nan heue.
19 Pui Ai-cix eue,
tag caw tix mad kaux ka bau tix,
mai rien mox tix to heue!
Naw-fu tag sang tieh tix lih mawh dee riem,
mai ka haktiex dee an ang yaox pui tix kaux heue.
20 Ai-cix awm moi siang pa kluing tix mu,
moi pa kaoh khaing plak laong,
yia tix hoek lauh ka nawh.
21 Pui naing pa vai nawh awm kawn moi kluing pa laung daux kawg yuh nan,
mai kling tix tang yaog tix to ku mu,
ang koe pui pa yuh naing tix kaux kawx.
Singaix bawg yam si pup kix krax laux krax leeing hoik hoek;
ka daux bawg yam kham kix krax ciyang hoik hoek heue!
22 Yam hoik khin pui koeng tix dex,
Pui Ai-cix yaog tix to,
hoik awm lox luah si uing yuh nan,
pui koeng teei moe kid nawh goh,
hoik awm pui pa gu khaox gu khaox goh yuh nan,
23 mai hoik yuh nong khaox laux leeing heue.
Pui naing kix kawn lheu khaing sido yuh nan,
mai ang lai uik simin kah.
24 Pui Ai-cix hoek krax kaig krax tua;
pui haktiex plak laong hoik pon kuang ka kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
25 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang ciyang ka siyiex tang kaux Ya-meung ka haktiex Ti-pi-see,
tag sang ciyang nang simiang Ai-cix,
khawm siyiex Ai-cix mai simiang.
Aux tag sang tawx simiang Ai-cix mai can pui pa hoik deuh rhawm tix ka nawh khawm uik
26 ka Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng mai ka pui naing cie nawh khankix.
Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
Ai-cix tag sang awm singaix bawg yam pa hoik luan dix dix,
pui hoik paung ot ka yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Simiang Siyiex tag sang taoh ka pui cie tix
27 cPui cie aux khankix eue,
paw vi lhat;
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
paw vi yaoh yee.
Aux tag sang kaoh khaing dee singai,
khaing meung pa vuk vied vuk viang pex kah,
mai taoh pex lih khaing.
Pex tag caw tix deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix,
mai yaox krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
ang lai koe krax lhat krax graung.
28 Pui cie aux eue,
paw vi lhat,
mawh kheu hoik ot aux tau mai pex heue.
Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka meung haktiex pa hoik ploi aux pex hu ot ka khankix,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang yuh pex laux leeing.
Aux tag sang hawt tix sidiex gau pex awm pa khaw pex tix pon yuh nan,
ang doh yuh pex laux lak pak leeing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
