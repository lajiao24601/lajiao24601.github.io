3
Laux leeing heue,
veng in pa pauh ka pui heue!
Veng in hoik naog ka pa pie lhien mai pa tud pa rhim khawm uik;
tud rhim con cam ang cud ang jai.
2 Lox siviex ah buk bag buk bag,
ling law ah ku kit ku kit!
Brung naing brah to,
ling law naing yaog tix pu!
3 Pui naing brug preeng tix hu,
vaig mug ka pui heue!
To kaux pui yum pum tix awm gawng yuh nan,
si-ang pui yum ang lai uik simin;
pui hoik sideeing tix goh ka to kaux pui yum khawm uik heue.
4 Sibun coh kaux pui Ni-ni-we tag sang hoek krax pa ciyang;
nawh kieh pui glup ka daux krax pa bre nawh tix mhawm.
Krax pa phit vao nawh hoik nyaw glaw ka meung piang haktiex;
krax si meed sat pa yuh nawh hoik pie lhien ka kawn pui ku ceu heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
Ni-ni-we eue,
aux tag sang ciyang ka maix.
Aux tag sang peeik ka kraung maix ghle,
kieh meung ka piang haktiex yaox to kaux dee kaig maix,
kieh kawn pui piang haktiex yaox grawng pa kaig maix
6 Aux tag sang map nai ka maix,
mai tik kraung ceu pa seeing ka piang to kaux maix;
kawn pui tag sang teei tawm ngai pa yaoh yee tix dong ngai tix ka maix.
7 Pui pa yaox ka maix tag sang deeh ka plak khaix tix.
Kix ah nin:
veng Ni-ni-we hoik lih tix riem heue!
Pui mawx sang caox rhawm ka nawh lie?
Pui mawx sang cu tix kieh nawh nyawm rhawm lie?
8 Ni-ni-we eue,
maix kawn lheu ting riang khaing veng ting Ti-pi-see lie?
Nawh kawx koe rawm klawng tix klawng pa sivie ka nawh,
awm guh ding pa kia ngien ka nawh yuh nan,
pa mawh rawm klawng Ni-law pa kieh nawh kaux cong.
9 Ku-seu mai Ai-cix mawh riang rao ka nawh;
siawp sibrawm nawh ang chix uik chix leeing.
Pui Fu mai pui Li-pi-yax mawh pa yuh paox grawm tau mai nawh.
10 Viang mawh nan,
pui hoik giex pui Ti-pi-see hu meung tang meung khankix.
Kawn ngie kix ot ka toh lah,
mai pui hoik si blat kix yum;
pui pa koe min ka daux kix,
pui hoik phrawk kix ka ceng rhiem,
mai viex ki hu awm guah pui tix viex kraung khrai yuh nan heue.
11 Ni-ni-we eue,
maix kawx tag sang yum pi mai yoek tix;
maix kawx hoik keud tix sang pheeik luan khaing pui koeng.
12 Tab cie maix tag sang awm khaox plix ruh yuh nan,
yam hoik teem plix,
yung yang ka khaox,
plix khaox an tag sang cawd laig daux jheeh pui.
13 Pui naing maix hoik awm bun pa iak riang yuh nan khawm uik.
Maix ang pon pau siviex veng tix ka plak sivoe ngai pui koeng;
ngu tag sang gleeing ka jhong siviex maix laux leeing.
14 Maix tag caw tix kin rawm,
son sang rien khre pa lai lawm pui koeng!
Maix tag sau riang rien khre tix mhawm mhawm!
Maix tag caw tix dig glaox yuh mawh lon tiex gleeing,
rien khre tix yuh dang lon tiex gleeing heue!
15 Viang maix yuh lih yuh ka mawx ang ah,
mai kawn ca sang gleeing yum,
phan ang mawh nan pauh yum.
Maix tag sang awm to ma pa uik ih sido yuh nan heue.
Singaix pa hoik luan,
maix hoik pre haoh hun awm sido yuh nan;
16 kraung khrai pa yuh maix lih kawn lheu nie khaing simuing daux rao ma khankix.
Viang mawh nan,
yam in ang lai yaox khawm uik,
mai hoik awm sido pa yah preeik tix pu yuh nan.
17 Siliex pa ting maix awm phung sido yuh nan,
yam kuat praix kaog ot ka ding,
yam lih singaix taug pu hu khawm uik;
ang koe pui pa tawng dee ot kix heue.
 
18 Vawng Ya-sux eue,
siliex pa ting maix hoik yum khawm uik;
pa koe min maix hoik laig it.
Pui cie maix hoik prung prang tix ot ka si nax gawng lua,
mai ang koe pui pa viex kix deeh hoek.
19 Rhoi khaix saux maix ang lai chix caing mhawm;
rhoi khaix mat maix ang lai chix pai moi.
Can pui pa mhong gum lox pa laux leeing maix hoik dieh taix tix khawm uik.
Koe pui mawx pa pon luan khaing krax tud krax rhim maix pa ang chix naox tix lie?
