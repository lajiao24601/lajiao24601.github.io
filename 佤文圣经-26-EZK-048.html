Ceu pui ku ceu guah ka haktiex
48
1-7 Mee haktiex ka plak laong jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong hu ka plak lih singaix hoek ka veng Si-theu-loeng,
diang moing Ha-ma,
veng Yi-nang,
mai nih tix hoek ka dee kleuk haktiex simiang meung Ta-ma-see-keux mai simiang meung Ha-ma paox tix.
Ceu pui ku ceu tag caw tix jauh ka haktiex in,
pa mawh mee haktiex plak lih singaix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong plak klip singaix,
jauh ka Tang,
Ya-seux,
Na-fu-tha-lix,
Ma-na-si,
Yi-fa-liien,
Lui-piien,
Yo-tax tia tix hawt khaix paox,
mai guah kix pon haktiex ku kaux ku nyum,
jauh ka plak laong hoek ka plak seh heue.
Haktiex pa jawg sigreung
8 Tag caw tix pat haktiex lih tix baux kieh mawh pa blawk tix siao.
Haktiex baux in jauh ka plak laong hoek ka plak seh,
krax vieh koe kao ra kongli plak,
jauh ka plak lih singaix hoek ka plak klip singaix hoik sidu lang mai haktiex pa guah ceu pui ku ceu pon.
Nyiex buan tag caw tix sum ot baoh haktiex in heue.
9 Sigreung baux haktiex in tag caw tix kieh plak lang koe kao ra kongli plak,
krax vieh koe kao konglia tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
10 Tag caw tix guah cao giex kaing li pon ka haktiex pa singax in tix nyum.
Haktiex pa guah kix pon,
jauh ka plak lih singaix hoek ka plak klip singaix koe kao ra kongli plak,
jauh ka plak laong hoek ka plak seh koe phuan kongli.
Nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex ot sigreung haktiex in.
11 Haktiex pa singax in mawh pa tawx ka cao giex kaing li doeh ceu cad Sa-tu.
Kix yum rhawm tix yuh kaing jhaw aux,
ang awm ceu pui Li-we hawt khaix pui Yi-seu-liex tieh vheen ka aux.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tag sang pon haktiex tix nyum ka baoh haktiex pa dex ka ceu pui Li-we;
haktiex baoh in mawh pa kied singax.
13 Ceu pui Li-we tag sang pon nyum haktiex pa koe ka plak seh cie cao giex kaing li,
jauh ka plak lih singaix hoek ka plak klip singaix koe kao ra kongli plak,
jauh ka plak laong hoek ka plak seh koe phuan kongli.
14 Haktiex in pa tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex mawh pa kied blawn mhawm khaing paox tix ka daux meung,
ang pon tix coh cai lai gee,
lai loh,
phan ang mawh nan tawx pui yuh.
Haktiex in mawh pa singax,
mawh cie Simiang Siyiex heue.
15 Haktiex in kawn deuk tix dauh,
krax lang koe kao ra kongli plak,
krax vieh koe ra kongli plak;
haktiex in ang mawh haktiex pa singax,
pui chix tix paung ot kah,
mai chix tix siao.
Tag caw tix sum veng ot ka sigreung haktiex in,
16 haktiex in mawh pon pak,
ku daog krax lang koe ra rheng ra yieh tix phuan kongjhee.
17 Vung viang veng khawm pon daog tag caw tix kieh yi,
krax vieh koe tix yieh tix nga phuan kongjhee.
18 Khaix hoik sum veng,
haktiex pa singax plak seh tix dauh pa deuk krax lang plak lih singaix koe phuan kongli,
krax vieh koe ra kongli plak,
krax lang plak klip singaix koe phuan kongli,
krax vieh koe ra kongli plak;
haktiex in mawh pa tawx ka pui pa paung ot daux veng sum.
19 Can pui pa paung ot daux veng,
mawh ceu pui tix ang ah,
tag chix tix sum khawm uik heue.
20 Sigreung haktiex in mawh haktiex dee blawn mhawm,
haktiex in ku daog krax vieh koe kao ra kongli plak awm paox tix,
ka daux koe haktiex pa siao son veng.
21-22 Dee ot nyiex buan ka haktiex in plak lih singaix mai ka plak klip singaix mawh haktiex cie cao giex kaing li,
mawh haktiex cie ceu pui Li-we,
mawh haktiex pa siao son veng.
Haktiex pa deuk mawh cie simiang khawm uik,
plak lih singaix hoek ka mee daog plak lih singaix,
plak klip singaix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong,
plak laong kleuk ka mee haktiex Yo-tax,
plak seh kleuk ka mee haktiex Piien-ya-ming heue.
Haktiex ceu pui tang ceu khankix
23-27 Tag caw tix guah ceu pui tang ku ceu pon ka haktiex plak seh;
jauh ka mee haktiex plak lih singaix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong plak klip singaix,
jauh ka Piien-ya-ming,
Si-miien,
Yi-sa-cia,
Si-pu-loeng,
Cia-teux hawt khaix paox tix,
jauh ka plak laong hoek ka plak seh kix hoik pon tix cub tix nyum heue.
28 Mee haktiex ceu pui Cia-teux plak seh jauh ka Tha-ma hu plak klip singaix dex plak seh hoek dee mhawm klai rib Cia-ti-see,
mai baox hu plak klip singaix dex plak laong hawt mee haktiex Ai-cix,
mai nih tix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong heue.
29 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Tag caw tix yuh krax pa cawk lai,
guah pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pon ka haktiex in,
mai kieh mawh sin ngao kix heue.”
Siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung
30-34 bVeng Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik koe kao ra siviex veng.
Guh veng khawm pon daog koe ra rheng ra yieh tix phuan kongjhee,
mai tang koe siviex veng loe mu.
Tix mu siviex hoik hawt tix tiiem ceu kaux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex sum.
Guh veng plak laong loe mu siviex mawh ceu kaux Lui-piien,
Yo-tax,
Li-we;
guh veng plak lih singaix loe siviex mawh ceu kaux Yaw-seux,
Piien-ya-ming,
Tang;
guh veng plak seh loe mu mawh ceu kaux Si-miien,
Yi-sa-cia,
Si-pu-loeng;
guh veng plak klip singaix loe mu mawh ceu kaux Cia-teux,
Ya-seux,
Na-fu-tha-lix.
35 Guh veng khawm pon daog krax lang khawm uik koe dim rheng kongjhee.
Jauh yam in,
veng in tag sang kok mawh “Simiang Siyiex ot dee in” heue.
