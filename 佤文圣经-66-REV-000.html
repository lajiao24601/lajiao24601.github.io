Lai Plak Jhi
Krai dawt
Yam tiiem Yaw-hang Lai Plak Jhi, jiet mawh bawg yam pa si pup kawn Khrit krax pa tuk tiak tuk te kheu yum rhawm ka Ye-sux Khrit, krai mawh Ye-sux Simiang. Cao tiiem tiiem lai in kaing ting mawh ka daux pa tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm mai pa joh jhan ka pui cao phat, tawx lox nhie kix ca nied tix giex kaux cong ka krax yum rhawm yam si pup ka krax kha rhawm, mai krax tuk tiak tuk te heue.
Lai in plak nie mawh khawm gum lox ka daux pa phlu lih mai riang su sai, teei lox pa tiag krai lih ka gum lox pa krai cao tiiem. Pa koe daux lai in ang mawh pa nu tix tawng ka kawn Khrit ka daux singaix yam an, viang mawh nan ka son pui pa ang bax yum rhawm, mai ka pui cao phat ka daux singaix khaix, kied mawh pa nu tix tawng sidawng. Awm pa luah lox ting lox siao yuh nan, lai in kaing ting teei riang su sai ku ceu, daux krax pa ang awm paox tix jiiem tix lih. Viang pa kah ka dee bleeh riang su sai in kix ang awm paox tix, dee bleeh kaing ting hoik kied sidawng yuh nin: dee si dauh Siyiex taug sang puing pex ka ceu pa koeng khawm Sa tan ka daux Simiang Ye-sux Khrit, khaix an tag sang tawx khawng phak buan son ka pui cie tix pa kaux sijax, tawx rao ma pa khraox mai haktiex pa khraox ka kix heue.
Lai in kieh mawh phuk lai sigang khri pa nghoik khaix paox tix tix phuk kied mawh pa khaw tete. Mawh kheu dee nghoik simin ting ra loe mu tiiem ka singaix bawg yawm si dauh haktiex, mai pa lih mawh grawh grawng kaing ceu pa ting krax taoh cie Siyiex heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–8)
2. Riang su sai pa jauh mai lox lai pa tawx ka ceu cao aliah mu (1.9–3.22)
3. Phuk lai pa koe cum aliah mu (4.1–8.1)
4. Teet tawng aliah mu (8.2–11.19)
5. Si yawng mai to sat ra mu (12.1–13.18)
6. Riang su sai pa koe ku ceu (14.1–15.8)
7. Klong pa song rhawm song rhi Siyiex aliah mu (16.1–21)
8. Pa laux leeing Pa-pi-loeng mai pa laux leeing to sat, cao jhai pa pie lhien, mai phi caox (17.1–20.10)
9. Krax prawng ca pa nghoik tix koe (20.11–15)
10. Rao ma haktiex pa khraox, mai Yie-lu-sa-leung pa khraox (21.1–22.5)
11. Lox pa nghoik tix krai (22.6–21)
