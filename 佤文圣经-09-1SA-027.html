Ta-wue ot daux pui Fe-li-see
27
Ka daux rhawm Ta-wue keud ah nin:
“Tix singaix sax,
aux rien tix cawd ka daux tawm taix Sao-law;
lheu khaing to aux tix hu ot daux pui Fe-li-see.
Yuh nin,
Sao-law tee rien tix ang lai sawg aux ka haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux tom yaox tix luat luan khaing tawm taix nawh.”
2 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue viex ka pui cie tix liah Siyiex kaux,
hoek tix peung rhawm simiang Cia-theux pa mawh kawn simie Ma-eux pa kaux Ya-cie.
3 Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh viex khawm pui nyiex pui ma tix ot tau mai Ya-cie ka Cia-theux.
Moeng Ta-wue khawm ra kaux pa mawh Ya-si-nuang mai kawn praix Na-pax ceu pui Yie-see-liex;
Ai-pi-kai ceu pui Cia-mix ot tau mai nawh.
4 Sao-law hoik mhong to Ta-wue mox tix ka Cia-theux,
tom ang lai sawg ka nawh heue.
5 Ta-wue ah nin ka simiang Ya-cie:
“Phan simiang sang kieh aux mawh paox grawm tix,
tawx aux cie kawn veng iak tix mu son sang ot aux kah.
Yuh ka mawx lai kawn kieh aux ot tau mai simiang ka daux veng ting lie?”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-cie tawx veng Si-keu-la ka Ta-wue.
Jauh yam in,
veng Si-keu-la tom yaox tix mawh veng cie simiang pui Yo-tax.
7 Ta-wue hoik paung ot ka haktiex cie pui Fe-li-see,
hoik koe been kao liah khix heue.
8 Ka daux baux bawg yam an,
Ta-wue thung pui pa ot greem tix hu puing ka pui Ci-sux pui Ci-seux mai pui Ya-ma-lix.
Pui in kix hoik kied rheeng paung ot haktiex an.
Ta-wue tud rhim ka haktiex kix,
singai hoek tom Su-eux,
tom hoek ka Ai-cix.
9 Nawh pauh ka can simex,
bun,
mai baox tud rhim ka phung yung kix,
phung moi kix,
la kix,
ka-la-uk kix,
khawm kraung ceep kix hu.
Ta-wue hoik puing ka naing,
nawh deeh ing ka simiang Ya-cie dee an,
10 simiang Ya-cie chawk nawh ah nin:
“Bawg in maix hoik puing ka dee mawx?”
Ta-wue krai mawh ka haktiex plak seh Yo-tax,
phan ang mawh nan krai hu tix ka daux haktiex Yie-la-miiex,
phan ang mawh nan ka dee ot pui Ci-ni.
11 Ta-wue hoik pauh can simex bun yum khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh klawd pie tix kaux kawx son sang hu ka Cia-theux krai ka kaing pa yuh nawh mai pui cie nawh.
In mawh kaing pa hoik yuh Ta-wue yam ot tau mai pui Fe-li-see.
12 Simiang Ya-cie yum rhawm ka Ta-wue;
nawh keud ah nin:
“Paox ceu cad Ta-wue pui Yi-seu-liex hoik koeng ka nawh,
nawh tag sang mawh pa yuh kaing jhaw aux leeing ju tix heue.”
