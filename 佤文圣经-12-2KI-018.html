Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Si-si-cia
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 29.1–2; 30.1)
18
Yam giex simiang pui Yi-seu-liex kawn simex Yi-la pa kaux Heu-si-yax kaing daux loe num,
kawn simex Ya-ha-see pa kaux Si-si-cia mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
2 Yam an ju nawh koe tix nga phuan num;
nawh giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix nga dim num.
Miex nawh Ya-pi-yax mawh kawn bun Sa-cia-li-ya.
3 Si-si-cia hawt ka kaing mhawm pa yuh tax du tix simiang Ta-wue,
mai yuh kaing pa mhawm plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
4 bNawh yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak,
yieh ka rong simaox,
kid goh ka rong riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la,
mai baox pauh max ka riang si uing pa yuh Maw-si,
pa mawh Ni-fu-see-tanga,
mawh kheu pui Yi-seu-liex hoek yam an ca kawn gleeing nyawng bhawng ka riang si uing an.
5 Si-si-cia yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex;
meung pui Yo-tax pa koe sivoe nawh mai pa koe khaix nawh,
mai ang koe simiang tix kaux kawx pa awm nawh yuh nan.
6 Nawh yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang sax lieh lheu ka Simiang Siyiex tix bawg kawx;
nawh hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si khawm uik.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai nawh;
mai can kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik hoik pon yaox tix mhawm.
Nawh tieh vheen ka Vawng Ya-sux,
ang cu tix nghiet ka lox nawh.
8 Nawh pon pex ka pui Fe-li-see,
mai hoik pon ka haktiex kix,
khawm Cia-sa mai haktiex sivie siveng kix,
jauh ka glawk dee lhaong kok jhak pui tom hoek ka veng pa kieh kaux cong heue.
9 Yam giex Si-si-cia kaing daux pon num,
pa mawh nang yam giex simiang Heu-si-yax kaing pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux aliah num,
Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Sa-mang-yi-seux puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai lai lawm ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
10 Khaix hoik lai lawm pui loe num,
Sa-ma-li-yax laux leeing;
in mawh yam giex Si-si-cia ka kaing daux liah num,
kaing in hoik lih tix mawh yam giex Heu-si-yax ka kaing daux dim num.
11 Vawng Ya-sux giex viex pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka Ya-sux,
mai kieh kix prung prang tix ot ka veng Ha-lax,
dex ka rawm klawng Ha-paw baoh haktiex Keu-sang,
mai veng yaong ot pui Mi-ti-yax heue.
12 Sa-ma-li-yax laux leeing hoik mawh kheu ang pui Yi-seu-liex nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix,
mai lieh lheu ka sijax mukheu pa yuh Simiang Siyiex tau mai kix,
ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik krai kawn cau Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Maw-si ka kix khawm uik.
Kix ang nghiet,
mai ang priex tix hawt heue.
Pui Ya-sux ghlat ka pui veng Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 32.1–9; Lai Yi-sai-yax 36.1–22)
13 Yam kawn giex Si-si-cia simiang ka daux kao pon num,
Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Si-na-ci-lix puing ka veng pa kaux cong Yo-tax,
mai baox teei tix cie.
14 Si-si-cia ploi pui hu ka simiang Si-na-ci-lix haktiex La-ciex,
mai ah nin:
“Aux hoik lud loe,
khiox khaw maix naox puing ka yix heue!
Aux cu tix tawx pa sang simie maix tix lo khawm uik heue.”
Vawng pawk lox Si-si-cia,
nhie nawh tawx mau luah tix mheen kongcing,
mai khri tix rheng kongcing.
15 Si-si-cia tom teei tix tawx mau pa deuh ot daux nyiex buan mai daux nyiex haw ka nawh khawm uik,
16 mai baox kheet tix teei khri pa ot ka diang siviex nyiex buan mai pa hoik om ot ka rong nyiex buan lih,
tawx tau ka Si-na-ci-lix.
17 Vawng Ya-sux ploi pui naing lih khaing La-ciex,
mai hu puing Si-si-cia ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
lien mawh cao bung naing pa ot greem nawh,
cao giex kaing lien,
mai cao bung pui naing pa ting pa thung.
Yam hoek kix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kix tom dia tix ot ka praok rawng piang dung,
ka krax plak hu pui ka dee si daux man khankix.
18 Khaix an,
kix ploi pui hu kok ka simiang Si-si-cia.
Kawn cau pa ting Si-si-cia loe kaux hu ka kix;
kawn cau in kix loe kaux mawh:
kawn simex Si-leu-cia cao pa kuang ka nyiex haw simiang,
pa kaux Yi-li-ya-cing,
cao tiiem lai ka daux nyiex simiang pa kaux Seu-paw-nax,
cao pa si kaw ka been pa mawh kawn simex Ya-sa pa kaux Yaw-yax.
19 Simiang naing Ya-sux chawk ah nin:
“Vawng Ya-sux son sang pon tix tawng ka Si-si-cia,
nawh hoik koe siawp sibrawm ceu pa tix yuh nin lie.
20 Nawh ce lox pa krai tix giex going sang pon mawh tang kru naing mai riang rao tix lie?
Nawh een rhawm tix ka pui mawx son sang keud tix tieh vheen ka aux lie?
21 Nawh ce pui Ai-cix sang hoek teem tix,
mai an hoik mawh pa teei ka gawng priem pa pot,
kieh mawh khaox gix pa gix hu krax;
khaox gix an tag sang klauh ka tawm taix maix.
Simiang Ai-cix tag sang yuh nan ka pui pa hoik deuh rhawm tix ka nawh khankix heue.”
22 Simiang naing Ya-sux ca nied tix ah nin:
“Pex tee rien tix ah nin,
pa een pex rhawm tix ka mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Viang mawh nan,
ang Si-si-cia hoik yieh ka nyiex vad pa ot dee lung ka piang gawng mai khu dee tan nawh lie?
Ang nawh hoik tawx lox ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
sang kieh ngie kix krub Simiang Siyiex an ka khu dee tan pa koe ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung khaing lie?
23 Yam in aux sang tiex tu mai pex ka daux ceu kaux Vawng:
Phan Si-si-cia pon yaox ka pui naing brug brung ra rheng kaux,
aux tag sang tawx brung naing ka nawh ra rheng mu!
24 Tete te lai,
pie simiang naing Ya-sux pa blawn iak khaing paox tix,
nawh ang pon pex,
nawh ce pui Ai-cix sang tawx ling law naing,
mai pui naing brug brung sang hoek teem coi ka nawh!
25 Nawh ce aux ang koe krax teem Simiang Siyiex tom sang hoek puing ka nawh,
sang yuh laux leeing ka meung cie nawh lie?
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux hoek puing ka nawh,
mai yuh laux leeing ka haktiex cie nawh in heue.”
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-ya-cing,
Seu-paw-nax,
mai Yaw-yax ah nin ka simiang naing pa ting “Sara,
maix si kah ka lox Ya-lang tau mai yix,
yix hoik chix nghiet kah.
Paw si kah ka lox Si-paw-lai,
mawh kheu hoik mhong kawn pui,
pa ot dee lhaong daux veng ka heue.”
27 Simiang naing ah nin:
“Pex ce Vawng nhie aux krai lox ka pex mai ka simiang meung haktiex khaing lie?
Ang doh mawh nan,
aux kawx hoik krai ka pui pa ngawm ka piang nyiex lhaong veng khankix;
kix hoik awm pex yuh nan,
tag sang ih ka aing to kaux tix,
nyaux ka rawm neem to kaux tix heue.”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh yaog tix kaoh cung,
mai kok yo ka lox Si-paw-lai tom ting ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox lai tawx Vawng Ya-sux.
29 Nawh ne raw ka kix ah nin:
paw vi glup ka lox pa pie lhien Si-si-cia;
Si-si-cia ang pon taoh ka pex.
30 Paw nghiet ka lox krai nawh,
son sang kieh pex hu een rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex.
Paw ce Simiang Siyiex sang chix taoh ka pex;
paw ce pui naing Ya-sux ang pon yuh laux ka veng pex.
31 Paw nghiet ka lox pa krai Si-si-cia!
Vawng Ya-sux tawx lox nhie pex lih krai lox mhawm.
Yuh nin,
pex chix tix kawn tang yaox tix ih ka plix siiet mai plix ruh cie tix,
tang nyaux ka rawm dung cie tix.
32 Krox jun tom hoik gah Vawng pex hu ka haktiex tang dauh,
awm haktiex pex yuh nan,
pa hoik koe nghox koi,
plix siiet,
khao mun mai plai.
Haktiex an hoik mawh dee kuah khaox sime,
dee lih bee sime,
mai dee koe rawm hia khankix.
Phan pex priex tix hawt ka lox tawm jhawm krai nawh,
pex tag yaox tix im ju,
mai ang yaox tix yum.
Paw vi kieh Si-si-cia pie lhien ka pex,
mai ce Simiang Siyiex sang chix hoek taoh pex.
33 “Koe Siyiex meung haktiex mawx pa hoik yaox pex pon taoh ka meung haktiex tix luat luan khaing tawm taix Vawng Ya-sux lie?
34 Siyiex Ha-ma,
siyiex Ya-eu-pa ot dee mawx yam in lie?
Siyiex Si-fa-wa-ying,
siyiex Si-nax,
siyiex Yi-wa hoik ot dee mawx lie?
Koe pui mawx pa pon taoh pui Sa-ma-li-yax luat luan khaing daux tawm taix aux lie?
35 Koe siyiex mawx pa hoik sax taoh ka meung tix khaing daux tawm taix simiang yix lie?
Pex hoik yum rhawm ka sang chix taoh Simiang Siyiex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung grawng ceu pa tix lie?”
36 Kawn pui ang nye ang nyux;
kix priex tix hawt ka lox tawm simiang Si-si-cia,
ang nye pie tix gum,
mai ang pawk lox pa krai nawh.
37 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing piang daux nyiex haw Yi-li-ya-cing,
cao tiiem lai ka daux nyiex simiang pa kaux Seu-paw-nax,
mai simiang cao pa si kaw been meung pa mawh Yaw-yax,
laux rhawm riah kraung ceep tix griah,
mai deeh ing ka veng krai lox simiang naing Ya-sux ka simiang Si-si-cia heue.
