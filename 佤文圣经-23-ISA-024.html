Simiang Siyiex sang ciyang ka haktiex
24
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka haktiex,
mai kieh haktiex riem.
Nawh tag sang pu pliak tix klup ka haktiex,
mai kieh kawn pui prung prang tix tang hu.
2 Mawh cao giex kaing li ang ah,
pui hun ang ah,
pui yuh siliex cawng ang ah,
simiang ang ah,
pa liag ang ah,
pa coh ang ah,
pui pa tawx pui ceuk ka mau ang ah,
pui pa yaog tix ceuk ka mau ang ah,
pui hot sibhawm ang ah,
pui pa mi pa koe ang ah,
mai kix ku kaux tag sang si pup ka krax pa awm paox yuh nan heue.
3 Haktiex hoik riem,
mai ang lai tawx to ma to koi pie ceeix kawx.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
4 Haktiex hoik kroh hoik riem;
piang haktiex khawm uik hoik laux leeing;
rao ma haktiex hoik mawh pa giex pa going heue.
5 Pui cie Siyiex ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai hoik tieh vheen ka sijax mukheu pa yuh nawh leeing ju,
hoik kieh haktiex dee ot kix seeing brai.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex tom jhax lawg ka haktiex in,
kieh haktiex in laux leeing.
Pui haoh pui hun tag sang klawm ka kaing pa hoik yuh tix.
Pui pa kawn im ju nied ot nied tux heue.
7 Nhu plix siiet hoik kroh,
plai kawx hoik yawm.
Krax gaux rhawm hoik tieh tix lih mawh krax laux rhawm;
8 Ting kraung kawx hoik naox tix;
lox gaux rhawm kawx ang lai koe.
9 Ang lai koe pui pa nyaux plai yuh krax gaux rhawm,
ang lai koe pui pa cim ka jhawm pa nyawm tie plai.
10 Ka daux veng hoik suk sag khawm uik,
pui hoik nyeep siviex ku nyiex ku ma,
mai lhat pui laig daux diang siviex ting.
11 Koe pui riag yo ka daux laih mai mhaing tix nyaux plai;
krax gaux rhawm hoik grai,
ka piang haktiex ang lai koe tid tix.
12 Guh veng hoik ghrup,
siviex veng hoik laux.
13 Meung pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik tag sang si pup krax pa awm paox,
hoik awm singaix bawg yam nghoik tix khrawm ka to ma,
ka piang khaox sime ang lai koe plix,
ka piang nhu plix siiet ang lai koe ka plix siiet heue.
14 Pui pa deuk khaix hoik tud pui tag sang gaux rhawm mai rhax lai.
Pui ka plak klip singaix tag sang krai grawng pa ting pa lhaong Simiang Siyiex;
15 pui ka plak lih singaix khankix tag sang rhax lai yaog yawg ka nawh;
pui pa paung ot ka hawt rawm sigrit sigra tag sang yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
16 Jauh tom dee singai haktiex ex hoik mhong ka lox rhax pa yaog yaw meung pui Yi-seu-liex ka pa rauh see heue.
Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik yaox krax laux leeing,
aux hoik yum heue!
Aux hoik yum heue!
Pui pa lieh lox nied tix lieh lox;
kaing pa yuh kix hoik mawh pa kied ting lhat graung pui kah.
17 Pui pa paung ot ka piang haktiex eue,
koe krax pa lhat graung,
daux o,
daux rub hoik krox jun ka pex.
18 Pui pa bheeik luan khaing krax pa lhat pa graung,
tag sang cawd ka daux o;
pui pa pon lih khaing ka daux o tag sang krix ka rub heue.
Lhiex ting lih khaing rao ma,
riah haktiex yung yang.
19 Haktiex tag sang bih,
sang laux leeing,
sang bih.
20 Haktiex hoik yung yang,
vawk jee vawk blaong awm pui pa yoek ka plai yuh nan,
hoik yung yang awm nyiex nyhee pa phlu bhaung yuh nan.
Haktiex hoik laux leeing,
hoik max,
hoik phawng kheu phi map,
mai ang lai pon yaog tix kaoh cung leeing ju heue.
21 Bawg yam pa ciyang Simiang Siyiex hoik hoek.
Nawh tag sang ciyang ka cao pa kuang ka siawp sibrawm mee rao ma,
mai ka simiang haktiex.
22 Nawh tag sang khrawm tix giex laung ka simiang awm pui lud yuh nan,
mai grheu kix laig daux tiex.
Nawh tag sang laung kix ot dee an,
mai krox jum tom hoik lih hoek bawg yam bawg yam ciyang.
23 Khix tag sang tieh tix lih faik,
pa riang singaix ang lai koe.
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
tag sang giex kaing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ka gawng Shi-ang;
simiang kawn pui ka daux haktiex tag sang yaox ka min plak ngai nawh khawm uik heue.
