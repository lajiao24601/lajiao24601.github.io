Hit krax kheem rawm
8
Siyiex ang sax piawm ka Naw-yax mai to sat pa ot tau mai nawh daux reu.
Nawh kieh bhaung phlu ka haktiex,
rawm tom jauh tix dieh.
2 Dee keet rawm khaing dee raux mai siviex rao ma hoik nyeep tix,
lhiex ting kawx ang lai lih.
3 Daux tix yieh tix phuan ngaix,
rawm hoik dieh si nyek si nyek.
4 Ka daux khix aliah kao aliah singaix,
reu an kaog ka piang choek gawng Ya-la-lax.
5 Rawm dieh hu si nyek si nyek,
tom hoek khix kao tix ngaix,
choek gawng bhlien khaing daux rawm.
6 Khaix hoik naog tipon singaix,
Naw-yax paoh ka daux doh reu,
7 ploi lak tix mu lih ka plak praix.
Lak in pu hu pu hoek,
tom si dauh hoek bawg yam dieh rawm,
ang lai deeh hoek.
8 Naw-yax baox ploi si kaux tix mu,
son sang tawng ka grawng hit rawm ang hit.
9 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu kawn kheem rawm ka piang haktiex,
si kaux ang nyang yaox dee sang tom caong tix,
deeh pu hoek daux reu,
Naw-yax nyeu taix tix deeh teei si kaux laig daux reu.
10 Baox deeh pau aliah singaix,
nawh baox ploi si kaux lih hu khaing daux reu;
11 plak bo praix,
si kaux an teei tix viex lhax khaox sime pa khraox ka gaoh tix deeh pu hoek ka nawh,
yam an Naw-yax hoik tawng hit rawm ka piang haktiex.
12 Baox pau aliah singaix,
nawh deeh baox ploi si kaux lih;
si kaux in ang lai deeh hoek tix bawg kawx.
13 Yam hoek koe Naw-yax liah yieh mai tix num num an,
daux khix tix,
rawm hoik hit khawm uik,
Naw-yax plak ka braux reu,
jhak ka plak praix,
mai yaox hoik kroh haktiex.
14 Hoek daux khix ra tix nga aliah ngaix,
piang haktiex hoik kroh khawm uik heue.
15 Siyiex ah nin ka Naw-yax:
16 “Maix khawm moeng maix,
kawn simex maix,
mai mun maix ku kaux chix tix lih khaing daux reu.
17 Viex to sat pa mawh sim,
khawng brie mai to sat,
viag vua pa ot tau mai maix lih khaing daux reu,
mai kieh kix pre ka piang haktiex,
naog ka piang haktiex.”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Naw-yax mai moeng nawh,
kawn simex nawh,
mai mun nawh lih khaing daux reu.
19 Can to sat,
viag vua,
mai sim,
ceu mawx ceu an,
lih khaing daux reu heue.
Naw-yax yuh krax tan
20 Naw-yax yuh khu dee tan son Simiang Siyiex tix mu;
nawh laug to sat mai sim pa singax singeh tix ceu tix brawm hoek,
mai tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu dee an.
21 Simiang Siyiex khaw rhawm mai rieb ka pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
keud daux rhawm tix:
“Aux tag ang lai baox jhax lawg ka haktiex kheu jao kaing pa yuh kawn pui tix bawg kawx.
Aux hoik tawng,
kawn pui jauh yam iak hoik koe krax pa caox daux rhawm.
Aux tag ang lai baox yuh can pa im ju khankix laux leeing awm pa hoik yuh aux bawg in.
22 Ca mawh yam kawn koe haktiex in,
ka piang haktiex tag koe krax pa rhuat simie,
koe yam vawg nghox,
yam kuat praix,
yam si aoh praix,
yam raux,
yam rung,
yam pon singaix mai pon sawm.”
