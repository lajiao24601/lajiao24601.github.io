Deeh tawx ka teug sijax mukheu
6
Khaix hoik ot teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex ka haktiex Fe-li-see aliah khix,
2 pui Fe-li-see kok cao giex kaing li mai pui pa chix si pawk khankix hoek,
mai chawk kix ah nin:
“Ex sang yuh ka mawx ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex lie?
Phan ex deeh ing tawx ka dee ot sivoe an,
kawn sang caw ex tix viex ceu pa tix lie?”
3 Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan pex sang deeh tawx ka teug sijax mukheu Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
tag caw tix tawx khawng phak krai grawng lud loe ka nawh,
ang pon tix tawx giex ka teug sijax mukheu.
Yuh nin,
jhawm saux pex tag sang pon tix moi,
khaix an pex tag tawng yuh ka mawx Siyiex lai ciyang pex ang jud ang jai heue.”
4 Pui Fe-li-see chawk ah nin:
“Ex sang tawx khawng phak ceu pa tix lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Riang muad pa yuh ka khri phuan mu mai riang kawn kiang pa yuh ka khri phuan mu.
Tix mu riang muad an mai riang kawn kiang an,
hoik mawh pa yuh son simiang pui Fe-li-see tix kaux,
mawh kheu saux muad pa awm paox,
hoik lih tix koe ka pex mai ka to kaux simiang pui Fe-li-see.
5 Pex tag caw tix yuh ka riang muad mai ka riang kawn kiang pa hoik laux yuh ka kaung ma pex,
mai krai doh ka phi map tix ka Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Yuh nin,
nawh khaw tix ang lai ciyang ka pex mai ka siyiex pex,
khawm haktiex pex khankix.
6 Pex lai kawn sang yuh tix kuah awm simiang Ai-cix mai pui Ai-cix yuh nang lie?
Khaix hoik laux kix ka pa yuh Siyiex,
kix kawx hoik cu tix ploi pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Ai-cix lie?
7 “Mawh kheu nan,
pex rien khre ka ling law khraox tix lhang mai ka moi miex pa ang nyang sax thai yuh kaing ra mu,
mai sau moi miex an ka ling law,
grheu kawn moi khankix deeh ing ka kawg.
8 Tag caw tix sau teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex ot ka piang ling law,
mai sau teug tang mu ot ka praok,
ka daux teug an sau riang khri an kix ot kah,
pa mawh khawng phak pa sang krai grawng krax lud loe pex,
khaix an,
kieh moi an reu ka ling law an hu.
9 Pex kuan tix jhak plak hu ling law an,
phan hu plak Paw See-mai,
an hoik ah,
in mawh krax laux leeing pa ploi Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka piang ex;
phan ang hu ka plak krax an,
ex tom tawng Siyiex ang bax ploi krax laux leeing lih,
krax laux leeing in hoik mawh pa tang lih tix mawh heue.”
10 Pui Fe-li-see hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai kix.
Kix teei moi miex ra mu tok ot ka ling law,
mai plaik kawn moi iak khankix ot daux kawg,
11 mai sau teug sijax mukheu ot ka piang ling law,
khawm teug pa sau riang kawn kiang khri mai riang saux muad khri kawx,
hoik sau ot ka piang ling law.
12 Moi miex an hu ka plak krax Paw See-mai,
hu rauh see ang via plak dawm plak goix;
moi miex an ca bawx mai hu tix.
Simiang pui Fe-li-See phuan kaux an hawt hu khaix moi an,
mai hu tom ka mee haktiex Paw See-mai heue.
13 Yam vawg pui Paw See-mai nghox ca ka piang gawng;
kix yaog kaing tix jhak,
mai yaox ka teug sijax mukheu,
kix khawm uik kied gaux rhawm kah.
14 Ling law an hoek dee ot pui Paw See-mai ka daux kaung cie Yaw-su-yax,
mai kaog ot ka praok simaox ting tix lon.
Pui haktiex an lug ka ling law,
mai pauh ka moi an kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
15 Pui Li-we grawng ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
mai ka teug pa saux riang khri khankix ot daux lih,
deuh ot ka piang simaox ting an.
Singaix an,
pui Paw See-mai tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie tang ceu khankix.
16 Simiang pui Fe-li-see phuan kaux an kix jhak grawng pa yuh pui khankix,
ngaix an,
tom deeh ing ka Yi-keu-loeng heue.
17 Pui Fe-li-see tawx riang muad khri phuan mu kieh mawh khawng phak pa krai grawng lud tix,
tix mu mawh son Ya-seu-thu,
tix mu mawh son Cia-sa,
tix mu mawh son Ya-seu-ci-loeng,
tix mu mawh son Cia-theux,
tix mu mawh son Yi-keu-loeng.
18 Kix kawx baox tawx ka riang kawn kiang khri,
tix mu mawh son veng pa kuang simiang pui Fe-li-see tix kaux,
khawm veng pa hoik kuan kix mai nyiex yaong pa ang kix bax lai lawm.
Simaox ting an pa ot daux kaung cie Yaw-su-yax pui Paw See-mai,
pa mawh simaox pa hoik sau kix teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex ot ka piang,
kawn ot tan tom in singaix,
kieh mawh mhai sakse kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh heue.
19 Simiang Siyiex hoik pauh ka pui Paw See-mai atigliah kaux,
mawh kheu jiang kix ka teug sijax mukheu.
Pui haktiex an laux rhawm kheu pauh Simiang Siyiex pui yum tom nie heue.
Teug sijax mukheu ot ka veng Ci-liex Yie-ling
20 Pui Paw See-mai ah nin:
“Ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex pa kied singaix singeh in,
mawh pui mawx pa pon cung kaux cong lie?
Ex sang tawx nawh hu dee mawx,
kieh nawh singai hu khaing ex lie?”
21 Kix ploi kawn cau hu ah nin ka pui veng Ci-liex Yie-ling:
“Pui Fe-li-see hoik tawx ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex;
hu rieb pex teei ka heue!”
