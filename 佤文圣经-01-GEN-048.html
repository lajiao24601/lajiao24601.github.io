Ya-keux nawb buan ka Yi-fa-liien mai Ma-na-si
48
Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
koe pui krai ka Yaw-seux,
krai saux keeing nawh.
Khaix an nawh tom viex kawn simex tix ra kaux Ma-na-si mai Yi-fa-liien hu jhak ka keeing tix.
2 Ya-keux mhong ka hoek kawn simex nawh Yaw-seux hoek tix jhak ka nawh,
nawh tom khin tix kaoh ngawm piang ku it tix.
3 aYa-keux ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm hoik lih bleeh ka aux ka veng Lu-see daux haktiex Cia-nang,
tawx buan ka aux.
4 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
‘Aux tag sang kieh maix pre haoh hun,
mai kieh ceu pui maix lih mawh ceu pui meung tom nie;
aux tag tawx nang haktiex in ka doeh ceu cad maix,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix leeing ju leeing phan.’ ”
5 Ya-keux baox ah nin:
“Yaw-seux eue!
Sivoe ang aux nyang hoek ka Ai-cix,
kawn simex maix ra kaux hoik keh dee in,
kix hoik sawn tix mawh cie aux;
Yi-fa-liien mai Ma-na-si,
mai Lui-piien mai Si-miien kawx mawh cie aux.
6 Kawn praix tang kaux maix ang sawn tix mawh cie aux;
viang mawh nan,
kix tag pon sin ngao ka greem ceu kaux Yi-fa-liien mai Ma-na-si.
7 bAux yuh nin kheu mawh jao son miex maix Le-ciiex.
Yam hoek aux khaing Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax,
miex maix hoik yum hawt krax haktiex meung Cia-nang,
dex hoek ka Yi-fa-tha;
mai aux kied laux rhawm.
Aux si paung kiex dee dex krax hu ka veng Yi-fa-tha.”
(Yi-fa-tha pa mawh Paw-li-haung in singaix heue.)
8 Ya-keux yaox ka kawn simex Yaw-seux khankix,
tom chawk:
“Kawn in kix mawh mawx?”
9 Yaw-seux pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh kawn simex aux,
mawh pa tawx Siyiex ka aux yam hoek aux ka Ai-cix.”
Ya-keux ah nin:
“Viex kix hoek ka aux dee in,
aux sang nawb buan ka kiex.”
10 Ya-keux hoik kuad,
ngai nawh ang lai chix yaox,
Yaw-seux tom viex kawn simex tix hoek sivoe ngai nawh,
nawh tom kawm tix ngheet kiex.
11 Ya-keux ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Aux keud tix ang lai yaox ka pak ngai maix;
yam in,
Siyiex hoik jhi aux yaox khawm kawn praix maix.”
12 Khaix an Yaw-seux teei kiex lih khaing ngawng Ya-keux,
mai to kaux nawh gup pak ngai tix sivoe keeing nawh.
13 Yaw-seux kieh Yi-fa-liien cung plak goix Ya-keux,
Ma-na-si cung plak dawm.
14 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-keux lai laig taix tix,
deuh taix plak dawm ka piang kaing Yi-fa-liien pa mawh pux,
mai deuh taix khawn goix tix piang kaing Ma-na-si pa mawh pux ke.
15 Nawh nawb buan ka Yaw-seuxc ah nin:
Kieh Siyiex pa krub keeing aux Ya-paw-la-hang mai Yi-sha tawx buan ka piang kawn in kiex ra kaux!
Kieh Siyiex pa thung aux tom in singaix tawx buan ka piang kiex!
16 Kieh kawn cau Siyiex cao pa taoh aux khaing pa caox pa caig khawm uik,
nawb buan ka kawn nyawm in kiex!
Son daux ceu kaux aux,
ceu kaux tax du aux Ya-paw-la-hang mai ceu kaux Yi-sha,
kieh si doe tix hu khaing ka daux kiex mai kieh kawn praix kiex ting lhaong,
haok huan tom nie ka piang haktiex in heue!
17 Yam yaox Yaw-seux deuh keeing tix taix khawn dawm nawh ka piang kaing Yi-fa-liien,
daux rhawm nawh ang gaux rhawm kah.
Khaix an nawh sang teei taix keeing tix khaing kaing Yi-fa-liien mai gah tix deuh ka piang kaing Ma-na-si.
18 Nawh ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Keeing,
yuh nin ang khaw,
in mawh kawn ai,
deuh taix dawm maix piang kaing nawh liak.”
19 Keeing nawh thiang ah nin:
“Kawn simex aux eue,
aux tawng,
aux tawng,
doeh ceu cad Ma-na-si tag mawh meung pa ting;
viang mawh nan,
pux nawh tag lheu ting khaing nawh,
doeh ceu cad pux nawh sang mawh meung pa kied ting heue.”
20 dDaux singaix an,
Ya-keux nawb buan ka piang kiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex sang siao ka ceu kaux pex nawb buan piang pui.
Kix sang ah nin:
‘Kieh Siyiex kieh pex awm Yi-fa-liien mai Ma-na-si yuh nan.’ ”
Yuh nin,
Ya-keux kieh Yi-fa-liien ting sivoe Ma-na-si heue.
21 Khaix an,
Ya-keux ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Maix tawng aux dex sang yum!
Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex tag ot tau mai pex,
viex pex deeh ing ka haktiex tax du dee an.
22 Aux tawx haktiex See-ciien pa kied mhawm an ka maix,
ang tawx ka pux aik maix tang kaux;
an mawh pa pon teei ka lawm vaig mai ak,
khaing daux tawm taix Ya-maw-lix heue.”
