Lox rawg buan krax teem krax coi
12
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix teem ka yix,
pui pa koe krax nab yam yung yi ang lai koe heue;
pui pa kaux sijax kawx mai ang lai baox yaox ka heue.
2 Pui hun ra lai pie lhien ka paox tix,
mai teei lox pa nyaw glaw tix ra lai pie lhien ka paox tix heue.
 
3 Khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex tat dut ka dak pa nyaw glaw,
mai thox si nyeep jheeh pui pa cau nyhau!
4 Kix ah nin:
ex sang krai lox awm pa simie ex tix krai;
pui mawx pon kuang ka lox krai ex lie?
 
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
aux tag sang hoek,
mawh kheu pui hot pui sibhawm kham krax pa gut pa kheem,
pui pa kham krax pa tuk tiak tuk te kha rhawm tix mai yiiem ngia;
aux tag sang tawx krax pa simie kix tix cie ka kix heue.
 
6 Gum lox pa hoik ap Simiang Siyiex tix deuh,
hoik mawh pa kaux sijax mai pa singax tete,
awm mau luah pa hoik raug tix ka daux glah ngu aliah bawg.
7-8 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig hoik nieb pon yuh ku dauh ku dee;
pui ku kaux rhax lai yaog yaw pa caox pa caig.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka yix,
kieh yix bheeik luan khaing pui baux ju in heue.
