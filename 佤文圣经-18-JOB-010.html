10
Aux song rhawm ka ju tix.
Aux tag sang ploi ka lox pa yiiem tix;
aux tag sang krai lao ka daux pa suk sag rhawm tix.
2 Siyiex eue,
paw vi prawng ca aux koe phi map;
viex maix sidiex gau ka aux,
aux lud loe kheu ceu pa tix?
3 Maix tiiem jhak ka pui pa hoik phan hoik yuh ka taix tix,
mai veeh ngai tix nyiah ka krax keud krax sawn pui pa caox pa caig.
Krax pa kha maix in,
mawh khaw lie?
4 Krax pa lam lia kia ngien maix,
khex hoik khaw mai krax keud krax sawn kawn pui lie?
5 Ju maix khex hoik dawt awm ju kawn pui yuh nin lie?
6 Phan ang mawh nan,
maix lai si kaw ka phi map aux?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai sawg jiang ka krax pa lud loe aux?
7 Maix hoik tawng aux ang koe phi map;
mai maix hoik tawng,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon taoh aux luat luan khaing daux tawm taix maix heue.
 
8 Maix hoik teei taix tix miien aux lih mawh pui;
yam ina maix hoik deeh yuh aux laux ka daux tawm taix tix.
9 Khiox khaw maix doh keud hoik teei tiex glaox phan yuh aux kah;b
yam in maix tag sang kieh aux deeh ka phawng tiex lie?
10 Maix kieh keeing aux koe riang rao tix keh aux;
maix kieh aux lih tix mawh ka daux vaig miex tix.
11 Maix kieh si-ang si niag to kaux aux kleuk tix tau;
kieh nex hak kawb ka si-ang si niag aux.
12 Maix tawx ju mai buan son krax caox rhawm ka aux;
krax pa kia ngien maix kieh aux im ju.
13 Yam in aux hoik tawng ka rhawm rhi maix;
maix hoik dawg koe rhawm tix sang yuh aux laux.
14 Maix hoik jiang ka aux,
yuh ang aux yuh phi map;
maix hoik but rhawm tix ang lai pluat ploi ka phi map aux.
15 Yam yuh aux phi map,
dum mawx dum an pon krax pa ciyang,
viang ang yuh phi map kawx,
mai ang pon yaog ka kaing tix.
Aux hoik sak kham gee krax kaig krax tua,c
hoik sak kha rhawm kha rhi tix.
16 Yam pon aux pa nyawm rhawm,
maix tag leue ka aux,
awm leue giex pui ka sivai sang si;
maix kawx hoik siao mhai pa daox pa am hoek tix yuh aux laux.
17 Maix hoik ca koe krax tix lauh ka aux;
maix kieh krax pa song rhawm aux nied tix nie;
maix ca nied tix lauh ka aux ang jud ang jai.
18 Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh aux lih khaing daux vaig miex tix?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai ang kieh aux dut rhawm tom sivoe sang yaox aux ka kawn pui haktiex?
19 Jauh tom lih aux khaing daux vaig miex tix,
tag sang yaox tix hu laig ka daux si meeig,
awm ang bax koe yuh nan,
ang nie blawn mhawm lie?
20 Ka daux ju aux ang hoik sang si dauh lie?
Paw vi kuang aux,
kieh aux gaux rhawm ceeix ceeix ka daux singaix pa kawn deuk in.
21 Aux hoik dex sang hu,
phan hoik hu mai ang lai chix deeh hoek;
aux sang hu ka dee pa faik meung yum,
22 Plak an mawh ngie pa faik,
veei krax yum,
vu vi;
ka dee an,
pie pa riang kawx hoik mawh pa faik heue.
