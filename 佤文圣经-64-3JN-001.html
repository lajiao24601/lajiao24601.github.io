Lai Yaw-hang Phuk Loe
1
aAux mawh kuad song;
aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka Kai-yo pa muh aux,
pa mawh paox grawm pa hoik muh aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh nin:
2 Paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
aux nawb maix mhawm kaing yuh ku ceu,
mhawm nex nham khawm uik,
awm hoik grum mhawm khuan ngao maix yuh nan.
3 Koe kawn Khrit tix blah hoek dee in,
krai aux mhong grawng pa kaux sijax maix ka daux pa mawh pa meh,
mai kieh pa mawh pa meh mawh kaing ting ka krax ot krax koe tix,
aux kied gaux rhawm ka tete.
4 Ang lai koe kaing tang ceu pa lheu kieh aux gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
kheu hoik mhong aux ot kawn nyawm khankix ka daux krax pa mawh pa meh khawm uik heue.
Yaog yaw ka Kai-yo
5 Paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
maix hoik sidaing yuh kaing son kawn Khrit khankix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix tete,
pie khawm kawn Khrit pa ang bax tawng kawx maix hoik yuh nan nang.
6 Kix hoik sax krai grawng krax muh maix ka ceu cao dee in.
Khiox khaw maix hawt tix yuh awm pa khaw rhawm Siyiex,
teem coi kix niied tix lawng ka krax pa lawng kix.
7 Kix lih hu yuh kaing plak praix kheu son Khrit,
mai ang rieb krax teem coi pa ang mawh kawn Khrit.
8 Mawh kheu nan ex tag caw tix rieb ka pui pa yuh nin,
mai yuh kaing son pa mawh pa meh tau mai kix heue.
Ti-theu-fe mai Ti-mi-theux
9 Aux hoik sax tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka ceu cao;
viang mawh nan,
Ti-theu-fe pa simie tix mawh simiang ceu cao,
ang simi ka lox pa hoik krai aux.
10 Yam hoek aux,
aux tag sang jhi lih ka kaing pa hoik yuh nawh.
Nawh teei lox caox thung thiang ka ex,
mai yuh lih ka lox pa pie lhien lox pa salao.
In kawn ang bax kub,
to kaux nawh ang rieb ka kawn Khrit pa hoek khaing tang praix tang klawng,
pie pui pa sang gaux rhawm tix rieb nawh kawx tieb tie kah,
mai sang grheu kix lih khaing daux ceu cao heue!
11 Paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
paw vi gau tix yuh ceu pa caox,
gau tix yuh ka ceu pa mhawm.
Pui mawx yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
pui an tag mawh pa dex dawt tau mai Siyiex;
pui mawx yuh kaing ceu pa caox,
pui an tag mawh pa ang sax yaox ka Siyiex heue.
12 Pui hun hoik yaog yaw Ti-mi-theux khawm uik.
Pa mawh pa meh kham sakse son nawh;
ex kawx hoik kham sakse son nawh.
Maix hoik tawng lox pa krai ex mawh tete heue.
Lox pa nghoik tix chawk mhawm ot
13 Aux kawn koe lox pa sang krai ka maix tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang rhawm tawx hu ka daux rawm meug.
14 Aux simie tix ang rheeng bawg yam mai pon yaox ka maix,
mai krai lao ka plak sivoe ngai maix heue.
15 Kieh maix mhawm ot heue!
Paox grawm maix khankix hoik chawk maix mhawm ot khawm uik.
Khiox khaw maix chawk paox grawm ku kaux mhawm ot son aux liak.
