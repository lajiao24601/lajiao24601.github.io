Lai Yi-si-ciex
Krai dawt
Sivoe sang koe baux khix mee num phuan yieh tix daix liah num ka daux bawg yam laux leeing veng Yie-lu-sa-leung, cao jhai Yi-si-ciex paung ot ka Pa-pi-loeng, mai singaix krax ot krax koe nawh mawh pa vuk vied vuk viang. Gum lox nawh mawh pa krai ka pui pa vuk vied vuk viang hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng, mai ka pui pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung. Lai Yi-si-ciex chix tix guah lih koe liah baux:
1. Siyiex laug Yi-si-ciex mawh cai jhai.
2. Ne raw ka kawn pui son grawng krax prawng krax ca Siyiex mai pa laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung.
3. Lox jhai pa krai krax pa tuk tiak tuk te khankix, mai grawng meung pa nyaw glaw pui cie tag sang pon krax ciyang.
4. Lox khu rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex khaix hoik laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung, khaix an lox jhai pa krai son singaix mhawm pa sang hoek.
5. Lox jhai pa krai laux leeing Keu-keu.
6. Yi-si-ciex krai grawng son sang deeh sum meung, mai ka grawng pa deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan.
Yi-si-ciex mawh pui pa kied kaux krax yum rhawm mai pa kied mhawm rhawm keud tix kaux. Ka kaing ceu pa hoik yuh nawh tom nie, mai hoik mawh pa si kah nawh ka daux riang su sai, gum lox pa krai nawh hoik mawh pa jhi lih ka son kaing pa sang yuh nawh khankix. Yi-si-ciex hoik kieh pa koe daux rhawm rhi tix mawh pa ting ceu, mai kieh khuan tix loh tix lih mawh pa khraox, krai sidawng ka pui ku kaux tag caw tix tang klawm ka phi map tix, khaix an mai krai nang ka grawng hoik koe krax keud pon jun pot nawh ka sang haok huan meung haktiex. Nawh yuh kaing cao giex kaing li mai kaing pa yuh cao jhai in kiex ra ceu, mai nie chix kia ngien ka nyiex buan mai ka kaing pa singax singeh heue.
Krai kaing
1. Kok ka Yi-si-ciex (1.1–3.27)
2. Lox jhai pa krai sang laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung (4.1–24.27)
3. Siyiex prawng ca ka meung piang haktiex (25.1–32.32)
4. Gum lox pa deuh Siyiex ka pui cie tix (33.1–37)
5. Lox jhai pa krai sang laux leeing Keu-keu (38.1–39.29)
6. Pa krai grawng nyiex buan son singaix pa sang hoek, mai grawng ka daux haktiex ot kix (40.1–48.35)
