Simiang Siyiex pawk lox Ha-pa-ku
2
Aux sang haok ka glawk dee jhak,
mai krox jun tix jhak,
Simiang Siyiex sang kieh aux krai lox ceu pa tix,
jhak ka sang pawk nawh lox pa krai aux kha rhawm tix yuh ka mawx.
2 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox aux ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix tiiem ka ceu pa hoik plak jhi aux ka maix sidawng ka piang plah phia,
mai kieh pui yaox pon phat lih.
3 aMaix tag caw tix tiiem lih,
mawh kheu ang singaix bawg yam lih tix mawh bax hoek.
Viang mawh nan,
bawg yam tag sang hoek,
can ceu pa hoik jhi aux ka maix hoik dex sang lih tix mawh.
Maix kawx tee een singaix in kied nhim,
viang mawh nan,
krox jun heue,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh tete,
mai ang lha.
4 bIn hoik mawh gum lox pa hoik plak jhi aux ka maix ah nin:
‘Pui caox pui caig ang pon tix im ju,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa rauh see tag sang pon ju kheu krax yum rhawm tix heue.’ ”
Pui pa ang rauh ang see tag sang laux leeing
5 Sin ngao ang pon tix een rhawm tix kah.
Pui pa koe rhawm ghoing cau nyhau,
ang pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai awm pui yum yuh nan,
ang chix sak chix naog leeing ju.
Kix hoik pon kuang ka meung haktiex tix meung hoek tix meung,
kaing ting ot dee in.
6 Kawn pui pa pon kuang tix tag sang jhax lawg kix ah nin:
“Pex hoik teei ka kraung pui tang kaux mawh cie tix,
pex tag sang laux leeing heue.
Pex sawg ka ceuk,
mai kieh tix haok huan ka daux sin ngao pa ang rauh ang see kawn sang koe miex glang lie?”
7 Yam ang maix bax doh keud kah,
maix pa mawh pa pon kuang ka pui tang kaux in tag sang lih mawh pui pa plud ka ceuk,
pui tag sang khin maix plud ka mau lhaong rao.
Pui koeng tag sang hoek tuk tiak ka maix;
kix tag sang tud rhim ka maix.
8 Maix hoik sax tud rhim ka meung tom nie sivoe an,
yam in pui pa deuk khaix tud rhim tag sang hoek tix tud rhim ka maix;
mawh kheu hoik pauh maix pui yum,
maix hoik con ka kawn pui piang haktiex mai ka veng ot kix heuec.
9 Maix hoik laux leeing heue!
Maix hoik kieh nyiex ma tix mag mi ka ceu pa tud rhim tix.
Maix yuh mheem tix ot dee lhaong son keud tix sang luat luan khaing krax laux leeing.
10 Viang mawh nan,
krax keud pa pie lhien maix tag kieh nyiex ma maix pon krax pa kaig tua.
Maix hoik yuh laux leeing ka meung tom nie,
mai tang deeh kieh to kaux tix laux leeing.
11 Pie simaox pa koe ka ding nyiex kawx tag sang kok yo lauh ka maix,
nuk nyiex kawx tag sang rom tix pawk heue.
12 Maix hoik laux leeing heue!
Maix sum veng ka phi map,
mai ka daux krax pa pauh.
13 Can meung pa hoik pon kuang maix ka daux pa taug mao tix mawh pa giex going khawm uik;
can pa sum kix ngu hoik gleeing khawm uik.
In mawh kaing yuh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing.
14 dViang mawh nan,
jiet awm hoik naog rawm ka daux rawm sigrit sigra yuh nan,
ka piang haktiex tag sang naog ka krax tawng min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
15 Maix hoik laux leeing heue!
Maix hoik map nai paox plawg paox nyawm tix ka daux krax pa song rhawm;
maix hoik kieh kix vuk vied tix awm pui pa yoek ka plai yuh nan.
16 Maix kawx tag sang cim ka krax kaig krax tua,
mai ang lai koe min plak ngai.
To kaux maix tag sang vuk vied kheu yoek maix ka plai.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh maix nyaux ka kawk pa ciyang ka to kaux maix;
min plak ngai maix tag sang tieh tix lih mawh krax kaig krax tua.
17 Maix hoik kid ka nong khaox Li-pa-noeng,
yam in tag sang kid maix goh.
Maix hoik pauh ka to tiag pa koe daux nong,
yam in to tiag in kix tag sang ghlat ka maix.
Kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh;
mawh kheu hoik pauh maix ka pui,
maix hoik con ka pui piang haktiex mai ka veng pa ot kix heue.
18 Riang rao pa krub pui koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
Riang an mawh ngie pa yuh pui ka taix tix khaing;
lheu khaing krai lox pie,
riang an ang chix krai lox ceu pa tid tix lih.
Pui pa yuh ka riang an yum rhawm ka riang an,
sang chix koe dee mhawm yuh ka mawx lie?
Riang an pie lox kawx ang chix krai heue!
19 Maix hoik laux leeing heue!
Maix krai ka khaox tix gawng ah nin:
“Siduad heue!”
Mai krai ka simaox tix lon ah nin:
“Kaoh heue!”
Riang rao an sang pon plak jhi ceu pa tix ka maix lie?
Riang an kawx tee hoik teei mau khri om kah,
viang mawh nan,
plak daux ang koe ju heue.
20 Simiang Siyiex ot ka daux nyiex buan nawh;
kawn pui piang haktiex caw tix yien yawg ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.
