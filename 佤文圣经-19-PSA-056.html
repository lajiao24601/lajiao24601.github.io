Lox rawg buan krax yum rhawm a
56
bSiyiex eue,
caox rhawm ka aux,
mawh kheu tuk tiak pui ka aux;
pui koeng ka aux gut kheem ka aux leeing lang singaix.
2 Pui koeng ka aux tuk tiak ka aux ang jud ang jai;
pui pa tieb tie ka aux hoik kied nie yuh nin heue!
3 Yam lhat graung aux,
aux een rhawm tix ka maix.
4 Aux yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka lox pa hoik deuh nawh;
aux een rhawm tix ka nawh,
aux ang lhat graung.
Kawn pui pa koe nex koe nham sang chix pon yuh ceu pa tix ka aux lie?
 
5 Pui koeng ka aux tuk tiak aux leeing lang singaix;
kix keud jee keud blaong ka grawng sang yuh aux laux leeing.
6 Kix khrawm paox tix kuan ka aux,
jiang ka khaix caong aux,
mai krox tix pauh ka aux.
7 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix ciyangc ka phi map kix;
khiox khaw maix tawx krax pa song rhawm tix piang kix,
mai lauh kix goh heue.
 
8 Maix hoik tawng ka krax laux krax leeing pa si pup aux;
maix hoik doh keud ka pa roik rawm ngai aux.
In khawm uik ang hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux phuk lai maix lie?
9 Yam khiox khaw aux ka maix,
pui koeng tom dak tix deeh;
aux hoik tawng kheu grawng in,
mawh Siyiexd pa teem ka aux.
10 Aux yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka gum lox pa hoik deuh nawh;
aux rhax lai yaog yaw ka gum lox pa hoik deuh Simiang Siyiex;
11 Aux een rhawm tix ka nawh,
mai ang lhat ang graung.
Kawn pui pa koe nex nham sang chix pon yuh tix ka aux lie?
 
12 Siyiex eue,
aux sang ap pong tix tawx ka lox pa hoik ap aux tix deuh;
aux sang tan lu krax tan pa yaog yaw ka maix.
13 Mawh kheu hoik taoh maix aux luat luan khaing krax yum,
ang kieh aux yaox tix laux leeing som nai,
pon tix nyawm ot plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
ka daux pa tawx nawh pa jan riang heue.
