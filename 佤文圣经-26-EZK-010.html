Min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex lih hu khaing nyiex buan
10
aAux yaog kaing tix jhak ka toh lawm le ka piang kaing Ci-lu-paw,
ka piang koe dang ngeung tix mu pa awm teei siang simaox klai yuh kah.
2 bSiyiex krai ka pui pa ceep kraung man keei sum an ah nin:
“Maix laig,
mai teei taix tix dawm gaox ngu pa koe ka si nax greem jaong ling Ci-lu-paw,
khaix an rhuat gaox ngu ka daux veng khawm uik heue.”
Aux hoik yaox yam laig nawh.
3 Yam laig nawh,
Ci-lu-paw cung ka plak seh nyiex buan,
koe pai awm naog ka biang brawk plak daux.
4 Min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex lih hu khaing Ci-lu-paw,
mai kaog ka diang siviex nyiex buan;
khaix an koe pai awm naog ka daux nyiex buan.
Min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex jhan riang ka biang brawk khawm uik.
5 Pui ot ka biang brawk plak praix hoik chix mhong ka lox luah preeik Ci-lu-paw khawm uik;
lox luat an jiet awm lox si kah Siyiex heue.
6 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka pui pa ceep kraung man keei sum an teei gaox ngu ka si nax greem jaong ling Ci-lu-paw;
nawh tom laig,
mai cung ka praok jaong ling an tix mu.
7 Ka daux Ci-lu-paw an tix mu nyeu taix tix hoek ka pum ngu pa koe si nax kix,
mai teei gaox ngu tix blah deuh ka daux tawm taix pui pa ceep kraung man keei sum an.
Nawh tom teei gaox ngu an hu heue.
8 Aux yaox taix ku khawn ka greem preeik Ci-lu-paw an kix,
pa awm taix pui yuh nan.
9-10 cAux baox yaox jaong ling pa awm pak peung paox pon mu,
ka praok Ci-lu-paw hoik koe ku mu.
Jaong ling an hoik toh riang awm siang simaox yuh nan;
ka praok jaong ling an ku mu baox koe jaong ling tang mu,
mai ra lai yia paox tix.
11 Yam yaog Ci-lu-paw tix hu;
kix ang siao kling to kaux tix,
mai kix hoik tang chix yaog tix hu khawm uik.
12 dTo kaux kix,
blawg kix,
taix kix,
preeik kix,
mai jaong kix hoik koe ngai khawm uik.
13 Jaong ling in kix mai jaong ling pa hoik yaox aux ka daux riang su sai bawg tix hoik awm paox tix yuh nan heue.
14 fCi-lu-paw ku mu hoik koe plak ngai pon mu yuh nin:
tix mu mawh plak ngai moie,
ra mu mawh plak ngai pui,
loe mu mawh plak ngai sivai sang si,
pon mu mawh plak ngai klang.
15 Kix hoik mawh to sat pa yaox aux ka praok rawm klawng Cia-pa-lu khawm uik.
Yam yaog Ci-lu-paw tix pu tau
16 phan ang mawh nan,
jaong ling kawx hoik hawt khaix kix yuh nang.
Yam nih kix preeik tix pu,
jaong ling hoik hawt khaix kix yuh nan.
17 Yam kaog kix,
jaong ling kawx kaog nang.
Yam pu kix,
jaong ling kawx hawt khaix kix,
mawh kheu hoik giex gang Ci-lu-paw ka jaong ling an heue.
18 Khaix an,
min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex lih hu khaing dee laig ka diang siviex nyiex buan,
mai gah tix hu ka piang Ci-lu-paw.
19 Yam kawn jhak aux kah,
kix nih preeik tix,
mai yaog tix pu khaing piang tiex;
jaong ling hawt khaix kix.
Kix kaog ka diang siviex nyiex buan plak lih singaix,
min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex ot ka piang kix.
20 Aux tawng kix mawh to sat pa ot greem Siyiex Yi-seu-liex,
pa hoik yaox aux ka praok rawm klawng Cia-pa-lu heue.
21 Ci-lu-paw ku mu koe pak ngai pon mu,
koe preeik pon mu;
ka greem preeik ku mu koe pa awm taix pui tix khawn.
22 Pak ngai kix awm pak ngai pa hoik yaox aux ka praok rawm klawng Cia-pa-lu yuh nan.
Ci-lu-paw ku mu hoik hu rauh see plak sivoe ngai tix heue.
