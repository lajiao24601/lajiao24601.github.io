Pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux kroh taix moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.9–14; Lai Lu-cia 6.6–11)
3
Ye-sux baox laig ka daux nyiex sidiex;
ka dee an koe pui tix kaux,
taix nawh hoik kroh tix khawn.
2 Koe pui tix blah sang kuan tix yaox,
sang pai ang Ye-sux pai jhawm saux moi ka daux singaix tuah,
son sang deuh phi map piang nawh.
3 Ye-sux ah nin ka pui pa koe jhawm saux kroh taix an:
“Cung ka plak sivoe”,
4 khaix an chawk pui hun ah nin:
“Phan mawh grawng singaix tuah,
lox tat jhawm prawng ex hoik tat tix deuh yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh yuh kaing pa mhawm mawh yuh kaing pa caox lie?
Mawh taoh ka ju pui mawh pauh ka ju pui lie?”
Pui hun ang nye tix gum kawx.
5 Ye-sux song rawm mai kling ngai tix jhak ka plak goix plak dawm tix,
rhawm rhi nawh hoik laux son pui in kix,
mawh kheu hoik kiien rhawm rhi kix.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka pui pa koe jhawm saux an:
“Nyat taix maix rauh heue!”
Pui an nyat taix tix,
taix nawh tom moi heue.
6 Khaix hoik lih pui Fa-li-saix khankix khaing daux nyiex sidiex,
dum mawx dum an,
kix kum kawng tau mai pui cub caw Si-luix keud sawn sang yuh ka Ye-sux,
son sang pauh nawh yuh ka mawx heue.
Pui hun ka praok rawm nhawng
7 Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh khankix lih hu khaing dee an hoek ka praok rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix,
cub pui hun hawt khaix nawh.
Kix mawh pa kaoh khaing Cia-li-lix,
Yo-thaix,
8 Yie-lu-sa-leung,
Yi-thu-mai,
baoh haktiex toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang khankix,
mai veng pa koe dex Thai-eux mai Si-toeng.
Cub pui hun hoek tix sawg ka Ye-sux mawh son pa hoik mhong kix ka kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik.
9 aPui kied hun,
Ye-sux tawx lox nhie pui gau tix rien khre reu iak tix mu,
son ang pui sang si ghox ka nawh.
10 Mawh kheu hoik pai nawh pui pa koe jhawm saux moi tom nie,
can pui pa koe jhawm saux hoik su shiet tix hoek ka nawh khawm uik,
son sang pu pi ka nawh.
11 Pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai daux an kix yaox ka nawh,
mai gup tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
kok yo ah nin:
“Maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex heue!”
12 Ye-sux bre tix tawx lox ka kix ang cu kix krai mawh tix pui mawx heue.
Ye-sux laug ka kawn cau kao ra kaux
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.1–4; Lai Lu-cia 6.12–16)
13 Ye-sux haok ka gawng,
mai khrawm ka can pui pa sang lo nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix khawm uik.
Kix tom hoek heue,
14 Ye-sux laug pui kao ra kaux,
kok kix mawh kawn cau,
mai baox ah nin ka kix:
“Aux laug ka pex,
tag sang kieh pex ot tau mai tix jheeng jheeng,
tag sang ploi nang pex hu tawx lox buan ka pui;
15 pex tag sang koe siawp sibrawm tix grheu ka phi heue.”
16 Pui pa hoik laug Ye-sux kao ra kaux mawh:
Si-meung (Ye-sux baox sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Pi-teux),
17 Ya-keux kawn simex Si-pi-thaix mai Yaw-hang pux aik Ya-keux (Ye-sux baox sum ceu kaux mawh Pang-ni-chix,
dee bleeh mawh “rhawm rhi nawh awm pui pa yuh tuh sah yuh nan”).
18 Ang-teu-liex,
Fe-lix,
Pa-to-law-mai,
Ma-thaix,
To-ma,
Ya-keux kawn simex Ya-leu-fe,
Ta-thaix,
Si-meung pa mawh cub caw pui pa kin jhawm,
19 mai Yo-tax pui Cia-liiex pa coh Ye-sux khaix an heue.
Ye-sux mai Piie-si-phux
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.22–32; Lai Lu-cia 11.14–23; 12.10)
20 Ye-sux ing ka nyiex;
cub pui hun baox su shiet tix hoek,
pie bawg yam sawm nawh mai pui gau nawh ang koe.
21 Pui daux nyiex ma Ye-sux hoik tawng grawh grawng in,
mai sang lih tix tieb tie ka nawh,
mawh kheu koe pui pa ah nin:
“Nawh yhot heue!”
22 bKoe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri tix blah pa hoek khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
“Mawh Piie-si-phux pa laig ka daux nawh heue!
Nawh hoik een rhawm tix ka simiang phi grheu phi heue!”
23 Ye-sux kok pui in kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
mai teei gum lox phek tiiem ah nin ka kix:
“Sa tan sang pon chix grheu Sa tan yuh ka mawx lie?
24 Meung tix meung phan simah paox tix,
meung an tag ang chix kaux cong.
25 Nyiex tix nyiex phan simah ka paox tix,
nyiex an kawx tag sang laux leeing.
26 Yuh nin,
phan meung cie Sa tan simah ka paox tix,
nawh tag ang chix kaux cong,
dee si dauh tag sang laux leeing heue.
27 “Ang koe pui pa pon laig daux nyiex ma pui naing pa kua,
mai teei hu ka sin ngao nawh;
nawh tag ran phrawk ka pui naing pa kua an sivoe,
tom taug chix pon tud ka nyiex ma nawh heue.
28 “Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
phi map pa yuh kawn pui khawm uik mai lox pa map nai khawm uik chix tix pon krax pa pluat ploi.
29 cViang mawh nan,
pui pa map nai ka Khuan Singax Singeh tag ang pon krax pa pluat ploi leeing ju leeing phan,
mawh kheu pa yuh nawh mawh phi map leeing ju heue.” (
30 Ye-sux krai ka gum lox in mawh kheu hoik koe pui pa ah nin:
“Mawh khuan pa seeing brai pa laig daux nawh heue.”)
Miex Ye-sux mai pux aik nawh
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.46–50; Lai Lu-cia 8.19–21)
31 Bawg yam in,
miex Ye-sux mai pux aik nawh hoek;
kix cung ka plak praix,
mai kieh pui krai ka Ye-sux,
krai sawg kix ka nawh.
32 Koe cub pui hun ngawm ka praok Ye-sux;
kix krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Eue,
miex maix mai pux aik maix ot plak praix,
sawg ka maix heue!”
33 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui mawx mawh miex aux?
Pui mawx mawh pux aik aux?”
34 Nawh kling ngai tix ka pui pa ngawm ka sivie siveng tix ah nin:
“Pex jhak,
pui in kix hoik mawh miex aux pux aik aux heue!
35 Can pui pa hawt ka alo Siyiex tag sang mawh pux aik aux,
pux o aux,
mai miex aux heue.”
