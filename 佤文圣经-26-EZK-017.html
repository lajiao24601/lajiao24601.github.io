Lox phek tiiem son klang ting mai khaox plix siiet
17
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai lox phek tiiem tix ceu ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
3 mai kieh kix tawng lox pa sang krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka kix.
Koe klang ting tix mu preeik kied ting,
hauk kied nie kied mhawm.
Klang an nih preeik tix,
pu ka piang gawng Li-pa-noeng,
mai kaoh dut ka nyoik rao khaox si oe bhawng,
4 viex hoek ka sigreung daux laih daux cau,
daux veng pa yuh pui lai gee.
5 Khaix an,
klang an teei kawn khaox khaing haktiex Yi-seu-liex tix gawng,
mai viex hoek sum ka dee mhawm haktiex,
dee an rawm hoik kub tix kieh kawn khaox an ting lhaong.
6 Khaox in hoik glaoh tix ting lhaong,
mai lih mawh khaox plix siiet pa tiiem,
pa tawx tix hu khawm pon daog.
Kak khaox an tawx tix hu ka klang ting,
riah hoik kied raux laig daux tiex,
mai lih mawh khaox plix siiet pa tawx kak tix hu heue.
7 “Klang ting baox koe tix mu,
mai baox koe nang preeik pa kied ting tix brawm,
mai hauk kied nie.
Khaox plix siiet in tawx riah tix mai kak tix hu ka klang ting in,
mai simie tix nie pon dee keet rawm lheu khaing si peem khaox plix an.
8 Tete te lai,
khaox plix siiet an hoik dawg sum tix ot daux si peem dee mhawm tiex dee haoh rawm,
pa kub tix kieh khaox an plaung kak,
plix plix,
mai lih mawh khaox plix siiet pa mhawm plix heue.
9 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong chawk ah nin:
khaox plix siiet in sang pon chix ting lhaong lie?
Khex ang klang ting in sang pon laoh khaox in hu khawm riah,
pieh ka plix uik,
raig kak pot,
mai kieh khaox kroh rham lie?
Ang siao lo meung haktiex ting tag sang pon laoh khaox an hu khawm riah yie khaing.
10 Mawh tete,
khaox an rug hoik sum kah,
viang mawh nan,
kawn sang chix pon ting lhaong lie?
Nawh hoik pon kham pa phlu bhaung plak lih singaix lie?
Ang chix kroh rham ka daux si peem an lie?”
Kah dee bleeh lox phek tiiem
11 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
12 a“Maix chawk pui an kix pa lieh lox,
tawng ang kix tawng ka dee bleeh lox phek tiiem in.
Krai ka kix,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai teei ka simiang kix mai siliex nawh giex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng,
13 mai laug pui lih khaing ka daux ceu pui nyiex simiang tix kaux,
yuh sijax mukheu tau mai nawh,
nhie nawh tiex tu son sang cu lox.
Simiang Pa-pi-loeng baox giex ka pui pa ting ceu tix blah,
sang kieh mawh pa nhie pui cu ka lox tix,
14 son ang sang kieh meung haktiex in haok huan,
mai kieh nawh priex tix hawt ka lox sijax mukheu in.
15 Viang mawh nan,
simiang Yo-tax hoik tieh vheen.
Nawh ploi kawn cau tix hu ka Ai-cix,
khiox khaw brung naing mai pui naing pa hun hoek teem coi ka tix.
Nawh sang pon yuh lie?
Nawh sang pon tix luat luan lie?
Nawh sang pon tik vud ka sijax mukheu in mai ang yaox krax ciyang lie?”
16 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka ju tix leeing ju yuh nin:
simiang in tag sang yum ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Mawh kheu hoik ap simiang Pa-pi-loeng nawh mawh simiang,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik lieh lheu ka lox sijax mukheu,
mai hoik tik vud ka lox sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh nawh tau mai simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
17 Yam hoek simiang Pa-pi-loeng tix puing veng,
sum ding tiex,
kaong daux rawng,
yuh krax pa pauh,
viang simiang Ai-cix hoik ploi pui naing pa kied chix puing naing kawx,
mai ang pon taoh teem ka nawh.
18 Nawh hoik lieh lheu tik vud ka sijax mukheu.
Nawh hoik yuh ka kaing in khawm uik,
yam in ang yaox tix luat luan heue.”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka ju tix leeing ju yuh nin:
aux tag sang ciyang ka nawh;
mawh kheu hoik tik vud nawh ka gum lox pa tiex tu nawh ka daux ceu kaux aux,
mai hoik yuh laux ka sijax mukheu pa tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux aux.
20 Aux tag sang nih rub tix giex ka nawh,
viex nawh hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng ciyang ka nawh,
mawh kheu ang nawh cu nghiet ka aux.
21 Pui naing nawh pa kied kua tag sang yum khawm uik;
pui naing pa ang pex khankix tag sang to mox tix hu ku dauh ku dee.
Yam an pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex;
aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Gum lox pa tat Siyiex tix deuh singaix pa sang hoek
22 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai yuh nin:
Aux tag sang caik dut ka rao nyoik khaox si oe bhawng,
mai raig pot ka kak khaox pa awn nawh;
aux tag sang sum ot ka piang gawng,
23 ka dee blawn lhaong gawng Yi-seu-liex.
Khaox an tag sang ting lhaong,
mai plix plix,
lih mawh khaox si oe bhawng pa ting lhaong tix gawng.
Sim ku ceu tag sang hoek tix yuh mheem tix ot kah,
mai sawg dee sang laux lee tix ka greem veei khaox.
24 Can khaox pa koe daux tiag hoik tawng khawm uik yuh nin:
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex kid ka khaox ting in khrup,
mai kieh khaox iak khankix ting lhaong;
aux tag sang kieh khaox klai kroh rham,
mai kieh khaox kroh rham ting lhaong.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin.
Aux hoik krai,
aux tag sang yuh lih tete heue.
