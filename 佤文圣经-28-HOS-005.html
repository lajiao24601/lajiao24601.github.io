5
“Cao giex kaing li khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet heue!
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix heue!
Pui nyiex ma Vawng eue,
pex kawx tag caw tix nghiet heue!
Pex khaw tix prawng ca pui ka daux krax pa khaw caw,
yam in pex hoik tieh tix kham krax prawng ca;
mawh kheu hoik lih pex tix mawh jhawk ka Mi-see-pa,
mawh rub pa nih tix ka gawng Tha-paw,
2 mawh o pa kaong ka See-thing.
Aux tag sang ciyang ka pex heue!
3 Aux hoik tawng yung ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
kix ang pon mox ka aux.
Kix ang cu nghiet ka aux,
mai ang keung krub ka aux heue.”
Heu-si-ax ne raw ka pui pa krub riang rao
4 Krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tieb tie kix ang hoek ka Siyiex.
Mawh kheu krub kix riang rao,
kix tom ang tawng ka Simiang Siyiex.
5 Krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi pui Yi-seu-liex hoik lih mawh pa kham ka phi map to kaux kix.
Phi map kix hoik kieh kix goh;
pui Yo-tax kawx hoik goh tau mai kix.
6 Kix tan lu moi yung ka Simiang Siyiex;
in kix ang koe dee mhawm tid tix son kix.
Kix ang yaox ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik tik vud Simiang Siyiex ka kix.
7 Kix ang cu nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex;
kawn praix pa keh kix mawh kawn praix pui tang ceu.
Mawh kheu nan,
haktiex brex brum kix pui hoik dug dig ka heue.
Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex puing paox tix
8 Tag caw tix phlu teet tawng pui naing ka Ci-pi-yax heue!
Tawx lox pa so ka La-max heue!
Ploi lox riag yo ka Paw-theu-lix heue!
Pui Piien-ya-ming eue,
kaoh puing naing heue!
9 Singaix pa ciyang hoik hoek;
meung pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang laux leeing.
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh tete heue!
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang tawx krax pa song rhawm ka simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai ciyang kix awm rawm ting pa chun tix hoek yuh nan,
mawh kheu tud kix ka haktiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex.
11 Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang kham krax gut krax kheem,
mai kieh haktiex pa tawx Siyiex ka kix grai,
mawh kheu ca kawn khiox khaw nawh meung pa ang koe riang koe raoa pa ot ka sivie siveng tix khankix teem coi tix.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang yuh laux ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
khawm ka pui Yo-tax nang heue.
13 “Yam hoik yaox pui Yi-seu-liex kied kin jhawm saux tix,
yam hoik yaox pui Yo-tax kied nie mat tix,
pui Yi-seu-liex tom hu ka simiang ting Ya-sux,
mai khiox khaw nawh teem coi tix;
viang mawh nan nawh ang pon pai moi ka kix,
ang pon kie ka dee mat kix.
14 Aux tag sang yuh ka pui Yi-seu-liex awm sivai sang si yuh nan,
mai yuh ka pui Yo-tax awm sivai sang si pa nhum ju pa koe riang yuh nan.
Aux tag sang hoek tix riah ka kix,
reu kix hu,
mai ang koe pui pa pon taoh ka kix heue.
15 “Aux tag sang tik vud ka pui cie tix,
tom hoek doh keud kix ka phi map tix,
mai deeh hoek tix sawg ka aux.
Yam ot kix ka daux krax pa kha rhawm,
kix tag sang nied tix hoek khiox khaw ka aux heue.”
