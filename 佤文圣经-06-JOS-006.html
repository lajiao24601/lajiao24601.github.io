Veng Yie-li-keu laux leeing
6
Pui Yie-li-keu nyip ka siviex veng kaux,
mai rien tix mhawm mhawm,
son sang tieb tie ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa sang hoek tix puing.
Ang koe pui pa pon laig lih khaing siviex veng.
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Aux hoik ap veng Yie-li-keu mai simiang kix khawm pui naing kix ka daux tawm taix maix.
3 Maix thung ka pui naing,
lai lawm ka veng tix ngaix tix bawg,
mai yuh si dauh liah singaix.
4 Maix kieh cao giex kaing li aliah kaux tang giex ka teet rung yung tix kaux tix mu,
hu ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu.
Hoek daux aliah singaix,
maix thung pui naing lai lawm aliah bawg,
cao giex kaing li an kix aliah kaux phlu ka teet.
5 Khaix an,
kix tag phlu ka teet lang luah tix bawg;
yam mhong pex luah teet lang an,
pex tag ah lox pa kied ting si num,
ding veng tom ghrup.
Yam an,
pui naing khawm uik tom pheet tix laig ka daux veng heue.”
6 Yaw-su-yax khrawm ka cao giex kaing li khankix hoek,
ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag klawm ka teug sijax mukheu,
ka daux pex pui aliah kaux giex ka teet hu sivoe teug sijax mukheu.”
7 Khaix an,
nawh tawx lox jauh tix kieh pui naing lai lawm ka veng.
Mawh pui naing pa hu sivoe,
hu sivoe teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex.
8-9 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax tawx lox tix gum,
pui naing hu sivoe,
khaix an mawh cao giex kaing li pa phlu ka teet,
baox mawh cao giex kaing li pa klawm ka teug sijax mukheu,
dee nghoik mawh pui naing pa rien plak khaix.
Cao giex kaing li an aliah kaux phlu teet khawm hu tix krax;
10 viang mawh nan,
Yaw-su-yax tawx lox ka pui naing ang cu kix ah lox si num,
krox tom tawx nawh lox,
ang pon tix nye.
11 Yuh nin,
Yaw-su-yax tawx lox kieh kix klawm ka teug sijax mukheu,
lai lawm ka veng tix vua,
khaix an tag deeh ing ka bang,
it ka dee an.
12-13 Ngaix khaix,
Yaw-su-yax yaog tix kaoh cao cao plak pon ngawb.
Cao giex kaing li khankix mai pui naing bawg ra lai lawm ka veng.
Kix hawt ka been ngaix tix hu mhawm mhawm:
mawh pui naing cao thung krax,
khaix an mawh cao giex kaing li aliah kaux pa phlu ka teet,
baox khaix mawh cao giex kaing li pa klawm ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
dee nghoik mawh pui naing pa rien plak khaix.
Cao giex kaing li an aliah kaux phlu ka teet khawm hu tix.
14 Daux ngaix in,
kix baox lai lawm ka veng tix vua,
khaix an deeh ing ka bang.
Yuh nin,
been liah singaix,
kix lai lawm ka veng hu.
15 Daux aliah singaix,
kix kaoh cao cao yam taug riang praix,
kix yuh awm pa yuh kix lai lawm veng aliah bawg.
Mawh ngie daux in singaix,
kix lai lawm ka veng aliah bawg.
16 Hoek aliah bawg,
yam phlu cao giex kaing li khankix teet,
Yaw-su-yax tawx lox kieh pui naing ah lox si num,
mai baox krai ka kix:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx veng in ka pex heue!
17 Can kraung pa ot daux veng tag yuh laux khawm uik,
kieh mawh krax tan tawx ka Simiang Siyiex;
mawh ngie La-heux sibun coh kaux mai pui pa ot daux nyiex nawh,
pex tag deuh ka ju kix,
mawh kheu nawh hoik mox pui pa jiang ex.
18 Pex ang pon tix teei kraung pa khaw tix kieh laux;
phan teei kah,
pex taug kieh nyiex bang pui Yi-seu-liex yaox krax jhax lawg,
mai krax laux leeing.
19 Can kraung pa raug ka mau,
khri,
tawng,
rhiem kieh mawh ceu pa singax singeh tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
deuh ot ka daux nyiex krao cie Simiang Siyiex.”
20 aMawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li khankix jauh tix phlu teet tawng,
yam mhong pui khankix ka tom ah lo si num,
guh veng tom ghrup ka heue.
Pui naing khawm uik pheet tix laig,
mai pon ka veng in.
21 Kix siao ka vaig pauh ka pui pa ot daux veng,
ang guah simex bun,
kawn nyawm pui kuad pauh ka khawm uik.
Kix kawx pauh khawm moi,
yung,
mai la heue.
22 Yaw-su-yax ah nin ka pui jiang an kieh ra kaux:
“Pax hu ka nyiex La-heux sibun coh kaux an,
hawt ka lox pa tiex tu pax ka nawh,
viex nawh mai pui nyiex ma nawh hoek.”
23 Kiex tom hu,
viex La-heux hoek,
mai baox viex ka miex keeing nawh,
pux aik nawh,
mai pui nyiex tang kaux nawh hoek,
kieh kix ot ka plak praix nyiex bang pui Yi-seu-liex dee nyawm ot ang yaox krax tuk tiak.
24 Khaix an kix tom ploi ngu gleeing ka veng.
Kix viex ngie kraung pa raug ka mau,
khri,
tawng,
rhiem lih,
deuh ot ka nyiex krao cie Simiang Siyiex,
kraung tang ceu gleeing ka khawm uik.
25 bYaw-su-yax tawx krax klawd ju ka La-heux sibun coh kaux mai daux nyiex nawh khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik mox nawh pui jiang Yie-li-keu ra kaux pa ploi Yaw-su-yax hu.
(Jauh yam an,
La-heux mai daux nyiex ma nawh ot ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex.)
26 cYam in,
Yaw-su-yax ploi lox pa kin tix gum:
kieh Simiang Siyiex jhax lawg ka pui pa keud tix sang deeh sum ka veng Yie-li-keu:
Pui pa sum ka simaox veng,
tag som nai ka kawn ai tix;
pui pa sum ka siviex veng,
tag som nai ka kawn nghoik tix.
27 Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai Yaw-su-yax;
mai min Yaw-su-yax hoik tauh hu ka daux haktiex baoh an khawm uik heue.
