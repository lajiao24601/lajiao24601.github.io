Simaox pa im ju mai meung pa singax
2
Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw teei tix tik ka kaing ceu pa caox khawm uik,
paw lai baox pie lhien,
lhien tix mawh pui mhawm,
pa boh rhawm boh rhi,
phan ang mawh nan,
krai lox pa ang mhawm son pui.
2 Tag caw tix awm kawn ngie pa khraox keh yuh nan,
simie ka rawm teeh pa singax ku bawg ku yam,
son sang pon tix ting huan mai pon krax taoh ka daux pa peux rawm teeh.
3 aAwm krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pex hoik cim ka buan son krax caox rhawm Simiang Siyiex heue.”
4 Tag caw tix hoek dex ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simaox pa im ju;
viang pui hoik tik vud ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex laug ka nawh,
muh ka nawh.
5 Pex kawx tag caw tix awm simaox an,
pa teei pui tix sum nyiex buan pa dex dawt mai khuan,
nawh mawh cao giex kaing li dee an,
mai tan lu krax tan pa dex dawt mai khuan pa hoik khaw rhawm Siyiex ka khaing daux Ye-sux Khrit.
6 bMawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Aux hoik laug simaox pa ngawh tix lon
mai kieh mawh simaox jaong ding Shi-ang;
can pui pa een rhawm ka nawh,
ang yaox tix som nai tix kaux kawx heue.
7 cKa son pex pa mawh pui pa yum rhawm,
simaox in mawh pa ting ngawh;
viang mawh nan,
ka son pui pa ang yum rhawm khankix mawh nin:
Simaox pa hoik tik cang mai yuh nyiex
hoik lih mawh simaox jaong nyiex pa blawn ting ceu!
8 dKa daux phuk lai sigang khri an tix dauh ah nin:
Lon simaox pa sideeing in tix lon
mawh simaox pa kieh pui goh ka heue.
Kix hoik goh kah,
mawh kheu ang kix yum rhawm ka gum lox pa mawh pa meh;
in kawx hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo Siyiex heue.
9 eViang mawh nan,
pex hoik mawh ceu pui pa laug tix teei,
mawh cao giex kaing li ka daux nyiex ma simiang,
ka daux meung pa singax singeh pui cie Siyiex.
Siyiex kok pex lih khaing daux pa faik,
laig ka daux pa riang nawh,
mai hoek tix krai hu ka kaing pa daox deng yuh nawh.
10 fKa daux singaix pa hoik luan,
pex ang mawh pui cie Siyiex,
yam in hoik mawh pui cie nawh;
ka daux singaix pa hoik luan ang bax pon krax pa caox rhawm Siyiex,
yam in hoik pon krax pa caox rhawm nawh heue.
Siliex Siyiex
11 Paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
pex ot ka piang haktiex mawh pui paung ot,
mawh nyo si nie.
Aux sidiex gau pex paw vi hawt ka alo nex nham;
alo nex nham ca kawn yuh naing ka ju khuan.
12 Ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad,
pex caw kieh tix mhawm jhawm,
kieh pui pa ah lox ang mhawm,
pa ne raw pex yuh kaing pa ang mhawm khankix,
kheu yaox kaing pa mhawm pa yuh pex,
ka daux singaix yam deeh hoek Simiang Siyiex,
tag tawx min plak ngai ka Siyiex heue.
13 Kheu son Simiang Siyiex,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka cao giex kaing khaing si nax kawn pui yuh nin:
pa mawh simiang ka plak piang
14 mai simiang naing pa tawx nawh hoek tix tawx khawng phak ka ceu pa mhawm,
ciyang ka ceu pa caox.
15 Mawh kheu alo Siyiex tag sang nhie pex teei kaing pa yuh tix ceu pa mhawm,
hoek thox jheeh cub pui daox pui aw khankix kah.
16 Pex mawh pui pa yaox tix luat luan,
viang mawh nan,
paw vi teei ceu pa luat luan tawb kheem ka ceu pa caox pa caig tix ceu kawx;
tag caw tix mawh siliex Siyiex.
17 Pex tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka pui ku kaux;
tag caw tix muh ka pux aik pux o pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex;
tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex;
tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka simiang meung heue.
Khrit kham gee krax kha rhawm son ex
18 Pa mawh siliex khankix,
pex tag caw tix nab yam nghiet ka lox simiang tix,
ang joeng yuh nin ka simiang pa mhawm rhawm awn rhawm,
khawm simiang pa cau nyhau khankix kawx tag caw tix yuh nin.
19 Phan pex hoik tawng kheu in hoik mawh alo Siyiex,
cu tix kham gee krax kha rhawm rhi pa ang pex caw tix sang kham,
Siyiex tag sang tawx buan son ka pex.
20 Phan pex kham gee pa lauh kheu yuh tix kaing pa phit lud,
an sang chix koe min plak ngai yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh ngie pui pa kham gee krax kha rhawm kheu pa yuh tix kaing ceu pa mhawm tom tag pon ka buan son pa tawx Siyiex.
21 Siyiex kok ka pex,
kaing ting ot dee in;
mawh kheu Khrit hoik kha rhawm tix son pex,
mai yuh tix deuh pex yaox kaing ceu pa mhawm,
son sang kieh pex pon hawt ka khaix caong nawh.
22 gNawh ang bax lud loe;
ang sax koe pui pa mhong pie lhien nawh.
23 hNawh kham pa jhax lawg pui,
mai ang pawk,
nawh kham pa nu nai pui,
mai ang krai lox pa ghlat,
nawh een rhawm tix ka cao prawng ca pa rauh pa see khaing.
24 iKhrit teei phi map ex viex hoek ka khaox lhak,
mai ang kieh ex lai baox ot ka daux phi map,
im ngie khaing krax rauh see.
Mawh kheu rhoi khaix mat nawh,
ex tom pon krax pa pai moi.
25 Ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
pex hoik awm yung pa grai khawm uik,
yam in hoik deeh hoek,
mai hawt khaix cao liang ju khuan pex mai ka cao pa kia ngien heue.
