Grawng yum Sa-mu-eu
25
Sa-mu-eu hoik yum,
can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik khrawm paox tix yiiem son nawh,
khaix an,
si paung nawh ot ka daux haktiex La-max pa keh nawh heue.
Ta-wue mai Ai-pi-kai
Khaix kaing in,
Ta-wue lih ka daux tiag Pa-lang.
2-3 Koe ceu pui Cia-leux tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Na-pax,
mai nawh kaoh khaing veng Ma-yuing.
Nawh ot tix yuh lai gee ka veng Cia-mix,
mai hoik mawh pui pa koe mau,
nawh koe kawn yung loe rheng mu mai pex tix rheng mu.
Ceu kaux moeng nawh mawh Ai-pi-kai,
mawh bun pa kied rien rhawm tix kaux,
mai Na-pax mawh pui caox pa cau nyhau tix kaux heue.
4 Ta-wue mhong grawng pa ot Na-pax tix ghrip hauk yung ka Cia-mix daux tiag,
5 nawh tom ploi pui pa nhum ju kao kaux hu ka Cia-mix mai sawg ka Na-pax,
son sang nawb lox pa ting buan son ka nawh.
6 Ta-wue nhie kix ah lox nawb buan ka Na-pax son tix ah nin:
“Kieh maix lang ju pu rhang!
Kieh maix mai pui nyiex ma maix pon pa nhim!
Kieh kaing yuh maix pon yaox tix mhawm khawm uik!
7 Nawh mhong ot maix tix ghrip hauk yung tin,
simie maix tawng,
cao liang khawng brie maix hoik yie ot tau mai yix,
mai yix ang yuh krax pa ang khaw ka kix,
yam ot kix ka Cia-mix,
kix ang bax kieh ceu tid tix grai.
8 Maix chawk tix yaox ka kix,
kix tag sang krai maix mhong.
Ka daux poi yix hoek ka maix tin,
Ta-wue nhie pex kia yix mhawm mhawm.
Maix yaox tix guah yix cie ceu pa tix,
maix chix tix tawx ka yix,
mai ka paox grawm maix kawx Ta-wue,
chix tix tawx ka heue.”
9 Pui pa ot greem Ta-wue teei min ceu kaux Ta-wue krai lox in kix ka Na-pax,
mai krox jun nawh pawk kah.
10 Na-pax pawk ah nin:
“Ta-wue mawh mawx?
Kawn simex Yie-si mawh mawx?
Ka dee in kix siliex cawng pa to mox tix hoik kied haoh,
11 Aux ang nieb pon tawx khao mun,
rawm,
mai nex pa rien son pui pa ghrip hauk yung ka pui pa ang tix tawng khankix pa kaoh khaing dee mawx lie!”
12 Pui pa ot greem Ta-wue deeh ing,
mai krai lox Na-pax ka Ta-wue.
13 Ta-wue tom tawx lox ah nin:
“Kawg vaig tix ku kaux!”
Kix hawt tix yuh heue.
Ta-wue kawx kawg ka vaig tix,
mai viex ka pui ot greem tix pon yieh kaux hu,
deuh ra yieh kaux mawh pa pau kraung khrai pa siao heue.
14 Siliex Na-pax tix kaux ah nin ka moeng Na-pax pa kaux Ai-pi-kai:
“Hoik mhong maix ka lie?
Ta-wue ploi pui cau tix hoek ah lox nawb ka simiang ex khaing daux tiag,
viang mawh nan,
cao simiang ex map nai ka kix.
15 Kix mhawm ka ex tete,
mai ang sax yuh krax pa nyhap ka ex tix bawg kawx.
Yam ot yix tau mai kix daux tiag daux tu,
kix ang sax briex ka kraung khrai yix.
16 Yam liang yix ka yung,
yix hoik ot tau mai kix,
mai kix kia ngien ka yix leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
17 Maix keud tix yaox sang yuh ex ka mawx lie?
Kaing in rien tix chix lih mawh krax laux krax leeing ka simiang ex mai ka pui nyiex pui ma khawm uik.
Simiang yix kied cau nyhau,
nawh ang nghiet ka lox krai pui tix kaux kawx heue!”
18 Ai-pi-kai tom nied tix rien khre ka khao mun ra yieh lon,
plai ra hau hak,
yung pa hoik tong sin phuan mu,
gaox nghox ca pa hoik tong sin kao aliah kongcing,
plix siiet kroh tix yieh cieh,
khao mun plix ruh ra yieh lon,
mai tang ka piang la,
19 khaix an nawh ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Pex hu sivoe ngai,
mai aux sang hawt khaix pex.”
Ai-pi-kai ang bax krai kaing in ka mex tix heue.
20 Ai-pi-kai brug ka la,
mai via lih khaing blaong gawng,
nawh yaox ka Ta-wue tau mai pui pa ot greem nawh hoek dum mawx dum an.
21 Ta-wue keud ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
“Aux yuh ka mawx lai kia ngien ka sin ngao cie pui an ka daux tiag in,
mai kieh nawh ang yaox tix grai ka kraung ceu tid tix.
Aux hoik teem coi nawh yuh nin,
nawh lai kawn tawx pa caox son pa mhawm!
22 Sivoe sang riang praix,
phan aux ang pauh pui kix yum uik,
kieh Siyiex ciyang ka auxa tom cien heue!”
23 Yam yaox Ai-pi-kai ka Ta-wue,
nawh nied tix lih khaing la,
mai grux gawng tix krub piang tiex,
24 ah nin ka sivoe caong Ta-wue:
“Simiang aux eue,
nghiet ka lox krai aux ha!
Phi map in kieh mawh aux pa klawm.
25 Maix paw vi si mi Na-pax pui pa ang mhawm an;
nawh hoik mawh pui pa kied daoxb tete!
Yam hoek siliex maix,
aux ang bax ot tin.
26 Simiang Siyiex ang bax kieh to kaux maix yaox tix pawk bix ka pui pa koeng,
mai pauh ka kix.
Yam in aux ah lox pa tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju:
pui pa koeng ka maix mai can pui pa keud tix sang yuh maix laux,
tag sang hoek krax ciyang awm Na-pax yuh nan.
27 Viex maix rieb ka khawng phak pa viex aux hoek,
mai guah tix tawx ka pui pa ot greem maix heue!
28 Maix kawx pluat ploi ka krax pa lud loe aux liak.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh maix mawh simiang,
mai doeh ceu cad maix kawx tag sang mawh simiang,
mawh kheu hoik yuh maix naing son Simiang Siyiex.
Ka daux ju maix tag ang lai chix yuh krax lud krax loec.
29 “Koe pui keud tix sang puing ka maix,
yuh maix laux,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix chix kia ngien ka maix,
awm hoik kia ngien ka siang pa koe ngawh yuh nan.
Phan mawh pui pa koeng ka maix,
mai Simiang Siyiex tag sang si vun tix tik ka kix,
awm hoik teei pui tix si vun simaox yuh nan.
30 Yam hoik hawt Simiang Siyiex tix tawx maix mhawm kaing yuh ku ceu,
mai kieh maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex awm lox pa hoik deuh tix,
31 maix tom ang yaox tix pauh ka pui pa ang koe grawng mai hoik klih rhawm tix kheu pawk bix son cao tix.
Yam hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex buan son ka maix,
khiox khaw maix paw vi piawm ka aux liak.”
32 Ta-wue ah nin ka nawh:
“Yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex!
Nawh ploi maix hoek ka aux daux in singaix!
33 Aux yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex ka pa hoik yaox krax keud krax sawn maix mai can kaing pa hoik yuh maix khawm uik daux in singaix,
mawh kheu maix ang lai kieh aux yaox tix pauh ka pui mai ka phi map pa pawk aux bix son to kaux tix.
34 Simiang Siyiex ang lai kieh aux yuh maix laux.
Viang mawh nan,
aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa im ju leeing ju,
phan ang mawh maix pa phai hoek ka aux,
sivoe sang riang praix,
can simex nyiex ma Na-pax tag sang yum uik!”
35 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue rieb ka khawng phak pa viex Ai-pi-kai hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Deeh ing ka nyiex mhawm mhawm heue.
Aux tag hawt tix yuh ka lox maix heue.”
36 Ai-pi-kai deeh ing ka Na-pax dee an,
mai Na-pax jiet yuh poi krax gaux rhawm ka daux nyiex tix,
awm hoik gaux rhawm simiang yuh nan heue.
Nawh gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mai nyaux plai tom hoik yoek tix,
mawh kheu nan,
ka daux bo an Ai-pi-kai ang bax krai nawh mhong kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh,
37 jun tom hoek ngawb ngaix khaix yam hoik haing nawh,
taug krai ka nawh.
Na-pax dum mawx dum an ang lai tawng praix,
mai hoik cong ang lai pon chu.
38 Khaix hoik luan kao singaix,
Simiang Siyiex baox lauh ka Na-pax,
nawh tom yum heue.
39 Ta-wue mhong hoik yum Na-pax,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex!
Nawh hoik pawk bix ka son pa hoik map nai Na-pax ka aux,
ang lai kieh aux pa mawh siliex yaox tix yuh kaing pa lud loe.
Simiang Siyiex ciyang Na-pax jao hoik yuh nawh kaing pa caox pa caig heue.”
Ang koe miex glang tix,
Ta-wue ploi pui hu mhaing tix moeng ka Ai-pi-kai.
40 Siliex Ta-wue hoek ka Cia-mix,
yaox ka Ai-pi-kai,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Ta-wue ploi aux hoek rieb maix hu ka dee ot nawh tan,
mai mawh moeng nawh heue.”
41 Ai-pi-kai grux gawng tix piang tiex,
ah nin:
“Aux mawh kawn cau bun nawh;
mai aux cu tix khoek caong can siliex nawh khankix.”
42 Ai-pi-kai nied yaog tix kaoh,
brug la cie tix,
mai viex ka kawn bun siliex tix phuan kaux hu tau mai siliex Ta-wue.
Ai-pi-kai tom mawh moeng Ta-wue heue.
43 Ta-wue hoik teei tix moeng ka Ya-si-nuang pui Yie-see-liex,
yam in nawh baox teei tix moeng ka Ai-pi-kai.
44 dYam in,
Sao-law hoik kieh kawn bun tix pa kaux Mi-cia pa hoik mawh moeng Ta-wue sivoe,
mex ka Pha-thix kawn simex La-yix pui Cia-ling heue.
