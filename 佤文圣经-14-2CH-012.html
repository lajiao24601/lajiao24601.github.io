Ai-cix yuh naing ka Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 14.25–28)
12
Law-paw-ang giex ka simiang mawh tix kaux cong,
nawh mai kawn pui tom tik vud ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex.
2-3 Yam mawh Law-paw-ang simiang ka daux phuan num,
Simiang Siyiex ciyang kix kheu lieh lheu kix lox.
Simiang Ai-cix pa kaux See-sha thung pui naing ling law tix rheng ra yieh lhang,
pui naing brug brung liah mheen,
khawm pui naing pa haoh hun,
khawm pui Li-pi-yax,
pui Su-ci,
mai pui naing Ku-seu,
hoek tix puing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
4 Nawh pon ka veng Yo-tax pa kaux cong,
mai hoek dex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
5 Cao jhai See-ma-ya hu ka simiang Law-paw-ang mai simiang pui Yo-tax khankix;
simiang in kix kheu to mox tix khaing See-sha mai hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
See-ma-ya ah nin ka kix:
“Simiang Siyiex koe lox krai ka pex ah nin:
‘Pex hoik tik vud ka aux,
mawh kheu nan,
yam in aux tik vud ka pex,
mai ap pex ka daux tawm taix See-sha heue.’ ”
6 Simiang meung mai simiang khankix hoik doh keud ka phi map pa yuh tix;
kix ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex mawh pa khaw caw heue.”
7 Simiang Siyiex yaox grawng pa tiiem rhawm rhi kix,
tom baox ah nin ka See-ma-ya:
“Mawh kheu hoik tiiem rhawm rhi kix mai doh keud ka phi map pa yuh tix,
aux ang lai yuh kix laux leeing.
Viang mawh nan,
yam hoek See-sha tix puing ka kix,
kix yaox ngie tix klawd ju khaing.
Aux ang ploi krax song rhawm tix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
8 Viang mawh nan,
See-sha sang pon kuang ka kix,
yam an kix tom tawng kah,
yuh kaing jhaw aux mai yuh kaing jhaw simiang ka piang haktiex hoik tang siyox yuh ka mawx heue.”
9 aSimiang See-sha puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hoik tud rhim can kraung khrai pa ngawh khawm uik ka nyiex buan mai daux nyiex haw,
khawm dieb tie naok khri pa yuh simiang Saw-law-meung khankix.
10 Simiang Law-paw-ang kheu sang naok tieb tie khri in kix,
tom yuh tieb tie naok tawng,
mai tawx ka cao kaing pa pau siviex ting nyiex haw mawh cao pa priex.
11 Yam hoek simiang ka nyiex buan miex bawg ang ah,
cao pau siviex tang an tom giex ka tieb tie naok in kix khaix an deeh deuh ot ka daux nyiex pau an.
12 Mawh kheu tiiem rhawm Law-paw-ang mai cu nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex deeh grai,
ang bax yuh nawh laux lak pak leeing;
krax ot pui Yo-tax kawx hoik pon krax pa yien yawg heue.
Krai lao grawng pa giex Law-paw-ang kaing
13 Law-paw-ang mawh simiang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai giex kaux ka sibrawm tix.
Yam mawh nawh simiang ju nawh koe tipon tix num,
nawh giex kaing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung kao aliah num.
Yie-lu-sa-leung mawh pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex khaing haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
son sang kieh mawh veng dee krub ka nawh.
Miex Law-paw-ang mawh pui Ya-meung pa kaux Na-ma.
14 Law-paw-ang yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig,
mawh kheu nawh ang sawg ka alo Simiang Siyiex heue.
15 Kaing yuh Law-paw-ang mai ru ceu cad pui nyiex nawh hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Cao Jhai See-ma-ya mai ka daux Lai Been Cao Pa Chix Yaox Sivoe Yi-to.
Si nax Law-paw-ang mai Yie-law-paw-ang lih koe krax naing jheeng jheeng.
16 Yam yum Law-paw-ang kix si paung nawh ka daux veng Ta-wue ka daux tiag si meeig pui nyiex simiang.
Kawn simex nawh Ya-pi-yax plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
