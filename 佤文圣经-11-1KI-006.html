Saw-law-meung sum nyiex buan
6
Khaix hoik lih pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Ai-cix pon yieh tix daix num,
yam yuh Saw-law-meung simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux pon num khix ra,
pa mawh khix Si-fu,
Saw-law-meung jauh tix sum ka nyiex buan.
2 Ka daux nyiex buan plak lang koe tix nga aliah kongjhee,
plak vieh koe dim kongjhee,
plak lhaong koe kao loe kongjhee plak.
3 Grah dee laig ka diang siviex been raux koe pon kongjhee plak,
vieh koe dim kongjhee,
been vieh khaw mai nyiex pa singax singeh yuh nan.
4 Ka piang ding nyiex buan kieh siviex daux doh koe kah;
siviex daux doh an plak praix iak,
plak daux ting.
5 Dee dex ka ding plak praix ka praok nyiex buan mai ka plak khaix,
sum ka nyiex loe jan,
tix jan plak lhaong koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing.
6 Daux nyiex pa ot greem paox tix jan,
been vieh koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing;
daux nyiex jan sigreung an krax vieh koe ra kongjhee mai aliah kongchuing;
daux nyiex jan plak piang paox plak vieh koe loe kongjhee mai tix kongchuing.
Ding nyiex buan ku jan hoik kieh rhi khaing jan plak greem;
yuh nin,
nyiex tom chix sum tix ot piang ding,
mai ang lai siao sau lat khaox ot daux ding heue.
7 Simaox pa siao ka nyiex buan hoik mawh pa saik khaing dee teei simaox,
mawh kheu nan,
yam sum ka nyiex buan mai ang lai mhong lox luah daix,
vaig moe,
mai lox luah kraung rhiem tang ceu khankix heue.
8 Ka daux laig ka diang siviex jan nyiex pa ot greem paox tix jana ot ka praok plak seh nyiex buan,
hoik kieh koe bawng pa phlat hoek ka jan ra mai jan loe.
9 Simiang Saw-law-meung hoik hoik sum ka nyiex buan.
Nawh siao ka khaox si oe bhawng kieh mawh khaox lat mai phia tawm piang daux nyiex.
10 Daux nyiex ka daux loe jan an,
ku jan krax lhaong koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing,
hoik sum tix dex ka ding nyiex buan plak praix,
mai siao nang khaox lat pa hoik yuh ka khaox si oe bhawng,
kieh kleuk tix ot ka piang ding heue.
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Saw-law-meung:
12 “Phan maix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux mai gum lox ping nyap aux,
aux tag sang hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik ah aux ka keeing maix Ta-wue.
13 Aux tag sang ot ka daux nyiex buan pa hoik sum maix in,
ka daux pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex,
aux ang tik vud ka kix leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
14 Yuh nin,
Saw-law-meung hoik sum ka nyiex buan mhawm mhawm heue.
Khre kraung pa ot ka daux nyiex buan
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 3.8–14)
15 Ding nyiex buan plak daux,
jauh ka piang tiex tom hoek ka phia pa tom ot plak piang,
hoik tawk tix ka phia khaox si oe bhawng khawm uik;
piang tiex hoik tawm tix ka khaox gix.
16 cKoe ka daux lawg nyiex tix mu kok mawh dee pa kied singax singeh,
sum tix ot ka daux nyiex buan plak khaix,
krax lang koe dim kongjhee;
jauh ka piang tiex tom hoek ka phia pa tom ot plak piangb,
mai hoik khan tang ot ka khaox si oe bhawng.
17 Nyiex ting krax lang koe kao daix kongjhee.
18 Phia khaox si oe bhawng hoik pawd riang si ngien mai riang tai ot kah.
Ka daux nyiex buan khawm uik hoik kawb ka khaox si oe bhawng,
son ang simaox guh ding nyiex buan sang bhlien tix lih heue.
19 Nawh sum daux nyiex tix lawg ot ka plak khaix nyiex buan;
teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex deuh tix ot dee an.
20 Daux nyiex in krax lang koe dim kongjhee,
krax vieh koe dim kongjhee,
krax lhaong koe dim kongjhee,
mai hoik plied khri mhawm ot kah.
Khu bhawng dee tan hoik siao khaox si oe bhawng om kah.
21 Ka daux nyiex buan plak daux hoik plied khri ot kah,
koe maox khri vhak tix ot ka diang siviex daux laig ka nyiex an;
diang siviex in kawx hoik plied khri ot kah.
22 dKa daux nyiex buan plak daux khawm uik hoik plied khri ot kah,
awm khu bhawng dee an ka daux dee pa kied singax singeh yuh nan heue.
23 eNawh teei riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu pa yuh ka khaox sime deuh ot ka dee pa kied singax singeh,
tix mu riang an krax lhaong koe pon kongjhee mai pon kongchuing.
24-26 Been ting riang Ci-lu-paw an ra mu mai greeh grang pak peung khaw paox,
koe preeik ra mu,
tix mu preeik krax lang koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing.
Mawh kheu nan,
jauh ka toh preeik plak in tom hoek ka toh preeik plak an,
koe pon kongjhee mai pon kongchuing.
27 Kiex hoik dia tix deuh ot ka dee pa kied singax singeh;
yuh nin,
kiex tom nih preeik tix kleuk paox tix si nax nyiex ting,
mai preeik an kiex tang mu tom cux ka ding nyiex khawm ra plak.
28 Riang Ci-lu-paw in ra mu hoik om khri kah.
29 Ding nyiex ting mai ding daux nyiex hoik pawd riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu,
riang khaox jhie yaong,
mai riang tai ot kah.
30 Piang tiex ka daux nyiex kawx hoik tawm plied ka khri heue.
31 Ka diang siviex dee laig ka dee pa kied singax singeh,
koe siviex ra plah pa hoik yuh ka khaox sime.
Bang siviex mawh pa via tix lawm,
32 Ka piang siviex hoik pawd ka riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu,
riang khaox jhie yaong,
mai riang tai ot kah;
siviex in mai riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu khawm riang khaox jhie yaong hoik om ka khri.
33 Dee laig ka daux nyiex ting,
mawh pang siviex pa lang pa yuh ka khaox sime.
34 Siviex ra plah pa nu niep tix in,
hoik mawh pa yuh ka khaox gix,
35 ka piang hoik pawd riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu,
riang khaox jhie yaong,
mai riang tai ot kah;
in kix hoik mawh pa om ka khri heue.
36 Nawh yuh brawk koe ka daux nyiex buan ka plak sivoe,
ding khawm pon daog mawh loe tia simaox khan lat khaox si oe bhawng ot si nax tix tia heue.
37 Jaong nyiex buan mawh pa yuh yam mawh Saw-law-meung simiang daux pon num khix ra,
pa mawh khix Si-fu.
38 Yam mawh Saw-law-meung simiang ka daux kao tix num khix daix,
pa mawh khix pu-leux,
nyiex buan hoik bre tix yuh awm greeh grang pak peung pa keud kix yuh nan.
Saw-law-meung siao aliah num tom hoik sum ka nyiex buan heue.
