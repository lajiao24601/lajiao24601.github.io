Gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Fe-ni-ci
23
aPlak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Thai-eux.
Pui pa yuh kaing daux reu daux rawm sigrit sigra khankix eue,
yiiem heue,
diang praok rawm sigrit sigra Thai-eux cie yaong pex hoik laux leeing,
nyiex ma pex mai diang praok rawm sigrit sigra kawx hoik laux leeing.
Yam deeh reu cie pex khaing Ci-thix,
tag sang mhong ka lox in.
2 Pui pa paung ot hawt rawm,
pa yuh lai gee ka Si-toeng khankix eue,
yiiem grai heue.
3 Pex tiang ka rawm sigrit sigra hu yuh lai gee,
mai teei to ma to koi pa pon pex khaing Ai-cix coh ka meung pui ku meung heue.
4 Veng Si-toeng eue,
maix hoik pon krax kaig krax tua;
rawm sigrit sigra pa raux ang tawng ka maix,
mai ah nin:
“Aux ang nyang sax eei ka kawn nyawm tix bawg kawx,
ang nyang sax keh eei ka kawn simex kawn bun tix bawg kawx heue.”
5 Pie pui Ai-cix yam hoik mhong kix ka grawng pa laux leeing Thai-eux,
mai kix kawx hoik laux rhawm khawm uik heue.
6 Pui Fe-ni-ci eue,
yiiem heue,
pex kawn sang simie tix to hu mox tix ka Tha-see heue!
7 In hoik mawh veng Thai-eux pa koe krax haok huan,
mai veng pa dawg koe tom dix dix lie?
Hoik mawh veng pa tawx pui hu ka meung tang meung son sang mawh pa yuh kaing siliex cawng lie?
8 Thai-eux mawh ngie veng ting tix mu khaing;
pui pa yuh lai gee hoik koe min ka daux.
Mawh pui mawx pa kieh veng in yaox krax pa laux yuh nin lie?
9 In mawh krax pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing;
nawh tag sang kieh krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi kix naox tix,
mai kieh pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi khankix tieh tix mawh pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi heue.
10 Pui Tha-see pa yuh siliex cawng eue,
hu yuh kaing kaung ma heue!
Ang lai koe pui pa hoek tix kia ngien ka pex.
11 Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx taix tix tiang ka rawm sigrit sigra,
hoik lauh ka meung pui haktiex pa koe plak an.
Nawh tawx lox kieh pui yuh ka Fe-ni-ci nyiex dee yuh pui laih lai laux leeing.
12 Veng Si-toeng eue,
ka daux bawg yam singaix cie maix hoik luan.
Pui cie maix kham krax gut kheem,
viang maix hoik to mox tix hoek ka klawn tiex Ci-thix kawx,
mai kawn ca ang pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.
(
13 Cub an pa viex to sat hoek tix bleet ka Thai-eux,
ang mawh pui Pa-pi-loeng,
ang mawh pui Ya-sux.
Pui Pa-pi-loeng sum ka nyiex gawng mu pa puing veng,
mai pon yieh ka tab guh veng Thai-eux,
kieh veng an tieh tix lih mawh haktiex dee riem heue.)
14 Pui pa chix rawm ka rawm sigrit sigra khankix eue,
yiiem heue,
veng pa hoik een pex rhawm tix hoik laux leeing heue.
15 Ka daux bawg yam singaix pa sang piawm pui ka veng Thai-eux dex sang hoek;
pui tag sang piawm ka veng an atigliah num,
mai hoik jiet mawh pa sidu mai num Vawng tix kaux.
Khaix hoik luan atigliah num,
Thai-eux tag sang awm sibun coh kaux pa hoik tiiem pui koe daux lai ah nin:
16 Teei ka ting pa jung lauh maix,
mai viex lai ling ka daux veng;
maix pa mawh sibun coh kaux pa hoik piawm pui kah,
tauh ka ting cie maix,
mai rhax ka lox rhax heue!
Pa mawh simex paox muh maix khankix tee rien tix sang chix deeh hoek heue.
17 Khaix hoik luan ka daux atigliah num,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pui deeh hoek tix yuh laih lai ka Thai-eux nyiex dee yuh pui lai gee.
Meung ka piang haktiex tom nie tag sang liag tix lo ka reu pa koe dee an.
18 Dee an tag sang teei rao pa pon tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex,
ang lai baox mox tix deuh ka mau khri pa koe tix,
mai teei tix tawx mau ka pui pa krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai kieh kix yaox tix kub ih kub nyaux heue.
