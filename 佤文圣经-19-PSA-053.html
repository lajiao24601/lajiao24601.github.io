Krax pa caox pa caig kawn pui
(Lai Yaog Yaw 14)
53
aPui pa cau nyhau tang ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
Siyiex ang koe.
Kix hoik caox caig khawm uik,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa seeing pa brai;
pie pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm ang koe tix kaux kawx heue.
 
2 Siyiex kok jhak kawn pui piang haktiex khaing rao ma,
mai jhak tix yaox koe ang koe pui pa lhak lhiao,
koe ang koe pui pa sawg ka nawh.
3 Viang mawh nan,
pui ku kaux hoik tieh vheen ka nawh khawm uik;
ku kaux ku pui hoik laux leeing awm paox tix.
Ang koe pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm,
pie tix kaux kawx ang koe heue.
 
4 Siyiex chawk ah nin:
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig ang tawng lie?
Khex hoik daox aw kix khawm uik lie?
Kix bleet ih ka pui cie aux;
kix ang tawng tix khiox khaw ka aux heue.
 
5 Kheu nan,
kix tag sang lhat graung si yaung,
kham ka krax pa lhat graung pa ang kix nyang sax yaox tix bawg kawx;
mawh kheu Siyiex tag sang pauh ka pui pa koeng ka pui cie tix.
Nawh tik vud ka kix,
mai kieh kix laux rhawm som nai mai kaig tua heue.
 
6 Kieh cao pa taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex lih hoek khaing Shi-ang heue!
Yam an,
Siyiex tag sang kieh pui cie nawh deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma tix.
Kawn praix Ya-keux tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
pui hun Yi-seu-liex tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
