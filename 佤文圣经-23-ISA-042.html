Siliex Simiang Siyiex
42
abSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
Jhak heue,
siliex aux,
aux joh jhan ka nawh;
aux laug ka nawh,
muh ka nawh.
Aux kieh khuan aux naog ka nawh;
nawh tag sang viex pa rauh pa see hoek ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik.
2 Nawh ang kok yo,
mai ang riag yo;
nawh kawx ang krai lox tom ting ka daux laih daux cau.
3 Gawng priem pa graik,
nawh ang raig pot;
ngu pa sang yeet,
nawh ang phlu yeet.
Nawh tag sang viex krax rauh see pa kaux cong tom rheeng hoek tawx ka kawn pui.
4 Nawh ang laux rhawm mai ang voi rhawm;
nawh tag sang yah tix tawx krax rauh see ka piang haktiex;
pui pa ot ka klawn tiex khankix een rhawm tix ka jhawm prawng nawh heue.
 
5 cSiyiex phan yuh ka rao ma,
mai nih ka rao ma;
nawh phan yuh ka haktiex mai ka ceu pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
nawh tawx ju ka kawn pui.
Simiang Siyiex krai pao ka siliex tix yuh nin:
6 dAux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex kok ka maix,
tawx riang rao ka maix,
kieh maix mawh cao pa yuh krax rauh see ka piang haktiex.
Aux sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kawn pui haktiex ka daux maix;
aux sang kieh maix lih mawh pa riang ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik.
7 Maix tag sang paoh ka ngai pui dug,
thung pui pa laung pui lih khaing daux thawng,
kieh pui pa laung ot daux thawng pa faik pon yaox ka pa riang heue.
 
8 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
Yie-heu-hua mawh ceu kaux aux heue.
Aux ang kieh siyiex tang kaux pon yaox ka pa riang min plak ngai tix,
ang kieh riang rao pa krub pui tix ceu kawx pon krax pa yaog yaw.
9 Kaing pa dha aux tix krai hoik lih tix mawh.
Yam in aux sang krai kaing pa khraox tix blah ka pex,
sivoe sang lih kaing tix mawh,
mai ran krai ka pex sivoe heue.
Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
10 Tag caw tix rhax lox rhax pa khraox tix ceu ka Simiang Siyiex;
piang haktiex khawm uik tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue!
Rawm sigrit sigra mai can ceu pa koe ka daux tag caw tix yaog yaw ka nawh;
klawn tiex mai pui pa ot daux khankix tag caw tix yaog yaw ka nawh heue!
11 Pak mhaik mai veng pa koe ka daux tag caw tix rhax lai pa ting si num;
pui haktiex Ci-tax tag caw tix yaog yaw ka nawh;
pui pa paung ot ka Si-la tag caw tix gaux rhawm dieh taix,
ploi lox tix hu ka piang choek gawng.
12 Kawn pui pa ot ka klawn tiex tag yaog yaw ka min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex;
kix tag yaog yaw ka nawh.
13 Simiang Siyiex awm pui naing pa kua pa hu puing ka naing;
nawh hoik rien khre ka kraung naing,
mai rien tix sang hu puing naing.
Nawh kok yo ka lox pa ting si num,
preeng tix hu sivoe ngai,
kieh pui pa koeng yaox ka grawng pa ting riang tix heue.
Siyiex cu tix teem coi ka pui cie tix
14 Siyiex ah nin:
Aux hoik yien yawg tom rheeng,
ang simi ka pui cie tix.
Yam in aux sang yaog tix yuh lih,
kok yo ka lox pa ting si num awm bun pa saux yam sang keh yuh nan.
15 Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka gawng ting gawng iak,
kieh khaox ox kroh rham.
Aux tag sang kieh rawm klawng lih mawh pak mhaik,e
kieh rawm dung kroh heue.
 
16 Aux tag sang thung pui pa dug ngai lawng krax,
lawng ka krax pa ang tix tawng;
aux tag sang kieh pa faik kix tieh tix lih mawh pa riang,
kieh krax iak pa khluk khlik tieh tix lih mawh krax ting pa vieh va.
Aux tag sang yuh kaing in lih tix mawh,
mai ang doh pie lhien.
17 Pui pa yum rhawm ka siyiex pa pie lhien khankix tag sang sum nai;
pui pa een rhawm tix ka riang rao pa krub pui tag sang pon krax kaig krax tua heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex ang rieb ka krax pa sidiex
18 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
pui pa lheet yhaok eue,
nghiet heue!
Pui pa dug ngai eue,
jhak heue!
19 Ka piang haktiex pui mawx kawn lheu dug ngai khaing siliex aux lie?
Pui mawx kawn lheu lheet yhaok khaing kawn cau pa ploi aux hu lie?
20 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
viang maix hoik yaox ka ku ceu tom nie,
mai maix ang bax pon priex ot daux rhawm tix;
viang maix hoik mhong ka ku ceu tom nie,
mai maix ang bax chix pon tix tawng sidawng kah.
21 Simiang Siyiex sang yah tix tawx krax pa rauh pa see khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix;
sang kieh lox tat jhawm prawng ting haok huan.
22 Khaix an,
pui cie nawh kham gee ka krax gut krax kheem;
pui laung kix ot daux tiex,
laung kix ka daux thawng.
Pui tud rhim ka kix,
mai dug dig ka kix,
ang koe pui pa hoek tix taoh kix heue.
 
23 Ka daux pex koe pui mawx pa sang nghiet ka lox in lie?
Jauh yam in pex sang priex rhawm rhi tix nghiet lie?
24 Mawh pui mawx pa ap tix tawx Yi-seu-liex ka pui con pui cam lie?
Mawh pui mawx pa ap tix tawx Ya-keux ka pui tud pui rhim lie?
Mawh cao Simiang Siyiex,
kheu hoik phit lud ex ka nawh.
Krax pa yuh ex ang khaw mai diag cie nawh;
ex ang bax priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom song rhawm song rhi ka ex,
kieh ex yaox krax naing pa kha rhawm.
Proe ngu lai lawm ka ex,
mai ex ca kawn ang bax gau tix tawng ka lox sidiex;
ngu ting gleeing ka ex,
mai ex ca kawn kieh tix pu rhawm pu rhi heue.
