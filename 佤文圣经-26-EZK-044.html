Pa siao ka siviex plak lih singaix
44
Pui an thung aux deeh hoek ka siviex plak lih singaix ka plak praix brawk nyiex buan.
Siviex in hoik nyeep tix.
2 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Siviex in tag caw nyeep tix leeing ju,
mai ang pon tix paoh.
Pui tix kaux kawx ang pon tix siao ka siviex in,
mawh kheu aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik laig ka daux siviex in.
Siviex in tag sang nyeep tix leeing ju.
3 Phan kawn caw tix mawh simiang,
nawh chix tix hoek ka dee in,
mai ih khao mun ka sivoe ngai aux.
Nawh tag caw tix laig lih ka siviex grah pa koe ka plak praix brawk heue.”
Been pa tat tix deuh yam laig daux nyiex buan
4 Khaix an,
pui an thung aux hu ka plak siviex plak laong,
mai hoek plak sivoe ngai nyiex buan.
Aux jhak kah,
mai yaox ka min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex hoik naog ka daux nyiex buan nawh;
aux tom kub tix piang tiex.
5 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
tag caw tix bre rhawm tix ka ceu pa hoik yaox pa hoik mhong maix khawm uik.
Aux tag sang krai lox tat jhawm prawng nyiex buan ka maix.
Maix tag caw kia tix yaox pui mawx pa chix tix laig ka daux nyiex buan,
pui mawx pa ang chix tix laig ka daux nyiex buan heue.
6 “Maix tag caw tix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa lieh lox khankix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
ang lai baox gun yeung ka can kaing pa caox pa caig pa hoik yuh kix khawm uik.
7 Yam tan kix nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai bee vaig ka aux,
kix lai kieh pui tang ceu tang cad khankix pa ang bax Pat-hak tix,
pa ang nghiet ka lox aux khankix laig daux nyiex buan,
kheu nan mai hoik kieh nyiex buan aux seeing brai.
Pui cie aux hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig in kix khawm uik,
hoik tik vud ka sijax mukheu aux.
8 To kaux kix ang yuh ka been pa singax ka daux nyiex buan aux,
viang mawh nan,
tieh tix kieh pui tang ceu mawh cao kuang heue.
9 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai pao yuh nin:
pui pa ang bax Pat-hak tix,
pui tang ceu pa ang nghiet ka aux,
ang pon tix kieh laig daux nyiex buan pa singax aux,
pie pui tang ceu pa paung ot ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex kawx ang pon tix kieh laig heue.”
Pui Li-we pa ang cu mawh cao giex kaing li
10 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Koe pui Li-we tix blah khankix hawt khaix pui Yi-seu-liex tik vud ka aux hu krub ka riang rao,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
11 Kix chix tix mawh cao pa pau ka siviex nyiex buan aux,
mai yuh kaing nyiex buan.
Kix chix tix mawh cao pa pauh ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Khawng-brie tang ceu son pui hun khankix,
mai lai loh tix yuh kaing son pui hun.
12 Viang mawh nan,
kix hoik sax mawh pa thung pui Yi-seu-liex krub ka riang rao mai hoik kieh pui hun yuh phi map,
mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu,
kix tag sang pon krax ciyang tete.
13 Kix ang pon tix mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux,
mai ang pon tix hoek dex ka kraung khrai pa singax aux,
phan ang mawh nan laig ka daux nyiex pa kied singax aux.
In hoik mawh krax ciyang pa khaw kix tix kham kheu hoik yuh kix kaing pa caox pa caig an kix.
14 Aux tag sang ploi kix mawh cao pa yuh kaing iak kaing lied ka daux nyiex buan heue.”
Cao giex kaing li
15 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Yam hoik tik vud pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux khankix ka aux,
ceu pui Li-we,
cao giex kaing li doeh ceu cad Sa-tu plud tix yuh kaing krub ka aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix ka daux nyiex buan.
Mawh kheu nan,
yam in mawh ngie kix pa pon chix tix hoek dex ka aux,
yuh kaing krub ka aux khaing.
Kix tag sang mawh cao pa tan ka bee mai nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka plak sivoe ngai aux.
16 Mawh ngie kix pa chix tix laig daux nyiex buan,
yuh kaing pa krub ka khu dee tan aux,
mawh cao pa thung pui krub ka nyiex buan.
17 aYam laig kix ka diang siviex plak daux brawk,
tag caw tix ceep kraung man keei sum.
Yam ot kix ka plak praix brawk ang ah,
yam yuh kaing pa pau nyiex buan ang ah,
ang pon tix ceep kraung hauk yung.
18 Kix tag caw tix ceep ka lawk kaing pa yuh ka man keei sum,
ceep ka khlax man keei sum,
viang mawh nan,
ang mad ka maox rawd nyoi,
son ang rawm lauh sang lih.
19 cSivoe sang hoek kix ka plak praix brawk dex dawt ka pui hun,
kix tag caw tix ran peeik ka kraung ceep yam pau tix ka nyiex buan,
mai deuh ot ka nyiex lawg ka daux nyiex buan,
ceep ka kraung ceep tang ceu,
son ang kraung pa singax sang kieh pui mat lheet heue.b
20 d“Caox giex kaing li ang pon tix khlao kiang tix ghle,
phan ang mawh nan,
vang ka hauk kaing tix lang.
Hauk kaing tag caw tix ghrip khaw yuh nan.
21 eSivoe sang laig cao giex kaing li ka plak daux brawk,
ang pon nyaux ka plai tix ceu kawx.
22 fCao giex kaing li ang pon tix moeng ka miex mai,
phan ang mawh nan,
bun pa hoik tang hu khankix.
Cao giex kaing li chix tix moeng ka bun krih Yi-seu-liex,
phan ang mawh nan ka miex mai paox pie pa yuh tix kaing krub tau heue.
23 g“Cao giex kaing lih tag caw tix sidiex gaux pui cie aux guah tix tawng ka ceu pa singax singeh,
khawm ceu pa singax singeh mai pa ang singax singeh ka daux been.
24 Cao giex kaing li tag caw tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux prawng simah khankix.
Kix tag caw tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
mai priex tix hawt ka poi been li,
priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah kieh mawh singaix pa singax heue.
25 h“Lheu khaing to kaux pa yum miex keeing tix,
kawn simex kawn bun,
pux aik tix,
mai pux o tix pa ang bax koe mex khankix,
cao giex kaing li ang pon tix theeh ka to kaux yum pui tang kaux,
kieh to kaux tix ang singax ka daux been.
26 Khaix hoik singax singeh nawh,
tag caw tix krox hoek aliah singaix
27 tom taug chix tix laig plak daux brawk nyiex buan,
mai tan lu Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map son tix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
28 i“Sin ngao pa guah cao giex kaing li pa mawh ara cao giex kaing li.
Kix ang pon tix koe sin ngao ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
Aux hoik mawh pa lo kix khawm uik.
29 jKrax-tan Nghox-koi,
Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map,
mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud hoik tawx mawh priex preem cie cao giex kaing li khawm uik.
Can ceu pa tan lu pui Yi-seu-liex ka aux khawm uik tag tawx ka kix.
30 To ma to koi pa liang tix pon ku num,
mai khawng tan pa blawk tix tan lu ka aux khankix,
tag caw tix tawx mawh cie cao giex kaing li khawm uik.
Yam tong kawn pui ka khao mun,
tag caw tix tawx khliep pa jauh ka cao giex kaing li,
yuh nin aux tom taug tawx buan son ka nyiex ma kix.
31 kCao giex kaing li ang pon tix ih ka ceu pa lawg yum,
phan ang mawh nan,
sim pa kiied to sat yum mai khawng brie heue.”
