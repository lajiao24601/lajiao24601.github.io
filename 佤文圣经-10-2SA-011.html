Ta-wue mai Pa-see-pa
11
aKa daux num ra yam rung praix,
jiet mawh yam hu puing simiang khankix ka naing,
Ta-wue ploi Yaw-ya thung puing naing hu tau mai pui naing Yi-seu-liex.
Kix pon pex ka pui Ya-meung,
mai pon teei ka veng La-pa.
Viang mawh nan,
to kaux Ta-wue kawn ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
2 Koe tix singaix,
yam dex sang faik boi praix,
Ta-wue kaoh khaing it tix,
mai lai ling tix hu ka piang grah nyiex haw ot tix,
nawh yaox ka bun tix kaux pa heem tix.
Bun an kied sidaing mhawm grang.
3 Ta-wue ploi pui hu si kaw tix yaox mawh nawh bun mawx,
mai tawng nawh kaux Pa-see-pa,
mawh kawn bun Yi-liien,
moeng Wu-li-yax pui Heux.
4 Ta-wue ploi kawn cau tix hu kok ka nawh.
Kix hu teei ka Pa-see-pa hoek,
Ta-wue tom phit tau mai nawh.
(Yam an Pa-see-pa taug khraox hit khix lih nham seeing bun.)
Khaix an,
Pa-see-pa deeh ing ka nyiex tix.
5 Ang koe miex glang tix,
Pa-see-pa hoik yaox koe kaux tix,
nawh tom ploi pui hu tawx lox ka Ta-wue,
mai krai hoik koe kaux tix heue.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ploi pui hu krai ah nin ka Yaw-ya:
“Maix nhie pui Heux pa kaux Wu-li-yax hoek ka dee ot aux tin.”
Yaw-ya tom nhie Wu-li-yax hu ka Ta-wue.
7 Wu-li-yax hoek,
Ta-wue chawk Yaw-ya mai pui naing mhawm ot ang mhawm ot,
grawng krax naing yuh ka mawx lie?
8 Khaix an ah nin ka Wu-li-yax:
“Maix ing laux lee tix ka nyiex ha!”
Khaix hoik ing Wu-li-yax,
Ta-wue ploi pui hu tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka nyiex nawh.
9 Viang mawh nan,
Wu-li-yax ang bax ing ka nyiex.
Nawh it ka diang siviex nyiex haw tau mai cao pa pau ka simiang.
10 Ta-wue mhong grawng pa ang Wu-li-yax bax ing ka nyiex,
tom chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix hoik lih hu khaing nyiex kied rheeng,
yam in hoik deeh hoek,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang ing ka nyiex tix lie?”
11 Wu-li-yax pawk lox ah nin:
“Pui naing pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui naing Yo-tax kawn ot ka daux bang naing,
mai teug sijax mukheu kawx kawn ot tan,
Yaw-ya simiang naing mai pui naing cie nawh khawm uik,
hoik sum bang ot ka daux tiag daux tu,
aux sang chix ing ih nyaux ka nyiex,
mai it tau mai moeng tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux tiex tu ka ju maix,
aux ang doh yuh kaing pa yuh nin heue!”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
daux in singaix maix baox laux lee tix tin,
pa sax aux lawk tawx maix ing.”
Ka daux singaix an,
Wu-li-yax tom laux lee tix ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
13 Ka daux ra singaix,
Ta-wue kok nawh hoek ih nyaux tau mai tix,
mai kieh nawh yoek.
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux bo an,
Wu-li-yax ca kawn ang ing ka nyiex tix.
Nawh hu ka nyiex haw dee ot cao pa pau khankix,
mai it ka piang diehb viex tix heue.
14 Ka daux ra singaix pon ngawb cao,
Ta-wue tiiem lai tix pheen tawx ka Yaw-ya,
mai nhie Wu-li-yax viex hu.
15 Lox lai ah nin:
“Tawx Wu-li-yax hu puing naing ka dee blawn nu sivoe paox,
khaix an pex dee deeh,
mai kieh nawh yum ka daux naing heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
yam puing Yaw-ya ka veng,
nawh ploi Wu-li-yax hu ka dee kuan pui koeng dee blawn nu khankix.
17 Pui koeng kaoh khaing daux veng,
puing ka pui naing Yaw-ya.
Cao kaing pui naing Ta-wue tix blah khankix hoik yum.
Pui Heux pa kaux Wu-li-yax kawx hoik yum heue.
18 Khaix an,
Yaw-ya hoek krai grawng pa hoik puing kix naing ka Ta-wue.
19 Yaw-ya krai ah nin ka kawn cau tix:
“Khaix hoik krai maix simiang mhong grawng naing pa hoik puing ex bawg in,
20 mai nawh tee rien tix song rhawm ne raw ah nin:
‘Yuh pa tix pex lai hoek puing dex ka guh veng pui koeng lie?
Khex ang pex tawng pui koeng sang kok puing pex ka te khaing guh veng lie?
21 cPex hoik piawm ka grawng pa yum kawn simex Ci-tiien pa kaux Ya-pi-mi-leux lie?
Nawh hoik yum ot ka daux Thi-pe-see,
hoik mawh bun tix kaux pa nyheen simaox laing lih cawd khaing guh veng,
mai dheem nawh yum.
Yuh ka mawx pex lai puing dex ka guh veng lie?’
Phan simiang chawk lox in kix,
maix tom krai nawh mhong ah nin:
‘Cao kaing naing simiang kawx pa kaux Wu-li-yax hoik yum nang heue.’ ”
22 Kawn cau hoek ka simiang Ta-wue dee an,
mai hawt tix krai awm lox pa tawm Yaw-ya yuh nan,
23 ah nin:
“Pui koeng lheu ting riang khaing yix.
Kix preeng tix lih khaing daux veng,
mai puing ka yix hawt vung viang veng.
Yix pheet pui koeng deeh ka diang siviex veng.
24 Kix tom kok puing yix ka te khaing guh veng.
Te tom krox ka simiang naing Vawng tix blah mai yum heue.
Pui Heux pa mawh Wu-li-yax kawx yum heue.”
25 Ta-wue ah nin ka kawn cau:
“Maix deeh ing khu rhawm Yaw-ya,
paw cu nawh lai laux rhawm,
mawh kheu ka daux naing,
pui mawx sang yum pui mawx sang im,
ang ex tawng tix kaux kawx.
Kieh nawh kaux rhawm,
nhie nawh cox bre rhawm tix mhawm mhawm,
son sang teei riang rao tix puing ka veng,
mai kieh laux leeing heue.”
26 Moeng Wu-li-yax pa kaux Pa-see-pa mhong grawng hoik yum mex tix,
nawh tom yiiem son mex tix.
27 Khaix hoik luan singaix bawg yam laux rhawm kix,
Ta-wue teei Pa-see-pa hoek ot ka daux nyiex haw tix,
kieh nawh mawh moeng tix,
mai nawh keh simiang cie kawn simex tix kaux.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex kied ang khaw rhawm ka kaing pa yuh Ta-wue in heue.
