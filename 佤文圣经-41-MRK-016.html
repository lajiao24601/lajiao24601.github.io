Ye-sux kaoh im
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.1–8; Lai Lu-cia 14.1–12; Lai Yaw-hang 20.1–10)
16
Khaix hoik luan singaix tuah,
Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la mai Ma-li-yax pa mawh miex Ya-keux,
mai Sa-law-mi liag ka sidah bhawng,
sang hu kraix ka to kaux yum Ye-sux.
2 Singaix tuah pon ngawb cao cao yam jauh singaix tix lih,
kix hu ka si meeig.
3-4 Ka daux krax,
kix keud daux rhawm tix ah nin:
“Pui mawx sang teem ex pu plie simaox yoi khaing moing si meeig lie?”
(Mawh kheu simaox an kied ting).
Viang mawh nan,
kix yaog kaing tix jhak,
simaox an hoik pu plie tix yoi hu.
5 Kix laig daux si meeig,
mai yaox pui nhum ju pa ngawm ka plak dawm,
to kaux nawh ceep ka kraung dha paing;
kix hoik kied lhat khawm uik heue.
6 Pui nhum ju an ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat graung;
aux hoik tawng sawg pex ka Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux,
kaw pa hoik tawk ka piang khaox lhak an.
Nawh ang ot dee in,
nawh hoik kaoh im!
Jhak heue,
in mawh dee deuh kix nawh heue.
7 aPex nied tix hu krai ka pui gau nawh khankix,
nie mawh Pi-teux,
mai ah nin:
‘Nawh tag sang ran hoek sivoe pex ka Cia-li-lix,
ka dee an,
pex tag sang yaox ka nawh,
awm hoik krai nawh ka pex sivoe an heue.’ ”
8 Kix lhat nang daox deng nang,
mai nied tix to lih khaing daux si meeig,
sau riang tix to hu.
Kix ang bax krai grawng kaing in ka pui tix kaux kawx,
mawh kheu lhat kix heue.
 
Koe phuk lai pa prim tix blah baox ka pa koe plak greem·
Ye-sux jhi Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la yaox tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.9–10; Lai Yaw-hang 20.11–18)
[
9 Ka daux singaix tuah pon ngawb cao,
khaix hoik kaoh im Ye-sux,
Ye-sux ran jhi Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la yaox tix (Ye-sux hoik sax grheu phi aliah mu lih khaing to kaux nawh sivoe an.)
10 Nawh hu krai ka pui an kix pa hawt ka Ye-sux;
kix kawn laux rhawm yiiem.
11 Yam mhong kix ka lox pa krai hoik kaoh im Ye-sux mai pa hoik yaox Ma-li-yax pa krai nawh in kix,
mai kix ang yum rhawm ka heue.
Jhi pui gau ra kaux yaox ka tix
(Lai Lu-cia 24.13–35)
12 Khaix an Ye-sux teei pak peung tang ceu jhi to kaux tix ka pui gau ra kaux pa hu ka plak haktiex nyiex yaong tix.
13 Kiex ra kaux hoek tix krai ka pui gau tang kaux,
kix kawn ang bax yum rhawm ka heue.
Jhi kawn cau kao tix kaux yaox tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.16–20; Lai Lu-cia 24.36–49; Lai Yaw-hang 20.19–23; Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 1.6–8)
14 Dee nghoik Ye-sux jhi kawn cau kao tix kaux yaox ka tix yam kawn sawn kix.
Nawh ne raw ka kix;
mawh kheu cud krax yum rhawm kix mai baox kiien rhawm,
ang yum rhawm ka lox pa krai pui pa hoik yaox grawng pa kaoh im nawh.
15 bNawh ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw tix hu ka piang haktiex ku dauh,
mai sidiex gum lox buan ka kawn pui khawm uik.
16 Pui pa yum rhawm mai pa rieb tix cub com,
tag sang pon krax taoh;
pui pa ang yum rhawm tag sang prawng ca ka phi map nawh.
17 Pui pa yum rhawm koe riang rao tix yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am ceu in kix:
kix tag sang grheu phi lih khaing daux ceu kaux aux,
mai si kah ka lox daux khuan pa khraox,
18 giex si uing ka taix tix,
nyaux ka ceu pa koe cai kawx ang pon kieh kix laux mat,
kix deuh taix tix piang kaing pui pa koe jhawm saux,
jhawm saux pui an taug moi heue.”
Rieb Ye-sux haok ka rao ma
(Lai Lu-cia 24.50–53; Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 1.9–11)
19 cKhaix hoik krai Simiang Ye-sux lox in kix ka kix,
mai rieb nawh haok ka rao ma,
nawh ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex.
20 Pui gau lih hu,
sidiex ka gum lox buan ku dauh ku dee;
Simiang Ye-sux yuh kaing tau mai kix,
mai kix yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
jhi grawng gum lox pa sidiex kix mawh tete heue.]
 
·Kawn koe phuk lai been prim pa tiiem mawh gum lox nghoik plak greem·
 
[
9 Bun miex an kix hu ka Pi-teux mai ka paox grawm nawh,
mai krai can pa hoik yaox hoik mhong tix ka kix khawm uik.
10 Khaix an,
to kaux Ye-sux ploi ka kawn cau tix hu,
jauh ka plak lih singaix tom hoek ka plak klip singai,
mai sidiex ka gum lox pa singax,
pa ang chix laux chix leeing,
pa kieh pui pon ju leeing ju leeing phan an heue.]
