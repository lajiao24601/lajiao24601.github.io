Pa sidiex Yaw-hang cao cub com
(Lai Ma-thaix 3.1–12; Lai Ma-kheu 1.1–8; Lai Yaw-hang 1.19–28)
3
Yam mawh Thi-pi-lio Vawng Law-ma ka daux kao phuan num,
Phang-shio Pi-la-to mawh cao giex kaing Yo-thaix,
Si-luix mawh simiang pui Cia-li-lix,
pux aik nawh Fe-lix mawh simiang Yi-thu-li-yax mai simiang Theu-la-kheu-ni,
Lui-sa-niie mawh simiang Ya-pi-li-ni,
2 Ya-na mai Kai-ya-fa mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting.
Yam an gum lox Siyiex lih hoek ka Yaw-hang kawn simex Sa-cia-li-yax ka daux tiag daux tu.
3 Yaw-hang hoik tauh hu ka baoh rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
nawh krai pao ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
rieb ka krax pa cub com,
mai Siyiex tag sang pluat ploi ka phi map pex.”
4 aAwm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai Yi-sai-yax ah nin:
koe pui kok yo ka daux tiag daux tu ah nin:
cox khre daux krax son Simiang Ye-sux,
mai cox rauh see ka krax pa sang lawng nawh heue!
5 Ngawd pa koe khawm uik tag caw tix thom naog;
gawng ting gawng iak tag caw tix cox yuh si dawm.
Krax vawk krax kawk tag caw tix cox rauh see;
krax pa khluk khlik tag caw tix cox si dawm.
6 Kawn pui piang haktiex tag sang yaox ka krax taoh Siyiex heue.
7 bPui hun si ghox ka paox tix,
lih rieb ka been Yaw-hang pa cub com.
Yaw-hang ah nin ka kix:
“Pex pa mawh si uing pa koe jai in kix heue!
Krax prawng ca Siyiex hoik dex sang hoek,
pex ce tix kawn sang pon mox tix lie?
8 cTag caw tix teei kaing pa yuh tix jhi grawng hoik loh pex rhawm rhi tix.
Paw vi ce Ya-paw-la-hang mawh tax du tix mai taug yaox tix luat luan khaing krax prawng krax ca.
Aux krai ka pex,
Siyiex pon teei simaox in kix lih mawh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang heue!
9 dMoe hoik deuh tix ka piang riah khaox,
can khaox plix pa ang plix tag sang kid kah,
mai taing sau daux ngu.”
10 Pui hun chawk nawh ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
sang yuh ex ka mawx lie?”
11 Yaw-hang pawk ah nin:
“Pui pa koe kraung ceep plak daux ra pheen,
tag caw guah tix tawx ka pui pa ang koe khankix;
pui pa koe priex preem kawx,
tag caw tix yuh nan nang heue.”
12 eKoe pui koh keen tix blah kawx tag caw tix hoek rieb ka been pa cub com;
kix chawk Yaw-hang ah nin:
“Sara,
sang caw yix tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
13 Nawh krai ka kix ah nin:
“Ang pon tix rieb ka mau koh keen pa ang mawh pa tat pui tix deuh heue.”
14 Koe pui naing tix blah kawx hoek tix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yix lie?
Sang caw tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
Nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Ang pon tix tud rhim ka mau khri pui,
ang pon tix gut kheem ka pui;
tag caw tix vhah rhawm mai mau pa pon tix heue.”
15 Krax keud pot jun pot kawn pui hoik haok huan,
pui hun hoik keud daux rhawm rhi tix,
Yaw-hang tee mawh Khrit.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-hang ah nin ka kix:
“Aux teei rawm cub com pex kah;
viang mawh nan,
koe pui pa lheu ting siawp sibrawm khaing aux tix kaux pa tag sang hoek,
pie sang kah ka maox kiiep nawh kawx aux ang keung kah.
Nawh tag sang teei Khuan Singax Singeh mai ngu cub com pex kah.
17 Ka daux taix nawh giex ka bia yeeb,
tag sang yeeb ka nghox koi singax,
teei nghox laig daux krao;
phan mawh ghrip,
nawh tag sang teei ngu pa ang chix yeet leeing ju gleeing ka heue!”
18 Yaw-hang sidiex gum lox buan ka kawn pui,
mai teei gum lox pa ang awm paox jheeh nyaw ka kix.
19 fViang mawh nan,
Yaw-hang ne raw ka simiang Si-luix,
mawh kheu hoik teei Si-luix tix moeng ka moeng pux aik nawh Si-law-tix,
mai baox yuh kaing caox tom nie.
20 Khaix an,
nawh yuh ka kaing pa lang teung ang mhawm tix ceu,
mai sau Yaw-hang laig daux thawng heue.
Ye-sux rieb krax cub com
(Lai Ma-thaix 3.13–17; Lai Ma-kheu 1.9–11)
21 Pui khankix hoik rieb krax pa cub com Yaw-hang khawm uik,
Ye-sux kawx hoek tix cub com.
Yam rawg nawh buan,
rao ma baoh,
22 gKhuan Singax Singeh pa koe pak peung lih hoek ka piang nawh awm si kaux yuh nan.
Koe lox lih hoek khaing rao ma ah nin:
“Maix mawh kawn simex pa muh aux,
aux khaw rhawm ka maix heue.”
Doeh nyiex ceu cad Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 1.1–17)
23 Yam jauh Ye-sux tix sidiex,
ju nawh koe been tix ngoe num.
Ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
nawh mawh kawn simex Yaw-seux,
Yaw-seux mawh kawn simex Si-lix,
24 Si-lix mawh kawn simex Ma-tha,
Ma-tha mawh kawn simex Li-we,
Li-we mawh kawn simex Me-ji,
Me-ji mawh kawn simex Ya-na,
Ya-na mawh kawn simex Yaw-seux.
25 Yaw-seux mawh kawn simex Ma-tha-thi-yax,
Ma-tha-thi-yax mawh kawn simex Ya-maw-see,
Ya-maw-see mawh kawn simex Na-hong,
Na-hong mawh kawn simex Yi-see-lix,
Yi-see-lix mawh kawn simex Na-kai,
26 Na-kai mawh kawn simex Ma-yax,
Ma-yax mawh kawn simex Ma-tha-thi-yax,
Ma-tha-thi-yax mawh kawn simex Si-me,
Si-me mawh kawn simex Yaw-seu-kheux,
Yaw-seu-kheux mawh kawn simex Yaw-tax.
27 Yaw-tax mawh kawn simex Yaw-ya-nax,
Yaw-ya-nax mawh kawn simex Li-sha,
Li-sha mawh kawn simex Saw-law-pa-paw,
Saw-law-pa-paw mawh kawn simex Sa-la-thiie,
Sa-la-thiie mawh kawn simex Ni-lix.
28 Ni-lix mawh kawn simex Me-ji,
Me-ji mawh kawn simex Ya-ti,
Ya-ti mawh kawn simex Keu-sang,
Keu-sang mawh kawn simex Yi-maw-tang,
Yi-maw-tang mawh kawn simex Eux,
29 Eux mawh kawn simex Yaw-six.
Yaw-six mawh kawn simex Yi-li-yi-shiex,
Yi-li-yi-shiex mawh kawn simex Yaw-ling,
Yaw-ling mawh kawn simex Ma-tha,
Ma-tha mawh kawn simex Li-we.
30 Li-we mawh kawn simex Si-miien,
Si-miien mawh kawn simex Yo-tax,
Yo-tax mawh kawn simex Yaw-seux,
Yaw-seux mawh kawn simex Yaw-nang,
Yaw-nang mawh kawn simex Yi-li-ya-cing.
31 Yi-li-ya-cing mawh kawn simex Mi-li-yax,
Mi-li-yax mawh kawn simex Mai-nang,
mai mawh kawn simex Ma-ta-tha,
Ma-ta-tha mawh kawn simex Na-tang,
Na-tang mawh kawn simex Ta-wue.
32 Ta-wue mawh kawn simex Yie-si,
Yie-si mawh kawn simex Eu-pe-teux,
Eu-pe-teux mawh kawn simex Paw-a-see,
paw-a-see mawh kawn simex Sa-meung,
Sa-meung mawh kawn simex Na-suing.
33 Na-suing mawh kawn simex Ya-mi-na-ta,
Ya-mi-na-ta mawh kawn simex Ya-ming,
Ya-ming mawh kawn simex Ya-ni,
Ya-ni mawh kawn simex Si-see-loeng,
Si-see-loeng mawh kawn simex Fa-leu-see,
Fa-leu-see mawh kawn simex Yo-tax.
34 Yo-tax mawh kawn simex Ya-keux,
Ya-keux mawh kawn simex Yi-sha,
Yi-sha mawh kawn simex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Ya-paw-la-hang mawh kawn simex Tha-la,
Tha-la mawh kawn simex Na-heux,
35 Na-heux mawh kawn simex Si-lux,
Si-lux mawh kawn simex La-wu,
La-wu mawh kawn simex Fa-leux,
Fa-leux mawh kawn simex Si-paw,
Si-paw mawh kawn simex Sa-la.
36 Sa-la mawh kawn simex Kai-nang,
Kai-nang mawh kawn simex Ya-fa-sa,
Ya-fa-sa mawh kawn simex Sang,
Sang mawh kawn simex Naw-yax,
Naw-yax mawh kawn simex La-maix.
37 La-maix mawh kawn simex Ma-thu-sha-la,
Ma-thu-sha-la mawh kawn simex Yi-naw,
Yi-naw mawh kawn simex Ya-liex,
Ya-liex mawh kawn simex Ma-leu-liex,
Ma-leu-liex mawh kawn simex Kai-nang,
38 Kai-nang mawh kawn simex Yi-naw-seex,
Yi-naw-seex mawh kawn simex Sai-theux,
Sai-theux mawh kawn simex Ya-tang,
Ya-tang mawh kawn simex Siyiex heue.
