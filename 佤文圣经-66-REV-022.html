22
aKawn cau Siyiex kieh aux yaox rawm klawng pa im ju tix klawng,
hoik sawng riang awm siang simaox paing yuh nan,
jauh lih hoek khaing dang ngeung Siyiex mai kawn yung,
2 bmai phlat hu krax pa koe ka sigreung veng.
Ka praok rawm klawng khawm ra plak khaox pa im ju koe kah,
tix num plix plix kao ra bawg,
tix khix tix bawg;
lhax khaox an pon pai ka jhawm saux meung piang haktiex moi khawm uik.
3 cKa daux veng ang lai baox koe kaing ceu pa jhax pa lawg Siyiex ka heue.
Dang ngeung Siyiex mai kawn yung tag sang ot ka daux veng in;
siliex nawh tag sang krub ka nawh.
4 Kix tag sang hoek tix krub ka Siyiex,
ceu kaux nawh tag sang tiiem tix ot ka dong re kix.
5 dDee an ang lai baox koe pa faik;
kix ang lai lo ka pa riang kawk ngu mai singaix,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh pa riang ka kix.
Kix tag sang mawh simiang pa giex kaing,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Ye-sux lih hoek
6 Kawn cau Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Gum lox in mawh pa mawh pa meh mai pa kaux sijax.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kaw pa tawx Khuan Singax Singeh an ka cao jhai khankix ploi kawn cau tix hu,
mai krai kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka daux bawg yam pa dex ka siliex cie nawh khankix heue.”
7 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
aux tag sang khran hoek heue!
Pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox jhai pa koe ka daux lai in koe buan son heue!”
8 Aux pa mawh Yaw-hang hoik mhong mai hoik yaox ka kaing ceu in kix khawm uik.
Khaix hoik mhong hoik yaox aux kah,
kawn cau Siyiex pa gup tix krai kaing ceu in kix ka aux,
tag sang krub ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
9 Viang mawh nan nawh krai ka aux ah nin:
“Paw doh yuh nin heue!
Aux mai maix,
khawm paox pa yuh maix kaing tau khankix,
pa mawh cao jhai khankix mai can pui pa hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa koe daux lai in,
hoik mawh siliex khawm uik nang.
Maix tag caw tix krub ka Siyiex heue!”
10 Nawh baox krai ka aux ah nin:
“Paw vi nyip ka gum lox pa koe ka daux lai in,
mawh kheu bawg yam sang lih kaing in kix tix mawh khawm uik,
hoik dex sang hoek.
11 ePa yuh pa caox pa caig tag sang niied tix yuh;
pa seeing pa brai ca kawn seeing brai.
Pa yuh kaing pa mhawm tag sang niied tix yuh;
ceu pa singax singeh ca kawn singax singeh heue.”
12 fYe-sux ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
aux tag sang khran hoek heue!
Aux tag sang viex krax pawk bix,
mai hawt tix tawx awm kaing pa hoik yuh pui ku kaux.
13 gAux mawh A-eu-fa mai Ya-mi-cia,
mawh pa koe sivoe mai pa nghoik khaix,
mawh pa jauh mai pa si dauh heue.”
14 hPui pa si daux ka kraung ceep tix khankix kied koe buan son heue!
Kix koe akhuang tix ih ka plix khaox pa im ju,
mai chix tix laig ka daux veng ka diang siviex veng.
15 Viang mawh nan,
sox khankix,
pa yuh si meed sat khankix,
pa phit vao khankix,
pa pauh pui yum khankix,
pa krub riang rao khankix,
mai pui pa pie lhien ka daux lox ah si kah krai tix,
mai ka daux kaing pa yuh tix khankix,
hoik deuh ot plak praix veng khawm uik heue.
16 i“Aux pa mawh Ye-sux ploi kawn cau Siyiex cie tix hu,
mai krai pao kaing ceu in kix ka pex ka ceu cao ku dauh.
Aux hoik mawh doeh ceu cad nyiex Ta-wue;
aux hoik mawh simuing pa riang heue.”
17 jKhuan singax singeh mai sibun pa yuh ka paox hoik ah nin:
“Hoek heue!”
Can pui pa mhong khawm uik kawx tag caw tix ah nin:
“Hoek heue!”
Hoek heue,
pui pa sang nyaux rawm hoek khawm uik;
pui pa gaux rhawm tix hoek chix tix hoek tix rieb ka rawm pa im ju heue.
Lox nghoik
18 kAux pa mawh Yaw-hang ne raw ka pex tom sidawng ka pui pa mhong ka lox jhai pa koe daux lai in ah nin:
phan koe pui pa baox thiem thie tang ceu piang gum lox in,
Siyiex tag sang thiem thie tix tawx krax laux leeing pa hoik tiiem ot daux lai in piang nawh.
19 Phan koe pui pa tieh ka gum lox jhai in pa hoik tiiem ot ka daux lai in,
Siyiex tag sang teei tik ka ceu kaux nawh,
mai ang kieh nawh pon buan son khaox pa im ju mai buan son veng pa singax pa hoik tiiem ot ka daux lai in heue.
20 Kaw pa hoik kham sakse son kaing in khawm uik ah nin:
“Mawh tete,
aux tag sang khran hoek heue!”
A-meung.
Simiang Ye-sux eue,
hoek heue!
21 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux tawx buan son ka pex ku kaux heue! l
