Bawg yam pa si dauh haktiex
3
Maix hoik tawng,
bawg yam pa si dauh haktiex hoik koe krax kha rhawm tom nie ceu.
2 Bawg yam an,
kawn pui kia ngien ngie ka to kaux tix khaing,
muh ka mau,
yaw rang di,
ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
map nai,
ang nghiet ka lox miex keeing tix,
ang tawng ka buan son,
ang singax singeh,
3 ang koe krax muh mai pui nyiex ma tix,
kin caox,
tawx lox pa pie lhien,
ang chix priex rhawm tix,
lang teung caox,
koeng ka ceu pa mhawm.
4 Kix coh ka sara paox grawm tix,
nieb pon yuh alo tix,
ting rhawm cau nyhau,
muh krax gaux rhawm lheu khaing muh Siyiex.
5 Kix ceep ka sibex been li,
viang mawh nan,
tieb tie ka been li tete.
Pui pa yuh nin,
pex tag caw mox tix khaing kix.
6 Ka daux kix koe pui tix blah hu laig ka daux siviex pui,
mai hoek ka daux nyiex pui tang kaux,
nyaw glaw ka bun miex pa tiiem rhawm keud,
pa gut kheem phi map kah,
pa giex gang alo nex nham ku ceu ka khankix.
7 Sibun miex in kix,
viang kix hoik rien gau tix jheeng jheeng kawx,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng pa mawh pa meh.
8 aAwm hoik tieb tie Ya-ni mai Yang-pu-le ka Maw-si yuh nan,
yam in pui in kix kawx hawt tix tieb tie ka pa mawh pa meh yuh nang.
Rhawm rhi kix caox caig,
ang pon kham krax pa cheuk cheu ka daux krax yum rhawm,
9 mai ang lai pon yuh ceu pa tid tix;
mawh kheu krax pa daox aw kix tag sang plih tix lih ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
awm Ya-ni mai Yang-pu-le yuh nan heue.
Lox pa nghoik tix krai
10 Viang mawh nan,
maix hoik hawt ka krax pa sidiex gau aux,
mai ka daux krax pa im ju kawn pui,
mai hoik hawt ka kaing yuh aux,
krax yum rhawm,
krax muh krax rhawm,
krax gun yeung,
11 bmai hoik guah tix klawm ka krax tuk tiak tuk te mai krax kha rhawm pa kham aux.
Ceu pa hoik si pup aux,
pa hoik kham aux yam ot aux ka daux haktiex Ang-thi-a,
Yi-keu-nien,
Lu-see-teux in kix,
maix hoik tawng ka khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Ye-sux hoik taoh aux luat luan khaing in kix khawm uik.
12 Can pui pa viex rhawm hawt ka Ye-sux Khrit pa yuh kaing krub,
hoik si pup krax pa tuk tiak tuk te khawm uik.
13 Pui pa caox pa caig mai pui pie pui lhien kawx hoik nie caox caig khaing tix ngaix hoek tix ngaix;
kix pie lhien ka pui tang kaux,
to kaux kix kawx hoik yaox krax pie lhien.
14 Phan mawh maix,
tag caw tix giex tom kaux cong ka ceu pa hoik rieb maix,
mai ka pa mawh pa meh tete.
Maix hoik tawng pui mawx mawh sara maix;
15 Maix kawx hoik dawg tawng sidawng ka phuk lai sigang khri,
jauh tom yam iak tix,
mawh phuk lai an pa pon tawx maix koe ping nyia cuyi,
pon thung maix pon krax taoh ka daux pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux Khrit.
16 Phuk lai sigang khri pa koe khawm uik,
hoik mawh pa tiiem lih khaing daux khuan Siyiexc,
mai koe dee mhawm ka son pa sidiex ka pa mawh pa meh,
pa ne raw ka phi map,
pa cox ka dee lud,
pa jhi ka krax pa rauh see ka kawn pui khawm uik.
17 Tag kieh pui pa yuh kaing krub ka Siyiex pon rien khre tix grum naog,
son sang yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm ku ceu heue.
