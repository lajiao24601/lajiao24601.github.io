Pa tiag goi rawm awm simiang Ai-cix
32
Yam vuk vud vuk viang ex ka daux kao ra num haok khix kao ra tix ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
2 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka simiang Ai-cix,
yuh lox rhax yiiem doe son nawh,
mai krai lox aux ka nawh ah nin:
maix tang ot awm sivai sang si yuh nan,
tete te lai,
maix mawh ngie goi rawm pa koe daux klawng.
Yam hu maix daux rawm klawng,
mai kieh caong tix chu chiah tiex glaox pa koe ka daux rawm klawng,
kieh rawm klawng si ku.
3 Yam khrawm pui meung haktiex tom nie ka paox tix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang ploi rub giex ka maix,
mai nhie kix bluh rub hu ka praok rawm.
4 Aux tag sang tik maix piang tiex,
si vun tix tik maix ka daux tiag,
kieh can sim pa pu rao ma mai to sat pa hu ka caong tix ih nex maix.
5 Aux tag sang teei to kaux yum maix pa hoik si um thom naog ka piang gawng,
thom naog ka daux ngawd;
6 aux tag sang klub ka nham maix,
kieh nham maix naog ka piang choek gawng,
hieh laig daux klawng iak.
7 aYam yuh aux maix laux leeing tag sang tawb kheem ka rao ma,
kieh simuing faik,
kieh pai awm kheem ka singaix,
khix kawx ang jhan riang.
8 Aux tag sang nyeep ka pa riang pa koe daux rao ma khawm uik,
kieh haktiex maix lih faik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
9 “Yam krai aux ka grawng pa laux leeing maix hu ka meung haktiex pa ang maix nyang sax mhong,
pui kix hoik yaoh yee ka khawm uik.
10 Aux yuh nin ka maix tag sang kieh meung haktiex tom nie yaoh yee.
Yam yaog aux tix chuh ka vaig tix,
cub simiang tag yaoh yee si yaung.
Singaix yam laux leeing maix an,
kix tag sang si yaung si kua khawm uik kheu ju tix heue.”
11 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka simiang Ai-cix ah nin:
“Maix tag sang si pup ka vaig gawn simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
12 Aux tag sang kieh meung haktiex pa kua khankix hoek tix puing ka maix;
pui nang kix tag sang lawd vaig pauh ka pui cie maix yum khawm uik.
Can pui cie maix khawm uik,
mai can pa hoik yaw maix rang di ka hoik pauh yum khawm uik.
13 Aux tag sang pauh khawng brie maix ka praok klawng,
mai ang lai baox koe pui mai khawng brie pa chu chiah ka tiex glaox pa koe daux klawng,
pa kieh rawm klawng si ku.
14 Aux tag sang kieh rawm klawng Ni-law si ngaong,
mai kieh singax;
aux tag sang kieh rawm klawng koi koi lih hu yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin.
15 Yam kieh aux Ai-cix lih mawh haktiex dee riem,
yam yuh pui kix laux leeing,
mai kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
16 Lox pa ne raw in tag sang lih mawh lox rhax yiiem doe tix ceu.
Bun miex ka piang meung haktiex tag sang rhax lox lai in,
kieh mawh pa yiiem son Ai-cix mai son pui meung haktiex cie nawh.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Meung yum
17 Yam vuk vied vuk viang yix ka daux kao ra num haok khixb tix kao phuan singaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
18 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix yiiem son pui Ai-cix tom nie.
Maix tag caw tix cot kix mai meung tang meung pa ting riang laig daux meung yum.
19 Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix ah nin:
Maix ce tix kawn lheu mhawm khaing pui lie?
Maix tag caw tix hu meung yum,
ot tau mai pui pa ang koe krax yum rhawm khankix heue.
20 “Pui Ai-cix tag sang goh tau mai pui pa pauh pui yum ka vaig gawn.
Vaig hoik lawd tix lih,
tag sang pauh kix yum khawm uik.
21 Pui naing pa kied kua khankix,
mai pui pa yuh paox grawm tau mai Ai-cix tag sang kuan rieb pui Ai-cix ka daux meung yum.
Kix kok yo ah nin:
‘Pui an kix pa ang koe krax yum rhawm,
mai pui pa pauh pui yum ka vaig gawn hoik hoek meung yum khawm uik;
kix hoik noi tix dee in heue!’
22 “Ya-sux ot dee an,
ka sivie siveng hoik mawh si meeig pui naing nawh.
Kix hoik yum ka vaig gawn khawm uik.
23 Si meeig kix ot ka meung yum dee blawn raux.
Can pui naing nawh hoik yum khawm uik,
si meeig kix lai lawm ka si meeig nawh.
Yam im ju kix,
kix hoik kieh pui lhat graung ka tix heue.
24 “Yi-lang ot dee an,
sivie siveng hoik mawh si meeig pui naing cie nawh khawm uik.
Kix hoik yum ka vaig gawn khawm uik;
kix ang bax Pat-hak tix hoek ka meung yum.
Yam im ju kix,
kix hoik kieh pui lhat graung ka tix,
yam in hoik yum,
mai kham krax pa map nai pui.
25 Yi-lang noi tix ka sigreung pui yum,
sivie siveng hoik mawh si meeig pui naing khawm uik.
Kix ang bax Pat-hak tix,
mai yum ka vaig gawn.
Yam im ju kix,
kix hoik kieh pui lhat graung ka tix,
yam in hoik yum,
mai kham krax pa kaig tua tau mai pui pa hoik yum khawm uik heue.
26 “Mi-seux mai Thu-pa kawx ot dee an,
sivie siveng hoik mawh si meeig pui naing cie kix khawm uik.
Kix mawh pui ang bax Pat-hak tix khawm uik,
hoik yum ka vaig gawn.
Yam im ju kix,
kix hoik kieh pui lhat graung ka tix.
27 Kix ang awm pui pa vao dix dix,
pon khawng phak pa koe min plak ngai yam yum tix,
ceep ka kraung naing khawm jung,
gaong ka vaig gawn,
tawb naok tix ka dieb tie naok,c
noi tix it ka meung yum.
Yam kawn im ju pui pa vao in kix ka piang haktiex,
kix hoik mawh pui pa ting chix,
hoik kieh pui lhat graung ka tix heue.
28 “Ai-cix tag sang hawt tix yuh laux leeing nang,
mai noi tix it ka sigreung pui pa ang bax Pat-hak tix mai pui pa yum ka vaig gawn khankix heue.
29 “Yi-tong ot dee an;
simiang meung nawh mai simiang khankix hoik mawh pui naing pa kied vao khawm uik.
Yam in kix hoik noi tix it ka daux meung yum tau mai pui pa ang bax Pat-hak tix pui pa hoik yum ka vaig gawn khankix heue.
30 “Can simiang pa koe ka haktiex plak laong hoik ot dee an khawm uik;
pui Si-toeng kawx ot dee an.
Siawp sibrawm kix hoik sax kieh pui lhat graung.
Yam in kix hoik hu kham krax kaig tua ka meung yum tau mai pui pa ang bax Pat-hak tix mai pui pa hoik yum ka vaig gawn khankix,
mai noi tix it dee an heue.”
31 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Simiang Ai-cix mai pui naing cie nawh tag sang pon krax pa nyawm rhawm ka yaox krax pa si pup pui pa yum ka greem lawm vaig in kix heue.
32 “Aux hoik sax teei simiang Ai-cix ghlat ka pui im ju,
viang mawh nan,
nawh mai pui naing cie nawh tag sang yum ka greem lawm vaig khawm uik,
khaix an noi tix it tau mai pui pa ang bax Pat-hak tix,
mai pui pa hoik yum ka vaig gawn khankix.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
