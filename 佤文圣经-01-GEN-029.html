Ya-keux hoek ka nyiex La-pang
29
Ya-keux ca nied tix hu ka krax hu tix,
mai hoek ka haktiex dee ot ceu pui plak lih singaix.
2 Nawh yaox ka rawm maw tix daux ka daux tiag tan,
koe phung yung loe cub pa it praok rawm maw an.
Cao liang yung kin tix tawx yung nyaux rawm hoek tix kin tin uik,
mai piang moing rawm maw an koe simaox ting ka tix mu.
3 Yam hoik grum hoek phung yung,
cao liang yung tom pu plie simaox an,
kieh yung nyaux ka rawm,
khaix an,
deeh tawp ka moing rawm maw an heue.
4 Ya-keux chawk ah nin ka kix:
“Paox grawm khankix eue,
kaoh pex khaing dee mawx?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Yix kaoh khaing Ha-lang heue.”
5 Nawh ah nin:
“Tawng pex La-pang kawn simex Na-heux lie?”
Kix ah nin:
“Yix tawng ka nawh heue.”
6 Ya-keux baox chawk ah nin:
“Mhawm ot nawh lie?”
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Nawh nyawm ot,
maix jhak,
kawn bun nawh Le-ciiex hoek plak in tau mai phung yung nawh heue.”
7 Ya-keux ah nin:
“Singaix kawn jung,
ang nyang hoek bawg yam khrawm pui ka phung yung,
yuh ka mawx ang tawx yung nyaux rawm mai deeh hu liang kix lie?”
8 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Yix krox phung yung grum,
cao liang yung tom khrawm tix pu plie simaox khaing moing maw an,
yung tom tag chix yaox tix nyaux ka rawm heue.”
9 Yam kawn si kah Ya-keux tau mai cao liang yung,
Le-ciiex hoek tau mai phung yung keeing nawh;
kheu mawh nawh cao liang yung.
10 Yam yaox Ya-keux ka Le-ciiex kawn bun La-pang mai phung yung prie miex tix,
nawh tom gah simaox khaing rawm maw an,
mai kieh phung yung nyaux rawm.
11 Khaix an,
Ya-keux ngheet ka Le-ciiex,
mai ploi lox yiiem tix.
12 Nawh krai ka Le-ciiex ah nin:
“Aux mawh kawn pha keeing maix,
kawn simex Li-pai-cia.”
Le-ciiex tom to ing krai ka keeing tix.
13 La-pang mhong hoek kawn pha tix Ya-keux,
nawh tom to tix hu rieb ka nawh,
kawm mai ngheet nawh,
viex nawh ing ka nyiex tix.
Ya-keux krai grawng tix khawm uik ka La-pang.
14 La-pang ah nin:
“Maix mawh si-ang aux mai nex nham aux!”
Ya-keux ot dee an tix khix heue.
Ya-keux yuh kaing son Li-yax mai Le-ciiex
15 Koe tix ngaix,
La-pang ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Viang maix mawh kawn pha aux kawx,
maix ang chix pon tix yuh kaing giex going son aux.
Krai aux mhong,
sang ih maix ngawh riang tix miex ding?”
16 La-pang koe kawn bun ra kaux;
ceu kaux kawn yex mawh Li-yax mai ceu kaux kawn i kaux Le-ciiex.
17 Li-yax awn ngai pa kied mhawm koe riang,a
Le-ciiex kied mhawm grang mai nyawm tix jhak heue.
18 Ya-keux kied muh ka Le-ciiex,
kheu nan,
nawh tom rawg mhaing ah nin:
“Phan maix cu aux moeng Le-ciiex,
aux tag yuh kaing son maix aliah num heue.”
19 La-pang pawk ah nin:
“Aux cu nawh mex ka maix,
lheu mhawm khaing mex nawh pui tan kaux.
Maix ot tau mai aux.”
20 Son sang yaox nawh tix moeng Le-ciiex,
Ya-keux yuh kaing aliah num,
kheu kied muh nawh Le-ciiex,
nawh keud singaix sawm an awm ngie tix vud khaing heue.
21 Ya-keux ah nin ka La-pang:
“Mawh kheu hoik naog bawg yam,
kieh aux yuh ka paox mai kawn bun maix heue!”
22 Kheu nan,
La-pang rien poi ka paox,
ghaok ka pui baoh haktiex an hoek.
23 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux bo an,
La-pang tawx kawn bun tix Li-yax kieh tang Le-ciiex ka Ya-keux,
Ya-keux tom ot tau mai nawh.
24 La-pang ap bun siliex tix Si-phax ka Li-yax son sang mawh siliex nawh.
25 Yam hoek plak pon ngawb cao,
Ya-keux tom tawng mawh Li-yax,
nawh tom sawg ka La-pang ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh nin ka aux?
Aux hoik yuh kaing son maix kheu Le-ciiex.
Yuh ka mawx maix pie lhien ka aux lie?”
26 La-pang pawk ah nin:
“Hawt been haktiex yix tin,
kawn bun pux ke ang nyang koe mex,
kawn bun pux ang caw tix chix koe mex.
27 Phan maix cu tix baox yuh kaing son aux aliah num,
krox jun hoik yuh kaing ka paox krax gaux rhawm in tix vang tuah,
aux sang baox tawx Le-ciiex ka maix heue.”
28 Ya-keux cu kah.
Khaix hoik luan tix vang tuah,
La-pang tom baox tawx kawn bun tix Le-ciiex mawh moeng Ya-keux.
29 La-pang kawx ap bun siliex tix Pi-la ka Le-ciiex,
son sang yuh siliex nawh.
30 Ya-keux kawx ot tau mai Le-ciiex;
nawh muh Le-ciiex lheu khaing Li-yax.
Kheu nan,
nawh baox yuh kaing son La-pang aliah num heue.
Kawn simex kawn bun Ya-keux
31 Simiang Siyiex yaox grawng pa lheu muh Ya-keux ka Le-ciiex khaing Li-yax.
Tom kieh Li-yax keh kawn,
mai Le-ciiex ang chix keh kawn.
32 Li-yax koe kaux,
mai keh kawn simex tix kaux.
Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik yaox ka krax kha aux rhawm tix tete.
Yam in mex aux khaw tix muh ka aux”.
Kheu nan,
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Lui-piien.b
33 Nawh baox koe kaux,
baox keh kawn simex daux ra kaux.
Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex baox tawx kawn simex ka aux tix kaux;
mawh kheu hoik mhong nawh ang aux yaox krax muh;
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Si-miien.”c
34 Nawh baox koe kaux,
mai keh kawn simex daux loe kaux,
nawh ah nin:
“Bawg in mex aux tag nie braong ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik keh aux kawn simex loe kaux son nawh”.
Mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Li-we.d
35 Nawh baox koe kaux,
keh kawn simex daux pon kaux;
nawh ah nin:
“Bawg in,
aux tag sang yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;”
mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yo-tax.e
Khaix an,
nawh ang lai chix koe kawn heue.
