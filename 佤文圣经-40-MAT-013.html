Gum lox phek tiiem pa rhuat simie
(Lai Ma-kheu 4.1–9; Lai Lu-cia 8.4–8)
13
Ka daux singaix an nang,
Ye-sux lih khaing nyiex,
mai hu ka praok nhawng.
Nawh ngawm.
2 aPui hun khrawm tix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh haok ka daux reu tix lhang,
mai ngawm ka daux;
pui hun cung ka praok rawm.
3 Nawh teei gum lox phek tiiem kah ka dee bleeh grawh grawng kaing tom nie ka kix heue.
Nawh ah nin:
“Koe pui pa rhuat simie tix kaux hu rhuat ka simie.
4 Yam rhuat nawh ka simie,
koe simie tix blah cawd ka praok krax,
sim khankix pu hoek kaoh tix ih.
5 Tix blah cawd ka tiag simaox dee thaw tiex,
simie an kied phai kuah,
mawh kheu ang tiex raux,
6 viang mawh nan,
yam lih singaix,
rat hax ka riah simie an;
mai baox mawh kheu ang bax kub raux,
simie an kroh.
7 Koe tix blah cawd ka daux pang kat,
mai pang kat haok huan,
kheem su shiet ka simie an.
8 Koe simie tix blah cawd ka dee mhawm tiex,
haok huan ting lhaong,
mai pon tix khrawm tix yieh son,
tix gliah son,
mai tix ngoe son heue.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui pa koe yhaok,
tag caw tix nghiet khawm uik heue!”
Grawh grawng krai ka gum lox phek tiiem
(Lai Ma-kheu 4.10–12; Lai Lu-cia 8.9–10)
10 Pui gau hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai teei gum lox phek tiiem si kah ka pui hun lie?”
11 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Grawng Pa raux pa mox meung Siyiex hoik tawx ka pex,
ang bax tawx ka kix.
12 bMawh kheu pui pa hoik koe an,
tag sang nie baox tawx kah,
mai kieh nawh kub tom nie;
pui pa ang koe an,
pie pa hoik koe nawh ceeix ceeix kawx tag sang teei hu heue.
13 Mawh kheu son in,
aux teei gum lox phek tiiem si kah ka kix;
mawh kheu kix jhak mai ang tix yaox,
nghiet mai ang tix mhong,
mai baox ang tawng yung nang.
14 cGum lox jhai pa krai Yi-sai-yax hoik lih tix mawh ka to kaux kix;
nawh ah nin:
pui hun in kix hoik nghiet baox nghiet,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng;
hoik jhak baox jhak,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix yaox.
15 Mawh kheu hoik klan kix rhawm rhi tix,
yhaok kix hoik thox tix,
ngai kix hoik nyip tix.
Phan ang mawh nan,
ngai kix tag sang chix yaox,
yhaok kix kawx tag sang chix mhong,
rhawm rhi kix kawx tag sang chix doh,
tag sang chix loh tix,
aux tom pai kix moi heue.
16 d“Viang mawh nan,
pex hoik ting buan son heue!
Mawh kheu hoik chix yaox ngai pex,
chix mhong yhaok pex.
17 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
koe cao jhai mai pui pa lhak tom nie tag sang jhak ceu pa hoik yaox pex,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix yaox;
tag sang nghiet ceu pa hoik mhong pex,
ang chix mhong nang heue.”
Kah dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem
(Lai Ma-kheu 4.13–20; Lai Lu-cia 8.11–15)
18 “Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem in.
19 Pui pa mhong gum lox grawng meung rao ma mai ang tawng dee bleeh an kix tag awm simie pa rhuat ka praok krax,
pui caox pui caig hoek mai teei hu ka gum lox pa rhuat ka daux rhawm rhi nawh.
20 Simie pa cawd ka daux tiag simaox mawh pui pa mhong ka gum lox mai gaux rhawm tix rieb dum mawx dum an khaing,
21 mawh kheu ang riah raux,
ang pon kham tom rheeng,
yam si pup ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
phan ang mawh nan,
krax tuk tiak tuk te,
dum mawx dum an deeh tik vud kah.
22 Simie pa rhuat ka daux pang kat an mawh pui pa mhong ka gum lox buan,
krax khi rhawm khi rhi ka daux krax ot krax koe mai krax nyaw krax glaw sin ngao haktiex kieh gum lox in yeet,
ang pon plix plix.
23 Simie pa rhuat ka dee mhawm tiex an mawh pui pa mhong gum lox,
mai tawng yung kah;
nawh pon plix plix,
mai pon khrawm ka tix yieh son,
tix gliah son,
tix ngoe son heue.”
Gum lox phek tiiem ka vhang
24 Ye-sux baox krai lox phek tiiem tang ceu tix ceu ah nin:
“Meung rao ma awm pui pa rhuat simie pa mhawm ka daux kaung ma yuh nan.
25 Yam laig it pui khankix,
pui koeng hoek mai rhuat vhang ka si nax nghox ca mai hu khaing heue.
26 Yam hoik ting si gleem nghox ca,
vhang kawx lih koe nang.
27 Siliex cao nyiex kaung ma an hoek tix ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
pa rhuat maix ka daux kaung ma mawh simie pa mhawm,
vhang in kix kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?’
28 Nawh pawk ah nin:
‘In kix mawh pa yuh pui koeng.’
Kix tom chawk ah nin:
‘Sang kieh maix yix hu reeig ka vhang lie?’
29 Nawh ah nin:
‘Ang siao hu heue!
Mawh kheu yam reeig tik pex ka vhang,
aux lhat pex caw tix reeig ka nghox ca.
30 Kieh nghox ca khawm vhang ting tau heue;
yam khrawm vawg kah,
aux tag sang tawx lox ka pui yuh kaing mai nhie kix reeig tix tik ka vhang,
mag tau gleeing ka ngu,
khaix an khrawm ka nghox ca,
mai deuh ot ka daux krao cie aux heue.’ ”
Gum lox phek tiiem ka simie taux klaox
(Lai Ma-kheu 4.30–32; Lai Lu-cia 13.18–19)
31 Ye-sux baox krai gum lox phek tiiem tang ceu tix ceu ah nin:
“Meung rao ma awm simie taux klaox tix sok yuh nan,
pui rhuat nawh ka daux kaung ma.
32 Simie in kawn lheu iak khaing simie tang ceu khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
yam hoik kuah nawh tix ting mai kawn lheu ting khaing taux lhax pa koe ku ceu;
nawh lih mawh khaox tix gawng,
mai sim khankix kawx yuh mheem tix ot ka piang kak khaox heue.”
Gum lox phek tiiem ka miien pa koe su saix
(Lai Lu-cia 13.20–21)
33 Ye-sux baox teei gum lox phek tiiem tang ceu tix ceu krai ka pui hun ah nin:
“Meung rao ma awm miien pa koe su saix yuh nan,
koe bun tix kaux teei tix sau ka daux miien tipon kongsheng,
mai kieh nawh lih ploh khawm uik heue.”
Teei ka gum lox phek tiiem
(Lai Ma-kheu 4.33–34)
34 Ye-sux teei gum lox phek tiiem krai lao in kix ka pui hun khawm uik;
lheu khaing teei nawh gum lox phek tiiem,
nawh ang krai ceu pa tid tix ka kix.
35 eNawh yuh nin hoik kieh gum lox pa krai cao jhai lih tix mawh ah nin:
“Aux sang teei gum lox phek tiiem krai lao ka kix,
mai krai kix mhong kaing glip ka daux pa phan rao ma haktiex dix heue.”
Kah ka dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem son vhang
36 Ye-sux lih hu khaing pui hun mai laig ka daux nyiex.
Pui gau nawh baox hoek tix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Krai ka yix liak,
gum lox phek tiiem son vhang pa koe daux kaung ma dee bleeh mawh ceu pa tix?”
37 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pa rhuat ka simie pa mhawm an mawh kawn kawn pui;
38 kaung ma mawh haktiex in;
simie pa mhawm mawh pui cie meung rao ma;
vhang mawh pui cie kaw pa caox pa caig;
39 pui koeng pa rhuat ka simie mawh Phi-caox.
Yam khrawm vawg ka mawh dee si dauh haktiex singaix yam in;
pui yuh kaing pa khrawm pa vawg mawh kawn cau Siyiex.
40 Yam hoik hoek dee si dauh haktiex,
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig khankix tag sang awm vhang pa reeig tix tik yuh nan,
mai taing gleeing daux ngu.
41 Kawn kawn pui tag sang ploi kawn cau Siyiex cie tix,
mai teei giex ka pui an kix pa kieh pui yuh phi map ka daux meung,
mai ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig khawm uik,
42 taing ka daux glah ngu.
Kix tag sang yiiem riag mai greeik rhang braing tix ka dee an.
43 Yam an,
pui rauh pui see ot ka daux meung keeing Siyiex tag sang jhan riang awm singaix yuh nan.
Pui pa koe yhaok kieh nghiet khawm uik heue!”
Gum lox phek tiiem ka siang
44 “Meung rao ma awm sin ngao pa mox ot daux kaung ma yuh nan.
Koe pui yaox kah,
mai kheem kah,
khaix an kied koe krax gaux rhawm tix coh ka can pa koe tix khawm uik,
hu liag ka kaung an heue.
Gum lox phek tiiem ka siang ploi
45 “Meung rao ma baox awm pui pa yuh lai gee tix kaux pa hu sawg ka siang ploi pa kied ngawh.
46 Yam yaox nawh ka siang ploi pa kied ngawh an tix mu,
nawh hu coh ka can pa koe tix khawm uik,
mai liag ka siang ploi an heue.”
Gum lox phek tiiem ka rub
47 “Meung rao ma baox awm rub ka daux nhawng,
mai rub ka kax pa koe ku ceu.
48 Yam hoik naog rub,
pui rub kax bluh rub haok,
mai ngawm tix guah kax ka praok rawm,
sut pa mhawm ka daux pung,
tik ka pa ang mhawm khankix.
49 Dee si dauh haktiex yam in kawx tag sang yuh nin nang:
kawn cau Siyiex tag sang lih hu tag sang guah pui caox lih khaing daux pui mhawm,
50 taing tix tik ka daux glah ngu.
Kix tag sang yiiem mai greeik rhang braing tix ka dee an heue.”
Pa khraox mai pa prim
51 Ye-sux chawk kix ah nin:
“Tawng pex ka lox in kix khawm uik lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Hoik tawng ka khawm uik heue.”
52 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri tag sang lih mawh pui gau meung rao ma,
awm pui cao nyiex tix kaux pa gah kraung khrai tix pa khraox mai pa prim lih khaing nyiex krao yuh nan heue.”
Pui ang rhawm yaox Ye-sux ka Na-sha-leux
(Lai Ma-kheu 6.1–6; Lai Lu-cia 4.16–30)
53 Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux ka gum lox phek tiiem in kix,
nawh lih hu khaing haktiex an,
54 mai deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix.
Nawh sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex sidiex;
pui pa mhong khankix kied daox deng kah,
mai ah nin:
“Pon nawh ping nyia cuyi pa yuh nin khaing dee mawx lie?
Nawh kawn yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am lie!
55 Pui in ang mawh kawn simex pui pa yuh cang mai khaox lie?
Miex nawh ang mawh Ma-li-yax lie?
Ya-keux,
Yaw-seux,
Si-meung,
mai Yo-tax ang hoik mawh pux aik nawh khankix lie?
56 Nan nawh khankix ang hoik ot dee in khawm uik lie?
Krax lhak krax lhiao nawh in khawm uik kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?”
57 fMawh kheu nan,
kix ang rhawm yaox ka nawh heue.
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Cao jhai pon krax yung yi pui plak praix yaong,
plak praix nyiex ma ot tix heue.”
58 Mawh kheu kix ang yum rhawm,
nawh tom ang yuh mhai sakse tom nie ka dee an heue.
