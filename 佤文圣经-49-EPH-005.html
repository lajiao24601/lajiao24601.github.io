Ot ka daux pa riang
5
Kheu hoik mawh pex kawn praix pa muh Siyiex,
pex tag caw tix hawt ka nawh.
2 aKa daux krax ot krax koe pex tag caw tix yuh lih ka daux krax muh,
awm hoik muh Khrit ka ex,
mai tawx ju tix son ex,
kieh mawh khawng tan pa bhawng mai ceu pa tan lu tix tawx ka Siyiex heue.
3 Phan mawh kaing ceu pa phit vao,
pa seeing brai,
mai pa ghoing rhawm,
pie ka daux pex kawx ang pon tix krai lih,
kheu hoik mawh pex pui cie Siyiex.
4 Pex kawx ang chix tix krai ka lox seeing,
lox pa aw,
lox cau nyhau;
tag caw tieh tix krai ka lox pa yaog yaw ka Siyiex.
5 Ang lai koe rhawm keud tang ceu,
pa phit vao,
pa seeing brai,
mai pa ghoing rhawm (pa ghoing rhawm hoik awm pa krub riang rao) ang doh pon tix lih mawh pui cie Khrit mai pui meung cie Siyiex heue.
6 Paw vi kieh pui tix kaux kawx pie lhien pex ka lox giex lox going;
kheu son kaing in kix,
krax pa song rhawm Siyiex tag sang lih hoek ka piang to kaux pui pa lieh lox khankix.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
paw vi lai ling tix yuh paox pie mai pui in kix.
8 Pex hoik mawh pui pa ot ka daux pa faik sivoe an,
viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik lih mawh pex kawn Khrit cie Simiang Siyiex,
pex tag ot ka daux pa riang.
Krax ot krax koe pex tag caw tix awm pui pa riang.
9 Pa lih plix pa riang khawm uik,
mawh pa mhawm rhawm rhi,
pa rauh see,
mai pa mawh pa meh.
10 Tag caw tix guah ceu pa tix mawh kaing ceu pa hoik khaw rhawm Siyiex.
11 Ang pon tix yuh kaing ceu pa yuh pui tang kaux ka daux pa faik pa ang koe buan son,
mai tag caw tieh tix plih kaing ceu pa yuh nin lih.
12 Kaing ceu pa hoik yuh kix pa ang mhawm ka daux pa faik,
pie krai kawx hoik mawh ceu pa kaig tua heue!
13 Yam hoik jhi kaing in khawm uik tix lih ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
ceu pa mawh mai pa ang mawh tag jhi tix lih,
14 mawh kheu can pa jhi tix lih tag mawh pa riang.b
Mawh kheu nan,
lox rhax hoik ah nin:
si duad heue,
pui pa laig it,
mai kaoh lih khaing daux krax yum heue!
Khrit tag sang kia ngien ka pex heue.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw rien rhawm ka kaing pa yuh tix.
Paw vi awm pui pa daox,
tag caw tix awm pui pa koe ping nyia.
16 cTag caw tix siao mhawm ka bawg yam ku bawg,
mawh kheu singaix pa hoik luan kied caox caig.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
paw vi mawh pui pa pheem,
tag caw tix tawng ka alo Siyiex mawh ceu pa tix heue.
18 Paw vi yoek ka plai,
an hoik mawh ceu pa chix laux leeing yuh pui;
tag caw tix naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh.
19 dTag caw tix teei krax yaog yaw,
lox rhax pa singax singeh,
lox rhax daux khuan ra lai rhax ka paox tix;
tag ploi lox lih khaing ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
rhax lai ka nawh.
20 Tag caw tix yaog yaw keeing Siyiex jheeng jheeng son kaing ku ceu,
ka daux ceu kaux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue.
Grawng mex moeng
21 Pex tag caw tix ra lai nghiet ka paox tix,
kheu hoik mawh pex pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Khrit heue.
22 ePa mawh moeng khankix,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka mex tix,
awm nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex yuh nan.
23 Kheu mawh mex kaing ka moeng tix,
awm hoik mawh Khrit kaing ka ceu cao,
ceu cao mawh to kaux,
nawh mawh kaing,
nawh kawx mawh cao taoh ka ceu cao.
24 Awm hoik nghiet ceu cao ka Khrit,
moeng khankix kawx caw tix nghiet ka mex tix ka daux kaing ku ceu heue.
25 fPa mawh mex khankix,
pex tag caw tix muh ka moeng tix,
awm hoik muh Khrit ka ceu cao,
mai hoik tawx to kaux tix son ceu cao yuh nan.
26 Nawh yuh nin hoik mawh son sang teei rawm hoek tix si ghrah ceu cao ka daux gum lox nawh,
27 mai kieh nawh koe min mhawm grang,
singax singeh,
ang koe dee ne,
ang koe dee cud mai pa rak reen tix ceu kawx,
son sang tan lu ka to kaux tix.
28 Mex caw tix muh ka moeng tix,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan;
muh ka moeng tix taug mawh muh ka to kaux tix.
29 Ang sax koe ka pui pa koeng ka to kaux tix;
nawh hoik chix kia ngien ka jheeng jheeng,
mai chix khre kah,
awm hoik yuh Khrit ka ceu cao yuh nan;
30 kheu hoik mawh ex pak peung ka to kaux nawh.
31 gPhuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
pui tag sang lih khaing miex keeing tix,
mai kleuk tix tau mai moeng tix,
kiex ra kaux tag lih mawh tix kaux.”
32 Phuk lai sigang khri in hoik plak jhi ka pa raux pa mox pa kied ting;
pa krai aux mawh ka son grawng Khrit mai ceu cao,
33 viang mawh nan,
chix tix siao ka to kaux pex nang.
Mex tag caw tix muh ka moeng tix,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan,
moeng tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka mex tix heue.
