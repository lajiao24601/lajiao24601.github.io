Lai Thi-to
Krai dawt
Thi-to mawh pui tang ceu tang cad pa laig li Khrit tix kaux, khaix an lih mawh paox pa yuh kaing pa sidiex tau mai Pao-law, mai cao pa teem coi ka nawh. Lox lai pa tawx Pao-law ka Thi-to mawh pa thiem tix tawx ka cao pa teem coi pui nhum ju in yam ot ka Kheu-li-theux; nawh hoik ot tix yuh kaing pa sidiex gau ka ceu cao dee an. Lox lai in krai kaing ceu pa ting ceu loe grawng yuh nin:
1. Pao-law krai ka Thi-to, yam ot ka daux pui Kheu-li-theux pa ang tawng been, ceu pa mhawm mai diag tong pa caw cao giex kaing li tix koe.
2. Pao-law krai ka grawng sang sidiex gau Thi-to ka pui pa koe ka daux ceu cao ku ceu, pa mawh kuad song, bun miex pa riex ju (kieh kix baox hu sidiex ka bun pa nhum ju), pui awn ju, mai siliex cawng khankix heue.
3. Pao-law krai grawng ceu pa mhawm pa caw kawn Khrit tix sang yuh, nie caw tix kia ngien pui ka daux pa yuh krax pa nhim, mai paw vi kieh krax pa koeng, pa simah, phan ang mawh nan pa yuh krax lawh krax maw lih koe ka daux ceu cao heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–4)
2. Simiang ceu cao (1.5–16)
3. Beeh glawm pui ka daux ceu cao (2.1–15)
4. Pa sidiex gau mai pa ne raw (3.1–11)
5. Lox nghoik (3.12–15)
