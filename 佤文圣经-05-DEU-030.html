Grawng pa sang pon krax haok huan mai krax mhawm ot
30
“Yam in,
aux sang kieh pex laug ka si nax krax pa nawb buan son mai si nax krax pa jhax lawg.
Khaix hoik lih krax kha rhawm in khawm uik,
yam ot pex ka daux meung pa ploi Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex pex hu ot ka ku meung,
mai pex tag sang doh keud ka pa hoik tawx aux pex laug.
2 Phan pex mai kawn praix pex,
dee deeh rhawm tix hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa hoik krai aux ka pex,
pa mawh gum lox ping nyap pa sang tawx nawh ka pex daux in singaix,
3 mai nawh tom tag chix caox rhawm ka pex.
Nawh tag sang deeh teei pex lih khaing daux haktiex pa hoik ploi nawh pex hu,
mai kieh pex dee baox yaox tix haok huan.
4 Viang pex hoik vung vied vung viang tix hu ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
kawn sang khrawm pex ot tau,
mai viex pex deeh hoek,
5 kieh pex jauh tix deeh pon ka haktiex pa hoik ot tax du pex ka sivoe an,
mai kieh pex kawn nie haok huan khaing tax du pex sivoe an,
kaux pui pex kawn nie haoh hun.
6 “Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag sang paoh ka rhawm rhi pex,
mai rhawm rhi kawn praix pex,
mai tag kieh pex muh tix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai pex ca kawn ot ka daux haktiex an jheeng jheeng.
7 Nawh tag sang kieh krax pa jhax lawg in kix,
baox thiem thie tix ka piang pui pa koeng pa khoi,
pa gut pa kheem ka pex khankix.
8 Pex tag caw tix baox cu nghiet ka nawh mai hawt tix yuh ka gum lox ping nyap pa krai aux ka pex daux in singaix,
pa sang tawx nawh ka pex khawm uik.
9 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kaing yuh pex haok huan,
kawn praix pex haoh hun,
khawng brie pex koe tom nie,
nghox koi pex pon tom nie.
Nawh hoik sax gaux rhawm tix kieh tax du pex khankix haok huan,
mai nawh kawx tag sang kieh pex haok huan yuh nan.
10 Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka nawh,
mai hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh mai ka gum lox ping nyap nawh pa hoik tiiem ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng khawm uik.
Pex viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai veeh tix hoek ka nawh heue.
11 “Gum lox ping nyap pa tawm aux ka pex daux in singaix,
ang mawh ceu pa nu tix hawt,
mai ang mawh pa singai khaing pex.
12 aGum lox ping nyap in ang mawh pa preue tix ot rao ma;
mai pex ang naox chawk ah nin:
‘Pui mawx sang haok ka piang rao ma,
mai teei tix viex ka lox gau in hoek ka ex,
son sang pon ex tix mhong mai hawt tix yuh lie?’
13 Gum lox ping nyap in kawx ang mawh pa ot ka plak toi rawm sigrit sigra;
pex ang naox chawk ah nin:
‘Pui mawx pa sang tiang hu ka plak toi rawm sigrit sigra,
mai teei tix viex ka gum lox ping nyap an hoek ka son ex,
son sang pon ex tix mhong mai hawt tix yuh lie?’
14 Tete te lai,
gum lox ping nyap in ang singai khaing pex,
hoik ot ka daux rhawm pex,
ot ka daux jheeh pex,
son sang hawt pex tix yuh heue.
15 “Daux in singaix,
aux hoik deuh krax im ju mai krax yum,
krax pa laux pa leeing mai krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ot ka plak sivoe pex,
kieh pex tang hu laug tix cie.
16 Phan pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa hoik tawm aux,
pa mawh gum lox ping nyap pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex ka pex,b
muh ka nawh,
nghiet ka nawh,
priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap nawh khawm uik,
pex tag yaox tix lih mawh meung pa haok huan.
Ka daux haktiex pa sang pon pex tix cie,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang tawx buan son ka pex.
17 Phan pex ang hawt kah,
ang nghiet lox,
mai tieh tix hu yuh kaing krub ka siyiex tang kaux ka daux krax pa nyaw glaw,
18 aux krai sidawng ka pex ka daux in singaix,
pex tag sang laux leeing tete,
mai ang yaox tix ot rheeng ka daux haktiex plak toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak klip singaix,
pa mawh haktiex pa sang pon pex tix cie an.
19 “Yam in,
aux kok yo rao ma mai haktiex mawh pa kham mhai sakse,
aux hoik deuh krax im ju mai krax yum,
krax nyawm ot mai krax jhax lawg ot ka plak sivoe pex.
Laug tix cie ju heue!
Pex mai kawn praix pex tag sang yaox tix im ju.
20 cMuh ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
cu nghiet ka lox nawh,
viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh son nawh.
Yuh nin,
pex mai kawn praix pex tom tag yaox tix im ju,
ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap nawh tix tawx ka tax du pex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux heue.”
