Lai Thie-sa-law-ni-cia Phuk Tix
1
aAux mawh Pao-law;
aux mai Si-la mai Thi-maw-thaix tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka ceu cao Thie-sa-law-ni-cia,
pa mawh kawn Khrit khankix pa dex dawt tau mai keeing Siyiex,
mai Simiang Ye-sux Khrit heue.
Kieh pex pon buan son mai pa nhim khawm uik heue!
Krax ot krax koe mai krax yum rhawm pui Thie-sa-law-ni-cia
2 Ex yaog yaw buan son Siyiex son pex jheeng jheeng,
mai doh keud ka pex ang jud ka daux krax pa rawg buan,
3 doh keud ka grawng pa yuh pex lih ka krax yum rhawm tix yuh ka mawx ka plak sivoe ngai keeing ex Siyiex.
Grawng pa yuh pex kaing pa taug mao ka daux krax muh krax rhawm,
mai grawng giex pex kaux cong ka krax keud pot jun pot ka Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
4 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
ex hoik tawng grawng muh Siyiex ka pex,
laug ka pex.
5 Ex sidiex gum lox buan ka pex,
ang joeng siao ngie lox krai tix khaing,
mai hoik siao khawm siawp sibrawm Khuan Singax Singeh,
khaix an hawt tix yum rhawm tete ka gum lox buan.
Pex hoik tawng khawm uik,
ka grawng yam ot ex mai pex dee an mawh pa yuh ka mawx;
mawh son sang koe buan son ka pex khawm uik.
6 bPex hoik hawt ka ex,
mai ka Simiang Ye-sux;
viang pex hoik si pup ka krax kha rhawm pa ting kawx,
mai ca kawn rieb ka gum lox pa hoik tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ka daux krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
pex hoik lih mawh pa gau kawn Khrit Ma-chi-toeng mai ka Ya-kai-yax yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm.
8 Gum lox cie Simiang Ye-sux ang joeng sidiex tix jauh khaing pex dee an,
hoek bleeh ka Ma-chi-toeng mai Ya-kai-yax,
krax yum rhawm pex ka Siyiex kawx hoik tauh hu ka dee dex dee singai ku dauh;
ang lai koe lox ceu pa sang krai ka tete.
9 Pui hoik krai ah nin ku kaux:
yam vhi vang ex ka pex,
pex hoik rieb ex yuh ka mawx,
mai baox yoi tix hu khaing riang rao,
veeh tix hoek ka Siyiex yuh ka mawx,
mai krub ka Siyiex pa im ju pa mawh pa meh in,
10 khaix an,
krax keud pot jun pot ka grawng pa sang lih hoek Ye-sux kawn simex nawh khaing rao ma.
Ye-sux in hoik mawh kaw an pa kieh Siyiex nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum;
nawh kieh ex bheeik luan khaing krax song rhawm Siyiex pa tag sang lih tix hoek heue.
