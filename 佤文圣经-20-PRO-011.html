11
Simiang Siyiex ang rhawm yaox jiang pa ang khaw caw;
nawh muh ka kla jiang pa khaw caw.
2 Pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi krax kaig krax tua hawt khaix nawh;
pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi ping nyia cuyi ot tau mai nawh.
3 Pui rauh pui see koe pa mawh pa meh chix duk thung ka kix;
pui pie pui lhien,
kheu pa kawk rhawm rhi kix kieh to kaux kix laux leeing.
4 Yam si pup ka krax yum,
mai sin ngao ang lai koe ceu;
pui pa mawh pa meh chix taoh ka ju pui.
5 Pa mawh pa meh chix kieh pui pa rauh see lawng daux krax pa si dawm;
pui pa caox pa caig tang deeh yuh to kaux tix laux leeing.
6 Pa rauh see chix taoh teem ka pui pa kaux sijax;
pui pie lhien tag deeh krix ka daux pa koe rhawm ghoing to kaux tix.
7 Yam yum pui pa caox pa caig khankix,
krax keud pot jun pot kix hoik yeet grai khawm uik;
pa een rhawm tix ka sin ngao,
dee si dauh mawh pa giex pa going.
8 Pui rauh see pon tix luat luan khaing krax laux leeing;
pui caox caig ang yaox tix bheeik luan khaing krax laux leeing.
9 Pui pa ang rauh ang see teei lox tix yuh paox plawg paox nyawm tix laux leeing;
ping nyia cuyi pui rauh pui see pon taoh ka pui tang kaux.
10 Pui pa mawh pa meh pon yaox tix haok huan,
khawm veng kawx pon krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
pui pa caox pa caig yum,
mai pui haoh pui hun gaux rhawm ka heue.
11 Veng pon krax pa haok huan kheu pa ot pui rauh pui see daux;
daux veng laux leeing kheu lox krai pui pa caox pa caig.
12 Pa map nai ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
hoik mawh pa ang koe krax keud krax sawn tete;
pui pa lhak lhiao yien yawg tix ang nye.
13 Pui pa yie krai lox ang koe ceu chix kieh kaing glip plih tix lih;
pui pa mawh pa meh chix priex ka lox pa tawx pui tom kaux.
14 Phan ang koe cao kaing,
meung tag sang laux leeing;
pui pa koe rhawm keud hao hun,
meung tag nyawm ot.
15 Pui pa kham tang pui pa ang tix sax meed sax tawng,
tag yaox krax pa som nai;
pui pa to hu khaing simah simaing,
tag pon krax pa yien pa yawg.
16 Kawn bun pa chix priex ka to kaux tix tag pon krax pa nab yam yung yi;
bun pa ang chix priex ka to kaux tix tag kieh to kaux tix kaig tua.
Pui pa nieh ang koe mau pie tix mita.
Pa koe rhawm keud dong sawn khankix tag chix hoek krax mag krax mi.
17 Pui pa chix caox rhawm tang kieh to kaux tix nyawm ot nyawm koe;
pui pa tud pa rhim tag kieh to kaux tix laux leeing.
18 Pui caox pui caig pon ngie sin ngao ka daux pa pie lhien;
pui rauh pui see tag pon khawng phak pa mawh pa meh.
19 Pui pa viex rhawm tix yuh kaing pa mhawm,
tag pon ju;
pui pa but rhawm rhi tix yuh pa caox pa caig,
tag kieh krax yum hoek.
20 Pa caox daux rhawm rhi,
Simiang Siyiex kied koeng kah;
pa mhawm kaing yuh,
tag pon krax pa khaw rhawm rhi nawh.
21 Pui caox pui caig nu sang pheeik luan khaing krax ciyang;
pui rauh pui see tag pon krax taoh teem.
22 Bun pa mhawm grang mai pa ang koe krax keud krax sawn,
tag awm meeh lig pa tauk ka kreem khri yuh nan.
23 Ceu pa simie pui rauh see chix tix plix plix pa mhawm;
krax keud pot jun pot pui pa caox caig kieh pui haoh hun song rhawm.
24 Pui pa si pieh rhawm tix tawx,
tag pon krax haok huan ku singaix;
pui pa ang tawx pie ceeix,
tieh tix hot sibhawm.
25 Pui pa si pieh rhawm tix tawx,
tag sang nie pon krax pa haok huan;
pui pa teem pui tang kaux khankix,
to kaux tag pon krax pa mhawm.
26 Khrawm tix deuh priex preem ang plih lih,
tag pon krax pa jhax lawg;
gaux rhawm tix coh ka priex preem,
kawn pui ku kaux yaog yaw ka nawh.
27 Sau riang yuh kaing mhawm,
pon krax pa yung yi kawn pui;
sawg krax pa caox caig,
krax laux leeing tag hoek piang to kaux.
28 Een rhawm ka sin ngao,
tag awm lhax khaox pa khrum bawg yam raux praix,
pui rauh see haok huan,
awm lhax khaox klai bawg yam rung praix.
29 Pui pa viex krax laux krax leeing laig ka nyiex,
dee si dauh tag sang kieh sin ngao nyiex ma tix uik pak lawg;
pui pa pheem tag sang mawh siliex pui pa lhak lhiao.
30 Pui rauh pui see chix plix plix ka ju;
pui tud pui rhim kieh ju pui laux leeing,
31 bPui rauh pui see ot ka piang haktiex kawn yaox krax pa pawk bix,
Pui caox mai pui phi map nie doh ang sang yaox yuh ka mawx lie!
