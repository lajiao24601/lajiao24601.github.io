27
aPaw vi ah lox ting mu son pa sax,
mawh kheu ang maix tawng kaing ceu pa sang lih tix mawh ku ngaix heue.
2 Kieh pui tang kaux yaw ka maix,
phan ang mawh nan,
kieh pui pa ang maix sax tawng yaw ka maix;
maix ang pon tix tang yaw ka to kaux tix heue.
3 Simaox,
mai mhaik viang cien kawx,
viang mawh nan,
krax laux leeing pa yuh pui pa daox aw kawn lheu cien khaing heue.
4 Krax song rhawm pa caox pa caig mawh pa chix kieh pui laux leeing,
viang mawh nan,
krax boh rhawm boh rhi nie mawh pa kied lhat tete.
5 Krax pa ne raw ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
lheu koe buan son khaing krax muh pa glip tix.
6 Krax pa ne raw paox grawm hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing rhawm rhi pa mhawm;krax pa kawm pui koeng,
tag caw tix rien tix ka heue.
7 Pui pa hoik sak sawm tieb tie ka rawm hia;pui pa cai rhawm pie priex preem pa song krin kawx,
hoik een mawh pa nyawm.
8 Pui pa lih khaing haktiex ot tix,
hoik awm kawn sim pa lih khaing mheen tix pu hu dee singai.
9 Sidah bhawng mai bee kraix chix kieh pui khaw rhawm;ka daux krax muh pa yuh paox grawm pa ting chix kieh pui gaux rhawm kah.
10 Paw vi tik vud ka paox grawm tix,
khawm paox grawm keeing maix.
Yam si pup maix krax laux paw lai sawg krax teem ka paox nyiex tix,
mawh kheu paox grawm pa dex maix kawn lheu khaing paox nyiex pa singai maix.
11 Pui nhum ju eue,
tag caw tix lhak lhiao,
aux tom tag gaux rhawm,
tom tag koe lox pa chix pawk ka pui pa nyiah ka aux heue.
12 Pui pa chix priex rhawm tix,
yam si pup ka krax laux krax leeing mai chix to mox tix khaing;pui pa daox aw hoek tix dex mai krax laux krax leeing,
khaix an klih rhawm tix.
13 Pa tang son pui pa ang tix sax tawng sax yungb,
tag caw tix teei kraung ceep tix kieh mawh pa tang heue.
14 Pa so paox grawm tix haing yam pon ngawb cao,
ah lox tix ting nawb buan piang nawh,
hoik awm jhax lawg ka nawh heue.
15 Moeng pa haoh lox hoik awm lih lhiex tix tix ta ta yuh nan;
16 phan sang nhie nawh yien yawg,
hoik awm tieb tie pui ka bhaung ting,
phan ang mawh nan,
hoik awm giex pui bee ka taix tix yuh nan.
17 Paox grawm ra lai gau tix mai paox tix,
hoik awm rhiem tau mai rhiem pa klaing tix lih lawm.
18 Pa kia ngien ka khaox ruh,
tag yaox tix ih ka plix;pui pa chix kia ngien ka cao simiang tix,
tag yaox tix koe min.
19 Pui chix yaox pak ngai tix ka daux rawm;ceu pa koe ka daux rhawm rhi hoik sidawng lih ka daux to kaux maix.
20 Meung yum ot pui yum ang pon yuh naog;alo nex nham kawn pui mawh pa ang chix kub chix naog heue.
21 Glauh ngu cheuk ka mau khri;krax yaog yaw cheuk tix yaox ka rhawm rhi kawn pui.
22 Phan maix lauh ka pui pa daox aw tom dex sang yum,
mai nawh ca kawn mawh pa daox aw yuh nan heue.
23 Tag caw tix kia ngien ka phung yung cie maix mhawm mhawm,
kia ngien ka khawng eek brie liang cie maix mhawm mhawm,
24 mawh kheu sin ngao ang chix kaux cong yuh maix leeing ju,
pie mhok khri kawx ang pon yaox tix koe jheeng jheeng.
25 Phan rib kroh hoik pat tix hu,
rib awn ka praok gawng baox deeh lih koe,
rib klai daux tiag baox deeh kuah.
26 Maix chix tix kip hauk yung yuh sibex kah,
teei mau pa coh kawn pex liag haktiex kah.
27 Kawn pex pa deuk khankix tag sang eei maix mai pui nyiex ma maix ka rawm teeh tix,
khawm bun siliex maix kawx hoik eei tix ka heue.
