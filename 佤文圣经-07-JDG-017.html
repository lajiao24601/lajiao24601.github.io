Riang pa krub Mi-cia
17
Koe pui tix kaux kok ceu kaux nawh mawh Mi-cia,
mai ot ka haktiex gawng Yi-fa-liien.
2 Nawh ah nin ka miex tix:
“Koe pui briex ka mau luah cie maix tix rheng mai tix yieh,
mai aux hoik mhong jhax lawg maix pui briex an.
Maix jhak,
mau luah an ot ka aux dee in,
hoik mawh aux pa teei hu heue.”
Miex nawh ah nin:
“Kawn aux eue,
kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka maix!”
3 Mi-cia tom teei mau luah an tix rheng mai tix yieh tawx ka miex tix.
Miex nawh ah nin:
“Son sang deeh taoh ka krax jhax lawg pa caw kawn aux tix kham,
aux gaux rhawm tix tan mau luah in ka Simiang Siyiex,
saik riang khaox pa mhawm tix mu,
plak praix om ka mau luah.
Yam in aux tawx mau luah in kix ka maix.”
4 Mi-cia teei mau luah an deeh tawx ka miex tix,
mai miex nawh teei mau luah ra yieh tawx ka pui cang mai mau tix kaux,
son sang kieh nawh teei khaox saik riang pa mhawm lih tix mu,
plak praix mai om ka mau luah.
Riang krub in tag sang deuh ot ka daux nyiex Mi-cia heue.
5 Mi-cia hoik tang koe nyiex vad cie to kaux tix,
mai baox yuh ka riang krub tang ceu khankix mai Yi-fu-teu tix pheen,
ciak kawn simex nawh tix kaux yuh cao giex kaing li.
6 aSingaix yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang nyang bax koe simiang,
pui ku kaux tang yuh ceu pa simie tix yuh heue.
7 Ka daux singaix bawg yam an kawx,
meung Yo-tax veng Paw-li-haung,
koe pui nhum ju tix kaux pa kaux Li-we;
8 nawh lih hu khaing veng Paw-li-haung,
keud tix sang sawg tix ot haktiex tang dauh tang dee.
Yam hoik greung krax hu nawh,
nawh hoek ka gawng Yi-fa-liien daux nyiex Mi-cia.
9 Mi-cia chawk nawh ah nin:
“Kaoh maix khaing dee mawx?”
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui Li-we,
kaoh khaing meung Yo-tax veng Paw-li-haung;
aux sang sawg dee ot paung tix heue.”
10 Mi-cia ah nin:
“Maix paung ot dee ot aux tin heue!
Maix yuh cao teem aux mai cao giex kaing li aux;
aux tawx maix cie mau luah tix num kao plah,
mai tawx maix ceep kraung sawm rheep.”b
11 Pui Li-we pa nhum ju in cu tix paung ot nyiex Mi-cia,
Mi-cia kia ngien nawh awm kawn simex tix.
12 Mi-cia tawx nawh yuh cao giex kaing li,
nawh tom paung ot nyiex Mi-cia.
13 Mi-cia ah nin:
“Yam in koe pui Li-we tix kaux yuh cao giex kaing li cie aux,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh aux haok huan tete heue.”
