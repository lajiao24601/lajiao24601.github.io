Rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex cao taoh
146
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
khuan aux eue,
maix caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
2 Ka daux singaix yam kawn im ju aux,
tag rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex aux leeing ju leeing phan;
3 paw vi een rhawm tix ka simiang piang haktiex,
paw vi een rhawm tix ka kawn pui pa chix laux chix leeing,
mai nawh ang pon taoh ka pex.
4 Phan kix yum mai hoik lih mawh phawng tiex;
krax keud krax sawn kix hoik lih mawh pang peue ngaix mawx ngaix an heue.
 
5 Pui an pa pon krax teem krax coi Siyiex Ya-keux kied koe buan son heue!
Pui pa deuh rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex nawh kied koe buan son heue!
6 aSiyiex phan yuh rao ma,
haktiex,
rawm sigrit sigra,
mai can pa koe ka daux khawm uik;
nawh giex ka lox pa ap tix kaux cong tom si dauh.
7 Nawh hoik yah tix tawx pa rauh pa see ka son pui pa gut kheem;
nawh hoik tawx priex preem ka pui pa cai rhawm cai rhi heue.
 
Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh pui pa laung pon krax pa pluat ploi,
8 hoik kieh pui dug ngai chix yaox.
Nawh yaog pui pa gut kheem kaoh;
nawh muh ka pui rauh pui see.
9 Nawh kia ngien ka pui tang ceu pa mawh nyo si nie;
nawh teem coi ka miex mai kawn doe,
viang mawh nan,
yuh laux ka krax keud pa pie lhien pui pa caox pa caig heue.
 
10 Simiang Siyiex sang mawh simiang leeing ju leeing phan;
Shi-ang eue,
Siyiex maix sang giex siawp sibrawm leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
