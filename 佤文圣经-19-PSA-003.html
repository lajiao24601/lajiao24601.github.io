Lox rawg buan krax teem coi pon ngawba
3
bSimiang Siyiex eue,
aux hoik koe ka pui koeng tom nie!
Koe pui tom nie puing ka aux!
2 Kix krai lao ka aux,
ah nin:
Siyiex ang taoh ka nawh heue!
 
3 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh dieb tie naok pa kia ngien ka aux;
maix kieh aux puing pex,
mai kieh aux pon yaog kaing tix.
4 Aux khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex taoh teem tix,
nawh pawk lox aux khaing gawng pa singax nawh heue.
 
5 Yam blauh aux tix it,
yam haing aux khaing it,
Simiang Siyiex hoik kia ngien ka aux.
6 Viang pui koeng hoek tix lai lawm aux kaing rheng kaing mheen,
aux kawx ang lhat heue.
 
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoek heue!
Siyiex aux eue,
taoh aux heue!
Maix lauh ka kap can pui pa koeng laux khawm uik,
laoh ka rhang pui pa caox pa caig.
8 Krax pa puing pex hoek khaing Simiang Siyiex dee an;
kieh nawh tawx buan ka piang pui cie tix heue!
