Kaing mhawm pa yuh Ya-ha-sui-lu mai Maw-ti-kai
10
Simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu yeen ka pui baoh rawm sigrit sigra mai pui haktiex yuh kaing.
2 Kaing ting lhaong pa yuh nawh khawm uik,
mai kaing pa yaog nawh Maw-ti-kai,
hoik tiiem tix ka daux lai simiang cie Paw-see mai Mi-ti-yax.
3 Sibrawm pui Yo-thaix pa mawh Maw-ti-kai iak ngie khaing simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu;
mai pon krax pa nab yam yung yi paox ceu paox cad tix;
mawh kheu yuh nawh kaing pa mhawm tang paox ceu paox cad tix,
mai teem nang kawn saux kawn sex kix pon krax nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.
