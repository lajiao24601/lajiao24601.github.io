Yaw-a-see simiang pui Yo-tax
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 24.1–6)
12
Yam giex Yie-hu simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ka kaing daux aliah num,
Yaw-a-see mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
Nawh hoik giex kaing ka piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tipon num.
Miex nawh pa kaux Si-pi-yax mawh pui veng Piie-see-pa.
2 Ka daux ju Yaw-a-see hoik yuh kaing pa khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik sidiex gau cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax ka nawh.
3 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang nyang bax yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak,
kawn pui ca kawn yuh krax tan gleeing nyawng bhawng dee an heue.
4 aYaw-a-see khrawm ka cao giex kaing li khankix hoek,
nhie kix tawx mau pa tan lu son pa yuh krax tan ka nyiex buan,
mawh mau pa tawx son krax pa yie yuh pui jheeng jheeng ang ah,
mawh mau pa tang gaux rhawm pui tix tan lu ang ah,
tag caw tix deuh priex mhawm.
5 Cao giex kaing li ku kaux caw tix tang priex ka mau pa tawx can pui pa yuh nawh kaing,
yam sang siao lo kah,
mai teei mau in kix kieh mawh pa cox sum son nyiex buan heue.
6 Viang mawh nan,
hoek yam giex Yaw-a-see ka kaing daux tix nga loe num,
cao giex kaing li khankix ang bax sum ka nyiex buan.
7 Kheu nan,
Yaw-a-see kok Yie-heu-yie-tax mai cao giex kaing li tang kaux,
mai chawk kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex ang bax cox sum ka nyiex buan?
Jauh yam in,
ang lai pon tix deuh ka mau pa hoik khrawm pex hoek,
tag caw tix tawx mau in hoek,
son sang kieh kaing pon yuh tix mhawm mhawm.”
8 Cao giex kaing li khankix cu kah,
mai ang lai baox khrawm ka mau,
mai ka kaing pa cox sum nyiex buan kawx ang lai kuang ka heue.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax teei teug hoek tix mu,
ka piang dawb teug toh daux koe ka tix mu,
mai deuh ot ka praok khu dee tan,
ka praok plak dawm dee laig ka daux nyiex buan.
Cao giex kaing li pa pau dee laig ka diang siviex,
teei mau pa tawx pui pa hoek tix krub khankix,
mai sau laig ka daux teug an khawm uik.
10 Mau pa ot daux teug kied nie,
cao tiiem lai daux nyiex haw simiang mai cao giex kaing li pa ting tom hoek,
teei tix ceed ka mau an,
khaix an ciang tix yaox ka been cien mau anb.
11 Khaix hoik tiiem kix tix yaox ka been koe miex ding sidawng,
kix tom teei tix tawx mau in ka pui cao kaing pa cox sum ka nyiex buan.
Pui in kix tom tawx mau riang cang mai pa yuh khaox,
mai pui yuh kaing,
12 cang mai yuh glaox,
mai cang mai pa yuh simaox,
khaix an mai liag ka khaox,
mai simaox pa sang siao tix,
khaix an tawx mau kraung tang ceu pa siao sum nyiex buan khawm uik heue.
13 Viang mawh nan,
mau in kix ang siao ka pa yuh kawk mau,
klong,
teet tawng,
si kiep ngu,
mai ka kraung khri tang ceu,
14 mai siao ngie ka pa tawx riang bix pui mai pa liag ka kraung pa cox sum son nyiex buan khankix.
15 cPui cao kaing pa kuang kaing pa cox nyiex buan khankix hoik mawh pui pa mawh pa meh tete,
mai ang lai siao lo tix sawn sidawng tau mai kix.
16 dMau Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud mai mau Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap ang lai siao sau laig daux teug in;
mau in kix hoik mawh cie cao giex kaing li heue.
17 Singaix bawg yam an,
simiang Shui-li-yax pa kaux Ha-shiiex haok puing ka veng Cia-theux,
hoik pon kah,
mai kix tom but rhawm tix sang puing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
18 Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-a-see teei khawng phak pa hoik tan lu tax du nawh simiang Yaw-sa-fa,
simiang Yaw-lang,
mai simiang Ya-ha-shiex khankix ka Simiang Siyiex,
khawm pa hoik tan lu to kaux nawh,
khawm khri pa koe ka daux nyiex buan mai nyiex haw simiang khawm uik,
kieh mawh khawng phak tawx ka simiang Ha-shiiex,
Ha-shiiex tom viex pui naing tix lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
19 Kaing yuh simiang Yaw-a-see tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik heue.
20-21 Siliex simiang Yaw-a-see tieh vheen;
ka daux kix pui ra kaux,
tix kaux mawh kawn simex See-mi-ya pa kaux Yaw-sha-cia,
an tix kaux mawh kawn simex Saw-mawx pa kaux Yaw-sa-pa.
Ka daux krax plak Si-la,
mai daux nyiex tix lhang,
pa mawh dee klawk tiex plak lih singaix ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung dee an,
kiex mox tix pauh ka Yaw-a-see.
Yaw-a-see si paung tix ot tau mai si meeig pui daux nyiex ma simiang ka veng Ta-wue;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
