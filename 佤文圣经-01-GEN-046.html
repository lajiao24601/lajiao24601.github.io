Ya-keux viex pui nyiex ma tix hu ka Ai-cix
46
Ya-keux viex kraung pa koe tix khawm uik hoek ka Piie-see-pa;
nawh yuh krax tan lu mai krub ka Siyiex keeing nawh Yi-sha dee an heue.
2 Daux sawm an,
Siyiex si kah tau mai nawh ka daux riang su sai.
Siyiex kok nawh:
“Ya-keux!
Ya-keux!”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Mawh,
aux ot dee in!”
3 Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux mawh Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex keeing maix.
Paw lhat paw graung sang hu maix Ai-cix.
Aux tag kieh kawn praix maix mawh ceu pui pa ting pa lhaong daux haktiex an.
4 Aux tag sang hu tau mai maix ka Ai-cix,
tag sang baox deeh viex kawn praix maix deeh hoek ka daux haktiex in.
Bawg yam yum maix,
to kaux Yaw-seux tag yaox tix mawh cao si beet ngai maix heue.”
5 Ya-keux lih hu khaing Piie-see-pa.
Kawn simex nawh khankix kieh keeing tix Ya-keux,
khawm kawn nyawm khankix mai moeng kix ngawm daux ling law pa hoik tawx simiang Ai-cix.
6 aKix viex khawng brie eei tix mai kraung khrai pa hoik pon tix daux haktiex meung Cia-nang mai hu ka Ai-cix.
Ya-keux viex can khawm doeh ceu cad pa koe mai kix hu ka Ai-cix.
7 Viex khawm kawn simex tix,
mai kawn saux kawn sex,
kawn bun,
mai doeh ceu cad tix.
8 Ceu kaux pui Yi-seu-liex pa hu tau mai Ya-keux ka daux Ai-cix ot plak greem:
kawn ai Ya-keux pa mawh Lui-piien mai
9 kawn simex nawh Ha-nawx,
Fa-lux,
Si-see-loeng mai Cia-mi.
10 Kawn simex tang kaux Si-miien mai kawn simex nawh Yie-mu-li,
Ya-ming,
A-sia,
Ya-cing,
Saw-sia,
mai Sao-law pa keh khaing bun Cia-nang.
11 Li-we mai kawn simex nawh Keu-soeng,
Keu-siax mai Mi-la-lix.
12 Yo-tax mai kawn simex nawh See-la,
Fa-lue-see mai Shie-la.
Yo-tax kawn tang koe kawn simex ra kaux Eux mai Eu-nang hoik yum daux haktiex Cia-nang,
kawn simex Fa-leu-see mawh Si-see-loeng mai Ha-mu-leux.
13 Yi-sa-cia mai kawn simex nawh Thaw-la,
Phu-wa,
Yaw-paw mai Seung-loeng.
14 Si-pu-loeng mai kawn simex nawh Si-liex,
Yi-loeng mai Ya-lix.
15 Plak piang in kix mawh Li-yax yam ot nawh Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax pa keh nawh khaing Ya-keux,
lheu khaing an kawn koe kawn bun Ti-na.
Ya-keux pa keh Li-yax kawn saux son nawh koe tix ngoe loe kaux.
16 Cia-teux mai kawn simex nawh Si-fe-yuing,
Ha-ci,
Su-ni,
Yi-see-peung,
Yi-lix,
Ya-law-ti mai Ya-lie-lix.
17 Ya-seux mai kawn simex nawh Ying-na,
Yi-see-wa,
Yi-see-we,
Pi-li-yax mai pux o kix mawh Si-la.
Kawn simex Pi-li-yax mawh Si-piie mai Ma-ciex.
18 Plak piang in kix mawh pa tawx La-pang kawn bun tix Li-yax bun siliex Si-phax pa keh kawn saux son Ya-keux,
khawm uik koe kao liah kaux.
19 Le-ciiex moeng Ya-keux keh kawn simex son nawh ra kaux,
mawh Yaw-seux mai Piien-ya-ming.
20 bYam ot Yaw-seux ka haktiex Ai-cix,
nawh koe kawn Ma-na-si mai Yi-fa-liien khaing kawn bun cao giex kaing li Paw-thi-fe-la,
pui veng Si-li-phaw-li pa kaux Ya-si-na.
21 Kawn simex Piien-ya-ming mawh Pi-la,
Pi-cie,
Ya-seu-piie,
Ci-la,
Nai-mang,
Yi-si,
Law-seux,
Mu-phing,
Hu-phing mai Ya-leux.
22 Plak piang in kix mawh kawn saux kawn sex Le-ciiex mai Ya-keux,
khawm uik koe kao pon kaux heue.
23 Tang mai kawn simex nawh Hu-seung;
24 Na-fu-tha-lix mai kawn simex nawh Ya-shiie,
Ku-ni,
Yie-seux mai See-leung.
25 Plak piang in kix mawh kawn simex Pi-la pa mawh kawn cau bun pa tawx La-pang ka kawn bun tix Le-ciiex pa kaux Pi-la keh kawn son Ya-keux aliah kaux heue.
26 Can kawn saux pa hu tau mai Ya-keux ka Ai-cix.
Ang nyang sawn ka mun nawh,
koe tix gliah liah kaux.
27 cBaox sawn kawn simex Yaw-seux ra kaux pa keh ka daux Ai-cix,
daux nyiex ma Ya-keux ka daux Ai-cix khawm uik koe atigliah kaux heue.
Ya-keux mai pui daux nyiex nawh hoek daux haktiex Ai-cix
28 Ya-keux ploi Yo-tax hu sivoe tix ka Yaw-seux tan,
son sang yaox paox tix daux krax Keu-sang.
Yam hoek kix ka haktiex Keu-sang,
29 Yaw-seux khre ling law mai hu rieb ka keeing tix.
Kix taug yaox paox tix,
Yaw-seux tom kawm keeing tix mai yiiem rheung nyao heue.
30 Ya-keux ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Yam in aux hoik yaox ka im ju maix,
phan aux yum aux hoik vhah rhawm ka heue.”
31 Khaix an,
Yaw-seux ah nin ka pux ke tix khankix mai can pui daux nyiex ma keeing tix:
“Aux sang hu krai ka simiang,
pux ke aux khankix mai can pui pa ot daux nyiex keeing aux hoik kaoh khaing daux haktiex Cia-nang hoek ka dee in.
32 Aux sang krai ka simiang,
pex mawh pui liang,
mai yuh kaing pa eei khawng brie pex hoik viex yung,
moi,
mai khawng brie can sin ngao pa koe tix hoek dee in.
33 Simiang kok tix chawk ka pex yuh pex kaing jhaw pa tix,
34 pex tag krai ka nawh,
pex awm tax tu tix,
yuh kaing pa liang khawng brie.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom tag kieh pex ot ka haktiex Keu-sang.”
Yaw-seux ah yuh nin mawh kheu ang pui Ai-cix sax lai ling mai ceu pui pa liag khawng brie heue.
