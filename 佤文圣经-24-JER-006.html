Yie-lu-sa-leung kham krax pa lai lawm pui koeng
6
Pui Piien-ya-ming eue,
to mox tix klawd ju heue!
Nied tix to lih khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung heue!
Pex tag caw tix phlu teet ka Thi-keu-yax,
mai gleeing ngu cap ka Paw Ha-ci-ling,
son sang kieh pui tawng ka heue!
Krax laux krax leeing pa ting tag sang khran lih hoek khaing haktiex plak laong heue.
2 Veng Shi-ang viang kied ting kied mhawm kawx,
viang mawh nan,
tag sang laux leeing heue!
3 Simiang meung ka piang haktiex tag sang ploi pui naing cie kix hu,
tag sang tang sum bang cie tix kaux mawx kaux an ot hawt siviex veng.
4 Kix tag sang tawx lox ah nin:
“Rien khre tix puing ka veng heue!
Puing ka daux bawg yam hoek greung ngawb!”
Khaix an kix baox ah nin:
“Ang lai mawh heue!
Praix hoik lha,
pa faik praix hoik hoek,
pui naing ang lai tien preeng tix hu.
5 Ex tag sang caw tix puing veng yam pon sawm,
mai yuh laux ka nyiex pa tieb tie kix heue.”
6 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik tawx lox ka cao kaing pui naing,
mai nhie kix kid khaox,
pum yuh mawh tab tiex,
lai lawm puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Nawh ah nin:
“Veng in hoik naog ka krax pie lhien,
krax gut krax kheem,
aux tag sang ciyang kah.
7 Awm rawm dung pa hoik kieh rawm si ngaong buing tix lih yuh nan,
veng in ca yuh ka phi map ang jud ang jai.
Aux mhong ka lox pa laux leeing cau nyhau lih khaing daux veng;
aux kawx hoik yaox ka jhawm saux pa kin mai ka rhoi khaix mat.
8 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
pex tag caw tix chix priex ka rhawm rhi tix kheu krax pa kha rhawm in kix.
Phan ang mawh nan,
aux tag sang tik vud ka pex,
mai kieh veng ot pex riem,
ang koe pui pa ot ka daux heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex pa lieh lox
9 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Pui koeng tag sang hoek tix con cam ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
awm pui pa pieh ka plix siiet hoik uik pieh ka singax khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
yam kawn koe bawg yam,
maix nied tix taoh ka pui pa kawn deuk khankix heue.”
10 Aux ah nin:
“Viang aux hoik krai lox pa nhie maix aux krai ka kix kawx,
ne raw ka kix,
pui mawx sang cu tix rieb lie?
Kix kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
mai ang cu tix rieb ka lox maix.
Kix kieh lox pa nhie maix aux krai ka kix mawh krax klieh krax nyiah.
11 Simiang Siyiex eue,
krax song rhawm maix ka kix kawx hoik lih gleeing ka to kaux aux,
aux ang lai baox pon kham ka heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Teei krax pa song rhawm aux,
hieh ka piang to kaux kawn nyawm,
mai ka piang to kaux cub pui nhum ju,
pa ot ka daux laih daux cau khankix.
Mex moeng tag sang laung ot tau,
kuad song kawx ang yaox tix pheeik luan khaing.
12 aNyiex ma kix mai khawm moeng kawn kix,
haktiex brex brum kix tag sang tawx mawh cie pui tang kaux.
Aux tag sang ciyang ka pui pa ot haktiex in;
13 viang kix mawh pui ting ceu ang ah pui iak min ang ah,
hoik simie tix cie ka sin ngao pa ang rauh ang see khawm uik,
pie khawm cao jhai mai cao giex kaing li kawx,
hoik pie lhien gut kheem ka pui khankix.
14 bKix jhak ka khaix mat pui cie aux,
een ang tie mawh tid tix.
Kix ah nin:
‘Ang caox,
ang caox’,
15 Tete te lai hoik kied kin.
Kix hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig yuh nin khaw tix koe krax kaig krax tua kah,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng tix kaig tua,
iak pak ngai kah.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tag sang sideeing tix goh awm pui tang kaux yuh nan.
Yam ciyang aux ka kix,
kix tag ang lai pon chuh tix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex tieb tie ka daux krax cie Siyiex
16 Simiang Siyiex ne ka pui cie tix ah nin:
“Pex tag caw lam lia tix cung ka gah krax.
Tag caw chawk tix yaox ka krax prim an kix,
mai si kaw tix yaox ka krax pa blawn mhawm an koe dee mawx.
Hu ka krax an,
pex tag sang pon ka krax pa nhim heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang chix heue!
Yix ang hu ka krax an heue.”
17 Simiang Siyiex baox tawx cao pau son kix,
mawh cao pa nghiet tix mhong ka lox pa so pui haing son kix,
viang mawh nan,
kix ah nin:
“Yix ang nghiet ka heue!”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Meung ka piang haktiex eue,
pex bre tix jhak mhawm mhawm ka ceu pa sang hoek ka piang pui cie aux heue!
19 Haktiex eue,
nghiet heue!
Kheu lieh pui cie aux lox,
aux tag sang ploi krax laux leeing lih hoek ka piang kix;
mawh kheu tieb tie kix ka lox pa sidiex gau aux,
mai ang nghiet ka lox pa krai aux.
20 Kix tan lu ka sidah bhawng Law-ban See-pa ka aux,
viex sidah bhawng kaoh khaing dee singai hoek tawx ka aux,
aux ang yuh tid tix ka heue!
Aux ang rieb ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing kix,
ang khaw rhawm ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie kix.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh pui in kix sideeing tix goh.
Keeing mai kawn tag sang yum,
paox plawg paox nyawm kawx tag sang laux leeing khawm uik heue.”
Pui tud pui rhim pa kaoh khaing haktiex plak laong
22 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Koe ceu pui tix ceu pa kaoh khaing haktiex plak laong;
mai mawh meung pa ting riang pa koe dee singaix tix meung,
rien tix sang yuh naing.
23 Kix teei te teei vaig rien khre son to kaux tix;
kix kied cau nyhau.
Kix brug ka brung pa kied brax,
awm lox si num rawm pa puk pak yuh nan.
Kix rien khre tix sang puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.”
24 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
“Yix mhong ka lox naing,
mai taix caong yix hoik awn.
Yix kied laux rhawm tete,
awm bun pa saux yha yam sang keh tix yuh nan.
25 Yix ang pon lih hu plak praix veng,
ka daux laih kawx ang pon laig;
kheu ku dauh ku dee hoik mawh pui koeng pa ot ka khawm uik,
sivie siveng hoik naog ka krax pa lhat pa graung heue.”
26 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka pui cie tix:
“Pex ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
mai lu lieh tix daux nyaox heue.
Pex tag sang laux rhawm yiiem ngia,
awm pui pa yum kawn pa koe tix kaux khaing yuh nan,
mawh kheu cao pa yuh laux tag sang puing ka pex heue.
27 Yie-li-mi eue,
maix tag caw tix cheuk tix yaox ka pui cie aux,
awm raug pui khri ka daux ngu yuh nan.
28 Kix hoik mawh pui tieh vheen pa kiien rhawm,
hoik kiien rhawm awm tawng awm rhiem yuh nan,
kix krai lox pie ku dauh ku dee,
hoik kied sidaing caox caig.
29 Viang hoik phlu bhaung lih tom ting,
glah ngu hoik kied ting cap,
mai rhiem pa tik pui kawx ang pon cia,
ang pon chuh.
Grawng pa cheuk tix yaox ka pui cie aux ang jud ang jai,
hoik mawh pa giex pa going,
mawh kheu ang bax teei tix tik singax ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig khankix.
30 Pui tag sang teei tix kieh kix mawh pa seeing pa brai,
mawh kheu aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik tik ka kix heue.”
