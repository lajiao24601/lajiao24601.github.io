Krai grawng priex preem pa tan lu ka riang rao
8
Phan mawh grawng son priex preem pa tan lu ka riang rao in,
ex ce tix mawh “pa hoik koe ngai lai grawh grawng in”.
Tete te lai,
ngai lai kieh ngie pui chix ting rhawm lhaong rhi khaing,
mawh ngie krax muh pa taug chix pon kieh pui mhawm.
2 Pui pa hoik eei tix tawng ka ku ceu ku cawng,
tete te lai,
nawh kawn ang bax tawng ka ceu pa khaw nawh tix tawng khankix.
3 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa muh ka Siyiex khankix,
hoik mawh pa tawng Siyiex ka heue.
4 Kheu nan,
phan mawh son ih priex preem pa tan lu pui ka riang rao grawng in,
ex hoik tawng,
riang rao ang pon tang ka son ceu pa hoik koe tete khankix;
ex kawx hoik tawng,
Siyiex koe ngie tix kaux khaing.
5 Viang mawh ka piang rao ma mai si nax kawn pui kawx hoik krai “siyiex” yuh nan (“siyiex” pa koe tom nie mai “simiang” pa koe tom nie),
6 viang mawh nan,
phan mawh ka ex,
Siyiex koe ngie tix kaux khaing,
pa mawh keeing Siyiex,
cao pa phan yuh ku ceu khawm uik;
ex hoik mawh pa im son nawh.
Khaix an,
simiang koe ngie tix kaux khaing pa mawh Ye-sux Khrit;
ku ceu khawm uik hoik phan yuh tix ka daux nawh,
mai ex kawx hoik im ka daux nawh heue.
7 Kheu nan,
ang mawh pui ku kaux pa koe ngai lai.
Koe pui tix blah hoik meed hawt tix krub ka riang rao,
mawh kheu nan tom hoek singaix yam in,
yam ih ka priex preem ceu in ku bawg,
mai hoik awm ih ka priex preem pa tan lu ka riang rao yuh nan.
Rhawm rhi kix hoik dawg mawh pa iak riang,
nie baox ih ka priex preem ceu in,
mai hoik tawng grawng chix seeing brai yuh tix.
8 Tete te lai,
priex preem ang chix pon tieh loh krax pa si doe ex tix tau mai Siyiex;
phan ang ih ang tie chix laux leeing kah,
phan ih ang tie chix pon ceu pa tid tix ka heue.
9 Viang mawh nan,
tag caw tix rien rhawm tix heue,
paw vi teei krax pa bheeik luan,
kieh pui pa iak krax yum rhawm lud.
10 Phan koe pui pa iak riang rhawm rhi ka grawng in,
yaox grawng pa ih nyaux maix pui in kix pa “kup ka ngai lai” ka daux nyiex vad riang rao,
in khex ang mawh maix pa kieh nawh hu ih ka priex preem pa tan lu ka riang rao ang mawh lie?
11 Phan mawh nan,
kawn Khrit pa iak krax yum rhawm in kix kawx,
pa hoik mawh pui pa yum Khrit son nawh,
tag sang laux leeing kheu ngai lai pa koe maix heue!
12 Phan pex yuh nin,
hoik phit lud ka kawn Khrit,
hoik kieh rhawm rhi pa iak kix laux,
hoik mawh pa phit lud ka Khrit.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
phan priex preem kieh kawn Khrit phit lud,
aux tag ang lai baox ih ka nex,
son ang sang kieh kawn Khrit lud heue.
