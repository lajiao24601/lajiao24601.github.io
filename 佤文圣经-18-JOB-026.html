26
Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
2 Maix hoik tawx krax teem krax coi ka aux pa mawh pui pa iak riang iak rao in tete te lai!
Maix hoik taoh aux pa mawh pui pa iak riang rao in tom nie!
3 Awm aux pa mawh pui pa daox aw yuh nin,
maix kawn tawx krax sidiex gau pa ting ngawh ka aux!
4 Khaix an maix keud,
pui mawx sang chix nghiet ka lox sidiex gau maix lie?
Mawh pui mawx pa paoh rhawm rhi maix krai ka lox pa yuh nin lih lie?
 
[Pi-leu-ta ah lox tix:a]
5 Rawm pa koe greem tiex mai to sat pa koe ka daux khankix hoik yaoh yee;
khuan ngao kix hoik si yaung si kua.
6 Meung yum hoik lih jhi tix ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex;
krax yum kawx ang lai pon tawb kheem.
7 Siyiex tawm rao ma ka daog plak laong,
mai teuk teue haktiex ot ka daux rao ma.
8 Siyiex kieh rawm laig ka daux pai awm,
ang kieh pai awm laux kheu pa cien rawm.
9 Nawh tawb kheem ka pak ngai pa naog mhawm riang khix,
tawm pai awm ot ka piang.
10 Nawh pawd rhoi si dawm ka piang rawm sigrit sigra,
kieh pa riang mai pa faik khan tix tang ot.
11 Yam ne raw nawh ka rong joh rao ma,
kix hoik si yaung si kua.
12 Siawp sibrawm pa ting nawh pon kieh rawm sigrit sigra yien yawg,
krax pa chix pa tawng nawh lauh goh ka La-ha-paw to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui kah.
13 Nawh phlu bhawm tix si ghrah ka rao ma singax;
taix nawh hoik pauh ka to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui ka yaog tix to.
14 Kheu nan,
in hoik mawh ngie siawp sibrawm pa iak pa cheu Siyiex tix yuh khaing,
mawh ngie lox pa iak pa lied pa mhong ex.
Pui mawx pon tiag tix yaox ka pa ting riang siawp sibrawm Siyiex lie?
