Yaw-seux mai moeng Paw-thi-fa
39
Pui Yi-seu-ma-lix viex Yaw-seux hu ka Ai-cix,
mai coh nawh ka Paw-thi-fa pa mawh cao giex kaing lien Ai-cix.
2 aSimiang Siyiex ot tau mai Yaw-seux,
kieh kaing jhaw pa yuh nawh mhawm ku ceu.
Nawh ot ka nyiex cao simiang tix haktiex Ai-cix;
3 simiang hoik yaox pa ot Simiang Siyiex tau mai nawh,
kieh kaing pa yuh nawh haok huan khawm uik.
4 Paw-thi-fa muh ka Yaw-seux,
kieh nawh mawh pui cau tix,
kuang ka kaing nyiex ma nawh mai pa koe nawh khawm uik.
5 Jauh bawg yam an,
kheu jao Yaw-seux,
Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka daux nyiex pui Ai-cix;
can pa ot daux nyiex nawh mai kaung ma nawh khawm uik hoik pon buan.
6 Paw-thi-fa ap can ceu pa koe tix khawm uik daux tawm taix Yaw-seux,
lheu khaing priex pa ih nawh,
nawh ang lai kuang tix ceu kawx heue.
Yaw-seux kied mhawm kaux hak,
koe grang jiet nyawm tix jhak;
7 ang rheeng,
moeng simiang nawh ghoing rhawm ka nawh,
sang it tau mai nawh.
8 Yaw-seux thiang kah,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix keud,
simiang aux hoik ap can pa koe tix khawm uik ka aux,
kieh aux kuang,
aux ot tin,
nawh ang lai taug ka kaing nyiex ma tix.
9 Ka daux nyiex ma in,
aux koe akhuang awm nawh,
lheu khaing maix,
nawh ang koe ceu pa ang tawx ka aux.
Mawh kheu mawh maix moeng nawh.
Aux doh sang chix yuh kaing pa ang khaw yuh nin,
phit lud ka Siyiex lie?”
10 Viang bun simiang tix hoik nyaw nawh ka lox ku ngaix,
Yaw-seux thiang ka tom si dauh,
ang it tau mai nawh heue.
11 Koe tix ngaix,
Yaw-seux laig yuh kaing daux nyiex,
yam an,
siliex daux nyiex kix ang ot tix kaux kawx.
12 Bun simiang giex sibex dha Yaw-seux,
ah nin:
“Hoek it tau mai aux!”
Yaw-seux to khaing nawh lih plak praix,
sibex dha ot ka daux taix nawh.
13 Nawh yaox prah Yaw-seux sibex dha tix lih to plak praix,
14 tom kok pui siliex pa ot ka daux nyiex tix hoek,
ah nin ka kix:
“Pex jhak,
pui Si-paw-lai pa viex mex aux hoek sang yuh pa ang khaw ka aux.
Nawh laig ka lawk dee it aux,
sang it tau mai aux.
Aux tom ploi lox riag yo tix;
15 nawh mhong lox riag yo aux,
tom tik sibex dha tix ka praok aux,
to lih plak praix heue.”
16 Bun an deuh sibex dha Yaw-seux ka tix;
simiang Yaw-seux hoek,
17 nawh krai grawng in ka mex tix:
“Siliex pui Si-paw-lai pa viex maix hoek an laig ka lawk dee it aux,
yuh pa ang khaw ka aux.
18 Aux riag yo,
nawh tom prah sibex dha tix praok aux mai to lih plak praix heue.”
19 Simiang Yaw-seux mhong ka lox krai moeng tix pa ah nin:
“Siliex maix yuh pa ang khaw ka aux yuh nin” son lox an,
kied song rhawm kah,
20 kieh pui giex ka Yaw-seux,
laung nawh ka daux thawng cie simiang;
Yaw-seux tom ot ka daux thawng tan.
21 bViang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai Yaw-seux,
mai tawx buan ka nawh;
mawh kheu nan,
cao kuang thawng kied muh ka nawh,
22 tawx nawh kuang ka pui pa ot daux thawng tang kaux,
khawm kaing ka daux thawng ku ceu.
23 Ka kaing pa kuang Yaw-seux daux thawng ku ceu,
cao kuang thawng ang lai suk sag rhawm kah;
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai Yaw-seux,
kieh nawh mhawm kaing yuh ku ceu heue.
