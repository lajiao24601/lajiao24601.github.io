Lai Fe-li-meung
Krai dawt
Fe-li-meung mawh kawn Khrit pa koe min tix kaux, nawh tee mawh paox grawm ceu cao Keu-law-si. Nawh kawx mawh nang siliex cawng ka simiang A-ni-si-mo. A-ni-si-mo to hu khaing simiang tix dee an, mai lai ling ka Pao-law pa laung ot daux thawng. A-ni-si-mo lih mawh kawn Khrit ka daux pa yuh paox pie mai Pao-law. Pao-law tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka Fe-li-meung, mai hu tawx A-ni-si-mo deeh ing ka nawh dee an, khiox khaw nawh va rhawm ka siliex cawng in, mai gaux rhawm ka sang ing A-ni-si-mo, ang joeng pluat ploi ka siliex cawng pa lih hu in, tag sang kia ngien nawh awm pux aik ka daux Khrit nang heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1–3)
2. Yaog yaw ka Fe-li-meung (4–7)
3. Khiox khaw son A-ni-si-mo (8–22)
4. Lox pa nghoik (23–25)
