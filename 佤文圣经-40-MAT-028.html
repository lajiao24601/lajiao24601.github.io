Pa kaoh im Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 16.1–10; Lai Lu-cia 24.1–12; Lai Yaw-hang 20.1–10)
28
Khaix hoik luan singaix tuah,
ka daux singaix aliah yam jauh praix tix riang,
Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la mai Ma-li-yax tang kaux hu kia si meeig tau mai kix.
2 Dum mawx dum an,
koe kla reem ting tok,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex lih hoek khaing rao ma,
mai yoi tix tik ka simaox,
ngawm ka piang.
3 Pak ngai nawh toh riang awm pu plak praix,
kraung ceep nawh paing awm rawm rha.
4 Pui naing pa pau yaoh yee mai si yaung khawm uik,
awm pui yum yuh nan heue.
5 Kawn cau Siyiex an krai ka bun khankix ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat graung,
aux hoik tawng sawg pex ka Ye-sux kaw pa tawk ka piang khaox lhak an.
6 Nawh ang ot dee in,
hawt awm lox pa krai nawh,
nawh hoik kaoh im.
Pex hoek tix jhak nawh ka dee deuh nawh.
7 Pex nied tix hu krai ka pui gau nawh khankix ah nin:
‘Nawh hoik kaoh im khaing krax yum,
nawh tag sang ran hoek sivoe pex ka Cia-li-lix;
ka dee an,
pex tag sang yaox ka nawh!’
Tag caw tix priex lox pa krai aux ka pex heue.”
8 Bun miex khankix tom nied lih hu khaing si meeig,
daox deng nang gaux rhawm nang,
mai to hu krai ka pui gau nawh khankix heue.
9 Dum mawx dum an,
Ye-sux jhi kix yaox tix ka daux krax,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Kieh pa nhim koe tau mai pex heue!”
Kix hoek dex nawh,
mai kawm caong nawh krub ka nawh.
10 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Paw vi lhat graung,
hu krai ka pux aik aux,
nhie kix hu ka haktiex Cia-li-lix;
kix tag sang yaox aux ka dee an heue.”
Lox pa hoek cao pau tix krai
11 Yam kawn lawng bun miex khankix krax,
koe lien tix blah pa pau ka si meeig ing daux veng,
mai krai grawng kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh in kix khawm uik ka simiang cao giex kaing li.
12 Khaix hoik rom simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song tix kum kawng paox tix,
kix teei tix tawx mau tom nie ka lien khankix,
13 mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw tix krai yuh nin,
yam pon sawm pui gau Ye-sux khankix hoek,
mai jawm yam laig it pex briex ka to kaux nawh hu.
14 Phan cao kuang kaing hoik tawng grawng kaing in,
ex tom tag lih tix krai ka lox in,
ang doh hoek piang pex heue.”
15 Lien rieb ka mau,
mai hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawm nawh yuh nan.
Lox pa pie lhien in hoek tom yam in pui ca kawn krai lao hu ka pui Yo-thaix heue.
Ye-sux tawx gum lox tix hu
(Lai Ma-kheu 16.14–18; Lai Lu-cia 24.36–49; Lai Yaw-hang 20.19, 23)
16 aPui gau kao tix kaux hoek ka haktiex Cia-li-lix,
ka piang gawng pa hoik tawm Ye-sux nhie kix hoek ka an.
17 Yam yaox kix ka Ye-sux,
kix tom krub ka nawh khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
kawn koe pui pa ang yum rhawm kah.
18 Ye-sux hu dex ka kix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Siyiex hoik tawx akhuang khaing rao ma mai ka daux kawn pui khawm uik ka aux.
19 bMawh kheu nan,
pex hu,
kieh kawn pui piang haktiex mawh pui gau aux,
cub com kix ka daux ceu kaux keeing,
kawn,
Khuan Singax Singeh,
20 mai sidiex gau kix priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa hoik tawm aux ka pex khawm uik.
Priex tix tawng heue!
Aux tag sang ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng,
tom hoek si dauh haktiex heue.”
