Lai Mi-cia
Krai dawt
Cao jhai Mi-cia mawh ka daux bawg yam cao jhai Yi-sai-yax tau; nawh hoek khaing yaong iak pa koe ka meung Yo-tax plak seh. Mi-cia yum rhawm grawng sang si pup meung Yo-tax krax laux krax leeing, awm hoik grox krai A-maw-see sang si pup meung plak laong krax laux leeing yuh nan; in hoik mawh grawng pa awm paox, Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka pui pa ang rauh ang see. Viang mawh nan, gum lox pa sidiex gau cao jhai Mi-cia nie blawn krai sidawng ka krax keud pot jun pot pa tag sang hoek heue.
Lai in koe gum lox tix blah nie kieh ex chix een tix tawng pa yuh nin: awm pa hoik tiiem ka grawng pa giex Siyiex kaing ka krax pa nhim pa tiiem piang haktiex (4.1-4); grox krai nyiex ma Ta-wue simiang ting tag sang lih koe tix kaux, mai kieh meung haktiex pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem tom rheeng (5.2-4); koe lox pa ao min tix gum, hoik rom tix krai ka gum lox cao jhai pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin: “Pa khiox khaw nawh mawh: tawx krax pa rauh see, yuh lih ka krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix, hu tau mai Siyiex ex ka daux krax pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi heue.” (6.8)
Krai kaing
1. Krax pa prawng ca ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax (1.1–3.12)
2. Krax haok huan mai pa nhim pa tiiem (4.1–5.15)
3. Krax pa ne raw mai gum lox krax keud pot jun pot (6.1–7.20)
