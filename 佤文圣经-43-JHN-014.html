Ye-sux mawh daux krax mai pa mawh pa meh mai ju
14
Ye-sux baox ah nin ka kix:
“Pex paw vi koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi;
tag caw tix yum rhawm ka Siyiexa,
ka aux kawx yum rhawm kah.
2 Ka daux nyiex keeing aux dee paung hoik koe ka tom nie,
aux hu mawh son sang rien khre dee ot son pex;
phan ang mawh nan,
aux tag ang krai lox in.b
3 Khaix hoik hu aux rien khre dee ot son pex,
aux tag sang deeh hoek tix,
mai rieb pex hoek ka dee ot aux dee an,
son sang kieh pex ot tau mai aux ka dee an.
4 Ka dee sang hu aux,
krax an pex hoik tawng ka heue.”
5 To-ma krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
yix ang tawng dee sang hu maix,
yix sang tawng ka krax an yuh ka mawx lie?”
6 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh daux krax,
mai pa mawh pa meh,
mai ju heue;
phan ang mawh ka daux aux,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon hoek ka Keeing dee an.
7 Kheu hoik tawng pex ka aux,
ka keeing aux kawx pex tag sang tawng kah.c
Jauh yam in pex hoik tawng ka nawh,
kheu nan mai hoik yaox ka nawh heue.”
8 Fe-lix krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
jhi ex yaox ka Keeing,
yix tom taug naog rhawm ka heue.”
9 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Fe-lix,
aux hoik ot tau mai pex tom rheeng,
maix kawn ang bax tawng yung ka aux lie?
Pui pa yaox ka aux hoik mawh yaox ka keeing.
Yuh ka mawx maix lai kawn ah nin ‘Jhi yix yaox ka keeing’ lie?
10 Aux hoik ot ka daux ju keeing,
keeing hoik ot ka daux ju aux,
maix ang yum rhawm ka lie?
Lox pa krai aux ka pex ang mawh pa kaoh khaing to kaux aux,
mai hoik mawh keeing pa ot ka daux ju aux pa hoek tix yuh ka kaing cie nawh.
11 Pex tag caw tix yum rhawm ka aux,
aux hoik ot ka daux ju keeing,
keeing hoik ot ka daux ju aux;
phan ang yum rhawm ka lox in,
tag caw tix yum rhawm ka aux kheu kaing pa hoik yuh aux.
12 Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
pui pa yum rhawm ka aux kawx,
tag sang yuh kaing pa hoik yuh aux,
phan ang mawh nan,
tag sang yuh kaing ceu nie blawn ting,
mawh kheu sang hu aux ka dee ot keeing dee an.
13 Phan pex rawg ceu pa tix ka daux ceu kaux aux,
aux tag sang yuh lih mawh,
son sang kieh min plak ngai keeing jhi tix lih ka daux kawn kawn pui.
14 Phan pex rawg ceu pa tix ka daux ceu kaux aux,
aux tag sang yuh lih mawh heue.”
Ap pong tix tawx krax teem coi Khuan Singax Singeh
15 “Phan pex muh ka aux,
tag caw tix priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawm aux.
16 Aux tag sang khiox khaw keeing,
nawh tag sang tawx cao teem coi tang kaux ka pex,
mai ot tau mai pex leeing ju.
17 Nawh hoik mawh khuan pa mawh pa meh.
Kawn pui piang haktiex ang rieb ka nawh;
mawh kheu ang kix chix yaox ka nawh,
mai ang tawng yung ka nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
pex hoik tawng yung ka nawh;
mawh kheu hoik ot nawh ka daux ju pex,
mai ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng heue.
18 “Aux ang prah ghram pex mawh kawn doe;
aux tag sang baox deeh hoek ka pex dee in.
19 Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
kawn pui piang haktiex tag ang lai yaox ka aux;
viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang yaox ka aux,
mawh kheu kawn im ju aux,
pex kawx tag sang kawn im ju.
20 Ka daux singaix yam hoek aux,
pex tag sang tawng ot aux ka daux ju Keeing,
mai pex ot ka daux ju aux,
awm hoik ot aux ka daux ju pex yuh nan heue.
21 “Can pui pa rieb ka gum lox tawm aux,
mai hoik priex tix hawt,
in hoik mawh pui pa muh ka aux.
Pui pa muh ka aux,
keeing aux kawx tag sang muh ka nawh;
aux kawx muh ka nawh,
khaix an mai jhi to kaux tix ka nawh heue.”
22 Yo-tax (ang mawh Yo-tax pui Cia-liiex) chawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai jhi yix yaox tix khaing,
ang jhi kawn pui piang haktiex yaox tix lie?”
23 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pui pa muh ka aux hoik chix priex tix hawt ka gum lox aux khawm uik.
Keeing aux tag sang muh ka nawh,
kheu nan keeing aux mai aux tag sang hoek ka nawh dee an,
mai ot tau mai nawh.
24 Pui pa ang muh ka aux tag ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox aux.
Gum lox pa hoik mhong pex ang mawh pa kaoh khaing aux,
mai hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing keeing kaw pa ploi aux hoek heue.
25 “Yam kawn ot aux tau mai pex,
mai aux hoik krai gum lox in kix ka pex.
26 Viang mawh nan,
cao teem coi an,
pa mawh Khuan Singax Singeh pa tag sang ploi Keeing hoek kheu ka daux ceu kaux aux,
tag sang krai pex mhong ka kaing in khawm uik,
khaix an mai kieh pex doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai aux ka pex khawm uik heue.
27 “Aux hoik deuh tix tawx pa nhim ot tau mai pex,
aux hoik teei tix tawx pa nhim aux ka pex.
Pa tawx aux ka pex ang awm pa tawx kawn pui piang haktiex ka pex.
Pex paw vi koe krax khix rhawm khix rhi,
mai paw vi lhat graung.
28 Pex hoik mhong lox pa krai aux ah ‘aux hoik hu,
viang mawh nan kawn sang deeh hoek’.
Phan pex muh ka aux,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm kheu hoik ing aux ka keeing dee an,
mawh kheu ting nawh khaing aux,
29 Aux hoik krai pex mhong sivoe sang lih kaing in kix tix mawh,
son sang kieh pex yum rhawm ka yam lih kaing in kix tix mawh.
30 Yam in aux ang pon yaox tix krai ka pex tom nie,
mawh kheu cao giex kaing haktiex in tag sang hoek.
Nawh ang tawng ka sang yuh tix ka aux;
31 viang mawh nan,
son sang kieh kawn pui piang haktiex tawng muh aux ka keeing aux,
mawh kheu nan aux hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawm nawh khawm uik heue.
“Kaoh,
hu ex heue!”
