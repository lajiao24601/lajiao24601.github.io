5
aSimiang Saw-law-meung hoik hoik kaing nyiex buan khawm uik,
nawh tom teei khri mau pa tan lu keeing tix Ta-wue ka Simiang Siyiex mai kraung khrai tang ceu khankix,
deuh ot ka nyiex krao nyiex buan heue.
Rieb teug sijax mukheu laig daux nyiex buan
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 8.1–9)
2 bSon sang viex ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex laig ka daux nyiex buan khaing Shi-ang veng Ta-wue,
Saw-law-meung khrawm ka kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex mai cao kaing ceu pui ku cub khankix,
hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
3 Ka daux khix aliah,
pa mawh bawg yam Poi Nyiex-gox-bang,
kix khrawm tix hoek ka simiang dee an.
4 Yam hoik grum hoek kuad song ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
pui Li-we grawng ka teug sijax mukheu hoek ka nyiex buan.
5 Cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we kawx viex ka teug sijax mukheu,
nyiex gox buan man,
mai kraung pa siao khawm uik laig daux nyiex buan.
6 Simiang Saw-law-meung mai pui haoh hun Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
khrawm paox tix ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu,
mai tan lu ka moi yung kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
ceu pa tan lu in kix kied nie,
mai ang lai dak uik simin.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li khankix viex teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex laig ka daux nyiex buan,
mai deuh ot ka greem Ci-lu-paw ra mu daux nyiex pa kied singax singeh.
8 Preeik Ci-lu-paw ra mu nih tix tawb teug sijax mukheu mai khaox grawng.
9 Khaox grawng kied lang,
pui pa cung see sivoe nyiex dee pa kied singax singeh,
tom tag chix yaox ka khaox grawng khawm ra toh,
phan ot tang dauh mai ang chix yaox.
(Khaox grawng in tom hoek singaix kawn ot dee an.)
10 cKa daux teug sijax mukheu deuh ngie khlawp simaox ka ra mu khaing,
mai ang deuh kraung tang ceu kah;
khlawp simaox in ra mu hoik mawh yam lih kawn pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Ai-cix,
Simiang Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
Maw-si ot ka gawng Si-nai mai deuh khlawp simaox in ot ka daux teug sijax mukheu heue.
Min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex
11-14 deCan cao giex kaing li khawm uik,
mawh cub caw mawx ang ah,
hoik kieh to kaux tix singax,
mai grum hoek khawm uik.
Can pui Li-we pa koe beeh glawm tix yuh lox rhax krax klieh,
pa mawh Ya-sa,
Si-mang,
Yie-tu-toeng,
mai ceu pui kix hoik ceep ka kraung man keei sum.
Pui Li-we cung dex ka khu dee tan pa ot plak lih singaix,
ka daux taix kix giex ka chiang,
ting jung lah,
koe cao giex kaing li tix yieh tix nga kaux phlu teet tawng hoek cung tau mai kix.
Yam yaog yaw pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka Simiang Siyiex,
lox luah teet tawng,
lox luah chiang,
mai lox ting tang ceu khankix rom tix luah tau,
mai kied sidaing khaw tau.
Kix rhax lai ah nin:
Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem nawh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
Yam an,
pai awm naog ka daux nyiex buan,
min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex jhan riang,
kheu nan cao giex kaing li ang lai pon thung pui yuh krax krub heue.
