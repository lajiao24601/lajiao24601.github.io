Meung pa nhim
11
aKhaox khaix kit cie Yie-si tag sang lih glaoh lhax pa awn;
koe simiang khraox tix kaux tag sang lih khaing doeh ceu cad nawh.
2 Khuan Simiang Siyiex tag sang laig ka daux to kaux nawh,
tawx nawh koe ping nyia cuyi,
krax lhak krax lhiao,
tawx nawh koe krax keud krax sawn,
riang rao,
tawx nawh koe ngai lig ngai lai mai rhawm rhi pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex.
3 Krax pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex mawh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi ka nawh:
nawh ciyang ang jhak ka plak ngai pui;
nawh ang prawng ca ka lox pa mhong ka yhaok tix khaing.
4 bNawh tag sang teei pa rauh pa see teem coi ka pui hot pui sibhawm;
nawh tag sang kia ngien ka siawp sibrawm pui pa ang koe cao teem cao coi pa kha rhawm kha rhi tix.
Nawh tag sang tawx lox kieh pui ciyang ka pui phi map;
nawh tag sang pauh ka pui pa caox pa caig yum.
5 cNawh tag sang kuang ka daux pa rauh pa see;
nawh tag sang yuh kaing son pui ka daux pa kaux sijax heue.
 
6 dSox la mai kawn yung tag sang ot tau ka daux pa nhim;
sivai kaoh ku mai kawn pex noi tix it tau.
Kawn moi mai kawn sivai sang si peux tau;
kawn nyawm tag sang mawh cao pa kuang ka piang kix.
7 Moi miex mai krih miex ih tau nyaux tau;
kawn moi mai kawn krih noi tix it tau.
Sivai sang si tag sang sawm rib awm moi yuh nan.
8 Kawn ngie pa peux tag sang klieh ot mai moeng daux si uing pa koe jai;
kawn nyawm pa hoik pluat peux tawx taix tix ka moeng daux si uing pa koe jai kawx,
mai ang lai chix tuh nawh mat.
9 eKa piang gawng Shi-ang pa mawh gawng singax singeh cie Siyiex,
ang lai koe ceu pa laux leeing,
mai ang lai koe ceu pa caox caig;
awm rawm sigrit sigra pa hoik naog rawm yuh nan,
haktiex khawm uik tag sang grum naog ka krax pa tawng yung ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
Pui pa vuk vied vuk viang deeh hoek ka nyiex
10 fYam hoek ka daux singaix an,
simiang khraox tix kaux tag sang lih hoek khaing riah rawng Yie-si.
Nawh tag sang lih mawh man chix meung ka piang haktiex;
pui ka piang haktiex tag sang tawx keen ka nawh;
veng pa ting cie nawh haok huan koe min.
11 Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang baox teei ka siawp sibrawm tix kieh pui pa vuk vied vuk viang deeh hoek ka nyiex.
Kix tag sang kaoh khaing Ya-sux mai Ai-cix,
khaing Pa-theu-law,
Ku-seu,
Yi-lang,
Pa-pi-loeng,
Ha-ma,
mai haktiex hawt rawm sigrit sigra ku dauh,
khawm klawn tiex ku dauh,
mai deeh hoek ka meung haktiex tix.
12 Simiang Siyiex tag sang yaog man chix,
mai kieh meung ka piang haktiex tawng grawng pa hoik kieh nawh pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax,
pa hoik prung prang tix tang ot khawm pon praok si daix daog khrawm paox tix,
viex kix deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix.
13 Pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai baox boh rhawm ka pui Yo-tax;
mai pui Yo-tax kawx ang lai baox koeng ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
14 Kix tag sang rom paox tix,
mai puing ka pui Fe-li-see pa ot ka plak klip singaix,
mai yuh krax laux ka pui pa ot ka plak lih singaix.
Kix tag sang pon kuang ka haktiex Yi-tong mai ka haktiex Maw-ya;
pui Ya-meung tag sang cu nghiet ka lox kix.
15 gSimiang Siyiex tag sang phlu ka rawm sigrit sigra Ai-cix hit;
nawh tag sang teei bhaung pa si aoh,
phlu ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti hit,
deuk ngie rawm klawng iak aliah klawng khaing,
mai hoik kieh pui pon tiang ka khawm uik heue.
16 Tag sang koe krax ting pa si dawm tix nyaing,
son sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka haktiex Ya-sux pa deuk khankix yaox tix lawng deeh hoek,
awm hoik lih tax du pui Yi-seu-liex yam an khaing Ai-cix yuh nan heue.
