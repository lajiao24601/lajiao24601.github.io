Grawh grawng singaix tuah
(Lai Ma-kheu 2.23–28; Lai Lu-cia 6.1, 5)
12
aAng koe miex glang tix,
Ye-sux laig ka daux kaung ma nghox ca ka daux singaix tuah.
Pui gau nawh cai rhawm,
mai caik tix ih ka si gleem nghox ca khankix.
2 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah yaox kah,
mai ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Maix jhak,
pui gau maix hoik yuh kaing pa ang cu pui yuh ka daux singaix tuah heue!”
3 bYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Hoik yuh Ta-wue mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh kaing ceu pa tix yam cai rhawm tix,
ang pex bax phat ka lie?
4 cYam laig nawh ka daux nyiex buan Siyiex,
pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix hoik ih ka khao mun pa tan lu pui ka Siyiex;
an hoik mawh kaing pa lud (mawh kheu khao mun ceu an mawh ngie cao giex kaing li pa pon tix ih khaing).
5 dKhex ang pex bax phat tix yaox ka lai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si pa hoik tiiem tix deuh lie?
Ku vang tuah,
cao giex kaing li hoik yuh laux ka been singaix tuah pa hoik tat tix deuh ka daux nyiex buan,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang sawn tix yuh kaing pa lud.
6 Aux krai ka pex,
dee in koe ceu pa lheu ting ceu khaing nyiex buan.
7 ePhuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Aux sang lo ka krax pa caox rhawm,
ang mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie.’
Phan pex tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox in tete,
pex taug ang prawng ca ka pui pa ang koe dee lud.
8 Mawh kheu kawn kawn pui hoik mawh simiang ka daux singaix tuah heue.”
Pai pui pa koe jhawm saux kroh taix moi
(Lai Ma-kheu 3.1–6; Lai Lu-cia 6.6–11)
9 Ye-sux lih hu khaing haktiex an,
mai hoek ka nyiex sidiex pui Yo-tax.
10 Ka dee an koe pui tix kaux,
taix nawh hoik kroh tix khawn.
Koe pui tix blah keud tix sang sawg ka dee lud Ye-sux,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Ka daux singaix tuah pai jhawm saux pui moi lud ang lud been lie?”
11 fYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Ka daux pex phan koe pui pa koe yung tix mu,
yung in cawd daux o ka daux singaix tuah,
nawh sang yuh ka mawx lie?
Khex ang nawh bluh yung in lih lie?
12 Pui kawn lheu ting ceu khaing yung heue!
Mawh kheu nan,
ka daux singaix tuah,
phan yuh kaing pa mhawm mawh kaing pa khaw mai lox tat jhawm prawng.”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka pui an:
“Nyat taix maix rauh heue!”
Nawh nyat taix tix,
taix nawh tom moi,
mai deeh mhawm awm taix tang khawn yuh nan.
14 Pui Fa-li-saix lih hu,
keud sawn grawng sang yuh ka Ye-sux,
son sang pauh ka nawh heue.
Siliex pa hoik laug Siyiex
15 Yam mhong Ye-sux ka grawng in,
nawh lih hu khaing dee an,
mai pui tom nie hawt khaix nawh.
Nawh hoik pai ka can kix pa koe jhawm saux moi khawm uik,
16 mai tawx lox ang cu kix krai hu.
17 In hoik kieh gum lox lih tix mawh awm pa krai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax ah nin:
18 gJhak heue,
nawh hoik mawh siliex pa laug aux;
aux muh ka nawh,
khaw rhawm ka nawh.
Aux tag sang tawx khuan tix ka nawh;
nawh tag sang krai pao krax prawng ca aux ka kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik.
19 Nawh ang yuh simah,
ang riag yo,
mai ang kok yo ka daux laih daux cau.
20 Gawng priem pa sang graik nawh ang raig pot;
ngu pa sang yeet nawh ang phlu yeet.
Nawh tag sang kawn ca yuh kaing pa rauh pa see tom si dauh,
tom hoek pon pa rauh pa see krax pa pex.
21 Kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik
hoik deuh krax keud pot jun pot tix ka nawh heue!
Ye-sux mai Piie-si-phux
(Lai Ma-kheu 3.20–30; Lai Lu-cia 11.14–23)
22 Yam an,
koe pui viex pui pa laig phi daux,
pa dug ngai pa ang chix si kah hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux.
Ye-sux pai nawh moi,
mai baox kieh kix pon si kah pon yaox.
23 Pui hun kied daox deng ka khawm uik,
mai ah nin:
“Khex hoik mawh nawh kawn praix Ta-wue lie?”
24 hPui Fa-li-saix mhong ka lox in,
mai ah nin:
“Nawh pon grheu ka phi,
tee rien tix een rhawm tix ka Piie-si-phux simiang phi heue.”
25 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka ceu pa keud kix ceu pa tix,
tom ah nin ka kix:
“Meung haktiex mawx ang ah,
phan simah paox tix,
tag sang hoek krax laux leeing som nai;
veng tix mu mai nyiex tix nyiex phan simah paox tix,
tag sang laux leeing.
26 Phan Sa tan grheu ka Sa tan,
hoik mawh simah ka paox tix,
meung cie nawh sang chix kaux cong yuh ka mawh lie?
27 Phan aux een rhawm tix ka Piie-si-phux grheu ka phi,
phan mawh nan,
pui gau tix mai pex grheu ka phi,
hoik een rhawm tix ka ceu pa tix lie?
Pui gau tix mai pex tag sang yuh mhai sakse grawng pa lud pex heue!
28 Phan aux een rhawm tix ka khuan Siyiex grheu ka phi,
in hoik mawh mhai sakse grawng siawp sibrawm Siyiex pa giex kaing ka daux pex heue.
29 “Koe pui mawx pon laig daux nyiex ma pui naing pa kua,
mai teei hu ka sin ngao nawh lie?
Lheu khaing ran phrawk ka pui naing pa kua an sivoe,
tom taug chix pon tud ka nyiex ma nawh heue.
30 i“Pa ang mawh pui pa yuh paox pie mai aux,
hoik mawh pa tieb tie ka aux;
pa ang khrawm tix tau mai aux,
hoik mawh pui pa yieh ka kaing yuh aux laux heue.
31 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
phi map pa yuh kawn pui khawm uik mai lox pa map nai khawm uik chix pon krax pa pluat ploi.
Viang mawh nan,
pui pa map nai ka Khuan Singax Singeh ang pon krax pa pluat ploi;
32 jpa lud lox ka kawn kawn pui,
kawn chix pon krax pa pluat ploi.
Viang mawh nan,
pa map nai ka Khuan Singaix Singeh,
mawh singaix baux yam in ang ah,
mawh baux singaix pa sang hoek ang ah,
tag ang pon krax pa pluat ploi.”
Khaox plix mai plix
(Lai Lu-cia 6.43–45)
33 kKhaox plix mhawm,
plix kawx mhawm;
khaox plix ang mhawm,
plix kawx ang mhawm.
Khaox plix mhawm ang mhawm tag pon tix tawng khaing plix nawh.
34 lPex pa mawh jai si uing in kix,
pex hoik dawg mawh pa caox pa caig,
pex ce tix sang pon krai lox pa mhawm lih lie?
Mawh kheu pa naog ka daux rhawm rhi,
mai jheeh tag sang krai lih.
35 Pui mhawm hoik plih lih ka ceu pa mhawm pa deuh tix;
pui caox hoik plih lih ka ceu pa ang mhawm pa deuh tix heue.
36 “Aux krai ka pex,
ka daux singaix pa prawng ca,
pui ku kaux tag sang plih lih sidawng ka gum lox gie going pa krai tix.
37 Mawh kheu Siyiex tag sang teei gum lox maix prawng ca ka grawng pa koe phi map maix,
tag sang teei nang ka gum lox maix krai pao ka grawng pa ang phi map maix koe heue.”
Khiox khaw ka mhai pa daox pa am
(Lai Ma-kheu 8.11–12; Lai Lu-cia 11.29–32)
38 mYam an,
koe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri ra loe kaux mai pui Fa-li-saix krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
yix simie maix yuh yix yaox mhai pa daox pa am.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
39 n“Pui singaix baux ju in lai kied caox caig yuh nin,
ang koe krax lhat graung yung yi!
Pex lai khiox khaw aux yuh mhai pa daox pa am heue!
Ang chix,
lheu khaing mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh ka Yaw-na,
ang lai baox koe mhai pa daox pa am tang ceu pa tawx pex yaox
40 oYaw-na ot ka daux vaig kax ting loe singaix loe sawm,
kawn kawn pui kawx tag sang ot ka greem tiex dee raux loe singaix loe sawm heue.
41 pKa daux singaix pa prawng ca,
pui Ni-ni-we tag sang kaoh cung tix deuh phi map ka pex pui baux singaix ju in,
mawh kheu hoik mhong kix ka gum lox pa sidiex Yaw-na,
kix tom loh rhawm rhi tix tik ceu pa caox mai yuh pa rauh pa see.
Aux krai ka pex,
ka dee in kawn koe kaing pa lheu ting khaing Yaw-na lie!
42 qKa daux singaix pa prawng ca,
bun simiang haktiex plak seh tag sang cung tix hoek deuh phi map ka pui ka daux baux ju singaix in,
mawh kheu hoek nawh nghiet ka lox ping nyia cuyi pa krai simiang Saw-law-meung khaing krax lang blaong dee singai.
Aux krai ka pex,
dee in kaing ceu pa blawn ting ceu khaing Saw-law-meung hoik koe ka heue!”
Khuan phi caox deeh hoek
(Lai Lu-cia 11.24–26)
43 “Khuan phi caox hoik lih hu khaing pui pa laig tix daux,
mai hoik tauh hu ka baoh haktiex dee kroh khawm uik,
sawg ka dee sang laux lee tix,
ang yaox,
44 tom ah nin:
‘Aux deeh hoek ka nyiex pa hoik ot tix ka sivoe an.’
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh deeh ing,
mai yaox nyiex kawn yi,
mai baox hoik pih tix mhawm singax.
45 Nawh baox deeh lih hu,
viex khuan pa kawn lheu caox caig khaing tix aliah mu hoek,
mai ot tau mai nawh.
Krax ot pui an kawn lheu caox caig khaing singaix pa hoik luan heue.
Grawh grawng pui baux singaix ju in kawx yuh nin nang heue.”
Miex Ye-sux mai pux aik nawh
(Lai Ma-kheu 3.31–35; Lai Lu-cia 8.19–21)
46 Yam kawn si kah Ye-sux tau mai pui hun khankix,
miex nawh mai pux aik nawh hoek.
Kix cung ka plak praix,
sang koe lox si kah ka nawh.
47 Koe pui krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Eue,
miex maix mai pux aik maix cung ka plak praix,
sang si kah ka maix heue!”
48 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pui mawx mawh miex aux?
Pui mawx mawh pux aik aux?”
49 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ci ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Pex jhak,
pui in kix hoik mawh miex aux pux aik aux heue!
50 Can pui pa hawt tix yuh ka alo keeing aux Siyiex,
tag sang mawh pux aik aux,
pux o aux,
mai miex aux heue.”
