9
Kawn cau Siyiex ka daux phuan kaux tix kaux phlu teet tawng,
aux yaox khrum simuing tix mu khaing rao ma,
mai cawd ka piang tiex.
Simuing in hoik rieb ka jiak saw daux kied raux pa ang koe dee die.
2 a Nawh paoh ka daux kied raux pa ang koe dee die,
taox ngu tag chu tix lih khaing,
awm chu taox ngu pa lih khaing daux mawk ngu ting yuh nan;
singaix mai rao ma hoik tieh tix lih faik kheu pa chu taox ngu tix lih khaing daux raux an.
3 bKoe sido lih khaing daux taox ngu an,
mai cawd ka piang tiex;
kix rieb ka riang rao,
awm hoik koe riang rao Tam-tim khawm uik ka piang tiex yuh nan.
4 cKix hawt awm lox pa tawm ang yuh rib riem,
khaox ox,
mai khaox ox tang ceu khankix laux leeing,
mai yuh ngie ka pui pa ang bax tawk cum cie Siyiex ot ka dong re tix khankix laux leeing khaing.
5 Sido ang koe akhuang tix pauh ka pui in kix yum,
mawh ngie chix kieh kix kha rhawm tix phuan khix khaing.
Krax kha rhawm ceu in tag sang awm mat yuh Tam-tim pui yuh nan.
6 dKa daux phuan khix in,
kix simie tix yum,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon yum heue.
7 ePak peung sido an kix hoik awm brung naing pa tag sang yuh naing yuh nan.
Ka piang kaing kix ceep pa awm mhok khri yuh nan;
sido an kix pak ngai awm pak ngai pui yuh nan.
8 fHauk kaing sido an kix awm hauk kaing sibun yuh nan,
rhang awm rhang sivai sang si yuh nan.
9 gCeu pa vheuk tix ka naok sido an kix,
awm dieb tie naok rhiem yuh nan,
lox luah pa lih khaing preeik awm lox luah hu ling law puing naing tom nie yuh nan.
10 Sido an kix kuah si dax mai pa lawm,
awm Tam-tim yuh nan,
taug mawh si dax in pa koe riang rao tix kieh pui kha rhawm tix phuan khix.
11 Koe simiang tix kaux kuang ka piang kix;
simiang an tag mawh kawn cau Siyiex pa kuang ka daux kied raux pa ang koe dee die.
Ceu kaux nawh lox Si-paw-lai kok mawh Ya-pa-toeng,
lox Si-lax kok mawh Ya-paw-loengh heue.
12 Khaix hoik luan hu krax kha rhawm ka daux bawg tix;
krox jun heue,
khaix in sax kawn sang koe krax kha rhawm ra ceu pa tag sang hoek heue.
13 iKawn cau Siyiex ka daux liah kaux tix kaux phlu teet tawng,
aux mhong lox pa lih hoek khaing plak sivoe ngai Siyiex ka rung khu khri dee tan an khawm pon daog.
14 Lox in krai ka kawn cau Siyiex pa phlu teet tawng ka daux liah kaux ah nin:
“Ploi ka kawn cau Siyiex pa laung tix ot ka rawm klawng ting Yo-fa-la-ti pon kaux lih heue!”
15 Kawn cau Siyiex an kix pon kaux taug pon tix ploi lih.
Kix hoik dawg rien tix mhawm,
tag sang pauh pui yum loe son tix son ka daux num an,
khix an,
singaix,
nari yam an.
16 Aux mhong krai pui koe kaux pui naing brug brung ra yieh lan.
17 Ka daux riang su sai,
aux yaox ka brung an kix mai ka pui naing brug brung.
Dieb tie naok pui naing brug brung hoik raoh awm ngu yuh nan,
klai si ngi awm siang simaox yuh nan,
lheung awm mhat yuh nan.
Kaing brung an awm kaing sivai sang si yuh nan,
ngu,
taox ngu,
mai mhat buing tix lih khaing daux jheeh.
18 Kawn pui loe son tix son laux leeing ka ngu,
ka taox ngu,
ka mhat pa buing tix lih khaing daux jheeh brung an.
19 Riang rao brung an ot ka daux jheeh mai ka piang si dax.
Si dax awm si uing yuh nan,
plak piang koe kaing;
kix hoik siao kaing in yuh pui laux heue.
20 jPui pa deuk pa ang bax yaox tix laux leeing ka krax kha rhawm in kix ca kawn ang bax loh rhawm rhi tix,
ang bax tik vud ka riang rao pa teei kix taix tix yuh kah.
Kix ca kawn hawt tix krub ka phi caox,
khri,
mau,
tawng,
simaox,
kraung khaox mai ceu pa hoik yuh tix pa ang chix yaox,
pa ang chix nghiet,
pa ang chix hu.
21 Kix hoik yuh ka phi map pa pauh pui,
si meed sat,
krax pa phit vao,
pa briex lak khankix,
mai ang bax loh rhawm rhi tix nang heue.
