2
Aux simie pex tawng,
kheu son pex mai son pui Lao-ti-cia,
khawm pui tom nie pa ang aux bax yaox ka ngai khankix,
aux sau riang tix yuh kaing.
2 Aux sang kieh kix pon krax pa joh jhan,
pon grum rhawm tau ka daux krax muh,
koe krax tawng krax yung pa mawh pa meh,
mai krax yum rhawm pa grum naog hoek tawng ka pa raux pa mox Siyiex;
pa raux pa mox in hoik mawh to kaux Khrit.
3 Nawh mawh ping nyia cuyi pa plak jhi pa hoik mox Siyiex glip khawm uik,
mai ciak saw ngai lai heue.
4 Aux krai lox in,
mawh son ang sang kieh pex glup ka lox nyaw pui pex lud krax.
5 Viang to kaux aux ang ot tau mai pex dee an,
rhawm rhi aux ot tau mai pex.
Aux kied gaux rhawm,
kheu pon yaox tix grawng pa mhawm ot pex,
khaix an krax pa yum rhawm pex ka Khrit hoik mawh riah pa kaux cong heue.
Ju pa grum naog ka daux Khrit
6 Kheu hoik rieb pex Ye-sux Khrit mawh simiang,
kaing pa yuh pex tag caw tix kieh nawh mawh kaing ting,
7 mai sum riah tix kaux cong ka daux nawh,
sum krax yum rhawm tix haok huan;
pex gau tix yuh mawh nin.
Pex kawx tag caw tix koe rhawm pa grum naog krax pa yaog yaw heue.
8 Pex tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
paw vi kieh tix glup daux krax nyaw glaw pa gau ka daux pa giex going;
mawh kheu krax pa gau ceu an hoik mawh ceu pa sidiex kawn pui,
mawh pa teei lox sidiex ka daux been haktiexa,
ang mawh teei khaing Khrit.
9 Mawh kheu to kaux Siyiex khawm uik hoik ot ka daux Khrit,
10 phan pex kleuk tix tau mai Khrit,
tag pon ka ju pa ting lhaong.
Nawh mawh simiang ting,
mai kawn lheu khaing simiang mai cao giex kaing ka daux haktiex khuan khawm uik heue.
11 Pex hoik Pat-hak tix ka daux krax pa im ju Khrit;
pa Pat-hak in ang mawh pa yuh kawn pui,
mai hoik mawh Pat-hak pa kieh nawh pex bheeik luan khaing ka daux nex nham phi map.
12 bMawh kheu yam cub com pex tix,
pex hoik si paung tix tau mai Khrit;
yam cub com pex tix,
mai hoik mawh nang pa deeh kaoh im tau mai nawh ka daux pa yum rhawm ka kaing pa yuh Siyiex,
pa kieh nawh deeh kaoh im an.
13 cSingaix pa hoik luan,
mawh kheu hoik koe phi map pex,
mai baox mawh nang pui pa ang koe ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
ka daux khuan pex hoik yum.
Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex kieh pex deeh kaoh im tau mai Khrit.
Nawh hoik pluat ploi ka phi map pa yuh pex khawm uik,
14 dhoik teei tix tik pa tiiem ka phi map ceuk ex pa ang mhawm ka son ex,
ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa phrawk ka ex,
mai tawk ka piang khaox lhak,
yuh laux leeing.
15 Ka piang khaox lhak,
Khrit hoik yuh lauxe ka siawp sibrawm simiang mai cao giex kaing khuan haktiex khankix,
mai kieh kix mawh pui laung pa dia paox tix hu ka plak sivoe ngai pui heue.
16 fMawh kheu nan,
paw vi kieh pui teei been pa tat tix deuh ka priex preem,
ka poi,
ka jhong khix mee num,
mai ka grawh grawng singaix tuah khankix phrawk pex kah.
17 In kix khawm uik mawh ngie pang peue kaing ceu pa sang lih tix hoek khaing;
Khrit taug mawh to kaux tete.
18 Paw vi kieh pui kawn ca sawg ka ceu pa tang pa siyox pa ang pui sax yaox,
pa yuh tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi krub ka kawn cau Siyiex,
mai kieh pex ang pon ka bawg yam pa tawx ka khawng phak pa mhawm.
Kix hawt ka krax keud krax sawn kawn pui pa giex pa going,
mai kieh tix ting rhawm cau nyhau ang koe ceu cad,
19 gkix ang si doe tix tau mai simiang Khrit.
Tete te lai,
mawh ngie kaing ka to kaux pa mawh Khrit,
to kaux khawm mu tom taug chix pon haok huan,
ka daux pa ra lai kleuk si-ang si niag paox tix,
mai ting huan si nyek si nyek ka pa hawt alo Siyiex heue.
Im tau yum tau mai Khrit
20-21 Kheu hoik yum tau pex mai Khrit,
hoik bheeik luan khaing lox sidiex ka daux been haktiex an kix,
yuh ka mawx pex lai ca kawn hawt ka been kawn pui haktiex yuh nan,
mai nied tix hawt ka been ceu pa “ang pon tix theeh ceu in,
ang pon tix ih ceu an,
ang pon tix giex ceu in” in kix pa ang cu pui yuh lie?
22 Ceu in kix phan hoik siao uik,
kheu hoik mawh sin ngao been pa tat kawn pui tix deuh khankix khaing.
23 Phan jhak tix yaox ka plak praix,
pa krub ka kawn cau Siyiex,
pa yuh tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
pa kha ka to kaux tix khankix,
awm mawh pa lhak lhiao,
viang mawh nan,
tete te lai,
mawh pa kha ka alo nex nham tix ang koe ceu ceeix kawx heue.
