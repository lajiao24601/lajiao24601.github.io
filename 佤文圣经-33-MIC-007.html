Pa laux leeing pui Yi-seu-liex
7
In mawh kaing pa kied ting laux leeing heue!
Aux hoik awm pui pa cai rhawm tix kaux,
ang lai yaox plix pa deuk ka khaox ruh tix mu kawx,
ang lai yaox plix siiet pa prah koe ka daux si peem plix siiet tix mu kawx.
Can plix siiet mai plix ruh hoik pieh singax khawm uik.
2 Ka piang haktiex in,
can pui rauh see hoik yum khawm uik,
pui pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex ang lai koe pie tix kaux.
Kawn pui krox jun ka bawg yam pa khaw tix pauh ka pui;
kawn pui deeh yuh paox ceu paox cad tix laux leeing.
3 Kix hoik mawh pui pa chix yuh ngie ka pa caox pa caig khawm uik;
simiang koh keen mai simiang prawng see hoik rieb ka mau chun praok khawm uik.
Pui pa koe siawp sibrawm hoik yuh paox pie tau mai pui hun,
hoik phit vao yuh kaing ceu pa ang mhawm.
4 Ka daux kix,
pie pui pa kied mhawm rhawm pa kied rauh see kawx,
hoik awn rieb riem yuh nan ang lai koe ceu pie ceeix kawx heue.
Singaix bawg yam prawng ca Siyiex pui cie tix hoik hoek.
In hoik mawh gum lox pa tawx nawh ka cao jhai pa mawh cao kia ngien sivoe an ne raw ka pui cie tix.
Yam in kix jauh tix si yaung si kua.
5 Pex paw vi yum rhawm ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
mai paw vi yum rhawm ka paox grawm tix,
khawm pa krai lox ka moeng tix kawx tag caw tix rien rhawm tix ka heue.
6 aKawn simex map nai ka keeing tix;
kawn bun tieb tie ka miex tix;
mun simah ka miex mex tix;
pui pa koeng ka pex tag mawh pui daux nyiex ma pex heue.
7 Phan mawh aux,
aux tag sang een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex;
aux tag sang krox jun ka Siyiex pa mawh cao taoh aux.
Siyiex aux tag sang nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan heue.
Simiang Siyiex taoh ka pui cie tix
8 Pui pa koeng ka ex ang caw tix gaux rhawm ka yaox ex krax laux krax leeing.
Viang ex hoik goh kawx,
ex kawn sang deeh kaoh cung.
Yam in ex ot ka daux pa faik,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang tawx pa riang ka ex.
9 Ex hoik sax phit ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan yam in ex tag caw tix gun yeung krax pa song rhawm nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
dee si dauh,
nawh tag sang krai lox tang ex,
mai prawng rauh see son ex.
Nawh tag sang viex ex laig ka daux pa riang;
ex tag sang yaox krax taoh nawh ka ngai tix.
10 Yam an,
pui pa koeng ka ex yaox ka ex,
tag sang koe krax kaig krax tua.
Kix hoik sax chawk cheng ka ex ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex ot dee mawx lie?”
Ex tag sang yaox grawng pa ang kix pon pex,
awm glaox pa dug dig pui pa koe daux laih daux cau yuh nan heue.
11 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
bawg yam deeh cox sum pex ka guh ding veng hoik hoek heue!
Yam an pex tag sang vih vieh ka haktiex tix.
12 Paox ceu paox cad pex tag sang deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khaing haktiex ku dauh.
Kix tag sang nied hoek khaing haktiex Ya-sux plak lih singaix,
khaing haktiex Ai-cix plak seh,
khaing mee rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
khaing rawm sigrit sigra plak an,
khaing dieb gawng plak an.
13 Viang mawh nan,
haktiex dee an tag sang tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag daux tu,
mawh kheu hoik caox caig pui pa ot haktiex an khawm uik heue.
Simiang Siyiex caox rhawm ka pui cie tix
14 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix eei ka pui ceu pa hoik laug maix khankix heue!
Ka sivie siveng pa paung ot kix nong nong,
hoik mawh daux tiag haktiex pa mhawm khawm uik.
Khiox khaw maix kieh kix hu ka gawng Pa-sang mai ka gawng Ci-liex;
khiox khaw maix eei kix ka daux pak rib pa vieh va,
awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan heue.
15 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix yuh mhai pa daox pa am son yix liak,
awm yam viex maix yix lih khaing Ai-cix sivoe an yuh nan.
16 Meung ka piang haktiex viang ting riang rao kawx,
viang mawh nan,
yam yaox kix ka pa ting riang rao maix,
kix tag yaoh yee kaig tua,
mai mox ngai thox jheeh tix.
17 Kix tag sang awm si uing pa mo piang tiex;
kix tag sang yaog yee to lih khaing veng,
mai si yaung si kua ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex heue.
18 Simiang Siyiex eue,
ang koe Siyiex tang kaux pa pon tiag tix tau mai maix;
maix hoik pluat ploi ka phi map pui ceu maix pa kawn deuk khankix.
Maix ang song rhawm tom rheeng,
viang mawh nan,
maix kia ngien yix ka daux krax muh tix pa ang chix tieh loh.
19 Maix tag sang deeh baox kia ngien yix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix.
Maix tag sang teei tix dig ka phi map yix bhliet greem tiag caong tix,
mai si vun laig greem rawm sigrit sigra.
20 Awm gum lox pa hoik ap maix ka tax du yix dix dix yuh nan,
maix tag sang jhi krax muh pa kaux sijax tix,
mai pa ang chix tieh loh tix ka doeh ceu cad Ya-paw-la-hang,
ka doeh ceu cad Ya-keux khankix heue.
