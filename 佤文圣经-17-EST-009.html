Pui Yo-thaix pauh ka pui pa koeng ka kix
9
Khix kao ra,
pa mawh khix Ya-ta kao ngaix loe hoik hoek;
in mawh singaix hoik lih mawh lox pa tawx simiang,
mai mawh nang singaix sang keud pui pa koeng tix yuh pui Yo-thaix laux leeing.
Viang mawh nan,
singaix an,
pui Yo-thaix tieh tix pex ka kix.
2 Pui Yo-thaix rien khre paox tix ka haktiex Yo-thaixa pa ot ka meung ku dauh,
sang puing ka pui pa koeng ka kix.
Pui haktiex ku dauh kied lhat ka kix,
ang koe pui pon tieb tie ka kix.
3 Mawh tete,
cao kaing daux haktiex ku dauh,
khawm cao kaing,
simiang naing,
mai cao pa teem simiang kied teem ka pui Yo-thaix,
mawh kheu lhat kix ka Maw-ti-kai.
4 Pui daux meung khawm uik hoik tawng:
yam in Maw-ti-kai mawh pa kied koe sibrawm ka daux nyiex haw simiang tix kaux,
sibrawm nawh nied ot nied ting lhaong.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yo-thaix nied teei rhawm pa sang yuh tix ka pui pa koeng ka kix;
kix siao vaig mug ka pui koeng,
pauh ka kix heue.
6 Pui Yo-thaix pauh pui yum ka veng ting ot simiang Su-sang phuan yieh kaux,
7-10 daux an,
koe kawn simex Ha-mang kao kaux:
Pa-sang-ta-tha,
Ta-feung,
Ya-see-pha-tha,
Phaw-la-tha,
Ya-ta-li-ya,
Ya-li-ta-tha,
Pha-ma-see-tha,
Ya-li-saix,
Ya-li-taix,
Wa-yie-sha-tha.
Ha-mang mawh kawn simex Ha-mi-ta-tha,
mawh pui pa koeng ka pui Yo-thaix.
Viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix ang rhim ka sin ngao kix.
11 Ngaix an,
pui pa pauh ka daux veng Su-sang krai ka simiang dee an,
12 simiang ah nin ka moeng tix Yi-see-thie:
“Pui Yo-thaix hoik pauh pui ka veng Su-sang phuan yieh kaux,
mawh khawm kawn simex Ha-mang kao kaux.
Phan mawh haktiex tang dauh,
kix rien tix pauh nang tom nie.
Yam in maix sang cie ceu pa tix?
Krai ka aux,
aux tag sang tawx ka maix heue.”
13 Yi-see-thie pawk lox:
“Phan aux pon krax muh Vawng,
pa sax baox kieh pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka daux veng Su-sang hawt tix yuh awm kaing pa yuh tix in singaix,
mai teuk teue kawn simex Ha-mang yum ka piang khaox heue.”
14 Simiang tom hawt tix tawx lox;
gum lox an tawx tix ka daux veng Su-sang.
Kawn simex Ha-mang kao kaux teuk teue tix ka piang khaox sivoe pui hun.
15 Khix Ya-ta kao ngai pon,
pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka veng Su-sang baox khrawm paox tix,
pauh pui yum loe yieh kaux,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax teei ka sin ngao cie kix heue.
16 Haktiex pui Yo-thaix tang dauh kawx khrawm paox tix rien ka to kaux tix;
kheu sang pauh kix pui pa koeng,
pauh ka pui pa koeng aliah mheen phuan rheng kaux,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax teei ka sin ngao cie kix.
17 Kaing in lih ka daux khix Ya-ta kao loe singaix.
Ngaix khaix,
kao ngaix pon,
kix ang lai pauh kah,
tat ngaix in mawh ngaix gaux rhawm heue.
18 Pui Yo-thaix ka veng Su-sang tat kao ngaix phuan in mawh singaix pa kied gaux rhawm;
mawh kheu pauh kix pui pa koeng ka daux kao ngaix loe,
kao ngaix pon,
tom naox ka kao ngaix phuan.
19 Kheu nan,
pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka yaong priex tix hawt ka khix Ya-ta kao ngaix pon,
kieh mawh ngaix pa kied gaux rhawm laux lee tix,
mai lai tawx ka khawng phak heue.
Grawng been kaoh Poi Phu-eu
20 Maw-ti-kai tiiem ka kaing in kix daux lai,
tawx lai ka pui Yo-thaix daux meung Ya-ha-sui-lu pa singai,
pa dex haktiex ku dauh,
21 krai ka kix,
ku num daux khix Ya-ta kao pon singaix mai kao phuan singaix kieh mawh ngaix poi pa priex tix hawt,
22 mawh kheu ngaix in ra ngaix mawh ngaix pauh pui Yo-thaix ka pui pa koeng.
Khix in mawh khix laux rhawm deek rhawm deek rhi pui Yo-thaix tieh tix mawh khix gaux rhawm.
Kix sang priex tix hawt ka poi in,
gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
lai tawx ka khawng phak,
mai teem ka pui hot.
23 Pui Yo-thaix priex tix hawt ka lox pa tawx Maw-ti-kai,
kheu nan,
poi in hoik lih tix mawh been ku num heue.
24 bPui pa koeng ka pui Yo-thaix,
pui Ya-cia kawn simex Ha-mi-ta-tha pa kaux Ha-mang,
tawx rhawm keud tix sang pauh ka pui Yo-thaix.
Nawh cawk ka lai (baox kok mawh Phu-eu) tat singaix.
Son sang kieh pui Yo-thaix laux leeing.
25 Viang mawh nan,
kheu rawg Yi-see-thie ka simiang dee an,
simiang tom tawx lox,
kieh krax pa sang keud Ha-mang tix pauh pui Yo-thaix hoek ka piang to kaux nawh mai kawn simex nawh,
kawn simex nawh teuk teue tix yum ka piang khaox ciyang.
26 In mawh dee bleeh poi in pa kaux “Phu-eu”.
Pui Yo-thaix kheu lai pa tiiem Maw-ti-kai mai kaing pa lih tix mawh ku ceu,
27 tom tat been son to kaux tix mai doeh ceu cad kix mai pui an kix pa tieh tix mawh pui cie kix,
tag hawt ka lox pa tawx Maw-ti-kai,
ku num priex tix hawt ka singaix in kiex.
28 Viang ot daux haktiex mawx veng mawx,
pui Yo-thaix ku nyiex tag priex tix hawt ka poi Phu-eu in leeing ju leeing phan.
29 Kawn bun Ya-pi-haix,
pa mawh moeng simiang Yi-see-thie kawx tiiem lai tix pheen,
teei sibrawm moeng simiang joh jan ka lai tiiem Maw-ti-kai grawh grawng poi Phu-eu an.
30 Lai in mawh pa tiiem ka pui Yo-thaix yam kuang simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu ka haktiex tix yieh tix nga aliah dauh.
Daux lai nawb buan son ka piang pui Yo-thaix nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mhawm ot yaox krax nyawm ot,
31 mai tawx lox kieh kix mai doeh ceu cad kix priex tix hawt ka Poi Phu-eu in ka daux been,
awm hawt ka grawng tuah priex preem mai been pa yiiem yuh nan.
In mawh lox pa rom Maw-ti-kai mai moeng simiang Yi-see-thie tix tawx.
32 Lox pa tawx Yi-see-thie hoik tat ka been Poi Phu-eu;
lox in tiiem tix ka daux lai heue.
