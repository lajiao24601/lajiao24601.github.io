Veng Yie-lu-sa-leung laux
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 36.13–21; Lai Yie-li-mi 52.3–11)
25
aSi-ti-cia tieh vheen ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
mawh kheu nin,
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha thung pui naing hu puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
yam an mawh bawg yam giex Si-ti-cia kaing ka daux dim num khix kao kao ngaix.
Kix sum bang ot ka plak praix veng,
mai lai lawm puing ka guh tab veng,
2 mai hoik lai lawm ka tom hoek giex Si-ti-cia simiang daux kao tix num.
3 Num an khix pon dim ngaix,
ka daux veng sibhawm praix kied kin kah,
kawn pui ang lai koe priex preem,
4 bpui hoik pon yieh ka guh veng.
Viang pui naing Pa-pi-loeng kawn lai lawm ka veng,
simiang mai pui naing pa pau khawm uik hoik tang yaog tix to hu pon sawm.
Kix to mox tix ka krax dex si peem nyiex haw simiang an,
luan ka siviex pa ot guh veng khawm ra plak,
hu plak ngawd Yaw-tang.
5 Viang mawh nan,
pui naing Pa-pi-loeng leue ka simiang Si-ti-cia,
mai krup nawh ka dee lung pa dex ka veng Yie-li-keu;
pui naing nawh hoik hu khaing nawh,
hoik tang to pru phrat tix.
6 Kix pon giex ka nawh,
mai viex nawh hoek ka simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha dee an.
Yam an Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ot ka veng Li-pi-la ka daux haktiex Ha-ma,
nawh tom prawng ca Si-ti-cia dee an,
7 cmai pauh kawn simex nawh ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
khaix an koik tawm ngai nawh lih khawm ra khawn;
kix phrawk giex nawh ka maox caing tawng,
mai viex nawh hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng heue.
Nyiex buan laux
(Lai Yie-li-mi 52.12–33)
8 Yam giex simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka kaing daux kao dim num khix phuan aliah ngaix,
pui cao teem coi simiang,
cao kaing pui pa pau simiang pa kaux Ni-pu-sha-la-tang laig ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
9 Nawh ploi ngu gleeing nyiex buan,
nyiex haw simiang,
mai can nyiex cao kaing pui pa ting ceu khawm uik haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung.
10 Pui naing nawh yieh ka guh ding veng laux.
11 Khaix an,
Ni-pu-sha-la-tang giex ka pui pa deuh tix ot daux veng,
pui pa mawh cang mai sum nyiex mai pui pa ap tix ang pex ka pui Pa-pi-loeng,
hoik giex kix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik.
12 Viang mawh nan,
deuh pui pa blawn hot khankix tix blah ot ka Yo-tax,
mai nhie kix yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix siiet,
mai ka daux kaung ma khankix heue.
13 dPui Pa-pi-loeng hoik lauh max ka rong tawng,
daux jhup jaong tawng,
mai rawm dung tawng pa ot ka daux nyiex buan,
mai teei tawng khawm uik viex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
14 eKix kawx hoik teei ka jauk pa siao kieh khu singax,
mai aw nyaox,
si kiep ngu,
klong ting pa sau nham khawng brie ot daux,
mai kraung tawng pa siao yam yuh kaing krub hoik viex hu khawm uik.
15 Kix kawx hoik viex hu ka can kraung ku ceu pa hoik raug ka khri mau,
khawm mawk ngu pa sau gaox ngu ot daux,
mai pan iak kawx hoik viex hu.
16 Ka daux kraung an kix,
koe kraung tawng pa yuh simiang Saw-law-meung son nyiex buan,
mawh rong tawng ra gawng,
daux jhup jaong tawng,
dung rawm tawng pa ting.
Kraung tawng in kix kied nhie mai ang lai pon ciang.
17 Rong tawng ra gawng hoik awm paox tix;
tix gawng krax lhaong koe daix kongjhee,
toh rong tawng krax lhaong koe tix kongjhee mai loe kongchuing,
vung viang hoik sau rub pa yuh ka tawng mai riang plix mak cang heue.
Giex pui Yo-tax hu ka Pa-pi-loeng
(Lai Yie-li-mi 52.24–27)
18 Simiang pa pau Ni-pu-sha-la-tang kawx hoik giex ka simiang cao giex kaing li Si-lai-ya,
ka simiang cao giex kaing li ka daux ra kaux Si-fang-yax,
mai ka simiang pa ting ceu ka daux nyiex buan loe kaux hu nang.
19 Nawh baox giex simiang cao bung naing tix kaux ka daux veng,
pui cao teem nyiex haw simiang pa deuh ot daux veng phuan kaux,
simiang cao bung naing na bat ra pa yuh kaing pa priex tix deuh ka lai ting ceu pa mox pui naing tix kaux,
mai pui pa ting ceu ka daux haktiex tix gliah kaux.
20-21 Ni-pu-sha-la-tang phrawk kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Pa-pi-loeng;
yam an,
simiang ot daux veng Li-pi-la haktiex Ha-ma.
Ka daux haktiex an,
nawh tawx lox nhie pui lauh ka kix,
mai pauh kix yum heue.
Yuh nin,
pui giex ka kawn pui Yo-tax lih hu khaing haktiex kix heue.
Cao kuang kaing pa ting pui Yo-thaix pa kaux Ci-ta-lix
(Lai Yie-li-mi 40.7–9; 41.1–3)
22 Simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha tawx kawn simex Ya-si-kang,
kawn saux Sa-fang pa kaux Ci-ta-lix mawh cao kuang kaing pa ting piang pui Yo-tax,
nhie nawh mawh cao pa kuang kia ka pui pa ang kix bax giex hoek Pa-pi-loeng.
23 Simiang naing pui Yo-tax pa ang bax ap tix ang pex mai pui naing khankix mhong ka lox in,
kix tom hoek daux haktiex Mi-see-pa peung rhawm tix ka Ci-ta-lix.
Simiang naing in kix koe kawn simex Ni-thang-ya pa kaux Yi-seu-ma-lix,
kawn simex Cia-li-yax pa kaux Yaw-ha-nang,
pui Ni-thaw-fax,
kawn simex Tang-hu-mawx pa kaux Si-lai-ya,
pui Ma-cia pa kaux Ya-sha-ni-yax.
24 Ci-ta-lix ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai lox tete ka pex,
pex paw vi lhat ka cao kaing pui naing Pa-pi-loeng.
Pex tawng mhawm ot tix ka daux haktiex in khaing,
yuh kaing son simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
pex tag sang yaox krax pa nyawm ot khawm uik heue.”
25 Viang mawh nan,
num an daux khix aliah,
koe pui daux nyiex ma simiang tix kaux pa mawh kawn simex Ni-thang-ya,
kawn saux Yi-li-sa-ma pa kaux Yi-seu-ma-lix,
viex pui kao kaux hoek ka Mi-see-pa,
mai klauh ka Ci-ta-lix yum,
mai pauh khawm pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Pa-pi-loeng pa hu tau mai nawh khankix.
26 fMawh kheu nan,
can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
pui hot ang ah,
pui mi ang ah,
khawm simiang naing khankix to mox tix ka Ai-cix,
mawh kheu lhat kix ka pui Pa-pi-loeng heue.
Ploi ka simiang Yaw-ya-cin
(Lai Yie-li-mi 52.31–34)
27 Yam mawh Yi-wex Mi-law-tax simiang Pa-pi-loeng daux num an,
nawh ploi simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-ya-cin lih khaing daux thawng.
Kaing in lih tix mawh khaix hoik giex hu ka Yaw-ya-cin ka daux tix ngoe aliah num khix kao ra tix nga aliah ngaix.
28 Yi-wex Mi-law-tax chix kia ngien Yaw-ya-cin,
mai tawx nawh mawh simiang pa lhaong,
kieh nawh lhaong khaing Vawng tang kaux pa giex hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng.
29 Yaw-ya-cin pon akhuang tix peeik ka kraung pa ceep pui pa laung daux thawng,
mai yaox tix laig ih praix preem tau mai simiang Pa-pi-loeng tom si dauh ju tix.
30 Ka daux singaix yam im ju nawh,
simiang baox tawx ngawh priex ih nawh ku ngaix.
