Ye-sux pai moi ka siliex simiang naing Law-ma
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.5–13)
7
Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux lox ka pui hun,
tom hu ka Cia-pai-nong.
2 Dee an koe simiang naing Law-ma tix kaux,
siliex pa hoik khaw rhawm nawh koe jhawm saux pa kin,
mai hoik dex sang yum.
3 Cao giex kaing naing an mhong grawh grawng Ye-sux,
nawh tom tawx lox ka kuad song pui Yo-thaix ra loe kaux,
nhie kix hu khiox khaw Ye-sux hoek tix pai ka jhawm saux siliex nawh moi.
4 Kix hoek ka Ye-sux dee an,
mai khiox khaw ka nawh ah nin:
“Pui in hoik mawh pa khaw maix tix teem coi tete;
5 nawh muh ka ex pui Yo-thaix,
hoik sax sum nyiex sidiex son yix heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux tom hu tau mai kix.
Yam hoik dex sang hoek nawh dee an,
simiang naing tawx lox ka paox grawm tix ra loe kaux hoek krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ang siao lai nyhap maix.
Maix hoik cu tix hoek,
aux kied ang nyawm rhawm kah;
7 to kaux aux kawx ang keung tix yaox ka pak ngaix maix.
Ca mawh tawx maix lox pie tix gum khaing,
siliex aux taug sang moi heue.
8 Awm hoik koe simiang naing pa thung ka aux,
plak greem koe pui naing aux thung ka kix yuh nan;
phan aux tawx lox ka pui tix kaux nhie nawh hu,
mai nawh tag hu.
Phan tawx lox nhie pui mawx hu,
pui an taug hu;
phan aux ah nin ka siliex tix:
‘Maix yuh kaing ceu in’,
nawh tag yuh hu heue.”
9 Ye-sux mhong ka lox in,
kied daox deng kah,
mai krai ka pui hun pa hawt khaix nawh ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex,
awm krax yum rhawm pa yuh nin,
ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux ang sax yaox tix kaux kawx heue!”
10 Pui pa ploi kix hu khankix deeh ing ka nyiex simiang naing,
mai hoik yaox grawng pa moi siliex simiang naing heue.
Kieh kawn simex miex mai kaoh im
11 Ang koe miex glang tixa,
Ye-sux hoek ka veng Na-ying;
pui gau nawh mai cub pui hun hu tau mai nawh.
12 Nawh hoek ka diang siviex veng,
koe cub pui pa hu si paung ka pui yum dia paox tix lih.
Pui yum an mawh kawn simex miex mai pa koe tix kaux khaing;
pui tom nie lih khaing daux veng,
mai hu si paung pa yum an tau mai miex mai an.
13 Simiang Ye-sux yaox miex mai an,
mai hoik kied caox rhawm caox rhi,
nawh tom krai ka miex mai an ah nin:
“Paw vi yiiem heue!”
14 Khaix an nawh hu yeen ka khaox grawng,
pui pa grawng tom kaog.
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui pa nhum ju,
aux tawx lox nhie maix kaoh heue!”
15 Pui yum an tom kaoh ngawm,
khaix an mai jauh tix si kah.
Ye-sux teei tix tawx nawh ka miex nawh heue.
16 Pui hun kied daox deng kah;
kix rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
“Koe cao jhai pa ting hoik lih khaing ka daux ex heue!”
Baox ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik hoek tix taoh ka pui cie tix heue!”
17 Grawh grawng kaing pa yuh Ye-sux hoik tauh ka haktiex Yo-thaix mai ka baoh haktiex pa dex dee an khawm uik heue.
Pui gau Yaw-hang cao cub com hoek ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 11.2–19)
18 Pui gau Yaw-hang krai grawng kaing in khawm uik ka Yaw-hang.
Yaw-hang nhie pui gau tix ra kaux hoek,
19 ploi kiex hu ka Simiang Ye-sux,
mai nhie kiex chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh maix kaw pa krai Yaw-hang pa tag sang lih hoek tix kaux an,
phan ang mawh nan,
yix kawn sang krox jun tang kaux lie?”
20 Kiex hoek ka Ye-sux dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Yaw-hang cao cub com ploi yiex hoek chawk maix ah nin:
‘Mawh maix kaw pa tag sang lih hoek an,
phan ang mawh nan,
yix kawn sang krox jun tang kaux lie?’ ”
21 Ka daux bawg yam an Ye-sux hoik pai moi ka pui pa koe jhawm saux,
jhawm saux preng,
mai pui pa laig khuan phi caox daux khankix,
khaix an nawh hoik kieh pui pa dug ngai chix yaox tom nie.
22 bMawh kheu nan,
nawh pawk lox pui gau Yaw-hang ah nin:
“Pex deeh ing,
mai krai ceu pa hoik mhong pex,
pa hoik yaox pex ka Yaw-hang,
pa mawh:
pui dug ngai hoik chix yaox,
pui laux caong hoik chix hu,
pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg hoik pon pa singax,
pui lheet yhaok hoik chix mhong,
pui yum hoik deeh kaoh im,
pui hot hoik pon mhong ka lox buan.
23 Pui pa koe krax yum rhawm ka aux kied ting buan son heue!”
24 Khaix hoik hu kawn cau Yaw-hang,
Ye-sux tom krai grawng Yaw-hang ka pui hun ah nin:
“Yam hu pex sawg Yaw-hang ka daux tiag daux tu sivoe an,
hoik simie pex tix yaox ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh gawng priem pa phlu bhaung lie?
25 Mawh sang jhak pex ka ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh sang jhak ka pui pa ceep kraung mhawm lie?
Pui pa ceep kraung mhawm,
pa mhawm krax ot krax koe ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang heue!
26 Phan mawh nan,
pex lih tix jhak ka ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh jhak ka cao jhai lie?
Mawh tete,
viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
nawh kawn lheu ting khaing cao jhai.
27 cNawh hoik mawh kaw pa hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri an tix kaux;
Siyiex ah nin:
‘Jhak heue,
aux tag sang ploi kawn cau tix mawh pa hu sivoe maix;
nawh tag sang paoh daux krax son maix.’ ”
28 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex,
ka daux kawn pui ang lai koe pui pa lheu ting khaing Yaw-hang;
viang mawh nan,
ka daux meung Siyiex,
pa blawn iak khaing paox tix tix kaux hoik mawh pa lheu ting khaing Yaw-hang heue!”
29 dPui hun mai khawm pui pa koh keen khankix,
mhong ka lox pa krai Ye-sux,
mai hoik nghiet ka lox pa krai Siyiex ka daux pa rauh pa see khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik rieb kix ka krax pa cub com Yaw-hang heue.
30 Viang mawh nan,
pui Fa-li-saix mai sara lox tat jhawm prawng khankix tieb tie ka krax pa hoik yuh Siyiex son kix,
mawh kheu ang kix bax rieb ka krax pa cub com Yaw-hang heue.
31 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
aux tag sang teei ceu pa tix tiag ka pui daux singaix baux ju in lie?
Hoik awm kix ceu pa tix lie?
32 Kix hoik awm kawn nyawm pa ngawm tix klieh ka daux laih yuh nan,
ka daux kix tix cub kok yo ka tang cub tix cub ah nin:
‘Yix hoik phlu pi daux poi ka paox son pex,
pex ang graoh heue!
Yix hoik rhax lai grawng yum pui,
pex kawx ang yiiem heue!’
33 Yaw-hang cao cub com hoek,
ang ih ang nyaux,
pex ah nin:
‘Nawh mawh pui pa laig phi daux heue!’
34 Kawn kawn pui hoek,
ih nang nyaux nang,
viang mawh nan,
pex ah nin:
‘Nawh mawh pui yoek ka plai,
pa yuh paox grawm tau mai pui koh keen mai pui caox heue!’
35 Viang mawh nan,
ping nyia Siyiex hoik mawh pa jhi tix lih khaing pui pa hoik rieb ka ping nyia heue.”
Pa loh bun phi map rhawm rhi tix
36 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix kaux kok Ye-sux sawm,
Ye-sux tom hu sawm mai nawh ka daux nyiex nawh.
37 eKa daux haktiex an koe bun tix kaux,
nawh hoik mawh pa ot ka daux krax phi map.
Nawh mhong hoek Ye-sux tix sawm daux nyiex pui Fa-li-saix an,
tom viex kawng tix mu pa hoik sau rawm bee sidah bhawng naog daux hoek.
38 Nawh ot ka plak khaix Ye-sux,
mai yiiem dex ka caong nawh.
Rawm ngai nawh hoik roik lan ka caong Ye-sux,
nawh tom teei hauk kaing tix awt kroh,
teei jheeh tix ngheet,
khaix an teei rawm bee sidah bhawng kraix kah.
39 Pui Fa-li-saix an pa kok Ye-sux hoek sawm yaox kah,
mai rhawm rhi nawh keud ah nin:
“Pui in phan mawh cao jhai tete,
nawh khaw tix tawng ka pui pa pu pi nawh in hoik mawh bun pa yuh ka mawx tix kaux;
nawh hoik mawh pui pa koe phi map heue!”
40 Ye-sux tom krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Si-meung,
aux koe lox pa sang krai ka maix heue.”
Si-meung pawk ah nin:
“Sara,
maix krai ka heue.”
41 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Koe pui ra kaux pa rom tix ceuk ka pui tix kaux,
tix kaux ceuk ka phuan yieh mau luah,
an tix kaux ceuk tix phuan plah mau luah.
42 Kiex khawm ra kaux ang koe riang tix pon plud,
pui pa ceuk kiex ka tom ang lai kieh kiex plud.
Maix keud tix yaox,
kiex ra kaux hoik mawh pui pa yuh ka mawx pa blawn muh ka nawh lie?”
43 Si-meung pawk ah nin:
“Aux keud mawh kaw pa blawn caox rhawm nawh ka an heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pa krai maix khaw heue.”
44 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh kling tix ka plak ot bun an,
mai krai ka Si-meung ah nin:
“Yaox maix ka bun in lie?
Aux hoek ka nyiex maix,
maix ang bax tawx rawm khoek caong ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
nawh teei rawm ngai tix khoek ka caong aux,
khaix an nawh teei hauk kaing tix awt kroh.
45 Maix ang bax teei been pa ngheet jheeh tix rieb hoek aux,
viang mawh nan,
jauh tom hoek aux nawh ngheet ka caong aux ang jud.
46 Maix ang bax teei rawm bee kraix ka kaing aux,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik teei bee sidah bhawng kraix ka caong aux.
47 Aux krai ka maix,
grawng pa hoik yuh nawh krax muh pa ting in,
hoik jhi grawng phi map nawh tom nie hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi heue.
Pui pa iak pon krax pa pluat ploi,
nawh kawx hoik iak yuh krax muh nang heue.”
48 Ye-sux tom krai ka bun ah nin:
“Phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi khawm uik heue.”
49 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa ngawm tix sawm tau mai nawh keud ka daux rhawm tix ah nin:
“In mawh pui mawx lie?
Nawh gah pon pluat ploi ka phi map pui!”
50 Ye-sux krai ka bun an ah nin:
“Krax yum rhawm maix hoik taoh ka maix;
deeh ing mhawm mhawm heue!”
