Grawng pa rawg buan
(Lai Ma-thaix 6.9–13; 7.7–11)
11
Koe tix bawg,
Ye-sux rawg buan ka haktiex tix dauh.
Khaix hoik rawg nawh buan,
koe pui gau tix kaux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
gau yix rawg buan liak,
awm hoik gau Yaw-hang pui gau tix yuh nan heue.”
2 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw tix rawg buan yuh nin:
keeing eue:
kieh pui yung yi ka ceu pa singax singeh maix;
kieh maix giex kaing ka piang haktiex.
3 Tawx priex preem pa sang lo ex ku singaixa.
4 Pluat ploi ka phi map ex,
mawh kheu hoik pluat ploi ex ka pui pa hoik lud loe ka ex yuh nan.
Paw kieh ex si pup ka krax cheuk cheu pa ang ex pon klawm.”
5 Khaix an Ye-sux krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Phan koe pui tix kaux ka daux pex pa hu ka nyiex paox grawm tix yam pon sawm,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Paox grawm,
tawx aux yaog lon khao mun loe lon liak.
6 Mawh kheu koe paox grawm aux tix kaux pa hoek ka nyiex aux,
aux ang koe ceu pa sang tawx nawh ih.’
7 Phan paox grawm maix pawk khaing daux nyiex ah nin:
‘Paw vi tuk tiak aux,
aux hoik nyeep ka siviex;
aux mai kawn praix aux khankix hoik it,
aux ang kaoh teei tix tawx ceu pa tid tix ka maix.’
8 Yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux krai ka pex,
viang nawh ang kaoh tix tawx khao mun ka pui an kheu hoik mawh nawh paox grawm tix,
nawh kawx tag sang caw tix kaoh kheu lox pa khiox khaw pui an ka nawh,
mai tawx ceu pa sang lo nawh ka nawh.
9 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
pex khiox khaw,
tom taug pon;
sawg tom taug yaox;
gleeh siviex tom taug paoh son pex.
10 Mawh kheu an pa khiox khaw,
tom taug pon;
pui pa sawg,
tom taug yaox;
pa gleuh siviex,
siviex tom taug paoh.
11 Ka daux pa mawh keeing daux pex khankix,
koe pui mawx yam mhaing kawn simex nawh kax,
sang teei si uing tawx ka nawh lie?
12 Yam mhaing ka tawm ia,
sang teei Tam-tim tawx ka nawh lie?
13 Viang pex hoik caox caig kawx,
mai hoik tawng tix tawx ceu pa mhawm ka kawn simex kawn bun tix;
phan mawh nan,
keeing pex Siyiex piang rao ma khex ang nie blawn tawx Khuan Singaix Singeh ka pui pa khiox khaw ka nawh lie?”
Ye-sux mai Piie-si-phux
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.22, 30; Lai Ma-kheu 3.20–27)
14 Ye-sux hoik grheu ka phi ang chix nye tix kaux hu,
khaix hoik lih hu phi an,
pui an hoik pon chix si kah.
Pui hun kied daox deng ka khawm uik,
15 bviang mawh nan,
ka daux kix koe pui pa ah nin:
“Nawh hoik pon grheu phi lih ka daux pa hoik een rhawm tix ka Piie-si-phux simiang phi heue.”
16 cHoik koe pui tix blah keud tix sang yuh nawh laux,
khiox khaw nawh yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
son sang jhi grawng kaing pa yuh nawh mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex.
17 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka ceu pa keud kix ceu pa tix,
tom ah nin ka kix:
“Meung haktiex mawx ang ah,
phan simah paox tix,
tag sang hoek krax laux leeing som nai;
nyiex tix nyiex phan simah ka paox tix,
tag sang laux leeing.
18 Phan Sa tan simah ka paox tix,
meung cie nawh sang chix kaux cong yuh ka mawh lie?
Pex krai grheu aux phi lih khaing pa een rhawm tix ka Piie-si-phux,
19 phan mawh nan,
pui gau tix mai pex grheu ka phi,
hoik een rhawm tix ka ceu pa tix lie?
Pui gau tix mai pex tag sang yuh mhai sakse grawng pa lud pex heue!
20 Tete te lai,
aux grheu phi lih phan aux een rhawm tix ka riang rao Siyiex,
in hoik mawh mhai sakse grawng siawp sibrawm Siyiex pa giex kaing ka daux pex heue.
21 “Koe pui naing pa grum kraung naing pa kied kua tix kaux,
yam pau nawh ka nyiex brawk ot tix,
sin ngao cie nawh hoik pon tix kaux cong.
22 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoek pui naing pa blawn ting riang khaing nawh pa kied kua tix kaux,
pon pex ka nawh,
tag teei ka can kraung naing pa hoik een nawh rhawm tix ka hu,
khaix an nawh guah ka kraung pa hoik briex lak tix heue.
23 d“Pa ang mawh pui pa yuh paox pie mai aux,
hoik mawh pa tieb tie ka aux;
pui pa ang khrawm tix tau mai aux,
hoik mawh pui pa yieh ka kaing yuh aux laux heue.”
Khuan pa seeing brai deeh hoek
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.43–45)
24 “Khuan pa seeing brai hoik lih hu khaing pui pa laig tix daux,
mai hoik tauh hu ka baoh haktiex dee kroh khawm uik,
sawg ka dee sang laux lee tix,
ang yaox,
tom ah nin:
‘Aux deeh hoek ka nyiex pa hoik ot tix ka sivoe an.’
25 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh deeh ing,
mai yaox nyiex an hoik pih tix mhawm singax.
26 Nawh baox deeh lih hu,
viex khuan pa kawn lheu caox caig khaing tix aliah mu hoek,
mai ot tau mai nawh.
Yuh nin,
krax ot pui an kawn lheu caox caig khaing singaix pa hoik luan heue.”
Koe buan son tete
27 Yam kawn si kah Ye-sux ka lox in,
koe bun tix kaux kaoh hoek khaing daux pui hun kok yo ka nawh tom ting ah nin:
“Pa nghe ka maix mai ka pa tawx maix peux khankix kied koe buan tete heue!”
28 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui pa nghiet ka lox Siyiex mai priex tix hawt,
tag mawh pa koe buan tete heue!”
Khiox khaw ka mhai pa daox pa am
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.38–42)
29 eYam an pui hun sivie ka Ye-sux,
Ye-sux ca nied tix krai lao ah nin:
“Pui singaix baux ju in kied caox caig yuh nin heue!
Kix lai mhaing tix yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am yuh nin,
viang mawh nan,
lheu khaing mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh ka Yaw-na,
ang lai baox koe mhai pa daox pa am tang ceu pa tawx kix yaox.
30 fYaw-na hoik lih mawh mhai pa daox pa am ka plak sivoe ngai pui Ni-ni-we yuh ka mawx,
kawn kawn pui kawx tag sang lih mawh mhai pa daox pa am ka plak sivoe ngai pui ka daux baux ju singaix in yuh nan nang.
31 gKa daux singaix pa prawng ca,
bun simiang haktiex plak seh tag sang cung tix hoek deuh phi map ka pui ka daux baux ju singaix in,
mawh kheu hoek nawh nghiet ka lox ping nyia cuyi pa krai simiang Saw-law-meung khaing krax lang blaong dee singai.
Aux krai ka pex,
dee in kaing ceu pa blawn ting ceu khaing Saw-law-meung hoik koe ka heue!
32 hKa daux singaix pa prawng ca,
pui Ni-ni-we tag sang kaoh cung tix deuh phi map ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik mhong kix ka gum lox pa sidiex Yaw-na,
kix tom loh rhawm rhi tix tik ceu pa caox mai yuh pa rauh pa see.
Aux krai ka pex,
ka dee in kawn koe kaing pa lheu ting khaing Yaw-na lie!
Kawk ngu pa riang to kaux
(Lai Ma-thaix 5.15; 6.22–23)
33 i“Ang koe pui pa tok kawk ngu mai mox ot ka daux tiex,
deeh tieh tix,
deuh greem phliex;
tieh tix,
nawh tee rien tix deuh ka piang jawk kawk ngu,
mai kieh pui pa laig daux nyiex yaox pa jhan riang.
34 Ngai maix awm kawk ngu to kaux.
Phan ngai maix mhawm,
to kaux maix riang khawm uik;
phan ngai maix laux,
to kaux maix tag faik khawm uik.
35 Mawh kheu nan,
tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
son ang pa riang pa koe plak daux maix sang tieh tix lih faik.
36 Phan to kaux maix naog ka pa riang,
mai ang koe pa faik pie ceeix kawx,
tag sang jhan riang khawm pon daog awm pa jhan riang kawk ngu ka maix heue.”
Ne raw ka pui Fa-li-saix mai ka sara lox tat jhawm prawng
(Lai Ma-thaix 23.1–36; Lai Ma-kheu 12.38–40)
37 Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux ka lox in kix,
pui Fa-li-saix hoek tix kok nawh sawm,
nawh tom laig ngawm tix ih priex tau mai kix.
38 Pui Fa-li-saix in yaox ang Ye-sux khoek taix tix sivoe sang sawm,
nawh kied daox deng kah.
39 Simiang Ye-sux tom krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Pex pa mawh pui Fa-li-saix hoik phak plak praix kawk pan singax singeh,
viang mawh nan,
ka plak daux pex hoik naog ka pa ghoing rhawm ghoing rhi mai pa caox pa caig.
40 Pui pa daox pa aw eue,
Siyiex pa phan yuh plak praix kawx ang hoik phan yuh nang plak daux lie?
41 Ca mawh tawx ceu pa ot daux kawk ka pui hot,
ka son pex hoik sawn tix mawh pa singax khawm uik heue.
42 j“Pex pa mawh pui Fa-li-saix tag sang si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!
Pex hoik teei ka ceu pa nyawm yuh priex preem khankix,
awm pa mawh lhax haw,
tau ki,
mai sidah bhawng tang ceu,
kao son tix son tan lu ka Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng yung ka pa rauh pa see mai krax muh ka Siyiex.
Tete te lai,
in tag mawh pa caw pex tix yuh;
phan mawh tang ceu kawx ka ang pon tix ang yuh eue.
43 “Pex pa mawh pui Fa-li-saix tag sang si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!
Pex muh tix ngawm ka nyiex sidiex pa blawk pui ngawm kah,
simie pui tawx krax nab yam yung yi ka tix sivoe ngai pui ka daux bang.
44 Pex hoik laux leeing heue!
Mawh kheu pex hoik awm si meeig pa ang koe mhai yuh nan,
pui hu ka piang kawx,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng ka heue.”
45 Koe sara lox tat jhawm prawng krai ka Ye-sux ah nin “Sara,
phan mawh nin pie yix kawx hoik map nai ka heue!”
46 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pex pa mawh cub sara lox tat jhawm prawng in kix tag sang si pup ka krax pa laux leeing heue!
Pex deuh beeh glawm pa cien ot ka piang klip pui tang kaux,
viang mawh nan,
to kaux kix ang cu tix tawx giien taix tix pie tix giien teei tix tieh ka beeh glawm kix.
47 Pex tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex hoik yuh si meeig son cao jhai,
mai cao jhai an kix hoik mawh pa pauh tax du pex yum.
48 Yuh nin,
pex hoik jhi grawng pa khaw rhawm pex ka kaing pa yuh tax du tix;
kix hoik pauh ka cao jhai yum,
mai pex deeh cox sum si meeig son cao jhai.”
49 “Mawh kheu nan,
ping nyia cuyi cie Siyiex hoik sax krai ah nin:
‘Aux tag sang ploi cao jhai mai kawn cau hu ka kix dee an;
koe kix tix blah pui tag sang pauh yum,
koe tix blah tag sang kham krax pa tuk tiak.’
50 Mawh kheu nan,
jauh tom phan ka haktiex dix dix,
can phi map pa pauh ka cao jhai khawm uik,
51 kpa mawh pa jauh tix pauh ka Ya-paw,
tom hoek ka nham pa pauh ka Sa-cia-li-yax ka si nax khu dee tan mai ka nyiex pa kied singax,
tag sang kieh pui ka daux baux singaix ju in plud kah.
Mawh tete,
aux krai ka pex,
ka daux pui baux singaix ju in tag sang kham krax ciyang in kix khawm uik heue!
52 “Pex pa mawh cub sara lox tat jhawm prawng tag sang si pup krax pa laux leeing heue!
Pex hoik teei tix giex ka jiak saw krao pa paoh ka ping nyia krax tawng krax yung,
viang mawh nan,
to kaux ang rhawm laig,
mai ang rhawm kieh nang pui tang kaux laig heue!”
53 Ye-sux hoik lih khaing dee an,
mai hoik jauh tix yaox krax pa thung thiang sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai pui Fa-li-saix ka tix,
kix chawk nawh ka grawng kaing ceu pa nu tix pawk tom nie,
54 son sang sawg tix yuh nawh laux kheu lox pa hoik krai nawh heue.
