Kok yo ka pui Yi-seu-liex loh rhawm rhi tix
4
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
phan pex sang deeh rhawm rhi tix,
viex pex deeh hoek ka aux dee in heue!
Phan pex tik ka riang rao krub pui pa koeng aux kah,
ang lai baox deeh kieh yung yang,
2 pex tag chix tix teei rhawm pa mhawm,
rhawm pa rauh see,
mai rhawm pa khaw caw tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux aux.
Yuh nin,
meung ka piang haktiex tom taug chix khiox khaw buan son ka aux,
yaog yaw ka aux khawm uik heue.”
3 aSimiang Siyiex krai ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix paih ka haktiex pa ang pui nyang sax thai sax pheu,
paw vi sau simie ka daux pang kat.
4 Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
pex tag caw tix Pat-hak ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
mai tan to kaux tix ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pex.
Phan ang yuh nan,
krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pex tag sang chix cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm,
mai kieh krax song rhawm aux rup,
mai ang koe pui pa pon preet ka heue.”
Krax laux leeing khaing plak praix hoek tix ghlat ka pui Yo-tax
5 Tag caw tix phlu teet tawng hu ka piang meung khawm uik,
mai ploi lox pa so rhawm pui haing,
kok yo lox ting si num ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung:
khrawm paox pex,
mai nied tix to laig daux veng pa hoik kieh kaux cong heue!
6 Tag caw tix sum mhai pa jhi hu plak Shi-ang heue!
Paw vi gla bawg yam,
nied tix to mox tix heue!
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ploi krax laux leeing pa ting lih hoek;
krax laux krax leeing pa ting tag sang hoek khaing haktiex plak laong.
7 Cao pa yuh meung ka piang haktiex laux leeing hoik yaog tix hu,
awm sivai sang si pa preeng tix hu ka daux nong khaox yuh nan.
Nawh tag sang hoek tix yuh pui Yo-tax laux leeing.
Haktiex pui Yo-tax tag sang lih tix riem,
ka daux veng ang koe pui pa ot kah.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
laux rhawm yiiem grai,
mawh kheu krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka pui Yo-tax ang bax naox tix heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ka daux singaix an,
simiang mai cao giex kaing tag sang grai riang rao;
cao giex kaing li tag sang daong ngai tix ang koe lox krai,
mai cao jhai kawx tag sang yaoh yee heue.”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tieb tie ka ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
maix rub hoik pie lhien ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung heue!
Maix krai sang koe pa nhim,
tete te lai,
vaig gawn pa lawm hoik ot ka ghok kix heue.”
11 Yam hoek daux bawg yam an,
koe pui tag sang krai ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
koe bhaung pa si aoh hoek khaing daux tiag daux tu,
mai phlu hoek ka kix,
ang mawh bhaung awn pa phlu ka kam an,
12 mai mawh bhaung pa blawn ting pa tawx Siyiex hoek an.
Simiang Siyiex hoek tix prawng ca ka pui cie tix heue.
Pui Yo-tax kham krax pa tuk tiak pui koeng
13 Jhak heue,
pui koeng awm bua pai awm lung pua tix hoek;
ling law naing nawh awm bhaung klia pa phlu tix hoek,
brung naing kawn brax khaing klang ting.
Ex hoik laux ju heue!
Ex hoik laux ju heue!
14 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
tag caw tix si ghrah phi map pa koe ka daux rhawm rhi tix lih,
son sang pon maix ka krax taoh.
Maix sang kawn braong doe ka phi map hoek yam mawx lie?
15 Nghiet heue,
koe kawn cau hoek khaing haktiex ceu pui Tang mai ka gawng Yi-fa-liien,
mai krai kaing ceu pa ang mhawm.
16 Kix hoek tix ne raw ka meung haktiex ku meung,
mai krai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
koe pui koeng tag sang kaoh hoek khaing dee singai.
Kix tag sang kok yo tom ting,
mai puing ka veng Yo-tax ku mu,
17 lai lawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
awm pau cao si peem ka ma si peem tix yuh nan.
In hoik mawh kheu pa tieh vheen pui Yo-tax ka Simiang Siyiex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
18 Pui Yo-tax eue,
ceu pa yuh maix hoik kieh krax laux krax leeing hoek ka piang maix.
Phi map maix kieh maix kha rhawm tix,
mai hoik suat ka rhawm maix doh heue.
Yie-li-mi laux rhawm son paox ceu tix
19 Eue,
aux ang lai pon kham saux tix heue!
Rhawm rhi aux hoik hax,
mai ang lai pon ngawm.
Aux mhong lox luah teed tawng pui naing,
lox si num naing.
20 Krax laux krax leeing si doe paox tix hoek;
meung haktiex hoik nhik hoik riem.
Nyiex gox bang aux hoik grup dum mawx dum an,
man nyiex gox bang hoik griah.
21 Aux kawn sang kham geu krax naing miex glang lie?
Kawn sang pon tix mhong ka lox teed tawng naing miex ding lie?
22 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
pui cie aux mawh pui pa daox aw;
kix ang tawng ka aux.
Kix awm kawn nyawm pa tiiem krax keud krax sawn,
ang koe ping nyia cuyi pie ceeix kawx.
Kix tawng ngie tix yuh ka kaing ceu pa caox khankix,
ang tawng tix yuh ka kaing ceu pa mhawm khankix heue.
Yie-li-mi grox yaox ka krax laux leeing pa sang lih tix mawh
23 Aux kok simi tix jhak ka piang haktiex,
mai riem yi khawm uik;
aux maong tix jhak ka rao ma,
mai hoik faik ang koe pa riang.
24 Aux maong ngai tix jhak ka gawng ting,
mai ka gawng iak;
gawng ting yung yang,
gawng iak yung yang.
25 Aux ang yaox pui tix kaux kawx;
pie kawn sim kawx hoik pu hu khawm uik.
26 Kaung mhawm tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag pa riem;
daux veng hoik awm pum tiex,
mawh kheu hoik hieh Simiang Siyiex krax pa song rhawm tix kah.
27 Simiang Siyiex hoik krai,
piang haktiex khawm uik tag lih tix riem nhik,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang sang yuh laux leeing khawm uik.
28 Ka piang haktiex tag sang yiiem sang ngia,
rao ma tieh tix lih faik.
Gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ang doh chix tieh loh tix;
kaing pa but nawh tix yuh ang doh chix tieh loh tix.
29 Yam mhong pui pa ot daux veng ka lox pa si num pui naing brug brung mai lox pa si num pui puing te,
pui ku kaux tag sang sau riang to mox tix;
koe pui tix blah to mox tix ka daux nong khaox,
laig mox tix ot ka daux dawh rang.
Kawn pui hoik hu,
daux veng hoik yi!
30 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
maix hoik laux leeing heue!
Yuh ka mawx maix lai kawn ceep ka kraung ngawh lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai kawn ceep ka kraung khri pa ting ngawh lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai kawn meug ka ngai baoh tix lie?
Krax pa jawg maix tix koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
Paox muh maix hoik tik vud ka maix,
tag sang pauh ka maix.
31 Aux mhong lox kok yo pa awm bun saux yha yam sang keh tix yuh nan;
aux mhong lox riag yo pa awm bun pa liang tix keh kawn yuh nan.
An hoik mawh krax saux krax ngia Yie-lu-sa-leung;
nawh nyeu ka taix tix khawm ra khawn,
mai ah nin:
Hoik laux leeing heue,
hoik laux leeing heue!
Kix tag sang hoek tix pauh ka aux heue!
