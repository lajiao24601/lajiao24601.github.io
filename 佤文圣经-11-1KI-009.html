Siyiex baox lih bleeh ka Saw-law-meung
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 7.11–22)
9
Khaix hoik sum simiang Saw-law-meung nyiex buan,
nyiex haw,
mai nyiex tang mu pa sang sum nawh khankix,
2 aSimiang Siyiex baox lih bleeh ka nawh,
awm yam lih bleeh ka Ci-piien sivoe an yuh nan.
3 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux hoik mhong lox pa rawg maix buan.
Aux tag kieh nyiex buan pa hoik sum maix in,
guah tix lih mawh pa singax singeh,
son sang kieh mawh dee krub ka aux leeing ju;
aux tag sang chix kia ngien ka tom si dauh,
priex ka tom si dauh.
4 Phan maix awm keeing tix Ta-wue yum rhawm krub ka aux yuh nan,
priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
mai yuh awm lox pa hoik tawx aux,
5 baux tag sang giex tom kaux ka lox pa ap aux tix deuh ka keeing maix Ta-wue,
pa mawh doeh ceu cad nawh tag sang kuang ka piang Yi-seu-liex leeing ju.
6 “Phan maix mai doeh ceu cad maix ang nghiet aux,
mai ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx aux ka pex,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
7 aux tag sang grheu pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka kix.
Khaix an mai singai hu khaing nyiex buan in pa hoik guah aux mawh pa singax,
pa mawh dee krub ka aux.
Pui ku dauh ku dee tag sang map nai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
iak jhak ka kix.
8 dNyiex buan in tag sang lih mawh dee riemc;
pui pa hu krax an tag sang daox deng kah,
mai map nyiah tix chawk ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx lai yuh nin ka meung in mai ka nyiex buan in lie?’
9 Pui tag sang pawk ah nin:
‘Mawh kheu hoik singai hu kix khaing Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix.
Simiang Siyiex hoik viex tax du kix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
mai kix lai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux yuh nin,
mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom ploi krax laux krax leeing in lih piang kix heue.’ ”
Saw-law-meung yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Si-lang
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 8.1–2)
10 Saw-law-meung sum nyiex buan mai nyiex haw simiang tix nga num.
11 Si-lang pa mawh simiang Thai-eux tawx khaox si oe bhawng,
khaox gix khawm can khri pa sang lo Saw-law-meung ka daux kaing khankix.
Khaix hoik kaing,
simiang Saw-law-meung tawx veng haktiex Cia-li-lix tix nga mu ka Si-lang.
12 Si-lang hoek kia ngien veng an kix khaing Thai-eux,
nawh kied ang khaw rhawm kah,
13 tom ah nin ka Saw-law-meung:
“Pux aik eue,
in mawh veng pa tawx maix aux cie lie?”
Mawh kheu nin,
haktiex an tom in singaix ca kawn kok mawh Cia-pu-leue.
14 Si-lang tawx khri ka Saw-law-meung been pon rheng kongcing heue.
Kaing tang ceu pa yuh Saw-law-meung
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 8.3–18)
15 Saw-law-meung sawg pui hoek yuh kaing sum ka nyiex buan mai nyiex haw,
kieh pui deeh cox si dawm ka haktiex dee klawk plak lih singaix veng,
mai sum guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung kah.
Nawh kawx kieh pui yuh kaing in kix deeh cox sum ka Sha-saw,
Mi-cie-to,
mai veng Ci-seux khankix.
16 (Simiang Ai-cix hoik puing ka Ci-seux sivoe an,
pon teei kah,
mai hoik pauh pui Cia-nang pa ot daux veng yum kah,
mai gleeing ka veng.
Khaix an,
yam mex kawn bun nawh ka Saw-law-meung,
nawh tom tawx veng in kix ka kawn bun tix kieh mawh pa viex mai koe mex tix.
17 Saw-law-meung deeh cox sum ka Ci-seux.)
Saw-law-meung baox teei pui yuh kaing tix deeh cox sum ka veng Paw Heu-loeng,
18 Pa-la,
Yo-tax pa koe ka daux tiag Ta-mawx,
19 veng pa mox kraung khrai ot daux,
veng pa deuh ling law ot daux,
mai veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
veng Li-pa-noeng,
khawm veng pa keud nawh tix sang sum khawm uik ka haktiex ka daux meung ku dauh.
20-21 Pui yuh kaing pa sawg Saw-law-meung mawh doeh ceu cad pui Cia-nang,
pa ang pui Yi-seu-liex bax pauh yum khankix yam hoik pon kuang kix ka haktiex Cia-nang.
Ka daux pui an kix,
koe pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Heux,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we,
mai pui Yie-pu-see khankix.
Doeh ceu cad kix ca kawn mawh siliex cawng tom hoek yam in.
22 Saw-law-meung ang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh siliex cawng,
mai kieh kix yuh ngie pui naing,
simiang naing,
simiang ting naing,
simiang naing pa ting,
cao kaing ling brung,
mai pui naing brug brung khankix.
23 Saw-law-meung koe cao kuang kaing phuan yieh tix phuan kaux,
mai kuang kaing ku ceu khawm uik heue.
24 Moeng Saw-law-meung,
pa mawh kawn bun simiang Ai-cix,
khaix hoik gah nawh tix hu khaing veng Ta-wue,
hoek ka nyiex haw pa khraox sum Saw-law-meung son nawh,
Saw-law-meung hoik cox haktiex si dawm dee klawk plak lih singaix veng heue.
25 fSaw-law-meung tix num loe bawg,
tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka piang khu dee tan pa hoik sum nawh son Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh kawx gleeing nyawng bhawng ka Simiang Siyiex.
Yuh nin,
nawh hoik hoik kaing pa sum nyiex buan heue.
26 Simiang Saw-law-meung baox yuh reu ka haktiex Yi-tong ka praok Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
dex ka veng Yi-shuing Cia-Piie pa koe ka haktiex Yi-lux,
mai kieh lih koe pui tix cub ka daux reu.
27 Simiang Si-lang ploi pui pa kub rhawm keud tix blah khaing pui pa ot daux reu tix,
hu daux reu tau mai pui cie Saw-law-meung ka piang rawm sigrit sigra.
28 Kix brug reu hoek ka Eu-fe,
mai viex khri hoek tawx ka Saw-law-meung tix mheen pon rheng kongcing kaoh khaing dee an heue.
