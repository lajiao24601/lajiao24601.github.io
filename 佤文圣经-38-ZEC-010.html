Simiang Siyiex cu tix taoh teem
10
Bawg yam rung praix,
pex tag caw tix khiox khaw lhiex lih ka Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex kieh pai awm tawx lhiex lih ka pui khawm uik,
mai kieh kaung ma pui ku kaux klai.
2 Kawn pui chawk cheng ka riang rao mai ka cao si pawk,
pa pon kix hoik mawh lox pa nieb krai.
Koe pui tix blah kah ca maox,
mai hoik mawh son sang pie ka pex;
krax pa nyawm rhawm pex ang koe ceu.
Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui vu vie vu viang,
awm kawn yung pa grai.
Kix si pup ka krax kha rhawm,
mawh kheu ang koe cao liang pa duk thung ka kix heue.
3 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux song rhawm ka pui tang ceu pa kuang ka piang pui cie tix;
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
Pui Yo-tax hoik mawh cie aux;
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing tag sang kia ngien ka kix.
Kix tag sang lih mawh brung naing pa koe riang son aux.
4 Ka daux kix tag sang laug lih simiang,
simiang ting,
mai simiang naing tag sang lih tix kuang ka piang pui cie auxa.
5 Pui Yo-tax tag sang awm pui pa kua,
mai pon giex ka pui koeng yuh nan,
mai baox awm phawng tiex pa dig pui ka daux laih yuh nan.
Mawh kheu ot Simiang Siyiex tau mai kix,
kix tom kua ka naing,
mai pon pex ka pui naing brug brung pui koeng heue.
 
6 Aux tag sang kieh pui Yo-tax haok huan;
aux tag sang kieh nyiex ma pui Yi-seu-liex pon pex.
Aux tag sang caox rhawm ka kix,
mai viex kix ing ka haktiex ot tix.
Kix tag sang awm yam ang aux baox tik vud ka tix yuh nan.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix;
aux tag sang nghiet ka lox rawg buan kix.
7 Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang awm pui naing pa kua yuh nan,
rhawm rhi kix nyawm,
awm pui pa yoek ka plai yuh nan.
Kawn praix kix tag sang doh keud ka krax pa pex bawg in;
kix tag sang gaux rhawm graoh kheu kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
8 Aux tag sang kok yo ka pui cie tix,
mai khrawm ka kix.
Aux tag sang taoh ka kix,
mai kieh kix haoh hun awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan.
9 Viang aux hoik prung prang kix hu ka meung piang haktiex,
mai kix ca kawn sang doh keud ka aux yam ot tix ka haktiex dee singai.
Kix mai kawn praix kix tag sang pon yaox tix klawd ju,
mai deeh ing ka nyiex ma tix.
10 Aux tag sang viex kix ing ka nyiex khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
mai viex kix deeh hoek khaing Ya-sux.
Aux tag sang deuh kix ot ka Ci-liex mai ka Li-pa-noeng;
haktiex khawm uik hoik naog ka kawn pui.
11 Yam tiang kix ka rawm sigrit sigra pa song,
aux tag sang lauh rawm puk pak,
rawm klawng Ni-law tag sang kroh.
Pui Ya-sux pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tag sang tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi;
pui Ai-cix pa ting riang tag sang grai riang rao.
12 Aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix ting lhaong;
kix tag sang krub ka aux,
nghiet ka aux.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
