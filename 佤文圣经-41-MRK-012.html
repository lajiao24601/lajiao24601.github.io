Phek tiiem ka cao vai pa caox
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.33–43; Lai Lu-cia 20.9–19)
12
aYe-sux baox teei gum lox phek tiiem sidiex gau ka kix.
Nawh ah nin:
“Koe pui tix kaux paih ka si peem plix shiet tix dauh,
mai lawm law die vung viang,
kaong daux greeik plai koe ka daux si peem tix mu,
mai sum nyiex pau koe ka tix lhang,
khaix an,
teei tix tawx pui vai ka si peem plix shiet,
to kaux nawh tom lih hu khaing nyiex.
2 Hoek bawg yam pieh plix shiet,
nawh ploi siliex tix hu teei ka pa khaw nawh tix pon ka pui pa vai tix yuh si peem.
3 Cao vai si peem an giex ka siliex an,
mai lauh ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh ing khawm taix yi tix.
4 Cao nyiex si peem baox ploi siliex tang kaux hu;
kix baox lauh ka kaing nawh,
khaix an,
map nai ka nawh.
5 Cao nyiex si peem baox ploi siliex hu tix kaux,
kix pauh ka nawh.
Kix baox yuh nang nan ka pui tom nie,
lauh ka tix blah,
pauh ka tix blah.
6 Cao nyiex si peem deuk ngie kawn simex pa kied muh tix kaux khaing.
Dee nghoik,
ploi nawh hu ka cao vai si peem,
nawh keud:
‘Kix tee rien tix nab yam yung yi ka kawn simex aux.’ ”
7 Viang mawh nan,
cao vai si peem an kum kawng paox tix ah nin:
“ ‘In mawh pui pa sang plud ka cao nyiex si peem,
hoek heue,
ex pauh ka nawh,
sin ngao cie nawh tag mawh cie ex khaix heue!’
8 Kix tom giex ka kawn simex an,
pauh ka nawh,
mai tik to kaux yum nawh ka plak praix si peem plix shiet heue.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux chawk ah nin:
“Yuh nin,
cao nyiex si peem plix shiet tag sang yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh tag sang hoek tix pauh ka pui vai si peem,
mai deeh teei si peem plix shiet tawx pui tang kaux vai heue.
10 bAng pex bax phat ka baux gum lox phuk lai sigang khri in lie?
Simaox pa hoik tik cang mai yuh nyiex
hoik lih mawh simaox jaong nyiex pa blawn ting ceu.
11 In mawh kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
ka plak sivoe ngai ex kied mawh pa daox deng heue!”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Yo-thaix tawng ka gum lox phek tiiem pa krai Ye-sux hoik mawh pa ne raw ka kix,
kix tom keud tix sang giex ka nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
kix lhat ka pui hun,
kix tom lih hu khaing nawh heue.
Grawng pa tawx mau koh keen ka Kai-sax
(Lai Ma-thaix 22-15, 22; Lai Lu-cia 20.19–26)
13 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah mai cub caw Si-luix tawx kix hu ka Ye-sux,
mai keud tix sang sawg dee lud lox nawh son sang pauh ka nawh.
14 Kix krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
yix hoik tawng mawh maix pui pa rauh see tix kaux;
maix ang kuang ka keud pui ceu pa tix,
mai ang jhak nang ka pak ngai pui,
maix hoik ca kaux sijax ka pa sidiex gau tix pui ka gum lox Siyiex.
Khiox khaw maix krai ka yix,
phan tawx mau koh keen ka Kai-sax Vawng Law-ma,
mawh lud ang lud ka lox tat jhawm prawng ex lie?
Sang tawx ang ex tawx lie?”
15 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka krax keud pa pie lhien kix,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai keud tix sang yuh aux laux?
Teei mau luah tix plah tawx aux jhak!”
16 Kix tawx mau luah ka nawh tix plah,
Ye-sux chawk ah nin:
“Riang pa koe ka mau in mai ceu kaux pa koe ka mawh mawx?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh Kai-sax heue.”
17 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
cie Kai-sax tawx ka Kai-sax,
cie Siyiex tawx ka Siyiex heue.”
Kix mhong ka lox in,
mai kied daox deng ka nawh heue.
Grawng pa deeh kaoh im
(Lai Ma-thaix 22.23–33; Lai Lu-cia 20.27–40)
18 cKoe pui Sa-tu-kai pa ang yum rhawm ka grawng pa kaoh im tix blah hoek ka Ye-sux.
19 dKix ah nin:
“Sara,
Maw-si hoik yuh lox tat jhawm prawng son ex ah nin:
‘Phan pui tix kaux yum,
prah ka moeng tix,
ang bax koe kawn,
pux aik nawh tag caw tix teei tix moeng ka o tix,
son sang kieh plud ka doeh ceu cad pux ke tix.’
20 Sivoe an,
koe pux aik aliah kaux yuh nin:
pux ke lhong koe moeng,
nawh yum,
ang bax koe kawn;
21 nyi teei tix moeng ka o tix,
tom yum nang,
ang bax koe kawn;
sam hoik yuh nan nang.
22 Kix pux aik aliah kaux hoik moeng ka bun an khawm uik,
hoik yum khawm uik,
ang bax koe kawn.
Dee si dauh,
bun an kawx yum nang.
23 Yuh nin,
ka daux singaix pa kaoh im,
yam deeh kaoh im kix khaing krax yum,
bun in sang sawn tix mawh moeng pui mawx lie?
Mawh kheu kix pux aik khawm aliah kaux hoik moeng ka nawh khawm uik heue.”
24 Ye-sux pawk ka kix ah nin:
“Pex lud heue,
yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh kheu ang pex tawng ka phuk lai sigang khri,
mai ang tawng nang ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex.
25 Yam kaoh im kix khaing krax yum,
kix tag sang awm kawn cau Siyiex daux rao ma yuh nan,
mai ang koe mex koe moeng heue.
26 ePhan mawh grawng pa kaoh im pui yum,
ang pex bax phat ka lox lao been pa tiiem daux lai Maw-si,
grawng pa cap ngu daux pang kat an lie?
Siyiex krai ka Maw-si ah nin:
‘Aux mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Siyiex Yi-sha,
Siyiex Ya-keux.’
27 Dee bleeh gum lox in krai mawh Siyiex cie pui im ju,
ang mawh Siyiex pui yum.
Pex hoik lud khawm uik heue!”
Gum lox ping nyap pa blawn ting khaing paox tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 22.34–40; Lai Lu-cia 10.25–28)
28 fKoe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mhong pa thung thiang kix ka paox tix,
mai tawng hoik kied chix pawk Ye-sux ka lox pui Sa-tu-kai,
tom hoek tix chawk Ye-sux ka kaing tix ceu ah nin:
“Ka daux gum lox ping nyap mawh grawng pa yuh ka mawx pa blawn ting ceu lie?”
29 gYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Mawh grawng tix pa ah nin:
‘Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex caw tix nghiet heue!
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex hoik mawh cao tix kaux khaing.
30 Maix tag caw tix muh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi,
khawm ju,
khawm pa chix keud mai khawm riang rao tix khawm uik.’
31 hKa daux grawng ra mawh nin:
‘Maix tag caw tix muh ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan.’
Ang lai koe gum lox ping nyap tang ceu pa lheu ting ceu khaing in kix heue.”
32 iSara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri an krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara pa krai maix khaw heue!
Awm hoik krai maix ka yuh nan,
Siyiex mawh tix kaux khaing,
lheu khaing nawh ang lai koe tang kaux.
33 jTag caw tix muh Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi,
khawm riang rao tix khawm uik,
mai baox muh ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan.
In kawn lheu ting ceu khaing tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai krax tan tang ceu ka Siyiex tom nie heue.”
34 Ye-sux tawng lox pa pawk nawh hoik grum naog mai ping nyia,
tom krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix ang lai singai khaing meung Siyiex heue.”
Jauh yam an,
mai ang lai koe pui pa pon baox chawk ka Ye-sux heue.
Grawh grawng Khrit
(Lai Ma-thaix 22.41–46; Lai Lu-cia 20.41–44)
35 Yam sidiex gau Ye-sux pui ka daux nyiex buan nawh krai lox pa ah nin tix grawng:
“Sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri yuh ka mawx lai ah Khrit mawh kawn praix Ta-wue lie?
36 kTa-wue hoik sax ah nin ka lox daux Khuan Singax Singeh:
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka simiang aux:
maix ngawm ka plak khawn dawm aux,
krox aux kieh pui koeng ka maix kreet tix ot greem caong maix heue.
37 Ta-wue hoik tang kok nawh mawh ‘simiang’,
Khrit sang chix mawh kawn praix Ta-wue yuh ka mawx lie?”
Tag caw rien tix ka sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri
(Lai Ma-thaix 23.1, 36; Lai Lu-cia 20.45–47)
Pui hun hoik muh tix nghiet ka lox pa krai Ye-sux khawm uik.
38 Yam sidiex gau ka kix,
nawh ah nin:
“Tag caw rien tix ka sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri.
Kix muh tix ceep ka kraung dha hu daux laih,
simie pui tawx krax nab yam yung yi ka tix sivoe ngai pui ka daux bang,
39 mai baox muh tix ngawm ka nyiex sidiex pa blawk pui ngawm,
mai ka dee ngawm pa koe sivoe yam yuh poi.
40 Kix hoik bleet ka sin ngao cie miex mai,
khaix an rawg buan tom rheeng ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
Kix tag sang kham krax ciyang pa lang teung kin heue!”
Krax tan miex mai
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.1–4)
41 Ye-sux ngawm ka see krao nyiex buan,
mai sang jhak tix yaox ka pui hun sang sau mau ka daux teug tan yuh ka mawx.
Pui pa koe mau khankix hoik sau mau tom nie ka daux teug tan;
42 khaix an,
koe miex mai tix kaux hoek,
nawh sau mau tawng ra plak iak,
hoik khaw been tix piak mau.
43 Ye-sux kok pui gau tix hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
pa sau miex mai in daux teug tan kawn lheu khaing pui tang kaux khankix.
44 Pui tang kaux hoik tan lu khaing ka daux sin ngao pa haoh lheu tix;
viang mawh nan,
miex mai in hoik kied hot sibhawm,
mai nawh hoik tan lu ka mau can pa koe tix khawm uik heue.”
