46
Singaix bawg yam si dauh siyiex cie Pa-pi-loeng hoik hoek heue!
Pui hoik sax krub ka pui Pi-leux mai Ni-paw sivoe an,
yam in hoik tang ot ka piang blawg la,
lih mawh beeh glawm khawng brie pa cien.
2 Riang rao krub pui ang pon taoh ka to kaux tix;
kix lih mawh kraung khrai pa pon khaing daux naing,
mai pui hoik viex hu heue.
In hoik mawh singaix bawg yam pa si dauh siyiex cie Pa-pi-loeng heue.
 
3 Kawn praix Ya-keux eue,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux;
pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix eue,
tag caw tix nghiet khawm uik.
Yam keh pex lih khaing daux vaig miex tix,
aux hoik chix rien khre ka pex.
4 Aux mawh Siyiex pex,
aux tag sang kia ngien ka pex,
tom hoik paing hauk kaing pex.
Aux phan yuh ka pex,
mai eei nang pex im ju;
aux tag sang teem coi ka pex,
mai taoh ka pex heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
pex tag sang tiag aux mai pui mawx lie?
Ka greem rao ma koe pui mawx pa awm aux lie?
6 Koe pui paoh ka bau hak,
mai teei khri lih khaing daux ceeix,
baox ciang mau ot ka piang jiang tix blah;
kix vai tix siao ka cang mai yuh ka riang rao krub pui,
khaix an ngeeh kaing tix krub ka riang an heue.
7 Kix yaog tix klawm ka riang an,
mai klawm ot ka piang klip tix,
deuh kah,
kieh nawh cung ka dee an,
riang rao an tom ang lai baox deeh pon chuh.
Koe pui rawg buan ka riang an,
mai riang an ang chix pawk;
riang an kawx ang chix pon taoh pui luat luan khaing krax kha rhawm kha rhi heue.
 
8 Pui pa koe phi map eue,
tag caw tix doh keud kah,
pex tag caw keud tix yaox ka can kaing pa hoik yuh aux khawm uik.
9 Tag caw tix doh keud ka kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh singaix pa hoik luan;
aux mawh Siyiex,
mai ang lai koe tang kaux;
aux mawh Siyiex,
mai ang lai koe tang kaux pa pon tiag tix mai aux.
10 Tom yam jauh aux hoik dha tix krai ka singaix pa si dauh;
aux hoik dawg krai pao ka kaing pa sang lih tix hoek ka daux singaix pa sang hoek.
Aux ah nin,
aux ang doh tieh loh ka kaing pa keud aux tix sang yuh lih;
alo aux,
mai aux tag sang yuh lih tete.
11 Aux tag sang kok pui tix kaux hoek khaing plak lih singaix;
nawh tag sang awm klang ting pa pu tix lih hoek,
yuh grum naog ka kaing pa keud aux tix sang yuh.
Aux hoik krai pao yuh nin,
mai aux tag sang yuh lih tete heue.
 
12 Nghiet ka aux heue,
pui pa kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
pex kawn ce krax pa pon pex singai khaing pex;
13 to kaux aux tag sang viex krax pa pex hoek.
Singaix bawg yam an ang lai singai heue.
Aux ang doh kieh krax pa pex lha hoek.
Aux tag sang taoh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kieh Yi-seu-liex pon min plak ngai.
