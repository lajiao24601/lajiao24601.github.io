Simiang Sai-lio-hu giex ka kaing Pa-leu-see-thang, kix mawh doeh ceu pui simiang naing Ya-li-sang-tax tix kaux, pui an hoik sax giex ka kaing Shui-li-yax baux num sivoe 198-166
