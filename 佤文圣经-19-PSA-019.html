Min pak ngai pa phan pa yuh Siyiex
19
Rao ma krai pao min plak ngai Siyiex;
daux rao ma sidiex ka kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik.
2 Hoik krai lao ka ku singaix,
hoik krai hu ka ku sawm;
3 Ang koe lox tid tix,
ang mhong lox luah tid tix.
4 bKheu nan,
lox krai kixa hoik tauh ka daux kawn pui;
lox kix hoik tauh ka caong daog haktiex.
Siyiex yuh nyiex gox man ka rao ma son singaix;
5 singaix awm simex pa yuh ka paox lih khaing nyiex it tix,
awm pui pa ting riang pa gaux rhawm yam lawng tix krax.
6 Nawh lih khaing rao ma plak in,
mai via tix hoek ka rao ma plak an;
bhawm pa hax nawh hoik tauh ka can ceu pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex
7 Lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex hoik grum naog,
mai hoik kieh ju kawn pui tieh tix lih mawh pa khraox;
gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex kaux cong,
mai hoik kieh pui pa daox aw pon ping nyia cuyi.
8 Lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex khaw caw,
mai hoik kieh pui pa nghiet ka pon krax gaux rhawm;
gum lox pa sidiex gau Simiang Siyiex sidawng sidu,
mai hoik kieh rhawm rhi kawn pui riang.
9 Been daux krax cie Simiang Siyiex c singax singeh,
mai koe leeing ju leeing phan;
krax prawng krax ca Simiang Siyiex khaw caw,
mai rauh see tom si dauh sidu.
10 Ceu in kix kawn lheu ngawh khaing khri,
mai kawn lheu khaing khri pa mhawm;
ceu in kix kawn lheu tie khaing rawm hia,
mai kawn lheu singax khaing rawm hia.
11 Ceu in kix khawm uik hoik kieh kawm cau maix koe krax rien rhawm rhi tix;
siliex priex tix hawt,
mai pon krax pa mhawm tom ang lai chix uik chix leeing heue.
 
12 Pui mawx pon chix yaox ka krax pa lud loe to kaux tix lie?
Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka krax lud loe pa ang aux su tix yuh.
13 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux su tix yuh ka phi map,
paw vi kieh phi map giex gang ka aux.
Yuh nin,
aux tom pon tix mawh pui pa ang koe dee lud,
son ang lieh lheu yuh phi map pa ting heue.
 
14 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh nyiex dee mox aux tix,
cao pa taoh ka aux eue,
kieh gum lox aux,
rhawm keud aux pon khaw ka rhawm maix liak!
