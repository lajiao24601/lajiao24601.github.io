Krai grawng pui pa koeng ka Yi-seu-liex
129
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix krai heue;
jauh tom yam mawh maix kawn nyawm,
pui koeng hoik tuk tiak tuk te ka maix yuh ka mawx.
2 Jauh tom yam mawh aux kawn nyawm hoek yam in,
pui koeng hoik tuk tiak tuk te ka aux tom kin,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax pon pex ka aux.
3 Kix hoik deuh rhoi khaix mat tom raux ot ka piang blawg aux,
hoik awm rawng rhoi khaix thai pa raux pa lang pa kaong pui yuh kaing khankix.
4 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex mawh pa rauh see;
nawh hoik ploi aux lih khaing daux pa yuh siliex cawng heue.
 
5 Kieh pui pa koeng khoi ka Shi-ang an kix,
pon krax kaig krax tua ka daux pa ang tix pex.
6 Kieh kix awm rib pa kuah ot ka piang braux nyiex,
yam ang nyang bax huan hoik kroh hoik vhit;
7 ang koe pui pa hu vawg hu khrawm,
mawh kheu ang kub tix mawh tix mad.
8 Ang koe pui pa lawng krax tix kaux kawx pa ah nin:
kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka maix heue!
Ex nawb buan ka maix ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex heue!
