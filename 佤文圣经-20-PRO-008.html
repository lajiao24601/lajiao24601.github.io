Yaog yaw ping nyia cuyi
8
aNghiet heue,
ping nyia cuyi kok yo,
krax tawng krax yung ploi lox tix lih.
2 Nawh cung ka praok krax piang dee choek,
mai cung ka gah krax.
3 Nawh ot ka diang siviex veng,
mai ot ka praok siviex veng,
kok yo ah nin:
4 kawn pui eue,
aux sang krai ka pex;
aux sang kok yo ka kawn pui ku kaux pa ot ka piang haktiex.
5 Pex ang koe rhawm lhak lie?
Caw gau tix koe ping nyia cuyi heue!
Pex daox aw lie?
Caw sawg ka krax keud krax sawn heue!
6 Nghiet heue,
gum lox aux kied sidaing mhawm;
gum lox aux singax singeh.
7 Can pa krai aux mawh pa mawh pa meh;
aux koeng ka pa pie lhien.
8 Gum lox aux hoik mawh tete khawm uik,
ang koe pa kawk pa vawk,
ang koe pa pie lhien.
9 Ka son pui pa koe krax keud krax sawn,
mai hoik sidawng ka khawm uik;
ka son pui pa lhak lhiao,
mai hoik sidawng ka khawm uik.
10 Cu tix laug ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux,
ang teei mau luah;
cu tix rieb ngai lig ngai lai,
ang teei khri heue.
 
11 Aux mawh ping nyia cuyi,
aux lheu khaing siang;
can pa sawg maix ang koe ceu pa pon tiag tix tau mai aux tix ceu kawx.
12 Aux mawh ping nyia cuyi,
aux koe krax keud krax sawn;
aux koe ngai lig ngai lai,
koe riang rao tix tat ka see.
13 Pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix koeng ka pa caox pa caig;
aux koeng ka pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
pa cau nyhau,
ang rhawm yaox kaing ceu pa caox,
mai lox pie lox lhien.
14 Aux koe rhawm keud tix yuh lih,
aux koe rhawm lhak tix keud sawn.
Aux koe krax lhak lhiao,
aux koe riang kaux cong.
15 Aux joh jhan teem coi simiang giex kaing,
teem coi simiang prawng ka daux krax pa rauh pa see.
16 Simiang ka piang haktiex hoik een rhawm tix ka aux ku kaux;
simiang ka piang haktiex mai pui pa ting ceu khankix ang doh ang koe aux.
17 Pui pa muh ka aux,
aux kawx muh ka nawh;
pui pa sawg ka aux,
tag sang yaox ka aux.
18 Sin ngao mai min plak ngai hoik mawh aux pa tawx;
krax pa mag mi mai kaing pa pon yuh lih hoik mawh cie aux.
19 Pa pon maix khaing aux,
kawn lheu khaing khri,
kawn lheu khaing mau pa mhawm.
20 Aux lawng ka daux krax pa khaw caw;
aux hu ka daux krax pa rauh pa see.
21 Aux teei tix tawx sin ngao pa koe tix ka pui pa muh ka aux;
aux teei sin ngao pa koe tix kieh naog ka nyiex ma nawh heue.
 
22 bSivoe sang phan Simiang Siyiex ku ceu,
tom dix dix,
aux hoik dawg koe.
23 Yam jauh dix dix,
sivoe sang lih haktiex tix koe,
aux hoik dawg koe.
24 Sivoe sang lih koe rawm sigrit sigra,
sivoe sang lih koe dee keet rawm ting pa haok huan,
aux hoik lih tix keh.
25 Sivoe sang phan yuh ka gawng lua pa ting,
sivoe sang lih koe gawng lua pa iak tix kaux cong,
aux hoik dawg koe.
26 Yam an,
Siyiex ang nyang bax phan yuh haktiex mai daux tiag tu,
pie phawng tiex tix ceeix ceeix kawx kawn ang bax koe.
27 Nawh ang nyang bax yuh rao ma lih koe,
ang nyang bax pawd rhoi haktiex ot ka piang rawm sigrit sigra,
aux hoik dawg ot dee an.
28 Siyiex yuh pai awm ot ka daux rao ma,
paoh daux lih rawm ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
29 tat mee rawm sigrit sigra lih koe,
ang kieh nawh jao mee;
yam yuh nawh jaong haktiex,
aux hoik dawg ot dee an heue.
30 Aux awm cang maic sum nyiex pa ot ka praok nawh tix kaux,
kieh nawh gaux rhawm ku singaix;
aux gaux rhawm mai jang luh tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh:
31 Muh ka haktiex cie nawh,
muh ka kawn pui piang haktiex heue.
 
32 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
yam in tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux,
hawt tix yuh ka lox aux,
pex tom tag koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
33 Tag caw tix nghiet ka gum lox pa hoik sidiex gau aux ka pex,
tag caw tix koe krax lhak lhiao,
ang pon tix tieb tie.
34 Pui pa nghiet ka lox aux khankix kied koe buan son heue!
Nawh pau ka diang siviex aux ku singaix,
krox jun ka siviex nyiex aux.
35 Yaox ka aux,
hoik mawh yaox ka ju;
nawh tag sang chix pon buan son Simiang Siyiex.
36 Pui pa ang bax yaox ka aux hoik mawh pa yuh to kaux tix laux leeing;
pui pa koeng ka aux hoik mawh pui pa muh ka krax yum heue.
