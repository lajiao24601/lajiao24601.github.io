Simiang Siyiex ne raw pa tuah priex preem ka daux pa pie lhien
7
Yam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng ka daux num pon khix dim,
pa mawh khix Ci-see-lio pon ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex tawx gum lox ka aux tix gum heue.
2 Pui Paw-theu-lix hoik ploi Sa-li-seux,
Li-cian Mi-leux,
mai pui pa hawt khaix kix hoek ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing,
khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son.
3 Khaix an,
mai chawk ka cao giex kaing li mai cao jhai ah nin:
“Ang ex sang awm ka daux yam num pa hoik luan yuh nan,
mai tuah priex preem ka daux khix phuan,
son laux rhawm ka pa laux leeing nyiex buan lie?”
4 Plak greem mawh gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ka aux.
5 Nawh ah nin:
“Maix hu krai ka pui haktiex in mai ka cao giex kaing li,
ka daux singaix pa hoik luan atigliah num,
kix laux rhawm tuah priex preem ka daux khix phuan mai ka daux khix aliah,
mai ang mawh son sang yung yi ka aux.
6 Kix ih,
kix nyaux,
mai hoik mawh son sang kieh to kaux tix grum naog heue.”
7 In hoik mawh lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka cao jhai pa koe sivoe an.
Yam pon Yie-lu-sa-leung krax pa haok huan,
pui hoik naog ka ku dauh ku dee;
pui ang joeng naog ngie ka nyiex yaong pa koe ka sivie siveng veng an,
pie khawm haktiex plak seh mai ka caong gawng pa koe ka plak klip kawx,
pui hoik paung ot ka heue.
Kheu lieh lox kieh kix vuk vied vuk viang
8 Plak greem mawh gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Sa-cia-li-yax.
Nawh ah nin:
9 “Aux hoik dawg tawx lox ka pui cie tix ah nin:
‘Pex tag caw tix prawng ka daux pa rauh see,
tag caw tix kia ngien paox tix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm.
10 Ang pon tix gut kheem ka miex mai,
ka kawn doe,
ka nyo si nie,
mai ka pui hot;
ang pon tix keud sawn son sang yuh pui tang kaux laux leeing heue.’
11 “Viang mawh nan,
pui cie aux kiien rhawm ang nghiet lox.
Kix thox daux yhaok tix,
12 mai kieh rhawm rhi kix kiien awm simaox yuh nan.
Mawh kheu ang kix nghiet ka lox pa tawx aux cao jhai dix dix sidiex gau,
aux kied song rhawm tete.
13 Aux si kah ka kix,
kix ang nghiet;
kix rawg buan ka aux,
aux kawx ang nghiet.
14 Aux klia kix hu awm bhaung klia yuh nan,
mai kieh kix paung ot ka meung haktiex tang meung pa ang kix tawng.
Haktiex pa mhawm in lih tix mawh dee riem,
ka dee ang pui koe heue.”
