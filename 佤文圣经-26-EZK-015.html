Lox phek tiiem ka khaox plix siiet
15
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
khaox plix siiet sang chix tiag tix tau mai khaox tang ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
Kak khaox plix siiet sang pon tiag tix tau mai khaox pa koe daux nong yuh ka mawx lie?
3 Maix sang chix teei tix siao nawh yuh ceu pa tix ka lie?
Maix sang chix teei nawh mawh khaox tawk jim rhiem pa vhak kraung ka lie?
4 Nawh chix ngie tix phlu ngu khaing.
Yam hoik gleeing toh khawm ra plak,
sigreung kawx hoik gleeing tix lih mawh suah,
mai maix kawn sang chix teei tix siao ka lie?
5 Viang ang bax gleeing kawx,
nawh ang tie koe dee sang siao;
phan hoik gleeing,
hoik mawh suah,
mai nie ang lai chix tix siao heue.”
6-7 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang grheu pui haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung lih hu,
ciyang ka kix,
awm hoik sut aux tix viex hoek gleeing ka khaox plix siiet khaing daux nong yuh nan.
Viang kix hoik pon yaox tix luat luan khaing pa gleeing ngu tix bawg,
mai ngu ting tang bawg tag sang gleeing kix khawm uik.
Yam ciyang aux ka kix,
pex tag tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
8 Kix ang cu nghiet ka aux,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang kieh haktiex kix riem.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
