Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau
Krai dawt
Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau mawh pa toe tix tiiem khaix Lai Lu-cia, kaing ting tiiem grawng duk thung Khuan Singax Singeh yuh ka mawx ka pui gau Ye-sux bawg yam sivoe “sidiex gum lox buan ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung, meung haktiex Yo-thaix mai Sa-ma-li-yax khawm uik, tom hoek ka ku caong daog haktiex” (1.8). Been li Khrit jauh tix ka daux pui Yo-thaix, khaix an pre hu ka piang haktiex ku si dauh, in hoik mawh kaing ting ka phuk lai in. Cao tiiem hoik krai tom sidawng ka been li Khrit, ang mawh pa yuh krax pa tieh ka been meung Vawng Law-ma, mai baox krai sidawng ka been li Khrit hoik kieh been li pui Yo-thaix grum naog heue.
Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau chix tix guah lih koe loe baux ting, krai lao ka grawng pa tauh hu gum lox Ye-sux ka daux haktiex pa yah tix ting ku singaix, mai pa hoik sum ka ceu cao yuh nin:
1. Khaix hoik haok Ye-sux ka rao ma, kawn Khrit sau riang tix yuh kaing gum lox buan ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2. Yuh haktiex Yo-thaix mai khawm haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax haok huan.
3. Baox sau riang tix yuh sivie siveng Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong haok huan, tom hoek ka Law-ma.
Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau grawng pa blawn ting ceu mawh kaing Khuan Singax Singeh. Singaix yuh ka poi tix phuan ngaix, siawp sibrawm Khuan Singax Singeh lih hoek ka piang to kaux kawn Khrit ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung, kheu nan lai in krai lao ka kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh grawng pa nied tix naok riang rao ka ceu cao, mai ka simiang been li ceu cao khankix. Gum lox pa jauh been li ceu cao tix, chix tix yaox ka lox pa krai pa blawn ting ceu khaing daux lai in ra loe simin ting. Ka daux lai in pa hoik tiiem ka kaing ting ceu tom nie, hoik jhi ka grawng riang rao gum lox in ka kawn Khrit pa jauh tix koe khankix, mai krax ot krax koe ceu cao heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pa rien khre ka kham sakse (1.1–26)
(1) Gum lox pa nghoik Ye-sux tix tawm mai pa hoik ap nawh tix yuh (1.1–14)
(2) Pui pa loh tix plud ka Yo-tax (1.15–26)
2. Sakse pa kham ka Yie-lu-sa-leung (2.1–8.3)
3. Sakse pa kham ka Yo-thaix mai ka Sa-ma-li-yax (8.4–12.25)
4. Kaing yuh cao Pao-law (13.1–14.28)
(1) Krax hu sidiex daux bawg tix (13.1–14.28)
(2) Ka kum kawng ting ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung (15.36–18.22)
(3) Krax hu sidiex daux bawg ra (15.23–21.16)
(4) Krax hu sidiex daux bawg loe (18.23–21.16)
(5) Pao-law ot daux thawng ka Yie-lu-sa-leung, Khai-sha-li-yax, mai Law-ma (21.17–28.31)
