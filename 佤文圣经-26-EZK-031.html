Pa tiag khaox si oe bhawng awm Ai-cix
31
Yam vuk vied vuk viang ex ka daux kao tix num haok khix ra phuan singaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
2 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai ka simiang Ai-cix mai ka can pui cie nawh khawm uik ah nin:
Maix kied ting riang ting rao heue!
Sang teei aux ceu pa tix tiag tau mai maix lie?
3 Maix awma khaox si oe bhawng pa kuah ka Li-pa-noeng yuh nan,
kak khaox jiet mhawm sidu yeem awm paox tix yuh nan,
khaox an cux ka pai awm.
4 Rawm keet caux ka khaox an;
rawm pa koe ka greem tiex kieh khaox an ting huan.
Rawm klawng vung viang ka si peem khaox an,
kak rawm klawng an caux ka khaox pa koe ka daux nong ku gawng.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
khaox an lhaong khaing khaox tang ceu;
kak khaox an baox huan baox yeem;
6 Sim khankix yuh mheem tix ka kak;
to sat khankix keh ot ka greem veei;
meung ka piang haktiex sum tix ot nyiex ka greem veei.
7 Eue,
khaox ting pa kied mhawm heue!
Gaw khaox kied lhaong,
kak khaox kied tawx tix lang;
riah khaox kied raux,
raux hoek tom dee keet rawm greem tiex.
8 bKhaox si oe bhawng pa koe daux si peem cie Siyiex ang lhaong khaing khaox an;
kak khaox gix ang lang khaing kak khaox an;
kak khaox fong kawx ang pon awm kak khaox an.
Khaox pa koe daux si peem Siyiex ku gawng ang mhawm awm khaox an yuh nan.
9 Aux kieh khaox an mhawm grang,
kieh lhax yeem;
khaox pa koe daux si peem cie Siyiex pa mawh si peem Yi-tiien,
hoik kied ghoing ka khaox an khawm uik heue.
 
10 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
mawh kheu kied lhaong khaox in,
gaw khaox cux pai awm,
khaox in nied lhaong nied ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
11 aux tom ap tix tawx nawh ka simiang pa hoek khaing plak praix;
nawh tag sang pawk bix kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh khaox an.
12 Pui meung tang meung pa kied kua tag sang kid nawh goh,
tik kah,
kieh kak khaox ting kak khaox iak khankix prung prang tix ka piang gawng mai ka daux ngawd meung haktiex an.
Pui ku meung pa paung ot ka greem veei khaox an tag sang lih hu khawm uik.
13 Sim pa pu ka rao ma tag sang tom ka piang gaw khaox pa goh an;
to sat tag sang dug dig ka kak khaox an.
14 Jauh yam in,
viang dee keet rawm kied haoh kawx,
ang lai baox koe khaox tix gawng pa lhaong yuh nan,
rao gaw khaox an hoik cux pai awm.
Can khaox pa koe khawm uik tag sang kroh sang rham,
tag sang yum awm pui pa tag sang laux leeing yuh nan,
mai laig ka daux meung yum tau mai pui yum heue.”
15 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Yam cawd khaox ting an ka daux meung yum,
aux tag sang kieh rawm pa koe greem tiex kheem ka nawh,
son sang jhi ka krax pa laux rhawm.
Aux tag sang thox ka klawng,
mai ang kieh rawm klawng iak lih hu.
Aux tag sang kieh pa faik tawb kheem gawng Li-pa-noeng,
kieh khaox pa koe ka daux nong kroh rham khawm uik.
16 Aux tik khaox an laig daux meung yum,
mai lox cawd khaox an piang tiex tag sang si num yuh meung piang haktiex.
Khaox pa hoik ot daux meung yum pa mawh khaox ka daux si peem Yi-tiien,
rawm khaing Li-pa-noeng an hoik kub,
khaox pa blawn mhawm tag sang gaux rhawm kheu pa goh nawh.
17 Khaox in kix tag sang hoek ka meung yum tau mai khaox ting an,
tag sang ot tau mai pa pauh pui yum ka vaig ka pliah.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa paung ot ka greem veei khaox an tag sang ploi tix hu ka meung piang haktiex heue.
18 “Khaox ting in mawh pa tang son simiang Ai-cix mai son pui cie nawh.
Khaox pa ot daux si peem Yi-tiien ang sidu lhaong khaing nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
khaox in tag sang cawd ka daux meung yum awm khaox pa koe ka daux si peem Yi-tiien,
mai ot tau mai pui an kix pa ang koe krax yum rhawm pa pauh pui ka vaig ka pliah khankix.
In mawh pa krai ka simiang Ai-cix mai ka pui cie nawh.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
