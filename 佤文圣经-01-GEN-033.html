Ya-keux mai Yi-sao si pup paox tix
33
Ya-keux yaox viex Yi-sao pui hoek pon yieh kaux,
nawh tom tang guah kawn praix tix khankix,
tawx ka Li-yax mai Le-ciiex mai moeng iak tix ra kaux.
2 Nawh kieh moeng iak tix ra kaux mai kawn praix kix khankix hu sivoe,
Li-yax mai kawn praix nawh khankix hawt khaix,
Le-ciiex mai Yaw-seux nghoik khaix.
3 Ya-keux hu sivoe kix.
Yam hu dex nawh ka pux ke tix,
nawh tom grux ngawng tix krub ka piang tiex aliah bawg.
4 Yi-sao to tix hu rieb nawh,
mai kawm ka ngawg nawh mai ngheet ka nawh;
mai kiex yiiem heue.
5 Yi-sao jhak khawm pon daog,
yaox ka bun mai kawn nyawm khankix,
tom chawk ah nin:
“Pui in kix pa hu tau mai maix mawh mawx lie?”
Ya-keux ah nin:
“Kawn nyawm in kix mawh khawng phak buan son pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka aux heue.”
6 Khaix an,
moeng iak in kiex viex kawn tix khankix hu plak sivoe,
mai krub ka Yi-sao.
7 Khaix an,
Li-yax mai kawn praix nawh khankix hawt khaix,
nghoik khaix Yaw-seux mai Le-ciiex kawx hoek tix krub sivoe heue.
8 Yi-sao baox chawk ah nin:
“Cub khawng brie pa yaox aux sivoe in mawh pa yuh ka mawx?”
Ya-keux ah nin:
“In mawh khawng phak pa yaox ax ka paox tix heue.”
9 Yi-sao ah nin:
“Pux aik aux eue,
ceu in aux hoik kub hoik naog kah;
deuh cie maix khankix son cao maix heue!”
10 Ya-keux ah nin:
“Ang,
phan maix gaux rhawm ka aux,
viex maix rieb ka khawng phak aux.
Yam in maix hoik chix kia ngien ka aux,
aux hoik yaox ka pak ngai maix,
awm hoik yaox aux ka pak ngai Siyiex.
11 Viex maix rieb ka khawng phak pa viex aux;
Siyiex hoik kia ngien aux ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix,
mai hoik tawx ka can pa sang lo aux khawm uik.”
Ya-keux loe bawg baox nhie Yi-sao rieb ka khawng phak,
nawh tom taug rieb ka heue.
12 Yi-sao ah nin:
“Ex hu phaox!
Aux hu sivoe maix heue.”
13 Ya-keux pawk ah nin:
“Maix hoik tawng kawn nyawm khankix kawn iak;
kheu nan aux kawx chix caox rhawm ka kawn moi,
kawn pex,
kawn yung pa peux khankix.
Phan ex ca baox kawn hu krax tix ngaix,
aux lhat kawn yung,
kawn pex khankix yum ka taug tix.
14 Viex maix ran hu sivoe,
aux lawk koi hawt khaix maix,
moi,
yung,
pex mai kawn nyawm in pon hu kix brax yuh ka mawx,
yix pon hu brax yuh nan nang.
Ex lawk si pup paox tix ka haktiex Yi-tong heue.”
15 Yi-sao ah nin:
“Phan yuh nin,
aux deuh pui tix blah ing tau mai maix.”
Viang mawh nan,
Ya-keux pawk ah nin:
“Phan maix cua,
ang siao deuh.”
16 Kheu nan,
daux ngaix an Yi-sao tom deeh ing ka haktiex Yi-tong.
17 Ya-keux kawn hu plak Su-keu,
mai sum nyiex son cao tix tix lhang tan,
khawm nyiex dee ot khawng brie kawx yuh kah.
Kheu nin,
tom kok dee an mawh Su-keub heue.
18 Ya-keux jauh ka Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax mhawm hoek ka haktiex Cia-nang veng See-ciien,
tom sum nyiex bang ka dee lung dex daux veng an.
19 cNawh teei mau tix yieh liag haktiex an ka keeing See-ciien doeh ceu cad Ha-mawx.
20 Nawh yuh khu tan dee an,
mai sum ceu kaux mawh Yi-lix Yi-law-yi Yi-seu-liexd heue.
