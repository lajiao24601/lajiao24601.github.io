Ya-keux rien khre tix si pup ka Yi-sao
32
Ya-keux ca kawn hu krax hu tix;
ka daux krax,
kawn cau Siyiex hoek ka nawh.
2 Ya-keux yaox ka kix,
tom ah nin:
“In mawh cub kru naing Siyiex heue!”
Kheu nan,
nawh tom kok ceu kaux dee an mawh Ma-ha-nian.a
3 Ya-keux ploi pui cau tix ran hu ka haktiex Yi-tong dee ot pux ke tix Yi-sao,
4 tawx lox ka kix nhie kix krai ka Yi-sao:
“Simiang yix,
pa mawh siliex maix Ya-keux,
nhie yix krai lox ka simiang aux Yi-sao ah nin:
‘Aux hoik ot tau mai La-pang tix baux bawg yam,
kied rheeng yam in tag sang deeh hoek.
5 Aux hoik koe moi,
la,
yung,
pex,
mai siliex.
Yam in aux sang ploi pui hoek krai ka simiang aux,
simie tix kieh simiang aux gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.’ ”
6 Kawn cau khankix deeh hoek,
krai ka Ya-keux ah nin:
“Yix hoik yaox ka pux ke maix Yi-sao;
nawh hoik viex pui pon yieh kaux,
son sang rieb ka maix.”
7 Ya-keux kied lhat mai khi rhawm khi rhi.
Nawh tom guah pui tix hu ra cub,
khawm yung,
pex,
moi,
mai ka-la-uk mawh ra cub.
8 Nawh keud:
“Phan Yi-sao puing ka tix cub,
an tix cub moi kawn deuk,
mai tag pon tix luat luan heue.”
9 Ya-keux rawg buan ah nin:
“Siyiex tax aux Ya-paw-la-hang,
Siyiex keeing aux Yi-sha eue,
rawg maix nghiet!
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawx lox nhie aux deeh ing ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
hu ka nyiex ma pui tix tan,
khaix an krai sang mhawm kaing yuh aux ku ceu.
10 Aux ang doh keung tix pon krax pa caox rhawm mai pa kaux sijax maix ka siliex.
Sivoe an,
daux taix aux viex ngie khaox gix tix gawng tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Yam in,
aux hoik viex ka pui ra cub hoek.
11 Rawg maix taoh aux luat luan khaing daux tawm taix pux ke tix Yi-sao liak.
Aux lhat nawh hoek puing yix,
mai pauh ka khawm moeng aux mai kawn simex kawn bun aux yum.
12 bKhiox khaw maix doh keud ka lox pa ap maix tix deuh,
kieh aux mhawm kaing yuh ku ceu,
mai tawx aux koe kawn praix tom haoh hun,
awm mhaik ka praok rawm,
ang koe pui pa uik simin ka heue.”
13-15 Ya-keux it tan tix sawn.
Nawh tom laug ka khawng brie tix lih tix blah,
kieh mawh khawng phak pa tawx ka pux ke tix Yi-sao:
pex siang ra yieh mu,
pex mhaing tix nga mu;
yung siang ra yieh mu,
yung mhaing tix nga mu,
ka-la-uk pa kawn peux kawn tix ngoe mu,
pa tang viex ka kawn tix;
moi miex tipon mu,
moi mhaing kao mu;
la siang tix nga mu,
la mhaing kao mu.
16 Nawh tag guah ka khawng brie in kix tang lih koe phung mawx phung an,
tix phung tawx siliex tix kuang tix kaux.
Nawh tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Pex ran hu,
tix phung mai tix phung kieh si nax koe ka daux pex.”
17 Nawh tawx lox ka siliex pa thung hu sivoe an tix kaux ah nin:
“Yam yaox pux ke aux Yi-sao ka maix,
phan chawk maix:
‘Simiang maix mawh mawx?
Hu maix dee mawx?
Khawng brie in kix sivoe ngaix maix mawh cie mawx?’
18 Maix tom pawk lox:
‘In kix mawh cie siliex maix Ya-keux khawm uik;
nawh sang tawx mawh khawng phak ka simiang tix Yi-sao.
Cao nawh kawn ot plak khaix heue.’ ”
19 Nawh krai awm paox ka cub ra cub loe,
khawm pui pa hawt khawng brie hu ah nin:
“Yam yaox pex Yi-sao krai yuh nin uik,
20 caw tix krai ah nin:
‘Siliex maix Ya-keux kawn ot plak khaix.’ ”
Ya-keux keud daux rhawm:
“Aux sang kieh khawng phak in kix nyawm yuh rhawm nawh,
yam yaox ka paox tix,
nawh taug chix va rhawm ka aux.”
21 Kheu nan,
nawh kieh pui pa viex khawng tan ran hu sivoe,
to kaux nawh kawn it laux tix ka nyiex bang heue.
Ya-keux blok ka haktiex Phi-nu-yi-leux
22 Ka daux bo an,
Ya-keux kaoh,
viex moeng tix ra kaux,
mai moeng iak nawh ra kaux,
mai kawn simex nawh kao kaux tix tiang ka rawm klawng Ya-paw.
23 Khaix hoik tiang kix kah,
nawh baox tawx ka kraung pa koe tix khawm uik tiang,
24 cto kaux nawh kawn ot plak khaix heue.
Koe pui tix kaux hoek blok ka Ya-keux,
blok tom dex sang riang praix.
25 Pui an yaox ang tix pex,
tom dieh ka si kiak ba Ya-keux,
si kiak ba Ya-keux tom pu pliak.
26 Pui an ah nin:
“Praix hoik dex riang,
ploi aux hu heue!”
Ya-keux pawk lox:
“Phan maix ang nawb buan piang aux,
aux ang ploi ka maix heue!”
27 Pui an chawk:
“Maix kaux ceu pa tix?”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Ya-keux.”
28 ePui an baox ah nin:
“Jauh yam in hu sivoe,
maix ang lai kaux Ya-keux.
Maix blok Siyiex mai kawn pui,
maix hoik pex kah,
kheu nin,
ceu kaux maix sang loh mawh Yi-seu-liexd heue.”
29 fYa-keux chawk:
“Krai ka aux liak,
maix kaux ceu pa tix?”
Pui an pawk lox:
“Yuh ka mawx lai chawk ka ceu kaux aux?”
Khaix hoik ah lox in,
mai nawb buan piang Ya-keux heue.
30 Ya-keux ah nin:
“Pak ngai aux hoik yaox ka Siyiex,
kawn klawd ju!”
Kheu nan,
nawh tom kok dee an mawh Pi-nu-yi-leux.g
31 Yam lih hu Ya-keux khaing haktiex Pi-nu-yi-leux,
singaix hoik lih.
Mawh kheu pu pliak si kak ba nawh,
nawh tom hu chawh.
32 Bleeh tom in singaix,
doeh ceu cad pui Yi-seu-liex tom ang ih si niag ba,
kheu ku khlaik si niag si kiak ba Ya-keux tix heue.
