Lai Heu-si-a
1
aBawg yam plak jhi Simiang Siyiex tix ka Heu-si-ax kawn simex Pe-lix,
meung Yo-tax mawh simiang Wu-si-ya,
simiang Yaw-thang,
simiang Ya-ha-see,
simiang Si-si-cia khankix pa plud tix giex kaing;
meung Yi-seu-liex mawh simiang Yie-law-paw-ang kawn simex Yaw-hua-see pa giex kaing.
Pa tiiem ot plak greem mawh gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka Heu-si-ax heue.
Moeng Heu-si-ax mai kawn simex kawn bun nawh
2 Yam si kah Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux ceu kaux Heu-si-ax ka daux bawg tix,
nawh ah nin ka Heu-si-ax:
“Maix hu teei tix koe moeng heue!
Moeng maix tag yuh kaing pa seeing brai ka piang maix;
kawn simex kawn bun pex kawx hoik ang rauh see ka maix awm nawh yuh nanb.
Awm an,
meung Yi-seu-liex khawm uik kawx hoik ang rauh see awm bun pa phit vao an yuh nang,
kix hoik tieh vheen ka aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Heu-si-ax hu teei tix moeng ka kawn bun Ti-la-ying pa kaux Keu-mix.
Bun an nghe,
mai keh kawn simex son Heu-si-ax tix kaux.
4 eSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Heu-si-ax:
“Sum nawh kaux Yie-see-liex;c
mawh kheu ang rheeng,
aux tag sang ciyang ka nyiex simiang Yie-hu,
mai pawk bi ka tax du kix yam pauh pui yum tom nie ka haktiex Yie-see-liex.
Aux sang naox ka simiang giex Yie-hu.d
5 Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang puing pui naing Yi-seu-liex laux leeing ka daux ngawd Yie-see-liex heue.”
6 Keu-mix baox nghe,
mai keh kawn bun tix kaux.
Simiang Siyiex krai ka Heu-si-ax ah nin:
“Sum nawh kaux Law Lu-ha-max;f
mawh kheu ang aux lai muh ka pui Yi-seu-liex nyiex in,
ang lai baox pluat ploi ka phi map kix.
7 Khaix an,
aux tag sang muh ka pui Yo-tax nyiex in.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh Siyiex kix;
aux tag sang taoh ka kix;
viang mawh nan,
aux ang siao krax naing,
ang siao ak te,
vaig gawn,
brung naing,
pui naing brug brung taoh ka kix heue.”
8 Khaix hoik pluat kawn bun Keu-mi pa kaux Law Lu-ha-max peux,
nawh baox nghe mai baox keh kawn simex tix kaux.
9 Simiang Siyiex krai ka Heu-si-ax ah nin:
“Sum nawh kaux Law A-mixg;
mawh kheu ang pui Yi-seu-liex lai mawh pui cie aux,
aux ang lai mawh Siyiex kix heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex pon krax pa haok huan
10 hKaux pui Yi-seu-liex baox thiem thie tix,
awm mhaik pa ang uik simin ang uik tiag yuh nan.
Yam in Siyiex ah nin ka kix:
“Pex ang mawh pui cie aux”;
viang mawh nan,
koe tix ngaix,
nawh tag sang ah nin ka kix:
“Pex mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex pa im leeing ju heue.”
11 Nyiex ma pui Yo-tax mai nyiex ma pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang baox khrawm paox tix tix bawg,
mai laug simiang son to kaux tix.
Kix tag sang ot ka daux haktiex tix,
mai cox tix deeh pon krax pa haok huan.
Mawh tete,
singaix bawg yam cie Yie-see-liexi tag sang lih tix mawh singaix pa ting lhaong heue.
