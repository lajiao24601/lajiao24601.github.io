Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
67
Siyiex eue,
caox rhawm ka yix,
tawx buan ka yix;
khiox khaw maix kia ngien yix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm maix.
2 Khiox khaw maix kieh ka piang haktiex khawm uik tawng ka alo maix,
kieh meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik tawng ka krax taoh maix heue.
 
3 Siyiex eue,
kieh pui ku kaux rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix;
kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix heue.
 
4 Kieh meung ka piang haktiex gaux rhawm gaux rhi khawm uik;
mawh kheu hoik teei maix pa khaw caw tix prawng ca ka kawn pui piang haktiex,
mai hoik duk thung ka meung piang haktiex ku meung heue.
 
5 Siyiex eue,
kieh pui ku kaux rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix;
kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex yaog yaw ka maix khawm uik heue.
 
6 Haktiex tawx ka plix nghox plix koi;
Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex tawx buan son ka ex.
7 Siyiex tawx buan son ka ex;
kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex lhat graung yung yi ka nawh khawm uik heue.
