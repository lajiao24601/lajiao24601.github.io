12
Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
2 Mawh heue,
pex hoik mawh pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi;
viang mawh nan,
yam hoik yum pex,
ping nyia cuyi tag sang yum tau mai pex.
3 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik koe krax lhak lhiao awm pex,
ang koe ceu pa ang aux krup pex kah;
lox pa krai lao pex,
pui mawx pa ang chix krai lie?
4 Yam in pie paox grawm aux kawx hoik nyiah ka aux;
aux hoik rauh see singax,
mai kix ca kawn nyiah ka aux;
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan.
5 Maix ang koe krax laux krax leeing,
mai maix hoek tix nyiah ka aux;
maix lauh ka pui pa dex sang goh an.
6 Nyiex gox bang cie pui briex pui lak hoik kaux cong yuh nin;
pui pa cheeik Siyiex song rhawm hoik teei riang rao to kaux tix kieh mawh siyiex kix yuh nin heue.
 
7 Maix hu chawk ka to sat khankix,
kix sang chix ang chix sidiex gau ka maix;
maix hu chawk tix yaox ka sim pa pu daux rao ma khankix,
kix sang chix ang chix krai maix mhong.
8 To sat ka piang haktiex chix sidiex gau ka maix;
kax pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra kawx chix krai maix mhong sidawng.
9 Ka daux kix pui mawx ang tawng,
hoik mawh taix Simiang Siyiex pa phan yuh ka kix?
10 Ju to sat hoik ot ka daux tawm taix Siyiex khawm uik;
bhawm pa im ju kawn pui kawx hoik ot ka daux tawm taix nawh.
11 Awm dak pa lied ka priex preem yuh nan,
yhaok hoik guah tix tawng ka lox si kah heue.
 
12-13 Pui riex ju koe ping nyia cuyi,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik koe ping nyia cuyi mai baox koe siawp sibrawm.
Pui riex ju koe krax keud krax sawn,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex koe krax keud krax sawn mai baox koe siawp sibrawm tix pon yuh lih.
14 Pa hoik yieh Siyiex laux,
pui mawx pon deeh cox sum?
Pa hoik laung Siyiex kah,
pui mawx pon deeh ploi lih?
15 Yam ang Siyiex lai tawx rawm lhiex lih,
pa kroh praix lih hoek;
yam paoh nawh daux rawm,
rawm tom kheem ka haktiex heue.
 
16 Siyiex ting riang ting rao,
mai puing pex tom si dauh;
pui pa pie lhien mai pui pa glup ka daux pa pie lhien hoik ot ka daux tawm taix nawh khawm uik.
17 Nawh hoik teei hu ka ping nyia cuyi simiang ka piang khawm uik;
nawh hoik kieh krax pa yuh cao giex kaing khankix mawh pa daox aw.
18 Nawh tik ka simiang,
mai kieh kix lih mawh pui pa giex pui tix laung.
19 Nawh hoik teei ka ping nyia cuyi cao giex kaing li;
nawh hoik cot ka pui pa koe siawp sibrawm.
20 Nawh hoik kieh pui pa koe sibrawm lox si kah ka piang pui hun yien yawg tix ang koe lox krai;
nawh hoik teei hu ka ping nyia cuyi pui riex ju.
21 Nawh hoik kieh simiang pon krax kaig krax tua,
hoik kieh pui pa koe siawp sibrawm grai riang grai rao.
22 Nawh hoik kieh pa raux pa mox lih jhi tix khaing daux pa faik;
nawh hoik kieh veei krax yum lih koe pa riang.
23 Nawh hoik kieh meung piang haktiex haok huan ting lhaong,
khaix an mai deeh puing ka kix,
yuh kix laux leeing.
24 Nawh kieh simiang pui haoh pui hun daox aw,
kieh kix ang lai tawng ka grawng ceu pa sang yuh tix;
25 kix hoik awm pui pa yoek ka plai yuh nan,
vawk jee vawk blaong ka daux pa faik heue.
