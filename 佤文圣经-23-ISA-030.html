Yuh sijax mukheu pa ang koe ceu tau mai Ai-cix
30
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Pui pa kuang ka Yo-tax tag sang yaox krax pa laux leeing heue!
Mawh kheu hoik tieh vheen kix ka aux.
Kix ang hawt grawng lawng khaix aux,
tang teei rhawm keud tix yuh,
mai yuh sijax mukheu pa tieh vheen ka aux tau mai pui,
phi map baox thiem thie tix ka piang phi map.
2 Kix ang bax khiox khaw chawk cheng ka aux,
mai khiox khaw Ai-cix taoh tix.
Kix khiox khaw Ai-cix kia ngien tix;
kix een rhawm tix ka simiang Ai-cix.
3 Viang mawh nan,
simiang Ai-cix ang koe riang tix pon teem ka kix;
krax pa kia ngien Ai-cix dee si dauh mawh pa kieh krax laux krax leeing hoek.
4 Viang kawn cau pa ting cie pui Yo-tax hoik hoek ka veng Saw-ang mai ka veng Ha-noe-see ka haktiex Ai-cix;
5 pui Yo-tax dee si dauh,
tag sang klih rhawm tix kheu een rhawm ka meung haktiex pui pa ang kaux ang cong.
Kix ang bax pon ka ceu pa mhawm tix ceu kawx,
koe ngie krax kaig tua,
krax laux rhawm heue.”
6 In mawh lox pa hoik krai Siyiex pa koe grawh grawng mai to sat plak seh pa ah nin:
“kawn cau pa ting cie pui Yo-tax hoik lai ling tix hu ka baoh haktiex dee koe krax pa khi rhawm ka tix dauh;
dee an koe sivai sang si,
si uing pa koe jai,
mai si yawng caox pa chix pu.
La viex kix mai ka-la-uk kix hoik tang naog ka khawng phak pa ting ngawh,
son sang hu tan tix tawx ka meung haktiex pui pa ang tie pon teem ka kix heue.
7 Krax pa teem coi pui Ai-cix ang tie koe ceu.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom sum pui Ai-cix koe ceu kaux tix ceu,
mai kok mawh ‘to sat pa ang koe ceu heue’.”
Pui pa ang nghiet lox
8 Siyiex kieh aux tiiem kaing pa yuh pui cie nawh ot ka daux lai,
kieh pui ju phaox khaix doh keud ka kaing pa caox pa caig yuh kix leeing ju.
9 Kix mawh cub pui pa tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex,
kix hoik pie lhien ang jud,
ang nghiet ka gum lox pa sidiex gau Simiang Siyiex jheeng jheeng.
10 Kix hoik nhie cao jhai khankix thox jheeh tix.
Kix ah nin:
“Paw vi krai yix mhong ceu pa khaw yix tix sang yuh;
mai krai yix mhong ngie ka lox pa muh yix tix nghiet,
pa hoik mawh kaing ceu pa ang koe mu khankix.
11 Yoi singai!
Paw vi tie ka krax pa lawng yix.
Yix ang nghiet ka krax cie Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh heue.”
12 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh hoik krai yuh nin:
“Pex ang simi ka lox pa hoik krai aux ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
pex tieh tix een rhawm tix ka riang rao tix,
mai krax pa pie lhien tix.
13 Pex hoik yuh phi map.
Pex hoik awm guh ding pa koe rhoi daux tuh,
pa dex sang ghrup yuh nan,
14 Pex hoik awm kraung yuh aw pa laux leeing,
ang lai yaox pie tix blah son sang teei mawh pa kiiep ka gaox ngu,
phan ang mawh nan,
son sang teei mawh pa pug rawm naog ka daux dung heue.”
15 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh ah nin ka pui cie tix:
“Deeh hoek heue,
yum rhawm ka aux mhawm mhawm khaing,
pex tom taug pon krax pa nyawm ot;
yawg tix krox jun ka aux,
pex tom taug koe riang rao.”
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang cu.
16 Pex keud tix sang brug brung pa brax to tix bheeik luan khaing pa leue pui koeng ka pex.
Ang lud,
pex tag caw to tix klawd ju.
Pex hoik ce brung brug tix kied brax,
viang mawh nan brung brug pui pa koeng nie kawn lheu brax khaing pex heue.
17 Viang pex hoik koe pui tix rheng kaux kawx,
yam yaox pex ka pui pa koeng pie tix kaux khaing,
mai pex hoik tang yaog tix to;
pui pa koeng pie phuan kaux khaing,
pex taug ang pex ka kix,
pui naing pex hoik laux leeing,
mai deuk ngie rong man chix tix gawng khaing pa koe ka piang gawng.
18 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ca kawn krox jun ka pex;
nawh krox tix sang tawx buan son ka pex.
Nawh tag sang caox rhawm ka pex,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex pa rauh see.
Pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh kied ting buan son tete heue!
Siyiex tag sang tawx buan ka pui cie tix
19 Pui pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
paw vi lai baox yiiem grai heue.
Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh krax muh;
phan pex khiox khaw nawh taoh teem tix,
nawh tag sang nghiet ka lox pex.
20 Simiang Siyiex sang kieh pex kha tix ka cai rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ca kawn ot ka praok pex sidiex gau ka pex,
mai pex ang siao lai sawg ka nawh.
21 Phan pex via hu khaing daux krax,
via hu plak dawm ang ah,
plak goix ang ah,
pex tag sang mhong ka plak khaix pex,
koe lox pa ah nin ka pex:
“In mawh krax pa rauh see,
lawng hu daux heue!”
22 Pex tag caw teei tix tik ka riang rao krub pui pa hoik om ka khri,
mai pa hoik raug pui ka mau khankix kieh mawh dee tik pa seeing brai,
mai kok yo lox tom ting si num ah nin:
“Maix hu khaing heue!”
23 Bawg yam sau pex simie,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang tawx rawm lhiex lih,
kieh simie kuah,
khaix an mai kieh pex pon to ma tom nie;
phung moi een pex tag yaox tix ot ka pak rib pa vieh pa va.
24 Moi pa bluh krong thai tang pex mai la tag yaox tix sawm rib pa blawn awn.
25 Pui pa koeng ka pex mai pui hoik pauh kix yum,
yam hoik ghrup tab kix,
gawng pa ting pa iak cie pex tag sang buing tix lih rawm keet khawm uik.
26 Khix tag sang riang awm singaix yuh nan;
pa riang singaix tag sang nie riang khaing sivoe an aliah son,
awm pa riang daux aliah singaix mai hoik lih jhan riang daux tix singaix khaing.
Yam kie Simiang Siyiex ka rhoi khaix mat pui cie tix,
pai kix moi,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh khawm uik heue.
Siyiex sang ciyang ka Ya-sux
27 Pa ting siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex mai min plak ngai nawh,
ot dee singai kawx hoik chix yaox ka khawm uik.
Ngu mai taox ngu hoik jhi krax pa song rhawm nawh lih.
Nawh ang jheeh tix ne raw;
lox nawh hoik awm ngu ting.
28 Bhawm nawh hoik awm rawm ting,
kheem pui tom hoek ka ngawg;
nawh tag sang yuh laux ka meung ka piang haktiex,
yuh laux ka krax pa caox pa caig kix.
29 Viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang gaux rhawm mai rhax lai,
awm pui pa krub ka daux bo yam yuh poi yuh nan.
Pex tag gaux rhawm tix awm pui pa krub pa ot ka daux lox luah pi,
pa hu kia ngien ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex Yi-seu-liex heue.
30 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pui ku kaux mhong ka lox pa ting siawp sibrawm tix;
nawh tag sang teei rao ngu,
pai awm,
mu pre,
mai lhiex ting,
son sang kieh kawn pui tawng ka krax pa song rhawm tix.
31 Pui Ya-sux mhong ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
mai kix tom si yaung si kua;
kix hoik tawng ka siawp sibrawm krax pa ciyang nawh.
32 Yam puing Simiang Siyiex ka pui Ya-sux tix bawg hoik tix bawg,
pui cie nawh tag sang tauh kraung tauh ting.
To kaux Simiang Siyiex tag sang puing ka pui Ya-sux.
33 Tom yam jauh dix dix,
Simiang Siyiex hoik rien khre ka haktiex tix dauh,
koe ngu ting tag sang gleeing ka Vawng Ya-sux;
dee an hoik mawh haktiex pa vieh va,
mai khix khaox hoik pum tix naog kah.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang phlu bhawm tix gum,
hoik awm ngu mhat pa gleeing ka pum khix an heue.
