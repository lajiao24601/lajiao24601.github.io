Kawk nyaux Yaw-seux grai
44
Yaw-seux tawx lox ka cao kuang nyiex tix ah nin:
“Maix hawt been pon viex kix sau nghox naog daux chee kix ku kaux,
mai deeh sau mau daux chee kaux mawx kaux an.
2 Phan mawh pa blawn awn ju khaing paox tix an,
maix sau kawk mau pa nyaux aux mai mau pa liag nawh nghox daux chee.”
Cao pa kuang nyiex an hawt tix yuh.
3 Daux ra ngaix pon ngawb cao cao,
pux aik in khankix mai la kix tawx deeh ing.
4 Yam ang kix nyang luan singai veng,
Yaw-seux ah nin ka cao pa kuang nyiex tix:
“Maix nied leue ka kix,
yam krup maix kix,
chawk ka kix:
‘Yuh ka mawh pex lai deeh pawk pa caox ka pa mhawm lie?
5 Yuh ka mawx pex lai briex kawk nyaux cao simiang aux hua,
kawk mau an mawh pa nyaux nawh plai kah,
mai mawh nang pa siao cao si pawk;
pex hoik yuh phi map pa ting heue!’ ”
6 Yam krup cao kuang nyiex an ka kix,
tom ah lox in ka kix.
7 Kix pawk lox:
“Sara,
lox maix in mawh son tix lie?
Yix tiex tu,
yix ang bax yuh kaing in.
8 Maix hoik tawng,
mau pa yaox yix daux chee sivoe an kawx,
yix hoik viex hoek khaing Cia-nang sang deeh kah.
Yix doh chix briex mau khri cao simiang maix lie?
9 Sara,
phan maix yaox kawk an ka daux chee pui siliex mawx ang ah,
pui an khaw tix yum;
pui tang kaux kawx chix tix yuh siliex cawng maix heue.”
10 Cao pa kuang nyiex an ah nin:
“Aux cu lox pa krai pex.
Viang mawh nan,
aux sang kieh ngie pui pa briex kawk plai an ot tix yuh siliex cawng aux,
pui tang kaux chix tix ing.”
11 Khaix an kix nied tah chee tix piang tiex,
mai tang paoh ka chee tix ku kaux heue.
12 Cao pa kuang nyiex an sawg kraung kix,
jauh ka pui pa ting tom hoek ka pa iak.
Dee nghoik,
kawk plai an yaox ka daux chee cie Piien-ya-ming.
13 Pux aik nawh khankix laux rhawm mai riah kraung ceep tix,
mai deeh sau lhang la tix ku kaux,
deeh daux veng heue.
14 Yam hoek Yo-tax mai pux aik nawh khankix ka daux nyiex Yaw-seux,
Yaw-seux kawn ot tan;
mai kix krub ka nawh.
15 Yaw-seux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai yuh nin?
Ang pex tawng aux pa lhaong min yuh nin chix si pawk ka kaing yuh pex lie?”
16 Yo-tax pawk lox:
“Simiang aux eue,
yix kawn sang koe lox pa tix lie?
Kawn sang chix krai grawng mawh ang mawh lie?
Siyiex hoik plih phi map yix!
Yam in,
ang mawh ngie pui pa yaox ex kawk ka an,
khawm yix kawx sang yuh siliex cawng maix heue.”
17 Yaw-seux ah nin:
“Ang!
Aux ang doh yuh kaing yuh nin.
Mawh ngie pui pa teei kawk plai aux hu an pa sang mawh siliex cawng aux;
pui tang kaux chix tix ing mhawm mhawm ka nyiex keeing pex tan heue.”
Yo-tax khiox khaw son Piien-ya-ming
18 Yo-tax hoek dex,
ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Simiang aux eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux krai sidawng.
Maix sidu maix simiang,
rawg maix paw vi song rhawm ka siliex maix.
19 Simiang aux eue,
maix hoik chawk ka yix:
‘Pex kawn koe keeing mai pux aik tang kaux lie?’
20 Yix deeh pawk:
‘Yix koe keeing pa hoik kuad tix kaux,
mai pux nghoik pa iak tix kaux,
pa pon keeing yam kuad tix.
Pux ke lih kawn nyawm in hoik yum;
nawh mawh ngie pa keh khaing miex tix kaux khaing,
kheu nan,
keeing yix kied muh ka nawh.’
21 Simiang aux eue,
maix tawx lox kieh yix viex hoek,
kieh ngai maix yaox.
22 Viang mawh nan,
yix hoik krai,
kawn in ang pon tix hu khaing keeing nawh;
phan lih hu,
keeing nawh rien tix yum.
23 Kheu maix simiang aux ah nin:
‘Lheu khaing viex pex pux nghoik tix hoek tau,
ang pon tix lai hoek yaox ka aux heue.’
24 “Yix deeh hoek ka keeing tix dee an,
krai lox simiang aux ka nawh.
25 Khaix an,
nawh baox kieh yix hoek liag nghox.
26 Yix ah nin ka nawh:
‘Yix ang pon hu;
lheu khaing viex yix pux pa iak tix hu tau,
yix ang pon tix yaox pui an;
lheu khaing viex yix pux tix hu tau,
yix ang pon tix hu heue.’
27 Keeing yix ah nin ka yix:
‘Pex hoik tawng,
moeng aux Le-ciiex keh ngie kawn simex ra kaux.
28 An tix kaux hoik hu khaing aux.
Khaix hoik hu nawh,
aux ang lai sax yaox nawh;
to tiag rien tix hoik kiied nawh phawng.
29 Yam in pex sang viex pux iak in hu khaing aux.
Phan nawh yaox kaing pa ang mhawm daux krax,
pex sang kieh aux pa paing hauk in,
laux rhawm laux rhi khawm laig tix daux si meeig heue!’ ”
30-31 Yo-tax kawn ca ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
simiang aux eue,
ju keeing aux hoik si doe tix mai kawn nyawm in.
Phan yix ang viex kawn nyawm in ing,
nawh ang yaox ka kawn nyawm in,
rien tix yum;
khex ang mawh yix pa kieh tax kuad pa paing hauk in,
laux rhawm laux rhi khawm laig tix daux si meeig lie?
32 Ang joeng mawh nin,
aux hoik kham ka keeing tix,
krai nawh mhong,
phan aux ang viex kawn nyawm in hoek tawx ka nawh,
aux cu tix klawm phi map ka ju tix.
33 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang aux eue,
aux cu tix ot tin,
yuh siliex cawng son kawn nyawm in.
Kieh nawh ing tau mai pux ke tix khankix liak.
34 Phan kawn nyawm in ang ing tau mai aux,
aux sang chix ing yaox keeing tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux ang pon yaox krax laux hoek ka piang keeing tix heue.”
