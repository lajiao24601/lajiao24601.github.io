Pao-law sidiex lox buan ka pui tang ceu tang cad
3
Mawh kheu nan,
aux Pao-law in pa mawh pui laung cie Ye-sux Khrit son pui tang ceu tang cad khiox khaw ka Siyiex son pex.
2 Pex hoik tawng,
son sang koe dee mhawm son pex,
Siyiex tawx buan son,
mai ap kaing in ka aux.
3 Nawh plak jhi ka aux,
mai kieh aux tawng ka pa raux pa mox nawh.
(Aux hoik tiiem kaing pa raux pa mox in lih pie dawt;
4 aPhan pex phat mai taug sang tawng ka grawng pa hoik rieb aux ka pa raux pa mox Khrit mawh ceu pa tix.)
5 Singaix pa hoik luan,
Siyiex ang bax jhi pa raux pa mox in ka pui tix kaux kawx,
yam in nawh hoik plak jhi ka kawn cau nawh mai ka cao jhai ka daux Khuan Singax Singeh.
6 Gum lox pa raux pa mox in mawh yuh nin:
ka daux gum lox buan,
pui tang ceu tang cad hoik rom tix pon buan son Siyiex tau mai pui Yo-thaix.
Kix hoik mawh pak peung to kaux tix mu khaing,
khaix an hoik pon yuh lih ka gum lox pa tawx Siyiex ka daux Ye-sux Khrit heue.
7 Mawh kheu buan son pa blawk Siyiex tix tawx,
aux hoik pon tix mawh siliex gum lox buan in;
in hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh siawp sibrawm pa ting pa tawx nawh ka aux.
8 Viang aux hoik mawh pa iak pa lied ka daux kawn Khrit khawm uik,
mai aux hoik pon sibrawm in pa tawx Siyiex ka aux,
pon yaox tix sidiex gum lox buan pa grum naog cie Khrit ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
9 mai kieh pui piang haktiex khawm uik tawng grawng sang yuh Siyiex pa raux pa mox tix yuh ka mawx.
Ka daux baux ju pa hoik luan,
cao pa phan yuh ku ceu khawm uik pa mawh Siyiex mox ka pa raux pa mox in,
10 kaing ting mawh son sang kieh ka daux baux ju yam in,
ot ka daux ceu cao,
kieh simiang rao ma,
pa giex kaing tawng ka ping nyia Siyiex ku ceu.
11 Siyiex yuh nin hoik mawh pa hawt ka daux alo nawh pa koe leeing ju;
alo in hoik lih mawh ka daux to kaux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
12 Ka daux Khrit,
pa yum rhawm ka nawh.b
Tag pon tix ang ring hoek ka plak sivoe Siyiex.
13 Mawh kheu nan aux khiox khaw ka pex,
paw vi laux rhawm grawng pa kham aux krax kha rhawm son pex,
mai tieh tix kieh mawh min plak ngai heue.
Krax muh Khrit
14 Mawh kheu nan,
aux grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe keeing Siyiex.
15 Ceu pui can pa koe ka rao ma mai haktiex hoik mawh pa lih koe khaing nawh khawm uik.
16 Aux khiox khaw Siyiex hawt awm min plak ngai tix,
tawx riang rao ka pex ka daux Khuan Singax Singeh,
mai kieh ju pa koe daux pex koe riang.
17 Aux baox khiox khaw ka Khrit,
teei krax yum rhawm pex,
ot ka daux rhawm pex,
mai kieh pex koe riah rawng ka daux krax muh nawh,
18 son sang kieh pui cie Siyiex pon tix ot ka daux krax muh Khrit,
mawh pa kied ting kied lhaong tete.
19 Kieh pex pon tawng ka krax muh Khrit an pa hoik pon tix,
pa kawn lheu lhaong khaing ngai lai,
son sang kieh pa grum mhawm Siyiex yuh pex grum naog heue.
20 Siyiex pon yuh siawp sibrawm pa ting pa koe ka daux ex lih mawh khawm uik,
mai kawn lheu khaing ceu pa khiox khaw pa simie ex.
21 Kieh nawh ot ka daux ceu cao,
mai ka daux Ye-sux Khrit pon krax pa yaog yaw tix ju hoek tix ju,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue!
A-meung.
