12
“Kaing pa hoik yuh pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
hoik mawh awm pa giex pa going;
ka daux kix,
krax pie krax lhien,
krax pa cau nyhau nied ot nied nie.
Kix yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pui Ya-sux,
mai baox teei bee tawx keen ka pui Ai-cix heue.”
2 Simiang Siyiex deuh phi map ka pui Yo-tax;
nawh kawx tag sang hawt tix pawk bix ka pui Yi-seu-liex awm kaing pa hoik yuh kix,
mai ciyang ka kix.
3 abYam kawn ot tax du kix Ya-keux ka daux vaig miex tix,
nawh hoik jauh tix yuh naing ka pux ke tix Yi-sao,
khaix hoik ting nawh,
nawh baox yuh krax pa tieb tie ka Siyiex.
4 dNawh blok mai kawn cau Siyiex,
mai pon pex kah.
Khiox khaw tix yiiem kawn cau Siyiex nawb buan piang tix.
Siyiex si pup ka tax du ex Ya-keux ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
mai si kah ka nawh.c
5 Nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing;
Yie-heu-hua mawh ceu kaux nawh.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
doeh ceu cad Ya-keux eue,
pex tag caw tix hoek ka Siyiex;
tag caw tix yuh krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix,
yuh pa rauh pa see lih,
mai krox jun ka Siyiex pex tom si dauh heue.
Ca nied tix krai ka grawng krax prawng ca
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex ang rauh rhawm awm pui Cia-nang pa yuh lai gee khankix yuh nan;
kix hoik meed tix siao jiang ra mu gut kheem ka pui.
8 Kix ah nin:
‘Ex koe mau,
ex hoik mag mi;
mai ang koe pui pa pon deuh phi map ka ex,
son sang mag mi ka daux krax pa ang rauh ang see.’
9 eAux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh Siyiex pa viex pex lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix.
Aux tag sang baox kieh pex paung ot ka daux nyiex gox bang,
awm yam hoik jauh aux tix yaox pex ka daux tiag daux tu yuh nan heue.
10 “Aux si kah ka cao jhai,
kieh kix yaox ka riang su sai tom nie,
mai baox teei kix sidiex gau ka pui cie tix.
11 Viang mawh nan haktiex Ci-liex ca kawn naog ka phi map mai ka riang rao;
pui pa krub ka riang rao an kix tag sang yum khawm uik.
Kix hoik yuh krax pa tan lu ka riang rao kawn moi mhaing ka daux haktiex Cie-cia;
khu dee tan kix tag sang tieh tix lih mawh pum simaox ka daux tiag daux tu heue.”
12 fTax du kix Ya-keux to mox tix hoek ka haktiex Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax;
son sang yaox nawh tix koe moeng,
nawh liang yung son pui dee an.
13 gSimiang Siyiex ploi cao jhai tix kaux hu ka haktiex Ai-cix,
mai taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa yuh siliex cawng khankix lih,
kia ngien ka kix.
14 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex cheeik tix kieh Siyiex song rhawm;
nham pa kieh kix jauh tag sang deeh hoek piang to kaux kix.
Kix nu nai ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan nawh tag sang ciyang ka kix tom nyawm nyiem heue.
