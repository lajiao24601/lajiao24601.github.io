Been singaix tuah
35
Maw-si khrawm cub pui hun ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“In mawh kaing pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex nhie pex hawt tix yuh:
2 aPex chix tix yuh kaing liah singaix,
singaix aliah mawh singaix pa singax singeh,
caw tix tawx ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex kieh mawh singaix laux.
Can pui pa yuh kaing ka daux singaix in,
tag sang kieh nawh yum.
3 Ka daux singaix laux,
ka daux nyiex cao tix kawx,
ang pon tix phlu ka ngu heue.”
Krax tan son nyiex gox buan man
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 25.1–9)
4 Maw-si ah nin ka cub pui hun ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix:
“Simiang Siyiex tawx lox yuh nin:
5 Pex caw tix tan khawng phak ka Simiang Siyiex.
Can pui pa koe krax gaux rhawm chix tix tan lu ka khri,
mau,
tawng;
6 man keei sum,
hauk yung pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt;
man pa taing ka hauk pex.
7 Hauk yung mhaing pa siang tix raoh;
hak pa mhawm,
khaox Cing-heu-huang;
8 bee kawk ngu;
nyawng bhawng pa yuh ka bee pa singax singeh mai ceu sidah bhawng;
9 siao kraix ka sibex Yi-fu-teu cao giex kaing li pa ting,
mai na bat ka riang naok siang simaox bawd raoh mai siang simaox tang ceu heue.”
Kraung daux nyiex gox buan man
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 39.32–43)
10 “Can pui pa koe min pa chix yuh ka kaing taix ka daux si nax pex,
tag caw tix hoek yuh ka kraung khrai pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex khawm uik:
11 Pa mawh nyiex gox buan man,
toh choek nyiex gox buan man mai dawb tawb ka piang plak praix,
goik,
pang joh phia jung,
lat gi,
rong,
khawm daux jhup jaong.
12 Teug sijax mukheu,
khaox grawng teug sijax mukheu,
dawb teug sijax mukheu,
mai man tie pa tieb.
13 Pheen tan,
khaox grawng pheen,
mai kraung pa siao khawm uik;
khao mun pa tan lu ka Siyiex.
14 Jawk kawk ngu mai kraung pa siao tau;
kawk ngu mai bee kawk ngu.
15 Khu dee tan pa gleeing nyawng bhawng mai khaox grawng;
bee pa singax singeh mai ceu pa nyawng bhawng;
man tie ka dee laig daux nyiex gox buan man.
16 Khu dee tan pa tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tau mai rub tawng,
khaox grawng,
mai kraung pa siao ka khawm uik;
pan khoek mai jaong pan.
17 Man tie vung viang biang brawk,
rong mai daux jhup jaong;
man tie ka dee laig ka biang brawk.
18 Jim nyiex gox buan man mai vung viang brawk mai maox.
19 Kraung ceep pa kied mhawm pa ceep cao giex kaing li yam laig tix yuh kaing krub daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
pa mawh kraung singax singeh ceep Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix heue.”
Krax tan kawn pui
20 Cub pui hun ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik deeh lih hu khaing sivoe Maw-si;
21 can pui ku kaux pa gaux rhawm tix tan lu khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
tag caw tix viex krax tan hoek,
mai tan lu kieh mawh pa siao son yuh ka nyiex gox buan man.
Kix viex kraung siao khawm uik hoek,
son pa yuh kaing krub mai kraung ceu pa siao tix caing yuh sibex singax singeh cao giex kaing li kah.
22 Simex mai bun ku kaux pa gaux rhawm tix tan khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai kix viex nyex caing,
krim yhaok,
jeep giien,
soi ngawg,
mai kraung khri ku ceu tan lu ka Siyiex.
23 Can pui pa koe man keei sum,
pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt,
mai man pa taing tix ka hauk pex,
hak pex pa siang tix raoh,
mai hak pa mhawm plang,
viex hoek khawm uik.
24 Can pui pa pon tan khawng tan ka mau mai tawng,
viex hoek tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex;
pui pa koe khaox Cing-heu-huang son pa sang siao ka kaing jhaw khankix,
viex hoek nang.
25 Bun pa chix taing khankix,
tang viex hoek ka man taing man keei sum pa taing tix,
man keei pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
man keei hauk pa raoh rawt.
26 Kix baox siao hauk pex lih mawh keei heue.
27 Simiang khankix chix tix caing ot ka sibex Yi-fu-teu pa singax singeh mai ka piang na bat naok,
siang simaox raoh mai siang simaox tang ceu,
28 mai bee kawk ngu,
nyawng bhawng pa yuh bee pa singax singeh mai sidah bhawng,
mai bee khankix hoek.
29 Can ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
simex ang ah,
bun ang ah,
son kaing ting pa tawm Simiang Siyiex Maw-si,
yuh hoik viex khawng phak lak sawng khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
Cang mai pa sum nyiex gox buan man
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 31.1–11)
30 Maw-si ah nin ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik laug ka Pi-sha-liex kawn simex Wu-lix,
doeh doe ceu pui Hu-eux,
ka daux ceu pui Yo-tax.
31 Khaix an,
tom kieh khuanb tix naog ka nawh,
mai tawx nawh koe krax lhak lhiao,
ping nyia cuyi,
krax keud krax sawn,
pon chix yuh kaing ku ceu,
32 awm pa yuh ka riang pak peung,
mai raug kraung ku ceu ka khri,
mau,
tawng.
33 Pon chix saik chix kleuk ka siang simaox;
pon chix saik ka khaox riang pak peung;
mai baox pon yuh ka kaing taix pa mhawm ku ceu.
34 Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx riang rao krax keud krax chix ka nawh mai ka Ya-si-sha-maw kawn simex Ya-heu-li-ya-pawx doeh ceu pui Tang.
35 Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx kix grum naog ka ping nyia pa chix,
pon yuh ka kaing ku ceu,
awm kaing pa saik,
pa chix yuh koe pak peung,
pa chix taing ka man keei sum,
pa caing breeh lih ka keei pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
man keei pa koe lai tai pa raoh rawt,
mai pa taing ka man ku ceu khankix.
Kix pon yuh ka kaing ku ceu,
mai pon yuh lih ka riang pak peung ku ceu heue.”
