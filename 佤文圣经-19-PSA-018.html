Lox rhax grawng pa pon pex Ta-wuea
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 22.1–51)
18
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh riang rao aux;
aux kied muh maix tete heue!
 
2 Simiang Siyiex mawh rang simaox mai tab pa kia ngien ka aux;
Siyiex aux mawh nyiex dee mox aux tix,
mawh dieb tie naok aux,
mawh riang rao pa taoh ka aux,
mawh guh veng pa tieb tie ka aux.
3 Aux khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex pa khaw tix pon ka krax yaog yaw,
nawh tom tag taoh aux luat luan khaing pui koeng heue.
 
4 Maox krax yum hoik vung vhien ka aux;
rawm ting krax laux leeing hoek tix chun ka aux.
5 Maox krax yum hoik vung vhien ka aux;
rub si meeig krox jun ka aux;
6 Ka daux krax pa kha rhawm aux khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
Aux khiox khaw Siyiex teem coi tix.
Nawh mhong lox aux khaing ka daux nyiex buan;
lox khiox khaw aux hoik laig daux yhaok nawh heue.
 
7 Yam an,
haktiex yung yang;
riah gawng lua khankix hoik si yaung si kua,
mawh kheu pa song rhawm Siyiex.
8 Ka daux meeh nawh taox ngu lih khaing,
ngu mai gaox ngu pa cap lih khaing daux jheeh nawh.
9 Nawh plak ka rao ma,
mai to kaux nawh lih hoek;
koe pu pai awm pa faik ot ka greem caong nawh.
10 Nawh brug ka Ci-lu-paw pu;
nawh kieh preeik bhaung nied tix pu brax.
11 Nawh kieh pa faik tawb to kaux tix;
pai awm pa viex rawm lai lawm ka nawh;
12 Si tap,
gaox ngu lih khaix pa jhan riang ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
mai hoik yieh ka pu pai awm lung an heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex ploi sah lih tuh khaing rao ma;
cao pa ting siawp sibrawm ploi lox tix lihb.
14 Nawh puing te grheu pui pa koeng to;
nawh ploi ngu sah kieh kix suk sag.
15 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yam ne raw maix ka pui koeng,
si brum ka kix ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
haktiex ka greem rawm sigrit sigra hoik jhi tix lih,
riah haktiex kawx hoik ot ka kaong heue.
 
16 Simiang Siyiex tawx taix tix giex ka aux khaing rao ma dee lhaong,
mai bluh aux lih khaing daux rawm pa raux.
17 Nawh taoh aux luat luan khaing tawm taix pui koeng pa ting riang,
taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa caox pa caig,
mawh kheu kawn lheu ting riang kix khaing aux.
18 Singaix yam yaox aux ka krax kha,
kix puing ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex teem coi ka aux.
19 Nawh thung aux luat luan khaing daux krax pa kha rhawm;
nawh taoh aux,
kheu muh nawh ka aux heue.
 
20 Simiang Siyiex pawk buan son ka aux kheu rauh see aux,
mawh kheu mawh aux pui ang koe dee lud tom tawx buan ka aux.
21 Aux priex tix hawt ka daux krax Simiang Siyiex;
aux ang sax tieh vheen ka Siyiex.
22 Aux priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh khawm uik;
aux ang sax tik vud ka gum lox tawx nawh.
23 Nawh tawng ang aux koe dee lud;
aux singai hu khaing pa caox pa caig.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom hawt tix pawk buan son ka pa rauh see aux,
mawh kheu hoik tawng nawh mawh aux pui pa ang koe dee lud.
 
25 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix teei krax pa kaux sijax tix tawx ka piang pui pa kaux sijax;
maix teei krax pa grum mhawm tix tawx ka piang pui pa grum mhawm.
26 Pui pa singax,
maix teei pa singax singeh kia ngien ka nawh;
pui pa caox pa caig,
maix ang rhawm yaox ka nawh.
27 Maix taoh ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi;
maix kieh pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tiiem heue.
 
28 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix tok kawk ngu aux riang;
Siyiex aux teei tix tik pa faik son aux.
29 Maix tawx aux koe riang pa tieb tie pui pa koeng,
pon yuh tab dee ot kix laux leeing heue.
 
30 Siyiex in
daux krax cie nawh mawh pa grum mhawm!
Gum lox nawh mawh pa kied kaux cong!
Nawh awm dieb tie naok mai pon kia ngien ka can pui pa deuh rhawm ka nawh.
31 Lheu khaing Simiang Siyiex,
ang lai koe Siyiex tang kaux;
mawh ngie Siyiex pa mawh rang simaox pa pon teem coi ka ex.
32 Nawh mawh Siyiex pa tawx riang rao ka aux;
nawh kieh krax hu aux mhawm.
33 cNawh kieh aux awm cak miex pa brax to,
kieh aux pon cung kaux cong ka gawng lhaong.
34 Nawh gau aux son sang puing naing yuh ka mawx,
pon kieh aux vaik ak puh pa kied kiien heue.
 
35 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh dieb tie naok pa taoh ka aux.
Maix teei taix khawn dawm tix yaog ka aux;
krax pa kia ngien maix kieh aux haok huan.
36 Maix kieh daux krax lawng aux vieh va,
ang sax goh tix bawg kawx.
37 Aux leue ka pui koeng,
mai pon giex ka kix;
phan ang puing pex ka kix,
mai aux ang deeh plak khaix tix.
38 Aux puing kix goh,
mai kieh kix ang pon cung;
kix hoik goh ot greem caong aux khawm uik.
39 Maix tawx riang rao pa pon puing aux naing;
maix pon kieh aux giex ka pui pa koeng.
40 Maix kieh pui pa koeng ka aux yaog tix to;
maix kieh aux yuh laux ka pui pa koeng ka aux.
41 Kix kok yo pui taoh tix,
mai ang koe pui pa taoh ka kix.
Kix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang pawk ka lox kix.
42 Aux yuh kix laux leeing,
awm bhaung pa phlu nyaox;
aux dug dig ka kix,
awm dug dig ka phawng tiex daux laih heue.
 
43 Maix hoik taoh aux lih khaing daux pui pa tieh vheen ka aux,
yaog aux mawh simiang ting ka piang meung haktiex;
pui tang ceu tang cad kawx nghiet ka lox aux.
44 Yam mhong kix ka aux,
tom tag nghiet ka aux;
pui tang ceu kawx hoik koe krax cu krax nghiet ka plak sivoe ngai aux khawm uik.
45 Rhawm rhi pa kua kix hoik grai,
si yaung si kua hoik lih khaing daux tab ot kix heue.
 
46 Simiang Siyiex im ju leeing ju leeing phan,
mai tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka cao pa kia ngien ka aux heue!
Siyiex mawh cao pa taoh aux,
tag caw tix krai pao pa ting pa lhaong nawh heue!
47 Nawh kieh aux pon pex ka pui pa koeng;
nawh kieh pui ku ceu cu nghiet ka aux khawm uik,
48 taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa koeng khankix heue.
 
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik kieh aux pon giex ka pui pa koeng;
maix hoik teem coi aux luat luan khaing pui tud pui rhim.
49 dMawh kheu nan,
aux sang yaog yaw maix ka meung piang haktiex;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix.
 
50 Siyiex hoik kieh simiang pa laug tix puing pex ku bawg;
nawh tawx krax muh tix leeing ju ka Ta-wue pa hoik laug tix mai khawm doeh ceu cad nawh khankix heue.
