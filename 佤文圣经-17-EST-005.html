Yi-see-thie khre priex son simiang mai Ha-mang
5
Khaix hoik tuah Yi-see-thie priex preem ka daux loe singaix,
nawh ceep ka kraung moeng simiang mai laig ka daux nyiex haw simiang,
cung ka daux brawk nyiex haw simiang,
sivoe nawh mawh dee ot simiang.
Simiang jiet ngawm ka piang dang ngeung,
veeh pak ngai tix plak siviex nyiex buan.
2 Simiang yaox ka cung moeng tix Yi-see-thie plak praix,
caox rhawm ka nawh,
mai tom tawx ka khaox gix khri.
Kheu nan,
Yi-see-thie haok sivoe,
mai giex ka toh khaox gix.
3 Simiang chawk nawh:
“Moeng aux Yi-see-thie eue,
koe kaing ceu pa tix lie?
Simie maix tix cie ceu pa tix,
aux tag chix tawx ka maix,
mawh meung aux tix plak kawx,
aux tag chix tawx ka maix heue.”
4 Yi-see-thie pawk lox:
“Phan aux pon krax muh Vawng,
khiox khaw Vawng mai Ha-mang daux bo in hoek tix ih priex pa khre aux heue.”
5 Simiang tom kieh pui kok ka Ha-mang hoek dum mawx dum an,
son sang ih tau priex pa khre Yi-see-thie heue.
Kheu nan,
simiang mai Ha-mang hoek tix ih tau priex.
6 Yam nyaux ka plai,
simiang baox chawk ka Yi-see-thie:
“Simie maix tix cie ceu pa tix,
aux tag chix tawx ka maix.
Mawh meung aux tix plak kawx,
aux kawx chix tawx ka maix heue.”
7 Yi-see-thie ah nin:
8 “Phan aux pon krax muh Vawng,
cu ka lox pa rawg aux,
khiox khaw simiang mai Ha-mang pa sax baox hoek tix ih priex pa khre aux.
Yam an,
aux tag krai ka simiang pa simie aux cie heue.”
Ha-mang keud tix sang pauh ka Maw-ti-kai
9 Ha-mang kied gaux rhawm tix lih khaing poi.
Viang mawh nan,
yam hoek nawh ka diang sivie nyiex haw simiang,
mai yaox Maw-ti-kai ngawm ka dee an ang cung,
ang koe krax yung yi tix,
tom song rhawm kah.
10 Viang mawh nan,
nawh yeen ka krax song rhawm tix,
hoek ka daux nyiex tix,
khaix an kok ka paox grawm tix hoek ka daux nyiex,
mai kok nang moeng tix Si-li-see hoek.
11 Ha-mang tawx kix yaox krax mi tix mai kawn simex kawn bun tix,
mai baox krai ghaok simiang min tix ting,
kieh nawh ting khaing cao kaing tang kaux mai simiang tang kaux.
12 Ha-mang baox ah nan:
“Ang ngie mawh nin,
moeng simiang Yi-see-thie kawn khre priex son simiang mai son nawh,
pa sax kawn kok nawh baox hu heue!
13 Viang mawh nan,
yam yaox aux ka pui Yo-thaix Maw-ti-kai an ngawm ka diang siviex nyiex haw simiang,
aux tom ce in ang lai koe dee bleeh son aux heue.”
14 Moeng nawh mai paox grawm nawh krai ah nin ka nawh:
“Yuh ka mawx maix ang tawx lox kieh pui yuh khaox pa ciyang pui koe tix nga ra kongjhee tix gawng lie?
Pa sax pon ngawb,
maix chix tix khiox khaw simiang teuk teue Maw-ti-kai ka piang.
Yuh nin,
maix tag gaux rhawm tix hu ih priex preem heue.”
Ha-mang keud kaing in mhawm,
tom kieh pui yuh ka khaox pa ciyang heue.
