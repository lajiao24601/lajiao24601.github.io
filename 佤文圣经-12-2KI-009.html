Yaog Yie-hu mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex
9
Yam an,
cao jhai Yi-li-sa kok cao jhai pa nhum ju tix kaux hoek,
mai tawx lox ah nin ka nawh:
“Nied rien khre maix mhawm!
Viex kawk bee sime in hu ka veng La-mawx haktiex Ci-liex.
2 Yam hoik hoek dee an,
maix tom sawg kawn simex Yaw-sa-fa,
kawn saux Ling-see pa kaux Yie-hu,
viex nawh hu khaing paox grawm nawh,
hoek ka daux lawg nyiex ang pui koe kah,
3 mai hieh bee sime in ka piang kaing nawh,
ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex krai pao ah nin,
nawh yaog maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.’
Khaix an,
maix caw tix paoh ka siviex mai nied to hu khaing dee an heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
cao jhai pa nhum ju an hu ka La-mawx.
5 Yam hoek nawh,
nawh yaox cao bung naing khankix kum kawng paox tix,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Simiang naing,
aux koe lox sang krai ka maix heue.”
Yie-hu chawk ah nin:
“Maix sang si kah mai pa kaux tix lie?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Si kah mai maix.”
6 aMawh kheu nan,
kiex ra kaux tom hu ka daux lawg nyiex;
cao jhai pa nhum ju an hieh bee sime ka piang kaing Yie-hu,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex krai pao ah yuh nin:
‘Aux yaog maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex cie aux.
7 Maix tag caw tix pauh ka cao simiang maix pa mawh kawn simex Ya-ha,
pa mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex yam in.
Yuh nin,
aux tom ciyang ka phi map pa pauh Yie-si-pix ka cao jhai aux mai ka siliex tang kaux nawh.
8 Can pui nyiex ma Ya-ha mai doeh ceu cad nawh tag caw tix yum;
aux tag sang yuh laux ka can simex nyiex nawh khawm uik khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
mawh pui pa hoik luat luan ang ah,
pa ang bax luat luan ang ah,
tag sang yuh laux lak pak leeing.
9 Aux sang yuh cub nyiex ma nawh awm hoik yuh aux ka cub nyiex simiang Yie-law-paw-ang pui Yi-seu-liex mai cub nyiex ma simiang Pa-sa yuh nan.
10 bPhan mawh Yie-si-pix,
ang koe pui pa hu si paung ka nawh,
sox haktiex pui Yie-see-liex tag sang ih to kaux pa yum nawh.’ ”
Khaix hoik krai cao jhai pa nhum ju lox tom paoh siviex yaog tix to heue.
11 Yie-hu deeh ing ka dee ot paox grawm tix dee an;
kix chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix ang bax yuh ka mawx tix lie?
Pui yhot an sang kieh maix yuh ceu pa tix?”
Yie-hu pawk ah nin:
“Pex hoik tawng nawh mai tawng nawh hoek yuh ceu pa tix heue.”
12 Kix ah nin:
“Yix ang tawng heue,
khiox khaw maix krai ka yix liak!”
Yie-hu ah nin:
“Nawh krai ka aux,
Simiang Siyiex krai pao ah yuh nin:
‘Aux yaog maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.’ ”
13 Paox grawm Yie-hu khankix dum mawx dum an teei tom sibex ting tix ot ka piang glawk bawng,
mai kieh Yie-hu cung piang,
khaix an phlu teet tawng kok yo ah nin:
“Yie-hu mawh simiang heue!”
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-heu-lang
14-15 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-hu keud tix sang tieh vheen ka simiang Yaw-heu-lang.
Yam an simiang Yaw-heu-lang ot tix pai jhawm saux tix ka Yie-see-liex,
mawh kheu hoik mat nawh yam puing tix simiang Shui-li-yax pa kaux Ha-shiiex ka haktiex La-mawx.
Yie-hu ah nin ka paox grawm tix khankix:
“Phan pex hawt rhawm aux,
caw tix paw kieh pui tix kaux kawx to lih khaing La-mawx,
mai hoek tawx lox ka Yie-see-liex.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-hu brug ka ling law cie nawh hu ka plak Yie-see-liex.
Khaix saux mat Yaw-heu-lang ang bax nyang moi;
simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex hoek jhak nawh dee an heue.
17 Pui Yie-see-liex cao pa pau ot glawk dee lhaong,
kok yaox ka Yie-hu mai pui cie nawh hu plak in,
nawh tom kok yo ah nin:
“Aux yaox pui tix cub hu plak in heue!”
Yaw-heu-lang ah nin:
“Ploi pui naing brug brung tix kaux hu jhak ka kix mawh ang mawh pui mhawm heue.”
18 Pui naing brug brung an hu ka Yie-hu dee an,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang simie tix tawng grawng pa hoek maix mawh ang mawh grawng sang yuh paox grawm pa mhawm.”
Yie-hu ah nin:
“Yuh maix tix ka lie?
Maix veeh to kaux tix hawt plak khaix aux khaing.”
Pui pa pau tix jhak an kok krai ah nin,
kawn cau pa ploi hu hoik hoek dee ot kix tan,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax deeh hoek.
19 Simiang baox ploi pui naing brug brung tang kaux baox hu.
Pui naing brug brung in baox chawk lox pa awm paox ka Yie-hu yuh nan.
Yie-hu baox pawk ah nin:
“Yuh maix tix ka lie?
Maix veeh to kaux tix hawt plak khaix aux khaing heue.”
20 Pui pa pau tix jhak an,
baox kok krai ah nin,
pui naing brug brung pa ploi hu an,
hoik hoek dee ot kix tan,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax deeh hoek.
Nawh baox deeh krai tix gum ah nin:
“Simiang pui cub an hoik awm pui yhot mai grheu ling law,
kied awm grang Yie-hu heue.”
21 Simiang Yaw-heu-lang tawx lox ah nin:
“Rien khre ling law cie aux!”
Kix hoik rien khre ka ling law,
nawh mai simiang Ya-ha-shiex tom tang brug ling law cie tix hu rieb ka Yie-hu.
Kix yaox nawh ka daux kaung Na-paw.
22 Yaw-heu-lang chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix viex pa nhim hoek lie?”
Yie-hu pawk ah nin:
“Koe pa nhim ceu pa tix lie?
Ka daux meung kawn koe miex maix Yie-si-pix pa yuh si meed sat mai riang krub,
pa nhim chix koe yuh ka mawx lie?”
23 Yaw-heu-lang kling deeh ka ling law cie tix,
riag yo mai to tix ah nin:
“Ya-ha-shiex eue,
Yie-hu hoik tieh vheen!”
24 Yie-hu vaik ak puh,
greeik riang tix lih khawm uik,
tix taix te khaing krox puing ka blawg Yaw-heu-lang,
mai phlat ka rhawm nawh;
Yaw-heu-lang goh yum ka piang ling law.
25 Yie-hu ah nin ka Pi-cia pui cao teem coi nawh:
“Teei tik to kaux yum nawh ka daux kaung Na-paw heue!
Doh keud yam hawt ax ka ling law khaix keeing simiang Yaw-heu-lang pa kaux Ya-ha,
Simiang Siyiex hoik ah lox pa yuh nin ka Ya-ha:
26 c‘Kawx aux yaox ka briex pui tix pauh Na-paw mai kawn simex nawh khankix yum;
aux tag sang ciyang maix ka daux kaung in tete!’
Mawh kheu nin,
maix tag caw tix tik to kaux yum Yaw-heu-lang ka daux kaung cie Na-paw,
son sang mawh pa hawt tix yuh ka gum lox Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Pui pauh ka simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex yum
27 Simiang Ya-ha-shiex yaox ka can kaing pa lih tix mawh,
nawh tom brug ka ling law to hu plak veng Paw Ha-kangd.
Yie-hu leue khaix nawh;
nawh tawx lox ka pui pa ot greem tix ah nin:
“Nawh kawx pauh ka heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
pui greem Yie-hu ot daux krax hu ling law cie Ya-ha-shiex ka plak Ku-eux,
dex ka Yi-paw-liien,
mai puing nawh mat ka te.
Viang mawh nan,
Ya-ha-shiex ca nied tix to hu plak sivoe ngai,
hoek ka veng Mi-cie-to tom taug yum dee an.
28 Siliex nawh teei ling law tang to kaux yum nawh deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai si paung nawh ot tau mai si meeig cub nyiex simiang ka veng Ta-wue heue.
29 Yam mawh Ya-ha-shiex simiang pui Yo-tax,
mawh yam giex kawn simex Ya-ha simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-heu-lang kaing daux kao tix num heue.
Pui pauh ka Yie-si-pix moeng simiang yum
30 Yie-hu hoek ka Yie-see-liex.
Yie-si-pix hoik mhong can kaing pa lih tix mawh,
nawh tom sat hauk tix,
loh kraung mhawm tix,
mai kraix sidah ka ngai tix,
nawh tom ot daux doh siviex nyiex haw simiang,
kok jhak plak daux laih.
31 Yam laig Yie-hu ka diang siviex veng,
Yie-si-pix kok yo ah nin:
“Maix Sing-lixe in,
pui pauh simiang!
Maix chix viex pa nhim hoek lie?”
32 Yie-hu yaog kaing tix jhak,
ah nin:
“Pui mawx hawt rhawm aux lie?
Mawx lie?”
Cao kaing daux nyiex haw simiang ra loe kaux,
kok jhak nawh ka daux doh siviex.
33 Yie-hu ah nin ka kix:
“Nyheen nawh cawd!”
Kix tom nyheen Yie-si-pix cawd;
nham nawh phruah ka piang ding mai piang brung.
Yie-hu grheu ling law dig piang to kaux yum nawh,
34 mai hu laig daux nyiex haw simiang,
ih nang nyaux nang,
khaix an ah nin:
“Teei viex hu si paung ka bun pa hoek krax jhax lawg an;
kheu hoik mawh nawh kawn bun simiang.”
35 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa hu si paung ka nawh khankix,
yaox ngie ka si-ang kaing nawh mai si-ang taix caong nawh khaing.
36 fYam deeh ing kix krai ka Yie-hu,
Yie-hu ah nin:
“In hoik mawh kaing pa sang lih tix mawh pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex Yi-li-yax krai.
Nawh ah nin:
‘Sox sang ih to kaux yum Yie-si-pix ka daux veng Yie-see-liex;
37 khlawp si-ang pa deuk tag sang awm aing ka daux kaung,
mai ang koe pui pa pon tawng ka heue.’ ”
