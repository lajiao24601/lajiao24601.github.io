Kaing mhawm pa yuh Saw-law-meung
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 9.10–28)
8
Saw-law-meung sum nyiex buan mai nyiex haw simiang tix nga num.
2 Nawh kawx hoik cox sum ka veng pa tawx simiang Si-lang ka nawh,
mai nawh deuh pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka veng an kix.
3 Nawh pon ka haktiex Ha-ma Saw-pa,
4 sum ka veng pa ot ka daux tiag Pa-me-la,
mai baox cox sum veng pa deuh kraung khrai pa ot ka daux haktiex Ha-ma.
5 Saw-law-meung kawx deeh cox sum ka veng in kix plak greem:
paw Heu-loeng plak laong mai Paw Heu-loeng plak seh (koe siviex veng,
mai chix tix sau jhong veng kaux),
6 veng Pa-la,
can veng pa mox kraung khrai ot daux,
mai veng pa deuh ling law ot daux.
Nawh kawx hoik hoik kaing pa keud nawh tix yuh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Li-pa-noeng,
mai kaing pa yuh nawh ka piang meung ku dauh khawm uik.
7-8 Saw-law-meung khin tix kieh can doeh ceu cad pui Cia-nang khawm uik yuh kaing pa kha rhawm tix;
kix mawh doeh ceu cad pui pa ang pui Yi-seu-liex bax pauh yum yam pon ka haktiex in,
khawm pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we,
mai pui Yie-pu-see khankix.
Doeh ceu cad kix ca kawn mawh siliex cawng tom hoek yam in.
9 Saw-law-meung ang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh siliex cawng,
mai kieh kix yuh ngie pui naing,
simiang naing,
cao kaing ling brung,
mai pui naing brug brung khankix.
10 Nawh koe cao kuang kaing ra yieh tix phuan kaux,
mai kuang kaing ku ceu khawm uik heue.
11 Saw-law-meung teei moeng tix pa mawh kawn bun simiang Ai-cix,
gah tix khaing veng Ta-wue hoek ka daux nyiex haw pa khraox sum tix son nawh.
Nawh ah nin:
“Moeng aux in ang khaw tix ot daux nyiex haw cie simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ta-wue,
mawh kheu can dee hoek teug sijax mukheu ku dauh hoik mawh dee pa singax heue.”
12 Saw-law-meung tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing piang khu pa hoik yuh tix pa ot ka plak sivoe nyiex buan.
13 aNawh hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa deuh Maw-si,
mai tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing son singaix pa singax ku singaix;
singaix pa singax in kix mawh singaix tuah,
yam haok khix,
mai tix num loe mu poi,
pa mawh Poi Tik-su-saix,
Poi Khrawm Nghox Koi,
Poi Nyiex-gox-bang.
14 Nawh hawt been pa hoik deuh keeing tix Ta-wue,
mai guah tix tawx kaing pa sang yuh cao giex kaing li mai kaing pa sang yuh pui Li-we ku singaix;
pui Li-we teem coi cao giex kaing li rom tix rhax lai tau mai yuh kaing tang ceu khankix.
Nawh kawx hawt ka lox tawx Ta-wue pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
mai khrawm cub caw pa guah tix pau nyiex buan,
kieh kix yuh kaing pa pau siviex veng ku dauh ku singaix.
15 Kaing pa krai Ta-wue ka cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we son nyiex krao mai kaing tang ceu khankix,
kix hoik hawt tix yuh ku ceu heue.
16 Ka daux bawg yam in,
can kaing pa keud Saw-law-meung tix yuh khawm uik hoik si dauh khawm uik,
jauh tom yam yuh kix jaong nyiex buan tom hoik si dauh,
mai kaing pa yuh kix hoik pon mhawm khawm uik heue.
17 Khaix an,
Saw-law-meung hoek ka Yi-shuing Cia-piie mai Yi-lux;
in hoik mawh diang praok rawm sigrit sigra Yi-tong ka dee laig ka siviex rawm A-kha-pa-wang ra dauh.
18 Simiang Si-lang tawx reu ka Saw-law-meung tix blah,
reu an kix kieh mawh cao kaing pui naing cie simiang Si-lang pa thung,
mai hoik kieh mawh pui pa kub rhawm keud pa mawh cao phat.
Kix brug reu hu tau mai simiang naing pui cie Saw-law-meung hoek ka Eu-fe,
mai kix viex tix tawx khri ka Saw-law-meung khaing dee an tix mheen phuan rheng kongcing heue.
