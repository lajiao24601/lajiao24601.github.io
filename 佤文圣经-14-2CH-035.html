Yaw-si-yax yuh Poi Luat-luan
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 23.21–23)
35
Simiang Yaw-si-yax yuh Poi Luat-luan ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex.
Ka daux khix tix kao pon singaix,
kix pauh khawng eei to liang son poi in.
2 Simiang tawx cao giex kaing li khankix yuh kaing ka daux nyiex buan,
ah lox kaux cong nhie kix yuh kaing yuh tix mhawm mhawm.
3 Nawh kawx krai ka pui Li-we,
pa mawh sara Yi-seu-liex khankix pa hoik tan lu to kaux tix ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex deuh teug sijax mukheu ot daux nyiex buan pa hoik sum simiang Saw-law-meung kawn simex Ta-wue,
mai ang lai siao gah jee gah blaong.
Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix yuh kaing son Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex mai son pui cie nawh Yi-seu-liex.
4 aPex tag caw tix hawt ka kaing pa hoik guah simiang Ta-wue mai kawn simex nawh simiang Saw-law-meung tix tawx ka pex,
mai hawt tix yuh awm ceu cad cub nyiex tix mai bawg yam sang caw tix yuh kaing pa pau nyiex buan.
5 Pex tag caw tix tang guah lih mhawm mhawm,
son sang kieh pui tix blah ka daux pex yaox tix teem coi ka pui Yi-seu-liex ku nyiex ku ma.
6 Pex pauh ka kawn yung mai pex Poi Luat-luan,
kieh to kaux tix singax,
rien khre ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
son sang kieh paox ceu paox cad pex Yi-seu-liex pon priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.”
7 Simiang Yaw-si-yax tan lu yung khaing phung yung cie to kaux tix loe mheen mu,
kawn yung,
kawn pex,
mai moi mhaing loe rheng mu,
son pa sang lo kawn pui ka daux Poi Luat-luan.
8 Siliex nawh kawx gaux rhawm tix tan lu son kawn pui,
son cao giex kaing li,
mai son pui Li-we.
Cao kaing ka nyiex buan,
pa mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting Si-leu-cia,
Sa-cia-li-yax,
mai Yie-shiie tawx kawn yung mai kawn pex ka cao giex kaing li tang kaux khankix kix ra rheng liah yieh mu,
moi mhaing loe yieh mu,
son sang kieh mawh krax tan.
9 Simiang pui Li-we,
pa mawh Keu-nang-ya,
See-ma-ya mai pux aik nawh Na-thang-yiex,
Ha-sa-pi-ya,
Yie-lix,
mai Yaw-sa-pa tawx kawn yung mai kawn pex phuan rheng mu,
mai moi mhaing phuan yieh mu ka pui Li-we,
son sang kieh mawh krax tan heue.
10 Khaix hoik rien khre ka Poi Luat-luan mhawm mhawm,
cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx simiang,
mai kaux mawx kaux an hoik hawt tix yuh awm bawg yam kaing pa khaw nawh tix yuh heue.
11 Pui Li-we tawh ka hak kawn yung mai hak kawn pex pa hoik pauh tix,
cao giex kaing li phruah nham ka piang khu dee tan,
12 khaix an kix hawt awm cub nyiex mawx cub nyiex an,
mai guah tix tawx khawng tan pa kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka kawn pui,
son sang kieh kix pon hawt tix tan lu awm lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik krai Maw-si yuh nan.
13 bPui Li-we hawt tix yuh awm been pa deuh mai tong khawng tan Poi Luat-luan ka piang ngu,
mai baox ngaik khawng tan ka daux aw,
daux ngoi,
daux aw khang,
khaix an nied tix guah kawn pui ih nex.
14 Khaix hoik yuh kiang in si dauh,
pui Li-we tom taug rien nex son to kaux tix,
mai son cao giex kaing li doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng,
mawh kheu cao giex kaing li gleeing ka khawng tan khawm mu mai khawm bee vaig Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
yuh kaing in tom hoek pon bo.
15 cPui Li-we ceu cad cub nyiex Ya-sa pa kied chix rhax lai pa mawh Ya-sa Si-mang,
mai simiang Yie-tu-toeng cao pa chix yaox sivoe,
kix kaux mawx kaux an hoik tang yuh kaing yuh tix awm lox tawx simiang Ta-wue.
Pui pau diang siviex nyiex buan ang lai yoi tix hu khaing dee yuh tix kaing,
mawh kheu pui Li-we tang kaux hoik rien ka khawng tan Poi Luat-luan son kix.
16 Yuh nin,
can kaing pa sang yuh son Poi Luat-luan mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu khankix hoik hawt tix yuh awm lox pa tawx simiang Yaw-si-yax,
mai hoik rien khre mhawm ka daux singaix an.
17 dBeen ka daux aliah singaix,
can pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot dee an,
hoik gaux rhawm tix yuh Poi Luat-luan mai Poi Tik-su-saix khawm uik.
18 Jauh tom ju cao jhai Sa-mu-eu,
ang nyang sax yuh Poi Luat-luan pa ting yuh nin.
Simiang sivoe an kawx ang nyang sax koe pui tix kaux pa awm yuh simiang Yaw-si-yax,
cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
mai pui Yo-tax,
pui Yi-seu-liex,
pui Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix bawg in,
Poi Luat-luan pa ting yuh nin.
19 Poi Luat-luan pa gaux rhawm tix yuh bawg in hoik mawh pa yuh yam mawh Yaw-si-yax simiang ka daux kao daix num heue.
Kaing giex Yaw-si-yax hoik si dauh
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 23.28–30)
20 Khaix hoik yuh simiang Yaw-si-yax kaing in khawm uik son nyiex buan,
Ni-keu simiang Ai-cix thung pui naing hu puing ka Cia-ci-mi-see pa koe ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
Yaw-si-yax keud tix sang tieb tie ka nawh,
21 viang mawh nan,
Ni-keu tawx lox ka Yaw-si-yax ah nin:
“Naing pa puing aux in ang mawh pa dex dawt tau mai maix simiang Yo-tax;
aux ang hoek puing ka maix,
viang mawh nan,
hoek puing ka pui pa koeng ka aux.
Siyiex hoik nhie aux nied tix yuh kaing in.
Nawh teem coi ka aux,
maix paw vi tieb tie ka aux;
phan ang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang yuh maix laux leeing.
22 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-si-yax kawn ca sang puing.
Nawh ang nghiet ka lox pa tawx Siyiex ka simiang Ni-keu krai,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh loh kraung ceep tix,
mai hu ka bang naing pak lung Mi-cie-to heue.”
23 Te puing pui Ai-cix krox simiang Yaw-si-yax ka bang naing;
nawh tawx lox ka siliex tix ah nin:
“Viex aux hu heue,
aux kied nie mat!”
24 Kix kawm nawh lih khaing ling lawx,
mai deuh nawh ot ka piang ling law ka daux ra lhang,
viex nawh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Nawh yum dee an,
mai si paung nawh ka gawng si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang.
Can pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik yiiem doe son nawh heue.
25 Cao jhai Yie-li-mi yuh lox rhax yiiem doe son simiang Yaw-si-yax.
Tom hoek in singaix,
can simex bun pa kied chix rhax lai khankix hoek tix rhax lox rhax in khawm uik,
mai yiiem doe son nawh;
mai in lih tix mawh been ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex tix ceu.
Lox rhax in hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Lox Rhax Yiiem Doe heue.
26 Can kaing pa hoik yuh Yaw-si-yax khankix,
khawm pa hoik yum rhawm nawh ka Simiang Siyiex,
hawt nawh ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
27 mai grawng krax ot krax koe nawh tom yam jauh hoek yam si dauh,
hoik tiiem ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Yi-seu-liex mai Lai Been Simiang Yo-tax heue.
