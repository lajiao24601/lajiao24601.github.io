Simiang Siyiex pawk lox Yaw-paw
38
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex si kah tau mai Yaw-paw khaing ka daux bhaung klia tix.
2 Mawh maix mawx,
yuh ka mawx lai krai lox pa daox yuh nan,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang yum rhawm ka ping nyia cuyi aux?
3 Maix kaoh cung,
awm pui pa koe riang tix kaux;
maix pawk ka lox pa chawk aux ka maix.
4 Yam phan yam yuh aux haktiex,
ot maix dee mawx?
Phan maix hoik tawng tom nie ceu,
viex maix krai heue.
5 Maix tawng mawh mawx pa tat tix deuh ka to kaux nawh koe miex ding?
Pui mawx pa hoik prawk maox mhawm ka piang an?
6 Rong pa joha haktiex hoik jung tix kaux cong ka piang kraung ceu tix?
Pui mawx pa deuh jaong simaox son haktiex?
7 Ka daux ngaix an,
pa riang simuing pon ngawb khankix rom tix rhax lai tau;
kawn praix Siyiexb khankix kawx gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
 
8 dYam buing rawm sigrit sigrac tix lih khaing daux vaig haktiex,
pui mawx pon nyeep siviex,
mai pon thox ka lie?
9 Mawh aux pa teei pai awm tawb ka rawm sigrit sigra,
mai pua ot ka daux pa ting ceep pai awm.
10 Aux hoik tat mee praix son rawm sigrit sigra,
baox sau ka jhong siviex.
11 Aux krai ka nawh ah nin:
maix die dee in,
mai ang pon tix jao luan;
rhawm maix pa awm rawm puk pak pa cau nyhau,
tag sang kaog ot tin.
12 Yaw-paw eue,
ka daux ju maix,
maix hoik tawx lox ka pa riang pon ngawb lie?
13 Hoik sax tawx lox ka pa riang lih jhan haktiex,
hoik grheu ka tax yiex miex keeing pa caox pa caig lih khaing daux dee mox tix lie?
14 Pa riang singaix kieh gawng lua klawng ngawd tieh loh tix,
Awm kraung ceep pa hoik nu niep tix mhawm,
awm cum ka piang tiex glaox.
15 Pa riang pon ngaix kied hax,
kieh pui pa caox pa caig ang pon yuh kaing pa caox heue.
 
16 Hoik sax hu maix ka dee kied raux rawm sigrit sigra lie?
Hoik sax lai ling hu maix ka dee pa kied raux greem tiex lie?
17 Koe pui mawx pa jhi maix yaox ka siviex krax yum?
Hoik sax yaox maix ka pui pa pau ka siviex nyiex meung yum lie?
18 Hoik tawng maix haktiex koe miex ding lie?
Phan maix hoik tawng kah,
krai ka aux heue.
 
19 Maix hoik tawng pa riang kaoh khaing dee mawx?
Num dee kaoh pa faik ot dee mawx?
20 Maix pon jhi ka mee haktiex dee ot nawh lie?
Maix pon tawx kix deeh ing ka dee ot kix sivoe an lie?
21 Aux yum rhawm maix pon yuh,
mawh kheu ting num maix;
yam phan yam yuh haktiex maix hoik ot dee an heue!
 
22 Maix hoik laig daux nyiex krao rha,
phan ang mawh nan,
hoik yaox ka nyiex krao dee deuh pre lie?
23 Aux priex tix deuh ceu in kix,
son sang teei tix siao ka singaix bawg yam ploi krax laux krax leeing mai krax naing lih.
24 Maix hoik hoek ka dee lih singaix,
phan ang mawh nan,
hoek ka dee phlu bhaung plak lih singaix lie?
25 Pui mawx kaong daux hu rawm son lhiex ting?
Pui mawx paih krax son sah?
26 Pui mawx ploi lhiex lih dee ang pui ot?
Ka daux tiag daux tu pa ang pui koe?
27 Pui mawx pa jaux ka haktiex dee kroh,
kieh haktiex kuah ka rib klai?
28 Lhiex koe keeing lie?
Rawm ciex mawh pui mawx pa keh lie?
29 Pui mawx mawh miex si dap lie?
Si dap ka daux rao ma mawh pui mawx pa keh lie?
30 Pui mawx kieh rawm kiien awm simaox yuh nan?
Kieh piang rawm klon tix lih mawh si dap?
 
31 eMaix pon phrawk ka simuing khi max ot tau lie?
Maix pon yah ka maox simuing grawng lat lie?
32 Maix pon thung ka simuing haoh hun hawt ka bawg yam khix num lie?
Maix pon thung ka simuing pa awm krih pa ting pa iak lie?
33 Maix tawng ka been pa gah to kaux rao ma tix lie?
Maix pon but tix tiag tix yaox ka been koe ka piang haktiex lie?
 
34 Maix pon tawx lox ka pai awm lie?
Maix pon kieh lhiex ting lih lie?
35 Phan maix tawx lox ka pa riang tuh sah,
Nawh tag sang hoek ka plak sivoe ngai maix ah nin “hawt tix yuh ku bawg ku yam” lie?
36 Pui mawx krai ka sim cu lu f pa ah lox tix,
mai rawm klawng Ni-law tag sang haoh huan yam mawx?
Pui mawx krai ka ia simex,
sang lih lhiex yam mawx?
37 Pui mawx koe ping nyia cuyi tix hu simin ka lon pai awm?
Pui mawx pon pu pliak lon pai awm kieh rawm lhiex lih?
38 Pui mawx pon kieh tiex klon tix lih mawh lon?
Pui mawx pon kieh tiex glaox pid tix kaux tau?
 
39-40 Sivai sang si ngawm ka daux dawh,
phan ang mawh nan mox tix ot ka daux dawh;
maix pon sawg miex sivai sang si ih priex preem lie?
Maix pon kieh kawn sivai sang si sawm sak lie?
41 Yam pu hu pu hoek lak kheu cai rhawm tix,
yam yiiem kawn lak tix ih priex preem ka aux,
pui mawx hu tawx kix ih tom sak lie?
