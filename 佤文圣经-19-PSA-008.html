Min plak ngai Siyiex mai siawp sibrawm kawn pui
8
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao aux eue,
krax pa ting pa lhaong maix hoik jhi ka haktiex!
 
Lox rhax pa yaog yaw maix hoik haok ka rao ma;
2 akawn ngie mai kawn nyawm rhax lai tau.
Maix sum ka tab pa kaux cong son sang kuan ka pui koeng;
maix tieb tie ka krax pa puing pui koeng ka maix.
3 Aux yaog kaing tix jhak ka rao ma pa hoik teei maix taix tix phan yuh kah,
jhak ka khix ka simuing pa hoik phan yuh maix tix deuh khankix.
4 bEue,
kawn pui sawn mawh ceu pa tix,
maix lai kawn chix kia ngien ka nawh!
Kawn pui pa chix laux chix leeing sawn mawh ceu pa tix,
maix lai kawn chix muh ka nawh!
 
5 Kheu nan,
maix phan yuh nawh tiiem ngie khaing to kaux maixc;
maix teei min plak ngai,
pa koe min mawh mhok khri nawh.
6 dMaix kieh nawh kuang ka can pa phan pa yuh tix khawm uik,
hoik deuh ku ceu khawm uik ot greem caong nawh:
7 Pa mawh moi,
yung,
mai to sat ka daux tiag,
8 sim pa pu ka rao ma,
mai kax ka daux rawm klawng,
ceu pa im ju ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
mai hoik tawx nawh kuang khawm uik heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang ex eue,
krax pa ting pa lhaong maix hoik jhi ka haktiex khawm uik heue!
