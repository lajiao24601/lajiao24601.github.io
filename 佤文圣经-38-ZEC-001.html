Lai Sa-cia-li-yax
Simiang Siyiex kok pui cie tix deeh veeh tix hoek ka tix
1
aYam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng Paw-see ka daux num ra khix daix,
Simiang Siyiex plak jhi tix tawx gum lox in plak greem ka cao jhai Sa-cia-li-yax pa mawh kawn saux Yi-to,
kawn simex Pi-li-cia.
2-3 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing nhie Sa-cia-li-yax krai ka kawn pui ah nin:
“Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
aux hoik sax song rhawm ka tax du pex sivoe an;
viang mawh nan yam in aux krai ka pex ah nin:
‘Pex deeh veeh tix hoek ka aux,
mai aux tom taug deeh veeh tix hoek ka pex.
4 Pex paw vi awm tax du pex yuh nan.
Cao jhai pa koe sivoe an hoik krai gum lox aux ka kix,
nhie kix paw lai baox ot ka daux krax phi map pa caox pa caig,
viang mawh nan kix ang nghiet,
mai ang cu tix hawt ka lox aux.
5 Tax du pex mai cao jhai an kix ang lai koe khawm uik heue.
6 Aux teei siliex tix,
pa mawh cao jhai an kix tawx lox,
mai ne raw ka tax du pex;
viang mawh nan kix ang simi,
dee si dauh kix tang deeh hoek krax kha rhawm.
Khaix an kix loh ka rhawm rhi tix;
kix doh keud ka aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm kaing pa keud aux tix sang yuh,
hawt tix yuh awm pa kham kix tix pon,
mai ciyang ka kix heue.’ ”
Riang su sai brung
7 Yam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng ka daux num ra khix kao tix,
pa mawh Khix Si-pa-theux tix nga pon ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex teei riang su sai tawx gum lox ka aux.
8 bYam pon sawm,
aux yaox ka kawn cau Simiang Siyiex brug ka brung raoh tix mu,
mai kaog ka daux nong khaox plix mak cang pa koe daux ngawd;
ka plak khaix nawh koe brung raoh,
brung bawd,
mai brung paing.
9 Aux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
brung an kix mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux tag sang krai dee bleeh ka maix.
10 Kix mawh pa ploi Simiang Siyiex hoek lai ling tix jhak ka haktiex heue.”
11 Brung an kix krai pao ka kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Ex hoik tauh hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai hoik yaox grawng pa yien yawg haktiex khawm uik heue.”
12 Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing eue,
maix hoik song rhawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka veng Yo-tax ku veng atigliah num,
kawn sang koe miex glang maix tom taug sang chix caox rhawm ka kix lie?”
13 Simiang Siyiex teei lox pa nyawm rhawm pawk ka kawn cau Siyiex;
14 kawn cau Siyiex tag sang nhie aux krai pao lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing lih.
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux kied muh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung pa mawh veng pa singax cie aux tete,
15 Viang mawh nan,
aux kawn song rhawm ka meung pa pon krax nyawm ot nyawm koe an kix.
Kheu yam een aux krax pa song rhawm tix ka pui cie tix,
viang mawh nan kix nie baox thiem thie krax kha rhawm ka piang pui cie aux.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
aux deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tag sang caox rhawm ka veng in.
Nyiex buan aux tag sang deeh cox sum kah,
veng pa singax tag sang deeh cox ka heue.”
17 Kawn cau Siyiex baox nhie aux krai pao ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai ah nin,
veng cie nawh tag sang baox deeh pon krax pa haok huan;
nawh tag sang baox teem ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai laug veng in mawh cie to kaux tix heue.”
Riang su sai rung
18 Aux baox yaox rung pon mu ka daux riang su sai tang mu tix mu.
19 Aux chawk ka kawn cau Siyiex pa si kah ka aux sivoe ah nin:
“Rung in kix mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Kix mawh riang rao pa grheu pui Yo-tax,
pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.”
20 Khaix an Simiang Siyiex baox kieh aux yaox ka cang mai pon kaux.
21 Aux chawk ah nin:
“Pui in kix hoek yuh ceu tix lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Kix mawh riang rao pa hoek tix ghlat mai pa cot tix klup ka meung in kix;
mawh kheu meung in kix hoik laux leeing yuh haktiex Yo-tax,
grheu hu ka pui Yo-tax heue.”
