4
Bun miex pui Sa-ma-li-yax eue,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux heue!
Pex hoik kluing awm moi miex Pa-sang yuh nan.
Pex map nai ka pui pa iak riang iak rao,
gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai kieh mex kix tawx pex nyaux plai heue!
2 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka ceu kaux pa singax tix ah nin:
“Singaix an tag sang hoek tete,
koe pui tag sang teei goik vheuk pex hu kah,
awm vheuk pui kax yuh nan.
3 Pui tag sang viex pex hoek dex ka dee laux guh veng,
mai tik pex ka plak praix veng.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex ca kawn ang cu ka lox sidiex
4 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex hu yuh phi map ka haktiex singax Paw-theu-lix heue!
Pex hu sau riang tix yuh phi map ka haktiex Cie-cia heue!
Pex tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie ku singaix,
hoek loe singaix tan lu pa koe pex kao son tix son!
5 Pex tawng hu tan lu ka khao mun kieh mawh krax tan pa yaog yaw!
Pex tawng sau riang tix yaw rang di ka daux pa si pieh rhawm tix!
Viang mawh nan,
in kix hoik mawh kaing pa muh pex tix yuh heue!
6 “Mawh tete,
hoik mawh aux pa ploi sibhawm praix lih hoek ka daux veng ot pex,
mai kieh priex preem cud.
Viang mawh nan,
pex kawn ang bax deeh hoek ka aux.
7 Yam kied simie to ma pex ka rawm lhiex,
aux ang ploi rawm lhiex lih.
Aux sang kieh rawm lhiex lih ka veng in,
mai ang kieh lih ka veng an;
aux kieh rawm lhiex koe ka ma kaung nyiex in,
kieh ma kaung nyiex an kroh.
8 Koe pui pa ot daux veng ra loe mu veng hoik uik riang ka simie tix nyaux rawm,
mai hu sawg tix nyaux rawm ka veng pa koe sivie siveng kix,
viang mawh nan,
ang kub kix nyaux.
Kheu nan,
pex ca kawn ang bax hoek ka aux heue.
9 “Aux tawx bhaung si aoh,
mai kieh gleeing ka nghox koi pex;
aux ploi sido ih ka taux lhax,
plix siiet,
sime,
mai ka plix khaox ruh pex uik.
Kheu nan,
pex ca kawn ang bax hoek ka aux heue.
10 “Aux ploi jhawm saux lih hoek ka daux pex,
awm hoik ploi lih hoek ka Ai-cix yuh nan.
Aux hoik pauh ka pui pa nhum ju pex yum ka bang naing,
mai hoik tud rhim ka brung law pex.
Aux hoik kieh meeh pex ngheut ka bhawm cux to kaux yum pa ot ka daux bang.
Kheu nan,
pex ca kawn ang bax deeh hoek ka aux heue.”
11 aSimiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Aux hoik yuh laux leeing ka pui pa ot ka daux pex tix blah,
awm hoik yuh aux laux leeing ka Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la yuh nan.
Pex pa mawh pui kawn deuk khankix awm khix pa nge pa teei lih khaing daux phung ngu.
Kheu nan pex ca kawn ang bax hoek ka aux.
12 Grawh grawng nawh,
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux tag sang ciyang ka pex.
Mawh kheu sang yuh aux ka pex,
pex nied tix hoek krai ka aux sidawng heue!”
 
13 Siyiex phan yuh ka gawng mai ka bhaung,
mai krai alo tix ka kawn pui;
nawh kling pon ngaix mawh pon sawm,
mai kuang ka piang haktiex.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing heue.
