Lai Yam-jauh
Pa phan pa yuh Siyiex
1
Yam jauh,
Siyiex phan rao ma mai haktiex.a
2 Haktiex ang koe pak peung,
ang bax lih tix mawh.
Pa faik kheem ka piang tom dee raux an,
khuan Siyiexb ot vung viang piang rawm khankix.
3 cSiyiex tawx lox:
“Kieh pa riang lih koe.”
Pa riang lih koe heue.
4 Siyiex jhak ka mhawm pa riang an,
nawh tom guah pa riang khaing pa faik.
5 Tom kok pa riang mawh “pon ngaix”,
kok pa faik mawh “pon sawm”.
Pon bo hoik luan,
pon ngawb deeh hoek;
an mawh singaix tix heue.
6-7 dSiyiex baox tawx lox:
“Kieh mee koe ka daux sigreung rawm khankix,
kieh rawm plak piang guah tix khaing rawm plak greem.”
An khawm uik hawt lox pa tawx nawh lih tix mawh heue.
Kheu nan,
Siyiex phan ka mee praix,
mai guah rawm plak piang khaing plak greem.
8 Siyiex kok ceu kaux daux sigreung rawm an mawh “rao ma” pon bo hoik luan,
pon ngawb deeh hoek;
an mawh singaix ra heue.
9 Siyiex baox tawx lox:
“Kieh rawm pa koe greem rao ma khrawm paox tix tau,
mai kieh dee kroh tiex lih koe.”
An khawm uik hawt lox pa tawx nawh lih tix mawh heue.
10 Siyiex kok dee kroh tiex an mawh “haktiex”,
rawm pa hoik khrawm tix tau mawh “rawm sigrit sigra”.
Siyiex jhak ka haktiex mai rawm sigrit sigra mawh pa mhawm.
11 Khaix an,
Siyiex tawx lox:
“Haktiex sang kuah rib klai taux lhax ku ceu,
pa koe simie mai khaox pa koe plix ku ceu.”
An khawm uik hawt lox pa tawx nawh lih tix mawh heue.
12 Khaix an haktiex kuah rib klai taux lhax pa koe simie ceu mawx ceu an,
mai khaox ku ceu pa koe plix simie awm ceu mawx ceu an.
Siyiex jhak ka khaox an kix mawh pa mhawm.
13 Pon bo hoik luan,
pon ngawb deeh hoek,
an mawh singaix loe heue.
14 Siyiex baox tawx lox:
“Kieh mu pa riang lih koe ka daux mee praix rao ma,
son sang guah pon ngaix khaing pon sawm,
mai kieh mawh mhai ka son praix klawng mai son singaix sawm,
mai jhong khix mee nume.
15 Kieh pa riang pa koe daux mee praix an,
jhan riang ka piang haktiex.”
An khawm uik hawt lox pa tawx nawh lih tix mawh.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex phan ka pa riang ting ra mu,
singaix kuang ka pon ngaix,
khix kuang ka pon sawm,
mai nawh baox phan ka simuing.
17 Nawh tawx mu pa riang an koe daux rao ma,
son sang jhan ka haktiex,
18 son sang kuang pon ngaix mai pon sawm,
khan pa riang khaing pa faik.
Siyiex jhak ka mu pa riang an mawh pa mhawm.
19 Pon bo hoik luan,
pon ngawb deeh hoek;
an mawh singaix pon heue.
20 Siyiex tawx lox:
“Kieh to sat ku ceu lih koe daux rawm tom haoh hun,
kieh sim khankix pu ka rao ma.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex phan to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa ting pa im ju pa lhat ex kah,
mai can ceu to sat daux rawm mai sim pa pu ka rao ma khankix,
awm pak peung ceu mawx ceu an.
Siyiex jhak ka to sat in kix mawh pa mhawm.
22 Siyiex nawb buan ka piang ceu in kix:
“Kieh kax pre daux rawm sigrit sigra,
kieh sim saik khankix pre ka piang haktiex.”
23 Pon bo hoik luan,
pon ngawb deeh hoek,
in mawh singaix phuan heue.
24 Siyiex baox tawx lox:
“Kieh to sat ceu mawx ceu an lih koe ka piang haktiex,
khawng brie,
to sat,
viag vua.”
An khawm uik hawt lox pa tawx nawh lih tix mawh.
25 Kheu nan Siyiex phan khawng brie ku ceu ku cawng ka piang haktiex,
Siyiex jhak ka to sat in kix mawh pa mhawm heue.
26 hKhaix an,
Siyiex ah nin:
“Kieh ex yuh kawn pui awm riang pak peung,
greeh grang ex,f
mai kieh kix kuang kax khankix,
sim khankix,
mai khawng brie,
to sat,
khawm viag vua g khankix.”
27 jkMawh kheu nan,
Siyiex phan yuh kawn pui awm riang pak peung tix.i
Nawh phan yuh kawn pui,
koe simex koe bun.
28 Siyiex nawb buan ka piang kiex mai ah nin:
“Kieh pax pre ka piang haktiex.
Kieh kawn praix pax naog ka piang haktiex,
giex gang ka haktiex.
Aux tag sang kieh pex kuang kax khankix,
mai sim khankix,
khawm to sat khawm uik.
29 Aux tawx nghox koi mai plix ku ceu son sang mawh priex preem son pax.
30 Viang mawh nan,
can to sat mai sim khankix,
aux sang tawx rib riem mai taux lhax son priex ih kix.”
An khawm uik hawt lox pa tawx nawh lih tix mawh.
31 Siyiex jhak ka can pa yuh tix khawm uik mawh pa kied mhawm.
Pon bo hoik luan,
pon ngawb deeh hoek;
an mawh singaix liah heue.
