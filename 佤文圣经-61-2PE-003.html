Simiang Ye-sux deuh gum lox son sang deeh hoek tix
3
Paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
yam in aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka pex ka daux ra pheen.
Ka daux lox lai in ra pheen aux hoik krai pex mhong ka kaing in kix,
son sang so tix kieh rhawm rhi pex pa singax doh keud.
2 Aux tag sang nhie pex doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai cao jhai khankix dix dix,
khawm gum lox pa hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao taoh kawn cau sidiex tix tawx ka pex.
3 aKaing ting mawh nin,
pex tag caw tix tawng,
ka daux singaix bawg yam si dauh,
koe pui tix blah alo nex nham giex gang ka kix,
mai hoek tix lih nyiah ka pex,
4 ah nin:
“Ang nawh bax deuh lox son sang deeh hoek tix lie?
Nawh ot dee mawx lie?
Tax du ex hoik yum,
can pa koe khawm uik ang hoik awm yam jauh tix phan yuh ka haktiex yuh nan lie?”
5 bKix su tix ang simi ka grawng pa hoik teei Siyiex gum lox tix phan yuh ka rao ma tom yam jauh tete.
Haktiex mawh pa kaoh khaing rawm mai pa lih tix mawh khaing rawm;
6 cHaktiex pa prim kawx mawh pa kaoh khaing rawm,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik kheem rawm laux.
7 Rao ma haktiex pa koe yam in kawx,
hoik mawh nang pa priex tix deuh khaing daux gum lox Siyiex,
tag sang krox jun ka singaix yam prawng ca ka pui pa ang koe krax yum rhawm laux leeing ka daux ngaix an,
son sang teei ngu gleeing laux leeing ka heue.
8 dViang mawh nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
koe kaing tix ceu pex ang pon tix piawm yuh nin:
ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
tix rheng num awm tix ngaix,
tix ngaix awm tix rheng num.
9 Krax keud Simiang Siyiex ang doh awm krax keud kawn pui yuh nan,
dha ka bawg yam ang yuh lih ka gum lox pa deuh tix.
Deeh tieh tix,
nawh va rhawm ka pex;
mawh kheu ang nawh rhawm kieh pui tix kaux kawx hoek krax laux leeing,
viang mawh nan,
nawh simie pui loh ka rhawm rhi tix ku kaux heue.
10 fViang mawh nan,
singaix bawg yam sang deeh hoek Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang awm yam lih hoek pui briex dum mawx dum an yuh nan.
Ka daux singaix an,
ceu pa kied ting luah ka daux rao ma tag sang grai,
to kaux rao ma tag sang gleeing ka daux pa kied hax ngu,
haktiex mai can ceu pa koe khawm uik tag sang laux leeinge.
11 Kheu sang laux leeing ceu in kix khawm uik yuh nin,
pex caw tix sang mawh pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Pex caw tix ot ka daux krax pa singax singeh,
ka daux krax pa yung yi,
12 krox jun ka singaix cie Siyiex,
mai sau riang tix nied phai phai ka grawng sang lih hoek.
Ka daux singaix an,
rao ma tag sang laux leeing,
to kaux rao ma tag sang ceh ka daux pa kied hax ngu.
13 gViang mawh nan,
ex kawn krox jun ka rao ma haktiex pa khraox pa hoik deuh Siyiex;
ka dee an pa rauh pa see koe ka jheeng jheeng heue.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
kheu hoik krox jun pex ka singaix an,
pex tag caw sau riang tix leue tix sawg ka ceu pa singax singeh,
kieh tix ot ka daux krax pa ang koe dee ne raw,
pa khaw caw mai nawh.
15 Tag caw tix teei ka krax gun yeung cie Simiang Siyiex kieh mawh ceu pa viex pex hoek krax taoh,
awm hoik teei pux aik pa muh ex Pao-law ping nyia cuyi pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka nawh,
tiiem tix tawx lox lai sidiex gau pex ka yuh nan.
16 Nawh hoik krai kaing in kix ka daux lox lai pa tawx tix khawm uik.
Ka daux lox lai pa tiiem nawh pa nu tix tawng tix blah,
koe pui pa daox aw mai pui pa ang hiex awm paox pui tix yuh nan,
nieb pon kah dee bleeh,
awm hoik nieb kix pon kah dee bleeh phuk lai sigang khri yuh nan,
deeh si dauh kix tang deeh kieh to kaux tix laux leeing heue.
17 Viang mawh nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
pex hoik tawng ka kaing in kix sidawng,
tag caw rien tix,
son ang pui caox khankix sang pon teei lox pa pie lhien kieh pex lud krax lawng tix,
kieh pex singai hu khaing krax pa kaux cong.
18 Pex tag caw kieh tix ot ka daux buan son Simiang Ye-sux Khrit pa mawh cao taoh,
nied kieh tix haok huan ka daux krax pa tawng yung ka nawh.
Kieh min plak ngai mawh cie nawh khawm uik,
jauh yam in tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue!
A-meung.
