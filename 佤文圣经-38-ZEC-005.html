Riang su sai bua lai pa pu
5
Aux baox maong tix jhak,
bawg in aux yaox phuk lai pa pu luan daux rao ma.
2 Kawn cau Siyiex chawk aux ah nin:
“Yaox maix ceu pa tix?”
Aux ah nin:
“Aux yaox phuk lai tix phuk pa pu luan daux rao ma,
been lang koe dim kongjhee,
been vieh koe pon kongjhee plak heue.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh krai ka aux ah nin:
“Ka piang bua lai an tiiem lox pa jhax lawg pa tawx hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
tix plak tiiem mawh nin:
can pui briex pui lak tag sang teei tix tik lih khaing haktiex;
an tix plak tiiem mawh nin:
can pui pa tiex tu lox pa pie lhien kawx tag sang teei tix tik khawm uik.
4 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin,
nawh tag sang tawx lox pa jhax lawg in,
mai kieh lox pa jhax lawg in laig daux nyiex ma pui briex mai ka daux nyiex ma pui pa tiex tu lox pa pie lhien ku kaux.
Lox jhax lawg in tag sang deuh ot daux nyiex ma kix,
mai kieh kix lih tix riem khawm uik heue.”
Riang su sai bun
5 Kawn cau Siyiex baox lih hoek,
ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
tang ceu tix ceu baox lih hoek heue!”
6 Aux chawk ah nin:
“Mawh ceu pa tix?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Mawh khrawng tix mu;
khrawng in mawh son phi mapa haktiex khawm uik heue!”
7 Khrawng in koe dawb pa yuh ka si vun tix mu.
Yam jhak aux kah,
dawb in hoik paoh tix,
jhak heue,
koe bun pa ngawm ka daux tix kaux heue!
8 Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Bun in mawh pa tang son pa caox pa caig.”
Khaix an nawh nyheen bun an laig daux khrawng,
mai tawb ka dawb heue.
9 Khaix an aux maong tix jhak plak piang,
mai yaox ka bun ra kaux pa pu tix hoek plak aux;
bun an kiex koe preeik pa ting riang,
pa awm preeik klang kax.
Bun an kiex kiiep ka khrawng an,
mai pu hu heue.
10 Aux chawk ka kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang viex kiex khrawng hu dee mawx lie?”
11 Nawh ah nin:
“Viex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Kix sang sum nyiex buan dee an son khrawm in tix lhang.
Khaix hoik sum ka nyiex buan in,
khrawng in sang deuh ot ka daux mai kieh pui krub ka heue.”
