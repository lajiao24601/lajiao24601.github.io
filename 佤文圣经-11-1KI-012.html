Ceu pui plak laong tieh vheen
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 10.1–19)
12
Law-paw-ang hu ka See-ciien;
pui Yi-seu-liex khrawm tix dee an khawm uik,
mai ap nawh mawh simiang.
2 Kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang,
kheu mox tix ka simiang Saw-law-meung mai hu ka haktiex Ai-cix;
yam hoik mhong nawh mawh Law-paw-ang simiang,
nawh tom nied deeh hoek khaing haktiex Ai-cixa.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui plak laong ploi pui hu kok nawh hoek,
khaix an hu tau mai nawh hoek ka Law-paw-ang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
4 “Keeing maix Saw-law-meung kieh yix peeh beeh glawm pa cien.
Phan maix tieh ka beeh glawm yix,
mai kieh krax ot krax koe yix nyawm,
yix tag sang hawt khaix maix heue.”
5 Law-paw-ang ah nin:
“Khaix in loe singaix deeh hoek,
aux tag sang pawk ka lox pex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom ing heue.
6 Simiang Law-paw-ang chawk tix yaox rhawm keud kuad song ra loe kaux;
kix hoik sax mawh pui pa teem ka keeing nawh simiang Saw-law-meung khankix.
Nawh chawk kix ah nin:
“Pex keud aux sang khaw tix pawk ka lox pui in kix yuh ka mawx lie?”
7 Kix ah nin:
“Phan maix keud tix sang yuh kaing son kawn pui,
yam pawk maix ka lox kix,
caw tix riex krai lox mhawm,
kix tom tag yuh kaing jhaw maix leeing ju heue.”
8 Viang mawh nan,
Law-paw-ang ang simi ka lox pa krai kuad song in kix,
mai tieh tix hu sawg ka pui awn ju tix blah;
pui awn ju in kix hoik mawh paox pa ting tau mai nawh,
yam in kieh kix mawh cao pa teem tix.
9 Law-paw-ang chawk kix ah nin:
“Pex koe rhawm keud ceu pa tix lie?
Pui an kix khiox khaw aux tieh beeh glawm kix,
aux sang khaw tix pawk lox kix yuh ka mawx lie?”
10 Kix ah nin:
“Maix chix tix krai kix mhong ah nin:
‘Giien taix iak aux kawn lheu ting khaing nyoi keeing aux!
11 Keeing aux tawx beeh glawm pa cien ka pex,
aux tag sang baox tawx beeh glawm pa blawn cien ka pex;
nawh si vied pex ka maox,
aux tag sang lauh pex ka khaox pa koe kat heue!’ ”
12 Khaix an loe singaix,
Yie-law-paw-ang mai can kawn pui hawt ka lox pa hoik krai simiang Law-paw-ang,
mai baox hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
13-14 Simiang ang nghiet ka lox pa krai kuad song khankix,
mai tieh tix hawt ka lox pa krai pui nhum ju,
briah lox pa caox ka kawn pui.
Nawh ah nin:
“Keeing aux tawx beeh glawm pa cien ka pex,
aux tag sang baox tawx beeh glawm pa blawn cien ka pex;
nawh si vied pex ka maox,
aux tag sang lauh pex ka khaox pa koe kat heue!”
15 Simiang ang simi ka lox pa khiox khaw kawn pui;
nawh ang tawng in hoik mawh alo Simiang Siyiex,
son sang lih tix mawh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx nawh ka pui See-law cao jhai Ya-si-ya,
krai ka kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang heue.
16 bKawn pui Yi-seu-liex yaox ang simiang rieb ka lox pa khiox khaw kix,
tom kok yo ah nin:
Ta-wue hoik mawh pa tix ka ex lie?
Kawn simex Yie-si ang koe dee mhawm son ex pie ceeix kawx heue!
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
ex tang ing ka nyiex tix khaing!
Kieh nyiex Ta-wue tang jhak cao tix heue!
Kheu nan,
kawn pui Yi-seu-liex tieh vheen mai tang ing ka nyiex ma tix heue;
17 Law-paw-ang mawh ngie simiang kawn pui ka daux haktiex Yo-tax khaing heue.
18 Simiang Law-paw-ang ploi Ya-to-lang cao kuang pui yuh kaing hu ka pui Yi-seu-liex dee an,
pui Yi-seu-liex teei simaox dheem nawh yum kah.
Yam in,
Law-paw-ang si yaung mai ngawm ka piang ling law,
mai to ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
19 Jauh yam an,
meung pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot plak laong tom tieh vheen khaing meung Ta-wue heue.
20 Pui Yi-seu-liex mhong hoek Yie-law-paw-ang khaing Ai-cix,
tom kok nawh hu kum kawng mai pui hun,
mai ap nawh mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Mawh ngie ceu pui Yo-tax tix ceu khaing pa ca kawn hawt rhawm doeh ceu cad Ta-wue heue.
Lox jhai See-ma-ya pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 11.1–4)
21 Yam hoik hoek Law-paw-ang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tom khrawm ka pui naing pa kied vao ceu pui Yo-tax mai ceu pui Piien-ya-ming kao daix mheen,
mai keud tix sang kieh kix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
son sang deeh pon tix giex gang ka ceu pui pa ot plak laong.
22 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex krai See-ma-ya pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex mhong,
23 nhie nawh hu krai ka Law-paw-ang mai ka ceu pui Yo-tax ceu pui Piien-ya-ming khawm uik ah nin:
24 “Paw vi hu puing ka pux aik pex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pex deeh ing ka nyiex tix heue!
Kaing pa lih tix mawh bawg in,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing aux.”
Kix tom nghiet ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai deeh ing ka nyiex tix khawm uik heue.
Yie-law-paw-ang tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex
25 Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang sum tab ot ka veng See-ciien gawng haktiex Yi-fa-liien,
mai paung ot dee an tix baux bawg yam.
Khaix an,
nawh lih hu khaing veng an,
mai baox sum tab ot ka Pi-nu-yi-leux.
26-27 Nawh ah nin ka to kaux tix:
“Jhak tix yaox awm singaix pa hoik luan,
phan kawn pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex haok ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai yuh krax tan lu ka daux nyiex buan dee an ka Simiang Siyiex,
kix rien tix tieh rhawm tix hawt ka simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Law-paw-ang,
khaix an tag sang hoek pauh ka aux heue.”
28 cNawh keud jee keud blaong,
tom raug yuh riang kawn moi khri ra mu,
khaix an ah nin ka kawn pui:
“Pex haok krub ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung kied taug mao heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
in hoik mawh siyiex pa viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix heue!”
29 Yie-law-paw-ang tom jung riang kawn moi khri an tix mu ot ka Paw-theu-lix,
an tix mu tom jung ot ka Tang.
30 In tom lih tix mawh krax pa kieh kawn pui phit lud,
mawh kheu hu kix yuh krax krub ka Paw-theu-lix mai Tangd.
31 Yie-law-paw-ang baox yuh nyiex krub siyiex khankix ot ka piang gawng,
mai laug cao giex kaing li lih khaing cub nyiex pa ang mawh ceu pui Li-we heue.
Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka pui pa krub ka Paw-theu-lix
32 eYie-law-paw-ang baox tat khix daix kao phuan ngaix mawh singaix poi been li,
awm poi yam ot ka daux Yo-tax yuh nan.
Nawh yuh krax tan ka kawn moi khri pa raug tix ot ka piang khu Paw-theu-lix,
mai baox sum cao giex kaing li ot ka Paw-theu-lix,
son sang kieh kix yuh kaing ka nyiex siyiex pa hoik sum tix.
33 Ka daux khix daix kao phuan ngaix,
pa hoik tat Yie-law-paw-ang,
pa mawh pa tat nawh son poi kawn pui Yi-seu-liex,
son sang hu ka Paw-theu-lix,
mai gleeing nyawng bhawng ka piang khu dee tan heue.
