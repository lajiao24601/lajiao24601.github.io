Siyiex cao pa kia ngien ka ex
91
Can pui pa sawg krax nyawm ot ka cao pa ting siawp sibrawm,
tag sang yaox tix ot ka greem preeik cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik,
2 tag sang ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
maix mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux,
mawh tab ka aux.
Maix mawh Siyiex aux,
aux yum rhawm ka maix.
3 Nawh tag sang taoh aux luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing khawm uik.
Luat luan khaing jhawm saux preng pa teei ju pui yum.
4 Nawh tag sang kia ngien maix ka preeik tix;
maix tag sang nyawm ot ka daux krax pa kia ngien nawh;
pa kaux sijax nawh tag sang kia ngien ka maix.
5 Yam pon sawm,
maix ang yaox tix lhat graung ka daux krax pa khi rhawm,
pon singaix,
maix kawx ang yaox tix lhat graung ka daux pa puing pui;
6 Maix ang yaox tix lhat graung ka jhawm saux pa koe yam pon sawm,
ang yaox tix lhat graung ka krax laux krax leeing pa koe yam jung singaix heue.
 
7 Koe pui kaing rheng kaux pa goh tix yum ka praok plak goix maix,
to kaux yum pui kaing mheen kaux nyat tix ot ka praok plak dawm maix,
krax laux krax leeing kawx ang lih hoek piang maix.
8 Maix tag sang yaox ka ngai tix sidawng,
tag sang yaox ka ceu pa hoek piang pui pa caox pa caig heue.
 
9 Mawh kheu maix hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex mawh nyiex dee mox tix,
hoik kieh cao pa ting siawp sibrawm mawh nyiex dee paung ot tix,
10 Krax laux krax leeing ang pon chuh ka maix,
krax laux krax leeing ang pon dex ka nyiex ma maix.
11 aSiyiex tag sang ploi kawn cau tix hoek tix kia ngien ka maix,
viang maix hu dee mawx,
mai nawh tag sang kia ngien ka maix.
12 bKix tag sang bluh maix ka taix tix,
kieh caong maix ang yaox tix sideeing mat ka piang simaox.
13 cMaix tag sang dig ka sivai sang si mai ka si uing;
maix tag sang dig bhliet ka sivai sang si pa kua mai ka si uing pa koe jai heue.
 
14 Siyiex ah nin:
aux tag sang taoh ka pui pa muh ka tix;
aux tag sang kia ngien ka pui pa krub ka tix.
15 Kix khiox khaw ka aux,
aux tag sang pawk ka lox kix;
kix ot ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
aux tag sang ot tau maix kix.
Aux tag sang taoh ka kix,
mai kieh kix pon min plak ngaix.
16 Aux tag sang kieh kix lang ju lang phan;
aux tag sang taoh ka kix heue.
