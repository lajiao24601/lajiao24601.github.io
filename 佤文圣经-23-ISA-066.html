Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka meung piang haktiex
66
abSimiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Rao ma mawh dang ngeung aux,
haktiex mawh ngot dee deuh aux caong tix.
Maix tag sang sum nyiex buan yuh ka mawx son aux,
mai tawx aux ot nyiex dee laux lee pa yuh ka mawx lie?
2 Aux teei taix tix phan yuh ka rao ma mai can ceu pa koe khawm uik.
Aux kia ngien ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
mai pui pa laux rhawm laux rhi khankix,
mai muh ka pui pa nghiet ka lox aux khankix heue.
3 “Phan mawh pui tang kaux,
kix hoik yuh awm pa simie kix tix yuh.
Kix pauh ka moi kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
phan ang mawh nan teei pui kieh mawh khawng tan,
ka son kix hoik awm paox tix yuh nan.
Kix tan lu ka kawn yung,
phan ang mawh nan pauh ka sox tix mu,
tan lu ka Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
phan ang mawh nan tan lu ka nham lig,
gleeing ka nyawng,
phan ang mawh nan khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka riang rao pa krub,
mai ang lai tang siyox khaing paox tix yuh nan.
Kheu nan kix hoik gaux rhawm teei tix siao lo ka krax krub pa caox pa caig.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang ploi krax laux krax leeing pa kied lhat graung kix hoek ka piang kix.
Kheu aux kok yo,
mai kix ang pawk;
aux krai lox,
kix ang nghiet.
Kix su tix ang nghiet ka aux,
mai yuh kaing ang mhawm pa ang aux khaw rhawm ka heue.”
5 Pui pa yung yi lhat graung ka Simiang Siyiex khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox nawh.
Nawh ah nin:
“Mawh kheu koe krax cu krax nghiet pex ka aux,
ka daux paox ceu paox cad pex koe pui tix blah koeng ka pex,
mai ang hoek tix lai ling ing tiang ka pex.
Kix nyiah pex ah nin:
‘Kieh Simiang Siyiex pex jhi krax pa ting pa lhaong tix lih,
son sang jhak yix tix yaox ka krax pa gaux rhawm pex heue!’
Viang mawh nan,
kix tag sang hoek krax kaig krax tua heue!
6 Nghiet heue,
ka daux veng koe lox pa lawh pa maw;
an mawh lox pa hoek khaing nyiex buan,
mawh lox pa ciyang Simiang Siyiex ka pui pa koeng khankix heue!
7 c“Ka daux veng pa singax singeh aux jiet awm bun miex pa yie keh pa ang yaox tix kham krax pa kha rhawm yuh nan;
8 pex hoik sax yaox kah,
phan ang mawh nan hoik sax mhong ka kaing pa daox deng pa yuh nin lie?
Koe meung haktiex mawx ka daux tix singaix khaing pon lih tix keh lie?
Viang mawh nan,
Shi-ang ang lai siao yaox tix kha rhawm rhi tix tom rheeng,
tag sang pon sum tix kaux cong.
9 Paw vi ce aux kawn ang nyang kieh pui cie tix ang lih keh yam hoik hoek bawg yam keh.”
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin.
10 Pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm tau mai Yie-lu-sa-leung;
can pui pa muh ka veng in tag caw tix gaux rhawm son nawh heue.
Pex pa hoik sax laux rhawm ka son nawh khankix,
yam in tag caw tix gaux rhawm tau mai nawh heue!
11 Pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm ka daux krax pa haok huan nawh,
awm kawn ngie pa peux ka daux deem miex tix yuh nan heue.
12 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh pex haok huan tom rheeng;
sin ngao meung ka piang haktiex tag sang awm rawm klawng pa ang chix hit,
pa jauh tix hoek ka pex dee an.
Pex tag sang awm miex pui tix kaux pa tawx kawn tix peux yuh nan,
mai miex nawh teem nawh ot ka daux deem tix kied koe krax nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi.
13 Aux tag sang tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka pex daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
awm miex pui pa tawx krax nyawm rhawm ka kawn tix yuh nan heue.
14 Yam yaox pex ka grawng pa lih kaing in khawm uik,
pex tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
pex tag sang pon krax pa haok huan brax kua.
Bawg yam an,
pex tom taug tawng aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex teem coi ka pui pa nghiet ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang song rhawm ka pui pa koeng ka aux heue.”
15 Jhak heue,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang viex ngu hoek;
nawh tag sang brug ka piang preeik bhaung klia,
mai hoek tix ciyang ka pui pa cheeik nawh song rhawm.
16 Nawh tag sang teei ngu mai vaig ciyang ka can pui pa yuh phi map khawm uik;
tag sang pauh pui yum tom nie heue.
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui an kix pa yuh been pa yuh been li pui tang ceu khankix,
an kix pa dia tix hu ka pa ah pui mawh ‘si peem tai pa singax singeh’ mai ih ka nex lig,
ih ka nex kiang,
mai an kix pa ih ka nex ceu pa seeing brai khankix,
singaix bawg yam pa si dauh cie kix hoik hoek heue.
18 Aux hoik tawngd ka daux rhawm rhi kix mai ka kaing yuh kix.
Auxe tag sang khrawm ka pui piang meung haktiex.
Yam khrawm kix paox tix,
kix tag sang yaox ka min plak ngai aux,
19 mai tawng ka pui pa ciyang kix mawh aux heue.
“Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang deuh ka pui tix blah,
mai ploi kix hu ka meung ku meung mai haktiex ka dee singai;
haktiex dee an ang nyang sax koe pui tix kaux kawx pa mhong ka ceu kaux aux,
ang nyang sax koe pui tix kaux kawx pa hoik yaox ka min plak ngai aux mai ka siawp sibrawm aux.
Aux ploi pui pa kawn deuk hu ka haktiex Tha-see,
Li-pi-yaxf mai haktiex Lui-ti-yax,
Thu-pa mai Si-lax pa kied ao min ka puing tix te.
Kix tag sang yaog min aux ting lhaong ka meung in kix.
20 Yam deeh hoek,
kix tag sang viex paox ceu paox cad pex hoek khaing meung ku meung,
mai kieh kix mawh khawng phak lak sawng pa tan ka aux.
Pui pa viex kix hoek khawm uik,
brug ka brung,
law,
ka-la-uk,
ngawm ka piang ling law naing,
mai ka piang ling pa tang ka kraung khrai haok ka gawng pa singax singeh aux,
jiet awm viex pui Yi-seu-liex ka tawg klong pa singax singeh,
hu tan lu Krax-tan Nghox-koi hoek ka nyiex buan yuh nan.
21 Aux tag sang ap pui tix blah ka daux kix mawh cao giex kaing li,
mai mawh pui Li-we heue.
22 g“Awm hoik phan yuh aux ka rao ma mai haktiex pa khraox koe leeing ju leeing phan,
kawn praix pex mai ceu kaux pex kawx tag sang toe tix tix ju hoek tix ju.
23 Ka daux pa haok khix tix ngaix ku khix mai ka daux singaix tuah,
kawn pui ku meung haktiex tag caw tix hoek krub aux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao ah nin heue.
24 h“Ka daux bawg yam lih hu kix,
tag sang yaox ka to kaux yum pui pa tieh vheen ka aux khankix.
Graik pa ih ka to kaux yum kix ang chix yum leeing ju;
ngu pa gleeing ka kix ang chix yeet leeing ju.
Pui piang haktiex khawm bee ma hoik seeing ka kix khawm uik heue.”
