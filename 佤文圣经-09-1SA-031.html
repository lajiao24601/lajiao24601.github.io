Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh khankix yum
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 10.1–12)
31
Pui Fe-li-see mai pui Yi-seu-liex puing paox tix ka gawng Ci-li-paw.
Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik kied yum tom nie,
pui pa deuk khankix,
khawm Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh khankix hoik tang to mox tix.
2 Viang mawh nan,
pui Fe-li-see leue ka Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
mai pauh ka Yaw-na-tang,
Ya-pi-na-ta mai Me-ji Su-yax yum.
3 Krax naing ka sivie siveng Sao-law nied ot nied kin,
te puing pui koeng tom krox nawh,
nawh kied nie mat.
4 Nawh tom ah nin ka pui naing cao pa pau tix:
“Leed vaig viex maix,
klauh aux yum!
Son ang pui Fe-li-see pa ang bax Pat-hak,
pa ang koe Siyiex sang yaox tix nu nai ka aux,
klauh aux yum.”
Viang mawh nan,
pui naing cao pau ka nawh kied lhat,
mai ang pon yuh.
Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law tom lawd vaig viex tix,
mai tang klauh tix yum ka piang lawm vaig.
5 Pui cao pau an yaox hoik yum Sao-law,
mai nawh kawx baox gup klauh to kaux yum piang lawm vaig viex tix,
mai yum tau mai Sao-law.
6 Ka daux singaix an,
Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh loe kaux,
mai pui nhum ju cao pa pau an,
mai can pui pa hawt khaix nawh hoik yum khawm uik.
7 Pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka ngawd Yie-see-lie plak an mai toi rhawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mhong grawng pa hoik to pui naing Yi-seu-liex,
mai grawng pa hoik yum Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
kix tom ghram veng mai yaog tix to.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Fe-li-see tom pon cax ka veng kix heue.
8 Khaix hoik puing naing ka daux singaix khaix,
pui Fe-li-see hu peeik tix teei ka kraung ceep pui naing pa hoik pauh kix yum,
mai yaox ka to kaux yum Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh loe kaux noi tix ka gawng Ci-li-paw,
9 kix tom tat ka kaing Sao-law,
mai yah ka kraung naing pa viex nawh.
Kix tom ploi kawn cau hu ka haktiex Fe-li-see ku dauh ku dee,
mai krai grawng kaing pa mhawm in ka riang krub kix mai ka kawn pui.
10 Kix deuh kraung naing Sao-law ot daux nyiex vad bun Ya-see-tha-lux,
mai tawk to kaux yum nawh ka piang guh veng Paw Sang.
11 Pui pa ot daux veng Ya-pi haktiex Ci-liex mhong grawng pa yuh pui Fe-li-see ka Sao-law,
12 can pui pa kua ka naing tom lih hu,
hu ka leeing sawm,
mai hoek ka Paw Sang.
Kix tom teei to kaux yum Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh khankix lih khaing guh veng,
mai viex ing ka Ya-pi,
mai gleeing ka dee an.
13 Khaix an tom si paung si-ang kix ot ka daux veng greem khaox si glai,
mai tuah priex preem aliah singaix heue.
