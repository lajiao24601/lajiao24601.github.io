Na-eu-me teem Lu-teux sawg dee peung
3
Koe tix ngaix,
Na-eu-me ah nin ka Lu-teux:
“Kawn bun eue,
aux tag teem maix sawg tix koe mex tix kaux,
son sang kieh maix koe dee laux tix.
2 Kawn doh keud maix ka Paw-a-see mawh paox nyiex dex ex tix kaux lie?
Maix hoik yuh tau kaing mai bun yuh kaing nawh.
Nghiet lox aux,
daux sawm in nawh tag hoek tix boe nghox ca daux glauh nghox.
3 Maix hu heem khoek tix,
awt ka sidah bhawng,
ceep kraung pa kied mhawm,
khaix an mai hu ka glauh nghox ca.
Viang mawh nan,
paw kieh nawh tawng ot maix dee an,
krox nawh hoik sawm,
hoik nyaux.
4 Jhak sidawng dee it nawh;
khaix hoik it nawh,
maix tom hu,
plak ka man lhop,
it ka praok caong nawh.
Nawh tag chix krai ka kaing yuh maix heue.”
5 Lu-teux pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix ah yuh ka mawx,
aux tag hawt tix yuh yuh ka mawx.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Lu-teux hu dee glauh nghox ca,
hawt tix yuh awm pa ah miex mex nawh.
7 Khaix hoik ih hoik nyaux Paw-a-see,
mai koe krax nyawm rhawm,
tom hoek tix it ka praok pum nghox ca.
Lu-teux koi mox tix hu,
mai plak man lhop,
it ka praok caong nawh.
8 Hoek greung sawm,
Paw-a-see si duad,
veeh to kaux tix,
daox deng ka praok caong tix it ka kawn bun tix kaux,
9 tom chawk:
“Mawh maix mawx?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
aux mawh Lu-teux,
siliex maix.
Maix mawh paox nyiex dex aux,
khaw tix priex ka aux.
Teei tix moeng aux liak!”
10 Paw-a-see ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka maix!
Sivoe in maix nghiet ka miex mex tix,
yam in maix nie koe krax nab yam yung yi ka paox nyiex mex maix pa hoik yum.
Maix chix tix hu sawg ka pui awn ju,
viang nawh koe mau ang koe mau,
maix kawx ang yuh nin.
11 Lu-teux,
paw khi rhawm!
Maix sang cie ceu pa tix ang ah,
aux tag teem maix yuh uik;
pui daux veng hoik tawng maix mawh kawn bun pa kied rien rhawm tix kaux.
12 aAux mawh paox nyiex pa dex mai maix tete,
khaw tix priex ka maix,
viang mawh nan,
koe paox nyiex pa lheu dex khaing aux tix kaux.
13 Ot tin daux bo in,
pon ngawb pa sax,
ex sang jhak nawh cu ang cu tix priex maix ka mawh tix paox nyiex pa dex mai maix.
Phan nawh cu,
an kied mhawm;
phan nawh ang cu,
aux tiex tu ka daux Simiang Siyiex leeing ju:
aux tag yuh kaing in son maix.
Yam in maix it tin,
tom hoek riang praix heue.”
14 Lu-teux tom it ka praok caong nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
praix ang bax riang,
yam ang pui nyang tawng ka nawh,
nawh tom kaoh,
mawh kheu Paw-a-see ang rhawm kieh pui tawng hoik hoek nawh dee in.
15 Paw-a-see ah nin ka nawh:
“Peeik man fua maix,
lhop ka dee in.”
Nawh hawt tix yuh heue.
Paw-a-see tong nghox ca pa lang mu tix nga kongcing ka piang,
teem nawh sau ka piang klip nawh,
nawh tom ing ka veng heue.
16 Yam hoek nawh ka daux nyiex,
miex mex nawh chawk ka nawh:
“Yuh ka mawx?
Kawn bun!”
Lu-teux tom krai miex mex tix mhong kaing pa yuh Paw-a-see ka nawh khawm uik.
17 Lu-teux ah nin:
“Nawh krai aux mhong,
ang pon tix deeh ing ka maix dee in khawm yi taix tix,
mawh kheu nan,
tom tawx nghox ca ka aux niex ding.”
18 Na-eu-me ah nin ka nawh:
“Lu-teux,
maix paw khi,
krox tix jhak grawng sang haok huan kaing in.
Paw-a-see ang hoik yuh kaing in in singaix,
ang chix laux tix heue.”
