Siyiex laug ka Ya-paw-lang
12
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-paw-lang:
“Maix lih hu khaing haktiex ot maix,
paox ceu paox cad maix,
mai ka daux nyiex ma keeing maix,
hoek ka haktiex pa jhi aux maix heue.
2 Aux tag kieh kawn praix maix haoh hun;
kix tag lih mawh meung pa ting pa lhaong.
Aux tag tawx buan ka maix,
kieh maix ting min;
yuh nin,
kawn pui tag pon buan son kheu maix heue.
3 bPui pa nawb buan ka piang maix,
aux tag tawx buan son ka nawh;
pui pa jhax lawg ka maix,
aux tag jhax lawg ka nawh.
Aux sang tawx buan son ka kawn pui ku ceu ka daux maixc.”
4 Yam lih hu Ya-paw-lang khaing Ha-lang,
Ya-paw-lang koe ju atigliah phuan num,
hawt awm hoik tawm Siyiex lox ka nawh,
Law-teux kawx hu tau mai nawh.
5 Ya-paw-lang viex moeng tix Sa-lai,
mai Law-teux kawn pha nawh,
khrawm kraung khrai sin ngao khawm siliex cawng pa pon kix khaing meung Ha-lang,
viex hu ka haktiex Cia-nang.
Kix tom hoek ka haktiex Cia-nang,
6 Ya-paw-lang nied tix hu krax,
mai hoek ka haktiex See-ciien dee singax singeh,
dee dex Maw-lix khaox yang yit.
(Yam an pui Cia-nang kawn ot ka haktiex an.)
7 dSimiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka Ya-paw-lang mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux tag sang tawx haktiex in ka doeh ceu cad maix.”
Ya-paw-lang yuh khu dee tan tix mu ka son Simiang Siyiex pa hoik lih bleeh ka nawh tan.
8 Khaix an,
nawh gah tix hu ka plak caong ma seh,
mai hoek ka gawng veng Paw-theu-lix plak lih singaix,
gawng plak klip singaix toe tix ka Paw-theu-lix,
mai plak lih singaix dex mai veng Ai;
nawh sum nyiex bang ot tan,
mai baox deeh sum khu tix mu,
krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
9 Khaix an,
nawh gah tix hu plak caong ma seh si nyek si nyek,
hoek ka Cia-nang plak seh heue.
Ya-paw-lang hoek ka Ai-cix
10 Sibhawm praix lih koe ka daux haktiex Cia-nang,
kied sidaing kin.
Ya-paw-lang ca nied gah tix hu caong ma seh,
hoek ka Ai-cix,
son sang ot tan tix vud tix cu.
11 Kix hoek ka mee haktiex,
yam dex laig kix ka Ai-cix,
Ya-paw-lang ah nin ka moeng tix Sa-lai:
“Maix mawh bun pa mhawm grang tix kaux,
12 yam yaox pui Ai-cix ka maix,
kix tag ah nin:
‘In mawh moeng nawh’,
mai kix tag pauh aux,
mai kieh maix im ju.
13 eYuh buan son krai mawh maix nan aux,
son sang mawh pa mhawm son aux,
kheu buan son maix aux tag sang yaox tix im ju.”
14 Yam hoek Ya-paw-lang daux Ai-cix,
pui Ai-cix yaox ka kied mhawm grang moeng nawh.
15 Cao kaing cao kap greem simiang yaox ka nawh mai yaog yaw nawh sivoe vawng,
mai kix viex nawh laig ka nyiex haw simiang.
16 Mawh kheu jao nawh,
Vawng tom priex Ya-paw-lang mhawm mhawm,
mai tawx phung yung,
phung moi,
la,
ka-la-uk mai siliex cawng tom nie ka nawh heue.
17 Viang mawh nan,
kheu teei Vawng moeng Ya-paw-lang Sa-lai,
Simiang Siyiex tom ploi krax laux krax leeing,
mai kieh Vawng khawm pui nyiex simiang pon jhawm saux ting pa kied lhat pui kah.
18 Vawng tom kok ka Ya-paw-lang chawk nawh ah nin:
“Grawng pa yuh maix ka aux in mawh pa yuh ka mawx?
Yuh pa tix maix lai ang krai mawh nawh moeng tix ka aux lie?
19 Yuh ka mawx maix lai krai,
mawh nawh nan tix?
Aux tom teei nawh mawh moeng tix jao grawng in.
Yam in deeh teei moeng maix hu.”
20 Vawng tawx lox ka pui plak goix plak dawm tix khankix mai grheu Ya-paw-lang khawm moeng nawh mai can pa koe nawh khawm uik lih khaing tan heue.
