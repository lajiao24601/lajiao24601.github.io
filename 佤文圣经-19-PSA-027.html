Lox rawg buan ka daux pa rhax lai
27
Simiang Siyiex mawh pa riang ka aux,
pa taoh ka aux;
aux kawn sang chix lhat ka pui mawx lie?
Simiang Siyiex mawh cao pa priex pau ka ju aux mhawm;
aux kawn koe pa lhat ka ceu pa tix lie?
2 Pui pa caox puing ka aux,
yam sang yuh pui koeng aux laux,
kix hoik sideeing tix goh khawm uik.
3 Viang pui naing kaing mheen hoek tix lai lawm ka aux,
aux kawx ang lhat ang graung;
viang pui koeng hoek tix puing ka aux,
aux ca kawn yum rhawm ka Siyiex heuea.
 
4 Aux khiox khaw ngie kaing tix ceu ka Simiang Siyiex;
mawh ngie kaing in tix ceu khaing pa mawh krax keud pot jun pot aux:
pa mawh pa ot aux ka daux nyiex ma Simiang Siyiex leeing ju leeing phan,
ka daux nyiex buan yaog yaw ka pa grum mhawm nawh,
sawg krax pa duk thung nawh.
5 Yam si pup aux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
nawh kia ngien ka aux;
nawh kieh aux pon pa nhim ka daux nyiex gox buan man nawh,
mai deuh aux ot ka piang rang simaox pa kaux cong.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang puing pex ka pui koeng pa ot ka vung viang aux.
Aux sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix yuh krax tan krax lu ka daux nyiex buan nawh;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux liak!
Khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux,
cu nghiet ka lox aux liak!
8 Maix ah nin ka aux:
hoek ka aux tin,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang sawg ka maix;
9 khiox khaw maix paw vi mox tix ang simi ka aux liak!
 
Khiox khaw maix paw vi tawx krax pa song rhawm,
mai grheu siliex maix hu;
maix hoik dawg mawh krax teem krax coi ka aux.
Siyiex pa taoh ka aux eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi tik vud ka aux.
10 Viang miex keeing aux tik vud ka aux,
Simiang Siyiex kawn sang chix kia ngien ka aux heue.
 
11 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix jhi krax pa sang khaw aux tix lawng;
pui koeng jiang ka aux,
khiox khaw maix duk thung aux hu daux krax pa si dawm.
12 Kix teei lox pie lhien ah ka aux,
ghlat ka aux;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux cawd ka daux tawm taix kix heue.
 
13 Aux hoik yum rhawm,
yam kawn im ju aux ka piang haktiex,
aux tag sang chix yaox pa grum mhawm Simiang Siyiex.
14 Tag caw yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Tag yuh tix kaux cong ting riang!
Tag caw yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
