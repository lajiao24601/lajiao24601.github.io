Siyiex tawx buan son ka Ya-keux daux haktiex Paw-theu-lix
35
aSiyiex ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Maix hu ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
mai ot tan;
maix yuh khu dee tan tix mu son aux.
Aux mawh Siyiex pa kieh maix yaox tix yam ot maix luat luan khaing tawm taix pux ke tix Yi-sao heue.”
2 Kheu nan,
Ya-keux ah nin ka pui nyiex tix mai can pui pa hu mai nawh khawm uik:
“Tik ka riang siyiex pui tang kaux tang hak khankix khaing daux si nax pex,
kieh to kaux pex singax singeh,
mai loh kraung ceep pa singax tix.
3 Ex lih hu khaing tin,
hu ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
mai yuh khu dee tan tix mu tan son Siyiex.
Yam si pup aux krax pa nu,
Siyiex teem coi ka aux;
yam vung viang aux hu ku dauh ku dee,
nawh ot tau mai aux.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
kix teei tawx riang siyiex pui tang ceu mai kreem pa taug ka yhaok kix ka Ya-keux;
Ya-keux teei mox kraung in kix ka dee dex haktiex See-ciien greem khaox yang yit heue.
5 Yam yaog Ya-keux mai kawn simex nawh khankix tix hu,
Siyiex kieh pui pa ot vung viang baoh veng an yaoh yee tete,
mai ang pon leue ka kix.
6 Ya-keux mai pui nyiex nawh hoek ka veng Lu-see,
pa koe daux haktiex Cia-nang,
yam in kieh dee an kaux Paw-theu-lix.
7 Nawh sum khu dee tan tix mu,
mai kok dee an mawh Yi-leux Paw-theu-lixb,
mawh kheu yam to mox nawh tix khaing pux ke tix,
Siyiex kieh nawh yaox tix dee an.
8 Miex eei Li-pai-cia pa mawh Ti-paw-la yum tan,
mai kix si paung nawh ka Paw-theu-lix greem khaox yang yit plak seh,
kheu nan khaox an tom mawh khaox yang yit “pa yiiem grai heue.”
9 Yam deeh hoek Ya-keux khaing Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax,
Siyiex lih bleeh ka nawh mai nawb buan ka nawh.
10 cSiyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Ceu kaux maix mawh Ya-keux,
viang mawh nan,
khaix in sax ceu kaux maix tag kaux Yi-seu-liex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex loh ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-seu-liex heue.
11 dSiyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm;
aux sang kieh maix pre haoh hun.
Meung ceu pui tom tag lih koe khaing kawn saux maix;
maix tag mawh keeing simiang ceu pui tom nie khankix.
12 Aux sang tawx haktiex pa tawx aux ka Ya-paw-la-hang mai Yi-sha khankix ka maix mai doeh ceu cad maix.”
13 Khaix hoik krai lox in,
Siyiex hu khaing nawh.
14 eYa-keux sum rong simaox tix mu ka dee hoik si kah Siyiex tau mai nawh,
kieh mawh mhai pa doh keud,
mai groi plai mai bee sime ka piang simaox an.
15 Nawh kok ceu kaux dee an mawh Paw-theu-lix heue.
Le-ciiex yum ka nu keh tix
16 Ya-keux mai ceu pui daux nyiex nawh lih hu khaing Paw-theu-lix.
Kix hoek ka Yi-fa-tha kawn koe tix baux krax,
bawg yam sang keh Le-ciiex hoik hoek.
Nawh nu keh,
17 yam kham gee nawh saux sang keh tix,
bun pa khre keh ah nin ka nawh:
“Paw lhat,
baox mawh kawn simex tix kaux.”
18 Yam ang nawh lai pon lih bhawm,
dex sang dut rhawm,
nawh sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Piien Eu-nif;
keeing nawh sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Piien-ya-mingg heue.
19 Le-ciiex yum,
mai si paung nawh praok krax hu kix ka veng Yi-fa-tha;
Yi-fa-tha yam in kaux Paw-li-haung.
20 Ya-keux sum rong simaox ka piang si meeig.
Rong simaox an kawn koe ka si meeig Le-ciiex tom in singaix heue.
21 Ya-keux kawn hu krax hu tix,
sum nyiex gox man plak seh Yi-teu-thax heue.
Kawn simex Ya-keux
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 2.1–2)
22 iYam kawn ot Ya-keux ka haktiex an,
kawn ai nawh Lui-piien it tau mai moeng iak keeing tix Pi-la.
Ya-keux mhong ka grawng in,
kied sidaing meet rhawmh.
Ya-keux koe kawn simex kao ra kaux.
23 Pa keh Li-yax mawh:
kawn ai Ya-keux mawh Lui-piien,
kawn koe Si-miien,
Li-we,
Yo-tax,
Yi-sa-cia,
Si-pu-loeng.
24 Pa keh Le-ciiex mawh:
Yaw-seux,
Piien-ya-ming.
25 Pa keh bun siliex Le-ciiex pa kaux Pi-la mawh:
Tang mai Na-fu-tha-lix.
26 Pa keh bun siliex Li-yax pa kaux Si-phax mawh:
Cia-teux mai Ya-seux.
In kix mawh kawn simex pa keh daux haktiex Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax heue.
Grawng yum Yi-sha
27 jYa-keux hoek ka keeing nawh Yi-sha ka baoh Mang-lix;
Mang-lix mawh Si-paw-loeng,
pa mawh dee ot Ya-paw-la-hang mai Yi-sha sivoe an.
28 Yi-sha koe ju tix yieh tix daix num.
29 Nawh yum ka kied kuad tix;
kawn simex nawh Yi-sao mai Ya-keux si paung nawh heue.
