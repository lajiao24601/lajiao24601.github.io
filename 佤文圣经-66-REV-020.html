Tix rheng num
20
Khaix an,
aux yaox kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux lih hoek khaing rao ma,
daux taix nawh giex ka jiak saw daux dee kied raux ang koe dee die,
mai maox ceng pa ting tix nyaing.
2 aNawh giex ka si yawng an,
pa mawh si uing prim an,
phi caox an,
mai baox kok mawh Sa tan,
phrawk ka tix rheng num.
3 Kawn cau Siyiex tik nawh laig daux pa kied raux pa ang koe dee die,
mai laung dee an,
tawk cum piang,
kieh nawh ang lai baox pon hu tix nyaw glaw ka meung piang haktiex,
tom hoek luan tix rheng num in.
Khaix hoik luan bawg yam pa laung kah,
taug sang ploi nawh tix vud tix cu heue.
4 bAux baox yaox dang ngeung tix blah;
pui pa ngawm piang hoik pon ka akhuang krax prawng ca.
Aux kawx hoik yaox ka khuan ju pui pa pauh pui yum kheu pa hoik krai yum rhawm tix ka krax pa mawh pa meh pa hoik plak jhi Ye-sux mai ka gum lox Siyiex khankix.
Kix ang bax krub ka to sat an mai ka riang to sat an,
ceu kaux kix kawx ang bax koe ka dong re mai ka taix to sat an.
Kix hoik kaoh im heue,
mai yuh simiang tau mai Khrit tix rheng num.
5 (Pui pa deuk pa hoik yum khankix tag sang krox jun tix rheng num in naog tom taug sang deeh kaoh im.)
In mawh pa kaoh im bawg tix.
6 Pui an kix khawm pa pon tix deeh kaoh im ka daux bawg tix hoik mawh pui pa singax singeh pa pon buan son.
Krax yum daux ra bawg tag ang lai koe akhuang tix pon giex gang ka kix;
kix tag sang mawh cao giex kaing li Siyiex mai cao giex kaing li Khrit,
tag sang yuh simiang tau mai Khrit tix rheng num heue.
Pon pex ka Sa tan
7 Khaix hoik luan tix rheng num in,
Sa tan tag sang ploi tix lih khaing daux dee laung kah;
8 cNawh tag sang hu nyaw glaw ka meung pa koe piang haktiex khawm pon daog,
pa mawh Keu-keu mai Ma-keux.
Sa tan tag sang khrawm kix ot tau,
mai hu puing ka naing;
kaux pui kix haoh hun awm mhaik ka praok rawm sigrit sigra.
9 Kix naog ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai lai lawm ka dee bang pui cie Siyiex mai ka veng pa hoik muh nawh.
Viang mawh nan koe ngu lih hoek khaing rao ma,
d mai bleet ka kix.
10 Phi caox pa nyaw glaw ka kix an,
tik ka daux nhawng ngu mhat;
to sat an mai cao jhai pa pie lhien hoik dawg ot dee an.
Ka dee an,
kix tag sang kham krax kha rhawm leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
leeing ju leeing phan ang yeet heue.
Krax prawng ca pa nghoik
11 eKhaix an,
aux yaox dang ngeung paing pa ting tix mu mai ka kaw pa ngawm piang an.
Rao mai mai haktiex hoik mox tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh khawm uik,
mai ang lai baox yaox kah.
12 Aux baox yaox ka pui pa hoik yum,
mawh pui ting min pui iak min ang ah,
hoik cung ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung an khawm uik.
Phuk lai see hoik nih tix khawm uik;
kawn koe phuk lai pa im ju tix pheen hoik nih tix nang.
Pui pa hoik yum tag sang hawt awm kaing pa yuh kix,
mai yuh awm pa hoik tiiem tix deuh ka daux phuk lai see in kix prawng ca kah.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
rawm sigrit sigra teei tix tawx pui yum lih;
krax yum mai meung yum kawx hoik teei tix tawx pui yum pa hoik giex gang kix lih.
Pui in kix hoik hawt tix prawng ca awm kaing pa yuh kix yuh nan.
14 Krax yum mai meung yum kawx tik laig daux nhawng ngu.
(Nhawng ngu in mawh krax yum ka daux ra bawg heue.)
15 Can pui pa ang ceu kaux bax tiiem ot ka daux phuk lai pa im ju in,
tag sang tik laig daux nhawng ngu khawm uik heue.
