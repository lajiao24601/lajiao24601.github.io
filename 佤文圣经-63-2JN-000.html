Lai Yaw-hang Phuk Ra
Krai dawt
Lai Yaw-hang Phuk Ra mawh “kuad song” tix kaux pa tiiem tix tawx ka “bun pa hoik laug kah” mai kawn praix nawh, pa mawh paox grawm khankix ceu cao tix dauh dauh. Kaing ting tiiem tix tawx lox lai in mawh son sang nhie kawn Khrit ra lai muh ka paox tix, khawm pa ne raw kawn Khrit rien tix ka sara cao gau mai ka lox pa krai kix heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1–3)
2. Krax muh pa ting ceu (4–6)
3. Rien tix ka lox pa pie lhien (7–11)
4. Lox pa nghoik tix krai (12–13)
