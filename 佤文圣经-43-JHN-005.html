Pai jhawm saux ka praok rawm dung Pi-see-tax
5
Khaix hoik kaing in,
jiet mawh bawg yam poi pui Yo-thaix,
Ye-sux haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
2 ka dee dex ka Siviex Yung ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung,
koe rawm dung ka tix mu,
lox pui Si-paw-lai kok mawh Pi-see-tax,
ka praok rawm dung,
grah koe ka phuan mu.a
3 Pui hun pa koe jhawm saux it ka piang grah,
ka dee an mawh pui pa dug ngai,
pui pa chawh caong,
pui pa yum taix caong.b
5 Ka dee an koe pui pa koe jhawm saux tix ngoe daix num.
6 Ye-sux yaox it nawh dee an,
mai hoik tawng grawng pa saux nawh tom nie num,
nawh tom chawk pui an ah nin:
“Tag sang simie maix tix moi lie?”
7 Pui pa koe jhawm saux an pawk ah nin:
“Sara,
yam chuh rawm ang koe pui pa teem coi ka aux,
deuh aux laig daux rawm dung,
yam taug sang lih aux,
mai hoik koe pui pa ghrang tix lih sivoe aux heue.”
8 Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Kaoh,
teei kraung it maix ing heue!”
9 Pui an pon tix moi dum mawx dum an,
mai teei kraung it tix ing heue.
Singaix an jiet mawh singaix tuah,
10 cMawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Yo-thaix krai ka pui pa hoik moi an ah nin:
“In hoik mawh singaix tuah,
maix teei kraung it tix hu mawh pa ang khaw heue.”
11 Nawh ah nin:
“Mawh pui pa pai aux moi an pa tawx lox ka aux ah nin:
‘Teei kraung it maix hu heue!’ ”
12 Kix chawk ah nin:
“Pui pa tawx lox nhie maix teei kraung it tix hu mawh pui mawx?”
13 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa pai moi an ang tawng mawh pui mawx;
mawh kheu kied hun pui ka dee an,
kheu nan Ye-sux tom baox mox tix lih hu khaing dee an heue.
14 Khaix an,
Ye-sux sawg nawh ka daux nyiex buan,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Jhawm saux maix hoik moi,
ang pon tix lai baox yuh phi map,
son ang sang kieh krax laux leeing pa ting sang lih hoek heue.”
15 Khaix hoik hu pui an,
nawh krai ka simiang pui Yo-thaix,
mai krai mawh Ye-sux pa pai nawh moi.
16 Jauh yam an,
kix jauh tix tuk tiak ka Ye-sux,
mawh kheu pai nawh pui moi ka daux singaix tuah.
17 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Keeing aux ca yuh kaing ang jud,
aux kawx khaw tix yuh yuh nan nang heue.”
18 Gum lox in kieh simiang pui Yo-thaix but rhawm tix sang pauh ka nawh;
ja lao nawh ang lieh lheu ka been pa deuh ka daux singaix tuah,
kheu nan nawh kawn krai mawh Siyiex keeing tix,
hoik teei to kaux tix sidu mai Siyiex heue.
Akhuang cie kawn simex
19 Khaix an Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai sidawng ka pex,
kawn ang pon yuh ceu pa tid tix khaing to kaux nong nong;
phan nawh yaox yuh Keeing ceu tix,
nawh tom taug yuh ceu an.
Ceu pa yuh keeing,
kawn kawx hoik pon yuh kah.
20 Keeing muh ka kawn,
mai jhi kaing ceu pa yuh tix ka Kawn.
Nawh tag sang jhi kaing ceu pa blawn ting khaing in ka kawn,
tag sang kieh pex daox deng kah.
21 Hoik kieh keeing pui pa hoik yum deeh kaoh im yuh ka mawx,
tawx ju ka kix yuh ka mawx;
kawn kawx tag sang hawt tix yuh awm pa simie nawh tix yuh,
mai tawx ju ka kawn pui yuh nan nang.
22 Keeing cao ang prawng ca ka pui tix kaux kawx;
nawh hoik ap tix tawx akhuang krax pa prawng ca ka kawn,
23 kaing ting mawh son sang kieh pui khawm uik nab yam yung yi ka kawn,
awm nab yam yung yi pui ka keeing yuh nan.
Pa ang nab yam yung yi ka kawn,
hoik mawh pa ang nab yam yung yi ka Keeing kaw pa ploi nawh hoek heue.
24 “Aux krai ka pex tete,
pui pa nghiet ka aux,
mai baox yum rhawm ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek,
tag koe ju leeing ju.
Nawh ang hoek krax prawng krax ca,
mai hoik lih khaing krax yum laig daux ju.
25 Aux krai ka pex tete,
bawg yam tag sang hoek,
yam in hoik mawh heue,
pui pa hoik yum tag sang mhong lox kawn simex Siyiex;
pui pa hoik mhong tag sang kaoh im.
26 Awm hoik mawh Keeing num riah ju,
nawh kawx hoik kieh nang kawn lih mawh num riah ju.
27 Nawh baox tawx akhuang krax pa prawng ca ka kawn,
mawh kheu hoik mawh nawh kawn kawn pui.
28 Pex paw vi daox deng kheu son kaing in;
mawh kheu bawg yam sang hoek,
can pui pa ot ka daux si meeig tag sang mhong ka lox nawh khawm uik;
29 dtag sang lih khaing daux si meeig yuh nin:
pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm,
kaoh im tix pon ka ju.
Pui pa yuh kaing pa caox pa caig,
kaoh im tix pon krax prawng krax ca heue.”
Sakse pa kham son Ye-sux
30 “Aux ang pon yuh ceu pa tid tix ka son tix;
aux hawt tix prawng ca ka daux pa hawt ka alo Siyiex,
krax pa prawng ca aux hoik mawh pa rauh pa see;
mawh kheu ang aux sawg ka ceu pa khaw mai alo tix,
mai yuh ngie ka alo ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an heue.
31 “Phan aux kham sakse to kaux tix,
sakse pa kham aux ang kub tix yum rhawm.
32 Kheu nan,
koe pui tix kaux pa kham sakse son aux,
aux hoik tawng sakse pa kham nawh son aux mawh tete.
33 ePex hoik sax ploi pui hu ka Yaw-hang,
mai hoik mhong ka sakse pa kham nawh pa mawh pa meh.
34 Tete te lai,
aux ang siao lo ka sakse pa kham kawn pui;
aux krai ka gum lox in mawh son sang kie pex pon krax taoh.
35 Yaw-hang hoik awm kawk ngu pa jhan riang tix mu,
pex hoik simie tix ran ot ka daux pa riang nawh.
36 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik chix tix krai lih ka sakse pa blawn ting khaing Yaw-hang,
an hoik mawh kaing yuh aux,
pa mawh kaing pa tawx keeing aux ka aux pa nhie aux yuh hoik.
Kaing in kix hoik mawh sakse pa kham grawng pa hoik ploi keeing aux tix hoek.
37 fKeeing aux pa hoik ploi aux hoek,
nawh kawx hoik kham sakse son aux.
Pex ang sax mhong ka lox nawh,
mai ang sax yaox ka pak ngai nawh,
38 ang sax priex gum lox nawh ot ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
mawh kheu ang pex yum rhawm ka kaw pa hoik ploi nawh hoek an.
39 Pex si kaw tix sawg ka daux phuk lai sigang khri,
kheu hoik een pex tix chix pon ju leeing ju ka daux;
tete te lai,
gum lox phuk lai sigang khri hoik mawh pa kham sakse son aux heue!
40 Kheu nan,
pex ang cu tix hoek sawg ju ka aux dee in heue.
41 “Aux ang doh sawg ka krax pa yaog yaw kawn pui.
42 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng ka daux rhawm pex sidawng,
pex ang koe rhawm pa muh ka Siyiex.
43 Aux hoek ka daux ceu kaux keeing aux,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang rieb ka aux;
viang mawh nan,
koe pui pa hoek ka daux ceu kaux aux,
mai pex hoik tieh tix rieb ka nawh.
44 Pex hoik ra lai simie ka krax pa yung yi,
viang mawh nan,
ang sawg ka krax pa yaog yaw pa kaoh khaing Siyiex pa koe tix kaux khaing;
phan yuh nan,
pex sang chix pon yum rhawm yuh ka mawx lie?
45 Pex paw ce aux sang ne raw tix ka plak sivoe ngai keeing aux;
pa sang ne raw ka pex hoik mawh Maw-si kaw pa hoik dawg simie pex.
46 Phan pex rug hoik yum rhawm ka Maw-si tete,
pex kawx tag sang chix yum rhawm ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik tiiem nawh grawh grawng aux ka daux phuk lai nawh.
47 Mawh kheu ang pex yum rhawm ka gum lox pa hoik tiiem nawh,
mai pex sang chix pon yum rhawm ka gum lox aux yuh ka mawx lie?”
