Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang pai pui chawh moi
3
Koe tix ngaix,
bawg yam rawg buan greung ngaix loe taix nari,
Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang haok ka nyiex buan.
2 Pa kok mawh Me-meung tix dauh,
koe pui chawh tom yam lih khaing daux vaig tix kaux;
pui klien tix deuh nawh ot ka diang siviex ku ngaix,
son sang caung tix ih ka pui pa laig ka nyiex buan.
3 Nawh yaox Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang sang laig ka nyiex buan,
nawh tom khiox khaw kiex caox rhawm ka tix.
4 Kix dong ngai tix ka nawh;
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Maix jhak ka yiex heue!”
5 Nawh tom dong ngai tix jhak ka kiex,
son sang simie tix pon tix ceu ceu khaing kiex.
6 Pi-teux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Mau khri aux ang koe,
viang mawh nan,
aux sang tawx can pa koe tix khawm uik ka maix:
aux tawx lox ka maix ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux Khrit pui Na-sha-leux,
yaog maix kaoh heue!”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-teux bluh ka taix khawn dawm nawh,
yaog nawh kaoh cung.
Caong pui an mai si-ang loek nawh deeh koe riang dum mawx dum an,
8 nawh tom jang loh tix kaoh cung,
cung rauh see,
mai jauh tix hu ka caong tix.
Khaix an nawh laig tau mai kiex ka daux nyiex buan,
jang loh tix mai hu tix,
mai yaog yaw Siyiex.
9 Pui hun yaox yam hu nawh ka caong tix mai yaog yaw ka Siyiex;
10 Yam tawng kix mawh nawh pui caung pa ngawm tix caung ka diang siviex nyiex buan Me-meung,
mai kix kied daox deng ka grawng kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh in heue.
Pi-teux sidiex gum lox buan ka daux nyiex buan
11 Ka greem grah Saw-law-meung,
pui an sau riang tix bluh ka Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang;
pui pa ot dee an hoik daox deng ka tete,
mai to hu jhak ka kiex.
12 Pi-teux yaox ka pui tom nie,
nawh tom ah nin ka kix:
“Paox ceu paox cad Yi-seu-liex khankix eue,
yuh ka mawx lai daox deng ka grawng in lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai ca dong ngai tix jhak ka yiex lie?
Pex ce yiex pon kieh pui in hu ka caong tix kheu riang rao tix,
phan ang mawh nan,
kheu krax yum rhawm tix lie?
13 aSiyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du ex,
hoik yaog min plak ngai siliex tix Ye-sux;
Ye-sux in pex hoik teei tix tawx nawh ka simiang,
Pi-la-to hoik but tix ploi ka nawh,
viang mawh nan pex hoik tieh tix tik vud nawh ka plak sivoe ngai Pi-la-to.
14 bNawh mawh pa singax singeh mai pa rauh pa see,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik tik vud ka nawh,
mai khiox khaw Pi-la-to ploi tix tawx pui caox ka pex.”
15 Yuh nin,
pex hoik pauh ka simiang ju;
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex kieh nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum.
Yix hoik mawh pui pa kham sakse kaing in.
16 Ceu kaux Ye-sux in pa hoik tawx lih ka riang rao,
kieh pa chawh deeh koe riang.
Kaing pa hoik yaox hoik tawng pex ka to kaux pui in,
hoik mawh kheu krax yum rhawm ka ceu kaux nawh.
Mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm nawh ka Ye-sux,
nawh tom taug pon tix moi khawm uik;
pex ku kaux kawx hoik yaox ka khawm uik heue.
17 “Phan mawh nan,
paox grawm khankix,
aux hoik tawng grawng pa yuh pex mai simiang pex ka Ye-sux,
hoik mawh kheu pa daox aw kix.
18 Siyiex hoik dawg kieh cao jhai khankix grox krai sang kham Khrit krax pa kha rhawm;
gum lox in hoik lih tix mawh tete.
19 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai deeh kling tix hoek ka Siyiex,
nawh tom taug pluat ploi ka phi map pex.
20 Yuh nin,
Simiang Siyiex tom taug tawx pex pon krax ot krax koe riang rao khuan pa khraox ka pex;
awm an,
nawh tom taug sang ploi Ye-sux,
pa mawh Khrit pa hoik ciak tix deuh son pex hoek.
21 Khrit tag sang prah nawh ot ka rao ma,
tom hoek loh can ku ceu lih mawh pa khraox khawm uik;
in hoik mawh pa tawx Siyiex cao jhai pa singax cie tix krai pao tom yam jauh dix dix.”
22 d“Maw-si hoik sax krai ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag sang yaog cao jhai tix kaux son pex lih khaing ka daux paox ceu paox cad pex,
awm hoik yaog nawh ka aux yuh nan.
c Phan nawh si kah mai pex,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka khawm uik.
23 ePui pa ang nghiet ka lox cao jhai in,
tag sang teei tix tik khaing ka daux pui cie Siyiex.’
24 Can cao jhai pa sidiex ka gum lox in khankix,
khawm Sa-mu-eu mai pui pa sang plud ka nawh hu sivoe ngai,
hoik krai pao ka kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka daux singaix in kix.
25 fPex hoik mawh pui pa sang plud ka cao jhai;
pex sang plud tix rieb ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh Siyiex tau mai tax du pex.
Siyiex hoik krai ka Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
‘Aux tag sang tawx buan son ka kawn pui piang haktiex khaing ka daux doeh ceu cad maix heue.’
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex yaog siliex cie tix,
mai ran ploi nawh hoek ka pex dee in,
tawx buan ka pex kieh pex ku kaux deeh veeh tix hoek khawm uik,
mai lih hu khaing daux krax pa caox pa caig heue.”
