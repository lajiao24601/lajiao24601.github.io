Lai Ma-thaix
Ru ceu cad nyiex ma Ye-sux Khrit
(Lai Lu-cia 3.23–38)
1
Ru ceu cad nyiex ma Ye-sux tiiem plak greem:
Ye-sux Khrit mawh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
Ta-wue mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
2 Ya-paw-la-hang keh Yi-sha,
Yi-sha keh Ya-keux,
Ya-keux keh Yo-tax mai pux aik nawh khankix.
3 Yo-tax mai Tha-ma keh Fa-leu-see mai Shie-la,
Fa-leu-see keh Si-see-loeng,
Si-see-loeng keh Ya-lang,
4 Ya-lang keh Ya-mi-na-ta,
Ya-mi-na-ta keh Na-suing,
Na-suing keh Sa-meung,
5 Sa-meung mai La-heux keh Paw-a-see,
Paw-a-see mai Lu-teux keh Eu-pe-teux,
Eu-pe-teux keh Yie-si,
6 aYie-si keh simiang Ta-wue heue.
Ta-wue mai moeng Wu-li-yax keh Saw-law-meung,
7 Saw-law-meung keh Law-paw-ang,
Law-paw-ang keh Ya-pi-ya,
Ya-pi-ya keh Ya-sa,
8 Ya-sa keh Yaw-sa-fa,
Yaw-sa-fa keh Yaw-lang,
Yaw-lang keh Wu-si-ya,
9 Wu-si-ya keh Yaw-thang,
Yaw-thang keh Ya-ha-see,
Ya-ha-see keh Si-si-cia,
10 Si-si-cia keh Ma-na-si,
Ma-na-si keh Ya-meung,
Ya-meung keh Yaw-si-yax.
11 Ka daux glang bawg yam pa giex laung ka pui Yi-seu-liex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng,
Yaw-si-yax keh Yaw-ya-cin mai pux aik nawh heue.
12 Khaix hoik giex laung hu ka Pa-pi-loeng,
Yaw-ya-cin keh Sa-la-thiie,
Sa-la-thiie keh Saw-law-pa-paw,
13 Saw-law-pa-paw keh Ya-pi-yi,
Ya-pi-yi keh Yi-li-ya-cing,
Yi-li-ya-cing keh Ya-saw,
14 Ya-saw keh Sa-tu,
Sa-tu keh Ya-cing,
Ya-cing keh Yi-lui,
15 Yi-lui keh Yi-li-ya-sa,
Yi-li-ya-sa keh Ma-tang,
Ma-tang keh Ya-keux,
16 Ya-keux keh Yaw-seux,
pa mawh mex Ma-li-yax.
Pa kok mawh Ye-sux Khrit an mawh pa keh khaing Ma-li-yax heue.
17 Phan sawn tix yaox yuh nin,
jauh ka Ya-paw-la-hang hoek ka Ta-wue khawm uik koe kao pon ju,
jauh ka Ta-wue hoek ka yam giex laung pui Yi-seu-liex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng koe nang kao pon ju,
jauh ka yam giex laung hoek ka yam keh Khrit baox koe kao pon ju heue.
Pa keh Ye-sux Khrit
(Lai Lu-cia 2.1–7)
18 bGrawng pa keh Ye-sux Khrit yuh nin:
miex nawh Ma-li-yax hoik ap tix mex ka Yaw-seux,
viang mawh nan,
sivoe sang yuh ka paox,
Ma-li-yax hoik tawng koe kaux tix kheu Khuan Singax Singeh.
19 Mex nawh Yaw-seux mawh pui pa rauh pa see,
viang mawh nan,
ang rhawm kieh nawh koe krax kaig krax tua ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
mai keud tix sang briex mox tix tik ka nawh.
20 Yam kawn keud nawh ka kaing in,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex jhi nawh yaox tix ka daux ca maox,
mai ah nin:
“Yaw-seux doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
paw vi lhat,
maix teei ka Ma-li-yax mawh moeng maix khaing,
kheu pa nghe nawh mawh pa hoek khaing Khuan Singax Singeh.
21 cNawh tag sang keh kawn simex tix kaux,
maix tag sang sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Ye-sux,
mawh kheu nawh tag sang taoh pui cie tix luat luan khaing phi map heue.”
22 Kaing in pa lih tix mawh khawm uik mawh tag sang kieh gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka cao jhai lih tix mawh ah nin:
23 d“Koe bun krih pa singax tix kaux tag sang nghe kawn simex,
mai ceu kaux nawh tag sang kok mawh Yi-ma-noe-lix.”
(“Yi-ma-noe-lix” dee bleeh mawh “Siyiex ot tau mai ex heue.”)
24 Yam haing Yaw-seux,
nawh hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik krai kawn cau Simiang Siyiex,
teei tix moeng ka Ma-li-yax;
25 eviang mawh nan,
sivoe sang keh ka kawn nyawm ang dex dawt tau mai nawh.
Yam keh kawn nyawm,
Yaw-seux sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Ye-sux heue.
