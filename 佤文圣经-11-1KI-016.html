16
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka cao jhai Yie-hu kawn simex Ha-na-ni,
mai nhie nawh krai lox in plak greem ka Pa-sa:
2 “Maix hoik mawh pui iak pui lied,
viang mawh nan,
aux yaog maix khaing daux phawng tiex,
mai kieh maix mawh simiang pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
Maix khex kawn yuh phi map,
awm Yie-law-paw-ang yuh nan,
mai baox kieh pui cie aux yuh phi map.
Phi map kix cheeik aux song rhawm,
3 mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang yuh maix mai pui nyiex ma maix laux,
awm hoik yuh aux nan ka Yie-law-paw-ang.
4 Pui daux nyiex ma maix,
mawh pui mawx ang ah,
pa yum ka daux veng khankix,
sox tag sang ih ka nex,
pa yum ka daux tiag khankix,
sim tag sang tuh tix ih ka nex heue.”
5 Kaing pa yuh Pa-sa tang ceu khankix mai grawng pa kua nawh,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
6 Yam yum Pa-sa,
kix si paung nawh ka Teu-sa.
Kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yi-la plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
7 Cao jhai Yie-hu krai lox pa ne raw Simiang Siyiex ka Pa-sa mai pui nyiex ma nawh;
mawh kheu Pa-sa yuh ka phi map,
hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm,
ang joeng mawh ngie kheu pa caox pa caig pa yuh nawh awm Yie-law-paw-ang,
hoik mawh nang kheu pa hoik pauh nawh ka pui nyiex ma Yie-law-paw-ang khawm uik heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yi-la
8 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa ka kaing ka daux tix nga liah num,
kawn simex Pa-sa pa kaux Yi-la mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh giex kaing ka Teu-sa ra num.
9 Koe simiang naing tix kaux,
pa bung ka ling law nyiex simiang tix plak pa kaux Sing-lix,
keud sawn tix sang tieh vheen ka nawh.
Koe tix ngaix,
simiang nyaux plai yoek ot ka Teu-sa daux nyiex pa yuh kawn cau pa ting nawh Ya-cax kaing kah;
10 Sing-lix preeng tix laig daux nyiex,
pauh ka Yi-la yum,
mai teei ka simiang mawh nawh.
Kaing in hoik lih tix mawh yam yuh Ya-sa simiang pui Yo-tax ka daux tix nga aliah num heue.
11 Yam yaog Sing-lix tix mawh simiang,
nawh pauh ka pui nyiex ma Pa-sa yum khawm uik,
khawm paox nyiex nawh mai paox grawm nawh,
can pa mawh simex,
nawh hoik pauh yum uik.
12 Kaing in hoik lih tix mawh awm gum lox pa hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex cao jhai Yie-hu krai ka Pa-sa,
Sing-lix pauh ka pui nyiex ma Pa-sa yum uik.
13 Pa-sa mai kawn simex nawh Yi-la krub ka riang,
mai baox kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map,
hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex song rhawm.
14 Kaing yuh Yi-la tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Sing-lix
15 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa ka kaing ka daux tix nga aliah num,
Sing-lix mawh simiang ka Teu-sa aliah singaix.
Yam an,
pui naing Yi-seu-liex kawn lai lawm puing ka veng Ci-pi-toeng pui Fe-li-see.
16 Yam mhong kix tieh vheen Sing-lix ka simiang,
mai klauh simiang yum,
kix tom yaog simiang naing Ang-lix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex dee an.
17 Ang-lix mai pui naing cie nawh lih hu khaing Ci-pi-toeng,
mai hu lai lawm puing ka Teu-sa.
18 Sing-lix yaox hoik laux veng,
nawh tom laig daux veng ka nyiex haw simiang,
gleeing ka nyiex haw,
mai gleeing to kaux tix yum.
19 In hoik mawh kheu jao phi map pa yuh Sing-lix,
dee si dauh krax lawng nawh kheu phit ka Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh hoik awm Yie-law-paw-ang yuh nan,
ang joeng mawh ngie to kaux nawh pa yuh phi map,
mai baox kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map,
hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm.
20 Kaing yuh Sing-lix tang ceu khankix,
khawm krax pa hoik tieh vheen nawh simiang khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ang-lix
21 Pui Yi-seu-liex guah tix lih koe ra cub:
tix cub sang yaog kawn simex Ci-na pa kaux Thi-pi-ni mawh simiang,
an tix cub hawt rhawm Ang-lix.
22 Dee si dauh,
pui pa sang yaog Ang-lix mawh simiang khankix pon pex.
Khaix hoik yum Thi-pi-ni,
Ang-lix mawh simiang.
23 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa ka kaing ka daux tix ngoe tix num,
Ang-lix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh giex ka kaing kao ra num,
sivoe an liah num nawh ot ka Teu-sa.
24 Khaix an,
nawh liag gawng Sa-ma-li-yax ka mau liah rheng plah,
khaing pui pa kaux Sa-ma tix kaux dee an.
Ang-lix sum veng ot ka piang gawng in tix mu,
mai hawt awm ceu kaux Sa-ma pui simiang gawng an sivoe,
sum ceu kaux veng an mawh Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
25 Phi map pa yuh Ang-lix,
kawn lheu ting khaing phi map pa yuh can simiang pa koe sivoe nawh khawm uik,
nawh hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
26 Nawh hoik awm Yie-law-paw-ang yuh nan,
ang joeng mawh ngie to kaux nawh pa yuh phi map,
nawh kawx hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map,
krub riang krub rao,
hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex song rhawm.
27 Kaing yuh Ang-lix tang ceu khankix mai grawng pa mhawm kaing yuh nawh khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
28 Ang-lix yum,
kix si paung nawh ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
Kawn simex nawh Ya-ha plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ya-ha
29 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-sa ka kaing ka daux tix ngoe daix num,
kawn simex Ang-lix pa kaux Ya-ha mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh giex kaing ka Sa-ma-li-yax tix nga ra num.
30 Ya-ha yuh phi map,
hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
phi map pa yuh nawh kawn lheu kin khaing pa yuh simiang pa koe sivoe nawh ku kaux.
31 Nawh hoik yuh phi map awm simiang Yie-law-paw-ang kawn ang bax kub,
mai kawn baox teei tix moeng kawn bun Yie-pa-lix simiang pui Si-toeng pa kaux Yie-si-pix,
mai krub ka Pa-lix.
32 Nawh sum nyiex vad ka Sa-ma-li-yax son Pa-lix tix mu,
mai baox yuh khu tix mu deuh ot ka daux nyiex vad an.
33 Nawh baox jung rong riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la tix gawng mai krub kah.
Phi map pa hoik yuh nawh kawn lheu kin khaing pa yuh can simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa koe sivoe nawh khawm uik,
nawh hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex song rhawm.
34 aYam kawn giex nawh kaing,
pui Paw-theu-lix pa kaux Si-yi-leu,
cox sum ka veng Yie-li-keu.
Awm lox jhai pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kawn simex Noeng pa kaux Yaw-su-yax krai,
yam sum Si-yi-leu ka jaong veng Yie-li-keu,
Ya-pi-lang kawn ai yum;
yam deuh kix nawh ot ka diang siviex veng,
Si-keux kawn nyi yum heue.
