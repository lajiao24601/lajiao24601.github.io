Yaog yaw ka pa khaw caw Siyiex
9
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix;
aux sang krai lao ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh maix khawm uik.
2 Aux sang rhax lai gaux rhawm kheu maix;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux pa ting siawp sibrawm maix heue.
 
3 Yam lih hoek maix,
pui pa koeng ka aux tom deeh ka plak khaix tix;
kix hoik goh tix laux leeing khawm uik.
4 Maix ngawm tix prawng ca pa rauh see ka piang dang ngeung;
maix prawng rauh see son aux,
mai teem ka sibrawm aux heue.
 
5 Maix ne raw ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai yuh laux ka pui pa caox pa caig;
maix hoik preet grai ka ceu kaux kix leeing ju leeing phan.
6 Pui pa koeng ka aux ang lai baox yaox tix koe;
maix hoik yuh ka veng ot kix laux leeing;
pui hoik piawm ka kix khawm uik heue.
 
7 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex giex ka siawp sibrawm simiang leeing ju leeing phan;
nawh yuh dang ngeung son krax prawng ca.
8 Nawh kieh pa khaw caw kuang ka haktiex;
nawh kieh pa khaw caw prawng ka kawn pui haktiex khawm uik heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex mawh nyiex dee mox pui pa hoek krax gut krax kheem,
mawh tab yam si pup ka krax khi rhawm.
10 Simiang Siyiex eue,
pui pa tawng ka maix tag sang een rhawm tix ka maix;
maix paw vi prah ghram ka pui pa sawg ka maix heue.
 
11 Tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex pa kuang ot ka Shi-ang!
Tag sang krai ka kaing pa yuh nawh ka kawn pui haktiex khawm uik!
12 Siyiex muh ka pui pa ot ka daux krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi;
nawh ang piawm ka krax pa rawg kix,
tag sang ciyang ka pui pa kieh kix kha rhawm tix heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux liak!
Jhak pui pa koeng ka aux an yuh ka mawx ka aux!
Simiang Siyiex eue,
taoh aux luat luan khaing krax yum,
14 kieh aux pon cung ka plak sivoe ngai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
krai kix mhong grawng pa yaog yaw aux ka maix khawm uik.
Aux tag sang gaux rhawm kheu krax pa hoik taoh maix ka aux heue.
 
15 Pui tang ceu tang cad hoik cud ka daux o pa kaong to kaux kix,
hoik krix ka rub pa taong cao tix.
16 Simiang Siyiex jhi to kaux lih ka daux krax prawng ca pa khaw caw;
pui pa caox pa caig hoik goh ka phi map cao tix heue.
 
17 Can pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig,
pa tieb tie ka pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
hoik pon tix laig daux meung yum khawm uik.
18 Pui hot pui sibhawm ang yaox tix piawm ka kix leeing ju;
krax keud pot jun pot pui hot pui sibhawm ang yaox tix rheeng kha rhawm tix heue.
 
19 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kaoh heue,
paw kieh kawn pui haktiex map nai ka maix!
Kok pui tang ceu tang cad hoek ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
kieh kix rieb tix kham ka krax prawng ca.
20 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kieh kix lhat graung ka maix;
kieh kix tawng mawh to kaux tix pui pa chix yum heue.
