Tieb tie ka pui Fe-li-see
13
a
2 Sao-law laug pui loe rheng lih khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai deuh kix ot ka praok tix ra rheng kaux,
ka daux haktiex Mi-mawx mai ka gawng Paw-theu-lix,
teei pui an kix lih tix rheng kaux,
mai ploi hu ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming haktiex Ci-pi-yax,
mai kieh mawh kawn simex tix Yaw-na-tang mawh cao pa thung kix hu,
pui pa deuk khankix deeh nhie kix ing ka nyiex heue.
3 Yaw-na-tang pauh ka simiang naing pui Fe-li-seeb yum ka Cia-pa;
pui Fe-li-see hoik mhong ka kaing in.
Sao-law ploi pui cau tix tauh hu ka daux meung ku dauh,
mai phlu teet tawng,
khrawm ka pui Si-paw-lai lih hu puing naing.
4 Can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik mhong grawng pa hoik pauh Sao-law ka simiang naing pui Fe-li-see,
mai mhong nang grawng pa koeng pui Fe-li-see ka kix,
tom hawt ka lox pa kok Sao-law,
kix tom khrawm paox tix hoek ka daux haktiex Cie-cia heue.
5 Pui Fe-li-see khrawm paox tix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kix koe ling law naing loe mheen lhang,
pui naing brug brung liah rheng kaux,
mai pui naing pa hu ka caong tix pa ang uik simin,
hoik haoh hun awm mu mhaik ka praok rawm ting yuh nan.
Kix tawx pui naing hu ka Mi-mawx plak lih singaix Paw Ya-weung,
sum bang dee an.
6 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tawng grawng sang hoek kix krax laux krax leeing kheu pa kied ting riang pui koeng,
koe pui tix blah tom mox tix ot ka daux rang,
ka daux nong khaox,
ka daux dawh rang,
ka daux dung rawm,
mai ka daux o khankix.
7 Koe pui tix blah tiang ka rawm klawng ting Yaw-tang,
mai to hoek mox tix ka haktiex Cia-teux mai haktiex Ci-liex heue.
Sao-law kawn ot ka Cie-cia,
pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix hoik lhat graung mai si yaung.
8 cNawh hawt awm lox pa hoik krai Sa-mu-eu,
mai krox jun ka Cie-cia aliah singaix,
viang mawh nan,
Sa-mu-eu ca kawn ang bax hoek.
Kawn pui hu khaing Sao-law,
9 kheu nan,
Sao-law ah nin ka kix:
“Viex khawng tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim hoek ka aux tin!”
Nawh tom tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
10 Yam jiet hoik yuh nawh krax tan,
Sa-mu-eu tom bleeh.
Sao-law lih tix rieb ka nawh,
11 viang mawh nan,
Sa-mu-eu ne raw ka nawh ah nin:
“Yuh maix ceu pa tix lie?”
Sao-law ah nin:
“Kawn pui hu khaing aux,
maix kawx ang hoek bawg yam pa hoik tat tix deuh,
kheu pui Fe-li-see hoik khrawm paox tix ot ka Mi-mawx.
12 Aux keud tix yaox,
pui Fe-li-see sang puing aux ka haktiex Cie-cia,
ex ang nyang bax pon krax teem krax coi Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan to kaux aux ang tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ang chix heue.”
13 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Maix hoik yuh ka kaing pa daox aw heue!
Maix ang bax priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix.
Phan maix hoik priex tix hawt,
mai nawh tag sang kieh maix mai doeh ceu cad maix pon kuang ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju.
14 dViang mawh nan,
yam in,
singaix pa giex maix cao kaing ang yaox tix rheeng,
mawh kheu ang maix nghiet ka lox nawh.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang sawg pui pa khaw ka rhawm nawh,
mai kieh nawh mawh simiang ka pui cie nawh khankix heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu lih hu khaing Cie-cia,
mai ca nied tix hu ka krax hu tix.
Pui hun pa deuk khankix hawt khaix Sao-law,
mai hu rom tix tau mai pui naing cie nawh.
Kix jauh tix kaoh khaing Cie-ciae hoek ka Ci-pi-yax haktiex Piien-ya-ming.
Sao-law simin kaux pui naing cie tix,
mai koe been liah yieh kaux.
16 Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh Yaw-na-tang khawm pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix sum bang ot ka Cia-pa haktiex Piien-ya-ming;
pui Fe-li-see sum bang cie kix ot ka Mi-mawx.
17 Pui naing Fe-li-see guah tix lih koe loe cub khaing bang,
tix cub hu plak Eu-fu-la haktiex Su-yax,
18 tix cub hu plak Paw Heu-loeng,
an tix cub hu plak dee jhak daux ngawd Si-paw-ying mai si nax gawng daux tiag daux tu heue.
19 Yam an,
ka daux haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex ang koe cang mai yuh rhiem,
mawh kheu ang pui Fe-li-see kieh pui Si-paw-lai yuh vaig gawn. (
20 Pui Yi-seu-liex tag caw tix sawg pui Fe-li-see klaing ka to thai,
khaw,
moe,
si vawgf.
21 Teei ngawh klaing moe mai ngawh pa cox rhang khaox nguh tix plah mau tawng;
pa klaing ka to thai mai khaw tag sang mawh ra plah mau tawng.)
22 Mawh kheu nan,
yam puing ka naing,
lheu khaing Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh Yaw-na-tang,
pui naing Yi-seu-liex ang koe vaig gawn pie tix kaux kawx heue.
23 Pui Fe-li-see ploi pui naing tix cub hu kuan ka diang moing gawng Mi-mawx heue.
