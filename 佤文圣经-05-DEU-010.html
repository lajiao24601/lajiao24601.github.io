Maw-si rieb gum lox ping nyap pa khraox
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 34.1–10)
10
“Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
‘Maix saik khlawp simaox ra khlawp,
awm khlawp sivoe yuh nan,
mai baox yuh teug khaox tix mu pa sau khlawp simaox ot ka piang.
Maix haok ka piang gawng,
mai hoek ka aux dee in.
2 Aux sang tiiem gum lox sijax mukheu pa koe ka piang khlawp simaox pa hoik tik maix max an,
deeh tiiem ot ka khlawp in ra khlawp.
Maix caw tix deuh khlawp simaox in ot mhawm ka daux teug khaox heue.’
3 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom yuh teug tix mu ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai saik khlawp simaox ra khlawp,
awm sivoe an ra khlawp yuh nan,
mai aux sang viex khlawp simaox haok ka piang gawng.
4 Yam khrawm pex paox tix ka piang gawng sivoe an,
Simiang Siyiex krai pao tix tawx gum lox ping nyap kao gum ka pex daux rao ngu.
Nawh deeh baox tiiem gum lox ping nyap in ot ka khlawp simaox in ra khlawp,
khaix an ap tix tawx ka aux.
5 Aux deeh veeh tix lih khaing gawng,
mai hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex,
teei tix deuh khlawp simaox in ra khlawp ot ka daux teug khaox pa hoik yuh aux an,
tom yam in kawn deuh ot tan heue.”
6 aPui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih hu khaing rawm maw pui Ya-kang tan,
mai hoek ka Maw-si-la.
Ya-loeng yum tan,
mai kix si paung nawh tan.
Kawn simex nawh Yi-li-ya-sa dee plud tix yuh kaing cao giex kaing li tang nawh.
7 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing tan,
mai hoek ka haktiex Ku-keu-tax,
mai kix deeh veeh tix hoek ka haktiex Yaw-pa-tha;
dee an hoik mawh dee haoh rawm klawng.
8 cYam ot ka haktiex gawng Heu-liexb,
Simiang Siyiex tawx ceu pui Li-we mawh cao pa klawm teug sijax mukheu tix,
mawh cao giex kaing li yuh ka kaing nawh,
mai pa nawb buan son ka daux ceu kaux nawh.
Tom singaix,
ceu kawn praix Li-we ca kawn mawh cao pa yuh kaing jhaw in.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Li-we tag ang awm ceu pui tang ceu khankix koe nyum haktiex cie;
kix hoik hawt awm lox pa deuh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai pon ka buan son pa hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex mawh sin ngao ka kix heue.
10 d“Awm sivoe yuh nan,
aux kaog ka piang gawng tipon singaix tipon sawm;
Simiang Siyiex deeh baox nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg aux,
ang lai yuh pex laux leeing.
11 Nawh krai ka aux,
mai nhie aux thung pex hu,
son sang pon laig pex cax tix cie haktiex an pa hoik ap nawh tix tawx ka tax du pex khankix heue.”
Pa nhie Siyiex ex yuh
12 “Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix eue,
bre rhawm pex nghiet grawng pa nhie Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex pex yuh,
pex lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap nawh khawm uik.
Pex caw tix muh nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai yuh kaing son nawh,
13 mai priex tix hawt lox tat jhawm prawng nawh khawm uik.
Son sang pon pex buan son ceu pa mhawm,
ka daux singaix,
aux hoik krai lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex ka pex.
14 Meung rao ma mai ka piang rao ma pa kied lhaong mawh cie Simiang Siyiex;
haktiex mai khawm pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik kawx mawh cie nawh.
15 Simiang Siyiex muh ka tax du pex,
nawh tom laug tix teei ka pex,
mai ang laug tix teei ka pui tang ceu khankix mawh kawn praix cie tix.
Tom hoek singaix,
mai pex ca kawn mawh kawn praix pa hoik laug nawh.
16 “Mawh kheu nan,
khaix in sax,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang pon tix baox deeh kiien rhawm kiien rhi heue.
17 eSimiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
hoik mawh Siyiex piang Siyiex,
mai simiang piang simiang.
Nawh mawh Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong pa khaw pui tix lhat graung yung yi kah.
Nawh ang jhak pak ngai pui,
mai ang teei ka khawng phak pa chun praok khankix.
18 Nawh priex rien mai prawng rauh see son miex mai kawn doe ka daux pa rauh pa see,
mai muh ka pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka daux pex,
mai tawx kix ih nyaux ceep kraung khrai.
19 Mawh kheu nan,
pex teei krax pa caox rhawm tix ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
mawh kheu pex kawx hoik mawh nang pui tang ceu tang cad ka daux Ai-cix.
20 “Pex tag caw tix koe krax lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai yuh kaing jhaw nawh.
Pex tag caw tix hawt nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai yuh ngie krax tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux nawh khaing.
21 Pex tag caw tix yuh krax pa yaog yaw nawh,
mawh kheu mawh nawh Siyiex pex;
pex hoik yaox kaing ceu pa ting pa lhaong,
mai ceu pa daox pa deng pa yuh nawh son pex khankix.
22 fYam hoek tax du pex khankix ka daux Ai-cix,
mawh ngie pui atigliah kaux khaing,
yam in,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
hoik kieh kaux pui pex baox thiem thie tix haoh hun awm simuing ka daux rao ma khankix heue.”
