Ye-sux kok ka pui gau tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 4.18–22; Lai Ma-kheu 1.16–20)
5
aKoe tix bawg,
Ye-sux cung ka praok rawm nhawng Keu-ni-sa-leux,
pui hun preeng tix hoek,
son sang nghiet ka gum lox Siyiex pa krai nawh.
2 Nawh yaox reu ra mu pa kaog ot ka praok rawm nhawng,
pui rub kax lih hu khaing reu,
mai si ghrah rub ka praok rhawm.
3 Ye-sux laig ka daux reu cie Si-meung,
mai tawx lox nhie Si-meung vai reu yoi tix hu ceeix.
Ye-sux ngawm daux reu,
mai sidiex ka pui hun heue.
4 Khaix hoik krai nawh lox,
nawh krai ka Si-meung ah nin:
“Vai reu hu dee raux rawm,
khaix an maix mai paox maix rug ka kax heue.”
5 bSi-meung ah nin:
“Sara,
yix hoik taug mao leeing lang sawm,
mai ang pon tid tix;
kheu hoik krai maix yuh nin,
aux tag sang rub ka kax heue!”
6 cMawh kheu nan,
kix rub ka kax,
mai hoik pon ka kax tom nie,
rub dex sang griah.
7 Kix tom yaog taix tix ka paox tix,
mai kok paox kix pa ot ka daux reu tang mu hoek teem ka kix.
Kix hoek,
mai sau kax naog ka daux reu ra mu,
reu hoik dex sang com.
8 Si-meung Pi-teux yaox ka grawng kaing in,
nawh tom grux gawng tix plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix lih hu khaing aux,
kheu mawh aux pui phi map heue!”
9 Nawh mai paox nawh tang kaux kied daox deng ka kax pa pon tix tom nie.
10 Paox nawh Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang kawn simex Si-pi-thaix kawx hoik daox deng kah.
Ye-sux krai ka Si-meung ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat,
jauh yam in maix tag sang pon ka pui heue.”
11 Kix kieh reu hoek dex ka praok rawm,
mai prah ka can pa koe tix khawm uik,
hawt khaix Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux pai pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.1–4; Lai Ma-kheu 1.40–45)
12 Koe tix bawg,
Ye-sux ot ka daux veng,
dee an koe pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg ka to kaux khawm uik tix kaux.
Nawh yaox ka Ye-sux,
mai grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
khiox khaw nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ca mawh cu maix kah,
maix pon kieh aux singax heue!”
13 Ye-sux tawx taix tix giex ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux cu,
kieh maix singax heue!”
Jhawm saux si pawg pa koe ka to kaux nawh tom grai dum mawx dum an.
14 dYe-sux tawx lox ka nawh ah nin:
“Paw vi krai kaing in ka pui tix kaux kawx,
hu ka cao giex kaing li,
kieh nawh lam lia ka maix.
Khaix an hawt tix tan lu awm been pa hoik tat Maw-si tix deuh yuh nan,
mai jhi grawng pa hoik singax maix ka pui hun khankix heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu kaux Ye-sux nied tix tauh hu.
Cub pui hun khrawm paox tix,
son sang nghiet ka pa sidiex nawh,
mai baox simie jhawm saux kix pon moi nang.
16 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux deeh ing rawg buan ka dee yien yi heue.
Ye-sux pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux yum taix caong moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.1–8; Lai Ma-kheu 2.1–12)
17 Koe tix ngaix,
yam sidiex gau Ye-sux ka pui,
koe pui Fa-li-saix mai sara lox tat jhawm prawng kawx pa kaoh khaing veng iak ka haktiex Cia-li-lix khankix,
mai ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung haktiex Yo-thaix ngawm ka praok nawh.
Siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai Ye-sux,
mai kieh nawh pon pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux moi.
18 Koe pui ra loe kaux grawng ka pui pa yum taix caong pa it piang ku tix kaux hoek,
mai keud tix sang grawng laig ka daux nyiex,
deuh ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux.
19 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu hun pui,
kix ang pon grawng nawh laig,
tom grawng nawh haok piang braux nyiex,
yieh ka khlawp beung,
ploi pui an khawm ku lih daux nyiex,
mai deuh nawh ot ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux ka daux pui hun.
20 Ye-sux yaox krax yum rhawm kix,
tom ah nin ka pui pa yum taix caong an:
“Paox grawm,
phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi heue.”
21 Sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai pui Fa-li-saix salao ah nin:
“Pui in mawh mawx lie?
Nawh lai krai lox pa cau nyhau yuh nin heue!
Lheu khaing Siyiex,
pui mawx koe akhuang pa pluat ploi phi map lie?”
22 Ye-sux hoik tawng krax pa keud kix,
tom ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai keud pa yuh nin lie?
23 Ah nin ka pui pa koe jhawm saux ‘phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi’ yie lie?
Mawh ah ‘Kaoh hu’ yie lie?
24 Aux tag sang kieh pex tawng,
kawn kawn pui koe akhuang tix pluat ploi phi map ka piang haktiex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka pui pa yum taix caong an:
“Aux tawx lox ka maix,
kaoh,
teei ka ku it maix,
ing ka nyiex heue!”
25 Pui an tom kaoh cung ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun dum mawx dum an,
teei ka ku it tix ing,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex.
26 Pui hun kied daox deng kah,
mai rhawm rhi kix hoik naog ka krax pa lhat graung yung yi rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex ah nin:
“Ka daux singaix in ex hoik yaox ka kaing ceu pa ang tix sax yaox ka heue!”
Ye-sux kok ka Li-we
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.9–13; Lai Ma-kheu 2.13–17)
27 Khaix hoik luan khaing in,
Ye-sux lih hu,
yaox pui pa koh keen tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Li-we,
ngawm ka piang dee koh keen.
Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Hawt khaix aux heue!”
28 Li-we tom yaog tix kaoh,
mai prah ka can pa koe tix khawm uik,
hawt khaix Ye-sux heue.
29 Li-we yuh poi ting son Ye-sux ka daux nyiex tix;
pui koh keen tom nie mai pui tang kaux khankix hoek tix yuh poi in khawm uik.
30 eKoe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah mai pa mawh cub caw sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix salao ka pui gau Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai sawm nyaux tau mai pui koh keen mai pui caox lie?”
31 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pui pa brax kua ang lo ka cao sidah,
mawh pui pa koe jhawm saux pa lo kah.
32 Kaing ting aux hoek ang mawh son sang kok ka pui mhawm,
mai mawh son sang kok ka pui caox loh rhawm rhi tix heue.”
Grawng tuah ka priex preem
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.14–17; Lai Ma-kheu 2.18–22)
33 Koe pui ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Pui gau Yaw-hang rien rawg buan tuah ka priex preem jheeng jheeng,
pui gau pui Fa-li-saix kawx hoik yuh nan nang,
mawh ngie pui gau maix pa ih nang nyaux nang khaing heue.”
34 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yam kawn ot kaw pa yuh ka paox ka bang poi ka paox,
maix pon chix kieh nyo pa hoek tix yuh krax gaux rhawm tuah ka priex preem lie?
In ang doh chix heue!
35 Viang mawh nan,
hoek singaix bawg yam,
pui tag sang viex kaw pa yuh ka paox hu khaing kix,
yam an kix taug sang tuah ka priex preem heue.”
36 Ye-sux baox krai gum lox phek tiiem ka kix ah nin:
“Ang koe pui pa riah ka kraung ceep pa khraox,
mai teei man pa riah an blaox ka piang kraung ceep pa prim;
phan yuh nin,
pa khraox an tag grah,
mai man khraox pa riah lih an ang doh chix khaw tau mai pa prim.
37 Ang koe nang pui pa teei plai pa khraox sau ka daux hau hak pa prim;
phan yuh nin,
plai pa khraox an tag sang kieh hau hak pa prim bih,
plai tag sang roik,
hau hak kawx hoik laux heue.
38 Plai khraox tag caw tix saux ot ka daux hau hak pa khraox.
39 Ang koe pui pa nyaux ka plai pa prim,
mai baox keud tix sang nyaux ka plai pa khraox,
nawh hoik ca krai nyawm plai pa prim an heue.”
