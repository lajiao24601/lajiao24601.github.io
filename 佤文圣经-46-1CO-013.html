Krai lao grawng krax muh
13
Viang aux hoik chix si kah ka lox kawn pui tom nie ceu,
pie lox kawn cau Siyiex kawx hoik chix si kah kah,
phan ang koe krax muh,
lox pa krai aux tag sang lawh awm lox luah bra mai lox luah chiang yuh nan.
2 aViang aux hoik koe riang rao tix sidiex kawx,
mai hoik pon tawng ka ngai lai ku ceu,
pa raux pa mox ku ceu,
pie krax yum rhawm pa ting pa pon gah gawng laig daux rawm kawx hoik koe,
phan ang koe krax muh,
mai ang sawn mawh pa tid tix.
3 Viang aux hoik tawx sin ngao pa koe tix khawm uik ka pui kawx,
pie to kaux tix kawx hoik tawx pui gleeing kah,b
phan ang koe krax muh,
can ceu pa hoik yuh aux khawm uik ca kawn mawh pa ang koe buan son heue.
4 Krax muh mawh pa pon gun yeung mai krax pa caox rhawm.
Phan koe krax muh tag ang boh rhawm,
ang ting mu lox,
ang ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
5 Ang nieb pon yuh kaing pa ang khaw,
ang keud son tix nong nong,
ang song rhawm yie yie,
ang tiiem tix deuh ka krax pa lud loe pui,
6 ang muh ka ceu pa ang rauh ang see,
mai muh ka ceu pa mawh pa meh khaing.
7 Krax muh pon lang rhawm ka ku ceu khawm uik,
ka son krax yum rhawm khawm uik,
ka son krax keud pot jun pot khawm uik,
hoik pon gun yeung ka khawm uik heue.
8 Krax muh mawh leeing ju leeing phan.
Pa koe riang tix sidiex mawh tix vud tix cu khaing;
khawng phak buan son pa chix si kah ka lox daux khuan,
tix ngaix sax kawn sang chix uik;
ngai lai kawx taug sang chix luan hu.
9 Mawh kheu ngai lai pa chix ex chix deek chix tie,
khawng phak buan son pa sidiex kawx chix deek chix die.
10 Viang mawh nan,
yam lih hoek ceu pa grum naog,
ceu pa chix deek chix tie tag sang grai hu heue.
11 Yam mawh aux kawn nyawm,
aux krai lox awm kawn nyawm,
rhawm muh aux awm kawn nyawm,
krax keud krax sawn aux kawx awm kawn nyawm.
Yam in aux hoik huan lih mawh pui ting,
kaing ceu pa dex mai kawn nyawm aux hoik tik ka khawm uik.
12 Yam in ceu pa chix yaox ex mawh riang pang peue aux ka daux si liag,
ang chix yaox sidawng,
ka daux singaix tag sang hoek,
aux tag sang yaox ka sidawng plak ngai tawx plak ngai.
Yam in krax pa tawng yung aux ka Siyiex ang bax grum naog,
singaix pa sang hoek taug sang grum naog,
awm hoik grum tawng yung Siyiex ka aux yuh nan heue.
13 Kheu nan,
krax yum rhawm,
krax keud pot jun pot,
mai krax muh loe ceu in kix mawh pa koe leeing ju leeing phan heue,
mai ka daux kix ceu pa blawn ting mawh krax muh heue.
