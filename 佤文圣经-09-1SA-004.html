4
Gum lox krai Sa-mu-eu hoik tauh hu ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Pui tud rhim ka teug sijax mukheu
Bawg yam an, pui Fe-li-see hoek tix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liexa, pui Yi-seu-liex lih rieb tix puing ka naing. Pui Yi-seu-liex sum nyiex bang ot ka Yi-piien-yi-shiex; pui Fe-li-see sum nyiex bang ot ka Ya-fu.
2 Pui Fe-li-see hoek tix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
khaix hoik puing kix ka paox tix,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang pex,
pui Fe-li-see pauh kix yum been dex pon rheng kaux.
3 Yam deeh hoek pui naing pa mat ka dee bang kix,
kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Ka daux in singaix,
Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx lai kieh pui Fe-li-see pex ka ex lie?
Ex hu ka haktiex See-law,
mai teei ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex heue!
Yuh nin,
nawh tom tag hu tau mai ex,
mai pon taoh ex khaing daux tawm taix pui koeng.”
4 bMawh kheu nan,
kix tom ploi pui hu ka See-law,
mai grawng Ci-lu-paw pa mawh teug sijax mukheu cie Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing pa kieh kix ngawm ka piang si nax Ci-lu-paw ra mu hoek.
Kawn simex Yi-lix ra kaux Heu-fu-ni mai Fe-ni-ha hoek tau mai teug sijax mukheu heue.
5 Yam hoik viex kix teug sijax mukheu hoek daux bang naing,
pui Yi-seu-liex gaux rhawm ah lox pa ting pa si num,
pie haktiex kawx hoik yung yang.
6 Pui Fe-li-see mhong lox pa kok yo pui,
tom ah nin:
“Hoik mhong ka lox yo pa kaoh khaing bang pui Si-paw-lai!
Mawh grawh grawng ceu pa tix lie?”
Yam hoik tawng kix mawh teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa hoik hoek ka daux bang pui Si-paw-lai,
7 mai kix kied lhat graung,
ah nin:
“Mawh siyiex pa hoik hoek ka daux bang kix heue.
Ex hoik phaox heue!
Ex ang sax si pup ka kaing pa yuh nin!
8 Mawx pa pon taoh ex luat luan khaing siyiex pa ting pa lhaong an kix eue!
Pa pauh pui Ai-cix ka daux tiag,
hoik mawh siyiex an kix heue!
9 Pui Fe-li-see eue,
tag yuh tix ting riang!
Yuh tix mawh simex mhaing.
Phan ang mawh nan,
ex tag sang yaox tix yuh siliex cawng pui Si-paw-lai,
awm hoik sax yuh kix siliex cawng ex singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan.
Mawh kheu nan,
kieh ex mawh kawn simex mhaing,
viex rhawm kua tix puing ka naing heue!”
10 Pui Fe-li-see sau riang tix puing naing.
Pui Yi-seu-liex ang pex,
mai tang yaog tix to ing ka nyiex tix.
Pui naing pa yum khankix kied nie tete,
pui Yi-seu-liex koe loe mheen kaux pa yum.
11 Teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex hoik tud tix hu;
kawn simex Yi-lix khawm ra kaux,
pa mawh Heu-fu-ni mai Fe-ni-ha kawx hoik yum heue.
Grawng yum Yi-lix
12 Ngai an,
koe ceu pui Piien-ya-ming kaoh khaing daux naing,
mai to tom hoek ka haktiex See-law.
Nawh riah ka sibex tix,
mai groi nyaox piang kaing tix,
son pa jhi ka krax laux rhawm laux rhi tix.
13 Yi-lix kied keud ka teug sijax mukheu Siyiex,
mai ngawm ka jhak praok krax.
Pui an krai lox ka daux veng,
pui ku kaux lhat graung yiiem grai khawm uik.
14 Yi-lix mhong lox pa yiiem pui,
nawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx lai lih koe lox yiiem?”
Pui an kawx hoek tix krai lox an ka plak sivoe Yi-lix.
15 (Yam an,
Yi-lix hoik koe ju tix dim daix num,
mai ngai nawh hoik dug khawm uik.)
16 Pui an ah nin:
“Daux in singaix aux kaoh khaing bang naing,
mai to hoek tin heue.”
Yi-lix chawk nawh:
“Kawn aux,
yuh ka mawx?”
17 Pui an ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex tang yaog tix to khaing plak sivoe pui Fe-li-see;
ex kied laux leeing tete.
Khaix an,
kawn simex maix Heu-fu-ni mai Fe-ni-ha khawm ra kaux hoik yum.
Teug sijax mukheu Siyiex kawx hoik tud tix hu heue!”
18 Yam krai pui an ka teug sijax mukheu,
Yi-lix goh hu plak khaix khaing dee ngawm tix praok diang siviex veng.
Yi-lix kuad nang kluing nang,
mai yam goh nawh raig ngawg tix,
yum heue.
Nawh yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tipon num.
Grawng yum doeh ceu cad Fe-ni-ha
19 Mun Yi-lix moeng Fe-ni-ha koe kaux,
mai dex sang hoek singaix bawg yam sang keh.
Nawh mhong grawng teug sijax mukheu Siyiex hoik tud tix hu,
khawm keeing mex nawh mai mex nawh hoik yum,
dum mawx dum an nawh tom saux,
mai keh ka kawn.
20 Yam hoik dex sang yum nawh,
bun miex ot ka praok nawh pa kia ngien nawh khankix ah nin ka nawh:
“Giex rhawm maix kaux ceeix!
Maix hoik keh kawn simex tix kaux.”
Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang simi,
mai ang pawk lox kix.
21 Nawh sum ceu kaux kawn tix mawh Yi-cia-pawc,
mai krai dee bleeh ah nin:
“Min plak ngai Siyiex hoik lih hu khaing Yi-seu-liex heue”,
mawh kheu hoik tud pui teug sijax mukheu,
mai keeing mex nawh mai mex nawh kawx hoik yum.
22 Nawh ah nin:
“Min plak ngai Siyiex hoik lih hu khaing Yi-seu-liex,
mawh kheu hoik tud pui ka teug sijax mukheu Siyiex hu heue.”
