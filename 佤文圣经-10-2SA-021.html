Pauh doeh ceu cad Sao-law yum
21
Bawg yam singaix pa hoik giex Ta-wue kaing,
mai hoik koe sibhawm praix pa kied kin been loe num.
Ta-wue khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex son in.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“In hoik mawh jao phi map pa hoik yuh Sao-law mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh.
Nawh pauh ka pui pa ot Ci-piien yum.” (
2 aPui Ci-piien ang mawh pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kix mawh ceu pui pa iak tix ceu ka daux pui Ya-maw-lix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
son sang kia ngien ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
Sao-law keud ceu pa sang pauh ka kix,
kheu muh nawh ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax.)
3 Ta-wue kok pui Ci-piien hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Sang chix pon yuh aux pa tix son pex lie?
Aux simie tix deeh vied ka krax kha rhawm pa hoik kham pex glang ka an,
son sang kieh pex nawb buan ka piang pui cie Simiang Siyiex heue.”
4 Kix ah nin:
“Krax pa simah yix ka nyiex ma Sao-law ang mawh khri mau pa pon kieh mhawm.
Yix kawx ang doh simie tix pauh ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
Ta-wue chawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pex keud aux khaw tix sang yuh ceu pa tix son pex lie?”
5 Kix ah nin:
“Sao-law yuh yix laux leeing sang yuh yix laux leeing khawm uik,
mai ang kieh yix lai yaox tix im ju ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
maix teei simex aliah kaux lih khaing ka daux doeh ceu cad nawh.
Yix sang ot ka haktiex Ci-pi-yax,
mai sang teuk teue kix ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai teuk teue kix yum ka pa mawh ka daux haktiex pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex Sao-law mawh simiang heue.”
Simiang ah nin:
“Aux tag sang tawx kix ka pex heue.”
7 bMawh kheu hoik yuh simiang Ta-wue sijax mukheu tau mai Yaw-na-tang,
kheu nan,
nawh tom deuh ka kawn simex Yaw-na-tang kawn saux Sao-law pa kaux Mi-fe-paw-seu,
8 cviang mawh nan,
tom tawx Ya-maw-ni mai Mi-fe-paw-seu pa mawh kawn simex ra kaux pa keh Sao-law tau mai kawn bun Ai-yax pa kaux Li-see-pa.
Nawh baox tawx kawn simex phuan kaux pa keh kawn bun Sao-law pa kaux Mi-la tau mai Ya-teu-liex kawn simex Pa-si-lai ceu pui Mi-heu-la.
9 Ta-wue tawx kix ka pui Ci-piien.
Pui Ci-piien tom ot piang gawng,
mai teuk teue kix yum ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
Pui in kix aliah kaux yum tau.
Yam pauh kix yum jiet caw bawg yam sivoe sang hoek raux praix,
bawg yam pa jauh tix vawg ka nghox ca pa lang mu heue.
10 Moeng iak Sao-law,
pa mawh kawn bun Ai-yax pa kaux Li-see-pa,
ot dex ka dee deuh to kaux yum an,
mai teei man keei sum,
yuh nyiex gox tix lhang son to kaux tix ot ka piang simaox ting,
mai paung ot tan.
Jauh tom yam vawg ka nghox ca pa lang mu tom hoek bawg yam lih lhiex raux,
yam pon ngaix nawh ang kieh sim khankix pu tix hoek tom piang to kaux yum,
yam pon sawm nawh ang kieh to sat hoek dex tan heue.
11 Koe pui krai kaing pa hoik yuh Li-see-pa moeng iak Sao-law ka Ta-wue,
12 dTa-wue tom hu ka veng Ya-pi haktiex Ci-liex,
mai teei ka to kaux yum Sao-law mai kawn nawh Yaw-na-tang hoek.
(Pui haktiex dee an briex tix teei ka to kaux yum hu khaing brawk Paw Sang.
Pui Fe-li-see pauh Sao-law yum ka gawng Ci-li-paw,
ka daux ngaix an mai vhak to kaux yum kix ka Paw Sang.)
13 Ta-wue viex ka si-ang:
Sao-law mai si-ang:
Yaw-na-tang ing,
khawm pui pa teuk teue kix yum an aliah kaux nang.
14 Hawt awm lox pa krai simiang,
kix tom teei ka si-ang Sao-law mai si-ang Yaw-na-tang,
mai si paung ot ka daux haktiex Piien-ya-ming yaong Si-la,
dee koe si meeig keeing Sao-law pa kaux Ci-see.
Jauh yam an,
Siyiex nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg kix son meung haktiex heue.
Puing ka pui ting Fe-li-see
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 20.4–8)
15 Pui Fe-li-see mai pui Yi-seu-liex puing ka paox tix.
Ta-wue viex pui naing cie tix hu puing ka pui Fe-li-see.
Kix puing ka paox tix,
Ta-wue hoik taug mao.
16 Koe pui ting tix kaux kaux Yi-seu Pi-naw.
Nawh viex pliah tawng pa koe cien loe kongcing plak,
mai kawg ka vaig,
sang pauh ka Ta-wue.
17 eViang mawh nan,
Ya-pi-sai kawn simex Si-lu-ya hoek teem Ta-wue lauh ka pui Fe-li-see an,
mai pauh nawh yum.
Khaix an,
pui naing cie Ta-wue khiox khaw Ta-wue cu lox kix ah nin:
“Maix paw lai baox hu yuh naing tau mai yix,
nu ka yeet kawk ngu cie pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
18 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex baox yuh naing ka pui Fe-li-see ka Keu-pawx.
Pui Hu-sa pa kaux Si-pi-kai pauh ka pui ting pa kaux Sa-fux yum heue.
19 Ka daux haktiex Keu-pawx,
pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Fe-li-see baox puing ka paox tix tix bawg.
Pui Paw-li-haung kawn simex Ya-euxf,
pa kaux Yi-leu-ha-nang pauh ka pui Cia-theux pa kaux Keu-li-yax.
Pliah pa viex pui in hoik ting awm jaong cag taing man yuh nan heue.
20 Khaix an,
naing baox lih koe ka Cia-theux.
Haktiex an koe pui ting tix kaux pa brax yuh naing.
Giien taix giien caong nawh koe tix plak liah mu,
khawm uik koe tix nga pon giien.
21 Nawh si lah ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pux ke Ta-wue kawn simex See-mi-yax pa kaux Yaw-na-tang pauh nawh yum heue.
22 Pui in plak piang pon kaux,
hoik mawh doeh ceu cad pui Cia-theux pui ting.
Kix hoik yum daux tawm taix Ta-wue mai pui pa ot greem nawh heue.
