Lai Yaw-na
Yaw-na lieh lheu ka Simiang Siyiex
1
aKoe tix singaix,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka Yaw-na kawn simex Ya-mi-thai ah nin:
2 “Maix hu ka veng ting Ni-ni-we pa ot plak lih singaix,
ne raw pui dee an ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
mawh kheu hoik yaox aux grawng pa kied ting phi map kix heue!”
3 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-na deeh tieh tix hu ka tang plak,
mai keud tix sang mox tix khaing Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh hoek ka diang praok rawm sigrit sigra Yaw-pha,
mai yaox reu pa sang phat hu ka haktiex Tha-see plak klip singaix.
Nawh tawx ngawh brug reu,
laig daux reu,
mai sang hu tau mai pui pa brug reu hoek ka haktiex Tha-see.
Nawh keud,
phan hoek ka dee an taug sang singai ot khaing Simiang Siyiex heue.
4 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh bhaung ting phlu ka piang rawm,
bhaung ting puk pak rawm klauh ka reu,
mai dex sang kieh reu laux.
5 Pui pa ot daux reu hoik yaoh yee khawm uik,
mai kix ku kaux khiox khaw Siyiex tix teem coi tix.
Son sang tieh kraung cien pa ot daux reu,
bkix tom tik kraung c pa ot daux reu ka daux rawm sigrit sigra.
Yam an,
Yaw-na ot ka daux reu,
noi tix it ka dee an,
mai hoik laig it heue.
6 Cao kaing phat reu yaox Yaw-na ka daux reu,
nawh tom ah nin ka nawh:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai kawn laig it dee in lie?
Kaoh,
khiox khaw ka siyiex maix heue!
Ha nawh ka kawn chix caox rhawm ka ex,
mai taoh ka ju ex heue.”
7 Pui pa brug reu lai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Ex yuh krax pa cawk lai,
jhak tix yaox mawh phi map pui mawx pa kieh ex si pup ka krax pa laux leeing in.”
Kix tom yuh krax pa cawk lai,
Yaw-na caw tix cawk.
8 Kix chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Krai ka yix,
krax pa laux leeing in mawh ang mawh maix pa kieh lih lie?
Mawh yuh maix kaing pa tix?
Kaoh maix khaing dee mawx?
Mawh maix pui meung mawx lie?”
9 Yaw-na pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui Si-paw-lai.
Aux mawh pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh mawh Siyiex piang meung rao ma,
mawh cao pa phan pa yuh rawm sigrit sigra mai haktiex.”
10 Yaw-na baox krai ka kix,
mai krai mox tix khaing Simiang Siyiex in heue.
Pui pa ot daux reu mhong ka lox in yaoh yee kah,
kix tom ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix ce kaing ind kawn mawh pa iak lie?”
11 Mawh kheu bhaung nied ot nied tix ting,
kix tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sang yuh yix maix yuh ka mawx tom taug chix kieh bhaung yiien lie?”
12 Yaw-na pawk lox ah nin:
“Pex yaog tix taing aux ka daux rawm,
bhaung tom taug chix yiien,
mawh kheu hoik tawng aux phi map tix kieh pex si pup ka bhaung ting in heue.”
13 Pui pa ot daux reu tieh tix nied sau riang tix vai reu,
son sang kieh reu hoek praok rawm.
Viang mawh nan,
bhaung nied tix ting,
kix ang lai chix hu plak sivoe ngai tix.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
kix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi ciyang yix yum kheu pa lieh lheu pui in ka lox tawm maix.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
kaing in khawm uik hoik hawt tix mawh awm alo maix heue.”
15 Khaix hoik uik lox krai kix,
kix tom yaog ka Yaw-na,
mai taing nawh daux rawm;
bhaung tom yien dum mawx dum an.
16 Pui pa ot daux reu hoik lhat graung yung yi Simiang Siyiex kheu kaing pa lih tix mawh in,
kix tan lu ka nawh,
khaix an ap pong tix krub ka nawh heue.
17 eYam an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx kax ting tix mu hoek tix bleet ka Yaw-na.
Yaw-na ot ka daux vaig kax loe singaix loe sawm heue.
