Yi-li-yax haok meung rao ma
2
Bawg yam teei Simiang Siyiex bhaung klia rieb Yi-li-yax haok meung rao ma hoik hoek.
Yi-li-yax mai Yi-li-sa yaog tix hu khaing Cie-cia;
2 Ka daux krax,
Yi-li-yax ah nin ka Yi-li-sa:
“Maix ot dee in;
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux hu ka Paw-theu-lix heue.”
Yi-li-sa pawk ah nin:
“Aux sang tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju mai ka maix,
aux ang doh hu khaing maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kiex tom hu tau hoek ka Paw-theu-lix heue.
3 Koe cao jhai pui Paw-theu-lix tix cub hoek ka Yi-li-sa,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Hoik tawng ang maix tawng daux in singaix,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang rieb tix teei ka sara maix hu lie?”
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawng kah;
viang mawh nan,
ex paw lai krai ka kaing in heue.”
4 Yi-li-yax ah nin ka Yi-li-sa:
“Maix ot dee in;
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux hu ka Yie-li-keu heue.”
Yi-li-sa pawk ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju mai ka maix,
aux ang doh hu khaing maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kiex tom hu tau ka Yie-li-keu heue.
5 Koe cao jhai tix cub pa ot ka Yie-li-keu hoek ka Yi-li-sa,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Hoik tawng ang maix tawng daux in singaix,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang rieb tix teei ka sara maix hu lie?”
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawng kah;
viang mawh nan,
ex paw lai krai ka kaing in heue.”
6 Yi-li-yax ah nin ka Yi-li-sa:
“Maix ot dee in;
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux hu ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue.”
Yi-li-sa pawk ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju mai ka maix,
aux ang doh hu khaing maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kiex tom hu tau.
7 Koe cao jhai tix phuan kaux hu tau mai kiex ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Yi-li-yax mai Yi-li-sa kaog ka praok klawng;
cao jhai an kix tix phuan kaux cung tix ot ka dee ang singai khaing dee ot kiex.
8 Yi-li-yax peeik ka sibex dha tix,
pua kah,
mai lauh rawm klawng kah,
rawm tom lih guah tix tang ot.
Nawh mai Yi-li-sa hu ka piang tiex kroh,
hoek ka rawm klawng plak an.
9 aKhaix hoik tiang kiex ka rawm klawng,
Yi-li-yax chawk Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Sivoe sang haok aux meung rao ma,
maix simie aux yuh pa tix son maix lie?”
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix tawx siawp sibrawm maix ra son ka aux,
son sang pon aux tix mawh pui pa plud ka ju maix heue.”
10 Yi-li-ya ah nin:
“An kied mawh pa nu tix tawx tete.
Viang mawh nan,
phan maix yaox yam haok aux ka rao ma,
maix tag sang pon kah;
phan ang mawh nan,
maix tag ang pon ka heue.”
11 Kiex hu nang,
si kah nang.
Dum mawx dum an,
brung ngu tix mu pa viex ka ling law ngu lih hoek ka si nax kiex,
bhaung klia tom rieb Yi-li-yax haok meung rao ma.
12 bYi-li-sa yaox kah,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Keeing aux eue,
keeing aux eue!
Cao pa teem coi kia ngien,
pa ting riang pui Yi-seu-liex eue!”
Yi-li-sa jauh yam an mai ang lai yaox ka Yi-li-yax khaix heue.
Yi-li-sa laux rhawm mai riah griah ka kraung ceep tix lih koe ra plak,
13 mai sut ka sibex dha pa cawd khaing to kaux Yi-li-yax,
khaix an deeh ing mai cung ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
14 Nawh lauh rawm klawng ka sibex dha Yi-li-yax,
ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-li-yax ot dee mawx lie?”
Khaix an,
nawh baox lauh ka rawm klawng,
rawm klawng tang guah tix,
nawh tom hu ka toi klawng plak an heue.
15 Cao jhai pa kaoh khaing Yie-li-keu tix phuan kaux kok jhak ka dee singai,
mai ah nin:
“Siawp sibrawm pa koe ka to kaux Yi-li-yax lih koe ka to kaux Yi-li-sa heue!”
Kix hu rieb ka nawh,
mai krub ka sivoe ngai nawh,
16 ah nin:
“Yix pui ot dee in tix phuan kaux hoik mawh pui pa mhawm kaux hak khawm uik;
kieh yix hu sawg ka cao simiang maix.
Rien tix mawh khuan Simiang Siyiex pa viex nawh hu,
mai deuh nawh ot ka piang gawng tix dauh dauh,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka daux ngawd tix dauh dauh heue.”
Yi-li-sa pawk ah nin:
“Ang,
pex paw vi hu heue!”
17 Viang mawh nan,
kix kawn ca sang hu,
Yi-li-sa tom cu tix kieh kix hu.
Pui an kix tix phuan kaux hu sawg ka Yi-li-yax ku dauh ku dee,
hoik sawg ka loe singaix mai ang yaox ka nawh.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom deeh ing ka Yi-li-sa dee an.
Yi-li-sa krox jun kix ka Yie-li-keu;
nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Ang aux hoik krai ka pex ang cu pex hu sawg lie?”
Yi-li-yax yuh mhai pa daox am
19 Koe pui kaoh khaing veng Yie-li-keu hoek ka Yi-li-sa,
mai ah nin:
“Sara,
maix hoik tawng in mawh veng pa mhawm tix mu;
viang mawh nan,
rawm ang mhawm,
haktiex kroh krawx heue.”
20 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Tawx klong khraox hoek tix mu,
mai sau kih ka daux ceeix.”
Kix tom teei tix tawx ka nawh.
21 Yi-li-sa hu ka praok dee keet rawm,
mai hieh kih daux rawm,
ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Aux tag kieh rawm in singax,
ang lai kieh pui baox yum,
mai haktiex ang lai baox dee kroh krawx.’ ”
22 Jauh yam an,
rawm an tom singax jheeng jheeng,
awm lox pa hoik krai Yi-li-sa yuh nan heue.
23 Yi-li-sa kaoh khaing Yie-li-keu mai haok ka Paw-theu-lix.
Ka daux krax,
nawh si pup ka pui awn ju khankix pa kaoh khaing daux veng.
Kix nyiah ka nawh,
mai kok yo lox tom ting ah nin:
“Pa ghle kaing in,
yoi heue!”
24 Yi-li-sa veeh to kaux tix,
dong ngai tix ka kix,
mai jhax lawg kix ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
miex krih ra mu lih hoek khaing daux tiag khaox,
mai kiied pui pa awn ju ka daux kix tipon ra kaux phawng vid heue.
25 Yi-li-sa hu ka gawng Cia-mix,
khaix an,
mai deeh ing ka Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
