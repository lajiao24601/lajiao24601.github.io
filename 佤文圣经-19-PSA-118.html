Rawg buan son krax pa puing pex
118
aTag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem nawh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
2 Kieh pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
3 Kieh cao giex kaing li Siyiex khankix ah nin:
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
4 Kieh can pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
5 Aux khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka Simiang Siyiex ka daux krax pa khi rhawm;
nawh pawk ka lox aux,
mai hoik pluat ploi ka aux.
6 bSimiang Siyiex ot tau mai aux,
mai aux ang koe krax lhat graung;
kawn pui pa koe nex koe nham khankix sang chix pon yuh ceu pa tix ka aux lie?
7 Cao pa teem coi ka aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux tag sang yaox grawng pa laux leeing som nai pui koeng.
8 Khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex kia ngien,
kawn lheu khaing een rhawm tix ka pui pa sang yum.
9 Khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex kia ngien,
kawn lheu khaing een rhawm tix ka simiang piang haktiex heue.
 
10 Pui koeng tom nie hoik lai lawm ka aux;
viang mawh nan,
aux een rhawm tix ka riang rao Simiang Siyiex yuh kix laux leeing.
11 Kix hoek tix lai lawm aux khawm pon daog;
viang mawh nan,
aux een rhawm tix ka riang rao Simiang Siyiex yuh kix laux leeing.
12 Kix awm phung hia pa hoek tix lai lawm ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
hoik uik gleeing awm yeem kat pa proe ngu tix vud khaing yuh nan;
aux hoik yuh kix laux leeing ka daux riang rao pa tawx Simiang Siyiex.
13 Kix sau riang tix puing ka aux,
mai kieh aux goh;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik teem coi ka aux.
14 cSimiang Siyiex mawh riang rao aux,
mawh lox rhax aux;
nawh hoik taoh ka aux heue.
 
15 Lox gaux rhawm krax pa puing pex hoik luah ka daux nyiex gox buan man pui cie Siyiex:
Simiang Siyiex hoik yah tix tawx pa ting riang ting rao tix.
16 Riang rao nawh hoik kieh ex puing pex;
Simiang Siyiex hoik yah tix tawx pa ting riang ting rao tix heue.
 
17 Aux ang yaox tix hoek krax yum,
aux kawn sang im hu sivoe ngai,
krai pao ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex hu.
18 Nawh hoik sax ciyang ka aux tom cien,
viang mawh nan,
mai ang kieh aux yum heue.
 
19 Paoh siviex nyiex buan son aux liak;
aux sang laig tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
20 Siviex in mawh siviex Simiang Siyiex;
mawh ngie pui pa rauh pa see tag pon tix laig daux khaing heue.
 
21 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik nghiet maix ka lox rawg aux,
mai kieh aux puing pex heue.
 
22 deSimaox in pa hoik tik cang mai yuh nyiex
hoik lih mawh simaox jaong nyiex pa blawn ting ceu.
23 In mawh kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex;
mawh pa kied daox deng heue!
24 In hoik mawh singaix pa puing pex Simiang Siyiex;
ex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
ex tag caw tix yuh poi ting krax pa yaog yaw.
25 fSimiang Siyiex eue,
taoh ka yix liak,
taoh ka yix liak;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh yix mhawm ot mhawm koe heue.
 
26 gKieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka pui an pa hoek tix krub ka daux ceu kaux nawh!
Yix nawb buan ka piang maix ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
27 Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex;
nawh kia ngien ka ex.
Ex jauh tix yuh ka poi,
teei ka kak khaox viex hu ka plak ot khu dee tan heue.
 
28 Maix mawh Siyiex aux,
mai aux yaog yaw ka maix;
Siyiex aux eue,
aux sang krai pao pa ting pa lhaong maix hu heue.
 
29 Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem nawh heue.
Krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
