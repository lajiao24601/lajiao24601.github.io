Pui Ya-sux ghlat Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 18.13–37; 19.14–19, 35–37; Lai Yi-sai-yax 36.1–22; 37.8–38)
32
Khaix hoik yuh simiang Si-si-cia kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex in khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Si-na-ci-lix puing ka Yo-tax.
Nawh lai lawm ka veng pa yuh kix kaux cong,
mai keud tix sang yuh veng in laux.
2 Si-si-cia yaox hoik keud Si-na-ci-lix mai but rhawm tix sang puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
3-4 nawh mai simiang pui haktiex mai simiang naing khankix but tix tat toh rawm nyaux pui pa koe plak praix veng dut,
son sang kieh pui Ya-sux yam hoek dex kix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ang yaox tix nyaux rawm.
Kix kieh pui tom nie hu klat ka toh rawm,
mai ang lai kieh rawm lih.
5 Simiang Si-si-cia yuh guh veng,
sau riang tix yuh veng plak daux kieh kaux cong,
ka piang guh veng sum nyiex lhaong koe kah,
mai baox sum guh veng plak praix.
Lheu khaing an,
nawh baox cox sum ka tab prim Yie-lu-sa-leung pa koe ka plak lih singaix ka piang haktiex pa klawk pa hoik thawm an,
mai baox yuh nang pliah mai dieb tie naok tom nie.
6 Simiang Si-si-cia tawx lox ka can simex pa ot ka daux veng,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa krai cao bung naing khawm uik,
mai khrawm kix hoek ka brawk diang siviex veng,
mai ah nin ka ki:
7 “Yuh tix kaux cong,
yuh tix kua rhawm!
Paw vi lhat ka Vawng Ya-sux mai ka pui naing pa thung nawh khankix,
mawh kheu pui pa ot tau mai ex an kawn lheu ting riang khaing pa ot tau mai nawh.
8 Can pa koe nawh kok mawh ngie riang rao kawn pui khaing;
mai ex hoik koe Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex pa teem coi ka ex,
puing naing son ex.”
Kawn pui kheu mhong lox pa krai simiang in,
mai kix hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi heue.
9 Khaix hoik luan hoek tix bawg yam,
yam kawn ot Si-na-ci-lix mai pui naing cie nawh ka haktiex La-ciex,
nawh ploi pui cau ra loe kaux ah nin ka Si-si-cia mai ka kawn pui Yo-tax pa ot tau mai nawh ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung:
10 “Aux,
pa mawh Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Si-na-ci-lix chawk pex,
pex tag koe krax yum rhawm miex ding,
yam lai lawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung pex kawn ot dee an lie?
11 Si-si-cia krai ka kix,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex tag sang taoh pex luat luan khaing yix.
Si-si-cia hoik pie lhien ka pex,
nawh sang kieh pex yum ka cai rhawm,
yum ka sang nyaux rawm.
12 “Pa yieh laux ka nyiex Simiang Siyiex mai khu dee tan,
tawx lox ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
krai chix ngie tix krub gleeing nyawng bhawng ka khu dee tan tix mu khaing hoik mawh nawh heue.
13 Khex ang pex tawng tax du aux mai grawng pa yuh aux ka mawx ka meung tang meung lie?
Koe Siyiex meung mawx pa pon taoh kawn pui luat luan khaing Vawng Ya-sux lie?
14 Koe Siyiex meung mawx pa pon taoh meung tix khaing daux tawm taix aux lie?
Mawh kheu nan,
pex hoik een rhawm tix ka ceu pa tix keud Siyiex pex sang pon taoh tix lie?
15 Paw vi kieh Si-si-cia pie lhien ka pex,
nyaw glaw ka pex.
Paw vi yum rhawm ka lox nawh!
Siyiex meung pui tix meung kawx ang nyang sax taoh pui cie tix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix Vawng Ya-sux.
Siyiex pex kawx ang doh pon taoh ka pex heue!”
16 Pui cau Ya-sux khankix kawn ah lox map pa ang nyawm tix nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex mai ka siliex Simiang Siyiex pa kaux Si-si-cia.
17 Vawng baox tiiem lai map nai ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex.
Lox lai ah nin:
“Siyiex tang meung ang sax taoh kawn pui cie tix luat luan khaing tawm taix aux,
Siyiex Si-si-cia kawx ang pon taoh pui cie tix luat luan khaing tawm taix aux.”
18 Pui cau khankix siao lox Si-paw-lai kok yo tom ting si num ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa ot ka piang guh veng,
son sang ghlat ka kix,
kieh kix ang lai koe riang tix puing ka naing,
mai pon teei veng in yie khaing.
19 Kix krai lao Siyiex Yie-lu-sa-leung,
awm hoik krai lao pui riang rao pa yuh pui ka taix tix mai siyiex pui tang ceu khankix yuh nan.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Si-si-cia mai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax kawn simex Ya-maw-see rawg buan ka Siyiex,
khiox khaw nawh teem coi kix.
21 Simiang Siyiex ploi kawn cau Siyiex lih yuh simiang naing Ya-sux mai simiang naing khankix laux leeing khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
pak ngai Vawng Ya-sux hoik naog ka pa kaig mai deeh ing ka meung tix.
Koe tix ngaix,
nawh ot ka daux nyiex vad tix,
pa mawh kawn simex lih nawh khankix klauh nawh yum heue.
22 Yuh nin,
Simiang Siyiex taoh ka simiang Si-si-cia mai ka kawn pui Yie-lu-sa-leung luat luan khaing tawm taix Si-na-ci-lix Vawng Ya-sux,
mai hoik luan nang khaing khawm pui koeng tang kaux khankix,
mai kieh kix yuh krax pa nhim tau mai meung pa ot sivie siveng kix.
23 Pui tom nie hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai tawx nang khawng phak pa ngawh ka simiang Si-si-cia.
Jauh yam an,
can meung piang haktiex hoik koe krax nab yam yung yi ka Si-si-cia khawm uik heue.
Jhawm saux Si-si-cia mai pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi nawh
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 20.1–3, 12–19; Lai Yi-sai-yax 38.1–3; 39.1–8)
24 Ka daux bawg yam in,
simiang Si-si-cia saux,
hoik dex sang yum.
Nawh rawg buan,
Simiang Siyiex tom tawx mhai sakse ka nawh tix ceu,
mai jhi grawng sang moi saux nawh.
25 Viang mawh nan,
Si-si-cia ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai ang bax yaog yaw grawng pa hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex son nawh,
mawh kheu nan krax laux krax leeing tom lih hoek piang nawh,
piang Yo-tax,
mai piang Yie-lu-sa-leung.
26 Viang mawh nan,
khaix an,
Si-si-cia mai pui pa ot Yie-lu-sa-leung deeh koe krax tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
Simiang Siyiex tom ang lai ciyang kix glang yam kawn im ju Si-si-cia ka piang haktiex heue.
Pa mag mi Si-si-cia mai pa koe min nawh
27 Si-si-cia hoik koe sin ngao tom nie,
pui ku kaux hoik koe krax yung yi ka nawh.
Nawh sum nyiex krao,
deuh mau khri,
siang simaox,
sidah bhawng,
dieb tie naok,
mai kraung tang ceu pa koe ngawh khankix ot daux.
28 Lheu khaing an,
nawh baox sum ka krao pa deuh nghox koi,
plai,
bee sime,
mai baox sum kawg son phung moi son phung yung.
29 Lheu khaing in kix khawm uik,
Siyiex kawn baox tawx moi,
yung,
mai sin ngao tang ceu ka nawh tom nie,
nawh ang sum veng pa khraox tom nie ang chix.
30 Si-si-cia thox ka dee lih rawm Ci-suing,
mai bluh hoek ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Can kaing pa yuh nawh hoik pon mhawm khawm uik;
31 khaix an,
yam hoek pui cau Pa-pi-loeng tix chawk grawng kaing pa tang pa siyox pa lih tix mawh ka Si-si-cia dee in,
Siyiex kawx hoik kieh nawh tang prawng ca,
mai kieh grawng in hoek cheuk tix yaox ka krax keud krax sawn nawh heue.
Pa giex Si-si-cia kaing hoik si dauh
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 20.20–21)
32 Kaing pa yuh simiang Si-si-cia tang ceu khankix,
mai grawng pa hoik yum rhawm nawh khawm rawm khawm rhi tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
hoik tiiem tix ka daux Lai Riang Su sai Cao Jhai Yi-sai-yax pa mawh kawn simex Ya-maw-see,
khawm ka daux Lai Been Simiang Yo-tax mai Lai Been Simiang Yi-seu-liex.
33 Yam hoik yum Si-si-cia kix si paung nawh ot ka gawng si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang ka jan plak piang.
Yam yum nawh,
can pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik yiiem doe son nawh,
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Ma-na-si plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
