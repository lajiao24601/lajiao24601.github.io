Lox tawx Ta-wue ka Saw-law-meung sivoe sang yum tix
2
Yam hoik dex sang yum Ta-wue,
nawh tawx lox ka kawn simex tix Saw-law-meung ah nin:
2 “Singaix lih hu aux khaing haktiex hoik hoek.
Maix tag caw kieh tix kiien geeik,
mawh simex mhaing,
3 Maix tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix,
mai nghiet ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap nawh khawm uik,
pa mawh lox tawx pa hoik tiiem tix ot daux lai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si.
Yuh nin,
phan pex hoek dee mawx ang ah,
kaing pa yuh pex ku ceu tag sang pon lih tix mawh.
4 Phan pex nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag sang chix giex kaux ka lox pa ap tix,
pa mawh pa ah nin,
ca mawh priex doeh ceu cad aux tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh,
hawt grawng lawng khaix nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai kix tom tag yaox tix kuang tom rheeng ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
5 c“Kawn koe kaing tix ceu.
Maix hoik tawng Yaw-ya pauh ka cao bung kaing naing pui naing Yi-seu-liex ra kaux,
pa mawh kawn simex Ni-eu pa kaux Ya-ni-eu mai kawn simex Yi-thie pa kaux Ya-ma-sha.
Maix caw kieh tix doh keud kah,
nawh pauh kiex yum singai yam yien yawg nhim tiiem haktiex,
awm yam pauh pui paox tix yum daux naing yuh nan.
Nawh pauh pui ang koe dee luda yum,
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik klawm ka kaing pa yuh nawh,
kha rhawm tix son kaing inb.
6 Maix hoik tawng,
sang khaw tix yuh yuh ka mawx ka nawh,
mai ang pon tix kieh nawh yum mhawm heue.
7 d“Maix tag caw tix kia mhawm mhawm ka pui Ci-liex kawn simex Pa-si-lai,
mai priex kix mhawm mhawm;
mawh kheu yam to aux mox tix khaing pux ke maix Ya-sa-long,
kix hoik kia ngien ka aux mhawm mhawm heue.
8 e“Kawn koe,
pui Piien-ya-ming yaong Pa-hu-ling,
kawn simex Ci-la pa kaux See-me;
yam hu aux plak Ma-ha-nian singaix an,
nawh briah mai jhax lawg ka aux.
Viang mawh nan kawx,
khaix an yam hoek nawh rieb aux ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
aux hoik tiex tu ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex ang pauh ka nawh.
9 Viang mawh nan,
maix ang pon tix kieh nawh bheeik luan khaix lox tat jhawm prawng.
Maix mawh pui pa lhak lhiao,
maix hoik tawng khaw tix sang yuh ka mawx;
maix caw tix keud grawng sang pauh nawh yum heue.”
Ju Ta-wue hoik si dauh
10 Ta-wue hoik yum,
mai si paung nawh ot ka veng Ta-wue.
11 fNawh yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tipon num:
yuh simiang ka Si-paw-loeng aliah num,
yuh simiang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tix ngoe loe num.
12 gSaw-law-meung plud khaix keeing tix Ta-wue mawh simiang;
mai meung dee giex nawh kaing kied sidaing kaux cong heue.
Ya-to-ni-ya yum
13 Khaix an,
Ya-to-ni-ya kawn simex Ha-ci hu ka Pa-see-pa miex Saw-law-meung.
Pa-see-pa chawk nawh:
“Maix hoek mawh son sang yuh krax pa mhawm lie?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Mawh heue.”
14 Khaix an nawh baox ah nin:
“Aux koe kaing tix ceu sang khiox khaw ka maix.”
Pa-see-pa ah nin:
“Maix krai heue.”
15 Ya-to-ni-ya ah nin:
“Maix hoik tawng aux caw tix sang mawh simiang;
pui Yi-seu-liex ku kaux kawx simie aux mawh simiang.
Viang mawh nan,
grawng kaing in hoik tieh tix ang lai mawh nin;
pux aux deeh tieh tix mawh simiang,
kheu in hoik mawh alo pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex.
16 Yam in aux koe lox khiox khaw tix ceu,
khiox khaw maix paw vi tieb tie liak.”
Pa-see-pa chawk ah nin:
“Mawh kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
17 hYa-to-ni-ya ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix rawg mhaing lox tang aux ka simiang Saw-law-meung,
nhie nawh tawx kawn bun Su-niang an pa kaux Ya-pi-sang ka aux,
son sang kieh mawh moeng aux;
aux hoik tawng ang nawh doh chix thiang lox maix heue.”
18 Pa-see-pa ah nin:
“Chix tix,
aux hu khiox khaw ka simiang son maix heue.”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
Pa-see-pa hu ka simiang,
son sang krai lox in tang Ya-to-ni-ya ka simiang.
Simiang yaog tix kaoh cung,
mai yuh krax pa nab yam yung yi ka miex tix,
khaix an nawh deeh ngawm ka piang dang ngeung,
mai baox nhie pui sau deuh dang ngeung tix mu ot ka plak dawm tix,
tawx miex nawh ngawm.
20 Pa-see-pa ah nin:
“Aux koe kaing pa iak tix ceu sang khiox khaw ka maix,
khiox khaw maix paw vi tieb tie ka liak.”
Simiang chawk ah nin:
“Miex,
mawh kaing ceu pa tix?
Aux ang doh chix tieb tie ka heue.”
21 Pa-see-pa ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix tawx Ya-pi-sang ka pux ke maix Ya-to-ni-ya,
kieh mawh moeng nawh heue.”
22 Simiang ah nin:
“Maix yuh ka mawx lai nhie aux tawx Ya-pi-sang ka nawh lie?
Phan yuh nin,
ang lheu khaing khin maix aux tawx nawh mawh simiang khaing.
Nawh mawh pux ke aux,
mai nawh baox mawh pa hoik yaog Ya-pi-ya-tha cao giex kaing li mai Yaw-ya ang mawh lie!”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
Saw-law-meung tiex tu ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Phan aux ang kieh Ya-to-ni-ya deeh teei ju to kaux tix,
hoek plud ka lox pa khiox khaw nawh in,
kieh Siyiex ciyang aux tom kin heue!
24 Simiang Siyiex hoik laug ka aux,
mai kieh aux kaux cong ngawm ka piang dang ngeung keeing aux Ta-wue,
mai hoik baox ap tix tawx meung in ka aux mai ka doeh ceu cad aux.
Aux sang tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
Ya-to-ni-ya ang yum daux in singaix ang chix!”
25 Mawh kheu nan,
Saw-law-meung tawx lox ka Pi-na-ya kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax;
Pi-na-ya tom hu pauh ka Ya-to-ni-ya yum heue.
Dee si dauh krax pa lawng Ya-pi-ya-tha mai Yaw-ya
26 iSimiang Saw-law-meung ah nin ka Ya-pi-ya-tha cao giex kaing li:
“Maix deeh ing ka nyiex ma cie tix Ya-na-thu heue!
Maix hoik khaw tix yum,
viang mawh nan,
yam in aux ang pauh ka maix;
mawh kheu ka daux singaix ju keeing aux Ta-wue,
maix hoik klawm ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
mai hoik kham krax kha rhawm kha rhi tau mai keeing aux.”
27 jSaw-law-meung pluat tix tik ka kaing pa yuh Ya-pi-ya-tha,
mai ang lai kieh nawh mawh cao giex kaing li Simiang Siyiex.
Grawng in hoik lih tix mawh awm lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka son cao giex kaing li Yi-lix mai doeh ceu cad nawh khankix ka See-law heue.
28 Yam mhong Yaw-ya ka kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh,
to hu mox tix ka daux nyiex gox buan dee deuh teug sijax mukheu,
nawh kawm ka rong khu dee tan.
(Viang nawh ang bax hawt rhawm yaog min Ya-sa-long,
nawh hoik hawt rhawm yaog min Ya-to-ni-ya.)
29 Yam hoik hoek lox in ka daux yhaok Saw-law-meung,
krai hoik to Yaw-ya tix hoek ka daux nyiex gox buan dee deuh teug sijax mukheu,
mox tix ka praok khu dee tan,
Saw-law-meung tom ploi pui hu chawk Yaw-ya,
yuh ka mawx lai to hu mox tix hoek ka khu dee tan dee an.
Yaw-ya tom krai to mox tix dee an mawh kheu lhat nawh ka Saw-law-meung.
Kheu nan,
Saw-law-meung tom ploi Pi-na-ya khu pauh ka Yaw-ya.
30 Pi-na-ya hoek ka daux nyiex gox buan dee deuh teug sijax mukheu dee an,
mai ah nin ka Yaw-ya:
“Simiang tawx lox nhie maix lih heue!”
Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Ang!
Aux sang yum tin heue.”
Pi-na-ya deeh ing ka simiang dee an,
mai krai lox pa krai Yaw-ya ka simiang heue.
31 Saw-law-meung ah nin:
“Hawt awm lox pa krai Yaw-ya pauh nawh yum,
mai si paung ka nawh.
Doeh ceu cad aux mai Ta-wue tix ceu kawx ang lai siao klawm ka phi map grawng pauh Yaw-ya ka pui ang koe dee lud.
32 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh Yaw-ya tang klawm phi map pa pauh pui.
Mawh kheu nawh ang kieh keeing aux Ta-wue tawng mai hu pauh ka pui,
Yaw-ya hoik pauh ka pui ra kaux pa kawn lheu mhawm lheu rauh see khaing nawh,
pa mawh cao bung kaing naing pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ya-ni-eu,
mai cao bung kaing naing pui naing Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-sha.
33 Phi map pa pauh Yaw-ya ka pui,
tag sang hoek piang nawh mai ka piang doeh ceu cad nawh khankix leeing ju;
viang mawh nan Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue plud ka dang ngeung cie nawh,
mai haok huan leeing ju heue.”
34 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-na-ya kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax hu ka daux nyiex buan dee deuh ka teug sijax mukheu ot dee an,
mai pauh ka Yaw-ya yum,
mai si paung nawh ot ka dex daux tiag pui nyiex nawh khankix.
35 Simiang ploi Pi-na-ya mawh cao bung kaing naing khawm uik,
plud son kaing yuh Yaw-ya,
mai baox tawx Sa-tu cao giex kaing li tang son kaing yuh Ya-pi-ya-tha heue.
See-me yum
36 Simiang ploi pui hu kok See-me hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hoek,
sum nyiex son cao maix ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai paung ot tan ang pon tix lih hu khaing veng.
37 Phan maix lih hu,
jao luan khaing klawng iak Ci-loeng,
maix tag sang yum tete,
mai grawng pa yum maix hoik mawh pa tang yuh maix heue.”
38 See-me ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
Vawng.
Aux tag sang hawt tix yuh ka lox pa krai maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
See-me paung ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tom rheeng bawg yam heue.
39 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik luan loe num,
siliex cawng See-me ra kaux to hoek ka Ya-cie kawn simex Ma-cia simiang Cia-theux dee an.
See-me mhong ot kiex ka Cia-theux,
40 nawh tom rien khre ka la,
mai hu sawg ka siliex cawng tix ka Ya-cie simiang Cia-theux dee an,
nawh yaox ka kiex,
mai viex kiex deeh ing.
41 Saw-law-meung mhong grawng pa hoik lih hu See-me khaing veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
42 tom ploi pui hu kok nawh,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux hoik nhie maix tiex tu ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
ang doh lai pon tix lih hu khaing veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Khaix an,
baox krai ka maix,
ka daux singaix pa lih hu maix an tag mawh singaix pa yum maix;
ang maix hoik cu tix nghiet ka lox in lie?
43 Yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh laux ka lox pa tiex tu tix,
lieh lheu ka gum lox pa tawx aux lie?
44 Maix hoik tawng sidawng,
maix hoik yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka keeing aux Ta-wue.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang maix kheu kaing in;
45 viang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang tawx buan ka aux,
mai kieh dang ngeung dee ngawm simiang Ta-wue kaux cong leeing ju heue.”
46 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang tawx lox ka Pi-na-ya kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax,
mai nawh tom hu pauh ka See-me yum.
Yuh nin,
Saw-law-meung tom pon giex kaux ka piang meung cie nawh khawm uik heue.
