Pui pa yaox krax laux leeing klih rhawm rhi tix
39
Aux hoik krai ah nin:
aux sang priex rhawm tix ka kaing pa yuh tix,
ang kieh dak tix yuh phi map;
yam ot pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai aux nyeep jheeh tix ang nye.
2 Aux yawg tix,
ang ah lox pie tix gum,
pie lox mhawm kawx ang kieh lih khaing daux jheeh tix.
Viang mawh nan,
krax kha rhawm nied tix kin;
3 ka daux rhawm rhi aux hoik kied hax awm ngu.
Aux nied tix keud,
mai nied tix ang nyawm ot nyawm koe,
aux tom ang lai pon kham mai chawk ah nin:
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
ju aux koe miex glang?
Aux sang yum yam mawx?
Khiox khaw maix krai ka aux,
dee si dauh krax pa lawng aux sang hoek yam mawx?
 
5 Maix hoik kieh ju aux dawt yuh nin!
Ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
krax pa im ju aux hoik awm ang mawh tid tix.
Ju awm ngie tix toek bhawm yuh nan khaing;
6 kawn pui awm pang peue khaing.
Krax taug krax mao hoik mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik;
nawh khrawm tix deuh ka sin ngao tix,
mai ang tawng mawh pui mawx pa sang pon tix cie heue.
 
7 Simiang aux eue,
aux sang kawn koe ceu pa simie pa tix lie?
Aux sang een rhawm tix ka maix khaing.
8 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing krax lud krax loe khawm uik,
paw vi kieh pui pa daox aw nyiah ka aux.
9 Aux sang yien yawg tix ang nye,
mai ang ploi lox tix lih tix gum kawx,
mawh kheu hoik kieh maix aux kha rhawm tix yuh nin.
10 Khiox khaw maix paw vi lai baox ne raw ka aux;
pa si vied maix ka aux hoik dex sang kieh rhawm aux dut.
11 Maix teei krax pa ne raw ciyang ka pui phi map,
awm ceu pa muh greeing tix lhawn khawm uik yuh nan.
Mawh tete,
kawn pui hoik awm ngie tix toek bhawm khaing heue.
 
12 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg aux,
bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux.
Yam yiiem aux tix khiox khaw ka maix,
khiox khaw maix teem coi ka aux.
Aux mawh nyo si nie maix,
hoik awm tax du tix yuh nan,
mawh nyo si nie pa hu krax khaing.
13 Khiox khaw maix va rhawm ka aux,
kieh aux koe krax nyawm rhawm ceeix ceeix sivoe sang hu mai ang lai chix deeh heue.
