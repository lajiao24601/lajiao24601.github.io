10
Pex hoik yaox ka krax laux krax leeing heue!
Pex hoik yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa ang rauh ang see kieh gut kheem ka pui cie aux.
2 Pex hoik teei ka grawng in nieb pon tud ka siawp sibrawm pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai kieh kix ang pon krax pa rauh pa see;
pex hoik teei ka grawng in tud ka sin ngao miex mai kawn doe.
3 Yam ciyang Siyiex ka pex,
pex sang yuh ka mawx lie?
Yam teei nawh meung dee singai yuh pex laux leeing,
pex sang yuh ka mawx lie?
Pex sang hu sawg krax teem krax coi dee mawx lie?
Pex sang mox sin ngao tix dee mawx lie?
4 Phan pex ang yum ka daux bang naing,
mai pui tag sang giex laung ka pex.
Viang hoik yuh nin kawx,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex tag ang chix naox tix;
nawh ca kawn yaog taix tix lauh ka pex heue.
Siyiex teei simiang Ya-sux mawh kraung siao tix
5 aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux sang teei Ya-sux mawh khaox nguh mai lauh ka pui pa cheeik aux song rhawm.
6 Aux ploi Ya-sux hu puing ka meung haktiex pui pa caox pa caig,
pa mawh pui pa cheeik tix kieh aux chu rhawm.
Aux ploi kix hu tud hu rhim,
mai dug dig pui hun khankix ka caong tix,
awm dug dig phawng tiex ka daux laih daux cau yuh nan heue.”
7 Viang mawh nan,
simiang Ya-sux hoik tang koe rhawm cau nyhau pa nieb pon yuh tix ka piang pui.
Nawh hoik but rhawm tix sang yuh laux leeing ka meung tom nie.
8 Nawh ah lox ting mu ah nin:
“Simiang naing aux hoik chix tix mawh simiang ka piang meung ku kaux heue!
9 Aux hoik pon kuang ka veng Cia-leu-naw mai veng Cia-ci-mi-see.
Aux hoik pon kuang ka veng Ha-ma mai veng Ya-eu-pa.
Aux kawx hoik pon kuang ka veng Sa-ma-li-yax mai veng Ta-ma-see-keux.
10 Aux tawx taix tix ciyang ka meung an kix,
mawh kheu krub kix riang rao;
riang rao meung an kix kawn lheu khaing pa koe ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka daux veng Sa-ma-li-yax.
11 Aux tag sang yuh laux ka veng Sa-ma-li-yax mai ka riang rao pa krub pui dee an,
tag sang yuh laux nang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka riang rao pa krub pui dee an heue.”
12 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Khaix hoik aux kaing yuh tix ka gawng Shi-ang mai ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
aux sang ciyang ka lox ting mu simiang Ya-sux mai krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi nawh heue.”
13 Simiang Ya-sux ah lox ting ah nin:
“In hoik mawh ka daux kaing pa yuh aux tix kaux khaing.
Aux kied ting riang ting rao,
kied lhak lhiao chix yuh kaing heue!
Aux hoik tik ka mhai mee meung haktiex in kix,
mai hoik teei ka sin ngao kix khawm uik.
Aux hoik awm moi pa ting riang ting rao yuh nan pa dug dig ka pui haktiex an.
14 Meung ka piang haktiex hoik awm mheem sim yuh nan,
aux tud hu ka sin ngao kix mai hoik yie awm sut aux ka tawm sim pa koe daux mheem yuh nan.
Ang koe preeik tix mu pa pon chuh ka aux;
ang koe jheeh tix mu pa pon ah lox ka aux.”
15 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Khex kawn lheu ting moe khaing cao moe lie?
Khex kawn lheu ting jhi khaing cao jhi lie?
Khaox nguh ang pon yaog ka pui,
viang mawh nan,
kawn pui pon teei tix siao ka khaox nguh an heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
pa mawh simiang kru naing tag sang teei jhawm saux ciyang ka pui pa sawm sak khankix.
Ka daux nex nham kix tag sang mawh ngu pa gleeing ang jud ang jai.
17 Siyiex pa mawh pa jhan riang Yi-seu-liex tag sang lih mawh rao ngu.
Ka daux tix ngaix khaing,
tag sang gleeing uik pak lawg,
pie yeem kat kawx ang lai deuh koe.
18 Ka daux nong khaox mai ka daux haktiex pa mhawm tag sang laux leeing khawm uik,
awm pui pa koe jhawm saux pa dut rhawm tix kaux yuh nan.
19 Khaox pa deuk khaing daux nong ang lai koe miex ding tix,
pie kawn nyawm kawx hoik pon simin tix yaox ka heue.
Pui pa deuk khankix deeh ing
20 Yam deeh ing pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix,
kix ang lai baox deeh een rhawm tix ka meung haktiex an pa yie yuh kix laux leeing jheeng jheeng;
kix tag sang yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa singaix singeh Yi-seu-liex tete.
21 Pui Yi-seu-liex pa tux tag sang deeh hoek ka Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm dee an.
22 bViang pui Yi-seu-liex hoik haoh hun awm mhaik ka praok rawm sigrit sigra yuh nan,
mawh ngie pui pa deuk khankix pa yaox tix deeh hoek khaing.
Pui haoh pui hun tag sang si pup ka krax laux krax leeing,
krax pawk bix pa khaw kix tix pon,
tag sang awm rawm ting pa kheem ka kix yuh nan.
23 Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik but rhawm tix yuh meung laux leeing heue.
Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka Ya-sux
24 Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong pa mawh simiang kru naing ah yuh nin ka pui Shi-ang:
“Viang pui Ya-sux hoik sax tuk tiak ka pex,
mai yuh kaing ceu pa ang khaw caw ka pex awm pui Ai-cix yuh nan,
pex paw vi lhat ka kix.
25 Tix vud tix cu khaing,
krox jun krax pa song rhawm aux ka pex grai,
aux tag sang yuh kix laux leeing.
26 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing,
tag sang teei maox si vied ka kix,
awm si vied pui Mi-tiien ka rang Eu-lix yuh nan;
aux sang ciyang ka pui Ya-sux,
awm hoik ciyang pui Ai-cix ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh yuh nan.
27 Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang taoh pex lih khaing greem siawp sibrawm pui Ya-sux;
khaox gang cie kix ang lai baox yaox tix lih mawh beeh glawm pa cien ka klip pex heue.”
Pui tud pui rhim puing tix laig
28 Pui koeng hoik pon teei ka veng Aic!
Kix hoik tiang laig ka haktiex Mi-ci-loeng;
kix hoik deuh kraung khrai tix ka Mi-mawx.
29 Kix hoik tiang laig ka daux ngawd,
mai sum bang ot ka Cia-pa.
Pui pa ot ka daux veng La-max hoik si yaung si kua;
pui Ci-pi-yax pa ot ka haktiex dee ot simiang Sao-law hoik yaog tix to hu.
30 Pui Cia-ling eue,
pex tag caw tix kok yo lox pa ting heue!
Pui Lai-sax eue,
pex tag caw bre tix nghiet mhawm mhawm heue!
Pui Ya-na-thu pa kha rhawm tix eue,
pex pawk heue!
31 Pui Ma-teu-mi-na mai pui Ci-ping hoik yaog tix to hu khawm uik.
32 Ka daux singaix in pui koeng hoik sum bang ot ka Naw-pawx;
kix lua tawm taix tix tiex pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ka gawng Shi-ang heue.
33 Jhak eue,
Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong pa mawh simiang kru naing tag sang puing ka kix,
awm kak khaox pa hoik kid pui cawd khaing khaox.
Ka daux kix pui pa blawn ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai pui pa blawn ting riang ting rao,
tag sang bluh kix lih khawm uik,
mai kieh kix hoek krax kaig krax tua.
34 Simiang Siyiex tag sang cot kix goh,
awm pui pa teei lawm moe kid ka khaox pa koe daux nong yuh nan,
pie khawm khaox pa blawn ting ka Li-pa-noeng kawx tag sang kid goh heue!
