Lai Lu-cia
Gum lox pa jauh tix krai
1
Simiang "Thi-a-fe-law ah nin":
Hoik koe pui tom nie yuh kaing pa tiiem ngai lai,
krai grawng ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh ka daux ex.
2 Pa krai kix hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm kaing pa hoik yaox pui an kix ka ngai tix tom yam jauh,
khaix an hoik mawh pa sidiex pui pa krai lao ka gum lox in.
3 In khawm uik aux hoik si kaw tix yaox jauh tom ka kaing,
mawh kheu nan,
aux sang hawt tix krai ka maix baux mawx baux an,
4 kaing ting mawh son sang kieh maix tawng ka ceu pa hoik gau tix mawh pa khaw caw heue.
Grox krai pa sang keh Yaw-hang cao cub com
5 aYam giex simiang Si-luix kaing piang meung Yo-thaix,
koe cao giex kaing li tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Sa-cia-li-yax,
nawh mawh cub caw tau mai cao giex kaing li Ya-pi-ya;
moeng nawh kaux Yi-li-sa-pai,
hoik mawh nang doeh ceu cad cub nyiex Ya-loeng.
6 Kiex ra kaux hoik mawh pui pa rauh see ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap mai ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
7 Kiex ang koe kawn;
mawh kheu Yi-li-sa-pai ang chix keh kawn,
mai kiex hoik kuad ju khawm ra kaux tix heue.
8 Koe tix ngaix,
Sa-cia-li-yax pau nyiex,
mai yuh kaing ara cao giex kaing li ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
9 Kix hawt awm been pa hoik tat cao giex kaing li tix deuh cawk ka lai,
nawh pon tix mawh pui pa laig tok nyawng bhawng ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
10 Yam tok nawh nyawng bhawng,
pui hun rawg buan ka plak praix.
11 Dum mawx dum an,
koe kawn cau Simiang Siyiex cung ka plak dawm khu dee tan mai jhi nawh yaox tix;
12 Sa-cia-li-yax yaox kah,
mai kied lhat.
13 Viang mawh nan,
kawn cau Siyiex an krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Sa-cia-li-yax,
paw vi lhat heue!
Siyiex hoik nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg maix;
moeng maix Yi-li-sa-pai tag sang keh kawn simex son maix tix kaux,
maix tag caw tix sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-hang.
14 Maix tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
pui hun kawx tag sang gaux rhawm son pa lih keh nawh.
15 cKa plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag sang mawh pui pa ting ceu tix kaux.
Nawh tag ang pon tix nyaux ka plai num ka plai khien;
ka daux vaig miex nawh,
nawhb hoik naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh.
16 Nawh tag sang thung ka pui Yi-seu-liex tom nie hoek ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix.
17 dNawh tag sang mawh kaw pa hoek sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
pa koe riang,
awm cao jhai Yi-li-yax yuh nan.
Nawh tag sang kieh keeing mai kawn deeh khaw rhawm paox,
kieh pui lieh lox deeh cox rhawm tix,
mai hu ka krax lhak lhiao pa lawng pui rauh see;
nawh tag sang teem coi kawn pui hoek rieb ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
18 Sa-cia-li-yax krai ka kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang tawng aux kaing in ka daux ceu pa tix lie?
Aux hoik kuad ju;
moeng aux kawx hoik riex ju nang heue.”
19 eKawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux mawh Cia-pai-liex pa cung ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex;
tawx aux hoek krai lox ka maix,
mai krai maix mhong gum lox pa gaux rhawm in.
20 Viang mawh mawx nan,
maix ang yum rhawm ka lox pa krai aux.
Yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
gum lox in kix tag sang lih tix mawh,
maix tag sang lih mawh pui ang chix nye tom hoik hoek ka daux singaix pa lih gum lox aux tix mawh,
maix tom tag chix si kah heue.”
21 Bawg yam in,
pui hun krox jun ka Sa-cia-li-yax,
mai ang tawng nawh ot daux nyiex buan yuh ka mawx lai lha hoek yuh nan.
22 Nawh lih khaing nyiex buan mai ang chix si kah ka kix,
pui hun tom taug tawng nawh hoik yaox riang su sai ka daux nyiex buan.
Nawh ang chix si kah,
mai ca yuh taix tix kieh pui hun yaox heue.
23 Yam hoik naog singaix pa yuh kaing ka daux nyiex buan,
Sa-cia-li-yax tom ing ka nyiex.
24 Ang koe miex glang tix,
moeng nawh Yi-li-sa-pai rug nghe,
mai ang lih hu khaing nyiex been phuan khix.
25 Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik chix kia ngien ka aux yuh nin,
mai hoik teei tix tik krax pa kaig ngai aux khaing plak sivoe ngai pui hun heue.”
Grox krai sang lih keh Ye-sux
26 Yam nghe Yi-li-sa-pai ka daux liah khix,
Siyiex ploi kawn cau tix Cia-pai-liex lih hoek ka Cia-li-lix,
pa mawh veng Na-sha-leux,
27 fson sang krai ka kawn bun tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ma-li-yax.
Kawn bun in hoik ap tix mex ka simex cub nyiex Ta-wue pa kaux Yaw-seux tix kaux.
28 Kawn cau Siyiex hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh ah nin:
“Kieh pa nhim koe tau mai maix heue!
Maix hoik mawh bun pa pon buan son pa ting,
Simiang Ye-sux ot tau mai maix heue!”
29 Mawh kheu hoik mhong Ma-li-yax ka lox kawn cau Siyiex in,
nawh kied lhat mai ang nyawm ot,
mai nawh keud jee keud blaong ka dee bleeh lox pa chawk nawh nyawm ot in.
30 Kawn cau Siyiex krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ma-li-yax,
paw vi lhat,
mawh kheu Siyiex tawx buan son ka maix.
31 gMaix tag sang nghe kawn simex tix kaux,
tag sang sum ceu kaux mawh Ye-sux.
32 hNawh tag sang lih mawh pui pa ting ceu tix kaux,
tag sang kok nawh mawh kawn simex Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex tag sang ap nawh plud ka simiang Ta-wue.
33 Nawh tag sang mawh simiang nyiex Ya-keux leeing ju leeing phan,
siawp sibrawm simiang nawh ang chix uik chix leeing heue!”
34 Ma-li-yax krai ka kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux ang bax koe mex,
kaing ceu pa yuh nin sang chix lih tix mawh yuh ka mawx lie?”
35 Kawn cau Siyiex pawk ah nin:
“Khuan Singax Singeh tag sang lih hoek ka piang to kaux maix;
siawp sibrawm Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang kia ngien ka maix.
Mawh kheu nan,
kawn ngie pa tag sang lih tix keh an,
tag sang kok mawh kawn simex Siyiex.
36 Maix jhak tix yaox ka paox nyiex maix Yi-li-sa-pai,
viang nawh hoik riex ju kawx,
viang pui hun hoik krai ang nawh chix keh kawn,
viang mawh nan,
yam in nawh hoik koe kaux liah khix.
37 iMawh kheu kaing ceu pa ang Siyiex chix yuh ang koe tix ceu kawx heue.”
38 Ma-li-yax ah nin:
“Aux mawh bun siliex Simiang Siyiex;
kieh lox maix lih tix mawh ka to kaux aux heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau Siyiex lih hu khaing nawh heue.
Ma-li-yax hu vhi ka Yi-li-sa-pai
39 Ang koe miex glang tix,
Ma-li-yax yaog tix hu,
mai nied tix hu plak haktiex grawng,
hoek ka veng Yo-tax tix mu.
40 Nawh laig daux nyiex Sa-cia-li-yax,
chawk Yi-li-sa-pai mhawm ot.
41 Yi-li-sa-pai mhong chawk Ma-li-yax tix mhawm ot,
kawn ngie pa ot daux vaig nawh cang luh tix.
Yi-li-sa-pai pon pa naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
42 nawh kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Maix mawh pa blawn pon buan son khaing daux paox bun tix;
kawn ngie pa nghe maix kawx hoik pon ka buan son heue!
43 Miex simiang hoek tix kia ka aux,
aux sang pon yuh ka mawx lie?
44 Yam mhong aux ka lox pa chawk maix aux mhawm ot,
kawn ngie pa ot daux vaig aux hoik gaux rhawm cang luh tix.
45 Bun pa yum rhawm ka gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka nawh pa tag sang lih tix mawh,
kied ting buan son tete heue!”
Lox rhax pa yaog yaw Ma-li-yax ka Simiang Siyiex
46 jMa-li-yax ah nin:
rhawm rhi aux yaog Simiang Siyiex ting;
47 khuan aux kieh Siyiex pa mawh cao taoh aux mawh krax gaux rhawm;
48 kmawh kheu hoik kia ngien nawh ka bun siliex pa iak pa lied tix.
Jauh yam in,
kawn pui piang haktiex tag sang kok aux mawh pa koe buan son,
49 mawh kheu Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm hoik yuh kaing ting son aux.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh pa singax singeh;
50 nawh tawx krax pa caox rhawm ka pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh,
leeing ju leeing phan.
51 Nawh nyeu plawng taix tix pa ting siawp sibrawm,
mai teei tix tik ka krax keud krax sawn pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi khawm uik.
52 lNawh cot simiang pa kied ting riang lih khaing dang ngeung ngawm tix;
nawh baox yaog pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi lhaong.
53 Nawh kieh pui pa cai rhawm ih sak ka priex nyawm,
mai kieh pui mi pui koe ing mai taix yi tix.
54-55 mNawh hoik giex tom kaux ka lox pa deuh tix ka tax du ex,
mai hoik teem coi ka siliex tix Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh hoik kia ngien ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai tawx krax caox rhawm pa ting tix ka nawh,
khaix an tom hoek ka doeh ceu cad nawh leeing ju leeing phan heue!
56 Ma-li-yax paung ot tau mai Yi-li-sa-pai been loe khix,
khaix an deeh ing ka nyiex heue.
Yaw-hang cao cub com keh
57 Bawg yam sang keh Yi-li-sa-pai hoik hoek,
nawh keh kawn simex tix kaux.
58 Paox plawg paox nyawm nawh mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh mhong grawng pa tawx krax caox rhawm pa ting yuh nin ka nawh,
hoik gaux rhawm tau mai nawh khawm uik heue.
59 nYam hoik naog keh kawn nyawm an tix vang tuah,
kix hoek tix Pat-hak nawh,
mai keud tix sang kieh nawh kaux ceu kaux keeing tix Sa-cia-li-yax.
60 Viang mawh nan,
miex nawh ah nin:
“Ang chix heue!
Nawh tag sang kaux Yaw-hang heue.”
61 Paox nyiex paox ma nawh krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ka daux ceu pui nyiex ma maix ang sax kaux ceu kaux pa yuh nin.”
62 Kix tom yuh taix tix ka keeing nawh,
mai chawk nawh sang kieh kawn nyawm in kaux ceu kaux pa tix heue.
63 Sa-cia-li-yax chawk tix lo ka phia tiiem tix plah,
mai tiiem ah nin:
“Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-hang.”
Pui hun kied daox deng kah.
64 Yam an,
Sa-cia-li-yax hoik pon si kah,
nawh tom ang jheeh tix yaog yaw Siyiex.
65 Paox plawg paox nyawm nawh kied daox deng kah.
Grawng kaing in hoik tauh hu ka haktiex gawng Yo-thaix khawm uik;
66 can pui pa mhong kix hoik keud ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
“Kawn nyawm in tag sang lih tix mawh pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?”
Mawh kheu hoik lih siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex tix ot tau mai nawh heue.
Lox jhai pa krai Sa-cia-li-yax
67 Sa-cia-li-yax keeing Yaw-hang naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
mai krai hu ka gum lox Siyiex ah nin:
68 Ex rhax lai yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex heue!
Nawh hoik kia ngien ka pui cie tix,
hoik pluat ploi ka kix.
69 Nawh hoik yaog cao taoh pa ting siawp sibrawm tix kaux lih son ex,
khaing daux nyiex ma siliex tix Ta-wue.
70 Yam jauh dix dix,
nawh hoik kieh cao jhai pa singax krai ah nin:
71 nawh tag sang taoh ex luat luan khaing pui koeng,
hoik teei tix tik ka sibrawm pui pa koeng ka ex khawm uik.
72 Nawh tag sang tawx krax pa caox rhawm tix ka tax du ex,
mai priex tix deuh ka sijax mukheu pa singax tix heue.
73-75 Nawh hoik ap lox ka tax du ex Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai hoik tiex tu tix sang taoh ex luat luan khaing pui koeng,
kieh ex tix ju hoek tix ju,
ang lai koe rhawm pa lhat pa graung ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
mai teei pa singax singeh mai pa rauh see krub ka nawh.
76 oKawn nyawm aux eue,
pui tag sang kok maix mawh cao jhai Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong.
Maix tag sang mawh kaw pa hu sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
pa cox khre daux krax son nawh;
77 tag caw tix krai ka pui cie nawh ah nin:
mawh kheu hoik pon kix krax pa pluat ploi phi map,
kix tag sang pon krax taoh heue.
78 Siyiex ex mawh Siyiex pa koe krax caox rhawm mai pa awn rhawm awn rhi;
Nawh hoik kieh pa riang krax taoh jhan ka ex,
79 pmai baox kieh pa riang khaing rao ma dee lhaong
jhan riang ka piang pui pa ot greem veei krax yum khawm uik,
duk thung ex hu ka krax pa nhim pa tiiem.
80 Kawn nyawm an ting si nyek si nyek,
to kaux khawm rhawm rhi nawh hoik brax kua.
Nawh paung ot daux tiag daux tu,
tom hoek bawg yam pa krai nawh kaing ceu pa sang yuh tix ka daux pui hun Yi-seu-liex heue.
