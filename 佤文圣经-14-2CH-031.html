Si-si-cia loh ka krax been li
31
Khaix hoik uik poi,
can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hu ka veng Yo-tax ku dauh,
lauh max ka rong simaox,
kid ka riang rong bun siyiex Ya-seu-la dut,
teei tik ka khu dee tan mai nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak.
Kix kawx yuh kaing pa awm paox ka daux haktiex Yo-tax tang dauh mai haktiex Piien-ya-ming,
Yi-fa-liien,
Ma-na-si,
khaix an kix tang deeh ing haktiex tix heue.
2 Simiang Si-si-cia cox khrawm ka cao giex kaing li mai ka pui Li-we cub mawx cub an,
ka greem lox been in,
pui ku kaux hoik koe beeh glawm ka kaing pa caw tix sang yuh.
Kaing yuh kix mawh khawm pa tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
kaing krub ka daux nyiex buan,
tan lu ka lox rhax mai krax yaog yaw ka daux nyiex buan.
3 aNawh baox tan moi yung cie to kaux tix kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ku ngawb mai ku bo,
khawm son singaix tuah,
yam haok khix ku khix,
mai krax tan daux poi tang ceu pa hoik tat lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex tix deuh heue.
4 bLheu khaing an,
simiang tawx lox ka pui pa ot Yie-lu-sa-leung viex krax tan pa sang caw cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we tix sang pon hoek,
son sang pon yuh kix ka kaing lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik ciak ci Simiang Siyiex tix deuh mhawm mhawm.
5 Yam hoik hoek lox pa tawx in,
pui Yi-seu-liex tom viex khawng phak pa blawn mhawm cie kix,
pa mawh nghox koi,
plai,
bee sime,
rawm hia,
mai to ma tang ceu khankix kao son tix sonc hoek.
6 Pui pa ot ka daux veng Yo-tax ku dauh hoik teei moi yung cie kix kao son tix son hoek,
mai hoik tan lu nang khawng phak tang ceu tom nie ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix.
7 Jauh ka khix loe,
khawng phak hoek ang jud ang jai,
mai pum tix awm gawng yuh nan.
Kaing in ca yuh tix tom hoek aliah khix.
8 Simiang Si-si-cia mai simiang khankix yaox ka khawng phak pa nie yuh nin,
kix tom yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
mai baox yaog yaw pui cie nawh Yi-seu-liex.
9 Simiang si kaw chawk tix yaox ka cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we yuh ka mawx khawng phak nie yuh nin.
10 Doeh ceu cad Sa-tu cao giex kaing li pa ting Ya-sa-li-ya pawk lox ah nin:
“Jauh tom yam viex kawn pui khawng phak hoek ka nyiex buan,
ja lao ex ang kub ih ka priex preem,
mai kawn yaox tix haoh tom nie,
mawh kheu hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex buan son ka piang pui cie nawh heue.”
11 Kix hawt awm lox tawm simiang,
mai rien khre tix yuh nyiex dee deuh priex preem ot ka nyiex buan,
12 mai deuh can khawng phak mai pa hoik tan lu kao son tix son ot daux.
Kix tawx pui Li-we pa kaux Keu-nang-ya mawh cao pa kuang kia,
tawx See-me pux Keu-nang-ya mawh cao pa teem coi nawh.
13 Kix baox tawx pui Li-we kao kaux mawh cao pa yuh kaing greem kix;
ceu kaux kix mawh:
Yie-shiie,
Ya-sha-si-ya,
Na-ha,
Ya-sha-he,
Yie-li-maw,
Yaw-sa-pa,
Yi-liex,
Yi-see-ma-ci-ya,
Ma-ha,
Pi-na-ya.
Kaing in hoik hawt tix yuh awm lox tawx simiang Si-si-cia mai Ya-sa-li-ya cao giex kaing li pa ting.
14 Pui Li-we kawn simex Ying-na pa kaux Kheu-lix mawh cao kaing pa pau siviex nyiex buan plak lih singaix;
nawh mawh cao kaing pa rieb ka khawng phak pa tan lu pui ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh kawx mawh nang cao pa guah khawng phak.
15 Ka dee ot cao giex kaing li tang dauh,
koe pui Li-we pa tiiem ot plak greem mawh cao pa teem coi nawh ka daux krax pa kaux sijax tix.
Ceu kaux kix mawh:
Yi-tiien,
Ming-ya-ming,
Yie-su-yax,
See-ma-ya,
Ya-ma-li-ya,
See-cia-ni-ya.
Pui in kix guah tix tawx priex preem sidu ka pui Li-we,
hoik hawt tix yuh awm pa koe beeh glawm kaux mawx kaux an,
16 mai ang guah hawt awm ceu cad cub nyiex mawx cub nyiex an.
Can simex pa koe tix ngoed num haok plak piang,
pa yuh kaing daux nyiex buan ku singaix,
hoik hawt awm beeh glawm pa koe kix mai kaing pa yuh kix miex bawg,
tix kaux chix tix pon tix nyum.
17 Pa mawh cao giex kaing li tag hawt tix guah awm cub ceu pui nyiex tix;
pa mawh pui Li-we pa koe tix nga num haok plak piang tag hawt tix guah awm beeh glawm pa koe kix mai kaing pau kix miex bawg.
18 Kix hoik tiiem tau ceu kaux tix mai moeng tix,
kawn simex kawn bun tix khawm pui nyiex pui ma tix khawm uik,
mawh kheu kix tag caw tix rien rhawm tix yuh kaing ku bawg ku yam,
awm pa hoik koe beeh glawm tix yuh nan.
19 Pa paung ot ka veng pa pon doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng,
phan ang mawh nan,
cao giex kaing li ka daux tiag,
koe pui tix blah mawh cao pa guah tix tawx priex preem ka kawn simex can pa koe daux nyiex ma cao giex kaing li,
khawm pui pa koe ru ceu kaux ka daux ceu pui Li-we ku kaux heue.
20 Simiang Si-si-cia hoik yuh kaing pa rauh pa see,
pa khaw caw,
pa kaux sijax ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh ka daux haktiex Yo-tax khawm uik.
21 Nawh kied mhawm kaing yuh tete;
mawh kheu can pa yuh nawh,
mawh kaing pa dex mai nyiex buan ang ah,
mawh kaing pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng ku grawng ang ah,
mai nawh hoik yuh ka daux alo Siyiex khawm uik heue.
