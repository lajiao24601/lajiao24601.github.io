Tieh vheen ka Ya-tha-li-ya
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 11.4–16)
23
Yam hoek ka daux aliah num,
cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax hoik keud mawh bawg yam pa khaw tix chuh.
Nawh yuh paox pie tau mai simiang phuan kaux;
pui in kix phuan kaux hoik mawh Ya-sa-li-ya kawn simex Yie-law-hang,
kawn simex Yaw-ha-nang pa kaux Yi-seu-ma-lix,
kawn simex Eu-pe-teux pa kaux Ya-sa-li-ya,
kawn simex Ya-ta-ya pa kaux Ma-si-ya,
kawn simex Si-ci-lix pa kaux Yi-li-sa-fa.
2 Kix hu ka veng pa koe ka daux haktiex Yo-tax ku dauh ku dee,
mai khrawm ka pui Li-we mai cao kaing ceu pui ku ceu hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
3 aKix khrawm paox tix ka nyiex buan,
mai yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Yaw-a-see kawn simex simiang.
Yie-heu-yie-tax ah nin ka kix:
“In mawh kawn simiang phaox ka.
Yam in khaw tix yaog nawh mawh simiang;
hawt awm gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex,
doeh ceu cad simiang Ta-wue khaw tix mawh simiang.
4 Rhawm keud ex mawh nin:
ka daux singaix tuah,
yam pau cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we ka nyiex,
ka daux kix loe son tix son caw tix pau ka siviex nyiex buan,
5 mai an loe son tix son caw tix pau ka nyiex haw simiang,
pui pa deuk khankix pau ka siviex Keung-ci;
can pui khawm uik caw khrawm tix ot ka daux biang brawk nyiex buan plak daux.
6 Lheu khaing cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we pa pau nyiex,
pui tix kaux kawx ang pon tix laig daux nyiex buan.
Mawh kheu cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we hoik mawh cie Simiang Siyiex,
mai kix tom chix tix laig;
pui pa kawn deuk khankix tag caw priex tix hawt ka been pa deuh Simiang Siyiex mai kaog ot plak praix.
7 Pui Li-we caw tix viex vaig ot vung viang simiang kia nawh mhawm;
phan simiang hu dee mawx mai caw tix hu dee an.
Pui mawx ce tix sang laig daux nyiex buan tag caw tix pauh yum heue.”
8 Pui Li-we mai pui Yo-tax tag caw priex tix hawt ka lox pa krai cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax.
Pui pa ang hu yuh kaing ka daux singaix tuah kawx tag caw tix pau ka nyiex;
mawh kheu nan,
pui pau nyiex ang ah,
ang pau ang ah,
simiang ku kaux tag caw tix yuh kaing heue.
9 Yie-heu-yie-tax guah tix tawx pliah mai dieb tie naok pa deuh simiang Ta-wue ot ka daux nyiex buan sivoe an ka simiang khankix,
10 mai tawx pui hun viex vaig pau sivoe ngai nyiex buan,
kia ngien ka simiang.
11 Khaix an,
nawh viex Yaw-a-see lih,
ceep mhok khri ka piang kaing nawh,
mai tawx lai grawng pa dex dawt mai simiang ka nawh,
mai ap nawh mawh simiang.
Cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax mai kawn simex nawh khankix ap Yaw-a-see mawh simiang;
pui haoh pui hun yaog yaw ah nin:
“Kieh simiang lang ju pu rhang heue!”
12 Ya-tha-li-ya miex simiang mhong lox pa yaog yaw pui hun ka simiang,
mai nied tix hu ka nyiex buan dee khrawm pui hun paox tix.
13 Nawh yaox simiang pa khraox hoik hawt tix yuh awm been pa tawx mawh simiang,
cung ka praok rong dee laig pui ka daux nyiex buan,
simiang khankix,
mai pui pa phlu teet tawng vung viang ka nawh.
Pui haoh pui hun hoik gaux rhawm khawm uik,
mai phlu teet tawng;
pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan,
tauh ting tauh kraung,
thung pui ah lox rhax yaog yaw.
Ya-tha-li-ya laux rhawm mai riah kraung ceep tix,
kok yo ah nin:
“Tieh vheen heue!
Tieh vheen heue!”
14 Cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax ang cu pui pauh Ya-tha-li-ya ka nyiex buan,
mai tawx lox ka simiang khankix ah nin:
“Teei nawh lih hu khaing pui pa pau;
pui mawx keud tix sang taoh ka nawh,
tag sang pauh yum heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
kix giex ka Ya-tha-li-ya,
viex nawh hoek ka nyiex haw simiang,
mai pauh nawh yum ka Siviex Brung dee an heue.
Been pa loh Yie-heu-yie-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 11.17–20)
16 Cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax kieh simiang Yaw-a-see mai kawn pui yuh sijax mukheu tau maix tix,
son sang mawh pui cie Simiang Siyiex.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
pui hun hu ka nyiex vad Pa-lix,
mai yieh nyiex vad laux,
tawk khu mai riang rao khankix max,
mai baox pauh Ma-tang cao giex kaing li Pa-lix yum sivoe khu.
18 Yie-heu-yie-tax tawx cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we mawh cao kuang ka kaing nyiex buan.
Kix sang yuh kaing pa hoik ciak ci Ta-wue kix yuh,
mai hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai baox kuang ka lox rhax mai yam yuh poi krax gaux rhawm.
19 Yie-heu-yie-tax kawx tawx pui pau ka diang siviex nyiex buan,
mai ang kieh pui pa ang singax singeh ka daux been laig heue.
20 Yie-heu-yie-tax bung kaing can pui simiang,
pui pa koe min,
simiang ka daux pui,
mai kawn pui pa kawn deuk khankix rieb simiang daux nyiex buan hoek ka nyiex haw simiang;
kix laig daux plak siviex ting;
simiang ngawm ka piang dang ngeung.
21 Kawn pui kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
daux veng kawx hoik pon krax yien yawg,
mawh kheu singaix yam an,
pui hoik pauh ka Ya-tha-li-ya yum heue.
