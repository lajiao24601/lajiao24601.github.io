9
Viang mawh nan,
kieh si-ang kaing aux lih mawh dung rawm,
kieh sok ngai aux lih mawh rawm ngai keet,
son sang yaox aux tix yiiem leeing ngaix leeing sawm son paox ceu paox cad tix pa hoik yum ka pauh pui tix.
2 Viang mawh nan,
kieh aux koe dee mox tix ka daux tiag daux tu,
pon yaox tix singai ot khaing paox ceu paox cad tix.
Eue!
Kix hoik mawh cub caw pa tieh vheen,
ang koe krax yum rhawm ceeix kawx.
3 Kix hoik pie lhien ku bawg ku yam;
krax pa pie lhien hoik mawh tang son krax pa mawh pa meh,
hoik giex gang ka haktiex in heue.
 
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
pui cie aux ca baox yuh ka pa caox pa caig,
ang doh keud ka mawh aux Siyiex kix heue.
 
4 Pui ku kaux hoik tang rien tix ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
pux aik lih tix kawx ang pon tix yum rhawm kah;
mawh kheu pux aik hoik chix pie lhien awm Ya-keux yuh nan,
5 Paox grawm ku kaux hoik chix yuh ka kaing ceu pa ang mhawm khankix.
Pui ku kaux hoik pie lhien ka paox grawm tix;
ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa krai ka lox pa mawh pa meh;
dak pui ku kaux hoik pie lhien.
Kix ca nied tix yuh ka phi map;
6 kix ca baox toe tix yuh ka krax tud krax rhim mai krax gut krax kheem heue.
 
Simiang Siyiex krai,
pui cie nawh hoik tieh vheen ka nawh.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai ah nin:
Aux tag sang gaux ka pui cie tix,
mai cheuk cheu ka kix.
Pui cie aux yuh phi map,
mai aux doh ang sang ciyang ka kix lie?
8 Dak kix hoik lawm awm te yuh nan;
kix hoik pie lhien ang jud ang jai.
Pui ku kaux hoik chix krai ka lox mhawm jhawm jiet,
tete te lai,
sang yuh paox tix laux leeing daux o.
9 Aux ang khaw tix ciyang ka kix kheu kaing in kix lie?
Aux ang khaw tix pawk bix ka meung haktiex pa yuh nin lie?
 
10 Aux ah nin:
aux tag sang yiiem ngia son gawng lua,
rhax lox yiiem doe tix ceu son pak rib;
mawh kheu ceu an kix hoik gleeing lih mawh tiex nyaox,
ang lai baox koe pui pa laig daux,
ang lai mhong ka lox moi yung khankix,
pie khaix caong sim mai to tiag kawx ang yaox heue.
 
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
aux tag sang kieh Yie-lu-sa-leung riem,
lih mawh mheem dee ot sox tiag;
aux tag sang kieh veng Yo-tax ku mu riem,
ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa paung ot ka heue.
 
12 Aux chawk ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx haktiex in lai riem yuh nin,
awm pak mhaik pa kroh,
ang yaox pui ka tix kaux kawx lie?
Pui mawx pon lhak lhiao,
pon tawng ka grawng kaing in lie?
Maix hoik sax krai sidawng ka pui mawx,
kieh nawh pon kah dee bleeh lih ka pui tang kaux lie?”
13 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“In hoik mawh kheu pa tieh vheen pui cie aux ka lox pa sidiex gau aux,
ang nghiet ka lox aux,
ang hawt ka lox tawx aux.
14 Kix ca nieb pon hawt ka alo tix,
mai nghiet ka lox sidiex gau tax du tix,
hu krub ka Pa-lix.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin:
aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix ih plix song,
nyaux rawm pa koe jai.
16 Aux tag sang ploi kix hu ka meung ku dauh ku dee,
ka dee ang tax du kix mai ka to kaux kix nyang sax mhong.
Aux tag sang kieh pui naing pa koeng leue ka kix,
mai pauh kix yum khawm uik heue.”
Yie-lu-sa-leung khiox khaw krax teem
17 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai ah nin:
Pex tag caw tix keud ka kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh:
tag caw tix teei bun miex hoek yiiem tau mai pex,
teei ka bun pa kied chix rhax lox rhax yiiem doe hoek tix yiiem heue.
 
18 Kawn pui ah nin:
tag caw tix nied tix dhiet kix hoek tix rhax lox rhax yiiem doe son ex,
rhax tom hoik kieh ex lih rawm ngai bai rawm meeh,
lih awm rawm lhiex yuh nan heue!
 
19 Nghiet ka lox yiiem ngia ka daux veng Shi-ang pa ah nin:
Ex hoik laux leeing heue!
Ex hoik pon krax kaig tua pa ting heue!
Ex tag caw tix to hu ot haktiex pui tang meung;
nyiex ma ex hoik laux leeing heue!
 
20 Aux ah nin:
bun miex khankix eue,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex;
pex tag caw tix bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox nawh.
Tag caw tix gau kawn simex kawn bun tix yiiem ngia,
gau ka paox grawm tix rhax lox yiiem doe.
21 Krax yum laig ka daux doh nyiex,
hoik laig ka daux nyiex haw simiang ex;
krax yum hoik lauh goh ka kawn nyawm pa ot ka daux laih daux cau,
hoik yuh laux ka pui nhum ju pa ot ka daux pak ting.
22 To kaux pui yum hoik naog ka haktiex khawm uik,
hoik awm pum aing ka daux tiag daux tu,
hoik awm nghox ca pa vawg pui,
ang koe pui pa sut.
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
23 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
pui pa koe ping nyia ang pon tix yaw rang di ping nyia to kaux tix;
pui pa kua ang pon tix yaw rang di riang rao to kaux tix;
pui pa mi pa koe ang pon tix yaw rang di sin ngao to kaux tix.
24 aPhan mawx sang yaw rang di,
tag caw tix yaw rang di ka daux pa tawng yung ka aux;
sidawng ka aux;
kheu mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
aux koe krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix.
Aux yuh ka daux pa rauh pa see,
mai pa khaw caw;
pa muh aux mawh ceu in kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
25-26 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Koe tix singaix,
aux tag sang ciyang ka pui Ai-cix,
pui Yo-tax,
pui Yi-tong,
pui Ya-meung,
pui Maw-ya,
mai ceu pui pa paung ot daux tiag daux tu,
pa hoik ghrip ka hauk kaing tix ngaing an kix.b
Kix hoik Pat-hak ka daux nex nham,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux rhawm rhi kix,
ka daux rhawm rhi pui Yi-seu-liex ang bax Pat-hak tix,
ang priex tix hawt ka dee bleeh sijax mukheu Pat-hak heue.”
