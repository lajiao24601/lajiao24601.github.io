Lai Si-fang-ya
Krai dawt
Sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num ka daux aliah bawg yam, pa mawh yam loh simiang Yaw-si-yax ka been lih sivoe an kao num, sivoe sang lih koe jhong khix mee num liah yieh tix nga tix num, cao jhai Si-fang-ya lih tix sidiex. Phuk lai in koe cao jhai khankix rien krai lao ka kaing tix blah yuh nin: krax laux krax leeing mai singaix pa laux leeing tag sang lih hoek; mawh kheu krub pui Yo-tax ka riang rao, mai hawt ka been pui tang ceu, gut kheem ka pui pa iak riang iak rao, een tix hoik kub hoik paw, ce Siyiex ang lai kia ngien ka kix. Mawh kheu nan, Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka kix. Nawh kawx tag sang ciyang ka meung tang dauh. Viang Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik yaox krax pa laux leeing kawx, singaix pa sang hoek tag sang deeh pon krax pa haok huan, pui pa kawn deuk pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi mai pa rauh pa see tag paung ot dee an heue.
Krai kaing
1. Bawg yam singaix pa prawng ca Simiang Siyiex (1.1–2.3)
2. Meung pa ot sivie siveng Yi-seu-liex hoik laux leeing (2.4–15)
3. Krax laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung mai krax taoh (3.1–20)
