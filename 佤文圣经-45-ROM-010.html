10
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux kied simie paox ceu cad tix pon krax taoh,
mai khiox khaw kaing in ka Siyiex ang jud ang jai heue!
2 Aux chix tix kham sakse grawng pa kied koe krax gaux rhawm kix ka Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
krax gaux rhawm kix ang mawh pa kieh ngai lai mawh riah rong.
3 Kix ang tawng pa rauh see Siyiex,
mai keud tix sang tang sawg daux krax,
ang hawt tix yuh ka pa rauh see Siyiex.
4 Tete te lai,
Khrit hoik kieh lox tat jhawm prawng ang lai koe ceu,
mai kieh pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh lih tix mawh pui rauh see.
Tawx krax taoh ka kawn pui piang haktiex
5 aPhan mawh grawng kaing in pa kieh pui lih tix rauh see,
ka daux pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
Maw-si hoik sax tiiem ah nin:
“Pui pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
tag sang im kheu lox tat jhawm prawng heue.”
6 bViang mawh nan,
phan mawh grawng kaing in pa kieh pui lih tix rauh see kheu krax yum rhawm,
phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Maix paw vi tang chawk cheng tix ah nin:
pui mawx sang haok piang rao ma lie?
(In mawh nin,
hu teei viex Khrit lih.)
7 Paw vi chawk ah nin:
pui mawx sang hu meung yum lie?
(In kawx mawh nin,
teei viex Khrit lih khaing daux krax yum.)”
8 Deeh bleeh lox in mawh:
“Gum lox Siyiex ang singai khaing maix,
hoik ot ka daux jheeh maix,
ot ka daux rhawm maix heue.”
Phan loh tix krai,
hoik ot ka daux gum lox pa hoik krai pao ex.
9 Phan jheeh maix krai pao grawng mawh Ye-sux simiang,
rhawm maix yum rhawm grawng hoik kieh Siyiex nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
maix tag sang pon krax taoh.
10 Mawh kheu rhawm ex hoik yum rhawm yuh nin,
tag sang pon tix lih mawh pui pa rauh see,
jheeh ex hoik krai pao yuh nin,
tag sang pon krax taoh.
11 cPhuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pui pa yum rhawm ang yaox tix hoek krax pa som nai.”
12 Gum lox in hoik krai ka son pui khawm uik,
mawh kheu ang pui Yo-thaix mai pui tang ceu tang cad lai tang siyox khaing paox tix;
Siyiex hoik mawh simiang ka piang kawn pui khawm uik,
nawh tag sang tawx buan son pa kub ka can pui pa khiox khaw ka nawh khawm uik.
13 dAwm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Can pui pa kok tix khiox khaw ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex tag sang pon krax taoh heue.”
14 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang koe krax yum rhawm,
sang chix khiox khaw ka Siyiex yuh ka mawx lie?
Ang bax mhong,
sang chix yum rhawm yuh ka mawx lie?
Ang bax sidiex,
sang chix pon mhong yuh ka mawx lie?
15 eAng bax ploi hu,
sang chix pon sidiex ka yuh ka mawx lie?
Awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pui pa sidiex ka gum lox buan,
khaix caong kix kied sidaing mhawm heue!”
16 fKheu nan,
ang mawh pui ku kaux pa pon rieb ka gum lox buan.
Yi-sai-yax hoik sax krai ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
pui mawx chix yum rhawm ka lox pa sidiex ex lie?”
17 Ex chix tix yaox,
krax yum rhawm hoik kaoh khaing pa mhong,
mai pa mhong hoik kaoh khaing gum lox Khrit heue.
18 gViang mawh nan,
aux tag sang chawk ah nin:
rug ang kix sax mhong ka gum lox buan tete lie?
Kix hoik mhong ka heue.
Awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
lox krai kix hoik tauh ka daux kawn pui;
lox si kah kix hoik tauh ka caong daog haktiex heue.
19 hAux tag sang baox chawk ah nin:
pui Yi-seu-liex rug ang bax tawng lie?
Yam jauh,
Maw-si ran teei gum lox Siyiex pawk ka grawng in ah nin:
aux tag sang kieh “ceu pui pa ang sawn tix mawh” pui ceu aux,
kieh kix boh rhawm,
teei meung pa daox aw cheeik tix kieh pex song rhawm.
20 iYi-sai-yax nie kua rhawm tix ah nin:
aux hoik kieh pui pa ang bax sawg ka aux yaox tix,
mai hoik jhi tix ka pui pa ang bax khiox khaw ka aux heue.
21 jViang mawh nan,
phan mawh son pui Yi-seu-liex,
nawh ah nin:
aux nyeu taix tix leeing lang singaix,
son sang kieh ceu pui pa lieh lox pa kiien rhawm khankix deeh hoek ka tix heue.
