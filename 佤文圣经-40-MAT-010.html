Kawn cau kao ra kaux
(Lai Ma-kheu 3.13–19; Lai Lu-cia 6.12–16)
10
Ye-sux khrawm pui gau tix kao ra kaux ot tau,
tawx akhuang pa grheu khuan pa seeing brai mai pa pai moi ka jhawm saux ku ceu ka kix.
2 Plak greem mawh ceu kaux kawn cau kao ra kaux:
ka daux tix kaux mawh Si-meung (ceu kaux tang ceu nawh kok mawh Pi-teux),
kawn koe Ya-keux pux aik Ang-teu-liex,
kawn simex Si-pi-thaix,
Yaw-hang pux Ya-keux,
3 Fe-lix,
Pa-to-law-mai,
To-ma,
Ma-thaix pa koh keen,
Ya-keux kawn simex Ya-leu-fe,
Ta-thaix,
4 Si-meung cub caw pui pa kin jhawm,
kawn koe Yo-tax pui Cia-liiex kaw pa coh ka Ye-sux heue.
Kaing yuh pa tawx son kawn cau kao ra kaux
(Lai Ma-kheu 6.7–13; Lai Lu-cia 9.1–6)
5 Ye-sux ploi pui in kix kao ra kaux hu,
mai tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Paw vi hu ka haktiex pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai paw vi laig ka daux veng pui Sa-ma-li-yax.
6 Pex tag caw tix hu ka cub yung pa grai ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex dee an.
7 aDee pa hoik hu pex tag sang krai pao ah nin:
‘Meung rao ma tag sang lih hoek heue!’
8 Pex tag caw tix pai jhawm saux pui moi,
kieh pui yum deeh kaoh im,
kieh pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg pon singax,
grheu phi lih hu.
Pex hoik pon ka yie yie,
tag caw tix tawx hu yie yie nang.
9 Ka daux hau mau paw vi viex ka khri,
mau,
mai tawng khankix;
10 byam lih hu khaing nyiex paw vi viex bau mai kraung ceep plak daux ra pheen,
mai paw vi viex kiiep mai khaox gix.
Mawh kheu pui pa yuh kaing hoik mawh pa khaw tix pon ka ngawh riang tix heue.
11 “Yam hoek ka veng mai ka nyiex yaong veng iak,
caw tix ran nghiet tix mhong ka dee an koe pui mawx pa cu tix rieb ka pex,
pex tag caw tix paung ka nyiex ma nawh,
tom hoik lih hu pex khaing dee an.
12 Yam laig pex daux nyiex pui tix nyiex,
tag caw tix ah nin:
‘Kieh pex nyawm ot nyawm koe.’
13 Phan pui nyiex in rieb ka pex,
yam khiox khaw pex krax nyawm ot nyawm koe son kix,
tag sang lih hoek ka nyiex in.
Phan kix ang rieb ka pex,
pex tag caw tix deeh teei ka krax nawb buan pex.
14 cNyiex ma mai veng pa ang rieb ka pex,
pa ang nghiet ka lox pex an,
pex tag caw tix lih hu khaing dee an,
mai khruah phawng tiex pa koe ka kiiep pex cawd.
15 dAux krai ka pex tete te lai,
ka daux singaix pa prawng ca,
krax ciyang pa si pup veng Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la kawn lheu chaong khaing pa kham haktiex an heue!”
Krax tuk tiak tuk te pa tag sang lih hoek
(Lai Ma-kheu 13.1–13; Lai Lu-cia 21.12–17)
16 e“Tag bre ka rhawm tix heue!
Aux ploi pex lih hu,
awm laig yung ka daux cub phung sox la yuh nan.
Pex tag caw kieh tix lhak lhiao awm si uing yuh nan,
cu lox awm si kaux yuh nan.
17 fRien rhawm tix,
koe pui tag sang giex ka pex,
viex pex laig daux nyiex prawng see,
tag sang si vied pex ka daux nyiex sidiex kix.
18 Mawh kheu son aux,
pex tag sang viex tix hoek kham krax chawk sivoe ngai simiang mai simiang meung,
yuh mhai sakse gum lox buan ka kix mai pui tang ceu tang cad.
19 Yam chawk cheng kix ka pex,
paw vi khi rhawm ka lox pa sang krai tix,
phan ang mawh nan ka lox pa sang pawk tix;
yam an,
Siyiex tag sang gau pex ka lox pa sang khaw pex tix krai.
20 Mawh kheu lox pa krai pex,
ang mawh lox pa kaoh khaing to kaux pex,
mai hoik mawh gum lox pa tawx khuan Siyiex meung rao ma pex krai ka heue.
21 g“Pux aik tag sang coh ka pux aik,
mai kieh kix yum;
keeing tag sang yuh nin ka kawn simex kawn bun tix nang;
kawn simex kawn bun tag sang yuh krax pa tieh vheen ka miex keeing tix,
mai kieh kix yum.
22 hKheu son aux,
pui tag sang koeng ka pex;
viang mawh nan,
pui pa pon gun yeung tom si dauh tag sang pon krax taoh heue.
23 Phan kix yuh krax pa tuk tiak tuk te ka pex daux veng in,
pex to mox tix ka veng tang mu.
Aux krai ka pex,
sivoe sang tauh hu pex ka veng Yi-seu-liex,
kawn kawn pui tag sang hoek heue.
24 i“Pui gau ang lhaong khaing sara,
siliex ang lhaong khaing pui cao nyiex.
25 jMawh kheu nan,
krax pa si pup pui gau hoik awm pa si pup sara yuh nan,
krax pa si pup siliex cawng hoik awm pa si pup pui cao nyiex yuh nan,
mai hoik khaw tix naog rhawm kah.
Phan pui cao nyiex kok tix mawh simiang phi Piie-si-phux,
khex ang pui pa ot daux nyiex an sang kham krax kaig tua pa nie blawn ting lie?”
Paw vi lhat
(Lai Lu-cia 12.2–7)
26 k“Mawh kheu nan,
paw vi lhat ka pui,
kaing ceu pa glip khawm uik tag sang plih lih,
kaing pa mox kawx tag sang plih lih.
27 Pa hoik krai aux pex mhong dee glip,
pex tag caw tix krai lih ka greem pa riang singaix;
lox pa hoik mhong pex pa briex krai pui,
mai tag caw tix krai pao ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun.
28 An pa pon pauh ngie to kaux nex nham pex,
ang pon yuh ka khuan ngao pex laux leeing,
paw vi lhat kah;
pa caw pex tix lhat ka mawh Siyiex,
mawh ngie nawh pa pon taing khuan ngao pui mai to kaux nex nham pui khawm uik laig ka daux Maw-na-rhok.
29 Saik ra mu chix teei mau tawng tix plah pon liag kah,
viang mawh nan,
phan ang bax mawh ka daux alo pa tat keeing pex Siyiex,
tix mu kawx ang chix cawd ka piang tiex.
30 Phan mawh pex,
pie khawm hauk kaing pex kawx hoik simin ka khawm uik.
31 Mawh kheu nan,
paw vi lhat,
pex kawn lheu ting ngawh khaing sim saik tom nie heue!”
Krai pao tawng yung ka Ye-sux
(Lai Lu-cia 12.8–9)
32 “Pui pa krai tawng yung aux ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
aux kawx tag sang tawng yung nawh ka plak sivoe ngai keeing aux Siyiex meung rao ma;
33 lpui pa krai ang tawng aux ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
aux kawx krai ang tawng nawh ka plak sivoe ngai keeing Siyiex ka meung rao ma heue.”
Ang mawh pa nhim, hoik mawh lawm vaig
(Lai Lu-cia 12.51–53; 14.26–27)
34 “Paw vi ce aux viex pa nhim hoek ka piang haktiex;
aux ang viex pa nhim hoek,
mai viex lawm vaig hoek.
35 mAux hoek tix sang kieh kawn simex tieb tie ka keeing tix,
kawn bun tieb tie ka miex tix,
mun tieb tie ka miex mex tix.
36 Pui koeng hoik mawh pui ka daux nyiex ma to kaux tix heue.
37 “Pui pa muh miex keeing tix lheu khaing muh ka aux,
ang keung tix hawt khaix aux;
pui pa muh kawn praix tix lheu khaing muh ka aux,
ang keung tix hawt khaix aux.
38 nPui pa ang cu tix klawm khaox lhak to kaux tix hawt khaix caong aux,
ang keung tix hawt khaix aux nang.
39 oPui pa keud tix sang taoh ka ju tix,
tag sang tieh tix som ju;
pui pa som ju son aux,
tag sang tieh tix pon ju.”
Grawng khawng phak
(Lai Ma-kheu 9.41)
40 p“Pui mawx rieb ka pex hoik mawh rieb ka aux;
pa rieb ka aux hoik mawh pa rieb ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
41 Pa yuh son pui tix kaux kaux pa mawh kawn cau Siyiex,
mai rieb ka nawh,
tag sang pon tix cie ka buan son pa pon kawn cau.
Pa yuh son pui tix kaux kaux pa mawh pui rauh pui see,
mai rieb ka nawh,
tag sang pon tix cie ka buan son pa pon pui rauh pui see nang.
42 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
pui mawx ang ah,
pa teei tix tawx rawm si kied tix kawk ka pui gau pa blawn iak khaing paox tix nyaux,
ca mawh pui an pui gau aux,
nawh tag sang pon khawng phak heue.”
