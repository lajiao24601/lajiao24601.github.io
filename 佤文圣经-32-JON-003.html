Yaw-na nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex
3
Simiang Siyiex baox si kah ka Yaw-na tix bawg.
2 Nawh ah nin:
“Maix hu ka veng ting Ni-ni-we an,
mai krai pao lox pa tawm aux ka maix ka pui pa ot dee an.”
3 Yaw-na nghiet ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tom hu plak veng Ni-ni-we.
An mawh veng ting tix mu,
tag caw tix hu loe singaix tom taug tauh hu daux.
4 aYaw-na laig daux veng,
hu tix ngaix krax,
tom krai pao ah nin:
“Khaix hoik baox luan tipon singaix,
veng Ni-ni-we tag sang laux leeing heue!”
5 Pui pa ot daux veng Ni-ni-we yum rhawm ka lox Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix but rhawm tix sang tuah priex preem;
can pui pa ot daux veng khawm uik,
pui iak pui ting ang ah tag caw tix ceep kraung man keei sum jhi grawng pa laux rhawm tix heue.
6 Yam mhong simiang veng Ni-ni-we ka lox in,
nawh lih hu khaing dang ngeung ngawm tix,
peeik kraung dha simiang tix,
ceep ka man keei sum,
mai ngawm daux nyaox.
7 Nawh tawx lox hu ka pui veng Ni-ni-we ah nin:
“In hoik mawh gum lox tawm simiang mai simiang pa greem nawh ah nin:
pui,
khawng brie,
moi yung ang pon tix ih nyaux;
ang pon tix ih,
mai ang pon tix nyaux.
8 Kawn pui mai khawng brie tag caw tix ceep ka kraung man keei sum khawm uik.
Pui ku kaux tag caw tix khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
khaix an ang lai yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig,
paw yuh ka kaing pa tud pa rhim.
9 Phan yuh nin Siyiex tee rien tix deeh chix tieh loh ka rhawm keud tix,
mai ang lai baox song rhawm,
ex tom taug ang lai hoek krax laux leeing heue.”
10 Siyiex hoik yaox ka kaing pa yuh kix khawm uik,
hoik yaox ka pa tik vud kix ka kaing pa caox pa caig,
mawh kheu nan deeh tieh loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
mai ang lai tawx krax laux leeing pa hoik dawg krai pao tix lih hoek piang kix heue.
