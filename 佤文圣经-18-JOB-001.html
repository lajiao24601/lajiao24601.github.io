Lai Yaw-paw
Sa tan nyaw glaw Yaw-paw
1
Koe pui tix kaux ceu kaux nawh kaux Yaw-paw,
paung ot ka haktiex Wu-see;a
nawh mawh pui rauh see pa mhawm tix kaux,
mai lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
ang yuh kaing ceu pa ang mhawm tix ceu kawx.
2 Nawh koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
kawn bun loe kaux.
3 Nawh koe yung aliah rheng mu,
ka-la-uk loe rheng mu,
moi tix rheng mu,
la phuan yieh mu.
Lheu khaing an,
nawh kawn koe siliex tom haoh hun;
ka daux pui plak lih singax,
nawh sawn tix mawh pui pa kied mi khaing pui khawm uik heue.
4 Kawn simex Yaw-paw khankix lai loh tix yuh priex preem ghaok nyo si nie hoek ka nyiex cao tix jheeng jheeng,
mai ka pux o kix loe kaux kawx ghaok hoek ih nyaux tau mai kix.
5 Yam hoik luan bawg yam singaix yuh kix krax gaux rhawm ku bawg,
Yaw-paw tom kaoh khaing it tix cao cao,
yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing son kawn simex kawn bun tix ku kaux,
yuh been pa singax.
Nawh yuh nin jheeng jheeng,
mawh kheu nawh keud,
kawn simex kawn bun nawh khankix tee chix loi tix yuh kaing pa ang khaw phit lud ka Siyiex heue.
6 dKoe tix singaix,
kawn Siyiexb daux rao ma khankix cung ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai Sa tanc kawx ot ka daux kix.
7 Simiang Siyiex chawk Sa tan ah nin:
“Hu maix dee mawx lie?”
Sa tan pawk ah nin:
“Aux lai ling ka piang haktiex,
hu ka dee in,
hu ka dee an heue.”
8 Simiang Siyiex chawk ah nin:
“Maix yaox ang yaox ka siliex aux Yaw-paw lie?
Ka piang haktiex ang lai baox yaox pui pa awm nawh tix kaux kawx.
Nawh hoik mawh pui rauh see tix kaux,
lhat graung yung yi ka aux,
mai ang yuh kaing pa ang mhawm tix ceu kawx heue.”
9 eSa tan pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan ang keud son sang mhawm to kaux tix,
Yaw-paw kawn chix sang lhat graung yung yi ka maix yuh ka mawx lie?
10 Maix hoik kia ngien nawh,
khawm ka nyiex ma nawh,
mai can pa koe nawh khawm uik jheeng jheeng.
Maix hoik tawx buan ka nawh,
hoik kieh kaing yuh nawh mhawm ku ceu;
khawng eei brie liang nawh hoik naog ka daux tiag daux tu,
mai ang lai pon uik simin kah.
11 Yam in phan maix teei can pa koe nawh khawm uik,
kuan jhak tix yaox jhax ang nawh jhax lawg ka maix heue!”
12 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Sa tan:
“Chix tix,
can pa koe nawh khawm uik hoik ot ka daux tawm taix maix,
maix sang muh tix kieh nawh yuh ka mawx koe mai maix,
mawh ngie ang cu maix yuh ju nawh laux khaing.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Sa tan lih hu khaing plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
Yaw-paw som nai kawn simex kawn bun mai sin ngao tix khawm uik
13 Koe tix singaix,
kawn simex kawn bun Yaw-paw khankix nyaux plai gaux rhawm sawm rheep ka daux nyiex pux ke lhong kix,
14 koe pui to hoek krai ka Yaw-paw.
Nawh tom ah nin:
“Yam kawn taug thai moi ka daux kaung,
sawm la rib ot ka praok kaung khankix,
15 Pui See-paf hoek tix puing kix tix vud khaing,
mai tud khawng brie kix hu,
pauh ka siliex maix khawm uik,
deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing pa pon to tix luat luan,
mai hoek krai ka maix heue.”
16 Lox si kah nawh ang nyang bax uik,
baox koe siliex tang kaux tix kaux to hoek,
ah nin:
“Sah lih hoek khaing rao ma,
tuh ka phung yung maix mai pui liang yung maix,
deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing pa klawd,
mai hoek krai ka maix heue.”
17 Yam kawn si kah nawh,
baox koe siliex tix kaux pa hoek,
ah nin:
“Pui Cia-leu-tixg guah tix koe loe cub hoek puing ka yix,
mai tud ka ka-la-uk,
pauh ka can siliex maix khawm uik,
deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing pa pon to tix luat luan,
mai hoek krai ka maix heue.”
18 Lox si kah nawh ang nyang bax uik,
baox koe siliex tix kaux hoek,
ah nin:
“Yam kawn taug nyaux kawn simex kawn bun maix khankix plai gaux rhawm sawm rheep ka daux nyiex pux ke lhong kix,
19 koe bhaung ting phlu hoek khaing daux tiag,
mai phlu nyiex ghrup,
gut ka pui pa ot daux nyiex yum khawm uik,
deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing pa klawd,
mai hoek krai ka maix heue.”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-paw yaog tix kaoh,
laux rhawm mai riah ka kraung ceep tix grah,
khlao ka hauk kaing tix,
gup tix krub ka piang tiex,
21 ah nin:
“Aux keh viex taix yi tix,
mai deeh viex taix yi tix hu nang;
pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex deeh teei kah.
Ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex mawh pa khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw heue!”
22 Viang hoik si pup krax pa laux leeing in kix khawm uik,
Yaw-paw ang doh bax yuh ka phi map,
mai ang bax salao ka Siyiex heue.
