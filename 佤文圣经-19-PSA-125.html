Krax nyawm ot pui cie Siyiex
125
Pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex awm gawng Shi-ang,
kaux cong leeing ju,
kaux cong ang yung yang!
2 Awm can gawng lua pa sivie ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
Simiang Siyiex kawx hawt tix kia ngien ka pui cie tix yuh nan nang,
jauh yam in tom hoek leeing ju heue.
 
3 Pui cie Siyiex ang yaox tix kham krax pa giex gang pui koeng leeing ju;
phan ang mawh nan,
kix kawx tag chix lawng krax pa caox pa caig.
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka pui mhawm khankix,
tawx buan ka pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix.
5 Phan mawh pui pa lawng daux krax pa vawk pa kawk khankix,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kix yum tau mai pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig heue.
 
Kieh pui Yi-seu-liex pon krax pa nhim leeing ju leeing phan heue!
