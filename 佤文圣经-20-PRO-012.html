12
Pui pa muh tix koe ngai lig ngai lai gaux rhawm pui sidiex gau tix;
mawh ngie pui pa daox aw pa koeng ka lox pa sidiex.
2 Simiang Siyiex muh ka pui rauh pui see;
nawh ne raw ciyang ka pui pa yuh krax keud pa pie lhien.
3 Pa yuh pa caox ang pon kieh pui nyawm ot nyawm koe;
pui rauh pui see kaux cong ang yung yang.
4 Bun pa kied rien rhawm mawh mhok khri ka mex tix;
bun pa yuh krax pa kaig pa tua hoik awm muad ka daux si-ang mex tix.
5 Pui pa mawh pa meh kia ngien pui ka daux krax pa khaw caw;
pui pa caox caig tawng ngie krax pie lhien gut kheem.
6 Dak pui pa caox pa caig pon pauh ka pui;
lox pui rauh pui see pon taoh ka pui.
7 Pui caox pui caig grai hu,
mai kawn praix kix ang pon pre hu;
nyiex ma pui rauh pui see pon yaox tix kaux cong im ju jheeng jheeng.
8 Pui pa lhak lhiao tag sang pon krax pa yaog yaw pui;
pui pa daox aw tag sang pon krax pa map nai pui.
9 Pui ka daux haktiex khankix een rhawm tix ka daux pa yuh tix kaing,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing pui pa cai rhawm pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
10 Pui rauh pui see muh ka to sat;
pui pa caox pa caig sawg tix yuh khawng brie yum.
11 Pui haktiex pa rien yuh kaing khankix ang koe krax cud krax cha ka pa ih pa un;
pui pa leue tix sawg ka ceu pa giex pa going khankix hoik mawh pui pa daox aw tete.
12 Pui caox pui caig keud ce tix yuh kaing pa caox pa caig leeing lang singaix;
pui rauh pui see kaux cong ang yung yang.
13 Pui caox pui caig cawd ka daux jhawk lox pa krai cao tix;
pui pa mawh pa meh pon yaox tix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing.
14 Kawn pui pon khawng phak pa mhawm khaing lox pa krai kaing pa yuh tix;
nawh tag sang pon krax pawk bi pa khaw nawh tix pon.
15 Pui pa daox aw een cie tix kied khaw;
pui pa koe krax doh rhawm doh rhi nghiet ka lox pa sidiex pui.
16 Yam song rhawm pui pa daox aw,
dum mawx dum an hoik plih tix lih;
pui pa koe krax doh rhawm doh rhi pon gun yeung ka krax pa map nai pui.
17 Pa krai ka lox pa mawh pa meh hoik jhi ka pa khaw caw;
pa kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh hoik laux yuh pui mhawm.
18 Pa ang chix rien ka jheeh tix hoik awm vaig pa mat yuh pui;
lox mhawm pa kraix pui lhak lhiao hoik awm sidah pa chix pai pui moi.
19 Dak pa pie lhien tix vud tix cu khaing tag sang grai hu;
siyieb jheeh pa krai lox pa mawh pa meh tag sang koe leeing ju leeing phan.
20 Pa keud sawn ka kaing pa caox pa caig rhawm rhi kix mawh pa pie lhien;
pa yuh kieh lih koe krax pa nhim hoik naog ka krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
21 Pui rauh pui see ang yaox krax laux krax leeing;
pui pa caox pa caig yaox krax laux krax leeing ang jud ang jai.
22 Jheeh pa pie lhien,
Simiang Siyiex koeng kah;
giex kaux cong ka lox pa ap tix,
nawh tag sang pon krax pa gaux rhawm.
23 Pui pa lhak lhiao chix mox ka krax lhak lhiao tix;
pui pa daox aw hoik plih lih grawng ang tix koe krax lhak lhiao.
24 Taix pa rien tag sang pon giex ka siawp sibrawm;
pui pa nieh tag sang mawh pui pa yuh kaing kin.
25 Krax khi rhawm khi rhi kieh pui krawx rhao;
lox mhawm jhawm jiet kieh pui koe riang koe rao.
26 Pui rauh pui see mawh sara pa mhawm ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix;
pui pa caox pa caig hoik yie lud krax lawng tix jheeng jheeng.
27 Pui nieh ang pon hawt lox krai tix;
pui rien hoik koe mau khri tom nie.
28 Pa rauh pui see mawh cao pa thung pui hu daux krax pa im ju;
pa caox pa caig mawh pa viex pui hu daux krax yum heue.
