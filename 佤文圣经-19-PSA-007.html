Khiox khaw krax rauh krax seea
7
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
maix mawh nyiex dee deuh aux rhawm tix;
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa leue ka aux,
2 paw kieh kix awm sivai sang si pa giex ka aux,
riah aux phawng vid,
ang koe pui pa pon taoh teem ka aux heue.
 
3 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
phan aux koe ceu pa ang rauh ang see,
taix aux hoik naog ka phi map khawm ra khawn,
4 pawk pa caox ka piang pa mhawm pa yuh paox grawm tix,
pui koeng pa nieb pon yuh kaing pa ang mhawm ka aux,
5 kieh pui koeng leue ka aux,
giex ka aux;
kieh nawh kid aux goh,
pauh ka aux,
kieh aux goh tix yum ka daux phawng tiex heue!
 
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix yaog tix kaoh cung,
lauh pui pa koeng ka aux ka daux krax pa song rhawm.
Khiox khaw maix kaoh teem coi ka aux.
Kheu krax pa rauh see mawh pa khaw ka rhawm maix.
7 Kieh kawn pui piang haktiex hoek tix sivie ka maix;
kieh maix kuang ka piang kix.
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix prawng ca ka kawn pui piang haktiex.
Khiox khaw maix prawng aux ang koe phi map,
mawh kheu mawh aux pui ang koe dee lud.
9 bMaix mawh Siyiex pa khaw caw,
maix chawk cheng ka rhawm rhi tawm nhao kawn pui,
khiox khaw maix tieb tie ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui caox pa caig,
tawx buan ka piang pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm khankix heue.
 
10 Siyiex mawh dieb tie naok pa kia ngien ka aux;
nawh taoh ka pui rauh see pa mhawm daux vaig.
11 Simiang Siyiex mawh cao pa prawng pa rauh see;
nawh ciyang ka pui yuh pa caox pa caig leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
12 Phan kix ang loh rhawm rhi tix,
Siyiex tag sang klaing ka vaig tix lawm;
nawh vaik ak sau te,
13 mai hoik rien mhawm ka kraung naing pa teei ju pui yum,
tag sang puing ka te pa cap ngu ka heue.
 
14 Pui pa caox pa caig keud tix sang yuh ka kaing pa caox;
kix yuh krax pa suk sag,
mai pie lhien ka pui.
15 Kix kaong ka daux,
mai sang yuh pui tang kaux laux kah.
To kaux kix tieh tix deeh cawd ka daux.
16 Kix tag sang kham krax ciyang kheu pa caox pa caig tix,
hoik mat lheet kheu pa yuh tix kaing pa tud pa rhim heue.
 
17 Aux tag sang yaog yaw ka pa khaw caw Simiang Siyiex;
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux cao pa ting siawp sibrawm heue.
