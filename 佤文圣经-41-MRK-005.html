Ye-sux pai ka pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai daux moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.28–34; Lai Lu-cia 8.26–39)
5
Kix hu ka haktiex plak toi rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix,
pa mawh haktiex cie pui Keu-la-seung.
2 Yam lih Ye-sux khaing daux reu nawh yaox pui pa lih khaing daux si meeig tix kaux;
pui in mawh pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai daux,
3 nawh paung ot ka daux si meeig jheeng jheeng,
ang koe pui pa pon giex gang ka nawh,
maox ceng kawx ang pon phrawk ka nawh.
4 Pui hoik teei gang,
gang ka taix caong nawh tom nie bawg,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik tawk ka maox ceng max,
rud ka gang taix dut.
Ang koe pui pa ting riang tix pon kieh nawh nge.
5 Nawh ot ka daux si meeig leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
mai kok yo lox tix tom ting ka daux si nax gawng,
mai baox teei simaox lauh to kaux tix ka heue.
6 Nawh kok yaox Ye-sux ka dee singai,
mai nied tix to hoek,
grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux
7 kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Ye-sux kawn simex Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
yuh ka mawx maix lai hoek tix tuk tiak ka aux lie?
Aux khiox khaw maix ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex,
paw vi kha rhawm aux heue!” (
8 Nawh krai lox in mawh kheu hoik tawx Ye-sux lox ka nawh ah nin:
“Khuan pa seeing brai,
lih khaing to kaux pui an heue!”)
9 Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix kaux ceu pa tix?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Aux kaux ‘tix cub’,
mawh kheu hun kaux pui yix heue!”
10 Nawh baox khiox khaw Ye-sux tom nie bawg paw vi grheu kix lih hu khaing haktiex an heue.
11 Ka baoh dex blaong gawng dee an koe phung lig ih ceu pa ih;
12 khuan pa seeing brai tom khiox khaw Ye-sux ah nin:
“Grheu yix laig daux phung lig,
kieh yix ot ka daux lig heue.”
13 Ye-sux cu ka lox kix;
khuan pa seeing brai tom lih khaing to kaux pui an,
mai laig ka daux phung lig;
phung lig (koe been ra rheng mu) tom preeng tix lih ka yoeh rang,
mai laig daux nhawng,
yum khawm uik heue.
14 Pui pa liang lig tom yaog tix to;
kix ing ka daux veng mai ka nyiex yaong pa koe sivie siveng dee an,
mai krai ka grawh grawng in.
Pui hun sang jhak tix yaox mawh kaing ceu pa tix pa sang lih tix mawh.
15 Kix hoek ka Ye-sux dee an,
mai yaox ka pui an pa laig phi daux sivoe an ngawm ceep ka kraung,
rhawm rhi nawh haing,
kix tom lhat.
16 Pui pa yaox ka grawng pa lih kaing in tix mawh ka to kaux pui pa laig phi daux,
mai ka grawng phung lig krai ka pui hun.
17 Kix tom khiox khaw Ye-sux lih khaing haktiex tix heue.
18 Yam laig Ye-sux daux reu,
pui pa hoik laig phi daux sivoe an hoek tix khiox khaw ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kieh aux hu tau mai maix heue.”
19 Ye-sux ang cu,
viang mawh nan,
krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix ing ka nyiex,
krai ka paox nyiex maix,
Simiang Siyiex hoik kia maix mai yuh son maix yuh ka mawx ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix heue.”
20 Pui an hu,
mai jauh tix krai grawng kaing pa hoik yuh Ye-sux ka to kaux tix ka baoh haktiex See-yix;
pui pa mhong ang koe pa ang daox deng ka heue.
Kawn bun Ye-lu mai bun pa koe saux lih nham
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.18–26; Lai Lu-cia 8.40–56)
21 Ye-sux baox brug reu tiang nhawng hu plak an;
ka praok nhawng koe cub pui hun khrawm paox tix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
22 Ka daux haktiex an koe cao kuang nyiex sidiex tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Ye-lu,
nawh kawx hoek nang.
Yam yaox nawh ka Ye-sux,
nawh gup tix ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux,
23 mai khiox khaw ka nawh ah nin:
“Kawn bun aux saux dex sang yum,
khiox khaw maix deuh taix tix ka piang nawh,
pai nawh moi,
taoh ka ju nawh heue!”
24 Ye-sux tom hu tau mai nawh.
Koe pui tix blah hu tau mai nawh,
su shiet ka nawh heue.
25 Ka daux haktiex an koe bun tix kaux pa saux lih nham kao ra num,
26 hoik jhak ka cao sidah tom nie,
hoik kied kha rhawm tix sigrien sigre,
mai hoik uik yuh sin ngao nyiex ma tix;
viang mawh nan,
jhawm saux nawh lao sang moi,
mai tieh tix nie khaing tix ngaix hoek tix ngaix.
27 Nawh mhong grawng kaing pa yuh Ye-sux,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom pu plaik tix laig daux pui hun,
hoek ka plak khaix Ye-sux,
mai pu pi ka jaong sibex nawh,
28 nawh keud ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
“Phan aux pu pi ka jaong sibex nawh,
aux tag sang moi heue.”
29 Nham nawh tom hit dum mawx dum an,
nawh mhong grawng pa hoik moi jhawm saux khaing to kaux tix.
30 Ye-sux tawng hoik lih riang rao khaing to kaux tix dum mawx dum an,
tom kling kaing tix ka daux cub pui hun,
mai ah nin:
“Pui mawx pu pi ka jaong sibex aux lie?”
31 Pui gau nawh pawk ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
pui tom nie hoik su shiet ka maix,
yuh ka mawx mai lai chawk mawh pui mawx pa pu pi ka maix lie?”
32 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux kling ngai tix jhak plak goix plak dawm,
son sang tawng mawh mawx pa pu pi ka nawh.
33 Bun an hoik tawng grawng kaing pa lih tix mawh ka to kaux tix,
tom si yaung si kua tix grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux,
mai krai ka kaing ceu pa yuh tix lih khawm uik.
34 Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Kawn eue,
krax yum rhawm maix hoik taoh ka maix.
Ing mhawm mhawm heue,
jhawm saux maix hoik grai heue.”
35 Yam kawn si kah Ye-sux,
koe pui kaoh khaing daux nyiex cao kuang nyiex sidiex,
mai krai ka Ye-lu ah nin:
“Kawn bun maix hoik yum,
paw siao lai nyhap sara heue?”
36 Ye-sux ang simia ka lox pa hoik krai kix,
nawh krai ka pui cao kuang nyiex sidiex ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat,
yum rhawm khaing heue!”
37 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh viex Pi-teux,
Ya-keux,
mai Yaw-hang pux aik Ya-keux hu tau mai tix,
mai ang cu pui tang kaux hawt kix.
38 Yam hoek kix ka daux nyiex pui cao kuang nyiex sidiex,
Ye-sux yaox grawng pa suk sag kix,
krax yiiem kix.
39 Ye-sux laig daux ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai yiiem lie?
Kawn nyawm in ang bax yum,
mawh laig it ngie khaing heue.”
40 Kix nyiah ka Ye-sux,
Ye-sux tom grheu kix lih hu,
kieh ngie miex keeing kawn nyawm an mai pui gau nawh loe kaux laig daux lawg it kawn nyawm bun an.
41 Nawh bluh taix kawn nyawm bun an,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Ta-li-ta,
ku-mi!”
Dee bleeh mawh:
“Kawn nyawm bun,
aux tawx lox ka maix,
kaoh heue!”
42 Kawn nyawm bun an yaog tix kaoh hu dum mawx dum an (yam an kawn nyawm bun an hoik koe kao ra num).
Grawh grawng kaing in hoik kieh pui hun daox deng ka tete heue!
43 Ye-sux tawx lox ka kix sidawng,
ang cu kix krai hu ka pui,
mai baox tawx lox nhie kix tawx kawn nyawm in ih ka ceu pa ih khankix heue.
