Khaox gix Ya-loeng
17
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix nhie pui Yi-seu-liex tawx khaox gix ka maix kao ra gawng,
kieh simiang ku ceu pui teei lih tix kaux tix gawng,
mai tiiem ceu kaux tix ka piang.
3 Tiiem ceu kaux Ya-loeng ka piang khaox gix,
son tang pui Li-we.
Tix ceu pui simiang kieh koe tix gawng khaox gix.
4 Maix tag viex khaox gix in kao ra gawng hoek ka nyiex gox buan man,
deuh ot sivoe teug sijax mukheu,
pa mawh dee si pup maix mai aux paox tix.
5 Khaox gix cie pui pa laug aux an,
tag lih plaung.
Yuh nin,
aux sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai baox salao,
ang lai pon simah ka pex heue.”
6 Khaix an,
Maw-si tawx lox ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu,
nhie kix tawx khaox gix ka tix khawm uik kao ra gawng;
khaox gix cie Ya-loeng mai khaox gix cie pui tang kaux deuh ot tau.
7 Maw-si tom deuh khaox gix an khawm uik ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
sivoe teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex heue.
8 aNgaix khaix,
Maw-si hoek ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
mai yaox khaox gix cie Ya-loeng tang son Li-we hoik plaung,
lih mawh lawm,
pruh mawh tai,
mai plix khaox Batan pa teem!
9 Maw-si viex khaox gix khawm uik tawx pui Yi-seu-liex jhak;
kix hoik yaox.
Simiang ku kaux tang viex khaox gix cie tix ing.
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Deeh viex khaox gix Ya-loeng,
deeh deuh ka plak sivoe teug sijax mukheu,
priex ka khaox gix an,
kieh mawh mhai sakse son pui Yi-seu-liex pa tieh vheen.
Phan kix ca kawn salao,
kix tag yaox tix yum.”
11 Maw-si hawt tix yuh awm lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex heue.
12 Pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Ex hoik phaox heue!
13 Pui mawx pa hu dex ka nyiex gox buan man,
pui an tag yum.
Kheu nan,
ex tag yum uik heue!”
