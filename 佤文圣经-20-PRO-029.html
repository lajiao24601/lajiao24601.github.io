29
Pui pa yaox tix kham krax pa ne raw mai nied tix kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
tag chix laux leeing dum mawx dum an,
mai ang lai pon taoh ka heue.
2 Simiang mhawm chix priex ka meung,
kawn pui koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi;simiang caox giex kaing,
kawn pui koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi heue.
3 Pa muh ka ping nyia cuyi,
keeing kied nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi;pa yuh paox pie tau mai bun coh kaux,
tag kieh sin ngao nyiex ma tix singax pak lawg.
4 Simiang pa prawng ka daux pa rauh pa see,
mai meung ot nawh tag sang ting lhaong;pa ih ka mau pa seeing brai,
mai meung ot nawh tag sang laux leeing.
5 Pa chix nyaw glaw ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
hoik awm nih tix taong rub ka to kaux tixa.
6 Pui caox pui caig cawd ka daux o phi map;pui rauh pui see pon yaox krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux pa luat luan.
7 Pui rauh pui see yung yi ka siawp sibrawm pui hot pui sibhawm;pui pa caox pa caig ang koe krax pa chix caox rhawm yuh nin.
8 Pui pa cau nyhau kieh veng suk sag;pui pa lhak lhiao yuh haktiex koe krax yien krax yawg.
9 Pui pa lhak lhiao deuh phi map ka pui pa daox aw;pui pa daox aw tawng ngie tix chix nyiah ka daux krax pa jhax lawg.
10 Pa pauh pui yum koeng ka pui pa rauh pa see;pui rauh pui see mai chix kia ngien ka ju tix mhawmb.
11 Pui pa daox aw chix jhi krax pa song rhawm tix lih khaing pak ngai tix;pui pa lhak lhiao koe rhawm awn rhawm tiiem.
12 Phan koe simiang tix kaux kaux muh tix mhong ka lox pie lox lhien,siliex nawh rien tix mawh pui pa chix pie lhien.
13 Pui hot mai pui tud pui rhim koe dee awm kix mawh:Ngai kix hoik mawh pa tawx Siyiex awm paox tix.
14 Phan koe simiang tix kaux kaux pa chix kia ngien ka siawp sibrawm pui hot pui sibhawm,meung pa giex nawh tag sang pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem.
15 Kham krax sidiex gau pa si vied,
tag nie pon ping nyia cuyi;kawn nyawm pa ang chix priex rhawm rhi tix tag kieh miex tix kaig tua.
16 Pui caox pui caig giex kaing,
kieh phi map haok huan,viang mawh nan,
pui rauh pui see tag sang yaox grawng pa laux leeing kix.
17 Pa chix sidiex gau ka kawn simex tix,
nawh tag sang kieh maix pon krax pa nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi tom si dauh heue.
18 Phan ang koe krax pa duk thung Siyiex,
kawn pui tag sang hu krax pa ang khaw caw;pui pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex kied koe buan son tete heue!
19 Phan sidiex gau ka siliex tix,
ang pon tix sidiex gau ka lox tix khaing,
viang nawh hoik tawng kawx mai ang tie hawt heue.
20 Pui pa nieb pon krai lox,
kawn lheu ang koe krax keud pot jun pot khaing pui daox aw.
21 Pa ghram kaing yuh siliex tix,
tag kieh nawh nieb pon yuh kaing pa ang khaw tom yam iak tix,
koe tix ngaix nawh tag sang teei ka sin ngao pa koe maix khawm uik heuec.
22 Pui pa hax rhawm yie cheeik simah lih,
mai yuh krax pa suk sag.
23 Pui pa cau nyhau kieh pui laux leeing;pui pa tiiem rhawm awn rhi pon krax pa nab yam pui.
24 Pa yuh paox pie tau mai pui briex pui lak,
hoik mawh pa yuh krax tieh vheen ka to kaux tix;yam ot nawh ka daux nyiex prawng see kawx,
mai nawh ang pon krai ka lox pa mawh pa meh.
25 Nawh lhat ka lox pa ah pui hoek piang tix.Pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe.
26 Pui ku kaux simie tix pon krax muh simiang,mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex pa pon yuh krax pa rauh pa see.
27 Pui rauh pui see ang rhawm yaox ka pui pa ang rauh ang see;pui pa caox pa caig koeng ka pui pa rauh pa see heue.
