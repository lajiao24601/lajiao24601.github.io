Lai Lox kab
Krai dawt
Lai lox kab mawh gum lox lai pa khrawm kaing ceu pa mhawm mai pa sidiex gau been li, pa hoik teei gum lox tawx satix tiiem lih mawh, kaing ting plak nie mawh pa dex pa dawt tau mai krax ot krax koe kawn pui. Phuk lai in jauh tix krai ah nin ka pui pa phat: “Pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex mawh dee jauh ping nyia cuyi.” Khaix an mai si doe tix krai lao been li mai kaing pa ceu pa mhawm ka daux kawn pui, mai kawn krai nang been pa khaw kawn pui tix hawt khankix. Gum lox pa tawx satix pa dawt ka daux phuk lai in, hoik jhi grawng ping nyia pa hoik yuh pui Si-paw-lai tom dix dix: awm pa mawh ka daux nyiex ma pui, kaing yuh pui, been ot pui, mai pa pheuk ka to kaux tix khankix. Ka daux phuk lai in kawx kawn krai nang ka krax pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi, krax pa lang gun lang yeung, krax pa yung yi ka pui hot pui sibhawm, mai krax pa mhawm ka daux pa yuh paox yuh pie tau mai paox grawm paox grux tix khankix heue.
Phuk lai sigang khri pa khraox hoik yie siao ka gum lox pa sidiex gau Lai Lox Kab heue.
Krai kaing
1. Lox rhax krax pa yaog yaw ka ping nyia cuyi (1.1–9.18).
2. Gum lox tawx satix Saw-law-meung (10.1–29.27).
3. Gum lox ping nyia pa tawx Ya-ku-eu (30.1–33).
4. Gum lox pa sidiex gau tang ceu (31.1–31).
