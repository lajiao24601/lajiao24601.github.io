18
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 anawh ah nin:
“Gum lox prim in pa hoik krai lao pui hu ka daux haktiex Yi-seu-liex,
dee bleeh mawh ceu pa tiex lie?
Kix ah nin:
Keeing hoik ih ka plix siiet pa niex,
rhang kawn simex hoik kied si ki ka heue.”
3 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai yuh nin:
“Aux hoik tiex tu ka ju tix leeing ju yuh nin:
ka daux haktiex Yi-seu-liex ang doh lai baox krai lao hu ka gum lox prim in.
4 Ju pa im kawn pui ku kaux hoik mawh cie aux;
mawh ju miex keeing ang ah,
kawn praix ang ah hoik mawh cie aux khawm uik.
Pui mawx yuh phi map,
pui an tag sang yum heue.
5 “Phan koe pui mhawm tix kaux pa rauh see mai pa khaw caw,
6 nawh ang krub ka riang rao cie pui Yi-seu-liex,
ang ih nex tan lu ka nyiex vad pa ot piang gawng,
ang nyaw glaw ka moeng pui tang kaux,
ang it tau mai bun yam hoek khix lih nham seeing bun,
7 ang pie lhien,
ang tud rhim.
Nawh deeh tawx kraung pa tang khankix ka cao nyiex,
nawh tawx pui pa cai rhawm sawm,
mai tawx pui pa ot ka kaong ceep kraung heue.
8 Nawh tawx pui yaog mau ang teei ka rao,
ang yuh kaing pa caox pa cai,
mai prawng khaw caw ka simah simaing.
9 bPui pa yuh nin cu ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
mai hoik priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux.
Nawh mawh pui mhawm;
nawh tag sang im ju tete.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
10-11 “Phan pui in koe kawn simex tix kaux,
nawh mawh pui pa tud rhim,
pui pa pauh pui yum,
yuh kaing pa caox caig pa ang keeing nawh nyang sax yuh.
Nawh hoik ih nex tan lu ka nyiex vad piang gawng,
mai hoik nyaw glaw ka moeng pui tang kaux.
12 Nawh hoik pie lhien ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
hoik tud rhim ka pui,
teei kraung pa tang cie pui tang kaux khankix.
Nawh hu krub ka riang rao krub been li tang ceu ka nyiex vad,
13 mai tawx pui yaog mau son sang teei ka rao.
Pui pa yuh nin sang pon yaox tix im ju lie?
Ang,
nawh ang pon!
Nawh hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig in kix khawm uik,
nawh tag sang yum tete;
nawh tang deeh yuh tix yum heue.
14 “Phan pui in koe kawn simex tix kaux,
mai nawh yaox ka phi map pa yuh keeing tix khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang hawt ka kaing pa ang mhawm pa yuh keeing tix.
15 Nawh ang krub ka riang rao krub pui Yi-seu-liex,
ang ih nex tan lu ka nyiex vad piang gawng.
Nawh ang nyaw glaw ka moeng pui tang kaux,
16 ang gut kheem ka pui,
ang tud rhim.
Nawh tawx kraung pa tang pui ka cao nyiex,
tawx pui pa cai rhawm sawm,
tawx pui pa ot ka kaong ceep kraung.
17 Nawh ang yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig,c
tawx pui yaog mau ang teei ka rao.
Nawh hoik priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
hoik nghiet ka gum lox ping nyap aux.
Nawh tag ang caw tix yum kheu phi map pa hoik yuh keeing tix.
Nawh tag sang yaox tix im ju tete.
18 Phan mawh keeing nawh,
hoik pie lhien,
hoik tud rhim,
hoik nieb pon yuh tix ka piang pui,
mai nawh tag sang yum kheu phi map pa hoik yuh tix heue.
19 “Viang mawh nan,
pex chawk ah nin:
kawn simex yuh ka mawx lai ang kha rhawm tix kheu phi map pa hoik yuh keeing tix lie?
Lox pa pawk aux mawh nin:
kawn simex hoik yuh kaing pa khaw caw;
nawh hoik priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
hoik yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa nhie aux pui yuh,
mawh kheu nan nawh tag sang im ju tete.
20 dMawh ngie pui pa yuh phi map pa khaw tix yum.
Kawn simex ang caw tix kha rhawm son phi map pa yuh keeing tix;
keeing kawx ang caw tix kha rhawm son phi map pa yuh kawn simex tix.
Pui mhawm tag sang pon buan son kheu kaing pa yuh tix;
pui caox tag sang kham krax ciyang kheu pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix heue.
21 “Phan pui caox loh rhawm rhi tix,
tik vud ka can phi map pa yuh tix khawm uik,
priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
yuh kaing ceu pa khaw,
nawh tag ang yaox tix yum,
tag sang im ju tete.
22 Phi map pa hoik yuh nawh hoik pon pa pluat ploi;
mawh kheu hoik yuh nawh kaing pa khaw nawh tix yuh,
nawh tag sang yaox tix im ju tete.
23 Pex ce aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong muh tix yaox grawng pa yum pui caox lie?
Ang!
Aux muh tix yaox grawng pa im ju nawh kheu hoik loh rhawm rhi tix.
24 “Phan pui mhawm ang lai baox yuh ka kaing ceu pa mhawm,
mai yuh kaing ang mhawm pa yuh pui caox pa hoik kieh pui seeing brai kah,
nawh kawn sang yaox tix im ju lie?
Ang!
Kaing mhawm pa yuh nawh sivoe an pui ang lai doh keud kah,
nawh tag sang yum ka lieh lox,
ka phi map heue.
25 “Viang mawh nan,
pex ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex yuh nin ang khaw heue!’
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
bre rhawm rhi nghiet ka lox pa kah aux heue!
Pex ce pa yuh aux ang khaw lie?
Tete te lai,
mawh pa yuh pex pa ang khaw heue!
26 Pui mhawm ang lai baox yuh kaing pa mhawm,
mai tieh tix hu yuh kaing caox,
nawh yum hoik mawh kheu kaing caox pa yuh tix.
27 Pui caox loh rhawm rhi tix,
jauh tix yuh kaing pa khaw caw,
tag sang deeh pon taoh ka ju tix.
28 Nawh hoik lam lia ka to kaux tix,
loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
mawh kheu nan tag ang caw tix yum,
mai tieh tix pon krax pa im ju.
29 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex krai ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex yuh nin mawh pa ang khaw heue!’
Pex ce pa yuh aux mawh pa ang khaw lie?
Tete te lai,
mawh pa yuh pex pa ang khaw heue!
30 “Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai ka pex yuh nin:
aux tag sang hawt tix prawng ca ka pex awm kaing pa yuh pex kaux mawx kaux an.
Pex tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
lih hu khaing krax pa caox pa caig,
son ang phi map pex sang yuh pex laux leeing.
31 Tag caw tix tik vud ka kaing pa ang mhawm pa yuh pex khawm uik,
mai loh rhawm pa khraox,
khuan pa khraox heue!
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
yuh ka mawx lai kieh tix yum lie?
32 Aux ang simie pui tix kaux kawx yum.
Phan pex loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
mai pex tag sang yaox tix im ju.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
