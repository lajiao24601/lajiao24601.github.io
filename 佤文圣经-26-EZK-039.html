Keu-keu ang pex
39
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka simiang piang meung Mi-seux mai meung Thu-pa in kiex ra meung pa mawh Keu-keu.
Tag caw tix krai ka nawh,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
aux tag sang tieb tie ka maix.
2 Aux tag sang deeh kling maix deeh,
mai thung maix lih hu khaing haktiex plak laong,
viex maix hoek ka gawng cie pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
3 “Khaix an,
aux tag sang lauh ka taix khawn goix maix pa jhawp ka ak,
mai taix khawn dawm maix pa giex ka te.
4 Maix mai pui naing cie maix,
khawm pui naing pa rom pex tix tau tag sang yum ka piang gawng pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
Aux tag sang teei tix tawx to kaux yum pex mawh priex preem pa ih sim mai to sat khankix.
5 To kaux yum pex tag sang naog ka daux tiag daux tu.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin.
6 Aux tag sang ploi ngu gleeing ka haktiex Ma-keux,
mai ka pui pa paung ot hawt rawm sigrit sigra khankix.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
7 Aux tag sang kieh pui cie aux tawng sidawng ka ceu kaux pa singax singeh aux tete,
aux ang doh lai baox kieh ceu kaux tix hoek krax kaig krax tua.
Yuh nin,
meung ka piang haktiex tom taug tawng yung aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
hoik mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh heue.”
8 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Ka daux singaix pa hoik grox krai aux an tag sang lih tix hoek tete.
9 Pui pa paung ot ka veng Yi-seu-liex ku veng,
tag sang hu sut ka kraung naing pa hoik tik khankix,
kieh mawh khix taung.
Kix tag sang teei ka dieb tie naok,
ak,
te,
pliah,
khaox dung khankix mawh khix taung;
kraung in kix hoik kub tix taung aliah num.
10 Kix ang siao hu sut khix ka daux tiag,
mai ang siao hu kid khix ka daux nong khaox,
mawh kheu hoik koe kraung naing pa hoik tik pui khankix,
mai kieh mawh khix taung.
Kix tag sang tud rhim ka pui pa hoik tud rhim ka kix sivoe an,
tag sang con ka pui pa hoik con ka kix sivoe an.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kieh lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Si paung ka Keu-keu
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Khaix hoik lih kaing in tix mawh,
aux tag sang ot ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
ka daux ‘ngawd dee ot pui’ pa koe ka plak lih singaix ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
mai sawg haktiex si meeig tix dauh tawx ka Keu-keu.
Keu-keu mai pui naing cie nawh tag sang si paung ot dee an khawm uik.
Ngawd in tag sang kok mawh ‘ngawd pui naing Keu-keu’.
12 Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang teei bawg yam aliah khix si paung ka can to kaux yum khawm uik,
son sang kieh haktiex an singax singeh.
13 Kix tag sang nhie pui ka daux meung khawm uik hoek tix si paung;
ka daux singaix bawg yam pa pon pex aux,
kix tag sang pon krax pa yaog yaw pui kheu kaing in.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
14 Khaix hoik luan aliah khix,
kix tag sang ploi pui ca lai ling tix hu jhak ka haktiex ku dauh,
mai cox si paung mhawm ka to kaux yuma pa bhlien tix lih plak praix,
yuh nin tom taug chix kieh haktiex singax singeh.
15 Kix lai ling tix jhak ka daux meung khawm uik,
yam yaox kix ka si-ang kaing pui yum,
kix tom mhai ka praok si-ang pui an,
son sang kieh pui pa kaong si meeig si paung nawh ka ‘ngawd pui naing Keu-keu’.
16 (Koe yaong tix mu pa dex ka dee an,
tag sang sum yaong an hawt awm ceu kaux pui naing.)
Yuh nin,
haktiex tom taug deeh chix singax singeh heue.”
17 bSimiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix kok yo sim,
to sat ku ceu hoek tix ih nex tan lu pa hoik rien khre aux son kix khaing pon praok si daix daog.
Gawng pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag sang yuh ka poi,
kix tag sang ih nex nyaux nham ka dee an.
18 Kix tag sang ih ka nex pui naing,
nyaux ka nham simiang naing khankix.
Pui naing in kix tag sang awm yung mhaing,
kawn yung,
pex,
moi mhaing,
mai moi kluing Pa-sang pa tok pui tix hu pauh yuh nan.
19 Khaix hoik pauh ka pui naing in kix awm pauh ka khawng tan yuh nan,
sim to sat tag sang sau riang tix ih nex,
nyaux nham,
tom hoik sak hoik yoek tix.
20 Kix tag sang sak ka ih nex brung,
nex pui naing brug brung,
mai nex can pui naing pa ot ka piang pheen aux khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Pa haok huan pui Yi-seu-liex
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh meung ka piang haktiex yaox min plak ngai aux,
kieh kix yaox grawng pa teei aux riang rao tix prawng ca yuh kaing pa khaw caw.
22 Jauh yam in,
pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix.
23 Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang tawng ka pui Yi-seu-liex kheu pa phit lud kix ka aux,
aux tom ploi kix hu.
Aux tik vud ka kix,
ang sang kia ngien ka kix,
kieh pui koeng puing ka kix,
pauh ka kix.
24 Aux yuh krax pawk bix kheu pa seeing brai kix,
mai pa caox caig kix.
Aux tik vud ka kix,
ang kia ngien ka kix heue.”
25 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang caox rhawm ka doeh ceu cad Ya-keux pa mawh pui Yi-seu-liex,
kieh kix deeh cox sum tix ot nyiex ma,
aux tag sang priex ka ceu kaux pa singax aux.
26 Kix deeh cox paung ot ka haktiex cie tix,
ang lai baox koe pui pa ghlat ka kix,
kix tag sang piawm ka krax kaig krax tua pa kham kix kheu hoik tieh vheen kix ka aux.
27 Son sang kieh meung ka piang haktiex tawng grawng mawh aux pa singax singeh,
aux tag sang teei kix khaing pui koeng dee an mai thung kix deeh hoek ka haktiex tix.
28 Yam an,
pui cie aux tag sang tawng yung aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix;
mawh kheu hoik ploi aux kix hu sivoe an,
yam in deeh khrawm ka kix,
mai viex kix deeh hoek ka haktiex tix,
pie tix kaux kawx ang lai deuh kah.
29 Aux tag sang teei khuan tix hieh ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ang doh lai baox tik vud ka kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
