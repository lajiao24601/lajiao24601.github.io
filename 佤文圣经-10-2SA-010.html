Ta-wue pon pex ka pui Ya-meung mai pui Shui-li-yax
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 19.1–19)
10
Khaix hoik luan been tix baux bawg yam,
pui Ya-meung simiang pa kaux Na-sia hoik yum.
Kawn simex nawh pa kaux Ha-nuing plud tix yuh simiang.
2 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Aux sang yuh paox grawm tau mai Ha-nuing,
awm hoik yuh keeing nawh Na-sia paox grawm tau mai aux yuh nan.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom ploi kawn cau tix hu khu rhawm nawh.
Yam hoek kix,
3 simiang pui Ya-meung ah nin ka simiang Ha-nuing:
“Maix ce Ta-wue ploi pui in kix hoek mawh son sang nab yam yung yi ka simiang sivoe,
mai son sang khu rhawm rhi maix lie?
Ang doh mawh nan!
Nawh ploi pui in kix hoek tin mawh son sang jiang tix yaox ka kaing pa koe ka daux veng in,
son sang pon kuang kix ka heue!”
4 Ha-nuing tom giex ka kawn cau pa ploi Ta-wue hoek,
mai khlao ka hauk jheeh kix tix plak,
mai baox tat dut ka sibex kix tom beem kre,
khaix an grheu kix lih hu khaing daux haktiex.
5 Pui in kix yaox krax map krax nai,
kied kaig kied tua kah,
mai ang pon ing ka nyiex.
Ta-wue mhong grawng in,
nawh tom nhie pui hu krai kix mhong kaog ot ka Yie-li-keu,
krox jun hauk jheeh tix baox deeh kuah,
mai tag deeh ing khaix heue.
6 Pui Ya-meung hoik tawng ang kix khaw tau mai Ta-wue,
kix tom khrawm pui naing Shui-li-yax pa hu ka caong tix ra mheen kaux khaing Paw Li-haw mai Saw-pa,
khaing Thaw-pawx tix mheen ra yieh kaux,
mai baox vai tix siao khaing simiang Ma-cia dee an tix rheng kaux.
7 Ta-wue hoik tawng ka grawng in,
tom tawx lox nhie Yaw-ya thung pui naing khawm uik hu puing.
8 Pui Ya-meung ploi pui naing lih,
mai kieh diang siviex veng Yi-li-pa mawh dee rien khre kix tix sang puing ka naing.
Pui naing Shui-li-yax tang kaux mai pui pa kaoh khaing Thaw-pawx mai Ma-cia khankix dia tix ot ka vung viang veng plak praix heue.
9 Yaw-ya yaox pui naing pa koeng hoek puing kix ka plak ngai khawm plak khaix,
tom laug pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa kied kua khankix,
mai kieh kix dia tix puing ka pui naing Shui-li-yax,
10 mai ap pui naing pa deuk khankix ka pux tix pa kaux Ya-pi-sai mawh cao thung.
Ya-pi-sai kieh cub pui naing in kix dia tix puing ka pui Ya-meung.
11 Yaw-ya ah nin ka Ya-pi-sai:
“Phan maix yaox ting riang pui Shui-li-yax khaing aux,
maix tom hoek tix taoh teem ka aux.
Phan pui Ya-meung ting riang khaing maix,
aux tom taoh teem ka maix.
12 Yuh tix kaux cong kua rhawm,
mai sau riang tix yuh naing son paox ceu paox cad ex mai veng cie Siyiex!
Kieh Simiang Siyiex yuh alo tix lih mawh heue.”
13 Yaw-ya mai pui naing cie nawh puing hu sivoe ngai.
Pui Shui-li-yax hoik yaog tix to.
14 Pui Ya-meung yaox hoik yaog pui Shui-li-yax tix to,
kix kawx yaog tix to khaing Ya-pi-sai,
mai deeh ing ka daux veng.
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya lih khaing bang pui naing Ya-meung,
deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
15 Pui Shui-li-yax yaox ang tix pex pui Yi-seu-liex,
kix tom deeh cox khrawm pui naing.
16 Simiang Ha-ta-ti-shiex ploi pui hu khrawm pui naing Shui-li-yax pa ot ka plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti hoek,
kix tom hoek ka Si-lang ka daux pa thung cao kaing pui naing Ha-ta-ti-shiex pa kaux Saw-fax.
17 Ta-wue hoik tawng kah,
nawh tom ploi pui naing Yi-seu-liex tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai hu plak Si-lang,
pa mawh dee pa hoik dia pui naing Shui-li-yax tix sang puing ka Ta-wue.
Krax naing jauh tix,
18 pui Yi-seu-liex pheet ka pui naing Shui-li-yax deeh.
Ta-wue mai pui naing cie nawh pon pauh ka pui pa hawt tix grheu ka ling law aliah yieh kaux,
mai pui naing brug brung pon mheen kaux,
mai baox pon pauh ka simiang naing pui koeng pa kaux Saw-fax yum ka daux bang naing.
19 Simiang pa ot greem Ha-ta-ti-shiex khankix yaox ang tix pex ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
kix tom yuh krax pa khaw rhawm mai pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai hawt ka rhawm kix.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Shui-li-yax ang lai pon joh jhan pui Ya-meung puing naing heue.
