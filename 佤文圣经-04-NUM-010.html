Mawk teet tawng mau
10
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix tawk mawk teet tawng ra mu ka mau,
son sang mawh pa khrawm pui haoh pui hun mai pa siao ka yam gah pui ka nyiex bang.
3 Yam phlu ka mawk teet tawng an khawm ra luah lox lang,
cub pui hun khawm uik tag khawm paox tix hoek ka sivoe diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
hawt vung viang maix.
4 Phan phlu ngie ka mawk teet tawng luah tix mu khaing,
mawh ngie son simiang pa kuang ka piang ceu pui ceu mawx ceu an,
pa khrawm paox tix hoek ka plak sivoe maix.
5 Yam phlu luah ka lox ngaing pa dhiet,
ceu pui pa sum tix ot nyiex bang ka plak lih singaix,
tag caw gah ka nyiex bang cie tix hu.
6 Yam phlu luah ka lox ngaing daux bawg ra,
ceu pui pa sum tix ot nyiex bang ka plak caong ma seh,
tag caw gah ka nyiex bang cie tix hu.
Mawh kheu nan,
yam phlu lox luah ngaing,
mawh son pa nhie gah ka nyiex bang,
7 yam phlu luah ka lox lang,
mawh son sang khrawm ka pui haoh pui hun.
8 Mawk teet tawng an khawm ra,
tag sang mawh kawn praix Ya-loeng pa yuh cao giex kaing li pa phlu heue.
“Been in plak greem,
pex caw hawt tix yuh ku ju leeing ju.
9 Yam si pup pex krax naing ka daux haktiex tix,
son sang rien khre pex ka to kaux tix,
mai tieb tie ka pui koeng pa hoek tix puing ka pex,
pex tag caw tix phlu ka mawk teet tawng an luah khawm ra,
son sang so pui hu puing naing.
Aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag sang teem ka pex,
mai taoh pex luat luan khaing pui pa koeng.
10 Ka daux singaix gaux rhawm gaux rhi pex,
yam haok khix,
yam yuh pex poi tang ceu,
yam yuh pex Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
pex tag caw tix phlu ka teet.
Aux tag sang teem ka pex,
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex yaog tix hu krax
11 Khaix hoik lih hu ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing haktiex Ai-cix ka daux num ra khix ra tix nga ngaix,
pai awm pa ot ka piang nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex hoik yaog tix haok.
12 Khaix an,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex jauh tix yaog tix lih hu krax,
mai lih hu khaing daux tiag Si-nai.
Pai awm hoek ka daux tiag Pa-lang,
mai kaog ot tan heue.
13 Kix hawt lih hu awm gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
14 yam lih hu kix miex bawg ang ah,
mai dia tix hawt paox tix awm bawg sivoe.
Pa jauh tix lih hu sivoe paox tix,
mawh pui pa ot greem man chix cie Yo-tax,
cub mawx cub an.
Na-suing kawn simex Ya-mi-na-ta mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Yo-tax;
15 Na-thang-yiex kawn simex Su-yax mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Yi-sa-cia;
16 Yi-li-ya kawn simex Si-loeng mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Si-pu-loeng.
17 Khaix hoik yieh nyiex gox buan man,
Keu-soeng mai Mi-la-lix ra ceu pui mawh cao pa klawm ka nyiex gox buan man hu heue.
18 Khaix an pui pa ot ka greem man chix cie Lui-piien cub mawx cub an lih hu.
Yi-li-su kawn simex See-tio-eux mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Lui-piien;
19 Su-li-sa-taix kawn simex See-lu-mi mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Si-miien;
20 Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Tiu-eu mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Cia-teux heue.
21 Khaix an,
ceu pui Keu-siax lih hu,
mai klawm kraung ceu pa singax singeh.
Yam hoik hoek kix ka dee ot nyiex bang,
nyiex gox buan man hoik deeh cox sum tix jung mhawm mhawm heue.
22 Khaix an mawh pui pa ot greem man chix cie Yi-fa-liien cub mawx cub an pa lih hu.
Yi-li-sa-ma kawn simex Ya-mi-fu mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Yi-fa-liien;
23 Cia-ma-liex kawn simex Pi-ta-su mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Ma-na-si;
24 Ya-pi-tang kawn simex Ci-to-ni mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Piien-ya-ming heue.
25 Pa nghoik tix hu khaix paox,
mawh pui pa ot greem man chix cie Tang,
pa mawh kru naing cao kuang khaix,
mai cub mawx cub an lih hu.
Ya-si-yi-shiex kawn simex Ya-mi-sa-tai mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Tang;
26 Pha-ciex kawn simex Eu-lang mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Ya-seux:
27 Ya-si-la kawn simex Yi-nang mawh cao bung kaing ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix.
28 In mawh grawng khaix hoik gah ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka nyiex bang,
mai cub mawx cub an dia tix hawt khaix paox tix hu sivoe heue.
29 Maw-si ah nin ka Heu-pa kawn simex Lio-eu ceu pui Mi-tiien pa mawh pux aik nawh khankix:
“Ex sang lih hu ka dee pa hoik ap Simiang Siyiex tix sang tawx ka ex.
Nawh hoik ap sang kieh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex haok huan,
mawh kheu nan hu tau mai ex,
mai ex chix kieh maix pon krax pa haok huan heue.”
30 Heu-pa ah nin:
“Aux ang hu.
Aux sang deeh ing ka haktiex paox ceu paox cad tix.”
31 Maw-si ah nin:
“Viex maix paw vi hu khaing ex liak,
maix hoik tawng ka daux tiag in,
ex sang yuh bang ka dee mawx,
maix chix tix mawh cao thung ex hu.
32 Phan maix hu tau mai ex,
buan son pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka ex,
ex tag sang mhawm tau mai maix heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu
33 Cub pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing gawng Si-nai Simiang Siyiex,
hoik luan been loe singaix krax;
teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex hu sivoe,
son sang sawg ka dee sum bang son kix.
34 Kix jauh khaing tix bang gah tix hoek khaing tix bang.
Pon ngaix,
pai awm Simiang Siyiex koe ka piang kix.
35 aYam lih hu teug sijax mukheu yam mawx ang ah,
Maw-si ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kaoh heue,
kieh pui koeng maix prung prang rai rhuat;
kieh pui koeng to sivoe maix.”
36 Yam kaog teug sijax mukheu,
Maw-si ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh hoek ka dee ot ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kaing rheng kaing mheen liak.”
