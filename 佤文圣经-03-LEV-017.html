Nham pa kied singax
17
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si
2 nhie Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
khawm ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix khawm uik krai ka lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem hu.
3-4 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lheu khaing pauh tix tan ka moi mhaing,
yung,
pex mhaing ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
son sang kieh mawh khawng tan ka Simiang Siyiex khaing,
phan nieb teei alo tix pauh ka khawng tan an tang dauh,
tag mawh pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
Kheu pa hoik kieh nawh nham jauh,
tag sang teei tix tik ceu kaux nawh khaing ka daux ceu pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex.
5 Lox tat jhawm prawng in kaing ting mawh son sang nhie ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix viex khawng tan hoek tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Singaix pa hoik luan,
kix hoik pauh ka to sat ka daux tiag daux tu,
yam in tag caw tix viex ka khawng tan hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
mai tawx ka cao giex kaing li,
son sang tan mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
6 Cao giex kaing li tag phruah nham khawng tan an ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
hawt vung viang khu dee tan khawm pon daog,
ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
mai gleeing ka khlawp bee,
kieh taox bhawng pa muh Simiang Siyiex ka khankix lih.
7 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag ang lai baox deeh lieh lheu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pauh ka khawng tan daux tiag,
ap pong ka phi pex.
Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tag caw tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng in leeing ju leeing phan heue.
8-9 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka daux kix,
lheu khaing tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
mai khawng tan tang ceu pa tawx ka Simiang Siyiex khaing,
phan nieb hawt alo tix tan khawng tan tang dauh,
tag sang teei tix tik ceu kaux nawh lih khaing ka daux ceu pui Siyiex heue.
10 aPhan koe pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax kix ang ah,
ih nex pa koe nham ka daux,
Simiang Siyiex tag veeh pak ngai tix khaing nawh,
mai tag tik ceu kaux nawh khaing ka daux ceu pui Siyiex.
11 bJu to tiag ot ka daux nham,
mawh kheu nan Simiang Siyiex tom tawx lox,
caw tix hieh nham ot ka piang khu dee tan,
son sang pluat ploi ka phi map kawn pui,
nham hoik mawh ju,
chix tix pluat ploi ka phi map.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom ang cu ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka daux kix ih nex pa koe nham heue.
13 Phan koe ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ang ah,
pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax pex ang ah,
pon ka to tiag pa singax singeh mai sim ang ah,
nawh tag caw teei tix tik ka nham,
kheem ka phawng tiex.
14 Ju to tiag ot ka daux nham,
mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom ang cu pui Yi-seu-liex ih ka nex pa koe nham khankix;
pui mawx lieh lheu,
pui an tag sang teei tix tik khaing ka daux ceu pui Siyiex heue.
15 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu tang cad ang ah,
mawh pui mawx ang ah,
pa ih ka nex to tiag pa lawk yum,
mai nex pa hoik kiied to sat khankix phawng,
nawh tag caw tix si daux ka kraung ceep tix,
heem tix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang krox jun tom hoek ka daux bo singaix an,
tom tag sawn mawh pa singax singeh.
16 Phan nawh ang si daux ka kraung ceep tix,
ang heem tix,
nawh tag sang klawm ka phi map to kaux tix heue.
