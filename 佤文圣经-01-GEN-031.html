Ya-keux to khaing La-pang
31
Ya-keux mhong lox pa salao kawn simex La-pang khankix ah nin:
“Ya-keux hoik teei sin ngao cie keeing ex hu khawm uik;
sin pa koe nawh hoik mawh sin keeing ex.”
2 Ya-keux hoik tawng grawng ang La-pang lai chix muh tix awm singaix pa hoik luan.
3 Yam in,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Maix deeh ing ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
dee ot tax du maix mai paox ceu paox cad maix;
aux sang ot tau mai maix heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-keux ploi pui tawx lox ka Le-ciiex mai Li-yax,
nhie kiex hoek yaox paox tix ka dee liang tix phung yung.
5 Nawh krai ka kiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawng grawng ang keeing pax lai chix muh aux awm singaix pa hoik luan;
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex keeing aux hoik ot tau mai aux ku jheeng jheeng.
6 Pax ra kaux hoik tawng,
aux hoik sau riang rao tix khawm uik,
yuh kaing son miex keeing pax;
7 kheu nan,
nawh pie lhien ka aux,
mai hoik tieh ka ngawh riang aux kao bawg;
viang mawh nan,
keeing aux Siyiex ang cu nawh ih riang yie aux.
8 Phan La-pang ah nin:
‘Kawn pex pa preeh,
kieh mawh ngawh riang maix’,
phung yung hoik lih keh pa preeh khawm uik;
phan nawh ah nin:
‘Kawn pex pa bawd,
kieh mawh ngawh riang maix;’
kawn pex hoik lih keh pa bawd khawm uik.
9 Siyiex teei ka phung pex cie keeing pax tawx ka aux heue.
10 “Koe tix bawg,
daux yam si keeing phung pex paox tix,
aux ca maox tix yaox ka pex pa si keeing paox tix,
koe pa preeh,
pa bawd,
pa lai.
11 Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin ka daux ca maox:
‘Ya-keux eue!’
Aux deeh pawk ah nin:
‘Aux ot tin.’
12 Nawh ah nin:
‘Maix jhak,
can kawn pex mhaing pa si keeing lih koe pa preeh,
pa bawd,
pa lai.
Aux kieh kaing in lih tix mawh,
mawh kheu kaing pa yuh La-pang ka maix,
aux hoik yaox kah.
13 aAux mawh Siyiex pa jhi maix yaox tix ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
maix hoik sum simaox tan tix khlawp kieh mawh pa doh keud,
mai groi rawm bee sime ka piang,
kheu nan mhaing lox ka aux dee an.
Yam in,
maix caw rien tix deeh ing ka haktiex dee keh tix heue.’ ”
14 Le-ciiex mai Li-yax pawk lox Ya-keux ah nin:
“Yiex ang tie sang chix pon plud tid tix ka keeing tix.
15 Nawh hoik kieh yiex awm pui tang,
coh ka yiex,
mai baox bleet ka ngawh yiex.
16 Sin ngao pa teei Siyiex khaing keeing yiex,
hoik mawh cie ex mai son kawn simex kawn bun ex.
Maix yuh hawt awm lox pa ah Siyiex ka maix heue.”
17-18 Kheu nan,
Ya-keux hoik rien tix,
sang deeh ing haktiex Cia-nang dee ot keeing tix Yi-sha.
Nawh kieh kawn simex kawn bun tix mai moeng tix brug ka-la-uk,
mai hawt grheu ka can khawng brie pa koe tix,
khawm sin ngao pon khrawm tix khaing haktiex Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax khawm uik mai viex hu.
19 La-pang hu kip hauk yung;
Le-ciiex jawm yam ang keeing tix ot ka nyiex mai briex tix teei riang krub cie pui nyiex keeing tix khankix khaing daux nyiex.
20 Ya-keux briex mox tix lih hu khaing La-pang.
21 Nawh viex ka can pa koe tix khawm uik,
nied tix hu laung laung,
mai tiang ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
hu haktiex ka plak gawng Ci-liex heue.
La-pang leue ka Ya-keux
22 Khaix hoik luan loe singaix,
koe pui krai ka La-pang Ya-keux hoik to.
23 La-pang viex pui tix leue ka Ya-keux.
Kix leue ka aliah singaix,
tag krup Ya-keux ka gawng Ci-liex.
24 Ka daux bo an,
Siyiex hoek ka La-pang daux ca maox,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix rien tix!
Paw nieb pon ghlat ka Ya-keux.”
25 Yam an,
Ya-keux yuh nyiex bang ot ka piang gawng;
La-pang mai pui nyiex nawh kawx yuh nyiex bang ka gawng Ci-liex heue.
26 La-pang ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai mox tix ka aux,
viex kawn bun aux hu awm pui pa laung pui yuh nan lie?
27 Yuh ka mawx maix lai mox tix ka aux,
pie lox tix gum ang krai nieb pon ghlua tix hu lie?
Phan maix krai aux mhong lox tix gum,
aux sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix tawx maix hu,
mai tauh kraung tauh ting,
ah lox rhax tawx maix hu.
28 Maix yuh nin kied mawh kaing pa daox,
pie bawg yam sang ngheet aux kawn saux tix mai kawn bun tix,
sivoe sang guah ex paox tix,
maix ang kieh koe.
29 Tete aux koe rhawm tix yuh maix laux,
viang mawh nan,
daux bo kawx,
Siyiex keeing maix hoik ah ka aux,
ang cu aux nieb pon ghlat ka maix.
30 Aux tawng grawng braong doe maix tix sang deeh ing ka plak nyiex,
viang mawh nan,
yuh pa tix maix lai briex ka riang siyiex daux ceu pui nyiex aux khankix lie?”
31 Ya-keux pawk lox:
“Aux keud maix viex yuh krax naing mai teei kawn bun tix khaing daux tawm taix aux,
aux tom kied lhat graung kah.
32 Viang mawh nan,
phan maix yaox riang siyiex an ka pui mawx,
chix tix kieh pui an yum;
ka plak ngai pui nyiex pui ma,
phan maix yaox ceu pa tix pa mawh cie maix,
maix teei hu.”
Tete,
Ya-keux ang tawng Le-ciiex hoik briex ka riang siyiex een keeing tix La-pang khankix heue.
33 La-pang laig sawg ka daux nyiex bang man Ya-keux,
mai baox laig sawg ka daux nyiex bang ot Li-yax mai bun siliex nawh ra kaux,
mai ang yaox tid tix;
kheu nan,
nawh laig ka daux nyiex bang ot Le-ciiex.
34 Le-ciiex teei tix mox riang krub an kix daux chee an tang ka-la-uk,
mai nawh ngawm ka piang.
La-pang hoik tauh sawg ka nyiex bang,
ang yaox tid tix.
35 Le-ciiex ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Maix paw vi song rhawm.
Aux kawn ot khix lih nham seeing bun,
ang chix yaog tix kaoh cung ka plak sivoe ngai maix.”
La-pang hoik tauh sawg mai ang yaox ka riang siyiex pui nyiex nawh khankix.
36 Kheu nan,
Ya-keux song rhawm mai ah nin ka La-pang:
“Mawh phit aux ka ceu tix,
yuh krax pa ang khaw yuh ka mawx?
Maix hawt leue aux yuh nin lie?
37 Maix hoik sawg ka kraung aux khawm uik,
mawh hoik yaox maix kraung nyiex ma tix tix ceu ceu lie?
Teei lih deuh sivoe ngai pux aik aux mai pux aik maix,
kieh kix prawng si nax ax mawh cie mawx pa khaw.
38 Aux hoik ot tau mai maix tix nga num,
yung mai pex maix ang sax laux kawn tix bawg kawx,
aux ang sax ih ka nex kawn pex maix pie tix mu kawx.
39 Kawn yung kawn pex pa kiied to tiag khankix,
aux ang sax viex hoek ka maix;
mai kham sakse krax lud ang koe piang aux.
Viang mawh nan,
to kaux aux hoik plud kah,
pon ngaix pon sawn ang ah,
phan pui briex hu,
maix hoik kieh aux plud kah.
40 “Aux hoik kham gee hax koek hax ngu pon ngaix jheeng jheeng,
mai hoik kham gee kuat lhong si duad praix plak pon sawm,
ang yaox tix laig it laig tom.
41 Aux hoik ot daux nyiex maix yuh kaing hoik naog tix nga num:
son sang pon tix moeng ka kawn bun maix ra kaux,
aux yuh kaing maix kao num pon;
son sang pon aux tix cie phung pex cie maix,
aux yuh kaing maix liah num.
Viang hoik yuh nin kawx,
maix kawn tieh ka ngawh riang aux kao bawg.
42 Phan ang mawh Siyiex keeing aux,
pa mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang mai Siyiex pa lhat graung yung yi Yi-sha pa ot tau mai aux,
maix tee ploi aux ing mai taix yi tix tete.
Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik yaox ka krax kham gee aux,
mai kaing pa hoik yuh aux ka taix tix,
ka daux bo kawx nawh ah ka maix heue.”
Ya-keux yuh sijax mukheu mai La-pang
43 La-pang deeh pawk lox Ya-keux:
“Kawn bun in kix mawh kawn bun aux,
mai kawn praix in kix kawx mawh kawn praix aux,
khawm cub phung pex khankix kawx,
mawh cie aux;
can pa yaox maix khawm uik kawx mawh cie aux.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux ang lai pon giex kawn bun tix mai kawn saux tix ot,
44 Lheu khaing yuh aux sijax mukheu tau mai maix.
Aux pum simaox tix pum,
kieh mawh sakse pa yuh ax sijax mukheu heue.”
45 Kheu nan,
Ya-keux teei simaox tix lon mai sum mawh mhai pa doh keud.
46 Nawh nhie pui nyiex tix sut simaox,
kieh mawh tix pum.
Khaix an,
pui haoh pui hun sawm rheep ot tan praok pum simaox.
47 La-pang kok ceu kaux simaox in mawh Yi-cia-eu Sa-ha-du-tha.b
Ya-keux kok mawh Cia-loe-teuxc.
48 La-pang ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Daux in singaix,
simaox in kieh mawh mhai sakse sijax mukheu pa yuh ex si nax aux mai maix.”
Kheu nin,
haktiex in tom kok mawh Cia-loe-teux.
49 La-pang baox ah nin:
“Khaix hoik tang hu ex,
kieh Siyiex lam lia ex.”
Kheu nin,
haktiex in tom kok mawh Mi-see-pad.
50 La-pang baox dee ah nin:
“Phan maix nieb pon yuh ka kawn bun aux,
phan ang mawh nan,
baox teei tix koe moeng tang kaux,
viang pui ang tawng,
maix caw tix doh keud,
Siyiex hoik lam lia ka ex.
51 Aux pum simaox in si nax maix mai aux;
kieh mawh mhai pa doh keud.
52 Kieh pum simaox mai rong simaox pa doh keud in mawh sakse ka ex,
aux tag ang pon luan khaing mee pum simaox in son sang tuk tiak ka maix,
maix kawx tag ang pon tix luan khaing mee pum simaox in mai rong simaox pa doh keud in,
son sang tuk tiak ka aux.”
53 Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang mai Siyiex Na-heuxe sang prawng ot si nax ax.
Khaix an Ya-keux tiex tu ka Siyiex pa lhat graung yung yi keeing tix Yi-sha,
caw tix hawt ka lox pa ap tix deuh in.
54 Nawh pauh khawng brie yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka piang gawng,
mai kok ka pui nyiex tix sawm tau;
khaix hoik sawm,
kix it ka piang gawng.
55 Ngaix khaix plak pon ngawb cao cao,
La-pang ngheet ka kawn saux tix mai kawn bun tix,
khaix an lih hu khaing tan,
mai ing ka nyiex tix heue.
