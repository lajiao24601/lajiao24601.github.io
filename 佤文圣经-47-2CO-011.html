Pao-law mai kawn cau pa pie lhien
11
Aux simie pex chix gun yeung ka aux,
viang sawn aux mawh pui daox aw kawx ceeix ceeix,
aux yum rhawm grawng kawn sang chix gun yeung pex ka heue!
2 Aux muh ka pex tom hoik pon krax pa boh rhawm,
awm hoik yuh Siyiex ka pex yuh nan.
Mawh kheu hoik awm pex kawn bun krih pa singax singeh yuh nan,
aux sang kieh pex koe mex tix kaux,
pa mawh Khrit.
3 aAux lhat pex laux leeing,
mai tik vud ka krax muh ka Khrit pa singax,
awm krax pie lhien pa hoik nyaw glaw si uing ka Sha-wa yuh nan.
4 Mawh kheu nieb koe pui ku ceu tix sidiex ka Ye-sux tang kaux,
ang mawh kaw pa sidiex ex an,
pex lai gun yeung ka khawm uik;
koe khuan tang ceu mai gum lox buan tang ceu,
ang awm ceu pa hoik sidiex ex ka pex,
pex kawx hoik gaux rhawm tix rieb ka heue!
5 Aux hoik yum rhawm grawng ang aux doh ang lheu khaing “kawn cau pa blawn ting” cie pex khankix heue!
6 Aux tee ang hiex krai lox,
viang mawh nan,
phan mawh ka ngai lai aux ang cud cha khaing pui tang kaux;
grawng in aux hoik jhi pex yaox lih ka kaing pa yuh tix ku ceu heue.
7 Aux sidiex gum lox buan Siyiex ka pex,
mai ang bax teei ceu pa tid tix ka pex;
aux kieh to kaux tix tiiem,
son sang yaog pex lhaong.
Khex mawh aux pa lud lie?
8 Yam yuh aux kaing ka daux pex,
koe ceu cao tang dauh hoik tawx ka ceu pa lo aux;
aux awm pa gut kheem ka ceu cao tang dauh mai hoek tix yuh kaing son pex.
9 bYam ot aux tau mai pex,
sang lo ceu pa tix,
ang sax khiox khaw pex teem coi tix.
Ceu pa lo aux khawm uik,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing kawn Khrit Ma-chi-toeng pa tawx ka aux.
Singaix pa hoik luan mawh yuh nan,
singaix pa sang hoek kawx kawn mawh yuh nan.
Aux ang doh chix lih mawh beeh glawm ka pex heue!
10 Aux pon krai ka Khrit pa ot ka daux rhawm aux ah nin,
ceu pa yaw aux rang di tag sang mawh pa sidiex ka daux haktiex Ya-kai-yax khawm uik.
11 Yuh ka mawx aux krai yuh nin lie?
Khex ang aux muh ka pex lie?
Siyiex hoik tawng grawng muh aux ka pex heue!
12 Aux tag sang nied tix yuh kaing pa yuh tix yam in,
son sang kieh pui an kix pa ah mawh tix “kawn cau” ang lai koe lox pa yaw rang di,
kieh kix tawng pa yuh tix hoik awm pa yuh aux yuh nan.
13 Cub pui in kix ang mawh kawn cau tete;
kaing pa yuh kix mawh pa pie lhien,
kix hoik pie lhien tix mawh kawn cau Khrit.
14 Tete te lai,
ceu in kix kawx ang yuh tix daox deng kah,
pie Sa tan kawx chix yuh tix mawh kawn cau Siyiex pa mhawm riang heue!
15 Mawh kheu nan,
phan lien Sa tan yuh tix mawh siliex pa rauh pa see,
ceu in kix kawx ang yuh tix daox deng kah.
Dee si dauh cie kix,
tag sang khaw mai kaing ceu pa yuh kix heue.
Pao-law kham krax pa kha rhawm
16 Aux baox ah nin:
pui tix kaux kawx ang pon tix kieh aux awm pui daox aw yuh nan.
Phan pex sang kieh aux awm pui pa daox aw yuh nan,
pex kieh heue,
son sang yaox aux tix yaw rang di tix vud.
17 Gum lox pa krai aux yam in ang mawh pa sang krai kaing ting;
aux yaw rang di yuh nin,
hoik awm ngie pui pa daox aw yuh nan.
18 Kheu hoik koe pui tom nie pa teei kaing pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex yaw rang di kah,
aux kawx hoik khaw tix yaw rang di tix vud nang.
19 Pex hoik mawh pui kied lhak,
yuh ka mawx lai kawn gaux rhawm tix gun yeung ka ceu pa daox aw heue!
20 Pui mawx ang ah pa kieh pex yuh siliex cawng,
ih riang yie pex,
yuh pex laux,
map nai ka pex,
mai baox si mang ka dawng yhaok pex,
pex hoik pon gun yeung ka khawm uik.
21 Kieh aux krai lox pa kaig ngai tix gum,
kaing pa yuh nin ex ang koe riang rao tix pon yuh heue.
Viang mawh nan,
phan koe pui pa pon viex rhawm ting tix yuh kaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
kieh aux ang siao ih dieb dong tix krai lox,
aux kawx taug chix koe rhawm ting pa yuh nin nang.
22 Kix hoik mawh pui Si-paw-lai lie?
Aux kawx hoik mawh nang.
Kix hoik mawh pui Yi-seu-liex lie?
Aux kawx hoik mawh nang.
Kix mawh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang lie?
Aux kawx hoik mawh nang.
23 cKix hoik mawh siliex Khrit lie?
Krai lox cau nyhau,
aux kawx nie mawh nang heue!
Kaing yuh aux nie blawn kha tix,
bawg yam pa ot daux thawng blawn nie,
mai nie blawn kham krax pa si vied jheeng jheeng,
nie blawn kham pa dex mai krax yum.
24 dPui Yo-thaix si vied ka aux phuan bawg,
tix bawg si vied tix ngoe dim taix;
25 epui Law-ma lauh aux ka khaox nguh loe bawg,
pui tawk aux ka simaox tix bawg,
si pup krax laux ka daux rawm sigrit sigra loe bawg,
lu lax ka daux rawm tix nga pon taix nari tix bawg.
26 fKa daux krax lawng aux tom nie bawg,
aux hoik kham krax pa dex yum ka pa kheem rawm,
pui con,
pa kaoh khaing pui Yo-thaix mai pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai baox kham krax pa dex yum ka daux veng,
ka daux tiag,
ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
mai ka pa dex yum ka pa yuh pux aik pux o pa pie lhien khankix.
27 Aux hoik koe krax pa taug mao kha rhawm tix ka daux pa yuh tix kaing,
ang laig it laig tom leeing sawm jheeng jheeng,
kham krax pa simie tix nyaux rawm,
pa cud cha ka priex preem,
ang koe dee paung,
kraung ceep ang kub tix lhop ka to kaux.
28 In kix kawn ang krai,
aux kawn khi rhawm ka son kaing ceu cao ku dauh,
awm beeh glawm pa gut piang to kaux aux ku singaix yuh nan.
29 Koe pui mawx iak riang,
aux ang iak riang lie?
Koe pui mawx goh tix yuh phi map,
aux ang koe krax pa khi rhawm ka lie?
30 Phan aux sang yaw rang di,
aux cu tix yaw rang di ka kaing ceu pa jhi ka krax pa iak riang to kaux tix.
31 Keeing Simiang Ye-sux,
Siyiex hoik mawh pa khaw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka leeing ju;
nawh hoik tawng ang aux pie lhien.
32 gYam ot aux ka Ta-ma-see-keux,
cao kuang kaing pa ot greem simiang Ya-li-ta,
ploi pui pau ka siviex veng,
mai sang giex ka aux.
33 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui teei khrawng ting ploi aux lih khaing daux doh guh veng,
mai kieh aux bheeik luan khaing daux tawm taix nawh heue.
