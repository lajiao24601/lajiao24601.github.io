Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix
Jauh ka Ya-tang hoek ka Ya-paw-la-hang
(Lai Yam-jauh 5.1–32; 10.1, 32; 11.10–26)
1
Ya-tang keh Sai-theux;
Sai-theux keh Yi-naw-seex;
Yi-naw-seex keh Kai-nang;
2 Kai-nang keh Ma-leu-liex;
Ma-leu-liex keh Ya-liex;
3 Ya-liex keh Yi-naw;
Yi-naw keh Ma-thu-sha-la;
Ma-thu-sha-la keh La-maix;
4 La-maix keh Naw-yax;
Naw-yax keh kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Sang,
Hang,
Ya-fu heue.
5 Doeh ceu pui Ya-fu mawh pui Keu-miiex,
pui Ma-keux,
pui Ma-taix,
pui Ya-wang,
pui Thu-pa,
pui Mi-seux,
pui Thi-la.
6 Doeh ceu pui Keu-miiex mawh pui Ya-seu-ci-na,
pui Li-fax,
pui Thaw-cia-max.
7 Doeh ceu pui Ya-wang mawh pui Yi-li-sa,
pui Tha-see,
pui Ci-thix,
pui Law-teux heue.
8 Doeh ceu pui Hang mawh pui Ku-seu,
pui Ai-cix,
pui Fu,
pui Cia-nang.
9 Doeh ceu pui Ku-seu mawh pui Si-pa,
pui Ha-fe-la,
pui Sa-fu-tha,
pui La-max,
pui Sa-fu-thi-cia.
Doeh ceu pui La-max mawh pui See-pa,
pui Ti-tang. (
10 Ku-seu koe kawn simex tix kaux mawh Ling-lu,
nawh mawh pui vao pa jauh tix koe ka daux been tix kaux.)
11 Doeh ceu pui Ai-cix mawh pui Lu-tix,
pui Ya-na-mi,
pui Li-ha-pi,
pui Na-fu-thu-si,
12 pui Pha-see-lu-six,
pui Cia-see-lu-si,
pui Cia-fe-thaw (pui Fe-li-see hoik mawh doeh ceu cad pui in).
13 Doeh ceu cad Cia-nang mawh pui Si-toeng (Si-toeng hoik mawh kawn ai),
pui Heux.
14 Cia-nang kawx mawh nang tax du pui Yie-pu-see,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Keu-cia-sha,
15 pui Si-we,
pui Ya-ci,
pui Si-ni,
16 pui Ya-wa-ti,
pui Si-ma-lix,
pui Ha-ma.
17 Doeh ceu pui Sang mawh pui Yi-lang,
pui Ya-sux,
pui Ya-fa-sa,
pui Lu-teux,
pui Ya-lang,
pui Wu-see,
pui Hu-leux,
pui Ci-thiex,
pui Ma-see.
18 Ya-fa-sa keh Sa-la;
Sa-la keh Si-paw.
19 Si-paw koe kawn simex ra kaux:
tix kaux mawh Fa-leuxa,
mawh kheu ka daux baux bawg yam nawh,
pui piang haktiex hoik prung prang tix ot ku daux ku dee;
an tix kaux mawh Yaw-thang.
20 Doeh ceu pui Yaw-thang mawh pui Ya-maw-ta,
pui Sa-liex,
pui Ha-sa-ma-fe,
pui Yie-la,
21 pui Ha-to-lang,
pui Wu-sax,
pui Teu-la,
22 pui Eu-pa-lux,
pui Ya-pi-ma-lix,
pui See-pa,
23 pui A-fe,
pui Ha-fe-la,
pui Yaw-pa heue.
24 Doeh ceu pui Sang hoek ka Ya-paw-lang tiiem tix ot plak greem:
mawh Sang,
Ya-fa-sa,
Sa-la,
25 Si-paw,
Fa-leux,
La-wu,
26 Si-lux,
Na-heux,
Tha-la,
27 Ya-paw-lang (mai deeh loh ceu kaux mawh Ya-paw-la-hang) heue.
Doeh ceu pui Yi-seu-ma-lix
(Lai Yam-jauh 25.12–16)
28 Ya-paw-la-hang koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Yi-sha mai Yi-seu-ma-lix.
29 Kawn simex Yi-seu-ma-lix mawh simiang ceu pui in kix plak greem kao ra ceu:
Ni-pai-yaw (kawn ai Yi-seu-ma-lix),
Ci-tax,
Ya-teu-piie,
Mi-pi-sang,
30 Mi-see-max,
Tu-max,
Ma-sha,
Ha-tax,
Thi-max,
31 Yi-thu,
Na-fe-see,
Ci-ti-max heue.
32 Moeng iak Ya-paw-la-hang pa kaux Ci-thu-la keh nawh cie kawn simex liah kaux,
pa kaux Sing-lang,
Yaw-sang,
Mi-tang,
Mi-tiien,
Yi-see-pa mai Su-yax.
Yaw-sang koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh See-pa mai Ti-tang.
33 Mi-tiien koe kawn simex phuan kaux,
pa mawh Yi-fa,
Yi-fu,
Ha-nawx,
Ya-pi-tax mai Yi-leu-tax heue.
Doeh ceu pui Yi-sao
(Lai Yam-jauh 36.1–19)
34 Kawn simex Ya-paw-la-hang pa kaux Yi-sha koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Yi-sao mai Ya-keux.
35 Kawn simex Yi-sao mawh Yi-li-fa,
Lio-eu,
Yie-wu-see,
Ya-lang,
Kheu-la heue.
36 Yi-li-fa mawh tax du ceu pui Thi-mang,
A-mawx,
Si-paw,
Cia-thang,
Ci-na-see,
Thing-na,
Ya-ma-lix khankix.
37 Lio-eu mawh tax du ceu pui Na-ha,
Shie-la,
Sa-ma,
Mi-sha khankix heue.
Doeh ceu pui Si-eux
(Lai Yam-jauh 36.20–30)
38-42 Pui pa dawg ot haktiex Yi-tong,
mawh doeh ceu cad kawn simex Si-eux khankix:
Law-thang mawh tax du Heu-lix mai Si-mang ra ceu pui.
Law-thang koe nan tix kaux mawh Thing-na.
Saw-pa mawh tax du ceu pui Ya-leu-weung,
Ma-na-sia,
Yi-pa-lu,
See-paw,
A-nang khankix.
Ci-piien koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Ya-ya mai Ya-na.
Ya-na mawh keeing Ti-soeng;
Ti-soeng mawh tax du ceu pui Sing-tang,
Yi-see-pang,
Yi-lang,
Ci-lang khankix.
Yi-ca mawh tax du ceu pui Pi-hang,
Sa-fang,
Ya-kang khankix.
Ti-sang mawh tax du Wu-see mai Ya-lang ra ceu pui heue.
Simiang Yi-tong khankix
(Lai Yam-jauh 36.31–43)
43-50 Sivoe ang simiang pui Yi-seu-liex lih koe yam an,
simiang in kix plak greem lai plud paox tix giex ka kaing piang Yi-tong:
Pui Thing-ha-pa kawn simex Pi-eux pa kaux Pi-la.
Pui Paw-see-la kawn simex Shie-la pa kaux Yaw-pa.
Hu-sang pui haktiex Thi-mang.
Pui Ya-wui-teu kawn simex Pi-tax pa kaux Ha-tax (nawh pon pex ka pui Mi-tiien ka daux naing Maw-ya).
Pui Ma-see-li-cia pa kaux Sang-la.
Pui Li-heu-paw pa ot praok klawng ting pa kaux Sao-law.
Kawn simex Ya-keu-paw pa kaux Pa-leu Ha-nang.
Pui Pa-wu pa kaux Ha-tax (moeng nawh Mi-si-tha-piiex mawh kawn bun Ma-theu-liex,
kawn saux Mi Sa-hawx).
51 Simiang ceu pui Yi-tong ku ceu pui mawh:
Thing-na,
Ya-leu-wa,
Yie-thie,
52 A-heu-li-pa-ma,
Yi-la,
Pi-nuing,
53 Ci-na-see,
Thi-mang,
Mi-pi-sa,
54 Ma-ci-tiie,
Yi-lang khankix heue.
