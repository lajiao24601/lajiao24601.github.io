[Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix]
6
Maix pa kied sidaing mhawm grang ka daux nyawm bun in,
Pui pa muh maix hu dee mawx lie?
Krai ka yix liak,
hu nawh krax plak mawx,
son sang pon teem yix maix sawg heue.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
2 Pui pa muh aux hu ka si peem cie to kaux tix,
ka daux si peem pruh tai Feung-sing.
Nawh liang phung yung dee an,
caik ka tai siyag.
3 Pui pa muh aux mawh cie aux,
mai aux mawh cie nawh;
nawh liang phung yung ka daux tiag tai siyag heue.
Lox rhax ka daux phuan ceu
[Simex pa sang koe moeng]
4 Paox muh aux,
maix hoik mhawm awm Teu-sa yuh nan heue!
Hoik mhawm awm Yie-lu-sa-leung yuh nan heue!
Hoik nyawm tix jhak awm man chix cie pui naing yuh nan heue!
5 Yoi ngai maix heue,
paw vi daong ngai tix jhak ka aux;
tawm ngai maix hoik kieh aux si yaung si kua.
Hauk kaing maix hoik awm phung pex yuh nan.
Jang luh tix lih khaing gawng Ci-liex.
6 Rhang maix hoik awm hauk pa khraox kip tix yuh nan,
hoik paing awm hauk yung pa taug si ghrah singax yuh nan,
dia tix ku brawm hawt paox,
tix plah kawx ang cud heue.
7 Si bax maix khawm ra plak ka plak khaix man tie hoik mhawm raoh,
hoik awm plix mak cang pa vih tix max koe ra plak yuh nan.
8 Simiang koe moeng ting tix gliah kaux,
moeng iak tix daix kaux;
koe bun siliex pa ot ka daux nyiex haw tom ang lai dak uik simin.
9 Viang mawh nan,
aux muh ngie tix kaux khaing;
nawh hoik nyawm tix jhak awm si kaux yuh nan.
Nawh hoik mawh kawn bun tix kaux ka miex tix,
hoik mawh kawn pa kied muh miex nawh tix kaux.
Nyawm bun tang kaux khankix yaox ka nawh,
mai hoik yaog yaw ka nawh khawm uik;
moeng ting mai moeng iak simiang kawx hoik yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
10 An pa simie pa riang praix yam pon ngawb mawh mawx?
Nawh kied mhawm grang,
hoik mhawm sawng awm riang khix yuh nan heue!
Hoik mhawm riang awm pa riang singaix yuh nan heue!
Hoik nyawm tix jhak awm man chix cie pui naing pa sum jung tix heue!
11 Aux lih ka si peem khaox Batan,
sang jhak simi ka khaox iak pa koe ka daux ngawd,
nghoik lhax khaox plix siiet pa mhawm klai,
tai khaox plix mak cang.
12 Aux ang lai pon kham pa si yaung tix;
maix hoik kieh rhawm rhi aux simie ka rhawm muh dut,
awm pui naing pa kua pa nied tix grheu ka law ling hu,
son sang krox jun tix yuh naing heue.
[Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix]
13 Graoh heue,
klieh heuea,
nyawm bun Su-la-mi eue,
maix graoh heue,
son sang pon aux tix yaox ka greeh grang maix heue.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
Yam ot aux tix graoh ka daux pui haoh pui hun khawm ra plak,
yuh ka mawx pex lai sang yaog kaing tix jhak ka aux lie?
