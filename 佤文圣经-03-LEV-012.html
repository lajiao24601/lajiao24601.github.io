Been singax khaix keh bun
12
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
2 nhie nawh krai grawng lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Phan bun keh,
keh kawn simex,
khaix hoik keh aliah singaix,
nawh tag mawh pa ang singax ka daux been,
awm ang singax yam hoek khix lih nham seeing bun yuh nan.
3 aYam hoek daix singaix,
tag caw tix Pat-hak kawn simex an.
4 Khaix an,
bun keh an tag baox krox jun hoek tix ngoe loe singaix,
tom hoik hit lih nham seeing tix,
tag sawn nawh mawh pa singax singeh,
ka daux glang bawg yam sivoe sang hoik been sang singax,
nawh tag ang pon tix hu teeh ka kraung ceu pa singax singeh tix ceu kawx,
ka daux nyiex buan pa singax singeh kawx,
tag ang pon tix laig ka heue.
5 Phan bun keh,
keh ka kawn bun,
khaix hoik keh kao pon singaix,
nawh tag mawh pa ang singax singeh ka daux been,
awm ang singax singeh yam hoek khix lih nham seeing bun.
Nawh tag baox deeh krox jun hoek tix gliah liah singaix,
tom hoik hit lih nham seeing bun,
tag sawn nawh singax singeh heue.
6 Khaix hoik naog singaix singax singeh bun pa keh,
keh kawn simex kawn bun ang ah,
nawh tag caw tix viex ka kawn yung tix mu pa koe tix num hoek,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
si kaux krie tix mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
si kaux tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
hoek tawx ka cao giex kaing li ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex.
7 Cao giex kaing li tag tan an kix son nawh ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai yuh been krax pa kieh singax singeh son nawh.
Yuh nin,
nawh tag singax khaing ka daux been krax an heue.
8 bPhan bun keh an ang pon liag ka kawn yung tix mu,
nawh chix tix siao ka si kaux ra mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
si kaux krie ra mu yuh tang kawn yung an,
tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
an tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
Cao giex kaing li tag yuh krax pa singax singeh son nawh.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom tag pon tix singax singeh ka daux been krax an heue.
