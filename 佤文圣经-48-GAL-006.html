Guah tix klawm khaox glawm pa cien
6
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
phan koe pui pa loe tix lud,
pex pa mawh pui pa dex dawt tau mai khuan in kix,
tag sang cox nawh ka daux krax pa awn rhawm awn rhi.
To kaux pex kawx tag caw tix priex rhawm tix,
son ang sang glup ka daux pa nyaw glaw.
2 Tag caw tix ra lai teem ka been glawm paox tix,
yuh nin tom tag kieh gum lox tawx Khrit pon lih grum naog.
3 Pui pa ang mawh tid tix mai een to kaux tix mawh pui pa cong,
hoik tang deeh pie ka to kaux tix.
4 Pui ku kaux tag caw tix lam lia ka kaing pa yuh to kaux tix;
phan yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
nawh chix tix yaw kieh mawh min plak,
ang siao teei tix tiag tau mai kaing ceu pa mhawm pa pon yuh pui tang kaux.
5 Pui ku kaux tag caw tix klawm ka beeh glawm cie to kaux tix heue.
6 Pui pa ot ka daux krax pa rauh see cie Khrit an,
tag caw tix cim ka ceu pa mhawm pa koe khawm uik tau mai sara tix heue.
7 Pex paw vi tang deeh pie lhien ka to kaux tix,
mai ka Siyiex kawx paw vi pie lhien kah.
Pui tix kaux sum ceu pa tix,
tag sang vawg ceu an.
8 Phan nawh sum son sang kieh alo nex nham to kaux tix naog rhawm,
nawh tag sang rieb tix teei krax yum khaing alo nex nham;
phan nawh sum son sang mawh krax muh Khuan Singax Singeh,
nawh tag sang pon krax pa im ju leeing ju khaing Khuan Singax Singeh.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
phan ex yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
ang pon tix laux rhawm;
phan ex ang laux rhawm,
yam hoik hoek bawg yam tag sang pon ka ceu pa sawg tix.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
yam mawx ang ah,
phan koe bawg yam tag caw tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm son pui hun,
ka son pui pa mawh nyiex tix lhang ka daux krax yum rhawm khankix,
tag caw tix yuh nin heue.
Pa ne raw mai pa chawk mhawm ot
11 Pex jhak,
ngai lai pa ting in kix hoik mawh pa tiiem aux tix tawx ka pex heue!
12 Pui an kix pa muh tix yuh min plak ngaix ka plak praix,
tag mawh pui pa khin pex rieb ka been pa Pat-hak.
Kix yuh nin,
in hoik mawh lhat kix ka sang kham krax pa tuk tiak son khaox lhak Khrit.
13 Tete te lai,
pie pui pa rieb ka been pa Pat-hak khankix kawx ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng;
kix khin pex rieb been pa Pat-hak,
mawh son sang yaw rang di ka daux pa hoik koe krax cu nghiet pex ka been li plak praix.
14 Phan mawh son aux,
aux ang yaw rang di ka tang ceu,
aux yaw ngie rang di ka khaox lhak Simiang Ye-sux Khrit khaing.
Mawh kheu ka daux khaox lhak in,
haktiex mai aux hoik tawk yum;
aux mai haktiex kawx hoik tawk yum nang.
15 Pa Pat-hak mai pa ang Pat-hak ang sawn tix mawh tid tix;
kaing ting ex caw tix lih mawh pui pa khraox heue.
16 Kieh can pui pa priex tix hawt ka been in khawm uik,
mai ka pui cie Siyiex khawm uik,
pon ka pa nhim mai krax pa caox rhawm heue!
17 Jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai,
paw lai sawg tix kieh aux nyhap ka daux kaing in kix,
mawh kheu koe rhoi khaix mat Ye-sux ka to kaux aux heue.
18 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
kieh Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son ka pex ku kaux heue!
A-meung.
