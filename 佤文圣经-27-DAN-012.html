Singaix bawg yam si dauh
12
aKawn cau Siyiex pa ceep ka kraung man keei sum ah nin:
“Yam an,
Mi-cia-leux cao kaing kawn cau Siyiex tag sang lih hoek.
Khaix an tag sang lih koe ka daux baux bawg yam krax pa kha rhawm,
pa mawh krax kha rhawm pa blawn ting khaix hoik sum ka meung.
Ka daux bawg yam an,
ceu kaux paox ceu paox cad maix hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux lai pa im ju,
mai pon krax taoh khawm uik.
2 bKoe pui pa hoik yum tag sang deeh kaoh im.
Koe pui tix blah tag sang pon ju leeing ju leeing phan;
koe pui tix blah tag sang pon krax pa kaig pa tua leeing ju.
3 Simiang pa lhak lhiao khankix tag sang jhan riang,
mai jhan riang awm to kaux pa riang rao ma yuh nan.
Pui pa sidiex gau khankix prawng ka daux krax pa rauh pa see,
tag sang jhan riang awm riang simuing yuh nan heue.”
4 cNawh ah nin ka aux:
“Tang-yi-li eue,
yam in maix nyeep ka phuk lai in,
mai baox tawk jum piang,
tom hoik hoek si dauh haktiex.
Koe pui tom nai riang rao tix,
mai simie tix si kaw ka kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh heue.”
5 Khaix an,
aux yaox ka pui ra kaux,
tix kaux cung ka praok klawng,
an tix kaux ot ka plak an.
6 Ka daux kix tix kaux chawk ka kawn cau Siyiex pa ceep ka kraung man keei sum an ah nin:
“Kaing pa daox pa am in kix tag sang krox hoek miex glang tom taug uik lie?”
7 dKawn cau Siyiex an yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn ka rao ma,
mai tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex pa im ju.
Aux mhong lox nawh ah nin:
“Sang mawh loe num plak.
Yam hoik si dauh krax pa kham pui cie Siyiex ka krax pa tuk tiak tuk te,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh khawm uik heue.”
8 Aux mhong lox pa krai nawh,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng dee bleeh.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom chawk ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
dee si dauh kaing in kix mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
9 Nawh ah nin:
“Tang-yi-li eue,
maix hu heue,
mawh kheu lox in kix tag sang nyip tom hoek yam si dauh haktiex.
10 ePui tom nie tag sang si ghrah tix singax,
mai gaux tix tom mawh meh heue.
Pui caox pui caig tag sang ca nied tix yuh pa caox pa caig;
kix ang chix loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
mai mawh ngie pui pa lhak lhiao pa chix loh ka rhawm rhi tix khaing heue.
11 f“Jauh tom ang lai kieh yuh krax tan ku ngaix ka daux singaix an,
pa mawh nang yam jauh tix deuh ka ‘to tiag pa yum koeng pa kieh pui laux leeing’ an,
tag sang koe been tix rheng ra yieh tix dim singaix.
12 Pui an kix pa kaux sijax tom si dauh,
pa hoik luan khaing been tix rheng loe yieh tix ngoe phuan singaix kied ting buan son heue!
13 “Phan mawh son maix,
Tang-yi-li eue,
tag caw tix kaux sijax tom si dauh,
tom hoik hoek laux lee maix tix;
viang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix si dauh bawg yam,
tag sang kaoh im,
mai rieb ka khawng phak buan son heue.”
