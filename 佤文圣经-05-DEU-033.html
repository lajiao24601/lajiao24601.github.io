Maw-si nawb buan ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui
33
Sivoe sang hu pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex Maw-si khaing kawn pui haktiex,
nawh ah lox nawb buan,
son sang guah tix tang hu khaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa nawb Maw-si buan son:
 
2 Simiang Siyiex hoek khaing gawng Si-nai;
nawh awm singaix pa lih khaing Yi-tong,
mai jauh ka gawng Pa-lang,
jhan riang ka piang pui cie nawh khankix.
Kawn cau rao ma kaing mheen khankix hawt khaix nawh;
taix khawm dawm nawh giex ka ngu proh pa cap.
3 Simiang Siyiex muh ka pui cie nawh khankixa,
nawh kia ngien ka pui cie tix khankix.
Ex tag caw tix gup tix krub ot sivoe caong nawh,
rieb ka lox pa sidiex gau nawh.
4 Ex tag caw priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tawm Maw-si;
an hoik mawh siang pa ting ngawh ka meung pui Yi-seu-liex.
5 Yam khrawm simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa mawh ku ceu pui khankix paox tix,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang mawh simiang ka daux pui cie nawh heue.
 
6 Son ceu pui Lui-piien,
mai Maw-si nawb buan ah nin:
Kaux pui ceu Lui-piien viang pui kix tux kawx,
kieh kix ang laux leeing ju.
 
7 Son ceu pui Yo-tax,
mai nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa kok Yo-tax khankix,
deeh kieh kix rom tau tix mai ceu pui tang ceu khankix.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix yuh naing son kix liak,
mai teem kix puing pex ka pui pa koengb khankix heue.
 
8 cSon ceu pui Li-we,
nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix siao “Wu-ling” mai “Thu-ming” plak jhi alo maix ka daux ceu pui Li-we khankix;
kix hoik mawh ceu pui cie maix pa koe krax cu krax nghiet khankix.
Maix hoik cheuk tix yaox kix baoh Ma-sha,
Mai hoik yaox grawng koe kix krax cu krax nghiet ka dee lih rawm Mi-li-pa.
9 Kix hoik jhi grawng pa koe kix krax cu krax nghiet ka maix,
kawn lheu khaing miex tix,
pux aik tix,
mai kawn praix tix khankix.
Kix hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap maix,
giex kaux ka sijax mukheu maix.
10 Kix sidiex gau ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix mai gum lox ping nyap maix,
gleeing nyawng bhawng ka plak sivoe maix,
mai tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu dee tan.
11 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh kix ting lhaong;
khiox khaw maix rieb ka kaing pa yuh kix.
Khiox khaw maix yieh tix tik ka pui pa koeng ka kix,
son ang sang kieh pui pa koeng ka kix deeh yaog tix kaoh heue.
 
12 Phan mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex muh mai kia ngien ka Piien-ya-ming;
mox kix leeing lang singaix,
ka plak sivoe ngai nawh tag yaox krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe.
 
13 Son ceu pui Yaw-seux,
mai nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Kieh Simiang Siyiex nawb buan ka haktiex kix,
kieh lhiex khaing rao ma lih tix jauh,
kieh rawm keet buing tix lih khaing greem tiex.
14 Kieh singaix kub jhan riang ka daux kaung ma kix,
kieh pon plix nghox plix koi tom nie ku num;
kieh haktiex kix plix pa mhawm khawm pon bawg yam.
15 Kieh gawng ot kix tom dix dix,
kuah naog ka khaox plix pa mhawm.
16 Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa si kah khaing daux yeem pang kat pa gleeing ka ngu an,
tawx buan son tom kub ka kix,
kieh haktiex pa ot kix daux naog ka priex preem pa nyawm.
Kieh buan son in khawm uik lih hoek ka piang ceu pui Yaw-seux,
mawh kheu hoik mawh nawh simiang ka daux pux aik tix.
17 Yaw-seux mawh kawn moi mhaing pa koe riang,
mai moi tiag pa koe rung;
rung nawh mawh kaing rheng ka Ma-na-si,
Mawh kaing mheen ka Yi-fa-liien,
Son sang teed ka meung pui kaing mheen,
Grheu kix hu tom hoek ka haktiex ku dauh ku dee heue.
 
18 Son ceu pui Si-pu-loeng mai Yi-sa-cia,
mai nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Kieh reu pui lai gee ceu pui Si-pu-loeng luan ka piang rawm;
kieh sin ngao haktiex ceu pui Yi-sa-cia baox tiiem thie tix.
19 Kix kok ceu pui tang ceu hoek ka piang gawng ot kix,
mai tan ka krax tan pa khaw kix tix sang tan dee an.
Kix tag pon kraung khrai khaing piang rawm,
mai pon siang ploi khaing hawt praok rawm daux pak mhaik.
 
20 Son ceu pui Cia-teux mai nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik vih va nawh ka haktiex ceu pui Cia-teux.
Cia-teux awm sivai sang si pa krox jun,
nawh tag riah ka khawn caong plak ngai to sat,
mai tawh ka hak kaing to sat an.
21 Kix hoik pon teei haktiex pa mhawm son to kaux tix,
pa hoik mawh nyum pa khaw simiang tix pon.
Yam khrawm simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex paox tix,
mai kix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
22 Son ceu pui Tang,
mai nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Ceu pui Tang mawh kawn sivai sang si pa kluing,
tag yaog tix lih khaing haktiex Pa-sang heue.
 
23 Son ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix,
mai nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Na-fu-tha-lix hoik pon ka buan son pa tawx Simiang Siyiex tom nie,
mai hoik cim ka krax muh nawh;
mee haktiex kix jauh ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix tom vih tix hoek plak caong ma seh heue.
 
24 Son ceu pui Ya-seux,
mai nawh nawb buan ah nin:
Buan son pa pon ceu pui Ya-seux,
mai nie khaing ceu pui tang ceu.
Kieh nawh mawh kawn pa blawn pon krax muh khaing pux aik tix;
mai kieh haktiex cie nawh kuah naog ka khaox sime.
25 Kieh veng yaong nawh koe siviex tawng mai jhong rhiem pa priex pau kieh kaux cong;
kieh baux singaix im ju nawh pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.
 
26 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
ang koe siyiex tang kaux pa lheu khaing Siyiex maix.
Nawh brug pai awm hoek tix taoh teem ka maix;
siawp sibrawm nawh pon giex ka rao ma.
27 Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju leeing hoik mox ka maix tom si dauh;
plawng taix nawh pa koe leeing ju hoik joh jhan teem coi ka maix.
Nawh hoik grheu ka pui koeng son maix,
tang rai rhuat tix hu khaing maix;
nawh tawx lox kieh maix yuh kix laux lak pak leeing.
28 Kawn praix ceu pui Ya-keux tag pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem.
Kix ot ka daux haktiex dee pa koe nghox koi mai plai pa khraox,
lhiex khaing rao ma lih jaux ka haktiex an.
29 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix kied koe buan heue!
Maix hoik mawh meung pa taoh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh pui mawx pa kawn lheu khaing maix lie?
Simiang Siyiex mawh dieb tie naok ka maix,
mai vaig gawn maix;
nawh kia ngien ka maix,
mai kieh maix puing pex.
Pui koeng ka maix tag ap tix ang pex ka maix;
maix tag dig kix ka greem tiag caong tix heue.
