Lai Thi-maw-thaix Phuk Tix
1
Aux mawh Pao-law;
aux mawh kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit khaing daux gum lox pa tawm Siyiex cao taoh ex,
mai Ye-sux Khrit pa keud pot jun pot ex kah.
2 aAux tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka maix Thi-maw-thaix,
pa mawh kawn simex aux tete ka daux krax yum rhawm:
Kieh keeing Siyiex mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son,
krax pa caox rhawm mai pa nhim ka maix heue!
Ne raw ka lox pa sidiex pa pie lhien
3 Aux simie maix ot ka Yi-fu-saw;
yam hu aux ka Ma-chi-toeng aux hoik sax tawx lox ka maix yuh nin.
Koe pui tix blah sidiex ka been ceu pa ang khaw caw dee an,
maix tag caw tix tawx lox nhie kix naox.
4 Nhie kix tik ka lox lao pui pa ang tie mawh,
mai ka doeh nyiex ma pa kied lang khankix;
mawh kheu ceu in kix chix kieh simah lih,
ang doh pon teem coi tawng yung ka kaing pa yuh Siyiex pie ceeix kawx,
kaing in mawh pa tag pon tawng khaing daux krax yum rhawm.
5 Gum lox tawm in kaing ting mawh son sang kieh krax muh krax rhawm lih;
krax muh krax rhawm in mawh pa kaoh khaing rhawm rhi pa singax,
khaing krax keud krax sawn pa singax,
mai khaing krax yum rhawm pa mawh pa meh.
6 Koe pui tix blah hoik tik vud ka ceu in kix,
mai hoik glup ka daux lox lao pa pie lhien.
7 Kix keud tix sang mawh sara ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
pie lox krai to kaux kix kawx,
kix ang tawng kah,
pie kaing ceu pa ah kix sang yuh,
kix ang sidawng ka heue.
8 Ex hoik tawng,
ca mawh siao pui ka lox tat jhawm prawng ka daux pa khaw caw,
lox tat jhawm prawng hoik dawg mawh ceu pa mhawm.
9 Grawh grawng nawh,
ex tag caw tix tawng grawng in yuh nin:
lox tat jhawm prawng ang mawh pa yuh tix lih son pui mhawm,
mawh son pui caox,
pa ang nghiet lox,
pa ang rauh see,
pa yuh phi map,
pa ang nab yam yung yi ka been li,
pa ghoing rhawm ka been pa dex mai haktiex,
pa pauh ka miex keeing tix,
pa pauh ka pui.
10 Pa phit vao,
pa yuh ceu pa ang khaw mai paox simex tix,
pa pie lhien tix coh ka paox pui tix,
pa pie lhien,
pa kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh,
pa tieh vheen ka been ceu pa grum mhawm khankix.
11 Been pa grum mhawm in mawh pa teei khaing daux gum lox buan,
pa tawx Siyiex pa koe min plak ngai,
pa yaog yaw pui,
pa tawx aux krai pao heue.
Yaog yaw ka lox pa ting buan son ka krax pa caox rhawm Siyiex
12 Aux yaog yaw ka Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit;
mawh kheu tawx nawh riang rao ka aux,
een aux mawh pui pa kaux sijax tix kaux,
mai tawx aux hoek tix yuh kaing jhaw nawh.
13 bViang aux hoik salao ka nawh,
mai tuk tiak tuk te map nai ka nawh sivoe an.
Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex caox rhawm ka aux;
mawh kheu ang aux bax yum rhawm ka nawh bawg yam an,
mai ang tawng ka ceu pa yuh tix mawh ceu pa tix.
14 Simiang Ye-sux ex tawx buan son pa ting tix ka aux,
kieh aux koe krax yum rhawm mai krax muh krax rhawm,
in mawh pa pon kheu pa kleuk tix tau mai Ye-sux Khrit.
15 “Kaing ting Ye-sux Khrit lih hoek ka piang haktiex mawh son sang taoh kawn pui phi map” gum lox in mawh tete,
mawh pa khaw tix rieb khawm uik.
Aux mawh pui phi map pa blawn caox tix kaux,
16 viang mawh nan,
hoik mawh kheu krax pa caox rhawm Siyiex,
pa kieh Ye-sux Khrit jhi krax pa gun yeung tix pa grum naog ka aux pui phi map pa blawn caox in tix kaux.
Kieh aux mawh ceu pa mhawm pa gau pui koe khaix pa yum rhawm ka nawh,
pa rieb ka ju leeing ju khankix.
17 Kieh krax yung yi,
min plak ngai mawh cie simiang pa ting ka piang khawm uik leeing ju leeing phan,
pa mawh pa ang chix laux leeing,
pa ang chix yaox,
Siyiex pa koe tix kaux khaing heue!
A-meung.
18 Kawn aux Thi-maw-thaix eue,
hawt awm lox pa grox krai ka son maix sivoe an,
aux hoik ap tix tawx gum lox tawx in ka maix.
Gum lox in tag caw tix kieh mawh kraung naing son maix yam puing maix naing pa mhawm.
19 Tag caw tix giex kaux cong ka krax yum rhawm,
mai rhawm pa tawng yung tix singax.
Koe pui tix blah ang nghiet ka rhawm rhi pa mhawm tix,
kheu nan krax yum rhawm kix hoik si gleeing tix,
20 ka daux koe Su-mi-nai mai Ya-li-sang-tax;
aux hoik ap kix ka daux tawm taix Sa tan,
mai kieh kix gau tix ang lai baox map nai ka Siyiex heue.
