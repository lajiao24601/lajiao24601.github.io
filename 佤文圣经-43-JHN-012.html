Pon pa groi rawm bee ka Paw-ta-ni
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.6–13; Lai Ma-kheu 14.3–9)
12
Sivoe poi Luat-luan liah ngaix,
Ye-sux hoek ka haktiex Paw-ta-ni,
pa mawh haktiex dee paung ot La-sa-lux (Ye-sux hoik sax kieh La-sa-lux deeh kaoh im dee in.)
2 Koe pui hoik rien khre priex bo son Ye-sux dee an;
Ma-tax teem nawh khre,
La-sa-lux mai nyo si nie tang kaux sawm tau mai Ye-sux.
3 aYam in,
Ma-li-yax teei rawm bee bhawng na tax pa singax pa kied ngawh tix kawng,
krai ka caong Ye-sux,
khaix an teei hauk kaing tix awt kroh kah;
ka daux nyiex hoik naog ka taox bhawng khawm uik.
4 Pui gau Ye-sux tix kaux pa mawh Yo-tax pui Cia-liiex ah nin:
5 “Yuh ka mawx lai ang teei rawm bee in coh mawh mau luah loe yieh,
guah tix tawx ka pui hot pui sibhawm lie?”
6 Nawh krai ka lox in,
tete ang tie mawh keud nawh son pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai hoik mawh kheu mawh nawh pui briex;
nawh kuang mau,
mai hoik teei ka mau een tix mau pui hun jheeng jheeng heue.
7 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Ghram nawh yuh heue!
In hoik mawh pa deuh son sang siao tix si paung ka aux.
8 bPui hot pui sibhawm hoik ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng.”
Krax keud tix pauh ka La-sa-lux
9 Cub pui hun pui Yo-thaix mhong grawng pa ot Ye-sux ka haktiex Paw-ta-ni,
kix tom hu ka dee an.
Kix ang joeng hu ngie son Ye-sux,
mai hoik keud nang tix sang jhak ka La-sa-lux pa hoik kieh Ye-sux deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang cao giex kaing li khankix hoik keud tix sang pauh khawm La-sa-lux nang,
11 mawh kheu hoik lih hu pui Yo-thaix tom nie khaing kix jao La-sa-lux,
mai hoik yum rhawm ka Ye-sux heue.
Viex min plak ngai laig ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.1–11; Lai Ma-kheu 11.1–11; Lai Lu-cia 19.28–40)
12 Ka daux ra singaix,
cub pui hun pa hoek tix yuh poi ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix,
mhong grawng tag sang laig Ye-sux daux veng.
13 cMawh kheu nan,
kix teei kak khaox jhie yaong hu rieb ka nawh,
mai gaux rhawm kok yo ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Siyiex heue!
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka pui an pa hoek tix krub ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex!
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex heue!”
14 Ye-sux yaox la tix mu,
mai brug kah,
awm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
15 dKawn simex kawn bun ka daux veng Shi-ang eue,
paw vi lhat.
Jhak heue,
simiang pex
brug kawn la hoek heue!
16 Yam jauh,
pui gau nawh ang tawng dee bleeh grawng kaing in,
yam hoik pon Ye-sux ka min plak ngai,
kix taug doh keud ka gum lox phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ka son nawh,
khaix an kix tom hawt tix yuh awm an heue.
17 Yam kok yo Ye-sux ka La-sa-lux,
kieh nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
lih hu khaing daux si meeig.
Cub pui pa ot tau mai Ye-sux khankix sidiex ka can pa hoik yaox kix hu.
18 Pui tom nie hu rieb ka nawh khawm uik,
kheu hoik mhong kix grawng mhai pa daox pa am in kix.
19 Pui Fa-li-saix ra lai krai ka paox tix ah nin:
“Ex ang lai mawh tid tix;
maix jhak pui piang haktiex hoik hawt ka nawh khawm uik heue!”
Pui Si-lax khiox khaw tix yaox ka Ye-sux
20 Yam yuh poi,
ka daux pui pa hoek tix krub ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tix blah mawh pui Si-lax.
21 Kix hoek ka Fe-lix pui Paw-sai-tax haktiex Cia-li-lix,
mai khiox khaw nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
yix simie tix yaox ka Ye-sux heue.”
22 Fe-lix hu krai ka Ang-teu-liex,
kiex ra kaux hu krai ka Ye-sux.
23 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Bawg yam pon kawn kawn pui min plak ngai hoik hoek.
24 Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
phan simie nghox ca tix mu ang cawd daux tiex yum,
mai ca kawn mawh simie tix mu khaing;
phan simie an yum tom taug chix lih plix plix tom nie.
25 ePui pa muh ka ju to kaux tix,
tag sang som ju;
pui pa cu tix tawx ju to kaux tix ka haktiex in,
tag sang tieh tix pon ju in tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan.
26 Pui mawx sang yuh kaing aux,
tag caw tix hawt ka aux;
aux ot dee mawx ang ah,
siliex aux kawx tag sang ot dee an nang.
Pui pa yuh kaing aux an,
keeing aux tag sang siao tix yaog nawh ting heue.”
Ye-sux krai grawng pa sang yum tix
27 “Yam in aux hoik koe krax khix rhawm khix rhi,
sang khaw aux tix krai ceu pa tix lie?
Aux sang khiox khaw keeing aux taoh aux luat luan khaing bawg yam in lie?
Viang mawh nan,
aux hoek son in,
tag sang kham gee bawg yam pa kha rhawm in.
28 Keeing eue,
kieh maix yaog yaw min ceu kaux tix heue!”
Yam an,
koe lox lih hoek khaing rao ma ah nin:
“Aux hoik yaog yaw min ceu kaux tix,
mai kawn baox sang yaog yaw ka heue!”
29 Can pui hun pa cung dee an mhong ka lox in,
mai kix tom ah nin:
“Sah hoik tuh heue!”
Pui tix blah ah nin:
“Mawh kawn cau Siyiex pa si kah tau mai nawh heue!”
30 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Gum lox in ang mawh son aux,
mai hoik mawh pa tawx son pex.
31 Yam in haktiex in tag sang pon krax ciyang;
yam in cao giex kaing ka piang haktiex,
pui tag sang pu pliak ka kix.
32 Yam yaog aux khaing haktiex,
aux tag sang kieh pui kaing mheen hoek ka tix.” (
33 Gum lox nawh in hoik mawh pa krai nawh tag sang yum tix yuh ka mawx heue.)
34 fPui hun pawk ah nin:
“Lox tat jhawm prawng ex hoik krai ka ex,
Khrit mawh pa koe leeing ju leeing phan;
yuh ka mawx maix lai krai kawn kawn pui tag sang yaog ka nawh?
Kawn kawn pui in mawh pui mawx lie?”
35 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pa riang ot ka daux pex ang lai rheeng,
pex khaw nied tix hu yam kawn koe pa riang,
son ang pa faik sang krup ka pex,
mawh kheu pui pa hu ka daux pa faik ang tawng ka dee hu nawh.
36 Yam kawn koe pa riang pex tag caw tix hoek ka pa riang,
son sang lih mawh pex pui pa ot ka daux pa riang heue.”
Pui Yo-thaix ang yum rhawm
Khaix hoik krai nawh ka gum lox in,
Ye-sux lih hu khaing kix,
mai mox tix.
37 Viang nawh hoik yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka plak sivoe ngai kix tom nie,
kix kawn ang bax yum rhawm ka nawh.
38 gIn hoik kieh gum lox pa krai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax lih tix mawh ah nin:
Simiang eue,
pui mawx chix yum rhawm ka lox pa sidiex ex lie?
Siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex lih jhi tix ka pui mawx lie?
39 Grawng ang kix yum rhawm,
Yi-sai-yax kawx hoik krai ah nin:
40 hSiyiex hoik kieh kix dug ngai,
hoik kieh rhawm dee chix keud chix tawng kix mu mi,
son ang sang kieh ngai kix chix yaox,
rhawm rhi kix kawx tag sang chix doh.
Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex ah nin:
kix ang chix veeh tix hoek ka aux,
son sang pai aux kix moi heue.
41 Yi-sai-yax krai ka gum lox in kix hoik mawh kheu yaox nawh ka min plak ngai Ye-sux,
mai krai ka son nawh heue.
42 Viang hoik mawh nan kawx,
ka daux simiang pui Yo-thaix kawx hoik koe tom nie pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux,
mawh ngie hoik lhat kix ka pui Fa-li-saix,
mai ang pon krai ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
son ang pui sang grheu kix lih khaing nyiex sidiex.
43 Kix hoik muh pui yaog yaw tix lheu khaing Siyiex heue.
Gum lox Ye-sux tag sang prawng ca ka pui
44 Ye-sux kok yo lox tom ting ah nin:
“Pui pa yum rhawm ka aux,
ang joeng mawh yum rhawm ka aux,
mai hoik mawh pa yum rhawm ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
45 Pui pa yaox ka aux,
hoik mawh pui pa yaox ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
46 Aux mawh pa riang pa lih hoek ka piang haktiex,
son sang kieh can pui pa yum rhawm ka aux,
ang lai paung ot ka daux pa faik.
47 Pui pa mhong ka gum lox aux an pa ang priex tix hawt,
aux ang prawng ca ka nawh.
Kaing ting,
aux hoek ka piang haktiex ang lai prawng ca ka pui,
mai hoik mawh son sang taoh ka kawn pui haktiex.
48 Pui pa tieb tie ka aux,
pa ang rieb ka gum lox aux,
pui pa sang prawng ca ka nawh hoik tang koe tix kaux;
ka daux singaix si dauh,
can gum lox pa krai aux khawm uik tag sang prawng ca ka nawh heue!
49 Mawh kheu ang aux krai ceu pa tid tix son to kaux tix,
mai hoik krai lox ceu pa tawx keeing aux kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
50 Aux hoik tawng gum lox pa tawx nawh tag chix viex ju leeing ju hoek.
Mawh kheu nan,
gum lox pa krai aux hoik mawh pa nhie Keeing aux krai heue.”
