30
Le-ciiex ang chix keh kawn son Ya-keux,
tom boh rhawm ka pux ke tix,
ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Maix tawx aux koe kawn,
phan ang mawh nan,
mawh ngie yum aux khaing heue!”
2 Ya-keux song rhawm ka Le-ciiex,
ah nin:
“Aux ang chix mawh tang Siyiex;
pa ang kieh maix koe kawn mawh nawh.”
3 Le-ciiex ah nin:
“Bun siliex aux Pi-la ot tin,
maix ot tau mai nawh,
kieh nawh keh kawn tang aux;
yuh nin,
aux tom yaox tix mawh miex.”
4 Khaix an Le-ciiex tom tawx bun siliex tix Pi-la ka mex tix,
Ya-keux tom ot tau mai nawh.
5 Pi-la koe kaux,
mai keh Ya-keux cie kawn simex tix kaux.
6 Le-ciiex ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik prawng rauh see son aux,
mai hoik mhong lox rawg aux buan,
tawx kawn simex ka aux tix kaux;”
mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Tang.a
7 Pi-la baox koe kaux,
mai keh Ya-keux cie kawn simex daux ra kaux.
8 Le-ciiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik simah mai pux ke tix,
mai hoik pex kah;”
mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Na-fu-tha-lixb heue.
9 Li-yax yaox hoik naox koe kawn tix,
nawh tom tawx bun siliex tix Si-phax ka Ya-keux.
10 Si-phax keh kawn simex tix kaux son Ya-keux.
11 Li-yax ah nin:
“Aux hoik mhawm son!”
Tom sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Cia-teux.c
12 Si-phax baox keh kawn simex daux ra kaux son Ya-keux.
13 Li-yax ah nin:
“Aux kied sidaing koe son!
Kawn bun tag kok aux mawh pa koe buan son;”
mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Ya-seuxd heue.
14 Yam hoek bawg yam vawg nghox ca,
Lui-piien yaox ka plix pa kieh pui haing rhawme,
nawh tom teei tix tawx ka miex tix Li-yax.
Le-ciiex ah nin ka Li-yax:
“Tawx plix pa kieh pui haing rhawm cie kawn maix tix blah ka aux liak.”
15 Li-yax pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix hoik rhim ka mex aux kawn ang baox vhah rhawm lie?
Yam in,
maix baox sang tud ka plix pa kieh pui haing rhawm cie kawn simex aux lie!”
Le-ciiex ah nin:
“Phan maix tawx plix pa kieh pui haing rhawm cie kawn simex tix ka aux,
daux bo in singaix maix chix tix ot tau mai Ya-keux heue.”
16 Yam boi faik praix,
Ya-keux kaoh khaing daux kaung nghox ca;
Li-yax lih hu rieb ka nawh,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Daux sawm in maix caw tix ot tau mai aux,
mawh kheu hoik tawx aux plix pa kieh pui haing rhawm cie kawn aux jao maix.”
Daux bo an,
Ya-keux ot tau mai Li-yax heue.
17 Siyiex nghiet ka lox rawg Li-yax buan,
nawh tom koe kaux,
mai keh Ya-keux cie kawn simex daux phuan kaux.
18 Li-yax ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik pawk buan son ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik tawx aux bun siliex tix ka mex tix;”
kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Yi-sa-cia.f
19 Li-yax baox koe kaux,
mai keh kawn simex daux liah kaux son Ya-keux.
20 Li-yax ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik tawx khawng phak pa koe jum ka aux;
yam in mex aux tag chix muh ka aux jheeng jheeng,
kheu hoik keh aux kawn simex liah kaux son nawh;”
mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Si-pu-loeng.g
21 Khaix an,
nawh baox keh kawn bun tix kaux,
tom sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Ti-na.
22 Siyiex deeh doh keud ka Le-ciiex,
mai nghiet ka lox rawg nawh buan,
mai kieh nawh chix keh kawn.
23 Nawh koe kaux,
keh kawn simex tix kaux.
Nawh ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik teei krax pa kaig aux hu.
24 Kieh Simiang Siyiex baox tawx kawn simex ka aux tix kaux”.
Mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux kawn an mawh Yaw-seux.h
Ya-keux kum kawng mai La-pang
25 Khaix hoik keh Le-ciiex ka Yaw-seux,
Ya-keux ah nin ka La-pang:
“Ploi aux hu liak!
Son sang deeh aux ka haktiex dee ot tix.
26 Cu aux viex ka moeng tix khankix,
mai kawn simex kawn bun tix,
aux tom lih hu;
kix hoik mawh pa pon aux khaing daux pa yuh aux kaing maix.
Maix hoik tawng hoik sau aux riang tix yuh kaing maix heue.”
27 La-pang ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix nghiet ka lox krai aux!
Aux sawn tix yaox,
kheu maix,
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx buan ka aux.
28 Krai aux mhong,
maix sang ih ngawh riang tix miex ding,
aux sang tawx ka maix heue.”
29 Ya-keux pawk ah nin:
“Maix hoik tawng aux yuh kaing yuh ka mawx son maix,
ka daux pa kia ngien aux,
phung moi,
phung yung maix hoik haok huan tom nie.
30 Sivoe ang aux nyang hoek,
kraung khrai cie maix koe ngie ceeix khaing,
yam in hoik haok huan tom nie;
kheu aux,
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx buan ka maix.i
Yam in aux khaw tix yuh kaing son to kaux cao tix heue.”
31 La-pang chawk:
“Aux sang tawx ceu pa tix ka maix lie?”
Ya-keux pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux ang ih ngawh riang tix!
Phan maix cu ka rhawm pa keud aux,
aux chix tix baox kia ngien ka phung pex maix.
32 Daux in singaix,
kieh aux laig ka daux phung pex maix,
mai laug ka kawn pex can pa lung haukj mai pa koe bawd,
mai pa koe preeh teei lih khawm uik,
in kix tom sawn mawh ngawh riang aux.
33 Yuh nin,
khaix in maix tom tag tawng aux mawh ang aux mawh pa mawh pa meh.
Yam hoek maix tix kia ka ngawh riang pa pon aux,
phan maix yaox ka pex pa ang preeh,
pa ang bawd,
phung pex pa ang lung hauk,
maix tom tawng an mawh pa briex aux heue.”
34 La-pang pawk ah nin:
“Aux cu ka lox pa ah maix,
hawt yuh yuh nin.”
35 Ka daux singaix an,
nawh teei kawn pex mhaing,
mai pa preeh,
pa bawd,
khawm pa paing,
lai laig tix tau mai kawn pex siang,
mai baox laug tix teei ka kawn pex pa lung hauk lih,
kieh kawn simex tix mawh cao pa kuang kia,
36 khan an,
viex pex in kix hu,
hoek dee singai khaing dee ot Ya-keux been loe singaix krax.
Ya-keux liang phung pex cie La-pang pa kawn deuk khankix heue.
37 Ya-keux teei gawng khaox pai-yang,
khaox Batan,
mai khaox Vu-thong,
mai kheet tix tik lhox,
kieh bawd paing khaox lih.
38 Nawh jung tix deuh gawng khaox pa hoik kheet tix lhox sivoe phung yung,
daux hawt dawng rawm nyaux pex rawm,
mawh kheu yam hoek nyaux pex rawm mai si keeing ka paox tix.
39 Phung kawn pex kheu si keeing kix paox tix sivoe dee jung kak khaox an,
tom lih keh ka kawn pex,
kawn pex pa preeh,
pa bawd,
pa lai heue.
40 Ya-keux tang guah yung mai pex,
kieh kix veeh ngai tix ka kawn yung,
kawn pex cie La-pang pa bawd preeh mai pa lung.
Ya-keux siao ka grawng in kieh kawn pex cie tix haok huan,
mai guah phung pex tix khaing cie La-pang heue.
41 Yam si keeing phung pex pa ting riang ka paox tix,
Ya-keux tom teei kak khaox an sau ka daux dawng rawm ka plak sivoe ngaix kix,
kix tom si keeing paox tix si nax khaox an.
42 Viang mawh nan,
yam si keeing phung pex pa krawx paox tix,
nawh ang sau ka khaox an.
Ang koe miex glang tix,
kawn pex pa krawx pa rhao mawh cie La-pang,
mai pa kluing mawh cie Ya-keux.
43 Yuh nin,
Ya-keux baox nie mag mi hu si pie si pie,
koe phung pex,
siliex cawng,
ka-la-uk,
mai la tom haoh hun heue.
