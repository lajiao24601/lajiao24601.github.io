Cox jauh tix yuh krax krub
3
Yam hoek ka daux khix aliah,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tang nyawm ot mhawm mhawm ka haktiex tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix khawm uik grum rhawm tau mai khrawm paox tix ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 bYie-su-yax kawn simex Yaw-sa-ta mai cao giex kaing li pa yuh kix kaing tau khankix,
Saw-law-pa-paw kawn simex Sa-la-thiie mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh khankix,
cox sum ka khu dee tan Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
son sang hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik krai Maw-si pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,a
mai tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu.
3 dViangc pui pa vuk vied vuk viang pa deeh hoek khankix lhat graung ka pui haktiex,
kix ca kawn cox sum khu dee tan ka dee prim ot khu dee tan,
khaix an baox deeh jauh tix tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu dee tan ku ngawb ku bo.
4 eKix hawt awm been pa deuh kix yuh ka Poi Nyiex-gox-bang,
ku ngaix hawt awm diag tong pa khaw kix tix sang tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
5 fLheu khaing an,
kix baox tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing pa tan lu kix jheeng jheeng,
khawm krax tan pa khaw kix tix sang tan yam haok khix tix ngaix mai yam rom kix tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex ka tang bawg khankix,
mai baox koe krax tan pa tang kaoh rhawm tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
6 Viang kawn pui ang bax jauh tix sum ka nyiex buan,
viang mawh nan,
jauh ka khix aliah ka daux tix ngaix,
kix hoik jauh tix tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
Jauh tix cox sum ka nyiex buan
7 Pui haoh pui hun tawx ngawh riang ka cang mai simaox mai cang mai khaox,
mai baox teei priex preem,
rawm plix,
bee sime,
loh khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noeng ka pui veng Thai-eux mai pui veng Si-toeng.
Khaox in kix caw tix viex laig ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
si bloe hoek ka Yaw-pha.
Kaing in hoik mawh pa cu Vawng Paw-see pa kaux Sai-lu-seex kix yuh.
8 Yam deeh hoek pui haoh pui hun ka Yie-lu-sa-leung daux ra khix num an,
kix jauh tix yuh kaing ka dee prim ot nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
Saw-law-pa-paw,
Yie-su-yax mai paox ceu paox cad nawh pa kawn deuk khankix,
cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we,
tete te lai,
can pui pa vuk vied vuk viang pa deeh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
hoik yuh ka kaing in khawm uik.
Can pui Li-we pa koe ju tix nga num haok plak piang,
hoik tawx kix hoek tix mawh cao pa kuang kaing pa cox sum ka nyiex buan.
9 Yie-su-yax pui Li-we mai kawn praix nawh,
pui nyiex ma nawh,
khawm Cia-miiex mai kawn praix nawh (ceu cad cub nyiex ma Heu-ta-we-yaxg),
kix khrawm paox tix hoek kuang ka kaing pa cox sum ka nyiex buan.
Kawn praix Si-na-tax pui Li-we kawx teem coi ka kix heue.
10 hYam jauh kix tix yuh ka jaong nyiex buan,
cao giex kaing li ceep ka kraung dha,
mai tang cung ka dee cung tix kaux mawx kaux an,
taix kix giex ka teet;
pui Li-we kawn praix Ya-sa cung ka dee an tauh ka chiang.
Kix hawt tix yuh awm been pa hoik deuh ju simiang Ta-wue,
mai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
11 iKix rhax lox rhax tau,
mai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex pa kied mhawm;
krax muh nawh ka pui Yi-seu-liex koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Pui ku kaux hoik kok ka lox pa ting si num,
yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik jauh tix sum ka kaing jaong nyiex buan.
12 Cao giex kaing li pui kuad ju khawm uik,
pui Li-we mai cao kaing ceu pui ku ceu,
to kaux kix hoik yaox nyiex buan pa koe sivoe an,
yam in yaox ka jaong nyiex buan hoik baox cox sum tix,
mai ang lai pon kham ploi ka lox yiiem tix;
pui pa kawn deuk dee an hoik kok yo lox pa ting gaux rhawm si num.
13 Ang koe pui pa pon guah ka lox kok yo pa gaux rhawm mai lox pa yiiem,
mawh kheu kied ting si num lox,
mai hoik mhong ka tom dee singai heue.
