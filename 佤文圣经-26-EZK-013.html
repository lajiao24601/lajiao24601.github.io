Ne raw ka cao jhai pa pie lhien
13
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai ka lox jhai;
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka cao jhai pui Yi-seu-liex an kix pa nieb pon krai ka lox jhai.
Krai kix mhong nhie kix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex heue.”
3 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Cao jhai pa daox aw in kix tag sang hoek krax laux leeing heue!
Pa keud kix ka daux rhawm tix mawh pa giex pa going;
riang su sai pa yaox kix hoik mawh pa nieb pon yuh kix.
4 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
cao jhai pex khankix ang koe ceu awm sox la pa ot dee riem yuh nan.
5 Dee doh guh veng,
kix ang cox blaox;
guh ding pa ghrup,
kix ang cox sum.
Mawh kheu nan,
singaix bawg yam lih hoek Simiang Siyiex,
bawg yam lih naing,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang pon rien khre ka to kaux tix.
6 Riang su sai pa koe kix hoik mawh pa giex pa going;
lox jhai pa krai kix hoik mawh pa pie lhien;
kix ce tix krai ka gum lox aux,
tete te lai,
aux ang bax ploi kix hu.
Kix lai sang kroh ka lox pa hoik krai tix lih heue!
7 Aux krai ka kix ah nin:
‘Riang su sai pa hoik yaox pex mawh pa giex pa going tete;
lox jhai pa hoik krai pex mawh pa pie lhien tete.
Pex ce lox in kix hoik mawh lox pa krai aux,
tete te lai,
aux ang bax krai ceu pa tid tix ka pex heue!’ ”
8 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong baox krai ka kix ah nin:
“Pa krai pex mawh lox pa pie lhien;
riang su sai pa hoik yaox pex mawh pa giex pa going,
mawh kheu nan aux tieb tie ka pex heue.
9 Pex pa mawh cao jhai pa nieb pon yuh tix riang su sai,
pa nieb pon krai lox jhai eue,
aux tag sang ciyang ka pex heue!
Pex ang pon tix rom tau tix mai pui cie aux;
ceu kaux pex ang pon tix tiiem ot tau mai ca rang nyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pex ang doh pon tix deeh ing ka haktiex ot pui Yi-seu-liex.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong heue.
10 a“Cao jhai pie lhien ka pui cie aux,
ah nin:
‘Koe pa nhim!’
Tete te lai,
kix ang tie bax koe pa nhim.
Guh ding pa sum pui cie aux ka simaox,
cao jhai kraix simaox ceed kah.
11 Maix tag caw tix krai ka cao jhai,
guh ding pa hoik sum kix tag sang ghrup.
Aux tag sang kieh bhaung ting lhiex ting,
mai mu pre pa ting lauh guh ding in,
12 mai guh ding tag sang ghrup.
Pui tag sang chawk ah nin:
‘Pex kraix ka guh ding in koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?’ ”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang yuh guh ding in laux ka bhaung ting lhiex ting,
mai ka mu pre pa ting ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
14 Aux tag sang yieh laux ka guh ding pa hoik kraix pex an,
mai yieh si dawm,
kieh jaong bhlien tix lih,
guh ding tag sang ghrup,
mai gut pex yum.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
15 “Aux tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang pui sum guh ding,
mai ka piang pui pa kraix guh ding.
Aux tag sang krai pao ka pex,
pui pa sum guh ding mai pui pa pu phat hoik laux leeing heue.
16 Cao jhai pa ang bax pon krax pa nhim pa krai lox kaux cong ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix hoik laux leeing khawm uik.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Ne raw ka cao jhai bun pa pie lhien
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
yam in maix tag caw tix ne raw ka bun miex pa nieb pon krai ka lox jhai ka daux pui haktiex khankix.
18 Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“ ‘Pex pa mawh cub bun in kix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex ce pui ku kaux hoik mad taix tix ka maox mad phi,
hoik tawb kaing tix ka man tawb phi,b
son sang kieh mawh mhai ngai lai krax pa jhax lawg,
son sang mawh pui pa yuh kaing ka piang ju kix.
Pex keud tix sang koe riang rao tix giex gang ka krax pa im ju,
mai krax yum pui cie aux,
son sang mhawm ka to kaux tix lie?
19 Maix lai map nai aux ka plak sivoe ngai pui cie aux,
son nghox ca pa lang mu ra loe jhawp khaing,
mai son khao mun ra loe lon khaing.
Pui pa ang khaw tix yum,
pex hoik pauh yum;
pui pa khaw tix yum,
pex hoik kieh kix im.
Pex pie lhien ka pui cie aux,
maix kix kawx hoik yum rhawm ka pex heue.’ ”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux koeng ka pex pa teei maox mad phi yuh krax pa im ju pui kah.
Aux tag sang rud maox mad phi an khaing taix pex,
mai taoh ka pui pa hoik giex gang pex kah.
21 Aux tag sang riah phawng ka man tawb phi pex an,
mai ploi ka pui cie tix,
kieh kix ang lai baox ot ka daux pa giex gang pex.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
22 “Aux ang kieh pui mhawm laux rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik kieh kix laux rhawm som nai ka krax pa pie lhien tix.
Pex ang kieh pui caox tik vud krax pa caox mai pon krax taoh,
23 Mawh kheu nan,
riang su sai yuh pex mai lox jhai pa nieb pex pon krai ka hoik si dauh khawm uik.
Aux tag sang taoh pui cie tix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix pex.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
