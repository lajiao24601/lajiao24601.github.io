Lox rawg buan pui pa yaox krax laux leeing
38
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi song rhawm ne raw ka aux;
khiox khaw maix paw vi ciyang aux ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi maix.
2 Te puing maix klauh aux mat tom raux;
taix maix gut ka aux tom cien heue.
 
3 Krax pa song rhawm maix hoik kieh nex nham aux ang lai koe dee mhawm;
phi map aux hoik kieh laux leeing.
4 Aux ot ka daux phi map pa ting pa lhaong;
phi map pa cien hoik gut ka aux heue.
 
5 Mawh kheu krax pa daox aw aux,
khaix dee mat aux hoik laux hoik cux.
6 Nyoi aux hoik vawk,
blawg aux hoik kawk kheu krax pa nie saux tix.
Aux laux rhawm laux rhi leeing lang singaix.
7 Aux kham jhawm saux pa hax pa si aoh,
hoik kieh nex nham aux ang nyawm ot.
8 Aux hoik uik riang uik rao,
mai dex sang dut rhawm;
aux laux rhawm laux rhi,
mai ngia kha rhawm tix heue.
 
9 Simiang aux eue,
maix hoik tawng ka daux rhawm rhi aux;
maix hoik mhong ka lox ngia aux,
10 aux hoik greeing nex nham,
riang rao aux hoik uik;
tawm ngai aux ang lai koe pa riang.
11 Paox grawm paox blawg aux ang lai kuang krax saux aux;
pie paox nyiex pa dex ka aux kawx hoik mox tix singai hu khaing aux.
12 Pui pa sawg tix teei ka ju aux nih ka rub tix;
pui pa keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing ghlat aux ka lox pa caox pa caig tix,
ce tix sang teei krax keud pa pie lhien yuh aux ka heue.
 
13 Aux awm pui lheet yhaok,
mai ang chix mhong,
awm pui ang chix nye,
pa ang chix si kah;
14 Aux hoik awm pui pa ang chix mhong ka lox pui yuh nan,
ang chix krai lox son to kaux tix heue.
 
15 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux een rhawm tix ka maix;
cao aux pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix pawk ka lox aux.
16 Paw vi kieh pui pa caox pa caig gaux rhawm kheu krax pa laux leeing aux;
paw vi kieh kix yaw rang di kheu krax pa som nai aux.
17 Krax kha rhawm ca hawt ka aux,
aux tom sang goh tix yum pi.
18 Aux doh keud ka phi map tix;
aux laux rhawm laux rhi son krax pa lud pa loe tix.
19 Pui koeng ka aux mhawm nex nham koe riang koe rao.
Kaux pui koeng ka aux khawm ang koe krax koe grawng kied haoh hun.
20 Pui pa pawk pa caox son pa mhawm,
yuh krax pa tieb tie ka aux,
mawh kheu sang hu aux krax pa rauh see heue.
 
21 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi prah ghram ka aux;
Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi singai hu khaing aux.
22 Simiang pa mawh cao taoh ka aux eue,
khiox khaw maix nied tix hoek teem coi ka aux liak!
