Ye-sux pai moi ka pui saux si pawg
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.40–45; Lai Lu-cia 5.12–16)
8
Ye-sux lih hoek khaing piang gawng,
pui hun hawt khaix nawh.
2 Koe pui saux si pawg tix kaux hoek ka nawh,
grux ngawng tix krub ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ca mawh cu maix,
maix pon kieh aux singax heue.”
3 Ye-sux tawx taix tix giex ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux cu,
kieh maix singax heue!”
Saux si pawg nawh tom grai dum mawx dum an,
mai singax heue.
4 aMawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Paw vi krai kaing in ka pui tix kaux kawx,
tag hu ka cao giex kaing li,
kieh nawh lam lia ka maix,
khaix an hawt tix tan lu awm been pa hoik tat Maw-si tix deuh yuh nan,
mai jhi grawng pa hoik singax maix ka pui hun khankix heue.”
Ye-sux pai moi ka siliex simiang naing Law-ma
(Lai Lu-cia 7.1–10)
5 Ye-sux hoek ka haktiex Cia-pai-nong,
koe simiang naing Law-ma tix kaux hoek rieb ka nawh,
khiox khaw nawh teem coi tix,
6 ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
siliex aux koe jhawm saux yum taix caong,
mai it ka nyiex kied kha rhawm tix heue.”
7 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux hu pai ka nawh heue.”
8 Simiang naing an pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
to kaux cao maix hoek daux nyiex aux,
mai aux ang pon;
ca mawh tawx maix lox tix gum khaing,
siliex aux tag pon moi heue.
9 Awm hoik koe simiang naing pa thung ka aux,
plak greem koe pui naing aux thung ka kix yuh nan;
phan aux tawx lox ka pui tix kaux nhie nawh hu,
mai nawh tag hu.
Phan tawx lox nhie pui mawx hu,
pui an taug hu;
phan aux ah nin ka siliex tix:
‘Yuh kaing ceu in’,
nawh tag yuh hu heue.”
10 Ye-sux mhong ka lox in,
kied daox deng kah,
mai krai ka pui pa hawt khaix nawh ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
awm krax yum rhawm pa yuh nin,
ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux ang sax yaox tix kaux kawx heue.
11 bAux krai ka pex,
tag sang koe pui tom nie pa hoek khaing plak lih singaix plak klip singaix,
pa ngawm tau mai Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux ka daux meung rao ma.
12 cPui an kix caw tix sang lih mawh pui ceu meung rao ma,
tag sang tieh tix grheu kix lih ot plak praix ka daux pa faik;
ka dee an,
kix tag sang yiiem grai greeik rhang braing tix heue.”
13 Khaix an,
Ye-sux krai ka simiang naing an ah nin:
“Deeh ing heue,
hoik hawt tix yuh awm krax yum rhawm maix heue!”
Jhawm saux siliex nawh hoik moi yam an heue.
Ye-sux hoik pai moi ka pui saux tom nie
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.29, 34; Lai Lu-cia 4.38–41)
14 Ye-sux hoek ka nyiex ma Pi-teux,
mai yaox miex moeng nawh saux si aoh,
it ka piang ku.
15 Ye-sux giex ka taix nawh,
saux si aoh an hoik grai,
nawh kaoh rieb tix khan kheu ka Ye-sux dum mawx dum an heue.
16 Yam hoek plak pon bo,
koe pui viex pui pa laig phi daux tom nie hoek ka Ye-sux.
Ye-sux siao ngie lox tix gum khaing mai hoik grheu phi hu khawm uik,
mai baox pai moi ka can pui pa koe jhawm saux.
17 dNawh yuh nin jiet hoik kieh gum lox pa krai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax lih tix mawh ah nin:
nawh hoik teem ka pa iak riang iak rao ex,
hoik klawm ka jhawm saux ex heue.
Pui pa sang hawt khaix Ye-sux
(Lai Lu-cia 9.57–62)
18 Yam yaox Ye-sux lai lawm pui hun ka tix,
tom tawx lox nhie pui gau tix tiang rawm nhawng hu ka toi plak an.
19 Koe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri hoek ka nawh,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Sara,
maix hu dee mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang hawt ka maix heue.”
20 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sox praix koe daux,
sim saik koe mheem,
viang mawh nan,
pie dee deuh kawn kawn pui kaing tix piang ang koe heue.”
21 Khaix an koe pui gau tang kaux ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Simiang eue,
kieh aux ran ing si paung ka keeing tix heue.”
22 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix tawng hawt ka aux khaing,
kieh pui yum tang hu si paung ka pui yum heue!”
Ye-sux kieh bhaung ting yiien
(Lai Ma-kheu 4.35–41; Lai Lu-cia 8.22–25)
23 Ye-sux laig daux reu,
pui gau nawh hu tau mai nawh.
24 Dum mawx dum an,
bhaung ting phlu piang rawm nhawng,
puk pak rawm kheem ka reu,
viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux laig it.
25 Pui gau hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
mai so ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
taoh ka yix liak,
yix hoik dex sang laux ju heue!”
26 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai lhat yuh nin lie?
Krax yum rhawm pex kied iak heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh kaoh,
mai ne raw ka bhaung mai ka puk pak rawm,
bhaung mai puk pak rawm tom yiien heue.
27 Pui hun kied daox deng kah,
mai ra lai ah nin:
“Pui in mawh pui mawx,
pie bhaung mai puk pak rawm kawx hoik nghiet ka lox nawh heue!”
Pai moi ka pui pa laig phi daux ra kaux
(Lai Ma-kheu 5.1–20; Lai Lu-cia 8.26–39)
28 Ye-sux hu ka haktiex plak toi rawm nhawng Cia-ta-la,
mai yaox ka pui pa lih khaing daux si meeig ra kaux.
Pui in kiex mawh pui pa laig phi daux,
kied cau nyhau,
kheu nan ang koe pui pa pon hu ka krax in.
29 Kiex yaox ka Ye-sux,
mai kok yo dum mawx dum an ah nin:
“Kawn Siyiex,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tuk tiak ka yiex lie?
Bawg yam ang bax hoek maix lai hoek kieh yiex kha rhawm tix lie?”
30 Yam an,
baoh dex dee an koe cub phung lig ih ceu pa ih.
31 Phi caox tom khiox khaw Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan maix grheu yiex hu,
grheu yiex laig ka daux cub phung lig heue.”
32 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Hu heue!”
Phi caox tom lih,
mai laig ka daux cub phung lig;
cub phung lig tom preeng tix lih ka yoeh rang,
mai laig daux nhawng,
yum khawm uik heue.
33 Pui pa liang lig tom yaog tix to ing ka veng,
mai krai grawng kaing pa hoik yaox tix sidawng,
mai ka grawng pui pa laig phi daux ka pui hun.
34 Pui pa ot daux veng khawm uik hoek tix jhak Ye-sux,
kix hoik yaox ka nawh,
tom khiox khaw nawh lih hu khaing haktiex kix heue.
