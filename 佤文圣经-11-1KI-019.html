Yi-li-yax ot ka gawng Heu-liex
19
Simiang Ya-ha krai ka can kaing pa yuh Yi-li-yax ku ceu,
mai ka grawng pa pauh nawh ka can cao jhai Pa-lix ka moeng tix Yie-si-pix.
2 Yie-si-pix tom ploi kawn cau tix hu ka Yi-li-yax ah nin:
“Pa sax bawg yam in,
phan aux ang hawt tix yuh awm pa yuh maix ka cao jhai aux khankix,
kieh siyiex pauh ka aux!”
3 Yi-li-yax lhat kah,
nawh tom to mox tix,
mai viex ka siliex tix hu to mox tix ka Piie-see-pa haktiex Yo-tax heue.
Yi-li-yax deuh siliex tix ot dee an,
4 acao nawh hu krax been tix ngaix,
mai hoek daux tiag.
Nawh ngawm ka greem veei khaox gix khiox khaw tix yum;
nawh rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux ang lai pon kham gee,
teei ka ju aux hu heue,
kieh aux yum khaing!”
5 Nawh blauh tix it greem veei khaox,
mai laig it.
Dum mawx dum an,
koe kawn cau Siyiex hoek tix dieh ka nawh,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Haing heue,
ih ka ceu pa ih!”
6 Nawh blah ngai tix jhak ka khawm pon daog,
mai yaox ka khao mun pa hoik tong tix ka gaox ngu tix lon mai rawm tix kawng pa ot ka kaing gaong tix.
Nawh nyaux ih kah,
mai baox blauh tix it.
7 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex hoek ka daux ra bawg,
dieh nawh mai ah nin:
“Kaoh,
ih ceu pa ih!
Phan ang mawh nan,
krax hu maix kawn singai,
maix rien tix ang pon kham.”
8 Yi-li-yax kaoh,
ih nyaux kah.
Priex preem kieh nawh koe riang rao,
nawh hu krax tipon ngai tix pon sawm,
mai hoek ka gawng Heu-liex pa singax singeh.
9 Nawh paung it ka daux dawh rang dee an tix sawm heue.
Dum mawx dum an,
Simiang Siyiex si kah mai nawh ah nin:
“Yi-li-yax,
maix ot tin yuh ceu pa tix?”
10 bYi-li-yax pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing eue,
aux hoik viex rhawm tix muh ka maix.
Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex tik vud ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh maix tau mai kix,
kix hoik yieh ka khu dee tan maix,
mai baox pauh ka cao jhai maix khawm uik.
Yam in,
deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing,
kix kawn sang pauh ka aux!”
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Lih hu,
cung ka piang gawng,
cung ka sivoe aux.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoek tix lai ling,
kieh bhaung ting hoek tix phlu gawng bih simaox max.
Bhaung hoik naox tix,
khaix an kla reem tuk,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang ot daux kla reem.
12 Khaix hoik tuk kla reem ngu lih,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex kawn ang ot nang ka daux ngu.
Khaix ngu,
Yi-li-yax mhong lox pa luah iak heue.
13 Yi-li-yax mhong lox an,
tom tawb pak ngai tix ka sibex tix,
mai lih cung ka moeng daux rang.
Koe lox pa ah nin ka nawh:
“Yi-li-yax,
maix yuh pa tix tin?”
14 Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing eue,
aux hoik viex rhawm tix muh ka maix.
Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex tik vud ka maix,
mai ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh maix tau mai kix,
kix hoik yieh ka khu dee tan maix,
mai baox pauh ka cao jhai maix khawm uik.
Yam in,
deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing,
kix kawn sang pauh ka aux!”
15 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Maix deeh ing ka krax prim,
hoek ka Ta-ma-see-keux dex ka daux tiag,
khaix an laig daux veng mai groi rawm bee yaog Ha-shiiex mawh simiang pui Shui-li-yax,
16 dGroi rawm bee yaog kawn simex Ling-see pa kaux Yie-hu mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
baox groi rawm bee yaog kawn simex Sa-fa pa kaux Yi-li-sa pui Ya-paw Mi-heu-la,
rieb tix mawh cao jhai tang maix.
17 Pui mawx pa luat luan khaing lawm vaig Ha-shiiex,
Yie-hu tag sang pauh pui an yum;
pui mawx pa luat luan khaing lawm vaig Yie-hu,
Yi-li-sa tag pauh ka pui an.
18 eViang mawh nan,
aux tag sang teei tix deuh pui khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liex aliah rheng kaux;
pui in kix hoik mawh pui pa yum rhawm ka aux,
mai ang bax grux ngawng tix krub ka Pa-lix,
mai ang bax ngheet nang riang rao pa krub nawh heue.”
Kok ka Yi-li-sa
19 Yi-li-yax lih hu khaing dee an,
mai yaox ka kawn simex Sa-fa pa kaux Yi-li-sa.
Yi-li-sa jiet taug hawt ka moi tix brawm pa thai ka kaung,
kawn koe moi kao brawm tix pa koe sivoe nawh;
nawh hawt ka pa ot khaix paox tix tix brawm.
Yi-li-yax peeik ka sibex dha tix,
mai lhop ka piang Yi-li-sa.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-sa lih hu khaing moi tix,
mai hawt leue ka Yi-li-yax ah nin:
“Krox jun aux ngheet ka miex keeing tix son sang hu khaing kix ha,
khaix an aux tom hawt khaix maix heue.”
Yi-li-yax ah nin:f
“Chix tix,
maix ing heue.
Aux ang tieb tie ka maix!”
21 Yi-li-sa tom ing,
mai pauh ka moi tix tix brawm,
kieh khaox thai mawh khix taung,
mai ngaik nex kah.
Nawh guah pui hun ih nex;
kix tom ih kah,
khaix an,
Yi-li-sa hawt ka Yi-li-yax,
mai mawh cao pa teem coi nawh heue.
