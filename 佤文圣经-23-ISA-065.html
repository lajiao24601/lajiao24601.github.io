Siyiex ciyang ka pui pa tieh vheen
65
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw kix ku bawg ku yam,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang khiox khaw.
Aux krox jun kix hoek sawg tix,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang hoek.
Viang aux hoik ca kok yo ka kix ah nin:
‘Aux ot tin,
aux tag sang teem coi ka maix.’
Viang mawh nan,
in hoik mawh meung pa ang rawg ang mhaing ka aux heue.
2 bAux hoik ca rieb tix teei ka pui cie tix pa hoik tieh vheen ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
kix ca kawn kiien rhawm nieb pon yuh tix,
mai ca hawt ka alo tix.
3 Kix ot ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai ca kawn cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm.
Kix hoik yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa ah kix mawh ka daux ‘si peem tai pa singax singeh’,
mai gleeing nyawng ka piang khu dee tan been li pui tang ceu tang cad.
4 Yam pon sawm,
kix hoek ka daux dawh rang,
mai hu sawg tix chawk cheng khuan pui yum ka gawng si meeig.
Kix ih ka nex lig,
nyaux ka rawm rheep nex krax tan,
5 khaix an ah nin ka pui tang kaux:
‘Paw vi hu dex ka yix;
yix hoik kied singax singeh tete,
mai ang pon tix theeh ka heue.’
Aux ang lai pon kham ka kaing pa yuh kix;
krax pa song rhawm aux ka kix hoik awm ngu pa ang chix yeet yuh nan heue.
6 “Aux hoik but rhawm tix grawng sang ciyang ka kix;
aux hoik tiiem ka lai prawng.
Aux ang doh lai va rhawm ka kix;
7 aux tag sang pawk bix ka kix pa yuh kix mai pa yuh tax du kix kheu phi map.
Kix hoik gleeing nyawng ka daux nyiex vad pa ot piang gawng,
mai hoik salao aux ka dee an,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang hawt tix ciyang ka kix awm krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix heue.”
8 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Ang koe pui pa dug dig ka plix siiet pa mhawm;
kix tag sang teei plix siiet pa mhawm cia plai lih kah.
Aux kawx ang yuh laux leeing ka pui cie aux pa hoik muh aux kah;
aux tag sang taoh ka pui pa yuh kaing krub ka aux khankix heue.
9 Aux tag sang tawx buan ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa mawh ceu pui Yo-tax khankix;
kawn praix kix tag sang pon tix cie ka gawng cie aux.
Ceu pui pa hoik laug aux pa yuh kaing jhaw pa krub ka aux tag sang pon ka haktiex an,
mai paung ot dee an.
10 cKix tag sang krub ka aux,
mai liang yung ka dee lung Sa-loeng pa koe ka plak klip singaix,
mai liang moi ka dee ‘ngawd krax laux leeing’ pa koe ka plak lih singaix heue.
11 “Viang mawh nan,
pex pa mawh pui in kix pa hoik tik vud ka aux ang mawh nan.
Pex map nai ka Shi-ang pa mawh gawng pa singax singeh aux,
mai hu krub ka Cia-tix mai Mi-ni pa ah pui mawh pa mhawm son siyiex pa tawx krax pa im ju.
12 Pex hoik tat tix yum greem lawm vaig;
mawh kheu kok yo aux ka pex,
pex ang pawk,
aux krai lox,
pex ang nghiet.
Pex su tix ang nghiet ka aux,
mai yuh kaing ceu pa ang mhawm pa ang aux khaw rhawm kah.
13 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka pex,
can pui pa krub ka aux,
nghiet ka lox aux hoik koe priex preem tom kub,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang kham krax pa cai rhawm.
Kix tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang hoek krax kaig krax tua heue.
14 Kix tag sang gaux rhawm rhax ka lai,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang yiiem grai,
mai laux rhawm laux rhi.
15 Pui pa hoik laug aux tag sang kieh ceu kaux pex mawh pa jhax pa lawg.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pex yum,
viang mawh nan,
ka pui pa nghiet ka aux,
mai aux tag sang tawx ceu kaux pa khraox ka kix.
16 Ka piang haktiex in,
pui pa khiox khaw ka buan son ku kaux tag sang pon tix hoek khiox khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex pa kaux sijax;
pui tag sang tiex tu ku kaux,
tag sang tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex pa kaux sijax.
Mawh kheu krax kha rhawm ka daux singaix pa hoik luan hoik grai hu khawm uik heue.”
Pa phan yuh pa khraox
17 dSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang phan yuh ka rao ma pa khraox mai ka haktiex pa khraox.
Kaing ceu pa koe sivoe an hoik grai khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh koe rhoi khaix ceeix kawx.
18 Pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu pa hoik phan yuh aux ka ku ceu khawm uik.
Yie-lu-sa-leung pa khraox pa hoik phan yuh aux tag sang naog krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
pui pa ot ka daux veng tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
19 eAux kawx tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu Yie-lu-sa-leung,
gaux rhawm gaux rhi son pui pa ot daux veng.
Dee an,
ang lai baox koe pa yiiem,
ang lai koe lox riag yo krax laux rhawm.
20 Kawn ngie ang lai chix yum yam keh tix;
kuad song hoik lang ju ku kaux.
Pui pa im ju koe tix yieh num,
mai kawn sawn nawn mawh pui num ju;
pui pa yum yam ang tix nyang koe tix yieh num,
chix tix een mawh pui pa pon krax jhax lawg aux.
21 “Kix sum ka nyiex,
mai to kaux kix yaox tix laig ot daux;
kix sum ka plix siiet,
mai to kaux kix yaox tix nyaux ka plai pa greeik tix.
22 Kix ang doh kieh pui tang kaux laig ka daux nyiex ma tix,
phan ang mawh nan,
ang kieh nyaux ka plai cie tix.
Pui pa hoik laug aux khankix tag sang lang ju awm khaox ox yuh nan;
kix tag sang yaox tix ih nyaux khaing daux kaing pa yuh tix tom rheeng.
23 Can kaing pa yuh kix hoik pon lih tix mhawm khawm uik;
kawn praix kix ang yaox ka krax laux krax leeing.
Aux tag sang tawx buan son ka kix mai ka kawn praix kix.
24 Sivoe sang kok yo khiox khaw kix,
aux hoik pawk ka lox kix;
sivoe sang uik lox rawg buan kix,
aux hoik nghiet ka lox kix.
25 fSox la mai kawn yung tag sang ih ceu pa ih tau;
sivai sang si tag sang sawm rib tau mai moi;
si uing ang lai baox chix tud pui mat leet.
Shi-ang pa mawh ka piang gawng pa singax singeh cie aux,
ang lai baox koe kaing ceu pa kieh pui laux leeing,
mai ka kaing ceu pa caox pa caig.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
