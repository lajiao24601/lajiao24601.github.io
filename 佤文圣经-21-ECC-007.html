Dee ang krax lhak mai krax daox khaw paox
7
aMin ceu kaux pa mhawm kawn lheu mhawm khaing rawm sidah bhawng pa ngawh;
singaix yam yum kawn lheu mhawm khaing singaix yam keh heue.
2 Hu ka nyiex pui pa yum kawn lheu mhawm khaing hu ka nyiex pa khan kheu ka priex preem;
mawh kheu pui pa kawn im ju khankix caw tix kieh to kaux tix tawng jheeng jheeng,
krax yum hoik krox jun ka pui ku kaux heue.
3 Krax laux rhawm lheu mhawm khaing krax gaux rhawm;
viang pak ngai koe krax laux rhawm kawx,
mai kieh daux rhawm nie koe pa lhak lhiao heue.
4 Pui pa daox aw chix keud ngie krax gaux rhawm;
pui pa lhak lhiao hoik tawng grawng pa chix die ju.
5 Krax pa ne raw pui pa lhak lhiao kawn lheu mhawn khaing krax pa yaog yaw pui pa daox aw.
6 Krax pa nyiah pui pa daox aw hoik mawh awm lox taoh pang kat daux ngu yuh nan.
In mawh nang pa giex pa going heue.
7 Yam gut kheem pui pa lhak lhiao ka pui tang kaux khankix,
krax pa yuh nawh hoik awm pui pa daox aw yuh nan.
Pui pa rieb ka mau chun praok hoik laux yuh pak peung to kaux nawh.
8 Dee si dauh kaing lheu mhawm khaing yam jauh.
Krax gun krax yeung lheu mhawm khaing krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
9 bPaw vi song rhawm yie yie;
pa kieh krax pa koeng koe ka daux to kaux tix mawh pui daox aw.
10 Paw vi chawk:
“Yuh ka mawx singaix pa hoik luan dix kawn lheu mhawm khaing singaix yam in lie?”
In ang mawh lox pa chawk pui pa lhak lhiao heue.
11 Pui pa koe ju caw tix mawh pa koe ping nyia cuyi;
phan hoik koe ping nyia cuyi mai baox koe sin ngao nyiex ma nie blawn mhawm.
12 Grawng pa kieh ping nyia cuyi maix nyawm ot,
ang lai yawm khaing pa kieh mau khri maix mhawm.
Ping nyia cuyi pon kieh maix kaux cong;
in hoik mawh dee mhawm ngai lig ngai lai heue.
13 Tag caw tix keud ka kaing pa yuh Siyiex.
Phan nawh hoik yuh pa vawk,
pui mawx pon kieh deeh chix rauh lie?
14 Yam mhawm kaing yuh maix ku ceu,
maix tag caw tix gaux rhawm;
yam yaox maix krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
paw vi piawm ka krax gaux rhawm mai krax kha rhawm kha rhi hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex;
kaing pa sang lih tix mawh singaix pa sang hoek sax,
pui mawx sang pon tawng liec?
15 Ka daux krax pa im ju kawn pui daux pa giex pa going,
aux hoik lam lia ka kaing pa koe tom nie ceu.
Pui mhawm yuh kaing pa mhawm,
mai tieh tix dawt ju,
pui caox yuh kaing pa caox,
mai tieh tix lang ju.
16 Maix ang siao pon tix yuh kaing pa mhawm tom nie,
mai ang pon tix nie luan koe krax lhak pa lhiao,
tang deeh kieh to kaux tix laux leeing yuh ka mawx lie?
17 Viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix nie caox jhawm cau nyhau,
mai kieh tix dawt ju yuh ka mawx lie?
18 In kiex khawm ra ceu caw ang kieh lih,
mawh ngie pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex pa pon yaox tix mhawm kaing yuh khaing heue.
19 Krax pa teem coi pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing kaing mhawm pa yuh simiang naing ka veng kao kaux.
20 Ka piang haktiex ang koe pui pa pon yuh kaing pa mhawm jheeng jheeng,
mai ang sax yuh krax lud loe tix bawg kawx.
21 Paw vi deuh lox krai pui ka daux rhawm tix;
maix tee rien tix chix mhong lox pa map nai siliex maix ka maix.
22 Viang mawh nan,
paw vi piawm,
maix hoik sax map nai nang pui tang kaux miex bawg mawx heue.
23 Aux hoik siao krax ping nyia cuyi tix cheuk tix yuh ka kaing in khawm uik.
Aux hoik but rhawm tix sawg ka krax koe ping nyia cuyi,
viang mawh nan,
ping nyia cuyi kied singai ot khaing aux.
24 Koe pui mawx pa pon tawng dee bleeh krax im ju lie?
Nawh kied raux,
nu tix tawng sidawng heue.
25 Aux kied simie tix tawng,
sang keud grawng in,
sang sawg krax ping nyia cuyi,
pawk ka lox pa chawk aux;
aux tom tawng krax caox mawh pa daox,
krax daox mawh pa cau nyhau heue.
26 Aux yaox koe bun tix blah kawn lheu kin lhat khaing krax yum.
Krax muh nawh hoik awm daux o,
awm rub;
plawng taix pa kawm nawh maix hoik awm maox ceng tix nyaing.
Pui pa pon krax khaw rhawm Siyiex hoik pon tix luad luan khaing daux taix nawh,
viang mawh nan,
nawh tag pon giex ka pui pa yuh phi map.
27 Cao tawx lox sidiex ah nin:
in mawh kaing pa yaox aux tix ceu hoik tix ceu yam sawg tix lox pawk lih.
28 Aux sang sawg ka lox pawk tang ceu,
viang mawh nan,
ang aux yaox.
Ka daux si nax simex kaing rheng kaux,
aux chix yaox pa khaw rhawm tix tix kaux,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux bun,
tix kaux kawx ang yaox.
29 Kaing pa gau aux koe ngie tix grawng:
Siyiex yuh kawn pui hoik mawh pa yie,
viang mawh nan,
kix kieh to kaux tix mawh pa nu yuh nin.
