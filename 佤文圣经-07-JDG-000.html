Lai Cao-prawng
Krai dawt
“Cao prawng” mawh pui pa ao min ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex dix dix. Lai Cao-prawng mawh pa tiiem grawng khaix hoik laig pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux haktiex Cia-nang, hoek ka baux bawg yam pa sum ka meung haktiex, daux yam pa suk sag heue. Grawng been in kaing ting mawh pa krai grawng ao min pui kao ceu ra; tom nie kix mawh simiang naing, mai ang mawh pui cao prawng pa giex been. Ka daux kix pa kied ao min mawh Chang-suing; grawh grawng kaing pa yuh nawh hoik tiiem ka daux lai in simin ting kao loe tom hoek kao liah heue.
Phuk lai in kaing ting krai sidawng, ka krax pa im ju pui Yi-seu-liex hoik mawh ka daux pa een rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix. Yam ang kix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex, kix hoik yaox krax pa laux pa leeing; viang mawh nan, yam deeh veeh kix tix ka Siyiex, mai Siyiex tag pluat ploi ka phi map kix jheeng jheeng, mai taoh ka kix heue.
Krai kaing
1. Kaing pa ting khankix sivoe sang yum Yaw-su-yax (1.1–2.10)
2. Cao prawng ceu pui Yi-seu-liex (2.11–16.31)
3. Grawng kaing tang ceu (17.1–21.25)
