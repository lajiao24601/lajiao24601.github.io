Maw-ya laux leeing
48
aPhan mawh Maw-ya,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex krai yuh nin:
Ni-paw hoik yaox krax laux leeing,
mai riem heue!
Ci-lie-thing kham krax kaig krax tua,
mai pui hoik pon teei kah.
Tab cie nawh hoik yieh laux,
pui pa ot daux hoik kham krax kaig krax tua heue.
2 Pa jhan riang Maw-yax hoik grai hu.
Pui koeng hoik pon teei ka Si-seu-peung,
mai hoik keud sawn tix sang yuh Maw-ya laux leeing.
Pui hoik gut kheem ka Ma-teu-miien,
mai nawh ang lai koe lox krai;
pui pa ot ka daux nawh,
vaig gawn tag sang leue ka kix.
3 Pui haktiex Heu-law-nian kok ngia lox si num ah nin:
hoik riem,
hoik laux leeing heue!
 
4 Maw-ya hoik laux leeing;
lox yiiem kawn nyawm hoek ka Saw-eux.
5 Pa haok ka blaong Lu-si,
hoik mhong lox yiiem kix;
pa lih ka Heu-law-nian hawt krax,
hoik mhong ka lox laux rhawm riag yo kix.
6 Kix kok yo ah nin:
to heue,
nied to tix klawd ju heue!
To hu awm la tiag yuh nan heue!
 
7 Maw-ya eue,
maix hoik sax een rhawm tix ka riang rao pui naing tix,
mai sin ngao tix,
yam in,
pui kawx tag sang pon kuang ka maix.
Siyiex tang kaux maix Ci-mawx tag sang vu vie;
cao giex kaing li,
cao kaing nawh kawx tag sang giex kix hu tau.
8 Can veng khawm uik tag sang laux leeing,
mai tix mu kawx ang deuh kah;
ka daux ngawd khankix,
tag sang dug dig kah,
ka dee lung khankix,
tag sang kham krax pa dig pui.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
9 Tag caw tix sum na bat si meeig son Maw-ya;
ang rheeng nawh tag sang laux leeing.
Veng nawh tag sang lih mawh dee riem,
ang lai baox koe pui pa paung ot kah.
(
10 Pui pa ang yuh kaing Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix tag kham krax pa jhax lawg!
Pui pa ang chuh taix teei vaig pauh ka pui koeng,
tag kham krax pa jhax lawg heue!)
Veng Maw-ya laux leeing
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Jauh tom yam sum ka meung,
Maw-ya hoik nyawm ot ang koe krax khi rhawm,
mai ang sax pon krax pa giex pui.
Maw-ya awm plai pa hoik nyip pui moing ka daux kawng yuh nan,
ang lai hieh jee hieh blaong,
mai pon yaox tix koe taox bhawng tom rheeng,
taox bhawng ang lai chix tieh loh tix heue.
12 “Jhak heue,
yam hoek bawg yam,
aux tag sang ploi pui hieh ka Maw-ya,
awm hieh ka plai yuh nan.
Kix tag sang hieh uik singax ka kawng plai,
mai pauh max ka kawng plai.
13 Pui Maw-ya tag sang grai krax yum rhawm khaing siyiex tang kaux kix Ci-mawx,
awm hoik grai krax yum rhawm pui Yi-seu-liex khaing siyiex tang kaux Pe-theu-lix,
pa hoik een kix rhawm tix ka yuh nan.
14 Pui Maw-ya eue,
yuh ka mawx lai tang kok tix mawh pui vao lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai tang kok tix mawh pui naing pa kua pa mhawm puing naing lie?
15 Maw-ya mai veng nawh tag sang riem khawm uik;
pui naing pa nhum ju pa kua khankix hoik laux leeing.
Aux mawh simiang;
ceu kaux aux mawh Yie-heu-hua,
pa mawh simiang kru naing,
aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
16 Singaix bawg yam si dauh Maw-ya hoik nied tix dex,
ang lai koe miex glang tix tag sang riem heue.
 
17 Meung pa ot sivie siveng Maw-ya eue,
ceu pui pa hoik mhong grawng pa ao min nawh eue,
pex tag sang yiiem doe ah nin:
siawp sibhawm pa yuh kaing ceu pa ang khaw hoik laux leeing heue!
Min plak ngai riang rao nawh ang lai yaox heue!
18 Pui pa paung ot ka Ti-peung eue,
lih khaing dee ngawm pex pa koe min plak ngai,
mai hu ngawm ka daux phawng tiex heue!
Cao pa yuh laux leeing ka Maw-ya hoik koek;
nawh hoik yuh laux leeing ka tab Maw-ya.
19 Pui haktiex Ya-law-eu eue,
cung tix jhak ka praok krax,
mai chawk tix yaox ka pui to pa ang koe nyiex koe ma,
hoik mawh kaing ceu pa tix pa lih tix mawh lie?
20 Kix tag sang pawk ah nin:
Maw-ya hoik pon krax pa kaig tua laux leeing heue!
Yiiem grai son nawh heue!
Pex tag caw tix krai pao ka praok rawm klawng Ya-noeng ah nin:
Maw-ya hoik laux leeing heue!
21 “Krax prawng ca lih hoek ka piang veng pa koe ka daux lung khankix,
pa mawh:
Heu-loeng,
Ya-cax,
Mi-fa-ya,
22 Ti-peung,
Ni-paw,
Paw Ti-pi-la-thai-ying,
23 Ci-lie-thing,
Paw Cia-mawx,
Paw Mi-eung,
24 Cia-liiex,
Paw-see-la.
Krax prawng ca lih hoek ka piang veng pa singai pa dex ka Maw-ya khawm uik.
25 Riang rao pui naing Maw-ya hoik laux,
siawp sibhawm nawh hoik laux leeing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Maw-ya kham krax pa kaig kua
26 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix kieh Maw-ya yoek,
mawh kheu ting rhawm nawh,
mai hoik tieh vheen ka aux.
Maw-ya tag sang lu lieh tix ot ka daux ceu pa hoik hao tix lih khaing daux jheeh tix,
mai pui tag sang nyiah ka nawh.
27 Maw-ya eue,
maix tawng ang tawng ka pa hoik sax nyiah maix ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
yam krai ka ceu kaux kix,
maix tag vuk vied kaing tix,
hoik kieh kix awm giex pui briex ang mawx lie?
28 “Pui Maw-ya eue,
maix tag caw tix to lih khaing veng,
mai hu paung ot ka daux rang heue!
Awm yuh si kaux mheem tix ka daux ngawd pa raux yuh nan heue!
29 Maw-ya kied ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
Aux hoik mhong grawng pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi kix,
ting rhawm cau nyhau kix,
pa een to kaux tix mawh pa ting ceu heue!
30 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik tawng grawng pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi kix.
Tete te lai,
pa yaw rang di kix hoik mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik,
kaing pa yuh kix hoik mawh pa ang koe riah koe rawng.
31 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang laux rhawm yiiem grai son pui Maw-ya,
mai pui Cie-eu Ha-lie-seux khawm uik.
32 Aux yiiem doe son pui Si-pi-ma,
tag sang nie laux rhawm yiiem doe son pui Ya-shiex.
Veng Si-pi-ma eue,
maix hoik awm khaox plix siiet tix gawng,
kak khaox in hoik tawx tix laig ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
mai tawx tix hoek tom Ya-shiex.
Viang mawh nan,
cao pa yuh laux leeing hoik yuh laux ka plix bawg yam raux praix mai ka plix siiet maix.
33 Haktiex Maw-ya pa mhawm khankix,
ang lai baox koe lox gaux rhawm.
Aux hoik kieh dung krid plai ang lai baox lih jauh ka plai pa nyawm;
dee an ang lai koe pui pa gaux rhawm ka krid tix plai heue.
34 “Pui Si-seu-peung,
mai pui Yi-li-ya-lix yiiem doe.
Lox yiiem kix hoek ka Ya-cax;
pui Saw-eux hoik mhong kah,
pie pui Heu-law-nian,
mai pui Yi-ci-la See-li-see-yax kawx hoik mhong kah;
mawh kheu hoik kroh rawm klawng iak Ling-ling.
35 Aux tag sang tieb tie Krax-tan Pa-gleeing pa yuh pui Maw-ya ka piang khu dee tan pa koe ka gawng iak,
mai ang kieh kix gleeing nyawm bhawng ka siyiex tang kaux tix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
36 “Rhawm rhi aux hoik laux rhawm son pui Maw-ya,
mai pui Cie-eu Ha-lie-seux,
awm pui pa teei siao phlu rhax ka lox rhax yiiem doe,
mawh kheu ceu pa koe kix hoik uik khawm uik heue.
37 Kix hoik khlao ka hauk kaing tix ghle,
mai hoik khlao ka hauk jheeh tix singax.
Kix hoik pat ka taix tix mat,
mai ceep ka kraung man keei sum.
38 Can ka piang braux Nyiex ma ka daux haktiex Maw-ya,
mai ka pak dee klieh pui,
hoik mawh lox yiiem pui khawm uik;
mawh kheu hoik lauh max aux Maw-ya,
awm hoik lauh max ka kawng pa ang pui lai siao yuh nan.
39 Ploi lox yiiem tom ting heue,
hoik yuh Maw-ya laux leeing heue!
Maw-ya hoik kham krax pa kaig tua heue!
Maw-ya hoik riem;
meung haktiex pa koe sivie siveng nawh khankix hoik nyiah ka nawh khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Maw-ya ang lai koe krax to
40 Simiang Siyiex ah nin,
tag sang koe meung tix meung pa sang awm klang ting pa nih preeik tix pu,
mai preeng tix lih tuh ka Maw-ya,
41 mai pon tix cie ka veng nawh,
mai ka tab cie nawh.
Yam an,
pui naing Maw-ya tag sang si yaung awm bun yha pa saux sang keh yuh nan.
42 Maw-ya tag sang laux leeing,
ang lai baox lih mawh meung tix meung,
mawh kheu hoik ting rhawm lhaong rhi nawh,
hoik tieh vheen ka aux.
43 Krax lhat krax graung,
daux o,
daux jhawk taong hoik krox jun ka pui Maw-ya.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
44 Pa keud tix sang luat luan khaing krax lhat krax graung,
tag sang goh tix laig daux o;
pa mo tix lih khaing daux o;
tag sang krix ka daux jhawk;
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik but rhawm tix tat ka singaix bawg pa sang ciyang ka Maw-ya.
45 Pui pa hoik uik riang rao pa ang koe nyiex koe ma khankix,
ce tix sang to hu mox tix ka Si-seu-peung,
son sang khiox khaw krax teem coi,
viang mawh nan,
veng pa giex simiang Si-hong kaing ka piang,
hoik laux leeing khawm uik.b
Ngu hoik gleeing ka mee pa tieb tie,
mai ka tab cie pui Maw-ya laux leeing khawm uik.
46 Pui Maw-ya hoik si pup krax pa laux leeing heue!
Pui pa krub siyiex Ci-mawx khankix hoik laux leeing heue!
Kawn praix kix hoik giex hu khawm uik heue.
47 Viang mawh nan,
tix ngaix sax,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pui Maw-ya deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma tix heue.
Grawng pa prawng ca Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-ya hoik die tin heue.
