Lox rawg buan pa khiox khaw krax teem coia
142
bAux khiox khaw krax teem krax coi Simiang Siyiex;
aux kok yo lox pa ting si num ka nawh.
2 Aux krai lih grawng pa kha rhawm rhi tix ka nawh khawm uik;
aux sang krai lao krax kha rhawm kha rhi ka nawh khawm uik.
3 Yam hoik dex sang deek rhawm deek rhi aux,
nawh hoik tawng kaing pa sang khaw aux tix yuh.
Ka piang krax lawng aux,
pui pa koeng ka aux nih tix taong rub mai sang yuh aux laux leeing.
4 Aux jhak plak goix plak dawm,
ang yaox paox grawm pa hoek teem coi tix;
aux ang yaox krax pa sang to tix luat luan;
ang koe pui pa kia ngien ka aux heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang khiox khaw krax pa teem coi maix.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux;
maix mawh pa sang lo aux ka daux singaix yam in khawm uik.
6 Khiox khaw maix nghiet lox pa kok yo aux maix teem coi tix,
mawh kheu hoik cawd aux daux haktiex deek rhawm deek rhi.
Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pa gut kheem pui,
mawh kheu ting riang rao kix khaing aux.
7 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeingc,
kieh aux pon tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux maix;
pui rauh pui see tag sang ot sivie siveng aux,
mawh kheu hoik kia ngien maix aux ka daux buan son tix heue.
