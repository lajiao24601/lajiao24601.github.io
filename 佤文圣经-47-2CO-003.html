Siliex sijax mukheu pa khraox
3
Ex sang baox yaog to kaux tix lhaong lie?
Ex kawx khex sang awm pui tix blah pa sang lo tix tawx ka pex,
phan ang mawh nan,
tag sang teei tix tawx lai in ka pex lie?
2 Pex hoik mawh lai pa teei ex tix tawx in,
tiiem ot ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
kieh pui ku kaux tawng,
kieh pui ku kaux phat.
3 aIn hoik tawng sidawng,
to kaux Khrit hoik tiiem ka lox lai in,
mai kieh ex mawh cao pa sidiex.
Lox lai in ang mawh pa teei pui rawm meug tiiem ka piang khlawp simaox,
hoik mawh pa teei khuan Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju tiiem ot daux rhawm kawn pui heue.
4 Ex krai yuh nin,
mawh kheu hoik koe ex krax yum rhawm pa yuh nin ka daux Khrit.
5 In ang mawh krai een ex rhawm tix ka krax pa chix to kaux tix yuh kaing in;
krax pa chix ex hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex.
6 bNawh kieh ex koe krax pa chix mawh siliex sijax mukheu pa khraox;
sijax mukheu in ang mawh lox tat jhawm prawng ka ngai lai,
mai hoik mawh sijax mukheu Khuan Singax Singeh.
Lox tat jhawm prawng ka ngai lai viex krax yum,
viang mawh nan,
Khuan Singax Singeh tawx ju heue.
7 cLox tat jhawm prawng in mawh pa teei ngai lai tiiem ka piang khlawp simaox,
yam krai tix tawx hu,
min plak ngai Siyiex hoik jhi tix lih.
Viang pa riang rawm siang pa jhan ka pak ngai Maw-si hoik bheeik hu si nyek si nyek,
pa jhan riang an ca kawn ting,
kieh pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai pon dong ngai tix ka nawh.
Phan lox tat jhawm prawng pa kieh pui yum an ca kawn viex min plak ngai hoek,
8 phan mawh nan,
riang rao pa siao Khuan Singax Singeh khex ang sang nie blawn koe min plak ngai lie?
9 Phan riang rao pa siao krax prawng ca phi map chix viex min plak ngai hoek,
riang rao pa siao pa krai pao ang pui koe phi map an,
tag sang nie viex min plak ngai pa blawn ting hoek heue!
10 Tete te lai,
ex chix tix krai yuh nin,
kheu hoik koe ex min plak ngai pa nie pruh riang yam in,
pa jhan riang sivoe an tag sang grai.
11 Phan pa grai an hu si nyek si nyek kawn koe min plak ngai,
ceu pa koe tom rheeng an tag sang nie blawn ting min plak ngai heue.
12 Mawh kheu hoik koe ex krax keud pot jun pot pa yuh nin,
ex koe rhawm kua pa kied ting.
13 dEx ang awm Maw-si yuh nan,
nawh tag caw tix teei man khoek tawb pak ngai tix,
son sang kieh pa riang ang pui Yi-seu-liex chix yaox grai hu si nyek si nyek.
14 Viang mawh nan,
krax pa chix keud chix tawng kix hoik klan.
Hoek tom singaix yam in,
yam phat kix ka phuk lai sijax mukheu pa prim khankix,
rhawm rhi kix kawn tawb tix ka man khoek yuh nan.
Kheu nan mawh ngie yam si doe pui tix tau mai Khrit,
man tawb in tom tag pon khleeing tix hu.
15 Tom hoek singaix yam in,
yam phat kix ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si ku bawg,
man khoek an ca kawn tawb rhawm rhi kix.
16 fViang mawh nan,
awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Yam veeh Maw-si tix plak Simiang Siyiex,
man khoek ka pak ngai nawh tag cawd heue.e”
17 Pa krai dee in “simiang” hoik mawh Khuan Singax Singeh.
Phan khuan Simiang Siyiex ot dee mawx,
dee an taug lih koe krax pa bheeik luan.
18 Ex pui ku kaux ang bax siao man tawb in ka pak ngai tix,
kieh mawh min plak ngai Simiang Ye-sux.
Simiang pa mawh Khuan Singax Singeh an,
min plak ngai pa ploi nawh hoik tieh loh ka ex,
kieh ex lih tix mawh greeh grang pak peung nawh,
kieh ex koe min plak ngai pa blawn pruh riang heue.
