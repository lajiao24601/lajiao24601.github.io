Grawng keh Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 1.18–25)
2
Yam an Vawng Law-ma pa kaux Ao-ku-see-tu tawx lox,
pui ka daux meung Law-ma tag caw tix tiiem lai ca rang tix khawm uik.
2 Yam bawg jauh tix tiiem ka lai ca rang ceu kaux kawn pui bawg tix in,
hoik mawh ka daux bawg yam pa giex Cui-li-nio cao giex kaing Shui-li-yax.
3 Pui hun hoik tang ing tiiem lai ca rang tix ka daux haktiex ot tix khawm uik.
4 Yaw-seux kawx kaoh khaing veng Na-sha-leux meung Cia-li-lix hu ka haktiex Yo-thaix,
mai hoek ka veng Ta-wue,
pa kok mawh Paw-li-haung;
kheu hoik mawh Yaw-seux ceu pui Ta-wue,
5 nawh tag sang tiiem lai ca rang tix tau mai Ma-li-yax bun pa hoik but nawh tix sang moeng.
Ma-li-yax hoik nghe,
6 yam ot kiex ka daux veng Paw-li-haung,
bawg yam sang keh nawh hoik hoek,
7 mai keh ka kawn simex tix kaux teei man kie kah,
deuh ot ka daux dawng sawm brung,
mawh kheu ang nyiex nyo lai koe kiex paung heue.
Pui liang yung mai kawn cau Siyiex
8 Ka sivie siveng Paw-li-haung plak praix,
koe pui liang yung lai loh tix it pau ka cub yung ka daux tiag daux du yam pon sawm.
9 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex jhi kix yaox tix;
min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex jhan riang ka piang kix,
kix tom kied lhat si yaung.
10 Viang mawh nan,
kawn cau Siyiex krai ka kix ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat heue!
Aux koe gum lox gaux rhawm pa sang krai ka pex;
gum lox in tag sang viex krax gaux rhawm pa ting tawx ka kawn pui piang haktiex.
11 Singaix in ka daux veng Ta-wue cao taoh pex pa mawh Khrit hoik keh heue!
12 Pex tag sang yaox kawn ngie tix kaux pa hoik kie ka man,
mai it ka daux dawng sawm brung;
an hoik mawh mhai pa tag sang tawx ka pex heue.”
13 Dum mawx dum an,
koe cub kru naing rao ma lih hoek tau mai kawn cau Siyiex,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
14 Kieh min plak ngai mawh cie Siyiex ka dee pa kied ting kied lhaong khaing paox tix heue!
Kieh pa nhim mawh cie pui pa hoik khaw rhawm rhi nawh ka piang haktiex heue!
15 Yam deeh lih hu haok kawn cau Siyiex khaing kix ka piang rao ma,
pui liang yung khankix ra lai krai ka paox tix ah nin:
“Laig ex ka daux veng Paw-li-haung,
mai jhak tix yaox ka kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka ex heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
kix nied tix hu laung laung,
mai hoik yaox ka Ma-li-yax,
Yaw-seux mai kawn ngie pa it ka daux dawng sawm brung.
17 Khaix hoik yaox pui liang yung kah,
kix krai pa hoik krai kawn cau Siyiex grawng kawn ngie an ka pui hun.
18 Yam mhong pui ka lox pa hoik krai pui liang yung khankix,
mai kied daox deng ka khawm uik.
19 Viang mawh nan,
Ma-li-yax priex kaing ceu in kaux ot ka daux rhawm tix,
mai hoik keud jee keud blaong tix yaox kah.
20 Pui liang yung deeh ing,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ka kaing ceu pa hoik mhong kix hoik yaox kix,
mawh kheu hoik khaw caw kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh mai lox krai kawn cau Siyiex ka kix heue.
Sum ceu kaux Ye-sux
21 aKhaix hoik luan daix singaix,
bawg yam pa Pat-hak kawn ngie hoik hoek,
kix tom sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Ye-sux;
ceu kaux in hoik mawh pa sum kawn cau Siyiex sivoe sang lih keh nawh heue.
Tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex
22 bHawt awm been pa hoik deuh Maw-si,
khaix hoik naog singaix bawg yam pa singax singeh,
Yaw-seux mai Ma-li-yax viex kawn nyawm an haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
son sang tan lu Simiang Ye-sux.
23 cIn hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm lox tat jhawm prawng pa tiiem Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kawn nyawm simex pa liang tix keh tag caw tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.”
24 Kix kawx tag sang hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
mai tan lu ka si kaux krie,
phan ang mawh nan,
kawn si kaux ra mu kieh mawh khawng tan heue.
25 Yam an,
ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung koe pui tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Si-miien.
Nawh mawh pui mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex tix kaux,
nawh hoik dawg simie pui Yi-seu-liex pon krax taoh.
Khuan Singax Singeh ot tau mai nawh;
26 nawh pon pa plak jhi Khuan Singax Singeh,
mai hoik tawng sivoe sang hu tix khaing haktiex tag sang yaox Khrit pa ap Simiang Siyiex tix tawx.
27 Mawh kheu hoik laig Khuan Singax Singeh ka daux nawh,
nawh tom hoek ka nyiex buan.
Yam in,
miex keeing Ye-sux klien kawn nyawm an laig daux nyiex,
son sang hawt tix yuh ka been lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tat tix deuh.
28 Si-miien klien kawn nyawm an ot ka naok tix,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
29 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik yuh lih ka gum lox pa hoik deuh tix;
yam in chix tix kieh siliex maix ing mhawm mhawm heue.
30 Aux hoik yaox krax taoh maix ka ngai tix,
31 pa mawh krax pa hoik rien khre maix son kawn pui piang haktiex yuh nin:
32 dnawh tag sang lih mawh pa riang pa plak jhi ka pui tang ceu,
lih mawh min plak ngai pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex heue.
33 Miex keeing kawn nyawm an hoik daox deng ka pa krai Si-miien.
34 Si-miien nawb buan ka kix mai krai ka miex kawn nyawm an Ma-li-yax ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik laug ka kawn nyawm in,
nawh tag sang kieh pui tom nie ka daux Yi-seu-liex laux leeing,
kieh pui tom nie pon krax taoh.
Nawh tag sang lih mawh kaw pa salao pui tom nie,
35 mai plih lih ka rhawm rhi krax keud pui in kix.
Krax laux rhawm tag sang lawm awm vaig klauh ka rhawm maix heue.”
36 Koe cao jhai bun tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh An-na,
mawh kawn bun Fa-loe-lix ceu pui Ya-seux.
Nawh hoik kied kuad,
mai hoik mawh pui pa koe mex,
hoik sax ot tau mai mex tix aliah num,
37 khaix an,
nawh lih ot nong tix,
yam in hoik koe tix daix pon num.e
Nawh ang sax lih hu khaing nyiex buan,
mai krub ka Siyiex leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
tuah ka priex preem,
rawg buan.
38 Ka daux yam an,
nawh kawx hoik hoek rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex,
khaix an mai krai pao grawng kawn nyawm in ka can pui pa krox jun tix yaox pa hoek Siyiex tix taoh ka ceu pui Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Deeh ing ka Na-sha-leux
39 fYaw-seux mai Ma-li-yax hawt awm lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex yuh ka kaing pa hoik tat tix deuh khawm uik,
mai deeh ing ka Cia-li-lix hoek ka haktiex ot tix Na-sha-leux.
40 Kawn nyawm an ting si nyek si nyek,
mhawm nex nham mai koe ping nyia;
buan son Siyiex ot tau mai nawh heue.
Kawn nyawm Ye-sux ot daux nyiex buan
41 gMiex keeing nawh haok yuh Poi Luat-luan ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ku num.
42 Yam koe Ye-sux kao ra num,
kix hawt tix yuh ka poi awm been pa tat tix deuh.
43 Khaix hoik poi,
kix yaog tix deeh ing ka nyiex,
viang mawh nan,
kawn nyawm Ye-sux kawn ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Miex keeing nawh ang tawng grawng kaing in,
44 mai ce nawh hu tau ka daux pui hun,
yam hoik hu been tix ngaix krax,
mai tag jauh tix sawg nawh ka daux pui nyiex ma tix.
45 Kix sawg mai ang yaox ka nawh,
kix tom deeh sawg nawh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
46 Khaix an loe singaix,
kix tom taug yaox nawh ka daux nyiex buan.
Nawh kawn ngawm tix ot ka sigreung sara pui Yo-thaix,
mai nghiet nang ka kix chawk nang ka kix;
47 can pui pa hoik yaox ka nawh kied daox deng ka krax pa lhak nawh mai lox pa pawk nawh.
48 Miex keeing nawh yaox ka nawh,
mai kied daox deng kah.
Miex nawh krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Kawn,
yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh nin ka yix lie?
Keeing maix mai aux kied khi rhawm tete,
mai hoik sawg ka maix ku si dauh!”
49 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx lai sawg ka aux?
Khex ang pex tawng grawng pa tag caw aux tix ot daux nyiex keeing aux lieh?”
50 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng ka dee beeh gum lox nawh in heue.
51 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux deeh ing tau mai kix ka Na-sha-leux,
mai nghiet ka miex keeing tix khawm uik.
Miex nawh priex grawng kaing in mhawm khawm uik ka daux rhawm rhi tix.
52 iTo kaux Ye-sux mai ping nyia nawh hoik haok huan tau,
Siyiex mai kawn pui kied muh ka nawh heue.
