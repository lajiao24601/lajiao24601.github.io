Siyiex laug ka simiang
2
aYuh ka mawx meung khankix keud tix sang yuh krax tieh vheen?
Yuh ka mawx kawn pui piang haktiex kum kawng paox tix yuh kaing pa ang mawh ang meh?
2 Simiang kix preeng tix hoek,
simiang ting kix kum kawng tau mai kix,
mai sang puing ka Simiang Siyiex mai ka simiang pa hoik laug nawh.
3 Kix ah nin:
ex hoek tix yah ka pa prawk kix!
Ex hoek tix pu pliak ka krax pa kuang kix heue!
 
4 Simiang Siyiex ploi lox nyiah tix khaing dang ngeung rao ma;
nawh nyiah ka krax pa daox aw pui in kix.
5 Nawh ne raw kix ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix,
kieh kix yaoh yee ka daux pa meet rhawm tix.
6 Nawh ah nin:
ka Shi-angb piang gawng pa singax aux,
aux hoik yaog ka simiang pa laug aux.
 
7 cSimiang an ah nin:
aux sang tawx gum lox Simiang Siyiex hu.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
maix mawh kawn simex aux;
in singaix aux mawh keeing maix.
8 Maix khiox khaw aux,
aux tag sang tawx meung ka piang haktiex ka maix;
piang haktiex khawm uik kawx tag sang mawh cie maix.
9 dMaix tag caw tix teei plawng taix rhiem kuang ka piang kix;
maix tag sang yuh kix laux leeing,
awm yuh laux ka aw tiex yuh nan.
10 Simiang khankix eue,
priex rhawm tix!
Simiang ting eue,
gau tix tawng!
11-12 Kieh tix koe rhawm pa lhat graung yung yi mai yuh kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex,
si yaung si kua,
mai kup tix krub ka sivoe ngai nawhe;
phan ang mawh nan,
krax pa song rhawm nawh tag sang phai awm pa rup ngu yuh nan,
pex tag sang yum dum mawx dum an.
Pui pa khiox khaw nawh kia ngien tix hoik pon buan son tete heue.
