Lox rawg buan daux krax pa yum rhawm
31
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sawg krax pa kia ngien maix;
khiox khaw maix paw kieh aux yaox krax som nai leeing ju.
Maix mawh Siyiex pa khaw caw;
khiox khaw maix taoh aux liak!
2 Khiox khaw maix nghiet lox pa rawg mhaing aux,
mai hoek tix taoh ka aux dum mawx dum an liak!
Khiox khaw maix mawh nyiex dee mox aux tix mai kia ngien ka aux,
mawh tab pa hoek tix taoh ka aux heue.
 
3 Maix mawh nyiex dee mox aux tix,
mawh tab ka aux;
khiox khaw maix hawt tix duk thung aux awm lox pa hoik deuh maix.
4 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux luat luan khaing rub pa sang yuh aux laux leeing,
kheu hoik mawh maix nyiex dee mox ka aux.
5 aAux tawx khuan ju tix ka daux tawm taix maix;
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa kaux sijax eue,
maix tag chix taoh ka aux heue.
 
6 Maix yum koeng ka pui pa krub ka siyiex pa pie lhien;
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik yum rhawm ka maix.
7 Kheu hoik koe maix krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix,
aux sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
Maix yaox ka pa hoik kha aux rhawm tix;
maix tawng ka pa hoik taug maox aux.
8 Maix paw vi kieh aux cawd ka daux tawm taix pui koeng;
maix tag caw tix deuh aux ot ka dee kaux dee cong heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix teei krax muh maix kia ka aux,
mawh kheu si pup aux krax kha rhawm kha rhi;
tawm ngai aux hoik uah kheu yiiem aux,
to kaux aux kawx hoik krawx hoik rhao.
10 Krax khi rhawm khi rhi hoik sawm ka ju aux uik;
krax laux rhawm laux rhi hoik dawt yuh ju aux.
Krax kha rhawm kha rhib hoik kieh aux iak riang iak rao;
si-ang aux hoik kroh khawm uik heue.
 
11 Can pui pa koeng hoik map nyiah ka aux,
mai paox plawg paox nyawm hoik nyiah ka aux;
pie khawm paox grawm ting aux kawx hoik lhat ka aux;
yam yaox kix aux ka daux laih daux cau,
mai kix to mox tix khaing aux ku kaux;
12 pui haoh pui hun hoik piawm ka aux,
mai kieh aux awm pui yum yuh nan;
aux awm pa seeing brai pa tik pui.
13 Aux mhong lox pa su shiep pui koeng tom nie,
mai vung viang hoik naog krax pa lhat pa graung.
Krax keud krax sawn kix sang kieh aux laux leeing,
mai sang teei ka ju aux heue.
 
14 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux yum rhawm ka maix;
maix mawh Siyiex aux.
15 Aux ot daux pa kia ngien maix leeing ju;
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui koeng,
mai pui pa tuk tiak tuk te ka aux.
16 Khiox khaw maix teei krax muh tix kia ngien ka siliex maix;
maix hoik koe krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux.
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang khiox khaw ka maix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux laux rhawm som nai.
Kieh pui pa caox pa caig laux rhawm som nai;
kieh kix kle ang koe lox krai tom laig ka daux meung yum.
18 Kieh maix thox daux jheeh pui pa krai lox pie lox lhien an kix;
kieh can pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
cau nyhau,
pa ah ka pui rauh pui see khawm uik,
ang lai koe lox krai heue.
 
19 Kraung khrai pa mhawm pa hoik deuh maix son pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix,
kied sidaing haoh tete!
Pui pa deuh rhawm tix ka maix pa hoik kia ngien maix ka plak sivoe ngai pui haktiex,
kied sidaing nyawm ot tete!
20 Maix hoik mox kix ot plak sivoe ngai tix dee kaux dee cong dee an,
son ang kix sang yaox ka krax pa yuh pui ceu pa ang mhawm ka kix;
maix hoik tawb kheem mox kix nyawm ot,
son ang kix sang yaox krax pa nu nai pui koeng heue.
 
21 Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Yam lai lawm pui ka aux,
nawh hoik jhi krax muh tix ka aux.
22 Ka daux rhawm aux kawn ang yum rhawm mai lhat graung,
mai ce maix hoik tik vud tix;
viang mawh nan,
yam khiox khaw aux krax teem coi maix;
maix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux heue.
 
23 Pui ceu Simiang Siyiex pa singax singeh eue,
tag caw tix muh ka Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka can pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh,
mai ciyang tom nyawm nyiem ka pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
24 Can pui pa een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex eue,
pex tag caw tix kaux cong,
tag caw tix koe rhawm kua heue.
