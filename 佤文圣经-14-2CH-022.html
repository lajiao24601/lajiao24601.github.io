Ya-ha-shiex simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 8.25–29; 9.21–28)
22
Pui naing pui A-la-pawx pauh ka can kawn simex simiang Yaw-lang yum uik,
mai deuk ngie Ya-ha-shiex kawn simex pa blawn iak khaing paox tix khaing.
Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui Yie-lu-sa-leung yaog Ya-ha-shiex plud simiang mawh keeing nawh.
2-3 Yam koe ju Ya-ha-shiex tix nga ra numa nawh mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix num.
Nawh hawt ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh cub nyiex simiang Ya-ha yuh nan.
Miex nawh kaux Ya-tha-li-ya mawh kawn bun simiang Ya-ha,
kawn saux bun Ang-lix simiang Yi-seu-liex;
nawh teem Ya-ha-shiex teei rhawm keud,
mai viex nawh yuh kaing pa caox pa caig.
4 Ya-ha-shiex hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex;
mawh kheu khaix hoik yum keeing nawh,
cub nyiex simiang Ya-ha lih mawh cao pa teem coi nawh,
mai kieh nawh laux leeing.
5 Nawh nghiet ka lox pa krai kix,
mai rom tix puing ka Ha-shiiex simiang Shui-li-yax tau mai Yaw-heu-lang simiang Yi-seu-liex.
Pui naing khawm ra plak puing paox tix ka La-mawx haktiex Ci-liex,
Yaw-heu-lang mat,
6 mai deeh ing pai mat tix ka Yie-see-liex;
Ya-ha-shiex hoek jhak nawh ka dee an heue.
7 Ya-ha-shiex si pup krax laux kheu hu jhak tix ka Yaw-heu-lang;
mai in hoik mawh alo Siyiex.
Yam ot Ya-ha-shiex ka Yie-see-lie,
nawh mai Yaw-heu-lang si pup Yie-hu kawn simex Ling-see;
Yie-hu hoik mawh pa laug Simiang Siyiex tix yuh meung Ya-ha laux leeing.
8 Yam kawn yuh nawh ka krax pa ciyang Siyiex,
mai nawh si pup ka simiang Yo-tax khankix mai kawn pha simex Ya-ha-shiex khankix hu tau mai Ya-ha-shiex hoek tix kum kawng,
Yie-hu pauh kix yum khawm uik.
9 Ya-ha-shiex mox ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
pui cie Yie-hu yaox ka kix,
mai tawx nawh ka Yie-hu pauh yum.
Kix si paung ka nawh,
mawh kheu kix ah nin:
“Nawh hoik mawh nang kawn saux simiang Yaw-sa-fa an pa hoik sax sawg ka Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix heue.”
Cub nyiex Ya-ha-shiex ang bax koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon plud ka simiang heue.
Ya-tha-li-ya miex simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 11.1–3)
10 Ya-tha-li-ya miex simiang Ya-ha-shiex hoik mhong pauh pui kawn simex tix,
nawh tom tawx lox kieh pui pauh ka cub nyiex simiang Yo-tax khawm uik.
11 Ya-ha-shiex koe pux bun pa rom keeing tang miex tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-see-pa;
nawh mex ka cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax.
Yaw-see-pa briex mox tix taoh ka Yaw-a-see kawn simex Ya-ha-shiex tix kaux lih,
viex nawh lih khaing daux cub kawn simiang pa sang pauh ka khankix,
mai mox kiex miex eei nawh ot daux lawg nyiex it ka daux nyiex buan.
Yaw-see-pa mox ka Yaw-a-see,
taoh nawh luat luan khaing tawm taix Ya-tha-li-ya,
mai ang kieh nawh yaox tix yum.
12 Ka daux yam teei Ya-tha-li-ya grawng pa hoik mawh tix miex simiang giex ka kaing,
Yaw-a-see mox tix ka daux nyiex buan liah num heue.
