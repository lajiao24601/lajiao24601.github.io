Pui pon teei ka Pa-pi-loeng
50
aPhan mawh grawng veng Pa-pi-loeng,
mai pui pa ot ka daux,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
2 tag caw tix krai gum lox in ka meung ka piang haktiex,
mai ploi lox lai in hu heue!
Tag caw tix ploi lox lai in hu,
mai krai lao hu ah nin:
Pa-pi-loeng hoik laux leeing heue!
Siyiex tang kaux Ma-tu-kheux hoik laux leeing heue!
Riang rao pa krub pui ka Pa-pi-loeng hoik pon krax kaig krax tua;
Riang rao siyiex tang kaux pa yum koeng pa krub pui hoik max heue!
3 “Koe meung tix mu hoek tix puing Pa-pi-loeng khaing haktiex plak laong,
mai kieh meung haktiex nawh riem,
ang koe pui pa paung ot kah;
pui mai to tiag hoik to khawm uik heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yam hoek ka daux singaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax tag sang hoek tau,
mai yiiem yam hu tix sawg ka aux pa mawh Siyiex kix.
5 Kix tag sang hu sawg ka krax hu plak Shi-ang,
mai veeh tix hu ka plak an.
Kix tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai aux leeing ju,
mai ang tik vud leeing ju leeing phan heue.
6 “Pui cie aux hoik awm yung pa lud krax.
Cao liang kix kieh kix grai ka sigreung gawng,
jauh ka gawng in vu vie tix hoek ka gawng an,
mai ang yaox ka kawg yung tix.
7 Can pa si pup ka kix tag sang bleet ka kix.
Pui koeng ka kix ah nin:
‘Ex yuh nin ang mawh pa lud,
mawh kheu hoik phit lud kix ka Simiang Siyiex.
Tax du kix yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai kix kawx tag khaw tix yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex heue.’
8 b“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
to lih khaing Pa-pi-loeng heue!
Pex tag caw tix ran to lih khaing haktiex an sivoe!
9 Aux tag sang khrawm pui pa ting riang ka haktiex plak laong,
kieh kix rom paox tix puing ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Kix tag sang dia tix puing hu,
mai pon pex ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Toh te kix hoik awm te pa puing pui pa kied chix puing ka te yuh nan,
mai krox puing ka ku taix.
10 Pui tag sang hoek con ka Pa-pi-loeng;
pui pa con ka nawh tag sang giex awm pa simie kix tix giex khawm uik yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Pa-pi-loeng laux leeing
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Pa-pi-loeng eue,
pex hoik tud rhim ka meung haktiex aux,
viang mawh nan,
pex kawn yuh krax gaux rhawm son in,
awm kawn moi pa dig nghox ka daux bang liang khawng brie mai brung pa riag yuh nan;
12 viang mawh nan,
veng ting cie to kaux maix tag sang pon krax kaig krax tua.
Pa-pi-loeng tag sang lih mawh meung pa blawn iak khaing paox tix,
lih mawh daux tiag pa kroh dee ang rawm koe.
13 Mawh kheu krax pa song rhawm aux,
mai ang koe pui pa paung ot ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Nawh tag sang nhik sang riem,
mai pui pa lawng krax ku kaux hoik yaoh yee kah,
ghak baig ka heue.
14 “Pui pa kied chix puing ak eue,
tag caw dia tix puing hu,
mai lai lawm ka Pa-pi-loeng heue!
Tag caw tix vaik ka ak puh,
sau toh te jhi ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mawh kheu hoik phit lud nawh ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex.
15 Tag caw tix kok yo ka lox si num ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm pon daog!
Pa-pi-loeng hoik kreet tix ot greem ex,
guh veng nawh hoik laux.
Aux hoik pawk bix ka Pa-pi-loeng,
pex kawx tag sang pawk bix ka kix;
yuh kix ka pui tang kaux yuh ka mawx,
pex kawx tag sang hawt tix pawk bix ka kix yuh nan nang.
16 Paw vi kieh kix sau simie,
mai paw vi kieh kix yaox tix vawg.
Pui tang ceu pa ot daux veng khawm uik,
son sang to mox kix tix khaing pui naing pa hoek tix puing ka kix,
mai kix hoik deeh to ing ka meung haktiex tix heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex deeh ing ka haktiex tix
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex awm phung yung pa hoik leue sivai sang si to hu rai rhuat tix yuh nan.
Simiang Ya-sux ran tix bleet ka kix sivoe;
simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha rieb tix kuan khliak ka si-ang kix.
18 Son kaing in,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang ciyang ka simiang Ya-sux;
aux kawx tag sang hawt tix ciyang ka simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
mai meung haktiex nawh.
19 Aux tag sang thung pui Yi-seu-liex deeh ing ka haktiex kix.
Kix tag sang ih ka to ma to koi pa pon khaing gawng Cia-mix,
mai ka gawng Pa-sang,
pon ka nghox koi pa koe ka haktiex Yi-fa-liien mai ka haktiex Ci-liex.
20 Hoek daux singaix yam an,
ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai yaox ka pui lud,
ka daux pui Yo-tax kawx ang lai yaox ka ceu pa caox pa caig,
mawh kheu aux tag sang ploi ka pui pa kawn deuk,
pa hoik cu aux tix pluat ploi ka khankix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Siyiex prawng ca Pa-pi-loeng
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix hu puing pui Mi-la-ta-weng mai pui haktiex Pi-keu,
mai pauh kix yum.
Pex tag caw hawt tix yuh ka gum lox tawx aux.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin.
22 Ka daux haktiex Pa-pi-loeng mhong ka lox puing pui naing,
pa mawh krax laux leeing pa ting.
23 Pa-pi-loeng hoik sax awm daix ting,
pa pon kuang ka haktiex khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
yam in daix in hoik toing max.
Meung ka piang haktiex hoik si yaung kheu yaox ka pa riem tix.
24 Pa-pi-loeng eue,
maix hoik tieb tie ka aux;
maix hoik goh tix laig ka daux rub pa taong aux son maix,
maix kawn ang bax tawng,
25 Aux hoik paoh ka nyiex krao kraung naing,
hoik gah lih ka kraung naing ka daux krax pa song rhawm.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
simiang kru naing tag sang tieb tie ka Pa-pi-loeng.
26 Tag sang hu puing pa pon praok si daix daog!
Paoh ka kraox nghox nawh,
mai pum sin ngao pa pon khaing naing tix daux,
awm pum ka nghox koi yuh nan!
Tag sang yuh meung haktiex in laux leeing khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh tid tix koe heue!
27 Pauh ka pui naing kix yum heue!
Yuh laux leeing khawm uik heue!
Hoik laux leeing heue,
pui Pa-pi-loeng heue!
Singaix bawg yam kham kix krax ciyang hoik hoek heue!”
(
28 Pui si pup krax laux pa to lih khaing Pa-pi-loeng hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix,
kix krai lao ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex,
kheu pa gleeing pui Pa-pi-loeng ka nyiex buan,
mai nawh tom yuh krax pa pawk bix heue.)
29 c“Tag caw tix kok pui pa kied chix puing ak puing ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Can pui pa chix puing ka ak hoek khawm uik,
mai lai lawm ka veng in,
ang kieh pui tix kaux kawx yaox tix luat luan.
Tag sang hawt tix pawk bix awm pa khaw nawh tix pon;
hoik yuh nawh ka mawx ka pui tang kaux,
tag sang yuh nan ka nawh nang.
Mawh kheu cau nyhau nawh tix tieb tie ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh cao pa singax singeh pui Yi-seu-liex.
30 Pui tag sang pauh pui nhum ju kix yum ka daux laih ting laih iak;
can pui naing tag sang yuh laux leeing tau ka daux tix ngaix khaing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
31 “Pa-pi-loeng eue,
maix kied cau nyhau heue!
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
simiang kru naing tieb tie ka maix heue!
Singaix bawg yam pa ciyang aux ka maix hoik hoek.
32 Maix pa mawh meung pa cau nyhau in tag sang goh,
ang koe pui pa yaog maix kaoh.
Aux tag sang ploi ngu gleeing ka veng maix,
ceu pa koe sivie siveng tag sang gleeing khawm uik heue.”
33 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax hoik kham krax gut kheem.
Pui pa giex ka kix hoik jhawp ka kix tom sied,
ang cu tix ploi kix hu.
34 Viang mawh nan,
cao pa taoh ka kix ting riang ting rao;
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh simiang kru naing.
Nawh tag sang mawh cao pa hoek tix prawng rauh see son kix,
mai kieh haktiex pon krax pa yien pa yawg;
viang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang kieh pui Pa-pi-loeng yaoh yee ang nyawm ot heue.
35 Pa-pi-loeng hoik laux leeing heue!
Krax yum hoik lih hoek ka piang pui cie nawh,
simiang nawh,
mai pui pa koe ping nyia cie nawh.
36 Krax yum hoik lih hoek ka piang cao jhai pa pie lhien cie nawh;
kix kied daox aw tete te lai heue!
Krax yum lih hoek ka piang pui naing cie nawh;
kix kied yaoh yee sigrien sigre;
37 Yuh laux leeing ka cub pui pa brug brung,
mai ling law puing naing nawh heue!
Pauh yum ka pui naing pa vai nawh tix siao;
kix kied iak riang iak rao heue!
Yuh laux leeing ka sin ngao cie nawh;
tud rhim awm pa simie tix yuh heue!
38 Ploi sibhawm praix kroh lih hoek ka daux haktiex cie nawh;
kieh rawm klawng cie nawh kroh heue!
Pa-pi-loeng mawh meung haoh riang rao krub pui ka daux;
riang rao krub pui hoik kieh kawn pui daox aw heue.
39 e“Mawh kheu nan,
siyiex phi,
khuan pa seeing brai,d
mai sim ka-la-uk tag sang lih hu khaing Pa-pi-loeng;
ang koe pui pa paung ot kah,
leeing ju leeing phan ang koe pui pa paung ot dee an.
40 fSivoe an aux pa mawh Siyiex,
hoik yuh laux leeing yuh ka mawx ka Saw-to-ma,
mai ka Eu-maw-la,
mai ka veng pa koe ka sivie siveng,
Pa-pi-loeng kawx aux tag sang hawt tix yuh laux leeing yuh nan nang,
mai ang lai baox koe pui pa paung ot dee an.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
41 Kuan jhak tix yaox heue,
koe ceu pui tix ceu pa kaoh hoek khaing haktiex plak laong,
mai mawh meung pa ting riang pa koe dee singai tix meung;
simiang tom nie hoik rien khre tix puing ka naing.
42 Kix teei ak puh vaig gawn rien khre ka to kaux tix,
mai baox caox jhawm mai baox cau nyhau.
Kix brug brung to,
awm puk pak rawm pa si num;
Kix rien tix sang puing ka Pa-pi-loeng.
43 Yam mhong simiang Pa-pi-loeng ka lox in,
taix caong nawh si kua;
nawh kied kha rhawm tix,
awm bun yha yam saux sang keh yuh nan heue.
44 “Kuan jhak tix yaox heue,
jiet awm sivai sang si pa lih hu khaing daux nong khaox pa koe ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang yuh nan,
pa hoek ka pak rib klai,
awm an,
mai aux tag sang kieh pui Yi-tong lih hu khaing haktiex kix dum mawx dum an.
Khaix an aux laug ka simiang lih,
mai nawh tag sang giex kaing ka piang meung haktiex in.
Pui mawx sang pon tiag tix tau mai aux lie?
Pui mawx sang pon tiex ka aux lie?
Simiang mawx sang pon tieh vheen ka aux lie?
45 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka kaing pa sang yuh aux yuh nin:
aux tag sang tieb tie ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai ka pui pa ot daux veng,
pie kawn nyawm kix kawx pui tag sang reu hu;
pui ku kaux tag sang lhat graung.
46 Yam laux leeing Pa-pi-loeng,
koe lox pa si num hoik kieh haktiex yung yang;
lox yiiem pa si yaung tag sang hu ka meung haktiex tang dauh heue.”
