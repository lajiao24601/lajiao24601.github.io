Bun miex khankix pa hawt ka Ye-sux
8
Khaix an ang koe miex glang tix,
Ye-sux hoik tauh hu ka veng ku veng ka yaong ku yaong,
sidiex ka gum lox buan siawp sibrawm Siyiex;
kawn cau kao ra kaux hu tau mai nawh.
2 aKhaix an kawn koe bun miex tix blah,
kix hoik mawh pa laig khuan phi caox daux mai pa kha rhawm tix jhawm saux,
hoik pon tix moi khawm uik;
ka daux kix koe Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la,
phi pa grheu lih khaing daux to kaux nawh mawh aliah mu.
3 Kawn koe Yaw-ya-na moeng Khu-sha simiang pa ot greem Si-luix,
mai Su-sha-na khawm bun tang kaux tix blah.
Kix hoik teei sin ngao pa koe tix tawx ka Ye-sux mai ka pui gau nawh heue.
Phek tiiem ka simie
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.1–9; Lai Ma-kheu 4.1–9)
4 Pui hun kaoh khaing veng ku veng hoek ka Ye-sux.
Yam khrawm cub pui hun paox tix,
Ye-sux krai gum lox phek tiiem plak greem ka kix ah nin:
5 “Koe pui pa rhuat simie tix kaux hu rhuat ka simie.
Yam rhuat nawh ka simie,
koe simie tix blah cawd ka praok krax,
pui dug dig kah,
sim khankix pu hoek kaoh tix ih.
6 Tix blah cawd ka tiag simaox,
khaix hoik kuah simie tag kroh,
mawh kheu ang haktiex koe rawm.
7 Koe simie tix blah cawd ka daux pang kat,
pang kat ting huan tau mai simie an,
kheem su shiet ka simie an.
8 Koe simie tix blah cawd ka dee mhawm tiex,
kuah ting lhaong,
mai plix plix tix yieh son heue.”
Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Pui pa koe yhaok,
tag caw tix nghiet ka khawm uik heue!”
Kaing ting grawng pa siao ka gum lox phek tiiem
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.10–17; Lai Ma-kheu 4.10–12)
9 Pui gau Ye-sux hoek tix chawk nawh ka grawng dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem in.
10 bNawh ah nin:
“Grawng pa raux pa mox meung Siyiex hoik tawx ka pex;
phan mawh ka son pui tang kaux,
tag sang teei gum lox phek tiiem,
mai kieh kix jhak mai ang yaox,
mhong mai ang tawng heue.”
Kah dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.18–23; Lai Ma-kheu 4.13–20)
11 “Dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem in mawh nin:
simie mawh gum lox buan Siyiex.
12 Simie pa cawd ka praok krax mawh pui pa hoik mhong ka lox buan,
Phi-caox hoek tix tuk tiak,
mai teei ka gum lox buan in hu khaing daux rhawm nawh,
ang kieh nawh pon krax taoh kheu krax yum rhawm tix.
13 Simie pa cawd ka piang tiag simaox mawh pui pa rieb ka gum lox buan,
mai gaux rhawm tix rieb dum mawx dum an,
viang mawh nan,
gum lox buan ang riah kaux cong ka daux rhawm rhi nawh;
kix yum rhawm tix vud khaing,
viang mawh nan,
yam si pup krax cheuk cheu tag ang kaux cong.
14 Simie pa cawd daux pang kat mawh pui pa mhong gum lox buan,
viang mawh nan,
krax khi rhawm khi rhi ka daux krax ot krax koe,
krax nyaw krax glaw sin ngao haktiex mai krax gaux rhawm kieh gum lox buan in yeet,
ang pon plix plix pa teem.
15 Simie pa cawd ka dee mhawm tiex,
mawh pui pa mhong ka gum lox buan,
pa hoik viex rhawm pa mhawm tix mai pa rauh see tix hawt,
mai sau riang tix krox jun tom hoek plix plix heue.”
Kawk ngu ka greem phliex
(Lai Ma-thaix 4.21–25)
16 c“Ang koe pui pa tok kawk ngu,
mai teei phliex tawb kah,
phan ang mawh nan,
deuh greem ku.
Nawh tee rien tix deuh ka piang jawk kawk ngu,
mai kieh pui pa laig daux nyiex yaox pa jhan riang.
17 dKaing ceu pa glip khawm uik tag sang krai lih,
kaing ceu pa tawb kheem ku ceu kawx tag sang plih lih,
mai kieh pui tawng khawm uik heue.
18 e“Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw priex rhawm tix nghiet;
mawh kheu pui pa hoik koe an,
tag sang nie baox tawx kah,
mai kie nawh kub tom nie;
pui pa ang koe an,
pie pa hoik koe nawh ceeix ceeix kawx tag sang teei hu heue.”
Miex Ye-sux mai pux aik nawh
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.46–50; Lai Ma-kheu 3.31–35)
19 Miex Ye-sux mai pux aik nawh hoek tix jhak ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu hun pui,
kix ang pon yaox tix hoek dex ka nawh.
20 Koe pui krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Miex maix mai pux aik maix cung plak praix sang sawg ka maix heue!”
21 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pui an kix pa hoik mhong ka gum lox Siyiex mai hawt tix yuh,
tag mawh miex aux mai pux aik aux heue!”
Ye-sux kieh bhaung ting yiien
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.23–27; Lai Ma-kheu 4.35–41)
22 Koe tix ngaix,
Ye-sux laig daux reu tau mai pui gau tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Hu ex ka rawm nhawng plak an heue.”
Kix tom yaog tix hu.
23 Yam hu reu,
Ye-sux hoik laig it.
Dum mawx dum an,
bhaung ting phlu klauh ka piang rawm,
reu hoik naog ka rawm,
mai kix hoik kied koe krax khi rhawm.
24 Pui gau so ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
sara,
ex hoik dex sang laux ju heue!”
Ye-sux haing,
mai ne raw ka bhaung ting an,
bhaung ting an tom yiien,
piang rawm nhawng yien yawg tix.
25 Ye-sux chawk kix ah nin:
“Krax yum rhawm pex koe dee mawx lie?”
Kix daox deng nang yaoh yee nang,
mai ra lai ah nin:
“Pui in mawh mawx lie?
Nawh tawx lox ka bhaung mai puk pak rawm,
bhaung mai puk pak rawm kawx hoik nghiet ka lox nawh heue!”
Ye-sux pai ka pui pa laig phi daux moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.28–32; Lai Ma-kheu 5.1–20)
26 Kix tiang ka haktiex Keu-la-seung toi rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix.
27 Yam hoik haok Ye-sux ka praok rawm,
dee an koe pui pa laig phi ka daux tix kaux hu rieb ka Ye-sux.
Pui in hoik ang ceep ka kraung khrai tom rheeng,
ang paung ot ka nyiex,
viang mawh nan,
paung ot ka daux si meeig.
28 Yam yaox nawh ka Ye-sux,
nawh riag yo,
mai gup tix ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Ye-sux kawn simex Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
yuh ka mawx maix lai sang tuk tiak ka aux lie?
Aux khiox khaw maix paw vi kha ka aux liak!”
29 Nawh krai yuh nin mawh kheu hoik tawx Ye-sux lox nhie khuan pa seeing brai an lih khaing to kaux pui an.
Hoik mawx miex bawg mawx khuan pa seeing brai hoik giex ka nawh,
pie yam hoik giex gang pui ka nawh,
phrawk taix caong nawh ka maox ceng,
mai nawh kawx hoik pon rud dut,
phi hoik viex nawh hu daux tiag daux tu heue.
30 Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix kaux ceu pa tix?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Aux kaux ‘tix cub’ ”,
mawh kheu koe phi tom nie pa hoik laig daux nawh.
31 Phi an khiox khaw ka Ye-sux,
ang cu nawh grheu kix laig daux pa kied raux heue.
32 Ka baoh dex blaong gawng dee an koe phung lig ih ceu pa ih.
Phi caox tom khiox khaw Ye-sux,
cu kix laig ka daux phung lig;
Ye-sux cu ka lox kix.
33 Phi caox tom lih khaing to kaux pui an,
mai laig ka daux phung lig;
cub phung lig tom preeng tix lih ka yoeh rang,
laig daux nhawng,
yum khawm uik heue.
34 Pui pa liang lig yaox grawng kaing in tom yaog tix to,
mai hoek tix krai lao grawng kaing in ka veng ka yaong.
35 Pui pa ot haktiex an lih,
mai sang jhak tix yaox ka grawng kaing ceu pa lih tix mawh.
Kix hoek ka Ye-sux dee an,
mai hoik yaox pui an pa lih phi khaing ngawm ka plak sivoe caong Ye-sux,
ceep ka kraung khrai,
dieb dong nawh hoik haing,
kix tom lhat kah.
36 Pui pa liag lig khankix yaox ka grawng kaing in pa hoik pon pai moi ka pui an krai ka pui hun.
37 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa ot haktiex Keu-la-seung tom khiox khaw Ye-sux lih hu khaing dee an,
mawh kheu kied lhat graung kix.
Ye-sux tom laig daux reu lih hu.
38 Pui pa hoik lih phi khaing an khiox khaw ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kieh aux hu tau mai maix heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux tawx lox nhie nawh deeh ing ah nin:
39 “Maix deeh ing ka nyiex,
mai krai lao ka kaing pa hoik yuh Siyiex ka maix heue.”
Nawh tom ing,
mai krai hu ka kaing pa hoik yuh Ye-sux ka nawh,
tauh ka daux veng khawm uik heue.
Kawn bun Ye-lu mai bun saux lih nham
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.18–26; Lai Ma-kheu 5.21–43)
40 Ye-sux hoek ka toi rawm nhawng,
pui hun gaux rhawm tix rieb ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik krox jun pui hun nawh hoek.
41 Koe cao kuang nyiex sidiex tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ye-lu,
nawh kawx hoek nang.
Nawh gup tix ka plak sivoe caong Ye-sux,
mai khiox khaw Ye-sux ing ka nyiex nawh,
42 mawh kheu hoik dex sang yum kawn bun nawh pa koe kao ra num tix kaux khaing heue.
Yam hu Ye-sux,
pui hun hoik su shiet ka nawh khawm plak ngai plak khaix.
43 Koe bun tix kaux pa saux lih nham kao ra num;
nawh hoik uik mau ka pai nawh tix ka daux tawm taix cao sidahf,
viang mawh nan,
kawn ang koe pui pa pon pai nawh moi.
44 Nawh hoek ka plak khaix Ye-sux,
pu pi ka jaong sibex dha Ye-sux,
nham nawh tom hit dum mawx dum an.
45 Ye-sux chawk ah nin:
“Pui mawx pa pu pi ka aux lie?”
Pui hun thiang ka khawm uik.
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Sara,
ka plak sivoe maix,
ka plak khaix maix,
mai ka plak goix plak dawm maix hoik mawh pui khawm uik,
pui hoik su shiet ka maix heue!”
46 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Koe pui hoik pu pi ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik tawng aux lih riang rao khaing to kaux tix heue!”
47 Bun an hoik tawng mawh tix,
tom si yaung si kua grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux,
mai krai mawh tix ka Ye-sux plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
yuh ka mawx nawh lai pu pi ka Ye-sux,
yuh ka mawx lai pon moi dum mawx dum an nang.
48 Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Kawn eue,
krax yum rhawm maix hoik taoh ka maix heue!
Ing mhawm mhawm heue.”
49 Yam kawn si kah Ye-sux,
koe pui kaoh khaing daux nyiex cao kuang nyiex sidiex,
mai krai ka Ye-lu ah nin:
“Kawn bun maix hoik yum,
paw siao lai nyhap sara heue.”
50 Ye-sux mhong lox in nawh tom krai ka Ye-lu ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat,
yum rhawm khaing heue!
Nawh tag sang chix moi heue.”
51 Yam hoek Ye-sux ka daux nyiex Ye-lu,
lheu khaing Pi-teux,
Yaw-hang,
Ya-keux,
mai miex kawn bun an,
ang kieh pui tang kaux laig tau mai tix.
52 Pui pa ot daux nyiex hoik yiiem yum kawn bun in khawm uik.
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Paw vi yiiem heue!
Kawn bun in ang bax yum,
mawh laig it ngie khaing heue.”
53 Pui hun hoik nyiah ka nawh khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik tawng kix grawng hoik yum kawn bun in.
54 Ye-sux bluh taix nawh kok yo ah nin:
“Kawn,
kaoh heue!”
55 Khuan nawh tom deeh hoek,
mai kaoh dum mawx dum an.
Ye-sux tawx lox nhie kix tawx nawh ih ka ceu pa ih.
56 Miex keeing kawn bun in kied daox deng kah,
Ye-sux tawx lox ka kix,
mai ang cu kix krai kaing pa lih tix mawh in ka pui tix kaux kawx heue.
