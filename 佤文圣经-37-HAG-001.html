Lai Ha-kai
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox cox sum ka nyiex buan
1
aYam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng Paw-see ka daux num ra khix liah tix ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka cao jhai Ha-kai nhie nawh si kah ka Saw-law-pa-paw kawn Simex Sa-la-thiie simiang haktiex Yo-tax,
mai Yaw-su-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting kawn simex Yaw-sha-ta heue.
2 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai ka Ha-kai ah nin:
“Kawn pui in kix krai yam in ang mawh bawg yam pa sum ka nyiex buan.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka cao jhai Ha-kai nhie nawh deeh krai gum lox in plak greem ka kawn pui.
4 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui cie aux eue,
nyiex buan aux hoik riem,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik sum tix ot ka nyiex pa kied mhawm heue!
5 Yam in aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing,
tag sang kieh pex deeh cox chawk cheng ka to kaux tix.
6 Pex hoik rhuat hoik van tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
pon ngie tix khrawm ceeix ceeix khaing;
pex koe priex preem,
viang mawh nan,
ca ang chix sak;
pex koe plai nyaux,
viang mawh nan,
ca ang chix sak nyaux;
pex koe kraung ceep,
viang mawh nan,
ca ang chix si u.
Ngawh bix pui pa yuh kaing ang kub eei kix.
7 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing tag sang kieh pex deeh cox chawk cheng ka to kaux tix.
8 Yam in pex tag sang haok kid khaox ox piang gawng,
deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan;
yuh nin,
aux tom tag khaw rhawm ka pex,
khaix an mai pon min pa khaw tix pon heue.
9 “Pex kied simie tix pon priex preem tom nie,
dee si dauh pon ngie ceeix ceeix khaing;
pex viex pa pon tix hoek ka nyiex,
viang mawh nan,
aux phlu tix tik kahb.
Yuh ka mawx aux yuh nin lie?
Mawh kheu hoik riem nyiex buan aux,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik ki son sang sum ka nyiex cie to kaux tix.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
rao ma ang tawx lhiex lih,
haktiex ang lai kuah ka to ma.
11 Aux kieh haktiex pa mawh gawng iak,
kaung ma,
si peem plix siiet,
si peem sime,
haktiex pa kuah ka to ma,
kawn pui,
khawng brie,
mai can ceu pa sum pui uik si pup ka sibhawm praix kroh praix klawng heue.”
Kawn pui nghiet ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Saw-law-pa-paw,
Yaw-su-yax,
mai can kawn pui pa kaoh khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix ka Pa-pi-loengc,
hawt tix yuh ka gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix.
Mawh kheu lhat kix,
kix tom nghiet ka gum lox pa krai siliex Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao jhai Ha-kai.
13 Ha-kai krai pao gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka kawn pui ah nin:
“Aux cu tix sang ot tau mai pex.”
14 Simiang Siyiex tawx gum lox tix laig ka daux rhawm Saw-law-pa-paw simiang haktiex Yo-tax,
mai ka Yaw-su-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting,
khawm can pui pa kaoh khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix khankixd;
kix tom deeh cox sum nyiex buan son Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing pa mawh Siyiex kix.
15 Kaing in lih tix mawh ka daux yam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng ka daux num ra khix liah tix nga pon ngaix heue.
