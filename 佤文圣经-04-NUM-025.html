Pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka haktiex Phi-eu
25
Yam sum pui Yi-seu-liex bang ot ka daux ngawd See-thing,
koe pui pa phit vao tau mai bun ceu pui Maw-ya dee an.
2-3 Bun an kix ghaok kix hoek tix yuh krax tan ka siyiex pa krub pui Maw-ya.
Pui Yi-seu-liex ih ka pa tan lu kix,
mai krub ka siyiex kix Pa-lix haktiex Phi-eu.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom song rhawm ka kix,
4 mai ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Viex maix hawt awm gum lox pa krai aux,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tag yum ot greem pa riang singaix khankix,
mai aux tag ang lai song rhawm ka kawn pui.”
5 Maw-si ah nin ka cao prawng see:
“Pex ku kaux tag caw tix pauh ka ceu pui pex khankix pa krub ka Pa-lix heue.”
6 Koe pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux,
viex bun pui Mi-tiien tix kaux laig ka daux nyiex gox bang cie tix ka sivoe ngai Maw-si mai ka sivoe ngai cub kawn pui;
yam an,
caw tix mawh bawg yam yiiem grai pui hun ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man.
7 Fe-ni-ha kawn simex Yi-li-ya-sa pa mawh ceu pui kawn praix Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li yaox kah,
nawh tom yaog tix kaoh,
mai lih hu khaing si nax cub pui hun.
Nawh giex pliah ka daux tawm taix tix tix gawng;
8 mai hawt pui an mai bun an laig ka daux nyiex gox bang,
mai suat kiex khawm ra kaux phlat ka pliah.
Yuh nin,
jhawm saux preng pa hoek piang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag naox tix;
9 viang mawh nan,
pui pa hoik yum ka jhawm saux preng an,
koe ra mheen pon rheng kaux heue.
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
11 “Mawh kheu pa yuh Fe-ni-ha,
aux ang lai song rhawm ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
Nawh kieh ngie pui krub ka aux khaing,
ang kieh krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
kheu nan aux tom ang baox yuh kix laux leeing ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
12 Maix krai ka nawh,
aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai maix leeing ju.
13 Nawh mai doeh ceu cad nawh,
tag sang yuh cao giex kaing li leeing ju;
mawh kheu nawh ang cu pui tix kaux kawx hawt ka siyiex pa ang khaw tau mai nawh,
son sang pluat ploi ka phi map kawn pui heue.”
14 Pui pa hoik pauh tix tau mai bun pui Mi-tiien an mawh pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Sing-lix,
mawh kawn simex Sa-lux,
mawh cao kaing tix nyiex ka daux cao kaing ceu pui Si-miien khankix.
15 Bun Mi-tiien an kaux Keu-see-pi;
keeing nawh Su-eux mawh simiang ka daux ceu pui Mi-tiien tix cub heue.
16 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si:
17 “Maix puing ka ceu pui Mi-tiien khankix,
mai pauh kix laux leeing;
18 mawh kheu kix hoik yuh krax keud pa pie lhien sang kieh pex laux ka daux haktiex Phi-eu,
mai baox mawh son grawng bun Keu-see-pi an,
pa mawh bun pa hoik pauh yum ka haktiex Phi-eu ka daux yam lih jhawm saux preng pa kin heue.”
