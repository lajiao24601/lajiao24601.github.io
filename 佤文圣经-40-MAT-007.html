Paw vi prawng ca ka pui tang kaux
(Lai Lu-cia 6.37–38, 41–42)
7
“Paw vi prawng ca ka pui,
Siyiex tom ang prawng ca ka pex.
2 aKheu nan,
phan pex prawng ca ka pui yuh ka mawx,
Siyiex kawx tag sang prawng ca ka pex yuh nan nang.
Pex siao ka kraung tong ceu pa tix,
Siyiex kawx tag sang siao ka kraung tong ceu an ka pex nang.
3 Yuh ka mawx maix lai jhak ka bliah khaox pa koe ka daux tawm ngai pux aik tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang simi ka nuk pa koe ka daux tawm ngai tix lie?
4 Ka daux ngai maix koe nuk,
sang pon ah nin ka pux aik maix:
‘Kieh aux teei ka bliah khaox pa koe ka daux ngai maix’ lie?
5 Maix pa mawh pui pa pie lhien in,
ran teei ka nuk pa koe daux tawm ngai maix sivoe ha,
khaix an tom taug chix yaox sidawng grawng sang teei lih ka bliah khaox pa koe daux tawm ngai pux aik tix heue.
6 “Paw vi tik tix tawx ceu pa singax singeh ka sox,
mai kix chix kling tix deeh kiied ka pex;
paw vi tik tix tawx siang ploi pa ting ngawh ka lig,
mai kix chix dug dig siang ploi an ot ka greem caong tix heue.”
Khiox khaw, sawg jiang, gleuh siviex
(Lai Lu-cia 11.9–13)
7 “Pex khiox khaw,
tom taug pon;
sawg tom taug yaox;
gleeh siviex tom taug paoh son pex.
8 Mawh kheu can pui pa khiox khaw,
tom taug pon;
pui pa sawg,
tom taug yaox;
pa gleuh siviex,
siviex tom taug paoh.
9 Ka daux pex koe pui mawx,
kawn simex nawh mhaing khao mun mai tawx simaox ka nawh lie?
10 Sang lo kax mai tawx si uing ka lie?
11 Viang pex hoik caox caig kawx,
mai hoik tawng tix tawx ceu pa mhawm ka kawn simex kawn bun tix;
keeing pex Siyiex piang rao ma khex ang nie blawn tawx ceu pa mhawm ka pui pa khiox khaw ka nawh lie?
12 b“Mawh kheu nan,
Pex simie pui tang kaux yuh ka mawx ka tix,
pex kawx tag caw tix yuh nang nan ka kix heue;
in hoik mawh deeh bleeh lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si mai gum lox pa sidiex cao jhai khankix heue.”
Siviex pa si khawp
(Lai Lu-cia 13.24)
13 “Pex tag caw tix laig ka daux siviex pa si khawp;
mawh kheu siviex pa laig krax laux leeing mawh pa vieh,
krax an kied nyawm tix hu,
pui pa hu plak krax in kied hun.
14 Siviex pa laig krax ju mawh pa kied si khawp,
krax an mawh pa nu tix lawng tete,
pui pa yaox tix lawng ka krax an kawx kied tux heue.”
Khaox mai plix
(Lai Lu-cia 6.43–44)
15 “Pex tag caw rien tix ka cao jhai pa pie lhien.
Kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai pex,
jhak plak praix kix hoik awm kawn yung,
viang mawh nan,
plak daux kix hoik mawh sox la pa caox heue.
16 Pex pon tawng kix ka kaing pa yuh kix.
Pang kat ang pon plix lih mawh plix shiet,
pang kat heeik kawx ang pon plix lih mawh plix ruh.
17 Khaox pa mhawm chix plix pa mhawm;
khaox pa ang mhawm kawx chix plix pa ang mhawm.
18 Khaox pa mhawm ang chix plix ka plix pa caox,
khaox pa caox kawx ang chix plix ka plix pa mhawm;
19 ckhaox pa ang chix plix pa mhawm tag caw tix kid khawm uik,
mai taing sau daux ngu.
20 dMawh kheu nan,
kheu kaing pa yuh kix,
pex pon tawng kix mawh cao jhai pa pie heue.”
Aux ang tawng ka pex
(Lai Lu-cia 13.25–27)
21 “Ang mawh pui ku kaux pa kok ka aux ‘Simiang eue,
Simiang eue’ tag pon tix laig daux meung rao ma khawm uik;
mawh ngie pa hawt ka alo keeing aux Siyiex pa pon tix laig khaing.
22 Yam si dauh haktiex,
pui tom nie tag sang krai ka aux ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
simiang eue,
yix hoik sidiex gum lox Siyiex hu ka daux ceu kaux maix,
mai hoik grheu phi tom nie,
yuh mhai pa daox pa am tom nie ka daux ceu kaux maix.’
23 eYam an,
aux tag sang krai ka kix ah nin:
‘Ang aux sax tawng yung ka pex;
pex pa mawh pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig in kix,
hu singai heue!’ ”
Riah jaong ra ceu
(Lai Lu-cia 6.47–49)
24 “Mawh kheu nan,
can pui pa mhong ka gum lox aux in mai hawt tix yuh,
tag sang awm pui lhak tix kaux pa sum nyiex ka piang rang simaox yuh nan.
25 Viang bhaung ting hoek tix phlu,
lhiex lauh,
rawm klah,
nyiex an kawx ang goh,
mawh kheu riah jaong hoik sum tix ka piang rang simaox.
26 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa mhong ka gum lox aux mai ang hawt tix yuh,
tag sang awm pui daox tix kaux pa sum nyiex ot ka piang mhaik yuh nan,
27 yam hoek bhaung tix phlu,
lhiex lauh,
rawm klah,
nyiex an tag sang goh,
mai pa goh nyiex an kied ting laux heue!”
Siawp sibrawm Ye-sux
28 fKhaix hoik uik krai Ye-sux gum lox in kix,
pui hun kied sidaing daox deng ka gum lox pa sidiex gau nawh khawm uik;
29 mawh kheu ceu pa gau Ye-sux ang awm sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri yuh nan,
gum lox pa sidiex gau nawh kieh koe siawp sibrawm heue.
