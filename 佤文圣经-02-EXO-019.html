Pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka piang gawng Si-nai
19
Yam lih hu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
jiet naog loe khix daux ngaix an,
kix hoek ka daux tiag mhaik Si-nai.
2 Kix ran lih hu khaing Li-fe-ting,
khaix an hoek ka pak mhaik Si-nai,
mai sum bang ot greem gawng Si-nai.
3 Maw-si haok krub Siyiex ka piang gawng heue.
Simiang Siyiex kok Maw-si ka piang gawng,
mai nhie nawh krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
4 “Pex hoik yaox ka aux Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh ka mawx ka pui Ai-cix.
Aux hoik pux ka pex,
awm pux miex klang kawn tix ka piang preeik tix,
mai viex pex hoek tin mawh cie aux.
5 abPhan pex cu nghiet ka gum lox aux,
mai priex gum lox sijax mukheu aux,
pex tag mawh pui cie aux.
Piang haktiex khawm uik mawh cie aux,
viang mawh nan,
mawh ngie pex pa mawh pui pa laug aux,
6 cmawh kawn praix meung pa singax singeh,
mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub aux heue.”
7 Khaix an Maw-si lih khaing piang gawng,
khrawm ka simiang kawn pui,
mai krai gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka nawh sidawng ka kix.
8 Kawn pui Yi-seu-liex rom tix ah nin tau:
“Ex tag sang yuh awm lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex”,
Maw-si tom deeh krai gum lox pa krai kix ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
9 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Aux tag hoek sawg maix ka dee kied pu ceep pai awm,
son sang kieh pui cie aux mhong,
yam si kah aux ka maix,
mai yum rhawm ka maix khaix heue.”
Maw-si deeh krai gum lox pa krai pui Yi-seu-liex ka Simiang Siyiex,
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hu krai ka kawn pui,
daux singaix mai pa sax,
kix caw tix kieh to kaux tix singax singeh.
Kix caw tix si daux kraung ceep tix,
11 hoek daux loe singaix rien khre tix mhawm.
Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang lih hoek ka gawng Si-nai,
mai kieh pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik yaox ka aux.
12 dMaix tag yuh mee hawt vung viang gawng,
krai ka kawn pui ang pon tix jao luan;
mai ang pon tix haok ka piang gawng,
phan ang mawh nan hoek dex ka gawng in.
Can pui pa dig ka gawng in,
tag sang pauh yum,
13 phan ang mawh nan tag sang dheem yum ka simaox,
puing yum ka te,
mai pui mawx ang ah,
ang pon tix teeh ka to kaux yum nawh.
Pui mai Khawng brie tag caw priex tix hawt kah;
can pa lieh lheu tag sang pauh yum.
Tom hoek yam baox luah teet,
kawn pui tag chix tix haok ka gawng heue.”
14 Maw-si lih khaing piang gawng mai tawx lox ka kawn pui,
nhie kix kieh to kaux tix singax singeh,
mai rien khre tix krub.
Kheu nan kix si daux kraung tix;
15 Maw-si krai ka kix:
“Bleeh an ngaix sax pex rien khre tix mhawm;
mai ang pon tix dex ka bun heue.”
16 efYam hoek daux loe singaix plak pon ngawb,
lox tuh sah,
mai puk plak praix lih,
pai awm pa kied pu ceep ka piang gawng,
mai lox pa kied ting si num teet tawng lih.
Pui pa ot ka daux nyiex bang mhong kah,
mai si yaung ka khawm uik.
17 Maw-si thung pui khankix lih khaing daux nyiex bang mai krub ka Siyiex;
kix cung hawt caong gawng.
18 Gawng Si-nai hoik naog ka taox ngu,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka daux ngu.
Taox ngu an awm taox ngu pa kaoh khaing daux glah ngu;
kawn pui yaoh si yaung ka khawm uik.
19 Maw-si si kah,
Siyiex pawk lox nawh ka daux pa si num praix.
20 Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang gawng Si-nai,
mai kok Maw-si haok ka piang gawng,
Maw-si tom haok.
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hu ah ka pui khankix,
paw cu kix jao mee,
son sang jhak ka aux,
phan ang nghiet,
pui tom nie khankix tag laux leeing.
22 Khawm pa mawh cao giex kaing li kawx,
tag caw tix ran kieh to kaux tix singax singeh tom tag chix hoek dex ka aux,
phan ang nghiet,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix heue.”
23 Maw-si ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Pui cie maix khankix ang pon haok;
mawh kheu hoik tawx maix lox ka yix,
nhie yix kieh gawng in singax,
mai yuh mee praok gawng heue.”
24 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix lih hu,
viex Ya-loeng haok tau mai maix.
Viang mawh nan,
paw kieh cao giex kaing li mai kawn pui,
jao mee haok ka aux tin,
phan ang nghiet,
aux tag ciyang ka kix.”
25 Khaix an Maw-si lih hu khaing gawng,
deeh hoek ka kawn pui tan,
mai krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka kix heue.
