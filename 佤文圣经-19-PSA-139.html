Krax pa grum tawng Siyiex mai krax pa kia ngien nawh
139
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix lam lia ka aux,
maix tawng ka aux.
2 Kaing pa yuh aux ku ceu maix hoik tawng ka khawm uik;
jauh tom dee singai kawx maix hoik tawng ka rhawm keud aux.
3 Yam yuh aux kaing,
phan ang mawh nan yam laux lee aux tix,
maix hoik yaox ka aux;
maix hoik tawng ka can kaing pa yuh aux khawm uik.
4 Sivoe ang aux jheeh tix,
maix hoik tawng ka ceu pa sang krai aux.
5 Maix hoik sivie ka sivoe ngai aux plak khaix aux,
ka plak goix plak dawm aux;
maix hoik teei riang rao tix kia ngien ka aux.
6 Krax pa tawng maix ka aux hoik kied raux,
ang mawh pa pon tawng aux sidawng heue.
 
7 Aux hu dee mawx mai tag pon mox tix ka maix lie?
Aux hoek dee mawx mai tag pon mox tix ka maix lie?
8 Aux haok ka rao ma,
maix rien tix ot dee an;
aux laig daux meung yum,
maix kawx tee ot dee an.
9 Phan aux pu hu plak lih singaix,
phan ang mawh nan ka daog dee die rawm sigrit sigra plak klip singaix,
10 maix tee rien tix chix duk thung aux ka dee an;
maix tag teem coi ka aux dee an.
11 Aux chix tix khiox khaw pa faik tawb kheem ka tix,
phan ang mawh nan,
khiox khaw pa riang pa ot sivie siveng tix tieh tix lih mawh pa faik;
12 viang mawh nan,
phan mawh ka maix,
pa faik ang sawn tix mawh pa faik,
pon sawm hoik riang awm pon ngaix yuh nan.
Pa faik mai pa riang hoik awm paox tix heue.
 
13 Ka daux vaig tu tawm nhao aux hoik mawh maix pa phan pa yuh;
yam ot aux ka daux vaig miex tix maix hoik kleuk ka aux.
14 Aux rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix,
kheu hoik mawh maix pa yung yi lhat graung pui kah;
kaing yuh maix mawh pa daox pa deng.
Rhawm rhi aux kied sidaing tawng ka tete.
15 Yam phan aux ot ka daux vaig miex tix,
haok huan ting lhaong si nyek si nyek ka daux pa glip,
si-ang aux lih tix mawh yuh ka mawx,
maix hoik tawng khawm uik.
16 Sivoe sang keh aux,
maix hoik yaox ka aux;
singaix sawm pa hoik yuh son aux pa ang nyang bax lih tix mawh khankix,
hoik tiiem tix ka daux phuk lai maix heue.
17 Siyiex eue,
rhawm keud rhawm sawn maix kied sidaing mawh pa rauxa tete heue!
Krax keud krax sawn maix kied nie tom ang lai pon chix simin heue!
18 Phan aux simin,
kawn lheu nie khaing mhaik pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra;
viang aux hoik simin tom si dauh sidu,
mai aux ca kawn ot tau mai maix.
19 Siyiex eue,
aux kied simie maix pauh ka pui pa caox pa caig heue!
Aux kied simie pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig khankix singai hu khaing tix heue!
20 Kix ang jheeh tix salao ka maix;
kix krai lox pa caox pa caig lud loe ka maix.
21 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux kied yum koeng ka pui pa koeng khoi ka maix heue!
Aux kied yum koeng ka pui pa tieh vheen ka maix heue!
22 Aux kied koeng ka kix tete;
aux kieh kix mawh pui pa koeng ka tix heue.
 
23 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix lam lia tix jhak ka aux,
mai tawng yung ka rhawm rhi aux;
khiox khaw maix cheuk tix yaox ka aux,
mai tawng sidawng ka rhawm keud rhawm sawn aux.
24 Khiox khaw maix jhak tix yaox ka aux koe ang koe rhawm rhi pa caox pa caig;
khiox khaw maix duk thung aux lawng daux krax pa im ju leeing ju leeing phan heueb.
