Sang pon krax pa khaw rhawm Siyiex yuh ka mawx
13
Pex tag caw tix muh paox tix awm pux aik pux o yuh nan.
2 aTag caw tix rien khre ka pui haktiex tang dauh hoek ka daux nyiex ma tix.
Sivoe an koe pui pa hoik sax yuh nin,
mai ang tawng pa rien khre tix mawh kawn cau Siyiex.
3 Tag caw tix doh keud ka pui pa laung ot daux thawng an kix,
awm hoik ot pex daux thawng tau mai kix yuh nan.
Tag caw tix doh keud ka pui pa ot daux krax kha rhawm an kix,
awm hoik ot pex daux krax kha rhawm yuh nan nang heue.
4 Pui ku kaux tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka grawng pa koe mex koe moeng;
mex moeng tag caw tix ra lai kia ngien paox tix ka daux krax pa kaux sijax.
Siyiex tag sang prawng ca ka pui pa pleeng tix ka daux alo nex nham mai pa phit vao heue.
5 bAng pon tix muh ka mau khri,
tag caw tix naog rhawm ka ceu pa koe tix khawm uik.
Mawh kheu Siyiex hoik sax krai ah nin:
“Aux ang lih khaing maix leeing ju,
ang tik vud ka maix leeing ju.”
6 cGrawh grawng nawh,
ex caw tix viex rhawm ting krai ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex mawh pa teem coi ka aux,
aux ang lhat graung;
kawn pui sang pon yuh ceu pa tix ka aux lie?
7 Pex tag caw tix doeh keud ka pui pa duk thung ka pex,
pa sidiex gum lox Siyiex ka pex ka singaix pa hoik luan.
Keud tix yaox ka krax ot krax koe pa yum kix,
yam im kix ka piang haktiex yuh ka mawx;
tag caw tix hawt ka krax yum rhawm kix.
8 Ye-sux Khrit,
kawx kawx,
singaix,
tom hoek leeing ju,
hoik awm paox tix.
9 Paw vi kieh krax gau pa tang siyox ku ceu thung pex lud krax.
Tag caw tix een rhawm tix ka buan son Siyiex,
hoek tix kieh rhawm rhi pex kaux cong.
Paw vi een rhawm tix ka been pa tat tix deuh ka son pa ih pa nyaux;
pui pa een rhawm tix ka been pa tat tix deuh khankix,
ang bax pon krax pa teem coi heue.
10 Cao giex kaing li pui Yo-thaix pa yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
ang koe akhuang tix ih ka khawng tan pa koe ka piang khu dee tan.
11 dCao giex kaing li pa ting pui Yo-thaix viex nham khawng brie laig daux nyiex pa singax,
mai kieh mawh krax tan lu pa pluat ploi phi map;
to kaux khawng brie gleeing ot ka plak praix bang.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux kawx yum ot ka plak praix veng,
son sang teei nham to kaux tix si ghrah ka phi map kawn pui.
13 Phan mawh nan,
ex caw tix hoek sawg nawh ka plak praix bang,
mai guah tix klawm ka krax kaig tua pa kham nawh.
14 Ka piang haktiex ang koe veng pa koe leeing ju;
ex hoik sawg ka veng pa tag sang hoek an.
15 Ex caw tix teei lox rhax krax pa yaog yaw mawh krax tan ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux jheeng jheeng,
mai tan tix tawx ka Siyiex,
pa mawh pa teei ex siyieb jheeh tix hoek krai pao ka ceu kaux nawh.
16 Ang pon tix piawm yuh ka kaing ceu pa mhawm mai pa teem ka pui tang kaux,
mawh kheu krax khawng tan pa yuh nin mawh pa khaw rhawm rhi Siyiex heue.
17 Tag caw tix nghiet ka pui pa duk thung ka pex,
nghiet ka lox kix.
Kix hoik kia ngien ka khuan ngao pex jheeng jheeng,
mawh kheu ka daux singaix pa tag sang hoek,
kix tag sang hoek tix krai kaing yuh tix ka Siyiex sidawng.
Phan pex nghiet ka lox kix,
kix tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi mai yuh tix kaing,
phan ang mawh nan,
kix tag sang laux rhawm laux rhi,
in koe dee mhawm ka pex ceu pa tix lie?
18 Rawg buan son yix ang cud liak.
Yix kaux sijax mai koe rhawm rhi pa singax,
mai cu tix hu krax rauh krax see ku bawg ku yam heue.
19 Aux nie sau riang tix khiox khaw pex rawg buan son tix.
Rawg Siyiex kieh aux pon deeh ing ka pex dee an heue.
Rawg buan
20-21 Siyiex hoik kieh Simiang ex Ye-sux kaoh in khaing krax yum.
Nawh hoik jhi sijax mukheu pa koe leeing ju khaing daux pa hoik jauh nham Ye-sux,
mai kieh nawh lih mawh cao liang phung yung pa ting tix kaux.
Kieh Siyiex pa tawx krax pa nhim ka daux pa hoik yuh pex kaing ceu pa mhawm ku ceu,
kieh pex pon priex tix hawt ka alo nawh heue!
Kieh nawh yuh kaing pa hoik khaw rhawm ka to kaux ex khaing daux Ye-sux Khrit heue!
Kieh min plak ngai mawh cie Khrit,
leeing ju leeing phan ang chix grai heue!
A-meung.
Lox pa nghoik tix krai
22 Pux aik pux o aux khankix eue,
aux khiox khaw pex viex rhawm gun yeung rieb ka lox pa sidiex gau in;
aux ang bax tiiem lox lai in tom lang.
23 Aux sang kieh pex tawng,
pux aik aux Thi-maw-thaix hoik lih khaing daux thawng.
Phan nawh phai hoek ceeix,
nawh taug yaox tix hu kia ngien pex tau mai aux heue.
24 Chawk pui pa duk thung ka pex mai ka can kawn Khrit khawm uik mhawm ot.
Kawn Khrit pa kaoh khaing Yi-ta-lix chawk pex mhawm ot heue.
25 Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka pex ku kaux heue!
