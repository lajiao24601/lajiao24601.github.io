Ya-ma-shiex simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 14.2–6)
25
Yam koe ju Ya-ma-shiex tix nga phuan num nawh mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix nga dim num.
Miex nawh Yaw-yie-tang mawh pui Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 Ya-ma-shiex hoik yuh kaing pa mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax yuh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
3 Yam hoik pon giex nawh simiang mawh tix kaux cong,
nawh tom pauh ka siliex pa pauh keeing nawh khankix yum.
4 aViang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax pauh ka kawn praix pui an kix yum,
mai hawt tix yuh ka gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex khaing daux lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si ah nin:
“Miex keeing tag ang khaw tix yum tang phi map pa yuh kawn praix tix;
kawn praix kawx tag ang khaw tix yum tang phi map pa yuh miex keeing tix.
Kaux pui mawx kaux an tag sang tang klawm ka phi map pa yuh cao tix heue.”
Naing ka daux Yi-tong
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 14.7)
5 Simiang Ya-ma-shiex khrawm ka ceu pui Yo-tax mai ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
mai hawt awm ceu pui kix yuh lih koe pui naing tix cub tix rheng kaux mai tix cub tix yieh kaux,
ku cub tawx simiang naing mawh cao bung.
Hoik mawh khawm simex pa koe ju tix nga num haok plak piang,
khawm uik koe pui tix ngoe mheen kaux.
Kix hoik mawh pui naing pa bre tix laug lih,
pa pon puing ka naing,
mai pa mhawm siao ka pliah mai dieb tie naok.
6 Lheu khaing an,
Ya-ma-shiex baox teei mau luah loe rheng pon yieh kongcing vai tix siao pui naing Yi-seu-liex ka kao mheen kaux.
7 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex tix kaux hu ka simiang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Paw vi viex pui naing Yi-seu-liex hu tau mai maix;
Simiang Siyiex ang ot tau mai pui meung in kix pa ot plak laong.
8 Maix tee ce kix sang chix teem coi tix puing naing;
tete te lai,
hoik mawh Siyiex pa mawh cao pa sang chix pon tat grawng sang pex ang pex;
nawh tag sang kieh pui koeng ka maix pon pex ka maix heue.”
9 Ya-ma-shiex chawk pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawx mau luah ka kix,
sang khaw tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
Pui cie Siyiex ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex tag sang chix tawx ka maix tom nie!”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-ma-shiex nhie pui naing pa vai tix deeh ing ka nyiex,
kix tom song rhawm ka pui Yo-tax,
mai yaog tix ing khawm song rhawm tix heue.
11 Ya-ma-shiex greeik riang tix lih,
mai thung pui naing hoek ka ngawd kih;
nawh pauh pui Yi-tong yum dee an tix mheen kaux,
12 mai baox pon giex im pui tix mheen kaux.
Kix viex pui pa giex kix hoek ka veng Si-la ka yoeh rang,
mai cot kix cud dee an,
to kaux kix ku kaux hoik laux leeing heue.
13 Ka daux bawg yam an kawx,
pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa ploi Ya-ma-shiex deeh ing puing ka veng Yo-tax ku veng ka Sa-ma-li-yax mai ka si nax Paw Heu-loeng,
pauh ka pui loe rheng kaux,
tud ka kraung khrai tom nie heue.
14 Ya-ma-shiex deeh hoek khaing pon pex tix ka pui Yi-tong,
riang rao pa krub pui Yi-tong kawx kix viex hoek tan lu kah,
mai gleeing nyawng bhawng krub ka riang an kix.
15 Kaing in hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh ploi cao jhai hu ka Ya-ma-shiex,
mai ne raw ka nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai krub ka siyiex pui tang ceu tang cad an kix lie?
Pie sang taoh nawh pui cie tix luat luan khaing tawm taix maix kawn ang pon heue!”
16 Ya-ma-shiex sieb lox ah nin:
“Yix ang bax kok maix mawh cao pa teem coi Vawng.
Naox heue,
phan ang mawh nan aux tag sang pauh maix yum heue!”
Sivoe sang naox cao jhai lox tix nawh baox krai ah nin:
“Yam in aux tawng Siyiex hoik but tix yuh maix laux leeing;
mawh kheu hoik yuh maix kaing in,
mai ang rieb ka lox pa sidiex gau aux heue.”
Puing pui Yi-seu-liex
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 14.8–20)
17 Khaix hoik kum kawng Yo-tax mai simiang Ya-ma-shiex mai pui cao teem coi nawh,
nawh ploi kawn cau tix hu ka kawn saux Yie-hu,
kawn simex Yaw-ha-see simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see,
tiex kix puing paox.b
18 Yaw-hua-see pawk lox nawh ah nin:
“Koe tix bawg,
pang kat pa koe piang gawng Li-pa-noeng tix gawng ah nin ka khaox si oe bhawng tix gawng:
‘Tawx kawn bun maix mex kawn simex aux liak!’
Koe to sat tix mu hoek tan,
mai dug dig pang kat an laux.
19 Ya-ma-shiex eue,
maix ting rhawm lhaong rhi kheu hoik pex tix ka pui Yi-tong,
viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka maix,
maix ot laux tix ka daux nyiex mhawm mhawm khaing!
Yuh pa tix lai teei si mu tix,
mai kieh to kaux tix mai pui cie tix hoek krax laux krax leeing lie?”
20 Ya-ma-shiex ang nghiet.
In hoik mawh alo pa yuh Siyiex son sang kieh Ya-ma-shiex ang pex,
mawh kheu hoik krub nawh riang rao pa krub pui Yi-tong.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see,
rieb tix puing simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex ka veng Paw See-mai haktiex pui Yo-tax.
22 Pui naing Yo-tax ang pex,
can pui naing khawm uik tang yaog tix to ing ka nyiex.
23 Yaw-hua-see pon giex ka Ya-ma-shiex,
mai viex nawh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai baox yieh laux ka guh veng,
jauh ka siviex Yi-fa-liien tom hoek ka Rung Siviex,
been lang koe ra yieh kongjhee.
24 Nawh hoik teei ka khri,
mau,
kraung khrai nyiex buan pa pau doeh ceu cad Eu-piie Yi-tong,
khawm sin ngao nyiex haw simiang viex hoek ka Sa-ma-li-yax khawm uik,
khawm pui pa pon giex kix kawx hoik viex hu tix blah heue.
25 Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex kawn lheu lang im ju khaing simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see kao phuan num.
26 Yam giex nawh kaing pa hoik yuh nawh tang ceu khankix,
jauh ka num tom hoek ka rao hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Yo-tax mai Lai Been Simiang Yi-seu-liex.
27 Jauh yam jauh nawh tix tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex,
ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik koe pui pa sang keud tix yuh nawh laux.
Dee si dauh nawh to hu mox tix ka daux veng La-ciex,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa koeng hawt nawh hoek ka dee an,
mai pauh nawh heue.
28 Koe pui tang to kaux yum nawh ka brung hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang ka veng Ta-wue.
