Been ap pong Ya-loeng mai kawn praix nawh yuh cao giex kaing li
(Lai Li-we 8.1–36)
29
“Maix ap pong ara ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex Ya-loeng khankix,
ap kix mawh cao giex kaing li mai yuh kaing krub ka aux.
Kaing pa caw maix tix sang yuh mawh in kix plak greem:
teei kawn moi mhaing tix mu,
mai kawn yung mhaing ra mu pa ang koe dee ne pa mhawm loig;
2 Ping khao mun pa ang koe su saix tix blah ka mien phawng nghox ca pa blawn mhawm,
pa klu klao mai bee pa ang koe su saix,
mai baox ping khao mun rhi khlawp pa ang koe su saix pa hoik kraix bee kah.
3 Sau khao mun in kix ka daux khrawng,
mai viex hoek tau yam tan kawn moi mhaing mai yung mhaing ra mu ka aux heue.
4 “Maix viex Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
tawx lox nhie kix khoek heem tix.
5 Khaix an tawx Ya-loeng ceep kraung cao giex kaing li,
pa mawh sibex dha plak daux,
sibex dha plak praix pa vhak Yi-fu-teu,
na bat naok,
mai maox.
6 Teei na bat naog pa pawd tix koe ‘rhoi tawx mawh cie Simiang Siyiex’,
vien ot ka piang mhok khri,
khaix an ceep ka kaing nawh,
7 baox teei bee pa singax singeh groi ka piang kaing nawh,
ap ka nawh heue.
8 “Maix viex kawn simex nawh khankix hoek,
mai ceep kix cie sibex dha plak daux,
9 rhawt maox rawd nyoi,
ceep ka mhok.
In hoik mawh been beeh glawm cao giex kaing li ka Ya-loeng mai ka kawn simex nawh khankix.
Kix mai doeh ceu cad kix,
tag sang mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux leeing ju leeing phan heue.
10 “Maix teei moi mhaing tuk hoek ka sivoe nyiex gox buan man,
mai tawx lox kieh Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix deuh taix tix piang kaing moi.
11 Ot ka diang siviex dee laig ka nyiex gox buan man,
ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai pauh ka moi mhaing an heue.
12 Teei ka nham moi an tix blah,
mai teei giien taix tix kraix ka piang dee bhlien rung khu tix lih pon rung;
mai hieh nham pa kawn deuk ka jaong khu.
13 Khaix an teei bee pa kheem kraung vaig,
tawm dee blawn mhawm,
mai sok lax ra mu pa khawn bee,
deuh ot piang khu mai gleeing tix tan ka aux.
14 Viang mawh nan,
nex moi mhaing an,
mai hak khawm kraung vaig khankix,
tag gleeing ka plak praix nyiex bang.
In mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap cao giex kaing li heue.
15 “Maix tuk yung mhaing tix mu hoek,
mai tawx lox kieh Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix deuh taix tix ka piang kaing yung mhaing an.
16 Pauh ka yung mhaing an,
khaix an teei nham tix blah mai phruah hawt vung viang khu dee tan.
17 Pat nex yung mhaing an lih mawh khik;
si ghrah kraung vaig mai ba plak kreue singax,
mai deuh ot ka piang kaing mai ka piang khik khankix.
18 a Gleeing nex yung mhaing khawm mu ka piang khu dee tan,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex.
Taox pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Ngu in mawh pa muh aux heue.
19 “Maix tuk yung mhaing tang mu hoek,
tawx lox kieh Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix deuh taix tix ka piang kaing yung an.
20 Pauh ka yung mhaing an,
khaix an teei nham tix blah kraix ka piang dawng yhaok plak dawm Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
mai baox kraix ka piang giien taix ting khawn dawm mai giien caong ting khawn dawm kix.
Nham pa kawn deuk khankix,
phruah hawt vung viang khu dee tan.
21 Teei nham pa koe ka piang khu dee tan ceeix,
mai bee pa singax singeh ceeix,
phruah ka piang Ya-loeng mai ka piang sibex nawh,
mai baox phruah ka piang kawn simex nawh khankix,
mai ka piang kraung kix.
Yuh nin,
nawh mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
khawm sibex kix tag yaox tix singax singeh tix tawx mawh cie aux khawm uik heue.
22 “Maix pat khlawp bee yung mhaing mai si dax,
bee pa kheem kraung vaig,
tawm dee blawn mhawm,
bee pa lhawp sok lax ra mu,
mai ba khawn plak kreue khawn dawm.
23 Caw teei khao mun pa ang koe su saix pa deuh ot ka daux khrawng pa tan ka aux tix lon,
khao mun pa yuh ka bee tix khlawp mai khao mun pa rhi tix khlawp.
24 Deuh priex preem in kix khawm uik ka daux tawm taix Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
kieh mawh krax tan pa blawk tix tan ka aux.
25 Khaix an maix rieb tix teei priex preem khaing daux tawm taix kix,
deuh piang khu dee tan gleeing ka ngu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex.
Taox pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Ngu in,
mawh pa muh aux ka heue.
26 “Maix teei nex eek yung mhaing an,
kieh mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka aux.
Nyum nex tan in hoik mawh cie maix heue.
27 “Nex eek yung mai ba pa hoik siao son ap pong ara cao giex kaing li an,
tag sang gleeing ka uik,
kieh mawh krax tan pa blawk tix tan ka aux,
khaix an tawx ka cao giex kaing li khankix.
28 Lox tat jhawm prawng pa ang aux chix tieh loh mawh:
yam tan pui cie aux ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
nex eek khawng brie mai ba,
caw tix tawx ka cao giex kaing li khankix;
kheu an hoik mawh khawng tan pa blawk pa tan pui cie aux ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.
29 “Khaix hoik yum Ya-loeng,
kraung ceep pa singax singeh nawh,
tag sang deeh tawx ka kawn praix nawh khankix,
mai kieh kix ceep yam rieb tix yuh kaing pa singax singeh in.
30 Kawn simex Ya-loeng pa plud tix mawh cao giex kaing li an,
yam hoek nawh ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
mai yuh kaing krub ka aux ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
caw tix ceep ka sibex pa singax singeh in,
aliah singaix heue.
31 “Maix caw tix teei nex yung mhaing pa hoik siao Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix yam ap pong ara cao giex kaing li,
hu ngaik ka dee pa singax singeh.
32 Kix tag ih ka nex yung mai khao mun pa koe daux khrawng ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man.
33 Kix kawx tag ih ka priex preem pa siao pluat ploi phi map son pa ap pong.
Mawh ngie cao giex kaing li pa pon tix ih khaing,
kheu priex preem in mawh pa singax singeh.
34 Phan koe nex mai khao mun ang uik ih,
pa kawn deuk tom hoek plak pon ngawb,
tag gleeing tix tik daux ngu,
ang lai pon tix baox deeh ih,
kheu priex preem in mawh pa singax singeh heue.
35 “Maix hawt ka gum lox pa tawm aux khawm uik,
mai yuh ka poi pa ap pong ara son Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix aliah singaix.
36 Ku singaix,
maix tag caw tix tan ka moi mhaing tix mu,
mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
mai kieh khu dee tan singax singeh,
khaix an teei bee sime kraix ka khu dee tan,
kieh singax.
37 Maix yuh nin ku singaix,
si dauh aliah singaix,
mai kieh khu dee tan singax khawm uik.
Can pui pa teeh ka khu dee tan mai khawng brie ang ah,
tag sang mat ka siawp sibrawm riang rao pa singax singeh heueb.”
Krax tan pa sang yuh ku ngaix
(Lai Simin-pui 28.1–8)
38 “Maix caw tix tan lu kawn yung pa koe tix num ka piang khu dee tan ku singaix,
ang pon tix kieh khan paox tix;
39 Plak pon ngawb tan tix mu,
plak pon bo tan tix mu.
40 Yam tan ka daux tix mu,
caw tan tau mai phawng mien tix kongcing,
klao tau mai bee sime tix kongsheng,
khaix an tan lu plai tix kawng kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-jauh.
41 Yam tan maix yung ka daux ra mu plak pon bo,
tag tan awm plak pon ngawb tan Krax-tan Nghox-koi mai Krax-tan Pa-jauh.
Taox bhawng Krax-tan Ngu tix tan ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
kieh aux muh ka heue.
42 Leeing ju leeing phan,
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing in caw tan ot sivoe ngai aux,
ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man;
pa mawh dee si pup aux ka pui cie tix,
mai dee si kah tau mai maix tan.
43 Aux tag sang si pup ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tan.
Min plak ngai aux tag sang singax yuh haktiex an.
44 Aux sang kieh nyiex gox buan man mai khu dee tan singax,
tag kieh Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix tang guah tix lih,
mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux.
45 Aux sang ot ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai mawh Siyiex kix.
46 Kix sang tawng mawh aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex kix;
aux teei kix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
mawh son sang ot aux ka daux kix.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix heue.”
