Lox rawg buan pui pa ang koe dee lud
17
Simiang Siyiex eue,
nghiet ka lox khiox khaw pa kok yo aux pa rauh pa see;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox yiiem aux,
nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix liak!
2 Kieh maix krai pao grawng hoik prawng aux ang koe dee lud,
mawh kheu hoik tawng maix ceu tix mawh pa rauh see heue.
 
3 Maix hoik tawng ka daux rhawm rhi aux,
maix hoek tix lam lia aux yam pon sawm;
maix hoik cheuk ka aux tom si dauh,
mai ang yaox grawng pa koe aux krax pa caox pa caig.
4 Aux ang awm pui tang kaux krai lox pa caox pa caig;
aux hawt ka lox pa tawx maix,
ang hu krax hu pui tud pui rhim.
5 Aux hoik hawt khaix diang caong maix tom si dauh,
ang sax guah hu khaing heue.
 
6 Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw ka maix,
mawh kheu maix chix pawk ka lox aux jheeng jheeng;
khiox khaw maix veeh yhaok tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux.
7 Khiox khaw maix jhi krax muh pa daox deng tix taoh ka aux;
phan aux een rhawm tix ka maix,
aux tag sang pon mox tix khaing pui koeng heue.
 
8 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka aux,
awm kia ngien ka tawm ngai tix;
kieh aux mox tix ot ka greem preeik maix,
9 yaox tix luat luan khaing krax pa tuk tiak pui pa caox pa caig heue.
 
Pui koeng hoik lai lawm aux ka daux krax pa ting koeng tix;
10 rhawm rhi kix kiien,
mai krai lox cau nyhau.
11 Viang aux hu plak mawx kawx,
kix hoik lai lawm ka aux khawm uik;
kix sawg bawg yam tix sang cot aux goh.
12 Kix awm sivai sang si yuh nan sang riah aux phawng,
awm sivai sang si nhum ju pa kuan mox tix ka dee glip,
mai krox jun ka aux heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kaoh maix rieb tix puing ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
mai giex ka kix heue!
Khiox khaw maix teei vaig tix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa caox pa caig.
14 Khiox khaw maix teei siawp sibrawm tix taoh aux luat luan khaing kix,
pa mawh pui an kix pa mag mi ka daux baux yam in.
Khiox khaw maix tawx krax ciyang pa cien ka piang kix;
mai kieh kawn praix kix yaox tix kham krax laux krax leeing tom kub,
kawn koe khawm ceu pa deuh son doeh kawn simex kawn bun kix heue.
 
15 Mawh kheu rauh see aux,
mai aux pon yaox tix hoek krub ka maix;
yam haing aux,
krax pa ot tau maix mai kieh aux grum naog ka krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
