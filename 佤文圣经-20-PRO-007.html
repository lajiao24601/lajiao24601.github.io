7
Pui pa nhum ju eue,
tag caw tix priex ka lox krai aux,
ang pon tix piawm ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux.
2 Priex tix hawt ka lox sidiex lox gau aux,
tag chix kieh maix pon krax pa im ju.
Tag caw rien tix ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux,
awm kia ngien maix ka tawm ngai tix yuh nan.
3 Tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux ku bawg ku yam,
mai tiiem ot ka daux rhawm rhi tix.
4 Kieh ping nyia cuyi mawh pux o maix,
kieh krax keud krax sawn mawh paox grawm ting ka maix.
5 Kix tag sang kieh maix singai hu khaing moeng pui tang kaux,
mai mox tix khaing lox pa nyaw pa glaw bun coh kaux heue.
Bun pa coh kaux
6 Koe tix bawg yam,
aux kok jhak khaing daux doh siviex nyiex tix,
7 mai yaox ka pui nhum ju pa daox aw khankix;
aux nie bre tix jhak ka pui pa blawn daox ka daux kix tix kaux.
8 Nawh hu krax daux laih plak dee ot bun coh kaux,
9 plak pon bo khaix hoik faik praix,
nawh hu ka diang siviex nyiex ot nawh;
10 bun an lih rieb ka nawh,
jawg tix awm sibun coh kaux,
ka daux rhawm rhi nawh hoik keud ceu pa ang mhawm.
11 Bun an hoik dawg mawh pui pa ang koe krax kaig tua,
hoik yie kok yeeb taix tix ka pui daux laih jheeng jheeng,
12 mai cung tin,
lai ling tan,
bawg tix blah ot ka daux laih,
bawg tix blah ot ka daux bang hun pui.
13 Nawh kawm ka pui pa nhum ju an,
ngheet ka nawh,
mai nyiah ah nin:
14 “Ka daux singaix,
aux ap pong tix yuh krax tan lu,
ka daux nyiex aux hoik deuh ka nex tan,
15 mawh kheu nan,
aux tom lih hoek tix sawg ka maix.
Aux sang sawg ka maix,
mai maix rub ot tin heue!
16 Aux hoik teei prux bi Ai-cix pa koe rawm siang tawm ka ku,
17 teei Muran,
Acaw,
cai hung sau ot ka ku.
18 Hoek heue,
kieh ax klieh tom phlat riang praix,
mai kawm paox yuh krax gaux rhawm.
19 Mex aux ang ot ka nyiex,
nawh lih hu singai khaing nyiex.
20 Nawh hoik viex mau ka to kaux tix tom nie,
ka daux ra vang tuah nawh ang nyang chix deeh hoek ka nyiex.”
21 Bun pui nyaw glaw nawh ka greeh grang nex nham tix,
mai nyaw glaw nawh ka lox mhawm jhawm jiet tix.
22 Pui pa nhum ju an hawt khaix nawh dum mawx dum an,
awm moi pa tuk pui tix hu pauh,
awm cak pa cawd ka daux o,
23 mai klauh rhawm nawh pang phlat;
awm kawn sim pa krix ka rub taong pui,
mai kawn ang tawng ju tix ot ka daux krax pa laux leeing heue.
24 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
yam in nghiet ka lox pa krai aux;
bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux.
25 Paw vi kieh bun pa yuh nin pon nyaw ka rhawm rhi pex;
paw vi kieh tix daox aw hawt khaix nawh.
26 Nawh hoik laux yuh pui tom nie,
hoik kieh pui yum tom ang lai uik simin.
27 Phan maix hu plak nyiex nawh,
hoik mawh hu maix plak krax yum,
plak krax dawt pa hu ka meung yum heue.
