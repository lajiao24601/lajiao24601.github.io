Siyiex tag sang ciyang pui koeng
34
Pui meung ka piang haktiex eue,
hoek khawm uik heue!
Pex hoek tix nghiet khawm uik.
Pui piang haktiex hoek tix nghiet ka plak sivoe ngai aux khawm uik.
2 Simiang Siyiex hoik song rhawm ka meung ku meung,
mai ka pui naing ku meung.
Nawh hoik tat ka phi map kix,
mai yuh kix laux lak pak leeing.
3 To kaux yum kix hoik jhi tix ot plak praix,
hoik cux hoik si um;
ka piang gawng daux tiag hoik mawh nham khawm uik.
4 aSingaix,
khix,
mai simuing hoik ceh tix lih mawh nyaox.
Rao ma tag sang grai,
mai awm bua lai pa hoik pua pui tix deuh yuh nan;
simuing hoik khrum,
awm hoik khrum plix ruh mai lhax khaox plix siiet yuh nan heue.
5 bSimiang Siyiex hoik klaing vaig ka piang rao ma;
yam in nawh tag sang pauh ka pui Yi-tong laux leeing pa hoik dawg prawng nawh tix deuh.
6 Nham kix mai bee kix tag sang kraix tix naog ka lawm vaig nawh,
awm nham kawn yung mai nham kawn pex,
mai bee vaig pa koe ka sok lax kawn yung mhaing pa tan lu,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang yuh krax tan ka Paw-see-la,
mai pauh pui yum tom nie ka Yi-tong heue.
7 Kawn pui tag sang goh tix yum awm moi tiag mai kawn moi yuh nan;
nham hoik naog ka haktiex kix,
bee vaig hoik kheem ka daux tiag khawm uik.
8 In hoik mawh singaix pa taoh Simiang Siyiex ka Shi-ang,
mai singaix pa pawk nawh bix ka pui koeng heue.
9 Rawm klawng Yi-tong tag sang tieh tix lih mawh bee cap,
haktiex tieh tix lih mawh mhat;
meung haktiex khawm uik tag sang lih mawh bee cap,
10 ctag cap leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
taox ngu haok ka rao ma,
mai ang chix yeet leeing ju.
Haktiex in tag sang riem tix ju hoek tix ju,
mai ang lai koe pui pa hoek.
11 Haktiex an mawh ngie dee laux klang rawm,
goh,
pang plawh,
mai lak.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh dee an mawh dee yi pa faik,
pa ang koe pak peung awm yam jauh tix phan yuh haktiex sivoe an yuh nan.
12 Ang koe simiang,
mai ang koe cao pa kuang ka haktiex in.
13 Pang kat yeem yaw hoik naog ka nyiex haw simiang mai guh veng khawm uik,
mawh ngie sox tiag mai sim ka-la-uk khankix pa koe ka dee an.
14 Sox la,
pex tiag,
mai to tiag tang ceu khankix tag sang lai ling dee an;
si ngie phi tag sang kok yo,
mai ra lai pawk lox paox tix;
si ngie yam pon sawm,d
hoek tix sawg dee ot tix.
15 Pang plawh yuh mheen tix dee an,
tawm dee an,
num dee an,
eei kawn tix ting dee an.
Klang teei ia pu tix hoek ku mawx ku an,
mai khrawm paox tix dee an heue.
16 Pex hu si kaw tix yaox ka lai Simiang Siyiex,
jhak tix yaox ka daux lai krai ah ka mawx.
To tiag in kix ang bax piawm tix ceu kawx,
mai kix hoik yuh paox yuh pie tau.
In hoik mawh gum lox pa tawm to kaux Simiang Siyiex;
nawh tag sang kieh kix khrawm paox tix tau,
17 mai to kaux nawh tag sang guah ka haktiex in,
kieh kix koe nyum cie ku mu,
khaix an mai ot dee an tix ju hoek tix ju.
Haktiex in tag sang mawh cie kix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
