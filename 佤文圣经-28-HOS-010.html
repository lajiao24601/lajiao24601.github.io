10
Pui Yi-seu-liex awm khaox plix siiet pa kied nie plix yuh nan.
Kix nied tix haok huan,
khu dee tan kawx nied tix haoh;
haktiex kix nied tix lih to ma tom nie,
rong simaox pa krub kix kawx nied tix yuh mhawm.
2 Ka daux rhawm rhi kix naog ka pa pie lhien,
mawh kheu nan kix tag sang kham krax ciyang kheu phi map tix.
Siyiex tag sang pauh max ka khu dee tan kix,
mai yuh laux ka can rong simaox pa krub kix heue.
3 Pui in kix ang rheeng tag sang salao ah nin:
“Yix ang koe simiang,
mawh kheu ang ex lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex.
Viang mawh nan,
simiang sang pon yuh ceu pa tix son ex lie?
4 Kix krai lox pa giex going,
ap ka lox pa pie lhien,
yuh sijax mukheu pa ang koe ceu.
Krax pa rauh pa see hoik lih mawh pa ang rauh ang see,
mai hoik awm rib riem pa koe jai ka daux tiag daux tu yuh nan heue.”
5 Pui Sa-ma-li-yax tag sang yaoh yee kheu pa hoik grai riang kawn moi khri Paw-theu-lix.
Kix hoik rom tix ngia tau mai cao giex kaing li pa kuang ka riang rao an,
mai hoik laux rhawm yiiem grai khawm uik kheu pa hoik grai min plak ngai riang kawn moi khri an.
6 Riang kawn moi khri in tag sang viex hu ka Ya-sux,
mai kieh mawh khawng phak pa tan ka simiang ting Ya-sux.
Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang pon krax pa kaig tua,
mawh kheu krub kix riang rao.
7 Simiang kix pui tag sang viex nawh hu,
awm bliah khaox iak pa hoik chun rawm hu yuh nan.
8 bKhu dee tan pa ot ka gawng iak Ya-weung,a
pa mawh dee krub pui Yi-seu-liex ka riang rao,
tag sang laux leeing khawm uik.
Khu dee tan kix tag sang lih kuah naog ka pang kat rib riem pa koe jai.
Kix tag sang ah nin ka gawng ting:
“Tawb kheem ka yix heue!”
Ah nin ka gawng iak:
“Ghrup piang to kaux yix heue!”
Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka pui Yi-seu-liex
9 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Jauh khaix yuh kix phi map ka haktiex Ci-pi-yax,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik ca yuh krax pa phit lud ka aux ang jud,
mawh kheu nan Ci-pi-yax tag sang kham krax laux leeing ka naing.
10 Aux tag sang yuh naingd ka cub pui pa yuh phi map in kix,
mai ciyang ka kix.
Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang khrawm tix hoek lai lawm tix puing ka kix;
kix tag sang kham krax ciyang kheu phi map ku ceu heue.
11 “Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik sax awm moi siang pa pheuk pui tix mu yuh nan,
mai hoik nieb pon dig ka nghox jheeng jheeng.
Viang mawh nan,
aux but rhawm tix sau khaox glawme ot piang ngawg pa kluing nawh,
mai kieh nawh yuh kaing pa nie ting ceu.
Aux kieh pui Yo-tax thai kaung,
kieh Yi-seu-liex thai ma.
12 fAux ah nin:
‘pex tag caw tix paih ma son tix,
rhuat simie pa rauh pa see,
khrawm vawg ka krax muh krax rhawm.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
yam in hoik mawh bawg yam pa khaw pex tix hoek ka aux.
Aux tag sang hoek,
mai kieh pa khaw caw lih piang pex awm rawm lhiex yuh nan.’
13 Viang mawh nan,
pex ang nghiet,
mai tieh tix rhuat ka simie pa caox pa caig,
khrawm vawg ka pa ang rauh ang see,
mai kieh krax pa caox caig deeh hoek ka piang to kaux tix heue.
“Mawh kheu hoik een pex rhawm tix ka ling law naingg cie to kaux tix mai ka pui naing pa haoh hun,
14 naing tag sang lih hoek ka daux kawn pui,
can veng tag sang laux leeing khawm uik.
Grawng kaing in tag sang kieh kix kha rhawm tix awm pa hoik yuh simiang Sa-leu-mang ka veng Paw Ya-pi-leux laux leeing yuh nin:
pui hoik si blat miex kix mai kawn simex kawn bun kix yum khawm uik.
15 Pui Paw-theu-lix eue!
Mawh kheu hoik yuh pex phi map pa ting,
pex kawx tag sang si pup ka krax laux leeing pa yuh in nang.
Yam hoik lih naing,
pui tag sang yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex laux leeing heue.”
