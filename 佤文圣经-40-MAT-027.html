Ap tix tawx Ye-sux ka Pi-la-to
(Lai Ma-kheu 15.1; Lai Lu-cia 23.1–2; Lai Yaw-hang 18.28–32)
27
Pon ngawb cao cao,
can simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song pui Yo-thaix kum kawng paox tix,
son sang pauh ka Ye-sux.
2 Kix phrawk ka Ye-sux,
giex nawh hu tawx ka Pi-la-to cao bung kaing Law-ma heue.
Grawng yum Yo-tax
(Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 1.18–19)
3 aYo-tax pa coh Ye-sux yaox grawng pa prawng ca kix ka Ye-sux,
nawh klih rhawm tix,
mai teei mau tix ngoe plah viex hu tawx ka simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song khankix,
4 ah nin:
“Aux hoik yuh phi map,
mai hoik coh ka ju pui pa ang koe dee lud heue!”
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“An mawh kaing cie to kaux maix,
ang koe grawh grawng mai yix?”
5 Yo-tax tik mau ka daux nyiex buan mai hu khaing,
nawh sau maox ka ngawg tix tang plaux tix heue.
6 Simiang cao giex kaing li teei ka mau an ah nin:
“In mawh mau nham,
phan sau ka daux teug tan mawh pa lud.”
7 Grawng krax kum kix,
kix cu tix teei mau in hu liag haktiex cie cang mai yuh aw tix dauh,
kieh mawh haktiex dee si paung ka pui pa ot haktiex tang dauh.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
hoek tom yam in pui kawn kok haktiex in mawh “Kaung nham”.
9 bGrawh grawng kaing in hoik kieh gum lox pa krai cao jhai Yie-li-mi lih tix mawh ah nin:
“Kix hoik teei mau tix ngoe plah,
pa hoik cu pui Yi-seu-liex tix tawx ngawh son nawh,
pa liag ka haktiex cie cang mai yuh aw;
10 in hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka aux heue.”
Kham krax prawng ka plak sivoe ngai Pi-la-to
(Lai Ma-kheu 15.2–5; Lai Lu-cia 23.3–5; Lai Yaw-hang 18.33–38)
11 Ye-sux cung ka plak sivoe ngai cao kuang kaing,
cao kuang kaing chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh maix simiang pui Yo-thaix lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“In hoik mawh maix pa krai heue.”
12 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song deuh phi map ka piang nawh,
nawh ang pawk pie tix gum kawx heue.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-la-to krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ang maix bax mhong lox pa deuh kix phi map piang maix tom nie ceu lie?”
14 Ye-sux ca kawn ang pawk pie tix gum;
Pi-la-to kied daox deng ka heue.
Prawng nawh yum
(Lai Ma-kheu 15.6–15; Lai Lu-cia 23.13–25; Lai Yaw-hang 18.39–19.16)
15 Ka daux Poi Luat-luan ku num,
cao kuang kaing hawt awm been pa tat kix son pui hun ploi ka pui lud pa sang lo kix tix kaux.
16 Yam an,
jiet koe pui lud pa ao min tix kaux kok mawh cPa-la-pa.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
yam khrawm pui hun paox tix,
Pi-la-to chawk kix ah nin:
“Sang kieh pex aux ploi pui mawx son pex lie?
dPa-la-pa lie?
Mawh pa kok mawh Ye-sux Khrit lie?”
18 Pi-la-to hoik tawng mawh jao boh rhawm kix tom ap tix tawx Ye-sux ka nawh heue.
19 Yam paoh Pi-la-to krax pa prawng ca,
moeng nawh ploi pui hoek krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Phan mawh grawng kaing pui pa ang bax lud,
maix paw vi kuang,
mawh kheu aux ca maox tix kha rhawm tix son nawh ka daux bo kawx heue.”
20 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song khankix cheeik pui hun,
kix tom mhaing Pi-la-to ploi ka Pa-la-pa,
mai pauh Ye-sux yum.
21 Viang mawh nan,
cao kuang kaing chawk kix ah nin:
“Ka daux pui in kiex ra kaux,
sang kieh pex aux ploi pui mawx lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Pa-la-pa heue!”
22 Pi-la-to chawk kix ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
aux sang pauh ka kaw pa kok tix mawh Ye-sux Khrit yuh ka mawx lie?”
Kix tom kok yo khawm uik ah nin:
“Tawk nawh piang khaox lhak heue!”
23 Pi-la-to chawk ah nin:
“Hoik yuh nawh kaing lud ceu tix lie?”
Kix nied tix kok tom ting ah nin:
“Tawk nawh piang khaox lhak heue!”
24 ePi-la-to jhak tix yaox grawng an,
viang nawh hoik krai yuh ka mawx kawx ang lai koe ceu,
mai tieh tix kieh pui lawh maw,
nawh tom teei rawm khoek taix tix ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai ah nin:
“Pa jauh nham pui in,
phi map ang koe piang aux,
kieh to kaux pex tang klawm heue!”
25 Pui hun rom tix ah nin:
“Nham pa jauh nawh yix mai kawn praix yix tag klawm ka heue!”
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-la-to ploi tix tawx Pa-la-pa ka kix,
mai baox tawx lox nhie kix si vied ka Ye-sux,
khaix an tawx pui tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue.
Pui naing map nai ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 15.16, 20; Lai Yaw-hang 19.2–3)
27 Pui naing Pi-la-to viex Ye-sux laig daux nyiex cao kuang kaing;
pui khawm uik khrawm tix ot praok nawh.
28 Kix peeik ka kraung ceep Ye-sux,
mai tawx nawh ceep ka kraung dha raoh tix pheen,
29 mai baox teei mhok pa yuh ka kat lawm tawx nawh ceep,
teei maox gawng priem tix gawng deuh ot ka plak taix khawn dawm nawh,
khaix an grux ngawng tix plak sivoe ngai nawh ah nin:
“Simiang pui Yo-thaix lang ju pu rhang heue!”
30 Kix baox baig ka nawh,
mai teei maox priem si vied ka kaing nawh.
31 Khaix hoik map nai kix ka nawh,
kix tom peeik ka kraung dha khaing nawh,
mai deeh tawx nawh ceep ka kraung ceep tix,
khaix an viex tix tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue.
Tawk Ye-sux piang khaox lhak
(Lai Ma-kheu 15.21–32; Lai Lu-cia 23.26–43; Lai Yaw-hang 19.17–27)
32 Yam lih hu kix,
kix si pup ka pui Ku-li-naix tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh kaux Si-meung,
tom khin nawh klawm khaox lhak Ye-sux.
33 Kix hoek ka haktiex tix dauh,
ceu kaux kok mawh Keu-keu-tha,
dee bleeh mawh “Gawng Si-ang kaing”.
34 fKix teei plai pa klao mai ghin pa song tawx Ye-sux nyaux;
khaix hoik cim Ye-sux kah,
mai nawh ang lai cu tix nyaux heue.
35 gMawh kheu nan,
kix tawk Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak,
mai baox yuh krax pa cawk lai guah ka kraung ceep nawh,
36 khaix an ngawm dee an pau ka nawh.
37 Kix sau na bat phia tix khlawp pa tiiem ka phi map piang kaing nawh,
ka piang na bat phia an hoik tiiem ah “in mawh Ye-sux,
simiang pui Yo-thaix.”
38 Kix baox tawk pui caox ra kaux mai nawh ka piang khaox lhak,
tix kaux ot plak dawm nawh,
tix kaux ot plak goix nawh heue.
39 hPui pa hoek dee an map nai ka Ye-sux,
mai vuk vied kaing tix,
40 iah nin:
“Maix pa keud tix sang yieh ka nyiex buan in,
ka daux loe singaix deeh cox sum ka heue!
Phan maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex,
taoh ka to kaux maix,
mai lih khaing piang khaox lhak heue!”
41 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai kuad song khankix kawx hoik nyiah ka nawh nang,
mai ah nin:
42 “Nawh hoik taoh ka pui tang kaux,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon taoh ka to kaux tix heue!
Nawh ang mawh simiang Yi-seu-liex lie?
Yam in phan nawh pon lih khaing piang khaox lhak,
ex tag sang yum rhawm ka nawh heue!
43 jNawh hoik yum rhawm ka Siyiex mai tang kok to kaux tix mawh kawn simex Siyiex;
chix tix,
yam in ex sang jhak tix yaox hoek ang Siyiex hoek taoh ka nawh heue!”
44 Pie khawm pui caox pa tawk tau mai nawh kawx hoik map nai ka nawh heue.
Grawng yum Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 15.33–41; Lai Lu-cia 23.44–49; Lai Yaw-hang 19.28–30)
45 Yam hoek greung ngaix,
pa faik tawb kheem ka haktiex been loe taix nari.
46 kYam hoek pon ngaix loe taix nari,
Ye-sux kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Yi-lix!
Yi-lix!
La-ma-sha-pa-keu-ta-ni?”
Dee bleeh mawh:
“Siyiex aux,
Siyiex aux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tik vud ka aux?”
47 Pui pa cung ka praok nawh khankix,
tix blah hoik mhong kah,
mai ah nin:
“Nawh kok yo ka Yi-li-yax heue!”
48 lKa daux kix koe pui tix kaux to tix hu dum mawx dum an,
mai teei ka man luat tix plah cub ka daux plai niex an,
khaix an phrawk ot ka piang maox priem,
son sang kieh nawh nyaux ka heue.
49 Pui tang kaux ah nin:
“Krox ha,
ex sang jhak tix yaox hoek ang Yi-li-yax hoek tix taoh ka nawh heue!”
50 Ye-sux baox kok yo tom ting tix gum,
mai dut rhawm heue.
51 mKa daux bawg yam in,
man tie pa vhak ot ka daux nyiex buan,
jauh ka plak piang hoek ka plak greem hoik griah koe ra plak.
Haktiex yung yang,
rang simaox bih,
52 si meeig kawx hoik paoh tix,
kawn Khrit pa hoik yum tom nie hoik kaoh im.
53 Kix lih hu khaing si meeig,
mai laig ka daux veng khaix hoik kaoh im Ye-sux;
ka dee an koe pui tom nie hoik yaox ka kix heue.
54 Simiang naing mai pui naing pa pau ka Ye-sux hoik yaox grawng pa yung yang haktiex mai kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh,
kix kied lhat kah,
mai ah nin:
“Nawh mawh kawn simex Siyiex tete heue!”
55 nKa dee an kawn koe bun miex tix blah kok jhak kah,
kix mawh pa hawt khaix Ye-sux khaing Cia-li-lix,
son sang hoek tix khre ka nawh.
56 Ka daux kix mawh Ma-li-yax pa mawh Maw-ta-la,
Ya-keux mai miex Yaw-seux pa kaux Ma-li-yax,
mai miex kawn simex Si-pi-thaix ra kaux heue.
Si paung ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 15.42–47; Lai Lu-cia 23.50–56; Lai Yaw-hang 19.38–42)
57 Yam pon bo,
koe cao mi pa kaux Ya-li-ma-thaix hoek;
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-seux,
nawh kawx mawh pui gau Ye-sux nang.
58 Nawh hu ka Pi-la-to,
mhaing tix si paung ka to kaux yum Ye-sux;
Pi-la-to tawx lox nhie pui tawx to kaux Ye-sux ka nawh.
59 Yaw-seux teei tix viex ka to kaux Ye-sux hu,
teei man keei sum pa singax kie kah,
60 mai deuh nawh ot ka daux si meeig cie tix;
daux si meeig in mawh pa taug khraox saik nawh khaing rang simaox.
Nawh baox yung yang ka simaox pa ting hoek thox ka moing si meeig kah,
khaix an nawh lih hu heue.
61 Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la mai Ma-li-yax tang kaux ngawm veeh ngai tix plak si meeig,
mai pau ot dee an heue.
Pui naing pa pau si meeig
62 Ka daux singaix khaix,
pa mawh singaix nghoik tix khre,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai pui Fa-li-saix khankix hoek tau ka Pi-la-to ah nin:
63 o“Simiang,
yix hoik tawng pui pa pie an,
yam kawn im nawh nawh hoik sax krai ah nin:
‘Khaix hoik luan ka daux loe singaix,
aux tag sang kaoh im.’
64 Mawh kheu nan,
khiox khaw maix tawx lox nhie pui pau ka si meeig mhawm,
tom hoek ka daux loe singaix,
pui gau nawh tom tag ang pon hoek tix briex nawh hu,
khaix an hu krai ka pui ‘Nawh hoik kaoh im heue’,
lox pa pie yuh nin kawn sang nie lang teung khaing sivoe an heue!”
65 Pi-la-to ah nin ka kix:
“Pex viex pui naing pa pau hu,
mai sau riang tix pau ka si meeig mhawm mhawm heue!”
66 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom hu,
mai tawk cum ka piang simaox,
thox moing si meeig kaux,
mai deuh pui naing pau heue.
