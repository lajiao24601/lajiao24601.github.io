Me-li-ang yaox krax ciyang
12
Maw-si teei tix moeng kawn bun pui Ku-seua tix kaux;
Me-li-ang mai Ya-loeng ne raw ka Maw-si ka kaing in.
2 Kiex ah nin:
“Khex si kah Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si ngie lie?
Nawh kawx ang si kah mai yiex lie?”
Lox in Simiang Siyiex hoik mhong.
3 Maw-si mawh pa kied koe rhawm awn rhawm tiiem tix kaux;
nawh lheu koe rhawm tiiem khaing pui ka piang haktiex ku kaux.
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si,
Ya-loeng mai Me-li-ang dum mawx dum an:
“Aux sang kieh pex pui loe kaux hoek ka nyiex gox buan man aux tin.”
Kix tom hu heue.
5 Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka daux rong pai awm,
mai cung ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
kok ka kix:
“Ya-loeng!
Me-li-ang!”
Kiex ra kaux tom lih cung.
6 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex nghiet ka lox krai aux!
Yam koe cao jhai ka daux pex,
aux hoik jhi kix yaox tix daux riang su sai,
mai si kah ka kix ka daux ca maox.
7 cViang mawh nan,
ang awm yam si kah aux mai siliex tix Maw-si;
aux kieh nawh kuang ka piang pui cie aux Yi-seu-liexb khawm uik.
8 Kheu nan,
aux tom pak ngai tawx pak ngai si kah tau mai nawh,
sidawng laing laing,
ang siao lox pa mox.
Nawh kawx hoik yaox ka pak peung aux!
Pex kawn pon ne raw ka siliex aux Maw-si lie?”
9 Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka kiex,
kheu nan,
yam hoik lih hu nawh,
10 yam gah pai awm tix khaing piang nyiex gox buan man,
hak nex Me-li-ang lih koe saux si pawg dum mawx dum an,
mai paing awm rawm rha.
Ya-loeng yaox ka saux si pawg nawh,
11 ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Paw kieh yix yaox krax ciyang kheu phi map pa yuh tix khawm daox tix.
12 Paw kieh to kaux Me-li-ang yum laux awm yum kawn ngie daux vaig miex tix tix plak.”
13 Khaix an,
Maw-si khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Siyiex eue,
yuh buan son pai nawh moi liak.”
14 dSimiang Siyiex pawk lox:
“Phan keeing nawh baig rawm mia tix ka pak ngai nawh,
nawh tag pon kham krax kaig aliah singaix!
Grawh grawng nawh,
tag sang laung nawh plak praix nyiex bang aliah ngaix,
khaix an,
tag yaox tix viex nawh deeh hoek.”
15 Me-li-ang laung tix ot plak praix nyiex bang aliah ngaix;
kawn pui krox nawh hoek tom tag gah tix hu khaing bang.
16 Kix lih khaing Ha-si-lux,
hoek sum nyiex bang ka daux tiag Pa-lang heue.
