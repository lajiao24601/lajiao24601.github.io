Yaox ka yung mhaing mai pex mhaing
8
Yam mawh Paw-sa-sha simiang ka daux loe num,
aux yaox ka riang su sai ka daux ra ceu.a
2 Ka daux riang su sai an,
aux tawng grawng pa ot tix ka daux veng Su-sang ka haktiex Yi-lang dum mawx dum an.
Aux cung ka praok rawm klawng Wu-lai,
3 mai yaox ka yung mhaing tix mu,
pa kuah rung ra khawn,
cung ka dee an,
rung an kiex been lang been ngaing ang khaw paox tix,
rung pa blawn lang an mawh pa taug khraox kuah tix lih.
4 Aux yaox ka yung mhaing an teei rung tix teed hu plak klip singaix,
plai laong,
plak seh;
to tiag tix ceu kawx ang pon tieb tie kah,
mai ang pon luat luan khaing pa ting riang nawh.
Yung mhaing an simie tix yuh ka mawx tag yuh,
nied ot nied cau nyhau heue.
5 Yam kawn keud aux an mawh grawng dee bleeh ceu pa tix,
dum mawx dum an koe pex mhaing tix mu to tix hoek khaing plak klip singaix,
mai hoik brax tom ang caong tix lai cux tiex.
Ka si nax tawm ngai pex mhaing an rung pa hoik yaox ngai pui ka kuah ka tix khawn.
6 Nawh an preeng tix hu ka yung mhaing pa hoik yaox aux sivoe pa cung ka praok klawng.
7 Aux yaox nawh lauh ka yung mhaing an,
mai sau riang tix teed,
kieh rung yung mhaing an pot ra khawn.
Yung mhaing an ang lai koe riang tix tieb tie,
goh piang tiex,
kham pa dug dig,
ang koe pui pa pon taoh ka heue.
8 Pex mhaing an nied ot nied tix cau nyhau;
viang mawh nan,
yam hoik die siawp sibrawm nawh,
rung nawh pot heue.
Ka dee ot pa prim rung pa hoik kieh ngai pui yaox lih kuah ka pon khawn,
mai tang veeh tix krax pa ang awm paox.
9 Ka sigreung rung pon khawn in rung iak baox kuah ka tix khawn;
rung iak in veeh tix teei riang tix vieh va hu ka plak seh,
plak lih singaix,
mai ka haktiex pa mhawm.
10 bRiang rao nawh hoik kub tix yuh naing ka kru naing rao ma mai simuing,
nawh si blat tix tik ka kru naing rao ma mai simuing ka piang haktiex,
mai dug dig ka kix heue.
11 Viang mawh nan,
nawh pon tieb tie ka simiang kru naing rao ma,
mai tieb tie pui tan lu tix ka nawh ku ngaix,
khaix an yuh nyiex buan laux leeing.
12 Kawn pui yuh phi map dee an,
tan lu ka ceu pa ang khaw tix tan lu ku ngaix,
mai hoik tik vud ka ceu pa mawh pa meh piang tiex.
Kaing pa yuh rung iak in khawm uik hoik pon mhawm ku ceu heue.
13 Khaix an,
aux mhong lox pa chawk kawn cau Siyiex ka an tix kaux ah nin:
“Kaing in kix pa lih koe khaing daux riang su sai sang mawh been miex glang lie?
Phi map pa yum koeng an tang son krax tan pa khaw tix tang ku ngaix sang mawh miex glang lie?
Pa dug dig ka kru naing rao ma,
mai ka nyiex buan sang si dauh yam mawx lie?”
14 Aux mhong lox pawk kawn cau an ah nin:
“Kaing in tag sang mawh nin been tix rheng tix yieh tix phuan singaix.c
Ka daux baux bawg yam in,
ang lai koe pui pa tan lu krax tan pa tan pon bo mai pon ngawb.
Khaix hoik luan ka daux baux bawg yam in,
nyiex buan tag sang deeh awm singaix bawg yam pa hoik luan yuh nan heue.”
Kawn cau Siyiex Cia-pai-liex kah dee bleeh riang su sai
15 Aux yaox ka riang su sai in,
yam keud aux tix sang kieh tix tawng yung,
dum mawx dum an,
koe pa greeh grang pa awm pui cung ka sivoe ngai aux.
16 dAux mhong lox pa kok yo hoek khaing rawm klawng Wu-lai ah nin:
“Cia-pai-liex eue,
kah nawh mhong dee bleeh riang su sai pa yaox nawh.”
17 Cia-pai-liex hoek ka praok aux;
aux kied lhat,
tom gup tix piang tiex heue.
Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw yuh tix tawng yung ka riang su sai in.
Riang su sai in mawh pa dex mai dee si dauh been heue.”
18 Yam kawn si kah nawh,
aux gup tix piang tiex,
mai aux ang lai tawng tid tix.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh yaog aux kaoh,
19 mai ah nin ka aux:
“Aux tag sang jhi krax pa song rhawm Siyiex maix tag sang mawh pa yuh ka mawx;
riang su sai in mawh pa dex mai bawg yam pa si dauh heue.”
20 “Rung yung mhaing pa hoik yaox maix an ra khawn,
mawh son meung Mi-ti-yax mai meung Paw-see.
21 Pex mhaing an mawh son meung Si-lax,
rung pa hoik kieh ngai pui yaox ka an si nax ngai nawh,
mawh simiang tix kaux.
22 Phan mawh rung pa kieh rung pa kuah khaix pon khawn an pot,
mawh son meung in pa tag sang guah tix lih koe pon kaux simiang meung,
viang mawh nan,
riang rao kix ang pex ka riang rao meung pa koe sivoe heue.
23 “Yam hoik naog krax pa caox pa caig meung in kix,
koe simiang pa caox pa caig,
pa pie lhien tix kaux tag sang lih hoek.
24 Siawp sibrawm nawh nied ot nied ting,
viang mawh nan,
ang mawh een rhawm ka riang rao to kaux tix.
Nawh tag sang yuh krax pa laux leeing pa ang pui sax yaox tix bawg kawx;
can pa yuh nawh tag sang pon lih tix mawh.
Nawh tag sang yuh laux leeing ka pui pa kied ting riang,
mai ka pui cie Siyiex.
25 Mawh kheu kied lhak khoik nawh,
krax keud pa pie lhien yuh nawh hoik pon tix lih mawh khawm uik.
Nawh ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai jhawm tix puing pui laux leeing tom nie yam ang pui bax rien khre tix.
Nawh kawx pon tieb tie ka cao simiang piang simiang,
viang mawh nan,
dee si dauh riang rao pui pa ting riang kawn sang yuh nawh laux leeing.
26 Riang su sai pa dex tau mai krax tan pon bo mai pon ngawb,
pa hoik kah maix mhong dee bleeh sidawng.
Riang su sai in tag sang lih tix mawh tete;
viang mawh nan,
yam in maix tag caw tix mox glip,
kheu hoik mawh pa krai son kaing pa sang lih tix mawh heue.”
27 Aux pa mawh Tang-yi-li kied ang nyawm rhawm,
mai aux hoik koe jhawm saux ra loe singaix.
Khaix an aux sang kaoh,
mai deeh hoek ka dee ot tix,
yuh ka kaing pa hoik tawx simiang aux yuh;
viang mawh nan,
riang su sai in hoik kieh aux suk sag,
mai ang tawng ka dee bleeh heue.
