See ang bax prawng dut ka pa hoik pauh pui yum
21
“Phan pex yaox pui tix kaux kaux,
pa hoik pauh pui yum,
ka daux haktiex pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex ka pex,
to kaux yum an hoik tik tix ka daux tiag daux tu,
mai ang tawng cao pa pauh mawh mawx.
2 Kuad song mai cao kaing prawng see pex tag sang hoek ka dee ot to kaux pui yum an tan,
mai tiag tix yaox,
jauh ka dee an tom hoek ka vung viang veng ku mu singai koe miex glang,
3 mai jhak mawh veng mawx pa blawn dex ka dee an.
Kuad song pa blawn dex ka veng an,
tag sang laug tix teei ka kawn moi siang tix mu pa ang nyang sax yuh kaing kah,
4 mai tuk nawh hoek ka daux ngawd dee koe rawm ang jud,
ka haktiex dee ang pui nyang sax sum sax rhuat simie kah,
mai lauh ngawg kawn moi siang an pot.
5 “Cao giex kaing li pui Li-we tag hawt hu nang,
mawh kheu kix tag sang mawh cao pa hawt tix prawng ka grawng see pa simah simaing,
mai grawng see pa mat pui yuh paox tix khankix.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
hoik laug kix yuh kaing jhaw tix,
hoik kieh kix nawb buan ka daux ceu kaux nawh.
6 Kuad song pa blawn dex ka veng an,
tag sang khoek taix tix ka piang kawn moi siang an,
7 khaix an,
mai rawg buan ah nin:
‘Yix ang bax pauh ka pui in,
mai ang tawng mawh pui mawx pa pauh ka nawh.
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa hoik taoh maix lih khaing Ai-cix.
Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map yix liak,
mai paw kieh yix klawm ka nham pui ang koe dee lud in liak.’
9 Pex priex tix hawt yuh ka pa rauh pa see Simiang Siyiex,
tom tag ang yaox tix klawm ka phi map pa pauh ka pui an heue.”
Been pa giex laung bun khaing daux naing
10 “Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
kieh pex pex ka daux pa puing tix naing,
mai pon giex laung pui ka daux naing.
11 Phan maix yaox bun pa mhawm grang ka daux si nax pui pa giex pa laung khankix,
mai muh ka nawh,
simie tix teei tix moeng.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag chix tix viex nawh ing ka nyiex tix.
Nawh tag sang khlao hauk kaing tixa mai kip nghim tix,
13 mai loh kraung tix,
mai ot ka nyiex maix yuh krax pa yiiem doe son miex keeing tix tix khix.
Khaix hoik luan daux glang bawg yam in,
maix tag chix tix teei tix moeng ka nawh.
14 Khaix an,
phan maix ang lai muh ka nawh,
maix tag caw tix ploi nawh hu ka dee pa sang hu nawh;
mawh kheu hoik rhim maix tix moeng ka nawh,
maix tag ang pon tix coh nawh awm mawh bun siliex khankix heue.”
Been plud kawn ai ka sin ngao
15 “Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa koe moeng ra kaux,
nawh muh ka tix kaux,
mai ang muh ka tix kaux.
Bun an kiex khawm ra kaux mai hoik keh kawn son nawh,
viang mawh nan,
kawn ai mawh moeng pa ang nawh muh pa keh;
16 yam hoek singaix guah nawh ka sin ngao,
nawh tag ang pon tix blawn muh ka kawn simex pa keh moeng pa muh tix an,
mai tawx akhuang kawn ai ka nawh.
17 Kawn ai die mawh moeng pa ang nawh muh pa keh,
nawh tag caw tix rieb nawh mawh akhuang kawn ai,
yam guah ka sin ngao,
tag caw tix nie tawx ka nawh tix nyum.
Nawh mawh kawn ai,
mai hoik khaw tix pon ka akhuang kawn ai heue.”
Been prawng ka kawn praix pa lieh lox
18 “Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux,
pa koe kawn praix pa kiien rhawm cau nyhau,
viang hoik prawng ciyang miex bawg kawx,
nawh ca kawn ang nghiet lox miex lox keeing tix,
19 miex keeing nawh tag sang giex tix viex nawh hu ka sivoe kuad song khankix pa ot ka daux veng ot tix,
son sang prawng ca ka nawh.
20 Kix tag ah nin ka kuad song khankix:
‘Kawn simex yix in kied kiien rhawm cau nyhau,
ang nghiet ka lox yix,
nawh hoik nyaux plai brai meung ku ngaix.’
21 Pui pa ot ka daux veng khankix tag sang dheem nawh yum ka simaox.
Yuh nin,
pex tag pon teei tix tik ka kaing pa caox pa caig khaing daux si nax pex.
Pui Yi-seu-liex tag lhat graung ka mhong tix ka grawng an heue.”
Been tang ceu
22 “Phan koe pui pa hoik pauh pui yum kheu pa phit lud tix,
to kaux yum nawh preue tix ot ka piang rong khaox,
23 bto kaux yum in ang lai pon tix teuk teue ka piang rong khaox tom hoek singaix khaix,
tag caw tix si paung ka daux singaix an.
Mawh kheu phan to kaux yum an preue tix ot ka piang rong khaox,
tag cheeik Simiang Siyiex jhax lawg ka haktiex an.
Pex tag si paung ka to kaux yum an,
son ang sang seeing brai yuh haktiex pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pex heue.”
