Sibun mai si yawng
12
Yam an,
rao ma lih koe ka mhai pa ting pa raux pa mox tix ceu.
Koe sibun tix kaux to kaux nawh ceep singaix,
caong nawh dig ka khix,
ka piang kaing nawh ceep ka mhok khri simuing kao ra mu.
2 Sibun an dex sang keh,
saux sang keh kieh nawh kok yo heue.
3 aRao ma baox lih koe ka mhai tang ceu pa raux pa mox tix ceu.
Koe si yawng ting pa raoh tix mu,
kaing koe aliah mu,
rung kao mu,
kaing ku mu hoik ceep ka mhok khri simiang khawm uik.
4 bSi yawng ting an si vied si dax tix yaog simuing ka rao ma loe son tix son,
mai si blat ka piang tiex.
Si yawng an cung ka plak sivoe ngai sibun pa sang keh an,
krox jun kawn ngie keh,
mai tag sang bleet ka nawh.
5 cSibun an keh ka kawn simex tix kaux;
kawn nyawm in tag sang teei khaox gix kuang ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik.
Viang mawh nan,
yaog kawn nyawm in haok ngawm ka piang dang ngeung cie Siyiex.
6 Sibun an to hu mox tix plak daux tiag,
hoek ka dee hoik rien khre Siyiex son nawh;
nawh ot dee an,
tag sang pon krax pa kia ngien tix rheng ra siyiex tix gliah singaix heue.
7 dKhaix an,
rao ma lih koe naing.
Mi-cia-leux mai kawn ceu Siyiex nawh yuh naing ka si yawng,
si yawng mai kawn cau cie nawh kawx kaoh tix yuh naing.
8 Viang mawh nan si yawng ang pex;
nawh mai kawn cau cie nawh ang lai pon yaox tix ot ka rao ma.
9 eMawh kheu nan si yawng an si blat tix piang tiex;
nawh hoik mawh si uing prim an,
ceu kaux kok mawh phi caox,
phan ang mawh nan,
kok mawh Sa tan,
pa nyaw glaw kawn pui piang haktiex.
Si blat nawh ka piang tiex;
kawn cau nawh kawx hoik si blat ka piang tiex khawm uik nang heue.
10 fAux baox mhong lox pa ting rao ma ah nin:
“Yam in hoik mawh bawg yam krax taoh cie Siyiex heue!
Siyiex hoik jhi akhuang tix mai pa giex tix kaing.
Khrit pa hoik sum nawh kawx hoik jhi ka akhuang tix nang.
Pa deuh phi map ka kawn Khrit ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex ex leeing ngaix leeing sawn,
hoik si blat tix lih hoek khaing rao ma.
11 Kawn Khrit khankix hoik pon pex ka si yawng kheu nham kawn yung mai lox pa mawh pa meh pa krai pao kix.
Kix kawx hoik cu tix tawx ju tix yum.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
rao ma khawm uik mai pa koe ka daux,
pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm heue!
Viang mawh nan,
haktiex mai rawm sigrit sigra hoik laux leeing heue!
Phi caox hoik naog ka krax pa koeng tix sang lih ka pex dee an,
mawh kheu hoik tawng nawh bawg yam singaix cie tix ang lai rheeng heue.”
13 Si yawng an hoik tawng grawng pa si blat tix piang tiex,
nawh tom leue ka sibun pa keh ka kawn nyawm simex an.
14 gSibun an hoik pon ka preeik klang ting ra mu,
mai hoik pon pu hoek ka daux tiag,
hoek ka dee ot tix.
Ka dee an,
mai hoik pon krax pa kia ngien loe num plak,
ang kieh nawh yaox tix kham krax pa tuk tiak si uing.
15 Si uing an ot ka plak khaix sibun an,
si uing an plih rawm ting lih khaing daux jheeh tix,
tag sang chun sibun an hu.
16 Viang mawh nan,
haktiex teem coi ka sibun an,
mai ang jheeh tix bleet ka rawm pa plih si uing ting an lih khaing daux jheeh tix.
17 Si yawng an song rhawm ka sibun an,
mai hu sawg tix yuh naing ka kawn praix pa deuk cie nawh khankix,
pa mawh pa yuh naing ka pui pa hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Siyiex,
pa hoik yum rhawm tix hawt ka krax pa mawh pa meh pa hoik plak jhi Ye-sux.
18 Si yawng an cung ka pak mhaik rawm sigrit sigra heue. h
