Lai Rhax Pa Kied Mhawm
1
bLox rhax Saw-law-meunga,
mawh lox rhax pa nie blawn mhawm khaing lox rhax pa koe khawm uik heue.
Lox rhax ka daux tix ceu
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
2 Kieh maix teei siyieb jheeh tix ngheet aux;
Krax muh maix kawn lheu tie khaing plai.
3 To kaux maix koe taox pa nyawm bhawng kah;
ceu kaux maix pon phruah lih ka ceu pa baux pa bhawng,
kawn nyawm bun doh ang muh maix lie?
4 Kieh maix viex aux hu,
mai kieh ax viex paox tix to;
mawh simiang aux,
mai viex aux laig daux lawg it maix.
Ax gaux rhawm tau,
nyaux plai tom kub,
heem tix ka daux klawng rhawm muh.
Ang tawng kawn bun rub kied muh maix yuh nin heue!
5 Kawn bun Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
viang aux lung kawx,
hoik kied mhawm grangc,
awm nyiex gox bang Ci-tax yuh nan,
awm man tie ka daux nyiex haw Saw-law-meung heue.
6 Paw vi map nai aux kheu hak nex nham aux,
hoik mawh singaix pa rat aux lung.
Pux aik aux khankix hoik song rhawm ka aux,
nhie aux yuh kiang ka daux si peem plix siiet,
kieh aux ang lai koe bawg yam tix kia ka si peem cie tix.
7 Pui pa muh aux,
khiox khaw maix krai aux mhong,
liang maix yung dee mawx lie?
Bawg yam greung ngaix kieh maix kawn yung laux lee tix dee mawx?
Aux doh sang chix ot ka daux cub phung yung cie cao liang yung tang kaux
nong nong,
mai sawg ka maix lie?
[Simex pa sang koe moeng]
8 Nyawm bun pa kied mhawm grang eue,
khex ang maix tawng ka dee an lie?
Hawt khaix caong yung,
ka praok nyiex goh pui liang yung,
kieh pex cie maix sawm rib heue.
 
9 Pui pa muh aux eue,
maix hoik kieh kawn nyawm simex chix muh tix,
awm brung siang ka daux cub brung naing cie Pha-raw yuh nan.
10 Hauk kaing pa koe maix khawm ra plak kied sidaing mhawm grang,
awm soi siang pa vung vhien tix ka ngawg yuh nan.
11 Viang mawh nan,
ex sang yuh soi khri son maix,
kieh maix ceep ka kraung mau heue.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
12 Yam ngawm simiang aux tix sawm,
rawm sidah bhawng aux hoik phruah taox bhawng lih.
13 Pui pa muh aux hoik neuh tix ka naok aux jheeng jheeng;
nawh hoik bhawng awm Muran tix gie yuh nan.
14 Pui pa muh aux hoik awm pruh tai Feung-sing yuh nan,
pruh ka daux si peem plix siiet cie Yin Ci-ti heue.
[Simex pa sang koe moeng]
15 Pui pa muh aux,
maix kied sidaing mhawm grang tete!
Tawm ngai maix hoik awm tawm ngai si kaux yuh nan,
kieh pa riang krax muh lih jhan heue!
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
16 Paox muh dut aux,
maix kied sidaing mhawm grang,
mai kied nyawm tix jhak tete!
Ax kieh rib klai mawh ku it,
17 kieh khaox si oe bhawng mawh lat nyiex,
kieh khaox gix mawh phia pa tawm ot plak piang heue.
