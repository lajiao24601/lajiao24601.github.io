Poi ka paox ka daux veng Cia-nax
2
Ka daux loe singaix,
koe pui yuh ka paox tix ka daux veng Cia-nax haktiex Cia-li-lix.
Miex Ye-sux ot nang dee an;
2 pui hoik ghaok Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh ih poi ka paox.
3 Plai hoik uik,
miex Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Kix ang lai koe plai heue.”
4 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Miex,
paw vi khin aux yuh ceu pa tid tix,
bawg yam cie aux ang bax hoek heue.”
5 Viang mawh nan,
miex Ye-sux tawx lox ka siliex tix ah nin:
“Phan nawh nhie pex yuh ceu tix,
pex hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai nawh heue.”
6 Dee an koe ang simaox liah mu,
mawh pa siao pui Yo-thaix yam yuh ka been pa singax,
tix mu ang,
pon sau rawm naog ka been tix yieh kongsheng.
7 Ye-sux krai ka siliex an ah nin:
“Sau rawm naog ka daux ang.”
Kix tom kuh rawm laig daux ang,
tom hoek naog ka moing ang.
8 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Yam in chix tix pug lih,
tawx ka pui pa kuang poi.”
Kix tom tawx hu.
9 Pui pa kuang poi cim tix yaox ka rawm pa hoik tieh tix lih mawh plai an,
ang tawng plai in kaoh khaing dee mawx (mawh ngie siliex pa pug rawm pa tawng),
mawh kheu nan,
kok kaw pa yuh ka paox hoek,
10 ah nin ka nawh:
“Pui hoik khan ka plai pa nyawm sivoe,
krox jun nyo si nie hoik nyaux sak,
tom tag teei tix khan ka plai pa ang nyawm khaix,
maix lai tieh tix deuh plai pa blawn nyawm hoek tom yam in heue!”
11 In hoik mawh mhai pa daox pa am yam jauh Ye-sux tix yuh ka daux bawg tix,
pa yuh ka daux veng Cia-nax meung Cia-li-lix.
Kaing in hoik jhi lih ka min plak ngai nawh;
pui gau nawh hoik yum rhawm ka nawh khawm uik heue.
12 aKhaix hoik luan kaing in,
Ye-sux hu tau mai miex tix,
pux aik tix,
mai pui gau tix ka haktiex Cia-pai-nong,
kix hoik paung ot dee an ra loe singaix heue.
Ye-sux kieh nyiex buan singax singeh
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.12–13; Lai Ma-kheu 11.15–17; Lai Lu-cia 19.45–46)
13 bPoi Luat-luan cie pui Yo-thaix hoik dex sang hoek,
Ye-sux haok ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
14 Ka biang brawk nyiex buan,
nawh yaox pui pa coh moi,
yung,
si kaux,
mai pui pa ngawm tix loh ka mau luah.
15 Nawh tom teei maox yuh maox si vied ka tix nyaing,
mai grheu moi yung lih khaing ka daux nyiex buan,
mai klup ka pheen pa loh mau piang,
mau luah cawd ka piang tiex.
16 Nawh baox krai ka pui pa coh ka si kaux ah nin:
“Teei kraung khrai in kix hu khawm uik,
paw vi kieh nyiex buan keeing aux mawh dee yuh pui lai gee heue!”
17 cPui gau nawh doh keud ka gum lox phuk lai sigang khri pa ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
aux hoik koe krax gaux rhawm pa ting ka nyiex buan maix,
awm ngu pa rup yuh nan heue!”
18 Simiang pui Yo-thaix khankix tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Pon yuh maix yix yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am ceu pa tix,
son sang jhi grawng pa hoik koe maix akhuang tix yuh ka kaing in lie?”
19 dYe-sux ah nin:
“Pex yieh ka nyiex buan in,
ka daux loe singaix aux tag sang pon deeh cox sum ka heue.”
20 Kix ah nin:
“Nyiex buan in hoik sum tipon liah num tom taug hoik,
ka daux loe singaix maix pon deeh cox sum ka lie?”
21 Tete te lai,
lox pa hoik krai Ye-sux ka nyiex buan,
hoik mawh son to kaux nawh.
22 Khaix hoik kaoh im Ye-sux khaing krax yum,
pui gau nawh doh keud ka lox pa hoik sax krai nawh,
kix tom yum rhawm ka phuk lai sigang khri mai ka lox pa krai Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux hoik tawng ka rhawm keud kawn pui
23 Yam yuh Ye-sux Poi Luat-luan ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui hun yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik yuh nawh,
kix tom yum rhawm ka nawh.
24 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ang kieh to kaux tix yi ka kix,
mawh kheu hoik tawng yung nawh ka can pui pa koe khawm uik sidawng.
25 Nawh ang siao lai lo pui krai grawh grawng kaing pa dex dawt mai kawn pui ka tix,
mawh kheu nawh hoik tawng yung ka rhawm keud kawn pui heue.
