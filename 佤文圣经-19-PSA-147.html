Rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm
147
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
 
Rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex ex hoik mawh pa kied sidaing mhawm heue!
Yaog yaw ka nawh hoik mawh krax pa kied gaux rhawm,
kied khaw tete heue!
2 Simiang Siyiex cox sum ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
nawh khrawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa vuk vied vuk viang deeh hoek.
3 Nawh pai pui pa laux rhawm voi rhi moi,
kie khaix dee mat kix heue.
 
4 Nawh hoik tat ka been koe simuing miex mu;
nawh hoik sum ceu kaux kix ku mu.
5 Simiang ex pa ting pa lhaong,
mai pa ting siawp sibrawm;
ping nyia cuyi pa koe nawh ex ang pon tiag tix yaox.
6 Nawh yaog ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
yuh pui pa caox pa caig khankix goh ka piang tiex heue.
 
7 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
tag caw tix tauh ting jung lah rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex ex.
8 Nawh kieh pai awm tawb kheem ka piang rao ma;
nawh ploi rawm lhiex lih caux ka piang haktiex,
kieh rib riem kuah ka piang gawng lua.
9 Nawh tawx priex preem ka to tiag to tu,
eei kawn lak pa ah lox tix heue.
 
10 Nawh ang khaw rhawm ka brung pa mhawm grang;
nawh ang rhawm yaox ka pui naing pa ting riang ting rao.
11 Viang mawh nan,
nawh muh ka pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh khankix,
khaw rhawm ka pui pa yum rhawm ka krax muh nawh heue.
 
12 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
Shi-ang eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex maix.
13 Nawh hoik kieh siviex veng maix kaux cong;
nawh hoik tawx buan son ka kawn simex kawn bun maix.
14 Nawh hoik kia ngien rien khre haktiex maix pon krax pa nhim khawm pon daog;
nawh hoik teei nghox ca pa blawn mhawm eei maix ka heue.
 
15 Yam tawx nawh lox ka piang haktiex;
gum lox nawh lih hu dum mawx dum an.
16 Nawh ploi rha lih awm hauk yung yuh nan;
nawh rhuat rawm si tab awm phawng nyaox yuh nan.
17 Nawh ploi pre lih awm mu mhaik yuh nan;
bhaung kuat pa phlu nawh hoek,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tieb tie.
18 Yam tawx nawh lox,
rha hoik ceh khawm uik;
yam kieh nawh bhaung phlu,
mai rawm hoik hu heue.
 
19 Nawh teei tix tawx gum lox tix ka Ya-keux,
teei tix tawx gum lox pa sidiex gau tix,
mai lox tat jhawm prawng tix ka Yi-seu-liex.
20 Nawh ang sax yuh nin ka meung tang meung;
mawh kheu ang kix tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh heue.
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
