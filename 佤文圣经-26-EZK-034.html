Cao liang pui Yi-seu-liex
34
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai krai lox jhai ka kix.
Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka kix ah nin:
cao liang pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag sang hoik krax laux leeing heue!
Pex chix kia ngien ka to kaux tix khaing,
viang mawh nan ang liang ka phung yung.
3 Pex nyaux ka rawm teeh yung,
ceep ka kraung hauk yung,
pauh ka yung pa blawn mhawm kluing,
ih ka nex yung,
viang mawh nan,
ang sax liang ka phung yung.
4 Yung pa iak riang,
pex ang een kluing;
pa koe jhawm saux,
pex ang pai moi;
pa mat,
pex ang kie;
pa lud krax,
pex ang thung deeh hoek;
pa grai,
pex ang hu sawg.
Pex tieh tix yuh kaing pa ang khaw caw ka kix.
5 aMawh kheu ang koe cao liang,
phung yung khankix hoik prung prang tix hu;
to sat hoek tix kiied kix phawng,
mai bleet ka kix;
6 mai kieh phung yung cie aux prung prang piang gawng daux ngawd,
prung prang tix hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
ang koe pui pa kia ngien kah,
mai ang koe nang pui pa hu sawg heue.
7 “Mawh kheu nan,
cao liang eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux pa mawh gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka pex.
8 Aux tiex tu ka ju tix leeing lu,
pex tag caw tix nghiet mhawm mhawm.
To sat hoik leue tix bleet phung yung cie aux kheu ang koe cao liang.
Cao liang cie aux ang hu sawg ka kix;
kix kia ngien ngie ka to kaux tix khaing,
mai ang kia ngien ka phung yung.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
cao liang eue,
nghiet ka lox krai aux heue!
10 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang tieb tie ka pex.
Aux tag sang deeh teei ka phung yung tix.
Ang kieh pex lai baox mawh cao liang kix,
mai ang kieh nang pex kia ngien ngie ka to kaux tix khaing.
Aux tag sang taoh phung yung cie tix khaing daux jheeh pex,
mai ang kieh pex ih ka nex kix heue.”
Cao liang pa mhawm
11-12 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka pex,
to kaux aux tag sang hoek tix sawg ka phung yung.
Cao liang yung sang sawg yung pa hoik grai yuh ka mawx,
aux kawx tag sang hawt tix sawg yuh nan nang.
Kix hoik prung prang tix hu ku dauh ku dee ka daux pa faik dee ang pa riang koe,
dee haoh krax laux krax leeing,
aux tag sang taoh kix lih khaing daux haktiex an.
13 Aux tag sang thung kix lih khaing meung tang meung,
mai khrawm kix ot tau,
viex deeh ka haktiex tix.
Aux tag sang thung kix deeh ing ka si nax gawng Yi-seu-liex mai ka dee koe rawm klawng iak,
mai eei kix ka bang dee koe rib klai.
14 Aux tag sang liang kix ka pak rib gawng si nax Yi-seu-liex,
mai kieh kix sawm rib mhawm mhawm ka pak rib klai mai si nax ngawd.
15 Aux tag sang hoek tix mawh cao pa liang ka phung yung cie tix,
mai sawg ka dee sang laux lee kix tix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
16 “Yung pa hoik grai,
aux tag sang sawg hoek;
pa lud krax,
aux tag sang thung deeh hoek;
pa mat,
aux tag sang kie;
pa koe jhawm saux,
aux tag sang pai moi.
Viang mawh nan,
pa kluing khankix,
aux tag sang yuh laux leeing,
mawh kheu hoik hawt aux tix liang phung yung ka daux krax pa khaw caw heue.
17 “Phan mawh phung yung cie aux khankix eue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka pex,
aux tag sang guah ka yung mai pex,
pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm tang ot,
mai guah tix tang prawng ca ka pex.
18 Ka si nax pex koe tix blah sawm ka rib pa mhawm mai kawn ang nyang bax vhah rhawm,
mai tieh tix dug dig rib khaix sawm tix heue!
Pex hoik nyaux ka rawm pa si ngaong,
mai tieh tix teei caong tix chu chiah ka rawm khaix nyaux tix si ku.
19 Yung pa kawn deuk aux khankix sawm ngie rib pa hoik dug dig pex khankix,
mai nyaux ka rawm pa chu chiah pex si ku heue.
20 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka pex yuh nin:
aux tag sang yuh kaing pa khaw caw ka si nax yung pa kluing mai yung pa krawx pex in kix.
21 Pex teei klip tix suk shiet paox tix,
teei rung tix klauh ka pa iak riang,
mai grheu kix prung prang ka haktiex ku dauh.
22 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang taoh ka yung cie tix,
mai ang kieh kix lai baox yaox ka krax pa tuk tiak tuk te.
Aux tag sang guah pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm tang ot,
mai guah tix prawng ca ka yung cie tix.
23 bAux tag sang tawx simiang tix kaux pa awm siliex aux Ta-wue yuh nan,
mai kieh nawh mawh cao liang kix,
eei ka kix.
24 cAux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang mawh Siyiex kix,
awm siliex aux simiang Ta-wue yuh nan,
tag sang giex kaing piang kix.
Aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
25 Aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
kieh kix pon krax pa nyawm ot.
Aux tag sang teei tix tik ka to sat pa kied kua ka daux haktiex an,
mai kieh yung cie tix pon yaox tix nyawm ot,
pon yaox tix sawm rib ka pak rib,
laux lee tix ka daux nong khaox heue.
26 “Aux tag sang tawx buan son ka kix,
mai kieh kix paung ot ka hawt vung viang gawng pa singax aux.
Aux tag sang tawx buan ka kix dee an,
mai hawt tix tawx rawm lhiex lih awm bawg yam.
27 Khaox plix tag sang plix plix,
kaung ma tag sang tawx to ma to koi;
pui ku kaux tag sang paung ot ka haktiex cie tix.
Yam tat aux maox ceng dut khaing pui cie tix,
yam ploi kix lih khaing daux tawm taix pui pa kieh kix yuh siliex cawng,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
28 Meung ka piang haktiex ang lai baox tud rhim ka kix;
to sat ang lai baox bleet ka kix.
Kix tag sang pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
ang lai yaox krax pa ghlat pui.
29 Aux tag sang tawx haktiex pa mhawm ka kix;
ka daux haktiex kix ang lai baox koe sibhawm praix.
Meung ka piang haktiex ang lai baox map nai ka kix.
30 Pui ku kaux tag sang tawng grawng aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tau mai kix;
kix mawh pui cie aux.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
31 “Pex mawh yung cie aux,
mawh phung yung cie aux ka pak rib.
Pex mawh pui cie aux,
aux mawh Siyiex pex.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
