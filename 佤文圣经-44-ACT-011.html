Pi-teux krai lox ka ceu cao Yie-lu-sa-leung
11
Kawn cau mai kawn Khrit khankix pa ot haktiex Yo-thaix khawm uik,
hoik mhong grawng pa hoik rieb pui tang ceu tang cad tix blah ka gum lox Siyiex.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
Yam hoek Pi-teux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui pa nhie pui tang ceu tang cad tag caw tix rieb ka been pa Pat-hak ne raw ka nawh ah nin:
3 “Yuh ka mawx maix lai paung ot ka nyiex ma pui tang ceu tang cad pa ang bax Pat-hak,
khaix an kawn sawm tau mai kix heue!”
4 Pi-teux tom krai kix mhong grawng kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh khawm uik sidawng ka kix heue.
5 Nawh ah nin:
“Yam rawg buan ka daux veng Yaw-pha,
aux hoik pon riang su sai tix ceu.
Aux yaox kraung tix ceu pa lih hoek khaing rao ma,
pa awm man vieh tix plah,
man in khawm pon daog hoik phrawk tix kaux,
mai cawd ka praok aux;
6 aux bre tix jhak kah,
ka daux koe to sat sim saik,
mai baox koe viag via.
7 Khaix an,
aux mhong koe lox pa ah nin ka aux:
‘Pi-teux,
kaoh,
pauh tix ih ka heue!’
8 Aux ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
ang doh chix!
Ceu pa seeing pa brai pa ang singax tix ceu kawx,
aux ang sax ih ka heue.’
9 Lox pa lih khaing rao ma baox ah nin:
‘Ceu pa hoik een Siyiex mawh pa singax,
maix ang pon tix kieh mawh pa seeing pa brai.’
10 Yuh nin,
ka daux loe bawg.
Dee si dauh,
kraung ceu an kix tom deeh yaog tix haok ka rao ma heue.”
11 “Ka daux bawg yam an,
pui loe kaux pa ploi Khai-sha-li-yax hoek tix sawg aux,
kix hoek ka diang siviex nyiex paung aux an.
12 Khuan Singax Singeh jhi aux hu tau mai kix,
ang lai haoh rhawm keud.
Kawn Khrit liah kaux pa kaoh khaing Yaw-pha kawx,
hu tau mai aux.
Yix hoek ka nyiex ma Keu-ni-lio khawm uik.
13 Keu-ni-lio krai ka yix,
mawh hoik yaox nawh yuh ka mawx ka kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux cung ka daux nyiex nawh,
pa krai ah nin:
‘Maix tag caw tix ploi pui hu ka Yaw-pha,
kok pui pa kaux Si-meung Pi-teux hoek.
14 Nawh koe lox pa sang krai ka maix,
maix mai pui nyiex ma maix hoik pon krax taoh.’
15 Yam jauh aux tix sang krai lox,
Khuan Singax Singeh lih hoek ka piang to kaux kix,
awm hoik lih hoek piang ex singaix yam jauh yuh nan.
16 aMawh kheu nan,
aux doh keud ka lox pa hoik sax krai Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
‘Yaw-hang cub com pui ka rawm,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang rieb tix com tix ka daux Khuan Singax Singeh heue.’
17 In hoik mawh kaing pa sidawng,
Siyiex hoik teei tix tawx khawng phak buan son in ka pui tang ceu tang cad nang,
awm yam yum rhawm ex ka Simiang Ye-sux Khrit yuh nan,
pa tawx nawh ex hoik awm paox.
Mawh aux pui mawx,
sang chix pon tieb tie ka kaing jhaw Siyiex lie?”
18 Yam mhong kix lox in,
ang lai baox ne raw,
mai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
“Siyiex kawx hoik tawx bawg yam pa kieh pon ju kheu pa loh rhawm rhi tix ka pui tang ceu tang cad nang heue!”
Ceu cao Ang-thi-a
19 bYam an,
kawn Khrit khankix hoik tang prung prang tix hu haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
kheu krax pa tuk tiak tuk te pa kieh lih ka grawng pa yum See-thi-fang,
koe tix blah hu dee singai Fe-ni-ci,
Sai-phu-lu-see,
mai Ang-thi-a;
kix sidiex ngie gum lox in hu ka pui Yo-thaix.
20 Viang mawh nan,
koe kawn Khrit Sai-phu-lu-see,
mai Ku-li-naix khankix tix blah,
hu ka Ang-thi-a;
kix kawx krai gum lox buan grawng pa dex dawt mai Simiang Ye-sux ka pui Si-lax.
21 Siawp sibrawm Simiang Ye-sux ot tau mai kix;
koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux heue.
22 Gum lox in hoik tauh hu ka ceu cao Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kix tom ploi Pa-na-pa hu ka Ang-thi-a.
23 Yam hoek Pa-na-pa,
nawh yaox tawx Siyiex buan ka pui plak an,
mai kied gaux rhawm;
nawh sidiex ka kix,
nhie kix yum rhawm ka Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
24 Pa-na-pa mawh pui pa mhawm tix kaux,
mai hoik naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
krax yum rhawm nawh hoik kaux cong.
Mawh kheu nan,
koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux heue.
25 Khaix an,
Pa-na-pa hoek sawg Sao-law ka Ta-sux,
26 mai hoik yaox ka nawh,
tom viex nawh hoek ka Ang-thi-a.
Koe ka daux tix num,
kiex ra kaux yuh paox pie tau mai ceu cao,
sidiex gau ka pui tom nie.
Pui gau kok mawh “pui cie Khrit” in mawh pa jauh tix sum ka daux Ang-thi-a.
27 Yam an,
koe cao jhai ra loe kaux kaoh khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
lih hoek ka Ang-thi-a.
28 cKa daux kix koe pui tix kaux kok mawh Ya-cia-pu;
nawh hoik pon pa duk thung Khuan Singax Singeh,
cung tix krai lox jhai grawng sang lih koe sibhawm praix pa kin ka piang haktiex.
(Kaing in rug lih tix mawh ka daux yam giex Kheu-lao-tix simiang.)
29 Mawh kheu nan,
pui gau but rhawm tix hawt awm pa koe riang rao tix,
teem coi kawn Khrit ka mau khankix pa ot ka haktiex Yo-thaix.
30 Kix tom yuh kaing in,
mai tawx Pa-na-pa mai Sao-law viex mau in hu tawx ka kuad song ceu cao khankix heue.
