Ya-pi-yax mai Yie-law-paw-ang puing paox tix
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 15.1–8)
13
Yam giex simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang kaing ka daux kao daix num,
Ya-pi-yax yaog tix mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
2 Nawh giex kaing loe num ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Miex nawh kaux Ma-cia,
mawh pui veng Ci-pi-yax kawn bun Wu-liex heue.
Si nax Ya-pi-yax mai Yie-law-paw-ang lih koe naing jheeng jheeng;
3 Ya-pi-yax thung pui naing tipon mheen kaux puing ka pui naing cie Yie-law-paw-ang tix daix mheen kaux heue.
4 Pui naing in kiex kleuk paox tix ka gawng Yi-fa-liien.
Simiang Ya-pi-yax haok ka gawng Si-ma-liien,
kok yo Yie-law-paw-ang mai pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh ah nin:
“Pex nghiet!
5 Khex ang pex tawng Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh sijax mukheu pa ang pon tix yieh laux lie?
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx Ta-wue mai doeh ceu cad nawh mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju.
6 Kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang tieh vheen ka simiang tix pa mawh Saw-law-meung,
7 khaix an,
nawh khrawm ka cub pui pa cau nyhau pa vu vie khankix,
mai sau riang tix tieb tie ka kawn simex Saw-law-meung pa kaux Law-paw-ang;
yam an Law-paw-ang kawn nhum ju,
ang bax kub rhawm keud,
mai ang tawng grawng sang tieb tie ka kix.
8 “Yam in pex keud tix sang puing ka siawp sibrawm simiang pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka doeh ceu cad Ta-wue;
pex hoik koe pui naing pa ting riang ting rao,
mai baox koe riang kawn moi pa hoik raug yuh Yie-law-paw-ang tix kieh mawh Siyiex tang kaux tang hak pex.
9 Pex hoik grheu doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li Simiang Siyiex hu,
mai pui Li-we kawx pex hoik grheu hu;
pex hawt ka been tang meung mai tawx cao giex kaing li tang kaux yuh kaing tang kix.
Pui mawx ang ah phan tuk moi mhaing tix mu,
phan ang mawh nan yung aliah mu hoek,
pex tag kieh nawh mawh cao giex kaing li pa krai pex mawh siyiex tix heue.
10 “Viang mawh nan,
yix ca kawn yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix,
mai ang tik vud ka nawh.
Doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng yuh kaing pa mawh cao giex kaing li;
pui Li-we teem coi ka kix.
11 Pon ngawb mai pon bo ku singaix kix gleeing nyawng bhawng ka nawh,
tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai baox tan lu khao mun pa singax singeh ot piang pheen.
Pon bo ku singaix,
kix tok jawk kawk ngu khri riang.
Yix priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix,
viang mawh nan,
pex tik vud ka nawh.
12 Siyiex ot tau mai yix,
nawh mawh cao pa duk thung yix.
Cao giex kaing li nawh teei ka teet tawng,
sang phlu teet tawng kok yix yuh naing ka pex.
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
paw vi yuh naing ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex!
Pex ang doh chix pex heue!”
13 Bawg yam in,
Yie-law-paw-ang ploi pui naing tix blah mox tix ot khaix pui naing Yo-tax,
pui pa deuk khankix preeng tix puing hu plak sivoe ngai.
14 Pui Yo-tax jhak khawm pon daog,
mai yaox hoik lai lawm pui tix,
kix tom khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex taoh tix;
cao giex kaing li khankix phlu teet tawng.
15 Pui Yo-tax kok yo lox si num,
Ya-pi-yax thung kix puing.
Siyiex puing ka pui naing Yie-law-paw-ang mai pui naing Yi-seu-liex.
16 Pui Yi-seu-liex to mox tix ka pui Yo-tax;
Siyiex kieh pui Yo-tax pex ka kix.
17 Ya-pi-yax mai pui naing cie nawh pex ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai hoik pon yuh laux ka pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa kied kua khankix tix phuan mheen kaux.
18 Pui Yo-tax hoik puing pex,
mawh kheu hoik een kix rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix heue.
19 Ya-pi-yax leue ka pui naing cie Yie-law-paw-ang,
mai pon ka veng cie nawh ra loe veng,
pa mawh Paw-theu-lix,
Yie-sa-na,
Yi-fu-loeng,
mai nyiex yaong pa ot sivie siveng veng in kix.
20 Glang yam kawn giex Ya-pi-yax cao kaing,
Yie-law-paw-ang ang lai yaox tix baox koe sibrawm tix bawg kawx.
Dee si dauh Simiang Siyiex lauh ka nawh,
nawh tom yum heue.
21 Ya-pi-yax ting riang hu si nyek si nyek;
nawh koe moeng kao pon kaux,
kawn simex tix nga ra kaux,
kawn bun kao liah kaux.
22 Kaing yuh Ya-pi-yax pa deuk khankix,
mai lox ah si kah krai nawh hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Cao Jhai Yi-to heue.
