Maw-ya hoik deuk rhawm deuk rhi
16
Pui si pup krax laux haktiex Maw-ya teei kawn yung tix mu khaing veng Si-la pa ot daux tiag,
mai tawx ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa koe sibrawm,
son sang kieh mawh khawng phak lak sawng.
2 Pui si pup krax laux in kix ot tix krox jun ka praok rawm klawng Ya-noeng,
kix hoik nieb pon hu pon ing tix ang koe ceu koe cak,
hoik awm kawn sim pa grheu pui lih khaing daux mheem tix yuh nan heue.
3 Kix chawk ka pui Yo-tax ah nin:
“Krai yix mhong sang yuh yix ka mawx lie?
Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka yix,
awm veei khaox ting pa tawb ka yix yam hax koek pa si aoh,
kieh yix yaox tix laux lee greem veei khaox in.
Yix mawh pui pa si pup krax laux,
khiox khaw maix mox ka yix liak,
mai paw vi kieh pui yaox ka yix heue.
4 Kieh yix pa mawh pui pa ang koe nyiex koe ma in kix yaox tix paung ot ka daux haktiex pex liak.
Khiox khaw pex kia ngien ka yix,
kieh yix pon yaox tix luat luan khaing tawm taix pui pa caox pa caig liak heue.”
(Krax gut krax kheem mai krax laux krax leeing tag sang kieh naox tix tete;
pui pa tud pa rhim tag sang kieh laux leeing tete.
5 Khaix an koe kawm praix Ta-wue tix kaux tag sang mawh simiang;
nawh tag sang giex kaing ka piang kawn pui ka daux krax muh pa kaux sijax pa ang chix tieh loh tix.
Nawh tag sang sawg ka daux krax pa khaw caw,
mai yuh kaing pa rauh pa see heue.)
6 Pui Yo-tax ah nin:
“Ex hoik mhong grawng pa kied ting rhawm lhaong rhi pui Maw-ya tete.
Kix ting rhawm cau nyhau,
viang mawh nan,
lox ting mu pa ah kix hoik mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik heue.”
7 Pui Maw-ya tag sang yiiem son pa yaox kix krax laux leeing.
Yam doh keud kix ka ih priex nyawm rawm lhong ka daux veng Cie-eu Ha-lie-seux,
kix hoik yiiem ku kaux,
mai hoik laux rhawm deek rhawm khawm uik heue.
8 Yaong Si-seu-peung mai si peem plix siiet Si-pi-ma hoik laux leeing,
pa mawh plai pa hoik yuh lih khaing si peem plix siiet an,
hoik kieh simiang ka piang meung ku meung goh tix yoek heue.
Koe ka daux tix bawg yam,
nhu maox plix siiet kix tawx tix tom hoek ka Ya-shiex,
tom hoek ka daux tiag plak lih singaix,
tom hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog plak klip singaix.
9 Yam in aux yiiem son khaox plix siiet Si-pi-ma,
awm hoik yiiem son pui Ya-shiex yuh nan.
Rawm ngai aux hoik roik awm lih lhiex son Si-seu-peung mai son Yi-li-ya-lix,
mawh kheu dee an ang lai baox chix lih to ma kieh kawn pui koe krax gaux rhawm.
10 Ang lai baox koe pui pa gaux rhawm son haktiex kaung ma pa mhawm tix;
ang lai baox koe pui pa gaux rhawm rhax lai son si peem plix siiet tix;
ang lai baox koe pui pa dig ka plix siiet ka daux greeik plai.
Lox gaux rhawm hoik grai hu khawm uik.a
11 Mawh kheu nan,
rhawm rhi aux hoik laux son Maw-ya,
laux son Cie-eu Ha-lie-seux.
12 Pui Maw-ya nied tix haok khiox khaw ka nyiex vad kix pa ot piang gawng mai ka nyiex vad kix ku mu ang jud ang jai,
tom hoik uik riang rao tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon pa tid tix heue.
13 Plak piang mawh gum lox pa hoik krai pao Simiang Siyiex sivoe an pa koe grawh grawng mai Maw-ya.
14 Yam in Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Khaix in loe num,
sin ngao Maw-ya tag sang nyao grai khawm uik,
mawh ngie pui pa tux pa kawn deuk khaing;
kix kawx hoik mawh pui pa iak riang iak rao heue.”
