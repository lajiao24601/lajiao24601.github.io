Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Maw-ya puing paox tix
3
Yam giex Yaw-sa-fa simiang pui Yo-tax ka kaing ka daux kao daix num,
kawn simex Ya-ha pa kaux Yaw-heu-lang mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh giex kaing ka piang Sa-ma-li-yax kao ra num.
2 Nawh hoik yuh ka phi map,
yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang awm keeing tix mai miex tix Yie-si-pix yuh kaing pa ang mhawm yuh nan.
Nawh hoik yieh tix tik ka rong riang siyiex pa yuh keeing tix son Pa-lix.
3 Viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik awm kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang sivoe an yuh nan,
mai hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex krix ka daux phi map ang jud ang jai heue.
4 Mi-sa simiang Maw-ya eei ka yung tom nie;
nawh tawx keen ka hauk kawn pex kao mheen mu mai kawn yung mhaing kao mheen mu ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ku num.
5 Khaix hoik yum Ya-ha simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
Mi-sa tieh vheen ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
6 Simiang Yaw-heu-lang lih hu khaing Sa-ma-li-yax dum mawx dum an,
mai khrawm ka pui naing tix khawm uik,
7 ploi pui hu ah nin ka Yaw-sa-fa simiang pui Yo-tax:
“Simiang Maw-ya tieh vheen ka aux;
cu ang maix cu tix hu puing ka nawh tau mai aux lie?”
Simiang Yaw-sa-fa pawk ah nin:
“Aux cu tix hu.
Aux mai maix ang lai chix tang guah paox.
Pui naing cie aux hoik mawh pui naing cie maix,
brung naing cie aux hoik mawh brung naing cie maix.
8 Ex sang hu puing nawh ka krax yuh ka mawx lie?”
Simiang Yaw-heu-lang ah nin:
“Ex sang hu krax lang,
mai jauh tix kieh pui naing laig ka daux tiag Yi-tong heue.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Yaw-heu-lang hu tau mai simiang Yo-tax mai simiang Yi-tong.
Kix via ka krax,
hu aliah singaix,
rawm nyaux hoik uik,
pui naing mai khawng brie ang yaox tix nyaux rawm.
10 Simiang Yaw-heu-lang kok yo ah nin:
“Yum ex phaox,
Simiang Siyiex khrawm ex simiang loe kaux hoik mawh son sang ap ex ka daux tawm taix simiang Maw-ya heue!”
11 Simiang Yaw-sa-fa chawk ah nin:
“Dee in koe ang koe cao jhai pa khiox khaw son ex ka Simiang Siyiex lie?”
Siliex simiang Yaw-heu-lang tix kaux pawk ah nin:
“Dee in koe kawn simex Sa-fa pa kaux Yi-li-sa;
sivoe an nawh hoik mawh cao pa teem coi Yi-li-yax heue.”
12 Simiang Yaw-sa-fa ah nin:
“Gum lox Simiang Siyiex rien tix koe tau mai nawh heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang loe kaux hu tau hoek ka Yi-li-sa heue.
13 Yi-li-sa ah nin ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Aux sang chix teem ka pex yuh ka mawx lie?
Hu chawk ka keeing maix mai cao jhai miex maix khankix heue!”
Simiang Yaw-heu-lang ah nin:
“Paw lai ah nin liak,
in mawh Simiang Siyiex pa hoik tawx ex simiang loe kaux ka daux tawm taix simiang Maw-ya heue.”
14 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krub aux im ju leeing ju,
phan ang mawh kheu pa yung yi aux ka paox grawm maix,
simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa,
aux ang simi ka maix,
ang pawk lox maix.
15 Yam in,
hu sawg sara pa chix tauh ting tix kaux hoek ka aux heue.”
Yam tauh sara pa chix tauh ting phia jung lah,
siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang to kaux Yi-li-sa.
16 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Pex caw tix hawt hu kaong daux rawng ku dauh ka daux ngawd klawng iak in.
17 Viang pex ang yaox ka lhiex mai ang yaox nang bhaung,
ka daux ngawd klawng iak in tag sang naog ka rawm.
Pex mai khawng brie pex,
tag sang yaox tix nyaux rawm tom sak.’ ”
18 Yi-li-sa ca nied tix ah nin:
“Kaing in mawh kaing pa iak ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex;
nawh kawx tag sang kieh pex pon pex ka pui Maw-ya.
19 Pex tag sang pon teei ka tab kix pa kied kaux cong mai veng pa kied mhawm kix,
mai kid grup ka can khaox plix kix,
mai thox klat ka daux rawm nyaux kix khawm uik,
khaix an,
mai teei simaox yuh laux ka kaung ma kix khawm uik heue.”
20 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
bawg yam tan lu ka krax tan pon ngawb,
rawm rug lih hoek khaing Yi-tong mai naog ka piang tiex uik heue.
21 Pui Maw-ya khawm uik mhong hoek simiang loe kaux rom tix puing ka kix,
kix tom khrawm ka pui pa pon viex ka kraung naing khawm uik,
jauh ka pui kuad ju tom hoek ka pui awn ju mai kieh kix pau mee haktiex.
22 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
bawg yam kaoh kix khaing it,
riang singaix jhan ka piang rawm,
mai kieh rawm raoh awm nham yuh nan.
23 Kix kok yo ah nin:
“An mawh nham heue!
Rien tix mawh pui naing an kix loe cub pa puing ka paox tix!
Ex hu tud tix teei ka kraung khrai sin ngao kix heue!”
24 Yam hoek bleeh pui Maw-ya ka nyiex bang,
pui Yi-seu-liex puing ka kix,
mai grheu kix deeh.
Pui Yi-seu-liex ca leue tix pauh ka kix ang jud,
25 mai yieh veng kix laux.
Yam laig preeng pui Yi-seu-liex daux kaung ma pa mhawm tix dauh khankix,
kix tik simaox laig daux,
tom hoik grux simaox tix naog daux kaung khawm uik.
Kix kawx thox klat daux lih rawm,
kid grup ka khaox plix.
Dee si dauh,
mai deuk ngie veng ting daux tix mu ot simiang Cie-eu Ha-lie-seux,
pui naing pa nyiak ka simaox,
jauh tix lai lawm ka veng an heue.
26 Simiang Maw-ya jhak tix yaox ang lai mhawm,
nawh tom viex pui naing vaig aliah yieh kaux,
keud tix sang preeng tix lih khaing daux lai lawm pui kix,
mai to hu mox tix ka simiang Shui-li-yaxa dee an,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon preeng tix lih.
27 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom tawx kawn ai pa sang plud tix mawh simiang mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai tan lu nawh ka siyiex Maw-ya ka piang guh ding veng.
Pui Yi-seu-liex kied lhat graung kah,
kix tom deeh lih khaing daux veng,
mai deeh ing ka meung tix heue.
