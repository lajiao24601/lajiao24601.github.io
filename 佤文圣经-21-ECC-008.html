8
Mawh ngie pui pa lhak lhiao pa tawng deeh bleeh kaing.
Ping nyia cuyi kieh pak ngai nawh koe,
mai kieh krax pa khi rhawm nawh grai heue.
Nghiet ka lox simiang
2 Tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx simiang;
paw vi nieb pon ap pong tix tiex tu ka Siyiex.
3 Simiang chix tix teei alo tix yuh awm pa simie tix yuh,
dee mhawm maix hu singai khaing nawh;
paw vi ot ka dee sang laux leeing in.
4 Lox simiang koe siawp sibhawm,
ang koe pui pa pon tieb tie ka nawh.
5 Ca mawh priex maix tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh,
tag yaox tix nyawm ot.
Pui pa lhak lhiao hoik tawng ka bawg yam pa khaw tix yuh kaing mai grawng krax sang yuh tix.
6 Yuh kaing ceu pa tix ang ah hoik koe bawg yam pa khaw mai grawng krax pa sang yuh tix,
viang mawh nan,
pa tawng kawn pui koe ngie ceeix khaing.
7 Ang koe pui pa tawng kaing ceu pa sang lih tix mawh singaix pa sang hoek,
mai ang koe pui pa krai nawh mhong.
8 Ang koe pui pa ang chix yum,
mai ang koe nang pui pa pon tat tix deuh ka singaix yam yum tix.
In hoik mawh krax naing pa ang kawn pui pon luat luan khaing,
viang nawh kua miex ding kawx ang pon luat luan heue.
Pui caox pui caig mai pui rauh pui see
9 Yam lam lia aux tix yaox ka grawng kaing pa lih tix mawh ka piang haktiex,
aux hoik yaox ka khawm uik.
Pui tix blah koe siawp sibhawm,
mai tix blah kha rhawm rhi tix ka greem kix.
10 Aux hoik yaox ka si paung pui ka pui pa caox pa caig.
Yam kawn im ju kix,
mai kix hoik lai ling tix laig daux nyiex pa singax singeh;
aux mhong koe pui hoik yaog yaw kix ka dee yuh kix pa caox pa caig,
mai in hoik mawh pa giex pa going heue.
11 Yuh ka mawx pui piang haktiex ting rhawm tix yuh phi map yuh nin lie?
Mawh kheu ang phi map hoek krax pa ciyang dum mawx dum an.
12 Viang pui tix kaux kaux hoik yuh phi map kaing yieh bawg kawx,
mai nawh ca kawn yaox tix lang ju,
kheu nan,
aux hoik tawng,
pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex pon yaox tix mhawm kaing yuh ku ceu;
13 pui pa caox pa caig ang doh chix pon ka buan son.
Ju kix hoik awm ngie pang peue,
kix tag sang yum yam nhum ju tix;
mawh kheu ang kix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex heue.
14 Viang mawh nan,
ka piang haktiex kawn koe kaing ceu pa giex pa going tix ceu:
koe miex bawg mawx,
pui rauh pui see hoik pon krax ciyang pa khaw pui caox tix pon;
pui pa caox pa caig tieh tix pon ceu pa mhawm pa caw pui rauh pui see tix pon.
Aux krai,
in kawx hoik mawh nang pa giex pa going heue.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui khaw tix yuh krax gaux rhawm;
mawh kheu can krax gaux rhawm ka greem singaix,
ang lai chix lheu khaing pa ih,
pa nyaux,
mai pa yuh ka krax gaux rhawm.
In hoik mawh krax ih nyaux pa khaw kawn pui tix pon ceeix ceeix khaing daux pa taug mao tix ka daux ju pa kied dawt pa hoik tawx Siyiex heue.
16 Yam sawg aux ka ping nyia cuyi,
mai keud tix sang si kaw tix yaox ka kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh ka piang haktiex,
aux hoik tawng kawn pui viang sau riang tix keud leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
17 kawn ang chix tawng sidawng ka kaing pa yuh Siyiex khankix.
Viang nawh hoik cheu tix yuh yuh ka mawx kawx,
mai simie tix tawng ka kaing ceu pa lih tix mawh ka greem singaix,
mai ang chix yaox nang lox pa pawk.
Pui pa lhak lhiao ce tix tawng,
tete te lai,
nawh ang tie tawng heue.
