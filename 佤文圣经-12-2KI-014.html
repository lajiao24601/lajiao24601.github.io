Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 25.1–24)
14
Yam giex simiang pui Yi-seu-liex kawn simex Yaw-ha-see pa kaux Yaw-hua-see ka kaing daux ra num,
kawn simex Yaw-a-see pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
2 Yam koe ju nawh tix nga phuan num nawh mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix nga dim num.
Miex nawh Yaw-yie-tang mawh pui Yie-lu-sa-leung.
3 Ya-ma-shiex hoik yuh kaing mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang awm tax du tix simiang Ta-wue,
mai hawt ka kaing pa yuh keeing tix pa kaux Yaw-a-see.
4 Nawh ang bax yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak,
kawn pui ca kawn yuh krax tan lu gleeing nyawng bhawng dee an heue.
5 Ya-ma-shiex yuh kaux cong ka siawp sibrawm pa giex tix kaing,
nawh tom pauh ka siliex pa pauh keeing nawh khankix yum,
6 aviang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun kix.
Nawh hawt tix yuh ka gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka daux lai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si;
Maw-si ah nin:
“Miex keeing tag ang khaw tix yum tang phi map pa yuh kawn praix tix;
kawn praix kawx tag ang khaw tix yum tang phi map pa yuh miex keeing tix.
Kaux pui mawx kaux pui an tag sang tang klawm ka phi map pa yuh cao tix heue.”
7 Ya-ma-shiex pauh pui Yi-tong yum tix mheen ka daux ngawd kih plak seh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
baox puing ka veng Si-la,
mai loh ceu kaux mawh Yaw-thie,
tom hoek yam in,
ca kawn hawt ka ceu kaux in heue.
8 Ya-ma-shiex ploi kawn cau tix hu ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see,
tiex kix puing paox,
ah nin:
“Ax yaox paox tix ka daux bang naing!”
9 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-hua-see pawk lox nawh ah nin:
“Koe tix bawg,
pang kat pa koe piang gawng Li-pa-noeng tix gawng ah nin ka khaox si oe bhawng tix gawng:
‘Tawx kawn bun maix mex kawn simex aux liak!’
Koe to sat tix mu hoek tan,
mai dug dig pang kat an laux.
10 Ya-ma-shiex eue,
maix ting rhawm lhaong rhi kheu hoik pex tix ka pui Yi-tong;
maix khaw tix kieh rhawm rhi tix naog kheu in.
Maix ot laux tix ka nyiex mhawm mhawm khaing,
yuh pa tix lai teei si mu tix,
mai kieh to kaux tix mai pui cie tix hoek krax laux krax leeing lie?”
11 Ya-ma-shiex ang nghiet.
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see,
rieb tix puing simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex ka veng Paw See-mai haktiex pui Yo-tax.
12 Pui naing Yo-tax ang pex,
can pui naing khawm uik tang yaog tix to ing ka nyiex.
13 Yaw-hua-see pon giex ka Ya-ma-shiex,
mai viex pui naing laig ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai baox yieh ka guh veng,
jauh ka Yi-fa-liien-meung tom hoek ka Rung Siviex,
been lang koe ra yieh kongjhee.
14 Yaw-hua-see baox teei ka khri,
mau,
kraung pa siao ka daux nyiex buan,
khawm sin ngao nyiex haw simiang,
viex hoek ka Sa-ma-li-yax khawm uik,
khawm pui pa pon giex kix kawx hoik viex hu tix blah heue.
15 Kaing yuh Yaw-hua-see tang ceu khankix,
khawm grawng pa yuh nawh naing ka simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
16 Yaw-hua-see hoik yum,
kix si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang,
ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex yum
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 25.25–28)
17 Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex im ju kao phuan num khaix hoik yum simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see.
18 Kaing yuh Ya-ma-shiex tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax heue.
19 Ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung koe pui sang briex mox tix pauh Ya-ma-shiex,
mawh kheu nan nawh tom to hu mox tix ka veng La-ciex,
viang mawh nan,
kix hawt nawh hoek ka dee an,
mai pauh ka nawh heue.
20 Koe pui tang to kaux yum nawh ka brung hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang ka veng Ta-wue.
21 Can pui Yo-tax khawm uik,
hoik yaog kawn simex nawh pa kaux Wu-si-ya pa koe kao liah num plud tix mawh simiang khaix keeing nawh.
22 Khaix hoik yum Ya-ma-shiex,
Wu-si-ya deeh teei ka veng Yi-la-tha hoek,
mai deeh cox sum kaux cong heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra bawg yam
23 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax kawn simex Yaw-a-see,
pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex ka kaing daux kao phuan num,
kawn simex Yaw-hua-see pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hoik giex kaing piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax tipon tix num.
24 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt kaing pa ang mhawm pa hoik yuh tax du tix,
kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang yuh nan,
thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map.
25 bNawh deeh teei ka can haktiex pa caw tix mawh cie pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
jauh ka diang moing gawng Ha-ma tom hoek ka plak caong ma seh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog.
In hoik mawh lox jhai pa hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex kawn cau tix pui Cia-theux Si-fu kawn simex Ya-mi-thai pa mawh cao jhai Yaw-na grox krai heue.
26 Simiang Siyiex hoik yaox ka can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mawh pui pa yaox tix luat luan ang ah,
pui pa ang yaox tix luat luan ang ah,
hoik si pup ka krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi pa ting khawm uik,
mai ang koe pui pa teem coi ka kix.
27 Simiang Siyiex ang rhawm kieh pui Yi-seu-liex grai grup leeing ju,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh kieh Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux lih taoh kix heue.
28 Kaing yuh Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux tang ceu khankix,
grawng pa kua nawh ka daux naing mai pa deeh teei nawh ka veng Ta-ma-see-keux mai veng Ha-ma,
deeh cox kieh mawh haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
29 Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux hoik yum,
kix si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Sa-cia-li-ya plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
