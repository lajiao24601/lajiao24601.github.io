Khiox khaw ka mhai pa daox pa am
(Lai Ma-kheu 8.11–13; Lai Lu-cia 12.54–56)
16
aKoe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah mai pui Sa-tu-kai ra loe kaux hoek ka Ye-sux,
keud tix sang yuh nawh laux,
mawh kheu nan,
khiox khaw nawh yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
son sang jhi grawng kaing pa yuh nawh mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex.
2 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yam pon bo pex ah nin:
‘Pa sax praix klawng tag sang koek,
mawh kheu koe mhai raoh riang ka daog caong ma heue.’
3 Yam pon ngawb pex ah nin:
‘Daux in ngaix koe bhaung lhiex,
mawh kheu raoh faik rao ma heue.’
Pex hoik kied chix jhak ka praix klawng,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix tawng ka mhai baux singaix ju in! b
4 cPui singaix baux ju in lai kied caox caig,
phit vao yuh nin heue!
Pex khiox khaw ka mhai pa daox pa am lie?
Lheu khaing mhai pa daox pa am Yaw-na,
mai pex ang lai baox yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am tang ceu heue.”
Khaix hoik uik krai Ye-sux lox,
nawh tom lih hu khaing kix heue.
Su saix cie pui Fa-li-saix mai pui Sa-tu-kai
(Lai Ma-kheu 8.14–21)
5 Pui gau tiang hoek ka toi nhawng,
mai piawm tix viex ka khao mun.
6 dYe-sux krai ka kix ah nin:
“Pex tag caw rien rhawm tix,
tag caw rien tix ka su saix cie pui Fa-li-saix mai pui Sa-tu-kai heue.”
7 Pui gau ra lai krai lao ah nin:
“Nawh krai lox in mawh kheu ang ex viex ka khao mun heue.”
8 Ye-sux hoik tawng lox pa krai kix ceu pa tix,
nawh tom chawk kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex krai lao grawng ang tix bax viex ka khao mun lie?
Krax yum rhawm pex kied iak heue!
9 eYuh ka mawx pex lai kawn ang bax tawng tom hoek yam in heue!
Khex hoik piawm pex ka grawng pa teei aux khao mun phuan lon,
mai kieh pui phuan rheng kaux sak ka lie?
Yam an pa max phawng pa deuk hoik koe miex khrawng lie?
10 fKawn koe pui pon rheng kaux ih ka khao mun aliah lon an sak kah,
mai pex baox khrawm pa max phawng hoik koe miex khrawng lie?
11 Yuh ka mawx kawn ang bax tawng grawng pa krai lao aux khao mun ka pex?
Pex tag caw rien tix ka su saix cie pui Fa-li-saix mai cie pui Sa-tu-kai heue!”
12 Pui gau taug tawng Ye-sux ang mawh kieh kix rien tix ka su saix pa koe ka daux khao mun an,
mawh kieh kix rien tix ka lox sidiex pui Fa-li-saix mai pui Sa-tu-kai heue.
Pi-teux doh keud ka Ye-sux mawh Khrit
(Lai Ma-kheu 8.27–30; Lai Lu-cia 9.18–21)
13 Ye-sux hoek ka daux haktiex Khai-sha-li-yax Fe-li-pi;
ka dee an,
nawh chawk pui gau tix ah nin:
“Pui ah mawh kawn kawn pui mawx lie?”
14 gKix pawk ah nin:
“Koe tix blah ah mawh maix Yaw-hang cao cub com;
koe tix blah ah mawh maix Yi-li-yax;
mai tix blah ah mawh maix Yie-li-mi,
mai ah mawh cao jhai tix kaux kaux heue.”
15 Ye-sux chawk kix ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pex ah mawh aux mawx lie?”
16 hSi-meung Pi-teux pawk ah nin:
“Maix mawh Khrit,
mawh kawn simex Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju heue.”
17 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Si-meung kawn sime Yaw-hang,
maix kied koe buan son tete;
mawh kheu daux krax pa mawh pa meh in ang mawh kawn pui pa tawx ka maix,
hoik mawh keeing aux Siyiex piang rao ma pa plak jhi ka maix.
18 Aux krai ka maix,
maix mawh Pi-teux mawh rang simaox;
ka piang rang simaox in,
aux sang sum ceu cao ot kah,
pie sibrawm krax yum kawx ang pon pex ka nawh.
19 iAux tag sang tawx jiak saw meung rao ma ka maix,
ceu pa ang maix cu pui yuh ka piang haktiex,
ka piang rao ma kawx tag ang cu yuh nang;
ceu pa cu maix pui yuh ka piang haktiex,
ka piang rao ma kawx tag sang cu yuh nang heue.”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux tawx lox ang cu pui gau krai mawh tix Khrit ka pui tix kaux kawx heue.
Ye-sux grox krai sang kha rhawm tix mai sang yum tix
(Lai Ma-kheu 8.31–19.1; Lai Lu-cia 9.22, 27)
21 Jauh tom yam an,
Ye-sux krai sidawng ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Aux tag sang haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tag sang kham krax kha rhawm tom nie ka daux tawm taix kuad song,
cao phung kaing,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix.
Khaix an kix pauh ka aux,
ka daux loe singaix aux tag sang kaoh im heue.”
22 Pi-teux bluh Ye-sux hoek ka plak in,
mai tieb tie ka nawh ah nin:
“Ang!
Simiang eue,
kaing in ang doh lih hoek ka piang to kaux maix heue!”
23 Ye-sux kling to kaux tix krai ka Pi-teux ah nin:
“Sa tan,
yoi heue!
Maix mawh simaox pa sideeing ka aux;
mawh kheu ceu pa keud maix ang mawh pa keud Siyiex,
hoik mawh pa keud kawn pui heue.”
24 jMawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka pui gau tix:
“Phan koe pui pa hawt khaix aux,
tag caw tix tawx ka to kaux tix,
mai klawm ka khaox lhak tix hawt khaix aux.
25 kMawh kheu pui pa keud tix sang taoh ka ju tix,
tag sang tieh tix som ju;
pui pa som ju son aux,
tag sang tieh tix pon ju.
26 Pui tix kaux kaux phan hoik pon ka haktiex khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
plud ka ju to kaux tix,
koe dee mhawm ceu pa tix lie?
Ang koe heue!
Nawh tag sang teei ceu pa tix loh ka ju to kaux tix lie?
27 lKawn kawn pui tag sang ot ka daux min plak ngai keeing tix,
mai lih hoek tau mai kawn cau Siyiex.
Yam an,
nawh tag sang hawt tix pawk bix awm kaing pa yuh pui kaux mawx kaux an.
28 Aux bre tix krai ka pex,
pui pa cung dee in,
koe tix blah sivoe sang yum kix,
tag sang yaox kawn kawn pui lih hoek tix giex kaing ka daux kix heue.”
