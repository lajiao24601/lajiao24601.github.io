Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Naw-yax
9
aSiyiex tawx buan ka Naw-yax mai ka kawn simex nawh khankix ah nin:
“Kieh pex baox deeh keh haoh hun,
mai kieh kawn praix pex naog ka haktiex.
2 To sat ku ceu pa koe piang haktiex,
sim daux rao ma mai can pa chix chuh piang tiex,
mai kax ku ceu pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra tag koe krax lhat krax graung ka pex,
kieh pex kuang kah.
3 Sivoe an,
aux tawx taux lhax kieh mawh priex preem ka son pex;
yam in aux kawx tawx to sat in kix khawm uik ka pex,
kieh mawh priex preem son pex.
4 bViang mawh nan,
pex ang pon tix ih nex tau mai nham,
kheu koe ju daux nham.
5 Pui pa teei ka ju kawn pui,
aux tag sang teei ka ju nawh;
can to sat pa teei ka ju pui laux,
mai tag sang teei ju nawh laux nang.
6 cCan pa kieh nham kawn pui lih,
pui tang kaux kawx tag kieh nham nawh lih,
mawh kheu aux,
pa mawh Siyiex phan yuh kawn pui hoik hawt awm riang pak peung to kaux tix.
7 dKieh pex deeh keh koe kawn praix tom nie,
mai kieh kawn praix pex pre ka piang haktiex heue.”
8 Siyiex ah nin ka Naw-yax mai kawn simex nawh khankix:
9 “Yam in aux tag yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pex,
mai doeh ceu cad pex,
10 khawm to sat pa koe ka piang haktiex,
pa mawh to sat,
sim saik khankix pa hoik lih tau mai pex khaing daux reu khawm uik.
11 Aux ap tix deuh lox mai pex,
can pa im ju khankix ang baox lai laux leeing ka pa kheem rawm tix bawg kawx,
mai ang lai baox kieh rawm kheem ka piang haktiex.
12-13 Aux sang kieh si yawng mukheu lih khaing daux pai awm,
kieh mawh mhai sakse leeing ju.
Sijax mukheu in mawh pa yuh aux mai pex khawm can pa im ju khawm uik;
si yawng mukheu mawh mhai sakse sijax mukheu pa yuh aux mai haktiex.
14 Bawg yam mawx ang ah,
aux tawm pai awm ka daux rao ma,
si yawng lih ka daux pai awm,
15 aux tag doh keud ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tix si nax pex mai aux,
khawm can pa im ju:
rawm ang lai kheem ka to sat pa im ju ku ceu khawm uik laux leeing.
16 Yam yaox aux si yawng mukheu pa koe daux pai awm,
aux tag doh keud ka sijax mukheu pa yuh tix tau mai pex khawm can to sat pa im ju pa koe ka piang haktiex in.
17 In mawh mhai sakse sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tix deuh mai can to sat pa im ju heue.”
Naw-yax mai kawn simex nawh
18 Kawn simex Naw-yax pa lih khaing daux reu mawh Sang,
Hang mai Ya-fu.
(Hang mawh keeing Cia-nang.)
19 In kix loe kaux mawh kawn simex Naw-yax,
mawh tax du kawn pui pa pung prang ka piang haktiex.
20 Naw-yax mawh pui pa yuh kaung ma,
mai mawh pa jauh tix sum si peem plix siiet.
21 Koe tix bawg,
nawh nyaux plai tom yoek tix,
mai it ka kaong daux nyiex gox.
22 Hang keeing Cia-nang yaox it ka kaong keeing tix,
tom lih krai pux aik tix ra kaux mhong.
23 Sang mai Ya-fu teei kraung dha tix pheen,
mai kiex deuh piang klip tix,
mai hu deeh khaix,
lhop kraung dha an ka piang keeing kix;
kiex vud ngai tix jhak plak praix,
mai ang chix yaox ka kaong keeing tix.
24 Yam si duad Naw-yax khaing yoek tix,
hoik tawng pa yuh kawn simex pa iak tix,
25 tom ah nin:
Kieh Cia-nang pon krax pa jhax lawg!
Nawh tag sang mawh siliex cawng pux aik tix.
26 Tag yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Sang!
Cia-nang tag yuh siliex cawng Sang.
27 Kieh Siyiex joh jhan Ya-fu ting haok huan!
Kieh kawn praix nawh ot tau mai kawn praix Sang!
Kieh Cia-nang mawh siliex cawng Ya-fue heue.
28 Khaix hoik kheem rawm,
Naw-yax baox im ju loe yieh tix phuan num.
29 Yam yum nawh,
nawh koe ju dim yieh tix phuan num heue.
