Lox rhax pa pon krax pa pex
68
Siyiex kaoh grheu ka pui koeng ka nawh hu;
pui koeng ka nawh hoik to mox tix khaing sivoe ngai nawh khawm uik.
2 Awm taox ngu pa phlu bhaung hu,
nawh hoik grheu kix to;
awm ngu tien pa hoik ceh,
pui caox hoik grai grup ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
3 Viang mawh nan,
pui rauh pui see gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
gaux rhawm rhax lai ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex heue.
 
4 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux nawh;
cox rien khre daux krax son pui pa brug pai awm ana.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua;
pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm graoh ka sivoe ngai nawh heue.
 
5 Siyiex ot ka daux nyiex buan nawh;
nawh kia ngien ka kawn doe,
mai teem coi miex mai.
6 Nawh kieh pui pa ang koe paox koe pie yaox tix koe nyiex koe ma,
kieh pui pa laung deeh pon krax pa pluat ploi;
viang mawh nan,
pui pa tieh vheen tag sang paung ot ka haktiex pa riem heue.
 
7 Siyiex eue,
yam hu maix ka sivoe ngai pui cie tix,
yam thung kix tiang ka daux tiag daux tu,
8 bHaktiex tag yung yang,
mai rao ma kawx hoik cawd ka rawm lhiex;
mawh kheu Siyiex Si-naic lih hoek,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex lih bleeh.
9 Siyiex eue,
maix ploi ka lhiex ting lih;
maix kieh haktiex pa kroh pon caux.
10 Pui cie maix nyawm ot nyawm koe dee an;
maix teei krax pa caox rhawm tix kia ngien ka pui hot pui sibhawm heue.
 
11 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox tix,
bun miex tom nie tawx lox ah nin:
12 simiang mai pui naing kix hoik yaog tix to heue!
Bun miex pa ot ka nyiex khankix hoik guah ka kraung khrai pa tud tix pa mawh:
13 koe riang si kaux pa hoik om ka mau luah,
mai preeik an hoik mawh khri pa toh riang kah.
(Yuh ka mawx koe pui tix blah mox tix ka daux kawg yung singaix yam puing naing lie?)
14 Yam grheu Siyiex pa ting riang ting rao ka simiang khankix gawng Sa-meung,
nawh hoik kieh haktiex an lih ka rha ting heue.
 
15 Gawng Pa-sang kied koe grang heue;
maix koe gawng pa lhaong tom nie.
16 Yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh ngai tix vawk hu khaing gawng lhaong,
gawng Shi-ang pa hoik laug Siyiex mawh dee ot tix lie?
Simiang Siyiex tag sang paung ot ka piang gawng in leeing ju leeing phan heue!
 
17 Simiang Siyiex viex ka ling law cie tix kaing rheng kaing mheen,
jauh ka Si-nai laig daux nyiex pa singax singehd.
18 eNawh haok ka rao ma,
mai viex ka pui laung tom nie;
nawh rieb ka khawng phak lak sawng pui tieh pui vheen.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang paung ot dee an heue.
 
19 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik peeh nawh ka beeh glawm ex ku ngaix;
nawh mawh Siyiex pa taoh ka ex.
20 Siyiex ex mawh Siyiex pa taoh;
nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh cao pa taoh ex luat luan khaing krax yum heue.
 
21 Siyiex tag sang tawk ka kaing pui koeng max,
lauh max ka dong pui pa ang chix naox yuh ka phi map.
22 Simiang ah nin:
aux tag sang viex pui pa koeng ka pex
hoek khaing gawng Pa-sang;
aux tag sang deeh viex kix hoek khaing ka dee kied raux rawm sigrit sigra,
23 son sang kieh pex laig ka daux nham rawm klawng kix;
sox eei pex tag sang lied ka nham kix heue.
 
24 Siyiex eue,
grawng pa pex maix pui hoik yaox ka ngai tix;
pui hun hoik yaox ka Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang laig daux nyiex pa singax singeh.
25 Pui rhax lai hu sivoe,
pui tauh ting hu plak khaix,
nyawm bun krih pa tauh kru hu daux greung.
26 Pex tag caw tix khrawm paox tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex;
kawn praix pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik heue!
27 Pui ceu Piien-ya-ming pa iak khaing paox hu sivoe,
khaix an koe simiang ceu pui Yo-tax,
mai ceu pui cie nawh hawt khaix,
khaix an koe ceu pui Si-pu-loeng mai simiang ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix pa baox hawt khaix heue.
 
28 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix jhi pa ting riang ting rao maix,
jhi ka riang rao pa hoik sax siao maix son yix.
29 Jauh yam ot maix ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa mawh dee viex simiang khankix khawng phak lak sawng hoek tix tan ka maix,
khiox khaw maix jhi pa ting riang ting rao tix.
30 Khiox khaw maix ne raw ka Ai-cix,
pa mawh to sat pa ot ka daux pang priem an;
ne raw meung ka piang haktiex,
pa mawh phung moi mhaing mai kawn moi iak kix,
tom hoik pon kieh kix cu lox,
mai tan lu mau ka maix.
Khiox khaw maix grheu ka ceu pui pa brax yuh naing an kix.
31 Koe kawn cau pa ting tag sang kaoh khaing Ai-cix;
pui Ku-seu tag sang yaog taix tix rawg buan ka Siyiex heue.
 
32 Meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex;
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
33 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka cao pa brug ka rao ma tom dix dix;
nghiet heue,
nawh ploi lox pa luah siawp sibrawm tix lih.
34 Pex tag caw tix krai pao ka pa ting riang ting rao Siyiex;
pa ting siawp sibrawm nawh in hoek ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
pa ting riang ting rao nawh hoik naog ka daux pai awm khawm uik.
35 Siyiex eue,
maix ot ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh kied mawh pa lhat graung pui ka heue!
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex tawx riang rao,
mai siawp sibrawm ka pui cie tix.
 
Pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex heue!
