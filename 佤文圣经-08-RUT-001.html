Lai Lu-teux
Nyiex Yi-li-mi-leux khawm uik gah tix hu ka haktiex Maw-ya
1
1-2 Sivoe dix dix,
yam kuang cao prawng see ka Yi-seu-liex ka baux bawg yam an,
sibhawm praix lih tix koe ka daux haktiex an.
Koe ceu pui Yi-fa-tha tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh kaux Yi-li-mi-leux,
sivoe an ot ka veng Paw-li-haung meung Yo-tax;
nawh viex moeng tix Na-eu-me mai kawn simex tix ra kaux,
pa mawh Ma-loeng mai Ci-liien,
hu tau ka meung Maw-ya,
vai tix ot ka dee an.
Yam ot kix ka dee an,
3 Yi-li-mi-leux hoik yum,
mai deuh ka Na-eu-me mai kawn simex nawh ra kaux;
4 kawn simex nawh ra kaux teei tix moeng kawn bun haktiex Maw-ya pa mawh Eu-eu-pa mai Lu-teux.
Khaix an kao num,
5 Ma-loeng mai Ci-liien kawx yum,
deuh ka Na-eu-me,
ang lai koe mex,
mai ang lai koe kawn simex heue.
Na-eu-me mai Lu-teux deeh ing ka Paw-li-haung
6 Yam kawn ot Na-eu-me ka haktiex Maw-ya,
hoik mhong grawng kia ngien Simiang Siyiex ka pui cie nawh,
mai kieh kix pon plix nghox tom nie,
tom rien tix lih khaing haktiex Maw-ya deeh ing tau mai mun tix ra kaux ka haktiex tix.
7 Kix deeh ing tau ka Yo-tax,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux krax,
8 Na-eu-me ah nin ka mun tix khawm ra:
“Pax tang ing ka nyiex miex keeing tix khaing heue!
Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx krax pa caox rhawm tix kia ngien ka pax,
awm kia ngien pax aux mai pui pa hoik yum yuh nan.
9 Kieh Simiang Siyiex baox kieh pax koe bawg yam tix koe mex,
koe nyiex dee ot.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Na-eu-me ngheet ka kiex.
Mun kiex ra kaux tom yiiem,
10 ah nin ka Na-eu-me:
“Ang!
Yiex tag ing tau mai maix ka daux haktiex dee ot pui maix dee an.”
11 Na-eu-me ah nin:
“Kawn bun aux eue,
pax deeh ing khaing!
Pax sang ing tau mai aux yuh ka mawx?
Mawh kheu kawn chix keh kawn simex aux,
mai son sang mawh mex pax lie?
12 Deeh ing heue!
Kawn bun aux eue,
hu heue!
Aux hoik kied kuad,
ang lai chix koe mex.
Sawn aux kawn koe bawg yam ceeix ceeix chix koe mex daux bo in kawx,
mai keh kawn simex,
13 pax cu tix krox kix ting ka lie?
Pax pon krox ka mai ang mex ka pui lie?
Ang!
Kawn bun aux,
in ang doh chix.
Simiang Siyiex hoik ciyang ka aux;
aux kied laux rhawm son pax heuea.”
14 Kiex baox yiiem kah.
Eu-eu-pa tom ngheet ka miex mex tix,
mai deeh ing ka nyiex miex keeing tix;b
viang mawh nan,
Lu-teux kawn ang cu tix lih hu.
15 Na-eu-me ah nin ka nawh:
“Lu-teux,
o maix hoik ing ka pui ceu tix mai ka siyiexc nawh dee an.
Maix kawx ing hawt nawh heue!”
16 Viang mawh nan,
Lu-teux ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix paw lai kieh aux lih khaing maix.
Kieh aux hu tau mai maix liak!
Maix hu dee mawx,
aux kawx hu dee an;
maix ot dee mawx,
aux kawx ot ka dee an;
ceu pui maix tag mawh ceu pui aux;
Siyiex maix tag mawh Siyiex aux.
17 Maix yum dee mawx,
aux kawx yum dee an,
mai si paung ka dee an.
Lheu khaing krax yum,
kaing ceu pa tix ang ahd,
ang doh pon kieh ax guah paox tix!
Phan aux tieh vheen,
kieh Simiang Siyiex ciyang aux tom cien heue!”
18 Na-eu-me yaox Lu-teux viex rhawm tix hawt nawh,
mai ang lai thiang ka heue.
19 Mawh kheu nan,
kiex ra kaux kawn ca hu krax hu tix,
hoek ka veng Paw-li-haung.
Yam hoek kiex,
pui veng khawm uik kied gaux rhawm.
Bun miex khankix gaux rhawm tix ah lox:
“Nawh mawh Na-eu-me tete lie?”
20 Na-eu-me ah nin:
“Paw lai kok aux kaux Na-eu-me,
kok aux Ma-laxe heue!
Mawh kheu Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm kieh aux laux rhawm.
21 Yam lih aux kied mag mi,
yam deeh aux Simiang Siyiex kieh aux ang koe tid tix.
Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka aux;
cao pa ting siawp sibrawm kieh aux laux rhawm.
Yuh ka mawx kawn lai kok aux kaux Na-eu-me lie?”
22 In hoik mawh grawng deeh hoek Na-eu-me mai mun nawh Lu-teux ceu pui Maw-ya khaing haktiex Maw-ya.
Kiex hoek ka Paw-li-haung,
jiet mawh bawg yam vawg nghox ca pa lang mu heue.
