Khu dee tan nyawng bhawng
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 37.25–28)
30
“Yuh khu dee tan pa gleeing nyawng bhawng tix mu ka khaox Cing-heu-huang.
2 Khu an mawh pon plak,
plak lang tipon phuan kongfeeng,
plak vieh tipon phuan kongfeeng,
plak lhaong tix dim kongfeeng.
Rung pa bhlien an pon rung kleuk tix tau mai khu lih mawh tix mu heue.
3 Om khri mhawm ka toh plak piang,
hawt praok,
khawm pon khawn rung,
mai kawb khri ka praok.
4 Yuh daux jhawk khri ra mu son sang chup ka kao grawng khu,
kieh rieb ka greem khri khu khawm ra plak pa ot ka praok,
son sang chix chup khaox grawng khu gleeing nyawng bhawng tix laig.
5 Yuh khaox grawng ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka khri.
6 “Khu gleeing nyawng bhawng an,
deuh ot plak praix dee vhak man tie sivoe teug sijax mukheu.
Dee an mawh dee sang si pup aux ka pex heue.
7 Ku ngaix pon ngawb cao cao,
yam hoek Ya-loeng cox khre ka kawk ngu,
caw tix gleeing nyawng bhawng ka piang khu an.
8 Yam tok ka kawk ngu plak pon bo,
tag caw tix gleeing ka nyawng bhawng nang.
Tix ju hoek tix ju,
tag caw tix tan ka nyawng bhawng yuh nin,
ang pon tix kieh jud heue.
9 Ang pon tix gleeing nyawng bhawng pa ang khaw ka piang,
phan tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ang ah,
Krax-tan Pa-nhim ang ah,
ang pon tix tan Krax-tan Pa-jauh ka piang.
10 Tix num tix bawg,
Ya-loeng caw tix yuh krax tan pa kieh singax singeh,
siao nham khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
mai phruah ka piang dee bhlien jaong khu pon khawn.
Tix ju hoek tix ju,
tag caw tix hawt yuh ka been in.
Khu dee tan in tag caw tix kieh singax singeh khawm uik,
mai tan ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Pa tan ka nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
12 “Yam si kaw maix ka ca rang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
pui ku kaux tag caw tix tawx mau pa taoh ju tix ka aux,
son sang luat luan khaing krax laux leeing pa si pup yam si kaw kah.
13 aCan pui pa hoik tiiem ca rang ku kaux,
tag hawt tix tawx mau awm diag tong pa hoik ap son nyiex pa singax singeh,
mai kieh mawh khawng phak pa tan lu ka aux heue.
14 Pui pa hoik tiiem kix ca rang ku kaux,
pa mawh simex jauh tix nga num haok plak piang,
tag tawx mau in khawm uik.
15 Pa tawx mau taoh ka ju in ang guah pui mi pui hot;
pui mi ang nie tawx,
pui hot ang yawm tawx.
16 Mau in kix pa koh khaing pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
caw tix siao mawh mau pa yuh son nyiex gox buan man.
In tag mawh pa tan kix tix yuh son ju to kaux tix;
aux tag chix doh keud tix kia ngien ka kix kheu in heue.”
Pan tawng
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
18 b“Maix yuh pan tawng mai jaong dee deuh pan tawng tix mu,
mai sau rawm naog ka daux si nax.
19-20 Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix laig ot ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
yam hu dex kix ka khu,
tan lu ka khawng brie,
khoek taix caong tix ka rawm pa ot daux pan in.
Yuh nin kix tag ang yaox tix yum;
21 kix tag khoek taix caong tix,
tom tag chix yaox tix luat luan khaing krax yum.
In mawh lox tat jhawm prawng pa sang hawt kix mai kawn praix kix leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
Been yuh bee pa singax singeh
22 cSimiang Siyiex deeh ah nin ka Maw-si:
23 “Teei sidah bhawng pa kied mhawm:
pa mawh Muran liah kongcing,
ro kui bhawng loe kongcing,
chang phu bhawng loe kongcing,
24 khaox cai hung liah kongcing (hawt awm been cien diag tong pa ap son nyiex pa singax singeh),
mai baox bee sime pon kongsheng,
25 teei ka bee pa singax singeh,
pa mawh pa hoik klao tix yuh ka rawm sidah bhawng.
26 Kraix bee pa singax singeh in ka nyiex gox buan man aux,
mai teug sijax mukheu,
27 pheen sawm mai kraung siao piang pheen khawm uik,
jawk kawk ngu mai kraung siao ka khawm uik,
khu dee gleeing nyawng bhawng,
28 khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai kraung siao ka khawm uik,
khawm pan mai jaong khankix.
29 Tan lu in kix khawm uik,
kieh mawh pa singax singeh khawm uik;
can pui pa dex kah,
khawng brie ang ah,
tag sang mat ka siawp sibrawm pa singax singeh an.
30 Kraix bee pa singax singeh ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
mai ap kix mawh cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka aux.
31 “Maix krai ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ah nin:
‘Tix ju hoek tix ju,
yam krub pex ka aux,
caw tix siao ka bee pa singax singeh in.
32 Ang pon tix hieh bee pa singax singeh in ka pui tang kaux,
ang pon tix yuh bee pa awm paox ka been tix ceu khaing.
Bee pa singax singeh in;
pex caw tix kieh mawh kraung ceu pa singax singeh.
33 Phan koe pui pa yuh bee ceu pa awm in,
phan ang mawh nan,
kraix bee in ka pui tang kaux pa ang mawh cao giex kaing li,
tag sang teei tix tik ceu kaux nawh khaing daux pui cie aux heue.’ ”
Been yuh pa si oe bhawng
34 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Teei ceu nyawng bhawng in kix plak greem ku ceu pa mawh:
sidah bhawng Su-heu,
sidah bhawng hoi,
sidah bhawng Pai-song,
sidah bhawng Law-ban sidu awm paox,
35 Yuh lih mawh awm pa yuh rawm bhawng khankix.
Baox sau kih kah,
mai kieh singax singeh.
36 Teei lih tix blah krid mawh mien,
viex hoek ka nyiex gox buan man aux,
mai rhuat ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu,
pa mawh dee si pup aux ka pex.
Pa bhawng in mawh pa siao ka dee pa blawn dee singax singeh.
37 Ang pon tix yuh pa bhawng pa awm paox ka sidah tix ceu khaing son to kaux tix;
in hoik mawh khawng phak ceu pa singax singeh pa tan ka aux.
38 Phan koe pui pa yuh ka pa bhawng ceu in,
son sang siao ka to kaux tix,
tag sang tik nawh lih khaing daux pui cie aux khankix heue.”
