Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk Tix
1
Aux mawh Pao-law;
mawh kheu alo Siyiex,
aux hoik pon krax pa kok mawh kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit.
Aux mai pux aik aux Saw-thi-ni
2 atiiem lai tawx ka kawn Khrit ceu cao Siyiex ka veng Keu-ling-to khankix.
Pex mai pui pa ot haktiex ku dauh pa khiox khaw ka ceu kaux simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit,
hoik pon kok nang tix mawh kawn Khrit pui cie Siyiex ka daux Ye-sux Khrit.
Khrit mawh simiang kix,
mai hoik mawh nang simiang ex.
3 Kieh keeing ex Siyiex mai simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son,
krax nyawm rhawm ka pex heue!
Buan son ka daux Khrit
4 Aux yaog yaw Siyiex tix son pex jheeng jheeng,
mawh kheu hoik tawx nawh buan son ka pex ka daux Ye-sux Khrit,
5 hoik kieh pex kub ku ceu ka daux Khrit,
hoik grum koe ka ngai lai mai lox ah si kah krai.
6 Phan mawh grawh grawng pa kham sakse Khrit,
hoik sum kaux cong ot ka daux pex,
7 mai krox jun tom yam hoik jhi Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tix lih bleeh,
ang lai cud cha ka khawng phak buan son tix ceu kawx.
8 Simiang Siyiex tag sang teem coi ka pex tom si dauh,
mai kieh pex ang lai koe dee ne raw ka daux singaix pa lih hoek Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
9 Siyiex mawh pa kaux sijax;
nawh kok ka pex,
mai kieh pex rom paox tix tau mai kawn simex nawh,
pa mawh simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue.
Ceu cao guah paox tix
10 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux sidiex pex ka daux ceu kaux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit,
krai lox caw tix kieh khaw caw,
ang pon tix guah paox,
tag caw tix ih rhawm paox tix,
kieh rhawm keud tix khaw caw paox ka daux kaing yuh tix.
11 Pux aik pux o aux khankix eue,
koe pui nyiex ma Keu-lai krai sidawng ka aux,
krai koe simah ka daux pex.
12 bRhawm keud aux mawh nin,
pex pui hun tang koe lox pa krai,
koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui cie Pao-law”;
tix blah ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui cie Ya-paw-law”;
tix blah ah nin:
“Aux mawh cie pui Pi-teux”;
tix blah ah nin:
“Aux mawh cie Khrit heue.”
13 Yuh ka mawx lai guah Khrit tang ot heue!
Khex mawh Pao-law pa yum tang pex ka piang khaox lhak lie?
Pex cub com ka daux ceu kaux Pao-law lie?
14 cTing buan son Siyiex,
lheu khaing Ci-li-see-pu mai Kai-yo,
aux ang bax cub com pex tix kaux kawx,
15 mawh kheu nan,
ang koe pui pa pon krai,
pex hoik cub com tix ka daux ceu kaux aux. (
16 dMawh tete,
aux kawx hoik cub com ka See-thi-fa-na mai ka pui nyiex ma nawh,
lheu khaing an,
kawn baox cub com ka pui mawx,
aux ang lai sidawng kah.)
17 Khrit ploi aux hoek ang mawh son sang cub com pui,
hoik mawh son sang nhie aux sidiex gum lox buan,
ang siao ka lox ping nyia cuyi,
son ang krax pa yum Khrit ka piang khaox lhak sang grai ceu heue.
Khrit mawh sibrawm Siyiex mai ping nyia cuyi
18 Grawh grawng pa yum Khrit ka piang khaox lhak,
hoik mawh kaing pa daox aw ka son pui an kix pa hu krax pa laux leeing;
phan mawh ka son pui pa pon krax taoh in kix,
hoik mawh siawp sibrawm Siyiex.
19 eMawh kheu nan,
phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
aux tag sang yuh laux ka ping nyia cuyi pui pa lhak lhiao;
aux tag sang teei tix tik ka krax chix krax tawng pui pa lhaong ngai lai heue.
20 fPhan mawh nan,
pui pa lhak lhiao ot dee mawx lie?
Pui pa lhaong ngai lai ot dee mawx lie?
Pui pa chix krai lox thiang ot dee mawx lie?
Siyiex hoik kieh ping nyia cuyi haktiex in lih tix mawh pa dao aw heue!
21 Siyiex siao ping nyia cuyi nawh,
mai kieh kawn pui haktiex ang pon tawng yung nawh kheu ping nyia cuyi to kaux tix;
deeh tieh tix,
Siyiex but rhawm tix,
tieh tix taoh pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh khaing ka daux gum lox “pa daox aw” pa hoik sidiex ex.
22 Pui Yo-thaix sawg mhai pa daox pa am,
pui Si-lax sawg ping nyia cuyi,
23 viang mawh nan,
ex krai pao ka Khrit pa tawk piang khaox lhak.
Gum lox in hoik mawh krax pa kaig tua ka pui Yo-thaix,
mawh kaing pa daox aw ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
24 Viang mawh nan,
ka pui pa hoik laug Siyiex,
mawh pui Yo-thaix ang ah,
mawh pui Si-lax ang ah,
gum lox in hoik mawh Khrit;
nawh mawh siawp sibrawm Siyiex,
ping nyia cuyi Siyiex.
25 Mawh kheu “krax pa daox aw Siyiex” kawn lheu khaing ping nyia cuyi kawn pui,
“krax pa iak riang Siyiex” kawx kawn lheu khaing pa ting riang kawn pui heue.
26 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
tag caw tix doh keud ka yam kok Siyiex ka pex,
pex hoik ot ka daux pa yuh ka mawx.
Phan jhak tix yaox ka rhawm keud kawn pui,
pex hoik mawh pui pa yawm krax pa lhak lhiao,
pa yawm ka riang rao,
pa yawm ka ceu kaux pa ting min.
27 Siyiex hoik laug ka pa ce kawn pui mawh pa daox aw,
mai kieh pui pa lhak lhiao kaig tua;
Siyiex laug ka pa ce kawn pui ang lai koe riang,
mai kieh pui pa kaux cong kaig tua.
28 Siyiex kawx hoik laug pa map nai kawn pui,
pa ang kawn pui rhawm yaox,
pa ang kawn pui een koe ceu,
hoek tix pu pliak ka pa hoik een kawn pui ting ceu,
29 mai kieh kawn pui ang lai krai lox ting mu ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
30 Kheu nan,
Siyiex hoik kieh pex pon tix si doe tix tau mai Ye-sux Khrit,
mai baox kieh nawh lih mawh ping nyia cuyi ex;
ka daux nawh,
ex hoik pon tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
mai lih mawh ceu pui pa singax singeh cie Siyiex,
khaix an pon krax pa luat luan.
31 gAwm hoik krai daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pui mawx sang ting lox,
tag caw tix yaw ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Ye-sux heue.”
