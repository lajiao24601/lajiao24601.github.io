Lai Law-ma
Krai dawt
Pao-law tiiem ka Lai Law-ma, mawh son sang rien khre tix sivoe sang hu vhi ka ceu cao Law-ma. Pao-law keud tix sang ran yuh kaing ka daux kawn Khrit Law-ma tix baux bawg yam, khaix an nied tix hu ka Si-pang-ya ka daux krax pa teem coi kix. Nawh tiiem lai in kieh kah dee bleeh grawng krax pa yum rhawm nawh ka Khrit, mai krax pa yum rhawm in sang caw tix yuh lih yuh ka mawx ka daux kawn Khrit. In mawh gum lox pa sidiex, pa kah Pao-law pa blawn sidawng ka daux phuk lai in heue.
Pao-law ran tix chawk cheng kawn Khrit ka daux ceu cao Law-ma, krai ka kix, nawh hoik rawg buan son kix jheeng jheeng. Khaix an, nawh krai lih ka kaing ting krax yum rhawm sidawng ah nin: “Mawh kheu gum lox buan in plak jhi grawng pa kieh Siyiex kawn pui. Si doe tix mai nawh ka daux krax pa khaw caw yuh ka mawx: pa kaoh khaing krax yum rhawm, pa dex mai krax yum rhawm heue.” (1.17)
Pao-law baox nied tix krai grawng kaing ting in. Nawh krai yuh nin, kawn pui ka piang haktiex, mawh pui Yo-thaix ang ah, mawh pui tang ceu tang cad ang ah, tag caw tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux krax pa khaw caw. Kawn pui ka piang haktiex hoik ot ka greem veei siawp sibrawm phi map khawm uik, mawh ngie tag caw tix yum rhawm ka Ye-sux Khrit, tom taug pon tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux krax pa khaw caw. Khaix an, Pao-law tiiem grawng khaix hoik si doe kawn pui tix tau mai Siyiex, tom tag pon tix kleuk ju pa khraox tix tau mai Khrit. Pui pa yum rhawm pon tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux krax pa khaw caw; khuan Siyiex kieh nawh pon krax pa pluat ploi khaing daux phi map, mai khaing daux siawp sibrawm krax yum. Simin ting phuan mu hoek daix mu, Pao-law kawx krai lao grawng kaing ting lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex mawh pa yuh ka mawh, khawm grawng ot khuan Siyiex ka daux ju kawn Khrit koe ceu yuh ka mawx. Khaix an, Pao-law krai lao ka pui Yo-thaix mai pui tang ceu tang cad grawh grawng krax pa keud Siyiex tix sang yuh ka daux kawn pui. Dee si dauh gum lox pa pon nawh mawh: pui Yo-thaix tieb tie ka grawng mawh Ye-sux pa yuh son pui piang haktiex tix blah, Siyiex tag sang tawx Ye-sux Khrit kieh kawn pui piang haktiex pon buan son nawh khawm uik. Pao-law yum rhawm ka pui Yo-thaix ang doh sang tieb tie ka Ye-sux leeing ju. Dee si dauh nawh krai lao ka kawn Khrit caw tix koe rhawm pa yuh ka mawx, mai nie krai lao tag caw tix ngien paox tix ka daux krax muh krax rhawm. Nawh kawx hoik krai lao ka beeh glawm pa caw kawn Khrit tix sang yuh ka Siyiex, pa caw tix sang yuh ka meung haktiex, mai ka pui tang kaux khankix, grawng dee chix keud chix tawng khankix. Nawh teei grawh grawng to kaux tix mai gum lox pa yaog yaw ka Siyiex, nghoik tix krai ka lox lai in heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai mai dee bleeh kaing ting (1.1–17)
2. Kawn pui sang lo ka krax taoh (1.18–3.20)
3. Daux krax taoh Siyiex (3.21–4.25)
4. Ju pa khraox ka daux Khrit (5.1–8.39)
5. Pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux krax pa yuh Siyiex (9.1–11.36)
6. Kaing pa yuh kawn Khrit (12.1–15.13)
7. Gum lox pa nghoik mai pa chawk cheng (15.14–16.27)
