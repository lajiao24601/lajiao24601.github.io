Grawng krax pa phit vao ka daux ceu cao
5
aAux rug hoik mhong koe grawng krax pa phit vao ka daux pex;
grawng krax pa phit vao ceu in,
pie ka daux pui laig li tang ceu kawx ang yeung.
Aux mhong krai pui koe pui pa it tau mai miex iak tix heue!
2 Pex kawn sang chix ah lox ting mu yuh ka mawx lie?
Pex tag caw tieh tix laux rhawm laux rhi,
mai teei tix tik pui pa yuh kaing yuh nin khaing ka daux pex.
3-4 Phan mawh son aux,
viang to kaux aux hoik singai ot khaing pex,
rhawm rhi aux hoik ot tau mai pex.
Aux hoik prawng ca ka pui pa yuh krax pa phit vao in kix ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Ye-sux,
awm ot aux tau mai pex dee an yuh nan.
Yam khrawm pex paox tix,
rhawm rhi aux hoik ot tau mai pex.
Een rhawm tix ka siawp sibrawm Simiang Ye-sux,
5 pex khaw teei tix tawx pui in ka Sa tan,
kieh Sa tan yuh to kaux nawh laux leeing,
son sang kieh khuan nawh pon krax taoh,
ka daux singaix yam deeh hoek Simiang Ye-sux heue.
6 bPex krai lox ting mu ang khaw heue!
Pex hoik tawng koe lox pa ah nin:
“Su saix ceeix ceeix khaing,
pon chix kieh glua mien khawm uik lih ploh.”
7 cTag caw teei tix tik ka su saix pa prim,
pex tom tag pon tix singax khawm uik.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug awm glua mien khraox pa ang sau su saix yuh nan;
tete te lai,
pex caw tix dawg mawh pa ang sau su saix.
Priex preem Poi Luat-luan,
ex hoik rien khre ka mhawm mhawm,
kawn yung Poi Luat-luan,
Khrit hoik tan lu to kaux tix yum tang.
8 dMawh kheu nan,
yam yuh ex poi in,
paw vi siao lo ka su saix pa prim,
pa mawh su saix pa koe krax pa caox pa caig,
viang mawh nan,
tag caw tix siao lo khao mun pa ang sau su saix,
pa mawh khao mun pa singax mai pa mawh pa meh heue.
9 Lox lai pa tawx aux ka pex sivoe an,
hoik mawh pa ne raw aux pex ang pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai pui pa phit vao.
10 Pa krai aux ang mawh ka daux pui pa laig li tang ceu,
pa phit vao,
pa ghoing rhawm,
pa briex lak,
mai pa krub riang rao khankix;
lheu khaing lih hu pex khaing haktiex in,
pex ang doh sang pon guah tix khaing pui in kix.
11 Dee bleeh lox krai aux mawh:
ang pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai pui an kix pa tang kok tix mawh kawn Khrit,
pa yuh krax phit vao,
ghoing rhawm,
krub riang rao,
map nai ka pui,
yoek ka plai,
mai briex lak.
Pie sawm tau mai pui pa yuh nin kawx ang chix heue.
12-13 eBeeh glawm aux ang mawh ka daux pa prawng ca ka pui pa ang mawh ceu cao,
Siyiex koi lawk prawng ca ka kix.
Kheu nan,
ang pex khaw tix prawng ca ka pui daux ceu cao lie?
Awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang Khrit ah nin:
“Tag teei tix tik ka pui pa caox pa caig an kix lih khaing daux pex heue!”
