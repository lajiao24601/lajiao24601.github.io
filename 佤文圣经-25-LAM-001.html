Lai Yiiem Doe Yie-li-mi
Krax pa laux rhawm Yie-lu-sa-leung
1
Veng pa hoik si khox hoik sied pui ka sivoe an,
yam in ang koe paox koe pie blauh tix it dee an.
Sivoe an hoik mawh pa yung yi pui ka piang haktiex,
yam in awm miex mai pa ang koe paox koe pie.
Sivoe an mawh moeng simiang veng ting tix kaux,
yam in mai lih mawh siliex heue.
 
2 Nawh yiiem leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
mai rawm ngai naog ka pak ngaix.
Sivoe an nawh koe paox grawm ting khankix,
yam in ang koe pui pa hoek tix kieh nawh nyawm rhawm tix kaux kawx.
Paox grawm nawh hoik coh ka nawh,
mai hoik lih tix mawh pui koeng ka nawh heue.
 
3 Pui Yo-tax mawh siliex cawng pa kha rhawm tix,
mai hoik khin kix lih hu khaing haktiex tixa.
Kix vu vie ka daux haktiex pui tang meung,
mai ang koe haktiex ot.
Pui koeng puing ka kix khawm pon daog,
mai ang koe krax pa to tix klawd ju heue.
 
4 Krax pa hu plak Shi-ang hoik nhik;
ang koe pui pa haok yuh poi.
Bun pa rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan hoik laux rhawm laux rhi;
cao giex kaing li chix yiiem ngie khaing.
Siviex veng hoik laux leeing;
Shi-ang hoik khi rhawm khi rhi heue.
 
5 Pui koeng ka nawh lih mawh simiang nawh;
siawp sibhawm kix hoik kieh nawh cu lox.
Mawh kheu yuh nawh pa caox pa caig tom nie,
Simiang Siyiex kieh nawh kha rhawm tix;
pui koeng hoik giex viex kawn simex kawn bun nawh hu dee singai heue.
 
6 Min plak ngai Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mawh pa prim.
Simiang nawh awm cak yuh nan,
hoik iak riang kheu cai rhawm tix;
kix mox tix khaing pa leue pui jaw,
hoik uik riang rao heue.
 
7 Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik nhik hoik riem,
deeh keud tix yaox yam koe min plak ngai kix ka daux singaix pa hoik luan.
Yam cawd nawh ka daux tawm taix pui koeng,
ang koe pui pa taoh teem ka nawh.
Pui pa pon kuang ka nawh jhak ka nawh,
mai nyiah ka grawng pa laux leeing nawh heue.
 
8 Min plak ngai nawh hoik uik;
nawh ot ka kaong,
mai kham krax pa map nai pui.
Nawh ngia ngie khaing,
mai ot ka daux pa kaig pa tua ang pon jhi pak ngai tix.
Phi map Yie-lu-sa-leung kied ting tete,
to kaux hoik seeing khawm uik heue.
 
9 Krax pa seeing brai nawh hoik naog ka kraung ceep nawh,
ang kuang ka dee si dauh to kaux tix.
Nawh hoik laux leeing;
ang koe pui pa kieh nawh nyawm rhawm.
Pui koeng ka nawh pon krax pa pex,
nawh tom taug khiox khaw krax pa caox rhawm Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
10 Pui koeng hoik tud rhim ka can sin ngao nawh khawm uik;
nawh yaox laig kix ka nyiex buan,
pa mawh dee ang Simiang Siyiex cu pui tang ceu tang cad laig heue.
 
11 Kawn pui riag yo ngia,
mai hu sawg tix ih ka ceu pa ih ku dauh ku dee;
kix teei siang pa ngawh loh priex preem kah,
mai khiox khaw son sang pon tix im ju.
Veng khawm uik kok yo ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix simi tix jhak;
jhak tix yaox grawng pa kied kha aux rhawm tix heue!
 
12 Nawh yiiem doe khiox khaw ka pui pa lawng krax ku kaux ah nin:
jhak tix yaox ka grawng aux heueb!
Ang sax koe pui pa kha rhawm tix awm aux yuh nin;
in hoik mawh krax kha rhawm pa thiem thie yam song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka piang aux heue.
 
13 Nawh ploi ngu hoek khaing plak piang,
hoik gleeing ka si-ang aux.
Nawh hoik tawm ka rub,
hoik kieh aux goh sideeing tix piang tiex.
Nawh hoik tik vud ka aux,
mai hoik kieh aux kha rhawm tix leeing lang singaix heue.
 
14 Nawh bre tix jhak ka krax pa lud aux,
mai hoik cieh kix ot tau,
vhak ot ka piang ngawg aux,
kieh gut aux tom hoik uik riang rao aux.
Simiang tawx aux ka pui koeng;
aux ang koe riang rao tix tieb tie heue.
 
15 Simiang nyiah ka can pui naing pa kua aux khawm uik;
nawh ploi pui naing pa haoh hun tix yuh pui naing pa kua aux laux.
Simiang dug dig ka pui cie aux,
hoik awm dug dig ka plix siiet yuh nan heue.
 
16 Aux yiiem son kaing in kix,
rawm ngai aux hoik buing tix lih ang cud.
Ang koe pui pa kieh aux nyawm rhawm;
ang koe pui pa pon tawx krax pa cong rhawm ka aux.
Pui koeng hoik puing pex ka aux;
pui cie aux ang lai koe ceu pa tid tix heue.
 
17 Aux tawx taix tix khiox khaw krax teem krax coi,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa teem coi ka aux.
Simiang Siyiex kok yo ka pui koeng,
mai hoek tix puing aux khawm pon praok si daix daog;
kix hoik kieh aux mawh ceu pa seeing pa brai heue.
 
18 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex mawh pa rauh see,
mawh kheu lieh lheu aux ka gum lox tawx nawh.
Kawn pui haktiex ku dauh ku dee eue,
nghiet heue;
jhak tix yaox pa kham aux krax kha rhawm.
Pui hoik giex simex bun pa nhum ju hu heue!
 
19 Aux khiox khaw krax teem coi ka paox grawm tix,
mai kix ang tawx taix tix teem coi.
Kheu sang sawg ceu pa ih pa un,
son sang yaox tix im ju,
cao giex kaing li mai simiang khankix hoik yum ka daux laih heue.
 
20 Simiang Siyiex eue,
jhak tix yaox ka grawng krax pa khi rhawm aux heue!
Jhak tix yaox ka grawng pa kha aux rhawm tix heue!
Rhawm rhi aux hoik max kheu laux rhawm aux son phi map.
Ka daux laih koe pa pauh pui yum,
ka daux nyiex koe pui yum heue.
 
21 Nghiet tix mhong ka lox ngia aux heue;
ang koe pui pa kieh aux nyawm rhawm.
Pui koeng hoik gaux rhawm kheu ploi maix krax laux leeing hoek piang aux.
Kieh singaix bawg yam pa deuh maix lih hoek;
kieh pui koeng kha rhawm tix awm aux yuh nan heue.
 
22 Kieh maix ne raw ka krax pa caox caig kix;
khiox khaw maix ciyang ka kix,
awm hoik ciyang maix aux kheu phi map aux yuh nan.
Aux khi rhawm riag yo;
rhawm rhi aux hoik saux tom ang lai chix krai heue.
