Lox rawg buan pui pa koe jhawm saux tix kaux
41
Pui pa chix kia chix ngien ka pui hot pui sibhawm kied koe buan son tete heue!
Yam ot ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka kix.
2 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien,
mai priex ka ju kix mhawm,
kieh kix pon buan son ka daux haktiex in;
Simiang Siyiex ang tik vud ka kix,
mai ang kieh kix cawd ka daux tawm taix pui koeng.
3 Yam koe jhawm saux kix,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang pai kix moi;
kix tag sang deeh pon krax brax krax kua heue.
 
4 Aux hoik sax khiox khaw ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux phit lud ka maix;
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux,
mai pai aux moi.
5 Pui koeng ka aux su tix teei lox caox lox caig si briem ka aux ah nin:
nawh sang yum yam mawx,
mai pui tag sang piawm ka nawh khawm uik lie?
6 Pui pa hoek tix jhak ka aux viex rhawm pa caox pa caig;
kix hoek sawg tix khrawm ka lox pa ang mhawm khankix,
khaix an lih krai ku dauh ku dee.
7 Pui pa koeng ka aux lai tawx kaing daong yhaok tix ka paox;
kix keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeinga.
8 Kix ah nin:
nawh pon jhawm saux pa ang pui lai pon pai moi;
nawh ang lai deeh baox pon kaoh khaing ku it tix.
9 bPie khawm paox grawm pa ting meed yiex paox tix,
pa hoik sawm priex nyiex aux,
nawh kawx teei caong tix tiied ka aux heue.
 
10 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux,
kieh aux brax kua,
son sang pon pawk bix.
11 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh pui koeng puing pex ka aux,
aux tom tawng grawng muh maix ka aux.
12 Maix tag teem coi ka aux,
mawh kheu singax singeh aux;
maix sang kieh aux cung ka plak sivoe ngai tix leeing ju heue.
 
13 cKieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pon krax pa yaog yaw heue!
Kieh nawh pon krax pa yaog yaw tix ju hoik tix ju heue!
 
A-meung!
