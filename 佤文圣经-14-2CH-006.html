Gum lox pa sidiex Saw-law-meung kawn pui
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 8.12–21)
6
Yam an,
Saw-law-meung rawg buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix but rhawm tix sang paung ot ka daux pa pu ceep pai awm;
2 aux hoik sum nyiex buan pa mhawm son maix tix lhang,
son sang kieh mawh nyiex dee ot maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
3 Pui hun Yi-seu-liex cung dee an khawm uik.
Simiang Saw-law-meung veeh tix hu plak in,
ot ka plak sivoe pui hun,
mai rawg buan ka Siyiex son kix.
4 aNawh ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex!
Nawh hoik giex kaux ka lox pa ah tix ka keeing aux Ta-wue;
nawh hoik ah nin ka Ta-wue:
5 ‘Jauh tom viex aux pui cie tix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
aux ang sax laug ka veng Yi-seu-liex tix dauh kawx son sang sum ka nyiex buan dee krub ka aux.
Khaix an,
aux kawx ang sax laug pui tix kaux kawx son sang mawh simiang pa thung ka pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
6 Viang mawh nan yam in,
aux hoik laug ka Yie-lu-sa-leung lih mawh dee krub ka aux,
khaix an mai hoik laug maix pa mawh Ta-wue mawh cao pa kuang ka piang pui cie aux heue.’ ”
7 Saw-law-meung ca nied tix ah nin:
“Keeing aux Ta-wue keud tix sang sum ka nyiex buan,
son sang kieh mawh dee krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
8 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix koe rhawm tix sang sum nyiex buan son aux,
hoik mawh pa mhawm;
9 viang mawh nan,
maix ang pon tix sum,
mawh ngie kawn simex maix,
pa mawh kawn lih maix taug pon tix sum nyiex buan aux khaing heue.’
10 “Yam in Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh lih ka lox pa ap tix.
Aux plud keeing tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
khaix an mai hoik sum ka nyiex buan dee krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
11 Aux hoik deuh teug sijax mukheu ka daux nyiex buan,
ka daux teug hoik deuh khlawp simaox sijax mukheu daux;
sijax mukheu in hoik mawh pa yuh Simiang Siyiex tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
Lox rawg buan Saw-law-meung
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 8.22–53)
12 Khaix an,
Saw-law-meung kieh pui hun Yi-seu-liex yaox tix yam hu cung ka sivoe khu dee tan Simiang Siyiex,
nawh yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn rawg buan. (
13 Saw-law-meung hoik kieh pui yuh glawk bawng tawng tix mu deuh ot sigreung biang brawk,
krax lang koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing,
krax vieh koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing,
krax lhaong koe tix kongjhee mai loe kongchuing.
Saw-law-meung cung ka piang glawk bawng an,
grux ngawng tix ka dee kok chix yaox pui ka khawm uik,
mai yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn ka rao ma.)
14 Nawh rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
greem rao ma piang haktiex ang koe siyiex pa pon tiag tix tau mai maix!
Maix hoik giex kaux ka sijax mukheu,
pa hoik yuh maix tau mai pui cie tix:
yam nghiet kix ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
maix tag caw tix jhi ka krax muh tix lih.
15 Maix hoik giex kaux ka lox pa hoik ah tix ka keeing aux Ta-wue,
hoek yam in singaix,
gum lox in hoik lih tix mawh ku gum heue.
16 b“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
yam in aux khiox khaw maix kieh lox pa hoik ah maix tix deuh ka keeing aux tang ceu khankix lih tix mawh:
maix hoik krai ka nawh,
phan doeh ceu cad nawh priex rhawm nghiet ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
awm hoik nghiet maix ka nawh yuh nan,
tag sang koe pui pa yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh gum lox pa hoik ah maix tix deuh ka siliex maix,
pa mawh keeing aux Ta-wue lih tix mawh heue.
18 c“Siyiex eue,
maix rub sang chix ot ka piang haktiex,
ka si nax kawn pui lie?
Viang hoik mawh daong rao ma pa ang koe dee die,
mai ang doh kub maix ot,
nyiex buan pa hoik sum aux doh nie sang kub maix ot yuh ka mawx lie?
19 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
aux mawh siliex maix;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox buan rawg aux,
nghiet ka lox khiox khaw aux plak sivoe ngai maix liak.
20 dKhiox khaw maix kia ngien ka nyiex buan in ku singaix ku sawm;
maix hoik sax ah in hoik mawh pa laug maix tix kieh mawh dee krub ka maix.
Yam rawg aux buan ka nyiex buan in,
maix nghiet ka liak.
21 Yam rawg siliex maix mai pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex buan ka dee in,
khiox khaw maix nghiet khaing rao ma,
mai pluat ploi ka phi map yix heue.
22 “Phan koe pui pa deuh phi map phit lud ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
mai viex nawh hoek ka nyiex buan in,
pa mawh sivoe khu dee tan maix,
tiex tu krai ang tix mawh pui ang koe dee lud.
23 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg yix khaing rao ma,
mai prawng son siliex maix:
pa koe krax phit lud,
tag hawt tix pawk bix awm pa khaw nawh tix kham;
pui ang koe dee lud,
tag hawt tix prawng awm ang nawh mawh pui ang koe dee lud heue.
24 “Phan pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex kheu phit lud ka maix,
mai pui koeng pon pex ka kix,
khaix an kix deeh veeh tix hoek ka maix,
krai doh keud ka maix,
mai hoek ka nyiex buan in viex rhawm tiiem khiox khaw maix pluat ploi phi map tix,
25 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox kix khaing rao ma.
Khaix an,
baox pluat ploi ka phi map pui cie maix,
viex kix deeh ing ka haktiex pa hoik tawx maix ka kix mai ka tax du kix heue.
26 “Phan koe pui cie maix phit lud ka maix,
mai kieh rao ma ang lai tawx rawm lhiex,
khaix an,
kix deeh loh rhawm tix,
krai doh keud ka maix,
mai viex rhawm tiiem veeh tix hoek ka nyiex buan in,
rawg buan ka maix,
27 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix khaing nyiex rao ma.
Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi phi map siliex maix mai pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex,
sidiex gau kix hu ka daux krax rauh krax see.
Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix tawx rawm lhiex lih ka piang haktiex cie maix,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik tawx maix mawh sin ngao pui cie maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
28 “Phan ka daux meung lih koe sibhawm praix,
jhawm saux preng,
bhaung pa kroh yuh praix klawng,
pa moh laux,
viag vua,
sido,
phan ang mawh nan pui koeng lai lawm pui cie maix,
mai si pup jhawm saux krax laux krax leeing,
29 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg buan kix.
Phan ka daux pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex,
pa koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm nawh tete,
tawx taix tix khawm ra khawn rawg buan ka nyiex buan in,
30 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg buan kix,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix khaing nyiex rao ma,
pluat ploi ka kix,
mai teem coi ka kix.
Mawh ngie maix pa tawng ka daux rhawm pui khaing.
Khiox khaw maix hawt tix kia kix awm pa khaw pui ku kaux tix pon,
31 son sang lhat graung yung yi pui cie maix,
nghiet ka maix ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap maix tix tawx ka tax du yix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
32 “Phan koe pui tang ceu tang cad pa paung ot dee singai kheu son ceu kaux pa ting min maix,
taix pa ting riang maix,
plawng taix pa taoh teem maix ku bawg ku yam,
mai hoek tix rawg buan ka nyiex buan in,
33 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg nawh.
Khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg nawh khaing nyiex rao ma,
mai hawt tix yuh awm lox pa khiox khaw nawh ka maix,
son sang kieh pui khawm bee ma tawng maix,
lhat graung yung yi ka maix,
awm pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex yuh nan,
tawng ka nyiex buan pa hoik sum aux in mawh dee krub ka maix heue.
34 “Phan koe pui cie maix hawt tix yuh gum lox tawm maix,
hu puing pui koeng ka daux bang naing,
viang kix ot dee mawx ang ah,
mai veeh ngai tix ka plak veng pa hoik laug maix in,
mai rawg buan ka nyiex buan in pa hoik sum aux son maix,
35 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix liak.
Khiox khaw maix nghiet lox buan rawg kix khaing rao ma,
mai kieh kix puing pex heue.
36 “Phan koe pui cie maix phit lud ka maix,
(ang koe pui pa ang yuh phi map,)
ka daux pa song rhawm maix,
maix kieh pui koeng pon pex ka kix,
mai giex laung kix hu ka haktiex tang dauh,
viang hoik mawh haktiex pa kied singai an kawx,
37 khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg pui cie maix buan.
Phan kix deeh loh rhawm rhi tix ka dee an,
mai khiox khaw ka maix,
krai doh ka phi map tix pa lang teung caox,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix buan.
38 Phan kix loh rhawm rhi tix tete ka daux haktiex an,
mai veeh tix rawg buan ka maix,
ka daux haktiex pa hoik tawx maix ka tax du yix in,
veeh tix rawg buan ka veng pa hoik laug maix in,
mai ka nyiex buan in pa hoik sum aux son maix,
39 khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa rawg kix.
Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa rawg kix khaing nyiex dee ot maix rao ma,
caox rhawm ka kix,
mai pluat ploi ka phi map pui cie maix khawm uik heue.
40 “Siyiex aux eue,
yam in khiox khaw maix jhak,
daong yhaok maix nghiet ka krax pa rawg yix buan dee in ka maix.
41 eSimiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix yaog tix kaoh,
awm laig teug sijax mukheu pa ting siawp sibrawm maix tau ka daux nyiex buan,
mai ot dee an leeing ju.
Khiox khaw maix tawx buan ka can kaing pa yuh cao giex kaing li maix khankix khawm uik;
kieh can pui cie maix khawm uik gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu pa mhawm pa mhiem maix.
42 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex eue,
paw vi tik vud ka simiang pa hoik laug maix.
Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka krax muh pa hoik tawx tix ka siliex maix Ta-wue heue.f”
