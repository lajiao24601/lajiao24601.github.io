Lai Yaw-su-yax
Krai dawt
Khaix hoik vung viang ka daux haktiex tom nie num, pui Yi-seu-liex taug yaox tix laig ka daux haktiex pa ap ka kix. Lai Yaw-su-yax mawh tiiem grawng pa toe khaix Maw-si, Yaw-su-yax thung pui Yi-seu-liex laig ka daux haktiex Cia-nang. Kaing pa ting mawh khawm tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang, puing pex ka veng Yie-li-keu, veng Ai, khawm pa cox yuh Siyiex sijax mukheu tau mai kawn praix kix. Phuk lai in pa kieh pui tawng mawh simin ting tix ngax pon simin iak kao phuan: “In singaix caw tix kieh sidawng, pa caw pex tix yung yi mawh mawx, phan mawh aux mai daux nyiex ma aux, yix tag caw tix yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Krai kaing
1. Pon kuang ka Cia-nang (1.1–12.24)
2. Guah ka haktiex (13.21–45)
(1) Haktiex plak lih singaix Yaw-tang (13.1–33)
(2) Haktiex plak klip singaix Yaw-tang (14.1–19.51)
(3) Veng mox (20.1–9)
(4) Veng pui Li-we (21.1–45)
3. Ceu pui ot plak lih singaix deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix (22.1–34)
4. Lox krai Yaw-su-yax sivoe yum tix (23.1–16)
5. Baox yuh sijax mukheu ka haktiex See-ciien (24.1–33)
