Khiox khaw krax pa kia ngien
61
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa kok yo aux;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa rawg aux buan.
2 Yam singai aux khaing nyiex ma tix,
rhawm rhi aux ang nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai aux khiox khaw ka maix.
Khiox khaw maix thung aux hoek nyiex dee mox pa nyawm ot,
3 kheu mawh maix cao pa kia ngien ka aux,
mawh tab pa tieb tie ka pui koeng heue.
 
4 Khiox khaw maix cu tix kieh aux paung ot daux nyiex gox bang maix tom si dauh ju tix;
kieh aux pon krax pa kia ngien ka greem preeik maix.
5 Siyiex eue,
maix hoik mhong ka lox pa hoik ah aux tix deuh;
maix tawx sin ngao pa khaw pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix tix pon tawx ka aux heue.
 
6 Khiox khaw maix thiem thie krax pa lang ju simiang;
khiox khaw maix kieh nawh lang ju pu rhang.
7 Siyiex eue,
kieh nawh koe siawp sibrawm leeing ju ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
kieh maix tawx krax muh tix pa ang chix tieh loh,
mai pa kaux sijax tix kia ngien ka nawh,
 
8 yuh nin,
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix leeing ju leeing phan;
aux tag sang ap pong tix tawx lox pa hoik ap tix deuh ka maix ku ngaix heue.
