Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk Tix mawh lox lai pa kah Pao-law grawh grawng krax ot krax koe kawn Khrit, mai grawh grawng krax yum rhawm kix; grawh grawng in lih tix mawh bawg yam sum nawh ka ceu cao Keu-ling-to.
Yam an, Keu-ling-to mawh veng ting ka Si-lax tix mu, pa mawh veng ting dee ot simiang Law-ma tix kuang ka haktiex Ya-kai-yax. Veng in kok mawh dee yuh pui laih lai, mai dee gau pui krax lhak lhiao; viang mawh nan, been ka daux veng in caox caig, kaing ceu pa mhawm iak hu, kawn pui krub ka been li tom nie ceu heue.
Pao-law kied simi ka grawng pa guah ceu cao tix, mai ka grawng hoik laux leeing kaing ceu pa mhawm ka daux ceu cao. Nawh kawx hoik krai ka grawng krax pa phit vao, mai ka grawng mex moeng, rhawm rhi, pa sum ka ceu cao, khawng phak buan son Khuan Singax Singeh, mai grawng pa kaoh im. Phan mawh son kaing ceu in kix, nawh hoik hawt tix krai sidawng awm pa tiiem phuk lai sigang khri yuh nan.
“Lox rhax krax muh” (simin ting kao loe) mawh ka daux lox lai tiiem Pao-law pa blawn ting ceu, mai blawn muh pui tix phat ka heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–9)
2. Cub caw ka daux ceu cao (1.10–4.21)
3. Kaing pa phit vao mai krax ot krax koe (5.1–7.40)
4. Kawn Khrit mai pui laig li tang ceu (8.1–11.1)
5. Grawh grawng ceu cao mai krax krub (11.2–14.40)
6. Khrit mai pa deeh kaoh im kawn Khrit (15.1–58)
7. Teem coi ka kawn Khrit Yo-thaix (16.1–4)
8. Kaing tang kaux mai gum lox pa nghoik (16.1–4)
