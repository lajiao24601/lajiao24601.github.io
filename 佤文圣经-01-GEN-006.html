Kawn pui pa caox pa caig
6
aYam jauh kawn pui tix lih haoh hun,
pung prang ka piang haktiex,
mai kix koe kawn bun kawn simex tom nie,
2 “kawn Siyiexb” tang kaux khankix yaox mhawm grang kawn bun kawn pui,
mai kix tang laug tix teei kix mawh moeng tix.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kheu mawh kawn pui nex nham,
khuan aux tag ang lai ot ka daux kix leeing ju,
kheu nan,
krax im ju kix tag ang lai lang khaing tix yieh tix nga num.”
4 cJauh yam an,
pui ting lhaong lih koe ka piang haktiex,
kix mawh doeh “kawn Siyiex” tau mai bun kawn pui;
kix mawh kawn pui pa ao min mai pa ting riang tom yam an heue.
5 dSimiang Siyiex yaox pa caox pa caig yuh kawn pui hoik ting lhaong ka piang haktiex,
mai krax keud kix mawh pa ang mhawm,
6 tom klih ka phan yuh tix kawn pui piang haktiex.
Nawh kied laux rhawm,
7 ah nin:
“Aux sang yuh can pa koe ka piang haktiex in laux leeing,
jauh ka kawn pui hoek ka to sat,
viag vua,
khawm sim saik,
mawh kheu kied klih aux ka hoik phan tix khawng brie in kix.”
8 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex kied muh ka Naw-yax.
Naw-yax
9-10 eIn plak greem mawh grawng kaing Naw-yax:
Naw-yax koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Sang,
Hang,
mai Ya-fu.
Naw-yax mawh pui mhawm ka daux singaix bawg yam an,
mawh pui grum naog pa hu tau mai Siyiex.
11 Kawn pui pa deuk hoik mawh pa caox caik sivoe ngai Siyiex,
ka piang haktiex hoik naog ka ceu pa lang teung caox.
12 Siyiex jhak ka haktiex,
yaox ka laux leeing seeing brai haktiex,
kawn pui khankix kawx hoik hu ka daux krax pa caox caig.
13 Siyiex ah nin ka Naw-yax:
“Aux hoik tat tix sang pauh kawn pui,
mawh kheu hoik naog pa kied lang teung caox piang haktiex jao kix,
aux tag sang yuh kix laux tau mai haktiex.
14 Maix laug tix siao ka khaox pa kied mhawm,
yuh reu tix mu son cao maix,
daux reu tag koe lawg,
mai kraix peeh khaox gix ka plak daux mai plak praix.
15 Plak lang reu kieh koe tix yieh tix ngoe loe kongjhee,
plak vieh kieh koe tix nga ra kongjhee,
mai plak lhaong kieh koe kao loe kongjhee.
16 Reu tag koe daux doh;f
daux doh dex mai braux reu,
lhaong koe tipon pon kongfeeng,
daux reu guah koe loe jan,
pa mawh jan plak greem,
jan sigreung mai jan plak piang,
praok reu tag deuh siviex koe.
17 “Aux sang kieh rawm kheem ka piang haktiex,
son sang yuh pa im ju laux leeing.
Can pa koe piang haktiex tag sang laux leeing uik.
18 Viang mawh nan,
aux sang yuh sijax mukheu mai maix,
maix sang viex kawn simex maix,
moeng maix,
mai mun maix laig daux reu.
19-20 Maix tag viex can khawng brie,
viag vua,
sim saik tix ceu tix brawm,
tix mu mhaing mai tix mu siang viex laig ka daux reu,
son sang priex ka ju kix.
21 Maix tag khrawm priex preem son cao maix,
mai son to sat khawm uik.”
22 gNaw-yax yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Siyiex ka nawh ku ceu heue.
