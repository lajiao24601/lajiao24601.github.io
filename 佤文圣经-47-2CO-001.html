Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk Ra
1
aAux mawh Pao-law;
aux mawh kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit kheu alo Siyiex.
Aux mai pux aik ex Thi-maw-thaix tiiem lai tawx ka ceu cao Siyiex Keu-ling-to,
mai ka can kawn Khrit Ya-kai-yax khawm uik.
2 Kieh keeing ex Siyiex mai Simiang Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son,
mai krax pa nhim ka pex heue!
Pao-law yaog yaw Siyiex
3 Ex tag sang yaog yaw keeing ex Siyiex Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue!
Nawh mawh keeing Siyiex pa grum mai krax muh,
mai mawh num dee kaoh krax pa nyawm rhawm khawm uik nang.
4 Yam ot ex ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi ku ceu,
nawh tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka ex,
mai kieh ex pon tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm pa hoik tawx nawh ka ex mai ka pui pa si pup krax kha rhawm kha rhi ku ceu.
5 Awm hoik kham ex krax pa kha rhawm tau mai Khrit tom nie yuh nan,
ex hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm ka daux Khrit tom nie nang.
6 Phan ex si pup krax pa kha rhawm,
an hoik mawh son sang kieh pex pon krax pa nyawm rhawm,
pon krax taoh;
phan ex hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm,
pex kawx hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm nang,
hoik pon riang rao krax gun yeung ka daux pa si pup ex krax kha rhawm.
7 Krax yum rhawm ka son pex ang sax yung yang tix bawg kawx;
ex hoik tawng,
pex hoik teei tix klawm ka krax pa kha rhawm ex tix blah,
tag sang ot ka daux krax nyawm rhawm pa hoik pon ex nang heue.
8 bPux aik pux o khankix eue,
ex simie pex tawng ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi pa hoik si pup ex ka haktiex Ya-si-yax.
Beeh glawm pa gut piang to kaux ex kied mawh pa ting,
mai kied cien tete,
pie sang simie ex tix klawd ju ang pon.
9 Ex awm pui pa hoik prawng ca pui kieh yum yuh nan.
Grawng kaing ceu pa yuh nin,
hoik kieh ex tawng ang pon tix een rhawm ka to kaux tix,
tag caw een rhawm tix ka Siyiex pa hoik kieh pui yum deeh kaoh im an.
10-11 Nawh hoik sax taoh ex lih khaing yoeh krax yum,
khaix an kawn sang baox taoh ka ex;
ca mawh teei pex krax pa rawg buan teem coi ka ex,
ex hoik yum rhawm grawng sang baox tawx Siyiex in krax taoh.
Yuh nin,
koe pui tom nie hoik rawg buan son ex,
mai kieh ex pon buan son,
tee hoik koe nang pui tom nie pa tag sang yaog yaw Siyiex son ex heue.
Pao-law tieh loh kaing pa keud nawh tix sang yuh
12 Ceu pa hoik yaw ex mawh:
rhawm rhi ex hoik jhi grawng pa yuh ex son pui piang haktiex,
nie mawh grawng pa si doe ex tix tau mai pex,
hoik pon ceu pa singaxc pa hoik tawx Siyiex,
pa viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh.
In hoik mawh riang rao pa kaoh khaing buan son Siyiex,
ang mawh pa kaoh khaing ping nyia pa dex mai cie haktiex.
13-14 Aux tiiem tix tawx lai in ka pex,
hoik mawh ngie son sang kieh pex phat,
mai kieh pex tawng.
Yam in pex hoik tawng ngie tix blah khaing,
khaix in sax aux simie pex grum tawng khawm uik.
Yuh nin,
ka daux singaix bawg yam deeh lih hoek simiang ex Ye-sux,
pex tag sang yaog yaw min plak ngai ex,
awm hoik yaog yaw ex min plak ngai pex yuh nan heue.
15 Kheu hoik yum rhawm tix yuh nin,
aux tom keud tix sang hu vhi ka pex,
mai kieh pex baox thiem thie tix pon krax pa nyawm rhawm tom nie.
16 dYam hu aux ka plak Ma-chi-toeng,
aux ran keud tix sang hu ka pex dee an,
yam deeh hoek aux,
mai deeh baox hu tix bawg,
son sang kieh pex teem tix hu ka Yo-thaix.
17 Krax pa keud tix sang yuh nin,
pui mawx sang pon ne ka aux lie?
Khex mawh krax pa keud aux tix sang yuh,
mawh pa kaoh khaing alo pa bak to kaux aux ka ceu pa mawh mai ceu ang mawh,
ceu pa khaw mai ceu pa ang khaw lie?
18 Aux tiex tu ka Siyiex pa kaux sijax ah nin,
gum lox pa ap aux tix deuh ang bax mawh “pa mawh” mai pa baox mawh “pa ang mawh” yuh nan.
19 eMawh kheu Ye-sux Khrit kawn simex Siyiex pa hoik sidiex Si-la,
Thi-maw-thaix mai aux ka pex,
nawh ang mawh kaw pa mawh mai baox ang mawh yuh nan.
Deeh tieh tix,
nawh mawh ngai lai “pa mawh” Siyiex.
20 Gum lox pa ap Siyiex khawm uik,
hoik lih tix mawh “pa mawh” ka daux to kaux Khrit khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
ex tom ah “A-meung” yaog yaw min plak ngai Siyiex.
21 Siyiex hoik tawx lox pa kaux cong ka maix mai aux,
mai kieh ex ot ka daux Khrit;
nawh hoik guah ex lih tang ot,
22 mai tawk cum tix ka piang to kaux ex,
khaix an tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ot daux rhawm ex,
mai kieh mawh pa tang pa hoik rien khre nawh son ex khawm uik heue.
23 Aux khiox khaw Siyiex mawh pa kham mhai sakse son aux;
phan aux pie lhien,
nawh tag sang ciyang ka aux.
Aux but rhawm tix ang hu yuh son pex ka Keu-ling-to.
24 Yix ang mawh son sang nhie pex yum rhawm ka ceu pa tid tix,
mawh kheu hoik cung pex kaux cong ka daux krax yum rhawm tix.
Yix tieh tix simie tix yuh kaing tau mai pex,
son sang kieh pex koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
