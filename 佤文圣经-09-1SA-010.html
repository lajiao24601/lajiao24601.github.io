10
Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu teei bee sime tix kawng,
hieh ka piang kaing Sao-law,
mai ngheet ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex groi rawm bee sum maix mawh simianga pui cie nawh Yi-seu-liex;
maix tag sang kia ngien ka piang pui cie nawh,
mai taoh kix luat luan khaing can pui pa koeng.
In plak greem hoik mawh mhai sakse pa laug Simiang Siyiex maix mawh simiang ka piang pui cie nawh,
2 khaix hoik lih hu maix khaing aux in singaix,
maix tag sang si pup pui ra kaux ka Siie-sha haktiex Piien-ya-ming,
dex ka si meeig Le-ciiex tan heue.
Kiex tag sang krai ka maix,
la pa sawg maix mai hoik yaox kah.
Keeing maix ang lai khi rhawm son la,
viang mawh nan,
hoik khi rhawm son maix,
mai ca chawk ka pui ang jud ah nin:
‘Kawn simex aux yuh ka mawx lie?’
3 Maix jauh dee an baox nied tix hu plak sivoe,
hoek ka khaox pa singax singeh Tha-pawx dee an,
maix tag sang yaox ka pui loe kaux;
mai kix sang hu ka Paw-theu-lix son sang yuh krax tan ka Siyiex.
Koe pui tix kaux ka daux kix,
viex kawn pex loe mu,
an tix kaux viex ka blauh khao mun loe lon,
ka daux loe kaux viex ka plai tix si ngien hak.
4 Kix tag sang chawk maix ka daux krax pa nhim,
mai tawx blauh khao mun ra lon ka maix;
maix tag rieb ka heue.
5 “Khaix an,
maix tag sang hu ka Ci-pi-yax,
mai hoek ka gawng ot Siyiex;
dee an koe nyiex bang pui Fe-li-see kah.
Ka dee laig maix daux veng,
maix sang si pup cao jhai tix cub pa kaoh khaing khu dee tan ka piang gawng;
mai kix tauh ka kraung,
phlu ka shiao,
tauh ka ting jung mai ting pa koe lauh aliah gawng,
mai ah lox pa lih khaing khuan.
6 Khuan Simiang Siyiex sang laig ot ka daux maix dum mawx dum an;
maix tag sang ah lox pa lih khaing khuan tau mai kix,
awm pa mawh pui tang kaux.
7 Yam lih kaing in kix tix mawh,
maix caw tix yuh awm krax pa thung gau Siyiex maix yuh.
8 Maix tag caw tix ran hoek sivoe aux ka Cie-cia;
mai aux tag sang hoek ka maix dee an,
tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
Maix tag caw tix krox jun dee an aliah singaix;
khaix hoik hoek aux,
aux tag sang krai ka maix grawng kaing pa sang khaw maix tix yuh heue.”
9 Yam hoik lih hu Sao-law khaing Sa-mu-eu,
Siyiex tawx rhawm pa khraox ka Sao-law tix mu.
Sa-mu-eu krai kaing ka nawh ku ceu,
mai hoik lih tix mawh ka daux singaix an heue.
10 Yam hoek Sao-law mai siliex nawh ka Ci-pi-yax,
koe cao jhai tix cub hoek tix rieb ka nawh.
Khuan Siyiex laug ka daux nawh dum mawx dum an,
nawh ah lox pa lih khaing khuan tau mai cao jhai an kix.
11 Pui pa hoik tawng ka Sao-law sivoe an yaox ka pa yuh nawh mai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Kawn simex Ci-see yuh ka mawx lie?
Sao-law hoik lih tix mawh cao jhai nang lie?”
12 bKoe pui haktiex an ah nin tix kaux:
“Phan mawh nan,
pui tang kaux lie?
Keeing kix mawh cao jhai lie?”
In mawh pa kaoh khaing lox been “Sao-law hoik lih tix mawh cao jhai lie?”
13 Khaix hoik ah Sao-law lox pa lih khaing khuan ka dee an,
tom haok ka piang gawng ka nyiex dee krub kix tan heue.
14 Keeing iak Sao-law yaox ka Sao-law mai ka siliex nawh,
mai chawk kiex ah nin:
“Pax kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?”
Sao-law pawk lox ah nin:
“Yiex hu sawg la heue!
Ang yaox,
yiex tom hu ka Sa-mu-eu.”
15 Keeing iak Sao-law chawk ah nin:
“Nawh krai pa tix ka pax lie?”
16 Sao-law pawk ah nin:
“Nawh krai ka yiex,
hoik yaox ka la heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
Sao-law ang bax krai keeing iak tix mhong grawng lox pa krai Sa-mu-eu ka nawh son sang kieh nawh mawh simiang heue.
Yaog Sao-law mawh simiang
17 Sa-mu-eu khrawm kawn pui kum kawng ka Mi-see-pa,
18 ah nin ka kix:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin,
nawh viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
mai taoh pex luat luan khaing pui Ai-cix mai pui pa gut kheem ka pex khawm uik.
19 Nawh mawh Siyiex pex,
Siyiex pa hoik taoh pex luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing mai krax kha rhawm kha rhi khawm uik.
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux in singaix,
pex hoik tik vud ka nawh,
mai nhie aux yaox simiang tix kaux son pex.
Yam in pex caw tix hawt ka ceu pui tix mai ceu pui tix,
mai khrawm dia tix hawt paox ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex heue.”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu nhie ku ceu pui hoek dex sivoe ngai;
Simiang Siyiex laug ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming.
21 Sa-mu-eu nhie cub nyiex ceu pui Piien-ya-ming hoek dex sivoe ngai,
mai laug ka cub nyiex Ma-theu-lix;
khaix an cub nyiex Ma-theu-lix hoek dex sivoec ngai,
mai laug ka Sao-law kawn simex Ci-see.
Pui haoh pui hun sawg ka Sao-law,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang yaox ka nawh.
22 Kix khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kawn koe ang koe pui tang kaux lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox:
“Sao-law ot plak an,
mai mox tix ot plak khaix pum kraung viex pex heue.”
23 Koe pui tix kaux to tix hu bluh ka Sao-law lih,
mai viex nawh hoek ka sivoe ngai pui haoh pui hun;
nawh kawn lhaong khaing pui khawm uik been tix kaing.
24 Sa-mu-eu ah nin ka kawn pui:
“In hoik mawh pui pa laug Simiang Siyiex!
Ka daux ex ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa awm nawh heue.”
Pui hun kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang ex lang ju heue!”
25 Sa-mu-eu krai sidawng kaing pa caw simiang tix sang klawm,
mai siawp sibrawm pa koe nawh ka kawn pui,
mai tiiem ot ka daux lai,
deuh ot ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an deeh nhie pui ku kaux tang deeh ing ka nyiex tix.
26 Sao-law deeh ing ka nyiex Ci-pi-yax heue;
koe pui kua tix blah pa hoik laig gum lox Siyiex ot daux rhawm khankix,
hu tau mai Sao-law.
27 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui pa cau nyhau khankix tix blah ah nin:
“Pui in sang pon taoh ka ex lie?”
Kix ang een Sao-law koe ceu,
khawng phak kawx ang tawx ka nawh.
Viang mawh nan Sao-law ang ah tid tix heue.
