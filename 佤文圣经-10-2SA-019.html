Yaw-ya khu rhawm Ta-wue
19
Koe pui krai Yaw-ya mhong,
simiang Ta-wue laux rhawm yiiem son Ya-sa-long,
2 krax gaux rhawm pui naing Ta-wue khawm uik ka daux singaix an kheu hoik puing pex kix,
mai tieh tix lih mawh krax laux rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik mhong pui haoh pui hun laux rhawm simiang son kawn tix.
3 Kix yien yawg mai ang nye yam deeh ing daux veng,
awm pui naing pa ang pex pa hoek khaing daux bang naing,
pa koe krax kaig yuh nan.
4 Simiang tawb kaing tix,
mai ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting ah nin:
“Eue,
kawn eue!
Kawn eue!
Ya-sa-long kawn eue!”
5 Yaw-ya laig yaox simiang ka daux nyiex ot simiang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Ka daux in singaix,
maix hoik map nai ka pui naing cie maix,
pa mawh pui pa hoik taoh ju maix,
mai ju kawn simex kawn bun maix,
mai ju moeng iak maix khankix.
6 Maix hoik koeng ka pui pa muh tix an,
mai muh ka pui pa koeng ka tix an.
Maix ang een simiang naing pa ot greem tix,
mai pui naing tix koe ceu.
Aux jhak tix yaox,
phan Ya-sa-long kawn im ka daux in singaix,
mai Yix hoik yum uik,
maix tag sang gaux rhawm heue.
7 Yam in maix lih khu rhawm pui naing cie maix khaing heue!
Aux tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
phan maix ang yuh nin,
hoek pa sax pon ngawb,
mai tag ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa ot tau mai maix tin.
In tag sang lih tix mawh krax laux krax leeing pa ting,
pa hoik si pup maix ka daux ju tix.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang yaog tix kaoh,
mai lih hu ngawm ka praok diang siviex veng.
Pui naing cie nawh mhong hoek nawh dee an,
kix tom khawm tix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.
Ta-wue deeh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
Yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux hoik tix kaux tang to tix deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix.
9 Pui khawm meung ku dauh lawh ka paox ah nin:
“Ta-wue hoik taoh ex luat luan khaing pui pa koeng.
Nawh hoik taoh ex luat luan khaing pui Fe-li-see.
Viang mawh nan yam in,
nawh hoik lih hu khaing meung ot tix,
jao to mox nawh tix khaing Ya-sa-long.
10 Ex hoik sum Ya-sa-long mawh simiang,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik yum daux naing.
Phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx ex lai ang nied tix hu rieb simiang Ta-wue hoek lie?”
11 Simiang Ta-wue ploi Sa-tu mai cao giex kaing li Ya-pi-ya-tha hu chawk kuad song pui Yo-tax khankix ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai lao grawng sang kok simiang deeh hoek ka nyiex haw,
yuh ka mawx pex lai dah khaix kix lie?
12 Pex hoik mawh pui nyiex pui ma aux,
hoik mawh nex nham to kaux aux,
yuh ka mawx pex lai kawn sang krox jun khaix paox,
tag sang kok simiang hoek lie?”
13 Ta-wue kawx nhie nang kix hu krai ka Ya-ma-sha ah nin:
“Maix hoik mawh nex nham aux.
Jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai,
aux tag sang mawh cao kaing pa bung pui naing maix,
tang Yaw-ya.
Phan aux ang yuh nin,
kieh Siyiex pauh ka aux!”
14 Lox krai Ta-wue hoik sidaing khaw ka rhawm pui Yo-tax khawm uik,
kix tom tawx lox ka Ta-wue,
mai nhie nawh mai siliex nawh khankix deeh hoek heue.
15 Yam ing kix daux krax,
Ta-wue hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai si pup ka pui Yo-tax.
Pui Yo-tax koe krax gaux rhawm tix rieb nawh tiang rawm klawng ka Cie-cia.
16 aYam an,
See-me kawn simex Ci-la pui Piien-ya-ming,
pa kaoh khaing veng Pa-hu-ling kawx nied tix hu ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang rieb ka simiang Ta-wue.
17 Nawh viex ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming tix rheng kaux hoek tau mai tix.
Kawn koe Si-pa siliex cub nyiex Sao-law,
viex nang kawn simex tix kao phuan mai siliex tix tix nga kaux hoek.
Kix kawn hoek sivoe simiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
18 Kix tiang ka rawm klawngb,
mai tawx pui nyiex pui ma simiang tiang rawm klawng,
khaix an hawt tix yuh ka lox pa hoik krai simiang ka daux kaing ku ceu heue.
Ta-wue pluat ploi ka See-me
Yam sang tiang simiang ka rawm klawng,
See-me gup ka sivoe ngai nawh,
19 mai ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi phi map pa hoik yuh aux yam lih hu maix khaing daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Khiox khaw maix paw vi baox deuh phi map piang aux jao in,
mai paw vi deeh si kaw ka kaing an heue.
20 Simiang eue,
aux tawng grawng hoik koe phi map tix,
mawh kheu nan daux in singaix,
ka daux ceu pui pa ot plak laong,
mawh aux pa jauh tix rieb ka simiang heue.”
21 Kawn simex Si-lu-ya pa kaux Ya-pi-sai ah nin:
“Khaw tix pauh See-me yum,
mawh kheu hoik jhax lawg nawh ka simiang pa laug Simiang Siyiex heue.”
22 Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue ah nin ka Ya-pi-sai mai pux ke nawh Yaw-ya:
“Mawh mawx pa hoik mhaing ka lox pex lie?
Pex lai sang kieh krax pa nyhap hoek piang aux lie?
Yam in aux hoik mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Daux in singaix,
mai ang lai kieh koe pui Yi-seu-liex pa sang pauh yum tix kaux kawx.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang ah nin ka See-me:
“Aux ang doh kieh maix yum heue.”
Ta-wue chix kia ngien ka Mi-fe-paw-seu
24 cMi-fe-paw-seu kawn saux Sao-law,
hoek tix rieb ka simiang Ta-wue nang.
Jauh yam lih simiang khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tom hoek puing pex tix khaing naing,
nawh ang sax yaox tix khoek ka caong tix,
klao ka hauk jheeh tix,
si dauh ka kraung tix.
25 Yam hoekd Mi-fe-paw-seu ka simiang daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
simiang chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mi-fe-paw-seu,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang hu tau mai aux lie?”
26 Mi-fe-paw-seu pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng,
maix hoik tawng aux mawh pui laux meung.
Aux hoik tawx lox nhie siliex tix rien ka la pa brug tix,
son sang brug aux la hawt khaix Vawng,
viang mawh nan,
siliex aux coh ka aux.
27 Nawh lhien lox salao aux ka sivoe Vawng.
Viang mawh nan,
Vawng hoik awm kawn cau Siyiex yuh nan,
kheu nan khiox khaw Vawng hawt alo maix yuh heue!
28 Can pui nyiex pui ma aux khawm uik,
hoik mawh pa khaw tix yum,
viang mawh nan,
Vawng baox thiem thie ka buan son,
mai kieh aux pon yaox tix sawm rheep tau mai simiang,
aux kawn sang koe akhuang ceu pa tix mai baox mhaing Vawng tawx buan son ka tix lie?”
29 Simiang ah nin:
“Kawn krai maix ka kaing in kix yuh pa tix lie?
Aux hoik but rhawm tix,
maix chix tix pon nang sin ngao cie Sao-law tau mai Si-pa heue.”
30 Mi-fe-paw-seu ah nin:
“Sin ngao in kix khawm uik tawx ka Si-pa heue.
Vawng maix deeh ing mhawm mhawm ka nyiex haw ot tix,
mai aux ang lo ceu pa tid tix heue.”
Ta-wue chix kia ngien ka Pa-si-lai
31 ePa-si-lai pui Ci-liex kaoh lih hoek khaing Law-ci-ling,
mai rieb simiang tiang ka rawm klawng.
32 Nawh hoik mawh kuad pa koe ju tix daix num.
Nawh mawh pui pa mi pa koe tix kaux.
Yam ot simiang ka daux Ma-ha-nian,
nawh hoik tawx simiang ih ka priex preem.
33 Simiang ah nin ka Pa-si-lai:
“Maix hu tau mai aux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue,
aux tag sang kia ngien ka maix.”
34 Viang mawh nan,
Pa-si-lai ah nin:
“Aux keud tix ang lai yaox tix im ju miex greeng tix,
yuh ka mawx lai kawn sang lih hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tau mai Vawng lie?
35 Yam in aux hoik koe ju tix daix num,
kaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
ang lai chix kieh aux koe rhawm kah.
Aux ih aux nyaux mai ang lai tawng jhawm.
Aux kawx ang lai chix mhong ka lox rhax simex mai bun pui pa kied chix rhax lai.
Aux tee rien tix mawh beeh glawm ka Vawng khaing.
36 Aux ang keung tix pon buan son pa ting yuh nin khaing Vawng.
Aux sang hu ngie tix baux krax tau mai Vawng,
khaix hoik tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
37 khaix an,
kieh aux deeh ing ka nyiex,
mai yaox tix yum ot ka praok si meeig miex keeing tix heue.
In mawh kawn simex aux Cing-hang.
Khiox khaw Vawng viex nawh hu,
mai kia nawh awm pa khaw ka rhawm maix heue.”
38 Simiang pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux tag sang viex nawh hu tau mai tix,
mai kia nawh awm lox pa mhaing maix yuh nan.
Maix koe lox ceu pa rawg ceu pa tix ang ah,
aux tag sang cu ka heue.”
39 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue mai can pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix,
hoik tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Simiang ngheet ka Pa-si-lai,
mai nawb buan piang nawh,
Pa-si-lai tom deeh ing ka nyiex heue.
Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex thung thiang son simiang
40 Simiang tiang ka rawm klawng,
can pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex been tix plak gaux rhawm tix rieb ka nawh.
Simiang hoek bleeh ka Cie-cia.
Cing-hang ot tau mai simiang.
41 Khaix an,
can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoek bleeh ka simiang,
mai ah nin ka simiang:
“Vawng,
yuh ka mawx pux aik ex pui Yo-tax,
kix cie tix koe akhuang pa briex mox tix hu tawx simiang,
pui nyiex ma simiang,
mai pui naing khankix tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang lie?”
42 Pui Yo-tax pawk ah nin:
“Yix yuh nin kheu hoik mawh simiang pui pa dex dawt tau mai yix.
Yuh ka mawx pex lai yuh tix saux rhawm kah lie?
Simiang ang doh baox tawx yix ih priex preem,
mai ka ceu pa tid tix kawx ang baox tawx ka yix.”
43 Pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Viang pex hoik mawh pa dex dawt tau mai simiang,
krax muh yix mai simiang Ta-wue kawn lheu khaing pex kao son.
Yuh ka mawx pex lai ang een yix koe ceu?
Paw vi piawm,
mawh yix pa jauh tix kum kawng paox tix kok simiang hoek heue.”
Kix khawm ra plak simah paox tix,
viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi pui Yo-tax kawn lheu kiien khaing pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
