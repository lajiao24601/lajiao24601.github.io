Kawn cau Siyiex mai phuk lai iak
10
Aux baox yaox ka kawn cau Siyiex pa ting riang tix kaux lih hoek khaing rao ma.
Nawh ceep ka pai awm,
ka piang kaing nawh mawh si yawng mukheu tix nyaing;
pak ngai nawh awm singaix,
gawng caong nawh awm rong ngu.
2 Taix nawh giex ka phuk lai iak pa plak tix.
Caong khawn dawm nawh dig ka rawng sigrit sigra,
caong khawn goix nawh dig ka haktiex.
3 Nawh kok yo lox si num,
awm sivai sang si pa graox;
yam kok yo nawh taug mawh lox luah tuh sah aliah taix.
4 Yam taug sang tiiem aux lih ka lox pa tuh sah in aliah taix,
koe lox pa lih hoek khaing rao ma ah nin:
“Lox pa tuh sah aliah taix,
maix tag caw tix priex glip mhawm mhawm,
ang pon tix tiiem lih heue!”
5 aKhaix hoik luan kaing in,
kawn cau Siyiex an pa hoik yaox aux cung ka piang rawm mai ka piang tiex,
yaog taix khawn dawm tix,
6 tiex tu ka rao ma,
haktiex,
rawm sigrit sigra,
mai ka Siyiex pa im leeing ju pa phan yuh ku ceu khawm ah nin:
“Ang lai baox lhax hoek heue!
7 Yam phlu kawn cau Siyiex ka daux aliah kaux teet tawng,
Siyiex taug sang yuh lih ka ceu pa raux pa mox pa hoik krai pao nawh ka siliex tix,
pa mawh cao jhai khankix heue.”
8 bKhaix an,
aux mhong lox pa kaoh khaing rao ma sivoe mai krai ka aux ah nin:
“Maix hu,
teei ka phuk lai pa plak tix daux tawm taix kawn cau Siyiex pa cung ka piang rawm sigrit sigra,
mai ka piang tiex hoek heue.”
9 Aux hu,
khiox khaw kawn cau Siyiex an tawx phuk lai iak ka aux.
Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Teei hu,
bleet laig heue!
Vaig tu maix taug sang mhong song,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux jheeh hoik tie awm rawm hia yuh nan.”
10 Aux rieb phuk lai iak an khaing daux tawm taix nawh,
ih kah,
daux jheeh aux rug hoik tie awm rawm hia yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
krox jun aux bleet laig ha,
vaig tu aux tom taug mhong song heue.
11 Kawn cau Siyiex baox krai ka aux ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix baox krai pao gum lox jhai pa krai Siyiex ka pui ku ceu,
ku meung,
mai ka lox si kah ku ceu mai simiang khankix heue.”
