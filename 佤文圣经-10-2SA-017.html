Hu-sai yuh laux ka krax pa tieh vheen Ya-si-to-fu
17
Ya-si-to-fu ah nin ka Ya-sa-long:
“Maix cu aux laug pui tix mheen mai ra rheng kaux.
Ka daux bo in aux sang hu leue ka Ta-wue.
2 Aux sang jawm tix puing nawh yam taug maox iak riang nawh.
Nawh rien tix yaoh yee lhat graung,
mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh tag sang yaog tix to.
Aux sang pauh ngie ka simiang tix kaux khaing,
3 mai viex pui naing nawh hawt rhawm maix,
awm bun pa yuh ka paox sang hoek ka mex tix yuh nan.
Maix pauh ngie tix kaux khainga,
mai pui tang kaux khankix tag yaox tix mhawm ot mhawm koe.”
4 Ya-sa-long mai kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hoik khaw rhawm ka lox nawh in heue.
5 Ya-sa-long ah nin:
“Hu kok Hu-sai pa mawh pui Ya-ci hoek,
kieh ex nghiet tix yaox ka lox nawh.”
6 Hu-sai hoek,
Ya-sa-long ah nin ka nawh:
“Ya-si-to-fu tawx rhawm keud tix ka yix yuh nin.
Mawh sang khaw ex tix hawt awm lox pa krai nawh lie?
Phan maix ang cu,
maix koe rhawm keud tix sang kieh ex yuh ka mawx lie?”
7 Hu-sai ah nin:
“Rhawm keud Ya-si-to-fu bawg in ang mhawm.
8 Maix hoik tawng keeing maix mai pui naing cie nawh khankix hoik mawh pui pa meed kua ka naing.
Kix kied kua awm miex krih pa hoik pon tud pui ka kawn tix yuh nan.
Keeing maix hoik mawh pui naing pa kub rhawm keud grawng sang puing naing tix kaux,
yam pon sawm nawh ang it tau mai pui naing.
9 Yam in keud nawh hoik mox tix ot daux dawng rang,
phan ang mawh nan hoik mox tix ot tang dauh tang dee.
Phan nawh yaog tix puing ka pui naing cie maix,
pui pa mhong rien tix ce pui cie maix ang pex.
10 Yam an,
viang hoik mawh pui pa kua awm sivai sang si kawx tag kied yaoh yee.
Mawh kheu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik mawx pa ang tawng keeing maix mawh pui vao,
mai pui naing cie nawh kawx hoik mawh nang pui pa mhawm puing naing khawm uik.
11 “Mawh kheu nin,
aux simie maix khrawm can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik ot tix dauh khaing,
jauh ka Tang plak laong haktiex,
tom hoek ka Piie-see-pa plak seh haktiex,
haoh hun awm mhaik ka praok rawm ting yuh nan,
maix mawh cao pa thung kix hu puing ka naing.
12 Viang Ta-wue ot dee mawx ang ah,
ex tag sang pon tix yaox ka nawh.
Khaix an,
ex yaox tix puing ka nawh.
Yuh nin,
tom hoik awm rawm ciex pa jauh piang tiex,
nawh mai pui cie nawh tag ang yaox tix luat luan pie tix kaux kawx heue.
13 Phan nawh deeh laig ka daux veng,
pui ex plak in tom viex maox hu bluh veng anb laig daux ngawd.
Pie simaox tix lon mai tag ang lai kieh ot ka piang gawng heue.”
14 Ya-sa-long mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik ah nin:
“Rhawm keud Hu-sai kawn lheu mhawm khaing rhawm keud Ya-si-to-fu.”
Simiang Siyiex but tix ang kieh kix siao rhawm keud Ya-si-to-fu pa mhawm,
son sang kieh krax laux leeing lih hoek ka piang to kaux Ya-sa-long heue.
Ta-wue pon krax ne raw mai yaox tix luat luan
15 Hu-sai krai Sa-tu mai Ya-pi-ya-tha cao giex kaing li mhong lox pa hoik krai nawh ka Ya-sa-long mai kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
khawm lox pa hoik krai Ya-si-to-fu kawx hoik krai ka kix.
16 Hu-sai baox ah nin:
“Nied ploi kawn cau hu krai ka Ta-wue,
ka daux bo in paw vi it daux tiag ka dee diang,
caw tix khan tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Yuh nin,
nawh mai pui cie nawh tom tag ang yaox tix yum heue.”
17 Yam an,
Yaw-na-tang pa mawh kawn simex Ya-pi-ya-tha mai Ya-si-ma-see pa mawh kawn simex Sa-tu,
krox jun ot ka praok rawm keet Yin Law-cie plak praix veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu ang kix pon laig ka daux veng,
lhat kix yaox tix.
Koe bun tix kaux rien hoek krai kix mhong grawng kaing pa lih tix mawh jheeng jheeng,
mai kix tom deeh toe tix krai ka simiang Ta-wue.
18 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui nhum ju tix kaux yaox ka kix,
nawh tom hu krai ka Ya-sa-long.
Kiex ra kaux tom nied tix to hu mox tix ka Pa-hu-ling,
ka daux nyiex pui tix nyiex.
Nyiex pui an koe dung rawm tix mu,
kiex tom laig ot ka daux dung rawm an.
19 Moeng pui an teei dawb tawb moeng dung kah,
mai baox rhuat simie nghox ca ot ka piang,
son ang pui sang yaox kiex.
20 Simiang naing pa ot greem Ya-sa-long hoek ka daux nyiex pui an,
mai chawk bun an ah nin:
“Ya-si-ma-see mai Yaw-na-tang ot dee mawx?”
Bun an ah nin:
“Kiex hoik tiang ka rawm klawng heue.”
Pui in kix sawg jee sawg blaong,
mai ang yaox kiex,
kix tom deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
21 Khaix hoik ing pui pa leue kiex khankix,
Ya-si-ma-see mai Yaw-na-tang lih khaing daux dung,
kiex tom nied hu krai grawng pa hoik tieh vheen leue Ya-si-to-fu ka simiang Ta-wue,
mai ah nin:
“Nied tix tiang ka rawm klawng!”
22 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh tom yaog tix tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Yam hoek riang praix,
kix hoik tiang luan khawm uik heue.
23 Ya-si-to-fu yaox ang kix hawt ka lox pa krai tix,
tom sau tix brug la,
mai deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix.
Khaix hoik khre nawh kaing daux nyiex tix mhawm mhawm,
nawh tang plaux tix ka maox.
Pui si paung nawh ka daux haktiex si meeig pui nyiex pui ma nawh.
24 Yam tiang Ya-sa-long mai pui naing cie nawh rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
Ta-wue hoik hoek ka Ma-ha-nian. (
25 Ya-sa-long tawx Ya-ma-sha mawh simiang naing,
plud ka Yaw-ya.
Ya-ma-sha mawh pui Yi-seu-ma-lixc kawn simex Yi-thie.
Miex nawh Ai-pi-kai mawh kawn bun Na-sia,
pa mawh pux o miex Yaw-ya pa kaux Si-lu-ya.)
26 Ya-sa-long mai pui Yi-seu-liex sum bang ot ka baoh Ci-liex heue.
27 Yam hoek Ta-wue ka Ma-ha-nian,
koe pui loe kaux lih rieb ka nawh.
Kix mawh ceu pui Ya-meung pui veng La-pa kawn simex Na-sia pa kaux Saw-pi,
mai pui veng Law Ti-pa kawn simex Ya-mi-lix pa kaux Ma-ciex,
mai Ci-liex pui veng Law-ci-ling pa kaux Pa-si-lai.
28-29 Kix viex ka aw,
klong,
prux it,
mai priex preeng hoek tawx ka Ta-wue mai khawm pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix.
Can pa ih mawh nghox ca,
nghox ca pa lang mu,
khliep miien nghox ca,
si gleem nghox ca pa hoik ping sin,
ceem,
teu teu,
tom goed,
rawm hia,
khlawp rawm teeh moi,
bee rawm teeh,
mai yung.
Kix hoik tawng cai rhawm mai taug mao Ta-wue mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh heue.
