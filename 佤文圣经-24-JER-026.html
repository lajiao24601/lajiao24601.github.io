Prawng ka Yie-li-mi
26
aYaw-ya-cing kawn simex Yaw-si-yax,
yam jauh nawh tix mawh cao giex kaing piang meung Yo-tax,
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Maix tag caw tix cung ka biang brawk nyiex buan,
mai krai pao gum lox pa tawx aux maix krai ka pui hun pa hoek tix yuh krax krub,
pa kaoh khaing meung Yo-tax ku veng khankix,
tix gum kawx pa pon kieh roik.
3 Phan kix cu tix nghiet kah,
cu tix tik vud ka krax pa caox pa caig,
aux tag sang preet grai ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix,
mai krax laux leeing pa sang naok tix tawx hoek piang kix heue.”
4-5 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux deeh krai ka pui hun ah nin:
“Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx aux ka pex,
nghiet ka lox siliex aux pa mawh cao jhai khankix,
mai cu nghiet ka aux.
Aux baox ploi siliex tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix si kah ka pex,
pex ca ang cu tix nghiet,
6 bPhan pex ca nied tix lieh lheu,
aux tag sang yuh nyiex buan in laux leeing,
awm hoik yuh aux See-law laux leeing sivoe an yuh nan.
Meung haktiex ku meung tag sang teei ceu kaux veng in kieh mawh gum lox pa jhax lawg heue.”
7 Cao giex kaing li,
cao jhai,
mai pui hun khankix,
hoik mhong lox pa krai aux in kix ka daux nyiex buan khawm uik.
8 Yam hoik uik lox krai aux,
kix tom giex ka aux,
mai graox ka aux ah nin:
“Maix khaw tix yum tete heue!
9 Yuh ka mawx maix lai teei ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex krai pao sang laux leeing nyiex buan in awm See-law yuh nan,
baox krai veng in tag sang lih mawh dee riem dee ang pui ot lie?”
Mawh kheu nan pui hun lai lawm ka aux heue.
10 Cao kaing meung Yo-tax mhong grawng kaing in,
tom lih khaing nyiex haw simiang mai hoek ka nyiex buan,
mai paoh krax prawng ka daux “siviex khraox”.
11 Cao giex kaing li mai cao jhai khankix deuh phi map piang aux ka cao kaing,
mai ka pui hun khankix;
kix ah nin:
“Pui in khaw tix pauh yum,
mawh kheu nawh hoik krai lox jhai pa yuh veng in laux;
pex hoik mhong ka yhaok tix khawm uik heue.”
12 Aux krai lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ploi aux hoek krai pao lox pa jhax lawg nyiex buan in mai veng in,
pex hoik mhong ka khawm uik.
13 Pex tag caw tix loh rhawm tix,
mai nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom taug kieh grai ka krax laux leeing pa sang rien nawh tix sang tawx piang pex.
14 Phan mawh aux,
aux hoik ot ka daux tawm taix pex.
Pex sang keud tix yuh ka mawx ka aux tag sawn mawh pa khaw caw,
mai pex chix tix yuh heue.
15 Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix tawng yuh nin:
phan pex pauh ka aux,
pex mai veng in,
khawm pui pa ot daux veng,
tag sang plud ka nham pui pa ang bax lud loe,
mawh kheu hoik mawh aux pa ploi Simiang Siyiex hoek tix ne raw ka pex heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
cao kaing mai pui hun krai ka cao giex kaing li,
mai ka cao jhai khankix ah nin:
“Pui in si kah ka ex ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex,
ang khaw tix pauh nawh yum heue.”
17 Khaix an,
ka daux kuad song hoek tix kaux yaog tix kaoh cung krai ka pui ah nin:
18 c“Sivoe an,
bawg yam giex simiang Si-si-cia ka kaing piang Yo-tax,
pui Maw-li-sa cao jhai Mi-cia krai pao ka pui Yo-tax,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
gawng Shi-ang tag sang pu pliak tix awm ma kaung yuh nan.
Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang tieh tix lih mawh dee riem;
gawng pa sum nyiex buan ot piang,
tag sang tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag khaox pa nhik heue.
19 Yam an,
Simiang Si-si-cia mai pui Yo-tax ang bax pauh ka Mi-cia yum.
Si-si-cia tieh tix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai pon krax pa khaw rhawm Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex loh rhawm keud tix,
mai ang lai ploi ka krax laux leeing pa hoik tat tix deuh hoek ka piang to kaux kix.
Viang mawh nan,
yam in ex tag sang kieh krax pa laux leeing lih heue.”
20 Yam an,
koe pui Ci-liex Yie-ling kawn simex See-ma-ya pa mawh Wu-li-yax tix kaux;
pui in hoik awm Yie-li-mi yuh nan krai ka gum lox jhai ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
mai ah lox pa ang mhawm ka veng in mai ka meung in.
21 Simiang Yaw-ya-cing khawm pui naing cie nawh,
mai simiang naing cie nawh,
mhong ka lox Wu-li-yax,
tom sang pauh ka nawh.
Wu-li-yax tawng ka krax pa tieh vheen in,
mai yaoh yee to hu mox tix ka Ai-cix.
22 Simiang Yaw-ya-cing ploi Yi-li-na-tang kawn simex Ya-keu-paw viex pui hu tix cub hoek ka Ai-cix,
giex ka Wu-li-yax,
23 mai viex nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Yaw-ya-cing.
Simiang pauh ka nawh,
mai tik to kaux yum nawh ka gawng si meeig pui haktiex khankix heue.
24 Viang mawh nan,
aux pon krax pa kia ngien Ya-si-kang kawn simex Sa-fang,
ang koe pui pa tawx aux ka pui hun pauh ka aux heue.
