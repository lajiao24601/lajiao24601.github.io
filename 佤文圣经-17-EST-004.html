Maw-ti-kai khiox khaw Yi-see-thie teem coi tix
4
Maw-ti-kai tawng ka kaing in khawm uik,
kied laux rhawm kah,
riah ka sibex tix,
ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
mai sau nyaox ka piang kaing tix,
mai yiiem tom ting,
hu si dauh ka veng Su-sang,
2 hoek ka tiang siviex nyiex haw simiang,
ang laig kah,
mawh kheu hawt been,
pui pa ceep ka kraung man keei sum ang pon tix laig ka nyiex haw simiang.
3 Lai pa tawx simiang hoek ka haktiex mawx ang ah,
pui Yo-thaix pa ot haktiex dee an kied laux rhawm laux rhi.
Kix tuah priex preem,
riang yiiem,
pao lox;
pui tom nie ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
it ka piang nyaox.
4 Bun cao cau Yi-see-thie pa ot ka daux nyiex haw mai pui sieh krai grawng kaing pa yuh Maw-ti-kai ka nawh,
nawh kied laux rhawm laux rhi,
tom tawx sibex ka Maw-ti-kai tix blah,
simie nawh peeik ka sibex man keei sum,
viang mawh nan,
Maw-ti-kai ang rieb kah.
5 Kheu nan,
Yi-see-thie tom kok cao pa pau pui sieh tix pa kaux Ha-tha-keux hoek,
kieh nawh hu ka Maw-ti-kai dee an,
mai jhak lih kaing pa yuh ka mawx,
mai lih yuh ka mawx.
6 Ha-tha-keux yaox Maw-ti-kai ka brawk dee laig ka nyiex haw simiang.
7 Maw-ti-kai tom krai kaing pa lih ka nawh khawm uik,
mai ah nin,
Ha-mang hoik cu,
phan hoik pauh ka pui Yo-thaix yum khawm uik,
daux krao meung sang baox mau khri tom nie.
8 Maw-ti-kai baox tawx lai pa tawx kix piang veng Su-sang ka nawh tix plah,
daux lai krai sang pauh tix pui Yo-thaix.
Maw-ti-kai kieh nawh tawx lai in ka Yi-see-thie,
mai krai Yi-see-thie mhong sidawng,
kieh nawh hu ka simiang,
khiox khaw ka nawh,
khiox khaw son paox ceu cad tix.
9 Ha-tha-keux tom krai lox Maw-ti-kai khawm uik.
10 Yi-see-thie baox tawx lox ka Maw-ti-kai,
ah nin:
11 “Simex bun ang ah,
pui mawx ang ah,
phan ang mawh simiang pa kok,
to kaux nawh laig yaox ka simiang,
tag sang pauh nawh yum.
In mawh lox tat jhawm prawng,
daux meung khawm uik,
mawh pa teem simiang ang ah,
kawn pui daux haktiex ku dauh ang ah,
tag tawng ka khawm uik.
Koe ngie tix grawng:
phan simiang tawx khaox gix khri ka pui pa ang kok an,
pui an tag ang yaox tix yum.
Viang mawh nan,
hoik koe tix khix simiang ang lai hoek tix yaox ka aux heue.”
12 Maw-ti-kai rieb ka lox pa krai Yi-see-thie,
13 tom tawx pui ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix paw lai keud,
keud tix ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang tag chix nyawm ot khaing pui Yo-thaix tang kaux.
14 Maix ang krai lox yam in,
pui Yo-thaix chix pon krax taoh tang krax,
maix mai nyiex ma maix tag yaox krax laux krax leeing.
Pui mawx tawng,
kieh maix mawh moeng simiang jiet mawh grawng kaing yam in heue!”
15 Yi-see-thie kieh pui tawx lox ka Maw-ti-kai:
16 “Maix hu khrawm ka can pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka daux veng Su-sang,
kieh kix tuah priex preem rawg buan son aux,
loe ngaix loe sawm ang sawm ang rheep;
aux mai bun pa ot mai aux daux nyiex haw kawx yuh nang nan.
Khaix an,
aux tag hu yaox ka simiang,
viang in mawh kaing pa lud,
aux kawx sang hu heue.
Phan aux yuh nin yaox krax yum,
aux kawx cu ka heue.”
17 Kheu nan,
Maw-ti-kai lih hu,
mai hawt ka lox pa krai Yi-see-thie heue.
