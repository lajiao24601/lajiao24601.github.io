Ca rang ceu kaux pui pa deeh hoek khankix
(Lai Ni-si-mi 7.4–73)
2
Pui pa vuk vied vuk viang tom haoh hun lih hu khaing haktiex Pa-pi-loeng,
deeh hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka haktiex Yo-tax,
deeh hoek ka nyiex ma dee ot tix.
Jauh tom pon giex simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha kix hu,
pui nyiex ma kix hoik ca vuk vied vuk viang tix ot ka Pa-pi-loeng.

58 Doeh ceu cad pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan mai doeh ceu cad siliex Saw-law-meung,
kaux pui pa deeh hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix,
khawm uik koe loe yieh tix dim ra kaux heue.
59-60 Ceu cad cub nyiex ma pa mawh Ti-lai-ya,

mai Ni-keu-tax koe liah yieh tix phuan ra kaux.

61-62 Ang lai yaox sang kham sakse ru ceu cad grawng sang krai ceu cad cub nyiex ma tax du kix cao giex kaing li mawh:
Ha-pa-ya,
Ha-keu-see,
Pa-si-lai.
(Tax du cao giex kaing li Pa-si-lai teei tix moeng ka bun pui Ci-liex ceu pui Pa-si-lai tix kaux,
mai sum tix kaux ceu kaux ceu cad cub nyiex ma keeing moeng tix.)
Mawh kheu ang kix pon kham sakse tax du kix mawh pui mawx,
tom sawn tix mawh pa ang singax singeh,
mawh kheu nan tom ang pon tix mawh cao giex kaing li.
63 aSimiang haktiex pui Yo-thaix krai ka kix,
ang pon tix ih ceu pa singax pa hoik tan lu ka Siyiex,
tom siao Wu-ling mai Thu-ming ka dee bleeh cao giex kaing li heue.
64-67  Kaux pui pa deeh hoek khawm uikkoe 42360 kaux.
Simex siliex mai bun siliex kixkoe 7337 kaux.
Simex bun pa kied chix rhax lai khankixkoe 200 kaux.
Brungkoe 736 mu.
Lawkoe 235 mu.
Ka-la-ukkoe 435 mu.
Lakoe 6720 mu.
68 Yam hoek pui pa vuk vied vuk viang in kix ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
koe cao kaing ceu pui tix blah tan lu ka khawng phak lak sawng pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm tix,
mai teem coi pui cox sum nyiex buan ka dee haktiex pa prim.
69 Kix tan lu son kaing in awm riang pa koe tix,
khawm uik mawh khri phuan yieh kongcing,
mau luah ra rheng daix yieh kongcing,
kawn koe kraung ceep pa tawx cao giex kaing li ceep tix yieh pheen heue.
70 cCao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we mai pui hun tix blah pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai pa ot ka dex baoh haktiex dee an khankix.b
Pui pa kied chix rhax lai,
pui pa pau nyiex buan,
mai pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan pa paung ot ka veng pa ot ka sivie siveng Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix deeh ing ka haktiex tix khawm uik heue.
