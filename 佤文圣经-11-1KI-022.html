Cao jai Shui-li-yax ne raw ka Ya-ha
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 18.2–27)
22
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Shui-li-yax khaix an ra num ang lai lih koe krax naing,
2 viang mawh nan,
hoek ka daux loe num,
simiang Yaw-sa-fa pui Yo-tax hu vi ka simiang Ya-ha pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
3 Ya-ha ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Yuh ka mawx ex ang bax teei ka veng La-mawx pa koe ka daux haktiex Ci-liex kaing simiang Shui-li-yax lie?
An kix hoik mawh cie ex heue!”
4 Ya-ha chawk Yaw-sa-fa ah nin:
“Cu maix tix hu puing ka La-mawx tau mai aux lie?”
Simiang Yaw-sa-fa ah nin:
“Phan maix hu,
aux kawx hu nang.
Pui naing cie aux mai brung naing cie aux kawx chix tix hu ku bawg ku yam.
5 Viang mawh nan,
ex caw tix ran chawk ka Simiang Siyiex ha!”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-ha khrawm ka cao jhai pon yieh kaux,
mai chawk kix ah nin:
“Aux khaw ang khaw tix hu puing ka La-mawx lie?”
Kix ah nin:
“Hu heue,
Siyiex tag sang kieh maix puing pex.”
7 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-sa-fa chawk ah nin:
“Dee in ang kawn koe cao jhai Simiang Siyiex lie?
Ex chix tix khiox khaw nawh chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
8 Ya-ha ah nin:
“Kawn koe tix kaux,
mawh kawn simex Ying-la pa kaux Mi-kai-ya.
Viang mawh nan,
aux kied koeng ka nawh,
mawh kheu ang nawh sax krai lox mhawm ka aux;
nawh hoik krai lox caox jheeng jheeng heue.”
Yaw-sa-fa ah nin:
“Maix ang chix tix ah nin heue!”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-ha kok cao kaing nyiex haw tix kaux hoek,
mai nhie nawh hu sawg Mi-kai-ya hoek dum mawx dum an heue.
10 Simiang in kiex ra kaux ceep ka kraung dha,
ot ka plak praix siviex veng Sa-ma-li-yax daux glauh pauh nghox ca,
mai ngawm ka piang dang ngeung tix kaux tix lhang;
can cao jhai khawm uik krai ka lox jhai ka plak sivoe ngai kiex.
11 Ka daux kix koe cao jhai tix kaux,
mawh kawn simex Ci-na-na pa kaux Si-ti-cia,
nawh yuh ka teet rhiem khankix,
mai ah nin ka Ya-ha:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Maix teei teet rhiem in kix yuh naing ka pui Shui-li-yax,
mai yuh kix laux leeing khawm uik.’ ”
12 Cao jhai tang kaux kawx hoik ah lox pa awm paox tix;
kix ah nin:
“Hu puing ka La-mawx,
maix tag puing pex tete;
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ap kix ka daux tawm taix simiang ex heue.”
13 Yam in,
simiang naing an pa hu kok Mi-kai-ya hoek,
ah nin ka Mi-kai-ya:
“Can cao jhai khawm uik hoik rom tix ah simiang tag sang pex,
maix khaw tix ah nang lox pa mhawm heue.”
14 Viang mawh nan,
Mi-kai-ya ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
Siyiex nhie aux krai ceu pa tix,
aux tom tag krai heue.”
15 Yam hoek Mi-kai-ya ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Ya-ha,
simiang chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mi-kai-ya,
simiang Yaw-sa-fa mai aux khaw ang khaw tix hu puing ka La-mawx lie?
“Maix hu puing heue!”
Mi-kai-ya ah nin:
“Maix tag sang pex;
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ap La-mawx ka daux tawm taix maix heue!”
16 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-ha ah nin:
“Maix ah lox pa mawh pa meh ka aux ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex!
Lox in aux hoik krai ka maix miex bawg mawx lie?”
17 aMi-kai-ya ah nin:
“Aux hoik yaox ka pui naing Yi-seu-liex prung prang ka gawng daux tiag khawm pon daog,
hoik awm cub phung yung pa ang koe cao liang yuh nan.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
‘Pui in kix ang koe simiang,
nhie kix tang deeh ing mhawm mhawm ka haktiex tix heue.’ ”
18 Ya-ha ah nin ka Yaw-sa-fa:
“Aux hoik krai ang nawh sax ah lox pa mhawm ka aux tix bawg kawx lie?
Nawh hoik ca krai ka lox pa caox jheeng jheeng heue!”
19 bMi-kai-ya ca nied tix ah nin:
“Nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex.
Aux yaox Simiang Siyiex ngawm ka piang dang ngeung rao ma,
mai can kawn cau Siyiex cung ka plak goix plak dawm nawh.
20 Simiang Siyiex chawk ah nin:
‘Mawh mawx pa hu pie lhien ka Ya-ha,
mai kieh nawh hu tawx tix yum ka La-mawx lie?’
Tix blah ah yuh nin,
koe tix blah ah yuh nan.
21 Khaix an,
koe khuan tix mu hoek,
mai hoek ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
‘Aux hu pie lhien ka nawh.’
Simiang Siyiex chawk ah nin:
‘Pie maix yuh ka mawx?’
22 Khuan an ah nin:
‘Aux sang kieh Ya-ha mai can cao jhai khawm uik krai lox pie.’
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
‘Hu heue,
maix tag sang pex tete heue.’ ”
23 Mi-kai-ya nghoik tix ah nin:
“In hoik mawh grawng kaing pa lih tix mawh.
Simiang Siyiex kieh cao jhai cie maix krai lox pie ka maix,
mai nawh hoik tat tix kieh maix si pup ka krax laux krax leeing heue.”
24 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn simex Ci-na-na pa mawh cao jhai Si-ti-cia hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Mi-kai-ya,
simiang nawh tix taix,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hu khaing aux yam mawx,
mai krai ka maix lie?”
25 Mi-kai-ya pawk ah nin:
“Singaix yam to hu mox maix tix ot ka nyiex iak pa ot dee glip an,
maix tag sang pon tix tawng ka heue.”
26 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-ha tawx lox ka cao kaing tix kaux ah nin:
“Giex ka Mi-kai-ya,
hu tawx nawh ka cao kaing veng Ya-meung mai ka Yaw-a-see kawn simiang dee an,
27 tawx lox nhie kix laung Mi-kai-ya daux thawng,
mai tawx nawh ih ngie ka khao mun,
mai nyaux ngie ka rawm ceeix khaing,
son ang sang kieh nawh yum,
krox jun tom deeh hoek aux mhawm mhawm heue.”
28 Mi-kai-ya ah nin:
“Phan maix pon tix deeh hoek mhawm mhawm,
in tom tag mawh pa kham sakse sidawng grawng ang Simiang Siyiex bax si kah ka aux!”
Nawh baox ah nin:
“Pui ku kaux,
priex pex tawng ka lox pa hoik krai aux yam in heue!”
Ya-ha yum
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 18.28–34)
29 Ya-ha simiang pui Yi-seu-liex mai Yaw-sa-fa simiang pui Yo-tax hu ka haktiex Ci-liex puing ka veng La-mawx.
30 Ya-ha ah nin ka Yaw-sa-fa:
“Yam hu ex puing ka naing,
kieh aux loh kraung ceep tix,
phan mawh maix ca kawn ceep ka kraung dha pa ceep simiang.”
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex loh ka kraung ceep tix mai hu ka daux naing heue.
31 Simiang pui Shui-li-yax tawx lox ka kru naing ling law cao bung naing tix ngoe ra kaux,
puing ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux khaing.
32 Mawh kheu nan,
yam yaox kix ka simiang Yaw-sa-fa,
een nawh mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
tom hu puing ka nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-sa-fa kok yo lox tix,
33 mai kix tom tawng ang nawh mawh simiang Yi-seu-liex,
tom ang lai puing ka nawh.
34 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui naing Shui-li-yax tix kaux,
nieb pon puing ka ak puh,
mai jiet krox ka dee kleuk gawng caing sibex rhiem ceep simiang Ya-ha.
Ya-ha kok yo ah nin ka pui pa grheu ling law:
“Aux kied nie mat,
kling tix deeh ing,
mai deeh ing khaing bang dee puing naing heue!”
35 Ka daux ngaix an naing nied ot nied kin,
simiang Ya-ha brug ka ling law naing,
mai hu plak ot pui Shui-li-yax,
nham jauh lih khaing khaix mat nawh,
ka daux ling law naing hoik naog ka nham,
yam hoek plak poi praix bo nawh yum.
36 Yam hoik klip singaix khaing gawng,
koe lox tawx hoik tauh hoek ka dee ot bang pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa ah nin:
“Pui ku kaux tang deeh ing ka nyiex tix,
tang deeh ing ka haktiex tix heue!”
37 Simiang Ya-ha tom hoik yum yuh nin heue.
Koe pui viex to kaux yum nawh hoek ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
mai si paung nawh dee an;
38 kix si ghrah ling law cie nawh daux dung Sa-ma-li-yax dee heem sibun coh kaux tix kah;
sox hoek tix lied ka nham nawh,
awm hoik sax krai Simiang Siyiex ka yuh nan heue.
39 Kaing pa yuh simiang Ya-ha tang ceu khankix,
khawm pa hoik yuh nawh ka nyiex haw rhang sang,
mai grawng pa hoik sum nawh veng tom nie khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
40 Khaix hoik yum nawh,
kawn simex nawh Ya-ha-shiex plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 20.31–21.1)
41 Yam giex Ya-ha simiang pui Yi-seu-liex kaing ka daux pon num,
kawn simex Ya-sa pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
42 Yam mawh nawh simiang,
ju nawh tag koe tix ngoe phuan num.
Nawh giex kaing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tix nga phuan num.
Miex nawh mawh A-su-pa kawn bun See-li-si.
43 Yaw-sa-fa awm keeing tix Ya-sa yuh nan,
ang guah khaing krax mai yuh kaing pa khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa koe ka piang gawng iak;
kawn pui ca kawn gleeing tix tan lu ka nyawng bhawng dee an.
44 Yaw-sa-fa kawx yuh krax pa nhim tau mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
45 Kaing pa yuh Yaw-sa-fa tang ceu khankix,
grawng pa kua nawh mai pa chix nawh ceng,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik,
46 nawh hoik pih tix tik ka can simex pa coh kaux tix pa koe ka daux nyiex vad khawm uik pa kawn koe daux baux bawg yam ju keeing tix Ya-sa khankix heue.
47 Singaix yam an,
haktiex Yi-tong ang koe simiang,
simiang Yo-tax ploi cao kuang kaing pa ting tix kaux hu kuang kia dee an.
48 Yaw-sa-fa yuh ka reu tix lhang,
mai tang khri hu ka Eu-fe;
viang mawh nan,
reu laux ka Yi-shuing Cia-Piie,
mai ang lai pon tix hu.
49 Khaix an,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex tawx lox,
mai nhie pui cie tix hu tau mai pui cie Yaw-sa-fa;
viang mawh nan,
Yaw-sa-fa tieb tie ka heue.
50 Yam yum Yaw-sa-fa,
kix si paung nawh ot tau mai si meeig pui nyiex haw simiang ka veng Ta-wue,
kawn simex nawh Yaw-lang plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex
51 Yam giex Yaw-sa-fa simiang pui Yo-tax kaing ka daux kao aliah num,
kawn simex Ya-ha pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh giex kaing ka Sa-ma-li-yax ra num.
52 Nawh hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
mai hoik gau tix yuh kaing pa ang mhawm awm keeing tix Ya-ha,
miex tix Yie-si-pix,
mai Yie-law-paw-ang yuh nan,
hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex krix ka daux phi map.
53 Nawh hoik awm keeing tix yuh nan,
mai krub ka Pa-lix,
cheeik Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex song rhawm heue.
