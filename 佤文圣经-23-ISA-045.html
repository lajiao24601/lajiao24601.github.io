Gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Sai-lu-seex
45
Simiang Siyiex groi rhawm bee ka Sai-lu-seex,
mai laug nawh mawh simiang.
Simiang Siyiex ploi nawh hu pon kuang meung ka piang haktiex,
ploi nawh hu tieh ka siawp sibrawm simiang khankix yawm.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh meung ka piang haktiex paoh siviex veng ting son Sai-lu-seex,
ang lai baox nyip ka siviex.
Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin ka nawh:
2 To kaux aux tag sang paoh daux krax son maix,
kieh gawng iak gawng ting si dawm son maix.
Aux tag sang yieh ka siviex tawng,
kid dut ka jhong siviex rhiem.
3 Aux tag sang teei ka kraung pa ting ngawh pa koe dee glip,
sin ngao pa koe daux krao tawx ka maix khawm uik;
aux tag sang kieh maix tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
tawng ka grawng pa kok yo Siyiex Yi-seu-liex maix ka daux ceu kaux tix.
4 Aux hoik ciak ci maix teem coi ka siliex aux Ya-keux,
teem coi ka Yi-seu-liex pa hoik laug aux.
Aux kok maix ka daux ceu kaux tix;
maix ang tawng ka aux,
viang mawh nan kawx,
aux hoik tawx ceu kaux ka maix heue.
 
5 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
ang lai baox koe tang kaux;
Lheu khaing aux ang lai koe Siyiex tang kaux.
Viang maix ang tawng ka aux,
aux tag sang tawx riang rao ka maix.
6 Aux yuh nin,
mawh son sang kieh piang haktiex khawm uik,
jauh dee lih singaix hoek dee klip singaix,
hoik tawng khawm uik:
Lheu khaing aux ang lai koe tang kaux;
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
ang lai baox koe tang kaux.
7 Aux phan yuh pa riang,
mai baox phan yuh pa faik;
aux tawx buan son,
maix baox tawx krax laux krax leeing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh ka kaing in khawm uik.
8 Aux kieh kawn pui puing pex,
awm hoik tawx rao ma ka rawm lhiex yuh nan;
haktiex tag sang nyeu taix tix kuan rieb,
tag sang pruh ka tai pa kieh luat luan mai tai pa rauh pa see.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kaing in lih tix mawh heue.
Cao pa phan pa yuh mai cao giex kaing been
9 aPui pa tieb tie ka cao pa phan pa yuh tix hoik yaox krax laux krax leeing heue!
Aw tawng sang chix pon simah mai cao yuh tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Tiex glaox sang chix pon chawk ka cao yuh tix yuh ka kaing ceu pa tix lie?
Nawh sang pon chix salao ka cao pa yuh tix ang chix yuh lie?
10 Pui mawx pon tieb tie ka miex keeing tix ah nin:
Yuh ka mawx lai keh aux mawh pui pa yuh nin lie?
11 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh,
pa mawh cao pa phan pa yuh singaix pa sang hoek krai ah nin:
maix ang koe akhuang tix chawk ka grawng kawn simex kawn bun aux,
ang koe akhuang tix krai ka aux,
aux khaw tix sang yuh ceu pa tix.
12 Aux hoik mawh cao pa phan pa yuh haktiex in;
aux phan yuh ka kawn pui piang haktiex.
Aux siao ka sibrawm tix nih ka rao ma,
aux giex ka singaix,
khix,
mai simuing.
13 To kaux aux kieh Sai-lu-seex pon pex;
aux tag sang kieh krax pa lawng nawh si dawm.
Nawh tag sang cox deeh sum ka veng cie aux Yie-lu-sa-leung;
kieh pui cie aux pa hoik laung pui pon krax pa luat luan.
Ang koe pui pa vai tix lo ka nawh,
phan ang mawh nan chun praok nawh yuh kaing in.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik krai pao ah nin heue.
 
14 Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin ka Yi-seu-liex:
Sin ngao Ai-cix mai sin ngao Ku-seu hoik mawh cie maix khawm uik;
pui Si-pa pa kied ting lhaong tag sang yuh siliex cawng maix,
tag sang viex maox ceng hawt khaix maix.
Kix tag sang doh keud mai grux ngawng tix ka sivoe ngai maix ah nin:
Siyiex ot tau mai maix,
ang lai koe tang kaux;
lheu khaing nawh ang lai koe Siyiex tang kaux.
15 Siyiex an pa taoh pui Yi-seu-liex,
mawh Siyiex pa mox to kaux tix glip.
16 Pui pa yuh riang krub pui khankix hoik laux leeing;
kix tag sang pon krax pa kaig pa tua.
17 Viang mawh nan,
Yi-seu-liex pon krax pa taoh Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh hoik pon krax pa pon pex leeing ju;
pui nawh ang lai baox hoek krax kaig krax tua leeing ju heue.
 
18 Simiang Siyiex phan yuh rao ma;
mawh ngie nawh pa mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing.
Nawh phan yuh ka haktiex,
sum riah haktiex kaux cong.
Nawh ang kieh haktiex riem,
viang mawh nan,
nawh kieh haktiex lih mawh nyiex ot pui.
Nawh ah nin:
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
ang lai baox koe tang kaux.
19 Aux ang sax krai lox ka dee glip tix bawg kawx,
ang sax mox ka alo tix tix bawg kawx.
Aux ang lo ka pui Yi-seu-liex sawg tix ka daux krax pa giex pa going.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
aux krai pao ka pa mawh pa meh;
aux krai lao ka pa rauh see hu heue.
Cao haktiex mai riang rao krub pui Pa-pi-loeng
20 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik rom tix kaoh tau heue,
khaix hoik laux leeing meung pui pa kawn deuk khankix hoik kham krax prawng krax ca khawm uik heue!
Pa klawm rong khaox krub pui lai ling hu daux laih khankix,
pa mawh riang rao krub pui pa ang pon taoh ka kix,
hoik mawh pui pa daox aw khawm uik heue!
21 Hoek heue,
hoek krai lao ka daux nyiex prawng see heue!
Kieh pui pa hoik deuh pui phi map piang ra lai kum kawng ka paox tix heue.
Mawh pui mawx pa hoik dha tix krai ka kaing pa sang lih tix mawh lie?
Ang mawh aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex lie?
Lheu khaing aux ang lai koe Siyiex tang kaux;
aux mawh Siyiex pa khaw caw mai Siyiex pa taoh,
lheu khaing aux ang lai koe tang kaux heue.
 
22 Pui piang haktiex khawm uik eue,
phan pex hoek ka aux taug pon krax taoh.
Aux mawh Siyiex,
ang lai baox koe tang kaux.
23 bAux krai lox pa rauh see,
gum lox pa hoik krai aux tix deuh tag kaux leeing ju leeing phan.
Aux tiex tu ka daux ju to kaux tix ah nin:
pui ku kaux tag sang grux ngawng tix krub ka aux;
jheeh kawn pui khawm uik tag sang tiex tu tix cu nghiet ka aux heue.
24 Kix tag sang ah nin,
mawh ngie ka daux ceu kaux aux tix kaux khaing,
tom tag pon krax pa pon pex mai riang rao;
pui pa koeng ka aux tag hoek krax kaig krax tua khawm uik.
25 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang taoh kawn praix tix Yi-seu-liex khawm uik;
kix tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka aux heue.
