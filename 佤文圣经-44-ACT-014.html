Ot ka Yi-keu-nien
14
Kaing pa awm paox lih tix mawh ka Yi-keu-nien.
Pao-law hu tau mai Pa-na-pa hoek ka nyiex sidiex pui Yo-thaix;
kheu lox pa krai kiex hoik kieh pui Yo-thaix tom nie mai pui tang ceu tang cad lih mawh kawn Khrit.
2 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix pa ang yum rhawm khankix suk sag yuh pui tang ceu tang cad,
kieh kix ang rhawm yaox ka kawn Khrit.
3 Kiex ra kaux paung ot dee an tom rheeng bawg yam;
kiex viex rhawm ting krai lao ka kaing cie Simiang Ye-sux.
Simiang Ye-sux tawx kiex koe siawp sibrawm kieh kiex pon yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am,
kieh kaing pa yuh kiex kham grawng gum lox pa sidiex pa dex dawt mai krax taoh mawh tete.
4 Pui hun ka daux veng hoik tang guah paox tix kheu in,
koe pui tix blah ot plak pui Yo-thaix,
koe tix blah hawt rhawm kawn cau heue.
5 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix mai pui tang ceu tang cad khawm simiang kix,
but rhawm tix sang yuh kawn cau laux leeing,
sang tawk kiex yum ka simaox.
6 Kiex hoik tawng ka kaing in,
kiex tom hu mox tix plak veng Lu-see-teux haktiex Lui-kao-ni,
mai ka veng Theu-pix in ra mu,
khawm haktiex pa dex ka dee an,
7 mai nied tix sidiex lox buan ka haktiex dee an heue.
Ot ka Lu-see-teux mai ka Theu-pix
8 Ka daux veng Lu-see-teux koe pui pa laux meung ka tix kaux,
pui an hoik dawg mawh pui pa chawh tom yam lih khaing daux vaig,
mai ang sax hu krax tix bawg kawx.
9 Nawh ngawm ka dee an,
mai nghiet ka lox pa sidiex Pao-law.
Pao-law yaox grawng pa koe krax yum rhawm nawh,
chix tix pon moi,
Pao-law tom dong ngai tix ka nawh,
10 kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Kaoh,
caong khawm ra khawn cung rauh heue!”
Pui an cang luh tix,
mai jauh tix hu krax.
11 Pui hun yaox kaing pa yuh Pao-law,
kix tom teei lox pui Lui-kao-ni kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Mawh Siyiex pa tieh tix lih mawh kawn pui,
mai lih hoek ka daux ex heue!”
12 Kix kok Pa-na-pa mawh Co-see,
kok Pao-law mawh Si-eu-mi,
mawh kheu pa thung pui krai lox mawh nawh.
13 Ka praok veng nyiex vad Co-see cao giex kaing li koe ka tix kaux,
pa tuk ka moi,
viex ka tai,
hoek diang siviex veng,
son sang hawt tix yuh krax tan mai pui hun ka kawn cau heue.
14 Pa-na-pa mai Pao-law mhong kaing pa keud kix tix sang yuh,
kiex tom riah ka kraung ceep tix,
preeng tix hu ka daux pui hun,
mai kok yo lox tom ting ah nin:
15 a“Pui ku kaux ku pui,
yuh ka mawx pex lai sang yuh kaing pa yuh nin lie?
Yiex hoik mawh ngie kawn pui khaing,
ka daux pa mawh yiex kawn pui hoik awm pex yuh nan.
Yiex hoek sidiex lox buan dee in,
mawh son sang kieh pex tik vud ka riang rao pa giex pa going,
mai deeh veeh tix hoek ka Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
cao pa phan yuh ka rao ma,
haktiex,
rawm sigrit sigra mai ka can pa koe ka daux pa im ju khawm uik.
16 Ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
nawh ghram kawn pui piang haktiex tang hu krax hu tix.
17 Kheu nan,
nawh teei kaing pa mhawm ku ceu hoik kham grawng pa koe tix,
awm pa mawh:
pa tawx nawh rawm lhiex ka pex khaing rao ma,
mai kieh pex pon tix khrawm vawg hawt bawg yam;
nawh tawx priex preem ka pex,
mai kieh pex naog krax pa gaux rhawm heue.”
18 Viang kawn cau hoik krai ka lox in kix kawx,
mai dex ang pon tieb tie pui hun yuh krax tan ka kiex heue.
19 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui Yo-thaix tix blah hoek khaing Ang-thi-a haktiex Pi-si-tix mai ka Yi-keu-nien;
kix kieh pui hun teei simaox tawk Pao-law kah,
mai ce nawh hoik yum,
kix tom reu nawh lih plak praix veng.
20 Yam rub pui gau khankix ka nawh,
nawh kaoh cung,
mai deeh ing daux veng.
Singaix khaix,
nawh hu tau mai Pa-na-pa ka plak haktiex Theu-pix heue.
Deeh ing ka Ang-thi-a
21 Pao-law mai Pa-na-pa sidiex gum lox buan ka haktiex Theu-pix,
mai kieh pui tom nie lih mawh pui gau.
Kiex deeh ing ka Lu-see-teux,
khaix an hoek ka Yi-keu-nien,
mai baox hoek ka Ang-thi-a haktiex Pi-si-tix.
22 Kiex hu tawx krax pa kaux cong ka pui gau ku dauh,
mai joh jhan krax yum rhawm kix kaux cong,
mai baox ah nin ka kix:
“Ex tag sang kham krax kha rhawm tom nie,
tom tag pon tix lih mawh meung pui meung cie Siyiex.”
23 Kiex ra kaux baox tawx ara kuad song ka ceu cao ku dauh,
khaix hoik rawg buan tuah ka priex preem,
mai ap tix tawx kix ka Simiang Ye-sux pa hoik yum rhawm kix ka heue.
24 Kiex ra kaux laig ka haktiex Pi-si-tix,
hoek ka Phang-fe-li-yax.
25 Kiex ra kaux ot tix sidiex gum lox buan ka Piie-cia,
khaix an hu ka Ya-ta-lix,
26 mai brug reu dee an deeh ing ka Ang-thi-a.
Yam jauh,
hoik mawh ka dee in,
grawng pa hoik ap tix yuh kaing khaing daux buan son Siyiex,
yam in kaing in hoik yuh tix heue.
27 Kiex deeh ing ka Ang-thi-a,
kiex tom khrawm pux aik pux o ceu cao,
mai krai grawng pa hoik kieh Siyiex kiex yuh kaing yuh ka mawx ka kix,
grawng pa hoik paoh siviex krax yum rhawm ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
28 Kiex paung ot dee an tau mai kawn Khrit tom rheeng bawg yam heue.
