21
Simiang Siyiex chix tix hawt alo tix yuh ka daux rhawm rhi simiang haktiex,
awm kieh nawh rawm klawng gah plak hu tix yuh nan.
2 Kawn pui ce kaing pa yuh tix hoik khaw khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik chawk cheng tix yaox ka grawng pa yuh nawh.
3 Simiang Siyiex kawn lheu khaw rhawm ka pa yuh pa rauh pa see khaing krax tan lu.
4 Pui caox pui caig cau nyhau mai ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai yaw rang di to kaux tix,
in kix hoik mawh phi map.
5 Pui pa chix bre rhawm keud tix mhawm mhawm pon tix mag mi;
pui pa hax rhawm hot sibhawm kha rhawm rhi tix.
6 Sin ngao pa ang rauh ang see awm taox pai awm pa grai hu tix khiep ngai khaing,
mai kieh pui krix ka daux krax yum.
7 Pui caox pui caig hoik laux leeing ka daux krax pa tud rhim son to kaux tix,
mawh kheu kix tieb tie ang hu ka daux krax pa rauh pa see.
8 Krax lawng pui pa yuh phi map kawk;
kaing yuh pui pa singax singeh rauh see.
9 Kawn cu tix paung ot ka dee kawk choek nyiex,
ang sang rom tix paung ot tau mai moeng pa haoh lox ka daux nyiex pa vieh va.
10 Pui caox pui caig keud tix yuh kaing pa caox leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
mai ang koe krax muh ka paox plawg tix pie ceeix kawx.
11 Pui pa cau nyhau pon krax ciyang,
mai pui daox aw kawx tag pon ka lox sidiex;
pui pa lhak lhiao nied tix koe ngai lig ngai lai khaing daux pa rieb tix ka lox pa sidiex gau.
12 Siyiex pa khaw caw hoik tawng sidawng ka rhawm rhi pui pa caox pa caig;
nawh sang kieh kix laux leeing.
13 Pa yuh tix ang mhong ka lox khiox khaw pui hot pui sibhawm,
yam khiox khaw to kaux nawh ang koe pui pa simi ka nawh nang heue.
14 Khawng phak pa tawx ka daux pa glip pon preet ka krax song rhawm grai;
pa siao lo ka mau chun praok pon kieh krax saux rhawm grai.
15 Pa yuh pa rauh pa see pon kieh pui rauh see gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig,
rhawm rhi kix koe krax lhat krax graung.
16 Pui pa lud hu khaing krax pa lhak lhiao,
mai krax yum hoik krox jun ka nawh.
17 Pa yuh krax gaux rhawm leeing lang singaix,
tag sang hot sibhawm;
pa ca nie luan ih luan nyaux,
sang chix pon mag mi yuh ka mawx lie?
18 Pui caox pui caig keud tix sang yuh pui rauh see laux,
viang mawh nan,
krax laux krax leeing tieh tix deeh hoek piang to kaux nawh.
19 Kawn cu tix paung ot ka daux tiag pa riem khaing,
ang sang rom tix paung ot tau mai moeng pa haoh lox,
ah lox salao.
20 Pui pa lhak lhiao pon yaox tix nyawm ot si u koe;
pui pa daox aw ca nieb pon cai mau,
mai kieh sin ngao nyiex ma tix pak lawg.
21 Pui pa rauh see pa koe krax caox rhawm tag pon yaox tix lang ju lang phan,
mai kawn pui hoik koe krax yung yi kah.
22 Pa chix siao rhawm keud rhawm sawn tix pon teei ka guh veng cie pui naing pa kua,
pon yieh laux ka tab pa hoik een nawh rhawm tix kah.
23 Pui pa chix rien khre ka dak tix pon mox tix khaing krax laux krax leeing.
24 Pui pa tang hawt ka alo tix ting rhawm cau nyhau,
ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
25 Pui pa nieh hoik awm tang plaux tix,
mawh kheu ang nawh cu tix yuh kaing jhaw;
26 nawh ca simie tix ih nyaux leeing lang singaix;
pui rauh pui see koe krax gaux rhawm tix tawx ka pui,
mai ang koe krax pa si vien pie ceeix kawx.
27 Simiang Siyiex koeng ka krax tan lu pui pa caox pa caig,
mai nie ang rhawm yaox krax tan lu khaing ka daux pa pie lhien.
28 Lox kham pui pa pie lhien ang nyang chix tix yum rhawm kah:
Lox krai pui pa bre rhawm tix nghiet mhawm mhawm,
mai tag caw tix yung yi kah.
29 Pui rauh see yuh kaing koe krax yum rhawm;
pui caox caig yuh tix kua ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
30 Phan Simiang Siyiex yuh krax pa tieb tie ka maix,
can ping nyia cuyi,
pa lhak lhiao,
rhawm keud rhawm sawn tag mawh pa ang koe ceu ka maix.
31 Kawn pui chix tix khrawm ka pui naing,
liag ka kraung naing,
son sang rien khre tix puing ka naing,
viang mawh nan,
pa kieh kawn pui puing pex mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.
