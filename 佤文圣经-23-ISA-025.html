Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
25
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh Siyiex aux;
aux sang nab yam yung yi ka maix,
sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux maix.
Maix hoik yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am;
maix hoik yuh lih ka kaing ceu pa hoik dawg keud maix tix yuh.
2 Maix hoik yuh laux ka veng,
hoik yieh ka veng pa koe ka daux.
Veng pa sum pui koeng hoik lih tix mawh daux tiag pa riem,
ang lai pon deeh cox sum leeing ju.
3 Kawn pui pa ting riang tag sang yaog yaw ka maix;
veng pa kaux cong tag sang lhat graung ka maix.
4 Pui hot pa ang koe tid tix tag sang hoek tix een rhawm ka maix,
yam yaox kix krax pa kha rhawm,
tag sang pon pa nyawm ot khaing maix.
Maix hoik tawx dee mox kix tix ka bhaung ting,
hoik kieh kix yaox tix laux lee tix ka veei yam hax koek.
Krax pa tuk tiak pui cau nyhau awm bhaung ting pa phlu yam rung praix,a
5 awm bhawm si aoh ka daux tiag mhaik.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik thox ka jheeh pui pa koeng;
maix hoik tieb tie ka lox riag yo pui pa cau nyhau,
hoik awm lon pai awm pa hoik kieh bhawm rawm si mawg grai yam hax koek yuh nan heue.
Siyiex hoik rien khre ka priex preem
6 Ka gawng Shi-ang,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
tag sang rien khre ka priex preem pa nyawm,
ka plai pa nyawm,
son meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
7 Ka piang gawng in,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang khleeing man tawb krax laux rhawm laux rhi khaing pak ngai kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik,
teei tix tik pai awm pa hoik kheem ka piang meung ka piang haktiex.
8 bNawh tag sang teei tix tik ka krax yum leeing ju;
nawh tag sang awt rhawm ngai pui ku kaux,
mai teei tik ka krax pa kaig pa tua,
pa hoik kham pui cie nawh ka daux meung pui ku meung.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
9 Yam an,
kawn pui tag sang ah nin ku kaux:
“Nawh rub mawh Siyiex ex tete,
mai ex hoik yum rhawm ka nawh;
nawh hoik taoh ka ex.
Nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mai ex hoik yum rhawm ka nawh.
Ex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu hoik taoh nawh ka ex heue.”
Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka Maw-ya
10 cSimiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka gawng Shi-ang,
viang mawh nan,
pui Maw-ya tag sang si pup krax pa dug dig pui,
awm hoik dug dig pui nyhee glauh ka daux pum aing yuh nan.
11 Kix tag sang tawx taix tix lih,
awm pui pa sang chix rawm yuh nan;
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex tag sang yuh krax pa kaig pa tua ka kix,
mai kieh taix pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi kix yeen tix tiiem.
12 Siyiex tag sang yuh ka tab Maw-ya laux leeing,
mai yieh laux ka guh veng,
kieh mawh haktiex pa si dawm,
kieh lih tix mawh phawng tiex heue.
