Gum lox kied mhawm pa sidiex piang gawng
5
Ye-sux yaox ka cub pui hun,
tom haok piang gawng;
mai ngawm,
pui gau nawh ot ka plak goix plak dawm nawh.
2 Nawh jauh tix sidiex gau ka kix heue.
Grawng krax buan son
(Lai Lu-cia 6.20–23)
3 “Pui pa krai doh mawh tix pa hot ka daux khuan kied koe buan son heue;
kix mawh pui cie meung rao ma!
4 aPui pa laux rhawm son phi map tix kied koe buan son heue;
Siyiex tag sang khu rhawm kix!
5 bPui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi kied koe buan son heue;
kix tag sang pon tix cie ka can sin ngao Siyiex.
6 cPui pa yuh ka alo Siyiex kied koe buan son heue;
Siyiex tag sang tawx kix ih tom naog!
7 Pui pa kia ngien pui ka daux krax pa caox rhawm kied koe buan son heue;
Siyiex kawx tag sang kia ngien kix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix!
8 dPui pa singax singeh rhawm rhi kied koe buan son heue;
kix tag sang yaox ka Siyiex!
9 Pui pa kieh pui khaw caw rhawm rhi paox kied koe buan son heue;
Siyiex tag sang kok kix mawh kawn simex kawn bun tix!
10 ePui pa kham krax tuk tiak tuk te son pa yuh ka alo Siyiex kied koe buan son heue;
kix mawh pui cie meung rao ma!
11 f“Yam map nai pui tang kaux pex kheu hawt pex ka aux,
tuk tiak ka pex,
krai lox ang mhawm tuk tiak pex,
pex kied koe buan son heue.
12 gTag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu piang rao ma tag sang koe buan son pa ting son pex;
cao jhai pa koe sivoe an kawx hoik kham krax pa tuk tiak yuh nin nang heue.”
Kih mai pa riang
(Lai Ma-kheu 9.50; Lai Lu-cia 14.34–35)
13 “Pex mawh kih ka kawn pui.
Phan pa laoh kih hoik grai,
tag ang lai baox chix laoh.
Nawh hoik lih mawh ceu pa ang koe ceu,
mai pui tik kah,
ghram pui dug dig ka heue.
14 h“Pex mawh pa riang ka haktiex.
Veng pa sum ot piang gawng mawh pa ang pui pon tawb kheem kah.
15 iAng koe pui pa tok kawk ngu deuh ka greem phliex,
tag sang deuh ot ka piang jawk kawk ngu,
son sang kieh jhan riang ka pui khawm nyiex.
16 jAwm an,
pa riang pex kawx tag caw tix jhan riang ka sivoe ngai pui,
son sang kieh kix yaox ka kaing mhawm pa yuh pex,
mai hoek tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka keeing pex pa ot rao ma heue.”
Grawng lox tat jhawm prawng
17 “Paw vi ce aux hoek mawh son sang tik ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si mai ka gum lox pa sidiex gau cao jhai.
Aux ang hoek tix tik,
mai hoek tix sang kieh deeh bleeh grum naog.
18 lAux krai ka pex tete te lai,
ca mawh kawn koe rao ma haktiex,
lox tat jhawm prawng ang chix grai pie ceeix kawx,
tom hoik si dauh kaing pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uikk.
19 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa lieh lheu ka gum lox ping nyap an pa blawn iak khaing paox tix tix grawng,
khaix an mai gau pui tang kaux yuh nang,
ka daux meung rao ma nawh tag sang lih mawh pa blawn iak khaing paox tix.
Deeh tieh tix,
pui pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng an,
khaix an mai gau pui tang kaux priex tix hawt nang,
ka daux meung rao ma nawh tag sang lih mawh pa blawn ting khaing paox tix.
20 Aux krai ka pex,
pex tag caw tix lheu kaux sijax tix hawt ka alo Siyiex khaing sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai ka pui Fa-li-saix,
pex tom tag pon yaox tix laig daux meung rao ma heue.”
Grawng krax pa song rhawm
21 m“Pex hoik nghiet tix mhong,
pui ju dix dix hoik sax ang cu pui yuh nin:
‘Ang pon tix pauh pui yum;
pa pauh pui yum tag caw tix kham krax prawng ca.’
22 Viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
pa song rhawm ka pux aik tixn,
tag caw tix kham krax prawng ca.
Pa jhax lawg ka pux aik tix mawh ‘ceu pa brai’ yuh nan,
tag caw tix hoek ka nyiex prawng see;
jhax lawg pux aik tix mawh ‘ceu pa pheem’ yuh nan,
ang yaox tix luat luan khaing ngu Maw-na-rhok.
23 Mawh kheu nan,
yam sang tan lu maix khawng phak ka Siyiex plak sivoe khu dee tan,
phan kawn keud grawng pa ang tix khaw rhawm ka pux aik tix,
24 maix tag caw tix deuh khawng tan an ka sivoe khu dee tan,
hu yuh krax pa khaw rhawm tau mai nawh dum mawx dum an,
khaix an,
deeh hoek tix tan lu ka Siyiex heue.
25 “Phan koe pui pa sang deuh phi map ka piang maix,
mai viex maix hoek ka nyiex prawng see,
maix caw tix nied yuh krax pa khaw rhawm tau mai nawh sivoe sang hoek ka nyiex prawng see.
Phan ang mawh nan,
yam hoik laig daux nyiex prawng see,
nawh tag tawx maix ka cao prawng see,
cao prawng see tawx maix ka cao pa kuang thawng,
mai laung maix ka daux thawng.
26 Aux krai ka maix,
maix tag ot daux thawng,
tom hoek nghoik maix tix tawx ka mau sux tix piak heue.”
Grawng krax phit vao
27 o“Pex hoik mhong lox sidiex pui dix dix pa krai yuh nin:
‘Ang pon tix phit vao.’
28 Viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
pa jhak ka bun miex mai koe rhawm tix sang phit tau mai nawh,
ka daux rhawm nawh hoik phit vao tau mai nawh heue.
29 pPhan ngai khawn dawm maix kieh maix yuh phi map,
tag caw tix koik lih,
mai tik ka heue!
Laux to kaux tix blah kawn lheu mhawm khaing laig to kaux khawm uik ka daux Maw-na-rhok.
30 qPhan taix khawn dawm maix kieh maix yuh phi map,
tag caw kid tix tik ka heue!
Som nai pak peung nex nham maix tix ceu kawn lheu mhawm khaing sang laig to kaux maix khawm uik ka daux Maw-na-rhok heue.”
Grawng pa tik moeng tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 19.9; Lai Ma-kheu 10.11–12; Lai Lu-cia 16.18)
31 r“Baox koe krax pa sidiex pa ah nin:
‘Can pa sang tik ka moeng tix,
tag tiiem tix tawx lai tik ka nawh.’
32 sViang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
lheu khaing ang moeng pex singax,
pa mawh mex ang pon tix tik ka nawh.
Mawh kheu nan,
phan moeng nawh baox koe mex,
hoik awm kieh nawh moeng tix yuh krax pa phit vao;
mai simex pa moeng ka nawh kawx hoik sawn tix mawh pui pa yuh krax pa phit vao heue.”
Grawng krax pa tie tu
33 t“Pex baox mhong lox pa sidiex gau pui dix dix ah nin:
‘Ang pon tix lieh lheu ka lox pa hoik tiex tu tix;
gum lox pa hoik tiex tu ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex tag caw tix yuh lih.’
34 uViang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
pex ang pon tix tiex tu.
Ang pong tix tiex tu ka rao ma,
kheu rao ma mawh dang ngeung Siyiex.
35 vAng pon tix tiex tu ka haktiex,
kheu haktiex mawh ngot dee deuh Siyiex caong tix piang.
Ka Yie-lu-sa-leung kawx ang pon tix tiex tu kah,
kheu mawh nawh veng dee ot simiang ting;
kheu haktiex mawh ngot dee deuh Siyiex caong tix piang.
36 Pie khawm hauk kaing tix kawx ang pon tix tiex tu kah,
mawh kheu ang maix pon kieh hauk kaing tix tieh tix lung,
phan ang mawh nan tieh tix paing pie tix gawng.
37 Pex krai lox,
pa mawh ah mawh,
pa ang mawh,
ah ang mawh;
phan kawn baox krai tom nie hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing pui caox heue.”
Grawng krax pa pawk bix
(Lai Lu-cia 6.29–30)
38 w“Pex hoik mhong lox pa sidiex gau pa ah nin:
‘Ngai son ngai,
rhang son rhang.’
39 Viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
ang pon tix pawk bix ka pui pa gut kheem ka pex.
40 Phan koe pui pa si mang ka si bax khawn dawm maix,
pie khawn goix kawx kieh nawh si mang ka heue!
Koe pui pa viex maix hoek nyiex prawng see,
sang teei ka kraung ceep plak daux maix,
pie kraung ceep plak praix kawx tawx ka nawh heue!
41 Phan koe puix pa khin pex klawm kraung tix hu tix kongli krax,
hu tau mai nawh ra kongli krax heue!
42 Koe pui pa sang mhaing kraung khrai ka pex,
tag caw tix tawx ka nawh;
koe pui pa mhaing tix vai kraung khrai ka pex,
tag caw tix tawx ka nawh heue.”
Grawng pa muh ka pui koeng
(Lai Lu-cia 6.27–28; 32–36)
43 y“Pex baox mhong ka lox pa sidiex gau ah nin:
‘Muh ka paox grawm maix,
koeng ka pui koeng ka maix.’
44 Viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
tag caw tix muh ka pui koeng ka pex,
khaix an rawg buan ka son pui pa map nai ka pex.
45 Yuh nin,
pex tom taug pon tix mawh kawn simex kawn bun keeing Siyiex.
Mawh kheu keeing Siyiex kieh singaix jhan ka piang pui mhawm,
khawm ka piang pui caox kawx nawh kieh jhan piang;
tawx lhiex lih ka piang pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm,
mai ka piang pui caox kawx nawh tawx kah.
46 Phan pex muh ngie ka pui pa muh ka pex khaing,
Siyiex sang chix pawk ceu pa mhawm ka pex yuh ka mawx lie?
Pie khawm pui koh keen kawx,
hoik chix yuh nang nin heue!
47 Phan pex chix si kah ngie tau mai paox grawm tix khaing,
sawn ex in mawh ceu pa tix lie?
Pie khawm been li pui tang ceu kawx hoik chix yuh nang nin heue!
48 zPex tag caw tix grum mhawm awm hoik grum mhawm keeing pex Siyiex piang rao ma yuh nan heue.”
