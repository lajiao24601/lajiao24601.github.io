Ya-sa-long tieh vheen
15
Khaix an,
Ya-sa-long rien khre ka ling law puing naing tix lhang mai brung tom nie,
mai baox ploi pui naing cao kia ngien tix tix phuan kaux mawh cao pa paoh daux krax son tix.
2 Nawh rien hoek cung ka praok krax ka diang siviex veng jheeng jheeng ka pon ngawb cao,
phan si pup pui pa simah paox tix mai sang hoek tix nhie simiang prawng,
Ya-sa-long tom nhie kix hoek ka tix,
mai chawk mawh kaoh nawh khaing veng dee mawx.
Khaix hoik krai pui an mawh tix ceu pui mawx ka nawh,
3 Ya-sa-long tom ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
maix hoik koe krax koe grawng,
viang mawh nan,
simiang ang ploi pui hoek nghiet ka lox pa viex maix.”
4 Khaix an,
nawh kawn baox ah nin tix gum:
“Aux simie tix mawh cao prawng see!
Yam si pup pui ka krax pa simah paox tix,
phan ang mawh nan koe lox pa sang khiox khaw,
tag chix tix hoek ka aux tin,
aux tag sang chix prawng ca nawh ih rauh see.”
5 Phan koe pui pa hu dex ka Ya-sa-long,
son sang krub ka nawh,
Ya-sa-long tag tawx taix tix yaog ka nawh,
mai ngheet ka nawh.
6 Ya-sa-long yuh nin ku kaux ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa hoek tix khiox khaw simiang prawng rauh see son tix,
mawh kheu nin,
nawh tom pon krax pa khaw rhawm kawn pui heue.
7 Khaix hoik luan pona num,
Ya-sa-long ah nin ka simiang Ta-wue:
“Keeing simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux hu ka Si-paw-loeng son sang deeh vied aux lox sijax mukheu pa hoik ah aux tix deuh ka Simiang Siyiex!
8 Yam ot ka veng Shui-li-yax haktiex Ci-sux,
aux hoik ap pong tix deuh lox sijax mukheu ka Simiang Siyiex,
phan nawh viex aux hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
aux tag sang krub nawh ka Si-paw-loengb heue.”
9 Simiang ah nin:
“Maix hu mhawm mhawm heue!”
Ya-sa-long tom hu ka Si-paw-loeng.
10 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ploi kawn cau pa jiang hu ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku cub,
ah nin:
“Yam mhong pex luah lox teet tawng,
pex caw tix kok yo ah nin:
‘Ya-sa-long hoik mawh simiang ka daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng!’ ”
11 Ya-sa-long kok pui ra yieh kaux hoek khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
hu tau mai tix.
Kix ang tawng kaing pa sang yuh nawh pie ceeix kawx,
mai ca tawng ngie tix hawt khaix nawh khaing.
12 Yam tan Ya-sa-long ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
nawh kawx ploi pui hu kok Ya-si-to-fu hoek khaing veng Ci-lawx.
Pui in hoik mawh cao teem ka simiang Ta-wue tix kaux.
Mawh kheu nan,
lox pa kok yo son pa tieh vheen nied ot nied tix ting,
mai pui pa hawt khaix Ya-sa-long nied ot nied tix haoh hun heue.
Ta-wue to hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung
13 Koe kawn cau hoek tix krai ka Ta-wue ah nin:
“Rhawm rhi pui Yi-seu-liex hu plak Ya-sa-long khawm uik heue.”
14 Ta-wue tom ah nin ka siliex tix khankix pa ot tau mai nawh ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung:
“Ex nied tix hu khaing tin,
phan ang mawh nan,
ex ang yaox tix pon luan khaing Ya-sa-long.
Nied tix phai hu heue!
Phan ang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang hoek tix yuh ex laux,
mai pauh pui pa ot ka daux veng yum khawm uik heue!”
15 Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
Vawng.
Yix hoik rien khre tix mhawm,
hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik tat maix heue!”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang mai pui daux nyiex ma nawh khawm uik khawm siliex nawh khankix lih hu,
mai deuh ngie moeng iak tix pau nyiex haw kao kaux khaing heue.
17 Simiang mai can pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix lih hu khaing veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kix tom paung ot ka nyiex pa ot ka siviex veng pa nghoik tix ot khaix paox tan.
18 Yam hu pui pa hawt tix pau simiang khankix ka plak sivoe simiang,
siliex simiang hoik cung praok nawh khawm uik.
Pui liah yieh kaux pa hawt rhawm nawh hoek khaing Cia-theux hu nang ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.
19 Simiang chawk cao kaing kix Yi-thaix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai hu tau mai yix lie?
Deeh ing ot tau mai simiang khraox heue!
Maix hoik mawh pui tang meung pa hu singai khaing meung to kaux tix,
mai to hoek mox tix dee in.
20 Bawg yam pa ot maix dee in kied dawt,
aux doh sang kieh maix vu vie tix hu tau mai aux lie?
Pie to kaux aux ang tawng dee sang hu tix paung ot.
Deeh ing heue,
viex paox ceu paox cad maix deeh ing tau mai maix!
Kieh Simiang Siyiex kia ngien maixc ka daux krax muh mai pa kaux sijax tix heue!”
21 Yi-thaix ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
aux tiex tu ka maix ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
maix hu dee mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang hawt ka maix,
yum ang ah,
im ang ah,
aux hoik but rhawm tix hawt maix heue.”
22 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Mhawm,
nied tix hu sivoe heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-thaix mai pui naing cie nawh,
khawm pui nyiex pui ma kix,
hawt tix hu krax tau mai kix.
23 Yam lih hu Ta-wue mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh,
kawn pui ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting.
Simiang tiang ka klawng iak Ci-loeng,
pui pa hawt khaix nawh kawx hawt tix tiang kah.
Kix hu ka plak daux tiag tau heue.
24 Yam an,
cao giex kaing li Sa-tu hoek nang.
Pui Li-we pa grawng teug sijax mukheu kawx ot tau mai nawh dee an.
Kix tahd ka teug sijax mukheu,
krox tom hoik lih luan pui khawm uik baox yaog ka heue.
Cao giex kaing li Ya-pi-ya-tha kawx ot dee ane.
25 Simiang ah nin ka Sa-tu:
“Maix grawng teug sijax mukheu deeh ing ka daux veng heue!
Phan Simiang Siyiex khaw rhawm ka aux,
tix singaix sax,
nawh tag sang deeh kieh aux hoek,
mai deeh pon tix yaox nawh ka dee pa deuh nawh ot kah.
26 Phan nawh ang khaw ka rhawm aux,
kieh nawh yuh aux awm pa simie nawh tix yuh heue!”
27 Simiang ca nied tix baox ah nin ka Sa-tu:
“Ang maix hoik mawh cao pa chix yaox sivoe lie?
Maix viex kawn simex tix Ya-si-ma-see mai Yaw-na-tang pa mawh kawn simex Ya-pi-ya-tha,
deeh ing mhawm mhawm ka daux veng.
28 Aux sang krox jun lox maix ka diang tiang plak an.”
29 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-tu mai Ya-pi-ya-tha grawng teug sijax mukheu deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai tom paung ot tan heue.
30 Ta-wue yiiem mai haok tix ka gawng sime.
Nawh haok ka caong giex tix,
mai laux rhawm tawb ka kaing tix.
Can pui pa hawt khaix nawh tawb ka kaing tix nang,
mai yiiem khawm hu tix.
31 Koe pui hoek tix krai ka Ta-wue,
krai hoik tieh vheen Ya-si-to-fu tau mai Ya-sa-long.
Ta-wue tom rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh krax pa tieh vheen Ya-si-to-fu,
ang pon tix lih mawh heue!”
32 Ta-wue hoek ka piang choek gawng,
ka dee krub Siyiex dee an,
paox grawm pa hawt rhawm nawh pa kaux Hu-sai pui Ya-ci riah kraung ceep tix griah,
mai lhop nyaox ka piang kaing tix,
hoek tix rieb nawh.
33 Ta-wue ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hawt ka aux,
mai ang lai mawh pa mhawm son aux pie ceeix kawx.
34 Phan maix deeh ing ka daux veng,
mai krai ka Ya-sa-long,
maix krai sang hawt tix rhawm nawh,
awm hoik hawt maix rhawm keeing nawh sivoe yuh nan,
khaix an maix sau riang tix tieb tie ka krax pa tieh vheen Ya-si-to-fu,
yuh nin hoik mawh teem maix ka aux heue.
35 Cao giex kaing li Sa-tu mai Ya-pi-ya-tha hoik ot dee an.
Maix chix tix krai can kaing pa hoik mhong tix ku ceu ka daux nyiex haw ka kix.
36 Kawn simex kix Ya-si-ma-see mai Yaw-na-tang hoik ot tau mai kix,
maix kieh kix viex lox pa hoik pon mhong tix khawm uik hoek tawx ka aux heue.”
37 Mawh kheu nan,
pao grawm Ta-wue pa kaux Hu-sai deeh ing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Yam an jiet caw bawg yam hoek Ya-sa-long dee an heue.
