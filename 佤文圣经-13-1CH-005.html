Doeh ceu pui Lui-piien
5
aPlak greem mawh doeh ceu cad kawn ai Ya-keux pa kaux Lui-piien.
(Lui-piien seeing brai yuh moeng iak keeing tix,
mawh kheu nan nawh tom kieh siawp sibrawm min kawn ai grai.
Siawp sibrawm in kix tom tawx ka Yaw-seux.
2 bCeu pui Yo-tax kied ting riang,
lih simiang khaing nawh tix kaux,
mai kuang ka piang ku ceu pui khawm uik.)
3 Kawn ai Ya-keux pa kaux Lui-piien koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ha-nawx,
Fa-lux,
Si-see-loeng,
Cia-mi heue.
4-6 cPlak greem mawh Yaw-eux pa hawt doeh ceu cad keeing tix toe tix hu:
pa mawh See-ma-ya,
Keu-keu,
See-me,
Mi-cia,
Li-ya-ya,
Pa-lix,
Pe-la.
Pe-la mawh cao kaing ceu pui Lui-piien,
Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux hoik giex nawh hu heue.
7 Ceu kaux simiang ceu pui cub nyiex Lui-piien hoik tiiem tix ot plak greem:
mawh Yie-lix,
Sa-cia-li-ya,
8 Pi-la.
Pi-la mawh kawn simex Ya-sa,
kawn saux See-ma,
pa mawh ceu pui nyiex ma Yaw-eux an.
Ceu pui nyiex ma in ot ka Ya-law-eu,
mai ot nang plak laong Ya-law-eu hoek ka si nax haktiex Ni-paw mai ka Pa-lix Mien.
9 Kix hoik koe khawng brie tom nie ka haktiex Ci-liex,
mawh kheu nan kix tom pon tix cie ka daux tiag plak lih singaix,
tom vih tix hoek ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti heue.
10 Ka daux baux bawg yam singaix simiang Sao-law,
ceu pui Lui-piien puing ka pui Sa-cia,
pauh ka kix,
mai pon tix cie ka haktiex kix pa ot plak lih singaix Ci-liex khawm uik heue.
Doeh ceu pui Cia-teux
11 Ceu pui Cia-teux ot ka daux haktiex Pa-sang,
pa mawh plak laong ceu pui Lui-piien,
vih tix hu plak lih singaix hoek ka Sa-cia.
12 Ceu pui cub nyiex an pa jauh tix pang mawh Yaw-eux;
khaix an ka daux ra kaux mawh Sa-fang;
haktiex Pa-sang ceu pui cub nyiex tang ceu khankix,
pa jauh tix pang mawh Ya-nai mai Sa-fa.
13 Ceu pui Cia-teux pa deuk khankix mawh ceu pui cub nyiex in plak greem aliah liah cub:
pa mawh Mi-cia-leux,
Mi-su-lang,
See-pa,
Yaw-laix,
Ya-kang,
Si-yax,
Si-paw.
14 Kix hoik mawh doeh ceu cad kawn simex Hu-lix pa kaux Ya-pi-hai.
Tax du kix phan ex krai deeh mawh:
Ya-pi-hai,
Hu-lix,
Yie-law-yax,
Ci-liex,
Mi-cia-leux,
Yie-see-sai,
Yie-ha-to,
Pu-see.
15 Kawn simex Ya-pi-tiie,
kawn saux Ku-ni pa kaux Ya-si mawh cao kaing ceu pui cub nyiex in kix.
16 Kix ot ka haktiex Pa-sang mai ka haktiex Ci-liex,
khawm daux pak rib Sa-loeng ku dauh. (
17 Ca rang in kix hoik mawh pa tiiem yam giex simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-thang mai simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ka kaing heue.)
Pui naing ceu pui plak lih singaix
18 Ceu pui Lui-piien mai ceu pui Cia-teux ra ceu,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singaix hoik koe pui naing pa kied chix puing naing pon mheen pon rheng aliah yieh tix gliah kaux;
kix mhawm siao ka kraung naing dieb tie naok,
vaig gawn,
mai ak puh.
19 Kix hu puing ka ceu pui Yi-thu,
ceu pui Na-fe-see,
mai Naw-tax pa mawh ceu pui Sa-cia.
20 Kix yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
mai khiox khaw nawh teem coi tix,
Siyiex nghiet lox rawg buan kix,
ap pui Sa-cia mai meung pa yuh paox pie tau maix kix ka daux tawm taix kix.
21 Kix hoik pon teei ka-la-uk khaing pui koeng dee an phuan mheen mu,
kawn yung tix nga phuan mheen mu,
la ra rheng mu,
mai baox giex ka pui kao mheen kaux.
22 Kix hoik pauh ka pui koeng tom nie,
mawh kheu ka daux glauh naing in,
hoik mawh alo Siyiex.
Kix ot ka daux haktiex dee an,
tom hoek daux singaix bawg yam uik grai kixd heue.
Ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singaix
23 Ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singaix paung ot ka haktiex Pa-sang,
hu ka plak laong hoek ka Pa-lix He-meung,
See-ni-eux,
mai gawng He-meung.
Kaux pui kix kied phai haok huan.
24 Ceu kaux cao kaing ceu pui kix tiiem tix plak greem:
mawh Yi-fu,
Yi-seex,
Yi-liex,
Ya-see-liex,
Yie-li-mi,
Heu-ta-we-yax,
Ya-tiie.
Pui in kix hoik mawh pui naing pa mhawm kaing yuh khawm uik,
mawh simiang pa hoik ao min ka daux ceu pui kix heue.
Hoik giex ka ceu pui plak lih singaix
25 Ceu pui plak lih singaix ra ceu,
mai kawn pui tix plak ceu,
ang yum rhawm ka Siyiex tax du kix,
tik vud ka nawh,
mai hu krub ka Siyiex pui pa ot haktiex an;
Siyiex hoik sax grheu ka pui haktiex an lih khaing haktiex in.
26 eMawh kheu nan,
Siyiex tom kieh Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Phu-leux (baox kaux Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux) hoek puing ka kix.
Vawng Phu-leux pon giex ka ceu pui Lui-piien,
ceu pui Cia-teux,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singaix,
mai deuh kix ot ka Ha-lax,
Ha-paw,
Ha-la,
mai ka praok rawm klawng Keu-sang heue.
