Saw-law-meung khre tix sum nyiex buan
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 2.1–18)
5
Si-lang simiang Thai-eux hoik dawg mawh paox grawm Ta-wue;
nawh mhong Saw-law-meung plud simiang mawh keeing tix Ta-wue,
tom ploi kawn cau pa ting tix hoek ka Saw-law-meung.
2 Saw-law-meung pawk lox Si-lang ah nin:
3 “Maix hoik tawng keeing aux Ta-wue ang pon sum nyiex buan son Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh,
mawh kheu puing nawh naing ka meung pui pa koeng,
pa ot sivie siveng nawh khawm pon daog ang jud.
Nawh tag sang krox jun khaix hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex nawh pon pex ka can pui pa koeng khawm uik,
tom tag pon yaox tix sum nyiex buan khaix heue.
4 Yam in,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux,
hoik kieh haktiex ot aux khawm pon daog pon krax pa nhim;
aux ang lai koe pui pa koeng,
mai ang lhat ka pui pa sang hoek puing ka tix heue.
5 aSimiang Siyiex hoik ap pong tix ah nin ka keeing aux Ta-wue:
‘Aux tag sang kieh kawn simex maix,
pa plud simiang mawh maix an sum nyiex buan son aux.’
Kheu nan,
aux ap tix sum nyiex buan son sang krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux.
6 Maix ploi pui hu kid khaox si oe bhawng ka haktiex Li-pa-noeng son aux.
Pui cie aux plak in tag sang hu yuh kaing tau mai kix;
aux kawx tag sang tawx mau riang kix awm pa hoik tat maix tix deuh.
Maix hoik tawng,
yix ang awm pex pui Si-toeng yuh nan,
chix tawng grawng sang kid tix khaox heue.”
7 Si-lang mhong lox tawx Saw-law-meung,
kied gaux rhawm kah;
mai ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex pon krax pa yaog yaw daux in singaix!
Nawh hoik tawx Ta-wue koe kawn simex pa koe ping nyia cuyi yuh nin,
son sang kuang kia ka ceu pui pa ting min!”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Si-lang pawk lox Saw-law-meung ah nin:
“Aux hoik rieb ka lox tawx maix;
aux tag hawt tix yuh awm lox pa tawx maix,
mai tawx ka khaox si oe bhawng mai khaox gix.
9 Pui pa yuh kaing aux khankix,
tag sang reu khaox rong hoek daux rawm sigrit sigra khaing Li-pa-noeng,
mad kaux kieh dia tix tau,
mai si bleue hoek ka dee pa hoik ciak maix.
Pui pa yuh kaing aux khankix tag yah ka khaox rong pa mad tix tau,
mai tawx ka pui pa yuh kaing maix priex.
Phan mawh ka plak maix an,
aux simie maix tawx priex preem ka daux nyiex haw aux heue.”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
Si-lang hawt tix tawx khaox si oe bhawng mai khaox gix khankix awm lox pa tawx Saw-law-meung;
11 Saw-law-meung tawx nghox ca ra rheng kongtoeng,
mai bee sime pa mhawm tipon mheen kongshengb ka Si-lang ku num,
kieh mawh priex preem ka daux nyiex haw nawh heue.
12 Simiang Siyiex hawt tix yuh awm lox ah tix,
mai tawx ping nyia cuyi ka Saw-law-meung.
Si-lang mai Saw-law-meung lai ling ka paox tix ka daux pa nhim,
mai yuh ka sijax mukheu heue.
13 Simiang Saw-law-meung sawg pui yuh kaing khaing ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex loe mheen kaux,
14 cmai tawx Ya-to-ni-lang mawh cao kuang ka piang kix.
Nawh guah kix lih koe loe cub,
tix cub tix mheen.
Tix cub ot ka Li-pa-noeng tix khix,
ot ka nyiex ra khix.
15 Saw-law-meung kawx tawx pui daix mheen kaux ot tix saik simaox ka piang gawng,
aliah mheen kaux mawh cao pa gah hu,
16 mai baox koe cao kuang kaing loe rheng loe yieh kaux pa kuang ka piang kaing yuh kix.
17 Kix hawt awm lox tawx simiang Saw-law-meung,
saik ka simaox ting pa koe ngawh,
mai kieh mawh jaong simaox nyiex buan.
18 Pui pa yuh kaing cie Saw-law-meung mai Si-lang,
khawm pui pa kaoh khaing veng Cia-pa-leux,
saik ka simaox,
rien khre ka khaox ox simaox rang,
son sang sum ka nyiex buan heue.
