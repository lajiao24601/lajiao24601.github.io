Puing ka ceu pui Mi-tiien
31
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix tag sang ciyang ka ceu pui Mi-tiien kheu pie lhien kix ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
khaix hoik yuh maix ka kaing in hoik,
maix tag sang hu khaing haktiex heue.”
3 Khaix an Maw-si ah nin ka kawn pui:
“Pex rien khre tix hu yuh naing,
hu puing ka ceu pui Mi-tiien,
son sang pawk pex bix son Simiang Siyiex.
4 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tix cub tix rheng kaux tawx hu puing naing heue.”
5 Khaix an,
tix ceu pui laug lih tix rheng kaux,
kaux pui pa pon hu yuh naing khawm uik koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux.
6 Maw-si ploi Fe-ni-ha kawn simex Yi-li-ya-sa cao giex kaing li mawh cao bung kaing kix hu;
Fe-ni-ha viex ka kraung pa koe ka daux nyiex buan mai teet tawng pa phlu luah.
7 Kix hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si puing ka pui Mi-tiien,
mai pauh ka can simex ku kaux,
8 khawm simiang pui Mi-tiien,
Yi-wex,
Li-cing,
Su-eux,
Hu-eux,
Li-pa phuan kaux.
Kix kawx pauh ka Pa-lang kawn simex Pi-eux heue.
9 Pui Yi-seu-liex giex ka bun Mi-tiien mai ka kawn nyawm khankix,
mai rhim ka moi yung kix,
mai tud rhim ka sin ngao kix khawm uik,
10 mai gleeing veng ot kix mai bang kix laux leeing.
11 Kix viex ka can pa pon giex tix hu,
khawm pui khawm khawng brie,
12 mai viex kix hu ka bang dee lung Maw-ya,
ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
si tieb veng Yie-li-keu,
mai tawx kix ka Maw-si,
Yi-li-ya-sa,
mai pui hun Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
Pui naing deeh ing
13 Maw-si,
Yi-li-ya-sa mai simiang pui tang kaux hu rieb ka pui naing khankix.
14 Maw-si song rhawm ka simiang naing mai cao kaing pa kaoh khaing puing tix naing khankix,
15 chawk ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai kawn kieh bun in kix klawd lie?
16 aAng pex tawng bun in kix hoik mawh pui pa nghiet ka lox pa krai Pa-lang,
hoik nyaw glaw pui tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex haktiex gawng Phi-eu,
mai kieh pui cie Simiang Siyiex pon ka jhawm saux preng.
17 Yam in,
pex pauh ka simex yum khawm uik,
mai ka khawm bun pa hoik it tau mai simex khankix,
18 viang mawh nan,
deuh ka can kawn nyawm bun pa awn ju,
mai bun krih son pex.
19 Ka daux pex pa hoik pauh ka pui khankix,
phan ang mawh nan,
pa theeh ka pui yum khankix,
mai tag caw tix ot ka plak praix nyiex bang aliah singaix.
Ka daux loe singaix mai ka daux aliah singaix,
mai pex tag caw tix kieh to kaux tix singax,
khawm bun miex pa pon giex pex hoek,
20 mai kieh singax khawm man ku plah,
khawm kraung pa yuh ka hak,
pa yuh ka hak pex,
mai ka khaox khankix heue.”
21 Yi-li-ya-sa cao giex kaing li ah nin ka pui pa hoek khaing puing tix naing khankix:
“Been pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si mawh nin:
22-23 Can kraung khrai pa ang chix laux ka daux pa gleeing ka ngu khankix,
awm pa mawh khri,
mau,
tawng,
rhiem,
hek,
si vun,
tag caw tix kieh singax khaing daux ngu,
mai kraung khrai pa deuk khankix tag caw tix teei rawm kieh singax singeh kah.
24 Ka daux aliah singaix,
pex tag caw tix si daux kraung ceep tix singax,
yuh nin,
pex tom tag sawn tix mawh pa singax singeh,
tom tag pon tix laig ka daux nyiex bang heue.”
Guah ka kraung pa pon khaing naing
25 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
26 “Maix mai Yi-li-ya-sa,
khawm simiang pui tang kaux khankix,
viex pa pon giex pex hoek khawm uik,
mai ka khawm pui khawm khawng brie simin uik ha,
27 khaix an guah greung lih koe ra plak,
tix plak tawx ka pui naing,
an tix plak tawx ka pui hun pa deuk khankix.
28 An tix plak pa guah tix tawx ka pui naing,
caw tix teei deuh lih phuan yieh son tix son,
mai tan mawh khawng phak ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh phuan yieh kaux pui pa pon giex pex mai deuh lih tix kaux;
moi,
la,
yung,
pex hoik awm paox tix.
29 Teei ceu in kix tawx ka cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa,
mai kieh mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
30 An tix plak pa guah tix tawx ot ka kawn pui,
mawh pui pa pon giex ang ah,
moi,
la,
yung,
pex ang ah,
tag caw tix teei lih tix phuan son tix son,
mai tawx ka pui Li-we pa kuang kia ka nyiex gox buan Simiang Siyiex khankix.”
31 Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex heue.
32-35 Lheu khaing kraung khrai pa kawn deuh pui naing khankix,
can pa pon tud rhim kix mawh:
yung tix gliah aliah mheen phuan rheng mu,
moi aliah mheen ra rheng mu,
la liah mheen tix rheng mu,
bun krih loe mheen ra rheng kaux.
36-40 An tix plak pa pon cao yuh naing mawh:
yung tix ngoe loe mheen aliah rheng phuan yieh mu,
ka daux phung yung in,
liah yieh atigliah phuan mu,
mawh son khawng phak pa sang tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
Pa mawh moi loe mheen liah rheng mu,
ka daux phung moi in kix atigliah ra mu,
mawh son khawng phak pa sang tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
La loe mheen phuan yieh mu,
ka daux phung la in kix tix gliah mai tix mu;
mawh son khawng phak pa sang tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex;
bun krih tix mheen liah rheng kaux,
ka daux bun in kix tix ngoe ra kaux,
tag sang tawx mawh cie Simiang Siyiex.
41 Maw-si hawt awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex tawx khawng phak ka Yi-li-ya-sa,
son sang kieh mawh khawng phak krax tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
42-46 Pa pon kawn pui tix plak hoik khaw mai pa pon pui naing:
yung tix ngoe loe mheen aliah rheng phuan yieh mu,
moi loe mheen liah rheng mu,
la loe mheen mai phuan yieh mu,
bun krih tix mheen liah rheng kaux.
47 Pa deeh pon Maw-si khaing ka daux cie kawn pui,
khawm pui khawm khawng brie,
teei lih phuan son tix son,
hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex,
mai tawx ka pui Li-we cao pa kuang kia ka nyiex gox buan Simiang Siyiex khankix heue.
48 Simiang naing pa bung pui naing khankix hoek ka Maw-si,
49 mai ah nin:
“Yix hoik simin ka pui naing pa ot greem tix,
tix kaux kawx ang lai cud.
50 Mawh kheu nan,
yix hoik viex kraung pa pon rhim tix pa mawh khri,
ble,
si gu,
jeep giien,
kreem,
mai maox soi pa pon teei tix ku kaux hoek,
mai tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
kieh mawh krax taoh ka ju ex,
son sang kia ngien Simiang Siyiex ka ex heue.”
51 Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa rieb tix teei ka kraung khri in kix,
pa hoik mawh kraung siao pa mhawm khawm uik.
52 Khri pa tan simiang naing khankix,
hoik mawh been ra yieh kongcing.
53 Pui naing khankix hoik tang teei tix deuh ka kraung khrai pa pon tix ka daux naing son to kaux tix.
54 Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa viex ka kraung khri in kix hoek ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
mai khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
