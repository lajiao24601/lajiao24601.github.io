Kum kawng ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
15
aKoe pui tix blah kaoh khaing Yo-thaix hoek ka Ang-thi-a,
mai jauh tix sidiex ka kawn Khrit khankix ah nin:
“Lheu khaing priex pex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
rieb ka been krax Pat-hak,
pex ang pon krax taoh.”
2 Kheu son kaing in,
Pao-law mai Pa-na-pa kiex ra kaux thung thiang ka kix.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui hun but rhawm tix ploi Pao-law,
Pa-na-pa,
mai ceu cao ra loe kaux haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mhaing lox ka kawn cau mai kuad song khankix son kaing in heue.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu cao tawx kix hu.
Ka daux krax,
kix laig ka daux haktiex Fe-ni-ci mai ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
mai krai pao grawng hoik yum rhawm pui tang ceu tang cad ka Siyiex ku dauh.
Can kawn Khrit mhong ka gum lox in,
mai kied koe krax pa nyawm rhawm ka khawm uik.
4 Yam hoek kix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kix pon krax pa rieb ceu cao,
kawn cau,
mai kuad song khankix ka kix.
Kix krai pao pui hun mhong ka grawng kaing pa hoik kieh Siyiex kiex yuh khawm uik.
5 Viang mawh nan,
koe kawn Khrit pui Fa-li-saix tix blah kaoh cung ah nin:
“Pui tang ceu tang cad tag caw tix rieb been krax pa Pat-hak,
tag caw priex tix hawt nang ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau mai kuad song khankix kum kawng son grawng kaing in.
7 bKhaix hoik thung thiang kix paox tix ka tom rheeng,
Pi-teux yaog tix kaoh cung ah nin:
“Paox grawm ka daux Khrit ku kaux,
pex hoik tawng grawng pa hoik dawg laug Siyiex aux khaing daux pex,
mai nhie aux sidiex gum lox buan hu ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
son sang kieh kix mhong mai yum rhawm kah.
8 cSiyiex pa hoik tawng sidawng ka daux rhawm rhi kawn pui an,
hoik teei tix tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
awm hoik tawx ka ex yuh nan,
mai teei kaing in jhi grawng nawh kawx rieb ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
9 Ka si nax ex mai kix,
Siyiex ang yuh kaing pa tang siyox,
viang mawh nan,
hoik kieh rhawm rhi kix singax kheu krax yum rhawm kix.
10 Kheu hoik mawh nin,
yam in yuh ka mawx pex lai kawn sang nyaw glaw ka Siyiex,
mai teei khaox glawm pa ang tax du ex mai to kaux ex pon klawm,
deuh ka piang klip pui gau pui tang ceu tang cad lie?
11 In ang khaw heue!
Ex hoik yum rhawm grawng pon ex krax taoh mawh ka daux buan son Simiang Ye-sux,
mai hoik awm kix yuh nan heue.”
12 Pui hun yien yawg tix nghiet ka lox pa krai pao Pa-na-pa mai Sao-law,
ka grawng pa tawx Siyiex kiex yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad khawm uik.
13 Khaix hoik uik krai ka lox,
Ya-keux kawx ah nin:
“Paox grawm ka daux Simiang Ye-sux ku kaux,
nghiet heue!
14 Si-meung hok krai sidawng grawng hoik kia ngien Siyiex yuh ka mawx ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai hoik kok pui hoek mawh cie tix khaing daux kix.
15 Gum lox pa krai cao jhai hoik khaw caw mai gum lox khawm uik.
Phuk lai sigang khri hoik tiiem ah nin:
16 dSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
khaix an,
aux tag sang deeh hoek,
tag sang deeh cox sum nyiex ma Ta-wue pa hoik ghrup.
Aux tag sang deeh cox sum khaing ka daux dee riem,
mai sum kieh mhawm heue.
17 Yuh nin,
can pui pa deuk khawm uik,
pui tang ceu tang cad pa hoik kok aux mawh cie tix,
tag sang sawg ka Simiang Ye-sux khawm uik heue.
18 “Simiang Ye-sux kaw pa jauh tom dix dix hoik tawx lox krai pao yuh nin heue.”
19 Ya-keux nied tix ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
phan mawh rhawm keud aux,
ex ang caw tix tawx krax pa nu ka piang pui tang ceu tang cad pa hoik hoek ka Siyiex.
20 eEx caw tix tiiem lai tawx lox ka kix,
ang pon tix ih ka priex preem pa ang singax kheu pa tan lu pui ka riang rao,
ang pon tix yuh krax pa phit vao,
ang pon tix ih ka khawng brie pa rhawt pui yum mai nham.
21 Mawh kheu tom yam jauh dix dix,
ku singaix tuah,
ka daux nyiex sidiex ku dauh,
koe pui pa krai pao ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
ka daux veng ku dauh pui hoik krai pao ka krax pa sidiex nawh hu khawm uik heue.”
Tawx lai ka kawn Khrit pui tang ceu tang cad
22 Yam in,
kawn cau mai kuad song khankix,
khawm ceu cao khawm uik but rhawm tix sang laug lih ka pui krai lox son pui,
ploi kix hu tau mai Pao-law,
Pa-na-pa hoek ka haktiex Ang-thi-a.
Kix laug ceu kaux tang pa kaux Yo-tax pui Pa-sha-pa mai Si-la.
Pui in kiex hoik dawg mawh pui pa nab yam yung yi kawn Khrit khankix kah.
23 Lai pa viex kix hu tiiem tix ah nin:
“Kawn cau mai pux aik pa mawh kuad song khankix chawk ka kawn Khrit tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka Ang-thi-a,
Shui-li-yax,
Ci-li-cia khankix mhawm ot heue!
24 Ex hoik koe pui tix blah hoik mawh pa lih hu khaing ex dee in;
lox pa hoik krai kix kieh pex ang tawng ka sang yuh tix.
Tete te lai,
ex ang bax tawx lox nhie kix hu krai lox an kix.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
ex rom tix kum kawng tau,
pui hun hoik khaw rhawm ka pa laug ex tix ploi pui pa krai lox son pui,
hoek ka pex dee an.
Kix tag sang hu tau mai pux aik pa muh ex Pa-na-pa mai Pao-law;
26 Pui in kiex hoik sax ang lhat ka sang laux ju tix kheu son pa yuh kaing Simiang Ye-sux Khrit.
27 Mawh kheu nan,
ex laug Yo-tax mai Si-la lih;
kiex tag sang krai pex mhong ka grawng kaing pa tiiem ot ka daux lai in.
28 Mawh kheu hoik cu Khuan Singax Singeh ka lox krai ex,
lheu khaing lox tat jhawm prawng pa ting ceu tix blah,
ang pon tix teei beeh glawm pa cien sau piang pex.
29 Pex ang pon tix ih ka priex preem pa tan lu ka riang rao;
ang pon tix ih ka nham mai ka khawng brie pa rhawt pui yum;
mai ang pon tix yuh krax pa phit vao nang.
Phan pex ang yuh ka ceu in kix ra loe ceu hoik mawh pa mhawm.
Kieh pex pon krax nyawm ot nyawm koe heue!”
30 Pui pa krai lox son pui khankix,
pon lox tawm yaog tix hu ka Ang-thi-a,
mai khrawm kawn Khrit khawm uik ot dee an,
tawx lai ka kix.
31 Yam phat kix ka lox lai in,
kix hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm tete,
kheu krax pa joh jhan lox lai in.
32 Yo-tax mai Si-la kawx hoik mawh pui krai lox jhai son pui tang Siyiex;
kiex krai lox tom nie ka kawn Khrit,
joh jhan ka kix,
tawx krax yum rhawm pa kaux cong ka kix.
33 Khaix hoik paung ot kiex dee an tom rheeng singaix bawg yam,
kawn Khrit tom tawx kiex ing ka daux krax pa gaux rhawm,
mai tawx lox nhie kiex ing mhawm mhawm heue.f
35 Pao-law mai Pa-na-pa paung ot ka Ang-thi-a tix vud tix cu;
kiex sidiex gau mai sidiex gum lox Simiang Ye-sux tau mai pui tang kaux khankix heue.
Pao-law mai Pa-na-pa guah paox tix
36 Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
Pao-law krai ka Pa-na-pa ah nin:
“Deeh ing ax ka nyiex yaong pa hoik sidiex ex gum lox Simiang Ye-sux ka sivoe an ku yaong,
mai jhak tix yaox grawh grawng kawn Khrit khankix.”
37 Pa-na-pa keud tix sang viex Yaw-hang Ma-kheu hu tau mai tix,
38 gviang mawh nan,
Pao-law ang cu;
mawh kheu bawg sivoe nawh ang cu tix ot yuh kaing tau mai kiex,
mai lih hu khaing kiex ka Phang-fe-li-yax.
39 Kiex hoik simah paox tix son kaing in,
dee si dauh guah paox tix.
Pa-na-pa viex Ma-kheu brug reu hu ka Sai-phu-lu-see,
40 Pao-law laug cie tix ka Si-la,
Khaix hoik pon kix krax kia ngien pa ap kawn Khrit khankix tix tawx ka daux buan son Simiang Ye-sux,
kix kawx hoik lih hu.
41 Nawh hoik tauh hu ka haktiex Shui-li-yax mai Ci-li-cia,
mai tawx krax yum rhawm pa kaux cong ka pux aik pux o ceu cao ku dauh heue.
