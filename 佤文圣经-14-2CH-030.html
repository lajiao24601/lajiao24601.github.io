Rien khre tix yuh Poi Luat-luan
30
1-3 aPui haoh pui hun ang pon yuh Poi Luat-luan ka daux khix tix pa keud tix sang yuh,
mawh kheu cao giex kaing li pa singax singeh ka daux been ang kub,
khaix an koe pui tom nie kawn ang bax hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu nan,
simiang Si-si-cia mai simiang khankix,
khawm pui pa ot Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik khaw rhawm ka sang yuh Poi Luat-luan ka daux khix ra.
Simiang ploi pui hu krai ka pui hun Yi-seu-liex khawm uik mai ka pui Yo-tax,
mai baox blawk tix tiiem lai tawx ka ceu pui Yi-fa-liien mai ceu pui Ma-na-si,
kok kix hoek gaux rhawm tau yuh Poi Luat-luan ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung,
yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex.
4 Simiang mai pui hun kied gaux rhawm ka grawng in.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
kix kok can pui Yi-seu-liex,
jauh ka Tang pa ot plak laong hoek ka Piie-see-pa pa ot plak seh,
rom tix hoek tau ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hawt awm been pa deuh lox tat jhawm prawng gaux rhawm yuh ka Poi Luat-luan;
sivoe in,
ang pui sax haoh hun awm in.
6 Pui cao tawx lai lih hu awm lox pa tawx simiang mai cao kaing khankix,
mai tawx lai kok in plak greem tauh hu haktiex Yo-tax mai haktiex Yi-seu-liex ku daux ku dee ah nin:
“Kawn pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex hoik mawh pui pa yaox tix luat luan khaing yam hoek simiang Ya-sux tix puing ka pex.
Pex tag caw veeh tix hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux,
nawh tom tag veeh tix ka pex.
7 Paw vi hawt ka tax du pex mai ka paox ceu paox cad pex;
kix mawh paox ceu paox cad pex pa ang yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix.
Awm pa hoik yaox pex ka ngai tix yuh nan,
Siyiex hoik ciyang kix tom kin.
8 Paw vi kiien rhawm awm kix yuh nan;
caw tix nghiet lox Simiang Siyiex.
Pex tag caw tix hoek ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung;
in hoik mawh dee pa kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex singax singeh leeing ju leeing phan.
Pex hoek tix krub ka nawh,
nawh tom ang lai baox song rhawm ka pex.
9 Phan pex veeh tix hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
pui an kix pa giex tix viex pui nyiex pui ma pex hu tag sang chix caox rhawm ka pex,
mai kieh kix deeh hoek.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex mawh pa grum naog mai krax muh krax rhawm,
mai koe krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi.
Pex veeh tix hoek ka nawh,
nawh tag rieb ka pex heue.”
10 Pui tawx lai an hoik tauh hu ka haktiex Yi-fa-liien mai haktiex Ma-na-si,
hu plak laong mai kawn hoek ka veng ceu pui Si-pu-loeng ku dauh.
Viang mawh nan,
pui dee an nyiah ka kix,
map nai ka kix.
11 Viang mawh nan kawx,
ka daux ceu pui Ya-seux,
ceu pui Ma-na-si,
ceu pui Si-pu-loeng ca kawn koe pui pa gaux rhawm tix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
12 Siyiex kawx hoik yuh kaing ka daux pui Yo-tax,
mai kieh kix grum rhawm tix priex tix hawt ka alo nawh,
nghiet ka lox tawx simiang mai ka lox tawx cao kaing khankix heue.
Gaux rhawm yuh Poi Luat-luan
13 Pui tom nie khrawm tix gaux rhawm yuh Poi Tik-su-saix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung daux khix ra.
14 Kix teei tix tik ka can khu pa gleeing tix tan lu nyawng bhawng ka daux klawng iak Ci-loeng pa ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
15 Ka daux khix an kao pon singaix,
kix pauh kawn yung kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie Poi Luat-luan.
Cao giex kaing li pa ang singax khankix mai pui Li-we hoik koe krax kaig kah,
kix tom kieh to kaux tix singax,
mai viex Krax-tan Pa-gleeing hoek ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
16 Kix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa krai Maw-si pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
mai cung ka nyiex buan ka dee khaw kix tix cung.
Pui Li-we tawx nham khawng tan ka cao giex kaing li,
cao giex kaing li tom phruah nham ka piang khu dee tan.
17 Pui tom nie kheu ang singax singeh,
to kaux kix ang pon tix pauh ka kawn yung Poi Luat-luan,
mawh kheu nan pui Li-we tom pauh son kix,
mai tan lu kawn yung ka Simiang Siyiex.
18 Lheu khaing an,
ceu pui Yi-fa-liien,
ceu pui Ma-na-si,
ceu pui Yi-sa-cia,
ceu pui Si-pu-loeng tom nie khankix ang bax kieh to kaux tix singax singeh,
mai ih ka nex kawn yung Poi Luat-luan;
in mawh pa ang khaw mai been.
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Si-si-cia rawg buan son kix ah nin:
19 “Simiang Siyiex pa koe krax caox rhawm,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du yix eue,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka pui ang singax singeh,
mawh kheu kix ca hoik krub maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.”
20 Simiang Siyiex nghiet lox rawg buan Si-si-cia,
mai pluat ploi ka kix.
21 Pui haoh pui hun pa khrawm paox tix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix,
tix neh aliah singaix,
mai gaux rhawm gaux rhi yuh ka Poi Tik-su-saix.
Ku singaix,
pui Li-we mai cao giex kaing li sau riang tixb yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
22 Si-si-cia yaog yaw pui Li-we,
mawh kheu hoik chix duk thung kix kawn pui krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
Baox yuh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi
Pui haoh pui hun tan lu Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
mai yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix.
Khaix hoik luan aliah singaix,
23 pui hun but rhawm tix baox deeh yuh krax gaux rhawm aliah singaix,
mawh kheu nan,
kix baox gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix yuh.
24 Simiang Si-si-cia tan moi mhaing tix rheng mu mai yung aliah rheng mu,
tawx pui hun pauh tix ih;
cao kaing khankix baox tan moi mhaing tix rheng mu mai yung tix mheen mu.
Cao giex kaing li tom nie hoik kieh to kaux tix singax.
25 Pui Yo-tax,
cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
mai kawn pui pa ot plak laong,
mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka daux Yi-seu-liex mai ka daux Yo-tax khankix,
hoik kied gaux rhawm khawm uik.
26 Veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik naog krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
mawh kheu singaix yam jauh ka kawn simex Ta-wue simiang Saw-law-meung,
ang sax yuh ka poi pa ting yuh nan tix bawg kawx.
27 Cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka kawn pui.
Siyiex mhong ka lox kix,
mai nghiet lox rawg kix buan khaing nyiex rao ma heue.
