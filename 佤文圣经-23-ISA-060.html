Min plak ngai Yie-lu-sa-leung singaix pa sang hoek
60
Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
kaoh heue,
riang awm pa riang singaix yuh nan;
min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex jhan riang ka maix.
2 Jhak heue,
pa faik tawb kheem ka haktiex;
veei pa faik lhop ka piang kawn pui piang haktiex.
Viang mawh nan,
pa riang Simiang Siyiex jhan riang ka maix;
pa riang min plak ngai nawh tag sang jhan riang ka piang to kaux maix.
3 Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang hoek dex ka pa riang maix;
simiang haktiex tag sang ot ka daux pa riang maix heue.
 
4 Yaog ngai tix jhak ka kaing pa lih tix mawh ka sivie siveng maix:
paox ceu paox cad maix khrawm paox tix,
mai sang hoek tix sawg ka maix.
Kawn simex maix tag sang kaoh khaing dee singai.
Kawn bun maix tag sang awm kawn ngie pa pux pui hoek;
5 yam yaox maix ka in khawm uik,
mai rhawm maix tag sang gaux ka tete;
rhawm rhi maix tag sang gaux rhawm jang luh tix.
Rawm sigrit sigra plak an,
mau khri tag sang lih hoek khaing;
sin ngao meung piang haktiex khawm uik tag sang laig ka daux tawm taix maix heue.
 
6 Jauh ka Mi-tiien,
Yi-fa,
ka-la-uk tag sang tia tix hoek;
kix tang khri mai sidah bhawng Law-ban kaoh khaing See-pa.
Kawn pui tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex.
7 Can kawn yung Ci-tax khawm uik tag sang viex hoek ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
can kawn yung mhaing Ni-pai-yaw khawm uik hoik kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
tan lu ka piang khu dee tan,
kieh Simiang Siyiex khaw rhawm kah.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh nyiex buan nawh lheu koe min plak ngai khaing sivoe an heue.
 
8 An kix pa awm pai awm pu tix hoek,
pa awm si kaux pa deeh hoek ka mheem tix,
kix mawh mawx lie?
9 Kix mawh pui yuh lai gee pa brug reu deeh hoek khaing klawn tiex dee singai,
tang ka pui cie Siyiex deeh ing ka haktiex tix heue.
Kix viex ka mau khri hoek,
tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh;
mawh kheu nawh hoik kieh kawn pui piang meung haktiex yung yi ka pui cie tix heue.
 
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yie-lu-sa-leung:
pui tang ceu tang cad tag sang yuh guh veng son maix;
Vawng kix tag sang yuh kaing jhaw son maix.
Aux hoik sax ciyang ka maix yam song rhawm song rhi tix,
viang mawh nan,
yam in aux tag sang tawx buan son krax muh ka maix.
11 aSiviex veng maix tag sang paoh tix leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
mawh son sang kieh simiang meung ka piang haktiex hoek tix tawx ka sin ngao.
12 Ceu pui pa ang yuh kaing jhaw maix tag sang laux leeing khawm uik;
meung haktiex kix tag sang lih tix riem khawm uik heue.
 
13 Rong khaox pa blawn mhawm ka daux tiag khaox Li-pa-noeng mawh:
khaox gix,
khaox paw,
khaox huang-yang,
hoik viex hoek cox sum ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kieh nyiex buan aux mhawm greeh mhawm grang,
kieh veng pa singax singeh aux kied sidaing mhawm grang heue.
14 bKawn praix pa hoik gut kheem ka maix tag sang grux ngawng ka maix;
can pui pa hoik map nai ka maix tag sang krub maix khawm uik.
Kix tag sang kok maix mawh “veng Simiang Siyiex”,
kok maix mawh “veng Shi-ang Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh heue.”
 
15 Pui ang lai baox tik vud ka maix,
koeng khoi ka maix,
ang lai baox mawh veng pa ang kawn pui ot ka.
Aux tag sang kieh maix mhawm pak mhawm peung,
lih mawh haktiex dee gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
16 Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang hoek tix kia ngiien ka maix;
simiang ka piang haktiex tag sang hoek tix eei ka maix.
Singaix yam an maix tag sang tawng aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa taoh ka maix;
aux mawh cao taoh pa ting siawp sibrawm pa taoh ka Yi-seu-liex heue.
 
17 Aux tag sang teei khri tang son tawng,
mau tang son rhiem,
tawng tang son khaox,
rhiem tang son simaox heue.
Simiang ka piang maix ang lai baox pon gut kheem ka maix;
aux tag sang kieh kix teei pa rauh pa see priex ka maix.
18 Ka daux haktiex ot maix ang lai baox mhong ka lox pa tud pa rhim;
ka daux meung haktiex maix ang lai baox yaox krax dug dig pui.
Aux tag sang kia ngiien ka maix awm kia ngiien tix ka guh veng yuh nan;
maix tag sang yaog yaw aux kheu krax pa taoh aux heue.
 
19 cYam pon ngaix,
maix ang lai lo ka singaix;
yam pon sawm,
maix ang lai lo ka khix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex maix,
hoik mawh pa riang ka maix leeing ju;
aux pa mawh pa riang min plak ngai Siyiex maix,
tag sang jhan riang ka maix.
20 Singaix ang lai laig daux gawng leeing ju;
khix kawx ang lai mox tix glip leeing ju.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
hoik mawh pa riang maix leeing ju;
singaix bawg yam laux rhawm laux rhi maix tag sang naox tix.
21 Pui cie maix tag sang prawng rauh see;
kix tag sang plud tix cie ka haktiex in leeing ju.
Aux teei taix tix phan yuh ka kix,
sum ka kix,
mawh son sang jhi ka min plak ngai aux heue.
22 Pex pa mawh nyiex ma pa iak pa lied,
tag sang lih mawh ceu pui pa ting pa lhaong,
ceu pui pa iak riang iak rao tag sang lih mawh meung pa ting riang ting rao.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
yam hoik khaw hoik caw bawg yam,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kaing in lih tix mawh heue.
