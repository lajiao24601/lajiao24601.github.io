Lai Thi-maw-thaix Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Thi-maw-thaix mawh kawn Khrit pui pa nhum ju ka Ya-si-yax pa iak tix kaux; miex nawh mawh pui Yo-thaix, keeing nawh mawh pui Si-lax. Ka daux pa yuh Pao-law kaing jhaw, Thi-maw-thaix lih mawh paox pa yuh nawh kaing tau mai cao teem coi nawh. Lai Thi-maw-thaix Phuk Tix pa tawx Pao-law, kaing ting koe loe grawng:
1. Ne raw ceu cao rien tix ka ceu pa gau been li pa pie lhien. Ceu pa gau been li in neuex tix tau mai rhawm keud pui Yo-thaix mai pui pa ang mawh pui Yo-thaix, kix yum rhawm ka haktiex mawh pa caox pa caig; phan pui sang kieh tix pon krax taoh, tag caw een ngie rhawm tix ka ngai lai pa raux pa glip, mai tuah ka priex preem ceu pa caw pui tix tuah, mai ot nong nong tix heue.
2. Lox pa krai ka ceu cao mai krax krub, krai lao pak peung pa sang caw simiang ceu cao mai pui cao teem coi tix koe heue.
3. Sidiex gau grawng mawh Thi-maw-thaix siliex pa kaux sijax Ye-sux Khrit yuh ka mawx, grawng sau kix riang tix ne raw sidiex gau ka kawn Khrit pa ang awm paox tix khankix heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–2)
2. Lox pa krai ka ceu cao mai ka simiang ceu cao khankix (1.3–3.16)
3. Tuk thung Thi-maw-thaix sang mawh nawh siliex pa kaux sijax yuh ka mawx (4.1–6.21)
