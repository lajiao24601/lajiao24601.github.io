Dee ot nyiex bang ceu pui mawx ceu pui an
2
Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng:
2 Yam sum ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ka nyiex bang,
kix tag sang tang ot ka greem man chix cie tix ku kaux,
hawt ka mhai man chix ceu pui cie tix,
mai sum nyiex bang hawt praok nyiex gox buan man heue.
3-9 Plak lih singaix mawh haktiex dee ot Yo-tax:
pui pa ot ka greem man chix cie Yo-tax khankix,
tag sang hawt ka kru naing cub caw tix,
kix tag sum nyiex bang ot ka sigreung dee ot simiang ceu pui cie kix tan heue.
Ceu pui Yo-tax:
simiang mawh Na-suing pa mawh kawn simex Ya-mi-na-ta,
kaux pui koe aliah mheen pon rheng liah yieh kaux.
Ceu pui Yi-sa-cia:
simiang mawh Na-thang-yiex pa mawh kawn simex Su-yax,
kaux pui koe phuan mheen pon rheng pon yieh kaux.
Ceu pui Si-pu-loeng:
simiang mawh Yi-li-ya pa mawh kawn simex Si-loeng.
Kaux pui koe phuan mheen aliah rheng pon yieh kaux.
Can kaux pui pa ot ka haktiex in khawm uik koe kao daix mheen liah rheng pon yieh kaux.
Cub kru naing ceu pui Yo-tax khankix mawh cao pa bung hu sivoe heue.
10-16 Ka plak seh mawh haktiex dee ot Lui-piien:
pui pa ot ka greem man chix cie Lui-piien khankix,
tag sang hawt ka kru naing cub caw tix,
kix tag sum nyiex bang ot ka sigreung dee ot simiang ceu pui cie kix tan heue.
Ceu pui Lui-piien:
simiang mawh Yi-li-su pa mawh kawn simex See-tio-eux,
mai kaux pui koe pon mheen liah rheng phuan yieh kaux.
Ceu pui Si-miien:
simiang mawh See-lu-mi pa mawh kawn simex Su-li-sa-taix,
mai kaux pui koe phuan mheen dim rheng loe yieh kaux.
Can pui Cia-teux:
simiang mawh Yi-li-ya-sa pa mawh kawn simex Tiu-eu,
mai kaux pui koe pon mheen phuan rheng liah yieh tix phuan kaux.
Can pui pa ot ka daux haktiex in khawm uik koe kao phuan mheen tix rheng pon yieh tix phuan kaux.
Cub kru naing Lui-piien tag hawt khaix cub kru naing Yo-tax hu heue.
17 Khaix an,
ka si nax pa hu sivoe ra cub mai pa dah khaix ra cub,
ceu pui Li-we tag sang grawng ka nyiex gox buan man hu sivoe.
Kru naing ku cub tag sang hawt tix sum ka nyiex bang yuh nan,
tang hu ot ka greem man chix cie tix laing laing heue.
18-24 Ka plak klip singaix mawh haktiex dee ot Yi-fa-liien:
pui pa ot ka greem man chix cie Yi-fa-liien khankix,
tag sang hawt ka kru naing cub caw tix,
kix tang sum nyiex bang ot ka sigreung dee ot simiang ceu pui cie kix tan heue.
Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien:
simiang mawh Yi-li-sa-ma pa mawh kawn simex Ya-mi-fu,
mai kaux pui koe pon mheen mai phuan yieh kaux.
Ceu pui Ma-na-si:
simiang mawh Cia-ma-liex pa mawh kawn simex Pi-ta-su,
mai kaux pui koe loe mheen ra rheng ra yieh kaux.
Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming:
simiang mawh Ya-pi-tang pa mawh kawn simex Ci-to-ni,
mai kaux pui koe loe mheen phuan rheng pon yieh kaux.
Can pui pa ot ka daux haktiex in khawm uik mawh kao mheen daix rheng tix yieh kaux.
Kru naing pui Yi-fa-liien hawt hu khaix kru naing pui Lui-piien mai pui Li-we heue.
25-31 Ka plak laong,
mawh haktiex dee ot Tang:
pui pa ot ka greem man chix cie Tang khankix,
tag sang hawt ka kru naing cub caw tix,
kix tag sum nyiex bang ot ka sigreung dee ot simiang ceu pui cie kix tan heue.
Ceu pui Tang:
simiang mawh Ya-si-yi-shiex pa mawh kawn simex Ya-mi-sa-tai,
mai kaux pui koe liah mheen ra rheng aliah yieh kaux.
Ceu pui Ya-seux:
simiang mawh Pha-ciex pa mawh kawn simex Eu-lang,
mai kaux pui koe pon mheen tix rheng phuan yieh kaux.
Ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix:
simiang mawh Ya-si-la pa mawh kawn simex Yi-nang,
mai kaux pui koe phuan mheen loe rheng pon yieh kaux.
Can pui pa ot ka daux haktiex in khawm uik mawh kao phuan mheen aliah rheng liah yieh kaux,
Kru naing cub caw Tang hu hawt khaix paox tix uik heue.
32 Hawt awm ka daux haktiex kix ku dauh,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
can pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang koe tix gliah mheen loe rheng phuan yieh tix phuan kaux.
33 Viang mawh nan,
yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
ceu pui Li-we ang tiiem ca rang tix ot tau mai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux khankix heue.
34 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si khawm uik,
kix sum nyiex bang tang ot ka greem man chix cie tix,
tang hu hawt ceu pui cie tix heue.
