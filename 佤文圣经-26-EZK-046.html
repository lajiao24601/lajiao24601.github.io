Simiang mai poi
46
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Ka daux singaix yuh kaing jhaw liah singaix,
tag caw tix nyip ka siviex pa koe plak lih singaix ka plak praix brawk.
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix tuah mai yam haok khix tix ngaix,
tag caw tix paoh ka siviex pa koe plak lih singaix.
2 Yam tan lu cao giex kaing li ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka simiang,
simiang tag sang laig ka grah siviex jauh ka plak praix brawk,
mai cung ka praok rong siviex.
Nawh tag sang krub ka dee an,
khaix hoik krub tag sang deeh lih.
Siviex in tag sang paoh tix ang jud,
sivoe sang faik praix ang pon tix nyip.
3 Singaix tuah ku ngaix mai yam haok khix tix ngaix,
kawn pui meung haktiex khawm uik tag caw tix krub Simiang Siyiex ka sivoe siviex in.
4 Ka daux singaix tuah,
simiang tag caw tix tan lu kawn yung pa mhawm loig liah mu,
mai yung mhaing tix mu ka Simiang Siyiex,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
5 Pex gawng ku mu tag caw tix tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi kao aliah kongsheng plak;
kawn yung ku mu pa hoik tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi mai chix tix hawt tix tan lu awm rhawm keud simiang yuh nan.
Krax-tan Nghox-koi kao aliah kongsheng plak tag caw tix tan tau mai bee sime loe kongsheng.
6 “Yam haok khix tix ngaix,
nawh tag sang tan lu ka kawn moi mhaing pa mhawm loig tix mu,
kawn pex liah mu,
yung mhaing tix mu.
7 Moi mhaing tix mu,
yung mhaing tix mu tag caw tix tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi kao aliah kongsheng plak;
kawn yung ku mu pa hoik tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi chix tix hawt tix tan lu awm rhawm keud simiang yuh nan.
Krax-tan Nghox-koi kao aliah kongsheng plak,
tag caw tix tan tau mai bee sime loe kongsheng.
8 Simiang tag sang laig lih ka grah siviex jauh ka plak praix brawk heue.
9 “Mawh poi ceu tix ang ah,
yam hoek kawn pui tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa laig ka diang siviex plak laong,
caw tix lih khaing diang siviex plak seh;
pa laig ka ding siviex plak seh,
tag caw tix lih khaing diang siviex plak laong.
Pui ang pon tix kieh laig lih ka diang siviex tix mu khaing,
tag caw tix lih khaing diang siviex pa veeh tix plak paox.
10 Yam laig kawn pui daux nyiex,
simiang kawx tag sang laig;
yam lih kawn pui khaing daux nyiex,
simiang kawx tag sang lih.
11 Yam yuh poi mai singaix yam yuh krax gaux rhawm pa ting,
yam tan ka moi mhaing tix mu,
yung mhaing tix mu,
tag caw tix tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi kao kongsheng aliah plak.
Kawn yung ku mu pa hoik tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi chix tix tan hawt awm rhawm keud pui pa yuh krax tan.
Krax-tan Nghox-koi kao aliah kongsheng plak,
tag caw tix tan tau mai bee sime loe kongsheng heue.
12 “Yam siao simiang Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim tan krax tan pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix paoh ka siviex pa koe plak lih singaix phlat hu ka plak daux brawk.
Nawh tag sang hawt ka been pa tat singaix tuah tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
Khaix hoik lih hu nawh,
tag caw tix nyip ka siviex heue.”
Krax tan ku singaix
13 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ku singaix pon ngawb cao,
tag caw tix tan ka kawn yung pa mhawm loig tix mu pa koe tix num,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tawx ka Simiang Siyiex.
Tag caw tix tan ka krax tan ceu in ku singaix.
14 Ku singaix pon ngawb cao tag caw tix tan nang ka miien nghox ca ra kongcing,
mai bee sime tix kongsheng kieh mawh Krax-tan Nghox-koi;
bee in mawh pa siao tix klao ka miien nghox ca.
Been Krax-tan Nghox-koi ka Simiang Siyiex koe dee bleeh leeing ju.
15 Ku singaix pon ngawb cao tag caw tix tan ka kawn yung,
ka miien nghox ca,
mai bee sime ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang pon tix kieh jud heue.”
Simiang mai haktiex
16 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Phan simiang tawx haktiex cie tix ka kawn simex tix,
mai haktiex an tag mawh sin ngao cie kawn simex nawh leeing ju leeing phan.
17 bPhan simiang tawx haktiex cie tix ka siliex tix,
mai haktiex an vai tix mawh ngie cie siliex nawh khaing,
yam hoek daux ‘num pa deeh’a,
siliex nawh tag sang deeh tawx haktiex an ka simiang tix.
Mawh kheu in hoik mawh sin ngao nyiex ma simiang,
mawh ngie simiang mai kawn simex nawh pa pon tix cie ka haktiex in leeing ju.
18 Simiang ang pon tix tud rhim ka sin ngao cie kawn pui.
Nawh tag caw tix tawx kawn simex tix pon ka haktiex pa hoik guah lih khaing nyiex ma simiang,
son ang nawh sang gut kheem ka kawn pui,
tud rhim ka haktiex pui cie aux heue.”
Nyiex priex nyiex buan
19 Khaix an,
pui an thung aux laig dex ka diang siviex plak daux nyiex buan pa koe ka plak seh,
hoek ka dee laig ka lawg nyiex an kix pa veeh tix plak laong;
lawg nyiex pa koe daux nyiex mawh pa singax singeh,
mawh pa tawx cao giex kaing li siao.
Nawh jhi aux yaox ka lawg nyiex in ka plak klip singaix tix dauh.
20 Nawh ah nin:
“Dee in mawh pa tawx ka cao giex kaing li son sang ngaik tix yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map mai pa siao ka son Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud,
mai mawh nang pa siao ka Krax-tan Nghox-koi.
Yuh nin,
kix tag ang lai siao viex kraung pa singax lih plak praix brawk,
kieh mat yuh pui heue.”
21-22 Khaix an,
nawh thung aux lih hu plak praix brawk,
mai jhi aux yaox ka plak praix brawk khawm pon daog,
daog ku mu hoik koe ka brawk iak khawm uik,
krax lang koe tix nga kongjhee,
krax vieh koe kao phuan kongjhee.
23 Brawk ku mu hoik mawh ding simaox pa koe ka khawm uik,
koe mawk ngu pa neung tix ka ding simaox.
24 Pui an krai ka aux ah nin:
“In mawh nyiex priex.
Pui pa yuh kaing nyiex buan ngaik ka krax tan pa tan kawn pui dee in heue.”
