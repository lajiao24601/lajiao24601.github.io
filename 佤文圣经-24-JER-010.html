Pa krub riang krub rao mai pa krub ka daux pa mawh pa meh
10
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa krai Yie-heu-hua ka pex.
2 Nawh krai ah nin:
pex paw vi hawt ka been krax ot krax koe meung tang meung khankix.
Viang kix hoik yaox krax pa tuk tiak riang su sai rao ma kawx,
pex paw vi yaoh yee awm kix yuh nan.
3 Pa krub kix ang tie koe ngawh jum dee bleeh pie ceeix kawx,
hoik mawh ngie rong khaox pa kid pui khaing daux nong khaox khaing,
pa hoik saik cang mai khankix khaing,
4 pa hoik teei khri mau yuh kah,
pa hoik teei jim rhiem tawk kaux kah,
kieh nawh ang chix goh.
5 Riang rao an kix hoik awm riang pui nyhee glauh ka daux kaung ma ke khankix yuh nan:
Kix ang chix si kah,
ang chix hu krax,
kieh pui grawng tix hu.
Mawh kheu nan,
paw vi lhat ka kix heue!
Kix ang pon yuh pex laux leeing,
ang pon teem ka pex heue!
 
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tiag tix tau mai maix;
maix kied sidaing ting,
ceu kaux pa singax maix ting siawp sibrawm.
7 aMaix mawh simiang ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik,
pui mawx ang lhat graung yung yi ka maix lie?
Maix khaw tix pon krax pa nab yam yung yi.
Pui pa koe ping nyia mai simiang meung ka piang haktiex ang koe pa pon lheu khaing maix tix kaux kawx.
8 Kix hoik daox aw pheem jhawm khawm uik;
kix sang chix pon gau tix pon ceu pa tix khaing riang rong khaox an lie?
9 Riang an hoik sau mau pa kaoh khaing Tha-see koe kah,
khri pa viex kaoh khaing Wu-fax.
Kraung pa ceep kix pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
hoik mawh ceu pa yuh cang mai khaox,
cang mai mau ka taix tix.
10 Simiang Siyiex eue,
mawh ngie maix pa mawh Siyiex pa mawh pa meh tix kaux khaing,
pa mawh Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
simiang pa im ju leeing ju.
Yam song rhawm maix,
haktiex hoik yung yang;
krax pa song rhawm maix hoik kieh meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik ang lai pon cung kaux cong heue.
 
11 Pui cie aux eue,
pex tag caw tix krai pao ka kix,
riang rao ang mawh siyiex pa phan rao ma haktiex.
Ceu an kix tag sang laux leeing tete,
mai grai grup khaing ka piang haktiex heue.
Lai rhax pa yaog yaw ka Siyiex
12 Simiang Siyiex teei ka ting siawp sibrawm tix phan yuh ka haktiex;
nawh teei ping nyia cuyi tix sum ka haktiex kaux cong,
mai teei krax pa lhak lhiao tix nih ka rao ma.
13 Yam tawx nawh gum lox tix,
rawm ka piang rao ma tag sang puk pak;
nawh kieh lon pai awm lih khaing mee haktiex pa si dawm.
Nawh kieh ngu sah jhan riang ka daux rawm lhiex;
nawh kieh bhaung lih phlu khaing nyiex krao tix.
14 Ceu in kix hoik kieh pui daox aw kah;
cang mai yuh khri hoik laux rhawm son riang rao pa krub yuh tix,
mawh kheu siyiex pa yuh nawh,
hoik mawh pa pie lhien,
ang koe ju.
15 Riang rao krub ang koe ngawh jum pie ceeix kawx,
mawh pa pie lhien pa nyiah pui kah.
Simiang Siyiex puing ka kix,
kix tag sang laux leeing.
16 Siyiex Ya-keux ang awm riang rao krub in kix;
nawh mawh cao pa phan pa yuh ku ceu khawm uik,
nawh kok pui Yi-seu-liex mawh pui cie tix.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh simiang kru naing heue.
Grox krai ka pui pa vuk vied vuk viang
17 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa hoik lai lawm pui eue,
pex tag caw tix khrawm ka kraung khrai tix.
18 Simiang Siyiex tag sang si vun tix tik pex lih khaing haktiex in;
nawh tag sang gut pex laux leeing,
pie tix kaux kawx ang lai deuk;
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
19 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
sau riang tix yiiem tom ting ah nin:
eue,
krax pa mat ex hoik kied sidaing kin tete,
kheu nan in hoik mawh krax kha rhawm pa khaw ex tix kham.
20 Nyiex gox ot ex hoik laux,
maox nyiex gox kawx hoik dut;
kawn simex kawn bun ex hoik lih hu khaing ex heue;
ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa hoek tix sum ka nyiex gox,
ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa baox vhak ka man heue.
 
21 Aux pawk lox ah nin:
simiang ex hoik kied daox aw;
kix ang sawg ka Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix som nai,
pui hun khankix vuk vied vuk viang tix.
22 Nghiet heue,
lox pie lox lhien hoik naog ka rao ma heue!
Meung ka caong ma laong hoik chuh,
pui naing cie nawh kieh veng Yo-tax ku dauh lih tix riem,
lih mawh mheem dee ot sox tiag heue.
 
23 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux hoik tawng ang koe pui pon tawng ka krax pa im ju tix;
ang koe pui pa pon tawng ka grawng pa si pup tix singaix pa sang hoek.
24 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix lang rhawm tix sidiex gau ka yix liak;
khiox khaw maix paw vi yuh yix laux leeing ka daux krax pa song rhawm maix.
25 Khiox khaw maix hieh krax pa song rhawm tix ka meung ka piang haktiex pa ang lhat graung yung yi ka maix,
ka piang to kaux pui pa ang doh keud ka maix khankix.
Mawh kheu hoik pauh kix ka pui cie maix;
kix hoik yuh yix laux leeing,
kieh nyiex ma yix riem heue.
