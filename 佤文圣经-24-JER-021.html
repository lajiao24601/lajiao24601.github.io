Grox krai grawng sang laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung
21
Si-ti-cia simiang Yo-tax ploi Pa-see-hu-eux kawn simex Ma-ci-ya,
mai Si-fang-ya cao giex kaing li kawn simex Ma-si-ya hoek ka aux.
Kix mhaing lox ah nin:
2 a“Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng ploi pui naing lai lawm ka veng in,
mhaing ex khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh tee rien tix chix yuh mhai pa daox pa am son ex,
son sang kieh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha dak tix deeh heue.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux ka kix,
mai nhie kix
4 krai ka Si-ti-cia,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex krai ah nin:
“Si-ti-cia eue,
pui naing maix mai Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
mai pui naing pa ploi nawh puing ka paox tix.
Aux tag sang pon pex ka pui naing maix,
mai pum tix deuh kraung naing ot ka daux sigreung veng.
5 Aux tag sang teei riang rao tix khawm uik,
mai puing pex ka daux krax pa song rhawm,
meet rhawm mai ka daux krax pa hax rhawm tix.
6 Aux tag sang pauh ka can pui pa ot daux veng yum khawm uik;
kawn pui mai to sat tag sang yum kheu jhawm saux.
7 Phan mawh maix,
simiang pa ot greem maix khawm pui pa deuk khaing puing naing,
khaing sibhawm praix mai khaing saux preng khankix,
aux tag sang ap kix ka daux tawm taix simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
mai ka daux tawm taix pui koeng pa keud tix sang pauh ka maix.
Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ang lai caox rhawm ka pex,
yuh krax pa khaw rhawm ka pex;
nawh tag sang yuh pex yum.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
8 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux krai ah nin ka kawn pui:
“Jhak heue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik teei tix deuh krax im mai krax yum ka plak sivoe ngai pex,
mai kieh to kaux pex tang laug.
9 Pui pa kawn deuh ot ka daux veng khankix,
phan ang yum ka naing,
tag yum ka cai rhawm tix,
phan ang mawh nan yum ka jhawm saux.
Viang mawh nan,
pui pa lih hoek plak praix veng,
mai kreet tix ka greem pui Pa-pi-loeng pa hoik lai lawm ka pex khankix,
tag ang yaox tix yum,
mai koe bawg yam to tix klawd ju.
10 Aux hoik but rhawm tix ciyang ka veng in,
mai yuh ka veng in laux leeing.
Aux tag sang ap tix tawx nawh ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng;
nawh tag sang gleeing ka veng in.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Prawng ca ka nyiex simiang Yo-tax
11-12 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux krai ka doeh ceu cad Ta-wue pa mawh nyiex ma simiang Yo-tax ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet ka aux pa mawh gum lox Simiang Siyiex.
Pex tag caw tix prawng ca ku ngaix ka daux krax pa khaw caw,
mai taoh ka pui pa gut kheem pui khaing daux tawm taix pui cao gut kheem.
Phan ang mawh nan,
krax pa yuh pex pa caox pa caig tag chix cheeik tix kieh aux chu rhawm;
an ang koe pui pa pon preet yeet.
13 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
aux tag sang yuh ka maix heue!
Maix ngawm ka piang gawng dee lhaong,
awm simaox pa gawp paox tix ka daux dee lung yuh nan.
Maix hoik krai lox vao ang koe pui pa pon pex ka maix,
pon yieh tix laig ka mhai dee kuan maix;
14 viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang ciyang maix kheu kaing pa yuh maix.
Aux tag sang gleeing ka nyiex haw maix;
cap ngu tag sang bleet ka sivie siveng maix khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
