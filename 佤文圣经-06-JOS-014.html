Guah ka haktiex plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang
14
Plak greem in mawh grawng haktiex pa guah pui Yi-seu-liex ka haktiex Cia-nang plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng,
khawm simiang ku nyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
guah haktiex ka kawn pui khankix.
2 bKix hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
cawk lai a tat ka haktiex pa guah haktiex plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang ka si dim tix plak ceu pui.
3-4 cMaw-si hoik guah haktiex plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang ka ra ceu plak ceu pui.
(Doeh ceu pui Yaw-seux guah tix mawh ceu pui Ma-na-si mai Yi-fa-liien.)
Pui Li-we ang pon ka haktiex,
viang mawh nan,
kix koe veng dee ot,
koe daux tiag liang moi liang pex.
5 Pui Yi-seu-liex hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si guah ka haktiex heue.
Cia-leux pon ka Si-paw-loeng
6 dKoe tix ngaix,
koe ceu pui Yo-tax tix blah hoek ka Yaw-su-yax ka Cie-cia.
Ka daux kix koe pui tix kaux kaux Cia-leux,
mawh kawn simex Yie-fu-ni pui Ci-ni-si.
Nawh ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Yam ot ka haktiex Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex pa mawh Maw-si,
grawng maix mai aux,
maix hoik tawng ka heue.
7 eYam hoek aux tipon num,
siliex Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Maw-si kieh aux hu jauh ka Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax hu jiang ka haktiex in;
aux hoik krai sidawng ka nawh can pa jiang tix;
8 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa hu jiang tau mai aux kieh pui lhat ka lox pa krai kix,
aux hoik hawt ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux.
9 fNgaix an,
Maw-si hoik cu ka lox aux,
tix ngaix sax aux mai kawn saux aux sang pon ka haktiex in,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik dig aux ka caong tix,
kieh mawh sin ngao aux,
mawh kheu aux priex tix hawt ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux heue.
10 “Yam in,
jhak liak,
jauh yam krai Simiang Siyiex lox an kix ka Maw-si hoek yam in,
hoik luan ka tipon phuan num heue.
Yam an pui Yi-seu-liex vung viang ka daux tiag.
Simiang Siyiex hoik hawt ka lox pa ap tix teem coi aux tom hoek in singaix.
Jhak!
Aux hoik koe tix daix phuan num heue.
11 In singaix aux kawn brax kua,
awm sivoe yam ploi Maw-si aux lih yuh nan.
Aux kawn koe riang rao,
chix tix hu puing naing mai yuh kaing tang ceu.
12 Yam in,
khiox khaw maix tawx gawng pa ap Simiang Siyiex tix tawx ka aux liak,
pa mawh yam hoek aux mai paox aux tix krai singaix an pa ap Simiang Siyiex tix tawx ka aux.
Yam an yix hoik krai ka maix,
dee an koe pui ting lhaong Ya-na kah,
ot ka daux veng ting pa lhaong.
Aux keud Simiang Siyiex chix ot tau mai aux,
aux pon grheu kix lih,
awm lox pa ap Simiang Siyiex heue.”
13 Yaw-su-yax nawb buan ka piang kawn simex Yie-fu-ni pa mawh Cia-leux,
mai tawx veng Si-paw-loeng ka nawh,
kieh mawh sin ngao nawh heue.
14 Si-paw-loeng tom yam in kawn mawh cie pui Ci-ni-si,
kawn simex Yie-fu-ni doeh ceu cad Cia-leux,
mawh kheu nawh hawt Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex khawm rhawm rhi tix heue.
15 Sivoe in,
Si-paw-loeng kok mawh veng Ya-pa.
(Pui Ya-pa mawh pui pa kied ting lhaong ka daux si nax ceu pui Ya-na.)
Khaix an,
haktiex in hoik kied mhawm,
ang koe naing heue.
