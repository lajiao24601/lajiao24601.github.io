22
Min plak ngai kawn lheu ting ngawh khaing sin ngao;
min ceu kaux kawn lheu mhawm khaing mau khri.
2 Pui hot pui sibhawm mai pui pa koe mau hoik khaw paox tix ka daux tix ceu,
mai kiex khawm ra kaux hoik mawh pa phan pa yuh Simiang Siyiex heue.
3 Pui pa koe krax doh rhawm doh rhi,
yam yaox nawh krax laux krax leeing nawh nied tix to hu mox tix khaing;
pa daox aw hoek tix dex,
mai pon krax laux leeing,
khaix an nawh klih rhawm rhi tix.
4 Krax pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai pa koe rhawm awn rhawm tiiem,
hoik mawh krax mag mi,
min plak ngai,
krax lang ju lang phan.
5 Pui pa muh ka ju tix,
mai tawng tix to hu mox tix khaing rub pa giex ka pui pa caox pa caig.
6 Pa sidiex kawn nyawm lawng ka daux krax pa rauh sa see,
jauh tom yam iak nawh hoek tom yam kuad nawh mai ang chix piawm ka heue.
7 Pui hot pui sibhawm mawh siliex cawng pui pa koe mau;
pui pa koe ceuk mawh siliex cawng pui cao pa ceuk nawh mau kah.
8 Pa rhuat ka simie pa caox pa caig,
dee si dauh mawh krax laux leeing pa ting;
krax ciyang pa kin tag sang yuh nawh laux leeing.
9 Pa si pieh rhawm tix tawx ka pui hot pui sibhawm ih priex preem,
mai tag pon buan son.
10 Grheu hu ka pui pa cau nyhau,
krax pa simah simaing,
pa lawh pa maw,
pa kaig pa tua tag yaox tix yien khawm uik heue.
11 Pui pa muh ka rhawm pa singax singeh mai pui pa chix krai ka lox mhawm khankix,
tag pon krax pa khaw rhawm simiang heue.
12 Ngai Simiang Siyiex chix kia ngien ka pa mawh pa meh;
nawh yuh laux ka gum lox krai pui pie pui lhien.
13 Pui pa nieh caox ot ka daux nyiex;
Nawh krai koe sivai sang si ka plak praix,
mai krox jun tix sang bleet ka nawh.
14 Jheeh bun pa phit vao mawh daux o;
can pui pa koeng Simiang Siyiex ka khawm uik,
tag sang cawd ka daux.
15 Rhawm keud kawn nyawm iak hoik dex sang awm pui daox aw yuh nan,
krax pa si vied ka kix chix kieh rhawm rhi kix tieh loh tix.
16 Pui pa tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka pui pa koe mau,
phan ang mawh nan,
pa gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
tag sang hoek krax cud krax cha heue.
Gum lox ping nyia tix ngoe grawng
17 Nghiet heue,
aux sang sidiex gau lox pa krai pui pa lhak lhiao ka maix,
maix caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix gau.
18 Phan maix pon priex kaux ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
teei tix siao ku bawg ku yam,
mai an hoik mawh kaing pa mhawm tete te lai.
19 Aux sang kieh maix een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
kheu an,
mai teei gum lox ping nyia in kix krai ka maix.
20 Aux hoik tiiem gum lox in son maix tix ngoe grawng,
pa ot ka daux mawh ngai lig ngai lai mai gum lox pa sidiex,
21 mai sang sidiex ka maix ceu pa tix mawh krax pa mawh pa meh,
son sang kieh maix pon tix tawng sidawng yam sawg kah,
mai deeh pawk ka pui pa ploi maix hoek an heue.
(Tix)
22 Ang pon tix teei ka cie pui hot pui sibhawm ka daux siawp sibrawm tix;
ang pon tix gut kheem ka pui pa ang koe cao teem cao coi ka daux nyiex prawng see.
23 Simiang Siyiex sang krai lox son kix;
pui mawx yuh ju kix laux leeing,
mai Simiang Siyiex tag sang hawt tix teei ka ju pui an nang heue.
(Ra)
24 Paw vi yuh paox grawm tau mai pui pa hax rhawm;
25 maix tee rien tix gau tix hax rhawm awm nawh,
mai ang lai pon loh heue.
(Loe)
26 Paw vi tang son ceuk pui tang kaux;
27 phan maix ang pon plud,
pie ku it maix kawx pui tag sang teei hu heue.
(Pon)
28 Mee haktiex prim pa hoik mhai tax du maix,
maix ang pon tix chuh heue.
(Phuan)
29 Maix hoik sax yaox pui pa chix cu ca tix yuh ka kaing lie?
Nawh ang awm cub pui tang kaux;
nawh tag sang cung ka plak sivoe ngai simiang heue.
