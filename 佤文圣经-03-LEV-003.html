Been Krax-tan Pa-nhim
3
Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa tan moi ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
nawh tag caw tix tan moi mhaing,
phan ang mawh nan,
moi siang pa mhawm loig.
2 Nawh tag deuh taix tix ka piang kaing Krax-tan Khawng-brie an,
mai pauh ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man.
Doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng khankix pa mawh cao giex kaing li tag phruah nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie an hawt vung viang piang khu dee tan,
3-4 mai teei pa ot ka daux tix blah,
pa mawh bee pa kawb kraung vaig mai sok lax ra mu khawm bee ot ka piang sok lax,
mai tawm nhao dee blawn mhawm pat lih,
gleeing ka ngu,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
5 Cao giex kaing li tag teei ceu in khankix deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan,
mai gleeing tix tan tau mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
Taox bhawng Krax-tan Ngu in hoik mawh pa muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb heue.
6 Phan nawh sang tan yung,
phan ang mawh nan,
pex,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag caw tix laug ka pex mhaing pa mhawm loig,
phan ang mawh nan,
pex siang pa mhawm loig khankix.
7 Phan nawh tan ka kawn yung tix mu,
8 caw tix deuh taix tix ka piang kaing Krax-tan Khawng-brie an,
pauh ka sivoe nyiex gox buan man.
Cao giex kaing li khankix tag phruah nham khawng brie an hawt vung viang khu dee tan,
9-10 mai teei pa ot ka daux tix blah,
pa mawh bee,
jauh ka dee dex ot si-ang krawng,
pat tix lih tom khawm num si dax,
bee pa kawb ka kraung vaig,
sok lax ra mu mai bee pa ot ka piang sok lax,
tawm dee blawn mhawm khankix teei lih,
gleeing ka ngu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim mai tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
11 Cao giex kaing li tag teei ceu in kix,
deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan mai gleeing kah,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Ngu tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
12 Phan nawh tan ka pex,
tag caw tix tuk hoek ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
13 Nawh tag deuh taix tix ka piang kaing khawng brie an,
mai pauh ka sivoe nyiex gox buan man.
Cao giex kaing li khankix tag phruah nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie an hawt vung viang piang khu dee tan,
14-15 Mai teei pa ot ka daux tix blah,
pa mawh bee pa kawb ka piang kraung vaig,
sok lax ra mu mai bee pa ot ka piang sok lax,
mai tawm dee blawn mhawm khankix tix blah lih,
teei ngu gleeing kah,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Ngu tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
16 Cao giex kaing li khankix tag gleeing an kix ka piang khu dee tan,
kieh mawh taox bhawng Krax-tan Ngu.
Can bee khawm uik tag mawh cie Simiang Siyiex.
17 Pex pui Yi-seu-liex,
viang ot dee mawx ang ah,
ang doh pon tix ih bee mai nham.
In hoik mawh been gum lox sijax mukheu pa sang priex pex tix hawt ku ju leeing ju leeing phan heue.
