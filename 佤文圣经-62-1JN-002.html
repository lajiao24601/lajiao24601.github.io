Khrit krai sidawng ka ex
2
Kawn aux khankix eue,
aux tiiem in kix mawh son ang sang kieh pex yuh phi map;
phan koe pui pa yuh phi map,
ex hoik koe Ye-sux Khrit kaw pa rauh pa see tix kaux,
nawh hoik krai son ex sidawng ka keeing ex Siyiex.
2 Khrit tawx to kaux tix yum,
mai hoik taoh ka phi map ex;
nawh ang joeng yuh son phi map ex,
mai hoik yuh son phi map kawn pui haktiex khawm uik,
mai hoik tawx to kaux tix yum heue.
3 Phan ex priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawm Siyiex,
ex tag sang tawng grawng pa hoik tawng yung ka nawh.
4 Phan koe pui pa ah nin:
“Aux tawng yung ka nawh”,
viang mawh nan ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh,
pui pa yuh nin mawh pui pa pie lhien,
mai krax pa mawh pa meh ang dex dawt tau mai nawh.
5-6 Viang mawh nan pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox Siyiex,
krax muh nawh ka Siyiex tag hoek diag pa grum naog.
Pui pa krai koe tix ka ju Siyiex,
tag caw hawt tix yuh ka gum lox ah si kah krai pa yuh Ye-sux Khrit ka daux krax ot krax koe tix;
yuh nin,
ex tom tag pon krai kaux cong ka grawng pa hoik koe tix ka ju nawh heue.
Gum lox tawx pa khraox
7 aPaox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
pa tiiem aux tix tawx ka pex,
ang mawh gum lox tawx pa khraox,
hoik mawh gum lox tawx pa prim,
mawh gum lox pa hoik rieb pex yam jauh.
Gum lox tawx pa prim in mawh gum lox pa hoik mhong pex.
8 Khaix an pa tiiem aux tix tawx ka pex,
mawh gum lox tawx pa khraox;
gum lox tawx pa mawh pa meh in ot ka to kaux Khrit,
mai jhi tix lih ka to kaux pex nang.
Mawh kheu pa faik tag sang grai hu,
pa riang pa mawh pa meh hoik jauh tix jhan riang heue.
9 Phan koe pui pa krai ot tix ka daux pa riang,
viang mawh nan,
koeng ka pux aik pux o tix,
nawh ca kawn ot ka daux pa faik.
10 Pui pa muh pux aik pux o tix,
tag ot ka daux pa riang,
nawh ang chix kieh pui tang kaux goh tix yuh phi map.
11 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa koeng ka pux aik pux o tix,
tag mawh pa ot ka daux pa faik;
nawh hu ka daux pa faik,
mai ang tawng ka dee sang hu tix,
mawh kheu hoik kieh pa faik nawh dug ngai heue.
12 Kawn nyawm khankix eue,
aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik pon phi map pex krax pa pluat ploi ka daux Khrit.
13 Tax yiex miex keeing khankix eue,
aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik tawng pex ka kaw pa dawg koe tom yam jauh.
Pui num ju eue,
aux tiiem tix tawx lai ka pex,
mawh kheu pex hoik pon pex kaw pa caox caix an heue.
14 Kawn nyawm khankix eue,
aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik tawng yung pex ka keeing Siyiex.
Tax yiex miex keeing khankix eue,
aux tiiem tix tawx lai in ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik tawng pex ka kaw pa dawg koe tom yam jauh.
Pui nhum ju eue,
aux tiiem tix tawx lai ka pex,
mawh kheu kied koe riang pex;
gum lox Siyiex ot ka daux ju pex,
mai pex hoik pon pex ka kaw pa caox caix an heue.
15 Paw vi muh ka haktiex,
mai ka ceu pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex ku ceu.
Phan pex muh ka haktiex,
pex tag ang koe ka krax muh keeing Siyiex.
16 Ceu pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex khawm uik,
awm pa mawh alo nex nham,
alo ngai jhak,
mai min plak ngai pa giex going khawm uik,
ang mawh pa kaoh khaing keeing Siyiex,
mai hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing haktiex.
17 Haktiex in mai alo ceu pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex khawm uik tag sang grai hu;
viang mawh nan,
pui pa hawt tix yuh ka alo Siyiex tag sang koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Pa yuh krax pa tieb tie ka Khrit
18 Kawn nyawm khankix eue,
bawg yam si dauh haktiex tag sang hoek heue!
Pex hoik sax mhong krai pui kaw pa tieb tie ka Khrit tag sang hoek;
yam in pui pa koeng ka Khrit tom nie hoik lih hoek,
mawh kheu nan ex hoik tawng bawg yam si dauh tag sang hoek.
19 Cub pui in kix ang mawh pa dex dawt tau mai ex,
mawh kheu nan,
lih hu singai khaing ex;
phan kix mawh pa dex dawt tau mai ex,
kix tag sang ot tau mai ex.
Viang mawh nan,
kix yoi tix singaix khaing ex,
chix tix yaox grawng ang kix mawh pa dex dawt tau mai ex heue.
20 Pex hoik pon pa groi rawm bee khaing kaw pa singax singeh anb,
mawh kheu nan,
pex hoik tawng pa mawh pa meh khawm uik.
21 Aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka pex,
ang mawh kheu ang pex tawng yung ka pa mawh pa meh,
mai hoik mawh kheu tawng pex pa mawh pa meh khawm uik,
khaix an mai hoik tawng ceu pa pie lhien ang mawh pa kaoh khaing pa mawh pa meh.
22 Kheu nan,
pui mawx mawh kaw pa pie lhien an lie?
Ang hoik mawh kaw pa thiang ka Ye-sux mawh Khrit lie?
Pui pa yuh nin tag mawh kaw pa tieb tie ka Khrit;
nawh hoik tieb tie keeing cao,
mai baox tieb tie ka kawn cao.
23 Mawh kheu can pui pa tieb tie ka kawn cao,
mai hoik mawh pa tieb tie ka keeing cao;
can pui pa doh keud ka kawn cao ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
mai hoik pon nang ka keeing cao heue.
24 Kheu nan,
pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa hoik mhong tix yam jauh.
Phan pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa hoik mhong pex yam jauh,
pex tag im ka daux ju kawn cao mai ka daux ju keeing cao.
25 In hoik mawh gum lox pa hoek Khrit tix tawx ju ka ex leeing ju heue.
26 Aux tiiem kaing in kix mawh pa krai son pui pa keud tix sang pie lhien ka pex.
27 Phan mawh ka son pex,
Khrit hoik teei tix tawx khuan nawh ka pex.
Ca mawh ot khuan nawh tau mai pex,
pex tag ang siao krax pa sidiex gau pui tang kaux;
mawh kheu khuan nawh tag sang sidiex gau pex ka kaing ceu pa koe khawm uik,
mai krax pa sidiex gau nawh hoik mawh pa mawh pa meh khawm uik,
ang mawh pa pie lhien.
Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka pa sidiex gau Khuan Singax Singeh,
mai im ka daux ju Khrit tom si dauh heue.
28 Mawh tete,
kawn nyawm khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix im ka daux ju nawh;
yuh nin,
yam lih hoek nawh,
ka daux singaix pa sang deeh hoek,
ex tag chix tix ang koe rhawm ring,
ang siao mox tix.
29 Pex hoik tawng Khrit mawh pa rauh pa see;
mawh kheu nan pex tag caw tix tawng,
pui pa priex tix hawt ka pa rauh pa see tag mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex heue.
