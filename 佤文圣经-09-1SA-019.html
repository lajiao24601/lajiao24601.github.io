Sao-law gut kheem ka Ta-wue
19
Sao-law krai kawn simex tix Yaw-na-tang mai siliex nawh khawm uik mhong,
nawh sang pauh ka Ta-wue.
Yaw-na-tang kied muh ka Ta-wue,
2 tom ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Keeing aux sang pauh ka maix.
Pa sax pon ngawb cao,
maix mox tix ka dee glip tix dauh dauh,
mai ot ka dee an.
3 Aux tag sang hoek ka dee mox maix tix,
mai ot ka praok keeing aux kum kawng grawng maix.
Phan aux yaox grawng ceu pa tix ang ah,
aux tag sang krai ka maix heue.”
4 Yaw-na-tang ot ka sivoe ngai Sao-law mai yaog yaw ka Ta-wue,
mai baox ah nin:
“Keeing,
maix paw vi yuh ka siliex maix Ta-wue laux;
mai nawh ang sax yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka maix tix bawg kawx.
Deeh tieh tix,
can pa yuh nawh hoik mawh pa mhawm son maix.
5 Nawh viex ju tix hu pauh ka Keu-li-yax;
Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex puing pex.
Yam an maix hoik gaux rhawm kah,
yam in maix lai sang pauh pui ang koe dee lud tix kaux yum,
mai ang koe grawng sang pauh Ta-wue yum lie?”
6 Sao-law cu ka lox pa krai kawn tix Yaw-na-tang,
tom tiex tu ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
ang lai sang pauh ka Ta-wue yum.
7 Yaw-na-tang kok Ta-wue hoek,
mai krai nawh mhong grawh grawng khawm uik,
khaix an viex nawh hu ka Sao-law.
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue yuh kaing jhaw pa pau ka simiang awm singaix pa hoek luan yuh nan heue.
8 Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Fe-li-see baox puing ka paox tix.
Ta-wue puing ka pui Fe-li-see,
mai pex ka kix,
kix tom yaog tix to heue.
9 Koe tix singaix,
khuan phi caox lih hoek khaing Simiang Siyiex dee an,
mai laig daux Sao-law.
Sao-law giex pliah ka daux tawm taix tix,
mai ngawm ka daux nyiex;
Ta-wue kawx ot tan nang,
mai tauh ka ting.
10 Sao-law keud tix sang klauh Ta-wue ka pliah mai ding,
viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue yoi tix hu,
pliah klauh ka ding.
Ta-wue to mox tix,
mai hu singai khaing tan heue.
11 Ka daux bo singaix an,
Sao-law ploi pui hu jiang ka nyiex Ta-wue,
mai rien tix sang pauh nawh yum ka daux ngawb ngaix khaix.
Moeng Ta-wue pa kaux Mi-cia ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Daux bo in phan maix ang to mox tix,
pa sax maix tag sang yum.”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Mi-cia ploi Ta-wue lih daux doh nyiex,
nhie nawh to.
13 Mi-cia deuh riang krub nyiex kix ot ka piang ku,
mai teei mhawn gaong hauk pex,
mhawn ot greem kaing riang krub an,
mai lhop prux ka piang.
14 Yam hoek pui cie Sao-law tix sang giex ka Ta-wue,
Mi-cia krai kix mhong Ta-wue saux heue.
15 Viang mawh nan,
Sao-law baox nhie kix hu,
ang yaox ka Ta-wue ang chix.
Sao-law baox tawx lox ah nin:
“Grawng nawh hoek khawm pui khawm ku,
aux sang pauh ka nawh.”
16 Kix laig ka daux nyiex,
yaox pa it piang ku mawh riang krub nyiex kix,
mai yaox mhawn gaong hauk pex mhawn tix ot greem kaing riang an.
17 Sao-law chawk ka Mi-cia ah nin:
“Maix yuh ka mawx lai pie lhien ka aux,
mai kieh pui pa koeng aux to lie?”
Mi-cia ah nin:
“Nawh ah,
phan aux ang teem nawh mai kieh nawh to,
nawh tag sang pauh ka aux heue.”
18 Ta-wue to hoek ka La-max dee ot Sa-mu-eu,
mai krai Sa-mu-eu mhong sidawng grawng kaing pa hoik yuh Sao-law ka tix khankix.
Khaix an,
nawh hu ka Na-yaw tau mai Sa-mu-eu,
mai paung ot tan heue.
19 Koe pui krai Sao-law mhong,
Ta-wue ot ka La-max nyiex Na-yaw,
20 Sao-law tom ploi pui hu giex ka Ta-wue.
Kixa yaox ka cao jhai tix cub,
Sa-mu-eu mawh cao kaing kix,
mai ah lox pa lih khaing khuan.
Khuan Siyiex lih laig daux pui pa ploi Sao-law hu khankix dum mawx dum an,
kix kawx jauh tix ah lox pa lih khaing khuan.
21 Sao-law mhong ka grawng in,
nawh baox ploi pui hu tom nie;
pui in kix kawx hoik awm pui pa hu bawg sivoe yuh nan,
baox ah lox pa lih khaing khuan.
Sao-law baox ploi pui hu daux loe bawg,
mai baox yuh nan nang heue.
22 Dee si dauh,
to kaux cao Sao-law hu ka La-max.
Yam hoik hoek nawh ka praok rawm dung ting Si-kux an,
nawh chawk tix mhong,
Sa-mu-eu mai Ta-wue ot dee mawx.
Koe pui krai nawh mhong,
kix ot ka nyiex Na-yaw.
23 Yam yaog Sao-law tix hu ka dee an,
khuan Siyiex dum mawx dum an laig ka daux nawh,
mai ah lox pa lih khaing khuan leeing gawng krax,
tom hoek ka Na-yaw.
24 bNawh peeik kraung khrai tix ka plak sivoe ngai Sa-mu-eu,
mai ah lox pa lih khaing khuan,
leeing ngaix leeing sawm bhla ka to kaux tix it piang tiex.
Tom koe lox been prim pa ah nin:
“Sao-law khex sang lih tix mawh cao jhai nang lie?”
