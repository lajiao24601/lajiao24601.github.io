Pui Fe-li-see ang yum rhawm ka Ta-wue
29
Pui Fe-li-see khrawm pui naing kix khawm uik ot ka haktiex Ya-fu.
Pui Yi-seu-liex sum bang ot ka praok ngawd Yie-see-liex.
2 Simiang pui Fe-li-see phuan kaux viex pui naing kix tix cub tix yieh kaux mai tix cub tix rheng kaux preeng tix hu sivoe.
Ta-wue mai pui naing cie nawh hawt plak khaix Ya-cie.
3 Simiang pui naing Fe-li-see yaox kix,
chawk an nin:
“Pui Si-paw-lai in kix yuh ceu pa tix dee in lie?”
Ya-cie ah nin:
“In mawh Ta-wue,
simiang naing pa ot greem Sao-law simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux.
Nawh hawt grawng aux hoik koe been tix baux bawg yam.
Jauh yam hoek nawh tix peung rhawm aux tom hoek yam in,
aux ang nyang sax yaox grawng pa phit nawh tix ceu kawx heue.”
4 Viang mawh nan,
simiang naing pui Fe-li-see ang naog rhawm kah,
mai ah nin ka Ya-cie:
“Deeh ing tawx pui in ka veng pa hoik tawx maix nawh cie an!
Paw kieh nawh hu puing naing tau mai ex.
Yam puing ex ka naing nawh rien tix chix deeh tieh tix puing ka ex.
Yuh ka mawx ex lai sang tawx ju tix laux,
mai kieh nawh pon krax pa yum rhawm cao nawh ka nawh lie?
5 aNawh ang mawh Ta-wue lie?
Khex ang nawh hoik mawh Ta-wue pa hoik rhax bun miex khankix lox rhax ah nin:
‘Sao-law pon pauh pui kaing rheng;
Ta-wue pon pauh pui kaing mheen’ lie?”
6 Ya-cie kok Ta-wue hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux tiex tu ka Siyiex pa im ju pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin,
maix hoik koe krax cu krax nghiet ka aux.
Aux muh maix hu puing naing tau mai tix.
Jauh yam peung maix rhawm tix ka aux tom hoek yam in,
aux ang sax yaox grawng krax phit lud maix,
viang mawh nan,
simiang pui tang kaux ang khaw rhawm ka maix.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
maix deeh ing mhawm mhawm ka nyiex,
paw cheeik tix kieh kix song rhawm heue.”
8 Ta-wue pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux lud yuh kaing ceu pa tix lie?
Phan hawt awm lox pa hoik krai maix,
jauh yam hoek aux peung rhawm tix ka maix ka daux singaix an,
maix ang sax yaox dee phit dee lud aux.
Yuh ka mawx lai ang kieh aux hu puing pui koeng tau mai maix pa mawh cao aux simiang aux lie?”
9 Ya-cie ah nin:
“Aux cu lox maix.
Aux hoik tawng koe maix krax cu krax nghiet awm kawn cau Siyiex yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
simiang pui tang kaux ang rhawm kieh maix hu puing naing tau mai yix.
10 Kheu nan,
Ta-wue eue,
yam hoik riang praix pa sax,
maix viex can pui pa hoik lih khaing Sao-law hoek peung rhawm tix ka aux dee in deeh ing heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom hawt tix ing tau mai pui cie nawh ka daux ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao ka plak haktiex Fe-li-see.
Pui Fe-li-see nied tix hu puing ka Yie-see-liex heue.
