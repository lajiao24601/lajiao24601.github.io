Ta-wue krai grawng sang sum ka nyiex buan
28
Simiang Ta-wue tawx lox ka can cao kaing pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
nhie kix khrawm tix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang ku ceu pui,
simiang ting ka piang meung haktiex,
simiang naing kaing rheng mai simiang naing kaing yieh kaux,
cao kuang sin ngao mai khawng brie ka daux nyiex simiang,
simiang pa ot ka daux nyiex haw,
simiang naing,
mai can pui pa ting ceu khawm uik,
hoik khrawm paox tix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
2 aTa-wue kaoh cung,
mai krai ka kix ah nin:
“Paox ceu paox cad,
pex nghiet!
Aux keud tix sang sum ka nyiex pa kaux cong leeing ju tix lhang,
son sang deuh teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex ot daux;
teug sijax mukheu in hoik mawh ngot dee deuh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex caong tix ot piang.
Aux hoik rien khre ka mhawm,
sang sum nyiex buan tix lhang kieh yaog yaw min plak ngai nawh.
3 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang cu aux sum;
mawh kheu hoik mawh aux pui naing,
mai hoik kieh nham pui jauh tom nie.
4 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik laug aux mai doeh ceu cad aux mawh cao pa kuang kia ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju leeing phan.
Nawh hoik laug ceu pui Yo-tax lih khaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
hoik laug pui nyiex aux lih khaing daux ceu pui Yo-tax,
hoik bre tix laug aux lih khaing daux nyiex ma keeing aux,
mai yaog aux mawh simiang,
kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
5 Nawh hoik tawx kawn simex ka aux tom nie,
ka daux kawn simex in kix nawh hoik laug Saw-law-meung mawh cao pa kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh meung cie Simiang Siyiex heue.
6 “Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
‘Kawn simex maix Saw-law-meung sang sum nyiex buan aux.
Aux hoik laug nawh mawh kawn simex aux,
aux sang mawh keeing nawh.
7 Phan nawh awm yam in yuh nin,
mai ca nied tix priex rhawm tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai lox pa tawx aux khawm uik,
aux tag sang kieh simiang pa hoik giex nawh kaing kaux cong leeing ju heue.’
8 “Yam in,
paox ceu paox cad,
ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex mai sivoe ngai pui haoh pui hun Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh pui cie Simiang Siyiex,
aux tawx lox nhie pex priex rhawm tix hawt ka kaing pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex ka ex ku ceu.
Yuh nin,
son sang pon yaox pex tix pon ka haktiex pa mhawm in,
khaix an mai yaox tix plud tix ju hoek tix ju heue.”
9 Ta-wue ah nin ka Saw-law-meung:
“Kawn aux,
mai caw tix tawng ka Siyiex keeing maix,
mai yuh kaing jhaw nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
Nawh hoik tawng ka rhawm keud ex mai krax pa simie ex tix yuh khawm uik.
Maix khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka nawh,
nawh tag rieb ka maix;
phan maix hu singai khaing nawh,
nawh tag tik vud ka maix leeing ju.
10 Maix hoik tawng,
Simiang Siyiex hoik laug maix sum ka nyiex buan nawh.
Yam in maix yuh heue!
Maix caw kieh tix koe riang pon yuh tom si dauh heue.”
11 Ta-wue hoik krai grawng sang sum nyiex buan yuh ka mawx ka Saw-law-meung,
awm pa mawh can kraung pa sum nyiex buan,
nyiex krao,
nyiex lhaong jan,
nyiex buan plak daux,
mai dee pa kied singax singeh,
pa mawh dee pluat ploi ka phi map.
12 Nawh kawx hoik krai lai riang nyiex buan pa mhai tix ka Saw-law-meung,
awm pa mawh biang brawk mai ka nyiex pa vung viang khawm pon daog,
khawm dee priex ka kraung khrai nyiex buan mai nyiex krao pa deuh khawng tan pa tan lu pui ka Simiang Siyiex.
13 Ta-wue baox krai grawng sang khrawm ka cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we yuh ka mawx,
son sang kieh kix yuh ka kaing nyiex buan,
kuang kia ka can kraung khrai nyiex buan khawm uik.
14 Nawh baox jhi grawng sang khaw tix siao mau khri yuh aw lih kah,
15 kawk ngu mai jawk kawk ngu,
16 yuh ka pheen mau,
khawm pheen khri pa deuh khao mun pa tan lu ka Siyiex.
17 Nawh kawx jhi grawng sang khaw tix siao khri mhawm yuh cuan,
pan,
kawk nyaux,
klong khri,
klong mau,
18 sang lo khri miex ding yuh khu gleeing nyawng bhawng mai daux jhup jaong ling pa giex Ci-lu-paw ra mu kaux;
preeik Ci-lu-paw in kiex ra mu tawb ka piang teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex.
19 Simiang Ta-wue ah nin:
“Kaing in khawm uik hoik tiiem tix hawt lih awm gum lox pa hoik jhi Simiang Siyiex ka aux heue.”
20 Simiang Ta-wue ah nin ka kawn simex tix Saw-law-meung:
“Maix caw kieh tix kaux cong kua geeik!
Yam in maix yuh kaing,
paw vi lhat,
mai paw vi khi rhawm khi rhi.
Simiang Siyiex pa krub aux ot tau mai maix;
nawh ang chix tik vud ka maix,
nawh tag sang ot tau mai maix,
tom hoik yuh maix nyiex buan.
21 Kieh cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we mawh pa koe beeh glawm tix yuh kaing ka daux nyiex buan.
Cang mai pa blawn chix khankix hoik koe krax gaux rhawm tix teem coi ka maix;
kawn pui mai simiang pui haktiex khankix tag sang nghiet ka lox tawx maix heue.”
