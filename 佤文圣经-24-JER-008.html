8
“Hoek ka daux singaix an,
pui koeng tag sang kaong si-ang simiang Yo-tax,
simiang naing,
cao giex kaing li,
mai pui pa ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung koik lih khaing daux si meeig,
2 kieh jhi tix ka greem singaix,
khix,
mai simuing khankix.
To kaux rao ma in kix hoik mawh ceu pa kied simie kix kah,
pa hawt kix kah,
pa gau kix tix sawn kah,
pa krub kix ka yam im ju tix.
Si-ang in kix pui ang lai khrawm tix si paung,
tag sang tieh tix lih mawh phon ka piang haktiex.
3 Pui meung haktiex pa caox pa caig in pa kawn deuk khankix,
tag sang ot ka daux haktiex pa ploi aux kix ot,
mai khiox khaw tix yum ang khiox khaw tix im.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Phi map mai krax ciyang
4 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka pui cie tix ah nin:
“Kawn pui hoik sideeing tix goh,
ang lai keud tix kaoh cung lie?
Kawn pui hoik lud krax,
mai ang lai keud veeh tix deeh lie?
5 Pui cie aux eue,
yuh ka mawx pex lai tieh vheen ka aux,
ang lai tawng tix deeh lie?
Pex sau riang jhawp ka riang rao pa krub pui,
ang lai cu tix deeh hoek ka aux dee in.
6 Aux bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang krai lox tete te lai pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm tix.
Ang koe pui pa klih rhawm tix son krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix;
ang koe pui pa tang deeh jhak simi ka to kaux tix ‘aux hoik lud loe yuh ka mawx lie?’
Kix ku kaux daux pui hoik nieb pon hawt ka alo to kaux tix,
awm brung naing pa brug pui,
pa to hu ka daux bang naing yuh nan.
7 “Hai!
Pie klang kax hoik tawng ka bawg yam,
hoik tat ka bawg yam sang pu tix hoek;
si kaux krie,
si voeg,
sim paing lang caong kawx hoik hawt tix gah dee ot tix awm bawg yam.
Viang mawh nan,
pui cie aux eue,
pex lai ang tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux yuh nan heue!
8 Pex lai een to kaux tix lhak lhiao,
een tix tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux yuh nin lie?
Tete te lai,
lox tat jhawm prawng aux sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri pa pie lhien khankix hoik tieh loh ka heue.
9 Pex pui pa een tix lhak lhiao khankix hoik hoek krax kaig krax tua,
mai krax pru praoh yaoh yee.
Kix hoik tieb tie ka gum lox aux,
kawn sang chix krai ka ping nyia cuyi yuh ka mawx lie?
10 a“Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang teei tix tawx moeng kix ka pui tang kaux,
mai teei tix tawx kaung ma kix ka cao nyiex pa khraox khankix.
Kix mawh pui ting ceu ang ah mawh pui iak ceu ang ah,
hoik koe rhawm ghoik pa simie ka sin ngao pa ang rauh ang see,
pie cao jhai mai cao giex kaing li kawx hoik pie lhien ka kawn pui.
11 bKix jhak khaix mat pui cie aux mai een nawh ang mawh tid tix.
Kix ah nin:
‘Ang ot caox,
ang ot caox’,
tete te lai,
hoik mawh kaing pa kied kin.
12 Kix yuh ka kaing ceu pa caox pa caig yuh nin,
khaw tix koe krax kaig krax tua,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng tix kaig tua,
iak pak ngai.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tag sang sideeing tix goh awm pui tang kaux yuh nan;
yam ciyang aux ka kix,
kix tag ang pon chuh.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
13 “Aux tag sang khrawm ka pui cie tix,
awm khrawm pui ka to ma to koi yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
kix awm khaox plix siiet pa ang plix yuh nan,
awm khaox plix ruh pa ang plix yuh nan,
pie lhax kawx hoik kroh khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom kieh pui tang meung khankix hoek tix cax ka haktiex kix heue.”
14 Pui cie Siyiex chawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx ex lai kawn ngawm tix ang chuh ang chiah lie?
Kaoh,
to hu ka veng pa sum kaux cong,
kieh ex yum ka dee an heue!
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex hoik prawng ex yum.
Nawh tawx ex nyaux ka rawm pa awp pa koe jai,
mawh kheu hoik phit lud ex ka nawh.
15 Ex krox jun pa nhim,
simie tix pon pa pai moi,
viang mawh nan,
ang lai koe ceu,
mai tieh tix mawh krax pa pru praoh yaog yee.
16 Pui koeng hoik hoek ka haktiex Tang,
ex mhong ka lox bhawm bliah brung naing.
Brung naing riag ah lox tix,
mai haktiex yung yang.
Pui koeng tag sang hoek tix yuh haktiex ex,
mai ka ceu pa koe ka daux haktiex ex khawm uik laux leeing;
kix tag sang hoek tix yuh veng ex mai pui pa ot ka daux laux leeing heue.”
17 Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka pui cie tix ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
aux tag sang teei tix deuh si uing pa koe jai ka daux pex,
viang siao lox lai pa jhax lawg kawx,
ang pon grheu hu;
si uing tag sang tuh pex mat heue.”
Yie-li-mi laux rhawm son paox ceu paox cad tix
18 Aux kied laux rhawm laux rhi;
aux kied koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi.
19 Aux hoik mhong lox yiiem paox ceu paox cad tix ku dauh ku dee ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex ang ot ka Shi-ang lie?
Simiang Shi-ang ang ot daux veng lie?
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kix pawk lox ah nin:
Yuh ka mawx pex lai krub riang krub rao lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai krub ka siyiex tang meung pa giex pa going,
mai cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm lie?
20 Kawn pui kok yo ah nin:
Bawg yam rung praix hoik luan,
bawg yam vawg nghox kawx hoik si dauh,
ex ca kawn ang bax hoek krax taoh heue!
 
21 Krax sau krax mat paox ceu paox cad hoik kieh rhawm rhi aux max;
aux kied laux rhawm tete,
mai pru praoh yaoh yee ang jud.
22 Khex ang Ci-liexc koe sidah kraix lie?
Khex ang cao pai jhawm saux lai koe dee an lie?
Yuh ka mawx paox ceu paox cad aux ang yaox tix pon moi lie?
