Pux aik Yaw-seux viex Piien-ya-ming hu ka Ai-cix
43
Sibhawm praix daux haktiex Cia-nang nied ot nied kin.
2 Pui nyiex Ya-keux hoik uik ih nghox pa liag tix hoek khaing Ai-cix;
Ya-keux tom ah nin ka kawn simex tix khankix:
“Pex baox hu liag nghox hoek heue.”
3 Yo-tax ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Pui an hoik ah ka yix,
lheu khaing viex yix pux pa iak tix hu tau,
nawh ang cu yix hoek yaox tix.
4 Phan maix cu pux iak hu tau mai yix,
yix tom tag hu liag nghox son maix;
5 Mawh kheu pui an hoik krai,
lheu khaing viex yix pux pa iak tix hu tau,
nawh ang cu yix hu yaox tix heue.”
6 Ya-keux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawh pex lai kieh aux ang tawng sang yuh tix lie?
Mawx nhie pex krai kawn koe pux tix tix kaux lie?”
7 Kix pawk lox:
“Pui an chawk grawng ex mai grawng daux nyiex ma ex:
‘Keeing pex kawn brax lie?
Kawn koe pux aik tang kaux ang koe?’
Yix ang doh ang krai ka nawh;
yix ang tie tawng nawh sang kieh yix viex pux iak tix hoek lie?”
8 Yo-tax ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Tawx kawn nyawm ka yix,
yix sang yaog tix hu,
yuh nin ex khawm nyiex pui iak pui ting tag ang yaox tix yum ka cai rhawm tix.
9 Aux lawk mawh cao kham,
cu tix klawm khawm uik.
Phan aux ang viex nawh hoek mhawm mhawm,
mai tawx ka maix,
aux sang cu tix klawm phi map ka ju tix.
10 Phan ang gla rheung,
yix hoik hoek ra bawg heue.”
11 Keeing kix ah nin:
“Phan yuh nin,
pex viex bee plix khaox,
rawm hia,
sidah bhawng,
Muran,
plix khaox sok kram jung mai plix khaox Batan khankix,
tawx mawh khawng phak ka simiang.
12 Pex baox viex mau hu ra son,
mawh kheu caw pex tix sang deeh mau pa yaox pex daux chee tix sivoe an;
an tee mawh pa lud.
13 Pex viex pux tix hu,
phai hu yaox pui an.
14 Kieh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm tawx krax caox rhawm ka pex,
kieh Piien-ya-ming mai pux aik an tix kaux deeh hoek,
phan mawh ju aux,
sang tik kawn tix kawx tik heue!”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
kix viex khawng phak mai mau ra son,
khawm Piien-ya-ming lih hu ka Ai-cix.
Kix hoek ka Ai-cix,
hu yaox ka Yaw-seux.
16 Yaw-seux yaox ka Piien-ya-ming hoek tau mai kix,
tom ah nin ka pui cau tix daux nyiex:
“Viex pui in kix laig ka daux nyiex aux;
priex ngawb kix sang sawm tau mai aux.
Pex pauh khawng brie tix mu,
khre ka priex preem.”
17 Cao kuang nyiex an nghiet lox viex kix hoek ka nyiex Yaw-seux heue.
18 Mawh kheu viex kix hoek ka nyiex Yaw-seux,
kix kied lhat graung,
keud daux rhawm tix:
“Rien tix mawh son mau pa yaox ex daux chee sivoe an kawx,
kix tom viex ex hoek tin.
Lhat kix yuh ex laux,
mai tud ka la ex,
rhim ex mawh siliex cawng tix.”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
kix hoek ka diang siviex,
tom ah nin ka cao kuang nyiex:
20 “Rawg maix nghiet!
Son pa liag yix nghox sivoe in kawx,
yix hoik hoek tin.
21 Yix ing ka nyiex,
yam it yix daux krax,
mai paoh ka chee,
kaux mawx tang yaox mau khaw paoh ka chee cie tix.
Yam in yix deeh viex mau in kix hoek,
sang deeh tawx ka maix.
22 Yix baox viex mau tang dauh hoek liag nghox;
yix ang tawng mawh mawx pa deeh sau mau ka daux chee yix sivoe an kawx.”
23 Cao kuang ah nin:
“Paw lu lax rhawm,
paw lai lhat graung,
rien tix mawh Siyiex pex,
Siyiex keeing pex,
pa deeh sau mau ka daux chee pex;
mau pex aux hoik dawg rieb kah.”
Khaix an nawh teei Si-miien tawx ka kix.
24 Cao kuang nyiex an viex kix laig daux nyiex,
tawx rawm kieh kix khoek caong tix,
mai baox tawx la kix sawm.
25 Kix rien khawng phak tix mhawm,
krox yam hoek Yaw-seux yam pon ngaix tawx ka nawh;
mawh kheu pon kix lox pa kok,
kix sang sawm tau mai Yaw-seux in singaix.
26 Yaw-seux hoek ka nyiex,
kix tom viex khawng phak pa viex tix laig,
tawx ka nawh,
mai krub ka nawh.
27 Yaw-seux chawk kix mhawm ot,
mai baox rieb tix chawk:
“Pex hoik krai ka aux ka keeing kuad pex,
mhawm ot nawh lie?
Kawn koe nawh lie?”
28 Kix pawk lox:
“Siliex maix pa mawh keeing yix kawn koe.”
Khaix an baox krub ka nawh.
29 Yaw-seux yaox ka pux tix Piien-ya-ming,
tom ah nin:
“In mawh pa krai pex aux mhong pux iak tix lie?
Kawn nyawm eue,
kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka maix!”
30 Yaw-seux yaox ka pux tix,
daux rhawm nawh kied gaux,
dex sang man lox yiiem tix ka daux kix.
Nawh tom lih hu khaing kix,
mox tix ka daux lawg it tix,
yiiem tix cu.
31 Nawh khoek ngai tix mai lih,
gun rhawm tix,
tawx lox kieh kix khan priex.
32 Yaw-seux tang ngawm tix pheen,
pux aik nawh khankix tang ngawm tix pheen.
Pui Ai-cix pa hoek tix sawm tau tang ngawm tix pheen,
mawh kheu pui Ai-cix sawm tau mai pui Si-paw-lai kied mawh pa tik pak ngai.
33 Pux aik nawh khankix ngawm tix sawm veeh plak ngai tix plak Yaw-seux,
dee ngawm jauh ka kawn ai tom hoek ka kawn nghoik dia tix hawt paox tix.
Yuh nin kix kied daox kah,
mai lai jhak paox tix.
34 Taux lhax mawh pa guah hoek khaing pheen ngawm Yaw-seux mai tawx ka kix;
cie Piien-ya-ming mawh cie pui tang kaux phuan son.
Kix sawm tau mai Yaw-seux kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
