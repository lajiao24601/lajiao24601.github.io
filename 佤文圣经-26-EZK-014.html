Siyiex ne raw ka pui pa krub riang rao
14
Kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex ra loe kaux hoek ka aux,
mai chawk ka aux.
2 Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
3 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
pui in kix hoik kied braong doe ka riang rao,
pa hawt khaix riang rao an hoik cawd ka daux phi map.
Kix kawn ce aux sang pawk ka lox tix lie?
4 “Pex tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
pui Yi-seu-liex ku kaux ang ah,
pa hoik braong doe ka riang rao,
pa hawt khaix riang rao an,
pa cawd ka daux phi map,
khaix an mai baox hoek tix khiox khaw ka cao jhai,
aux tag sang sidiex gau ka nawh kheu hoik viex nawh riang rao hoek tom nie heue!
5 Riang rao an kix hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex singai hu khaing aux,
viang mawh nan,
aux simie krax pa sidiex gau aux ka kix deeh pon kieh kix yum rhawm ka aux heue.
6 “Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
deeh hoek heue,
tik vud ka riang rao pa krub pex,
tik vud ka ceu pa hoik seeing brai pui ka khankix heue!
7 “Mawh pui Yi-seu-liex ang ah,
mawh nyo si nie pa ot ka daux pex khankix ang ah,
pa hoik tik vud ka aux,
pa hoik braong doe ka riang rao,
pa hawt khaix riang rao an,
pa hoik cawd ka daux phi map,
khaix an mai baox hoek tix khiox khaw ka cao jhai khankix,
aux tag sang hoek tix sidiex gau ka nawh heue!
8 Aux tag sang ciyang ka nawh,
mai teei nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun kieh pui hun nyiah ka nawh.
Aux tag sang teei tix tik kix khaing daux cub pui cie aux.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
9 “Phan koe cao jhai pon krax pa pie lhien tix pawk lox pui ka daux krax pa lud,
an hoik mawh kheu aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa hoik pie lhien ka nawh.
Aux ciyang ka nawh,
mai teei tix tik nawh khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
10 Cao jhai mai pui pa sawg ka nawh tag sang kham krax ciyang awm paox tix.
11 Aux yuh nin mawh son sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai baox tik vud ka aux,
ang lai baox yuh ka phi map mai kieh to kaux tix seeing brai.
Kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux;
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Naw-yax, Ta-ni-eux, Yaw-paw
12 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
13 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
phan meung tix meung yuh phi map,
ang nghiet ka lox aux,
aux tag sang nyeu taix tix klat ka krax dee hoek priex preem kix,
mai kieh sibhawm praix lih,
pui mai khawng brie tag sang yum ka cai rhawm khawm uik.
14 Viang Naw-yax,
Ta-ni-eux,a
mai Yaw-paw loe kaux hoik ot dee an kawx,
krax pa rauh see kix pon taoh ngie ka to kaux kix khaing.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
15 “Phan aux kieh to sat naog ka haktiex an,
kied pui yum,
kieh pui lhat graung kah,
mai pui ang lai pon hoek dee an,
16 aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux tix pa im ju leeing ju,
viang pui an kix loe kaux hoik paung ot dee an kawx,
mai kix kawx ang pon taoh ka kawn praix tix.
Kix pon taoh ngie ka to kaux tix khaing,
mai haktiex an tag sang tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag daux tu pa riem heue.
17 “Phan aux kieh naing lih ka haktiex an,
vaig gawn pa lawm hoik pauh pui mai khawng brie yum khawm uik,
18 aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux tix pa im ju leeing ju,
viang pui an kix loe kaux hoik paung ot dee an kawx,
mai kix kawx ang pon taoh ka kawn simex kawn bun tix;
kix pon taoh ngie ka to kaux tix khaing heue.
19 “Phan aux kieh jhawm saux preng lih ka haktiex an,
mai yuh khawm uik laux leeing ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
pauh pui mai khawng brie yum khawm uik.
20 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux tix pa im ju leeing ju,
viang Naw-yax,
Ta-ni-eux,
mai Yaw-paw hoik ot dee an kawx,
mai kix kawx ang pon taoh ka kawn simex kawn bun tix;
krax pa rauh see kix pon taoh ngie ka to kaux kix khaing heue.”
21 bSimiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh naing,
sibhawm praix,
to sat,
mai jhawm saux preng krax laux leeing pa kied lhat graung pui ka in kix pon ceu,
hoek tix ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kieh pui mai khawng brie laux leeing khawm uik.
22 Phan koe pui pa yaox tix luat luan,
mai hoik baox taoh ka kawn praix tix,
yam to kix tix hu mox tix ka pex dee an,
pex tag caw tix lam lia tix yaox krax pa kied caox caig kix,
mai pex tom taug tawng sidawng,
aux ciyang yuh nin ka Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mawh pa khaw tix yuh tete.
23 Pex kawx hoik tawng grawng pa yuh aux hoik mawh pa koe krax koe grawng tete.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
