Paoh cum
6
Aux yaox kawn yung paoh cum aliah mu ka daux tix mu.
Bawg yam an,
aux mhong lox to sat pa im ju pon mu an kix tix mu pa ploi lox tix luah awm lox si num praix ah nin:
“Hoek heue!”
2 aAux jhak,
mai yaox brung paing tix mu;
pui pa brug brung an viex ka ak puh,
mai rieb ka mhok khri pa tawx ka nawh,
nawh lih hu puing naing,
pex baox pon pex heue.
3 Yam paoh kawn yung cum ka daux ra mu tix mu,
aux mhong lox to sat pa im ju pon mu an kix ka daux ra mu tix mu ah nin:
“Hoek heue!”
4 bMawh kheu nan koe brung tang mu tix mu lih hoek,
mawh brung raoh.
Kaw pa brug brung an hoik pon ka siawp sibrawm,
mai kieh naing lih ka piang haktiex,
kawn pui ra lai pauh paox tix yum;
nawh baox rieb ka vaig lang ting tix mag heue.
5 cYam paoh kawn yung cum ka daux loe mu tix mu,
aux mhong lox to sat pa im ju pon mu an kix ka daux loe mu tix mu ah nin:
“Hoek heue!”
Aux jhak,
mai yaox brung lung tix mu;
kaw pa brug brung an ka daux taix nawh giex ka jiang tix mu.
6 Aux awm pa mhong lox pa lih hoek khaing daux to sat pa im ju an kix pon mu ah nin:
“Mau riang pui tix ngaix pon liag ngie nghox ca tix kongsheng khaing;
mau riang pui tix ngaix pon liag ngie nghox ca pa lang mu loe kongsheng khaing.
Bee sime mai plai ang pon tix du dig heue!”
7 Yam paoh kawn yung cum ka daux pon mu tix mu,
aux mhong lox to sat pa im ju pon mu an kix ka daux pon mu tix mu ah nin:
“Hoek heue!”
8 dAux jhak,
mai yaox brung paing pau tix mu;
kaw pa brug brung an ceu kaux nawh mawh “krax yum”,
meung yum hawt khaix nawh.
Kix hoik pon ka siawp sibrawm,
kuang ka haktiex pon son tix son,
kix teei krax naing,
krax sibrawm praix,
jhawm saux preng,
mai to sat ka piang haktiex pauh ka pui heue.
9 Yam paoh kawn yung cum ka daux phuan mu tix mu,
aux yaox khuan ju pui pa pauh pui yum kheu son pa hoik sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex,
pa kham sakse kaux sijax khankix ka greem khu dee tan.
10 Kix kok yo lox si num ah nin:
“Cao pa singax pa kaux sijax eue!
Tag sang prawng ca maix kawn pui ka piang haktiex yam mawx,
pawk bix son pa hoik jauh nham yix lie?”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
kix hoik rieb ka kraung ceep dha paing tix kaux tix pheen,
mai baox mhong lox pa kok yo ka kix,
mai nhie kix baox laux lee tix tix vud,
krox jun ka pui pa yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex tau mai kix,
mai kawn Khrit khankix kawx hoik pauh yum awm kix,
yam hoik khrawm kaux pui kub heue.
12 eAux baox yaox kawn yung paoh cum ka daux liah mu tix mu.
Bawg yam an,
haktiex yung yang khawm uik;
singaix tieh tix lih faik,
awm man keei sum lung tix plah;
khix tieh tix lih raoh khawm uik,
awm nham yuh nan;
13 fsimuing cawd ka piang haktiex khaing rao ma,
awm cawd plix khaox ruh pa ang bax teem khaing khaox pa phlu bhaung cawd yuh nan.
14 gRao ma awm phuk lai pa pua tix yuh nan ang lai yaox;
gawng lua mai klawn tiex gah tix hu khaing dee ot.
15 hSimiang piang haktiex,
cao giex kaing,
simiang naing,
pui pa koe mau koe siawp sibrawm,
siliex cawng,
mai pui pa pon tix bheeik luan,
to mox tix ot daux gawng mai daux rang khawm uik.
16 iKix kok yo ka gawng lua mai ka rang simaox ah nin:
“Ghrup piang to kaux yix heue!
Kheem mox ka yix,
son sang mox tix khaing pak ngai kaw pa ngawm piang dang ngeung an,
mai khaing krax song rhawm yung heue!
17 jMawh kheu hoik hoek singaix bawg yam pa song rhawm kix;
pui mawx sang pon cung kaux cong lie?”
