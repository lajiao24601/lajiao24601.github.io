Khiox khaw krax teem ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi
6
aSimiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi song rhawm ne raw ka aux;
khiox khaw maix paw vi ciyang aux ka daux krax pa song rhawm maix liak!
2 Khiox khaw maix caox rhawm,
mawh kheu iak riang iak rao aux;
khiox khaw maix pai aux moi,
mawh kheu hoik uik riang uik rao aux.
3 To kaux rhawm rhi aux hoik ot ka daux krax pa kha rhawm;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
krax kha rhawm in kawn sang koe miex glang lie?
 
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh hoek tix taoh ka aux liak!
Mawh kheu muh maix ka aux,
khiox khaw maix taoh aux liak!
5 Ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa doh keud ka maix ka daux si meeig!
Ka daux meung yum kawx ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa yaog yaw ka maix!
 
6 Aux hoik iak riang iak rao kheu laux rhawm tix;
ku it aux hoik caux lan kheu pa roik rawm ngai aux ku sawm,
mhawn gaong aux hoik lan khawm uik.
7 Pui pa koeng ka aux kieh aux yiiem;
ngai aux hoik uah,
pa riang ngai aux hoik vu vi heue.
 
8 bPui pa yuh kaing pa caox pa caig eue,
yoi heue!
Mawh kheu hoik mhong Simiang Siyiex ka lox yiiem aux.
9 Nawh nghiet ka lox pa kok yo aux krax teem coi.
Nawh rieb ka lox buan pa rawg aux.
10 Pui koeng ka aux tag sang si pup ka krax kaig krax tua kheu ang tix pon pex;
kix dak tix deeh ka daux krax pa lhat pa si yaung dum mawx dum an heue.
