Lai Eu-pa-ti-yax
Krai dawt
Phuk lai pa dawt in tiiem tix khaix hoik laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung been baux sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num phuan yieh tix daix liah num, mawh ka daux bawg yam mawx tete ang lai pon tawng sidawng. Yam an pui pa koeng ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa mawh pui Yi-tong, jau lao ang gaux rhawm ka laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung, mai kawn jawm yam laux leeing kix yieh ka veng, khaix an lih mawh cao pa teem ka pui con. Eu-pa-ti-yax krai lox jhai Yi-tong son sang pon krax ciyang, mai laux leeing tau mai meung Yi-seu-liex pui koeng tang meung khankix heue.
Dee si dauh, nawh krai pao pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang deeh hoek ka daux haktiex pa ap tix, mai meung Simiang Siyiex tag sang koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pui Yi-tong pon krax ciyang (1-14)
2. Singaix cie Simiang Siyiex (15-21)
