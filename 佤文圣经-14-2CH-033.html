Ma-na-si simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 21.1–9)
33
Yam koe ju Ma-na-si kao ra num nawh mawh simiang Yo-tax,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix phuan phuan num.
2 aMa-na-si hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui pa ot haktiex an sivoe;
pui an kix hoik mawh pa grheu Simiang Siyiex kix lih hu yam laig pui cie nawh ka daux haktiex in.
3 Nawh deeh cox sum ka nyiex vad pa ot ka dee lung pa hoik yieh keeing nawh Si-si-cia laux,
mai baox yuh khu krub ka Pa-lix,
sum rong riang bun siyiex Ya-seu-la,
mai krub ka riang khix simuing.
4 bNawh hoik yuh khu dee tan been li tang ceu ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex lox nhie pui krub tix ka leeing ju,
5 Mai baox yuh khu krub riang khix simuing ka biang brawk nyiex buan khawm ra.
6 Nawh tawx kawn simex tix mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai pauh nawh yum ka ngawd Sing-noeng-cee.
Nawh yum rhawm ka cao yuh si pawk,
pa chix si meed,
mai sawg tix chawk ka cao chix kia lai tiag taix,
pa kok ka khuan.
Nawh yuh ka phi map pa ting,
tieb tie ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai cheeik tix kieh Simiang Siyiex song rhawm.
7 cNawh deuh riang rao pa krub ot ka daux nyiex buan.
Son nyiex buan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ka Ta-wue mai kawn simex nawh Saw-law-meung ah nin:
“Nyiex buan daux Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mawh pa laug aux lih khaing daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu,
son sang mawh dee krub pui ka aux.
8 Phan pui Yi-seu-liex nghiet ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik krai siliex aux Maw-si ka kix,
aux tag ang kieh kix yaox tix lih hu khaing haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka tax du kix in.”
9 Ma-na-si thung pui Yo-tax yuh phi map,
kawn lheu kin khaing phi map pa yuh pui haktiex an pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex lih hu ka plak sivoe ngai pui cie tix khankix heue.
Ma-na-si loh rhawm rhi tix
10 Viang Simiang Siyiex hoik ne raw ka Ma-na-si mai ka kawn pui cie nawh,
mai kix kawn ca ang cu tix nghiet.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh simiang naing Ya-sux puing ka Yo-tax.
Kix pon giex ka Ma-na-si,
mai koik nawh ka goik,
mad nawh ka maox ceng tawng,
viex nawh hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
12 Yam yaox Ma-na-si krax pa kha rhawm,
nawh tom deeh tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
mai veeh tix hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh,
khiox khaw krax pa teem coi nawh.
13 Siyiex nghiet lox rawg nawh buan,
mai kieh nawh deeh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
deeh cox tix giex ka kaing.
Kaing in hoik kieh Ma-na-si yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex tete heue.
14 Khaix hoik luan kaing in,
Ma-na-si baox thiem thie guh veng Ta-wue plak praix ka plak lih singaix lhaong,
jauh ka ngawd pa dex dee lih rawm Ci-suing,
hu plak laong hoek ka mee Siviex Kax mai Eu-fe-leux.
Nawh kawx hoik kieh pui naing ot pau ka veng Yo-tax ku dauh,
mai kieh simiang naing mawh cao bung.
15 Nawh teei tix tik ka riang rao siyiex pa krub pui tang ceu tang cad pa hoik deuh ot daux nyiex buan sivoe an,
mai yieh laux khawm khu dee tan been li pui tang ceu pa koe ka blaong gawng nyiex buan mai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tang dauh.
Ma-na-si gah tix tik ceu in kix ka plak praix veng.
16 Nawh deeh cox sum ka khu krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai tan lu Krax-tan Pa-nhim mai Krax-tan Pa-yaog-yaw ot ka piang.
Nawh tawx lox nhie pui Yo-tax khawm uik krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex.
17 Viang kawn pui ca kawn ot tix yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka dee krub tang dauh khankix,
mai kix tan lu ngie ka Simiang Siyiex khaing heue.
Kaing giex Ma-na-si hoik si dauh
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 21.17–18)
18 Kaing pa yuh Ma-na-si tang ceu khankix,
mai pa rawg nawh buan ka Siyiex,
khawm cao pa chix yaox sivoe khankix pa si kah ka nawh khaing ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex,
hoik tiiem tix ka daux Lai Been Simiang Yi-seu-liex.
19 Lox rawg buan simiang mai lox pa pawk Siyiex,
khawm phi map pa yuh nawh sivoe sang loh nawh rhawm tix,
pa mawh kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh nawh,
nyiex vad piang gawng iak pa sum nawh,
riang rong bun siyiex Ya-seu-la pa yuh nawh,
mai riang rao krub tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Cao Pa Chix Yaox Sivoe.
20 Yam hoik yum Ma-na-si,
kix si paung nawh ka daux nyiex haw simiang;
kawn simex nawh Ya-meung plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Ya-meung simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 21.19–26)
21 Yam koe ju Ya-meung tix nga ra num nawh mawh simiang Yo-tax,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung ra num.
22 Nawh hoik awm keeing tix Ma-na-si yuh nan,
yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
krub riang rao pa krub keeing tix.
23 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi deeh veeh tix ka Simiang Siyiex awm keeing tix,
nawh tieh tix yuh phi map pa lheu ting khaing keeing tix heue.
24 Siliex Ya-meung tieh vheen ka nawh,
mai klauh nawh yum daux nyiex haw simiang.
25 Kawn pui Yo-tax pauh pui pa briex mox tix pauh Ya-meung yum,
mai ap kawn simex nawh Yaw-si-yax plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
