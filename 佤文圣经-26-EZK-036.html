Siyiex tawx buan ka pui Yi-seu-liex
36
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai gum lox aux ka gawng Yi-seu-liex khankix,
2 krai ka kix,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong yuh nin:
pui pa koeng ka Yi-seu-liex hoik gaux rhawm yaox pui krax laux rhawm ah nin:
‘Gawng iak pa dawg koe khankix tag sang mawh cie ex heue!’
3 “Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix krai lox jhai,
krai pao aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
yam tud rhim meung pa koe sivie siveng khankix ka gawng iak Yi-seu-liex,
mai pon teei tix cie ka kix,
kawn pui hoik nyiah ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
pa mawh gawng ting gawng iak,
klawng iak daux ngawd,
dee riem pa hoik tik pui,
mai meung pa hoik tud rhim pui,
veng pa hoik map nai pui khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka pex heue.
5 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ne raw meung pa koe sivie siveng khankix ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
nie blawn mawh Yi-tong.
Kix hoik gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
ang een pui koe ceu,
hoik teei tix cie ka haktiex aux,
hoik bleet ka pak rib aux heue.
6 “Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix krai lox jhai ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex;
tag caw tix krai ka gawng ting gawng iak,
ka klawng iak daux ngawd yuh nin:
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
hoik song rhawm kheu krax kaig tua pa yuh meung piang haktiex ka kix tom nie.
7 Mawh kheu nan aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
yaog taix tix tiex tu yuh nin:
meung pa koe sivie siveng khankix tag sang kham krax pa kaig tua tete.
8 “Viang mawh nan,
gawng Yi-seu-liex khankix eue,
khaox ox pex tag sang baox yaox tix haok huan,
mai plix plix son pui cie aux.
Pui cie aux eue,
pex hoik dex sang yaox tix deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix heue.
9 Aux tag sang joh jhan ka pex,
tag sang kieh haktiex pex baox sum baox rhuat.
10 Aux tag sang kieh kaux pui pex baox thiem thie tix haoh hun;
pex tag sang paung ot ka daux veng,
mai cox deeh sum ka nyiex ma tix.
11 Aux tag sang kieh pui mai khawng brie haok huan;
pex tag sang koe kawn praix tom nie,
kaux pui pex nie khaing sivoe an.
Aux tag sang kieh pex paung ot ka haktiex pa hoik paung ot pex sivoe an,
mai kieh pex haok huan khaing sivoe an.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
12 Pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux tag sang thung pex deeh hoek,
mai kieh pex deeh yaox tix paung ot ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex.
In tag sang lih mawh haktiex cie pex,
mai ang lai baox kieh kawn praix pex cai rhawm heue.
13 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
kawn pui krai mawh haktiex in pa ih ka pui,
mawh haktiex pui pa laux leeing pa grai meung.
14 Viang mawh nan,
jauh yam in haktiex in ang lai chix ih ka pui,
ang lai baox deeh kieh pex dut simie ang koe kawn praix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin.
15 Haktiex in ang lai baox deeh kieh pui meung tang meung map nai ka pex,
nyiah ka pex,
ang lai baox deeh kieh pui pex laux leeing meung grai.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Krax ot krax koe pa khraox pui Yi-seu-liex
16 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
17 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
yam paung ot pui Yi-seu-liex ka haktiex kix,
krax ot krax koe kix hoik seeing brai yuh haktiex an.
Ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
kaing pa yuh kix hoik awm yam lih nham seeing bun miex ang singax ka daux been.a
18 Kix hoik pauh pui yum ka daux haktiex in,
hoik krub ka riang rao,
hoik kieh haktiex seeing brai,
mawh kheu nan,
aux tom klup krax pa song rhawm tix piang kix.
19 Aux ciyang kix kheu kaing yuh kix pa ang singax singeh,
mai ploi kix hu ka haktiex meung tang meung.
20 Kix hoek dee mawx ang ah hoik kieh ceu kaux pa singax aux pon krax pa kaig tua;
pui ah nin:
‘Pui in kix mawh pui cie Simiang Siyiex,
kix mawh pa hoek khaing haktiex cie Simiang Siyiex dee an.’
21 Pui Yi-seu-liex viang kix hoik vuk vied vuk viang tix hoek dee mawx,
mai hoik kieh ceu kaux pa singax aux pon krax pa kaig tua,
viang mawh nan,
aux kia ngien ka ceu kaux pa singax tix heue.
22 “Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
kaing pa sang yuh aux ang mawh pa yuh son pex,
hoik mawh son ceu kaux pa singax aux,
pa mawh ceu kaux pa hoik kieh pex pon krax kaig tua ka meung ku meung.
23 Pex hoik sax kieh ceu kaux aux pon krax pa kaig tua ka meung piang haktiex;
aux tag sang kham sakse grawng ceu kaux aux mawh pa singax ka daux kix.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
24 Aux tag sang khrawm pex ot tau khaing meung haktiex ku dauh,
mai thung pex deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix.
25 Aux tag sang teei rawm singax singeh phruah ka pex,
si ghrah tix tik ka ceu pa seeing brai pex khawm uik,
yieh tix tik ka riang rao pa krub pex khawm uik.
26 bAux tag sang tawx pex koe rhawm pa khraox,
khuan pa khraox.
Aux tag sang loh ka rhawm rhi pex,
mai teei tix tik ka rhawm rhi pa kiien awm simaox yuh nan,
loh pex koe rhawm rhi pa koe nex koe nham,
rhawm rhi pa chix cu chix nghiet.
27 Aux tag sang tawx khuan tix ka pex,
kieh pex pon priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
cu nghiet ka gum lox ping nyap aux.
28 “Yuh nin pex tom taug pon yaox tix paung ot ka haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka tax du pex sivoe an.
Pex tag sang mawh pui cie aux;
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex pex.
29 Aux tag sang taoh pex luat luan khaing ceu pa kieh pex seeing brai khawm uik.
Aux tag sang kieh pex pon nghox koi tom nie,
ang lai koe krax sibhawm praix.
30 Aux tag sang kieh khaox cie pex plix plix tom nie,
kaung ma lih to ma to koi tom nie,
pex tom taug ang lai baox yaox krax pa kaig tua meung haktiex kheu sibhawm praix.
31 Pex tag chix doh keud ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix mai krax pa lud loe pa yuh tix,
koeng ka to kaux tix kheu phi map krax lud loe pa yuh tix.
32 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang kieh pex tawng,
aux yuh in kix khawm uik ang mawh son pex.
Pex tag caw tix koe krax kaig krax tua son kaing pa hoik yuh tix heue.”
33 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Khaix hoik si ghrah aux ka phi map pex singax singeh,
tag sang kieh pex paung ot ka daux veng,
mai deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma.
34 Sivoe an,
pui pa hu krax hoik krai ma kaung pex kied riem tete,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang kieh pex deeh cox sum ka kaung ma in.
35 Kix tag sang krai yuh nin,
kaung ma pa awm daux tiag daux tu sivoe an,
yam in hoik tieh tix lih mawh si peem Yi-tiien;
veng pa hoik yieh pui laux,
tud rhim pui sivoe an,
yam in hoik tieh tix lih mawh dee haoh hun pui,
mai baox koe tab pa kaux cong.
36 Yam an,
meung pa koe sivie siveng pa ang bax laux leeing tom taug tawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa deeh cox sum ka daux dee riem,
deeh cox sum ka kaung ma pa riem.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin.
Aux hoik krai,
tag sang yuh mawh heue.”
37 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex deeh cox khiox khaw aux teem coi tix;
aux tag sang kieh kaux pui kix baox haoh hun awm phung yung yuh nan.
38 Veng pa hoik riem tag sang si ghox ka pui,
mai hoik si ghox awm teei pui phung yung tan lu ka yam yuh poi ka Yie-lu-sa-leung sivoe an.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
