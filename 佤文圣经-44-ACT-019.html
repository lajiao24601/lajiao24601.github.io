Pao-law ot ka Yi-fu-saw
19
Yam ot Ya-paw-law ka Keu-ling-to,
Pao-law lai ling ka daux haktiex plak daux,
hoek ka Yi-fu-saw.
Nawh yaox pui gau tix blah ot dee an,
2 nawh tom chawk kix ah nin:
“Yam yum rhawm pex ka Simiang Ye-sux,
hoik rieb ang pex rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Yix ang sax mhong ka grawng pa koe Khuan Singax Singeh heue.”
3 Pao-law baox chawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pex cub com ceu pa tix lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh krax cub com Yaw-hang heue.”
4 aPao-law ah nin:
“Krax cub com Yaw-hang mawh pa yuh son pui pa hoik loh rhawm rhi tix khankix;
nawh krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex tag caw tix yum rhawm ka kaw pa hoek khaix nawh an,
pa mawh Ye-sux heue.”
5 Kix mhong ka lox in,
kix tom cub com ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux.
6 Pao-law deuh taix tix piang kix,
Khuan Singax Singeh lih hoek ka piang to kaux kix,
kix tom si kah ka lox daux khuan,
mai baox krai pao gum lox Siyiex.
7 Kix khawm uik koe been kao ra kaux heue.
8 Tix neh loe khix,
Pao-law hoek ka nyiex sidiex awm been pa deuh kix,
mai viex rhawm ting sidiex gum lox Siyiex,
thung thiang pui ka grawng meung Siyiex,
nhie pui yum rhawm.
9 Ka daux kix koe pui tix blah kiien rhawm ang cu tix yum rhawm kah;
salao gum lox Simiang Ye-sux ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun.
Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law viex pui gau lih hu khaing kix,
mai ot tix yuh krax pa kum kawng ka nyiex sidiex ka Thoe-la-nu ku singaix.b
10 Kaing in hoik yuh tix ka daux ra num,
mawh kheu nan,
can pui pa paung ot ka haktiex Ya-si-yax,
mawh pui Yo-thaix ang ah,
pui tang ceu tang cad ang ah,
hoik mhong ka gum lox cie Simiang Ye-sux khawm uik heue.
Kawn simex See-ci-wa
11 Siyiex hoik tawx Pao-law yuh mhai pa daox pa am khankix.
12 Kheu nan hoik koe pui pa teei ka man awt taix pa siao nawh,
mai ka man pa fua nawh deuh ot piang to kaux pui pa koe jhawm saux,
mai chix kieh jhawm saux an grai,
mai chix kieh nang khuan phi caox lih khaing to kaux kawn pui.
13 Koe pui Yo-thaix pa hawt hu tix pie lhien,
pa grheu phi si ngie kawx,
keud tix sang pie lhien tix teei ka ceu kaux Ye-sux yuh kaing in.
Kix krai ka khuan phi caox ah nin:
“Aux tawx lox nhie pex lih ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux pa hoik een Pao-law rhawm tix kah.”
14 Pa yuh kaing in kix mawh kawn simex See-ci-wa simiang cao giex kaing li Yo-thaix aliah kaux heue.
15 Viang mawh nan,
khuan phi caox pawk ka kix ah nin:
“Aux tawng ka Ye-sux,
mai hoik tawng nang ka Pao-law mawh pui mawx;
viang mawh nan,
mawh pex pui mawx?”
16 Pui an pa laig khuan phi caox daux sau riang tix lauh ka kix,
mai pon pex ka kix.
Kix hoik mat khawm uik,
kraung ceep kix hoik griah phawng vid,
mai to tix lih khaing daux nyiex.
17 Can pui Yo-thaix mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka Yi-fu-saw,
hoik mhong ka kaing in,
mai kied lhat kah;
ceu kaux Ye-sux jauh yam an nie pon krax pa yung yi.
18 Koe kawn Khrit tom nie,
jauh tix krai doh ka kaing ceu pa hoik yuh kix sivoe an sidawng ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
19 Pui pa yuh si meed yuh sat tom nie viex lai cie kix,
hoek pum tix dauh,
mai gleeing ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
Kix sawn tix yaox ka ngawh lai in,
khawm uik hoik mawh been phuan mheen ting mau luah.
20 Yuh nin,
gum lox Simiang Ye-sux hoik tauh hu ku dauh ku dee,
hoik pon krax pa haok huan heue.
Krax pa suk sag Yi-fu-saw
21 Khaix hoik luan kaing in,
Pao-law but rhawmc tix laig ka daux haktiex Ma-chi-toeng mai Ya-kai-yax,
hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Nawh ah nin:
“Yam hoik hoek aux ka dee an,
aux kawx tag sang vhi ka Law-ma.”
22 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ploi pui pa teem coi tix ra kaux pa mawh Thi-maw-thaix mai Yi-la-to hoek ka Ma-chi-toeng,
to kaux nawh kawn ot ka haktiex Ya-si-yax tix vud heue.
23 Ka daux bawg yam an,
mawh kheu gum lox Simiang Ye-sux,
ka daux veng Yi-fu-saw lih krax pa ting suk sag kah.
24 Koe cang mai mau pa kaux Ti-mi-theux tix kaux,
nawh mawh pui pa eei tix ka pa teei mau yuh riang bun siyiex Ya-ti-mi;
kaing in hoik kieh cang mai pon ngawh riang tix tom nie.
25 Ti-mi-theux khrawm ka pui pa yuh kaing in kix,
mai ka paox pa yuh kix kaing in tau,
ah nin ka kix:
“Ex ku kaux ku pui,
pex hoik tawng krax pa kieh ex haok huan mawh ka daux kaing in.
26 Yam in pex hoik mhong,
mai hoik yaox Pao-law in yuh nawh ceu pa tix.
Nawh ah nin:
‘Siyiex pa teei kawn pui taix tix yuh ka ang mawh Siyiex heue.’
Gum lox in hoik kieh pui Yi-fu-saw mai pui haktiex Ya-si-yax tom nie yum rhawm.
27 Jhak tix yaox ka kaing pa yuh ex in tee rien tix sang pon ka ceu kaux pa caox mai ka daux krax laux leeing.
Ja lao ang mawh nan,
pie nyiex vad bun Ya-ti-mi kawx hoik pon krax pa map nai pui,
min plak ngai siyiex bun pa hoik dawg krub haktiex Ya-si-yax mai kawn pui piang haktiex kawx,
tag sang si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!”
28 Pui hun mhong lox in,
kix tom song rhawm riag yo ah nin:
“Bun siyiex Ya-ti-mi pui Yi-fu-saw kied ting kied lhaong heue!”
29 Mawh kheu nan,
suk sag yuh veng khawm uik.
Pui pa cau nyhau khankix giex ka Pao-law mai ka pui Ma-chi-toeng ra kaux pa mawh Kai-yo mai Ya-li-ta-ku,
kix preeng tix laig daux nyiex klieh.
30 Pao-law ce tix sang hu ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
viang mawh nan pui gau khankix ang kieh nawh laig.
31 Kawn koe simiang haktiex Ya-si-yax ra loe kaux pa mawh paox grawm Pao-law,
mai ploi pui hu krai ka Pao-law,
ang cu nawh nieb pon laig daux nyiex klieh.
32 Bawg yam in,
nyiex bang hoik suk sag khawm uik,
koe pui tix blah kok ka pui in,
koe tix blah kok ka pui an,
pui plak nie ang tawng grawng khrawm kix tix dee an mawh sang yuh ceu tix.
33 Pui Yo-thaix nyheen Ya-li-sang-tax hu plak sivoe ngai,
koe pui tix blah ce kaing in mawh pa caw nawh tix klawm.
Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-li-sang-tax vu vied taix tix sang krai ka pui hun.
34 Viang mawh nan,
yam tawng pui hun nawh mawh pui Yo-thaix,
kix rom tix krawh tau ah nin:
“Bun siyiex Ya-ti-mi pui Yi-fu-saw kied ting kied lhaong heue!”
Kix hoik krawh yuh nin tom hoek ra taix nari heue.
35 Khaix an,
simiang ting pa tiiem lai ka daux veng an kieh pui hun yien yawg tix.
Nawh ah nin:
“Pui Yi-fu-saw eue,
pui mawx ang tawng veng Yi-fu-saw mawh pa kia ngien ka bun siyiex vad Ya-ti-mi,
mai simaox singax an pa hoek khaing piang rao ma lie?
36 Kheu ang lai koe pui pa thiang ka kaing in,
pex tag caw yien yawg tix,
ang pon tix nieb pon yuh ceu pa ang khaw caw.
37 Pex viex pui in hoek dee in,
viang mawx nan,
kix ang bax briex kraung pa koe daux nyiex vad,
mai ang bax map nai ka siyiex bun ex.”
38 “Phan Ti-mi-theux mai sin ngao cie nawh sang deuh phi map ka pui mawx,
hoik tang koe singaix tang ngaix pa tat tix deuh,
mai ka simiang pa nghiet tix mhong ka lox krai pui,
kix chix tix rom jheeh paox tix tau.
39 Phan koe kaing tang ceu,
mai chix tix prawng ka daux bang kum pa khaw tix kum.
40 Awm krax suk sag ka daux in singaix,
pui tag sang si kaw ka beeh glawm ex kah.
Krax pa suk sag in khawm uik ang khaw,
ex kawx ang tawng ka lox pa sang krai tix lih.”
41 Khaix hoik krai ka lox in,
nawh tom prung prang kix tang hu khaing dee khrawm kix paox tix heue.
