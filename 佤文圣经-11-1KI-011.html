Saw-law-meung tik vud ka Siyiex
11
aSimiang Saw-law-meung muh ka bun meung pui tang ceu tom nie.
Lheu khaing kawn bun simiang Ai-cix,
nawh baox teei tix moeng kawn bun pui Heux mai pui Maw-ya,
pui Ya-meung,
pui Yi-tong,
mai pui Si-toeng khankix.
2 bSimiang Siyiex tawx lox ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
ang cu kix mex moeng ka pui tang ceu,
mawh kheu kix chix nyaw glaw pui Yi-seu-liex hawt ka Siyiex kix.
Viang mawh nan,
Saw-law-meung kied muh ka kawn bun in kix,
3 mai teei tix moeng kawn bun simiang aliah yieh kaux,
khaix an kawn koe moeng iak loe yieh kaux;
kix kieh Saw-law-meung tik vud ka Siyiex.
4 Yam hoik kuad ju nawh,
bun an kix nyaw glaw nawh hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux;
nawh ang lai awm keeing tix Ta-wue yuh nan yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh.
5 Nawh krub ka bun siyiex Ya-see-tha-lux pa krub pui Si-toeng,
mai siyiex Maw-law pa krub pui Ya-meung,
pa yum koeng Siyiex kah.
6 Nawh phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang awm keeing tix Ta-wue yuh nan,
grum nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex.
7 Nawh sum khu tix mu ka piang gawng plak lih singaix ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
son sang krub ka siyiex Ci-mawx pui Maw-ya,
pa yum koeng Siyiex kah,
mai nawh baox sum khu pa ot piang gawng iak tix mu son sang krub ka Maw-law siyiex pa yum koeng pui Ya-meung.
8 Nawh baox sum dee krub can moeng iak tang meung khankix ka riang rao tom nie,
mai kieh kix tok nyawng tan lu ka siyiex kix dee an heue.
9-10 Viang Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik lih bleeh ka Saw-law-meung ra bawg,
tawx lox ang cu nawh krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
viang mawh nan,
Saw-law-meung ang nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
mai tieh tix tik vud ka nawh.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka Saw-law-meung,
11 mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Mawh kheu su maix tix tik vud ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai maix,
mai ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx aux,
aux tag sang deeh teei meung cie maix hu,
mai tawx ka kawn cau maix tix kaux.
12 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu son keeing maix Ta-wue,
aux ang nyang yuh kaing in lih mawh yam kawn im ju maix ka piang haktiex,
krox kawn simex maix mawh simiang,
tom tag kieh kaing in lih tix mawh.
13 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang teei meung khawm uik hu khaing daux tawm taix nawh,
mawh kheu son siliex aux Ta-wue,
mawh kheu son veng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa hoik laug aux son to kaux tix in,
aux tag sang deuh pui tix ceu ka nawh heue.”
Pui pa koeng ka Saw-law-meung
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh nyiex simiang Yi-tong pa kaux Ha-tax tieb tie ka Saw-law-meung.
15-16 Singaix pa hoik luan,
yam pon kuang Ta-wue ka Yi-tong,
Yaw-ya cao bung kaing naing cie Ta-wue hoik hu si paung pui pa yum daux naing dee an.
Yaw-ya kaog ot tau mai pui naing cie tix ka Yi-tong liah khix.
Ka daux glang baux bawg yam an,
nawh hoik pauh simex pui Yi-tong yum khawm uik,
17 mawh ngie Ha-tax mai pui Yi-tong siliex keeing nawh tix blah pa pon to ka Ai-cix,
pa ang yaox tix yum khaing.
(Yam an Ha-tax kawn mawh kawn nyawm.)
18 Kix lih hu khaing Mi-tiien hoek ka Pa-lang,
mai neuex tix tau mai pui dee an tix blah,
khaix an,
peung rhawm tix ka Ai-cix.
Simiang Ai-cix tawx Ha-tax cie kaung ma nyiex ot,
mai baox tawx nawh ih priex preem.
19 Ha-tax hoik pon pa khaw rhawm simiang ka daux krax pa yuh paox grawm;
simiang tom tawx pux moeng tix,
pa mawh pux moeng simiang pa kaux Ta-pi-ni mawh moeng Ha-tax.
20 Nawh tom keh kawn simex tix kaux son Ha-tax,
mai kok ceu kaux mawh Ci-nu-pa.
Moeng simiang eei ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh ot tau mai kawn ai simiang khankix ka daux nyiex haw heue.
21 Grawng yum Ta-wue mai Yaw-ya khaing haktiex hoik hoek ka daux yhaok Ha-tax ka haktiex Ai-cix,
Ha-tax tom ah nin ka simiang:
“Kieh aux deeh ing ka meung haktiex tix liak heue!”
22 Simiang ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx lie?
Khex mawh kawn cud cha maix ka ceu pa tix,
mawh kheu nan,
tom keud tix sang deeh ing ka nyiex lie?”
Ha-tax pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang mawh,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux deeh ing liak!”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom deeh ing ka meung haktiex tix heuec.
Ha-tax mawh simiang Yi-tong,
mai lih mawh pui pa yum koeng ka pui Yi-seu-liex heued.
23 Siyiex kieh nang Li-shuing kawn simex Yi-li-ya-tax tieb tie ka Saw-law-meung.
Li-shuing to tix lih khaing simiang tix Ha-ta-ti-shiex simiang Saw-pa,
24 nawh khrawm ka cub pui rhim tix cub,
mawh cao kaing kix.
Kaing in lih khaix hoik pon pex Ta-wue ka Ha-ta-ti-shiex,
mai pauh ka paox grawm nawh,
kix hoik mawh pui Shui-li-yax khawm uik.
Li-shuing hawt khaix pui pa ot greem nawh hoek ka Ta-ma-see-keux,
mai paung ot dee an,
mai pui yaog nawh mawh simiang heue.
25 Yam kawn im ju Saw-law-meung,
Li-shuing hoik awm Ha-tax yuh nan,
mawh pui koeng ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
nawh baox thiem thie krax laux leeing hoek ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai baox giex gang ka Ya-lang heue.
Siyiex cu nghiet ka Yie-law-paw-ang
26 Koe pui tang kaux tix kaux pa tieb tie ka simiang Saw-law-meung,
pa mawh kawn cau simiang kawn simex Ni-pa ceu pui Yi-fa-liien pui Si-li-tax pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang.
Miex nawh mawh miex mai,
kaux Si-lu-ya.
27 Plak greem mawh grawng pa tieh vheen nawh.
Yam tawm Saw-law-meung ka daux klawk Mi-lawx plak lih singaix,
veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai cox guh veng,
28 Yie-law-paw-ang hoik mawh pui awn ju pa pon yuh kaing tix kaux.
Saw-law-meung yaox grawng pa yuh nawh kaing khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
tom tawx nawh mawh cao pa kuang ka can pui yuh kaing haktiex Ma-na-si mai haktiex Yi-fa-liien khawm uik.
29 Koe tix ngaix,
Yie-law-paw-ang kaoh khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui See-law cao jhai Ya-si-ya si pup nawh ka daux krax,
Ya-si-ya ceep ka kraung khraox tix pheen.
Yam an mawh ngie kiex pa ot ka daux tiag nong nong.
30 Ya-si-ya peeik kraung khraox tix,
mai riah lih koe kao ra bliah,
31 khaix an ah nin ka Yie-law-paw-ang:
“Maix teei tix viex hu kao bliah,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin ka maix:
‘Aux tag sang teei siawp sibrawm pa kuang meung hu khaing Saw-law-meung dee an,
mai tawx ka maix kao ceu.
32 Viang mawh nan,
kheu son siliex aux Ta-wue,
kheu son veng Yie-lu-sa-leung in pa hoik laug aux lih khaing haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
kieh mawh cie to kaux tix,
Saw-law-meung chix tix deuh tix ceu pui.
33 Aux yuh mawh nin,
kheu hoik mawh Saw-law-meung pa tik vud ka aux,
hu krub ka siyiex tang meung:
pa mawh bun Siyiex Ya-see-tha-lux pa krub pui Si-toeng,
Siyiex Ci-mawx pa krub pui Maw-ya,
siyiex Maw-law pa krub pui Ya-meung.
Saw-law-meung lieh lheu ka aux;
nawh yuh krax pa phit lud,
ang lai awm keeing tix Ta-wue yuh nan priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap aux.
34 “ ‘Viang mawh nan,
aux ang bax teei ka meung khawm uik khaing nawh dee an;
aux ca kawn kieh nawh koe siawp sibrawm yam kawn im ju tix.
Aux yuh nin,
hoik mawh kheu son pa laug aux ka siliex aux Ta-wue,
mawh kheu nawh hoik priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap aux.
35 Aux tag sang teei siawp sibrawm lih hu khaing kawn simex Saw-law-meung,
mai tawx ka maix kao ceu pui,
36 viang mawh nan,
kieh kawn simex Saw-law-meung deuh pui tix ceu.
Yuh nin,
aux tom koe doeh ceu cad siliex aux Ta-wue tix kaux pa kuang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung leeing ju leeing phan heue;
veng in mawh dee pa laug aux tix kieh mawh dee krub ka aux.
37 “ ‘Yie-law-paw-ang eue,
aux sang yaog maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
maix tag sang kuang ka haktiex pa simie maix tix yaox kah.
38 Phan maix grum nghiet ka aux,
yuh kaing pa khaw ka rhawm aux khankix,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
hawt yuh lox pa tawx aux,
awm siliex aux Ta-wue yuh nan,
aux tag sang ot tau mai maix leeing ju leeing phan.
Aux tag sang kieh maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
khaix an mai kieh doeh ceu cad maix plud tix giex ka kaing,
awm pa hoik yuh aux ka Ta-wue yuh nan.
39 Aux tag sang ciyang doeh ceu cad Ta-wue jao phi map Saw-law-meung,
viang mawh nan,
krax ciyang in ang mawh leeing ju heue.’ ”
40 Mawh kheu nan,
Saw-law-meung keud sawn grawng sang pauh tix Yie-law-paw-ang;
viang mawh nan,
Yie-law-paw-ang to hu mox tix hoek ka simiang Ai-cix pa kaux See-sha dee an,
nawh paung ot ka Ai-cix,
tom hoik yum Saw-law-meung,
nawh tag deeh ing ka meung ot tix heue.
Saw-law-meung yum
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 9.29–31)
41 Kaing yuh Saw-law-meung tang ceu,
kaing haok huan pa yuh nawh ang ah,
ping nyia cuyi pa koe nawh ang ah,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Saw-law-meung ku grawng.
42 Nawh mawh simiang ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex tipon num.
43 Yam hoik yum nawh,
kix si paung nawh ka veng Ta-wue;
kawn simex nawh Law-paw-ang plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
