Tiang Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh
14
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix hu krai ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nhie kix kling tix deeh plak khaix,
mai yuh bang sivoe veng Pi Ha-si-lu,
dee dex ka Pa-lix Si-feung,
si nax veng Mi-tawx mai Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
3 Simiang Ai-cix ce pui Yi-seu-liex sang lud krax ka daux tiag,
pa mawh mhaik hoek tie ka krax.
4 Aux tag kieh rhawm nawh kiien,
mai nawh tag leue hawt khaix pex.
Yam an aux tag puing pex ka simiang Ai-cix mai pui naing nawh,
kix tom tag chix yung yi ka aux,
mai tawng aux mawh Simiang Siyiex.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex hawt tix yuh ka gum lox an heue.
5 Simiang Ai-cix mhong grawng hoik to pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nawh mai siliex nawh khawm uik deeh tieh tix,
ah nin:
“Ex hoik yuh kaing ceu pa tix lie?
Kieh pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih hu,
mai ang lai yaox tix yuh khaing siliex cawng son ex!”
6 Kheu nan simiang Ai-cix brug ling law naing tix,
mai khrawm pui naing,
7 viex ling law naing khawm uik hu,
khawm ling law naing pa blawn kua liah yieh lhang,
mai kieh simiang naing mawh cao pa bung.
8 Simiang Siyiex kieh rhawm simiang Ai-cix kiien,
nawh hawt leue khaix ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mawh kheu ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih hu khaing Ai-cix ang koe krax lhat krax graunga.
9 Pui naing Ai-cix khankix,
khawm pui naing brug brung,
pui naing ling law khawm uik,
leue ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
hoek ka praok Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh baoh hawt dee sum kix nyiex bang;
dee an dex ka haktiex Pi Ha-si-lu mai haktiex Pa-lix Si-feung heue.
10 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix yaox simiang Ai-cix mai pui naing cie nawh krup ka kix,
kied koe krax lhat krax graung,
tom rawg Simiang Siyiex taoh kix.
11 Kix ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Khex ang si meeig dee sang si paung yix lai koe daux Ai-cix lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai hu tawx yix yum daux tiag?
Maix hoik viex yix hu khaing Ai-cix;
maix jhak,
kaing ceu pa mhawm pa yuh maix!
12 Khex ang yix hoik dawg krai ka maix sivoe sang lih hu yix,
kaing in sang lih ang mawh lie?
Yix hoik dawg krai ka maix,
ang cu maix kuang ka yix,
ghram yix yuh kaing siliex cawng ka daux Ai-cix khaing.
Yuh yix kaing siliex cawng kawn lheu mhawm khaing sang yum yix daux tiag heue!”
13 Maw-si pawk ah nin:
“Paw lhat graung!
Cung kaux cong.
Daux singaix,
pex sang yaox grawng pa taoh Simiang Siyiex ka pex!
Pex ang lai sang baox yaox ka pui Ai-cix khankix.
14 Simiang Siyiex tag puing naing son pex;
pex tawng yawg tix mhawm mhawm khaing heue.”
15 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix yiiem yo ka aux yuh ka mawx?
Maix krai ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nhie kix hu plak sivoe ngai khaing.
16 Maix yaog ka khaox gix tix,
mai nyeu ka piang rawm sigrit sigra.
Rawm sigrit sigra tag sang guah tix koe ra plak,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag yaox tix hu piang dee kroh tiex daux rawm sigrit sigra.
17 Aux tag kieh pui Ai-cix khankix kiien rhawm,
kheu nan kix tag laig daux rawm mai hawt leue khaix pex.
Viang mawh nan,
aux tag pon pex ka simiang Ai-cix,
ka pui naing cie nawh,
khawm ling law naing khawm pui naing brug brung cie nawh khawm uik,
mai kix tag sang yung yi ka aux.
18 Yam hoik pon pauh aux ka kix,
mai kix tag tawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
19 Kawn cau Siyiex pa hoik dawg hu sivoe pui Yi-seu-liex,
yam in deeh gah tix ot plak khaix;
mai rong pai awm kawx deeh gah tix ot plak khaix,
20 mai khan ka pui Ai-cix mai pui Yi-seu-liex tang ot.
Pai awm kieh plak pui Ai-cix faik,
viang mawh nan,
kieh pa riang jhan ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
kheu nan ka daux leeing lang sawm,
pui naing Ai-cix ang pon dex ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
21 Maw-si nyeu khaox gix tix ka piang rawm sigrit sigra,
mai Simiang Siyiex tom kieh bhaung ting plak lih singaix,
phlu ka rawm sigrit sigra deeh plak khaix.
Phlu ka leeing sawm,
mai greem rawm sigrit sigra lih mawh tiex kroh.
Rawm khankix tag guah tix lih koe ra plak,
awm guh ding;
22 bPui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
lih hu ka piang tiex kroh,
tiang ka rawm sigrit sigra.
23 Pui Ai-cix khankix hawt tix leue ka kix,
khawm pui naing brug brung,
pui naing brug brung hoik laig daux rawm sigrit sigra khawm uik.
24 Yam hoek plak pon ngawb,
Simiang Siyiex jhak tix yaox ka piang pui naing Ai-cix khankix khaing ka daux rong ngu mai rong pai awm,
mai kieh kix lih suk sag.
25 Nawh kieh jaong ling law naing pui Ai-cix blawn daux glaox,
ang lai chix chuh.
Pui Ai-cix khankix ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex teem ka pui Yi-seu-liex puing ka ex,
ex nied tix to hu khaing heue.”
26 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Nyeu taix maix ka piang rawm sigrit sigra,
kieh rawm deeh kleuk paox tix,
mai kheem ka piang pui Ai-cix,
ka piang ling law naing mai ka piang pui naing brug brung kix.”
27 Kheu nan Maw-si nyeu taix tix ka piang rawm sigrit sigra;
yam hoek riang praix,
rawm deeh kleuk paox tix awm diag prim.
Pui Ai-cix khankix ce tix sang lih khaing daux rawm,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh rawm theen ka kix.
28 Rawm sigrit sigra deeh awm diag prim,
mai kheem ka ling law naing Ai-cix,
ka pui naing brug brung khawm pui naing khawm uik pa hawt tix leue ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
pie tix kaux kawx ang lai deuk.
29 Viang mawh nan pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
yaox tix hu ka piang tiex kroh,
daux rawm sigrit sigra,
mai rawm hoik lih guah tix awm guh ding ra plak heue.
30 Ka daux ngaix an,
Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex khaing ka daux tawm taix pui Ai-cix,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yaox ka to kaux yum pui hawt praok rawm.
31 Yam yaox pui Yi-seu-liex ka pa teei Simiang Siyiex taix pa ting riang tix yuh laux ka pui Ai-cix,
kix tom lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai yum rhawm ka nawh,
khawm siliex nawh Maw-si heue.
