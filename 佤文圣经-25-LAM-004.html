Khaix hoik laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung
4
Khri pa mhawm toh riang ex ang lai koe pa riang;
simaox nyiex buan pum tix ot ka daux laih heue.
 
2 Pui pa nhum ju Shi-ang hoik sawn tix mawh khri pa mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai ex,
yam in mai hoik lih mawh aw tiex khankix heue.
 
3 Sox tiag hoik tawng grawng sang eei ka kawn tix,
mai pui cie aux lai awm sim ka-la-uk daux tiag pa cau nyhau yuh nan heue.
 
4 Kix kieh kawn ngie yum ka simie tix nyaux rawm;
kawn nyawm sawg tix ih priex preem,
mai ang koe pui pa tawx kix ih heue.
 
5 Pui pa ih ka priex nyawm rawm lhong jheeng jheeng,
yam in hoik yum ka cai rhawm daux laih daux cau;
pui pa mhawm krax ot krax koe jheeng jheeng,
yam in hu sawg tix ih priex preem ka dee tik pui kram nyiex heue.
 
6 aKrax ciyang pa yaox pui cie aux tix kham,
kawn lheu kin khaing pa kham pui Saw-to-ma.b
Siyiex kieh Saw-to-ma laux leeing,
mawh ngie tix khiep ngai khaing heue.
 
7 Krax pa ting pa lhaong ex kawn lheu paing khaing rawm rha mai khaing rawm teeh;
Riang rao kix hoik naog,
kaux hak kix hoik kluing heue.
 
8 Yam in kix it ka daux laih,
ang koe pui pa tawng ka kix;
pak ngai kix kawn lheu lung khaing suah,
hak kix kawb si-ang kix,
hoik krawx kroh awm nge khix yuh nan heue.
 
9 Pui pa yum ka bang naing,
mai kawn lheu mhawm khaing pui pa yum ka cai rhawm;
mawh kheu pui pa koe plak khaix khankix hoik yum kheu cai rhawm tix.
 
10 cKrax laux leeing pa si pup pui cie aux hoik mawh krax lhat graung pa ting,
miex pa koe krax caox rhawm hoik ngaik tix ih ka nex kawn tix heue!
 
11 Simiang Siyiex klup krax pa song rhawm tix lih,
krax song rhawm nawh rup,
mai gleeing ka Shi-ang lih mawh haktiex pa si dawm heue.
 
12 Ka piang haktiex ang koe pui pa yum rhawm,
simiang meung ka piang haktiex kawx ang yum rhawm ah nin:
Koe pui tud pui rhim mawx pa sang pon puing laig ka diang siviex Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
 
13 Viang mawh nan,
kaing in hoik lih tix mawh;
mawh kheu hoik yuh cao jhai nawh ka phi map,
caox giex kaing li nawh hoik pauh ka pui pa ang koe krax lud loe yum heue.
 
14 Simiang nawh khankix hoik pu pi ka daux laih awm pui dug ngai yuh nan;
to kaux kix khawm jung hoik mawh nham pa phrit kah,
ang koe pui pa pon theeh ka kix heue.
 
15 Kawn pui kok yo ka kix ah nin:
yoi singai,
pex pa mawh ceu pa ang singax singeh heue!
Paw vi dex ka aux heue!
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom vu vie tix hu ku dauh ku dee,
ang koe meung haktiex tix meung kawx pa cu tix rieb ka kix heue.
 
16 Simiang Siyiex ang lai baox kia ngien ka kix;
nawh hoik grheu kix hu.
Nawh ang lai baox deeh kieh cao giex kaing li ex mawh pa koe ceu;
nawh ang lai baox deeh chix kia ngien ka simiang ex heue.
 
17 Ex keud pot jun pot krax taoh teem,
mai krax taoh teem ang lih tix hoek tom si dauh;
Ex kawn ca keud pot jun pot ka meung haktiex pa ang koe riang rao tix teem coi ka ex an heue.
 
18 Pui koeng hoek tix leue ka ex khawm pon daog,
kieh ex ang pon lawng krax ka daux laih.
Singaix bawg yam cie ex hoik uik heue!
Krax lawng ex hoik si dauh heue!
 
19 Pui pa leue ka ex kawn lheu brax khaing klang pa koe daux rao ma;
kix leue tix giex ex piang gawng,
mai kuan krox jun tix sang yuh ex laux leeing ka daux tiag daux tu heue.
 
20 Kix hoik pon giex ka simiang pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex kah,
hoik awm giex hu ka riah ju ex,
pa mawh simiang pa hoik een ex rhawm tix kah,
pa sang kia ngien ex luat luan khaing pui koeng pa ting riang ting rao heue.
 
21 Pui Yi-tong eue,
pui haktiex Wu-see eue,
pex sau riang sau rao tix yuh krax gaux rhawm heue!
Krax laux krax leeing pex kawx dex sang hoek heue!
Pex kawx ot ka kaong tix pon krax kaig krax tua heue.
 
22 Shi-ang hoik deeh tawx ka ngawh riang phi map;
Simiang Siyiex ang kieh ex vuk vied vuk viang tom rheeng.d
Viang mawh nan,
Yi-tong eue,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka maix;
Nawh tag sang plih ka phi map maix lih heue.
