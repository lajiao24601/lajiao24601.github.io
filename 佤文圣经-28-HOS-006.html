Pui Yi-seu-liex ang loh ka rhawm rhi tix tete
6
Pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Kieh yix deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Nawh hoik riah ka yix,
deeh chix pai ka yix;
nawh hoik lauh yix mat,
mai tag deeh chix kie ka yix.
2 Khaix hoik luan ra loe singaix,
nawh tag deeh tawx riang rao pa khraox ka yix,
mai kieh yix ot ka plak sivoe ngai tix jheeng jheeng.
3 Kieh yix sau riang tix sawg tix tawng ka Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh tag sang hoek tete,
awm lih hoek pa riang yuh nan,
awm rawm lhiex pa caux ka haktiex yuh nan heue.”
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pui Yo-tax eue,
sang kia ngien aux pex yuh ka mawx lie?
Krax muh pex ka aux hoik awm pai awm yam pon ngawb,
tix vud khaing tag grai hu,
awm rawm ciex hoik grai hu yam riang praix.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ploi cao jhai tix khankix hoek ka pex dee an,
krai grawng pa sang prawng ca aux ka pex,
grawng pa sang yuh aux pex laux leeing.
6 aPa simie aux mawh krax muh pa kaux cong,
ang mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie;
aux simie pui cie tix tawng ka tix,
mai ang mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing heue.
7 “Viang mawh nan,
kix tieh vheen ka aux ka daux Ya-tang,b
mai hoik lieh lheu ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai kix.
8 Ci-liex mawh veng pa naog krax pa caox pa caig tix mu,
hoik mawh khaix jauh nham pui ku dauh.
9 Cao giex kaing li hoik awm pui con yuh nan,
mai hoik lih tix mawh cub caw con pa kuan yieh ka pui,
kix hoik keud sawn tix yuh pui laux con sin ngao pui ka krax an pa hu plak See-ciien.
10 Aux yaox kaing pa caox pa caig ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nin:
pui cie aux hoik krub riang rao dee an,
mai hoik kieh to kaux tix seeing brai heue.
11 “Phan mawh pex pa mawh pui Yo-tax in kix,
aux kawx hoik deuh tix een ka bawg yam singaix pa ciyang ka pex heue.”
