Shi-ang pa mawh veng Siyiex
48
Ka daux veng cie Siyiex ex,
ka piang gawng pa singax nawh,
Simiang Siyiex mawh pa ting pa lhaong,
nawh khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw.
2 aGawng Shi-ang cie Siyiex kied lhaong kied mhawm;
veng cie simiang kieh kawn pui piang haktiex gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
3 Siyiex ot daux tab daux veng,
nawh hoik jhi grawng mawh to kaux tix nyiex dee mox pa kaux cong heue.
 
4 Simiang khawm uik yuh paox pie tau,
hoek tix puing ka gawng Shi-ang.
5 Yam yaox kix ka veng in,
mai kix hoik lhat graung yaoh yee;
kix to mai khawm si yaung si kua tix.
6 Krax lhat krax graung pon giex ka kix,
kha rhawm tix awm yam sang keh bun yuh nan,
7 awm reu ting pa klauh bhaung plak lih singaix yuh nan.
8 Ex hoik sax mhong ka kaing pa yuh Siyiex;
yam in ka daux veng cao bung kru naing kaing mheen pa mawh veng Siyiex,
ex hoik pon tix yaox ka ngai tix;
nawh tag sang priex pau ka veng in leeing ju heue.
 
9 Siyiex eue,
ka daux nyiex buan maix,
yix keud krax muh maix pa koe leeing ju leeing phan.
10 Pui piang haktiex hoik rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix khawm uik;
ceu kaux maix hoik tauh hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
maix teei pa rauh pa see tix giex ka kaing;
11 Kieh Shi-ang gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue!
Kieh veng Yo-tax gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue!
Mawh kheu krax pa prawng ca maix khaw caw heue.
 
12 Pex tag caw tix hu vung viang ka gawng Shi-ang,
simin tix yaox ka nyiex lhaong veng koe miex jan;
13 Pex tag caw tix lam lia mai jhak tix yaox ka tab guh veng nawh,
son sang yaox tix krai lao ka doeh ceu cad pex ah nin:
14 Siyiex in hoik mawh Siyiex ex leeing ju leeing phan;
nawh tag sang duk thung ka ex tix ju hoik tix ju heue.
