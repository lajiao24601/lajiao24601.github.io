Ang-noeng mai Tha-ma
13
Kawn simex Ta-wue pa kaux Ya-sa-long,
koe nan tix kaux pa kied mhawm grang,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Tha-ma.
Kawn simex Ta-wue tang kaux pa kaux Ang-noeng muh ka nawh.
2 Ang-noeng lih koe jhawm saux kheu keud tix nawh,
kheu mawh Tha-ma bun krih,
nawh keud tix sang ang koe bawg yam tix dex dawt mai nawh.
3 Ang-noeng koe paox grawm tix kaux pa kaux Yaw-na-ta,
pa mawh kawn simex pux ke Ta-wue pa kaux See-mi-yax.
Pui in kied lhak khoik,
4 nawh ah nin ka Ang-noeng:
“Maix hoik mawh kawn simiang,
yuh pa tix maix lai kroh krawx tix singaix lheu khaing tix singaix lie?
Maix chix tix krai aux mhong lie?”
Ang-noeng ah nin:
“Aux muh Tha-ma nan Ya-sa-long,
pa mawh yieh rom keeing tang miex heue.”
5 Yaw-na-ta ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix chix tix lhien tix koe jhawm saux,
mai noi tix it ka piang ku.
Yam hoek keeing maix tix kia ngien maix,
maix mhaing lox ka nawh ah nin:
‘Khiox khaw nan aux Tha-ma hoek kia ngien ka aux.
Aux sang kieh nawh ot dee in,
tong aux ih khao mun ra loe lon,
ka dee pa kok chix yaox aux,
mai kieh to kaux nawh tawx aux ih.’ ”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Ang-noeng lhien tix koe jhawm saux,
mai noi tix it ka piang ku heue.
Simiang Ta-wue hu jhak ka nawh.
Ang-noeng ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Khiox khaw nan aux Tha-ma hoek dee ot aux tin liak,
mai tong aux ih khao mun ra loe lon,
ka dee pa kok chix yaox aux,
mai hoek tawx aux ih heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ploi pui hu ka nyiex haw mai krai ka Tha-ma ah nin:
“Maix hu ka nyiex Ang-noeng,
mai yuh viex ceu pa ih hu tawx nawh ih.”
8 Tha-ma tom hu.
Ang-noeng kawn noi tix it ka piang ku.
Tha-ma teei glua mien,
mai khre yuh mawh lon khao mun ra loe lon ka dee pa kok chix yaox Ang-noeng kah,
khaix an viex hu tong kah.
9 Tha-ma teei khao mun lih khaing daux pan pa tong khao mun,
tawx ka Ang-noeng,
viang mawh nan,
Ang-noeng ang rieb tix ih.
Nawh ah nin:
“Nhie pui khawm uik hu!”
Mawh kheu nan,
pui khankix hu.
10 Ang-noeng ah nin:
“Viex khao mun hoek ka praok ku dee it aux,
maix teei taix tix sau aux ih.”
Tha-ma tom teei khao mun hu tawx ka nawh.
11 Yam teei Tha-ma taix tix tawx nawh ih ka khao mun,
nawh giex ka Tha-ma ah nin:
“Nan aux,
it tau mai aux heue!”
12 Paw!
Tha-ma ah nin:
“Pux ke,
paw khin aux yuh kaing pa seeing brai yuh nin.
Ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex ang pon tix kieh kaing pa seeing brai yuh nin koe!
13 Aux kawn sang chix yaog kaing tix yuh ka mawx sivoe pui lie?
Phan mawh maix,
maix kawx tag sang tik pak ngai tix khawm uik ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
Maix hu krai ka simiang,
nawh tag sang chix tawx aux ka maix.”
14 Viang mawh nan,
Ang-noeng ang cu,
nawh ting riang khaing Tha-ma,
mai khin tix phit tau mai nawh heue.
15 Khaix hoik yuh ka kaing in,
Ang-noeng kied koeng ka Tha-ma,
ka krax pa koeng nawh kawn lheu ting khaing krax pa hoik muh nawh sivoe an.
Nawh ah nin ka Tha-ma:
“Maix lih hu!”
16 “Ang!”
Tha-ma ah nin:
“Maix grheu aux hu,
krax pa phit maix nie ting khaing sivoe heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
Ang-noeng ang nghiet.
17 Nawh nhie siliex pa kia ngien ka tix laig daux nyiex,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux ang rhawm yaox ka bun in,
maix grheu nawh hu,
mai sau jhong siviex!”
18 Siliex an tom grheu Tha-ma lih hu,
mai sau jhong siviex heue.
Yam an Tha-ma ceep ka kraung dhaa,
pa hoik yie ceep kawn simiang yam ang tix nyang bax koe mex.
19 Nawh groi nyaox ka piang kaing tix,
mai riah kraung ceep tix,
taix nawh khawm ra khawn tawb pak ngai tix,
mai yiiem khawm hu tix heue.
20 Pux ke nawh Ya-sa-long yaox nawh,
tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh Ang-noeng pa yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka maix lie?
Nan eue,
maix paw vi laux rhawm.
Ang-noeng hoik mawh pux ke maix pa rawm keeing tang miex,
maix ang pon tix krai grawng in ka pui tix kaux kawx.”
Tha-ma tom paung ot daux nyiex pux ke tix Ya-sa-long khawm laux rhawm tix heue.
21 Simiang Ta-wue mhong grawng in,
nawh kied song rhawm kah.
22 Mawh kheu hoik seeing brai yuh Ang-noeng nan tix Tha-ma,
Ya-sa-long hoik yum koeng ka Ang-noeng,
mai ang lai si kah tau mai nawh heue.
Ya-sa-long pawk bix
23 Khaix hoik luan daux ra num,
Ya-sa-long ot dex ka Yi-fa-liien yaong Pa-lix Sha-saw,
kieh pui cang tix ghrip hauk yung,
yuh ka poi.
Nawh ghaok simiang hoek dee an khawm uik.
24 Ya-sa-long hu ka simiang Ta-wue,
mai ah nin:
“Keeing pa mawh simiang eue,
aux hoik kok pui pa ghrip hauk yung hoek,
khiox khaw maix mai siliex maix khankix gaux rhawm tix hoek liak.”
25 Simiang pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang chix,
kawn eue,
phan yix hu khawm uik,
kied sidaing mawh pa nyhap yuh maix heue.”
Ya-sa-long baox khiox khaw simiang ra loe bawg,
mai simiang ca kawn ang cu tix hu,
viang mawh nan,
simiang hoik nawb buan piang nawh heue.
26 Ya-sa-long ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
nhie pux ke aux Ang-noeng hu tau mai aux heue.”
Simiang ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawh kawn sang kieh nawh hu lie?”
27 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-sa-long baox khiox khaw ra loe bawg,
Ta-wue tom tag cu Ang-noeng mai kawn simiang tang kaux hu tau mai Ya-sa-long heue.
Ya-sa-long hoik rien khre ka priex nyawmb rawm lhong tom nie,
28 mai tawx lox ka kawn cau pa hawt khaix tix ah nin:
“Pex pau tix jhak mhawm mhawm,
yam yaox pex yoek Ang-noeng ka plai,
aux sang tawx lox ka pex,
mai kieh pex pauh nawh yum.
Paw vi lhat,
grawng in aux lawk klawm khawm uik.
Pex kuan ngie tix viex rhawm ting sau taix tix ka nawh khaing,
paw vi lai keud jee keud blaong!”
29 Kawn cau pa hawt khaix nawh tom hawt tix yuh awm lox pa nhie Ya-sa-long mai pauh ka Ang-noeng.
Kawn Ta-wue tang kaux khankix tom brug law to heue.
30 Yam kawn lawng kix krax,
koe pui khan viex lox hoek krai ka Ta-wue ah nin:
“Ya-sa-long pauh ka kawn simex maix khawm uik,
tix kaux kawx ang deuh!”
31 Simiang yaog tix kaoh cung,
laux rhawm riah kraung ceep tix griah,
mai noi tix it piang tiex.
Siliex pa ot tau mai simiang khankix kawx,
hoik riah kraung ceep tix griah nang.
32 Viang mawh nan,
kawn simex pux ke Ta-wue pa kaux See-mi-yax pa kaux Yaw-na-ta ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
kawn simex maix khankix ang bax yum khawm uik,
mawh ngie Ang-noeng tix kaux khaing pa pauh yum.
Jauh tom yam yuh Ang-noeng kaing pa seeing brai ka nan Ya-sa-long pa kaux Tha-ma,
Ya-sa-long hoik but rhawm tix sang pauh nawh yum,
pie jhak maix tix yaox ka pak ngai Ya-sa-long,
khaw tix hoik tawng kah.
33 Khiox khaw maix Vawng paw vi yi ka lox pa ang tie mawh tete an kix,
pa krai hoik yum kawn simex maix.
Tete te lai,
mawh ngie Ang-noeng pa yum tix kaux khaing.”
34 Yam an,
Ya-sa-long hoik to mox tix heue.
Jiet mawh bawg yam an,
pui naing cao pa pau bang yaox pui tix phung,
kaoh lih hoek khaing blaong krax Heu-law-niangc,
mai nawh tom krai grawng pa hoik yaox tix ka simiangd.
35 Yaw-na-ta ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Maix jhak,
kawn simex maix khankix hoek,
awm lox pa hoik taug krai aux ka maix.”
36 Lox pa krai nawh jiet hoik uik,
kawn simex Ta-wue khankix hoek bleeh.
Kix ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting.
Ta-wue mai siliex nawh kawx laux rhawm mai yiiem heue.
37-38 eYa-sa-long to hu mox tix hoek ka simiang Ci-sux kawn simex Ya-mi-fu pa kaux Ta-mai dee an,
nawh paung ot tan loe num.
Ta-wue yiiem son kawn tix Ang-noeng tom rheeng.
39 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik luan krax pa laux rhawm nawh,
mai nawh kied sidaing braong ka Ya-sa-long heue.
