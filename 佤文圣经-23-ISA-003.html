Krax suk sag Yie-lu-sa-leung
3
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang teei ka can ceu pa lo pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai pui Yo-tax hu khawm uik;
nawh tag sang teei ka priex preem pa ih kix mai rawm nyaux kix hu.
2 Nawh tag sang yuh laux leeing ka pui pa ting riang kix mai pui naing pa kua kix,
simiang cao prawng see mai cao jhai kix,
cao chix si pawk mai kuad song kix,
3 simiang naing mai simiang ka daux haktiex kix,
pui pa koe min ka daux krax chix tawng khawm uik pa chix si meed khankix.
4 Simiang Siyiex sang teei tix siao pui pa nhum ju pa ang bax kub rhawm keud hoek tix kuang ka piang kix.
5 Pui ku kaux hoik tang simie tix ih kaing paox tix;
pui pa nhum ju tag sang map nai ka pui pa riex ju;
pui pa ang koe ceu koe cak tag sang map nai ka pui pa koe min khankix heue.
6 Yam hoik hoek ka daux singaix an,
koe pui tag sang laug pui tix kaux lih khaing ka daux ceu pui cie to kaux tix,
mai ah nin:
“Yam hoik hoek ka daux singaix pa kha rhawm in,
maix kawn koe tix ceep kraung tix pheen,
maix hoek tix mawh simiang yix heue.”
7 Pui an pawk lox ah nin:
“Paw vi laug aux mawh simiang;
aux ang pon teem coi ka maix.
Aux ang koe pa ih pa nyaux,
mai ang koe kraung ceep nang heue.”
8 Mawh tete,
veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang laux leeing heue!
Meung Yo-tax tag sang som nai heue!
Can lox pa hoik krai kix mai kaing pa hoik yuh kix ang koe tix ceu kawx pa ang lieh lheu ka Simiang Siyiex.
Kix hoik map nai Siyiex ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
9 Pui hoik chix tawng grawng pa ang kix mhawm ka yaox pak ngai kix.
Kix hoik awm pui Saw-to-ma yuh nan,
mai ang lai mox ka krax pa lud loe tix.
Kix tag sang yaox krax laux krax leeing;
kaing pa yuh kix tag sang deeh hoek ka piang kix heue.
10 Pui pa yuh kaing pa rauh pa see tag sang pon krax gaux rhawm gaux rhia,
mai mhawm kaing yuh ku ceu;
kix tag sang pon yaox tix ih nyaux khaing daux kaing pa yuh tix.
11 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig tag sang yaox krax laux leeing;
kix tag sang yaox krax pa pawk bix kheu kaing ceu pa caox pa yuh kix heue.
12 Phan mawh pui cie aux,
kawn nyawm tag sang gut kheem ka pex,
bun miex tag sang giex gang ka piang pex heue.
Pui cie aux eue,
simiang pex ang bax duk thung ka pex mhawm mhawm,
mai pex tom ang tawng ka krax pa sang hu tix heue.
Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka pui cie tix
13 Simiang Siyiex hoik chawk cheng tix yaox ka grawng see khawm uik;
nawh tag sang cung tix krai pao ka grawng pa prawng ca ka pui cie nawh.b
14 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kok ka kuad song mai simiang khaing ka daux pui cie tix hoek,
mai krai pao grawng pa lud loe kix ah nin:
“Pex hoik yuh laux ka si peem plix siiet;
ka daux nyiex ma pex hoik pum naog ka kraung khrai pa tud pex khaing pui hot pui sibhawm.
15 Pex ang koe akhuang tix yuh kaing ceu pa ang khaw ka pui cie aux,
ang koe akhuang tix pie lhien ka pui hot pui sibhawm.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Pa ne raw ka bun miex Yie-lu-sa-leung
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex jhak,
bun miex Yie-lu-sa-leung kied sidaing ting rhawm lhaong rhi heue!
Yam hu kix krax,
kix yaox ngai baoh tix jhak ka rao ma.
Kix jhak pui ka daog ngai tix,
mai yuh tix koe jhawm yam lawng krax,
son sang kieh hing pa naog ka caong tix luah ah khring khring yuh nan.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix;
aux tag sang khlao ka hauk kaing kix,
mai kieh to kaux dee kaig kix lih heue.”
18 Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang teei hu ka can kraung khrai pa ting ceu ka bun miex khankix,
khawm kraung pa koe ka caong kix,
kaing kix,
ngawg kix,
19 mai ka plawng taix kix.
Nawh tag sang teei hu ka man tawb pak ngai kix,
20 mai ka mhok kix,
kraung khrai pa koe ka plawng taix kix mai ka nyoi kix,
21 mai ka jeep giien kix,
kreem pa koe ka meeh kix,
22 mai kraung ceep kix pa ngawh khawm uik,
sibex dha,
man tawb kaux,
hau mau.
23 Kawn koe kraung dha man rhi,
man rhi keei sum awt jheeh,
kawn koe lawk kaing mai man rhi tawb ngai kix;
tix ceu kawx ang lai deuk ka heue.
24 Kix ang lai baox siao ka rawm pa si oe bhawng,
to kaux kix hoik cux si oe.
Kix ang lai baox siao ka maox khlax pa mhawm,
mai teei siao maox man keei sum pa ting gawng mad nyoi tix kah.
Kix ang lai baox duing hauk kaing tix mhawm,
mai hauk kaing kix tag sang khrum uik;
kix ang lai baox ceep ka kraung mhawm,
mai kix tag sang ceep ka kraung blaox.
Krax pa mhawm grang kix tieh tix lih mawh krax kaig krax tua heue.
25 Simex pa koe ka daux veng,
viang kix hoik mawh pui pa ting riang ting rao kawx,
mai kix tag sang yum ka daux bang naing.
26 Siviex veng tag sang riag yo mai yiiem doe;
veng ot kix tag sang awm bun pa peeik pui kraung,
pa ngawm ka daux bhawng tiex heue.
