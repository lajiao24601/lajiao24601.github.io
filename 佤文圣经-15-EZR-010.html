Grawng pa ang lai kieh mex moeng ka pui tang ceu
10
Yam gup Yi-see-la tix rawg buan yiiem ka sivoe nyiex buan,
doh keud ka phi map in kix,
koe cub pui Yi-seu-liex tom nie,
khawm simex bun mai kawn nyawm,
mai laux rhawm yiiem grai vung viang ka nawh.
2 See-cia-ni pa mawh kawn simex Yie-shiie ceu cad cub nyiex Yi-lang ah nin ka Yi-see-la:
“Ex teei tix moeng ka bun tang ceu hoik mawh kaing pa ang khaw ka Siyiex;
viang hoik mawh nan kawx,
pui Yi-seu-liex ca kawn koe krax keud pot ceeix ceeix.
3 Yam in,
ex tag caw tix yuh sijax mukheu pa kaux pa cong ka Siyiex:
ex caw tix tawx bun in kix mai kawn praix kix hu.
Ex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox maix mai ka pui pa yung yi ka Siyiex.
Yix sang yuh hawt lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex.
4 Maix hoik koe beeh glawm tix yuh ka kaing in.
Ex joh jhan teem coi ka maix;
maix caw tix koe rhawm kua yuh kaing in heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-see-la nhie cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
khawm simiang pui haoh pui hun pa kawn deuk khankix tiex tu,
mai krai grawng sang hawt kix tix yuh ka gum lox pa hoik krai See-cia-ni.
Kix hawt tix yuh heue.
6 Khaix an,
Yi-see-la kaoh khaing sivoe nyiex buan mai hu laig daux lawg nyiex Yaw-ha-nang pa mawh kawn simex Yi-li-ya-seu,
nawh ita dee an,
nawh ang ih ang nyaux,
laux rhawm son pui pa vuk vied vuk viang pa lieh lox khankix heue.
7-8 Simiang ka daux pui haktiex khankix tawx lox hoek ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai pui Yo-tax,
nhie can pui pa kaoh khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix khrawm tix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Phan koe pui pa ang hoek ka daux loe singaix,
sin ngao nawh tag sang teei kah;
tag sang teei tix tik nawh lih khaing daux pui hun.
9 Ka daux loe singaix,
pa mawh khix dim tix nga ngaix,
can pui pa ot ka daux haktiex Yo-tax mai Piien-ya-ming,
tag sang hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai khrawm tix ka daux brawk nyiex buan.
Singaix an,
lhiex kied sidaing ting,
mawh kheu lhiex praix mai pa khrawm kix paox tix kied ting ceu,
pui ku kaux hoik si yaung khawm uik heue.
10 Cao giex kaing li Yi-see-la kaoh,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex ang nghiet lox,
teei tix moeng ka bun tang ceu,
mai hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map.
11 Yam in,
pex caw tix doh keud ka phi map tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex,
yuh kaing pa khaw ka rhawm nawh,
mai caw tix naox yuh paox grawm tau mai pui tang ceu pa ot daux haktiex ex,
lih hu khaing moeng pex pa mawh pui tang ceu heue.”
12 Pui haoh pui hun deeh pawk lox pa ting ah nin:
“Maix ah ka mawx,
ex tag hawt mawh nan.”
13 Viang mawh nan,
kix baox ah nin:
“Pui kied hun,
lhiex kied ting,
yix khaw tix ang lai meung cung plak praix.
In ang mawh kaing pa pon yuh ex tix ngaix ra ngaix khaing,
mawh kheu koe pui tom nie hoik yuh ka phi map in.
14 Kieh simiang yix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
son sang yuh ka kaing in son pui hun.
Nhie pui pa hoik teei tix moeng ka bun tang ceu khankix,
ciak tix deuh ka bawg yam sang hoek tau mai simiang veng nawh,
mai simiang cao prawng khankix.
Yuh nin,
krax pa song rhawm Siyiex ka kaing in tom grai heue.”
15 Pui haoh pui hun tom cu tix hawt grawng in khawm uik,
mawh ngie Yaw-na-tang pa mawh kawn simex Ya-sha-he mai Ya-ha-shiex kawn simex Theu-wa pa ang khaw rhawm kah;
mawh Mi-su-lang mai Sa-pi-thaix pui Li-we joh jhan ka kix heue.
16 Pui pa vuk vied vuk viang hoik rieb tix hawt ka grawng in khawm uik,
mawh kheu nan Yi-see-la cao giex kaing li tom ciak pui tix blah khaing daux cao kaing pui hun,
mai tiiem ka ca rang kix.
Haok khix kao tix ngaix,
pui in kix jauh tix si kaw,
17 hoek ka haok khix tix tix ngaix,
mai kix hoik pon si kaw tix yaox ka ceu kaux can pui pa hoik teei tix moeng bun tang ceu khankix heue.
Pui pa teei tix moeng bun pui tang ceu

19 Kix cu tix tang hu khaing moeng tix,
mai tan lu ka yung mhaing tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
son sang tang ka phi map kix.
