Pui Yi-seu-liex pa ang nghiet lox
3
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Phan koe simex tix kaux pa hoik tik ka moeng tix,
moeng nawh hu khaing nawh,
mai mex pui tang kaux,
nawh sang deeh chix rieb moeng tix hoek yuh ka mawx lie?
Phan nawh yuh nin tag mawh pa seeing brai yuh haktiex heue.
Viang mawh nan,
Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix hoik viex paox muh tix to,
yam in maix lai ce tix sang deeh hoek sawg ka aux heue!
2 Yaog ngai maix jhak ka gawng khankix;
sang pon yaox maix ka haktiex dee ang maix bax yuh krax phit krax lud pa seeing pa brai lie?
Maix hoik krox paox muh tix ka praok krax,
awm pui A-la-pawx pa kuan tix jawm ka daux tiag,
mai krox jun ka to tiag yuh nan.
Krax pa phit pa lud maix hoik seeing brai yuh haktiex.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
lhiex ang lih,
rawm lhiex cao ang koe.
Ka pak ngai maix hoik naog ka krax phit krax lud pa seeing pa brai,
maix lai ang tawng tix koe krax kaig krax tua yuh nan heue!
4 “Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
yam in maix lai ah nin ka aux:
‘Maix mawh keeing ka aux,
jauh tom yam iak aux maix hoik kied muh ka aux.
5 Maix ang chix song rhawm ka aux tom si dauh,
song rhawm tom rheeng.’
In mawh lox pa ah maix,
viang mawh nan,
maix ca kawn thiang lox,
mai sau riang tix yuh pa caox pa caig heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax tag caw tix loh rhawm tix
6 aYam mawh Yaw-si-yax simiang Yo-tax,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Mawh hoik yaox maix ka kaing pa seeing brai pa yuh bun Yi-seu-liex lie?
Nawh hoik tik vud ka aux,
hoik yuh krax phit krax lud ka piang gawng ku mu,
mai ka greem khaox klai ku gawng heue.
7 Aux ce nawh sang chix deeh hoek ka aux dee in,
khaix hoik yuh tix kaing in khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang hoek heue;
pux o pa seeing brai nawh Yo-tax kawx hoik yaox kaing in kix khawm uik.
8 Yo-tax hoik yaox grawng pa tik pa vud aux ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa tieh vheen pa seeing brai khankix,
mai tawx lai son pa tik ka nawh;
viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-tax pa seeing brai ang tie lhat,
mai tieh tix yuh kaing ceu pa ang khaw,
mai lih tix mawh bun pa coh kaux,
9 ang tawng tix kaig.
Nawh kawx hoik seeing brai yuh haktiex;
nawh krub ka simaox rang,
khaox ox,
yuh pa seeing brai ka aux.
10 Khaix hoik yuh ka ceu in khawm uik,
pui Yo-tax pux o pui Yi-seu-liex pa seeing brai khankix yuh tix deeh hoek,
viang mawh nan,
ang tie mawh pa kaoh khaing rhawm rhi tix tete.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
11 Simiang Siyiex baox krai ka aux,
pui Yi-seu-liex viang hoik tik vud ka aux kawx,
viang mawh nan kawn lheu mhawm khaing pui Yo-tax pa seeing brai khankix.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tawx lox nhie aux hu ka haktiex plak laong,
mai krai pao ka pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex pa ang nghiet lox eue,
deeh hoek ka aux dee in heue!
Krax muh aux ka maix ang tieh tix;
aux ang song rhawm ka maix leeing ju.
13 Aux simie ngie maix doh keud ka phi map tix khaing,
doh keud ka hoik phit tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix,
hoik sax yuh krax phit vao tau mai siyiex meung haktiex tang kaux tang hak ka greem khaox klai ku gawng,
lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx aux.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
14 “Ceu pui pa ang nghiet lox eue,
deeh hoek heue!
Aux mawh simiang pex.
Aux tag sang laug pui tix kaux lih khaing daux veng ot pex ku mu,
khaing daux tix ceu pui ra kaux,
mai viex kix deeh hu ka gawng Shi-ang.
15 Aux tag sang tawx simiang pa khaw rhawm tix son pex,
mai kix tag sang teei krax tawng krax yung tix mai ping nyia cuyi tix giex kaing ka piang pex.
16 Yam hoik deeh pon pex krax pa haok huan ting lhaong ka daux haktiex in,
kawn pui tag ang lai si muad ka teug sijax mukheu aux,
ang lai baox deeh krai lao,
mai ang lai baox dee doh keud kah.
Kix ang lai baox deeh lo ka teug sijax mukheu,
mai ang lai baox yuh ka tang mu.
17 Hoek bawg yam an,
kix tag sang kok Yie-lu-sa-leung mawh ‘dang ngeung Simiang Siyiex’,
kawn pui piang haktiex khawm bee ma tag sang khrawm tix krub ka aux dee an.
Kix ang lai baox deeh kiien rhawm,
mai ang lai baox deeh viex rhawm pa caox pa caig.
18 Yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang rom tix tau mai pui Yo-tax;
kix tag sang lih hu tau khaing haktiex plak laong dee vuk vied vuk viang tix an,
mai deeh hoek ka haktiex pa hoik tawx aux mawh sin ngao tax du pex leeing ju heue.”
Pui cie Siyiex krub ka riang rao
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
19 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux kied sidaing gaux rhawm tix kieh maix mawh kawn simex tix,
teei ka haktiex pa blawn mhawm pa koe ka piang haktiex,
pa mawh
pa blawn mhawm an tawx ka maix.
Aux ce maix sang kok aux mawh keeing,
ang lai tieh vheen ka aux leeing ju.
20 Viang mawh nan,
maix ang nghiet ka lox aux,
awm moeng pa seeing brai pa briex tix to yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
21 Nghiet heue,
ka piang gawng koe lox pa kok yo kah,
pa mawh lox yiiem grai roik rawm ngai pui Yi-seu-liex;
mawh kheu kix hoik singai hu khaing krax pa rauh pa see,
hoik piawm ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix.
22 Pui pa hoik tik vud Simiang Siyiex eue,
deeh hoek heue!
Nawh tag sang pai pex moi,
kieh pex ang lai baox deeh tieh vheen heue.
 
Pex deeh pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh heue!
Yix tag sang deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex dee an,
kheu hoik mawh nawh Siyiex yix.
23 Yix krub riang rao ka piang gawng,
mai ang tie pon krax pa mhawm tid tix ceeix kawx.
Mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix pa pon taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex khaing.
24 Viang mawh nan,
yix krub ka Pa-lix,
pa mawh riang rao pa kaig pa tua an,
kheu nan yix tom grai yuh phung yung,
phung moi,
kawn simex kawn bun,
pa mawh sin ngao pa pon tax du yix tom dix dix khawm uik.
25 Yix khaw tix teei krax kaig krax tua mawh ku it,
kieh krax kaig ngai mawh prux it.
Yix mai tax du yix hoik phi lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix jheeng jheeng;
yix ang sax nghiet ka lox tawx nawh jheeng jheeng heue.”
