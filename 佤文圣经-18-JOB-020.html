20
Khaix an,
pui Na-ma pa kaux Saw-fax krai lox ah nin:
2 Yaw-paw eue,
maix hoik kieh rhawm rhi aux suk sag,
aux ang lai pon kham son sang pawk lox maix.
3 Lox pa krai maix hoik mawh lox pa nu nai,
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng grawng sang pawk ka heue.
 
4 Maix hoik tawng,
jauh tom dix dix,
yam jauh kawn pui tix ot ka piang haktiex,
5 Pui caox pui caig gaux rhawm mawh tix vud tix cu khaing,
pui pa ang rauh ang see,
krax gaux rhawm kix kawx ang rheeng.
6 Viang nawh ting rhawm lhaong rhi pon tiag tix sidu mai rao ma,
kaing nawh cux ka pai awm,
7 Dee si dauh nawh tag sang awm aing pa pih pui tix tik yuh nan;
pui pa hoik tawng ka nawh ang lai tawng nawh hu ka dee mawx.
8 Nawh awm ca maox pa grai hu,
leeing ju leeing phan ang lai baox deeh yaox;
nawh awm pa faik yam pon sawm,
mai grai hu ang lai yaox khaix.
9 Pui pa hoik yaox ka nawh sivoe an ang lai baox deeh yaox nawh;
nawh tag sang grai khaing haktiex dee paung ot tix.
10 Kawn simex kawn bun nawh tag sang caung tix ih ka pui hot pui sibhawm;
nawh tag sang deeh tawx ka sin ngao pa pon tix khaing daux krax pa gut kheem.
11 Nawh hoik mawh pui pa nhum ju koe riang,
viang mawh nan,
ang rheeng nawh tag sang lih mawh phawng tiex heue.
 
12 Nawh ce pa caox pa caig mawh pa tie nyawm,
mai deuh nawh ot plak greem dak tix,
13 mai kum ot ka daux jheeh tix,
nghiet ka jhawm nyawm nawh;
14 viang mawh nan,
priex preem pa ot ka daux jheeh nawh hoik niex,
tieh tix lih mawh rhawm jai si uing pa koe jai.
15 Pui caox pui caig hao lih ka sin ngao pa pon tix khaing daux krax briex lak tix;
Siyiex tag sang deeh teei,
pie pa ot daux vaig nawh kawx tag sang hoe lih.
16 Pui caox pui caig tawt ka rawm jai si uing pa koe jai;
dak si uing pa koe jai hoik lied nawh yum.
17 Nawh ang pon yaox tix im ju,
mai yaox ka rawm klawng sime pa jauh tix lih,
mai ang pon tix yaox nang ka dee jauh rawm teeh,
mai ka dee jauh rawm klawng iak rawm hia pa tie.
18 Nawh tag prah ghram ka pa pon tix khaing daux pa taug mao tix khawm uik;
nawh ang pon ih nyaux ka sin ngao tix,
19 mawh kheu nawh gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai tieb tie ka kix,
tud rhim ka nyiex ma pa sum pui tang kaux.
20 Mawh kheu krax pa ghoing rhawm nawh ka daux alo nex nham ang bax naox tix,
can ceu pa muh nawh ku ceu kawx ang lai kieh koe,
21 nawh bleet ka priex preem khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuk tix ceu kawx,
mawh kheu nan,
krax pa mhawm ot nawh ang pon yaox tix rheeng.
22 Singaix yam naog krax pa gaux rhawm nawh,
krax laux krax leeing tag sang lih hoek.
23 Kieh nawh nied tix bleet ka priex preem heue;
Siyiex tag sang song rhawm ka nawh,
awm bhaung ting pa hoek phlu ka nawh.
24 Nawh ce tix sang mox tix khaing vaig rhiem,
te tawng tag sang krox ka nawh.
25 Te cieh to kaux nawh phlat,
toh te pa sawng hoik siang tix raoh ka nham nawh;
krax pa lhat pa graung pon giex ka nawh.
26 Can pa khrawm nawh tix deuh,
hoik laux leeing khawm uik;
ang mawh ngu pa cux taix kawn pui,
pa gleeing ka nawh mai ka nyiex ma nawh.
27 Rao ma tag sang jhi lih ka phi map nawh;
haktiex tag sang mawh mhai sakse pa puing ka nawh.
28 Ka daux rawm ting krax song rhawm Siyiex,
sin ngao nawh ang lai deuh tix koe pie ceeix kawx.
29 In hoik mawh dee si dauh ju cie pui pa caox pa caig,
hoik mawh dee si dauh krax pa ciak ci Siyiex tix tawx ka nawh heue.
