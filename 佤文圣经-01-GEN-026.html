Yi-sha ot daux veng Ci-la-eux
26
Ka daux baux ju Ya-paw-la-hang,
koe sibhawm praix tix bawg;
yam in baox lih koe sibhawm praix.
Yi-sha hu deuh rhawm tix ka simiang Fe-li-see pa mawh Ya-pi-mi-leux daux Ci-la-eux.
2 Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka Yi-sha mai ah nin:
“Paw lih hu plak Ai-cix,
maix ot ka haktiex pa sang nhie aux maix ot kah.
3 aMaix ot ka daux haktiex in,
aux tag ot tau mai maix,
mai tawx buan ka maix,
aux tag tawx haktiex in ka maix mai ka doeh ceu cad maix,
aux tag sang kieh gum lox sijax mukheu pa hoik tiex tu aux ka keeing maix Ya-paw-la-hang kaux cong.
4 Aux tag kieh doeh ceu cad maix haoh hun awm simuing ka daux rao ma,
tag sang tawx kix cie haktiex in khawm uik.
Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang pon buan son khaing ka daux doeh ceu cad maix heue.
5 Mawh kheu Ya-paw-la-hang hoik cu nghiet ka lox aux,
priex tix hawt lox tat jhawm prawng mai lox pa tawx aux heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-sha ot ka daux Ci-la-eux.
7 bPui pa ot baoh haktiex an chawk ka grawng moeng nawh,
nawh tom krai Li-pai-cia mawh nan tix;
mawh kheu kied mhawm grang Li-pai-cia,
nawh tom lhat pui haktiex an pauh tix,
mai rhim tix teei Li-pai-cia hu.
8 Khaix hoik rheung ot kix tan.
Koe tix bawg,
simiang Ya-pi-mi-leux meung Fe-li-see,
jhak plak praix khaing daux doh siviex nyiex mai nawh yaox Yi-sha klieh tau mai moeng tix Li-pai-cia.
9 Ya-pi-mi-leux kok ka Yi-sha mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Bun an hoik sidawng mawh moeng maix;
yuh ka mawx maix lai ah mawh nan tix?”
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan aux ang ah yuh nin,
aux lhat tix yum kheu jao nawh heue.”
10 Ya-pi-mi-leux ah nin:
“Maix lai yuh kaing yuh nin ka yix yuh ka mawx?
Phan pui tix kaux kaux cie aux ot tau mai moeng maix,
maix tag caw tix klawng krax phi map tang yix.”
11 Ya-pi-mi-leux tawx lox hu ka pui ah nin:
“Pui mawx ang ah phan tuk tiak ka pui in mai moeng nawh,
tag sang pauh nawh yum heue.”
12 Yi-sha rhuat simie ka haktiex an,
mai nawh yaox tix vawg plix nghox daux num an tix yieh son,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka nawh.
13 Sin ngao nawh haok huan ting lhaong si nyek si nyek,
mai lih tix mawh pui pa kied ting min tix kaux.
14 Pui Fe-li-see yaox koe phung yung,
phung moi,
mai siliex cawng tom nie,
tom boh rhawm ka nawh,
15 thom rawm maw cie nawh ka tiex khawm uik,
rawm maw in kix hoik mawh pa kaong siliex keeing nawh daux baux ju Ya-paw-la-hang.
16 Khaix an,
Ya-pi-mi-leux ah nin ka Yi-sha:
“Maix lih hu khaing yix liak,
mawh kheu maix hoik nie ting siawp sibrawm khaing yix.”
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-sha lih hu khaing tan,
mai sum nyiex gox man ka daux lung baoh Ci-la-eux,
mai ot tan.
18 Yam im ju Ya-paw-la-hang,
nawh kaong ka rawm maw khankix,
khaix hoik yum nawh,
pui Fe-li-see hoik thom rawm maw nawh ka tiex khawm uik heue.
Yi-sha baox kaong rawm maw dee hoik kaong siliex keeing nawh Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai ca kawn hawt ka ceu kaux pa sum keeing nawh heue.
19 Siliex Yi-sha khankix kawx baox deeh kaong rawm maw daux ngawd,
kix yaox ka dee keet rawm heue.
20 Cao liang khawng brie Ci-la-eux mai cao liang khawng brie Yi-sha thung thiang ka paox tix,
mai ah nin:
“Rawm maw in mawh cie yix.”
Mawh kheu thung thiang kix mai paox tix tan,
nawh tom kok ceu kaux rawm maw an mawh Ai-seuxc heue.
21 Siliex Yi-sha baox kaong rawm maw pa khraox,
kix baox simah ka son rawm maw an,
nawh tom kok ceu kaux rawm maw an mawh Si-thi-na.d
22 Nawh deeh gah tix khaing tan mai baox kaong rawm maw tang dauh.
Kix ang lai simah ka rawm maw an,
mawh kheu nan nawh tom kok ceu kaux rawm maw an mawh Li-heu-paw.e
Nawh ah nin:
“Yam in Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx haktiex dee vieh va son ex,
kieh ex tang haok huan ka daux haktiex in heue.”
23 Yi-sha lih hu khaing tan,
mai hu ot ka Piie-see-pa.
24 Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka nawh bo an,
ah nin:
“Aux mawh Siyiex keeing maix Ya-paw-la-hang;
paw koe krax lhat krax graung,
aux ot tau mai maix.
Son lox pa ap aux ka siliex aux Ya-paw-la-hang,
aux tag tawx buan ka maix mai doeh ceu pui maix.”
25 Yi-sha sum khu dee tan tix mu ka dee an,
mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an nawh sum nyiex gox man ot tan.
Siliex Yi-sha deeh kaong rawm maw dee an tix mu heue.
Yi-sha yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Ya-pi-mi-leux
26 fYa-pi-mi-leux viex paox teem tix Ya-hu-sha mai simiang naing tix Fe-keux hoek ka Yi-sha ka haktiex Ci-la-eux.
27 Yi-sha ah nin ka kix:
“Mawh kheu pa tix pex hoek ka aux?
Sivoe an,
ang pex hoik koeng ka aux,
mai grheu aux hu khaing tix ang mawx lie?”
28 Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Yam in yix hoik tawng ot Simiang Siyiex tau mai maix.
Yix keud,
kieh ex yuh krax pa tiex tu si nax paox tix,
mai yuh sijax mukheu;
yix kieh maix ap tix deuh lox,
29 maix paw lai tuk tiak ka yix,
awm ang yix lai tuk tiak ka maix.
Singaix pa hoik luan,
yix hoik chix kia ngien ka maix,
kieh maix lih mhawm mhawm;
yam in yix hoik tawng Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka maix heue.”
30 Yi-sha rien khre ka priex preem,
kix tom ih nyaux tau tan.
31 Daux ngaix khaix,
kix kaoh plak pon ngawb cao cao,
mai ah lox pa tiex tu yuh sijax mukheu ka paox tix.
Yi-sha tom tawx kix ing,
mai guah tix khaing daux krax pa nhim heue.
32 Ka daux singaix an,
siliex Yi-sha hoek tix krai grawng pa kaong kix rawm maw ah nin:
“Yix hoik yaox dee keet rawm.”
33 Nawh kok ceu kaux rawm maw an mawh See-pa.
Mawh kheu nan,
ceu kaux veng in tom mawh Piie-see-pa,
tom in singaix heue.
Bun tang ceu moeng Yi-sao
34 Yam koe ju Yi-sao tipon num,
nawh teei tix moeng pui Heux ra kaux.
Kawn bun in ra kaux mawh kawn bun Pi-lix pa kaux Yo-ti mai kawn bun Yi-loeng pa kaux Pa-seu-mawx heue.
35 Kiex kied kieh krax ot Yi-sha mai Li-pai-cia laux rhawm heue.
