Lox tat jhawm prawng mai krax yum rhawm
3
Pui Cia-la-thaix pa daox pa aw eue,
mawh pui mawx pa baox nyaw glaw ka pex lie?
Grawng pa tawk Ye-sux Khrit ka piang khaox lhak,
ang pex yaox ka riang yuh pui pa koe sivoe ngai tix lie?
2 Pex krai ngie kaing ka aux tix ceu khaing yuh nin:
pa pon pex ka khuan Siyiex hoik mawh kheu priex pex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
hoik mawh kheu pa yum rhawm pex ka gum lox buan lie?
3 Pex lai kieh tix daox aw yuh nan lie?
Pex hoik mawh pa jauh tix rieb khaing khuan Siyiex,
yam in pex lai sang een rhawm tix ka riang rao to kaux tix mai hu yuh ka kaing in lie?
4 Kaing pa yuh pex ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
mawh pa giex pa going lie?
Ang doh mawh heue!
5 Siyiex hoik tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ka pex,
mai baox yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka daux pex,
in mawh kheu pa een pex rhawm tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
mawh kheu pa nghiet pex yum rhawm pex ka gum lox buan lie?
6 aAwm yam hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
“Nawh yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
kheu krax yum rhawm nawh,
Siyiex tom sawn nawh mawh pui rauh see heue.”
7 bMawh kheu nan,
pex hoik tawng,
pui pa koe krax yum rhawm tag mawh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang tete.
8 cPhuk lai sigang khri hoik dawg grox yaox,
Siyiex tag sang kieh pui tang ceu tang cad pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai tix ka daux pa khaw caw kheu krax yum rhawm,
mawh kheu nan hoik dawg sidiex tix tawx gum lox buan in ka Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
“Kawn pui piang haktiex tag sang pon buan son ka daux maix!”
9 Ya-paw-la-hang hoik yum rhawm,
mawh kheu nan,
hoik pon buan son;
yuh nin,
can pui pa yum rhawm khawm uik kawx tag sang rom tix pon buan son tau mai nawh heue.
10 dPui pa een rhawm tix ka daux pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
tag im ka greem lox jhax lawg khawm uik.
Mawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Can pui pa ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng ku grawng,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex khawm uik.”
11 fPhan ex jhak tix yaox,
ang koe pui pa pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
kheu pa een rhawm tix ka pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
Mawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pui pa hoik pon si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux krax pa khaw caw kheu krax yum rhawm,
tag sang pon ju heue.”e
12 gViang mawh nan,
lox tat jhawm prawng ang mawh pa een rhawm tix ka krax yum rhawm,
mai awm krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pui pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
tag sang im kheu lox tat jhawm prawng heue.”
13 hKhrit hoik klawm krax pa jhax lawg son ex,
hoik taoh ex bheeik luan khaing krax pa jhax lawg lox tat jhawm prawng kheu in;
mawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Can pui pa vhak ot ka piang khaox lhak,
tag pon krax pa jhax lawg Siyiex khawm uik heue.”
14 Kaing ting grawng yuh Khrit nin,
hoik mawh son sang kieh pui tang ceu pon buan son gum lox pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka Ya-paw-la-hang kheu Ye-sux Khrit;
yuh nin,
ex hoik pon rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex kheu krax yum rhawm heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox pa ap
15 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
kieh aux tiag tix yaox ka grawng kaing tix ceu khaing krax ot krax koe yuh nin:
phan koe pui ra kaux pa khaw rhawm paox tix yuh kaing tix ceu ceu,
khaix an hoik tiiem ka lai pa yuh sijax mukheu,
mai ang lai koe pui pa pon tieh.
16 iAwm an,
Siyiex hoik ap tix tawx lox ka Ya-paw-la-hang mai ka kawn praix nawh.
Phuk lai sigang khri ang bax siao “krai mawh kawn praix khankix”,
ka pui hun khankix,
mai hoik siao “krai mawh kawn praix maix”,
pa krai mawh tix kaux,
hoik mawh Khrit.
17 jDee bleeh lox krai aux mawh nin:
Siyiex hoik yuh sijax mukheu,
mai hoik cu tix giex kaux cong ka sijax mukheu in.
Lox tat jhawm prawng pa taug lih koe khaix an pon yieh tix ngoe num,
ang pon yieh ka sijax mukheu laux,
mai kieh grai ka gum lox pa hoik tawx Siyiex.
18 kPhan Siyiex tawx sin ngao mawh ka daux pa hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
an tag ang mawh pa een rhawm tix ka gum lox pa tawx Siyiex;
kheu nan,
Siyiex tawx sin ngao ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
hoik mawh pa een rhawm tix ka gum lox pa tawx Siyiex heue.
19 Phan mawh nan,
lox tat jhawm prawng kaing ting mawh ceu tix lie?
Lox tat jhawm prawng mawh pa yuh lih son ceu tix mawh krax pa phit lud,
tom hoek lih bleeh gum lox pa ap ka kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang,
tom taug si dauh.
Lox tat jhawm prawng mawh pa tawx kawn cau Siyiex ka daux pui ot si nax tix kaux krai hu.
20 Viang mawh nan,
phan mawh ngie pui pa ot tix plak khaing,
tag ang lai siao lo pui pa ot si nax,
Siyiex hoik mawh pa ot tix plak heue.l
Kaing ting lox tat jhawm prawng
21 Phan ex krai yuh nin,
lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex mai gum lox pa tawx nawh ang khaw caw ka paox lie?
Ang doh mawh nan heue!
Phan lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx hu pon tawx ju ka kawn pui,
kawn pui chix tix pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
kheu een rhawm tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
22 Kheu nan,
phuk lai sigang khri ah nin,
haktiex khawm uik hoik ot ka greem siawp sibrawm phi map,
mawh son sang lih mawh riah rawng pa rieb ka gum lox pa ap Siyiex tix tawx ka daux krax pa yum rhawm Ye-sux Khrit;
gum lox in mawh pa ap ka can pui pa yum rhawm khawm uik heue.
23 Viang mawh nan,
sivoe sang lih hoek singaix bawg yam “krax yum rhawm”,
lox tat jhawm prawng pau ka ex,
awm pau ka pui pa laung daux thawng yuh nan,
tom hoek jhi “krax yum rhawm” tix lih.
24 Yuh nin,
lox tat jhawm prawng tom lih mawh pui pa kia ngien ka ex,
mai thung ex hoek ka Khrit,m
kaing ting hoik mawh son sang kieh ex yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw kheu “krax yum rhawm”.
25 Kheu yam in hoik mawh ka daux singaix bawg yam “krax yum rhawm”,
lox tat jhawm prawng tag ang lai baox kia ngien ka ex heue.
26 Pex pui ku kaux hoik lih mawh tix mu tau mai Khrit ka daux “krax yum rhawm”,
mai lih mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex.
27 Pex hoik cub com tix lih mawh tix mu tau mai Khrit,
awm pa hoik ceep ka Khrit,
mai hoik koe ka ju nawh yuh nan.
28 Ang lai guah pui Yo-thaix,
phan ang mawh nan,
pui tang ceu tang cad,
siliex cawng,
phan ang mawh nan,
pui bheeik luan,
simex,
bun,
ka daux pa im ju Ye-sux Khrit,
pex hoik lih mawh to kaux tix mu khaing.
29 nPhan pex mawh pa dex dawt tau mai Khrit,
pex hoik mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-paw-la-hang,
tag sang pon gum lox pa ap Siyiex ka Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
