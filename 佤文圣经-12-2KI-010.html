Pauh doeh ceu cad simiang Ya-ha yum
10
Simiang Ya-ha koe kawn praix atigliah kaux ot ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
Yie-hu tiiem tix tawx lai ka kuad song,
simiang,
mai ka cao khre kawn praix Ya-ha pa ot ka daux venga khankix.
Lox lai an ah nin:
2 “Pex mawh pui pa khre ka doeh ceu cad simiang;
pex hoik tang koe ling law cie tix,
brung tix,
kraung naing tix,
mai veng pa yuh kaux.
Mawh kheu nin,
khaix hoik rieb pex ka lox lai in,
3 mai tag caw tix laug pui pa kied chix tix kaux khaing daux doeh ceu cad simiang,
ap nawh mawh simiang,
puing naing son nawh,
mai kia ngien nawh mhawm heue.”
4 Simiang pui Sa-ma-li-yax yaoh yee;
kix ah nin:
“Pie simiang Yaw-heu-lang mai simiang Ya-ha-shiex ang pon tieb tie ka Yie-hu,
ex sang chix pon tieb tie nawh yuh ka mawx lie?”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau pa ting pa kuang ka piang nyiex haw simiang mai simiang veng,
khawm kuad song mai cao pa khre khankix,
hu tawx lai in tau ka Yie-hu,
ah nin:
“Yix mawh kawn cau maix,
kaing pa nhie maix yi yuh,
yix hoik gaux rhawm tix yuh khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
yix ang lai yaog pui tang kaux mawh simiang.
Maix hawt tix yuh ka ceu pa tawng maix khaw ka rhawm tix heue.”
6 Yie-hu baox tawx lai ka kix tix pheen,
ah nin:
“Phan pex hawt rhawm aux,
mai cu nghiet ka lox tawx aux,
pa sax bawg yam in,
viex tawx kaing kawn praix simiang Ya-ha ka aux ka Yie-see-liex heue.”
Kawn praix Ya-ha atigliah kaux khankix mawh pui Sa-ma-li-yax pa koe min pa eei kix.
7 Yam hoik hoek lox lai tawx Yie-hu,
pui pa koe min in kix pauh ka kawn praix Ya-ha atigliah kaux yum khawm uik,
khaix an mai sau kaing kix ka daux khrawng,
tawx ka Yie-hu ka Yie-see-liex heue.
8 Koe kawn cau hoek krai Yie-hu mhong,
krai hoik viex tix kaing kawn praix Ya-ha hoek.
Nawh tom tawx lox nhie pui pum kaing pui in kix ot ka diang siviex veng,
pum kieh koe ra pum,
mai deuh ot dee an tom hoek ngaix khaix.
9 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Yie-hu hoek ka diang siviex veng,
mai ah nin ka pui hun pa ot dee an:
“Mawh aux pa tieh vheen ka simiang Yaw-heu-lang,
mai pauh ka nawh;
pex ang koe beeh glawm kah.
Viang mawh nan,
pui in kix mawh mawx pa pauh kix yum lie?
10 In hoik kham sakse son gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ku gum pa lih tix mawh ka piang doeh ceu cad Ya-ha.
Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh lox jhai pa krai tix ka cao jhai Yi-li-yax lih heue.”
11 bMawh kheu nan,
Yie-hu pauh ka pui nyiex ma Ya-ha pa ot haktiex Yie-see-liex khankix khawm uik,
khawm simiang naing nawh,
paox grawm pa ting meed nawh,
mai cao giex kaing li nawh khankix yum khawm uik,
tix kaux kawx ang lai deuh heue.
Pauh pui nyiex ma Ya-ha-shiex yum
12 Yie-hu lih khaing Yie-see-liex hu plak Sa-ma-li-yax.
Ka daux krax,
koe dee kok mawh “bang cao liang” tix dauh,
13 nawh si pup ka pui nyiex ma simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex ra loe kaux,
tom chawk kix ah nin:
“Mawh pex pui mawx?”
Kix ah nin:
“Yix mawh pui nyiex Ya-ha-shiex,
sang hu ka Yie-see-liex,
son sang chawk tix yaox ka kawn simex Yie-si-pix moeng simiang khankix,
mai pui nyiex ma simiang tang kaux khankix,
mhawm ang kix mhawm ot.”
14 Yie-hu tawx lox ka pui pa ot greem tix ah nin:
“Giex kix heue!”
Pui pa ot greem nawh tom giex ka kix,
Yie-hu pauh kix yum ka praok bang dex rawm dung,
khawm uik koe pui tipon ra kaux,
mai ang deuh tix kaux kawx heue.
Pauh pui nyiex ma Ya-ha pa deuk khankix yum
15 Yie-hu baox hu sivoe,
ka daux krax si pup ka kawn simex Li-cia pa kaux Yaw-na-ta pa hoek rieb ka nawh,
tom chawk nawh mhawm ot ah nin:
“Maix yuh pa mhawm ka aux awm hoik yuh aux pa mhawm ka maix lie?”
Yaw-na-ta ah nin:
“Hoik yuh awm paox.”
Yie-hu pawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
tawx taix maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-hu tom bluh taix nawh haok piang ling law,
16 ah nin:
“Hawt khaix aux,
hoek jhak maix yaox grawng gaux rhawm aux tix yuh kaing son Simiang Siyiex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom brug ling law hu tau ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
17 Yam hoek ka dee an,
Yie-hu hoik pauh yum khawm uik ka pui nyiex ma tang kaux Ya-ha,
mai ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
In khawm uik hoik hawt tix lih mawh awm gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka Yi-li-yax heue.
Pauh pui pa krub ka Pa-lix yum
18 Yie-hu khrawm ka pui ot haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax khawm uik,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Krax pa krub simiang Ya-ha ka Siyiex Pa-lix,
kawn ang nyang bax kub,
aux sang nie baox krub nawh tom ting.
19 Pex hu khrawm ka can cao jhai Pa-lix mai pui pa krub ka Pa-lix khawm uik,
mai cao giex kaing li hoek,
paw kieh cud tix kaux kawx.
Aux sang tan lu krax tan pa ting ka Pa-lix,
pui mawx ang ot dee an,
tag sang kieh nawh yum.”
In mawh krax pa sang tieh vheen Yie-hu;
nawh sang pauh ka can pui pa krub ka Pa-lix yum khawm uik.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-hu tawx lox ah nin:
“Sang krai pao tix ngaix kieh mawh son krub ka Pa-lix nong nong!”
Lox in hoik krai tix hu,
21 Yie-hu tom ploi pui hu tawx lai ka pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh.
Can pui pa krub ka Pa-lix hoik hoek khawm uik,
mai ang lai cud tix kaux kawx.
Kix laig daux nyiex vad Pa-lix,
plak sivoe mai plak khaix hoik sied pui kah.
22 Yie-hu tawx lox ka cao giex kaing li pa kuang ka kraung dha pa singax singeh,
mai nhie nawh tawx pui pa krub ka Pa-lix ceep kraung an,
nawh hawt tix yuh.
23 Khaix an,
Yie-hu mai kawn simex Li-cia pa kaux Yaw-na-ta kawx laig daux nyiex vad,
mai ah nin ka pui hun pa ot dee an:
“Pex caw kieh tix tawng sidawng,
caw tix kieh ngie pui pa krub ka Pa-lix ot dee in;
pui pa krub ka Simiang Siyiex ang pon tix kieh laig.”
24 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh mai Yaw-na-ta laig tix yuh krax tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka Pa-lix.
Yie-hu hoik ploi pui tix daix kaux pau ka plak praix nyiex vad,
mai tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix pauh ka pui in kix yum khawm uik;
phan kieh kix to pie tix kaux,
tag sang teei ju to kaux pex plud ka heue!”
25 Yam hoik uik tan lu Yie-hu ka krax tan,
nawh ah nin ka cao pa pau diang siviex,
mai ka simiang naing:
“Laig daux nyiex,
pauh kix yum khawm uik heue!
Paw kieh pon to tix kaux kawx heue!”
Kix tom laig daux nyiex,
teei vaig mug kix yum khawm uik,
mai reu to kaux yum kix lih.
Khaix an,
kix hoek ka daux nyiex vad,
26 mai gah li gleeing ka riang rong Siyiex ot dee an.
27 Yuh nin,
kix yieh ka riang rong Siyiex mai ka nyiex vad,
mai kieh nyiex vad mawh nyiex aing pui tix tom hoek yam in singaix heue.
28 Yuh nin,
Yie-hu hoik pon teei tix tik ka kaing pa krub ka Pa-lix khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
29 cViang mawh nan,
nawh yuh ka phi map pa hoik yuh Yie-law-paw-ang;
Yie-law-paw-ang yuh ka riang kawn moi khri ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix mai ka haktiex Tang,
mai thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh ka phi map pa krub ka kawn moi khri.
30 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yie-hu:
“Maix hoik yuh ka kawn praix Ya-ha awm kaing pa khaw ka rhawm aux,
mawh kheu nan aux tag ap ka maix:
doeh ceu pui maix tag sang plud tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khaix maix,
tom hoek ka daux pon ju heue.”
31 Viang mawh nan,
Yie-hu ang bax viex rhawm tix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
Deeh tieh tix,
nawh tieh tix deeh hawt ka kaing yuh Yie-law-paw-ang,
mai thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map heue.
Yie-hu yum
32 Bawg yam an,
Simiang Siyiex jauh tix kieh haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex iak.
Simiang shui-li-yax pa kaux Ha-shiiex hoik pon kuang ka haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex
33 pa koe ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix khawm uik,
plak seh hoek tom veng Ya-law-eu ka praok rawm klawng Ya-noeng,
khawm haktiex Ci-liex mai haktiex Pa-sang,
pa mawh dee ot ceu pui Cia-teux,
ceu pui Lui-piien,
ceu pui Ma-na-si pa ot plak lih singaix heue.
34 Kaing yuh Yie-hu tang ceu khankix,
khawm grawng pa kied kua nawh hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux lai been Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
35 Yam yum nawh,
kix si paung nawh ot haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yaw-ha-see plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh.
36 Yie-hu ot ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax,
mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tix nga daix num heue.
