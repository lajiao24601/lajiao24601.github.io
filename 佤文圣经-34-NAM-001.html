Lai Na-hong
1
aPlak greem mawh grawng pa dex mai pui Ni-ni-we,
mawh riang su sai pa yaox pui Yi-leu-keu-see pa kaux Na-hong heue.
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Ni-ni-we
2 Simiang Siyiex mawh pa ang gun yeung ka Siyiex pa tieb tie ka nawh;
Simiang Siyiex yuh krax pa pawk bix,
mai naog krax pa song rhawm song rhi.
Nawh ciyang ka pui pa tieb tie ka nawh khankix;
nawh song rhawm ka pui pa koeng.
3 Simiang Siyiex ang song rhawm yie yie,
viang mawh nan,
ting riang ting rao;
nawh ang doh kieh pui pa koe phi map lih mawh pa ang koe phi map heue.
 
Khaix krax hu Simiang Siyiex,
bhaung ting lih phlu kah;
pai awm mawh phawng tiex pa chu lih hu khaing caong nawh heue!
4 Nawh ne raw ka rawm sigrit sigra,
rawm sigrit sigra tom lih tix kroh;
nawh kieh klawng kroh.
Nawh kieh haktiex Pa-sang lih tix iak,
mai kieh gawng Cia-mix lih tix hax,
kieh lawm tai Li-pa-noeng khrum.
5 Gawng lua khankix si yaung si kua ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex;
gawng iak pa koe dee lung khankix cieh ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
Simiang Siyiex lih hoek,
haktiex yung yang;
haktiex mai pui pa ot daux hoik si yaung si kua khawm uik.
6 Yam song rhawm nawh,
pui mawx kawn sang pon klawd ju lie?
Pui mawx kawn sang pon kham krax pa song rhawm nawh lie?
Krax pa song rhawm nawh awm ngu pa klup tix hieh yuh nan;
rang simaox laux leeing ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.
 
7 Simiang Siyiex pa kied mhawm kied mhiem.
Nawh kia ngien ka pui cie tix pa ot daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi;
nawh kia ngien ka pui pa deuh rhawm tix ka nawh.
8 Nawh awm rawm ting tix nyaing,
mai yuh pui pa koeng ka nawhb laux lak pak leeing;
Nawh grheu pui pa tieb tie ka nawh laig daux krax yum.
9 Yuh ka mawx pex lai keud sawn tix tieb tie ka Simiang Siyiex lie?
Nawh tag sang yuh pex laux leeing khawm uik;
mai ang lai baox koe pui pa tieb tie ka nawh.
10 Awm pang kat pa vung vhien tix mai nyhee glauh nghox ca pa kroh yuh nan,
ngu tag sang gleeing pex laux lak pak leeing heue!
 
11 Pui Ni-ni-we eue,
ka dee ot maix koe krax keud pa pie lhien lih hoek ka tix kaux,
mai keud tix tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex.
12 Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Pui Ya-sux viang ting riang haoh hun,
kix tag sang laux leeing,
mai grai grup.
Pui cie aux eue,
aux hoik sax kieh pex kha rhawm tix,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang lai baox kieh pex kha rhawm tix.
13 Yam in aux sang kieh pa giex gang pui Ya-sux ka pex naox tix,
mai rud dut ka maox ceng pa phrawk ka pex heue.”
14 Plak greem mawh grawng pa dex dawt mai pui Ya-sux pa krai pao ah nin:
“Kix ang lai chix deuh koe ka doeh ru ceu cad tix.
Aux tag sang yieh tix tik ka riang rao pa koe daux nyiex vad kix.
Aux tag sang rien khre ka si meeig son pui Ya-sux,
kix ang keung tix im ju heue!”
15 cJhak heue,
koe pui pa krai pao ka lox pa mhawm tiang gawng tiang lua hoek heue!
Nawh hoek krai pao ka gum lox pa nhim pa tiiem.
Pui Yo-tax eue,
gaux rhawm ka poi pa yuh pex,
plud ka lox pa hoik ap pong pex tix tawx ka Siyiex heue!
Pui caox ang lai baox pon hoek tix tud rhim ka haktiex pex.
Kix hoik laux leeing khawm uik heue.
