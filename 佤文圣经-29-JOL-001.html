Lai Yaw-eux
Kawn pui yiiem son laux leeing to ma
1
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kawn simex Pi-thu-eux pa kaux Yaw-eux.
2 Pui pa riex ju eue,
nghiet heue!
Pui Yo-tax eue,
tag bre tix nghiet heue!
Ka daux singaix baux bawg yam pex mai ka daux baux bawg yam tax tu pex hoik lih kaing ceu pa yuh nin lie?
3 Krai ka kawn praix pex,
nhie kix baox deeh krai ka kawn praix tix,
tix ju hoek tix ju,
ang chix uik heue.
 
4 Phung sido kaog ka piang nghox koi.
Pa deuk khaix ih sido kip kah,
sido nghox hoek tix ih;
pa deuk khaix ih sido nghox kah,
sido hauk mi hoek tix ih,
pa deuk khaix ih sido hauk mi kah,
sido ngai liag hoek tix ih.
5 Pui pa yoek ka plai eue,
haing heue,
yiiem heue!
Pui pa brax nyaux ka plai eue,
yiiem doe heue!
Mawh kheu hoik laux plix siiet pa greeik ka plai pa khraox heue.
 
6 aKoe phung sido tix cub hoek tix yuh haktiex ex laux.
Kix kied ting riang ting rao,
mai ang koe pui pa pon uik simin;
rhang kix hoik lawm awm rhang sivai sang si yuh nan.
7 Kix hoik laux leeing yuh nhu plix siiet ex,
hoik ih ka khaox ruh ex pak lawg,
kix hoik paih ka lhox khaox khawm uik,
kieh kak lih tix paing heue.
 
8 Yiiem heue,
pex pui in kix,
awm yiiem doe bun krih kheu hoik yum mex tix pa ang nyang bax yuh ka paox.
9 Ka daux nyiex buan ang lai baox koe nghox koi mai plai nyawm,
cao giex kaing li hoik yiiem doe kheu ang lai koe ceu pa sang tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
10 Kaung ma hoik riem,
haktiex hoik laux rhawm;
mawh kheu hoik laux leeing nghox koi,
hoik kroh nhu plix siiet,
hoik kroh vhit khaox sime heue.
 
11 Pui pa yuh kaing kaung ma eue,
laux rawm heue!
Pui pa kia ngien ka si peem plix siiet eue,
yiiem doe heue!
Mawh kheu nghox ca pa lang mu,
nghox ca pa iak mu,
can nghox koi pa koe khawm uik hoik laux heue.
12 Nhu plix siiet hoik kroh,
khaox ruh hoik vhit;
khaox plix mak cang,
khaox jhie yaong,
khaox plix kawk tie
pa mawh can khaox plix hoik yum kroh khawm uik;
krax gaux rhawm pui kawx hoik grai hu heue.
 
13 Cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub ka khu dee tan khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix ceep kraung man keei sum yiiem heue!
Tag caw tix laig daux nyiex buan yiiem grai leeing sawm heue!
Mawh kheu ang lai koe nghox koi,
plai nyawm pa sang tan lu ka Siyiex pex.
14 Tag caw tix krai pao tuah ka priex preem,
yuh poi krax pa khrawm ka paox tix.
Tag caw tix kok ka kuad song mai ka can pui pa ot daux haktiex Yo-tax khawm uik,
laig ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex pex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
khiox khaw kok yo ka nawh heue.
15 bBawg yam singaix cie Simiang Siyiex hoik dex sang hoek;
cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik hoik sang ploi krax laux krax leeing lih.
Ka daux singaix an mawh pa kied lhat graung heue!
 
16 Nghox koi yix hoik laux leeing;
nyiex buan Siyiex yix ang lai koe krax gaux rhawm.
17 Simie hoik si um ka daux tiex khawm uik,
ang lai koe nghox koi pa sang deuh tix priex;
krao nghox koi pa yi lih tix mawh dee riem khawm uik.
18 Khawng brie yiiem doe.
Phung moi hoik vu vie tix hu ku dauh ku dee,
mawh kheu ang lai yaox dee sang sawm tix rib;
phung yung kawx hoik cai rhawm nang.
19 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik bleet ngu pak rib pa koe ka daux tiag daux tu,
rao ngu hoik proe ka khaox pa koe ka daux tiag khawm uik.
20 To sat kawx hoik yiiem doe ka maix;
mawh kheu hoik kroh klawng iak,
ngu hoik proe pak rib laux leeing khawm uik heue.
