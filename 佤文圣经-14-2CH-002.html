Rien khre tix sum ka nyiex buan
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 5.1–18)
2
Simiang Saw-law-meung but rhawm tix sang sum ka nyiex buan,
son sang krub Simiang Siyiex dee an.
Nawh kawx but rhawm tix sum nyiex haw simiang son to kaux tix tix lhang.
2 Nawh kieh pui aliah mheen kaux glawm ka kraung khrai,
daix mheen kaux mawh cao pa saik simaox,
loe rheng liah yieh kaux mawh cao kia ngien been yuh kaing.
3 Saw-law-meung ploi pui hu ah nin ka simiang Thai-eux pa kaux Si-lang:
“Maix hoik reu tix tawx keeing aux Ta-wue sum nyiex haw simiang ka khaox si oe bhawng,
khiox khaw maix yuh nin ka aux nang.
4 Aux jiet tag sang sum ka nyiex buan,
mai tawx min plak ngai ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux.
Nyiex buan in tag sang mawh dee pa singax singeh,
kieh aux mai pui cie aux yaox tix gleeing nyawng bhawng krub ka Simiang Siyiex dee an,
mai tan lu khao mun ang jud.
Ku ngaix pon ngawb cao,
ku vang tuah,
ku haok khix,
mai pa tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tang ceu khankix ku singaix,
mai yaog min ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex ting lhaong.
Nawh hoik tawx lox nhie pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nin leeing ju leeing phan.
5 Aux tag sang sum nyiex buan pa mhawm grang tix lhang,
mawh kheu Siyiex ex hoik ting lhaong khaing siyiex khawm uik.
6 aAng koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon sum nyiex buan son Siyiex,
mawh kheu viang hoik mawh ka piang haktiex pa ang koe dee die kawx,
ang pon kub nawh ot daux.
Aux doh sang pon sum ka nyiex buan pa gleeing pui nyawng bhawng daux ka son nawh tix lhang khaing lie?
7 “Yam in,
khiox khaw maix ploi pui pa kied chix yuh kaing taix yuh kraung khrai tix kaux hoek,
pa chix saik yuh ka riang,
pa chix teei khri mau rhiem tawng raug kraung lih kah,
pa chix taing man klai rao ma,
man lung pliex,
man raoh rawt.
Pui in tag caw tix yuh kaing tau mai cang mai pui Yo-tax mai cang mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix,
pa mawh cang mai pa hoik laug keeing aux Ta-wue lih.
8 Aux hoik tawng cao kaing pex mawh pui pa mhawm kid khaox,
mawh kheu nan khiox khaw maix reu tix tawx khaox si oe bhawng,
khaox gix,
khaox gix bhawng ka aux khaing Li-pa-noeng.
Aux tag sang ploi pui hu teem coi ka pui pa yuh kaing maix,
9 mai rien khre ka khaox ox tom nie,
mawh kheu nyiex pa sum aux hoik mawh nyiex buan pa kied ting mhawm grang tix lhang.
10 Aux tag chix tawx pui pa kid khaox maix ih nghox ca ra rheng kongtoeng,
nghox ca pa lang mu ra rheng kongtoeng,
plai tipon mheen kongsheng mai bee sime tipon mheen kongsheng heue.”
11 Simiang Si-lang tawx lox ka Saw-law-meung ah nin:
“Mawh kheu muh Simiang Siyiex ka pui cie tix,
mai yaog maix mawh simiang kix.
12 Yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
cao pa phan pa yuh rao ma haktiex!
Nawh tawx simiang Ta-wue cie kawn simex pa koe krax lhak pa lhiao yuh nin tix kaux;
kawn simex in hoik keud tix sang sum nyiex buan son Simiang Siyiex,
mai baox keud tix sang sum nyiex haw son to kaux tix.
13 Yam in aux ploi pui pa kied lhak lhiao pa kied min ka kaing,
cang mai pa blawn chix taix ka maix;
ceu kaux nawh mawh Hu-lang,
14 miex nawh mawh ceu pui Tang,
keeing nawh mawh pui Thai-eux.
Nawh chix teei mau khri,
rhiem tawng,
simaox rang,
khaox ox khankix yuh kraung khrai lih kah,
mai chix caing nang man klai rao ma,
man lung pliex,
man raoh rawt,
mai man keei sum.
Nawh chix saik riang lih ka kraung ku ceu,
mai chix saik yuh nang riang ku ceu khankix lih.
Maix chix tix kieh nawh yuh kaing tau mai pui cang mai cie maix mai cang mai pui cie keeing maix simiang Ta-wue.
15 “Yam in,
khiox khaw maix reu tix tawx nghox ca,
nghox ca pa lang mu,
plai,
bee sime ka aux heue!
16 Yix tag chix tix kid khaox ox pa sang lo maix pa ot ka gawng Li-pa-noeng,
mai mad dia tix kaux tau,
si bloe hoek ka Yaw-pha khaing krax lawng rawm,
maix tom kuan rieb khaing dee an viex hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.”
Jauh tix yuh ka kaing nyiex buan
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 6.1–38)
17 Simiang Saw-law-meung si kaw ka can nyo si nie pa ot ka daux Yi-seu-liex,
si kaw ca rang awm pa yuh keeing maix Ta-wue sivoe an yuh nan,
khawm uik koe pui kao phuan mheen loe rheng liah yieh kaux.
18 Nawh ploi ka daux kix aliah mheen kaux mawh cao pa glawm khaox ox,
daix mheen kaux mawh pa ot tix saik simaox ka piang gawng,
mai baox ciak ci loe rheng liah yieh kaux mawh cao pa kia ngien ka kaing yuh kix heue.
