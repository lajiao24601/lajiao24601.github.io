Naing ka daux Yi-tong
20
Ang koe miex glang tix,
Maw-ya,
Ya-meung,
mai paox grawm nawh pui naing Mi-wu-nia puing ka Yo-tax.
2 Koe pui krai ka simiang Yaw-sa-fa ah nin:
“Yi-tong plak an koe pui naing pa kaoh khaing toi Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog hoek puing ka maix;
mai kix hoik pon cam ka Ha-si-suing Tha-ma.”
(Ha-si-suing Tha-ma baox kok mawh Yin Ci-ti.)
3 Yaw-sa-fa lhat,
mai khiox khaw krax pa duk thung Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an tawx lox nhie pui khawm meung tuah priex preem.
4 Kawn pui kaoh khaing veng Yo-tax ku dauh mai hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
khiox khaw krax pa duk thung Simiang Siyiex.
5 Kix mai pui pa ot Yie-lu-sa-leung khrawm paox tix ot biang brawk khraox nyiex buan.
Simiang Yaw-sa-fa cung ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
6 mai rawg buan ploi lox tix tom ting ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du yix eue,
maix ot ka piang rao ma mai kuang meung haktiex khawm uik;
maix koe riang rao siawp sibrawm pa ting,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tieb tie ka maix.
7 bSiyiex yix eue,
yam gah pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex tix hoek dee in,
maix hoik grheu pui pa ot haktiex in sivoe an,
mai tawx haktiex in ka doeh ceu cad paox grawm maix Ya-paw-la-hang,
kieh mawh cie kix leeing ju.
8 Kix ca ot dee in jheeng jheeng,
mai sum nang nyiex buan yaog yaw ka maix.
Kix hoik tawng,
9 phan koe krax laux leeing hoek piang kix,
ciyang ka kix,
awm krax naing,c
jhawm saux preng,
sibhawm praix khankix,
mai kix chix tix hoek ka nyiex buan pa krub ka maix in,
mai cung ka sivoe nyiex buan;
kix chix tix khiox khaw maix ka daux krax laux krax leeing,
maix tom nghiet ka kix,
mai teem coi ka kix heue.
10 d“Yam in,
pui Ya-meung,
pui Maw-ya,
mai pui Yi-tong hoek puing ka yix.
Yam lih tax du yix khaing Haktiex Ai-cix sivoe an,
maix ang cu kix laig teei ka haktiex pui an kix,
mawh kheu nan tax du yix tom via tiang ka krax,
mai ang bax yuh kix laux leeing.
11 Yam in kix lai pawk bix yix yuh nin!
Kix sang grheu yix lih khaing haktiex pa hoik tawx maix ka yix.
12 Siyiex yix eue,
khiox khaw maix ciyang ka kix,
mawh kheu ang yix koe riang rao tix tieb tie ka kru naing pa hoek tix puing ka yix.
Yix ang tawng pa sang yuh tix,
mai een rhawm tix ka maix khaing heue.”
13 Can simex pui Yo-tax khawm uik,
khawm moeng kix,
kawn simex kawn bun kix cung ka plak sivoe nyiex buan.
14 Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang to kaux pui Li-we pa ot ka daux phung pui hun khankix.
Nawh kaux Ya-ha-si,
mawh kawn simex Sa-cia-li-ya;
nawh mawh ceu cad pui Ya-sa,
kawn saux Ma-thang-ya,
kawn sex Yie-lix,
kawn praix Pi-na-ya.
15 eYa-ha-si ah nin:
“Vawng mai pui Yo-tax mai khawm pui Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik eue,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin,
pex paw vi laux rhawm,
mai paw vi lhat ka pui naing pa koeng pa ting riang in.
Krax pa pex mai pa ang pex hoik ot ka daux tawm taix Siyiex,
mai ang ot ka pex.
16 Pa sax,
yam kaoh kix khaing diang moing Si-see,
pex hu kuan rieb ka kix.
Pex caw tix si pup ka kix diang ngawd sivoe daux tiag Yie-lu-yi-leux.
17 fPex ang siao puing ka naing in,
pau ngie ka bang naing,
cung kaux cong khaing,
mai pex tag sang yaox pa hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex pex puing pex.
Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
paw vi koe krax khi rhawm,
mai paw vi koe krax lhat graung.
Pa sax pex hu kuan rieb ka kix.
Simiang Siyiex tag ot tau maix pex heue.”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Yaw-sa-fa gup tix krub piang tiex;
can pui hun khawm uik kawx gup tix krub Simiang Siyiex tau mai nawh.
19 Ceu pui Li-we pa mawh Keu-siax mai ceu pui Kheu-la yaog tix kaoh cung,
mai kok yo lox pa si num yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
20 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
kawn pui hoek ka daux tiag Thi-keu-yax.
Yam yaog kix tix hu,
Yaw-sa-fa ah nin ka kix:
“Pui Yo-tax mai kawn pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
tag caw tix yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai pex tom tag pon kaux cong;
tag caw tix yum rhawm ka gum lox cao jhai nawh khankix,
mai pex tom tag pon pex.”
21 Khaix hoik hoik krai simiang lox in ka kawn pui khankix,
nawh tawx lox ka pui pa kied chix rhax lai,
nhie kix ceep ka kraung dha pa ceep yam yuh poi,
hu plak sivoe ngai pui naing,
mai rhax lai hawt lawng krax ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex!
Krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan heue!”
22 Yam jauh kix tix rhax lai yaog yaw,
Simiang Siyiex yuh ka pui naing pa koeng suk sag.
23 Pui Ya-meung mai pui Maw-ya puing ka pui naing Yi-tong,
mai yuh kix laux leeing uik,
khaix an deeh veeh tix ra lai pauh paox tix yum.
24 Pui naing Yo-tax hoek ka nyiex lhaong gawng mu pa koe daux tiang tix lhang,
kok jhak ka bang naing pui koeng,
mai kix yaox to kaux pui yum hoik naog ka haktiex uik,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tix luat luan heue.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-sa-fa mai pui naing cie nawh laig teei ka kraung khaing naing khankix;
kix yaox ka khawng brie tom nie,g
mai kraung siao,
kraung khrai,
mai kraung tang ceu pa koe ngawh koe jum khankix.
Kix teei bawg yam loe singaix khrawm ka kraung pa pon tix khaing naing,
viang mawh nan,
kied sidaing nie,
mai ang uik viex.
26 Ka daux pon singaix,
kix khrawm tix ka daux ngawd Pi-la-cia,
mai yaog yaw krax pa hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
ngawd in tom kok mawh “Pi-la-cia.”h
27 Yaw-sa-fa thung pui naing deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu hoik puing pex Simiang Siyiex ka pui pa koeng ka kix.
28 Kix tauh ting phlu teet deeh ing ka veng hawt krax,
mai laig daux nyiex buan.
29 Meung ku meung mhong grawng pa hoik puing pex Simiang Siyiex ka pui pa koeng ka Yi-seu-liex,
kix kied lhat kah.
30 Yuh nin,
daux yam giex Yaw-sa-fa kiang,
meung haktiex pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem;
Simiang Siyiex tawx krax pa yien yawg ka haktiex nawh khawm pon daog heue.
Grawng si dauh giex Yaw-sa-fa kaing
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 22.41–50)
31 Yam koe ju Yaw-sa-fa tix ngoe phuan num nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
nawh giex kaing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tix nga phuan num.
Miex nawh mawh A-su-pa kawn bun See-li-si.
32 Yaw-sa-fa awm keeing tix Ya-sa yuh nan,
ang goix khaing krax mai yuh kaing pa khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
33 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa koe ka piang gawng iak;
kawn pui ca kawn ang bax krub Siyiex tax du kix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix heue.
34 Kaing yuh Yaw-sa-fa tang ceu khankix,
jauh ka kaing tom hoek ka rao hoik tiiem tix ka daux Lai Been Yie-hu kawn simex Ha-na-ni;
lai in hoik mawh pa koe daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex tix phuk heue.
35 Koe tix bawg,
Yaw-sa-fa simiang Yo-tax yuh paox pie tau mai Ya-ha-shiex simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kied kin yuh kaing pa caox pa caig;
36 kiex yuh reu ka Yi-shuing Cia-piie diang praok rawm sigrit sigra son sang hu dee singai.
37 Pui veng Ma-li-sa kawn simex To-ta-wa pa mawh Yi-li-yi-shiex ne raw ka Yaw-sa-fa ah nin:
“Mawh kheu yuh maix paox pie tau mai Ya-ha-shiex,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang yuh reu pa yuh maix laux.”
Reu an rug laux,
mai ang bax lai yaox tix hu heue.
