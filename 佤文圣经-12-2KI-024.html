24
aYam giex Yaw-ya-cing kaing,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha puing ka pui Yo-tax.
Yaw-ya-cin khin tix yuh kaing nawh loe num,
khaix an tieh vheen ka nawh.
2 Simiang Siyiex kieh pui naing Pa-pi-loeng,
pui naing Shui-li-yax,
pui naing Maw-ya,
mai pui naing Ya-meung hoek puing ka Yaw-ya-cin,
pon pex ka pui Yo-tax,
awm hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex kawn cau tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix krai.
3 Kaing in lih tix mawh pa kaoh khaing lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
son sang grheu ka pui Yo-tax lih hu.
In hoik mawh kheu jao phi map pa yuh simiang Ma-na-si khawm uik;
4 nawh baox kieh nham pui ang koe dee lud jauh tom nie ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
Simiang Siyiex ang cu tix pluat ploi ka phi map Ma-na-si heue.
5 Kaing yuh Yaw-ya-cing tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
6 Nawh hoik yum;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yaw-ya-cin plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
7 Simiang Ai-cix mai pui naing cie nawh ang lai baox ploi pui naing hu plak praix haktiex Ai-cix,
mawh kheu hoik giex gang simiang pui Pa-pi-loeng ka can haktiex pa mawh cie pui Ai-cix sivoe khawm uik,
jauh ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti tom hoek ka daog plak laong Ai-cix heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-ya-cin
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 36.9–10)
8 Yam koe ju Yaw-ya-cin kao daix num,
nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai hoik giex kaing ka piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung loe khix.
Miex nawh mawh Ni-hu-see-tha,
kawn bun Yi-li-na-tang pui veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
9 Yaw-ya-cin hawt ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh keeing tix,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
10 Yam giex Yaw-ya-cin kaing,
simiang naing cie simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha thung pui naing Pa-pi-loeng hoek puing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai lai lawm ka veng an.
11 Yam puing kix ka veng,
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
12 bMawh kheu nan,
simiang Yaw-ya-cin mai miex nawh,
kawn simex nawh,
simiang naing,
siliex nyiex haw simiang khankix,
ap tix ang pex ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Yam giex Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha kaing ka daux daix num,
nawh giex ka Yaw-ya-cin,
13 mai gah can sin ngao pa koe ka daux nyiex buan mai ka daux nyiex haw simiang khawm uik hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Awm lox jhai pa krai Simiang Siyiex,
nawh hoik yuh laux ka kraung pa yuh simiang Saw-law-meung ka khri khawm uik pa ot ka daux nyiex buan.
14 Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha giex ka pui veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai can simiang mai pui naing khankix,
khawm uik koe pui tix mheen kaux.
Nawh baox giex ka can cang mai pa thung pui yuh kaing mai cang mai pa yuh rhiem khankix hu khawm uik,
deuh ngie pui pa blawn hot sibhawm khankix heue.
15 cNi-pu-cia-ni-sha viex Yaw-ya-cin hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng,
khawm miex nawh,
moeng iak nawh,
siliex nawh,
mai simiang pui Yo-tax kawx hoik viex hu tau.
16 Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoik giex tix viex ka pui pa ting ceu khawm uik hu,
khawm uik koe pui aliah rheng kaux,
kawn koe cang mai pa thung pui yuh kaing mai cang mai pa yuh rhiem tix rheng kaux;
kix hoik mawh pui pa kua ka naing uik heue.
17 dNi-pu-cia-ni-sha yaog pux keeing Yaw-ya-cin pa kaux Ma-thang-ya mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai baox loh ceu kaux nawh mawh Si-ti-cia heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Si-ti-cia
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 36.11–12; Lai Yie-li-mi 52.1–3)
18 eYam koe ju Si-ti-cia tix nga tix num,
nawh mawh simiang Yo-tax,
mai hoik giex ka kaing piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung kao tix num.
Miex nawh kaux Ha-maw-tha,
mawh kawn bun Ye-li-mi pui veng Li-na.
19 Simiang Si-ti-cia hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
awm kaing pa yuh Yaw-ya-cing yuh nan.
20 fSimiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai pui Yo-tax,
mai grheu kix hu khaing sivoe ngai tix heue.
