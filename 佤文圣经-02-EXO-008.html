(2) Krax laux ka khiet
8
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix hu ka simiang Ai-cix,
mai krai ka nawh Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin:
‘Ploi pui ceu aux lih hu,
son sang hu kix yuh kaing krub ka aux.
2 Phan maix baox kawn thiang kah,
aux tag sang ciyang ka meung haktiex maix,
mai kieh meung haktiex maix khawm bee lih koe khiet.
3 Daux rawm klawng Ni-law hoik naog ka khiet khankix.
Khiet lih khaing daux klawng,
mai laig ka daux nyiex haw maix,
ka daux lawg it maix,
mai phuh tix haok piang ku it maix,
bleeh ka daux nyiex siliex maix khankix,
mai kawn pui cie maix khankix,
khawm nyiex priex,
mai ka daux aw.
4 Khaix an,
haok ka piang to kaux maix,
mai ka piang to kaux siliex maix,
mai ka piang to kaux kawn pui cie maix heue.’ ”
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix krai ka Ya-loeng,
nhie nawh teei khaox gix in,
nyeu ka piang rawm klawng iak,
piang rawm klawng ting,
piang rawm nhawng mai piang rawm dung khankix,
kieh khiet lih hoek,
mai kieh naog ka piang haktiex khawm uik.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-loeng nyeu khaox gix tix ka piang rawm,
mai khiet tom phuh tix lih,
mai naog ka piang tiex khawm uik.
7 Viang mawh nan,
cao chix si meed khankix kawx yuh awm pa chix tix,
mai kieh khiet khankix lih hoek ka piang tiex nang heue.
8 Vawng kok ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hoek,
ah nin:
“Viex maix khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex,
grheu khiet khankix lih hu,
aux tom tag ploi kawn pui cie maix lih hu,
son sang kieh kix hu krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
9 Maw-si pawk lox:
“Aux cu tix khiox khaw son maix.
Maix tat ka bawg yam,
aux sang khiox khaw son maix,
son siliex maix mai son kawn pui cie maix.
Ka daux bawg yam an,
can khiet khankix khawm uik tag sang grai,
mai kawn deuk ngie pa ot ka daux rawm klawng Ni-law heue.”
10 Vawng pawk lox:
“Maix khiox khaw son aux pa sax heue.”
Maw-si ah nin:
“Aux chix tix hawt yuh awm lox pa mhaing maix,
maix tag tawng ka greem rao ma ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa awm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix.
11 Khiet tag lih hu khaing maix,
khaing siliex maix,
khaing kawn pui cie maix,
kawn koe ngie ka daux rawm klawng Ni-law khaing.”
12 Khaix an Maw-si mai Ya-loeng lih hu khaing simiang Ai-cix;
Maw-si rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
rawg Simiang Siyiex kieh khiet pa hoik ploi nawh lih ka piang simiang Ai-cix grai.
13 Simiang Siyiex yuh awm pa rawg mhaing Maw-si.
Can khiet pa ot daux nyiex,
daux brawk mai daux kaung ma khankix hoik yum uik.
14 Pui Ai-cix pum tix deuh ka khiet yum,
mai haktiex hoik si oe cux ku dauh ku dee.
15 Viang mawh nan,
awm lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex,
yam yaox Vawng hoik yum khiet,
nawh deeh kiien rhawm,
mai ang nghiet ka lox Maw-si mai Ya-loeng heue.
(3) Krax laux ka breeik
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix krai ka Ya-loeng,
nhie nawh teei khaox gix tix lauh ka piang tiex,
mai kieh phawng tiex ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik tieh tix mawh breeik.”
17 Kheu nan,
Ya-loeng tom teei khaox gix tix lauh piang tiex,
mai phawng tiex ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik tom tieh tix mawh breeik,
ka piang to kaux pui mai ka piang khawng brie hoik lih mawh breeik.
18 Cao chix si meed khankix ce tix sang yuh awm pa chix tix,
kieh breeik lih,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang pon yuh.
Ka daux bawg yam an,
ka piang to kaux pui mai ka piang khawng brie hoik lih mawh breeik.
19 aCao chix si meed khankix ah nin ka Vawng:
“Kaing in hoik mawh pa yuh Siyiex!”
Viang mawh nan,
hoik awm lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex,
Vawng ca kawn kiien rhawm,
ang nghiet ka lox pa krai Maw-si mai Ya-loeng heue.
(4) Krax laux ka roi
20 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Pa sax pon ngawb cao,
mai hu ka Vawng ka praok rawm klawng,
krai ka nawh Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Ploi pui cie aux lih hu,
son sang hu kix yuh kaing krub ka aux.
21 Aux krai lox tete ka maix,
phan maix kawn baox thiang,
aux tag ploi phung roi khankix hoek ciyang ka piang maix,
mai ka piang siliex maix,
mai ka piang kawn pui cie maix.
Haktiex Ai-cix ku dauh ku dee,
ku nyiex ku ma,
hoik naog ka phung roi.
22 Viang mawh nan,
dee ot pui cie aux ka daux haktiex Keu-sang,
mai roi tag ang koe pie tix mu kawx.
Aux yuh nin mawh son sang kieh maix tawng,
aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex cao haktiex khawm uik.
23 Aux sang kieh pui cie tix mai kawn pui cie maix tang guah tixb sidawng.
Mhai pa daox pa am in tag lih mawh pa sax.’ ”
24 Simiang Siyiex ploi phung roi khankix pu hoek ka daux nyiex haw Vawng mai ka daux nyiex siliex nawh;
haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik hoik laux leeing ka roi heue.
25 Vawng kok Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hoek,
ah nin:
“Hu heue,
krub ka Simiang Siyiex pex ka daux haktiex aux heue.”
26 Maw-si ah nin:
“In ang chix mawh?
Phan yix tan lu tix ka khawng brie,
mai tawx ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix,
yix tee chix kieh pui meet rhawm.
Pui Ai-cix yaox pa teei yix khawng brie tan lu tix kah,
hoik mawh pa ang khaw ka kix,
mai kix tag dheem yix ka simaox!
27 Yix caw tix hawt yuh awm lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix,
hu loe singaix krax,
bleeh ka daux tiag mai tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
28 Vawng ah nin:
“Aux cu tix kieh pex hu bleeh ka daux tiag mai tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
viang mawh nan pex ang pon tix hu tom singai.
Pex caw tix rawg buan son aux heue.”
29 Maw-si pawk lox:
“Aux lih hu khaing maix mai aux tag rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
son sang lih hu roi khankix khaing maix pa sax,
mai khaing siliex maix,
kawn pui cie maix khankix.
Viang mawh nan,
maix ang lai pon tix kawn baox pie lhien ka yix,
mai tieb tie yix ang hu krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
30 Maw-si lih hu khaing simiang Ai-cix,
mai rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex.
31 Simiang Siyiex yuh awm lox pa rawg Maw-si,
mai kieh roi lih hu khaing simiang Ai-cix,
khaing siliex nawh,
mai khaing kawn pui cie nawh khankix,
pie tix mu kawx ang lai deuh koe.
32 Viang mawh nan bawg in,
Vawng ca kawn kiien rhawm,
mai ang ploi kawn pui Yi-seu-liex hu heue.
