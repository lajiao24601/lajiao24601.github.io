Si kaw ka ca rang pui bawg ra
26
aKhaix hoik naox jhawm saux preng tix,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Ya-loeng:
2 “Pex si kaw ca rang pui haoh hun Yi-seu-liex khankix khawm uik,
si kaw cub nyiex mawx cub nyiex an,
ceu pui mawx ceu pui an,
mai jauh ka pui koe ju tix nga num haok plak piang,
simex pa pon hu yuh naing tiiem ceu kaux lih.”
3-4 Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa tom khrawm can pui pa kub num ku kaux.
Kix khrawm tix daux lung Maw-ya praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
plak toi veng Yie-li-keu heue.
Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix koe:
5 Ceu pui Lui-piien (Lui-piien mawh kawn ai Ya-keux):
mawh khawm ceu pui Ha-nawx,
Fa-lux,
6 mai ceu pui Si-see-loeng,
Cia-mi khankix;
7 can pui pa hoik tiiem ka ceu kaux khawm uik koe pon mheen loe rheng aliah yieh tix ngoe kaux.
8 Doeh ceu pui Fa-lux mawh Yi-li-ya,
9 mai kawn simex Yi-li-ya mawh Ni-mu-lix,
Ta-thang,
Ya-pi-lang.
(Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang mawh pui pa hoik laug lih khaing ka daux pui hun;
kiex tieh vheen ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng,
mai rom tix yuh paox pie tau mai Kheu-la,
mai tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex,
10 haktiex ang jheeh tix bleet ka kiex.
Yam gleeing ngu ka pui ra yieh tix phuan kaux,
kix yum tau mai cub caw Kheu-la,
mai lih tix mawh krax pa rien tix.
11 Viang mawh nan,
kawn praix Kheu-la khankix ang yum heue.)
12 Ceu pui Si-miien:
mawh khawm Ni-mu-lix,
Ya-ming,
Ya-cing,
13 mai ceu pui Shie-la,
Sao-law khankix;
14 pui pa tiiem kix ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe ra mheen ra rheng ra yieh kaux heue.
15 Ceu pui Cia-teux:
mawh khawm Si-feung,
Ha-ci,
Su-ni,
16 A-see-ni,
Yi-lix,
17 Ya-lui mai Ya-lie-lix khankix;
18 pui pa tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe pon mheen phuan yieh kaux heue.
19-21 Ceu pui Yo-tax:
mawh Khawm See-la,
Fa-leu-see,
Shie-la,
Si-see-loeng,
mai Ha-mu-leux khankix.
(Kawn simex Yo-tax ra kaux pa kaux Eux mai Eu-nang hoik yum ka daux meung Cia-nang.)
22 Ceu pui in kix pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe aliah mheen liah rheng phuan yieh kaux heue.
23 Ceu pui Yi-sa-cia:
mawh khawm Thaw-la,
Phu-wa,
24 Ya-sux,
mai Seung-loeng khankix;
25 pui pa tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe liah mheen pon rheng loe yieh kaux heue.
26 Ceu pui Si-pu-loeng:
mawh khawm Si-liex,
Yi-loeng,
mai Ya-lix khankix;
27 pui pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe liah mheen mai phuan yieh kaux heue.
28 Yaw-seux koe kawn simex ra kaux:
mawh Ma-na-si mai Yi-fa-liien,
mawh kheu nan,
kiex guah tix koe ra ceu pui heue.
29 Ceu pui Ma-na-si:
mawh Ma-na-si keh Ma-ciex,
Ma-ciex keh Ci-liex.
30 Ci-liex mawh ceu pui tax du Yi-yi-shiex,
Si-leux,
31 Ya-see-liiex,
See-ciien,
32 See-mi-tax mai Si-fu ku ceu pui khankix.
33 Si-law-fe-ha kawn simex Si-fu ang koe kawn simex,
nawh koe ngie kawn bun;
ceu kaux kix mawh:
Ma-lax,
Naw-a,
Heu-la,
Mi-cia mai Teu-sa.
34 Ceu pui in kix pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe phuan mheen ra rheng aliah yieh kaux heue.
35 Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien:
mawh khawm Su-thi-la,
Pi-cie mai Tha-hang khankix,
36 mai baox mawh doeh ceu cad Su-thi-la ceu pui Yi-lang.
37 Ceu pui in kix pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe loe mheen ra rheng phuan yieh kaux heue.
In plak piang ra ceu pui,
mawh doeh ceu cad Yaw-seux khankix heue.
38 Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming:
mawh khawm Pi-la,
Ya-seu-piie,
Ya-si-lang,
39 Su-fang mai Hu-fang khankix,
40 mai kawn koe doeh ceu cad Pi-la pa mawh Ya-leux mai Nai-mang ra ceu pui.
41 Ceu pui in kix pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe pon mheen phuan rheng liah yieh kaux heue.
42 Ceu pui Tang:
mawh cub nyiex ma Su-hang;
43 pui pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe liah mheen pon rheng pon yieh kaux heue.
44 Ceu pui Ya-seux:
mawh khawm Ying-na,
Yi-see-we,
mai Pi-li-yax khankix;
45 mai baox koe doeh ceu cad Pi-li-yax,
pa mawh ceu Si-piie mai ceu Ma-ciex ra ceu pui.
46 Ya-seux koe kawn bun tix kaux mawh Si-la.
47 Ceu pui in kix pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe phuan mheen loe rheng pon yieh kaux heue.
48 Ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix:
mawh khawm pui Ya-shiie,
pui Ku-ni,
49 Yie-seux mai See-leung khankix;
50 pui pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe pon mheen phuan rheng pon yieh kaux heue.
51 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
khawm uik koe tix gliah mheen mai tix rheng aliah yieh tix ngoe kaux heue.
52 bSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
53 “Maix tag caw tix guah haktiex hawt awm pa hoik koe ceu kaux pui,
54-56 yuh pa cawk lai mai tawx cub nyiex pui pa nie cie haktiex pa vieh,
cub nyiex pui pa tux,
tawx cie haktiex pa iak heue.”
57 Ceu pui Li-we mawh Keu-soeng,
Keu-siax mai Mi-la-lix loe cub nyiex khankix.
58 Doeh ceu pui kix mawh Li-ni,
Si-paw-loeng,
Ma-lix,
Mu-seex mai Kheu-la khankix.
Keu-siax keh Ang-lang.
59 Ang-lang teei tix moeng ka Yaw-ci-piie kawn bun Li-we,
pa keh ka daux haktiex Ai-cix.
Nawh keh Ang-lang cie kawn simex ra kaux pa mawh Ya-loeng mai Maw-si,
mai kawn bun tix kaux pa mawh Me-li-ang.
60 cYa-loeng koe kawn simex pon kaux pa mawh Na-ta mai Ya-pi-hux,
Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yi-tha-ma.
61 dNa-ta mai Ya-pi-hux hoik yum yam gleeing kiex tix tan ngu ka Simiang Siyiex.
62 Kawn nyawm pa koe tix khix haok plak piang,
pui Li-we koe ra mheen loe rheng kaux.
Kix tang guah tix tiiem ka ca rang tix khaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix,
mawh kheu ang sin ngao nyum cie kix koe ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
63 Yam ot pui Yi-seu-liex dee lung haktiex Maw-ya ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
plak toi veng Yie-li-keu,
Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa hoik hoik si kaw ka ca rang pui.
Cub nyiex pui in kix plak piang,
hoik mawh ca rang pa tiiem kix,
64 ka daux kix ang koe pui tix kaux kawx,
pa hoik tiiem Maw-si mai Ya-loeng ka ca rang bawg tix,
yam ot ka daux tiag Si-nai.
65 eSimiang Siyiex hoik krai,
kix tag sang yum ka daux tiag uik;
lheu khaing Cia-leux kawn simex Yie-fu-ni mai Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng khaing,
kix tag yum uik heue.
