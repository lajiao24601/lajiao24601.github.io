Lai Yi-fu-saw
Krai dawt
Num Lai Yi-fu-saw mawh: “Ka daux bawg yam cie Siyiex mawh pa sang yuh grum naog yuh nin: tag sang kieh can ceu pa phan pa yuh rao ma mai haktiex khawm uik mawh cie Khrit, mai kieh nawh mawh cao pa giex kaing.” (1.10) Cao pa tiiem kawx khiox khaw pui cie Siyiex sau riang tix sawg ka daux pa khaw mai krax ting lhaong in pa sang yuh Siyiex, son sang kieh kawn pui khawm uik lih mawh tix mu ka daux pa im Ye-sux Khrit heue.
Phuk lai in ka plak sivoe ngai, cao tiiem krai lao lox pa lih kleuk mawh tix mu, son grawng pa hoik laug Siyiex ka pui cie tix, mai pa hoik pluat ploi phi map kix ka daux kawn simex nawh Ye-sux Khrit, kieh kix pon tix luat luan khaing daux pa phrawk phi map, khaix an krai gum lox pa ting lhaong pa ap Siyiex, mai hoik kieh mawh Khuan Singax Singeh pa kham sakse. Khaix an phuk lai in ka plak khaix, cao tiiem sidiex gau ka cao pa phat, tag caw kieh tix lih mawh tix mu tau mai Khrit ka daux krax ot krax koe yam rub rom paox tix heue.
Cao tiiem phuk lai in teei lox phek tiiem, krai grawng pa lih mawh pui cie Siyiex tix mu ka daux krax pa im ju Khrit yuh nin: ceu cao awm to kaux pui yuh nan, Khrit mawh kaing; ceu cao awm nyiex tix lhang yuh nan, Khrit mawh jaong simaox; ceu cao awm moeng yuh nan, Khrit mawh mex. Cao tiiem hoik tawng buan son pa tawx Siyiex ka daux Khrit, nawh tom teei gum lox pa raux pa mox jhi lih. Nawh kieh krax muh Khrit, pa yum nawh, krax pa pluat ploi nawh, buan son nawh, mai pa singax singeh nawh kieh mawh diag tong pa prawng ka ceu pa koe khawm uik heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–2)
2. Khrit mai ceu cao (1.3–3.21)
3. Ju pa khraox ka daux Khrit (4.1–6.20)
4. Lox pa nghoik tix krai (6.21–24)
