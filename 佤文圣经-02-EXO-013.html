Tan lu ka pa liang tix keh
13
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 a“Pex caw tix tan lu kawn ai khankix ka aux,
mawh kheu ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
can pa mawh kawn ai mai khawng brie pa liang tix keh mawh cie aux heue.”
Poi Tik-su-saix
3 Maw-si ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Doh keud ka singaix in;
singaix in,
pex lih khaing Ai-cix ka dee pa yuh pex siliex cawng.
Singaix in mawh singaix pa hoik teei Simiang Siyiex taix pa ting siawp sibrawm tix viex pex lih.
Pex ang pon tix ih khao mun pa koe su saix.
4 Ka daux singaix lih hu pex khaing Ai-cix in mawh daux khix Ya-pix,
pa mawh khix jauh.
5 Simiang Siyiex hoik tiex tu ka tax du pex khankix,
haktiex pui Cia-nang,
pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Si-we,
pui Yie-pu-see sang ap tix tawx ka pex.
Khaix hoik viex Simiang Siyiex pex laig ka daux haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia,
pex tag caw priex tix yuh ka poi in ku num daux khix tix.
6 “Glang daux aliah singaix,
pex tag ih ka khao mun pa ang koe su saix;
mai yam hoek daux singaix aliah,
pex tag caw tix yuh poi doh keud ka Simiang Siyiex.
7 Glang ka daux aliah singaix,
pex tag caw tix ih ka khao mun pa ang koe su saix.
Ka daux haktiex ot pex,
ang pon tix kieh koe su saix,
mai khao mun pa koe su saix.
8 Yam jauh pex tix yuh ka poi in,
pex tom krai kawn praix tix mhong dee bleeh,
poi in mawh pa yuh son yam lih pex khaing daux Ai-cix,
grawng mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
9 Grawng yuh ka poi in mawh son sang krai ka pex,
tiiem mawh mhai ka plawng taix tix mai ka kaing re tix,
mai kieh pex doh keud nied tix cu ca phat ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik teei taix pa ting siawp sibrawm tix viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix.
10 Pex tag caw priex tix hawt yuh ka poi in ku num ka daux bawg yam pa hoik tat tix deuh in heue.”
Kawn ai
11 “Simiang Siyiex hoik tiex tu ka pex mai tax du pex,
sang viex pex laig daux haktiex ceu pui Cia-nang,
12 bCan pa liang tix keh khawm uik tag caw tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
Can khawng brie pa liang tix keh tag mawh cie Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
13 Viang mawh nan,
pex teei kawn yung iak hu taoh kawn la mhaing.
Phan pex keud tix ang sang taoh kawn la mhaing,
pex caw tix raig ngawg kawn la mhaing an pot.
Viang yuh ka mawx pex caw tix taoh ka kawn ai tix.
14 Yam chawk kawn simex pex grawng in kix daux bawg yam pa sang hoek sax,
ah nin:
‘In mawh pa tix lie?’
Pex tag sang pawk ah nin:
‘Mawh taix pa ting sibrawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa hoik viex ex lih khaing Ai-cix khaing daux nyiex pa yuh ex siliex.
15 “ ‘Yam an,
simiang Ai-cix kiien rhawm ang ploi ex lih hu,
Simiang Siyiex tom pauh ka can pa liang tix keh ka daux haktiex Ai-cix,
khawm kawn ai mai khawng brie pa liang tix keh khankix.
Mawh kheu nan,
ex tag caw tix tan lu can khawng brie pa mawh mhaing pa liang tix keh ka Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
taoh ka kawn ai khawm uik.
16 Ex hawt tix yuh been in,
mawh son sang krai ka ex,
tiiem mawh mhai ka plawng taix ex mai ka kaing re ex,
sang kieh ex doh keud ka hoik teei Simiang Siyiex sibrawm ting tix viex ex lih khaing daux Ai-cix heue.’ ”
Rong pai awm mai rong ngu
17 Yam ploi simiang Ai-cix pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih hu,
Simiang Siyiex ang thung kix hu ka krax dawt plak haktiex Fe-li-see.
Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux lhat pui cie tix khankix si pup ka naing mai klih rhawm tix,
deeh ing ka haktiex Ai-cix.”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex thung kix via hu krax singai,
laig krax daux tiag daux tu,
plak koe Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raohc.
Yam lih hu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
kix hoik rien khre ka kraung naing tix mhawm mhawm,
son sang puing ka naing heue.
19 dMaw-si viex ka si-ang Yaw-seux hu,
mawh kheu Yaw-seux hoik dawg nhie pui Yi-seu-liex tiex tu ka plak sivoe ngaix tix,
ah nin:
“Yam taoh Siyiex ka pex,
viang yuh ka mawx pex caw tix viex si-ang aux hu khaing tin heue.”
20 Pui Yi-seu-liex hu khaing haktiex Su-keu,
mai yuh bang ka praok pak mhaik daux haktiex Yi-thang.
21 Plak pon ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex hu sivoe kix,
mai kieh rong pai awm thung krax;
plak pon sawm,
Simiang Siyiex hu sivoe kix,
mai kieh rong ngu jhan riang piang kix.
Yuh nin,
kix tom chix hu krax plak pon ngaix plak pon sawm.
22 Plak pon ngaix koe rong pai awm,
plak pon sawm koe rong ngu,
ca hu ang jud ka plak sivoe ngai kix heue.
