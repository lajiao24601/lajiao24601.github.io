Nyiex ra lhang pa dex ka nyiex buan
42
Pui an thung aux hu ka plak praix brawk,
mai laig ka nyiex pa ot ka plak laong nyiex buan;
nyiex in ang singai khaing nyiex an pa ot ka plak klip singaix.
2 Nyiex in krax lang koe tix phuan kongjhee,
krax vieh koe tix nga phuan kongjhee.
3 Nyiex in tix plak veeh tix ka plak haktiex pa yi,
pa koe krax vieh kao kongjhee pa ot ka praok nyiex buan,
an tix plak veeh tix ka plak brawk pa tawm ka simaox.
Nyiex in koe loe jan,
jan plak piang iak khaing jan plak greem.
4 Nyiex in plak laong koe krax kah,
krax vieh koe phuan kongjhee,
krax lang koe tix phuan kongjhee,
siviex veeh tix plak laong.
5 Lawg nyiex jan plak piang paox iak khaing jan si greung mai jan pa ot greem paox,
tix jan iak khaing tix jan laig plak daux.
6 Lawg nyiex in hoik kawb tix kaux ka piang grah khawm uik,
mai ang lai siao rong joh kieh kaux awm lawg nyiex tang mu pa koe ka brawk yuh nan.
7-8 Guh ding plak greem bu koe tix nga phuan kongjhee,
lawg nyiex kawx beeh vieh koe tix nga phuan kongjhee.
Jan plak piang hoik mawh lawg nyiex khawm uik.
9-10 Ka greem lawg nyiex in kix,
ka toh guh ding plak lih singaix,
siviex koe ka chix tix phlat hu ka plak praix brawk heue.
Ka plak seh nyiex buan,a
ka dee pa ang singai ka plak klip singaix ka nyiex buan koe ka nyiex tang mu pa awm paox tix tix lhang.
11 Sivoe ngai lawg nyiex an krax phlat kah,
hoik awm nyiex pa koe plak laong yuh nan.
Khaox tiag nyiex an,
mai pak peung pa sum,
mai siviex hoik awm paox khawm uik.
12 Ka greem lawg nyiex an kix ka plak seh,
toh guh ding plak lih singaix kawx koe siviex ka heue.
13 Pui an krai ka aux ah nin:
“Lawg nyiex in kix ka plak lih singaix mai ka plak seh hoik mawh pa singax khawm uik.
Cao giex kaing li pa hoek ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
ih khawng phak krax tan pa kied singax ka dee in.
Mawh kheu lawg nyiex in hoik mawh pa singax,
caox giex kaing li tag sang teei tix deuh ka khawng phak krax tan,
pa mawh Krax-tan nghox koi mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud ka dee an.
14 Khaix hoik laig cao giex kaing li ka daux nyiex buan,
yam sang hu ka plak praix brawk,
kix tag caw tix deuh kraung ceep pa singax pa ceep ka yam yuh tix kaing krub ot dee in.
Kix tag caw tix ceep ka kraung ceep tang mu tom taug chix hu ka dee khrawm pui tix heue.”
Khaox diag vung viang nyiex buan
15 Khaix hoik tiag pui an tix yaox ka plak daux nyiex buan,
nawh tom thung aux laig daux siviex plak lih singaix,
mai hu tiag tix yaox ka vung viang nyiex buan khawm pon daog.
16 Nawh teei khaox diag tiag kah,
krax lang plak lih singaix koe ra yieh tix phuan kongjhee,
17-19 baox tiag tix yaox ka plak laong,
plak seh,
mai plak klip singaix,
ku plak koe ra yieh tix phuan kongjhee.
20 Guh ding vung viang ka haktiex in pa mawh pak peung lang gi ra yieh tix phuan kongjhee.
Guh ding vung viang in khan si nax haktiex pa singax mai haktiex pa ang singax heue.
