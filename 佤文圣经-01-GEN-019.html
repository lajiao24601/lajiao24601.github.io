Phi map Saw-to-ma
19
Ka daux bo ngaix an,
kawn cau Siyiex ra kaux hoek ka veng Saw-to-ma;
Law-teux ngawm ka diang siviex veng.
Nawh yaox ka kiex tom kaoh hu rieb kiex,
mai grux gawng krub ka plak sivoe ngai kiex,
2 ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
hoek daux nyiex aux,
kieh aux khre pax.
Khoek caong pax laux lee tix bo ngaix,
pa sax plak pon ngawb kaoh cao,
deeh hu krax heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
kiex pawk lox:
“Ang chix!
Yix sang it ka daux laih heue.”
3 Law-teux khin tix ghaok ka kiex,
mai kiex tom taug hawt nawh ing ka nyiex.
Law-teux tawx lox ka siliex tix rien khre ka priex preem,
mai tong ka khao mun pa ang sau su saix,
kix tom ih heue.
4 Sivoe ang kix nyang it,
kawn simex pa ting pa iak daux veng Saw-to-ma,
hoek tix lai lawm ka nyiex Law-teux.
5 aKix kok yo ka Law-teux mai chawk ah nin:
“Pui pa laig daux nyiex maix bo in ot dee mawx?
Teei kix lih plak praix,
yix sang it mai kix heue.”
6 Law-teux lih,
mai deeh nyeep siviex plak khaix tix,
7 mai ah nin ka kix:
“Paox grawm eue,
paw doh yuh pa caox pa caig in kix liak!
8 Jhak,
aux koe kawn bun ra kaux,
kawn mawh bun krih;
aux tawx kiex ka pex,
mai yuh pa simie pex tix yuh ka kiex.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang pon tix chuh ka pui in kiex;
kiex mawh nyo si nie aux,
mai aux sang priex kiex mhawm heue.”
9 Viang mawh nan,
kix pawk lox:
“Yoi!
Maix pui tang meung in khex sang pon kuang ka yix!
Yoi singai!
Nu bre yix maix,
kawn lheu kin khaing bre yix kix heue.”
Kix nyheen ka Law-teux hu sivoe,
mai sang lauh siviex preed tix laig.
10 Viang mawh nan,
pui an kiex ra kaux tawx taix tix bluh Law-teux laig daux nyiex,
mai nyeep ka siviex.
11 bKiex kieh simex pa ot plak praix,
khawm pui iak pui ting,
dug ngai uik,
ang yaox siviex heue.
Law-teux lih khaing veng Saw-to-ma
12 Pui an kiex ra kaux ah nin ka Law-teux:
“Ka daux veng kawn koe maix pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Koe kawn simex kawn bun,
kheue maix,
pui nyiex pui ma maix ot tin lie?
Nhie kix to lih uik,
13 mawh kheu yix sang yuh veng in laux.
Grawng pa krai pui kaing pa ang mhawm son veng in,
Simiang Siyiex hoik mhong,
kheu nan nawh tom ploi yiex hoek tix yuh veng in laux heue.”
14 Khaix an,
Law-teux tom hu sawg kheue tix pa ang nyang yuh ka paox,
mai krai ka kiex:
“Nied to hu khaing tin,
Simiang Siyiex sang yuh ka veng in laux heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
kix een nawh mawh lox klieh heue.
15 Yam hoik riang praix,
kawn cau Siyiex dhiet ka Law-teux ah nin:
“Nied maix viex moeng maix mai kawn bun maix khawm ra kaux lih khaing tin!
Son ang pex sang yaox tix laux tau mai veng in heue.”
16 cLaw-teux kawn ang sang rhawm lih,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex caox rhawm ka nawh,
mai kawn cau an kiex ra kaux bluh taix nawh khawm moeng nawh,
kawn bun nawh ra kaux,
mai viex kix hu singai khaing veng an.
17 Yam hoek ka praok veng,
kawn cau Siyiex an tix kaux ah nin:
“To mox pex heue!
Paw lai jhak plak khaix,
mai paw lai cung piang gawng.
To hoek ka caong gawng mai taug ang yaox tix yum heue.”
18-19 Viang mawh nan,
Law-teux ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
khiox khaw paw yuh nin liak!
Maix hoik teem ka aux,
taoh ka ju aux,
paw vi lai kieh aux to dee singai yuh nin.
Gawng an kied singai,
aux lhat sivoe ang aux nyang hoek dee an,
krax laux leeing hoik lih hoek,
aux kawx hoik yum heue.
20 Maix hoik yaox ka veng pa iak an lie?
Ang lai singai khaing tin,
kieh aux haok dee an.
Kieh aux mox tix dee an mai yaox tix klawd heue.”
21 Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Chix tix,
aux cu ka lox maix.
Aux ang yuh veng iak an laux.
22 Nied to phai!
Maix ang nyang hoek dee an,
aux kawx ang nyang yuh laux heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Law-teux kok veng iak an mawh Saw-eux.d
Singaix si dauh veng Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la
23 Yam lih singaix,
Law-teux hoek ka veng Saw-eux.
24 eDom mawh dom an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh mhat awm ngu lih cawd proe ka daux veng Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la.
25 Nawh yuh ka veng in laux khawm ra,
khawm dee lung haktiex,
kawn pui khawm uik mai rib riem khaox ox khawm uik.
26 fMoeng Law-teux deeh gi tix jhak plak khaix,
mai nawh tieh tix lih mawh rong kih heue.
27 Ka daux ra singaix plak pon ngawb cao cao,
Ya-paw-la-hang hoek ka dee cung nawh sivoe Simiang Siyiex tan.
28 Nawh kok jhak ka veng Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la,
khawm dee lung haktiex khankix,
mai yaox chu taox ngu ka piang tiex awm taox ngu pa kaoh khaing glah ngu heue.
29 Viang mawh nan,
yam yuh Siyiex ka veng ra mu ot dee lung haktiex in laux,
nawh doh keud ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai cu Law-teux to tix klawd heue.
Dee kaoh pui Maw-ya mai pui Ya-meung
30 Law-teux lhat ot ka veng Saw-eux,
nawh tom gah tix khaing tan mai haok ot ka piang gawng,
daux dawh rang khawm kawn bun nawh ra kaux.
31 Kawn bun yex ah nin ka pux tix:
“Keeing hoik kuad,
piang haktiex ang lai koe simex pa koe been tix chix mex ax,
mai kieh ax koe kawn.
32 Hoek,
ax tawx keeing nyaux plai,
son sang yaox tix it tau mai nawh,
mai yaox tix keh kawn khaing nawh.”
33 Ka daux ngaix an plak pon bo,
kiex tom tawx keeing tix nyaux plai,
kawn bun yex tom it mai keeing tix.
Law-teux hoik kied yoek tete,
mai ang lai tawng kaing pa yuh tix pie ceeix kawx heue.
34 Ka daux singaix khaix,
kawn bun yex ah nin ka pux tix:
“Aux hoik it tau mai keeing;
daux bo in singaix,
ax baox tawx nawh nyaux plai,
mai kieh maix it mai nawh.
Yuh nin,
maix mai aux tag koe kawn khaing keeing heue.”
35 Kheu nan,
ka daux bo an,
kiex baox tawx Law-teux nyaux plai,
kawn bun i tom it mai keeing nawh.
Keeing nawh baox yoek tete,
mai ang lai tawng kaing pa yuh kiex tix ceu kawx.
36 Yuh nin,
kawn bun Law-teux in kiex ra kaux tom koe kaux khaing keeing tix.
37 Kawn bun yex keh kawn simex tix kaux,
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Maw-ya;g
nawh mawh keeing ceu pui Maw-ya tom in singaix heue.
38 Kawn bun i keh kawn simex tix kaux,
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Piien Ya-mi;
h nawh mawh keeing ceu pui Ya-meung tom in singaix heue.
