Yaw-ya tawx lox nhie Ya-sa-long deeh hoek
14
Kawn simex Si-lu-ya pa kaux Yaw-ya hoik tawng simiang Ta-wue kied braong ka ya-sa-long,
2 nawh tom ploi pui hu sawg bun miex pa lhak lhiao tix kaux ka haktiex Thi-keu-yax.
Ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix lhien tix yiiem son pui yum,
mai ceep ka kraung ceep son pa yum,
paw vi sat hauk kaing tix,
yuh tix awm bun pa hoik yiiem son pui yum tom rheeng yuh nan,
3 khaix an hu ka dee ot simiang dee an,
mai krai simiang mhong lox pa hoik krai aux ka maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya krai bun miex an mhong lox pa sang khaw nawh tix krai heue.
4 Bun miex Thi-keu-yax an tom hu ka simiang,
mai grux ngawng tix krub piang tiex,
ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
khiox khaw maix teem coi aux liak!”
5 Simiang chawk nawh ah nin:
“Koe maix kaing ceu pa tix?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux mawh miex mai pa hot,
mex aux hoik dawg yum.
6 Aux koe kawn simex ra kaux,
koe tix singaix kiex simah paox tix yam ot daux kaung,
mai ang koe pui pa kieh ka kiex,
mawh kheu nan,
ka daux kieh an tix kaux pauh an tix kaux yum.
7 Vawng eue,
yam in pui nyiex pui ma aux hoik koeng ka aux khawm uik,
kix nhie aux tawx kawn simex tix lih,
mai kix sang pauh nawh kheu jao phi map pa hoik pauh nawh ka pux aik tix.
Phan kix yuh nin,
aux tag ang lai yaox kawn tix tix kaux kawx.
Kix tag sang yuh laux ka krax keud pot jun pot tom dee si dauh,
kieh mex aux dut simie doeh ceu cad heue.”
8 Simiang ah nin:
“Deeh ing heue,
aux tag sang chix prawng kaing in son maix.”
9 Bun miex an baox ah nin:
“Vawng,
ang kuang maix yuh ka mawx,
phi map in kieh hoek piang aux mai piang pui nyiex pui ma aux,
simiang mai pui nyiex simiang hoik mawh pui ang koe dee lud heue.”
10 Simiang pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan koe pui pa ghlat maix,
maix viex nawh hoek ka aux tin,
nawh tag ang lai baox deeh pon chuh maix khaix heue.”
11 Bun miex an ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
maix rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix liak,
son ang pui pawk bix sang baox pauh ka pui,
pie kawn simex aux pa deuh tix kaux khaing sang pauh ka heue.”
Ta-wue pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im leeing ju cu lox maix,
hauk kaing kawn simex maix pie tix gawng kawx,
ang yaox tix cawd piang tiex heue.”
12 Bun miex an baox ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
baox kieh aux krai tix ceu liak.”
Simiang pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix krai heue.”
13 Bun miex an ah nin ka simiang:
“Maix yuh ka mawx lai keud tix sang yuh kaing pa ang mhawm yuh nin ka pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex lie?
Maix ang cu tix kieh kawn simex pa hoik ploi ot plak praix deeh hoek.
Lox pa taug krai maix,
hoik awm tat maix ka phi map tix.
14 Yix tag sang yum khawm uik,
awm rawm pa hoik hieh tix piang tiex yuh nan,
mai ang lai pon deeh teei hoek.
Viang Siyiex ang kieh pui pa yum deeh kaoh im,
simiang tag sang chix koe rhawm keud tix sang kieh pui pa vung viang deeh yaox tix hoek.
15 Vawng eue,
aux hoek krai lox ka maix,
hoik mawh kheu koe pui pa ghlat ka aux,
mawh kheu nan,
aux tom krai ka simiang,
kieh simiang yuh awm lox pa hoik khiox khaw aux liak.
16 Aux keud simiang tag sang chix nghiet ka lox aux,
mai taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa sang pauh ka kawn simex aux mai aux,
pa sang teei yix ka haktiex pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka pui cie nawh khankix.
17 bAux keud,
lox Vawng tag sang chix kieh aux nyawm rhawm,
mawh kheu simiang hoik awm kawn cau Siyiex yuh nan,
pon guah ka ceu pa khaw mai pa ang khawa.
Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix ot tau mai maix heue!”
18 Simiang pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux sang chawk maix ka lox tix gum,
maix tag caw tix pawk ka aux sidawng heue.”
Bun miex an ah nin:
“Vawng,
chawk heue.”
19 Ta-wue chawk ah nin:
“Mawh ang mawh Yaw-ya pa nhie maix hoek krai lox in lie?”
Bun miex an pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka ju Vawng,
phan mawh grawng in,
aux ang krai ang chixc.
Mawh heue,
mawh simiang naing maix Yaw-ya pa nhie aux yuh nin,
mai krai mawh nin.
20 Nawh yuh nin,
mawh son sang pon deeh chix taoh ka grawng kaing in.
Vawng hoik lhak lhiao awm kawn cau Siyiex yuh nan,
mai hoik tawng ka kaing pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.”
21 Khaix an,
simiang ah nin ka Yaw-ya:
“Aux cu ka grawng kaing pa yuh maix in.
Maix hu,
mai viex ka pui nhum ju an Ya-sa-long hoek dee in heue!”
22 Yaw-ya yuh krax pa yung yi krub ka piang tiex sivoe Ta-wue,
mai ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
Siyiex tawx buan ka maix!
Yam in aux hoik tawng grawng krax pa muh maix,
mawh kheu hoik cu maix lox pa khiox khaw aux.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya yaog tix kaoh,
hu ka Ci-sux,
mai viex Ya-sa-long deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
24 Viang mawh nan,
simiang tawx lox ang cu kix kieh Ya-sa-long paung ot ka daux nyiex haw ot tix.
Simiang ah nin:
“Aux ang lai kieh tix yaox ka nawh.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-sa-long tom paung ot ka daux nyiex tix,
mai ang yaox ka ngai simiang heue.
Ta-wue pluat ploi ka Ya-sa-long
25 Ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa mhawm grang awm Ya-sa-long,
mai pui kied muh ka nawh,
jauh ka kaing tom hoek ka caong mai ang koe dee cud dee cha.
26 Hauk kaing nawh kied sied,
tix num tix bawg tag sang pon tix ghrip,
phan ang mawh nan,
kied nie lang kied nie pu.
Hauk kaing pa hoik ghrip,
tag hawt ka jiang nyiex simiang ciang kah,
been cien koe ra kongcing.
27 Kawn simex Ya-sa-long koe loe kaux,
kawn bun tix kaux.
Ceu kaux kawn bun kaux Tha-ma,
nawh kied sidaing mhawm grang heue.
28 Ya-sa-long paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ra num,
mai ang bax yaox ka simiang.
29 Nawh ploi pui hu kok ka Yaw-ya hoek,
sang nhie nawh hu krai lox mhawm ka simiang,
viang mawh nan,
Yaw-ya ang hoek.
Ya-sa-long baox ploi pui hu kok nawh,
Yaw-ya baox ang cu tix hoek.
30 Ya-sa-long tom ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Pex jhak,
kaung ma cie Yaw-ya hoik ot dex kaung ma cie aux,
ka daux mawh nghox ca pa lang mu pa kuah ot kah.
Pex hu ploi ngu gleeing kah.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom hu ploi ngu gleeing ka kaung ma an heue.
31 Yaw-ya hu ka nyiex Ya-sa-long chawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx siliex maix lai ploi ngu gleeing ka kaung ma aux lie?”
32 Ya-sa-long ah nin:
“Aux nhie pui hu kok maix,
maix ang hoek.
Aux keud tix sang kieh maix hu krai lox mhawm ka simiang tang aux,
chawk nawh yuh ka mawx nawh nhie aux lih hu khaing Ci-sux mai hoek dee in.
Aux ot dee an kawn lheu mhawm khaing ot aux dee in.
Yam in,
maix sawg bawg yam pa khaw mai kieh aux hu yaox ka simiang.
Phan aux koe phi map,
kieh simiang pauh aux yum khaing heue.”
33 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya hu ka simiang,
mai krai lox pa hoik krai Ya-sa-long ka nawh,
simiang tom ploi pui hu kok Ya-sa-long hoek.
Ya-sa-long hoek,
mai krub ka plak sivoe ngai simiang.
Simiang tom ngheet ka nawh heue.
