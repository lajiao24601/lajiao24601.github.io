Doeh ceu cad kawn simex Naw-yax loe kaux
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.5–23)
10
In plak greem mawh grawng doeh ceu pui kawn simex Naw-yax,
pa mawh Sang,
Hang mai Ya-fu.
Khaix kheem rawm,
kix loe kaux tom keh ka kawn simex.
2 Kawn simex Ya-fu mawh Keu-miiex,
Ma-keux,
Ma-taix,
Ya-wang,
Thu-pa,
Mi-seux,
mai Thi-la.a
3 Doeh ceu pui Keu-miiex mawh pui Ya-seu-ci-na,
pui Li-fax,
pui Thaw-cia-max.
4 Doeh ceu pui Ya-wang mawh pui Yi-li-sa,
pui Tha-see,
pui Ci-thix,
pui Law-teux;
5 kix mawh tax du pui pa ot ka haktiex baoh praok Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong mai daux klawn tiex an khankix.
Doeh ceu pui Ya-fu hoik tang lih koe nyiex ma,
ceu mawx ceu an,
meung mawx meung an,
mai tang siao ka lox tix heue.
6 Kawn simex Hang mawh Ku-seu,
Ai-cix,
Fu,
mai Cia-nang.
7 Doeh ceu pui Ku-seu mawh ceu pui Si-pa,
ceu pui Ha-fe-la,
ceu pui Sa-fu-tha,
ceu pui La-max,
ceu pui Sa-fu-thi-cia,
doeh ceu pui La-max mawh ceu pui see-pa mai ceu pui Ti-tang.
8 Ku-seu koe kawn simex tix kaux mawh Ling-lu,
nawh mawh pui vao pa jauh tix koe ka daux been tix kaux.
9 Mawh kheu teem coi Simiang Siyiex ka nawh,
nawh tom lih mawh pui jaw pa ting riang,
mawh kheu nan,
pui tom ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex kieh maix awm pui jaw pa ting riang Ling-lu yuh nan.”
10 Yam jauh,
meung nawh mawh khawm Pa-pi-loeng,
Yi-lix,
mai Ya-cia;
haktiex in koe daux haktiex Pa-pi-loeng.
11 Nawh jauh khaing haktiex in yuh tix vieh va plak Ya-sux,
mai sum ka veng Ni-ni-we,
veng Li-heu-paw mai veng Cia-la,
12 mai si nax veng Cia-la mai veng Ni-ni-we nawh baox sum veng ting Li-shiien.
13 Doeh ceu pui Ai-cix mawh pui Lu-tix,
pui Ya-na-mi,
pui Li-ha-pi,
pui Na-fu-thu-si,
14 pui Pha-see-lu-six,
pui Cia-see-lu-si,
pui Kheu-li-theu;
pui Fe-li-see mawh doeh ceu cad pui Kheu-li-theu.
15 Kawn simex Cia-nang mawh Si-toeng (kawn ai) mai Heux.
16 Cia-nang kawx mawh nang pui Yie-pu-see,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Keu-cia-sha,
17 pui Si-we,
pui Ya-ci,
pui Si-ni,
18 pui Ya-wa-ti,
pui Si-ma-lix,
pui Ha-ma.
Pui Cia-nang lih hu prung prang tix ot ku dauh;
19 mee haktiex kix jauh ka Si-toeng tom hoek Cia-sa mai Ci-la-eux,
khaix an plak lih singaix hoek ka Saw-to-ma,
Eu-maw-la,
Ya-ma mai Si-piien praok La-sa.
20 Doeh ceu cad Hang in kix tang ot ka ceu pui mawx ceu pui an,
meung mawx meung an,
tang siao lox si kah cao tix heue.
21 Sang mawh pux ke Ya-fu,
nawh mawh tax du kawn praix Si-pawb.
22 Kawn simex Sang mawh Yi-lang,
Ya-sux,
Ya-fa-sa,
Lu-teux mai Ya-lang.
23 Doeh ceu pui Ya-lang mawh pui Wu-see,
pui Hu-leux,
pui Ci-thiex,
pui Ma-see.
24 Ya-fa-sa keh Sa-la;
Sa-la keh Si-paw.
25 Si-paw koe kawn simex ra kaux:
tix kaux mawh Fa-leuxc,
mawh kheu ka daux baux bawg yam nawh,
pui piang haktiex hoik prung prang tix ot ku daux ku dee;
an tix kaux mawh Yaw-thang.
26 Doeh ceu pui Yaw-thang mawh pui Ya-maw-ta,
pui Sa-liex,
pui Ha-sa-ma-fe,
pui Yie-la,
27 pui Ha-to-lang,
pui Wu-sax,
pui Teu-la,
28 pui Eu-pa-lux,
pui Ya-pi-ma-lix,
pui See-pa,
29 pui A-fe,
pui Ha-fe-la,
pui Yaw-pa,
in kix khawm uik mawh doeh ceu cad Yaw-thang.
30 Kix ot ka piang gawng plak lih singaix,
jauh ka Mi-sa tom hoek ka Si-fa.
31 In kix mawh doeh ceu cad Sang pa tang ot mai ceu pui tix,
cub mawx cub an,
meung mawx meung an,
pa tang siao lox si kah cao tix heue.
32 In plak piang mawh cub ceu pui kawn simex Naw-yax loe kaux,
mai hawt tix tiiem awm ceu mawx ceu an,
cub mawx cub an.
Khaix kheem rawm,
ceu pui piang haktiex ku cub jauh tix lih khaing kawn Naw-yax heue.
