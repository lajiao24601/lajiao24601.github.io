Lox rawg buan krax teem coi
13
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix sang piawm ka aux hoek yam mawx?
Hoek leeing ju lie?
Maix ang jhak ang simi ka aux hoek yam mawx?
2 Aux kawn sang kham krax kha rhawm kha rhi miex glang,
mai kieh krax laux rhawm laux rhi leeing ngaix leeing sawm hoek tix gut ka aux?
Kawn koe miex glang,
pui koeng sang tiex tix puing ka aux?
 
3 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka aux,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox aux;
khiox khaw maix deeh tawx riang rao ka aux,
mai kieh aux yaox tix luat luan khaing krax yum.
4 Paw kieh pui pa koeng ka aux ah nin:
ex hoik pex ka kix heue!
Paw kieh kix gaux rhawm kheu pa ang aux pon pex heue.
 
5 Kheu nan,
aux een rhawm tix ka krax muh maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix;
aux sang gaux rhawm gaux rhawm rhi kheu krax pa taoh maix.
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux tag sang rhax lai ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik tawx maix buan son pa ting ka aux heue.
