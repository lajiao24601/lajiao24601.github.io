Grox krai grawng sang laux leeing nyiex buan
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.1–2; Lai Lu-cia 21.5–6)
13
Yam lih hoek Ye-sux khaing nyiex buan,
pui gau nawh tix kaux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
maix jhak,
in mawh simaox pa kied ting,
pa sum ka nyiex pa kied mhawm grang heue!”
2 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex hoek tix jhak ka nyiex pa ting lhaong pa sum in kix lie?
Simaox pa koe dee in ku lon tag sang yieh tix tik khawm uik,
mai ang lai koe simaox tix lon kawx pa deuh ot ka piang paox tix heue.”
Krax laux leeing mai krax tuk tiak tuk te
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.3–14; Lai Lu-cia 21.7–19)
3 Ye-sux ngawm ka piang gawng sime,
mai ngawm ka see nyiex buan.
Pi-teux,
Ya-keux,
Yaw-hang,
mai Ang-teu-liex briex tix chawk ka nawh ah nin:
4 “Krai ka yix,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh bawg yam mawx lie?
Yam lih kaing in tix mawh tag sang koe mhai sakse ceu pa tix lie?”
5 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw rien rhawm tix,
paw vi kieh pui pie lhien ka tix.
6 Mawh kheu koe pui tom nie tag sang pie tix hoek ka daux ceu kaux aux,
mai ah nin:
‘Aux mawh Khrit’,
mai pie lhien ka pui tom nie.
7 Paw vi yaoh yee ka mhong pex lox puing pui naing ka baoh haktiex dee dex,
mai ka haktiex dee singai.
Kaing in kix tag sang ran lih tix mawh;
viang mawh nan,
ang mawh krai hoik si dauh been.
8 Pui tix ceu mai tix ceu tag sang yuh naing ka paox tix;
tix meung mai tix meung tag sang puing ka paox tix;
kla reem,
sibhawm praix,
tag sang lih koe ku dauh ku dee.
Grawng kaing ceu in kix khawm uik tag sang awm jhawm saux yha bun yam jauh tix keh heue.
9 a“To kaux pex tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
mawh kheu pui tag sang giex ka pex,
mai tawx pex ka nyiex dee prawng see.
Pex tag sang kham krax pa si vied ka nyiex sidiex;
mawh kheu son aux pex cung ka plak sivoe ngai simiang mai simiang meung,
kham sakse son gum lox buan.
10 Viang mawh nan,
gum lox buan tag caw tix ran tawx ka kawn pui piang haktiex.
11 Yam giex kix ka pex,
yam viex pex hoek ka nyiex dee prawng see,
pex paw vi khi ka lox pa sang krai tix;
hoek yam an,
krai Siyiex ceu pa tix,
pex tom krai ceu an;
mawh kheu pa krai pex ang mawh lox to kaux pex,
mai hoik mawh lox pa tawx Khuan Singax Singeh pex krai.
12 Yam an,
pui tag sang coh ka pux aik lih tix,
mai pauh kix yum,
keeing kawx tag sang yuh nin ka kawn tix nang;
kawn kawx tag sang tieb tie ka miex keeing tix,
mai pauh kix yum.
13 bMawh kheu aux,
pui tag sang koeng ka pex.
Viang mawh nan,
pui pa pon gun yeung tom si dauh tag sang pon krax taoh heue.”
Krax laux leeing pa ting
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.15–28; Lai Lu-cia 21.20–24)
14 c“Pex tag sang yaox ka ‘to sat pa seeing brai pa yuh pui laux leeing’ an cung ka dee pa ang nawh khaw tix cung (pui pa phat tag caw tix tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox in).
Yam an,
pui pa paung ot ka Yo-thaix tag caw to mox tix ka gawng;
15 dpa ot ka piang braux nyiex,
paw vi lih,
paw vi lih teei kraung khrai ka daux nyiex tix ceu kawx.
16 Pa ot daux ma kawx,
paw vi ing teei kraung ceep tix ka daux nyiex.
17 Ka daux singaix yam an,
bun pa nghe mai bun miex pa tawx kawn tix peux tag sang kha rhawm tix heue!
18 Pex tag caw tix khiox khaw Siyiex,
paw kieh kaing in kix lih hoek bawg yam kuat praix.
19 eMawh kheu krax laux leeing ka daux singaix yam an,
hoik mawh ceu pa ang sax lih tix koe tom yam jauh Siyiex tix phan yuh haktiex,
singaix pa sang hoek kawx tag ang baox lai koe.
20 Phan ang mawh Simiang Siyiex pa klat dawt ka singaix pa laux leeing an kix,
ang koe pui pa pon klawd ju.
Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu son pui pa hoik laug nawh,
nawh hoik klat dawt ka singaix an kix heue.
21 “Phan koe pui pa krai ka pex ah nin:
‘Jhak,
Khrit ot dee in heue!
Khrit ot dee an heue!’
Paw yum rhawm ka nawh.
22 Mawh kheu Khrit pa pie lhien mai cao jhai pa pie lhien tag sang lih hoek;
kix tag sang yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
mai teei riang rao pa chix yuh tix mai pie lhien ka pui pa laug Siyiex.
23 Pex tag caw tix rien rhawm tix heue!
Aux hoik grox krai kaing in kix khawm uik ka pex heue.”
Pa lih hoek kawn kawn pui
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.29–31; Lai Lu-cia 21.25–28)
24 f“Khaix hoik luan hu singaix pa laux leeing an kix,
singaix tag sang tieh tix faik,
khix ang lai baox tawx pa riang,
25 gsimuing tag sang cawd khaing rao ma,
ka daux rao ma kawx tag sang yung yang khawm uik.
26 hBawg yam an,
kawn kawn pui tag sang lih hoek,
mai naog ka riang rao,
mai brug pai awm lih hoek ka daux min plak ngai.
27 Nawh tag sang ploi kawn cau Siyiex hu ka ku caong daog haktiex,
jauh ka daog haktiex plak in hoek ka daog haktiex plak an,
mai khrawm ceu pui pa hoik laug nawh heue.”
Gum lox pa sidiex ka khaox ruh
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.32–35; Lai Lu-cia 21.29–33)
28 “Pex tag caw gau tix tawng pa sidiex khaing khaox ruh.
Yam awn kak,
lih lhax klai pa khraox,
pex hoik tawng ka bawg yam rung praix dex sang hoek.
29 Awm an,
yam yaox pex ka sakse in kix khawm uik hoik tawng ka bawg yam dex sang hoek,
i hoik ot ka diang siviex.
30 Pex tag caw priex tix tawng,
sivoe sang yum pui baux ju in khaing haktiex,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh heue.
31 Rao ma mai haktiex tag sang grai,
viang mawh nan,
gum lox aux ang grai leeing ju heue.”
Singaix nari bawg yam an ang koe pui pa tawng
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.36–44)
32 j“Phan mawh singaix mai bawg yam nari pa tag sang lih hoek an,
ang koe pui pa tawng;
kawn cau Siyiex daux rao ma ang tawng,
kawn kawx ang tawng;
mawh ngie keeing pa tawng khaing.
33 Pex tag caw briex rhawm tix,
rien rhawm tix,
mawh kheu ang pex tawng ka nari bawg yam sang lih hoek.
34 kAwm pui tix kaux pa lih hu dee singai,
ap tix tawx kaing nyiex tix ka siliex mawh cao kuang,
guah kaing pa yuh kix ku kaux,
mai baox tawx lox nhie kix pui pau siviex pau mhawm mhawm.
35 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag rien rhawm tix,
mawh kheu ang pex tawng ka bawg yam pa sang hoek cao nyiex,
tee rien tix hoek yam pon bo,
tee rien tix hoek yam greung sawm,
tee rien tix hoek sivoe yam sang riang praix,
tee rien tix hoek khaix lih singaix.
36 Phan nawh hoek dum mawx dum an,
paw kieh nawh yaox grawng pa laig it pex.
37 Lox pa krai aux ka pex hoik mawh nang pa krai aux ka pui hun ah nin:
pex tag rien rhawm tix heue!”
