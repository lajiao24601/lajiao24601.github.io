Yie-li-mi mai sijax mukheu
11
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
2 “Maix tag caw tix nghiet sidawng ka gum lox pa koe daux sijax mukheu in,
khaix an krai pao ka pui Yo-tax mai ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
3 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex krai ah nin:
pui mawx ang priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu in,
pui an tag sang pon krax jhax lawg.
4 Yam viex aux tax du kix lih khaing daux Ai-cix,
lih khaing daux glah ngu pa cap,
aux yuh sijax mukheu in tau mai kix.
Aux nhie kix nghiet ka aux,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx aux.
Aux krai ka kix,
phan kix nghiet ka aux,
tag yaox tix mawh pui cie aux,
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix.
5 Yuh nin,
aux tom taug yuh gum lox pa hoik tiex tu aux ka tax du kix lih tix mawh,
mai tawx haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia pa paung ot ka yam in ka kix heue.”
Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh tete,
Simiang Siyiex.”
6 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex baox ah nin ka aux:
“Maix tag caw tix tauh hu ka daux veng Yo-tax ku mu,
mai ka daux laih ting laih iak pa koe daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai krai pao sijax mukheu in ka kawn pui,
kieh kix tawng gum lox pa tiiem ka daux sidawng,
mai cu nghiet ka been sijax mukheu in.
7 Jauh ka singaix yam viex aux tax du tix lih khaing Ai-cix,
aux hoik ca baox sidiex kix nghiet ka lox tix ang jud,
8 Kix ca kawn ang cu tix nghiet,
ang simi nang,
tieh tix kieh mawh rhawm rhi pa kiien,
mai rhawm rhi pa caox pa giex gang ka to kaux tix.
Aux kieh kix priex tix hawt ka lox sijax mukheu in,
viang mawh nan,
kix tieb tie kah.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux hawt tix ciyang ka kix awm been lox sijax mukheu in yuh nan heue.”
9 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tieh vheen ka aux.
10 Kix baox yuh ka phi map pa hoik yuh tax du tix,
ang nghiet ka lox aux,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux tang hak.
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax hoik yuh ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai tax du kix laux.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka kix yuh nin:
aux tag sang yuh kix laux leeing,
kix ang yaox tix luat luan.
Kix khiox khaw ka aux,
mai aux ang nghiet heue.
12 Singaix yam an,
pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang veeh tix ka siyiex pa hoik krub kix kah,
mai khiox khaw kah.
Viang mawh nan,
siyiex khankix ang doh pon taoh kix luat luan khaing krax laux leeing in.
13 Siyiex pui Yo-tax hoik haoh hun awm veng kix yuh nan;
khu pa hoik yuh pui Yie-lu-sa-leung son Pa-lix pa seeing pa brai an kawx,
hoik haoh hun awm daux laih kix yuh nan.
14 Yie-li-mi eue,
maix paw vi khiox khaw son kix,
paw vi rawg buan ka aux.
Yam si pup kix ka krax laux krax leeing,
kix khiox khaw ka aux,
mai aux kawx ang nghiet ka heue.”
15 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui cie aux pa muh aux kheu kied ting phi map kix,
kix kawn sang chix koe siawp sibrawm tix hoek ka nyiex buan aux yuh ka mawx lie?
Kix yaox hoik yuh tix krax pa ap pong,
tan lua ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
mai ce tix sang pon luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing lie?
Kix yaox hoik yuh tix kaing in,
ce tix sang pon krax gaux rhawm lie?
16 Aux hoik sax kok kix mawh kak khaox pa mhawm yeem,
khaox sime pa plix plix haoh hun,
viang mawh nan,
yam in aux tag sang awm ngu sah pa gleeing ka lhax kix uik,
raig pot ka kak kix yuh nan heue.
17 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing hoik sum ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai ka pui Yo-tax,
viang mawh nan,
yam in aux tag sang ploi krax laux krax leeing hoek piang kix.
Kix yuh pa caox pa caig,
to kaux kix hoik kieh krax laux leeing in hoek;
kix tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Pa-lix,
mai cheeik aux song rhawm heue.”
Krax pa keud tix sang pauh ka Yie-li-mi
18 Simiang Siyiex krai krax pa tieh vheen pui koeng ka aux.
19 Aux awm kawn yung iak pa cu lox,
pui tuk hu ka bang dee pauh,
nawh ang tawng krax pa tieh vheen kix ka tix.
Kix ah nin:
“Jawm yam yeem lhax khaox,
mai kid ka nawh.
Hoek heue,
ex pauh ka nawh,
kieh pui ang lai doh keud ka ceu kaux nawh heue!”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
aux rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing eue,
maix mawh simiang cao prawng pa khaw caw;
maix hoik lam lia kia ngien ka rhawm rhi kawn pui.
Aux tawx see in ka daux tawm taix maix;
khiox khaw maix kieh aux yaox grawng pa prawng rauh see maix son aux.”
21 Pui Ya-na-thu sang pauh ka aux,
kix ghlat ka aux,
ang kieh aux krai pao ka gum lox Simiang Siyiex,
phan aux ang nghiet,
kix tag sang pauh ka aux.
22 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang ciyang ka kix heue!
Pui pa nhum ju kix tag sang yum ka daux bang naing;
pui pa mawh kawn nyawm kix tag sang yum ka cai rhawm tix ka daux laih daux cau.
23 Aux hoik laug ka bawg yam sang kieh pui Ya-na-thu laux leeing.
Yam an,
ang lai koe pui pa pon tix luat luan khaing tix kaux kawx heue.”
