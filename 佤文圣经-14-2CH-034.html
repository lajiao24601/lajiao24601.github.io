Yaw-si-yax simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 22.1–2)
34
aYam koe ju Yaw-si-yax daix num nawh mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix ngoe tix num.
2 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt kaing mhawm pa yuh tax du tix simiang Ta-wue,
ang guah hu khaing daux pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex khawm uik heue.
Yaw-si-yax yuh laux ka krax krub been li tang ceu
3 Yam mawh Yaw-si-yax simiang ka daux daix num,
nawh kawn kied awn ju mai jauh tix krub ka Siyiex tax du tix Ta-wue.
Khaix hoik luan pon num,
nawh yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa ot piang gawng iak,
ka riang rong siyiex bun Ya-seu-la,
mai ka can riang rao tang ceu khankix khawm uik.
4 bSiliex nawh ka daux krax pa duk thung nawh,
mai yieh laux ka khu pa krub ka Pa-lix,
kid goh ka khu nyawng bhawng pa dex ka dee an.
Kix hoik lauh max ka riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la mai ka can riang rao tang ceu,
mai krid lih mawh nyaox,
khaix an rhuat ka piang si meeig pui pa tan Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka riang rao heue.
5 cNawh teei si-ang kaing cao giex kaing li tang ceu mai deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan kix gleeing kah.
Yuh nin,
nawh baox kieh Yo-tax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung singax singeh.
6 Nawh ot ka veng ku mu mai ka Ma-na-si,
Yi-fa-liien,
Si-miien,
khawm Na-fu-tha-lix pa singai ot ka daog plak laong khankix ka haktiex pa hoik duk dig pui mai hawt tix yuh nan nang.
7 Nawh hoik yuh laux ka khu pa koe ka meung plak laong khawm uik,
riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la,
mai riang rao tang ceu khankix,
mai krid lih mawh nyaox,
mai baox lauh max ka can khu bhawng khawm uik,
khaix an deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Yaox ka lai lox tat jhawm prawng
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 22.3–20)
8 Yam giex Yaw-si-yax kaing ka daux kao daix num,
khaix hoik kieh meung mai nyiex buan singax singeh,
mai khaix hoik naox ka pa krub been li tang ceu,
nawh ploi pui loe kaux cox ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh nyiex buan Siyiex nawh.
Pui in kix loe kaux mawh:
kawn simex Ya-sa-li-ya pa kaux Sa-fang,
simiang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa kaux Ma-si-ya mai kawn simex Yaw-ha-see pa kaux Yaw-yax cao kaing pui koh mau.
9 Pui Li-we cao pa pau diang siviex nyiex buan,
teei tix tawx mau pa pon khrawm hoek ka daux nyiex buan ka Si-leu-cia cao giex kaing li pa ting.
Mau in kix hoik mawh pa khrawm khaing Yi-fa-liien,
Ma-na-si,
mai pui pa deuk ka meung plak laong khankix;
mai mawh nang pa khrawm khaing pui hun Yo-tax,
pui Piien-ya-ming,
mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix.
10 Kix deeh teei tix tawx mau in ka pui an kix loe kaux pa hoik koe beeh glawm tix cox ka nyiex buan,
mai kieh kix mawh pa tawx mau in ka
11 pui pa yuh cang mai khaox mai ka pui pa yuh kaing khankix,
hu liag simaox,
khaox ox hoek cox ka dee laux nyiex buan kah,
pa mawh dee hoik kieh simiang Yo-tax laux mai ang jhak simi kah.
12 Pui pa yuh kaing in hoik mawh pui pa kied kaux sijax;
pui Li-we cao pa kuan jhak ka kaing pon kaux mawh:
ceu cad cub nyiex Mi-la-lix pa kaux Ya-ha mai Eu-pa-tix,
khawm Sa-cia-li-yax ceu cad cub nyiex Keu-siax,
mai Mi-su-lang.
Pui Li-we hoik mawh pui pa kied mhawm ah lox rhax.
13 Pui Li-we tang kaux khankix mawh pa koe beeh glawm tix bluh ka kraung siao mai cao pa kuang kia ka kaing ku ceu;
pui pa deuk khankix,
tix blah mawh cao pa priex kraung,
tix blah mawh cao pa tiiem lai,
mai tix blah mawh cao pa pau diang siviex heue.
14 Yam teei mau lih khaing daux nyiex krao mau,
Si-leu-cia cao giex kaing li yaox lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh lai lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx Siyiex ka Maw-si.
15 Si-leu-cia ah nin ka Sa-fang:
“Aux yaox lai lox tat jhawm prawng ka daux nyiex buan.”
Si-leu-cia tawx lai an ka Sa-fang,
16 Sa-fang viex hoek ka simiang dee an,
mai krai ah nin ka simiang:
“Gum lox pa tawx maix yix yuh,
yix hoik hawt tix yuh.
17 Yix hoik tawx mau daux nyiex buan ka cao pa kuang kaing mai ka pui pa yuh kaing khankix.”
18 Sa-fang baox ah nin:
“Si-leu-cia tawx lai ka aux tix phuk.”
Khaix an nawh tom phat tom ting ka plak sivoe ngai simiang heue.
19 Simiang mhong ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai si yaung riah kraung ceep tix griah.
20 Tom tawx lox ka Si-leu-cia,
ka Ya-si-kang kawn simex Sa-fang,
ka Ya-pi-toengd kawn simex Mi-kai-yax,
ka Sa-fang cao tiiem lai daux nyiex haw simiang,
mai ka Ya-sha-ya pui cau simiang.
Nawh ah nin kix:
21 “Pex hu khiox khaw chawk cheng Simiang Siyiex son aux,
mai son pui hun pa ca kawn ot ka daux Yi-seu-liex mai ka daux Yo-tax,
si kaw tix yaox ka gum lox pa sidiex phuk lai in mawh pa yuh ka mawx.
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka aux mawh kheu ang tax du ex bax nghiet ka lox nawh,
ang bax hawt tix yuh ka pa hoik tiiem tix ot daux lai in.”
22 Mawh kheu nan,
Si-leu-cia mai pui tang kaux ra loe kaux khankix hawt awm lox pa tawx simiang ka kix,
hu khiox khaw chawk cheng ka bun tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Hu-leu-tax cao jhai bun,
cao jhai bun in paung ot ka haktiex pa khraox Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Mex nawh Sa-long mawh kawn simex Theu-wa,
kawn saux Ha-eu-ha-see,
cao pa kuang ka kraung ceep pui pa yuh kaing ka nyiex buan.
Kix krai bun an mhong grawng kaing pa lih tix mawh.
23 Bun an nhie kix deeh ing ka simiang,
24 mai deeh krai lox Simiang Siyiex ka simiang ah nin:
“Aux sang hawt tix yuh awm lox jhax lawg pa hoik mhong simiang pa tiiem tix ot daux lai an,
ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka can kawn pui cie nawh.
25 Kix hoik tik vud ka aux,
gleeing nyawng bhawng ka siyiex tang kaux,
mai cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm ka riang rao pa yuh tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag song rhawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ang jud ang jai.
26 “Phan mawh to kaux aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix hoik mhong lox pa tiiem ka daux lai,
27 mai hoik koe krax pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai hoik loh rhawm rhi tix.
Maix hoik nghiet lox pa ne raw aux,
son sang ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka pui cie nawh,
mai tom riah kraung ceep tix griah,
mai yiiem ka aux.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux hoik mhong lox rawg maix buan.
28 Sivoe sang yum maix,
aux ang nyang ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
mai aux tag sang kieh maix lih hu khaing haktiex ka daux krax pa nhim heue.’ ”
Pui in kix tom viex lox in deeh ing ka simiang Yaw-si-yax heue.
Yaw-si-yax yuh sijax mukheu son sang nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 23.1–20)
29 Yaw-si-yax khrawm can cao kaing Yo-tax mai simiang Yie-lu-sa-leung,
30 hoek tau ka nyiex buan.
Kawn koe cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
mai can kawn pui tang kaux,
mag mi ang ah,
hot shiah ang ah,
hoek ka nyiex buan khawm uik.
Simiang phat lai sijax mukheu pa yaox kix ka daux nyiex buan tom ting ka plak sivoe ngai kix.
31 Simiang cung ka praok rong ting,e
mai yuh sijax mukheu ka Simiang Siyiex,
son sang nghiet ka lox nawh,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap lox tat jhawm prawng nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai hawt tix yuh ka gum lox pa hoik tiiem tix deuh ka daux lai an.
32 Nawh hoik kieh pui Piien-ya-ming mai can pui pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik ap tix priex hawt ka sijax mukheu in.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh kix tau mai Siyiex tax du kix.
33 Yaw-si-yax hoik yieh laux ka can riang rao pa yum koeng pa koe daux haktiex khawm uik,
mai nhie pui Yi-seu-liex krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
Yam kawn im ju nawh ka piang haktiex,
kix yum rhawm hawt khaix Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix heue.
