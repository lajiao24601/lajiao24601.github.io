Lox rhax Ti-paw-la mai Pa-la
5
Ka daux singaix an,
Ti-paw-la mai kawn simex Ya-pi-naw-ang pa kaux Pa-la rhax ka lai rhax in ah nin:
 
2 Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex!
Pui Yi-seu-liex but rhawm tix yuh ka naing;
kawn pui gaux rhawm tix yuh pui naing.
3 Simiang eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet!
Simiang eue,
daong yhaok tix nghiet!
Aux sang tauh ting rhax lai ka Simiang Siyiex,
sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yam taug lih hu maix khaing gawng Si-eu,
yam taug lih maix khaing Yi-tong
haktiex yung yang,
lhiex lih khaing rao ma,
mai pu pai awm hieh ka rawm lhiex.
5 aKa plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ka gawng Si-nai,
gawng lua hoik yung yang khawm uik.
 
6 Ka daux baux ju Sang-cia kawn praix Ya-na,
ka daux singaix bawg yam Ya-yix,
pui pa yuh lai gee ang lai laig ka dee in,
mai nyo si nie hoik via krax khawm uik heue.
7 Ti-paw-la eue,
yaong veng ot pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tik ghram kah,
mai ang koe pui tix kaux kawx,
tom hoik hoek maix;
maix hoek tix yuh miex pui Yi-seu-liex.
8 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik loh ka siyiex,
ka daux haktiex kix tom yuh naing.
Ka daux ceu pui naing Yi-seu-liex pon mheen kaux
Pie dieb tie naok tix khlawp mai pliah tix gawng ang lai koe.
9 Daux rhawm aux sang ot plak cao bung kaing naing pui Yi-seu-liex,
Ot plak pui naing Yi-seu-liex.
Yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex!
10 Pex pa brug ka la paing,
pa ngawm ka piang an brung,
Pa hu ka caong tix,
tag caw tix krai lao hu.
11 Nghiet heue,
bun miex pa ot tix kin rawm ka praok dung khankix,
sidiex ka lox pa gaux rhawm;
kix krai ka grawng pa puing pex Simiang Siyiex,
yuh krax gaux rhawm son pa pex pui Yi-seu-liex.
 
Pui ceu Simiang Siyiex rhax lox pa yaog yaw hu plak diang siviex veng ah nin:
12 Ti-paw-la eue,
kaoh heue!
Kaoh heue!
Rhax lai,
hu plak sivoe ngai!
Pa-la kawn simex Ya-pi-naw-ang eue!
Kaoh heue!
Viex pui pa laung maix hu!
13 Pui pa kaux sijax hu ka simiang kix;
pui cie Simiang Siyiex hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawhb,
mai rien khre tix yuh ka naing.
14 Kix kaoh khaing Yi-fa-liienc,
laig ka daux ngawdd,
hawt khaix ceu pui Piien-ya-ming.
Koe cao bung kaing naing kaoh khaing Ma-ciex;
koe simiang naing lih hoek khaing Si-pu-loeng.
15 Simiang Yi-sa-cia hoek tau mai Ti-paw-la;
Ya-sa-cia hoek,
Pa-la kawx hoek;
kix hoik laig tau mai nawh ka daux ngawd.
Viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi ceu pui Lui-piien ang khaw paox;
kix ang pon but rhawm tix.
16 Yuh ka mawx kix lai kaog tix ot plak khaix phung yung lie?
Khex sang nghiet ka lox pa kok yo cao liang yung lie?
Mawh tete,
rhawm rhi ceu pui Lui-piien ang khaw paox;
kix ang pon but rhawm tix.
17 Ceu pui Cia-teux ot ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix;
ceu pui Tang ot ka piang reu.
Ceu pui Ya-seux paung ot ka praok rawm sigrit sigra;
kix kaog ot tix laux lee ka praok rawm.
18 Viang mawh nan,
pui Si-pu-loeng pon tawx ka ju tix;
pui Na-fu-tha-lix pon sau riang tix ka daux bang naing.
 
19 Ka praok haktiex Tha-na dee keet rawm klawng Mi-cie-to,
simiang khawm uik hoik hoek tix puing naing.
Simiang Cia-nang khankix puing paox tix;
kix ang viex ka mau hu.
20 Simuing pon ngawb cao cao kaoh khaing rao ma teem coi ka kix,
Tiang ka rao ma mai puing ka Si-si-la.
21 Rawm klawng Ci-suing hoik pu pak,
mai chun kix hu.
Aux sang hu sivoe,
mai sau riang tix hu sivoe!
22 Yam an,
brung kluing yaog tix pu,
Lox luah gip yam to ah tata yuh nan.
 
23 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
tag sang jhax lawg Mi-law-see,
Tag sang jhax lawg ka pui pa ot tan tom kin.
Kix ang bax hoek teem ka Simiang Siyiex,
ang teem nawh puing pui pa koeng khankix.
 
24 Ya-yix moeng Si-pai ceu pui Ci-ni,
mawh pa khaw tix pon min plak ngai ka daux paox bun miex tix khankix;
mawh pa khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw ka daux paox bun miex nyiex tix khankix.
25 Si-si-la mhaing tix nyaux rawm ka nawh,
nawh tawx nawh nyaux rawm teeh moi;
nawh teei kawk pa ngawh,
mai sau nawh nyaux rawm teeh kah.
26 Taix Ya-yix tix khawn giex ka jim khaox nyiex gox,
an tix khawn giex ka daix cang mai;
nawh lauh ka Si-si-la,
mai tawk kaing max,
phlat ka dawng re nawh heue.
27 Si-si-la gup kaux tix goh,
mai it ka greem caong Ya-yix;
Si-si-la gup kaux tix,
mai goh yum piang tiex.
 
28 Miex Si-si-la kok jhak daux doh plak praix;
Nawh ang chix sidawng jhake khaing plak khaix daux doh.
Nawh chawk ah nin:
yuh ka mawx ling law naing nawh lai ang hoek yuh nin lie?
Yuh ka mawx brung naing nawh kawn ang bax chix hoek lie?
29 Bun pa lhak lhiao ka daux nyiex haw nawh khankix pawk lox ah nin:
Nawh kawx tang su shiep ah nin:
30 Kix tee rien tix tud ka kraung pui,
mai guah paox tix cie,
tix kaux pui naing pon tix guah tix cie bun tix kaux bleeh ra kaux.
Si-si-la pon tix cie ka sibex pa mhawm rawm siang;
nawh viex man koe breeh pa fua ngawg hoek ka bun simiangf.
 
31 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kieh pui pa koeng ka maix khankix yum yuh nan nang heue!
Kieh paox grawm maix khankix jhan riang awm singaix heue!
 
Kheu nan,
haktiex in hoik yien yawg tipon num heue.
