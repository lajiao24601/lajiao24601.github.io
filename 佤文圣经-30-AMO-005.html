Kok yo pui Yi-seu-liex loh rhawm rhi tix
5
Nghiet heue,
pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux tag sang rhax lai yiiem doe son maix tix ceu ah nin:
2 bun krih Yi-seu-liex hoik goh,
mai ang lai baox pon kaoh heue!
Nawh goh si blat tix piang tiex,
mai ang koe pui pa pon yaog nawh kaoh.
3 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Veng Yi-seu-liex,
tawx pui naing hu tix rheng kaux,
mai deuk ngie tix yieh kaux pa klawd tix deeh hoek khaing;
tawx pui hu tix yieh kaux,
mai deuk ngie kao kaux pa hoek khaing heue.”
4 Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Tag caw tix sawg ka aux tom taug yaox tix klawd ju.
5 Paw vi hoek yuh krax krub ka Piie-see-pa.
Paw vi hoek sawg aux ka Paw-theu-lix;
Paw-theu-lix tag ang mawh tid tix.
Paw vi hu ka Cie-cia;
pui Cie-cia chix vuk vied vuk viang.”
6 Tag caw tix sawg ka Simiang Siyiex tom taug yaox tix klawd ju.
Phan pex ang hu sawg ka nawh,
nawh tag sang awm ngu gleeing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Ngu in tag sang gleeing ka pui haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
mai ang koe pui pa pon preet yeet.
7 Pex tag sang yaox krax laux krax leeing heue!
Mawh kheu kieh pex krax rauh krax see lih mawh krax kawk krax vawk,
mai hoik nieb pon teei ka siawp sibrawm pui khankix heue!
 
8 aSimiang Siyiex hoik phan yuh ka simuing,
hoik phan yuh ka simuing khi max mai simuing grawng lat.
Nawh kieh pon singaix tieh tix lih mawh pon sawm,
mai kieh pa faik tieh tix lih mawh pa riang.
Nawh tawx lox ka rawm sigrit sigra,
hieh ka piang haktiex.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua.
9 Nawh kieh pui kua si pup ka krax laux krax leeing,
kieh pui yieh tab laux leeing heue.
 
10 Pex pui in kix koeng khoi ka pui yuh pa khaw caw ka daux nyiex prawng see,
rauh see mai kied singax rhawm rhi.
11 Pex gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai tud rhim ka pa ih pa nyaux kix;
mawh kheu nan pex doh pon paung ot ka nyiex lhaong pa hoik teei pex simaox sum kah,
mai ang pon nyaux nang ka plai pa hoik yuh pex ka si peem plix siiet pa mhawm cie tix heue.
12 Phi map pex kied ting,
see krax pa phit pex kied nie.
Pex hoik gut kheem ka pui mhawm,
rieb ka mau chun praok,
mai tieb tie pui hot pui sibhawm ang kieh pon krax pa khaw caw daux nyiex prawng see.
13 Ka daux baux singax pa caox pa caig yuh nin,
pui lhak pui lhiao rub ang pon krai lox heue!
14 Tag caw tix leue ka pa mhawm pa mhiem,
tik ka pa caox pa caig,
mai pex tom taug pon yaox tix klawd ju.
Yuh nin,
hawt awm lox pa hoik ah pex,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
tom taug ot tau mai pex tete.
15 Tag caw tix koeng ka pa caox pa caig,
muh ka pa mhawm pa mhiem,
mai yuh krax pa rauh pa see ka daux nyiex prawng see.
Yuh nin,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing tom tag chix tawx krax pa caox rhawm tix ka pui pa kawn deuk daux meung in heue.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
“Ka daux laih hoik mawh lox yiiem pui khawm uik.
Pui pa yiiem tau mai kix,
pui hoik uik teei mawh pui cang khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
ca hu teei pui yuh kaing hoek tix yiiem.
17 Ka daux si peem plix siiet hoik mhong ngie ka lox yiiem pui khaing.
Kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh tete,
mawh kheu tag sang ciyang aux ka pex.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
18 Pui pa krox jun bawg yam singaix cie Simiang Siyiex lih hoek eue,
pex laux leeing heue!
Bawg yam singaix cie Simiang Siyiex koe dee mhawm ka pex yuh ka mawx lie?
Bawg yam singaix cie Simiang Siyiex hoik awm ngie pa faik ka pex,
ang mawh pa riang.
19 Yam lih hoek bawg yam singaix an,
pex ang yaox dee sang to mox tix,
awm pui tix kaux pa taug khraox mox tix ka sivai sang si,
mai baox si pup ka krih yuh nan heue!
Baox awm pui tix kaux ing ka nyiex tix,
hoik uik riang rao,
mai neung taix tix ka piang guh ding,
mai si uing pa ot daux guh ding tuh ka nawh heue!
20 Bawg yam singaix cie Simiang Siyiex tag sang viex pa faik,
ang mawh pa riang;
an hoik mawh singaix pa yaox krax pa laux leeing,
ang lai koe krax gaux rhawm pie ceeix kawx heue.
21 bSimiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Aux ang rhawm yaox ka poi pa yuh pex,
mai ang lai pon kham grawng krax gaux rhawm pa yuh pex heue!
22 Aux ang rieb ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi pa yuh pex,
mai ang yuh tid tix ka khawng tan pa kluing pa kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim pa tan pex.
23 Aux ang khaw rhawm ka lox rhax pex pa lawh yuh nan,
mai ang muh nang tix nghiet ka lox ting tauh pex.
24 Tete te lai,
pex khaw tix buing tix lih hu awm rawm klawng ting yuh nan,
mai sau riang tix yuh pa rauh pa see!
Kieh awm rawm klawng pa ang chix hit yuh nan,
mai yuh kaing pa rauh pa see tom si dauh heue!
25 c“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
yam ot pex daux tiag daux tu ka daux tipon num an,
hoik cheu pex tix yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai krax tan tang ceu ka aux yam mawx lie?
26 Yam in pex tag sang klawm ka riang rao pa saik to kaux tix,
pa mawh riang siyiex simiang Sha-kux mai riang simuing siyiex Cia-wuing,
vuk vied vuk viang tau.
27 Aux tag sang grheu pex hu ka haktiex Ta-ma-see-keux pa ot plak laong pa singai heue.”
Siyiex simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin;
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua heue.
