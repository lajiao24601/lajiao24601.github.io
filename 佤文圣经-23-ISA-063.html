Simiang Siyiex hoik puing pex ka meung piang haktiex
63
a“Pa kaoh khaing haktiex Yi-tong veng Paw-see-la an mawh mawx?
Pa ceep ka kraung dha pa mhawm an mawh mawx?
Pa preeng tix hu sivoe ngai pa koe riang koe rao an mawh mawx?”
“Nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex cao taoh pa ting siawp sibrawm;
nawh hoek tix krai pao grawng pa hoik puing pex tix heue.”
2 “Yuh ka mawx nawh lai ceep ka kraung dha pa raoh rawm siang,
mai awm pui pa dig tix yuh ka plai plix siiet yuh nan lie?”
3 bSimiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux dug dig ka meung piang haktiex,
mai awm dig ka plix siiet yuh nan;
aux ang siao lai lo pui teem coi tix.
Aux hoik dug dig kix ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix,
mai hoik yuh kix laux leeing;
nham kix hoik phrit ka kraung ceep aux.
4 Aux hoik dawg but ka rhawm rhi tix,
ka daux singaix pa taoh aux ka pui cie tix hoik hoek;
singaix pa ciyang aux ka pui pa koeng ka pui cie aux khankix hoik hoek.
5 cAux daox deng kah,
yuh ka mawx lai ang koe pui pa hoek teem coi ka aux,
hoek joh jhan ka aux lie?
Krax song rhawm aux kieh aux nie koe riang koe rao;
aux hoik hoek tix yuh naing.
6 Krax song rhawm aux dug dig ka meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai hoik yuh kix laux leeing;
aux hoik kieh nham kix jauh lih mawh rawm klawng heue.”
Siyiex chix kia ngien ka Yi-seu-liex
7 Aux tag sang krai lao ka krax muh Simiang Siyiex;
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh ka piang to kaux ex.
Nawh hoik koe krax muh krax rhawm mai krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix,
nawh hoik tawx buan son pa ting ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kix rub hoik mawh pui cie aux tete;
kix ang doh pie lhien.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh hoik taoh ka kix.
9 Yam ot kix ka daux krax kha rhawm,
nawh kawx hoik kha rhawm tix.
Pa taoh ka kix ang mawh kawn cau Siyiex,
mai hoik mawh to kaux Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu krax muh nawh mai krax pa caox rhawm nawh,
nawh hoik taoh ka kix.
Nawh hoik chix kia ngien ka kix ku jheeng jheeng,
10 viang mawh nan,
kix tieh vheen ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh laux rhawm laux rhi.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom lih tix mawh pa koeng ka kix,
mai tieb tie ka kix heue.
11 Viang mawh nan,
kixd ca kawn doh keud ka kaing pa hoik luan,
doh keud ka bawg yam singaix Maw-si siliex Simiang Siyiex.
Kix chawk ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ot dee mawx lie?
Nawh hoik sax taoh ka pui cie tix mai ka simiang pui cie tix khaing daux rawm sigrit sigra.
Yam in,
Simiang Siyiex pa hoik tawx Khuan Singax Singeh tix ka Maw-si ot dee mawx lie?
12-13 eNawh hoik sax teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix,
mai yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka daux Maw-si,
mai kieh rawm tang guah tix,
mai thung pui cie tix tiang ka rawm sigrit sigra pa raux,
kieh kawn pui doh keud ka ceu kaux tix leeing ju leeing phan.
Yam in nawh ot dee mawx lie?”
Simiang Siyiex hoik sax thung ka pui cie tix,
mai kieh caong kix kaux cong,
awm brung pa ang sax chix goh ka daux lung yuh nan.
14 Awm viex phung moi laig ka daux ngawd yuh nan,
mai khuan Simiang Siyiex kieh kix laux lee tix.
Nawh thung ka kix,
mai kieh ceu kaux tix pon krax yaog yaw heue.
Lox rawg buan son krax caox rhawm mai krax teem coi
15 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kia ngien aux khaing rao ma;
khiox khaw maix simi tix jhak aux khaing daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
khaing daux nyiex pa koe min plak ngai.
Krax muh maix mai krax pa caox rhawm maix ot dee mawx lie?
Khiox khaw maix paw vi ang jhak tix simi ka yix liak.
16 Maix hoik mawh keeing ka yix.
Tax du yix Ya-paw-la-hang,
Ya-keux hoik ang lai yeung ka yix;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik mawh keeing ka yix,
jauh tom dix dix hoek tom yam in maix hoik mawh cao taoh yix heue.
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh yix lih hu khaing daux krax cie maix lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh yix kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
ang lhat graung yung yi ka maix lie?
Son pui pa yuh kaing jhaw maix tom si dauh,
son pui pa hoik dawg mawh pui cie maix khankix,
khiox khaw maix deeh veeh rhawm rhi tix ka yix liak.
18 Yix hoik mawh pui cie maix pa singax singeh.
Pui koeng hoik grheu yix hu;
kix hoik dug dig ka nyiex pa singax singeh maix.
19 Maix jhak ka yix,
awm pa ang sax kuang kia ka yix yuh nan,
awm pa ang yix sax mawh pui cie maix yuh nan heue.
