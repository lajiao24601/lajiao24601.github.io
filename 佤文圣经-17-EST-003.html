Ha-mang keud tix sang pauh ka pui Yo-thaix yum
3
Khaix hoik luan tix bawg yam,
simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu ghaok pui Ya-ciaa kawn simex Ha-mi-ta-tha pa kaux Ha-mang mawh cao kuang ka daux meung.
2 Simiang tawx lox ka cao pa ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang khawm uik,
kieh kix krub ka Ha-mang,
kieh mawh krax yung yi;
kix tom nghiet ka lox pa tawx simiang khawm uik,
mawh ngie Maw-ti-kai pa thiang kah.
3 Cao kaing ka daux nyiex haw simiang tang kaux chawk nawh yuh ka mawx ang nghiet ka lox simiang.
4 Kix nhie nawh tieh rhawm keud tix ku singaix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh kawx ang cu.
Khaix an,
nawh tom mox tix krai ka kix ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui Yo-thaix,
aux ang chix krub ka Ha-mang.”
Kheu nan,
kix tom hu krai ka Ha-mang,
mai jhak nawh chix ang chix gun yeung krax yuh Maw-ti-kai.
5 Ha-mang hoik tawng ang Maw-ti-kai krub ka tix,
kied song rhawm kah.
6 Yam baox tawng nawh mawh Maw-ti-kai pui Yo-thaix,
tom ap tix ang ciyang ka Maw-ti-kai nong nong;
nawh keud tix sang pauh ka pui Yo-thaix khawm uik ka daux meung Ya-ha-sui-lu.
7 Yam yuh Ya-ha-sui-lu simiang ka daux kao ra num khix tix,
pa mawh Khix Ni-sa,
Ha-mang tawx lox kieh pui cawk lai sivoe tix (baox kok mawh cawk Phu-eu) laug singaix yuh lih rhawm pa keud nawh,
krax pa cawk lai mawh khix kao ra (khix Ya-ta) singaix kao loe.
8 Kheu nan,
Ha-mang krai ka simiang:
“Koe pui tix ceu ot ka daux meung haktiex ku dauh.
Kix tang koe been,
ang awm ka pui tang ceu;
viang mawh nan,
kix kawx ang yung yi ka lox tat jhawm prawng meung.
Gun yeung pui ceu in kix ang mhawm son Vawng.
9 Phan Vawng keud mawh pa khaw,
khiox khaw tawx lox kieh kix grai ka khawm uik.
Aux chix tix kieh mau ot ka daux krao meung baox tix tix ngoe pon mheen kongcing,
kieh mawh mau pa siao bawg yuh kaing ka daux meung.”
10 Khaix an,
simiang teei cum giien tix mai tawx ka pui pa koeng ka pui Yo-thaix ceu pui Ya-cia kawn simex Ha-mi-ta-tha pa kaux Ha-mang.
11 Simiang ah nin ka nawh:
“Pui in kix mai mau kix tag mawh cie maix,
maix sang yuh ka mawx maix chix tix yuh!”
12 Khix tix kao ngaix loe,
Ha-mang tom kok ka cao tiiem lai simiang hoek,
kieh kix tawx ka lai,
mai siao ka ngai lai pa tawng pui ku ceu ka daux meung haktiex ku dauh,
mai tawx ka can simiang haktiex,
cao kuang kaing,
mai cao kaing khawm uik.
Kaing in mawh pa tiiem ka ceu min simiang,
mai tawk ka cum giien simiang.
13 Pui pa tawx lai in tawx ka haktiex ku dauh ka daux meung,
tawx lox sang pauh pui Yo-thaix khawm uik ka daux Khix Ya-ta kao ngaix loe,
simex bun ang ah,
ang kuang kawn nyawm pui kuad,
sang pauh ka khawm uik,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx,
mai sang teei ka kraung kix.
14 Grawng ka daux lai sang krai ka pui khawm uik ka daux haktiex ku dauh,
pui ku kaux son sang tag rien rhawm tix ka daux singaix an heue.
15 Hawt ka lox pa tawx simiang,
lai in tag sang krai ka daux veng ting ot simiang Su-sang;
pui pa tawx lai in kawx nied tix tawx lai hu ka daux haktiex ku dauh.
Yam suk sag pui ka daux veng Su-sang,
simiang mai Ha-mang caw tix nyaux ka plai heue.
