Prawng ca ka phi map Yie-lu-sa-leung
11
Khuan Siyiex yaog ka aux,
mai viex aux hoek ka diang siviex nyiex buan plak lih singaix.
Aux dex ka diang siviex plak lih singaix,
mai yaox pui tix nga phuan kaux,
ka daux kix koe simiang pui haktiex ra kaux,
pa mawh kawn simex Ya-sawx pa kaux Ya-sha-ni-yax mai kawn simex Pi-na-ya pa kaux Pi-la-thi heue.
2 Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
pui in kix mawh pui pa keud tix yuh pa caox pa caig daux veng,
koe krax keud pa pie lhien ang mhawm,
3 kix ah nin:
‘Ang rheeng ex tag sang deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma tixa.
Veng in awm aw yuh nan,
ex awm nex ka daux aw,
mai pon krax pa teem coi aw heue.’
4 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw kix ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun heue.”
5 Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang aux dum mawx dum an.
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux,
mai nhie aux krai gum lox in ka kawn pui ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
lox pa krai pex khaing jheeh tix,
pa keud pex daux rhawm tix,
aux hoik tawng ka khawm uik.
6 Pex hoik pauh pui yum tom nie ka daux veng in,
mai to kaux yum pui hoik pum tix naog ka daux laih khawm uik heue.
7 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
mawh tete,
veng in hoik mawh aw;
nex pa koe daux aw mawh to kaux yum pui pa pauh pex.
Pex tag ang yaox tix ot daux veng in,
aux tag sang tik pex ot plak praix veng.
8 Pex hoik lhat graung ka vaig gawn lie?
Aux tag sang teei vaig gawn mug pex kah.
9 Aux tag sang viex pex hoek ka plak praix veng,
mai ap pex ka daux tawm taix pui meung tang meung.
Aux hoik prawng pex yum.
10 Pex tag sang yum ka naing daux haktiex ot tix.
Yuh nin pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
11 Veng in ang doh pon teem coi ka pex,
awm aw chix teem coi ka nex pa ot daux aw yuh nan.
Viang pex ot ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex dee mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang ciyang ka pex khawm uik.
12 Pex tag caw tix tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
Pex hu hawt ka been krax ot krax koe meung pa ot sivie siveng pex,
mai in hoik mawh pa lud loe ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
pa lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx aux heue.”
13 Yam kawn krai aux ka lox jhai,
kawn simex Pi-na-ya pa kaux Pi-la-thi hoik goh tix yum dum mawx dum an.
Aux tom gup tix krub piang tiex,
mai kok yo lox pa ting si num ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
maix tag sang yuh pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix laux leeing khawm uik lie?”
Siyiex deuh lox son pui pa vuk vied vuk viang khankix
14 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux;
15 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
pui veng Yie-lu-sa-leung krai lao ka grawng maix,
mai ka grawng paox ceu paox cad pui Yi-seu-liex pa vuk vied vuk viang pa hu tau mai maix khankix.
Kix ah nin:
‘Paox ceu paox cad pa vuk vied vuk viang an kix,
hoik lih hu singai khaing nyiex pa singax Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx haktiex in ka yix heue.’
16 “Viang mawh nan,
maix tag caw tix krai lox aux ka paox ceu paox cad pa vuk vied vuk viang tau khankix.
Viang aux hoik grheu kix hu ka meung piang haktiex pa ot dee singai,
mai kieh kix prung prang tix ot ka haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
Viang mawh nan,
aux kawn sang ot tau mai kix ka daux haktiex pa vuk vied vuk viang kix,
mai mawh nyiex pa singax singeh kix tix vud tix cu heue.
17 “Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix krai lox pa krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka kix.
Aux tag sang thung kix lih hu khaing meung haktiex pa hoik kieh aux kix prung prang tix ot daux ku meung khankix,
mai khrawm kix ot tau;
aux tag sang deeh tawx haktiex Yi-seu-liex ka kix.
18 Khaix hoik deeh hoek kix ka haktiex tix,
mai tag caw tix teei tik ka riang rao pa yum koeng pui khawm uik kah.
19 bAux tag sang tawx khuan pa khraox,
rhawm pa khraox ka kix.
Aux tag sang teei tix tik ka simaox pa kiien khaing daux rhawm rhi kix an,
mai deeh tawx kix koe rhawm pa koe nex koe nham,
pa chix cu nghiet ka kix.
20 Yuh nin,
kix tom taug chix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
mai cu nghiet ka lox tawx aux khawm uik.
Kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux;
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix.
21 Phan mawh pui an kix pa muh tix krub ka riang rao pa yum koeng an,
aux tag sang hawt tix ciyang ka kix awm kaing pa yuh kix.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Min plak ngai Siyiex lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung
22 cCi-lu-paw yaog tix pu,
jaong ling an hawt khaix kix;
min plak ngai Siyiex Yi-seu-liex ot ka piang kix.
23 Min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex haok rao ma,
mai lih hu khaing veng,
kaog ot ka piang gawng pa ot ka praok veng plak lih singaix an.
24 Ka daux riang su sai,
khuan Simiang Siyiex yaog aux haok,
mai viex aux hoek ka paox ceu paox cad pa vuk vied vuk viang ka haktiex Pa-pi-loeng dee an.
Khaix an riang su sai grai.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai gum lox pa nhie Simiang Siyiex aux krai ka paox ceu paox cad pa vuk vied vuk viang khawm uik heue.
