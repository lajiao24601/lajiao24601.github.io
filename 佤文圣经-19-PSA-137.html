Pui Yi-seu-liex pa vuk vied vuk viang keud ce ka Shi-ang
137
Yix ngawm ka praok rawm klawng Pa-pi-loeng,
yam keud ka Shi-ang,
mai ang lai pon gun lox yiiem tix heue.
2 Ka piang khaox si glai pa ot ka praok klawng,
yix hoik vhak ting jung lah ot kah.
3 Pui pa giex laung ka yix kieh yix rhax ka lox rhax;
pui pa tuk tiak ka yix kieh yix yuh ka krax klieh.
Kix ah nin:
hoek,
rhax lox rhax Shi-ang son yix heue!
 
4 To kaux yix ot ka daux meung pui tang ceu tang cad,
yix sang chix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx lie?
5 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
phan aux hoik piawm ka maix,
kieh taix khawn dawm aux kroh vhit,
son ang sang lai pon tauh ka ting heue!
6 Phan aux ang lai doh keud ka maix,
phan aux ang kieh Yie-lu-sa-leung
mawh krax pa ting gaux rhawm ka aux,
kieh dak aux lih tix kiien,
son ang sang lai pon rhax ka lox rhax heue!
 
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka krax pa ang khaw ang jiet pa yuh pui Yi-tong.
Singaix yam laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kix hoik kok yo ah nin:
yieh laux heue!
Yieh laux heue!
Kieh haktiex in si dawm heue!
8 aPa-pi-loeng eue,
maix tag sang laux leeing;
hawt tix pawk bix ka pui cie maix awm hoik yuh maix krax pa cau nyhau ka yix,
nawh kied sidaing koe son heue!
9 Pui pa giex ka kawn ngie maix,
mai si blat ka piang simaox,
nawh kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
