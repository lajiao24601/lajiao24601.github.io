41
aKa daux num an khix aliah,
ceu pui nyiex ma simiang tix kaux,
pa hoik mawh nang simiang tix kaux,
kawn saux Yi-li-sa-ma,
kawn simex Ni-thang-ya pa kaux Yi-seu-ma-lix,
viex pui kao kaux hoek ka Ci-ta-lix simiang ting cao pa kuang kaing ka haktiex Mi-see-pa.
Yam sawm kix tau,
2 Yi-seu-ma-lix mai pui pa hu tau mai nawh kao kaux an lawd vaig lih,
mai pauh Ci-ta-lix yum.
3 Yi-seu-ma-lix kawx hoik pauh ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot tau mai Ci-ta-lix ka haktiex Mi-see-pa,
mai pui naing Pa-pi-loeng pa ot tau mai kix nang heue.
4 Ka daux ra singaix,
sivoe sang tawng hoik pauh pui ka Ci-ta-lix,
5 koe pui tix daix kaux kaoh khaing See-ciien,
See-law,
mai khaing Sa-ma-li-yax.
Kix hoik khlao ka hauk jheeh tix,
Griah ka kraung ceep tix,
pat ka to kaux tix,
mai viex hoek ka Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai sidah bhawng Law-ban,
sang haok tan lu ka nyiex buan.
6 Yi-seu-ma-lix kaoh khaing Mi-see-pa,
mai kuan rieb kix mai yiiem tix.
Yam yaox nawh ka kix,
nawh tom kok kix ah nin:
“Hoek tix jhak ka Ci-ta-lix heue!”
7 Yam laig kix daux veng,
Yi-seu-ma-lix mai pui cie nawh tom tawx taix tix pauh ka kix,
mai tik to kaux yum kix ka daux o heue.
8 Ka daux kix koe pui kao kaux yiiem mai khiox khaw ka Yi-seu-ma-lix ah nin:
“Paw vi pauh ka yix heue!
Yix hoik koe nghox ca pa lang mu,
mai nghox ca,
bee sime,
mai rawm hia tom nie,
mox ka daux kaung.”
Yi-seu-ma-lix tom pluat ploi ka kix.
9 Dee tik Yi-seu-ma-lix to kaux pui yum an kied ting.
In hoik mawh daux pa kaong simiang Ya-sa,
son sang tieb tie pa puing simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pa-sa ka kix sivoe an.
Yi-seu-ma-lix teei to kaux yum an kix thom naog ka daux an.
10 Khaix an,
Yi-seu-ma-lix giex ka kawn bun simiang mai ka can pui pa paung ot ka Mi-see-pa,
mai to hu ka haktiex Ya-meung heue.
Pui in kix mawh cao pa pau simiang Ni-pu-sha-la-tang mai tawx ka Ci-ta-lix mawh cao kuang heue.
11 Yaw-ha-nang mai cao kaing pui naing cie nawh khankix,
mhong ka phi map pa yuh Yi-seu-ma-lix,
12 kix tom ploi pui naing leue ka Yi-seu-ma-lix,
mai krup nawh ka praok rawm dung dee dex ka Haktiex Ci-piien.
13 Pui pa giex Yi-seu-ma-lix khankix yaox ka Yaw-ha-nang,
mai ka simiang naing cie nawh,
kix kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi kah,
14 mai kling to kaux tix to hu plak ot kix.
15 Viang mawh nan,
Yi-seu-ma-lix,
mai pui pa ot greem nawh daix kaux pon to tix luat luan,
mai kix peung ka rhawm pui haktiex Ya-meung heue.
16 Yaw-ha-nang mai simiang naing pa ting cie nawh khankix,
mawh cao pa kia ngien ka pui pa hoik pon taoh kix khaing daux tawm taix Yi-seu-ma-lix.
Pui in kix hoik mawh pa giex kix hu khaing Mi-see-pa,
khaix hoik pauh Yi-seu-ma-lix ka Ci-ta-lix,
ka daux kix koe pa mawh pui naing,
bun miex,
kawn nyawm,
mai pui sieh khankix.
17-18 Kix lhat ka pui Pa-pi-loeng,
mawh kheu hoik pauh Yi-seu-ma-lix ka Ci-ta-lix,
pa hoik tawx simiang Pa-pi-loeng mawh simiang ting pa kuang kaing.
Mawh kheu son sang to mox tix khaing pui Pa-pi-loeng,
kix tom to hu haktiex Ai-cix.
Ka daux krax,
kix paung ot ka Cing-hang dex ka Paw-li-haung heue.
