Yie-li-mi ot daux dung kroh
38
Kawn simex Ma-tang pa kaux See-fa-thi-ya,
Ci-ta-lix kawn simex Pa-see-hu-eux,
kawn simex See-li-mi-ya pa kaux Yo-cia,
Pa-see-hu-eux kawn simex Ma-ci-ya mhong lox pa krai aux ka pui hun
2 gum lox Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui mawx pa kaog ot daux veng in,
pui an tag sang yum ka vaig gawn,
ka cai rhawm,
ka jhawm saux preng.
Viang mawh nan,
pui mawx greed tix ot greem pui Pa-pi-loeng,
pui an tag ang yaox tix yum,
tag yaox tix klawd ju.”
3 Aux baox krai kix Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang teei tix ap veng in ka pui naing Pa-pi-loeng;
kix tag sang hoek tix pon ka heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
cao kaing khankix hu ka simiang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Khiox khaw maix yuh pui in yum;
mawh kheu kieh nawh pui naing ot daux veng in,
mai pui hun greed tix ot greem pui,
mai kieh kix laux rhawm laux rhi.
Nawh ang mawh keud son sang mhawm pui hun,
mai hoik keud son sang yuh kix laux leeing heue.”
5 Simiang Si-ti-cia pawk lox ah nin:
“Mhawm heue,
nawh hoik ot ka daux tawm taix pex,
pex sang yuh ka mawx ka nawh aux ang koe lox kah.”
6 Mawh kheu nan kix giex ka aux,
mai teei maox ploi aux cawd daux dung Ma-ci-ya-thai-cee.
Daux dung in ot ka daux thawng nyiex haw simiang,
rawm ang koe ka daux,
mawh ngie glaox khaing,
aux tom ot daux glaox in heue.
7 Yam an,
simiang ot tix prawng see ka siviex Piien-ya-ming.
Pui sieh nyiex haw simiang pui Ku-seu pa kaux Yi-paw Mi-leux mhong grawng pa ploi kix aux cawd daux dung,
8 nawh tom hu krai simiang mhong ka kaing in ah nin:
9 “Vawng,
pui in kix yuh nin ka cao jhai Yie-li-mi kied mawh kaing pa ang khaw.
Kix ploi nawh cawd daux dung,
nawh tee rien tix yum ka cai rhawm dee an,
mawh kheu daux veng hoik ang lai koe ka priex preem.”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang tawx lox nhie Yi-paw Mi-leux viex pui hoek loe kaux,a
mai taoh aux lih sivoe sang yum aux.
11 Yi-paw Mi-leux tom viex pui in kix hoek ka nyiex krao nyiex haw simiang,
sawg ka man caox kraung caox khankix,
teei maox ploi cawd ka daux dung,
12 mai nhie aux teei man caox kraung caox in kix deuh ot greem klaik tix,
son ang maox sang kheet aux mat.
Aux hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai nawh,
13 Kix bluh aux lih khaing daux dung.
Khaix an,
kix ca kawn laung aux ot ka daux thawng heue.
Simiang Si-ti-cia chawk tix yaox ka Yie-li-mi
14 Khaix an,
simiang Si-ti-cia nhie pui viex aux hoek yaox tix ka dee laig ka diang siviex nyiex buan ka daux loe mu siviex.
Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Aux chawk maix ka kaing tix ceu;
aux tag sang kieh maix pawk sidawng heue.”
15 Aux ah nin:
“Phan aux krai lox tete,
maix tee rien tix pauh ka aux;
phan aux mhaing tix krai lox,
maix kawx ang nghiet ka heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Si-ti-cia briex mox tix tiex tu ka aux ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju,
pa mawh Siyiex cao pa phan pa yuh ju ex,
aux ang doh pauh ka maix,
mai ang tawx maix ka pui pa keud tix sang pauh ka maix heue.”
17 Aux tom krai ka Si-ti-cia,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex krai yuh nin:
“Phan maix kreet tix ot greem simiang naing Pa-pi-loeng,
maix tag ang yaox tix yum;
veng in kawx ang yaox tix gleeing.
Maix mai pui nyiex ma maix ang yaox tix yum.
18 Phan maix ang cu tix greed tix ot greem kix,
veng in tag sang cawd ot daux tawm taix pui Pa-pi-loeng.
Kix tag sang ploi ngu gleeing veng,
maix ang pon yaox tix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix kix heue.”
19 Viang mawh nan,
simiang pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux lhat ka pui Yo-tax an kix pa kreet tix ot greem Pa-pi-loeng.
Pui Pa-pi-loeng tee rien tix tawx aux ka kix,
mai kieh aux kha rhawm tix.”
20 Aux ah nin:
“Pui Pa-pi-loeng ang doh tawx maix ka kix.
Aux khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa nhie Simiang Siyiex aux deeh krai ka maix,
maix tom tag yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai ang yaox tix yum.
21 Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ka aux,
phan maix ang kreet tix ot greem kix,
22 Bun pa ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang Yo-tax,
pui tag sang viex kix hu ka plak sivoe ngai simiang naing Pa-pi-loeng.
Bun an kix ah nin khawm hu tix:
simiang hoik glup ka lox kawn cau pa een tix rhawm;
nawh hoik cu lox kix.
Yam in caong nawh khawm ra khawn hoik cawd daux glaox;
kawn cau pa een nawh rhawm tix tik vud ka nawh heue.”
23 Aux baox ah nin:
“Moeng iak maix,
kawn praix maix pui hoik viex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik;
to kaux maix kawx ang yaox tix luat luan.
Maix tag sang mawh pui pa giex simiang Pa-pi-loeng tix laung;
veng in tag sang gleeing tix laux leeing heue.”
24 Simiang Si-ti-cia pawk ah nin:
“Paw vi kieh pui tawng grawng lox pa si kah ax tau,
yuh nan ju maix tom taug yaox tix klawd.
25 Phan koe cao kaing pa tawng grawng lox pa si kah ax tau,
kix tee rien tix hoek sawg ka maix,
mai si kaw grawng lox pa si kah ax tau.
Kix rien tix ah nin ka maix:
‘Phan maix krai sidawng ka yix,
yix ang pauh ka maix.’
26 Viang mawh nan,
maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
‘aux mawh ngie khiox khaw simiang paw vi deeh tawx aux ing daux thawng,
mai yum dee an heue.’ ”
27 Khaix an,
can cao kaing rug hoek tix si kaw chawk ka aux,
aux tom hawt tix pawk ka lox kix awm gum lox pa hoik tawx simiang ka aux.
Kix ang lai koe lox krai,
mawh kheu ang koe pui tix kaux kawx,
pa mhong ka lox pa si kah simiang tau mai aux.
28 bYuh nin,
pui laung aux ot daux thawng nyiex haw simiang,
tom hoek laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung ka daux singaix an heue.
