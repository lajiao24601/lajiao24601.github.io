Lai Yaw-hang
Gum lox pa im ju
1
Sivoe sang phan yuh ka rao ma haktiex,
gum lox in hoik dawg koe.
Gum lox in ot tau mai Siyiex;
gum lox in mawh Siyiex.
2 Tom yam jauh gum lox in ot tau mai Siyiex.
3 Siyiex phan yuh can pa koe khawm uik ka daux nawh;
can ka daux pa phan pa yuh khawm uik,
ang koe ceu pa ang phan ang yuh ka daux nawh.
4 Gum lox in mawh num ju,
ju in tawx pa riang ka kawn pui.
5 Pa riang jhan riang ka daux pa faik,
pa faik ang sax pex ka pa riang heue.
6 aKoe pui tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yaw-hang,
nawh mawh kawn cau pa hoik ploi Siyiex hoek.
7 Nawh hoek tix kham sakse son pa riang an,
son sang kieh pui ku kaux mhong mai yum rhawm ka gum lox nawh.
8 Nawh ang mawh pa riang an,
mai hoik mawh pa kham mhai sakse son pa riang.
9 Pa riang an hoik mawh pa riang tete,
pa hoek tix jhan riang ka kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
10 Gum lox ot ka piang haktiex,
Siyiex phan yuh haktiex ka daux nawh,
viang mawh nan,
kawn pui piang haktiex ang tawng ka nawh.
11 Nawh hoek ka haktiex ot tix,
viang mawh nan,
pui cie nawh ang rieb ka nawh.
12 Khaix an can pui pa rieb ka nawh,
hoik mawh pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh,
nawh tom tawx akhuang kieh kix mawh kawn praix Siyiex.
13 Pui in kix ang lai mawh pa dex dawt tau mai nex nham,
ang lai mawh pa kaoh khaing alo kawn pui,
mai ang lai mawh pa keh khaing simex,
mai hoik mawh pa keh khaing Siyiex heue.
14 Gum lox in lih mawh kawn pui pa ot ka daux ex,
mai hoik naog ka buan son mai ka pa mawh pa meh.
Ex hoik yaox ka min plak ngai nawh,
min plak ngai in hoik mawh kawn simex keeing pa koe tix kaux khaing pa khaw tix pon heue.
15 Yaw-hang kham mhai sakse son nawh,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Phan mawh son nawh,
aux hoik sax krai ah nin:
‘Nawh hoek khaix aux,
viang mawh nan,
tieh tix lheu ting khaing aux;
mawh kheu sivoe keh aux,
nawh hoik dawg koe heue.’ ”
16 Ex hoik pon buan son piang buan son ka daux pa grum naog nawh.
17 Siyiex hoik tawx lox tat jhawm prawng ka daux Maw-si,
viang mawh nan,
buan son mai pa mawh pa meh hoik tawx ka daux pa hoek Ye-sux Khrit.
18 Ang koe pui hoik sax yaox ka Siyiex,
mai mawh ngie kawn simexb nawh tix kaux khaing pa kied muh nawh kah,
pa plak jhi nawh lih heue.
Gum lox Yaw-hang cao cub com
(Lai Ma-thaix 3.1–12; Lai Ma-kheu 1.1–8; Lai Lu-cia 3.1–18)
19 Plak greem mawh sakse pa kham Yaw-hang.
Yam an pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ploi cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we hoek ka Yaw-hang,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh maix pui mawx?”
20 Yaw-hang pawk ka ang bax tieb tie,
viang mawh nan,
nawh krai sidawng ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh Khrit heue.”
21 cKix chawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan maix mawh pui mawx?
Mawh Yi-li-yax lie?”
Yaw-hang pawk ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh.”
Kix baox chawk ah nin:
“Mawh cao jhai an tix kaux lie?”
Nawh baox pawk ah nin:
“Ang mawh.”
22 Kix baox ah nin:
“Krai ka yix mawh maix pui mawx,
son sang kieh yix yaox tix deeh ing krai ka pui pa ploi yix hoek.
Maix tang krai,
mawh maix pui mawx lie?”
23 dYaw-hang teei gum lox cao jhai Yi-sai-yax pawk ah nin:
Aux mawh lox kaw pa kok yo daux tiag daux tu ah nin:
Cox rauh see ka krax pa sang lawng nawh heue!
24 Pui an kix pa ploi pui Fa-li-saix hoek
25 chawk Yaw-hang ah nin:
“Phan maix ang mawh Khrit,
ang mawh Yi-li-yax,
mai ang mawh cao jhai an tix kaux,
phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx maix lai cub com lie?”
26 Yaw-hang pawk ah nin:
“Aux cub com pui ka rawm;
viang mawh nan,
koe pui pa cung ka daux pex tix kaux,
hoik mawh pui pa ang pex tawng yung,
27 nawh hoik mawh kaw pa hoek khaix aux,
aux kah ka maox kiiep nawh kawx aux ang keung ka heue.”
28 Pa lih khaing in kix tix mawh,
mawh ka haktiex Paw-ta-ni toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
jiet mawh dee cub com Yaw-hang pui ka rawm heue.
Kawn yung Siyiex
29 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
Yaw-hang yaox Ye-sux hu ka nawh,
mai ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
kawn yung Siyiex hoik teei tix tik ka phi map kawn pui heue!
30 Pui in mawh pa hoik ah aux ‘nawh hoek khaix aux,
viang mawh nan,
tieh tix lheu ting khaing aux;
mawh kheu sivoe keh aux,
nawh hoik dawg koe heue.’
31 Aux ang tawng yung ka nawh;
yam in aux hoek tix cub com pui ka rawm,
mawh son sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex tawng yung ka nawh heue.”
32 Yaw-hang baox kham sakse ah nin:
“Aux yaox Khuan Singax Singeh lih hoek khaing rao ma awm si kaux yuh nan ka piang to kaux nawh.
33 Aux ca kawn ang tawng yung ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex pa ploi aux hoek cub com pui ka rawm ah nin ka aux:
‘Phan maix yaox Khuan Singax Singeh lih hoek tix tom piang to kaux pui mawx,
pui an tag sang cub com pui ka Khuan Singax Singeh.’
34 Aux hoik yaox kah,
mawh kheu nan,
hoik krai grawng mawh nawh kawn simex Siyiex ka pex heue.”
Yam jauh tix kok ka pui gau bawg tix
35 Khaix hoik luan ka daux tix ngaix,
Yaw-hang mai pui gau nawh ra kaux baox ot dee an;
36 nawh yaox pa hu Ye-sux,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
kawn yung Siyiex!”
37 Pui gau ra kaux mhong ka lox in,
kiex tom hawt ka Ye-sux.
38 Ye-sux kling to kaux tix,
mai yaox hawt kiex tix,
nawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Sang simie pex tix lo ka ceu pa tix lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“La-pi,
paung ot maix dee mawx?”
(“La-pi” dee bleeh mawh “sara.”)
39 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex hoek tix jhak heue!”
Kix hu tau mai nawh,
mai hoik yaox ka dee paung ot nawh,
kix tom paung ot tau mai nawh ka daux singaix an.
(Yam an hoik mawh bawg yam pon ngaix pon taix nari heue.)
40 Pui an kiex pa mhong ka lox Yaw-hang pa hawt ka Ye-sux,
tix kaux mawh Ang-teu-liex pa mawh pux aik Si-meung pa kaux Pi-teux.
41 Nawh ran hu sawg pux ke tix Si-meung,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Yix hoik si pup ka Mi-sai-yax heue.”
(Dee bleeh “Mi-sai-yax” mawh “Khrit” heue.)
42 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh viex Si-meung hu yaox ka Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux dong ngai tix ka nawh,
mai ah nin:
“Mawh maix Si-meung kawn simex Yaw-hang,
ceu kaux maix tag sang kok mawh Ci-fax.”
(Dee bleeh Ci-fax mai Pi-teux mawh “Rang simaox” heue.)
Kok ka Fe-lix mai Na-tang-yiex
43 Khaix hoik luan ka daux tix ngaix,
Ye-sux but rhawm tix hu ka haktiex Cia-li-lix.
Nawh yaox ka Fe-lix,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Hawt khaix aux heue!” (
44 Fe-lix mawh pui Paw-sai-tax,
kix Ang-teu-liex mai Pi-teux mawh haktiex tau.)
45 Fe-lix yaox ka Na-tang-yiex,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Kaw an pa hoik tiiem Maw-si ot ka daux phuk lai lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai ka daux lai cao jhai khankix,
ex hoik yaox kah.
Nawh hoik mawh kawn simex Yaw-seux,
Ye-sux pui veng Na-sha-leux heue.”
46 Na-tang-yiex tom chawk ah nin:
“Pui Na-sha-leux tag sang chix yuh ceu pa mhawm ceu pa tix lie?”
Fe-lix ah nin:
“Maix hoek tix jhak heue!”
47 Yam yaox Ye-sux hoik hu Na-tang-yiex ka tix,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Jhak,
nawh hoik mawh pui Yi-seu-liex tete;
krax pie lhien ang koe ka daux rhawm rhi nawh pie ceeix kawx heue!”
48 Na-tang-yiex chawk nawh ah nin:
“Hoik tawng yung maix aux yuh ka mawx lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yam ot maix ka greem khaox ruh,
Fe-lix ang bax si kah tau mai maix,
aux hoik yaox ka maix heue.”
Na-tang-yiex ah nin:
49 “Sara,
maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex;
maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex heue!”
50 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh kheu aux krai ka maix,
aux hoik yaox maix ka greem khaox ruh,
hoik yum rhawm maix ka lie?
Maix tag sang yaox kaing pa lheu ting khaing in lie!”
51 eBaox ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
pex tag sang yaox rao ma hoik baoh,
kawn cau Siyiex tag sang ot ka praok kawn kawn pui,
mai lai ling tix lih lih haok haok heue.”
