Lai Sa-cia-li-yax
Krai dawt
Lai Sa-cia-li-yax chix tix guah koe ra baux:
1. Simin ting tix mu hoek daix mu tiiem gum lox pa krai cao jhai Sa-cia-li-yax, daux bawg yam tiiem lai in mawh sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num phuan yieh tix nga num hoek phuan yieh kao daix num. Gum lox jhai in kix plak nie mawh pa krai lih ka daux riang su sai, pa koe ka daux mawh khawm grawng pa haok huan Yie-lu-sa-leung, pa cox sum ka nyiex buan, pa singax pui cie Siyiex, mai bawg yam pa lih Mi-sai-yax heue.
2. Simin ting dim mu hoek kao pon mawh gum lox pa tiiem bawg yam phaox khaix; gum lox in kix mawh grawng pa simie ka Mi-sai-yax mai krax pa prawng ca pa hoek khaix heue.
Simiang pa nhim brug la laig daux veng (9.9) mai cao liang pa mhawm (11.4), riang in kiex ra mu siao tix ka daux phuk lai sigang khri pa khraox pa hoik tiiem riang Mi-sai-yax heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pa ne raw mai gum lox krax keud pot jun pot (1–8.23)
2. Pa prawng ca ka meung pa koe ka sivie siveng Yi-seu-liex (9.1–8)
3. Krax haok huan mai krax pa nhim pa tag sang hoek (9.9–14.21)
