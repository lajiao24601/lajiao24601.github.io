Dee si dauh pui Yi-seu-liex hoik dex
7
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
in hoik mawh gum lox pa tag sang krai pao aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka Yi-seu-liex yuh nin:
Yi-seu-liex hoik laux leeing,
mai dee si dauh kix khawm uik hoik dex heue!
3 “Yi-seu-liex eue,
dee si dauh maix hoik hoek.
Aux tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang maix.
Aux tag sang ciyang maix kheu kaing pa hoik yuh maix;
aux tag sang ciyang maix kheu hoik yuh maix kaing pa koeng pui kah.
4 Aux ang pluat ploi ka maix,
mai ang caox rhawm ka maix.
Aux tag sang ciyang maix kheu hoik yuh maix kaing pa caox pa caig.
Yuh nin,
maix tom tag tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
5 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Krax laux krax leeing tag sang si doe tix lih piang maix ra loe bawg.
6 Hoik laux leeing,
dee si dauh hoik dex hoek.
Maix hoik laux leeing heue!
7 Dee si dauh pui Yi-seu-liex hoik hoek.
Khu dee tan ka piang gawng ang lai koe bawg yam tix yuh poi krax gaux rhawm,
mawh ngie krax pa suk sag pa lih koe khaing;
singaix bawg yam an hoik dex sang hoek heue.
8 “Aux hoik dex sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix piang maix khawm uik.
Aux tag sang hawt tix prawng ca maix awm kaing pa hoik yuh maix;
aux tag sang hawt tix prawng ca maix awm kaing yuh maix pa hoik kieh maix pui seeing brai kah.
9 Aux ang pluat ploi ka maix,
mai ang caox rhawm ka maix.
Aux tag sang hawt tix ciyang ka maix awm kaing pa caox pa caig pa hoik yuh maix.
Yuh nin,
maix tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
mai mawh aux pa ciyang ka maix heue.”
10 Singaix krax laux krax leeing hoik hoek.
Krax simah simaing hoik ting,
krax ting rhawm lhaong rhi hoik naog ka rao ma heue.
11 Krax cau nyhau lih tix mawh kraung naing cie pui caox.
Sin ngao kix,
pak ngai kix,
kaing mhawm pa yuh kix ang lai koe khawm uik heue.
12 Yam hoik hoek bawg yam pa yuh nan,
pui pa liag ang siao lai gaux rhawm,
pui pa coh ang siao lai khi rhawm,
mawh kheu krax pa song rhawm Siyiex tag sang klup yuh nan ka piang kix ku kaux nang.
13 Ang lai koe pui pa yuh lai gee tix kaux kawx pa pon klawd ju tix deeh pon teei hoek ka mau pa hoik som nai tix,
mawh kheu hoik klup Siyiex krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang pui ku kaux.
Pui caox pui caig ang yaox tix im ju.
14 Yam hoik luah lox teet tawng pui naing;
pui ku kaux tag caw tix rien khre tix khawm uik.
Viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa hu puing naing,
mawh kheu hoik klup Siyiex krax pa song rhawm tix yuh nan ka piang pui ku kaux heue.
Ciyang ka phi map Yi-seu-liex
15 Plak praix mawh naing,
plak daux mawh jhawm saux preng sibhawm praix.
Pui pa ot plak praix veng tag sang yum ka greem lawm vaig khawm uik;
pui pa ot daux veng tag sang yum ka jhawm saux,
ka cai rhawm khawm uik.
16 Koe pui tix blah tag sang awm si kaux pa preeng pui yaoh ka daux ngawd,
mai pu haok ka piang gawng.
Kix hoik riag yo son phi map tix khawm uik heue.
17 Taix pui ku kaux hoik awn si kua khawm uik;
caong pui ku kaux hoik si yaung si kua khawm uik.
18 Kix tag sang ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
mai to kaux kix si yaung,
kix hoik khlao ka hauk kaing tix,
mai hoek krax kaig krax tua.
19 Kix tag sang teei tix tik mau khri tix ka daux laih,
awm hoik tik ka kraung ceu pa ang pui lai siao yuh nan.
Mawh kheu singaix pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
mau khri ang pon taoh ka kix,
ang pon naog yuh rhawm rhi kix,
ang pon kieh kix sak vaig naog tu.
Mau khri nyaw glaw kix yuh phi map.
20 Kix hoik sax jhi pui yaox ka siang pa kied mhawm cie tix,
viang mawh nan,
kix teei siang an raug tix yuh riang pa caox pa caig an kah.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex deeh kieh kix koeng ka siang cie to kaux tix heue.
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh pui meung tang meung tud rhim ka kix,
kieh pui nieh caox con ka siang cie kix,
mai baox yuh pa kaig pa tua ka kix.
22 Yam yuh pui pa ang khaw ang jiet ka nyiex buan aux,
yam laig pui con tix yieh ka nyiex buan,
mai kieh seeing brai,
aux ang doh tieb tie ka kix heue.
23 “Hoik suk sag khawm uik.
Ka piang meung mawh krax pauh krax toing khawm uik,
veng hoik naog ka krax cau nyhau.
24 Aux tag sang thung pui pa lang teung caox hoek dee in,
mai cax tix ot ka nyiex ma pex.
Yam kieh aux meung ka piang haktiex yuh pa ang khaw ang jiet ka nyiex dee krub pex,
pui naing pa kied kua pex kawx tag sang grai rhawm rhi pa kua heue.
25 Bawg yam deek rhawm hoik hoek heue!
Pex tag caw tix sawg ka krax pa nhim,
viang mawh nan,
ang lai koe krax pa nhim.
26 Krax pa laux leeing si doe tix hoek;
lox pa caox hoek ang jud ang jai.
Pex tag caw tix sawg cao jhai krai lox jhai;
cao giex kaing li ang lai koe ceu pa sidiex gau ka pui hun;
kuad song kawx ang lai koe lox pa sang krai ka pui.
27 Simiang riag yo,
simiang ting laux rhawm;
kawn pui tag sang si yaung si kua.
Aux tag sang ciyang pex kheu kaing pa hoik yuh pex khawm uik;
pex hoik ciyang pui yuh ka mawx,
mai aux kawx tag sang hawt tix ciyang pex yuh nan nang.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
