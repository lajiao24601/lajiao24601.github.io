Krax song rhawm Yaw-na mai krax pa va rhawm Siyiex
4
Kheu grawng kaing in,
Yaw-na kied ang gaux rhawm kah,
mai nawh kied song rhawm tete.
2 aMawh kheu nan,
nawh rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
sivoe sang lih hu aux khaing haktiex ot tix,
ang aux hoik krai maix tag sang yuh mawh nin lie?
Kheu son grawng kaing in,
aux hoik sau riang rao tix to hu ka plak haktiex Tha-see!
Aux hoik tawng grawng mawh maix Siyiex pa koe krax caox rhawm caox rhi.
Maix ang chix song rhawm yie yie,
maix hoik naog krax muh krax rhawm,
hoik tieh loh rhawm rhi tix jheeng jheeng ang lai ciyang ka pui.
3 bMawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
kieh aux yum khaing heue!
Phan aux yum kawn lheu mhawm khaing im aux heue!”
4 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Song rhawm maix kheu ceu pa tix lie?”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-na lih plak praix veng,
mai ngawm ka plak lih singaix ka veng.
Nawh sum nyiex gox tix lhang,
mai ngawm ka greem veei nyiex gox an,
son sang jhak tix yaox ka veng Ni-ni-we sang yuh ka mawx.
6 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh khaox bee ia lih kuah,
mai kieh khaox an lhaong khaing Yaw-na,
veei khaox an kheem ka kaing nawh,
mai kieh nawh mhong nyawm lhong veei.
Yaw-na kied khaw rhawm ka khaox an.
7 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Siyiex kieh viag vua hoek kiied ka khaox an,
khaox an tom yum.
8 Yam hoik lih singaix,
Siyiex baox kieh bhaung si aoh hoek tix phlu lih khaing plak lih singaix.
Mawh kheu pa hax singaix jhan ka piang kaing Yaw-na,
mai kieh nawh saux kaing ka pa hax koek,
mawh kheu nan nawh tom khiox khaw tix yum.
Nawh ah nin:
“Kieh aux yum khaing kawn lheu mhawm khaing im aux heue!”
9 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Song rhawm maix yuh pa tix ka son khaox bee ia in lie?”
Yaw-na pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux chix ang song rhawm yuh ka mawx heue!
Aux song rhawm tom hoik dex sang yum tix heue!”
10 Simiang Siyiex krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Khaox in ting huan ka daux tix sawm khaing,
ka daux ngaix khaix hoik yum;
viang maix ang bax sum ka nawh,
mai ang bax kieh nawh ting huan,
maix kawn lhawk ka nawh heue.
11 Phan mawh nan,
aux nie ang khaw tix caox rhawm ka veng ting Ni-ni-we an lie?
Kheu pui pa ot ka daux veng an pa ang bax pon guah tix tawng ka taix khawn dawm mai taix khawn goix koe ra mheen kaux,
khaix an kawn koe khawng brie tom nie heue!”
