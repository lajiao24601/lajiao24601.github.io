Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang pon krax taoh
12
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa plak jhi Simiang Siyiex pa dex dawt mai Yi-seu-liex.
Simiang Siyiex nih tawm ka rao ma,
phan yuh ka haktiex,
tawx ju ka kawn pui.
Nawh ah nin:
2 “Aux tag sang kieh Yie-lu-sa-leung awm kawk plai tix mu,
meung pa koe sivie siveng kix nyaux kah,
tag sang yung yang awm pui yoek yuh nan.
Yam lai lawm kix tix puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tag sang lai lawm nang veng pa ot daux haktiex Yo-tax pa deuk khankix.
3 Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang kieh Yie-lu-sa-leung awm simaox tix lon pa cien yuh nan,
meung pa keud tix sang gah ka simaox in hoik mat khawm uik.
Meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik tag sang khrawm paox tix hoek puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
4 Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang kieh brung kix yaoh,
mai kieh pui naing pa brug yhot.
Aux tag sang kia ngien ka pui Yo-tax,
mai kieh brung cie pui pa koeng ka kix dug ngai.
5 Yam an,
ceu pui Yo-tax ku cub tag sang tang ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
‘Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing,
tawx riang rao ka ceu pui pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.’
6 “Ka daux singaix an,
aux sang kieh ceu pui Yo-tax ku cub awm ngu cap ka daux nong khaox,
awm ngu proh ka daux brex nghox,
kix tag sang gleeing ka meung pa koe plak goix plak dawm kix laux leeing,
viang mawh nan,
pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ca kawn nyawm ot nyawm koe daux veng heue.
7 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang ran kieh pui naing Yo-tax pon pex,
son sang kieh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
mai min plak ngai pa hoik pon ceu pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ang lheu khaing pui Yo-tax.
8 Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka pui pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
viang hoik mawh pa blawn iak riang ka daux kix kawx,
tag sang kaux cong awm Ta-wue yuh nan.
Doeh ceu cad Ta-wue tag sang duk thung ka kix,
awm kawn cau Simiang Siyiex,
awm Siyiex,
duk thung ka kix yuh nan.
9 Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka meung pa keud tix sang puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ku meung heue.
10 ab“Aux tag sang kieh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa ot tang dauh naog ka krax pa caox rhawm mai ka khuan pa rawg buan.
Kix tag sang een rhawm tix ka pui pa hoik klauh kix yum an,
mai yiiem son nawh,
awm pui pa yum kawn tix pa koe tix kaux khaing yuh nan;
kix tag sang laux rhawm yiiem grai,
awm pui pa grai kawn liang tix keh yuh nan.
11 Ka daux singaix an,
pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang laux rhawm awm Ha-ta Ling-meung c pa koe ka dee lung haktiex Mi-cie-to yuh nan.
12-14 Nyiex ma pa koe ka daux haktiex in ku nyiex tag sang yiiem son pui nyiex tix yuh nin:
cub nyiex ma Ta-wue,
Na-tang,
Li-we,
See-me,
khawm can nyiex ma pui tang kaux khankix,
hoik yiiem son pui nyiex tix khawm uik,
simex bun hoik tang guah tix yiiem heue.”
