Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Heu-si-yax
17
Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ha-see kaing ka daux kao ra num,
kawn simex Yi-la pa kaux Heu-si-yax mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh giex kaing ka piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax dim num.
2 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang nyang bax mawh pa caox pa caig awm simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa koe sivoe nawh khankix.
3 Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Sa-mang-yi-seux hoek tix puing ka nawh;
Heu-si-yax ap tix ang pex,
mawh kheu nan nawh tom tawx mau koh keen ku num ka Sa-mang-yi-seux.
4 Koe tix num,
Heu-si-yax ploi pui hu ka simiang Ai-cix pa kaux Saw,
mai khiox khaw nawh teem coi tix,
ang lai baox tawx mau keen ka Ya-sux ku num.
Sa-mang-yi-seux hoik tawng kah,
nawh tom giex ka Heu-si-yax,
mai laung nawh daux thawng heue.
Sa-ma-li-yax laux leeing
5 Khaix an,
Sa-mang-yi-seux laig puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai lai lawm ka Sa-ma-li-yax.
Yam lai lawm kix hoek ka daux loe num,
6 pa mawh ka daux yam giex Heu-si-yax kaing daux dim num,
Vawng Ya-suxa puing ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
giex ka pui Yi-seu-liex hu ka Ya-sux,
mai deuh kix ot ka veng Ha-lax,
dex ka rawm klawng Ha-paw haktiex Keu-sang,
mai ka pui veng Mi-ti-yax heue.
7 Sa-ma-li-yax laux leeing hoik mawh kheu pui Yi-seu-liex hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix.
Simiang Siyiex hoik sax taoh kix luat luan khaing simiang Ai-cix,
mai duk thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix,
viang mawh nan kix tieh tix krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
8 mai hawt ka been pui haktiex an,
pa mawh pui tang ceu pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex kix hu khaing plak sivoe ngai pui cie tix,
mai baox rieb ka been pa viex simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hoek.
9 Pui Yi-seu-liex briex mox tix yuh ka kaing pa ang Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix cu kix yuh.
Ka daux veng daux yaong khawm uik,
jauh ka yaong iak hoek ka veng ting,
kix hoik sum nyiex vad ot ka piang gawng iak,
10 bmai baox sum rong simaox mai rong riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la ot ka piang gawng iak khawm uik,
mai ka greem veei khaox ku gawng.
11 Kix hawt ka pui tang ceu an kix pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex kix hu,
mai gleeing nyawng bhawng ka daux nyiex vad.
Kix yuh krax pa caox pa caig,
cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm song rhi,
12 mai hu krub ka riang rao pa ang Simiang Siyiex cu kix krub heue.
13 Simiang Siyiex hoik sax ploi cao jhai cao pa chix yaox sivoe hu ne raw ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax ah nin:
“Veeh tix hu singai khaing krax pa caox pa caig yuh pex,
priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
pa mawh lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tawx aux ka tax du pex,
mai lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx aux kawn cau tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix sidiex gau pex ka heue.”
14 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang cu tix nghiet;
kix hoik kiien rhawm awm tax du tix yuh nan,
ang yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix.
15 Kix tieb tie ka gum lox pa sidiex gau Simiang Siyiex,
ang hawt ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh nawh tau mai tax du kix,
ang simi ka gum lox pa sidiex nawh.
Kix hu krub ka riang rao pa giex pa going,
mai to kaux kix kawx hoik tieh tix lih mawh pa giex pa going.
Kix hawt ka been pui meung haktiex pa ot sivie siveng kix,
ang nghiet ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt ka pui tang ceu tang cad khankix.
16 cKix tik vud ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix khawm uik,
raug yuh tix krub ka riang kawn moi khri ra mu,
mai baox yuh ka rong riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la;
kix krub ka simuing daux rao ma,
mai yuh kaing krub Pa-lix.
17 dKix gleeing tix tan lu kawn praix tix ka been li siyiex tang kaux,
mai baox hu kok ka khuan,
sawg ka cao si pawk,
chawk ka cao jhai.
Kix coh ka to kaux tix,
hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai cheeik nawh song rhawm song rhi.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai grheu kix lih hu khaing plak sivoe ngai tix,
deuk ngie meung Yo-tax nong nong khaing heue.
19 Viang mawh nan,
kawn pui Yo-tax kawx ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix;
kix hawt ka been pa hoik rieb pui Yi-seu-liex.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang rieb ka can pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ciyang ka kix,
ap tix tawx kix daux tawm taix pui pa koeng khankix,
dee si dauh,
mai grheu kix lih hu khaing plak sivoe ngai tix heue.
21 Khaix hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax guah paox tix,
pui Yi-seu-liex yaog kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang mawh simiang kix.
Yie-law-paw-ang nyaw glaw kix tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai kieh kix krix ka daux phi map pa ting.
22 Kix hawt ka Yie-law-paw-ang,
mai ca baox yuh ka phi map pa hoik yuh nawh,
23 tom hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex kix hu khaing plak sivoe ngai tix,
awm lox pa hoik tawx nawh ka kawn cau tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix ne raw ka kix sivoe yuh nan.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui giex tix viex ka pui Yi-seu-liex hu ka Ya-sux,
mai kieh kix ot dee an tom si dauh heue.
Pui Ya-sux gah tix ot ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex
24 Vawng Ya-sux kaoh khaing Pa-pi-loeng,
Ku-tha,
Ya-wa,
Ha-ma,
mai Si-fa-wa-ying gah kawn pui ot ka veng Sa-ma-li-yaxe ka dauh,
tang ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa hoik giex kix.
Pui Ya-sux pon yaox tix cax ka daux veng in kix,
mai paung ot dee an.
25 Yam jauh kix tix laig,
kix ang krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai Simiang Siyiex ploi sivai sang si hoek tix kiied ka kix tix blah.
26 Koe pui krai ka Vawng Ya-sux,
krai ang pui Ya-sux pa paung ot ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax tawng ka been pa krub pui ka siyiex haktiex an,
mawh kheu nan Siyiex tom ploi sivai sang si hoek tix kiied kix yum.
27 Mawh kheu nan,
Vawng tawx lox ah nin:
“Ploi cao giex kaing li pa hoik giex ex deeh ing tix kaux,
kieh nawh paung ot dee an,
son sang sidiex gau nawh ka been siyiex pui haktiex an ka kawn pui.”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
koe cao giex kaing li pa laung kix ka Sa-ma-li-yax tix kaux,
deeh ing paung ot haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
mai sidiex gau pui haoh pui hun grawng sang krub kix ka Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx heue.
29 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa gah tix hoek ot ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax ca yuh riang siyiex ang jud,
mai deuh riang rao an ot ka daux nyiex vad pa hoik sum pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pui ku ceu tang yuh riang ka daux veng ot tix khawm uik;
30 pui Pa-pi-loeng yuh riang siyiex Su-keu Pi-na.
Pui Ku-tha yuh riang Ni-cia;
pui Ha-ma yuh riang Ya-see-ma;
31 pui Ya-wa yuh riang Ni-ha mai riang Tha-eu-tha;
pui Si-fa-wa-ying gleeing tix tan lu kawn simex kawn bun tix ka siyiex kix pa mawh Ya-teu-mi-leux mai Ya-na-mi-leu.
32 Pui in kix hoik mawh nang pui pa krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
kix hoik laug pui pa ang awm paox lih khaing daux cub pui pa awm paox mawh cao giex kaing li,
mai yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex vad ka piang gawng iak,
khaix an mai yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie son kix dee an.
33 Kix krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
kix hawt nang been krax krub pui haktiex meung tix krub ka siyiex tix heue.
34 fTom yam in singaix,
kix ca kawn hawt ka been pa prim khankix;
kix ang krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai ka gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka doeh ceu cad Ya-keux.
(Simiang Siyiex hoik sax sum ceu kaux Ya-keux mawh Yi-seu-liex.)
35 gSimiang Siyiex hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
mai tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Ang pon tix krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
ang pon tix krub ka kix,
ang pon tix yuh kaing jhaw son kix,
mai ang pon tix yuh krax tan lu ka kix.
36 hPex tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux hoik teei siawp sibrawm pa ting riang rao tix viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix.
Pex tag caw tix krub ka aux,
yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka aux.
37 Pex tag caw tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tiiem aux tix tawx ka pex leeing ju.
Pex ang pon tix krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
38 mai ang pon tix piawm ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai pex.
39 Pex tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pex.
Phan yuh nan,
aux tom taoh pex luat luan khaing tawm taix pui pa koeng khankix.”
40 Viang mawh nan,
pui an kix ang nghiet,
mai ca nied tix hawt ka been prim kix heue.
41 Viang pui an kix hoik krub ka Simiang Siyiex kawx,
mai kix baox krub nang ka riang rao;
hoek tom yam in,
doeh ceu cad kix ca kawn yuh nan heue.
