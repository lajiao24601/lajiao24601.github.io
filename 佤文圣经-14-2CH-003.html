3
aKeeing Saw-law-meung simiang Ta-wue hoik rien khre ka dee sang ot nyiex buan,
pa mawh ka gawng Maw-li-yax daux Yie-lu-sa-leung dee hoik lih bleeh Simiang Siyiex ka Ta-wue,
ka glauh pauh nghox ca cie Ya-lao-na pui Yie-pu-see.
2 Yam mawh Saw-law-meung simiang ka daux pon num khix ra,
kix jauh tix sum ka nyiex buan.
3 Simiang Saw-law-meung sum nyiex buan krax lang koe tix nga aliah kongjhee,
krax vieh koe dim kongjhee.
4 Siviex dee laig pui sidu mai been viex nyiex buan,
krax vieh koe dim kongjhee,
lhaong koe tix phuan pon kongjhee.
Siviex grah plak daux hoik plied khri mhawm kah.
5 Daux nyiex buan hoik tawk phia khaox gix bhawng ot ka tix tia,
khaix an plied khri kah,
mai saik riang khaox jhie yaong mai riang maox ceng ot ka piang.
6 Simiang teei siang simaox pa mhawm mai khri pa viex hoek khaing Pa-wa-ying sau ot ka nyiex buan.
7 Nawh teei khri plied ot ka ding nyiex buan,
ot ka nuk,
ot ka ngam siviex,
mai ka siviex.
Cang mai saik riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu ot ka ding.
8 bPa kok mawh dee pa kied singax nyiex buan plak daux,
krax lang koe dim kongjhee,
vieh koe dim kongjhee,
sidu mai been vieh nyiex buan yuh nan.
Ding nyiex dee pa kied singax hoik plied khri ot ka tix nga kongtoeng.
9 Been cien jim khri koe phuan yieh atigliah kongkheu.
Ding nyiex lhaong kawx hoik plied ka khri heue.
10 cSimiang baox tawx lox nhie cang mai teei khri khankix yuh riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu kah,
plak praix om ka khri,
mai deuh ka daux nyiex pa kied singax.
11-13 Riang Ci-lu-paw in kieh dia tix cung,
mai kieh veeh ngai tix plak siviex dee laig pui.
Tix mu Ci-lu-paw koe preeik ra mu,
tix mu preeik krax lang koe ra kongjhee ra kongchuing,
preeik in ra brawm nih tix,
mai kleuk paox tix si nax nyiex buan,
daog khawm ra plak cux ka ding nyiex,
mai nyat tix si dauh ka been vieh nyiex pa kied singax khawm uik,
koe been dim kongjhee.
14 dMan daux doh nyiex pa kied singax hoik yuh tix ka man keei sum mai man tang ceu khankix,
mai siang lih mawh pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt,
mai breeh riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu ot ka piang heue.
Rong tawng ra gawng
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 7.15–22)
15 Simiang nhie pui raug yuh rong tawng ra gawng,
krax lhaong ku gawng koe kao phuan plak kongjhee,
mai jung ka sivoe ngai nyiex buan;
hoik kieh koe toh kaing ku gawng,
krax lhaong koe ra kongjhee ra kongchuing.
16 Ka piang choek rong sau riang maox ceng pa hoik duing tix,
mai riang plix mak cang tawng tix yieh mu ot kah.
17 Rong tawng in ra gawng jung tix ot ka siviex dee laig ka nyiex buan khawm ra plak,
gawng pa ot plak seh kok mawh Ya-cing,e
gawng pa ot plak laong kok mawh Paw-a-seef.
