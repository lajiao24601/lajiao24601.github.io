Pux aik Yaw-seux hu liag nghox ka Ai-cix
42
Yam mhong Ya-keux koe nghox daux Ai-cix,
tom ah nin ka kawn simex tix khankix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex ang lai keud son in lie?
2 aAux hoik mhong koe nghox daux Ai-cix,
pex hu sawg tix liag hoek,
son ang ex sang yum ka cai rhawm tix.”
3 Kheu nan,
pux ke Yaw-seux kao kaux hu ka Ai-cix son sang liag nghox.
4 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-keux ang kieh Piien-ya-ming pux Yaw-seux hu mai kix,
kheu lhat Ya-keux nawh yaox krax laux heue.
5 Kawn simex Ya-keux khankix mai pui tang kaux hu liag nghox uik,
mawh kheu haktiex Cia-nang kawx koe sibhawm praix.
6 Yaw-seux mawh simiang ka haktiex Ai-cix,
mawh cao coh nghox ka pui haktiex tang dauh.
Mawh kheu nan,
pux ke Yaw-seux khankix hoek ka sivoe nawh,
grux ngawng krub piang tiex.
7 Yaw-seux yaox ka pux ke tix khankix,
mai tawng ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh yuh tix ang tawng,
briah ka kix ah nin:
“Kaoh pex khaing dee mawx?”
Kix pawk lox:
“Yix mawh pa kaoh khaing haktiex Cia-nang,
sang hoek tix liag nghox.”
8 Yaw-seux tawng ka pux ke tix khankix;
kix ang lai tawng ka nawh.
9 bNawh doh keud ka ca maox tix sivoe an dix,
tom su tix ah nin ka kix:
“Pex mawh pui jiang,
pa hoek tix jiang ka grawng kaing daux meung yix heue.”
10 Kix pawk lox:
“Simiang aux eue,
yix siliex ang doh mawh pui jiang,
mawh pa hoek tix liag nghox ka meung pex.
11 Yix mawh pux aik,
mawh pui pa rauh see,
ang mawh pui pa jiang.”
12 Yaw-seux kawn ca ah nin:
“Ang!
Pex mawh pui jiang grawng kaing daux meung yix.”
13 Kix ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
yix siliex mawh pux aik kao ra kaux,
mawh pa keh khaing keeing tix kaux;
nawh ot ka Cia-nang.
Koe pux yix tix kaux hoik yum,
pux pa iak yix ot tau mai keeing yix.”
14 Yaw-seux baox kawn ca ah nin:
“Ang!
Aux hoik khraox krai,
pex rien tix mawh pui jiang.
15 Aux sang cheuk tix yaox ka pex,
mawh ang pex mawh pa mawh pa meh.
Aux tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux simiang.
Lheu khaing viex pex pux pa iak tix hoek tin,
aux ang doh kieh pex lih hu.
16 Pex ploi pui tix kaux ing teei nawh hoek;
pui pa deuh khankix laung ot tin,
krox lox pa ah pex mawh ang mawh.
Phan ang mawh nan,
aux tiex tu ka daux ju simiang,
pex mawh pui jiang.”
17 Kheu nan,
nawh laung kix loe ngaix heue.
18 Ka daux loe singaix,
Yaw-seux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux mawh pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
phan pex cu ka lox aux,
aux tag deuh ka ju pex.
19 Son sidawng aux mawh pex pui pa mawh pa meh,
pex caw tix deuh pui tix kaux ot daux thawng kieh mawh pa kham son pex;
pui tang kaux chix tix deeh ing,
viex nghox pa liag pex ing tawx pui pa cai rhawm pex ih.
20 Khaix an viex pux aik pa iak pex hoek dee tin ka aux.
Yuh nin tom kham sakse lox pex mawh tete,
pex tom ang yaox tix yum.”
Kix cu ka lox in,
21 kix ah nin ka paox tix:
“Sivoe an,
ex yuh ka pux ex laux,
yam in krax pa pawk bix hoek piang ex!
Ex yaox grawng pa kha nawh tix,
nawh khiox khaw ka ex,
ex ang jhak simi!
Mawh kheu nan,
ex tag sang klawng krax phi map in heue.”
22 cLui-piien ah nin:
“Aux hoik krai ka pex ang cu pex yuh kawn nyawm an laux,
pex ang nghiet;
yam in son nham nawh hoik pawk bix son ex.”
23 Yaw-seux hoik tawng ka lox pa krai kix,
kix ang tawng nawh hoik tawng,
mawh kheu si kah Yaw-seux mai kix,
koe pui pa toe lox.
24 Yaw-seux veeh tix deeh plak khaix,
yiiem been tix cu,
khaix an deeh hoek,
laug ka Si-miien,
phrawk nawh sivoe ngai kix heue.
Pux ke Yaw-seux khankix deeh ing ka Cia-nang
25 Yaw-seux tawx lox mai kieh pui sau nghox naog daux chee pux ke tix khankix,
mai deeh sau mau daux chee kix kaux mawh kaux an,
mai khre priex pa sang ih kix hawt krax.
Hoik rien tix mhawm khawm uik.
26 Kix tom tang nghox pa liag tix ka piang la,
lih hu heue.
27 Hoek ka dee it kix,
ka daux kix tix kaux paoh chee sang tawx la sawm,
yaox kawn ot mau daux chee,
28 tom ah nin ka pux aik tix khankix:
“Kix deeh mau ka aux!
Maix jhak,
ot ka daux chee aux!”
Kix lhat graung yaoh si yaung ka ah nin:
“Siyiex lai kieh ex yuh nin yuh ka mawx lie?”
29 Kix hoek ka Cia-nang,
dee ot keeing nawh tan,
mai krai grawng kaing pa yaox kix ka nawh.
30 Kix ah nin:
“Simiang daux haktiex Ai-cix briah ka yix,
ah mawh yix pui hoek jiang ka haktiex meung kix.
31 Yix pawk lox:
‘Yix mawh pui rauh see,
ang mawh pui jiang;
32 yix koe pux aik kao ra kaux,
pa kaoh khaing keeing tix kaux khaing.
Koe pux yix tix kaux hoik yum,
pux pa iak ot mai keeing yix Cia-nang.’
33 Simiang an ah nin:
‘Aux sang cheuk tix yaox ka pex mawh ang pex mawh pa mawh pa meh.
Pex deuh pui tix kaux tin,
tang kaux chix tix ing,
viex nghox ing ka nyiex tawx pui pa cai rhawm ih,
34 Khaix an viex pux nghoik pex hoek ka aux.
Yuh nin aux tom yum rhawm mawh pex pui pa mawh pa meh,
ang mawh pui jiang.
Aux deeh chix tawx pux aik pex ka pex;
pex kawx chix tix ot dee tin,
yuh lai gee heue.’ ”
35 Khaix an,
yam thawk kix nghox,
kix ku kaux yaox ot hau mau ka daux chee tix.
Kix mai keeing kix yaox hau mau,
kied lhat kah.
36 Ya-keux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex sang kieh aux tik ka kawn simex tix khawm uik lie?
Yaw-seux hoik grai!
Si-miien baox grai!
Yam in baox sang viex Piien-ya-ming hu!
Krax kha rhawm hoik hoek ka piang aux heue!”
37 Lui-piien ah lox pa cong ka keeing tix:
“Phan aux ang deeh viex Piien-ya-ming hoek ka maix;
maix chix pauh ka kawn simex aux ra kaux.
Tawx pux pa nghoik ka aux,
aux chix kia nawh mhawm,
aux tag deeh viex nawh hoek.”
38 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-keux ah nin:
“Kawn simex aux in ang doh pon tix hu mai pex!
Pux ke lih nawh hoik yum,
yam in deuh ngie nawh tix kaux khaing!
Ka daux krax lhat nawh yaox kaing pa ang mhawm.
Pex sang kieh aux tax kuad pa paing hauk in laux rhawm laux rhi khawm laig tix daux si meeig lie?”
