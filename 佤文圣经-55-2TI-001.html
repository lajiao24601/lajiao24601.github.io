Lai Thi-maw-thaix Phuk Ra
1
Aux mawh Pao-law;
aux mawh kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit kaing daux alo Siyiex,
mai hoek tix krai pao ju pa khraox pa hoik tat nawh tix deuh ka daux Ye-sux Khrit.
2 aAux tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka maix,
pa mawh kawn simex pa muh aux Thi-maw-thaix:
Kieh keeing Siyiex mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son mai krax caox rhawm,
krax pa nhim ka maix!
Pa yaog yaw ting buan son mai pa joh jhan
3 Aux yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pa hoik teei aux mai tax du aux rhawm rhi pa singax krub ka yuh nan.
Aux keud ka maix ka daux krax rawg buan leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
4 yam doh keud aux ka maix,
yam roik rawm ngai ka guah paox tix,
aux taug nie simie tix yaox ka maix tete;
phan yaox ka maix,
aux tag naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
5 bAux doh keud ka krax yum rhawm pa singax maix an,
pa mawh krax yum rhawm yiex maix Law-chix plak kawn saux bun,
mai miex maix Yo-ni-ci yuh nan.
Krax yum rhawm kix,
aux kawx hoik yum rhawm grawng koe maix nang.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka maix yuh nin:
buan son pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka maix an yam deuh aux taix tix ka piang maix,
maix tag sang kieh rup awm ngu pa ang chix yeet yuh nan.
7 Mawh kheu khuan pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka ex ang kieh ex lhat graung;
deeh tieh tix,
khuan cie nawh kieh ex chix naog ka riang rao,
krax muh,
mai ka chix priex ka rhawm rhi tix heue.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
maix paw vi kieh mhai sakse pa yuh ex son Simiang Ye-sux mawh kaing ceu pa kaig tua,
mai paw vi een tix kaig tua ka pa lih mawh tix pui laung kheu Simiang Ye-sux.
Maix tag caw tix hawt awm riang rao pa tawx Siyiex ka maix,
mai klawm krax kha rhawm son gum lox buan.
9 Siyiex taoh ka ex,
kok ex mawh pui cie tix,
ang mawh kheu pon yuh ex kaing ceu pa mhawm pa tid tix,
mai hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo nawh mai buan son nawh.
Nawh hoik teei tix tawx buan son in ka ex sivoe ku ceu khawm uik khaing daux Ye-sux Khrit,
10 Yam in hoik lih tix mawh ka daux pa lih hoek cao taoh ex Ye-sux Khrit.
Khrit hoik yuh laux ka siawp sibrawm krax yum,
mai nie jhi lih ka ju pa ang chix laux leeing khaing daux gum lox buan heue.
11 cKheu son gum lox buan in,
Siyiex tawx aux mawh cao sidiex,
kawn cau,
mai sara.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom taug kham krax kha rhawm in kix.
Viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn naog ka krax yum rhawm;
mawh kheu hoik tawng aux pa yum rhawm tix mawh mawx,
hoik yum rhawm ka grawng pa chix priex pau nawh ka ceu pa hoik ap pong nawh tix tawx ka auxd tom singaix,
tom hoek singaix yam deeh lih hoek Simiang Ye-sux.
13 Maix tag caw tix giex kaux cong ka gum lox pa grum mhawm pa hoik tawx aux ka maix an,
kieh mawh ceu pa cox mhawm ka maix,
mai tag caw tix pau nang ka krax yum rhawm,
mai krax muh pa hoik pon khaing pa kleuk tix tau mai Ye-sux Khrit.
14 Tag caw tix giex kaux cong ka ceu pa mhawm,
pa hoik ap pong tix tawx ka maix khaing Khuan Singax Singeh pa kieh ot ka daux rhawm rhi ex heue.
15 Maix hoik tawng,
pui haktiex Ya-si-yax hoik tik vud ka aux khawm uik,
khawm pui pa ot ka daux Fe-cie-lux mai He-maw-chi-ni.
16 Kieh Siyiex caox rhawm ka nyiex ma A-ni-seu-fu;
mawh kheu hoik joh jhan nawh ka aux tom nie bawg,
mai hoik kieh aux pon krax pa nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi.
Nawh ang een tix kaig tua kheu pa mawh aux pui laung,
17 yam hoek nawh ka Law-ma,
tom hu sawg aux ku dauh ku dee,
dee si dauh yaox ka aux.
18 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux kieh nawh pon krax pa caox rhawm ka daux singaix an heue!
Phan mawh pa yuh nawh kaing son aux tom nie ka Yi-fu-saw,
maix hoik tawng sidawng khawm uik heue.
