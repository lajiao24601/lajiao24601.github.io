Lox pa krai Na-tang mai grawng pa loh Ta-wue rhawm tix
12
Simiang Siyiex ploi cao jhai Na-tang hu ka Ta-wue.
Na-tang hoek ka Ta-wue dee an,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Ka daux veng tix veng koe pui ka ra kaux,
tix kaux mawh pui mi pui koe,
tix kaux mawh pui hot pui sibhawm.
2 Pui mi pui koe mau an hoik koe yung moi tom nie.
3 Pui hot pui sibhawm an koe ngie kawn yung iak tix mu khaing,
hoik mawh pa liag nawh tix eei.
Nawh kia ngie ka kawn yung,
kieh kawn yung iak an ting tau mai kawn simex kawn bun tix.
Nawh kawx hoik eei kawn yung iak an ka pa ih pa nyaux tix tix blah,
mai kieh kawn yung iak an it ka naok tix,
awm hoik yuh nawh ka kawn bun tix yuh nan heue.
4 Koe tix singaix,
koe nyo si nie hoek ka daux nyiex pui mi pui koe an,
mai nawh lhawk pauh ka phung moi phung yung tix pie tix mu khaing son sang khan kheu ka nyo si nie.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh teei ka kawn yung iak nyiex pui hot an mai khan kheu nyo si nie tix ka heue.”
5 Ta-wue mhong kah,
mai kied koe krax song rhawm ka pui mi pui koe an,
nawh ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
pui pa yuh kaing pa yuh nin,
tag khaw tix kieh yum heue!
6 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa ang khaw yuh nin,
mai tag caw tix deeh kieh nawh plud ka pa hoik teei tix pon son heue.”
7 Na-tang ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Maix hoik mawh pui an heue!
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex hoik ah nin:
‘Aux hoik sum maix mawh simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai hoik taoh maix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix Sao-law.
8 Aux hoik ap tix tawx meung cie Sao-law mai moeng iak nawh khankix ka maix khawm uik,
mai kieh maix mawh simiang pa kuang ka piang Yi-seu-liex mai Yo-tax.
Phan ang baox kub,
aux kawn sang baox tawx ka maix.
9 Yuh ka mawx maix lai lieh lheu ka lox tawx aux?
Yuh ka mawx lai yuh kaing pa yum koeng yuh nin?
Maix hoik kieh Wu-li-yax yum ka daux naing,
mai teei tix vai lawm vaig pui Ya-meung pauh nawh yum kah,
khaix an teei ka moeng nawh.
10 Maix hoik lieh lheu ka aux yuh nin,
mai tud rhim ka moeng Wu-li-yax,
doeh ceu cad nyiex ma maix tag ang yaox tix luat luan khaing vaig gawn leeing ju.
11 aAux tiex tu ka maix ah nin,
aux tag sang kieh pui daux nyiex ma to kaux maix lih tix suk sag yuh krax naing ka maix.
Maix tag yaox pa tawx aux moeng iak maix khankix ka simex pa hoik dex dawt mai maix,
pui in tag sang it tau mai kix daux pa riang praix.
12 Maix hoik yuh phi map ka daux krax pa glip,
mai aux tag sang kieh kaing in jhi tix lih ka daux pa riang praix,
mai kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yaox ka khawm uik heue.’ ”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ah nin ka Na-tang:
“Aux hoik phit ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Na-tang pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik pluat ploi phi map maix.
Maix ang lai yaox tix yum.
14 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu hoik yuh maix ka kaing in,
pa hoik map nai yuh Simiang Siyiex,
kawn maix tag sang yum.”
15 Khaix hoik krai ka lox in,
Na-tang deeh ing ka nyiex tix heue.
Kawn simex Ta-wue yum yam keh tix
Simiang Siyiex kieh kawn simex Ta-wue pa keh nawh tau mai Pa-see-pa pon jhawm saux ting.
16 Ta-wue khiox khaw Siyiex kieh kawn tix moi saux.
Nawh tuah priex preem,
yam hoek plak pon bo ku singaix,
nawh laig ka daux lawg it tix,
noi tix it piang tiex leeing sawm.
17 Pui cau pa riex ju pa ot ka daux nyiex nawh glaw nawh kaoh,
nawh ang cu,
mai ang sawm rheep tau mai kix.
18 Khaix hoik luan tix vang tuah,
kawn nyawm an yum.
Siliex Ta-wue lhat krai grawng in ka nawh.
Kix ah nin:
“Yam kawn im ju kawn nyawm,
ex si kah tau mai nawh,
nawh ang cu tix pawk lox ex,
yam in ex doh sang pon krai grawng pa hoik yum kawn nyawm ka nawh yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh tee rien tix yuh kaing pa ang mhawm ka to kaux tix heue!”
19 Ta-wue hoik briex mhong ka lox pa si kah kawn cau tix mai yhaok paox tix,
grawng pa hoik yum kawn nyawm,
nawh tom chawk kix ah nin:
“Kawn nyawm hoik yum lie?”
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Hoik,
kawn nyawm hoik yum heue.”
20 Ta-wue tom yaog tix kaoh khaing piang tiex,
heem tix,
sat hauk kaing tix,
mai loh sibex tix,
khaix an,
nawh tom hu krub ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
Yam deeh ka nyiex haw ot tix,
tom tawx lox nhie pui rien khre ka priex preem.
Priex preem hoik tang tix piang pheen,
nawh tom ih.
21 Siliex nawh chawk nawh ah nin:
“Ex ang tawng ka grawng nawh,
yam im kawn nyawm,
hoik yiiem mai tuah priex preem son nawh.
Kawn nyawm hoik yum,
mai maix tieh tix ih nyaux heue!”
22 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Mawh tete,
yam kawn im kawn nyawm,
aux yiiem mai tuah priex preem.
Mawh kheu keud aux Simiang Siyiex sang chix caox rhawm ka aux,
mai ang kieh kawn nyawm yum.
23 Yam in kawn nyawm hoik yum,
aux kawn sang tuah priex preem yuh pa tix lie?
Aux kawn sang chix kieh kawn nyawm im lie?
Koe tix singaix sax,
aux tag sang hu ka dee ot nawh tan,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang pon hoek dee ot aux tin leeing ju heue!”
Grawng keh Saw-law-meung
24 Ta-wue kieh moeng tix Pa-see-pa nyawm rhawm,
mai it tau nawh.
Khaix an moeng nawh keh kawn simex tix kaux.
Ta-wue sum ceu kaux mawh Saw-law-meung.
Simiang Siyiex kied muh ka kawn nyawm in,
25 mai tawx lox nhie cao jhai Na-tang sum ceu kaux kawn nyawm in mawh Yie-ti-ti-yaxb,
mawh kheu kied muh Simiang Siyiex ka nawh heue.
Ta-wue pon teei ka veng La-pa
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 20.1–3)
26 Yaw-ya ca nied tix puing ka veng La-pa pa mawh veng ting pui Ya-meung,
mai dex hoik sang pon teei kah.
27 Nawh ploi pui hu krai ka Ta-wue ah nin:
“Aux puing ka veng La-pa,
mai hoik pon teei ka rawm daux pa nyaux kix,
28 yam in khiox khaw maix Vawng khrawm pui naing pa kawn deuk khankix,
mai thung kix hu teei heue!
Aux ang rhawm kieh min pa hoik pon teei ka veng in mawh cie to kaux tix.”
29 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom khrawm pui naing pa haoh hun,
hu plak veng La-pa,
pon teei ka veng an.
30 Koe mhok khri ka piang kaing riang siyiex Mi-leu-kong pa krub pui Ya-meungc tix mu,
been cien koe tix ngoe phuan kongcing,
sau siang simaox ot kah,
Ta-wue tom teei siang simaox an lih,
mai sau ot ka mhok simiangd pa ceep tix.
Nawh kawx pon tud kraung tom nie khaing daux veng,
31 mai tawx lox ka pui pa ot daux veng an,
kieh kix teei tix yuh kaing ka jhi,
rhiem,
moe,
mai baox khin kix yuh kaing lon tiex gleeing.e
Ta-wue kawx yuh nang nan ka pui tang kaux pa ot ka daux veng Ya-meung.
Khaix an Ta-wue mai pui naing cie nawh deeh ing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
