Sao-law yaox ka Sa-mu-eu
9
Koe ceu pui Piien-ya-ming tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Ci-see;
nawh mawh kawn simex Ya-piie,
kawn saux Si-lawx,
ceu pui Ya-fe-yax,
pui daux nyiex Pi-keu-la.
Ci-see mawh pui pa koe mau pa koe min.
2 Nawh koe kawn simex tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Sao-law,
nawh hoik mawh pui nhum ju pa mhawm grang tix kaux.
Sao-law kawn lhaong khaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku kaux been tix kaing,
mai kied mhawm grang nang heue.
3 Ci-see koe la ra loe mu hoik grai;
nawh ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Maix viex siliex tix kaux hu sawg la heue!”
4 Kiex hoik tauh hu sawg ka gawng Yi-fa-liien mai ka haktiex Sa-li-sa khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox.
Kiex ca nied tix sawg,
mai laig ka daux haktiex Sa-ling,
la kawx ang ot tan.
Mawh kheu nan,
kiex hoek tix sawg ka haktiex Piien-ya-ming,
ang yaox nang heue.
5 Dee si dauh,
kiex hoek ka Su-fux,
Sao-law ah nin ka siliex nawh:
“Ax ing khaing heue!
Aux keud keeing aux ang lai khi rhawm son la,
mai tieh tix khi rhawm son ax heue.”
6 Siliex ah nin:
“Krox tix vud.
Ka dee in koe pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex tix kaux,
kied pon krax yung yi pui,
mawh kheu kaing pa krai nawh ku ceu hoik lih tix mawh.
Ax hu chawk nawh,
nawh rien tix chix krai ax mhong,
sang hu ax sawg la dee mawx heue.”
7 Sao-law ah nin:
“Phan ax hu ka nawh,
ax sang tawx ceu pa tix ka nawh lie?
Ka daux bau ax ang lai koe priex preem,
mai ang lai koe ceu tid tix tawx ka nawh heue.”
8 Siliex ah nin:
“Aux koe mau luah tix plah.
Aux chix tix tawx ka nawh,
mai nawh tag chix krai ax mhong,
sang hu ax sawg la dee mawx heue.”
9-11 Sao-law ah nin:
“Rhawm keud maix mhawm!
Ax hu heue.”
Kiex tom hu dee ot pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an.
Kiex haok ka blaong,
yam sang laig kiex daux veng,
yaox kawn bun tix blah hoek kin ka rawm,
tom chawk kix ah nin:
“Cao pa chix yaox sivoe ot daux veng lie?”
(Yam an,
cao jhai hoik kok mawh cao pa chix yaox sivoe.
Phan koe pui pa sang sawg ka Siyiex,
tom ah nin:
“Ex hu ka cao pa chix yaox sivoe heue!”)
12-13 Kawn bun pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh,
nawh ot ka daux veng.
Tete,
nawh kawn ot sivoe pex!
Phan pex nied phai ceeix,
chix tix krup nawh,
hoik laig pex daux veng pex tag yaox ka nawh.
Nawh hoek ka daux veng ka daux in ngaix,
mawh kheu pui haoh pui hun sang yuh krax tan ka piang khu dee tan pa ot ka blaong gawng.
Pui haoh pui hun tag sang krox nawh,
mawh kheu caw nawh tix sang ran nawb buan piang krax tan sivoe,
mai kix tom tag pon tix ih ceu pa ih khaix.
Phan pex hu yam in,
tag sang yaox nawh sivoe sang haok ih nawh ceu pa ih ka piang gawng heue.”
14 Sao-law mai siliex nawh ca nied tix hu sivoe,
yam hoik sang laig ka daux veng,
mai yaox ka Sa-mu-eu hoik hu ka plak lawng krax pa hu kiex,
sang hu ka nyiex dee krub kix heue.
15 Sivoe sang hoek Sao-law tix ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ka Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
16 “Singaix pa sax bawg yam in,
aux sang ploi ceu pui Piien-ya-ming hoek dee ot maix tin tix kaux.
Maix teei bee groi ka piang nawh,
mai ap nawh mawh simiang ka piang pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh tag sang chix kuang kia ka kix,
mai taoh kix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix pui Fe-li-see.
Aux hoik yaox grawng pa kha pui cie aux rhawm tix;
mai aux mhong lox kok yo kix heue.”
17 Yam yaox Sa-mu-eu ka Sao-law,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“In hoik mawh pui pa krai aux ka maix;
nawh tag sang kuang ka pui cie aux heue.”
18 Yam an,
Sa-mu-eu hoek ka diang siviex veng;
Sao-law hu dex mai chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux chawk maix,
cao pa chix yaox sivoe ot dee mawx lie?”
19 Sa-mu-eu pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh cao pa chix yaox sivoe heue.
Maix hu sivoe ha,
ran hu ka nyiex dee krub.
Daux ngaix in pax ra kaux caw tix sawm tau mai aux;
pa sax pon ngawb cao,
aux tag chix tix pawk ka lox can pa chawk pax khawm uik,
khaix an,
mai deeh tawx pax ing.
20 Phan mawh la pa hoik kieh pex grai sivoe an loe singaix khankix,
maix paw vi lai khi rhawm kah,
hoik yaox ka heue.
Viang mawh nan,
pa simie kawn pui Yi-seu-liex mawh ceu pa tix liea?
Hoik mawh maix mai pa mawh ceu pui nyiex ma keeing maix heue.”
21 Sao-law pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
pa mawh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa blawn iak khaing paox tix.
Cub nyiex max aux nie baox mawh nang pa iak pa ang koe ceu khaing daux ceu pui in.
Maix lai krai aux mhong lox pa ah nan lie?”
22 Sa-mu-eu viex Sao-law mai siliex nawh laig ka daux nyiex ting mai kieh kiex ngawm ka sivoe paox.
Dee an nyo hoik koe ka khawm uik tix ngoe kaux,
mai kix hoik ngawm khawm uik heue.
23 Sa-mu-eu ah nin ka cao pa khre priex:
“Tawx khik nex an pa hoik nhie aux maix deuh tin.”
24 Cao pa khre priex tom teei ka khik nex ba an pa hoik blawk tix deuh lih,
deuh ot ka sivoe ngai Sao-law,
Sa-mu-eub ah nin:
“In mawh nex pa hoik deuh son maix.
Ih heue!
Mawh aux pa blawk tix deuh,
sang tawx ka maix sivoe ngai nyo nyiex heue.”
Ngaix an,
Sao-law sawm tau mai Sa-mu-eu.
25 Khaix hoik kaoh kix khaing nyiex dee krub tix mai deeh ing daux veng,
koe pui hoik rien khre tix tawm ka kuc mhawm ot ka choek nyiexd son Sao-law;
26 nawh tom it tan heuee.
Sa-mu-eu groi bee yaog Sao-law mawh simiang
Yam hoik riang praix, Sa-mu-eu hoek tix kok Sao-law ka piang choek nyiex ah nin: “Kaoh! Aux tawx maix hu krax heue.” Sao-law kaoh, nawh tom hu tau mai Sa-mu-eu daux laih.
27 Yam hoik hoek kix ka praok veng,
Sa-mu-eu ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Nhie siliex maix ran hu.”
Khaix hoik hu siliex nawh,
Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Kaog ot tin tix vud ha,
aux sang krai gum lox Siyiex ka maix heue.”
