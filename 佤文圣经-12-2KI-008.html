Bun miex Su-niang deeh ing ka haktiex tix
8
aYi-li-sa hoik krai ka bun miex pa paung ot haktiex Su-niang (Yi-li-sa hoik taoh ka kawn simex nawh singaix pa hoik luan),
Simiang Siyiex sang kieh haktiex in si pup ka krax sibhawm praix aliah num,
nawh caw tix lih hu khaing dee in pui nyiex ma tix,
mai hoek paung ot ka haktiex tang dauh.
2 Bun miex an cu nghiet ka lox pa krai Yi-li-sa,
nawh hoek tau mai pui nyiex ma tix ka Fe-li-see,
mai paung ot dee an aliah num heue.
3 Hoik naog ka daux aliah num,
nawh deeh hoek ka Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hu ka simiang,
mai khiox khaw simiang deeh tawx nyiex ma tix mai kaung ma tix ka nawh.
4 Nawh yaox simiang chawk siliex Yi-li-sa pa kaux Ci-ha-si,
grawh grawng mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh Yi-li-sa khankix.
5 Yam krai Ci-ha-si simiang mhong grawng kieh Yi-li-sa pui yum deeh kaoh im yuh ka mawx,
bun miex an hoek ka sivoe ngai simiang,
mai khiox khaw son nyiex ma tix mai kaung tix ka nawh.
Ci-ha-si ah nin ka simiang:
“Vawng,
in mawh bun miex an heue;
in mawh kawn simex nawh pa mawh pui pa taoh Yi-li-sa im an heue.”
6 Simiang chawk tix yaox ka bun miex an,
lox pa pawk nawh hoik jhi sidawng ka grawng kaing pa krai Ci-ha-si.
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang tom kok siliex pa ting tix tix kaux mai tawx lox nhie nawh deeh tawx can ceu pa mawh cie bun miex an ka nawh,
khawm to ma pa pon yam ang bun an ot ka daux aliah num heue.
Yi-li-sa mai simiang Shui-li-yax pa kaux Piien Ha-tax
7 Yam hu Yi-li-sa ka Ta-ma-see-keux,
simiang Shui-li-yax pa kaux Piien Ha-tax koe jhawm saux.
Koe pui krai ka simiang,
Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex ot ka Ta-ma-see-keux.
8 Simiang ah nin ka kawn cau pa ting tix pa kaux Ha-shiiex:
“Maix viex khawng phak hu ka pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
nhie nawh khiox khaw chawk mhaing ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai jhak tix yaox jhawm saux aux,
sang chix moi ang chix moi.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ha-shiiex teei ka-la-uk tipon mu,
kieh kix tang ka nghox koi ku ceu pa blawn mhawm khaing pa pon ka daux haktiex Ta-ma-see-keux hu ka Yi-li-sa.
Ha-shiiex yaox ka Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Siliex maix simiang Piien Ha-tax ploi aux hoek chawk ka maix,
jhawm saux nawh sang chix ang chix moi lie?”
10 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux,
nawh tag sang yum.
Viang mawh nan,
maix deeh ing krai ka nawh,
nawh tag sang deeh pon yaox tix moi heue.”
11 Khaix an,
Yi-li-sa dong ngai tix ka Ha-shiiex,
tom hoik ang nawh lai nyawm rhawm kah.
Dum mawx dum an,
Yi-li-sa jauh tix lih rawm ngai.
12 Ha-Shiiex chawk ah nin:
“Sara,
yuh ka mawx maix yiiem lie?”
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Mawh kheu hoik tawng aux maix sang chix yuh kaing pa lang teung caox,
mai laux yuh pui Yi-seu-liex.
Maix tag sang teei ngu gleeing tab kix laux,
pauh ka pui nhum ju kix yum,
pauh ka kawn nyawm kix yum,
mai baox sang pih ka vaig bun koe kaux heue.”
13 bHa-shiiex ah nin:
“Aux sawn tix mawh pa tix,
awm ngie sox tix mu khaing,
aux doh sang pon yuh kaing pa ting yuh ka mawx lie?”
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux,
maix tag sang mawh simiang pui Shui-li-yax heue.”
14 Ha-shiiex deeh ing ka Piien Ha-tax dee an,
Piien Ha-tax chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yi-li-sa krai ceu pa tix lie?”
Ha-shiiex ah nin:
“Nawh krai tag sang moi jhawm saux maix tete.”
15 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Ha-shiiex teei prux pa hoik cub lan daux rawm tix plah,
gut simiang yum ka heue.
Mawh kheu nan,
Ha-shiiex plud tix mawh simiang Shui-li-yax tang Piien Ha-tax heue.
Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-lang
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 21.1–20)
16 Yam giex kawn simex Ya-ha simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-heu-lang ka kaing daux phuan num,
kawn simex Yaw-sa-fa pa kaux Yaw-langc mawh simiang pui Yo-tax;
17 yam an nawh koe ju tix ngoe ra num.
Nawh giex kaing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung daix num.
18 Nawh teei tix moeng kawn bun Ya-ha.
Nawh awm pui nyiex ma Ya-ha yuh nan,
yuh kaing pa caox pa caig pa hoik yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
19 dViang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang rhawm cu tix yuh pui Yo-tax laux leeing;
mawh kheu hoik ap nawh lox ka kawn cau tix Ta-wue,
sang kieh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue koe pui mawh simiang leeing ju heue.
20 eGlang yam giex Yaw-lang ka kaing,
Yi-tong tieh vheen ka pui Yo-tax,
mai tang yuh tix lih mawh meung tix mu.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-lang thung ling law naing tix khawm uik hu ka Sa-yix,
pui Yi-tong lai lawm kix dee an.
Plak pon bo,
Yaw-lang mai cao bung pui naing ling law naing cie nawh yaog tix to;
pui naing cie nawh kawx tang deeh to ing ka nyiex tix.
22 Jauh yam an,
Yi-tong pon guah tix lih khaing pui Yo-tax.
Ka daux bawg yam an nang,
veng Li-na kawx tieh vheen heue.
23 Kaing yuh Yaw-lang tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
24 Yam yum nawh,
kix si paung nawh ka si meeig pui nyiex simiang ka daux veng Ta-wue;
kawn simex nawh Ya-ha-shiex plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 22.1–6)
25 Yam giex kawn simex Ya-ha pa kaux Yaw-heu-lang simiang pui Yi-seu-liex kaing daux kao ra num,
kawn simex Yaw-lang pa kaux Ya-ha-shiex mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
26 Yam koe ju Ya-ha-shiex tix nga ra num nawh mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix num.
Miex nawh kaux Ya-tha-li-ya,
mawh kawn bun simiang Ya-ha,
kawn saux bun Ang-lix simiang Yi-seu-liex.
27 Ya-ha-shiex kheu krax pa lai teei kix tix mex moeng paox tix,
lih tix mawh paox nyiex paox ma simiang Ya-ha;
nawh hoik awm pui nyiex ma Ya-ha yuh nan,
yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
28 Simiang Ya-ha-shiex rom tix mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-heu-lang,
hu puing ka simiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Ha-shiiex.
Pui naing khawm ra plak puing paox tix ka La-mawx haktiex Ci-liex.
Yaw-heu-lang mat,
29 mai deeh ing pai mat tix ka Yie-see-liex;
Ya-ha-shiex hoek jhak nawh ka dee an heue.
