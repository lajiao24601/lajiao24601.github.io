Pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan
31 Khaix hoik gah teug sijax mukheu hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
simiang Ta-wue ploi pui hoek mawh cao kuang lox rhax ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
32 Sivoe ang simiang Saw-law-meung bax sum ka nyiex buan baux yam an,
kix lai loh tix yuh kaing ka daux nyiex gox man Simiang Siyiex.

Doeh ceu pui Ya-loeng
49 Ya-loeng mai doeh ceu cad nawh khankix mawh cao pa yuh krax tan lu ngu bhawng,
khaix an mai tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu.
Kaing pa yuh kix kaing ting mawh son kaing krub khawm uik ka daux nyiex dee pa kied singax,
khaix an mai yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie.
Kix hawt tix yuh kaing in kix awm lox pa hoik krai siliex Siyiex pa mawh Maw-si ka kix,
son sang pluat ploi ka phi map pui Yi-seu-liex.
50 Doeh ceu pui Ya-loeng hawt ka keeing nawh toe tix hu mawh:

Dauh deeh ot pui Li-we
54 Plak greem mawh haktiex pa hoik guah ka doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng ceu pui cub nyiex Keu-siax pon.
Ka daux haktiex pa hoik pon pui Li-we,
ceu pui cub nyiex Keu-siax hoik pon ka daux nyum tix.
55 Haktiex in kix hoik mawh khawm haktiex Si-paw-loeng pa ot daux haktiex Yo-tax mai pak rib pa dex dee an,
56 viang mawh nan,
kaung ma pa mawh cie veng Si-paw-loeng mai nyiex yaong khankix,
mai hoik guah tix tawx ka kawn simex Yie-fu-ni pa kaux Cia-leux heue.
57-59 Veng pa hoik guah kix doeh ceu pui Ya-loeng pon plak greem mawh:
veng moxc Si-paw-loeng,
Ya-thi-eu,
veng pa dex ka Li-na,
Yi-seu-thi-maw,
Si-loeng,
Ti-pix,
Ya-sang,
Paw See-mai,
khawm pak rib pa dex ka veng in kix.
60 Ka daux haktiex Piien-ya-ming,
hoik guah kix pon ka Cia-pa,
A-leu-miiex,
mai Ya-na-thu,
khawm pak rib pa dex ka veng in kix.
Cub nyiex kix hoik yaox tix paung ot ka veng,
khawm uik koe kao loe mu veng.
61 Ceu pui cub nyiex Keu-siax pa deuh khankix,
cawk lai pon ka veng pa koe daux haktiex Ma-na-si plak klip singaix kao mu veng heue.
62 Ceu pui cub nyiex Keu-soeng ku cub,
hoik guah kix pon veng kao loe mu veng,
Yi-sa-cia,
Ya-seux,
Na-fu-tha-lix,
mai ka daux haktiex Ma-na-si plak lih singaix pa ot daux haktiex Pa-sang.
63 Ceu pui cub nyiex Mi-la-lix ku nyiex kawx,
hoik guah kix pon nang veng kao ra mu ka daux pa cawk lai,
ka haktiex Lui-piien,
Cia-teux,
mai ka daux haktiex Si-pu-loeng.
64 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik guah tix tawx pui Li-we ot ka veng yuh nin,
khawm pak rib pa dex ka veng hoik tawx ka kix. (
65 Veng pa hoik krai plak piang pa ot ka daux haktiex Yo-tax,
haktiex Si-miien,
haktiex Piien-ya-ming kawx,
hoik guah ka daux pa cawk lai heue.)
66 Ceu pui cub nyiex Keu-siax,
koe nyiex tix blah hoik guah kix pon ka veng pa koe ka daux haktiex Yi-fa-liien,
67 koe veng mox pa ot ka gawng Yi-fa-liien,
pa mawh See-ciien,
Ci-seux,
68 Yaw-miien,
Paw Heu-loeng,
69 Ya-ya-loeng,
Cia-theux Ling-meung,
mai pak rib pa dex tan.
70 Ka daux haktiex Ma-na-si plak klip singaix,
kix pon ka veng Ya-nai mai veng Pi-liien ra mu veng khawm pak rib pa dex tan heue.
71 Veng plak greem mawh pa hoik guah ka ceu pui cub nyiex Keu-soeng ku nyiex pon,
khawm pak rib pa dex tan mawh:
Ka daux haktiex Ma-na-si plak lih singaix mawh veng Keu-lang mai Ya-see-tha-lux pa koe ka daux Pa-sang heue.
72 Ka daux haktiex Yi-sa-cia,
koe veng Ci-ti-see,
veng Ta-pi-la,
73 veng La-mawx,
mai veng Ya-ning heue.
74 Ka daux haktiex Ya-seux,
koe veng Ma-sa,
veng Ya-toeng,
75 veng Hu-keux,
mai veng Li-haw heue.
76 Ka daux haktiex Na-fu-tha-lix,
koe veng Cia-ti-see,
veng Ha-meung,
mai veng Ci-lie-thing ka daux meung Cia-li-lix heue.
77 Veng plak greem pa hoik guah ceu pui cub nyiex Mi-la-lix pon,
khawm pak rib pa dex tan mawh:
Ka daux haktiex Si-pu-loeng,
mawh veng Ling-maw-naw mai veng Tha-pawx heue.
78 Ka daux haktiex Lui-piien,
mawh veng Pi-si pa ot luan khaing rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singax,
ka dee lung lhaong haktiex plak an ka Yie-li-keu,
khawm veng Ya-ha-sha,
79 veng Ci-ti-maw,
mai veng Mi-fa-ya heue.
80 Ka daux haktiex Cia-teux koe veng La-mawx pa ot ka haktiex Ci-liex,
veng Ma-ha-nian,
81 veng Si-seu-peung,
mai veng Ya-shiex pa ot ka daux Ci-liex heue.
