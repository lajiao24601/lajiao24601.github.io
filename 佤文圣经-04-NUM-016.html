Grawng pa tieh vheen Kheu-la, Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang
16
1-2 aKawn simex Yi-see-ha pa kaux Kheu-la mawh ceu pui Keu-siax cub pui Li-we;
nawh tieh vheen ka Maw-si.
Paox nawh mawh kawn simex Yi-li-ya pa kaux Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang ceu pui Lui-piien,
mai kawn simex Pi-leux pa kaux Ang,
khaix an kawn koe simiang pa ao min pa laug pui hun ra yieh tix phuan kaux.
3 Kix khrawm paox tix sivoe Maw-si mai Ya-loeng,
ah nin ka kiex:
“Pax kied sidaing kin pa yuh!
Can pui hun hoik mawh cie Simiang Siyiex;
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai yix.
Yuh ka mawx pax yaog to kaux tix ting lhaong,
lheu khaing pui hun cie Simiang Siyiex?”
4 Yam mhong Maw-si lox in,
nawh tom gup tix piang tiex rawg buan.
5 Khaix an nawh ah nin ka Kheu-la mai cub pui pa hawt nawh ah nin:
“Pon ngawb pa sax,
Simiang Siyiex tag jhi ex yaox tix,
mawx pa mawh pui cie nawh;
nawh sang kieh pui cie nawh,
pa laug nawh tix teei,
hoek dex mai nawh ka khu dee tan.
6-7 Pon ngawb pa sax,
maix mai pui pa hawt grawng maix viex nyawng bhawng hoek,
sau gaox ngu mai pa bhawng ot daux,
viex hoek ka sivoe khu dee tan.
Khaix an ex tom jhak,
ka daux ex mawh mawx pa laug Simiang Siyiex.
Pex pui Li-we in kix mawh pa sidaing kin tete!”
8 Maw-si baox ah nin ka Kheu-la:
“Pex pui Li-we,
nghiet!
9 Siyiex Yi-seu-liex hoik laug pex khaing pui hun,
son sang pon dex pex mai nawh,
yuh kaing ka daux nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
mawh cao pa liang mai yuh kaing son pui hun.
Pex ce in kawn mawh kaing pa iak lie?
10 Nawh tawx pex mai pui Li-we koe min,
pex ce sang baox pon beeh glawm cao giex kaing li lie?
11 Pex salao ka Ya-loeng,
ang pex tawng,
mawh pex mai paox pa hawt rhawm pex,
pa tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex!”
12 Maw-si ploi pui hu kok ka Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang,
viang mawh nan,
kieh ah nin:
“Yiex ang hu!
13 Maix viex yix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix pa mhawm,
mai kieh yix yum daux tiag,
in kawn ang nyang kub lie?
Maix kawn sang mawh simiang pa kuang ka piang yix lie?
14 Maix ang tie baox viex yix hoek ka haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia pa mhawm,
ang baox tawx kaung ma mai si peem plix siiet kieh mawh sin ngao yix.
Maix pie lhien ka yix;
yix ang hu!”
15 Maw-si kied song rhawm;
nawh ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Khiox khaw maix paw lai rieb ka krax tan pui in kix.
Aux ang sax yuh krax pa laux rhawm kix;
pie la iak kix tix mu aux ang sax teei.”
16 Maw-si ah nin ka Kheu-la:
“Pa sax maix mai pui pa hawt grawng maix ra yieh tix phuan kaux hoek ka nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex;
Ya-loeng kawx hoek dee an.
17 Pex ku kaux tag viex nyawng bhawng cie tix hoek,
sau pa bhawng ka daux,
mai tan ka piang khu dee tan.”
18 Khaix an kix ku kaux viex nyawng bhawng hoek,
sau gaox ngu mai pa bhawng ka daux,
cung tau mai Maw-si mai Ya-loeng ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man.
19 Kheu-la khrawm cub kawn pui hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
cung si tiep Maw-si mai Ya-loeng.
Dum mawx dum an,
pa riang min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex lih hoek piang pui hun.
20 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng:
21 “Yoi pax,
singai khaing pui in kix;
aux sang yuh kix laux ka daux yam in heue.”
22 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng gup pak ngai tix piang tiex,
ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
maix mawh num krax im ju khawm uik.
Pui tix kaux phit lud,
maix tom song rhawm ka pui hun khawm uik lie?”
23 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
24 “Maix krai ka pui hun,
nhie kix singai hu khaing nyiex bang Kheu-la,
Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang.”
25 Maw-si mai kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hu tau,
hoek ka Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang dee an.
26 Nawh ah nin ka kawn pui:
“Pex hu singai khaing nyiex bang pui caox in kix,
paw theeh ka kraung cie kix.
Phan pex ang nghiet,
pex tag yum tau mai kix kheu phi map pa yuh kix.”
27 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom lih hu singai khaing nyiex bang Kheu-la,
Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang.
Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang viex moeng kawn tix lih,
cung ka diang siviex nyiex bang kix.
28 Maw-si ah nin ka kawn pui:
“Pex tag tawng,
mawh Simiang Siyiex pa ploi aux yuh kaing in,
ang mawh to kaux aux pa yuh nin ka kix.
29 Phan pui in kix yum mhawm awm pui,
ang yaox krax ciyang,
Simiang Siyiex ang ploi aux hoek.
30 Viang mawh nan,
phan Simiang Siyiex yuh kaing ceu pa ang ex sax mhong,
haktiex bih,
mai bleet ka pui in kix khawm kraung kix khawm uik,
kieh kix cawd tix yum im ka daux meung yum,
pex tom taug tawng mawh pui in kix pa tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
31 Khaix hoik ah Maw-si lox,
tiex greem caong Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang bih,
32 mai bleet kix khawm pui nyiex ma kix,
khawm can pui pa hawt grawng Kheu-la mai kraung kix khawm uik.
33 Kix cawd tix yum im tau mai kraung krai kix meung yum.
Haktiex ka piang kix deeh nyeep tix,
mai ang lai yaox kix heue.
34 Can pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot hawt vung viang kix,
mhong ka lox yiiem kix,
tom to mai kok yo ah nin:
“Nied to!
Lhat haktiex bleet ka ex!”
35 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh ngu lih gleeing ka pui pa tan ka pa bhawng an ra yieh tix phuan kaux.
Mawk ngu nyawng bhawng
36 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
37 “Maix krai ka Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li,
kieh nawh teei pan ngu tawng pa deuh pui pa yum ka gleeing ngu khankix hu,
mai gaox ngu pa ot ka daux pan ngu an rhuat hu tang dauh,
mawh kheu pan ngu mawh pa singax singeh.
38 Yam tan pan ngu an ka khu dee tan Simiang Siyiex,
tag lih mawh pa singax singeh.
Grawh grawng nawh,
tag sang teei pan ngu pui pa khaw tix yum an kix hu,
tawk mawh khlawp,
yuh dawb tawb khu dee tan kah.
In tag mawh pa kieh pui Yi-seu-liex rien tix.”
39 Khaix an,
cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa teei pan ngu hoek,
tawk mawh khlawp,
yuh dawb tawb khu dee tan kah.
40 In mawh son sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex rien tix,
phan ang mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng ang pon tix hoek gleeing pa bhawng ka Simiang Siyiex ka khu dee tan;
phan koe pui pa lieh lheu,
tag sang yum awm Kheu-la mai pui pa hawt nawh khankix.
Kaing in mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm lox tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si pa kieh Yi-li-ya-sa yuh heue.
Ya-loeng taoh ka kawn pui
41 Ngaix khaix,
pui haoh pui hun salao ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng ah nin:
“Pex pauh ka pui cie Simiang Siyiex.”
42 Khaix hoik khrawm kix tix tieb tie ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng,
mai deeh veeh tix hu ka nyiex gox buan man.
Kix yaox pai awm hoek kheem ka piang nyiex gox buan man;
pa riang min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex lih hoek.
43 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hoek tix cung sivoe nyiex gox buan man,
44 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
45 “Lih hu singai khaing pui in kix.
Aux sang yuh kix laux leeing tin heue!”
Kiex ra kaux gup tix piang tiex;
46 Maw-si ah nin ka Ya-loeng:
“Teei pan ngu cie maix hoek,
mai sau gaox ngu pa koe ka piang khu dee tan ka daux,
mai baox sau nyawng bhawng tix blah ka piang gaox ngu an,
nied tix viex hoek ka pui hun dee an,
mai yuh been pa singax singeh son kix.
Nied hu!
Krax song rhawm Simiang Siyiex hoik hoek,
mai saux preng hoik jauh tix tauh hu.”
47 Ya-loeng nghiet ka lox Maw-si,
viex pan ngu bhawng laig ka dee khrawm pui hun tix.
Nawh yaox ka saux preng hoik lih,
tom sau pa bhawng piang gaox ngu,
yuh been pa singax singeh son pui haoh pui hun.
48 Jhawm saux preng naox tix.
Nawh cung ka si nax pui im mai pui yum.
49 Ang lai sawn pui pa yum kheu grawng pa tieh vheen Kheu-la,
pui pa yum bawg in koe tix mheen pon rheng aliah yieh kaux.
50 Khaix hoik naox jhawm saux preng tix,
Ya-loeng deeh ing ka Maw-si diang siviex nyiex gox buan man dee an heue.
