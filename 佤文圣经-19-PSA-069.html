Khiox khaw kok yo krax pa teem coi
69
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux liak!
Rawm ting hoik kheem hoek ka ngawg aux.
2 Aux hoik blawn ka daux glaox,
mai ang koe dee deuh caong tix;
aux hoik cawd ka dee raux rawm,
mai puk pak rawm hoik kheem ka aux.
3 Kheu aux hoik uik riang uik rao ka kok yo tix krax pa teem coi,
mai ghok aux hoik kroh;
son pa hoik een rhawm tix ka krax pa teem coi maix ngai aux hoik pu pi heue.
 
4 aPui pa nieb pon koeng ka aux ang koe krax koe grawng kawn lheu nie khaing hauk kaing aux;
pui pa tieb tie ka aux ang koe ceu koe cak,
pa keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing kied ting riang ting rao.
Kix tag sang nhie aux tawx ka ceu pa ang aux bax tud rhim.
5 Siyiex eue,
maix hoik dawg tawng aux mawh pui pa daox aw;
aux ang sax mox phi map tix ka maix tix bawg kawx.
6 Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong ka piang khawm uik,
pa mawh cao giex kaing kru naing kaing mheen eue.
Paw vi kieh pui pa yum rhawm ka maix hoek krax kaig krax tua kheu aux.
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
paw vi kieh pui pa krub ka maix kham krax pa map nai kheu aux.
7 Aux hoik mawh pui pa kham krax pa map nai jhax lawg kheu maix,
pak ngai aux hoik naog ka pa kaig pa tua.
8 Pui nyiex dex aux hoik singai hu khaing aux;
pui nyiex pui ma aux hoik kieh aux awm pui tang pui praw yuh nan heue.
 
9 bAux hoik koe krax gaux rhawm pa ting ka nyiex buan maix,
awm ngu pa rug yuh nan;
lox jhax lawg pui ka maix hoik cawd ka piang to kaux aux khawm uik.
10 Aux kieh to kaux tix kha rhawm tixc ka daux pa tuah priex preem,
kix tieh tix yuh krax pa kaig pa tua ka aux;
11 Aux ceep ka kraung man keei sum pa ceep yam laux rhawm laux rhi,
kix hoik nyiah ka aux khawm uik.
12 Kix krai lao ka aux ka daux laih daux cau,
pie pui yoek kawx tiiem lox rhax map nai ka aux heue.
 
13 Siyiex eue,
phan mawh aux,
aux tag sang khiox khaw ka maix;
Siyiex eue,
kheu hoik koe maix krax muh pa koe leeing ju,
kheu pa hoik tiex tu maix tix sang tawx ka krax taoh,
khiox khaw maix hawt ka bawg yam pa hoik tat maix tix deuh cu nghiet ka lox aux.
14 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux lih khaing daux glaox,
mai paw vi kieh aux blawn tix laig;
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui koeng ka dee raux rawm.
15 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh rawm kheem ka aux,
paw vi kieh dee kied raux bleet ka aux,
paw vi kieh nang daux si meeig si paung ka aux heue.
 
16 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix hawt awm pa mhawm pa mhiem krax muh maix cu nghiet ka aux;
khiox khaw maix hawt awm krax pa caox rhawm maix pa kied raux kia ngien ka aux.
17 Khiox khaw maix paw vi mox tix ang simi ka siliex tix;
yam ot aux ka daux krax pa khi rhawm,
khiox khaw maix nied tix cu nghiet ka lox aux.
18 Khiox khaw maix hoek tix taoh ka aux;
khiox khaw maix deeh taoh aux khaing daux tawm taix pui koeng heue.
 
19 Maix hoik tawng grawng pa kham aux krax pa map nai,
krax pa kaig tua;
maix hoik sidawng ka can pui pa koeng ka aux khawm uik.
20 Krax pa map nai hoik kieh rhawm rhi aux max,
hoik kieh aux deek rhawm deek rhi.
Aux simie krax pa nyawm rhawm,
mai ang pon;
aux keud ce ka krax pa nyawm rhawm,
mai ang pon nang.
21 dAux cai rhawm,
mai kix tawx sidah awp ka aux;
aux hoik simie tix nyaux rawm,
mai kix tawx rawm niex ka aux.
22 eKieh krax pa yuh kix poi ih priex preem kieh kix laux leeing;
kieh krax gaux rhawm pa yuh kix kieh kix som nai.
23 Kieh ngai kix faik,
mai ang chix yaox;
kieh nyoi blawng kix saux si klua ang jud ang jai heue.
24 Khiox khaw maix klup krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix ka piang kix;
kieh pa song rhawm rauh see maix krup ka kix.
25 fKieh dee bang kix riem;
kieh nyiex gox bang kix ang koe pui pa paung ot daux.
26 Kix tuk tiak tuk te ka pui pa hoik lauh maix;
kix nieb pon nyiah ka pui pa hoik lauh maix.
27 Khiox khaw maix ciyang ka kix tom kin,
kieh kix ang yaox tix pon ka krax pa taoh maix.
28 gKieh ceu kaux kix preet tix khaing daux phuk lai pa im ju maix;
kieh kix ang lai yaox tix ot ka daux lai ca rang pui cie maix heue.
 
29 Kheu nan,
aux saux nex saux nham,
aux laux rhawm laux rhi;
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka aux,
taoh ka aux heue.
 
30 Aux sang teei lox rhax yaog yaw Siyiex;
aux sang teei krax pa yaog yaw krai pao ka pa ting pa lhaong nawh.
31 In tag sang kieh Simiang Siyiex gaux rhawm,
mai kawn lheu khaing krax pa tan lu ka moi mhaing,
kawn lheu khaing pa tan lu ka kawn moi iak pa grum mhawm.
32 Pui pa gut kheem pui yaox ka tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
pui pa krub ka Siyiex hoik kied khaw rhawm kah.
33 Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox pui pa kha rhawm tix;
nawh ang piawm ka pui cie tix pa ot daux thawng heue.
 
34 Kieh rao ma mai haktiex yaog yaw ka Siyiex khawm uik;
rawm sigrit sigra mai to sat pa koe ka daux hoik rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
35 Siyiex tag sang taoh ka Shi-ang,
mai deeh cox sum ka veng Yo-tax;
pui cie nawh tag sang paung ot ka dee an,
mai pon tix cie ka haktiex an.
36 Doeh ceu cad siliex nawh tag sang plud tix pon ka haktiex an;
pui pa muh ka nawh tag sang paung ot dee an heue.
