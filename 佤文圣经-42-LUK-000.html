Lai Lu-cia
Krai dawt
Lai Lu-cia krai ka ex yuh nin: Ye-sux mawh cao taoh pa hoik ap Siyiex tix tawx ka pui Yi-seu-liex, nawh kawx hoik mawh nang cao taoh kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik. Lai Lu-cia tiiem grawng pon Ye-sux ka pa kok khuan Siyiex ka nawh “Sidiex gum lox buan ka pui hot pui sibhawm” (4.18), gum lox buan in hoik grum naog ka pa sang muh kawn pui tix siao ku ceu. Phuk lai in hoik naog ka krax gaux rhawm ku si dauh, nie mawh dee jauh simin ting ra loe mu, grawng pa lih keh Ye-sux mai pa nghoik nawh tix sidiex yam haok rao ma. Phan mawh pa krai hu mai pa vih ting ka gum lox buan khaix hoik haok nawh piang rao ma, cao tiiem phuk lai in hoik tiiem sidawng ka daux Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau heue.
Grawng ra, mai ka daux baux liah (jhak kaing ting) can phuk lai khawm uik, awm lox rhax kawn cau Siyiex ka sivie siveng Paw-li-haung, pa krub cao liang yung ka kawn ngie pa singax, yam mawh Ye-sux kawn nyawm ka daux nyiex buan, mai pui mhawm Sa-ma-li-yax khawm lox lao been ka keeing pa koe krax caox rhawm khankix, hoik yaox ngie ka daux phuk lai in khaing. Phuk lai in nie blawn kieh krax rawg buan, Khuan Singax Singeh, mai krax pa teem coi bun miex khankix ka kaing pa yuh Ye-sux, buan son pa pluat ploi Siyiex phi map, mai gum lox tawx Khrit ka kawn pui haktiex heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–4)
2. Yaw-hang cao cub com mai pa keh Ye-sux, khawm yam mawh nawh kawn nyawm (1.5–2.52)
3. Kaing pa yuh Yaw-hang cao cub com (3.1–20)
4. Pa com Ye-sux rawm mai pa kham krax nyaw glaw (3.21–4.13)
5. Kaing pa yuh Ye-sux ka Cia-li-lix (4.14–9.50)
6. Jauh ka Cia-li-lix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung (9.51–19.27)
7. Pa nghoik tix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai haktiex dex dee an (19.28-23.56)
8. Pa kaoh im Simiang Ye-sux, pa jhi nawh ka yix, mai pa haok nawh ka rao ma (24.1–53)
