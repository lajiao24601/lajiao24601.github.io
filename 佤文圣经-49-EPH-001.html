Lai Yi-fu-saw
1
bAux mawh Pao-law;
aux mawh kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit kheu alo Siyiex.
Aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka kawn Khrit pa kaux sijax khankix,
ka daux Ye-sux Khrit pa ot haktiex Yi-fu-sawa ah nin:
2 Kieh keeing Siyiex mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son,
mai pa nhim ka pex heue!
Khawng phak son pa dex mai khuan ka daux Khrit
3 Yaog yaw ka keeing Siyiex Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue!
Mawh kheu hoik tawx nawh buan son pa dex mai khuan khaing rao ma ku ceu ka daux Khrit ka ex.
4 Sivoe sang phan yuh haktiex,
nawh hoik laug ex ka daux Khrit,
mai kieh ex lih mawh pui pa singax singeh,
ang koe dee cud pie ceeix kawx ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.
5 Siyiex muh ka ex,
mai hoik grox tat tix kieh ex mawh pui cie tix ka daux Ye-sux Khrit,
kieh ex koe min ceu kawn simex kawn bun nawh;
in hoik mawh pa khaw rhawm nawh,
mawh alo nawh.
6 Kieh ex rhax lai yaog yaw buan son min plak ngai in ka Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik tawx nawh kawn simex pa muh tix ka ex giex giex going going heue!
7 cMawh kheu krax pa hoik yum Khrit,
ex tom pon tix luat luan,
phi map ex hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi.
In hoik mawh buan son pa kub pa kaoh khaing Siyiex.
8 Buan son pa tawx nawh ka ex hoik kub tete heue!
Siyiex teei krax pa lhak lhiao mai ping nyia
9 yuh alo nawh grum naog kah,
mai baox kieh ex tawng grawng pa hoik tat nawh tix yuh kaing pa raux pa mox ka daux Khrit.
10 Bawg yam cie Siyiex,
kaing pa sang yuh nawh hoik si dauh mawh nin:
tag sang kieh can ceu pa phan pa yuh ka piang rao ma,
mai ka piang haktiex khawm uik lih mawh cie Khrit,
kieh nawh mawh cao giex kaing heue.
11 Can ceu pa koe ka daux rao ma khawm uik,
tag sang hawt tix yuh awm alo Siyiex mai krax pa tat nawh yuh lih hoik uik.
Siyiex hawt ka alo pa dawg tat nawh tix deuh,
mai hoik laug ex mawh pui cie nawh ka daux Khrit.
12 Alo nawh mawh son sang kieh ex pa mawh pui in kix pa ran tix deuh rhawm tix ka Khrit sivoe,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka min plak ngai Siyiex heue!
13 Pex kawx hoik mawh yuh nin:
Yam hoik mhong pex ka gum lox pa mawh pa meh,
pa mawh gum lox buan pa kieh pex pon krax taoh an,
pex hoik yum rhawm ka Khrit,
Siyiex tom ap Khuan Singax Singeh gum lox pa hoik deuh nawh ka pex,
mai kieh lih mawh mhai cum grawng mawh pex cie nawh.
14 Khuan Singax Singeh giex kaux cong grawng sang pon ex ka gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex,
mai tawx krax pa luat luan khawm uik ka pui cie tix.
Ex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka min plak ngai nawh heue!
Lox rawg buan Pao-law
15 Mawh kheu nan,
jauh yam mhong aux grawng krax yum rhawm pex ka Simiang Ye-sux,
khawm grawng krax muh pex ka kawn Khrit khankix,
16 aux tag ah lox pa ting buan son ka Siyiex son pex ang jud.
Aux doh keud ka pex ka daux krax pa rawg buan,
17 khiox khaw ka Siyiex pa mawh Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit,
keeing Siyiex pa ting min,
mai tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ka pex.
Khuan Singax Singeh in chix tawx ping nyia ka pex,
mai jhi pex tawng ka Siyiex.
18 Aux kawx khiox khaw Siyiex paoh ka rhawm rhi pex,
son sang kieh pex tawng:
krax keud pot jun pot pa kok nawh pex hoek tix pon mawh ceu tix;
sin ngao pa hoik ap nawh tix tawx ka pui cie tix,
kied haoh hun tete.
19-20 dSiawp sibrawm pa hoik yuh nawh ka daux ex pa yum rhawm ka nawh,
kied ting lhaong tete.
Siawp sibrawm in hoik sidu tau mai riang rao pa kieh nawh Khrit deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
kieh nawh Khrit ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm tix ka piang rao ma.
21 Ka dee an,
krax pa giex Khrit kaing hoik lhaong khaing khuan pa caox pa caig,
pa mawh krax kuang simiang,
krax pa ting siawp sibrawm,
krax cao giex kaing,
mai krax cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik;
nawh hoik lhaong khaing min ceu kaux pa koe yam in mai singaix pa sang hoek.
22 efSiyiex baox kieh rao ma mai ceu pa koe khawm uik,
dip ot ka greem caong Khrit,
kieh nawh mawh cao pa giex kaing ka ceu cao,
mai kuang ka ceu pa koe khawm uik.
23 Ceu cao mawh to kaux Khrit,
mawh Khrit kaw pa kieh grum naog ka rao ma mai ka ceu pa koe khawm uik heue.g
