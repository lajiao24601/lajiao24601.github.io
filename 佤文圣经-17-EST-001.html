Lai Yi-see-thie
Simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu tik ka moeng tix Hua-seu-tix
1
1-2 aKaing in plak greem mawh pa kaoh khaing baux bawg yam simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu.
Ya-ha-sui-lu jauh tix kuang ka veng ting ot simiang Su-sang,
mee haktiex jauh ka Ying-tux hoek ka Ku-seu,
khawm uik koe tix yieh tix nga aliah dauh.
3 Yam mawh Ya-ha-sui-lu simiang ka daux loe num,
nawh yuh poi ting son cao pa ting min mai simiang pa ot greem nawh.
Paw-see mai simiang Mi-ti-yax,
simiang haktiex ku dauh mai pui pa ting min hoek ku kaux heue.
4 Ka daux liah khix,
nawh tawx kix yaox krax mi,
krax ting lhaong,
krax koe siawp sibrawm meung tix heue.
5 Khaix an,
simiang baox yuh poi ting son simex ka daux veng ting ot simiang Su-sang,
hot ang ah,
mi ang ah,
ka daux si peem tai simiang,
yuh hoek aliah ngaix.
6 Si peem tai in bre ka man dah pa klai rao ma mai pa paing,
teei maox pa lung pliex man keei sum vien ka piang rong ka daux chawk mau simaox pa mhawm plang.
Khaox ngawm pa yuh ka mau khri deuh ka piang tiex pa paing,
pa raoh,
pa klai simaox pa mhawm plang mai khlawp siang ploi.
7 Kawk pa nyaux plai mawh kawk khri pa koe tai ku ceu.
Simiang si pieh rhawm tix tawx ka pui nyiex simiang siao kah,
8 mai tawx lox ka siliex pa ot ka daux nyiex haw,
kieh pui ku kaux teei rhawm tix nyaux.
9 Khaix an,
ka daux nyiex haw,
moeng simiang Hua-seu-tix kawx yuh nang poi son bun miex khankix heue.
10 Ka daux poi aliah ngaix,
simiang nyaux plai kied gaux rhawm,
mai tawx lox ka pui sieh tix aliah kaux pa mawh Mi-hu-mang,
Pi-see-tha,
Ha-paw-na,
Pi-keu-tha,
Ya-pa-tha,
Si-ta mai Cia-cia
11 hu kok ka moeng simiang Hua-seu-tix ceep ka mhok moeng simiang hoek.
Moeng simiang mawh bun pa kied mhawm;
simiang keud tix sang tawx pui greem tix yaox pa kied mhawm nawh.
12 Viang mawh nan,
yam tawx pui sieh lox pa kok simiang ka moeng simiang Hua-seu-tix,
nawh thiang kah.
Kaing in kieh simiang kied song rhawm,
ha rhawm.
13 Simiang koe been tix ceu,
yam si pup nawh ka kaing grawng lox tat jhawm prawng mai krax ot krax koe,
tom hu chawk ka rhawm keud pui pa ting ping nyia,
kheu nan,
nawh tom khrawm ka pui pa kied tawng grawng kaing in hoek.
14 Pui pa kied yum rhawm nawh mawh:
Cia-see-na,
See-tax,
Ya-ma-tha,
Tha-see-see,
Mi-lix,
Ma-si-na,
Mi-maw-kang.
Pui in kix mawh cao kaing aliah kaux ka daux Paw-see mai Mi-ti-yax,
dex mai simiang si jheeing,
ka daux meung kix mawh pa kied ting min.
15 Simiang chawk ka kix:
“Aux,
pa mawh simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu hoik kieh pui sieh tawx lox ka moeng simiang Hua-seu-tix,
nawh thiang ka mai ang hoek.
Hawt lox tat jhawm prawng,
ex sang prawng yuh ka mawx?”
16 Mi-maw-kang ah nin ka simiang mai cao kaing tang kaux:
“Moeng simiang Hua-seu-tix ang joeng kieh ngie simiang laux pak ngai,
mai hoik kieh nang pui simiang ka daux haktiex ku daux laux pak ngai!
17 Yam mhong bun ka daux meung grawng kaing pa yuh moeng simiang,
tag jauh tix iak jhak ka mex tix.
Kix tag chix ah:
‘Simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu tawx lox ka moeng simiang Hua-seu-tix,
kieh nawh hoek ka tix,
nawh thiang kah.’
18 Yam mhong bun ka daux Paw-see mai Mi-ti-yax khankix ka kaing pa yuh moeng simiang,
mai phai krai kaing in ka mex tix;
yuh nin,
tag koe krax iak jhak mai lox pa ang naog rhawm tom nie.
19 Phan Vawng keud khaw,
mai tawx lox tix gum:
jauh in singaix,
Hua-seu-tix ang pon tix hoek yaox ka simiang,
mai tiiem ka lox in lih,
kieh mawh lox tat jhawm prawng ka daux Paw-see mai Mi-ti-yax,
ang pon tix leeing ju.
Vawng chix tix tawx min moeng simiang ka bun tang kaux pa lheu mhawm khaing nawh.
20 Khaing hoik hoek lox Vawng ka meung ku daux,
bun ku kaux,
mex nawh hot ang ah,
mi ang ah,
tom tag nab yam yung yi ka mex tix heue.”
21 Simiang mai cao kaing greem nawh ah lox in kied mhawm,
tom hawt tix yuh awm lox pa ah Mi-maw-kang.
22 Simiang tawx lox ka haktiex ku dauh ka lai ku ceu:
mex pui ku kaux tag mawh cao ka daux nyiex;
lox nawh tag koe siawp sibrawm heue.
