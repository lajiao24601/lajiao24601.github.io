24
Yam in Pa-lang hoik tawng Simiang Siyiex sang nawb buan ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
kheu nan ka daux bawg in,
nawh ang lai hu rawg awm bawg sivoe,
son sang yaox ka mhai sakse.
Nawh veeh ngai tix hu ka plak daux tiag,
2 yaox ka nyiex bang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
pa hoik sum tix ot dee an cub mawx cub an,
Khuan Siyiex lih laig ka daux nawh,
3 nawh tom krai ka lox jhai in plak greem:
Gum lox Pa-lang kawn simex Pi-eux,
pui pa pon tix yaox an sidawnga hoik krai,
4 Nawh hoik pon mhong,
Siyiex hoik krai ceu pa tix.
Aux yaox riang su sai cao pa ting siawp sibrawm,
ka daux ca maox tix pa ang ngai tix hiex sidawng ka an.
5 Nyiex gox bang Yi-seu-liex kied mhawm!
Bang dee ot Ya-keux kied grang!
6 Nawh awm khaox jhie yaong pa dia tix hawt paox,
awm si peem tai ka praok rawm klawng,
awm khaox Acaw pa hoik sum Simiang Siyiex,
awm khaox si oe bhawng pa kuah ka praok klawng iak.
7 Kix koe rawm ciex tom nie,
kix rhuat simie ka daux kaung ma pa caux.
Simiang kix kawn lheu ting lhaong khaing Ya-cia;
meung haktiex nawh tag tauh hu ka piang haktiex khawm pon daog.
8 Siyiex viex kix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix;
awm moi tiag yuh nan pa yuh naing son kix.
Kix tag yuh laux ka pui pa koeng,
tag gut max ka si-ang kix,
tag raig pot ka te puing kix.
9 bMeung in tag awm sivai sang si mhaing pa nyat tix it,
awm sivai sang si siang pa laig it,
ang koe pui pon so haing.
Pui pa nawb buan ka Yi-seu-liex,
tag pon buan son tete,
pui pa jhax lawg ka Yi-seu-liex,
tag yaox krax pa laux leeing tete.
10 Pa-leux song rhawm mai lua tawm taix tix,
ah nin ka Pa-lang:
“Aux kok maix hoek jhax lawg ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
mai maix hoik nawb buan ka piang kix loe bawg.
11 Maix deeh ing khaing!
Aux hoik ap tix sang tawx ka riang maix,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang kieh maix pon heue.”
12 Pa-lang pawk ah nin:
“Aux hoik krai ka pui pa ploi maix hoek,
13 Viang maix sang tawx kawx mau khri can pa koe daux nyiex haw tix khawm uik,
aux kawx tag ang pon tix hawt alo tix,
mai lheu lox tawm Simiang Siyiex.
Aux tag sang krai ngie lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux khaing heue.”
Lox jhai Pa-lang bawg nghoik
14 Pa-lang ah nin ka Pa-leux:
“Yam in aux tag sang deeh hu ka paox ceu paox cad tix khankix.
Sivoe sang hu aux,
aux tag sang krai ka maix,
mai sang kieh maix tawng,
singaix pa sang hoek,
pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang yuh ka mawx ka kawn pui cie maix heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Pa-lang krai lox jhai in plak greem:
Gum lox Pa-lang kawn simex Pi-eux,
pui pa pon tix yaox an sidawng hoik krai,
16 Nawh pon mhong Siyiex hoik krai ceu pa tix,
hoik pon ka ngai lig ngai lai khaing cao pa ting siawp sibrawm.
Aux yaox riang su sai cao pa ting siawp sibrawm,
ka daux ca maox pa ang ngaix tix hiex sidawng kah an.
17 Aux kok simi tix jhak ka daux singaix pa sang hoek;
aux yaox ka meung ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Koe simuing tix mu tag sang lih hoek khaing Ya-keux;
koe simiang tix kaux lih kaoh khaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh tag toing max ka simiang Maw-ya,
yuh can pui Si-eux laux khawm uik.
18 Nawh tag sang pon kuang ka pui pa koeng ka Yi-tong,
mai pon cax ka haktiex kix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex puing pex ka jheeng jheeng.
19 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag sang dug dig ka kix,
Tag sang yuh pui pa deuk khankix laux uik.
20 Khaix an,
Pa-lang yaox pui Ya-ma-lix ka daux riang su sai,
nawh tom krai ka lox jhai in plak greem:
Viang ceu pui Ya-ma-lix mawh meung pa kied ting riang ting rao kawx,
dee si dauh tag mawh pa grai grup.
21 Pa-lang yaox ceu pui Ci-ni ka daux riang su sai,
nawh tom krai ka lox jhai in plak greem:
Dee ot maix dawg mawh pa nyawm ot,
awm mheem sim ot ka daux rang.
22 Viang mawh nan,
pui Ci-ni tag laux leeing;
pui Ya-sux tag sang giex tix laung ka pex.
23 Pa-lang baox krai ka lox jhai;
pa khrawm ka pui haktiex plak laong mawh pui mawx lie?
24 Koe pui tud rhim brug ka reu kaoh khaing haktiex Ci-thix,
kix sang yuh tix pon kuang ka meung Ya-sux mai meung Si-paw,
viang mawh nan kawx,
kix tag laux leeing.
25 Khaix an,
Pa-lang kaoh ing ka nyiex tix,
mai Pa-leux kawx deeh ing heue.
