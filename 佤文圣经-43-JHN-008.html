Bun pa yuh krax pa phit vao
8
[
1 Pui hun hoik ing khawm uik,
viang mawh nan Ye-sux hoek ka gawng sime.
2 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
nawh deeh ing ka nyiex buan;
pui hun hoek tix sawg nawh khawm uik,
nawh tom ngawm,
mai jauh tix sidiex gau ka kix.
3 Sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai pui Fa-li-saix viex bun tix kaux hoek;
bun an mawh pa pon giex yam yuh krax pa phit vao.
Kix kieh nawh cung si nax kix,
4 mai chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
bun in mawh pa pon giex yam yuh krax pa phit vao.
5 a“Ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si tawx lox ka ex,
bun pa yuh nin tag caw tix teei simaox tawk yum kah.
Maix krai sang yuh ex ka mawx ka nawh lie?”
6 Kix keud tix sang teei lox in yuh Ye-sux laux,
mai sawg grawng sang deuh phi map piang nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux nom tix,
mai teei giien taix tix tiiem ngai lai piang tiex.
7 Kix ca kawn chawk nawh ang jud,
Ye-sux tom rauh nyoi tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Ka daux pex pui mawx ang sax yuh ka phi map,
pui an chix tix ran teei simaox tawk nawh ka heue.”
8 Khaix hoik krai nawh lox in,
nawh baox nom tix tiiem ngai lai ka piang tiex.
9 Kix mhong ka lox in,
mai tang lhua tix hu kaux mawx kaux an,
jauh ka pa riex ju hoek ka pa num ju,
deuk ngie Ye-sux mai bun pa ca kawn cung dee an khaing.
10 Ye-sux tom rauh nyoi tix,
mai chawk bun an ah nin:
“Bun,
kix hoik hu dee mawx khawm uik lie?
Ang koe pui pa ot tix prawng ca ka phi map maix tix kaux kawx lie?”
11 Nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
ang koe heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mhawm,
aux kawx ang prawng ca ka phi map maix.
Hu heue,
paw lai baox yuh ka phi map heue!”] b
Ye-sux mawh pa riang haktiex
12 cYe-sux baox ah nin ka pui hun:
“Aux mawh pa riang haktiex;
pui pa hawt ka aux,
tag sang pon ka pa riang ju,
mai ang doh lai hu ka daux pa faik heue.”
13 dPui Fa-li-saix ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hoik kham mhai sakse son to kaux tix;
gum lox pa krai maix ang koe ceu heue.”
14 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Viang aux hoik kham mhai sakse son to kaux tix kawx,
gum lox pa krai aux kawx hoik mawh tete;
mawh kheu hoik tawng aux kaoh tix khaing dee mawx,
mai sang hu ka dee mawx.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang tawng aux kaoh khaing dee mawx,
mai sang hu ka dee mawx.
15 Pex hoik jhak ka diag tong pui mai prawng ca ka pui;
viang mawh nan,
aux ang prawng ca pui tix kaux kawx.
16 Phan aux prawng ca ka pui,
krax pa prawng ca aux kawx hoik mawh pa khaw caw;
mawh kheu ang mawh aux pa prawng ca nong nong,
mai hoik mawh keeing aux pa mawh kaw pa ploi aux hoek an pa prawng ca tau mai aux.
17 eKa daux lai lox tat jhawm prawng hoik tiiem tix deuh,
phan koe sakse pa kham pui ra kaux khaw paox tix,
sakse pa kham kiex tag sawn tix koe ceu.
18 Aux kham sakse son to kaux tix;
kaw pa ploi aux hoek an kawx,
hoik kham sakse son aux nang heue.”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
kix chawk ah nin:
“Keeing maix ot dee mawx?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pex ang tawng yung ka aux,
mai ka keeing aux kawx pex ang tawng yung kah;
phan pex tawng yung ka aux,
ka keeing aux kawx pex hoik tawng yung ka heue.”
20 Gum lox in hoik mawh pa krai Ye-sux yam sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex krao nyiex buan.
Yam an ang koe pui pa giex ka nawh,
mawh kheu ang bawg yam cie nawh nyang bax hoek heue.
Dee hu aux pex ang pon hu kah
21 Ye-sux baox ah nin ka kix:
“Aux tag sang hu heue;
pex tag sang sawg ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang yum ka daux phi map to kaux tix.
Dee sang hu aux,
pex ang pon hu ka heue.”
22 Simiang pui Yo-thaix tom ah nin:
“Nawh ah nin:
‘Dee sang hu aux,
pex ang pon hu kah’,
khex sang plaux nawh tix yum lie?”
23 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing haktiex,
aux mawh pa kaoh khaing rao ma;
pex hoik mawh pui pa dex mai haktiex,
aux ang mawh pa dex mai haktiex.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ah nin,
pex tag sang yum ka daux phi map to kaux tix.
Phan pex ang yum rhawm ka grawng pa mawh aux kaw ‘pa tang koe leeing ju’ an tix kaux,
pex tag sang yum ka daux phi map to kaux tix heue.”
25 Kix tom chawk ah nin:
“Mawh maix pui mawx?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux hoik dawg krai ka pex tom yam jauh heuef.
26 Phan mawh son pex,
koe grawh grawng tom nie aux caw tix krai,
caw tix prawng ca.
Viang mawh nan,
kaw pa ploi aux hoek an hoik mawh pa mawh pa meh;
aux krai ngie ka ceu pa hoik mhong tix khaing nawh dee an ka kawn pui haktiex heue.”
27 Kix ang tawng grawng kaing ceu pa hoik krai Ye-sux pa dex dawt mai Keeing.
28 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux krai kix mhong ah nin:
“Yam yaog pex kawn kawn pui,
pex tag sang tawng aux tag mawh kaw ‘pa tang koe leeing ju’,
khaix an tawng grawng ang aux yuh ceu tid tix ka daux to kaux tix,
aux krai ngie ka krax pa sidiex gau keeing aux ka aux khaing.
29 Kaw pa ploi aux hoek an ot tau mai aux;
nawh ang tik vud aux ot nong nong tix,
mawh kheu hoik yuh aux kaing ceu pa khaw ka rhawm nawh tom si dauh sidu heue.”
30 Pui hun mhong lox pa krai Ye-sux in kix,
kix tom yum rhawm ka nawh heue.
Pui pa hoik bheeik luan mai siliex cawng
31 Ye-sux krai ka pui Yo-thaix pa hoik yum rhawm ka nawh khankix ah nin:
“Phan pex priex tix hawt ka krax pa sidiex gau aux jheeng jheeng,
mai pex tag mawh pui gau aux tete.
32 Pex tag sang chix tawng yung ka pa mawh pa meh,
mai pa mawh pa meh tag sang chix kieh pex pon krax pa bheeik luan heue.”
33 gKix pawk ah nin:
“Yix mawh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang;
yix ang sax yuh siliex cawng pui mawx tix tix bawg kawx,
maix krai ‘pex tag pon tix bheeik luan’,
gum lox in dee bleeh mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
34 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
pui pa yuh phi map ku kaux hoik mawh siliex cawng phi map khawm uik.
35 Siliex cawng ang chix yaox tix kaux cong ka daux nyiex ma tix,
viang mawh nan,
kawn simex hoik mawh pa dex dawt tau mai nyiex ma tix tom si dauh.
36 Phan kawn simex Siyiex hoik kieh pex pon krax pa bheeik luan,
pex tag mawh pui pa pon tix bheeik luan tete heue.
37 Aux hoik tawng grawng pa mawh pex doeh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik keud tix sang yuh aux laux,
mawh kheu ang pex rieb ka krax pa sidiex gaux aux.
38 Pa krai aux hoik mawh jhi keeing aux ka aux,
mai pex hoik yuh ka ceu pa krai keeing pex ka pex heue.”
39 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Tax du yix mawh Ya-paw-la-hang heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan pex rug mawh doeh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang tete,
pex tee rien tix yuh kaing ceu pa hoik yuh Ya-paw-la-hang.
40 Aux hoik krai ngie pex mhong ka ceu pa mawh pa meh pa mhong aux khaing Siyiex dee an,
viang mawh nan,
pex keud tix sang pauh ka aux.
Ya-paw-la-hang ang sax yuh ka kaing ceu in heue!
41 Pex hoik yuh ka kaing ceu pa hoik yuh keeing pex heue.”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Siyiex mawh keeing yix pa koe tix kaux khaing;
yix ang mawh kawn pa nghe nga heue!”
42 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Phan Siyiex mawh keeing pex tete,
pex tee rien tix muh ka aux;
mawh kheu mawh aux pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an,
mai aux hoik ot dee in.
Aux ang hoek son to kaux tix,
mai mawh nawh pa ploi aux hoek.
43 Yuh ka mawx pex lai ang tawng ka lox aux lie?
Mawh kheu ang pex kieh gum lox aux laig daux yhaok tix.”
44 “Pex hoik dawg mawh kawn praix Phi-caox,
mai simie tix hawt tix yuh ka alo keeing tix.
Tom yam jauh nawh hoik mawh cao pa keud tix sang pauh ka pui,
mai ang sax cung tix ot plak krax pa mawh pa meh,
mawh kheu ang nawh sax koe ka krax pa mawh pa meh.
Krax pa pie lhien nawh hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing rhawm rhi nawh;
mawh kheu hoik dawg mawh nawh kaw pa pie lhien,
mai hoik mawh nang num riah krax pa pie lhien.
45 Kheu hoik krai aux krax pa mawh pa meh,
pex tom ang yum rhawm ka aux.
46 Ka daux pex pui mawx sang pon jhi ka phi map aux lie?
Aux hoik krai krax pa mawh pa meh,
yuh ka mawx pex lai ang yum rhawm ka lie?
47 Can pa mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex ku kaux,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox Siyiex.
Pex ang mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an,
mawh kheu nan,
pex ang nghiet ka heue.”
Ye-sux mai Ya-paw-la-hang
48 Pui Yo-thaix chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yix krai mawh maix pui Sa-ma-li-yax,
khaix an koe phi laig ka daux to kaux maix,
khex ang lox krai yix mawh lie?”
49 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phi ang bax laig ka daux to kaux aux;
aux hoik nab yam ka keeing tix,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik map nai ka aux.
50 Aux ang sawg min plak ngai to kaux tix,
viang mawh nan,
koe tix kaux pa khiox khaw son aux,
pa yuh ka daux pa rauh pa see.
51 Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
pui pa priex tix hawt ka krax pa sidiex gau aux,
tag ang yaox tix yum leeing ju heue.”
52 Kix krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Yam in yix nie hoik tawng sidawng grawng laig phi ka daux to kaux maix heue!
Ya-paw-la-hang hoik yum,
cao jhai khankix kawx hoik yum,
viang mawh nan,
maix krai ‘pui pa priex tix hawt ka krax pa sidiex gau maix tag ang yaox tix yum leeing ju heue’.
53 Maix lai ah kawn lheu ting tix khaing tax du yix Ya-paw-la-hang lie?
Ya-paw-la-hang hoik yum,
cao jhai khankix kawx hoik yum,
maix hoik kieh to kaux tix mawh pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?”
54 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Phan aux yaog yaw min plak ngai to kaux tix,
min plak ngai aux ang koe ngawh jum pie ceeix kawx.
Kaw pa yaog yaw min plak ngai aux hoik mawh keeing aux,
pa mawh kaw pa hoik krai pex Siyiex an.
55 Pex ang sax tawng yung ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng yung ka nawh.
Phan aux krai ang tawng yung ka nawh,
aux tag pie lhien awm pex yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng yung ka nawh,
khaix an mai hoik priex tix hawt ka krax pa sidiex gau nawh.
56 Tax du pex Ya-paw-la-hang hoik sax gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix keud pot jun pon ka singaix pa sang hoek aux;
yam yaox nawh kah,
nawh tag gaux rhawm gaux rhi eue.”
57 Kix krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix kawn ang nyang bax koe tix phuan num,
hoik yaox maix ka Ya-paw-la-hang lieh?”
58 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
sivoe sang lih koe Ya-paw-la-hang,
aux hoik dawg ‘koe’ heue.”
59 Mawh kheu nan,
kix sut simaox sang tawk nawh kah,
viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux mox tix,
mai lih hu khaing nyiex buan heue.
