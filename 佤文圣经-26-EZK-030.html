Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka Ai-cix
30
Simiang Siyiex baox si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai lox jhai,
krai pao ka lox pa tag sang krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex.
Maix tag caw tix kok yo lox pa ting si num ah nin:
Hoik laux leeing heue,
singaix pa lhat graung hoik hoek heue!
3 Singaix bawg yam sang yuh Simiang Siyiex krax laux hoik hoek;
an hoik mawh singaix pa ceep pai awm ka rao ma,
mai pa kham meung ka piang haktiex krax ciyang.
4 Ai-cix tag sang si pup krax pa laux leeing ka naing;
Ku-seu tag sang kha rhawm tix.
To kaux yum pui Ai-cix hoik naog ka daux haktiex khawm uik;
pui tud rhim ka piang meung khawm uik,
mai kieh lih mawh haktiex pa si dawm heue.
5 “Jauh ka Ku-seu,
Fu,
Lui-ti-yax,
A-la-pawx,
Ku-pux,
khawm can pui naing pa vai hoek khaing ka daux pui cie aux khankix tag sang yum khawm uik heue.”
6 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Jauh ka Mi-tawx pa koe ka haktiex plak laong hoek ka Seu-fu-ni pa koe ka haktiex plak seh,
can pui pa kia ngien ka Ai-cix tag sang yum khawm uik;
pui naing pa kua cie Ai-cix tag sang yum.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin.
7 Ai-cix tag sang lih mawh haktiex dee blawn riem ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
veng Ai-cix tag sang lheu riem khaing veng pa koe khawm uik.
8 Yam ploi aux ngu gleeing ka Ai-cix,
khaix hoik pauh aux ka pui naing pa pau ka veng yum uik,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
9 “Yam laux leeing Ai-cix ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang ploi kawn cau brug reu hu yuh krax pa suk sag ka pui Ku-seu pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai kieh kix pru praoh yaoh yee.
Ka daux singaix an hoik hoek heue!”
10 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang teei taix simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha yuh sin ngao Ai-cix laux leeing.
11 Nawh tag sang thung pui naing pa kied kua khankix hoek tix yuh Ai-cix laux leeing.
Kix tag sang teei vaig pliah yuh naing kah,
to kaux yum tag sang naog ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik.
12 Aux tag sang kieh rawm klawng Ni-law kroh,
mai ap tix tawx Ai-cix ka daux tawm taix pui caox.
Pui meung tang meung tag sang hoek tix yuh meung khawm uik laux leeing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
13 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka riang rao krub pui mai ka siyiex pa pie lhien pa ot ka Naw-fu.
Ang koe pui pa giex kaing piang Ai-cix;
aux tag sang kieh pui Ai-cix pru praoh yaoh yee.
14 Aux tag sang kieh haktiex Ai-cix plak seh riem;
aux tag sang ploi ngu gleeing ka veng Saw-ang pa koe ka haktiex plak laong,
mai ciyang ka veng ting Ti-pi-see.
15 Aux tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang veng Suing,
pa mawh tab veng pa blawn kaux cong Ai-cix,
mai yuh laux ka sin ngao Ti-pi-see.
16 Aux tag sang ploi ngu gleeing ka Ai-cix;
veng Suing tag sang pon krax pa laux leeing.
Guh veng Ti-pi-see tag sang laux;
pui koeng tag sang awm rawm ting pa buing tix laig ka daux veng yuh nan.
17 “Pui pa nhum ju ka daux veng Si-li-phaw-li,
mai ka daux veng Pi-paw-see tag sang yum khawm uik;
pui tag sang giex laung ka pui pa deuk khankix.
18 Yam yuh aux ka riang rao Ai-cix mai ka puing naing kua pa hoik yaw nawh rang di ka khankix laux leeing,
pa faik tag sang tawb kheem ka Ta-pi-nix.
Lon pai awm tag sang kheem ka Ai-cix,
pui pa paung ot haktiex Ai-cix ku veng tag sang lih mawh pui pa giex tix laung.
19 Aux ciyang Ai-cix yuh nin,
mai kix taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Riang rao simiang Ai-cix pa laux leeing
20 Yam vuk vied vuk viang yix ka daux kao tix num haok khix phuan aliah singaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
21 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
aux hoik lauh pot ka plawng taix simiang Ai-cix,
ang koe pui pa kie son nawh,
pai nawh moi,
son sang kieh nawh deeh baox pon giex ka vaig.
22 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
aux tag sang tieb tie ka simiang Ai-cix.
Aux tag sang lauh ka plawng taix nawh khawm ra khawn pot,
mawh plawng taix pa mhawm ang ah,
mawh pa hoik pot ang ah,
tag sang lauh pot khawm uik,
kieh vaig pa koe daux tawm taix nawh cawd piang tiex.
23 Aux tag sang ploi pui Ai-cix hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
24 Khaix an,
aux tag sang kieh plawng taix simiang Pa-pi-loeng koe riang,
mai tawx vaig cie tix ka daux tawm taix nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang lauh ka plawng taix simiang Ai-cix pot,
nawh tag sang riag yo ka sivoe ngai pui koeng,
mai yum heue.
25 Aux tag sang kieh nawh iak riang iak rao,
mai kieh simiang Pa-pi-loeng ting riang ting rao.
Khaix hoik tawx aux vaig tix ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng;
nawh tag sang nyeu vaig ka plak Ai-cix,
mai pui ku kaux tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
26 Aux tag sang ploi pui Ai-cix hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
yam an,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
