Ya-paw-lang taoh ka Law-teux
14
Simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa mawh Ang-la-fe,
simiang Yi-la-sha pa mawh Ya-liiex,
simiang Yi-lang pa mawh Ci-ta-lao-max,
mai simiang Keu-ying pa mawh Thi-ta,
2 Kix yuh naing ka simiang Saw-to-ma pa mawh Pi-la,
simiang Eu-maw-la pa mawh Pi-sa,
simiang Ya-ma pa mawh See-na,
simiang Si-piien pa mawh Sang-yi-piiex,
simiang Pi-la (baox kaux Saw-eux) phuan kaux.
3 Simiang in phuan kaux khrawm paox tix daux ngawd Si-ting;
ngawd Si-ting hoik mawh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog yam in.
4 Kix yuh siliex simiang Ci-ta-lao-max kao ra num,
mai yam hoek kao loe num kix tieh vheen ka nawh.
5 Ka daux kao pon num,
simiang Ci-ta-lao-max mai simiang pa ot plak nawh viex pui naing tix,
puing pex ka pui Li-fa-ying ka veng Ya-theu-lui Cia-ling,
puing pex ka pui Su-si ka lung Ha-mai,
mai puing pex ka pui Yi-mi ka haktiex Ci-lie-thing,
6 puing pex ka pui Heu-lix ka piang gawng Yi-tong,
mai leue kix tom hoek ka praok daux tiag Yi-leu-pa-lang.
7 Khaix an,
kix dee deeh hoek ka veng Cia-ti-see (yam an kaux An-mi-pa),
mai pon cam ka can haktiex cie pui Ya-ma-lix khawm uik,
mai puing pex ka pui Ya-maw-lix ka pui pa ot ka veng Ha-si-suing Tha-ma.
8 Khaix an,
simiang Saw-to-ma,
simiang Eu-maw-la,
simiang Ya-ma,
simiang Si-piien,
mai pui naing simiang Pi-la (baox kaux Saw-eux) khrawm paox tix ka daux lung ting Si-ting,
9 son sang yuh naing mai simiang Yi-lang,
simiang Keu-ying,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
simiang Yi-la-sha khankix.
Simiang pon kaux tix plak mai simiang phuan kaux tix plak puing ka paox tix.
10 Daux ngawd Si-ting koe dung peeh khaox gix ka ku dauh ku dee.
Yam to simiang Saw-to-ma mai simiang Eu-maw-la mai cawd daux tiex dee an tix blah;
simiang pa deuk loe kaux to ka piang gawng.
11 Simiang pon kaux an teei can kraung pa mhawm mai priex preem khawm uik khaing veng Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la.
12 Yam an,
kawn pha Ya-paw-lang pa kaux Law-teux ot ka veng Saw-to-ma;
kix kawx giex nawh hu heue.
13 Koe pui pa pon to tix kaux hoek krai grawng in khawm uik ka Ya-paw-lang pui Si-paw-lai.
Yam an,
Ya-paw-lang ot mai pui Ya-maw-lix dee dex khaox yang yit pa singax singeh cie Mang-lix.
Mang-lix mai pux nawh Yi-seu-keux mai Ya-nai hoik mawh paox pie mai Ya-paw-lang.
14 Ya-paw-lang hoik mhong grawng pa giex kix kawn pha tix Law-teux,
nawh tom khrawm ka cub nyiex pui naing tix loe yieh kao daix kaux,
leue ka simiang pon kaux,
tom hoek ka haktiex Tang.
15 Nawh ot tan mai guah cub kru naing tix koe cub mawx cub an daux plak pon sawm,
mai puing pex ka pui koeng.
Nawh ca nied tix leue ka pui koeng,
tom hoek ka Ta-ma-see-keux plak caong ma laong Heu-pa,
16 mai deeh teei can pa tud kix hoek khawm uik,
mai taoh ka kawn pha tix Law-teux khawm kraung khrai nawh,
bun miex,
khawm can pui cie nawh pa giex kix hu heue.
Mai-ci-si-teux nawb buan ka Ya-paw-lang
17 Yam pex Ya-paw-lang ka simiang Ci-ta-lao-max mai cub simiang pa ot tau mai nawh khankix,
simiang veng Saw-to-ma lih rieb Ya-paw-lang ka daux ngawd Sa-we (pa mawh ngawd simiang) tan heue.
18 aPa mawh simiang Sa-leung pa kaux Mai-ci-si-teux cao giex kaing li Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
viex khao mun mai plai lih rieb ka Ya-paw-lang,
19 mai nawh nawb buan ka piang nawh ah nin:
“Kieh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm,
pa phan pa yuh tawx buan ka Ya-paw-lang!
20 Kieh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm an,
pa kieh maix puing pex ka pui koeng khankix pon krax pa yaog yaw!”
Ya-paw-lang tom teei kraung pa pon tix ka daux naing kao son tix son tawx ka Mai-ci-si-teux.
21 Simiang Saw-to-ma ah nin ka Ya-paw-lang:
“Kraung pa pon ka daux naing tawx ka maix uik,
viang mawh nan,
pui cie aux deeh tawx ka aux uik!”
22 Ya-paw-lang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm,
cao pa phan haktiex mai rao ma,
23 aux ang doh teei kraung cie maix tix ceu kawx,
pie keei tix nyaing mai maox kiiep tix gawng aux ang cie,
nu maix ah nin sax:
‘Aux kieh Ya-paw-lang mag mi heue.’
24 Khiox khaw maix deeh tawx ngie can pa hoik uik aux khaing,
pa haoh khaing an,
aux ang lai cie.
Viang mawh nan,
pa sang caw paox grawm aux Ya-nai,
Yi-seu-keux mai Mang-lix tix pon,
caw guah tix tawx ka kix heue.”
