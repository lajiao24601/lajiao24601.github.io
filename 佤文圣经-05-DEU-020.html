Been son krax naing
20
“Yam hu puing pex pui pa koeng,
yaox ka ling law naing kix,
brung naing kix,
mai ka pui naing kix pa haoh hun khaing pex,
paw lhat paw graung,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
pa hoik viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
ot tau mai pex.
2 Sivoe sang puing pex ka naing,
cao giex kaing li tag sang hu sivoe,
mai krai ka pui naing ah nin:
3 ‘Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex nghiet!
Daux in singaix pex hu yuh naing mai pui koeng khankix,
paw lhat graung,
paw si yaung,
mai paw yaoh yee.
4 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex tag hu tau mai pex;
nawh tag sang kieh pex puing pex heue.’
5 “Khaix an,
simiang naing tag sang ah nin ka pui pa ot greem tix:
‘Ka daux pex koe pui mawx pa sum nyiex,
mai ang nyang baox yuh ka krax tan lie?
Pui pa yuh nin chix tix deeh ing.
Phan ang mawh nan,
phan nawh yum ka daux naing,
pui tang kaux tag caw tix yuh krax tan lu nyiex ma tang nawh.
6 Ka daux pex koe pui mawx pa hoik sum si peem plix siiet,
mai ang nyang baox yaox tix pieh plix lie?
Pui pa yuh nin chix tix deeh ing.
Phan ang mawh nan,
phan nawh yum ka daux naing,
pui tang kaux tag ih ka plai plix siiet cie nawh.
7 Ka daux pex koe pui mawx pa chawk tix koe moeng,
mai ang nyang baox yuh ka paox lie?
Pui pa yuh nin chix tix deeh ing.
Phan ang mawh nan,
phan nawh yum,
pui tang kaux tag sang teei tix moeng ka moeng nawh pa ang bax yuh ka paox heue.’
8 “Simiang naing tag sang krai ka pui pa ot greem tix ah nin:
‘Ka daux pex phan koe pui pa lhat si yaung rhawm,
pa yaoh yee,
chix tix deeh ing.
Phan ang mawh nan,
nawh tag suk sag yuh rhawm rhi pui naing khawm uik.’
9 Khaix hoik krai simiang naing lox ka pui pa ot greem tix,
tag tawx kix tang koe ka simiang kru naing cub mawx cub an heue.
10 “Yam puing pex ka veng,
tag caw tix ran kieh pui pa ot ka daux veng koe bawg yam pa ap tix son ang lai pex.
11 Phan kix paoh siviex veng ap tix ang lai pex,
mai kix tag sang yuh siliex cawng pex,
mai yuh kaing jhaw pa kha tix son pex heue.
12 Phan kix ang ap tix ang lai pex,
mai yuh naing ka pex,
pex tom tag chix tix lai lawm ka veng an.
13 Khaix hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex pex pon teei ka veng an,
pex tag pauh ka can simex pa ot ka daux veng yum khawm uik.
14 Phan mawh bun,
kawn nyawm,
khawng brie,
mai kraung khrai tang ceu,
sawn tix mawh kraung khrai pa pon khaing daux naing,
pex tag chix teei tix cie,
mawh kheu an mawh pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pex.
15 Grawng been in tag siao ngie ka veng pa ot singai khaing dee ot pex khankix heue.
16 “Viang mawh nan,
yam cax pex tix cie ka veng haktiex pa hoik ap Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex tix tawx ka pex,
pex tag pauh ka can pui pa koe khawm uik.
17 Pex tag sang hawt awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex,
yuh laux ka pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Cia-nang,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we,
mai pui Yie-pu-see khankix laux leeing.
18 Pex yuh kix laux,
son ang kix sang nyaw glaw pex hawt yuh ka been pa caox pa caig riang rao pa krub kix,
kheu in phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex heue.
19 “Yam puing pex ka veng an kix,
viang pex hoik lai lawm ka tom rheung,
pex kawx ang pon tix kid khaox plix.
Pex chix tix ih ka plix khaox an,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix kid ka khaox an,
mawh kheu khaox an ang mawh pui pa koeng ka pex.
20 Pex chix tix kid ka khaox pa ang plix plix khankix mai siao kah,
yuh kraung pa sang puing ka veng,
tom si dauh hoik pon teei ka veng heue.”
