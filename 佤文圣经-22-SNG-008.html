8
Aux simie maix mawh pux aik tix,
mawh pui pa hoik peux ka rawm teeh miex aux;
yuh nin,
yam si pup aux maix ka daux laih,
ngheet ka maix,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa ang naog rhawm kah.
2 Aux sang viex maix laig daux nyiex miex aux,
kieh maix krai grawng sang muh aux ka maix yuh ka mawx.
Aux sang tawx maix nyaux ka plai pa yuh lih khaing sidah bhawng pa mawh:
plai pa yuh lih khaing plix mak cang heue.
 
3 Taix khawn goix maix giex ka kaing aux;
taix khawn dawm maix kawm ka aux heue.
 
4 Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix eue,
viex pex cu ka lox aux,
pex paw vi so yix haing,
paw vi tuk tiak ka rhawm muh dut yix heue.
Lox lai rhax ka daux liah ceu
[Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix]
5 Pui pa tuk ka taix pui pa muh tix an,
pa kaoh khaing daux tiag mawh pui mawx lie?
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
Ka greem khaox mak kawk tie,
aux so maix haing.
Ka dee an,
miex maix hoik kham gee krax kha rhawm son maix,
ka dee an,
miex maix hoik keh maix.
6 Kieh maix paoh rhawm rhi tix ka aux nong nong;
kieh plawng taix maix kawm ka aux tix kaux khaing.
Rhawm muh dut hoik kaux awm krax yum yuh nan;
rhawm braong hoik kaux cong awm meung yum yuh nan.
Nawh chix plih lih ka rao ngu,
awm ngu pa cap yuh nan.
7 Rawm ang chix yeet yuh rhawm muh dut,
rawm ting kawx ang pon kheem.
Phan koe pui pa keud tix sang teei mau khri loh ka rawm muh dut,
nawh tag kieh pui map nai nyiah tix heue.
[Prie bun pa sang koe mex]
8 Daux nyiex aux koe nan aux tix kaux,
teeh nawh khawm ra khawn ang bax mhawm naog.
Pui pa nhum ju hoek chawk tix moeng ka nawh,
ex sang yuh ka mawx lie?
9 Phan nawh mawh guh ding tix mu,
ex sang sum nyiex gawng mu pa yuh ka mau ot piang;
phan nawh mawh siviex tix plah,
ex sang teei phia khaox si oe bhawng,
mai sivie tix priex nawh mhawm mhawm heue.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
10 Aux mawh guh ding tix mu,
teeh aux khawm ra khawn mawh gawng mu pa sum tix ot ka piang.
Pui pa muh aux hoik tawng,
aux hoik naog krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe khaing nawh heue.
[Simie pa sang koe moeng]
11 Saw-law-meung koe si peem plix siiet tix dauh ka Pa-lix Ha-meung,
nawh tawx pui pa yuh kaung ma vai tix yuh si peem in;
kix ku kaux tag caw tix tawx mau ngawh si peem an tix kaux tix rheng plah mau luah.
12 Tix rheng plah mau luah mawh cie Saw-law-meung,
ra yieh mau luah mawh cie pui pa yuh kaung ma an;
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tang koe si peem plix siiet cie to kaux tix heue.
 
13 Paox muh aux,
kieh aux mhong lox maix khaing daux si peem;
paox grawm aux hoik krox jun tix nghiet ka lox maix heue.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
14 Paox muh aux,
nied hoek heue,
awm kiah tix mu,
mai kawn cak tix mu,
lai ling ka piang gawng pa koe rib pa si oe bhawng heue.
