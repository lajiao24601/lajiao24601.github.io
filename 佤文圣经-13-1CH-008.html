Doeh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming
8
Piien-ya-ming koe kawn simex phuan kaux;
kix jauh ka pa ting hoek ka pa iak mawh:
Pi-la,
Ya-seu-piie,
Ya-ha-la,
2 Naw-ha,
La-fax heue.
3 Doeh ceu pui Pi-la mawh Ya-tax,
Ci-la,
Ya-pi-fu,
4 Ya-pi-su,
Nai-mang,
Ya-heu-yax,
5 Ci-la,
See-fu-shuing,
Hu-lang heue.
6-7 Doeh ceu pui Yi-fu mawh Nai-mang,
Ya-si-yax,
Ci-la.
Kix mawh cao kaing cub pui nyiex an kix ka haktiex Cia-pa,
viang mawh nan,
khin kix gah tix hu ka Ma-na-sia.
Keeing Wu-sha mai Ya-si-fu pa kaux Ci-la,
thung kix gah tix hu heue.
8-9 Moeng Sa-ha-liien khawm ra kaux,
pa mawh Hu-seung mai Pa-la,
nawh hoik tik vud kah;
khaix an yam ot nawh haktiex Maw-ya,
nawh moeng ka Heu-teux.
Kiex koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
pa mawh Yaw-pa,
Si-pi-ya,
Mi-sa,
Ma-la-kang,
10 Yie-wu-see,
Sa-cia,
Mi-max.
Pui in kix hoik mawh cao kaing cub pui nyiex kix heue.
11 Hu-seung keh nawh cie kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Ya-pi-thu mai Yi-li-pa-lix heue.
12 Yi-li-pa-lix koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Si-paw,
Mi-sang,
Sa-mai.
Sa-mai sum veng A-naw mai veng Law-teux ra mu veng in,
khawm nyiex yaong pa dex tan heue.
Pui Piien-ya-ming pa ot Cia-theux mai Ya-ya
13 Pi-li-yax mai See-ma mawh cao kaing ceu pui pa ot daux veng Ya-ya khankix;
kix hoik grheu ka pui haktiex Cia-theux hu.
14 Doeh ceu pui Pi-li-yax mawh:
Ya-si-yaw,
Sa-sa,
Yie-li-maw,
15 Si-pa-ti-ya,
Ya-la-teu,
Ya-teu,
16 Mi-cia-leux,
Yi-see-pa,
Yaw-ha heue.
Pui Piien-ya-ming pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung
17 Doeh ceu pui Yi-li-pa-lix mawh Si-pa-ti-ya,
Mi-su-lang,
Si-si-ci,
Si-paw,
18 Yi-see-mi-laix,
Yi-see-li-yax,
Yaw-pa heue.
19 Doeh ceu pui See-me mawh Ya-cing,
Si-ci-lix,
Sa-ti,
20 Yi-li-nai,
Si-leu-thaix,
Yi-liex,
21 Ya-ta-ya,
Pi-la-ya,
Sheung-la heue.
22 Doeh ceu pui Sa-sa mawh Yi-see-pang,
Si-paw,
Yi-liex,
23 Ya-paw-toeng,
Si-ci-lix,
Ha-nang,
24 Ha-na-ni-ya,
Yi-lang,
An-thaw-thi-ya,
25 Yi-fu-ti-ya,
Pi-nu-yi-leux heue.
26 Doeh ceu cad Yie-law-hang mawh Sang-see-lai,
See-ha-lix,
Ya-tha-li-ya,
27 Ya-li-si,
Yi-li-yax,
Si-ci-lix heue.
28 Plak piang hoik mawh cao kaing cub ceu pui nyiex kix khawm uik;
doeh ceu cad kix kaing ting ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Pui Piien-ya-ming pa ot ka Ci-piien mai Yie-lu-sa-leung
29 Yie-lixa pang ka veng Ci-piien,
mai paung ot tan.
Ceu kaux moeng nawh mawh Ma-cia;
30 kawn ai nawh kaux Ya-paw-toeng.
Kawn simex tang kaux nawh mawh Su-eux,
Ci-see,
Pa-lix,
Ni-eub,
Na-ta,
31 Ci-to,
Ya-si-yaw,
Sa-cia,
32 Mi-ci-lawx.
Mi-ci-lawx mawh keeing See-mi-ang.
Doeh ceu pui kix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ang singai khaing ceu pui cub nyiex kix tang nyiex miex glang tix heue.
Ceu pui cub nyiex simiang Sao-law
33 Ni-eu keh Ci-see;
Ci-see keh simiang Sao-law.
Sao-law koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Yaw-na-tang,
Me-ji Su-yax,
Ya-pi-na-ta,
Yi-see Pa-lixc.
34 Yaw-na-tang keh Mi-li Pa-lix;
Mi-li Pa-lixd keh Mi-cia heue.
35 Mi-cia koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Pi-toeng,
Mi-leux,
Tha-li-yax,
Ya-ha-see.
36 Ya-ha-see keh Yie-heu-a-ta;
Yie-heu-a-ta koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Ya-la-miiex,
Ya-see-ma-we,
Sing-lix.
Sing-lix keh Maw-sha;
37 Maw-sha keh Pi-ni-yax;
Pi-ni-yax keh La-fax;
La-fax keh Yi-li-ya-sa;
Yi-li-ya-sa keh Ya-si heue.
38 Ya-si koe kawn simex liah kaux,
pa mawh Ya-see-li-kang,
Paw-ci-lux,
Yi-seu-ma-lix,
See-ya-li-ya,
Eu-pa-ti-ya,
Ha-nang.
39 Pux Ya-si pa kaux Yi-seux koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Wu-lang,
Yie-wu-see,
Yi-li-fa-liex heue.
40 Kawn simex Wu-lang khankix,
hoik mawh pui pa kied kua ka naing mai pui pa kied chix puing ak.
Kawn simex nawh mai kawn saux nawh,
khawm uik koe tix yieh tix phuan kaux.
Plak piang pa hoik tiiem tix lih,
mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming khawm uik heue.
