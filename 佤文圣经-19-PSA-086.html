Lox rawg buan pa khiox khaw krax teem
86
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg aux,
mai pawk ka lox rawg aux,
mawh kheu iak riang aux mai ang koe cao teem.
2 Taoh aux luat luan khaing krax yum,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm aux ka maix;
taoh ka aux,
kheu mawh aux siliex maix,
mai aux yum rhawm ka maix.
3 Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux;
aux yiiem tix khiox khaw ka maix leeing lang singaix.
4 Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh siliex maix gaux rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik khiox khaw aux ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
5 Simiang eue,
maix mhawm rhawm mai baox koe krax pa va rhawm,
maix hoik tawx krax muh tix pa grum naog ka piang pui pa khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka maix heue.
 
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg aux;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa kok yo aux maix teem coi tix.
7 Ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
aux khiox khaw kok yo ka maix,
mawh kheu maix hoik nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux heue.
 
8 Simiang eue,
ang lai koe siyiex tang kaux pa lheu ting khaing maix;
ang lai koe siyiex tang kaux pa pon yuh ka kaing pa hoik yuh maix.
9 aMeung ka piang haktiex khawm uik pa hoik phan yuh maix,
tag sang grux ngawng ka maix;
kix tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka pa ting pa lhaong maix.
10 Maix hoik ting riang ting rao,
maix hoik pon yuh ka kaing pa daox pa deng;
mawh ngie maix pa mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing.
11 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix jhi ka krax pa sang khaw aux tix lawng,
aux tag sang hawt ka pa mawh pa meh maix;
khiox khaw maix sidiex gau ka aux,
son sang chix lhat graung yung yi aux ka maix yuh ka mawx.
12 Cao pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix;
aux sang krai pao pa ting pa lhaong maix hu leeing ju leeing phan.
13 Krax muh maix ka aux kied sidaing ting lhaong;
maix hoik taoh aux luat luan khaing daux si meeig.
14 Siyiex eue,
pui cau nyhau hoek tix puing ka aux;
pui caox pui caig sang pauh ka aux,
kix ang deuh maix koe ceu.
15 Simiang eue,
maix mawh Siyiex pa koe krax pa caox rhawm mai krax muh krax rhawm.
Maix ang chix song rhawm yie yie,
maix hoik grum naog mai krax muh pa kaux sijax.
16 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka aux,
caox rhawm ka aux;
khiox khaw maix tawx riang rao ka aux,
mai taoh ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik awm aux miex tix,
mai yuh ka kaing jhaw maix yuh nan.
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix jhi mhai sakse pa mhawm pa mhiem maix ka aux,
kieh pui pa koeng khoi ka aux khankix kaig tua,
mawh kheu hoik teem coi maix ka aux,
hoik tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka aux heue.
