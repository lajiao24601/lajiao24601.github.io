Pux o ra kaux pa yuh phi map
23
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
sivoe an koe pux o ra kaux,
2 paung ot ka Ai-cix;
3 Yam nhum ju kiex,
kiex yuh sibun coh kaux,
kieh pui kloek teeh tix,
mai ang lai kieh to kaux singax.
4 Ceu kaux bun pux ke mawh A-heu-la,
mawh pa tang son Sa-ma-li-yax;
ceu kaux bun pux nawh mawh A-heu-li-pa,
mawh pa tang son Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Aux hoik moeng ka kiex pux o ra kaux;
kiex hoik keh kawn son aux.
5 “Khaix hoik mex A-heu-la ka aux,
mai nawh ca kawn yuh sibun coh kaux.
Nawh yuh paox muh tau mai simex Ya-sux.
6 Kix hoik ceep ka kraung naing pa lung pliex,
kraung ngawh pa ceep pui ting ceu,
mai pa ceep simiang naing;
kix hoik mawh pui nhum ju pa mhawm grang,
mawh simiang naing pa brug brung.
7 Nawh hoik yuh paox muh tau mai can simiang naing Ya-sux khawm uik,
yuh moeng iak kix,
krub riang rao pa krub kix mai kieh to kaux tix seeing brai.
8 Nawh ca kawn yuh kaing pa phit vao khaix ang to kaux tix lai singax ka daux Ai-cix.
Yam kawn mawh nawh kawn nyawm,
simex hoik it tau mai nawh,
mai kieh nawh mawh sibun coh kaux.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ap tix tawx nawh ka can simex Ya-sux pa hoik yuh nawh paox muh tau mai khawm uik.
10 Kix peeik kraung nawh ghle,
mai giex ka kawn simex kawn bun nawh,
khaix an teei vaig klauh nawh yum kah.
Krax laux leeing pa si pup nawh lih mawh pa nyiah bun miex khankix heue.
11 “Pux nawh pa mawh A-heu-li-pa yaox grawng pa laux leeing in,
mai kawn lheu khaing pa laux leeing pa phit vao pux ke nawh pa mawh A-heu-la.
12 Nawh kawx hoik yuh paox muh tau mai pui Ya-sux pa koe min mai simiang naing khankix,
pa mawh pui naing pa ceep ka kraung naing pa mhawm grang mai simiang naing pa brug brung;
kix hoik mawh pui nhum ju pa mhawm grang khawm uik,
13 Aux hoik yaox krax phit vao pui mai nawh,
nawh hoik phit vao awm pux ke tix yuh nan heue.
14-15 “Krax pa phit vao nawh hoik nie ang lai yawm,
pie riang rao pa mawh riang simiang ting Pa-pi-loeng pa saik pui ot ka piang ding khankix mai hoik muh nang.
Riang an kix awm pa hoik teei rawm siang raoh yuh ka yuh nan,
ka nyoi hoik phrawk maox rawd nyoi ka khawm uik,
ka piang kaing hoik ceep lawk kaing pa preue tix piang.
16 Yam yaox nawh ka riang an kix nawh tom muh kah,
mai ploi pui hu ka kix ka Pa-pi-loeng.
17 Pui Pa-pi-loeng hoek tix it tau mai nawh,
phit vao mai nawh,
map nai ka nawh,
dee si dauh nawh tag koeng ka kix heue.
18 Nawh ot ka kaong ka plak sivoe pui hun,
mai kieh pui ku kaux tawng grawng mawh tix bun coh kaux.
Aux koeng ka nawh,
awm koeng aux ka pux ke nawh yuh nan.
19 Nawh ca ang chix piawm ka kaing phit vao pa yuh nawh ka Ai-cix,
mai ca kawn coh ka to kaux tix ang jud.
20 Nawh hoik yuh paox muh tau mai simex pa mhawm kluing nex nham awm la tiag,
pa mhawm naog riang rao awm brung tiag yuh nan.”
21 A-heu-li-pa eue,
maix hoik simie krax ot krax koe tix mawh pa coh to kaux tix,
pa kieh pui kloek teeh tix,
pa ang lai kieh to kaux tix singax yam nhum ju tix ka daux haktiex Ai-cix heue.
Siyiex prawng ca ka pux
22 “Mawh kheu nan,
A-heu-li-pa eue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka maix yuh nin:
maix hoik ang lai rhawm yaox ka simex an kix,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang cheeik kix song rhawm,
mai viex kix hoek tix lai lawm ka maix.
23 Aux tag sang thung can pui Pa-pi-loeng,
mai pui Cia-leu-tix,
pui Pi-keu,
pui Su-yax,
pui Keu-yax,
mai can pui Ya-sux hoek khawm uik.
Aux tag sang kok can pui nhum ju pa koe min,
cao kaing,
simiang pa ting ceu khankix,
mai simiang naing pa ting pa brug brung hoek khawm uik.
24 Kix tag sang thung pui naing pa hun,
hoik rien ling law mhawm mai ling law pa tang kraung naing,
hoek puing maix khaing haktiex plak laong.
Kix tag sang teei dieb tie naok,
mai mhok rhiem tie to kaux tix puing ka maix.
Aux tag sang kieh kix ciyang ka maix,
hawt lox tat jhawm prawng kix prawng ca maix.
25 “Mawh kheu song rhawm aux ka maix,
aux tag sang kieh kix klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang maix.
Kix sang pat ka meeh maix mai yhaok maix,
mai pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun maix.
Kix tag sang giex ka kawn simex kawn bun maix,
mai gleeing kix yum im.
26 Kix tag sang peeik ka kraung maix,
mai tud ka siang pa koe maix.
27 Aux tag sang tieb tie ka krax pa si lao maix,
kieh krax phit vao maix ka daux Ai-cix ang lai koe,
kieh maix ang lai baox simie ka riang rao,
mai ang lai baox braong doe ka Ai-cix heue.”
28 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang ap tix tawx maix ka pui pa hoik koeng maix kah,
pa ang maix rhawm yaox.
29 Mawh kheu koeng kix ka maix,
kix sang tud hu ka sin ngao pa pon maix khaing daux pa taug mao tix,
mai peeik ka kraung maix ghle,
kieh maix ot ka kaong awm sibun coh kaux yuh nan.
In hoik mawh krax pa si lao maix,
mai krax pa phit vao maix pa kieh hoek.
30 Maix hoik yuh bun coh kaux ka piang meung haktiex,
hoik krub ka riang rao pa krub pui,
kieh to kaux tix seeing brai.
31 Maix leue tix si kaw khaix caong pux ke tix,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang teei tix tawx kawk plai pa ciyang pa nyaux nawh sivoe mai tawx maix nyaux ka heue.”
32 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
maix tag sang nyaux ka kawk plai pa nyaux pux ke maix;
kawk plai in kied raux kied ting.
Kawn pui tag sang nyiah si briem maix;
kawk plai in hoik naog krax ciyang heue.
33 Kawk plai pux ke maix Sa-ma-li-yax,
mawh kawk plai pa kied lhat graung kied laux rhawm pui kah,
mai kieh maix yoek tix goh,
mai laux leeing heue.
34 Maix tag sang nyaux uik ka kawk in,
pie pa seeing pa brai kawx hoik bleet kah;
Bliah max kawk an tag sang pat mat ka si gleem teeh maix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
35 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Mawh kheu hoik piawm maix ka aux,
hoik tik vud ka aux,
maix tag sang kha rhawm tix kheu krax pa si lao tix mai krax pa phit vao tix heue.”
Siyiex prawng ca ka kiex pux o ra kaux
36 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix hoik rien khre tix sang ciyang ka A-heu-la mai A-heu-li-pa lie?
Maix tag sang ciyang ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh kiex.
37 Kiex hoik yuh ka phi map pa phit vao mai phi map pa pauh pui,
pa mawh pa krub ka riang rao,
mai hoik pauh ka kawn simex pa hoik keh kiex son aux.
Kiex hoik teei kawn simex aux kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie tan ka riang rao pa krub kiex.
38 Ja lao ang yuh nan,
kiex kawn map nai ka nyiex buan aux,
lieh lheu ka singaix tuah pa tat aux.
39 Kiex pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun aux,
singaix tan kiex kawn praix mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka riang rao,
mai hoek tix kieh nyiex buan aux seeing brai heue!
40 “Kiex tix bawg hoek tix bawg ploi kawn cau tix hu kok pui pa ot dee singai hoek,
mai ghaok kix hoek.
Kiex pux o ra kaux tom heem tix,
jawg tix,
meug ka hauk ngai tix,
ceep ka kraung pa ngawh.
41 Kiex ngawm ka piang khaox ngawm pa mhawm,
mai deuh pheen ot ka sivoe ngai,
ka piang pheen sau sidah bhawng mai bee sime pa tawx aux naog piang.
42 Koe cub pui pa ang koe kaing yuh pa meed vu vie tix khankix kaoh khaing daux tiag,
mai yuh krax gaux rhawm,
kix teei ble ceep ka piang taix kiex pux o ra kaux,
mai ceep mhok khri ka kaing kiex.
43 Ka daux rhawm rhi aux keud:
kix hoik yuh krax gaux rhawm tau mai sibun coh kaux pa kroh krawx.
44 Kix ca baox hoek tix sawg ka sibun coh kaux in kix,
mai hoek tix sawg ka A-heu-la mai A-heu-li-pa,
bun pa phit vao in kiex ang jud.
45 Pui pa khaw caw tag sang prawng ka phi map pa phit vao kiex,
mai ka phi map pa pauh pui;
mawh kheu yuh kiex krax pa phit vao,
mai ka daux tawm taix kiex hoik naog ka nham pa pauh pui heue.”
46 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Tag caw tix khrawm pui tud pui rhim hoek ghlat ka kiex,
tud rhim ka kiex.
47 Kieh pui tud pui rhim teei simaox tawk kiex kah,
teei vaig klauh kiex kah,
khaix an pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun kiex yum,
ploi ngu gleeing nyiex ma kiex.
48 Aux tag sang tieb tie ka krax pa phit vao pui ka daux meung haktiex khawm uik,
son sang ne raw ka can bun miex khawm uik,
ang pon tix gau tix hawt ka krax pa phit vao kiex.
49 Pax pux o ra kaux eue,
aux tag sang ciyang ka pa phit vao mai ka phi map pa krub pax riang rao.
Yuh nin,
pax tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhoang heue.”
