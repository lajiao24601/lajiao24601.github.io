Daux krax pa singax singeh
35
Pak mhaik tag caw tix gaux rhawm;
Daux tiag daux tu hoik pruh ka tai.
2 Pak mhaik hoik pruh ka tai raoh rawt ku ceu,
rhax ka lox rhax pa gaux rhawm.
Nawh tag sang nyawm tix jhak awm Li-pa-noeng yuh nan,
mhawm awm Cia-mix mai Sa-loeng yuh nan.
Pui ku kaux tag sang yaox ka min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex,
ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex heue.
 
3 aPex tag caw tix kieh plawng taix pa taug mao tix koe riang koe rao,
kieh ba pa si yaung tix pon cung see.
4 Tag caw tix krai ka pui pa laux rhawm laux rhi ah nin:
Kaux cong heue,
paw vi lhat!
Siyiex tag sang taoh ka pex;
nawh tag sang pawk bix ka pui pa koeng ka pex heue.
 
5 bYam an,
pui dug ngai tag sang chix yaox,
pui lheet yhaok tag sang chix mhong,
6 pui chawh caong tag sang jang loh tix awm cak,
pui ang chix nye chix rhax lai.
Daux tiag daux tu,
rawm tag sang buing tix lih khaing;
rawm keet tag sang jauh tix lih khaing pak mhaik.
7 Pak mhaik pa si aoh tag sang tieh tix lih mawh dung rawm;
haktiex pa kroh pa riem,
rawm tag sang keet lih khaing.
Dee laux sox tiag tix
tag sang lih kuah ka rib klai mai gawng priem heue.
 
8 Krax ting tag sang lih koe tix nyaing,
tag sang kok mawh “krax pa singax singeh.”
Pui phi map ang pon lawng ka krax in;
pui pa daox aw ang pon lai ling hu daux krax in;
9 haktiex an ang koe sivai sang si,
to sat kawx ang koe heue.
Pui pa taoh Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang deeh ing ka daux krax ting in.
10 Pui pa hoik pon krax pa taoh Simiang Siyiex khankix,
tag sang deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
gaux rhawm gaux rhi rhax ka lox rhax.
Kix tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
ang lai baox deeh koe krax laux rhawm laux rhi heue.
