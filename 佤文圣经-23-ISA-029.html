Krax si pup Yie-lu-sa-leung
29
Pie khawm khu dee tan Siyiex,
pa mawh Yie-lu-sa-leung kawx hoik sang laux leeing heue!
Veng pa hoik ot Ta-wue kaux cong daux tag sang laux leeing heue!
Viang hoik luan tix num hoik tix num,
bawg yam singaix yuh poi hoik baox deeh jauh tix hoek hawt paox tix,
2 Siyiex ca kawn sang kok in mawh krax laux leeing pa ploi veng “khu dee tan Siyiex” lih hoek.
Tag sang koe lox yiiem riag yiiem yo;
ka daux veng khawm uik tag sang tieh tix lih mawh khu dee tan pa ting tix mu pa hoik klag nham im tix kah.
3 Siyiex tag sang giex ka veng in,
lai lawm ka veng in,
puing ka veng in.
4 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ang pon nye,
mai ngawm ka piang tiex,
mip ghok tix si kah,
awm si ngie krai lox khaing greem tiex yuh nan heue.
5 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
can pui koeng pa puing ka maix tag sang awm phawng tiex pa hoik phlu bhaung yuh nan;
pui naing pa hoik lhat graung pui ka khankix,
tag sang awm kam pa hoik phlu bhaung hu yuh nan.
Dum mawx dum an,
kaing ceu pa ang pui bax tien tawng,
6 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing tag sang teei pa ting tuh sah mai pa tuk kla reem,
tag sang ploi bhaung ting,
pu pak rhawm ting,
mai ngu hoek tix taoh maix.
7 Khaix an,
pui naing ku meung haktiex pa hoek tix puing ka khu dee tan Siyiex,
tag sang laux leeing khawm uik;
kraung naing kix mai krax pa rien khre pui naing tix,
hoik awm ca maox yuh nan grai hu awm ca maox pui yam pon sawm yuh nan.
8 Pui meung haktiex ku meung pa hoek tix puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix,
tag sang awm pui pa dex sang yum ca,
pa sai rhang tix ka daux ca maox kheu cai rhawm yuh nan,
yam hoik haing nawh ca kawn cai rhawm.
Kix hoik awm pui pa dex sang yum ca,
pa yaox tix nyaux rawm ka daux ca maox kheu simie tix nyaux rawm yuh nan,
yam hoik haing kix ca kawn simie tix nyaux ka rawm heue.
Ang nghiet ka lox pa ne raw
9 Pex ca nied tix yuh kaing pa daox aw heue!
Ca nied tix dug ngai heue!
Sivoe sang nyaux pex ka plai pex hoik yoek goh,
plai kawx ang bax pied hoik yung yang heue!
10 aSimiang Siyiex hoik kieh pex laig it;
nawh hoik nyeep ka ngai pex.
Cao jhai khaw tix mawh ngai kawn pui,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik tawb ka ngai kix heue.
11 Can dee bleeh riang su sai hoik mox tix khaing pex khawm uik,
awm phuk lai pa hoik nyeep tix yuh nan.
Pex viex phuk lai in hu tawx pui pa chix lai phat,
mai nawh tag sang krai ka pex,
nawh ang chix phat,
mawh kheu hoik nyeep phuk lai in tix heue.
12 Pex mhaing pui pa ang tawng ngai lai phat,
mai nawh tag sang krai ka maix,
nawh ang chix phat ka heue.
13 bSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui in kix ce tix krub ka aux lie!
Kix teei siyieb jheeh tix nab yam yung yi ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi kix singai khaing aux.
Been li kix mawh ngie been kawn pui haktiex khaing,
mawh chix phat suk chix phat glip ngai khaing heue.
14 cMawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang baox tawx krax laux leeing hoek yuh ka kix khawm ang kix rien tix,
kieh kix yaoh yee.
Ka daux kix,
krax lhak lhiao pui pa lhak lhiao tag ang lai koe ngawh jun pie ceeix kawx;
krax pa doh riang pui pa doh riang tag ang lai koe ceu pie ceeix kawx heue.”
Krax keud pot jun pot pa sang hoek
15 Pui pa mox krax keud krax sawn tix ka Simiang Siyiex khankix tag sang yaox krax laux krax leeing heue!
Kix hoik keud sawn ka daux pa faik,
mai ce pui ang yaox,
ce pui ang tawng ka ceu pa yuh kix.
16 dKix hoik plu pliak ka ceu pa mhawm mai ka ceu pa caox.
Mawh cang mai pa yuh aw pa ting ceu,
phan ang mawh nan mawh aw pa ting ceu lie?
Kraung ceu pa yuh pui khex sang chix ah nin ka pui cao yuh “maix ang bax yuh ka aux”,
phan ang mawh nan,
ah nin “maix ang tawng ka ceu pa yuh tix?”
17 Awm lox been ah nin:
“Khaix hoik luan tix bawg yam,
haktiex pa riem tag tieh tix lih mawh kaung pa mhawm;
kaung pa mhawm tag tieh tix lih mawh haktiex pa riem heue.”
18 Ka daux singaix an,
pui lheet yhaok tag sang chix mhong ka lox pa phat pui lai;
pui dug ngai pa ot ka daux pa faik tag sang blah ngai tix jhak.
19 Pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi tag sang rieb ka gaux rhawm pa tawx Simiang Siyiex;
pui hot pui sibhawm tag sang yaog yaw ka Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh.
20 Pui pa gut kheem pui khankix tag sang teei tix tik;
pui pa phit lud ka Siyiex tag sang kham krax ciyang;
pui phi map tag sang laux leeing khawm uik.
21 Pui pa si briem ka pui tang kaux,
mai pa tieb tie ka krax pa rauh pa see,
pa nieb pon ah ka pui pa mawh pa meh,
Siyiex tag sang yuh kix laux leeing khawm uik heue.
22 Yam in,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa hoik sax taoh ka Ya-paw-la-hang an hoik krai yuh nin:
“Pui cie aux eue,
pex ang lai baox yaox tix kham krax kaig krax tua,
mai pak ngai pex ang lai baox yaox krax kha rhawm tom paing hauk kaing tix heue.
23 Yam yaox pex grawng pa hoik tawx aux kawn praix ka kix,
pex tag sang krai doh grawng pa mawh aux Siyiex pa singax singeh.
Pex tag sang yung yi ka aux,
lhat graung ka aux.
24 Pa daox pa aw tag sang chix gau tix lhak lhiao;
pui pa brax salao cu tix rieb krax pa gau heue.”
