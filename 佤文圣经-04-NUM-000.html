Lai Simin-pui
Krai dawt
Ceu kaux Lai Simin-pui mawh pa pon khaing pa simin kawn praix Siyiex, tiiem grawng lih cub pui Yi-seu-liex khaing gawng Si-nai, mai hoek ka plak lih singaix haktiex pa hoik ap Siyiex tix tawx ka kix, khawm uik koe tix tipon num. Ceu kaux phuk lai in mawh pa koe grawh grawng khaing ka daux baux been in, pa mawh pa teei ca rang kawn pui. Sivoe sang lih ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing gawng Si-nai, Maw-si yuh kaing teei ca rang kawn pui ka daux tix bawg; khaix an hoik luan tipon num, ka daog plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang haktiex Maw-ya baox yuh nang kaing pa teei ca rang kawn pui tix bawg. Ka daux si nax pa teei ca rang in ra bawg, pui Yi-seu-liex hu ka haktiex plak seh Cia-nang hoek ka Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax, viang mawh nan, kix ang chix laig ka haktiex Cia-nang dee an. Kix ot tan tom nie num, kix hoek ka daog plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang; koe pui tix blah ot ka dee an, mai pui pa deuk khankix hoik rien tix sang tiang ka rawm klawng mai hu laig ka haktiex Cia-nang heue.
Phuk lai in hoik tiiem grawng yam si pup pui Yi-seu-liex ka kaing ceu pa nu khankix, kix hoik yie laux rhawm, lhat graung jheeng jheeng, mai tieh vheen ka Siyiex, salao ka simiang kix Maw-si pa hoik kieh Siyiex mawh cao pa thung ka kix. Phuk lai in mawh phuk lai pa krai Siyiex mawh pa kaux sijax. Viang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex yie lhat graung, ang rawm cu tix hu sivoe jheeng jheeng, mai salao ka Siyiex, viang mawh nan, Siyiex ca kawn kia ngien ka kix tom si dauh. Phuk lai in baox krai nang ka grawng to kaux Maw-si; bawg tix blah kawx, nawh ang lai pon viex ka krax gun krax yeung, viang mawh nan, nawh ca kawn kaux cong, mai muh ka Siyiex, muh ka kawn pui khawm uik heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pui Yi-seu-liex sang rien tix lih hu khaing gawng Si-nai (1.1–9.23)
(1) Pa teei ka ca rang kawn pui bawg tix (1.1–4.49)
(2) Grawng lox tat jhawm prawng mai been jhawm prawng ku ceu (5.1–8.26)
(3) Poi Luat-luan ka daux ra bawg tix bawg (9.1-23)
2. Jauh ka gawng Si-nai hoek ka Maw-ya (10.1–21.35)
3. Grawng kaing pa yuh ka daux Maw-ya (22.1–32.42)
4. Grawng kaing ting pa yuh ka daux krax Ai-cix hoek ka Maw-ya (33.1–49)
5. Lox pa tawx sivoe sang tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang (33.50–36.13)
