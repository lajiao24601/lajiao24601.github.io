Krax laux leeing ka sido mawh krax prawng ca pa ne raw Simiang Siyiex
2
Phlu teet tawng ka Shi-ang heue!
Tauh glawk mawng ka gawng cie Siyiex pa singax singeh!
Pui haktiex Yo-tax eue,
tag caw tix si yaung si kua!
Singaix bawg yam cie Simiang Siyiex dex sang hoek.
2 An hoik mawh singaix pa faik veei krax yum.
Mawh singaix pa naog bhawm pai awm taox kah.
Phung sido pa haoh hun hu sivoe ngai,
hoik awm pa faik tawb kheem ka gawng yuh nan.
Sivoe an ang sax koe ceu pa yuh nin,
singaix khaix kawx tag ang koe heue.
 
3 Sido hoik uik ih can khaox ku ceu awm gleeing ngu yuh nan.
Sivoe an haktiex hoik awm si peem Yi-tiien yuh nan,
khaix an hoik tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag pa riem;
Sido an kix ang lai deuh ka ceu pa tid tix.
4 aPhan jhak tix yaox kix hoik awm brung yuh nan,
hoik brax to awm brung naing yuh nan.
5 Lox graoh kix ka piang choek gawng
hoik awm si num lox hu ling law naing yuh nan,
hoik awm lox cap khix kroh yuh nan.
Kix hoik dia tix mhawm,
awm pui naing pa rien tix sang puing ka naing yuh nan heue.
6 Yam hoek kix,
pui hoik lhat si yaung khawm uik,
mai pak ngai pui hoik laux.
7 Kix hoik awm pui naing pa puing naing,
awm pui naing pa haok ding.
Kix sau riang tix hu ka plak sivoe ngai,
mai ang loh ka plak hu tix,
8 mai ang si ghox ka paox tix.
Kix preeng tix laig khawm phung ka dee tieb tie pui,
pui mawx ang lai pon tieb tie kah.
9 Kix puing laig ka daux veng,
mai haok ka guh ding veng,
haok ka piang choek nyiex,
awm yieh pui briex tix laig ka daux doh siviex nyiex yuh nan heue.
 
10 bYam hoek kix,
haktiex hoik yung yang,
rao ma hoik si yaung,
singaix,
khix hoik faik khawm uik,
simuing kawx ang baox tawx pa riang.
11 cSimiang Siyiex tawx lox ka pui naing cie tix.
Pui naing cie nawh ting riang,
pui naing pa khrawm nawh kied hun.
Singaix pa prawng ca Simiang Siyiex kied mawh pa lhat pa graung tete heue!
Pui mawx sang pon klawm lie?
Pa kok yo loh ka rhawm rhi tix
12 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
viang hoik mawh yam in kawx,
pex ca kawn chix tix tuah priex preem,
yiiem grai,
yiiem doe,
loh ka rhawm rhi tix tete,
mai deeh hoek ka aux heue.
13 Riah ka kraung ceep tix khaing ang koe ceu;
tag caw tix loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
mai doh keud ka phi map tix tete heue.
 
Deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex heue!
Nawh hoik koe buan son,
mai naog krax pa caox rhawm;
Nawh ang song rhawm yie yie,
kheu hoik grum naog nawh mai krax muh krax rhawm;
Nawh cu tix pluat ploi ka phi map ku bawg ku yam.
14 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex tee chix loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
mai tawx buan son ka pex tom nie;
Yam an pex tom taug pon yaox tix tan nghox koi mai plai nyawm ka nawh heue.
 
15 Phlu teet tawng ka gawng Shi-ang heue!
Krai pao tuah ka priex preem,
yuh ka poi.
16 Tag caw tix khrawm ka pui hun,
rien khre tix laig yuh poi pa singax heue.
Tag caw tix teem coi ka kuad mai kawn nyawm,
mai klien ka kawn ngie,
pui mex moeng pa khraox tag caw tix lih hu khaing nyiex khraox tix.
17 Cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krub Simiang Siyiex eue,
tag caw tix rawg buan yiiem grai ka si nax khu dee tan mai diang siviex nyiex buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka pui cie maix.
Paw vi kieh meung ka piang haktiex map nai yix;
paw vi kieh kix nyiah yix ah nin:
Siyiex pex ot dee mawx lie?
Siyiex tawx buan son
18 Simiang Siyiex baox kia ngien ka haktiex cie tix,
mai caox rhawm ka pui cie tix.
19 Nawh pawk lox kix ah nin:
Aux tag sang tawx nghox koi,
plai nyawm,
bee sime ka pex;
pex tag sang yaox tix naog rhawm;
meung tang meung ang lai baox yuh krax pa kaig tua ka pex.
20 Aux tag sang kieh sido pa hoek khaing haktiex plak laong singai hu khaing pex;
aux tag sang grheu kix hu ka haktiex pa riem,
pa hu sivoe grheu kix laig ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog;
pa hu khaix grheu kix laig ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
Taox cux to kaux yum kix haok ka rao ma.
Mawh kheu krax laux leeing pa kieh kix hoek piang pex,
aux tag sang yuh kix laux leeing heue.
 
21 Haktiex eue,
paw vi lhat,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue!
Mawh kheu hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex kaing ting son pex.
22 To sat eue,
paw vi lhat heue!
Rib riem jiet mhawm klai,
khaox plix hoik mhawm plix plix,
Khaox ruh,
khaox plix siiet baox deeh plix plix pa mhawm heue.
 
23 Pui Shi-ang eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue!
Gaux rhawm son kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Nawh hoik tawx rawm lhiex ka pex awm pa khaw mai diag tong;
d
nawh tawx rawm lhiex cao mai rawm lhiex lha awm sivoe yuh nan.
24 Ka daux glauh pauh nghox tag pum nghox koi naog kah;
ka daux dawng krid tag sang naog lih ka plai mai ka bee sime.
25 Ka daux num an kix,
aux ploi phung sido lih tix cub cub;
to ma pa ih,
aux tag sang plud ka pex.
26 Pex tag sang koe priex preem tom nie,
mai yaox tix sak;
pex tag sang yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Nawh tag sang yuh kaing pa daox deng son pex;
pui cie aux ang lai baox hoek krax pa map nai leeing ju.
27 Yi-seu-liex eue,
yam an pex tom taug tawng
aux ot ka daux pex;
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex;
lheu khaing aux ang lai koe tang kaux.
Pui cie aux ang lai baox hoek krax kaig krax tua leeing ju heue.
Singaix cie Simiang Siyiex
28 eKhaix an,
aux tag sang klup tix tawx khuan tix ka pui ku kaux:
kawn simex kawn bun pex tag sang krai pao ka gum lox aux;
pui riex ju pex tag sang ca maox ka daux riang su sai;
pui nhum ju pex tag sang yaox ka riang su sai.
29 Yam an,
aux tag sang klup khuan tix
tawx ka siliex mai bun siliex.
 
30 Aux tag sang jhi mhai pa daox pa am ka rao ma mai piang haktiex,
koe nham,
koe ngu,
koe taox ngu.
31 fSingaix tag sang faik ang koe pa riang;
khix hoik raoh awm nham yuh nan.
Sivoe sang hoek singaix min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh khawm uik.
32 gCan pui pa kok tix khiox khaw ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex
tag sang pon krax taoh heue.
Awm hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Ka piang gawng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa singax singeh tag sang lih koe pui pa luat luan;
pui pa hoik laug aux tag sang pon tix im ju heue.
