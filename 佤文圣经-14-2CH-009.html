Simiang bun See-pa hoek tix vhi
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 10.1–13)
9
aSimiang bun See-pa mhong grawng krax ao min simiang Saw-law-meung,
tom hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
keud tix sang teei kaing ceu pa nu mai cheuk tix yaox Saw-law-meung.
Nawh viex pui hawt khaix tix kied hun,
kawn koe nang ka-la-uk tom nie pa tang ka sidah bhawng,
siang,
mai khri tom nie hoek.
Yam yaox nawh ka Saw-law-meung,
nawh hoik krai lih ka can lox pa keud nawh tix sang chawk ka Saw-law-meung ku gum.
2 Saw-law-meung pawk lox pa chawk nawh khawm uik,
mai ang koe pa nu ka nawh tix ceu kawx.
3 Simiang bun See-pa mhong grawng koe ping nyia cuyi Saw-law-meung,
yaox ka nyiex haw pa hoik sum nawh,
4 mai baox yaox ka priex nyawm rawm lhong pa ot ka piang pheen nawh,
ka nyiex ot siliex nawh,
ka cub pui cau nyiex haw simiang mai kraung pa ceep kix,
siliex pa prauk plai mai kraung pa ceep kix,
khawng tanb pa koe ka daux nyiex buan khankix,
nawh hoik daox deng tom ang lai koe lox pa sang krai ka heue.
5 Nawh ah nin ka simiang Saw-law-meung:
“Pa mhong aux ka daux meung tix grawng pa haok huan maix,
c mai grawng pa koe ping nyia cuyi maix mawh tete.
6 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoek ka dee in mai hoik yaox ka khawm uik,
aux tag yum rhawm ka tete.
Grawng pa ting ping nyia cuyi pa koe maix,
pa hoik mhong aux ang bax greung;
maix kawn lheu ting ping nyia cuyi khaing lox pa hoik krai pui.
7 Siliex maix kied sidaing mhawm ot mhawm koe tete heue!
Kix tag yaox tix ot ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
mai nghiet ka lox ping nyia cuyi pa krai maix ku jheeing jheeng.
8 Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix pon krax pa yaog yaw!
Nawh yaog maix mawh simiang,
mai kieh maix giex kaing ka daux ceu kaux tix,
in hoik mawh jhi nawh grawng pa kied muh ka maix tete.
Mawh kheu muh nawh ka pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex,
tag sang kia ngien ka kix leeing ju leeing phan,
mawh kheu nan,
tom yaog maix mawh simiang kix,
son sang kieh maix pon prawng ka daux krax pa rauh see heue.”
9 Simiang bun See-pa tawx khawng phak pa viex tix ka simiang Saw-law-meung:
mawh khri been pon rheng kongcing,
sidah bhawng mai siang tom nie.
Simiang Saw-law-meung ang nyang sax rieb khawng phak sidah bhawng pa ting ngawh yuh nin,
awm pa hoik tawx simiang bun See-pa heue.
10 Siliex simiang Si-lang mai siliex simiang Saw-law-meung viex khri kaoh khaing Eu-fe,
mai viex nang khawm khaox gix bhawng mai siang hoek.
11 Simiang Saw-law-meung yuh nyiex buan mai glawk bawng nyiex haw simiang ka khaox gix bhawng in kix,
mai baox yuh nang ting jung lah mai ting aliah lah son pui pa kied chix rhax lai.
Pui Yo-tax ang nyang sax yaox ka khaox gix bhawng pa mhawm yuh nin tix bawg kawx heue.
12 Simiang Saw-law-meung lheu khaing vied nawh khawng phak pa tawx simiang bun See-pa ka nawh khankix,
can ceu pa mhaing bun simiang See-pa,
nawh hoik tawx khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
bun simiang an mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix,
deeh ing haktiex See-pa heue.
Sin ngao simiang Saw-law-meung
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 10.14–25)
13 Simiang Saw-law-meung tix num rieb khri ra mheen loe rheng kongcing haok plak piang,
14 khaix an kawn koe keen pa tawx pui yuh lai gee.
Simiang A-la-pawx khankix mai cao kaing haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh kawx tawx keen mau khri ka nawh.
15 Simiang Saw-law-meung yuh dieb tie naok ra yieh plah,
tix plah yuh ka khri been aliah kongcing.
16 Nawh baox yuh dieb tie naok pa iak loe yieh plah,
tix plah yuh ka khri been loe kongcing.
Nawh deuh dieb tie naok in kix ka daux nyiex haw Li-pa-nuing-ling heue.
17 Simiang Saw-law-meung baox yuh ka dang ngeung ka rhang sang tix mu,
mai om ka khri mhawm.
18 Sivoe dang ngeung koe glawk bawng ka liah glawk,
mai si toe tix tau mai ngot,
mai hoik om khri ka khawm uik.
Ka dang ngeung khawm ra plak hoik kieh koe dee deuh taix tix piang,
tix plak koe sivai sang si ka tix mu.
19 Ka toh glawk bawng an khawm ra toh,
hoik kieh tang koe riang sivai sang si pa ngawm kah,
khawm uik koe kao mu ra.
Meung piang haktiex tix dauh kawx ang sax koe dang ngeung pa yuh nin heue.
20 Can kawk plai simiang Saw-law-meung khawm uik hoik yuh tix ka khri;
can kraung pa siao ka priex preem pa ot ka daux nyiex haw Li-pa-nuing-ling khawm uik,
hoik yuh tix ka khri mhawm,
ang yuh ka mau;
mawh kheu ka daux baux bawg yam Saw-law-meung,
mau ang sawn tix mawh pa ting jum.
21 Cub caw reu pui cie Saw-law-meung pa hu dee singai tix lhang,
hu tau mai cub caw reu pui cie simiang Si-lang;
loe num kix hoek tix bawg,
mai viex khri,
mau rhang sang,
rao khaox khankix hoek heue.
22 Simiang Saw-law-meung kawn lheu mag mi,
lheu koe krax lhak pa lhiao khaing simiang piang haktiex khawm uik.
23 Can simiang piang haktiex ku kaux simie tix yaox ka Saw-law-meung,
simie tix nghiet ka gum lox ping nyia cuyi pa tawx Siyiex ka nawh.
24 Kix viex tix tawx khawng phak ka nawh ku num,
awm pa mawh khri,
mau,
kraung ceep,
kraung naing,
sidah bhawng,
brung,
law khankix heue.
25 dSaw-law-meung koe nyiex gox pa deuh ling law naing mai brung ot daux pon rheng lhang,
mai baox koe pui naing brug brung tix mheen ra rheng kaux.
Nawh guah kix tix blah ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa deuk khankix guah kix ot ka veng tang dauh.
26 eSaw-law-meung jauh tix kuang ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti hoek ka Fe-li-see mai daog haktiex can dee ot simiang Ai-cix.
27 Baux bawg yam giex nawh kaing,
mau Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik haoh nie awm simaox khankix yuh nan,
mai khaox si oe bhawng kawx hoik haoh nie awm khaox liah khankix ka piang gawng iak yuh nan.
28 gSaw-law-meung liang brung khaing Maw-cee-lif mai meung tang meung khankix heue.
Krai lao grawng pa giex Saw-law-meung kaing
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 11.41–43)
29 Kaing yuh Saw-law-meung pa deuh khankix,
jauh ka kaing hoek ka rao,
hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Cao Jhai Na-tang,
ka daux Lai Cao Jhai Ya-si-ya pui See-law,
mai ka daux Lai Cao Pa Chix Yaox Sivoe Yi-taw pa rien yaox ka riang suk sai tom nie khankix.
Lai dee nghoik tix phuk mawh pa hoik tiiem ka kaing yuh Yie-law-paw-ang simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
30 Saw-law-meung mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tipon num.
31 Yam hoik yum nawh,
kix si paung nawh ka veng Ta-wue;
kawn simex nawh Law-paw-ang plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
