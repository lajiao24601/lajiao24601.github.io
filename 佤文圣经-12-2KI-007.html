7
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Tag caw tix nghiet ka gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex.
Pa sax bawg yam in ka daux laih Sa-ma-li-yax,
pex teei mau luah tix plah,
mai hoik kub tix liag nghox ca pa mhawm loe kongcing,
phan ang mawh nan,
nghox ca pa lang mu liah kongcing heue.”
2 Pui cau simiang ah nin ka Yi-li-sa:
“Ang doh sang pon yuh nan.
Viang Simiang Siyiex hoik paoh ka siviex rao ma mai ploi ka nghox koi lih hoek kawx,
ang doh sang pon yuh nan heue!”
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Maix tag sang yaox ka kaing in pa lih tix mawh ka ngai tix,
viang mawh nan,
maix ang yaox tix ih ka heue.”
Pui naing Shui-li-yax to tix deeh
3 Ka plak praix veng Sa-ma-li-yax,
koe pui saux si pawg ka pon kaux,
kix lai si kah ka paox tix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx lai kieh ex krox tix yum ot dee in lie?
4 Ex hu ka daux veng,
ka daux veng sibhawm praix lih koe kah,
ex chix tix yum ka dee an;
ex ngawm dee in,
lha cao ex ka yum,
lheu khaing ap ex tix ang pex ka pui Shui-li-yax.
Kix tee rien tix pauh ka ex,
phan ang mawh nan tee rien tix deuh ka ju ex.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
yam sang dex faik praix,
kix hu ka nyiex bang pui naing Shui-li-yax,
yam hoek kix dee an,
mai ang yaox pui tix kaux kawx.
6 Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh pui Shui-li-yax mhong lox luah pa hoek cub pui naing brung mai ling brung ce simiang pui Yi-seu-liex vai pui Heux mai pui Ai-cix mai khawm pui naing khankix hoek puing ka kix.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
yam boi praix daux ngai an,
pui Shui-li-yax hoik tang yaog tix to,
tik ka nyiex bang kix,
brung kix,
la kix;
mai nyiex bang kawn ot dee an mhawm mhawm heue.
8 Pui saux si pawg an kix pon kaux hoek ka bang,
laig daux nyiex bang,
mai ih nyaux dee an;
kix yaox ka khri,
mau,
mai kraung ceep,
kix tom teei mox,
khaix an deeh lih hu khaing nyiex bang in,
laig ka nyiex bang tang mu,
mai baox yuh ka kaing pa awm paox tix.
9 Khaix an,
kix lai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Ex khaw tix ang yuh nin;
ex hoik koe lox pa mhawm,
mai ang pon tix ang krai ka pui.
Phan ex taug krox tix krai pa sax pon ngawb,
ex rien tix pon krax pa ciyang.
Yam in ex hu krai ka cao kaing pa ot greem simiang khaing heue!”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
kix lih hu khaing dee bang pui naing Sa-ma-li-yax deeh hoek ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
mai kok yo ah nin ka pui pa pau veng:
“Yix hoek ka dee bang pui naing Shui-li-yax,
mai ang yaox pui kah,
pie lox si kah pui kawx ang mhong.
Brung mai la kawn phrawk tix ot,
nyiex bang kawx kawn ot mhawm dee an heue.”
11 Pui cao pau diang siviex krai pao ka lox in,
khaix an mai krai hu ka daux nyiex haw simiang.
12 Yam an kawn mawh plak pon bo,
viang mawh nan,
simiang kaoh khaing it tix,
mai ah nin ka siliex:
“Aux krai pex mhong grawng krax pa keud pui Shui-li-yax.
Kix hoik tawng lih sibhawm praix ka daux haktiex ex dee in,
mawh kheu nan,
kix tom su tix lih hu khaing nyiex bang mai kuan mox tix ot ka daux tiag.
Kix keud ex sang lih hu khaing veng sawg ka priex preem,
phan ex yuh nan,
kix tag giex im ka ex,
mai pon cax ka veng in heue.”
13 Siliex simiang pa ting tix kaux ah nin:
“Pui pa ot daux veng lha cao tag sang nyao yum,
awm pui an kix pa hoik yum yuh nan,
lha cao tag sang sum ju.
Mawh kheu nan,
yuh ka mawx ang ploi pui hu ra loe kaux mai brug ka brung pa kawn deuk khankix phuan mu hu jhak tix yaox mawh kaing pa yuh ka mawx heue.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
kix laug pui ra loe kaux lih.
Simiang tom tawx pui in kix brug ka ling brung ra lhang,
mai tawx lox nhie kix hu jiang tix yaox ka pui naing Shui-li-yax.
15 Pui in kix hu tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai yaox ka praok krax hoik pum tix naog ka kraung ceep mai ka kraung siao tang ceu khankix pa hoik tik pui Shui-li-yax yam tang yaog tix to,
pui in kix deeh ing krai ka simiang.
16 Pui pa ot daux haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax,
nied tix lih khaing veng phai phai,
son sang tud ka nyiex bang pui Shui-li-yax.
Yam an,
awm hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
loe kongcing nghox ca pa mhawm,
mai liah kongcing nghox ca pa lang mu,
coh ngie ka mau luah tix plah khaing heue.
17 Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ploi pui cau tix hu mawh cao bung ka diang siviex veng,
viang mawh nan,
cub pui lai ling tix hoek hu hoik dig nawh yum dee an,
awm lox jhai pa hoik krai Yi-li-sa sivoe yam hu jhak simiang ka nawh.
18 Yi-li-sa hoik sax krai ka simiang,
pa sax bawg yam in,
ka daux Sa-ma-li-yax,
mau luah tix plah mai hoik kub tix liag nghox ca pa mhawm loe kongcing,
phan ang mawh nan,
nghox ca pa lang mu liah kongcing heue.
19 Pui cau simiang ah nin:
“Ang doh sang pon yuh nan.
Viang Simiang Siyiex hoik paoh ka daux doh rao ma mai ploi ka nghox koi lih hoek kawx,
ang doh sang pon yuh nan heue!”
Yi-li-sa pawk ah nin:
“Maix tag sang yaox ka kaing in pa lih tix mawh ka ngai tix,
viang mawh nan,
maix ang yaox tix ih heue.”
20 Gum lox in rug lih tix mawh.
Cub pui lai ling tix hoek hu dig nawh yum ka diang siviex veng heue.
