Siyiex tawx Yi-si-ciex mawh cao pa pau
(3.16–21)
33
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
tag caw tix krai ka paox ceu paox cad maix,
yam kieh aux meung tix meung si pup krax pa laux leeing ka naing,
pui meung an tag sang laug pui tix kaux mawh cao pa pau.
3 Yam yaox cao pa pau hoik dex pui koeng ka kix,
nawh tag sang phlu teet tawng kieh pui rien rhawm tix.
4 Phan pui hoik mhong ka lox teet an,
mai ang simi,
kheu nan pui koeng pauh nawh yum,
phi map tag sang hoek piang to kaux nawh.
5 Nawh tag caw tix tang klawm ka phi map tix,
mawh kheu ang nawh simi ka lox teet.
Phan nawh rieb ka lox teet an,
mai nawh tag yaox tix klawd ju tete.
6 Viang mawh nan,
phan cao pau yaox pui koeng hoik dex ka kix,
nawh ang phlu teet tawng,
pui koeng hoik hoek,
mai pauh pui yum,
pui yum an viang hoik mawh pui phi map kawx,
mai aux kawx sang si kaw nham nawh ka cao pa pau heue.
7 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
yam in aux tawx maix mawh cao pa pau ka meung Yi-seu-liex.
Maix tag caw tix krai lox pa ah aux ka maix ne raw ka kix.
8 Phan aux krai pao pui caox tix kaux kaux tag caw tix yum,
maix ang hu ne raw ka nawh,
sidiex gau nawh loh rhawm rhi tix,
nawh rub yum kheu phi map tix,
aux kawx tag sang si kaw nham nawh ka maix.
9 Phan maix hoik ne raw ka pui caox an,
nawh ang loh rhawm tix,
mai ca kawn yuh pa caox,
nawh rub yum kheu phi map tix,
maix tag ang yaox tix klawm heue.”
Beeh glawm pui kaux mawx kaux an
10 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix siao ka lox pa krai pui Yi-seu-liex jheeng jheeng ah nin:
‘Phi map krax pa lud loe ex hoik gut to kaux ex awm beeh glawm pa cien yuh nan,
ex krawx hu si nyek si nyek,
mai ex kawn sang pon im ju yuh ka mawx lie?’
11 Tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka ceu kaux pa im ju tix leeing ju,
aux ang rhawm yaox yum pui phi map,
mai simie tix yaox pa im ju nawh kheu pa hoik loh rhawm rhi tix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai tik vud ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix heue!
Yuh ka mawx pex lai kieh tix yum yuh nin lie?
12 “Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
tag caw tix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
pui mhawm yuh phi map,
kaing mhawm pa hoik yuh nawh sivoe an ang pon taoh ka nawh.
Pui caox loh rhawm rhi tix,
ang yaox tix kham krax ciyang;
pui mhawm yuh phi map,
nawh ang pon krax pa pluat ploi.
13 Viang aux hoik ap tix kieh pui mhawm yaox tix im ju,
phan nawh een to kaux tix hoik kub yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
mai jauh tix yuh ka phi map,
aux ang chix doh keud ka kaing mhawm pa hoik yuh nawh sivoe an.
Nawh tag sang yum kheu phi map tix.
14 Viang aux hoik ne raw ka pui caox tag sang yum tete,
viang mawh nan,
phan nawh loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai yuh kaing pa khaw caw.
15 Tiag tix yaox,
phan kraung khrai pa hoik tang nawh deeh tawx ka cao kraung,
phan ang mawh nan,
kraung khrai pa hoik briex nawh deeh tawx ka cao kraung,
nawh hoik loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx krax pa im ju,
nawh tag yaox tix klawd,
mai ang yaox tix yum heue.
16 Aux tag sang pluat ploi ka can phi map pa hoik yuh nawh khawm uik;
nawh tag sang yaox tix im ju kheu kaing pa khaw caw pa hoik yuh tix heue.
17 “Khaix an,
paox ceu paox cad maix krai yuh aux nin ang khaw.
Tete te lai,
mawh pa yuh kix pa ang khaw.
18 Pui rauh pui see ang lai baox yuh kaing pa mhawm,
mai tieh tix yuh kaing pa caox,
nawh tag sang yum kheu yuh tix nin.
19 Pui caox phan loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai yuh ka kaing ceu pa khaw caw,
nawh tag sang taoh ka ju tix.
20 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex krai yuh aux nin mawh pa ang khaw.
Aux tag sang hawt tix prawng ca awm kaing pa hoik yuh pex ku kaux heue.”
Gum lox pa laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung
21 aYam vuk vied vuk viang ex ka daux kao num ra haok khix kao phuan ngaix,
koe pui pa si pup krax laux pa mox tix to khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung tix kaux krai ka aux ah nin,
veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik laux leeing.
22 Sivoe sang hoek pui in tix bo,
khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang aux.
Ka daux ngaix khaix pon ngawb,
sivoe sang hoek pui in,
Simiang Siyiex hoik deeh tawx aux koe riang rao tix krai lox heue.
Phi map kawn pui
23 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
24 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
pui pa paung ot ka veng pa riem an ka daux haktiex Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
‘Ya-paw-la-hang tix kaux khaing hoik pon haktiex in,
kaux pui ex hoik nie yuh nin,
ex khaw tix nie pon ka haktiex in heue.’
25 “Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
pex hoik ih nex pa koe nham,
krub ka riang rao,
pauh ka pui,
kawn sang pon krai pex haktiex in mawh cie tix yuh ka mawx lie?
26 Pex hoik een rhawm tix ka riang rao pa kua tix,
yuh kaing pa yum koeng pui kah,
phit vao mai moeng paox plawg tix ku kaux,
kawn sang pon krai pex haktiex in mawh cie tix yuh ka mawx lie?
27 “Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tiex tu ka ju tix yuh nin:
pui pa paung ot ka veng pa riem an tag sang yum ka greem lawm vaig;
pui pa paung ot ka daux tiag daux tu to sat tag sang ih ka kix;
pui pa mox tix ot ka piang gawng mai ka daux dawh rang tag yum ka jhawm saux.
28 Aux tag sang kieh haktiex in lih tix riem khawm uik;
riang rao pa yaw kix rang di kah tag sang grai khawm uik.
Gawng haktiex Yi-seu-liex tag sang lih tix riem khawm uik,
mai ang koe pui pa laig daux.
29 Yam ciyang aux ka phi map kawn pui,
yam kieh aux haktiex kix lih tix riem khawm uik,
kix taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Grawng pa si dauh lox pa sidiex cao jhai
30 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
yam ot paox ceu paox cad maix ka praok guh veng,
mai ka diang siviex nyiex tix krai lao ka maix.
Kix ra lai krai ka paox tix ah nin:
‘Ex hu nghiet tix mhong ka Simiang Siyiex sang koe nawh lox ceu pa tix heue!’
31 Mawh kheu nan,
pui cie aux preeng tix hoek,
sang nghiet tix mhong ka lox pa krai maix.
Yam hoik mhong kix,
ang tie hawt tix yuh.
Kix hoik kieh lox maix ang laig daux yhaok tix,
mai ca kawn ot ka daux rhawm ghoing pa prim.
32 Ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
maix mawh ngie pui pa chix rhax lai tix kaux khaing,
chix rhax ngie lai rhawm muh khaing,
chix tauh ngie ting jung lah khaing.
Lox maix kix hoik nghiet ka ku gum,
viang mawh nan,
ang tie hawt tix yuh tix gum kawx.
33 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoik lih gum lox pa hoik krai maix tix mawh,
aux hoik tawng tag sang lih tix mawh tete,
yam an kix tag sang tawng,
cao jhai hoik sax koe tix kaux pa ot ka si nax kix heue.”
