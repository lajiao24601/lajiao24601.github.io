Kawn pui khiox khaw tix sum ka simiang
8
Yam hoik kuad Sa-mu-eu,
mai ap kawn simex nawh khankix mawh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex.
2 Kawn pux ke kaux Yaw-eux,
kawn pux nghoik kaux Ya-pi-yax,
kix hoik mawh cao prawng ka haktiex Piie-see-pa khawm uik.
3 Kix ang gau tix hawt grawng keeing tix,
mai sawg ngie ka mau cai,
mai rieb ka mau chun praok,
prawng ka daux krax pa ang rauh ang see heue.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
can kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex tom khrawm paox tix hoek ka La-max,
mai hu ka Sa-mu-eu,
5 aah nin ka nawh:
“Maix jhak,
maix hoik kuad,
kawn simex maix ang gau tix hawt grawng maix.
Mawh kheu nan,
maix sum simiang tix kaux son ex,
kieh nawh kia ngien ka ex,
awm meung tang meung khankix yuh nan.”
6 Sa-mu-eu ang gaux rhawm ka grawng pa mhaing kix nawh sum ka simiang,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Maix hawt tix yuh awm rhawm keud kawn pui heue!
Kix ang mawh sang tik sang vud ka maix,
hoik mawh sang tik sang vud ka aux,
ang sang lai kieh aux mawh simiang kix.
8 Jauh tom viex aux kix lih khaing daux Ai-cix,
kix hoik dawg tik vud ka aux,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux.
Yam in pa yuh kix ka maix,
hoik mawh pa yuh kix ka aux.
9 Maix hawt tix yuh awm lox kix heue!
Viang mawh nan,
maix caw tix ne raw ka kix mhawm mhawm,
mai krai kix mhong sidawng,
singaix pa sang hoek,
simiang kix tag sang yuh ka mawx ka kix heue.”
10 Sa-mu-eu krai lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka nawh ku gum ka kawn pui pa nhie nawh sum simiang son kix.
11 Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang pex tag sang yuh nin ka pex,
nawh tag sang sawg kawn simex pex hu yuh naing,
tix blah mawh pui naing brug ling law,
tix blah mawh pui naing brug brung,
mai tix blah mawh pui naing pa hu sivoe paox tix.
12 Nawh tawx pui tix blah mawh simiang naing,
tix blah viex ka pui naing tix rheng kaux,
tix blah viex ka tix phuan kaux.
Kawn simex pex tag sang sum kaung,
teem nawh teei nghox koi,
teem nawh vawg nghox,
yuh kraung naing mai kraung naing pa siao ka ling law khankix son nawh.
13 Kawn bun pex tag sang yuh sidah bhawng,
khre priex,
tong khao mun khankix son nawh.
14 Nawh tag sang teei tix tawx kaung ma pex,
si peem plix siiet pex,
si peem khaox sime pex pa blawn mhawm khankix ka siliex nawh.
15 Nawh tag sang teei tix tawx nghox koi pex,
plix siiet pex kao son tix son ka kawn cau pa ting nawh mai simiang tang kaux khankix.
16 Nawh tag sang viex hu ka siliex pex,
khawng brieb pex pa blawn mhawm,
mai la,
mai kieh kix yuh kaing son tix.
17 Nawh tag sang teei ka yung eei pex kao son tix son,
mai pex kawx tag sang yuh siliex cawng nawh.
18 Hoek daux singaix an,
mai pex tag sang yiiem son simiang pa hoik sum pex son to kaux tix,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang nghiet ka lox salao pex heue.”
19 Kawn pui ang nghiet ka lox Sa-mu-eu,
mai tieh tix ah nin:
“Ang,
yix sang kieh simiang kia ngien piang yix!
20 Yuh nin,
yix tom tag yaox tix awm kawn pui tang meung khankix,
mai hoik tang koe simiang to kaux cao tix kia ngien ka piang ex,
mai yaox tix thung ex hu puing naing ka daux bang naing heue.”
21 Sa-mu-eu nghiet ka lox kix,
mai krai ka Simiang Siyiex.
22 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Hawt tix yuh awm lox pa rawg mhaing kix,
tawx simiang ka kix heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu tom kok ka can pui Yi-seu-liex tang deeh ing ka nyiex ma tix heue.
