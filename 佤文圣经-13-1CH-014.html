Grawng kaing pa yuh Ta-wue ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 5.11–16)
14
Simiang Thai-eux pa mawh Si-lang ploi pui yuh lai gee tix cub hu ka Ta-wue dee an,
tawx khaox si oe bhawng ka Ta-wue,
mai baox viex cang mai khaox mai cang mai simaox hoek,
son sang mawh cao pa sum nyiex haw tang nawh.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue hoik tawng grawng hoik ap Simiang Siyiex tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kheu pui cie nawh khankix hoik kieh meung nawh haok huan heue.
3 Ta-wue baox laug tix koe moeng iak tom nie ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hoik keh kawn simex kawn bun khankix tom nie.
4 Pa keh ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung mawh Sa-mu-yax,
Saw-pax,
Na-tang,
Saw-law-meung,
5 Yi-sia,
Yi-li-su-yax,
Yi-fa-liex,
6 Naw-cia,
Ni-fe,
Ya-fe-yax,
7 Yi-li-sa-ma,
Pi-li-ya-taxa,
Yi-li-fa-liex heue.
Puing pex ka pui Fe-li-see
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 5.17–25)
8 Pui Fe-li-see hoik mhong grawng pa ap Ta-wue tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
tom khrawm pui naing hu,
mai sawg ka Ta-wue.
Ta-wue kawx thung pui naing tix,
kuan rieb tix puing naing.
9 Pui Fe-li-see hoek ka daux ngawd Li-fa-ying,
mai jauh tix tud rhim.
10 Ta-wue khiox khaw chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang khaw aux tix haok puing ka pui Fe-li-see lie?
Maix sang kieh aux puing pex lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix haok,
aux tag sang kieh maix puing pex heue!”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue haok ka Pa-lix Pi-la-sing,
mai pon pex ka pui Fe-li-see dee an.
Nawh ah nin:
“Siyiex siao aux pung prang ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
awm rawm ting pa chun die tiex laux yuh nan.”
Mawh kheu nan,
dee an tom kok mawh Pa-lix Pa-la-singb.
12 Yam to pui Fe-li-see,
mai kix tik riang rao pa krub kix dee an,
Ta-wue tawx lox nhie pui gleeing ka riang khankix heue.
13 Ang koe miex glang tix,
pui Fe-li-see deeh hoek ka daux ngawd Li-fa-ying,
mai tud rhim kraung khrai.
14 Ta-wue baox khiox khaw chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex;
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Paw vi puing kix khaing dee in,
caw tix via krax hoek plak khaix kix,
mai puing kix dee dex koe khaox liah plak an.
15 Yam mhong maix luah lox caong pui ka piang lhax khaox,
tom yaog tix puing heue,
mawh kheu aux hoik hu sivoe maix,
mai puing ka pui naing Fe-li-see.”
16 Khaix an,
Ta-wue hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh puing ka pui Fe-li-see deeh,
jauh ka haktiex Ci-piien mai leue tom hoek ka haktiex Ci-seux.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu kaux Ta-wue hoik ao min tom dee singai;
Simiang Siyiex kieh meung ku dauh lhat graung ka nawh heue.
