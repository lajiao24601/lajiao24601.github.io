Pa rhax lai mai pa yaog yaw
65
Siyiex aux eue,
yix khaw tix rhax lai yaog yaw maix ka Shi-ang;
yix khaw tix plud ka lox pa hoik ap pong tix tiex tu ka maix,
2-3 mawh kheu hoik nghiet maix ka lox pa khiox khaw yix.
Kawn pui hoik hoek sawg maix khawm uik kheu pa yuh tix phi map.
Krax pa lud loe yixa hoik kieh yix laux leeing,
viang mawh nan,
maix hoik pluat ploi ka khawm uik.
4 Pui pa hoik laug maix tix viex hoek ka daux nyiex buan,
mai kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
Yix tag sang yaox tix paung ot ka dee mhawm cie maix,
pa mawh dee gaux rhawm ka daux nyiex buan maix heue.
 
5 Maix hoik nghiet ka lox yix,
mai tawx krax puing pex ka yix;
maix hoik teei ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh tix taoh yix.
Kawn pui ka piang haktiex ku ceu,
pui pa ot tom caong ma dee singai hoik yum rhawm ka maix khawm uik.
6 Maix jhi pa ting riang ting rao tix;
teei siawp sibrawm tix kieh gawng lua kaux cong.
7 Maix kieh krax pa song rhawm song rhi rawm sigrit sigra mai lox luah pa puk pak rawm ting pon tix yien yawg;
maix pon kieh krax pa lawh kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik yien yawg.
8 Haktiex khawm uik lhat graung yung yi ka son kaing ting pa hoik yuh maix lih khankix;
kawn pui haktiex jauh plak lih singaix tom hoek plak klip singaix hoik gaux rhawm kheu kaing pa yuh maix heue.
 
9 Maix kia ngien ka haktiex,
mai ploi rawm lhiex lih caux;
maix kieh haktiex mhawm,
hoik haok huan ka priex preem.
Rawm klawng pa tawx maix hoik naog ka rawm;
maix kieh haktiex hoik kuah ka nghox koi ang jud ang jai.
10 Maix kieh lhiex pa thai pui ma kaung ka hoik lih tom kub,
kieh rawm caux ka haktiex leet;
maix ploi lhiex shiah mu lih kieh tiex bhaw,
kieh to ma to koi ka daux kaung ma khankix kuah.
11 Maix tawx buan son ka piang jhong khix mee num pon krax pa haok huan;
rhoi khaix caong maix hoik kieh kaing ku ceu pon yaox tix mhawm.
12 Ka dee liang ka khawng brie hoik lih mawh phung moi phung yung;
ka si nax gawng iak hoik koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
13 Moi yung hoik naog ka daux tiag daux tu;
nghox koi hoik naog ka piang tiex.
To sat ku ceu hoik gaux rhawm rhax ka lai heue.
