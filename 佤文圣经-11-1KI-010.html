Simiang bun See-pa hoek tix vhi
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 9.1–12)
10
aSimiang bun See-pa mhong grawng pon simiang Saw-law-meung krax ao min kheu pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex buan,
tom hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
keud tix sang teei kaing ceu pa nu mai cheuk tix yaox Saw-law-meung.
2 Nawh viex pui hawt khaix tix tom nie,
kawn koe nang ka-la-uk tom nie pa tang ka sidah bhawng,
siang,
mai khri tom nie hoek.
Yam yaox nawh ka Saw-law-meung,
nawh hoik krai lih ka can lox pa keud nawh tix sang chawk ka Saw-law-meung ku gum.
3 Saw-law-meung pawk lox pa chawk nawh khawm uik,
mai ang koe pa nu ka nawh tix ceu kawx.
4 Simiang bun See-pa mhong grawng koe ping nyia cuyi Saw-law-meung,
yaox ka nyiex haw pa hoik sum nawh,
5 mai baox yaox ka priex nyawm rawm lhong pa ot ka piang pheen nawh,
ka nyiex ot siliex nawh,
cub pui cau nyiex haw simiang mai kraung pa ceep kix,
siliex pa prauk plai,
khawng tan pa koe ka daux nyiex buan khankix,
nawh hoik daox deng tom ang lai koe lox pa sang krai ka heue.
6 Nawh ah nin ka simiang Saw-law-meung:
“Pa mhong aux ka daux meung tix grawng pa haok huan maix,
mai grawng pa koe ping nyia cuyi maix mawh tete.
7 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoek ka dee in mai hoik yaox ka khawm uik,
aux tag yum rhawm ka tete.
Pa hoik mhong aux ang bax greung!
Ping nyia cuyi pa koe maix mai krax pa haok huan maix,
kawn nhie khaing pa krai pui ka aux heue.
8 Moeng maix mai moeng iakb maix kied sidaing mhawm son tete!
Siliex maix kied sidaing mhawm ot mhawm koe tete heue!
Kix tag yaox tix ot ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
mai nghiet ka lox ping nyia cuyi pa krai maix ku jheeng jheeng.
9 Kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix pon krax pa yaog yaw!
Nawh yaog maix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai hoik jhi grawng pa kied muh nawh maix.
Mawh kheu muh nawh Yi-seu-liex leeing ju leeing phan,
kheu nan,
tom yaog maix mawh simiang,
son sang kieh maix prawng ka daux krax pa rauh see heue.”
10 Simiang bun See-pa tawx khawng phak pa viex tix ka simiang Saw-law-meung:
mawh khri been pon rheng kongcing,
sidah bhawng mai siang tom nie.
Simiang Saw-law-meung ang nyang sax rieb sidah bhawng tom nie yuh nin tix bawg kawx heue.
11 Cub caw reu pui cie Si-lang tang khri kaoh khaing Eu-fe,
mai tang nang ka khaox gix bhawng mai siang hoek.
12 Simiang Saw-law-meung yuh nyiex buan mai khaox tieb nyiex haw simiang ka khaox gix bhawng in kix,
mai baox yuh nang ting jung lah mai ting aliah lah son pui pa kied chix rhax lai.
Pui Yi-seu-liex ang nyang sax tang ka khaox gix bhawng tom nie yuh nin tix bawg kawx,
mai khaix in sax ka ang sang lai yaox tix bawg kawx heue.
13 Simiang Saw-law-meung si pieh rhawm tix tawx nang khawng phak ku ceu ka simiang bun See-pa,
lheu khaing hawt ka been pa hoik tat kix tix tawx khawng phak,
can ceu pa mhaing bun simiang See-pa,
nawh hoik tawx khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
bun simiang an mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix,
deeh ing haktiex See-pa heue.
Sin ngao cie simiang Saw-law-meung
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 9.13–28)
14 Simiang Saw-law-meung tix num rieb khri ra mheen loe rheng kongcing haok plak piang,
15 khaix an kawn koe keen pa tawx pui yuh lai geec,
rao lai pa pon kix,
khawng phak keen pa tawx simiang A-la-pawx khankix,
mai khawng phak pa tawx cao kaing haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh heue.
16 Simiang Saw-law-meung yuh dieb tie naok ra yieh plah,
tix plah yuh ka khri been aliah kongcing.
17 Nawh baox yuh dieb tie naok pa iak loe yieh plah,
tix plah yuh ka khri been ra kongcing.
Nawh deuh dieb tie naok in kix ka daux nyiex haw Li-pa-nuing-ling heue.
18 Saw-law-meung baox yuh ka dang ngeung ka rhang sang tix mu,
mai om ka khri pa kied mhawm.
19 Sivoe dang ngeung koe glawk bawng ka liah glawk,
ka plak khaix kieh koe rong lawm le tix mu.
Ka dang ngeung khawm ra plak hoik kieh koe dee deuh taix tix piang,
tix plak koe sivai sang si ka tix mu.
20 Ka toh glawk bawng an khawm ra toh,
hoik kieh tang koe riang sivai sang si pa ngawm kah,
khawm uik koe kao mu ra.
Meung piang haktiex tix dauh kawx ang sax koe dang ngeung pa yuh nin heue.
21 Can kawk plai simiang Saw-law-meung khawm uik hoik yuh tix ka khri;
can kraung pa siao ka priex preem pa ot ka daux nyiex haw Li-pa-nuing-ling khawm uik,
hoik yuh tix ka khri mhawm,
ang yuh ka mau;
mawh kheu ka daux baux bawg yam Saw-law-meung,
mau ang sawn tix mawh pa ting jum.
22 Cub caw reu pui cie Saw-law-meung pa hu dee singai tix lhang,
hu tau mai cub caw reu pui cie simiang Si-lang;
loe num kix hoek tix bawg,
mai viex khri,
mau,
rhang sang,
rao khaox khankix hoek heue.
23 Simiang Saw-law-meung kawn lheu mag mi,
lheu koe krax lhak krax lhiao khaing simiang piang haktiex khawm uik.
24 Pui piang haktiex khawm uik simie tix yaox ka Saw-law-meung,
simie tix nghiet ka gum lox ping nyia cuyi pa tawx Siyiex ka nawh.
25 Kix viex tix tawx khawng phak ka nawh ku num,
awm pa mawh khri,
mau,
kraung ceep,
kraung naing,
sidah bhawng,
brung,
law khankix heue.
26 dSaw-law-meung sum pui naing brug brung koe tix rheng pon yieh lhang mai pui naing brug brung tix mheen ra rheng kaux.
Nawh deuh kix tix blah ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa deuk an kix tix blah guah kix ot ka veng tang dauh.
27 Baux bawg yam giex nawh kaing,
mau Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik haoh awm simaox khankix yuh nan,
mai khaox si oe bhawng kawx hoik haoh awm khaox liah khankix ka piang gawng iak yuh nan.
28 gPui pa yuh lai gee simiang pon giex gang ka brung pa viex laig ka Maw-cee-lie mai Ci-li-ciaf,
29 khawm ling law pa hoek khaing Ai-cix.
Kix deeh coh brung mai ling law ka simiang pui Heux mai simiang pui Shui-li-yax khankix:
Ling law tix lhang coh liah yieh plah mau luah,
brung tix mu coh tix yieh tix phuan plah mau luah heue.
