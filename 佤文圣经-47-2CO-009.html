Teem coi paox pa yuh kaing tau
9
Phan mawh son kaing pa teem coi ka kawn Khrit pui Yo-thaix,
aux ang siao tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka pex.
2 Aux hoik tawng pex gaux rhawm tix lih riang rao tix,
mawh kheu nin,
hoik sax yaw krax pa si pieh rhawm pex ka pui Ma-chi-toeng.
Aux ah nin:
“Kawn Khrit Ya-kai-yax khankix hoik koe krax pa rien khre tix tom num pa hoik luan.”
Krax gaux rhawm pex hoik chuh yuh rhawm pui tom nie.
3 Yam in aux ploi pux aik ex in ra loe kaux hu,
simie pex hawt awm lox pa krai aux,
dha tix rien khre mhawm ka mau pa sang teem tix,
son sang kieh gum lox pa yaw yix pex ka daux kaing in ang mawh pa giex going.
4 Phan koe pui Ma-chi-toeng pa hu tau mai ex,
mai yaox grawng pa ang pex bax koe krax pa rien khre tix,
ex tag sang kied laux pak ngai kah,
mawh kheu hoik kied yum rhawm ex ka pex heue.
Phan mawh ka son pex taug sang chix kaig tua kah,
an tag nie mawh pa ang siao krai heue!
5 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom keud tix sang ploi pux aik ra loe kaux ran hoek ka pex dee an,
mai khrawm ka mau pa tat pex tix sang teem,
krox yam hoek aux mai hoik rien khre tix mhawm khawm uik.
In tom tag jhi grawng krax pa teem coi pex,
mawh pa kaoh khaing krax gaux rhawm pex,
ang mawh ka daux pa khin tix tawx heue.
6 Tag yuh tix tawng:
pa sum ceeix ceeix tag sang khrawm ceeix ceeix;
pa sum tom nie tag sang khrawm tom nie.
7 Maw kheu nan,
pui ku kaux tag caw hawt tix teem coi awm krax pa gaux rhawm tix yuh nan,
paw kieh tix koe rhawm ra mu,
paw kieh mawh ka daux pa khin tix tawx,
mawh kheu Siyiex muh ka pui pa gaux rhawm tix tan lu heue.
8 Siyiex tag nie blawn tawx pa haoh pa lheu ka piang pex,
mai ang joeng kieh pex kub kawn haoh lheu khaing,
khaix an kawn kieh pex koe riang rao tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm ku ceu.
9 aAwm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
nawh si pieh rhawm ka pui hot sibhawm;
krax pa caox rhawm nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
10 bSiyiex kaw pa tawx simie ka cao pa rhuat pa sum,
mai pa baox tawx pui ih ka priex preem,
tag sang tawx simie pa sang lo ka pex,
mai baox kieh simie an ting huan,
kieh krax pa rauh see pex lih tix plix plix tom nie.
11 Nawh kawx tag sang kieh pex mag mi,
mai koe riang rao tix si pieh rhawm tix tawx ka pui ku bawg ku yam,
kie pui tom nie yaog yaw Siyiex kheu pa rieb pui khawng phak khaing daux tawm taix yix.
12 Mawh kheu krax pa teem coi pex ang joeng mawh ngie pa tawx ka son pa lo kawn Khrit khaing,
mai hoik kieh pui tom nie baox tawng tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex.
13 Kaing ceu pa mhawm in mawh sakse tix ceu,
mai kieh pui tom nie tawng ka grawng pa hoik cu nghiet pex ka Khrit,
grawng pa kaux sijax pex ka gum lox buan pa hoik krai pao pui doh keud,
mai hoik pon si pieh rhawm tix teem coi ka pui tang kaux,
kix tag sang tawx min plak ngai ka Siyiex khawm uik.
14 Kix kawx tag sang nie braong rhawm ka pex kheu pa hoik tawx Siyiex buan son pa tang siyox ka pex,
mai nie gaux rhawm tix rawg buan son pex heue.
15 Ex tag sang yaog yaw Siyiex heue!
Nawh hoik koe buan son pa ang lai chix tiag tix yaox heue.
