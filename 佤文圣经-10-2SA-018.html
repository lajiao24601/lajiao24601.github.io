Ya-sa-long ang pex naing
18
Ta-wue khrawm can pui pa hawt khaix tix ot tau,
nawh guah kix lih koe tix cub tix rheng mai tix cub tix yieh,
mai ku cub hoik tang kieh koe cao kaing bung naing,
2 Khaix an mai kieh kix lih koe loe cub,
guah mawh Yaw-ya,
pux Yaw-ya pa kaux Ya-pi-sai,
Yi-thaix pui Cia-theux cao bung.
Simiang ah nin ka pui pa ot greem nawh:
“Aux kawx sang hu puing naing tau mai pex heue.”
3 Kix ah nin:
“Maix ang pon tix hu puing naing tau mai yix.
Phan yix ang pex yaog tix to,
pui been tix plak rien tix yum,
pui koeng ang lai kuang.
Viang mawh nan,
maix tix kaux khaing hoik tang yix kaing mheen kaux,
dee mhawm,
maix ot ka daux veng khaing,
mai kuan rien tix teem coi ka yix heue.”
4 Simiang ah nin:
“Pex keud,
mhawm yuh ka mawx,
aux sang hawt ka yuh nan heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh cung tix ot ka praok diang siviex veng,
mai kok jhak yam lih hu pui naing cie tix tix rheng kaux,
tix yieh kaux yuh nan.
5 Simiang tawx lox ka Yaw-ya,
Ya-pi-sai mai Yi-thaix ah nin:
“Mawh kheu son aux,
pex paw vi yuh ka Ya-sa-long pui pa nhum ju an laux.”
Pui naing khawm uik hoik mhong lox pa tawx Ta-wue ka cao bung kaing naing pa ot greem nawh khankix heue.
6 Pui naing cie Ta-wue hoek ka daux tiag,
mai kix pui naing Yi-seu-liex puing paox tix ka daux nong khaox Yi-fa-liien.
7 Pui naing Yi-seu-liex ang pex pui naing cie Ta-wue,
pui naing Yi-seu-liex kied nie yum nie mat.
Ka daux singaix an,
hoik yum pui ra mheen kaux.
8 Mawh kheu hoik lih naing hawt vung viang daux tiag khawm uik,
pui pa hoik yum daux tiag kawn nie khaing pa hoik yum daux bang naing heue.
9 Pui naing Ta-wue ra loe kaux si pup ka Ya-sa-long.
Yam an Ya-sa-long brug ka law,
mai law hu laig ka greem khaox yang yit.
Hauk kaing nawh vung vhien tix ka kak khaox,
law hoik to tix luan,
nawh tom vhak tix ot ka khaox.
10 Pui naing cie Ta-wue tix kaux yaox kah,
nawh tom krai ka Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Aux yaox Ya-sa-long vhak tix ot ka khaox yang yit tix gawng heue.”
11 Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Maix yaox ka nawh,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang pauh nawh yum lie?
Phan maix pauh nawh yum,
aux sang tawx maix cie mau luah kao plah mai maox khlax hak tix nyaing heue!”
12 Viang mawh nan,
pui an pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix viang tawx aux cie mau luah tix rheng plah,
mai aux ang doh pon chuh ka kawn simiang pie tix giien taix heue!
Ex hoik mhong lox pa tawx simiang ka maix ka Ya-pi-sai mai ka Yi-thaix,
ah nin:
‘Mawh kheu son aux,
pex caw tix kia ngien mhawm ka Ya-sa-long pui pa nhum ju an.’
13 Phan aux lieh lheu ka lox tawx simiang,
mai pauh ka Ya-sa-long,
simiang rien tix tawng,
kaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
ang chix glip ka nawh!
Yam an,
maix tag ang pon yuh pa tid tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.”
14 Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Aux ang koe bawg yam tix lak lheet mai maix!”
Nawh nied tix mai teei tix viex pliah hu loe mu,
jiet caw yam kawn vhak Ya-sa-long tix ot ka khaox yang yit an,
mai klauh dieb rhawm Ya-sa-long ka pliah.
15 Koe pui cau Yaw-ya kao kaux to tix hu pauh ka Ya-sa-long heue.
16 Yaw-ya nhie pui phlu teet naox puing ka naing.
Pui naing cie nawh khankix deeh hoek khawm uik,
mai ang lai leue ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
17 Kix tik to kaux yum Ya-sa-long ka daux tiag khaox daux o tix mu,
mai pum simaox ot ka piang tom nie.
Can pui Yi-seu-liex tom tang to mox tix ka daux nyiex tix heue.
18 Yam kawn im ju Ya-sa-long singaix pa hoik luan,
nawh ot ka daux ngawd simiang mai sum simaox tix mu,
kieh mawh mhai son to kaux tix.
Mawh kheu ang nawh koe kawn simex pa plud ju,
kheu nan tom sum ceu kaux simaox an hawt ceu kaux tix Ya-sa-long.
Tom hoek daux in singaix,
simaox an kawn kok mawh simaox pa kieh doh keud ka Ya-sa-long heue.
Ta-wue mhong grawng pa yum Ya-sa-long
19 Kawn simex Sa-tu pa kaux Ya-si-ma-see ah nin ka Yaw-ya:
“Kieh aux to ing krai lox in ka simiang heue.
Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh simiang luat luan khaing pui pa koeng ka nawh heue.”
20 Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Ang chix,
ka daux in singaix paw ing krai lox ceu pa tid tix,
mai koi ing krai ngaix tang ngaix heue.
Ang mawh in singaix,
mawh kheu in singaix kawn simex simiang hoik yum heue.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya ah nin ka pui Ku-seu siliex cawng nawh:
“Maix ing,
mai krai can kaing pa hoik yaox ex ka simiang.”
Pui siliex cawng an ngeuh kaing tix krub,
khaix an to tix ing heue.
22 Ya-si-ma-see kawn ca sang hu,
mai baox ah nin:
“Viang yuh ka mawx kawx,
kieh aux viex lox in ing nang heue.”
Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Kawn aux eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai sang ing lie?
Maix ing krai lox in,
maix ang doh pon khawng phak heue.”
23 Ya-si-ma-see ah nin:
“Viang yuh ka mawx kawx,
aux sang ing heue!”
Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
ing heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-si-ma-see to hu plak jee ka krax hawt ngawd Yaw-tang,
ang koe miex glang tix nawh krub ka pui Ku-seu heue.
24 Yam an,
Ta-wue ngawm ka diang siviex veng,
ka dee si nax plak praix mai plak daux.
Pui pa pau tix jhak haok ka guh veng,
mai cung ka diang siviex dee choek nyiex kok jhak kah.
Nawh yaox pui tix kaux to tix hoek,
25 nawh tom kok yo hu plak jee,
mai krai ka simiang.
Simiang ah nin:
“Phan mawh pui tix kaux khaing mawh pa hoek kraix lox mhawm.”
Pui an nied to nied dex heue.
26 Khaix an,
pui pa pau tix jhak baox yaox pui tang kaux tix kaux pa baox to tix hoek,
nawh tom kok yo ah nin ka pui pa pau veng:
“Jhak,
baox koe pui tix kaux pa baox to tix hoek heue!”
Simiang ah nin:
“Pui in kawx hoek tix krai lox pa mhawm heue.”
27 Pui pa pau tix jhak ah nin:
“Aux jhak tix yaox,
pui pa to tix hoek phaox an greeh grang nawh hoik awm Ya-si-ma-see.”
Simiang ah nin:
“Nawh mawh pui mhawm.
Nawh rien tix viex lox pa mhawm hoek heue.”
28 Ya-si-ma-see ploi lox tix tom ting ah nin ka simiang:
“Simiang aux lang ju!”
Nawh tom gup tix krub piang tiex,
mai ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix,
nawh hoik kieh Vawng pon pex ka pui pa tieh vheen ka nawh heue!”
29 Simiang chawk ah nin:
“Ya-sa-long pui pa nhum ju an mhawm ot nang lie?”
Ya-si-ma-see pawk ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
cao bung naing cie maix Yaw-ya ploi aux hu,
aux yaox pui haoh pui hun hoik law ka paox tix,
viang mawh nan ang tawng mawh grawng ceu pa tix pa lih tix mawh.”
30 Simiang ah nin:
“Yoi singai!”
Ya-si-ma-see tom deeh tix hu cung plak an heue.
31 Yam an,
pui Ku-seu hoek bleeh,
mai krai ka simiang ah nin:
“Aux koe lox pa mhawm sang krai ka Vawng.
Ka daux in singaix,
Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh maix pon pex ka can pui pa tieh vheen ka maix heue!”
32 Simiang chawk ah nin:
“Ya-sa-long pui pa nhum ju an mhawm ot lie?”
Pui Ku-seu pawk ah nin:
“Kieh kaing pa lih hoek piang nawh,
lih hoek ka piang pui koeng ka maix,
mai ka piang can pui pa tieh vheen ka maix heue!”
33 Simiang nghiet tix mhong,
mai kied laux rhawm kah,
nawh tom haok yiiem ka nyiex ot piang guh veng.
Nawh yiiem khawm hu tix ah nin:
“Eue,
kawn eue!
Kawn eue!
Ya-sa-long kawn eue!
Kieh aux yum tang maix khaing.
Kawn aux Ya-sa-long eue,
kawn eue!”
