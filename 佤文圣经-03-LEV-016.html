Been singaix pa yuh krax pluat ploi phi map
16
Khaix hoik yum kawn simex Ya-loeng ra kaux kheu jao pa tan ka ngu pa bhawng pa ang kiex khaw tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
2 aSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Krai ka pux ke maix Ya-loeng,
nawh tag pon ngie tix laig ka daux nyiex pa kied singax singeh plak khaix man tie kaing,
ka daux bawg yam pa hoik ciak kix laig,
phan ang mawh nan nawh tag yum tete.
Mawh kheu aux tag sang plak jhi tix ka daux pai awm,
piang dawb teug sijax mukheu,
ka daux nyiex pa kied singax singeh.
3 bYa-loeng tag caw tix tan ka kawn moi mhaing tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
kawn yung mhaing tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
khaix an tom tag chix tix laig ka daux nyiex pa kied singax singeh heue.”
4 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx ka gum lox in plak greem.
Sivoe sang laig Ya-loeng ka daux nyiex pa kied singax singeh,
nawh tag caw heem tix,
ceep ka kraung ceep cao giex kaing li,
pa mawh sibex dha pa yuh ka man keei sum,
mai khlax,
rhawt nyoi tix ka maox rawd nyoi pa yuh ka man keei sum,
ceep lawk kaing pa yuh ka man keei sum khankix heue.
5 Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik tag caw tix tawx khawng brie pa tan ka Ya-loeng,
pa mawh kawn pex mhaing ra mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
kawn yung mhaing tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
6 Nawh tag caw tix tan ka moi mhaing tix mu,
son to kaux tix mai son pui nyiex tix,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
7 Khaix an nawh tag sang viex pex mhaing an ra mu hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex.
8 Nawh tag sang yuh cawk lai ka dee an,
cawk lai tix mu tiiem mawh “Tawx mawh cie Simiang Siyiex”,
mai cawk lai an tix mu tiiem mawh “Tawx mawh cie A-sa-shie-leu.”c
9 Ya-loeng tag tan ka pex mhaing pa hoik caw tix cawk lai “Tawx mawh cie Simiang Siyiex” an kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
10 mai tan ka pex mhaing pa hoik caw tix cawk lai “Tawx mawh cie A-sa-shie-leu” an tix mu khawm im tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Khaix an ploi nawh hu daux tiag,
tawx mawh cie A-sa-shie-leu son sang pluat ploi ka phi map pui hun heue.
11 Yam tan Ya-loeng ka moi mhaing son to kaux tix mai son pui nyiex pui ma tix,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
12 nawh tag teei pan ngu pa bhawng,
pa hoik sau gaox ngu naog kah,
ka piang khu dee tan mai mien bhawng tix jhawp,
viex laig ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh.
13 Nawh tag gleeing pa si oe bhawng an ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
kieh taox ngu kheem ka dawb teug sijax mukheu,
son ang nawh sang yum ka yaox dawb.
14 Nawh tag teei nham moi mhaing tix blah,
teei giien taix tix cub ka daux nham,
phruah ot ka sivoe dawb,
mai ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu aliah bawg heue.
15 dKhaix an nawh tag pauh ka pex mhaing,
yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son pui khawm uik.
Nawh tag viex nham pex laig ka daux nyiex dee pa kied singax,
mai phruah ka piang dawb teug mai sivoe ngai teug sijax mukheu,
awm hoik phruah ka nham moi yuh nan.
16 Nawh tag siao ka grawng krax in,
kheu jao ang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix singax,
krax phit lud kix,
nawh tom yuh been pa singax singeh son daux nyiex dee pa singax singeh.
Nawh kawx yuh nang been pa singax singeh son nyiex gox buan man,
jao hoik sum nyiex gox buan man tix ot ka sigreung nyiex bang,
nyiex bang hoik mawh pa ang singax singeh ka daux been.
17 Jauh tom laig Ya-loeng tix yuh been pa singax singeh ka daux nyiex dee pa kied singax,
tom hoek si dauh singaix pa hoik lih nawh,
ka daux nyiex gox buan man ang pon tix kieh pui ot ka daux tix kaux kawx.
Khaix hoik yuh nawh been pa singax singeh son to kaux tix mai son pui nyiex tix,
mai son pui khawm uik,
18 nawh tag hu ka khu dee tan sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
mai tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
kieh khu dee tan singax.
Nawh teei nham moi mhaing tix blah,
mai nham pex tix blah,
kraix ka piang rung khu dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog.
19 Nawh tag caw cub giien taix tix laig ka daux nham an,
mai phruah ka piang khu dee tan aliah bawg.
Kheu jao phi map ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nawh tag siao ka grawng krax an,
hoik singax yuh khu dee tan,
mai kieh kix lih mawh pa singax singeh khawm uik heue.
Pex gawng pa tang son phi map
20 Khaix hoik yuh Ya-loeng been pa singax singeh son nyiex dee pa kied singax,
mai son nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
mai son khu dee tan,
nawh tan ka pex mhaing im an pa mawh “Tawx mawh cie A-sa-shie-leu” ka Simiang Siyiex.
21 Nawh tag deuh taix tix khawm ra khawn ka piang kaing pex an,
mai krai doh keud ka phi map ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai grawng krax pa lieh lheu kix,
khawm krax pa caox pa caig kaing pa yuh kix khawm uik,
nawh tag klawng phi map ceu an kix hoek ka piang kaing pex,
khaix an,
mai tawx lox ka pui tix kaux,
kieh nawh grheu pex an hu ka daux tiag.
22 Pex gawng an tag viex phi map kix hu dee ang pui ot;
mai pui in tag ploi pex an ot ka daux tiag heue.
23 eKhaix an,
Ya-loeng tag laig ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
peeik ka sibex cao giex kaing li,
pa ceep tix yam laig ka daux nyiex dee pa kied singax singeh ot tan.
24 Nawh tag heem tix ka daux nyiex dee pa singax singeh,
mai ceep kraung tix,
khaix an lih plak praix,
tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing son phi map to kaux tix,
mai son phi map pui khawm uik khankix.
25 Nawh tag sang gleeing khlawp bee khawng tan an ka piang khu dee tan,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
26 Pui “Tawx mawh cie A-sa-shie-leu” pa kieh grheu pex hu ka daux tiag an,
tag caw tix ran si daux ka kraung ceep tix,
mai heem tix,
khaix an tom tag chix tix deeh hoek ka nyiex bang.
27 fCaw tix viex moi mhaing mai pex mhaing Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
hoek gleeing ka plak praix nyiex bang;
hak,
nex,
kraung vaig khankix kawx,
tag caw tix gleeing.
Nham khankix hoik viex laig daux nyiex dee pa singax singeh,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
28 Pui pa hu gleeing ka khawng tan an khankix tag caw tix si daux ka kraung ceep tix,
mai heem tix,
khaix an tom tag chix tix deeh hoek ka daux bang heue.
Priex tix hawt ka singaix pa pluat ploi phi map
29 gPui Yi-seu-liex tag caw tix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem leeing ju leeing phan.
Ku num daux khix aliah haok khix kao ngaix,
pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka si nax pex khankix,
tag sang tuah priex preem,
mai ang pon tix yuh kaing jhaw.
30 Ka daux singaix an,
cao giex kaing li tag yuh krax pa pluat ploi phi map son kix,
mai kieh kix singax singeh ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
31 In mawh singaix pa kied singax;
kix tag tuah priex preem,
mai ang pon tix yuh kaing jhaw.
Lox tat jhawm prawng in kix tag mawh pa khaw kix tix priex tix hawt leeing ju leeing phan.
32 Cao giex kaing li pa ting hoik pon beeh glawm,
plud ka kaing pa yuh keeing tix pa hoik ap pong to kaux tix yuh kaing krub cao giex kaing li,
tag yuh ka been pa kieh singax.
Nawh tag ceep ka kraung pa ceep cao giex kaing li,
33 mai yuh been pa singax singeh son nyiex pa kied singax,
mai son nyiex buan man,
mai son khu dee tan,
mai son cao giex kaing li khankix,
khawm son cub pui hun khawm uik.
34 Lox tat jhawm prawng in kix tag caw tix hawt leeing ju leeing phan.
Tix num tix bawg tag caw hawt tix yuh ka been in,
son sang pluat ploi ka phi map ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Kheu nan,
Maw-si hawt yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex heueh.
