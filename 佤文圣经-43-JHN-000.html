Lai Yaw-hang
Krai dawt
Lai Yaw-hang kaing ting tiiem grawng mawh Ye-sux gum lox pa im ju Siyiex leeing ju; nawh “lih mawh kawn pui, mai paung ot ka daux ex”. Cao tiiem kaing ting mawh son sang kieh pui pa phat ka phuk lai in yum rhawm ka Ye-sux mawh Khrit, mawh kawn simex Siyiex, khaix an mawh kheu krax yum rhawm pa yuh nin, tom taug pon ju leeing ju heue (20.31).
Lai in ka daux tix mu simin ting krai gum lox Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju lih mawh kawn pui, mai kieh in mawh num lai khawm uik. Ka daux simin ting ra mu hoek kao ra mu, tiiem grawng pa yuh Ye-sux ka mhai pa daox pa am tom nie, kham sakse grawng mawh nawh cao taoh, mawh nawh kawn simex Siyiex. Phan ex hawt tix yaox ka pa tiiem simin ting kao ra mu, ex tawng koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka nawh, rieb nawh mawh cao taoh; viang mawh nan kawx, hoik koe nang pui pa tieh vheen ka nawh, ang yum rhawm ka nawh. Ka daux simin ting kao loe mu hoek kao aliah mu tiiem grawng yuh nawh paox pie tau mai kawn cau, khawm ka gum lox pa hoik sidiex gau nawh ka kawn cau, gum lox pa tawx nawh krax kaux cong ka kix sivoe sang yum tix. Ka daux simin ting kao daix mu hoek tix nga tix mu tiiem grawng pa giex pui ka nawh, pa prawng ka nawh, pa tawk nawh yum ka khaox lhak, pa deeh kaoh im nawh, khawm pa jhi nawh pui gau yaox tix khaix hoik kaoh im tix heue.
Lox lao pui son bun tix kaux pa pon giex yam yuh krax phit krax vao (8.1–11), baox thiem thie ka mhai […] ceu in, mawh kheu ngai lai pa tiiem ka taix tom nie, mai ngai lai pa thut dix dix ang bax tiiem koe ka baux in, mai hoik koe nang ngai lai pa tiiem ka taix tix blah, teei lox lao pui in deuh nawh ot tang dauh heue.
Cao tiiem lai in nie blawn krai mawh Ye-sux daux krax, mawh pa mawh pa meh, mawh ju. Can pui pa yum rhawm mai pa hoik rieb ka nawh khankix, tag sang pon ju leeing ju leeing phan. Lai Yaw-hang blawn krai sidawng ka can kaing ceu pa koe ka piang haktiex khankix, mawh pa siao lo ka daux krax ot krax koe, awm teei ka rawm, khao mun, pa riang, cao liang yung, cub yung, khaox plix shiet, mai ka plix khaox tang ceu khankix, tiag ka dee bleeh ju khuan heue.
Krai kaing
1. Lox pa jauh (1.1–18)
2. Yaw-hang cao cub com mai pui gau Ye-sux pa liang tix koe sivoe ra loe kaux (1.19–51)
3. Kaing yuh Ye-sux (2.1–12.50)
4. Pa ghoik tix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai haktiex pa dex dee an (13.1–19.42)
5. Pa kaoh im Ye-sux mai pa jhi nawh tix (20.1–31)
6. Lox nghoik: pa baox jhi nawh to kaux tix ka haktiex Cia-li-lix (21.1–25)
