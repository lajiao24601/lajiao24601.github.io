Pui pa ang loh rhawm tix tag sang yum
13
Yam an,
ka dee an koe pui krai ka Ye-sux,
krai koe pui Cia-li-lix tix blah yam yuh kix krax khawng tan ka Siyiex Pi-la-to pauh kix yum.
2 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh kheu pui Cia-li-lix in kix hoik yum ka daux pa ang tix bax lud,
pex tom ce kix kawn lheu ting phi map khaing pui Cia-li-lix tang kaux lie?
3 Aux krai ka pex,
ang mawh nan;
lheu khaing loh pex rhawm rhi tix,
pex kawx tag ang si pup ka ceu pa awm in heue.
4 Yam ghrup gawng mu Si-law-ya pa gut pui yum kao daix kaux an,
pex hoik ce kix kawn lheu caox caig khaing pui tang kaux pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung lie?
5 Aux krai ka pex,
ang mawh;
phan pex ang loh rhawm rhi tix,
pex kawx tag sang yum awm kix nang heue.”
Phek tiiem ka khaox ruh pa ang plix
6 Khaix an,
Ye-sux krai gum lox phek tiiem ka kix tix ceu ah nin:
“Koe pui tix kaux sum khaox ruh tix gawng ka daux si peem plix shiet tix.
Nawh ce tix sang sawg tix ih ka plix khaox an,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh krai ka pui pa pau si peem ah nin:
‘Maix jhak,
hoik koe loe num,
aux sang hoek sawg tix ih plix khaing khaox ruh in,
mai ang yaox tid tix.
Kid tix tik ka heue!
Yuh ka mawx lai kawn kieh nawh kham haktiex lie?’
8 Pui pa pau si peem ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
thiien baox va rhawm ka nawh tix num ha,
krox jun aux kaong bhaw ka tiex pa koe sivie siveng nawh,
mai sau phon ka num nawh.
9 Num khaix,
phan chix plix plix,
mhawm heue;
phan ang mawh nan,
maix tag kid tix tik ka nawh heue.’ ”
Pai bun pa uah gao moi ka daux singaix tuah
10 Yam hoek ka daux singaix tuah,
Ye-sux sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex sidiex.
11 Koe bun tix kaux pa laig khuan phi caox ka daux,
hoik koe jhawm saux kao daix num,
nyoi nawh hoik kawk ang lai pon cung rauh.
12 Ye-sux yaox ka nawh,
mai kok nawh,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Bun,
jhawm saux maix hoik lih hu khaing maix heue!”
13 Ye-sux deuh taix tix piang nawh,
nawh kaoh cung dum mawx dum an,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex heue.
14 aPui cao kuang ka nyiex sidiex yaox pai Ye-sux jhawm saux ka daux singaix tuah,
nawh kied song rhawm kah,
tom krai ka pui hun ah nin:
“Ex yuh kaing ka daux liah singaix,
phan sang pai ka jhawm saux,
tag caw tix mawh ka daux liah singaix,
ang caw tix mawh ka daux singaix tuah heue.”
15 Simiang Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh pui pa pie lhien eue,
khex ang maix kah ka moi,
ka la,
pa ot ka praok dawng tuk hu nyaux rawm lie?
16 Bun in hoik mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-paw-la-hang,
Sa tan hoik phrawk ka nawh kao daix num,
khex ang khaw tix yah ka maox ceng pa phrawk ka nawh ka daux singaix tuah lie?”
17 Lox pa krai Ye-sux in hoik kieh pui koeng kaig ngai,
viang mawh nan,
pui hun hoik kied gaux rhawm ka kaing pa daox deng pa hoik yuh nawh khawm uik heue.
Phek tiiem ka simie sok taux klaox
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.31–32; Lai Ma-kheu 4.30–32)
18 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Meung Siyiex awm ceu pa tix lie?
Aux tag sang teei ceu pa tix hoek tiag tix yaox ka lie?
19 Meung Siyiex hoik awm simie sok taux klaox tix mu,
koe pui sum nawh ot ka daux si peem tix.
Khaix hoik ting simie taux klaox an,
mai lih tix mawh khaox tix gawng,
pie sim khankix kawx hoik yuh mheem tix ot ka piang kak heue.”
Phek tiiem ka lon khao mun pa sau su saix
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.33)
20 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Aux sang teei ceu pa tix hoek tiag tix yaox ka meung Siyiex lie?
21 Meung Siyiex hoik awm su saix,
koe bun teei hoek sau nawh ka daux miien tipon kongsheng,
mai kieh lua miien an lih tix ploh heue.”
Siviex pa si khawp
(Lai Ma-thaix 7.13–14, 12–23)
22 Ye-sux laig ka daux nyiex yaong tom nie,
nawh veeh tix hu ka plak Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai sidiex gau ka pui yam lawng tix krax.
23 Koe pui chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
pui pa pon krax taoh ang hun lie?”
24 Ye-sux pawk lox kix ah nin:
“Pex sau riang tix laig ka daux siviex pa si khawp.
Aux krai ka pex,
koe pui tom nie simie tix laig,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon laig.
25 Krox jun cao nyiex hoik nyeep ka siviex,
pex tom taug cung plak praix gleuh ka siviex ah nin:
‘Sara,
paoh siviex ka yix liak!’
Nawh tag sang pawk ah nin:
‘Aux ang tawng pex kaoh khaing dee mawx heue!’
26 Pex tag sang ah nin:
‘Yix hoik sax mawh pui pa sawm tau mai maix;
maix kawx hoik sidiex gau yix ka daux laih heue.’
27 bViang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang ah nin:
‘Aux krai ka pex,
aux ang tawng pex kaoh khaing dee mawx.
Pex pa mawh pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig in kix hu heue!’
28 cdPex jhak tix yaox ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux,
mai cao jhai khankix hoik ot ka daux meung Siyiex.
Viang mawh nan,
yam grheu pex lih ot plak praix,
pex tag sang yiiem grai,
mai greeik rhang braing tix heue!
29 Jauh ka haktiex khawm pon daog,
pui tag sang hoek tix ih ka priex preem pa khan kheu ka daux meung Siyiex khawm uik.
30 ePui pa ot khaix,
tag sang hu sivoe,
pui pa ot sivoe,
tag sang ot khaix heue.”
Yiiem son Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Ma-thaix 23.37–39)
31 Ka daux bawg yam an,
koe pui Fa-li-saix ra loe kaux hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix tag caw tix lih hu dee in,
hu ka haktiex tang dauh,
mawh kheu sang pauh Si-luix ka maix heue.”
32 Ye-sux pawk lox kix ah nin:
“Pex hu krai ka sox praix an ah nin:
‘Maix jhak heue!
Daux in singaix mai pa sax aux tag sang grheu ka phi,
pai ka jhawm saux,
ka daux loe singaix aux tag sang yuh hoik ka kaing pa yuh aux.’
33 Viang hoik yuh nin kawx,
daux in singaix,
pa sax,
ngaix khaix,
aux tag sang hu sivoe ngai tix,
mawh kheu pauh pui cao jhai ka daux haktiex pa ang mawh Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mawh pa ang khaw heue.
34 “Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
maix hoik pauh ka cao jhai,
mai baox teei simaox tawk kawn cau pa ploi Siyiex hoek ka maix dee in heue!
Aux hoik sang kia ngien ka kawn praix maix miex bawg mawx,
awm khrawm miex ia kawn tix ot greem preeik tix yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang cu heue!
35 fJhak heue,
nyiex ma pex tag sang lih tix mawh haktiex pa riem.
Aux krai ka pex,
jauh yam in pex ang lai baox yaox ka aux,
tom hoek ah pex nin:
‘Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka kaw an pa hoek tix yuh ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Ye-sux heue!’ ”
