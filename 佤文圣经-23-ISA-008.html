Teei kawn simex Yi-sai-yax mawh mhai
8
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Teei phia ting tix plah hoek,
mai tiiem ngai laia ka piang ‘nied tud,
nied rhim’ pon mu ngai lai.
2 Hu sawg pui pa rauh see ra kaux,
pa mawh cao giex kaing li Wu-li-yax mai Sa-cia-li-yax kawn simex Yie-pi-li-cia,
hoek mawh cao pa kham sakse heue.”
3 Ang koe miex glang tix,
aux ot tau maix moeng tix;
nawh koe kaux,
mai keh kawn simex tix kaux.
Khaix hoik keh kawn simex an,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Sum ceu kaux nawh mawh ‘nied tud,
nied rhim’.
4 Sivoe sang chix kok kawn nyawm an ka miex keeing tix,
can sin ngao Ta-ma-see-keux pa koe khawm uik,
mai kraung khrai pa pon khaing daux naing Sa-ma-li-yax khankix simiang Ya-sux hoik teei hu khawm uik heue.”
Simiang Ya-sux dex sang hoek
5 Simiang Siyiex baox krai ka aux ah nin:
6 “Mawh kheu kawn pui in ang khaw rhawm ka rawm klawng iak Si-law-yaxb pa nhim hu,
mai tieh tix hu khaw rhawm ka simiang Li-shuing mai simiang Pi-cia.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh cao maix sang thung simiang Ya-sux mai pui naing cie nawh hoek tix puing ka Yo-tax;
krax pa puing kix naing hoik awm rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti pa ting puk plak tix yuh nan,
mai lih tix haok huan.
8 Kix tag sang awm rawm ting pa lih khaing gawng,
mai kheem ka haktiex,
bleet ka haktiex Yo-tax khawm uik heue.”
Yi-ma-noe-lix,
Siyiex ot tau mai ex heue!
Nawh tag sang nih preeik tix,
mai kia ngien ka haktiexc cie ex heue.
9 Meung ka piang haktiex eue,
nghiet heue,
pex tag sang yaoh yee,
tag sang laux leeing heue.
Pui pa ot dee singai khankix eue,
rien pex hu puing naing heue,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang laux leeing heue!
Mawh tete,
rien pex hu puing ka naing!
Viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang laux leeing heue!
10 Viang pex hoik rom tix keud sawn tau,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang pex;
viang pex hoik rom tix krai lao tau,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon lih tix mawh,
mawh kheu Siyiex ot tau mai ex heue.
Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka cao jhai
11 Simiang Siyiex teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix ne raw ka aux,
ang pon tix hawt tix lawng ka krax pa lawng kawn pui in kix.
Nawh ah nin:
12 d“Paw vi laig yuh krax pa tieh vheen tau mai kix;
ceu pa lhat graung kix kah,
maix paw vi lhat kah,
paw vi graung kah.
13 Caw tix doh keud ka aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing mawh pa singax singeh;
mai mawh ngie aux pa khaw maix tix lhat graung ka khaing.
14 eMawh kheu pa singax singeh aux mawh pa lhat graung pui kah,
mai aux awm simaox tix lon,
pa chix kieh pui goh tom nie;
aux awm rub tix plah,
pa rub giex ka meung Yo-tax mai meung Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
15 Pui tom nie tag sang sideeing tix goh.
Kix si blat tix mat;
rub vung vhien ka kix,
mai pon giex kix heue.”
Paw vi sawg tix chawk ka pui yum
16 Kawn nyawm pui gau tix ka aux khankix eue,
pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex gau Siyiex,
mai hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh.
17 fSimiang Siyiex hoik mox to kaux tix ka sivoe ngai pui cie tix,
viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn yum rhawm ka nawh,
mai hoik deuh krax keud pot jun pot tix ka nawh heue.
18 gAux mai kawn simex kawn bun pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka aux,
hoik kieh mawh gum lox pui in pa ngawm ka piang dang ngeung Shi-ang,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing heue.
19 Koe pui khiox khaw ka pex,
mai nhie pex chawk cheng ka cao chix si meed pa jiet chix krai lox lih khaing daux jheeh tix mai sara pa chix sawn ka ju pui;
kix tag sang ah nin:
“Pui tag caw tix hu kok khuan pui yum ku ceu,
mai khiox khaw chawk cheng ka pui yum son pui im heue.”
20 Pex tag caw tix pawk lox kix yuh nin:
“Tag caw tix nghiet ka lox sidiex gau Siyiex mai lox tat jhawm prawng nawh khaing heue!
Ang doh pon tix nghiet ka lox cao chix si meed mai sara pa chix sawn ka ju pui;
can lox pa krai kix ang koe dee mhawm ka pex pie ceeix kawx heue.”
Yam yaox krax kha rhawm kha rhi
21 Pui tag sang vu vie tix hu ku dauh ku dee,
tag sang yaox krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
krax cai rhawm.
Yam cai rhawm kix,
kix tag sang song rhawm mai jhax lawg ka simiang Vawng mai ka Siyiex kix.
Kix tag sang maong ka rao ma,
22 phan ang mawh nan,
jhak ka piang tiex,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox tid tix,
mawh ngie krax kha rhawm kha rhi mai pa faik khaing heue.
