Riang su sai pa yaox ka praok klawng Ti-keu-li-see
10
Yam mawh Sai-lu-seex simiang Paw-see ka daux loe num,
Siyiex tawx gum lox tix ka Tang-yi-li pa baox kaux Paw-thi-sa-sha.
Gum lox in mawh pa mawh pa meh,
viang mawh nan,
kied mawh pa nu tix tawng.a
Tang-yi-li pon kah dee bleeh ka daux riang su sai heue.
2 Yam an,
aux pa mawh Tang-yi-li hoik laux rhawm loe vang tuah.
3 Ka daux loe vang tuah mhawm mhawm aux ang ih ka priex preem,
ang ih ka nex,
ang nyaux ka plai,
mai ang sat hauk kaing tix heue.
4 Yam haok khix tix tix nga pon ngaix,
aux cung ka praok klawng Ti-keu-li-see.
5 bAux maong tix jhak ka rao ma,
mai yaox ka pui tix kaux pa ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
mai mad nyoi tix ka maox rawd nyoi pa yuh ka khri.
6 To kaux nawh hoik toh riang awm siang simaox yuh nan;
pak ngai nawh jhan riang awm tuh sah;
tawm ngai nawh hoik rup awm ngu yuh nan,
plawng taix mai ba nawh hoik sawng riang awm tawng pa awt plang yuh nan;
lox si kah nawh hoik mhawm luah awm lox yo pui hun yuh nan heue.
7 Mawh ngie aux tix kaux khaing pa yaox riang su sai an.
Pui pa hu tau mai aux ang yaox ceu tid tix;
viang mawh nan,
kix kied lhat graung,
mai to mox tix.
8 Aux ot dee an tix kaux nong nong,
mai jhak ka riang pa daox in.
Aux ang lai koe riang rao pie ceeix kawx,
pak ngai aux tieh tix,
mai ang lai koe pui pa tawng ka aux.
9 Yam mhong aux ka lox nawh,
aux gup tix piang tiex,
mai ang lai tawng praix,
10 Dum mawx dum an,
koe taix pui tix khawn yaog ka aux,
mai kieh aux rien tix kaux ka taix tix mai ngawng tix,
viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn si yaung khawm gaw kaux tix heue.
11 Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Tang-yi-li eue,
Siyiex kied muh ka maix.
Kaoh cung,
mai bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux ka maix;
aux mawh pui pa ploi hoek ka maix dee in heue.”
Khaix hoik uik lox krai nawh,
aux tom kaoh cung,
mai ca kawn si yaung tix heue.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin:
“Tang-yi-li eue,
paw vi lhat.
Jauh ka daux singaix an,
maix hoik viex rhawm awn rhawm tiiem ot ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
mai khiox khaw tix tawng yung sidawng ka dee bleeh riang su sai in,
nawh hoik nghiet ka lox rawg maix buan.
Aux tom hoek kah dee bleeh son maix.
13 cViang mawh nan,
kawn cau Siyiex pa teem ka meung Paw-see tieb tie ka aux,
mai kieh aux dha tix nga tix ngaix.
Mawh kheu ot aux nong nong ka meung Paw-see,
koe cao kaing kawn cau Siyiex pa kaux Mi-cia-leux hoek tix teem coi ka aux.
14 Aux hoek mawh son sang kah dee bleeh kaing pa tag sang si pup paox ceu paox cad maix ka singaix pa sang hoek;
in mawh riang su sai pa dex mai kaing pa sang hoek heue.”
15 Yam si kah nawh mai aux,
aux dong ngai tix piang tiex,
mai kle ang koe lox krai.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau Siyiex pa awm kawn pui an tawx taix tix giex ka siyieb jheeh aux,
aux tom ang jheeh tix krai lox,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang aux eue,
riang su sai in kieh aux ang lai koe riang koe rao,
mai si yaung ang jud.
17 Aux awm siliex cawng pa cung ka plak sivoe ngai simiang tix yuh nan;
aux sang krai lox ka maix yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux hoik uik riang rao,
mai bhawm aux hoik uik heue.”
18 Nawh baox yaog ka aux tix bawg,
mai kieh aux deeh koe riang koe rao.
19 Nawh ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat heue!
Siyiex kied muh ka maix.
Maix tag pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe;
maix tag caw tix kaux cong heue.”
Khaix hoik uik lox krai nawh,
aux hoik mhong nie blawn koe riang tix.
Aux ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
maix hoik kieh aux deeh koe riang koe rao;
aux khiox khaw maix krai lox pa sang krai maix ka aux heue!”
20-21 Nawh ah nin:
“Maix hoik tawng yuh ka mawx aux lai hoek dee in lie?
Aux hoek mawh son sang tawx phuk lai pa mawh pa meh pa hoik plak jhi ka maix.
Yam in aux tag sang deeh ing,
mai yuh naing ka kawn cau Siyiex pa pau meung Paw-see.
Khaix an,
kawn cau Siyiex pa pau meung Si-lax tag sang lih hoek.
Lheu khaing kawn cau Siyiex Mi-cia-leux pa pau ka Yi-seu-liex,
mai ang lai koe pui pa pon teem ka aux heue.d
