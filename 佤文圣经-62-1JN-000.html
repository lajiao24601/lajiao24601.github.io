Lai Yaw-hang Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Lai Yaw-hang Phuk Tix kaing ting koe ra grawng yuh nin:
1. Tawx krax pa joh jhan kieh pui cao phat yuh paox pie ot ka daux krax muh tau mai Siyiex, tau mai kawn simex nawh Ye-sux Khrit.
2. Ne raw ka pui cao phat ang pon tix nghiet ka lox pa pie lhien, pa chix kieh pa yuh paox pie ceu in laux. Lox pie lhien ceu in een mawh pa yuh nin: pa caox pa caig mawh pa lih tix mawh kheu pa hu theeh ka ceu pa koe ka daux haktiex, mawh kheu nan, kawn simex Siyiex Ye-sux ang tau tix doh mawh kawn pui tete. Cub sara pa pie lhien in kix krai cie tix mawh nin: krax taoh mawh pa kieh bheeik luan khaing pa muh ka krax ot krax koe piang haktiex yam in; krax taoh mai kaing ceu pa mhawm, mai pa muh ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix ang koe grawh grawng tau heue.
Son sang tieb tie ka lox pie lhien pa yuh nin, pui cao tiiem krai sidawng ah nin: Ye-sux Khrit mawh pui tete te lai, khaix an nawh baox krai ka pui pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux, pui pa muh ka Siyiex tag caw tix ra lai muh ka paox tix heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–4)
2. Pa riang mai pa faik (1.5–2.29)
3. Kawn praix Siyiex mai kawn praix phi caox (3.1–24)
4. Pa mawh pa meh mai pa pie lhien (4.1–6)
5. Beeh glawm krax muh (4.7–21)
6. Krax yum rhawm pa pex (5.1–21)
