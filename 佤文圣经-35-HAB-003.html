Lox rawg Ha-pa-ku buan
3
In mawh lox rawg cao jhai Ha-pa-ku buan ah nin:
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux hoik mhong ka kaing pa yuh maix,
mai daux rhawm rhi aux hoik naog ka krax pa lhat graung yung yi.
Khiox khaw maix deeh cox yuh ka kaing ting pa hoik yuh maix ka daux singaix bawg yam koe yix;
khiox khaw maix jhi krax pa caox rhawm tix ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix nang heue.
 
3 Siyiex tag sang baox deeh lih hoek khaing Yi-tong;
Siyiex pa singax singeh tag sang lih hoek khaing gawng Pa-lang.
Min plak ngai nawh tawb kheem ka rao ma;
ka piang haktiex hoik naog ka lox rhax pa yaog yaw ka nawh.
4 Nawh viex pa riang tuh sah;
Pa riang lih hoek khaing daux tawm taix nawh,
pa mawh taix pa ting sibrawm pa glip ot pa ploi lih.
5 Ka plak sivoe ngai nawh koe jhawm saux preng;
ka plak khaix nawh koe krax yum.
6 Yam kaog nawh,
haktiex tag yung yang;
yam dong nawh ngai tix,
meung ka piang haktiex tag si yaung.
Gawng lua pa kaux cong leeing ju bih;
gawng iak pa koe dee lung leeing ju ghrup;
in hoik mawh ka dee hu nawh tom ju dix dix.
7 Aux yaox pa lhat graung pui Ku-sang,
mai pa si yaung pui Mi-tiien.
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
mawh rawm klawng pa kieh maix song rhawm lie?
Mawh rawm sigrit sigra pa kieh maix meet rhawm lie?
Maix brug pai awm hoek;
lon pai awm mawh ling law naing maix.
Maix hoik kieh pui cie tix puing tix pex.
9 Maix leed ak puh lih,
mai rien tix sang puing;
maix kieh rawm klawng bih yuh haktiex.
10 Yam yaox gawng lua ka maix tag sang si yaung;
rawm hieh tix lih hoek khaing rao ma.
Rawm pa koe greem haktiex buing puk pliak tix,
mai puk plak tix lih.
11 Yam phai toh riang te puing maix,
yam toh riang pliah viex maix,
singaix mai khix hoik kaog khawm uik.
12 Krax pa song rhawm maix jao luan ka piang haktiex;
krax pa song rhawm maix dug dig ka meung piang haktiex.
13 Maix hu taoh ka pui cie tix,
mai taoh ka simiang pa hoik laug maix.
Maix hoik lauh ka simiang pui caox goh,
hoik yuh ka pui cie nawh laux lak pak leeing.
14 Yam grheu pui naing cie nawh ka yix awm bhaung ting lhiex ting yuh nan,
awm pui cau nyhau pa briex gut kheem ka pui hot pa jiang ka yix yuh nan,
te puing maix klauh phlat ka simiang naing nawh.
15 Maix brug ka brung dug dig ka rawm sigrit sigra,
mai rawm ting tag sang puk pliak tix heue.
 
16 Yam mhong aux kah,
mai aux tag si yaung;
siyieb jheeh aux si kua kheu lhat aux.
Taix caong aux ang lai koe riang;
caong aux hoik yung yang.
Aux tag sang yien yawg krox jun ka bawg yam pa sang hoek an,
yam an Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka pui pa puing ka ex heue.
 
17 Viang khaox ruh ang chix plix plix kawx,
khaox plix siiet ang koe plix kawx;
viang khaox sime ang chix plix plix kawx,
kaung ma ang kuah to ma to koi kawx;
viang hoik kieh yung yum khawm uik kawx,
moi ang koe ka daux kawg kawx;
18 aux kawn ca sang gaux rhawm kheu Simiang Siyiex,
gaux rhawm kheu Siyiex pa mawh cao taoh.
19 aSimiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tawx riang rao ka aux.
Nawh kieh caong aux cong awm caong miex cak yuh nan;
nawh kieh aux cong hu ka piang gawng lhaong heue.
