23
Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
2 Hoek tom yam in singaix,
aux kawn ca tieb tie ka Siyiex,
lox salao lih khaing daux jheeh,
krax ciyang pa cien tawx nawh,
kieh aux ngia ang jud.
3 Viang mawh nan,
kieh aux tawng grawng sang hu sawg nawh ka dee mawx,
kieh aux tawng grawng sang yaox ka dee ot nawh.
4 Aux sang krai nawh mhong grawng see tix,
mai sang krai son to kaux cao tix.
5 Aux sang tawng ka krax pa prawng nawh,
sang sidawng ka lox pa pawk nawh ka aux.
6 Siyiex sang chix teei ka siawp sibrawm pa ting tix mai simah ka aux lie?
Ang,
nawh tag nghiet ka lox pa krai aux tete.
7 Aux mawh pa mawh pa meh,
mai aux chix tix krai lox tau mai nawh;
nawh tag chix tix tat dut ka see in,
mai prawng aux ang koe phi map heue.a
 
8 Viang mawh nan,
aux hu sawg ka plak lih singaix,
Siyiex ang ot dee an;
aux hu sawg ka plak klip singaix,
mai ang yaox ka nawh nang.
9 Siyiex yuh kaing ka haktiex plak seh,
mai aux ang yaox ka nawh;
nawh yuh kaing ka haktiex plak laong,
aux kawx ang yaox ka nawh.
10 Nawh hoik tawng ka krax pa hu aux ku diang caong;
phan nawh cheuk tix yaox ka aux,
tom tag sang tawng grawng pa singax aux.
11 Aux sau riang tix hawt ka diang caong nawh tom krup;
aux hawt tix lawng ka daux krax nawh,
mai ang via hu khaing.
12 Aux priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh ku bawg ku yam,
aux hawt ka alo nawh,
mai ang hawt ka alo to kaux cao tix heue.
 
13 Nawh ang chix tieh chix loh tix leeing ju,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tieb tie ka nawh;
ang koe pui pa pon tieb tie ka kaing pa yuh nawh.
14 Nawh tag sang yuh lih ka kaing pa keud nawh tix sang yuh ka piang aux;
nawh kawn koe kaing pa keud tix sang yuh awm in tom nie ceu.
15 Aux kied lhat graung ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
yam keud aux ka nawh,
aux hoik lhat graung.
16 Siyiex hoik yuh laux leeing ka krax pa kua aux;
Cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik hoik kieh aux yaoh yee.
17 Viang pa faik yuh ngai aux dug kawx,
pa kieh aux lhat graung mawh Siyiex,
ang mawh pa faik heue.
