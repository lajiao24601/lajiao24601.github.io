Krax prawng ca Simiang Siyiex
9
Aux yaox Simiang Siyiex cung ka praok khu dee tan;
nawh ah nin:
“Aux tag sang teei daix tawk nuk nyiex buan,
kieh nyiex ghrup khawm uik,
mai tawk max ka kaing pui ku kaux.
Aux tag sang teei vaig gawn pauh yum ka pui pa kawn deuk khankix,
tix kaux kawx ang kieh yaox tix luat luan,
ang yaox tix kieh pon to.
2 Viang kix hoik kaong kawx ka daux tiex,
laig ka daux meung yum,
aux kawx tag sang giex kix lih heue!
Viang kix haok ka rao ma,
aux kawx tag sang bluh kix lih!
3 Viang kix hoik to mox tix hoek ka gawng Cia-mix kawx,
aux tag sang hu sawg ka kix,
mai yaox kix heue!
Viang kix hoik mox tix ka greem rawm sigrit sigra kawx,
aux tag sang kieh to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat pui ka bleet ka kix heue!
4 Viang pui hoik giex laung ka kix kawx,
aux tag sang kieh pui koeng pauh kix yum heue!
Aux hoik but rhawm tix sang yuh kix laux leeing,
mai ang teem coi ka kix heue.”
5 Yam theeh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting lhaong pa mawh simiang kru naing haktiex,
haktiex tag sang ceh;
pui piang haktiex tag sang yiiem grai khawm uik,
haktiex tag sang naog dieh awm rawm klawng Ni-law yuh nan heue.
 
6 Simiang Siyiex sum nyiex ot ka piang rao ma,
mai kieh rao ma tawm kheem ka haktiex.
Nawh tawx lox ka rawm pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra
hieh ka piang haktiex.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua heue.
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux muh ka pui Ku-seu,
awm muh aux ka pex yuh nan.
Aux thung pui Fe-li-see lih khaing Cia-fe-thaw,
thung pui Shui-li-yax lih khaing Cie-eux,
hoik awm thung aux pex lih khaing Ai-cix yuh nan.
8 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong dong ngai tix jhak ka meung Yi-seu-liex pa kied ting phi map in.
Aux tag sang teei tix tik kix khaing daux haktiex,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang yuh doeh ceu cad Ya-keux laux leeing khawm uik heue.
9 “Aux tag sang tawx lox nhie pui sau pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux grheeng,
pa mawh ka daux grheeng meung ka piang haktiex,
mai grheeng tix tik ka pa laux pa leeing lih khawm uik.
10 Ka daux pui cie aux,
pui phi map an kix pa ah ‘ang krax laux krax leeing lih hoek piang ex’,
tag sang yum ka greem vaig gawn khawm uik heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex haok huan
11 aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Koe tix ngaix,
aux tag sang deeh cox sum ka meung Ta-wue pa hoik laux leeing an.
Aux tag sang blaox ka guh veng nawh;
aux tag sang deeh cox sum kah,
mai kieh nawh grum mhawm awm phaox ka yuh nan,
12 mai kieh pui Yi-seu-liex pon pex ka pui Yi-tong pa kawn deuk khankix,
mai deeh teei hoek ka haktiex pa hoik kieh aux grai.”
Gum lox in mawh son sang kieh kaing in khawm uik pa mawh gum lox Simiang Siyiex lih tix mawh heue.
13 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
singaix an hoik dex sang hoek,
nghox koi kuah,
ang lai tien khrawm;
plix siiet teem,
ang lai tien greeik.
Gawng ting jauh lih plai nyawm,
mai hoik kheem ka gawng iak.
14 Aux tag sang thung pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix;
Kix tag sang deeh cox sum ka veng pa hoik laux leeing,
mai paung ot dee an leeing ju;
kix tag sang deeh cox paih ka si peem plix siiet,
mai nyaux ka plai pa hoik greeik tix;
kix tag baox co khre ka si peem,
ih ka plix pa kuah khaing daux si peem.
15 Aux tag sang kieh ceu pa sum pui cie tix ka daux haktiex pa tawx aux ka kix;
kix tag ang doh lai laoh kix khawm riah.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
