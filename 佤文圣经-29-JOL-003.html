Siyiex tag sang prawng ca ka meung piang haktiex
3
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang kieh pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung deeh pon krax pa haok huan.
2 Aux tag sang kok yo ka meung piang haktiex hoek ka “ngawd prawng ca”;
aux tag sang prawng ca kix kheu kieh kix pui cie aux mat.
Kix hoik sax kieh pui Yi-seu-liex prung prang tix hu ka meung tang meung,
hoik guah ka haktiex cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
3 Kix yuh krax pa cawk lai sang mawh pui mawx pa pon kuang ka pui pa giex laung;
Kix teei kawn nyawm simex loh ka sibun coh kaux,
mai coh kawn nyawm bun liag tix nyaux plai ka heue.
4 a“Pui Thai-eux,
pui Si-toeng,
mai pui haktiex Fe-li-see ku dauh khankix eue,
pex keud tix sang pawk bix ka aux lie?
Phan mawh nan,
aux sang nied tix pawk bix ka pex.
5 Pex hoik teei ka mau khri,
tud rhim ka sin ngao,
mai deuh ot ka daux nyiex vad pex.
6 Pex teei pui haktiex Yo-tax mai ka pui haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung coh kix ka pui Si-lax,
mai kieh kix singai hu khaing meung haktiex ot tix.
7 Yam in aux tag sang viex kix deeh hoek khaing haktiex pa coh pex kix hu.
Aux tag sang yuh pex awm hoik yuh pex ka kix yuh nan.
8 Aux tag sang kieh pui coh kawn praix pex ka pui Yo-tax;
kix tag sang deeh coh kawn praix pex ka pui See-pa pa ot ka dee singai.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
 
9 Tag sang krai pao ka meung piang haktiex ah nin:
Rien khre tix puing naing heue!
Khrawm ka pui naing heue!
Khrawm ka pui naing hu sivoe ngai heue!
10 bTo thai tag sang raug lih mawh vaig gawn;
si vawg tag sang raug lih mawh pliah.
Viang mawh pui pa iak riang kawx tag sang puing ka naing.
11 Meung pa ot sivie siveng khankix eue,
nied hoek heuec!
Khrawm tix ot ka daux ngawd heue.
 
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix ploi pui naing tix hoek puing ka kix heue!
 
12 Meung ka piang haktiex tag caw rien khre tix mhawm,
hoek ka “ngawd prawng ca”.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang prawng see dee an,
tag sang prawng ca ka meung pa ot sivie siveng pa koe khawm uik.
13 dPhi map kix kied ting lhaong;
aux tag sang kid kix goh,
awm yam vawg ka nghox koi yuh nan.
Aux tag sang gut kix phawng vid,
awm greeik ka plix siiet daux dung plai yuh nan,
tom hoik naog lih plai heue.
 
14 Pui kaing rheng kaing mheen khrawm tix ot ka “ngawd prawng ca”;
singaix prawng ca Simiang Siyiex tag sang lih hoek ka dee an.
15 Singaix,
khix hoik faik khawm uik;
simuing kawx ang lai baox tawx pa riang heue.
Siyiex tag sang tawx buan son ka pui cie tix
16 eKrax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex lih khaing gawng Shi-ang;
nawh ploi lox si num tuh sah lih hoek khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Rao ma haktiex si yaung khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka pui cie tix,
mai mawh dee pa kaux cong ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
 
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin,
Yi-seu-liex eue,
Yam an maix tag tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix.
Aux paung ot ka gawng pa singax singeh tix Shi-ang;
veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang lih mawh veng pa singax singeh,
pui tang ceu tang cad ang lai baox pon kuang ka heue.
 
18 Hoek yam an,
plai khraox buing tix lih khaing piang gawng daux tiag khawm uik;
rawm teeh moi hoik jauh lih khaing gawng iak khawm uik;
rawm klawng Yo-tax ang chix hit leeing ju.
Koe rawm keet tix klawng tag sang jauh lih khaing nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
mai jaux ka ngawd See-thing heue.
 
19 Ai-cix tag sang lih mawh dee riem;
Yi-tong lih mawh daux tiag pa riem.
Mawh kheu hoik sax yuh kix krax pa tuk tiak ka Yo-tax,
hoik kieh nham pui pa ang bax lud jauh.
20-21 Aux tag sang pawk bixf son pui pa laux leeing;
aux ang doh ploi ka pui cao pauh.
Viang mawh nan,
haktiex Yo-tax pui paung ot ka leeing ju,
Yie-lu-sa-leung pui hoik naog ka ku ju.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang paung ot ka gawng Shi-ang heue.
