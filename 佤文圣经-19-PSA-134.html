Hoek tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex khawm uik
134
Siliex Simiang Siyiex khankix eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
can pui pa ot tix yuh kaing jhaw ka daux nyiex buan yam pon sawm khankix,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
2 Pex tag caw tix yaog taix tix rawg buan ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh nawh;
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
3 Kieh Simiang Siyiex cao pa phan pa yuh rao ma haktiex
tawx buan son ka pex khaing Shi-ang heue!
