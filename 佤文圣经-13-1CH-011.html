Ta-wue mawh simiang Yi-seu-liex mai simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 5.1–10)
11
Can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoek ka Ta-wue daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Yix hoik mawh nex nham to kaux paox ceu paox cad maix.
2 Singaix pa hoik luan,
yam kawn mawh Sao-law simiang ex,
maix hoik thung ex kawn pui Yi-seu-liex puing ka naing.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix hoik tawx lox ka maix ah nin,
maix tag sang mawh cao kaing pa liang ka pui cie nawh,
mai mawh simiang ka piang kix.”
3 Can kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka simiang Ta-wue daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng.
Ta-wue yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
kix tom ap nawh mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
awm lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex Sa-mu-eu grox krai heue.
4 aSimiang Ta-wue mai can pui Yi-seu-liex hu puing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung (yam an kok mawh veng Yie-pu-see),
pui haktiex an pa mawh pui Yie-pu-see kawn paung ot dee an.
5 Pui Yie-pu-see krai ang Ta-wue pon laig ka daux veng leeing ju,
viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue pon ka tab kix Shi-ang,
jauh yam an haktiex in tom kok mawh veng Ta-wue.
6 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Pui pa ran pauh ka pui Yie-pu-see sivoe,
tag sang kieh mawh cao giex kaing naing heue!”
Yaw-ya (miex nawh mawh Si-lu-ya) tom ran hu puing sivoe,
mawh kheu nan nawh yaox tix mawh cao giex kaing.
7 Mawh kheu ot Ta-wue ka daux tab,
daux tab in tom kok mawh veng Ta-wue.
8 Ta-wue deeh cox sum ka veng in,
jauh tix sum dee thawm tiex plak lih singaix,
mai Yaw-ya cox sum ka veng an ka dee ang bax hoik khankix.
9 Ta-wue tix ngaix hoik tix ngaix nied tix ting riang,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa kuang ka piang kru naing khawm uik hoik ot tau mai nawh heue.
Pui pa kua cie Ta-wue
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 23.8–39)
10 Plak greem mawh ceu kaux pui pa kied kua pa ot greem Ta-wue.
Kix mai pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix yaog Ta-wue mawh simiang,
awm gum lox pa hoik dawg krai Simiang Siyiex yuh nan.
Kix baox joh jhan ka meung cie Ta-wue kaux cong heue.
11 Ka daux tix kaux mawh ceu pui cub nyiex Ha-keu-maw-ni pa kaux Ya-saw-pang.
Nawh mawh simiang “Pui ting kaing loe kaux”,b
yam puing naing ka daux tix bawg,
hoik pauh pui yum loe yieh kaux ka pliah.
12 “Pui ting kaing loe kaux” ka daux ra kaux mawh pui Ya-heux kawn simex Taw-to pa kaux Yi-li-ya-sa.
13 Ka daux naing Pa-see Ta-min,
nawh tieb tie ka pui Fe-li-see tau mai Ta-wue.
Yam yaog pui Yi-seu-liex tix to,
nawh jiet ot ka daux kaung nghox ca pa lang mu tix dauh,
14 mai thung pui naing tix tieb tie ka daux kaung,
giex dee an kaux cong,
mai pauh pui Fe-li-see yum.
Simiang Siyiex kieh nawh pon pex heue.
15 Yam ot pui Fe-li-see ka ngawd Li-fa-ying,
koe tix ngaix,
pui pa kied kua khankix tix ngoe kaux,
koe loe kaux hu ka Ta-wue ka riang simaox pa dex ka daux dawh rang Ya-tu-lang.
16 Yam an,
Ta-wue ot dee rien ka gawng tix mu;
pui Fe-li-see tix cub hoik pon cam ka Paw-li-haung.
17 Ta-wue braong doe ka haktiex ot tix,
mai ah nin:
“Aux kied simie tix nyaux rhawm dung ot praok diang siviex veng Paw-li-haung heue!”
18 Pui pa kied kua pa koe min an kix loe kaux tom hu laig ka daux bang ot pui Fe-li-see,
mai hoek tix kin rawm dung pa ot ka praok siviex veng Paw-li-haung,
viex hoek tawx Ta-wue nyaux.
Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue ang nyaux,
mai hieh ka rawm,
kieh mawh krax tan pa tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
19 Nawh ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
aux sang nyaux ka rawm in yuh ka mawx!
Phan yuh nin,
awm hoik nyaux aux nham pui pa kied kua an kix pa hu kin rawm ka daux krax pa kha rhawm tix lie!”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ang nyaux.
In hoik mawh kaing pa yuh pui pa kied kua an kix loe kaux heue.
20 Pux Yaw-ya pa kaux Ya-pi-sai mawh simiang “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux.”c
Nawh hoik pauh pui yum ka pliah loe yieh kaux,
mawh kheu nan ka daux “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux” nawh tom ao min.
21 Nawh mawh ka daux “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux” pa blawn ao min khaing paox tix tix kaux,
mai lih mawh simiang kix,
viang mawh nan,
kawn ang nyang ao min awm “pui ting kaing loe kaux” heue.
22 Pui Cia-shiiex kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya mawh pui pa kied kua pa ao min tang kaux tix kaux.
Nawh hoik koe kaing pa yuh pa ao min tom nie,
khawm pa hoik pauh nawh ka pui naing Maw-ya pa kied kua ra kaux.
Nawh hoik laig ka daux ox yam lih rha,
mai pauh ka sivai sang si yum tix mu.
23 Nawh baox pon pauh ka pui Ai-cix pa kied ting tix kaux;
pui an krax lhaong koe ra yieh kongfeeng haoh,
mai nawh viex pliah lang ka daux taix tix.
Pi-na-ya lauh nawh yum ka khaox nguh,
teei pliah pa koe daux taix nawh,
mai klauh nawh yum ka pliah an.
24 In kix hoik mawh kaing pa ao min pa yuh Pi-na-ya;
nawh mawh ka daux “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux”d tix kaux,
25 mai mawh pui pa ao min tix kaux,
viang mawh nan,
krax pa ao min nawh kawn ang nyang baox lheu khaing “pui ting loe kaux” an.
Ta-wue tawx nawh mawh cao giex kaing lien heue.
