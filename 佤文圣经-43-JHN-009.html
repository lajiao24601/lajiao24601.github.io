Pai pui dug ngai tom yam lih khaing daux vaig moi
9
Ye-sux yaox pui pa dug ngai tom yam lih khaing daux vaig tix kaux.
2 Pui gau nawh chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
pui in hoik dug ngai tom yam lih khaing daux vaig,
hoik mawh phi map pui mawx pa kieh nawh yuh nan lie?
Hoik mawh phi map nawh,
phan ang mawh nan,
mawh phi map miex keeing nawh lie?”
3 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Nawh dug ngai ang mawh kheu phi map to kaux nawh mai miex keeing nawh,
mai hoik mawh son sang jhi kaing pa yuh Siyiex lih ka to kaux nawh.
4 Yam pon singaix,
pex tag caw tix nied yuh kaing cie kaw pa ploi aux hoek an;
yam hoik faik praix pon bo,
tag ang lai koe pui pa pon yuh ka kaing.
5 aYam ot aux ka piang haktiex,
aux tag mawh pa riang haktiex heue.”
6 Khaix hoik krai ka lox in,
Ye-sux baig rawm mia tix piang tiex,
mai teei rawm mia tix mai tiex lan kraix ka ngai pui dug an,
7 khaix an ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hu khoek ka rawm dung Si-law-yax heue.”
(Dee bleeh Si-law-yax mawh “pa ploi hu.”)
Nawh tom hu khoek,
yam deeh hoek nawh,
tag chix yaox heue.
8 Paox plawg paox nyawm nawh mai pui pa rien yaox yam caung nawh jheeng jheeng ah nin:
“Pui in ang mawh pui pa hoik ngawm tix caung priex dee in lie?”
9 Tix blah ah nin:
“Mawh nawh”;
tix blah ah nin:
“Ang mawh nawh,
mawh pui pa awm nawh khaing heue.”
Pui an ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui an heue.”
10 Kix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Ngai maix hoik riang yuh ka mawx lie?”
11 Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Pui pa kaux Ye-sux kraix tiex lan ka ngai aux,
mai nawh ah nin:
‘Maix hu khoek ka rawm dung Si-law-yax heue.’
Aux hu khoek kah,
ngai aux tom riang heue.”
12 Kix chawk ah nin:
“Pui an ot dee mawx lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng heue.”
Pui Fa-li-saix si kaw grawng pui pa dug ngai an
13 Kix viex pui pa dug ngai an hoek ka pui Fa-li-saix.
14 Singaix pa kraix Ye-sux tiex lan ka ngai pui an mawh singaix tuah.
15 Pui Fa-li-saix baox chawk tix yaox ka grawng pa chix yaox pui an yuh ka mawx.
Nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Nawh teei tiex lan kraix ka ngai aux,
aux hu khoek kah,
aux tom chix yaox heue.”
16 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah ah nin:
“Pui pa yuh kaing yuh nin ang doh mawh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an,
mawh kheu ang nawh priex tix hawt ka been singaix tuah heue.”
Baox koe pui tang kaux tix blah ah nin:
“Pui pa koe phi map sang pon chix yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh nin yuh ka mawx lie?”
Kix simah paox tix kheu grawng in heue.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Fa-li-saix baox chawk pui an ah nin:
“Kheu hoik paoh nawh ngai maix riang,
maix krai hoik mawh nawh pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Nawh hoik mawh cao jhai tix kaux heue.”
18 Simiang pui Yo-thaix ang yum rhawm grawng hoik mawh pui an pui dug sivoe,
yam in nawh hoik chix yaox;
kix krox jun miex keeing nawh hoek,
19 mai chawk ah nin:
“Pui in hoik mawh kawn simex maix lie?
Ang pex hoik krai mawh nawh pui dug ngai tom yam lih khaing daux vaig lie?
Phan mawh nan,
yam in yuh ka mawx nawh chix yaox lie?”
20 Miex keeing nawh pawk ah nin:
“Nawh hoik mawh kawn simex yix,
mai hoik dug ngai tom yam lih khaing daux vaig,
yix hoik tawng kah.
21 Phan mawh grawng pa chix yaox nawh yam in yuh ka mawx,
mawh mawx pa paoh ka ngai nawh,
yix ang tawng.
Nawh hoik mawh pui ting,
pex hu chawk ka nawh,
kieh nawh krai ka pex heue!”
22 Miex keeing pui an krai yuh nin hoik mawh kheu lhat kix ka simiang pui Yo-thaix;
yam an kix hoik kum kawng paox tix,
phan koe pui pa krai mawh Ye-sux Khrit,
tag sang grheu nawh lih khaing nyiex sidiex.
23 Kheu nan,
miex keeing nawh tom pawk ah nin:
“Nawh hoik mawh pui ting,
pex hu chawk nawh heue!”
24 Kix baox kok pui pa dug ngai an tom yam lih khaing daux vaig hoek,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix krai lox tete ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex heue!
Yix hoik tawng Ye-sux mawh pui phi map heue.”
25 Pui an pawk ah nin:
“Mawh ang nawh mawh pui phi map aux ang tawng;
aux tawng ngie grawng kaing tix ceu khaing yuh nin:
sivoe an,
aux mawh pui dug ngai,
yam in aux hoik chix yaox heue.”
26 Kix chawk ah nin:
“Yuh nawh kaing ceu pa tix son maix lie?
Hoik paoh nawh ngai maix riang yuh ka mawx lie?”
27 Pui an pawk ah nin:
“Aux hoik krai ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang cu tix nghiet.
Yam in yuh ka mawx pex tag sang nghiet ka lie?
Pex kawx khex keud tix sang yuh pui gau nawh lie?”
28 Kix jhax ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh pui gau pui an;
yix hoik mawh pui gau Maw-si.
29 Yix hoik tawng Siyiex si kah ka Maw-si;
phan mawh pui an yix ang tawng,
mawh kaoh nawh khaing dee mawx heue!”
30 Pui an pawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
ex kied daox deng ka tete.
Nawh hoik paoh ka ngai aux,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang tawng mawh kaoh nawh khaing dee mawx!
31 Yix hoik tawng Siyiex ang nghiet lox pa khiox khaw pui phi map;
nawh nghiet ngie ka pa pui pa krub ka nawh,
mai pa hawt tix yuh ka alo nawh khankix.
32 Tom yam jauh tix phan yuh haktiex,
ang nyang sax mhong pui pa hoik sax paoh ka ngai pui dug tom yam lih khaing daux vaig.
33 Lheu khaing mawh nawh pa kaoh hoek khaing Siyiex dee an,
mai ang pon yuh ceu tid tix heue.”
34 Kix ne raw ka pui an ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh pui pa lih keh khaing daux phi map in,
mai gah sang sidiex gau ka yix heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix grheu pui an lih hu khaing daux nyiex sidiex heue.
Pa dug ka daux khuan
35 Ye-sux hoik mhong grheu kix pui an lih hu khaing nyiex sidiex.
Khaix an Ye-sux tom sawg ka pui an,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Mawh yum rhawm maix ka kawn kawn pui lie?”
36 Pui an pawk ah nin:
“Sara,
khiox khaw maix krai aux mhong mawh nawh pui mawx,
son sang yum rhawm aux ka nawh heue!”
37 Ye-sux krai ka pui an ah nin:
“Maix hoik yaox ka nawh,
yam in pa si kah tau mai maix mawh nawh heue.”
38 Pui an ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux yum rhawm ka heue!”
Nawh tom krub ka Ye-sux heue.
39 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kaing ting aux hoek ka piang haktiex mawh son sang hoek tix prawng ca,
mai kieh pui pa dug ngai chix yaox;
pui pa chix yaox tieh tix dug ngai heue.”
40 Pui Fa-li-saix pa ot dee an tix blah mhong ka lox in,
kix tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Khex sang een maix yix mawh pui pa dug ngai lie?”
41 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Phan pex mawh pui dug,
pex ang koe phi map;
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik ah ‘yix hoik chix yaox tix’,
kheu nan pex hoik ca kawn koe phi map heue.”
