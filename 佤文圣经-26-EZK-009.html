Yie-lu-sa-leung kham krax ciyang
9
Khaix an,
aux mhong Siyiex kok yo lox pa ting si num ah nin:
“Pex pa hoek tix ciyang ka veng ting in eue,
viex kraung naing pex hoek heue!”
2 Dum mawx dum an koe pui liah kaux viex ka kraung naing tix,
mai lih hoek khaing siviex biang brawk plak praix nyiex buan ka plak laong,
ka daux kix tix kaux ceep ka kraung man keei sum pa viex ka gawng meug.
Kix hoek khawm uik,
mai cung ka praok khu tawng heue.
3 Min plak ngai Siyiex Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing Ci-lu-paw,
mai gah tix hu ka dee laig ka diang siviex nyiex buan;
min plak ngai in caw tix sang kaog ot ka piang riang Ci-lu-paw.
Simiang Siyiex kok yo pui pa ceep ka kraung man keei sum an hoek,
krai ka nawh ah nin:
4 a“Maix hu,
hu tauh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik,
phan maix si pup ka pui pa suk sag rhawm khankix,
kheu kaing pa caox caig pa hoik lih tix mawh ka daux veng,
tag caw tix tiiem mhai koe ka dong re kix heue.”
5 Aux baox mhong Siyiex krai ka pui tang kaux ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix hawt khaix nawh,
hu tauh ka daux veng khawm uik,
tawx taix tix pauh pui yum,
paw vi ploi tix kaux kawx,
paw vi caox rhawm kah.
6 Mawh kuad song ang ah,
pui nhum ju ang ah,
bun krih ang ah,
bun miex ang ah,
mai kawn ngie ang ah,
tag caw tix pauh yum khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix pauh ka pui pa koe mhai ka dong re.
Yam in jauh dee in,
jauh tix tawx taix tix ka nyiex pa singax aux heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom tawx taix tix pauh ka kuad song pa cung ka daux nyiex buan khankix heue.
7 Siyiex krai ka kix ah nin:
“Pum to kaux yum naog ot ka biang brawk nyiex buan,
kieh seeing yuh nyiex buan,
yam in jauh tix yuh heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom jauh tix pauh ka pui pa ot ka daux veng heue.
8 Yam yuh kix krax pa pauh pui ka daux veng,
aux ot daux nyiex buan nong nong.
Aux gup tix piang tiex kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
rug sang klup maix krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik,
mai yuh pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix laux leeing khawm uik lie?”
9 Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Phi map pui Yi-seu-liex mai phi map pui Yo-tax hoik kied ting tete.
Ka piang meung khawm uik hoik mawh see cawng krax pa pauh pui yum;
Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik naog ka kaing ceu pa ang rauh ang see.
Kix ah nin,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik tik vud ka meung haktiex kix,
mai ang lai kia ngien ka kix.
10 Viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn ang pluat ploi ka kix,
ang caox rhawm ka kix.
Kix hoik yuh ka mawx ka pui tang kaux,
mai aux tag sang hawt tix yuh nan ka kix nang heue.”
11 Khaix an,
pui pa ceep ka kraung man keei sum an viex gawng meug deeh hoek,
mai krai ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawx maix yuh nan hoik khawm uik heue.”
