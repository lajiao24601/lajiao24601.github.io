5
Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
ih rhawm rhi khri mau paox tix heue!
Pui hoik lai lawm ka ex heue!
Pui koeng hoik si mang ka jheeh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Cao pa giex kaing pa hoek khaing Paw-li-haung
2 aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Paw-li-haung pa ot haktiex Yi-fa-tha eue,
ka daux veng Yo-tax khankix,
maix hoik mawh veng iak tix mu.
Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang laug simiang lih khaing maix dee an tix kaux son Yi-seu-liex;
pui nyiex ma nawh tag sang si kaw hoek tom dix dix heue.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ap tix tawx pui cie tix ka pui pa koeng,
tom hoik dex sang keh bun miex an kawn simex.
Khaix an,
paox ceu paox cad nawh tag sang deeh hoek khaing haktiex pa vuk vied vuk viang tix,
mai deeh rom tix tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix.
4 Yam lih hoek nawh,
tag sang awm cao liang yung pa kia ngien ka phung yung yuh nan;
nawh tag sang teei pa ting riang pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex,
teei siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex nawh giex kaing ka piang pui cie tix.
Pui cie nawh tag sang pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem,
mawh kheu kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik hoik doh keud grawng pa ting pa lhaong nawh;
5 Nawh tag sang viex pa nhim lih hoek heue.
Pa taoh mai pa ciyang
Yam teei pui Ya-sux ka haktiex ex, yieh laux ka mee pa kuan ex, ex tag sang ploi simiang naing pa blawn ting riang yuh naing ka kix.
6 cSimiang naing in kix tag sang teei riang pui naing pon kuang ka Ya-sux,
pa mawh haktiex Ling-lu.
Yam puing pui Ya-sux ka meung haktiex ex,
kixb tag sang taoh ka ex heue.
7 Ka daux kawn pui piang haktiex,
pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix tag sang awm rawm si mawg pa ploi Simiang Siyiex lih yuh nan,
awm lhiex pa caux leet ka khaox ox rib riem yuh nan.
Kix hoik een rhawm tix ka Siyiex,
mai ang een ka kawn pui.
8 Ka daux meung piang haktiex khawm uik,
pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix tag sang awm sivai sang si pa kua pa sawg tix ih kug ka daux nong khaox yuh nan,
awm kawn sivai sang si pa preeng tix laig daux bang liang,
yuh phung yung laux leeing yuh nan,
pui tix kaux ang pon tieb tie.
9 Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang pon kuang ka pui koeng khankix,
mai yuh kix laux leeing heue.
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yam an,
aux tag sang teei ka brung naing pex hu,
mai yuh laux leeing ka ling law naing pex.
11 Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka tab veng pa koe daux haktiex pex,
mai yieh ka can veng pa koe pex khawm uik.
12 Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka pui pa yuh si meed sat cie pex,
mai ang deuh ka pui pa chix si pawk koe tix kaux kawx.
13 Aux tag sang cot ka riang krub pex,
mai ka rong simaox pex ghrup;
pex ang pon tix baox krub ka ceu pa teei pui taix tix yuh kah.
14 Aux tag sang yieh ka riang siyiex bun pa koe daux haktiex pex,
mai yuh ka veng pex laux leeing.
15 Aux tag sang pawk bix ka meung piang haktiex pa ang nghiet ka aux ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix heue.”
