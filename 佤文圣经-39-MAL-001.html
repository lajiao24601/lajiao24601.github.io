Lai Ma-la-ci
1
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex Ma-la-ci krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Krax muh Simiang Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex
2 abSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka pui cie tix:
“Aux hoik muh ka pex jheeng jheeng.”
Viang mawh nan,
kix pawk ah nin:
“Sang jhi maix grawng muh tix ka yix yuh ka mawx lie?”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yi-sao mai Ya-keux mawh pux aik,
viang mawh nan aux muh ka Ya-keux,
3 mai koeng ka Yi-sao.
Aux hoik yuh laux leeing ka gawng lua cie Yi-sao,
mai teei tix tawx haktiex cie nawh ka sox tiag pa koe daux tiag heue.”
4 Phan pui Yi-tong pa mawh doe ceu cad Yi-sao ah nin:
“Veng ot ex hoik laux leeing,
viang mawh nan,
ex tag sang deeh cox sum kah”;
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing tag sang pawk ah nin:
“Kieh kix deeh cox sum ka heue,
aux tag sang baox yuh laux leeing.
Kawn pui tag sang kok kix mawh ‘meung phi map’ mai ‘pui pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka leeing ju’ heue.”
5 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yaox kaing in ka ngai tix sidawng;
kix tag sang ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik pon nang min plak ngai ka daux haktiex pa ang mawh cie pui Yi-seu-liex heue!”
Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka cao giex kaing li
6 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai ka cao giex kaing li khankix ah nin:
“Kawn simex yung yi ka keeing tix;
siliex yung yi ka simiang tix.
Aux mawh keeing pex,
yuh ka mawx pex lai ang yung yi ka aux lie?
Aux mawh simiang pex,
yuh ka mawx pex lai ang lhat graung yung yi ka aux lie?
Pex hoik map nai ka aux,
mai kawn chawk ah nin:
‘Map nai yix maix yuh ka mawx lie?’
7 Pex hoik tan lu priex preem pa seeing brai ka piang khu dee tan aux,
mai kawn chawk ah nin:
‘Seeing brai yix yuh ka mawx ka maix lie?’
Aux krai ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik map nai pex ka khu dee tan aux heue!
8 cPex hoik teei khawng brie pa dug ngai,
pa chawh caong,
pa koe jhawm saux hoek tix tan lu ka aux;
in kawn ang mawh kaing pa caox pa caig lie?
Pex cheu tix tawx to sat pa yuh nin ka simiang pex,
khaw ang nawh khaw rhawm kah,
cu ang nawh cu tix rieb ka ceu pa tawx pex lie?”
9 Yam in,
pex pa mawh caox giex kaing li in kix,
hu khiox khaw Siyiex tawx buan ka pex heue!
Nawh ang doh cu tix nghiet ka lox rawg pex buan;
in hoik mawh pex pa lud.
10 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Aux kied simie koe pui tix kaux ka daux pex pa nyeep ka siviex nyiex buan,
son ang sang kieh pex tok ngu pa ang koe ceu ka piang khu dee tan cie aux.
Aux ang khaw rhawm ka pex,
mai ang rieb ka khawng tan pa tan lu pex ka aux.
11 Pui ka piang haktiex ku caong daog hoik nab yam yung yi ka aux khawm uik,
hoik gleeing nyawng bhawng ka aux ku dauh ku dee,
mai hoik tan lu ka khawng tan pa singax singeh.
Kix hoik nab yam yung yi ka aux khawm uik heue!
12 Viang mawh nan,
pex salao ka aux,
mai ah khu dee tan aux seeing brai;
pex tan lu ka priex preem pa hoik seeing tix kah.
13 “Pex ah nin:
‘Kaing in kix hoik mawh pa ang ex rhawm yaox tete heue!’
Yuh ka mawx pex lai map nyia ka aux.
Pex hoik teei to sat pa briex tix,
pa chawh caong,
pa koe jhawm saux tan lu ka aux.
Aux sang chix rieb ka khawng tan pa yuh nin lie?
14 Pui pa ap pong tix sang tan yung mhaing pa mhawm ka aux ka daux phung yung,
viang mawh nan,
mai tan pa laux meung pa pie,
tag pon krax pa jhax lawg.
Kheu mawh aux simiang ting,
kawn pui meung piang haktiex hoik lhat graung yung yi ka aux khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
