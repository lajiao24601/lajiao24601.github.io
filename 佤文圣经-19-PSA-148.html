Kok yo rao ma khawm uik rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex
148
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex!
 
Pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex khaing piang rao ma;
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw nawh ka rao ma dee lhaong.
2 Kawn cau Siyiex khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
kru naing rao ma eue,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
 
3 Singaix eue,
khix eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
pa jhan riang simuing eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
4 Rao ma pa koe ka piang rao ma khankix eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
Rawm pa koe ka piang rao ma khankix eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
5 Kieh ku ceu khawm uik rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Yam tawx nawh lox tix,
mai ku ceu khawm uik hoik lih tix mawh,
6 ceu mawx ceu an hoik tang koe dee ot,
mai ang chix tieh loh tix leeing ju;
gum lox tawx nawh pui ang pon lieh lheu ka heuea.
 
7-8 To sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui ka eue,
rawm pa kied raux eue,
pu plak praix,
pre,
rha mai bhawm rawm eue,
bhaung ting pa hawt tix phlu awm gum lox tawx eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex khaing ka piang haktiex heue.
 
9 Gawng ting gawng iak eue,
khaox plix,
nong khaox eue,
10 mai to tiag to tu,
khawng eei brie liang khankix eue,
viag vua,
sim saik khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
11 Simiang ka piang haktiex eue,
kawn pui ka piang haktiex eue,
simiang eue,
simiang cao giex kaing khawm uik eue,
12 simex pui nhum ju heue,
bun pa nhum ju eue,
kuad song eue,
kawn nyawm eue,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
13 Kieh kix rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
Mawh ngie ceu kaux nawh pa yaog yaw yung yi pui kah;
min plak ngai nawh kawn lheu ting khaing rao ma haktiex.
14 Nawh kieh pui cie tix haok huan ting riang,
mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex
pa mawh can pui pa muh nawh ka khankix,
hoik rhax laix yaog yaw ka nawh khawm uik heue.
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
