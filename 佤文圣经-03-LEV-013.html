Been son saux si pawg
13
Simiang Siyiex krai grawng lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng.
2 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux koe krax saux uah ang ah,
saux dao ang ah,
saux muad saux leem ka piang hak nawh ang ah,
mai jhawm saux in tee chix tieh tix lih mawh saux si pawg,
caw tix viex nawh hoek ka cao giex kaing li pa mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng dee an.
3 Cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak khaix saux nawh;
phan hak khaix saux an lih tix paing,
khaix saux an raux laig greem hak,
an mawh jhawm saux si pawg,
cao giex kaing li tag sang krai pao pui an hoik mawh pa ang singax ka krax been.
4 Phan dee saux pui an mawh pa paing,
viang mawh nan,
ang raux laig greem hak,
mai hauk kawx ang paing,
cao giex kaing li tag sang khan nawh tang ot been aliah singaix.
5 Ka daux aliah singaix,
cao giex kaing li tag sang baox lam lia ka nawh,
phan khaix saux an ang lai nieb caox,
mai ang lai tauh hu,
cao giex kaing li tag baox deeh kieh nawh tang ot been aliah singaix.
6 Ka daux aliah singaix,
cao giex kaing li tag deeh baox lam lia nawh;
phan khaix saux an hoik grai,
mai ang lai tauh hu,
nawh tom sang krai pao pui an hoik mawh pa singax singeh;
jhawm saux nawh mawh ngie dao khaing.
Pui an caw si daux kraung ceep tix.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom tag singax singeh ka daux been heue.
7 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik lam lia cao giex kaing li nawh,
khaix hoik krai pao nawh hoik singax singeh ka daux been,
phan khaix saux an deeh tauh hu,
nawh tag caw tix deeh hoek ka cao giex kaing li dee an.
8 Cao giex kaing li tag caw tix dee baox cox jhak ka nawh;
phan hoik yaox grawng khaix saux an tauh hu,
tag sang krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax ka daux been.
In mawh jhawm saux si pawg heue.
9 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa koe jhawm saux si pawg,
tag caw tix viex nawh hoek ka cao giex kaing li dee an;
10 mai cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak ka nawh.
Phan khaix saux hak an lih tix paing,
mai hauk kawx lih tix paing,
leem lih khaing,
11 in hoik mawh jhawm saux si pawg pa nhim hu.
Cao giex kaing li tag sang krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax singeh,
viang mawh nan,
ang siao lai khan nawh tang ot,
kheu hoik mawh nawh pa ang singax singeh.
12 Phan jhawm saux si pawg an hoik tauh hu ka khawm gaw kaux,
tauh khawm to kaux,
jauh ka kaing hoek ka caong,
13 cao giex kaing li tag caw tix baox dee jhak ka nawh.
Phan yaox khawm gaw kaux nawh hoik mawh jhawm saux si pawg tete,
tag caw tix krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been;
phan hak khawm gaw kaux nawh hoik lih tix paing khawm uik,
nawh tag sawn tix mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been heue.
14 Phan hoik yaox pa bih dee saux,
nawh tag sawn tix mawh pa ang singax.
15 Cao giex kaing li tag sang baox dee cox jhak ka nawh;
phan dee saux an hoik bih,
mai krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax.
Phan khaix saux an mawh jhawm saux si pawg,
pui an tag mawh pa ang singax singeh.
16 Viang mawh nan,
phan khaix saux hoik moi,
mai lih tix paing,
nawh tag sang deeh hoek ka cao giex kaing li dee an;
17 cao giex kaing li tag baox cox jhak ka nawh.
Phan khaix saux an hoik lih tix mawh pa paing tete,
nawh tag mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been;
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh heue.
18 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa koe jhawm saux muad ka hak,
phan hoik moi,
19 khaix an,
khaix saux prim an deeh uah,
mai lih paing,
mawh pa paing preeh raoh,
nawh tag caw tix hu ka cao giex kaing li;
20 cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak ka nawh.
Phan yaox dee saux an hoik laig daux hak,
mai hauk dee saux an lih tix paing,
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax;
an mawh jhawm saux muad pa lih tix mawh jhawm saux si pawg.
21 Yam jhak cao giex kaing li kah,
phan ang yaox grawng pa lih hauk tix paing ka dee saux,
khaix saux an kawx ang bax laig daux hak,
mawh ngie tang siyox rawm siang khaing,
cao giex kaing li tag khan nawh tang ot been aliah singaix.
22 Phan khaix saux an hoik tauh hu,
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax;
in hoik mawh jhawm saux si pawg.
23 Phan khaix saux an ang ot caox,
mai ang bax tauh hu tang dauh,
an mawh ngie saux muad khaing,
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been heue.
24 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa hax ka ngu,
khaix hax an lih tix paing,
phan ang mawh nan,
lih tix raoh paing preeh,
25 cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak ka nawh.
Phan hauk ka dee saux an tieh tix mawh pa paing,
mai dee saux an tauh laig daux hak,
in mawh saux si pawg pa lih khaing khaix saux hax ngu,
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax.
26 Phan hauk khaix dee saux ang baox tieh tix lih paing,
mai dee saux an ang baox rao tix laig daux hak,
mawh ngie rawm siang pa tang siyox khaing,
cao giex kaing li tag sang khan nawh tang ot been aliah singaix.
27 Yam hoik hoek ka daux aliah singaix,
cao giex kaing li tag deeh baox jhak ka nawh.
Phan dee saux an hoik tauh hu,
an mawh jhawm saux si pawg,
cao giex kaing li tag sang krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax.
28 Phan dee saux an ang ot caox,
ang nyang tauh hu,
mawh ngie rawm siang pa tang siyox khaing,
in ang mawh jhawm saux si pawg.
Cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been,
kheu an mawh ngie khaix rhoi saux pa hax ngu khaing heue.
29 Simex ang ah,
bun ang ah,
phan khaix saux koe ka piang kaing nawh,
mai ka si bax nawh,
30 cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak kah.
Phan dee saux an tauh tix laig ka greem hak,
hauk kaing ka dee saux an mai hauk jheeh tieh tix lih lheung,
mai kied rhi,
in mawh jhawm saux si pawg,
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao ka pui an mawh pa ang singax.
31 Cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak ka nawh;
phan yaox dee saux an ang tie bax tauh tix laig greem hak,
viang mawh nan,
ang nyang bax vhah rhawm ka hauk kaing mai hauk jheeh,
cao giex kaing li tag sang khan nawh tang ot been aliah singaix.
32 Yam hoik hoek ka daux aliah singaix,
cao giex kaing li tag sang baox deeh jhak ka dee saux an.
Phan dee saux an ang nyang tauh hu,
hauk kaing dee saux,
phan ang mawh nan,
hauk jheeh ka dee saux ang nyang bax tieh tix lih lheung,
dee saux ang nyang baox tauh tix laig greem hak,
33 pui in tag caw tix khlao ka hauk kaing tix,
mai ka hauk jheeh tix,
vung viang ka dee saux.
Khaix an,
cao giex kaing li tag sang baox khan nawh tang ot been aliah singaix.
34 Yam hoik hoek daux aliah singaix,
cao giex kaing li tag deeh baox jhak ka dee saux an;
phan dee saux an ang nyang tauh hu,
ang nyang bax tauh tix laig ka greem hak,
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been.
Nawh tag sang si daux kraung tix,
yuh nin,
nawh tom tag singax singeh.
35 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik singax singeh,
phan dee saux an deeh tauh hu,
36 cao giex kaing li tag caw tix deeh baox jhak ka nawh.
Phan dee saux an tauh hu tete,
ang lai siao sawg ka hauk kaing mai hauk jheeh pa lheung,
nawh hoik sidawng mawh pa ang singax singeh heue.
37 Phan hoik tawng dee saux an ang lai baox tauh hu,
hauk jheeh kawx hoik kuah,
dee saux an tom tag sawn tix moi;
cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been heue.
38 Simex ang ah,
bun ang ah,
phan ka piang hak nawh preeh paing,
39 cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak ka nawh.
Phan dee preeh an paing pau,
an mawh ngie hak pa lih mawh dao;
pui an mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been heue.
40-41 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa greeig ka hauk kaing,
mawh plak ngai kaing ang ah,
plak kreue kaing ang ah,
nawh tag mawh pa singax singeh.
42 Viang mawh nan,
phan dee ghle kaing nawh lih koe pa raoh uah mai pa paing,
in hoik mawh jhawm saux si pawg.
43 Cao giex kaing li tag sang jhak ka nawh;
phan dee ghle kaing nawh rub raoh uah mai paing tete,
44 cao giex kaing li tag krai pao nawh mawh pa ang singax singeh,
mawh kheu kaing nawh hoik mawh jhawm saux si pawg heue.
45 Pui pa saux si pawg an,
tag caw tix ceep ka kraung pa griah,
mai bhla hauk kaing tix,
kieh kheem pak ngai tix plak greem,
kok yo tix ah nin:
“Ang singax singeh,
ang singax singeh!”
46 Glang ang jhawm saux si pawg nyang moi,
nawh tag mawh pa ang singax singeh;
nawh tag sang ot ka plak praix nyiex bang,
tang khan tix khaing pui tang kaux khankix heue.
Been pa moh
47-48 Mawh kraung man hauk yung ang ah,
kraung man keei sum ang ah,
mawh keei pa taing ang ah,
maox pa sang taing ang ah,
mawh hak ang ah,
kraung pa yuh ka hak ang ah,
phan moha,
49 pa moh in phan klai ang ah,
raoh ang ah,
mawh pa moh tix tauh hu ang ah,
tag caw tix tawx cao giex kaing li jhak.
50 Cao giex kaing li tag jhak ka mhawm mhawm,
khan nawh tang ot been aliah singaix.
51 Yam hoek daux aliah singaix,
tag deeh baox jhak kah.
Phan pa moh in hoik tauh hu,
mai ceu in tag mawh pa ang singax singeh.
52 Cao giex kaing li tag sang gleeing kah,
kheu mawh an moh pa chix tauh hu,
tag caw tix teei ngu gleeing ka heue.
53 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik jhak cao giex kaing li kah,
phan hoik yaox grawng ang pa moh in lai tauh hu,
54 nawh tag caw tix nhie pui si ghrah ka ceu an,
baox khan tang ot been aliah singaix.
55 Khaix an cao giex kaing li tag deeh baox jhak kah;
phan pa moh in ang lai tauh hu,
ang lai chix tieh loh tix,
ceu in kawn sawn tix mawh pa ang singax singeh.
Pa moh in mawh ka plak daux ang ah,
plak praix ang ah,
ceu an tag sang teei ngu gleeing kah.
56 Viang mawh nan,
yam deeh baox jhak cao giex kaing li ka an,
phan yaox hoik grai pa moh an,
tag caw tix riah lih tix blah.
57 Khaix an,
phan pa moh an kawn koe,
kawn deeh baox tauh hu,
cao kraung an tag caw tix teei ngu gleeing ceu an kah.
58 Phan nawh si ghrah ka ceu an,
pa moh an kawx grai,
nawh caw tix baox si ghrah tix bawg,
yuh nin,
ceu an tag mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been heue.
59 In plak piang mawh grawng lox tat jhawm prawng pa moh kraung ceep.
Viang mawh sibex hauk yung ang ah,
kraung man keei sum ang ah,
mawh keei pa taing ang ah,
maox pa sang taing ang ah,
kraung pa yuh ka hak ang ah,
can pa hoik moh khawm uik,
tag sang hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng in prawng dut khawm uik,
son ka pa singax singeh mai pa ang singax heue.
