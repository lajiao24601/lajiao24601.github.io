Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ot ka Paw-cing
2
Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex kaoh khaing Cie-cia mai hoek ka Paw-cing,
ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Aux hoik thung pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
mai hoik viex pex hoek ka haktiex pa hoik ap aux tix tawx ka tax du pex.
Aux hoik krai ka glang ka an,
aux ang doh yieh tix tik ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tix tau mai pex.
2 aPex tag ang pon tix yuh paox pie sijax mukheu tix ceu kawx tau mai pui haktiex an.
Pex yieh ka khu dee tan kix.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang bax hawt tix yuh ka lox aux,
yuh ka kaing ceu pa ang aux cu pex yuh khankix.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
yam pheet pex tix hu sivoe ngai,
aux tag ang grheu ka pui haktiex an lih hu.
Kix tag sang mawh pui pa koeng ka pexb;
siyiex kix tag sang nyaw glaw pex laig ka daux rub heue.”
4 Khaix hoik krai kawn cau Simiang Siyiex lox in kix,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tom yiiem tom ting heue.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
haktiex an tom kok mawh Paw-cingc.
Kix yuh krax tan ka Simiang Siyiex dee an heue.
Grawng yum Yaw-su-yax
6 Yaw-su-yax tawx lox nhie pui Yi-seu-liex khankix prung prang tix tang hu,
kix tom tang cax ka nyum haktiex pa hoik guah kix ku kaux pon.
7 Yam kawn im Yaw-su-yax ka piang haktiex,
pui Yi-seu-liex yuh kaing jhaw krub ka Simiang Siyiex;
khaix hoik yum nawh,
yam kawn im ju can kuad song khankix pa hoik yaox ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh Simiang Siyiex son pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
pui Yi-seu-liex kawn ca yuh kaing jhaw krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
8 Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng pa mawh siliex Simiang Siyiex yum ka hoek tix tix yieh kao num;
9 dkix si paung nawh ka daux haktiex cie nawh,
ka plak laong gawng Cia-seu gawng haktiex Yi-fa-liien Thing-na Si-la.
10 Khaix hoik yum pui baux ju an,
baux ju phaox khaix ang lai tawng ka Simiang Siyiex mai khawm kaing ceu pa hoik yuh nawh son pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex
11 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
jauh tix yuh krax krub ka Pa-lix.
12 Kix hoik tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix,
pa hoik viex kix lih khaing Ai-cix,
hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
pa mawh siyiex ceu pui meung pa koe hawt vung viang kix,
cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm song rhi.
13 Kix tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex,
hu krub ka Pa-lix,
mai Ya-see-tha-lux.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
krax song rhawm song rhi Simiang Siyiex tom lih hoek ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ap kix ka daux tawm taix pui pa tud pa rhim,
tawx kix ka pui pa koeng pa ot hawt vung viang kix,
kieh kix ang lai pon tieb tie ka pui pa koeng.
15 Yam yuh kix ka naing miex bawg ang ah,
Simiang Siyiex hoik hawt awm lox pa tiex tu tix tieb tie ka kix.
Kix kham ka krax kha rhawm pa kin heue.
16 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tawx cao prawng see son pui Yi-seu-liex lih yuh cao pa thung son kix,
son sang taoh kix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix pui tud pui rhim khankix.
17 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang cu nghiet ka cao prawng see,
phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux.
Tax du kix hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
kix baux ju pa khraox in ang hawt tix yuh.
18 Yam tawx Simiang Siyiex cao prawng see lih ka son kix,
Simiang Siyiex tag ot tau mai cao prawng see an,
yam kawn im ju cao prawng see an,
mai nawh tag taoh kawn pui luat luan khaing pui pa koeng.
Simiang Siyiex caox rhawm ka kix,
kheu kha kix rhawm tix mai lox yiiem kheu krax pa gut kheem,
19 viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik yum cao prawng see,
kawn pui deeh tieh tix awm been prim,
mai yuh pa caox pa caig,
kawn nie lang teung khaing singaix pa hoik luan.
Kix hu yuh kaing jhaw siyiex tang kaux,
krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
ang pon tik ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix.
20 Kheu nan,
krax pa song rhawm song rhi Simiang Siyiex hoek ka piang kix,
ah nin:
“Pui in kix hoik lieh lheu ka sijax mukheu pa tawm aux tax du kix priex tix hawt,
ang cu nghiet ka aux,
21 aux kawx tag ang lai grheu ka pui pa hoik prah ot ka daux haktiex in hu khaix hoik yum Yaw-su-yax.
22 Aux sang kieh kix mawh pa cheuk tix yaox ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
kuan tix jhak ka kix,
sang awm ang kix awm tax du kix pa hoik priex tix hawt ka daux krax aux.”
23 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik cu tix prah ka ceu pui an kix ot ka daux haktiex in,
ang bax ap kix ka daux tawm taix Yaw-su-yax,
khaix hoik yum Yaw-su-yax kawx,
ang bax khran grheu kix lih hu heue.
