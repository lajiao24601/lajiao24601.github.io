19
Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
2 Yuh ka mawx pex lai ang chix naox tix kieh aux laux rhawm laux rhi,
teei lox tuk tiak aux ang jud ang jai?
3 Pex ang chix naox map nai ka aux;
pex yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka aux ang pex tawng tix kaig kah.
4 Sawn mawh aux pa hoik lud kawx,
krax pa lud loe aux mawh to kaux cao aux pa klawm.
5 Pex ce tix kawn lheu mhawm khaing aux,
een krax pa laux leeing aux mawh mhai sakse phi map pa yuh aux.
6 Ang pex tawng in hoik mawh alo kaing pa yuh Siyiex lie?
Nawh tawm rub hoek tix lai lawm ka aux.
7 Aux kok yo pui teem tix,
mai ang koe pui pa nghiet;
aux khiox khaw pa rauh see,
mai ang koe pui pa nghiet ka aux.
8 Siyiex tieb tie ka aux,
aux ang pon luan hu;
nawh kieh pa faik tawb kheem ka krax hu aux.
9 Nawh teei ka mhok khri aux hu,
yuh laux ka min ceu kaux aux.
10 Nawh hoek puing ka aux khawm pon praok si daix daog;
nawh hoik laoh tik ka krax keud pot jun pot aux khawm riah.
11 Siyiex song rhawm ka aux,
mai kieh aux mawh awm pui koeng ka nawh.
12 Nawh ploi pui naing puing ka aux,
pum ceu pa tieb tie lai lawm aux ka praok nyiex gox bang aux heue.
 
13 Siyiex kieh pui nyiex ma aux tik vud ka aux;
can pui pa tawng aux sidawng kieh aux mawh pui tang pui praw.
14 Paox nyiex pa dex mai aux hoik tik vud ka aux;
paox grawm aux hoik piawm ka aux.
15 Nyo pa hoik it paung ka nyiex ma aux kieh aux awm pui tang ceu tang cad;
bun siliex khankix kieh aux awm pui tang pui praw pa ang tix tawng;
16 aux kok yo ka siliex tix,
nawh ang simi ka aux;
aux khiox khaw nawh teem coi tix,
nawh kawx ang simi ka aux.
17 Moeng aux ang pon kham ka bhawm aux;
paox nyiex pa dex mai aux kawx ang cu tix hu dex ka aux.
18 Pie kawn nyawm kawx hoik nyiah aux;
yam kaoh cung aux,
kix tom map nai ka aux;
19 paox grawm pa kied dex mai aux ang rhawm yaox ka aux.
Pui pa kied muh aux kawx hoek tix puing ka aux.
20 Hak aux pid ka si-ang aux tom kaux;
pa koe aux mawh ngie bhawm tix gum khaing heue.
 
21 Pex hoik mawh paox grawm aux,
caox rhawm ka aux liak!
Plawng taix Siyiex cot aux goh.
22 Yuh ka mawx pex lai sang kieh tix awm Siyiex ciyang ka aux yuh nan?
Krax laux rhawm pa thiem thie pex ka piang aux kawn ang nyang bax kub cien lie?
 
23 Aux kied simie pui koe tix doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai aux,
mai tiiem lox aux ka daux phuk lai!
24 Phan ang mawh nan,
teei rhiem pawd kah,
teei si vun chun kah,
pawd ka piang rang simaox.
25 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng cao pa kia ngiena aux im ju;
dee si dauh nawh tag sang prawng rauh see son aux.
26 Viang jhawm saux hoik lhawn nex hak aux kawx,
aux kawn ca tawx to kaux tixb in ka Siyiex.
27 Aux sang kieh ngai tix yaox ka nawh,
nawh ang kieh aux awm pui tang pui praw pa ang tix tawng heue.
 
Rhawm rhi aux kied sidaing si kied tete!
28 Pex ah nin:
ex sang tuk tiak nawh kha rhawm tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Riah krax laux krax leeing nawh koe ka to kaux nawh.
29 Aux krai pex mhong,
tag caw tix lhat graung ka vaig gawn;
vaig gawn in viex hoek ka phi map krax pa song rhawm Siyiex.
Pex caw tix tawng koe Siyiex cao pa prawng ca tix kaux heue.c
