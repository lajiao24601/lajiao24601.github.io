Ya-paw-la-hang liag haktiex si meeig si paung ka Sa-la
23
Sa-la im ju tix yieh tix nga aliah num.
2 Nawh yum ka haktiex meung Cia-nang veng Ci-lie Ya-pa pa mawh Si-paw-loeng.
Ya-paw-la-hang laux rhawm mai yiiem doe son nawh heue.
3 Ya-paw-la-hang hu khaing dee een kix to kaux pa yum moeng nawh,
mai hoek ka ceu pui Heux dee an,
ah nin:
4 a“Aux hoik mawh pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax pex;
khiox khaw tawx aux liag haktiex tix dauh son gawng si meeig,
son sang si paung aux moeng tix ka heue.”
5 Kix ah nin:
6 “Sara eue,
khiox khaw nghiet kah,
ka sivoe ngai yix,
maix hoik mawh pui pa ting min tix kaux.
Khiox khaw maix si paung moeng tix ka haktiex yix dee blawn mhawm;
pui ku kaux hoik cu tix tawx haktiex in ka maix heue.”
7 Ya-paw-la-hang tom yaog tix kaoh,
mai krub ka kix,
8 ah nin:
“Phan pex cu tix tawx aux si paung moeng tix ot tin,
khiox khaw pex kok ka kawn simex Yi-fu-loeng pa mawh kawn simex Saw-sia
9 mai coh daux rang Me-pi-la ka aux;
kheu daux rang an hoik ot ka piang kaung nawh uik.
Khiox khaw nawh krai jum sang coh tix ka aux sivoe ngai pex,
kheu aux sang kieh mawh gawng haktiex si meeig heue.”
10 Yi-fu-loeng mai pui Heux ngawm tau ka diag siviex veng dee khrawm kix paox tix.
Nawh pawk lox sivoe ngai pui hun,
kieh pui pa laig daux veng mhong khawm uik.
Nawh ah nin:
11 “Sara eue,
khiox khaw nghiet kah,
aux sang tawx kaung in khawm gawng mai khawm pa koe ka daux kaung khawm uik ka maix;
aux sang tawx ka maix ka sivoe ngai paox ceu paox cad tix,
son sang si paung maix moeng tix ka heue.”
12 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-paw-la-hang krub ot plak sivoe ngai pui Heux,
13 mai krai ka Yi-fu-loeng,
kieh pui ku kaux pon mhong kah.
Nawh ah nin:
“Khiox khaw nghiet kah,
aux sang liag kaung in khawm gawng,
khiox khaw maix rieb ka ngawh,
aux tom tag si paung moeng tix ka heue.”
14 Yi-fu-loeng pawk lox:
15 “Sara eue,
haktiex an khaw ngie pon yieh mau luah,
si nax ax mai paox,
ang lai sawn pa tid tix?
Khiox khaw maix si paung moeng tix tan heue.”
16 Ya-paw-la-hang cu kah,
mai hawt awm jum ah Yi-fu-loeng,
ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
siao jiang cie pui yuh lai gee mai ciang tix tawx ka nawh pon yieh plah mau luah heue.
17 Yuh nin,
sin haktiex Yi-fu-loeng pa ot ka Mang-lix plak lih singaix daux rang Me-pi-la tom mawh cie Ya-paw-la-hang,
khawm daux rang,
mai khaox ox pa koe daux kaung khawm uik,
hoik mawh cie Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
18 Pui Heux pa khrawm tix ot diang siviex veng khawm uik hoik tawng mawh cie Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
19 Khaix an,
Ya-paw-la-hang tom teei moeng tix Sa-la si paung ot haktiex Cia-nang daux rang Me-pi-la,
Mang-lix plak lih singaix (Mang-lix pa mawh veng Si-paw-loeng).
20 Haktiex an mai daux rang an pa hoik dawg mawh cie pui Heux sivoe,
jauh yam in tom mawh gawng si meeig son cie Ya-paw-la-hang nong nong heue.
