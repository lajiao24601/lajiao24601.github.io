Kawn cau Khrit
4
Pex tag caw tix een ex mawh siliex Khrit,
pa mawh pui cau pa raux pa mox Siyiex.
2 Pa nhie simiang pui cau tix yuh mawh pa kaux sijax.
3 Phan pex prawng ca ka aux,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka pui mawx ang ah,
mai aux hoik een in mawh kaing pa iak;
to kaux aux ang prawng ca ka tix.
4 Aux hoik tawng grawng pa singax rhawm rhi tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang doh mawh krai aux ang tix koe phi map.
Mawh ngie Simiang Ye-sux pa pon prawng ca ka aux khaing.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
sivoe sang hoek bawg yam,
pex paw vi prawng ca ka pui tix kaux kawx.
Tag caw tix krox jun yam hoek Simiang Ye-sux,
tom taug nghoik tix prawng ca;
bawg yam an,
nawh kawx tag sang plih lih ka ceu pa mox tix glip ot daux pa faik,
mai jhi lih ka ceu pa keud kawn pui daux rhawm tix.
Bawg yam an,
pui ku kaux taug sang pon krax pa khaw tix yaog yaw khaing Siyiex heue.
6 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
mawh kheu son pex,
aux teei tix siao kaing in kix ka piang Ya-paw-law mai ka to kaux tix,
kieh mawh lox pa tiag,
son sang kieh pex gau dee bleeh gum lox “nghiet lox cu si kah” in.
Pex pui mawx ang ah,
ang pon tix tang yaog tix ting lhaong,
ting jhak ceu in,
iak jhak ceu an.
7 Pui mawx kieh pex pon pex ka pui tang kaux lie?
Khex ang ceu pa koe pex khawm uik mawh pa tawx Siyiex lie?
Phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx lai ah lox ting mu,
ka can pa koe tix khawm uik awm pa ang bax mawh khawng phak buan son Siyiex lie?
8 Pex hoik koe ku ceu khawm uik heue!
Pex hoik kub mi heue!
Pex mawh simiang,
mai hoik prah ghram ex ot tix plak.
Chix tix heue!
Aux hoik khaw rhawm ka mawh pex simiang tete,
son sang kieh yix kawx yaox tix mawh simiang tau mai pex nang.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
aux jhak tix yaox,
kheu hoik kieh Siyiex ex pa mawh kawn cau in kix,
nghoik tix dia tix ot khaix paox,
awm pui lud pa hoik prawng pui yum yuh nan,
mai lih mawh krax klieh ka daux haktiex yuh nan,
son sang kieh kawn cau Siyiex mai pui haktiex jhak.
10 Yix hoik lih mawh pui pa daox aw kheu son Khrit,
pex hoik tieh tix mawh pui pa lhak lhiao ka daux Khrit;
ex iak riang,
pex tieh tix ting riang;
pui map nai ka ex,
pex tieh tix pon krax pa yung yi pui.
11 Tom hoek yam in,
ex kawn ot ka daux krax pa cai rhawm,
ang yaox tix ceep kraung,
kham krax pa si vied jheeng jheeng,
vu vie ku dauh ku dee,
12 akhaix an yuh kaing khawm ra khawn pa taug mao eei to kaux tix im.
Phan pui jhax lawg ka ex,
ex tag ah lox pa nawb buan ka nawh;
phan pui tuk tiak ka ex,
ex tag sang gun yeung kah;
13 phan map nai ka ex,
ex tag sang pawk nawh ka lox mhawm.
Tom hoek yam in,
pui kawn een ex mawh kram nyiex ka piang haktiex,
bliah blak ka daux kawn pui heue.
14 Aux tiiem gum lox pa yuh nin,
ang mawh son sang kieh pex kaig tua,
hoik mawh son sang sidiex gau ka pex,
kieh pex awm kawn simex kawn bun pa muh tix yuh nan.
15 Viang pex hoik koe sara kaing mheen kaux,
ka daux krax ot krax koe mawh pex kawn Khrit kawx,
viang mawh nan,
pex koe ngie keeing tix kaux khaing.
Mawh kheu hoik sidiex aux gum lox buan ka pex,
ka daux Ye-sux Khrit aux hoik lih mawh keeing ka pex.
16 bMawh kheu nan,
aux jheeh nyaw pex gau ka kaing mhawm pa yuh aux.
17 Mawh kheu son kaing in,
aux ploi Thi-maw-thaix hu ka pex dee an;
ka daux Simiang Ye-sux,
nawh hoik mawh kawn simex pa muh mai pa kaux sijax tix kaux.
Nawh tag sang krai ka pex,
grawng pa hoik priex aux tix hawt ka daux krax ot krax koe,
pa khraox ka daux Ye-sux Khrit,
pa mawh krax pa sidiex gau aux ka ceu cao ku dauh ku dee heue.
18 Ka daux pex,
koe pui tix blah een aux ang doh hoek ka pex dee an,
kix tom ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
19 Tete te lai,
phan Simiang Ye-sux cu,
ang koe miex glang tix aux taug sang hoek tix jhak ka pex.
Bawg yam an,
ja lao aux ang nghiet ka lox pa krai pui pa cau nyhau an kix ceu pa tix,
aux kawx tag sang jhak tix yaox sang pon yuh kix kaing ceu pa tix.
20 Mawh kheu siawp sibrawm Siyiex ang ot ka daux lox ah si kah pui,
mawh hoik ot ka daux siawp sibrawm.
21 Pex sang laug ka ceu pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Sang muh pex aux viex maox si vied hu,
sang muh pex aux viex rhawm awn rhawm tiiem hu lie?
