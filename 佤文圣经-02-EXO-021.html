Been pa yuh ka siliex cawng
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 15.12–18)
21
“Tawx lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
2 aPhan maix liag ceu pui Si-paw-lai tix kaux mawh siliex cawng,
nawh tag sang yuh kaing son maix liah num.
Hoek ka daux aliah num,
nawh tag pon tix luat luan,
mai ang lai siao tawx mau taoh tid tix.
3 Yam mawh nawh siliex cawng maix,
phan ang baox koe moeng,
yam lih hu nawh paw kieh nawh viex moeng tix;
yam mawh nawh siliex cawng maix,
phan nawh hoik koe moeng,
nawh tag chix tix viex moeng tix hu tau mai tix.
4 Phan moeng nawh mawh pa tawx cao simiang,
can kawn simex kawn bun pa keh khaing moeng nawh khawm uik,
tag mawh cie cao simiang;
nawh tag sang lih hu nong nong.
5 Phan siliex cawng an krai lox sidawng grawng muh ka simiang tix,
mai moeng tix,
kawn simex kawn bun tix,
ang rhawm simie tix lih hu ka daux pa luat luan,
6 cao simiang nawh tag caw tix viex nawh hu ka nyiex dee krub Siyiex,
mai kieh nawh hoek dex ka diang siviex,
mai cung ka si dub siviex,
mai cieh yhaok nawh koe daux ka jim rhiem lawm.
Yuh nin,
nawh tag sang mawh pui pa yuh kaing cao simiang tix leeing ju heue.
7 “Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux coh kawn bun tix mawh siliex,
nawh tag ang pon tix lih hu awm simex siliex khankix.
8 Phan cao pa liag bun an keud tix sang moeng ka nawh,
khaix an ang lai muh ka nawh,
tag caw tix kieh miex keeing nawh deeh taoh ka nawh;
cao an ang pon tix coh nawh ka pui tang ceu,
mawh kheu hoik pie lhien nawh ka bun an.
9 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux liag bun siliex an mawh moeng kawn simex tix,
tag caw tix priex nawh awm kawn bun tix.
10 Phan koe pui pa baox teei tix koe moeng daux ra kaux,
nawh tag caw tix kia ngien ka moeng phaox ka awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan,
tawx nawh ih priex preem,
kraung ceep,
mai pa simie nex nham nawh,
ang pon tix kieh cud tix ceu kawx.
11 Phan nawh ang pon yuh ceu in loe ceu ka moeng phaox ka tix,
tag caw tix kieh nawh lih hu awm rhawm pa keud nawh,
mai ang pon tix lai teei tid tix khaing nawh heue.”
Been pa ciyang ka son simah simaing
12 b“Can pa lauh ka pui tix kaux kaux kieh yum,
tag caw tix deeh kieh nawh yum.
13 cPhan ang su tix pauh ka pui,
ang tawng yam luan taix tix kah,
nawh tag chix tix to hu mox tix ka dee pa hoik ciak ci aux.
14 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux,
su tix pauh ka pui,
tag caw tix pauh yum;
viang nawh to hu mox tix ka sivoe khu dee tan aux kawx,
tag ang pon tix luat luan.
15 “Can pui pa lauh ka miex keeing tix,
tag sang pauh nawh yum heue.
16 d“Can pui pa briex tix phrawk ka pui,
mai coh nawh mawh siliex cawng,
phan ang mawh nan,
tag deuh nawh mawh siliex cawng tix,
tag pauh nawh yum heue.
17 e“Can pui pa jhax lawg ka miex keeing tix,
tag caw tix pauh yum.
18 “Phan pui ra kaux lauh paox tix,
tix kaux dheem paox tix ka simaox,
phan ang mawh nan,
tap nawh ka taix tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax lauh paox tix yum,
chix tix ang nyang ciyang ka nawh.
Phan pui an it ka piang ku kheu mat tix,
19 viang mawh nan,
khaix an nawh deeh pon kaoh,
hu lai ling plak praix mai giex ka khaox gix,
pui pa hoik lauh ka nawh an,
tag sang vied ka singaix pa gla nawh,
mai pai nawh moi heue.
20 “Can pui pa tawk siliex cawng tix ka khaox,
mawh simex siliex bun siliex ang ah,
mai kieh siliex cawng yum dum mawx dum an,
nawh tag caw tix pon krax prawng ca.
21 Phan siliex cawng an koe ra loe ngaix tag yum,
cao pa lauh an ang hoek krax pa ciyang;
ka daux pa som nai sin ngao nawh hoik mawh pa ciyang piang nawh heue.
22 “Phan koe pui pa simah ka paox tix mai hu sideeing ka bun pa koe kaux,
mai bun an som nai kawn,
viang ang koe khaix mat tang dauh kawx,
pui an tag sang vied kah;
sang vied ka miex ding caw tix mawh mex bun miex pa mat an pa krai lih,
khaix an simiang cao prawng tom tat dut.
23 Phan to kaux bun an mat,
pui an tag plud ju son ju,
24 fngai son ngai,
rhang son rhang,
taix son taix,
caong son caong,
25 pa hax ngu son pa hax ngu,
pa pliag son pa pliag,
pa koe rhoi khaix si vied son pa koe rhoi khaix si vied heue.
26 “Phan koe pui tix kaux tawk ka ngai simex siliex mai bun siliex tix,
kieh laux tix khawn,
nawh tag caw tix ploi ka siliex cawng an lih hu,
kieh mawh pa hoik vied ka son ngai nawh.
27 Phan nawh tawk ka rhang siliex cawng tix goh,
nawh tag caw tix ploi siliex cawng an lih hu,
kieh mawh pa hoik vied son rhang heue.”
Beeh glawm cao kraung
28 “Phan koe moi teed pui yum tix kaux kaux,
tag caw tix dheem moi an yum ka simaox,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix ih ka nex;
mai cao moi an ang hoek krax ciyang.
29 Phan moi an dawg mawh moi teed ka pui jheeng jheeng,
mai cao moi an ang cu tix nghiet ka lox pa krai pui miex bawg mawx,
ca kawn ang phrawk moi mhawm mhawm,
mai moi an teed pui yum,
moi an tag caw tix dheem yum ka simaox,
cao moi kawx tag caw tix pauh yum heue.
30 Phan prawng ca cao moi vied ju tix ka mau,
nawh tag sang tawx ngawh awm pa hoik tat ka piang nawh.
31 Phan moi teed ka kawn simex kawn bun pui tix kaux kaux ang ah,
tag caw tix yuh hawt awm been pa hoik krai plak piang.
32 Phan moi teed ka simex siliex mai bun siliex,
cao moi tag sang tawx mau tix ngoe plah ka cao nyiex siliex cawng an,
mai tag dheem moi an yum ka simaox heue.
33 “Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux paoh ka daux o,
phan ang mawh nan,
kaong ka daux,
mai ang tawb ang kheem moing daux si glaing,
moi mai la cawd ka daux,
34 nawh caw tix teei mau vied ka cao khawng brie,
mai khawng brie pa yum an tag mawh cie nawh.
35 Phan koe moi tix mu mu hu teed ka moi tang mu tom yum,
kiex tag coh moi pa kawn im an,
mai guah mau,
moi pa hoik yum an kawx,
kiex tag guah greung nex.
36 Phan cao moi an tix kaux kaux hoik dawg tawng moi tix mawh moi brax teed,
phan nawh kawn ang phrawk kaux,
nawh caw tix teei moi pa koe tix,
vied ka plak cao moi pa yum an,
moi pa hoik yum an tag mawh cie nawh heue.”
