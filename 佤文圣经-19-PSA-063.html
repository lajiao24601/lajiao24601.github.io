Simie ka Siyiex a
63
bSiyiex eue,
maix mawh Siyiex aux;
aux cu ca tix sawg ka maix.
Hoik awm kaung ma pa kroh pa riem,
pa ang rawm koe ka tix dauh,
kied ce ka maix,
simie ka maix.
2 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux ot tix krub maix ka daux nyiex buan;
kieh aux yaox ka pa ting riang ting rao maix,
mai ka min plak ngai maix.
3 Krax muh maix pa koe leeing ju kawn lheu ngawh khaing ju,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix.
4 Aux tag sang yaog yaw ka maix tom si dauh ju tix;
aux tag sang yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn mai rawg buan ka maix.
5 Khuan aux tag sang yaox tix sawm sak;
aux tag sang rhax lox rhax krax gaux rhawm ka maix heue.
 
6 Yam blauh aux tix it piang ku,
mai aux doh keud ka maix;
aux keud ce ka maix leeing lang sawm.
7 Mawh kheu teem coi maix ka aux ku bawg ku yam;
aux gaux rhawm gaux rhi ot ka greem veei preeik krax pa kia ngien maix.
8 Aux sau riang tix hawt khaix maix;
taix khawn dawm maix joh jhan ka aux heue.
 
9 Pui pa keud tix sang yuh aux laux,
tag sang hoek meung yum.
10 Kix tag sang yum ka greem vaig gawn,
tawx sox la ih ka to kaux yum kix.
11 Viang mawh nan,
simiang tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu Siyiex;
can pui pa hoik ap pong tix tiex tu ka Siyiex,
tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh khawm uik;
jheeh pui pie pui lhien tag sang thox ka heue.
