Lox rawg buan khiox khaw krax taoh
33
Pui pa koeng ka yix tag sang laux krax leeing heue!
Kix hoik ca tud rhim ka pui tang kaux khankix,
viang mawh nan,
pui tang kaux ang sax tud rhim ka kix;
kix hoik sax pie lhien ka pui,
viang mawh nan,
pui tang kaux ang sax pie sax lhien ka kix.
Viang mawh nan,
phan hoik krox jun tom ang kix lai baox pie lhien ka pui,
mai pui tag sang pie lhien ka kix heue.
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka yix.
Yix hoik deuh krax keud pot jun pot tix ka maix.
Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka yix ku singaix,
yam ot yix ka daux krax pa kha rhawm taoh ka yix.
3 Yam puing maix naing son yix,
meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik tag sang yaog tix to ka daux pa hoik mhong ka lox kok yo pa pauh yum.
4 Pui koeng hoik tud kraung naing kix hu khawm uik,
awm hoik nyao ih sido pa jao mee haktiex ka to ma yuh nan heue.
5 Simiang Siyiex kied ting kied lhaong heue!
Nawh kuang ka piang ku ceu khawm uik.
Nawh hoik kieh Yie-lu-sa-leung naog ka krax pa rauh pa see,
6 khaix an kieh meung haktiex pon krax pa nyawm ot.
Nawh kia ngien ka pui cie tix leeing ju,
mai tawx kix koe ping nyia cuyi mai krax keud krax sawn.
Krax lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex mawh sin ngao pa blawn ting ka kix heue.
7 Pui pa kua khiox khaw krax teem;
kawn cau pa ting tag sawg pa nhim hoik laux rhawm yiiem grai.
8 Phan krax hoik nu tix lawng,
ang koe pui pa pon hu kah.
Phan lox sijax mukheu pa yuh pui hoik grai,
mai lox an tag ang lai koe ceu;
pui mai paox pui tix tag ra ang lai yum rhawm ka paox tix.
9 Haktiex hoik riem;
nong khaox Li-pa-noeng hoik kroh hoik rham;
ngawd Sa-loeng hoik nhik;
lhax khaox haktiex Pa-sang mai gawng Cia-mix hoik nyao khrum khawm uik heue.
Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka pui koeng
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka meung pa koe ka piang haktiex:
“Yam in aux tag sang jauh tix yuh.
Aux tag sang kieh pex yaox ka riang rao pa kied ting tix.
11 Krax pa keud pa sawn pex mawh pa giex pa going;
krax pa yuh pex ang koe ceu pie ceeix kawx.
Khuan aux tag sang awm ngu mai gleeing pex laux leeing yuh nan.
12 Pex tag sang awm simaox pa gleeing yuh nan,
pa lih mawh simaox ceed,
awm pang kat pa hoik pat pui tix sau gleeing ka daux glah ngu yuh nan,
pa lih mawh nyaox.
13 Pui pa ot dee singai khankix eue,
pex caw tix jhak pa hoik yuh aux khawm uik;
pui pa ot dee dex khankix eue,
pex caw tix krai doh ka siawp sibrawm aux heue.”
14 Pui phi map pa ot ka Shi-ang hoik yaoh yee lhat graung.
Kix ah nin:
“Krax prawng ca Siyiex awm ngu pa ang chix yeet leeing ju,
khex sang pon luat luan ex khaing daux ngu in lie?”
15 Ca mawh yuh pex kaing pa rauh see,
krai lox pa mawh pa meh,
pex tag pon tix im ju.
Paw vi gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm;
paw vi rieb ka mau chun praok;
paw vi rom tix yuh paox tau mai pa pauh ka pui;
paw vi yuh kaing pa caox pa caig.
16 Yuh nin,
pex tom tag yaox tix nyawm ot,
awm hoik pon tix kaux cong ka daux tab pa kaux cong yuh nan.
Pex tag ang lai koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi ka ceu pa sang ih sang nyaux tix heue.
Min plak ngai pa sang hoek
17 Pex tag sang yaox ka simiang pa ting min plak ngai tix kaux mai ka meung haktiex pa vieh pa va.
18 Son krax lhat krax graung pui pa koh keen meung tang meung mai son pui pa jiang hoik luan.
19 Pex ang lai baox deeh yaox ka pui meung tang meung pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi si kah ka lox pa ang pex tawng.
20 Pex jhak ka Shi-ang heue,
an hoik mawh veng pa yuh poi gaux rhawm pa singax singeh.
Pex jhak ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue,
ex hoik pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux heue!
Veng in hoik mawh nyiex gox pa ang chix gah tix hu heue;
jim veng in ang lai baox deeh lawd tix lih,
maox kawx ang lai chix dut leeing ju heue.
21 Simiang Siyiex tag sang jhi ka min plak ngai tix lih.
Ex tag sang ot ka praok rawm klawng pa vieh va;
reu cie pui koeng ang hoek dee an.
22-23 Viang reu cie pui koeng hoik laig ka diang praok rawm sigrit sigra cie ex kawx,
maox reu hoik ghah,
mai man reu ang chix yah tix;
kraung pa ot ka daux reu hoik lih mawh cie ex,
pie son cie pui chawh kawx hoik tang koe nyum cie.
Simiang Siyiex mawh cao pa prawng son ex,
cao pa yuh jhawm prawng son ex,
mai simiang ex;
nawh tag sang taoh ka ex.
24 Pui pa ot ka daux meung haktiex ex,
ang lai baox deeh yaox krax kha rhawm kheu jhawm saux,
phi map pui pa ot ka daux veng kawx tag pon krax pa pluat ploi khawm uik heue.
