Kai-yin mai Ya-paw
4
Ya-tang hoik ot tau mai moeng tix Sha-wa,
moeng nawh koe kaux,
mai keh kawn simex tix kaux.
Nawh tom ah nin:
“Mawh kheu krax pa teem coi Simiang Siyiex,
aux hoik pon kawn tix kaux.”
Nawh tom sum ceu kaux nawh kaux Kai-yina
2 Khaix an,
nawh baox keh kawn simex tix kaux,
tom sum ceu kaux nawh kaux Ya-paw.
Ya-paw mawh cao pa liang yung,
mai Kai-yin mawh cao pa yuh kaing ma kaung.
3 Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
Kai-yin viex plix nghox plix koi pa pon tix khaing ma kaung,
kieh mawh khawng tan tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
4 bYa-paw kawx laug kawn yung pa liang tix keh khaing ka daux phung yung cie tix,
kieh mawh khawng tan tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex rieb tix teei ka Ya-paw mai khawng tan nawh.
5 Viang mawh nan,
ang rieb tix teei ka Kai-yin mai khawng tan nawh,
mawh kheu nan,
Kai-yin kied song rhawm song rhi.
6 Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka Kai-yin:
“Song rhawm maix jao pa tix?
Laux pak ngai maix jao pa tix?
7 Phan maix yuh ka kaing pa khaw maix tix yuh,
krax nyiah maix tag lih,c
viang mawh nan,
kheu hoik yuh maix kaing pa ang khaw tix yuh,
phi map hoik pau maix ka diang siviex tan,
mai phi map an tag sang giex gang ka maix,
mai maix tag caw tix pex ka phi map an heue.”
8 eKai-yin ah nin ka pux tix Ya-paw:
“Hu ax ka daux kaung!”d
Yam hoek kiex daux kaung,
Kai-yin yuh simah ka pux tix,
mai pauh nawh yum heue.
9 Khaix an Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Kai-yin:
“Pux aik maix Ya-paw ot dee mawx?”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Aux ang tawng,
khex mawh aux pui pa kia ngien ka pux tix lie?”
10 fSimiang Siyiex ne raw nawh ah nin:
“Hoik yuh maix ceu pa tix?
Lox kok yo nham pux maix khaing daux tiex hoik yiiem hoek ka aux.
11 Yam pauh maix pux tix,
haktiex ang jheeh tix rieb ka nham nawh.
Yam in maix pon pa jhax lawg,
ang lai yaox tix sum kaung ma.
12 Viang maix yuh ma kaung,
haktiex kawx ang plix plix.
Maix tag sang mawh pui pa vung viang ka piang haktiex.”
13 Kai-yin deeh ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Krax ciyang in kied ting lhaong khaing pa pon kham aux.
14 Maix grheu aux hu khaing piang haktiex in,
son ang aux lai pon tix yaox ka pak ngai maix;
aux tag sang lih tix mawh pui pa vung viang piang haktiex,
mai vung viang ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
can pui pa yaox ka aux,
tag sang pauh aux yum heue.”
15 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Ang,
can pui pa pauh ka maix,
krax pawk bix tag deeh koe piang nawh aliah son.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex deuh mhai ka piang dong re Kai-yin son ang pui pa yaox ka nawh pon pauh nawh yum.
16 Khaix an,
Kai-yin lih hu khaing sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai ot ka haktiex “vung viang” pa koe plak lih singaix si peem Yi-tiien heue.
Doeh ceu cad Kai-yin
17 Kai-yin ot tau mai moeng tix,
mai moeng nawh koe kaux,
keh kawn simex tix kaux,
mai sum ceu kaux mawh Yi-naw.
Kai-yin sum veng tix mu,
mai kok ceu kaux veng an kaux Yi-naw,
awm ceu kaux kawn tix.
18 Yi-naw keh Yi-na,
Yi-na keh Mi-hu-ya-lix,
Mi-hu-ya-lix keh Ma-thu-sha-lix,
Ma-thu-sha-lix keh La-maix
19 La-maix teei tix koe moeng ra kaux mai ceu kaux mawh Ya-tax mai Si-la.
20 Ya-tax keh Ya-pa,
Ya-pa mawh keeing ka can pui pa eei khawng brie.
21 Ceu kaux pux nawh mawh Yo-pa,
Yo-pa pa mawh keeing ka can pui pa tauh ting mai pui phlu shiao khankix.
22 Si-la keh kawn simex Thu-pa Kai-yin,
Thu-pa Kai-yin mawh cao pa gau pui ka kaing cang lhek,
pa raug ka tawng mai rhiem,
mai nan Thu-pa Kai-yin mawh Na-ma heue.
23 La-maix ah nin ka moeng tix ra kaux:
Ya-tax,
Si-la eue,
nghiet ka lox aux,
moeng La-maix eue,
bre tix nghiet ka lox krai aux;
aux hoik pauh ka pui pa tuk tiak ka aux;
aux pauh ka pui nhum ju pa mat yuh aux heue.
24 gPhan pui mawx pauh ka Kai-yin tag pon pa pawk bix aliah son;
pui mawx pauh ka aux tag pon pa pawk bix atigliah aliah son heue.
Sai-theux mai Yi-naw-seex
25 Ya-tang baox ot tau mai moeng tix,
mai keh kawn simex tix kaux,
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Sai-theux,h
Sha-wa ah nin:
“Siyiex tawx kawn simex ka aux tix kaux,
tang ka Ya-paw pa hoik pauh Kai-yin.”
26 Sai-theux koe kawn simex tix kaux,
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-naw-seex.
Yam an,
kawn pui jauh tix khiox khaw ka ceu kaux “Yie-heu-hua” heue.
