Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa krub riang rao
8
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Phlu ka teet tawng pa so pui haing heue!
Pui koeng awm klang ting pa hoek tix lauh ka haktiex aux.
Pui cie aux tik vud ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai kix,
mai ang lai rhawm nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau aux.
2 Viang kix hoik tawng mawh aux Siyiex kix,
mai hoik een tix tawng yung ka aux,
3 viang mawh nan kix tieb tie ka ceu pa mhawm pa mhiem.
Kheu grawng in,
pui koeng tag sang leue ka kix heue.
4 “Pui cie aux hawt alo to kaux tix yaog tix sum ka simiang;
yam yaog kix tix sum ka simiang,
mai ang bax kieh mawh lox aux.
Kix teei mau khri tix yuh riang kah,
mai kieh krax pa laux leeing tang deeh hoek ka piang to kaux tix.
5 Aux koeng ka riang rao moi khri pa krub pui Sa-ma-li-yax,
mai ang rhawm yaox.
Kix tag sang krox hoek miex glang tag sang tik vud ka riang rao in lie?
6 Cang mai pui Yi-seu-liex yuh ka riang rao,
viang mawh nan,
riang rao in ang tie mawh Siyiex.
Riang rao kawn moi khri pa koe daux veng Sa-ma-li-yax tag sang yuh laux leeing.
7 Pa rhuat pa van kix mawh bhaung,
pa khrawm pa vawg kix mawh bhaung ting.
Nghox ca pa ang sok ang chix tix yuh khao mun;
phan chix tix yuh kawx,
tag sang mawh pui tang meung pa ih.
8 Pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai tang siyox khaing meung ka piang haktiex,
mai hoik awm kawng pa max,
ang chix tix siao.
9 Kix hoik awm la tiag pa lieh lox mai tang hu ka krax hu tix;
kix een rhawm tix ka meung Ya-sux,
mai baox teei mau chun praok khiox khaw meung tang meung kia ngien tix.
10 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang khrawm kix ot tau,
mai ciyang ka kix.
Yam gut kheem simiang Ya-sux kix,
kix tag sang kha rhawm kha rhi tix heue.
11 “Son sang teei tix tik ka phi map tix,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh ka khu dee tan tom nie;
khu dee tan nied nie,
dee yuh pui phi map nied tix nie nang.
12 Aux hoik tiiem lox sidiex gau son kix tom nie,
viang mawh nan kix hoik ce mawh kaing pa daox mai tieb tie kah.
13 Kix muh tix yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka aux,
son sang ih nie ka nex pa tan lu an;
viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ang muh kaing pa yuh nin,
aux tieh tix sang tiiem tix deuh ka phi map kix,
mai ciyang ka kix;
aux tag sang ploi kix deeh ing ka haktiex Ai-cix heue.
14 “Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh ka nyiex haw tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
hoik piawm ka cao pa phan pa yuh tix.
Pui Yo-tax hoik yuh ka veng pa kaux cong tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang ploi ngu gleeing ka nyiex haw kix mai ka veng kix laux leeing heue.”
