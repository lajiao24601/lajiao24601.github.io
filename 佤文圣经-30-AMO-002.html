Grawng Maw-ya
2
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Maw-ya ca kawn baox yuh ka phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
Kix hoik kieh si-ang to kaux yum simiang meung Yi-tong gleeing mawh nyaox nyia.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing ka Maw-ya,
mai yuh laux leeing ka nyiex haw simiang Cia-liiex.
Yam kok yo puing naing,
mai yam phlu ka teet tawng,
pui Maw-ya tag sang yum ka bang naing.
3 Aux tag sang pauh ka simiang pui Maw-ya mai pauh ka can simiang kix pa koe khawm uik.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Grawng pui Yo-tax
4 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Pui Yo-tax ca kawn baox yuh ka phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
Kix hoik tik vud ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
mai ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap aux.
Kix hoik kieh siyiex pa pie lhien pa hoik krub tax du tix nyaw glaw tix,
mai hoik singai hu khaing daux krax pa mawh pa meh.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing ka pui Yo-tax,
mai yuh laux leeing ka nyiex haw simiang Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.”
Siyiex prawng ca ka pui Yi-seu-liex
6 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex ca kawn baox yuh ka phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
Kix ghoing ka mau khri,
coh cai ka pui rauh pui see,
mai teei been ngawh jum kiiep tix ku coh pui hot pa ang pon plud ceuk tix mawh siliex cawng.
7 Kix dug dig ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
cot ka pui hot pa ot daux krax.
Keeing mai kawn rom tix hu it tau mai bun coh kauxb ka daux nyiex vad,
mai hoik kieh ceu kaux pa singax aux seeing brai.
8 Kix hoik pon teei ka kraung ceep pa tang pui hot ka kix,
mai kieh mawh prux bi ka praok khu dee tan,
kix hoik pon ot ka daux nyiex buan Siyiex mai teei ka mau pa sux tix ka pui hot liag tix nyaux plai ka heue.
9 c“Pui cie aux eue,
viang pui Ya-maw-lix hoik ting lhaong awm khaox si oe bhawng yuh nan,
kaux cong awm khaox yang yit yuh nan,
mawh kheu son pex aux kawn yuh kix laux lak pak leeing.
10 Aux viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
duk thung pex ka daux tiag daux tu tipon num,
mai tawx haktiex cie pui Ya-maw-lix ka pex.
11 eAux hoik laug cao jhai khaing ka daux pux aik pex,
hoik gau ka pui pa singax ka daux been (Na-si-euxd).
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
mawh hoik lud lox in kix lie?
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
12 “Viang mawh nan,
pex khin pui pa singax ka daux been (Na-si-eux) nyaux ka plai,
ang cu cao jhai sidiex gau ka lox aux hu.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang gut pex ka piang tiex,
awm gut ling law pa tang naog ka nghox koi luah ah ku kit ku kit yuh nan.
14 Viang hoik sawn mawh pa yuh lah to kawx ang pon luat luan khaing;
pui pa ting riang kawx tag sang grai riang rao;
pui naing kawx pie ju to kaux nawh ang pon luat luan.
15 Pui pa chix puing ka ak ang lai pon cung cong;
pui naing pa brax to kawx ang pon luat luan;
pui pa brug brung kawx ang pon luat luan.
16 Ka daux singaix an,
pui naing pa blawn kua khankix kawx tag sang tik ka kraung naing tix,
yaog tix to khawm si yaung tix.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
