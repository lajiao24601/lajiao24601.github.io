Lai Yiiem Doe Yie-li-mi
Krai dawt
Lai Yiiem Doe Yie-li-mi phuan ceu mawh lai pa tiiem son yiiem doe yam laux leeing, gleeing Yie-lu-sa-leung, mai yam giex kawn pui hu, sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num ka daux phuan yieh tix daix liah num. Ka daux lai in viang koe tom nie ka gum lox pa laux rhawm laux rhi, pa yiiem ngia son krax kha rhawm ka Shi-ang, mai krax kaig krax tua, viang mawh nan, cao tiiem ca kawn jhi krax muh Siyiex, mai siawp sibrawm pa hoik een pui rhawm tix ka khawm uik, khawm pa naog ka krax keud pot jun pot son krax pa lawng tix. Pui Yo-thaix doh keud ka singaix sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num ka daux phuan yieh tix daix liah num, mai kix kieh mawh krax pa doh keud ku num son laux leeing meung. Yam tuah kix priex preem, yuh kaing krub son krax pa laux rhawm, mai siao ka lox lai in kix jheeng jheeng heue.
Krai kaing
1. Krax laux rhawm Yie-lu-sa-leung (1.1–22)
2. Yie-lu-sa-leung kham krax ciyang (2.1–22)
3. Krax ciyang mai krax keud pot jun pot (3.1–66)
4. Khaix hoik laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung (4.1–22)
5. Krax khiox khaw mai krax caox rhawm (5.1–22)
