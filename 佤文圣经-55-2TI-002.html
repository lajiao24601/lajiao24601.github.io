Pui naing pa kua cie Khrit
2
Kawn aux eue,
maix tag caw kieh tix kaux cong ka daux buan son pa tawx Ye-sux Khrit.
2 Maix hoik rieb ka lox sidiex aux ka plak sivoe ngai pui pa kham sakse tom nie,
maix kawx caw ap tix tawx ka pui pa hoik yum rhawm maix kah,
pa pon sidiex gau ka pui tang kaux khankix heue.
3 Mawh pui naing pa kua cie Ye-sux Khrit,
maix tag caw tix guah tix klawm ka krax kha rhawm.
4 Pui pa laig lien phan sang kieh tix pon krax pa yaog yaw simiang,
tag caw tix ang kieh kaing ceu pa koe plak praix nyiex bang ku kram yuh to kaux tix.
5 Pui pa yuh lah to phan ang hawt ka been pa tat tix deuh yam yuh lah to,
tag ang pon ka khawng phak.
6 Pui pa taug mao ka yuh kaing haktiex khankix,
tag caw tix ran ih ka nghox koi pa pon tix sivoe.
7 Maix tag caw tix keud ka gum lox pa hoik krai aux,
mawh kheu Simiang Ye-sux tag sang kieh pon tawng sidawng ka kaing in kix khawm uik heue.
8 Maix tag caw tix doh keud ka Ye-sux Khrit,
nawh mawh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
Siyiex kieh nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum;
in taug mawh gum lox buan pa hoik sidiex aux pex.
9 Aux hoik kha rhawm tix kheu pa sidiex ka gum lox buan in,
mai pui hoik phrawk ka aux,
awm mawh aux pui laung yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
gum lox Siyiex pui ang pon phrawk kah.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
aux kham gee kaing in kix son pui cie Siyiex pa hoik laug nawh,
son sang kieh kix pon ka krax taoh mai min plak ngai pa hoek khaing Ye-sux Khrit nang.
11 Gum lox in plak greem mawh pa kaux sijax yuh nin:
phan ex hoik yum tau mai nawh,
ex kawx tag sang im tau mai nawh.
12 aPhan ex gun yeung tom si dauh,
ex kawx tag sang giex kaing tau mai nawh.
Phan ex ang tawng ka nawh,
nawh kawx tag ang tawng ka ex.
13 Phan ex ang kaux sijax,
nawh ca kawn kaux sijax,
mawh kheu ang nawh lieh lheu ka to kaux tix heue.
Pui yuh kaing pa pon krax pa yaog yaw
14 Maix tag caw tix krai ka pui ku kaux,
mai bre tix sidiex kix ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex ah nin:
paw vi thung thiang paox tix ka lox pa si kah;
an hoik mawh pa ang koe dee mhawm pie ceeix kawx,
mai chix kieh ngie pui pa mhong khankix laux leeing.
15 Tag caw sau riang tix yuh kaing ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
mai kieh tix mawh pui pa pon kham krax pa cheuk cheu,
pa ang koe dee kaig tua,
mai kah ka gum lox pa mawh pa meh sidawng.
16 Tag caw tix to singai khaing lox giex going pa ang koe ceu cad,
mawh kheu lox ceu pa yuh nin tag kieh pui singai khaing Siyiex;
17 ceu pa krai kix hoik awm muad pa kieh nex pui leem yuh nan.
Ka daux pui in kix mawh Su-mi-nai mai Fe-li-thux;
18 kix hoik singai hu khaing pa mawh pa meh,
krai mawh kaing ceu pa deeh kaoh im pa hoik luan,
kieh krax yum rhawm pui tix blah yung yang.
19 bViang mawh nan,
num jaong kaux cong an pa hoik sum Siyiex ang yung yang;
ka piang jaong simaox in hoik tiiem ah nin:
“Simiang Ye-sux tawng yung ka pui cie tix”,
mai baox pawd ah nin:
“Pui pa tang kok tix mawh pui cie Simiang Ye-sux tag caw tix tik vud ka pa caox pa caig heue.”
20 Ka daux nyiex ting ku nyiex hoik koe kraung tom nie,
ang joeng mawh ngie kraung khri,
kraung mau,
kraung khaox,
kraung pa yuh ka tiex;
tix blah siao ka daux pa ting ceu,
tix blah siao ka daux pa iak ceu.
21 Phan pui kieh tix singax singeh,
singai hu khaing kaing pa caox pa caig,
tag sang pon pa kieh Simiang Ye-sux ting ceu;
mawh kheu hoik tan lu nawh tix ka Simiang Ye-sux,
tawx mawh pa siao Simiang Ye-sux ting ceu,
ka daux kaing ceu pa mhawm ku ceu heue.
22 Maix paw vi kieh tix awm jhawm pui pa nhum ju yuh nan;
tag caw tix sawg ka pa rauh pa see,
krax yum rhawm,
krax muh krax rhawm,
mai pa nhim,
tau mai pui pa singax daux rhawm,
pa khiox khaw krax teem coi Simiang Ye-sux khankix.
23 Tag caw tix tik vud ka lox pa thung thiang pa daox pa aw ceu an;
maix hoik tawng lox pa thung thiang ceu in,
chix kieh simah lih.
24 Siliex Simiang Ye-sux ang pon tix simah;
nawh caw tix viex rhawm awn ka pui,
rien sidiex ka kaing ceu pa mhawm,
gun yeung ka daux kaing ku ceu,
25 teei lox awn sidiex ka pui pa koeng ka tix.
Siyiex tee koi chix tawx bawg yam pa loh kix rhawm rhi tix,
mai kieh kix tawng ka pa mawh pa meh.
26 Yuh nin,
viang phi caox hoik giex kix hu,
mai nghiet ka phi caox ka daux pa khin ka kix,
kix kawx tee kawn chix si duad,
mai bheeik luan khaing daux rub cie nawh heue.
