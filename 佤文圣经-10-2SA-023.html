Lox deuh Ta-wue sivoe sang yum tix
23
Kawn simex Yie-si pa kaux Ta-wue hoik mawh pui pa laug Siyiex tix siao.
Siyiex Ya-keux ap nawh mawh simiang.
Ta-wue thut lox rhax pa kied nyawm tix nghiet son pui Yi-seu-liex tom nie.
Lox rhax in plak greem mawh pa hoik tiiem nawh tix deuh ah nin:
 
2 Khuan Simiang Siyiex laig si kah ka daux aux;
Lox krai nawh ot ka piang siyieb jheeh aux.
3 Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex krai ka lox,
Cao pa teem coi ka pui Yi-seu-liex si kah ka aux.
Kuang kia kawn pui ka daux krax pa rauh see tix,
kieh krax lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex simiang pa koe siawp sibrawm,
4 Awm riang singaix pa jhan tix lih pon ngawb,
awm riang singaix pa jhan riang pon ngawb yam ang pai awm koe,
kieh rib klai gaux rhawm kuah khaix hoik lih lhiex heue.
 
5 Siyiex sang kia ngien ka doeh ceu cad aux yuh nin,
mawh kheu hoik yuh nawh sijax mukheu tau mai aux leeing ju,
sijax mukheu ang tik ka leeing ju.
Nawh tag sang kieh kaing pa simie aux lih tix mawh,
mai tawx krax taoh ka aux.
6 Pui pa ang rauh ang see khankix tag sang awm yeem kat pa tik pui khankix,
pui tix kaux kawx ang pon tawx taix tix chuh kah,
7 Phan sang chuh tag caw tix siao khaox nguh rhiem mai pliah rhiem.
Tag sang teei ngu gleeing nawh khawm uik heue.
Pui kua cie Ta-wue
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 11.10–41)
8 Ceu kaux ca rang pui pa ting riang pa ot greem Ta-wue tiiem tix ot plak greem:
ka daux tix kaux mawh pui Tha-keu-meung pa kaux Yaw-seux Pa-seu.
Nawh mawh simiang “pui ting kaing loe kaux.”a
Yam puing naing ka daux tix bawg,
nawh hoik pauh pui yum daix yieh kaux ka pliah.
9 “Pui ting kaing loe kaux” ka daux ra kaux mawh pui Ya-heux kawn simex Taw-to pa kaux Yi-li-ya-sa.
Hoik koe tix bawg,
nawh hawt tiex tix lauh ka pui Fe-li-see an kix tau mai Ta-wue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex deeh tix ing.
10 Viang mawh nan,
nawh cung dee an mai lauh ka pui Fe-li-see,
tom hoik si klua plawng taix tix,
mai ang lai pon yah vaig khaing daux tawm taix tix.
Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh nawh pon pex.
Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex deeh ing ka Yi-li-ya-sa dee an,
viang mawh nan,
kix peeik ngie tix teei ka mhok rhiem khaing to kaux yum pui pa koeng khankix heue.
11 “Pui ting loe kaux” daux loe kaux mawh pui Ha-la kawn simex Ya-ci pa kaux Sa-ma.
Yam khrawm pui Fe-li-see paox tix ka daux kaung Li-si pa koe ceem ka khankix,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yaog tix to khawm uik,
12 viang mawh nan,
Sa-ma cung ka daux kaung an,
mai rieb tix lauh ka kix,
mai pon pauh pui Fe-li-see tom nie.
Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh nawh pon pex heue.
13 Yam vawg to ma,
ka daux “pui ting riang tix ngoe kaux”,
koe pui loe kaux lih hoek ka daux rang Ya-tu-lang dee ot Ta-wue.
Yam an,
koe pui Fe-li-see tix cub ot ka ngawd Li-fa-ying.
14 Ta-wue ot ka gawng dee rien tix mu.
Pui Fe-li-see tix cub hoik pon cam ka Paw-li-haung.
15 Ta-wue braong doe ka haktiex ot tix,
mai ah nin:
“Aux kied simie tix nyaux rhawm dung ot praok diang siviex veng Paw-li-haung heue!”
16 Pui pa kied kua an kix loe kaux tom hu laig ka daux bang ot pui Fe-li-see,
mai kin rawm dung,
viex hoek tawx Ta-wue nyaux.
Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue ang nyaux,
mai hieh ka rawm,
kieh mawh krax tan pa tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
17 Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang nyaux ka rawm in yuh ka mawx!
Phan yuh nin,
ang hoik awm nyaux aux nham pui pa kied kua an kix pa hu kin rawm ka daux krax pa kha rhawm tix lie?”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ang nyaux.
In hoik mawh kaing pa yuh pui pa kied kua an kix loe kaux heue.
18 Pux Yaw-ya pa kaux Ya-pi-sai hoik mawh simiang “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux.b
” Nawh hoik pauh pui yum ka pliah loe yieh kaux,
mawh kheu nan ka daux “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux” nawh tom ao min.
19 Nawh mawh ka daux “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kauxc” pa blawn ao min khaing paox tix tix kaux,
mai lih mawh simiang kix,
viang mawh nan,
kawn ang nyang ao min awm “pui ting kaing loe kaux” heue.
20 Pui Cia-shiiex kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya,
mawh pui pa kied kua pa ao min tang kaux tix kaux.
Nawh hoik koe kaing pa yuh pa ao min tom nie,
khawm pa hoik pauh nawh ka pui naing Maw-ya pa kied kua ra kaux.
Nawh hoik laig ka daux ox yam lih rha,
mai pauh ka sivai sang si yum tix mu.
21 Nawh baox pon pauh ka pui Ai-cix pa kied ting riang tix kaux.
Pui an viex ka pliah lang,
viang mawh nan,
Pi-na-ya viex ngie ka khaox nguh tieb tie ka nawh.
Pi-na-ya pon teei pliah khaing daux tawm taix nawh,
mai deeh teei pliah an pauh nawh yum kah.
22 In mawh kaing pa ao min pa yuh Pi-na-ya.
Nawh mawh ka daux “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux” tix kaux,
23 mai mawh pui pa ao min tix kaux,
viang mawh nan,
krax pa ao min nawh kawn ang nyang baox lheu khaing “pui ting kaing loe kaux” an.
Ta-wue tawx nawh mawh cao giex kaing pui naing heue.
24-39 “Pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux” tang kaux khankix mawh:
Ya-sha-he pa mawh pux Yaw-ya.
Yi-leu-ha-nang pa mawh pui Paw-li-haung kawn simex Taw-to.
Sa-ma mai Yi-li-cia pa mawh pui Ha-luix.
Si-li-see pa mawh pui Pha-leu-thi.
Yi-la pa mawh pui Thi-keu-yax kawn simex Yi-ci.
Ya-pi-yi-shiex pa mawh pui Ya-na-thu.
Mi-peung-nai pa mawh pui Hu-sa.
Sa-meung pa mawh pui Ya-heux.
Ma-ha-lai pa mawh pui Ni-thaw-fax.
Si-lix pa mawh pui Ni-thaw-fax kawn simex Pa-na.
Yi-thaix pa mawh pa kaoh khaing ceu pui Piien-ya-ming pui Ci-pi-yax kawn simex Li-paix.
Pi-na-ya pa mawh pui Pi-la-toeng.
Si-thaix pa mawh pui ngawd Cia-seu.
Ya-pi Ya-peung pa mawh pui Ya-la-pa.
Ya-see-ma-fu pa mawh pui Pa-lu-mi.
Yi-li-ya-ha-pa pa mawh pui Sa-peung.
Kawn simex Ya-sang khankix.
Yaw-na-tang.
Sa-ma pa mawh pui Ha-la.
Ya-si-ang pa mawh pui Ha-la kawn simex Sa-la.
Yi-li-fa-liex pa mawh pui Ma-cia kawn simex Ya-ha-paix.
Yi-liien pa mawh pui Ci-lawx kawn simex Ya-si-to-fu.
Si-see-lai pa mawh pui Cia-mix.
Pha-lai pa mawh pui Ya-pa.
Yi-cia pa mawh pui Saw-pa kawn simex Na-tang.
Pa-ni pa mawh ceu pui Cia-teux.
Si-leux pa mawh pui Ya-meung.
Na-ha-lai pa mawh pui Pi-lux.
(Nawh mawh pa klawm kraung naing tang Yaw-ya kawn simex Si-lu-ya.)
Yi-la mai Cia-lix pa mawh pui Yi-thie.
Wu-li-yax pa mawh pui Heux.
Khawm uik kix koe tix ngoe aliah kaux heue.
