Ta-wue taoh ka veng Ci-yi-la
23
Ta-wue mhong krai pui puing pui Fe-li-see ka veng Ci-yi-la,
mai hoik tud rhim ka nghox koi pa khraox teei kix hoek.
2 Nawh tom khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux khaw tix hu puing ka pui Fe-li-see lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix hu puing ka kix,
mai taoh teem ka veng Ci-yi-la heue.”
3 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa ot greem Ta-wue ah nin ka nawh:
“Ex ot ka Yo-tax mai kawn koe krax lhat krax graung,
phan ex kawn hu puing ka pui Fe-li-see ka veng Ci-yi-la,
ang nie mawh pa kied ting lhat lie?”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue baox khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex tom ah nin ka nawh:
“Hu ka veng Ci-yi-la heue,
aux tag kieh maix pex ka pui Fe-li-see.”
5 Ta-wue mai pui ot greem nawh tom hu puing ka pui Fe-li-see tau ka veng Ci-yi-la.
Kix hoik pauh ka pui koeng tom nie,
mai baox pon giex ka khawng eei pui koeng khankix.
Ta-wue tom hoik taoh ka veng an yuh nin heue.
6 Yam hoik to hu kawn simex Ya-si-mi-leux pa kaux Ya-pi-ya-tha ka veng Ci-yi-la peung ka rhawm Ta-wue,
nawh hoik viex ka Yi-fu-teu heue.
7 Sao-law hoik tawng Ta-wue hoek ka veng Ci-yi-la,
tom ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik ap nawh ka daux tawm taix aux.
Ta-wue to hoek ka veng pa koe guh,
pa koe siviex,
pa koe jhong,
mai awm hoik tang tawx nawh to kaux tix yum.”
8 Kheu nan,
Sao-law tom khrawm ka pui naing,
mai puing ka veng Ci-yi-la,
lai lawm ka Ta-wue mai pui naing nawh khankix heue.
9 Ta-wue hoik jiang tix tawng grawng sang puing Sao-law ka nawh,
mai nawh tom ah nin ka cao giex kaing li pa kaux Ya-pi-ya-tha:
“Viex Yi-fu-teu hoek tin.”
10 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux hoik nghiet tix mhong Sao-law hoik rien tix sang hoek ka Ci-yi-la,
kheu son aux pa mawh jao siliex maix son sang yuh laux ka veng in.
11 Pui haktiex Ci-yi-la sang tawx ang kix tawx aux ka Sao-law?
Aux mhong Sao-law sang hoek,
nawh chix hoek tete lie?
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
khiox khaw maix pawk ka lox aux liak!”
Simiang Siyiex pawk ah nin:
“Sao-law sang hoek.”
12 Ta-wue baox chawk ah nin:
“Kheu nan,
pui haktiex Ci-yi-la sang tawx ang kix tawx aux mai pui cie aux ka Sao-law lie?”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kix sang tawx heue.”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue mai pui naing cie nawh been liah yieh kaux lih hu khaing Ci-yi-la dum mawx dum an,
mai vung viang hu ku dauh ku dee.
Sao-law mhong hoik to Ta-wue khaing Ci-yi-la,
tom ang lai hu puing ka dee an heue.
Ta-wue ot ka daux tiag
14 Ta-wue kawn ca mox tix ot ka baoh daux tiag Si-fu.
Sao-law kawn ca nied tix sawg ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex ang ap Ta-wue ka nawh.
15 Ta-wue hoik tawng Sao-law but rhawm tix sang pauh ka nawh.
Yam hoek dex Ta-wue ka Ha-liex daux tiag Si-fu,
16 Yaw-na-tang hoek ka nawh,
krai grawng sang teem coi Siyiex ka nawh,
17 mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Paw vi lhat!
Keeing aux Sao-law ang doh pon pauh ka maix;
nawh hoik tawng sidawng,
hu sivoe ngai sax maix tag sang yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kaing pa yuh aux tag sang ot ka greem maix heue.”
18 aKiex ra kaux cox tiex tu tix bawg ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
son pa kleuk paox tix mawh paox grawm daux ju tix.
Ta-wue ot ka Ha-liex;
mai Yaw-na-tang deeh ing ka nyiex tix heue.
19 Koe pui kaoh khaing Si-fu hoek ka Ci-pi-yax ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Ta-wue mox tix ot ka daux haktiex yix,
plak seh daux tiag Yo-tax ka Ha-liex piang gawng Ha-ci-la.
20 Yix hoik tawng Vawng kied simie tix giex ka nawh;
viex maix hoek ka haktiex yix,
mai yix tag sang teem Vawng giex ka nawh heue.”
21 Sao-law ah nin:
“Pex hoik yuh krax pa mhawm ka aux.
Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka pex!
22 Hu heue,
baox si kaw sidawng,
ot nawh dee mawx,
mawh mawx pa yaox ka nawh.
Aux mhong kied lhak khoik nawh.
23 Pex hu si kaw dee mox nawh tix sidawng,
mai nied tix hoek krai ka aux dum mawx dum an,
aux tom hu tau mai pex.
Phan nawh kawn ot ka daux haktiex in,
die aux hoik tauh sawg ka daux ceu pui Yo-tax khawm uik,
aux kawx tag sang sawg tom yaox tix nawh heue.”
24 Mawh kheu nan,
pui in kix yaog tix hu,
mai ran ing ka Si-fu sivoe Sao-law.
Ta-wue mai pui pa ot greem nawh mox tix ot daux tiag Ma-yuing ka ngawd kroh daux tiag plak seh Yo-tax.
25 Sao-law thung pui cie tix hu sawg ka Ta-wue;
Ta-wue mhong kah,
nawh tom to mox tix daux tiag Ma-yuing ka plak khaix rang simaox ting.
Sao-law hoik jiang tix tawng blawng hu Ta-wue,
nawh tom to tix leue.
26 Sao-law mai pui cie nawh ot ka rang simaox plak in;
Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh ot ka plak an.
Pui cie Sao-law plak in hoik dex ka kix,
dex sang pon giex ka kix,
kix nied yaog tix to.
27 Jiet caw bawg yam an,
koe kawn cau tix kaux hoek,
mai krai ka Sao-law ah nin:
“Viex maix nied deeh ing!
Pui Fe-li-see laig tix puing ka daux meung haktiex ex!”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law tom naox tix ang lai leue ka Ta-wue,
mai deeh ing puing ka pui Fe-li-see.
Mawh kheu nan,
dee an tom kok mawh “Rang simaox pa kieh tang ot.”
29 Ta-wue lih hu khaing dee an,
haok ka haktiex Yin Ci-ti,
mai mox tix dee an heue.
