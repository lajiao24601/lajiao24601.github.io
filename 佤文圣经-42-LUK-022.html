Krax keud tix sang pauh ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.1–5; Lai Ma-kheu 14.1–2; Lai Yaw-hang 11.45–53)
22
aPoi Tik-su-saix (mai baox kok mawh Poi Luat-luan) dex sang hoek.
2 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix,
kheu kied lhat kix ka pui hun,
kix tom keud grawng sang mox tix pauh ka Ye-sux.
Yo-tax cu tix coh ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.14–16; Lai Ma-kheu 14.10–11)
3 Yam an Sa tan laig ka daux rhawm Yo-tax pui Cia-liiex.
(Yo-tax mawh kawn cau Ye-sux ka daux kao ra kaux.)
4 Yo-tax hu kum tau mai simiang cao giex kaing li mai khawm pui pau ka nyiex buan khankix,
grawng sang tawx Ye-sux ka kix.
5 Kix kied gaux rhawm,
mai baox cu tix tawx mau ka nawh.
6 Yo-tax hoik cu kah,
mai jauh tix sawg ka bawg yam pa khaw,
yam ang pui hun yaox sang tawx Ye-sux ka kix heue.
Rien khre ka priex bo Poi Luat-luan
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.17–25; Lai Ma-kheu 14.12–21; Lai Yaw-hang 13.21–30)
7 Lheu khaing ka daux Poi Tik-su-saix,
bawg yam pa pauh ka kawn yung Poi Luat-luan hoik hoek.
8 Ye-sux ploi Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang hu,
mai tawx lox ka kiex ah nin:
“Pax hu rien khre priex bo Poi Luat-luan son ex heue.”
9 Kiex tom chawk ah nin:
“Sang kieh maix yiex hu rien khre ka dee mawx lie?”
10 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pax laig ka daux veng,
tag sang yaox pui tix kaux,
pa viex ka rawm tix kawng,
pax tom hawt ka nawh,
hoek ka nyiex pa laig nawh daux an,
11 tom chawk ka cao nyiex an ah nin:
‘Sara chawk,
lawg nyiex pa sang ih nawh mai pui gau priex bo Poi Luat-luan koe dee mawx lie?’
12 Nawh tag sang viex pax jhak ka lawg nyiex ting pa hoik khre mhawm ka jan plak piang;
pax tom rien khre dee an heue.”
13 Kiex hu,
can ceu pa hoik yaox kiex jiet awm pa hoik krai Ye-sux yuh nan,
kiex tom rien khre ka priex bo Poi Luat-luan heue.
Priex bo cie Simiang Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.26–30; Lai Ma-kheu 14.22–26; Keu-ling-to phuk tix 11.23–25)
14 Bawg yam ih priex bo hoik hoek,
Ye-sux sawm;
kawn cau kawx ngawm tau mai nawh.
15 Nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Aux dawg simie tix ih priex bo Poi Luat-luan in tau mai pex sivoe sang kham krax kha rhawm.
16 Aux krai ka pex,
lheu khaing krox priex bo in koe dee bleeh tete te lai ka daux meung Siyiex,
aux ang doh lai baox ih ka heue.”
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux teei kawk,
mai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex teei kawk in guah tix nyaux.
18 Aux krai ka pex,
jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai lheu khaing krox meung Siyiex lih hoek,
aux ang doh lai baox nyaux ka plai in.”
19 Khaix an,
nawh teei ka khao mun,
yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
mai si vit kah,
guah tix tawx ka kix,
ah nin:
“In mawh to kaux aux,
mawh pa hoik tawx son pex.
Pex yuh yuh nin kieh mawh pa doh keud ka aux heue.”
20 cKhaix sawm,
nawh baox teei ka kawk yuh nan nang,
mai ah nin:
“Kawk in mawh sijax mukheu pa khraox Siyiex,
hoik mawh pa teei nham aux pa jauh son pex pa yuh ka heue.b
21 d“Pex jhak,
pui pa coh aux an hoik ngawm tix sawm tau mai aux heue!
22 Kawn kawn pui viang hoik sang hawt tix kham krax yum awm pa tawx Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa coh ka nawh an tag sang hoek krax laux krax leeing heue!”
23 Kix ra lai chawk paox tix ah nin:
“Mawh pui mawx pa sang yuh kaing pa yuh nin lie?”
Pui gau thung thiang paox tix mawh mawx pa ting
24 ePui gau khankix thung thiang paox tix,
ka daux kix mawh mawx pa blawn ting.
25 fYe-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Simiang piang haktiex hoik koe akhuang tix kuang ka kawn pui,
mai simiang pui hoik kok kix mawh cao taoh teem;
26 gviang mawh nan,
pex ang khaw tix yuh nan.
Ka daux pex pui pa ting an,
tag caw tieh tix lih mawh pui pa awm kawn nyawm yuh nan;
pui pa mawh simiang tag caw tix lih awm siliex yuh nan.
27 hMawh pui pa ngawm tix ih nyaux pa ting,
mawh pui pa khre ka nawh an pa ting lie?
Hoik mawh pui pa ngawm tix ih nyaux an pa ting.
Khaix an aux ot ka daux pex,
mai hoik mawh pui pa khre ka pex heue.
28 “Yam ot aux ka daux krax cheuk cheu,
pex hoik ot tau mai aux tom si dauh.
29 Yam in tag sang tawx siawp sibrawm pa hoik tawx keeing aux ka aux ka pex nang.
30 iPex tag sang ngawm tix ih nyaux tau mai aux ka daux meung cie aux,
khaix an tag sang ngawm tix prawng ca ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu ka daux piang dang ngeung.”
Ye-sux grox krai ang Pi-teux tawng yung ka tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.31–35; Lai Ma-kheu 14.27–31; Lai Yaw-hang 13.36–38)
31 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Si-meung,
Si-meung!
Sa tan hoik pon tix hoek nyaw glaw ka pex,
nawh tag sang grheeng ka pex awm grheeng pui ka nghox ca yuh nan.
32 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik khiox khaw son pex,
mai ang kieh pex grai krax yum rhawm.
Khaix hoik veeh maix tix hoek ka aux,
tag caw tix joh jhan ka pux aik tix khankix heue.”
33 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux cu tix laig daux thawng tau mai maix,
sang yum tau mai maix heue!”
34 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pi-teux,
aux krai ka maix:
ka daux in singaix,
sivoe sang ux ia,
maix tag thiang tix ang tawng ka aux loe bawg heue.”
Hau mau, bau kraung, vaig
35 jYe-sux baox ah nin ka kix:
“Aux ploi pex lih hu sivoe an,
mai ang cu pex viex ka hau mau,
bau kraung,
mai kiiep,
hoik cud pex ka ceu pa tix lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Ang cud heue.”
36 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Viang mawh nan,
koe hau mau mai bau kraung dee mawx yam in,
tag caw tix viex hawt tix;
pui pa ang koe vaig gawn,
tag caw tix coh kraung tix liag hoek tix mag.
37 kAux krai ka pex,
phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ah nin:
‘Hoik dia nawh tix ot ka daux pui phi map’ gum lox in tag sang lih tix mawh ka to kaux aux.
Tete te lai,
phan mawh pa tiiem grawh grawng pa dex dawt mai aux,
mai hoik lih tix mawh khawm uik heue.”
38 Pui gau ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
maix jhak,
dee in vaig gawn hoik koe ra mag heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Hoik kub heue!”
Ye-sux rawg buan ka gawng sime
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.36–46; Lai Ma-kheu 14.32–42)
39 Ye-sux lih khaing daux veng,
mai ca hu ka gawng sime awm hu tix jheeng jheeng;
pui gau khankix hu tau mai nawh.
40 Hoek ka haktiex dee an,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw tix rawg buan,
son ang sang glup ka daux krax nyaw krax glaw heue.”
41 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux lih hu khaing kix,
mai singai ot khaing kix been glang pon nyak loi simaox hu,
nawh grux ngawng tix rawg buan,
42 ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
phan maix cu,
gah kawk pa song in hu.
Khaix an paw vi hawt tix yuh awm alo aux,
kieh mawh ka daux alo maix heue.”
43 Koe kawn cau Siyiex lih jhi nawh yaox tix khaing rao ma,
mai baox thiem thie riang rao ka nawh.
44 Ye-sux nied tix sau riang rao tix rawg buan ka daux krax pa kied laux rhawm tix,
rawm lauh nawh hoik jauh cawd piang tiex awm jauh nham yuh nan heuel.
45 Khaix hoik rawg buan,
Ye-sux kaoh,
deeh hoek ka dee ot pui gau tix dee an,
mai yaox grawng hoik laig it kix kheu kied laux rhawm tix.
46 Nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai laig it lie?
Kaoh rawg buan,
son ang sang glup ka daux krax nyaw glaw heue.”
Giex ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.47–56; Lai Ma-kheu 14.43–50; Lai Yaw-hang 18.3–11)
47 Yam kawn si kah Ye-sux,
koe pui tix cub hoek.
Yo-tax pa mawh ka daux kawn cau kao ra kaux tix kaux viex kix hoek,
nawh hu sivoe sang ngheet ka Ye-sux heue.
48 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Yo-tax,
maix teei jheeh pa ngheet tix coh ka kawn kawn pui lie?”
49 Pui gau pa ot tau mai Ye-sux khankix yaox grawng in,
kix tom ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ex chix tix teei vaig gawn mug lie”
50 Koe pui tix kaux ka daux kix yaog vaig ka siliex cao giex kaing li pa ting an,
mai thak ka yhaok khawn dawm nawh dut heue.
51 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Paw vi chuh kraung naing heue!”
Nawh tom nyeu taix tix pu pi ka yhaok pui an,
mai pai moi heue.
52 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka simiang cao giex kaing li mai pui pau nyiex buan,
mai ka kuad song pa hoek tix giex nawh:
“Pex viex vaig khaox hoek giex ka aux,
mai kieh aux awm pui tud pui rhim lie?
53 mAux hoik ot tau mai pex ka daux nyiex buan jheeng jheeng,
pex ang bax tawx taix tix;
viang mawh nan,
in hoik mawh bawg yam pa nieb pex pon yuh tix,
pa faik hoik giex kaing heue.”
Pi-teux ang tawng ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.57–58, 69–75; Lai Ma-kheu 14.53–54, 66, 72; Lai Yaw-hang 18.12–18, 25–27)
54 Bawg yam in,
kix giex ka Ye-sux,
mai viex nawh hu ka daux nyiex cao giex kaing li pa ting;
Pi-teux ot dee singai hawt ka nawh.
55 Kix phlu tix co ngu ka biang brawk,
pui hun ngawm tix lai lawm ka ngu,
Pi-teux kawx pu plaik tix laig ka daux kix.
56 Koe bun siliex tix kaux yaox Pi-teux ngawm tix co ngu,
mai dong ngai tix ka nawh ah nin:
“Pui in mawh paox pa hu tau mai nawh heue!”
57 Pi-teux thiang ka ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh bun in,
aux ang tawng ka nawh heue!”
58 Khaix an ang bax koe miex glang tix,
baox koe pui pa yaox ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix kawx hoik mawh paox pa hu tau mai kix heue!”
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh pui in,
aux ang mawh heue!”
59 Baox koe been tix taix nari,
pui tang kaux baox ah nin:
“Ang lud pie ceei kawx,
pui in hoik mawh paox pa hu tau mai nawh,
mawh kheu nawh kawx hoik mawh pui Cia-li-lix heue!”
60 Viang mawh nan,
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh pui in,
aux ang tawng hoik krai maix lox ceu pa tix heue!”
Lox pa krai nawh ang bax uik,
ia hoik ux.
61 Simiang Ye-sux veeh to kaux tix,
mai dong ngai tix jhak ka Pi-teux;
Pi-teux tom doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sivoe sang ux ia in singaix,
maix tag krai ang tix tawng ka aux loe bawg.”
62 Pi-teux tom lih hu,
mai yiiem heue.
Ye-sux kham krax pa map nai pui
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.67–68; Lai Ma-kheu 14.65)
63 Pui pa pau ka Ye-sux map nai ka nawh,
tawk ka nawh.
64 Kix tawb ka ngai nawh,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix krai yaox,
mawh mawx pa lauh ka maix lie?”
65 Kix baox krai lox pa map nai ka nawh tom nie heue.
Kham krax prawng ka bang kum pui Yo-thaix
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.59–66; Lai Ma-kheu 14.55–64; Lai Yaw-hang 18.19–24)
66 Yam riang praix,
kuad song pui Yo-thaix,
simiang cao giex kaing li,
mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khrawm paox tix tau,
mai baox viex Ye-sux hoek ka bang kum pui Yo-thaix.
67 Kix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Krai ka yix,
mawh ang maix mawh Khrit?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Viang aux hoik krai ka pex kawx,
pex tag ang yum rhawm ka aux;
68 phan aux chawk pex ka grawng kaing ceu pa tix,
pex kawx taug ang pawk.
69 Viang mawh nan,
jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai,
kawn kawn pui tag sang ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm.”
70 Kix khawm uik ah nin:
“Yuh nin,
maix hoik mawh kawn simex Siyiex lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pex krai hoik mawh aux heue!”
71 Mawh kheu nan,
kix ah nin:
“Ex ang siao lai lo ka mhai ceu pa tid tix heue!
Ex hoik mhong ka lox pa krai nawh lih khaing jheeh tix heue!”
