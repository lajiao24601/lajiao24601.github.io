(5) Krax laux ka jhawm saux preng
9
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix hu ka simiang Ai-cix,
krai nawh mhong,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Si-paw-lai ah yuh nin:
‘Ploi pui cie aux lih hu,
son sang hu kix yuh kaing krub ka aux.
2 Phan maix kawn thiang ang kieh kix lih hu,
3 aux sang ploi jhawm saux krax laux leeing,
pa kied lhat graung pui ka hoek ciyang ka maix:
khawng brie maix khawm uik,
pa mawh brung,
la,
ka-la-uk,
moi,
yung,
mai pex khankix tag sang pon ka jhawm saux preng ting.
4 Aux tag sang kieh khawng brie ceu pui Yi-seu-liex mai khawng brie pui Ai-cix tang siyox,
khawng brie ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag ang yaox tix yum tix mu kawx.
5 Aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik tat tix deuh,
bawg yam pa sax kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh heue.’ ”
6 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
Simiang Siyiex yuh awm lox pa hoik krai tix,
kieh khawng brie pui Ai-cix yum khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
khawng brie ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ang yum tix mu kawx.
7 Vawng ploi pui hu jhak,
mai tawng ka ang khawng brie ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix yum tix mu kawx.
Viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi nawh ca kawn kiien,
mai ang cu tix ploi kawn pui hu heue.
(6) Krax laux ka saux muad
8 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng:
“Pax teei nyaox khaing daux glah ngu,
mai kieh Maw-si rhuat plak sivoe ngai Vawng ka daux rao ma.
9 Phawng nyaox in tag sang awm si preue mai tauh ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik,
si preue in tom ka pui mai khawng brie,
mai lih mawh jhawm saux muad ka piang heue.”
10 aKheu nan Maw-si mai Ya-loeng teei nyaox khaing glah ngu,
mai hoek ka sivoe ngai Vawng.
Maw-si rhuat nyaox an plak rao ma;
nyaox an lih mawh jhawm saux muad ka piang pui mai khawng brie khankix.
11 Cao chix si meed khankix ang lai pon hoek sivoe ngai Maw-si,
mawh kheu jhawm saux muad koe ka piang kix awm koe ka piang pui Ai-cix tang kaux.
12 Viang mawh nan,
awm lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex,
nawh kieh rhawm simiang Ai-cix kiien,
mai ang nghiet ka lox Maw-si mai Ya-loeng heue.
(7) Krax laux ka pre
13 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka Maw-si:
“Pa sax pon ngawb cao cao maix hu ka Vawng,
mai krai ka nawh,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Si-paw-lai ah yuh nin:
‘Ploi pui cie aux lih hu,
son sang hu kix yuh kaing krub ka aux.
14 Bawg in phan maix kawn baox ang nghiet,
aux tag ang joeng ciyang jhawm saux preng ka piang siliex maix,
ka piang pui cie maix khaing,
khawm ka maix kawx sang ciyang kah.
Yuh nin,
maix tom tawng ka piang haktiex ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa awm aux.
15 Phan aux yuh jhawm saux preng ciyang maix mai kawn pui cie maix khankix,
pex hoik dawg grai grup khaing haktiex khawm uik heue.
16 bViang mawh nan,
son sang jhi aux maix yaox riang rao tix,
aux tom kawn deuh ka ju maix im,
son sang kieh ceu kaux aux tauh hu ka piang haktiex heue.
17 Kheu nan maix ca kawn ting rhawm,
mai ang ploi pui cie aux hu.
18 “ ‘Pa sax bawg yam in,
aux tag sang kieh pre lih;
ka daux Ai-cix,
tom yam jauh ang nyang sax lih pre pa ting yuh nin tix bawg kawx.
19 Yam in maix hu sawg dee sang khrawm mox khawng brie maix tix mai can pa koe daux kaung khankix khawm uik.
Pre tag sang toi pui mai can khawng brie pa koe daux kaung khankix,
mai kix tag sang yum uik heue.’ ”
20 Can siliex Vawng khankix lhat si yaung kheu gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex,
mai khrawm ka siliex cawng tix,
khawng brie tix laig ka daux nyiex.
21 Viang mawh nan,
kawn koe pui tix blah ang nghiet ka gum lox pa ah Simiang Siyiex,
mai ca kawn kieh siliex cawng tix mai khawng brie tix ot daux kaung heue.
22 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix yaog taix tix nyeu ka plak rao ma,
ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik pre tag sang lih koe kah,
mai lih ka piang kawn pui,
ka piang khawng brie mai ka piang khaox ox rib riem khankix.”
23 Kheu nan Maw-si yaog khaox gix tix ka plak rao ma,
mai Simiang Siyiex ploi lox si num praix,
pre mai sah lih ka piang haktiex.
24 cKheu nan,
pre ting mu tau mai sah pa jhan riang lih hoek tau;
in mawh pre pa kied ting ka daux haktiex Ai-cix,
tom yam jauh pa ang nyang sax koe tix bawg kawx.
25 Pre khankix dheem ka can pa koe daux kaung ma haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik,
khawm pui,
khawng brie,
nghox koi ka daux kaung,
mai can khaox ox khankix.
26 Mawh ngie daux haktiex Keu-sang,
dee ot dee koe pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
pa ang pre lih ka heue.
27 Simiang Ai-cix kok Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hoek ka tix,
ah nin:
“Bawg in aux hoik yuh pa phit lud.
Aux mai kawn pui cie aux khankix hoik lud.
Simiang Siyiex hoik khaw.
28 Maix rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex!
Aux ang lai pon kham gee krax laux leeing pre mai pa si num praix!
Aux tag ploi pex hu.
Pex ang pon tix lai ot tin heue.”
29 Maw-si ah nin ka nawh:
“Khaix hoik lih aux khaing daux veng in,
aux tag yaog taix tix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai lox si num praix tag yien,
pre khankix kawx tag ang lai baox lih.
Yuh nin,
maix tag tawng haktiex in mawh cie Simiang Siyiex.
30 Viang mawh nan,
maix mai siliex maix khankix,
aux tawng pex kawn ang nyang lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
31 Khaox gan mai nghox ca pa lang mu hoik laux leeing ka pre;
mawh kheu yam an,
nghox ca pa lang mu hoik kaux,
mai khaox gan khankix kawx hoik pruh tai.
32 Viang mawh nan,
nghox ca ang nyang bax laux leeing,
kheu ang bax kaux heue.
33 Maw-si lih hu khaing simiang Ai-cix,
lih khaing daux veng mai yaog taix tix rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Khaix an lox si num praix,
lhiex pre khankix yien.
34 Vawng yaox ka in khawm uik,
nawh deeh baox phit lud;
nawh mai siliex nawh khankix deeh baox kiien rhawm.
35 Awm gum lox pa hoik dawg krai Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
rhawm rhi simiang Ai-cix ca kawn kiien,
mai ang ploi kawn pui Yi-seu-liex hu heue.
