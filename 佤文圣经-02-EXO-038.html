Yuh khu dee tan
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 27.1–8)
38
Nawh yuh khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka khaox Cing-heu-huang.
Khu an kieh koe pon pak,
plak lang ra kongjhee ra kongchuing,
plak vieh ra kongjhee ra kongchuing,
plak lhaong tix kongjhee loe kongchuing.
2 Nawh yuh rung pa bhlien ka piang rung khu dee tan pon rung,
kieh si doe tix tau mai khu dee tan tix mu khaing,
mai om ka tawng.
3 Nawh yuh kraung pa siao son khu dee tan khawm uik pa mawh:
pan,
jauk,
klong,
cuan ciie nex,
mai aw ngu;
kraung in kix khawm uik hoik raug ka tawng.
4 Nawh yuh rub tawng tix mu son khu dee tan,
mai nih ot ka vung viang nyoi khu dee tan plak greem,
tom hoek ka nyoi khu plak piang.
5 Nawh baox yuh daux jhawk tawng pon mu son khaox grawng,
mai kap ot ka rung khu khawm pon daog;
6 caw tix yuh khaox grawng khu ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka tawng,
7 mai chup laig ka daux jhawk khu khawm ra plak.
Khu dee tan mawh pa yuh ka phia,
kieh daux sigreung yi heue.
Yuh pan tawng
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 30.18)
8 Nawh yuh pan tawng mai jaong dee deuh pan tix mu,
ka si nax liag pa siao bun,
pa ot tix yuh kaing krub khankix ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man heue.
Yuh brawk nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 27.9–19)
9 Nawh teei man tie pa hoik taing ka man keei sum mai yuh die vung viang nyiex gox buan Simiang Siyiex kah,
kieh mawh brawk.
Man tie plak seh plak lang koe tipon pon kongjhee,
10 kieh mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong tawng tix nga gawng pa giex kaux,
goik pa ot ka piang rong khaox an mai lat mawh pa yuh ka mau.
11 Man tie ka plak laong hoik awm paox,
plak lang koe tipon pon kongjhee,
mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong tawng tix nga gawng pa giex kaux.
Goik pa ot ka piang rong khaox an mai lat mawh pa yuh tix ka mau.
12 Man tie ka plak klip singaix koe lang tix nga ra kongjhee,
mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong kao gawng pa giex kaux,
goik pa ot ka piang rong khaox an mai lat khankix hoik mawh pa yuh tix ka mau.
13 Ka plak lih singaix,
ka diang siviex dee laig daux brawk,
man tie plak vieh koe tix nga ra kongjhee.
14-15 Ka daux laig ka diang siviex khawm ra plak,
man tie plak vieh koe liah kongjhee liah kongchuing,
mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong loe gawng pa giex kaux.
16 Can man tie vung viang biang brawk khankix,
hoik mawh pa taing ka man keei sum khawm uik.
17 Daux jhup jaong rong khaox mawh pa yuh ka tawng;
goik,
lat,
khawm pa hoik om ka piang toe rong khankix,
mawh pa om ka mau khawm uik.
Rong ot vung viang brawk khawm uik mawh pa lang lad kaux ka rong mau.
18 Man tie daux doh brawk mawh pa yuh ka man keei sum mai man pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
mai man pa raoh rawt pa hoik taing tix ka keei hauk yung,
ka piang koe lai tai,
plak viex dim kongjhee,
plak lhaong ra kongjhee,
been lhaong khaw mai man tie biang brawk,
19 mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong pon gawng pa giex kaux.
Goik pa koe ka piang rong,
toh pa hoik om tix,
mai lat khankix mawh mau khawm uik.
20 Can jim pa siao daux nyiex gox buan man mai vung viang biang brawk mawh pa raug ka tawng khawm uik heue.
Can khri pa siao son nyiex gox buan man
21 Khlawp gum lox ping nyap ra khlawp ka daux nyiex gox buan man pa hoik yuh ka khri been miex ding,
hoik tiiem ot plak greem khawm uik.
Grawng ca rang in mawh pa hoik hawt ka gum lox tawm Maw-si,
mawh kawn simex Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li pa kaux Yi-tha-ma pa thung ka pui Li-we rien khre tix heue.
22 Kaing pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mawh ceu pui Yo-tax kawn saux Hu-eux kawn simex Wu-lix pa kaux Pi-sha-liex pa hawt tix yuh ku ceu heue.
23 Pui pa teem coi nawh,
Ya-si-sha-maw kawn simex Ya-heu-li-ya-pawx ceu pui Tang,
nawh mawh pui pa chix yuh kaing taix,
mai pa chix yuh riang pak peung kaing,
mai pa chix taing man keei hauk yung pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt mai man keei sum heue.
24 Can khri pa tan ka daux kaing jhaw son nyiex pa singax singeh,
hawt awm diag tong pa siao ka nyiex pa singax singeh,
khawm uik koe tix rheng kongcing.
25 aMau pa hoik koh khaing daux pa tiiem ca rang lhang nyiex pui,
hawt awm diag tong pa siao ka nyiex pa singax singeh,
khawm uik mawh loe rheng pon yieh tix ngoe kongcing.
26 bDiag dong in mawh pa hoik hawt awm tiiem ca rang lhang nyiex pui khawm uik,
pui ku kaux tawx ka mau,
hawt awm diag tong pa siao ka nyiex pa singax singeh.
Pui pa tiiem ka ca rang,
tix nga num haok plak piang khawm uik koe tix gliah mheen loe rheng phuan yieh tix phuan kaux.
27 Kix yuh nyiex gox buan man ka mau loe rheng pon yieh kongcing,
ka man tie tix yieh daux jhup jaong mau,
tix mu been cien daux jhup jaong mau koe tix ngoe pon kongcing.
28 Mau pa deuk tix ngoe kongcing,
Pi-sha-liex teei om ka lat rong,
goik,
mai rao rong khankix.
29 Tawng pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
khawm uik koe ra rheng pon yieh tix nga phuan kongcing.
30 Nawh teei tawng in yuh daux jhup jaong diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
khu tawng,
rub tawng ka piang khu,
kraung siao ka piang khu khawm uik,
31 daux jhup jaong vung viang biang brawk,
mai ka diang siviex daux laig ka biang brawk,
khawm jim pa siao ka nyiex gox buan man mai vung viang biang brawk heue.
