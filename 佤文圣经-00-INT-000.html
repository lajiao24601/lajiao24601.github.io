Krai Grawng Thut Lih
Grawng pa pon thut Phuk Lai Sigang Khri, hoik mawh kaing ting ceu pa hoik dawg simie, dawg keud pot jun pot ceu cao kawn khrit pa ot haktiex meung hawx tom rheeng num. Kied ting buan son Siyiex, yam in rug hoik pon thut tix lih mhawm mhawm, mai ex kied sidaing gaux rhawm kah. Khawm uik kieh mawh min plak ngai keeing ex Siyiex pa ot piang meung rao ma heue! Phuk Lai Sigang Khri in jauh tom yam jauh tix thut hoek tom pon tix thut lih, hoik mawh buan son Siyiex pa duk thung, hoik mawh krax muh krax rhawm "Num Li Ting Daux Meung Hawx Cong Kawx " mai "Num Li Ting Daux Veng Ting Yuing Nang" mai krax joh jhan teem coi "Cub Caw Pa Rom Tix Yuh Phuk Lai Sigang Khri" son pui, son sang kieh kawn paraog pui cao phat blawn chix tawng chix yung sidawng ka gum lox Siyiex, ex siao "Phuk Lai Sigang Khri Pa Thut Lih Ka Lox Hawx Singaix Baux Yam In," mawh kheu yie tix tawng nyawm tix si kah, blawn khaw tix siao ka son singaix baux ju in. Yix kawx hoik pon krax teem coi sara "Cub Caw Pa Rom Tix Yuh Phuk Lai Sigang Khri," mai hoik tiag tix mai Phuk Lai Sigang Khri pa prim (Lai Si-paw-lai mai Si-lax). Ceu kaux kawn pui mai ceu kaux haktiex kawx hoik siao hawt awm lox hawx khawm uik, mawh kheu hoik thut tix lih khaing lai hawx, kawn paraog pa ot meung hawx plak nie hoik tawng ka lox hawx, yuh nin hoik koe dee mhawm son ex tete heue.
