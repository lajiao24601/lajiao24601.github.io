Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Tix mai Phuk Ra mawh lai tix phuk khaing, yam thut ka lai Si-lax tag chix tix guah koe Phuk Tix mai Phuk Ra. Phuk Tix krai grawng pui Yi-seu-liex jauh yam singaix pa tieh cao prawng tix hoek ka bawg yam koe been pa sum simiang koe khankix. Meung pui Yi-seu-liex tieh tix hoik kieh mawh pui daux loe kaux pa mawh kaing ting Sa-mu-eu mawh cao prawng tix kaux pa nghoik khaix paox; Sao-law mawh Vawng ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa jauh tix ka daux tix kaux; Ta-wue yam mawh nawh simiang ka daux bawg yam singaix pa hoik luan, kaing sakse pa yuh Sa-mu-eu mai Sao-law hoik mawh tau heue.
Phuk lai in kaing ting hoik awm been ka daux phuk lai sijax mukheu pa prim khankix, pa krai grawng pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex tag pon tix pex, lieh lheu ka gum lox tawm Siyiex tag pon krax pa laux pa leeing. Grawng gum lox in hoik krai tix sidawng ka daux gum lox pa tawx Siyiex ka Yi-lix ah nin: “Aux tag sang siao ka pui pa yung yi ka aux khankix, mai tag tik vud ka pui pa map nai ka aux khankix heue.” (2.30)
Phuk lai in krai ka grawng kaing pa suk sag pui Yi-seu-liex yam sum kix meung tix lih koe. Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh simiang cao kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex tete, viang mawh nan, son sang naog ka rhawm pui haoh pui hun khankix, Simiang Siyiex tom laug simiang tix kaux son kix. Ka daux pa blawn ting ceu, hoik mawh Vawng mai kawn pui pa ot ka greem siawp sibrawm Siyiex mai ka greem prawng ca nawh (2.7–10). Ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex, can kawn pui khawm uik, ang lai guah pui hot pui mi, pui ku kaux hoik kia nawh awm paox tix heue.
Krai kaing
1. Sa-mu-eu yuh cao prawng son pui Yi-seu-liex (1.1–7.17)
2. Sao-law yuh simiang (8.1–10.27)
3. Bawg yam giex Sao-law ka kaing sivoe an (11.1–15.35)
4. Ta-wue mai Sao-law (16.1–30.31)
5. Pa yum Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh khankix (31.11–13)
