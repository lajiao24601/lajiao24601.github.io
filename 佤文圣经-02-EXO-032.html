Riang moi khri
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 9.6–29)
32
bPui Yi-seu-liex khankix yaox rheung ang Maw-si lih khaing piang gawng,
kix tom khrawm paox tix hoek ka sivoe ngai Ya-loeng,
mai ah nin:
“Ex ang tawng Maw-si pa thung ex lih khaing Ai-cix hoik si pup ka kaing ceu pa tix;
mawh kheu nan viex maix yuh siyiexa son ex tix kaux pa sang thung ka ex.”
2 Ya-loeng ah nin ka kix:
“Pex peeik kreem khri pa viex moeng pex mai kawn simex kawn bun pex ka yhaok tix khankix,
mai viex hoek ka aux.”
3 Pui khawm uik tom peeik ka kreem khri pa koe ka yhaok tix,
mai viex hoek ka sivoe ngai Ya-loeng.
4 cYa-loeng raug ka kreem khri an,
mai thawk ka daux riang pak peung kraung pa yuh tix,
yuh lih mawh riang moi khri tix mu heue.
Pui khankix ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
in mawh siyiex pa thung pex lih khaing Ai-cix heue!”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-loeng sum khu dee tan ka sivoe kawn moi an tix mu,
mai kok pao lox ah nin:
“Pa sax mawh singaix pa yuh poi son Simiang Siyiex.”
6 dNgaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
pui khankix tuk ka khawng brie hoek tix blah,
tix blah yuh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa gleeing,
tix blah yuh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim mai ih kah.
Kix ngawm tix ih nyaux,
mai yuh krax klieh krax nyiah heue.
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Nied lih khaing gawng,
mawh kheu pui pa hoik thung maix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix khankix,
hoik yuh phi map,
mai tik vud ka aux.
8 Kix hoik veeh tix khaing daux krax pa hoik tawm aux ka kix.
Kix hoik raug ka riang kawn moi khri tix mu,
mai krub kah,
tan lu kah.
Kix ah nin,
moi in mawh siyiex pa thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix.
9 Aux hoik tawng pui in kix mawh pui pa kied kin lieh lox tete!
10 Yam in,
maix paw lai tieb tie ka aux;
aux sang tawx krax pa song rhawm tix piang kix,
mai yuh kix laux leeing.
Aux tag sang kieh maix mai kawn praix maix lih mawh meung pa ting pa lhaong heue.”
11 eViang mawh nan,
Maw-si khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawh maix lai tawx krax pa song rhawm ka piang pui cie tix lie?
Pui in kix ang hoik mawh pa taoh maix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix ka riang rao pa ting pa lhaong tix lie?
12 Yuh ka mawh lai kieh pui ah,
mawh maix pa su tix thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix,
son sang kieh kix laux leeing ka gawng lie?
Khiox khaw maix naox ka krax pa song rhawm tix,
mai dee deeh ka rhawm rhi maix,
paw lai kieh krax kha rhawm hoek piang pui cie maix liak.
13 fKhiox khaw maix doh keud ka siliex maix Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux,
mai doh keud ka pa hoik tiex tu maix tix ah,
tag sang kieh doeh doe ceu pui kix haoh hun awm simuing piang rao ma long,
haktiex in kix khawm uik hoik ap tix tawx ka doeh doe ceu pui kix,
kieh mawh sin ngao kix leeing ju.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex deeh doh keud tieh loh rhawm tix,
ang lai tawx krax laux leeing pa ce tix sang tawx hoek ka piang pui cie tix heue.
15 Maw-si veeh tix lih khaing piang gawng,
teei ka khlawp simaox pa tiiem gum lox ping nyap ot ka ra khlawp,
pa hoik tiiem ka khawm ra plak.
16 Siyiex yuh ka khlawp simaox ra khlawp an,
mai tiiem gum lox ping nyap ot ka piang heue.
17 Yaw-su-yax mhong ka lox riag yo pui khankix,
nawh ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Aux mhong koe lox si num naing ka nyiex bang!”
18 Maw-si ah nin:
“Lox an ang awm lox si num pa pex khaing naing,
ang awm nang lox pa yiiem yo khaing ang pex daux naing,
lox an mawh lox rhax lai heue.”
19 Yam hoek Maw-si dex ka nyiex bang,
nawh yaox ka riang kawn moi mai pui pa yuh krax graoh krax klieh,
ang lai pon kham ka krax pa song rhawm tix,
tom taing tix tik khlawp simaox pa viex tix hoek ka piang tiex,
khlawp simaox an max phawng vid heue.
20 Nawh teei ka riang kawn moi pa hoik raug kix,
mai ceed ka daux ngu,
krid phawng,
rhuat ka piang rawm,
khaix an kieh pui Yi-seu-liex khankix nyaux kah.
21 Nawh ah nin ka Ya-loeng:
“Pui in kix yuh pa tix ka maix,
maix lai kieh kix yuh phi map pa ting yuh nin?”
22 Ya-loeng pawk lox ah nin:
“Paw vi song rhawm ka aux liak.
Maix hoik tawng pui in kix mawh pui pa but rhawm tix yuh pa caox pa caig.
Kix ah nin ka aux:
23 ‘Ex ang tawng Maw-si pa thung ex lih khaing Ai-cix,
hoik si pup ka kaing ceu pa tix,
mawh kheu nan viex maix yuh siyiex son ex,
pa sang thung ka ex.’
24 Aux nhie kix teei ka kreem khri tix viex hoek tawx ka aux;
pui pa koe kreem khri khankix,
tom teei tix tawx ka aux.
Aux sau kreem khri in kix ka daux ngu,
moi in tom lih heue!”
25 Maw-si yaox ka pa ang Ya-loeng lai pon kuang ka pui khankix,
mai ghram kix son sang kieh pui pa koeng nyiah kix.
26 Nawh tom cung ka sivoe diang siviex nyiex bang,
mai ah lox tix ting ah nin:
“Pui pa mawh cie Simiang Siyiex,
kieh nawh hoek ka aux plak in!”
Can pui Li-we khankix khrawm paox tix hoek ka nawh.
27 Nawh tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tawx lox nhie pui ku kaux tang gieb vaig ka nyoi tix,
jauh ka siviex in hoek ka siviex an,
lai ling hu tau ka nyiex bang khawm uik,
pauh ka pux aik pex,
paox grawm pex,
mai paox plawg paox nyawm pex.”
28 Pui Li-we khankix hawt ka gum lox tawx an,
ka daux tix singaix khaing pon pauh ka pui been loe rheng kaux.
29 Maw-si ah nin ka pui Li-we:
“Daux singaix,
pex hoik tan ka to kaux tixg,
yuh kaing pa mawh cao giex kaing li ka Simiang Siyiex,
hoik pauh ka kawn praix tix khawm pux aik tix khankix,
mawh kheu nan pex hoik pon buan son pa tawx Simiang Siyiex heue.”
30 Singaix khaix,
Maw-si ah nin ka pui khankix:
“Pex hoik yuh phi map pa ting.
Yam in aux sang deeh haok ka piang gawng dee ot Simiang Siyiex tan,
son sang deeh pon aux tix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex pluat ploi phi map pex.”
31 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex tan,
mai ah nin:
“Pui khankix hoik yuh phi map pa ting;
kix hoik raug riang siyiex khri mai krub kah.
32 hKhiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map kix liak.
Phan maix ang cu,
khiox khaw maix preet ceu kaux aux khaing daux phuk lai pa hoik tiiem pa mawh pui cie tix heue.”
33 Simiang Siyiex deeh pawk lox ah nin:
“Pui pa sang preet aux ceu kaux khaing daux phuk lai tix,
mawh pui pa phit ka aux khankix.
34 Yam in pex hu heue,
viex pui khankix hu ka dee pa hoik ciak ci aux.
Aux sang ploi kawn cau Siyiex ot sivoe ngai maix,
mai duk thung maix hu.
Viang mawh nan,
yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
aux sang ciyang ka phi map pa yuh kix heue.”
35 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ploi krax kha rhawm jhawm saux preng lih hoek ka piang pui khankix,
kheu jao kieh kix Ya-loeng raug riang kawn moi khri heue.
