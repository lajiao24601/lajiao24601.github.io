Kawn cau Siyiex mai krax laux leeing pa nghoik
15
Aux baox yaox mhai pa raux pa mox tang ceu tix ceu ka rao ma,
mhai an mawh pa ting pa daox deng tete.
Koe kawn cau Siyiex aliah kaux kuang ka krax laux leeing pa nghoik an aliah ceu,
mawh kheu sang tawx Siyiex krax pa song rhawm tix ka daux krax laux leeing in kix heue.
2 Aux baox yaox rawm sigrit sigra pa awm si liag tix daux,
pa klao tix tau mai ngu.
Aux kawx yaox pui tix blah nang;
kix hoik pex ka to sat an,
riang to sat an,
mai pui an pa kieh krai grawh grawng ngai lai.
Kix hoik cung ka praok rawm sigrit sigra si liag an,
mai giex ka ting jung lah pa tawx Siyiex ka kix,
3 arhax lox rhax Maw-si siliex Siyiex mai lox rhax kawn yung ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm eue,
kaing yuh maix kied mawh pa ting lhaong pa daox deng heue!
Simiang meung ka piang haktiex b eue,
daux krax cie maix kied mawh pa rauh see pa mawh pa meh heue!
4 cSimiang Siyiex eue,
pui mawx pon ang lhat graung yung yi ka maix lie?
Pui mawx ang yaog yaw ka ceu kaux maix lie?
Mawh ngie maix pa mawh pa singax singeh tix kaux khaing.
Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang hoek khawm uik,
mai krub ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
mawh kheu pa rauh pa see pa yuh maix hoik jhi tix lih sidawng heue.
5 dKhaix hoik kaing in,
aux yaox pa hoik baoh nyiex buan rao ma,
ka daux koe nyiex gox buan man pa lih hoek Siyiex.
6 Kawn cau Siyiex an kix aliah kaux,
pa kuang ka krax pa laux leeing an aliah ceu lih khaing daux nyiex buan,
ceep ka kraung man keei sum pa paing singax,
naok mag tix ka maox khri.
7 To sat pa im ju pon mu ka daux tix mu,
teei tix tawx klong khri aliah mu pa hoik prauk naog ka krax pa song rhawm Siyiex pa im leeing ju ka kawn cau Siyiex an kix aliah kaux.
8 eKa daux nyiex buan hoik naog ka min plak ngai pa hoek khaing Siyiex,
mai taox ngu pa hoek khaing siawp sibrawm,
sivoe sang uik ploi kawn cau Siyiex an kix aliah kaux krax pa laux leeing aliah ceu,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon laig ka daux nyiex buan heue.
