Beeh glawm ceu pui Keu-siax
4
Simiang Siyiex nhie Maw-si
2 hawt tix si kaw ka ca rang ceu pui Keu-siax pui Li-we khankix,
cub mawx cub an,
nyiex mawx nyiex an,
3 can simex pa koe ju tix ngoe num hoek tix phuan num pa pon yuh ka kaing jhaw ka daux nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex hoik tiiem ca rang lih khawm uik.
4 Kix yuh ka kaing jhaw pa dex pa dawt mai ceu pa kied singax singeh nong nong khankix heue.
5 Simiang Siyiex thung gau ka Maw-si,
yam yaog kix tix lih hu khaing dee bang tix,
Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix tag laig ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
teei man tie lih khaing sivoe teug sijax mukheu,
mai cox lhop ka piang teug sijax mukheu.
6 Kix tag sang lhop hak pa mhawm plang ka piang,
baox lhop man klai rao ma tix plah ka piang,
khaix an kix tag chup khaox glawm luan ka piang heue.
7 Kix tag sang tawm man pa klai rao ma tix plah ka piang pheen khan khao mun,
mai baox deuh pan,
cuan pa sau pa bhawng,
si ngien pa prauk plai,
mai kawk nyaux khankix ot ka piang.
Ka piang pheen tag caw tix khan khao mun ot ka jheeng jheeng.
8 Kix tag sang tawb man pa raoh rawt tix plah ka piang ceu an kix,
baox deeh tawb hak pa mhawm plang ka piang tix jan,
khaix an mai chup khaox glawm luan ka piang heue.
9 Kix tag sang teei man pa klai rao ma tix plah mai tawb ka piang jawk kawk ngu,
mai kawk ngu,
si giep ngu,
pan nyia ngu,
mai ka kraung ceu pa sang sau kix bee sime ot kah.
10 Kix tag siao hak pa mhawm plang kie ka kraung an kix,
deuh ot ka piang khaox glawm heue.
11 Khaix an,
kix tag sang tawm man pa klai rao ma tix plah ot ka piang khu khri dee tan,
mai lhop hak pa mhawm plang ot piang,
chup khaox glawm luan ka piang khaix heue.
12 Tag caw tix sang teei man pa klai rao ma kie ka kraung khrai pa koe ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh khawm uik,
khaix an tom baox tawb hak pa mhawm kah,
mai deuh ot ka piang khaox glawm.
13 Teei tix tik ka bee nyaox khaing piang khu dee tan,
mai tawb man lung pliex ka piang khu dee tan,
14 teei can kraung pa siao ka khu khawn uik,
pa mawh kiang ngu,
goik ngu,
si peed ngu,
mai pan ngu,
deuh ot ka piang,
khaix an mai tawb ka hak pa mhawm,
mai chup khaox glawm luan ka piang.
15 Yam gah kix bang hu,
ceu pui Keu-siax tag caw tix krox jun Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
tawb ka kraung pa singax singeh mai kraung pa iak pa lied khawm uik ha,
tom tag chix tix grawng hu.
Viang mawh nan,
ceu pui Keu-siax khankix tag ang pon tix teeh ka kraung ceu pa singax singeh an,
phan yuh nan tag yum heue.
Yam gah ka nyiex gox buan man hu,
kaing in tag sang mawh pa yuh ceu pui Keu-siax khankix heue.
16 Beeh glawm kaing pa yuh Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li,
mawh pa kia ngien ka nyiex gox buan man khawm uik,
khawm bee tok,
nyawng bhawng,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
bee pa singax singeh,
mai can kraung khrai pa ot ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
pa hoik guah tix singax pa tawx ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik heue.
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng:
18-19 “Paw kieh ceu pui Keu-siax hoek dex ka kraung ceu pa singax singeh in kix,
son ang sang pauh ka kix heue.
Son ang sang kieh kix yaox ka kaing ceu in,
Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh tag sang laig ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
tawx lox ciak ci ka kaing pa sang yuh pui ku kaux,
krai kix mhong ceu pa sang grawng kix mawh ceu pa tix.
20 Phan ceu pui Keu-siax laig ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
jhak simi grawng pa gah cao giex kaing li ka kraung singax khankixa,
kix tag yum kah heue.”
Beeh glawm ceu pui Keu-soeng
21 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
22 caw hawt tix si kaw ca rang ceu pui Keu-soeng ceu pui Li-we khankix,
cub mawx cub an,
nyiex mawx nyiex an,
23 can simex pa koe ju tix ngoe num hoek tix phuan num,
pa pon yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
hoik tiiem ka ca rang lih khawm uik.
24 Beeh glawm kaing pa sang caw kix tix klawm,
mawh kraung in kix plak greem:
25 Man lhop piang braux nyiex gox buan man,
mai dawb braux plak praix,
hak pa mhawm plang pa tawb ot plak piang,
man tie diang siviex,
26 man tie vung viang biang brawk nyiex gox buan man mai khu dee tan mai maox khankix,
khawm kraung pa iak pa lied pa siao ka ceu an kix.
Kix tag sang yuh ka kaing pa kuang kia ka pa dex pa dawt mai kraung ceu in kix khawm uik.
27 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng tag mawh cao pa simi ka kaing jhaw pa yuh ceu pui Keu-soeng,
thung kix gah ka kraung pa nhie Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh kix gah.
28 Kaing jhaw in mawh kaing jhaw pa caw ceu pui Keu-soeng tix yuh yam ot ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
Kix tag sang yuh ka greem tawm taix Yi-tha-ma pa mawh kawn simex Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li heue.
Beeh glawm ceu pui Mi-la-lix
29 Simiang Siyiex nhie Maw-si hawt tix si kaw ka ca rang ceu pui Mi-la-lix ceu pui Li-we,
cub mawx cub an,
nyiex mawx yiex an,
30 can simex pa koe ju tix ngoe num tom hoek tix phuan num,
pa pon yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
hoik tiiem ca rang lih khawm uik.
31 Beeh glawm kaing pa sang caw kix tix yuh mawh pa klawm ka khaox pang joh phia jung,
lat,
rong,
mai daux jhup jaong nyiex gox buan man;
32 khawm rong hawt vung viang biang brawk nyiex gox buan man,
daux jhup jaong,
jim,
mai kraung pa iak pa lied pa siao ka ceu an khankix khawm uik.
Kix tag sang caw tix klawm ka beeh glawm kaing pa hoik ciak ci kix yuh ku kaux.
33 In kix mawh kaing jhaw pa sang caw ceu pui Mi-la-lix tix yuh yam ot ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
Kix tag sang yuh ka greem tawm taix Yi-tha-ma kawn simex Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li heue.
Si kaw ka ca rang ceu pui Li-we
34-48 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng khawm simiang kawn pui,
hoik hawt ka gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex,
mai si kaw ka ceu pui Keu-siax,
Keu-soeng,
Mi-la-lix loe ceu pui Li-we khankix.
Kix tag hawt awm pa koe kix cub mawx cub an,
nyiex mawx nyiex an,
can simex pa koe ju tix ngoe num tom hoek tix phuan num,
pa pon yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
hoik tiiem ka ca rang lih khawm uik heue.
Ceu cad Keu-siax koe ra rheng aliah yieh tix phuan kaux.
Ceu cad Keu-soeng koe ra rheng liah yieh tix ngoe kaux.
Ceu cad Mi-la-lix koe loe rheng ra yieh kaux heue.
Khawm uik koe daix rheng phuan yieh tix daix kaux heue.
49 Hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
pui ku kaux hoik tiiem ka ca rang;
hawt awm gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
pui hoik tang koe ka kaing pa ciak ci yuh,
beeh glawm pa yuh kaing krub mai beeh glawm kaing pa klawm khankix heue.
