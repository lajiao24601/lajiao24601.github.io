Ya-pi-mi-leux
9
Kawn simex Ci-tiien pa kaux Ya-pi-mi-leux hoek ka haktiex See-ciien,
mai prie miex nawh khankix ot dee an;
nawh nhie kix mai keeing miex nawh khawm uik
2 chawk ka pui See-ciien ah nin:
“Pex sang kieh pui mawx hoek kuang kia ka pex lie?
Mawh kawn simex Ci-tiien khawm atigliah kaux lie?
Mawh pui tix kaux kaux lie?
Doh keud kah,
aux pa mawh Ya-pi-mi-leux hoik mawh nex nham pex.”
3 Prie miex nawh khankix tom hu chawk pui See-ciien tang nawh;
mai pui See-ciien but rhawm tix hawt Ya-pi-mi-leux,
kheu hoik mawh nawh pui nyiex pui ma kix.
4 Kix viex mau luah kaoh khaing nyiex vad Pa-lix.
Pi-li-thux atigliah plah,
mai tawx ka Ya-pi-mi-leux;
nawh teei mau in,
tawx riang cub pui pa cau nyhau pa vu vie hawt khaix nawh.
5 Nawh hoek ka nyiex keeing nawh Eu-fu-la,
pauh kawn simex Ci-tiien atigliah kaux ka piang simaox ting tix khlawp,
mai pa mawh pux aik nawh khankix kawx,
hoik pauh yum uik heue.
Viang mawh nan kawn simex Ci-tiien pa iak khaing paox tix,
pa kaux Yaw-thang pon to mox tix,
mai ang yaox tix yum.
6 Can pui See-ciien mai pui Mi-lawx rom paox tix tau,
ka praok khaox yang yit singax an ka See-ciien,
mai sum Ya-pi-mi-leux mawh simiang heue.
7 Yaw-thang mhong ka grawng in,
nawh tom hu ka gawng Ci-li-sing,
mai cung ka piang gawng,
kok yo lox pa ting si num ka kix ah nin:
“Pui See-ciien eue,
pex nghiet ka aux,
Siyiex tom tag nghiet ka lox pex!
8 Singaix pa hoik luan,
khaox ka daux tiag tag sang sum simiang tix kaux.
Kix tag sang ah nin ka khaox sime:
‘Maix mawh simiang yix heue.’
9 Khaox sime pawk lox ah nin:
‘Aux khaw tix naox ka krax tan pa yuh ka siyiex mai bee sime pa hoik tawx ka kawn pui khankix,
mai kuang ka piang pex lie?’
10 Khaox ah nin ka khaox ruh khankix:
‘Maix mawh simiang yix.’
11 Khaox ruh an pawk lox ah nin:
‘Aux khaw tix naox pa plix tix ka plix pa mhawm tie,
mai hu kuang ka piang pex lie?’
12 Khaox an ah nin ka nhu plix siiet:
‘Maix mawh simiang yix heue.’
13 Nhu plix siiet an pawk lox ah nin:
‘Aux khaw tix naox greeik ka plai pa muh siyiex mai pui tix nyaux kah,
mai hu kuang ka piang pex lie?’
14 Khaox an ah nin ka khaox pang kat:
‘Maix mawh simiang yix heue.’
15 Pang kat an pawk lox ah nin:
‘Phan pex sang kieh aux mawh simiang pex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
pex tag caw tix hoek deuh rhawm tix greem veei aux,
mai aux tag sang kia ngien ka pex;
phan ang mawh nan,
khaox pang kat aux tag sang lih mawh ngu,
mai gleeing ka khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noeng uik heue.’ ”
16 Yaw-thang ca nied tix ah nin:
“Yam in,
kieh ang pex kieh Ya-pi-mi-leux mawh simiang khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix lie?
Pex doh ang doh keud ka Ci-tiien khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai hawt tix kia ngien ka pui nyiex ma nawh kheu pa mhawm rhawm keud nawh lie?
17 Pex tag caw tix doh keud ka pa hoik yuh keeing aux naing son pex;
nawh hoik kha rhawm kha rhi tix taoh pex luat luan khaing pui Mi-tiien.
18 Viang mawh nan,
daux in singaix pex lai tieh vheen ka pui nyiex ma keeing aux;
pex hoik pauh kawn simex nawh yum ka piang simaox ting tix khlawp.
Mawh kheu bun siliex kawn simex nawh Ya-pi-mi-leux,
hoik mawh paox ceu paox cad pex khankix,
pex tom ap nawh mawh simiang See-ciien.
19 Phan kaing pa yuh pex ka daux singaix,
mawh pa khaw khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
son sang kia ngien ka Ci-tiien mai pui nyiex nawh khankix,
aux tag sang nawb lox piang pex mai ka piang Ya-pi-mi-leux,
mai kieh pex ra lai gaux rhawm tau heue.
20 Phan ang mawh nan,
kieh Ya-pi-mi-leux ploi ngu gleeing pui See-ciien mai pui Mi-lawx yum uik,
mai kieh nang pui See-ciien mai Mi-lawx ploi ngu gleeing ka Ya-pi-mi-leux.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-thang kheu lhat nawh ka pux ke tix Ya-pi-mi-leux,
nawh tom to hu ka haktiex Pi-eux,
mai paung ot tan heue.
22 Ya-pi-mi-leux kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex loe num.
23 Khaix an Siyiex kieh Ya-pi-mi-leux mai pui See-ciien ang lai khaw rhawm paox tix,
pui See-ciien tom tieh vheen ka Ya-pi-mi-leux.
24 Yuh nin,
Ya-pi-mi-leux mai pui pa cheeik nawh pui See-ciien pauh ka kawn simex Ci-tiien atigliah kaux yaox krax pa pawk bix.
25 Pui See-ciien ploi pui hu kuan mox tix ka piang gawng,
son sang pauh ka Ya-pi-mi-leux yum,
son sang tud rhim ka can pui pa lawng ka krax an.
Koe pui krai grawng kaing in ka Ya-pi-mi-leux heue.
26 Kawn simex Yi-piie pa kaux Cia-leux mai pux aik nawh khankix hoek ka See-ciien;
mai pui See-ciien yum rhawm ka nawh uik.
27 Kix hoek ka si peem plix siiet,
mai pieh ka plix siiet,
greeik ka plai,
yuh ka poi.
Kix hoek ka nyiex vad cie kix,
mai ih nyaux tan,
mai jhax lawg ka Ya-pi-mi-leux.
28 Kawn simex Yi-piie pa kaux Cia-leux ah nin:
“Ex pui See-ciien mawh pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai yuh kaing son Ya-pi-mi-leux lie?
Nawh mawh pui mawx lie?
Ang nawh mawh kawn simex Ci-tiien khaing lie!
Si-pu-leux mawh ngie cao pa teem coi nawh khaing.
Ex yuh ka mawx lai yuh kaing son nawh lie?
Pex caw tix hawt ka tax du pex Ha-mawx,
nawh hoik sum ka ceu pui pex in heue!
29 Aux simie tix mawh cao pa thung ka pui ceu in heue!
Aux tag sang pauh ka Ya-pi-mi-leux heue!
Aux tag sang krai ka nawha ah nin:
‘Baox ploi pui naing maix,
hu puing ka naing heue!’ ”
30 Simiang veng See-ciien pa kaux Si-pu-leux,
mhong ka lox Cia-leux,
mai kied song rhawm kah.
31 Nawh ploi kawn cau tix hu ka Ya-lu-ma,
mai krai ka Ya-pi-mi-leux mhong ah nin:
“Kawn simex Yi-piie pa kaux Cia-leux mai pux aik nawh khankix hoek ka See-ciien,
mai cheeik pui pa ot ka daux veng khankix tieh vheen ka maix.
32 Mawh kheu nan,
maix nied tix ploi pui cie maix hu kuan mox tix ot ka plak praix veng yam pon sawm,
33 yam hoek pon ngawb cao pa sax,
yam riang praix,
maix puing ka veng an.
Yam lih Cia-leux mai pui cie nawh khankix tix rieb puing ka naing,
maix tag sang puing ka kix tom nyawm nyiem heue.”
34 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-pi-mi-leux ploi pui naing tix hu yam kawn faik praix,
guah kix koe pon cub,
mai kuan mox tix ot ka plak praix veng See-ciien.
35 Yam yaox Ya-pi-mi-leux mai pui naing nawh hoik lih Cia-leux,
cung ka diang siviex veng,
kix tom lih khaing dee kuan mox tix tan.
36 Cia-leux yaox kix,
mai ah nin ka Si-pu-leux:
“Maix jhak,
koe pui lih hoek khaing gawng heue!”
Si-pu-leux pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix lud jhak kah;
maix cie veei gawng mawh pui heue!”
37 Cia-leux baox ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
koe pui lih hoek khaing si nax gawng;
an tix cub hoek khaing krax hoek ‘Khaox yang yit pui si pawk’ an heue!”
38 Si-pu-leux ah nin ka nawh:
“Ang maix hoik krai ka lox pa ting pa vao lie?
Maix hoik krai,
ex lai yuh kaing son Ya-pi-mi-leux yuh ka mawx;
pui in kix hoik mawh pui pa map nai maix.
Yam in,
viex maix hu yuh naing ka kix heue!”
39 Mawh kheu nan,
Cia-leux thung pui See-ciien lih hu puing ka Ya-pi-mi-leux.
40 Ya-pi-mi-leux leue puing ka Cia-leux,
Cia-leux yaog tix to;
mai pui mat kied nie,
mai pum naog ot ka diang siviex veng.
41 Ya-pi-mi-leux paung ot ka haktiex Ya-lu-ma.
Si-pu-leux grheu Cia-leux mai pux aik nawh khankix lih hu khaing haktiex See-ciien,
mai ang kieh kix paung ot tan heue.
42 Ka daux singaix khaix,
Ya-pi-mi-leux tawng grawng pui See-ciien keud tix sang gah tix hu ot ka plak praix veng,
43 mai nawh tom guah pui naing tix koe loe cub,
kuan mox tix ot ka plak praix veng.
Yam yaox nawh kawn pui lih khaing veng,
tom lih khaing dee kuan mox tix,
mai pauh kix yum heue.
44 Ya-pi-mi-leux thung pui naing tix an preeng tix hu pau ka siviex veng;
mai an ra cub preeng tix hu ka plak praix veng,
mai pauh ka pui pa ot plak praix veng yum khawm uik.
45 Ya-pi-mi-leux puing ka naing leeing lang singaix,
tom tag pon ka veng,
mai pauh pui pa ot daux veng yum,
yuh laux ka veng an,
mai rhuat kih ka piang tiex heue.
46 Simiang pa ot ka daux veng See-ciien mhong ka grawng in,
mai laig mox tix ka daux pa koe ka daux nyiex vad Yi.
Pi-li-thux.
47 Koe pui krai ka Ya-pi-mi-leux mhong,
48 Ya-pi-mi-leux tom viex pui pa ot greem tix lih hoek ka gawng Sa-meung.
Nawh teei moe tix mag kid ka kak khaox ting tix gawng,
mai klawm piang klip tix,
mai kieh pui pa ot greem tix kawx nied hawt tix yuh laung laung.
49 Mawh kheu nan,
pui ku kaux tom kid ka kak khaox ting an tix gawng,
yuh hawt awm Ya-pi-mi-leux,
mai pum kak khaox an vung viang daux doh tiex an.
Kix ploi ngu gleeing ka daux doh tiex an,
mai gleeing ka pui pa ot ka daux veng See-ciien yum khawm uik,
simex bun koe been tix rheng kaux heue.
50 Khaix an,
Ya-pi-mi-leux hoek ka Thi-pe-see,
puing ka veng an,
mai pon ka veng.
51 Dee an koe nyiex gawng mu tix lhang pa kied kaux cong;
pui veng an khawm uik,
simex ang ah bun ang ah,
khawm simiang hoik to laig mox tix daux gawng mu uik.
Kix nyeep ka siviex,
mai haok tom choek nyiex gawng mu.
52 Ya-pi-mi-leux puing ka nyiex gawng mu in,
mai hu dex ka siviex,
mai ploi ngu gleeing kah gawng mu.
53 bKoe bun tix kaux tik ka simaox laing tix khlawp,
krox ka kaing nawh,
mai si-ang kaing kawb kaing nawh max;
54 nawh nied tix nhie pui cau pa nhum ju,
pa viex kraung naing son nawh hoek,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Lawd vaig pauh aux ka heue!
Aux ang rhawm kieh pui krai mawh bun tix kaux pa pauh aux yum.”
Mawh kheu nan,
pui cau pa nhum ju an tom suat nawh ka vaig tix taix,
nawh tom yum.
55 Pui Yi-seu-liex yaox hoik yum Ya-pi-mi-leux,
mai tom deeh ing ka nyiex heue.
56 Yuh nin,
Siyiex pawk bix ka piang Ya-pi-mi-leux,
jao pa hoik yuh nawh kaing pa caox pa caig khankix ka keeing tix,
mawh kheu hoik pauh nawh pux aik tix yum atigliah kaux.
57 Siyiex kawx hoik kieh pui See-ciien kham krax kha rhawm kheu phi map kix;
lox jhax lawg kawn simex Ci-tiien pa kaux Yaw-thang kawx,
hoik lih tix mawh heue.
