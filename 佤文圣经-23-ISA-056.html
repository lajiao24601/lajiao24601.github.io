Siyiex hoik cu tix kieh kawn pui piang haktiex mawh pui cie tix khawm uik
56
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka pui cie tix:
“Pex tag caw tix prawng rauh see,
mawh kheu aux tag sang hoek tix taoh ka pex.
2 Aux tag sang tawx buan son ka pui an kix pa priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah;
aux tag sang tawx buan son ka pui pa singai hu khaing pa caox pa caig heue.”
3 Pui tang ceu tang cad pa een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex ang chix tix ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ang doh kieh ex krub tau mai pui cie nawh khankix heue.”
Pui pa hoik sieh tix ang pon tix ah nin:
“Aux mawh khaox hoik rham.”
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka kix:
“Phan pex priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah,
yuh kaing pa khaw ka rhawm rhi aux,
mai giex kaux ka sijax mukheu aux,
5 mai aux tag sang kieh pui doh keud ka ceu kaux pex ka daux nyiex buan mai ka daux cie pui aux,
lheu khaing koe kawn praix pex tom nie.
Kawn pui tag ang chix piawm ka pex leeing ju heue.”
6 Koe pui tang ceu tang cad khankix tix blah,
kix hoik yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
kix muh ka nawh,
mai yuh kaing jhaw nawh,
khaix an kix hoik priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah tete,
mai hoik giex kaux cong ka sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka kix:
7 a“Aux tag sang viex pex hoek ka gawng pa singax tix Shi-ang,
mai kieh pex koe krax gaux rhawm ka daux nyiex rawg aux buan,
khaix an rieb tix teei ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Khawng-brie tang ceu pa hoik tan lu pex ot ka piang khu dee tan.
Nyiex buan aux tag sang kieh mawh nyiex dee rawg kawn pui piang haktiex buan son heue.”
8 Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong an pa hoik viex pui Yi-seu-liex pa vuk vied vuk viang hoek ka daux haktiex tix,
mai hoik sax ah tix deuh son sang kieh pui tang ceu tang cad laig ka daux cub caw kix nang heue.
Ne raw ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex
9 Simiang Siyiex hoik sax tawx lox ka pui tang ceu tang cad khankix,
mai nhie kix hoek tix bleet ka pui cie tix awm to sat yuh nan.
10 Nawh ah nin:
“Can pui pa pau tix jhak hoik dug ngai khawm uik;
kix khaw tix ne raw ka pui cie aux tete te lai,
viang mawh nan,
kix hoik daox hoik aw.
Kix hoik mawh cub sox pa ang chix raoh,
mai chix blauh ngie tix it mai ca maox khaing heue!
11 Kix awm sox pa simie tix ih priex,
mai simie tix ih ang jud ang jai;
kix hoik dawng mawh cao liang,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng tid tix,
mai nieb pon hawt alo to kaux tix,
mai sawg ngie son sang mhawm ka to kaux tix.
12 Kix pa mawh pui yoek ka plai ah nin:
‘Tawx plai tin,
ex sau riang tix nyaux heue,
pa sax nie kawn lheu mhawm khaing daux singaix heue!’ ”
