2
Yuh nin,
rao ma mai haktiex khawm can ceu pa koe ku ceu hoik phan yuh hoik khawm uik.
2 abKa daux singaix aliah,
Siyiex hoik hoik kaing jhaw pa phan pa yuh tix,
tom laux lee tix heue.
3 Nawh nawb buan ka singaix aliah,
kieh mawh pa kied singax singeh;
mawh kheu hoik hoik nawh kaing yuh tix singaix an,c
tom laux lee tix heue.
4 In mawh grawng been pa hoik phan hoik yuh Siyiex rao ma mai haktiex heue.
Si peem Yi-tiien
Yam phan Simiang Siyiex rao ma mai haktiex,
5 ka piang haktiex ang nyang koe ka khaox ox mai rib riem,
ang koe ka taux lhax,
mawh kheu ang nawh bax tawx rawm lhiex,
mai ang koe pui sum kaung;
6 Viang mawh nan,
rawm buing tix lih khaing haktiex kieh tiex caux heue.
7 eKhaix an,
Simiang Siyiex yuh riang pak peung kawn pui ka phawng tiexd mai phlu bhawm pa im ju daux meeh nawh,
mai nawh lih mawh pui pa im ju heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex sum si peem tix dauh ka plak lih singaix daux Si peem Yi-tiien,
mai deuh kawn pui pa hoik phan tix ot tan.
9 gSimiang Siyiex phan khaox ox pa nyawm tix jhak mai plix khaox pa mhawm son priex preem kuah khaing tiex.
Ka daux sigreung si peem an,
koe khaox im ju mai khaox pa tawng pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawmf.
10 Koe rawm klawng tix klawng lih khaing daux Si peem Yi-tiien,
son sang kieh tiex caux,
mai lih khaing plak praix Yi-tiien,
guah tix mawh pon klawng.
11 Ceu kaux rawm klawng pa jauh an tix klawng mawh rawm klawng Pi-suing,
mai lih hu vung viang plak haktiex Ha-fe-la.
12 Haktiex dee an koe khri,
sidah bhawng pa kied mhawm mai siang simaox.
13 Ceu kaux rawm klawng ra klawng tix klawng mawh klawng Ci-suing,
mai lih hu vung viang plak haktiex Ku-seuh.
14 Ceu kaux rawm klawng loe klawng tix klawng mawh rawm klawng Ti-keu-li-see,
mai lih hu plak lih singaix haktiex Ya-sux.
Rawm klawng pon klawng tix klawng mawh rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
15 Simiang Siyiex deuh pui an ka daux Si peem Yi-tiien,
kieh nawh sum si peem mai pau kah.
16 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka pui an:
“Maix pon tix ih plix khaox ku khaox,
pa koe daux si peem awm pa koe rhawm tix,
17 viang mawh nan,
maix tag ang pon tix ih khaing khaox pa tawng pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm,
mawh kheu singaix hoik ih maix plix khaox an,
maix tag yum tete heue.”
18 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui an ang khaw tix ot nong nong,
aux tag sang yuh pui teem coi,
pa mawh paox awm pa khaw mai nawh.”
19 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex phan to sat ku ceu piang haktiex,
mai sim pa pu khankix khaing tiex,
mai viex hoek ka sivoe ngai pui an,
kieh nawh sum ceu kaux kix,
nawh tom sum ceu kaux can to sat khawm uik.
20 Pui an tom sum ceu kaux khawng brie mai sim khawm to tiag ku ceu khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
pui an ang nyang yaox ka pui pa sang teem coi pa mawh paox pa khaw mai nawh heue.
21 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh pui an laig it.
Yam laig it nawh,
Simiang Siyiex teei si-ang praok nawh tix nyaing,
mai deeh nyeep nex kleuk paox tix.
22 Simiang Siyiex yuh bun tix kaux,
khaing si-ang praok pa hoik teei nawh,
mai viex hoek ka simex.
23 Simex ah nin:
In mawh si-ang khaing si-ang aux,
nex khaing nex aux;
aux tag kok nawh mawh “bun”,i
mawh kheu mawh nawh pa hoik teei lih khaing “simex”.
24 jMawh kheu nan,
simex tag lih khaing miex keeing tix,
mai kleuk tix tau mai moeng tix,
kiex ra kaux lih mawh tix kaux.
25 Pui an mai moeng nawh tom ot ka kaong,
viang mawh nan,
kiex ang koe krax kaig tua.
