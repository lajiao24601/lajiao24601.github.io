41
Khaix an,
pui an thung aux hu ka nyiex pa singax singeh.
Nawh tiag tix yaox ka rong guh ding,
bu koe loe kongjhee,
2 Krax vieh koe phuan kongjhee,
guh ding khawm ra plak bu koe tix plak ra kongjhee plak.
Nawh baox tiag tix yaox ka nyiex pa singax,
krax lang koe tix nga kongjhee,
krax vieh koe kao kongjhee heue.
3 Khaix an,
nawh hu ka plak daux nyiex buan.
Nawh tiag tix yaox ka rong guh ding,
bu koe tix kongjhee,
vieh koe loe kongjhee,
guh ding khawm ra plak bu koe tix plak loe kongjhee.
4 Nawh baox tiag tix yaox ka daux nyiex buan,
koe kao kongjhee awm paox tix.
Nawh krai ka aux:
“In mawh nyiex pa singax singeh heue.”
Nyiex khre krax tan pa dex ka praok ding nyiex buan
5 Pui an tiag tix yaox ka guh ding plak daux nyiex buan,
bu koe loe kongjhee.
Pa dex ka vung viang guh ding nyiex buan koe lawg,
ku lawg koe vieh ra kongjhee.
6 Lawg koe loe jan,
tix jan koe tix ngoe lawg.
Guh ding nyiex buan plak praix ku jan kawn lheu klawk khaing jan plak greem,
jan plak piang rhi khaing jan plak greem,
mawh kheu lawg nyiex chix si khap tix ka piang dee bhlien guh ding,
ang siao tawk jim rhiem kah.
7 Yuh nin,
phan kok jhak tix yaox ka plak praix,
guh ding nyiex buan jauh ka plak piang hoek ka plak greem,
been bu hoik khaw paox tix khawm uik.
Guh ding plak praix pa dex ka nyiex buan,
mai ka plak praix lawg nyiex in kix,
glawk bawng pa vieh koe ka ra mu,
pui chix tix jauh ka jan tix haok ka jan ra,
mai baox hoek ka jan loe.
8-11 Guh ding plak praix nyiex lawg bu koe ra kongjhee plak.
Ka plak laong nyiex buan siviex koe ka tix mu,
ka plak seh koe nang siviex tix mu chix tix phlat laig daux lawg nyiex ku mu.
Ka vung viang nyiex buan khawm pon daog aux yaox grah pa koe been vieh ra kongjhee plak;
grah in lhaong khaing tiex loe kongjhee,
khaw lhaong mai jaong lawg nyiex an kix pa dex ka piang guh ding nyiex buan.
Grah pa ot vung viang nyiex buan mai ka si nax lawg nyiex pa ot cao giex kaing li,
haktiex yi koe kah,
krax vieh koe kao kongjhee heue.
Nyiex ka plak klip singaix
12 Ka daog dee die haktiex pa yi in ka plak klip singaix nyiex buan,
koe nyiex ka tix lhang,
krax lang koe tipon phuan kongjhee,
krax vieh koe tix ngoe phuan kongjhee,
guh ding vung viang khawm pon daog,
bu koe ra kongjhee plak heue.
Khaox diag nyiex buan
13 Pui an tiag tix yaox ka nyiex buan,
krax lang koe tix phuan kongjhee.
Jauh ka plak khaix guh ding nyiex buan,
hoek ka nyiex pa ot ka plak klip singaix dee die haktiex pa yi,
mai ra lai khan paox tix tix phuan kongjhee.
14 Ka plak sivoe nyiex buan mai ka haktiex dee yi khawm ra plak krax lang koe tix phuan kongjhee.
15 Nawh baox tiag tix yaox ka been lang nyiex pa ot plak klip singaix an,
mai grah khawm ra plak,
mai hoik mawh tix phuan kongjhee nang heue.
Nyiex buan plak daux
Sivoe ngai nyiex buan,
nyiex pa singax,
mai nyiex pa kied singax,
16 jauh ka piang tiex hoek ka guh ding daux doh nyiex hoik kab khaox phia ka khawm uik.
Daux doh in kix chix tix nyip.
17 Guh ding vung viang nyiex buan khawm pon daog,
jauh ka paing tiex hoek ka piang siviex,
18 plak praix plak daux hoik saik riang Ci-lu-paw mai khaox jhie yaong koe kah.
Ci-lu-paw mai khaox jhie yaong tia tix tau.
Riang Ci-lu-paw ku mu koe pak ngai ra mu:
19-20 pak ngai pui veeh tix plak khaox jhie yaong plak in,
pa mawh pak ngai sivai sang si veeh tix plak khaox jhie yaong plak an.
Guh ding khawm pon daog jauh ka phia pa tawm piang tiex hoek ka piang siviex,
hoik yuh nin khawm uik.
21 Rong siviex nyiex pa singax mawh pon pak heue.
Khu dee tan pa yuh ka khaox
Ka sivoe ngai dee laig ka diang siviex nyiex pa singax,
22 phan jhak tix yaox awm pa koe khu dee tan pa yuh ka khaox,
krax lhaong koe tix kongjhee plak,
krax vieh koe tix kongjhee.
Rong khu dee tan pon jaong,
plak ngai khu dee tan,
mai ka praok hoik yuh tix ka khaox khawm uik.
Pui an krai ka aux ah nin:
“In mawh pheen pa khan ot ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Siviex
23 Nyiex pa singax mai nyiex pa kied singax hoik tang koe siviex tix nyiex tix mu.
24 Siviex ku mu hoik koe khlawp ku brawm,
ku khlawp siviex baox guah tix koe ra plak,
mai chix tix nu niep.
25 Ka piang siviex nyiex pa singax hoik saik khaox jhie yaong mai riang Ci-lu-paw koe kah,
awm ka piang guh ding yuh nan.
Jan plak praix siviex nyiex buan plak ngai mawh khaox phia pa tawb.
26 Nyiex lawg pa koe ka sivoe ngai nyiex buan khawm ra plak hoik mawh daux doh iak pa koe ka khawm uik,
ka piang guh ding hoik saik khaox jhie yaong koe ka heue.
