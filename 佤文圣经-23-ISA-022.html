Gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Yie-lu-sa-leung
22
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai “ngawd riang su sai”.
Kaing ceu tix pa lih tix mawh lie?
Pui pa ot daux veng yuh poi ceu pa tix ka piang braux nyiex lie?
2 Pui ka daux veng hoik riag yo khawm uik,
mai hoik yuh krax klieh krax nyiah ku dauh ku dee heue.
Grawng pa yum paox ceu paox cad pex,
ang mawh pa yum ka puing tix naing.
3 Simiang pex ang bax puing te pie tix taix kawx,
mai pui hoik giex laung ka nawh;
pui naing pa hoik to luan kawx pui hoik pon leue.
4 Yam in paw vi simi ka aux,
kieh aux yiiem heue;
paw vi hoek tix nyawm rhawm aux,
mawh kheu paox ceu paox cad aux hoik yum khawm uik heue.
5 Ka daux “ngawd riang su sai”,
in hoik mawh bawg yam pa yaoh yee,
pa som nai,
mai suk sag;
in hoik mawh pa baox thiem thie Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing tix tawx ka ex.
Pui hoik puing guh veng ex laux,
mai lox pa khiox khaw krax pa teem coi hoik luah ka daux ngawd heue.
6 Pui naing brug brung Yi-lang hoik hoek,
mai ka daux hau te kix hoik naog ka te.
Pui naing Cie-eux hu ka caong tix hoik hoek,
mai kix hoik rien khre tix puing ka naing.
7 Ngawd haktiex pa mhawm Yo-tax hoik su shiet paox tix ka ling law naing;
pui naing brug brung hoik dia paox tix ot ka sivoe diang siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
8 Pui naing Yo-tax pa pau khankix hoik ang pex heue.
Yam an,
pex teei kraung naing lih khaing daux nyiex pa deuh ka kraung naing.
9-10 Pex tag sang tawng ka piang guh ding veng Ta-wue,
kawn koe dee ang nyang bax thox tix si nyip tom nie,
hoik krox pex hu thox ka heue.
Pex lam lia tix yaox ka nyiex ma pa koe ka daux veng khawm uik,
yieh laux tix blah,
teei simaox pa hoik yieh lih,
mai blaox guh ding veng dee ang bax si nyip.
Yuh dee khrawm rawm tom kub,
11 mai pex tag caw tix kaong dung rawm koe ka si nax guh ding veng khawm ra plak,
kieh rawm ot daux dung rawm prim hoek dee an.
Kaing in kix khawm uik,
Siyiex pa mawh cao pa phan pa yuh pex hoik dawg rien tix yuh mhawm mhawm,
kheu nan mai kieh kaing in lih tix mawh,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang nghiet ka lox nawh heue.
12 Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang nhie pex yiiem riag yiiem yo,
khlao hauk kaing tix ghle,
ceep ka kraung man keei sum.
13 aViang mawh nan,
pex kawn nyiah,
kawn klieh.
Pex hoik pauh ka moi ka yung,
mai hoik ih nyaux tom sak tix.
Pex ah nin:
“Ex ih nyaux heue,
Pa sax ex tag sang yum heue.”
14 To kaux aux hoik pon tix mhong ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin ka aux:
“Yam kawn im ju kix tix ngaix,
mai aux taug ang pluat ploi ka phi map kix tix ngaix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Ne raw ka Seu-paw-nax
15 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing pa kied ting kied lhaong tawx lox nhie aux hu ka Seu-paw-nax simiang ting pa kuang ka daux nyiex haw,
mai ne raw ka nawh ah nin:
16 “Mawh maix pui mawx,
maix lai pon kaong daux si meeig son to kaux tix ka daux rang simaox ting ka daog gawng lie?
17 Maix hoik ce tix mawh pui pa ting ceu;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang teei ka maix,
18 tag sang miien maix lawm le,
mai si vun tix tik maix hoek ka meung haktiex ting tix mu.
Maix tag sang yum dee an,
mai yum ka praok ling law naing pa hoik kieh maix koe min plak ngaix dee an.
Maix hoik yuh krax kaig krax tua ka nyiex haw simiang.
19 Simiang Siyiex ang kieh maix ngawm dee lhaong,
mai bluh maix lih heue.”
20 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Seu-paw-nax:
“Yam an,
aux tag sang kok ka siliex tix pa mawh Yi-li-ya-cing kawn simex Si-leu-cia hoek.
21 Aux tag sang teei kraung ceep maix yam yuh kaing krub mai tawx nawh ceep,
teei maox rawd nyoi maix tawx nawh ceep;
aux tag sang tawx siawp sibrawm maix ka nawh.
Nawh tag sang kia ngien ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka pui Yo-tax awm keeing tix yuh nan.
22 bAux tag sang teei tix tawx jiak saw meung cie Ta-wue ka nawh.
Phan nawh paoh kah,
mai ang koe pui pa pon nyeep;
phan nawh nyeep kah,
mai ang koe pui pa pon paoh.
23 Aux tag sang deuh nawh ot ka dee kaux dee cong,
awm jim rhiem pa hoik tawk kaux yuh nan;
pui nyiex pui ma nawh khawm uik,
tag sang koe min plak ngai kheu nawh heue.
24 “Viang mawh nan,
pui daux nyiex ma nawh mai paox nyiex nawh tag sang lih mawh beeh glawm pa cien son nawh.
Kix hoik vhak tix ot ka to kaux nawh,
awm hoik vhak aw mai jauk tix ot ka khaox pa hoik tawk jim rhiem koe ka yuh nan.
25 Koe tix singaix,
khaox pa hoik tawk jim rhiem kaux,
pui tag sang kid,
hoik goh ghrup;
ceu pa vhak ot ka piang jim rhiem an tag sang max sang phawng khawm uik heue.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
