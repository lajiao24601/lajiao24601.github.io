Doeh ceu pui Yi-sa-cia
7
Yi-sa-cia koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Thaw-la,
Phu-wa,
Ya-sux,
Seung-loeng.
2 Thaw-la koe kawn simex liah kaux,
pa mawh Wu-si,
Li-fa-ya,
Yie-leux,
Ya-mai,
Yi-paw-sang,
See-mu-lix;
kix hoik mawh cao kaing ceu pui cub nyiex Thaw-la khawm uik,
mawh pui pa kua ka naing.
Ka daux baux bawg yam singaix simiang Ta-wue,
doeh ceu cad kix koe ra mheen ra rheng liah yieh kaux heue.
3 Wu-si koe kawn simex tix kaux,
pa mawh Yi-see-la-si;
Yi-see-la-si mai kawn simex nawh pon kaux,
pa mawh Mi-cia-leux,
Eu-pa-ti-yax,
Yaw-eux,
mai Yi-see-ya hoik mawh cao kaing ka daux nyiex kix.
4 Kix hoik koe moeng iak mai kawn praix tom nie,
mawh kheu nan,
pui pa pon puing naing ka daux doeh ceu cad kix koe loe mheen liah rheng kaux heue.
5 Ka daux ceu pui Yi-sa-cia ku yiex,
pui pa pon puing ka naing koe daix mheen aliah rheng kaux heue.
Doeh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming mai Tang
6 Piien-ya-ming koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Pi-la,
Pi-cie,
Yie-tiie heue.
7 Pi-la koe kawn simex phuan kaux,
pa mawh Yi-see-peung,
Wu-si,
Wu-shiie,
Yie-li-maw,
Yi-lix.
Kix hoik mawh cao kaing ceu pui kix khawm uik,
mai mawh nang pui naing pa kied kua.
Doeh ceu pui kix koe pui pa pon puing ka naing ra mheen ra rheng mai tix ngoe pon kaux heue.
8 Pi-cie koe kawn simex dim kaux,
pa mawh Si-mi-la,
Yaw-a-see,
Yi-li-yi-shiex,
Yi-li-yaw-nai,
Ang-lix,
Yie-li-maw,
Ya-pi-yax,
Ya-na-thu,
Ya-la-miiex.
9 Ka daux doeh ceu cad kix,
hawt ka cub nyiex kix pa hoik tiiem lih,
pui pa pon puing ka naing koe ra mheen mai ra yieh kaux heue.
10 Yie-tiie koe kawn simex tix kaux,
pa mawh Pi-leu-hang.
Pi-leu-hang koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
pa mawh Yie-wu-see,
Piien-ya-ming,
Yi-fu,
Ci-na-na,
Si-thang,
Tha-see,
Ya-si-sa-ha.
11 Kix hoik mawh cao kaing ceu pui kix khawm uik,
mai mawh nang pui naing pa kied kua.
Ka daux doeh ceu cad kix,
koe pui pa pon puing ka naing tix mheen aliah rheng mai ra yieh kaux.
12 Su-phing mai Hu-phinga kawx,
hoik mawh nang ceu pui in heue.
Tang koe kawn simex tix kaux pa mawh Hu-seung heue.
Doeh ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix
13 Na-fu-tha-lix koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ya-shiie,
Ku-ni,
Yie-seux,
Sa-long (kix hoik mawh doeh ceu cad Pi-lab heue).
Doeh ceu pui Ma-na-si
14 Moeng iak Ma-na-si (pui Ya-lang) keh nawh cie kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Ya-see-liex mai Ma-ciex.
Ma-ciex keh Ci-liex.
15 Ma-ciex teei Hu-phing mai Su-phing tang koe moeng.
Nan nawh kaux Ma-cia.
Kawn nyi Ma-ciex mawh Si-law-fe-ha.
Si-law-fe-ha koe kawn bun khaing heue.
16 Moeng Ma-ciex pa kaux Ma-cia keh kawn simex ra kaux;
kix sum ceu kaux kawn simex in kiex kaux Pi-li-see mai See-li-see.
Pi-li-see koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Wu-lang mai Li-cing.
17 Wu-lang koe kawn simex tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Pi-tang.
In kix hoik mawh doeh ceu cad Ci-liex khawm uik;
Ci-liex mawh kawn simex Ma-ciex,
kawn saux Ma-na-si heue.
18 Nan Ci-liex pa kaux Ha-maw-li-ciex koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Yi-see-heux,
Ya-pi-yi-shiex,
Ma-lax.
19 See-mi-tax koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ya-shien,
See-ciien,
Li-kheu-si,
A-ni-ang heue.
Doeh ceu pui Yi-fa-liien
20 Doeh ceu pui Yi-fa-liien,
hawt ka keeing nawh toe tix hu mawh:
Su-thi-la,
Pi-liex,
Tha-ha,
Yi-la-tax,
Tha-ha,
21 Sa-pa,
Su-thi-la.
Lheu khaing Su-thi-la,
Yi-fa-liien kawn koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Yi-shiex mai Yi-lix.
Kheu jao briex kiex ka moi pui haktiex Cia-theux an,
pui pauh kiex yum.
22 Keeing kiex Yi-fa-liien laux rhawm son kiex tom rheeng singaix,
pux aik nawh tom hoek tix kieh nawh nyawm rhawm.
23 Khaix an,
nawh deeh it tau mai moeng tix,
moeng nawh koe kaux,
mai keh kawn simex tix kaux.
Kix sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Pi-li-yaxc,
mawh kheu hoik si pup nyiex kix ka krax laux krax leeing sivoe an heue.
24 Yi-fa-liien koe kawn bun tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Seu-yi-la.
Seu-yi-la sum veng Paw Heu-loeng plak laong mai veng Paw Heu-loeng plak seh,
mai veng Wu-shian Seu-yi-la loe mu veng heue.
25 Yi-fa-liien kawn koe kawn simex tang kaux tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Li-fax;
doeh ceu cad Li-fax hawt ka keeing nawh toe tix hu mawh:
Li-si,
Tha-la,
Tha-hang,
26 La-tang,
Ya-mi-fu,
Yi-li-sa-ma,
27 Noeng,
Yaw-su-yax heue.
28 Haktiex pa ot kix mawh khawm Paw-theu-lix mai nyiex yaong pa dex tan,
hu plak lih singaix hoek ka Na-lang,
hu plak klip singaix hoek ka Ci-seux mai ka nyiex yaong pa dex tan,
kawn koe veng See-ciien mai veng Ai-ya ra mu veng,
khawm nyiex yaong pa dex mai ra mu veng tan heue.
29 Doeh ceu pui Ma-na-si hoik giex gang ka haktiex Paw Sang,
Tha-na,
Mi-cie-to,
To-eux,
mai ka nyiex yaong pa dex ka kix tan heue.
Plak piang mawh dee ot doeh ceu cad kawn simex Ya-keux pa kaux Yaw-seux heue.
Doeh ceu pui Ya-seux
30 Plak greem mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-seux.
Ya-seux koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ying-na,
Yi-see-wa,
Yi-see-we,
Pi-li-yax,
koe kawn bun tix kaux pa kaux Si-la heue.
31 Pi-li-yax koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Si-piie mai Ma-ciex.
Ma-ciex mawh pui pa pang ka veng Pi-sha-we heue.
32 Si-piie koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Ya-fu-leu,
Saw-mawx,
Heu-thang,
koe kawn bun tix kaux pa kaux Su-ya heue.
33 Ya-fu-leu kawx koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Pa-sax,
Ping-ha,
Ya-see-fa heue.
34 Pux nawh pa kaux Saw-mawx koe kawn simex loe kaux,
mawh Law-cia,
Yie-hu-pa,
Ya-lang heue.
35 Pux nawh pa kaux Heu-thangd koe kawn simex pon kaux,
mawh Saw-fax,
Ying-na,
See-li-see,
Ya-maw heue.
36 Doeh ceu pui Saw-fax mawh Su-yax,
Ha-ni-fu,
Su-a-leux,
Pi-lix,
Ying-la,
37 Pi-si,
Heu-teux,
Sang-max,
See-sa,
Yi-lang,
Pi-la heue.
38 Doeh ceu pui Yi-thie mawh Yie-fu-ni,
Pi-see-pa,
Ya-la.
39 Doeh ceu pui Wu-lax mawh Ya-la,
Hang-ni-yie,
Li-shiie heue.
40 In kix hoik mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-seux khawm uik.
Kix mawh simiang cub nyiex kix,
pa kied kua ka naing,
mai simiang pa mhawm kaing yuh.
Ka daux doeh ceu cad Ya-seux,
koe pui pa pon puing ka naing ra mheen liah rheng kaux heue.
