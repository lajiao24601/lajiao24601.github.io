Siyiex hoik ap tix taoh ka pui cie tix
43
Ya-keux eue,
Simiang Siyiex cao pa phan pa yuh maix krai yuh nin;
Yi-seu-liex eue,
Simiang Siyiex cao pa hoik kieh maix lih tix mawh pak peung krai yuh nin:
Paw vi lhat,
aux tag sang kia ngien ka maix;
aux kok ka ceu kaux maix,
maix mawh cie aux.
2 Yam lih maix ka daux pa kied raux,
aux ot tau mai maix;
maix tiang ka rawm klawng,
mai rawm klawng ang kheem ka maix.
Maix dig ka gaox ngu,
mai ang hax ka maix;
maix ot ka daux rao ngu,
mai ang chix mat yuh maix.
3 Kheu mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix;
aux mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh,
pa mawh cao taoh maix.
Aux tag sang teei pui Ai-cix hoek tix taoh maix pon krax pa luat luan;
aux tag sang kieh pui Ku-seu mai pui Si-pa kha rhawm tix tang maix.
4 Ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
maix kied koe ngawh koe jum tete;
aux muh ka maix,
aux kieh maix ting ceu.
Aux kawn cu tix tawx ju meung tang meung hoek tix taoh ka maix,
kawn cu tix tawx ju pui tang kaux hoek tix loh ka krax pa im ju maix.
5 Paw vi lhat,
kheu ot aux tau mai maix heue!
 
Jauh tom plak lih singaix mai plak klip singaix ka dee kied singai khankix,
aux tag sang thung ka paox ceu paox cad maix deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix.
6 Aux tag sang tawx lox ka plak laong kieh kix hu;
aux tag sang krai ka plak seh ang kieh giex kix ot.
Kieh pui cie aux kaoh khaing haktiex dee kied singai,
kaoh khaing haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
mai deeh hoek ka meung tix.
7 Kix mawh pui cie aux,
aux hoik phan yuh kix son min plak ngai tix heue.
Yi-seu-liex mawh pui pa kham sakse Simiang Siyiex
8 Siyiex ah nin:
kok pui cie aux hoek ka nyiex dee prawng see.
Kix koe ngai,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix yaox;
kix koe yhaok,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix mhong.
9 Kok meung ka piang haktiex mai pui ka piang haktiex hoek ka nyiex dee prawng see.
Koe siyiex meung mawx pa pon grox tawng ka kaing pa sang hoek lie?
Koe pui mawx pa pon dha tix krai grawng kaing pa sang lih tix mawh lie?
Nhie siyiex an kix viex pui pa kham sakse kix hoek,
kham sakse grawng ang kix koe krax lud krax loe,
kieh pui yaox grawng lox krai kix mawh tete heue.
 
10 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex mawh pui pa kham sakse aux;
aux laug pex mawh siliex tix,
kieh pex tawng yung ka tix,
yum rhawm ka tix,
tawng grawng mawh aux Siyiex tix kaux khaing.
Lheu khaing aux,
ang lai koe Siyiex tang kaux;
sivoe an ang koe,
singaix pa sang hoek kawx ang chix koe heue.
 
11 Mawh ngie aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tix kaux khaing;
mawh ngie aux pa mawh cao taoh pex tix kaux khaing.
12 Aux sang krai pao ka pex kaing pa tag sang lih;
aux hoek tix teem coi ka pex.
Ang koe siyiex meung tang kaux tix kaux kawx pa hoik pon yuh ka kaing yuh nin;
pex mawh pui pa kham sakse aux.
13 Aux mawh Siyiex,
mawh Siyiex leeing ju leeing phan.
Ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon bheeik luan khaing daux tawm taix aux;
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tieh loh ka kaing pa yuh aux heue.
Bheeik luan khaing Pa-pi-loeng
14 Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh,
cao taoh pex krai yuh nin:
son sang taoh ka pex,
aux sang ploi pui naing hu puing ka Pa-pi-loeng;
aux tag sang yieh laux ka diang siviex veng nawh;
lox gaux rhawm pui Pa-pi-loeng tag sang tieh tix lih mawh lox pa yiiem grai.
15 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex pa singax singeh,
cao pa phan pa yuh Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh simiang pex heue.
16 Sivoe an dix,
Simiang Siyiex paih krax tix nyaing ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
phan yuh krax ting tix nyaing ka daux pu pak rawm.
17 Nawh hoik kieh pui naing pa kied ting riang tix cub,
ling law mai brung naing laux leeing khawm uik.
Kix hoik goh tix yum khawm uik,
mai ang lai baox pon kaoh cung;
kix hoik laux hoik leeing,
mai awm ngu tok pa hoik ghrip dut yuh nan heue.
 
18 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
paw vi lai si muad tix keud ka kaing pa hoik luan;
paw vi lai ot ka daux pa keud ka singaix pa hoik luan.
19 Jhak heue,
aux tag sang yuh ka kaing ceu pa khraox lih koe,
kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh,
pex hoik pon tix yaox ka heue!
Aux tag sang paih krax koe ka daux tiag,
kaong rawm klawng koe ka daux pak mhaik.
20 Pie khawm to sat kawx tag sang yung yi ka aux;
sox tiag mai sim ka-la-uk hoik rhax lai yaog yaw ka aux khawm uik;
mawh kheu hoik kieh aux rawm klawng koe ka daux pak mhaik,
hoik paih rawm keet koe ka daux tiag,
kieh pui cie aux pa hoik laug aux yaox tix nyaux rawm.
21 Kix hoik mawh pui pa phan yuh aux son to kaux tix;
kix tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka aux heue.
Phi map pui Yi-seu-liex
22 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Viang mawh nan,
Ya-keux eue,
maix ang bax krub ka aux;
Yi-seu-liex eue,
yuh pa tix maix lai tik vud ka aux.
23 Maix ang bax gleeing tix tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing yung ka aux,
ang bax tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie nab yam yung yi ka aux.
Aux ang bax khiox khaw krax tan,
mai thiem tie ka beeh glawm maix,
ang bax khiox khaw ka nyawng bhawng pa tan,
kieh maix ang pon yuh.
24 Maix ang tie bax liag ka nyawm bhawm pa tan son aux,
ang tie bax tan lu bee Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka aux.
Deeh tieh tix,
phi map maix lih mawh beeh glawm ka aux;
krax pa phit lud maix hoik kieh aux taug mao sigrien sigre.
25 Viang mawh nan,
aux mawh ngie Siyiex tix kaux khaing.
Aux pluat ploi ka phi map maix;
aux ang bax pawk bix ka maix kheu phi map pa yuh maix heue.
 
26 Haok ex tau ka nyiex prawng see,
maix chix tix krai lao;
maix chix tix krai lih ka grawh grawng see,
kham sakse grawh grawng pa koe krax maix.
27 Tax du maixa hoik yuh ka phi map;
simiang maix hoik tieh vheen ka aux;
28 simiang maix hoik seeing brai yuh nyiex pa singax aux.b
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang yuh Yi-seu-liex laux lak pak leeing;
aux tag sang kieh Ya-keux kham krax kaig krax tua heue.
