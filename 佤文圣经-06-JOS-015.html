Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Yo-tax
15
Plak greem mawh haktiex pa guah ceu pui nyiex ma Yo-tax ku nyiex pon:
Haktiex kix vih tix plak seh hoek tom mee Yi-tong,
bleeh hoek daux tiag Shing plak seh.
2 Mee haktiex plak seh jauh ka plak seh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
3 plak seh jauh ka diang moing gawng Ya-kheu-la-ping hoek ka Shing,
mai baox jauh ka Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax plak seh hoek tom plak laong,
tiang ka Si-see-loeng,
tom hoek ka Ya-ta-eu,
mai baox veeh tix hoek ka Cia-cia,
4 tom hoek ka Ya-meung,
mai baox hawt ka praok rawm Ai-cix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
In mawh mee haktiex ceu pui Yo-tax plak seh heue.
5 Mee haktiex plak lih singaix mawh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
haok plak piang hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang laig ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog.
Mee haktiex plak laong jauh ka dee laig dee an,
6 phat hoek tom Paw Heu-la,
plak laong hoek ka gawng dee jhak ka daux lung rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
plak laong hoek tom simaox Paw-hang (Paw-hang mawh kawn simex Lui-piien heue),
7 baox jauh ka “ngawd krax laux leeing” hoek tom Ti-pix,
plak laong hoek ka Cie-cia.
(Plak an Cie-cia mawh lung plak seh diang moing gawng Ya-tu-ming) Mee haktiex baox hoek tom Yin See-mai,
laig ka Yin Law-cie heue,
8 hawt ka daux ngawd Sing-noeng-cee tom hoek ka gawng plak seh,
dex mai plak seh Yie-pu-see (Yie-pu-see mawh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung).
Baox plak piang hoek ka piang gawng plak klip singaix daux ngawd Sing-noeng,
pa mawh mee plak laong daux ngawd Li-fa-ying;
9 baox hoek ka dee lih rawm Ni-fu-to-ya,
hoek ka veng yaong dex ka gawng Yi-fu-loeng.
Khaix an,
veeh tix hu ka Pa-la (Veng Pa-la mawh veng Ci-liex Yie-ling) heue.
10 Vung viang ka plak klip singaix Pa-la hoek ka haktiex Yi-tong,
si doe tix mai plak laong gawng Yie-ling (baox kok mawh gawng Ci-sha-loeng),
plak greem hoek ka Paw See-mai,
tiang ka Thing-na,
11 Khaix an hoek ka gawng plak laong Yi-keu-loeng,
baox hoek ka See-ci-loeng,
tiang ka gawng Pa-la hoek ka Ya-pi-niiex,
bleeh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
12 Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong mawh mee haktiex plak klip singaix ceu pui Yo-tax heue.
Ceu nyiex ma pui Yo-tax ot ka haktiex in heue.
Cia-leux pon kuang ka Si-paw-loeng mai Ti-pix
(Lai Cao-prawng 1.11–15)
13 aYaw-su-yax hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai guah haktiex Yo-tax tix blah ka ceu pui Yo-tax pa mawh Cia-leux kawn simex Yie-fu-ni.
Cia-leux pon ka Si-paw-loeng,
pa mawh keeing Ya-na veng Ya-pa.
14 Nawh grheu doeh ceu cad kawn simex Ya-na loe kaux lih khaing veng;
pui in kix mawh ceu pui See-sai,
ceu pui Ya-si-mang,
mai ceu pui Ta-mai.
15 Cia-leux baox puing pui Ti-pix dee an.
(Sivoe an,
ceu kaux veng Ti-pix mawh veng Ci-lie Si-fu).
16 Cia-leux ah nin:
“Pui mawx pa pon puing ka veng Ci-lie Si-fu,
aux tag tawx nawh moeng kawn bun tix Ya-sa.”
17 Eu-thaw-niiex kawn simex Ci-na-see,
pux Cia-leux puing pex ka veng an,
mawh kheu nan,
Cia-leux tom tawx nawh moeng kawn bun tix Ya-sa.
18 Singaix yuh ka paox,
Eu-thaw-niiex cheeikb Ya-sa hu chawk kaung ka keeing nawh tix dauh.
Ya-sa lih khaing la;
Cia-leux tom chawk nawh sang cie ceu pa tix,
19 Nawh ah nin:
“Aux sang cie dee lih rawm,
mawh kheu haktiex pa tawx maix ka aux mawh dee kroh.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Cia-leux tom tawx dee lih rawm plak piang khawm plak greem ka nawh heue.
