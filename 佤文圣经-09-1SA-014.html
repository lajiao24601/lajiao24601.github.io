Grawng pa kua Yaw-na-tang
14
Koe tix singaix,
Yaw-na-tang ah nin ka pui naing nhum ju pa viex kraung naing tang nawh,
pa mawh kawn cau cao pau nawh:
“Hoek,
ex hu mox tix laig ka daux nyiex bang pui Fe-li-see!”
Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang krai ka keeing tix Sao-law.
2 Yam an,
Sao-law kawn ot ka plak praix veng Ci-pi-yax,
mai sum bang ot ka Mi-ci-loeng greem khaox mak cang tix gawng.
Pui pa hawt khaix nawh koe been liah yieh kaux.
3 (Cao giex kaing li pa ceep ka sibex dha Yi-fu-teu mawh Ya-si-yax;
nawh mawh pux aik Yi-cia-paw kawn simex Ya-si-thu.
Ya-si-thu mawh kawn simex Fe-ni-ha,
pa mawh kawn saux Yi-lix pa yuh cao giex kaing li ka See-law.)
Ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa tawng Yaw-na-tang hoik lih hu khaing bang heue.
4 Yaw-na-tang sang mox tix laig ka daux nyiex bang pui Fe-li-see,
caw tix laig tiang ka diang moing gawng Mi-maw.
Ka diang moing gawng khawm ra daog,
rang simaox ting koe ka ra mu,
tix mu kok mawh Paw-shiiex,
an tix mu kok mawh Si-ni;
5 tix mu ot ka diang moing gawng plak laong veeh tix plak Mi-mawx,
tix mu ot ka diang moing gawng plak seh,
veeh tix plak Cia-pa.
6 Yaw-na-tang ah nin ka pui nhum ju kawn cau cao pa pau tix an:
“Hoek,
ex hu laig ka daux bang pui an kix pa ang bax Pat-hak tix khankix!
Mai Simiang Siyiex chix teem coi ka ex.
Phan nawh teem coi ka ex,
pui ang pon tieb tie ka ex.
Viang kaux pui ex tux kawx,
mai nawh tag kieh ex puing pex heue.”
7 Kawn cau cao pa pau an ah nin:
“Maix yuh ceu pa tix ang aha,
aux tag hawt ka maix heue.”
8 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Mhawm heue!
Ex hu phaox,
ex su tix kieh pui Fe-li-see yaox tix.
9 Phan kix sang kieh ex ot tin mai krox kix hoek,
ex tag sang krox kix tin heue.
10 Phan kix sang kieh ex haok dee ot kix tan,
ex tom haok;
an hoik mawh mhai sakse pa kieh Simiang Siyiex ex pon pex heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom su tix kieh pui Fe-li-see yaox tix.
Pui Fe-li-see ah nin:
“Jhak,
koe pui Si-paw-lai lih khaing daux dawh pa hoik mox kix tix ot ka heue!”
12 Kix tom kok yo ah nin ka Yaw-na-tang mai pui pa pau an:
“Haok tin heue!
Yix sang koe lox krai ka pexb!”
Yaw-na-tang ah nin ka pui pa pau tix an:
“Haok tau mai aux!
Simiang Siyiex hoik ap kix ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
13 Yaw-na-tang haok ka diang moing gawng;
pui pa pau nawh hawt khaix nawh.
Yaw-na-tang blok pui Fe-li-see goh;
mai pui pa pau an pauh ka kix.
14 Ka daux naing bawg in,
Yaw-na-tang mai pui pa pau nawh hoik pauh pui koeng yum been tix nga kaux ka daux haktiex tix kongching pon son tix son.
15 Can pui Fe-li-see pa ot ka daux kaung khankix,
hoik si yaung si kua khawm uik;
pui naing kuan krax mai pui naing jawm khankix kawx hoik khi rhawm khi rhi,
haktiex hoik yung yang,
pui khawm uik hoik tang pru praoh yaoh yee heue!
Pui Fe-li-see ang pex ka naing
16 Pui naing Sao-law pa pau bang ot ka Ci-pi-yax haktiex Piien-ya-ming,
yaox grawng krax lhat graung yaog pui Fe-li-see tix to.
17 Sao-law tom ah nin ka kix:
“Sawg si kaw ka pui naing,
mai jhak tix yaox mawh pui mawx pa ang ot!”
Kix tom simin kah,
mai tawng mawh Yaw-na-tang mai pui pa pau nawh pa ang ot.
18 Sao-law ah nin ka Ya-si-yax cao giex kaing li:
“Viex Yi-fu-teu hoek!”
(Ngaix an,
Ya-si-yax viex ka Yi-fu-teuc hoek sivoe ngai pui Yi-seu-liexd.)
19 Yam kawn si kah Sao-law mai cao giex kaing li,
ka daux bang dee ot pui Fe-li-see nied ot nied suk sag,
Sao-law ah nin ka cao giex kaing li:
“Yix ang tien yaox tix rawg mhaing ka Simiang Siyiex heue!”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law thung pui naing tix preeng tix laig daux bang naing,
puing ka pui Fe-li-see;
pui Fe-li-see hoik suk sag khawm uik,
mai ra lai pauh ka paox tix.
21 Koe pui Si-paw-lai tix blah pa hoik deuh rhawm tix ot plak pui Fe-li-see pa mawh cao pa khre ka daux bang kix,
yam in kawx hoik dee veeh tixe laig mawh pui naing cie Sao-law mai pui naing cie Yaw-na-tang.
22 Pui Yi-seu-liex pa hoik mox tix ot ka haktiex Yi-fa-liien khankix mhong grawng pa hoik to pui Fe-li-see,
kix kawx hoek tix leue ka pui Fe-li-see,
23 mai puing kix tom hoek ka Paw Ya-weung plak an.
Ngaix an,
Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Kaing khaix hoik uik naing
24 Ka daux ngaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik taug mao nang,
cai rhawm nang,
mawh kheu Sao-law hoik tiex tu,
mai tawx lox ah nin:
“Ka daux ngaix in,
sivoe sang pawk aux bix ka pui pa koeng khankix,
pui mawx ih priex preem,
pui an tag sang hoek krax jhax lawg.”
Mawh kheu nan,
leeing lang singaix mai ang koe pui pa pon ih ka priex preem.
25 Kixf hoek ka dee nong khaox daux tiag,
mai rawm hia kied haoh kah.
26 Ka daux nong khaox rawm hia hoik jauh ka khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa pon ih kah,
mawh kheu lhat kix ka lox pa hoik tiex tu Sao-law.
27 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-na-tang ang nyang mhong lox ghlat keeing tix pui ka lox pa tiex tu,
mai ang cu pui ih priex preem,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom nyeu ka khaox gix tix,
blung laig daux nyiex hia,
mai teei tix ih ka rawm hia ceeix.
Nawh mhong koe riang tix dum mawx dum an heue.
28 Koe pui krai nawh mhong ah nin:
“Ex hoik kied cai rhawm mai taug mao yuh nin,
viang mawh nan,
keeing maix hoik ghlat ka ex ah nin:
‘Ka daux in singaix,
pui mawx ih ka priex preem,
pui an tag sang hoek krax jhax lawg heue.’ ”
29 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Keeing aux tawx been pa ang khaw yuh nin!
Jhak,
aux ih ka rawm hia mai hoik mhong koe riang tix ka heue!
30 Khaix hoik pex ex ka pui koeng,
phan ex ih ka priex preem pa pon tix,
ex rien tix nie koe riang ka tix yieh son,
mai pon pauh ka pui Fe-li-see tom nie heue!”
31 Daux ngaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex pex ka pui Fe-li-see,
jauh ka Mi-mawx kix puing tom hoek ka Ya-ya.
Yam in,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik kied awn riang ka cai rhawm tix;
32 kix nied tix teei ka yung,
moi,
mai kawn moi iak pa pon tix khaing naing,
pauh ka dee ot kix tan,
mai ih khawm nex pa koe nham kah.
33 gKoe pui krai ka Sao-law ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
kawn pui ih nex pa koe nham kah,
hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex heue!”
Sao-law ah lox ting ah nin:
“Pex pui pa brai in kix!
Nied pu plie simaox ting hoekh dee ot aux tin!”
34 Nawh baox tawx lox ah nin:
“Hoek ka kawn pui tan,
krai kix mhong,
nhie kix grheu phung moi tix mai phung yung tix hoek,
pauh tin ih nex tin;
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix ih ka nex pa koe nham phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
ka daux bo an,
kawn pui grheu ka moi kix hoek,
mai pauh ka dee an.
35 Sao-law yuh khu dee tan tix mu son Simiang Siyiex;
mai in hoik mawh khu dee tan pa jauh nawh tix yuh ka daux tix mu heue.
36 Sao-law ah nin ka pui pa ot greem tix:
“Plak pon bo ex hu puing ka pui Fe-li-see,
tud ka kraung khrai kix,
tom hoek riang praix,
mai pauh kix yum khawm uik heue.”
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix keud khaw yuh ka mawx,
ex tag hawt yuh nan heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
cao giex kaing li ah nin:
“Ex ran rawg mhaing ka Siyiex sivoe ha!”
37 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law rawg mhaing ka Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang khaw aux tix lih puing ka pui Fe-li-see lie?
Maix tag sang ap kix ka daux tawm taix pui Yi-seu-liex lie?”
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux ngaix an Siyiex ang pawk.
38 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law ah nin ka simiang kawn pui Yi-seu-liex khankix:
“Hoek ka aux tin,
si kaw ka phi map pa yuh ex daux in singaix.
39 Aux ah lox tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex im ju leeing ju,
pa mawh cao taoh pui Yi-seu-liex,
pui pa yuh phi map ku kaux tag sang kieh yum;
die mawh kawn simex aux Yaw-na-tang kawx,
ang pon tix prah ka heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon nye.
40 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law ah nin ka kix:
“Pex cung plak an tan,
Yaw-na-tang mai aux cung plak in tin heue.”
Kix ah nin:
“Maix keud khaw yuh ka mawx,
hawt kieh mawh nan heue!”
41 jSao-law ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
daux in singaix yuh ka mawx maix lai ang pawk lox aux lie?
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
rawg maix pawk lox aux khaing simaox pa singax singeh in liak!
Phan phi map koe kheu Yaw-na-tang,
phan ang mawh nan koe kheu aux,
rawg maix teei Wu-ling pawk kah;
phan phi map koe kheu pui cie maix Yi-seu-liexi,
rawg maix teei Thu-ming pawk kah.”
Pa teei lih mawh Yaw-na-tang mai Sao-law;
kawn pui tag ang lai yaox ka kaing ceu pa tid tix.
42 Sao-law baox ah nin:
“Mawh kawn simex aux Yaw-na-tang lie?
Phan ang mawh nan mawh aux lie?”
Dee si dauh mawh Yaw-na-tang pa caw tix teei lih.
43 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix hoik yuh kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Aux hoik ih ka rawm hia ceeix.
Aux ot tin,
aux cu tix yum.”
44 Sao-law ah nin ka nawh:
“Phan ang kieh maix yum,
kieh Siyiex pauh ka aux!”
45 Viang mawh nan,
kawn pui ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Yaw-na-tang hoik thung ex puing pex ka daux min plak ngai,
khex kawn sang khaw tix kieh nawh yum lie?
Ang!
Ex tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
pie hauk kaing nawh tix gawng kawx,
ang pon tix kieh pui yuh laux.
Kaing pa hoik yuh nawh ka daux singaix,
hoik mawh ka daux krax pa teem coi Siyiex heue.”
Yuh nin,
kawn pui taoh ka Yaw-na-tang,
mai ang kieh nawh yaox tix yum.
46 Sao-law ang lai baox leue ka pui Fe-li-see,
kix deeh ing ka haktiex ot kix heue.
Krax naing pa pex Sao-law mai ceu pui nyiex ma nawh
47 Khaix hoik giex Sao-law ka daux pa yuh tix simiang pui Yi-seu-liex kaux cong,
nawh tauh hu puing ka pui koeng ku dauh,
nawh puing ka simiang pui Maw-ya,
Ya-meung,
Yi-tong,
Saw-pa khankix,
mai pui Fe-li-see.
Nawh hu dee mawx ang ah,
hoik pon tix pexk.
48 Sao-law kied kua ka puing naing,
mai hoik pex nang ka pui Ya-ma-lix.
Nawh hoik taoh pui Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing krax khi pa kaoh khaing pui tang ceu tang cad heue.
49 Kawn simex Sao-law mawh Yaw-na-tang,
Yi-see-we,
Me-ji Su-yax.
Kawn yex nawh kaux Mi-la,
kawn bun daux ra kaux mawh Mi-cia.
50 Moeng nawh mawh Ya-si-nuang pa mawh kawn bun Ya-si-ma-see.
Cao kaing pui naing nawh,
mawh kawn simex pux keeing Ni-eu pa kaux Ya-ni-eu.
51 Keeing Sao-law pa kaux Ci-see,
keeing Ya-ni-eu pa kaux Ni-eu,
hoik mawh kawn simex Ya-piie heue.
52 Ka daux ju Sao-law,
hoik yuh naing ting ka pui Fe-li-see jheeng jheeng,
mawh kheu nan,
yam yaox nawh ka pui pa kua khankix,
nawh tom sawg kix mawh pui naing,
mai kieh kix yuh pui naing heue.
