Siyiex mai pui cie nawh
9
Aux krai lox tete ka pex;
aux mawh pui cie Khrit,
aux ang krai lox pie lhien.
Rhawm rhi aux kawx ot ka daux pa kia ngien Khuan Singax Singeh,
hoik kham sakse ang aux bax krai lox pa pie lhien yuh nin:
2 krax pa khi rhawm aux kied sidaing cien,
rhawm rhi aux kied laux tete heue!
3 Son paox ceu paox cad tix,
son pux aik kawn keeing tix,
viang hoik kieh to kaux aux pon krax pa jhax lawg Siyiex,
guah tix tang hu khaing Khrit,
aux kawx cu ka heue.
4 aKix mawh pui cie Siyiex;
Siyiex hoik kieh kix koe nyum cie kawn simex kawn bun tix,
mai pon ka min plak ngai nawh.
Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
tawx kix koe lox tat jhawm prawng;
kix hoik koe ngai lai tix tawng grawng sang krub ka Siyiex,
mai hoik rieb ka gum lox pa deuh nawh.
5 Kix mawh kawn praix cao giex kaing ceu pui kix,
phan hawt krai ka to kaux kix,
Khrit mai kix hoik mawh pui tix ceu.
Kieh Siyiex pa kuang ka piang khawm uikb,
pon krax pa yaog yaw leeing ju,
A-meung!
6 Ang mawh krai aux gum lox Siyiex mawh pa giex pa going.
Mawh kheu pui Yi-seu-liex ang mawh pui cie Siyiex khawm uik;
7 ckawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang kawx ang mawh kawn simex kawn bun nawh tete khawm uik.
Siyiex hoik sax ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Mawh ngie pa keh khaing Yi-sha pa taug sawn tix mawh kawn praix maix khaing.”
8 In mawh yuh nin,
pui pa lawk keh yuh nan ang dak mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex,
mawh ngie pa keh khaing gum lox pa ap Siyiex pa taug sawn tix mawh kawn simex kawn bun nawh.
9 eMawh kheu gum lox pa ap Siyiex hoik krai yuh nin:
“Yam hoik hoek ka daux bawg yam pa khawd,
aux tag sang deeh hoek,
Sa-la tag keh kawn simex tix kaux heue.”
10 Ang joeng mawh ngie nan,
kawn simex Li-pai-cia ra kaux hoik mawh keeing tix kaux khaing,
pa mawh pa keh tax du ex Yi-sha.
11-12 fKheu nan,
son sang jhi ka pa hawt nawh alo tix,
grawng hoik laug kawn simex ka daux kix tix kaux,
Siyiex krai ka Li-pai-cia ah nin:
“Kawn simex pa ting an tag sang yuh son kawn simex pa iak heue.”
Yam krai nawh lox in,
kix ang bax nyang keh,
ang bax nyang yuh kaing pa mhawm mai kaing pa caox pa caig;
ex chix tix yaox ka pa laug Siyiex,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo to kaux nawh,
ang koe grawh grawng tau mai kaing yuh kix.
13 gAwm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Aux muh ka Ya-keux,
lheu khaing muh Yi-sao heue.”
14 Phan mawh nan,
ex kawn sang krai Siyiex yuh kaing pa ang khaw lie?
Ang doh mawh.
15 hMawh kheu krai nawh ka Maw-si ah nin:
“Aux sang caox rhawm ka pui mawx,
tag sang caox rhawm kah,
pui an muh tix khaw rhawm ka pui mawx,
tag sang khaw rhawm ka pui an.”
16 Ex chix tix yaox pa laug Siyiex ang mawh pa hawt ka rhawm keud kawn pui,
mai krax pa sau nawh riang tix yuh,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing krax pa caox rhawm nawh.
17 iKa daux phuk lai sigang khri,
Siyiex ah nin ka Pha-raw:
“Aux yaog maix mawh simiang,
hoik mawh son sang teei tix kieh maix jhi ka siawp sibrawm aux,
son sang kieh ceu kaux aux tauh hu ka piang haktiex heue.”
18 Kheu yuh nin,
Siyiex muh tix caox rhawm ka pui mawx,
tag sang caox rhawm;
muh tix kieh pui mawx kiien rhawm,
tag sang kieh kiien rhawm heue.
Krax song rhawm mai krax caox rhawm Siyiex
19 Maix tee chix krai ka aux ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
Siyiex sang chix ne raw kawn pui yuh ka mawx?
Pui mawx sang pon tieb tie ka alo nawh lie?”
20 jViang mawh nan,
paox grawm eue,
mawh maix mawx,
maix lai pon thung thiang ka Siyiex lie?
Aw tiex pa yuh pui sang pon krai ka cao pa yuh tix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai phan yuh aux yuh nin lie?”
21 Cang mai yuh aw an koe akhuang tix teei tiex glaox yuh ka ceu pa sang lo tix;
nawh chix tix teei lua tiex glaox pa awm paox yuh kraung siao ka ra mu,
tix mu mawh pa ting ngawh jum,
tix mu mawh pa iak ngawh jum heue.
22 Ceu pa yuh Siyiex kawx hoik mawh nan nang.
Nawh tag sang jhi ka krax pa song rhawm tix,
mai ka siawp sibrawm tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh teei krax gun yeung,
mai krax va rhawm ka pui pa song rhawm tix kah,
pa mawh kraung siao pa khaw tix sang tawk laux khankix.
23 Nawh kawx sang jhi min plak ngai pa kied ting tix ka ex.
Ex mawh pui pa kied caox rhawm nawh,
hoik mawh pui pa rien khre nawh hoek tix rieb ka min plak ngai nawh khankix.
24 Mawh kheu ang ex joeng kaoh khaing pui Yo-thaix,
mai mawh nang pa kaoh khaing pui tang ceu,
pa kok ex hoek.
25 kIn hoik mawh pa krai nawh ka daux Lai Heu-si-ax ah nin:
kix pa ang mawh pui cie aux,
aux tag sang kok kix mawh “pui cie tix”;
meung tang meung pa ang aux muh,
aux tag sang kok mawh “pa muh tix”.
26 lAux hoik sax krai ka kix dee mawx ah nin:
pex ang mawh pui cie aux,
ka dee an tag sang kok kix mawh:
kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex pa im leeing ju heue!
27 mPhan mawh son pui Yi-seu-liex,
Yi-sai-yax hoik sax kok yo ah nin:
“Viang pui Yi-seu-liex hoik haoh hun awm mhaik ka praok rawm sigrit sigra yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
mawh ngie pui tux pa pon krax taoh khaing,
28 mawh kheu Simiang Ye-sux tag sang nied tix phai hoek prawng ca kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.”
29 nAwm hoik krai Yi-sai-yax sivoe an ah nin:
“Phan ang mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing,
pa deuh doeh ceu cad tix blah,
ex hoik dawg awm Saw-to-ma,
mai Eu-maw-la yuh nan heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai gum lox buan
30 Phan mawh nan,
sang khaw ex tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Pui tang ceu tang cad pa ang sawg ka pa rauh pa see Siyiex khankix,
viang mawh nan,
hoik pon tix lih mawh pui pa rauh see kheu krax yum rhawm.
31 Kheu nan,
pui pa laug Siyiex pa pon tix rauh see kheu pa sawg tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng khankix,
mai tieh tix ang pon hoek ka dee sang hu tix.
32 Yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh kheu ang kix koe krax yum rhawm,
mai hoik een rhawm tix ka kaing pa yuh tix,
dee si dauh sideeing caong tix ka piang “simaox sideeing caong” an.
33 oAwm hoik krai ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
jhak heue!
Aux hoik deuh simaox sideeing caong ka Shi-ang tix lon,
mawh simaox pa sideeing caong kix heue!
Kheu nan,
pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh ang yaox tix som nai heue.
