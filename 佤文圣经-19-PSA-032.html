Pa doh keud ka phi map mai pa pluat ploi
32
aPhi map pon krax pa pluat ploi,
pa lud loe pon krax pa va rhawm,
nawh hoik kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
2 Rhawm rhi pa ang koe krax gut kheem,
mai ang pon krax prawng ca Simiang Siyiex,
nawh hoik kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
 
3 Yam nyip aux jheeh tix ang krai doh ka phi map,
mai yiiem leeing lang singaix,
to kaux aux khawm uik hoik taug hoik mao.
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix ciyang aux leeing ngaix leeing sawm;
aux hoik uik riang uik rao,
mai awm bhawm rawm pa chu tix lih yam si aoh praix heue.
 
5 Mawh kheu nan,
aux sang krai doh keud phi map tix ka maix;
aux ang mox krax pa lud loe yuh to kaux tix.
Aux hoik but rhawm tix sang krai ka maix sidawng khawm uik,
maix tom pluat ploi ka phi map aux khawm uik heue.
 
6 Mawh kheu nan,
can pui pa koe krax nab yam yung yi,
tag caw tix jawm yam sawg maix kawn puib mai khiox khaw ka maix;
Yam haok huan rawm ting,
rawm tom ang lai yaox tix kheem,
7 Maix mawh dee mox ka aux;
maix tag sang chix taoh aux luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing tete.
Aux rhax lai yaog yaw krax pa taoh maix tom ting,
mawh kheu hoik kia ngien maix ka aux heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
aux sang jhi ka krax pa sang khaw maix tix lawng;
aux sang sidiex gau ka maix,
mai krai ka maix ku bawg ku yam;
9 Ang kieh tix daox aw awm brung law yuh nan,
tag sang teei maox keu mai jhawk jhup jheeh phrawk kaux,
tom hoik pon kieh nawh cu lox heue.
 
10 Pui yuh pa caox pa caig tag sang yaox krax pa kha rhawm tom nie tete;
pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai krax muh vung viang ka nawh.
11 Pui rauh see eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex;
can pui pa yuh kaing pa khaw caw eue,
mai pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi khawm uik heue.
