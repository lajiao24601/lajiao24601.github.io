Khaox glawm cie Yie-li-mi
27
aSingaix bawg yam jauh Si-ti-cia kawn simex Yaw-si-yax tix mawh simiang pa giex kaing ka piang meung Yo-tax,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux
2 teei hak mai khaox yuh khaox glawm ka tix mu,
mai vhak ot ka piang ngawg tix.
3 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux,
kieh mawh lox kawn cau pa ting khankix,
pa kum ka simiang Si-ti-cia ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai toe tix tawx gum lox in plak greem ka simiang Yi-tong,
simiang Maw-ya,
simiang Ya-meung,
simiang Thai-eux,
mai simiang Si-toeng.
4 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex tawx lox nhie aux kieh kawn cau pa ting an kix ku kaux,
deeh toe tix tawx lox nawh ka simiang haktiex;
nawh ah nin:
5 “Aux teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix phan yuh ka haktiex,
khawm kawn pui ka piang haktiex,
mai can to tiag ku ceu khawm uik.
Aux tag sang ap tix tawx haktiex ka pui mawx,
tag sang tawx heue.
6 Yam in aux hoik hoek tix tawx meung in kix ka siliex aux simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
kieh nawh mawh cao pa giex kaing,
pie khawm to tiag kawx hoik ap tix tawx ka daux tawm taix nawh.
7 Can meung haktiex khawm uik tag sang nghiet ka nawh,
mai ka greem kawn simex nawh,
kawn saux nawh,
tom hoek iak riang meung pa giex nawh kaing.
Hoek yam an,
nawh tag sang yuh kaing son simiang pa kied ting riang ting rao an kix heue.
8 “Phan koe ceu pui tix ceu ceu,
phan ang mawh nan,
meung tix mu mu ang ah,
ang cu tix nghiet ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
ang cu tix klawm ka khaox glawm pa sau ot ka ngawg kix,
aux tag sang kieh naing,
sibhawm praix,
mai jhawm saux preng ciyang ka meung an,
tom hoik kieh aux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha yuh meung an laux lak pak leeing.
9 Phan mawh pex,
pex ang pon tix nghiet ka lox cao jhai pex,
lox cao si pawk pex,
lox cao pa kah ka ca maox pex,
lox cao pa sawn ka ju pex,
mai lox cao pa chix si meed khankix.
Pui in kix rom jheeh tix ang cu pex nghiet ka lox simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
10 tete te lai,
kix hoik pie lhien ka pex,
sang kieh pui giex laung ka pex,
viex pex hu singai khaing haktiex tix.
Aux tag sang ploi pex hu;
pex tag sang laux leeing.
11 Viang mawh nan,
meung mawx ang ah,
pa cu tix klawm ka khaox glawm pa sang sau simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
cu tix nghiet ka nawh,
aux tag sang kieh meung an nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux haktiex tix,
sum ka kaung ma haktiex tix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
12 Aux kawx hoik krai lox pa awm paox ka simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Si-ti-cia ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix klawm ka khaox glawm pa sang sau simiang Pa-pi-loeng ot ka piang pex,
mai cu nghiet ka nawh mai ka pui cie nawh;
yuh nin,
pex tom taug pon yaox tix klawd ju.
13 Maix mai pui cie maix yuh ka mawx lai siao kawn yuh tix kieh krax pa laux leeing ka naing,
ka sibhawm praix,
mai jhawm saux preng hoek piang tix,
mai kieh tix yum ka lie?
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai sidawng,
meung mawx ang cu tix nghiet ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
meung an tag kieh krax laux leeing hoek piang tix.
14 Pex ang pon tix nghiet ang pon tix yum rhawm ka cao jhai khankix,
pa ang cu pex kreet tix ot greem simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Kix hoik pie lhien ka pex.
15 Simiang Siyiex hoik hoek tix krai,
nawh ang bax ploi kix hu,
mawh kix pa pie lhien pui ka daux ceu kaux nawh.
Phan pex nghiet,
phan pex yum rhawm ka kix,
aux tag sang ploi ka pex;
pex mai cao jhai pa pie lhien khankix tag sang laux leeing heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
aux sang toe tix krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka cao giex kaing li,
mai ka kawn pui khankix ah nin:
“Pex ang pon tix nghiet,
ang pon tix yum rhawm ka gum lox pa krai cao jhai khankix,
krai kraung pa ting ngawh pa ot ka daux nyiex buan,
ang koe miex glang tix tag sang gah tix deeh hoek khaing Pa-pi-loeng.
Kix hoik pie lhien ka pex.
17 Pex paw vi yum rhawm ka lox kix heue!
Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
tom tag pon yaox tix klawd ju heue!
Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox simiang Pa-pi-loeng tom taug yaox tix im ju!
Veng in chix lih tix mawh dee riem yuh ka mawx lie?
18 Phan pex mawh cao jhai pa mawh pa meh,
mai koe ka gum lox pa tawx aux,
kix tag sang khaw tix khiox khaw ka aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
khiox khaw aux paw cu pui gah kraung ngawh pa hoik deuh ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung hu ka Pa-pi-loeng heue.”
19-20 Yam giex laung simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka Yaw-ya-cin kawn simex Yaw-ya-cing simiang Yo-tax,
khawm cao kaing pui Yo-tax,
mai simiang pui Yie-lu-sa-leung hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai deuh ka rong tawng,
dung rawm tawng,
dang ngeung tawng,
mai kraung ting ngawh pa ot ka daux nyiex buan heue.
21 Phan mawh kraung ting ngawh pa deuh ot daux nyiex buan mai ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka daux nyiex haw simiang,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex ah yuh nin:
22 “Kraung in kix tag caw tix gah hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik,
deuh ot dee an,
tom hoik hoek yam deeh teei aux ka kix.
Yam an,
aux tag sang tang kix deeh hoek,
mai deeh deuh ot ka dee ot sivoe an.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
