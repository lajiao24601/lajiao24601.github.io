Ye-sux mai kawn cau nawh
7
Khaix an,
Ye-sux hu lai ling ka baoh haktiex Cia-li-lix,
mai ang lai rhawm ot ka haktiex Yo-thaix,
mawh kheu keud simiang pui Yo-thaix tix sang pauh ka nawh.
2 aBawg yam sang yuh poi nyiex gox bang cie pui Yo-thaix dex sang hoek,
3 mawh kheu nan,
pux aik Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix lih khaing haktiex in hu ka haktiex Yo-thaix heue,
son sang kieh pui gau maix yaox ka can kaing pa yuh maix.
4 Pui phan sang kieh tix koe min,
tag ang caw tix mox tix yuh kaing.
Kheu hoik pon yuh maix ka kaing in kix,
tag caw tix yuh lih ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun heue!” (
5 Yam an pie pux aik nawh kawx kawn ang nyang bax yum rhawm ka nawh heue.)
6 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Bawg yam cie aux ang nyang bax hoek;
viang mawh nan,
phan mawh pex hoik chix tix yuh awm pa simie pex tix yuh.
7 Kawn pui piang haktiex ang chix koeng ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
koeng ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik jhi aux kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix lih ang jud.
8 Pex hu yuh poi heue,
yam in aux ang nyang haokb,
mawh kheu ang bawg yam cie aux nyang hoek heue.”
9 Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux lox in kix,
nawh ca ot ka haktiex Cia-li-lix heue.
Ye-sux yuh poi nyiex gox bang
10 Khaix hoik hu pux aik Ye-sux,
Ye-sux kawx haok yuh poi nang.
Nawh ang kieh pui tawng grawng pa lih tix khaing nyiex,
mai nawh hoik briex mox tix hu.
11 Ka daux yam yuh poi,
simiang pui Yo-thaix hu sawg Ye-sux ku dauh ku dee,
son sang kieh tix tawng ot nawh dee mawx heue.
12 Ka daux pui hun krai lao son nawh,
koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Nawh hoik mawh pui mhawm tix kaux”,
koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Ang,
nawh hoik nyaw glaw ka pui hun.”
13 Mawh ngie ang pui hun pon krai lao ka grawh grawng nawh khaing,
mawh kheu lhat kix ka simiang pui Yo-thaix heue.
14 Yam hoik greung yuh pui poi,
Ye-sux tom haok sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan.
15 Simiang pui Yo-thaix kied daox deng kah,
ah nin:
“Pui in ang sax koe sara,
yuh ka mawx nawh lai koe krax chix krax tawng yuh nin lie?”
16 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Krax pa sidiex gau aux ang mawh cie to kaux aux,
hoik mawh cie kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
17 Pui tix kaux kaux phan nawh but rhawm tix hawt tix yuh ka alo Siyiex,
nawh tag sang chix tawng kah,
krax pa sidiex gau aux mawh pa kaoh khaing alo Siyiex,
mawh pa kaoh khaing lox pa krai to kaux aux.
18 Pui pa krai khaing to kaux tix,
hoik mawh pa keud tix sang sawg ka min plak ngai to kaux tix;
viang mawh nan,
pa sawg ka min plak ngai kaw pa ploi nawh hoek an,
tag sang mawh pa mawh pa meh tete.
19 Maw-si ang hoik tawx lox tat jhawm prawng ka pex lie?
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang koe pui pa priex tix hawt ka jhawm prawng tix kaux kawx.
Yuh ka mawx pex lai keud tix sang pauh ka aux lie?”
20 Pui hun pawk ah nin:
“Phi hoik laig ka daux to kaux maix,
pui mawx keud tix sang pauh ka maix lie?”
21 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux hoik yuh kaing pa ting ceu ka daux singaix tuah tix ceu,
pex hoik een mawh pa daox deng.
22 cViang mawh nan,
Maw-si tawx lox nhie pex Pat-hak tix (tete te lai pa Pat-hak ang mawh pa kaoh khaing Maw-si,
mai hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing tax du pex yam jauh),
pex tom Pat-hak kawn ngie ka daux singaix tuah.
23 dPhan pui hoik Pat-hak ka daux singaix tuah,
kaing ting hoik mawh priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
phan mawh nan aux hoik kieh pui tix kaux pon moi ka daux singaix tuah,
yuh ka mawx pex lai ah ka aux lie?
24 Paw vi hawt tix jhak plak praix prawng ca ka pui,
tag caw tix hawt ka diag tong ceu pa khaw caw prawng ca ka heue.”
Mawh ang nawh mawh Khrit
25 Koe pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tix blah ah nin:
“Pui in ang hoik mawh pui pa keud simiang ex tix sang pauh ka lie?
26 Maix jhak,
nawh hoik krai lox ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai ang koe pui pa kaoh tieb tie ka nawh heue!
Mawh rug hoik tawng kix mawh nawh Khrit lie?
27 Viang mawh nan,
yam lih hoek Khrit,
mai ang koe pui pa tawng mawh hoek nawh khaing dee mawx,
viang mawh nan,
pui in hoek khaing dee mawx ex hoik tawng sidawng heue.”
28 Yam an,
Ye-sux sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan,
nawh krai lox tom ting ah nin:
“Mawh rug hoik tawng yung pex ka aux,
mai tawng hoik kaoh aux khaing dee mawx lie?
Aux hoek ang mawh ka daux pa hawt aux ka alo tix.
Kaw pa ploi aux hoek an hoik mawh pa mawh pa meh.
Pex ang tawng yung ka nawh,
29 viang mawh nan,
aux tawng yung ka nawh;
mawh kheu kaoh aux khaing nawh dee an,
mai mawh nawh pa ploi aux hoek heue.”
30 Mawh kheu nan,
kix keud tix sang giex ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa tawx taix tix,
mawh kheu ang bawg yam cie nawh nyang bax hoek.
31 Ka daux pui hun kawx,
koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka nawh;
kix ah nin:
“Yam lih hoek Khrit,
kawn sang lheu yuh mhai pa daox pa am khaing pui in tom nie lie?”
Pui pau giex ka Ye-sux
32 Pui Fa-li-saix mhong grawng pa krai lao pui hun ka Ye-sux,
kix mai simiang cao giex kaing li tom ploi pui pau hu giex ka Ye-sux.
33 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux kawn koe bawg yam tix sang ot tau mai pex ceeix ceeix,
khaix an tag sang deeh ing ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek dee an.
34 Pex tag sang sawg ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox;
mawh kheu dee sang hu aux,
pex ang pon hu ka heue.”
35 Simiang pui Yo-thaix khankix ra lai chawk paox tix ah nin:
“Sang keud nawh tix hu dee mawx,
mai kieh ex ang yaox tix lie?
Khex sang hu nawh dee ot pui Yo-thaix pa prung prang tix ot ka veng Si-lax dee an,
mai sang sidiex gau ka pui Si-lax lie?
36 Nawh krai ‘pex tag sang sawg ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox’ mai ‘dee sang hu aux,
pex ang pon hu’,
gum lox in dee bleeh mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
Rawm klawng pa im ju
37 eKa daux singaix pa nghoik tix yuh poi,
hoik mawh ka daux singaix pa kied ting ceu tete.
Ye-sux kaoh cung,
mai krai pao lox tom ting ah nin:
“Pui pa simie tix nyaux rawm,
tag caw hoek tix nyaux ka aux dee in.
38 gKa daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Pui pa yum rhawm ka aux khankix,
rawm klawng pa im ju tag sang jauh lih khaing ka daux rhawm rhi nawh.’ ”f
39 Gum lox pa krai Ye-sux in,
hoik mawh pa krai ka pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh khankix tag sang rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh.
Yam an Khuan Singax Singeh ang nyang bax lih hoek,
mawh kheu ang Ye-sux bax pon min plak ngai heue.
Pui hun simah paox tix kheu Ye-sux
40 Ka daux pui hun koe pui tom nie mhong ka lox in,
kix tom ah nin:
“Pui in rug mawh cao jhai tix kaux heue!”
41 Koe nang pui pa ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Khrit heue!”
Baox koe pui pa ah nin:
“Khrit sang chix lih hoek ka Cia-li-lix yuh ka mawx lie?
42 hMawh kheu hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Khrit mawh doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
tag sang lih keh ka daux haktiex Ta-wue veng Paw-li-haung heue.”
43 Mawh kheu nan,
pui hun simah paox tix kheu Ye-sux.
44 Koe pui tix blah keud tix sang giex ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa tawx taix tix heue.
Simiang pui Yo-thaix ang yum rhawm ka Ye-sux
45 Pui pau khankix deeh ing ka simiang cao giex kaing li mai ka pui Fa-li-saix;
kix chawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai ang viex Ye-sux hoek lie?”
46 Pui pau pawk ah nin:
“Ang sax koe pui tix kaux kawx pa krai lox awm nawh yuh nan heue!”
47 Pui Fa-li-saix ah nin:
“Pex kawx hoik nghiet ka lox pie lox lhien nawh lie?
48 Khex hoik yum rhawm simiang ex mai pui Fa-li-saix ka nawh lie?
49 Pui pheem in kix pa ang tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si tag khaw tix pon ka krax pa jhax pa lawg heue!”
50 iKa daux kix koe pui Ni-keu-teu-mawx;
nawh hoik hu yaox ka Ye-sux sivoe an.
Nawh ne raw kix ah nin:
51 “Lox tat jhawm prawng ex mawh prawng ca ka phi map pui sivoe sang mhong ka lox pa kham pui,
mai ka sivoe sang tawng sidawng ka kaing an lie?”
52 Kix ah nin:
“Khex mawh maix nang pui Cia-li-lix lie?
Hu sawg si kaw tix yaox ka daux phuk lai sigang khri,
maix tom taug tawng,
ka daux pui Cia-li-lix ang chix lih koe cao jhai heue.”j
