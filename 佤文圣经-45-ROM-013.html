Beeh glawm ka plak simiang haktiex
13
Pui ku kaux tag caw tix cu nghiet ka siawp sibrawm simiang haktiex,
mawh kheu siawp sibrawm pa koe kix hoik mawh pa tawx Siyiex;
siawp sibrawm simiang haktiex hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
pa tieb tie ka simiang hoik mawh pa tieb tie ka gum lox tawx Siyiex;
pui yuh nin tag ang yaox tix luat luan khaing krax prawng ca.
3 Cao giex kaing ang mawh son sang kieh pui pa yuh kaing mhawm lhat graung,
hoik mawh son sang kieh pui pa yuh kaing pa caox lhat graung.
Phan maix sang kieh tix ang lhat ka simiang haktiex,
tag caw tix yuh kaing pa mhawm,
nawh tom tag sang yaog yaw ka maix;
4 kheu nawh hoik mawh pui cau Siyiex,
kaing pa yuh nawh hoik mawh pa mhawm son maix.
Phan maix yuh kaing pa caox,
maix tag caw tix lhat graung ka nawh,
mawh kheu krax pa ciyang nawh,
ang mawh krax klieh.
Nawh mawh pui pa siao Siyiex,
sang yuh kaing krax prawng ca Siyiex ka pui koeng.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix cu nghiet ka simiang haktiex,
ang joeng mawh ngie sang lhat ka krax ciyang Siyiex,
hoik mawh nang son mhawm rhawm rhi heue.
6 aPhan pex tawx mau koh keen,
hoik mawh son grawng kaing in nang,
mawh kheu yam yuh simiang haktiex kaing khankix,
hoik mawh pa yuh son Siyiex.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
tag caw tix plud ka ceu pa hoik ceuk tix khawm uik:
ceu pa khaw tix tan lu,
tag caw tix tan lu;
ceu pa khaw tix tawx,
tag caw tix tawx.
Ceu pa khaw tix lhat graung,
tag caw tix lhat graung;
ceu pa khaw tix nab yam yung yi,
tag caw tix nab yam yung yi heue.
Beeh glawm ka pui tang kaux
8 Paw vi doh ceuk ka pui heue!
Ra lai muh ka paox tix,
in hoik mawh pa khaw pex tix ceuk kah.
Pa muh ka pui tang kaux,
hoik mawh pa kieh lox tat jhawm prawng grum naog.
9 bLox tat jhawm prawng hoik tat tix deuh ah nin:
“Ang pon tix phit vao;
ang pon tix pauh pui yum;
ang pon tix briex lak;
ang pon tix koe rhawm ghoing.”
In kix khawm uik mai khawm gum lox tawm khankix,
hoik ot ka daux “muh pui awm muh ka to kaux tix” grawng gum lox tawm in heue.
10 Pui pa muh ka pui tang kaux,
ang chix yuh kaing pa laux leeing yuh pui tang kaux.
Mawh kheu nan,
krax muh hoik kieh lox tat jhawm prawng grum naog khawm uik heue.
11 Pex tag caw tix yuh nin,
mawh kheu hoik tawng pex in mawh bawg yam ceu pa tix;
in hoik mawh bawg yam pa khaw pex tix si duad khaing it.
Yam in ex hoik nie dex ka krax taoh Siyiex khaing yam jauh ex tix yum rhawm.
12 Pa faik dex sang luan,
pon singaix tag sang hoek.
Ex ang pon tix lai yuh kaing pa kied caox;
tag caw tix teei kraung naing,
mai rien khre tix yuh naing ka daux pa riang singaix.
13 Ex tag caw yuh kaing pa rauh see ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
awm pui pa yuh kaing yam pon singaix yuh nan.
Ang pon tix pleeng tix yoek ka plai,
ang pon tix yuh krax pa phit vao,
ang pon tix boh rhawm simah paox
14 Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix kieh to kaux tix,
ceep ka Simiang Ye-sux Khrit;
paw vi keud ngie son sang kieh alo nex nham tix grum naog heue!
