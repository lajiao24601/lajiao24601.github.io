23
aYam hoik kuad ju Ta-wue,
mai ap kawn simex tix pa kaux Saw-law-meung mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Kaing pa yuh pui Li-we
2 Simiang Ta-wue khrawm can simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai cao giex kaing li khawm pui Li-we.
3 Nawh si kaw simex pui Li-we pa koe tix ngoe num haok plak piang,
kaux pui kix khawm uik koe loe mheen daix rheng kaux.
4 Simiang ploi pui ra mheen pon rheng kaux mawh cao kuang pa yuh kaing nyiex buan,
liah rheng kaux mawh cao tiiem lai mai pa prawng krax pa simah simaing,
5 pon rheng kaux mawh cao pa yuh kaing pau nyiex,
pon rheng kaux mawh pui pa siao ka ting pa tawx simiang,
son kaing pa yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex heue.
6 Ta-wue hawt grawng cub pui Li-we,
mai guah kix lih koe Keu-soeng,
Keu-siax,
mai Mi-la-lix loe cub heue.
7 Keu-soeng koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh La-tang mai See-me.
8 La-tang koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Yie-shiie,
Si-thang,
Yaw-eux,
9 kix hoik mawh cao kaing doeh ceu cad La-tang khawm uik.
(See-me koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh See-law-mix,
Ha-shiiex,
Ha-langb.)
10-11 See-me koe kawn simex pon kaux,
mai jauh ka pa ting hoek ka pa iak mawh:
Ya-ha,
Si-nax,
Yie-wu-see,
Pi-li-yax.
Doeh ceu cad Yie-wu-see mai Pi-li-yax ang nie,
mawh kheu nan tom neuex tau,
mai sawn mawh ceu pui cub nyiex tix mu khaing heue.
12 Keu-siax koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ang-lang,
Yi-see-ha,
Si-paw-loeng,
Wu-shiie.
13 cKawn ai nawh Ang-lang mawh keeing Ya-loeng mai Maw-si.
(Ya-loeng mai doeh ceu cad nawh hoik laug kix mawh pui pa kuang kraung pa singax singeh leeing ju,
mai kix koe beeh glawm tix gleeing nyawng bhawng krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
yuh kaing jhaw nawh,
mai nawb buan piang kawn pui ka daux ceu kaux nawh.
14 Viang mawh nan,
kawn simex Maw-si pui cie Siyiex khankix hoik mawh ceu pui Li-we.)
15 Maw-si koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Keu-soeng mai Yi-li-yi-shiex.
16 Simiang ka daux kawn praix Keu-soeng mawh Si-pu-yiex.
17 Yi-li-yi-shiex koe kawn simex tix kaux khaing,
pa mawh Li-ha-pi-ya;
Li-ha-pi-ya hoik koe doeh ceu cad tom nie heue.
18 Kawn simex Keu-siax ka daux ra kaux pa mawh Yi-see-ha koe kawn simex tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh See-law-mix,
nawh mawh simiang ceu pui cub nyiex kix.
19 Kawn simex Keu-siax ka daux loe kaux pa mawh Si-paw-loeng koe kawn simex pon kaux mawh Yie-li-ya,
Ya-ma-li-yax,
Ya-ha-si,
Yie-cia-miien.
20 Kawn simex Keu-siax ka daux pon kaux pa mawh Wu-shiie koe kawn simex ra kaux mawh Mi-cia mai Yie-si-ya heue.
21 Mi-la-lix koe kawn simex ra kaux pa mawh Maw-lix mai Mu-seex.
Maw-lix kawx koe kawn simex ra kaux mawh Yi-li-ya-sa mai Ci-see.
22 Khaix hoik yum Yi-li-ya-sa,
nawh ang koe kawn simex,
mawh ngie kawn bun pa koe khaing.
Kawn bun nawh khankix mex ka kawn simex pux aik nawh,
pa mawh kawn simex Ci-see khankix.
23 Kawn nyi Mi-la-lix pa kaux Mu-seex koe kawn simex loe kaux mawh Maw-lix,
Yi-teu,
Yie-li-maw heue.
24 Plak piang mawh doeh ceu cad Li-we,
kieh ceu cub pui mai ceu nyiex pui mawh tix cub,
kix ku kaux hoik tiiem ka ca rang.
Can pui pa koe tix nga num haok plak piang,
hoik kieh kix yuh kaing nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex khawm uik heue.
25 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tawx krax pa nhim ka pui cie tix;
mai to kaux nawh tag sang ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung leeing ju leeing phan.
26 dMawh kheu nan,
pui Li-we ang lai siao grawng ka nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
mai kraung tang ceu khankix pa lo tix siao yam yuh kaing krub.”
27 Hawt awm lox krai Ta-wue sivoe sang yum tix,
can pui Li-we pa naog tix nga num haok plak piang,
caw tiiem ka ca rang kix khawm uik,
28 emai tawx kix mawh pa yuh kaing in kix plak greem:
teem coi doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng pui cao giex kaing li bung pui yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex buan,
kuang kia ka biang brawk nyiex buan mai lawg nyiex khankix,
mai koe beeh glawm tix kieh kraung singax singeh.
29 Baox koe beeh glawm tix rien khre ka khao mun pa tan lu ka Siyiex,
phawng mien nghox ca krax tan,
khao mun pa ang koe su saix,
krax tan pa tong,
mai phawng mien nghox ca pa hoik klao tau mai bee sime.
Kix kawx kawn sang ciang,
mai tiag tix yaox ka been cien khawng tan nyiex buan.
30 Ku singaix pon ngawb pon bo kix tag sang yaog yaw ka min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex;
31 ku vang tuah,
yam haok khix,
phan ang mawh nan ka daux poi tang ceu,
yam yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix hawt awm been pui pa ciak tix dauh,
mai been krax pa hawt tix yuh kaing krax krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
32 Kix mawh cao pa koe beeh glawm tix kuang kia nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex mai nyiex buan,
khaix an mai teem coi pui nyiex ma kix,
pa mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng bung pui yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex heue.
