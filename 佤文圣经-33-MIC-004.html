Simiang Siyiex giex pa nhim piang haktiex
(Lai Yi-sai-yax 2.1–4)
4
Ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
nyiex buan pa ot piang gawng tag sang jung tix ka piang gawng lua khawm uik,
tag sang yaog lhaong khaing gawng pa koe khawm uik.
Pui tom nie ceu tag sang preeng tix hoek.
2 Meung piang haktiex tag sang ghrang paox tix hoek sivoe ah nin:
Hoek heue,
hu ex tau ka gawng Simiang Siyiex,
hu ex tau ka plak nyiex buan Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh tag sang chix jhi ka krax pa sang khaw ex tix lawng;
ex tag caw tix hu ka daux krax in.
Krax pa sidiex Simiang Siyiex hoek tix lih khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung;
gum lox nawh hoik jauh tix sidiex tix hu khaing gawng Shi-ang.
 
3 aNawh tag sang kieh raw ka simah pa koe daux pui ku ceu,
tieb tie ka krax pa simah pa ting.
Kix tag sang raug vaig gawn lih mawh thai,
tawk pliah lih mawh si vawg.
Ka piang haktiex khawm bee ma ang lai baox lih koe naing,
ang lai baox rien khre son sang puing naing heue.
4 bPui tag sang ot ka daux si peem plix siiet cie to kaux tix ku kaux,
ot ka greem khaox plix ruh,
pon krax pa nhim;
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa kieh kix lhat graung.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
5 Viang meung ka piang haktiex hoik krub ka siyiex tix,
cu nghiet ka siyiex tix,
viang mawh nan,
ex tag sang krub ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex,
mai hawt ka alo nawh tom si dauh leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex deeh ing ka meung haktiex tix
6 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik ciyang ka pui cie tix,
viang mawh nan,
yam hoik hoek bawg yam pa khaw,
aux tag sang khrawm pui cie tix pa vuk vied vuk viang ka haktiex ku dauh ot tau.
7 Aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix pa deuk khankix lih mawh simie pa haok huan,
pa iak riang pa rai rhuat tix lih mawh meung ting tix meung.
Aux tag sang hoek tix giex kaing piang kix ka gawng Shi-ang,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
8 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
Siyiex ot ka maix dee an kia ngien pui cie tix awm cao liang yuh nan;
maix tag sang kieh meung yaog tix kaoh,
mai deeh baox lih mawh veng ting tix bawg.
9 Yuh ka mawx maix lai yiiem grai lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai saux lu lieh tix awm bun pa sang keh yuh nan lie?
Maix ang koe simiang lie?
Pui teem maix hoik yum uik lie?
10 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
pex tag sang saux kha rhawm tix awm bun pa sang keh,
mai lu lieh tix yiiem;
mawh kheu pex tag sang lih hu khaing veng in,
mai paung ot daux tiag daux tu.
Pui tag sang giex laung pex hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang taoh pex khaing dee an,
taoh pex lih khaing daux tawm taix pui koeng.
11 Meung tom nie khrawm tix puing ka pex.
Kix ah nin:
“Ex hoek tix yuh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung laux heue!
Ex hoek tix jhak grawng pa tieh veng in tix lih mawh haktiex pa riem heue!”
12 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng krax keud pa sang yuh Simiang Siyiex,
ang tawng Simiang Siyiex sang khrawm kix ot tau mawh son sang ciyang ka kix,
awm pauh pui yuh kaing jhaw ka nghox koi yuh nan heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
hu heue!
Hu ciyang ka pui koeng ka pex heue!
Aux tag sang kieh pex ting riang awm moi mhaing pa kuah rung rhiem gip tawng yuh nan.
Pex tag sang yuh laux leeing ka meung tom nie,
mai teei sin ngao pa tud kix hu,
tan lu ka aux pa mawh cao ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.”
