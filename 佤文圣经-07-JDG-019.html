Ceu pui Li-we mai moeng iak nawh
19
Ka daux singaix baux bawg yam ang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex nyang koe simiang,
koe ceu pui Li-we tix kaux,
ot ka haktiex gawng Yi-fa-liien dee kied singai khankix.
Nawh teei tix moeng kawn bun tix kaux pa koe ka daux veng Paw-li-haung meung Yo-tax mawh moeng iak tix.
2 Kawn bun in song rhawm ka nawh,
deeh ing ka nyiex miex tix daux veng Paw-li-haung pon khix.
3 Khaix an pui Li-we an but rhawm tix hu sawg ka nawh,
son sang deeh kok nawh hoek ot tau mai tix.
Nawh viex siliex tix tix kaux hu mai la ra mu.
Bun in nhie nawh laig daux nyiex;
mai keeing moeng nawh yaox ka nawh,
kied gaux rhawm mai rieb ka nawh,
4 kawn ca kieh nawh laux tix,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom paung ot tan loe singaix.
Kiex mex moeng ot tan,
ih tau nyaux tau paung tau.
5 Ka daux pon singaix pon ngawb,
kiex kaoh cao cao,
mai rien tix sang ing;
viang mawh nan,
keeing moeng nawh ah nin ka nawh:
“Ran ih priex ceeix!
Son sang mhong maix koe riang tix,
khaix an tag hu khaix heue!”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
kiex ra kaux ngawm tix ih nyaux tau.
Keeing moeng nawh ah nin:
“Baox it paung tix sawm ha,
kieh tix gaux rhawm gaux rhawm heue!”
7 Pui Li-we sang yaog tix kaoh hu,
mai keeing moeng nawh baox mhaing nawh laux tix,
kheu nan nawh baox laux tix tix sawm.
8 Ka daux phuan ngaix pon ngawb cao,
pui Li-we rien tix sang hu;
keeing moeng nawh baox ah nin:
“Ih priex ceeix ha,
krox jun praix lhax ceeix koi hu khaix!”
Mawh kheu nan,
kiex ra kaux baox ih tau nyaux tau.
9 Yam rien pui in mai moeng iak nawh khawm siliex nawh khankix tix sang hu,
keeing moeng nawh baox ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
yam in praix hoik sang faik,
ang lai lheu khaing baox laux pex tix!
Ang lai rheung praix hoik sang faik,
laux lee tix dee in kieh tix gaux rhawm mhawm mhawm tix vud ha.
Pa sax pon ngawb cao maix chix tix yaog tix ing heue.”
10-11 Viang mawh nan,
pui in ang lai rhawm ot laux lee tix,
nawh tom kieh kiex moeng iak tix yaog tix hu.
Kiex viex la ra mu pa tang naog ka kraung khrai.
Yam dex hoek kix baoh Yie-pu-see (pa mawh baoh dee dex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung),
mai praix hoik lhax,
siliex ah nin ka cao simiang tix:
“Ex kaog ot dee in,
mai laux lee tix ka daux veng Yie-pu-see tix sawm heue!”
12-13 Viang mawh nan,
cao simiang ah nin:
“Ex ang pon tix kaog ot ka daux veng pa ang pui Yi-seu-liex ot kah;
ex sang hu sivoe,
tag sang laux lee tix ka Ci-pi-yax,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka La-max.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
kix luan khaing Yie-pu-see,
mai ca nied tix hu plak sivoe.
Kix hoek ka Ci-pi-yax haktiex ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
singaix hoik laig daux gawng;
15 Kix laig daux veng,
son sang laux tix dee an tix sawm.
Kix hoek daux veng,
mai ngawm ka daux pak brawk,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa rieb kix paung laux lee tix ka daux nyiex heue.
16 Bawg yam an,
koe pui kuad ju tix kaux pa kaoh khaing daux kaung hu krax an.
Singaix pa hoik luan,
nawh ot ka gawng Yi-fa-liien,
yam in nawh ot ka Ci-pi-yax.
Pui pa ot daux veng Ci-pi-yax hoik mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming.
17 Pui pa kuad ju an yaox pui pa hu krax ot ka pak brawk,
tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Kaoh maix khaing dee mawx?
Sang hu dee mawx lie?”
18 Pui Li-we pawk lo ah nin:
“Yix jiet taug kaoh khaing veng Paw-li-haung meung Yo-tax,
yam in sang ing ka nyiexa,
sang hu ka gawng haktiex Yi-fa-liien pa kied singai,
mai ang koe pui pa rieb yix paung laux lee tix.
19 Yix hoik viex nyhee brex pa tawx la ih,
khao mun mai plai,
priex pa ih to kaux yix kawx hoik koe.
Can ceu pa lo yix khawm uik hoik grum heue.”
20 Pui kuad ju an ah nin:
“Gaux rhawm pex ing nyiex yix!
Aux tag sang kia ngien ka pex;
pex paw lai naox paung ot brawk vang.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh viex kix ing ka nyiex,
mai baox tawx la viex kix sawm.
Nyo khankix khoek caong tix,
mai sawm rheep heue.
22 bYam jiet taug nyawm rhawm kix,
dum mawx dum an koe pui pa cau nyhau ka daux veng khankix,
lai lawm ka nyiex an,
mai gleuh ka siviex ang jud,
ah nin ka pui kuad ju an:
“Viex pui pa ing tau mai maix ka nyiex lih,
yix sang it tau mai nawh!”
23 Pui kuad ju an lih ka plak praix siviex mai ah nin ka kix:
“Paox grawm eue,
ang pon tix yuh nin!
Paw yuh kaing ang mhawm yuh nin.
Pui in mawh nyo si nie aux.
24 Pex jhak,
moeng iak nawh ot dee in,
kawn koe kawn bun aux kawx,
nawh kawn mawh nyawm bun krih,
yam in aux tawx ka pex,
mai pex chix tix klieh ka kix awm pa simie pex tix yuh ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
paw yuh kaing pa ang mhawm ka pui in liak!”
25 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang nghiet.
Pui Li-we tom nyheen moeng iak tix lih plak praix siviex mai tawx ka kix.
Kix rhim tix phit vao tau mai nawh,
yuh krax kaig krax tuah ka nawh leeing sawm,
tom hoek riang praix taug ploi nawh hu heue.
26 Yam hoik jauh praix tix riang,
bun an deeh hoek,
mai goh ka diang siviex nyiex pui kuad ju an;
mex nawh ot ka daux nyiex.
Yam hoik ting riang praix,
nawh kawn it tan.
27 Mex nawh kao khaing it tix plak pon ngawb,
mai paoh siviex,
rien tix sang lih hu krax,
nawh yaox moeng iak tix it ka diang siviex,
taix nawh khawm ra khawn giex ka pang siviex.
28 Nawh ah nin:
“Kaoh,
hu ex heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
ang koe lox pa pawk.
Kheu nan,
nawh tom kliien to kaux yum moeng iak tix ot ka piang blawg la,
mai viex hu plak krax ing ka nyiex.
29 cYam hoek ka nyiex,
nawh tom teei gawn tix mag,
khik to kaux yum moeng iak tix lih koe kao ra khik,
mai guah tix tawx ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu dee an.
30 Pui pa yaox tag ah nin khawm uik:
“Ex ang nyang sax mhong grawng kaing pa yuh nin tix bawg kawx!
Jauh tom lih ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Ai-cix,
kaing pa yuh nin ang sax lih koe tix bawg kawx!
Ex khaw tix chuh kah!
Ex kum kawng tix yaox,
sang yuh ex ka mawx!”
