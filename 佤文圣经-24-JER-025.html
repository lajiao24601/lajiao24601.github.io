Pui koeng pa hoek khaing haktiex plak laong
25
aYam mawh Yaw-ya-cing kawn simex Yaw-si-yax simiang pa giex kaing ka piang meung Yo-tax ka daux pon num,
aux pon gum lox khaing Simiang Siyiex grawng pa nhie nawh tawx ka pui Yo-tax.
(In mawh yam mawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng ka daux tix num.)
2 Aux deeh krai ka pui meung Yo-tax khawm uik,
mai ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
3 “Jauh ka yam mawh Yaw-si-yax kawn simex Ya-meung simiang pa giex kaing ka piang meung Yo-tax ka daux kao loe num,
tom hoek daux in singaix,
khawm uik koe tix nga loe num,
Simiang Siyiex ca si kah ka aux ang jud,
aux kawx ca deeh krai lox nawh ka pex ang jud,
pex hoik ca ang nghiet ka heue.
4 “Simiang Siyiex baox ploi siliex tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix krai ka kix,
kix ca ang cu tix nghiet,
mai ang simi kah.
5 Kix sidiex pex tik ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pex,
mai tieh loh tix khaing daux pa yuh phi map,
son sang deeh pon yaox pex tix ot ka daux haktiex pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pex,
mai ka tax du pex kieh mawh sin ngao cie tix leeing ju.
6 Kix kawx hoik sidiex pex paw vi krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
paw vi krub ka riang rao pa yuh pex,
mai cheeik tix kieh Simiang Siyiex song rhawm,
nawh tom ang lai ciyang ka pex.
7 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai,
pex lao sang nghiet,
mai tieh tix teei riang rao pa yuh tix cheeik tix kieh nawh song rhawm,
tang deeh kieh krax ciyang hoek piang to kaux tix heue.
8 “Mawh kheu ang pex nghiet ka nawh,
kheu nan Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
9 ‘Jhak heue,
aux tag sang khrawm ceu pui pa ot plak laong,
mai siliex aux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng hoek tix puing ka haktiex in,
mai ka can pui pa ot daux haktiex in,
tag sang puing nang ka meung pa ot sivie siveng khawm uik.
Aux tag sang yuh kix laux leeing,
kieh haktiex in mai meung baoh an riem nhik leeing ju,
lih mawh haktiex pa kieh pui yaoh yee ghak baig.
Aux Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
10 bAux tag ang lai baox pon kieh kix nyiah,
mai ang lai koe nang lox gaux rhawm poi ka paox.
Kawk ngu kix ang lai koe rawm bee;
ka piang mhaw kix ang lai koe nghox.
11 cHaktiex in tag sang riem sang nhik,
mai lih mawh dee lhat graung pui.
Meung in kix tag sang ot greem simiang Pa-pi-loeng atigliah num.
12 “ ‘Khaix hoik atigliah num,
aux tag sang ciyang kix kheu phi map Pa-pi-loeng mai phi map pa yuh simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka meung haktiex in,
mai kieh meung in riem nhik leeing ju.
13 Aux tag sang teei krax laux leeing pa krai pao aux sang yuh ka meung in kix,
hoek tix ciyang ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Krax laux leeing in kix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux phuk lai in,
aux hoik tawx lox nhie Yie-li-mi krai pao heue.
14 Aux tag sang hawt tix pawk bix ka pui Pa-pi-loeng awm kaing pa yuh kix;
pui tag sang tud rhim ka kix,
mai simiang kix tag sang mawh siliex cawng heue.’ ”
Siyiex prawng ca ka meung piang haktiex
15 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex krai ah nin ka aux:
“Maix teei kawk plai pa hoik prauk naog ka krax song rhawm pa koe ka daux tawm taix aux,
hu tawx ka meung pa ploi aux maix hu ka ku meung,
kieh kix nyaux.
16 Khaix hoik nyaux kix kah,
tag sang vawk jee vawk blaong,
kleeing tix awm pui yhot yuh nan,
mawh kheu aux tag sang teei krax laux leeing naing yuh kix ka heue.”
17 Mawh kheu nan,
aux rieb tix teei kawk plai in khaing daux tawm taix Simiang Siyiex,
mai tawx ka pui pa hoik ploi Simiang Siyiex hu ka meung ku meung,
tag sang tawx kix nyaux.
18 Aux tawx ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka veng pa koe daux meung Yo-tax khawm uik,
pie khawm simiang kix kawx,
mai cao kaing khankix hoik nyaux ka plai in khawm uik,
kieh kix lih mawh pa salao pui,
pa ghak baig pui,
mai pa jhax lawg pui.
(Awm been in singaix kawx hoik yuh nan heue.)
19-26 Pa tiiem ot plak greem in kix kawx pa hoik nyaux ka plai in mawh:
Simiang Ai-cix,
simiang pa ot greem nawh mai simiang khankix.
Can pui Ai-cix mai pui tang ceu pa ot ka daux Ai-cix khankix;
can simiang Wu-see khawm uik.
Veng Fe-li-see ku veng pa mawh:
veng Ya-seu-ci-loeng,
veng Cia-sa,
can simiang veng Yi-keu-loeng khawm uik mai pui pa ot daux veng Ya-seu-thu pa kawn deuk khankix.
Pui Yi-tong,
pui Maw-ya,
mai pui Ya-meung.
Thai-eux mai can simiang pa ot daux Si-toeng khawm uik;
simiang pa ot hawt Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong khankix;
Ti-tang,
Thi-max,
mai pui veng Pu-see ku veng.
Can ceu pui pa ghrip hauk kaing tix ngaing khawm uik;
can simiang A-la-pawx khawm uik;
can cao kaing ceu pui pa ot hawt haktiex pak mhaik ku cub;
Sing-lix,
Yi-lang,
mai can simiang Mi-ti-yax khawm uik;
simiang pa ot haktiex plak laong dee singai dee dex khawm uik.
Meung pa koe ka piang haktiex ku meung hoik nyaux ka plai in khawm uik.
Dee nghoik,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng kawx tag sang nyaux ka heue.
27 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex tawx lox nhie pex nyaux ka plai pa koe daux kawk in,
kieh kix nyaux yoek,
hao,
goh,
mai ang lai pon yaog tix kaoh cung,
mawh kheu hoik kieh aux kix si pup krax laux leeing ka naing.
28 Phan kix ang rhawm cu tix rieb kawk plai an khaing daux tawm taix maix,
maix krai ka kix ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
phan kix ang nyaux ka kawk in ang chix.
29 Aux tag sang ran yuh ka veng cie to kaux tix laux leeing sivoe,
kix kawn sang pon luat luan khaing krax ciyang lie?
Kix ang pon tix luat luan,
tag sang kham krax ciyang,
mawh kheu sang teei aux naing yuh pui pa ot ka piang haktiex laux leeing khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
30-31 “Yie-li-mi eue,
maix tag caw tix krai gum lox pa hoik krai aux ka maix khawm uik,
mai deeh krai ka kix ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ploi krax pa song rhawm tix lih khaing rao ma,
ploi tuh sah lih khaing nyiex dee pa singax singeh nawh.
Nawh tag sang graox krax pa song rhawm ka pui cie tix,
riag yo awm ka pui pa dig ka plix siiet.
Lox nawh hoek tom dee die caong ma;
pui ka piang haktiex tag sang mhong ka khawm uik.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang deuh phi map ka meung in kix.
Nawh tag sang prawng ca ka kawn pui piang haktiex,
pauh pui pa yuh phi map yum.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
32 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai ah nin:
krax laux krax leeing tag sang lih hoek,
jauh ka meung in hoek ka meung an,
jauh ka dee die haktiex tag sang viex bhaung ting hoek.
33 Ka daux singaix an,
jauh ka daog haktiex plak in tom hoek ka daog haktiex plak an,
hoik mawh to kaux yum pui pa pauh Simiang Siyiex kah,
pa naog ka ku dauh ku dee.
Ang koe pui pa yiiem son kix,
mai ang koe pui pa khrawm tix si paung ka kix.
Kix tag sang awm phon ka piang tiex yuh nan heue.
34 Simiang kawn pui khankix eue,
cao liang pui cie aux khankix eue,
pex tag sang yiiem grai,
mai lu lie tix ka daux phawng tiex,
laux rhawm rhi.
Bawg yam pa pauh ka pex dex sang hoek heue;
pex tag sang awm kawn yung mhaingd pa pauh pui ka yuh nan,
35 pie tix mu kawx ang yaox tix pon luat luan.
36-37 Pex tag sang yiiem grai,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh meung haktiex pex laux leeing ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
kieh meung haktiex pa nyawm ot pex daux lih tix mawh dee riem.
38 Simiang Siyiex hoik tik vud ka pui cie tixe,
awm hoik lih hu sivai sang si khaing daux dawh rang ot tix yuh nan.
Krax lhat krax graung naing mai krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh meung haktiex in tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag daux tu heue.
