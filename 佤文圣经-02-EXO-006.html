6
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix tag sang yaox pa yuh aux ka simiang Ai-cix.
Aux sang khin nawh ploi pui cie aux hu.
Aux sang khin nawh,
ang grheu kix lih hu khaing meung tix ang chix heue.”
Siyiex kok ka Maw-si
2 aSiyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
3 Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm.
Aux hoik lih jhi tix ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux.
Viang mawh nan,
aux ang sax tawx ceu kaux pa singax singeh tix,
pa mawh Yie-heu-hua jhi ka kix.
4 Aux hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
mai hoik ap tix tawx haktiex Cia-nang pa hoik ot kix sivoe ka kix.
5 Yam in aux hoik mhong ka lox ngia pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
kheu pa kieh pui Ai-cix kix yuh kaing pa kin,
mai aux deeh doh keud ka gum lox sijax mukheu tix heue.
6 “Mawh kheu nan,
krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
ah nin ka kix:
‘Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux sang taoh ka pex,
mai ploi pex lih khaing kaing pa kin pa yuh pui Ai-cix.
Aux tag nyeu plawng taix pa ting siawp sibrawm tix ciyang ka pui Ai-cix;
aux sang taoh ka pex.
7 Aux tag teei pex mawh pui cie tix,
mai aux tag mawh Siyiex pex.
Yam pon pex tix bheeik luan khaing kaing pa khin pui Ai-cix,
mai pex tag tawng aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
8 Aux hoik ap tix tawx ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux,
kheu nan aux tag viex pex hoek ka haktiex an,
mai kieh haktiex an mawh sin ngao pex.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.’ ”
9 Maw-si krai grawng in ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang nghiet ka lox nawh,
mawh kheu hoik kied taug mao rhawm rhi kix ka yaox tix krax kha rhawm siliex cawng heue.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin ka Maw-si:
11 “Maix hu krai ka Vawng,
nawh tag ploi pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih hu khaing haktiex Ai-cix heue.”
12 Maw-si pawk lox ah nin:
“Pie pui Yi-seu-liex ang nghiet ka lox aux,
Vawng chix nghiet ka lox aux yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux mawh pui pa ang chix krai lox heue.”
13 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng:
“Hu krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai simiang Ai-cix,
krai tawm aux lox nhie aux pax viex pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing Ai-cix heue.”
Pui nyiex ma Maw-si mai Ya-loeng
14 In plak greem mawh pa tiiem grawng cao kaing daux nyiex ma pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kawn ai Ya-keux Lui-piien koe kawn simex pon kaux:
mawh Ha-nawx,
Fa-lux,
Si-see-loeng mai Cia-mi;
kix lih mawh ceu pui Lui-piien.
15 Si-miien koe kawn simex liah kaux:
mawh Yie-mu-li,
Ya-ming,
A-sia,
Ya-cing,
Saw-sia mai Sao-law,
Sao-law mawh kawn simex pa pon khaing bun Cia-nang;
kix lih tix mawh ceu pui Si-miien.
16 bLi-we koe kawn simex loe kaux:
mawh Keu-soeng,
Keu-siax mai Mi-la-lix;
kix lih tix mawh pui nyiex ma Li-we.
Li-we im ju koe tix yieh tix ngoe aliah num.
17 Keu-soeng koe kawn simex ra kaux:
mawh Li-ni mai See-me;
kiex tang koe kawn praix tom nie heue.
18 Keu-siax koe kawn simex pon kaux:
mawh Ang-lang,
Yi-see-ha,
Si-paw-loeng mai Wu-shiie.
Keu-siax im ju koe tix yieh tix ngoe loe num.
19 Mi-la-lix koe kawn simex ra kaux:
mawh Maw-lix mai Mu-seex.
In mawh pui daux nyiex ma Li-we,
mai doeh ceu cad ceu pui kix heue.
20 Ang-lang teei tix moeng nan keeing tix Yaw-ci-piie;
nawh keh Ya-loeng mai Maw-si.
Ang-lang im ju tix yieh tix ngoe aliah num.
21 Yi-see-ha koe kawn simex loe kaux:
mawh Kheu-la,
Ni-fe,
mai Si-ci-lix.
22 Wu-shiie koe kawn simex loe kaux:
mawh Mi-sa-lix,
Yi-li-sha-fang mai Si-thi-lix heue.
23 Ya-loeng teei tix moeng Yi-li-sa-pa;
nawh mawh kawn bun Ya-mi-na-ta,
nan Na-suing.
Yi-li-sa-pa keh Ya-loeng cie kawn simex pon kaux:
mawh Na-ta,
Ya-pi-hux,
Yi-li-ya-sa,
mai Yi-tha-ma.
24 Kheu-la keh kawn simex loe kaux:
mawh Ya-shi,
Yi-li-cia-na,
mai Ya-pi-ya-sha;
kix mawh ceu pui nyiex ma Kheu-la,
mai tax du cub nyiex mawx cub nyiex an.
25 Kawn simex Ya-loeng pa kaux Yi-li-ya-sa teei tix moeng kawn bun Phu-thiiex;
nawh keh Fe-ni-ha.
In kix mawh ceu pui pa kaoh khaing cub nyiex ma Li-we,
cao kaing cub nyiex mawx cub nyiex an heue.
26 Ya-loeng mai Maw-si mawh pui pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex lox ka ah nin:
“Hu teei ceu pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
hawt paox tix lih tix cub hoik tix cub” an kiex.
27 Kiex kawx hoik khiox khaw ka simiang Ai-cix,
ploi ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih hu heue.
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Ya-loeng mai Maw-si
28 Singaix yam si kah Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si daux haktiex Ai-cix sivoe an,
29 Nawh ah nin:
“Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
can lox pa krai aux ka maix khawm uik,
maix krai ka simiang Ai-cix heue.”
30 Viang mawh nan,
Maw-si pawk lox:
“Maix hoik tawng aux mawh pui pa ang chix krai lox,
Vawng sang chix nghiet ka lox aux yuh ka mawx lie?”
