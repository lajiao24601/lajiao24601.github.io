Pa nghoik tix prawng ca ka pui Yi-seu-liex
13
Sivoe an,
yam krai ceu pui Yi-fa-liien ka lox,
ceu pui tang ceu lhat kah,
mai kix yung yi ka ceu pui Yi-fa-liien.
Viang mawh nan,
ceu pui Yi-fa-liien krub ka Pa-lix yuh phi map,
tag sang laux leeing.
2 Kix ca nied tix yuh ka phi map,
mai keud tix yuh awm pa chix yuh tix,
kix teei mau yuh riang kah,
khaix an ah nin:
“Yuh krax pa tan lu ka kix heue!”
Kix ngheet ka jheeh riang kawn moi an heue!
3 Mawh kheu nan,
pui in kix tag sang awm taox pai awm yam pon ngawb,
yam lih singaix tag sang grai hu.
Kix tag sang awm nyhee glauh ka daux glauh pauh nghox,
awm taox ngu pa chu tix lih khaing mawk ngu,
yam hoek bhaung tix phlu tag sang grai hu heue.
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh Siyiex pa viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix.
Lheu khaing aux pex ang koe Siyiex;
mawh ngie aux pa mawh cao taoh pex tix kaux khaing.
5 aKa daux tiag daux tu pa kroh pa kram,
aux hoik hoek tix kia ngien ka pex;
6 viang mawh nan yam laig pex ka daux haktiex pa mhawm in,
pex tag sang ting rhawm lhaong rhi kheu hoik kub ih kub nyaux tix.
Yuh ka mawx pex lai piawm ka aux.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang lauh pex awm sivai sang si,
jawm pex ka praok krax awm sivai kaoh ku.
8 Aux tag sang hoek tix lauh pex awm miex krih pa grai kawn yuh nan,
mai kiied ka pex.
Aux tag sang awm sivai sang si bleet pex ka dee ot pex,
mai awm to tiag pa kiied pex phawng vid heue.
9 “Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux tag sang yuh pex laux leeing heue!
Pui mawx sang pon taoh pex lie?b
10 cPex hoik sax nhie aux tawx tix koe simiang mai simiang,
viang mawh nan hoik taoh kix ka pex lie?
11 dAux hoik tawx simiang ka pex ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
tag sang deeh tik pex ka daux krax pa kied song rhawm tix heue.
12 “Phi map krax pa lud loe pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tiiem ot daux lai khawm uik,
khaix an teei priex tix deuh.
13 Pui Yi-seu-liex kawn koe bawg yam im ju,
viang mawh nan kix daox aw,
ang tawng tix giex ka bawg yam.
Kix awm kawn ngie pa hoik hoek bawg yam pa sang keh,
ca simie tix ot ka daux vaig miex tix,
mai ang cu tix lih.
14 fAux ang rhawm taoh pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing daux meung yum;
aux ang rhawm pluat ploi kix lih khaing siawp sibrawm meung yum.
Krax yum eue,
rhuat van ka krax pa laux leeing maix heue!
Krax yum eue,
tawxe krax pa laux leeing maix heue!
Aux kawx ang lai baox koe krax pa caox rhawm ka kawn pui in kix.
15 Viang pui Yi-seu-liex hoik haok huan awm rib riem daux tiag yuh nan,g
aux tag sang kieh bhaung si aoh ka plak lih singaix khaing daux tiag daux tu hoek tix phlu,
mai phlu ka dee keet rawm mai ka rawm dung kroh;
aux tag sang teei ka sin ngao kix hu khawm uik.
16 Pui Sa-ma-li-yax tag sang kham krax ciyang kheu pa tieh vheen ka aux.
Pui pa ot dee an tag sang yum laux leeing kheu naing;
kawn ngie tag sang si blat ka piang tiex;
bun nghe kawx tag sang pih vaig heue.”
