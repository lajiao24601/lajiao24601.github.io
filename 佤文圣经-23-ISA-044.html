Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex pa koe tix kaux khaing
44
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Nghiet heue,
siliex aux Yi-seu-liex;
nghiet heue,
pui cie aux,
kawn praix Ya-keux.
2 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex cao pa phan pa yuh maix,
jauh tom keh maix,
aux hoik teem coi ka maix.
Paw vi lhat,
maix mawh siliex aux;
maix mawh pui muh pa hoik laug aux heue.
 
3 Aux tag sang tawx lhiex ka haktiex pa kroh,
tag sang kieh rawm keet pa si ngaong buing tix lih khaing pak mhaik.
Aux tag sang tawx riang rao ka kawn simex kawn bun maix,
tag sang tawx buan son ka kawn praix maix.
4 Kix tag sang kuah haok huan awm rib riem ka praok rawm,
ting lhaong awm khaox si glai pa koe ka praok klawng yuh nan heue.
 
5 Pui ku kaux tag sang ah nin:
aux mawh cie Simiang Siyiex;
kix tag sang laig mawh ka daux cub pui cie Yi-seu-liex.
Pui ku kaux tag sang tiiem ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex ot ka plawng taix tix;
kix tag sang laig mawh pui cie Ya-keux heue.
 
6 aSimiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang Yi-seu-liex,
simiang kru naing,
pa mawh cao taoh ah nin:
Aux mawh pa koe yam jauh,
aux mawh pa koe yam si dauh;
lheu khaing aux ang lai koe Siyiex tang kaux.
7 Pui mawx pon tiag tix tau mai aux,
kieh nawh teei sakse lih heue!
Pui mawx pon yuh ka kaing pa yuh aux lie?
Pui mawx pon dha tix krai lih ka kaing pa sang hoek lie?
Kieh nawh krai pao lih ka kaing pa sang hoek heue!
8 Pui cie aux eue,
paw vi lhat si yaung heue!
Ang aux hoik dawg dha tix krai ka kaing pa koe tom dix dix lie?
Pex hoik mawh pui pa kham sakse aux.
Lheu khaing aux,
kawn koe Siyiex tang kaux?
Kawn koe siyiex tang kaux pa ting riang pa ang aux bax yaox ka lie?
Riang rao pa giex pa going
9 Pui pa yuh riang krub hoik mawh pui pa ang koe ceu koe cak khawm uik;
kix hoik een riang rao pa koe ngawh koe jum ang koe ngawh jum pie ceeix kawx.
Pui pa krub ka siyiex an kix hoik mawh pa daox aw ang tawng tid tix;
kix tag sang pon krax kaig tua.
10 Pa raug yuh ka riang rao khri hoek tix krub mawh pa ang koe ceu koe cak heue!
11 Pui pa krub ka riang rao an tag sang pon krax kaig krax tua tix ngaix sax.
Pa yuh riang an kawx hoik mawh kawn pui,
ang tie lheu lhak lhiao khaing pui tang kaux.
Kieh kix hoek khawm uik,
krai lox ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
kix tag sang yaoh yee mai kaig tua heue.
12 Cang lhek teei gawng khri tix mu sau greeing ka daux ngu,
khaix an siao plawng taix pa cong tix tawk ka daix,
mai raug khri an lih tix mawh pak peung.
Cang lhek hoik cai rhawm ang koe riang rao,
ang yaox tix nyaux rawm hoik taug mao mai iak riang iak rao heue.
13 Cang mai khaox tiag tix yaox ka khaox,
mai teei rawm meug si vied keei,
teei kraung siao tix ku ceu saik riang,
kieh lih mawh pak peung pui,
kied mhawm grang,
mai tawx ot ka daux nyiex vad.
14 Nawh kawx tee rien tix rid ka khaox si oe bhawng,
phan ang mawh nan teei ka khaox yang yit,
phan ang mawh nan ka khaox tiah ka daux nong.
Nawh kawx tee hoik sum ka khaox gix,
mai krox jum kieh rawm lhiex jaux haok huan.
15 Pui chix tix kieh khaox tix blah mawh khix taung,
tix blah yuh riang rao kah.
Nawh teei tix phlu ngu ka tix blah son sang co kah,
mai son sang tong khao mun kah,
an tix blah yuh riang siyiex kah,
son sang kieh pui krub.
16 Nawh chix tix kieh khaox khankix mawh khix taung,
mai tong nex ka piang,
mai ih tom sak tom naog.
Nawh kawx chix tix gleeing khaox son sang co kah,
mai ah nin:
“Ngu in kied mhawm,
kied si u heue!”
17 Nawh teei pa deuk tix blah yuh riang rao kah,
mai krub ka khaox in rawg buan ah nin:
“Maix mawh siyiex aux,
taoh aux liak heue!”
18 Kix yuh kaing pa yuh nin kied mawh pa daox tete!
Kix yiep ngai tix ang jhak,
ang siao lo ka dieb dong tix,
mai ang tawng sidawng.
19 Ca mawh pui pa yuh riang rao an siao dieb dong tix,
keud ka tix vud,
tom tag ah nin:
“Aux teei khaox tix gawng khaing,
tix blah kieh mawh khix taung,
mai tong khao mun mai nex kah;
ang tix blah teei tix yuh riang rao kah,
mai yuh ka mawx aux lai krub ka sivoe ngai khaox yuh nin heue!”
20 In ang mawh pa hoik teei tix ih ka nyaox nyia lie?
Krax pa daox aw nawh hoik kieh nawh pheem,
mai ang lai koe sidah pa chix pai moi;
nawh ang doh yi riang rao an mawh Siyiex heue.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao phan cao yuh
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Ya-keux eue,
tag caw priex tix tawng kaing in;
Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix hoik mawh siliex aux.
Aux hoik phan yuh ka maix,
mai sang kieh maix mawh siliex tix;
aux ang piawm ka maix leeing ju.
22 Aux pluat ploi ka phi map maix,
mai kieh nawh awm yam grai pai awm;
veeh tix deeh heue,
aux hoik taoh ka maix heue.
 
23 Rao ma eue,
gaux rhawm heue!
Haktiex dee raux eue,
rhax lox pa ting si num heue!
Nong khaox gawng lua eue,
hoek tix gaux rhawm heue!
Khaox pa koe ka daux nong eue,
hoek tix gaux rhawm rhax lai heue!
Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh ka Ya-keux;
nawh hoik jhi siawp sibrawm pa ting tix khaing ka daux Yi-seu-liex heue.
 
24 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao taoh maix ah nin;
Siyiex pa phan pa yuh maix hoik krai pao yuh nin:
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
cao pa phan pa yuh ku ceu khawm uik.
Aux hoik nih ka rao ma nong nong;
aux hoik tawm ka haktiex nong nong.
25 bAux kieh pui pa si pawk lih tix mawh pui daox,
kieh lox dha tix krai cao chix si meed lih tix mawh pa giex pa going.
Aux pon tieb tie ka lox krai pui pa lhak lhiao,
krai “ping nyia cuyi” pa koe kix hoik mawh pa daox aw khawm uik.
26 Viang mawh nan,
yam dha siliex aux tix krai lox,
yam ploi aux kawn cau tix hu krai ka krax keud krax sawn pa sang yuh tix,
aux kieh krax keud krax sawn an kix mai lox dha tix krai lih tix mawh khawm uik.
Aux krai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kawn pui tag sang baox paung ot dee an;
aux krai ka pui Yo-tax,
aux tag sang deeh cox sum ka veng Yo-tax,
deeh kieh veng haok huan ka dee riem.
27 Aux tawx lox kieh rawm sigrit sigra kroh;
aux tawx lox kieh rawm klawng lih tix mawh haktiex pa kroh.
28 cAux ah nin ka Sai-lu-seex:
maix tag caw tix mawh cao pa liang son aux,
tag caw tix yuh hawt ka alo aux.
Maix caw tix ah nin ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
aux tag sang cox sum ka nawh,
ah nin ka nyiex buan,
aux tag sang kieh num jaong nawh kaux cong heue.
