47
Khaix an,
Yaw-seux viex pux aik tix phuan kaux hu yaox ka simiang.
Nawh ah nin ka simiang:
“Keeing aux mai pux ke aux viex khawm yung,
moi,
mai khawng brie kraung khrai kix khawm uik kaoh khaing meung Cia-nang hoek ka dee in;
yam in kix ot ka daux haktiex Keu-sang heue.”
2 Khaix an,
nawh viex pux aik tix khankix hu yaox ka simiang.
3 Simiang chawk ka kix:
“Yuh pex kaing ceu pa tix?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Vawng,
yix hoik awm tax du tix,
mawh cao liang yung.
4 Mawh kheu kin sibhawm praix ka daux meung Cia-nang,
ang lai koe dee liang ka phung yung,
yix tom hoek dee in jao.
Khiox khaw Vawng kieh yix ot ka Keu-sang tin liak.”
5 Simiang ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Yam in keeing maix mai pux ke maix khankix hoik hoek.
6 Haktiex Ai-cix koe sivoe kix,
kieh kix tang sawg tix ot dee mhawm;
kix chix tix ot ka dee mhawm meung haktiex Keu-sang.
Phan koe pui pa kied chix ka daux kix tix kaux kaux,
tom yaog nawh mawh cao giex kaing liang khawng brie aux heue.”
7 Yaw-seux viex keeing tix Ya-keux hoek sivoe simiang ka daux nyiex haw simiang Ai-cix.
Ya-keux nawb buan ka piang simiang.
8 Simiang chawk nawh:
“Hoik koe ju maix miex ding?”
9 Ya-keux pawk lox:
“Aux ot ka piang haktiex hoik koe tix yieh tix ngoe num.
Khix num aux in kied ngaing kied kha tix,
ang lang awm ju tax aux.”
10 Sivoe guah paox tix,
Ya-keux baox nawb buan ka simiang,
khaix an deeh lih hu khaing.
11 Yaw-seux nghiet ka lox simiang,
tawx keeing mai pux aik nawh ot ka Ai-cix;
nawh tawx ka pa dex mai veng Lang-sai haktiex pa mhawm ka kix kieh mawh haktiex kix,
12 mai baox tawx nghox ka keeing tix mai pux aik tix khankix,
khawm daux nyiex ma keeing tix,
tawx khawm pui pa iak num heue.
Sibhawm praix
13 Sibhawm praix kied kin,
ang lai koe nghox tang dauh;
pui Ai-cix mai pui Cia-nang cai rhawm khawm yuh tix kaing.
14 Yam hoek kix tix liag nghox,
Yaw-seux tom teei ka mau pa tawx kix,
mai tawx ka simiang.
15 Yam hoik uik cai pui Ai-cix mai pui Cia-nang mau can pa koe tix khawm uik,
pui Ai-cix hoek ka Yaw-seux mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Mau yix hoik uik,
tawx nghox ka yix liak,
yuh ka mawx yix lai sang yum ka plak sivoe ngai maix?
Mawh kheu hoik uik cai yix mau tix heue.”
16 Yaw-seux pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan mau pex hoik uik,
chix tix viex khawng brie pex mai loh ka nghox.”
17 Mawh kheu nan,
kix viex khawng brie tix hoek ka Yaw-seux dee an,
mai Yaw-seux teei nghox deeh loh ka brung,
moi,
mai pex,
mai la.
Daux num an,
nawh loh kix ih priex preem ka khawng brie cie kix heue.
18 Daux ra num,
kix baox hoek tix khiox khaw:
“Simiang aux eue,
yix ang doh pie ka maix,
mau yix hoik uik,
khawng brie kawx hoik tawx ka maix.
Yam in,
lheu khaing to kaux yix mai haktiex yix,
ang lai koe tid tix.
19 Khiox khaw maix teem yix keud,
son ang yix sang yum.
Kaung yix hoik riem;
khiox khaw maix teei nghox loh ka yix mai haktiex yix.
Simiang chix tix pon ka haktiex yix;
yix cu tix yuh siliex cawng simiang,
yuh kaing son nawh.
Tawx nghox ka yix liak,
son sang im ju yix;
tawx simie nghox ka yix,
kieh yix sum nghox yuh kaung heue.”
20 Yaw-seux liag haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik son simiang.
Yam an sibhawm praix nied tix kin;
pui hoik coh kaung tix;
haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik hoik mawh cie simiang.
21 Phan mawh pui,
Yaw-seux kieh pui pa ot daux Ai-cix khawm uik mawh siliex yuh kaing;
22 mawh ngie haktiex cao giex kaing li khankix pa ang nawh lai liag,
mawh kheu simiang hoik tawx ngawh riang ka cao giex kaing li khankix,
mai kix ang coh ka haktiex tix.
23 Yaw-seux ah nin ka pui khankix:
“In singaix aux hoik liag pex mai haktiex pex son simiang,
aux tawx simie son pex mai rhuat ka haktiex.
24 Bawg yam vawg pex nghox sax,
pex tag tawx ka simiang phuan son tix son,
pa lheu an mawh cie pex mai son daux nyiex ma pex,
pex kawx chix tix yuh simie kah.”
25 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
maix hoik taoh ka ju yix,
kieh yix mawh pa pon buan son,
yix kawx cu tix mawh siliex cawng simiang.”
26 Yaw-seux yuh lox tat jhawm prawng son Ai-cix:
can to ma pa pon,
phuan son tix son tawx ka simiang.
Lox tat jhawm prawng in tom in singaix kawx hawt kah,
mawh ngie haktiex cie cao giex kaing li nong nong pa ang mawh cie simiang heue.
Lox khiox khaw Ya-keux bawg nghoik
27 Pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka daux haktiex Ai-cix baoh Keu-sang,
ka dee an kix nie baox koe kraung khrai,
mai nied tix baox haoh hun si pie.
28 Ya-keux ot ka daux haktiex Ai-cix kao aliah num,
yam koe ju tix yieh tipon aliah num.
29 bYam hoek bawg yam dex sang yum nawh,
mai kieh pui kok ka Yaw-seux,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Deuh taix maix greem si viang ba auxa,
mai tiex tu paw lai si paung aux daux Ai-cix liak.
30 Khaix yum aux,
maix viex aux lih khaing Ai-cix,
si paung aux ka dee si paung tix tax du heue.”
Yaw-seux ah nin:
“Aux tag yuh awm lox pa krai maix heue.”
31 Ya-keux ah nin:
“Maix tiex tu ka aux!”
Yaw-seux tom tiex tu ka nawh;
khaix an,
Ya-keux tom yaog yaw Siyiex ka piang ku it tix heue.
