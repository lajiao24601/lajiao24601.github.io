Lox rawg buan krax teem krax coi
(Lai Yaog Yaw 40.13–17)
70
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux liak!
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nied tix hoek teem coi ka aux liak!
2 Kieh pui pa sang pauh ka aux laux leeing som nai;
kieh pui pa gaux rhawm ka yaox aux krax pa laux leeing kaig tua.
3 Kieh pui pa nyiah ka aux lhat graung mai dak tix deeh heue.
 
4 Kieh can pui hoek ka plak sivoe ngai aux gaux rhawm gaux rhi khawm uik;
kieh can pui pa muh ka krax pa taoh maix krai ah nin ang jud ang jai:
Siyiex kied ting kied lhaong heue!
5 Siyiex eue,
aux iak riang iak rao mai ang koe cao teem cao coi;
khiox khaw maix nied tix hoek ka aux dee in.
Maix mawh krax teem krax coi ka aux,
mawh cao pa taoh ka aux;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nied tix hoek teem coi ka aux heue!
