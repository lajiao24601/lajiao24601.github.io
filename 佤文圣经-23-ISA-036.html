Pui Ya-sux ghlat ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 18.13–27; Lai Been Phuk Ra 32.1–19)
36
Yam kawn giex Si-si-cia simiang ka daux kao pon num,
Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Si-na-ci-lix puing ka veng pa kaux cong Yo-tax,
mai pon teei kah.
2 Mawh kheu nan nawh tawx lox ka simiang naing pa kaux La-ciex hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
dia tix ot piang dung ka praok rawm rawng,
ka piang krax ting pa hu pui ka plak dee si daux ka man,
mai nhie Si-si-cia peung rhawm tix heue.
3 Pui pa tang son Yo-tax loe kaux hu tix kum kawng;
kix mawh kawn simex Si-leu-cia cao pa kuang ka nyiex haw simiang,
pa kaux Yi-li-ya-cing,
cao tiiem lai ka daux nyiex simiang pa kaux Seu-paw-nax,
cao pa si kaw ka been pa mawh kawn simex Ya-sa pa kaux Yaw-yax.
4 Simiang naing Ya-sux chawk ah nin:
“Vawng Ya-sux son sang pon tix tawng ka Si-si-cia,
nawh hoik koe siawp sibrawm ceu pa tix yuh nin lie?
5 Maix ce lox pa krai tix giex going sang pon mawh tang kru naing mai riang rao lie?
Maix een rhawm tix ka pui mawx son sang keud tix tieh vheen ka aux lie?
6 aMaix ce pui Ai-cix sang hoek teem tix,
mai an hoik mawh pa teei ka gawng priem pa pot,
kieh mawh khaox gix pa gix hu krax;
khaox gix an tag sang klauh ka tawm taix maix.
Simiang Ai-cix tag sang yuh nan ka pui pa hoik deuh rhawm tix ka nawh heue.”
7 Simiang naing Ya-sux ca nied tix ah nin:
“Pex tee rien tix ah nin,
pa een pex rhawm tix ka mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
viang mawh nan,
ang Si-si-cia hoik yuh laux leeing ka nyiex vad nawh mai ka khu dee tan nawh lie?
Ang nawh hoik tawx lox ka pui Yo-tax mai ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai nhie kix krub ngie Simiang Siyiex ka piang khu dee tan tix mu khaing lie?
8 Yam in aux sang tiex tu mai pex ka daux ceu kaux Vawng:
phan maix pon yaox ka pui naing brug brung ra rheng kaux,
aux tag sang tawx brung naing ka pex ra rheng mu.
9 Tete te lai,
pie simiang naing Ya-sux pa blawn iak khaing paox tix,
maix ang pon pex,
maix ce pui Ai-cix sang tawx ling law naing mai pui naing brug brung sang hoek teem coi tix.
10 Maix ce aux ang koe krax teem Simiang Siyiex tom sang hoek puing ka maix,
sang yuh laux leeing ka meung cie maix lie?
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux hoek puing ka maix,
mai yuh laux leeing ka meung cie maix in heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-ya-cing,
Seu-paw-nax,
mai Yaw-yax ah nin ka simiang naing pa ting “Sara,
maix si kah ka lox Ya-lang tau mai yix,
yix hoik chix nghiet kah.
Paw si kah ka lox Si-paw-lai,
mawh kheu hoik mhong kawn pui pa ot dee lhaong daux veng ka heue.”
12 Simiang naing ah nin:
“Pex ce Vawng nhie aux krai lox ka pex mai ka simiang meung haktiex khaing lie?
Ang doh mawh nan,
aux kawx hoik krai ka pui pa ngawm ka piang nyiex lhaong daux veng khankix;
kix hoik awm pex yuh nan,
tag sang ih ka aing to kaux tix,
nyaux ka rawm neem to kaux tix heue.”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh yaog tix kaoh cung,
mai kok yo ka lox Si-paw-lai tom ting ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox lai tawx Vawng Ya-sux.
14 Nawh ne raw ka kix ah nin:
paw vi glup ka lox pa pie lhien Si-si-cia;
Si-si-cia ang pon taoh ka pex heue.
15 Paw nghiet ka lox krai nawh,
son sang kieh pex hu een rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex.
Paw ce Simiang Siyiex sang chix taoh ka pex;
paw ce pui naing Ya-sux ang pon yuh laux ka veng pex.
16 Paw nghiet ka lox pa krai Si-si-cia!
Vawng Ya-sux tawx lox nhie pex lih krai lox mhawm.
Yuh nin,
pex chix tix kawn tang yaox tix ih ka plix siiet mai plix ruh cie tix,
tang nyaux ka rawm dung cie tix.
17 Krox jun tom hoik gah Vawng pex hu ka haktiex tang dauh,
awm haktiex pex yuh nan,
pa hoik koe nghox koi,
plix siiet,
khao mun mai plai.
18 “Paw vi kieh Si-si-cia pie lhien ka pex,
mai ce Simiang Siyiex sang chix hoek taoh pex.
Koe Siyiex meung haktiex mawx pa hoik yaox pex pon taoh ka meung haktiex tix luat luan khaing tawm taix Vawng Ya-sux lie?
19 Siyiex Ha-ma,
siyiex Ya-eu-pa,
siyiex Si-fa-wa-ying hoik hu dee mawx khawm uik lie?
Koe pui mawx pa hoik taoh ka Sa-ma-li-yax lie?
20 Koe siyiex mawx pa hoik sax taoh ka meung tix khaing daux tawm taix simiang yix lie?
Pex hoik yum rhawm ka sang chix taoh Simiang Siyiex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung grawng ceu pa tix lie?”
21 Kawn pui ang nye ang nyux;
kix priex tix hawt ka lox tawm simiang Si-si-cia,
ang nye pie tix gum,
mai ang pawk lox pa krai nawh.
22 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing piang daux nyiex haw Yi-li-ya-cing,
cao tiiem lai ka daux nyiex simiang pa kaux Seu-paw-nax,
mai simiang cao pa si kaw been meung pa mawh Yaw-yax,
laux rhawm riah kraung ceep tix griah,
mai deeh ing ka veng krai lox simiang naing Ya-sux ka simiang Si-si-cia heue.
