Yo-tax mai Tha-ma
38
Yam an,
Yo-tax lih hu khaing pux aik tix khankix,
hu ot mai ceu pui Ya-tu-lang tix kaux pa kaux Si-la.
2 Yo-tax yaox kawn bun ceu pui Cia-nang pa kaux Su-yax dee an,
mai teei tix moeng nawh.
3 Nawh koe kaux,
keh kawn simex tix kaux;
Yo-tax sum kaux nawh mawh Eux.
4 Nawh baox koe kaux,
mai keh kawn simex daux ra kaux,
sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Eu-nang.
5 Nawh baox keh kawn simex daux loe kaux,
sum ceu kaux nawh mawh See-la;
yam keh See-la,
Yo-tax ot ka Ci-si heue.
6 Yo-tax teei kawn ai tix Eux koe moeng,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Tha-ma.
7 Kaing yuh Eux mawh pa caox,
lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex tom teei ju nawh.
8 Khaix an,
Yo-tax ah nin ka Eu-nang pux Eux:
“Hu ot tau mai o maix,
yuh pa khaw maix keeing iak tix yuh,
son sang kieh maix plud doeh pux ke maix.”
9 Viang mawh nan,
Eu-nang tawng ka kawn an ang lai mawh cie tix,
kheu nan,
yam ot tau mai o tix ku bawg,
tom kieh rawm ngang tix lih piang tiex,
ang kieh tix koe kawn son pux ke tix.
10 Grawng pa yuh nawh in lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex baox teei ka ju nawh.
11 Khaix an Yo-tax ah nin ka mun tix Tha-ma:
“Ing ot awm miex mai ka nyiex miex keeing maix,
krox kawn simex aux See-la ting.”
Nawh ah lox in,
mawh kheu lhat nawh See-la baox yum awm pux ke nawh ra kaux.
Kheu nan,
Tha-ma tom ing ka nyiex miex keeing tix heue.
12 Ang koe miex glang tix,
moeng Yo-tax yum.
Krox hoik luan bawg yam pa laux rhawm nawh,
nawh hu tau mai paox grawm tix Si-la pui Ya-tu-lang ka haktiex Thing-na,
hoek ka dee ot pui pa teem nawh khrip hauk yung.
13 Koe pui krai Tha-ma mhong,
paox nawh sang hu ka Thing-na dee khrip hauk yung dee an,
14 nawh tom peeik kraung ceep miex mai yam laux rhawm,
kheem pak ngai tix ka man tawb,
ngawm plak krax laig ka haktiex Thing-na;
veng Yi-na-ying;
mawh kheu tawng nawh kawn simex pa iak Yo-tax pa kaux See-la hoik ting,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax hoek teei tix moeng nawh heue.
15 Yam yaox Yo-tax ka nawh,
keud nawh mawh bun coh kaux,
kheu kheem nawh pak ngai tix.
16 Yo-tax ang tawng nawh mawh mun tix,
mai hu sawg nawh praok krax,
chawk nawh:
“Sang ih maix mau miex ding?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Maix it mai aux sang tawx maix ceu pa tix ka aux lie?”
17 Nawh pawk lox:
“Aux tag tawx kawn pex ka maix tix mu khaing phung pex aux heue.”
Nawh ah nin:
“Chix tix,
sivoe sang tawx maix kawn pex hoek,
maix deuh kraung tix ceu kieh mawh pa tang sakse heue.”
18 Yo-tax chawk:
“Sang kieh maix aux tawx kraung sakse ceu pa tix lie?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Tang cum maix,
maox cum maix,
mai khaox gix maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yo-tax tawx kraung in kix ka Tha-ma.
Kiex it tau;
Tha-ma tom koe kaux.
19 Tha-ma ing ka nyiex miex keeing tix,
peeik man tawb pak ngai tix mai deeh ceep ka kraung ceep miex mai heue.
20 Yo-tax kieh paox grawm tix Si-la hu tawx kawn pex tix mu ka bun an,
son sang deeh teei ka kraung pa hoik tang tix,
viang mawh nan,
Si-la ang yaox ka bun an.
21 Si-la chawk ka pui Yi-na-ying pa ot ka haktiex an:
“Bun pa coh kaux an,
pa yie ot ka nyiex vad praok krax tin,
ot dee mawx?”
Kix pawk lox:
“Baoh in ang sax koe bun pa coh kaux daux nyiex vad.”
22 Si-la deeh ing ka Yo-tax dee an,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux ang yaox bun an.
Pui haktiex an ah nin,
haktiex in ang sax koe bun pa coh kaux daux nyiex vad.”
23 Yo-tax ah nin:
“Kraung an kix kieh nawh ih khaing,
son ang pui sang nyiah ka ex.
Aux koe rhawm tix sang tawx kawn pex ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
maix ang yaox ka nawh heue.”
24 Khaix hoik luan loe khix,
koe pui krai Yo-tax mhong:
“Mun maix Tha-ma yuh sibun coh kaux;
mai hoik koe kaux heue.”
Yo-tax meet rhawm ah nin:
“Teei nawh lih,
gleeing nawh yum heue!”
25 Yam viex kix Tha-ma lih plak praix,
kieh pui krai ka paox nawh:
“Aux hoik koe kaux khaing cao kraung in kix;
maix jhak cum in mai maox cum in,
khawm khaox gix in;
in kix mawh cie mawx lie?”
26 Yo-tax tawng ka kraung in kix,
tom ah nin:
“Nawh koe krax.
Aux ang baox yuh kaing pa khaw aux tix yuh ka nawh;
aux caw tix sang teei nawh mawh moeng kawn simex tix See-la.”
Jauh yam in,
Yo-tax ang lai it tau mai nawh.
27 Hoek bawg yam sang keh Tha-ma,
nawh tag tawng pa koe kaux tix mawh kawn brawm.
28 Yam saux keh nawh,
an tix kaux plih taix tix lih ka praix;
bun pa khre keh teei keei raoh tix nyaing mai phrawk ka taix nawh,
ah nin:
“In mawh pa sang keh sivoe heue.”
29 Viang mawh nan,
kawn an deeh leed taix tix laig,
pux aik nawh deeh lih sivoe.
Bun pa khre keh an ah nin:
“Maix hoik ghrang tix lih yuh nin!”
Tom sum ceu kaux kawn in mawh Fa-leu-seea.
30 Khaix an,
pux aik nawh pa phrawk keei raoh ka taix lih keh,
kheu nan tom sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Shie-lab heue.
