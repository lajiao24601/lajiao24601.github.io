Krax pa teem coi kawn Khrit
8
aPux aik pux o khankix eue,
yix simie pex tawng hoik kia ngien Siyiex ka ceu cao Ma-chi-toeng ku dauh yuh ka mawx.
2 Kix hoik cim ka krax pa cheuk cheu pa ting khaing daux krax pa laux leeing;
viang mawh nan kix hoik naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
viang hoik ot ka daux krax pa hot sibhawm kawx mai ca kawn si pieh rhawm tix teem coi.
3 Aux pon krai ka sidawng,
krax pa teem coi kix hoik mawh pa sau kix riang rao tix yuh,
viang mawh nan kawn lheu khaing pa koe riang rao kix.
Mawh krax pa gaux rhawm,
4 mai kix khiox khaw ka yix,
kieh kix koe nyum ka daux kaing mhawm in pa teem ka kawn Khrit pui Yo-thaix.
5 In kawn lheu khaing ceu pa simie yix tete.
Kix ran tan to kaux tix ka Simiang Ye-sux,
khaix an,
baox hawt tix teem coi yix ka daux alo Siyiex.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
yix joh jhan Thi-to ca nied tix teem coi ka pex,
yuh kaing pa mhawm in pa jauh nawh tix yuh hoik.
7 Mawh krax yum rhawm,
pa chix krai lox,
ngai lig ngai lai,
rien rhawm tix teem coi ka pui tang kaux,
mai krax muh ka ex,b
pex hoik koe ka tom nie,
aux kawx simie pex si pieh rhawm ka piang kaing ceu pa mhawm in kix heue.
8 Ang mawh aux pa tat tix deuh been ceu pa tid tix,
mai hoik mawh ngie son sang kieh pex tawng grawng pa rien rhawm tix teem coi ka pui yuh ka mawx,
kieh in hoek tix cheuk cheu tix yaox ka krax muh krax rhawm pex.
9 Pex hoik tawng ka buan son Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit yuh nin:
nawh hoik dawg mawh pui pa mag mi sivoe an,
viang mawh nan mawh kheu jao pex,
nawh kieh to kaux tix lih mawh pui hot sibhawm,
kaing ting hoik mawh son sang kieh pex lih tix mag mi kheu hoik hot sibhawm nawh heue.
10 Phan mawh son kaing in,
aux een mawh nin:
pex caw tix yuh hoik ka krax teem pa jauh pex tix yuh daux num pa luan ka daux bawg yam in.
Ja lao pex ang ran tix yuh kaing in sivoe,
mai caw tix gaux rhawm tix ran yuh sivoe mawh nin.
11 Phan mawh nan,
saux riang tix yuh hu,
son sang hoik yuh ka kaing in heue!
Pex khaw tix yuh ka daux krax pa gaux rhawm pa siao pex yam jauh yuh nan,
mai yuh kaing in hoik,
khaix an tag caw tix hawt tix yuh awm pa koe pex yam in.
12 Phan pex rug sang teem coi kix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
Siyiex tag sang rieb kah;
nawh tag sang kieh pex tan lu ka can pa koe tix khawm uik,
mai ang mawh ceu pa ang pex koe heue.
13-14 Ang mawh aux pa keud tix sang thiem thie ka beeh glawm pex,
son sang tieh ka beeh grawm pui tang kaux.
Kheu hoik mag mi pex yam in,
mai taug caw tix teem coi ka pui pa hot sibhawm khankix.
Hoek yam mag mi kix,
phan pex koe ceu pa sang lo,
kix kawx tag sang chix teem coi ka pex.
Yuh nin pex khawm ra plak tag sang koe bawg yam tix ra lai teem coi ka paox tix.
15 cAwm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
pa nie teei,
ang haoh;
pa yawm teei,
ang cud.
Thi-to mai paox pa yuh kaing nawh
16 Yaog yaw Siyiex,
nawh hoik kieh Thi-to rien rhawm tix teem coi pex awm yix yuh nan.
17 Nawh ang joeng mawh ngie kheu krax pa joh jhan yix,
mai hoik mawh pa tang kaoh khaing krax gaux rhawm nawh,
nawh nie blawn simie tix hoek ka pex dee an.
18 Yix ploi pux aik tang kaux tix kaux hu tau mai nawh;
pux aik ex in hoik mawh pui pa nab yam yung yi ku haktiex ceu cao,
ka daux kaing jhaw pa sidiex ka gum lox buan.
19 Ja lao ang mawh nan,
pie ka daux kaing mhawm in pa yuh son min plak ngai Simiang Ye-sux,
nawh kawx hoik pon krax pa laug ceu cao hu,
mawh paox pa hu yix tau,
in kawx hoik jhi grawng pa gaux rhawm pui hun tix teem coi heue.
20 Yix hoik kuang ka mau pa si pieh rhawm pui tix tan,
mai hoik chix rien rhawm tix tom si dauh,
son ang sang kieh pui hun haoh lox;
21 dMawh kheu hoik tat yix tix yuh ka kaing mhawm,
ang joeng yuh ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Ye-sux,
ka plak sivoe ngai kawn pui kawx yuh ka heue.
22 Yix baox ploi pux aik tang kaux tix kaux hu tau mai kix.
Pux aik ex in hoik kham tix yaox krax pa cheuk cheu yix miex bawg mawx,
yix hoik tawng grawng pa koe krax gaux rhawm nawh tix teem coi pui tang kaux ka daux kaing tom nie ceu.
Yam in,
nawh hoik kied yum rhawm ka pex tete,
nawh tom nie simie tix hu teem coi ka pex.
23 Phan mawh son Thi-to,
nawh hoik mawh paox pa yuh kaing tau mai aux,
paox pa yuh kaing aux pa teem coi yuh kaing jhaw pex.
Pux aik an kiex ra kaux pa hu tau mai nawh,
mawh cao pa krai lox tang ceu cao,
mawh min plak ngai Khrit.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix jhi grawng krax muh nawh ka kix,
ka plak sivoe ngai ku haktiex ceu cao,
khaix an kieh ceu cao ku dauh tawng,
krax pa yaw aux pex ang lud heue.
