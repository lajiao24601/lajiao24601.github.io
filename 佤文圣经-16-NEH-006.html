Tieb tie ka rhawm keud Ni-si-mi
6
Chang-pa-la,
To-pi-ya,
Ci-sang,
mai pui koeng tang kaux mhong hoik cox ex ka guh veng,
pie dee cud cha tix dauh kawx ang lai koe (tete te lai ex ang bax sau siviex veng koe ka dee laig pui).
2 Chang-pa-la mai Ci-sang tawx lox lai ka aux tix pheen,
tawx rhawm keud tix ka aux si pup kix ka daux yaong pa ot dee pak A-naw tix dauh.
In hoik mawh rhawm pa keud kix,
mai keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing.
3 Aux ploi pui hu krai ka kix ah nin:
“Aux kawn yuh kaing pa kied ting ceu tix ceu,
ang pon tix hu dee an.
Aux ang sang kieh kaing naox tix kheu sang hu kia tix pex heue.”
4 Kix tawx lox lai pa awm paox ka aux pon bawg,
mai aux deeh pawk lox pa awm paox ka kix ku bawg heue.
5 Khaix an,
Chang-pa-la ploi siliex tix hoek tawx lox lai ka aux daux phuan pheen;
in mawh lox lai ang tawp.a
6 Lox lai an ah nin:
“Ci-sang krai ka aux,
pui haktiex pa ot dex baoh an krai lox pie pa yuh nin,
krai sang tieh vheen maix mai pui Yo-thaix,
mawh kheu nan,
pex tom deeh cox sum ka guh veng.
Nawh kawn baox ah nin,
maix hoik keud tix sang mawh simiang,
7 mai hoik tawx lox ka cao jhai tix blah,
kieh kix krai pao hoik mawh maix simiang Yo-tax ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Vawng rien tix mhong grawng kaing in.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux kieh maix hoek ka aux,
kieh ex khawm uik kum kawng heue.”
8 Aux pawk lox nawh ah nin:
“Pa krai maix ang mawh tete te lai,
hoik mawh lox pa tang keud maix tix ah lih heue.”
9 Kix keud tix sang ghlat ka ex,
son sang kieh kaing yuh ex naox tix.
Aux rawg buan ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux kaux cong liak!”
10 Bawg yam in,
aux hu ka See-ma-ya.
Nawh mawh kawn saux Mi-si-ta-piiex,
kawn simex Ti-lai-ya;
kheu koe kaing nawh ang pon yaox tix lih hu khaing nyiex ot tix.
Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Maix mai aux tag caw tix hu mox tix ot ka dee nyiex buan pa kied singax singeh,
sau jhong siviex,
mawh kheu kix sang hoek tix pauh maix,
kix sang hoek tix pauh maix yam pon sawm heue.”
11 Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh pui pa sawg tix mox tix yuh nan.
Maix khex ce aux sang mox tix ot daux nyiex buan mai yaox tix klawd ju lie?
Aux ang hu!”
12 Aux hoik keud tix yaox ka kaing in tix bawg,
mai hoik tawng Siyiex ang bax si kah tau mai See-ma-ya,
mawh To-pi-ya mai Chang-pa-la pa liag nawh hoek ne raw ka aux.
13 Kix kieh nawh hoek ghlat ka aux,
kieh aux yuh phi map;
yuh nin,
kix tom tag pon yaox tix yuh ka aux laux,
map nai aux heue.
14 Aux rawg buan ah nin:
“Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka kaing pa hoik yuh To-pi-ya mai Chang-pa-la,
mai ciyang ka kiex.
Khiox khaw maix doh keud nang ka bun cao jhai Naw-ya-ti mai krax pa ghlat nawh tang ceu ka piang cao jhai aux heue.”
Grawng pa hoik si dauh kaing
15 Khaix yuh kaing ka daux tix phuan ra singaix,
guh veng khawm uik hoik hoik ka daux khix Yi-luxb tix nga phuan ngaix.
16 Pui koeng pa ot meung sivie siveng ex khankix hoik mhong kah,
kied koe krax kaig krax tua;
mawh kheu pui ku kaux hoik tawng,
grawng pa pon hoik si dauh kiang in,
hoik mawh krax pa teem coi Siyiex heue.
17 Ka daux singaix bawg yam an,
simiang pui Yo-thaix khankix rien tawx lai ka To-pi-ya jheeng jheeng.
18 Pui pa ot ka daux haktiex Yo-tax tom nie kawx hoik cung plak nawh,
mawh kheu hoik mawh keeing moeng nawh kawn simex Ya-la pa kaux See-cia-ni,
kawn simex nawh Yaw-ha-nang teei tix moeng kawn bun Mi-su-lang pa mawh kawn simex Pi-li-cia.
19 Kawn pui rien krai kaing mhawm pa yuh To-pi-ya ku ceu ka plak sivoe ngaix aux,
mai lox pa krai aux kawx kix hoik krai ka nawh.
Khawm yuh nan kawx,
To-pi-ya kawn ca tiiem lai ghlat ka aux jheeng jheeng heue.
