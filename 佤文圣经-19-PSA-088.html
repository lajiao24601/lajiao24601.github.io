Lox pa kok yo krax teem coi
88
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa taoh aux eue,
aux khiox khaw krax teem krax coi maix leeing lang singaix;
aux khiox khaw kok yo ka plak sivoe ngai maix leeing lang sawm.
2 Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg aux;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux krax teem krax coi heue.
 
3 Krax laux krax leeing hoek ka piang aux tom nie,
kieh aux dex hoek krax yum.
4 Aux hoik awm can pui pa dex sang yum yuh nan;
can riang rao aux khawm uik hoik uika.
5 Hoik tik aux ot ka daux pui yum,
awm pui pa pauh pui yum pa noi tix it ka daux si meeig;
aux lih mawh pui pa hoik piawm maix khawm uik,
ang lai baox pon krax pa teem coi maix.
6 Maix hoik tik aux ka daux dee raux,
baox ot ka dee kied raux dee kied faik.
7 Krax pa song rhawm maix hoik gut ka aux tom ciien;
rawm pa puk pak maix hoik chun ka aux heue.
 
8 Maix hoik kieh paox grawm aux tik vud ka aux;
maix hoik kieh kix seeing brai ka aux.
Aux ot ka daux pa giex gang,
mai to kaux aux ang pon tix luat luan;
9 ngai aux hoik faik kheu krax pa laux rhawm tix.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux hoik khiox khaw kok yo ka maix ku singaix;
aux hoik yaog taix tix rawg buan ka maix heue.
 
10 Maix hoik yuh mhai pa daox pa deng ka pui yum lie?
Khuan pui yum chix kaoh tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix lie?
11 Pui mawx chix krai lao krax muh maix ka daux si meeig lie?
Pui mawx chix krai pao krax pa kaux sijax maix ka daux meung yum lie?
12 Pui mawx pon yaox ka kaing pa daox pa deng maix ka daux haktiex pa faik lie?
Pui mawx pon yaox ka pa mhawm pa mhiem maix ka daux pa deek rhawm deek rhi lie?
 
13 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux hoik khiox khaw maix teem coi tix;
ku singaix pon ngawb cao,
aux hoik rawg buan ka maix.
14 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tik vud ka aux?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai mox tix ang simi ka aux?
15 Jauh tom yam mawh aux kawn nyawm,
aux hoik kham krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
mai hoik dex sang yum miex bawg mawx;
krax ciyang pa thiem thie maix piang aux hoik kieh aux iak riang taug mao.
16 Krax pa song rhawm maix hoik gut aux bhliet;
krax pa lauh maix hoik kieh aux laux leeing.
17 Krax pa kha rhawm hoik awm rawm ting pa sivie ka aux leeing lang singaix;
krax kha rhawm kha rhi hoik sivie ka aux khawm pon daog.
18 Maix kawx hoik kieh paox grawm ting aux singai hu khaing aux;
pa faik lih tix mawh ngie paox pie aux khaing heue.
