Lai Yie-li-mi
1
Phuk lai in mawh pa tiiem Yie-li-mi.
Si-leu-cia keeing Yie-li-mi mawh cao giex kaing li tix kaux ka daux veng Ya-na-thu haktiex Piien-ya-ming.
2 aKawn simex Ya-meung simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-si-yax giex kaing ka meung Yo-tax kao loe mun,
Simiang Siyiex si kah tau mai Yie-li-mi;
3 bka daux bawg yam pa giex kawn simex Yaw-si-yax pa kaux Yaw-ya-cing kaing ka meung Yo-tax,
Simiang Siyiex baox si kah tau mai Yie-li-mi.
Khaix yam an Simiang Siyiex si kah tau mai nawh jheeng jheeng,
tom yam hoek giex kawn simex Yaw-si-yax tang kaux pa kaux Si-ti-cia kaing ka piang meung Yo-tax kao tix num mai phuan khix,
yam an pui pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik giex laung kix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng heue.
Grawng pa kok ka Yie-li-mi
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
5 “Sivoe sang tawx aux ju ka maix,
mai aux hoik laug ka maix;
sivoe sang keh maix,
aux hoik ciak maix mawh cao jhai ka piang meung khawm uik heue.”
6 Aux pawk ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
aux kawn kied nhum ju;
aux ang hiex chix krai lox heue.”
7 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Maix paw vi lai een tix mawh pui pa nhum ju,
mai nied hu ka pui pa ploi aux maix hu ka daux khaing,
krai pao ka lox pa tawx aux maix krai khawm uik.
8 Maix paw vi lhat ka kix;
kheu aux ot tau mai maix,
mai sang kia ngien ka maix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue!”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex nyeu taix tix pu pi ka siyieb jheeh aux,
mai ah nin ka aux:
“Maix jhak,
aux hoik sau lox pa sang khaw maix tix krai ka daux jheeh maix heue.
10 Aux tawx akhuang ka maix in singaix;
maix tag caw tix mawh cao pa yuh kaing pa reeig tix tik,
pa yieh laux,
pa yuh laux,
pa klup tix tik,
pa deeh cox sum,
mai kaing ceu pa ciak pa sum ka kawn pui piang haktiex heue.”
Riang su sai ra mu
11 Simiang Siyiex chawk aux ah nin:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
yaox maix ceu pa tix lie?”
Aux pawk ah nin:
“Aux yaox kak khaox Batan tix gawng heue.”
12 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Khaw,
aux hoik pau tix jhak jheeng jheeng,c
son sang kieh gum lox pa krai tix lih mawh heue.”
13 Simiang Siyiex baox chawk aux ah nin:
“Yaox maix ceu pa tix lie?”
Aux pawk ah nin:
“Aux yaox aw pa lok tix mu ka haktiex plak laong,
mai veeh tix hieh ka plak in heue.”
14 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Tag sang koe krax laux leeing pa lih hoek khaing haktiex plak laong,
mai klup ka piang pui pa ot haktiex in khawm uik.
15 Kheu aux tag sang khrawm ka can meung pa koe ka haktiex plak laong khawm uik;
simiang kix tag sang ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hawt siviex veng khankix,
khawm daux haktiex Yo-tax ku veng tag sang ap tix giex kaux ka kaing pa giex tix heue.
16 Aux tag sang ciyang ka phi map pa yuh pui cie aux khawm uik;
kix hoik tik vud ka aux,
mai tan lu ka siyiex tang kaux,
mai krub ka riang rao pa yuh pui ka taix tix.
17 Yie-li-mi eue,
maix tag caw tix rien khre tix,
mai krai lox pa tawx aux maix krai ka kix khawm uik.
Maix paw vi lhat ka kix;
phan ang mawh nan,
aux tag sang kieh maix nie iak rhawm si kua ka plak sivoe ngai kix.
18-19 Yie-li-mi eue,
maix nghiet heue!
Ka daux meung haktiex Yo-tax khawm uik,
pa mawh simiang,
cao giex kaing,
cao giex kaing li,
mai pui hun khankix tag sang simah ka maix khawm uik.
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix in aux tag sang tawx riang ka maix,
son sang tieb tie ka kix;
maix tag sang kaux cong awm tab,
awm rong rhiem,
awm guh tawng yuh nan.
Kix ang pon pex ka maix;
kheu ot aux tau mai maix,
tag sang taoh ka maix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
