Lai Li-we
Been Krax-tan Pa-gleeing
1
Simiang Siyiex kok Maw-si ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
tawx lox ka nawh,
2 nhie nawh tawx lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ka Yi-seu-liex.
Ka daux pex phan koe pui sang tan Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Simiang Siyiex,
chix tix tan moi ka daux phung moi,
phan ang mawh nan,
yung ka daux phung yung.
3 Phan nawh yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka moi,
kieh nawh teei moi mhaing pa loig,
mai viex hu ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
mai Simiang Siyiex tom tag rieb ka heue.
4 Nawh tag deuh taix tix ka piang kaing Krax-tan Khawng-brie an;
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing an tag mawh pa pon rieb tix teei,
son sang pluat ploi ka phi map nawh.
5 Nawh tag pauh moi mhaing an sivoe Simiang Siyiex;
doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li tag tan ka nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie an,
khaix an teei nham in mai phruah hawt vung viang piang khu dee tan.
6 Khaix an,
nawh tag tawh hak Krax-tan Khawng-brie an,
mai sit nex lih khik mawx khik an;
7 cao giex kaing li khankix tag nawm khix ka piang khu dee tan an mai sau ngu kah,
8 mai teei khik nex,
khawm kaing khawm bee,
mai deuh ka piang khu dee tan glah ngu.
9 Nawh tag si ghrah kraung vaig mai caong plak khaix Krax-tan Khawng-brie an ka rawm;
cao giex kaing li tag sau khawng brie an khawm uik ka piang khu dee tan.
Taox ngu pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Ngu in mawh pa kied muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb heue.
10 Phan koe pui sang yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka yung mai pex,
nawh tag sawg pex mhaing pa loig.
11 Nawh tag ot ka plak laong khu dee tan,
mai pauh pex mhaing an ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex;
doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng pa mawh cao giex kaing li tag phruah nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie hawt vung viang piang khu dee tan khawm uik.
12 Khaix hoik sit nawh nex lih khik mawx khik an,
cao giex kaing li tag teei Krax-tan Khawng-brie an khawm uik,
khawm kaing khawm bee mai deuh ka piang khu dee tan,
gleeing ka ngu.
13 Nawh tag si ghrah kraung vaig mai caong plak khaix Krax-tan Khawng-brie an ka rawm;
cao giex kaing li tag viex Krax-tan Khawng-brie an khawm uik,
hoek gleeing ka piang khu dee tan.
Taox pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Ngu mawh pa kied muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb heue.
14 Phan koe pui sang yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka sim son Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag sawg ka keed tiag,
phan ang mawh nan si kaux krie.
15 Cao giex kaing li tag viex sim an hoek ka khu dee tan,
mai siyied ka kaing dut,
teei kaing deuh ka piang khu pa gleeing an,
mai kieh nham nawh jauh hit ka praok khu dee tan an.
16 Nawh tag pih ble sim an khawm can pa seeing pa ot ka daux khawm uik,
tik ka praok khu dee tan plak lih singaix dee tik nyaox.
17 Nawh tag giex preeik sim an khawm ra plak,
mai ciah sim an,
viang mawh nan,
mai ang ciah ka preeik,
khaix an teei tix gleeing khawm uik ka piang khu dee tan.
Taox pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Ngu in mawh pa kied muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb heue.
