3
aSimiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing pawk ah nin:
“Aux tag sang ploi kawn cau tix hu rien khre krax son tix.
Khaix an cao simiang pa sawg pex tag sang lih hoek ka nyiex buan dum mawx dum an;
pex simie tix yaox ka kawn cau tag sang hoek krai pao ka sijax mukheu aux heue.”
2 bViang mawh nan,
singaix hoek nawh,
pui mawx sang pon kham lie?
Yam lih hoek nawh,
pui mawx sang pon cung cong lie?
Mawh kheu hoik awm nawh si piak khoek pa laoh koe riang yuh nan,
awm ngu pa ceed pui ka khri yuh nan.
3 Nawh tag sang hoek tix prawng ca,
awm pui pa ceed ka mau yuh nan.
Nawh tag sang si ghrah ka cao giex kaing li khankix singax singeh,
awm ceed pui yuh kaing khri ceed ka khri yuh nan,
son sang kieh cao giex kaing li khankix tawng ka khawng tan pa khaw tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex yuh nan.
4 Yuh nin,
Simiang Siyiex tom taug rieb ka khawng tan pa tan pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
awn singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan heue.
5 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Aux tag sang lih jhi tix ka daux pex,
mai tawx krax prawng ca.
Aux tag sang mawh cao pa kham mhai sakse deuh phi map ka pui pa yuh si meed yuh sat,
krax pa phit vao,
pa kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh,
pa kut ka mau riang pui,
pa kheem ka miex mai kawn doe,
mai pa map nai ka nyo si nie khankix.
Aux tag sang deuh phi map ka can pui pa ang lhat graung yung yi ka aux khawm uik heue.”
Ceu pa tan lu kao son tix son
6 “Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux ang chix tieh loh tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
pex pa mawh doe ceu cad Ya-keux in kix kawn ang nyang baox lud krax khawm uik.
7 Pex hoik awm tax du tix yuh nan,
lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux ang jud,
mai ang bax priex tix hawt.
Pex tag sang caw tix veeh tix hoek ka aux,
aux tom taug veeh tix ka pex.
Viang mawh nan,
pex chawk ah nin:
‘Yix khaw tix yuh ka mawx tom taug sawn tix veeh tix hoek ka maix lie?’
8 Aux chawk pex ah nin:
kawn pui chix tix pie lhien ka Siyiex lie?
Ang doh chix heue!
Viang mawh nan,
pex hoik pie lhien ka aux.
Pex chawk ah nin:
‘Yix pie lhien maix yuh ka mawx lie?’
Aux krai ah nin,
hoik mawh ka daux pa khaw pex tix tan lu kao son tix son,
mai ka daux krax tan krax lu pex hoik pie lhien ka aux.
9 Krax jhax lawg tag sang lih hoek piang pex pui khawm uik,
mawh kheu meung piang haktiex hoik pie lhien ka aux khawm uik.
10 c“Pex tag caw tix viex kao son tix son hoek tawx ka daux nyiex buan,
son sang kieh daux nyiex buan koe ka priex preem tom nie.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh kru niang ka piang khawm uik;
cheu tix yaox ka aux,
pex tom taug yaox grawng pa paoh aux ka daux doh rao ma,
mai klup tix tawx buan son ceu pa mhawm piang pex tom nie.
11 Aux ang kieh viag vua yuh laux ka to ma to koi pex,
khaox plix siiet pex tag sang plix plix tom nie.
12 Yam an,
pui meung ku meung tag sang krai grawng pa mhawm ot mhawm koe pex,
mawh kheu haktiex pex tag sang mawh dee nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.”
Siyiex cu tix tawx krax pa caox rhawm
13 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex hoik salao ka aux;
viang mawh nan,
pex chawk ah nin:
‘Salao yix maix yuh ka mawx lie?’
14 Pex ah nin:
‘Krub ka Siyiex ang koe dee mhawm.
Priex tix hawt ka lox tawm jhawm krai nawh,
phan ang mawh nan jhi grawng pa klih ex rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
15 Phan ex jhak tix yaox,
pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi pon krax pa gaux rhawm,
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig pon krax pa haok huan,
kix pa nyaw glaw ka Siyiex lai pon tix luat luan khaing krax ciyang heue.’ ”
16 Yam an,
pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex ra lai krai lao ka lox;
Simiang Siyiex bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox kix.
Ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
koe phuk lai tix phuk pa tiiem ka ceu kaux pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex.
17 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux.
Ka daux singaix pa yuh aux,
kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux.
Aux tag sang caox rhawm ka kix,
awm caox rhawm keeing ka kawn tix pa yuh son nawh.
18 Yam an,
pui cie aux tag sang baox deeh yaox ka grawng pa si pup pui rauh pui see mai pui caox pui caig ang awm paox yuh ka mawx,
pui pa krub ka aux mai pui pa ang krub ka aux tang siyox yuh ka mawx heue.”
