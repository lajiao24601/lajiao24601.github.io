Rhax lai yaog yaw Yie-lu-sa-leung
87
Simiang Siyiex sum veng cie tix ot ka piang gawng pa singax singeh;
2 nawh muh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kawn lheu khaing veng Yi-seu-liex pa koe ku dauh.
3 Veng Siyiex eue,
maix nghiet,
hoik mhong grawng kaing pa riang min plak ngai maix ah nin:
 
4 Ka daux meung khankix pa nghiet ka aux,
khawm Ai-cix,
mai Pa-pi-loeng;
kawn koe Fe-li-see,
Thai-eux,
mai Ku-seu,
aux sang kieh kix mawh pui pa koe ca rang ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
 
5 Phan mawh Shi-ang,
mai meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik hoik mawh pui pa koe ca rang ka haktiex in;
cao pa ting siawp sibrawm tag sang kieh nawh haok huan.
6 Simiang Siyiex tag sang rien khre ka lai ca rang kawn pui,
kieh kix mawh cie pui Yie-lu-sa-leung.
7 Kix tag sang graoh mai rhax lai ah nin:
num dee keet krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe hoik koe ka Shi-ang heue.
