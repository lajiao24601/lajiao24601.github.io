Lai Tang-yi-li
Krai dawt
Lai Tang-yi-li mawh yam pa tiiem lih yam ot pui Yo-thaix ka daux krax pa tuk tiak tuk te Vawng meung tang meung ka kix. Cao pa tiiem siao lox lao pui been mai riang su sai tawx lox pa joh jhan ka kawn pui, mai krai ka kix yuh nin: Siyiex tag sang pauh ka cao giex kaing pa tud pa rhim, mai deeh tawx siawp sibrawm ka pui cie tix heue.
Yam ot ka daux krax pa gut kheem, cao tiiem siao lox lai pa tang siyox tiiem lih, mai teei lox tiag mai mhai kah ka kaing ceu pa hoik luan, ka kaing pa koe yam in, mai ka kaing ceu pa tag sang hoek ku ceu khankix heue.
Phuk lai in chix tix guah lih koe ra baux:
1. Mawh grawng lox lao been pa vuk vied vuk viang Tang-yi-li mai paox pui pa hu tau mai nawh; kix pon pex ka pui koeng kheu krax pa yum rhawm tix ka Siyiex mai krax pa cu pa nghiet tix. Lox lai been in kieh simiang Pa-pi-loeng mai simiang Paw-see mawh bawg yam heue.
2. Riang su sai pa yaox Tang-yi-li pa si diiet tix hoek, kieh kaing tang ceu khankix tiag tix yaox, son sang jhi grawng pa iak riang meung Pa-pi-loeng, mai baox grox krai grawng pa laux leeing been li tang pui pa tuk tiak ka kix, grawng pa pon pex pui cie Siyiex heue.
Krai kaing
