Lai Fe-li-pix
1
bAux,
pa mawh Pao-law mai Thi-maw-thaix hoik mawh siliex Ye-sux Khrit.
Yix tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka pui cie Siyiex pa ot ka Fe-li-pix,
pa mawh kawn Khrit pa dex dawt tau mai Ye-sux Khrit,
khawm simiang ceu cao mai pui cao teem coi khankixa heue.
2 Kieh keeing ex Siyiex mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son mai pa nhim ka pex heue!
Pao-law rawg buan ka son pui pa rieb ka lox lai
3 Yam keud aux pex ku bawg,
aux yaog yaw ka Siyiex aux,
4 yam rawg aux buan son pex ku bawg,
mai hoik koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
5 mawh kheu tom yam jauh ka daux singaix an hoek yam in,
ka daux kaing pa sidiex pex gum lox buan,
pex hoik teem coi ka aux jheeng jheeng.
6 Aux hoik yum rhawm grawng Siyiex kaw pa jauh tix yuh kaing pa mhawm in ka daux pex,
hoik chix teem coi ka kaing in,
mai yuh kaing in hoik si dauh ka daux singaix pa lih hoek Ye-sux Khrit.
7 Pex pui hun hoik koe ka daux rhawm aux jheeng jheeng heue!
Aux braong ka pex pui hun hoik mawh pa khaw tete.
Mawh kheu yam in aux ot ka daux thawng ang ah,
phan ang mawh nan yam krai aux lox son gum lox buan singaix pa hoik luan,
ka daux krax pa ang koe pa kieh raw mai yam kham sakse,
pex pui hun hoik yaox tix ot ka sibrawm pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka aux khawm uik.
8 Siyiex hoik tawng grawng pa teei aux pa siao krax muh Ye-sux Khrit,
sidaing braong rhawm ka pex tete,
gum lox in mawh tete heue.
9 Pa rawg aux buan son pex mawh:
krax muh krax rhawm pex hoik haok huan tau mai ngai lai mai krax keud krax sawn pex,
10 mai kieh pex pon laug ka ceu pa blawn mhawm an.
Yuh nin,
yam deeh lih hoek Khrit,
pex tag singax singeh mai ang koe dee duh ka phi map.
11 Krax ot krax koe pex tom taug lih koe plix pa rauh pa see ka daux pa naog ka Ye-sux Khrit,
mai hoek tix yaog yaw min plak ngai Siyiex heue.
Im son Khrit
12 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux tag sang kieh pex tawng,
krax pa si pup aux hoik tieh tix lih mawh pa teem coi gum lox buan haok huan.
13 cDee si dauh,
pui pau ka daux nyiex cao kuang kaing,
mai pui tang kaux pa ot dee in khawm uik,
hoik tawng laung pui aux kheu Khrit.
14 Aux ot daux thawng,
hoik kieh kawn Khrit plak nie blawn koe krax yum rhawm,
blawn koe rhawm kua,
mai ang lhat graung ceeix kawx ka pa sidiex tix ka gum lox Siyiex heue.
15 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui tix blah,
sidiex ka gum lox Khrit mawh ka daux krax pa boh rhawm mai rhawm rhi pa simie tix lah cha ka paox tix,
viang mawh nan,
koe nang pui tix blah hoik mawh pa viex khawm rhawm rhi tix.
16 Pui pa koe khaix hoik yuh nin ka daux pa kaoh khaing krax muh,
mawh kheu kix hoik tawng grawng pa tawx Siyiex beeh glawm pa krai aux lox son gum lox buan.
17 Pui an sivoe,
grawng pa sidiex kix ka Khrit ang khaw,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing rhawm pa vao,
kix keud tix sang jawm yam ot aux ka daux thawng,
mai yuh krax pa nyhap son aux.
18 Viang mawh nan,
ceu in koe dee bleeh ceu pa tix lie?
Ceu pa yuh kix khaw ang khaw,
ca mawh kieh Khrit pon sidiex tix hu,
aux hoik gaux rhawm ka heue.
Aux kawn sang nied tix gaux rhawm;
19 mawh kheu hoik tawng aux ka daux krax pa rawg pex buan,
mai ka daux krax pa teem coi Khuan Ye-sux Khrit,
aux tag sang deeh chix pon ka krax pa bheeik luan.
20 Krax pa simie aux mai krax pa keud pot jun pot aux mawh:
ang mawh koe beeh glawm aux ka daux pa ceuk cha,
ku bawg ku yam mai aux tieh tix,
nie mawh ka daux yam in,
hoik grum naog ka riang rao,
yum im ang ah,
aux hoik teei to kaux tix khawm uik yaog yaw ka min plak ngai Khrit.
21 Phan mawh ka son aux,
pa im aux mawh son Khrit;
phan yum nie koe min plak ngai heue!
22 Viang mawh nan,
phan grawng pa im aux pon yuh tom nie ka kaing ceu pa koe buan son khankix,
aux tag ang tawng ka grawng ceu pa sang laug tix.
23 Aux hoik ot ka si nax ceu pa nu khawm ra ceu.
Aux cu tix lih hu khaing haktiex in,
ot tau mai Khrit,
an tag mawh kaing ceu pa mhawm tete.
24 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu son pex,
aux nie khaw tix im.
25 Grawng in aux hoik yum rhawm ka tete.
Aux hoik tawng grawng sang kawn im tix,
kheu nan mai ca kawn ot tau mai pex pui hun,
teem coi pex haok huan ka daux krax yum rhawm,
mai nie baox koe krax gaux rhawm,
26 kaing ting mawh son sang yam baox yaox aux ka pex,
pex tag sang nie baox kieh aux koe min plak ngai ka daux Ye-sux Khrit heue!
27 Pa blawn ting ceu mawh:
krax ot krax koe pex caw tix kieh khaw mai pa kieh gum lox buan Khrit pui yuh.
Yuh nin,
aux pon ang pon hoek tix jhak ka pex,
aux hoik tawng grawng pa koe pex rhawm rhi tix mu khaing,
kaux cong ang yung yang,
mai ih rhawm rhi khri mau paox tix ka son naing krax yum rhawm gum lox buan.
28 Ka daux kaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
ang chix lhat ka pa ghlat pui pa tieb tie ka pex;
tag caw tix kua rhawm,
teei grawng in kham sakse ka pex,
kix tag sang laux leeing,
mai pex tag sang pon krax taoh,
kheu hoik mawh in kaing pa yuh Siyiex.
29 Pex hoik pon sibrawm pa hoek tix yuh kaing cie Khrit,
ang joeng mawh ngie yum rhawm ka nawh,
tag caw tix kham nang krax kha rhawm son nawh.
30 dYam in,
pex chix tix yuh naing tau mai aux;
naing in pex hoik yaox grawng pa puing aux kah,
pex tag sang chix mhong,
aux ca kawn yuh naing ka daux kaing ceu pa yuh tix yuh nan heue.
