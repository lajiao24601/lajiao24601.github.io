Alo Siyiex, pa lieh lheu kawn pui
20
Yam ploi yix hu ka daux aliah num haok khix phuan daux kao ngaix,
koe kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex tix blah hoek tix ngawm ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai chawk cheng aux ka alo Siyiex.
2 Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
3 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
tag caw tix krai ka kix,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ah nin:
pex hoek khiox khaw tix chawk cheng ka alo aux lie?
Aux tiex tu ka ju tix leeing ju yuh nin:
ang cu pex khiox khaw chawk cheng.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
4 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix hoik rien khre tix sang krai pao ka phi map kix lie?
Krai pao heue!
Nhie kix doh keud ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa hoik yuh tax du kix khankix.
5 aMaix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
yam laug aux ka Yi-seu-liex,
mai hoik sax tiex tu ka kix;
aux hoik jhi kix yaox tix ka haktiex Ai-cix,
mai tie tu ah nin:
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex heue.
6 “Yam an,
aux hoik ap tix sang thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix,
mai viex kix hoek ka haktiex an pa mhawm pa laug aux haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia,
pa mawh haktiex pa blawn mhawm ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
7 Aux tawx lox nhie kix tik ka riang rao krub pui pa hoik muh kix pa yum koeng pui ka khankix,
ang pon tix krub ka siyiex pa pie lhien Ai-cix,
mai kieh seeing brai yuh to kaux tix,
mawh kheu hoik mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix.
8 Viang mawh nan,
kix tieh vheen ka aux,
ang nghiet ka aux.
Kix ang bax tik ka riang rao krub pui pa seeing brai an kix,
mai ang bax ploi ka siyiex pa pie lhien Ai-cix nang.
Yam an,
aux ce tix sang kieh kix cim ka krax pa song rhawm tix ka Ai-cix.
9 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang bax yuh yuh nin.
Aux ang rhawm kieh ceu kaux pa singax tix hoek krax pa laux leeing;
mawh kheu aux hoik sax krai pao Yi-seu-liex ka plak sivoe ngai pui tang ceu,
aux tag sang thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix heue.
10 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix,
mai viex kix hoek ka daux tiag daux tu.
11 bAux tawx gum lox ping nyap tix ka kix,
mai sidiex gau kix ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
kieh pui pa priex tix hawt pon yaox tix im ju ku kaux.
12 cAux kawx tawx lox nhie kix priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah,
mai kieh mawh mhai pa yuh ex sijax mukheu.
Tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex kieh kix lih tix mawh ceu pui pa singax singeh.
13 Viang mawh nan,
kix tieh vheen aux ka daux tiag daux tu.
Kix ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
mai tieb tie gum lox ping nyap aux,
viang hoik kieh pui pa priex tix hawt pon yaox tix im ju.
Kix hoik seeing brai yuh singaix tuah.
Aux keud tix sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix khawm uik ka piang kix daux tiag,
mai yuh kix laux leeing.
14 “Viang mawh nan,
aux ang bax yuh yuh nin.
Aux ang rhawm kieh ceu kaux pa singax tix hoek krax laux leeing;
mawh kheu hoik sax krai pao aux ka kix ka plak sivoe ngai pui tang ceu khankix,
aux tag sang thung kix lih khaing Ai-cix.
15 dMawh kheu nan,
aux tiex tu ka daux tiag daux tu,
aux ang thung kix hoek ka haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia pa tawx aux ka kix an,
in hoik mawh haktiex pa blawn mhawm ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
16 Aux tiex tu yuh nin;
mawh kheu hoik tieb tie kix ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
mai hoik seeing brai yuh singaix tuah.
Kix ca kawn krub ka riang rao krub kix heue.
17 “Viang mawh nan kawx,
aux ca kawn caox rhawm ka kix,
mai but rhawm tix ang yuh kix laux leeing ka daux tiag daux tu.
18 Aux ne raw ka pui nhum ju kix ah nin:
ang pon tix hawt been pa hoik yuh tax du kix;
ang pon tix hawt been krax ot krax koe kix;
ang pon tix krub riang rao krub pui kieh to kaux tix seeing brai.
19 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap aux,
20 mai priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah kieh mawh singaix pa singax singeh,
kieh mawh mhai pa yuh ex sijax mukheu,
tag kieh pex rien rhawm rhi tix,
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex heue.
21 “Viang mawh nan,
pui baux ju an hoik tieh vheen ka aux.
Kix ang priex hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux mai ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
viang hoik kieh pui pa priex tix hawt khankix yaox tix im ju.
Kix hoik seeing brai yuh singaix tuah.
Aux ce tix sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix khawm uik ka piang kix ka daux tiag daux tu,
mai pauh kix yum khawm uik,
22 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang bax yuh yuh nin.
Aux ang rhawm kieh ceu kaux pa singax tix hoek krax laux leeing,
mawh kheu meung ka piang haktiex hoik yaox grawng thung aux pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Ai-cix.
23 eMawh kheu nan,
aux baox tiex tu ka daux tiag daux tu tix bawg,
tag sang kieh kix prung prang ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
24 Aux yuh nin mawh kheu ang kix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
mai gum lox ping nyap aux,
hoik seeing brai yuh singaix tuah,
mai krub ka riang rao pa hoik krub tax du kix heue.
25 “Khaix an,
aux tom tawx been pa ang mhawm,
mai gum lox ping nyap pa ang pon kieh pui im ju ka kix,
26 kieh kix teei krax tan lu cie to kaux tix deeh seeing brai yuh tix,
kieh kix tan lu nang kawn ai tix mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie.
In mawh pa sang ciyang ka kix,
mai kieh kix tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
27 “Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
tag caw tix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
tax du pex ang cu nghiet ka aux,
mai hoik baox map nai ka aux tix bawg.
28 Aux thung kix hoek ka haktiex pa hoik ap aux tix tawx ka kix.
Yam yaox kix ka gawng ting mai ka khaox klai,
mai kix tom yuh krax tan lu ka dee an.
Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa taox bhawng pa tan lu kix mai plai pa jauh kix hoik cheeik aux song rhawm.
29 Aux ne raw ka kix ah nin:
dee lhaong an kix pa hu pex mawh ceu tix lie?
Jauh yam an,
dee an tom kok mawh ‘dee lhaongf’ tom si dauh heue.
30 “Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
yuh ka mawx pex lai ca kawn yuh ka phi map pa hoik yuh tax du tix,
mai krub ka riang rao pa hoik krub kix ang mawh lie?
31 Viang mawh daux singaix in,
pex ca kawn tan lu ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa hoik tan kix,
mai hoik teei kawn simex tix kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa gleeing tix tan ka riang rao pa krub kix,
mai kieh seeing brai yuh to kaux tix.
Pex pa mawh pui Yi-seu-liex in kix kawn pon hoek khiox khaw tix chawk cheng ka alo aux heue!
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka ju tix leeing ju ka pex yuh nin:
ang cu pex chawk cheng heue!
32 Pex hoik but rhawm tix hawt ka meung piang haktiex,
awm pui meung tang meung pa hoik krub ka khaox ox mai simaox rang yuh nan.
An ang doh pon lih tix mawh heue!”
Krax pa ciyang Siyiex mai krax pa pluat ploi
33 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka ju tix leeing ju ka pex yuh nin:
aux ne raw ka pex,
aux tag sang teei taix pa ting siawp sibrawm tix,
plawng taix pa ciyang tix,
klup krax song rhawm kuang ka piang pex.
34 Yam khrawm aux pex ot tau,
ka deeh hoek khaing meung pa vuk vied vuk viang pex tix ot ka ku daux ku dee,
aux tag sang kieh pex cheu tix yaox ka taix pa ting siawp sibrawm tix,
plawng taix pa ciyang tix,
mai krax song rhawm pa klup tix.
35 Aux tag sang viex pex hoek ka ‘daux tiag daux tu cie pui kaing mheen,g
’ mai ciyang pex dee an.
36 Singaix pa hoik luan hoik ciyang aux yuh ka mawx ka tax du pex daux tiag daux tu Si-nai,
yam in aux tag sang hawt tix ciyang ka pex yuh nan.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
37 “Aux tag sang kuang ka piang pex tom nyawm nyiem,
mai kieh pex priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu aux.
38 Aux tag sang teei tix tik ka pui pa tieh vheen mai pui pa yuh phi map ka si nax pex.
Aux tag sang gah kix hu,
kieh kix lih hu khaing haktiex pa vai kix tix ot yam in,
viang mawh nan,
ang doh kieh kix deeh hoek ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
39 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik eue,
kieh to kaux pex tang tat heue!
Ca krub ka riang rao pa krub pex heue!
Viang mawh nan,
aux ne raw ka pex,
jauh yam in,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka aux,
paw vi lai baox tan lu ka riang rao pa krub an kieh seeing brai yuh ceu kaux pa singax aux.
40 Pex pa mawh pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik tag sang ot ka haktiex an,
ot ka piang gawng pa singax an,
pa mawh gawng lhaong Yi-seu-liex,
mai krub ka aux.
Aux tag sang chix khaw rhawm ka pex,
krox pex viex krax tan,
ceu pa blawn mhawm,
mai khawng phak lak sawng pa singax singeh hoek tan lu ka aux.
41 Khaix hoik khrawm aux pex ot tau,
thung pex lih khaing haktiex pa vuk vied vuk viang pex kah,
aux tag sang rieb ka taox bhawng Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa tan pex;
meung ka piang haktiex kawx tag sang tawng grawng pa mawh aux pa singax singeh.
42 “Yam thung aux pex deeh ka Yi-seu-liex,
hoek ka haktiex pa hoik ap aux tix tawx ka tax du pex,
mai pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
43 Yam an,
pex tag sang keud ka kaing pa kaig pa tua pa hoik yuh tix ka singaix pa hoik luan,
mai tawng grawng pa kieh pex to kaux tix seeing brai.
Pex tag sang koeng ka to kaux tix kheu kaing pa caox pa caig pa hoik yuh tix.
44 Yam teei aux kaing pa yuh to kaux tix hoek jhi ka min plak ngaix tix,
pex pui Yi-seu-liex in kix tag sang tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu ang aux hawt tix ciyang pex awm kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh pex,
mai kaing pa seeing pa brai pa yuh pex,
pa khaw pex tix pon.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Ngu ting ka haktiex plak seh
45 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
46 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix jhak ka haktiex plak seh heue!
Veeh ngai maix plak seh,
mai krai lox jhai sivoe nong khaox.
47 Krai ka nong khaox pa koe plak seh,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong.
Jhak heue,
aux hoik ploi ngu heue!
Ngu tag sang gleeing ka khaox ox pa koe ka daux nong khaox khawm uik,
mawh khaox pa klai lhax ang ah,
pa kroh ang ah,
hoik gleeing khawm uik.
Ngu ting ang lai pon preet,
jauh tix gleeing ka plak seh tom hoek ka plak laong,
pui ku kaux tag sang mhong ka pa hax rao ngu an.
48 Kix tag sang tawng pa ploi ngu an mawh aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang koe pui pa pon preet yeet heue.”
49 Viang mawh nan,
aux thiang ka ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
paw vi kieh aux yuh kaing in;
pui hoik salao ka aux mai krai lox si nieb jheeng jheeng heue.”
