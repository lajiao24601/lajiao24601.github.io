Krax caox pa hoek piang pui Yo-tax
15
aSimiang Siyiex baox krai ka aux ah nin:
“Viang Maw-si mai Sa-mu-eu hoik cung tix khiox khaw ka plak sivoe ngai aux kawx,
aux ang caox rhawm ka pui in kix.
Grheu kix hu heue!
Nhie kix yoi tix singai hu khaing plak sivoe ngai aux heue!
2 bPhan kix chawk,
caw kix tix sang hu dee mawx,
maix tom krai ka kix ah nin:
Pui pa khaw tix yum ka saux,
yum heue!
Pui pa khaw tix yum ka naing,
yum heue!
Pui pa khaw tix yum ka cai rhawm,
yum heue!
Pui pa khaw tix giex laung hu,
giex laung hu heue!
3 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik but tix kieh kix si pup ka krax laux krax leeing pon ceu:
pa yum ka vaig gawn;
sox tiag kiied tix viex to kaux yum hu;
sim pa pu rao ma kaoh tix ih;
to sat bleet tix ih.
4 cMawh kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh Ma-na-si kawn simex Si-si-cia simiang Yo-tax ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
aux tag sang kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex yaox kix,
tag yum koeng ka heue.”
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung heue,
pui mawx sang chix caox rhawm ka pex lie?
Pui mawx sang chix laux rhawm son pex lie?
Pui mawh sang chix veeh tix chawk pex nyawm ot lie?
6 Pex hoik tieh vheen ka aux;
pex hoik veeh tix ang simi ka aux.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang nyeu taix tix lauh max yuh pex;
aux ang lai baox pon gun ka krax pa song rhawm tix.d
7 Aux tag sang keem tix tik pex awm keem pui tix tik ka kam yuh nan ka daux veng ku dauh.
Pui cie aux eue,
aux tag sang yuh pex laux leeing;
aux tag sang pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun pex,
mawh kheu ang pex loh ka rhawm rhi tix;
8 miex mai ka daux haktiex pex tag sang kawn lheu nie khang mhaik pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra.
Aux pauh ka pui nhum ju pa ting riang khankix,
kieh miex kix laux rhawm laux rhi.
Aux teei krax kha rhawm,
krax pa yaoh yee puing ka kix.
9 Bun miex pa yum kawn aliah kaux an hoik goh tix yum pi;
nawh ang lai pon toek bhawm tix lih.
Krax keud pot jun pot nawh hoik grai khawm uik;
nawh pon krax kaig krax tua,
mai krax laux leeing som nai.
Aux tag sang kieh pui koeng pauh ka pui pa kawn deuk khankix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Yie-li-mi krai krax pa laux rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex
10 Aux kied koe krax laux krax leeing heue!
Miex aux yuh ka mawx lai keh ka aux lie?
Aux kawn sang caw tix yuh simah ka pui pa ot daux meung ku kaux heue!
Aux ang bax tawx pui tang kaux yaog mau,
mai ang bax yaog mau ka pui tang kaux,
viang mawh nan pui ku kaux hoik jhax lawg ka aux khawm uik.
11 Simiang Siyiex eue,
phan aux ang bax sau riang rao tix yuh kaing maix,
phan aux ang bax khiox khaw son kix yam si pup pui koeng ka krax kha rhawm,
kieh krax jhax lawg kix lih tix mawh khaing heue! (
12 Ang koe pui pa pon raig pot ka gawng rhiem,
nie mawh gawng rhiem pa yuh pui tau mai tawng pa viex khaing caong ma laong,
nie ang koe pui pa pon raig pot heue.)
13 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Son sang ciyang ka phi map pui cie aux pa ot ka daux meung haktiex ku dauh,
aux tag sang ploi pui koeng hoek tix tud rhim ka sin ngao cie kix.
14 Aux tag sang kieh kix ot ka haktiex pa ang kix meed ang kix tawng,
kieh kix yuh siliex cawng pui koeng;
mawh kheu krax pa song rhawm aux hoik awm ngu pa rup,
mai ang chix yeet leeing ju heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawng khawm uik.
Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka aux,
teem coi ka aux,
pawk bix ka pui pa tuk tiak tuk te ka aux khankix liak!
Khiox khaw maix pawk bix,
paw vi dha bawg yam,
paw vi kieh kix koe bawg yam tix yuh aux laux.
Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka aux,
grawng hoik kham aux ka krax kaig krax tua hoik mawh kheu son maix heue.
16 Maix si kah ka aux,
aux hoik priex ot daux rhawm tix ku gum.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing eue,
aux hoik mawh pui cie maix;
gum lox maix hoik kieh rhawm aux naog ka krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
17 Aux ang bax kieh bawg yam nai,
ang bax yuh krax gaux rhawm mai pui tang kaux,
ang bax ot ka daux krax gaux rhawm yuh kawn pui.
Son sang priex aux tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix,
aux tang ot nong nong tix,
rhawm rhi aux hoik naog ka krax pa song rhawm.
18 Yuh ka mawx aux lai kha rhawm tix ang jud ang jai lie?
Yuh ka mawx khaix mat aux lai ang pon tix pai moi lie?
Khex sang awm maix klawng iak pa kroh yuh nan,
ang pon tix een rhawm tix kah,
mai hoik kieh aux laux rhawm laux rhi lie?”
19 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox aux ah nin:
“Phan maix deeh hoek,
aux tag sang rieb tix priex ka maix,
kieh maix deeh mawh siliex aux.
Phan maix ang krai ka lox giex lox going,
ca krai lao ka gum lox pa khaw tix krai,
maix tag sang deeh mawh pui pa si kah tang aux.
Pui hun tag sang deeh tang tawng tix hoek ka maix;
maix ang siao khin ka kix.
20 Aux tag sang kieh maix kiien awm guh ding rhiem yuh nan ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun.
Kix puing ka maix,
ang pex ka maix.
Aux tag sang ot tau maix,
kia ngiien ka maix nyawm ot.
21 Aux tag sang taoh maix lih khaing ka daux tawm taix pui tud pui rhim khankix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
