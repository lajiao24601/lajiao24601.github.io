Simiang Maw-ya kok ka Pa-lang
22
Pui Yi-seu-liex baox nied tix hu sivoe,
mai sum nyiex bang ot ka daux lung Maw-ya,
ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
toi rawm veng Yie-li-keu heue.
2 Pa-leux kawn simex Si-paw simiang Maw-ya mhong ka pa hoik yuh pui Yi-seu-liex ka ceu pui Ya-maw-lix,
mai hoik tawng ka nie pui kix,
3 nawh mai kawn pui cie nawh kied lhat graung.
4 Kheu nan,
pui Maw-ya ah nin ka simiang ceu pui Mi-tiien:
“Cub pui hun in kix ang rheeng tag sang yuh can ceu pa koe sivie siveng ex laux leeing khawm uik,
awm hoik uik sawm moi ka rib pa koe daux tiag yuh nan.”
Simiang Pa-leux
5 aploi pui cau khankix hu kok ka Pa-lang kawn simex Pi-eux hoek;
Pa-lang ot ka haktiex pui Ya-meung dex rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti haktiex Pi-tawx.
Pui cau an krai lox pa krai Pa-leux ka nawh ah nin:
“Koe pui tix ceu hoek khaing Ai-cix,
mai hoik tauh hu ka haktiex ku dauh,
yam in kix hoik dex sang pon cax ka haktiex ex.
6 Kaux pui kix kawn nie khaing ex,
yuh buan son jhax lawg ka kix son aux liak.
Phan ang mawh nan pon pex ka kix,
mai grheu kix lih khaing daux haktiex in.
Aux hoik tawng,
phan maix nawb buan son,
pui khankix tag pon buan son;
phan maix jhax lawg,
mai kix tag pon krax laux leeing heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Maw-ya mai simiang pui Mi-tiien,
viex khawng phak mau son sang yuh si meed hu ka Pa-lang tan,
mai krai lox pa krai simiang Pa-leux ka nawh.
8 Pa-lang ah nin ka kix:
“Pex paung tin daux sawm in,
pa sax aux tag deeh krai ka pex lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux.”
Simiang Maw-ya khankix tom ot tix paung mai Pa-lang tan heue.
9 Siyiex lih hoek ka Pa-lang dee an,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Pui in kix pa ot tau mai maix mawh pui mawx?”
10 Pa-lang pawk ah nin:
“Simiang Maw-ya pa mawh Pa-leux ploi kix hoek krai ka aux,
11 koe pui tix ceu hoek khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
mai hoik tauh hu ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
Nawh nhie aux jhax lawg kix son tix;
mai son sang pex ka kix,
grheu kix hu heue.”
12 Siyiex ah nin ka Pa-lang:
“Paw hu tau mai pui in kix,
mai ang pon tix jhax lawg ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mawh kheu aux hoik tawx buan ka kix heue.”
13 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Pa-lang hu ka pui cau Pa-leux ah nin:
“Deeh ing heue,
Simiang Siyiex ang cu aux hu mai pex.”
14 Kix tom deeh ing ka Pa-leux,
mai krai ka nawh ang Pa-lang hoek tau mai kix heue.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Pa-leux baox ploi pui pa ting ceu khaing sivoe pa nie hu tix cub.
16 Kix hoek ka Pa-lang,
mai krai lox pa krai Pa-leux ka nawh ah nin:
“Yuh buan son paw vi koe ceu pa du ang sang hoek ka aux!
17 Aux tag sang tawx maix cie mau tom nie,
maix ah ceu pa tix ang ah,
aux tag sang hawt tix yuh.
Yuh buan son hoek jhax lawg pui in kix son aux liak heue.”
18 Viang mawh nan,
Pa-lang ah nin:
“Viang Pa-leux sang tawx mau khri can pa koe ka daux nyiex haw tix ka aux khawm uik kawx,
aux tag ang pon lheu lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux ka daux kaing iak mai kaing ting ku ceu.
19 Viang mawh nan,
viex pex paung tin daux sawm in,
awm pui pa hoek sivoe,
son sang tawng aux Simiang Siyiex kawn koe ang koe lox tang ceu pa sang baox krai ka aux heue.”
20 Sawm an,
Siyiex hoek ka Pa-lang dee an,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Phan pui in kix hoek kok maix ing tau mai kix,
maix khre tix ing tau mai kix heue;
viang mawh nan,
maix hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai aux.”
21 Ngaix khaix plak pon ngawb cao,
Pa-lang kaoh,
mai sau an brug tix ka piang la,
mai lih hu tau mai simiang Maw-ya khankix heue.
Pa-lang mai la nawh
22 Siyiex ang muh Pa-lang lih hu tau mai kix,
Pa-lang brug la,
yam hu tau siliex mai nawh ka daux krax,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex cung tix tieb tie nawh ka sigreung krax.
23 La yaox cung kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ka dee an,
viex vaig pa lawm ka daux taix tix,
la veeh tix hu ka praok krax mai hu laig daux kaung.
Pa-lang si vied ka la,
son sang kieh la deeh lawng daux krax.
24 Kheu nan,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex cung ka si nax si peem plix siiet khawm ra plak dee si khawp krax,
guh simaox lhaong hoik koe ka ra plak.
25 La yaox ka kawn cau Siyiex,
la tom greed tix dex ding,
mai kheet caong Pa-lang mat ka guh;
Pa-lang si vied ka la.
26 Kawn cau Siyiex baox nied tix hu sivoe,
mai cung ka dee si khawp krax,
dee ang krax via lai koe tix hu khawm ra plak.
27 Yam yaox la ka kawn cau Siyiex,
la gup tix it piang tiex.
Pa-lang song rhawm,
mai si vied la ka khaox gix tix.
28 Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh la chix si kah,
mai ah nin ka Pa-lang:
“Hoik yuh aux ceu tix ka maix lie?
Maix lai si vied aux yuh nin loe bawg lie?”
29 Pa-lang pawk ah nin:
“Mawh kheu hoik map nai maix ka aux!
Phan vaig koe daux tawm taix aux,
aux tag pauh ka maix heue!”
30 La deeh ah nin:
“Ang aux mawh la pa hoik brug maix tom yam iak tix ang mawh lie?
Aux hoik sax yuh nin ka maix lie?”
Pa-lang deeh ah nin:
“Maix ang sax yuh nin heue!”
31 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh ngai Pa-lang yaox ka kawn cau,
pa viex vaig mai cung ka daux krax tan.
Pa-lang tom gup tix piang tiex.
32 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai si vied la tix loe bawg lie?
Aux hoek tix tieb tie ka krax pa hu maix,
mawh kheu maix ang khaw tix hu.
33 La maix yaox ka aux,
mai via mox tix khaing aux loe bawg,
phan nawh ang via mox tix khaing aux,
aux hoik dawg pauh ka maix,
mai deuh la im heue.”
34 Pa-lang deeh pawk ah nin:
“Aux hoik phit lud,
kheu ang aux tawng maix cung tix tieb tie aux ka daux krax.
Phan maix krai ang aux khaw tix hu,
mai aux tag sang deeh ing heue.”
35 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Ing tau mai pui an kix heue.
Viang mawh nan,
maix tag sang hawt ngie tix krai,
awm lox pa hoik krai aux ka maix.”
Pa-lang tom ing tau mai kix heue.
Pa-leux gaux rhawm rieb hoek Pa-lang
36 Yam mhong Pa-leux hoek Pa-lang,
mai hu rieb nawh ka veng Ya-eux;
veng an ot ka praok rawm klawng Ya-noeng daog haktiex Maw-ya.
37 Pa-leux ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux ploi pui hu kok maix hoek ka daux tix bawg,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang cu tix hoek lie?
Maix keud aux ang pon tawx tix ih mau tom kub lie?”
38 Pa-lang ah nin:
“Aux hoik hoek ang mawh lie?
Yam in,
aux kawn sang krai ceu pa tix lie?
Aux sang krai ngie lox pa nhie Siyiex aux krai khaing.”
39 Pa-lang hu tau mai Pa-leux hoek ka veng Hu-saw.
40 Pa-leux pauh ka moi mai yung dee an,
mai tawx nex ka Pa-lang khawm simiang khankix pa hu tau mai nawh heue.
Lox jhai Pa-lang bawg tix
41 Ka daux singaix khaix plak pon ngawb cao,
Pa-leux viex Pa-lang hu ka Pa-maw Pa-lix;
jauh ka dee an,
Pa-lang pon tix yaox ka pui Yi-seu-liex tix blah heue.
