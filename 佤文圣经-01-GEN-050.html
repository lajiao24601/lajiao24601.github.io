50
Yaw-seux gup tix yiiem piang keeing tix,
mai ngheet ka nawh.
2 Khaix an Yaw-seux tawx lox ka cao chix sidah mai kieh kix khre to kaux yum keeing tix ka sidah bhawng.
Cao chix sidah khankix hawt tix yuh heue.
3 Kix hawt been daux kix,
yuh kaing sau sidah pa ang rhawm kieh laux ka to kaux yum nawh deuh lawng tipon singaix.
Pui khankix yiiem doe son Ya-keux atigliah singaix ka haktiex Ai-cix heue.
4 Khaix hoik luan singaix yiiem doe kix,
Yaw-seux ah nin ka siliex simiang:
“Pex hu krai lox in ka simiang liak!
5 a‘Sivoe sang yum keeing aux,
nawh hoik kieh aux tiex tu si paung nawh ka daux si meeig pa tang rien nawh ka daux haktiex Cia-nang.
Grawh grawng nawh,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux hu si paung ka keeing tix;
khaix hoik kaing,
aux tag deeh hoek.’ ”
6 Simiang pawk lox:
“Maix Hu heue,
maix yuh awm hoik kieh nawh maix tiex tu si paung ka nawh heue.”
7 Khaix an,
Yaw-seux hu si paung ka keeing tix.
Can siliex simiang mai khawm kuad song daux nyiex haw mai cao kaing ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khankix hu tau mai Yaw-seux.
8 Pui nyiex ma Yaw-seux,
pux ke nawh ku kaux,
mai pui nyiex keeing nawh khankix kawx hu tau dee an;
mawh ngie kawn nyawm iak mai cub yung,
moi mai pex pa prah kix daux haktiex Keu-sang.
9 Pa hu tau mai nawh kawn koe cub ling law mai pui naing brug brung.
Pui pa hu kied koe sibrawm.
10 Kix hoek ka glauh nghox Ya-ta pa koe toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
kix yiiem dee an tix cu;
Yaw-seux yiiem doe son keeing tix tan aliah singaix.
11 Pui Cia-nang yaox can pui pa yiiem ka daux glauh nghox Ya-ta dee an,
tom ah nin:
“Lox yiiem pui Ai-cix kied sidaing!”
Mawh kheu nan,
dee an tom kok mawh Ya-paw Me-sib.
12 Kawn simex Ya-keux khankix yuh awm pa hoik tawm keeing kix lox ka kix,
13 cViex to kaux nawh hu ka haktiex Cia-nang,
mai si paung nawh daux rang pa koe daux kaung daux rang Me-pi-la,
plak lih singaix veng Mang-lix;
daux rang in mawh pa hoik liag Ya-paw-la-hang ka Yi-fu-loeng,
ceu pui Heux,
son gawng si meeig tix.
14 Khaix hoik si paung Yaw-seux keeing tix,
nawh mai pux aik nawh khankix deeh ing ka Ai-cix khawm pui pa hu si paung keeing nawh khankix heue.
Yaw-seux kieh pux aik tix nyawm rhawm
15 Khaix hoik yum keeing kix,
pux aik Yaw-seux khankix keud:
“Phan Yaw-seux saux rhawm ka ex,
mai hawt kaing pa yuh ex ka nawh sivoe an,
pawk bix ka ex,
ex sang yuh ka mawx?”
16 Khaix an kix ploi pui cau hu ka Yaw-seux mai ah nin:
“Sivoe sang yum keeing,
17 hoik tawx lox ka ex kieh ex krai ka maix tix gum:
‘Pux ke maix hoik lud ka maix,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map kix.’
Yix mawh siliex Siyiex keeing maix;
grawh grawng nawh,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map yix liak.”
Yam mhong Yaw-seux ka lox an nawh yiiem ka heue.
18 Khaix an,
pux ke nawh khankix hoek ka nawh mai gup tix krub ka sivoe nawh mai ah nin:
“Yix mawh siliex maix heue.”
19 Yaw-seux krai ka kix ah nin:
“Paw lhat graung,
khex keud pex aux mawh Siyiex lie?
20 Viang pex keud sang yuh pa caox ka aux kawx,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik kieh mawh ceu pa mhawm,
son sang taoh ju pui tom nie;
mawh kheu kaing pa lih tix mawh sivoe in,
in singaix pui tom yaox tix im ju tom nie.
21 Pex paw koe krax lhat graung,
aux tag eei pex mai kawn simex kawn bun pex ku kaux heue.”
Yaw-seux krai lox nyawm rhawm ka kix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm,
mai kieh kix vhah rhawm heue.
Grawng yum Yaw-seux
22 Yaw-seux mai pui nyiex ma keeing nawh kawn ot daux Ai-cix.
Yam yum nawh koe tix yieh kao num.
23 Yam im ju nawh,
nawh hoik yaox ka kawn saux Yi-fa-liien;
mai hoik teem ka kawn saux Ma-na-si kawn praix Ma-ciex khankix.
24 Yaw-seux ah nin ka pux aik tix khankix:
“Aux dex sang yum,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka pex,
viex pex lih khaing haktiex in,
mai hu ka haktiex pa hoik tiex tu Siyiex tix tawx ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux tan heue.”
25 dYaw-seux kieh pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tiex tu;
nawh ah nin:
“Pex tag tiex tu ka aux.
Yam viex Siyiex pex hu khaing dee an,
pex tag viex si-ang aux hu khaing tin.”
26 Yam koe ju Yaw-seux tix yieh kao num,
nawh yum ka daux Ai-cix.
Kix khre nawh ka sidah pa ang kieh to kaux yum nawh laux,
mai sau to kaux yum nawh ka daux lawng heue.
