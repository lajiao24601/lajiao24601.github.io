Kaw-li-yax tiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex
17
Pui Fe-li-see khrawm tix ot ka veng Saw-keux haktiex Yo-tax,
rien tix sang puing ka naing.
Kix sum bang ot ka Yi-fu Ta-ming,
si nax Saw-keux mai Ya-si-cia.
2 Sao-law mai pui Yi-seu-liex khrawm tix sum bang ot ka ngawd Yi-la,
rien tix puing ka pui Fe-li-see tan heue.
3 Pui Fe-li-see dia tix ot ka piang gawng;
pui Yi-seu-liex dia tix ot ka piang gawng tang mu ka si nax kix ngawd hoik khan ka tix mu heue.
4 Koe pui veng Cia-theux tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Keu-li-yax,
nawh kaoh khaing bang pui Fe-li-see,
mai tiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pui in been lhaong dex koe loe kongjheea,
5 nawh ceep ka kraung tawng,
been cien koe tix phuan aliah kongcing,
mai kaing nawh ceep ka mhok tawng,
6 ba nawh kawx mawh kraung tawng pa tie kah.
Piang klip nawh klawm ka pliah tawng tix gawng.
7 Kraung pliah nawh hoik ting awm gawng kling cag taing man,
pie toh pliah rhiem been cien koe aliah kongcing.
Koe pui naing tix kaux viex ka dieb tie naok nawh,
mai paoh daux krax sivoe nawh.
8 Keu-li-yax kaoh cung,
mai kok yo pui naing Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Pex ot tan yuh pa tix lie?
Dia tix sang puing ka naing lie?
Aux mawh pui Fe-li-see;
mai pex mawh siliex cawng Sao-law.
Sawg pui tix kaux lih lauh ka aux heue!
9 Phan nawh pex,
pon pauh ka aux,
yix tag yuh siliex cawng pex;
phan aux pex,
pon pauh ka nawh,
pex tag yuh siliex cawng khre ka yix.
10 Ka daux in singaix,
aux sang tiex pui naing Yi-seu-liex.
Pex pon ang pon ploi pui lih tix lauh ka aux heue!”
11 Sao-law mai pui naing cie nawh mhong ka lox in hoik si yaung mai kied lhat graung ka heue.
Ta-wue ot daux bang pui naing Sao-law
12 Ta-wue mawh pui veng Paw-li-haung meung Yo-tax ceu pui Yi-fa-tha kawn simex Yie-si.
Yie-si koe kawn simex daix kaux.
Yam yuh Sao-law simiang,
Yie-si hoik kied riex ju.
13 Kawn simex nawh loe kaux hu puing naing tau mai Sao-law:
pux ke lhong kaux Yi-li-ya,
nyi kaux Ya-pi-na-ta,
sam kaux Sa-ma.
14 Ta-wue mawh kawn simex pux nghoik pa iak khaing paox.
Yam ot pux ke nawh loe kaux tau mai Sao-law,
15 bawg tix blah Ta-wue ing ka Paw-li-haung liang ka yung keeing tix heue.
16 Keu-li-yax ku ngawb ku bo lih tiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
hoek koe been tipon singaix heue.
17 Koe tix singaix,
Yie-si ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Viex si gleem nghox ca pa hoik ping tix kao kongcing mai khao mun kao khliep,
nied tix hu ka bang naing mai kia ka pux ke maix khankix.
18 Maix viex khliep rawm teeh in kao khliep mai tawx ka simiang naing.
Jhak tix yaox grawh grawng pux ke maix khankix yuh ka mawx,
khaix an teei tix viex kraung tix ceu ceu hoek,
son sang kham sakse hoik yaox maix ka kix,
grawng pa hoik mhawm ot kix khawm uik.
19 Simiang Sao-law,
pux ke maix khankix,
mai pui naing Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai yuh naing mai pui Fe-li-see ka ngawd Yi-la heue.”
20 Ka daux ra singaix,
Ta-wue kaoh cao,
mai tawx phung yung ka pui tang kaux kuang kia,
nawh tom hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai keeing tix Yie-si,
mai viex ka priex preem hu heue.
Yam hoek nawh dee ot bang pui naing,
pui Yi-seu-liex jiet caw tix lih dia tix mai kok yo lox son sang yuh ka naing;
21 Pui naing Fe-li-see mai pui naing Yi-seu-liex dia tix veeh pak ngai tix plak paox.
22 Ta-wue deuh pa viex tix ot dee ot cao kaing pa tawx priex preem tan,
mai to hoek sawg pux ke tix ka plak sivoe dee dia kix paox tix son sang puing ka naing,
chawk grawh grawng pux ke tix yuh ka mawx.
23 Yam si kah nawh mai pux ke tix,
Keu-li-yax lih hoek,
awm singaix pa luan yuh nan,
simah ka pui naing Yi-seu-liex;
Ta-wue mhong kah.
24 Yam yaox pui Yi-seu-liex ka Keu-li-yax,
tom lhat mai tang yaog tix to.
25 Kix lai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Jhak ka pui an!
Nawh simah ka pui naing Yi-seu-liex!
Simiang Sao-law sang tawx ka pui pa pon pauh Keu-li-yax cie mau tom nie,
mai baox tawx kawn bun tix mex nawh,
ang lai kieh mau koh keenb nyiex ma keeing nawh koe heue.”
26 Ta-wue chawk ka pui pa ot praok tix ah nin:
“Phan koe pui pa pon pauh ka pui Fe-li-see yum,
mai teei tix tik krax kaig krax tua khaing Yi-seu-liex,
nawh tag sang pon ceu pa tix lie?
Pui Fe-li-see pa ang bax Pat-hak tix an mawh mawx lie?
Nawh lai pon nieb map nai ka pui naing cie Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju!”
27 Kix tom krai ka Ta-wue,
pui pa pon pauh ka Keu-li-yax,
tag sang pon ngawh riang tix pa koe min plak ngai heue.
28 Pux ke Ta-wue pa kaux Yi-li-ya mhong lox pa si kah Ta-wue tau mai pui khankix,
tom ang gaux rhawm kah,
tom ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Hoek maix yuh tix dee in lie?
Mawh mawx pa liang yung daux tiag tang maix lie?
Maix pa lhak luan in!
Mawh hoek maix tix jhak ka naing heue!”
29 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Aux lud yuh ceu pa tix lie?
Kheu aux ang khaw tix chawk cheng lie?”
30 Ta-wue deeh veeh tix plak ot pui tang kaux tix kaux,
mai baox chawk cheng ka lox pa hoik awm paox.
Ta-wue chawk cheng pui miex kaux ku bawg,
lox pa pon nawh tix mhong hoik pawk tix awm paox tix heue.
31 Koe pui mhong ka lox pa chawk cheng Ta-wue,
mai hu krai ka Sao-law,
Sao-law tom kok Ta-wue hoek.
32 Ta-wue ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Vawng,
ex ang khaw tix lhat ka pui Fe-li-see!
Aux sang hu lauh ka nawh heue.”
33 Sao-law ah nin:
“Ang chix,
maix sang chix lauh nawh yuh ka mawx lie?
Maix mawh ngie kawn nyawm,
mai nawh hoik mawh pui naing pa hoik sax puing ka naing kaing yieh bawg heue!”
34 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Vawng,
aux mawh cao pa liang yung keeing tix.
Koe bawg tix blah,
sivai sang si mai krih hoek giex ka kawn yung,
35 aux leue tix lauh kah,
mai deeh pon taoh ka kawn yung.
Phan sivai sang si mai krih deeh plab tix ka aux,
aux tom ka hauk jheeh reeig kah,
mai lauh nawh yum heue.
36 Aux hoik sax lauh ka sivai sang si mai krih yum,
aux kawx chix tix pauh ka pui Fe-li-see an pa hoik map nai ka pui naing cie Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju.
37 Simiang Siyiex hoik sax taoh aux luat luan khaing nghim sivai sang si mai nghim krih,
mai nawh kawx tag sang chix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui Fe-li-see an heue.”
Sao-law ah nin:
“Chix tix,
maix hu heue!
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai maix.”
38 Sao-law teei kraung naing pa ceep tix tawx Ta-wue ceep,
pa mawh mhok tawng tix mu mai kraung tawng tix jung.
Nawh ceep mhok tawng ka kaing Ta-wue.
39 Ta-wue teei vaig kawg Sao-law,
shiet ot ka daog kraung tawng an,
sang yaog tix hu ang chix hu,
mawh kheu ang nawh meed ceep tix kraung in kix.
Nawh ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Aux ang chix ceep ka kraung in hu puing ka naing,
aux ang meed.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom peeik ka kraung in khawm uik.
40 Ta-wue teei ka khaox gix pa liang tix yung kah,
mai baox sut ka simaox pa mhawm sawng phuan mu khaing praok klawng,
mai sau ot ka daux bau pa viex pui liang yung tix siao,
khaix an,
nawh viex ka ak puh pa puing ka loi simaox,
hu rieb tix yuh naing ka Keu-li-yax heue.
Ta-wue pex ka Keu-li-yax
41 Pui Fe-li-see an yaog tix hu ka Ta-wue,
pui pa viex dieb tie naok son nawh hu sivoe.
Yam hoik dex nawh,
42 nawh yaox sidawng ka Ta-wue,
tom map nai ka nawh,
kheu hoik mawh ngie nawh kawn nyawm si naw pa mhawm raoh pak ngai mai pa mhawm grang tix kaux khaing.
43 Nawh chawk Ta-wue ah nin:
“Khaox gix an yuh maix tix kah?
Maix ce aux mawh sox lie?”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ci ka siyiex tix jhax lawg ka Ta-wue,
mai tiex ka Ta-wue ah nin:
44 “Hoek,
aux sang tawx sim saik to sat ih to kaux yum maix!”
45 Ta-wue pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix hoek tix puing ka aux,
mai siao ka vaig gawn,
lawm bhoik,
pliah,
viang mawh nan,
aux puing ka maix,
mawh hoik een rhawm tix ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing;
nawh mawh pa hoik map nai maix,
Siyiex pui naing Yi-seu-liex.
46 In singaix,
Simiang Siyiex sang ap maix ka daux tawm taix aux.
Aux tag sang pex ka maix,
mai tat ka kaing maix.
Aux tag sang tawx sim saik to sat ih ka to kaux yum pui naing Fe-li-see.
Yuh nin,
pui haktiex tom tag tawng,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik koe Siyiex tix kaux;
47 pui haktiex in ku kaux kawx hoik tawng Simiang Siyiex ang siao vaig gawn,
mai lawm bhoik taoh ka pui cie tix.
Nawh yuh ka naing ang koe pa ang nawh pex;
nawh tag sang ap pex ka daux tawm taix yix heue.”
48 Keu-li-yax jauh tix sang hoek dex Ta-wue;
Ta-wue nied tix to hu plak ot pui Fe-li-see,
son sang rieb tix yuh ka naing.
49 Nawh cawk loi simaox tix mu khaing daux bau tix,
mai teei ak puh puing Keu-li-yax kah.
Loi simaox krox dong re Keu-li-yax,
mai max yuh kaing nawh,
Keu-li-yax tom goh plak ngai tix piang tiex.
50 cYuh nin,
Ta-wue ang bax chuh vaig gawn,
siao ngie ak puh tix mu,
loi simaox tix mu khaing,
hoik pex ka Keu-li-yax heue.
51 dTa-wue to tix haok sivoe,
dig ka to kaux Keu-li-yax,
lawd vaig lih khaing daux phak vaig Keu-li-yax,
mai pauh nawh yum,
tat kaing nawh dut heue.
Pui Fe-li-see yaox hoik yum pui pa kua cie kix,
tom tang yaog tix to.
52 Pui naing Yi-seu-liex mai pui naing Yo-tax leue khaix kix,
kok yo mai leue tix pauh ka kix,
mai leue kix tom hoek ka diang siviex veng Cia-theux mai diang siviex veng Yi-keu-loeng.
Pui yum pui mat pui Fe-li-see hoik tauh ka daux tiag khawm uik,
hawt krax Sa-la-ying tom hoek Cia-theuxe mai Yi-keu-loeng.
53 Khaix hoik deeh pui Yi-seu-liex khaing leue ka pui Fe-li-see,
kix tud ka bang pui Fe-li-see.
54 Ta-wue viex ka kaing Keu-li-yax hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai deuh kraung naing cie Keu-li-yax ot daux nyiex gox bang cie tix heue.
Sao-law kok ka Ta-wue
55 Sao-law yaox hoik hu puing Ta-wue ka Keu-li-yax,
nawh tom chawk simiang naing ot greem tix Ya-ni-eu ah nin:
“Ya-ni-eu,
pui si naw nhum ju an mawh kawn simex pui mawx?”
Ya-ni-eu pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng,
aux tiex tu,
aux ang tawng heue.”
56 Sao-law ah nin:
“Hu si kaw ka nawh mawh kawn simex pui mawx heue!”
57 Khaix hoik pauh Ta-wue ka Keu-li-yax yum,
deeh ing ka daux bang ot pui naing,
Ya-ni-eu viex nawh hu ka Sao-law,
Ta-wue kawn viex ka kaing Keu-li-yax.
58 Sao-law chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Si naw nhum ju,
maix mawh kawn simex pui mawx?”
Ta-wue pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui cau maix kawn simex Yie-si pui veng Paw-li-haung heue.”
