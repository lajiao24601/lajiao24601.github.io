Lai Pi-teux Phuk Ra
1
Aux pa mawh Si-meung Pi-teux,
mawh siliex mai kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit.
Aux tiiem tix tawx lai ka pui pa hoik pon ceu pa ting ngawh krax yum rhawm,
awm ex khaing ka daux pa rauh see Siyiex mai cao taoh Ye-sux Khrit yuh nin:
2 Kieh pex pon buan son pa kub mai pa nhim khaing ka daux pa tawng yung ka Siyiex,
mai ka Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue!
Pa kok Siyiex mai pa laug nawh
3 Siawp sibrawm Siyiex hoik tawx krax ot krax koe pa yung yi ceu pa sang lo ex khawm uik ka ex;
buan son in hoik mawh pa pon khaing ka daux pa tawng yung ka Siyiex,
kaw pa kok ex hoek tix pon min plak ngai tix mai pa mhawm tix an.
4 Yuh nin,
nawh hoik tawx buan son pa hoik krai nawh tix deuh pa blawn ting mai pa blawn ngawh ka ex;
ka daux buan son in,
pex hoik to hu khaing alo nex nham pa yuh pui laux leeing an,
mai hoik pon ka alo Siyiex.
5 Kheu son grawng in,
pex tag sau riang tix thiem thie krax mhawm ka piang krax yum rhawm,
thiem thie ngai lai ka piang krax pa mhawm,
6 thiem thie krax priex rhawm ka piang ngai lai,
thiem thie krax gun yeung ka piang krax priex rhawm,
thiem thie krax yung yi ka piang krax gun yeung,
7 thiem thie krax muh ka paox tix ka piang krax yung yi,
thiem thie krax muh ka pui hun ka piang krax muh ka paox tix.
8 In kix tag mawh ceu pa mhawm pa caw pex tix sau riang tix yuh lih;
phan pex hoik koe ka ceu in kix khawm uik,
pex tag sang nie blawn koe riang,
nie blawn chix tawng yung ka Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
9 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa cud cha ka ceu pa mhawm in kix,
mawh pui pa ang chix yaox dee singai,
mawh pui dug ngai,
hoik piawm grawng pon si ghrah phi map pa prim kix heue.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix nie blawn sau riang tix,
kieh krax pa kok Siyiex mai krax pa laug nawh,
lih mawh krax pa cheuk cheu ka pex leeing ju;
phan hoik koe krax pa cheuk cheu in kix,
pex tag ang yaox tix hoek krax pa laux leeing.
11 Yuh nin,
pex tag sang pon ka siawp sibrawm pa grum naog,
mai pon tix laig ka daux meung cie Simiang Siyiex mai cie cao taoh Ye-sux Khrit leeing ju heue.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
viang pex hoik tawng ka kaing in kix,
khaix an mai hoik kaux cong ka daux pa mawh pa meh pa hoik pon tix,
aux ca kawn krai ka pex ang cud.
13 Aux keud yuh nin,
ca mawh kawn im ju aux tix ngaix,
aux tag caw tix teem coi pex doh keud ka kaing in kix jheeng jheeng.
14 Aux hoik tawng,
ang rheeng aux tag sang bheeik luan khaing ghlaok to kaux pa chix cux chix si um;
in hoik mawh pa krai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit ka aux
15 Aux sang sau riang tix keud sawn,
grawng sang kieh pex chix doh keud ka kaing in khaix hoik yum tix heue.
Kham sakse min plak ngai Khrit
16 Pa hoik krai ex ka pex pa mawh grawh grawng siawp sibrawm pa ting Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit mai grawng pa sang deeh hoek nawh,
ang mawh lox lao pa nieb ex pon krai tix;
ex hoik sax yaox pa ting pa lhaong nawh ka ngai tix.
17 aYam pon nawh pa nab yam yung yi mai pa ting min plak ngai keeing Siyiex,
ex kawx ot dee an khawm uik.
Yam an,
Siyiex pa ting min plak ngai ah nin ka nawh:
“In mawh kawn simex pa muh aux,
aux muh ka nawh heue.”
18 Ex ot tau mai nawh piang gawng pa singax,
mai yhaok ex hoik mhong ka lox in lih hoek khaing rao ma heue.
19 Yuh nin,
ex nie yum rhawm ka gum lox pa hoik krai pao cao jhai khankix.
Pex tag caw tix bre rhawm tix ka gum lox in mhawm mhawm;
mawh kheu hoik awm gum lox in kawk ngu pa jhan ka daux pa faik yuh nan,
tom hoek riang praix,
tom hoek jhan riang simuing pon ngawb daux rhawm rhi pex ka daux ngaix an.
20 Kaing ting mawh,
pex tag caw tix doh keud ka yuh nin:
gum lox jhai pa koe ka daux phuk lai sigang khri khawm uik,
ang pon hawt alo tix kah dee bleeh.
21 Mawh kheu ang sax koe gum lox jhai mawh pa kaoh khaing alo kawn pui,
hoik mawh Khuan Singax Singeh pa kieh cao jhai pon krai ka gum lox Siyiex lih heue.
