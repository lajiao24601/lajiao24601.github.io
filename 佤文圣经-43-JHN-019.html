19
Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-la-to tawx lox nhie kix viex Ye-sux hu,
mai si vied ka nawh.
2 Pui naing teei mhok pa taing ka kat lawm,
ceep ka piang kaing nawh,
mai baox tawx nawh ceep kraung dha raoh pliex.
3 Kix hoek dex ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang pui Yo-thaix lang ju pu rhang heue!”
Khaix an lua taix tix tawk ka nawh heue.
4 Pi-la-to baox lih krai ka pui hun ah nin:
“Mhawm!
Aux viex nawh lih kieh pex tawng,
aux ang yaox phi map dee phit nawh tix ceu kawx.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux lih,
ceep ka mhok kat lawm,
ceep ka kraung dha pa raoh pliex.
Pi-la-to ah nin ka kix:
“Jhak!
Pui in heue!”
6 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai pui pau nyiex buan khankix yaox ka Ye-sux,
kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Tawk nawh piang khaox lhak heue!
Tawk nawh piang khaox lhak heue!”
Pi-la-to ah nin ka kix:
“Pex viex nawh hu tawk piang khaox lhak heue.
Aux ang yaox phi map dee phit nawh tix ceu kawx heue.”
7 Pui hun ah nin:
“Yix hoik koe lox tat jhawm prawng,
phan hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh hoik khaw tix yum,
mawh kheu tang sum nawh tix mawh kawn simex Siyiex heue.”
8 Pi-la-to mhong krai kix yuh nin,
nawh nie lhat.
9 Nawh baox laig ka daux nyiex cao kuang kaing,
mai chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Tete mawh kaoh maix khaing dee mawx lie?”
Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ang pawk lox kix.
10 Pi-la-to krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix ang pawk lox aux lie?
Maix hoik tawng aux koe akhuang tix ploi ka maix,
hoik koe akhuang tix tawk maix piang khaox lhak nang heue.”
11 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh kheu hoik tawx Siyiex akhuang in ka maix,
maix tom taug koe sibrawm tix yuh nin ka aux.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui an kix pa tawx aux ka maix nie blawn ting phi map heue.”
12 Pi-la-to mhong ka lox in,
nawh simie tix ploi ka Ye-sux.
Viang mawh nan,
pui hun kok yo ah nin:
“Phan maix ploi ka nawh maix taug ang lai mawh paox grawm Vawng heue!
Pui mawx tang sum tix mawh simiang,
pui an tag mawh pui koeng ka Vawng heue.”
13 Pi-la-to mhong ka lox pa krai kix yuh nin,
nawh tom viex Ye-sux lih,
mai prawng Ye-sux ka dee an pa kok mawh “glawk simaox bawng” (lox pui Si-paw-lai kok mawh Cia-pa-tax).
14 Ka daux singaix an mawh singaix pa rien khre ka Poi Luat-luan,
bawg yam greung ngaix,
Pi-la-to krai ka pui hun ah nin:
“Jhak!
Simiang pex heue.”
15 Kix tom kok yo ah nin:
“Pauh ka nawh heue!
Pauh ka nawh heue!
Tawk nawh piang khaox lhak heue!”
Pi-la-to chawk kix ah nin:
“Sang kieh aux tawk simiang pex piang khaox lhak lie?”
Simiang cao giex kaing li khankix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh ngie Kai-sax pa mawh simiang yix khaing heue!”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-la-to tawx kix tawk Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak heue.
Tawk Ye-sux piang khaox lhak
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.32–44; Lai Ma-kheu 15.21–32; Lai Lu-cia 23.26–43)
Kix viex Ye-sux hu.
17 Ye-sux lih mai klawm khaox lhak cie tix hoek ka “gawng si-ang kaing” (lox pui Si-paw-lai kok mawh Keu-keu-tha.)
18 Kix tawk Ye-sux piang khaox lhak dee an;
kix kawn baox tawk pui tang kaux ra kaux tau mai nawh,
tix kaux tix plak ka si nax Ye-sux.
19 Pi-la-to tiiem na bat phia tix plah,
nhie pui tawk ka khaox lhak.
Na bat phia an tiiem tix ah nin:
“Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux,
simiang pui Yo-thaix heue.”
20 Pui hun yaox ngai lai pa tiiem tix ka na bat phia an,
mawh kheu dee tawk kix Ye-sux piang khaox lhak ang singai khaing veng miex glang tix;
na bat phia in hoik siao ngai lai Si-paw-lai,
ngai lai La-ting,
mai ngai lai Si-lax loe ceu tiiem kah.
21 Simiang cao giex kaing li pui Yo-thaix ah nin ka Pi-la-to:
“Paw vi tiiem mawh ‘simiang pui Yo-thaix’,
tag caw tix tiiem mawh ‘pui in tang kok tix mawh simiang pui Yo-thaix heue’.”
22 Pi-la-to pawk ah nin:
“Pa tiiem aux,
ang lai chix tix tieh loh heue!”
23 Khaix hoik tawk pui naing Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak,
kix teei tix guah kraung dha nawh lih koe pon nyum,
tix kaux tix nyum.
Kix baox teei ka kraung ceep plak daux nawh;
kraung dawt in ang koe rhoi khaix caing,
hoik mawh pa yuh ka man khawm plah.
24 aMawh kheu nan,
lien kum kawng paox tix ah nin:
“Ex paw vi riah,
ex cawk lai,
pui mawx sang pon tix cie.”
In hoik kieh gum lox phuk lai sigang khri lih tix mawh ah nin:
Kix hoik guah ka kraung ceep aux,
mai baox cawk lai ka kraung dawt aux heue.
Pui naing rug hoik yuh ka kaing in heue.
25 Pui pa cung ka praok khaox lhak Ye-sux,
mawh miex Ye-sux,
pux o miex nawh,
Ma-li-yax moeng Keu-law-pa,
mai Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la.
26 Ye-sux yaox miex tix mai pui gau pa muh nawh cung ka praok,
nawh tom krai ka miex tix ah nin:
“Miex,
maix jhak ka kawn simex maix heue!”
27 Khaix an,
nawh baox krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Jhak,
miex maix!”
Jauh tom yam an,
pui gau rieb miex Ye-sux hoek paung ot ka daux nyiex tix heue.
Grawng yum Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.45–56; Lai Ma-kheu 15.33–41; Lai Lu-cia 23.44–49)
28 bYe-sux hoik tawng kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh khawm uik,
son sang kieh gum lox phuk lai sigang khri lih tix mawh,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Aux simie tix nyaux rawm heue.”
29 Dee an koe si ngien rawm ka tix mu,
pa hoik prauk naog plai niex;
kix tom teei maox priem caux daux plai niex an,
mai phrawk ka gawng rao rib im krim an,
tawx ka jheeh nawh.
30 Khaix hoik cim Ye-sux kah,
nawh ah nin:
“Hoik lih tix mawh heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh num kaing tix,
mai dut rhawm heue.
Klauh ka paw Ye-sux
31 Ka daux singaix an hoik mawh singaix pa rien khre,
singaix tuah pa mawh singaix poi ting pa tag sang hoek.
Simiang pui Yo-thaix,
son ang sang kieh to kaux yum ot ka khaox lhak ka daux singaix tuah,
kix hu khiox khaw ka Pi-la-to,
nhie pui tawk caong pui pa prawng kix yum pot,
khaix an gah to kaux yum hu.
32 Pui naing hawt tix yuh awm lox tawx simiang,
mai tawk caong pui pa tawk kix tau mai Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak tix plak tix kaux pot.
33 Kix hu dex ka Ye-sux,
mai yaox hoik yum Ye-sux,
kix tom ang bax tawk ka caong nawh pot.
34 Viang mawh nan,
koe lien tix kaux teei pliah klauh paw nawh kah,
dum mawx dum an,
nham mai rawm jauh lih khaing. (
35 In hoik mawh sakse kaing pa kham pui in pa hoik yaox ka ngai tix tete;
nawh hoik tawng sakse pa kham nawh mawh pa mawh pa meh,
son sang kieh pex yum rhawm ka nang.)
36 cMawh kheu kaing in tag sang kieh gum lox pa hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri lih tix mawh ah nin:
“Pie si-ang nawh tix gawng ang pon tix kieh pot.”
37 dPhuk lai sigang khri baox koe tix baux ah nin:
“Kix tag sang maong tix jhak ka pui pa klauh tix ka pliah heue.”
Si paung ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.57–61; Lai Ma-kheu 15.42–47; Lai Lu-cia 23.50–56)
38 Khaix hoik luan kaing in,
koe Yaw-seux pui Ya-li-ma-thaix khiox khaw ka Pi-la-to,
kieh nawh viex to kaux yum Ye-sux hu.
(Yaw-seux mawh pui gau Ye-sux,
mawh kheu lhat nawh ka simiang pui Yo-thaix,
nawh tom ang pon krai mawh tix pui gau tix mai Ye-sux.)
Pi-la-to cu ka lox pa khiox khaw nawh,
Yaw-seux tom viex to kaux yum Ye-sux hu heue.
39 ePa mawh Ni-keu-teu-mawx pa hoek ka Ye-sux yam pon sawm sivoe an hu tau mai Yaw-seux.
Nawh viex Muran mai Acaw pa hoik klao tau mai rawm bee pa si oe bhawng been tix ngoe kongcing.
40 Kiex ra kaux teei man keei sum pa sau sidah bhawng koe kah,
kie to kaux Ye-sux mhawm kah;
in hoik mawh been pa si paung pui Yo-thaix ka pui yum.
41 Ka dee tawk kix Ye-sux piang khaox lhak,
si peem koe ka tix dauh,
ka daux si peem an daux si meeig khraox yi pa ang bax si paung pui koe ka tix mu.
42 Mawh kheu daux singaix an hoik mawh singaix pa rien khre pui Yo-thaix tix,
daux si meeig an kied dex,
kix tom si paung Ye-sux dee an heue.
