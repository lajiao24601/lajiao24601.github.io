Ceu pui pa ot plak lih singaix praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 3.12–22)
32
Ceu pui Lui-piien mai ceu pui Cia-teux ra ceu khankix,
koe moi yung tom haoh hun.
Kix yaox haktiex Ya-shiex mai haktiex Ci-liex dee mhawm tix liang ka khawng brie,
2 tom hu ka Maw-si mai Yi-li-ya-sa,
mai ka simiang kawn pui.
Kix tom ah nin:
3-4 “Simiang Siyiex hoik teem ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pon cax ka haktiex in kix,
pa mawh haktiex Ya-tha-lux,
Ti-peung,
Ya-shiex,
Ling-la,
Si-seu-peung,
Yi-li-ya-lix,
See-pang,
Ni-paw mai Pi-veng,
pa mawh haktiex dee mhawm tix liang ka khawng brie;
ex hoik koe khawng brie tom nie.
5 Phan yix hoik keung tix pon buan son,
tawx haktiex in mawh sin ngao cie yix liak,
paw kieh yix hu tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai paung ka plak an heue.”
6 Maw-si pawk lox:
“Khex sang ot pex dee in,
mai kieh paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yi-seu-liex hu puing ka naing lie?
7 Pex lai sang kieh paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yi-seu-liex khankix laux rhawm laux rhi,
ang yaox tix tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
ang yaox tix laig ka daux haktiex pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kix lie?
8 aYam ploi aux tax du pex jauh ka haktiex Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax hu jiang ka haktiex dee an,
kix hoik mawh nang nan heue.
9 Yam hoek kix ka daux ngawd Yi-seu-keux,
mai yaox ka haktiex an,
viang mawh nan,
yam deeh hoek kix,
kix kieh kawn pui laux rhawm laux rhi,
ang lai cu tix laig ka daux haktiex pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kix.
10 b“Daux ngaix an,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm,
tiex tu ah nin:
11 ‘Can pui pa koe tix nga num haok plak piang ka daux kix,
pa kaoh khaing daux Ai-cix,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon laig ka haktiex an pa ap aux tix tawx ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux,
mawh kheu ang kix nghiet ka aux.’
12 Lox in mawh pa ah ka pui ku kaux,
mawh ngie Cia-leux kawn simex Yie-fu-ni pa mawh ceu pui Ci-ni-si mai Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng pa ang ot tan,
mawh kheu kiex hoik hawt khaix Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix
13 Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka kawn pui,
kieh kix vung viang ka daux tiag tipon num,
tom si dauh hoik laux leeing cub pui pa kieh nawh song rhawm.
14 Yam in pex kawn ca hawt tax du tix,
mawh pui phi map ka daux bawg yam pa khraox,
son sang baox kieh Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
15 Yam in,
phan pex ceu pui Lui-piien mai Cia-teux in kix kawn ca ang cu tix hawt khaix nawh,
nawh tag baox deeh prah pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux tiag daux tu,
mai pex tag glawm ka phi map pa yum kix.”
16 Kix hoek dex ka sivoe Maw-si ah nin:
“Yam in,
khiox khaw maix kieh yix sum kawg yung tin,
mai yuh veng son moeng kawn yix khankix,
17 khaix an,
yix tom rien tix hu yuh naing tau mai paox ceu paox cad tix Yi-seu-liex,
hu puing sivoe,
tom hoek nyawm ot kix ka haktiex cie kix.
Moeng kawn yix tag nyawm ot ka veng pa kaux cong in,
mai ang yaox krax pa tuk tiak pui haktiex in.
18 Khaix hoik sang krox yix pui Yi-seu-liex paox ceu paox cad tix pon cax ka haktiex pa hoik tawx ka kix,
taug yaox tix ing ka nyiex cie tix heue.
19 Yix ang teei tix cie sin ngao pa koe toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang ka plak klip singaix tau mai kix,
mawh kheu hoik cie yix sin ngao pa koe plak lih singai rawm klawng Yaw-tang.”
20 Maw-si pawk lox:
“Phan pex yuh nin tete,
rien khre pex hu yuh naing ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex heue!
21 Kru naing pex tag sang tiang ka plak toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang khawm uik;
kix sang hawt awm gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex puing ka pui pa koeng,
tom hoik pex Simiang Siyiex ka kix,
22 mai pon cax ka haktiex an.
Khaix an pex chix tix deeh ing,
mawh kheu pex hoik yuh pa khaw pex tix yuh ka Simiang Siyiex mai paox ceu paox cad pex Yi-seu-liex.
Simiang Siyiex chix tix tawx haktiex pa koe toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang in plak lih singaix mawh cie pex.
23 Phan pex ang hawt lox pa ap tix,
aux ne raw ka pex,
pex hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex;
pex tag yaox krax ciyang kheu phi map to kaux tix,
mai ang pon tix luat luan.
24 Yam in pex hu sum dung veng,
sum kawg yung son yung.
Viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix piawm lox pa ap pex heue.”
25 Ceu pui Cia-teux mai ceu pui Lui-piien ah nin:
“Yix tag hawt tix yuh awm gum lox maix.
26 Moeng kawn yix,
phung moi yix,
mai phung yung yix deuh ot ka veng Ci-liex tan.
27 Viang mawh nan,
yix ku kaux tag hawt awm lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex rien tix hu puing naing;
yix sang tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang hu puing kah,
awm lox pa ah maix heue.”
28 cMawh kheu nan,
Maw-si krai ka Yi-li-ya-sa,
Yaw-su-yax mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux ah nin:
29 “Phan ceu pui Cia-teux mai ceu pui Lui-piien hoik tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
hawt ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex puing kah.
Phan pex pon kuang ka haktiex an kheu jao teem kix ka pex,
pex tag caw tix tawx haktiex Ci-liex ka kix,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix.
30 Phan kix ang tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang hu puing tau mai pex,
kix tag awm pex pon ngie sin ngao ka daux haktiex Cia-nang tix son.”
31 Ceu pui Cia-teux mai ceu pui Lui-piien pawk lox:
“Yix tag hawt tix yuh awm lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex.
32 Yix tag hawt lox pa tawx nawh tiang ka rawm klawng,
laig ka haktiex Cia-nang puing naing,
son sang pon yix sin ngao ka plak toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix heue.”
33 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si teei haktiex cie Si-hong pa mawh simiang Ya-maw-lix,
mai haktiex Eux pa mawh cie simiang Pa-sang,
mai veng vung viang kix tawx ka ceu pui Cia-teux mai ceu pui Lui-piien,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak.
34 Ceu pui Cia-teux cox sum ka veng Ti-peung,
Ya-tha-lux,
mai veng Ya-law-eu,
35 veng Ya-tha-lu Saw-fang,
veng Ya-shiex mai veng Yaw-pi-ha,
36 veng Paw Ling-la mai veng Paw Ha-lang mawh veng pa kaux pa cong.
37 Ceu pui Lui-piien cox sum ka veng Si-seu-peung,
veng Yi-li-ya-lix mai veng Ci-lie-thing,
38 veng Ni-paw,
veng Pa-lix Mien (ceu kaux in hoik loh tix),
mai veng Si-pi-ma khankix.
Kix sum ka ceu kaux veng pa khraox in kix heue.
39 Kawn simex Ma-na-si ceu pui Ma-ciex hu puing ka haktiex Ci-liex,
pon cax kah,
mai grheu pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot dee an.
40 Kheu nan,
Maw-si tawx veng Ci-liex ka ceu pui Ma-ciex;
kix tom ot tan heue.
41 Ceu pui Ma-na-si pa mawh Ya-eux hu puing ka yaong tix blah,
tom kieh yaong in kix kaux “Yaong Ya-eux.”
42 Naw-pa hu puing ka veng Ci-na mai yaong hawt vung viang an,
tom hawt ceu kaux tix kieh yaong an kaux Naw-pa heue.
