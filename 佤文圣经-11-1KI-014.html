Kawn simex Yie-law-paw-ang yum
14
Yam an,
kawn simex Yie-law-paw-ang pa kaux Ya-pi-yax koe jhawm saux.
2 Yie-law-paw-ang ah nin ka moeng tix:
“Maix loh kraung ceep tix tix vud ha,
kieh pui ang lai tawng ka maix,
khaix an hu ka See-law.
Cao jhai Ya-si-ya ot paung dee an;
nawh mawh pui pa krai chix yuh tix simiang pui Yi-seu-liex an.
3 Maix caw tix viex khao mun kao lon tawx ka nawh,
mai khao mun kroh tix blah,
mai rawm hia tix kawk.
Nawh tag sang krai ka maix grawng kawn simex ax yuh ka mawx heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
moeng Yie-law-paw-ang hu ka nyiex Ya-si-ya haktiex See-law.
Ya-si-ya hoik kied kuad ju,
ngai nawh ang lai riang.
5 Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ka nawh,
grawng sang hoek moeng Yie-law-paw-ang son sang chawk ka jhawm saux pa pon kawn simex tix;
Simiang Siyiex kawx hoik krai ka nawh,
son lox pa khaw nawh tix sang krai heue.
Yam hoik hoek moeng Yie-law-paw-ang,
nawh yuh tix mawh pui tang kaux.
6 Ya-si-ya mhong lox hoek caong nawh ka diang siviex,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Laig daux nyiex!
Aux hoik tawng mawh maix moeng Yie-law-paw-ang,
yuh ka mawx mai lai yuh tix lih mawh pui tang kaux lie?
Aux koe lox pa ang mhawm sang krai ka maix.
7 Deeh ing krai ka Yie-law-paw-ang,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
‘Aux laug maix khaing ka daux kawn pui,
mai yaog maix mawh simiang pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
8 Aux hoik teei siawp sibrawm pa kuang meung khaing doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
mai tawx ka maix,
viang mawh nan,
maix ang awm siliex aux Ta-wue nghiet ka aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh nan,
nghiet ka gum lox pa tawx aux,
mai yuh ngie kaing pa cu aux nawh yuh khaing.
9 Maix kawn lheu ting phi map khaing simiang sivoe an.
Maix tieh vheen ka aux,
mai baox raug tix krub ka riang,
cheeik aux song rhawm song rhi heue.
10 a“ ‘Mawh kheu nin,
aux tom sang ploi krax laux krax leeing lih ka piang nyiex haw maix;
can simex pa koe daux nyiex maix,
mawh pui pa hoik yaox tix luat luan ang ah,
pa ang bax yaox tix luat luang ang ah,
aux tag sang pauh yum pak lawg.
Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka cub nyiex ma maix,
awm hoik pih pui tix tik phon aing yuh nan.
11 Pui daux nyiex ma maix khankix,
pa yum ka daux veng,
sox tag sang ih ka nex kix,
pa yum ka daux tiag khankix,
sim tag sang tuh tix ih ka nex kix.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux krai pao yuh nin heue.’ ”
12 Ya-si-ya ca ah nin ka moeng Yie-law-paw-ang:
“Maix deeh ing ka nyiex heue,
yam hoik laig maix daux veng,
kawn simex maix tag yum.
13 Pui meung Yi-seu-liex khawm uik tag yiiem son nawh,
mai si paung nawh.
Nawh mawh pa yaox pui tix si paung ka daux pui nyiex ma Yie-law-paw-ang tix kaux,
kheu mawh ngie nawh pa muh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux khaing.
14 Simiang Siyiex sang yaog simiang tix kaux pa giex kaing ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex;
simiang in tag sang kieh meung Yie-law-paw-ang laux leeing.
Daux in singaix,
tete te lai,
yam in hoik mawh bawg yam.
15 Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
kieh kix yung yang,
awm gawng priem pa vung vied tix ka daux rawm klawng iak yuh nan.
Nawh tag sang reeig tix tik ka kawn pui Yi-seu-liex khaing daux haktiex pa mhawm pa hoik ap nawh tix tawx ka tax du kix in,
mai kieh kix prung prang tix hoek ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti plak an,
mawh kheu raug kix tix yuh rong riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la,
mai cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm.
16 Simiang Siyiex tag sang tik vud ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
mawh kheu yuh Yie-law-paw-ang ka phi map,
mai kieh nang pui Yi-seu-liex krix ka daux phi map heue.”
17 Moeng Yie-law-paw-ang deeh ing ka Teu-sa,
yam taug laig nawh daux nyiex,
kawn nyawm tag yum.
18 Kawn pui Yi-seu-liex yiiem son kawn nyawm in,
mai si paung nawh,
awm hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka siliex tix cao jhai Ya-si-ya yuh nan heue.
Yie-law-paw-ang yum
19 Kaing yuh simiang Yie-law-paw-ang tang ceu khankix,
grawng pa puing nawh naing mai grawng pa giex nawh kaing hoik tiiem tix sidawng khawm uik ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
20 Yie-law-paw-ang mawh simiang tix nga num ra,
khaix an nawh yum.
Kawn simex nawh Na-ta plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Law-paw-ang
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 11.5–12.15)
21 Yam mawh kawn simex Saw-law-meung pa kaux Law-paw-ang simiang pui Yo-tax,
nawh koe tipon num tix.
Nawh kuang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung kao aliah num;
veng in hoik mawh pa laug Simiang Siyiex lih khaing ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui,
son sang kieh mawh dee krub nawh.
Miex Law-paw-ang mawh pui Ya-meung,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Na-ma heue.
22 Pui Yo-tax phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
kawn lheu cheeik nawh song rhawm khaing pa yuh tax du kix.
23 bKix yuh nyiex krub son siyiex ot ka piang gawng iak mai ka greem veei khaox,
mai baox sum tix krub ka rong simaox mai ka riang bun siyiex Ya-seu-la.
24 cPa nie lang teung caox mawh ka daux nyiex pa krub kix hoik kieh lih koe simex pa coh kaux tix.
Pui Yo-tax yuh kaing pa caox pa caig in khawm uik,
hoik awm pa yuh pui tang ceu tang cad an kix pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex hu yam jauh pui Yi-seu-liex tix laig ka daux haktiex in sivoe an heue.
25 Yam mawh Law-paw-ang simiang ka daux phuan num,
simiang Ai-cix pa kaux See-sha thung pui naing hu puing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
26 dNawh hoik tud rhim can kraung khrai pa ngawh khawm uik ka nyiex buan mai daux nyiex haw,
khawm dieb tie naok khri pa yuh Saw-law-meung khankix.
27 Simiang Law-paw-ang kheu sang naok tieb tie khri in kix,
tom yuh dieb tie naok tawng,
mai tawx ka cao kaing pa pau siviex ting nyiex haw mawh cao pa priex.
28 Yam hoek simiang ka nyiex buan miex bawg ang ah,
cao pau siviex tom giex ka dieb tie naok in kix,
khaix hoik deeh hu simiang mai deeh deuh ot ka daux nyiex pau an.
29 Kaing yuh simiang Law-paw-ang tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng khawm uik ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khankix.
30 Ka daux baux bawg yam in,
Law-paw-ang mai Yie-law-paw-ang rien lih yuh naing ang jud.
31 Yam yum Law-paw-ang,
mai si paung nawh ka daux veng Ta-wue ka daux tiag si meeig pui nyiex simiang.
Miex nawh mawh pui Ya-meung,
ceu kaux mawh Na-ma;
kawn simex nawh Ya-pi-yax plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
