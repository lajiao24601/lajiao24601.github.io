Lox rhax si peem plix siiet
5
aNghiet lox rhax pa rhax aux tix ceu ka son paox pa muh aux,
Pa mawh lox rhax pa muh aux,
mai lox rhax si peem plix siiet nawh pa ah nin:
Ka piang gawng dee mhawm haktiex,
pa muh aux paih ka si peem plix siiet tix dauh.
2 Nawh kaong tiex,
mai teei tix tik lon simaox;
nawh hoik sum ka plix siiet pa mhawm simie.
Nawh hoik sum ka nyiex gawng mu tix lhang mai kieh mawh dee kok jhak;
nawh hoik kaong ka daux dung tix mu pa sang dig biiet ka plix siiet.
Nawh kied simie plix siiet nied teem,
ang koe miex glang tix,
mai plix siiet lih mawh plix pa niex pa krin ku khaox heue.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa muh aux ah nin:
“Pui pa ot ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung mai haktiex Yo-tax eue,
viex pex krai tix yaox si peem plix siiet mai grawng aux heue.
4 Mawh ang mawh aux pa ang bax kia ngien mhawm mhawm lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai lih mawh khaox plix siiet pa niex yuh nin,
mai ang lih tix mawh khaox plix siiet tie pa hoik kied simie aux lie?
5 “Jhak heue,
aux tag sang cox yuh nin ka si peem plix siiet aux:
aux tag sang laoh tix tik ka khaox die pa lai lawm khawm pon daog,
mai yieh ka guh ding pa vie ka si peem in,
kieh to sat hoek tix ih ka plix pa koe ka daux si peem in,
khaix an duk dig ka si peem an.
6 Aux sang kieh haktiex an kuah naog ka rib tiag;
aux ang lai cox pawt ka nu plix siiet an,
mai ang lai kaong tiex kieh bhaw;
aux sang kieh haktiex an kuah naog ka yeem kat.
Aux kawx tag ang kieh pai awm tawx rawm lhiex lih heue.”
7 Yi-seu-liex hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao kru naing si peem plix siiet ka piang khawm uik;
Pui Yo-tax hoik mawh khaox plix siiet pa jawg nawh bre rhawm tix sum.
Nawh kied simie kix yuh kaing pa rauh pa see,
viang mawh nan,
kaing ceu pa pauh kieh nham pui jauh,
mai ngai pui hoik yaox khawm uik.
Nawh kied simie kix yuh kaing pa rauh pa see,
viang mawh nan,
hoik mhong ka lox pa kok yo pui pa ang koe dee lud ku dauh ku dee heue.
Krax caox caig pa yuh kawn pui
8 Pex tag sang yaox krax laux krax leeing heue!
Pex hoik koe kaung ma nyiex ma,
mai pex kawn simie tix pon ka kaung ma nyiex ma tom nie.
Pui tang kaux pie dee paung ot kix ang koe,
pex lai ghrang tix cie ka haktiex pa vieh in tix dauh.
9 Aux mhong lox Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Nyiex pa lhaong mhawm grang in tag sang riem khawm uik,
mai ang koe pui pa paung ot kah.
10 Si peem plix siiet koe kongching ah nie tix chix tix greeik plai kao kongsheng;
simie nghox ca kao khrawng kawn ang bax pon khrawm pie tix khrawng nghox ca khaing heue.”
11 Pex tag sang yaox krax laux krax leeing heue!
Yam kaoh pex khaing it tix pon ngawb cao nyaux ka plai khien,
hoik nyaux tom yoek tix leeing lang sawm.
12 Ka daux poi,
pex nyawm nghiet ka lox ting jung lah,
lox kraung,
lox siao,
mai hoik uik nyaux plai tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
pex kawn ang bax tawng ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
pui tag sang ploi pex hu;
simiang pex tag sang yum kheu cai rhawm tix,
pui hun tag sang yum ka ang yaox tix nyaux rawm.
14 Meung yum tag ang jheeh tix sang bleet ka kix,
tag sang yuh pui ting ceu pa gaux rhawm laux leeing tau mai pui pa yuh krax klieh krax nyiah ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
15 Pui ku kaux tag sang hoek krax kaig krax tua;
pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi khankix tag sang yeen kix tiiem.
16 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing teei ka daux pa khaw caw,
mai jhi grawng pa ting pa lhaong tix;
Siyiex pa singax singeh teei tix prawng ca ka pui cie tix,
mai jhi grawng pa singax singeh tix.
17 Veng pa riem khankix rib tiag hoik kuah naog ka daux,
kawn yung iak sawm rib dee an,
mai kawn pex kawx hoik yaox tix sawm rib riem dee an heue.
18 Pex tag sang laux leeing heue!
Phi map phi ghram hoik prawk nhawt ka pex.
19 Pex ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex nied tix yuh ka ceu pa sang yuh nawh;
ex sang jhak tix yaox ka kaing pa yuh nawh.
Kieh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh yuh ka kaing ceu pa keud nawh tix sang yuh;
ex simie tix sang tawng ka krax keud krax sawn nawh heue.”
20 Pex tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex ang guah tix tawng ka ceu pa mawh maix ceu pa caox,
tieh pa riang mawh pa faik,
mai ang guah pa tie mai pa song heue.
21 Pex tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex kawn een tix kied koe krax lhak lhiao lie!
22 Pex tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex mawh ngie pui pa nyaux plai sawm yie;
krax pa kua pex mai krax pa ting rhawm pex lih koe ngie yam nyaux pex ka plai khaing.
23 Pex hoik kieh pui pa rieb pex mau chun praok khankix to hu mox tix ot dee singai,
mai gut kheem ka sibrawm pui pa ang koe dee lud.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
hoik awm rib kroh pa cap ka daux glah ngu yuh nan,
riah pex tag sang kroh,
lawm tai pex tag sang vhit;
mawh kheu hoik lieh lheu pex ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh,
hoik tieb tie pex ka krax pa sidiex gau Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang song rhawm ka pui cie tix;
nawh tag sang tawx taix tix lauh ka kix.
Gawng lua tag sang yung yang,
to kaux pui yum pum tix ot ka praok krax,
awm ceu pa seeing brai yuh nan.
Hoek bawg yam an,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex kawn ang nyang bax grai,
mai nawh kawn sang baox tawx taix tix lauh ka kix heue.
26 Simiang Siyiex tag sang ploi mhai kok ka meung pa koe dee singai tix meung;b
nawh tag sang phlu vhit,
mai kok kix hoek khaing dee si dauh haktiex;
kix tag sang nied tix phai hoek laung laung.
27 Ka daux kix ang koe pui pa taug mao,
mai ang koe nang pui pa sideeing tix goh;
kix ang gaing mai ang laig it.
Maox rawd nyoi kix mad tix kaux,
maox kiiep kix prawk tix kaux.
28 Te viex kix kied lawm;
ak puh viex kix hoik vaik tix.
Gip caong brung brug kix kawn kiien khaing simaox;
jaong law ling brung naing kix hoik yia tix awm bhaung klia.
29 Pui naing kix kok yo,
awm lox pa graox sivai sang si,
awm kawn sivai sang si pa hoik pon giex tix ih ka to sat,
mai ang koe pui pa pon giex hu heue.
30 Hoek singaix bawg yam an,
kix tag sang graox lox pa song rhawm ka Yi-seu-liex,
awm lox puk plak rawm sigrit sigra.
Pex jhak tix yaox ka meung in heue,
hoik mawh pa faik mai pa laux rhawm ku dauh ku dee!
Pa faik hoik bleet ka pa riang heue.
