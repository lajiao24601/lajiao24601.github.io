Krax krub khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix
50
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm krai lox,
kok yo jauh tom lih singaix hoik tom klip singaix.
2 Jauh ka veng pa kied mhawm grang Shi-ang,
Siyiex tawx pa riang tix jhan heue.
 
3 Siyiex ex tag sang lih hoek,
mai ang doh yien yawg tix.
Ka plak sivoe ngaix nawh ngu pa ting koe kah;
ka sivie siveng nawh bhaung ting koe kah.
4 Nawh kok yo rao ma haktiex mawh cao pa kham sakse,
son sang prawng ka pui cie tix.
5 Nawh ah nin:
khrawm pui pa singax singeh cie aux hoek ka aux tin,
pa mawh pui pa hoik teei aux Krax-tan Khawng-brie yuh sijax mukheu tau.
6 Rao ma khawm uik krai pao ka pa khaw caw Siyiex hu;
to kaux Siyiex mawh cao pa prawng ca heue.
 
7 Siyiex ah nin:
pui cie aux eue,
nghiet,
aux sang krai lox;
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux sang deuh phi map ka maix.
Aux mawh Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix.
8 Aux ang ne raw maix kheu Krax-tan Khawng-brie maix,
mai ang mawh nang kheu pa tan lu maix ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ang jud ang jai.
9 Aux ang lo tix teei ka moi mhaing maix ka daux kawg moi,
ang lo ka kawn pex maix ka daux kawg kawn pex;
10 mawh kheu to sat pa koe ka daux nong khaox ku ceu mawh cie aux;
to tiag to tu pa koe ka gawng khawm uik kawx hoik mawh cie aux.
11 Can sim saik pa koe khawm uik hoik mawh cie aux;
can to tiag to tu pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik kawx hoik mawh cie aux heue.
 
12 Yam cai rhawm aux kawx ang siao lo tix krai ka maix,
mawh kheu haktiex in mai pa koe ka daux khawm uik hoik mawh cie aux.
13 Khex kawn sang ih aux nex moi mhaing lie?
Khex kawn sang nyaux aux nham kawn pex lie?
14 Maix tag caw tix teei krax yaog yaw tan lu ka Siyiexa;
maix tag caw tix yuh krax pa hoik ap pong maix tix tawx ka cao pa ting siawp sibrawm.
15 Yam hoek krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
tag caw tix khiox khaw ka aux;
aux tag sang chix taoh ka maix,
mai maix tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka aux heue.
 
16 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex tieb tie ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig ah nin:
maix koe sibrawm ceu pa tix,
son sang phat ka gum lox ping nyap aux lie?
Mawh maix mawx,
maix lai krai lao ka sijax mukheu aux lie?
17 Maix hoik koeng khoi ka krax pa sidiex gau aux;
hoik tieb tie ka lox tawx aux.
18 Yam si pup maix ka pui briex,
mai maix hoik yuh paox grawm tau mai nawh;
maix hoik yuh paox pie tau mai pui phit pui vao heue.
 
19 Jheeh maix hoik krai ka lox caox ku bawg ku yam;
dak maix hoik pie lhien ku dauh ku dee.
20 Maix nieb pon salao ka pui nyiex pui ma tix,
ot tix krai lox giex lox going ka kix.
21 Maix hoik yuh ka kaing in khawm uik,
mai aux yien yawg tix ang nye,
maix kawn ce aux awm tix yuh nan lie?
Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang ne raw ka maix,
kieh maix yaox sidawng ka kaing in kix heue.
 
22 Pui pa piawm ka aux eue,
tag caw bre rhawm tix nghiet,
phan ang mawh nan,
aux tag sang yuh pex laux leeing,
mai ang koe pui pa pon taoh ka pex.
23 Can pui pa tawx krax yaog yaw mawh khawng tan,
mai hoik mawh pa nab yam yung yi ka aux;
pui pa hawt ka lox tawx aux,
mai aux tag sang taoh ka nawh heue.
