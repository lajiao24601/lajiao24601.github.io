Lai Si-fang-ya
1
aPlak greem mawh singaix bawg yam pa mawh kawn simex Ya-meung pa kaux Yaw-si-yax simiang Yo-tax,
gum lox plak jhi Simiang Siyiex ka Si-fang-ya.
Si-fang-ya mawh kawn simex Ku-see,
kawn saux Ci-ta-lix,
kawn sex Ya-ma-li-ya,
kawn sawk simiang Si-si-cia heue.
Bawg yam singaix pa prawng ca Simiang Siyiex
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang yuh laux lak pak leeing ka to sat pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
3 aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka kawn pui,
ka to sat,
ka sim saik,
mai kax khankix.
Aux tag sang kieh pui pa caox caig laux leeingb;
aux tag sang yuh kawn pui laux leeing khaing haktiex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
4 “Aux tag sang ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka pui haktiex Yo-tax.
Aux tag sang teei tik ka rhoi khaix dee krub pui ka Pa-lix;
ang lai koe pui pa doh keud ka cao giex kaing li an kix pa krub ka Pa-lix.
5 Aux tag sang yuh pui an kix laux leeing pa haok piang braux pa krub ka singaix,
ka khix,
mai ka simuing khankix.
Aux kawx tag sang yuh laux leeing ka pui an kix pa krub ka aux,
pa hoik tiex tu tix cu nghiet ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
mai baox tiex tu tix krub ka ceu kaux siyiex Mi-leu-kong.
6 Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka pui an kix pa deeh plak khaix tix pa ang lai baox hawt ka aux,
mai pui an kix pa ang nghiet ka aux,
pa ang khiox khaw aux duk thung tix heue.”
7 Tag caw tix yien yawg ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
mawh kheu hoik dex sang hoek bawg yam singaix pa prawng ca Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex rien tix sang kieh pui cie tix tawx ju tix,
mai kieh pui koeng tud rhim ka pui Yo-tax.
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ka daux bawg yam singaix pa tan lu an,
aux tag sang ciyang ka simiang,
ka kawn simiang,
mai ka pui pa hawt ka been pui tang ceu khankix khawm uik.
9 Ka daux ngaix an,
aux tag sang ciyang ka pui an kix pa tud rhim,
pa pie lhien gut kheem ka pui pa iak riang iak rao khankix;
kix simie ngie tix tud rhim ka kraung khrai naog ka daux nyiex ma simiang tix khaing heue.c”
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ka daux ngaix an,
pex tag sang mhong lox yiiem pui pa kha rhawm tix ka Siviex Kax veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ka daux veng pa khraox mhong lox yiiem ngia pui daux,
ka blaong gawng mhong lox luah ceu pa ting ghrup.
11 Pui pa paung ot ka greem veng eue,
yiiem ngia heue;
mawh kheu hoik yum pui pa yuh lai gee khawm uik,
pui pa koe mau hoik teei tix tik hu heue.
12 “Yam an,
aux tag sang teei kawk ngu sawg ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Aux tag sang ciyang ka pui an kix pa een tix hoik kub hoik paw,
hoik chix hoik tawng;
kix tang si kah ka to cao tix ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex ang ploi buan son ang ploi nang krax laux leeing.’
13 Pui tag sang tud hu sin ngao kix;
pui hoik yuh laux nyiex ma kix.
Kix ang pon laig ot ka daux nyiex pa hoik sum tix leeing ju,
mai ang pon nyaux ka plai pa kaoh khaing daux si peem plix siiet tix leeing ju heue.”
14 Bawg yam singaix pa ting cie Simiang Siyiex hoik dex sang hoek,
hoik dex sang hoek phaox heue!
An hoik mawh ngaix pa laux leeingd.
Yam an,
viang hoik mawh pui naing pa kied kua kawx tag sang laux rhawm yiiem grai heue!
15 An mawh singaix pa song rhawm,
mawh singaix pa kha rhawm laux leeing,
mawh singaix pa yuh laux,
mawh singaix pa faik meung yum,
mawh singaix pa naog ka krax laux rhawm laux rhi.
16 Ka daux ngaix an tag sang mawh lox luah teet tawng pui naing mai lox ngia pui pa naog ka khawm uik,
mawh singaix pa laig tix puing ka veng pa kaux cong,
mai pa yieh laux ka nyiex lhaong heue.
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang ploi krax laux leeing in hoek ka piang to kaux kawn pui,
mai kieh kix pu pi awm pui dug yuh nan khawm uik.
Kix hoik phit ka aux,
mawh kheu nan nham kix tag sang klup tix hieh awm rawm yuh nan;
to kaux yum kix laux si un ka piang tiex heue.”
18 Ka daux singaix pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
pie mau khri pa koe kix khawm uik kawx ang lai pon taoh ka kix.
Krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex tag sang phai hoek gleeing haktiex laux leeing khawm uik;
nawh tag sang yuh kawn pui ka piang haktiex laux lak pak leeing heue.
