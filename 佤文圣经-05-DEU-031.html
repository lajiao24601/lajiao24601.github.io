Yaog Yaw-su-yax mawh cao pa plud ka Maw-si
31
Maw-si ca baox krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ah nin:
2 a“Yam in aux hoik koe ju tix yieh tix nga num,
mai ang lai pon mawh cao kaing pa duk thung ka pex.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai aux mhong,
aux ang lai pon tiang ka plak toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
3 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag sang ot sivoe ngai pex mai thung pex hu,
mai yuh laux ka pui ku ceu khankix,
son sang kieh pex yaox tix pon cie ka haktiex cie kix.
Hawt awm pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex,
Yaw-su-yax tag sang mawh simiang pex.
4 bSimiang Siyiex hoik yuh ka mawx ka simiang ceu pui Ya-maw-lix,
pa mawh Si-hong mai Eux ra kaux,
hoik yuh ka meung kix laux leeing,
khawm pui pa ot tan ku ceu pui kawx,
tag sang kieh kix laux leeing heue.
5 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pex pex ka kix.
Pex tag caw hawt awm lox pa tawm aux,
mai yuh ka kix.
6 Paw lhat paw graung,
yuh tix kaux cong,
yuh tix kua rhawm,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag ot tau mai pex.
Nawh ang doh piawm ka pex,
tik vud ka pex heue.”
7 Khaix an,
Maw-si kok ka Yaw-su-yax hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh ka plak sivoe ngai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix:
“Yuh tix kaux cong,
yuh tix kua rhawm,
mawh kheu sang duk thung maix ka kawn pui hu pon kuang ka haktiex pa hoik ap Simiang Siyiex tix tawx ka tax du kix.
8 cSimiang Siyiex tag sang hu ka plak sivoe maix,
mai duk thung ka maix,
ot tau mai maix.
Nawh ang doh piawm ka maix,
ang doh tik vud ka maix.
Mawh kheu nan,
maix paw vi laux rhawm mai paw vi lhat graung heue.”
Aliah num tix bawg tag caw tix phat ka lox tat jhawm prawng
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si tiiem ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex,
mai tawx ka cao giex kaing li ceu pui Li-we cao pa khre ka teug sijax mukheu mai kuad song ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
10 dKhaix an,
tom tawx lox ah nin ka kix:
“Yam hoik naog aliah num ka daux num pa nghoik,
pa mawh daux num pa pluat ploi ceuk ka daux Poi Nyiex-gox-bang an,
pex tag sang ah lox pa ting si num,
mai phat ka lox tat jhawm prawng in.
11 Yam hoek bleeh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka dee krub pa hoik ciak ci Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
pex tag caw phat ka lox tat jhawm prawng in ka kix.
12 Pex khrawm ka can simex,
bun,
kawn nyawm mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka si nax pex,
ot tau mai pex,
son sang pon pui tix mhong ku kaux ka lox tat jhawm prawng in,
mai son sang gau tix chix koe krax lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai priex tix hawt yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh.
13 Yuh nin,
kawn praix pex,
pa mawh pa ang nyang sax mhong ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex an,
mai tag koe bawg yam tix mhong kah.
Glang yam kawn im ju kix ka piang haktiex,
tag sang gau tix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
ka daux haktiex pa sang pon kuang tix cie ka plak toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue.”
Pa nghoik Simiang Siyiex tix krai ka Maw-si
14 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Ang lai rheeng maix sang hu khaing kawn pui haktiex.
Maix kok Yaw-su-yax hoek ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
aux sang tawx beeh glawm ka nawh.”
Maw-si mai Yaw-su-yax hoek ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
15 Koe rong pai awm hoek kaog ot ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
Simiang Siyiex tag jhi kix yaox tix ka daux rong pai awm heue.
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix hoik dex sang hu khaing kawn pui haktiex heue.
Khaix hoik yum maix,
kawn pui khankix tag sang yuh pa ang khaw ka aux,
mai lieh lheu ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai kix,
tik vud ka aux,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
ka daux haktiex pa sang laig pex ka dee an.
17 Yam an,
krax song rhawm aux tag sang lih ka piang kix,
mai tik vud ka kix,
mai ang simi ka kix;
kix tag hoek krax laux krax leeing.
Krax pa laux pa leeing tag sang hoek piang to kaux kix,
mai kix tag tawng,
in mawh kheu ang aux,
pa mawh Siyiex kix lai ot tau mai kix.
18 Yam an,
aux ang teem coi ka kix;
mawh kheu hoik yuh kix kaing ceu pa caox pa caig,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux khankix heue.
19 “Yam in,
maix tiiem tix deuh ka lai rhax in tix ceu,
mai gau kawn praix pui Yi-seu-liex khankix kah,
kieh kix pon rhax glip ngai,
son sang mawh lai rhax in mhai sakse pa sidiex ne raw aux ka kix.
20 Aux tag sang hawt awm lox pa ap aux tix deuh ka tax du kix,
mai viex kix hoek ka daux haktiex pa mhawm,
pa mawh dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia.
Ka dee an,
mai kix tag sang kub ih kub nyaux,
mai pon tix yaox ka singaix pa nyawm ot nyawm koe.
Viang mawh nan,
kix tag deeh tik vud ka aux,
mai hu yuh kaing krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
mai tieb tie ka aux,
lieh lheu ka sijax mukheu aux heue.
21 Krax laux krax leeing tag sang hoek ka piang to kaux kix.
Viang mawh nan,
lai rhax in tag ca kawn toe tix hu,
son sang kieh mawh pa jhi mhai sakse pa ne raw ka kix.
Viang aux ang nyang bax viex kix hoek ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap aux tix tawx ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng ka daux rhawm rhi krax keud krax sawn kix heue.”
22 Ka daux singaix an,
Maw-si hoik tiiem ka lai rhax in,
mai gau pui Yi-seu-liex khankix kah.
23 eKhaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng ah nin:
“Maix caw tix kaux cong,
caw tix koe krax yum rhawm,
mawh kheu maix tag sang viex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix laig ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap aux tix tawx ka kix.
Aux tag ot tau mai maix heue.”
24 Maw-si tiiem ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex ot ka daux phuk lai,
nawh bre rhawm tix tiiem lih khawm uik.
25 Khaix hoik tiiem kah,
nawh tawx lox ka cao giex kaing li ceu pui Li-we,
cao pa kia ngien ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
26 “Pex teei ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex in,
mai deuh ot ka praok teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
deuh ot tan mai kieh mawh mhai sakse pa sidiex ne raw nawh ka pui cie tix.
27 Aux hoik tawng mawh kix pa kiien rhawm,
mai pa ang khaw.
Yam kawn im ju aux,
kix hoik tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex ang jud ang jai,
khaix hoik yum aux,
mai kix tee nie baox tix yuh pa ang khaw miex ding.
28 Pex tag caw tix khrawm kuad song ku ceu pui mai cao kaing khankix hoek ka sivoe aux,
mai aux tag sang krai kaing in kix ka kix.
Aux tag sang ne raw ka kix,
mai kok yo rao ma haktiex lih mawh mhai sakse.
29 Aux hoik tawng,
khaix hoik yum aux,
kawn pui hoik kied hu ka daux kaing pa ang mhawm,
mai tieb tie ka krax pa sidiex gau aux ka kix.
Singaix pa sang hoek,
kix tag sang si pup ka krax laux krax leeing,
kheu jao yuh kix kaing pa ang Simiang Siyiex cu kix yuh,
cheeik nawh song rhawm heue.”
Lox rhax Maw-si
30 Khaix an,
Maw-si ot ka sivoe pui haoh pui hun mai rhax ka lai rhax an heue.
