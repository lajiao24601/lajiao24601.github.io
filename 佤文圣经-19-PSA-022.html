Pa khiox khaw daux krax kha rhawm mai lox rhax pa yaog yaw
22
aSiyiex aux,
Siyiex aux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tik vud ka aux?
Aux yiiem son khiox khaw krax teem krax coi,
yuh ka mawx maix ang hoek teem coi ka aux?
2 Siyiex aux eue,
yam pon ngaix aux kok yo ka maix,
mai maix ang pawk ka lox aux;
yam pon sawm aux yiiem tix khiox khaw ka maix,
mai ang yaox tix laux lee tix nang.
3 Viang mawh nan,
pui hoik yaog maix mawh cao pa singax singeh;
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik teei maix mawh cao pa yaog yaw kix heue.
4 Tax du yix hoik yum rhawm ka maix;
kix hoik yum rhawm ka maix,
mai maix tom taoh ka kix.
5 Kix yiiem mai khiox khaw ka maix,
tom pon yaox tix luat luan khaing krax kha rhawm;
kix hoik yum rhawm ka maix,
mai ang yaox tix laux leeing heue.
 
6 Kheu nan,
aux ang lai deeh baox awm pui;
aux awm viag vua tix mu,
mai pui nu nai nyiah aux.
7 bPui pa yaox ka aux hoik nyiah aux khawm uik,
mai vuk vied kaing blih dak tix ka aux ah nin:
8 cMaix een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
yuh ka mawx nawh lai ang hoek taoh ka maix?
Phan Simiang Siyiex muh ka maix,
yuh ka mawx ang hoek taoh ka maix?
 
9 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik kieh aux lih mhawm khaing daux vaig miex tix;
yam mawh aux kawn ngie,
maix hoik kia ngien ka aux mhawm.
10 Yam jauh aux tix keh khaing daux vaig,
mai aux hoik deuh rhawm tix ka maix;
yam hoik lih aux khaing daux vaig miex tix,
maix hoik mawh Siyiex aux.
11 Khiox khaw maix paw vi singai hu khaing aux!
Yam hoik hoek krax laux krax leeing,
aux ang koe dee sang khiox khaw heue.
 
12 Pui koeng tom nie hoik awm moi mhaing lai lawm ka aux;
kix awm moi mhaing Pa-sang pa kua mai lai lawm ka aux.
13 Kix graox awm sivai sang si,
ang jheeh tix sang bleet ka aux heue.
 
14 Riang rao aux hoik grai,
awm rawm pa hieh piang tiex.
Si-ang aux hoik khluk khlaik khaing jhong;
rhawm rhi aux awm khlawp ngu tien pa ceed khawm tawm nhao.
15 Rawng ghok auxd hoik kroh awm phawng tiex yuh nan,
dak aux hoik kawb ka si-ang kap aux.
Maix lai kieh aux krox jun tix yum daux phawng tiex heue.
 
16 Pui caox pa caig khrawm paox tix lai lawm ka aux,
awm cub phung sox caox pa tuk tiak ka aux;
Kix riah ka taix caong auxe,
17 si-ang aux hoik chix simin ka khawm uik;
Pui koeng hoik kok simi tix nyiah ka aux.
18 fKix guah ka sibex dha aux,
mai baox cawk lai son sibex dawt aux heue.
 
19 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi singai hu khaing aux liak!
Cao taoh aux eue,
nied hoek taoh ka aux liak!
20 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing vaig gawn;
khiox khaw maix taoh ju aux luat luan khaing sox caox.
21 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux khaing daux jheeh sivai sang si;
aux ot ka plak sivoe ngai moi tiag mai ang koe cao teem heueg!
 
22 hAux tag sang krai pao kaing yuh maix ka paox ceu paox cad tix;
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw maix ka daux pa khrawm kix paox tix ah nin:
23 pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex eue,
pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh!
Doeh ceu cad Ya-keux eue,
tag caw tix yaog min plak ngai nawh!
Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka nawh!
24 Nawh ang map nai ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai ang piawm ka krax pa kha kix rhawm tix;
Lao nawh sang tik vud ka kix,
mai tieh tix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw kix,
25 Aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw kaing yuh maix ka sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai tan lu ka pa hoik ap pong aux tix deuh ka plak sivoe ngai pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix khankix.
26 Pui hot pui sibhawm tag yaox tix sawm sak;
pui pa sawg ka Simiang Siyiex hoik rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
Kieh rhawm rhi kix gaux rhawm gaux rhi leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
27 Kieh meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik doh keud ka Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
mai nghiet ka nawh;
kieh kawn pui ku ceu ka piang haktiex krub ka nawh khawm uik!
28 Simiang Siyiex mawh simiang;
nawh kuang ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
 
29 Pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi ka piang haktiex,
tag sang krub ka plak sivoe ngai nawh khawm uiki;
pui pa hoik yum kawx tag sang krub ka nawh khawm uik.
30 Kawn pui tix ju hoek tix ju tag sang yuh kaing jhaw nawh.
Pui ku kaux tag sang krai lao grawng Simiang Siyiex ka doeh ceu cad tix.
31 Pui pa ang bax keh tag sang mhong krai pui ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh ka pui cie tix khankix heue.
