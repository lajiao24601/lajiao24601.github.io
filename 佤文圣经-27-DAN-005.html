Poi pa yuh son Paw-sa-sha
5
Koe ka daux tix bo,
simiang Paw-sa-sha ghaok kawn cau pa ting ot greem tix tix rheng kaux hoek tix ih poi ting pa yuh nawh,
hoek tix nyaux plai yuh krax gaux rhawm.
2 Yam kawn yuh kix krax gaux rhawm,
Paw-sa-sha tawx lox nhie kix teei kawk khri,
mai klong mau pa tud keeing nawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha khaing nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung lih,
son sang teei tix mai kawn cau pa ting tix,
mai moeng iak tix,
mai moeng iak pa muh tix khankix nyaux plai kah.
3 Pui cau nawh teei kawk khri mai klong mau an kix lih dum mawx dum an,
mai kieh kix nyaux plai kah.
4 Kix nyaux nang,
yaog yaw nang ka siyiex pa teei khri,
mau,
tawng,
rhiem,
simaox yuh lih mawh ka heue.
5 Dum mawx dum an,
koe taix pui tix khawn lih hoek,
mai teei giien taix tix tiiem ngai lai ka ding nyiex haw simiang pa hoik kraix paing ka dee blawn riang ngu dee an.
Simiang yaox taix an pa tiiem ka ngai lai,
6 pak ngai nawh tieh loh tix paing dum mawx dum an,
mai yaoh yee si yaung caong khawm ra khawn.
7 Nawh kok yo tom ting si num,
mai tawx lox nhie cao chix si meed,
pa sawn singaix hoek.
Yam hoek kix,
simiang ah nin ka kix:
“Pui mawx chix phat ka ngai lai pa koe ka ding,
chix kah ka dee bleeh,
aux tag sang tawx nawh ceep ka kraung dha mhawm pa ceep simiang,
tawx nawh tauk maox soi khri,
mai kieh nawh ngawm glawk na bat loe ka greem simiang meung heue.”
8 Pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi ka daux nyiex haw simiang hoek dex sivoe ngai;
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa chix phat ngai lai an kix,
ang koe pui pa pon kah ka dee bleeh.
9 Simiang Paw-sa-sha kied sidaing yaoh yee,
pak ngai nawh tieh loh tix paing;
kawn cau pa ting ang tawng ka sang yuh tix heue.
10 Moeng simiang mhong lox simiang mai kawn cau pa ting simiang,
nawh tom laig daux nyiex pa yuh poi,
mai ah nin ka simiang:
“Vawng lang ju lang phan heue!
Khiox khaw maix paw vi pru praoh yaoh yee.
11 Ka daux meung maix koe pui tix kaux,
nawh koe khuan siyiexa pa kied singax pa ot tau mai nawh.
Yam kawn mawh keeing maix simiang,
pui in hoik sax mawh pui pa lhak,
mai pa koe ping nyia cuyi awm siyiex yuh nan.
Keeing maix simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoik tawx nawh mawh cao kaing ka piang can pui pa chix si pawk,
cao chix si meed yuh sat,
mai pa sawn singaix khankix.
12 Nawh koe riang rao pa ang pui koe,
koe ping nyia cuyi mai siawp sibrawm pa tang pa siyox,
pa chix kah ka ca maox,
ka lox si nieb,
ka kaing ceu pa raux pa mox.
Mawh kheu nan,
ploi pui hu kok nawh hoek.
Ceu kaux nawh kaux Tang-yi-li;
simiang baox sum ceu kaux nawh kaux Paw-thi-sa-sha.
Nawh chix pon kah dee bleeh ngai lai in kix son maix heue.”
Tang-yi-li kah ka ngai lai pa ot ka ding
13 Kix viex Tang-yi-li hoek ka plak sivoe ngai simiang dum mawx dum an.
Simiang chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix mawh Tang-yi-li pui Yo-thaix pa giex hoek khaing meung Yo-tax lie?
14 Aux mhong krai pui koe khuan siyiex pa singax singeh mai maix;
maix tom taug koe krax chix,
koe ngai lig ngai lai,
mai koe ping nyia cuyi.
15 Aux kok pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi in kix mai cao chix si meed hoek,
nhie kix phat ka ngai lai pa koe ka ding,
nhie kix kah dee bleeh ngai lai son tix;
viang mawh nan,
kix ang chix phat chix kah.
16 Aux mhong krai pui chix kah maix ka ceu pa raux pa mox,
chix krai ka dee bleeh pa nu tix tawng.
Phan maix pon phat ka ngai lai in kix,
mai pon kah dee bleeh son aux,
maix tag sang pon khawng phak lak sawng,
mai pon ceep ka kraung mhawm pa dha simiang,
pon ka soi khri pa koe min plak ngai,
mai yaox tix ngawm ka glawk na bat loe ka greem simiang meung heue.”
17 Tang-yi-li pawk lox ah nin:
“Khiox khaw Vawng deuh ka khawng phak lak sawng,
phan ang mawh nan tawx ka pui tang kaux.
Aux sang phat ngai lai in kix son Vawng,
tag sang kah dee bleeh ngai lai in kix heue.
18 “Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong kieh keeing maix Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha lih mawh simiang pa ting tix kaux,
tawx nawh koe min plak ngai mai siawp sibrawm;
19 mawh kheu nan,
meung ku meung,
pui ku ceu,
mai pui pa si kah ka lox ku ceu hoik lhat graung ka nawh khawm uik.
Nawh koe sibrawm tix kieh pui im kieh pui yum,
mai chix tix yaog pui lhaong,
phan ang mawh nan yuh pui tiiem awm pa simie nawh tix yuh yuh nan.
20 Viang mawh nan,
yam ting rhawm lhaong rhi mai tang teei to kaux tix ting lhaong,
pui teei ka simiang pa mawh nawh,
mai nawh ang lai koe min plak ngai.
21 Pui grheu nawh lih khaing daux kawn pui,
nawh ang baox koe rhawm pa chix keud chix sawn awm kawn pui,
mai awm ngie to sat.
Nawh ot tau mai la tiag,
sawm rib awm moi yuh nan,
mai it ka daux tiag,
kham pa lan rawm ciex.
Dee si dauh,
nawh deeh doh keud ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
pa kuang ka piang meung kawn pui,
pa koe siawp sibrawm tix tawx meung ka pui pa hoik laug tix khankix heue.
22 “Simiang Paw-sa-sha eue,
maix mawh kawn simex Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
viang maix hoik tawng ka in kix khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
maix ang tawng tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
23 maix tieh tix ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai tieb tie ka cao Siyiex rao ma.
Maix teei lih ka kawk ka klong pa tud nawh khaing nyiex buan,
mai teei to kaux tix mai kawn cau pa ting,
moeng iak tix,
moeng iak pa muh tix nyaux plai daux,
khaix an rhax lai yaog yaw ka siyiex pa teei pui khri,
mau,
tawng,
rhiem,
khaox,
simaox khankix yuh kah,
pa mawh riang pa ang chix yaox,
pa ang chix mhong,
pa ang chix tawng tid tix khankix.
Maix ang bax krub ka Siyiex pa pon tat ka krax yum mai krax im,
mai pa pon tawng ka kaing ceu pa yuh maix.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex tom tawx taix tix tix khawn hoek tiiem ka ngai lai in kix heue.
25 “Ngai lai in kix mawh:
‘Simin tix yaox,
simin tix yaox,
ciang tix yaox,
guah tang hu.’
26 ‘Simin tix yaox’,
dee bleeh mawh:
Siyiex hoik simin ka singaix meung maix,
mai kieh meung uik.
27 ‘Ciang tix yaox’,
dee bleeh mawh:
hoik sau maix ka piang ciang,
mai ciang ka ang maix pon kub.
28 ‘Guah tang hu’,
dee bleeh mawh:
meung maix tag sang guah tix tang hu,
mawh cie pui Mi-ti-yax mai pui Paw-see.b”
29 cMawh kheu nan,
Paw-sa-sha tawx lox nhie pui cau tix tawx Tang-yi-li ceep kraung dha mhawm pa ceep simiang dum mawx dum an,
tawx nawh tauk maox khri pa koe min plak ngai,
mai baox krai pao tawx nawh ngawm glawk na bat loe ka greem simiang meung.
30 Pui briex pauh ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Paw-sa-sha yum.
31 Pui Mi-ti-yax pa koe ju tix gliah ra num pa kaux Ta-lio-see tud tix mawh simiang heue.
