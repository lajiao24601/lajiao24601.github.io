Simiang pa khaw caw
32
Koe tix singaix,
tag sang lih koe Vawng pa giex kaing ka daux krax pa rauh pa see,
mai simiang pa kuang meung ka daux krax pa khaw caw.
2 Kix ku kaux tag awm diang praok rawm sigrit sigra dee mox tix ka bhaung,
mai daux dawh dee mox pui tix ka lhiex,
awm rawm keet ka daux pak mhaik,
awm veei simaox ting pa ot pui ka daux tiag.
3 Dawng yhaok kix mai ngai baoh kix tag sang yah tix son kawn pui.
4 Kix ang lai baox deeh seeing brai ka kawn pui,
viang mawh nan tag sang viex krax pa caox rhawm tix yuh,
ah lox ang lai baox deeh kieh kle heue.
5 Ang lai baox deeh koe pui pa teei krax pa daox aw kieh mawh pa ting lhaong,
mai ang lai koe pui pa kok pui pa ang mhawm rhawm mawh pui pa rauh pui see.
6 Pui pa daox aw krai lox ang koe dee bleeh,
mai kix ca keud tix yuh ka kaing ceu pa caox pa caig;
lox kraix kix ku gum mai kaing yuh kix ku ceu hoik mawh pa map nai ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
Nawh ang sax tawx pui pa cai rhawm sawm tix bawg kawx,
ang tawx pui pa simie tix nyaux rawm nyaux ka rawm tix bawg kawx.
7 Pui pa lhak khoik yuh kaing ceu pa caox jheeng jheeng;
nawh yuh krax pa pie lhien pa gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm khankix,
mai teei krax pie krax lhien kieh pui hot pui sibhawm ang pon siawp sibrawm pa khaw kix tix pon.
8 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa koe min yuh kaing ceu pa koe min;
nawh joh jhan ka kaing ceu pa koe min tom si dauh sidu heue.
Krax pa prawng ca mai krax pa haok huan
9 Bun miex pa ang koe krax khi krax glaoh,
pa yaox krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe khankix eue,
tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet ka lox aux.
10 Yam in pex kawx tee nyawm ot nyawm koe,
viang mawh nan,
bawg yam in ka daux num hoek sax,
pex tag sang khi rhawm khi rhi,
mawh kheu ang pex lai yaox tix khrawm ka plix siiet.
11 Pex hoik yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai ang lai koe krax pa khi pa glaoh sivoe an;
yam in tag sang pru praoh yaoh yee heue.
Pex tag sang peeik ka kraung ceep tix,
mai teei man caox mad ka nyoi tix.
12 Pex tag sang dieh naog tix yiiem yo,
mawh kheu hoik laux leeing kaung ma pa mhawm pex mai si peem plix pex heue.
13 Ka daux haktiex pui cie aux,
tag sang lih kuah ka pang kat mai yeem kat daux.
Pex tag caw tix yiiem son nyiex ma pa gaux rhawm mai veng pa haok huan sivoe an.
14 Nyiex haw simiang tag sang lih mawh haktiex pa riem,
veng pa haoh hun pui daux tag ang lai koe pui pie tix kaux,
nyiex yaong mai daux tab lih mawh mheen dee ot to tiag leeing ju.
La tiag tag sang klieh tix ot ka dee an,
phung yung tag sang sawm rib dee an heue.
15 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex tag sang baox ploi khuan tix hoek ka daux yix.
Haktiex pa riem tag sang lih mawh ma kaung pa mhawm;
ma kaung pa mhawm tag sang lih mawh nong khaox.
16 Ku dauh ku dee hoik lih koe ceu pa rauh pa see,
mai ceu pa khaw caw heue.
17 Mawh kheu hoik yuh kawn pui kaing pa rauh pa see ku kaux;
kix tag sang pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
18 Nyiex ma pui cie Siyiex hoik pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai ang lai koe krax khi krax glaoh. (
19 Viang mawh nan,
mu pre tag sang lih ka daux nong khaox,
mai veng hoik laux leeing.)
20 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa hoik sum kaung ma ot ka praok rawm,
mai pa kieh moi mai la yaox tix nyawm sawm rib ka pak rib kied sidaing ting buan son heue!
