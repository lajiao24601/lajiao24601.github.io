Ye-sux khoek ka caong pui gau tix
13
Sivoe Poi Luat-luan,
Ye-sux hoik tawng bawg yam sang hu tix khaing haktiex in,
mai deeh ing ka keeing tix dee an.
Nawh hoik muh ka pui cie tix piang haktiex,
nawh hoik muh kix tom si dauh sidu heue.
2 Yam sawm Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh priex bo,
Phi-caox laig daux rhawm Yo-tax kawn simex Si-meung pui Cia-liiex,
mai kieh nawh but rhawm tix coh ka Ye-sux.
3 Ye-sux hoik tawng keeing hoik ap tix tawx akhuang ka nawh khawm uik;
nawh hoik tawng to kaux tix hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an,
tag sang deeh ing ka Siyiex dee an.
4 Nawh yaog tix kaoh khaing dee ngawm tix,
mai peeik ka kraung dha tix,
teei man khoek mad nyoi tix kah,
5 khaix an hieh rawm daux pan,
mai jauh tix khoek caong pui gau tix,
mai baox teei man khoek awt kroh singax.
6 Yam hoek nawh ka sivoe ngai Si-meung Pi-teux,
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
maix khoek caong aux lie?”
7 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Can pa yuh aux,
yam in maix ang tawng,
khaix an hu sivoe ngai maix taug sang tawng sidawng heue.”
8 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Aux ang doh kieh maix khoek caong tix heue!”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan aux ang khoek caong maix,
maix taug ang dex dawt tau mai aux heue.”
9 Si-meung Pi-teux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
phan mawh nan,
ang joeng mawh caong aux,
pie khawm taix aux mai kaing aux kawx khoek ka heue!”
10 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui pa hoik khoek heem tix hoik singax khawm to kaux tix khawm uik,
mai lo ngie tix khoek caong tix khainga.
Pex hoik singax,
viang mawh nan,
ang mawh pui ku kaux pa hoik singax.” (
11 Ye-sux hoik tawng mawh pui mawx pa coh ka nawh,
mawh kheu nan ah nin “Ang mawh pui ku kaux pa hoik singax heue.”)
12 bYe-sux hoik khoek ka caong kix,
mai ceep kraung dha,
khaix an deeh hu ngawm ka dee ngawm tix.
Nawh chawk pui gau tix khankix ah nin:
“Pa yuh aux son pex yam in,
hoik tawng pex ka lie?
13 Pex hoik nab yam yung yi aux mawh sara,
mawh simiang,
in hoik mawh tete,
mawh kheu hoik dawg mawh aux simiang.
14 Aux mawh simiang pex,
sara pex,
mai kawn baox khoek ka caong pex,
pex kawx khaw tix ra lai khoek ka caong paox tix.
15 Aux hoik yuh pex yaox kaing ceu pa mhawm,
hoik mawh son sang kieh pex hawt tix yuh awm hoik yuh aux ka pex yuh nan.
16 cAux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
siliex cawng ang lheu ting khaing simiang tix,
pui pa ploi hoek kawx ang lheu ting khaing kaw pa ploi nawh hoek.
17 Phan pex hoik tawng sidawng ka kaing in,
mai pon hawt tix yuh hoik kied mawh pa ting buan son heue!
18 d“Gum lox pa krai aux in ang mawh krai son pex khawm uik;
aux hoik tawng ka pui pa laug tix.
Mawh ngie hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin ‘Pui pa sawm tau mai aux deeh teei caong tix tiied ka aux’ Gum lox in tag sang lih tix mawh.
19 Aux sang krai pex mhong sivoe sang lih kaing in tix mawh,
son sang kieh pex yum rhawm ka hoik mawh aux ‘kaw pa tang koe leeing ju leeing phan’ an.
20 eAux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
can pui pa hoik rieb ka pui pa ploi aux hoek,
hoik mawh pa rieb ka aux,
can pui pa hoik rieb ka aux,
hoik mawh pa rieb ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an heue.”
Ye-sux grox krai grawng sang coh pui ka tix
(Ma-thaix 26.20–25; Lai Ma-kheu 14.17–21; Lai Lu-cia 22.21–23)
21 Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux ka lox in,
nawh kied laux rhawm tete,
mai nawh tom krai pao ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
ka daux pex koe pui tix kaux tag sang coh ka aux heue.”
22 Pui gau ra lai jhak pak ngai paox tix,
ang tawng mawh ah nawh ka pui mawx.
23 Ka daux pui gau tix kaux pa blawn muh Ye-sux,
nawh ngawm ka praok Ye-sux.
24 Si-meung Pi-teux khiep ngai tix ka nawh ah nin:
“Chawk ka nawh mawh ah nawh ka pui mawx heue.”
25 Mawh kheu nan,
pui gau an greed tix dex ka Ye-sux,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
mawh pui mawx?”
26 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Phan aux cux tix tawx khao mun ka pui mawx,
tag mawh pui an heue.”
Khaix hoik krai nawh lox in,
nawh teei khao mun cux kah,
mai tawx ka Yo-tax kawn simex Si-meung pui Cia-liiex.
27 Yo-tax rieb ka khao mun,
Sa tan tom laig daux nawh.
Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Pa sang yuh maix nied maix hu yuh heue!”
28 Pui pa ngawm dee an ang tawng dee bleeh lox pa krai Ye-sux ka nawh khawm uik.
29 Mawh kheu Yo-tax mawh pui cao kuang mau,
koe pui gau tix blah ce Ye-sux tawx lox nhie nawh hu liag ceu pa sang lo kix daux poi,
phan ang mawh nan,
nhie nawh viex ceu pa ih hu tawx ka pui hot pui sibhawm heue.
30 Yo-tax ih ka khao mun an,
dum mawx dum an lih hu.
Bawg yam an,
hoik mawh bawg yam pon sawm heue.
Gum lox tawx pa khraox
31 Khaix hoik lih hu Yo-tax,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yam in kawn kawn pui hoik pon ka min plak ngai;
min plak ngai Siyiex kawx hoik jhi tix lih ka to kaux kawn kawn pui nang.
32 Kheu hoik jhi min plak ngai Siyiex tix lih ka daux kawn kawn pui,
to kaux nawh kawx tag sang jhi lih min plak ngai kawn kawn pui,
kheu nan tag sang yaog min plak ngai nawh lih dum mawx dum an.
33 fKawn praix khankix,
bawg yam ot aux tau mai pex ang lai nie.
Pex tag sang sawg ka aux;
viang mawh nan,
yam in aux tag sang krai ka pex,
awm hoik krai aux ka simiang pui Yo-thaix ah nin:
‘Dee sang hu aux,
pex ang pon hu heue.’
34 gAux tawx gum lox tawx pa khraox ka pex tix grawng:
tag caw tix ra lai muh ka paox tix.
Aux hoik muh ka pex yuh ka mawx,
pex kawx tag caw tix ra lai muh ka paox tix yuh nan.
35 Phan pex ra lai muh ka paox tix,
kawn pui piang haktiex tom taug tawng grawng hoik mawh pex pui gau aux heue.”
Ye-sux grox krai ang Pi-teux tawng yung tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.31–35; Lai Ma-kheu 14.27–31; Lai Lu-cia 22.31–34)
36 Si-meung Pi-teux chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
hu maix ka dee mawx lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Ka dee hu aux,
yam in maix ang pon hu tau mai aux,
viang mawh nan,
khaix an sax maix tag sang hu tau mai aux heue.”
37 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
yuh ka mawx yam in aux ang pon hu tau mai maix lie?
Aux cu tix tawx ju tix son maix heue!”
38 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix cu tix tawx ju tix son aux lie?
Aux krai ka maix tete te lai,
sivoe sang ux ia,
maix tag sang thiang tix ang tawng ka aux loe bawg heue.”
