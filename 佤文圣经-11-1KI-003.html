Saw-law-meung khiox khaw ping nyia cuyi
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 1.3–12)
3
Saw-law-meung yuh paox grawm tau mai simiang Ai-cix,
mai teei tix moeng kawn bun simiang Ai-cix.
Sivoe sang sum nawh nyiex haw simiang,
nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
mai guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
nawh hoik dawg kieh kawn bun simiang Ai-cix paung ot ka daux veng Ta-wue.
2 Sivoe sang sum ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
kawn pui tan lu krax tan ka piang khu pa ot piang gawng iak.
3 Saw-law-meung muh ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex keeing tix Ta-wue,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ca kawn yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka piang khu pa ot piang gawng iak mai gleeing nyawng bhawng ku dauh ku dee.
4 Koe tix bawg,
Saw-law-meung hu yuh krax tan ka Ci-piien,
mawh kheu dee an hoik koe khu pa ot piang gawng iak pa kied ao min.
Nawh hoik sax yuh Krax tan Pa-gleeing dee an kaing rheng bawg.
5 Ka daux bo an,
Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka nawh ka daux ca maox,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Krai ka aux,
maix sang kieh aux tawx ceu pa tix ka maix?”
6 Saw-law-meung pawk ah nin:
“Maix hoik rien tawx krax muh tix ka siliex maix,
pa mawh keeing aux Ta-wue,
mawh kheu yum rhawm,
rauh see,
mai koe rhawm pa mhawm ka maix.
Maix hoik tawx krax muh pa ting,
pa ang chix tieh loh tix ang jud ka nawh,
mai tawx nawh koe kawn simex tix kaux,
yam in hoik ngawm piang dang ngeung dee ngawm nawh.
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
viang aux awn ju,
mai ang tawng grawng sang priex tix meung mhawm yuh ka mawx,
maix kawn kieh aux plud tix mawh simiang khaix keeing aux.
8 Aux hoik mawh simiang ka daux pui pa laug maix mawh cie tix;
kix hun tom ang lai pon simin.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
khiox khaw maix tawx aux koe rhawm pa chix guah ceu pa mhawm,
mai pon prawng kaing pa khaw mai pa ang khaw khankix,
son sang chix priex kia aux ka kawn pui cie maix.
Phan ang mawh nan,
aux doh sang pon kuang ka kawn pui cie maix in yuh ka mawx lie?”
10 Simiang Siyiex khaw rhawm ka lox pa khiox khaw Saw-law-meung,
11 tom ah nin ka nawh:
“Mawh kheu ang maix bax khiox khaw son to kaux tix lang ju,
mag mi,
phan ang mawh nan,
khiox khaw yuh laux pui pa koeng,
mai hoik khiox khaw ping nyia cuyi pa chix guah pa mhawm,
son sang chix priex kia kawn pui ka daux krax pa khaw caw,
12 aux tag sang tawx awm lox pa khiox khaw maix ka maix.
Aux tag sang tawx maix koe krax lhak lhiao,
mai rhawm pa chix guah pa mhawm khankix,
pa ang pui sivoe an sax koe,
pui singaix khaix kawx ang pon koe.
13 Pa ang maix bax khiox khaw khankix,
aux kawx tag sang tawx ka maix;
ka daux ju maix hoik pon krax pa haok huan mag mi,
tag sang nie khaing simiang ku kaux.
14 Phan maix nghiet ka aux,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux mai lox tawm jhawm krai aux,
awm keeing tix Ta-wue yuh nan,
aux tag sang tawx maix lang ju lang phan heue.”
15 Saw-law-meung haing,
mai tawng mawh Siyiex pa si kah tau mai nawh ka daux ca maox.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh deeh ing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai cung ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
khaix an khan kheu priex preem,
son can siliex khawm uik heue.
Saw-law-meung prawng ka see pa nu tix prawng
16 Koe tix singaix,
koe sibun coh kaux ra kaux hoek ka simiang Saw-law-meung,
mai cung ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
17 Tix kaux ah nin:
“Vawng,
bun in rom tix paung tau mai aux ka daux nyiex tix lhang;
yam keh aux ka daux nyiex,
nawh ot ka dee an.
18 Aux keh kawn nyawm simex tix kaux;
khaix an ra singaix nawh kawx keh kawn nyawm simex tix kaux.
Ka daux nyiex in mawh ngie yiex ra kaux khaing,
mai ang koe pui tang kaux.
19 Koe ka daux tix bo,
nawh loi tix gut kawn tix yum.
20 Nawh tom kaoh hoek greung sawm,
jawm yam liak it aux,
mai teei kawn simex aux hu,
mai deuh ot ka daux deem tix,
khaix an nawh teei tix deuh kawn nyawm pa yum an ot ka daux deem aux.
21 Ka daux ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
aux haing tix mai sang tawx kawn nyawm peux,
aux jhak nawh hoik yum;
mai aux bre tix cox jhak,
mai hoik tawng an ang mawh kawn aux heue.”
22 Bun an tix kaux ah nin:
“Ang!
Kawn nyawm pa im mawh cie aux,
pa yum hoik mawh cie maix heue!”
Bun sivoe an pawk ah nin:
“Ang!
Kawn nyawm pa yum mawh cie maix,
pa im mawh cie aux heue!”
Kiex tom simah paox tix ka plak sivoe ngai simiang heue.
23 Saw-law-meung keud daux rhawm tix ah nin:
“Kiex khawm ra kaux krai kawn nyawm pa im mawh cie tix,
mai pa yum ang mawh cie tix.”
24 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin:
“Tawx vaig ka aux tix mag!”
Pui pa ot plak goix plak dawm nawh viex vaig hoek,
25 simiang tom tawx lox ah nin:
“Lug kawn nyawm pa im in lih koe ra plak,
tix plak tawx ka bun in,
tix plak tawx ka bun an heue.”
26 Pa mawh miex kawn nyawm pa im an,
kheu caox rhawm nawh ka kawn simex tix,
tom ah nin ka simiang:
“Vawng,
paw vi pauh ka kawn nyawm in,
tawx nawh ka bun an khaing heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
bun an tix kaux ah nin:
“Ang siao tawx ka aux,
ka nawh kawx paw vi tawx;
lug kawn nyawm in lih koe ra plak heue!”
27 Simiang Saw-law-meung ah nin:
“Paw vi pauh ka kawn nyawm in!
Tawx nawh ka bun sivoe an,
mawh kheu nawh tag mawh miex kawn nyawm an tete heue.”
28 Pui Yi-seu-liex mhong grawng pa prawng simiang Saw-law-meung yuh nin,
mai kied yaw ka nawh tete;
mawh kheu tawng kix Siyiex hoik tawx nawh koe ping nyia cuyi,
nawh tom pon prawng rauh see ka see cawng heue.
