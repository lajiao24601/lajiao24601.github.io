Been krax tan
15
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si,
2 yam hoek pui Yi-seu-liex ka haktiex pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kix,
kix caw tix hawt been in plak greem.
3 Gleeing tix tawx krax tan pa mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
krax tan pa hawt awm lox pa hoik deuh tix,
krax tan pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
phan ang mawh nan,
krax tan pa caw tix tan ka daux poi,
chix tix siao moi mhaing,
pex mhaing,
yung,
kieh mawh krax tan.
Taox pa si oe bhawng krax tan in mawh pa muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb.
4-5 Phan tan yung mai pex ka Simiang Siyiex kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
yam tan ka khawng brie an kix ku mu,
mai caw tix viex hoek khawm Krax-tan Nghox-koi pa mawh phawng mien tix kongcing pa klao tix mai bee sime tix kongsheng,
kawn baox tawx khawm plai tix kongsheng.
6 Yam tan ka kawn yung mhaing,
tag caw tix tan khawm Krax-tan Nghox-koi pa mawh phawng mien ra kongcing pa klao tix mai bee sime tix kongsheng plak,
7 kawn baox tawx khawm plai tix kongsheng plak kieh mawh pa tan tau mai Krax-tan Pa-jauh.
Taox pa si oe bhawng krax tan in mawh pa muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb.
8 Yam tan moi mhaing ka Simiang Siyiex kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Pa hawt awm lox pa hoik deuh tix mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
9 mai tag caw tix tan khawm Krax-tan Nghox-koi pa mawh phawng mien loe kongcing pa klao tix tau mai bee sime ra kongsheng,
10 kawn baox tawx khawm plai ra kongsheng kieh mawh pa tan tau mai Krax-tan Pa-jauh.
Taox pa si oe bhawng krax tan in mawh pa muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb heue.
11 Yam tan ka moi mhaing tix mu,
pex mhaing tix mu,
yung tix mu mai pex tix mu tag caw yuh yuh nin.
12 Phan tan tix mu haok plak piang,
khawng brie pa tan tau mai an caw tix kieh lheu khaing an.
13 Yam tan pui Yi-seu-liex pa keh ka daux haktiex ot tix ku kaux ka Krax-tan Ngu pa si oe bhawng pa muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb,
caw tix yuh yuh nin uik.
14 Yam mawx ang ah,
pui tang ceu pa ot daux pex,
pa ot tix vud ang ah,
pa ot rheeng ang ah,
yam tan ka Krax-tan Ngu pa si oe bhawng pa muh Simiang Siyiex tix rieb,
mai caw tix yuh hawt awm been an nang.
15 Been in kieh mawh pa caw pex mai pui tang ceu pa ot daux si nax pex tix hawt tix ju hoek tix ju.
Pex mai kix awm paox ka plak ngai Simiang Siyiex.
16 aMawh kheu nan,
been pa awm paox mai kaing pa awm paox tag caw tix siao ka piang pex,
mai baox siao ka piang kix heue.
17 Simiang Siyiex krai ka Maw-si,
18 khaix hoik hoek pui Yi-seu-liex ka haktiex pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kix,
tag caw tix hawt ka been in plak greem.
19 Yam ih kix priex preem pa koe haktiex an,
kix tag caw tix teei priex preem an tix blah kieh mawh krax tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
20 Yam tong pex khao mun,
tag caw tix siao khao mun pa jauh pex tix tong tix khlawp khaing nghox ca pa liang pex tix pon,
tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai tan tau mai nghox ca pa khraox pauh pex,
kieh mawh awm krax tan pa blawk tix tan.
21 Pex tix ju hoek tix ju tag caw tix teei khao mun pa jauh pex tix tong tix khlawp,
kieh mawh krax tan pa blawk tix tan heue.
22 Phan koe pui pa ang tawng yam lud loe tix ka been pa tawm Simiang Siyiex lox ka Maw-si;
23 mai phan baox koe pui hun khaix in sax,
ang nghiet ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si.
24 Krax lud an mawh pa kaoh khaing pa ang pui hun tawng,
kix tag caw tix tan ka moi mhaing tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai kieh Simiang Siyiex muh tix rieb taox bhawng an,
mai baox tan ka Krax-tan Nghox-koi mai Krax-tan Pa-jauh pa khaw tix tan.
Lheu khaing an,
kix kawn baox tan ka pex mhaing tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
25 Cao giex kaing li tag yuh krax been pa singax singeh son pui hun,
kieh kix pon krax pa pluat ploi;
mawh kheu krax lud loe kix mawh pa ang kix tawng,
kheu nan kix kawn baox tan ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Ngu tawx ka Simiang Siyiex.
26 Yuh nin,
pui hun Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot daux si nax kix tag pon krax pa pluat ploi khawm uik,
mawh kheu lud loe kix ka daux pa ang tix tawng awm paox heue.
27 bPhan koe pui phit lud ka daux pa ang tix tawng,
tag caw tix tan ka pex siang tix mu pa koe tix num,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
28 Cao giex kaing li tag yuh krax been pa singax singeh son nawh ka piang khu dee tan,
teei tix tik phi map nawh,
son sang pluat ploi ka phi map nawh.
29 Mawh pui Yi-seu-liex ang ah,
pui tang ceu ang ah,
phit lud ka daux pa ang tix tawng,
tag hawt tix yuh been in pa awm paox heue.
30 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa su tix phit lud,
mawh pui Yi-seu-liex ang ah,
pui tang ceu ang ah,
hoik mawh pa map nai ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix pauh yum;
31 Mawh kheu hoik nu nai nawh ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
mai su tix lieh lheu ka gum lox tawm nawh.
Pauh nawh yum hoik mawh kheu phi map nawh heue.
Pui pa lud ka singaix tuah
32 Yam kawn ot pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ka daux tiag,
koe pui tix kaux hu sut ka khix daux singaix tuah,
pui yaox kah.
33 Viex pui in hoek ka Maw-si,
Ya-loeng mai pui hun dee an,
34 mai laung nawh,
kheu yam an kix ang nyang tawng grawng pa sang prawng ka nawh.
35 Khaix an Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Pui in tag pauh nawh yum,
cub pui hun tag dheem nawh yum ka rang simaox,
ka plak praix nyiex bang.”
36 Khaix an pui hun viex nawh hoek ka plak praix bang,
hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai dheem nawh yum ka rang simaox heue.
Been pa caing
37 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
38 cnhie nawh krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ah nin:
“Ka jaong sibex pex caw tix caing ka maox,
ka piang maox an kieh maox pa klai tix nyaing rieb paox tix kah.
Tix ju hoek tix ju pex caw tix yuh mawh nan.
39 Maox in mawh son sang kieh pex doh keud,
yam yaox pex ka maox an,
pex tag chix doh keud ka gum lox ping nyap aux khawm uik,
mai hawt tix yuh.
Yuh nin,
pex tom ang lai singai hu khaing aux,
hawt ka rhawm pa simie pex tix yuh mai pa simie pex tix pon.
40 Maox in sang kieh pex doh keud mai hawt tix yuh ka gum lox ping nyap aux khawm uik.
Pex tag caw tix singax singeh mawh cie aux.
41 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex;
aux hoik viex pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
son sang mawh Siyiex pex,
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex heue.”
