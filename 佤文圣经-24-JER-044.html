Simiang Siyiex tawx gum lox ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa vuk vied vuk viang ka haktiex Ai-cix
44
Phan mawh krax pa im ju pui Yi-seu-liex pa vuk vied vud viang tix hoek ka Ai-cix,
pa paung ot ka Mi-tawx,
Ta-pi-nix,
Naw-fu,
mai ka plak seh haktiex Ai-cix,
Simiang Siyiex hoik si kah ka aux.
2 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai yuh nin:
“Pex hoik yaox krax laux leeing pa ploi aux lih piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka piang veng Yo-tax ku mu ka ngai tix.
Hoek tom yam in,
veng in kix ca kawn mawh haktiex pa riem,
dee ang pui paung ot kah;
3 mawh kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui daux veng,
hoik cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm.
Kix hoik yuh krax tan krax lu ka siyiex tang kaux,
hoik krub ka siyiex tang kaux pa ang kix,
pex,
khawm tax du pex sax tawng sax yung khankix.
4 “Aux baox ploi ka siliex tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix hu ne raw ka pex,
ang pon tix yuh ka kaing pa yum koeng aux kah,
5 mai pex ca kawn ang cu tix nghiet,
ang cu tix simi,
ang cu tix tik ka been pa tan lu ka siyiex tang kaux.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka veng Yo-tax ku mu,
mai ka daux laih ting laih iak pa koe ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ploi ngu gleeing laux leeing.
Tom hoek daux in singaix,
haktiex in ca kawn mawh dee riem dee ang tid tix koe heue.
7 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang leue tix chawk cheng pex ah nin:
yuh ka mawx pex lai kieh krax laux leeing pa yuh nin hoek ka piang ex lie?
Pex tag sang kieh can simex,
bun,
kawn nyawm,
mai kawn ngie dut riah,
pie tix kaux kawx ang lai deuh koe lie?
8 Yuh ka mawx pex lai krub ka riang rao,
yuh krax tan lu ka siyiex tang kaux ka daux haktiex Ai-cix,
pa mawh haktiex dee mox pex tix,
mai cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm lie?
Pex hoik tang kieh krax laux krax leeing lih hoek piang tix,
hoik kieh meung ka piang haktiex kieh pex mawh pa jhax lawg pui lie?
9 Pex hoik piawm ka kaing pa caox pa caig an kix pa yuh tax du pex,
mai simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai moeng simiang,
khawm to kaux pex,
moeng pex pa ot ka veng Yo-tax mai daux laih ting laih iak Yie-lu-sa-leung lie?
10 Tom hoek daux in singaix,
pex kawn ang bax cu tix loh rhawm tix,
mai ca kawn ang lhat graung yung yi ka aux,
ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx aux ka pex mai ka tax du pex khankix heue.
11 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang ciyang ka phi map pex,
yuh ka Yo-tax laux leeing khawm uik.
12 Phan mawh pui Yo-tax pa kawn deuk pa khin tix hu paung ot haktiex Ai-cix.
Aux kawx tag sang yuh kix laux leeing khawm uik.
Kix mawh pui ting ceu ang ah pui iak ceu ang ah,
tag sang yum ka naing mai ka cai rhawm.
Kix tag sang lih mawh pa yum koeng,
pa yaoh yee,
pa jhax lawg,
pa map nai pui kah.
13 Aux hoik sax teei krax naing,
krax cai rhawm,
mai jhawm saux preng ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
aux kawx tag sang hawt tix ciyang ka pui pa hu haktiex Ai-cix yuh nan.
14 Can pui Yo-tax pa to hu mox tix hoek haktiex Ai-cix,
ang yaox tix luat luan tix kaux kawx.
Ang koe pui pa pon deeh ing ka haktiex Yo-tax,
mai yaox tix ing ka haktiex tix pa kied simie kix tix paung ot kah.
Lheu khaing pui pa si pup krax laux pa tux khankix,
ang koe pui pa pon deeh ing heue.”
15 Yam an,
koe cub sime khankix pa hoik tawng moeng tix gleeing nyawng bhawng ka siyiex tang kaux,
mai bun miex pa cung dee an,
khawm pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka plak seh Ai-cix pawk lox aux ah nin:
16 “Yix ang nghiet lox pa krai maix ka yix ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex.
17 Yix tag sang hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik krai yix tix deuh.
Sivoe an,
yix mai tax du yix,
simiang yix mai cao kaing hoik gleeing nyawng bhawng,
tan lu plai ka siyiex bun yix ka daux veng Yo-tax ku mu,
mai ka daux laih ting laih iak pa koe ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
yam in yix tag sang hawt tix yuh awm in.
Yam an,
yix yaox tix sawm sak,
mai kied sidaing nyawm ot nyawm koe,
ang yaox krax laux krax leeing.
18 Viang mawh nan,
jauh tom yam hoik naox aux gleeing nyawm bhawng tan lu plai ka siyiex bun an,
yix tag yaox krax cud krax cha ku ceu,
paox ceu paox cad yix phan ang yum ka naing,
tag sang yum ka cai rhawm heue.”
19 Bun miex khankix baox ah nin:
“Mex yix hoik cu yix tong khao mun lih awm riang siyiex bun an,
mai gleeing nyawm bhawng tan lu plai ka nawh heue.”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom krai ka can simex bun pa hoik krai lox yuh nin ku kaux ah nin:
21 “Pex mai tax du pex,
simiang pex mai cao kaing,
khawm pui hun pa ot ka veng Yo-tax ku mu,
mai ka pui pa gleeing nyawng bhawng ka dauh laih ting laih iak pa koe daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pex ce Simiang Siyiex ang tawng,
phan ang mawh nan piawm ka lie?
22 Daux in singaix,
haktiex ot pex hoik riem,
ang koe pui pa paung ot kah,
mai lih tix mawh pa yaoh yee pui kah,
pa jhax lawg pui kah,
in hoik mawh kheu ang Simiang Siyiex lai pon gun yeung ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pex.
23 Pex si pup krax pa laux leeing yuh nin singaix in,
hoik mawh kheu gleeing pex nyawng bhawng ka siyiex tang kaux,
ang nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap Siyiex,
hoik phit lud ka nawh heue.”
24-25 Aux si kah ka can pui khawm uik,
nie mawh bun miex khankix,
mai krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ka pui Yo-tax pa hoik to hu haktiex Ai-cix.
Nawh ah nin:
“Pex mai moeng pex hoik sax ap pong tix tiex tu ka siyiex bun an khawm uik.
Pa hoik ap pong tix gleeing nyawng bhawng tan lu plai ka nawh,
pex kawx hoik hawt tix yuh awm lox pa tiex tu tix.
Chix tix,
pex hawt tix yuh ka lox pa hoik tiex tu pex,
plud ka lox pa hoik ap pong pex tix deuh heue!
26 Pui Yo-tax pa to hu haktiex Ai-cix eue,
yam in pex tag caw tix nghiet.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tiex tu ka pex ka daux ceu kaux pa kied ting kied lhaong,
mai pa singax singeh yuh nin:
aux ang lai baox cu pex tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux aux ah nin:
‘Aux tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju.’
27 Aux tag sang kieh pex si pup ka krax leeing,
ang mawh krax pa haoh huan.
Pex tag sang yum yuh nin:
phan ang yum ka naing,
tag sang yum ka jhawm saux,
tom hoek si dauh laux leeing haktiex khawm uik.
28 “Viang mawh nan,
ka daux pex mawh pui pa tux pa yaox tix klawd ju,
jauh ka Ai-cix deeh hoek ka Yo-tax.
Pui pa yaox tix klawd ju in kix tag tawng kah,
mawh lox aux pa lih tix mawh,
phan ang mawh nan mawh lox kix pa lih tix mawh.
29 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang tawx mhai ka pex tix mu,
mai kieh pex tawng aux tag sang ciyang pex ka daux haktiex in,
mai aux sang krai pao tag sang lih gum lox pa yuh aux pex laux leeing tix mawh tete.
30 aAux hoik sax ap tix tawx simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Si-ti-cia ka pui koeng pa keud tix sang pauh ka nawh,
pa mawh simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
aux kawx hoik hawt tix ap simiang Ai-cix pa kaux Haw-fu-la ka pui koeng pa keud tix sang teei ka ju nawh yum heue.”
