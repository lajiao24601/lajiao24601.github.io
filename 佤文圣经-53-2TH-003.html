Rawg buan son yix
3
Lox pa nghoik,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
khiox khaw pex rawg buan son ex liak,
son sang kieh gum lox cie Simiang Ye-sux nied tix phai sidiex tix hu,
khaix an pon krax pa nab yam yung yi,
awm hoik pon tix sidiex mai pa nab yam yung yi ka daux pex yuh nan.
2 Baox khiox khaw pex rawg Siyiex taoh ex luat luan khaing pui caox,
mawh kheu ang mawh pui ku kaux pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux heue.
3 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Ye-sux mawh pa kaux sijax;
nawh tag sang kieh pex kaux cong,
mai ang kieh pui caox pon yuh pex laux.
4 Simiang Ye-sux hoik kieh ex yum rhawm ka pex,
krax pa yum rhawm ex ka pex pon yuh tix lih tete,
kheu nan tag sang nied tix yuh ka gum lox pa tawm ex heue.
5 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux duk thung ka pex,
kieh krax muh pex ka Siyiex nie baox ting hu,
kheu nan grum koe mai rhawm gun pa hoik tawx Khrit ka pex heue.
Kaing pa caw pui tix yuh
6 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
ex tawx lox ka pex ka daux ceu kaux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit yuh nin:
tag caw kieh tix singai hu khaing kawn Khrit pa neh kram,
pa ang priex tix hawt ka lox pa sidiex ex khankix.
7 To kaux pex hoik tawng sidawng,
grawng sang caw pex tix hawt ka ex.
Yam ot ex tau mai pex,
ex ang doh nieh,
8 mai ang ih priex yie pui,
tieh tix yuh kaing pa taug mao leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
son ang sang kieh pex nyhap.
9 Ex yuh nin,
ang mawh ang ex koe akhuang tix nhie pex tawx ex ih nyaux,
mai hoik mawh son sang kieh pex gau tix ka ceu pa mhawm pa yuh ex.
10 Yam ot ex tau mai pex dee an ex hoik sax ah nin:
“Pui pa ang yuh kaing ang yaox tix sawm heue.”
11 Ex krai lox in,
mai mawh kheu hoik mhong ex koe pui ka daux pex nieh,
ang yuh ceu pa tid tix leeing lang singaix,
mai simie tix kuang ngie ka kaing cie pui tang kaux.
12 Pui pa yuh nin,
ex ne raw kix ka daux ceu kaux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit,
tawx lox nhie kix yien yawg tix,
mai yuh kaing son sang pon eei ka tix heue.
13 Viang mawh nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex paw ploi kaing mhawm pa yuh tix.
14 Tee koe nang pui pa ang cu tix nghiet ka gum lox pa hoik krai ex daux lox lai in;
pui pa yuh nin,
pex tag caw rien tix kah,
paw vi yuh paox pie tau mai kix,
son sang kieh kix tawng tix kaig tua.
15 Viang mawh nan,
paw vi kieh nawh awm pa mawh pui koeng,
tag caw tix sidiex gau nawh awm yuh pex ka kawn Khrit yuh nan heue.
Lox nawb buan
16 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux pa tawx krax pa nhim,
teei tix tawx krax pa nhim ka pex ku bawg ku yam heue!
Kieh Simiang Ye-sux ot tau mai pex ku kaux heue!
17 Aux tiiem ah nin:
“Pao-law nawb buan ka pex.”
Lox lai in ku pheen hoik tiiem yuh nin;
in mawh ngai lai pa tiiem aux heue.
18 Kieh Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son ka pex ku kaux heue!
