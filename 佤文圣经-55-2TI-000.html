Lai Thi-maw-thaix Phuk Ra
Krai dawt
Lai Thi-maw-thaix Phuk Ra plak nie mawh gum lox pa sidiex Pao-law ka paox pa yuh kaing tix pa nhum ju, mai ka pui pa teem coi nawh. Pa krai lao kaing ting mawh kieh tix kaux cong ka daux krax pa gun yeung. Pao-law sidiex gau ka Thi-maw-thaix, paw vi lhat graung ka ceu pa nu tix ceu kawx, kieh tix kaux sijax ka daux pa kham sakse Ye-sux Khrit tom si dauh, giex tom kaux cong ka gum lox buan Khrit, mai ka been pa singax ka daux phuk lai sigang khri pa prim, klawm ka beeh glawm sara cao gau mai beeh glawm cao sidiex heue.
Pao-law bre tix ne raw ka Thi-maw-thaix, tag caw tix tik vud ka “lox lai pa daox pa aw”; mawh kheu lox lao ceu an ang koe dee mawh pie ceeix kawx, chix kieh ngie pui pa mhong laux leeing heue.
Pao-law simie Thi-maw-thaix pon kieh krax pa im ju tix mawh pa gau ka ceu pa mhawm, gau tix hawt ka krax yum rhawm nawh, krax pa gun yeung nawh, krax muh krax rhawm nawh, pa kaux cong ka daux krax gun yeung, mai pa kham krax kha rhawm ka daux pa tuk tiak tuk te heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–2)
2. Pa yaog yaw mai pa sidiex gau (1.3–2.13)
3. Pa sidiex gau mai pa ne raw (2.14–4.5)
4. Krax ot krax koe Pao-law (4.6–18)
5. Lox pa nghoik (4.6–18)
