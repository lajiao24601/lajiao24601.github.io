Ya-kang yuh ka phi map
7
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx lox ang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex teei ka kraung pa khaw tix yuh laux,
viang mawh nan,
koe pui pa ang nghiet.
Pui in kaux Ya-kang,
mawh kawn simex Cia-mi,
kawn saux Sa-ti,
mawh ceu pui Yo-tax cub nyiex ma Shie-la.
Nawh ang nghiet ka lox in,
kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom song rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
2 Yaw-su-yax ploi pui hu khaing veng Yie-li-keu hoek ka praok Paw Ya-weung,
veng Ai pa ot plak lih singaix Paw-theu-lix,
kieh kix hu jiang ka haktiex dee an.
Khaix hoik jiang kix,
3 deeh hoek krai ka Yaw-su-yax ah nin:
“Ex ang siao kieh pui khawm uik lih puing ka veng Ai,
pie pui ra loe rheng hoik kub,
mawh kheu pui ot daux veng an ang nie.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
pui been loe rheng kaux pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu puing ka veng Ai,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang pex kah.
5 Pui veng Ai pheet ka kix,
jauh ka siviex veng tom hoek ka See-pa-ling,
mai pauh kix dee lih jee caong gawng tix ngoe liah kaux.
Mawh kheu nan,
rhawm rhi pui Yi-seu-liex hoik laux ka heue.
6 Yaw-su-yax mai kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex khankix riah kraung ceep tix,
mai sau nyaox piang kaing tix,
gup pak ngai tix ka plak sivoe teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
tom hoek plak bo.
7 Yaw-su-yax ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong eue,
mawh jao pa tix maix lai viex yix tiang ka plak toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang lie?
Mawh son sang ap maix tix tawx yix ka daux tawm taix ceu pui Ya-maw-lix kieh yix laux leeing lie?
Yuh ka mawx ang kieh yix ot ka toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak an lie?
8 Simiang eue,
yam in pui Yi-seu-liex hoik to sivoe pui pa koeng,
aux kawn sang ah lox pa tix lie?
9 Yam mhong pui Cia-nang mai can pui pa ot ka daux haktiex in grawng in,
kix tag lai lawm ka yix,
mai pauh ka yix khawm uik.
Yam an,
maix sang kieh min tix kaux cong yuh ka mawx lie?”
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Kaoh heue!
Yuh ka mawx gup pak ngai tix piang tiex?
11 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh ka phi map!
Kix hoik phit lud ka gum lox aux,
tieh vheen ka lox sijax mukheu pa yuh aux mai kix.
Kix hoik teei kraung ceu pa khaw tix kieh laux;
kix briex ka kraung in kix,
deuh ot mai kraung tix,
mai mox kah.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai pon tieb tie ka pui pa koeng.
Kix ang pex,
mawh kheu kix hoik yuh phi map son to kaux tix.
Lheu khaing yuh pex kraung pa ang khaw tix teei an kieh laux,
aux ang lai ot tau mai pex!
13 Yam in,
maix kaoh cung,
kieh pui khankix singax,
kieh kix rien tix mhawm mhawm,
pa sax hoek sivoe aux,
mawh kheu aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex sang krai ka kix:
‘Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex hoik teei kraung pa khaw tix kieh laux pa ang aux kieh pex teei.
Phan pex ang tik ka kraung in kix,
pex tag ang pon tieb tie ka pui pa koeng!’
14 “Maix tawx lox ka pui khankix,
pa sax pon ngawb cao cao,
tag hawt ka ceu pui tix khrawm paox tix,
ceu mawx ceu an haok sivoe.
Aux kok ceu pui mawx hoek,
ceu pui an tag hawt ka cub ceu tix,
cub mawx cub an haok sivoe.
Aux kok cub mawx hoek,
cub nyiex an tag hawt cub nyiex tix,
nyiex mawx nyiex an haok sivoe.
Aux kok nyiex mawx hoek,
nyiex an tag hawt ka nyiex tix,
kaux pui mawx kaux pui an tag haok sivoe.
15 Ciak ka pui mawx,
tag mawh pui pa teei ka kraung pa khaw tix kieh laux,
pex tag gleeing nawh yum,
khawm daux nyiex ma nawh mai kraung cie nawh khawm uik kawx gleeing kah;
mawh kheu nawh tieh vheen ka lox sijax mukheu aux,
kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yaox krax pa kaig tua.”
16 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
Yaw-su-yax kieh pui Yi-seu-liex hoek sivoe,
tix ceu hoik tix ceu,
ceu pui Yo-tax mawh pa teei lih.
17 Nawh kieh ceu pui Yo-tax hoek,
tix cub hoik tix cub,
ceu nyiex ma Shie-la caw tix teei lih.
Nawh kieh ceu pui Shie-la hoek,
tix cub hoik tix cub,
ceu nyiex ma Sa-ti caw tix teei lih.
18 Dee nghoik nawh kieh pui nyiex ma Sa-ti hoek,
tix kaux hoik tix kaux,
kawn saux Sa-ti,
kawn simex Cia-mi Ya-kang teei lih.
19 Yaw-su-yax ah nin ka Ya-kang:
“Kawn simex aux eue,
maix krai grawng phi map maix sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex.
Krai ka aux,
hoik yuh maix ceu pa tix;
paw lai mox ka aux.”
20 Ya-kang ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
aux hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
In hoik mawh pa yuh aux:
21 Ka daux kraung pa pon ex khaing naing,
aux yaox ka sibex dha Pa-pi-loeng pa kied mhawm tix pheen,
kawn koe mau ra kongcing mai khri tix plak kongcing.
Aux kied muh ka kraung in kix,
tom teei hu heue.
Kraung in kix hoik si paung ot greem tie nyiex bang aux,
mau ot tom plak greem.”
22 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax ploi pui hu ka nyiex bang dee an,
hoik yaox tete kraung pa khaw tix kieh laux an si paung tix ot greem tiex,
mau ot tom plak greem.
23 Kix viex kraung an lih khaing nyiex bang,
viex hoek ka Yaw-su-yax mai can pui Yi-seu-liex dee an,
deuh sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
24 Yaw-su-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex giex ka Ya-kang,
tawx mau,
sibex dha,
maox khri,
khawm kawn simex kawn bun Ya-kang,
moi,
yung,
la,
nyiex bang,
khawm can kraung nawh khawm uik viex hu ka “ngawd krax laux leeing.”
25 Yaw-su-yax ah nin ka Ya-kang:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai viex krax laux in hoek piang ex lie?
In singaix Simiang Siyiex sang ploi krax laux ka piang maix!”
Kheu nan,
pui hun tom dheem Ya-kang mai pui nyiex ma nawh yum ka simaox,
mai gleeing ka kraung cie nawh khawm uik.
26 Kix pum simaox ka piang to kaux yum nawh.
Simaox in tom in singaix kawn koe dee an.
Mawh kheu nan,
dee an kawn kok kaux “ngawd krax laux leeing” tom in singaix heue.
Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom naox ka krax song rhawm tix heue.
