50
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
pex ce aux grheu ka miex pex hu,
mawh kheu nan,
lai pa tik aux ka nawh koe dee mawx lie?
Pex ce aux coh tix ka pui pa ceuk pex ka lie?
Mawh kheu nan,
tete mawh hoik coh ka pui mawx lie?
Coh ka pex hoik mawh kheu phi map pex;
hoik tik vud ka miex pex mawh kheu yuh pex kaing pa caox pa caig heue.
 
2 Aux hoek tix taoh teem,
yuh ka mawx lai ang koe pui lie?
Aux kok yo,
yuh ka mawx lai ang koe pui pa pawk lie?
Mawh iak riang aux ang pon taoh teem lie?
Aux tawx lox,
rawm sigrit sigra tag lih kroh;
aux kieh rawm klawng tieh tix lih mawh pak mhaik,
kieh kax khankix yum kheu ang rawm koe.
3 Aux pon kieh praix klawng tieh tix faik,
awm yiiem ka pui yum khankix heue.
Siliex Simiang Siyiex pa koe krax cu krax nghiet
4 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong gau aux krai ka lox pa khaw tix krai,
kieh aux pon tawx krax nyawm rhawm ka pui pa koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi.
Yam pon ngawb cao ku ngaix,
nawh so aux haing,
kieh aux simie tix nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau tix.
5 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong kieh aux pon tix tawng sidawng;
aux ang tieh vheen,
ang lieh lox.
6 aAux ghram pui si vied blawg tix,
reeig pui hauk jheeh tix,
baig pui ngai tix;
aux hoik gun yeung ka krax kaig krax tua heue.
 
7 Viang mawh nan,
krax kaig krax tua pa yuh kix ang pon kieh aux laux,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik teem coi ka aux.
Aux viex rhawm kua tix gun yeung khawm uik,
tawng grawng ang tix hoek krax kaig krax tua.
8 bMawh kheu ot Siyiex tau maix aux,
prawng rauh see son aux,
pui mawx kawn sang pon deuh phi map ka aux lie?
Ex hu tau ka daux nyiex prawng see heue!
Kieh nawh sau riang tix deuh phi map ka aux heue!
9 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang hoek tix prawng lox son aux;
koe pui mawx sang pon kham sakse grawng koe phi map aux lie?
Pui pa deuh phi map ka aux tag sang grai khawm uik,
awm kraung ceep pa hoik griah pa ih greeing yuh nan heue.
 
10 Can pui pa nab yam yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa nghiet ka lox sidiex gau siliex nawh khankix,
viang nawh hoik hu ka daux krax pa faik kawx,
nawh ca kawn yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
deuh rhawm ka Siyiex heue.
11 Can pui pa keud tix sang yuh pui tang kaux laux leeing,
tag sang deeh laux leeing ka daux pa yuh to kaux tix.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang mawh cao pa tat dut ah nin:
pui pa yuh nin tag sang hoek krax laux leeing tete heue.
