16
“Aux krai kaing in kix ka pex,
mawh son ang sang kieh krax yum rhawm pex yung yang.
2 Kix tag sang grheu pex lih hu khaing nyiex sidiex;
khaix an bawg yam dex sang hoek,
pui pa pauh ka pex an kawn ce kaing pa yuh tix in mawh pa krub tix ka Siyiex.
3 Tete te lai,
kix yuh kaing pa yuh nin hoik mawh kheu ang kix tawng yung ka Keeing,
mai ka aux kawx ang tawng yung kah.
4 Aux krai kaing in kix ka pex,
mawh son sang kieh pex doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai aux ka pex yam lih hoek bawg yam in heue.”
Kaing pa yuh Khuan Singax Singeh
“Yam jauh,
aux ang bax krai kaing in kix ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik ot aux tau mai pex ang jud.
5 Aux tag sang deeh ing ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek dee an yam in,
ka daux kix ang koe pui chawk ka aux ‘sang hu maix dee mawx?’
6 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu hoik krai aux kaing in kix ka pex,
pex lai kawn kieh tix naog krax pa khi rhawm.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex tete,
phan aux hu hoik koe dee mhawm son pex;
phan aux ang hu,
cao teem coi ka pex an ang chix hoek ka pex dee in;
phan aux hu,
aux tom ploi nawh hoek.
8 Yam hoek nawh,
nawh tag sang kham sakse ka kawn pui haktiex,
mai krai hoik lud krax keud kix ka son phi map,
ka son pa rauh pa see,
mai ka son krax pa prawng ca Siyiex.
9 Kix hoik lud krax keud ka phi map,
mawh kheu ang kix yum rhawm ka aux;
10 kix hoik lud krax keud ka pa rauh pa see,
mawh kheu hoik hu aux ka Siyiex dee an,
pex ang lai yaox ka aux.
11 Kix hoik lud keud ka krax pa prawng Siyiex,
mawh kheu simiang haktiex in hoik kham krax pa prawng ca heue.
12 “Aux kawn koe kaing tom nie ceu sang krai ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
yam in pex ang lai pon klawm kah.
13 Krox jun Khuan Singax Singeh pa mawh pa meh lih hoek,
nawh tag sang thung pex laig ka daux krax pa mawh pa meh khawm uik.
Nawh ang krai lox pa kaoh khaing to kaux tix,
mai hoik krai lox pa mhong tix ka pex,
khaix an baox sang krai kaing pa tag sang lih singaix pa sang hoek.
14 Nawh tag sang yaog min plak ngai aux,
mawh kheu sang krai nawh lox pa sang krai aux ka pex.
15 Can pa koe keeing aux khawm uik hoik mawh cie aux,
mawh kheu nan aux krai ah nin,
Khuan Singax Singeh tag sang krai lox pa sang krai aux ka pex heue.”
Krax khi rhawm tieh tix lih mawh krax gaux rhawm
16 “Krox tix vud pex taug ang lai yaox ka aux;
kheu nan,
baox krox tix vud pex kawn tag sang yaox ka aux heue.”
17 Ka daux pui gau koe pui ra loe kaux,
ra lai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Nawh krai ka ex ‘krox tix vud,
pex tag ang lai yaox ka aux;
khaix an baox deeh krox tix vud,
pex kawn sang yaox ka aux’ baox ah nin ‘mawh kheu sang hu aux ka keeing dee an’;
dee bleeh lox in mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
18 Baox koe pui pa chawk ah nin:
“Lox pa krai nawh ‘krox tix vud’ mawh krai ceu pa tix lie?
Ex ang tawng mawh hoik krai nawh ceu pa tix heue!”
19 Ye-sux hoik tawng ceu pa sang chawk kix,
nawh tom ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai ‘krox tix vud,
pex ang lai yaox ka aux;
khaix an baox deeh krox tix vud,
pex kawn sang yaox ka aux;’
pex ra lai kum paox tix ka lox in lie?
20 Aux krai lox tom sidawng ka pex,
pex tag sang yiiem ka kha rhawm tix,
viang mawh nan,
tag sang gaux rhawm;
pex tag sang khi rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
krax pa khi rhawm pex tag sang tieh tix lih mawh krax pa gaux rhawm.
21 Bun hoik khi rhawm yam dex sang keh tix,
mawh kheu hoik hoek bawg yam pa kha rhawm tix;
viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik keh kawn ngie tag sang piawm ka grawng pa kha rhawm tix,
mawh kheu hoik gaux rhawm ka hoik keh kawn ngie in hoek ka piang haktiex.
22 Pex kawx hoik yuh nin:
yam in pex hoik koe krax pa khi rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang deeh yaox ka pex,
mai rhawm rhi pex tag sang naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi pex,
ang koe pui tix kawx pa pon teei hu heue.
23 “Ka daux singaix an,
pex ang lai khiox khaw ceu pa tid tix ka aux.
Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
pex rawg ka daux ceu kaux aux,
rawg ceu pa tix ang ah ka keeing,
nawh tag sang tawx ka pex.a
24 Tom hoik hoek yam in,
pex ang bax khiox khaw ceu pa tid tix ka daux ceu kaux aux;
pex khiox khaw,
tom taug pon,
son sang kieh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi pex grum naog heue.”
Pon pex ka haktiex
25 “Aux teei gum lox phek tiiem krai kaing in kix ka pex.
Viang mawh nan,
bawg yam tag sang hoek,
aux ang lai siao ka gum lox phek tiiem,
tag sang krai kaing cie keeing aux sidawng ka pex.
26 Ka daux singaix an,
pex tag sang khiox khaw ka daux ceu kaux aux;
ang mawh krai aux sang khiox khaw ka keeing aux son pex,
27 mawh kheu hoik muh keeing aux ka pex.
Nawh hoik muh ka pex;
mawh kheu hoik muh pex ka aux,
khaix an hoik yum rhawm pex ka mawh aux pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an.
28 Aux kaoh khaing Siyiex deeh an hoek ka haktiex in;
yam in aux tag sang lih hu khaing haktiex in,
mai deeh ing ka Keeing dee an heue.”
29 Pui gau krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
maix hoik krai sidawng,
mai ang lai siao ka gum lox phek tiiem ceu pa tid tix.
30 Yix hoik tawng,
ceu pa ang maix tawng ang koe,
maix ang siao lai lo pui chawk ka tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
yix hoik yum rhawm ka mawh maix pa kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an heue.”
31 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yam in maix hoik yum rhawm lie?
32 Bawg yam hoik hoek,
yam in hoik mawh heue,
pex tag caw tix prung prang tix hu,
mai tang deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix kaux mawx kaux an,
deuh ngie aux ot tix kaux khaing.
Tete te lai,
ang mawh to kaux aux pa krai tix kaux khaing,
mai hoik mawh keeing pa ot tau mai aux.
33 Aux krai kaing in ka pex,
hoik mawh son sang kieh pex pon pa nhim kheu hoik kleuk tix tau mai aux.
Ka piang haktiex pex hoik kha rhawm tix;
viang mawh nan pex tag caw kieh tix kua,
kheu hoik pex aux ka haktiex heue!”
