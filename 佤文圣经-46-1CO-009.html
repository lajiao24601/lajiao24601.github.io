Akhuang cie kawn cau mai beeh glawm
9
Ang aux mawh pui pa bheeik luan lie?
Ang aux mawh kawn cau lie?
Ang aux bax yaox ka Simiang ex Ye-sux lie?
Ang pex hoik mawh plix ka daux kaing jhaw pa yuh aux son Simiang Ye-sux lie?
2 Viang tang kaux ang een aux mawh kawn cau,
pex hoik kieh aux mawh kawn cau heue!
Mawh kheu grawng pa hoik lih mawh pex kawn Khrit cie Simiang Ye-sux in hoik kham mhai sakse grawng pa mawh aux kawn cau heue.
3 Yam si briem pui ka aux,
aux tag krai lox son tix yuh nin:
4 Khex ang aux koe akhuang tix ih nyaux ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka pa sidiex ka gum lox buan lie?
5 Khex ang aux pon awm kawn cau tang kaux,
pon awm pux aik ka daux Simiang Ye-sux,
pon awm Pi-teux yuh nan,
viex moeng pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux Khrit tix hu tau mai tix plak praix lie?
6 Khex ang krai mawh ngie Pa-na-pa mai aux,
phan ang yuh kaing son vaig tu tix ang chix lie?
7 Koe pui mawx pa hu yuh lien,
hoik tang rien khre mau pa sang liag ka priex preem lie?
Koe pui mawx pa sum ka plix shiet,
mai ang ih ka plix shiet pa sum tix khaing daux si peem lie?
Koe pui mawx pa liang ka phung yung,
mai ang nyaux ka rawm teeh phung yung cie tix lie?
8 Pa krai aux ang mawh ngie lox pa tiag ka krax ot krax koe khaing,
lox tat jhawm prawng kawx hoik krai yuh nin lie?
9 aLox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si hoik tat tix deuh ah nin:
“Yam dig moi nghox ka daux bang pauh nghox,
ang pon tix jup jheeh nawh.”
Ceu pa muh Siyiex khex mawh moi lie?
10 Nawh krai lox in khex ang mawh pa krai son ex lie?
In hoik mawh pa tiiem son ex tete heue!
Mawh kheu pa sum lih hu yuh kaing tau mai pa vawg,
mai simie tix pon ka nghox koi pa khaw kix tix pon.
11 bYix hoik rhuat hoik van simie pa dex dawt mai khuan ka daux pex,
phan hoik pon priex preem khaing ka daux cie pex,
in hoik sawn tix nie luan lie?
12 Phan pui tang kaux koe akhuang tix simie ka pex yuh nin,
khex ang ex nie blawn koe akhuang pa yuh nin lie?
Viang mawh nan,
ex ang sax siao lo ka akhuang ceu pa yuh nin,
viang mawh nan,
tieh tix kham krax gun yeung pa koe khawm uik,
son ang sang kieh gum lox buan Khrit si pup krax pa tieh tie.
13 cPex tag caw tix tawng,
pui pa yuh kaing ka daux nyiex buan hoik pon priex preem khaing nyiex buan;
pie pui pa kia ngien ka praok khu dee tan kawx,
hoik guah kix pon ka khawng tan pa koe ka piang khu dee tan.
14 dAwm an,
Simiang Ye-sux kawx hoik tawx lox ah nin:
can pui pa yuh kaing pa sidiex gum lox buan khawm uik,
tag caw eei tix ka pa een rhawm tix ka pa sidiex ka gum lox buan heue.
15 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang bax siao ka akhuang in kix;
yam in aux tiiem ka lox in kix,
ang mawh nang son sang sawg tix pon ka akhuang ceu in kix.
Aux yum kawx,
aux ang kieh pa hoik yaw tix rang di ka mawh pa giex going heue!
16 Aux ang koe ceu pa yaw rang di ka son pa sidiex gum lox buan;
aux hoik pon ngie lox tawx.
Aux ang sidiex gum lox buan tag sang laux leeing heue!
17 Phan pa sidiex aux gum lox buan mawh krax gaux rhawm tix yuh,
aux tag sang pon ka krax pa vied ka buan son;
viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu hoik tawx Siyiex beeh glawm in ka aux,
aux tag een in mawh beeh glawm tix ceu.
18 Phan mawh nan,
buan son pa hoik vied ka aux mawh ceu pa tix lie?
Hoik mawh min plak ngai ka gum lox buan pa sidiex aux,
pa ang kieh pui tawx ngawh,
pa hoik mawh akhuang pa khaw tix pon khaing ka daux pa ang tix sidiex ka gum lox buan heue.
19 Aux hoik mawh pui pa bheeik luan,
ang mawh pui pa yuh siliex cawng pui tix kaux kawx;
viang mawh nan,
aux gaux rhawm tix mawh siliex pui khawm uik,
son sang kieh tix pon pui tom nie heue.
20 Aux yuh kaing tau mai pui Yo-thaix,
aux tag awm pui Yo-thaix khankix,
son sang kieh tix pon ka pui Yo-thaix.
Viang aux ang ot ka daux pa giex gang lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
mai aux hoik awm pui pa ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng yuh nan,
son sang kieh tix pon ka pui pa ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng.
21 Awm an,
yam ot aux tau mai pui pa ang ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng,
aux tag sang awm pui pa ang ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng yuh nan,
ang ot ka daux pa giex gang lox tat jhawm prawng Yo-thaix,
son sang kieh tix pon ka pui pa ang ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng.
Khaix an,
in ang mawh krai aux ang tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex;
tete te lai,
aux ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng pa giex gang Khrit.
22 Yam ot aux ka daux pui pa iak krax yum rhawm,
aux tag mawh pui pa iak riang,
son sang kieh tix pon ka kix.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux ot ka daux pui ceu pa tix,
aux tag mawh pui ceu an;
yuh ka mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang taoh ka pui tix blah.
23 Aux hoik yuh kaing ceu in kix son gum lox buan,
kaing ting mawh son sang yaox tix ot ka daux ceu pa mhawm gum lox buan tau mai pui tang kaux khankix heue.
24 Pex tee rien tix tawng,
pui pa yuh lah to ka pak klieh pui kied hun,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa pon khawng phak mawh ngie tix kaux khaing.
Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw viex rhawm pa keud tix sang pon mhai sau riang tix to.
25 Pui pa yuh klieh ku kaux,
hoik mawh pui pa sau riang tix gau tix,
son sang kieh tix pon ka mhok khri pa ang chix laux leeing an;
viang mawh nan,
ceu pa sawg ex hoik mawh mhok khri pa ang chix laux leeing an.
26 Mawh kheu nan,
aux to rauh see ka dee koe mhai;
aux baox awm pui pa yuh krax pa tawk paox yuh nan,
mai ang kieh taix tix yi ku bawg.
27 Aux sau riang tix pheuk ka to kaux tix,
son sang kieh tix pon giex gang ka khawm uik,
son ang aux sang kok pui tang kaux yuh lah to,
mai to kaux aux tieh tix plih lih heue.
