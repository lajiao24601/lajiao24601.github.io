Ye-sux pai ka pui pa yum taix caong moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.1–8; Lai Lu-cia 5.17–26)
2
Khaix hoik luan ra loe singaix,
Ye-sux baox hoek ka haktiex Cia-pai-nong;
grawng pa ot nawh ka nyiex hoik tauh hu.
2 Pui tom nie khrawm paox tix naog ka daux nyiex,
pie diang siviex kawx ang lai koe dee yi.
Yam tawx Ye-sux lox buan ka kix,
3 koe pui pon kaux grawng pui pa yum taix caong tix kaux hoek ka Ye-sux.
4 Mawh kheu hun pui,
kix ang pon grawng nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom yieh braux nyiex pa ot Ye-sux daux,
kieh daux koe ka tix mu,
khaix an ploi pui pa koe jhawm saux an khawm kraung it nawh lih.
5 Ye-sux yaox krax yum rhawm pui in kix,
tom ah nin ka pui pa yum taix caong an:
“Kawn eue,
phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi heue.”
6 Koe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri ra loe kaux ngawm ka dee an,
mai ka daux rhawm rhi kix ah nin:
7 “Pui in lai pon ah lox pa cau nyhau yuh nin heue!
Lheu khaing Siyiex,
pui mawx koe akhuang pa pluat ploi phi map lie?”
8 Ye-sux hoik tawng krax pa keud kix dum mawx dum an,
tom ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai koe rhawm keud pa yuh nin lie?
9 Ah nin ka pui pa koe jhawm saux in ‘phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi’ yie lie?
Mawh ah ‘Kaoh,
teei kraung it maix hu’ yie lie?
10 Aux tag sang kieh pex tawng,
kawn kawn pui koe akhuang tix pluat ploi phi map ka piang haktiex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka pui pa yum taix caong an:
11 “Aux tawx lox ka maix,
kaoh,
teei ka kraung it maix,
ing ka nyiex heue!”
12 Pui an tom kaoh,
dum mawx dum an teei kraung it tix,
mai lih hu ka daux pa dong pui hun ngai tix ka nawh.
Pui hun kied daox deng kah,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ah nin:
“Ex ang sax yaox kaing pa yuh nin tix bawg kawx heue!”
Ye-sux kok ka Li-we
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.9–13; Lai Lu-cia 5.27–32)
13 Ye-sux baox hoek ka praok rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix,
cub pui hun khrawm paox tix ot ka praok nawh,
nawh tom sidiex gau ka kix.
14 Yam baox hu nawh ka plak sivoe,
nawh yaox pui koh keen tix kaux,
pa mawh Li-we kawn simex Ya-leu-fe,
ngawm ka piang dee koh keen.
Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Hawt khaix aux heue!”
Li-we tom yaog tix kaoh,
mai hawt khaix nawh heue.
15 Khaix an Ye-sux sawm daux nyiex Li-we,
pui koh keen mai pui caox tom nie kawx hawt khaix nawh;
ka daux kix koe pui tix blah sawm tau mai Ye-sux khawm pui gau nawh.
16 Koe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai pui Fa-li-saix tix blah yaox sawm Ye-sux tau mai pui koh keen tau mai pui phi map,
kix tom chawk pui gau nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx nawh lai sawm nyaux tau mai pui koh keen mai pui caox lie?”
17 Ye-sux mhong kah,
tom ah nin:
“Pui pa brax kua ang lo ka cao sidah,
mawh pui pa koe jhawm saux pa sang lo kah.
Kaing ting aux hoek ang mawh sang kok ka pui mhawm,
mai mawh son sang kok ka pui caox heue.”
Grawng pa tuah ka priex preem
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.14–17; Lai Lu-cia 5.33–39)
18 Koe tix bawg,
pui gau Yaw-hang cao cub com mai pui Fa-li-saix tuah ka priex preem.
Koe pui hoek chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx Yaw-hang cao cub com mai pui gau pui Fa-li-saix tuah ka priex preem,
viang mawh nan,
pui gau maix ang tuah ka priex preem lie?”
19 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yam kawn ot kaw pa yuh ka paox ka daux bang poi ka paox,
nyo pa hoek tix yuh krax gaux rhawm sang chix tuah ka priex preem lie?
Ca mawh kawn ot kaw pa yuh ka paox tau mai kix,
kix ang tuah ka priex preem.
20 Viang mawh nan,
hoek singaix bawg yam,
pui tag sang viex kaw pa yuh ka paox hu khaing kix,
yam an kix taug sang tuah ka priex preem heue.
21 “Ang koe pui pa teei man khraox blaox ka kraung prim,
phan yuh nin,
khaix blaox pa khraox an tag sang griah yuh kraung ceep pa prim,
kieh rhoi griah nie ting.
22 Ang koe nang pui pa teei plai pa khraox sau ka daux hau hak pa prim,
phan yuh nin,
plai pa khraox an taug bih yuh hau hak pa prim,
plai mai hau hak tag laux khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
plai pa khraox tag sang sau ka daux hau hak pa khraox heue!”
Grawng singaix tuah
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.1–8; Lai Lu-cia 6.1–5)
23 aKa daux singaix tuah tix ngaix,
Ye-sux laig ka daux kaung ma nghox ca.
Pui gau nawh hu tau mai nawh;
kix caik ka si gleem nghox ca yam hu tix.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Fa-li-saix ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Maix jhak,
pui gau maix hoik yuh kaing pa ang cu pui yuh ka daux singaix tuah heue!”
25 bYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yam sang lo Ta-wue ka priex preem hoik yuh nawh ka mawx,
ang pex bax phat ka lie?
Nawh mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh cai rhawm,
26 ckix tom laig ka daux nyiex buan Siyiex,
mai ih ka khao mun pa tan lu pui ka Siyiex.
Grawng kaing in lih tix mawh yam mawh Ya-pi-ya-tha cao giex kaing li pa ting.
Phan hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng ex,
mawh ngie cao giex kaing li pa chix tix ih ka khao mun in khaing;
viang mawh nan,
to kaux Ta-wue ih kah,
mai baox guah pui pa hawt khaix tix ih ka heue.”
27 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Singaix tuah mawh pa yuh son pui;
pui ang mawh pa yuh son singaix tuah.
28 Kheu nan,
kawn kawn pui hoik mawh simiang ka singaix tuah heue.”
