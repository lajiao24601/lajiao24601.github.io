Rien tix sang hu gah ka teug sijax mukheu
15
Ta-wue sum nyiex haw son to kaux cao tix ka daux veng Ta-wuea,
nawh kawx khre dee tix dauh son sang sum nyiex gox bang tix lhang son teug sijax mukheu Siyiex.
2 bYam an,
Ta-wue ah nin:
“Mawh ngie pui Li-we pa chix tix grawng ka teug sijax mukheu,
mawh kheu kix hoik mawh pui pa laug Simiang Siyiex,
mawh cao grawng teug sijax mukheu,
mai yuh kaing jhaw nawh leeing ju leeing phan.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue khrawm kawn pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik,
son sang gah teug sijax mukheu hoek ka dee pa hoik rien khre Ta-wue.
4 Ta-wue baox khrawm doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng mai pui Li-we khankix:
5 cub ceu pui Keu-siax pa kaux Wu-liex viex pui tix yieh tix nga kaux;
6 cub ceu pui Mi-la-lix pa kaux Ya-soi-ya viex pui ra yieh tix nga kaux;
7 cub ceu pui Keu-soeng pa kaux Yaw-eux viex pui tix yieh tix ngoe kaux.
8 Baox koe,
cub ceu pui Yi-li-sha-fang pa kaux See-ma-ya viex pui ra yieh kaux;
9 cub ceu pui Si-paw-loeng pa kaux Yi-liex viex pui tix daix kaux;
10 cub ceu pui Wu-shiie pa kaux Ya-mi-na-ta viex pui tix yieh kao ra kaux heue.
11 Ta-wue kok ka cao giex kaing li Sa-tu mai Ya-pi-ya-tha hoek,
khawm pui Li-we liah kaux,
pa mawh Wu-liex,
Ya-soi-ya,
Yaw-eux,
See-ma-ya,
Yi-liex,
Ya-mi-na-ta.
12 Tom ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoik mawh simiang cub ceu pui Li-we ku cub,
caw tix kieh to kaux pex mai paox ceu paox cad pex pui Li-we singax,
son sang gah ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka dee pa hoik rien khre aux.
13 Mawh kheu bawg sivoe,
pex ang bax hu grawng teug sijax mukheu tau maix yix,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex tom ciyang ka ex,
mawh kheu ang ex bax krub nawh awm been pa hoik tat tix deuh heue.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we tom kieh to kaux tix singax,
son sang gah ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
15 cPui Li-we hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
mai teei khaox glawm,
grawng teug sijax mukheu ot ka piang klip tix heue.
16 Ta-wue tawx lox nhie simiang pui Li-we khankix,
ploi pui Li-we tom nie rhax ka lai,
teei ting pawd jung lah mai chiang,
tauh lox rhax krax gaux rhawm.
17-21 Kix laug pui lih khaing ceu cub pui pa blawn chix rhax lai khankix,
mawh cao pa tauh chiang plak greem:
kawn simex Yaw-eux pa kaux Si-mang,
kawn simex Pi-li-cia paox nyiex ma nawh pa kaux Ya-sa,
mai ceu pui Mi-la-lix kawn simex Ku-sa-ya pa kaux Yi-thang.
Kix baox laug pui Li-we plak greem lih mawh cao pa tauh ting pawk jung lah pa lhaong lox,
mai kieh khaw tau mai lox chiang;
pui in kix mawh Sa-cia-li-ya,
Ya-shiie,
See-mi-la-maw,
Yie-shiie,
Wu-ni,
Yi-li-ya,
Ma-si-ya,
Pi-na-ya heue.
Kix kawx hoik laug ka pui Li-we pa tauh ting pawd jung lah pa tiiem lox lih;
pui in kix mawh Ma-tha-thi-ya,
Yi-li-fe-li-hu,
Mi-kheu-ni-ya,
Ya-sha-si-ya,
mai pui pa pau nyiex buan pa mawh Eu-piie Yi-tong mai Yie-lix heue.
22 Ci-na-ni-ya kied mawh pui pa chix yuh lox rhax krax lieh,
mawh kheu nan tom laug nawh lih mawh cao thung cub pui Li-we ka lox rhax.
23-24 Pi-li-cia,
Yi-li-cia-na,
khawm Eu-piie Yi-tong mai Yie-si-yax lih mawh cao priex kia ka teug sijax mukheu.
See-pa-ni,
Yaw-sa-fa,
Na-thang-yiex,
Ya-ma-saix,
Sa-cia-li-ya,
Pi-na-yax,
mai Yi-li-yi-shiex cao giex kaing li khankix,
lih mawh cao phlu teet tawng ka plak sivoe teug sijax mukheu heue.
Viex teug sijax mukheu hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 6.12–22)
25 Simiang Ta-wue mai kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
khawm simiang naing khankix hu yaog teug sijax mukheu khaing nyiex ma Eu-piie Yi-tong.
Kix yuh poi ting krax gaux rhawm.
26 Kix tan lu moi mhaing aliah mu mai kawn yung aliah mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
son sang kieh mawh sakse krax teem coi Siyiex ka pui Li-we,
cao pa grawng ka teug sijax mukheu khankix.
27 Ta-wue ceep ka kraung dha man keei sum tix jung;
cub caw pui rhax lai mai cao kaing kix pa mawh Ci-na-ni-ya,
khawm pui Li-we pa grawng ka teug sijax mukheu khankix kawx ceep ka kraung dha man keei sum.
Ta-wue ceep ka Yi-fu-teu.
28 Mawh kheu nan,
can kawn pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
hu tau mai teug sijax mukheu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Kix gaux rhawm leeing gawng krax,
phlu lox teet tawng,
lox rung,
tauh ka lox chiang,
mai baox tauh ka ting pawd jung lah,
mai ting aliah lah khankix heue.
29 Yam hoik viex kix teug sijax mukheu laig ka daux veng,
kawn bun Sao-law pa kaux Mi-cia kok jhak ka daux doh,
mai yaox ka simiang Ta-wue gaux rhawm tix graoh,
tom iak jhak ka nawh heue.
