Cao jhai pa pie lhien
2
Ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
cao jhai pa pie lhien lih koe ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex;
yuh nan nang sara cao gau pa pie lhien kawx hoik lih koe ka daux pex.
Kix briex tix viex lox pa chix kieh pui laux leeing hoek,
ang rieb ka simiang pa taoh ka kix,
mai nied tix tang deeh kieh to kaux tix laux leeing.
2 Viang yuh nan kawx,
ca kawn koe pui tom nie pa tag sang hawt ka krax pa yuh kix pa caox pa caig,
tom hoik kieh pa mawh pa meh si pup krax laux krax leeing kheu kaing pa map nai yuh kix.
3 Cao jhai pa pie lhien in kix sang kieh rhawm ghoing to kaux tix naog,
kix nieb pon krai lox lao been pa ang tie mawh tete pie lhien tix teei ka sin ngao pex.
Jauh tom dix dix,
krax prawng ca Siyiex ca kawn krox jun ka cub pui ceu in kix;
kix ang doh yaox tix luat luan khaing krax laux leeing heue!
4 Siyiex ang bax va rhawm ka kawn cau Siyiex pa yuh phi map,
viang mawh nan,
taing tix tik kix ka daux Maw-na-rhok,
laung kix ot ka daux pa faik,
mai krox jun krax prawng ca.
5 aSiyiex kawx ang bax va rhawm ka baux ju singaix pa hoik luan,
viang mawh nan teei rawm ting kheem ka piang pui pa ang tawng ka Siyiex,
mai taoh ngie ka Naw-yax pa sidiex ka ceu pa rauh pa see,
mai ka pui daux nyiex ma nawh aliah kaux khaing.
6 bSiyiex ciyang ka veng Saw-to-ma mai veng Eu-maw-la ra mu,
ploi ngu lih gleeing laux,
son sang kieh mawh daux krax pa kieh raw ka pui pa ang nghiet lox.
7 cNawh taoh ka Law-teux pa mawh pui mhawm;
Law-teux kied khi rhawm khi rhi ka krax pa phit vao pui caox tete.
8 Yam ot pui mhawm ka daux pui caox,
kix hoik yaox hoik mhong ka kaing pa caox pa caig an kix ku singaix,
mai kied laux rhawm laux rhi ka tete.
9-10 Simiang Siyiex hoik tawng grawng sang taoh ka pui pa cu lox luat luan khaing krax nyaw krax glaw,
mai hoik tawng nang ka grawng sang deuh ka pui caox,
nie mawh pui pa hawt ka alo nex nham,
pa map nai ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex khankix,
son sang ciyang kix ka daux singaix pa prawng ca heue.
Cub sara cao gau pa pie in kix ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
ja lao sang nab yam yung yi ka cao pa koe min piang rao ma,
kix tieh tix map nai ka kix.
11 Pie kawn cau Siyiex khankix pa kawn ting riang ting siawp sibrawm khaing cub sara cao gau pa pie in kix kawx,
kix ang bax teei gum lox pa map nai deuh phi map piang kix ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
12 Viang mawh nan,
cub pui in kix awm to sat yuh nan,
pui hoik sawg tix giex pauh kix tom yam jauh tix lih keh;
kix yuh kaing ka daux riang rao to kaux tix,
mai hoik map nai ka kaing ceu pa ang tix tawng.
Kix tag sang laux leeing awm to sat yuh nan.
13 Kix yuh krax kha rhawm lih koe,
mai tang deeh hoek piang kix.
Phan mawh ka son kix,
pa hoik een kix mawh krax gaux rhawm,
tag mawh pa kieh kaing ceu pa kieh alo nex nham grum naog ka greem pa riang singaix.
Kix hoik seeing brai cux si um;
kix sawm tau mai pexd,
mai kieh krax pie lhien mawh krax gaux rhawm tom si dauh heue!
14 Ngai kix simie tix jhak ka bun pa phit vao,
alo nex nham phi map kix ang sax pon tix naog rhawm tix bawg kawx.
Kix nyaw glaw ka pui pa iak riang;
rhawm rhi kix meed ghoing tix;
kix mawh pui pa jhax lawg Siyiex heue!
15 eKix singai hu khaing krax rauh krax see,
mai lud krax lawng tix,
kix hoik lawng krax pa lawng Pa-lang kawn simex Pi-eux yuh nan.
Pa-lang muh ka mau pa ang rauh see,
16 mai pon krax pa ne raw kheu phi map pa yuh tix.
La pa ang chix si kah tag si kah awm si kah pui yuh nan,
tieb tie ka kaing ang khaw pa yuh cao jhai heue.
17 Pui in kix awm hoik kroh dee keet rawm yuh nan,
awm pai awm pa phlu bhaung ting yuh nan;
Siyiex hoik deuh pa kied raux pa faik son kix.
18 Kix krai lox vao pa daox aw,
teei alo nex nham nyaw glaw pui pa taug khraox bheeik luan khaing krax pa ang khaw caw.
19 Kix cu tix tawx krax luat luan ka pui,
mai to kaux tieh tix mawh siliex cawng ka daux krax pa seeing brai;
mawh kheu pa tawx pui krax pa giex gang ceu pa tix,
mai taug mawh siliex cawng ceu an.
20 Phan pui pon tix bheeik luan khaing siawp sibrawm krax pa seeing brai haktiex,
khaing ka daux pa tawng yung ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao taoh Ye-sux Khrit,
khaix an pui baox giex nawh hu,
mai kieh nawh nge,
dee si dauh krax lawng pui pa yuh nin kawn lheu caox khaing singaix pa hoik luan.
21 Kix hoik tawng koe krax pa rauh see,
viang mawh nan,
kawn singai hu khaing gum lox tawm pa hoik pon kix pa singax singeh;
pui pa yuh nin phan ang tawng krax pa rauh see kawn lheu mhawm khaing.
22 fLox been hoik khaw krai tix,
grawh grawng kix hoik awm “sox pa deeh ih ka ceu pa hao tix yuh nan”,
mai baox awm “lig pa hoik heem tix singax,
mai deeh hu lu lieh tix ka daux glaox yuh nan heue.”
