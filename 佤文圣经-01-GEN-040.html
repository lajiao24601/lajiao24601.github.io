Yaw-seux kah ca maox paox pa ot tau daux thawng
40
Khaix kaing in,
cao kaing pa tawx kawk nyaux mai cao kaing pa tawx priex ih simiang Ai-cix phit lud ka simiang tix.
2 Simiang song rhawm,
3 mai tawx lox kieh pui sau kiex daux thawng,
cao giex kaing lien ot tau daux thawng mai Yaw-seux.
4 Cao giex kaing lien kieh Yaw-seux kia ngien ka kiex;
kix ot daux thawng tix baux bawg yam heue.
5 Koe ka daux tix sawm,
cao kaing pa tawx kawk nyaux mai cao kaing pa tawx priex ih tang ca maox tix kaux tix ceu ka daux thawng,
ca maox kiex dee bleeh ang awm paox.
6 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
Yaw-seux hu jhak ka kiex,
yaox ang kiex nyawm ot nyawm koe,
7 tom chawk:
“Pax lai laux rhawm yuh nin daux in singaix yuh ka mawx lie?”
8 Kiex pawk lox:
“Yiex ca maox tix kaux tix ceu,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa pon kah dee bleeh son yiex.”
Yaw-seux ah nin:
“Pa chix kah dee bleeh mawh Siyiex pa tawx.
Krai ca maox pax ka aux liak!”
9 Khaix an cao kaing pa tawx kawk nyaux ah nin:
“Aux ca maox tix yaox nhu plix siiet tix gawng sivoe aux,
10 ka piang nhu plix siiet an koe loe kak.
Yam jiet pruh tai,
plix siiet kawx hoik teem,
11 daux taix aux viex ka kawk nyaux simiang;
aux pieh ka plix siiet,
mai biiet rawm plix siiet daux kawk,
tawx kawk plai an ka simiang heue.”
12 Yaw-seux ah nin:
“Dee bleeh ca maox in mawh nin:
kak loe kak an mawh loe singaix,
13 khaix in loe singaix,
simiang sang ploi maix lih,
pluat ploi ka maix,
mai deeh tawx kaing yuh maix;
maix sang deeh awm yam mawh maix cao kaing tawx kawk nyaux sivoe yuh nan,
tawx simiang nyaux plai.
14 Viang mawh nan,
yam lih maix mhawm mhawm,
doh keud ka aux liak,
teem aux krai lox mhawm sivoe simiang,
son sang pon lih aux khaing daux thawng.
15 Mawh kheu hoik mawh aux pui pa briex phrawk kix hoek khaing haktiex pui Si-paw-lai tete;
viang mawh ka daux haktiex,
aux kawx ang sax yuh kaing pa lud pa khaw tix laig daux thawng heue.”
16 Yam yaox cao kaing pa tawx priex ih simiang mhawm kah nawh ca maox cao pa tawx kawk nyaux,
tom ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Aux kawx ca maox tix ceu,
ca maox tix yaox ka piang kaing aux koe khrawng pa naog khao mun loe mu.
17 Khrawng pa ot plak piang hoik naog ka khao mun pa ih simiang ku ceu;
koe sim pu hoek tix ih ka khao mun in heue.”
18 Yaw-seux ah nin:
“Dee bleeh ca maox in mawh nin:
khrawng loe mu mawh loe singaix.
19 Khaix in loe singaix,
simiang tag tat kaing maix,
teuk teue maix ka piang khaox,
sim khankix tag tuh tix ih nex maix heue.”
20 Baox luan daux loe singaix,
mawh singaix keh simiang.
Daux ngaix an,
simiang yuh poi singaix keh tix mai kok ka siliex tix yuh krax gaux rhawm.
Nawh ploi ka cao kaing pa tawx kawk nyaux mai pa tawx priex ih lih,
viex hoek sivoe ngai siliex tix.
21 Nawh deeh tawx kaing pa yuh cao kaing pa tawx kawk nyaux,
22 teuk teue ka cao kaing pa tawx priex ih.
Kaing in kix hoik lih awm pa krai Yaw-seux ka kiex heue.
23 Viang mawh nan,
cao kaing pa tawx kawk nyaux ang lai doh keud ka Yaw-seux,
piawm nawh khawm uik heue.
