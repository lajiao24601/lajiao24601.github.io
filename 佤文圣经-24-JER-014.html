Sibhawm kroh praix pa kied lhat pui
14
Simiang Siyiex krai grawng lih sibhawm kroh praix klawng ka aux:
2 Pui Yo-tax laux rhawm,
veng laux leeing,
pui haktiex yiiem ngia it piang tiex;
pui Yie-lu-sa-leung yiiem grai khiox khaw krax teem krax coi.
3 Nyiex pui mag mi ploi siliex tix hu kin ka rawm;
yam hoek kix ka praok dung ang yaox rawm,
viex si ngien yi deeh ing.
Kix tawb kaing tix,
mai laux rhawm kaig tua,
4 rao ma ang tawx rawm lhiex lih,
haktiex hoik tuh;
pui yuh kaing haktiex tawb kaing tix mai laux rhawm heue.
5 Miex cak tiag tik ka kawn tix pa taug khraox keh;
mawh kheu ang rib klai lai koe.
6 La tiag cung ka piang gawng,
toek bhawm tix awm sox tiag yuh nan;
mawh kheu ang rib lai koe kix sawm,
ngai kix hoik iak riang.
7 Pui cie aux khiox khaw ka aux ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
viang phi map yix deuh phi map piang yix,
khiox khaw maix ca hawt tix teem coi yix awm lox pa ap maix tix ah.
Yix baox ca tik vud ka maix;
yix hoik phit lud ka maix heue.
8 Maix mawh krax keud pot jun pot pui Yi-seu-liex tix ceu khaing,
mawh cao taoh yix yam yaox yix krax kha rhawm.
Ka daux yix,
yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh tix awm pui pa ang tawng ka yix yuh nan?
Awm nyo si nie pa hu krax kaoh hu cao cao yuh nan?
9 Yuh ka mawx maix lai awm pui pa ang bax rien khre tix pie ceeix kawx,
awm pui naing pa ang koe riang rao yuh nan?
Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix ot tau mai yix tete heue!
Yix hoik mawh pui cie maix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi tik vud ka yix heue.
 
10 Phan mawh pui in kix,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Kix muh tix tieh vheen ka aux,
nieb pon hawt ka alo to kaux tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux yum koeng ka kix;
aux tag sang yuh tix tawng krax pa lud loe kix,
mai ciyang ka phi map kix heue.”
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Maix ang siao khiox khaw aux teem coi ka pui in kix.
12 Viang kix hoik tuah ka priex preem,
aux kawx ang nghiet ka lox khiox khaw kix;
viang kix hoik gleeing tix tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
aux kawx ang rieb kah.
Aux sang tieh tix teei krax naing,
krax sibhawm praix mai jhawm saux preng yuh kix laux leeing heue.”
13 Aux ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
cao jhai dha tix krai ang naing koe,
ang koe sibhawm praix.
Kix ah nin,
maix hoik cu tix tawx yix pon krax pa nyawm ot ka daux haktiex ot tix heue.”
14 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Cao jhai pa pie an kix hoik pie lhien ka daux ceu kaux aux.
Tete te lai,
aux ang bax ploi kix hu sidiex,
ang bax tawx nang lox,
phan ang mawh nan ang bax krai nang lox ka kix pie tix gum kawx.
Mhai sakse pa yaox kix ang mawh pa tawx aux;
lox dha kix tix krai mawh ngie pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm rhi kix khaing.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex krai ka maix yuh nin:
aux tag sang yuh ka cao jhai pa pie an kix,
pa ang aux bax tawx kix hu;
kix briex tix teei ceu kaux aux,
mai dha tix krai ang naing koe,
ang sibhawm praix koe ka daux haktiex in.
Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang teei naing mai sibhawm praix yuh kix laux leeing.
16 Pui pa nghiet ka lox pa dha kix tix krai tag sang si pup krax pa laux leeing yuh nan.
To kaux yum kix,
khawm to kaux yum moeng kawn kix,
tag sang tik ot ka daux laih daux cau Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ang koe pui pa si paung ka kix.
Aux tag sang kieh kix yaox krax pa pawk bix,
kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix heue.”
17 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux krai lao krax pa laux rhawm tix ka pui hun khankix ah nin:
Kieh rawm ngai aux buing tix lih leeing ngaix leeing sawm heue!
Kieh aux yiiem grai ang chix naox ka heue!
Mawh kheu hoik mat paox ceu paox cad aux;
kix hoik yaox tix kham krax laux leeing pa kin.
18 Aux hu daux tiag daux tu,
yaox ka to kaux yum pui pa yum greem lawm vaig;
aux laig ka daux veng,
yaox pui pa goh ka cai rhawm.
Cao jhai mai cao giex kaing li yuh ka kaing jheeng jheeng,
ang tawng grawng pa yuh kaing ceu pa tix heuea.
Kawn pui khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex
19 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tik vud ka pui Yo-tax khawm uik lie?
Maix hoik yum koeng ka pui pa paung ot ka Shi-ang lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh naing pa kin ka yix lie,
ang kieh yix yaox tix pon pa pai moi lie?
Yix kied simie ka krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox singaix mhawm pa yuh nin;
yix krox jun pa pai moi,
viang mawh nan,
mai tieh tix si pup ka krax pa yaoh yee.
20 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yix hoik phit lud ka maix.
Yix hoik doh keud ka phi map pa yuh tix,
hoik doh keud ka phi map tax du yix heue.
21 Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka gum lox pa hoik deuh tix,
mai paw vi tik vud ka yix;
khiox khaw maix paw kieh dang ngeung Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa mawh pa jhan riang maix hoek krax kaig krax tua;
khiox khaw maix paw vi tik ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh maix tau mai yix.
22 Riang rao krub meung ka piang haktiex sang pon chix kieh lhiex lih khaing rao ma lie?
Rao ma sang pon chix ploi rawm lhiex lih lie?
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
krax keud pot jun pot yix mawh ka daux maix,
kheu ceu in kix hoik mawh pa yuh maix khawm uik heue.
