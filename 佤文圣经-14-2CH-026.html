Wu-si-ya Simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 14.21–22; 5.1–7)
26
Can pui Yo-tax khawm uik hoik laug Wu-si-ya kawn simex Ya-ma-shiex pa koe kao liah num plud tix mawh simiang mawh keeing nawh.
2 Khaix hoik yum Ya-ma-shiex,
Wu-si-ya deeh teei ka Yi-la-tha hoek,
mai deeh cox ka heue.
3 Yam koe ju Wu-si-ya kao liah num,
nawh mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix phuan ra num.
Miex nawh Yie-kheu-li-ya mawh pui Yie-lu-sa-leung.
4 Wu-si-ya hoik hawt ka kaing pa yuh keeing tix,
mai yuh kaing pa mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
5 Yam kawn im ju Sa-cia-li-yax pui cao teem coi nawh ka daux been li,
nawh hoik yum rhawm tix yuh kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix;
Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka nawh heue.
6 Wu-si-ya mai pui Fe-li-see puing paox tix.
Nawh hoik yieh laux ka veng Cia-theux,
veng Ya-pi-ni,
mai guh veng Ya-seu-thu,
khaix an mai sum veng pa kaux cong ot ka dex Ya-seu-thu mai ka haktiex Fe-li-see tang tauh.
7 Siyiex teem coi nawh puing pex pui Fe-li-see,
pui A-la-pawx mai pui Mi-wu-ni pa ot ka haktiex Ku-eu Pa-lix.
8 Pui Ya-meung tawx keen ka pui Wu-si-ya.
Riang rao nawh ting lhaong,
mai min ceu kaux nawh hoik tauh hu dee singaix hoek haktiex Ai-cix heue.
9 Wu-si-ya son sang nied tix kieh kaing yuh ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung kaux cong,
hoik sum nyiex lhaong koe ka Rung Siviex,
Ku-meung,
mai ka dee kawk guh ding.
10 Nawh kawx sum nyiex gawng mu pa kaux koe ka plak praix veng,
mai kaong ka dung rawm tom nie,
mawh kheu hoik koe nawh khawng brie tom nie ka caong gawng plak klip singaix mai ka haktiex dee lung dee an.
Mawh kheu muh nawh tix sum kaung sum ma,
nawh tawx lox nhie kawn pui sum plix siiet,
sum ka haktiex pa mhawm heue.
11 Wu-si-ya koe pui naing pa ting riang ting rao,
ku bawg ku yam pon hu puing ka naing.
Lai ca rang pui naing mawh Yie-lix cao tiiem lai mai Ma-si-ya cao priex,
cao pa kuang mawh Ha-na-ni-ya cao bung kaing naing nyiex haw simiang.
12 Pui naing mawh cao bung kaing naing ra rheng liah yieh kaux pa thung;
13 pui naing ot greem kix koe tix ngoe mheen aliah rheng phuan yieh kaux,
kix hoik mawh pui pa pon mhawm puing naing ku kaux,
mai pon teem simiang pex ka pui naing pa koeng.
14 Wu-si-ya tawx pui naing cie dieb tie naok,
pliah,
mhok rhiem,
sibex ceep yam yuh naing,
ak puh te,
mai loi simaox nyiak.
15 Ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui pa koe ping nyia pa yuh kaing nawh ot ka nyiex lhaong veng mai ka dee kawk guh veng sau cag koe kah,
mai chix tix puing te khaing dee lhaong,
dheem ka simaox ting.
Min ceu kaux nawh hoik tauh hoek ku dauh ku dee,
riang rao nawh kied ting,
mawh kheu hoik teem coi Siyiex nawh heue.
Kheu ting rhawm lhaong rhi Wu-si-ya tag pon krax ciyang
16 Kheu pa ting riang simiang Wu-si-ya,
hoik kieh nawh nied ot nied ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
tom hoik yaox nawh tix laux leeing.
Nawh phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh laig gleeing nyawng ka piang khu nyawng ka daux nyiex buan.
17 Ya-sa-li-ya cao giex kaing li viex cao giex kaing pa kua tix daix kaux hawt simiang,
18 asang tieb tie ka nawh.
Kix ah nin:
“Wu-si-ya eue,
maix ang koe akhuang tix gleeing nyawng ka Simiang Siyiex;
mawh ngie doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng pa mawh cao giex kaing li pa hoik guah tix singax pa pon yuh kaing in khaing.
Maix lih hu khaing haktiex pa singax singeh in.
Maix hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex;
mai maix ang lai baox pon ka min plak ngai pa tawx nawh ka maix heue.”
19 Wu-si-ya jiet cung ka praok khu nyawng ka daux nyiex buan,
mai taix nawh giex ka kiang ngu nyawng.
Nawh song rhawm ka cao giex kaing li an kix,
ka daux bawg yam an,
piang dong re nawh dum mawx dum an lih koe jhawm saux si pawg.
20 Ya-sa-li-ya mai cao giex kaing li tang kaux yaoh yee ka yaox kaing re simiang.
Kix dhiet nawh lih khaing nyiex buan;
mai nawh tom nied tix lih hu,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik ciyang ka nawh heue.
21 Ju pa kawn koe simiang Wu-si-ya hu sivoe ngai,
kheu jhawm saux in nawh ang singax ka daux been,
ang pon laig ka daux nyiex buan,
mai paung ot ka daux nyiex haw tix khaing;
kaing ting piang meung mawh kawn simex nawh Yaw-thang pa kuang heue.
22 Kawn simex Ya-maw-see cao jhai Yi-sai-yax,
hoik tiiem kaing yuh Wu-si-ya yam giex kaing khawm uik lih.
23 bYam yum Wu-si-ya kix si paung nawh ot ka bang si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang,
mai ang bax ot ka daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang,
mawh kheu koe jhawm saux si pawg nawh.
Kawn simex nawh Yaw-thang plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
