Ca rang cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we
12
Plak greem mawh ca rang cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we;
kix hoik mawh pui pa kaoh khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix tau mai Saw-law-pa-paw kawn simex Sa-la-thiie,
mai Yie-su-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting heue.
2-7 Cao giex kaing li mawh:
Si-lai-ya,
Yie-li-mi,
Yi-see-la,
Ya-ma-li-ya,
Ma-lux,
Ha-thux,
See-cia-ni,
Li-hong,
Mi-li-mawx,
Yi-to,
Cing-toeng,
Ya-pi-yax,
Mi-ya-ming,
Ma-ti-ya,
Pi-ciax,
See-ma-ya,
Yaw-ya-lix,
Yie-ta-ya,
Sa-lux,
Ya-mu,
Si-leu-cia,
mai Yie-ta-ya heue.
Pui in kix mawh simiang ka daux cao giex kaing li bawg yam singaix ju Yie-su-yax heue.
8 Pui Li-we mawh:
Yie-su-yax,
Ping-noex,
Cia-miiex,
See-li-pi,
Yo-tax,
Ma-tha-ni cao pa thung pui rhax lox yaog yaw;
9 pui pa kuang ka daux nyawng cawng rhax lai mawh Pa-pu-cia,
Wu-ni,
mai pui Li-we paox pa yuh kix kaing tau heue.
Doeh ceu cad Yie-su-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting
10 Yie-su-yax mawh keeing Yaw-ya-cing;
Yaw-ya-cing mawh keeing Yi-li-ya-seu;
Yi-li-ya-seu mawh keeing Yie-heu-yie-tax;
11 Yie-heu-yie-tax mawh keeing Yaw-na-tang;
Yaw-na-tang mawh keeing Ya-tu-yax heue.
Cao kaing ceu cad cub pui nyiex cao giex kaing li
12-21 Yam mawh Yaw-ya-cing cao giex kaing li pa ting,
cao giex kaing li in plak greem,
mawh cao kaing ceu cad cub pui nyiex cao giex kaing li:
Mi-la-ya ceu pui Si-lai-ya,
Ha-na-ni-ya ceu pui Yie-li-mi,
Mi-su-lang ceu pui Yi-see-la,
Yaw-ha-nang ceu pui Ya-ma-li-ya,
Yaw-na-tang ceu pui Mi-li-kux,
Yaw-seux ceu pui See-pa-ni,
Ya-na ceu pui Ha-ling,
Si-leu-khai ceu pui Mi-la-yaw,
Sa-cia-li-yax ceu pui Yi-to,
Mi-su-lang ceu pui Cing-toeng.
Si-ci-lix ceu pui Ya-pi-yax,
pui an ceu pui Mi-na-ming,
Pi-leu-thaix ceu pui Maw-ya-ti,
Sa-mu-yax ceu pui Pi-ciax.
Yaw-na-tang ceu pui See-ma-ya.
Ma-theu-nai ceu pui Yaw-ya-lix,
Wu-si ceu pui Yie-ta-ya,
Si-paw ceu pui Ya-mu,
Cia-lai ceu pui Sa-lai,
ceu pui Ya-mu pa kaux Si-paw,
Ha-sa-pi-ya ceu pui Si-leu-cia,
Na-thang-yiex ceu pui Yie-ta-ya khankix heue.
Pa tiiem ka cao giex kaing li mai ka cub pui nyiex Li-we
22 Yam mawh Yi-li-ya-seu,
Yie-heu-yie-tax,
Yaw-ha-nang,
Ya-tu-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting,
cub nyiex pui Li-we mai cub nyiex cao giex kaing li,
hoik tiiem tix deuh tix lih koe.
Lai pa tiiem tix deuh in,
mai hoik tiiem tix si dauh yam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng Paw-see heue.
23 Viang mawh nan,
cao kaing nyiex pui Li-we pa tiiem ot daux lai simiang,
hoik die ngie ka baux ju Yaw-ha-nang kawn saux Yi-li-ya-seu heue.
Guah kaing pa yuh son nyiex buan
24 Ka greem krax pa duk thung Ha-sa-pi-ya,
See-li-pi,
Yaw-su-yax,
Ping-noex,a
Cia-miiex,
pui Li-we tix cub hoik tix cub khrawm paox tix,
mai hawt awm lox pa krai pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
pa mawh Ta-wue,
tix bawg ra cub,
lai veeh ngai tix plak paox,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex heue.
25 Plak greem pui pau nyiex buan,
pa pau ka nyiex krao siviex nyiex buan mawh:
Ma-tha-ni,
Pa-pu-cia,
Eu-pa-ti-yax,
Mi-su-lang,
Ta-meung,
Ya-kux heue.
26 Pui in kix mawh baux ju Yaw-ya-cing kawn simex Yie-su-yax,
kawn saux Yaw-sa-ta;
yam an kawx mawh nang baux ju Ni-si-mi simiang haktiex,
mai baux ju Yi-see-la cao giex kaing li pui pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai heue.
Ni-si-mi tan lu guh ding
27 Yam tan lu guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kok pui Li-we lih khaing dee ot kix,
hoek yuh poi krax tan lu.
Kix teei lox rhax krax yaog yaw,
chiang,
lox ting lox siao hoek tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
28 Cub nyiex pui Li-we pa rhax lai kaoh khaing haktiex pa ot sivie siveng Yie-lu-sa-leung yaong pa ot vung viang Ni-thaw-fax,
29 jauh tix khrawm paox tix ka haktiex Paw Cie-cia,
Cia-pa,
Ya-ma-fu khankix.
30 Cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we yuh been pa singax singeh son to kaux kix mai pui hun,
khawm siviex veng,
guh veng heue.
31 Aux khrawm simiang pui Yo-tax ka piang choek guh veng,
nhie kix guah pui koe ra cub ting,
mai hawt tix lai ling vung viang veng,
yaog yaw Siyiex heue.
Ka daux cub tix hu plak dawm,
ot ka piang choek guh veng,
mai veeh tix hu ka plak sivoe ka dee La-ci-meung.b
32 Heu-sa-ya hu khaix pui pa kied chix rhax lai khankix,
mai ka plak khaix nawh,
koe simiang pui Yo-tax tix plak pa hawt nawh.
33-35 Pa baox hu khaix khankix mawh cao giex kaing li pa phlu teet,
Ya-sa-li-ya,
Yi-see-la,
Mi-su-lang,
Yo-tax,
Piien-ya-ming,
See-ma-ya,
mai Yie-li-mi.
Pa baox hu khaix paox mawh Sa-cia-li-yax;
nawh mawh kawn simex Yaw-na-tang,
kawn saux See-ma-ya.
Tax du nawh mawh khawm ceu cad Ya-sa cub pui nyiex Ma-tha-ni,
Mi-kai-yax,
mai Sa-kheux pa mawh ceu pui Ya-sa.
36 Ceu cad cub pui nyiex pui tang kaux an kix pa hawt khaix nawh:
mawh See-ma-ya,
Ya-sha-lix,
Mi-la-lai,
Ci-la-lai,
Ma-aix,
Na-thang-yiex,
Yo-tax,
mai Ha-na-ni.
Kix hoik viex kraung ting siao pa tauh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang Ta-wue an.
Mawh Yi-see-la pui pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai pa duk thung cub caw in hu.
37 Ka Chuan-meung,
kix hu ka piang glawk bawng pa phlat hu plak veng Ta-wue,
kawn laig ka daux nyiex haw simiang Ta-wue,
khaix an deeh hoek ka guh veng dee lih Siviex Rawm pa koe ka plak lih singaix ka veng heue.
38 Cub caw an pa yaog yaw tix cub hu plak goix,
ka piang choek guh ding sivoe,
aux hu tau mai pui hun tix plak.
Ex tiang ka Lui-lo,
mai hoek ka dee vieh ding,
39 mai jauh tix yaog tix hu khaing dee an,
laig daux Yi-fa-liien-meung,
Yie-sa-na-meung,c
Siviex Kax,
Ha-nang-yiex-lo,
Pai-lo,
tom hoek Siviex Yung.
Ex hoik naox lai ling tix ka dee ot siviex pa pau nyiex buan heue.
40 Yuh nin,
cub pui pa yaog yaw Siyiex khawm ra plak hoik hoek ka mee haktiex nyiex buan heue.
Lheu khaing simiang pa hu tau mai aux,
41 cub caw pa hu tau mai aux mawh khawm cao giex kaing li pa phlu teet plak greem:
Yi-li-ya-cing,
Ma-si-ya,
Mi-na-ming,
Mi-kai-ya,
Yi-li-yaw-nai,
Sa-cia-li-yax,
Ha-nang-ni-yax.
42 Pa koe khaix kix mawh Ma-si-ya,
See-ma-ya,
Yi-li-ya-sa,
Wu-si,
Yaw-ha-nang,
Ma-ci-ya,
Yi-lang,
Yi-shiex.
Yi-see-la-si-ya thung pui pa kied chix rhax lai mai rhax lox rhax pa ting si num heue.
43 Ka daux singaix an,
ex tan lu krax tan tom nie;
pui haoh pui hun kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu Siyiex hoik kieh kix gaux rhawm tete.
Bun miex mai kawn nyawm khankix kawx yuh poi gaux rhawm tau mai kix.
Lox gaux rhawm gaux rhi Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mhong tom dee singai heue.
Rien khre pa sang lo yam yuh krax krub
44 Yam an,
koe pui tix blah tawx kix pau nyiex,
pa mawh dee deuh nghox koi pa liang tix pon,
mai plix khaox plix ox khawm ceu pa tan lu kao son tix son.
Pui in kix hoik mawh pa kuang pa kaoh khaing pa dex nyiex yaong pa yuh kaing haktiex,
pa khrawm tix priex khawng tan pa hoik tat lox tat jhawm prawng tix deuh,
son sang tawx ka cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we.
Can pui Yo-tax khawm uik kied khaw rhawm ka cao giex kaing li mai ka pui Li-we,
45 dMawh kheu hawt kix tix priex ka gum lox tawx Siyiex,
yuh ka been pa singax singeh mai ka been tang ceu.
Pui pa kied chix rhax lai mai pui pa pau nyiex buan kawx hawt tix yuh ka been pa hoik tat simiang Ta-wue mai kawn simex nawh Saw-law-meung.
46 Jauh ka singaix baux ju simiang Ta-wue mai ka singaix baux ju Ya-sa pa koe ping nyia son lox rhax,
pui pa kied chix rhax lai khankix hoik ca dawg mawh lox rhax pa ah pui cao kaing pa rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex.
47 Singaix baux ju Saw-law-pa-paw mai Ni-si-mi,
pui Yi-seu-liex ku singaix tag caw tix tawx khawng phak ka pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan mai pui pau nyiex buan.
Pui haoh pui hun tawx khawng phak krax tan pa khaw pui Li-we tix pon;
pui Li-we baox tawx khawng phak krax tan pa khaw cao giex kaing li khankix tix pon heue.
