Ya-loeng Yuh krax tan lu
9
Yam hoek daux daix singaix,
pa mawh singaix nghoik son hoik tawx kix ara,
Maw-si khrawm ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
mai kuad song ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hoek.
2 Nawh krai ka Ya-loeng ah nin:
“Maix viex kawn moi mhaing pa loig tix mu hoek,
kieh mawh son Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
mai kawn yung mhaing pa loig tix mu,
kieh mawh son Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
3 Khaix an,
maix nhie pui Yi-seu-liex viex kawn pex mhaing tix mu hoek,
kieh mawh son Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
mai baox viex kawn moi mhaing pa loig mai kawn yung mhaing pa loig tix ceu tix mu hoek,
pa hoik naog tix num,
kieh mawh son Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
4 kawn moi mhaing tix mu mai kawn yung mhaing tix mu,
kieh mawh son Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
Pex tan Krax-tan Nghox-koi in kix pa hoik klao tau ka bee sime ka Simiang Siyiex.
Pex tag caw hawt tix yuh ka grawng ceu in khawm uik,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tag sang lih bleeh ka pex ka daux in singaix heue.”
5 Kheu nan,
kix tom viex can pa hoik tawm Maw-si kix viex hoek khawm uik ka sivoe nyiex gox buan man pa singax singeh;
cub kawn pui khrawm tix ot dee an,
son sang krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
6 Maw-si ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie pex yuh ceu in kix khawm uik,
mawh son sang jhi pex yaox ka min plak ngai tix heue.”
7 aKhaix an,
nawh ah nin ka Ya-loeng:
“Maix hu ka sivoe khu dee tan,
mai tan ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap mai khawm Krax-tan Pa-gleeing;
yuh krax pa pluat ploi phi map son to kaux maix mai son pui khankix.
Maix caw hawt tix yuh ka gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex mai tan ka krax tan an,
son pa pluat ploi ka phi map kawn pui heue.”
8 Kheu nan,
Ya-loeng hu ka sivoe khu dee tan,
mai pauh kawn moi mhaing kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap ka son to kaux tix.
9 Kawn simex nawh khankix viex nham hoek ka sivoe nawh,
nawh tom cub giien taix tix ka daux nham,
kraix ot ka piang rung khu dee tan,
dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog,
hieh nham pa kawn deuk an ka piang jaong khu dee tan.
10 Khaix an,
nawh hawt awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
gleeing tix tan ka khlawp bee,
sok lax ra mu,
mai tawm dee blawn mhawm khankix khawm uik ka piang khu dee tan.
11 Nawh baox viex hak moi mai nex moi hu gleeing ka plak praix nyiex bang heue.
12 Nawh baox pauh ka khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-gleeing son to kaux tix.
Kawn simex nawh khankix viex nham hoek plak sivoe nawh,
nawh tom phruah nham an khankix hawt vung viang piang khu dee tan.
13 Khaix an,
kix viex kaing mai khik nex tawx ka nawh,
mai nawh tom deuh an khankix ka piang khu dee tan mai gleeing kah.
14 Khaix an,
nawh si ghrah kraung vaig mai ba khawn caong plak kreue,
mai deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai gleeing tix tan ka heue.
15 Khaix an,
Ya-loeng tan krax tan son pui khankix.
Nawh viex kawn pex mhaing Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son pui khankix hoek,
mai pauh kah,
khaix an yuh kawn pex mhaing an awm pa hoik tan ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son to kaux tix phaox kah.
16 Nawh baox viex Krax-tan Pa-gleeing hoek,
mai tan awm pa hoik koe daux lox tat jhawm prawng.
17 Nawh kawx baox tan ka Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai teei mien tix jhawp deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan,
gleeing kah.
(Lheu khaing pa tan kix ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ku ngawb,
mai kawn baox tan ka krax tan in.)
18 bNawh pauh ka moi mhaing mai yung mhaing,
mai tan ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim son pui khankix.
Kawn simex nawh khankix viex nham hoek ka sivoe ngaix nawh,
nawh tom phruah nham hawt vung viang khu dee tan.
19 Ya-loeng teei ka khlawp bee moi mhaing an mai yung mhaing an khawm uik,
20 mai deuh ot ka piang naog khawng brie tan an,
viex hoek ka piang khu dee tan.
Nawh tom gleeing khlawp bee ka piang khu dee tan,
21 mai hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Maw-si,
teei ka nex naok mai ba khawn caong plak kreue,
kieh mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
22 cKhaix hoik tan Ya-loeng ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
nawh tom yaog taix tix mai nawb buan ka piang pui hun,
khaix an mai deeh lih khaing ka piang khu dee tan.
23 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng laig ka daux nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
mai baox deeh lih khaing tan,
mai nawb buan ka piang pui khankix.
Yam an,
min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex plak jhi tix ka pui khawm uik.
24 Dum mawx dum an,
Simiang Siyiex ploi ngu lih gleeing ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing an mai ka khlawp bee pa ot ka piang khu dee tan.
Yam yaox pui khankix ka riang su sai an,
kix kok yo mai gup ngai tix piang tiex khawm uik heue.
