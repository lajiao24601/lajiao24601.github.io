Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Wu-si-ya
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 26.1–23)
15
Yam giex simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux ka kaing daux tix nga aliah num,
kawn simex Ya-ma-shiex pa kaux Wu-si-ya mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
2 Yam an,
Wu-si-ya koe ju kao liah num;
nawh giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix phuan ra num.
Ceu kaux miex nawh mawh Yie-kheu-li-ya,
mawh pui Yie-lu-sa-leung.
3 Wu-si-ya hoik hawt ka kaing pa yuh keeing tix,
mai yuh kaing pa mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
4 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak,
kawn pui ca kawn yuh krax tan lu gleeing nyawng bhawng dee an.
5 Simiang Siyiex tawx krax laux leeing hoek ka piang Wu-si-ya,
mai kieh nawh pon jhawm saux si pawg,
ka daux ju nawh kha rhawm tix ka jhawm saux in.
Nawh paung ot ka daux nyiex haw cie to kaux tix,
kaing ting ka piang meung,
mawh kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yaw-thang cao prawng heue.
6 Kaing yuh Wu-si-ya tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
7 aYam hoik yum nawh,
kix si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang ka veng Ta-wue;
kawn simex nawh Yaw-thang plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Sa-cia-li-ya
8 Yam giex simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Wu-si-ya ka kaing daux tix ngoe daix num,
kawn simex Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux pa kaux Sa-cia-li-ya mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hoik giex kaing ka piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax liah khix.
9 Nawh hoik awm tax du tix yuh nan,
yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
hawt ka kaing pa ang mhawm pa hoik yuh kawn simex Ni-pa pa mawh simiang Yie-law-paw-ang,
thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map.
10 Kawn simex Ya-pi pa kaux Sa-long tieh vheen ka nawh,
mai klauh nawh yum ka haktiex Yi-paw-liienb,
mai tud tix mawh simiang pa mawh nawh heue.
11 Kaing yuh Sa-cia-li-ya tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
12 dSimiang Siyiex hoik sax ah nin ka simiang Yie-hu:
“Kawn praix maix tom hoek pon juc,
tag sang mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.”
Gum lox in hoik lih tix mawh heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Sa-long
13 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Wu-si-ya ka kaing daux tix ngoe dim num,
kawn simex Ya-pi pa kaux Sa-long mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hoik giex kaing piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax tix khix heue.
14 Kawn simex Cia-tix pa kaux Mi-na-shien,
yaog tix hu khaing Teu-sa hoek ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
klauh ka Sa-long yum,
mai tud tix mawh simiang pa mawh nawh.
15 Kaing yuh Sa-long tang ceu khankix,
khawm krax pa tieh vheen nawh,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
16 Yam hoek Mi-na-shien khaing Teu-sa,
nawh pauh Thi-fe-sax mai pui daux veng yum ka daux krax khawm uik,
khawm pui pa ot vung viang haktiex an khawm pon daog kawx hoik pauh yum,
mawh kheu pui pa ot daux veng an ang bax ap tix ang pex ka nawh.
Nawh kawx hoik pih ka vaig can bun nghe khawm uik heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Mi-na-shien
17 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Wu-si-ya ka kaing daux tix ngoe dim num,
kawn simex Cia-tix pa kaux Mi-na-shien mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hoik giex kaing ka piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax kao num.
18 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh kawn simex Ni-pa simiang Yie-law-paw-ang;
Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ju nawh,
hoik thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map.
19 Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
Mi-na-shien tawx nawh cie mau luah loe mheen pon rheng kongcing,
son sang pon krax pa joh jhan nawh,
mai kieh simiang mawh nawh kaux cong.
20 Mau in kix mawh mau pa tud rhim Mi-na-shien khaing pui Yi-seu-liex pui pa koe mau khankix;
nawh khin pui ku kaux tawx mau luah tix kaux tix phuan plah.
Khaix hoik pon Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux ka mau,
nawh deeh ing ka meung haktiex to kaux tix heue.
21 Kaing yuh Mi-na-shien tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
22 Yam hoik yum nawh;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Pi-cia-sia plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pi-cia-sia
23 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Wu-si-ya ka kaing daux tix phuan num,
kawn simex Mi-na-shien pa kaux Pi-cia-sia mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hoik giex kaing ka piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax ra num.
24 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh kawn simex Ni-pa pa mawh simiang Yie-law-paw-ang,
thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map.
25 Simiang naing pui Pi-cia-sia,
kawn simex Li-ma-lix pa kaux Pi-cia,
viex pui pa hoek khaing Ci-liex tix phuan kaux tieh vheen,
mai klauh Pi-cia-sia yum ka brawk nyiex haw simiang Sa-ma-li-yax,
tud rhim tix mawh simiang pa mawh nawh heue.
26 Kaing yuh Pi-cia-sia tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pi-cia
27 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Wu-si-ya ka kaing daux tix phuan ra num,
kawn simex Li-ma-lix pa kaux Pi-cia mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hoik giex kaing ka piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax tix nga num.
28 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hoik hawt kaing ang mhawm pa yuh kawn simex Ni-pa pa mawh simiang Yie-law-paw-ang,
thung pui Yi-seu-liex yuh ka phi map heue.
29 Yam kawn giex Pi-cia ka kaing,
Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux hoik pon teei ka Yi-yuing veng Ya-paw Paw Ma-cia,
veng Ya-naw,
veng Ci-ti-see mai veng Sha-saw khankix,
mai kawn koe haktiex Ci-liex,
haktiex Cia-li-lix,
mai haktiex Na-fu-tha-lix khankix,
khaix an mai kawn baox giex tix viex kawn pui hu ka haktiex Ya-sux heue.
30 Yam giex simiang pui Yo-tax kawn simex Wu-si-ya pa kaux Yaw-thang ka kaing daux tix nga num,
kawn simex Yi-la pa kaux Heu-si-yax tieh vheen ka simiang Pi-cia,
klauh nawh yum,
mai tud rhim tix mawh simiang pa mawh nawh.
31 Kaing yuh Pi-cia tang ceu khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-thang
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 27.1–9)
32 Yam mawh kawn simex Li-ma-lix pa kaux Pi-cia simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux ra num,
kawn simex Wu-si-ya pa kaux Yaw-thang mawh simiang pui Yo-tax.
33 Yam an,
Yaw-thang koe ju tix nga phuan num;
nawh giex kaing Yie-lu-sa-leung kao liah num.
Miex nawh mawh Yie-lu-sa,
mawh kawn bun Sa-tu.
34 Yaw-thang hawt ka kaing mhawm pa yuh keeing tix,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
35 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax yieh tix tik ka nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak,
kawn pui ca kawn yuh krax tan lu gleeing nyawng bhawng ka dee an.
Yaw-thang sum siviex plak laong ka daux nyiex buan heue.
36 Kaing yuh Yaw-thang tang ceu hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax.
37 Yam kawn giex nawh kaing,
Simiang Siyiex ploi simiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Li-shuing mai simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pi-cia puing ka pui Yo-tax.
38 Yam hoik yum Yaw-thang,
kix si paung nawh ot daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang ka veng Ta-wue;
kawn simex nawh Ya-ha-see plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
