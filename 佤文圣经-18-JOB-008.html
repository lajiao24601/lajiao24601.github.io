8
Khaix an,
pui Su-yax pa kaux Pi-leu-ta krai lox ah nin:
2 Lox yuh nin maix tag sang krai miex glang lie?
In kix hoik mawh lox giex lox going khawm uik!
3 Khex sang pu pliak Siyiex ceu pa mawh mai ceu pa ang mawh lie?
Khex sang lieh lheu cao pa ting piang khawm uik ka ceu pa rauh pa see lie?
4 Kawn simex kawn bun maix tee rien tix phit lud ka nawh,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom ciyang ka kix awm pa khaw kix tix pon.
5 Maix caw veeh tix hoek ka Siyiex,
khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik.
6 Phan maix rug singax mawh pui rauh see tete,
Siyiex tag chix teem ka maix,
mai deeh tawx kaung ma maix mai nyiex ma maix ka maix.
7 Pa sang tawx Siyiex ka maix,
kawn sang nie khaing pa hoik som nai maix khawm uik heue.
 
8 Viex maix keud tix yaox ka ping nyia cuyi ju pui dix dix,
mai jawg bre si kaw tix yaox ka rhawm keud tax du pex khankix.
9 Ju ex kied dawt,
mai ang tawng tid tix;
ex hoik grai,
awm pang peue pa hoik phai luan hu ka piang haktiex.
10 Kieh ju pui phaox ka sidiex gau ka maix heue;
mai bre tix nghiet ka lox pa hoik krai kix heue.
 
11 Pang jo chix pon kuah ka dee ang rawm koe lie?
Gawng priem chix pon tang ot khaing dung rawm lie?
12 Yam hoik hit rawm,
an kix ran kroh sivoe,
mawh kheu kawn awn gawng kix,
mai ang pon tix vawg tix siao.
13 Pui pa ang krub ka Siyiex tag sang awm pang jo yuh nan;
yam hoik piawm kix ka Siyiex,
mai krax keud pot jun pot kix tag grai.
14 Kix hoik yum rhawm ka keei pa chix dut an;
kix hoik een rhawm tix ka nyiex greeih.
15 Kix een rhawm tix ka keei taing,
mai keei taing sang chix pon joh jhan kix lie?
Kix giex ka nyiex greeih,
mai nyiex greeih sang chix pon kieh kix kaux cong lie?
 
16 Pui pa caox pa caig ot ka greem pa riang singaix awm rib riem khankix,
lhax kak khaox kied nie,
mai tawx tix tom hoek ka brawk plak praix.
17 Riah kix vung vhien tix ka rang simaox,
mai mad tix kauxa ot ka si nax simaox.
18 Khaix an,
reeig nawh lih,
mai ang koe pui pa tawng hoik tawx riah tix ot ka dee an.
19 Mawh tete,
an hoik mawh ngie krax gaux rhawm cie pui pa caox pa caig khaing;
yam in hoik kieh pui tang kaux loh tix yuh ka kaing yuh kix heue.
 
20 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex ang chix tik vud ka pui pa ang koe dee lud loe;
mai ang doh chix teem coi nang ka pui pa caox pa caig khankix.
21 Nawh tag sang kieh maix baox dee gaux rhawm tix nyiah;
nawh tag sang kieh maix baox dee gaux rhawm.
22 Nawh hoik kieh pui pa koeng khoi ka maix hoek krax kaig krax tua;
nyiex ma pui pa caox pa caig tag sang hoek krax laux krax leeing heue.
