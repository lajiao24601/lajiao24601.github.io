Lox rhax krax yaog yaw
33
Can pui rauh pui see khawm uik tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi ka Simiang Siyiex;
can pui pa yuh kaing pa khaw caw khawm uik tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
2 Tag caw tix tauh ting yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
teei ting pa koe lah rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
3 Tag caw tix rhax lai rhax pa khraox ka nawh;
tag caw tix tauh tom sug tix,
mai ah lox tix tom ting heue.
 
4 Lox Simiang Siyiex mawh tete;
kaing yuh nawh mawh pa kaux sijax.
5 Nawh muh pa rauh pa see mai pa khaw caw;
krax muh nawh hoik naog ka piang haktiex heue.
 
6 Rao ma hoik phan tix lih mawh ka daux lox tawx Simiang Siyiex;
riang khix riang singaix simuing kawx,
hoik phan tix lih mawh ka daux lox nawh.
7 Nawh kieh rawm sigrit sigra khrawm tix ot tix dauh;
nawh khrawm mox deuh rawm sigrit sigra ot daux krao heue.
 
8 Piang haktiex khawm uik tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex!
Kawn pui piang haktiex tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex!
9 Nawh ploi lox tix lih,
mai haktiex lih tix koe;
nawh tawx lox tix,
mai ku ceu ku cawng khawm uik lih tix koe heue.
 
10 Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh laux ka krax keud pa caox meung ka piang haktiex;
nawh kieh krax keud kawn pui piang haktiex ang pon tix lih mawh kaing pa mhawm.
11 Krax pa keud pa sawn Simiang Siyiex koe leeing ju leeing phan;
alo nawh ang chix tieh loh tix kaing mheen ju.
12 Meung pa yaog Simiang Siyiex mawh meung cie Siyiex pa kied koe buan tete!
Pui pa hoik laug nawh mawh cie to kaux tix khankix kied koe buan tete heue!
 
13 Simiang Siyiex kok jhak khaing daux rao ma,
mai yaox ka kawn pui khawm uik.
14 Nawh ngawm khaing ka piang dang ngeung tix,
jhak kawn pui ka piang haktiex.
15 Nawh hoik phan yuh kawn pui koe rhawm keud dong sawn ku kaux,
hoik tawng sidawng ka kaing pa yuh kix khawm uik heue.
 
16 Simiang ang pon krax pa pex ka daux pa een rhawm ka kru naing pa ting riang ting rao;
pui naing ang klawd ju ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka pa ting riang rao.
17 Pa een rhawm tix ka brung naing ang pon krai grawng sang puing pex tix;
riang rao brung ang pon taoh ka pui heue.
 
18 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh khankix;
nawh kia ngien ka pui pa een rhawm tix ka krax muh nawh khankix.
19 Nawh taoh kix luat luan khaing krax yum,
yam sibhawm praix,
nawh hoik deuh kix klawd ju heue.
 
20 Ex een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex;
nawh mawh krax pa teem coi ka ex,
mawh cao pa kia ngien ka ex.
21 Ka daux rhawm rhi ex hoik naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm ex ka ceu kaux pa singax singeh nawh heue.
 
22 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yix een rhawm tix ka maix;
kieh krax muh maix ot tau mai yix jheeng jheeng heue.
