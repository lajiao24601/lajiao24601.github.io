Ca maox Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha
2
Yam mawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang ka daux ra num,
nawh ca maox tix ceu;
ca mao in kieh rhawm rhi nawh suk sag,
ang laig it laig tom.
2 aMawh kheu nan,
nawh tom ploi pui hu kok cao pa chix si pawk,
cao chix si meed,
pui yuh phi,
pui pa chix sawn singaix khankix hoek,
son sang kah ka ca maox nawh.
Yam hoik hoek kix khawm uik,
kix cung ka plak sivoe ngai simiang,
3 simiang krai ka kix ah nin:
“Aux ca maox tix ceu,
rhawm rhi aux suk sag tete,
aux simie tix tawng dee bleeh ca maox in heue.”
4 Kix siao lox Ya-langb pawk ka simiang,
mai ah nin:
“Vawng lang ju lang phan heue!
Krai ca maox maix ka yix liak,
son sang kah yix son maix heue.”
5 Simiang ah nin:
“Aux hoik tat yuh nin:
pex tag caw tix krai ca maox in ka aux,
khaix an kah dee bleeh;
phan pex ang pon kah,
aux sang tat ka taix caong pex,
mai kieh nyiex ma pex lih mawh dee riem.
6 Phan pex pon krai ca maox aux lih,
khaix an pon kah dee bleeh,
aux tag sang tawx khawng phak ka pex,
mai baox thiem thie tix tawx min plak ngai ka pex.
Yam in,
pex krai ca maox aux lih,
mai kah aux mhong heue.”
7 Kix baox pawk lox simiang ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix Vawng krai ca maox in ka yix liak,
son sang kah yix son maix heue.”
8 Ka daux bawg yam an,
simiang song rhawm ah nin:
“Aux hoik dawg tawng,
pex hoik keud tix sang dha bawg yam,
mawh kheu hoik tawng pex grawng pa tat aux tix deuh.
9 Phan pex ang krai ca maox aux lih,
aux koe ngie krax keud tix ceu sang yuh ka pex.
Pex hoik ra lai tawx lox ka paox tix,
mai keud tix sang pie lhien ka aux.
Pex ce bawg yam kawn sang chix tieh loh tix.
Yam in nied krai ca maox aux lih,
aux tom taug tawng grawng sang pon kah pex ka ca maox tix heue.”
10 Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Ka piang haktiex ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon krai lih ka ceu pa sang tawng Vawng.
Ang sax koe Vawng tix kaux kawx pa mhaing lox pa yuh nin ka pui pa chix si pawk,
ka pui pa chix si meed,
mai ka pui pa chix sawn singaix khankix;
viang hoik mawx Vawng pa blawn ting,
pa blawn koe siawp sibrawm kawx ang sax koe.
11 Can lox pa chawk pa mhaing Vawng kied mawh pa nu tete.
Lheu khaing siyiex,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon pawk,
viang mawh nan siyiex ang ot ka si nax kawn pui heue.”
12 Simiang kied song rhawm tete,
nawh tom tawx lox nhie pui pauh ka can pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi pa ot ka daux nyiex ma simiang yum khawm uik.
13 Mawh kheu nan simiang tawx lox hu,
sang pauh ka can pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi yum khawm uik,
mawh khawm Tang-yi-li mai paox grawm nawh khankix heue.
Siyiex krai dee bleeh ca maox ka Tang-yi-li
14 Tang-yi-li hu ka cao kaing pui pa tawx simiang lox nhie kix mawh cao giex kaing naing pa kaux Ya-liiex.
15 Tang-yi-li viex lox awn lox tiiem hoek chawk cheng ka Ya-liiex,
yuh ka mawx simiang lai tawx lox pa lang teung yuh nin.
Ya-liiex tom krai grawng kaing in ka nawh heue.
16 Tang-yi-li khiox khaw simiang dha ka bawg yam dum mawx dum an,
son sang koe bawg yam nawh tix kah dee bleeh ca maox an son simiang.
17 Khaix an Tang-yi-li deeh ing ka nyiex,
mai krai grawng kaing in ka paox grawm tix Ha-na-ni-ya,
Mi-sa-lix,
mai ka Ya-sa-li-ya khankix.
18 Nawh khiox khaw kix rawg buan ka Siyiex piang rao ma,
khiox khaw nawh caox rhawm ka kix,
tawx kix tawng ca maox pa raux pa mox in,
mai paw kieh kix yaox tix yum tau mai pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi pa deuk ka daux Pa-pi-loeng khankix.
19 Ka daux bo an,
Tang-yi-li pon tawng pa raux pa mox in ka daux riang su sai.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh yaog yaw Siyiex ka piang rao ma ah nin:
20 Kieh ceu kaux Siyiex pon krax pa yaog yaw,
ang chix uik leeing ju leeing phan heue!
Mawh kheu mawh nawh pa grum naog mai ping nyia cuyi,
pa grum naog mai siawp sibrawm.
21 Nawh tieh loh ka bawg yam mai yam raux yam rung;
nawh teei tix tik ka simiang,
nawh yaog tix sum ka simiang;
nawh tawx krax lhak lhiao ka pui pa lhak lhiao,
mai tawx ping nyia cuyi ka pa pui pa lhak lhiao.
22 Nawh jhi ka kaing pa raux pa mox;
nawh tawng yung sidawng ka kaing pa glip;
pa riang ot tau mai nawh ang jud.
23 Siyiex tax du aux eue,
aux ah lox pa ting buan son ka maix,
yaog yaw ka maix.
Maix tawx aux koe ping nyia cuyi mai riang rao;
maix hoik nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux,
hoik krai grawng sang kah aux yuh ka mawx ka ca maox simiang heue.
Tang-yi-li kah ca maox simiang
24 Mawh kheu nan,
Tang-yi-li hu ka cao kaing pui pa tawx simiang lox nhie kix mawh cao pa pauh ka pui pa kaux Ya-liiex,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Khiox khaw maix paw vi pauh kix yum;
viex aux hu ka simiang,
aux sang kah ca maox son simiang heue.”
25 Ya-liiex viex Tang-yi-li hu ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha dum mawx dum an,
mai ah nin ka simiang:
“Aux yaox pui pa chix kah ca maox Vawng tix kaux ka daux pui Yo-thaix pa giex laung pui khankix heue.”
26 Simiang krai ka Tang-yi-li (baox kok ceu kaux mawh Paw-thi-sa-sha) ah nin:
“Maix pon krai ka ca maox aux lih,
mai pon kah ka dee bleeh lie?”
27 Tang-yi-li pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
cao chix si meed,
yuh phi,
pa chix si pawk,
mai pa chix sawn singaix khankix,
ang pon krai maix mhong sidawng ka kaing in tix kaux kawx;
28 viang mawh nan,
koe Siyiex piang rao ma tix kaux pa pon jhi lih ka pa raux pa mox,
nawh hoik jhi ka kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka Vawng.
Yam in aux sang kah ka ca maox simiang,
pa mawh riang su sai ca maox nawh yam laig it tix heue.
29 “Yam laig it Vawng,
nawh ca maox ka kaing pa tag sang lih tix hoek.
Siyiex pa jhi pa raux pa mox sang krai kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka simiang.
30 Yam in,
aux kawx hoik pon pa jhi nawh;
in ang mawh kheu lhak lhiao aux khaing pui tang kaux,
mai in hoik mawh Siyiex pa sang kieh Vawng tawng ka dee bleeh ca maox in mai ka rhawm rhi simiang heue.
31 “Vawng eue,
ka daux ca maox riang su sai ka plak sivoe ngai maix koe riang pa ting tix mu,
toh riang pa pu plak tix,
kieh pui ang pon jhak.
32 Kaing riang in yuh tix ka khri,
naok riang in mai plawng taix yuh tix ka mau,
nyoi mai blawg hoik yuh tix ka tawng khawm uik,
33 ba yuh tix ka rhiem,
caong yuh tix ka rhiem mai klao tix tau mai tiex glaox.
34 Yam jhak maix ka riang an,
koe simaox pa kied ting tix khlawp pa ang pui bax saik,
jang lia tix hoek khaing gawng rang,
lauh ka riang an,
mai kieh caong rhiem riang an pa klao tix tau mai tiex glaox max khawm ra khawn.
35 Tiex,
rhiem,
tawng,
mau,
khri laux leeing dum mawx dum an,
awm phawng nyaox ka daux glauh pauh nghox bawg yam rung praix yuh nan,
bhaung phlu rai rhuat tix hu,
mai ang lai yaox bliah blak pie ceeix kawx.
Viang mawh nan,
simaox pa kied ting an nied ot nied tix ting,
mai tieh tix lih mawh gawng ting tix mu,
pa tawb kheem ka haktiex khawm uik heue.
36 “In mawh ca maox Vawng.
Yam in aux sang kah maix mhong dee bleeh ca maox.
37 Vawng eue,
maix mawh simiang pa blawn ting khaing can simiang pa koe khawm uik.
Siyiex piang rao ma yaog maix mawh Vawng,
mai tawx meung haktiex,
akhuang,
riang rao pa ting,
mai min plak ngai ka maix.
38 Nawh kieh maix giex kaing ka haktiex pa ot kawn pui ka daux,
mai kuang ka sim saik to tiag to tu.
Maix mawh kaing khri riang rao an.
39 Ka daux singaix pa sang hoek khaix maix,
koe meung ting tix mu tag sang lih hoek,
viang mawh nan,
ang ting khaing meung maix;
khaix an,
baox koe meung hoek daux tix loe mu,
pa mawh meung tawng,
sang kuang ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
40 “Khaix an baox lih koe meung ting ka daux pon mu,
nawh kiien awm rhiem yuh nan,
mai pon lauh max ka can kraung ku ceu.
Awm hoik lauh rhiem ka kraung tang ceu khankix max phawng yuh nan,
nawh tag sang lauh ka meung ting pa koe sivoe tix khawm uik laux.
41 Caong mai giien caong pa yaox maix pa mawh rhiem pa klao tix tau mai glaox tag sang jhi mawh meung pa ting tix mu pa sang guah tix;
nawh koe riang rhiem,
mawh kheu mawh nawh rhiem pa klao tix tau mai tiex glaox.
42 Caong rhiem mai giien caong pa klao tix tau mai tiex glaox hoik mawh meung ting an pa tag sang ting lhaong tix blah,
iak riang tix blah.
43 Maix hoik yaox ka rhiem pa klao tix tau mai glaox;
in hoik mawh simiang ka meung ting an pa keud tix sang teei krax mex moeng giex kaing ka piang pui ku ceu,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon yuh,
awm ang rhiem pon klao tix tau mai tiex glaox yuh nan.
44 “Yam kawn yuh simiang an kix cao kaing,
Siyiex ka piang rao ma tag sang sum simiang meung pa ang chix die pa koe leeing ju tix mu.
Simiang meung in pui ang doh pon kuang kah,
mai nawh tag sang tieh tix yuh laux ka can meung pa koe khawm uik,
tag sang koe leeing ju.
45 Pa hoik yaox maix ka simaox pa kied ting an pa ang taix pui bax saik,
jang lia tix lih khaing gawng rang;
maix baox yaox simaox an hoik lauh riang pa yuh tix ka khri,
mau,
tawng,
rhiem,
mai ka tiex glaox.
Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong sang kieh Vawng tawng ka kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh.
Ca maox in mawh tete te lai,
grawng pa kah ka mawh pa khaw ex tix yum rhawm ka heue.”
Simiang tawx khawng phak ka Tang-yi-li
46 Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha gup tix piang tiex krub Tang-yi-li,
mai tawx lox nhie pui tan lu krax tan tok nyawng bhawng.
47 Simiang ah nin:
“Mawh kheu hoik kah maix ka pa raux pa mox in,
aux tawng Siyiex maix mawh Siyiex pa blawn ting khaing Siyiex khawm uik,
mawh simiang piang simiang,
mawh Siyiex pa jhi pa raux pa mox.”
48 Khaix an,
simiang tawx khawng phan lak sawng pa ting ngawh tom nie ka Tang-yi-li,
mai baox yaog nawh ting lhaong,
tawx nawh kuang haktiex Pa-pi-loeng,
mai kieh nawh mawh cao kaing pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi ka daux nyiex simiang.
49 Simiang cu lox pa khiox khaw Tang-yi-li,
mai tawx Sa-teu-la,
Mi-sa,
Ya-paw-ni-keu yuh ka kaing pa kuang ka haktiex Pa-pi-loeng;
Tang-yi-li ca kawn ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang heue.
