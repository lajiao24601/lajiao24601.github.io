Grawng singaix tuah
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.1–8; Lai Ma-kheu 2.23–28)
6
aKa daux singaix tuah tix ngaix,
Ye-sux laig ka daux kaung ma nghox ca;
pui gau nawh caik ka si gleem nghox ca khankix,
mai boi tix ih.
2 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah yaox kah,
mai ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai yuh kaing pa ang cu pui yuh ka daux singaix tuah lie”.
3 bYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Hoik yuh Ta-wue mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh kaing ceu pa tix yam cai rhawm tix,
ang pex bax phat ka lie?
4 cYam laig nawh ka daux nyiex buan Siyiex,
teei tix ih ka khao mun pa tan lu pui ka Siyiex,
mai nawh baox tawx pui pa hawt khaix tix ih nang;
khao mun in lheu khaing cao giex kaing li,
pui mawx ih ka tag sawn mawh pa lheu lox been heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kawn kawn pui hoik mawh simiang ka daux singaix tuah heue.”
Pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux kroh taix moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.9–14; Lai Ma-kheu 3.1–6)
6 Ka daux singaix tuah tang ngaix,
Ye-sux hoek sidiex pui ka daux nyiex sidiex;
ka dee an koe pui tix kaux,
taix khawn dawm nawh hoik kroh.
7 Koe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai pui Fa-li-saix keud tix sang sawg dee lud Ye-sux,
son sang pon tix deuh phi map ka nawh,
mawh kheu nan,
kix tom ot tix jiang ka praok nawh,
mai jhak tix yaox sang pai ang nawh pai jhawm saux pui ka daux singaix tuah.
8 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka krax pa keud kix,
tom ah nin ka pui pa koe jhawm saux kroh taix an:
“Kaoh,
cung ka plak sivoe!”
Pui an tom kaoh cung.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux krai ka kix an nin:
“Aux chawk pex,
phan mawh grawng singaix tuah,
lox tat jhawm prawng ex hoik tat tix deuh yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh yuh kaing mhawm mawh kaing pa caox lie?
Mawh taoh ka ju pui mawh pauh ka ju pui lie?”
10 Nawh kling tix simi ka can pui pa ot praok tix,
khaix an krai ka pui an ah nin:
d “Nyat taix maix rauh heue!”
Pui an nyat taix tix,
taix nawh tom moi heue.
11 Kix kied song rhawm tete,
mai kum paox tix keud sawn grawng sang yuh ka Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux laug ka kawn cau kao ra kaux
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.1–4; Lai Ma-kheu 3.13–19)
12 Koe ka daux tix ngaix,
Ye-sux haok rawg buan ka gawng,
nawh rawg buan dee an ka Siyiex leeing sawm.
13 Yam riang praix,
nawh nhie pui gau tix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
mai laug pui kao ra kaux khaing ka daux kix,
kok kix mawh kawn cau.
14 Kix mawh Si-meung (Ye-sux baox sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Pi-teux) mai pux aik nawh Ang-teu-liex,
Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang,
Fe-lix mai Pa-to-law-mai,
15 Ma-thaix mai To-ma,
Ya-keux pa mawh kawn simex Ya-leu-fe mai “Cub caw pui pa kin jhawm” Si-meung,
16 Yo-tax pa mawh kawn simex Ya-keux pui Cia-liiex pa lih tix mawh kaw pa coh ka Ye-sux khaix an heue.
Pa sidiex Ye-sux mai pa pai nawh ka jhawm saux
(Lai Ma-thaix 4.23–25)
17 Ye-sux mai kawn cau nawh khankix lih tau khaing gawng.
Nawh mai pui gau tom nie cung ka dee si dawm haktiex,
pui hun pa su shiet paox tix kaoh hoek khaing haktiex Yo-thaix,
Yie-lu-sa-leung mai haktiex Thai-eux,
Si-toeng veng pa koe hawt rawm sigrit sigra khankix khrawm paox tix hoek dee an.
18 Son sang nghiet ka lox pa sidiex nawh,
mai simie nawh pai ka jhawm saux kix moi nang.
Pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai ka daux kix kawx hoek nang,
khaix an kix hoik pon yaox tix moi.
19 Pui hun hoik simie tix pu pi ka nawh khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik koe nawh siawp sibrawm pa lih khaing to kaux nawh,
tom pon pai kix moi heue.
Krax gaux rhawm mai krax khi rhawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 5.1–12)
20 Ye-sux kling tix plak ot pui gau tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
pui hot pui sibhawm khankix kied koe buan son heue;
pex mawh pui cie meung Siyiex heue!
21 Pui pa cai rhawm cai rhi yam in kied koe buan son heue;
pex tag sang pon tix sak heue!
Pui pa yiiem grai yam in khankix kied koe buan son heue;
pex tag sang nyiah heue!
22 e“Kheu son kawn kawn pui,
pui tag sang koeng ka pex,
tik vud ka pex map nai ka pex,
kieh pex awm pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig yuh nan,
pex tag sang koe buan son heue!
23 fTax du kix pa koe sivoe an kawx hoik yuh nin ka cao jhai nang.
Ka daux singaix an,
pex caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu piang rao ma tag sang koe buan son pa ting son pex.
24 Viang mawh nan,
yam in pex pa mawh pui pa mi pa koe khankix tag sag yaox krax pa laux leeing,
mawh kheu hoik kub yaox pex ka krax pa gaux rhawm heue!
25 Pui pa sak yam in khankix tag sang yaox krax pa laux leeing,
mawh kheu pex tag sang cai rhawm cai rhi,
Pui pa gaux rhawm pa nyiah yam in khankix tag sang yaox krax pa laux leeing,
mawh kheu pex tag sang laux rhawm yiiem grai heue!
26 “Yam yaog yaw pui ka pex ku kaux,
pex hoik laux leeing,
mawh kheu hoik krai tax du pex lox pa yuh nin ka cao jhai pa pie lhien yuh nan nang heue.”
Krai grawng pa muh ka pui koeng
(Lai Ma-thaix 5.38–48; 7.12)
27 “Viang mawh nan,
pex pa mawh pui nghiet ka lox aux,
aux krai ka pex,
tag caw tix muh ka pui koeng ka pex,
kia ngien ka pui pa koeng ka pex;
28 nawb buan ka pui pa jhax lawg ka pex,
rawg buan son pui pa map nai ka pex.
29 Phan koe pui pa si mang ka si bax maix plak in,
khawm plak an kawx kieh nawh si mang ka heue!
Phan koe pui pa teei hu ka kraung ceep plak praix maix,
pie kraung ceep plak daux maix kawx kieh nawh teei hu heue!
30 Phan pui mhaing tix lo ka maix,
tag caw tix tawx ka nawh;
phan koe pui pa teei kraung khrai maix hu,
paw vi hu sawg deeh hoek.
31 gPex simie pui tang kaux yuh ka mawx ka tix,
pex kawx tag caw tix yuh nang nan ka kix heue.
32 “Phan pex muh ngie ka pui pa muh ka pex khaing,
sang koe buan son yuh ka mawx lie?
Pie khawm pui phi map kawx hoik muh nang ka pui pa muh ka kix yuh nan nang heue!
33 Phan pex chix kia ngien ngie ka pui pa kia ngien ka pex khaing,
sang koe buan son yuh ka mawx lie?
Pie khawm pui phi map kawx hoik chix yuh nin nang heue!
34 Phan pex tawx ngie pui pa koe riang tix plud yaog yaing ka mau khaing,
sang chix koe buan son yuh ka mawx lie?
Pie khawm pui phi map kawx hoik tawx nang pui phi map yaog yaing,
mawh ngie tag sang deeh teei hoek tom kub heue!
35 Ang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix muh ka pui koeng,
mai kia ngien ka kix mhawm mhawm;
tawx pui yaog yaing mau,
mai ang simie tix deeh teei hoek.
Phan yuh nan,
pex tag sang pon khawng phak pa ting,
tag sang lih tix mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
mawh kheu nawh kawx tag sang kia ngien pui pa ang tawng buan son mai pui caox ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix.
36 Pex tag caw tix koe krax pa caox rhawm,
awm koe keeing pex Siyiex krax pa caox rhawm yuh nan heue.”
Paw vi prawng ca ka pui tang kaux
(Lai Ma-thaix 7.1–5)
37 “Paw vi prawng ca ka pui,
Siyiex tom ang prawng ca ka pex;
paw vi prawng ca ka phi map pui,
Siyiex tom ang prawng ca ka phi map pex;
tag caw tix pluat ploi ka pui,
Siyiex tom pluat ploi ka pex.
38 Phan pex teem ka pui,
Siyiex tom tag sang teem ka pex nang,
khaix an teei tong pa ting pa hoik yung yang yeen kah,
hawt pie pon viex pex kah,
tong tom naog tawx ka pex.
Pex siao ka kraung tong ceu pa tix,
Siyiex kawx tag sang siao ka kraung tong ceu an ka pex nang.”
39 hYe-sux krai kix mhong ka lox phek tiiem tix ceu ah nin:
“Pui dug ang pon tuk pui dug lawng krax;
phan yuh nan kiex khawm ra kaux tag sang cawd ka daux o heue.
40 iPui gau ang lheu lhaong khaing sara,
viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik gau nawh tix chix,
nawh tag sang awm sara tix yuh nan heue.
41 “Yuh ka mawx maix lai jhak ka bliah khaox pa koe ka daux tawm ngai pux aik tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang simi ka nuk pa koe ka daux tawm ngai tix lie?
42 Ka daux tawm ngai maix koe nuk kah,
maix sang chix pon krai ka pux aik tix ah nin ‘Pux aik,
kieh aux teei ka bliah khaox pa koe ka daux tawm ngai maix’ lie?
Maix pa mawh pui pa pie lhien,
ran teei nuk pa koe ka daux tawm ngai maix lih sivoe ha,
maix tom tag chix yaox sidawng,
mai pon teei ka bliah khaox pa koe ka daux tawm ngai pux aik tix lih heue.”
Khaox mai plix
(Lai Ma-thaix 7.16–20; 12.33–35)
43 “Khaox pa mhawm ang chix plix ka plix pa caox,
khaox pa caox kawx ang chix plix ka plix pa mhawm;
44 jKhaox pa mhawm mai pa caox hoik pon guah tix tawng ka plix kix sidawng.
Pex ang chix pieh plix ruh ka daux pang kat,
mai ang chix pieh plix shiet ka daux pang kat heeik.
45 kPui mhawm hoik plih lih ka ceu pa mhawm pa deuh tix;
pui caox hoik plih lih ka ceu pa ang mhawm pa deuh tix heue Naog ceu pa tix ka daux rhawm rhi kawn pui,
mai jheeh nawh tag sang krai ceu an lih heue.”
Riah jaong ra ceu
(Lai Ma-thaix 7.24–27)
46 “Yuh ka mawx pex lai kok yo aux ah ‘Simiang eue,
simiang eue’,
viang mawh nan,
ang hawt tix yuh ka gum lox tawx aux lie?
47 Aux krai ka pex,
pui pa hoek ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
nghiet ka gum lox aux mai hawt tix yuh,
tag sang awm pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?
48 Nawh tag sang awm pui pa sum nyiex tix kaux,
pa kaong daux tiex tom raux,
mai sum riah jaong ot ka piang rang simaox.
Yam huan rawm,
klauh ka nyiex an,
viang mawh nan,
nyiex an ang yung yang,
mawh kheu hoik sum tix mhawm kaux cong.
49 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa mhong ka gum lox aux mai ang hawt tix yuh,
tag sang awm pui pa sum nyiex tix kaux pa ang bax sum riah jaong kaux ka piang tiex,
yam hoek rawm tix klauh,
nyiex an goh dum mawx dum an,
mai pa laux leeing nyiex an kied lang teung heue!”
