Pao-law mai kawn cau tang kaux
2
aKhaix hoik luan kao pon num,
aux deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tau mai Pa-na-pa,
mai viex Thi-to hu tau mai tix.
2 Aux haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mawh son sang priex tix hawt ka daux pa plak jhi Simiang Ye-sux;
yam yaox aux ka simiang an kix nong nong tix,
aux krai kix mhong grawng pa sidiex tix gum lox buan ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad.
Aux ang rhawm kieh pa koe tix ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
phan ang mawh nan,
kaing pa yuh tix giex going.
3 Thi-to pa hu tau mai aux,
viang nawh mawh pui Si-lax,
mai pui ang bax khin nawh rieb been pa Pat-hak.
4 Viang mawh nan koe pui tix blah pie lhien tix mawh kawn Khrit,
mai briex tix laig ka daux ceu cao,
nhie nawh rieb been pa Pat-hak.
Pui in kix hoik pu plaik tix laig ka daux ceu cao awm pui jiang yuh nan,
mai sang jiang tix yaox grawng pa pon krax bheeik luan kheu yum rhawm ka Ye-sux Khrit,
kaing ting mawh son keud tix sang kieh ex mawh siliex cawng.
5 Viang mawh nan,
son sang deuh tix een gum lox pa mawh pa meh son pex,
ex ang lai yeung ka cub pui in kix pie ceeix kawx heue.
6 bViang mawh nan,
pui pa een pui mawh simiang pa koe min khankix (simiang mawh kix ting lhaong ang ah,
aux ang yuh tix tawng,
mawh kheu ang Siyiex jhak plak praix prawng ka pui),
pa krai aux mawh nin,
pui pa koe min khankix ang tie bax tawx krax ceu pa gau tid tix ka aux.
7 Deeh tieh tix,
kix hoik yaox grawng pa ap Siyiex tix tawx beeh glawm pa sidiex gum lox buan ka pui tang ceu tang cad ka aux,
awm hoik ap tix tawx beeh glawm pa sidiex gum lox buan ka pui Yo-thaix ka Pi-teux yuh nan.
8 Mawh kheu Siyiex hoik kieh aux lih mawh kawn cau pui tang ceu ka daux pa ting siawp sibrawm tix,
awm hoik lih mawh Pi-teux kawn cau pui Yo-thaix yuh nan.
9 Ya-keux,
Pi-teux,
Yaw-hang,
kix loe kaux pa mawh rong simaox pa koe min ka ceu cao,
kheu hoik tawng Siyiex ap tix tawx beeh glawm pa tang siyox in ka aux,
kix tag yang yheuh ka taix Pa-na-pa mai ka taix aux.
Kheu hoik lih mawh paox pa yuh kaing tau,
yix tom taug cu ah nin:
pa-na-pa mai aux tag caw tix hu yuh kaing ka daux pui tang ceu,
mai kix tom yuh cie tix ka daux pui Yo-thaix.
10 Kix simie ngie aux ca doh keud ka ceu pa lo pui hot sibhawm ka daux pui Yo-thaix;
grawng kaing in hoik mawh pa sau aux riang rao tix yuh ka heue.
Pao-law ne raw Pi-teux ka haktiex Ang-thi-a
11 Khaix an,
Pi-teux hoek ka Ang-thi-a;
mawh kheu hoik sidawng koe ceu pa lud nawh,
aux tom ne raw nawh ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun.
12 Sivoe sang hoek pui pa ploi Ya-keux,
Pi-teux sawm tau mai kawn Khrit pui tang ceu.
Viang mawh nan yam hoik hoek pui an kix,
nawh tom deeh plak khaix tix,
ang lai pon sawn tau mai kix,
mawh kheu lhat nawh ka pui khankix pa nhie pui tang ceu rieb ka been pa Pat-hak.
13 Kawn Khrit pui Yo-thaix tang kaux kawx hawt ka Pi-teux,
awm pui pa ang koe ka been yuh nan;
pie Pa-na-pa kawx hoik hawt rhawm kix.
14 Yam yaox aux ang kix priex tix hawt ka gum lox buan pa mawh pa meh,
aux tag krai ka Pi-eux ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun ah nin:
“Maix mawh pui Yo-thaix,
viang mawh nan krax ot krax koe maix hoik awm pui tang ceu yuh nan,
mai ang awm pui Yo-thaix.
Yuh nin,
yuh ka mawx maix lai khin pui tang ceu hawt ka krax ot krax koe pui Yo-thaix lie?”
Pui Yo-thaix mai pui tang ceu hoik pon krax taoh kheu krax yum rhawm
15 Ang lud,
yix pui in kix hoik mawh pui Yo-thaix tom yam lih keh tix,
ang mawh pui phi map tang ceu.
16 cKheu nan,
aux hoik tawng,
phan pui pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
hoik mawh ka daux krax pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux Khrit,
mai ang mawh pa een rhawm tix ka pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si.
Ex kawx hoik yum rhawm ka Ye-sux Khrit,
mai hoik mawh kheu yum rhawm ka Khrit,
tom taug pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw.
17 Yuh nin,
phan ex pui in kix sawg kheu yum rhawm ka Khrit,
tom taug pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
mai ca kawn awm pui tang ceu yuh nan,
mawh pui phi map khawm uik,
in ang hoik awm mawh Khrit pa nhie pui yuh phi map lie?
Ang doh mawh nan heue!
18 Mawh kheu phan aux deeh cox sum ka ceu pa hoik yieh tix laux,
in tag mawh jhi grawng yuh aux lox tat jhawm prawng laux.
19 Phan krai grawng lox tat jhawm prawng,
aux hoik yum,
mai hoik mawh lox tat jhawm prawng pa pauh yum,
son sang kieh aux pon im son Siyiex.
Hoik tawk aux tau mai Khrit ka piang khaox lhak,
20 yuh nin,
pa im aux yam in ang lai mawh to kaux aux,
hoik mawh Khrit pa im ka daux ju aux.
Pa im aux yam in,
hoik mawh ka daux pa yum rhawm aux ka kawn simex Siyiex;
nawh muh ka aux,
mai tawx ju tix son aux.
21 Aux ang tieb tie ka buan son Siyiex.
Phan koe pui pa pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
pa mawh pa een rhawm tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
phan mawh nan,
pa yum Khrit ang mawh pa giex going lie?
