Pa sidiex gau Pao-law
4
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex mawh paox grawm pa muh aux,
aux kied braong ka pex heue!
Pex mawh krax gaux rhawm aux,
mawh mhok khri aux.
Paox grawm pa muh aux khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix cung kaux cong ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
2 Yo-a-tiiex mai Suing-to-ci pux o ra kaux eue,
aux sidiex gau pax caw tix ih rhawm rhi khri mau paox tix ka daux Simiang Siyiex.
3 Paox aux pa kaux sijax eue,
aux kawx khiox khaw maix teem coi kiex ra kaux;
mawh kheu hoik taug mao kix tau mai aux,
mai Keu-li-miien,
khawm paox pa yuh kaing tau sidiex gum lox buan;
ceu kaux pui in kix hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux phuk lai pa im ju Siyiex heue.
4 Pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi ka daux pa kleuk tix tau mai Simiang Siyiex.
Aux baox krai ah nin,
pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue!
5 Pex kawx tag caw tix baox jhi grawng pa koe pex krax va rhawm va rhi ka pui hun.
Simiang Ye-sux tag sang hoek.
6 Pex caw tix ang koe krax pa khi rhawm khi rhi;
tag caw tix krai ceu pa sang lo pex ka Siyiex ka daux krax rawg buan,
teei rhawm pa yaog yaw khiox khaw.
7 Siyiex pa tawx krax pa nhim an pa kawn lheu khaing ceu pa chix keud chix sawn pui,
tag sang priex pau ka rhawm rhi pex ka daux Ye-sux Khrit heue.
8 Lox ghoik,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix ka ceu pa mhawm,
mai ka kaing ceu pa khaw tix yaog yaw jheeng jheeng.
Can kaing ceu pa mawh pa meh,
pa ao min,
pa khaw caw,
pa singax singeh,
krax muh krax rhawm,
mai ceu pa koe min plak ngai,
tag caw tix kieh mawh pa ting ceu.
9 Pex tag caw hawt tix yuh ka ceu pa gau pex,
pa rieb pex,
pa mhong pex,
pa yaox pex khaing aux khankix khawm uik.
Siyiex cao tawx pa nhim an tag sang ot tau mai pex heue.
Lox ting buan son
10 Aux hoik koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi pa kied ting ka daux ju Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
yam in pex baox hoek tix tawx ceu pa lo aux.
Tete te lai,
pex hoik dawg chix kia ngien ka aux,
mawh ngie ang pex koe bawg yam tix krai lih.
11 Aux krai yuh nin,
ang mawh kheu cud cha aux ka ceu pa tid tix;
aux hoik gau tix chix naog rhawm ka pa koe tix yam in.
12 Aux hoik tawng grawng sang ot ka daux krax pa hot sibhawm,
mai hoik tawng nang ka grawng sang ot ka daux krax pa mag mi.
Aux hoik pon ka ceu pa chix tix ceu,
ku bawg ku yam,
pa sak ang ah,
pa cai rhawm ang ah,
pa kub ang ah,
pa cud cha ang ah,
aux hoik vhah rhawm khawm uik.
13 Ka daux riang rao pa tawx Khrit,
aux hoik pon chix ot ka daux kaing ku ceu khawm uik heue.
14 Viang mawh nan,
yam hot sibhawm aux,
pex hoek tix teem coi ka aux,
aux kied simie tix ah lox ting buan son ka pex.
15 aPex pui Fe-li-pix hoik tawng khawm uik,
yam jauh aux tix sidiex ka gum lox buan,
aux lih hu khaing haktiex Ma-chi-toeng;
bawg yam an mawh ngie ceu cao pex pa teem coi ka aux khaing,
mai koe nyum cie ka daux pa som nai aux.
16 bcYam ot aux ka Thie-sa-law-ni-cia,
ang joeng mawh ngie tix bawg khaing,
yam koe ceu pa sang lo aux,
pex tag hoek tix teem coi ka aux.
17 Ang mawh aux pa simie ka khawng phak ceu pa tawx pex,
hoik mawh grawng pa simie pex pon ka ceu pa mhawm tom nie,
mai kieh mawh pa sawn hoek ka piang to kaux pex.
18 dIn hoik mawh mu khawng phak lak sawng pa tawx pex ka aux khawm uik;
pa tawx pex ka aux hoik lheu khaing ceu pa lo aux.
Yi-pa-fu-thi hoik viex khawng phak lak sawng pa tawx tom nie hoek son pex,
jiet awm sidah pa bhawng yuh nan,
mai hoik mawh khawng tan pa khaw rhawm Siyiex.
19 Siyiex aux tag sang hawt tix tawx buan son pa kub an ka daux Ye-sux Khrit,
pa sang lo pex khawm uik tawx ka pex heue.
20 Kieh min plak ngai mawh cie keeing ex Siyiex leeing ju leeing phan heue!
A-meung.
Lox ghoik pa chawk mhawm ot
21 Chawk kawn Khrit pa dex dawt tau mai Ye-sux Khrit ku kaux mhawm ot.
Kawn Khrit khankix pa ot tau mai aux chawk pex mhawm ot.
22 Can kawn Khrit khawm uik,
nie mawh pui ka daux nyiex haw simiang,
chawk pex mhawm ot khawm uik heue.
23 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son ka pex heue!
