Doeh ceu cad Ya-paw-la-hang tang kaux
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.32–33)
25
Ya-paw-la-hang baox teei tix koe moeng tix kaux,
mai ceu kaux nawh mawh Ci-thu-la.
2 Nawh keh Ya-paw-la-hang cie kawn,
pa kaux Sing-lang,
Yaw-sang,
Mi-tang,
Mi-tiien,
Yi-see-pa mai Su-yax.
3 Yaw-sang keh See-pa mai Ti-tang.
Doeh ceu cad Ti-tang mawh pui Ya-su-li,
pui Li-to-see,
mai pui Li-wu-mi.
4 Kawn simex Mi-tiien mawh Yi-fa,
Yi-fu,
Ha-nawx,
Ya-pi-tax mai Yi-leu-tax,
pui in kix khawm uik mawh doeh ceu cad Ci-thu-la heue.
5 Ya-paw-la-hang tawx can pa koe tix khawm uik ka Yi-sha.
6 Viang mawh nan,
yam kawn im ju nawh,
nawh guah tix tawx can pa koe tix tix blah ka kawn simex pa keh khaing moeng iak tix.
Khaix an,
nawh ploi kawn simex in kix hu ot haktiex plak lih singaix dee singai khaing Yi-sha heue.
Grawng yum Ya-paw-la-hang mai si paung kah
7-8 Ya-paw-la-hang kied lang ju,
yam koe ju tix yieh atigliah phuan num taug yum.
9 Kawn simex nawh Yi-sha mai Yi-seu-ma-lix si paung nawh ka daux rang Me-pi-la.
Daux rang in ot ka Mang-lix plak lih singaix,
sivoe in mawh daux kaung Yi-fu-loeng kawn Saw-sia ceu pui Heux.
10 aDaux kaung in hoik mawh pa liag Ya-paw-la-hang ka ceu pui Heux.
Ya-paw-la-hang mai moeng nawh Sa-la hoik si paung tix tan.
11 Khaix hoik yum Ya-paw-la-hang,
Siyiex tawx buan ka kawn simex nawh Yi-sha.
Yi-sha ot dex ka Pi-eu La-hai Lai tan heue.
Doeh ceu cad Yi-seu-ma-lix
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.28–31)
12 Bun siliex Sa-la pui Ai-cix pa kaux Sa-cia keh kawn simex son Ya-paw-la-hang pa kaux Yi-seu-ma-lix koe kawn simex plak greem,
13 ceu kaux kix hawt yam keh kix mawh Ni-pai-yaw;
Ci-tax,
Ya-teu-piie,
Mi-pi-sang,
14 Mi-see-max,
Tu-max,
Ma-sha,
15 Ha-tax,
Thi-max,
Yi-thu,
Na-fe-see mai Ci-ti-max.
16 Kix mawh simiang kao ra kaux daux simie ceu pui tix.
Ceu kaux yaong ot kix mai bang ot kix hawt ka ceu kaux kix heue.
17 Yam hoik koe ju Yi-seu-ma-lix tix yieh tix ngoe aliah num nawh yum,
18 Dee ot doe nawh jauh ka Ha-fe-la hoek ka Ai-cix plak lih singaix,
doh plak Su-eux haktiex Ya-sux.
Kix ang ot tau mai doe Ya-paw-la-hang tang kaux heue.
Grawng keh Yi-sao mai Ya-keux
19 Plak greem in mawh pa tiiem grawng doeh ceu cad Yi-sha kawn simex Ya-paw-la-hang.
20 Yam koe ju Yi-sha tipon num tom moeng ka Li-pai-cia;
Li-pai-cia mawh ceu pui Ya-lang haktiex Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax kawn bun Pi-thu-lix nan La-pang.
21 Li-pai-cia mawh bun neh,
Yi-sha khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
Simiang Siyiex nghiet lox khiox khaw nawh,
mai moeng nawh Li-pai-cia koe kaux.
22 Kawn ngie pa koe daux vaig nawh mawh kawn brawm,
lu lax thung theen paox tix,
nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawh aux lai kha tix ka daux ju tix yuh nin lie?”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh lih hu chawk cheng ka Siyiex heue.
23 bSimiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
Meung ra meung koe daux vaig maix,
ceu pui ra cub tag sang lih khaing to kaux maix,
ceu pui tix cub tag sang ting riang rao khaing an tix cub,
kawn pa ting tag sang yuh son kawn simex pa iak heue.
24 Yam hoik naog bawg yam,
nawh keh kawn brawm.
25 Kawn pa keh sivoe,
raoh nex nham,
kuah hauk ka to kaux,
hoik awm kraung ceep piang nawh,
kheu nan tom kok ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-sao.c
26 Khaix an pux nawh deeh keh,
mai giex ka loek caong Yi-sao,
mawh kheu nan,
kix kok ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-keux.d
Yam keh kawn an kiex,
Yi-sha hoik koe tix gliah num heue.
Yi-sao coh ka min kawn ai
27 Khaix hoik ting lhaong kawn nyawm an,
Yi-sao lih mawh pui rien jaw;
mai muh tix ot plak praix;
viang mawh nan,
Ya-keux mawh pui pa ang haoh lox tix kaux,
mai ot daux nyiex jheeng jheeng.
28 Yi-sha blawn muh ka Yi-sao,
kheu yaox nawh tix ih nex to tiag pa tawx nawh,
viang mawh nan,
Li-pai-cia blawn muh ka Ya-keux heue.
29 Koe tix singaix,
Ya-keux yuh priex mai ngaik rawm ceem,
Yi-sao kaoh khaing jaw tix,
kied cai rhawm.
30 Yi-sao ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Mawh kheu sidaing cai rhawm aux,
tawx aux ih rawm ceem raoh pa ngaik maix liak!”
(Mawh kheu nan,
ceu kaux nawh kawx lih tix mawh Yi-tonge heue.)
31 Ya-keux pawk lox ah nin:
“Guah maix tawx min kawn ai ka aux,
aux tom tawx rawm ceem raoh ka maix.”
32 Yi-sao ah nin:
“Chix tix!
Chix tix!
Aux dex sang yum,
sang yuh pa tix ka min kawn ai lie?”
33 fYa-keux pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix tiex tu ka aux sivoe ha,
mai tawx min kawn ai ka aux.”
Yi-sao tom tiex tu,
mai tawx min kawn ai ka Ya-keux.
34 Khaix an,
Ya-keux tawx Yi-sao ih khao mun mai rawm ceem raoh,
khaix hoik ih nyaux Yi-sao kah,
nawh lih hu,
Yi-sao ang nab yam ka min kawn ai tix heue.
