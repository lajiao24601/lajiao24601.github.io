Siyiex kok Yi-sai-yax mawh cao jai
6
aYam yum simiang Wu-si-ya ka daux num an,
aux hoik yaox ka Simiang Siyiex;
nawh ngawm ka piang dang ngeung dee lhaong,
kraung dha pa ceep nawh hoik lhawp kheem ka nyiex buan khawm uik.
2 Ka sivie siveng nawh koe kawn cau Siyiex Sa-la-fu pa cung kah,
kix hoik tang koe preeik tix kaux liah mu:
ra mu tawb ka ngai,
ra mu tawb ka to kaux,
ra mu nih preeik tix pu.
3 bKix lai ah nin ka paox tix:
Singax singax,
singax singax,
singax singax!
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing heue!
Min plak ngai nawh hoik naog ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
4 cLox pa kok yo kix hoik yung yang yuh riah jaong nyiex buan;
nyiex buan hoik naog ka taox ngu khawm uik heue.
5 Aux ah nin:
“Aux hoik yum phaox,
aux hoik laux leeing heue!
Mawh kheu lox pa kaoh khaing daux jheeh aux ku gum ang singax singeh;
pui pa ot sivie siveng aux lox pa krai kix kawx ang singax singeh nang.
Viang mawh nan,
ngai aux kawn yaox ka simiang,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing heue!”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
koe kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux pa koe preeik liah plak pu tix hoek ka aux,
pa viex ka si giep ngu tix mu,
mai kiiep ka gaox ngu cap ka piang khu dee tan,
7 nawh teei gaox ngu an pied siyieb jheeh aux kah,
ah nin:
“Gaox ngu pa raoh in pied ka siyieb jheeh maix,
krax pa lud loe maix hoik teei tix tik heue;
mai phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi heue.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
aux mhong lox Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux sang chix tix ploi pui mawx hu lie?
Pui mawx sang cu tix viex ka lox aux hu lie?”
Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux sang hu heue!
Ploi aux hu heue!”
9 dMawh kheu nan,
nawh ploi aux hu krai ka pui cie tix ah nin:
“Pex hoik nghiet baox nghiet,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng,
hoik jhak baox jhak,
viang mawh nan,
ang sidawng kah.”
10 Khaix an,
nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Kieh dieb dong pui in kix daox,
yhaok kix cien,
ngai kix vu vi,
ang chix yaox,
ang chix mhong,
ang chix keud;
phan ang mawh nan,
kix tom taug deeh doh rhawm hoek ka aux,
mai jhawm saux kix pon moi heue.”
11 Aux chawk ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
grawng kaing in tag sang lih koe miex greeng lie?”
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Kawn sang krox tom laux leeing veng,
tom ang lai yaox ka pui tix kaux kawx,
daux nyiex ma ang lai koe pui pa ot,
mai haktiex hoik riem khawm uik.
12 Aux sang ploi pui hun hu ka dee singai,
can haktiex khawm uik tag sang tik sang ghram kah.
13 Phan pui pa ot ka daux haktiex an kao kaux kawn deuk tix kaux,
mai pui an kawx tag sang laux leeing;
nawh tag sang awm khaox yang yit pa hoik gu pui,
mai deuk ngie khaix gu pui khaing.”
(Khaix gu pui an mawh pa krai grawng pui cie Siyiex khankix awm num pa jauh heue.)
