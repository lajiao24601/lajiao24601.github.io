Krax hu Ai-cix hoek ka haktiex Maw-ya
33
In plak greem mawh khaix hoik thung Maw-si mai Ya-loeng pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix dee sum kix bang hawt krax heue.
2 Maw-si hawt ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex tiiem tix deuh ceu kaux dee ot bang kix ku bawg heue.
3 Ka daux khix tix kao ngaix phuan,
pa mawh ngaix khaix Poi Luat-luan,
pui Yi-seu-liex jauh tix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix.
Mawh kheu ot kix ka daux pa teem coi Simiang Siyiex tix,
kix lih hu sivoe ngai pui ang koe krax lhat krax graung yam lih khaing veng Lang-sai;
4 Yam an ceu pui Ai-cix kawn si paung kawn ai tix pa hoik pauh Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex yuh ka kaing in,
mawh jhi pui tawng siawp sibrawm tix lheu ting khaing siyiex cie Ai-cix.
5 Pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing veng Lang-sai,
jauh tix yuh bang baoh Su-keu
6 khaix an yuh bang ka daux Yi-thang pa koe praok daux tiag daux tu.
7 Jauh dee an kix deeh hoek ka veng Pa-lix Si-feung pa koe plak veng Pi Ha-si-lu plak lih singaix,
mai sum bang ka baoh veng Mi-tawx.
8 Khaix hoik lih kix khaing veng Pi Ha-si-lu,
mai tiang ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
mai hoek ka daux tiag Su-eux,
lawng krax daux loe singaix,
khaix an sum bang ka baoh Ma-lax.
9 Kix jauh ka dee an baox hoek sum bang ka baoh Yi-ling,
mawh kheu Yi-ling koe rawm keet ka kao ra daux,
mai khaox jhie yaong atigliah gawng.
10 Khaix hoik lih khaing Yi-ling,
kix sum bang ka baoh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
11 Bawg khaix kix hoek daux tiag Shuing.
12 Khaix an hoek ka Thaw-cia
13 mai baox hoek ka Ya-lux.
14 Baox bawg khaix kix hoek ka Li-fe-ting;
ka dee an mai kix ang yaox tix nyaux rawm heue.
15-37 Jauh ka Li-fe-ting hoek ka gawng Heu-eu,
kix hoik sum bang ka daux ceu kaux haktiex in plak greem daux tiag Si-nai:
Ci-paw-law Ha-tha-wa.
(Baox kok mawh “si meeig pa koe rhawm ghoing”) Ha-si-lux,
Li-thi-max,
Ling-meung Pha-liie,
Li-na,
Leu-sha,
Ci-si-la-tha,
gawng Sa-fe,
Ha-la-tax,
Ma-cie-si-lux,
Tha-ha,
Tha-la,
Mi-cia,
Ha-maw-na,
Maw-si-lux,
Pi-ni Ya-kang,
Heu Ha-ci-cia,
Yaw-pa-tha,
A-paw-nax,
Yi-shuing Cia-piie,
daux tiag Shing (pa mawh Cia-ti-see),
gawng Heu-eu mee haktiex Yi-tong heue.
38-39 aKhaix hoik lih ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing daux Ai-cix tipon num,
khix phuan tix ngaix,
Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li hawt awm gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex haok ka piang gawng Heu-eu,
tom yum ka dee an.
Yam yum Ya-loeng ju koe tix yieh tix nga loe num heue.
40 bSimiang Ya-la-teu pa ot ka plak seh haktiex Cia-nang,
mhong ka hoek pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
41-49 Jauh ka gawng Heu-eu hoek ka dee lung Maw-ya,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik sum bang ka ceu kaux haktiex in plak greem:
Sa-maw-na,
Phu-noeng,
A-pawx,
Ya-pa-ling dee riem ang pui ot baoh daux haktiex Maw-ya,
Ti-peung,
Ya-meung Ti-pi-la-thai-ying,
gawng Ya-pa-ling pa koe ka dex gawng Ni-paw,
ka daux lung Maw-ya hawt rawm klawng Yaw-tang ka toi veng Yie-li-keu,
si nax Paw Yie-see-mawx mai ngawd See-thing heue.
Gum lox tawm sivoe sang tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang
50 Ka dee lung Maw-ya praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang toi veng Yie-li-keu,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
51 nhie nawh krai lox in plak greem ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh ah nin:
“Khaix hoik tiang pex rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
laig ka daux haktiex Cia-nang,
52 tag caw tix grheu pui pa ot ka haktiex an khawm uik to,
teei simaox mai riang khri khawm nyiex pa krub kix yuh laux khawm uik.
53 Pex tag pon cax ka haktiex an,
mai ot ka daux haktiex an,
kheu hoik tawx aux haktiex an mawh cie pex.
54 cPex cawk lai guah haktiex an ka ceu pui ku ceu.
Tag caw tix tawx haktiex pa vieh va ka ceu pui pa nie khankix,
haktiex pa iak tawx ka ceu pui pa tux.
55 Phan pex ang grheu pui pa ot ka haktiex an,
pui pa kawn ot ka daux pex,
tag mawh krax laux leeing ka pex,
mai mawh krix ka ngai pex,
kat lawm ka paw pex;
mai kix tag tuk tiak ka pex.
56 Phan pex ang grheu kix to,
aux tag sang yuh pex laux leeing,
awm pa hoik keud tix sang yuh kix laux leeing yuh nan heue.”
