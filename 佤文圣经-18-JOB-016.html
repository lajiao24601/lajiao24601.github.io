16
Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
2 Lox pa yuh nin aux hoik sak ka mhong tix;
krax pa kieh pex aux nyawm rhawm hoik tieh tix lih mawh pa viex krax pa laux rhawm.
3 Pex kawn ca ang bax naox krai ka lox pa brai yuh nin lie?
Pex ang nghoik tix krai tix gum ang chix lie?
4 Phan krax pa si pup aux loh tix hoek piang pex,
aux kawx tee rien tix chix krai lox pa krai pex.
Aux kawx tee rien tix koe krax vuk vied kaing tix ka pex.
Plih lox lih tom nie son sang ne raw ka pex.
5 Aux tawng grawng sang teei lox pa mhawm mai joh jhan ka pex,
mai chix teei dak tix hoek nyawm yuh rhawm pex nang heue.
 
6 Viang mawh nan,
lox krai aux mai ang koe ceu;
ang nye ang nyux kawx ang pon tik ka krax pa kha aux rhawm tix.
7 Siyiex eue,
maix hoik kieh aux uik riang uik rao;
maix hoik kieh daux nyiex ma aux laux leeing.
8 Maix giex ka aux;
mai tieb tie ka aux.
Singaix yam in aux deuk ngie hak pa kawb si-ang khaing;
pui tang kaux hoik kham sakse grawng in mawh phi map aux.
9 Siyiex hoik riah aux ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix;
nawh greeik rhang braing tix ka aux.
Pui pa koeng ka aux daong ngai pa song rhawm ka aux;
10 kawn pui ang jheeh tix nyiah ka aux.
Kix teei tawm taix tix si mang ka si bax aux;
kix lai lawm puing ka aux heue.
11 Siyiex ap aux ka daux tawm taix pui caox;
nawh tik aux ka daux tawm taix pui phi map.
12 Aux hoik nyawm ot nyawm koe sivoe an,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex kieh aux ang nyawm ot nyawm koe;
nawh miien ka rawng ghok aux,
mai si blat aux max.
Nawh kieh aux mawh pa tang son mhai pa puing ka te,
13 mai puing ka aux khawm pon daog;
te cie nawh krox ka daux rug aux,
kieh tawm nhao aux ghlua lih piang tiex.
14 Nawh ca baox puing aux mat tix bawg hoik tix bawg;
nawh hoik awm pui pa chix ceung pa koe riang tix dug dig ka aux heue.
 
15 Aux laux rhawm laux rhi,
mai ceep ka kraung man keei sum;
aux laux rhawm mai ngawm ka daux nyaox.
16 Kheu yiiem grai aux ngai aux hoik uah;
ngai aux hoik uah,
hak ngai aux hoik faik.
17 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang bax yuh ka phi map krax pa tud pa rhim;
lox buan pa rawg aux mawh pa kaoh khaing pa viex aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix heue.
 
18 Haktiex eue,
paw vi tawb kheem ka krax ang mhawm aux;
paw vi kieh lox pa kok yo aux son kaing pa ang khaw kheem tix glip.
19 aCao pa kham sakse son aux ot piang rao ma;
nawh sang kaoh krai lox son aux.
20 Paox grawm aux khankix ne raw ka aux;
rawm ngai aux hoik lih ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
21 Aux simie pui koe pa krai lox son tix ka Siyiex,
awm pui pa krai lox son paox grawm tix yuh nan.
22 Ka daux singaix pa im ju aux hoik luan hu,
aux tag sang hu ka krax pa ang lai chix deeh an heue.
