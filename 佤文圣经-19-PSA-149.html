Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
149
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex!
 
Tag caw tix teei lox rhax pa khraox yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex;
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw nawh ka dee khrawm pui cie nawh pa yum rhawm khankix paox tix.
2 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm kheu cao pa phan pa yuh pex;
kawn simex kawn bun Shi-ang eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm kheu simiang pex.
3 Tag caw tix graoh mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux nawh;
tag caw tix tauh kraung tauh ting mai hoek tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex muh ka pui cie tix khankix;
nawh teei tix tawx krax pa puing pex ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi.
5 Kieh pui cie nawh gaux rhawm kheu hoik pex tix;
kieh kix rhax lox ting si num tom phlat riang.
6 Kieh kix rhax lox pa ting pa si num,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex.
Ka daux tawm taix kix giex ka vaig pa lawm khawm ra plak,
7 mawh son sang pawk bix ka meung pa koe ka piang haktiex,
ciyang ka pui kix;
8 prawk ka simiang kix,
teei maox ceng mad ka simiang kix;
9 hawt tix ciyang meung ku meung awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Siyiex.
In hoik mawh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex pa puing pex heue.
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
