Siyiex krai lox kaux cong ka pui Yi-seu-liex
41
Siyiex ah nin:
klawn tiex khankix eue,
tag caw tix yien yawg,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux heue!
Kawn pui ka piang haktiex eue,
tag caw tix rien khre ka rhawm rhi tix mhawm mhawm heue!
Pex hoek tix krai lao heue;
kieh ex rom tix guah ka ceu pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm heue.
 
2 Mawh pui mawx pa nyaw pui pon kuang an hoek khaing plak lih singaix,a
kieh nawh ang koe pui pa ang pon pex lie?
Mawh pui mawx pa kieh nawh puing pex ka meung ka piang haktiex tom nie,
hoik kieh nawh pon pex ka simiang tom nie lie?
Lawm vaig nawh pauh ka pui,
awm hoik yuh ka kraung tang ceu laux leeing;
te nawh puing ka pui,
mai kieh kix awm nyhee glauh ka sivoe bhaung.
3 Nawh leue ka kix,
mai kaux cong hu sivoe,
been brax awm ang caong nawh cux ka tiex.
4 Pui mawx pa kieh kaing in lih lie?
Pui mawx tat plak hu been lie?
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tom yam jauh hoik ot dee an,
tom yam dee si dauh,
aux kawx ot dee an.
5 Klawn tiex khankix yaox ka aux taug lhat graung kah;
pui pa ot dee singai kawx hoik lhat mai si yaung kah.
Kix hoik hoek,
mai khrawm paox tix ot tau.
6 Pui cang mai kix ra lai teem paox tix,
ra lai joh jhan paox tix ah nin:
teei riang maix lih heue!
7 Cang mai khaox ah nin ka cang mai khri:
khaix taix maix mhawm;
cang mai pa raug tix yuh ka riang rao krub pui yaog yaw ka cang mai khaox pa tawk ka jim rhiem,
yaog yaw ah nin:
pex hoik mhawm yuh heue.
Kix teei jim rhiem mai tawk riang rao kaux,
kieh riang an kaux cong heue.
 
8 bViang mawh nan,
siliex aux Yi-seu-liex eue,
Ya-keux,
pa mawh pui pa laug aux eue,
maix mawh paox grawm aux,
kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang.
9 Aux viex maix hoek khaing caong daog haktiex;
aux kok yo maix hoek khaing caong daog haktiex pa koe dee singai.
Aux ah nin ka maix:
maix mawh siliex aux.
Aux ang bax tik vud ka maix,
aux hoik laug ka maix.
10 Paw vi lhat,
aux ot tau mai maix;
aux mawh Siyiex maix,
maix kawn sang lhat ka ceu pa tix lie?
Aux sang kieh maix kaux cong,
sang teem coi ka maix;
aux sang kia ngien ka maix,
sang taoh ka maix heue.
 
11 Pui an kix pa song rhawm ka maix tag sang pon krax kaig krax tua;
pui pa map nai maix tag sang yum.
12 Maix hu sawg ka pui pa sang hu puing naing tau mai maix,
mai maix ang yaox;
pui koeng ka maix tag sang laux leeing.
13 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix;
aux sang tawx riang rao ka maix.
Aux krai ka maix ah nin:
paw vi lhat,
aux sang teem coi ka maix heue.
 
14 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Yi-seu-liex,
pui cie aux eue,
viang maix iak riang kawx,
paw vi lhat.
Aux mawh cao taoh,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh;
aux sang teem coi ka maix.
15 Aux sang kieh maix mawh cag pauh nghox,
sau rhang pa lawm pa khraox kah;
maix tag sang pauh ka gawng lua phawng vid,
mai yuh kix laux leeing;
maix tag sang pauh ka gawng iak phawng vid,
mai kieh kix lih mawh phawng tiex.
16 Maix tag sang boe ka kix,
kieh bhaung phlu hu,
kieh bhaung ting phlu kix hu heue.
Viang mawh nan,
maix tag caw tix gaux rhawm,
mawh kheu mawh aux Simiang Siyiex maix;
maix tag caw tix yaog yaw aux,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh heue.
 
17 Yam sawg pui pa hot pa sibhawm cie aux tix nyaux rawm,
yam hoik krox ghok kix,
aux Simiang Siyiex tag sang nghiet ka lox rawg kix buan;
aux pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ang tik ang vud ka kix heue.
18 Aux tag sang kieh rawm klawng lih khaing gawng pa kroh pa riem,
kieh rawm keet buing tix lih khaing daux ngawd.
Aux tag sang kieh pak mhaik lih koe nhawng,
kieh haktiex kroh lih koe rawm keet heue.
19 Aux tag sang sum khaox si oe bhawng mai khaox Cing-heu-huang koe ka daux pak mhaik,
khaox plix mak cang,
khaox sime kawx sum koe kah.
Ka daux tiag daux tu tag sang koe khaox gix,
khaox sang,
mai khaox huang yang.
20 Yam yaox pui hun kah,
tom taug tawng,
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux hoik yuh lih ka kaing in khawm uik.
Kix tag sang chix tawng kah,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh hoik kieh kaing in lih tix mawh heue.
Simiang Siyiex yuh naing ka Siyiex pa pie lhien
21 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
Siyiex meung ka piang haktiex eue,
pex hoek tix krai lao heue.
Simiang Yi-seu-liex krai pao yuh nin:
pex krai ka daux krax pa blawn khaw cie tix lih hoek heue!
22 Pex hoek dha tix krai ka kaing ceu pa sang lih tix mawh,
ka dee bleeh kaing pa hoik luan.
Hoek tix thung gau ka yix,
kieh yix tawng ka dee si dauh kaing in kix,
hoik grox tiag tix tawng ka kaing ceu pa sang lih tix mawh.
23 Kieh yix tawng sidawng ka singaix pa sang hoek grawng sang yuh ka mawx,
yix tom taug tawng grawng mawh pex siyiex.
Yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm phan ang mawh nan ploi krax laux krax leeing lih,
kieh yix yaoh yee lhat graung.
24 Pex ang sawn tix mawh tid tix;
kaing pa yuh pex hoik mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik;
pui pa krub ka pex mawh pui caox pui caig heue!
 
25 Aux hoik laug pui tix kaux ka plak lih singaix;
aux sang hoek tix puing ka pex khaing haktiex plak laong.
Nawh dug dig ka simiang,
awm dug dig pui ka glaox yuh nan.
Awm dig cang mai yuh aw ka glaox yuh nan heue.
26 Ka daux pex koe pui mawx pa dha tix krai ka kaing ceu pa sang lih,
tag chix kieh ex krai grawng ang nawh koe krax lud lie?
Ka daux pex koe pui mawx pa ang sax krai lao ka kaing in;
ang koe pui pa mhong ka lox pa hoik krai pex in.
27 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ran tawx gum lox pa gaux rhawm in ka Shi-ang;
aux ploi kawn cau hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
Paox ceu paox cad pex hoik deeh hoek heue!
28 Aux hoik yaox ka siyiex pa koe khawm uik,
ang koe tix kaux kawx pa koe lox krai;
ang koe tix kaux kawx pa tag chix pawk ka lox chawk aux.
29 Siyiex tang kaux pa koe khawm uik hoik mawh pa ang lai chix keud chix sawn;
kix hoik iak riang iak rao khawm uik,
ang pon yuh kaing ceu pa tid tix heue.
