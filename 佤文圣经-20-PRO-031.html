Krax pa sidiex ka simiang
31
In plak greem mawh gum lox pa sidiex miex simiang Li-maw-yi-leux ka simiang ah nin:
2 “Kawn simex aux eue,
maix hoik mawh kawn pa muh aux,
hoik mawh pa pon aux khaing sijax mukheu pa yuh tix.
Sang khaw aux tix krai ceu pa tix ka maix lie?
3 Ang pon tix kieh riang rao tix uik ka daux alo nex nham,
kieh mau khri tix som nai ka to kaux bun;
kaing ceu pa yuh nin hoik sax laux leeing yuh simiang.
4 Nghiet heue,
Li-maw-yi-leux eue,
simiang ang pon tix nyaux plai,
mai ang pon tix ghoing ka kawk plai.
5 Phan kix nyaux plai mai hoik piawm ka jhawm prawng piang meung,
hoik ang lai tawng yung ka siawp sibhawm pui hot pui sibhawm khankix.
6 Plai khien mawh pa tawx pui pa dex sang yum,
mai pui pa laux rhawm laux rhi khankix nyaux kah.
7 Phan kix hoik nyaux kah,
mai kix tom taug chix piawm ka krax kha rhawm,
mai ka kaing ceu pa ang mhawm heue.
8 “Tag caw tix teem coi ka pui pa ang pon krai lox khankix,
mai kia ngien ka sibrawm pui pa ang koe cao teem coi.
9 Tag caw tix teem kix krai lox,
hawt tix prawng kix ka daux pa rauh pa see,
mai prawng rauh see son pui hot pa cud pa cha heue.”
Moeng pa chix priex rhawm rhi tix
10 Moeng pa chix priex rhawm rhi tix hu sawg nawh dee mawx heue!
Ngawh jum nawh kawn lheu ngawh khaing siang heue!
11 Mex nawh yum rhawm ka nawh,
mai ang yaox krax hot krax sibhawm.
12 Ka daux ju nawh hoik kieh mex tix pon mhawm,
mai ang sax kieh nawh yaox tix som nai tix bawg kawx.
13 Nawh sau riang tix teei hauk yung mai man keei sum yuh kraung ceep kah.
14 Nawh awm pui yuh lai gee ka daux reu yuh nan,
mai viex priex preem kaoh khaing dee singai son sang eei nyiex ma tix kah.
15 Nawh kaoh khaing it sivoe sang riang praix,
mai rien khre priex preem son nyiex ma tix,
mai guah tix tawx kaing jhaw ka bun siliex tix.
16 Nawh teei mau pa pon tix liag haktiex,
mai yuh kaing daux si peem plix siiet.
17 Nawh mhawm nex nham mai rien yuh kaing jhaw,
ang lhat ka kaing pa cien pa nu khankix.
18 Nawh hoik tawng ka can ngawh jum kraung khrai pa yuh tix ku ceu,
mai yuh kaing tom hoek greung sawm heue.
19 Nawh kling tix duing keei son tix,
mai caing man son tix.
20 Nawh koe krax gaux rhawm tix teem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai teem ka pui hot pa cud pa cha.
21 Nawh ang koe krax pa khi rhawm ka pa lih rha,
mawh kheu hoik yaox pui nyiex pui ma nawh tix si u ot,
mai yaox tix ceep kraung heue.
22 Nawh taing ka prux bi,
mai caing kraung ceep son tix ka man keei sum mai man lung pliex heue.
23 Mex nawh hoik mawh pa kied koe min tix kaux,
mawh simiang haktiex.
24 Nawh caing tix tawx kraung ceep mai maox rawd nyoi ka pui pa yuh lai gee.
25 Nawh kaux cong mai pon krax pa nab yam pui,
mai grum naog ka krax yum rhawm son kaing pa sang yuh tix hu sivoe ngai.
26 Nawh si kah hoik jhi lih koe ping nyia cuyi,
lox si kah nawh hoik jhi grawng krax pa caox rhawm.
27 Nawh sau riang rao tix yuh ka kaing jhaw nyiex ma tix,
mai ang ih ka priex giex heue.
28 Kawn simex kawn bun nawh yung yi ka nawh;
mex nawh yaog yaw ka nawh.
29 Mex nawh ah nin:
“Bun pa priex rhawm rhi tix hoik koe tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
maix kawn lheu khaing kix heue!”
30 Nex nham pa awn mawh pa yie tieh loh tix,
pa mhawm grang mawh pa pie lhien,
mawh ngie bun pa chix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex pa khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw heue.
31 Kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik hoik mawh pa koe ngawh koe jum;
nawh khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw ka plak sivoe ngai pui heue.
