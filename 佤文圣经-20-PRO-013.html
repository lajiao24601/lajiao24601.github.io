13
Kawn simex pa koe krax lhak lhiao chix bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau keeing tix;
pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi ang doh keud ka krax pa lud loe yuh tix.
2 Pui mhawm krai lox pon lih mu pa mhawm;
pui pie pui lhien ca simie tix yuh krax pa tud pa rhim.
3 Pa chix rien ka jheeh tix,
tag pon tix im ju;
pa nieb pon krai lox,
hoik mawh pa deeh yuh to kaux tix laux leeing.
4 Pui pa nieh ang pon yuh kaing ceu pa simie tix yuh lih;
pui pa rien yuh kaing pon krax pa haok huan.
5 Pui pa mawh pa meh koeng ka lox pie lox lhien;
lox krai pui pa caox pa caig mawh pa yum koeng.
6 Pa rauh pa see chix kia ngien ka pui pa mhawm;
pa caox pa caig chix kieh pui phi map goh tix laux leeing.
7 Koe pui pa pie lhien tix mag mi,
tete te lai hoik hot sibhawm tom ang tix koe tid tix;
koe pui yuh tix hot sibhawm,
viang mawh nan,
sin ngao nawh koe tom nie.
8 Pui pa koe mau teei mau taoh ka ju tix;
pui pa hot sibhawm ang koe krax pru praoh yaoh yee.
9 Pui pa rauh pa see awm riang pa jhan riang;
pui pa caox pa caig awm kawk ngu pa sang yeet.
10 Pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi viex krax simah simaing;
pui pa rieb ka lox pa sidiex hoik mawh ping nyia cuyi.
11 Sin ngao pa pon khaing daux krax pa yie,
mai chix uik tix vud tix cu khaing;
sin ngao pa khrawm tix deuh khaing daux krax pa taug mao tix,
mai chix haok huan ku singaix.
12 Phan krax keud pot jun pot grai,
daux rhawm rhi kawx chix hawt tix laux leeing;
phan krax keud pot jun pot lih tix mawh,
daux rhawm rhi tag naog ka krax gaux rhawm pa im ju.
13 Pa map nai lox sidiex lox gau,
hoik mawh pa deeh kieh to kaux tix laux leeing;
Pa nghiet ka lox sidiex lox gau,
pon yaox tix nyawm ot.
14 Lox sidiex pui pa lhak lhiao hoik mawh dee keet ping nyia cuyi;
ping nyia cuyi kieh pui luat luan khaing krax pa viex hoek krax yum.
15 Pui pa koe krax doh rhawm pon krax nab yam yung yi pui ku dauh ku dee;
pui pa ang rauh see hoik hu krax pa laux leeing.
16 Pui pa lhak lhiao chix priex rhawm rhi tix keud sivoe tag sang yuh lih;
pui pa daox aw yaw rang di ka daux pa ang tix tawng tid tix.
17 Kawn cau pa pheem hoik kieh krax laux krax leeing lih;
kawn cau pa kaux sijax hoik tawx krax pa nhim koe.
18 Pa tieb tie ka lox sidiex lox gau tag sang hot sibhawm kaig tua;
pa rieb tix teei ka lox sidiex lox gaux tag sang pon krax pa nab yam yung yi.
19 Kieh krax keud pot jun pot lih tix mawh kieh pui koe krax gaux rhawm;
pui pa daox aw ang cu tix tik vud ka pa caox pa caig.
20 Pa hu tau mai pui pa lhak lhiao,
hoik mawh pa koe ping nyia cuyi;
pa yuh paox yuh pie tau mai pui pa daox aw,
tag sang kham tau maix kix.
21 Krax laux krax leeing leue ka pui phi map;
pui rauh pui see hoik pon ka kaing ceu pa mhawm.
22 Pui mhawm chix deuh sin ngao son kawn praix tix;
sin ngao pa khrawm pui phi map tix deuh hoik mawh cie pui rauh pui see.
23 Haktiex pa ang bax pai tix chix kieh mawh son dee yuh pui hot pui sibhawm to ma to koi;
pui pa ang rauh ang see ang cu tix sum kaung sum ma.
24 Pa ang si vied tix sidiex gau ka kawn simex tix hoik mawh pa ang muh ka nawh;
pa muh ka kawn simex tix caw tix sau riang tix sidiex ka nawh.
25 Pui rauh pui see ang cud ang cha ka ceu pa ih pa nyaux;
pui pa caox pa caig cai rhawm ku bawg ku yam heue.
