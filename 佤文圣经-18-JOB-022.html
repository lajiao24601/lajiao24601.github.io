Lox pa ra lai pawk bawg loe
(22.1–27.23)
22
aPui Thi-mang pa kaux Yi-li-fa baox krai lox ah nin:
2 Kawn pui koe dee siao ka Siyiex ceu pa tix?
Viang hoik mawh pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi kawx,
koe dee mhawm son Siyiex lie?
3 Krax pa yuh maix kaing pa mhawm,
khaw ka cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik lie?
Krax mhawm rhawm maix pon kieh Siyiex teem coi tix lie?
4 Siyiex ang mawh kheu lhat graung yung yi maix ka nawh,
ne raw ka maix,
prawng ca ka maix.
5 Ang,
mawh kheu phi map maix kied cien,
mawh kheu phi map maix kied nie.
6 Kheu kieh maix pux aik tix plud ka mau pa ceuk tix,
maix peeik ka kraung ceep nawh khawm uik,
mai kieh nawh ot ka kaong.
7 Pui pa simie tix nyaux rawm,
maix ang tawx nawh nyaux;
pui pa cai rhawm maix ang tawx nawh sawm.
8 Maix siao ka siawp sibrawm tix mai min ceu kaux tix,
pon teei ka can haktiex khawm uik.
9 Maix tawx miex mai deeh ing khawm taix yi tix;
maix lauh ka plawng taix kawn doe pot.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
ka vung viang maix koe daux o kah;
dum mawx dum an maix tag sang yaox krax lhat krax graung pa lai lawm ka maix.
11 Pa faik tawb kheem ka piang maix,
mai kieh maix ang pon chix yaox;
rawm ting hoik kheem ka maix heue.
 
12 Siyiex ang hoik ot ka dee lhaong rao ma lie?
Nawh ang hoik jhak ka simuing pa ot ka dee lhaong lie?
13 Maix lai kawn chawk ah nin:
Siyiex tawng ceu pa tix?
Nawh pon prawng ca ex ka plak khaix jan pai awm lie?
14 Maix ce pu pai awm pon kheem mox ka nawh,
mai ang kieh nawh pon chix yaox,
tete te lai,
nawh hoik lai ling ka daux rao ma heue.
 
15 Krax pa hoik lawng pui pa caox pa caig,
maix tag sang hawt tix lawng ka lie?
16 Singaix bawg yam sang yum kix ang bax hoek,
rawm ting hoik chun hu heue.
17 Kix ah nin ka Siyiex:
maix lih hu khaing yix heue!
Cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik sang chix kieh yix yuh ka mawx?
18 Khaix an,
pa kieh kix haok huan mawh Siyiex;
krax keud krax sawn pui pa caox pa caig,
aux ang pon tawng sidawng.
19 Yam yaox kix krax ciyang,
pui rauh pui see pon krax gaux rhawm,
pui pa ang koe dee lud tom yaox tix nyiah.
20 Sin ngao cie pui pa caox pa caig tag sang laux leeing,
can pa deuk khankix tag gleeing ka ngu heue.
 
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-paw eue,
maix tag caw kieh tix mhawm tau mai Siyiex,
yuh nin,
nawh tom tag chix tawx buan son ka maix.
22 Maix tag caw tix rieb ka krax pa sidiex gau nawh,
mai priex lox nawh kaux ot ka daux rhawm rhi tix.
23 Mawh tete,
mai tag caw tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,b
mai deeh hoek ka cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik dee an,
tag caw tix teei tix tik ka can kaing pa ang rauh ang see khaing ka daux nyiex ma maix khawm uik.
24 Tik ka mau khri pa koe maix,
mai teei tix tik khri ka daux dawng ang koe rawm.
25 Kieh cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik mawh khri pa ting jum ka maix,
kieh mawh mau luah,
pa grux tix lhaong son maix.
26 Yuh nin,
maix tom tag chix pon krax pa yum rhawm ka cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik jheeng jheeng,
mai tawng Siyiex mawh num rawm keet krax gaux rhawm maix.
27 Maix rawg buan ka nawh,
nawh tag sang chix nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw maix;
maix kawx tag caw tix deeh plud ka lox pa hoik ap pong maix tix deuh.
28 Kaing yuh maix tag sang chix pon lih tix mhawm khawm uik;
ka piang krax lawng maix tag sang koe pa jhan riang.
29 Siyiex tawk ka pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi goh,
taoh ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi.
30 Maix mawh pui ang koe dee lud,
nawh tag taoh ka maix;
maix rauh rhawm mhawm kaing yuh,
nawh tom taoh teem ka maix heue.
