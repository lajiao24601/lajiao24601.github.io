32
Rao ma eue,
veeh yhaok tix nghiet ka pa krai aux;
haktiex eue,
bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux.
2 Lox pa sidiex gau aux awm rawm lhiex pa jauh tix lih,
mai lih kang tix mawh rawm ciex piang tiex.
Gum lox krai aux awm rawm lhiex pa jaux ka piang rib awn,
awm lih lhiex lih jaux ka rib klai.
3 Aux sang krai lao ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
aux sang yaog yaw ka Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex mawh cao pa ting siawp sibrawm;
kaing pa yuh nawh mawh pa grum pa naog,
mai khaw caw.
Siyiex mawh pa kaux sijax pa kaux cong.
Daux krax prawng ca nawh mawh pa khaw caw mai pa rauh pa see.
5 Viang mawh nan,
pex yuh pa ang khaw rhawm nawh,
pex ang keung tix mawh kawn praix nawh,
pex hoik mawh ceu pui pa phit pa lud,
pa pie lhien.
6 Pui pa daox aw pa ang koe ping nyia khankix eue,
pex sang pawk buan son ka Simiang Siyiex yuh nin lie?
Nawh mawh cao pa phan pa yuh ka pex,
pa mawh keeing pex;
nawh hoik phan yuh ka pex,
kieh pex pon lih tix mawh meung khankix heue.
 
7 Pex tag caw tix doh keud ka singaix pa hoik luan;
pex deeh keud tix yaox ka num pa hoik luan dix dix.
Pex khiox khaw chawk cheng tix yaox kuad song,
krai ka kaing pa hoik luan khankix;
pex khiox khaw ka pui pa riex ju lao ka been dix dix.
8 aCao pa ting siawp sibrawm guah haktiex cie meung,
kieh kawn pui guah tix;
nawh tag hawt awm koe kaux pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
tat ka mee haktiex son kawn pui piang haktiex ku ceu.
9 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex laug ka kawn praix Ya-keux mawh cie nawh khankix,
kieh kix mawh sin ngao pa blawk cie tix heue.
 
10 Nawh hoik yaox kix ka daux tiag daux tu,
si pup kix ka daux tiag mhaik dee koe lon simaox khankix.
Nawh muh ka kix,
mai kia ngien ka kix,
awm muh ka kawn bun cie tix.
11 Miex klang gau kawn klang pu,b
mai di preeik tix pux ka kawn tix
Simiang Siyiex kawx hoik di preeik tix khawm ra pux ka Yi-seu-liex khankix,
kieh kix ang chix cawd.
12 Simiang Siyiex tix kaux khaing mai duk thung ka pui cie tix,
ang kieh siyiex tang kaux teem coi tix heue.
 
13 Nawh kieh kix kuang ka dee kied lhaong haktiex,
tag pon tix ih can plix nghox plix koi pa lih khaing kaung ma.
Kix ih ka rawm hia khaing daux rang,
bee sime pa lih khaing daux haktiex tiag simaox.
14 Rawm teeh moi yung kix hoik haoh tom ang lai uik biiet;
kix koe yung,
pex,
moi khankix pa kluing pa mhawm;
kix koe nghox ca pa mhawm mai plai plix siiet pa nyawm heue.
 
15 Kawn praix Simiang Siyiex hoik yaox tix sak vaig mai kluing mu;
kix hoik haok huan mai tag tieh vheen.
Kix tik vud ka Siyiex pa phan pa yuh kix;
kix tieb tie ka krax taoh cao pa ting siawp sibrawm.
16 Kix krub riang krub rao mai cheeik tix kieh Simiang Siyiex song rhawm song rhi;
kix yuh pa caox pa caig mai cheeik nawh song rhawm song rhi.
17 cKix hoik tan lu ka Siyiex pa pie lhien,
hoik tawx ka siyiex pa ang kix sax tawng sax yung;
Siyiex in hoik mawh pa ang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex sax krub ka tix bawg kawx,
mai tax du kix kawx,
ang sax lhat kah.
18 Kix hoik piawm ka cao pa ting siawp sibrawm;
kix hoik piawm ka Siyiex cao pa tawx ju tawx phan heue.
 
19 Simiang Siyiex yaox ka pa caox pa caig an,
nawh tom song rhawm song rhi kah;
nawh tik vud ka kawn simex kawn bun tix.
20 Nawh ah nin:
aux tag ang lai teem coi ka kix,
sang krox tix jhak ka kix yam si dauh;
kix hoik mawh baux ju pui kiien rhawm cau nyhau,
hoik mawh kawn praix pa ang cu lox nghiet si kah.
21 dKix hoik cheeik krax pa song rhawm aux lih ka “son riang rao ceu pa ang mawh Siyiex;”
kix hoik cheeik krax pa koeng rhawm aux kah,
lih kheu pa yuh kix ka Siyiex pa pie lhien.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh “ceu pui pa ang sawn tix mawh” pui ceu aux cheeik kix song rhawm;
aux tag sang teei meung pa daox aw,
cheeik kix koe rhawm koeng rhawm khoi.
22 Krax pa song rhawm aux hoik lih rup awm ngu,
tag sang gleeing pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
ngu krax pa song rhawm song rhi aux hoik gleeing ka daux pa faik krax yum,
tag sang bleet ka caong gawng ting khankix heue.
 
23 Aux tag sang lauh kix ka daux krax pa laux pa leeing pa ang chix uik,
aux tag sang puing kix yum ka te pa ang chix uik.
24 Kix tag sang yum ka daux krax pa cai rhawm;
ka pa saux tix.
Mai jhawm saux preng pa lhat pui ka khankix,
tag sang kieh kix laux leeing.
Si uing pa koe jai tag sang tuh ka kix;
to sat pa kua tag sang kiied ka kix.
25 Krax naing tag sang kieh kawn doe vu viex tix ka daux laih daux cau;
krax lhat naing tag sang kieh miex mai pau lawg nyiex it tix nong nong.
Simex pa awn ju mai bun pa awn ju tag sang nyao yum uik;
kawn nyawm mai kuad song kawx ang yaox tix luat luan.
26 Aux dawg sang caw tix yuh kix laux lak pak leeing,
kieh kawn pui haktiex piawm ka kix;
27 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang kieh pui pa koeng khankix yaw rang di,
ang kieh pui caox nieb pon keud pa ang khaw ang jiet,
ah mawh kix pa pon pex ka pui cie aux;
tete te lai mawh aux pa yuh kix laux leeing heue.
 
28 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex hoik mawh pa ang koe krax keud krax sawn,
krax lhak lhiao,
kix ang koe ping nyia cuyi ceu pa tid tix.
29 Kix ang tawng grawng pa ang tix pon pex;
kix ang sidawng ka dee si dauh to kaux tix.
30 Yuh ka mawx pui tix kaux khaing lai pon pheet pui tix rheng kaux lie?
Pui ra kaux khaing lai pon kieh pui kaing mheen kaux tang yaog tix to yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh kheu cao pa kia ngien hoik tik vud ka kix,
Simiang Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm hoik tik vud ka kix.
31 Pui pa koeng khankix hoik tawng siyiex cie kix mawh pa ang koe siawp sibrawm,
ang awm cao pa kia ngien ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa ting siawp sibrawm.
32 Pui koeng hoik laux leeing awm Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la yuh nan,
hoik awm khaox plix siiet pa plix ka plix song pa koe jai.
33 Plai nyaux kix hoik awm bai pa lih khaing daux jheeh si uing ting,
hoik awm si uing pa koe jai heue.
 
34 Simiang Siyiex hoik tawng ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui pa koeng,
mai yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
nawh tag ciyang ka kix.
35 eSimiang Siyiex tag sang pawk bix ka pui pa koeng,
yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
an kix tag laux leeing.
Daux singaix pa laux pa leeing hoik koe sivoe ngai,
singaix pa si dauh tag sang khran hoek.
36 fSimiang Siyiex yaox ka pui cie tix ang pon pex,
mai tag taoh teem ka kix,
Simiang Siyiex yaox ka pui pa krub ka nawh ang lai koe riang koe rao,
mai tag caox rhawm ka kix.
37 Simiang Siyiex tag sang chawk ka pui pa krub ka nawh ah nin:
Siyiex tang kaux tang hak pa krub pex ot dee mawx lie?
Cao pa kia ngien een pex rhawm tix ka ot dee mawx lie?
38 Pex hoik teei bee khawng brie pa tan tawx kix ih kah,
pex hoik teei plai,
jauh ka piang kix;
yam in kok yo hoek teem coi ka pex heue!
Nhie kix hoek kia ngien ka pex heue!
 
39 Mawh ngie aux pa mawh Siyiex;
lheu khaing aux mai ang lai koe siyiex tang kaux.
Aux chix kieh pui yum,
mai deeh chix kieh pui im;
aux lauh pui mat,
mai deeh chix pai pui moi.
Ang koe pui tix kaux kawx,
pa pon luat luan khaing daux tawm taix aux.
40 Aux yaog taix tix ka piang rao ma,
tiex tu ka ju to kaux tix:
41 Aux tag sang klaing vaig lawm tom jhan riang,
son sang yuh ka pa rauh see;
aux sang pawk bix ka pui pa koeng khankix,
mai ciyang ka pui pa koeng ka pui cie aux.
42 Nham kix tag sang kraix tix naog ka te cie aux;
vaig cie aux pa kied lawm,
tag sang bleet ka nex kix.
Aux ang doh pluat ploi ka pui pa koeng ka aux;
khawm pa mawh pui naing pa mat,
mai pui pa pon giex daux naing kawx tag sang yum khawm uik heue.
 
43 gMeung ka piang haktiex eue,
pex caw tix gaux rhawm ka son pui cie Simiang Siyiex heue!
Mawh kheu hoik pawk Simiang Siyiex bix son nham kix.
Nawh hoik pawk bix ka pui pa koeng pa khoi ka nawh khankix;
nawh hoik pluat ploi ka phi map pui cie tix khankix heue.
 
44 Maw-si ot tau mai Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng,
mai phat lox lai leung in ka sivoe kawn pui khawm uik heue.
Gum lox pa krai Maw-si sivoe sang yum tix
45 Khaix hoik krai pao Maw-si gum lox pa sidiex gau Siyiex ka kawn pui,
46 mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap in pa hoik krai aux ka pex daux in singaix tete,
mai ka kawn praix pex kawx gau kah,
nhie kix hawt tix yuh heue.
47 Gum lox ping nyap in ang mawh lox pa giex pa going,
mai hoik mawh ju ka pex.
Pex priex tix hawt,
mai pex tom tag pon yaox tix ot ka toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak klip singaix tom rheeng,
pa mawh haktiex pa sang pon kuang pex tix cie an heue.”
48 hKa daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin ka Maw-si:
49 “Maix hu ka si tieb veng Yie-li-keu,
ka baoh gawng Ya-pa-ling haktiex Maw-ya,
mai haok ka piang choek gawng Ni-paw,
mai kok jhak haktiex Cia-nang khaing dee an,
pa mawh haktiex pa sang tawx aux ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
50 Maix tag sang yum ot ka piang gawng an,
awm hoik yum pux ke maix Ya-loeng ka piang gawng Heu-eu,
51 Mawh kheu hoik lieh lheu pex ka aux sivoe pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
Pex hoik ot ka daux tiag Shing,
ka praok dee lih rawm Mi-li-pa,
dex ka veng Cia-ti-see,
ka sivoe pui hun mai ang yung yi ka aux.
52 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag ot dee singai kok yaox ngie ka haktiex pa sang tawx aux ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ka ngai tix khaing,
mai ang pon tix laig kah heue.”
