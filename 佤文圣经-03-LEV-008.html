Ap Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix mawh cao giex kaing li
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 29.1–37)
8
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix viex Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man aux.
Maix baox viex nang kraung ceep cao giex kaing li mai bee pa singax singeh sang groi yam ap pong,
mai moi mhaing Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap tix mu,
mai kawn yung mhaing ra mu,
khao mun pa ang sau su saix tix khrawng hoek;
3 khaix an khrawm can pui haoh pui hun khawm uik hoek dee an heue.”
4 Maw-si yuh awm lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka nawh.
Cub pui hun khrawm paox tix ka plak khaix diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
5 nawh krai ah nin ka kix:
“Pa yuh aux yam in hoik mawh pa hawt ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex son sang kieh ex yuh heue.”
6 Maw-si viex Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix hu sivoe,
nhie kix khoek heem tix.
7 Nawh tawx Ya-loeng ceep sibex dha,
mai rhawt maox rawd nyoi,
mai ceep sibex dha plak praix,
baox ceep sibex Yi-fu-teua,
mai teei maox pa hoik bre tix taing mhawm,
rhawt Yi-fu-teu kaux ot ka piang to kaux tix.
8 Khaix an,
nawh sau na bat naok ot ka piang to kaux Ya-loeng,
mai teei “Wu-ling” mai “Thu-ming” b sau ot ka na bat naok an.
9 Khaix an,
nawh ceep lawk kaing ka piang kaing Ya-loeng,
sau khri ka sivoe ngai mhok khri an,
kieh lih tix mawh mhok khri pa singax singeh.
In khawm uik hoik hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex heue.
10 Maw-si teei bee pa singax singeh,
mai kraix ka nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
mai ka can pa koe daux nyiex khawm uik,
yuh nin,
nawh tag tan ku ceu khawm uik ka Simiang Siyiex.
11 Nawh baox groi bee ka piang khu dee tan mai kraung pa siao ka piang khu aliah bawg,
ka daux pan khoek mai jaong pan kawx phruah ka aliah bawg,
son sang tan ceu in ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
12 Nawh groi bee pa singax singeh ka piang kaing Ya-loeng,
ap nawh mawh cao giex kaing li.
13 Khaix an,
Maw-si viex kawn simex Ya-loeng khankix hu sivoe,
mai tawx kix ceep sibex dha,
rhawt maox rawd nyoi,
ceep ka mhok.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka kix heue.
14 Maw-si tuk kawn moi mhaing pa yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap hoek;
Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix deuh taix tix piang kaing moi mhaing.
15 Maw-si pauh ka khawng brie an,
mai teei giien taix tix cub ka daux nham an,
kraix ka piang rung khu dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog,
son sang tan ka ceu in kix.
Khaix an,
nawh hieh nham pa kawn deuk ka piang jaong khu dee tan.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom kieh khu dee tan singax singeh,
son sang guah tix lih mawh pa singax singeh.
16 Maw-si teei bee pa kheem kraung vaig khawng brie tan an,
tawm dee blawn mhawm,
mai sok lax ra mu,
bee pa koe piang sok lax,
deuh ot ka piang khu,
mai gleeing ka khawm uik.
17 Nawh tag teei moi an pa deuk tix blah,
pa mawh khawm hak,
nex,
kraung vaig kraung tu,
viex lih plak praix nyiex bang khawm uik,
mai gleeing kah.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex heue.
18 Khaix an,
Maw-si viex kawn yung mhaing pa yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix mu hoek,
Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix deuh taix tix ka piang kaing yung mhaing an.
19 Maw-si pauh ka khawng brie tan an,
mai phruah nham hawt vung viang khu dee tan.
20-21 Nawh sit nex kawn yung lih mawh khik,
mai si ghrah kraung vaig mai ba plak kreue ka rawm,
mai teei in kix khawm kaing,
khlawp bee khawm uik gleeing tix tan ka piang khu dee tan.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex.
Taox pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Ngu in mawh pa muh Simiang Siyiex heue.
22 Maw-si deeh viex yung mhaing tang mu hoek,
in mawh pa tan son sang ap pong ara cao giex kaing li.
Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix deuh taix tix ka piang kaing yung mhaing an.
23 Maw-si pauh ka khawng brie tan an,
teei nham tix blah kraix ka dawng yhaok plak dawm Ya-loeng,
ka piang giien taix ting khawn dawm,
mai ka piang giien caong ting khawn dawm.
24 Khaix an,
nawh viex kawn simex Ya-loeng khankix hoek sivoe,
mai teei nham kraix ka dawng yhaok khawn dawm kix,
mai ka giien taix ting mai giien caong ting khawn dawm kix,
khaix an nawh teei nham pa kawn deuk mai phruah hawt vung viang khu dee tan.
25 Nawh teei khlawp bee mai si dax,
bee pa kheem kraung vaig,
tawm dee blawn mhawm,
sok lax ra mu,
bee pa lhawp sok lax,
mai ba khawn plak kreue khawn dawm lih.
26 Nawh teei khao mun tix lon pa ang sau su saix khaing daux khrawng an pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
tix lon mawh khao mun pa teei bee sime kraix kah,
mai khao mun pa rhi tix khlawp,
deuh ot ka piang khlawp bee mai ka piang bax khawn dawm.
27 Khaix an deuh priex preem in kix ka daux tawm taix Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
kix tag kieh in kix mawh krax tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
28 Khaix an,
Maw-si rieb tix teei priex preem in kix khaing ka daux tawm taix kix,
mai gleeing tix tan ka piang khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
In hoik mawh krax tan son pa ap pong ara cao giex kaing li.
Taox pa si oe bhawng Krax-tan Ngu in mawh pa muh Simiang Siyiex ka heue.
29 Khaix an,
Maw-si teei nex eek,
kieh mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex;
in hoik mawh nyum pa caw Maw-si tix pon kah.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex heue.
30 Maw-si teei bee pa singax singeh tix blah mai nham pa koe ka piang khu dee tan tix blah,
phruah ka piang to kaux Ya-loeng mai ka piang kawn simex nawh khankix,
mai baox phruah ka piang kraung ceep kix.
Yuh nin,
nawh hoik kieh kix mai kraung ceep kix guah tix lih mawh pa singax singeh,
mai tan ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
31 Maw-si krai ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix ah nin:
“Pex caw tix hawt ka gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
mai viex nex hoek ka diang siviex nyiex buan man pa singax singeh Simiang Siyiex,
mai ngaik dee an ih dee an,
khaix an baox ih ka khao mun pa koe ka daux khrawng,
pa tan son pa ap pong ara cao giex kaing li.
32 Nex pa kawn deuk mai khao mun,
tag sang gleeing ka ngu khawm uik.
33 Pex caw tix ot ka diang siviex nyiex buan man pa singax singeh aliah singaix,
mai ang pon tix lih hu,
krox jun tom hoik si dauh ap pong ara cao giex kaing li.
34 Pa yuh ex ka daux singaix hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex ka ex;
gum lox tawm in hoik mawh pa yuh lih son sang pluat ploi ka phi map ex.
35 Pex tag caw tix ot ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man aliah singaix,
leeing ngaix leeing sawm ot dee an,
hawt tix yuh ka gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex.
Phan ang mawh nan pex tag yum.
In hoik mawh gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka aux heue.”
36 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.
