Chang-suing ot ka Cia-sa
16
Koe tix ngaix,
Chang-suing hoek ka veng Cia-sa pui Fe-li-see dee an,
yaox ka sibun coh kaux tix tix kaux,
tom it mai nawh.
2 Yam yaox pui Cia-sa ot Chang-suing ka daux veng,
kix tom lai lawm dee an,
mai mox tix kuan nawh ka diang siviex veng leeing sawm.
Kix ang nyang bax giex ka nawh,
kheu kix keud ka daux rhawm tix:
“Ex krox praix riang ha tom tag pauh ka nawh.”
3 Viang mawh nan,
Chang-suing it hoek greung sawm nawh tom kaoh,
mai yieh siviex veng laux,
khawm pang siviex mai khlawp siviex,
jhong siviex khankix hoik yieh laux uik,
klawm ot ka piang klip tix,
mai viex hu ka choek gawng dee kok jhak pui ka haktiex Si-paw-loeng heue.
Chang-suing mai Tai-li-la
4 Khaix an,
Chang-suing ot ka daux ngawd Saw-liie,
mai muh kawn nyawm bun ka tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Tai-li-la.
5 Koe simiang pui Fe-li-see phuan kaux hoek ka Tai-li-la,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix nyaw glaw ka Chang-suing,
sawg tix yaox yuh ka mawx grawng pa kied ting riang rao nawh,
yix tag sang pon chix pex yuh ka mawx ka nawh,
phrawk ka nawh,
kieh nawh nge.
Phan maix yuh nin,
yix tag sang tawx maix ih mau luah tix kaux tix rheng mai tix yieh heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Tai-li-la chawk Chang-suing ah nin:
“Krai ka aux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ting riang rao yuh nin.
Phan sang phrawk ka maix,
mai kieh maix nge,
ex sang yuh ka mawh lie?”
7 Chang-suing ah nin:
“Phan kix teei maox im pa khraox duing tix aliah nyaing phrawk aux kah,
mai aux tag sang awm pui tang kaux khankix ang lai koe riang heue.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Fe-li-see teei maox im pa khraox duing tix aliah nyaing tawx ka Tai-li-la,
nawh tom phrawk Chang-suing kah.
9 Nawh hoik dawg kieh pui pau ot ka daux nyiex tix.
Nawh kok yo ah nin:
“Chang-suing eue,
pui Fe-li-see hoek!”
Chang-suing greeik riang rao tix,
maox duing an awm man keei sum pa hoik theeh ka ngu yuh nan,
dum mawx dum an tag dut.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix ca kawn ang nyang bax tawng riang rao Chang-suing kaoh khaing dee mawx heue.
10 Tai-li-la ah nin ka Chang-suing:
“Maix hoik dieb pon yuh ka aux,
mai pie lhien ka aux!
Krai aux mhong,
yuh ka mawx tag sang pon tix phrawk ka maix lie?”
11 Chang-suing ah nin:
“Teei maox khraox duing pa ang nyang sax siao tix bawg kawx phrawk aux kah,
mai aux tag sang awm pui tang kaux khankix ang lai koe riang heue.”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Tai-li-la teei maox pa khraox duing an mai phrawk nawh kah,
khaix an kok yo ah nin:
“Chang-suing eue,
pui Fe-li-see hoek!”
Koe pui khankix hoik kuan mox tix ot ka daux nyiex.
Chang-suing rud maox khaing plawng taix tix,
hoik awm rud maox keei yuh nan khaing heue.
13 Tai-li-la ah nin ka Chang-suing:
“Maix ca kawn nieb pon yuh ka aux,
mai pie lhien ka aux!
Krai aux mhong,
yuh ka mawx tag sang pon tix giex ka maix lie?”
Chang-suing ah nin:
“Phan maix daing hauk kaing pa duing aux aliah gawng laig ka daux cag taing man,
mai teei jim rhiem tawk tom kaux,
aux tag sang awm pui tang kaux ang lai koe riang heue.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Tai-li-la glaw nawh laig it,
mai duing hauk duing pa koe piang kaing nawh aliah gawng laig ka daux cag taing mana,
mai teei jim rhiem tawk tom kaux,
khaix an kok yo ah nin:
“Chang-suing eue,
pui Fe-li-see hoek!”
Chang-suing haing,
dum mawx dum an,
nawh bluh hauk kaing tix lih khaing daux cag taing man heue.
15 Tai-li-la ah nin ka Chang-suing:
“Maix ang doh muh ka aux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai krai muh tix aux!
Maix hoik nieb pon yuh ka aux loe bawg,
mai ang krai ka aux,
maix ting riang rao yuh ka mawx.”
16 Tai-li-la chawk cheng nawh ku singaix,
nawh hoik sak ka lox pa lak lheet moeng tix,
17 dee si dauh nawh tom krai grawng pa raux pa mox in ka Tai-li-la.
Nawh ah nin:
“Hauk kaing aux ang aux nyang sax khlao tix bawg kawx,
mawh kheu yam taug keh aux hoik tan lu tix ka Siyiex,
son sang mawh pui pa tang guah tix khaing been pui haktiex.
Phan hauk kaing aux khlao tix,
aux tag sang grai riang rao,
hoik awn riang pui tang kaux khankix yuh nan heue.”
18 Tai-li-la tawng grawng hoik krai Chang-suing tix mhong lox pa raux pa mox an ka nawh,
tom ploi pui hu krai ka simiang pui Fe-li-see ah nin:
“Viex pex baox hoek tix bawg;
nawh hoik krai aux mhong lox pa raux pa mox an.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom baox hoek,
mai viex mau hoek.
19 Tai-li-la glaw Chang-suing it ka piang ba tix,
khaix an kok pui hoek,
mai khlao tix tik hauk duing Chang-suing aliah gawng khaing kaing nawh.
Khaix an,
Tai-li-la kha ka Chang-suing,
kheu hoik grai riang rao nawh tete.
20 Tai-li-la kok yo ah nin:
“Chang-suing eue,
pui Fe-li-see hoek!”
Chang-suing haing,
mai keud ka daux rhawm tix:
“Aux sang yuh awm sivoe an,
greeik tix dum mawx dum an,
to kaux aux tag sang luat luan.”
Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang tawng Simiang Siyiex hoik hu singai khaing tix.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Fe-li-see pon giex ka nawh,
mai koik tawm ngai nawh lih khawm ra khawn,
viex nawh hu ka Cia-sa,
teei maox ceng phrawk nawh kah,
kieh nawh krid mhaw ot ka daux thawng.
22 Viang mawh nan,
hauk kaing Chang-suing deeh koi koi lang heue.
Grawng yum Chang-suing
23 Simiang pui Fe-li-see khrawm paox tix yuh poi ting,
son sang yuh krax tan lu ka siyiex kix Ta-koeng;
kix rhax lai ah nin:
“Siyiex ex hoik ap pui koeng Chang-suing ka daux tawm taix ex!”
24-25 Kix yuh krax gaux rhawm,
tom kok yo ah nin:
“Teei Chang-suing lih,
ex sang map nyiah ka nawhb!”
Kix tom teei Chang-suing lih khaing daux thawng;
mai kix map nyiah ka nawhc,
kieh nawh cung si nax rong ra nyaing.
Pui haoh pui hun yaox Chang-suing mai yaog yaw siyiex cie kix ah nin:
“Pui koeng Chang-suing dug dig ka haktiex ex,
mai pauh pui yum tom nie;
siyiex ex hoik ap nawh ka daux tawm taix ex!”
26 Chang-suing ah nin ka kawn nyawm pa tuk taix nawh:
“Kieh taix aux giex ka rong pa joh nyiex vad in kaux liak;
aux sang neung tix ka heue.”
27 Yam an ka daux nyiex vad hoik naog ka simex bun khawm uik,
khawm simiang pui Fe-li-see phuan kaux kawx hoik ot tan.
Ka piang choek nyiex vad hoik koe been loe rheng kaux bun simex,
mai kuan tix jhak grawng pa map nyiahd pui ka Chang-suing.
28 Chang-suing rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong eue,
rawg maix doh keud ka aux liak!
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix baox tawx riang rao ka aux tix bawg,
tawx aux pawk bix tix bawg ka piang pui Fe-li-see,
kheu pa hoik koik kix tawm ngai aux khawm ra khawn.”
29 Chang-suing kawm ka rong pa joh nyiex vad kaux,
pa ot sigreung an ra gawng,
tix gawng kawm tix khawn taix,
30 kok yo ah nin:
“Kieh aux yum tau mai pui Fe-li-see heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom plih riang rao tix lih khawm uik,
mai nyheen hu plak sivoe ngai,
nyiex vad an tom ghrup,
mai dheem simiang an kix khawm pui hun khawm uik.
Yam yum Chang-suing,
can pui pa pauh nawh yum,
kawn lheu nie khaing pui pa pauh nawh yum yam kawn im ju nawh heue.
31 Pux aik Chang-suing mai pui nyiex ma nawh,
hoek teei ka to kaux yum nawh,
mai tang nawh ing,
si paung nawh ot si nax haktiex Saw-la mai Yi-seu-thao,
dee ot si meeig keeing nawh Ma-naw-yax.
Chang-suing yuh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex tix nga num heue.
