Khiox khaw krax pa caox rhawm
5
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka krax pa si pup yix,
mai lam lia ka krax kaig krax tua pa kham yix heue.
 
2 Sin ngao yix hoik laux leeing ka daux tawm taix pui pa ang yix sax tawng sax yung khankix;
pui meung tang meung hoik laig paung ot ka daux nyiex ma yix heue.
 
3 Yix hoik lih mawh kawn doe pa ang koe keeing;
miex yix hoik lih mawh miex mai heue.
 
4 Yix caw tix tawx mau tom taug yaox tix nyaux rawm,
pie khix pa sang taung yix kawn caw tix liag heue.
 
5 Yix awm brung moi pa grheu pui hu,
yam taug mao ka yuh tix kaing,
mai ang yaox tix laux lee tix heue.
 
6 Son sang yaox yix tix sawm,
yix caw tix kawn khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka pui Ai-cix,
ka pui Ya-sux heue.
 
7 Tax du yix hoik yuh ka phi map,
ka daux in singaix kix ang lai koe,
viang mawh nan,
yix kawn kha rhawm tix kheu phi map kix heue.
 
8 Yix mawh siliex cawng pa giex gang pui ka piang;
ang koe pui pa pon taoh yix luat luan khaing tawm taix kix heue.
 
9 Pui caox pa pauh pui yum hoik koe ka daux tiag daux tu ku dauh ku dee;
son sang sawg tix ih ka priex preem,
yix ang lai keud grawng sang som ju tix heue.
 
10 Yix hoik saux si aoh kheu cai rhawm tix,
hak yix hoik hax awm mawk ngu yuh nan heue.
 
11 Pui hoik giex tix phit tau mai moeng yix ka piang gawng Shi-ang;
pui hoik giex tix phit tau mai kawn bun yix ka nyiex yaong Yo-tax heue.
 
12 Pui hoik phrawk tix teuk teue simiang yix;
pui ang nab yam yung yix ka kuad song yix heue.
 
13 Pui hoik kieh pui awn ju yix kha rhawm tix awm siliex cawng pa krid mhaw yuh nan;
kawn nyawm yix hoik goh kheu peeh tix khix khaox heue.
 
14 Kuad song ang lai baox ngawm ka diang siviex veng;
pui pa nhum ju ang lai baox deeh tauh ka ting heue.
 
15 Krax gaux rhawm hoik grai hu khaing daux rhawm rhi yix;
krax laux rhawm hoik lih tix mawh tang son pa graoh heue.
 
16 Mhok khri yix hoik cawd khaing kaing yix;
yix hoik yuh phi map,
ka daux singaix in tom yaox ka krax laux krax leeing heue.
 
17 Yix hoik si kua mai vu vi son kaing in kix;
rawm ngai yix hoik kieh ngai yix pu pi ang lai sidawng heue.
 
18 Mawh kheu hoik yien hoik nhik gawng Shi-ang,
sox la hoik vung viang lai ling ka dee riem heue.
 
19 Khaix an Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix giex ka siawp sibrawm leeing ju;
pa giex maix kaing ka piang ang chix grai leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
20 Yuh ka mawx maix lai tik vud ka yix tom rheeng lie?
Maix sang deeh doh keud ka yix yam mawx lie?
 
21 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh kieh yix loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai deeh hoek ka maix.
Khiox khaw maix deeh tawx min plak ngai pa koe yix sivoe an heue!
 
22 Khex kawn sang tieb tie maix ka yix leeing ju leeing phan lie?
Khex kawn ang maix sang chix naox ka krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix leeing ju lie?
