Mex moeng
3
aAwm an,
pa mawh moeng khankix,
pex kawx caw tix nghiet ka lox mex tix,
son sang kieh mex pex pa ang bax rieb ka gum lox Siyiex pon doh rhawm kheu pa mhawm jhawm pex.
Pex ang siao krai lox tom nie,
2 mawh kheu kix hoik chix yaox ka grawng pa singax pex mai pa mhawm jhawm pex.
3 bPex paw vi kieh krax pa jawg pex greeh grang tix mawh pa pie lhien ka to kaux tix,
paw vi jawg tix son sang duing ka haok kaing tix yuh ka mawx,
jawg tix ka kraung mau khri ceu pa tix,
ceep ka kraung ceu pa tix.
4 Pex tag caw kieh tix mhawm plak daux,
teei ceu pa awn rhawm awn rhi pa yien yawg pa ang chix laux an jawg tix kah;
in mawh pa kied koe ngawh jum ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
5 Mawh kheu bun miex an kix pa een rhawm tix ka pa singax singeh cie Siyiex ka daux singaix pa hoik luan kawx,
hoik teei krax cu nghiet ka mex tix mawh pa jawg to kaux tix.
6 cSa-la kawx yuh nan nang;
nawh cu nghiet ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai kok nawh mawh “simiang”.
Phan pex yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
ang lhat ka ceu pa tid tix,
pex tag sang mawh kawn simex kawn bun Sa-la khawm uik heue.
7 dAwm an,
pa mawh mex khankix,
pex ot tau mai moeng tix,
tag caw tix chix kia ngien ka kix kheu mawh kix pa iak riang iak rao.
Tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka kix,
mawh kheu hoik pon kix ka ju pa khraox pa tawx Siyiex awm pex yuh nan.
Phan pon yuh nin,
lox rawg buan pex tag ang hoek krax pa tieb tie heue.
Kham gee krax kha rhawm son pa rauh see
8 Lox pa krai khawm uik mawh nin:
pex tag caw tix ih rhawm paox tix,
ra lai chix caox rhawm ka paox tix,
muh paox tix awm muh to kaux tix yuh nan,
mai teei krax pa caox rhawm ra lai yeung ka paox tix.
9 Paw vi pawk pa caox son pa caox,
pa jhax lawg son pa jhax lawg;
deeh tieh tix,
teei krax pa nawb buan vied,
mawh kheu Siyiex kok pex kaing ting mawh son sang tawx buan son ka pex.
10 eAwm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Pui mawx sang pon krax pa gaux rhawm kawn pui,
simie tix mhawm krax ot krax koe,
tag caw tix rien dak tix khaing lox caox,
rien siyieb jheeh tix khaing lox pie lox lhien.
11 Nawh tag caw tix tik krax pa caox hawt krax pa mhawm,
sau riang tix leue ka daux krax pa nhim pa tiiem heue.
12 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka pui rauh pui see,
mai nghiet ka lox rawg kix buan;
viang mawh nan,
nawh tag veeh pak ngai tix khaing pui pa yuh pa caox heue.
13 Phan pex gaux rhawm tix yuh kaing,
pui mawx sang chix yuh pex laux lie?
14 fgViang hoik kha rhawm tix ka son pa rauh pa see kawx,
mai kied mawh nang pa koe buan son heue!
Paw vi lhat ka krax pa ghlat pui,
mai paw vi yaoh yee nang.
15 Tag caw tix koe rhawm pa lhat graung yung yi ka Khrit,
kieh nawh mawh simiang.
Koe pui khiox khaw pex ploi ka krax keud pot jun pot pa koe ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
tag caw rien khre tix pawk lox son tix ku bawg ku yam;
16 viang mawh nan,
tag caw tix pawk lox pui ka daux rhawm awn rhawm tiiem.
Tag caw tix koe rhawm rhi pa chix keud pa singax singeh,
kieh pui pa map nai ka pex,
salao ka pex deeh kaig tua ka yaox tix kaing mhawm pa yuh pex lih son mawh pex kawn Khrit.
17 Phan kha rhawm tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo Siyiex,
mai ca kawn lheu mhawm khaing kham krax kha rhawm ka yuh tix kaing ceu pa caox pa caig.
18 Mawh kheu Khrit kawx hoik yuh nin tix bawg khaing,
mai hoik pon yumh son phi map khawm uik,
in mawh pa rauh see pa tang son pa ang rauh see,
son sang viex hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
Pui hoik pauh nex nham nawh yum,
viang mawh nan,
khuan ju nawh hoik pon taoh tix imi.
19 Nawh hoik sax teei khuan in hu sidiex ka khuan pui pa laung pui khankix.
20 jKhuan in kix ang yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
pa mawh khuan pa viex rhawm gun krox Siyiex yam yuh Naw-yax reu.
Yam an pui pa laig daux reu ang hun,
mawh ngie daix kaux khaing,
kix pon krax taoh khaing daux rawm;
21 rawm in hoik mawh pa jhi grawng been pa cub com.
Been cub com in,
yam in hoik taoh ka pex,
ang mawh pa khoek ka ceu pa seeing brai lih khaing to kaux pex,
hoik mawh pa teei rhawm rhi pa chix keud pa singax ap pong tix ka Siyiex;
been cub com in mawh pa hoik taoh pex ka daux pa deeh kaoh im Ye-sux Khrit.
22 Ye-sux Khrit hoik haok piang rao ma,
yam in ot ka plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex,
mai giex kaing ka piang kawn cau Siyiex mai ka piang simiang cao giex kaing rao ma heue.
