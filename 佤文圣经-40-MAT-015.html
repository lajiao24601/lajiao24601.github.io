Been pa deuh tax tu
(Lai Ma-kheu 7.1–13)
15
Koe pui Fa-li-saix mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri kaoh khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
2 “Yuh ka mawx pui gau maix ang priex tix hawt ka been pa deuh tax du lie?
Sivoe sang sawm kix ang bax khoek taix tix awm been pa hoik tat tix deuh heue!”
3 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai yuh son sang priex tix hawt ka been pa deuh tax tu,
mai lheu gum lox tawm Siyiex lie?
4 aSiyiex ah nin:
‘Tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka miex keeing tix;’
mai baox ah nin:
‘Pa jhax lawg ka miex keeing tix,
tag caw tix pauh yum.’
5 Pex ca kawn krai,
phan koe pui pa ah nin ka miex keeing tix:
‘Aux hoik teei ka ceu pa eei tix pex kieh mawh khawng phak pa tan lu ka Siyiex,’
6 mai nawh ang siao lai nab yam yung yi ka miex keeing tix.
Pex teei been pa deuh tax du tix kieh mawh pa tieb tie ka gum lox Siyiex.
7 Pui pa pie lhien eue,
Yi-sai-yax krai lox jhai ka pex pa mawh cub pui pa pie lhien in kix hoik khaw tete!
Nawh ah nin:
Siyiex krai yuh nin:
8 bPui in kix teei siyieb jheeh tix nab yam yung yi ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi kix singai khaing aux.
9 Kix kieh been pa tat kawn pui tix deuh mawh gum lox tawm aux;
krax pa krub kix ka aux mawh pa giex going khawm uik heue!”
Ceu pa kieh pui ang singax
(Lai Ma-kheu 7.14–23)
10 Ye-sux khrawm pui hun hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw tix nghiet,
tag caw tix tawng heue!
11 Ceu pa laig daux jheeh ang chix kieh pui ang singax;
mawh pa lih khaing daux jheeh pa kieh pui ang singax heue.”
12 Khaix an pui gau krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui Fa-li-saix hoik mhong ka lox maix in,
kix ang vhah rhawm kah,
hoik tawng maix ka lie?”
13 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Can khaox ox pa ang mawh keeing aux Siyiex meung rao ma pa sum khankix tag caw tix laoh tix tik khawm riah khawm uik.
14 cPaw vi simi ka kix heue!
Kix mawh pui dug pa thung pui;
pui dug thung pui dug lawng krax,
kiex khawm ra kaux tag sang cawd daux o heue.”
15 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix kah dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem in ka yix liak.”
16 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Tom yam in pex kawn ang tawng yung awm pui tang kaux lie?
17 Khex ang pex tawng,
ceu pa laig daux jheeh pui hoek ka daux vaig pui,
khaix an pui baox deeh aing lih lie?
18 dViang mawh nan,
pa kaoh khaing daux jheeh pui mawh pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm nawh,
ceu an tag chix kieh pui ang singax.
19 Mawh kheu pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm pui mawh pa caox caig ku ceu;
pa caox caig in kix kieh nawh yuh phi map pauh pui,
phit vao,
phit vao mai pui,
briex lak,
pie lhien,
map nai khankix.
20 In kix khawm uik tag chix kieh pui ang singax.
Phan mawh grawng pa ang khoek taix tix sivoe sang sawm ang chix kieh pui ang singax heue.”
Krax yum rhawm bun tix kaux
(Lai Ma-kheu 7.24–30)
21 Ye-sux lih khaing haktiex an,
mai mox tix hoek ka Thai-eux mai Si-toeng haktiex pa dex baoh an.
22 Koe bun Cia-nang tix kaux hoek ka nawh,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
kawn praix Ta-wue,
caox rhawm ka aux heue!
Kawn bun aux phi laig ka daux nawh,
mai kied kha rhawm tix heue.”
23 Ye-sux ang pawk lox nawh pie tix gum.
Pui gau hoek tix khiox khaw ka nawh,
mai ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix nhie nawh yoi heue!
Nawh hawt khaix ex mai kok yo leeing lawng krax heue!”
24 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux hoek sawg ngie ka kawn yung pa grai ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex khaing heue.”
25 Bun an mhong lox in,
tom grux ngawng tix krub ka sivoe caong nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix teem coi ka aux liak!”
26 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Teei tix tawx kawn sox ih ka priex preem cie kawn simex kawn bun ang khaw heue.”
27 Bun an ah nin:
“Mawh tete,
simiang eue,
viang mawh nan,
kawn sox kawx chix tix ih ka pa max pa phawng pa cawd khaing piang pheen sawm cao tix lie!”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Bun,
krax yum rhawm maix kied ting heue!
Hawt tix yuh awm pa sang lo maix heue!”
Kawn bun nawh tom kaoh ka daux bawg yam an heue.
Pai moi ka pui pa koe jhawm saux ku ceu
29 Ye-sux lih khaing haktiex an,
mai hoek ka praok rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix.
Nawh haok ngawm ka piang gawng.
30 Cub pui hun baox hoek tix sawg ka nawh,
mai viex pui pa koe jhawm saux chawh caong,
dug ngai,
laux meung,
pui ang chix nye,
mai pui pa koe jhawm saux ku ceu hoek,
deuh ot ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux;
Ye-sux pai kix moi ku kaux.
31 Cub pui hun an kix yaox hoik chix si kah pui ang chix nye,
hoik mhawm pui laux meung,
hoik hu pui chawh caong krax,
yaox hoik riang ngai pui dug,
kied daox deng ka tete,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Ye-sux kieh pui pon rheng kaux sawm sak
(Lai Ma-kheu 8.1–10)
32 Ye-sux kok pui gau hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Aux kied caox rhawm caox rhi ka cub pui in kix,
kix hoik ot tau mai aux loe singaix,
yam in ang koe ceu pa ih tid tix.
Aux ang rhawm kieh kix deeh ing khawm cai rhawm tix,
lhat kix goh lawng krax heue.”
33 Pui gau ah nin:
“Ka dee ang pui koe yuh nin,
ex sang hu sawg priex preem tom kub pui in kix ih sak dee mawx lie?”
34 Ye-sux chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Pex koe khao mun miex ding lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Aliah lon mai kax iak ra loe mu heue.”
35 Ye-sux tawx lox nhie pui hun ngawm piang tiex,
36 teei khao mun an aliah lon mai kax iak an ra loe mu yaog yaw Siyiex,
si vit kah,
tawx ka pui gau tix;
tom guah tix tawx ka pui hun.
37 Pui hun ih kah,
mai hoik ih sak.
Pui gau khrawm pa max phawng pa deuk khankix,
hoik naog aliah khrawng.
38 Pui pa hoik ih sak lheu khaing bun mai kawn nyawm,
koe pon rheng kaux heue.
39 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux kieh pui hun prung prang tix hu,
khaix an nawh laig daux reu,
mai hu plak haktiex Ma-cia-tang heue.
