Krax salao Tha-pe-la
11
Kawn pui yaox krax pa nu,
tom jauh tix salao ka Simiang Siyiex.
Yam mhong Simiang Siyiex kah,
nawh tom song rhawm,
mai kieh ngu lih ka daux si nax kix,
gleeing ka bang ot kix ka daog.
2 Kawn pui riag yo ka Maw-si.
Maw-si rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
ngu an tom yeet.
3 Kok ceu kaux dee an mawh Tha-pe-laa,
kheu ngu Simiang Siyiex hoik gleeing kix dee an heue.
Maw-si laug ka kuad song atigliah kaux
4 Koe pui tang ceu hu tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex;
kix kied simie tix ih nex.
Pui Yi-seu-liex jauh tix salao ah nin:
“Phan ex yaox tix ih nex kied mhawm!
5 Yam ot ex ka haktiex Ai-cix,
ex ih kax jheeng jheeng,
ang siao liag ka mau;
kawn koe ke,
ke lai,
lhax ka,
lhax mo,
lhax haw ku ceu pa nyawm tix ih.
6 Yam in ex ang lai koe riang.
Lheu khaing ih ex Ma-na ku ngaix,
ang yaox ceu tid tix heue!”
7 bPak peung Ma-na awm simie taux ki,
mai paing awm rawm teeh,
8-9 cpon bo ku ngaix lih awm rawm ciex ka daux bang.
Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
kawn pui hu sut kah,
krid phawng lih mawh mien,
khaix an ngaik yuh mawh khao mun.
Jhawm nyawm awm khao mun pa hoik sau bee sime.
10 Maw-si mhong yiiem kawn pui khaing diang siviex nyiex bang kix,
pui si briem kah,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka kix;
Maw-si kied laux rhawm kah,
11 Nawh ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh nin ka aux?
Yuh ka mawx ang muh ka aux?
Yuh ka mawx kieh aux mawh cao thung pui khankix?
12 Aux ang baox phan yuh ka kix,
keh ka kix,
yuh ka mawx maix sang kieh aux mawh miex eei kix,
klien kix awm kawn nyawm ka daux deem tix,
klien kix tom hoek ka haktiex pa tiex tu maix tix tawx ka tax du kix?
13 Aux sang pon hu teei nex tom nie dee mawx mai tawx kix ih lie?
Kix si briem ang jud tix sang ih nex!
14 Aux tix kaux khaing ang koe rhawm keud tix kuang ka kix;
aux ang pon kham!
15 Phan maix sang yuh nin ka aux,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux,
pauh aux yum,
son ang aux sang yaox tix kham gee ka kaing in heue.”
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix khrawm kuad song atigliah kaux pa yung yi pui kah,
viex kix hoek ka nyiex gox buan man ka sivoe aux,
mai cung praok maix.
17 Aux sang lih hoek si kah mai maix dee an.
Aux sang guah tix tawx khuan pa tawx aux ka maix ka kix,
kix tom pon teem maix klawm ka kaing in;
to kaux maix ang siao lai klawm nong nong.
18 Maix tawx lox ka kawn pui ah nin:
‘Pa sax pui haoh pui hun tag caw tix kieh to kaux tix singax,
pex tag yaox tix ih nex.
Simiang Siyiex mhong ka lox pa salao pex,
krai simie pex tix ih nex,
pex hoik keud haktiex Ai-cix kawn nyawm tix ot.
Simiang Siyiex tom tawx pex ih nex,
pex tag yaox tix nyawm ih.
19 Pex ang joeng ih tix ngaix ra ngaix,
phuan ngaix,
kao ngaix,
tix nga ngaix.
20 Pex tag yaox tix ih nex been tix khix,
tom hoik lih nex khaing daux meeh pex,
kieh pex seeing kah.
Kaing in sang lih tix mawh tete;
mawh kheu tik vud pex ka Simiang Siyiex pa ot si nax pex,
salao ka nawh,
ah ang pex khaw tix lih hu khaing Ai-cix heue.’ ”
21 Maw-si ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Can pui pa viex aux koe tix gliah mheen haoh,
maix kawx ah sang pon tawx kix ih nex been tix khix lie?
22 Khex koe moi yung tix pauh tom nie mai kieh naog yuh rhawm rhi kix lie?
Pie kax pa ot daux rawm ang tau kub kix ih!”
23 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox:
“Aux khex ang koe riang tix pon yuh lie?
Maix tag yaox ka lox ah aux sang lih tix mawh ang mawh!”
24 Khaix an Maw-si lih hu,
mai krai lox Simiang Siyiex ka kawn pui.
Nawh khrawm ka kuad song atigliah kaux,
kieh kix cung ka vung viang nyiex gox buan man.
25 Simiang Siyiex lih hoek khaing daux pai awm,
mai si kah ka Maw-si,
mai teei khuan pa tawx ka Maw-si guah tix tawx ka kuad song an kix atigliah kaux.
Yam lih khuan ka kix,
kix kok yo awm cao jhai,
viang mawh nan,
ang rheeng heue.
26 Ka daux kix koe pui kuad ju ra kaux pa kaux Yi-li-ta mai Mi-tax kawn ot ka daux bang,
ang bax hu ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
Ka daux bang,
khuan lih ka piang kiex,
kiex kawx kok yo awm cao jhai.
27 Koe pui awn ju tix kaux hu ka Maw-si,
mai krai grawng pa yuh Yi-li-ta mai Mi-tax ka nawh heue.
28 Yam in,
Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng ah lox tix.
Yaw-su-yax jauh tom awn ju,
nawh mawh cao pa teem coi Maw-si.
Nawh ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Simiang aux,
tieb tie ka kiex liak.”
29 Maw-si pawk lox:
“Maix boh rhawm jao aux lie?
Aux gaux rhawm kieh Siyiex tawx khuan tix ka piang pui hun cie nawh khawm uik,
kieh kix kok yo awm cao jhai!”
30 Khaix an,
Maw-si mai kuad song Yi-seu-liex atigliah kaux deeh ing ka nyiex bang heue.
Simiang Siyiex tawx keed lih
31 Dum mawx dum an,
Simiang Siyiex kieh bhaung lih phlu,
keed tom nie pu hoek khaing daux rawm sigrit sigra,
lhaong khaing haktiex been tix kongjhee.
Kix tom kaog ka dee nyiex bang mai vung viang bang ku dauh ku dee,
khawm pon daog haktiex been ra loe konglid.
32 Pui khankix giex ka keed leeing ngaix leeing sawm;
ngaix khaix kawx kawn giex kah,
tix kaux pui ah yawm tix pon giex keed tix rheng kongcing.
Kix hok keed kroh hawt vung viang bang.
33 Yam kawn yaox kix tix ih nex tom nie,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka kix,
kieh saux preng lih piang kix.
34 Dee an kok mawh “daux si meeig pa ghoing”,
mawh kheu si paung kix ka pui pa ghoing tix ih nex dee an.
35 Jauh haktiex dee an,
kawn pui gah tix hu ka haktiex Ha-si-lux,
mai yuh bang dee an heue.
