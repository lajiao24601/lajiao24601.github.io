Ka daux phuk phuan
(Lai Yaog Yaw 107–150)
Rhax lai yaog yaw pa mhawm pa mhiem Siyiex
107
aTag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem nawh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
2 Pui pa hoik taoh nawh khankix eue,
tag caw tix deeh baox rhax laix yaog yaw in;
nawh taoh pex luat luan khaing daux tawm taix pui koeng,
3 jauh ka plak lih,
ka plak klip,
ka plak sehb ka plak laong,
viex pex hoek khaing meung ku meung heue.
 
4 Koe pui tix blah lud krax mai vu vie tix ka daux tiag daux tu dee ang pui koe,
ang yaox ka krax pa sang hu ot ka daux veng.
5 Kix cai rhawm mai sang nyaux nang rawm,
krax keud pot jun pot kix hoik grai heue.
6 Ka daux krax pa khi rhawm,
kix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh taoh kix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing.
7 Nawh thung kix hu krax pa rauh pa see,
tom hoek yaox kix tix ot ka daux veng.
8 Kix tag caw tix yaog yaw krax muh Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh.
9 Nawh hoik kieh pui pa sang nyaux rawm yaox tix nyaux tom sak,
hoik kieh pui pa cai rhawm yaox tix ih priex preem tom sak heue.
 
10 Koe pui tix blah ot ka daux pa faik mai ka daux veei krax yum,
maox ceng pa kieh kha rhawm tix hoik prawk kah;
11 Mawh kheu kix hoik lieh lheu ka gum lox tawx Siyiex,
hoik tieb tie ka krax pa sidiex gau cao pa ting siawp sibrawm.
12 Kix yuh kaing pa taug pa mao,
mai hoik kied kroh krawx;
kix goh,
ang koe pui pa pon teem coi ka kix.
13 Ka daux krax pa khi rhawm,
kix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh taoh kix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing.
14 Nawh viex kix lih khaing daux pa faik veei krax yum,
rud dut ka ceng pa hoik prawk ka kix.
15 Kix tag caw tix yaog yaw ka krax muh Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh.
16 Nawh hoik yuh siviex tawng laux,
hoik tat dut ka jhong siviex rhiem heue.
 
17 Koe pui tix blah hoik pon jhawm sauxc kheu phi map tix,
hoik kha rhawm rhi tix kheu pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix;
18 kix ang lai pon ih ka priex preem,
hoik dex ka daog meung yum.
19 Ka daux krax pa khi rhawm,
kix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh hoik taoh kix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing,
20 Nawh teei gum lox tix pai ka jhawm saux kix moi,
taoh kix luat luan khaing krax yum.
21 Kix tag caw tix yaog yaw krax muh Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh.
22 Kix tag khaw tix tan lu krax tan pa yaog yaw ka nawh,
mai teei lox rhax krax pa yaog yaw krai lao ka kaing pa yuh nawh heue.
 
23 Koe pui tix blah brug ka reu hu,
sawg krax pa im ju ka piang rawm sigrit sigra.
24 Kix yaox ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
yaox ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh ka dee raux rawm.
25 Nawh tawx lox tix,
bhaung ting phlu tix hoek,
kieh rawm ting puk pak.
26 Reu pu pliak tix haok ka rao ma,
mai baox com hoek ka dee kied raux rawm;
krax khi krax lhat hoik kieh kix ang lai koe riang koe rao.
27 Kix vawk jee vawk blaong tix,
awm pui pa yoek ka plai yuh nan;
Krax pa chix keud chix sawn kix hoik grai khawm uik.
28 Ka daux krax pa khi rhawm,
kix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh taoh kix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing.
29 Nawh kieh bhaung ting yien,
kieh puk pak rawm yien yawg tix.
30 Kix hoik gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu pa yien yawg bhaung;
nawh viex kix hoek ka dee sang hu kix ka diang praok rawm sigrit sigra dee nyawm ot.
31 Kix tag sang yaog yaw krax muh Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh.
32 Kix khaw tix krai pao grawng pa ting pa lhaong nawh ka dee bang pui haoh pui hun,
rhax lai yaog yaw nawh ka dee bang kum pui kuad song.
 
33 Simiang Siyiex kieh rawm klawng tieh tix lih mawh haktiex dee kroh,
hoik thox ka dee buing rawm keet tix lih.
34 Nawh kieh haktiex dee mhawm tieh tix lih mawh haktiex dee lih kih pa riem,
mawh kheu pui pa ot dee an kied caox caig.
35 Nawh kieh pak mhaik lih mawh nhawng,
kieh haktiex dee kroh lih koe rawm keet.
36 Nawh kieh pui pa cai rhawm yaox tix paung ot dee an,
hoik sum ka veng pa kieh pui chix paung ot ka tix mu.
37 Kix sum kaung sum ma,
mai sum ka khaox plix siiet.
Hoik pon ka plix tom nie.
38 Nawh tawx buan ka pui cie tix,
mai kieh kawn praix kix haoh hun;
nawh kieh khawng brie kix haoh hun tom nie heue.
 
39 Khaix an kheu krax pa gut pa kheem mai krax pa laux pa leeing,
pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex baox pon tix kham ka krax pa tuk tiak tuk te,
krax pa kaig pa tua.
40 Siyiex ciyang ka pui pa gut kheem ka kix,
kieh kix lud krax ka daux tiag dee ang pui koe.
41 Viang mawh nan,
nawh taoh pui hot pui sibhawm luat luan khaing krax pa kha rhawm,
kieh pui daux nyiex ma kix haok huan awm phung yung yuh nan.
42 Pui rauh pui see yaox mai kied koe krax gaux rhawm rhi kah;
pui pa caox pa caig mai ang lai koe lox pa sang krai heue.
 
43 Kieh pui pa lhak lhiao si kaw ka kaing in;
kieh kix cim tix yaox krax muh Simiang Siyiex pa ang chix tieh loh tix heue.
