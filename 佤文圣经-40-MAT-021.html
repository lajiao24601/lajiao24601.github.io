Koe min plak ngai yam laig daux Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Ma-kheu 11.1–11; Lai Lu-cia 19.28–40; Lai Yaw-hang 12.12–19)
21
Kix hoek dex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hoek ka Paw-fa-chix baoh haktiex gawng sime.
Ye-sux tawx lox kieh pui gau tix ra kaux ran hu sivoe,
2 mai tawx lox ka kiex ah nin:
“Pax hu ka yaong pa koe sivoe.
Pax tag sang yaox la tix mu mai la iak tix mu prawk tix tau;
pax yah ka mai tuk hoek tawx ka aux.
3 Phan koe pui pa ah ceu tid tix ka pax,
tom krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Simianga sang siao’,
nawh tag sang kieh pax tuk hoek heue.”
4 Grawng kaing in tag sang kieh lih tix mawh awm gum lox pa hoik krai cao jhai ah nin:
5 bHu krai ka kawn simex kawn bun ka daux veng Shi-ang ah nin:
Jhak heue,
simiang pex hoek heue!
Nawh viex rhawm awn mai brug piang blawg la,
mai brug ka piang blawg la iak heue.
6 Pui gau ra kaux hawt tix yuh awm lox Ye-sux.
7 Kiex tuk la mai kawn la hoek,
teei kraung ceep tix tawm ka piang blawg la,
khaix an nhie Ye-sux brug.
8 Ka daux pui hun koe pui tom nie teei kraung ceep tix tawm ka daux krax,
koe pui tix blah teei kak khaox pa kid tawm ka daux krax.
9 cPui pa hu sivoe mai pa hawt khaix kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Rhax lai yaog yaw ka kawn praix Ta-wue heue!
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka pui an pa hoek tix krub ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex!
Rhax lai yaog yaw tawx ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong heue!”
10 Yam laig Ye-sux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
veng khawm mu hoik suk sag.
Koe pui chawk ah nin:
“Pui in mawh pui mawx lie?”
11 Pui hun pawk ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Ye-sux cao jhai pui veng Na-sha-leux haktiex Cia-li-lix heue.”
Ye-sux kieh nyiex buan singax singeh
(Lai Ma-kheu 11.15–19; Lai Lu-cia 19.45–48; Lai Yaw-hang 2.13–22)
12 Ye-sux laig ka daux nyiex buan,
mai grheu ka can pui pa coh cai lai gee ka daux nyiex buan lih.
Nawh klup tix tik ka pheen cie pui pa loh ka mau luah mai ka ngot cie pui pa coh ka si kaux khankix,
13 dmai ah nin ka kix:
“Ka daux phuk lai sigang khri Siyiex ah nin:
‘Nyiex buan aux tag sang kieh mawh nyiex dee rawg buan’,
viang mawh nan,
pex lai kieh lih mawh dee ot pui briex pui lak heue!”
14 Koe pui dug ngai mai pui chawh caong hoek tix sawg Ye-sux ka daux nyiex buan;
nawh tom pai kix moi.
15 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh Ye-sux,
mai baox mhong lox pa kok yo kawn nyawm ka daux nyiex buan ah nin:
“Rhax lai yaog yaw tawx ka kawn praix Ta-wue heue!”
Kix tom kied song rhawm heue.
16 eKix chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mhong maix lox pa kok yo kix ceu pa tix lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Mhong heue.
Ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin ‘Maix kieh kawn nyawm mai kawn ngie ploi lox rhax pa kied mhawm’ gum lox in,
ang pex bax phat lie?”
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux lih hu khaing kix,
mai lih khaing veng hoek ka Paw-ta-ni,
mai it paung dee an heue.
Jhax lawg ka khaox ruh
(Lai Ma-kheu 11.12–14; 20-24)
18 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
yam lawng krax ing ka daux veng,
Ye-sux cai rhawm.
19 Nawh yaox khaox ruh pa koe praok krax tix gawng,
nawh tom hu dex,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox ceu pa tid tix,
mai mawh ngie lhax pa koe khaing.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ci ka khaox an ah nin:
“Maix ang lai baox chix plix plix leeing ju heue!”
Khaox ruh an kroh dum mawx dum an heue.
20 Pui gau yaox grawng pa yuh nin,
kied daox deng kah.
Kix chawk ah nin:
“Khaox in yuh ka mawx lai kroh dum mawx dum an lie?”
21 fYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
phan pex koe krax yum rhawm,
yuh aux ka mawx ka khaox ruh in pex kawx pon yuh kah.
Lao pex ang pon yuh nin,
pex kawx tag sang pon ah nin ka gawng in:
‘Kaoh,
laig ka daux rawm sigrit sigra heue!’
Tag sang pon yuh lih.
22 Ca mawh koe krax yum rhawm pex,
ka daux pa rawg pex buan khawm uik tag sang pon ka heue.”
Chawk cheng ka akhuang cie Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-kheu 11.27–33; Lai Lu-cia 20.1–8)
23 Yam laig Ye-sux tix sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song pui Yo-thaix khankix hoek ka nawh,
chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh maix kaing in kix ka daux akhuang cie pui mawx?
Mawh mawx pa tawx akhuang in ka maix lie?”
24 Ye-sux pawk lox kix ah nin:
“Aux ran chawk pex ka lox tix gum,
phan pex pon pawk ka aux,
aux tag krai ka pex yuh aux kaing in kix ka daux akhuang pui mawx.
25 Akhuang cie Yaw-hang cao cub com kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?
Mawh kaoh khaing Siyiex,
mawh kaoh khaing kawn pui lie?”
Kix jauh tix ra lai thung thiang ka paox tix ah nin:
“Ex sang pawk ah ka mawx lie?
Phan ex krai ‘kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an’,
nawh tee ah nin:
‘Phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx pex lai ang yum rhawm ka Yaw-hang lie?’
26 Phan ex krai ‘kaoh khaing kawn pui dee an’,
ex lhat pui hun tieb tie ka ex,
mawh kheu kix hoik yum rhawm grawng mawh Yaw-hang cao jhai.”
27 Mawh kheu nan,
kix pawk lox Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yix ang tawng heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Phan mawh nan,
aux kawx ang krai ka pex yuh aux kaing in ka daux akhuang cie pui mawx heue.”
Phek tiiem kawn simex ra kaux
28 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Kawn koe gum lox phek tiiem tang ceu tix ceu,
aux simie tix tawng rhawm keud pex kah.
Koe pui tix kaux koe kawn simex ra kaux,
nawh krai ka kawn ai ah nin:
‘Kawn,
daux in singaix maix hu yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix shiet heue.’
29 Nawh pawk ah nin:
‘Aux ang hu.’
Viang mawh nan,
khaix an nawh deeh loh rhawm rhi tix mai deeh hu.
30 Keeing kawx krai ka kawn nyi awm krai ka kawn ai yuh nan nang.
Kawn nyi pawk ah nin:
‘Chix tix,
keeing,
aux sang hu.’
Viang mawh nan,
ang bax hu.
31 Kawn simex in kiex mawh pa kaux tix pa priex tix hawt ka lox keeing tix lie?”
Kix ah nin:
“Mawh kawn ai an heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
pui koh keen mai bun coh kaux tag sang lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex sivoe pex.
32 gMawh kheu nan,
Yaw-hang cao cub com hoek,
nawh jhi krax rauh krax see pa khaw pex tix sang lawng,
pex ang yum rhawm ka nawh;
viang mawh nan,
pui koh keen mai bun coh kaux tieh tix yum rhawm ka nawh.
Viang pex hoik yaox kah,
mai ca kawn ang loh rhawm rhi tix,
ang yum rhawm ka nawh heue.”
Phek tiiem ka pui cao vai si peem
(Lai Ma-kheu 12.1–12; Lai Lu-cia 20.9–19)
33 hYe-sux baox ah nin:
“Pex baox nghiet ka gum lox phek tiiem tang ceu tix ceu heue!
Koe cao nyiex si peem paih ka si peem plix shiet tix dauh,
mai lawm law die vung viang,
kaong daux greeik plai koe ka daux si peem tix mu,
mai sum nyiex pau koe ka tix lhang,
khaix an teei tix tawx pui vai ka si peem plix shiet,
to kaux nawh tom lih hu khaing nyiex.
34 Hoek bawg yam teem plix shiet,
nawh ploi siliex tix hu teei ka pa khaw nawh tix pon ka pui pa vai tix yuh si peem.
35 Cao vai si peem an giex ka siliex nawh,
mai lauh ka tix kaux,
pauh ka tix kaux,
mai baox teei simaox tawk ka tix kaux.
36 Cao nyiex si peem baox ploi siliex tang kaux hu,
kaux pui kix lheu khaing pa hu sivoe;
pui pa vai tix yuh si peem baox yuh ka kix yuh nan nang.
37 Dee nghoik,
cao nyiex si peem ploi kawn simex tix hu.
Nawh keud:
‘Kix tee rien tix nab yam yung yi ka kawn simex aux.’
38 Viang mawh nan,
yam yaox cao vai si peem ka kawn simex an,
mai ra lai ah nin ka paox tix:
‘In mawh pui pa sang plud ka cao nyiex si peem,
hoek heue,
ex pauh ka nawh,
tom tag pon tix teei ka sin ngao cie nawh heue!’
39 Mawh kheu nan,
kix giex ka nawh,
mai cot nawh lih plak praix si peem,
mai pauh ka nawh heue.
40 “Yuh nin,
yam deeh hoek cao nyiex si peem plix shiet,
nawh tag sang yuh ka mawx ka pui pa vai si peem an lie?”
41 Kix ah nin:
“Nawh tag sang pauh ka pui pa caox in kix,
mai deeh teei tix tawx si peem plix shiet ka pui vai pa pon tawx ngawh vai awm bawg yam pa hoik tat tix deuh heue.”
42 iYe-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Gum lox phuk lai sigang khri ang pex bax phat ka lie?
Simaox pa hoik tik cang mai yuh nyiex
hoik lih mawh simaox jaong nyiex pa blawn ting ceu.
In mawh kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh pa kied daox deng heue!”
43 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
Siyiex tag sang teei meung cie tix hu khaing daux pex,
mai tawx ka pui pa pon plix plix khankix.
44 Pui mawx goh piang simaox in,
pui an tag sang laux leeing;
simaox in cawd ka piang to kaux pui mawx,
tag sang lauh nang pui an max phawng vid heue.j”
45 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai pui Fa-li-saix khankix mhong lox phek tiiem pa krai Ye-sux in kix,
mai hoik tawng Ye-sux krai ka kix,
46 kix tom keud tix sang giex ka nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
kix lhat ka pui hun,
mawh kheu pui hun hoik tawng Ye-sux mawh cao jhai heue.
