Yi-sha nawb buan ka Ya-keux
27
Yam in Yi-sha hoik kuad ju,
ngai nawh hoik dug.
Nawh kok ka kawn ai tix Yi-sao mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Kawn simex eue!”
Yi-sao pawk lox:
“Aux ot tin heue.”
2 Yi-sha ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
aux hoik kuad,
ang lai tawng singaix sang yum tix,
3 maix teei kraung ak mai te,
lih hu mai sawg to tiag,
4 khre nex pa jiet nyawm,
pa muh aux tix ih,
mai viex tix tawx aux ih,
sivoe sang yum aux,
sang nawb buan ka piang maix heue.”
5 Li-pai-cia mhong lox krai Yi-sha ka Yi-sao,
mai jawm yam lih hu jaw Yi-sao ka daux tiag,
6 ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Aux hoik mhong lox krai keeing maix ka pux ke maix Yi-sao ah yuh nin:
7 ‘Maix hu jaw,
hawt tix tong awm ceu pa muh aux tix ih,
son sang nawb aux buan piang maix,
plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex sivoe sang yum tix.’
8 Kawn simex aux eue!
Nghiet ka lox aux,
yuh hawt lox pa ah aux.
9 Maix hu sawg kawn pex pa kluing ra mu khaing daux phung pex,
aux tag khre priex nyawm son keeing maix,
awm pa muh nawh tix ih.
10 Maix chix tix hu tawx nawh ih,
son sang nawb nawh buan ka piang maix sivoe sang yum tix.”
11 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-keux deeh ah nin ka miex tix Li-pai-cia:
“Pux ke aux Yi-sao mawh pa koe hauk,
aux mawh pui plang hak ang mawh lie?
12 Keeing aux tee rien tix pu pi aux,
yaox ka pie lhen aux,
ja lao aux sang pon krax pa nawb nawh buan,
mai tieh tix pon krax pa jhax lawg.”
13 Miex nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Kawn simex aux eue,
kieh aux kham krax jhax lawg,
hawt ka lox krai aux khaing heue.
Maix hu tuk kawn pex hoek khaing.”
14 Ya-keux lih hu mai tuk tix tawx pex ka miex tix;
miex nawh khre priex pa nyawm pa muh keeing kix tix ih.
15 Khaix an Li-pai-cia teei kraung ceep pa mhawm cie kawn ai tix Yi-sao pa koe daux nyiex,
tawx Ya-keux ceep;
16 nawh lhop hak pex ka piang taix nawh,
mai dee plang ngawg nawh;
17 mai tawx nex pa tong mai khao mun pa tong ka nawh.
18 Ya-keux hu ka keeing tix mai ah nin:
“Keeing eue!”
Keeing nawh pawk:
“Kawn eue,
mawh maix mawx?”
19 Ya-keux pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mawh kawn ai maix Yi-sao.
Pa nhie maix aux yuh,
aux hoik yuh mhawm,
kaoh ngawm,
ih nex to tiag pa viex aux,
son sang nawb buan ka piang aux heue.”
20 Yi-sha ah nin:
“Kawn eue,
maix kied phai yaox in kix yuh ka mawx?”
Ya-keux pawk ah nin:
“Mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex maix pa hoik viex hoek ka aux.”
21 Yi-sha ah nin ka Ya-keux:
“Kawn eue,
hoek dex tin,
kieh aux pu pi tix yaox,
maix rug mawh Yi-sao tete lie?”
22 Ya-keux hu dex,
Yi-sha pu pi ka nawh,
tom ah nin:
“Lox mawh lox Ya-keux,
taix mawh taix Yi-sao.”
23 Nawh ang tawng in mawh Ya-keux,
kheu taix Ya-keux hoik kua hauk awm taix Yi-sao yuh nan.
Yam sang nawb nawh buan ka piang Ya-keux,
24 deeh baox chawk tix bawg:
“Rug mawh maix Yi-sao lie?”
Ya-keux ah nin:
“Mawh aux heue.”
25 Yi-sha ah nin:
“Viex nex hoek tin,
khaix hoik ih aux tag nawb buan piang maix.”
Ya-keux viex nex hoek,
mai baox tawx nawh nyaux plai.
26 Khaix an,
Yi-sha ah nin:
“Kawn eue,
hoek tix ngheet ka aux.”
27 aYa-keux hoek tix ngheet ka nawh;
yam mhong jhawm si oe kraung ceep Ya-keux,
tom nawb buan piang nawh ah nin:
“Jhawm si oe kraung ceep kawn simex aux awm si oe pa kaoh khaing daux haktiex pa hoik nawb Simiang Siyiex buan.
28 Kieh Siyiex tawx rawm ciex rao ma ka maix,
mai kieh haktiex maix mhawm!
Kieh nawh tawx nghox koi mai plai pa nyawm tom nie ka maix.
29 bKieh pui meung ka piang haktiex ku ceu yuh kaing son maix,
kieh pui ku ceu tom nie khawm uik krub ka maix.
Kieh maix kuang ka piang pux aik kawn keeing tix,
mai kieh kawn simex miex maix khankix,
krub ka maix.
Can pui pa jhax lawg ka maix tag pon krax pa jhax lawg,
can pui pa nawb buan ka maix tag pon buan son heue.”
Yi-sao khiox khaw Yi-sha nawb buan piang tix
30 Khaix hoik nawb Yi-sha buan ka Ya-keux;
Ya-keux taug lih hu khaing keeing tix dum mawx dum an,
mai pux ke nawh Yi-sao hoek khaing jaw tix.
31 Yi-sao kawx hoik tong nex pa nyawm tix ih,
viex hoek sivoe keeing nawh ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
kaoh ngawm,
ih ka nex to tiag pa pon aux son maix,
son sang nawb maix buan ka piang aux heue.”
32 Yi-sha chawk:
“Mawh maix mawx?”
Yi-sao pawk ah nin:
“Aux mawh kawn ai maix Yi-sao heue.”
33 Khaix an Yi-sha yaoh si yaung ka tete,
chawk ah nin:
“Mawh pui mawx pa taug khraox viex aux ih nex to tiag hoek lie?
Sivoe ang maix nyang hoek,
aux hoik ih kah,
mai nawb buan ka piang nawh khawm uik;
buan son in hoik mawh cie nawh leeing ju.”
34 Yam hoik mhong Yi-sao lox in,
nawh tom laux rhawm mai ploi lox yiiem sigrien sigre,
nawh ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
maix kawx nawb buan ka aux liak.”
35 Yi-sha ah nin:
“Pux maix hoek pie lhien tix pon buan son pa sang caw maix tix pon hu heue.”
36 dYi-sao ah nin:
“In mawh daux ra bawg pie lhien nawh ka aux,
kheu kok ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-keux,c
nawh hoik teei tix cie min kawn ai aux,
yam in baox teei nyum buan son cie aux.
Keeing,
maix ang lai deuh buan son tix ceu ceu son aux lie?”
37 Yi-sha pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik ap nawh mawh simiang maix,
aux hoik kieh pux aik nawh khankix mawh kawn cau nawh,
aux hoik nawb buan son piang nawh,
mai kieh nawh grum naog ka nghox koi mai plai pa nyawm.
Kawn aux eue!
Yam in,
aux kawn sang chix yuh ceu pa tix son maix lie?”
38 eYi-sao baox ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Keeing eue,
maix koe ngie lox nawb buan tix ceu khaing lie?
Khiox khaw maix nawb buan ka aux liak!”
Khaix hoik krai nawh tom ploi lox yiiem tom ting heue.
39 fKheu nan,
Yi-sha ah nin:
Lhiex ka daux rao ma ang tawx ka maix;
haktiex dee blawn mhawm ang lai koe son maix.
40 gMaix tag eei ju tix ka vaig gawn,
tag yuh kaing son pux aik tix,
yam hoik pon tieh vheen maix ka nawh,
maix tag pon luan khaing khaox gang pa cienh nawh heue.
41 Yi-sao koeng ka Ya-keux,
mawh kheu jao nawh hoik nawb keeing nawh buan ka Ya-keux.
Yi-sao keud daux rhawm tix:
“Singaix pa sang yum keeing hoik dex,
khaix priex krax yum,
aux tag sang pauh ka Ya-keux heue.”
42 Koe pui krai kaing pa sang yuh Yi-sao ka Li-pai-cia,
nawh tom kok ka kawn simex tix Ya-keux,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix nghiet lox krai aux,
pux ke maix Yi-sao sang sawg tix yuh krax pawk bix ka maix,
pauh ka maix.
43 Kawn simex aux eue,
maix hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai aux.
Nied to hu ka haktiex Ha-lang,
hu paung ot ka prie aux La-pang dee an,
44 ot tau mai nawh tix vud tix cu,
krox jun krax song rhawm pux ke maix Yi-sao grai.
45 Piawm kaing pa yuh maix piang nawh,
aux tom tawx pui hu rieb ka maix deeh hoek.
Yuh ka mawx aux sang kieh kawn simex tix ra kaux grai ka daux tix ngaix khaing lie?”
Ya-keux paung ot ka La-pang
46 Li-pai-cia ah nin ka Yi-sha:
“Aux hoik kied koeng ka moeng Yi-sao pa mawh kawn bun Heux in kiex.
Phan Ya-keux baox teei tix moeng bun Heux,
lheu yum aux khaing heue!”
