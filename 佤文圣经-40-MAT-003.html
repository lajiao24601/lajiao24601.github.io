Yaw-hang cao cub com sidiex
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.1–8; lai Lu-cia 3.1–18; Lai Yaw-hang 1.19–28)
3
Yam an,
Yaw-hang cao cub com hoek sidiex ka daux tiag Yo-thaix.
2 aNawh ah nin:
“Loh rhawm rhi tix heue,
mawh kheu meung rao ma hoik dex sang hoek heue!”
3 bYaw-hang mawh pui an pa hoik krai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax;
nawh ah nin:
koe pui kok yo ka daux tiag daux tu ah nin:
cox khre daux krax son Simiang Ye-sux,
cox rauh see ka krax pa sang lawng nawh heue!
4 cYaw-hang ceep ka kraung hauk ka-la-uk,
mai mad nyoi tix ka maox hak;
pa ih nawh mawh sido mai rawm hia.
5 Pui hun kaoh khaing haktiex Yo-thaix veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai rawm klawng Yaw-tang hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
6 Kix doh keud ka phi map tix,
Yaw-hang tom cub com kix ka daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue.
7 dYaw-hang yaox pui Fa-li-saix mai pui Sa-tu-kai tom nie hoek nang mhaing tix cub com,
nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Pex pa mawh si uing pa koe jai in kix heue!
Krax prawng ca Siyiex hoik dex sang hoek,
pex ce tix kawn sang pon mox tix lie?
8 Tag caw tix teei kaing pa yuh tix jhi grawng hoik loh pex rhawm rhi tix.
9 ePaw vi ce Ya-paw-la-hang mawh tax du tix mai taug yaox tix luat luan khaing krax prawng krax ca.
Aux krai ka pex,
Siyiex pon teei simaox in kix lih mawh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang heue!
10 fMoe hoik deuh tix ka piang riah khaox,
can khaox plix pa ang plix tag sang kid kah,
mai taing sau daux ngu.
11 Aux teei rawm cub com pex kah,
mai in hoik mawh pa jhi grawng hoik loh pex rhawm rhi tix;
viang mawh nan,
kaw pa sang hoek khaix aux an tag sang teei Khuan Singax Singeh mai ngu cub com pex kah.
Nawh kawn lheu ting khaing aux tom nie,
pie kiiep nawh kawx ang keung tix jiang kah.
12 Ka daux taix nawh giex ka bia yeeb,
tag sang yeeb ka nghox koi singax,
teei nghox laig daux krao;
phan mawh ghrip,
nawh tag sang teei ngu pa ang chix yeet leeing ju gleeing ka heue!”
Ye-sux cub com tix
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.9–11; Lai Lu-cia 3.21–22)
13 Yam an,
Ye-sux kaoh khaing Cia-li-lix hu ka plak rawm klawng Yaw-tang hoek ka Yaw-hang,
mai sang nhie nawh cub com tix.
14 Yaw-hang keud tix sang tieh loh ka rhawm keud nawh,
tom ah nin:
“Aux khaw tix cub com tix ka maix,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tieh tix hoek sawg ka aux heue!”
15 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yam in tag caw tix yuh nin heue,
kheu yuh nin hoik mawh pa yuh ka alo Siyiex heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-hang cu ka lox nawh heue.
16 Ye-sux cub com ka rawm,
mai lih khaing daux rawm,
rao ma hoik baoh son nawh;
nawh yaox khuan Siyiex pa awm si kaux lih hoek,
mai tom piang to kaux nawh.
17 gKhaix an,
koe lox pa lih hoek khaing rao ma pa ah nin:
“In mawh kawn simex pa muh aux,
aux khaw rhawm ka nawh heue.”
