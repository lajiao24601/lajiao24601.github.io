Doeh ceu cad Ya-tang
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.1–4)
5
aIn plak greem mawh doeh ceu Ya-tang.
(Yam phan Siyiex ka kawn pui,
nawh yuh awm grang pak peung tix.
2 bNawh phan yuh ka kix,
koe simex koe bun,
nawb buan ka piang kiex,
mai nawh kok ceu kaux kiex mawh kawn pui.)
3 Yam koe ju Ya-tang tix yieh tix ngoe num,
nawh deeh koe kawn simex tix kaux,
pa awm grang pak peung nawh,
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Sai-theux.
4 Khaix hoik keh Sai-theux,
Ya-tang hoik koe ju daix yieh num,
nawh baox pon kawn simex kawn bun.
5 Yam yum Ya-tang,
nawh koe dim yieh tix ngoe num heue.
6 Sai-theux im ju tix yieh phuan num mai koe kawn simex tix kaux,
mai sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-naw-seex.
7 Khaix an nawh baox im ju daix yieh aliah num,
mai koe kawn simex kawn bun.
8 Yam yum nawh,
nawh koe ju dim yieh kao ra num heue.
9 Yi-naw-seex im ju tix dim num mai pon kawn simex tix kaux,
tom sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Kai-nang.
10 Khaix an baox im ju daix yieh kao phuan num,
mai koe kawn simex kawn bun khankix.
11 Yam yum nawh,
tom koe ju dim yieh phuan num heue.
12 Yam hoik koe ju Kai-nang atigliah num,
nawh koe kawn simex tix kaux kaux Ma-leu-liex.
13 Khaix an baox im ju daix yieh tipon num,
nawh baox koe kawn simex kawn bun khankix.
14 Yam yum nawh koe ju dim yieh kao num heue.
15 Yam koe Ma-leu-liex tix gliah phuan num,
nawh koe kawn tix kaux kaux Ya-liex.
16 Khaix an nawh baox im ju daix yieh tix ngoe num,
mai koe kawn simex kawn bun khankix.
17 Yam yum nawh koe ju daix yieh tix dim phuan num heue.
18 Ya-liex koe ju tix yieh tix gliah ra num,
mai nawh koe kawn tix kaux kaux Yi-naw.
19 Khaix an nawh baox im ju daix yieh num,
mai koe kawn simex kawn bun khankix.
20 Yam yum nawh koe ju dim yieh tix gliah ra num heue.
21 Yam koe ju Yi-naw tix gliah phuan num mai nawh koe kawn tix kaux kaux Ma-thu-sha-la.
22 Khaix in,
Yi-naw hawt hu tau mai Siyiex been loe yieh num,
mai nawh baox deeh koe kawn simex kawn bun khankix.
23 Yi-naw im ju tom hoek loe yieh tix gliah phuan num,
24 cnawh hu dex dawt mai Siyiex,
mai Siyiex rieb ka nawh,
tom ang lai yaox ka nawh heue.
25 Yam koe ju Ma-thu-sha-la tix yieh tix daix aliah num,
nawh koe kawn simex tix kaux,
mai kok ceu kaux nawh mawh La-maix.
26 Khaix an nawh baox im ju aliah yieh tix daix ra num,
mai baox deeh pon ka kawn simex kawn bun khankix.
27 Yam yum nawh koe dim yieh tix gliah dim num heue.
28 Yam koe ju La-maix tix yieh tix daix ra num,
nawh keh kawn simex tix kaux.
29 Ceu kaux nawh mawh Naw-yax,d
mawh kheu nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik jhax lawg ka haktiex,
mai kieh ex yuh kaing tom taug mao;
kawn nyawm in tag chix kieh ex pon krax nyawm rhawm ka daux pa yuh tix kaing pa taug mao.”
30 La-maix baox im ju tom hoek phuan yieh tix dim phuan num,
mai nawh deeh baox koe kawn simex kawn bun khankix.
31 Yam yum nawh koe ju aliah yieh atigliah aliah num heue.
32 Yam hoik koe ju Naw-yax phuan yieh num,
nawh koe kawn pa mawh Sang,
Hang mai Ya-fu heue.
