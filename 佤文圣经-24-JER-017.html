Phi map pui Yo-tax mai krax ciyang
17
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yo-tax eue,
phi map pex hoik teei rhiem rawm meug tiiem kah,
hoik teei nyex siang pa ting ngawh pawd ot ka daux rhawm rhi pex,
saik ot ka piang rung khu pex.
2-3 Ka greem khaox klai ku gawng ka daux haktiex pex,
ka piang gawng iak ku mu mai gawng ting daux tiag daux tu,
hoik sum koe khu riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la mai rong siyiex.
Pex lai krub ka khu mai riang rong siyiex in kix.
Mawh kheu yuh pex phi map ka daux meung haktiex ku dauh,
aux tag sang kieh pui koeng ka pex tud rhim ka sin ngao pex.
4 Pex tag sang ploi ka haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka pex;
aux tag sang viex hoek deuh pex ot ka haktiex pa ang pex tawng,
mai yuh siliex cawng pui koeng.
Pex hoik cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm,
krax pa song rhawm aux hoik cap,
mai ang chix yeet leeing ju heue.”
Gum lox lai tiiem pa sidiex
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
pa een rhawm tix ka kawn pui haktiex,
pa een rhawm tix ka riang rao pui pa chix yum khankix,
pui pa tieh vheen ka aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pui pa yuh nin khaw tix pon krax pa jhax lawg.
6 Nawh awm khaox kroh ka daux pak mhaik yuh nin:
pa kuah ka daux tiag pa kroh,
pa kuah ka daux haktiex pa koe kih;
kix ang tau tix doh pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.
 
7 Pui pa yum rhawm ka aux nong nong,
pui pa yuh nin kied koe buan son heue!
8 aNawh awm khaox pa sum tix ot ka praok rawm klawng iak yuh nan:
Riah khaox an tawx tix laig daux rawm,
ang lhat ka pa hax koek pied sibhawm praix,
lhax khaox an ca klai jheeng jheeng,
plix plix ang jud ang jai heue.
 
9 Rhawm rhi kawn pui lheu pie lhien khaing ku ceu,
kawn lheu caox caig khaing ku ceu khawm uik;
pui mawx sang pon tawng ka khawm uik lie?
10 bAux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex si kaw tix yaox ka rhawm rhi kawn pui,
lam lia tix yaox ka tawm nhao kawn pui;
aux hawt tix pawk bix ka kaing pa yuh pui ku kaux heue.
 
11 Pui pa mag mi ka daux pa ang khaw caw,
awm sim pa num pa ang mawh tawm tix yuh nan.
Sin ngao nawh tag sang grai yam koe riang koe rao nawh;
hoek dee si dauh nawh lih tix mawh pui daox aw heue.
 
12 Nyiex buan ex awm dang ngeung pa pruh riang,
jauh tom dix dix hoik jung tix ot ka piang gawng lhaong heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh krax keud pon jun pon pui Yi-seu-liex;
pui pa tik vud ka maix hoik pon krax kaig krax tua.
Kix hoik grai,c
awm ceu kaux pa tiiem ot ka piang phawng tiex yuh nan,
mawh kheu hoik tik vud kix ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh num dee keet rawm im ju heue.
Yie-li-mi khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex teem coi tix
14 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix pai aux moi,
mai aux tom taug chix pon brax;
khiox khaw maix taoh ka aux,
mai aux tom taug chix pon krax nyawm ot.
Maix mawh pa sang caw aux tix yaog yaw heue.
15 Pui ah nin ka aux:
“Lox pa ne raw Simiang Siyiex ka ex yuh ka mawx lie?
Kieh nawh lih tix mawh khaing heue!”
16 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux ang sax khiox khaw maix ploi krax laux krax leeing hoek ka piang to kaux kix tix bawg kawx,d
mai ang simie kix yaox nang singaix pa kha rhawm.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
in kix maix hoik tawng khawm uik;
maix tawng ka lox pa krai aux.
17 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux lhat graung,
kheu mawh maix nyiex dee mox son aux nong nong yam yaox aux krax kha rhawm.
18 Khiox khaw maix kieh pui pa tuk tiak ka aux hoek krax kaig krax tua,
paw vi kieh aux hoek krax kaig krax tua;
khiox khaw maix kieh kix lhat graung,
paw vi kieh aux yaoh yee.
Khiox khaw maix kieh kix yaox krax laux krax leeing,
mai yuh kix laux lak pak leeing heue.
Krai grawng sang priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah
19 Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin ka aux:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
maix hu laig ka ‘siviex pui hun’ dee caw simiang Yo-tax tix sang laig laig lih lih hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hu nang ka diang siviex veng ku dauh,
ot tix krai pao gum lox aux ka dee an heue.
20 Krai ka simiang Yo-tax mai ka pui hun khankix,
khawm can pui pa laig laig lih lih khaing veng diang siviex Yie-lu-sa-leung in kix,
tag caw nhie kix bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox aux.
21 eMaix tag caw tix krai ka kix ah nin:
phan kix muh ka ju tix,
tag ang pon tix gah kraung khrai tix ka daux singaix tuah,
ang pon tix gah kraung khrai tix laig ka diang siviex Yie-lu-sa-leung.
22 fAng pon tix gah kraung khrai tix hu khaing nyiex ka daux singaix tuah,
ang pon tix yuh kaing tix ceu kawx.
Tag caw tix nhie kix priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx aux ka tax du kix,
priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah kieh mawh singaix pa singaix singeh.
23 Tax du kix lao sang simi ka aux,
mai tieh tix kiien rhawm ang cu tix nghiet ka aux mai ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux heue.
24 “Krai ka kix,
nhie kix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap aux khawm uik.
Ang pon tix gah kraung khrai laig daux diang siviex veng singaix tuah.
Tag caw priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah kieh mawh singaix pa singax singeh;
ang pon tix yuh kaing tix ceu kawx.
25 Yuh nin,
simiangg kix tom taug pon yaox tix laig ka daux diang siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pon yaox tix koe siawp sibrawm simiang Ta-wue.
Kix tag sang brug ka ling law mai brung,
mawh simiang,
mai pui Yo-tax,
mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa preeng tix laig daux veng tau.
Veng in tag sang naog sang sied leeing ju leeing phan.
26 “Kawn pui tag sang kaoh khaing veng Yo-tax ku veng,
khaing haktiex Piien-ya-ming,
khaing daux lung,
khaing piang gawng lua,
mai khaing haktiex Yo-tax plak seh,
mai preeng tix laig ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Kix tag sang viex Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi mai sidah bhawng Law-ban,
khawm Krax-tan Pa-yaog-yaw hoek ka nyiex buan aux.
27 Viang mawh nan,
kix tag sang nghiet ka gum lox tawx aux,
priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah kieh mawh singaix pa singax singeh;
ang pon tix gah kraung khrai laig daux diang siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung singaix tuah.
Phan pex lieh lheu,
aux tag sang gleeing diang siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung laux;
cap ngu an tag sang bleet ka nyiex haw simiang,
mai ang koe pui pa pon preet yeet heue.”
