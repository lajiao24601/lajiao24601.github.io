Pao-law baox hoek vhi ka Ma-chi-toeng mai ka Si-lax
20
Khaix hoik yiien krax pa suk sag,
Pao-law khrawm ka kawn Khrit,
joh jhan ka kix,
mai krai lox sang tang guah tix hu khaing kix.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh lih khaing dee an,
mai hu plak Ma-chi-toeng.
2 Nawh hoik tauh hu ka haktiex an khawm uik,
mai krai lox pa joh jhan tom nie ka kawn Khrit khankix,
khaix an nawh hoek ka Si-lax,
3 nawh paung dee an loe khix.
Yam rien khre Pao-law tix sang brug reu hoek ka Shui-li-yax,
nawh hoik tawng pui Yo-thaix sang pauh ka tix,
mawh kheu nan nawh but rhawm tix deeh ing laig krax daux haktiex Ma-chi-toeng.
4 Pui pa hu tau mai nawh mawh Saw-pa-theux kawn simex Pi-law-see pui Pi-li-yax,
mawh Ya-li-ta-ku mai Si-kong-to pui Thie-sa-law-ni-cia,
mawh Kai-yo pui Theu-pix,
kawn koe Thi-maw-thaix mai Thoe-ci-ku,
mai Theu-law-fe-maw pui Ya-si-yax.
5 Kix ran tix hu sivoe,
mai krox ex ka Theu-law-yax.
6 Khaix hoik yuh ka Poi Tik-su-saix,
ex jauh tix phat reu hu khaing Fe-li-pix,
khaix hoik luan phuan singaix hoek ka Theu-law-yax,
deeh si pup ka kix,
mai paung ot dee an tix vang tuah heue.
Pao-law nghoik tix hu vhi ka Theu-law-yax
7 Ka daux bo singaix liah,a
ex khrawm paox tix ot tau,
mai ih ka priex preem.
Pao-law sidiex gau pui hun,
mawh kheu sang yaog nawh tix hu ka daux singaix khaix,
mawh kheu nan sidiex tom rheeng hoek greung sawm.
8 Ka lawg nyiex lhaong pa khrawm ex paox tix kah,
kawk ngu hoik koe ka tom nie.
9 Koe pui pa nhum ju Yo-thoe-kux tix kaux,
ngawm moing daux doh nyiex,
yam kawn sidiex gau Pao-law ka kix,
nawh hoik laig it si nyek si nyek,
mai cawd khaing nyiex pa koe loe jan.
Pui hun yaog ka nawh,
yaox hoik yum nawh.
10 Pao-law lih khaing nyiex,
mai gup tix piang to kaux nawh,
kawm ka nawh ah nin:
“Paw vi si yaung,
nawh kawn koe bhawm heue!”
11 Nawh deeh haok ka nyiex,
mai si vit ih ka khao mun.
Nawh ca nied tix krai lox sidiex tom rheeng,
hoek riang praix tom taug lih hu.
12 Kix tawx pui pa nhum ju an deeh ing ka nyiex,
pui hun hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm khawm uik heue.
Lih khaing Theu-law-yax hoek ka Mi-li-to
13 Ex ran laig daux reu,
phat hu ka plak Ya-saw,
rien khre tix rieb Pao-law dee an.
In hoik mawh lox krai nawh,
mawh kheu sang hu nawh ka krax piang tiex.
14 Nawh rieb tix si pup ex ka Ya-saw,
ex tom rieb nawh laig daux reu,
khaix an hu ka plak Mi-thoe-li-ni.
15 Ex baox jauh tix phat reu hu khaing dee an,
daux singaix khaix hoek ka toi rawm Ci-a;
baox hoek tix ngaix,
kaog ka praok rawm Sa-mawx,
ngaix khaix baox hoek ka Mi-li-to.
16 Pao-law hoik dawg but rhawm tix ang kaog ka Yi-fu-saw,
son ang kix sang gla bawg yam ka haktiex Ya-si-yax.
Nawh nied tix sang hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai simie tix nied hoek sivoe poi tix phuan ngaix heue.
Gum lox pa krai Pao-law yam guah paox tix
17 Pao-law ploi pui hu sawg kuad song ceu cao Yi-fu-saw khankix khaing Mi-li-to,
kok kix hoek ka tix.
18 Kix hoek,
Pao-law ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoik tawng,
yam jauh aux tix hoek ka haktiex Ya-si-yax ka daux tix ngaix.
Hoik ot tau aux mai pex tom rheeng yuh ka mawx,
19 hoik yuh aux kaing jhaw Simiang Ye-sux ka daux pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi mai ka daux pa khi rhawm yuh ka mawx,
mai baox kham krax cheuk cheu pa yuh pui Yo-thaix ka aux tom nie yuh ka mawx.
20 Pex kawx hoik tawng khawm uik,
yam ot aux ka daux bang pui hun,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka daux nyiex ma pex tix blah,
yam krai pao aux mai sidiex gaux aux ka pex,
ca mawh koe dee mhawm son pex,
aux ang lai deuh koe tix ceu kawx.
21 Pa krai ka pui Yo-thaix ang ah,
ka pui tang ceu tang cad ang ah,
aux hoik krai tom sidawng ka kix,
nhie kix loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai veeh tix hoek ka Siyiex,
khaix an yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux.
22 Yam in,
mawh son sang hawt ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
aux tag sang haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Aux ang tawng kaing ceu pa sang si pup tix dee an;
23 aux tawng ngie grawng hoik thung Khuan Singax Singeh ka tix ka daux veng ku veng,
daux thawng mai krax kha rhawm hoik krox jun ka aux.
24 bViang mawh nan,
aux ang lhawk ka ju tix son sang yuh grum naog ka gum lox tawm ka aux,
mai kieh kaing pa hoik ap Simiang Ye-sux tix tawx ka aux lih tix mawh,
pa mawh gum lox buan pa kham sakse buan son Siyiex heue.
25 “Aux hoik lai ling ka daux pui hun,
mai krai pao ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex.
Yam in aux hoik tawng,
hu sivoe ngai pex tag ang lai yaox ka aux.
26 Mawh kheu nan,
ka daux in singaix aux krai tom sidawng ka pex,
phan koe pui ka daux pex pa laux leeing,
phi map in ang lai hoek piang aux;
27 mawh kheu hoik sidiex aux alo Siyiex ka pex khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh pie ceeix kawx.
28 Pex tag caw rien rhawm tix,
tag baox caw tix rien rhawm tix ka cub yung pa hoik tawx Khuan Singax Singeh pex kia ngien.
Tag caw tix eei liang ka ceu cao Siyiex,c
pa mawh pa hoik loh ka pa yum kawn simex nawh.d
29 Aux hoik tawng khaix hoik lih hu aux,
koe sox la pa caox caig tag sang laig daux pex yuh cub yung laux leeing.
30 Ka si nax pex kawx,
koe pui tag sang yuh krax pa pie lhien,
mai nyaw glaw pui gau hawt ka kix.
31 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag rien rhawm tix,
tag caw tix doh keud,
ka daux loe num,
grawng hoik kieh aux rawm ngai tix roik leeing ngaix leeing sawm mawh krax pa sidiex ka pex ku kaux heue.
32 “Yam in aux hoik ap tix tawx pex ka daux tawm taix Siyiex,
mai ka daux gum lox buan son nawh;
nawh pon kieh krax yum rhawm pex kaux cong,
mai baox ap tix tawx buan son pa hoik rien khre nawh son pui cie tix ka pex.
33 Aux ang sax ghoing ka mau khri pui mai ka kraung ceep pui tix kaux kawx.
34 Pex hoik tang tawng,
aux hoik yuh kaing,
mai hoik pon eei to kaux tix mai ka paox pa yuh kaing tau mai aux khankix.
35 Aux hoik yuh tix deuh kaing ceu pa mhawm ku ceu ka pex,
krai pex mhong,
tag caw tix rien yuh kaing yuh nin son sang yaox tix teem coi ka pui pa iak riang iak rao khankix.
Tag caw tix doh keud ka gum lox pa hoik krai Ye-sux ah nin:
‘Pa tawx lheu pon buan son khaing pa rieb heue.’ ”
36 Khaix hoik krai lox in kix,
Pao-law tom grux ngawng tix rawg buan tau mai pui hun.
37 Pui hun hoik yiiem khawm uik,
kix kawm ngheet ka nawh,
mai guah paox tix tang hu.
38 Kix kied laux rhawm ka lox pa krai Pao-law ah nin tix gum,
jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai ang lai yaox ka nawh.
Kix tom hu tawx nawh laig daux reu heue.
