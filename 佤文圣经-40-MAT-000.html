Lai Ma-thaix
Krai dawt
Lai Ma-thaix krai ka ex ah nin: Ye-sux mawh cao taoh pa tawx Siyiex ka kawn pui piang haktiex. Ka daux nawh, Siyiex hoik yuh ka gum lox pa ap ka pui cie tix ka daux phuk lai sigang khri pa prim. Gum lox buan in ang joeng mawh ngie pa tawx ka son pui Yo-thaix, mai hoik mawh nang pa tawx ka kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
Pa tiiem lih ka phuk lai in kied mawh pa sidawng tete, jauh tom yam keh Ye-sux, krai grawng pa com nawh rawm, pa kham nawh krax nyaw krax glaw, pa sidiex nawh lox buan, pa sidiex gau nawh pui, mai pa pai nawh jhawm saux pui ka haktiex Cia-li-lix, khaix an krai lao ka kaing pa yuh Ye-sux yam kaoh nawh khaing Cia-li-lix haok ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung, mai ka kaing pa nghoik tix lih ka daux tix vang tuah, dee si dauh mawh pa tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak mai pa deeh kaoh im nawh heue.
Phuk lai in tiiem Ye-sux mawh sara pa ting min tix kaux. Nawh koe akhuang tix kah ka lox lai been li Siyiex; nawh sidiex ka pa mawh pa meh pa dex pa dawt mai siawp sibrawm Siyiex. Krax pa sidiex gau nawh chix tix teei lih koe phuan baux ting:
1. Gum lox pa sidiex pa kied ting ceu ka piang gawng, pa mawh pa krai lao ka rhawm pa mhawm, ka beeh glawm, ka sibrawm, mai krax haok huan pui meung cie Siyiex (simin ting 5 mu tom hoek simin ting 7 mu).
2. Beeh glawm kawn cau kao ra kaux (simin ting 10 mu).
3. Grawng lox phek tiiem son meung rao ma (simin ting 13 mu).
4. Grawng dee bleeh pa mawh pui gau (simin ting 18 mu).
5. Grawng pa si dauh baux yam in mai pa lih siawp sibrawm Siyiex tix mawh (simin ting 24 mu 25 mu.)
Krai kaing
1. Ru ceu cad nyiex ma Ye-sux mai pa keh nawh (1.1–2.23)
2. Kaing yuh Yaw-hang kaw pa cub com (3.1–12)
3. Pa com Ye-sux rawm mai pa kham nawh krax nyaw krax glaw (3.13–34.11)
4. Kaing sidiex pa yuh Ye-sux ka Cia-li-lix (4.12–18.35)
5. Jauh ka Cia-li-lix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung (9.1–20.34)
6. Pa ghoik tix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ka daux tix vang tuah mai ka haktiex pa dex dee an (21.1–27.66)
7. Pa kaoh im mai pa lih bleeh Simiang Ye-sux (28.1–20)
