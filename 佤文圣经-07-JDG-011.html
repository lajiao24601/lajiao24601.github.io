11
Pui Ci-liex pa kaux Yie-fu-tha mawh pui naing pa kua tix kaux.
Miex nawh mawh sibun coh kaux;
keeing nawh Ci-liex
2 keh kawn simex tang kaux tau mai moeng tix;
khaix hoik ting kawn simex in kix,
mai kix grheu Yie-fu-tha hu khaing nyiex.
Kix ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix ang pon tix plud ka sin nyiex ma keeing yix,
mawh kheu maix mawh pa keh khaing sibun coh kaux.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-fu-tha tom to hu khaing pux aik tix,
mai paung ot ka Thaw-pawx.
Nawh ot tan mai khrawm ka cub pui pa cau nyhau vu vie khankix;
kix hawt khaix nawh heue.
4 Yam hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
pui Ya-meung yuh naing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
5 Yam hoik taoh naing,
kuad song Ci-liex khankix hoek ka Thaw-pawx,
mai nhie Yie-fu-tha deeh ing.
6 Kix ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix mawh simiang yix,
mai thung yix hu puing ka pui Ya-meung heue.”
7 Yie-fu-tha ah nin:
“Singaix pa hoik luan ang pex hoik koeng ka aux,
mai grheu aux lih khaing nyiex ma keeing aux lie?
Yam in pex yaox ka krax kha,
sawg pex aux yuh pa tix lie?”
8 Kuad song Ci-liex khankix ah nin ka Yie-fu-tha:
“Yam in yix hoek sawg maix,
mawh son sang nhie maix thung ex puing ka pui Ya-meung,
mai yuh simiang ka piang pui Ci-liex khawm uik.”
9 Yie-fu-tha ah nin:
“Pex nhie aux ing hu puing ka pui Ya-meung,
phan Simiang Siyiex kieh aux pex,
aux tag mawh simiang pex heue.”
10 Kix ah nin:
“Yix cu kah,
tag sang nghiet ka lox tawm maix;
Simiang Siyiex kham sakse son ex.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-fu-tha ing tau mai kuad song Ci-liex khankix;
pui haoh pui hun tom ap nawh mawh simiang mai simiang naing.
Yie-fu-tha ot ka Mi-see-pa mai krai lox tix sidawng ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
12 Yie-fu-tha ploi kawn cau tix hoek ka simiang Ya-meung dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Maix simah yuh pa tix ka yix lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai sang tud ka haktiex yix?”
13 Simiang Ya-meung pawk lox kawn cau pa ploi Yie-fu-tha hoek ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex tom yam jauh kix tix kaoh hoek khaing Ai-cix,
kix hoik cam ka daux haktiex aux,
jauh ka rawm klawng Ya-noeng hoek ka rawm klawng Ya-paw,
tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Yam in,
pex tag caw tix deeh tawx haktiex in ka aux,
ka daux pa nhim heue.”
14 Yie-fu-tha baox ploi kawn cau tix hu ka simiang Ya-meung,
15 ah nin ka nawh:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex ang bax tud ka haktiex pui Maw-ya mai haktiex pui Ya-meung.
16 Tete,
khaix hoek pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Ai-cix,
mai laig ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
khaix an,
tom hoek ka haktiex Cia-ti-see.
17 aYam an,
kix ploi kawn cau hu khiox khaw ka simiang Yi-tong,
son sang cu kix laig ka daux haktiex nawh,
viang mawh nan,
simiang Yi-tong ang cu.
Kix kawx ploi kawn cau hu khiox khaw ka simiang Maw-ya,
nawh kawx ang cu,
kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka Cia-ti-see.
18 b“Khaix an,
kix hu plak sivoe,
laig ka daux tiag daux tu,
via haktiex Yi-tong mai haktiex Maw-ya,
hoek ka haktiex Maw-ya plak lih singaix.
Kix sum nyiex bang ot si tieb rawm klawng Ya-noeng,
mai ang bax tiang ka rawm klawng Ya-meung,
mawh kheu an mawh mee haktiex Maw-ya.
19 cPui Yi-seu-liex ploi kawn cau hu khiox khaw simiang Ya-maw-lix,
pa mawh simiang haktiex Si-seu-peung pa kaux Si-hong,
son sang cu kix laig ka daux haktiex nawh,
mai hoek ka daux haktiex cie tix pui Yi-seu-liex.
20 Viang mawh nan,
Si-hong ang cu;
nawh tieh tix thung pui naing khawm uik sum nyiex bang ot ka Ya-cax,
son sang puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
21 “Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ap Si-hong mai pui naing nawh khawm uik ka daux tawm taix pui Yi-seu-liex;
kix pex ka pui Ya-maw-lix,
mai pon tix cie ka haktiex kix.
22 Pui Yi-seu-liex pon ka haktiex pui Ya-maw-lix khawm uik,
jauh ka rawm klawng Ya-noeng plak caong ma seh,
tom hoek ka rawm klawng Ya-paw plak caong ma laong,
jauh ka daux tiag plak lih singaix,
tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak klip singaix.
23 “Mawh kheu nan,
hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa grheu pui Ya-maw-lix hu son pui cie tix.
24 Maix simie tix teei ka haktiex in lie?
Haktiex pa hoik tawx siyiex pex Ci-mawx ka pex,
pex tag chix tix kieh mawh cie tix;
viang mawh nan,
pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix ka yix khawm uik,
mai yix sang giex ka tom kaux tom cong.
25 dMaix ce tix kawn sang ting riang khaing simiang Maw-ya kawn simex Si-paw pa kaux Pa-leux lie?
Nawh ang sax yuh naing ka pui Yi-seu-liex tix bawg kawx,
mai ang sax puing ka yix tix bawg kawx.
26 Pui Yi-seu-liex pon tix ot ka Si-seu-peung,
mai ka nyiex ka yaong sivie siveng an,
pon tix ot ka Ya-law-eu,
mai ka nyiex ka yaong sivie siveng an,
mai ka veng pa koe ka see rawm klawng Ya-noeng khawm ra plak,
hoik koe loe yieh num heue.
Ka daux baux bawg yam in,
yuh pa tix pex lai ang teei haktiex in lie?
27 Aux ang doh sax yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka pex tix ceu kawx,
yuh ka mawx pex lai puing ka aux,
lai simie tix yuh naing ka aux.
Simiang Siyiex mawh cao prawng see;
daux in singaix nawh hoik ot si nax pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Ya-meung,
mai prawng dut ka kaing pa mawh mai pa ang mawh khankix heue.”
28 Viang mawh nan,
simiang Ya-meung ang nghiet lox pui pa ploi Yie-fu-tha hoek ka nawh heue.
29 Yam an,
khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang Yie-fu-tha,
nawh tom laig ka haktiex Ci-liex,
Ma-na-si,
deeh hoek ka Mi-see-pa haktiex Ci-liex,
hu plak Ya-meung.
30 Yie-fu-tha ap lox ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Phan maix kieh aux puing pex ka pui Ya-meung,
31 yam gaux rhawm aux khawm hoek tix,
aux tag sang tan pui pa jauh tix rieb aux khaing diang siviex nyiex ka maix tix kaux,
mai kieh nawh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka ngu heue.”
32 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-fu-tha tom tiang ka rawm,
mai hu puing ka pui Ya-meung;
Simiang Siyiex kieh nawh pex.
33 Nawh pauh kix pak lawg,
jauh ka haktiex Ya-law-eu tom hoek ka haktiex Mi-ni,
singai hoek tom haktiex Ya-pe-leu Ci-la-ming,
khawm uik tix nga veng,
saux riang tix pauh.
Yuh nin,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik pon pex ka pui Ya-meung heue.
Kawn bun Yie-fu-tha
34 Yam hoik deeh ing Yie-fu-tha ka nyiex tix Mi-see-pa,
kawn bun nawh pa koe ngie tix kaux khaing teei ka siyawp taix,
graoh mai rieb ka nawh.
35 eYie-fu-tha yaox kah,
kied koe krax laux rhawm mai riah sibex tix,
ah nin:
“Kawn bun aux eue,
maix hoik kieh rhawm aux laux tete!
Yuh ka mawx lai mawh maix lie?
Aux hoik ap lox ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang lai chix tix deeh tieh ka heue.”
36 Kawn bun nawh ah nin:
“Maix hoik ap lox ka Simiang Siyiex,
hawt tix yuh aux awm lox pa hoik ap maix,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh maix pawk bix ka piang to kaux pui koeng Ya-meung heue.”
37 Kawn nawh khiox khaw ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux koe ngie lox rawg ka maix tix ceu khaing.
Kieh aux lih hu khaing nyiex ra khix,
paw lai kuang ka aux;
aux sang haok ka piang gawng tau mai paox grawng tix,
mai yiiem grai son ang tix bax koe mex tom hoik si dauh ju tix.”
38 Yie-fu-tha cu nawh lih hu khaing nyiex ra khix,
nawh tom haok ka piang gawng tau mai paox grawm tix,
mai nawh yiiem grai tan,
son ang bax koe mex tom hoik si dauh ju tix.
39 Khaix hoik naog ra khix,
nawh deeh hoek ka nyiex ma keeing tix,
keeing nawh Yie-fu-tha hawt tix yuh awm lox pa ap nawh ka Simiang Siyiex mai deeh vied kah.
Yam yum kawn bun nawh,
mai kawn mawh bun krih heue.
Khaix an,
ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex koe been tix ceu,
40 bun miex pui Yi-seu-liex tix num lih hu khaing nyiex tix pon singaix,
mawh son sang hu yiiem ka pui Ci-liex mai kawn bun Yie-fu-tha heue.
