Been son pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui (Na-si-eux)
6
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
2 mai krai gum lox in plak greem ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
Simex ang ah,
bun ang ah,
phan hoik mhaing lox tiex tu to kaux tix mawh pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui,
hoik tan to kaux tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
3 anawh tag caw tix tuah ka plai num mai plai khien.
Nawh kawx tag ang pon tix nyaux ka rawm plix pa hoik yuh ka plix siiet,
mai ang pon tix ih ka plix siiet,
mai plix siiet kroh ang ah.
4 Glang yam kawn mawh nawh pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui,
nawh tag ang pon tix ih khaing nhu plix siiet tix ceu kawx,
sok plix siiet mai lhox plix siiet kawx,
tag ang pon tix ih ka heue.
5 Glang yam kawn ap nawh tix mawh pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui,
nawh tag ang pon tix khlao hauk kaing tix;
kheu son pa hoik ap nawh tix,
glang yam tan lu nawh tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag caw tix vang ka hauk kaing tix tom lang.
6-7 Hauk kaing nawh mawh mhai grawng pa hoik tan lu nawh tix ka Siyiex.
Nawh tag ang pon tix hu dex ka to kaux pa yum,
mai kieh to kaux tix seeing brai,
viang mawh miex keeing nawh ang ah,
mai pux aik pux o nawh ang ah,
tag ang pon tix hu dex.
8 Glang yam mawh nawh pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui,
nawh tag mawh pa singax singeh cie Simiang Siyiex heue.
9 Phan pui tix kaux kaux,
pa mawh pui pa dex pa dawt mai pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui,
yum tix taix neh khaing,
mai kieh hauk kaing pa hoik ap pong an tix mawh pa singax singeh seeing brai,
nawh tag sang krox jun hoek aliah singaix,
khaix an tag sang khlao ka hauk kaing,
mai khlao ka hauk jheeh.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom tag singax singeh ka daux been khankix heue.
10 Ka daux singaix daix,
nawh tag viex si kaux ra mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
si kaux krie ra mu hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
tawx ka cao giex kaing li.
11 Cao giex kaing li tag sang tan mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap tix mu,
tan mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka an tix mu,
son pa yuh been daux krax pa singax singeh son nawh,
kheu grawng hoik teeh nawh to kaux pa yum.
Singaix an,
nawh tag sang deeh kieh hauk kaing tix mawh pa singax singeh,
12 tag baox deeh tan lu to kaux tix mawh pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui;
singaix pa hoik luan ang lai sawn,
mawh kheu hoik seeing brai hauk kaing pa kieh singax glang ka an.
Nawh tag sang viex ka kawn yung mhaing pa koe tix num tix mu,
kieh mawh son Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud heue.
13 bYam hoik naog singaix glang yam mawh pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui khankix,
tag caw tix yuh ka been in plak greem.
Nawh tag sang hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man,
14 mai tan lu ka khawng brie pa loig loe mu ka Simiang Siyiex,
kawn yung mhaing pa koe tix num tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
kawn yung siang pa koe tix num tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
kawn yung mhaing pa loig pa koe tix num tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
15 Nawh kawn sang baox tan lu ka khao mun pa ang sau su saix tix khrawng:
khao mun pu khlawp pa hoik yuh ka phawng mien pa klao tau mai bee sime,
mai khao mun rhi khlawp pa hoik kraix bee sime kah,
baox tan lu ka khawm Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai Krax-tan pa-jauh heue.
16 Cao giex kaing li tag sang tan lu ceu in ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai tan Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
17 Nawh tag sang tan lu yung mhaing an mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an mai baox tan tau ka khao mun pa ang sau su saix an tix khrawng,
mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai Krax-tan pa-jauh.
18 Pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui tag sang khlao hauk kaing tix ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan,
teei hauk kaing tix,
sau gleeing ot ka piang ngu Krax-tan Pa-nhim heue.
19 Khaix an,
cao giex kaing li tag teei bax plak ngai yung mhaing pa hoik ngaik tix,
mai khao mun pu khlawp ka daux khrawng tix khlawp,
khao mun rhi tix khlawp,
deuh ot ka daux tawm taix pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui.
20 Khaix an,
mai cao giex kaing li tag kieh ceu an kix mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex;
mai an kix tag mawh khawng tan pa singax singeh son cao giex kaing li.
Lheu khaing an,
eek kawn yung mhaing mai ba pa hoik ciak ci lox tat jhawm prawng,
tag mawh cie cao giex kaing li.
Khaix hoik yuh mawh nan,
pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui tag chix tix nyaux ka plai heue.
21 In plak piang mawh been pa sang caw pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui tix hawt.
Phan pui pa guah tix singax khaing kawn pui hoik ap tix tan ka khawng tan tang ceu,
pa mawh pa ang ot ka daux pa hoik ap tix deuh,
nawh tag caw hawt tix yuh awm pa hoik ap tix heue.
Grawng pa nawb cao giex kaing li buan
22 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
23 nhie Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix siao gum lox in plak greem nawb buan ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
24 Kieh Simiang Siyiex nawb buan ka maix,
kia ngien ka maix.
25 Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx krax muh ka maix,
tawx buan son ka maix.
26 Kieh Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka maix,
tawx krax pa nhim ka maix.
27 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Phan kix nawb buan ka piang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux ceu kaux aux,
aux tag tawx buan ka kix heue.”
