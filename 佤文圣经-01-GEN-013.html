Ya-paw-lang mai Law-teux guah paox tix
13
Ya-paw-lang viex moeng tix mai can pa koe tix khawm uik,
lih khaing Ai-cix,
hu plak caong max laong,
laig ka haktiex Cia-nang plak seh;
Law-teux kawx hu tau mai nawh.
2 Ya-paw-lang mawh pui mi,
koe khawng brie,
mai mau khri tom nie heue.
3 Khaix an nawh lih khaing tan,
mai gah tix hu ka plak caong ma laong si nyek si nyek,
dex ka Paw-theu-lix.
Nawh deeh hoek ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix mai si nax kiex veng Ai,
pa mawh dee hoik sum nawh nyiex bang sivoe an,
4 dee yuh khu dee tan;
nawh tom krub ka Simiang Siyiex tan heue.
5 Law-teux pa hu tau mai Ya-paw-lang,
hoik koe nang phung moi,
phung yung,
mai pui nyiex pui ma khawm siliex cawng.
6 Phung moi,
phung yung kix pui ra nyiex kied sidaing haoh hun,
mai ang lai koe haktiex dee deuh ot,
7 kheu nan,
pui liang khawng brie Ya-paw-lang mai pui liang khawng brie Law-teux yie simah ka paox tix.
Yam an pui Cia-nang mai pui Pi-li-si kawn ot ka haktiex an tan heue.
8 Kheu an,
Ya-paw-lang ah nin ka Law-teux:
“Ax hoik mawh pui tix nyiex,
ang pon tix simah paox;
cao liang khawng brie maix mai cao liang khawng brie aux ang pon tix simah paox.
9 Ax guah tix khaing!
Haktiex vieh va in ot sivoe ngai maix,
maix laug plak dawm,
aux laug plak goix;
maix laug plak goix,
aux laug plak dawm heue.”
10 bLaw-teux yaog tix jhak khawm pon plak,
mai yaox ka dee lung haktiex Yaw-tang tom hoek ka haktiex Saw-eux;
dee koe rawm tom nie,
awm ka si peem Yi-tiien,a
mai baox awm haktiex Ai-cix;
yam an Simiang Siyiex ang nyang yuh veng Saw-to-ma mai veng Eu-maw-la laux leeing.
11 Khaix an,
Law-teux laug tix teei dee lung haktiex Yaw-tang,
mai lih hu plak lih singaix.
Kiex tom guah paox tix khaing tan.
12 Ya-paw-lang ot haktiex Cia-nang mai Law-teux hu ot daux veng khankix pa koe daux lung Yaw-tang mai sum nyiex gox dex baoh veng Saw-to-ma.
13 Pui veng Saw-to-ma mawh pui caox pui caig,
mai kix kied ting phi map sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
Ya-paw-lang gah tix hoek ka Si-paw-loeng
14 Khaix hoik lih hu Law-teux,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-paw-lang:
“Jauh ka haktiex dee ot maix,
mai jhak ka plak lih,
plak klip,
plak laong,
mai plak seh.
15 cHaktiex pa yaox maix khawm uik,
aux tag sang tawx ka maix mai doeh ceu cad maix leeing ju leeing phan.
16 Aux tag kieh kawn praix maix haoh hun awm phawng tiex;
phan pui tix kaux kaux pon simin ka phawng tiex,
tag pon tix simin ka kawn praix maix.
17 Kaoh heue!
Maix lih hu tauh ka daux haktiex an,
aux sang tawx ka maix.”
18 Khaix an Ya-paw-lang gah nyiex gox man tix khaing tan,
mai hu ot ka haktiex veng Si-paw-loeng,
dee koe Mang-lix khaox yang yit pa singax singeh,
nawh sum khu dee tan tix mu son Simiang Siyiex tan heue.
