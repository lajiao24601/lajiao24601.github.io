Simiang Siyiex pa ting lhaong
11
“Pex tag caw tix muh ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai priex tix hawt gum lox ping nyap mai lox tat jhawm prawng nawh tom si dauh.
2 Priex tix tawng,
ka daux in singaix,
aux hoik krai ka pex,
mai ang krai ka kawn simex kawn bun pex khankix,
mawh kheu to kaux pex hoik cim ka Siyiex mai hoik tawng ka nawh sidawng.
Pex hoik sax yaox ka grawng pa ting pa lhaong Simiang Siyiex,
mai siawp sibrawm nawh,
mai riang rao nawh,
3 amai mhai sakse pa daox pa am pa hoik yuh nawh.
Pex hoik yaox ka grawng pa yuh nawh ka simiang Ai-cix mai ka meung cie nawh,
4 bmai baox yaox nang ceu pa hoik yuh nawh ka kru naing Ai-cix,
khawm brung,
mai ling brung.
Yam leue kix ka pex,
nawh hoik kieh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh kheem kix,
mai kieh kix laux leeing khawm uik.
5 Pex hoik tawng,
sivoe sang hoek pex ka daux haktiex in,
yam ot pex ka daux tiag,
grawng kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex son pex khawm uik.
6 cPex hoik doh keud kah,
yuh nawh ka mawx ka doeh ceu cad Lui-piien,
Ta-thang mai Ya-pi-lang kawn simex Yi-li-ya.
Haktiex bih ka sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai bleet kix khawm ceu pui nyiex ma kix,
khawm nyiex gox kix,
khawm siliex kix,
mai khawng brie kix khawm uik.
7 Pex hoik yaox ka kaing pa ting pa lhaong pa yuh Siyiex khawm uik in heue.”
Buan son ka daux haktiex pa ap tix tawx
8 “Pex tag caw priex tix hawt gum lox ping nyap pa tawx aux ka pex daux in singaix khawm uik.
Phan yuh nin,
pex tom tag koe riang rao tix tiang ka rawm klawng,
mai pon cax ka haktiex pa sang laig pex ka daux,
9 yaox tix lang ju ka daux haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia,
pa hoik ap Simiang Siyiex tix tawx ka tax du pex mai kawn praix pex an heue.
10 Haktiex pa sang cax pex tix cie,
ang lai awm haktiex Ai-cix pa hoik ot pex ka sivoe an.
Ka daux Ai-cix,
khaix hoik sau pex to ma,
pex kawn taug maox tix jaux ka rawm,
tom tag yaox tix vawg,
11 viang mawh nan,
haktiex pa sang laig pex kah,
koe gawng lua,
koe klawng ngawd,
mai koe khawm rawm lhiex pa jaux leet.
12 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tom jauh khix num tix,
hoek ka si dauh khix num,
mai nawh hoik kia ngien ka haktiex in heue.
13 d“Mawh kheu nan,
pex caw tix rien rhawm tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa tawm aux ka pex daux in singaix.
Tag caw tix muh ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai yuh kaing jhaw nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
14 Phan yuh nan,
nawh tag tawx rawm lhiex cao mai rawm lhiex la ka pex,
awm pa khaw mai bawg yam,
mai kieh pex koe nghox koi,
plai khraox,
bee sime,
15 mai rib riem pa ih khawng brie pex khankix tom nie.
Pex yaox tix ih tom sak.
16 Pex caw rien rhawm tix,
ang pon tix tieh vheen khaing Simiang Siyiex ka daux krax pa nyaw glaw,
ang pon tix krub mai hu yuh kaing jhaw siyiex tang kaux.
17 Phan pex yuh nan,
krax song rhawm Siyiex tag lih hoek ka piang pex,
mai nawh tag ang tawx rawm lhiex lih,
mai kieh haktiex kroh,
mai ang chix kuah ka nghox koi;
viang nawh hoik tawx kaung ma pa mhawm ka pex kawx,
pex kawx kawn sang nied tix phai yum ka daux haktiex an heue.
18 “Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw doh keud ka gum lox ping nyap in,
mai tiiem ot ka daux rhawm tix.
Tag caw tix vung vhien gum lox ping nyap in ot ka daux tawm taix tix,
mai ceep ot ka piang dong re tix,
son sang kieh pex tawng sidawng.
19 Pex tag caw tix sidiex gum lox ping nyap in kix ka kawn simex kawn bun tix,
ot ka nyiex ang ah,
hu plak praix ang ah,
yuh kaing jhaw ang ah,
laux lee tix ang ah,
tag caw tix krai lao ka gum lox ping nyap in kix heue.
20 ePex tag caw tix tiiem gum lox ping nyap in ot ka si dub siviex mai ka siviex ting.
21 Yuh nin,
pex mai kawn simex kawn bun pex tag sang yaox tix lang ju ka piang haktiex pa hoik ap Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex tix tawx ka tax du pex khankix;
rao ma mai haktiex koe miex ngaix,
pex kawx tag sang ot tan miex ngaix heue.
22 “Pex tag caw priex rhawm tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa tawm aux ka pex khawm uik,
tag caw tix muh ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai hawt ka lox tawm nawh khawm uik,
mai hawt nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
23 Phan yuh nin,
yam greed pex tix hu ka plak sivoe,
nawh tag grheu ka ceu pui tang ceu khankix hu,
mai kieh pex pon tix cax ka haktiex ceu pui pa lheu ting lhaong khaing pex.
24 fKa haktiex pa hoik dig pex ka caong tix ku dauh,
tag sang tawx mawh cie pex.
Mee haktiex cie pex jauh ka daux tiag plak seh mai vih tix tom hoek ka si niag gawng Li-pa-noeng ka plak laong,
mai jauh ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti plak lih singaix,
mai vih tix tom hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong plak klip singaix.
25 Pex hu dee mawh ang ah,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag sang hawt awm lox pa hoik ah nawh tix tawx ka pex,
mai ang kieh pex koe pui pa ang pex kah,
mai kieh pui lhat graung ka pex khawm uik heue.
26 “Ka daux in singaix,
aux hoik deuh krax nawb buan mai krax jhax lawg ka plak sivoe pex,
to kaux pex tang laug ka heue.
27 Phan pex hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx aux in singaix,
pa mawh pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex khawm uik,
pex tag pon krax pa nawb buan.
28 Phan pex ang hawt ka gum lox ping nyap in kix,
deeh tieh vheen tix hu krub ka siyiex pa ang pex tawng,
mai hu krub ka kix,
pex tag pon krax jhax lawg.
29 gKhaix hoik viex Simiang Siyiex pex laig ka daux haktiex pa sang cax pex tix cie,
pex tag caw tix krai pao ka lox pa nawb buan piang gawng Ci-li-sing,
mai krai pao lox pa jhax lawg piang gawng Yi-pa-lux.
30 Gawng an kiex hoik mawh ka daog rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
ka plak dee klip singaix,
pa mawh dee ngawd ka haktiex cie pui Cia-nang,
ka plak klip singaix dex ka veng Cie-cia,
dee koe Maw-lix khaox pa singax singeh khankix.
31 Pex tag sang tiang ka plak toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai hu laig ka daux haktiex pa sang cax pex tix cie an,
pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka son pex.
Khaix hoik cax tix cie ka haktiex an,
khaix hoik ot paung pex tan,
32 mai pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
pa tawx aux ka pex daux in singaix khawm uik heue.”
