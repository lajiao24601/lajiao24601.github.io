Rawg buan son simiang
72
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix teei pa khaw caw tix sidiex gau ka simiang liak;
khiox khaw maix tawx pa khaw caw maix ka kawn simiang.
2 Yuh nin nawh tom tag chix kia ngien pui cie maix ka daux krax pa rauh pa see,
tag pon chix kia ngien ka pui hot pui shiah ka daux krax pa khaw caw.
3 Kieh piang meung khawm uik pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem;
kieh kawn pui khawm uik pon yaox tix cim krax rauh krax see.
4 Kieh simiang teei krax pa khaw caw hu plak pui hot pui shiah;
kieh nawh taoh ka pui hot pa ang koe cao teem cao coi,
puing ka pui pa gut kheem ka kix.
5 Ca mawh jhan riang singaix,
jhan riang khix,
kieh pui cie maix krub ka maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
6 Kieh simiang awm rawm lhiex lih yuh nan ka piang pak rib,
awm rawm ciex pa caux ka haktiex.
7 Kieh nawh tawx pa rauh pa see tix lih yam mawh tix simiang,
yam kawn koe khix kieh piang meung pon pa nhim,
kawn pui pon krax pa yien yawg heue.
 
8 aMeung cie nawh nih tix jauh ka rawm sigrit sigra plak in tom hoek plak an,
jauh ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti tom hoek ka daog dee uik haktiex.
9 Pui pa paung ot ka daux tiag daux tu tag sang krub ka plak sivoe ngai nawh;
pui pa koeng ka nawh tag sang grux tix krub ka nawh.
10 Tha-see mai simiang pa ot ka klawn tiex khankix tag sang tan lu khawng phak lak sawng ka nawh;
See-pa mai simiang Si-pa kawx hoek tix tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka nawh.
11 Simiang khawm uik tag sang krub ka plak sivoe ngai nawh;
meung khawm uik tag sang yuh kaing jhaw nawh heue.
 
12 Nawh taoh teem ka pui hot pui shiah pa kok yo ka nawh;
nawh teem coi ka pui hot pa ang koe cao teem.
13 Nawh caox rhawm ka pui pa iak riang hot sibhawm;
nawh taoh teem ka ju pui hot sibhawm pa kha rhawm tix.
14 Nawh taoh kix luat luan khaing krax pa tuk tiak tuk te mai krax pa tud pa rhim;
nawh kieh ju kix mawh pa ting ngawh ting jum heue.
 
15 Kieh simiang lang ju pu rhang!
Kieh nawh pon ka khri cie See-pa;
kieh koe pui pa rawg buan son nawh ang jud ang jai;
kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka nawh jheeng jheeng.
16 Kieh haktiex naog ka plix nghox plix koi,
gawng lua naog ka to ma to koi,
kieh pon tix khrawm tom nie awm Li-pa-noeng yuh nan;
kieh pui pa ot daux veng haok huan awm rib klai daux tiag yuh nan.
17 Kieh ceu kaux nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan;
kieh min ceu nawh koe leeing ju awm singaix yuh nan.
Kieh meung piang haktiex rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
kieh kawn pui piang haktiex rawg buan son ka nawh heue.
 
18 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex heue!
Mawh ngie nawh pa pon yuh lih ka kaing pa daox pa deng.
19 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux min plak ngai nawh;
kieh pa riang min plak ngai nawh naog ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
 
A-meung!
A-meung!
 
20 Lox rawg buan Ta-wue kawn simex Yie-si hoik si dauh heue.
