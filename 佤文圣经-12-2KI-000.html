Lai Simiang Phuk Ra
Krai dawt
Lai Simiang Phuk Ra mawh ca krai grawng kaing pui Yi-seu-liex plak seh mai plak laong khawm ra meung. Khawm uik chix tix guah koe ra ceu:
1. Been meung plak laong khawm meung plak seh jauh tix ka sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num dim bawg yam, tom hoek yam laux leeing Sa-ma-li-yax mai yam hoik uik naox meung plak laong tix ka sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num aliah yieh tix nga ra num, heue.
2. Jauh yam laux leeing meung plak laong Yi-seu-liex tom hoek ka sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num phuan yieh tix daix liah num, ka baux bawg yam pa hoik pon simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai yam gleeing kah. Phuk lai in hoik tiiem ka grawng pa tawx Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka Ci-ta-lix mawh cao kuang kaing pa ting ka piang pui Yo-tax, khawm pa nghoik tix krai grawng simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-ya-cin, yam ploi nawh lih khaing daux thawng heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax meung kix si pup ka krax pa kha rhawm, hoik mawh kheu jao simiang kix khawm ra meung, mai kheu jao kawn pui khankix pa ang koe krax cu krax nghiet ka Siyiex. Grawng pa laux leeing pui Yi-seu-liex, mai grawng pa pon giex pui ka pui Yo-tax hu, hoik mawh grawng pa ting ceu pa tieh loh tix ka daux been pui Yi-seu-liex tix ceu heue.
Cao jhai pa blawn krai lih ka daux phuk lai in, mawh Yi-li-sa cao pa plud ka Yi-li-yax heue.
Krai kaing
1. Meung guah paox tix (1.1–17.41)
(1) Cao jhai Yi-li-yax (1.1–8.15)
(2) Simiang Yo-tax mai simiang Yi-seu-liex khankix (8.16–17.4)
(3) Sa-ma-li-yax laux leeing (17.5–41)
2. Meung Yo-tax (18.1–25.30)
(1) Jauh ka Si-si-cia tom hoek ka Yaw-si-yax (18.1–21.26)
(2) Yaw-si-yax giex ka kaing (22.1–23.30)
(3) Simiang pui Yo-tax pa nghoik tix koe ra loe kaux khankix (23.31–24.20)
(4) Yie-lu-sa-leung laux leeing (25.1–30)
