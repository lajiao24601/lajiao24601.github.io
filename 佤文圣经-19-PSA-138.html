Lox rawg buan pa yaog yaw
138
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux yaog yaw ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix;
aux rhax lai yaog yaw maix ka plak sivoe ngai siyiex khankix.
2 Aux veeh ngai tix krub ka nyiex pa singax singeh maix,
aux rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux maix;
mawh kheu kaux sijax maix,
mai koe krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix,
maix hoik jhi ka ceu kaux tix,
mai ka gum lox pa hoik deuh tix sidawng khawm uika.
3 Yam khiox khaw rawg mhaing aux ka maix,
maix hoik pawk ka aux;
maix hoik teei riang rao tix joh jhan ka aux heue.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
simiang ka piang haktiex hoik rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik mhong kix gum lox pa hoik deuh maix.
5 Kix sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa yuh maix,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka pa ting min plak ngai maix.
6 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting min ka piang khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
nawh chix kia ngien ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi khankix;
pui pa ca nyhau khankix ang pon mox tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.
 
7 Yam kham gee aux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
mai maix hoik kia ngien priex ka aux nyawm ot.
Maix hoik puing ka krax pa song rhawm pui koeng khankix,
hoik teei riang rao tix taoh ka aux heue.
8 Maix tag caw tix yuh lih ka gum lox pa hoik krai maix tix deuh ka aux;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
krax muh maix koe leeing ju leeing phan.
Khiox khaw maix yuh si dauh ka kaing pa teei maix taix tix yuh ka heue.
