Yie-li-mi tawx lai ka pui Yo-tax pa vuk vied vuk viang
29
aAux tiiem lai tix pheen ka cao giex kaing li,
ka cao jhai,
ka kuad song,
mai ka pui tang kaux khankix pa hoik giex laung simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hu ka Pa-pi-loeng pa kaoh khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 Lai in mawh pa tiiem khaix hoik lih hu simiang Yaw-ya-cin,
miex simiang,
cao kaing nyiex haw,
cao kaing pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
cang mai,
mai pui pa chix kaing taix khankix khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung.
3 Yam an,
simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Si-ti-cia ploi Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Sa-fang,
mai Ci-ma-lix kawn simex Si-leu-cia,
hoek tix yaox simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Lai in hoik mawh pa tawx kiex ra kaux viex hu heue.
Gum lox pa koe daux lai in hoik tiiem plak greem ah nin:
4 “Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex krai ka can pui pa hoik giex laung khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng khankix ah nin:
5 ‘Pex tag caw tix sum ka nyiex ma,
son sang yaox tix nyawm ot,
sang sum ka kaung ma,
mai yaox tix ih ka to ma to koi khaing daux kaung ma.
6 Pex tag caw tix koe mex koe moeng,
keh kawn bun kawn simex;
kawn simex kawn bun pex kawx tag caw tix koe mex koe moeng,
keh kawn bun kawn simex,
kieh pex pre haok huan ka daux haktiex an,
kaux pui thiem thie tix haoh hun,
ang dieh.
7 Pex tag caw tix keud sawn son sang mhawm veng pa hoik ploi aux pex hu ot ka khankix,
mai khiox khaw son sang pon kix krax pa haok huan;
phan kix pon krax haok huan,
pex kawx tag pon nang heue.
8 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex ne raw ka pex ah nin:
paw vi kieh cao jhai pa ot ka daux pex,
mai ka cao si pawk pa krai chix tawng tix ka kaing pa sang hoek khankix pie lhien ka pex,
mai paw vi yum rhawm ka ca maox kix.
9 Kix pie lhien pex ka daux ceu kaux aux;
aux ang bax ploi kix hu.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.’
10 b“Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
‘Khaix hoik giex Pa-pi-loeng kaing atigliah num,
aux tag sang kia ngien ka pex,
mai yuh ka lox pa ap aux lih,
mai viex kix deeh hoek ka haktiex in.
11 Mawh ngie aux pa tawng ka kaing pa yuh tix son pex yuh nin:
kaing pa yuh aux ang mawh krax laux krax leeing,
mawh krax pa haoh huan;
aux tag sang kieh krax lawng pex mhawm.
12 Yam an,
pex khiox khaw ka aux,
rawg buan ka aux,
aux tag sang pawk lox pex.
13 cPex sawg ka aux,
tag sang yaox ka aux;
phan pex sawg aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
14 aux tag sang kieh pex yaox tix tete.
Aux tag sang kieh pex deeh hoek ka haktiex ka nyiex ma tix.
Aux tag sang khrawm pex khaing meung haktiex pa hoik ploi aux pex hu ot ka ku meung,
mai viex pex deeh hoek ka haktiex pa hoik giex laung pex hu khaing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.’
15 “Pex hoik krai yaog Simiang Siyiex cao jhai son pex ka Pa-pi-loeng.
16 Grawng simiang pa giex kaing ka daux bawg yam pa mawh Ta-wue simiang,
mai pui pa ot ka daux veng in,
mai paox ceu paox cad pa ang giex laung tau mai pex,
17 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
‘Aux tag sang kieh kix si pup ka naing,
ka sibhawm praix,
mai ka jhawm saux preng.
Aux tag sang kieh kix laux awm plix ruh pa ang chix tix ih yuh nan.
18 Aux tag sang teei naing,
sibhawm praix,
mai jhawm saux preng yuh kix laux leeing.
Viang aux hoik ploi kix hoek ka daux haktiex mawx ang ah,
pui ka piang haktiex ku meung tag sang kieh kix mawh pa jhax pa lawg,
pa yaoh pa yee,
pa ghak pa baig,
pa si briem pui ka khankix.
19 Mawh kheu ang kix nghiet ka lox aux.
Aux ca baox ploi ka siliex tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix krai ka kix,
mai kix ca ang cu tix nghiet kah.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa hoik giex laung khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng khankix eue,
pex tag caw bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.’
21 “Phan mawh Ya-ha kawn simex Keu-lai-ya,
mai Si-ti-cia kawn simex Ma-si-ya pa pie lhien pex ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing Siyiex Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
nawh tag sang tawx pui in kiex ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha tag sang pauh kix yum ka plak sivoe ngai pex.
22 Pex pa mawh pui pa giex laung khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng khankix,
yam tag sang jhax lawg pex ka pui tang kaux,
pex tag sang ah nin:
‘Kieh Simiang Siyiex yuh ka maix,
awm hoik yuh simiang Pa-pi-loeng ka Si-ti-cia mai Ya-ha,
mai hoik gleeing kiex yum im yuh nan heue!’
23 In hoik mawh krax pa yaox kix dee si dauh;
mawh kheu kix hoik yuh ka phi map pa ting,
mai phit lud tau mai moeng paox plawg paox nyhawm tix,
mai baox pie lhien ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh hoik tawng ka kaing pa yuh kix,
tag sang hoek tix ne raw ka kix.d
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Lai tiiem See-ma-ya
24-25 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex tag sang kieh aux tawx lox ka See-ma-ya pui Ni-si-lang.
See-ma-ya hoik sax teei ceu kaux tix,
mai tiiem tix tawx lai ka Si-fang-ya cao giex kaing li kawn simex Ma-si-ya,
mai cao giex kaing li tang kaux khankix,
khawm pui hun pa ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix.
Nawh krai yuh nin ka daux lai:
26 “Simiang Siyiex hoik ploi maix mawh cao giex kaing li son Yie-heu-yie-tax;
yam in maix hoik mawh cao kuang kaing ka daux nyiex buan.
Beeh glawm maix mawh pa ciyang ka pui pa cau nyhau,
pa tang ap tix mawh cao jhai khankix,
teei maox ceng phrawk ka nawh,
teei gang rhiem chup ka ngawg nawh heue.
27 Yam in,
ka daux pex koe pui Ya-na-thu pa kaux Yie-li-mi tix kaux hoik tang ap tix mawh cao jhai;
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang hawt tix ciyang ka nawh awm pa khaw maix tix yuh lie?
28 Nawh hoik sax tiiem lai krai ka paox ceu paox cad tix pa ot ka Pa-pi-loeng,
kix hoik vu vie tix tom rheeng bawg yam,
mawh kheu nan tawx lox nhie kix sum ka nyiex ma,
mai paung ot dee an mhawm mhawm,
mai sum nang ka kaung ma,
ih nang ka to ma khaing daux haktiex heue.”
29 Cao giex kaing li Si-fang-ya phat aux mhong lox lai in.
30 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux
31-32 tiiem lai tawx ka can paox ceu paox cad pa vu vie ka Pa-pi-loeng,
phan mawh See-ma-ya pui Ni-si-lang,
nawh ah nin:
“Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka See-ma-ya,
mai ka kawn praix nawh khankix.
Aux ang bax ploi nawh hu,
mawh nawh pa tang ap tix mawh cao jhai,
si kah ka pex,
nhie pex nghiet ka lox pa pie lhien tix.
Nawh ang koe kawn praix pa ot ka daux pex,
ang pon im ju tix yaox ka grawng kaing pa mhawm,
pa yuh aux ka piang to kaux pui cie aux,
mawh kheu hoik cheeik nawh kix tieh vheen ka aux.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
