26
Yaog yaw pui pa daox aw,
hoik awm ang rha khaw tix lih yam rung praix,
lih lhiex yam vawg nghox,
ang mawh pa khaw heue.
2 Lox jhax lawg pa ang koe ceu hoik awm ngie kawn sim khankix pa pu hu pu hoek,
ang chix pon kieh pui laux leeing.
3 Teei maox si vied brung kah,
teei jhawk jhup jheeh la kah,
ka pui pa daox aw kawx chix teei tix siao khaox ka heue.
4 Phan pawk lox pa chawk pui daox aw,
hoik lih tix awm pui daox pa chawk an yuh nan heue.
5 Tag caw tix teei lox pui pa daox aw pawk lox pui daox aw;
yuh nin,
pui pa chawk tom taug tawng,
nawh ang koe krax lhak lhiao awm rhawm pa keud to kaux tix yuh nan heue.
6 Phan tawm tix tawx lox ka pui pa daox aw,
hoik awm kid ka caong tix tix ru,
mai sawg krax nyhap son to kaux tix.
7 Pui pa daox aw teei tix siao ka lox kab,
hoik awm siao pui chawh ka caong pa chawh tix yuh nan.
8 Yaog yaw ka pui pa daox aw,
hoik awm teei lon simaox ting sau tix puing ka ak yuh nan.
9 Pui pa daox aw teei tix siao ka lox kab,
hoik awm sang keud pui yoek tix sang lawd krix khaing daux taix tix yuh nan.
10 Cao nyiex ca nieb pon siao ka pui pa daox aw,
tag kieh pui pa yuh nan ku kaux hoek krax sum krax nai heue.
11 Pui pa daox aw ca nied tix baox yuh kaing pa daox aw,
hoik awm sox pa deeh ih ka ceu pa hao tix yuh nan.
12 Pa ang bax koe krax keud krax sawn ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
hoik een tix mawh pui pa lhak lhiao,
pie pui pa blawn daox khankix kawx kawn pon pex ka nawh heue.
13 Pui pa nieh khankix laux tix ot ka nyiex,
nawh lhat ka ceu pa tix lie?
Lhat ka sivai sang si pa ot plak praix lie?
14 Pui pa nieh khankix tieh jee tieh blaong tix ka piang ku,
awm yia siviex tix ka piang pang jaong siviex yuh nan.
15 Pui pa nieh ploi taix tix teei ka pa ih,
pie sang sau ka daux jheeh tix kawx hoik mawh krax nyhap heue.
16 Pui pa nieh een to kaux tix lheu koe ping nyia cuyi khaing pui aliah kaux pa kied chix pawk ka lox pui khankix.
17 Simah mai pui grawng kaing pa ang dex dawt mai tix,
hoik awm pa hu tuk ka yhaok sox tiag ka daux laih daux cau yuh nan heue.
18-19 Pa pie lhien ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
een mawh krax klieh yuh nan,
hoik awm mawh pui yhot pa klieh lih mai kraung naing pa pauh pui yum heue.
20 Phan ang koe khix khaox,
ngu tag yeet;phan ang koe lox pa ang koe ceu koe cak,
krax simah tag naox tix heue.
21 Gaox ngu baox sau piang gaox ngu,
khix baox piang ngu,
pui pa brax yuh krax simah khankix jiet mawh yuh nin heue.
22 Lox pa ang koe ceu koe cak hoik awm priex nyawm rawm lhong,
yam taug hoek ka jheeh,
mai hoik bleet tix laig.
23 Pa ang chix rien ka lox pa krai tix,
hoik awm aw pa ang mhawm pa kraix aing mau ot ka tix jan yuh nan heue.
24 Pui pa pie lhien teei lox mhawm jhawm jiet,
mai tawb kheem ka rhawm pa koeng pa khoi tix kah.
25 Viang gum lox awn,
ang pon tix yum rhawm kah,
kheu naog krax pa koeng khoi ka daux rhawm rhi nawh.
26 Nawh tee rien tix pon tawb kheem krax pa koeng khoi,
viang mawh nan,
ngai pui haoh pui hun hoik jhak kah,
mai hoik yaox ka kaing pa caox caig yuh nawh.
27 Pa kaong daux o,
mai to kaux deeh cud ka daux;pa pu plieh simaox,
mai simaox deeh pu plieh tix ot piang to kaux.
28 Dak pa pie lhien tag veeh tix ka pui pa koeng ka tix;jheeh pa nyaw glaw chix kieh pui laux leeing heue.
