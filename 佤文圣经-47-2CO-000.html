Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk Ra
Krai dawt
Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk Ra mawh pa tiiem Pao-law yam koe krax pa simah si nax nawh mai ceu cao Keu-ling-to. Yam an, koe paox grawm khankix ka daux ceu cao Keu-ling-to simah ka Pao-law, viang mawh nan, nawh hoik dawg simie tix yuh krax pa khaw tau mai kix. Khaix an nawh tom tag pon tix yuh krax pa khaw tau mai ceu cao Keu-ling-to, kheu nan nawh kied gaux rhawm ka tete heue.
Phuk lai in ka plak sivoe ngai, Pao-law krai lao grawng krax pa si doe nawh tix tau mai ceu cao Keu-ling-to, kah dee bleeh yuh ka mawx nawh lai krai lox pa kin map nai ka daux ceu cao, mai cung ka plak pui pa tieb tie ka ceu cao. Nawh viex rhawm pa yuh nin, kieh lih mawh pa doh yuh rhawm pui, nawh kied koe krax pa gaux rhawm kah. Khaix an, nawh krai ka ceu cao Keu-ling-to, nhie kix sau riang tix tan lu ka mau, mai teem coi ka kawn Khrit pa hot sibhawm ka daux pui Yo-thaix. Dee nghoik ra loe mu simin ting, Pao-law krai lox son mawh to kaux tix kawn cau, mawh kheu yam an ceu cao Keu-ling-to, koe pui pa tux tang een tix tawng kawn cau pa mawh pa meh, mai ne raw Pao-law mawh kawn cau pa pie heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–11)
2. Pao-law mai ceu cao Keu-ling-to (1.12–7.16)
3. Teem coi ka kawn Khrit pui Yo-thaix (8.1–9.15)
4. Pao-law krai lox son mawh to kaux tix kawn cau (10.1–13.10)
5. Lox pa nghoik (13.11–13)
