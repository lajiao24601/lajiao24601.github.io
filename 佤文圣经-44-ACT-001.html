Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau
1
aSimiang Thi-a-fe-law ah nin:
Ka daux phuk lai pa hoik tiiem aux tix ceu,
aux hoik krai lao ka kaing pa yuh Ye-sux mai krax pa sidiex gau nawh khawm uik,
tom yam jauh nawh tix yuh kaing
2 hoek yam rieb nawh haok ka rao ma ka daux ngaix an.
Sivoe sang haok nawh ka rao ma,
nawh hoik krai ka kawn cau pa hoik laug tix tom nie ka daux riang rao Khuan Singax Singeh.
3 Khaix hoik kham nawh krax kha rhawm ka daux tix pon singaix,
nawh hoik sax jhi kix yaox tix tom nie bawg,
mai siao grawng krax pa ang awm paox krai hoik im to kaux tix;
nawh kieh kix yaox to kaux tix,
mai baox krai lao grawng meung Siyiex ka kix.
4 cYam ot kix tau,b
nawh tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Paw vi lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
hawt awm pa hoik krai aux ka pex,
tag caw tix krox jun ka pa hoik ap pong keeing aux tix deuh.
5 dYaw-hang cub com pui ka rawm,
viang mawh nan,
khaix an ra loe singaix pex tag sang com tix ka daux Khuan Singax Singeh heue.”
Rieb Ye-sux haok rao ma
6 Yam khrawm kawn cau tix tau mai Ye-sux,
kix chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ang in hoik mawh bawg yam pa deeh maix tawx siawp sibrawm meung ka Yi-seu-liex lie?”
7 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Nari yam an mai singaix bawg yam an hoik mawh pa tat keeing aux ka daux akhuang to kaux tix,
ang mawh pa caw pex tix tawng kah.
8 eViang mawh nan,
yam lih hoek Khuan Singax Singeh ka piang pex,
pex tag sang grum naog ka riang rao,
tag sang kham sakse son aux ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
meung Yo-thaix,
mai haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax khawm uik,
tom hoek ka ku caong daog haktiex heue.”
9 fKhaix hoik krai ka lox in,
kix dong ngai tix yam rieb Ye-sux haok rao ma;
koe lon pai awm tie ka ngai kix ang lai kieh kix chix yaox heue.
10 Yam lih hu nawh,
kix kawn maong ka rao ma.
Dum mawx dum an,
koe pui pa ceep kraung paing cung ka praok kix ra kaux,
11 ah nin:
“Pui Cia-li-lix eue,
yuh ka mawx pex lai cung tix maong rao ma dee in lie?
Ye-sux kaw in pa lih hu khaing pex,
pa rieb haok ka rao ma,
hoik yaox pex nawh haok ka rao ma yuh ka mawx,
nawh kawx tag sang deeh hoek yuh nan nang heue.”
Pui pa toe tix plud ka Yo-tax
12 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau kaoh khaing gawng sime deeh ing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
gawng sime singaix khaing veng been tix kongli.
13 gYam laig kix daux veng,
kix haok tix paung it ka nyiex lhaong;
dee an mawh Pi-teux,
Yaw-hang,
Ya-keux,
mai Ang-teu-liex,
Fe-lix mai To-ma,
Pa-to-law-mai mai Ma-thaix,
Ya-keux kawn simex Ya-leu-fe,
Si-meung cub caw pui pa kin jhawm,
mai Yo-tax kawn simex Ya-keux.
14 Kix ot tix rawg buan tau jheeng jheeng;
ka daux kix kawx bun miex koe ka ra loe kaux,
mai Ma-li-yax miex Ye-sux,
khawm pux aik nawh khankix heue.
15 Khaix an ra loe singaix kawn Khrit khrawm tix ot tau,
kix koe been tix yieh tix nga kaux.
Pi-teux kaoh cung ah nin:
16 “Paox grawm khankix eue!
Khuan Singax Singeh kieh jheeh Ta-wue krai gum lox jhai ka daux phuk lai sigang khri,
grawh grawng pa viex Yo-tax pui hoek giex ka Ye-sux tag sang lih tix mawh tete.
17 Yo-tax nawh hoik mawh pa laug lih koe beeh glawm ka daux pa yuh ex kaing krub,
hoik mawh pui ka daux ex tix kaux sivoe an heue.”
(
18 hYo-tax teei mau pa pon tix khaing daux krax pa caox caig liag haktiex tix dauh;
ka dee an,
nawh tom laux leeing,
mai nawh yum ka ghlua kraung vaig tix.
19 Pui pa ot daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mhong grawng in khawm uik,
mawh kheu nan,
siao lox kix kok kaung an mawh Ya-keu-ta-ma,
deeh bleeh mawh “kaung nham” heue.)
20 iAwm hoik tiiem ka daux Lai Yaog Yaw ah nin:
kieh nyiex ma nawh riem,
mai ang koe pui pa paung ot daux heue.
Baox koe tix dauh tiiem ah nin:
kieh pui tang kaux plud tix yuh kaing pa yuh nawh.
21-22 jPi-teux baox ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
tag caw tix baox sawg pui tix kaux laig ka daux cub caw ex,
son sang mawh pui pa kham sakse grawng pa kaoh im Ye-sux tau mai ex.
Pui in tag caw tix mawh pui pa ot tau mai ex tom si dauh,
tom yam ot Ye-sux ka daux ex,
pa mawh yam jauh Yaw-hang tix cub com,
hoek ka glang yam rieb Ye-sux haok ka rao ma heue.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
pui hun laug pui lih ra kaux,
pa mawh Yaw-seux (ceu kaux tang ceu Pa-sha-pa,
mai baox kaux Yo-see-tu) mai Ma-thi-yax.
24 Kix rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
maix hoik tawng ka rhawm rhi pui ku kaux;
khiox khaw maix krai ka yix,
pui in kiex ra kaux hoik laug maix pa yuh ka mawx
25 hoek tix plud ka ara kaing pa yuh kawn cau in.
Ara kaing in Yo-tax hoik tik kah,
nawh hoik hu ka dee pa sang caw nawh tix hu heue.”
26 Khaix hoik krai ka lox in kix cawk lai.
Ka daux ceu kaux pui in kiex,
kix yaog ka Ma-thi-yax lih;
nawh tom laig yuh cub caw ka daux kawn cau kao tix kaux heue.
