Lox rhax pa yaog yaw buan son
66
Kawn pui ka piang haktiex ku ceu tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex heue!
2 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka min plak ngai nawh;
tag caw tix tan lu lox rhax krax yaog yaw ka nawh.
3 Tag caw tix krai ah nin ka Siyiex:
kaing yuh maix kied mawh pa daox pa deng heue!
Mawh kheu pa ting riang ting rao maix,
pui koeng tag sang ap tix ang pex ka maix.
4 Kawn pui piang haktiex hoik krub ka maix khawm uik;
kix rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux pa ting min maix heue.
 
5 Hoek heue,
hoek tix jhak ka kaing ting pa yuh Siyiex;
hoek tix jhak ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh nawh ka daux kawn pui.
6 aNawh kieh rawm sigrit sigra tieh tix lih mawh piang tiex kroh,
kieh tax du ex tiang hu ka caong tix;
ex ot dee an mai gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu kaing pa yuh nawh.
7 Nawh teei pa ting riang ting rao tix giex ka kaing leeing ju;
ngai nawh lam lia ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik;
pui pa tieh vheen eue,
ang pon tix ting rhawm lhaong rhi.
8 Kawn pui ka piang haktiex eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex ex;
kieh lox pa rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh tauh hu tom hoek khawm pon praok.
9 Nawh kia ngien ka ju ex,
ang kieh ex sideeing tix goh heue.
 
10 Siyiex eue,
maix hoik cheuk cheu tix yaox ka yix;
maix hoik cheuk tix yaox yix,
awm mau pa hoik raug daux ngu yuh nan.
11 Maix hoik kieh yix cawd ka daux rub,
sau khaox glawm pa cien ka piang blawg yix.
12 Maix kieh pui koeng dig ka yix;
maix hoik kieh yix kham pa kheem rawm,
mai pa hax ngu,
viang mawh nan,
yam in maix hoik deuh yix ot ka dee pa kaux pa congb heue.
 
13 Aux tag sang viex Krax-tan Pa-gleeing laig ka daux nyiex buan maix;
aux tag sang plud ka lox pa hoik ap pong aux tix tiex tu ka maix.
14 Lox pa hoik ap pong aux tix tiex tu ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
aux tag sang plud kah.
15 Aux tag sang gleeing tix tan lu ka kawn yung mhaing;
aux tag sang tan lu ka moi mhaing mai kawn pex;
taox bhawng Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa gleeing tix tan tag sang haok ka pai awm;
16 pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex eue,
hoek tix nghiet ka khawm uik heue;
aux tag sang krai lao ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh son aux.
17 Aux khiox khaw krax teem krax coi nawh;
aux kawx sang rhax ka lox rhax pa yaog yaw ka nawh.
18 Phan aux ang doh keud ka phi map to kaux tix,
Simiang tag ang nghiet ka lox aux;
19 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik nghiet ka lox aux;
nawh hoik nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg aux heue.
 
20 Aux tag sang yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
mawh kheu ang nawh bax tieb tie ka lox buan pa rawg aux,
ang bax deeh teei ka krax muh pa koe tix leeing ju heue.
