Lai Eu-pa-ti-yax
1
Plak greem mawh riang su sai pa yaox Eu-pa-ti-yax,
mawh lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka son Yi-tong heue.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka Yi-tong
Simiang Siyiex ploi kawn cau hu ka meung piang haktiex;
ex mhong ka gum lox pa sidiex kawn cau.
Nawh ah nin:
kaoh cung heue!
Ex tag sang yuh naing ka pui Yi-tong!
2 Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui Yi-tong ah nin:
aux tag sang kieh maix iak riang ka piang meung haktiex;
pui ku kaux tag sang map nai ka maix.
3 Krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi maix hoik pie lhien ka to kaux maix.
Veng ting ot maix hoik mawh tab pa yuh ka simaox rang;
nyiex ma maix ot ka piang gawng lhaong.
Mawh kheu nan,
maix ah nin ka to kaux tix:
pui mawx pon bluh aux lih lie?
4 Viang maix pon pu lhaong awm klang ting yuh nan,
mai sum nyiex ma tix ot si nax simuing,
aux ca kawn sang bluh maix lih heue.
 
5 Pui briex hoek,
mai yieh tix laig daux siviex yam faik praix,
mai kix teei ngie tix viex hu ka ceu pa sang lo tix khaing;
yam pieh pui ka plix siiet,
mai kix ca kawn prah ka ceeix ceeix;
viang mawh nan,
pui koeng ka maix hoik rhim maix hu khawm uik.
6 Doeh ceu cad Yi-sao eue,
pui hoik tud sin ngao maix hu.
7 Paox grawm maix hoik pie lhien ka maix;
kix hoik grheu maix lih hu khaing meung haktiex ot maix.
Pui pa yuh paox pie ka maix ka daux krax pa nhim,
yam in hoik pon kuang ka maix;
paox grawm pa sawm tau mai maix,
hoik taong jhawk yuh maix laux leeing.
Kix nyiah maix ah nin:
krax lhak lhiao maix ot dee mawx lie?
 
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
singaix an pa ciyang aux ka pui Yi-tong,
aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka kix pui pa lhak lhiao,
mai teei tix tik ka kix can pui pa koe ping nyia.
9 Pui naing Thi-mang tag sang si yaung;
pui naing Yi-tong tag sang yum heue.
Grawh grawng pa ciyang ka Yi-tong
10 Mawh kheu yuh maix pux aik tix Ya-keuxa laux leeing,
maix tag sang hoek krax kaig tua,
mai laux leeing ju.
11 Singaix puing pui koeng ka siviex veng,
maix jhak ngie ang pon tawx taix tix.
Pui tang ceu tud rhim ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai guah tix cie ka sin ngao an,
kieh mawh cie to kaux tix,
maix hoik mawh krax tix nyaing tau maix kix.
12 Singaix yam si pup pux aik maix Yo-tax krax laux leeing,
maix khaw tix ang dong ngai tix jhak.
Singaix yam pa laux leeing kix,
maix khaw tix ang gaux rhawm ka laux leeing kix.
Singaix yam si pup kix pa laux leeing,
maix khaw tix ang nyiah kix.
13 Yam si pup pui cie aux ka krax laux leeing,
maix khaw tix ang laig ka daux siviex veng kix.
Singaix yam si pup kix pa laux leeing,
maix khaw tix ang blah ngai tix jhak ka krax kha kix rhawm tix,
singaix yam si pup kix pa laux leeing,
maix khaw tix ang tud rhim ka sin ngao kix.
14 Maix khaw tix ang cung ka gah krax,
mai giex ka pui pa to mox tix.
Singaix yam si pup kix ka pa laux leeing heue.
Maix khaw tix ang ap kix ka pui koeng.
Siyiex prawng ca ka meung piang haktiex
15 Singaix pa prawng ca aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ka meung piang haktiex hoik hoek heue.
Pui Yi-tong eue,
maix tag sang pon krax pa pawk bix awm kaing pa hoik yuh tix;
maix hoik yuh kaing ceu pa tix,
tag sang deeh pon ceu pa tix.
16 Pui cie aux hoik nyaux kawk pa song ka gawng pa singax aux.
Can meung pa koe sivie siveng khawm uik tag sang nyaux ka kawk pa nie blawn song;
kix tag sang nyaux ka singax khawm uik,
khaix an grai ang lai yaox pie ceeix kawx heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pon pex
17 Viang mawh nan,
ka piang gawng Shi-ang tee koe pui pa luat luan;
gawng in tag sang lih mawh gawng pa singax.
Nyiex ma Ya-keux tag sang deeh hoek ot ka haktiex ot tix sivoe an.
 
18 Nyiex ma Ya-keux tag sang lih mawh cap ngu ting;
nyiex ma Yaw-seux tag sang lih mawh rao ngu;
kix tag sang gleeing laux leeing ka nyiex ma Yi-sao,
awm gleeing ka nyhee glauh yuh nan.
Doeh ceu cad Yi-sao ang koe pui pa luat luan tix kaux kawx.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
19 Pui Yo-tax pa ot plak seh tag sang pon haktiex Yi-tong
pui pa ot ka caong gawng ka plak klip singaix tag sang puing ka pui Fe-li-see.
Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang pon tix cie ka haktiex Yi-fa-liien mai ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax;
pui Piien-ya-ming tag sang pon ka haktiex Ci-liex.
20 Pui naing Yi-seu-liex plak laong pa vuk vied vuk viang,
tag sang pon kuang ka haktiex Fe-ni-ci,
tom hoek ka haktiex Sa-leu-fax;
Pui pa jauh tix vuk vied vuk viang ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung hoek ka haktiex Sa-ti-see,
tag sang pon veng khankix pa koe ka haktiex plak seh Yo-tax.
21 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa puing pex
tag sang puing ka Yi-tong,
giex kaing piang pui Yi-tong.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang lih hoek mawh simiang heue.
