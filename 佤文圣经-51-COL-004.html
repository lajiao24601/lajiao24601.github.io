4
aPa mawh simiang khankix,
pex tag caw tix kia ngien siliex tix ka daux pa khaw caw,
mawh kheu pex hoik tawng,
ka piang rao ma pex kawn koe simiang tix kaux heue.
Gum lox pa sidiex gau
2 Pex tag caw sau riang tix rawg buan,
khaix an tag rien rhawm tix,
mai kieh rawm rhi tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex.
3 Tag caw tix rawg buan son yix nang,
khiox khaw Siyiex tawx bawg yam kieh ex sidiex ka gum lox nawh,
mai pon krai hu ka pa raux pa mox cie Khrit.
Yam in aux hoik ot daux thawng son kaing in.
4 Khiox khaw rawg buan son aux liak,
kieh aux pon sau riang tix krai pa raux pa mox in lih heue.
5 bYam lai ling pex ka pui pa ang mawh kawn Khrit,
tag caw tix koe ping nyia,
giex ka bawg yam.
6 Lox si kah tag caw tix awn koe jhawm,
tag caw tix tawng grawng sang pawk ka lox pa chawk pui ku kaux heue.
Lox nghoik pa chawk mhawm ot
7 cdGrawng pa ot aux dee in khawm uik,
pux aik pa muh ex Thoe-ci-ku tag sang krai pex mhong sidawng.
Nawh mawh siliex pa kaux sijax tix kaux,
ka daux kaing jhaw cie Simiang Siyiex,
nawh mawh pa yuh siliex tau mai aux.
8 Aux bre tix ploi nawh hu ka pex dee an,
krai grawng pa ot yix dee in,
son sang kieh pex pon krax pa joh jhan.
9 ePa hu tau mai nawh mawh pux aik pa muh pa kaux sijax tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh A-ni-si-mo;
nawh mawh pui pex dee an.
Kiex ra kaux taug sang krai pex mhong kaing pa koe dee in khawm uik ka pex heue.
10 fYa-li-ta-ku pa ot daux thawng tau mai aux chawk pex mhawm ot.
Ma-kheu pux simex Pa-na-pa kawx chawk pex mhawm ot.
(Phan mawh son Ma-kheu,
aux hoik tawx lox ka pex,
phan nawh haok ka pex dee an,
khiox khaw pex rieb nawh liak.)
11 Yie-su ceu kaux tang ceu pa kok mawh Yo-see-tu kawx chawk pex mhawm ot.
Ka daux kawn Khrit pui Yo-thaix,
mawh ngie plak piang pui loe kaux in pa yuh kaing meung cie Siyiex tau mai aux khaing,
kix kied koe krax pa teem coi ka aux heue.
12 gYi-pa-fu pa kaoh khaing pex dee an chawk pex mhawm ot,
nawh kawx hoik mawh siliex Ye-sux Khrit.
Nawh rien rawg buan son pex jheeng jheeng,
khiox khaw Siyiex kieh krax yum rhawm pex kaux cong,
mawh kawn Khrit pa haok huan tix kaux,
tag caw tix hawt ka alo Siyiex khawm uik.
13 Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa taug mao son pex,
mai son Lao-ti-cia khawm kawn Khrit Si-la-phaw-li;
14 hPux aik pa muh ex Lu-cia cao pai saux,
mai Ti-ma pui ra kaux kawx chawk pex mhawm ot heue.
15 Khiox khaw son yix chawk pux aik pux o pa ot ka Lao-ti-cia,
Ling-fa mai ka ceu cao pa ot daux nyiex nawh mhawm ot.
16 Khaix hoik phat pex ka lox lai in,
deeh tawx ceu cao Lao-ti-cia phat kah;
yam an,
pex kawx tag caw tix phat ka lox lai pa deeh tawx ceu cao Lao-ti-cia ka pex.
17 iTag caw tix krai ka Ya-ci-pux,
kaing jhaw cie Simiang Siyiex pa hoik rieb nawh ka an,
tag caw sau riang tix yuh hoik heue.
18 Aux tiiem ah nin:
“Pao-law nawb pex mhawm ot heue!”
Paw vi piawm grawng pa ot maox ceng ka aux heue.
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka pex heue!
