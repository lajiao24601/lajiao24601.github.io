Phek tiiem ka miex mai mai ka simiang cao prawng see
18
Ye-sux krai gum lox phek tiiem tix ceu ka pui gau tix,
mai nhie kix rawg buan ku jheeng jheeng,
paw vi laux rhawm.
2 Nawh ah nin:
“Ka veng tix veng koe simiang cao prawng see tix kaux,
nawh ang lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
mai ang yung yi ka pui nang.
3 Ka daux veng an koe mie mai tix kaux rien hu ka nawh jheeng jheeng,
mai khiox khaw nawh prawng rauh see son tix,
prawng ca ka pui pa phit lud ka nawh.
4 Simiang cao prawng see in ca dha ka bawg yam,
viang mawh nan,
khaix an nawh keud daux rhawm tix ah nin:
viang aux ang lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
ang yung yi ka pui nang.
5 Viang mawh nan,
miex mai in hoik lak lheet ka aux ang jud,
ang lheu khaing nied aux prawng rauh see son nawh,
son ang nawh lai sang hoek tix lak lheet aux ka nyiex jheeng jheeng heue.”
6 Simiang Ye-sux rieb tix ah nin:
“Pex nghiet tix yaox ka lox pa hoik krai simiang cao prawng see an heue!
7 Khex ang Siyiex chix teem coi tix prawng rauh see pui pa khiox khaw ka nawh khankix leeing ngaix leeing sawm lie?
Nawh tag lha hoek tix teem coi ka kix lie?
8 Aux krai ka pex,
nawh tee rien tix nied hoek prawng rauh see son kix.
Viang mawh nan,
yam lih hoek kawn kawn pui,
nawh sang pon yaox ka krax yum rhawm pa yuh nin piang haktiex lie?”
Phek tiiem ka pui Fa-li-saix mai ka pui koh keen
9 Ye-sux baox krai ka gum lox phek tiiem tang ceu tix ceu,
pa mawh pa krai ka pui pa een tix rauh see mai ang een pui tang kaux koe ceu.
Nawh ah nin:
10 “Koe pui ra kaux hoek rawg buan ka daux nyiex buan:
tix kaux mawh pui Fa-li-saix,
tix kaux mawh pui koh keen.
11 Pui Fa-li-saix an kaoh cung rawg buan ah nina:
‘Siyiex eue,
aux yaog yaw ka maix,
mawh kheu ang aux awm pui tang kaux pa koe krax ghoing rhawm,
pa ang rauh see,
pa phit vao yuh nan,
mai nhie ang awm pui pa koh keen an yuh nan.
12 Aux hoik tuah ka priex preem tix vang tuah ra bawg,
mai baox tan lu ka can mau pon tix kao son tix son heue.’
13 Viang mawh nan,
pui koh keen an kok cung dee singai,
mai ang pon yaog kaing tix jhak rao ma,
mai dieh naok tix ah nin khaing:
‘Siyiex eue,
caox rhawm ka aux pa mawh pui phi map in liak!’
14 bAux krai ka pex,
yam deeh ing pui in kiex,
pa mawh pui rauh see ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex hoik mawh pui koh keen an,
ang mawh pui Fa-li-saix an.
Mawh kheu Siyiex tag sang yeen pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tiiem,
mai yaog pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi lhaong heue.”
Ye-sux nawb buan ka kawn nyawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 19.13–15; Lai Ma-kheu 10.13–16)
15 Koe pui viex kawn ngie hoek ka Ye-sux,
son sang kieh Ye-sux deuh taix tix piang kix.
Pui gau yaox kah,
tom ne raw ka kix.
16 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux kok kawn nyawm khankix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix ah nin:
“Kieh kawn nyawm hoek ka aux dee in,
paw vi tieb tie ka kix,
mawh kheu pui cie meung Siyiex tag sang awm kix pa yuh nin heue.
17 Pex tag yuh tix tawng,
can pui pa ang rieb ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex awm kawn nyawm yuh nin,
ang doh pon lih mawh pui cie nawh.”
Ceu pa nu tix yuh ka pui mi pui koe
(Lai Ma-thaix 19.16–30; Lai Ma-kheu 10.17–31)
18 Koe simiang pui Yo-thaix tix kaux hoek sawg tix chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara pa mhawm,
sang khaw aux tix yuh ka mawx tag pon ka ju leeing ju lie?”
19 Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai kok aux mawh pa mhawm lie?
Lheu khaing Siyiex tix kaux khaing,
ang lai baox koe pa mhawm.
20 cMaix tee hoik tawng gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tat tix deuh ah nin:
‘Ang pon tix phit vao;
ang pon tix pauh pui;
ang pon tix briex lak;
ang pon tix kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh;
tag caw tix yung yi ka miex keeing tix heue.’ ”
21 Pui an pawk ah nin:
“Gum lox ping nyap in kix khawm uik aux hoik priex tix hawt yuh tom yam iak tix heue.”
22 Ye-sux mhong ka lox in,
mai baox ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix kawn cud tix ceu.
Hu coh can sin ngao pa koe maix khawm uik,
mai tan mau ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
maix tom tag koe sin ngao pa deuh ot ka piang meung rao ma;
khaix an hawt khaix aux heue.”
23 Pui an mhong ka lox in,
tom laux rhawm laux rhi tix hu,
mawh kheu kied mag mi nawh heue.
24 Ye-sux jhak ka nawh tom ah nin:
“Pui pa koe mau sang lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex kied mawh pa nu tete heue!
25 Pui pa koe mau sang lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex,
kawn nu khaing laig ka-la-uk ka daux kre nyex heue!”
26 Pui pa mhong lox in tom chawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pui mawx sang pon krax taoh lie?”
27 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Ceu pa ang kawn pui pon yuh,
Siyiex hoik pon yuh khawm uik heue.”
28 Yam in,
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
yix hoik prah ka nyiex ma tix hawt ka maix heue.”
29 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
can pui pa hoik prah ghram ka nyiex ma tix,
ka pux aik tix,
ka miex keeing tix,
mai ka kawn praix tix son meung Siyiex,
30 tag sang nie pon ka daux singaix yam in,
khaix an ka daux singaix pa sang hoek tag sang pon ka ju leeing ju heue.”
Ye-sux grox krai grawng sang yum tix ka daux loe bawg
(Lai Ma-thaix 20.17–19; Lai Ma-kheu 10.32–32–34)
31 Ye-sux viex kawn cau tix kao ra kaux hoek ka praok tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Yam in ex haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
kaing ceu pa hoik krai cao jhai grawng pa dex mai kawn kawn pui tag sang lih tix mawh ku ceu.
32 Nawh tag sang ap tix tawx ka daux tawm taix pui tang ceu tang cad;
kix tag sang map nai ka nawh,
nu nai ka nawh,
mai baig rawm mia ka nawh,
33 tag sang baox si vied ka nawh,
pauh ka nawh;
viang mawh nan,
ka daux loe singaix,
nawh tag sang deeh kaoh im heue.”
34 Pui gau ang tawng grawng kaing in kix tix ceu kawx,
mai ang tawng lox pa hoik krai Ye-sux pie ceeix kawx,
mawh kheu dee bleeh lox in kix hoik mawh kaing pa glip heue.
Ye-sux pai pui caung pa dug ngai moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 20.29–34; Lai Ma-kheu 10.46–52)
35 Ye-sux hoik dex ka haktiex Yie-li-keu,
koe pui dug ngai ngawm caung ka praok krax.
36 Nawh mhong hoek pui hun dee an,
nawh tom chawk mawh kaing ceu pa tix heue.
37 Koe pui krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux hoek dee in heue.”
38 Nawh tom kok yo ah nin:
“Ye-sux kawn praix Ta-wue eue,
caox rhawm ka aux liak!”
39 Pui pa hu sivoe nawh ne raw ka nawh,
ang cu nawh nye.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh nied kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Kawn praix Ta-wue eue,
caox rhawm ka aux liak!”
40 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux kaog,
mai tawx lox nhie kix viex pui dug ngai an hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix.
Yam hoek nawh dex,
Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
41 “Sang simie maix aux yuh ceu pa tix son maix lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux simie tix chix yaox heue!”
42 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix tag sang chix yaox heue!
Krax yum rhawm maix hoik pai ka maix heue.”
43 Pui dug ngai an chix yaox dum mawx dum an,
nawh tom hawt ka Ye-sux,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex.
Pui hun yaox ka kaing in,
mai kix kawx rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex heue.
