Ya-paw-la-hang mai Ya-pi-mi-leux
20
Ya-paw-la-hang gah tix khaing Mang-lix mai hoek ka Cia-nang plak seh,
ot si nax haktiex Cia-ti-see mai Su-eux.
Yam ot nawh ka haktiex Ci-la-eux,
2 amai krai ka pui mawh moeng tix Sa-la pux tix;
kheu nan,
simiang Ci-la-eux pa mawh Ya-pi-mi-leux ploi pui tix hu teei ka Sa-la.
3 Koe tix ngaix plak pon bo,
Simiang Siyiex hoek ka Ya-pi-mi-leux daux ca maox pon sawm,
ah nin:
“Maix caw tix yum!
Kawn bun pa teei maix in mawh moeng pui heue!”
4 Yam an,
Ya-pi-mi-leux ang nyang baox dex dawt mai Sa-la;
nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux mawh pui ang koe dee lud!
Maix tag sang pauh aux mai pui cie aux laux lie?
5 Ya-paw-la-hang krai mawh bun in pux tix,
bun in kawx hoik ah mawh tete.
Aux yuh kaing in hoik mawh pa khaw;
aux ang koe dee lud heue!”
6 Siyiex ah nin ka nawh daux ca maox:
“Mawh heue,
aux tawng hoik yuh maix ceu in mawh pa khaw,
mawh kheu nan,
aux tom tieb tie maix ang yuh krax phit lud ka aux,
son ang maix sang yuh krax phit ka nawh.
7 Yam in,
maix deeh tawx bun in ka mex nawh;
nawh mawh cao jhai,
mai nawh chix rawg buan son maix,
sang kieh maix ang hoek krax yum.
Viang mawh nan,
aux ne raw ka maix,
phan maix ang deeh tawx bun in ka mex nawh,
maix mai pui cie maix sang yum uik heue.”
8 Ngaix khaix plak pon ngawb cao,
Ya-pi-mi-leux khrawm ka siliex tix khawm uik,
mai krai kix mhong grawng kaing in kix;
mai kix kied lhat graung kah.
9 Ya-pi-mi-leux kok Ya-paw-la-hang mai chawk ah nin ka nawh:
“Yuh ka mawx maix chix yuh ceu in ka yix?
Mawh koe krax phit aux ka maix dee mawx?
Maix lai viex krax laux leeing hoek tawx ka aux mai meung cie aux lie?
Maix yuh kaing pa ang maix khaw tix yuh,
10 yuh pa tix maix lai yuh kaing pa yuh nin?”
11 Ya-paw-la-hang pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik keud pui haktiex in ang koe krax lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
mai teei ka moeng aux sang pauh aux yum kheu.
12 Tete,
nawh mawh nan aux.
Nawh mai aux mawh rom keeing tang miex,
khaix an tom mex ka aux.
13 Kheu nan,
yam kieh Siyiex aux lih khaing nyiex yaong ot tix,
mai vung viang ka piang haktiex tang meung,
aux krai nawh mhong;
‘ang kuang ax hoek dee mawx,
maix caw krai mawh aux pux ke tix,
in tom hoik mawh krax buan son pa muh maix ka aux heue.’ ”
14 Khaix an,
simiang Ya-pi-mi-leux deeh tawx Sa-la ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
khawm khawng brie,
phung yung,
phung moi,
khawm siliex cawng ka nawh.
15 Nawh ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Maix jhak,
in mawh haktiex aux,
maix muh tix ot dee mawx maix ot tan heue.”
16 Nawh ah nin ka Sa-la:
“Aux tawx mau tix rheng ka pux ke maix,
kieh mawh pa kham sakse pa singax singeh maix;
maix ang baox yuh kaing pa lud loe ka pui heue.”
17-18 Mawh kheu kaing pa si pup moeng Ya-paw-la-hang Sa-la,
Simiang Siyiex kieh moeng iak Ya-pi-mi-leux pa ot daux nyiex haw nawh ang chix keh kawn.
Ya-paw-la-hang rawg buan son Ya-pi-mi-leux,
Simiang Siyiex tom pai ka bun pa ot daux nyiex haw nawh moi,
deeh kieh kix chix keh kawn heue.
