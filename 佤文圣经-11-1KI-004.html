Siliex Saw-law-meung
4
Saw-law-meung mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik;
2 ceu kaux siliex nawh khankix tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Cao giex kaing li pa ting mawh:
kawn simex Sa-tu pa kaux Ya-sa-li-ya.
3 Cao tiiem lai daux nyiex simiang mawh:
kawn simex See-sa pa kaux Yi-li-heu-liex mai Ya-si-yax.
Cao kaing been mawh:
kawn simex Ya-si-lui pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa.
4 Simiang cao bung kaing naing mawh:
kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya.
Cao giex kaing li mawh:
Sa-tu mai Ya-pi-ya-tha.
5 Cao kuang kaing mawh:
kawn simex Na-tang pa kaux Ya-sa-li-ya.
Cao pa teem coi ka kix ka daux nyiex simiang mawh:
kawn simex Na-tang pa kaux Sa-pu-teu cao giex kaing li.
6 Kawn cau kuang kaing ka daux nyiex haw mawh:
Ya-si-sa.
Cao kuang kaing bun siliex mawh:
kawn simex Ya-pi-tax pa kaux Ya-to-ni-lang heue.
7 Saw-law-meung ploi pui kao ra kaux hu mawh cao kuang kaing ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh haktiex.
Kix tag caw tix tawx priex preem ka simiang,
mai ka nyiex ma simiang khaing daux haktiex pa kuang tix;
ka daux haktiex cao kuang ku dauh,
kix tag caw tix guah tix klawm ku num tix kaux tix khix.
8 Cao kuang haktiex in kao ra kaux mai haktiex pa kuang kix tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Haktiex pa kuang Piien Hu-eux mawh gawng Yi-fa-liien.
9 Haktiex pa kuang Piien Ti-ciax mawh Ma-cia-see,
Sa-ping,
Paw See-mai,
Yi-loeng Paw Ha-lang heue.
10 Haktiex pa kuang Piien Si-si mawh veng Ya-lu-paw mai veng Saw-keux ra mu,
khawm haktiex Si-fu uik heue.
11 Haktiex pa kuang Piien Ya-pi-na-tax pa teei tix moeng kawn bun Saw-law-meung pa kaux Tha-fa mawh haktiex baoh To-eux khawm uik heue.
12 Haktiex pa kuang kawn simex Ya-si-lui pa kaux Pa-na mawh veng Tha-na mai Mi-cie-to ru mu,
mai haktiex dex ka plak seh Paw Sang,
Sa-la-tang,
Yie-see-liex khawm uik,
tom hoek ka veng Ya-paw Mi-heu-la mai veng Yaw-nien heue.
13 Haktiex pa kuang Piien Ci-piiex mawh veng La-mawx pa koe daux haktiex Ci-liex,
nyiex yaong ceu pui Ya-eux,
pa mawh kawn praix Ma-na-si,
mai pa ot daux haktiex Ci-liex,
khawm haktiex Ya-eu-keu-paw pa ot ka Pa-sang,
khawm uik koe tix gliah yaong,
yaong in kix hoik mawh pa koe guh veng,
mai jhong siviex pa mawh tawng khawm uik heue.
14 Haktiex pa kuang kawn simex Yi-to pa kaux Ya-si-na-ta mawh haktiex Ma-ha-nian heue.
15 Haktiex pa kuang Ya-si-ma-see pa baox teei tix moeng kawn bun Saw-law-meung tix kaux pa kaux Pa-seu-mawx mawh haktiex Na-fu-tha-lix heue.
16 Haktiex pa kuang kawn simex Hu-sai pa kaux Pa-na mawh haktiex Ya-seux mai yaong Pi-ya-lux heue.
17 Haktiex pa kuang kawn simex Pha-lu-yax pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa mawh haktiex Yi-sa-cia heue.
18 Haktiex pa kuang kawn simex Yi-la pa kaux See-me mawh haktiex Piien-ya-ming heue.
19 Haktiex pa kuang kawn simex Wu-lix pa kaux Ci-piiex mawh haktiex Ci-liex,
pa mawh haktiex pa kuang simiang pui Ya-maw-lix pa kaux Si-hong mai simiang Pa-sang pa kaux Eux khankix heue.
Lheu khaing cao kuang kaing haktiex in kao ra kaux,
kawn koe haktiex pa kuang cao kuang kaing ting khawm uik heue.
Saw-law-meung haok huan ting lhaong
20 Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex hoik haoh hun awm mhaik ka praok rawm sigrit sigra yuh nan.
Kix hoik yaox tix ih yaox tix nyaux,
mai gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
21 aMeung haktiex pa kuang Saw-law-meung,
hoik jauh ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti tom hoek ka Fe-li-see mai khawm meung pa koe sivie siveng haktiex Ai-cix khankix.
Singaix yam kawn im ju Saw-law-meung ka piang haktiex,
kix tawx keen ka nawh,
mai hawt rhawm nawh heue.
22 Saw-law-meung caw tix sang lo phawng mien tix ngaix phuan rheng kongsheng,
phawng mien ting mu tix mheen kongsheng,
23 moi kluing pa eei khaing daux kawg kao mu,
moi kluing pa liang khaing daux tiag tix nga mu,
kawn yung tix yieh mu;
kawn koe cak,
pex,
kiah,
mai ia sim khankix heue.
24 Saw-law-meung kuang ka haktiex plak klip singaix ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti khawm uik,
jauh ka Thi-fu-sax pa koe ka Yo-fa-la-ti tom hoek ka veng Cia-sa plak klip singaix ka daog.
Can Vawng pa ot plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti nghiet ka nawh khawm uik;
nawh kawx yuh krax mhawm mai meung paoh an.
25 Yam kawn ot nawh ka piang haktiex,
pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoik pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
pui ku nyiex jauh ka haktiex Tang plak laong,
tom hoek ka haktiex Piie-see-pa plak seh,
hoik tang koe ka khaox plix siiet mai ka khaox ruh cie tix heue.
26 bSaw-law-meung koe brung pa reu ling pon mheen mu,
mai pui naing brug brung tix mheen ra rheng mu.
27 Cao kuang kaing haktiex nawh kao ra kaux,
hoik tang hawt tix tawx priex preem pa sang lo simiang Saw-law-meung mai pui daux nyiex haw simiang ku khix,
mai ang sax kieh cud tix bawg kawx.
28 Cao kuang haktiex ku kaux,
hoik tawx nang nghox ca pa lang mu mai brex nghox pa hoik tawx pui daux haktiex kix ka haktiex dee pa sang lo khankix,
son sang eei brung naing kah,
mai ka khawng brie pa yuh kaing khankix heue.
29 Siyiex tawx Saw-law-meung koe ping nyia cuyi pa ang kawn pui sax koe,
nawh koe siawp sibrawm tix pon tawng rhawm keud pui,
mai koe riang rao tix pon guah ka kaing pa ting pa lhaong.
30 Ping nyia cuyi Saw-law-meung kawn lheu khaing ping nyia cuyi pui pa ot plak lih singaix mai pui Ai-cix.
31 Nawh koe ping nyia cuyi khaing pui kaing mheen,
nawh pon pex ka pui Yi-see-la pa mawh Yi-thang,
kawn simex Ma-heu pa kaux Si-mang,
Cia-keux,
mai Ta-tax khankix.
Ceu kaux nawh hoik tauh hu ka meung haktiex khawm pon daog.
32 cNawh tiiem ka lox kab loe rheng gum mai lox rhax tix rheng phuan mu.
33 Nawh krai lao khaox ox rib riem,
jauh ka khaox si oe bhawng pa koe haktiex Li-pa-noeng tom bleeh ka rao rib im krim pa kuah piang guh ding,
mai baox krai ka sim saik ku ceu,
mai to sat,
viag vua,
mai kax khankix.
34 Simiang meung haktiex khawm uik hoik mhong grawng pa koe ping nyia cuyi nawh,
mai hoik ploi pui hu nghiet ka lox ping nyia cuyi pa krai nawh heue.
