Phek tiiem ka kawn yung pa grai
(Lai Ma-thaix 18.12–14)
15
aYam an koe tix bawg,
pui koh keen tom nie mai pui caox khankix hoik nghiet ka lox pa sidiex Ye-sux khawm uik.
2 Pui Fa-li-saix mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix salao ah nin:
“Pui in kawn rieb ka pui caox,
mai baox sawm tau mai kix!”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux tom krai kix mhong phek tiiem tix mu:
4 “Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux koe yung tix yieh mu ka daux pex,
ka daux kix hoik grai tix mu,
sang caw tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh tee rien tix prah ka an kix tix dim dim mu ot ka daux bang pak rib,
mai hu sawg ka pa grai an,
tom hoik yaox tix kah.
5 Yam hoik yaox nawh kah,
nawh tag sang gaux rhawm mai klien kawn yung an ot ka piang klip tix,
6 mai viex nawh ing ka nyiex,
khaix an,
ghaok paox grawm mai paox plawg paox nyawm tix hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
‘Hoek tix yuh krax gaux rhawm tau mai aux heue,
kawn yung cie aux pa grai an hoik yaox ka heue!’
7 Awm an,
aux krai ka pex,
phan pui phi map tix kaux kaux hoik loh rhawm rhi tix,
krax gaux rhawm ka daux meung rao ma kawn lheu ting khaing krax gaux rhawm pui rauh pui see an kix tix dim dim kaux,
pa ang siao lai loh ka rhawm rhi tix khankix lie!”
Phek tiiem ka mau pa grai
8 “Phan bun tix kaux kaux koe mau kao plah,
mai hoik grai tix plah,
sang caw tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh teei rien tix tok ngu riang,
pih ka daux nyiex singax,
mai bre rhawm tix sawg ku dauh ku dee,
tom hoik yaox kah.
9 Yam hoik yaox kah,
nawh tag sang ghaok paox grawm mai paox plawg paox nyawm tix hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
‘Hoek tix yuh krax gaux rhawm tau mai aux,
mau pa hoik cawd grai aux an hoik yaox ka heue!’
10 Awm an,
aux krai ka pex,
kawn cau Siyiex kawx tag sang gaux rhawm kheu hoik loh pui phi map rhawm rhi tix heue.”
Phek tiiem ka keeing pa koe krax caox rhawm
11 Ye-sux nied tix ah nin:
“Pui tix kaux koe kawn simex ra kaux.
12 Pa mawh kawn simex pa iak an ah nin ka keeing tix:
‘Keeing,
yam in maix guah tix tawx sin ngao pa khaw aux tix pon ka aux heue.’
Keeing nawh tom guah tix tawx sin ngao ka kawn simex tix khawm ra kaux.
13 Khaix hoik luan ra loe singaix,
kawn simex pa iak an coh ka sin ngao pa hoik guah nawh pon,
mai viex mau lih hu khaing nyiex.
Nawh hu ka haktiex pa koe dee singai tix dauh,
nawh ot dee an vuk vied vuk viang mai ang chix priex ka rhawm tix.
14 Yam hoik uik cai nawh ka mau pa viex tix khawm uik,
sibhawm praix ting lih ka haktiex dee an,
nawh tom ang lai koe ceu pa tid tix,
15 mai hu deuh rhawm tix ka pui haktiex an tix kaux;
pui an tawx nawh hu mawh cao pa eei lig ka bang dee eei lig.
16 Nawh kied simie tix teei ih ka ceem pa ih lig kheu hoik cai rhawm tix;
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa tawx nawh ih ka ceu pa ih.
17 Dee si dauh,
nawh doh keud ah nin:
‘Ka daux nyiex keeing aux hoik koe pui cang tix yuh kaing tom nie,
priex preem hoik haoh hoik kub son kix,
yuh ka mawx aux lai tieh tix cai rhawm dex sang yum dee in lie?
18 Aux tag sang kaoh,
deeh ing ka keeing tix dee an,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
keeing,
aux hoik phit lud ka rao ma,
mai hoik phit lud ka maix.
19 Aux ang lai keung tix mawh kawn simex maix;
kieh aux mawh pui cang pa yuh kaing maix liak!’
20 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh yaog tix deeh ing ka keeing tix dee an heue.
“Yam kawn singai ot nawh,
keeing nawh hoik yaox ka nawh,
mai kied koe krax caox rhawm caox rhi ka nawh,
to tix hu plak sivoe ngai rieb kawm ka nawh,
ngheet ka nawh.
21 Kawn simex nawh ah nin:
‘Keeing,
aux hoik phit lud ka rao ma,
hoik phit lud ka maix;
aux ang lai keung tix mawh kawn simex maix heue.’
22 Viang mawh nan,
keeing nawh tawx lox ka siliex tix ah nin:
‘Nied hu teei kraung ceep pa mhawm tawx nawh ceep,
teei jeep giien tawx nawh ceep,
teei kiiep tawx nawh ceep,
23 tuk kawn moi pa mhawm kluing an hoek pauh kah,
mai kieh ex yuh krax pa gaux rhawm heue!
24 Mawh kheu kawn simex aux in hoik yum mai deeh kaoh im,
hoik grai mai deeh pon yaox heue.’
Mawh kheu nan,
pui hun hoik gaux rhawm khawm uik heue.
25 “Yam an,
kawn ai kawn ot ka daux ma.
Yam deeh ing nawh,
ang lai singai khaing nyiex miex glang tix,
nawh mhong lox rhax mai lox graoh.
26 Nawh kok siliex tix kaux hoek,
mai chawk mawh kaing ceu pa tix.
27 Siliex pawk ah nin:
‘Pux aik maix deeh hoek,
keeing maix yaox hoek nawh khawm ang saux ang yawm,
nawh tom pauh kawn moi pa mhawm kluing tix mu heue.’
28 Kawn simex pa ting kied song rhawm kah,
mai ang cu tix laig daux nyiex;
keeing nawh lih si kah mai nawh.
29 Viang mawh nan,
nawh krai ka keeing tix ah nin:
‘Maix jhak,
tom nie num aux hoik awm siliex cawng yuh nan yuh kaing son maix,
ang sax lieh lheu ka gum lox tawm maix,
hoik tawx maix aux cie ceu pa tix lie?
Pie kawn pex tix mu khaing,
son sang kieh aux paox grawm tix gaux rhawm maix ang tawx heue!’ ”
30 “Viang mawh nan,
kawn simex maix in,
nawh hoik uik cai sin ngao maix ka daux bun pa coh kaux,
yam in nawh deeh hoek,
maix hoik pauh kawn moi pa mhawm kluing son nawh heue!
31 Keeing nawh krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Kawn eue,
maix hoik ot tau mai aux jheeng jheeng.
Can pa koe aux khawm uik hoik mawh cie maix.
32 Viang mawh nan,
pux aik maix in hoik yum mai deeh kaoh im,
hoik grai mai deeh pon yaox,
ex yuh krax gaux rhawm son nawh hoik mawh pa khaw heue.’ ”
