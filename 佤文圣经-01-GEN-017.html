Mhai sijax mukheu pa mawh Pat-hak
17
Yam hoik koe ju Ya-paw-lang tix dim dim num,
Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka nawh mai ah nin:
“Aux mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm;
maix caw tix nghiet ka lo aux,
mawh pui rauh see pa mawh pa meh tix kaux.
2 Aux tag yuh sijax mukheu mai maix,
kieh kawn praix maix haoh hun.”
3 Ya-paw-lang grux ngawng tix krub piang tiex;
Siyiex baox deeh ah nin ka nawh:
4 “Aux yuh sijax mukheu mai maix mawh nin:
maix sang mawh keeing ka piang ceu pui ku ceu tom nie.
5 bJauh yam in,
ceu kaux maix ang lai mawh Ya-paw-lang,
sang mawh Ya-paw-la-hang,a
mawh kheu aux hoik ap maix mawh keeing ka ceu pui tom nie.
6 Aux tag tawx kawn praix maix pre haoh hun,
been kaux pui kix hoik kub tix lih mawh meung tix blah,
mai simiang khankix tag lih koe khaing kix heue.”
7 c“Aux tag giex tom kaux ka lox sijax mukheu pa yuh aux mai maix,
mai kawn praix maix tix ju hoik tix ju;
in mawh sijax mukheu leeing ju leeing phan.
Aux sang mawh Siyiex maix mai Siyiex kawn saux kawn sex maix.
8 dAux sang tawx haktiex pa ot maix ka yam in ka maix,
mai doeh ceu cad maix.
Haktiex Cia-nang in sang mawh sin ngao doeh ceu cad maix leeing ju leeing phan;
aux kawx sang mawh Siyiex kix heue.”
9 Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Maix kawx caw tix giex sijax mukheu aux kaux cong;
maix mai kawn praix maix tix ju hoik tix ju caw tix giex kaux cong.
10 eKa daux pex pui simex ku kaux caw tix Pat-hak;
in mawh lox sijax mukheu pa yuh aux mai maix,
mai kawn praix maix tix ju hoik tix ju,
pex caw tix hawt mhawm.
11-12 Jauh yam in,
pex can pa koe kawn nyawm simex,
khawm siliex cawng pa hoik liag pex khaing pui tang meung mai pa keh ka daux nyiex pex,
khaix hoik keh koe daix singaix,
caw tix Pat-hak ku kaux,
kheu in hoik mawh mhai pa yuh aux mai pex.
13 Kawn simex ka daux nyiex pex ku kaux,
caw tix Pat-hak;
mhai in pa yuh ot ka piang nex nham pex,
hoik mawh sijax mukheu pa yuh aux mai pex leeing ju leeing phan.
14 Can simex pa ang rieb tix Pat-hak,
tag sang teei tix tik ceu kaux khaing ka daux pui cie aux,
mawh kheu ang nawh hawt tix priex ka sijax mukheu pa yuh aux heue.”
15 Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Maix ang pon tix kok moeng tix mawh Sa-lai,
ceu kaux nawh tag sang loh mawh Sa-la.
16 Aux tag sang tawx buan ka piang nawh,
mai kieh nawh keh kawn simex son maix tix kaux.
Aux sang tawx buan ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh mawh miex kawn pui ku meung;
doeh ceu cad nawh tag sang lih mawh simiang heue.”
17 Ya-paw-la-hang grux ngawng tix krub piang tiex mai briex nyiah ah nin daux rhawm tix:
“Pui kuad koe tix yieh num kawn sang chix koe kawn yuh ka mawx lie?
Sa-la hoik naog tix dim num,
nawh kawn sang chix keh kawn yuh ka mawx lie?”
18 Nawh ah nin ka Siyiex:
“Kieh Yi-seu-ma-lix mawh cao pa plud ju aux heue!”
19 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex ah nin:
“Ang!
Moeng maix Sa-laf yuh ka mawx kawn sang keh kawn simex son maix tix kaux,
maix tag sang sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-sha.
Aux tag sang kieh nawh mai kawn saux kawn sex nawh priex tix hawt lox sijax mukheu aux;
in mawh lox sijax mukheu leeing ju leeing phan.
20 Phan mawh Yi-seu-ma-lix,
aux kawx hoik mhong lox kok yo maix.
Aux sang tawx buan ka nawh,
mai kieh kawn praix nawh pre haoh hun tix ju hoik tix ju.
Nawh sang mawh simiang ka piang kix kao ra cub pui;
aux sang kieh doeh ceu cad nawh mawh meung pa ting.
21 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang kieh kawn simex maix Yi-sha priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu aux.
Num pa sang hoek ka daux bawg yam in,
Sa-la tag sang keh Yi-sha heue.”
22 Khaix hoik si kah Siyiex mai Ya-paw-la-hang,
Siyiex tom haok khaing nawh piang rao ma heue.
23 Jauh ka daux singaix an,
Ya-paw-la-hang tom hawt ka gum lox pa tawm Siyiex,
mai tawx kawn simex tix Yi-seu-ma-lix mai kawn simex pa ot daux nyiex nawh uik,
khawm pa keh daux nyiex nawh,
mai siliex cawng pa hoik liag nawh ka mau,
mai kieh kix Pat-hak.
24 Yam yuh Ya-paw-la-hang pa Pat-hak,
ju nawh koe tix dim dim num;
25 yam yuh Yi-seu-ma-lix pa Pat-hak koe ju kao loe num.
26-27 Can kawn simex pa koe ka daux nyiex nawh,
khawm pa keh khaing daux nyiex nawh khankix mai siliex cawng pui tang meung pa hoik liag ka mau,
hoik rom tix Pat-hak tau mai nawh daux tix ngaix khaing heue.
