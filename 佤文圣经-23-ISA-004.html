4
Ka daux singaix an,
bun aliah kaux tag sang ghrang ka simex tix kaux,
mai ah nin:
“Yix chix tix tang ih tang nyaux cie to kaux tix,
mai tang ceep cie to kaux tix,
ca mawh cu maix tix mawh min mex yix.
Yuh nin,
yix tom ang lai koe krax kaig krax tua grawng ang yaox tix koe mex heue.”
Cox sum ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung
2 Bawg yam dex sang hoek,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh khaox plix pa koe ka piang haktiex in ku gawng,
mai can ceu pa kuah ka daux haktiex khawm uik mawh pa ting lhaong pa mhawm grawng.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix tag sang yaw rang di kheu pa tawx haktiex,
mai een in mawh pa koe min.
3 Can pui pa kawn im ju ku kaux,
pa hoik laug Siyiex tix deuh ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tag sang mawh pa singax singeh khawm uik.
4 Simiang Siyiex sang prawng ca meung in ka daux riang rao to kaux tix,
mai kieh kix singax;
nawh tag sang si ghrah ka phi map veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka nham pa jauh dee an.
5 aYam an,
ka piang gawng Shi-ang dee khrawm pui hun paox tix,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang teei pai awm tawb kheem piang kix yam pon ngaix,
tag sang teei rao ngu jhan riang piang kix yam pon sawm.
Min plak ngai Siyiex tag sang tawb kheem mai kia ngien ka piang veng khawm uik;
6 min plak ngai nawh awm tawb nyiex gox bang yuh nan,
mai hoik pon tie pa kied hax pon ngaix,
mai kieh veng in lih mawh dee mox ka kix,
mox tix yam phlu bhaung ting lih lhiex heue.
