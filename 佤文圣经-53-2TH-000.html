Lai Thie-sa-law-ni-cia Phuk Ra
Krai dawt
Grawh grawng pa sang deeh hoek Ye-sux hoik kieh ceu cao Thie-sa-law-ni-cia suk sag ang jud. Pao-law tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka ceu cao Thie-sa-law-ni-cia ka daux ra pheen, krai grawh grawng pa yum rhawm kix, pa een kix singaix bawg yam pa sang deeh hoek Khrit hoik hoek ka kix. Nawh cox dee lud kix, ah nin: sivoe sang deeh hoek Khrit, krax nyaw glaw Khrit tang kaux “pa ang mawh ang meh” an ka daux pa glip, mai phi map ka piang haktiex tag sang ting lhaong heue.
Pao-law sau riang tix sidiex gau ka kawn Khrit, viang si pup ka ceu pa nu tix ang ah, tag caw tix giex krax yum rhawm kaux cong, awm nawh mai paox pa yuh nawh kaing tau yuh nan, hoik tang pon eei tix ka kaing pa yuh tix, khaix an sau riang tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–2)
2. Krax pa yaog yaw mai pa khaw rhawm (1.3–12)
3. Grawh grawng krai pa sang deeh hoek Khrit (2.1–17)
4. Kaing ceu pa caw kawn Khrit tix yuh (3.1–15)
5. Lox pa nghoik tix krai (3.16–18)
