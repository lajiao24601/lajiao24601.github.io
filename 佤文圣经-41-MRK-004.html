Phek tiiem pa rhuat simie
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.1–9; Lai Lu-cia 8.4–8)
4
aYe-sux baox sidiex gau pui ka praok rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix.
Mawh kheu kied si ghox pui hun pa khrawm paox tix ka sivie siveng nawh,
nawh tom laig ngawm ka daux reu.
Reu ot ka daux rawm,
pui hun tom cung ka praok rawm.
2 Ye-sux teei gum lox phek tiiem sidiex gau kix ka tom nie grawng,
mai sidiex gau ka kix ah nin:
3 “Pex bre rhawm tix nghiet heue!
Koe pui pa rhuat simie tix kaux hu rhuat ka simie.
4 Yam rhuat nawh ka simie,
koe simie tix blah cawd ka praok krax,
sim khankix pu hoek kaoh tix ih.
5 Tix blah cawd ka tiag simaox dee thaw tiex,
simie an kied phai kuah,
mawh kheu ang tiex raux,
6 yam lih singaix rat hax ka riah simie an,
mai baox mawh kheu ang bax kub raux,
simie an kroh.
7 Koe tix blah cawd ka daux pang kat,
mai pang kat haok huan,
kheem su shiet ka simie an,
ang pon kieh plix plix.
8 Koe simie tix blah cawd ka dee mhawm tiex,
haok huan ting lhaong,
plix plix tom nie,
mai pon tix khrawm tix ngoe son,
tix gliah son,
tix yieh son heue.”
9 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Pui pa koe yhaok,
tag caw tix nghiet ka khawm uik heue!”
Kaing ting grawng krai ka gum lox phek tiiem
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.10–17; Lai Lu-cia 8.9–10)
10 Yam ot Ye-sux nong nong,
koe pui tix blah pa hoik mhong ka lox pa krai nawh,
hoek ka Ye-sux tau mai kawn cau nawh kao ra kaux,
mai nhie nawh kah dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem in kix.
11 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pa raux pa mox meung Siyiex hoik tawx ka pex;
phan mawh pui haktiex tang dauh,
can pa hoik mhong kix khawm uik tag sang teei gum lox phek tiiem krai kah.
12 bAwm gum lox phuk lai sigang khri pa ah nin:
kix hoik jhak baox jhak,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox,
hoik nghiet baox nghiet,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng;
phan ang mawh nan,
kix hoik loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
Siyiex tag sang pluat ploi ka kix heue.”
Kah dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.18–23; Lai Lu-cia 8.11, 15)
13 Ye-sux baox chawk kix ah nin:
“Ang pex tawng ka gum lox phek tiiem in lie?
Phan mawh nan pex sang chix tawng ka gum lox phek tiiem tang ceu lie?
14 Pui pa rhuat ka simie rhuat ka gum lox Siyiex.
15 Koe pui tix blah awm simie pa cawd ka praok krax yuh nan;
yam mhong kix ka gum lox buan,
Sa tan lih hoek dum mawx dum an,
mai teei ka gum lox buan pa rhuat ot ka daux rhawm rhi kix hu.
16 “Koe pui tix blah awm simie pa cawd ka daux tiag simaox yuh nan.
Yam mhong kix ka gum lox buan dum mawx dum an gaux rhawm tix rieb,
17 viang mawh nan,
kheu ang gum lox buan pa koe daux rhawm nawh raux riah,
ang pon kham tom rheeng,
yam si pup ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi mai krax tuk tiak tuk te kheu son gum lox buan,
dum mawx dum an,
deeh tik vud kah.
18 Baox koe pui tix blah awm simie pa rhuat ka daux pang kat;
kix mhong ka gum lox buan,
19 viang mawh nan,
krax khi rhawm khi rhi ka daux krax ot krax koe,
mai krax nyaw krax glaw sin ngao haktiex,
khawm pa lih alo nex nham ku ceu tix hoek,
hoik yeet yuh ka gum lox buan,
ang pon plix plix.
20 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui tix blah awm simie pa rhuat ka dee mhawm tiex;
kix mhong ka gum lox buan,
mai rieb kah,
khaix an pon plix plix,
mai pon khrawm ka tix ngoe son,
tix gliah son,
tix yieh son heue.”
Kawk ngu pa koe ka greem phliex
(Lai Lu-cia 8.16–18)
21 cYe-sux baox ah nin:
“Koe pui mawx pa tok kawk ngu,
mai deuh ka greem phliex,
phan ang mawh nan ka greem ku lie?
Khex ang nawh deuh ka piang jawk kawk ngu lie?
22 dKaing ceu pa glip khawm uik tag sang krai lih;
kaing ceu pa tawb kheem ku ceu kawx tag sang plih lih.
23 Pui pa koe yhaok kieh nghiet ka heue!”
24 eNawh baox ah nin ka kix:
“Tag caw tix bre rhawm tix nghiet ka heue!
Pex siao ka kraung tong ceu pa tix,
Siyiex kawx tag sang siao ka kraung tong ceu an ka pex nang.
25 fPui pa hoik koe an,
tag sang nie baox tawx kah;
pui pa ang koe,
pie pa hoik koe nawh ceeix ceeix kawx tag sang teei hu heue.”
Phek tiiem ka simie pa ting lhaong
26 Ye-sux baox nied tix ah nin:
“Meung Siyiex awm pui pa rhuat simie tix kaux ka daux haktiex.
27 Nawh it yam pon sawm,
kaoh yam pon singaix,
simie an lih plaung ting huan,
mai nawh ang tawng yuh ka mawx lai mawh nin.
28 Haktiex lak chix kieh khaox ox rib riem kuah,
plix,
ran lih plaung sivoe,
khaix an tawx si gleem lih,
dee ghoik plix lih naog ka piang si gleem.
29 gYam hoik kaux to ma,
nawh teei si vawg khrawm vawg ka dum mawx dum an,
mawh kheu hoik hoek bawg yam pa khrawm ka heue.”
Phek tiiem ka simie sok taux klaox
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.31–32; Lai Lu-cia 13.18–19)
30 Ye-sux chawk ah nin:
“Ex krai meung Siyiex awm ceu pa tix lie?
Ex sang teei gum lox ceu pa tix krai sidawng yuh ka mawx lie?
31 Meung Siyiex awm simie taux klaox tix mu,
pa mawh simie pa iak khaing paox tix ka piang haktiex.
Koe pui sum ka daux tiex,
32 yam hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
nawh ting lhaong kawn lheu ting khaing simie taux lhax ku ceu;
nawh lih tix ting kak,
sim khankix kawx yuh mheem tix ka greem veei nawh heue.”
33 Ye-sux teei gum lox phek tiiem pa awm paox tom nie tawx lox buan ka pui hun,
mai sidiex gau kix awm pa tawng kix yuh nan.
34 Nawh ca teei gum lox phek tiiem krai ka kix;
viang mawh nan,
yam ot nawh tau mai pui gau tix nong nong,
nawh kah pui gau mhong dee bleeh khawm uik heue.
Ye-sux kieh bhaung ting yiien
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.23–27; Lai Lu-cia 8.22–25)
35 Ka daux bo singaix an,
Ye-sux ah nin ka pui gau tix:
“Ex tiang rawm nhawng plak an heue.”
36 Mawh kheu nan,
kix lih hu khaing pui hun.
Ye-sux hoik laig krox kix ka daux reu,
pui gau nawh laig ka daux reu tom vie nawh hu;
mai hu tau mai reu cie pui tang mu.
37 Bhaung ting phlu hoek ka piang rawm,
rawm chun tix puk pak,
puk pak rawm laig daux reu,
reu dex sang naog ka rawm.
38 Yam an Ye-sux ot ka kre reu,
mai neung kaing tix it piang gaong.
Kix so ka nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
ex dex sang yum heue,
maix ang lai khi ka lie?”
39 Ye-sux kaoh,
mai tawx lox ka bhaung:
“Yien yawg heue!”
Baox tawx lox ka puk pak rawm ah nin:
“Kaog heue!”
Bhaung tom kaog,
rawm nhawng tom yien yawg.
40 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx lhat si yaung,
ang pex koe krax yum rhawm lie?”
41 Kix kied lhat graung mai ra lai ah nin:
“Pui in mawh pui mawx,
pie bhaung mai puk pak rawm kawx hoik nghiet ka lox nawh heue!”
