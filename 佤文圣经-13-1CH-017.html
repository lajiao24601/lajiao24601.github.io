Na-tang tawx lox ka Ta-wue
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 7.1–17)
17
Simiang Ta-wue ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang.
Koe tix ngaix,
nawh ploi pui hu kok cao jhai Na-tang hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix jhak,
aux ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang pa hoik sum ka khaox si oe bhawng,
teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex hoik tieh tix ot ka daux nyiex gox bang heue!”
2 Na-tang ah nin:
“Maix simie tix yuh ceu tix yuh heue!
Mawh kheu ot Siyiex tau mai maix.”
3 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux bo an Siyiex ah nin ka Na-tang:
4 “Hu krai ka siliex aux Ta-wue,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix ang pon tix sum nyiex buan tawx aux ot daux.
5 Jauh yam taoh aux pui Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing Ai-cix hoek yam in,
aux ang sax ot ka nyiex buan;
aux ot ka daux nyiex gox bang ang jud ang jhai,
tom gah tix ot ku dauh.
6 Singaix num hoik vung vied vung viang aux tau mai kawn pui Yi-seu-liex sivoe an,
aux ang sax chawk cheng ka simiang mawx tix pa hoik kok aux liang ka pui cie tix,
yuh ka mawx lai ang sum nyiex buan pa yuh ka khaox si oe bhawng tang aux.’
7 “Mawh kheu nan,
maix caw tix krai ka Ta-wue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing ah mawh nin:
‘Maix liang yung ka daux tiag,
mai aux hoik kok ka maix hoek,
mai tawx maix mawh simiang pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
8 Maix hu dee mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang ot tau mai maix.
Aux hoik pon pex ka pui pa koeng maix ka khawm uik.
Aux tag sang kieh maix sidu min mai pui pa kied ting min ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
9-10 “ ‘Aux hoik laug haktiex tix dauh son pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx kix ot dee an,
ang lai kieh kix yaox krax pa tuk tiak tuk te.
Jauh tom yam laig kix ka daux haktiex in,
pui tud pui rhim hoik puing ka kix ang jud ang jai,
viang mawh nan,
hu sivoe ngaix,
kaing in kix ang lai baox lih tix koe.
Aux hoik ap tix puing pex ka can pui pa koeng ka maix khawm uik,
khaix an tag sang tawx maix koe doeh ceu cad khankix.
11 Yam hoik yum maix,
tag sang si paung maix ot tau mai tax du tix,
mai aux tag yaog kawn simex maix mawh simiang,
mai kieh meung nawh haok huan ting lhaong.
12 Nawh tag sang mawh pui pa sum nyiex buan son aux;
aux tag sang kieh meung pa giex nawh kaing yaox tix koe leeing ju leeing phan.
13 aAux tag sang mawh keeing nawh;
nawh tag sang mawh kawn simex aux.
Aux ang chix deeh teei ka krax pa muh tix ka nawh,
awm hoik yuh aux ka Sao-law sivoe an yuh nan,
tik ka nawh mai yaog maix mawh simiang.
14 Aux tag sang kieh nawh kuang kia ka pui cie aux mai meung cie aux leeing ju leeing phan;
meung pa giex nawh kaing tag koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.’ ”
15 Na-tang hoik krai kaing pa jhi Siyiex ka nawh ku ceu ka Ta-wue heue.
Lox rawg buan krax yaog yaw Ta-wue
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 7.18–29)
16 Simiang Ta-wue laig ka daux nyiex buan man,
hoek ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai ngawm tix rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
can pa hoik yuh maix son aux khawm uik,
aux ang keung tix pon,
mai ceu pui nyiex ma aux kawx ang keung.
17 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawx ka aux tom nie,
pie ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
maix hoik ap tix tawx buan ka kawn praix aux.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik chix kia ngien aux awm mawh aux pui pa ting ceu yuh nan.
18 Aux kawn sang koe lox krai ceu pa tix lie?
Maix tawng ka siliex maix,
mai maix kawn kieh aux pon min plak ngai.
19 Maix yuh nin ka aux,
khaix an mai jhi aux yaox grawng pa sang haok huan aux singaix pa sang hoek,
mai in kix hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo maix khawm uik.
20 Simiang Siyiex eue,
pui tix kaux kawx ang pon tiag tix tau mai maix;
yix hoik dawg tawng,
lheu khaing maix,
mai ang lai koe siyiex tang kaux.
21 “Ka piang haktiex ang koe meung tix meung kawx pa awm Yi-seu-liex;
maix hoik taoh pui Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing yuh tix siliex cawng,
mai kieh kix mawh pui cie tix.
Maix hoik yuh kaing pa ting daox deng son kix,
mai kieh ceu kaux tix tauh hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
Maix hoik taoh pui cie tix luat luan khaing Ai-cix;
yam greed kix tix laig,
maix hoik grheu ka pui tang ceu tang cad khankix hu.
22 Maix hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex mawh pui cie tix leeing ju,
maix pa mawh Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh Siyiex kix heue.
23 “Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh gum lox pa hoik ap maix tix deuh pa dex dawt mai aux,
khawm doeh ceu cad aux lih tix mawh,
hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik krai maix khankix.
24 Kieh gum lox pa hoik ap maix ang chix tieh loh tix leeing ju.
Kieh pui yung yi ka ceu kaux maix leeing ju.
Kieh kawn pui yaog yaw leeing ju:
‘Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
hoik mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex!’
Maix giex kaux cong ka meung pa hoik giex aux kaing ku ju leeing ju.
25 Siyiex eue,
aux viex rhawm ting rawg buan yuh nin ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
mawh kheu hoik jhi maix ku ceu khawm uik ka aux,
pa mawh siliex maix,
mai krai aux mhong grawng sang yaog maix doeh ceu cad aux mawh simiang.
26 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing;
maix hoik tawx gum lox pa daox deng pa hoik ap tix ka aux.
27 Aux khiox khaw maix tawx buan son ka doeh ceu cad aux,
mai kieh kix ca pon buan son maix ang jud ang jai.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawx buan son ka kix;
kieh maix kia ngien ka kix leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
