Veng Ni-ni-we laux leeing
2
Pui Ni-ni-we eue,
riang rao sibrawm pa tag sang pauh maix phawng hoik hoek heue.
Sau riang tix tau,
mai pau ka tab heue!
Pau daux krax tom nyawm nyiem,
mai rien khre tix puing naing heue!
(
2 Simiang Siyiex tag sang deeh tawx min plak ngai Yi-seu-liex,
mai deeh kieh nawh yaox grawng pa tud rhim pui koeng sivoe an.)
3 Pui koeng viex ka dieb tie naok pa raoh,
mai ceep ka kraung raoh;
kix rien tix sang puing ka naing.
Ling law naing kix hoik toh riang awm ngu yuh nan;
brung nainga kix brax to heue.
 
4 Ling law naing yia ka daux laih,
mai hoik nieb pon laig hu ka daux vang.
Kix hoik toh riang awm ngu proh yuh nan,
awm tuh sah yuh nan.
5 Pui kok simiang naing kix hoek;
kix si yaung si kua mai hoek tix heue.
 
Pui naing preeng hu ka guh veng,
mai tie tix ka dieb tie naok pa vieh.
6 Guh ding siviex rawm bih;
nyiex haw simiang ghrup.
7 Pui giex moeng simiang hu;
bun daux nyiex haw simiang khankix yiiem awm si kaux yuh nan,
pui ku kaux dieh naog tix yiiem.
8 Pui hun preeng tix lih khaing veng Ni-ni-we,
awm rawm pa yieh laux ka guh yuh nan.
Kix kok ah nin:
kaog heue!
Kaog heue!
Viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa deeh heue.
 
9 Tud rhim ka mau heue!
Tud rhim ka khri heue!
Sin ngao pa mox ot daux veng heue!
Ku dauh ku dee hoik mawh kraung pa ngawh heue!
 
10 Pui Ni-ni-we hoik laux leeing,
mai hoik mawh krax laux rhawm khawm uik heue!
Rhawm rhi kawn pui hoik ceh kheu krax lhat krax graung;
ngawng hoik si yaung si kua khawm ra khawn,
mai riang hoik uik;
pak ngai hoik paing yuh nan heue.
 
11 Yam in veng ting in ot dee mawx lie?
An awm daux dawh sivai sang si,
mai dee eei ka kawn sivai sang si ot dee mawx lie?
Sivai sang si mhaing mai sivai sang si miex laig laig lih lih,
dee mox kawn sivai sang si tix ot dee mawx lie?
12 Sivai sang si mhaing kiied to tiag yum,
mai riah lih mawh khik tawx miex sivai sang si mai kawn ih;
ka daux dawh hoik pum nex im pa riah phawng naog ka heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui pa koeng maix kah;
aux tag sang yuh ling law naing maix laux leeing.
Pui naing maix tag sang yum;
aux tag sang teei ka ceu pa hoik tud rhim maix khaing pui tang kaux dee an.
Gum lox pa krai pui cau maix kawx ang lai deeh baox mhong ka heue.”
