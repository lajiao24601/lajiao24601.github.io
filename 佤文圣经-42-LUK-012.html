Rien tix ka pui pa pie lhien
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.26–27)
12
aYam an,
koe pui kaing rheng kaing mheen si ghox ka paox tix,
mai ra lai dug dig paox tix.
Ye-sux ran krai ka pui gau tix sivoe ah nin:
“Pex tag caw rien tix ka su saix cie pui Fa-li-saix,
pa mawh krax pa pie lhien kix.
2 bKaing ceu pa glip khawm uik tag sang plih lih;
kaing pa mox kawx tag sang plih lih.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
lox pa hoik krai pex dee glip,
pui tag sang mhong yam riang praix;
lox pa su shiep pex mai yhaok paox tix ka daux lawg pa glip kawx,
tag sang krai pao lih khaing braux nyiex heue.”
Paw vi lhat
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.28–31)
4 “Paox grawm khankix,
aux krai ka pex,
an pa pon pauh ngie to kaux nex nham pex,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon hoek tix yuh pex laux,
paw vi lhat kah.
5 Aux tag sang krai ka pa khaw pex tix lhat ka mawh mawx:
pex tag caw tix lhat ka Siyiex kaw pa teei ju pui hu,
khaix an mai baox koe akhuang tix taing pex laig ka daux Maw-na-rhok.
Mawh tete,
aux krai ka pex,
pa khaw pex tix lhat ka mawh nawh heue!
6 “Saik phuan mu teei mau tawng ra plak hoik pon liag kah.
Viang mawh nan,
pie tix mu kawx Siyiex ang piawm;
7 pie khawm hauk kaing pex kawx Siyiex hoik simin ka khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
pex paw vi lhat,
pex kawn lheu ting ngawh khaing sim saik tom nie heue!”
Krai pao tawng ka Khrit
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.32–33; 12.32; 10.19–20)
8 “Aux krai ka pex,
can pui pa krai tawng yung aux ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
kawn kawn pui kawx tag sang krai tawng nawh ka plak sivoe ngai kawn cau Siyiex.
9 Can pui pa krai ang tawng aux ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
kawn kawn pui kawx krai ang tawng nawh ka plak sivoe ngai kawn cau Siyiex heue.
10 c“Pa lud lox ka kawn kawn pui,
kawn chix tix pluat ploi;
mawh ngie pa map nai ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
ang chix pon tix pluat ploi heue.
11 d“Yam viex pui pex laig daux nyiex sidiex,
phan ang mawh nan viex tix hoek chawk cheng pex ka plak sivoe ngai simiang naing mai simiang khankix,
pex paw vi khi rhawm ka son lox pa sang pawk tix,
phan ang mawh nan ka lox pa sang krai tix yuh ka mawx.
12 Mawh kheu bawg yam an,
Khuan Singax Singeh tag sang krai lox pa sang khaw pex tix krai heue.”
Cao mi pa daox pa aw
13 Ka daux pui hun koe pui tix kaux krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
tawx lox nhie pux aik aux guah sin ngao keeing aux tau mai aux heue.”
14 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Paox grawm,
pui mawx nhie aux mawh cao pa prawng see son pex,
phan ang mawh nan,
mawh cao guah sin ngao son pex lie?”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh nied tix krai ka pui hun ah nin:
“Pex tag caw rien rhawm rhi tix,
mai mox tix khaing krax pa ghoing rhawm ku ceu;
mawh kheu pui tix kaux viang mag mi yuh ka mawx,
ka daux ju nawh ang ot ka daux sin ngao pa koe nawh miex ding heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux krai lox phek tiiem ka kix tix ceu ah nin:
“Koe cao mi tix kaux,
sin ngao nghox koi nawh kied nie;
17 rhawm rhi nawh keud ah nin:
‘Aux ang koe deeh deuh tom nie ka can nghox koi pa koe tix khawm uik,
sang caw tix yuh ka mawx lie?’
18 Nawh tang deeh ah nin ka to kaux tix:
‘Mawh tete,
aux tag sang yieh ka krao pa koe tix sivoe an,
mai cox sum blawn ting,
son sang deuh nghox koi mai kraung khrai tang ceu ot kah.
19 Khaix an,
aux tag sang krai ka to kaux tix ah nin,
pui pa mhawm son eue,
pa koe maix ka ceu pa mhawm khawm uik,
hoik kub maix siao tom nie num,
maix koi ih koi nyaux mhawm mhawm,
ka daux krax ot krax koe pa nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi heue!’
20 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex tag sang ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix pa mawh pui pa daox pa aw in,
ka daux bo sawm in,
maix tag caw tix tawx ka ju tix;
phan mawh nan,
sin ngao pa hoik khrawm maix tix deuh son to kaux tix khawm uik,
tag sang tawx mawh cie pui mawx lie?’ ”
21 Ye-sux nghoik tix krai ah nin:
“Pui pa khrawm tix deuh ka sin ngao son tix,
viang mawh nan,
ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
pa mawh pui ang mi ang koe kawx hoik yuh nan nang heue.”
Deuh rhawm tix ka Siyiex
(Lai Ma-thaix 6.25–34)
22 Ye-sux baox krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
paw vi khi rhawm son priex preem pa sang lo pex,
phan ang mawh nan,
son kraung ceep to kaux pex.
23 Ju kawn pui lheu ting ngawh khaing priex preem tom nie;
to kaux kawx kawn lheu ting ngawh khaing kraung ceep tom nie.
24 Jhak tix yaox ka lak khankix heue.
Kix ang sum ang vawg,
ang koe nyiex ang koe krao,
Siyiex kawn eei ka kix,
pex kawn lheu ting ngawh khaing kawn sim tom nie heue!
25 Ka daux pex koe pui mawx pa pon klawd ju miex ngaixe ka daux krax khi rhawm khi rhi liex?
26 Phan pex ang pon yuh ka kaing pa iak yuh nin,
yuh ka mawx lai khi rhawm ka son kaing tang ceu khankix lie?
27 fJhak tix yaox ka tai siyag hoik kuah yuh ka mawx heue!
Kix ang yuh kaing mai baox ang caing kraung;
viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
pie kraung ceep simiang Saw-law-meung kawx,
kawn ang nyang bax lheu mhawm khaing lawm tai pa koe daux tiag daux tu tix kho yuh nan heue.
28 Tai rib pa koe daux tiag daux tu an lih pruh ka daux in singaix,
pa sax kroh vhit,
mai taing tik gleeing ka daux glah ngu,
Siyiex kawn jawg kix mhawm yuh nan,
nawh khex ang nie blawn tawx kraung ceep ka pex lie?
Krax yum rhawm pex kied iak heue!
29 “Mawh kheu nan,
pex paw vi khi rhawm son ceu pa ih tix,
ceu pa sang nyaux tix son ceu in kix.
30 Ceu in kix hoik mawh pa sawg pui pa ang koe krax yum rhawm ka piang haktiex.
Keeing pex Siyiex hoik tawng ka ceu pa sang lo pex khawm uik
31 Pex tag caw tix sawg ka ceu pa lih siawp sibrawm Siyiex tix mawh,
nawh tom tag tawx ceu in kix khawm uik ka pex heue.”
Krai grawng sin ngao ka piang rao ma
(Lai Ma-thaix 6.19–21)
32 “Pex pa mawh cub pui pa iak pa lied in kix,
paw vi lhat,
mawh kheu keeing pex hoik gaux rhawm tix tawx meung cie nawh ka pex.
33 Tag caw tix coh ka can pa koe pex khawm uik,
mai teei mau tix teem coi pui hot pui sibhawm;
tag caw tix rien khre ka hau mau pa ang chix laux,
mai teei sin ngao tix deuh ot ka piang rao ma.
Ka dee an hoik mawh dee ang chix uik siao ka sin ngao;
mawh kheu dee an pui briex ang pon hoek tix briex,
greeing kawx ang pon lhawn kah.
34 Sin ngao pex koe dee mawx,
rhawm pex kawx koe dee an heue.”
Siliex pa rien rhawm tix
35 g“Pex tag caw tix rien khre tix mhawm mhawm,
mad kaux ka maox nyoi tix,
mai tok riang ka kawk ngu,
36 hawm krox jun siliex simiang tix hoek ka bang poi ka paox yuh nan.
Yam hoek simiang tix gleuh ka siviex,
kix tag paoh siviex son nawh dum mawx dum an.
37 Simiang hoek,
yaox ka siliex tix khankix pa rien rhawm tix,
mai kix tom pon buan son heue!
Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
simiang tag sang mad ka maox nyoi tix,
kieh kix ngawm tix sawm rheep,
mai khan kheu ka kix.
38 Phan ang mawh nan,
simiang lha hoek bawg yam greung sawm,
phan ang mawh nan tag hoek yam ba riang praix,
phan nawh yaox siliex tix rien rhawm tix,
kix tag sang pon buan son heue!
39 iTag caw priex tix tawng:
cao nyiex tix nyiex phan tawng pui briex sang hoek bawg yam mawx,
nawh tee rien rhawm tix ang kieh pui briex yieh tix laig briex daux nyiex.
40 Pex caw rien khre tix ku bawg ku yam,
mawh kheu kawn kawn pui tag sang lih hoek ka daux bawg yam ang pex tawng dum mawx dum an heue.”
Siliex pa kaux sijax mai pa ang kaux sijax
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.45–51)
41 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Simiang,
gum lox phek tiiem in mawh krai maix ka son yix,
mawh krai maix ka son pui hun lie?”
42 Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pui mawx mawh cao kuang nyiex pa kaux sijax mai pa lhak lhiao lie?
Tag sang mawh siliex pa tawx simiang nawh hoek tix kuang ka nyiex tix,
pa hawt tix tawx priex preem ka siliex tang kaux awm bawg yam yuh nan.
43 Yam hoek simiang nawh,
yaox grawng pa hoik kaux sijax siliex in;
siliex in hoik mawh pa kied koe buan son heue!
44 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
simiang nawh tag sang tawx nawh kuang ka can sin ngao pa koe tix khawm uik.
45 Viang mawh nan,
phan siliex in sawn ka daux rhawm tix,
‘simiang aux tag ang nyang khran hoek’ mawh kheu nan,
lauh ka siliex tang kaux mai bun siliex,
khaix an ih mai nyaux tom yoek,
46 simiang nawh tag deeh hoek bawg yam ang nawh tawng,
yam ang nawh keud.
Simiang nawh tag sang lauh nawh tom nyawm nyiemj,
mai kieh nawh kham krax ciyang tau mai siliex pa ang kaux sijax khankix heue.
47 “Siliex hoik tawng pa sang nhie simiang nawh tix yuh,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang rien khre tix,
mai ang hawt nang tix yuh ka lox pa hoik nhie simiang nawh tix yuh;
yuh nin,
nawh tag sang kham krax pa si vied nie.
48 Phan mawh siliex an pa khaw tix si vied pa hoik yuh ka daux pa ang tix tawng ka lox simiang tix,
tag sang kham krax pa si vied pa chaong.
Siyiex hoik tawx nie ka pui mawx,
tag sang teei nie ka pui an;
nhie tawx lox ka pui mawx,
tag sang nhie pui an yuh tom nie nang heue.”
Grawh grawng pa guah paox tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.34–36)
49 “Aux hoek ka piang haktiex mawh son sang tok ngu,
aux kied simie ngu an cap heue!
50 kAux caw tix kham krax cub com pa kha rhawm tix.
Sivoe sang yaox aux krax pa kha rhawm in,
rhawm rhi aux kied suk sag heue!
51 Pex paw vi ce aux viex pa nhim hoek ka piang haktiex.
Aux krai ka pex,
aux ang viex pa nhim hoek,
sang viex krax pung prang hoek.
52 Jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai,
nyiex ma pa koe phuan kaux tag sang pung prang tang hu,
pui loe kaux simah ka pui ra kaux,
pui ra kaux lauh ka pui loe kaux.
53 lKix tag sang simah ka paox tix yuh nin:
keeing simah mai kawn simex,
kawn simex simah mai keeing;
miex simah mai kawn bun,
kawn bun simah mai miex;
miex simex simah mai mun tix,
mun simah mai miex mex tix heue.”
Chix tawng ka mhai sakse bawg yam
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.2–3)
54 Ye-sux baox krai ka pui hun ah nin:
“Yam yaox pex koe lon pai awm ka plak klip singaix,
dum mawx dum an,
pex ah ‘lhiex hoik dex sang lih’,
in mawh tete.
55 Yam phlu bhaung plak seh tix hoek,
pex ah ‘praix klawng tag sang si aoh’,
in rug mawh tete.
56 Pex pa mawh pui pa pie lhien khankix eue,
pex hoik kied chix jhak ka praix klawng,
yuh ka mawx ang chix tawng ka baux singaix ju in lie?”
Yuh krax pa mhawm mai pui pa yuh krax pa tieb tie
(Lai Ma-thaix 5.25–26)
57 “Yuh ka mawx to kaux pex lai ang prawng ka kaing ceu tix mawh pa khaw caw lie?
58 Phan koe pui pa deuh phi map ka maix,
tag sang bluh maix laig ka daux nyiex prawng see,
maix tag caw sau riang tix kieh to kaux tix mhawm tau mai nawh yam kawn ot lawng krax,
son ang nawh sang tawx maix ka cao prawng see,
cao prawng see tawx maix ka cao kuang thawng,
cao kuang thawng laung maix ka daux thawng.
59 Aux krai ka maix,
lheu khaing krox jun maix tawx ka mau se tix piak,
maix ang pon lih khaing daux thawng heue.”
