Lox rhax krax taoh
14
aAux baox jhak kah,
aux yaox kawn yung cung ka piang gawng Shi-ang,
pui pa ot tau mai nawh koe kao pon mheen pon rheng kaux;
ka piang dong re kix hoik tiiem ka ceu kaux nawh mai ka ceu kaux keeing nawh.
2 Aux mhong lox pa lih khaing rao ma,
awm lox luah rawm pa lih khaing rang yuh nan,
mai awm lox si num praix yuh nan nang.
Lox pa hoik mhong aux in baox awm lox tauh sara ting jung lah yuh nan nang.
3 Kix cung ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung mai to sat pa im ju an kix pon mu,
khawm kuad song khankix,
rhax lai rhax khraox tix ceu;
lai rhax in mawh ngie pui pa hoik pon krax taoh kao pon mheen pon rheng kaux pa chix rhax khaing.
4 Pui in kix hoik mawh simex pa ang bax dex dawt tau mai sibun khawm uik.
Phan kawn yung hoek dee mawx,
kix taug hoek dee an.
Kix mawh pa taoh lih khaing daux kawn pui,
mawh pa ran tix teei hoek tan lu ka Siyiex mai ka kawn yung sivoe.
5 bKix ang sax pie lhien,
ang koe dee seeing brai heue.
Kawn cau Siyiex loe kaux
6 Aux baox yaox kawn cau Siyiex tang kaux tix kaux pa pu daux rao ma;
nawh viex gum lox buan pa koe leeing ju,
tag sang krai pao ka meung ku meung,
pui ku cub,
pui ku ceu,
mai lox si kah ku ceu ka piang haktiex.
7 Nawh ah nin tom ting:
“Tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
yaog yaw ka pa ting lhaong nawh heue!
Mawh kheu bawg yam pa prawng ca nawh ka kawn pui hoik hoek.
Tag caw tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex cao pa phan pa yuh rao ma,
haktiex,
rawm sigrit sigra,
mai ka rawm keet khawm uik heue!”
8 Kawn cau Siyiex ka daux ra kaux tix kaux baox ah nin:
“Hoik ghrup heue!
Pa-pi-loeng pa ting hoik ghrup heue!
Nawh teei plai khien pa phit vao tawx kawn pui piang haktiex nyaux heue.”
9 Kawn cau Siyiex ka daux loe kaux tix kaux ah lox pa ting tau mai kawn cau pa lih sivoe ra kaux ah nin:
“Pui pa krub ka to sat an mai ka riang to sat an,
pa hoik tawk cum tix ot ka dong re mai tawm taix,
10 ctag caw tix nyaux ka plai khien cie Siyiex.
Plai in mawh pa ang bax yuh ceet,
mai hoik prauk ka daux kawk pa song rhawm cie nawh.
Kix tag sang kham krax pa kha rhawm ngu mai mhat ka plak sivoe ngai kawn cau Siyiex pa singax singeh,
mai ka plak sivoe ngai kawn yung.
11 dTaox ngu pa kieh kix kha rhawm tix,
chu tix lih ang jud ang jai leeing ju.
Pa krub ka to sat mai ka riang to sat an kix,
khawm pa koe cum ceu kaux nawh ka piang dong re khankix,
ang yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe leeing ngaix leeing sawm heue.”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
pui cie Siyiex pa nghiet ka gum lox tawx Siyiex,
pa kaux sijax ka Ye-sux an kix,
tag caw tix koe rhawm gun rhawm yeung heue.
13 Aux baox mhong lox pa kaoh khaing rao ma ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix tiiem gum lox in lih yuh nin:
jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai,
pui pa yum son Simiang Siyiex ting buan son heue!”
Khuan singax pawk ah nin:
“Mawh tete heue!
Kix tag sang naox yuh tix kaing pa taug mao,
mai pon tix laux lee tix,
mawh kheu ceu pa mhawm kaing yuh kix hawt khaix kix heue.”
To ma to koi ka piang haktiex
14 eAux baox jhak kah,
aux yaox lon pai awm,
ka piang lon pai awm,
awm pa mawh kawn kawn pui pa ngawm piang,
ka piang kaing nawh ceep ka mhok khri,
daux taix nawh giex ka si vawg pa lawm.
15 fKawn cau Siyiex tang kaux tix kaux baox lih khaing daux nyiex buan,
mai kok yo lox si num ka kaw pa ngawm piang lon pai awm an ah nin:
“Bawg yam hoik hoek,
to ma to koi ka piang haktiex hoik kaux heue!
Teei si vawg maix vawg heue!”
16 Mawh kheu nan kaw pa ngawm piang lon pai awm an ploi si vawg tix piang haktiex,
to ma to koi piang haktiex taug khrawm vawg tix heue.
17 Aux baox yaox kawn cau Siyiex tang kaux tix kaux pa lih hoek khaing daux nyiex buan khaing rao ma;
nawh kawx koe si vawg pa lawm tix mag nang heue.
18 Khaix an,
baox koe kawn cau Siyiex pa kuang ka ngu tix kaux lih khaing khu dee tan dee an,
mai kok yo lox si num ka kawn cau Siyiex pa giex si vawg lawm an ah nin:
“Teei si vawg maix vawg ka plix shiet piang haktiex,
mawh kheu hoik teem plix shiet heue!”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau Siyiex an ploi si vawg tix ka piang haktiex,
vawg ka plix shiet piang haktiex,
mai taing sau ka daux dung ting pa greeik plai song rhawm cie Siyiex.
20 gPlix shiet du dig ka daux dung pa greeik plai ka plak praix veng,
mai nham jauh lih khaing daux dung greeik plai,
kheem krax loe siyiex kongli,
been raux koe ra kongjhee heue.
