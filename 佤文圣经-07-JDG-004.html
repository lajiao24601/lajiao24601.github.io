Ti-paw-la mai Pa-la
4
Khaix hoik yum Yi-hux,
pui Yi-seu-liex khankix deeh phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
2 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ap kix ka daux tawm taix Yie-ping simiang haktiex Cia-nang,
pa kuang ka veng Sha-saw.
Cao kaing ting kru naing Yie-ping mawh Si-si-la,
pa ot ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad Sha-law-seux.
3 Simiang Yie-ping koe ling law naing brung pa yuh ka rhiem dim yieh lhang;
nawh khin jhawm,
mai gut kheem ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tix nga num.
Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex khankix khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex teem coi kix heue.
4 Koe bun cao jhai tix kaux pa kaux Ti-paw-la,
nawh mawh moeng La-pi-to.
Yam an nawh mawh cao prawng see ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
5 Ka piang gawng Yi-fa-liien,
si nax La-max mai Paw-theu-lix koe khaox jhie yaong ka tix gawng,
nawh ngawm ka piang khaox jhie yaong an si jheeing.
Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hoek ka nawh dee an,
kieh nawh prawng rauh see son kix.
6 Koe tix singaix,
nawh ploi pui hu kok Pa-la kawn simex Ya-pi-naw-ang khaing veng Ci-ti-see pa koe ka daux haktiex Na-fu-tha-lix hoek,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tawx lox ka maix ah nin:
‘Maix caw tix khrawm pui khaing daux ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix mai ceu pui Si-pu-loeng tix mheen kaux,
thung kix hu ka gawng Tha-paw.
7 Aux tag sang thung cao kaing pui Yie-ping pa kaux Si-si-la ploi ka ling law naing mai pui naing hoek rieb tix puing ka maix ka rawm klawng Ci-suing,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang ap nawh ka daux tawm taix maix heue.’ ”
8 Pa-la pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan maix hu tau mai aux,
aux tag hu;
maix ang hu tau mai aux,
mai aux tag ang hu heue.”
9 Ti-paw-la ah nin:
“Chix tix,
aux tag hu tau mai maix,
viang mawh nan,
viang maix pex kawx,
mai ang tie koe min plak ngai,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik ap Si-si-la ka daux tawm taix bun tix kaux”,
kheu nan,
Ti-paw-la mai Pa-la yaog tix hu tau ka Ci-ti-see.
10 Pa-la khrawm pui Si-pu-loeng mai pui Na-fu-tha-lix hoek ka Ci-ti-see,
koe pui tix mheen kaux mai hawt khaix nawh.
Ti-paw-la ot tau mai nawh heue.
11 Yam an,
Si-pai pui Ci-ni yuh nyiex gox ot dex ka veng Ci-ti-see praok Sa-na-ying ka khaox yang yit.
Nawh lih hu khaing pui Ci-ni tang kaux;
kix mawh ceu pui keeing moeng Maw-si doeh ceu cad Heu-pa heue.
12 Si-si-la hoik tawng Pa-la hoek ka piang gawng Tha-paw,
13 nawh tom khrawm ling law naing brung rhiem dim yieh lhang mai pui naing tix khawm uik,
ploi kix hu jauh ka pui tang ceu tang ceu tang cad Sha-law-seux tom hoek ka rawm klawng Ci-suing heue.
14 Ti-paw-la ah nin ka Pa-la:
“Kaoh hu heue,
Simiang Siyiex hoik thung ka maix!
Daux in singaix,
nawh hoik ap Si-si-la ka daux tawm taix maix.”
Kheu nan,
Pa-la thung pui naing lih hu khaing gawng Tha-paw tix mheen kaux.
15 Yam ploi Pa-la pui naing tix hu puing ka naing,
Simiang Siyiex kieh Si-si-la mai ling law naing brung rhiem nawh mai pui naing khawm uik prung prang.
Si-si-la lih khaing ling law naing brung rhiem tix,
mai to ka caong tix.
16 Pa-la hawt pheet ka pui naing kix mai ling law brung naing rhiem kix,
mai pheet tom hoek ka pui tang ceu tang cad Sha-law-seux.
Cub kru naing Si-si-la hoik yum ka khawm uik,
ang lai deuh tix kaux kawx heue.
17 Si-si-la to ka nyiex gox Ya-yix moeng Si-pai pui Ci-ni,
kheu jao yuh Yie-ping simiang pui Sha-saw paox pie tau mai nyiex ma Si-pai.
18 Ya-yix lih rieb Si-si-la,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang eue,
laig daux nyiex gox aux,
paw koe krax lhat krax graung.”
Si-si-la laig ka daux nyiex gox;
Ya-yix mox nawh plak khaix man lhop daux doh nyiex goxa.
19 Si-si-la ah nin ka nawh:
“Yuh buan son tawx aux nyaux rawm tix kawk,
aux kied simie tix nyaux rawm.”
Ya-yix paoh hau hak rawm teeh moi mai tawx nawh nyaux,
khaix an deeh mox ka nawh.
20 Si-si-la ah nin ka Ya-yix:
“Cung hawt diang siviex nyiex gox.
Phan pui hoek,
mai chawk ka maix ot ang pui ot tin,
maix tag caw tix krai ang ot heue.”
21 Si-si-la kied taug mao,
nawh tom laig it.
Ya-yix teei daix mai jim khaox tawk nyiex gox,
mai koi hu ka praok nawh,
mai tawk jim khaox an laig daux dawng yhaok nawh,
tom phlat ka tiex,
Si-si-la yum.
22 Pa-la hawt sawg Si-si-la,
Ya-yix lih rieb nawh plak praix,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Laig daux nyiex,
jhak ka pui pa hoik sawg maix.”
Pa-la laig ka daux nyiex tau mai nawh,
mai yaox Si-si-la hoik yum ot piang tiex,
mai jim khaox hoik tawk tix phlat ka dawng yhaok nawh heue.
23 Ka daux singaix an,
Siyiex kieh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pon giex gang ka Yie-ping simiang Cia-nang.
24 Jauh yam an,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex nied ot nied ting,
mai pon pex ka Yie-ping simiang haktiex Cia-nang,
tom si dauh hoik yuh nawh laux leeing heue.
