Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix
Krax kham gee ceu pui Yi-seu-liex daux Ai-cix
1
aKawn simex Ya-keux khankix,
viex pui nyiex tix hu tau mai Ya-keux,
hoek ka haktiex Ai-cix.
Ceu kaux kix mawh:
2 Lui-piien,
Si-miien,
Li-we,
mai Yo-tax,
3 Yi-sa-cia,
Si-pu-loeng,
mai Piien-ya-ming;
4 Tang,
Na-fu-tha-lix,
Cia-teux mai Ya-seux.
5 Pui in kix hoik mawh ceu kawn praix Ya-keux khawm uik,
kix koe atigliah kaux.
Kawn simex Ya-keux tang kaux pa kaux Yaw-seux hoik dawg hoek haktiex Ai-cix.
6 Khaix an,
Yaw-seux hoik yum,
mai pux aik nawh khawm doeh nyiex ma nawh khankix kawx hoik yum.
7 bViang mawh nan,
doeh doe ceu pui kix pa mawh pui Yi-seu-liex,
kaux pui kix kied phai pre haoh hun,
mai nied tix haoh hun koe riang si pie si pie,
mai naog ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik heue.
8 cKhaix an,
koe simiang khraox tix kaux daux Ai-cix,
nawh ang tawng ka grawng kaing pa yuh Yaw-seux pie ceeix kawx.
9 Nawh ah nin ka pui Ai-cix:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex in kix hoik kied haoh hun koe riang,
hoik mawh pa ang mhawm ka ex.
10 ePhan naing lih,
kix rien tix rom tix tau mai pui koeng mai puing ex,
to hud khaing haktiex ex.
Ex caw tix keud sawn,
son ang sang kieh kaux pui kix haok huan heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Ai-cix tawx cao kuang kaing ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kha ka kix,
tuk tiak ka kix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex sum veng Pi-tong mai veng Lang-sai,
kieh mawh dee deuh Vawng kraung khrai tix kah.
12 Viang mawh nan,
pui Ai-cix nie baox yeen ya ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui Yi-seu-liex nie baox pre haoh hun,
kaux pui kix nie baox pre tix ot tom vieh va,
kheu nan,
pui Ai-cix khi rhawm kheu pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
13 Pui Ai-cix tom kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh kaing pa kin,
14 mai kha ka kix,
kieh kix yuh kaing pa mawh kaing lon tiex gleeing mai kaing ma kaing kaung pa nu ku ceu,
mai kieh kix kha rhawm tix tete heue.
15 Simiang Ai-cix kok ka bun giex keh pui Si-paw-lai ra kaux hoek,
tix kaux kaux See-fu-la,
an tix kaux kaux Phu-a;
nawh ah nin ka kiex:
16 “Yam yuh pax kaing giex keh son bun miex Si-paw-lai,
phan kix keh kawn simex,
pax pauh,
phan mawh kawn bun,
deuh nawh im ju heue.”
17 Viang mawh nan,
cao giex keh an kiex mawh pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
mai ang nghiet ka lox tawx Vawng;
kiex deuh kawn nyawm simex khankix im ju.
18 Kheu nan,
Vawng kok ka bun giex keh an kiex hoek,
chawk kiex ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pax lai yuh nin?
Yuh ka mawx lai deuh kawn simex khankix im ju lie?”
19 Kiex pawk ah nin:
“Mawh kheu bun miex Si-paw-lai ang awm bun miex Ai-cix;
kix kied koe riang,
sivoe sang hoek yiex,
kix hoik keh heue.”
20-21 Kheu bun giex keh an kiex mawh pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
Siyiex kia ngien ka kiex,
kieh kiex tang koe nang nyiex ma.
Kaux pui Yi-seu-liex nied tix haoh hun mai baox ting riang.
22 fDee si dauh,
simiang Ai-cix tawx lox ka kawn pui cie tix:
“Can kawn nyawm simex pa keh khaing pui Si-paw-lai ku kaux,
pex caw tik ka daux rawm klawng Ni-law,
mai deuh ngie kawn nyawm bun ku kaux im ju heue.”
