9
aPui hoik grheu kix hu ka dee koe krax pa lhat graung pa faik.
Kix ang lai baox yaox ka krax pa sang luat luan tix khaing daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi heue.
Simiang pa sang lih hoek
Dee ot ceu pui Si-pu-loeng mai ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix ra ceu,
mai pui hoik sax map nai kah.
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
haktiex in pa mawh,
jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong hu plak lih singaix hoek ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
khawm pui tang ceu tang cad pa paung ot ka haktiex Cia-li-lix,
tag sang pon tix koe min plak ngai khawm uik heue.
2 bPui pa ot ka daux pa faik
tag sang pon yaox ka pa riang ting;
pui pa ot ka greem veei krax yum sivoe an
yam in pa riang hoik jhan ka piang kix heue.
3 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawx krax pa ting gaux rhawm ka kixc;
maix hoik thiem thie krax pa nyawm ot piang kix.
Krax pa gaux rhawm kix hoik awm gaux rhawm pui mai khrawm to ma,
hoik awm gaux rhawm pui mai guah ka kraung khrai pa pon khaing daux naing.
4 Mawh kheu maix hoik yuh laux leeing gang pa ot ka ngawg kix,
hoik rud ka maox pa si vied kix dut,
hoik raig pot ka khaox nguh pa yeen ya kix,
awm hoik yuh maix pui naing Mi-tiien laux leeing yuh nan heue.
5 Kiiep ceep pui tud pui rhim,
kraung ceep pui naing pa naog nham kah,
tag sang gleeing tix tik daux ngu heue.
6 Koe kawn ngie tix kaux keh son ex heue!
Koe kawn simex tix kaux tag sang tawx ka ex heue!
Nawh tag sang hoek tix kuang ka piang ex.
Pui tag sang kok nawh mawh:
“Cao gau pa daox dengd”,
“Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm”,
“keeing pa koe leeing ju leeing phan”,
“Simiang pa nhim pa tiiem.”
7 eSiawp sibrawm simiang pa mawh nawh tag sang nied tix haok huan;
meung haktiex nawh pon krax pa nhim pa tiiem leeing ju.
Nawh tag sang plud tix mawh simiang khaix Ta-wue;
nawh teei pa rauh pa see pa khaw caw giex ka kaing;
jauh yam in tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik but rhawm tix sang yuh kaing in lih heue.
Simiang Siyiex sang ciyang pui Yi-seu-liex
8 Simiang Siyiex hoik prawng ca ka meung Yi-seu-liex,
ka kawn praix Ya-keux.
9 Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai can pui pa ot ka daux veng Sa-ma-li-yax khawm uik,
tag caw tix hoik tawng ka krax pa prawng ca Siyiex yuh nin.
Kix ting rhawm lhaong rhi mai ah nin:
10 “Nyiex lon tiex gleeing hoik ghrup,
ex baox sum nyiex simaox;
nuk pa yuh ka khaox liah hoik pot,
ex baox loh nuk ting ka khaox si oe bhawng pa blawn mhawm heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex cheeik pui koeng ka kixf hoek tix puing ka kix.
12 Pui Shui-li-yax ot ka plak lih singaix,
pui Fe-li-see ot ka plak klip singaix,
ang jheeh tix sang bleet ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Viang hoik yuh nin kawx,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ang bax naox tix;
nawh ca kawn sang yaog taix tix lauh ka kix heue.
13 Viang Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik ciyang ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kix ca kawn ang cu tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
ang bax kling tix deeh ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
14 Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex mai ka pui hun ka daux tix ngaix khaing;
nawh tag sang kid tix tik ka kaing kix mai si dax kix.
15 Pui pa riex ju mai pui pa koe min khankix mawh kaing,
cao jhai pa krai lox pie lox lhien khankix mawh si dax.
16 Pui pa hoik dawg khaw tix thung kawn pui tieh tix viex pui lud krax,
mai kieh kix nie baox daox aw.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang doh prah ghram ka pui pa nhum ju,
mai ang lai koe krax pa caox rhawm ka miex mai kawn doe;
mawh kheu pui hoik yuh kaing pa ang khaw ang jiet khawm uik,
hoik krai lox pa daox aw khawm uik.
Viang hoik yuh nin kawx,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ang bax naox tix;
nawh ca kawn yaog taix tix lauh ka kix heue.
18 Krax pa caox pa caig kawn pui khankix hoik awm ngu pa gleeing yeem kat pa daw tix hu yuh nan,
mai baox awm ngu pa gleeing tiag khaox,
pa hoik klia lon taox ngu tix haok ka rao ma yuh nan,
19 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik song rhawm son rhi.
Krax pa ciyang nawh awm ngu yuh nan,
pa hoik gleeing ka haktiex khawm uik,
mai hoik kieh kawn pui laux leeing,
tix kaux kawx ang lai deuh heue.
20 Ka daux haktiex ku caong daog,
pui sawg ku dauh ku dee,
tud tix ih ceu pa ih,
viang mawh nan kawx,
kix ca kawn cai rhawm ang yaox tix sawm sak;
mawh kheu nan kix tom dee ih ka nex kawn tix heue!
21 Pui Ma-na-si mai pui Yi-fa-liien ra lai puing ka paox tix,
kix baox dee khrawm ka paox tix mai puing ka pui Yo-tax.
Viang hoik yuh nin kawx,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ang bax naox tix;
nawh ca kawn sang yaog taix tix lauh ka kix heue.
