Kawng aw tiex pa max phawng
19
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux hu liag kawng aw tiex tix mu,
mai baox nhie aux viex kuad song ka daux pui hun khankix mai cao giex kaing li pa riex ju khankix,
2 ahu laig ka daux “siviex max ngoi” hoek ka ngawd Sing-noeng-cee.
Nawh tag sang krai pao ka gum lox pa sang tawx tix ka aux dee an.
3 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh aux krai ah nin:
“Simiang pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
pex tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet;
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka gum lox krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex.
Aux tag sang ploi krax laux krax leeing pa ting lih ka haktiex in,
mai kieh pui pa mhong kaing in yaoh yee lheet yhaok kah.
4 Aux yuh in mawh kheu hoik tik vud pui cie aux ka aux;
kix ot dee in hoik tan lu krax tan ka siyiex,
hoik seeing brai yuh haktiex in.
Siyiex pa krub kix hoik mawh pa ang kix mai tax du kix sax tawng sax yung,
mai simiang Yo-tax kawx ang tawng.
Kix hoik kieh nham pui pa ang koe krax lud loe tauh ka daux haktiex in,
5 bmai sum khu son Pa-lix,
mai gleeing kawn simex kawn bun tix ot ka piang kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie.
Aux ang sax tawx lox nhie kix yuh nin;
pie keud kawx ang aux sax keud ka kaing ceu pa yuh nin.
6 “Mawh kheu nan,
koe tix ngaix haktiex in tag ang lai kok mawh Thaw-fe-theux,
phan ang mawh nan ngawd Sing-noeng-cee,
tag sang kok mawh ‘ngawd pauh pui’.
7 Aux tag yuh laux ka krax keud pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tix sang yuh khawm uik dee in.
Aux tag sang kieh pui koeng puing pex ka kix,
mai pauh kix yum.
Aux tag sang tawx to kaux yum kix mawh priex preem pa ih sim mai to tiag khankix.
8 Aux tag sang kieh veng in si pup ka krax laux leeing pa kied lhat graung pui kah;
yam yaox pui pa lawng krax ku kaux hoik yaoh yee baig ka khawm uik.
9 Pui koeng tag sang lai lawm ka veng in,
mai pauh ka pui pa ot daux.
Pui ka daux veng ang lai koe priex preem pa ih kheu hoik lai lawm pui kix,
kheu nan ang ih ka nex paox plawg paox nyawm tix ang chix,
pie nex kawn lih tix kawx hoik ih ka heue!”
10 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux lauh max ka kawng an plak sivoe ngai pui pa hu tau mai aux,
11 mai krai pao gum lox Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ka kix.
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux tag sang yuh pui in kix laux leeing,
yuh laux ka veng in,
awm hoik pauh pui kawng in max,
ang lai pon cox blaox.
Pui tag sang si paung to kaux yum kix ot ka Thaw-fe-theux,
mawh kheu ang koe haktiex yi pa sang si paung ka kix.
12 Aux tag sang hawt tix yuh veng in mai pui pa ot daux,
kieh veng in tieh tix lih mawh Thaw-fe-theux.
13 Nyiex ma Yie-lu-sa-leung,
nyiex haw simiang Yo-tax,
khawm ka piang nyiex pa gleeing nyawng bhawng ka simuing,
nyiex ma pa tawx siyiex nyaux ka plai,
tag sang awm Thaw-fe-theux yuh nan,
lih mawh haktiex pa ang singax singeh heue.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
aux lih hu khaing Thaw-fe-theux,
pa mawh haktiex pa ploi Simiang Siyiex aux hu krai pao gum lox in kah.
Aux hoek ka nyiex buan,
cung ka daux nyiex krai pao ka pui hun
15 gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
simiang kru naing.
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux tag sang hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik krai tix,
ploi krax laux krax leeing lih ka veng in mai ka sivie siveng nyiex yaong in,
mawh kheu kiien rhawm kix ang cu tix nghiet ka gum lox aux heue.”
