Cao jhai pa ot ka kaong
20
Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Sa-eu-keung tawx lox ka simiang naing pa ot greem tix,
mai nhie nawh puing ka veng Ya-seu-thu pa ot haktiex Fe-li-see.
2 Sivoe sang lih kaing in loe num,
Simiang Siyiex hoik sax tawx lox ka Yi-sai-yax kawn simex Ya-maw-see,
nhie nawh peeik kraung man keei sum khaing to kaux tix,
khawm kiiep pa ot ka caong nawh.
Nawh nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
mai ot ka kaong,
hu ka caong giex tix piang tiex.
3 Yam puing pui ka Ya-seu-thu,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Siliex aux Yi-sai-yax hoik ot ka kaong,
mai hu ka caong giex tix loe num;
in hoik mawh mhai sakse ceu pa sang lih tix mawh ka Ai-cix mai ka Ku-seu.”
4 Vawng Ya-sux sang teei tix giex laung kawn pui khaing daux meung in kiex hu,
mai peeik kraung ceep kix;
ka daux bawg yam an,
mawh kuad mawh kawn nyawm,
tag sang kieh kix ot ka kaong khawm uik,
hu ka caong giex tix,
mai kieh Ai-cix pon krax pa kaig pa tua.
5 Pui pa hoik een rhawm tix ka Ku-seu mai ka Ai-cix khankix,
tag sang deuk rhawm deuk rhi.
6 Ka daux bawg yam an,
pui pa paung ot ka hawt rawm Fe-li-see khankix tag sang ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
pui pa hoik een ex rhawm tix kah,
pa sang teem coi ex tieb tie ka Vawng Ya-sux,
yuh ka mawx lai yuh nin heue!
Ex kawn sang pon to tix klawd ju lie?”
