Ye-sux pai moi ka pui pa yum taix caong
(Lai Ma-kheu 2.1–12; Lai Lu-cia 5.17–26)
9
Ye-sux laig daux reu tiang ka rawm nhawng,
mai deeh hoek ka veng ot tix.
2 Koe pui grawng ka pui pa yum taix caong pa it piang ku hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
Ye-sux yaox krax yum rhawm kix,
tom ah nin ka pui pa yum taix caong an:
“Kawn eue,
paw vi khi,
phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi heue!”
3 Koe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri ra loe kaux keud ka daux rhawm tix ah nin:
“Pui in krai lox pa ting lhaong heue!”
4 Ye-sux hoik tawng krax pa keud kix,
nawh chawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai koe rhawm pa caox pa caig lie?
5 Ah nin ka pui in ‘phi map maix hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi’ yie lie,
phan ang mawh nan,
ah nin ‘kaoh hu’ yie lie?
6 Aux tag sang kieh pex tawng,
kawn kawn pui koe akhuang tix pluat ploi phi map ka piang haktiex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka pui pa yum taix caong an:
“Kaoh,
teei ku it maix,
ing ka nyiex heue!”
7 Pui an tom kaoh,
mai ing ka nyiex.
8 Pui pa yaox hoik daox deng lhat graung khawm uik;
kix rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik tawx nawh siawp sibrawm pa yuh nin ka kawn pui heue.
Kok ka Ma-thaix
(Lai Ma-kheu 2.13–17; Lai Lu-cia 5.27–32)
9 Ye-sux lih hu khaing dee an mai baox hu ka plak sivoe,
nawh yaox pui pa koh keen tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Ma-thaix,
ngawm ka piang dee koh keen,
Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Hawt khaix aux heue!”
Ma-thaix tom yaog tix kaoh,
mai hawt khaix nawh heue.
10 aYam sawm rheep Ye-sux ka daux nyiex ma Ma-thaix,
pui koh keen mai pui caox tom nie kawx hoek nang,
sawm rheep tau mai Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh khankix.
11 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah yaox ka nawh,
kix tom ah nin ka pui gau Ye-sux:
“Yuh ka mawx sara pex lai sawm nyaux tau mai pui koh keen mai pui caox lie?”
12 Ye-sux mhong ka lox in,
tom ah nin:
“Pui pa brax kua ang lo ka cao sidah,
mawh pui pa koe jhawm saux pa sang lo kah.
13 bPhuk lai sigang khri krai ah nin:
‘Aux sang lo ka krax pa caox rhawm,
mai ang mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie.’
Pex hu si kaw tix yaox ka dee bleeh gum lox in heue!
Mawh kheu kaing ting aux hoek ang mawh sang kok ka pui mhawm,
mai mawh son sang kok ka pui caox heue.”
Grawng pa tuah ka priex preem
(Lai Ma-kheu 2.18–22; Lai Lu-cia 5.33–39)
14 Koe tix bawg,
pui gau Yaw-hang cao cub com chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yix mai pui Fa-li-saix rien tuah ka priex preem jheeng jheeng,
viang mawh nan,
pui gau maix ang tuah ka priex preem,
yuh ka mawx lie?”
15 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yam kawn ot kaw pa yuh ka paox ka daux bang poi ka paox,
nyo pa hoek tix yuh krax gaux rhawm sang chix laux rhawm lie?
Ang doh mawh nan.
Viang mawh kawx nan,
hoek singaix bawg yam,
pui tag sang viex kaw pa yuh ka paox hu khaing kix,
yam an kix taug sang tuah ka priex preem heue.
16 “Ang koe pui pa teei man khraox blaox ka kraung prim,
mawh kheu khaix blaox pa khraox an tag sang kieh kraung prim an griah,
mai kieh rhoi khaix griah an nie vieh.
17 Ang koe nang pui pa teei plai pa khraox sau daux chee pa prim.
Phan yuh nin,
chee an tag bih,
plai tag roik,
pie khawm chee kawx hoik laux heue.
Tag caw tix sau plai pa khraox ka daux hau hak pa khraox;
yuh nan,
kiex khawm ra ceu tom tag pon tix kaux cong heue.”
Kawn bun pui pa kuang ka nyiex sidiex mai bun saux pa lih nham
(Lai Ma-kheu 5.21–43; Lai Lu-cia 8.40–56)
18 Yam kawn krai Ye-sux lox in kix ka pui gau Yaw-hang,
koe pui Yo-thaix cao kuang ka nyiex sidiex hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai krub ka plak sivoe ngai nawh ah nin:
“Kawn bun aux taug khraox yum,
khiox khaw maix deuh taix tix piang nawh,
mai kieh nawh deeh kaoh im heue.”
19 Ye-sux yaog tix kaoh hawt khaix nawh;
pui gau Ye-sux kawx hu tau mai nawh heue.
20 Koe bun tix kaux pa saux lih nham kao ra num;
nawh hoek ka plak khaix Ye-sux,
mai giex ka jaong sibex dha pa ceep nawh tix vud.
21 Ka daux rhawm nawh keud ah nin:
“Ca mawh pu pi aux ka jaong sibex nawh,
aux tag sang moi heue.”
22 Ye-sux kling ka to kaux tix yaox ka nawh,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Kawn eue,
paw khi heue,
krax yum rhawm maix hoik taoh ka maix heue!”
Ka daux bawg yam an,
jhawm saux bun an moi heue.
23 Ye-sux hoek ka daux nyiex pui cao kuang nyiex sidiex an,
nawh yaox ka cub pui pa phlu shiao tauh kraung mai pui pa yiiem pa hu si paung ka pui yum khankix,
24 nawh tom ah nin ka kix:
“Pex lih hu khawm uik,
kawn nyawm bun in ang bax yum,
mawh laig it ngie khaing heue!”
Pui hun nyiah ka nawh khawm uik.
25 Khaix hoik grheu kix pui hun hu,
Ye-sux laig ka daux lawg it kawn bun,
mai bluh taix nawh kaoh,
nawh tom yaog tix kaoh.
26 Grawng kaing in hoik tauh hu ka haktiex khawm uik heue.
Pai moi ka pui dug ngai ra kaux
27 Ye-sux lih hu khaing haktiex an,
mai baox hu sivoe ngai.
Koe pui dug ra kaux hawt khaix nawh mai kok yo ah nin:
“Kawn praix Ta-wue eue,
caox rhawm ka yiex liak!”
28 Ye-sux laig daux nyiex,
pui dug an kiex hoek plak sivoe ngai nawh,
nawh tom chawk kiex ah nin:
“Yum rhawm pax grawng sang pon yuh aux kaing in lie?”
Kiex pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
yiex yum rhawm heue.”
29 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux pu pi ka ngai kiex,
mai ah nin:
“Hawt tix yuh awm krax yum rhawm pax heue!”
30 Ngai kiex tom pon tix riang.
Ye-sux bre tix tawx lox ka kiex ah nin:
“Paw vi krai kaing in ka pui tix kaux kawx heue!”
31 Viang mawh nan,
yam lih hu kiex mai krai kaing pa hoik yuh Ye-sux ka baoh haktiex an khawm uik heue.
Pai moi ka pui ang chix nye
32 Yam lih hu kix,
koe pui viex pui pa ang chix nye pa laig phi daux hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux.
33 Yam hoik grheu phi lih,
pui an tag ang jheeh tix si kah.
Pui hun kied daox deng ka khawm uik ah nin:
“Ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex ang ex sax yaox ka kaing pa yuh nin heue.”
34 cViang mawh nan,
pui Fa-li-saix ah nin:
“Nawh grheu phi lih ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka simiang phi heue.”
Ye-sux kia ngien ka kawn pui
35 dYe-sux lai ling tix sidiex gaux pui ka daux nyiex sidiex,
pa koe ka veng ting veng iak yaong ting yaong iak khawm uik,
mai krai pao ka gum lox buan pa mhawm meung rao ma,
mai pai moi ka jhawm saux ku ceu ka daux kawn pui.
36 eYam yaox nawh ka cub pui hun,
nawh caox rhawm ka kix;
mawh kheu ang kix koe cao teem coi,
awm yung pa ang koe cao liang yuh nan.
37 fMawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka pui gau tix:
“Nghox koi pa sang khrawm pex kied nie,
viang mawh nan,
pui yuh kaing kied tux.
38 Pex tag caw tix khiox khaw ka cao si peem,
ploi pui yuh kaing hoek tix vawg ka to ma cie nawh heue.”
