Lox rawg buan Ha-naw
2
aHa-naw rawg buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex kieh rhawm rhi aux naog krax pa gaux rhawm;
Simiang Siyiex kieh aux pon yaog kaing tix.
Aux nyiah pui pa koeng,
mawh kheu teem coi Siyiex ka aux,
kieh aux gaux rhawm heue.
 
2 Mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex pa kied singax singeh;
lheu khaing nawh,
mai ang lai koe cao pa singax singeh.
Siyiex ex hoik mawh cao pa kia ngien tix kaux khaing.
3 Pex paw lai baox ah lox pa ca nyhau;
pex paw lai baox ah lox giex.
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh Siyiex pa tawng ka ku ceu khawm uik;
nawh prawng ca ka kaing pa yuh kaux pui mawx kaux pui an.
4 Ak puh cie pui pa kua hoik pot;
pui pa iak riang iak rao deeh tieh tix ting riang.
5 Pui pa mi pa koe deeh tieh tix taug mao son pa ih pa nyaux;
pui pa cai rhawm ang lai yaox tix cai rhawm.
Moeng pa ang chix keh kawn keh ka kawn simex kawn bun aliah kaux;
miex pa keh kawn tom nie tag som ka kawn simex kawn bun tix.
6 Simiang Siyiex pon kieh pui yum,
mai pon kieh pui im nang;
nawh kieh pui hu meung yum,
mai pon kieh pui deeh chix hoek.
7 Nawh kieh pui tix blah hot,
mai kieh pui tix blah mag mi;
nawh kieh pui tix blah iak min,
mai kieh pui tix blah ting min.
8 Nawh yaog pui hot pa kha rhawm tix kaoh khaing daux phawng tiex;
nawh joh pui hot kaoh khaing daux pum aing.
Nawh kieh kix yuh paox pie tau mai kawn simex simiang,
kieh kix pon min plak ngai pa jhan riang.
Nawh tom sum haktiex ot ka piang heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka diang caong kawn praix pa kaux sijax;
pui pa caox pa caig tag sang grai ka daux krax pa faik.
Pui ang chix pex ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka riang rao to kaux tix.
10 Simiang Siyiex tag sang yuh laux ka pui pa koeng ka nawh;
nawh tag sang puing kix ka sah daux rao ma.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang prawng ca ka piang haktiex khawm bee ma.
Nawh tag sang tawx siawp sibrawm riang rao ka simiang pa hoik sum nawh khankix,
kieh simiang pa hoik laug tix gaux rhawm khawm deeh hoek tix.
 
11 Khaix an,
Yi-li-cia-na deeh ing ka La-max;
Sa-mu-eu kawn nyawm iak an,
kawn prah tix ot ka See-law,
mai yuh kaing krub Simiang Siyiex ka daux krax pa sidiex gau cao giex kaing li Yi-lix heue.
Kawn simex Yi-lix ra kaux
12 Kawn simex Yi-lix khawm ra kaux mawh pui pa cau nyhau.
Kiex map naix ka Simiang Siyiex,
13 mai ang hawt ka been pa teei ka nex son krax tan pa hoik deuh cao giex kaing li.
Bawg yam tan pui ka krax tan,
kiex ploi siliex tix viex lawk khaox ciak jheeng jheeng,
yam kawn ngaik nex ka daux aw,
14 siliex tom ciak nex ka khaox cieh,
ciak ceu pa tix ang ah,
teei lih tom sawn mawh cie cao giex kaing li.
Kiex hoik yuh nin ka can pui Yi-seu-liex pa hoek tix yuh krax tan ka See-law.
15 Lheu khaing an,
pie sivoe sang gleeing ka bee pa koe ka daux vaig,
siliex cao giex kaing li hoek,
mai ah nin ka pui pa tan:
“Tawx aux ih nex tix khik,
aux sang hu tawx cao giex kaing li tong.
Nawh ang ih pa ngaik,
sang cie ka nex im heue.”
16 Phan pui an pawk lox ah nin:
“Ex sang hawt awm been pa hoik een tix deuh,
mai ran gleeing ka bee pa koe daux vaig sivoe ha,
khaix an maix tag chix tix teei tix viex hu awm pa simie maix!”
Siliex cao giex kaing li tom ah nin:
“Ang,
tawx yam in heue!
Phan ang tawx,
aux tag sang tud ka heue!”
17 Ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
phi map kawn Simex Yi-lix in kiex ra kaux kied cien tete,
mawh kheu ang kiex yung yi ka khawng phak krax tan ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
Sa-mu-eu ot ka See-law
18 Bawg yam an,
Sa-mu-eu kawn mawh kawn nyawm;
nawh ceep ka sibex dha Yi-fu-teu pa yuh ka man keei sum,
mai yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
19 Tix num tix bawg miex nawh yuh nawh ceep sibex dha iak tix pheen,
mai yam hoek bawg yam hu kieh mex nawh yuh krax tan tix num tix bawg,
mai viex tix tawx ka nawh.
20 Yi-lix hoik nawb buan ka Yi-li-cia-na mai moeng nawh ku bawg,
ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex baox tawx maix cie kawn simex kawn bun khaing moeng maix,
son sang tang ka kawn simex in pa hoik tan maix ka nawh heue.”
Kiex tom deeh ing ka nyiex.
21 Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka Ha-naw;
nawh tom keh kawn simex loe kaux,
kawn bun ra kaux.
Sa-mu-eu kawn nyawm iak in yuh kaing krub Simiang Siyiex,
mai ting lhaong tix ngaix hoik tix ngaix heue.
Yi-lix ne raw ka kawn simex tix ra kaux
22 Yi-lix hoik kied kuad.
Nawh mhong ka kaing pa cao pa caig pa yuh kawn simex tix ra kaux ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu,
pie bun pa yuh kaing ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan khankix,
kiex it tau mai kix.
23 Yi-lix ah nin ka kiex:
“Yuh ka mawx pax lai yuh kaing pa yuh nin lie?
Pui haoh pui hun hoik krai kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh pax ka aux.
24 Kawn aux eue,
ang pon tix lai baox yuh nin!
Ka daux pui cie Simiang Siyiex,
ceu kaux pax ang mhawm hoik tauh hu heue!
25 Phan mawh pui pa lud paox pui tix,
Siyiex kawn chix pluat ploi son nawh;
viang mawh nan,
pui pa phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pui mawx pa sang pon tix pluat ploi son nawh lie?”
Kiex ang nghiet lox keeing tix,
mawh kheu Siyiex sang but rhawm tix pauh kiex yum heue.
26 bSa-mu-eu kawn nyawm iak in,
hoik ting haok huan tix ngaix hoik tix ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex mai pui ku kaux kied khaw rhawm ka heue.
Grox krai pa si pup nyiex Yi-lix krax laux
27 Koe pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex tix kaux hoek ka Yi-lix,
mai krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka nawh ah nin:
“Yam yuh cub nyiex tax du pex Ya-loeng siliex cawng simiang Ai-cix,
aux hoik plak jhi ka nawh.
28 cAux hoik laug cub nyiex ma nawh yuh cao giex kang li aux,
khaing ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
kieh kix yuh kaing ka plak sivoe khu dee tan,
gleeing nyawng bhawng ka aux,
ceep ka sibex dha Yi-fu-teu yuh kaing aux.
Aux tawx kix koe siawp sibrawm pon ka nyum nex pa hoik gleeing ot ka piang khu dee tan.
29 Yuh ka mawx pex lai ghoing rhawm ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai khawn phak lak sawng pa nhie aux pui cie tix tan ka lie?
Yi-lix,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh kawn simex tix ting khaing aux,
mai cu tix kieh kix teei ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie can dee blawn mhawm khankix,
mai eei to kaux tix kluing mu ka lie?
30 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik deuh tix sang tawx cub nyiex ma maix mai ceu pui maix,
yuh cao giex kaing li,
yuh kaing krub cie aux leeing ju.
Viang mawh nan,
yam in aux sang teei tix tik ka kaing pa yuh pex;
aux sang siao ka pui pa yung yi ka aux khankix,
mai ang jhak simi ka pui pa map nai ka aux.
31 Jhak heue!
Bawg yam hoik dex sang hoek,
aux tag sang kieh pui nhum ju ka daux cub nyiex ma maix mai ceu pui cie maix khankix dawt ju,
mai ang koe pui lang ju heue.
32 Maix tag sang suk sag rhawm,
mai boh rhawm ka tawx aux buan son ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux khankix.
Ka daux nyiex ma maix mai ang lai koe pui im ju tom hoek kuad tix.
33 Viang mawh nan,
aux chix deuh ka doeh ceu cad maix tix kaux,
mai kieh nawh yuh cao giex kaing li krub aux,
viang mawh nan,
nawhd sang dug ngai,
deek rhawm deek rhi.
Can doeh ceu cad tang kaux maix khankix,
tag sang nyao yum ka daux kaing pa ang mhawm khawm uik.
34 eKawn simex maix ra kaux Heu-fu-ni mai Fe-ni-ha tag sang yum tau ka daux tix ngai khaing;
in hoik mawh mhai sakse lox pa krai aux ka maix pa hoik lih tix mawh.
35 “Aux sang laug ka cao giex kaing li tix kaux,
mai nawh chix cu nghiet ka aux,
hawt tix yuh kaing pa khaw ka rhawm aux.
Aux sang tawx nawh koe kawn praix;
nawh tag sang yuh kaing ka plak sivoe ngai simiang pa hoik laug aux leeing ju leeing phan.
36 Ka daux doeh ceu cad maix pa kawn klawd ju tag sang caung,
sang rawg tix sawm ka cao giex kaing li an,
son ceu priex preem ceeix ceeix khaing,
mai khiox khaw tix mawh cao teem coi cao giex kaing lih heue.”
