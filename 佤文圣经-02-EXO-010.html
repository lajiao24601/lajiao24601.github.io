(8) Krax laux ka sido
10
Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix hu ka simiang Ai-cix.
Aux kieh rhawm rhi nawh mai rhawm rhi siliex nawh khankix kiien,
mawh son sang jhi aux mhai pa daox pa am ka daux si nax kix,
2 mai maix chix tix krai kawn simex tix,
kawn saux tix khankix mhong,
yam yuh aux ka mhai pa daox pa am,
aux yuh nin kieh pui Ai-cix ang lai koe dee keud dee sawn,
son sang kieh pui ku kaux tawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hoek ka simiang Ai-cix,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Si-paw-lai ah yuh nin:
‘Bleeh yam mawx maix tag sang chix cu nghiet lox ka plak sivoe ngaix aux,
mai ploi ka pui cie aux hu,
son sang hu kix yuh kaing krub ka aux lie?
4 Phan maix ca kawn thiang kah,
pa sax aux tag kieh sido khankix hoek ka daux meung haktiex maix heue.
5 Sido khankix tag haoh hun mai kheem ka piang meung,
tom ang lai kieh pui chix yaox ka tiex,
can pa ang laux ka pre,
khawm lhax khaox lhax ox pa kawn deuk khankix,
sido hoik ih uik heue.
6 Kix tag sang naog ka daux nyiex haw maix,
ka daux nyiex ma dee ot siliex maix mai kawn pui cie maix khankix.
Grawng krax pa lhat graung yuh nin,
mawh pa ang pex nyang sax yaox tix bawg kawx,
jauh tom daux ju tax pex tom yam in singaix.’ ”
Khaix hoik krai Maw-si lox,
nawh deeh veeh tix lih hu khaing sivoe ngai simiang Ai-cix heue.
7 Siliex Vawng ah nin ka simiang:
“Pui in kawn sang sawg tix tawx krax nyhap ka ex miex ding lie?
Ploi kix pui Yi-seu-liex hu khaing heue,
kieh kix hu yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix.
Ang maix tawng ka hoik dex laux leeing Ai-cix lie?”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Vawng kok ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hoek,
ah nin ka kiex:
“Aux kieh pex hu yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa sang hu khankix mawh pui mawx lie?”
9 Maw-si pawk lox:
“Yix tag sang hu uik,
khawm kuad,
khawm kawn nyawm.
Yix tag sang viex kawn praix yix mai moi yung,
mawh kheu sang yuh yix poi dee an,
mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex yix heue.”
10 Vawng ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
aux ang doh kieh pex viex bun miex mai kawn tix hu!
In ang mawh grawng pa tieh vheen pex lie?
11 Ang chix!
Phan pex sang hu yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
aux kieh ngie pex pa mawh simex khankix hu.”
Khaix hoik krai ka lox in,
nawh tom grheu Maw-si mai Ya-loeng lih heue.
12 Khaix an Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Yaog ka khaox gix maix,
aux sang kieh sido khankix lih naog ka piang haktiex;
kix sang ih rib riem pa koe piang tiex khawm uik,
pa ang pre nyang bax yuh laux khankix tag sang kloi ih uik heue.”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si yaog ka khaox gix tix;
mai Simiang Siyiex kieh bhaung ting hoek phlu khaing plak lih singaix,
mai phlu ka piang haktiex tix ngaix tix sawm,
yam hoek plak pon ngawb,
phung sido hoek tom nie.
14 aPhung sido khankix pu tix hoek,
mai naog ka piang meung haktiex.
Phung sido pa nie yuh nin hoik mawh bawg yam pa hoik luan,
mai bawg yam khaix in sax pa ang sax koe tix bawg kawx heue.
15 Sido khankix hoik kheem ka piang tiex khawm uik;
haktiex hoik faik.
Sido khankix hoik ih rib riem pa ang pre nyang bax yuh laux uik,
khawm plix khaing khaox hoik ih uik.
Ka daux Ai-cix khawm uik,
can ku ceu pa koe ka daux kaung ma khaox ox rib riem tau lhax ang ah,
mai ang lai yaox ka pa klai lhax pie ceeix kawx heue.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Vawng nied tix kok ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng ah nin:
“Aux hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex;
aux kawx hoik phit ka pex.
17 Yam in yuh buan son pluat ploi phi map phi ghram aux pie bawg in ha,
mai khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
deeh teei ka krax prawng pa yum in heue.”
18 Maw-si lih hu khaing simiang,
mai rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex.
19 Simiang Siyiex deeh veeh bhaung plak lih singaix lih mawh bhaung pa kied ting riang plak klip singaix,
mai teei klia sido khankix hu,
mai phlu hu ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raohb.
Ka daux haktiex Ai-cix,
sido tix mu kawx ang lai koe kah.
20 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex deeh kieh rhawm rhi Vawng deeh baox kiien,
mai ang ploi pui Yi-seu-liex hu heue.
(9) Krax laux ka pa faik
21 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Yaog taix maix ka plak rao ma,
pa faik praix tag sang kheem ka haktiex Ai-cix heue.”
22 cMaw-si yaog taix tix plak rao ma,
mai pa faik lih kheem ka piang meung khawm uik been loe singaix.
23 Pui Ai-cix ang lai chix yaox paox tix,
mai ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon lih khaing nyiex kix;
viang mawh nan,
dee ot pui Yi-seu-liex khankix pa riang hoik koe ka khawm uik heue.
24 Vawng kok ka Maw-si ah nin:
“Pex hu yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex pex heue!
Khawm moeng kawn pex kawx chix tix kieh hu;
viang mawh nan,
moi,
yung pex caw tix prah ot tin heue.”
25 Maw-si dee pawk ah nin:
“Ang chix!
Maix caw tawx khawng brie pa sang tan lu yix Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
son sang tan krax tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix.
26 Yix sang viex Khawng brie cie yix hu khawm uik,
pie tix mu kawx,
tag ang prah.
Yix sang tang laug ka khawng brie pa siao tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
Sivoe sang hoek ka haktiex an,
yix ang tawng sang siao khawng brie ceu pa tix yuh kaing krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix heue.”
27 Simiang Siyiex deeh kieh rhawm rhi simiang Ai-cix kiien,
mai ca kawn ang cu tix ploi pui Yi-seu-liex hu.
28 Simiang ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix yoi!
Paw lai kieh aux yaox ka maix.
Aux yaox ka maix ngaix mawx,
maix tag yum heue!”
29 Maw-si ah nin:
“Lox pa krai maix ang lud pie ceeix kawx;
maix tag ang lai baox yaox ka pak ngai aux heue.”
