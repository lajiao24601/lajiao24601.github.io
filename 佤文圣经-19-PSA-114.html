Lox rhax daux Poi Luat-luan
114
aYam lih hu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing daux Ai-cix,
yam lih hu kawn praix Ya-keux khaing daux haktiex pui tang ceu tang cad,
2 pui Yo-tax lih mawh pui pa singax singeh cie Siyiex,
pui Yi-seu-liex lih mawh meung cie to kaux nawh heue.
 
3 bRawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh yaox ka mai yaog tix to;
rawm klawng Yaw-tang kawx dee deeh ka plak khaix tix.
4 Can gawng lua hoik jang luh tix awm yung mhaing yuh nan;
can gawng iak kawx hoik jang luh tix awm kawn yung heue.
 
5 Rawm sigrit sigra eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai to lie?
Rawm klawng Yaw-tang eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai deeh plak khaix lie?
6 Can gawng lua yuh ka mawx maix lai jang luh tix awm yung mhaing lie?
Gawng iak yuh ka mawx maix lai jang luh tix awm kawn yung lie?
 
7 Haktiex eue,
maix tag sang si yaung yam lih hoek Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang yung yang yam ot ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex Ya-keux.
8 cNawh kieh rang simaox tieh tix lih mawh rawm nhawng,
kieh rawm keet buing tix lih khaing simaox.
