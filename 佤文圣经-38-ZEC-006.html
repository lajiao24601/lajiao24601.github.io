Riang su sai ling law
6
Aux baox yaox ka riang su sai tix mu.
Bawg in,
aux yaox ling law pon mu lih khaing si nax gawng tawng ra mu.
2 aLing law ka daux tix mu mawh brung raoh pa bluh,
ling law ka daux ra mu mawh brung lung,
3 bka daux loe mu mawh brung paing,
ka daux pon mu mawh brung preeh.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
aux chawk ka kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Sara,
ling law in kix dee bleeh mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
5 cNawh ah nin:
“In mawh bhaung pon mu,
pa taug lih hoek khaing plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.”
6 Brung lung bluh ling law hu plak laong hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng,
brung paing hu plak klip,
brung preeh hu ka meung pa koe plak seh.
7 Brung pa kluing in kix lih hoek,
mai nied tix sang hu lai ling tix jhak ka haktiex.
Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Hu lai ling tix jhak ka haktiex heue!”
Kix tom hu.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau Siyiex krai lox pa ting si num ka aux ah nin:
“Brung in kix pa hu plak laong hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng,
hoik kieh krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex yien yawg tix heue.”
Tawx Yaw-su-yax ceep mhok khri
9 Simiang Siyiex tawx gum lox in plak greem ka aux.
10 Nawh ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix teei khawng phak lak sawng pa hoik tan lu He-taix,
To-pi-ya,
mai Yie-ta-ya viex hoek ka nyiex ma Yaw-si-yax kawn simex Si-fang-ya dum mawx dum an.
Pui in kix mawh pa hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix ka Pa-pi-loeng.
11 Maix tag caw tix teei mau khri pa hoik tan lu kix yuh mhok khri ka tix mu,
mai ceep ka kaing Yaw-su-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting kawn simex Yaw-sha-ta.
12 dMaix tag caw tix krai ka nawh,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
‘pa kok mawh “kak khaox” an,
tag caw tix yah tix lih khaing dee ot tix,
khaix an deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
13 Nawh tag sang mawh pui pa sum ka nyiex buan,
pa rieb ka min plak ngai simiang,
pa kuang ka piang pui cie tix an.
Koe cao giex kaing li tix kaux tag sang cung ka praok dang ngeung nawh;
kiex khawm ra kaux tag sang rom paox tix ka daux krax pa nhim heue.’
14 Mhok khri in tag sang deuh ot ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
son sang kieh mawh pa doh keud,
pa doh keud ka He-taixe,
To-pi-ya,
Yie-ta-ya,
mai Yaw-si-yax heuef.”
15 Pui pa paung ot ka dee singai tag sang hoek tix teem coi,
deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
Khaix hoik deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan,
pex tom taug tawng hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing pa ploi aux hoek ka pex dee in.
Phan pex sau riang priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh tete heue.
