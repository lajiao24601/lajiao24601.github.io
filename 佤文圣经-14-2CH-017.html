Yaw-sa-fa mawh simiang
17
Yaw-sa-fa plud tix mawh simiang khaix keeing tix Ya-sa;
nawh sau riang tix tieb tie ka Yi-seu-liex.
2 Nawh tawx pui naing ot ka Yo-tax ku dauh,
son sang giex veng mai nyiex yaong kaux cong,
khawm veng pa ot ka haktiex Yi-fa-liien ku dauh pa hoik pon Ya-sa.
3 Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka Yaw-sa-fa;
mawh kheu hoik hawt nawh kaing pa yuh keeing tix yam nhum ju keeing tix,
mai ang krub ka Pa-lix.
4 Nawh krub ka Siyiex keeing tix,
priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Siyiex,
mai ang yuh kaing pa yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
5 Simiang Siyiex joh jhan ka meung Yo-tax pa giex Yaw-sa-fa kaing ka piang;
kawn pui tan lu khawng phak lak sawng ka nawh,
mawh kheu nan nawh kied mag mi tete,
mai pui kied chix yung yi ka nawh tete.
6 Nawh kieh krax pa krub ka Simiang Siyiex mawh min plak ngai,
mai hoik yieh tix tik ka can nyiex vad pa koe ka piang gawng iak khawm uik,
khawm riang rong siyiex bun Ya-seu-la pa koe ka haktiex Yo-tax heue.
7 Yam giex nawh kaing ka daux loe num,
nawh ploi Piien Hai-yi-leux,
Eu-pa-tix,
Sa-cia-li-ya,
Na-thang-yiex,
mai Mi-kai-yax hu sidiex gau kawn pui ka veng Yo-tax ku dauh ku dee.
8 Pui pa hu tau mai kix mawh:
See-ma-ya,
Ni-thang-ya,
Si-pa-ti-ya,
Ya-sha-he,
See-mi-la-maw,
Yaw-na-tang,
Ya-to-ni-ya,
To-pi-ya,
Thaw-pa-to-ni-ya pui Li-we in kix dim kaux,
khawm Yi-li-sa-ma mai Yaw-lang cao giex kaing li ra kaux.
9 Kix viex ka phuk lai lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
tauh hu ka haktiex Yo-tax ku nyiex yaong,
mai sidiex gau kawn pui heue.
Kaing mhawm pa pon yuh Yaw-sa-fa
10 Simiang Siyiex kieh meung pa ot sivie siveng kix ang pon puing ka simiang Yaw-sa-fa.
11 Koe pui Fe-li-see tix blah tawx mau keen tom nie mai khawng phak tang ceu ka Yaw-sa-fa;
koe pui A-la-pawx tawx yung aliah rheng aliah yieh mu,
mai pex aliah rheng aliah yieh mu ka nawh.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-sa-fa nied ot nied ting riang.
Nawh sum tab mai veng ot ka haktiex Yo-tax ku dauh,
13 mai deuh kraung ot ka tom nie heue.
Nawh tawx pui naing pa kua ot pau ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
14 mai hawt tix guah tix tawx kix awm mawh kix ceu pui tix.
Ya-na mawh cao bung kaing pui naing ceu pui Yo-tax,
pui naing pa ot greem nawh koe tix ngoe mheen kaux.
15 Cao bung kaing naing ra kaux tix kaux mawh Yaw-ha-nang,
pui naing pa ot greem nawh koe tix nga daix mheen kaux.
16 Baox koe tix kaux mawh kawn simex Si-ci-lix pa kaux Ya-ma-see-ya,
pui naing pa ot greem nawh koe tix nga mheen kaux.
(Ya-ma-see-ya hoik sax mawh pui pa tang viex rhawm tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex.)
17 Cao bung kaing ceu pui naing Piien-ya-ming mawh Yi-li-ya-tax;
nawh mawh pui naing pa kua tix kaux,
mai bung pui naing pa hoik koe dieb tie naok mai pui naing ak puh tix nga mheen kaux.
18 Cao bung kaing naing pa ot greem nawh mawh Yaw-sa-pa,
nawh bung pui naing pa hoik rien khre tix mhawm,
pa chix tix hu yuh naing ku bawg ku yam kao daix mheen kaux.
19 Pui in kix hoik ot tix khre simiang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik.
Lheu khaing an,
simiang kawx hoik tawx pui naing ot pau ka veng Yo-tax pa giex kaux cong khankix heue.
