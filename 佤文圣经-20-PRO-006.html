Baox tawx lox sidiex lox gau
6
Pui pa nhum ju eue,
mawh cu ang maix cu tix mawh pui pa tang son paox plawg paox nyawm tix lie?
Hoik plud ceuk tang son pui pa ang tix sax tawng sax yung lie?
2 Mawh sideeing ang maix hoik sideeing tix ka lox pa hoik krai tix lie?
Lox pa ap maix tix deuh,
hoik prawk ang prawk tix giex ka maix lie?
3 Phan koe kaing pa yuh nin,
maix tag cawd ka daux tawm taix pui an.
Yuh nan,
maix tag sang chix bheeik luan yuh ka mawx lie?
Maix tag caw tix nied hu ka pui an,
mai khiox khaw nawh yah tix tik ka ceu pa giex gang ka maix.
4 Paw vi kieh tix laig it,
mai paw vi laux lee tix.
5 Tag caw tix awm kawn sim pa bheeik luan khaing daux rub yuh nan,
awm cak pa to tix bheeik luan khaing tawm taix pui jaw yuh nan heue.
6 Pui pa nieh khankix eue,
tag caw tix jhak simi ka krax ot krax koe meeik,
mai gau tix awm kix yuh nan.
7 Kix ang koe simiang,
ang koe cao thung,
ang koe simiang ka piang kix,
8 viang mawh nan,
kix chix rien tix deuh ka priex preem daux rung praix,
son pa ih yam raux praix.
9 Pui pa nieh khankix eue,
maix sang it hoek yam mawx lie?
Maix tag kaoh bawg yam mawx lie?
10 aMaix ah nin:
“Aux sang gaing khaing,
sang it khaing,
mai bhok taix tix laux lee tix tix vud tix cu khaing heue.”
11 Viang maw kawx nan,
yam nyawm it nyawm tom maix,
krax hot krax sibhawm tag sang awm pui briex pa hoek tix con ka maix yuh nan,
krax cud krax cha tag sang awm pui con pa viex kraung naing tix hoek puing ka maix yuh nan heue.
12 Pui pa cau nyhau,
pui pa caox pa caig hoik pie lhien ku dauh ku dee.
13 Kix chix lu lia tawm ngai tix ka paox tix,
chix tawx taix caong tix,
mai hoik mawh son sang pie lhien ka maix.
14 Rhawm pa seeing brai kix hoik keud tix yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ku bawg ku yam,
hoik yuh krax simah ku dauh ku dee.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
krax laux krax leeing tag sang lih hoek piang kix dum mawx dum an;
kix tag sang si pup ka krax laux krax leeing pa ting heue.
16-19 Koe kaing aliah ceu mawh pa hoik koeng Simiang Siyiex kah,
mawh kaing ceu pa ang nawh lai chix gun yeung kah.
Pa mawh:
ngai pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
dak pa pie lhien,
taix pa pauh pui pa ang bax lud loe,
rhawm rhi pa keud tix sang yuh krax pa tieh pa vheen,
caong pa to hu daux krax pa caox pa caig,
pa kham sakse pa pie lhien,
pa choek simah simaing lih koe ka daux paox grawm paox grux tix heue.
Ang cu phit vao
20 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex keeing tix,
mai paw vi piawm ka gum lox pa sidiex gau miex tix.
21 Maix tag caw tiiem tix deuh ka lox kix ot daux rhawm rhi tix,
mai vung vhien ot ka ngawg tix.
22 Gum lox pa sidiex gau kix tag sang duk thung ka krax pa hu maix;
chix kia chix ngien ka maix yam pon sawm,
chix ot tau mai maix yam pon ngaix.
23 Gum lox pa sidiex gau kix mawh pa riang;
krax pa sidiex gau kix mawh pa jhi ka daux krax lawng kawn pui,
24 mai chix kieh maix singai hu khaing bun coh kaux mai lox pa nyaw glaw moeng pui tang kaux.
25 Paw vi kieh tix glup ka daux pa nyaw nex nham kix;
paw vi kieh tawm ngai kix pon nyaw ka tix.
26 Maix pon teei ngawh khao mun tix lon kok bun pa coh kaux hoek,
viang mawh nan,
krax pa phit tau mai bun pa hoik koe mex tag sang kieh maix som nai khawm uik heue.
27 Maix pon teei tix klien gaox ngu ot ka naok tix mai ang kieh gleeing kraung ceep tix lie?
28 Maix pon hu piang gaox ngu mai ang kieh caong tix hax lie?
29 Pa it tau mai moeng pui tang kaux hoik mawh krax pa laux leeing awm paox.
30 Pa briex tix ih priex preem kheu pa cai rhawm tix,
tee kawn chix tix koe krax va rhawm kah,
31 viang mawh nan,
phan pon giex,
tag caw tix kieh plud aliah son,
mai teei ka can pa koe tix khawm uik plud kah.
32 Pui pa phit tau mai paox tix nie mawh kaing pa daox aw tete,
hoik awm yuh nawh to kaux tix laux leeing.
33 Nawh tag pon krax pa kaig tua,
krax pa tap toi,
krax kaig tua pa ang lai chix teei tix tik.
34 Mex bun pa cheeik song rhawm kheu boh rhawm tix,
yam pawk nawh bix tag ang lai yawn taix tix.
35 Nawh ang rieb ka mau plud;
khawng phak lak sawng tom nie kawx ang pon kieh krax song rhawm song rhi nawh grai heue.
