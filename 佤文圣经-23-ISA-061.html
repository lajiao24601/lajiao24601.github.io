Gum lox gaux rhawm krax taoh
61
abKhuan Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong lih hoek ka piang aux;
nawh groi rawm bee ka piang aux,
mai laug ka aux,
tag sang kieh aux sidiex gum lox pa gaux rhawm ka pui hot pui sibhawm.
Nawh ploi aux hu pai pui pa laux rhawm laux rhi moi;
kieh aux krai pao ah nin:
Pui pa giex,
tag pon krax pa pluat ploi;
pui pa laung,
tag pon krax pa luat luan.
2 cNawh ploi aux hu krai pao ah nin:
ka daux num buan son pa taoh Simiang Siyiex ka pui cie tix heue;
Siyiex ciyang ka singaix sawm cie pui pa koeng.
Nawh ploi aux hu tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka pui pa laux rhawm laux rhi;
3 nawh kieh pui pa ot daux Shi-ang pa koe krax laux rhawm laux rhi,
teei mhok khri tang nyaox nyia,
teei krax gaux rhawm tang krax laux rhawm,
teei lox rhax krax yaog yaw tang lox rhax krax voi rhawm.
Kix tag sang teei taix tix sum khaox awm Simiang Siyiex;
kix tag sang prawng ka daux krax pa rauh see.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang pon krax yaog yaw kheu kaing pa yuh tix.
4 Nawh tag sang cox deeh sum ka dee riem dix dix,
sum bang dee ang pui ot sivoe an kaux cong;
kix tag sang sum ka veng pa hoik ghrup tom rheeng heue.
 
5 Pui cie aux eue,
pui tang ceu tang cad tag sang hoek tix liang yung tang pex;
kix tag sang sum kaung ma tang pex,
kia ngien ka si peem plix siiet pex heue.
6 Kawn pui tag sang tawng mawh pex caox giex kaing li Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang kok pex mawh siliex Siyiex.
Pex tag sang pon sin ngao pa koe ka piang haktiex,
pon ka min plak ngai pa koe ka meung piang haktiex.
7 Pex ang lai baox pon krax kaig krax tua;
pex tag sang baox ot ka daux haktiex tix.
Pex tag sang pon ka buan son tom nie;
pex tag sang pon krax gaux rhawm leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
Aux muh krax pa rauh pa see;
aux koeng ka krax tud krax rhim mai phi map.
Aux tag sang kaux sijax ka pa tawx krax yaog yaw ka pui cie tix;
aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau maix kix leeing ju heue.
9 Kawn praix kix tag sang pon krax pa haok huan ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik;
doeh ceu cad kix tag sang pon krax pa nab yam ka daux kawn pui piang haktiex.
Can pui pa hoik yaox ka kix,
tag sang doh keud khawm uik ah nin:
Kix hoik mawh pui ceu pa tawx aux buan ka heue.
 
10 dYie-lu-sa-leung gaux rhawm kheu kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex tawx kix ceep kraung buan son krax taoh mai kraung pa khaw caw,
awm ceep simex pa yuh ka paox ka lawk kaing,
awm ceep bun pa yuh ka paox ka kraung mau yuh nan,
11 jiet awm yam jauh simie tix kuah yuh nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang taoh ka pui cie tix.
Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
