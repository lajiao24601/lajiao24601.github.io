Teei Ya-paw-la-hang tiag kah
4
Phan mawh grawh grawng mawh nex nham ka tax du ex Ya-paw-la-hang,
sang khaw ex tix yuh ka mawx lie?
2 Phan nawh hoik pon krax pa rauh see kheu kaing pa yuh tix,
nawh tag sang ting mu lox,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang pon yuh nan ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
3 aPhuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Ya-paw-la-hang yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
mawh kheu krax yum rhawm nawh,
Siyiex tom sawn nawh mawh pui rauh see heue.”
4 Pui pa yuh kaing pon ngawh riang tix,
ang sawn mawh buan son,
mai hoik mawh pa khaw nawh tix pon.
5 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa een rhawm tix ka Siyiex pa krai pao prawng ca pui phi map ang koe dee lud an,
Siyiex tag sang kieh nawh si doe tix tau mai tix,
ka daux pa khaw caw kheu krax pa yum rhawm,
mai ang mawh kheu kaing pa yuh nawh.
6 Pa krai Ta-wue ah nin,
pa hoik een Siyiex mawh pui rauh see pa ang mawh ka daux kaing pa yuh nawh koe buan son heue,
in hoik mawh dee bleeh.
Nawh ah nin:
7 bPui pa lud loe pon krax pa va rhawm,
phi map pon krax pa pluat ploi koe buan son heue!
8 Pui pa ang Simiang Siyiex tiiem tix deuh ka krax pa phit lud nawh koe buan son heue!
9 Buan son in pa hoik krai Ta-wue,
mawh ngie son pui pa Pat-hak lie?
Ang mawh.
Hoik mawh nang khawm pui pa ang bax Pat-hak tix;
mawh kheu hoik yaox ex nawh ka daux phuk lai sigang khri,
ah nin:
“Ya-paw-la-hang yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
kheu krax yum rhawm nawh,
Siyiex tom sawn nawh mawh pui rauh see heue.”
10 In hoik mawh pa lih tix mawh ka grawng pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh sivoe ang Ya-paw-la-hang nyang bax Pat-hak tix,
mawh khaix hoik Pat-hak tix lie?
Mawh sivoe,
ang mawh khaix.
11 Khaix an,
nawh Pak-hak tix;
in mawh mhai pa jhi grawh grawng,
krai grawng hoik lih mawh nawh pui rauh see kheu krax yum rhawm tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
phan mawh son pa ang bax Pat-hak tix,
viang mawh nan,
pa hoik een Siyiex kix mawh pui rauh see kheu krax yum rhawm tix khankix,
Ya-paw-la-hang hoik mawh keeing ka krax daux khuan son kix.
12 Nawh kawx hoik mawh keeing ka pui pa Pat-hak tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang joeng mawh ngie kheu pa hoik Pat-hak tix,
mai mawh kheu pa hoik hawt kix ka yum rhawm tax du ex Ya-paw-la-hang an yam ang nawh nyang bax Pat-hak tix yuh nan heue.
Gum lox pa deuh Siyiex lih tix mawh kheu krax yum rhawm
13 cSiyiex hoik deuh gum lox tix ka Ya-paw-la-hang mai ka kawn praix nawh,
krai sang pon nawh ka haktiex in.
Gum lox in ang mawh kheu pa priex Ya-paw-la-hang tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai hoik mawh kheu kok nawh mawh pui pa rauh see kheu krax yum rhawm.
14 dPhan gum lox pa deuh Siyiex mawh pa tawx ka pui pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
krax yum rhawm kawn pui tag sang mawh pa giex going,
mai gum lox pa deuh Siyiex kawx hoik mawh pa ang koe ceu.
15 Krax song rhawm Siyiex hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing lox tat jhawm prawng;
phan ang koe lox tat jhawm prawng,
tag ang koe kaing ceu pa lud ka lox tat jhawm prawng heue.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
gum lox pa deuh mawh riah krax yum rhawm.
In hoik mawh son sang kieh gum lox pa deuh Siyiex kaux cong,
ka daux pa tawx ka can kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang,
ang joeng mawh kheu pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai hoik mawh khawm pui pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex awm Ya-paw-la-hang yuh nan.
17 Awm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Aux hoik ap maix mawh keeing ka ceu pui tom nie heue.”
Ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
gum lox pa deuh in hoik koe ceu;
pa yum rhawm Ya-paw-la-hang ka an,
mawh Siyiex pa kieh pui yum deeh kaoh im,
pa phan yuh can ceu pa koe khawm uik.
18 eYam ang pui lai koe krax keud pot jun pot,
Ya-paw-la-hang ca kawn koe,
ca kawn yum rhawm,
mawh kheu nan,
tom lih mawh “keeing ka ceu pui tom nie”.
Awm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Maix tag sang lih koe kawn praix tom nie.”
19 Yam an,
Ya-paw-la-hang hoik dex sang naog tix yieh num.
To kaux nawh hoik awm pui yum yuh nan,
bawg yam sang keh Sa-la kawn kawx hoik uik,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax teei tix tik krax yum rhawm tix kheu in.
20 Nawh ang bax grai krax yum rhawm,
ang een gum lox pa ap Siyiex ang mawh;
krax yum rhawm nawh tieh tix nie kaux cong,
mai tawx min plak ngai ka Siyiex.
21 Nawh hoik yum rhawm ka grawng sang kieh Siyiex gum lox pa ap tix lih mawh tete.
22 In hoik mawh grawng krax yum rhawm Ya-paw-la-hang “Siyiex tom sawn nawh mawh pui rauh see.”
23 Viang mawh nan,
“hoik een nawh mawh pui rauh see” Gum lox in ang joeng krai son nawh,
24 hoik krai ka son ex kawx.
Mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm ex ka Siyiex pa kieh Simiang Ye-sux deeh kaoh khaing krax yum,
kheu nan hoik een mawh pui rauh see.
25 fAp tix tawx Simiang Ye-sux ka daux tawm taix kawn pui,
hoik mawh yum son phi map ex;
Siyiex kieh nawh deeh kaoh im,
mai kieh ex si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw heue.
