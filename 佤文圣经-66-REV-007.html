Pui Yi-seu-liex kao pon mheen pon rheng kaux
7
aKhaix an,
aux yaox kawn cau Siyiex pon kaux cung ka daog haktiex khawm pon daog,
tie ka bhaung piang haktiex khawm pon plak,
ang kieh bhaung phlu piang haktiex,
piang rawm sigrit sigra,
mai ka piang khaox ox.
2 Aux baox yaox kawn cau Siyiex tang kaux tix kaux,
giex ka cum Siyiex pa koe leeing ju,
pa hoek khaing plak lih singaix.
Nawh kok yo lox si num ka kawn cau Siyiex pon kaux an kix,
pa hoik pon tix rieb ka akhuang pa tawx Siyiex,
pa pon yuh laux ka haktiex mai rawm sigrit sigra ah nin:
3 b“Paw vi yuh laux ka haktiex,
rawm sigrit sigra,
mai khaox ox khankix,
krox jun yix tawk cum ka kaing re siliex Siyiex ha.”
4 Aux mhong ah nin,
ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pui pa hoik tawk cum kah,
khawm uik koe kao pon mheen pon rheng kaux yuh nin:
5 ceu pui Yo-tax pa hoik tawk cum,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Lui-piien,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Cia-teux,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux.
6 Ka daux ceu pui Ya-seux,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Ma-na-si,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux.
7 Ka daux ceu pui Si-miien,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Li-we,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Yi-sa-cia,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux,
8 ka daux ceu pui Si-pu-loeng,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Yaw-seux,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux;
ka daux ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
koe tix mheen ra rheng kaux heue.
Pui hun pa hoek khaing haktiex ku dauh
9 Khaix an aux jhak kah,
aux yaox pui hun tix cub,
kaux pui kix nu tix simin.
Kix mawh pa hoek khaing meung haktiex ku dauh,
khaing ceu pui ku cub,
ku ceu,
lox si kah ku ceu,
kix cung ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung mai kawn yung,
ceep kraung dha paing,
taix kix giex ka kak khaox jhie yaong.
10 Kix kok yo lox si num ah nin:
“Krax taoh lih hoek khaing Siyiex ex piang dang ngeung,
mai khaing kawn yung heue!”
11 Can kawn cau Siyiex khawm uik cung ka vung viang dang ngeung,
mai kuad song,
mai to sat pa im ju pon mu an kix khawm pon daog.
Kix gup tix piang haktiex ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung,
mai krub ka Siyiex,
12 ah nin:
“A-meung!
Kieh krax yaog yaw,
min plak ngai,
ping nyia cuyi,
lox pa ting buan son,
pa ting min ceu kaux,
siawp sibrawm,
pa ting riang ting rao mawh cie Siyiex ex khawm uik,
leeing ju leeing phan heue!
A-meung.”
13 Koe kuad song tix kaux chawk aux ah nin:
“Pui pa ceep kraung dha paing an kix mawh mawx?
Kix kaoh khaing dee mawx?”
14 cAux pawk ah nin:
“Sara,
maix taug tawng.”
Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Pui in kix hoik cim ka krax kha rhawm pa ting.
Kix teei nham kawn yung si daux kraung ceep tix paing singax.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
kix cung ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung Siyiex,
mai yuh kaing krub ka nawh daux nyiex buan leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
Siyiex kaw pa ngawm piang dang ngeung tag sang hoek tix kia ngien ka kix.
16 dKix ang lai baox cai rhawm,
ang lai baox simie tix nyaux rawm;
singaix mai ceu pa hax ang ah,
ang pon gleed yuh kix.
17 eMawh kheu kawn yung pa ot ka dang ngeung tag sang eei liang ka kix,
thung kix hoek ka dee koe rawm keet pa im ju;
Siyiex kawx tag sang awt rawm ngai roik kix singax heue.”
