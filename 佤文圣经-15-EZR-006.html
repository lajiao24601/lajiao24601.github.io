Deeh yaox lox tawx Vawng Sai-lu-seex
6
Mawh kheu nan,
Vawng Ta-lio-see tawx lox nhie pui si kaw ka lai tiiem pa hoik deuh ot daux nyiex haw Pa-pi-loeng.
2 Kix yaox ka bua lai prim tix phuk ka daux nyiex haw simiang veng Ya-ma haktiex Mi-ti-yax,
ka daux bua lai an hoik tiiem tix yuh nin:
3 “Yam giex Vawng Sai-lu-seex kaing ka daux tix num,
nawh tawx lox nhie pui cox sum ka nyiex buan ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kieh mawh dee yuh pui krax tan.
Nyiex buan in plak lhaong tag caw tix kieh koe tix nga aliah kongjhee,
plak vieh tix nga aliah kongjhee.
4 Guh ding loe tia simaox tag caw tix sau khaox ot ka tix tia.
Mau siao khawm uik tawx ka mau pa deuh ot daux krao cie meung.
5 Mau khri kraung khrai pa hoik teei Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha khaing nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung viex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng kawx,
tag sang deeh tawx ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai deuh ot ka dee ot sivoe an heue.”
Vawng Ta-lio-see tawx lox nied tix sum ka nyiex buan
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Vawng Ta-lio-see pawk lox plak greem ah nin:
“Ta-nai simiang haktiex Heu-si,
See-tha Paw-see-nai,
khawm simiang haktiex Heu-si tang kaux khankix:
Pex caw tix hu singai khaing nyiex buan an;
7 ang pon tix kieh raw ka kaing pa sum nyiex buan.
Kieh simiang haktiex Yo-tax mai simiang pui Yo-thaix deeh cox sum nyiex buan Siyiex ka dee ot pa prim.
8 Yam in aux tawx lox nhie pex teem coi kix cox sum nyiex buan.
Mau pa siao lo kix khawm uik nied tix teei lih khaing daux krao simiang,
teei lih khaing keen pa koh khaing haktiex Heu-si tix blah,
son ang kaing in sang naox tix.
9 Pex caw tix tawx pa sang lo cao giex kaing li veng Yie-lu-sa-leung ku singaix;
mawh kawn moi mhaing,
yung mhaing,
kawn yung mhaing,
nghox ca,
kih,
plai mai bee sime pa sang lo ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex ku singaix ang ah,
pex tag caw tix tawx khawm uik.
10 Yuh nin,
kix tom taug yaox tix tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa muh Siyiex piang rao ma tix rieb,
mai khiox khaw nawh tawx buan son ka aux mai ka kawn praix aux.
11 Lheu khaing an aux baox tawx lox:
phan koe pui pa tieh loh ka lox tawx aux,
tag sang teei ka khaox nuk nyiex ma nawh lih tix gawng,
mai klauh ka nawh,
teuk teue ka nawh;
nyiex ma nawh kawx tag sang lih mawh dee tik pa seeing brai.
12 Phan koe simiang,
phan ang mawh nan kawn pui tieh loh ka gum lox in,
mai keud tix sang yuh ka nyiex buan in laux,
kieh Siyiex pa hoik laug veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mawh dee krub pui ka nawh an,
yuh pui in laux leeing.
Aux,
pa mawh Ta-lio-see tawx gum lox in hu,
tag caw tix hawt yuh tom si dauh sidu heue.”
Tan lu ka nyiex buan
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-nai simiang haktiex Heu-si,
See-tha Paw-see-nai,
khawm paox simiang khankix hoik hawt tix yuh ka gum lox tawx Vawng khawm uik.
14 aKuad song simiang pui Yo-thaix hoik pon krax pa joh jhan cao jhai Ha-kai mai cao jhai Sa-cia-li-yax,
kaing pa sum nyiex buan hoik pon krax pa mhawm.
Kix priex tix hawt ka alo Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
khawm lox tawx Sai-lu-seex Vawng Paw-see,
mai lox tawx Ya-ta-shiie-si,
hoik hoik ka kaing pa sum nyiex buan.
15 Yam giex Vawng Ta-lio-see ka cao kaing daux liah num,
nyiex buan hoik hoik sum tix mhawm ka daux khix Ya-tax loe singaix heue.
16 Pui hun Yi-seu-liex,
khawm cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we mai can pui pa vuk vied vuk viang deeh hoek,
mai yuh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi tan lu ap ka nyiex buan.
17 Son khawng tan pa ap in,
kix tan lu ka moi mhaing tix yieh mu,
yung mhaing ra yieh mu,
mai kawn yung pon yieh mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
mai baox tan lu ka kawn pex mhaing kao ra mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
tix mu tang son tix ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
18 Kix hawt awm gum lox pa krai Maw-si ka daux lai,
mai khrawm cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we,
hawt awm cub caw mawx cub caw an,
yuh kaing krub Siyiex ka daux nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Poi Luat-luan
19 bKa daux num ra khix tix kao pon ngaix,
pui hun pa vuk vied vuk viang pa deeh hoek khankix yuh Poi Luat-luan.
20 Can cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we hoik hawt ka been,
kieh to kaux tix singax singeh khawm uik.
Pui Li-we yuh son pui pa deeh hoek khawm uik,
mai cao giex kaing li,
khawm to kaux kix pauh ka khawng tan Poi Luat-luan.
21 Can pui Yi-seu-liex pa vuk vied vuk viang pa deeh hoek hoik ih ka khawng tan in khawm uik;
pui an kix pa ot haktiex in,
pa hoik vud tix hu khaing been li tang ceu,
mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex kawx hoik ih ka khawng tan in.
22 Kix gaux rhawm gaux rhi yuh ka Poi Tik-su-saix,
khawm uik yuh poi in been aliah singaix.
Kix hoik naog krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh Vawng Ya-suxc yuh paox grawm tau mai kix,
mai joh jhan kix cox sum nyiex buan Siyiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
