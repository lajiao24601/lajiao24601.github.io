Been khaix hoik singax jhawm saux si pawg
14
1-2 aSimiang Siyiex tawx lox pa mawh lox tat jhawm prawng khaix hoik singax singeh jhawm saux si pawg ka Maw-si.
Singaix pa hoik krai pao singax singeh pui saux an,
pui tag caw tix viex nawh hoek ka cao giex kaing li dee an.
3 Cao giex kaing li tag sang viex lih jhak nawh ka plak praix nyiex bang,
phan hoik yaox grawng pa moi jhawm saux nawh,
4 tag sang tawx lox nhie nawh viex kawn sim ra mu,
pa hoik kieh mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been,
khaox si oe bhawng tix gawng,
mai maox keei raoh tix nyaing,
khawm rao rib im krim tix gawng.
5 Cao giex kaing li tag tawx lox nhie pui pauh ka sim an tix mu pa ot ka piang aw tiex pa hoik kin kix tix sau rawm ot ka daux,
6 khaix an mai nawh baox giex sim an tang mu,
cub daux nham tau mai khawm khaox si oe bhawng,
mai maox keei raoh rawt,
mai rao rib im krim.
7 Nawh tag phruah nham an aliah bawg ka piang pui saux pa hoik krai pao deeh kieh singax singeh,
khaix an mai krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh.
Nawh tag ploi ka sim pa kawn im an,
kieh sim an pu hu plak tiag heue.
8 Pui an caw tix si daux ka kraung ceep tix,
khlao hauk tix khawm uik,
mai heem tix,
khaix an nawh tom tag mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been,
tag chix tix laig ot ka daux nyiex bang,
viang mawh nan,
nawh tag caw tix ran ot ka plak praix nyiex gox cao tix aliah singaix.
9 Yam hoik hoek ka daux aliah singaix,
nawh tag deeh baox khlao hauk kaing tix,
mai hauk jheeh tix,
mai hauk ngai tix khawm can hauk pa koe ka gaw kaux tix khawm uik,
mai baox si daux kraung ceep tix,
mai heem tix,
khaix an,
tom tag sawn mawh pa singax singeh ka daux been heue.
10 Yam hoik hoek ka daux daix singaix,
nawh tag viex kawn yung mhaing ra mu,
pa ang koe dee mat dee ne,
kawn yung siang tix mu,
pa tag naog tix num,
pa ang koe dee mat dee ne,
phawng mien pa hoik klao tix tau mai bee sime loe kongcing,
mai bee sime loe son tix son kongsheng.
11 Cao giex kaing li tag viex nawh mai khawng tan an kix,
hu ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
12 khaix an tan ka kawn yung mhaing tix mu,
mai bee sime loe son tix son kongsheng,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud,
kieh pa tan an kix mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
13 Nawh tag pauh kawn yung an,
ka dee pauh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh.
Nawh tag sang yuh nan,
mawh kheu Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
hoik mawh cie cao giex kaing li,
hoik mawh pa kied singax singeh.
14 Cao giex kaing li tag teei nham Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud tix blah,
mai kraix ka toh yhaok plak dawm pui pa hoik krai pao deeh kieh mawh pa singax singeh an,
mai ka giien ting taix kawn dawm,
giien ting caong khawn dawm.
15 Cao giex kaing li tag hieh bee sime tix blah ot ka daux tawm taix khawn goix tix,
16 cub giien taix khawn dawm tix ka daux bee an,
phruah aliah bawg sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
17 Nawh kawx tag teei bee pa kawn deuk ot ka daux tawm taix tix tix blah,
nham Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud tix blah,
kraix ka yhaok khawn dawm pui pa hoik krai pao mawh pa deeh singax singeh an,
mai ka giien ting taix khawn dawm,
khawm ka giien ting caong khawn dawm.
18 Nawh kawx baox hieh bee pa kawn deuk ot ka daux tawm taix tix khankix ka piang kaing pui an.
Cao giex kaing li tag yuh pui an singax singeh ka krax ceu an sivoe Simiang Siyiex heue.
19 Khaix an cao giex kaing li tag tan Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
yuh been krax pa singax singeh son pui an,
pauh ka khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
20 tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi ka piang khu dee tan.
Cao giex kaing li yuh pui an singax singeh ka krax ceu an,
nawh tom tag singax singeh heue.
21 Phan pui an mawh pui pa hot pa shiah,
mai ang pon liag ka ceu an kix tom nie,
nawh chix tix siao ka kawn yung mhaing tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud,
tan mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh tag viex ngie phawng mien pa hoik klao tau mai bee sime tix kongcing hoek,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
baox tien viex ka bee sime loe son tix son kongsheng,
22 Nawh baox viex si kaux ra mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
si kaux krie hoek,
tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
an tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
23 Yam hoik hoek ka daux daix singaix,
pa mawh singaix pa hoik krai pao nawh mawh pa singax singeh,
nawh tag viex khawng tan an kix hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
tawx ka cao giex kaing li.
24 Cao giex kaing li tag teei ka kawn yung mhaing Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud mai bee sime,
kieh mawh khawng tan pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
25 Nawh tag pauh ka kawn yung an,
teei nham tix blah,
kraix ka piang dawng yhaok khawn dawm pui an,
mai ka piang giien ting taix khawn dawm,
khawm ka piang giien ting caong khawn dawm.
26 Cao giex kaing li tag hieh bee an khankix tix blah ka daux tawm taix khawn goix tix,
27 cub giien taix khawn dawm tix ka daux bee,
phruah nawh aliah bawg ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
28 Nawh tag kraix bee ka piang dawng yhaok khawn dawm pui an,
ka piang giien ting taix khawn dawm,
mai ka piang giien ting caong khawm dawm nawh,
pa mawh ka dee hoik kraix nham Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud.
29 Nawh tag kraix bee pa kawn deuk an,
pa koe daux tawm taix tix ka piang kaing pui an,
nawh tag siao grawng krax in yuh been pa singax singeh son pui an sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
30 Khaix an nawh tag sang tan si kaux tix mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
si kaux krie,
31 kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
an tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
tan tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi.
Cao giex kaing li tag siao grawng krax ceu in yuh been pa singax singeh son pui an ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
32 In mawh son been pa khaw pui pa hoik kham jhawm saux si pawg tix hawt,
mawh kheu ang nawh pon tan ka khawng brie pa khaw tix sang tan son lox tat jhawm prawng pa singax singeh heue.
Been son pa moh nyiex
33 Simiang Siyiex tawx grawng lox tat jhawm prawng son nyiex ma pa moh ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng.
34 Lox tat jhawm prawng in mawh pa siao khaix hoik laig pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux haktiex Cia-nang,
pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex mawh sin ngao cie kix.
35 Pui mawx ang ah,
pa hoik yaox grawng pa hoik moh nyiex ma tix,
tag caw tix krai ka cao giex kaing li.
36 Cao giex kaing li tag tawx lox nhie pui gah can kraung pa koe ka daux nyiex an lih khawm uik,
son ang kraung pa koe ka daux nyiex an sang krai pao tix mawh pa ang singax.
Khaix hoik gah kraung khrai lih,
cao giex kaing li tag laig ka daux nyiex,
37 jhak ka dee koe pa moh.
Phan yaox koe pa klai bawd ang ah,
pa raoh bawd pa haok laux yuh ding nyiex ang ah,
38 nawh tag lih plak praix nyiex,
mai sau jhong nyeep siviex nyiex an aliah singaix.
39 Yam hoik hoek ka daux aliah singaix,
nawh tag baox deeh jhak kah,
phan pa moh an hoik tauh hu,
40 tag tawx lox nhie pui pu plieh simaox pa koe moh an hu,
tik ka dee pa ang singax singeh ka plak praix veng.
41 Khaix an,
nawh tag sang tawx lox nhie pui kheet tix tik ka phawng tiex pa koe ka ding nyiex an,
tik ka dee pa ang singax singeh plak praix veng.
42 Ka dee hoik pu plieh simaox hu khaing dee an,
tag sang deeh sau simaox tang mu kah,
teei phawng tiex cox deeh kraix mhawm ka heue.
43 Khaix hoik pu plieh simaox,
kheet tix tik phawng tiex lih,
deeh kraix ding mhawm,
phan nyiex an ca baox kawn moh,
44 cao giex kaing li tag sang hu lam lia,
phan moh an hoik tauh hu,
nyiex an tag mawh pa ang singax singeh.
45 Tag sang yieh ka nyiex an,
simaox,
khaox rong,
phawng tiex mai gah tix tik plak praix veng ka dee pa ang singax singeh.
46 Ka daux bawg yam pa hoik nyeep sau ka jhong siviex,
pui mawx ang ah phan laig ka daux,
pui an tag mawh pa ang singax tom hoek plak pon bo.
47 Pui mawx ang ah pa it ka daux nyiex an,
phan ang mawh nan ih nyaux ka daux,
tag caw tix si daux ka kraung ceep tix heue.
48 Khaix hoik deeh kraix ka nyiex an mhawm,
cao giex kaing li tag deeh hoek tix jhak,
phan ding nyiex an ang lai baox deeh moh,
nawh tag krai pao nyiex an mawh pa singax singeh,
mawh kheu hoik grai pa moh an.
49 Nawh tag sang viex sim ra mu,
khaox si oe bhawng tix gawng,
maox keei pa raoh rawt tix nyaing,
khawm rao rib im krim tix gawng,
son sang yuh been nyiex an singax.
50 Nawh tag pauh sim an tix mu ka piang aw tiex,
pa hoik kin tix sau rawm naog kah,
51 khaix an teei khaox si oe bhawng mai rao rib im krim,
maox keei pa raoh rawt,
khawm sim pa kawn im an tang mu,
mai teei nham pa hoik cub kix ka daux pan,
mai phruah ka nyiex aliah bawg.
52 Nawh tag teei nham sim,
rawm im,
sim pa im mai khaox si oe bhawng,
rao rib im krim,
khawm keei raoh rawt,
yuh son nyiex an ka been pa singax singeh.
53 Khaix an nawh tag deeh ploi sim pa kawn im an,
kieh nawh deeh pu hu plak tiag.
Nawh siao ka grawng krax in,
yuh been pa singax singeh son nyiex an,
mai nyiex an tag mawh pa singax singeh heue.
54 In plak piang mawh grawng been son jhawm saux si pawg,
55-56 pa koe saux muad ka hak,
mai saux dao,
mai saux leem,
khawm lox tat jhawm prawng son pa moh kraung ceep mai ka nyiex ma ku ceu.
57 Lox tat jhawm prawng in kix tag kieh mawh mhai pa tat dut ka son ceu pa singax singeh mai pa ang singax singeh khankix heue.
