Siyiex sang ciyang ka Shui-li-yax mai Yi-seu-liex
17
aPlak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Ta-ma-see-keux.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ta-ma-see-keux tag ang lai baox mawh veng tix mu;
tag sang tieh tix lih mawh pum tiex pa riem.
2 Can veng Shui-li-yax khawm uik tag sang nhik sang riem leeing ju,b
mai lih mawh dee ot yung moi,
mai ang koe pui pa pon grheu kix hu.
3 Yi-seu-liex tag ang lai koe ceu pa deuh rhawm tix;
Ta-ma-see-keux tag ang lai koe riang ka daux pa sum tix kaux cong.
Pui Shui-li-yax pa deuk khankix tag sang kham krax kaig krax tua awm pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Bawg yam dex sang hoek,
min plak ngai Yi-seu-liex tag sang naox tix;
nawh tag sang tieh tix lih hot khaing daux krax pa mag mi.
5 Yi-seu-liex tag sang awm kaung ma pa hoik vawg pui tix dauh,
tag sang riem awm kaung ma pa koe daux ngawd Li-fa-ying pa khraox vawg pui yuh nan.
6 Pui Yi-seu-liex mawh ngie pui pa tux pa kawn deuk khaing;
kix jiet awm khaox sime pa hoik pieh pui yuh nan,
ka piang rao khaox deuk ngie plix sime ka ra loe mu khaing,
kak khaox plak greem deuk ngie plix pon phuan mu khaing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
7 Yam hoek bawg yam an,
kawn pui tag sang kling tix deeh ka cao pa phan pa yuh tix,
tag sang khiox khaw Siyiex pa singax singeh Yi-seu-liex taoh tix.
8 Kix ang lai baox deeh lai een rhawm tix ka khu pa hoik teei pui taix tix yuh kah,
mai ang lai yum rhawm ka ceu pa hoik yuh tix,
pa mawh riang rong siyiex bun Ya-seu-la an mai ka khu pa gleeing nyawng ka heue.
9 Yam hoik hoek singaix an,
veng pa hoik sum kieh kaux cong tag sang prah ghram kah,
mai lih mawh dee riem,
awm veng hoik tik pui Si-we mai pui Ya-maw-lixc yam to tix ka plak sivoe ngai pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nan heue.
10 Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex hoik piawm ka Siyiex pa taoh pex an,
nawh hoik awm simaox pa kaux cong mai kia ngien ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik tieh tix sang krub ka riang rao pa krub pui tang meung khankix,
mai hoik paih ka pa ah pex mawh si peem tai pa singax singeh.
11 Viang pa van pa sum pex hoik lih tix kuah,
hoik lih tix pruh tai ka daux tix ngaix kawx,
mai pex ang doh yaox tix teei to.
Pex ca koe ngie krax laux rhawm deuk rhawm khaing heue.
Hoik pon pex ka meung pa koeng
12 Pui pa ting riang ting rao yaog tix kaoh,
mai awm lox pa luah rawm ting pa puk pak,
awm rawm ting pa chun tix.
13 Pui pa ting riang ting rao hoek tix tud rhim,
mai hoik awm puk pak rawm ting yuh nan;
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik tieb tie ka kix,
mai kieh kix dak tix deeh,
awm phawng tiex pa koe daux ngawd pa phlu bhaung yuh nan,
awm kam nghox ca pa phlu bhaung klia yuh nan.
14 Pon sawm kix hoik kieh pui koe krax pa lhat graung,
ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
ang lai yaox tid tix.
In hoik mawh pa si pup pui pa tud rhim ka pa dug dig haktiex ex heue.
