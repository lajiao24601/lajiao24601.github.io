Ne raw ka Thai-eux
26
aYam vuk vied vuk viang yix ka daux kao tix num haok khix tix ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
2 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
pui pa paung ot ka veng Thai-eux gaux rhawm kok yo ah nin:
‘Ha ha,
Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik laux leeing heue!
Nawh hoik grai riang rao pa yuh lai gee heue!
Nawh ang lai baox mawh pa yuh krax lah krax cha heue!’
3 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
veng Thai-eux eue,
aux tag sang tieb tie ka maix heue!
Aux tag sang khrawm ka meung piang haktiex hoek tix puing ka maix awm rawm pa pu pak yuh nan.
4 Kix tag sang yuh laux ka guh veng maix,
mai yieh laux ka tab maix.
Aux kawx tag sang pih tix tik ka can phawng tiex khawm uik,
deuh ngie simaox pa plang khankix khaing.
5 Thai-eux tag sang ot nong nong ka sigreung rawm sigrit sigra;
pui rub kax tag sang hok rub kax piang klawn tiex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin.
Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang hoek tix tud rhim ka Thai-eux;
6 kix tag sang teei vaig pauh ka pui pa paung ot ka hawt siviex veng.
Yuh nin,
Thai-eux tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
7 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang kok ka Vawng pa blawn ting khaing paox tix ka piang haktiex,
pa mawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng hoek puing ka Thai-eux.
Nawh thung pui naing pa haoh hun hoek khaing plak laong,
pa mawh brung naing,
ling law naing,
pui naing pa brug brung khankix.
8 Pui pa paung ot ka hawt siviex veng hoik pauh yum khawm uik.
Pui koeng tag sang hoek tix kaong daux rawng,
pum tiex,
mai dia dieb tie naok puing ka maix.
9 Kix tag sang teei khaox dung ting pa yieh ka veng chun guh veng laux kah,
mai teei gawng rhiem yieh tab laux kah.
10 Brung kaing mheen pa to tix hu,
hoik kieh phawng tiex nyaox nyia chu;
pui naing brug brung kok yo,
mai lox luah ling law hoik yung yang yuh guh veng.
Kix preeng tix laig ka daux siviex veng,
awm pa preeng pui tix laig ka dee ang pui koe yuh nan.
11 “Pui naing pa brug brung preeng tix laig ka daux laih ting laih iak maix,
mai teei vaig pauh ka pui pa paung ot haktiex an.
Rong pa kaux cong nyiex vad maix pui tag sang cot goh piang tiex.
12 Pui koeng tag sang tud ka sin ngao kraung khrai pa coh cai lai gee maix,
cot goh veng maix,
yuh laux ka nyiex pa mhawm maix,
mai teei loe simaox,
rong khaox tik laig daux rawm sigrit sigra khawm uik.
13 bAux tag ang lai kieh maix rhax lox rhax,
ang lai kieh maix tauh ka lox ting jung.
14 Aux tag sang kieh maix tieh tix lih mawh simaox pa plang,
mawh ngie pa tawx pui rub kax hok rub ka piang.
Veng in ang lai pon cox sum.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
15 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka Thai-eux yuh nin:
“Yam pon kuang pui ka maix,
tag sang pauh ka kawn pui yum;
pui pa paung ot ka hawt rawm sigrit sigra khankix yaoh yee kheu mhong tix lox riag kix.
16 cSimiang ka piang rawm sigrit sigra khankix tag sang lih hu khaing dang ngeung ngawm tix khawm uik,
mai peeik ka kraung dha simiang mai kraung pa koe breeh tai,
mai si yaung si kua ngawm ka piang tiex.
Krax lawng maix hoik kieh kix si yaung ang jud ang jai.
17 Kix tag sang rhax lox yiiem doe son maix ah nin:
Veng pa ao min hoik laux leeing heue!
Reu cie nawh pa hu piang rawm khankix hoik pih tix tik khawm uik.
Pui cie nawh hoik sax een tix kua ka piang rawm,
mai ghlat ka pui pa paung ot hawt rawm sigrit sigra.
18 Viang mawh nan,
singaix pa laux leeing nawh,
klawn tiex hoik si yaung si kua,
pui pa ot ka piang klawn tiex tag pru praoh yaoh yee kheu krax pa yuh nin heue.”
19 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh maix riem,
awm veng pa ang pui lai koe ka yuh nan.
Aux tag sang teei rawm sigrit sigra pa kied raux kheem ka maix.
20 Aux tag sang cot maix laig daux meung yum,
mai kieh maix ot tau mai pui yum.
Aux tag sang laung maix ka daux meung yum,
mai ot tau mai pui yum ka daux veng riem an leeing ju.
Maix ang doh lai pon tix deeh hoek ka piang haktiex;
maix ang doh lai baox koe pui.
21 dAux tag sang kieh maix si pup krax pa laux pa kied lhat,
mai ang lai baox koe maix.
Tee koe pui pa sang sawg ka maix,
viang mawh nan,
ang doh lai yaox ka maix.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
