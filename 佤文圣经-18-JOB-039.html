39
Maix tawng ka bawg yam sang keh pex tiag lie?
Maix yaox ka yam keh cak tiag yuh ka mawx lie?
2 Kix nghe koe miex glang lie?
Kix sang keh bawg yam mawx maix tawng ka lie?
3 Maix tawng nyah tix ngawm yam mawx,
mai sang keh kawn lie?
4 Kawn kix ting si nyek si nyek ka daux tiag,
mai to tix hu,
ang lai baox deeh hoek.
5 Pui mawx ploi la tiag hu?
Pui mawx yah ka maox pa phrawk ka kix?
6 Aux tawx daux tiag daux tu mawh dee ot kix,
kieh kix ot ka daux haktiex koe kih.
7 Lox riag kix singai ot khaing veng,
ang nghiet ka lox pui pa hawt grheu.
8 Gawng daux tiag mawh dee liang kix ot;
kix yaox tix sawm rib klai ka dee an heue.
 
9 Moi tiag cu tix yuh kaing son maix lie?
Nawh cu tix it laux lee tix ka praok dawng maix lie?
10 Maix pon teei maox reu nawh thai kaung,
phan ang mawh nan,
kieh nawh bluh pheu ka daux kaung lie?
11 Maix pon een rhawm tix ka riang rao nawh,
tawx kaing pa cien ka nawh lie?
12 Maix pon yum rhawm kieh nawh viex pa pon khrawm maix ing ka nyiex,
khrawm nghox koi son maix khaing daux glauh pauh nghox lie?
 
13 Sim ka-la-uk pon yeeb preeik tix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang pon pu awm klang kax yuh nan.
14 Sim ka-la-uk tawm ka piang tiex,
mai kieh tawm tix si u ka bhawm pa si aoh tiex.
15 Nawh ang tawng caong pui chix dig max,
phan ang mawh nan,
to sat hoik dig max.
16 Nawh kieh kawn tix awm pa ang mawh pa keh khaing to kaux tix yuh nan,
nawh kawx ang jhak simi ka daux kaing yuh tix,
mawh ang mawh krax giex krax going.
17 Mawh kheu aux kieh nawh daox aw,
ang tawx ping nyia cuyi ka nawh.
18 Viang mawh nan,
yam yaog nawh tix to,
nawh tom nyiah ka brung pa brax to mai ka pui pa brug heue.
 
19 Yaw-paw eue,
mawh maix pa kieh brung mhawm kluing yuh nin lie?
Mawh maix pa tawx hauk nawh an vuk vied tix chuh lie?
20 Mawh maix pa kieh nawh tiied tix awm sido yuh nan,
kieh kix plih bhawm tix mai kieh pui lhat ka lie?
21 Kix gaux rhawm to tix klieh ka sigreung ngawd;
kix koe siawp sibrawm mai to hu laig dee bang naing.
22 Kix ang tawng krax lhat mawh ceu pa tix;
vaig gawn kawx ang pon kieh kix deeh plak khaix.
23 Kraung naing pa viex pui naing brug brung luah;
rao pliah mai pliah riang ka daux pa riang singaix.
24 Brung naing si yaung si kua,
mai yaog tix to hu plak sivoe ngai;
yam luah teet tawng,
kix tom ang lai cu tix kaog.
25 Yam mhong luah teet tawng ku bawg,
kix tom bliah bhawm tix lih;
kix pon chix ngheut bhawm si oe puing naing khaing dee singai,
pon chix mhong nang ka lox kok yo simiang naing heue.
 
26 Klang ting nih preeik tix pu hu plak caong ma seh;
in mawh maix pa gau nawh lie?
27 Klang teei ia yuh mheem tix ot ka piang to rang;
in mawh pa yuh ka daux lox pa tawx maix lie?
28 Nawh laux lee tix ot ka yoeh rang,
kieh rang simaox pa ot dee lhaong mawh tab pa kaux.
29 Nawh kok jhak dee singai dee dex khaing dee an,
sawg tix pauh giex ka to tiag.
30 aKoe to kaux yum pui dee mawx klang preng khrawm paox tix dee an.
Kawn klang kawx hoek tix yawt ka nham heue.
