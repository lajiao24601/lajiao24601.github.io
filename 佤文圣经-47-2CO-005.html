5
Ex hoik tawng,
phan nyiex gox bang haktiex pa hoik ot ex daux in yieh tix,
Siyiex tag sang tawx ex ot ka daux nyiex piang rao ma,
pa mawh nyiex pa hoek nawh tix sum,
pa koe leeing ju leeing phan.
2 Yam in ex hoik ngia,
mai simie tix laig ot ka nyiex pa koe ka piang rao ma an,
awm hoik ceep ka kraung ceep yuh nan;
3 khaix hoik ceep kah,
ex ang lai yaox tix ot ka kaong.
4 Ex ot ka daux nyiex gox bang pa koe ka piang haktiex in,
hoik awm ngia ex ka klawm tix kraung cien yuh nan.
In ang mawh krai ex sang peeik ka to kaux nex nham pa koe ka piang haktiex,
mai hoik mawh son sang kieh ex ceep ka to kaux nex nham pa dex dawt mai cie rao ma,
son sang kieh ju pon tang son pa chix laux chix leeing an.
5 Siyiex hoek tix rien khre ex khaw rhawm ka daux pa tieh loh tix;
nawh teei tix tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ka ex,
mai kieh mawh pa tang kaux cong son ex heue.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
ex hoik koe riang rao pa grum naog jheeng jheeng.
Ex hoik tawng,
phan ex ca kawn ot ka daux to kaux nex nham in,
ex tag sang singai khaing dee hoik rien khre Simiang Ye-sux ex ot.
7 Mawh kheu hoik een ex rhawm tix ka krax yum rhawm ka daux pa im ju tix,
mai ang mawh ka ceu pa chix yaox ngai ex.
8 Ex koe riang rao pa grum naog,
mai cu tix lih hu khaing to kaux nex nham piang haktiex in,
paung ot mai Simiang Ye-sux.
9 Ceu pa blawn ting ceu mawh,
mawh pa ot ka daux to kaux nex nham piang haktiex in ang ah,
mawh to kaux nex nham pa dex dawt mai cie khuan ang ah,
ex tag caw tix mawh pa khaw rhawm Simiang Ye-sux khawm uik.
10 aMawh kheu tag caw ex tix cung ka plak sivoe ngai Khrit khawm uik,
mai kham krax pa prawng ca nawh.
Pui ku kaux tag sang hawt tix kham krax pa pawk bix awm kaing ceu pa yuh nex nham tix,
mai kaing ceu pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm yuh tix heue.
Khaw caw mai Siyiex ka daux Khrit
11 Kheu hoik tawng ex grawng sang lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
ex tom teei grawng in sidiex gau pui.
Siyiex ang tawng ka ex tom sidawng;
aux kawx simie pex tawng tix ka daux rhawm rhi heue.
12 Ex ang mawh keud tix sang krai to kaux tix ka pex,
mai hoik mawh son sang kieh pex tawng tix koe grawh grawng plak ngai son ex,
son sang koe lox pex tix pawk ka pui pa jhak plak praix,
pa ang een pui mhawm jhawm koe ceu.
13 Phan ex yhot tete,
an hoik mawh kheu pa yuh son Siyiex;
phan dieb dong ex haing,
an hoik mawh pa yuh son pex.
14 Krax muh Khrit giex gang ka ex,
mawh kheu hoik tawng ex sidawng,
pui tix kaux pa yum son pui hun,
in kawx hoik mawh pui hun pa yum khawm uik.
15 Nawh yum son pui hun,
mawh son sang kieh pui pa im khankix ang lai baox dee im son to kaux tix,
hoik mawh pa im son Khrit,
pa hoik yum mai dee kaoh im son kix an heue.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
ex tom ang lai teei pa khaw mai diag tong kawn pui hoek tix tiag kah.
Viang ex hoik teei diag tong kawn pui hoek tix jhak ka Khrit,
yam in ex ang lai baox yuh nin.
17 Mawh pui mawx ang ah,
phan hoik koe ju Khrit,
tag sang mawh pui pa khraox;
pa prim hoik luan,
pa khraox hoik lih hoek.
18 In kix hoik mawh kaing pa yuh Siyiex khawm uik;
nawh kieh ex pon tix mhawm tau mai Siyiex ka daux Khrit,
mai baox tawx ara pa sidiex gum lox buan pa kieh pui khaw caw paox ka ex.
19 Gum lox buan pa sidiex ex mawh:
Siyiex hoik yuh krax pa si doe tix mhawm mai kawn pui ka daux Khrit.b
Nawh ang si kaw ka krax pa lud loe kix,
khaix an teei tix tawx gum lox pa mhawm nawh tau mai kawn pui ka ex heue.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
ex hoik mawh kawn cau pa ting Khrit.
Siyiex hoik tawx ex sidiex gau ka pex yuh nin:
ex khiox khaw pex tang Khrit,
kieh Siyiex kieh pex mhawm mai nawh.
21 Khrit mawh pa ang koe phi map,
viang mawh nan,
kheu hoik yuh son ex,
Siyiex kieh nawh klawm ka phi map ex,
mai kieh ex pon si doe tix khaw caw mai Siyiex ka daux nawh heue.
