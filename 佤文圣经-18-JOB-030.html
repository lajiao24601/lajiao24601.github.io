30
Viang mawh nan,
daux in singaix,
pui pa kawn lheu nhum ju khaing aux nyiah ka aux;
keeing kix hoik dawg mawh pui pa ang aux een koe ceu;
aux kawx ang kieh kix lai laig tix ot ka daux phung sox,
pa kia ngien ka yung son aux.
2 Kix mawh pui pa awn riang beuh meung;
kix ang koe dee siao ka aux pie ceeix kawx.
3 Kix hot sibhawm cai rhawm,
mai kied kroh krawx,
yam pon sawm kix kaong tix ih ka riah khaox ka daux tiag daux tu.
4 Kix reeig tix sau taux lhax ka daux jheeh tix ka daux pak rib,
pie riah khaox song kawx teei mawh priex preem.
5 Pui haoh pui hun kok yo leue ka kix,
awm leue ka pui tud rhim yuh nan.
6 Kix tom mox tix ot ngie ka daux ngawd dee raux khaing,
paung ot ka daux tiex mai ka daux dawh rang.
7 Kix ot ka daux tiag mai graox awm to sat,
kix su sied paox tix ot ka daux pak rib.
8 Kix mawh cub kawn praix pa brai meung,
pui si vied ka kix,
mai grheu kix lih khaing haktiex heue.
 
9 Ka daux in singaix,
pui in kix hoek tix nyiah ka aux;
ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
aux mawh pa nyiah si briem.
10 Kix koeng ka aux,
mai to tix hu singai khaing aux;
kix nieb pon baig rawm mia tix ka ngai aux.
11 Siyiex kieh aux awn riang ang koe cao teem,
kix tom sau riang tix map nai ka aux.
12 Pui pa vao pa meug in kix yaog kaing tix puing ka aux;
kix kieh aux yaog tix to;
kix hoik rien khre tix,
nghoik tix sang puing ka aux;
13 kix tat dut ka krax pa sang deeh aux,
mai rien tix sang yuh aux laux leeing;
ang koe pui pa tieb tiea ka kix.
14 Kix hoik pon yieh laux ka tab pa kuan aux,
gut ka piang to kaux aux tom cien.
15 Krax lhat krax graung lauh aux goh;
min plak ngai aux hoik grai hu hawt khaix bhaung,
krax mag mi hoik luan hu awm pai awm heue.
 
16 Yam in aux ang lai singai khaing krax yum;
krax kha rhawm kawn ca giex ka aux tom kaux;
17 yam pon sawm si-ang si niag aux khawm gaw hoik saux si klua,
krax saux krax ngia pa kin kiied ka aux ang jud ang jai.
18 Siyiex mad kaux ka ngawg sibex aux,
baox klia kaux ka kraung ceep aux.
19 Nawh blat aux ot ka daux glaox;
aux ang lai tang siyox khaing phawng nyaox tiex glaox khankix heue.
 
20 Siyiex eue,
aux sang khiox khaw ka maix,
mai maix ang nghiet;
aux sang rawg buan ka maix,
maix kawx ang simi.
21 Maix tieh loh rhawm rhi tix khaing aux,
mai kieh aux kha rhawm tix;
maix teei taix pa ting siawp sibrawm tix yeen yia ka aux.
22 Maix kieh bhaung ting phlu aux hu;
maix si vun tix tik aux ka daux bhaung ting.
23 Aux hoik tawng maix sang kieh aux hoek krax yum,
sang kieh aux hoek ka dee pa khaw pui yum tix sang hu.
24 Yuh ka mawx maix lai puing ka pui pa iak riang lie?
Nawh sang kok ngie ka krax pa caox rhawm maix khaing.
25 Khex ang aux caw tix yiiem tau mai pui pa yaox krax kha rhawm lie?
Khex ang aux caw tix laux rhawm son pui hot pui sibhawm lie?
26 Aux simie tix pon krax mhawm ot mhawm koe,
viang mawh nan,
mai aux yaox krax laux krax leeing;
aux krox jun ka pa riang,
mai aux si pup krax pa faik.
27 Aux hoik krawx kheu kha rhawm laux rhawm tix;
leeing ngaix leeing sawm aux ot ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi.
28 Aux lai ling ka daux krax pa laux rhawm laux rhi,
mai ang yaox pa riang singaix;
aux cung ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai khiox khaw krax teem krax coi.
29 Aux awm sox tiag pa riag yo;
aux awm sim ka-la-uk pa yiiem doe.
30 Hak nex aux hoik tieh tix lih lung mai cawd khrum;
si-ang aux hoik gleed kheu kied si aoh tix.
31 Aux hoik sax mhong ka lox rhax krax gaux rhawm,
singaix yam in mai mhong ka lox pa yiiem grai pui heue.
