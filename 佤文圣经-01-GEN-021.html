Grawng keh Yi-sha
21
Simiang Siyiex hawt awm lox pa ap tix kia ngien ka Sa-la.
2 aNawh koe kaux,
yam hoik kuad Ya-paw-la-hang keh nawh cie kawn simex tix kaux;
kawn nyawm an keh bawg yam pa ciak Siyiex tete.
3 Ya-paw-la-hang sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-sha.
4 bYa-paw-la-hang hawt ka been pa deuh Siyiex,
khaix hoik keh Yi-sha ka daux daix singaix kieh nawh Pat-hak.
5 Yam keh Yi-sha,
Ya-paw-la-hang hoik koe ju tix yieh num.
6 Sa-la ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik kieh aux gaux rhawm tix nyiah;
pui pa mhong kaing in kawx gaux rhawm nyiah tau mai aux heue.”
7 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Mawh pui mawx pa dawg ah ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Sa-la sang tawx kawn tix peux lie?
Viang mawh nan,
hoik mawh bawg yam kuad ju nawh,
aux khex keh nawh cie kawn simex tix kaux heue.”
8 Kawn nyawm hoik ting hu si nyek si nyek,
singaix hoik pluat nawh peux,
Ya-paw-la-hang yuh poi khan kheu priex preem heue.
Grheu ka Sa-cia mai Yi-seu-ma-lix
9 Koe tix ngaix,
kawn simex Ya-paw-la-hang pa kaux Yi-seu-ma-lix pa keh bun siliex Ai-cix pa kaux Sa-cia son nawh,
klieh tau mai Yi-shac kawn simex Sa-lad.
10 eSa-la yaox ka kiex,
tom ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Maix grheu bun siliex an mai kawn simex nawh lih hu.
Kawn simex bun in ang pon tix rom tix plud sin ngao tau mai kawn simex aux Yi-sha.”
11 Kaing in kied kieh Ya-paw-la-hang suk sag rhawm,
mawh kheu Yi-seu-ma-lix mawh nang kawn simex nawh.
12 fViang mawh nan,
Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Paw lai yuh maix khi rhawm ka kawn simex in mai bun siliex Sa-cia;
yuh hawt awm grawng pa krai Sa-la ka maix,
mawh kheu nan maix tag sang pon doeh ceu cad awm gum lox pa hoik deuh aux khaing Yi-sha.
13 Phan mawh kawn simex bun siliex in,
aux sang tawx kawn simex kawn bun ka nawh tom nie,
kieh kix mawh meung ting tix mu,
mawh kheu mawh nawh kawn simex maix nang heue.”
14 Ka daux ra singaix plak pon ngawb cao cao,
Ya-paw-la-hang teei priex preem mai rawm daux bau hak tawx ka Sa-cia,
sau kawn nyawm ka piang blawg Sa-cia,
nhie nawh hu.
Sa-cia lih khaing tan,
mai lud krax daux tiag Piie-see-pa.
15 Rawm nyaux hoik uik,
mai nawh deuh kawn nyawm greem yeem khaox,
16 nawhg to tix hu ngawm khaing kawn nyawm been glang hu tom te.
Nawh tang dee ah nin:
“Aux ang pon yaox ka sang yum kawn simex tix.
Nawh ngawm tan,
mai ploi lox yiiem tix ting heue.”
17 Siyiex mhong ka lox yiiem kawn nyawm an,
kawn cau Siyiex kaoh khaing rao ma lih si kah mai Sa-cia ah nin:
“Sa-cia eue,
yuh ka mawx lai laux rhawm lie?
Paw lhat graung,
Siyiex hoik mhong ka lox yiiem kawn nyawm an.
18 Kaoh heue,
teei tix klien ka kawn nyawm,
kieh nawh nyawm rhawm;
aux sang kieh doeh ceu cad nawh mawh meung ting tix mu.”
19 Khaix an,
Siyiex paoh ngai Sa-cia riang,
kieh nawh yaox dung rawm tix mu;
nawh hoek ka praok dung,
mai kin rawm naog ka daux bau hak,
tawx kawn nyawm nyaux.
20 Siyiex ot tau mai kawn nyawm;
kawn nyawm an ting hu si nyek si nyek,
mai ot ka daux tiang haktiex Pa-lang,
lih mawh pui pa chix jaw.
21 Miex nawh teei nawh moeng kawn bun Ai-cix tix kaux heue.
Ya-paw-la-hang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Ya-pi-mi-leux
22 hYam an,
Ya-pi-mi-leux mai simiang naing nawh Fe-keux hoek ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix yuh kaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
Siyiex ot tau mai maix.
23 Mawh kheu nan,
maix caw tix ah lox pa tiex tu sivoe ngai Siyiex,
paw vi pie lhien ka aux,
kawn simex kawn bun aux,
mai doeh ceu cad aux.
Yuh aux buan son ka maix yuh ka mawx,
maix caw tix yuh buan son ka aux khawm ka meung haktiex meung pa ot maix ka in yuh nan heue.”
24 Ya-paw-la-hang pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux cu tix tiex tu ka heue.”
25 Mawh kheu grawng siliex Ya-pi-mi-leux cax dung rawm tix mu,
Ya-paw-la-hang tom tieb tie ka Ya-pi-mi-leux.
26 Ya-pi-mi-leux ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng mawh pui mawx pa yuh kaing yuh nin.
Maix ka ang tie sax krai aux mhong ka kaing in,
in tag mawh kaing pa mhong aux daux tix bawg khaing.”
27 Kheu nan Ya-paw-la-hang tawx phung moi phung yung tix blah ka Ya-pi-mi-leux,
kiex ra kaux tom yuh sijax mukheu.
28 Ya-paw-la-hang teei kawn yung siang aliah mu deuh ot tix plak.
29 Ya-pi-mi-leux chawk nawh:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh nin lie?”
30 Ya-paw-la-hang pawk lox:
“Khiox khaw maix rieb ka kawn yung siang in aliah mu,
mawh kham sakse hoik cu maix dung in mawh pa kaong aux.”
31 Yuh nin,
haktiex an tom kok mawh Piie-see-pa,i
mawh kheu kiex ra kaux tiex tu paox tix tan.
32 Khaix hoik ah kiex lox sijax mukheu ka Piie-see-pa,
Ya-pi-mi-leux ing tau mai Fe-keux ka haktiex Fe-li-see.
33 Ya-paw-la-hang tom sum khaox si glai tix gawng ka Piie-see-pa,
mai ot tan krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex leeing ju leeing phan.
34 Ya-paw-la-hang kied rheung ot ka haktiex Fe-li-see heue.
