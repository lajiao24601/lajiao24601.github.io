Mawh mawx pa blawn ting
(Lai Ma-kheu 9.33–37; Lai Lu-cia 9.46–48)
18
aYam an,
pui gau hoek chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Ka daux meung rao ma mawh mawx pa blawn ting lie?”
2 Ye-sux kok kawn nyawm tix kaux hoek,
mai kieh nawh cung ka si nax kix,
3 bmai ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tete,
lheu khaing kling pex tix deeh tieh tix mawh kawn nyawm yuh nin,
pex ang doh pon tix lih mawh pui cie meung rao ma heue.
4 Pa tiiem rhawm awm kawn nyawm yuh nin,
ka daux meung rao ma tag sang mawh pa blawn ting.
5 Pa rieb ka kawn nyawm in tix kaux son aux,
hoik mawh rieb ka aux.”
Nyaw pui yuh phi map
(Lai Ma-kheu 9.42–48; Lai Lu-cia 17.1–2)
6 “Pui mawx ang ah pa kieh kawn Khrit pa iak khaing paox tix tix kaux tik vud ka aux,
ang lai lheu khaing teei nawh simaox laing pa ting phrawk ot ka ngawg nawh,
mai kieh nawh yum greem rawm sigrit sigra.
7 Ka piang haktiex in lai chix koe kaing ceu pa tik vud ka aux,
mawh pa kied laux rhawm heue!
Kaing ceu pa yuh nin rug chix lih tix koe,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa kieh kaing ceu pa yuh nin tag sang pon krax laux leeing heue!
8 c“Phan taix maix tix khawn,
phan ang mawh nan,
caong maix tix khawn kieh maix yuh phi map,
kid tix tik heue.
Cud taix caong maix mai pon ka ju leeing ju,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing grum taix caong maix khaing sang laig maix ka ngu pa ang chix yeet leeing ju tom nie.
9 dPhan ngai maix tix khawn kieh maix yuh phi map,
koik lih heue.
Koe ngai maix tix khawn mai pon ka ju leeing ju,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing grum ngai maix khaing sang laig ka daux ngu Maw-na-rhok heue.”
Phek tiiem ka yung pa lud krax
(Lai Lu-cia 15.3–7)
10 “Pex tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
ang pon tix een pui pa iak khankix ang koe ceu.
Aux krai ka pex,
ka daux meung rao ma,
kawn cau Siyiex kix cung tix kia ngien ka plak sivoe ngai keeing aux Siyiex jheeng jheeng heue.e
12 “Pex keud tix yaox,
phan koe pui tix kaux kaux koe yung tix yieh mu,
ka daux kix hoik grai tix mu,
khex ang nawh prah tix si dim dim mu an ot ka daux tiag piang gawng,
mai hu sawg ka pa grai an tix mu lie?
13 Aux krai ka pex,
yam hoik yaox nawh yung pa grai an,
mai nawh kied sidaing gaux rhawm tete,
kawn lheu gaux rhawm khaing pa koe tix dim dim an kix pa ang bax grai heue!
14 Awm an,
keeing pex Siyiexf meung rao ma ang rhawm kieh pui pa iak khaing paox tix grai tix kaux kawx heue.”
Pa yuh son pux aik pa lud loe
15 h“Phan pux aik maix phit lud ka maix,g
maix chix tix hu ka nawh,
mai jhi grawng pa lud loe nawh;
mawh ngie chix tix yuh nin yam ot maix mai nawh nong nong khaing.
Phan nawh nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau maix,
maix tag pon ka pux aik maix.
16 iPhan nawh ang nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau maix,
maix kok pui tang tix ra kaux hu tau mai maix;
yuh nin gum lox pa ne raw ku gum,
koe pui ra loe kaux chix tix mawh cao pa kham sakse.
17 Phan nawh ca kawn ang nghiet,
tag chix tix krai kaing in sidawng ka ceu cao.
Phan nawh ca kawn ang nghiet ka lox ceu cao nang,
maix tag chix tix kia nawh awm pui tang mai pui koh keen heue.”
Pa ang cu mai pa cu
18 j“Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
ceu pa ang pex cu pui yuh ka piang haktiex,
ka piang rao ma kawx tag ang cu yuh nang;
ceu pa cu pex pui yuh ka piang haktiex,
ka piang rao ma kawx tag sang cu yuh nang heue.
19 “Aux baox krai ka pex,
pex ot ka piang haktiex,
phan koe pui ra kaux pa ih rhawm paox tix khiox khaw,
khiox khaw ceu pa tix ang ah,
keeing aux Siyiex piang rao ma tag sang yuh son nawh.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
can pui ra loe kaux pa khrawm paox tix ot tau mai yuh ka daux ceu kaux aux,
aux tag sang ot ka si nax kix heue.”
Phek tiiem siliex pa caox pa ang pluat ploi ka pui
21 kYam an Pi-teux hoek tix chawk ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
pux aik aux phit lud ka aux,
aux sang khaw tix pluat ploi ka nawh miex bawg lie?
Aliah bawg kub lie?”
22 mYe-sux ah nin:
“Ang mawh aliah bawg,
mawh atigliah aliah bawg heue.l
23 Mawh kheu meung rao ma awm lox lao been plak greem yuh nin:
koe simiang tix kaux sang si kaw mau mai siliex tix.
24 Yam jauh nawh tix si kaw ka mau,
koe pui viex siliex pa ceuk ka nawh ra loe mheen mau luah hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
25 Mawh kheu pui in ang koe mau tix vied ka ceuk tix,
simiang tom tawx lox coh nawh mawh siliex cawng,
khawm moeng kawn nawh,
khawm can pa koe nawh khawm uik coh kah,
son sang vied ka ceuk tix.
26 Siliex an grux ngawng tix krub ka plak sivoe ngai simiang,
mai khiox khaw ah nin:
‘Va rhawm ka aux liak!
Aux tag sang vied ka ceuk tix sidawng khawm uik.’
27 Simiang caox rhawm caox rhi ka nawh,
mai ang lai kieh nawh vied,
khaix an ploi nawh lih heue.
28 “Khaix hoik lih siliex an,
nawh si pup ka paox tix pa yuh kiex kaing tau.
Paox nawh in ceuk ka mau luah nawh tix ra loe blah,
nawh giex ka paox tix in,
mai jhawp ghok nawh ah nin:
‘Tawx mau pa ceuk maix ka aux heue!’
29 Paox nawh grux ngawng tix mai khiox khaw ka nawh ah nin:
‘Va rhawm ka aux heue,
aux tag sang vied sidawng ka maix heue!’
30 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang cu,
mai tieh tix laung nawh daux thawng,
krox jun nawh vied mau sidawng.
31 Paox nawh tang kaux khankix yaox grawng kaing pa yuh nawh kied song rhawm kah;
kix hu ka simiang,
mai krai grawng kaing in sidawng ka simiang.
32 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang kok siliex an hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix siliex pa caox in,
mawh kheu hoik khiox khaw maix ka aux,
aux tom ang lai kieh maix vied ka ceuk tix khawm uik,
33 maix ang khaw tix caox rhawm ka paox tix awm hoik caox rhawm aux ka maix yuh nan lie?’
34 Simiang kied song rhawm,
mai laung nawh kha rhawm tix daux thawng,
krox jun nawh vied sidawng ka ceuk tix khawm uik heue.”
35 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan pex ang cu tix pluat ploi ka pux aik tix khaing daux rhawm rhi tix ku kaux,
keeing aux rao ma kawx tag sang yuh nin ka pex nang heue.”
