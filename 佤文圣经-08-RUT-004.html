Paw-a-see teei tix moeng Lu-teux
4
Paw-a-see hoek ka diang siviex veng dee khrawm pui hun tix,
mai ngawm tan.
Ang rheeng,
paox nyiex pa dex Yi-li-mi-leux pa krai Paw-a-see hoik hoek ka dee an,
Paw-a-see tom kok ka ceu kaux nawh:
“Hoek maix ngawm dee in!”
Nawh tom hoek tix ngawm.
2 Paw-a-see baox kok kuad song khaing daux yaong kao kaux,
mai kawx kieh kix ngawm nang ka dee an.
Pui khankix hoik ngawm ka khawm uik,
3 Nawh ah nin ka paox nyiex pa dex an:
“Na-eu-me hoik deeh hoek khaing haktiex Maw-ya,
nawh sang coh gawng haktiex cie pux aik ex Yi-li-mi-leux;
4 aux keud maix tawng ka kaing in.
Phan maix sang liag hoek,
tag liag ka sivoe ngai kuad song khankix pa ngawm dee in;
phan maix ang liag,
maix krai sidawng,
mawh kheu maix koe akhuang tix mawh sivoe,
khaix an tom tag mawh aux heue.”
Pui an ah nin:
“Aux cu tix liag hoek.”
5 Paw-a-see ah nin:
“Chix tix,
phan maix liag haktiex an khaing Na-eu-me,
maix kawx teei tix moenga miex mai Lu-teux pa mawh pui Maw-ya;
yuh nin,
haktiex an tag chix deuh min tix ka greem pui pa hoik yum an.”
6 Pui an pawk lox:
“Yuh nin,
aux tik ka akhuang pa khaw tix liag ka haktiex in;
phan aux mawh pa liag,
kawn aux kawx ang pon tix toe tix cie.
Maix liag heue,
aux ang lai liag.”
7 bYam an,
koe been coh liag pui Yi-seu-liex ka kraung khrai tix ceu:
pa mawh cao pa coh tag peeik kiiep tix mai tawx ka cao pa liag.
Kix siao ceu in hoik sawn tix mawh mhawm lai pa yuh heue.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
yam ah pui an nin ka Paw-a-see:
“Maix liag heue!”
Nawh tom peeik kiiep tix mai tawx ka Paw-a-seec.
9 Paw-a-see ah nin ka kuad song mai pui pa ngawm tan khawm uik:
“In singaix pex hoik mawh sakse grawng in;
aux hoik liag can sin ngao cie Yi-li-mi-leux mai kawn simex nawh Ci-liien mai Ma-loeng khawm uik khaing tawm taix Na-eu-me.
10 dLheu khaing an,
pui pa hoik yum Ma-loeng pa mawh moeng nawh pui Maw-ya pa kaux Lu-teux kawx mawh moeng aux.
Yuh nin,
can sin ngao khawm uik tag chix tix deuh ot ka greem pui pa hoik yum an,
khaix an,
min daux nyiex kix kawx kawn ca deuh ot ka nyiex yaong cao tix mai ceu pui cao tix.
In singaix,
pex hoik mawh sakse ka kaing in heue.”
11 eKuad song khankix mai pui tang kaux ah nin khawm uik:
“Mawh heue,
yix mawh mhai sakse.
Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx moeng maix koe kawn tom nie,
awm hoik keh Le-ciiex mai Li-yax pa kaux Ya-keux cie kawn tom nie yuh nan.
Kieh maix mag mi ka daux ceu pui Yi-fa-tha,
koe min ka daux Paw-li-haung.
12 fKieh Simiang Siyiex tawx doeh ceu cad maix ka kawn nyawm bun in,
kieh daux nyiex maix haok huan awm daux nyiex ma Fa-leu-see kawn simex Yo-tax mai Tha-ma yuh nan heue.”
Paw-a-see mai doeh ceu cad nawh
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Paw-a-see teei Lu-teux mawh moeng tix.
Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka nawh,
nawh tom koe kaux,
keh ka kawn simex tix kaux.
14 Cub bun miex khankix daux veng ah nin ka Na-eu-me:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex!
In singaix nawh tawx ka paox nyiex pa dex mai maix tix kaux priex ka maix.
Kieh kawn in ao min ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
15 Mun maix muh ka maix;
kaing pa yuh nawh son maix,
kawn nie khaing kawn simex aliah kaux.
Yam in nawh keh kawn saux son maix tix kaux;
kawn saux in tag tawx riang pa khraox ka maix,
kieh maix yaox tix nyawm ot yam kuad ju tix.”
16 Na-eu-me rieb ka kawn nyawm in,
mai teem ka nawh,
eei ka nawh heueg.
17 Bun miex paox plawg paox nyhawm khankix sum ceu kaux kawn nyawm in kaux Eu-pe-teux.
Kix ah nin ka pui ku kaux:
“Na-eu-me pon ka kawn simex tix kaux heue!”
Eu-pe-teux mawh keeing Yie-si;
Yie-si mawh keeing simiang Ta-wue heue.
18-22 Jauh ka nyiex Fa-leu-see hoek ka nyiex Ta-wue mawh nin:
Fa-leu-see,
Si-see-loeng,
Lang,
Ya-mi-na-ta,
Na-suing,
Sa-meung,
Paw-a-see,
Eu-pe-teux,
Yie-si,
Ta-wue heue.
