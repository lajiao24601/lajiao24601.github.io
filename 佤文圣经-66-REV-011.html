Pui pa kham sakse ra kaux
11
aAux rieb ka khaox tiag tix gawng pa awm khaox gix yuh nan,
koe lox krai ka aux ah nin:
“Kaoh,
tiag tix yaox ka nyiex buan Siyiex mai khu dee tan,
khaix an mai simin tix yaox ka pui pa krub ka daux nyiex buan.
2 bViang mawh nan,
paw vi tiag ka biang brawk nyiex buan,
mawh kheu hoik tawx biang brawk in ka pui tang ceu tang cad;
kix tag sang dug dig ka veng singax tipon ra khix.
3 Aux tag sang ploi pui pa kham sakse pa ceep kraung man keei sum ra kaux hu;
kix tag sang sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex tix rheng ra yieh tix gliah singaix heue.”
4 cPui pa kham sakse an kiex taug sang cung ka dee koe khaox sime ra gawng,
mai ka dee koe jawk kawk ngu ra mu ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex pa phan yuh ka haktiex.
5 Phan koe pui pa keud tix sang yuh kix laux,
kix taug sang plih rao ngu lih khaing daux jheeh tix,
mai gleeing ka pui koeng yum.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui mawx keud tix sang yuh kix laux,
taug sang pauh yum.
6 dKix koe akhuang tix nyeep ka rao ma,
ka daux singaix pa krai pao kix ka gum lox Siyiex,
mai ang kieh lhiex lih.
Kix kawx koe akhuang tix kuang ka dee keet rawm ku dauh,
kieh rawm tieh tix lih mawh nham,
mai koe akhuang tix teei alo tix yuh haktiex laux ka krax kha rhawm ku ceu heue.
7 eKhaix hoik krai pao kix ka gum lox in,
to sat an pa lih khaing daux kied raux tag sang yuh naing ka kix,
pon pex ka kix,
mai pauh kix yum.
8 fTo kaux yum kix tag sang goh ot ka daux laih veng pa ting,
pa mawh dee tawk simiang kix yum ka piang khaox lhak.
Veng in hoik mawh pa jhi grawng veng Saw-to-ma mai Ai-cix.
9 Koe pui tag sang hoek tix jhak to kaux yum kix loe singaix plak,
khaing pui ku ceu,
ku cub,
lox si kah ku ceu mai ku meung,
mai ang cu pui si paung ka kix.
10 Pui piang haktiex tag sang gaux rhawm kheu pa hoik yum kix.
Pui hun yuh poi,
mai ra lai tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka paox tix,
mawh kheu hoik kieh cao jhai in kiex pui piang haktiex kham krax kha rhawm pa ting.
11 gKhaix hoik luan loe singax plak,
koe bhawm pa im ju lih hoek khaing Siyiex dee an,
mai laig ka daux kix,
kix tom yaog tix kaoh cung;
pui pa yaox khankix hoik si yaung ka tete.
12 hCao jhai an kiex mhong lox pa ting lih khaing rao ma ah nin ka kix:
“Haok ka dee in heue!”
Kiex tom brug pai awm haok rao ma ka daux pa jeung pui caox ngai tix jhak ka kix.
13 iYam an haktiex yung yang,
veng an hoik ghrup kao son tix son,
kheu pui pa yum ka tok kla reem koe aliah rheng kaux.
Pui pa deuk khankix kied lhat graung kah,
mai yaog yaw grawng pa ting pa lhaong Siyiex rao ma heue.
14 Krax kha rhawm ka daux ra ceu tix ceu hoik luan;
krax kha rhawm ka daux loe ceu tix ceu taug baox lih hoek heue!
Teet tawng ka daux aliah mu
15 jKawn cau Siyiex ka daux aliah kaux phlu teet tawng,
rao ma taug lih koe lox pa ting ah nin:
“Siawp sibrawm pa ting pa giex kaing ka rao ma hoik mawh cie Simiang Siyiex ex,
mai cie Khrit pa hoik sum nawh;
nawh tag sang giex kaing leeing ju leeing phan heue!”
16 Kuad song tix nga pon kaux pa ngawm ka dee ngawm tix ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
hoik gup tix piang tiex khawm uik,
mai krub ka Siyiex,
17 ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm pa koe tom yam jauh,
yam in eue,
yix yaog yaw ka maix.
Mawh kheu hoik teei maix akhuang pa ting tix,
yuh krax pa giex kaing.
18 kMeung ka piang haktiex song rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
krax pa song rhawm maix hoik lih hoek heue.
Ka daux bawg yam pa khaw hoik hoek,
pui yum tag sang kham krax prawng ca;
siliex maix pa mawh cao jhai khankix,
pui cie maix mai can pui pa koe krax pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix khankix,
mawh pui pa koe min ang ah,
pa ang koe min ang ah,
hoik pon khawng phak buan son khawm uik.
Pui pa yuh haktiex laux an kix,
tag sang yuh laux leeing heue!
19 lYam in,
nyiex buan Siyiex ka piang rao ma hoik baoh;
teug sijax mukheu cie nawh lih khaing daux nyiex buan.
Khaix an pu plak praix,
si num praix,
lox luah,
kla reem,
mai mu pre tag sang lih koe heue.
