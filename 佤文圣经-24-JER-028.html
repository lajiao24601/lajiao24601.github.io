Yie-li-mi mai cao jhai Ha-na-ni-ya
28
bKa daux num ana,
pa mawh yam yuh Si-ti-cia simiang ka daux pon num khix phuan,
cao jhai Ha-na-ni-ya kawn simex Ya-sawx pui Ci-piien si kah mai aux ka daux nyiex buan.
2 Nawh krai ka aux ka plak sivoe ngai cao giex kaing li mai pui hun,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex krai yuh nin:
“Aux hoik yuh laux ka daux pa giex gang simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
3 Ka daux ra num,
kraung khrai pa hoik tud rhim simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hu ka Pa-pi-loeng,
aux tag sang deeh viex hoek ka haktiex in khawm uik.
4 Yaw-ya-cin kawn simex Yaw-ya-cing simiang Yo-tax,
mai pui Yo-tax pa giex laung pui hu ka Pa-pi-loeng,
aux kawx tag sang viex kix deeh hoek.
Mawh tete,
aux rug hoik yuh laux ka pa giex gang simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
5 Khaix an,
aux cung ka plak sivoe ngai cao giex kaing li,
mai ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun ka daux nyiex buan,
aux ah nin ka Ha-na-ni-ya:
6 “Kieh mawh nan heue!
Kieh Simiang Siyiex hawt tix yuh awm lox maix heue!
Aux simie Simiang Siyiex yuh lih ka lox jhai pa krai maix,
mai deeh viex kraung ngawh pa ot daux nyiex buan,
mai can pui pa giex laung pui khaing Pa-pi-loeng hoek ka haktiex in.
7 Viang mawh nan,
maix tag caw tix bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox krai aux ka maix,
mai ka pui hun khankix.
8 Jauh tom dix dix,
ka sivoe an cao jhai khankix hoik krai lox jhai grawng sang lih naing,
sibhawm praix,
mai jhawm saux preng hoek ka piang ceu pui tom nie,
mai ka piang meung pa ting riang ting rao.
9 Viang mawh nan,
lox jhai mai cao jhai ka daux pa nhim tag sang krox jun lox jhai lih tix mawh,
kawn pui tag sang doh keud nawh rug mawh cao jhai pa ploi Simiang Siyiex hoek heue.”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
Ha-na-ni-ya tag sang teei khaox glawm pa ot ka ngawg aux lih,
ma raig pot,
11 mai ah nin ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik krai,
nawh tag sang hawt tix raig pot ka khaox glawm pa sau simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka piang meung ku meung;
nawh tag sang yuh kaing in lih ka daux ra num.”
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom lih hu khaing haktiex an heue.
12 Khaix hoik hoik kaing in,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka aux
13 mai nhie aux krai ka Ha-na-ni-ya ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ah nin,
viang maix hoik pon raig pot ka khaox glawm,
viang mawh nan,
nawhc tag sang baox yuh khaox glawm rhiem tang tix mu.
14 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai,
nawh tag sang teei khaox glawm pa yuh ka rhiem sau piang meung ku meung;
kix tag sang nghiet lox Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin,
nawh tag sang baox ap tix tawx khawm to tiag ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng.”
15 Aux baox krai ka Ha-na-ni-ya ah nin:
“Ha-na-ni-ya eue,
maix nghiet heue!
Simiang Siyiex ang bax ploi maix hu,
maix lai nhie pui in kix yum rhawm ka lox pa pie lhien maix.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin,
nawh tag sang yuh maix laux leeing.
Maix tag sang som ju ka daux num in,
mawh kheu hoik cheeik maix pui tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
17 Ka daux num an khix aliah,
Ha-na-ni-ya hoik yum heue.
