[Simex pa sang koe moeng]
5
Paox muh aux,
moeng pa muh aux,
aux laig ka daux si peem cie tix,
pieh ka Muran mai sidah bhawng,
ih ka nyiex rawm hia mai rawm hia,
nyaux ka plai mai rawm teeh cie aux heue.
[Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix]
Paox muh aux khankix eue,
ih heue,
nyaux heue,
ih nyaux tom hoik yoek tix ka daux krax muh heue!
Lox rhax ka daux pon ceu
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
2 Viang aux hoik it kawx,
mai daux rhawm aux kawn haing;
aux ca maox tix yaox ka paox muh aux ot plak praix gleuh ka siviex heue.
[Simex pa sang koe moeng]
Paox muh aux,
kieh aux laig ka daux nyiex heue,
paox muh aux,
si kaux cie aux.
Ka piang kaing aux hoik naog ka rawm ciex;
hauk kaing aux hoik lan ka rawm ciex pa lih pon sawn heue.
(Bun pa sang koe mex)
3 Aux hoik peeik ka kraung ceep tix,
aux doh sang baox deeh kaoh ceep ka kraung tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux hoik khoek ka caong tix,
aux doh sang baox deeh kieh seeing yuh ka mawx lie?
 
4 Pui pa muh aux chup taix tix laig ka daux doh siviex,
mawh kheu hoik dex nawh mai aux,
rhawm rhi aux hoik chu tix kah.
5 Aux jiet tag sang paoh siviex kieh pui pa muh aux laig;
yam giex aux ka taix siviex,
taix aux khawm ra khawn hoik jauh lih ka rawm Muran,
giien tai aux hoik jauh lih ka rawm Muran.
6 Aux paoh siviex ka pui pa muh aux,
viang mawh nan nawh hoik hu heue.
Aux kied simie tix mhong ka lox nawh heue!
Aux sawg ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang yaox;
aux kok yo ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang mhong ka lox pawk nawh heue.
 
7 Pui pa lai ling tix pau ka daux veng pon sawm hoik yaox ka aux;
kix lauh ka aux,
mai hoik kieh aux mat heue;
pui pa pau ka guh veng tud hu ka man pa lhawp aux piang klip tix.
8 Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix eue,
viex pex nghiet ka aux.
Phan pex yaox ka pui pa muh aux,
pex krai ka nawh,
aux hoik saux kheu braong tix ka nawh heue.
[Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix]
9 Maix pa kied sidaing mhawm grang ka daux nyawm bun eue,
pui pa muh maix kawn lheu mhawm khaing pui tang kaux lie?
Nawh hoik koe ceu pa tang siyox ceu pa tix,
mawh ceu pa tix pa sang khaw tix sang kieh yix cu ka lox maix lie?
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
10 Pui pa muh aux kied mhawm grang,
mhawm kaux hak,
mawh pui pa lheu mhawm khaing kawn pui khawm uik.
11 Kaing nawh hoik awm khri;
hauk kaing nawh hoik awm pu pak rawm,
hoik lung awm lak yuh nan heue.
12 Tawm ngai nawh hoik awm si kaux pa ot ka praok rawm klawng iak yuh nan,
heem tix singax ka rawm teeh,
cung ka praok dee lih rawm klawng iak.
13 Si bax nawh khawm ra plak hoik mhawm awm si peem tai yuh nan,
naog ka tai rib pa si oe bhawng.
Siyieb jheeh nawh hoik awm tai siyag yuh nan,
hoik lan ka rawm bee Muran.
14 Plawng taix nawh khawm ra khawn kied khaw paox,
hoik ceep ka ble pa om ka siang simaox.
Ghlaok to kaux nawh hoik plang awm rhang sang yuh nan,
om ka siang simaox klai rao ma.
15 Ba nawh khawm ra khawn hoik awm rong siang simaox paing yuh nan,
mai deuh ka piang dang khri pa raug tix mhawm.
Greeh grang nawh hoik mhawm awm gawng Li-pa-noeng yuh nan,
hoik lhaong awm khaox si oe bhawng heue.
16 Jheeh nawh kied nyawm tix ngheet;
nawh hoik kieh pui yoek ka pa mhawm grang tix heue!
Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix eue,
nawh hoik mawh pui pa muh aux,
mawh paox meed aux heue.
