Pui Ci-piien pie lhien ka Yaw-su-yax
9
Lox puing pex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tauh hu ka haktiex gawng lua plak klip singaix Yaw-tang,
mai daux lung hawt Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong,
hoik tauh hu singai tom Li-pa-noeng plak laong;
mai hoek nang ka daux yhaok simiang pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Cia-nang,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we,
mai pui Yie-pu-see.
2 Kix khrawm paox tix tau son sang puing ka Yaw-su-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex.
3 Viang mawh nan,
pui Si-we pa ot ka Ci-piien hoik mhong ka ceu pa hoik yuh Yaw-su-yax ka veng Yie-li-keu mai veng Ai,
4 tom but tix pie lhien ka Yaw-su-yax.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix teei chee pa prim mai si ngien hak pa sau plai pa hoik griah mai pa hoik deeh cox caing tix sau khao mun daux,
mai tang ka piang la,
5 to kaux kix ceep ka kraung pa griah mai kiiep pa hoik blaox tix,
mai viex khao mun pa hoik moh heue.
6 Kix hoek ka Yaw-su-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex ka nyiex bang Cie-cia,
mai ah nin:
“Yix hoik kaoh khaing haktiex singai tix dauh,
khiox khaw pex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai yix liak.”
7 aPui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Yix tag ang yuh sijax mukheu mai pex?
Ah ang tawng pex hoik ot dee dex tin lie!”
8 Kix ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Yix hoek tix yuh siliex pex heue.”
Yaw-su-yax chawk ka kix:
“Pex mawh mawx?
Kaoh pex khaing dee mawx?”
9 Kix ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Yix kaoh khaing dee singai,
mawh kheu yix hoik mhong ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex pa ting min.
Kaing pa yuh nawh daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik,
10 bmai can pa hoik yuh nawh ka simiang haktiex Ya-maw-lix ra kaux pa ot ka plak lih singaix toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
pa mawh simiang Si-hong ka daux haktiex Si-seu-peung mai simiang Pa-sang pa kaux Eux ka daux haktiex Ya-see-tha-lux,
yix hoik mhong ka khawm uik heue.
11 Simiang yix mai pui yix khankix kieh yix rien ka khao mun,
kaoh khaing dee singai hu yaox ka pex,
nghiet ka lox pa ah pex,
khiox khaw pex yuh sijax mukheu mai yix liak.
12 Pex jhak khao mun pa viex yix,
yam lih yix khaing nyiex son sang yaox ka pex kawn mawh pa hax,
yam in maix jhak,
ceu in kix hoik kroh,
mai hoik moh.
13 Yam sau yix plai,
hak plai kawn mawh pa khraox,
viang mawh nan,
yam in hoik mawh pa prim.
Sibex yix,
kiiep ceep yix kheu singai krax hoik laux heue.”
14 Pui Yi-seu-liex teei ka khao mun kix tix plah,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax chawk ka Simiang Siyiex.
15 Yaw-su-yax mai pui Ci-piien yuh sijax mukheu pa nhim tau,
ap tix ang pauh ka kix.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix kawx tiex tu tix sang hawt ka lox sijax mukheu in.
16 Khaix hoik yuh sijax mukheu daux loe singaix,
pui Yi-seu-liex tom tag tawng pui in kix ot dee dex khaing.
17 Kix tom hu sawg ka kix.
Khaix loe singaix,
kix hoek ka dee ot pui in kix,
pa mawh Ci-piien,
Ci-fe-la,
Pi-lux,
mai Ci-liex Yie-ling heue.
18 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang pauh ka kix,
mawh kheu simiang kix hoik tiex tu son pui in kix ka daux min Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex.
Grawng kaing in,
pui hun hoik salao ka simiang tix khankix.
19 Viang mawh nan,
simiang khankix ah nin:
“Ex hoik tiex tu ka kix ka daux min Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
yam in ex ang pon tix yuh kix laux.
20 Kheu lox pa ap in,
ex tag sang deuh kix im ju,
phan ang mawh nan,
Siyiex chix ciyang ka ex.
21 Kieh kix im ju khaing heue,
viang mawh nan,
kix tag sang teem ex lug khix kin rawm.”
In mawh rhawm keud simiang khankix.
22 Yaw-su-yax tawx lox kieh pui viex pui Ci-piien hoek,
chawk ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai pie lhien ka yix?
Pex hoik ot dee dex,
yuh ka mawx ah kaoh pex khaing dee singai?
23 Mawh kheu pie lhien pex,
Siyiex hoik prawng ka phi map pex;
pex tag yuh siliex cawng leeing ju,
teem coi Siyiex yix lug khix kin rawm.”
24 Kix ah nin:
“Yix yuh nin mawh kheu hoik tawng yix tete,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex hoik jhi ka siliex tix Maw-si,
sang tawx haktiex in ka pex,
bawg yam laig pex ka haktiex in,
pauh pui pa ot dee in yum khawm uik.
Kheu lhat yix pex pauh tix yum,
yix tom yuh kaing yuh nin.
25 Yam in yix ot ka daux tawm taix pex,
hawt ka rhawm keud pex yuh ka yix liak!”
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax mox ka kix,
ang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex pauh ka kix;
27 viang mawh nan,
nawh kieh kix yuh siliex,
teem pui Yi-seu-liex mai Simiang Siyiex lug khix kin rawm.
Tom hoek in singaix,
kix kawn ca yuh kaing yuh nin ka dee krub pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex heue.
