(10) Can pa liang tix keh yum uik
11
Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Aux tag sang nghoik tix tawx krax laux leeing tix ceu ka piang simiang Ai-cix mai pui cie nawh.
Khaix hoik lih mawh ceu in,
nawh tag sang ploi pex hu.
Tete te lai,
nawh tag sang grheu pex lih khaing haktiex in.
2 Yam in maix hu krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai nhie kix hu mhaing tix cie kraung khri,
kraung mau,
jeep giien khankix khaing paox plawg paox nyhawm tix.”
3 Simiang Siyiex kieh pui Ai-cix yung yi ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Cao bung kaing pui Ai-cix mai pui haoh pui hun hoik kieh Maw-si mawh kawn pui pa yung yi ka piang khawm uik heue.
4 Kheu nan,
Maw-si ah nin ka simiang Ai-cix:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Ka daux bo in yam pon sawm,
aux tag hu lai ling ka daux Ai-cix.
5 Can pa mawh kawn ai haktiex Ai-cix,
jauh ka kawn ai pa plud simiang bleeh ka kawn ai pa keh kawn cau bun pa yuh kaing siliex pa krid mhaw ka daux nyiex nawh khankix,
tag yum uik.
Pa liang tix keh khaing khawng brie kawx tag yum uik.
6 Ka daux haktiex Ai-cix ku dauh ku dee,
tag sang lih koe lox yiiem pa kied ting kah;
in mawh pa ang nyang sax koe glang ka in dix,
mai khaix in kawx ang sax koe heue.
7 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux nyiex ma pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai sox khankix kawx ang lai koe tix raoh ka pui mai khawng brie.
Yam an,
maix tag tawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex;
aux kieh pui Ai-cix mai pui Yi-seu-liex tang siyox heue.’ ”
8 Maw-si nghoik tix ah nin:
“Siliex maix khankix,
tag hoek ka sivoe ngai aux,
mai grux ngawng tix piang tiex krub ka aux,
nhie aux viex pui cie tix hu.
Hoek singaix yam an,
aux tom tag lih hu khaing tin heue.”
Khaix an Maw-si song rhawm song rhi mai deeh lih hu tix khaing sivoe ngai simiang Ai-cix heue.
9 Simiang Siyiex hoik dawg krai ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Simiang Ai-cix ca kawn nied tix thiang ka lox maix,
yuh nin,
aux tom yaox tix yuh mhai pa daox pa am tom nie ka daux Ai-cix.”
10 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng yuh mhai pa daox pa am tom nie ka sivoe ngai simiang,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex deeh kieh rhawm rhi simiang kiien,
mai ang cu pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing daux haktiex nawh heue.
