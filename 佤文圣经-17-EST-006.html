Simiang tawx Maw-ti-kai ting min
6
Daux bo in,
simiang ang laig it,
tom tawx lox kieh pui teei lai been prim mai phat nawh mhong.
2 aDaux lai in koe tix grawng,
krai Maw-ti-kai kaing pa sang keud kix tix pauh simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu;
kaing in mawh cao pa pau siviex pui sieh Pi-thang mai Thi-liex pa keud tix sang yuh.
3 Simiang chawk:
“Kheu kaing in,
ex tawx ceu pa ting min yuh ka mawx ka Maw-ti-kai lie?”
Siliex simiang pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang tawx tid tix.”
4 Simiang chawk:
“Pui mawx pa ot ka brawk?”
Yam in,
Ha-mang jiet laig ka daux brawk nyiex haw simiang,
sang khiox khaw simiang tawk Maw-ti-kai ka khaox ciyang pa hoik yuh tix.
5 Siliex pawk ah nin:
“Ha-mang ot ka dee in,
krox tix sang yaox ka simiang.”
Simiang ah nin:
“Kieh nawh hoek heue.”
6 Ha-mang laig kah.
Simiang ah nin ka nawh:
“Koe pui tix kaux,
aux keud tix sang tawx nawh ting min.
Aux sang yuh yuh ka mawx lie?”
Ha-mang keud daux rhawm tix:
“Simiang keud tix sang tawx pui ting min kawn koe pui mawx lie?
Ang mawh aux lie!”
7-8 Kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka simiang:
“Pui pa sang tawx simiang ting min,
khaw tix yuh yuh nin:
kieh pui teei sibex pa hoik ceep simiang hoek tix pheen,
baox teei lawk kaing pa kied mhawm tix ceu mai sau ka piang kaing brung.
9 Khaix an,
simiang tawx lox kieh simiang pa ting min tix kaux teem pui in ceep ka sibex simiang,
giex nawh haok ka piang brung,
teem nawh tuk maox brung lai ling ka brawk daux veng.
Yam lai ling kix,
simiang pa ting min in tag krai pao sivoe nawh ah nin:
‘Jhak heue,
simiang yuh yuh nin ka pui pa sang tawx tix ting min heue!’ ”
10 Simiang ah nin ka Ha-mang:
“Nied teei sibex dha simiang mai brung hoek,
hawt ka lox pa ah maix,
tawx krax ting min ka Maw-ti-kai pui Yo-thaix pa ngawm ka diang siviex nyiex haw simiang,
tix ceu kawx ang pon tix yawm!”
11 Kheu nan,
Ha-mang tom tawx sibex dha simiang mai brung ka Maw-ti-kai ceep,
mai giex nawh haok piang brung,
teem nawh tuk maox law lai ling ka brawk ka daux veng,
hu khawm pao tix sivoe pui hun:
“Jhak heue,
simiang yuh yuh nin ka pui pa sang tawx tix ting min heue!”
12 Khaix hoik lai ling,
Maw-ti-kai deeh ing ka diang siviex nyiex haw simiang,
Ha-mang kied laux rhawm laux rhi khawm ing tix ka daux nyiex.
13 Nawh krai moeng tix Si-li-see mai paox grawm tix mhong grawng kaing pa yaox tix khawm uik.
Moeng nawh mai paox grawm pa lhak lhiao nawh krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix ang lai koe ceu pa yuh ka Maw-ti-kai.
Nawh mawh pui Yo-thaix,
maix ang pon tieb tie ka nawh;
maix tag ang pex sivoe nawh heue.”
Teuk teue Ha-mang yum
14 Yam kawn krai kix lox,
pui sieh ka daux nyiex haw simiang hoek,
dhiet Ha-mang phai hu ih priex pa khre Yi-see-thie heue.
