Siyiex sang yuh Maw-ya laux leeing
15
aPlak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Maw-ya.
Veng Ya-eux mai veng Ci-eux laux leeing ka daux tix sawm.
Krax yum hoik tawb kheem ka haktiex Maw-ya.
2 Pui Ti-peungb haok ka gawng,
mai yiiem ot ka daux nyiex vad kix;
pui Maw-ya hoik laux rhawm yiiem son Ni-paw mai Mi-ti-pa;
kix khlao kaing tix ghle,
mai khlao hauk jheeh tix singax ka daux yam laux rhawm tix.
3 Pui veng an pa lai ling tix hu ka daux laih,
hoik ceep ka kraung man keei sum khawm uik;
ka brawk mai ka piang braux nyiex,
hoik mawh lox pa yiiem riag pui khawm uik.
4 Si-seu-peung mai pui Yi-li-ya-lix hoik yiiem khawm uik;
lox yiiem kix pie pui Ya-cax kawx hoik mhong kah.
Kheu nan,
pui naing kawx hoik lhat khawm uik,
mai rhawm pa kua kix hoik grai heue.
5 Rhawm rhi aux laux rhawm son Maw-ya heue!
Pui haktiex an hoik to hu mox tix ka Saw-eux mai ka Yi-ci-la See-li-see-yax.
Koe pui tix blah haok ka blaong Lu-si,
to nang yiiem nang;
koe pui tix blah to hu mox tix ka Heu-law-nian,
mai ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting.
6 Ka daux klawng iak Ling-ling ang koe rawm,
rib klai pa koe ka praok klawng hoik kroh,
mai ang yaox rib pa klai pie ceeix kawx.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
kix hoik viex ka sin ngao pa deuh tix,
mai yaox tix pon phlat luan khaing daux ngawd Lio-si.
8 Ka mee haktiex Maw-ya,
mai hoik mhong ka lox yiiem ku dauh ku dee,
pie pui pa paung ot ka Yi-ci-liien mai ka Pi-eu Yi-ling kawx hoik mhong kah.
9 Rawm klawng pa koe ka daux veng Ti-meung hoik mawh nham khawm uik;
Siyiex tag sang ploi krax laux krax leeing pa blawn ting lih piang pui Ti-meung.
Pui si pup krax laux pa to tix lih hoek khaing Maw-ya tag sang pauh yum ku kaux heue.
