Ta-wue pex ka pui Ya-ma-lix mai pui Shui-li-yax
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 10.1–9)
19
Khaix hoik luan been tix baux bawg yam,
pui Ya-meung simiang pa kaux Na-sia hoik yum.
Kawn simex nawh pa kaux Ha-nuing plud tix yuh simiang.
2 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Aux sang yuh paox grawm tau mai Ha-nuing,
awm hoik yuh keeing nawh Na-sia paox grawm tau mai aux yuh nan.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom ploi kawn cau tix hu khu rhawm nawh.
Yam hoek kix khu rhawm simiang Ha-nuing ka haktiex Ya-meung,
3 Simiang pui Ya-meung ah nin ka simiang Ha-nuing:
“Maix ce Ta-wue ploi pui in kix hoek mawh son sang nab yam yung yi ka simiang phaox ka,
mai son sang khu rhawm rhi maix lie?
Ang doh mawh nan!
Nawh ploi kix hoek,
mawh sang kieh kix jiang tix yaox ka haktiex ex,
son sang pon kuang ka meung in heue!”
4 Ha-nuing tom giex ka kawn cau pa ploi Ta-wue hoek,
mai khlao ka hauk jheeh kix tix plak,
mai baox tat dut ka sibex kix tom beem kre,
khaix an grheu kix lih hu khaing daux haktiex.
5 Pui in kix yaox krax map krax nai,
kied kaig kied tua kah,
mai ang pon ing ka nyiex.
Ta-wue mhong grawng in,
nawh tom nhie pui hu krai kix mhong kaog ot ka Yie-li-keu,
krox jun hauk jheeh tix baox deeh kuah,
mai tag deeh ing khaix heue.
6 Pui Ya-meung hoik tawng ang kix khaw tau mai Ta-wue,
tom teei mau luah loe mheen pon rheng kongcing,
vai ling law mai pui naing brug brung khaing pui Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax pa ot plak blaong,
mai ka pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Ma-cia mai Saw-pa.
7 Kix vai ka ling law loe mheen mai ka pui naing simiang Ma-cia;
pui naing in kix sum nyiex bang ot dex ka Mi-ti-pa.
Can pui Ya-meung kawx kaoh khaing veng kix khawm uik,
mai rien khre tix sang yuh naing heue.
8 Ta-wue hoik tawng ka grawng in,
tom tawx lox nhie Yaw-ya thung pui naing khawm uik hu puing.
9 Pui Ya-meung hoek puing ka naing,
mai kieh veng ting kix La-pa mawh moing gawng dee ot kix,
mai simiang meung pa yuh paox pie tau khankix,
tom dia tix ot plak praix sivie siveng,
son sang yuh naing heue.
10 Yaw-ya yaox pui naing pa koeng hoek puing kix ka plak ngai khawm plak khaix,
tom laug pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa kied kua khankix,
mai kieh kix rien tix sivoe pui naing Shui-li-yax,
11 mai ap pui naing pa deuk khankix ka pux tix pa kaux Ya-pi-sai mawh cao thung.
Ya-pi-sai kieh cub pui naing in kix rien tix sivoe pui Ya-meung.
12 Yaw-ya ah nin ka Ya-pi-sai:
“Phan maix yaox ting riang pui Shui-li-yax khaing aux,
maix tom hoek tix taoh teem ka aux;
phan pui Ya-meung ting riang khaing maix,
aux tom hoek tix taoh teem ka maix.
13 Tag caw kieh tix kaux cong,
mai sau riang tix yuh naing son paox ceu paox cad ex mai veng cie Siyiex!
Kieh Simiang Siyiex yuh alo tix lih mawh heue!”
14 Yaw-ya thung pui naing puing hu sivoe ngai;
pui Shui-li-yax hoik yaog tix to.
15 Pui Ya-meung yaox hoik yaog pui Shui-li-yax tix to,
kix kawx yaog tix to khaing Ya-pi-sai,
mai deeh ing ka daux veng.
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
16 Pui Shui-li-yax yaox ang tix pex pui Yi-seu-liex,
kix tom teei pui naing Shui-li-yax pa ot ka plak lih singaix ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti ku cub hoek,
mai kieh simiang Saw-pa pa kaux Ha-ta-ti-shiex simiang naing ting pa kaux Saw-fax mawh cao thung.
17 Yam hoik mhong Ta-wue ka grawng in,
tom ploi pui naing Yi-seu-liex,
mai kieh kix tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
son sang tieb tie ka pui naing Shui-li-yax khankix.
Krax naing jauh tix,
18 pui Yi-seu-liex si gheet ka pui naing Shui-li-yax deeh.
Ta-wue mai pui naing cie nawh pon pauh ka pui naing ling law Shui-li-yax aliah rheng kaux,
mai pui naing hu ka caong tix pon mheen kaux,
khaix an mai baox pon pauh ka simiang naing pa ting pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Saw-fax.
19 Simiang pa ot greem Ha-ta-ti-shiex khankix yaox ang tix pex ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
kix tom yuh krax pa khaw rhawm mai Ta-wue,
mai hawt ka rhawm nawh.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Shui-li-yax ang lai pon baox joh jhan ka pui Ya-meung heue.
