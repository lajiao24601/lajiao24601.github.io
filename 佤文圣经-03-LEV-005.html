Been Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap
5
In plak greem mawh been Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa kok hoek ka nyiex dee prawng,
son sang kham mhai sakse,
nawh ang cu tix krai pa yaox tix mai pa tawng tix lih,
nawh tag koe phi map,
tag sang klawm ka phi map an heue.
2 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux loe tix giex ka ceu pa ang singax singeh ka daux been,
awm pa mawh to sat,
khawng brie,
viag vua pa yum khankix,
nawh tag ang singax singeh;
phan nawh hoik tawng ka can ceu pa yuh tix,
tag sawn nawh koe phi map heue.
3 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux loe tix giex ka ceu pa seeing pa brai,
pa koe ka pui khankix,
mawh ceu pa tix ang ah,
nawh tag ang singax singeh;
phan nawh hoik tawng ka ceu pa yuh tix,
tag sawn nawh koe phi map heue.
4 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux loe tix nieb tiex tu,
mawh pa mhawm ang ah,
pa caox ang ah,
phan nawh hoik tawng ka ceu pa yuh tix,
tag sawn nawh koe phi map heue.
5 Pui pa koe phi map,
tag sang krai doh keud ka phi map tix.
6 Nawh tag caw tix viex ka kawn yung siang tix mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
kawn pex siang tix mu,
tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
Cao giex kaing li tag yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap pa hoik yuh pui an heue.
7 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux ang pon liag ka yung tix mu,
nawh tag caw tix viex si kaux krie ra mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
si kaux hoek tan ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
son sang vied ka phi map pa yuh tix.
8 Nawh tag viex sim an hoek ka cao giex kaing li dee an,
cao giex kaing li tag ran tan lu Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap sivoe.
Nawh tag siyied ngawg sim an dut,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix kieh kaing tang ot,
9 khaix an teei nham lih tix blah,
phruah ot ka praok khu dee tan,
mai nham pa kawn deuk khankix hieh ot ka piang jaong khu dee tan khawm uik.
In hoik mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
10 Khaix an,
nawh tag hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng in,
deeh tan ka sim tang mu tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
Cao giex kaing li tag yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son phi map pui an,
mai nawh tag pon pa pluat ploi heue.
11 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux ang pon liag si kaux krie ra mu,
phan ang mawh nan,
si kaux,
nawh chix tix tan ka phawng mien tix kongcing son phi map pa yuh tix,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
Viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix sau bee sime mai sidah bhawng Law-ban ka daux,
kheu in hoik mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
ang mawh Krax-tan Nghox-koi.
12 Nawh tag viex phawng mien an hoek ka cao giex kaing li dee an,
cao giex kaing li tag teei mien phawng tix jhawp,
awm hoik mawh pa hoik tan ka khawm uik,
deuh ka piang khu dee tan mai gleeing kah,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Ngu tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
In hoik mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
13 Cao giex kaing li tag yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son phi map pa hoik yuh pui an,
nawh tag pon pa pluat ploi.
Phawng mien gaox nghox ca pa kawn deuk khankix,
tag hawt lox tat jhawm prawng Krax-tan Nghox-koi tawx mawh cie cao giex kaing li heue.
Been Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud
14 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ka Maw-si.
15 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux loi tix ang bax tan kraung pa singax singeh,
pa caw nawh tix sang tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag caw tix viex kawn yung mhaing pa loig tix mu hoek,
son sang vied ka phi map pa yuh nawh,
mai tan ka Simiang Siyiex kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud.
Ngawh jum yung an hawt awm diag tong been pa hoik tat nyiex buan pa singax singeh.
16 Nawh tag caw tix vied son pa ang tix bax tawx,
khaix an baox thiem thie ka piang tix yieh son tix nga son.
Nawh tag caw tix tawx in kix ka cao giex kaing li;
mai cao giex kaing li tag tan yung mhaing son nawh tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud,
mai son sang pluat ploi ka phi map nawh,
nawh tag pon pa pluat ploi heue.
17 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux loe tix yuh phi map,
mai phit lud ka gum lox ping nyap Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag koe phi map,
nawh tag sang klawng krax phi map an.
18 Nawh tag caw tix viex kawn yung mhaing pa loig tix mu hoek ka cao giex kaing li dee an,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud.
Ngawh jum yung an tag hawt awm diag dong been pa hoik tat nyiex buan pa singax singeh.
Cao giex kaing li sang pluat ploi ka phi map nawh,
nawh tag pon pa pluat ploi.
19 In mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud grawng pa phit lud nawh ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
