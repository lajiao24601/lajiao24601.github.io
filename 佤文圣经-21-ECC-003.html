Siyiex kieh ku ceu khawm uik koe bawg yam
3
Ku ceu khawm uik ka piang haktiex hoik tang koe bawg yam,
koe Siyiex hoik tat tix deuh ka bawg yam.
2 Koe bawg yam keh,
koe bawg yam yum;
koe bawg yam sum,
koe bawg yam reeig;
3 koe bawg yam pauh yum,
koe bawg yam pai moi;
koe bawg yam yieh laux,
koe bawg yam sum mhawm.
4 Koe bawg yam yiiem,
koe bawg yam nyiah;
koe bawg yam laux rhawm,
koe bawg yam graoh;
5 koe bawg yam rom ku,
koe bawg yam guah ku;
koe bawg yam chix gaux rhawm tau,
koe bawg yam ang chix ot tau.
6 Koe bawg yam sawg,
koe bawg yam grai;
koe bawg yam priex,
koe bawg yam tik;
7 koe bawg yam grah,
koe bawg yam blaox;
koe bawg yam yien yawg,
koe bawg yam si kah;
8 koe bawg yam muh,
koe bawg yam koeng;
koe bawg yam yuh naing,
koe bawg yam pa nhim heue.
9 Kheu nan,
kaing pa taug pa mao ex khawm uik hoik koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
10 Aux hoik yaox grawng pa kieh Siyiex ex klawm ka beeh glawm pa cien.
11 Nawh hoik tat tix deuh bawg yam pa khaw mai ku ceu khawm uik;
nawh hoik kieh ex koe krax pa chix keud chix sawn leeing ju,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang kieh ex grum tawng ka kaing pa yuh tix khawm uik.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom keud,
kawn pui ang lheu khaing gaux rhawm jheeng jheeng,
nyawm ot leeing ju.
13 Ex caw tix ih nyaux,
mai gaux rhawm ih nyaux khaing daux krax pa taug pa mao tix.
In hoik mawh khawng phak buan son pa tawx Siyiex heue.
14 Aux hoik tawng,
ceu pa yuh Siyiex khawm uik tag koe leeing ju leeing phan;
kawn pui ang chix naok ang chix tieh ka kaing pa hoik yuh Siyiex.
Siyiex yuh mawh nin,
mawh son sang kieh kawn pui lhat graung yung yi ka tix.
15 Kaing pa koe yam in,
phan ang mawh nan kaing pa tag sang lih singaix pa sang hoek,
hoik dawg mawh kaing pa koe singaix pa hoik luan,
Siyiex kieh kaing pa awm paox baox lih khaing heue.
Krax pa ang rauh see ka piang haktiex
16 Kawn koe,
aux hoik yaox ka piang haktiex in,
ka dee pa khaw tix koe krax pa khaw caw mai pa rauh pa see,
mai tieh tix lih mawh krax pa caox pa caig.
17 Aux keud ka daux rhawm tix ah nin:
“Mawh pui mhawm ang ah,
pui caox ang ah,
Siyiex tag sang prawng ca ka khawm uik;
mawh kheu kaing ku ceu,
mai ceu pa yuh pui ku ceu hoik tang koe bawg yam pa tat tix deuh khawm uik.”
18 Aux keud:
Siyiex sang cheuk cheu tix yaox ka ex,
mai sang kieh ex tawng,
ex ang tie bax lhak lhiao khaing to sat miex ding tix.
19 Dee si dauh,
ju kawn pui mai ju to sat khaw paox tix:
pa koe phaox ka hoik yum,
mai pa koe phaox khaix kawx hoik yum.
In kiex khawm ra ceu hoik mawh pa koe bhawm;
kawn pui ang lai lheu ting riang khaing to sat miex ding tix;
mawh kheu ku ceu khawm uik hoik mawh pa giex pa going.
20 Khawm ra ceu tag sang deeh ka phawng tiex;
kix kaoh khaing dee mawx tag sang deeh ing tan.
21 Pui mawx pon ah hoik haok khuan kawn pui ka rao ma,
laig khuan to sat ka greem tiex lie?
22 Mawh kheu nan,
aux jhak tix yaox,
kawn pui pon caw tix ih nyaux khaing daux pa taug mao tix;
in hoik mawh daux krax pa im ju nawh.
Pui mawx pon kieh nawh yaox ka kaing pa sang hoek khaix nawh lie?
