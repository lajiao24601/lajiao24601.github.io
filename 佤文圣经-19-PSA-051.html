Khiox khaw krax pa pluat ploia
51
bSiyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux,
mawh kheu maix koe krax muh leeing ju.
Khiox khaw maix teei tix tik krax pa lud loe aux,
mawh kheu koe maix krax caox rhawm pa ang chix uik chix leeing.
2 Khiox khaw maix teei tix tik ka krax pa caox pa caig aux,
mai si ghrah ka phi map aux heue.
 
3 Aux hoik tawng krax pa lud pa loe tix;
aux hoik tawng grawng pa ot tix ka daux phi map.
4 cAux hoik lud loe ka maix,
hoik yuh krax pa ang khaw ang jiet ka maix;
aux hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngaix maix.
Mawh kheu nan maix prawng ca ka aux hoik mawh pa khaw caw;
maix ciyang ka aux hoik mawh pa khaw aux tix kham.
5 Yam lih aux khaing daux vaig miex tix,
aux hoik mawh pa caox pa caig;
singaix yam keh aux,
hoik naog ka phi map heue.
 
6 Ceu pa sang lo maix,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm maix tete;
khiox khaw maix teei ping nyia cuyi tix,
mai kieh naog ka piang aux.
7 Khiox khaw maix teei tix tik ka phi map aux,
kieh aux singax singeh.
Khiox khaw maix si ghrah ka aux,
kieh aux lheu paing khaing rawm rha.
8 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux mhong ka lox pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
viang maix hoik yuh aux laux leeing,
mai aux kawn chix baox deeh gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
9 Khiox khaw maix yiep ngai tix ang jhak ka phi map aux,
teei tix tik krax lud krax loe aux khawm uik heue.
 
10 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix yuh rhawm pa singax singeh son aux tix mu;
khiox khaw maix tawx khuan pa khraox pa rauh see ka aux tix mu.
11 Paw vi grheu aux hu khaing plak sivoe ngai maix;
paw vi deeh kok khuan pa singax maix lih khaing to kaux aux.
12 Kieh aux deeh yaox tix cim ka krax taoh pa gaux rhawm maix,
kieh khuan aux gaux rhawm tix hawt ka maix.
13 Yuh nin,
aux tag sang sidiex gau pui phi map ka lox tawx maix;
kix tom taug chix nghiet ka maix heue.
 
14 Siyiex pa taoh ka aux eue,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map pa tat aux yumd;
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka pa khaw caw maix tom ting.
15 Simiang aux eue,
khiox khaw maix paoh ka jheeh aux;
aux tag sang yaog yaw ka maix heue.
 
16 Maix ang muh ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
phan ang mawh nan aux tag sang tan lu ka maix;
maix kawx ang lo ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
17 Siyiex eue,
pa tan lu aux ka maix mawh khuan pa laux rhawm laux rhi;
rhawm rhi pa laux,
maix ang tieb tie ka heue.
 
18 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix teei krax muh kia ngien ka Shi-ang;
khiox khaw maix deeh cox sum ka guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
19 Yam an,
maix tag sang rieb ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa khaw caw,
pa mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing khawm mu;
yam an,
koe moi mhaing tag sang tan lu ka piang khu dee tan maix heue.
