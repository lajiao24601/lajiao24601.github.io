Yaw-su-yax baox yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pui hun ka daux haktiex See-ciien
24
Yaw-su-yax khrawm ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu ka veng See-ciien.
Nawh khrawm ka kuad song,
simiang,
cao prawng see,
mai simiang naing,
kix tom hoek ka Sivoe Siyiex.
2 aYaw-su-yax ah nin ka pui hun:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
‘Sivoe dix dix,
tax du pex,
Tha-la khawm kawn simex nawh Ya-paw-la-hang mai Na-heux ot ka plak toi rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti;
kix krub ka siyiex tang kaux.
3 bAux viex keeing pex Ya-paw-la-hang lih khaing plak toi rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
thung nawh hu ka haktiex Cia-nang ku dauh,
mai baox tawx kawn praix ka nawh tom nie.
Aux tawx Yi-sha ka nawh,
4 cmai baox tawx kawn simex ra kaux ka Yi-sha,
pa mawh Ya-keux mai Yi-sao.
Aux tawx gawng lua Yi-tong ka Yi-sao,
kieh mawh sin ngao nawh;
viang mawh nan,
tax du pex Ya-keux mai kawn praix nawh lih hu ka haktiex Ai-cix.
5 d“ ‘Khaix an,
aux tawx Maw-si mai Ya-loeng,
aux kieh Ai-cix yaox krax laux;
viang mawh nan,
aux viex pex lih.
6 eAux viex tax du pex lih khaing Ai-cix;
pui Ai-cix viex brung naing mai pui naing tix hu leue ka kix.
Yam hoek tax du pex ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
7 Kix sawg aux taoh tix.
Aux siao ka pa faik khan ka si nax kix mai pui Ai-cix,
aux kieh rawm sigrit sigra kheem ka pui Ai-cix,
mai kheem ka kix.
Kaing pa yuh aux ka Ai-cix,
pex hoik yaox ka ngai tix heue.
“ ‘Pex hoik ot ka daux tiag kied rheeng bawg yam,
8 fkhaix an,
aux viex pex hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
hoek ka dee ot pui Ya-maw-lix.
Kix puing ka pex;
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawx kix ka daux tawm taix pex,
kieh pex pon rhim ka haktiex dee ot kix.
Yam pheet pex tix laig,
aux hoik kieh kix laux leeing.
9 gKhaix an,
Pa-leux kawn simex Si-paw simiang Maw-ya yaog tix puing ka pex,
mai ploi pui hu kok Pa-lang kawn simex Pi-eux hoek,
kieh nawh jhax lawg ka pex heue.
10 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang lai nghiet ka lox Pa-lang,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tieh tix nawb buan ka piang pex;
yuh nin,
aux tom taoh pex khaing daux tawm taix Pa-leux heue.
11 hPex tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
hoek ka veng Yie-li-keu.
Pui veng Yie-li-keu hoek tix puing ka pex,
awm puing pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Cia-nang,
pui Heux,
pui Keu-cia-sha,
pui Si-we,
mai pui Yie-pu-see ka pex.
Viang mawh nan,
aux tawx kix ka daux tawm taix pex.
12 iYam pheet pex tix laig,
aux kieh kix yaoh yee,
mai grheu simiang Ya-maw-lix lih hu;
in ang doh mawh hoik siao pex vaig mai ak.
13 jHaktiex pa tawx aux ka pex,
ang mawh pa pon pex khaing riang rao tix;
veng pa tawx aux ka pex ang doh mawh pa hoik sum pex.
Yam in pex tom ot dee an;
priex pa ih pex ang doh mawh plix siiet mai sime pa hoik sum pex heue.’ ”
14 Yaw-su-yax kawn ca ah nin:
“Pex tag lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
krub nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
Pex tag teei tix tik ka Siyiex pa krub tax du pex ka daux Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax mai ka daux Ai-cix.
Tag krub ka Simiang Siyiex nong nong khaing.
15 Phan pex ang rhawm krub ka nawh,
in singaix pex tag tat sidawng,
pex sang krub ceu mawx:
mawh siyiex pa krub tax du pex ka daux Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax,
phan ang mawh nan,
siyiex pa krub pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot pex yam in?
Phan mawh aux mai pui daux nyiex ma aux,
yix sang krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
16 Pui hun pawk lox:
“Yix ang doh tik ka Simiang Siyiex leeing ju mai krub ka siyiex tang kaux!
17 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex hoik viex tax du ex mai ex luat luan khaing krax yuh siliex cawng ka daux Ai-cix;
ex hoik yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh nawh.
Ka daux meung pa hoik tiang ex,
mawh dee mawx ang ah,
nawh tom teem coi ka ex nyawm ot.
18 Yam laig ex ka haktiex in,
Simiang Siyiex hoik grheu pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot dee in.
Mawh kheu nan,
ex tag khaw tix yuh kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex;
nawh mawh Siyiex ex heue.”
19 Yaw-su-yax ah nin ka pui hun:
“Viang mawh nan,
pex tag ang keud tix pon krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nawh mawh Siyiex pa singax singeh;
nawh ang gun yeung ka siyiex pa ang khaw mai nawh.
Nawh ang pluat ploi ka krax lud pex mai phi map pex.
20 Phan pex tik vud nawh mai krub siyiex tang kaux,
nawh tag kieh pex laux leeing,
ciyang ka pex.
Viang nawh hoik tawx buan son ka pex,
nawh kawx tag chix kieh pex laux leeing heue.”
21 Pui hun ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Yix tag khaw tix yuh kaing jhaw mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex tete heue!”
22 Yaw-su-yax ah nin:
“Pex mawh mhai sakse to kaux tix,
yuh mhai sakse tag ap tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Kix tom ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
yix mawh mhai sakse.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax tawx lox ka kix:
“Phan yuh nin,
pex tag tik ka can siyiex tang kaux,
but rhawm tix nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
24 Pui hun ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Yix tag khaw tix yuh kaing jhaw mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix tete;
yix tag hawt ka lox nawh heue.”
25 Ngaix an,
Yaw-su-yax yuhk sijax mukheu son pui hun;
nawh tawx lox tat jhawm prawng mai been jhawm prawng ka pui daux veng See-ciien heue.
26 Yaw-su-yax tiiem gum lox in ka daux phuk lai lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex,
khaix an jung simaox ting tix lon ka greem khaox yang yit ka nyiex singax singeh Siyiex heue.
27 Nawh ah nin ka kawn pui:
“Jhak,
simaox in tag mawh mhai sakse son ex;
nawh hoik mhong ka lox pa ah Simiang Siyiex ka ex khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tag yuh mhai sakse son pex,
lhat pex tieh vheen ka Siyiex pex.”
28 Khaix an,
Yaw-su-yax ploi kawn pui deeh ing,
pui ku kaux tang ing ka dee koe sin ngao tix heue.
Grawng pa yum Yaw-su-yax mai Yi-li-ya-sa
29 Khaix kaing in,
siliex Simiang Siyiex,
kawn simex Noeng pa mawh Yaw-su-yax yum,
yam yum nawh koe ju tix yieh kao num heue.
30 lPui Yi-seu-liex si paung nawh ka daux haktiex pa pon nawh,
pa mawh Thing-na Si-la pa ot ka gawng Yi-fa-liien ka praok plak laong gawng Cia-seu heue.
31 Yam klawd ju Yaw-su-yax ka piang haktiex,
pui Yi-seu-liex yuh kaing jhaw mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex;
khaix hoik yum nawh,
yam kawn im ju kuad song an kix,
pa hoik yaox krax yuh Simiang Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
kawn pui kawn ca yuh kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex heue.
32 mPui Yi-seu-liex si paung si-ang Yaw-seux pa viex tix hoek khaing Ai-cix ka veng See-ciien,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik liang Ya-keux ka mau tix yieh khaing kawn saux Ha-mawx,
keeing See-ciien.
Doeh ceu cad Yaw-seux plud ka haktiex in heue.
33 Kawn simex Ya-loeng pa mawh Yi-li-ya-sa kawx hoik yum,
mai kix si paung nawh ka Ci-pi-yax,
pa mawh gawng iak pa pon kawn simex nawh Fe-ni-ha ka gawng Yi-fa-liien heue.
