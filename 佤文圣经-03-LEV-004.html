Been Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap pa ang su tix yuh
4
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
2 nhie nawh krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ah nin:
Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux ang tawng yam yuh tix phi map,
phit lud ka gum lox ping nyap Siyiex,
nawh tag caw hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem.
3 Phan cao giex kaing li pa ting,
kheu to kaux nawh yuh phi map,
mai krax phit lud nawh hoek ka piang pui haoh hun khankix,
nawh tag caw tix tan ka kawn moi mhaing pa loig tix mu ka Simiang Siyiex son phi map to kaux tix,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
4 Nawh tag viex kawn moi mhaing an hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
deuh taix tix ka piang kaing Krax-tan Khawng-brie an,
mai pauh ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
5 Khaix an,
cao giex kaing li pa ting an tag teei nham kawn moi an tix blah,
viex hoek ka nyiex gox buan man,
6 cub giien taix tix ka daux nham an,
mai phruah ka plak sivoe man tie nyiex gox buan man aliah bawg,
7 mai baox teei nham lih tix blah,
kraix ot ka piang rung khu dee tan nyawng bhawng ka dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
Nawh teei nham pa deuk khankix,
mai hieh ka piang jaong khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing an ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man.
8 Nawh tag teei bee kawn moi mhaing pa mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap an khawm uik,
pa mawh bee kawb piang kraung vaig mai bee pa koe ka piang kraung vaig khawm uik,
9 sok lax ra mu mai bee pa koe ka piang sok lax,
mai tawm dee blawn mhawm khankix lih,
10 gleeing ot ka piang khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
awm pa gleeing ka bee khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-nhim yuh nan.
11 Viang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang teei hak kawn moi mhaing an,
khawm nex,
kaing,
ba,
kraung vaig khankix,
12 viex hoek ka nyiex bang plak praix pa kieh mawh dee singax singeh ka daux been,
pa mawh ka dee hoik tik nyaox,
mai pum piang khix gleeing ka ngu heue.
13 Phan pui haoh pui hun Yi-seu-liex khankix yuh phi map ka daux pa ang tix tawng,
mai phit lud ka gum lox ping nyap Simiang Siyiex,
14 yam deeh tawng kix ka phi map pa hoik phit lud tix,
pui haoh pui hun tag caw tix tan ka kawn moi mhaing tix mu,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
Kix tag viex kawn moi an hoek ka sivoe nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex;
15 kuad song cub pui haoh pui hun tag deuh taix tix ka piang kaing kawn moi mhaing an,
pauh sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
16 Cao giex kaing li pa ting tag teei nham kawn moi an tix blah,
viex hoek ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
17 teei giien taix tix cub ka daux nham,
mai phruah ka sivoe man tie nyiex gox buan man aliah bawg,
18 baox teei nham tix blah,
kraix ot ka piang rung khu dee tan nyawng bhawng dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog.
Khaix an,
teei nham pa kawn deuk khankix,
mai hieh ot ka piang jaong khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing an,
ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man.
19 Khaix an,
nawh tag teei khlawp bee kawn moi mhaing an khawm uik lih,
mai deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan,
mai gleeing kah.
20 Nawh tag hawt yuh kawn moi mhaing an awm krax pa hoik yuh tix ka kawn moi mhaing son Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
Cao giex kaing li tag hawt yuh awm krax in,
mai yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son pui hun,
kix tag pon krax pa pluat ploi phi map.
21 Khaix an,
nawh tag viex kawn moi mhaing an lih plak praix bang,
mai gleeing ka ngu,
awm gleeing ka kawn moi mhaing pa yuh nawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son to kaux tix.
In hoik mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
pa yuh son pui hun heue.
22 Phan simiang naing tix kaux kaux yuh phi map ka daux pa ang tix tawng,
mai phit lud ka gum lox ping nyap Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh,
23 phan nawh hoik tawng dee phit lud tix,
nawh tag caw tix tan ka kawn pex mhaing pa loig tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap pa yuh tix.
24 Nawh tag deuh taix tix ka piang kaing kawn pex mhaing an,
mai pauh ka dee gleeing kix Krax-tan Pa-gleeing plak laong khu dee tan.
In mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
25 Cao giex kaing li tag teei giien taix tix mai cub ka daux nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie in,
mai kraix ka piang rung khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog,
mai baox hieh nham pa kawn deuk khankix ka piang jaong khu dee tan.
26 Khaix an,
nawh tag gleeing bee kawn pex mhaing an khawm uik ka piang khu dee tan,
awm gleeing ka bee Krax-tan Khawng-brie son Krax-tan Pa-nhim yuh nan.
Cao giex kaing li tag hawt tix yuh ka krax an,
yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap simiang naing an,
nawh tag pon pa pluat ploi phi map heue.
27 aPhan koe pui tang kaux tix kaux kaux yuh phi map ka daux pa ang tix tawng,
mai phit lud ka gum lox ping nyap Simiang Siyiex,
28 phan nawh hoik tawng dee phit lud tix,
nawh tag caw tix yuh krax tan ka kawn pex siang pa loig tix mu,
son to kaux tix.
29 Nawh tag deuh taix tix ka piang kaing Krax-tan Khawng-brie an,
mai pauh ka dee gleeing Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka plak laong khu dee tan,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
30 Cao giex kaing li tag teei giien taix tix cub daux nham,
mai kraix ka piang rung khu dee tan dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog,
nham pa kawn deuk khankix tag hieh ka jaong khu dee tan.
31 Khaix an,
nawh tag teei bee pex siang an lih khawm uik,
awm hoik teei bee khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-nhim yuh nan.
Nawh tag teei bee an khawm uik,
deuh ka piang khu dee tan mai gleeing kah,
son sang kieh mawh krax tan pa bhawng pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Cao giex kaing li hawt ka grawng krax in,
mai yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son pui an,
nawh tom pon pa pluat ploi heue.
32 Phan nawh tan ka kawn yung kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
nawh tag caw tix tan ka kawn yung siang pa loig.
33 Nawh tag deuh taix tix ka piang kaing kawn yung Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap an,
mai pauh ka dee gleeing Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka plak laong khu dee tan.
34 Cao giex kaing li tag teei giien taix tix cub daux nham khawng tan an,
mai kraix ka piang rung khu dee tan an,
dee bhlien tix lih khawm pon daog,
mai hieh nham pa kawn deuk piang jaong khu dee tan.
35 Khaix an,
nawh tag teei bee kawn yung an khawm uik lih,
awm hoik teei ka bee kawn yung Krax-tan Pa-nhim yuh nan.
Nawh tag teei khawm uik mai deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan,
gleeing tau mai khawng tan pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Cao giex kaing li tag hawt krax grawng in,
mai yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap pui an,
mai nawh tom pon pa pluat ploi heue.
