Phek tiiem pui yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix shiet
20
“Meung rao ma awm lox lao pui been pa krai plak greem yuh nin:
koe cao nyiex si peem plix shiet tix kaux,
lih hu khaing nyiex tix pon ngawb cao cao,
sawg pui yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix shiet cie nawh.
2 Nawh hawt tix tawx mau luah tix plah ka kix ku ngaix,
awm pa hoik tat tix deuh yuh nan jheeng jheeng,
khaix an,
nhie kix hu yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix shiet.
3 Ka daux pon ngawb been dim taix nari,
nawh baox hu ka daux laih,
mai yaox pui tix blah cung dee an,
ang koe kaing yuh,
4 nawh tom ah nin ka kix:
‘Pex kawx laig yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix shiet heue,
aux tag sang tawx mau riang pex khaw.’
5 Kix tom hu.
Khaix an ka daux greung ngawb kao ra taix nari mai pon ngaix loe taix nari nawh baox hu,
mai baox lo pui tix blah yuh kaing awm sivoe an yuh nan.
6 Yam hoik dex hoek ka daux phuan taix nari,
nawh baox hu ka daux laih,
mai yaox pui kawn cung ka dee an,
nawh tom chawk kix ah nin:
‘Yuh ka mawx pex lai cung dee in leeing lang singaix ang koe kaing yuh lie?’
Kix pawk ah nin:
7 ‘Mawh kheu ang koe pui pa lo yix yuh kaing.’
Nawh tom ah nin ka kix:
‘Phan mawh nan,
pex kawx laig tix yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix shiet cie aux heue.’
8 a“Yam hoek bo praix,
cao nyiex si peem plix shiet ah nin ka cao thung pui yuh kaing:
‘Nhie pui yuh kaing hoek khawm uik,
tawx mau riang kix,
jauh ka pui pa hoek khaix tom hoek ka pui pa hoek sivoe.’
9 Pui an kix pa taug hoek yuh kaing ka daux phuan taix nari,
tawx tix kaux tix plah mau luah.
10 Ka pui an kix pa hoek yuh kaing sivoe hoek tix teei mau riang tix,
kix ce tix sang nie pon,
viang mawh nan,
kix ku kaux teei ngie tix kaux tix plah mau luah khaing nang.
11 Khaix hoik teei kah,
kix salao ka cao nyiex ah nin:
12 ‘Pui in kix pa hoek khaix yuh ngie kaing tix taix nari khaing,
viang mawh nan,
yix hoik yuh leeing lang singaix ka daux pa kied hax koek,
maix lai tawx kix sidu awm yix yuh nin heue!’
13 Cao nyiex si peem pawk lox pui ka daux kix tix kaux ah nin:
‘Paox grawm,
aux ang bax ih riang yie maix.
Ang maix hoik cu aux tawx mau riang tix tix ngaix tix plah mau luah lie?
14 Teei viex mau maix ing ka nyiex heue!
Aux sang tawx pui pa hoek khaix sidu awm maix yuh nan,
15 khex ang aux koe akhuang tix siao ka mau cie tix lie?
Mawh kheu si pieh rhawm aux ka pui,
maix tom boh rhawm ka lie?’ ”
16 bMawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh nin,
pui pa ot khaix tag sang hu sivoe,
pui pa ot sivoe tag sang ot khaix heue.”
Ye-sux grox krai sang yum tix ka daux loe bawg
(Lai Ma-kheu 10.32–34; Lai Lu-cia 18.31–34)
17 Yam haok Ye-sux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
nawh viex pui gau tix kao ra kaux hawt tix,
nawh krai ka kix yam lawng kix krax ah nin:
18 “Pex tag caw tix tawng,
yam in ex haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Kawn kawn pui tag sang coh tix tawx ka simiang cao giex kaing li mai ka sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix;
kix tag sang prawng ca nawh yum,
19 khaix an ap tix tawx nawh ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
Kix tag sang nu nai ka nawh,
si vied ka nawh,
mai tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak.
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux loe singaix,
nawh tag sang kaoh im heue.”
Lox pa khiox khaw miex
(Lai Ma-kheu 10.35–45)
20 Moeng Si-pi-thaix viex kawn simex tix ra kaux hoek ka Ye-sux krub ka nawh,
mai khiox khaw kaing tix ceu heue.
21 Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sang lo maix ceu pa tix?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix cu ka lox aux,
yam mawh maix simiang,
kieh kawn simex aux in kiex tix kaux ngawm ka plak dawm maix,
tix kaux ngawm ka plak goix maix heue.”
22 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex ang tawng ka ceu pa sang khiox khaw tix.
Kawk song pa sang nyaux aux,
sang pon nyaux pax ka lie?”
Kiex pawk ah nin:
“Yiex pon heue!”
23 Ye-sux ah nin ka kiex:
“Pex rug sang nyaux ka kawk pa song aux,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang koe akhuang tix sang kieh pui mawx ngawm ka plak goix plak dawm tix.
Dang ngeung in kix,
keeing aux hoik rien khre son pui mawx tag sang mawh cie pui an heue.”
24 Pui gau tang kaux kao kaux mhong ka lox pa khiox khaw yuh nin,
mai kied song rhawm ka kiex pux aik ra kaux.
25 cYe-sux kok kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoik tawng pui piang haktiex in,
simiang haktiex koe akhuang tix kuang kix giex gang kix.
26 dViang mawh nan,
pex ang pon yuh nin.
Ka daux pex pui mawx sang mawh pui ting ceu,
pui an tag caw tix yuh siliex pex;
27 pui mawx sang mawh cao kaing,
pui an tag caw tix yuh siliex pex.
28 Awm kawn kawn pui yuh nan,
nawh ang hoek son sang kieh pui khan kheu ka tix,
mawh son sang kia ngien ka pui,
khaix an tan ju tix son sang taoh ka pui hun heue.”
Pai pui dug ngai ra kaux moi
(Lai Ma-kheu 10.46–52; Lai Lu-cia 18.35–43)
29 Yam lih hu kix khaing Yie-li-keu,
koe cub pui hun hawt khaix Ye-sux.
30 Koe pui dug ngai ra kaux ngawm ka praok krax,
yam mhong kiex hoek Ye-sux,
tom kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
kawn praix Ta-wue,
caox rhawm ka yiex liak!”
31 Pui hun ne raw ka kiex,
mai ang cu kiex nye;
viang mawh nan,
kiex nied tix kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
kawn praix Ta-wue,
caox rhawm ka yiex liak!”
32 Ye-sux kaog,
mai kok kiex hoek ka tix ah nin:
“Pax sang kieh aux yuh ceu pa tix lie?”
33 Kiex pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh ngai yiex chix yaox heue!”
34 Ye-sux caox rhawm ka kiex,
mai pu pi ka ngai kiex;
kiex chix yaox dum mawx dum an,
tom hawt khaix Ye-sux heue.
