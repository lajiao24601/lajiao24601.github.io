Grawng phi map
(Lai Ma-thaix 18.6–7; 21.22; Lai Ma-kheu 9.42)
17
Ye-sux krai ka pui gau nawh ah nin:
“Kaing ceu pa kieh pui yuh phi map hoik koe tete,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa kieh kaing ceu pa yuh nin tag sang pon krax laux leeing heue!
2 Ang lai lheu khaing teei nawh simaox laing pa ting phrawk ot ka ngawg nawh,
mai kieh nawh com greem rawm sigrit sigra;
phan yuh nin,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing sang kieh nawh pui pa iak khaing paox tix yuh phi map.
3 aPex tag caw tix rien rhawm rhi tix heue!
“Phan pux aik maix yuh phi map,
maix sidiex gau ka nawh;
phan nawh loh rhawm rhi tix,
maix pluat ploi ka nawh.
4 Phan nawh phit lud ka maix ka daux tix ngaix khaing aliah bawg,
mai hoik veeh kaing tix krai ka maix ku bawg ah nin:
‘Aux hoik loh rhawm rhi tix’,
maix tag caw tix va rhawm ka nawh heue.”
Grawng krax yum rhawm
5 Kawn cau khankix krai ka Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
“Thiem thie ka krax yum rhawm yix liak!”
6 Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan pex ca koe krax yum rhawm pie mu simie taux klaox yuh nan khaing,
phan krai ka khaox liah in ah nin:
‘Laoh khawm riah,
viex hu sum daux rawm sigrit sigra heue!’
Khaox an kawx tag sang nghiet ka lox pex heue.”
Beeh glawm siliex
7 “Phan koe pui ka daux pex tix kaux kaux pa sum ka ma kaung,
phan ang mawh nan,
mawh siliex pa liang yung,
yam deeh hoek nawh khaing dee yuh tix kaing,
sang krai ang maix krai ka nawh ah nin ‘Nied hoek ngawm tix sawm?’
8 Maix ang doh ah nin heue!
Maix taug rien tix krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Rien khre priex bo son aux,
rhawt maox nyoi maix khre ka aux,
krox aux ih ha maix tom tag ih khaix heue.’
9 Siliex hawt tix yuh awm lox tawm simiang tix,
khex kawn sang krai simiang an lud lox krai tix ka nawh lie?
10 Pex kawx hoik mawh yuh nan nang.
Yam hoik si dauh yuh pex ka kaing pa hoik tawx Siyiex pex yuh khawm uik,
tag caw tix ah nin:
‘Yix hoik dawg mawh siliex pa ang koe ceu;
yix hoik yuh ngie kaing pa khaw tix yuh khaing heue.’ ”
Pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg moi kao kaux
11 Ye-sux ot ka daux krax pa hu plak Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai laig si nax haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax mai Cia-li-lix.
12 Yam laig nawh ka daux yaong,
koe pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg kao kaux hoek tix rieb ka nawh,
kix kok cung dee singai,
13 mai kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Ye-sux,
sara eue,
caox rhawm ka yix liak!”
14 bYe-sux yaox ka kix,
mai ah nin:
“Pex hu kieh cao giex kaing li lam lia tix yaox ka pex heue!”
Yam hu kix,
jhawm saux kix hoik pon singax.
15 Ka daux kix koe pui tix kaux yaox hoik moi tix,
nawh nied kling tix deeh ing,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
16 mai baox gup tix ka plak sivoe caong Ye-sux yaog yaw ka nawh.
Pui in mawh pui Sa-ma-li-yax.
17 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui pa pon yaox tix moi koe kao kaux,
tang kaux dim kaux ot dee mawx lie?
18 Yuh ka mawx lai mawh ngie pui tang ceu in pa hoek tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex khaing lie?”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Kaoh hu heue!
Krax yum rhawm maix hoik taoh ka maix heue.”
Grawng lih siawp sibrawm Siyiex tix mawh
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.23–28, 37–41)
20 Koe pui Fa-li-saix khankix tix blah chawk ka Ye-sux,
mai simie tix tawng ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex sang hoek tix lih yam mawx.
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pa lih siawp sibrawm Siyiex tix mawh ang mawh pa chix yaox ngai pui.
21 Ang koe pui pa pon ah nin:
‘Jhak heue,
ot dee in!’
Phan ang mawh nan ‘ot dee an!’
Mawh kheu siawp sibrawm Siyiex hoik ot ka daux rhawm rhi pex heuec!”
22 Khaix an,
nawh baox krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Bawg yam tag sang hoek,
pex hoik simie tix yaox ka singaix bawg yam pa lih hoek kawn kawn pui tix giex kaing,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox.
23 Koe pui tag sang krai ka pex ah nin:
‘Jhak heue,
ot dee an!’
Phan ang mawh nan,
‘Jhak heue,
ot dee in!’
Pex paw vi lih tix jhak,
mai paw vi hawt tix leue ka kix.
24 Hoik awm pu plak praix pa naog ka rao ma yuh nan,
jauh ka daog plak in jhan tom hoek ka daog plak an,
singaix bawg yam lih hoek kawn kawn pui kawx hoik yuh nin nang.
25 Viang mawh nan,
nawh tag caw tix ran kham krax kha rhawm tom nie,
mai pui singaix baux ju in tik kah.
26 dHoik awm singaix baux ju Naw-yax yuh nan,
singaix bawg yam kawn kawn pui kawx hoik yuh nan nang.
27 ePui ka daux singaix baux ju an ih nyaux teei tix koe mex koe moeng,
tom hoek singaix pa laig Naw-yax ka daux reu an,
rawm ting hoek tix yuh kix laux leeing khawm uik.
28 fKa daux singaix bawg yam pa lih hoek kawn kawn pui,
mai baox awm ka daux singaix baux ju Law-teux yuh nan;
ka daux singaix baux ju an pui ih nyaux,
liag coh,
sum nang kaung ma mai nyiex ma.
29 Yam hoek ka daux singaix an,
ngu mai mhat lih khaing rao ma,
mai yuh kix laux leeing khawm uik.
30 Singaix yam lih hoek kawn kawn pui kawx tag sang yuh nan nang heue.
31 g“Ka daux singaix an,
pa ot ka piang braux nyiex,
paw vi lih teei kraung khrai ka daux nyiex;
awm an,
pui pa yuh kaing ka daux tiag daux tu kawx,
paw vi ing ka daux nyiex.
32 hTag caw een tix tawng ka ceu pa si pup moeng Law-teux heue!
33 iPui pa keud tix sang taoh ka ju tix,
tag sang tieh tix som ju;
pui pa som ju tix,
tag sang tieh tix pon ju.
34 Aux krai ka pex,
ka daux bun an,
pui ra kaux pa it ka ku tix lhang,
tag sang teei hu tix kaux,
mai prah tix kaux;
35 Bun ra kaux pa krid ka mhaw,
tag sang teei hu tix kaux,
mai prah tix kaux heue.j”
37 Pui gau chawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh ka dee mawx lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Koe to kaux yum dee mawx,
klang preng kawx tag sang khrawm tix ot dee an heue.”
