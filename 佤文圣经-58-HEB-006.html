6
Phan mawh nan,
ex caw tix deuh gum lox grawh gawng pa jauh ex tix tawng ka Khrit ot ka plak khaix tix,
mai veeh tix hu ka plak krax yum rhawm pa grum mhawm.
Ex paw lai sau riang yuh awm ka daux baux yam jauh krax yum rhawm tix yuh nan,
awm ka daux been pa doh keud pa laux leeing,
pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
2 krax cub com pa ang awm paox tix,
pa deuh taix ka piang kaing,
pa deeh kaoh im pui yum,
krax prawng ca leeing ju khankix.
3 Kieh ex veeh tix hu ka plak krax yum rhawm pa grum mhawm heue!
Phan Siyiex cu,
in tag mawh ceu pa sang yuh ex heue.
4 Phan mawh son pui pa tik vud ka krax pa mawh pa meh khankix,
sang pon kieh kix deeh chix loh rhawm tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Kix hoik sax mawh pa pon ka pa riang Siyiex,
cim ka jhawm buan son pa dex dawt mai rao ma,
mai baox pon ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
5 kheu nan pa cim ka gum lox Siyiex hoik mawh pa mhawm tete.
Hoik pon nang tix tawng ka riang rao singaix pa si dauh,
6 yuh ka mawx lai kawn tik vud ka krax pa mawh pa meh.
Pui pa yuh nin ang koe rhawm keud grawng sang kieh kix deeh loh ka rhawm tix,
deeh doh keud doh sawn;
mawh kheu hoik baox tawk kix kawn simex Siyiex ka piang khaox lhak,
map nai nawh ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun heue.
7 Kaung ma tix dauh yawt ka rawm lhiex jheeng jheeng,
kieh taux lhax kuah,
kied mawh pa mhawm son pui pa sum,
mai in hoik mawh buan son pa tawx Siyiex.
8 aViang mawh nan,
phan kaung ma in kuah ka pang kat mai pang kat heeik pa ang koe ceu,
kaung ma in tag sang hoek krax pa jhax lawg,
khaix an tag sang gleeing ka ngu heue.
9 Kheu nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
viang hoik krai yuh nin,
ex ca kawn yum rhawm grawng pa nie blawn mhawm pex,
pa nie blawn dex pex ka krax taoh.
10 Siyiex mawh pa rauh see;
nawh ang chix piawm ka kaing pa yuh pex son nawh,
mai krax muh pex ka nawh,
pa mawh krax pa teem coi pa yuh pex ka kawn Khrit tang kaux ang jud.
11 Krax pa simie ex pa kied ting mawh:
pex ku kaux hoik koe krax rien rhawm tom si dauh,
son sang kieh kaing krax keud pot jun pot pex pon tix lih mawh khawm uik.
12 Paw vi nieh,
tag caw tix hawt ka pui pa yum rhawm pa pon sau ka riang tix khankix,
son sang pon tix rieb ka gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex heue.
Gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex tete te lai
13 Yam tawx Siyiex gum lox pa ap tix ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
nawh hoik sax tiex tu.
Mawh kheu ang koe ceu pa ting ceu khaing to kaux nawh,
nawh tom tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux tix.
14 bNawh ah nin:
“Aux tag tawx buan son ka maix,
tag kieh kawn praix maix haoh hun.”
15 Ya-paw-la-hang viex rhawm gun krox jun,
dee si dauh nawh pon ka gum lox pa deuh Siyiex.
16 Yam tiex tu pui,
nawh tiex tu ka ceu pa lheu ting ceu khaing to kaux tix;
gum lox pa tiex tu in hoik kieh mawh pa tang son krax pa thung thiang pa koe ka daux pui khawm uik.
17 Ka son pui khankix tag sang pon ka gum lox pa deuh in,
Siyiex tag sang krai nawh mhong sidawng,
nawh ang chix tieh loh ka kaing ceu pa sang yuh tix leeing ju,
mawh kheu nan,
tom baox thie gum lox pa tiex tu ka piang gum lox pa hoik deuh in.
18 Grawng in kiex ra ceu ang pon tix tieh loh,
mawh kheu ang Siyiex chix pie lhien.
Mawh kheu nan,
ex pa mawh pui pa khiox khaw nawh kia ngie tix pon krax pa joh jhan,
tag sang giex krax keud pot jun pot pa deuh ot sivoe ex an.
19 cEx hoik koe krax keud pot jun pot in,
awm jauh tix im ju yuh nan,
kaux cong ang chix ghah,
phlat ka man tie nyiex buan piang rao ma,
tom hoek ka daux nyiex buan pa singax singeh.
20 dKheu son ex,
Ye-sux hoik ran mawh pa hoek sivoe,
ran hoek ka dee an;
nawh hoik hawt ka doeh cub nyiex Mai-ci-si-teux mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting heue.
