Lox rawg buan Yaw-na
2
Yaw-na ot daux vaig kax rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex nawh ah nin:
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yam si pup aux krax pa kha rhawm,
aux khiox khaw ka maix,
maix hoik pawk la lox aux.
Aux khiox khaw maix teem coi tix khaing dee raux meung yum,
maix hoik nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux.
3 Maix hoik tik aux ka dee raux rawm;
mai hoek ka dee kied blawn raux;
rawm ting hoik lai lawm ka aux,
puk pak rawm ting maix hoik kheem ka aux.
4 Aux keud,
pui hoik grheu aux hu khaing plak sivoe ngai maix,
ang lai baox yaox ka nyiex buan maix.
5 Rawm ting kheem ka aux,
kieh aux eet;
rawm sigrit sigra hoik kheem aux khawm uik;
rib riem pa ot daux rawm hoik vung vhien tix ka kaing aux.
6 Aux lih hoek ka riah caong gawng lua khankix,
hoek ka dee song jhonga siviex tix leeing ju.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
maix taoh aux lih khaing dee raux meung yum.
7 Yam tawng aux ju to kaux tix hoik dex sang luan hu,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw ka maix;
maix hoik nghiet lox rawg aux buan ka daux nyiex buan;
8 Pui pa krub ka riang rao khankix,
hoik tik vud ka krax pa yum rhawm ka maix.
9 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix;
aux tag sang tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka maix,
mai vied ka can lox pa hoik ap pong tix khawm uik.
Krax taoh hoek ngie khaing Simiang Siyiex tix kaux khaing heue!
10 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox kieh kax ting an hu hao Yaw-na lih ka daux pak mhaik,
kax ting tom hawt tix yuh yuh nan heue.
