Lai Lu-teux
Krai dawt
Lai Lu-teux mawh been pa khaw tix yaog yaw, mai pa kieh pui nyawm rhawm tix phuk, mai teei krax kha rhawm pa ot daux Lai Cao-prawng mawh mhai sakse, krai grawng kaing been prim. Kawn bun Maw-ya pa kaux Lu-teux yuh ka paox mai pui Yi-seu-liex; khaix yum mex nawh, Lu-teux yuh krax nab yam yung yi ka miex mex tix mai Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex; dee nghoik, nawh yaox ka mex khraox ka daux cub ceu pui Yi-seu-liex cie mex tix sivoe an. Jauh ka mex in nawh tieh tix mawh miex yiex du Ta-wue pa mawh simiang ting cub pui Yi-seu-liex.
Ka daux Lai Cao-prawng koe been pa tiiem kawn praix Siyiex tieh vheen ka Siyiex, kix tom yaox ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi. Grawng kaing Lu-teux mawh tiiem pui tang ceu tang cad tix kaux, mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex mai kied pon buan son, mai tieh tix mawh kawn praix Siyiex tix kaux heue.
Lai pa raux daux phuk lai in mawh pa kaoh khaing jheeh Lu-teux: “Khiox khaw maix paw kieh aux lih luan khaing maix liak. Kieh aux hawt hu mai maix! Maix hu dee mawx, aux kawx hu dee an; maix ot dee mawx, aux kawx ot dee an; cub ceu pui maix mawh cub ceu pui aux; Siyiex maix mawh Siyiex aux. Maix yum dee mawx, aux kawx yum dee an, mai si paung ka dee an. Lheu khaing yum, kaing tang ceu kawx ang pon kieh ax guah paox tix! Phan aux lheu lox in, kieh Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka aux tom kin heue!” (1.16–17)
Krai kaing
1. Na-eu-me mai Lu-teux deeh hoek ka haktiex Paw-li-haung (1.1–22)
2. Lu-teux yaox ka Paw-a-see (2.1–3.18)
3. Paw-a-see yuh ka paox mai Lu-teux (4.1–22)
