Plix ruh ra khrawng
24
aNi-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng jauh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
giex laung ka Yaw-ya-cing kawn simex Yaw-ya-cin simiang Yo-tax,
simiang meung Yo-tax,
cang mai,
mai pui chix kaing taix khankix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Khaix hoik yuh kaing in,
Simiang Siyiex kieh aux yaox ka plix ruh ra khrawng deuh tix ot ka plak sivoe ngai nyiex buan.
2 Ka daux tix khrawng hoik sau naog ka plix ruh pa liang tix pon;
an tix khrawng hoik sau naog ka plix ruh pa laux pa ang chix tix ih.
3 Simiang Siyiex chawk aux ah nin:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
yaox maix ceu tix lie?”
Aux pawk ah nin:
“Plix ruh.
Pa mhawm kied mhawm,
pa laux kied laux,
mai hoik laux tom ang lai chix tix ih heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
5 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex tag sang kieh pui pa hoik giex pui tix laung hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix mawh plix ruh pa mhawm,
mai kia ngien ka kix mhawm mhawm.
6 Aux tag sang bre tix kia ngien ka kix,
mai viex kix deeh hoek ka haktiex in.
Aux tag sang ap ka kix,
ang mawh yieh kix laux;
aux sang sum ka kix,
ang doh tik ka kix.
7 Aux tag sang kieh kix koe krax yum rhawm,
mai doh keud ka mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
Kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux,
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix,
mawh kheu kix tag sang hawt ka aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix heue.
8 “Phan mawh Si-ti-cia simiang Yo-tax,
khawm simiang pa ot plak goix plak dawm nawh,
mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa kawn deuk khankix,
viang ot dee in ang ah,
to mox tix hoek ka Ai-cix ang ah,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kix laux awm plix ruh pa ang chix tix ih yuh nan.
9 Aux tag sang kieh kix si pup ka krax laux leeing pa ting,
kieh pui meung haktiex yaoh yee ka khawm uik.
Ka daux haktiex pa ploi aux kix ot,
pui tag nyiah ka kix,
si briem ka kix,
nu nai ka kix,
mai jhax lawg ka kix.
10 Aux tag sang kieh naing,
sibhawm praix,
mai jhawm saux preng yuh kix laux leeing;
ka daux haktiex pa tawx aux ka kix mai ka tax du kix,
mai tix kaux kawx ang lai deuh koe heue.”
