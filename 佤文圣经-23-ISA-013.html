Siyiex sang ciyang ka Pa-pi-loeng
13
aIn mawh gum lox pa pon Yi-sai-yax kawn simex Ya-maw-see khaing Siyiex son Pa-pi-loeng heue.
2 Tag caw tix sum jung man chix naing ot ka piang gawng riem;
tag caw tix tawx lox ka pui naing,
kieh kix yaog plawng taix tix,
son sang mawh mhai sang puing ka veng pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi an.
3 Simiang Siyiex hoik simin kub ka pui naing pa kua an kix pa hoik naog ka krax yum rhawm,
son sang hu puing ka naing pa ting ceu;
nawh tag sang ciyang ka meung pui pa hoik cheeik nawh song rhawm heue.
4 Nghiet ka lox pa riag yo piang gawng an,
an hoik mawh lox pa graox kawn pui,
hoik mawh lox pa kok yo ka daux pa khrawm meung pui tom nie ka paox tix.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik khrawm ka pui naing,
son sang rien khre tix yuh naing heue.
5 Kix hoik mawh pa hoek khaing daog haktiex plak an,
khaing meung pui pa ot dee singai.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang yuh meung an laux leeing ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix heue.
6 bYiiem riag heue!
Singaix bawg yam cie Simiang Siyiex hoik hoek;
singaix bawg yam pa tawx Siyiex pa ting riang ting rao ka krax laux krax leeing hoik dex.
7 Plawng taix pui ku kaux hoik si klua khawm uik;
krax pa kua pui ku kaux hoik grai heue.
8 Kix kied lhat kied graung tete;
kix hoik saux awm pa kha bun yam sang keh tix yuh nan.
Kix lai jhak ka ngai baoh paox tix,
mai nied tix lih mawh krax pa lhat pa graung;
pak ngaix kix si aoh kheu kaig tix.
9 Singaix bawg yam cie Simiang Siyiex hoik koek,
pa mawh singaix bawg yam pa laux leeing,
pa naog ka krax pa song rhawm song rhi hoik lih hoek heue.
Piang haktiex khawm uik tag sang lih mawh daux tiag pa riem;
pui phi map ku kaux tag sang yum khawm uik.
10 cSimuing ku mu ang lai jhan riang;
singaix hoik faik tom yam jauh tix lih,
khix kawx ang lai koe pa riang heue.
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang ciyang ka haktiex in,
ciyang ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui caox caig.
Aux tag sang tieb tie ka krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai yeen dip ka pui pa ting rhawm cau nyhau.
12 Pui pa pon kham ka krax pa cheuk cheu in mai kawn lheu yawm khaing khri pa mhawm.
13 Aux tag sang kieh rao ma si yaung,
kieh haktiex yung yang;
mawh kheu nan,
in mawh aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing,
singaix tawx aux krax pa song rhawm tix ka daux pa rauh pa see heue.
14 “Pui tang meung pa paung ot ka daux veng Pa-pi-loeng tag sang deeh to ing ka nyiex ma tix,
mai prung prang tix tang to,
awm cak pa leue pui jaw yuh nan,
awm phung yung pa ang koe cao liang yuh nan.
15 Phan pui ku kaux yaox tag sang pauh kah;
pui pa hoik pon giex ka ku kaux tag sang yum ka greem lawm vaig.
16 Pui tag sang gaing kawn ngie kix phawng vid ka plak sivoe ngaix kix;
pui tag sang yieh nyiex ma kix laux;
moeng kix tag sang kham pa kaig pa tua heue.”
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux sang cheeik pui Mi-ti-yaxd hu puing ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Pui Mi-ti-yax ang koe rhawm ghoing ka mau luah,
ang muh ka khri.
18 Kix tag sang teei te puing ka pui pa nhum ju;
kix ang lai koe krax caox rhawm ka kawn ngie,
mai ka kawn nyawm iak kawx kix ang lai koe krax muh kah.
19 ePa-pi-loeng sivoe an hoik mawh meung pa haok huan tix meung,
hoik mawh min plak ngai ka pui cie nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang puk pliak ka meung an,
awm hoik yuh aux ka veng Saw-to-ma mai veng Eu-maw-la laux leeing yuh nan.
20 Ang lai koe pui pa paung ot ka dee an;
ang lai koe pui A-la-pawx pa sum nyiex bang tix ot dee an,
mai ang lai koe nang pui pa liang yung dee an heue.
21 fDee an mawh dee to tiag;
pang plawh tag sang yuh mheem tix dee an,
sim ka-la-uk ot dee an,
pex tiag jang luh tix klieh ka daux pum nyiex pa ghrup,
22 Sox la tag sang kok yo ka daux nyiex haw simiang,
sox tiag tag sang raoh ka daux nyiex lhaong.
Krax kha rhawm hoik lih hoek ka piang Pa-pi-loeng heue!
Ka daux singaix bawg yam cie nawh hoik dex sang uik heue!”
