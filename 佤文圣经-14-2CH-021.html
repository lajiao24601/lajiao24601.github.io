21
Yam yum Yaw-sa-fa,
kix si paung nawh ot tau mai si meeig pui nyiex haw simiang ka veng Ta-wue,
kawn simex nawh Yaw-lang plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Yaw-lang simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 8.17–24)
2 Yaw-lang kawn simex Yaw-sa-fa simiang Yo-tax koe pux aik liah kaux;
ceu kaux kix mawh:
Ya-sa-li-ya,
Yie-shiie,
Sa-cia-li-ya,
Ya-sha-lie-fu,
Mi-cia-leux,
See-fa-thi-ya.
3 Kix hoik tawx kix cie khri mau tom nie mai kraung tang ceu pa koe ngawh jum khankix,
tawx kix ku kaux hu kuang ka veng pa kaux cong Yo-tax tix mu.
Kheu mawh Yaw-lang kawn ai,
Yaw-sa-fa tom ap nawh mawh pui pa plud tix.
4 Khaix hoik giex Yaw-lang ka simiang pa mawh tix kaux cong,
mai nawh pauh ka pux aik tix yum khawm uik,
mai pauh nang simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix yum tom nie heue.
5 Yam koe ju Yaw-lang tix ngoe ra num,
nawh mawh simiang,
giex kaing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung daix num.
6 Nawh hawt ka kaing pa ang mhawm pa yuh Ya-ha mai ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mawh kheu hoik teei nawh tix moeng kawn bun Ya-ha tix kaux.
Nawh yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
7 aviang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang rhawm yuh laux leeing ka meung cie Ta-wue;
mawh kheu hoik yuh nawh sijax mukheu tau mai Ta-wue,
hoik ap cu ka nawh,
krai sang kieh doeh ceu cad nawh mawh simiang tix ju hoek tix ju heue.
8 bGlang yam giex Yaw-lang kaing,
Yi-tong tieh vheen ka pui Yo-tax,
mai tang yuh tix lih mawh meung tix mu.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-lang thung simiang naing mai ling law naing hu puing ka Yi-tong.
Pui Yi-tong lai lawm ka kix dee an,
viang mawh nan,
hoek pon bo,
kix yaog tix to.
10 Jauh yam an Yi-tong pon guah tix lih,c
khaing pui Yo-tax.
Ka daux bawg yam an nang,
veng Li-na kawx tieh vheen heue,
mawh kheu Yaw-lang hoik tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix.
11 Nawh kawx kawn sum ka nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak ka dee lhaong haktiex Yo-tax,
kheu nan hoik kieh kawn pui haktiex Yo-tax mai kawn pui haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
cao jhai Yi-li-yax tiiem lai tawx ka Yaw-lang,
mai lox lai an ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex tax du maix Ta-wue ne raw ka maix.
Mawh kheu ang maix baox hawt ka kaing pa mhawm yuh keeing maix Yaw-sa-fa mai tax du maix simiang Ya-sa,
13 mai tieh tix hawt ka kaing pa caox pa caig yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
thung pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix tieh vheen ka Siyiex,
awm thung Ya-ha mai pui pa plud nawh pui Yi-seu-liex tieh vheen ka Siyiex yuh nan.
Maix kawn nie baox pauh ka pux aik tix khankix pa nie blawn mhawm khaing maix.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka pui cie maix,
kawn simex kawn bun maix,
mai moeng iak maix tom kin,
mai baox yuh laux ka sin ngao maix.
15 To kaux cao maix tag sang pon jhawm saux kraung vaig pa lang teung kin,
mai nied tix kin tix ngaix hoek tix ngai,
tom hoek ghlua kraung vaig maix heue.”
16 Koe pui Fe-li-see mai pui A-la-pawx paung ot ka dee ot pui Ku-seu ka hawt praok rawm;
Simiang Siyiex kieh kix hoek puing ka Yaw-lang.
17 Kix laig puing ka Yo-tax,
mai tud rhim ka nyiex haw,
giex ka moeng iak simiang mai kawn simex nawh khankix hu,
mawh ngie Ya-ha-shiex kawn simex pa iak khaing paox tix pa deuk khaing heue.
18 Khaix hoik hoik kaing in,
Simiang Siyiex kieh simiang pon jhawm saux kraung vaig pa lang teung kin;
19 jhawm saux in saux tom hoek been ra num,
tix ngaix hoek tix ngaix nied ot nied kin,
kraung vaig hoik ghlua,
mai kha rhawm tix yum heue.
Pui pa ot greem nawh ang bax phlu pum ngu ting hoek tix khu yum nawh,
awm hoik yuh kawn pui ka tax du nawh yuh nan heue.
20 Yam koe ju Yaw-lang tix ngoe ra num nawh mawh simiang,
mai hoik giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung daix num.
Yam yum nawh ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa laux rhawm son nawh.
Kix si paung nawh ot ka veng Ta-wue,
viang mawh nan,
ang ot ka si meeig pui nyiex simiang heue.
