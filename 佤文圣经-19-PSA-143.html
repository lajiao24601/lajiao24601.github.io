Lox rawg buan pa khiox khaw krax teem coi
143
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg buan aux;
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox pa kok yo aux.
Maix hoik kaux sijax,
khiox khaw maix pawk ka lox aux;
maix mawh pa khaw caw,
khiox khaw maix cu ka lox aux.
2 aKhiox khaw maix paw vi prawng ca aux pa mawh siliex maix,
mawh kheu ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
ang koe pui pa ang lud loe tix kaux kawx heue.
 
3 Pui pa koeng ka aux tuk tiak aux,
hoik pon pex ka aux.
Nawh laung aux ot ka daux nyiex thawng pa faik,
aux awm pui pa hoik yum tom rheeng yuh nan.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ang lai pon baox deeh koe krax keud pot jun pot;
aux hoik laux rhawm rhi heue.
 
5 Aux doh keud ka singaix pa hoik luan;
keud ka kaing pa yuh maix khawm uik,
deeh doh keud ka can kaing pa hoik yuh maix khawm uik.
6 Aux sang yaog taix tix rawg buan ka maix;
aux simie ka maix,
awm simie haktiex pa kroh ka rawm lhiex yuh nan heue.
 
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nied tix nghiet ka lox aux;
aux hoik grai krax pa keud pot jun pot.
Khiox khaw maix paw vi mox tix ang simi ka aux;
phan ang mawh nan,
aux tag awm pui pa cawd tix yum daux tiex yuh nan.
8 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux doh keud ka krax muh maix ku singaix,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm aux ka maix.
Khiox khaw maix jhi krax pa khaw aux tix lawng,
mawh kheu aux een rhawm tix ka maix heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang sawg krax pa kia ngien maix;
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa koeng ka aux.
10 Maix mawh Siyiex aux;
khiox khaw maix sidiex gau aux priex tix hawt ka alo maix.
Khiox khaw maix kia ngien aux ka daux buan son maix,
mai duk thung aux lawng daux krax pa si dawm heue.
 
11 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix hawt tix taoh aux awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix;
maix mawh pa rauh see,
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing krax kha rhawm kha rhi.
12 Hawt awm hoik muh maix ka aux,
khiox khaw maix teei tix tik ka pui pa koeng ka aux.
Khiox khaw maix yuh can pui pa gut kheem ka aux laux leeing khawm uik,
kheu hoik mawh aux siliex maix heue.
