Mee haktiex Cia-nang
34
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
2 nhie nawh krai lox in plak greem ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh ah nin:
“Khaix hoik laig pex ka daux haktiex Cia-nang pa tawx aux ka pex,
mee haktiex pex mawh nin:
3 Plak seh jauh daux tiag Shing vih va tom hoek mee daog haktiex Yi-tong,
mawh pa jauh ka plak seh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog ka plak lih singaix.
4 Khaix an,
deeh veeh tix bleeh ka plak seh diang moing gawng Ya-kheu-la-ping,
laig ka daux tiag Shing,
tom hoek ka plak seh Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax.
Khaix an mai deeh hu plak klip mai plak laong Ha-sa Ya-ta,
baox bleeh ka Ya-meung.
5 Jauh dee an deeh veeh tix hu ka ngawd gawng ka praok Ai-cix,
tom hoek si dauh sidu hawt Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
6 “Mee haktiex plak klip singaix,
mawh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
7 “Mee haktiex plak laong,
jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong hoek ka gawng Heu-eu,
8 baox hoek ka diang moing gawng Ha-ma,
baox phlat hu ka Si-ta-ta
9 mai Si-fe-loeng,
tom si dauh hoek ka Ha-sa Yi-nang.
10 “Mee plak lih singaix jauh ka Ha-sa Yi-nang tom hoek ka See-fang,
11 khaix an hu plak seh hoek ka Li-pi-la plak lih singaix Ya-yin,
baox hoek ka gawng iak plak lih singaix ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix.
12 Khaix an hoek hawt praok klawng Yaw-tang plak seh hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog.
“In tag mawh mee haktiex pex khawm pon daog.”
13 aKhaix an,
Maw-si ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
“In mawh haktiex pa sang pon pex tix cie ka daux krax pa cawk lai,
mawh haktiex pa guah Simiang Siyiex tix tawx ka ceu pui dim ceu plak.
14 Ceu pui Lui-piien mai ceu pui Cia-teux ra ceu,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak hoik pon tix cie ka sin ngao cie kix,
hawt tix guah awm cub nyiex kix,
15 ka haktiex plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang toi veng Yie-li-keu heue.”
Simiang pa koe beeh glawm tix guah ka haktiex
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
17 “Cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng,
sang mawh cao guah haktiex son pui hun.
18 Tix ceu pui tag laug simiang tix kaux teem ka kix.”
19-28 In plak greem mawh pui pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex tix siao:
Ceu pui Yo-tax:
Cia-leux kawn simex Yie-fu-ni
Ceu pui Si-miien:
See-mu-lix kawn simex Ya-mi-fu.
Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming:
Yi-li-ta kawn simex Ci-see-loeng
Ceu pui Tang:
Pu-ci kawn simex Yaw-lix
Kawn praix Yaw-seux ceu pui Ma-na-si:
Hang-niie kawn simex Yi-fu.
Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien:
Ci-mu-lix kawn simex See-fu-tang.
Ceu pui Si-pu-loeng:
Yi-li-sha-fang kawn simex Pha-nax.
Ceu pui Yi-sa-cia:
Pha-thiex kawn simex A-sang.
Ceu pui Ya-seux:
Ya-si-fu kawn simex See-law-mi.
Ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix:
Pi-ta-he kawn simex Ya-mi-fu.
29 In kix plak piang mawh pui pa ciak Simiang Siyiex pa guah haktiex Cia-nang son pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
