5
Yam an,
koe cao jhai ra kaux,
pa mawh Ha-kai mai kawn simex Yi-to pa kaux Sa-cia-li-yax,
sidiex pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka daux haktiex Yo-tax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
2 aSaw-law-pa-paw kawn simex Sa-la-thiie mai Yie-su-yax kawn simex Yaw-sa-ta,
mhong ka lox pa sidiex kix,
tom jauh tix cox sum ka nyiex buan pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
cao jhai ra kaux teem coi ka kix heue.
3 Ka daux bawg yam an,
Ta-nai simiang haktiex Heu-si,
See-tha Paw-see-nai,
khawm paox pa yuh nawh kaing tau khankix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Mawh mawx pa tawx lox kieh pex cox sum ka nyiex buan in lie?”
4 Kixb baox mhaing ka ca rang can pui pa teem kix sum ka nyiex buan.
5 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex kia ngien ka simiang kuad song pui Yo-thaix;
simiang Paw-see khankix but rhawm tix krox jun kix tawx lai an ka Vawng Ta-lio-see,
khaix hoik pon mhong kix ka lox lai an,
kix taug yaog tix yuh.
6 In plak greem mawh lox lai pa tawx kix ka Vawng:
7 “Vawng Ta-lio-see,
kieh Vawng pon krax pa nhim tom lang bawg yam!
8 Yix sang khaw tix krai ka maix Vawng,
yix hoik hu ka haktiex Yo-tax,
mai yaox ka nyiex buan Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong ca kawn sum tix yam in.
Nyiex buan in mawh pa sum tix ka simaox pa ting,
mai koe lat ting pa chup tix laig daux ding plak daux.
Pa yuh kaing in hoik mawh pa kied rien khre tix,
mai baox pon yaox tix mhawm tom si dauh heue.
9 Yix sang kieh simiang pui kuad ju krai yix mhong,
mawh mawx pa tawx lox kieh pex deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan an.
10 Yix kawx hoik mhaing ka ca rang kix,
yuh nin,
yix tom pon yaox tix krai ka Vawng,
mawh mawx pa mawh cao thung pui yuh kaing in heue.
11 Kix pawk ah nin:
‘Yix mawh siliex Simiang Siyiex pa yuh rao ma haktiex.
Yam in yix cox sum nyiex buan,
hoik dawg mawh pa yuh mai pa sum simiang pa ting siawp sibrawm pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux.
12 cMawh kheu tax du yix hoik cheeik tix kieh Siyiex piang rao ma song rhawm song rhi,
Siyiex tom kieh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
pa mawh simiang Cia-leu-tix tix kaux pon kuang ka yix.
Nyiex buan in hoik laux,
mai giex laung ka pui haoh pui hun,
mai vung viang hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng.
13 dKhaix an,
yam mawh Sai-lu-seex simiang Pa-pi-loeng Vawng ka daux tix num,
nawh tawx lox nhie pui cox sum ka nyiex buan in,
14 mai deeh tawx mau khri kraung khrai pa hoik tud rhim Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hu khaing nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa hoik deuh ot ka nyiex vad Pa-pi-loeng.
Vawng Sai-lu-seex tawx kraung khrai in kix ka pui pa kaux Seu-pa-sax tix kaux;
nawh mawh pui pa tawx Vawng Sai-lu-seex mawh simiang haktiex Yo-tax.
15 Vawng tawx lox niex kix deeh viex kraung khrai in kix ing ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai deeh cox sum nyiex buan pa khraox ot ka dee haktiex pa prim.
16 Kheu nan,
Seu-pa-sax deeh hoek,
mai sum ka jaong nyiex buan;
kaing in jauh tom yam an hoek yam in,
ca kawn yuh tix ang jud,
mai ang bax yaox tix pon yuh hoik heue.’
17 Yam in,
phan Vawng khaw rhawm kah,
tawx pui hu si kaw ka lai pa tiiem ka nyiex haw simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
jhak tix yaox Vawng Sai-lu-seex,
hoik ang nawh hoik tawx pui sum nyiex buan pa ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung in,
khaix an deeh pawk ka yix,
mai krai rhawm keud maix Vawng kaing in ka yix heue.”
