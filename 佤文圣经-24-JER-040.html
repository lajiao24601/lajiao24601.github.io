Yie-li-mi paung ot tau mai Ci-ta-lix
40
Khaix hoik ploi Ni-pu-sha-la-tang cao kaing pui pau aux ka haktiex La-max,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
Maox ceng hoik prawk ka aux,
mai viex aux hoek ka haktiex La-max tau mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai pui Yo-tax hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng heue.
2 Cao kaing pui pa pau kok aux hu ka tix,
mai ah nin ka aux:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix hoik krai pao tag sang yuh haktiex in laux leeing,
3 yam in nawh hoik hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai nawh.
In hoik mawh kheu krax pa phit lud pex ka Simiang Siyiex,
ang nghiet ka gum lox tawx nawh.
4 Yam in aux hoik kah maox ceng khaing taix maix,
kieh maix pon tix luat luan.
Phan maix sang hu tau mai aux ka Pa-pi-loeng,
chix tix hu,
aux chix tix kia ngien ka maix mhawm mhawm.
Phan maix ang cu tix hu,
tag chix tix ang siao hu.
Haktiex khawm uik hoik ot sivoe ngai maix,
maix muh tix hu ot dee mawx,
tag chix tix hu heue.”
5 Aux ang bax pawk,
Ni-pu-sha-la-tang baox ah nin:a
“Maix deeh ing ka kawn saux Sa-fang,
kawn simex Ya-si-kang pa kaux Ci-ta-lix dee an heue!
Simiang Pa-pi-loeng hoik tawx nawh mawh simiang haktiex Yo-tax ku mu.
Maix chix tix ot dee ot nawh dee an,
mai paung ot ka daux paox ceu paox cad tix.
Phan maix muh tix sang hu ka haktiex tang dauh,
chix tix hawt awm pa simie maix tix hu yuh nan nang.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tawx khawng phak,
mai priex preem ka aux,
khaix an ploi aux hu.
6 Aux hu sawg Ci-ta-lix ka haktiex Mi-see-pa,
mai paung ot tau mai paox ceu paox cad pui haktiex tix heue.
Ci-ta-lix mawh simiang ting pa kuang kaing
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 25.22–24)
7 bKoe simiang pui Yo-tax tix blah,
mai pui naing pa ang bax kreet tix ot greem pui khankix,
mhong ka grawng pa hoik tawx simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ci-ta-lix mawh simiang ting Yo-tax pa kuang kaing,
mai kieh nawh kuang ka pui pa blawn hot pa ang bax giex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
cao kaing in kix mawh khawm Yi-seu-ma-lix kawn Simex Ni-thang-ya,
Yaw-ha-nang kawn simex Cia-li-yax,
Si-lai-ya kawn simex Tang-hu-mawx,
kawn simex pui Ni-thaw-fax pa kaux Yi-fe khankix,
mai Yie-sha-ni-yax pui Ma-cia,
kix ploi pa mawh cub pui tix hoek ka Ci-ta-lix ka haktiex Mi-see-pa.
9 Ci-ta-lix krai lox pa kaux cong ka kix ah nin:
“Pex paw vi lhat ka kreet tix ot greem pui Pa-pi-loeng;
pex ca tawng ngie tix hu paung ot dee an khaing,
mai yuh kaing son simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
pex tag sang yaox krax pa nyawm ot khawm uik heue.
10 “To kaux aux tag sang paung ot ka haktiex Mi-see-pa;
yam hoek pui Pa-pi-loeng,
aux tag sang mawh pui pa krai lox tang pex.
Pex chix tix priex tix deuh ka plai,
ka plix khaox,
ka bee sime,
mai deuh ot ka daux veng pa hoik pon pex.”
11 Yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex pa to mox tix hoek ka haktiex Maw-ya,
haktiex Ya-meung,
haktiex Yi-tong,
mai haktiex tang dauh khankix,
mhong grawng pa hoik cu simiang Pa-pi-loeng pui Yi-seu-liex pa kawn deuk khankix paung ot ka Yo-tax,
mai pa hoik tawx Ci-ta-lix mawh simiang ting pa kuang kaing piang kix.
12 Pui hun tom lih hu khaing haktiex pa vuk vied vuk viang an,
mai deeh hoek ka haktiex Yo-tax.
Kix hoek ka Ci-ta-lix ka haktiex Mi-see-pa,
mai priex tix deuh ka plai mai plix khaox tom nie heue.
Pui pauh ka Ci-ta-lix
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 25.25–26)
13 Khaix hoik luan kaing in,
Yaw-ha-nang mai cao kaing pui naing pa ang bax kreet tix ot greem pui,
hoek ka Ci-ta-lix ka haktiex Mi-see-pa,
14 mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Hoik tawng maix simiang Ya-meung pa kaux Pa-li-see ploi Yi-seu-ma-lix hoek tix pauh ka maix lie?”
Ci-ta-lix ang yi ka lox in.
15 Khaix an,
Yaw-ha-nang briex mox krai ka Ci-ta-lix ah nin:
“Maix cu aux hu pauh ka Yi-seu-ma-lix,
mai ang koe pui pa tawng mawh pui mawx pa pauh.
Yuh ka mawx lai sang kieh nawh pauh ka maix lie?
Phan pui pauh ka maix,
pui tag sang grheu pui pa ot sivie siveng maix hu;
pui Yo-tax pa kawn deuk khankix tag sang laux leeing heue.”
16 Viang mawh nan,
Ci-ta-lix pawk ah nin:
“Aux ang kieh maix yuh nin.
Maix nieb pon ah ka Yi-seu-ma-lix heue!”
