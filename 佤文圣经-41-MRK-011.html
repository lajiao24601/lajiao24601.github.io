Koe min plak ngai laig ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.1–11; Lai Lu-cia 19.28–40; Lai Yaw-hang 12.12–19)
11
Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh hu dex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hoek ka Paw-fa-chix mai Paw-ta-ni baoh haktiex gawng sime.
Ye-sux tawx pui gau tix ra kaux ran hu sivoe,
2 mai tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Pex hu ka nyiex yaong pa koe plak sivoe pex.
Yam laig pex daux yaong,
pex tag sang yaox ka kawn la tix mu pa ang pui bax brug phrawk tix dee an.
Pex kah ka kawn la an,
tuk hoek dee in.
3 Phan koe pui pa chawk pex ah nin:
‘Yuh ka mawx lai yuh kaing pa yuh nin?’
Pex tom krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Simianga sang siao,
khaix hoik siao,
tag sang deeh hoek tawx ka dum mawx dum an heue.’ ”
4 Kix hu,
mai yaox kawn la pa phrawk tix ot plak praix siviex ka praok krax tix mu.
Yam taug sang kah kix maox,
5 koe pui pa cung dee an tix blah chawk kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai kah ka kawn la in?”
6 Kix tom hawt tix pawk awm lox pa tawx Ye-sux,
pui an kix tom kieh kix tuk la hu.
7 Kix tuk kawn la an hoek ka Ye-sux dee an,
mai teei kraung ceep tix tawm piang blawg la,
Ye-sux brug kah.
8 Koe pui tom nie teei kraung ceep tix tawm ka daux krax,
koe pui tix blah teei kak khaox pa kid hoek khaing daux tiag tawm ka daux krax.
9 bPui pa hu sivoe mai pa hawt khaix kok yo ah nin:
“Rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex heue!
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka pui an pa hoek tix krub ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex!
10 Kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka meung an pa tag sang lih hoek ka tax du ex Ta-wue heue!
Rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong heue!”
11 Ye-sux hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
laig daux nyiex buan,
mai lam lia tix yaox ku dauh.
Mawh kheu hoik lha praix klawng,
nawh tom lih khaing veng tau mai kawn cau tix kao ra kaux hu ka Paw-ta-ni heue.
Jhax lawg ka khaox ruh
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.18–19)
12 Ka daux singaix khaix,
kix kaoh khaing Paw-ta-ni mai ing ka veng;
ka daux krax,
Ye-sux cai rhawm.
13 Nawh yaox ka plak sivoe ngai tix ka dee ang singai,
koe khaox ruh tix gawng,
lhax kied yeem,
nawh tom hu,
mai sang jhak tix yaox khaox an koe ang koe plix.
Nawh hoek dex ka khaox an,
mai yaox ngie lhax khaing,
mawh kheu bawg yam an ang mawh bawg yam pa plix khaox ruh.
14 Ye-sux ah nin ka khaox ruh an:
“Jauh yam in,
ang lai baox koe pui pa ih ka plix maix heue!”
Pui gau nawh hoik mhong lox in khawm uik heue.
Ye-sux kieh nyiex buan singax singeh
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.12–17; Lai Lu-cia 19.45–48; Lai Yaw-hang 2.13–22)
15 Kix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
Ye-sux laig ka daux nyiex buan,
mai grheu ka can pui pa coh cai lai gee ka daux nyiex buan lih.
Nawh klup tix tik ka pheen cie pui pa loh ka mau luah mai ka ngot cie pui pa coh ka si kaux khankix,
16 mai ang cu pui tix kaux kawx gang gao kraung khrai lai ling hu ka biang brawk nyiex buan.
17 cNawh sidiex gau kix ah nin:
“Phuk lai sigang khri hoik tiiem ka gum lox Siyiex ah nin:
‘Nyiex buan aux tag sang kieh mawh nyiex dee rawg kawn pui piang haktiex buan’,
viang mawh nan,
pex lai kieh lih mawh dee ot pui briex pui lak heue!”
18 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix mhong lox in,
kix tom keud tix sang pauh ka Ye-sux.
Viang mawh nan,
kix lhat ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik khaw rhawm pui hun ka gum lox pa sidiex nawh heue.
19 Plak pon bo,
Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh lih hoek plak praix veng heue.
Pon lox sidiex gau khaing khaox ruh
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.20–22)
20 Ka daux ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh baox hu lawng ka daux krax an,
mai yaox ka khaox ruh an hoik kroh yum khawm riah tix.
21 Pi-teux een tix tawng ka grawng kaing in,
nawh tom krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
maix jhak,
khaox ruh pa jhax lawg maix hoik kroh yum heue.”
22 Ye-sux pawk ka kix ah nin:
“Caw tix koe krax yum rhawm ka Siyiex heue!
23 dAux krai ka pex tom sidawng,
phan pex ah nin ka gawng in:
‘Kaoh,
sau maix daux rawm sigrit sigra heue!’
Ca mawh ang lai kieh rhawm koe ra mu,
mai yum rhawm ka grawng sang lih tix mawh,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh son pex.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
yam rawg pex buan,
rawg ceu pa tix ang ah,
phan koe krax yum rhawm tag sang pon kah,
mai pex tag sang pon ka ceu pa rawg tix.
25 fYam cung pex tix rawg buan,
tag caw tix ran pluat ploi ka pui phit lud ka pex sivoe;
yuh nin keeing pex piang rao ma kawx tag sang pluat ploi ka krax pa lud loe pex heue.e”
Chawk cheng ka akhuang cie Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.23–27; Lai Lu-cia 20.1–8)
27 Kix baox hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Yam hu Ye-sux ka daux nyiex buan,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai kuad song khankix hoek ka nawh,
28 chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh maix kaing in kix ka daux akhuang cie pui mawx?
Mawh mawx pa tawx akhuang kieh maix yuh kaing in kix lie?”
29 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux ran chawk pex ka lox tix gum,
phan pex pon pawk ka aux,
aux tag krai ka pex yuh aux kaing in kix ka daux akhuang cie pui mawx.
30 Krai ka aux,
akhuang cie Yaw-hang cao cub com mawh kaoh khaing dee mawx?
Mawh kaoh khaing Siyiex mawh kaoh khaing kawn pui lie?”
31 Kix jauh tix thung thiang ka paox tix ah nin:
“Ex sang pawk ah ka mawx lie?
Phan ex ah nin ‘Kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an’,
nawh tee ah nin:
‘Phan mawh nan,
pex lai ang yum rhawm ka Yaw-hang yuh ka mawx lie?’
32 Phan ex krai ‘kaoh khaing kawn pui dee an’,
ex lhat pui hun tieb tie ka ex,
mawh kheu kix hoik yum rhawm grawng mawh Yaw-hang cao jhai.”
33 Mawh kheu nan,
kix pawk lox Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yix ang tawng heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Phan mawh nan,
aux kawx ang krai ka pex yuh aux kaing in ka daux akhuang cie pui mawx heue.”
