37
Bhaung ting kieh rhawm aux graoh ang jud,
tiied tix hu khaing dee ot sivoe an.
2 Nghiet heue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox Siyiex,
nghiet ka lox tuh sah pa kaoh khaing daux jheeh nawh an.
3 Nawh tawx pa riang tuh sah,
kieh jao ka rao ma,
jauh ka daog haktiex plak in tom hoek ka daog plak an.
4 Khaix an,
pui mhong lox luah nawh,
lox luah pa kied ting siawp tuh sah,
si diiet tix riang ang jud.
5 Yam tawx Siyiex lox tix,
kaing pa daox pa deng tom lih hoek,
hoik mawh kaing pa ang ex pon tawng.
6 Nawh tawx lox kieh rha lih koe ka piang haktiex,
nawh kawx kieh lhiex ting lih.
7 Nawh kieh pui naox kaing yuh tix,
kieh pui tawng ka kaing yuh tix.
8 To sat laig ka daux dawh rang,
mox tix ot ka daux ot kix.
9 Bhaung ting hoek khaing caong ma seh,
kuat praix lih hoek khaing caong ma laong.
10 Siyiex phlu bhawm tix,
mai kieh rawm klon tix,
kieh rawm lih mawh khliep si dap pa kiien.
11 Nawh kieh pai awm khrawm naog ka bhawm rawm,
kieh pa riang sah lih khaing daux jan pai awm.
12 Kix kling tix hawt ka alo pa tawx Siyiex;
kix hawt awm lox pa tawx Siyiex lai ling ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
13 Siyiex ploi lhiex lih caux ka haktiex,
jhi grawng krax muh tix ka kawn pui;
nawh kawx ploi lhiex kieh mawh krax ciyang ka kawn pui heue.
 
14 Yaw-paw eue,
bre tix nghiet heue;
viex maix yien yawg tix vud tix cu ha,
keud kaing yuh Siyiex pa daox pa deng.
15 Hoik tawng maix Siyiex tawx lox yuh ka mawx,
nawh kieh pa riang sah lih jhan riang khaing daux jan pai awm yuh ka mawx lie?
16 Maix tawng kaing pa daox pa deng yuh Siyiex,
nawh kieh pai awm chuh ka daux rao ma yuh ka mawx lie?
17 Ang,
yam phlu bhaung caong ma plak seh tix hoek,
haktiex kied si aoh,
to kaux maix hoik dex sang proe heue.
18 Maix pon teem Siyiex tawm ka rao ma,
kieh rao ma kiien awm tawng si liag pa toh riang lie?
19 Krai lox sidiex gau ka yix son sang si kah ka Siyiex yuh ka mawx;
yix daox aw,
ang tawng lox sang krai tix.
20 Aux ang pon khiox khaw tix si kah tau mai Siyiex;
khex koe pui pa cu tix sawg tix hoek krax laux krax leeing lie?
 
21 Yam in singaix,
pa riang daux rao ma jhan riang,
mai jhan riang tom ang yix lai pon maong kaing tix jhak;
bhaung kuat phlu tix hu,
daux rao ma blang.
22 Ka caong ma plak laong koe pa riang pa mhawm singaix jhan tix lih;
siawp sibrawm min plak ngai Siyiex kieh yix lhat graung.
23 Siawp sibrawm riang rao cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik kied ting,
yix ang pon hoek dex;
nawh khaw caw rauh see,
mai ang gut kheem ka pui.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
pui ku kaux tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka nawh khawm uik;
nawh ang jhak simi ka pui pa een tix koe krax lhak pa lhiao khankix heue.
