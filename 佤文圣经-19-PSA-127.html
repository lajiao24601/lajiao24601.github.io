Yaog yaw ka pa mhawm pa mhiem Siyiex
127
Phan ang mawh Simiang Siyiex pa sum nyiex,
kaing yuh cao pa sum tag mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik;
phan ang mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kia ngien ka veng,
kaing yuh cao pa pau tag mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik.
2 Kaoh tom cao it tom lhax,
leeing lang singaix taug mao son krax ot krax koe tix,
mai hoik mawh pa giex pa going,
mawh kheu nan Simiang Siyiex tawx krax pa laig it laig tom ka pui pa muh nawh ka heue.
 
3 Kawn simex kawn bun hoik mawh khawng phak pa tawx Simiang Siyiex;
kawn saux hoik mawh buan son pa tawx nawh ka ex.
4 Kawn simex pa pon keh yam nhum ju tix,
awm te pa koe ka daux tawm taix pui naing.
5 Pui pa naog te in ka daux hau kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
Nawh simah tau mai pui koeng ka diang siviex veng,
mai ang yaox tix hoek krax som nai heue.
