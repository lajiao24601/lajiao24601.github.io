Lai Yi-si-ciex
Riang su sai pa jauh Yi-si-ciex tix yaox
(1.1–7.27)
Dang ngeung Siyiex
1
bKa daux tix ngoe numa haok khix pon daux phuan ngaix,
aux paung ot tau mai pui Yo-thaix pa vuk vied vuk viang ka praok rawm klawng Cia-pa-lu haktiex Pa-pi-loeng.
Yam an,
rao ma hoik baoh,
mai aux yaox riang su sai Siyiex.
2 cKaing in lih tix mawh khaix hoik giex pui ka simiang Yaw-ya-cin ka daux phuan num.
3 Aux pa mawh kawn simex Pu-si,
cao giex kaing li Yi-si-ciex pa ot praok rawm klawng Cia-pa-lu haktiex Pa-pi-loeng mhong pa si kah Simiang Siyiex ka aux,
khaix an mai hoik mhong ka riang rao nawh hoik naog ka aux heue.
4 Aux maong kaing tix jhak,
mai yaox ka bhaung ting pa hoek tix phlu khaing haktiex plak laong;
koe ngu sah lih khaing jan dee pum pai awm tix,
jan pai awm an kied riang khawm pon daog,
hoik riang sawng awm tawng yuh nan.
5 dAux yaox to sat ka daux sigreung bhaung ting an pon mu:
greeh grang kix awm pui,
6 viang mawh nan,
kix hoik tang koe pak ngai pon mu,
preeik pon mu.
7 Caong kix rauh,
gip kix awm gip moi,
hoik awm tawng pa awt plang yuh nan.
8 Lheu khaing hoik tang koe kix pak ngai pon mu,
preeik pon mu,
kix kawn koe taix pui pon khawn,
pa tang ot ka greem preeik ku mu.
9 To sat an kix ku mu hoik nih preeik tix lih plak praix tix brawm,
mai ra lai si doe paox tix lih mawh pon plak yuh nan,
yam yaog tix hu tau,
ang siao kling ka to kaux tix heue.
10 eTo sat an kix hoik tang koe pak ngai pon mu:
ka plak ngai awm pui,
ka plak dawm awm sivai sang si,
ka plak goix awm moi,
ka plak khaix awm klang.
11 Preeik kix tang koe ku brawm yah tix lih,
mai si doe tix tau mai preeik pa dex tan,
mai baox koe preeik tix brawm pa tawb kheem ka to kaux kix.
12 Plak ngai to sat an kix ku mu veeh tix jhak khawm pon daog,
mawh kheu nan chix tix yaog tix hu tau awm pa simie tix hu yuh nan,
mai ang siao kling ka to kaux tix heue.
13 Ka sigreungf to sat an kix pon mu koe ceu pa awm gaox ngu pa cap yuh nan,
awm ngu proh pa chuh ang jud yuh nan;
ngu proh hoik cap lih khaing,
mai hoik pu plak tix jhan riang.
14 To sat an pu plak sivoe mai plak khaix,
hoik brax awm pu plak praix yuh nan heue.
15 hYam lam lia aux ka to sat an kix pon mu,
mai yaox ka praokg to sat an kix,
jaong ling pa cux tiex hoik tang koe ka ku mu.
16 Pak peung jaong ling an kix awm paox tix,
mai hoik toh riang awm siang simaox yuh nan.
Ka piang jaong ling an kix ku mu baox koe nang jaong ling,
mai ra lai yia tix teem paox tix;
17 Yuh nin,
jaong ling an pon mu hoik chix yia tix hu khawm ku plak,
ang siao kling ka to kaux tix.
18 iKa pang jaong ling an khawm uik hoik mawh ngai pui pa naog ka tom nie.
19 Yam yaog to sat an pon mu tix hu,
jaong an pon mu hoik hawt yaog tix hu nang;
yam yaog to sat an tix hu khaing piang tiex,
jaong ling an kawx yaog tix nang.
20 To sat an sang hu dee mawx,
jaong ling an kawx sang hu dee an,
mawh kheu hoik giex gang to sat an ka jaong ling an.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
yam yaog to sat an tix hu,
yam kaog,
phan ang mawh nan yam yaog tix hu khaing piang tiex,
jaong ling an tag hawt tix hu nang,
kaog nang,
mai yaog tix hu khaing piang tiex nang heue.
22 jKa piang kaing to sat an pon mu koe ceu pa lawm le pa awm pa yuh pui ka siang simaox pa toh riang.
23 Ka greem ceu pa lawm le cung ka to sat in pon mu;
kix tang nih preeik tix lih ku brawm,
mai si doe tix ka preeik pa dex tan,
mai baox koe preeik tang mu tix brawm pa tawb kheem ka to kaux tix.
24 kAux mhong lox luah preeik kix yam yaog kix tix pu;
lox luah an awm lox puk plak rawm sigrit sigra yuh nan,
mai awm nang ka lox si num pui naing pa ting,
mai baox awm nang lox Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm.
Yam naox kix pu tix,
mai kix tag deeh nawp ka preeik tix,
25 viang mawh nan,
ca kawn koe lox luah pa kaoh khaing lawm le pa ot ka piang kaing kix heue.
26 lKa piang lawm le koe dang ngeung tix mu awm pa teei pui siang simaox klai rao ma yuh kah;
pa ngawm ka piang awm pui yuh nan.
27 mAux yaox nyoi nawh haok plak piang awm pa mawh tawng pa hoik awt plang yuh nan,
plak daux khawm plak praix hoik awm pa mawh ngu khawm uik yuh nan;
nyoi nawh lih plak greem awm ngu yuh nan nang,
mai hoik jhan riang khawm pon daog.
28 Pa jhan riang in hoik awm riang si yawng mukheu yuh nan.
In hoik mawh pa jhi ka pa riang min plak ngai yam lih hoek Simiang Siyiex heue.
