Pui naing cie Ta-wue hoik puing pex
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 18.1–17)
8
Khaix an,
Ta-wue baox hu puing ka pui Fe-li-see,
hoik pon pex ka kix,
kieh kix ang lai koe siawp sibrawm tix pon kuang ka haktiex in heue.
2 Nawh baox pon pex ka pui Maw-ya.
Nawh nhie kix noi tix it piang tiex,
teei maox tiag kix kah,
mai pauh yum ka pa hoik tiag ka maox ra nyaing,
mai deuh klawd ka pa tiag ka maox tix nyaing.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Maw-ya hawt rhawm Ta-wue,
mai tawx keen ka nawh heue.
3 Ta-wue baox pon pex ka simiang Saw-pa kawn simex Li-haw pa kaux Ha-ta-ti-shiex.
Yam thung Ha-ta-ti-shiex pui naing tix hu hawt rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
son sang keud nawh tix sang deeh pon kuang ka haktiex tix,
4 Ta-wue pon giex ka pui naing brug brung nawh tix rheng aliah yieh kaux,
pui naing hu ka caong tix ra mheen kaux.
Brung pa pon giex kix,
Ta-wue hoik deuh ka brung tom kub tix bluh ling law naing tix yieh lhang,
mai mug dut ka gip si niag brung pa kawn deuk khankix heue.
5 Yam ploi pui Shui-li-yax pui naing hu khaing veng Ta-ma-see-keux teem ka simiang Ha-ta-ti-shiex,
Ta-wue puing ka kix,
mai pon pauh kix yum ra mheen ra rheng kaux.
6 Khaix an,
Ta-wue kieh pui naing cie tixa ot ka daux haktiex kix;
kix hawt rhawm Ta-wue khawm uik,
mai tawx keen ka nawh.
Simiang Siyiex kieh Ta-wue ang lai koe pui pa koeng pa ang nawh pex,
mai hoik puing pex ku dauh ku dee heue.
7 Ta-wue hoik pon teei ka dieb tie naok khri pa viex simiang naing pui Ha-ta-ti-shiex khankix,
mai viex hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
8 mai baox pon teei tawng tom nie ka veng pa kuang pui Ha-ta-ti-shiex,
pa mawh veng Pi-tha mai veng Pi-law-tha heue.
9 Simiang Ha-ma pa kaux Thaw-yi mhong grawng pa hoik pon pex Ta-wue ka pui naing Ha-ta-ti-shiex khawm uik,
10 tom ploi kawn simex tix pa kaux Yaw-lang hu chawk simiang Ta-wue mhawm ot,
mai ah lox pa yaog yaw grawng hoik puing pex nawh (sivoe an,
Thaw-yi mai Ha-ta-ti-shiex hoik yie puing ka paox tix).
Yaw-lang viex khawng phak lak sawng pa hoik yuh ka khri,
mau,
tawng khankix mai tawx ka Ta-wue.
11-12 Ta-wue tom rieb ka khawng phak lak sawng in kix mai ka khawm khri pa hoik pon tud tix khaing meung tang meung.
Pa mawh kraung pa teei khaing meung Yi-tong,
Maw-ya,
Ya-meung,
Fe-li-see,
Ya-ma-lix,
mai kawn koe tix blah hoik mawh pa pon kuang khaing simiang Saw-pa pa kaux Ha-ta-ti-shiex dee an,
mai hoik tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik,
son sang kieh mawh pa lo tix siao yam yuh krax krub heue.
13 Ta-wue pauh pui Yi-tong yum ka ngawd kih tix mheen daix rheng kaux,
khaix hoik hoek nawh,
ceu kaux nawh kied ting ao min.
14 Nawh tawx pui naingb cie tix ot ka daux haktiex Yi-tong ku dauh.
Pui haktiex an hoik hawt rhawm nawh khawm uik.
Simiang Siyiex kieh Ta-wue ang lai koe pui pa koeng pa ang nawh pex,
mai hoik puing pex ku dauh ku dee heue.
15 Ta-wue kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai nawh prawng rauh see son kawn pui.
16 Kawn simex Si-lu-ya pa kaux Yaw-ya mawh simiang naing.
Kawn simex Ya-si-lui pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa mawh cao kaing been.
17 Kawn simex Ya-si-thu pa kaux Sa-tu mai kawn simex Ya-pi-ya-tha pa kaux Ya-si-mi-leux mawh cao giex kaing li.
Si-lai-ya mawh cao tiiem lai ka daux nyiex simiang.
18 Kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya mawh cao giex kaing lien cie Ta-wue.
Kawn simex Ta-wue khankix mawh cao giex kaing li heue.
