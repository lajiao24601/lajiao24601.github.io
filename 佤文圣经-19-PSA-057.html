Lox rawg buan khiox khaw krax teem coia
57
bSiyiex eue,
caox rhawm ka aux,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux,
mawh kheu hoek aux tix deuh rhawm ka maix.
Aux sang khiox khaw krax kia ngien ka greem preeik maix,
krox jun tom hoik luan hu bhaung ting krax laux krax leeing heue.
 
2 Aux rawg mhaing ka Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm,
khiox khaw ka Siyiex cao pa tawx ceu pa lo aux khawm uik.
3 Nawh tag sang cu nghiet ka lox aux khaing daux rao ma,
mai taoh teem ka aux;
nawh tag sang puing ka pui pa gut kheem aux dak tix deeh.
Siyiex tag sang jhi ka krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix,
mai pa kaux sijax tix ka aux heue.
 
4 Pui koeng lai lawm ka aux;
kix hoik awm sivai sang si pa ih ka nex pui yuh nan;
rhang kix awm pliah awm te;
dak kix lawm awm vaig pa kied lawm yuh nan heue.
 
5 Siyiex eue,
kieh pa ting pa lhaong maix jhi tix ka rao ma long;
kieh min plak ngai maix naog ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
 
6 Pui koeng tawm ka rub tix,
mai sang giex ka aux;
krax laux krax leeing hoik gut aux goh.
Kix kaong daux o ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
to kaux kix deeh cawd ka daux heue.
 
7 Siyiex eue,
rhawm rhi aux kaux cong,
mai aux ang yung yang;
aux sang rhax ka lai,
aux sang yaog yaw ka maix.
8 Rhawm rhi aux eue,
haing si duad heue!
Chiang eue,
ting eue,
haing si duad heue!
Aux kawx sang so singaix haing si duad heue!
9 Simiang aux eue,
aux sang yaog yaw maix ka meung ka piang haktiex;
aux sang yaog yaw maix ka daux kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik.
10 Krax muh maix pa koe leeing ju,
mai hoik lhaong khaing rao ma;
pa kaux sijax maix,
mai hoik cux ka piawm heue.
 
11 Siyiex eue,
kieh pa ting pa lhaong maix jhi tix ka rao ma long;
kieh min plak ngai maix naog ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
