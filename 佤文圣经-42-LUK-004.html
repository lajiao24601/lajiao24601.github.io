Ye-sux kham krax pa nyaw glaw
(Lai Ma-thaix 4.1–11; Lai Ma-kheu 1.12–13)
4
Ye-sux hoek khaing rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh.
Khuan Singax Singeh thung nawh hoek daux tiag,
2 nawh ot dee an been tipon singaix,
mai kham krax pa nyaw glaw Phi-caox.
Ka daux singaix an kix,
nawh ang ih ceu tid tix,
khaix hoik luan singaix an kix,
nawh hoik cai rhawm heue.
3 Phi-caox ah nin ka nawh:
“Phan maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex,
tawx lox kieh simaox in tieh tix lih mawh khao mun heue!”
4 aYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Krax pa im ju pui ang joeng im ka priex preem nong nong.’ ”
5 Phi-caox baox viex nawh hoek ka gawng lhaong tix mu,
mai kieh nawh yaox ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik ka daux tix khiep ngai,
ah nin ka nawh:
6-7 “Phan maix krub ka aux,
aux tag sang teei tix tawx akhuang in khawm uik,
mai sin ngao ka maix.
Mawh kheu in kix khawm uik hoik ap tix tawx ka aux,
aux muh tix tawx ka pui mawx aux tag sang tawx heue.”
8 bYe-sux ah nin:
“Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Tag krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix,
tag krub ka nawh khaing!’ ”
9 Phi-caox baox viex Ye-sux hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kieh nawh cung ka piang choek nyiex buan pa blawn lhaong,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Phan maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex,
taing maix lih khaing dee in heue.
10 cMawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Siyiex tag sang tawx lox nhie kawn cau tix kia ngien ka maix;’
11 dmai baox ah nin:
‘Kix tag sang bluh maix ka taix tix,
kieh caong maix ang yaox tix sideeing mat ka piang simaox.’ ”
12 eYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Ang pon tix nyaw glaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix heue.’ ”
13 Phi-caox hoik uik teei ka krax pa nyaw glaw ku ceu,
mai tom lih hu khaing Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux jauh tix sidiex ka Cia-li-lix
(Lai Ma-thaix 4.12–17; Lai Ma-kheu 1.14–15)
14 Ye-sux deeh ing ka Cia-li-lix;
riang rao Khuan Singax Singeh ot tau mai nawh.
Ceu kaux nawh hoik tauh hu ka baoh haktiex an.
15 Nawh sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex sidiex ku dauh,
mai pui hoik yaog yaw ka nawh khawm uik heue.
Pui ang rhawm yaox Ye-sux ka haktiex Na-sha-leux
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.53–58; Lai Ma-kheu 6.1–6)
16 Ye-sux hoek ka Na-sha-leux pa mawh haktiex pa hoik ot nawh yam mawh nawh kawn nyawm.
Ka daux singaix tuah,
nawh rien hoek ka daux nyiex sidiex pui Yo-thaix yuh nan jheeng jheeng.
Nawh kaoh cung tix sang phat ka phuk lai sigang khri,
17 koe pui teei tix tawx phuk lai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax ka nawh.
Nawh yah ka phuk lai an,
mai yaox ka pa tiiem tix ah nin tix dauh:
18 fkhuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang aux,
mawh kheu nawh hoik laug ka aux,
tag sang kieh aux sidiex gum lox pa gaux rhawm ka pui hot pui sibhawm.
Nawh ploi aux hu krai pao ah nin:
pui pa laung,
tag pon krax pa pluat ploi;
pui pa dug,
tag pon krax pa riang;
pui kham krax pa gut kheem,
pon krax pa luat luan;
19 Khaix an mai krai pao ka daux num buan son pa taoh Simiang Siyiex ka pui cie tix heue.
20 Ye-sux pua ka phuk lai an,
mai deeh tawx ka pui pa kuang nyiex sidiex,
khaix an nawh ngawm.
Pui hun khawm uik hoik dong ngai tix ka nawh,
21 nawh tom ah nin ka kix:
“Ka daux in singax,
gum lox baux phuk lai in pa mhong pex hoik lih tix mawh heue.”
22 Pui hun hoik kied doh keud ka nawh tete,
son gum lox pa krai nawh pa kieh rhawm pui hoik kied daox deng kah.
Kix ah nin:
“Pui in ang mawh kawn simex Yaw-seux lie?”
23 Ye-sux krai ka kix ah nin:
“Hoik mawh tete,
pex tag sang teei gum lox prim krai ka aux ah nin:
‘Cao sidah eue,
pai to kaux maix moi heue!’
Pex kawn sang baox krai ka aux ah nin:
‘Pa hoik mhong yix ka kaing pa yuh maix ka Cia-pai-nong,
maix kawx khaw tix yuh lih ka haktiex ot tix heue!’ ”
24 gYe-sux baox ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
cao jhai ang sax pon krax pa yung yi pui ka haktiex ot tix heue.
25 hNghiet heue,
ka daux bawg yam singaix baux ju Yi-li-yax,
ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex rug hoik koe miex mai tom nie.
Yam an,
tix neh loe num plak,
praix klawng kroh lhiex ang lih,
sibhawm ting lih ka daux haktiex ku dauh.
26 iViang mawh nan,
Yi-li-yax ang bax hoek ka pui tix kaux kawx ka daux kix,
mai hoek ngie ka miex mai tix kaux pa ot ka veng Sa-leu-fax haktiex Si-toeng dee an.
27 jKa daux bawg yam singaix baux ju Yi-li-sa,
ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex hoik koe jhawm saux si pawg tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
lheu khaing Nai-mang pui Shui-li-yax,
ang koe pui pa singax tix kaux kawx heue.”
28 Khaix hoik mhong ka lox in,
pui hun ka daux nyiex sidiex khawm uik hoik kied song rhawm kah.
29 Pui hun yaog tix kaoh,
mai bluh nawh lih plak praix veng,
viex nawh hoek ka yoeh rang (veng kix hoik sum ot ka piang gawng),
son sang cot nawh cud.
30 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux nied tix preeng tix lih hu khaing ka daux kix heue.
Pui pa laig phi pa seeing pa brai ka daux tix kaux
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.21–28)
31 Ye-sux hoek ka veng Cia-pai-nong haktiex Cia-li-lix.
Ka daux singaix tuah,
nawh sidiex gau pui pa ot dee an.
32 kPui pa mhong ka pa sidiex gau nawh kied daox deng kah,
mawh kheu hoik kied koe siawp sibrawm gum lox pa krai nawh.
33 Ka daux nyiex sidiex,
koe pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai daux tix kaux,
kok yo lox tom ting ah nin:
34 “Hai!
Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tuk tiak ka yix lie?
Sang hoek maix tix yuh yix laux leeing lie?
Aux hoik tawng mawh maix pui mawx;
maix mawh pui pa singax singeh cie Siyiex heue!”
35 Ye-sux ne raw ka phi pa seeing pa brai an ah nin:
“Paw nye,
nied lih khaing to kaux pui in heue!”
Phi pa seeing pa brai si blat pui an ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
khaix an tom lih khaing to kaux pui an,
mai ang bax kieh nawh mat pie ceeix kawx heue.
36 Pui hun kied daox deng kah,
mai ra lai krai ka paox tix ah nin:
“In mawh lox ceu pa tix lie?
Pui in koe akhuang mai riang rao tix kuang ka khuan pa seeing brai,
mai khuan pa seeing brai kawx hoik cu tix lih hu heue!”
37 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu kaux Ye-sux hoik tauh hu ka baoh haktiex an khawm uik heue.
Ye-sux pai pui tom nie moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.14–17; Lai Ma-kheu 1.29–34)
38 Ye-sux lih hu khaing nyiex sidiex,
mai hoek ka nyiex Si-meung.
Miex moeng Si-meung koe jhawm saux si aoh;
kix khiox khaw Ye-sux teem coi tix.
39 Ye-sux hu jhak ka nawh,
mai cung ka praok nawh,
ne raw ka jhawm saux si aoh an,
jhawm saux an hoik grai.
Nawh tom kaoh cung dum mawx dum an,
mai rieb ka kix heue.
40 Yam laig singaix khaing gawng,
pui pa koe jhawm saux ku ceu,
pui nyiex kix viex kix hoek ka Ye-sux;
Ye-sux deuh taix tix piang kix ku kaux,
mai pai kix moi.
41 Baox koe phi pa lih hu khaing to kaux pui tom nie,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex heue!”
Ye-sux ne raw ka kix ang cu kix si kah,
mawh kheu hoik tawng kix mawh nawh Khrit heue.
Ye-sux tawx lox sidiex ka daux nyiex buan
(Lai Ma-kheu 1.35–39)
42 Yam hoik riang praix,
Ye-sux lih hu khaing veng hoek ka haktiex dee riem.
Pui hun hu sawg nawh ku dauh ku dee,
yaox ka nawh,
mai keud tix sang kieh nawh ot,
ang kieh nawh lih hu.
43 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux kawx tag caw tix hu ka veng tang mu,
mai sidiex ka gum lox buan pa dex dawt mai siawp sibrawm Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik ploi aux hoek son sang yuh kaing in heue.”
44 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux hu ka Yo-thaix mai tawx lox buan ka nyiex sidiex ku dauh.
