Grawng keh Maw-si
2
Koe pui nyiex ma Li-we tix kaux,
teei tix moeng ka kawn bun paox ceu paox cad tix,
2 amai keh ka kawn simex tix kaux.
Yam yaox nawh mhawm grang kawn simex an,
nawh mox ka nawh loe khix.
3 Yam yaox nawh ang tix lai pon mox ka kawn nyawm in,
nawh tom teei kreeb pa taing ka priem tix mu,
kraix peeh khaox gix son ang rawm sang laig daux,
mai sau kawn nyawm daux kreeb,
khaix an,
deuh ot si nax pang priem ka praok rawm klawng.
4 Pux ke bun kawn nyawm in kok pau tix jhak dee singai,
son sang tawng dee si dauh sang lih tix mawh yuh ka mawx heue.
5 Kawn bun Vawng lih hoek heem tix daux rawm klawng;
bun siliex nawh khankix lai ling hawt praok klawng,
mai nawh yaox ka kreeb tix mu si nax pang priem,
mai nhie bun pa ot daux nyiex haw tix hu teei hoek.
6 Yam paoh nawh ka kreeb an,
tom yaox ka kawn nyawm iak tix kaux yiiem.
Tom caox rhawm ka kawn nyawm an,
ah nin:
“In rien tix mawh kawn nyawm pui Si-paw-lai tix kaux kaux heue.”
7 Singaix yam an,
pux ke bun kawn nyawm lih hoek,
ah nin ka kawn bun simiang Ai-cix:
“Sang kieh ang maix kieh aux hu sawg ka bun Si-paw-lai tix kaux kaux son sang tawx kawn nyawm in peux son maix lie?”
8 Kawn bun simiang Ai-cix ah nin:
“Hu heue!”
Kawn bun an tom hu kok ka miex kawn nyawm hoek.
9 Kawn bun simiang Ai-cix ah nin ka nawh:
“Teei kawn nyawm in hu,
eei son aux,
aux lawk tawx riang eei maix nawh heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kawn bun an rieb tix teei ka kawn nyawm mai viex ing eei ka nawh.
10 cKhaix hoik ting kawn nyawm in,
miex nawh viex nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai kawn bun simiang,
mai kawn bun simiang jauh tix ap mawh kawn simex tix.
Nawh ah nin:
“Mawh kheu teei aux nawh lih khaing daux rawm,
tom sum ceu kaux mawh Maw-si b heue!”
Maw-si to ka haktiex Mi-tiien
11 deYam hoik ting Maw-si,
nawh yie hu kia ka paox ceu paox cad tix pui Si-paw-lai khankix;
nawh yaox ka kaing cien pa yuh kix.
Koe tix bawg,
nawh yaox pui Ai-cix pauh ka pui Si-paw-lai tix kaux,
pa mawh paox ceu pui nawh.
12 Maw-si jhak plak goix plak dawm,
nawh keud pui ang yaox tix kaux kawx,
tom pauh pui Ai-cix yum mai si paung nawh daux mhaik.
13 Ngaix khaix,
Maw-si baox hoek tan,
mai yaox pui Si-paw-lai ra kaux simah lauh paox tix,
nawh tom ah nin ka pui pa lud an:
“Yuh ka mawh maix lai nu nai ka paox ceu paox cad tix lie?”
14 Pui an pawk ah nin:
“Mawx pa kieh maix yuh simiang mai cao prawng see yix lie?
Maix pauh ka pui Ai-cix an,
mai sang pauh aux yum nang lie?”
Yam mhong Maw-si ka lox in,
rhawm rhi nawh hoik lhat,
mai keud:
“Pui hoik tawng ka kaing ceu pa yuh aux tete heue.”
15-16 fYam mhong Vawng ka grawng in,
nawh sang pauh ka Maw-si yum,
Maw-si tom to hu ka haktiex Mi-tiien,
mai ot tan heue.
Koe tix singaix,
Maw-si ngawm praok rawm maw,
kawn bun cao giex kaing li Mi-tiien Ye-theu-law aliah kaux hoek tix kin rawm,
mai sau rawm naog daux dawng,
son sang tawx yung keeing kix nyaux.
17 Koe cao liang yung tix blah hoek,
grheu kix hu dee singai.
Maw-si kaoh cung mai teem ka kix,
mai baox teem kix tawx yung mai pex khankix nyaux rawm.
18 Yam deeh ing kix ka keeing tix tan,
keeing kix chawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai cao hoek in ngaix?”
19 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Koe pui Ai-cix tix kaux pa teem ka yix,
hoik grheu pui liang yung pa nu nai ka yix to,
mai baox teem yix kin rawm tawx yung nyaux heue.”
20 Keeing kix chawk ah nin ka kawn bun tix:
“Ot nawh dee mawx?
Yuh ka mawx pex lai prah nawh?
Hu kok nawh hoek sawm tau mai ex heue.”
21 Khaix an,
Maw-si cu tix ot tau mai kix dee an;
Ye-theu-law tom tawx kawn bun tix Si-phaw-la mawh moeng Maw-si.
22 Si-phaw-la keh kawn simex tix kaux.
Maw-si ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh nyo si nie daux haktiex pui tang ceu,
tom sum ceu kaux mawh Keu-soengg heue!”
23 Yam hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
simiang Ai-cix hoik yum.
Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ca kawn yuh kaing siliex cawng.
Kix khiox khaw Siyiex taoh teem tix luat luan khaing krax kha rhawm.
24 hSiyiex mhong ka lox ngia kix,
deeh doh keud ka gum lox sijax mukheu pa yuh nawh tau mai tax du kix Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux.
25 Siyiex yaox grawng kaing pa kha ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nawh kia ngien ka kix heue.
