Yie-li-mi mai cao giex kaing li Pa-see-hu-eux simah paox tix
20
Yam mhong kawn simex Ying-mai,
cao giex kaing li Pa-see-hu-eux cao kuang nyiex buan ka gum lox pa krai pao aux,
2 nawh lauh ka aux,
mai laung aux ka siviex Piien-ya-ming,
pa koe ka plak laong nyiex buan.
3 Yam hoek ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Pa-see-hu-eux ploi ka aux,
aux tom an nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex ang bax sum maix kaux Pa-see-hu-eux.
Ceu kaux pa sum nawh maix kaux mawh ‘krax lhat krax graung khawm pon daog’.
4 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Aux tag sang kieh to kaux maix mai paox grawm maix lhat graung kheu maix;
maix tag sang yaox grawng pa yum kix ka greem vaig gawn pui koeng.
Aux tag sang teei tix tawx pui Yo-tax khawm uik ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng;
nawh tag sang giex laung kix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng,
phan ang mawh nan pauh kix yum.
5 Aux tag sang kieh pui koeng tud rhim ka sin ngao pa koe ka daux veng,
tud ka kraung khrai mai kraung ting ngawh pa koe ka daux veng,
pie siang pa mox simiang Yo-tax kawx hoik tud hu.
Kix tag sang gah kraung khrai ku ceu hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng.
6 Phan mawh maix,
Pa-see-hu-eux,
pui tag sang giex laung maix mai pui nyiex ma maix khawm uik hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Maix tag sang yum ot dee an,
mai si paung ot dee an;
paox grawm maix pa yie mhong lox pie lhien maix khankix kawx,
tag sang si pup ka kaing pa awm paox tix yuh nin heue.’ ”
Yie-li-mi salao ka Simiang Siyiex
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik nak nawn ka aux;
aux hoik som ka lox maix.
Maix lheu ting riang khaing aux,
maix pon pex ka aux.
Pui hoik map nai ka aux khawm uik;
kix hoik kieh aux mawh pui pa nu nai kix leeing lang singaix heue.
 
8 Yam ang aux jheeh tix kux bawg,
aux hoik kok yo tom ting lox tix,
kok yo tom ting ah nin:
krax pa tuk tiak tuk te heue!
Krax pa laux pa leeing heue!
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux krai pao gum lox maix,
ca yaox krax pa si briem pui,
map nai pui.
9 Viang mawh nan,
aux ah nin:
aux ang lai baox krai lao ka Simiang Siyiex,
ang lai baox krai pao ka daux ceu kaux nawh,
gum lox maix hoik awm ngu pa cap ka daux rhawm rhi aux,
hoik laig ka daux gua si-ang aux.
Aux ang lai pon kham gum lox maix ka daux rhawm tix;
aux ang krai lih ang chix.
10 Aux mhong lox pa su shiep pui tom nie ah nin:
Gum lox pa pie lhien an “krax lhat krax graung khawm pon daog” hoik hoek heue,
ex hoek tix pu pliak ka nawh heue!
Pie paox grawm pa ting meed aux kawx hoik krox jun grawng sang laux min ceu kaux aux.
Kix ah nin:
kieh nawh klup heue,
ex tom tag giex ka nawh,
pawk bix ka nawh.
11 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix ot tau mai aux;
maix hoik ting riang ting rao,
koe siawp sibrawm pa lhat pui kah.
Pui pa gut kheem ka aux hoik laux leeing som nai khawm uik;
krax tieh vheen kix hoik jhi tix lih.
Kix tag sang kaig tua leeing ju leeing phan;
kawn pui ang chix piawm ka krax pa kaig pa tua nawh leeing ju.
12 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing eue,
maix hawt tix prawng ka daux pa rauh pa see;
maix hoik chix tawng sidawng ka rhawm rhi kawn pui.
Khiox khaw maix kieh aux yaox grawng pa pawk maix bix ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik tawx aux see pa prawng tix ka maix.
13 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Nawh hoik taoh ka pui pa gut kheem pui khaing daux tawm taix pui caox heue.
 
14 aKieh singaix keh aux pon krax jhax lawg heue!
Kieh pui piawm ka aux tom yam jauh aux tix lih khaing daux vaig miex tix heue!
15 Kieh pui pa krai keeing aux mhong lox gaux rhawm pon krax pa jhax lawg heue!
Nawh krai ka keeing aux ah nin:
mawh kawn simex,
maix hoik pon kawn simex tix kaux heue!
16 Kieh nawh awm veng pa hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex laux leeing yuh nan,
ang pon krax pa caox rhawm Simiang Siyiex.
Kieh nawh kaoh mhong lox pa yiiem pui cao cao,
greung ngaix mhong ka lox pa si num naing.
17 Mawh kheu nawh ang pauh aux yum sivoe sang lih aux ka daux vaig miex tix,
son sang kieh vaig miex aux mawh si meeig.
18 Yuh ka mawx aux lai lih hoek ka daux haktiex lie?
Khex mawh son sang kha rhawm tix,
laux rhawm laux rhi,
kieh ju aux grai ka daux pa kaig pa tua lie?
