Lai Ha-pa-kux
Krai dawt
Ka daux Lai Ha-pa-kux koe loe ceu lox yuh nin: ka daux tix ceu mawh gum lox pa si kah cao jhai tau mai Siyiex; ka daux ra ceu mawh gum lox pa krai cao jhai ka daux been; ka daux loe ceu mawh gum lox rhax pa chix tix rhax yam yuh kaing krub heue.
Sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num ka daux aliah bawg yam, jiet mawh bawg yam giex Pa-pi-loeng ka kaing, cao jhai Ha-pa-ku krai ka gum lox Siyiex. Nawh kied si duad rhawm kheu pa yuh pui Pa-pi-loeng kaing pa tud pa rhim, nawh tom krai lox pa ne raw. Nawh chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin: “Yam yuh kix ka pui pa lheu khaw caw khaing to kaux tix laux leeing, yuh ka mawx maix lai ang nye lie?” (1.13) Simiang Siyiex pawk ah nin, nawh tag sang yaog tix chuh ka daux bawg yam pa hoik een nawh mawh pa khaw caw; khaix an kawn baox ah nin: “Viang mawh nan, pui rauh pui see sivoe Simiang Siyiex tag sang pon tix im ju kheu krax pa kaux sijax tix heue.” (2.4)
Phuk lai in tang dauh tix blah grox krai grawng pa tag sang laux leeing pui ang rauh ang see. Dee si dauh, Ha-pa-kux teei lox rhax yaog yaw grawng pa ting pa lhaong Siyiex, mai jhi grawng pa kaux cong ang yung yang krax yum rhawm nawh heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pa salao Ha-pa-ku mai pa pawk Simiang Siyiex (1.1–2.4)
2. Pui ang rauh ang see si pup krax pa laux leeing (2.5–20)
3. Lox rawg buan Ha-pa-ku (3.1–19)
