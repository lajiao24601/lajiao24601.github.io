23
Pao-law dong ngai tix ka pui ka daux bang kum,
mai ah nin:
“Paox ceu paox cad khankix eue,
krax ot krax koe aux hoik mhawm,
mai hoik singax rhawm rhi plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
tom hoek yam in heue.”
2 Ya-na-ni-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting tawx lox nhie pui cau tix si mang ka jheeh Pao-law.
3 aPao-law krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh ding krai pui paing,
Siyiex tang sang lauh ka maix heue!
Maix ngawm dee an hoik mawh son sang teei lox tat jhawm prawng ka aux,
kheu nan maix lai lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
tawx lox nhie kix lauh ka aux heue!”
4 Pui pa cung ka praok kix ah nin:
“Maix hoik nieb pon map nai ka cao giex kaing li pa ting cie Siyiex heue!”
5 bPao-law ah nin:
“Paox ceu paox cad khankix eue,
aux ang tawng nawh mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting;
phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ah nin:
‘Ang pon tix map nai ka simiang pui khankix heue.’ ”
6 cPao-law hoik tawng ka daux cub pui hun in kix,
tix blah mawh cub caw Sa-tu-kai,
tix blah mawh cub caw pui Fa-li-saix.
Mawh kheu nan nawh kok yo lox tom ting ka daux bang kum ah nin:
“Paox ceu paox cad khankix eue,
aux mawh pui Fa-li-saix tix kaux,
mawh kawn simex pui Fa-li-saix.
Daux in singaix aux kham krax pa prawng hoik mawh kheu simie aux pui yum deeh kaoh im heue!”
7 Yam krai nawh lox in lih khaing jheeh tix,
pui Fa-li-saix mai pui Sa-tu-kai simah ka paox tix,
pui hun kawx guah tix lih koe ra cub.
8 dMawh kheu ang pui Sa-tu-kai yum rhawm ka grawng pa deeh kaoh im,
mai ang yum rhawm ka pa koe kawn cau Siyiex mai khuan Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
pui Fa-li-saix hoik yum rhawm ka ceu in khawm loe ceu.
9 Lox simah nied ot nied ting,
koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah mai sara pa tawng ka phuk lai sigang khri kaoh cung,
mai sau riang tix tieb tie ah nin:
“Yix ang yaox dee lud pui in tix ceu kawx heue!
Tee rug mawh khuan Siyiex mai kawn cau Siyiex pa si kah ka nawh heue!”
10 Krax pa simah kix nied ot nied ting,
simiang lien lhat kix riah ka Pao-law,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tawx lox nhie lien lih teei Pao-law lih khaing si nax pui hun,
mai viex nawh laig daux nyiex bang heue.
11 Ka daux bo an,
Simiang Ye-sux cung ka praok Pao-law ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix koe rhawm kua heue!
Maix hoik kham sakse son aux ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka daux veng Law-ma kawx maix tag caw tix kham sakse in heue.”
Krax keud tix pauh ka Sao-law
12 Ka daux ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
pui Yo-thaix rom tix yuh krax tieh vheen tau,
mai tiex tu phan ang pauh ka Pao-law kix ang sawm ang rheep.
13 Pui pa kum paox tix koe tix pon kaux haoh.
14 Kix hu ka simiang cao giex kaing li mai ka kuad song khankix,
mai ah nin:
“Yix hoik tiex du yuh nin:
phan ang pauh Pao-law yix ang sawm ang rheep heue!
15 Yam in,
pex mai pui ka daux bang kum caw tix khiox khaw simiang naing Law-ma,
viex Pao-law lih hoek dee in,
pex yuh tix sang si kaw ka kaing pa yuh nawh;
yix hoik tawng grawng sang yuh tix ka mawh,
mai pauh nawh yum sivoe sang hoek nawh heue.”
16 Kawn pha Pao-law mhong lox pa tieh vheen in,
nawh tom laig daux nyiex bang krai ka Pao-law.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law kok pui naing hoek tix kaux,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix viex pui nhum ju in hu ka simiang naing;
nawh koe kaing ceu pa sang krai ka nawh heue.”
18 Pui naing an tom viex pui pa nhum ju an hoek ka simiang naing,
mai ah nin:
“Pao-law pui laung an kok aux hu ka tix,
nhie aux viex pui pa nhum ju in hoek ka maix,
nawh koe kaing ceu pa sang krai ka maix heue.”
19 Simiang naing an reu taix nawh hu,
mai viex nawh hu tang dauh,
chawk nawh ah nin:
“Koe lox krai maix ceu pa tix?”
20 Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Pui Yo-thaix hoik kum kawng paox tix,
pa sax kix sang mhaing tix viex Pao-law hoek ka daux bang kum,
mai yuh tix sang si kaw ka kaing pa yuh nawn.
21 Khiox khaw maix paw vi cu lox kix,
mawh kheu hoik koe pui tix pon kaux pa kuan tix krox jun ka nawh.
Kix hoik tiex tu,
phan ang pauh Pao-law,
kix ang sawm ang rheep.
Yam in kix hoik rien khre tix,
mai krox jun ngie ka lox tat maix khaing heue.”
22 Simiang naing an tawx lox ka nawh ah nin:
“Paw vi kieh pui tawng grawng pa hoik krai maix kaing in ka aux”,
khaix an nhie nawh deeh ing heue.
Ap tix tawx Pao-law ka simiang Fe-li-see
23 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang naing kok pui naing hoek ra kaux,
tawx lox ah nin:
“Rien khre pui naing hu ka caong tix ra yieh kaux,
pui naing brug brung atigliah kaux,
pui pa chix siao pliah ra yieh kaux,
ka daux bo in bawg yam dim taix nari lih hu plak Khai-sha-li-yax.
24 Khaix an baox rien khre brung pa sang brug Pao-law nang,
hu tawx ka simiang Fe-li-see dee an.”
25 Simiang naing an baox tiiem lai ah nin:
26 “Kheu-lao-ti Lui-si-yax tiiem lai tawx ka simiang Fe-li-see pa ting min,
kieh maix nyawm ot nyawm koe yuh nin:
27 Pui Yo-thaix hoik giex ka pui in,
kix sang pauh ka nawh,
aux tawng hoik mawh nawh pui Law-ma,
aux tom viex pui naing hu taoh ka nawh.
28 Aux sang si kaw ka grawng pa deuh kix phi map piang nawh,
mawh kheu nan,
tom viex nawh hoek ka daux bang kum kix.
29 Aux ang yaox grawng pa khaw nawh tix yum,
mai ka phi map pa khaw tix laung ka nawh;
grawng pa deuh kix phi map piang nawh,
mawh ngie son pa krai nawh ka grawng lox tat jhawm prawng khaing.
30 Khaix an koe pui hoek tix krai ka aux,
pui Yo-thaix keud tix sang pauh ka nawh,
aux tom nied but rhawm tix yah nawh hu ka maix dee an,
khaix an mai baox tawx lox nhie pui pa krai an hoek tix krai lao ka plak sivoe ngai maix heue.”
31 Pui naing khankix hawt tix yuh awm gum lox tawm,
teei viex ka Pao-law hu,
mai hu leeing sawm ka plak Ang-thi-pha-ti.
32 Ka daux singaix khaix,
pui naing pa hu ka caong tix deeh ing ka nyiex bang,
mai kieh pui naing pa brug brung hu tawx Pao-law.
33 Kix hoek ka Khai-sha-li-yax,
tawx lai an ka simiang,
mai baox tawx Pao-law ka nawh.
34 Simiang an phat ka lai an,
khaix an chawk Pao-law mawh nawh pui haktiex mawx,
mai tawng mawh nawh pui haktiex Ci-li-cia,
35 tom ah nin:
“Krox jun pui pa deuh phi map an ka maix hoek,
aux tom taug prawng ka see cie maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tawx lox nhie kix gang Pao-law ot daux nyiex cao kuang kaing Si-luix heue.
