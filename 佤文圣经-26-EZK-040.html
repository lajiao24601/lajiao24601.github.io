Riang su sai nyiex buan pa khraox
(40.1–48.35)
Pui viex Yi-si-ciex hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
40
Yam vuk vied vuk viang yix ka daux tix nga phuan num,
pa mawh ka daux bawg yam pa hoik laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung kao pon num haok khix tix kao ngaix,
siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang to kaux aux,
mai nawh viex aux hu.
2 bKa daux riang su sai an,
Simiang Siyiex viex aux hu ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex,
mai deuh aux ot ka piang gawng lhaong tix mu.
Aux yaox ka plak sivoe ngai tixa awm pa mawh veng pa hoik sum pui tix mu yuh nan.
3 cNawh viex aux hoek dee an;
aux yaox pui tix kaux hoik sawng riang awm tawng yuh nan,
ka daux tawm taix nawh giex ka maox keei sum mai khaox diag,
mai cung ka diang siviex heue.
4 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw bre tix jhak mhawm mhawm,
bre rhawm tix nghiet,
bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux ka maix,
mawh kheu in hoik mawh grawng pa viex aux maix hu dee in.
Maix tag caw tix krai lao ka can pa hoik yaox tix khawm uik ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
Siviex plak lih singaix
5 dPa yaox aux hoik mawh nyiex buan,
mai vung viang mawh guh ding.
Ka daux tawm taix pui an giex ka khaox diag pa koe loe kongjhee tix gawng,
mai diag tix yaox ka guh ding.
Guh ding krax lhaong koe loe kongjhee,
been bu koe loe kongjhee.
6 Khaix an,
nawh hu ka daux doh siviex pa veeh tix ka plak lih singaix,
haok ka glawk bawng diang siviex,
mai tiag tix yaox diang siviex plak praix ka piang choek glawk bawng,e
krax raux koe loe kongjhee.
7 Luan khaing diang siviex an koe krax phlat kah;
krax an ka praok khawm ra plak lawg nyiex pui pa pau hoik tang koe ka tix plak loe lawg.
Lawg nyiex pui pa pau an ku lawg koe loe kongjhee awm paox tix;
lawg nyiex an mawh ding pa khan si nax,
been bu koe ra kongjhee plak.
Luan khaing nyiex pui pa pau in kix,
mai baox koe diang siviex plak daux tag mu krax raux koe loe kongjhee,
mai phlat hoek ka lawg grah tix mu;
grah in veeh tix plak nyiex buan.
8-9 Nawh tiag tix yaox ka grah in,
krax raux koe pon kongjhee.
Grah in mawh pa ot ka krax ka daog,
mai dex ka nyiex buan plak an;
rong guh ding pa koe ka krax an khawm ra plak,
been bu koe tix kongjhee.
10 Krax an khawm ra plak lawg nyiex pui pa pau hoik tang koe ka tix plak loe lawg,
been ting iak khaw paox,
been bu khan paox tix kawx hoik khaw paox nang heue.
11 Pui an tiag tix yaox ka dee laig ka daux doh diang siviex,
diang siviex krax vieh koe liah kongjhee plak,
krax an vieh koe phuan kongjhee.
12 Ka plak sivoe lawg nyiex pui pa pau ku mu,
ding pa tiiem hoik koe ka khawm uik mawh nin:
krax lhaong koe tix phuan kongfeeng,
been bu koe tix phuan kongfeeng,
lawg nyiex an hoik mawh loe kongjhee khaw paox khawm uik.
13 Nawh baox tiag tix yaox ka guh ding plak praix nyiex pui pa pau,
been khan guh ding plak praix paox tix,
koe kao ra kongjhee plak.
14 Grah pa koe ka daog paox hu ka brawk;
nawh tiag tix yaox ka grah,
krax vieh koe kao kongjhee.
15 Krax an,
been lang jauh ka diang siviex ting plak in hoek ka guh ding grah plak khaix koe tix nga phuan kongjhee.
16 Guh ding nyiex pui pa pau plak praix,
daux doh iak hoik koe ka khawm uik,
ka si nax rong guh ding kawx daux doh iak hoik koe kah,
vung viang grah kawx daux doh iak hoik koe ka khawm uik.
Rong guh ding pa koe ka praok krax khawm ra plak hoik saik riang khaox jhie yaong ka heue.
Brawk plak praix
17 Pui an thung aux hoek ka brawk plak praix.
Vung viang plak praix brawk koe lawg nyiex ka tix ngoe mu pa dex ka guh ding plak praix,
sivoe lawg nyiex koe krax pa hoik tawm ka simaox,
18 krax pa hoik tawm tix ka simaox in vung viang brawk plak praix.
Brawk plak praix tiiem khaing brawk plak daux heue.
19 Brawk plak daux koe siviex ka tix mu,
mai kawn lhaong khaing siviex brawk plak praix tix glawk;
siviex in pa siao yam laig ka brawk plak praix phlat hoek ka brawk plak daux.
Pui an tiag tix yaox ka si nax siviex in kiex ra mu been khan paox tix,
krax lang koe tix phuan kongjhee heue.
Siviex plak laong
20 Pui an tiag tix yaox ka daux doh siviex pa phlat hu plak brawk plak praix pa veeh tix ka plak laong.
21 Ka krax an khawm ra plak lawg nyiex pui pa pau hoik koe ka tix plak loe lawg.
Rong guh ding khawm ra plak mai khaox diag grah,
hoik khaw mai siviex plak lih singaix yuh nan,
krax lang koe tix nga phuan kongjhee,
krax vieh koe kao ra kongjhee plak.
22 Grah,
daux doh,
mai rong guh ding pa saik ka khaox jhie yaong,
hoik khaw mai siviex plak lih singaix yuh nan.
Dee laig ka krax an koe glawk bawng ka aliah glawk pa haok ka siviex plak laong,
krax pa ot ka daog paox koe grah ka tix mu,
mai veeh tix ka plak brawk plak praix.
23 Siviex in khan ka brawk plak praix,
veeh tix plak siviex tang mu,
phlat hu plak brawk plak daux.
Pui an tiag tix yaox been khan si nax siviex in kiex paox tix,
krax lang koe tix phuan kongjhee heue.
Siviex plak seh
24 Pui an thung aux hu ka plak seh;
yix yaox daux doh pa veeh tix plak seh.
Nawh tiag tix yaox ka siviex in;
khaox diag pa tiag ka lawg nyiex pui pa pau,
ka rong guh ding,
mai ka grah,
hoik khaw mai siviex tang mu yuh nan.
25 Daux doh siviex in mai vung viang grah khawm pon daog hoik koe ka daux doh iak khawm uik.
Krax an been lang koe tix nga phuan kongjhee,
krax vieh koe kao ra kongjhee plak.
26 Dee laig ka krax an glawk bawng koe ka aliah glawk pa haok ka siviex plak seh,
krax pa ot ka daog paox grah koe ka tix mu,
mai veeh tix ka plak brawk plak praix.
Ka praok khawm ra plak rong guh ding koe kah,
mai saik riang khaox jhie yaong koe kah.
27 Siviex in veeh tix ka plak siviex pa phat hu ka brawk plak daux.
Pui an tiag tix yaox been khan si nax siviex in kiex paox tix,
krax lang koe tix phuan kongjhee heue.
Siviex brawk plak daux ka plak seh
28 Pui an thung aux laig ka brawk plak daux ka siviex pa koe plak seh.
Nawh tiag tix yaox ka siviex plak seh ka brawk plak daux,
been ting iak khaw mai brawk plak praix yuh nan.
29-30 Dee in kawx koe lawg nyiex pui pa pau,
grah,
mai rong guh ding,
been ting iak hoik khaw mai siviex tang mu yuh nan.
Daux doh siviex mai vung viang grah khawm pon daog,
dauh doh iak hoik koe ka khawm uik.
Krax an been lang koe tix nga phuan kongjhee,
krax vieh koe kao ra kongjhee plak.
31 Grah veeh tix plak brawk plak praix;
ka piang guh ding pa koe ka praok krax khawm ra plak hoik saik riang khaox jhie yaong koe kah.
Glawk bawng pa haok ka siviex in koe daix glawk heue.
Siviex brawk plak daux ka plak lih singaix
32 Pui an thung aux laig ka brawk plak daux ka siviex pa koe plak lih singaix.
Nawh tiag tix yaox ka siviex plak lih singaix ka brawk plak daux,
been ting iak khaw mai brawk tang dauh yuh nan.
33 Dee in kawx koe lawg nyiex pui pa pau,
grah,
mai rong guh ding,
been ting iak hoik khaw mai siviex tang mu yuh nan.
Daux doh siviex mai vung viang grah khawm pon daog,
dauh doh iak hoik koe ka khawm uik.
Krax an been lang koe tix nga phuan kongjhee,
krax vieh koe kao ra kongjhee plak.
34 Grah veeh tix plak brawk plak praix;
ka piang guh ding pa koe ka praok krax khawm ra plak hoik saik riang khaox jhie yaong koe ka khawm uik.
Glawk bawng pa haok ka siviex in koe daix glawk heue.
Siviex brawk plak daux ka plak laong
35 Pui an thung aux laig ka brawk plak daux ka siviex pa koe plak laong.
Nawh tiag tix yaox ka siviex plak laong ka brawk plak daux,
been ting iak khaw mai brawk plak praix yuh nan.
36 Dee in kawx koe lawg nyiex pui pa pau,
grah,
mai rong guh ding,
been ting iak hoik khaw mai siviex tang mu yuh nan.
Daux doh siviex mai vung viang grah khawm pon daog,
dauh doh iak hoik koe ka khawm uik.
Krax an been lang koe tix nga phuan kongjhee,
krax vieh koe kao ra kongjhee plak.
37 Grah veeh tix plak brawk plak praix;
ka piang guh ding pa koe ka praok krax khawm ra plak hoik saik riang khaox jhie yaong koe ka khawm uik.
Glawk bawng pa haok ka siviex in koe daix glawk heue.
Nyiex khre krax tan
38 Ka brawk plak praix koe nyiex khre krax tan ka tix lawg,
mai kleuk tix ka grah daux doh siviex ka plak laong,
pa phlat hu ka brawk plak daux,
mawh pa siao son sang si ghrah ka khawng tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
39 Ka grah plak daux deuh pheen koe ka pon mu,
tix plak ra mu,
pheen in kix mawh pa siao son sang pauh ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map,
mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud.
40 Ka dee laig ka siviex plak laong plak praix grah,
koe pheen ka pon mu,
tix plak ra mu.
41 Yuh nin,
khawm uik tom koe pheen daix mu pa siao son sang pauh Krax-tan Khawng-brie kah,
ka grah plak daux pon mu,
plak praix pon mu.
42 Pheen pa koe ka grah plak daux pon mu mawh pa siao son sang pauh ka khawng tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
Pheen in kix hoik mawh pa saik tix ka simaox,
krax lhaong koe tix phuan kongfeeng,
been vieh koe atigliah phuan kongfeeng awm paox tix.
Kraung khrai pa siao son sang pauh ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
hoik deuh ot ka piang pheen khawm uik.
43 Ka daog pheen khawm pon daog koe daux dawng atigliah kongfeeng plak.
Nex pa siao son krax tan lu tag sang deuh ot ka piang pheen khawm uik heue.
44 Pui an thung aux hu ka brawk plak daux.
Ka brawk plak daux koe lawg nyiex ka ra lawg,
ot ka praok siviex plak laong tix lawg,
mai veeh tix plak seh;
ot ka praok siviex plak seh tix lawg,
mai veeh tix plak laong.
45 Pui an krai ka aux,
lawg nyiex pa veeh tix plak seh mawh pa tawx ka cao giex kaing li yam yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex buan,
46 lawg nyiex pa veeh tix plak laong mawh pa tawx ka cao giex kaing li yam yuh kaing krub ka sivoe khu dee tan.
Can cao giex kaing li hoik mawh doeh ceu cad Sa-tu.
Ka daux ceu pui Li-we,
mawh ngie kix pa chix tix hoek yuh kaing krub ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex khaing heue.
Brawk plak daux mai nyiex buan
47 Pui an tiag tix yaox ka brawk plak daux koe tix phuan kongjhee awm paox tix,
nyiex buan sum tix ot ka plak klip singaix,
sivoe nyiex buan khu dee tan koe ka tix mu.
48 Khaix an,
nawh thung aux hu ka plak sivoe nyiex buan.
Nawh tiag tix yaox ka plak sivoe nyiex buan,
krax raux koe ra kongjhee plak,
krax vieh koe aliah kongjhee,f
guh ding been bu tix plak koe tix kongjhee plak.
49 Glawk bawng pa koe sivoe nyiex buan krax vieh koe kao kongjhee,
been raux koe liah kongjhee.
Ka praok sivoe nyiex buan khawm ra plak,
rong hoik koe ka tix plak tix gawng heue.
