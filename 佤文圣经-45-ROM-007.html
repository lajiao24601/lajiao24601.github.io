Teei grawng mex moeng tiag kah
7
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex hoik mawh pui pa tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng khawm uik,
mawh kheu nan,
pex tee rien tix tawng ka lox pa sang krai aux yuh nin:
grawng pa ot kawn pui ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai koe ceu ngie yam im tix khaing.
2 Phan tiag tix yaox,
bun pa hoik koe mex tix kaux,
ca mawh kawn im mex nawh,
in tag mawh pa ot greem lox tat jhawm prawng;
phan mex nawh hoik yum,
nawh tag ang lai ot ka daux pa giex gang lox tat jhawm prawng in.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
yam kawn im mex nawh,
phan nawh ot tau mai simex tang kaux,
nawh tag sang mawh bun pa phit vao;
phan mex nawh hoik yum,
nawh tag mawh pui pa luat luan khaing lox tat jhawm prawng,
phan nawh baox mex ka simex tang kaux,
mai ang lai sawn mawh krax pa phit vao.
4 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
grawh grawng pex kawx hoik awm an.
Phan krai ka plak lox tat jhawm prawng,
pex hoik yum,
kheu hoik mawh pex to kaux Khrit tix blah;
yam in pex hoik mawh cie Simiang Ye-sux kaw pa hoik kaoh im khaing krax yum,
mai hoik kieh ex pon yuh kaing mhawm mhawm son Siyiex.
5 Yam kawn im ex ka daux alo kawn pui,
lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si hoik kieh lih ka alo nex nham phi map ex,
mai kieh nex nham ex hu yuh,
dee si dauh mawh krax yum.
6 Viang mawh nan,
yam in lox tat jhawm prawng ang lai pon giex gang ka ex;
mawh kheu phan ex krai ka plak lox tat jhawm prawng pa giex gang ka ex,
ex hoik yum heue.
Ex ang lai baox hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng awm been pa prim yuh nan,
mai hawt ka krax pa khraox,
pa jhi Khuan Singax Singeh ex hoek tix yuh ka kaing jhaw Siyiex heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng mai phi map
7 aPhan mawh nan,
ex chix tix krai mawh lox tat jhawm prawng phi map lie?
Ang doh chix heue!
Kheu nan,
ex hoik tawng phi map mawh ceu tix ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng.
Phan ang mawh lox tat jhawm prawng pa krai ah nin:
“Ang pon tix koe rhawm ghoing”,
aux tag ang tawng rhawm ghoing mawh ceu tix.
8 Phi map ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng,
sawg bawg yam pa khaw,
mai kieh rhawm rhi aux keud lih ka alo nex nham ku ceu.
Phan ang koe lox tat jhawm prawng,
phi map tag ang koe bawg yam tix laig.
9 Sivoe an aux ang ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng;
khaix an hoik koe ka lox tawx lox tat jhawm prawng,
phi map tom im jang luh tix,
10 dee si dauh aux yum ka daux phi map.
Lox tawx lox tat jhawm prawng hoik dawg mawh pa kieh kawn pui pon tix im ju;
viang mawh nan,
ka son aux,
mai hoik tieh tix mawh pa viex krax yum hoek.
11 bMawh kheu phi map ot ka daux lox tawx lox tat jhawm prawng,
sawg ka bawg yam pa khaw tix pie lhien ka aux,
mai kieh aux yum nang heue.
12 Lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
hoik mawh pa singax singeh;
lox tat jhawm prawng mawh pa singax singeh,
pa rauh see,
mai pa mhawm pa mhiem.
13 Phan krai yuh nin,
ceu pa mhawm an sang kieh aux yum lie?
Ang doh mawh heue!
Mawh phi map heue!
Mawh ngie phi map pa ot ka daux pa mhawm an,
tom viex krax yum hoek,
son sang nie kieh mu phi map jhi tix sidawng.
Ka daux lox tawx lox tat jhawm prawng,
krax pa caox phi map tom nie lih tix kin heue.
Grawng pa guah son to kaux tix
14 Ex hoik tawng,
lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si mawh pa dex mai khuan;
viang mawh nan,
aux mawh pui pa laux leeing,
mai hoik coh tix tawx mawh siliex cawng phi map.
15 cAux lai kawn ang tawng ka kaing pa yuh tix;
mawh kheu ceu pa simie aux tix yuh,
aux tieh tix ang hu yuh;
ceu pa koeng aux,
aux tieh tix hu yuh.
16 Phan aux hu yuh ka kaing ceu pa ang aux simie tix yuh,
aux tag ang doh ang krai khaw caw lox tat jhawm prawng.
17 Kheu hoik mawh nan,
ceu pa hoik yuh aux ang bax mawh aux pa yuh tete,
mai hoik mawh phi map pa ot daux aux pa yuh.
18 Aux kawx hoik tawng,
ceu pa ot ka daux aux,
pa mawh pa ot ka daux alo to kaux aux,
ang koe ceu pa mhawm.
Mawh kheu koe aux rhawm pa mhawm pa simie tix yuh,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe riang tix yuh.
19 Kaing mhawm pa simie aux tix yuh,
aux tieh tix ang hu yuh;
ceu pa ang aux simie tix yuh,
aux tieh tix hu yuh.
20 Phan aux yuh ka ceu pa ang simie tix yuh,
in hoik jhi grawng ang mawh aux pa yuh,
mai hoik mawh phi map pa ot ka daux aux pa yuh heue.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
aux yaox ka been tix ceu pa yuh kaing ceu pa ang mhawm yuh nin:
yam simie aux tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
pa caox ca kawn hawt tix breuk ka aux.
22 Ka daux rhawm rhi aux hoik dawg muh ka been cie Siyiex,
23 viang mawh nan,
to kaux aux cu nghiet ka been tang mu tix mu,
been in yuh naing ka been pa muh aux,
pa koe ka daux rhawm aux,
mai kieh aux ang pon bheeik luan khaing been phi map pa phrawk ka aux an.
Been in yuh kaing ceu pa ang mhawm ka daux to kaux aux.
24 Aux kied kha rhawm tix heue!
Pui mawx sang pon taoh aux bheeik luan khaing pa kieh to kaux tix yum lie?
25 Ting buan son Siyiex,
ka daux ceu kaux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit,
nawh hoik pon taoh ka aux heue.
Grawh grawng aux hoik mawh yuh nin:
aux cu nghiet ngie been cie Siyiex ka daux khuan rhawm rhi tix khaing,
viang mawh nan,
alo nex nham aux cu nghiet ka been cie phi map heue.
