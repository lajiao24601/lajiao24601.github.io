2
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag caw tix ah nin ka pux aik tix:
“Pex mawh pui cie Siyiex”;
ah nin ka pux o tix:
“Pex mawh pa kied muh Siyiex heue.”
Keu-mix pa seeing brai pa mawh pui Yi-seu-liex pa lieh lox
2 Kawn simex kawn bun aux eue!
Viang miex pex ang lai mawh moeng aux,
aux ang lai mawh mex nawh,
viang mawh nan pex tag caw tix khiox khaw yiiem son nawh,
nhie nawh teei tix tik ka pak peung krax phit pa koe ka ngai nawh an,
mai paw lai kieh si gleem naok tix lih nyaw ka pui.
3 Phan nawh ang nghiet,
aux tag sang peeik kraung nawh ghle,
kieh nawh ot ka kaong,
awm kawn ngie pa taug sang lih khaing daux vaig miex tix yuh nan.
Aux tag sang kieh nawh awm daux tiag daux tu,
awm pak mhaik,
mai kieh nawh yum ka simie tix nyaux rawm.
4 Aux ang muh ka kawn simex kawn bun nawh,
kheu mawh kix pa keh khaing bun pa phit vao.
5 Miex kix yuh krax phit vao,
mawh bun coh kaux pa ang koe krax kaig tuah.a
Nawh ah nin ka to kaux tix:
“Aux tag sang hu deuh rhawm tix ka khun rhawm muh tix;
kheu priex preem,
rawm tie,
hauk yung,
kraung ceep,
bee sime,
mai plai nyawm cie aux hoik mawh pa tawx kix khawm uik heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang teei pang kat yuh die mai kieh mawh pa thox ka krax hu nawh;
aux tag sang teei simaox yuh guh veng kah,
mai kieh nawh ang yaox krax iak pa hu tix rieb ka khun rhawm muh tix.
7 Nawh tag sang leue ka khun rhawm muh tix,
viang mawh nan ang lai krup ka kix;
nawh hu sawg kix ku dauh ku dee,
viang mawh nan ang yaox.
Mawh kheu nan nawh ah nin:
“Aux tag sang deeh ing ka mex tix sivoe an khaing;
kaing pa koe sivoe an kawn lheu mhawm khaing yam in heue.”
8 Nawh ang doh keud ka pa tawx nawh koe nghox koi,
plai nyawm,
mai bee sime mawh aux;
tete te lai,
khawm mau khri pa hoik tawx nawh ka siyiex Pa-lix kawx hoik mawh pa tawx aux.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
hoek bawg yam vawg nghox,
aux tag sang deeh teei ka nghox koi,
plai nyawm,
pa hoik tawx aux ka nawh,
mai ka hauk yung,
ka kraung ceep pa hoik tawx aux nawh lhop to kaux tix.
10 Aux tag sang peeik kraung nawh ghle ka plak sivoe ngai khun rhawm muh nawh;
ang koe pui pa pon kieh nawh luat luan khaing daux tawm taix aux.
11 Aux tag sang thox ka krax pa gaux rhawm nawh khawm uik,
ang lai kieh nawh yuh poi,
krub khix,
tuah singaix,
mai poi ting ka daux been li tang ceu.
12 Aux tag sang kid ka khaox plix siiet mai ka khaox ruh nawh,
kheu hoik krai nawh in kix mawh pa tawx khun rhawm muh nawh ka nawh.
Aux tag sang kieh si peem khaox plix nawh lih mawh daux tiag khaox;
to tiag tag sang hoek tix dug dig.
13 Aux tag sang sawg tix jhax lawk ka nawh;
mawh kheu gleeing nawh nyawng ka Pa-lix daux poi,
teei siang kraung ngawh jawg to kaux tix kah,
leue ka khun rhawm muh tix,
mai piawm ka aux.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Simiang Siyiex muh ka pui cie tix
14 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang viex nawh hoek ka daux tiag daux tu,
kieh nawh nyawm rhawm,
kieh nawh deeh cu ka lox tix.
15 bAux tag sang deeh tawx si peem plix siiet ka nawh,
mai kieh “ngawd krax laux leeing” deeh lih mawh “siviex krax keud pot jun pot”;
ka dee an,
nawh tag sang deeh pon ka krax muh aux,
awm yam nhum ju tix,
yam bheeik luan tix khaing Ai-cix yuh nan.
16 Yam an,
nawh tag sang deeh doh keud ka mawh aux mex tix,
ang lai kieh aux mawh Pa-lix.c
17 Aux tag sang kieh nawh piawm ka Pa-lix,
mai ang lai baox krai ka ceu kaux nawh heue.
18 Yam an,
aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai sim saik,
to sat,
viag vua,
mai kieh kix ang lai mat yuh pui cie aux.
Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka ak puh te,
vaig gawn,
mai ka kraung naing pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
kieh pui cie tix pon krax nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.
19 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue!
Aux tag sang teei tix moeng ka maix;
aux tag sang tawx krax muh krax rhawm mai krax rauh krax see tix ka maix,
kia ngien maix ka daux krax muh pa ang chix tieh chix loh tix,
kieh maix mawh cie tix leeing ju heue.
 
20 Aux tag sang teei maix mawh moeng tix ka daux krax pa kaux sijax,
maix tom taug tawng aux mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.
21-22 Yam an,
aux tag sang nghiet ka lox rawg pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex buan;
Aux tag sang kieh rawm lie caux ka haktiex,
haktiex tom taug chix lih kuah nghox koi,
plix siiet,
sime heue.
23 dAux tag sang sum pui cie tix ot ka piang haktiex in;
aux tag sang tawx krax pa caox rhawm tix lih hoek ka piang pui “pa ang pon krax pa caox rhawm” khankix;
Aux tag sang ah nin ka pui “pa ang mawh pui cie aux” khankix:
pex hoik mawh pui cie aux.
Kix tag sang rom tix pawk ah nin:
maix hoik mawh Siyiex yix heue.
