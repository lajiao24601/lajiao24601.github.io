Yie-lu-sa-leung laux leeing
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 24.18–25.7)
52
Yam koe Si-ti-cia tix nga tix num nawh mawh simiang Yo-tax,
mai hoik giex ka kaing piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung kao tix num.
Miex nawh kaux Ha-maw-tha,
mawh pui veng Li-na kawn bun Ye-li-mi.
2 Simiang Si-ti-cia yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
awm kaing pa yuh Yaw-ya-cing yuh nan.
3 Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka pui Yo-tax,
mai grheu kix hu khaing sivoe ngai tix heue.
Si-ti-cia tieh vheen ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa mawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
4 amawh kheu nin,
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha thung pui naing tix hu puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
yam an mawh bawg yam giex Si-ti-cia kaing ka daux dim num khix kao kao ngaix.
Kix sum bang ot ka plak praix veng,
mai lai lawm puing ka guh tab veng,
5 mai hoik lai lawm ka tom hoek giex Si-ti-cia simiang daux kao tix num.
6 Num an khix pon dim singaix,
sibhawm praix kied kin ka daux veng,
kawn pui ang lai koe priex preem,
7 bpui hoik pon yieh ka guh veng.
Viang pui naing Pa-pi-loeng kawn lai lawm ka veng,
simiang mai pui naing pa pau khawm uik hoik tang yaog tix to pon sawm.
Kix to mox tix ka krax dex si peem nyiex haw simiang an,
luan ka siviex pa ot guh veng khawm ra plak,
hu plak ngawd Yaw-tang.
8 Viang mawh nan,
pui naing Pa-pi-loeng leue ka simiang Si-ti-cia,
mai krup nawh ka dee lung pa dex ka veng Yie-li-keu;
pui naing nawh hoik hu khaing nawh,
hoik tang to pru phrat tix.
9 Kix pon giex ka nawh,
mai viex nawh hoek ka simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha dee an.
Yam an Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ot ka veng Li-pi-la ka daux haktiex Ha-ma,
nawh tom prawng ca Si-ti-cia dee an.
10 Ka daux veng Li-pi-la,
ka plak sivoe ngai Si-ti-cia,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa mawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha pauh ka can kawn simex Si-ti-cia yum khawm uik,
khawm simiang ting Yo-tax khankix heue.
11 cKhaix an,
nawh koik tawm ngai Si-ti-cia lih khawm ra khawn,
teei maox ceng tawng phrawk nawh kah.
Simiang Pa-pi-loeng viex nawh hu ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai laung nawh ot daux thawng tom yum nawh heue.
Nyiex buan laux leeing
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 25.8–17)
12 Yam giex simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka kaing daux kao dim num khix phuan kao ngaix,
pui cao teem coi simiang,
cao kaing pui pa pau simiang pa kaux Ni-pu-sha-la-tang laig ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
13 dNawh ploi ngu gleeing nyiex buan,
nyiex haw simiang,
mai can nyiex ma ting pui pa koe min khawm uik.
14 Pui naing nawh yieh ka guh ding veng laux.
15 Khaix an,
Ni-pu-sha-la-tang giex ka pui pa deuh tix ot daux veng,
pui pa ap tix ang pex ka nawh,
khawm pui pa chix yuh kaing taix khankix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik.
16 Viang mawh nan,
deuh pui pa blawn hot khankix ot ka Yo-tax,
mai nhie kix yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix siiet,
mai ka daux kaung ma khankix heue.
17 ePui Pa-pi-loeng hoik lauh max ka rong tawng,
daux jhup jaong tawng,
mai rawm dung tawng pa ot ka daux nyiex buan,
mai teei tawng khawm uik viex hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
18 Kix kawx hoik teei ka jauk pa siao kieh khu singax,
mai aw nyaox,
si kiep ngu,
klong ting pa sau nham khawng brie ot daux,
mawk ngu bhawng,
mai tawng pa siao yam yuh kaing krub ku ceu hoik viex hu khawm uik.
19 Kix baox gah hu ka kraung ceu pa yuh ka khri mau ku ceu pa mawh:
klong iak,
mawk pa sau gaox ngu daux,
klong ting pa saux nham khawng brie daux,
si ngien nyaox,
jawk kawk ngu,
mawk ngu bhawng,
mai kawk pa prauk plai daux.
20 Yam sum simiang Saw-law-meung nyiex buan,
kraung tawng pa yuh nawh mawh:
rong tawng ra gawng,
daux jhup jaong tawng,
dung rawm tawng pa ting,
moi pa yuh ka tawng pa joh kaux cong ka dung rawm tawng kao ra mu;
kraung tawng in kix been cien ang lai pon ciang.
21-22 Rong tawng ra gawng hoik awm paox tix;
krax lhaong tix gawng koe daix kongjhee,
vung viang koe phuan kongjhee loe kongchuing.
Ka daux rong mawh pa yi,
been bu tawng koe atigliah phuan kongli.
Toh rong ku gawng yuh kaing rong koe kah,
krax lhaong koe ra kongjhee ra kongchuing,
vung viang hoik sau rub pa yuh ka tawng mai riang plix mak cang tawng kah.
23 Rub pa koe ka piang toh rong ku nyaing,
riang plix mak cang koe ka tix yieh mu,
jauh ka piang tiex chix yaox ka tix dim kao liah mu heue.
Giex pui Yo-tax hu ka Pa-pi-loeng
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 25.18–21, 27–30)
24 Simiang pa pau Ni-pu-sha-la-tang kawx hoik giex ka simiang cao giex kaing li Si-lai-ya,
ka simiang cao giex kaing li ka daux ra kaux Si-fang-ya,
mai ka simiang pa ting ceu ka daux nyiex buan loe kaux hu nang.
25 Nawh baox giex simiang ka cao phung naing tix kaux,
pui cao teem nyiex haw simiang pa deuh ot daux veng aliah kaux,
simiang cao phung naing na bat ra pa yuh kaing pa priex tix deuh ka lai ting ceu pa mox pui naing tix kaux,
mai pui pa ting ceu ka daux haktiex tix gliah kaux.
26-27 Ni-pu-sha-la-tang phrawk kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Pa-pi-loeng;
yam an,
simiang ot daux veng Li-pi-la haktiex Ha-ma.
Ka daux haktiex an,
nawh tawx lox nhie pui lauh ka kix,
mai pauh kix yum heue.
Yuh nin,
pui giex ka kawn pui Yo-tax lih hu khaing haktiex kix heue.
28 Plak greem mawh kaux pui pa hoik giex laung Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha:
yam mawh nawh simiang ka daux aliah num,
nawh hoik giex laung ka pui loe rheng mai tix nga loe kaux.
29 Ka daux kao daix num hoik giex laung pui khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung daix yieh tix ngoe ra kaux;
30 ka daux tix nga loe num,
Ni-pu-sha-la-tang hoik giex laung pui aliah yieh tipon phuan kaux.
Kaux pui khawm uik koe pon rheng liah yieh kaux heue.
31 Yam mawh Yi-wex Mi-law-tax simiang Pa-pi-loeng ka daux num an,
nawh chix kia ngien ka simiang Yo-tax pa mawh Yaw-ya-cin,
mai ploi nawh lih khaing daux thawng.
Kaing in lih tix mawh khaix hoik giex hu ka Yaw-ya-cin ka daux tix ngoe aliah num khix kao ra tix nga phuan ngaix.
32 Yi-wex Mi-law-tax chix kia ngien Yaw-ya-cin,
mai tawx nawh mawh simiang pa lhaong kaing yuh,
kieh nawh lhaong khaing Vawng tang kaux pa giex hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng.
33 Yaw-ya-cin pon akhuang tix peeik ka kraung pa ceep pui pa laung daux thawng,
mai yaox tix laig ih praix preem tau mai simiang Pa-pi-loeng tom si dauh ju tix.
34 Ka daux singaix yam im ju nawh,
simiang baox tawx ngawh priex ih nawh ku ngaix ka nawh,
tom hoek singaix yum nawh heue.
