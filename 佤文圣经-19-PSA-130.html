Lox rawg buan krax teem krax coi
130
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw maix ka daux krax pa deek rhawm deek rhi.
2 Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox yiiem aux;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa kok yo aux maix teem coi tix.
3 Phan maix tiiem tix deuh ka phi map yix,
mawh pui mawx pa sang pon tix luat luan khaing krax ciyang lie?
4 Kheu nan,
maix pluat ploi ka phi map yix,
mawh kheu nan yix tom khaw tix lhat graung yung yi ka maix heue.
 
5 Aux viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix krox jun ka krax pa teem coi Simiang Siyiex;
aux yum rhawm ka gum lox nawh.
6 Aux een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
kawn lheu simie khaing pui pa pau pon sawm son sang krox praix riang.
Mawh tete,
mai kawn lheu simie khaing pui pa pau pon sawm son sang krox praix riang!
 
7 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
tag caw tix yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik koe nawh krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix;
nawh hoik grum koe mai buan son krax taoh.
8 aNawh tag sang taoh pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex,
kieh kix luat luan khaing phi map phi ghram khawm uik heue.
