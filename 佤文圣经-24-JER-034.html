Gum lox pa tawx ka Si-ti-cia
34
aYam lai lawm Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
mai pui naing cie nawh,
khawm pui naing ku meung ku ceu pui pa giex nawh kaing piang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka vung viang veng,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
2 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tawx lox nhie aux deeh krai ka Si-ti-cia simiang Yo-tax ah nin:
“Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik ap tix tawx veng in ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng;
nawh tag sang ploi ngu gleeing veng in laux leeing.
3 Maix ang doh yaox tix luat luan;
pui tag sang giex ka maix,
mai ap maix ka daux tawm taix nawh.
Maix tag sang si kah tau mai nawh,
khaix an viex maix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
4 Si-ti-cia eue,
maix tag caw tix bre ka rhawm rhi tix nghiet heue!
Phan mawh maix,
aux ah nin:
maix ang yaox tix yum ka naing;
5 Maix tag yum ka daux pa nhim.
Kawn pui tag sang si paung ka maix,
gleeing nyawng bhawng son maix,
awm tax du maix yuh nan,
pa mawh simiang sivoe maix khankix,
koe pui si paung ka kix,
mai gleeing nyawng bhawng son kix yuh nan.
Kawn pui tag sang ah lox rhax yiiem doe son maix,
mai yiiem ah nin:
‘Eue,
simiang ex eue!’
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
aux deeh krai gum lox in ka simiang Si-ti-cia ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
7 Yam an,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng ploi pui naing tix hoek lai lawm tix puing ka veng pa koe daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai veng pa koe daux Yo-tax pa deuh in kiex ra mu,
pa mawh veng La-ciex mai veng Ya-si-cia,
mawh kheu haktiex Yo-tax kawn deuk ngie veng pa kaux pa cong in kiex ra mu khaing heue.
Pa lieh lheu ka sijax mukheu pa pluat ploi ka siliex cawng
8 Simiang Si-ti-cia hoik sax yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
sang pluat ploi
9 ka siliex cawng pui Si-paw-lai,
mawh simex ang ah,
bun ang ah,
tag sang pluat ploi ka khawm uik,
son sang kieh ka daux paox ceu paox cad pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai koe pui pa yuh siliex cawng.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
can pui pa tiiem ceu kaux tix ot daux sijax mukheu in,
mai simiang khankix hoik cu tix pluat ploi ka siliex cawng tix ku kaux,
mai ang lai baox kieh kix mawh siliex cawng.
Pui in kix hoik hawt ka lox sijax mukheu khawm uik,
hoik pluat ploi ka siliex cawng khankix.
11 Viang mawh nan,
khaix an kix deeh tieh loh ka rhawm keud tix,
mai giex ka siliex cawng khankix deeh hoek,
baox deeh kieh kix yuh siliex cawng heue.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex baox deeh si kah ka aux.
13 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tag nhie aux deeh krai ka kawn pui ah nin:
“Yam jauh aux tix taoh pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik yuh tax du pex siliex cawng kah,
mai aux hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau maix kix.
Aux tawx lox ka kix,
14 bYam hoek aliah num tix bawg,
kix tag caw tix pluat ploi ka paox ceu paox cad pui Si-paw-lai pa mawh siliex cawng kix.
Khaix hoik yuh kix kaing liah num,
tag caw tix kieh kix yaox tix luat luan.
Viang mawh nan,
tax du pex ang simi ka aux,
ang nghiet ka lox aux heue.
15 “Yam in,
pex hoik tieh loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
hoik yuh ka kaing pa khaw rhawm aux.
Pex hoik cu tix pluat ploi ka paox ceu paox cad pui Yi-seu-liex pa mawh siliex cawng khankix,
hoek ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai yuh sijax mukheu ka daux nyiex buan pa krub ka aux.
16 Viang mawh nan,
pex baox tieh loh ka rhawm keud tix,
mai hoik map nai ka aux.
Pex deeh giex ka siliex cawng pa hoik pluat ploi,
pa hoik pon krax pa luat luan khankix hoek,
mai baox deeh kieh kix mawh siliex cawng pex.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
pex ang nghiet ka aux,
ang bax pluat ploi ka paox ceu paox cad pui Yi-seu-liex pa mawh siliex cawng khankix.
Pex krox tix jhak ka heue!
Aux ploi pex hu,
mai kieh pex yum ka hu tix si pup ka naing,
ka jhawm saux preng,
mai ka cai rhawm.
Aux tag sang kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex yaoh yee ka ceu pa si pup pex.
18-19 “Simiang pui Yo-tax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung,
simiang nyiex haw mai cao giex kaing li,
khawm can simiang pa hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai aux khawm uik;
kix lug kawn moi lih koe ra plak,
mai hu si nax,
krai grawng pa hoik yuh ka sijax mukheu.
Viang mawh nan,
kix hoik yuh laux ka sijax mukheu,
ang priex tix hawt ka been pa tat sijax mukheu tix deuh.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang lug ka kix,
awm hoik lug kix ka kawn moi an yuh nan.
20 “Aux tag sang ap tix tawx kix ka pui koeng pa keud tix sang yuh kix laux leeing;
to kaux yum kix tag sang mawh priex preem sim pa pu ka rao ma mai to sat pa hu ka caong tix piang tiex.
21 Aux tag sang ap Si-ti-cia simiang Yo-tax mai simiang naing pa ot greem nawh ka pui koeng pa keud tix sang yuh kix laux leeing;
aux tag sang teei tix ap kix ka pui naing Pa-pi-loeng pa dak tix deeh tix vud tix cu khankix.
22 Aux tag sang tawx lox nhie pui naing Pa-pi-loeng deeh hoek ka veng in.
Kix tag sang puing kah,
pon kah,
mai gleeing ka veng in laux leeing.
Aux tag sang kieh veng Yo-tax ku mu riem,
ang koe pui pa paung ot kah.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
