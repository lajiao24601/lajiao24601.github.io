Lai Yaw-paw
Krai dawt
Lai Yaw-paw tiiem pa si pup pui mhawm grawng krax pa laux pa leeing ku ceu khankix: pa mawh kawn praix khawm uik mai sin ngao, ka to kaux baox pon jhawm saux pa seeing pa brai pui ka khankix. Ka daux lai yaog yaw simin ting loe mu gum lox pa ra lai pawk, cao tiiem tiiem ka krax keud krax sawn paox grawm Yaw-paw mai to kaux cao Yaw-paw son krax laux leeing pa jiiem tix hoek in. Dee nghoik, Siyiex lih si kah ka Yaw-paw. Grawng pa kia ngien Siyiex ka kawn pui, hoik mawh kaing ting pa krai lao ka daux phuk lai in heue.
Pa teei paox grawm Yaw-paw khankix krax been li hoek kieh mawh pa laux leeing Yaw-paw, hoik keud mawh Siyiex tawx pa mhawm ciyang pa caox, grawng pa si pup Yaw-paw ka krax laux krax leeing, hoik een mawh phi map pa yuh nawh. Viang mawh nan, Yaw-paw ang rieb ka grawng pa kieh kix yuh nin, ang yeung to kaux tix khaw tix pon krax ciyang pa kin yuh nin, mawh kheu hoik mawh nawh pui pa rauh pa see tix kaux. Nawh ang tawng Siyiex yuh ka mawx lai kieh krax laux leeing pa ting tom nie, lih hoek ka piang to kaux pui pa awm nawh yuh nin, mawh kheu nan nawh tom viex rhawm ting chawk cheng ka Siyiex. Viang mawh nan Yaw-paw ang baox grai krax yum rhawm; nawh kied simie tix pon buan son krax gaux rhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex, mai deeh cox pon min plak ngai pui rauh pui see heue.
Awm pa mawh grawng Yaw-paw, Siyiex ang bax pawk kah, viang mawh nan krax pa yum rhawm nawh, hoik teei dee bleeh gum lox lai yaog yaw, krai grawng siawp sibrawm pa singax singeh mai ping nyia cuyi to kaux tix. Dee si dauh, Yaw-paw viex rhawm awn rhawm tiiem doh keud ka ping nyia cuyi mai pa ting siawp sibrawm Siyiex, kied klih rhawm tix ka hoik ah lox pa vao ka Siyiex sivoe an heue.
Dee nghoik lox phuk lai in, tiiem grawng Yaw-paw deeh pon krax pa mhawm awn singaix pa hoik luan, mai kawn lheu nie haok huan khaing singaix pa hoik luan. Siyiex ne raw ka paox grawng Yaw-paw khankix, ang tawng dee bleeh grawng pa si pup Yaw-paw ka krax kha rhawm. Mawh ngie Yaw-paw tix kaux khaing, pa pon tawng alo Siyiex mawh pa kawn lheu ting khaing pa tiiem grawng been li heue.
