Lih rawm khaing daux rang
(Lai Simin-pui 20.1–13)
17
Cub kawn pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing daux tiag haktiex Shuing,
awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex,
tix dauh hoik tix dauh gah tix.
Kix hoek bang ka daux Li-fe-ting;
dee an rawm nyaux ang koe,
2 mai kix salao ka Maw-si ah nin:
“Tawx yix nyaux rawm heue.”
Maw-si pawk ah nin:
“Pex lai salao yuh ka mawx?
Pex lai cheuk cheu ka Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx?”
3 Viang mawh nan,
kawn pui kied simie tix nyaux rawm,
ca kawn nied tix salao ka Maw-si.
Kix ah nin:
“Maix lai viex yix lih khaing Ai-cix yuh ka mawx?
Kieh yix khawm kawn simex kawn bun yix,
khawng brie yix sang yum ka simie tix nyaux rawm lie?”
4 Maw-si khiox khaw rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux sang yuh ka mawx ka kawn pui in kix lie?
Kix dex sang dheem aux yum ka simaox.”
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix viex simiang kawn pui Yi-seu-liex hu tau mai maix sivoe pui hun,
ka daux taix maix,
caw tix viex ka khaox gix pa hoik lauh maix rawm klawng Ni-law kah.
6 Aux tag cung plak sivoe maix ka piang gawng Heu-liex.
Maix lauh ka rang an,
mai rawm tag lih khaing,
son sang nyaux pui kah.”
Maw-si yuh an ka sivoe ngai simiang Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
7 Dee an kok ceu kaux mawh Ma-sha,
mai baox kok mawh Mi-li-paa;
mawh kheu salao pui Yi-seu-liex tan,
mai cheuk cheu ka Simiang Siyiex.
Kix chawk ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ot ang ot tau mai ex lie?”
Yuh naing mai pui Ya-ma-lix
8 Pui Ya-ma-lix hoek ka baoh haktiex Li-fe-ting mai puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
9 Maw-si ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Maix hu laug pui khankix tix blah,
mai rien tix hu puing ka pui Ya-ma-lix.
Pa sax aux tag cung ka piang choek gawng,
mai viex ka khaox gix pa nhie Siyiex aux viex ka daux taix tix.”
10 Yaw-su-yax yuh awm pa ah Maw-si,
mai hu puing ka pui Ya-ma-lix,
Maw-si mai Ya-loeng,
mai Hu-eux haok ka piang choek gawng jhak ka grawng naing.
11 Ca mawh yaog Maw-si taix tix,
pui Yi-seu-liex pex kah;
mai yam ploi nawh taix tix,
pui Ya-ma-lix puing pex kah.
12 Taix Maw-si hoik si klua,
Ya-loeng mai Hu-eux tom teei simaox tix mu,
mai deuh nawh ngawm ka piang;
kiex khawm ra kaux cung praok nawh mai giex rien ka taix nawh,
tom klip singaix.
13 Yuh nin,
Yaw-su-yax tom pon pauh ka pui Ya-ma-lix heue.
14 bKhaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Tiiem tix deuh grawng pa puing pex bawg in ka daux phuk lai,
son ang ju khaix sang piawm.
Krai Yaw-su-yax mhong,
aux tag sang yuh pui Ya-ma-lix laux leeing khawm uik.”
15 Maw-si sum khu dee tan,
mai kok ceu kaux mawh “Simiang Siyiex mawh man chix aux.”
16 Nawh ah nin:
“Yaog man chix Simiang Siyiex!
Simiang Siyiex tag yuh naing ka pui Ya-ma-lix tix ju hoik tix ju heue.”
