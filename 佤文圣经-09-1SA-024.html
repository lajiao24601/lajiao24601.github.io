Ta-wue ang pauh ka Sao-law
24
Khaix hoek Sao-law khaing leue tix pui Fe-li-see,
mai tawng ot Ta-wue dex daux tiag riem Yin Ci-ti,
2 nawh tom thung pui naing pa kied kua Yi-seu-liex hu loe rheng kaux,
hoek ka plak lih singaix “simaox pex tiag” mai sawg ka Ta-wue mai pui greem nawh.
3 Sao-law hoek dex ka lai lawm kawn yung praok krax ka daux rang tix mu,
nawh laig aing tix ka daux,
Ta-wue mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh jiet mox tix ot ka daux rang dee raux daux an.
4 Pui pa ot greem Ta-wue ah nin ka nawh:
“Bawg yam cie maix hoik hoek!
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ka maix,
nawh sang ap pui pa koeng ka maix ka daux tawm taix maix,
maix chix tix yuh awm pa simie maix tix yuh ka nawh.”
Ta-wue mo tix hu,
mai briex pat ka jaong sibex dha Sao-law.
5 Khaix an Ta-wue ang nyawm rhawm ka kaing pa hoik yuh tix an,
6 aah nin ka pui pa ot greem tix:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex tieb tie aux paw yuh krax pa laux leeing ka cao simiang tix;
nawh hoik mawh simiang laug Simiang Siyiex tix kaux!
Aux ang doh yuh nawh laux leeing;
mawh kheu hoik mawh nawh simiang,
pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex tix kaux.”
7 Ta-wue hoik pon kieh pui pa ot greem tix cu lox,
ang cu kix yuh Sao-law laux leeing heue.
Sao-law yaog tix kaoh cung,
lih hu khaing daux rang an,
mai ca nied tix hu krax;
8 Ta-wue lih hawt khaix nawh,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang aux,
Vawng eue!”
Sao-law veeh ngai tix,
Ta-wue krub ka piang tiex,
mai ah nin:
9 “Yuh ka mawx Vawng lai cu nghiet ka lox pie lhien pui,
ah aux keud tix sang pauh ka maix lie?
10 In singaix,
ngai maix hoik yaox ka daux rang,
Simiang Siyiex hoik ap maix ka daux tawm taix aux.
Pui pa ot greem aux nhie aux pauh maix yum,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang cu,
aux ah nin ka kix,
aux ang doh yuh maix laux leeing,
mawh kheu hoik mawh maix simiang pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex tix kaux.
11 Keeing aux eue,
maix jhak!
Ka daux taix aux hoik jhawp ka jaong sibex maix!
Phan aux sang pauh ka maix ang mawh pa nu,
viang mawh nan,
aux pat ngie ka jaong sibex in khaing.
Grawng in maix tag caw tix yum rhawm kah,
aux ang sax tieh vheen ka maix,
mai ang sax koe rhawm tix sang yuh maix laux leeing tix bawg kawx.
Aux ang sax yuh kaing ceu pa lud loe ka maix tix ceu kawx,
maix lai kawn sang sawg ka aux,
pauh ka aux.
12 “Kieh Simiang Siyiex prawng si nax pa khaw mai pa ang khaw.
Kieh nawh pawk bix son aux,
mawh kheu ang aux bax keud tix sang yuh maix laux leeing.
13 Lox been prim ah nin:
‘Kaing pa caox lih khaing pui pa caox.’
Aux ang doh bax keud tix sang yuh maix laux leeing.
14 bSimiang pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang pauh ka ceu pa tix lie?
Leue nawh ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh sox yum tix mu,
mai diiep tix mu lie?
15 Simiang Siyiex tag sang prawng,
si nax maix mai aux son ceu pa khaw mai pa ang khaw.
Kieh nawh si kaw tix yaox sidawng,
kia ngien ka aux,
taoh aux luat luan khaing daux tawm taix maix heue.”
16 Khaix hoik uik krai Ta-wue lox,
mai Sao-law ah nin:
“Kawn aux Ta-wue eue,
hoik mawh maix tete lie?”
Mawh kheu nan nawh ploi lox yiiem tix.
17 Nawh ah nin:
“Maix khaw heue,
mai aux hoik lud.
Aux hoik viex krax pa caox ka maix yuh nin,
mai maix kawn viex krax pa mhawm ka aux.
18 In singaix,
maix hoik jhi kaing pa mhawm pa yuh maix lih;
viang Simiang Siyiex hoik ap aux ka daux tawm taix maix,
mai maix ang bax pauh ka aux.
19 Yam pon giex pui ka pui pa koeng khankix,
mai ang lai chix ploi nawh lih hu lie?
Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka maix son kaing pa hoik yuh maix ka aux daux in singaix!
20 Yam in aux hoik tawng grawng sang mawh maix simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
kheu nan,
piang meung khawm uik tag sang mawh maix pa kuang.
21 Maix caw tix tiex tu ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
maix deuh ka doeh ceu cad aux,
kieh ceu kaux aux mai ceu kaux pui nyiex aux ang yaox tix grai grup.”
22 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom ah lox pa tiex tu ka Sao-law heue.
Khaix hoik hoik kaing an,
Sao-law deeh ing ka nyiex;
Ta-wue mai pui greem nawh deeh ing ka dee mox kix tix heue.
