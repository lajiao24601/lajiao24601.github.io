Rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex kheu pon krax taoh
116
Aux muh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik nghiet nawh ka lox rawg aux;
nawh hoik nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan.
2 Yam khiox khaw rawg mhaing aux ka nawh ku bawg,
nawh hoik nghiet ka aux.
3 Maox krax yum hoik vung vhien tix ka aux;
pa lhat krax yum hoik tawb kheem ka aux;
aux hoik kha rhawm tix ka daux krax pa khi rhawm khi rhi.
4 Aux tom khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux koe krax khiox khaw ka maix,
khiox khaw maix taoh aux liak!
5 Simiang Siyiex hoik grum koe mai krax pa rauh pa see pa mhawm pa mhiem;
Siyiex ex hoik grum naog mai krax pa caox rhawm.
6 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka pui pa singax rhawm rhi;
yam ot aux ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
mai nawh hoik taoh ka aux.
7 Ka daux rhawm rhi aux eue,
tag caw tix deeh hoek ka daux pa yien pa yawg mhawm mhawm,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik dawg chix kia ngien ka aux heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh aux luat luan khaing krax yum;
nawh ang lai kieh rawm ngai aux jauh,
kieh aux ang lai sideeing tix goh.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang ot ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
hu lai ling ka daux haktiex pui pa im.
10 aAux hoik krai ah nin:
aux kied sidaing koe krax laux rhawm tete;
viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn yum rhawm.
11 Aux krai ah nin kheu lhat si yaung tix:
ang koe pui pa khaw tix een rhawm kah;
viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn kieh krax yum rhawm tix kaux cong heue.
 
12 Buan son pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka aux khawm uik,
aux tag sang pawk yuh ka mawx lie?
13 Aux tag sang yaog kawk ka Simiang Siyiex,
yaog yaw ka buan son krax pa taoh nawh.
14 Aux tag sang ot ka daux bang pa khrawm pui cie nawh paox tix,
plud ka gum lox pa hoik ap pong aux tix deuh ka nawh heue.
 
15 Ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
grawng pa yum pui cie nawh tix kaux hoik mawh kaing pa kied laux rhawm tete heue!
16 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux mawh siliex maix;
aux yuh kaing jhaw maix awm miex tix yuh nan.
Maix hoik pluat ploi ka aux.
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux tag sang tan lu krax tan pa yaog yaw ka maix,
tag sang khiox khaw ka ceu kaux maix.
18 Aux tag sang ot ka daux bang pa khrawm pui cie maix paox tix,
plud ka gum lox pa hoik ap pong aux tix deuh ka maix heue.
19 Aux tag sang ot ka biang brawk nyiex buan cie maix:
pa mawh Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai priex tix hawt kaux cong ka lox pa ap aux tix deuh heue.
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
