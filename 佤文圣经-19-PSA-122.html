Yaog yaw veng Yie-lu-sa-leung
122
Koe pui krai ka aux ah nin:
ex hu ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
yam an,
aux kied sidaing koe krax gaux rhawm heue!
2 Yam in ex hoek dee in,
cung ka siviex veng plak daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
 
3 Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mawh veng pa cox sum tix,
hoik jiet koe pak koe peung mai pa nhim.
4 Pui ku ceu,
pa mawh ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
hoek dee in khawm uik,
hawt ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
tawx krax yaog yaw ka nawh.
5 Dee in koe dang ngeung pa kieh yuh pa rauh pa see;
simiang Yi-seu-liex ot tix prawng ca pui hun dee in heue.
 
6 Tag caw tix khiox khaw pa nhim son Yie-lu-sa-leung:
kieh pui pa muh ka maix haok huan khawm uik.
7 Kieh ka daux veng ot maix koe pa nhim;
kieh nyiex haw ot maix nyawm ot.
8 Mawh kheu son paox grawm aux,
aux tom ah nin ka Yie-lu-sa-leung:
kieh maix pon krax pa nhim heue!
9 Mawh kheu son nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
aux sang khiox khaw krax nyawm ot son maix heue.
