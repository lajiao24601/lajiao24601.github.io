Siyiex kuang ka singaix pa sang hoek
48
Pui nyiex Ya-keux eue,
nghiet ka lox in heue!
Pex mawh pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh kawn praix Yo-tax.
Pex tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
krai krub pex ka Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex;
tete te lai pex ang tie hawt lox krai tix,
mai ca nieb pon krai ka khaing.
2 Pex kawn ce tix mawh pui veng pa singax singeh,
pa hoik een rhawm tix ka pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh simiang kru naing heue.
 
3 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
Aux hoik dawg dha tix krai ka kaing ceu pa sang lih tix mawh;
aux tag sang kieh kaing in kix lih tix mawh dum mawx dum an.
4 Aux hoik tawng grawng pa kiien rhawm kiien rhi maix,
hoik kiien awm rhiem,
kaux cong awm tawng.
5 Aux hoik dawg dha tix krai ka kaing pa sang lih hoek piang maix,
sivoe sang lih kaing in tix mawh aux hoik krai pao,
kieh maix ang pon krai kaing in kix kaoh khaing siyiex pa pie pa lhen,
mai kaoh khaing riang rao pa yuh to kaux tix heue.
 
6 Kaing pa hoik dha aux tix krai tag sang lih tix mawh khawm uik;
maix tag sang krai doh ka lox pa hoik dha aux tix krai mawh tete.
Yam in aux tag sang krai ka maix son kaing khraox pa sang lih tix mawh sivoe ngai,
pa mawh kaing glip ka daux singaix pa hoik luan pa ang maix sax tawng sax yung.
7 Hoek yam in aux tag sang kieh kaing in kix lih tix mawh;
kaing in kix maix ang sax mhong tix bawg kawx;
mawh kheu nan,
maix ang pon tix krai,
maix hoik dawg tawng ka heue.
8 Ang lud,
maix ang sax mhong kah;
ang lud,
maix ang tawng ang yung;
ang lud,
yhaok maix hoik thox tix.
Aux hoik dawg tawng grawng pa pie lhien maix;
tom yam jauh maix tix lih khaing daux vaig miex tix,
maix hoik mawh pui tieh pui vheen heue.
 
9 Son sang kieh pui yaog yaw ka ceu kaux aux,
aux ran gun yeung ka krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix;
aux baox deeh gun yeung,
mai ang yuh maix laux leeing.
10 Aux cheuk cheu ka maix,
viang mawh nan,
ang yuh awm raug mau ka daux glah ngu;
aux teei ngu krax kha rhawm kha rhi pheuk ka maix.
11 Aux yuh nin mawh son to kaux aux.
Aux ang kieh ceu kaux tix hoek krax kaig krax tua;
aux ang kieh pui tang kaux rom tix pon min plak ngai aux heue.
Sai-lu-seex pa mawh simiang pa laug Simiang Siyiex
12 aYa-keux eue,
nghiet ka lox krai aux heue!
Pui Yi-seu-liex pa hoik kok aux ka heue,
nghiet heue!
Aux mawh Siyiex,
mawh pa jauh,
mawh pa ghoik;
aux mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing heue!
13 Aux hoik teei taix tix yuh ka riah haktiex;
aux hoik teei taix khawn dawm tix nih ka rao ma.
Aux khrawm ka rao ma mai haktiex,
rao ma mai haktiex cung ka plak sivoe ngai aux dum mawx dum an heue.
 
14 Pex khrawm paox tix khawm uik,
hoek tix nghiet ka lox krai aux heue!
Ang sax koe siyiex tix kaux kawx pa hoik sax dha tix krai lox,
pui pa hoik laug aux tag sang puing ka Pa-pi-loeng;
nawh tag sang hawt tix yuh kaing jhaw ka daux alo aux.
15 Mawh aux pa krai lox ka nawh,
pa kok ka nawh;
aux viex nawh lih,
mai kieh nawh haok huan heue.
 
16 Hoek ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai nghiet lox krai aux heue!
Tom yam jauh aux hoik krai lox ka pex ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
hoik kieh gum lox tix lih mawh ku gum.
Yam in Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik tawx riang rao ka aux,
mai ploi aux hu heue.
Grawng kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex ka pui cie tix
17 Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh,
Simiang Siyiex pa taoh ka maix krai yuh nin:
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix;
son sang pon maix ceu pa mhawm,
aux sidiex gau ka maix;
aux hoik jhi krax pa khaw maix tix sang lawng.
18 Aux kied simie maix nghiet ka lox tawx tix heue!
Phan maix nghiet ka lox tawx aux,
pa nhim tag sang awm rawm klawng pa ang chix hit yuh nan;
pa rauh see tag sang hoek awm pu pak rawm ting yuh nan.
19 Kawn praix maix tag sang haoh hun awm mu mhaik yuh nan;
aux ang kieh kix laux leeing ju heue.
 
20 bTo lih khaing Pa-pi-loeng heue!
Lih hu awm pa simie pex tix lih hu heue!
Krai lox mhawm in tom ting si num;
krai hu tom hoek ka caong daog haktiex ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh ka siliex tix Yi-seu-liex heue!
21 Yam thung Simiang Siyiex pui cie tix hu laig ka daux pak mhaik,
kix ang simie tix nyaux rawm;
nawh hoik kieh rawm lih jauh khaing rang simaox;
nawh lauh ka rang simaox,
mai kieh rawm keet buing lih khaing heue.
 
22 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig:
pex ang yaox krax pa nhim heue.
