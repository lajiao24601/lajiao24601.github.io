Tieh loh ka pak peung tix
(Lai Ma-kheu 9.2–13; Lai Lu-cia 9.28–36)
17
aKhaix hoik luan liah singaix,
Ye-sux viex Pi-teux mai Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang pux aik ra kaux haok ka gawng lhaong tix mu.
2 Ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
Ye-sux tom tieh loh pak peung tix:
pak ngai nawh riang awm singaix,
kraung ceep nawh kawx paing singax awm pa riang singaix yuh nan.
3 Pui gau loe kaux yaox Yi-li-yax mai Maw-si si kah tau mai Ye-sux dum mawx dum an.
4 Pi-teux ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Simiang eue,
ex ot dee in kied mhawm heue!
Phan maix cu,
aux tag sang sum nyiex gox bang dee in loe lhang,
tix lhang mawh cie maix,
tix lhang mawh cie Maw-si,
tix lhang mawh Yi-li-yax heue.”
5 bYam kawn si kah Pi-teux,
lon pai awm tix lon tawb kheem ka kix;
koe lox lih hoek khaing pai awm ah nin:
“In mawh kawn simex pa muh aux,
aux muh ka nawh.
Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka nawh heue!”
6 Pui gau mhong lox in kied sidaing lhat graung kah,
kix gup tix krub piang tiex khawm uik.
7 Ye-sux hoek ka kix,
mai dieh kix ah nin:
“Kaoh,
paw vi lhat heue!”
8 Yam yaog kix kaing tix yaox ngie Ye-sux tix kaux khaing,
mai ang lai yaox pui tang kaux khawm uik heue.
9 Yam lih kix khaing piang gawng,
Ye-sux tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Sivoe sang kaoh im kawn kawn pui khaing krax yum,
ang pon tix krai riang su sai ceu pa hoik yaox pex ka pui tix kaux kawx heue.”
10 cMawh kheu nan,
pui gau Ye-sux chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri ah Yi-li-yax tag sang ran hoek sivoe lie?”
11 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yi-li-yax tag sang ran hoek tix mawh cao pa rien khre khawm uik.
12 dViang mawh nan,
aux krai ka pex,
Yi-li-yax hoik hoek,
pui ang tawng ka nawh,
mai tieh tix nieb pon yuh ka nawh.
Awm an,
kawn kawn pui kawx tag sang kham krax pa nu nai pui nang heue.”
13 Pui gau khankix tom taug tawng gum lox pa krai Ye-sux mawh pa krai son Yaw-hang cao cub com heue.
Pai ka kawn nyawm pa laig phi daux moi
(Lai Ma-kheu 9.14–29; Lai Lu-cia 9.37–43)
14 Yam deeh hoek kix ka dee ot pui hun dee an,
koe pui tix kaux hoek mai grux ngawng tix ka sivoe ngai Ye-sux,
15 ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka kawn simex aux liak!
Nawh koe jhawm saux laung,
kied kha rhawm tix,
mai yie goh ka daux ngu mai ka daux rawm jheeng jheeng.
16 Aux viex nawh hoek ka pui gau maix,
viang mawh kawx nan,
kix ang pon pai nawh moi heue.”
17 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui baux ju in kied ang koe krax yum rhawm,
kied caox caig heue!
Aux kawn sang ot mai pex dee in miex glang lie?
Kawn sang gun yeung ka pex miex glang lie?
Viex kawn nyawm hoek dee in heue!”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ne raw ka phi an,
phi an tom lih,
mai jhawm saux kawn nyawm an moi dum mawx dum an heue.
19 Khaix an,
pui gau briex chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx yix ang pon grheu phi an lih hu lie?”
20-21 fYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Mawh kheu ang krax yum rhawm pex kub.
Aux krai ka pex tete,
phan krax yum rhawm pex sidu ting pie simie taux klaox tix sok khaing,
mai ah nin ka gawng in:
‘Gah maix dee in hoek dee an heue!’
Gawng an tag sang gah tix hu.
Ang koe ceu pa ang pex pon yuh tix ceu kawx heue.e”
Ye-sux baox grox krai sang yum tix
(Lai Ma-kheu 9.30–32; Lai Lu-cia 9.43–45)
22 Yam khrawm pui gau tix ot ka Cia-li-lix khawm uik,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Kawn kawn pui tag sang ap tix tawx ka daux tawm taix kawn pui,
23 kix tag sang pauh ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik yum nawh loe singaix,
nawh tag sang kaoh im heue.”
Pui gau mhong ka mai kied laux rhawm ka heue.
Tawx ka keen nyiex buan
24 gYam hoek Ye-sux mai pui gau ka Cia-pai-nong,
koe pui pa koh keen nyiex buan ra loe kaux hoek ka Pi-teux mai chawk ah nin:
“Sara pex tawx ang tawx ka keen nyiex buan lie?”
25 Pi-teux pawk ah nin:
“Tawx ka heue.”
Yam laig Pi-teux ka daux nyiex,
Ye-sux ran chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Si-meung,
pui mawx pa tawx keen ka simiang haktiex in mai ka keen kaing pui,
mawh pui meung haktiex ot tix lie?
Mawh pui meung tang meung lie?
Rhawm keud maix sang ah ka mawx ka lie?”
26 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Mawh pui meung tang meung.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pui meung haktiex ot tix tag chix tix ang kieh tawx keen.
27 Viang mawh nan,
ex paw vi lud rhawm cub pui in kix.
Maix hu miiet kax ka praok nhawng,
mai viex kax pa jauh maix tix miiet tix mu hoek,
vih ka daux jheeh,
maix tom tag yaox mau tix plah;
mau in kub tix tawx keen son maix mai son nyiex buan aux,
maix teei tix tawx ka kix heue.”
