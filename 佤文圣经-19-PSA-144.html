Simiang yaog yaw buan son kheu pon pex tix
144
Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa kia ngien aux pon krax pa yaog yaw heue!
Nawh gau aux puing ka naing,
pheuk aux ka grawng naing heue.
2 Nawh mawh riang rao aux,
tab pa kaux cong ka aux,
mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux,
cao taoh ka aux;
aux deuh rhawm tix ka nawh,
mai khiox khaw nawh kia ngien ka tix.
Nawh kieh meung ka piang haktiex dip tix ot greem caong aux heue.
 
3 aSimiang Siyiex eue,
kawn pui sawn tix mawh pa tix,
maix khex kawn muh ka nawh lie?
Kawn pui pa chix laux chix leeing sawn tix mawh pa tix,
maix khex kawn chix kia ngien ka nawh lie?
4 Nawh hoik awm ngie tix toek bhawm khaing;
ka daux pa im ju nawh hoik awm pang peue pa chix grai hu yuh nan heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix plak ka rao ma,
kieh to kaux maix hoek tix lih khaing;
khiox khaw maix chuh ka gawng lua,
mai kieh taox ngu lih khaing gawng.
6 Khiox khaw maix tawx pu plak praix lih,
mai kieh pui koeng prung prang tix tang hu;
khiox khaw maix puing te,
kieh kix tang yaog tix to.
7 Khiox khaw maix tawx taix pa taoh teem tix lih khaing rao ma,
taoh ka aux,
bluh aux lih khaing dee raux rawm;
taoh aux luat luan khaing pui koeng pa ting riang pa hoek khaing plak praix;
8 kix ang sax krai lox pa mawh pa meh tix bawg kawx,
pie krai kix lox tiex tu kawx,
hoik mawh ka daux pa pie lhien heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang rhax lai rhax pa khraox yaog yaw ka maix;
aux sang tauh ting jung lah yaog yaw ka maix.
10 Maix hoik kieh simiang puing pex,
hoik taoh ka siliex maix Ta-wue.
11 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui koeng pa cau nyhau,
taoh aux luat luan khaing pui koeng pa ting riang pa hoek khaing plak praix;
kix ang sax krai lox pa mawh pa meh tix bawg kawx,
pie krai kix lox tiex lox tu kawx,
hoik mawh ka daux pa pie lhien heue.
 
12 Kieh kawn simex ex
yam nhum ju kieh nawh awm khaox plix pa haok huan yuh nan;
kieh kawn bun ex
awm rong nyiex haw pa jawg pui mhawm grang yuh nan.
13 Kieh nyiex krao ex naog,
mai deuh ka nghox koi ku ceu;
kieh phung yung pa liang ka daux pak rib keh kaing rheng kaing mheen.
14 Kieh khawng brie ex koe tom haoh hun,
ang koe pa laux kawn,
ang koe pa som nai.
Kieh ka daux laih ot ex ang koe lox yiiem heue.
 
15 Meung pa pon yaox krax pa yuh nin kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
Pui pa yaog Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex khankix kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
