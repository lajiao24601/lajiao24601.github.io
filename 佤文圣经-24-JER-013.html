Lox phek tiiem ka maox rawd nyoi
13
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka aux yuh nin:
“Hu liag maox rawd nyoi man keei sum tix nyaing phrawk ka nyoi tix;
viang mawh nan,
paw vi ce ot daux rawm.”
2 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom liag maox rawd nyoi tix nyaing,
mai prawk kah.
3 Simiang Siyiex baox krai ka aux ah nin:
4 “Maix hu ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
mai mox maox rawd nyoi pa prawk maix an ot ka daux dawh rang.”
5 Aux tom priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
hu ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
mai mox maox rawd nyoi dee an heue.
6 Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka aux ah nin:
“Maix hu teei maox rawd nyoi an ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.”
7 Aux tom deeh hoek dee an,
ka dee mox maox rawd nyoi an;
aux yaox ka maox rawd nyoi an hoik laux,
mai ang lai chix siao ka heue.
8 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex baox si kah ka aux.
Nawh ah nin:
9 “Aux tag kawn sang hawt tix yuh laux ka krax haok huan pa hoik yaw pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung rang di.
10 Cub pui pa caox pa caig in kix ang cu tix nghiet ka gum lox tawx aux.
Kix ca kawn kiien rhawm caox caig,
mai krub ka siyiex tang kaux.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tag sang awm maox rawd nyoi in yuh nin,
ang lai chix tix siao heue.
11 Tete te lai,
aux ce tix sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax kawb tix dex ka aux.
Awm maox rawd nyoi pa kawb tix ot ka to kaux yuh nan.
Aux sang kieh kix mawh pui cie tix,
son sang hoek ceu kaux aux krax pa yaog yaw,
pa yung yi;
viang mawh nan,
kix ang nghiet ka aux heue.”
Lox phek tiiem ka ngoi plai
12 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex krai ah nin ka aux:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
maix tag caw tix tawx lox ka pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
sau plai naog ka daux ngoi plai ku mu.
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
‘Yiex hoik tawng ka daux ngoi plai ku mu tag caw tix sau naog.’
13 Maix baox krai ka kix ah nin:
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang teei plai kieh pui pa ot ka daux haktiex in yoek khawm uik;
ka daux bawg yam pa giex Ta-wue kaing,
pa mawh simiang,
cao giex kaing li,
cao jhai,
mai pui pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix,
tag sang yoek khawm uik.
14 Aux tag sang yuh kix laux leeing,
mawh kuad ang ah,
kawn nyawm ang ah,
kieh kix ra lai simah ka paox tix,
awm ngoi plai pa kleuk ka paox tix max yuh nan.
Aux ang doh caox rhawm ka kix,
muh ka kix,
ang koe rhawm tix ot plak kix nang;
aux tag sang yuh kix laux leeing tete heue.”
Yie-li-mi ne raw ka pa ca nyhau
15 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Pex tag caw tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
mai bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox nawh.
16 Sivoe ang pa faik lih hoek,
sivoe ang nyang bax goh sideeing caong tix ka piang gawng pa kied faik,
pex tag caw tix yaog yaw min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Sivoe ang nawh nyang bax kieh krax keud pot pex
pa riang tieh tix lih mawh pa faik,
Pex tag caw tix yaog yaw min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex.
17 Phan pex ang nghiet,
aux tag sang mox tix yiiem kheu pa ca nyhau pex;
aux tag sang laux rhawm mai roik rawm ngai,
mawh kheu pui cie Simiang Siyiex hoik giex viex tix lih hu heue.
 
18 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Hu krai ka simiang mai ka miex simiang,
nhie kix lih khaing dang ngeung.
Mawh kheu mhok khri pa koe ka piang kaing kix hoik cawd heue.
19 Pui koeng hoik lai lawm ka veng Yo-tax pa koe ka plak seh ku mu;
ang koe pui pa pon teem ka kix.
Pui hoik giex ka pui Yo-tax khawm uik heue.”
20 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
jhak heue,
pui koeng kaoh khaing caong ma laong heue!
Pui pa tawx aux maix kia ngien,
pa hoik yaw maix rang di ka ot dee mawx lie?
21 Pui pa een maix mawh paox grawng tix lai pon puing ka maix,
mai pon kuang ka maix;
kawn sang koe maix lox pa tix lie?
Maix tag sang kha rhawm tix awm bun yha pa sang keh yuh nan.
22 Phan maix simie tix sang tawng,
yuh ka mawx maix lai si pup ka krax laux leeing in,
yuh ka mawx kraung ceep maix lai griah,
pui lai tud rhim ka maix,
in hoik mawh phi map pa tang yuh maix pa deeh hoek ka piang maix.
23 Pui pa lung hak sang pon chix tieh hak tix paing lie?
Sivai kaoh ku chix tik ka hak pa paing pawd tix lie?
Phan chix,
maix pa yuh kaing caox si jheeing tag chix yuh kaing pa mhawm.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang grheu maix hu,
awm nyhee ka daux tiag pa phlu bhaung yuh nan.
25 Nawh ah nin,
in hoik mawh krax pawk bix pa khaw maix tix pon.
Nawh but rhawm tix yuh nin ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik piawm maix ka nawh,
mai hu deuh rhawm tix ka siyiex pa pie lhien.
26 Simiang Siyiex tag sang peeik kraung maix ghle khaing to kaux maix,
kieh maix kaig tua.
27 Nawh hoik yaox ka kaing pa seeing pa brai yuh maix;
nawh hoik yaox grawng pa hawt maix khaix siyiex been li tang ceu ka piang gawng mai ka daux kaung,
awm bun coh kaux pa sawg ka simex paox muh tix yuh nan heue.
Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
pex hoik laux leeing heue!
Sang kieh pex to kaux tix singax singeh yam mawx lie?
