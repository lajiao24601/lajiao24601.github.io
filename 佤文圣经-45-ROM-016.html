Pao-law chawk kawn Khrit mhawm ot
16
Aux krai pux o ex Fe-pi ka pex;
nawh mawh pa yuh kaing jhaw ka daux ceu cao Cin-keu-li.
2 Kheu son Simiang Ye-sux,
pex hawt tix kia ngien nawh mhawm mhawm son bleeh glawm kawn Khrit.
Phan nawh koe ceu pa lo tix ceu ceu,
pex teem coi ka nawh.
Nawh hoik sax gaux rhawm tix teem coi ka pui tom nie,
to kaux aux kawx hoik pon krax pa teem coi nawh heue.
3 aChawk Pai-ci-la mai Ya-cui-la pa yuh kaing tau mai Ye-sux Khrit mhawm ot;
4 kix hoik tawx ju tix ka daux krax pa nu son aux.
Ja lao aux ang yaog yaw kix,
ceu cao pui tang ceu tang cad kawx hoik yaog yaw ka kix khawm uik.
5 Chawk mhawm ot khawm ceu cao pa khrawm tix ot daux nyiex kix kawx heue.
Chawk Yi-pai-ni-thu paox grawm pa muh ex mhawm ot;
nawh mawh pui haktiex Ya-si-yax pa yum rhawm ka Khrit ka daux tix kaux.
6 Krai lox pa nab yam ka son pa yuh pex kaing pa taug mao Ma-li-yax.
7 Chawk An-to-ni-kux mai Yo-ni-yax paox ceu paox cad pui Yo-thaix,
pa hoik sax laung ot daux thawng tau mai aux mhawm ot.
Kix mawh pui pa kied ting min ka daux kawn cau,
mai pa hoik yum rhawm ka Khrit sivoe aux nang heue.
8 Chawk Ang-paw-lix paox grawm pa muh ka daux Khrit mhawm ot;
9 chawk nang ka Eu-pa-nux pa yuh kaing tau mai aux,
mai See-ta-kux paox grawm pa muh aux,
pa yuh kaing jhaw Khrit mhawm ot.
10 Chawk Ya-pi-lix pa hoik cim krax kha rhawm,
pa hoik kaux sijax ka Khrit tom si dauh mhawm ot;
chawk nang ka pui daux nyiex ma Ya-li-to-pux mhawm ot.
11 Chawk ka Si-law-thiien paox ceu paox cad pui Yo-thaix mhawm ot;
chawk nang ka pux aik pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux,
pa mawh pui ka daux nyiex ma Na-chi-sux mhawm ot heue.
12 Chawk Thu-fe-nax mai Thu-fu-sha pa mawh paox grawm pa yuh kaing tau ka daux Simiang Ye-sux mhawm ot;
chawk nang ka Pi-si paox grawm pa muh,
pa rien yuh kaing son Simiang Ye-sux mhawm ot.
13 bChawk Lu-fux mhawm ot;
nawh hoik mawh pui pa yuh kaing mhawm ka daux Simiang Ye-sux tix kaux;
chawk nang ka miex keeing nawh,
pa mawh pui pa hoik kia ngien aux awm kawn simex kawn bun tix yuh nan mhawm ot.
14 Chawk Ya-shuing-chi-thu,
Fu-leu-kang,
He-mi,
Pa-law-pa,
He-ma,
mai pux aik pux o pa ot tau mai nawh khankix mhawm ot.
15 Chawk Fe-law-law-kux mai Yo-li-yax,
Ni-li-yax mai pux o nawh,
A-ling-pa khawm kawn Khrit pa ot tau mai kix mhawm ot ku kaux heue.
16 Pex tag caw tix ra lai ngheet paox tix ka daux krax pa singax singeh heue.
Ceu cao cie Khrit ku dauh hoik chawk pex mhawm ot khawm uik heue.
Pa nghoik tix sidiex gau
17 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux sidiex gau ka pex,
tag caw rien tix ka krax pa sidiex gau pui pa kieh guah paox tix,
pa yung yang yuh krax yum rhawm pui,
pa tik vud ka pui pa hoik rieb ka krax pa sidiex pex;
tag caw tix singai hu khaing kix.
18 Mawh kheu pui pa yuh nin ang lai ot ka daux pa yuh kaing pa krub ka Simiang Ye-sux Khrit,
hoik ot ka daux pa kieh tix sak vaig naog tu.
Kix teei lox mhawm jhawm jiet nyaw glaw ka rhawm pui pa cu lox.
19 Pui hun hoik tawng grawng pa yum rhawm pex ka gum lox buan,
mawh kheu nan,
aux kied gaux rhawm son pex.
Aux tag sang nhie pex kieh tix lhak lhiao ka kaing ceu pa mhawm,
mai kieh tix daox aw ka kaing ceu pa caox.
20 Siyiex tawx pa nhim tag sang yuh laux leeing ka Sa tan,
mai kieh nawh kreet tix ot greem caong pex heue.
Kieh Simiang ex Ye-sux tawx buan son ka pex heue!
21 cThi-maw-thaix paox pa yuh kaing tau mai aux,
khawm Lu-chiox,
Yie-soeng,
Saw-si-pa-teu pa mawh paox ceu paox cad aux,
hoik chawk pex mhawm ot ku kaux.
22 Aux pa mawh Teu-thi tiiem lai in,
mai chawk pex mhawm ot ka daux Simiang Ye-sux.
23 eKai-yo pa rieb ka aux,
mai pa baox kieh ceu cao khrawm tix ot ka daux nyiex ma tix an kawx,
chawk pex mhawm ot.
Yi-la-to pui cao priex mau ka daux veng mai Khaw-thu pux aik ex kawx chawk pex mhawm ot heue.d
Lox rawg buan pa nghoik mai krax yaog yaw
25 Kieh min plak ngai mawh cie Siyiex heue!
Nawh hoik pon hawt tix kieh gum lox buan pa sidiex aux kaux cong yuh krax yum rhawm pex.
Gum lox buan in hoik mawh gum lox pa dex dawt tau mai Ye-sux Khrit,
hoik plak jhi ka kaing ceu pa raux pa mox pa hoik glip tom dix dix.
26 Kaing pa raux pa mox in hoik jhi lih sidawng ka daux phuk lai cao jhai khankix,
khaix an hawt awm gum lox pa tawx Siyiex pa im leeing ju,
tawx ka meung piang haktiex,
son sang kieh kawn pui piang haktiex yum rhawm ka heue.
27 Kieh min plak ngai ka daux Ye-sux Khrit mawh cie Siyiex pa grum tawng tix kaux khaing,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue!
