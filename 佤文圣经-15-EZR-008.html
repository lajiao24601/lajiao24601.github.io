Pui pa deeh hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix
8
Plak greem mawh ceu kaux simiang cub ceu pui an kix pa kaoh tau mai aux,
pa mawh pa hoek tau mai Yi-see-la khaing Pa-pi-loeng,
hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung yam mawh Ya-ta-shiie-si Vawng;
ru ceu cad cub nyiex kix tiiem ot plak greem:
2-14 Keu-soeng,
pa mawh ceu cad cub nyiex Fe-ni-ha.
Tang-yi-li,
pa mawh ceu cad cub nyiex Yi-tha-ma.
Ha-thux,
pa mawh kawn simex See-cia-ni,
ceu cad cub nyiex Ta-wue.
Sa-cia-li-yax,
pa mawh ceu cad cub nyiex Pa-lux,
khawm simex ceu cad cub nyiex in tix yieh tix phuan kaux (ru ceu cad cub nyiex ma kix).
Yi-li-yaw-nai pa mawh kawn simex Si-la-si-ya,
ceu cad cub nyiex Pa-ha Maw-ya,
khawm simex ra yieh kaux.
See-cia-ni,
pa mawh kawn simex Ya-ha-si,
ceu cad cub nyiex Sa-thux,a
khawm simex loe yieh kaux.
Yi-piie,
pa mawh kawn simex Yaw-na-tang,
ceu cad cub nyiex Ya-ting,
khawm simex tix phuan kaux.
Yie-sai-yax,
pa mawh kawn simex Ya-tha-li-ya,
ceu cad cub nyiex Yi-lang,
khawm simex atigliah kaux.
Si-pa-ti-ya,
pa mawh kawn simex Mi-cia-leux,
ceu cad cub nyiex See-fa-thi-ya,
khawm simex tix daix kaux.
Eu-pa-ti-yax,
pa mawh kawn simex Yie-shiie,
ceu cad Yaw-ya,
khawm simex ra yieh kao daix kaux.
See-law-mix,
pa mawh kawn simex Yaw-si-fe,
ceu cad cub nyiex Pa-ni,b
khawm simex tix yieh tix gliah kaux.
Sa-cia-li-yax,
pa mawh kawn simex Pi-paix,
ceu cad cub nyiex Pi-paix,
khawm simex tix nga daix kaux.
Yaw-ha-nang,
pa mawh kawn simex Ha-cia-thang,
ceu cad cub nyiex Ya-cia,
khawm simex tix yieh kao kaux.
Yi-li-fa-liex,
Yie-lix,
mai See-ma-ya,
pa mawh ceu cad cub nyiex Ya-to-ni-kang,
khawm simex tix gliah kaux (kix lhax deeh hoek khaix paox tix).
Wu-thaix mai Sha-pux,
pa mawh ceu cad cub nyiex Pi-keu-wa-yi,
khawm simex atigliah kaux heue.
Yi-see-la sawg pui Li-we son nyiex buan
15 Aux khrawm pui haoh pui hun ot ka praok rawm klawng hu ka veng Ya-ha-wa;
yix sum nyiex bang ot dee an loe singaix.
Aux yaox cao giex kaing li koe ka daux pui haoh pui hun,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox pui Li-we.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
aux kok simiang hoek dim kaux:
pa mawh Yi-li-yi-shiex,
Ya-liex,
See-ma-ya,
Yi-li-na-tang,
Ya-lix,
Yi-li-na-tang,
Na-tang,
Sa-cia-li-yax,
Mi-su-lang,
khawm sara ra kaux pa mawh Yaw-ya-lix mai Yi-li-na-tang.
17 Aux ploi kix hu ka Yi-to simiang haktiex Cia-si-fe-ya,
mhaing nawh mai paox pa yuh kaing tau mai nawh,
pa mawh pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan,
tawx pui khankix ka aux,
son sang yuh kaing krub Siyiex ka nyiex buan.
18 Mawh kheu krax muh krax caox rhawm pa teem coi Siyiex,
kix tom tawx See-li-pi pui pa koe rhawm keud pa chix yuh kaing tix kaux hoek ka ex.
Nawh mawh pui Li-we ceu cad cub nyiex Maw-lix;
kawn simex nawh mai pux aik nawh pa hoek tau mai nawh,
khawm uik koe kao daix kaux.
19 Kix kawx tawx ceu cad cub nyiex Mi-la-lix pa kaux Ha-sa-pi-ya mai Yie-sai-yax,
khawm pui nyiex pui ma nawh tix nga kaux hoek.
20 Lheu khaing an,
baox koe pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan ra yieh tix nga kaux;
tax du pui in kix mawh pa pon lox khaing simiang Ta-wue mai kawn cau pa ting nawh khankix,
pa hu teem coi ka pui Li-we.
Ca rang kix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux lai heue.
Yi-see-la thung pui hun rawg buan tuah priex preem
21 Ka praok rawm klawng Ya-ha-wa,
aux tawx lox kieh pui hun tuah priex preem,
mai viex rhawm awn rhawm tiiem ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex ex,
khiox khaw nawh duk thung ex ka daux krax,
kia ngien ex mai kawn simex kawn bun ex,
khawm sin ngao pa koe ex khawm uik.
22 Aux keud,
caw tix khiox khaw Vawng kieh pui naing brug brung kia ngien ka ex tix cub,
son ang ex yaox krax pa tuk tiak tuk te,
in mawh kaing pa kaig pa tua tix grawng;
mawh kheu aux hoik krai ka Vawng ah nin,
Siyiex ex tawx buan ka pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh ku kaux,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa tik vud ka nawh,
nawh tag song rhawm ciyang kah.
23 Mawh kheu nan,
ex tuah priex preem,
khiox khaw Siyiex kia ngien ka ex;
mai nawh hoik nghiet ka lox rawg ex buan heue.
Khawng phak pa tan lu son nyiex buan
24 Ka daux simiang cao giex kaing li,
aux laug ka See-li-pi,
Ha-sa-pi-ya,
mai pui tang kaux kao kaux.
25 Khaix an,
aux ciang mau khri mai kraung khrai pa hoik tan lu Vawng mai pui cau pa ot greem nawh,
simiang,
khawm pui Yi-seu-liex son nyiex buan,
tawx ka cao giex kaing li.
26-27 Ceu kraung in kix plak greem mawh pa hoik tawx aux ka kix:
Mau luah tix nga ra kongtoeng.
Kraung pa yuh ka mau atigliah kongcing.
Khri loe rheng pon yieh kongcing.
Klong khri tix nga plah,
daix kongcing mai daix rawng.
Klong pa yuh ka tawng pa mhawm ra plah,
ngawh awm klong khri heue.
28 Aux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex mawh pa kied singax singeh ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex;
mai khri,
mau khawm can pa hoik tang tan lu pex kawx mawh pa singax singeh.
29 Pex caw tix bre rhawm tix pau mhawm mhawm,
tom hoek ka daux nyiex buan,
hoek ka dee an,
mai ciang tix yaox ka daux nyiex cao giex kaing li,
tawx ka cao giex kaing li mai ka simiang pui Li-we,
khawm simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.”
30 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we mawh cao pa koe beeh glawm tix kuang ka mau khri,
mai kraung khrai in kix,
mai viex laig ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Deeh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
31 Haok khix tix kao ra ngaix,
ex lih hu khaing rawm klawng Ya-ha-wa,
mai hu ka plak Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Siyiex ex ot tau mai ex,
mai kia ngien ka daux krax hu ex,
ang kieh pui koeng mai pui kuan mox tix puing ka ex.
32 Yam hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ex laux lee tix loe ngaix.
33 Ka daux pon singaix,
ex tom hoek ka nyiex buan,
ciang ka khri mau,
mai kraung khrai,
tawx ka Mi-li-mawx cao giex kaing li kawn simex Wu-li-yax.
Pui pa hu tau mai nawh kawn koe Yi-li-ya-sa pa mawh kawn simex Fe-ni-ha,
pui Li-we ra kaux pa mawh Yaw-sa-pa pa mawh kawn simex Yie-su-yax mai Naw-ya-ti pa mawh kawn simex Ping-noex.
34 Kraung ku ceu hoik simin tix yaox kah,
ciang tix yaox kah,
khaix an mai baox bre tix tiiem ot daux lai heue.
35 Can pui pa kaoh khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix,
hoik viex khawng tan khawm uik hoek,
son sang kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan lu ka Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kix tan lu moi mhaing kao ra mu,
yung mhaing tix dim liah mu,
kawn yung iak atigliah aliah mu son pui hun Yi-seu-liex,
mai baox tan lu pex mhaing kao ra mu son sang si ghrah phi map tix.
Khawng tan in kix hoik kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa gleeing tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
36 Kix baox tawx lai pa hoik tawx Vawng ka kix ka simiang haktiex Heu-si,
mai simiang tang kaux khankix;
kix tom teem coi pui hun mai kaing pa krub ka daux nyiex buan heue.
