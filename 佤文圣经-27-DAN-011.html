11
“Nawha klawm beeh glawm teem coi ka aux,
mai kia ngien ka kaing pa yuh aux.
2 Yam in lox pa sang krai aux ka maix mawh lox pa mawh pa meh heue.”
Ai-cix mai Shui-li-yax
Kawn cau Siyiex an ah nin: “Kawn koe simiang loe kaux tag sang giex kaing ka piang Paw-see, khaix an tom lih koe ka daux pon kaux simiang. Simiang in ka daux pon kaux kawn lheu mag mi khaing simiang tang kaux. Yam kied koe siawp sibrawm nawh, mai riang rao nawh, nawh sang yuh naing ka meung Si-lax heue.
3 “Khaix an koe simiang pa kua tix kaux lih hoek;
nawh pon giex kaing ka piang meung pa kied ting awm pa simie nawh tix yuh.
4 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoik naog siawp sibrawm nawh,
meung nawh tag laux,
mai guah tix lih koe pon mu.
Doeh ceu cad nawh ang lai pon plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh,
simiang meung mawh nawh tag sang mawh pui tang kaux pa hoek tix giex kaing piang,
viang mawh nan,
ang lai ting lhaong awm bawg yam pa giex nawh kaing yuh nan heue.
5 “Simiang Ai-cix tag sang ting lhaong,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux bawg yam pa giex nawh kaing,
koe pa ting lhaong khaing nawh tag sang lih hoek,
nawh tag sang giex kaing ka piang meung pa blawn ting.
6 Khaix hoik tom nie num,
simiang Ai-cix tag sang yuh paox pie tau mai simiang Shui-li-yax,
mai tawx kawn bun tix moeng ka nawh.
Viang mawh nan,
krax pa yuh kix paox pie tau ang pon koe miex glang tix.
Bun an mai mex nawh,
kawn nawh,b
khawm siliex pa ot tau mai nawh pui tag sang pauh kix yum.
7 Ang koe miex glang tix,
ka daux cub pui nyiex ma nawh tag sang lih mawh simiang tix kaux,
mai yuh naing ka pui naing Shui-li-yax,
pon laig ka dee koe tab kix,
mai pon pex ka kix.
8 Nawh tag sang viex riang siyiex pa krub kix,
mai kraung khrai pa ot daux nyiex siyiex kix ing ka Ai-cix.
Khaix hoik yaox kix ka daux krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ra loe num,
9 simiang Shui-li-yax tag sang laig puing ka Ai-cix,
viang mawh nan,
dee si dauh pui tag sang khin kix deeh heue.
10 “Kawn simiang Shui-li-yax khankix tag sang rien khre tix puing naing,
mai khrawm pui naing pa haoh hun hoek.
Ka daux kix koe kawn simiang tix kaux tag sang awm rawm ting pa klauh ka tab yuh nan.
11 Simiang Ai-cix tag sang thung pui naing tix yuh naing ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
mai puing ka simiang Shui-li-yax,
pon giex ka pui naing pa hun cie nawh.
12 Simiang Ai-cix tag sang ting rhawm lhaong rhi kheu hoik pon pex kix ka pui naing pa koeng,
viang mawh nan,
krax pa pon pex kix ang pon koe miex glang tix heue.
13 “Simiang Shui-li-yax tag sang deeh ing ka meung tix,
mai khrawm ka pui naing pa blawn hun blawn ting riang sivoe an;
yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
nawh tag sang thung pui naing hun pa hoik grum rien khre tix mhawm deeh hoek.
14 Yam an,
tag sang lih koe pui tom nie pa tieh vheen ka simiang Ai-cix.
Tang-yi-li eue,
ka daux paox ceu paox cad maix koe pui tud rhim tix blah tag sang tieh vheen ka maix nang,
son sang kieh riang su sai lih tix mawh;
viang mawh nan,
kix tag ang pon pex.
15 Simiang Shui-li-yax tag sang pon teei ka veng pa yuh kix kaux cong;
pui naing Ai-cix pa blawn chix puing ka naing khankix kawx ang pon tieb tie ka kix.
16 Pui tud pui rhim pa hoek khaing Shui-li-yax tag sang nieb pon yuh kaing pa ang khaw ang jiet,
ang koe pui pa pon tieb tie ka nawh.
Nawh tag sang pon cax ka haktiex pa mhawm,
mai pon giex gang ka haktiex an khawm uik heue.
17 “Simiang Shui-li-yax keud tix sang thung pui naing tix hu puing ka Ai-cix.
Son sang laux yuh ka Ai-cix mai pui pa yuh paox tau mai Ai-cix,
mai tawx kawn bunc tix mex haktiex an;
viang mawh nan,
krax keud nawh ang koe dee mhawm son nawh,
ang chix lih tix mawh.
18 Khaix an,
nawh baox sang puing ka meung vung viang rawm sigrit sigra,
mai pon kuang ka meung tom nie.
Viang mawh nan,
tag koe cao kaing tix kaux tag sang pex ka nawh,
tieb tie ka krax kin pa yuh nawh,
kieh krax kin pa yuh nawh tang laux yuh to kaux nawh.
19 Nawh tag sang deeh haktiex ot tix,
mai laig ka tab to kaux tix,
dee si dauh pui tom pex ka nawh;
in tom mawh krax si dauh nawh heue.
20 “Koe pui tix kaux tag sang plud ka simiang pa mawh nawh,
nawh tag sang gut kheem ka kawn pui,
nieb pon yuh tix ka kaing pa ang khaw,
son sang kieh sin ngao meung tix haok huan.
Viang mawh nan,
ang koe miex glang tix pui pauh nawh yum,
mai ang yum ka daux naing heue.”
Simiang Shui-li-yax pa caox pa caig
21 Kawn cau Siyiex an ca nied tix kah dee bleeh ah nin:
“Khaix an,
koe pui pa caox pa caig tix kaux tag sang tud hu ka simiang pa mawh Shui-li-yax.
Nawh ang koe akhuang tix plud,
viang mawh nan,
jawm yam ang pui bax rien tix,
siao krax pie krax lhien teei hu ka siawp sibrawm simiang.
22 Nawh tag sang yuh laux lak pak leeing ka can riang rao pa tieb tie ka nawh khawm uik,
khawm pa mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting pui cie Siyiex.
23 Nawh siao gum lox sijax mukheu pa caox pie lhien ka meung tang meung;
pa giex nawh kaing piang,
viang hoik mawh ngie meung pa iak tix mu khaing,
viang mawh nan,
nied ot nied ting laong.
24 Nawh tag sang jawm yam ang pui bax rien tix,
puing ka haktiex pa blawn mag mi khankix,
mai yuh ka kaing pa ang tax du nawh sax yuh.
Nawh guah tix tawx kraung pa pon daux naing ka pui pa ot greem tix.
Nawh tag sang keud sawn grawng sang puing ka tab,
viang mawh nan,
singaix cie nawh ang rheeng heue.
25 “Nawh tag sang sau riang tix thung pui naing puing ka simiang Ai-cix;
simiang Ai-cix kawx tag sang sau riang tix siao pui naing pa ting riang rieb puing ka nawh,
viang mawh nan ang pon pex,
mawh kheu hoik yuh pui pa tieh vheen nawh laux leeing.
26 Pa mawh pui pa hoik deuh nawh rhawm tix kah,
tag sang yuh nawh laux leeing;
pui naing cie nawh tag sang som nai ang lai pon lih mawh pui naing,
pui tom nie hoik yum daux bang naing;
27 khaix an simiang in kiex tag sang sawm rheep tau,
viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi kiex hoik ra lai koeng ka paox tix,
mai ra lai pie lhien ka paox tix.
Kiex ang pon yuh krax pa khaw rhawm paox tix,
mawh kheu ang bawg yam pa ciak tix deuh bax hoek.
28 Simiang Shui-li-yax tang naog ka kraung khrai pa pon tix khaing daux naing deeh ing ka meung ot tix,
mai hoik but rhawm tix sang yuh been li pui cie Siyiex laux leeing.
Nawh tag sang nieb pon yuh tix,
khaix an deeh ing haktiex ot tix heue.
29 “Yam hoik koek bawg yam,
nawh tag sang baox puing ka Ai-cix,
viang mawh nan,
bawg in ang lai awm bawg sivoe.
30 Reu cie pui Ci-thix tag sang hoek tix puing ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh pru praoh yaoh yee heue.
“Simiang Shui-li-yax tag sang song rhawm yam dak tix deeh,
mai kieh pa song rhawm tix mawh pa yuh krax yum rhawm pui cie Siyiex laux leeing.
Nawh tag sang nghiet ka lox pui pa tieh vheen ka krax yum rhawm an kix tix blah.
31 dPui naing pa ploi nawh tag sang nu nai ka nyiex buan,
tag sang naox ka krax tan pa caw nawh tix sang yuh ku ngaix,
khaix an mai teei tix deuh ‘kraung khrai pa yum koeng pa kieh pui laux leeing’ ot ka daux nyiex buan.
32 Nawh siao krax keud pa pie lhien kieh pui pie lhien joh jhan tix;
viang mawh nan,
pui pa rauh pa see pa hawt khaix Siyiex,
tag caw kieh tix koe riang koe rao,
mai kaoh tieb tie.
33 Simiang pa lhak lhiao ka daux si nax kawn pui tag sang gau kawn pui,
mai kieh kix tawng;
viang mawh nan,
kawn koe tix baux bawg yam,
koe pui tix blah tag sang yum ka naing,
yum ka pa gleeing ngu,
pui tud kix hu,
mai giex laung ka kix.
34 Yam yuh krax pa pauh pui tom nie,
pui cie Siyiex tag sang pon krax pa teem coi ceeix ceeix,
viang pui tix blah hoik plaik tix ot ka daux kix,
mai hoik mawh keud son alo to kaux tix.
35 Koe simiang pa lhak lhiao tix blah,
pui tag sang pauh ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
grawng pa yum kix tag sang si ghrah ka kawn pui,
pheuk ka kawn pui,
mai kieh kix singax,
tom hoik hoek si dauh bawg yam pa tat Siyiex heue.
36 e“Simiang Shui-li-yax tag sang nieb pon yuh tix,
mai kawn lheu ting rhawm lhaong rhi khaing siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
kawn lheu lhaong khaing siyiex pa koe khawm uik pa mawh Siyiex.
Nawh ca nied tix cau nyhau,
tom hoik hoek singaix pa ciyang Siyiex ka nawh.
Siyiex tag sang yuh kaing pa hoik keud tix lih mawh tete,
mai ang doh chix tieh lox tix.
37 Simiang in ang yung yi ka siyiex pa hoik krub tax du tix,
mai ang nab yam nang ka siyiex pa muh can bun miex khankix.
Tete te lai,
nawh ang yung yi ka siyiex tix kaux kawx,
mawh kheu hoik een nawh tix ting lhaong khaing siyiex.
38 Nawh hoik tieh tix hu krub ka siyiex pa pau tab pa ang tax du nawh bax krub kah.
Nawh hoik tan khri,
mau,
siang simaox,
mai khawng phak tang ceu pa ngawh khankix.
39 Nawh teei pui naing pa laig been li siyiex pau ka tab nawh.
Pui pa rieb nawh giex kaing piang tix,
nawh tawx kix mawh simiang ting,
mai guah kix cie haktiex,
mai kieh mawh ngawh riang kix heue.
40 “Bawg yam pa si dauh krax lawng simiang Shui-li-yax sang dex hoek,
simiang Ai-cix tag sang ploi pui naing tix hu puing ka nawh.
Simiang Shui-li-yax tag sang kieh can pui naing tix khawm uik,
teei ling law,
brung naing,
mai pui naing reu hoek tix puing.
Nawh tag sang puing ka meung tom nie,
awm rawm ting pa kheem ka haktiex yuh nan,
41 viang mawh nan,
tag sang laig ka daux haktiex pa mhawm,
mai pauh ka pui kaing rheng kaing mheen;
viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-tong,
Maw-ya,
mai pui Ya-meung pa deuk khankix yaox tix luat luan.
42 Yam puing nawh ka meung an kix,
pie Ai-cix kawx ang yaox tix luat luan.
43 Nawh tag sang tud ka mau khri,
sin ngao mai kraung ngawh pa koe daux Ai-cix.
Nawh tag sang pon kuang ka Li-pi-yax,
mai Ku-seu.
44 Khaix an lox pa lih hoek khaing plak lih singaix mai ka plak laong kieh nawh yaoh yee;
nawh sang sau riang tix yuh naing ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
mai pauh pui yum tom nie.
45 Nawh tag sang sum nyiex gox bang nyiex ma simiang ot ka si nax rawm sigrit sigra mai gawng pa mhawm grang.
Viang mawh nan,
bawg yam singaix yum nawh hoik hoek,
mai ang koe pui pa pon taoh ka nawh heue.”
