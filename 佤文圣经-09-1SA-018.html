18
Khaix hoik si kah Sao-law tau mai Ta-wue,
kawn simex Sao-law pa kaux Yaw-na-tang hoik kied khaw rhawm ka Ta-wue tete;
nawh muh ka Ta-wue awm hoik muh ka to kaux tix heue.
2 Jauh singaix yam an,
Sao-law deuh Ta-wue ot ka praok tix,
mai ang kieh nawh ing ka nyiex.
3 Yaw-na-tang mawh kheu kied muh nawh Ta-wue,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan,
nawh tom tiex tu kieh kiex yuh sijax mukheu tau.
4 Nawh peeik ka kraung dha ceep tix,
mai tawx ka Ta-wue,
baox tawx mhok ceep tix,
vaig gawn tix,
ak puh tix,
mai maox rawd nyoi tix ka Ta-wue.
5 Can kaing pa hoik ap Sao-law tix tawx ka Ta-wue khawm uik,
hoik pon yuh tix mhawm khawm uik,
mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law tom tawx nawh mawh simiang naing.
Grawng kaing in hoik kieh kawn pui mai siliex Sao-law gaux rhawm gaux rhi kah heue.
Sao-law boh rhawm ka Ta-wue
6 Khaix hoik pauh Ta-wue ka Keu-li-yax,
pui naing ah lox rhax hoek;
can bun miex pa ot ku veng ku yaong Yi-seu-liex lih hoek tix rieb ka simiang Sao-law.
Kix gaux rhawm ah lox rhax,
graoh,
tauh ka kraung,
tauh ka ting aliah lah.
7 aKix graoh mai rhax nang lai ah nin:
“Sao-law pauh ka pui kaing rheng;
Ta-wue pauh ka pui kaing mheen!”
8 Sao-law ang rhawm mhong ka lox rhax in,
mai song rhawm tix ah nin:
“Kix ah mawh Ta-wue kaing mheen,
mai ah mawh aux kaing rheng khaing,
cud ngie son ang bax yaog nawh mawh simiang khaing heue!”
9 Jauh singaix yam an,
Sao-law hoik boh rhawm ka Ta-wue heue.
10 Ka daux ra singaix,
koe khuan phi caox lih hoek khaing Simiang Siyiex dee an,
giex gang ka Sao-law,
nawh hoik awm pui yhot yuh nan,
nieb pon ah lox pa ang koe ceu daux nyiex.
Ta-wue tauh ting awm sivoe an yuh nan,
Sao-law giex ka pliah,
11 mai tang ah lox tix ah nin:
“Aux sang klauh nawh ka ding”,
tom puah plak ot Ta-wue,
puah ka ra bawg,
viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue yoi tix khaing ku bawg heue.
12 Sao-law lhat ka Ta-wue;
mawh kheu hoik ot Simiang Siyiex tau mai Ta-wue,
hoik tik vud ka Sao-law.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law tawx Ta-wue thung pui naing tix rheng kaux.
Ta-wue thung pui naing tix hu puing ka naing,
14 hoik pex ka pui koeng khawm uik,
mawh kheu ot Simiang Siyiex tau mai nawh.
15 Sao-law yaox grawng pa hoik pex Ta-wue ku ceu,
tom nie lhat ka nawh.
16 Viang mawh nan,
kawn pui Yi-seu-liex mai kawn pui Yo-tax khawm uik hoik kied muh ka Ta-wue,
mawh kheu hoik mawh nawh simiang pa mhawm tix kaux heue.
Ta-wue teei tix moeng kawn bun Sao-law
17 Sao-law ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Aux sang tawx kawn bun tix pa kaux Mi-la mex ka maix,
kaing ting mawh son sang kieh maix mawh pui naing pa kua tix kaux,
cu nghiet ka aux,
yuh naing son Simiang Siyiex.”
(Tete te lai,
Sao-law keud tix sang kieh Ta-wue yum ka lawm vaig pui Fe-li-see,
nawh ang siao lai tawx taix tix heue!)
18 Ta-wue pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mawh mawx?
Pui nyiex aux sawn mawh pa tix lie?
Aux sang keung tix mawh kheue simiang yuh ka mawx lie?”
19 Yam hoek bawg yam pa sang caw Mi-la mai Ta-wue tix sang yuh ka paox,
Sao-law tom tawx kawn bun tix mex ka pui Mi-heu-la pa kaux Ya-teu-liex.
20 Viang maw nan,
Sao-law kawn koe kawn bun tang kaux tix kaux pa kaux Mi-cia,
nawh kied muh ka Ta-wue.
Sao-law hoik tawng grawng in,
tom gaux rhawm kah,
21 nawh tom keud ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
“Aux sang tawx Mi-cia mex ka Ta-wue,
son sang kieh nawh mawh daux jhawk,
Ta-wue tom tag yaox tix yum ka daux tawm taix pui Fe-li-see.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law ah nin ka Ta-wue ka daux ra bawg:
“Maix mawh kheue aux!”
22 Nawh baox tawx lox ka pui pa ot plak goix plak dawm tix ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Simiang kied khaw rhawm ka maix,
siliex nawh kawx kied muh ka maix.
Maix teei tix moeng ka kawn bun nawh,
jiet caw bawg yam heue.”
23 Kawn cau simiang krai lox in ka Ta-wue.
Ta-wue pawk lox ah nin:
“Yaox tix mawh kheue simiang hoik mawh min plak ngai pa ting tete.
Awm aux pa mawh pui hot,
pui iak pui lied yuh nin,
sang chix khaw tix pon ka min pa tang pa siyox in yuh ka mawx lie?”
24 Siliex deeh krai lox Ta-wue ka Sao-law,
25 Sao-law baox tawx lox nhie kix hu krai ka Ta-wue ah nin:
“Ngawh tawng gawng singai pa sang cie simiang,
mawh ngie hak klix pui Fe-li-see tix yieh kaux khaing,
son sang kieh mawh krax pa pawk bix ka pui koeng khankix.”
(In hoik mawh krax pa sang tieh Sao-law,
nawh keud tix sang teei lawm vaig pui Fe-li-see pauh ka Ta-wue yum.)
26 Pa ot plak goix plak dawm Sao-law krai lox pa krai Sao-law ka Ta-wue,
Ta-wue kied gaux rhawm kah,
nawh cu tix mawh kheue simiang.
Sivoe sang hoek singaix poi ka paox,
27 Ta-wue mai pui pa ot greem nawh,
pauh ka pui Fe-li-see yum ra yieh kaux.
Nawh viex ka hak klix kix khawm uik mai hoek tawx ka Sao-law,
son sang yaox tix mawh kheue simiang.
Mawh khex nan,
Sao-law ang tawx kawn bun tix Mi-cia mex ka Ta-wue ang chix heue.
28 Sao-law hoik yaox grawng pa ot Simiang Siyiex tau mai Ta-wue tete,
kawn bun nawh kawx Mi-cia kied muh ka Ta-wue,
29 mai nie lhat ka Ta-wue,
yuh krax pa ang khaw ka nawh tom si dauh ju tix.
30 Pui naing Fe-li-see hoek tix puing ka kix jheeng jheeng,
yam lih Ta-wue tix puing ka naing miex bawg ang ah,
nawh kawn lheu chix thung naing khaing simiang cao bung naing Sao-law tang kaux khankix,
mawh kheu nan,
ceu kaux nawh hoik tauh hu tom dee singai heue.
