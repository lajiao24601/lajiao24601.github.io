Krax krub ka daux ceu cao
2
Ran sidiex gau ka maix sivoe,
tag caw tix khiox khaw ka Siyiex son pui khawm uik,
rawg buan,
khiox khaw son pui,
yaog yaw buan son.
2 Tag caw tix rawg buan son simiang mai ka can pui pa giex kaing khawm uik nang,
kieh ex pon tix ot ka daux krax pa yien yawg,
pa nhim,
pa rauh see,
mai krax pa nab yam yung yi.
3 In mawh ceu pa mhawm,
mawh pa khaw rhawm Siyiex cao taoh.
4 Siyiex simie pui ku kaux pon krax taoh,
mai tawng yung ka pa mawh pa meh khawm uik.
5 Pa mawh pa meh in tom krai ah nin:
koe ngie Siyiex tix kaux khaing,
ka si nax Siyiex mai kawn pui koe pui si nax tix kaux,
pa mawh Ye-sux Khrit pa lih mawh kawn pui.
6 Nawh tawx to kaux tix yum son kawn pui,
son sang kieh kawn pui pon krax pa bheeik luan,
jhi sidawng ka bawg yam pa khaw,
Siyiex simie kawn pui ku kaux pon krax taoh.
7 aKaing ting mawh son in,
aux pon tix mawh cao sidiex mai kawn cau ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
mawh sara pa hoek tix kieh kix yum rhawm mai tawng yung ka pa mawh pa meh.
Aux ang pie lhien;
ceu pa krai aux hoik mawh tete khawm uik heue!
8 Yam khrawm tix yuh krax krub ka haktiex ku dauh,
aux simie simex rawg buan,
yaog taix tix rawg buan ka daux krax pa yum rhawm,
paw vi song rhawm,
paw vi simah heue.
9 bAux kawx simie bun ceep ka kraung koe pak koe peung;
paw vi jawg tix ka hauk kaing pa tang siyox,
ka mau khri siang pa ngawh,
phan ang mawh nan,
jawg tix ka kraung ceep pa ngawh.
10 Tag caw tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
pa khaw tau mai krax yum rhawm.
11 Sibun tag caw gau tix ka daux pa yien yawg,
tiiem rhawm ka daux kaing ku ceu.
12 Aux ang cu sibun sidiex gau ka pui,
phan ang mawh nan,
kuang ka piang simex;
kix tag caw yien yawg tix.
13 cMawh kheu phan Siyiex ka Ya-tang sivoe,
khaix an phan yuh ka Sha-wa.
14 dPa glup ka daux krax pa nyaw glaw ang mawh Ya-tang,
mai hoik mawh bun;
bun glup ka daux krax pa nyaw glaw,
mai hoik lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex.
15 Viang mawh nan,
bun phan nawh priex to kaux tix mhawm mhawm,
giex kaux cong ka krax yum rhawm,
krax muh krax rhawm,
pa singax singeh,
tag sang pon krax taoh kheu daux pa keh tix kawn heue.
