Kawn simex kawn sibun Siyiex
3
aPex jhak,
keeing Siyiex kied muh ka ex heue!
Hoik kok ex mawh kawn simex kawn bun tix;
tete te lai,
ex hoik mawh kawn simex kawn bun nawh.
Pui piang haktiex ang tawng ka ex,
hoik mawh kheu ang kix tawng ka Siyiex.
2 Pao grawm pa muh khankix eue,
yam in ex hoik mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex,
ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
tag sang tieh tix yuh ka mawx,
ang nyang bax jhi tix lih.
Viang mawh nan,
ex hoik tawng yam lih hoek Khrit,
ex tag sang awm nawh yuh nan,
mawh kheu ex tag sang yaox ka to kaux nawh tete.
3 Pui pa simie Khrit lih hoek ku kaux tag sang kieh to kaux tix singax jheeng jheeng,
awm hoik singax Khrit yuh nan heue.
4 Pui pa yuh phi map,
tag mawh pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex,
kheu hoik mawh phi map pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
5 bPex hoik tawng,
kaing ting Khrit lih hoek mawh son sang teei tix tik ka phi map;
to kaux nawh ang koe phi map.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa im ka daux ju Khrit tag ang yuh ka phi map;
pui pa ca nied tix yuh ka phi map ang sax yaox ka Khrit,
mai ang sax tawng yung ka nawh nang heue.
7 Kawn nyawm khankix eue,
paw vi kieh pui pie lhien ka pex.
Pa priex tix hawt ka pa rauh pa see,
tag sawn tix mawh pa rauh see,
awm mawh Khrit pa rauh pa see yuh nan.
8 Pui pa ca nied tix yuh phi map,
mawh pa dex dawt mai phi caox,
kheu hoik mawh phi caox pa yuh phi map tom yam jauh.
Son grawh grawng in,
kawn simex Siyiex hoik jhi tix lih,
kaing ting mawh son sang yuh laux leeing ka kaing cie phi caox heue.
9 Kawn simex kawn sibun Siyiex tag ang lai nied tix yuh phi map,
mawh kheu ot ju Siyiex ka daux kix.
Kheu Siyiex hoik mawh keeing kix,
kix tom ang lai chix baox yuh phi map.
10 Kawn simex kawn sibun Siyiex mai kawn simex kawn bun phi caox hoik tang siyox sidawng yuh nin:
pui pa ang priex tix hawt ka pa rauh pa see,
pa ang muh ka pux aik pux o tix,
tag ang mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex heue.
Ra lai muh ka paox tix
11 cGum lox pa hoik mhong pex tom yam jauh tag mawh nin:
ex tag caw tix ra lai muh ka paox tix.
12 dEx paw vi awm Kai-yin yuh nan.
Nawh hoik mawh kaw pa caox pa caig;
nawh hoik pauh pux tix yum.
Yuh ka mawx nawh lai pauh pux tix yum lie?
Mawh kheu kaing pa yuh nawh mawh pa caox pa caig,
mai kaing pa yuh pux nawh mawh pa rauh pa see heue.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
phan pui haktiex in koeng ka pex,
pex paw vi kieh tix daox deng kah.
14 eEx hoik tawng tix lih khaing krax yum mai laig daux krax pa im;
ex hoik tawng ex yuh nin,
mai hoik mawh kheu muh ex pux aik pux o tix.
Pui pa ang koe krax muh khankix,
mai ca kawn ot ka greem siawp sibrawm krax yum.
15 Can pui pa koeng ka pux aik pux o tix khankix,
tag mawh pa pauh ka pui;
pex hoik tawng can pui pa pauh ka pui,
tag ang koe ju leeing ju.
16 Khrit hoik tawx ju tix yum son ex,
kheu grawng in,
ex hoik tawng pa muh mawh ceu pa tix.
Kheu nan ex kawx tag caw tix tawx ju tix yum son pux aik pux o tix heue!
17 Pui pa mi pa koe tix kaux yaox krax pa cud cha pux aik pux o tix,
mai kiien rhawm ang simi,
sang chix krai ex muh nawh Siyiex yuh ka mawx lie?
18 Kawn nyawm khankix eue,
krax muh ex ang caw tix mawh ka siyieb jheeh khaing,
tag caw tix kieh mawh tete,
yuh pui yaox mu lih heue!
Ang koe rhawm ring ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex
19 Kheu nan,
ex hoik tawng khaing ceu in,
ex hoik mawh pa dex dawt tau mai pa mawh pa meh;
ex hoik pon tix mhawm ot ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
20 Viang rhawm rhi dee chix keud chix tawng ex kawx ne raw ka ex.
Ex ca kawn tawng grawng pa kawn lheu ting Siyiex khaing rhawm ex;
nawh hoik chix tawng ka daux rhawm ex sidawng khawm uik.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
phan rhawm rhi dee chix keud chix tawng ex ang ne raw ka ex,
ex tag chix tix ang koe rhawm ring ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
22 Phan ex sang khiox khaw ceu pa tix ang ah ka nawh,
nawh tag sang tawx ka ex;
mawh kheu hoik priex ex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh,
mai hoik yuh ka kaing ceu pa khaw rhawm nawh.
23 fGum lox tawx nawh mawh:
ex tag caw tix yum rhawm ka ceu kaux kawn simex nawh Ye-sux Khrit,
khaix an hawt tix yuh ka gum lox tawx Khrit,
mai ra lai muh paox tix.
24 Pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Siyiex,
tag koe ka ju Siyiex,
mai Siyiex kawx ot ka daux ju nawh.
Sang tawng ex grawng pa ot Siyiex ka daux ju ex yuh ka mawx lie?
Mawh Khuan Singax Singeh pa hoik tawx nawh ka ex pa kieh ex tawng heue.
