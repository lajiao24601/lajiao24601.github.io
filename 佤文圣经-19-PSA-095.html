Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
95
Hoek heue,
hoek ex rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Hoek tix gaux rhawm ka cao pa kia ngien pa taoh ka ex heue!
2 Ex tag caw tix viex krax pa yaog yaw hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh;
ex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
3 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong,
kawn lheu ting khaing simiang ting pa mawh Siyiex pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
4 Nawh kuang ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
jauh ka dee raux haktiex tom hoek ka dee lhaong gawng.
5 Nawh kuang ka rawm sigrit sigra pa phan pa yuh tix;
nawh kuang ka haktiex pa hoik phan yuh nawh ka taix tix heue.
 
6 Hoek heue,
hoek ex grux gawng tix krub ka nawh heue!
Hoek tix grux gawng krub ka Simiang Siyiex cao pa phan pa yuh ex heue!
7 abNawh hoik mawh Siyiex ex,
ex hoik mawh pui cie nawh pa eei pa liang nawh,
hoik mawh phung yung pa duk pa thung nawh heue.
 
Yam in pex nghiet ka lox pa krai nawh ah nin heue:
8 cpaw vi kiien rhawm,
awm tax du pex yam ot ka Mi-li-pa;
paw vi awm kix yam ot ka daux tiag daux tu Ma-sha yuh nan,
9 Viang kix hoik yaox ka kaing pa yuh aux son kix,
kix ca kawn cheuk cheu ka aux,
nyaw glaw ka aux dee an.
10 Ka daux tipon num,
aux hoik seeing brai ka pui in kix.
Aux ah nin:
kix kied ang nghiet lox heue!
Kix ang nghiet ka lox tawx aux.
11 dMawh kheu nan,
aux tom tiex tu tix ah nin ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix:
kix ang doh pon tix laig ka daux pa mawh
haktiex pa sang tawx aux kix nyawm ot nyawm koe ka heue.
