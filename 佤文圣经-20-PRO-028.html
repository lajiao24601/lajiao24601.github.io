28
Pui caox pui caig pie ang pui bax leue kawx hoik yaog tix to.Pui rauh pui see hoik kua awm sivai sang si yuh nan.
2 Phan meung koe phi map,
kaing pa giex ca loh tix ang jud;phan meung koe simiang mhawm,
tag kieh pui koe krax yien yawg tom rheeng.
3 Simiang pa ang mhawm gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
awm bhaung ting lhiex ting pa chun ka nghox koi laux.
4 Pui pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng yaog yaw ka pa caox pa caig.Pui pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng tieb tie ka pa caox pa caig.
5 Pui caox pui caig ang tawng pa rauh pa see mawh ceu pa tix;pui pa sawg ka Simiang Siyiex tawng tix yuh ka pa rauh pa see.
6 Pa hot pa rauh see,kawn lheu khaing pa mi ka daux pa pie lhien.
7 Pui pa nhum ju pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng hoik mawh krax lhak pa lhiao;pa yuh paox grawm tau mai pui pa ih nieh yuh,
mawh pa kieh keeing tix hoek krax kaig krax tua heue.
8 Pa mi ka daux krax pa teei mau lhaong rao gut kheem pui kah,
sin ngao nawh dee si dauh tag sang hoek ka daux tawm taix pui pa koe krax pa caox rhawm ka pui hot pui sibhawm.
9 Phan maix ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
Siyiex tag sang vud ngai tix khaing lox rawg maix buan.
10 Pa nyaw glaw pui rauh pui see yuh phi map,
tag sang cawd ka daux jhawk pa taong tix;pui pa singax singeh tag sang pon khawng phak lak sawng pa mhawm.
11 Pui pa koe mau hoik ca een to kaux tix mawh pui pa lhak lhiao;pui hot pui sibhawm pa koe krax doh rhawm doh rhi khankix hoik tawng yung ka dee kaoh nawh.
12 Pui rauh pui see giex kaing,
mai pui ku kaux tag gaux rhawm gaux rhi kah;Pui pa ang mhawm giex kaing,
mai pui ku kaux to mox tix khaing.
13 Pui pa tawb kheem ka phi map to kaux tix,
mai ang pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe kawn pui;pui pa krai doh ka krax lud krax loe tix mai deeh loh rhawm rhi tix,
Siyiex tag sang tawx krax pa caox rhawm tix ka nawh.
14 Lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
hoik pon krax nyawm ot jheeng jheeng;kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
tang kieh to kaux tix laux leeing.
15 Simiang pa caox giex gang ka piang pui hot pui sibhawm,
hoik awm graox sivai sang si mai krih pa lai ling tix sawg ka ceu pa ih pa un heue.
16 Simiang ang sidawng ka grawng kaing,
nawh tag lih tix mawh simiang pa ang mhawm;pa koeng ka pa caox pa caig,
tag sang pon kuang ka tom rheeng.
17 Pa phit lud tix pauh pui yum,
hoik mawh pa tang kaong daux si meeig son to kaux tix,
ang caw tix tieb tie ka nawh heue.
18 Pui pa yuh kaing pa rauh pa see,
tag pon krax pa nyawm ot;pui pa yuh kaing pa pie lhien,
tag sang sideeing tix goh.
19 Pui pa yuh kaing haktiex pa rien rhawm,
tag pon ka priex preem tom kub;pui pa kieh bawg yam nai,
tag hoek krax hot krax sibhawm.
20 Pui pa mawh pa meh hoik naog ka krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe;pui pa keud tix sang mi ka daux krax pa ang rauh ang see,
ang chix luan khaing krax ciyang heue.
21 Pa koe rhawm pa ang khaw,
viang mawh nan,
koe cao prawng see tix blah,
kheu khao mun tix lon mai prawng ka daux pa ang khaw.
22 Pui pa koe rhawm ghoing ki simie tix pon sin ngao pa yie,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng krax pa hot pa sibhawm sang lih hoek piang tix heue.
23 Pui pa chix sidiex gau ka pui tang kaux,
mai pui kawn chix lheu muh nawh khaing pa chix krai lox nyaw lox glaw.
24 Pa briex ka kraung khrai miex keeing tix hu mai ah mawh kaing pa khaw,
mai ang lai tang siyox khaing pui briex yuh nan heue.
25 Pa tang keud son to kaux kieh krax simah simaing lih.Yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex tag pon buan son ang jud ang jai;
26 pui pa daox aw mai nieb teei alo tix yuh;pa nghiet ka lox pui pa koe krax doh krax riang,
tag sang pon krax nyawm ot.
27 Pa chix caox rhawm tix teem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
tag ang yaox krax cud krax cha.Pa yaox pui hot pui sibhawm mai ang taoh teem,
tag sang pon krax jhax lawg.
28 Pui caox pui caig giex kaing,
kawn pui to mox tix khaing ku kaux;kix laux leeing som nai,
pui rauh pui see baox deeh haok huan heue.
