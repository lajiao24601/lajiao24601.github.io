Doeh ceu cad Yi-sao
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.34–37)
36
Plak greem in mawh grawng doeh ceu cad Yi-sao;
ceu kaux Yi-sao tang ceu mawh Yi-tong.
2 aYi-sao moeng kawn bun Cia-nang khankix;
nawh teei tix moeng kawn bun Yi-loeng pa kaux Ya-tax ceu pui Heux,
pui Si-we kawn saux Ci-piien kawn bun Ya-na pa kaux A-heu-li-pa-ma,
3 bkawn bun Yi-seu-ma-lix nan Ni-pai-yaw pa kaux Pa-seu-mawx.
4 Ya-tax keh Yi-li-fa;
Pa-seu-mawx keh Lio-eu.
5 A-heu-li-pa-ma keh Yie-wu-see,
Ya-lang,
Kheu-la.
Kawn simex Yi-sao in kix mawh pa keh haktiex Cia-nang heue.
6 Yi-sao viex moeng ting tix mai moeng iak tix khankix,
kawn simex kawn bun tix,
mai can pui pa ot daux nyiex nawh khawm uik,
mai moi yung khawm uik;
kraung khrai pa hoik pon daux haktiex Cia-nang,
lih khaing pux aik tix Ya-keux,
hu ka haktiex tang dauh.
7 Mawh kheu kiex ra nyiex koe moi yung mai kraung khrai tom nie,
haktiex an ang lai kub kix ot,
ang chix tix ot tau,
Yi-sao tom lih hu khaing haktiex an,
8 mai hu ot ka gawng haktiex Yi-tong heue.
9 Plak greem in mawh tax du pui Yi-tong,
doeh ceu cad Yi-sao
10-13 Moeng Yi-sao Ya-tax keh kawn simex tix kaux,
pa kaux Yi-li-fa.
Yi-li-fa keh kawn simex phuan kaux,
pa mawh Thi-mang,
A-mawx,
Si-paw,
Cia-thang,
Ci-na-see.
Moeng iak Yi-li-fa pa kaux Thing-na keh kawn simex tix kaux,
pa kaux Ya-ma-lix.
Moeng Yi-sao Pa-seu-mawx keh kawn simex tix kaux,
pa kaux Lio-eu.
Lio-eu keh kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Na-ha,
Shie-la,
Sa-ma,
Mi-sha.
14 Moeng Yi-sao A-heu-li-pa-ma mawh kawn bun Ya-na kawn saux Ci-piien;
nawh keh Yi-sao cie kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Yie-wu-see,
Ya-lang,
Kheu-la heue.
15 Plak greem in mawh doeh ceu cad ceu pui Yi-sao.
Kawn ai Yi-sao Yi-li-fa mawh tax du ceu pui Thi-mang,
A-mawx,
Si-paw,
Ci-na-see,
16 Kheu-la,
Cia-thang,
Ya-ma-lix.
In kix plak piang mawh doeh ceu cad moeng Yi-sao Ya-tax heue.
17 Kawn simex Yi-sao pa kaux Lio-eu mawh tax du ceu pui Na-ha,
Shie-la,
Sa-ma,
Mi-sha in kix.
In kix plak piang mawh doeh ceu cad moeng Yi-sao pa kaux Pa-seu-mawx.
18 Ceu pui Yie-wu-see,
Ya-lang,
Kheu-la mawh doeh ceu cad moeng Yi-sao kawn bun Ya-na pa kaux A-heu-li-pa-ma.
19 In kix mawh doeh ceu cad Yi-sao heue.
Doeh ceu cad Si-eux
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.38–42)
20-21 Pui pa dawg ot haktiex Yi-tong,
mawh tax du pui Heu-lix doeh ceu cad Si-eux.
Kix mawh Law-thang,
Saw-pa,
Ci-piien,
Ya-na,
Ti-soeng,
Yi-ca,
Ti-sang.
22 Law-thang mawh tax du ceu pui Heu-lix mai Si-mang.
Law-thang koe nan tix kaux pa mawh Thing-na.
23 Saw-pa mawh tax du ceu pui Ya-leu-weung,
Ma-na-sia,
Yi-pa-lu,
See-paw,
A-nang.
24 Ci-piien koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Ya-ya mai Ya-na.
(Ya-na mawh pui pa yaox ka dee lih rawm si u,
yam liang la keeing tix.)
25-26 Ya-na mawh keeing Ti-soeng;
Ti-soeng mawh tax du ceu pui Sing-tang,
Yi-see-pang,
Yi-lang mai Ci-lang.
Ya-na koe kawn bun tix kaux pa kaux A-heu-li-pa-ma.
27 Yi-ca mawh tax du ceu pui Pi-hang,
Sa-fang,
Ya-kang.
28 Ti-sang mawh tax du ceu pui Wu-see mai Ya-lang.
29-30 Plak greem in mawh ceu pui Heu-lix pa ot ka haktiex Yi-tong:
Law-thang,
Saw-pa,
Ci-piien,
Ya-na,
Ti-soeng,
Yi-ca,
Ti-sang.
Simiang Yi-tong khankix
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.43–54)
31-39 Sivoe ang simiang pui Yi-seu-liex lih koe yam an,
simiang in kix plak greem lai plud paox tix giex ka kaing piang Yi-tong:
Kawn simex Pi-eux pa kaux Pi-la,
sum ka veng ting Thing-ha-pa,
pui Paw-see-la kawn simex Shie-la pa kaux Yaw-pa,
Hu-sang pui haktiex Thi-mang,
kawn simex Pi-tax pa mawh Ha-tax,
sum veng ting Ya-wui-teu.
(Nawh pon pex ka pui Mi-tiien ka daux naing Maw-ya).
Pui Ma-see-li-cia pa kaux Sang-la.
Pui pa ot praok klawng ting Li-heu-paw pa kaux Sao-law,
kawn simex Ya-keu-paw pa kaux Pa-leu Ha-nang,
Ha-tax sum veng ting Pa-wu,
(moeng nawh Mi-si-tha-piiex mawh kawn bun Ma-theu-liex kawn saux Mi Sa-hawx)
40-43 Yi-sao mawh tax du pa ot ka haktiex Yi-tong ceu pui Thing-na,
Ya-leu-wa,
Yie-thie,
A-heu-li-pa-ma,
Yi-la,
Pi-nuing,
Ci-na-see,
Thi-mang,
Mi-pi-sa,
Ma-ci-tiie,
Yi-lang.
Dee ot ceu pui mawx ceu pui an,
tang hawt ceu kaux ceu pui cao tix heue.
