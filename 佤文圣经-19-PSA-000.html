Lai Yaog Yaw
Krai dawt
Lai Yaog Yaw mawh lox rhax yaog yaw pa khrawm tix tau ka daux phuk lai sigang khri mai gum lox rawg buan, mawh kheu bawg yam tiiem ka ang mawh tau, mai cao tiiem kawx ang mawh pui tix kaux. Gum lox rhax in kix mai gum lox rawg buan pui Yi-seu-liex khrawm tix kieh ot tau, mai siao ka yam yuh kaing krub, dee si dauh tom tag pon khrawm ot ka daux phuk lai sigang khri heue.
Lox rhax ka daux Lai Yaog Yaw mawh khawm lox in kix: lox yaog yaw, lox rhax pa krub ka Siyiex; lox khiox khaw krax teem krax coi, krax pa kia ngien, lox rhax krax taoh; lox pa khiox khaw pluat ploi phi map, lox rhax pa yaog yaw buan son; kawn koe gum lox rawg buan krax pa khiox khaw pawk bix ka pui koeng. Mu gum lox rawg buan in kix koe pa mawh kaux mawx kaux an, mai ceu pui mawx ceu pui an; tix blah mawh pa krai ka rhawm keud kaux mawx kaux an lih, tix blah mawh pa jhi grawng pa keud pot jun pot pui haoh pui hun cie Siyiex heue.
Ye-sux hoik sax siao Lai Yaog Yaw; cao tiiem phuk lai sigang khri pa khraox kawx hoik rien siao ka jheeng jheeng. Ceu cao phaox ka dix dix, kix hoik siao ka Lai Yaog Yaw yam krub tix ka daux nyiex buan heue.
Krai kaing
Lai Yaog Yaw guah tix koe phuan phuk:
Ka daux phuk tix: simin ting tix mu hoek simin ting tipon tix.
Ka daux phuk ra: simin ting tipon ra mu hoek atigliah ra mu.
Ka daux phuk loe: simin ting atigliah loe mu hoek tix daix dim mu.
Ka daux phuk pon: simin ting tix dim mu hoek tix yieh mai liah mu.
Ka daux phuk phuan: simin ting tix yieh mai aliah mu hoek tix yieh tix phuan mu.
