Lai Simiang Phuk Ra
Yi-li-yax mai simiang Ya-ha-shiex
1
Khaix hoik yum Ya-ha simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
meung Maw-ya tieh vheen ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
2 Ya-ha-shiex simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
cawd khaing piang grah choek nyiex haw simiang Sa-ma-li-yax,
nawh kied nie mat.
Nawh ploi pui hu khiox khaw ka Siyiex veng Yi-keu-loeng pui Fe-li-see,
pa mawh Pa-lix Si-pu,
son sang tawng grawng sang moi ang nawh moi.
3 Viang mawh nan,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka cao jhai Yi-li-yax pui Thi-see-pi,
nhie nawh hu ka kawn cau simiang Ya-ha-shiex,
mai chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai hu sawg tix chawk ka siyiex Yi-keu-loeng pa mawh Pa-lix Si-pu lie?
Khex ang Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex koe lie?
4 Maix hu krai ka simiang,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Maix ang lai pon lih khaing ku pa hoik it maix ka piang;
maix tag sang yum heue!’ ”
Yi-li-yax hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex.
5 Kawn cau an kix deeh hoek ka simiang dee an,
simiang chawk kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex deeh hoek lie?”
6 Kix ah nin:
“Yix si pup ka pui tix kaux,
nawh nhie yix deeh ing krai ka simiang,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka maix:
‘Yuh ka mawx maix lai ploi pui sawg tix chawk ka siyiex Yi-keu-loeng,
pa mawh Pa-lix Si-pu lie?
Khex ang Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex koe lie?
Mawh kheu nan,
maix ang lai pon lih khaing ku pa hoik it maix ka piang;
maix tag sang yum heue!’ ”
7 Simiang chawk ah nin:
“Pui pa hoek rieb ka pex an,
yuh greeh grang nawh ka mawx?”
8 aKix ah nin:
“Nawh ceep ka sibex dha hak to tiag,
mai nhawt nyoi tix ka maox hak heue.”
Simiang ah lox tix ting ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Yi-li-yax eue!”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang ploi cao bung naing viex pui tix phuan kaux hu giex ka Yi-li-yax.
Cao bung naing an yaox ka Yi-li-yax,
nawh ngawm ka piang gawng iak tix mu,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex eue,
simiang tawx lo nhie maix lih heue!”
10 bYi-li-yax ah nin:
“Phan aux mawh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
kieh ngu lih hoek khaing rao ma,
gleeing ka maix mai ka pui pa ot greem maix!”
Dum mawx dum an,
koe ngu lih hoek khaing rao ma,
mai gleeing ka cao bung naing an mai ka pui pa ot greem nawh khankix heue.
11 Simiang baox ploi cao bung naing tang kaux,
viex pui tix phuan kaux hu ah nin ka Yi-li-yax:
“Pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex eue,
simiang tawx lox nhie maix nied lih heue!”
12 Yi-li-yax ah nin:
“Phan aux mawh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex,
kieh ngu lih hoek khaing rao ma,
gleeing ka maix mai ka pui pa ot greem maix!”
Dum mawx dum an,
koe ngu lih hoek khaing rao ma,
mai gleeing ka cao bung naing an mai ka pui pa ot greem nawh khankix heue.
13 Simiang baox ploi cao bung naing viex pui tix phuan kaux hu.
Cao bung naing an haok ka piang gawng,
grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe Yi-li-yax,
mai khiox khaw ah nin:
“Pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux mai ka pui pa ot greem aux khankix,
mai taoh ka ju yix liak!
14 Cao bung naing tang kaux an kiex ra kaux mai pui pa ot greem nawh khankix,
ngu pa lih hoek khaing rao ma gleeing kix yum khawm uik;
viang maw nan,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux liak!”
15 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yi-li-yax:
“Maix lih tau mai nawh,
paw vi lhat.”
Yi-li-yax tom hu tau mai cao bung naing an hoek ka simiang dee an,
16 nawh ah nin ka simiang:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Mawh kheu ploi maix pui sawg tix chawk ka siyiex Yi-keu-loeng,
pa mawh Pa-lix Si-pu,
awm pa ang Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex koe,
son sang sawg tix chawk ka nawh yuh nan,
mawh kheu nan,
maix ang lai pon li khaing ku pa hoik it maix ka piang;
maix tag sang yum heue!’ ”
17 Awm lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex Yi-li-yax krai,
Ya-ha-shiex hoik yum.
Ya-ha-shiex ang koe kawn simex,
mawh kheu nan,
pux nawh Yaw-heu-lang plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh;
daux num an,
mawh simiang Yo-tax kawn simex Yaw-sa-fa pa kaux Yaw-lang pa giex ka kaing ka daux ra num heue.
18 Kaing yuh simiang Ya-ha-shiex tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
