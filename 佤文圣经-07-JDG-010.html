Thaw-la
10
Khaix hoik yum Ya-pi-mi-leux,
kawn saux Taw-to pa mawh kawn simex Phu-wa pa kaux Thaw-la,
lih tix taoh ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Nawh mawh ceu pui Yi-sa-cia,
mai paung ot ka baoh gawng haktiex Yi-fa-liien pa mawh Sa-mi.
2 Nawh yuh simiang cao prawng ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tix nga loe num.
Nawh hoik yum,
si paung nawh ka Sa-mi heue.
Ya-eux
3 Khaix hoik yum Thaw-la,
koe pui Ci-liex pa kaux Ya-eux tom yuh simiang cao prawng ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tix nga ra num.
4 Nawh koe kawn simex tix ngoe kaux,
mai brug ka la tix ngoe mu.
Kix koe veng tix ngoe mu ka baoh haktiex Ci-liex;
tom hoek in singaix,
veng in kawn kok mawh Ha-waw-theu Ya-eux.a
5 Ya-eux hoik yum,
mai si paung nawh ka Cia-meung heue.
Yie-fu-tha
6 Pui Yi-seu-liex baox phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex.
Kix krub ka Pa-lix mai Ya-see-tha-lux,
mai kawn koe siyiex Shui-li-yax,
Si-toeng,
Maw-ya,
Ya-meung,
Fe-li-see khankix.
Kix singai hu khaing Simiang Siyiex,
ang krub ka nawh.
7 Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx kix daux tawm taix pui Fe-li-see mai pui Ya-meung.
8 Ka daux kao num daix,
kix kha rhawm gut kheem ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
pa mawh daux haktiex ceu pui Ya-maw-lix ka baoh haktiex Ci-liex.
9 Pui Ya-meung kawn tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang hu puing ka ceu pui Yo-tax,
ceu pui Piien-ya-ming mai ceu pui Yi-fa-liien.
Pui Yi-seu-liex kham ka krax pa taug pa mao heue.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yix phit lud ka maix;
yix singai hu khaing Siyiex yix,
mai hu krub ka Pa-lix heue.”
11 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Singaix pa hoik luan,
pui Ai-cix,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Ya-meung,
pui Fe-li-see,
12 pui Si-toeng,
pui Ya-ma-lix,
mai pui Ma-yuing hoik gut kheem ka pex,
pex khiox khaw ka aux,
khex ang aux hoik taoh pex lie?
13 Viang mawh nan,
pex ca kawn singai hu khaing aux,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux ang lai taoh ka pex.
14 Pex hu khiox khaw ka siyiex pa hoik laug pex heue!
Pex yaox krax pa kha rhawm,
mai kieh kix hoek tix taoh ka pex heue.”
15 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Yix hoik yuh phi map;
maix sang yuh yix yuh ka mawx,
yuh heue.
Ca rawg ngie maix taoh yix daux in singaix heue!”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
kix yieh tix tik ka riang siyiex pui tang ceu,
mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex laux rhawm kheu pa kham pui Yi-seu-liex krax pa kha rhawm heue.
17 Yam an,
pui naing Ya-meung ot ka Ci-liex,
mai rien tix yuh naing.
Pui Yi-seu-liex kawx khrawm paox tix sum nyiex bang ot ka Mi-see-pa haktiex Ci-liex.
18 Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui mai kawn pui,
pa ot ka baoh haktiex Ci-liex lai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Pui mawx ran hu puing ka pui Ya-meung sivoe,
tag kieh nawh mawh simiang pui haktiex Ci-liex heue.”
