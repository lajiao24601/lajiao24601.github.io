Lai Heu-si-a
Krai dawt
Sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num aliah yieh tix nga ra num, ka daux singaix bawg yam sivoe sang laux leeing Sa-ma-li-yax, cao jhai Heu-si-ax pa plud ka cao jhai A-maw-see, ot ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex plak laong mai sidiex gau gum lox Siyiex. Nawh kied khix rhawm lhat kawn pui krub ka riang rao, ang koe krax pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex. Nawh viex rhawm ting moeng ka bun pa seeing brai tix kaux, mai kieh kaing in mawh pa jhi ka son ang pui Yi-seu-liex cu lox. Awm yuh moeng nawh Keu-mix kaing pa seeing brai ka piang nawh yuh nan, pui cie Siyiex hoik tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex yuh nan nang heue. Mawh kheu nan, krax ciyang Siyiex tag sang lih hoek ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex. Viang mawh nan, dee si dauh krax muh pa tawx Siyiex pa ang chix tieh loh tix pa kaux sijax an tag sang pon pex; nawh tag sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex deeh veeh rhawm tix hoek ka tix, mai deeh yuh kaing pa khaw rhawm nawh awm sivoe an. Krax muh Siyiex krai gum lox pa chuh yuh rhawm rhi kawn pui plak greem lih ra loe gum ah nin: “Pui Yi-seu-liex eue, sang tik vud aux ka pex yuh ka mawx? Sang tieb tie aux ka pex yuh ka maw?…Aux ang rhawm yuh nin ka pex, mawh kheu kied muh aux ka pex heue!” (11.8)
Krai kaing
1. Grawng pa koe moeng Heu-si-ax mai grawng nyiex ma nawh (1.1–3.5)
2. Gum lox pa ne raw ka pui Yi-seu-liex (4.1–13.16)
3. Pa loh ka rhawm rhi tix mai ka gum lox pa hoik ap tix deuh (14.1–9)
