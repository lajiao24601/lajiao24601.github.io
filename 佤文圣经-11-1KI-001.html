Lai Simiang Phuk Tix
Yam hoik kuad ju Ta-wue
1
Ka daux bawg yam in,
Ta-wue hoik kied kuad ju;
viang pui cau nawh khankix teei man prux lhop piang to kaux nawh kawx,
mai nawh kawn ang nyang mhong si u.
2 Kawn cau pa ting nawh ah nin ka nawh:
“Vawng,
kieh yix hu sawg kawn bun krih pa awn ju hoek tix khre ka maix,
kia ngien ka maix,
mai kieh nawh it ka praok maix,
son sang si u yuh maix heue.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom hu sawg ka kawn bun awn ju pa mhawm grang ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh,
mai kix yaox kawn bun tix kaux ka Su-niang,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-pi-sang,
kix tom viex nawh hoek ka simiang dee an.
4 Kawn bun in kied mhawm grang tete;
nawh ot tix khre mai kia ngien ka simiang,
simiang ang bax dex bax dawt tau mai nawh heue.
Ya-to-ni-ya simie tix mawh simiang
5-6 aYam an,
Ya-sa-long hoik yum;
kawn simex pa keh Ta-wue tau mai Ha-ci pa kaux Ya-to-ni-ya,
hoik sawn tix mawh kawn pa ting ju khaing paox tix.
Nawh kied sidaing mhawm grang.
Ta-wue ang sax ne raw nawh son kaing tix ceu kawx,
viang mawh nan,
nawh kied ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai simie tix mawh simiang.
Nawh rien khre ling law mai pui naing brug brung son cao to kaux tix,
mai baox koe pui cao pa pau nawh tix phuan kaux.
7 Nawh hoik sax kum kawng tau mai Yaw-ya (miex nawh mawh Si-lu-ya) mai cao giex kaing li Ya-pi-ya-tha;
pui in kiex hoik cu tix joh jhan nawh.
8 Viang mawh nan,
cao giex kaing li Sa-tu kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya,
cao jhai Na-tang,
See-me,
Li-yi mai pui cao pa pau Ta-wue khankix ang hawt ka rhawm nawh heue.
9 Koe tix singaix,
Ya-to-ni-ya ot ka dex dee keet rawm Yin Law-cie ka “Rang si uing” dee an,
mai tan lu ka moi,
yung,
mai kawn moi iak pa mhawm kluing kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
mai ghaok ka kawn simex Ta-wue tang kaux khankix mai kawn cau pa ting khankix,
kaoh khaing Yo-tax hoek tix ih priex krax tan in.
10 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ang kok ka pux tix Saw-law-meung pa mawh pa rom keeing tang miex,
cao jhai Na-tang,
mai Pi-na-ya,
mai pui cao pau simiang heue.
Yaog Saw-law-meung mawh simiang
11 bMawh kheu nan,
Na-tang hu ka miex Saw-law-meung pa kaux Pa-see-pa,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix ang bax mhong kawn simex Ha-ci pa kaux Ya-to-ni-ya,
hoik yaog to kaux tix mawh simiang lie?
Simiang Ta-wue ang bax tawng ka kaing in.
12 Phan maix sang taoh ka ju tix mai ju kawn tix Saw-law-meung,
aux simie maix
13 yam in nied tix hu ka simiang Ta-wue,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
‘Vawng,
ang maix hoik cu ka lox aux mhawm mhawm kawn simex aux Saw-law-meung tag sang plud ka simiang mawh maix lie?
Yuh ka mawx yam in Ya-to-ni-ya hoik mawh simiang lie?’ ”
14 Na-tang baox ah nin:
“Yam kawn kraix maix lox tau mai simiang,
aux tag sang laig ka daux nyiex,
mai mawh cao pa kham lox krai maix heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Pa-see-pa hoek ka dee lawg it simiang.
Simiang hoik kied kuad;
kawn bun pa kaoh khaing Su-niang pa kaux Ya-pi-sang kia ngien ka nawh.
16 Pa-see-pa gup tix krub ka sivoe simiang;
simiang chawk ka nawh:
“Maix sang lo ceu pa tix?”
17 Pa-see-pa ah nin:
“Vawng,
maix hoik tiex tu ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix,
son sang kieh kawn simex aux saw-law-meung plud ka simiang mawh maix.
18 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-to-ni-ya hoik tang yaog to kaux tix mawh simiang,
maix kawn ang bax tawng.
19 Nawh hoik tan lu moi,
yung,
mai kawn moi iak pa mhawm kluing tom nie kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
nawh hoik ghaok ka kawn simex maix khankix,
mai Ya-pi-ya-tha cao giex kaing li,
mai cao bung kaing naing maix pa kaux Yaw-ya hoek tix ih priex preem,
ang bax kok ngie ka kawn simex maix Saw-law-meung.
20 Vawng,
pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoik kuan tix jhak,
krox jun maix krai ka kix mawh mawx pa sang plud ka simiang mawh maix.
21 Phan maix ang krai,
khaix hoik yum maix,
kix rien tix een kawn simex aux Saw-law-meung mai aux mawh pui pa tieh vheen heue.”
22 Yam kawn si kah Pa-see-pa tau mai simiang,
Na-tang hoek bleeh ka nyiex haw simiang.
23 Koe pui krai ka simiang,
cao jhai hoek bleeh.
Na-tang laig daux nyiex,
mai krub ka sivoe simiang,
24 khaix an nawh ah nin:
“Vawng,
maix hoik krai pao Ya-to-ni-ya sang plud ka simiang mawh maix lie?
25 Daux in singaix,
nawh hoik tan lu moi,
yung mai moi iak pa mhawm kluing tom nie kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
mai nawh hoik ghaok ka can kawn simex maix khawm uik,
cao bung kaing naing maix Yaw-yac,
mai Ya-pi-ya-tha cao giex kaing li hoek tix ih priex an.
Yam in kix hoik ih tau nyaux tau mai Ya-to-ni-ya,
mai kok yo ah nin:
‘Simiang Ya-to-ni-ya lang ju pu rhang!’
26 Viang mawh nin,
Ya-to-ni-ya ang bax kok ka aux,
mai ka Sa-tu cao giex kaing li,
mai ka Pi-na-ya kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax,
mai ka Saw-law-meung.
27 Vawng maix rug hoik cu ka kaing in lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai ang krai ka kawn cau pa ting maix,
mawh mawx pa sang plud ka simiang pa mawh maix lie?”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ta-wue tawx lox ka pui pa ot ka plak goix plak dawm tix,
nhie kix hu sawg ka Pa-see-pa hoek,
Pa-see-pa hoek,
mai cung ka sivoe simiang.
29 Simiang ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux hoik tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
pa hoik taoh aux luat luan khaing krax kha rhawm kha rhi ku ceu an,
cu ka lox maix,
30 Ka daux in singaix,
aux sang giex kaux ka lox pa hoik tiex tu tix ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex:
kawn simex maix Saw-law-meung tag sang plud ka simiang mawh aux heue.”
31 Pa-see-pa krub ah nin:
“Cao aux simiang aux kieh lang ju pu rhang heue!”
32 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ta-wue tawx lox nhie pui hu kok Sa-tu cao giex kaing li,
cao jhai Na-tang mai Pi-na-ya kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax hoek.
Yam hoik hoek kix,
33 simiang Ta-wue ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw rom tix mai simiang naing pa kuang nyiex haw,
kieh kawn simex aux Saw-law-meung brug ka law cie aux,
hu tawx nawh hoek ka praok dee keet rawm Ci-suing.
34 Ka dee an,
Sa-tu mai Na-tang tag sang ap nawh mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pex tag caw tix phlu ka teet tom ting luah ah nin:
‘Simiang Saw-law-meung kieh lang ju pu rhang!’
35 Khaix an pex tag caw tix hu tawx nawh hoek ka dee in;
nawh tag sang ngawm ka piang dang ngeung cie aux,
mai plud tix mawh simiang khaix aux,
mawh kheu hoik ap aux nawh mawh simiang piang pui Yi-seu-liex mai piang pui Yo-tax heue.”
36 Pi-na-ya ah nin:
“Kieh alo simiang lih tix mawh!
Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix yuh kaing in lih mawh.
37 Simiang Siyiex ot tau yuh ka mawx mai Vawng,
kieh nawh ot tau mai Saw-law-meung yuh nan nang,
khaix an mai kieh meung pa giex nawh kaing piang nie baox haok huan ting lhaong heue!”
38 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-tu cao giex kaing li,
cao jhai Na-tang,
Pi-na-ya kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax mai pui pa pau daux nyiex haw khankix nhie Saw-law-meung brug ka law cie simiang Ta-wue,
hu tawx nawh hoek ka praok dee keet rawm Ci-suing.
39 Sa-tu teei rong tix mu pa hoik sau bee naog ka khaing daux nyiex gox buan dee deuh teug sijax mukheu lih,
mai groi ka piang Saw-law-meung.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix phlu ka teet,
pui haoh pui hun gaux rhawm gaux rhi ah nin:
“Kieh Saw-law-meung lang ju pu rhang!”
40 Khaix an kix deeh ing tau mai Saw-law-meung,
mai gaux rhawm leeing lawng krax,
mai phlu ka siao,
lox yo kix hoik si num tom yung yang yuh haktiex heue.
41 Ya-to-ni-ya mai nyo si nie nawh khankix jiet taug sang naox ka priex preem pa ih tix,
kix mhong lox in.
Yaw-ya mhong lox luah teet,
nawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Ka daux veng lih kaing ceu pa tix lie?
Lox pa ting si num luah mawh grawh grawng ceu pa tix lie?”
42 Lox krai kix ang nyang bax uik,
Yaw-na-tang kawn simex Ya-pi-ya-tha cao giex kaing li tom hoek.
Ya-to-ni-ya ah nin:
“Laig daux nyiex,
maix mawh pui mhawm,
maix rien tix viex ka lox pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi hoek heue.”
43 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Kaing ting pa ang mhawm hoik lih!
Simiang Ta-wue hoik yaog Saw-law-meung mawh simiang.
44 Nawh hoik kieh Sa-tu cao giex kaing li,
mai cao jhai Na-tang mai kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya mai pui pa pau daux nyiex haw khankix tawx ka Saw-law-meung,
mai hoik kieh nawh brug ka law cie simiang.
45 Sa-tu mai Na-tang hoik ap nawh mawh simiang ka praok dee keet rawm Ci-suing.
Kix tawx nawh laig daux veng,
mai gaux rhawm leeing lawng krax;
pui haoh pui hun hoik kok krawh.
An hoik mawh lox pa taug sang mhong maix.
46 Saw-law-meung hoik haok ngawm ka piang dang ngeung.
47 Lheu khaing an,
can cao kaing ka daux nyiex haw khawm uik,
hoik hoek tix krub ka simiang Ta-wue,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
‘Kieh Siyiex maix kieh Saw-law-meung lheu ting min khaing maix,
meung pa giex nawh kaing piang lheu haok huan khaing maix!’
Simiang Ta-wue krub ka piang ku it tix.
48 Mai rawg buan ah nin:
‘Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex!
Daux in singaix,
nawh hoik kieh doeh ceu cad aux plud ka simiang mawh aux,
mai baox kieh ngai aux yaox kaing in yam kawn im ju tix heue!’ ”
49 Nyo si nie Ya-to-ni-ya khankix hoik yaoh yee,
tom yaog tix kaoh cung tang to kah.
50 Ya-to-ni-ya kawx hoik lhat graung ka Saw-law-meung,
mai to hoek ka nyiex gox buan dee deuh ka teug sijax mukheu,
nawh kawm ka rong khu dee tand.
51 Koe pui krai ka simiang Saw-law-meung,
ah kheu lhat Ya-to-ni-ya ka nawh tom hu giex ka rong khu dee tan,
mai ah nin:
“Aux sang kieh simiang Saw-law-meung ran tiex tu ka aux sivoe ha,
paw vi pauh aux yum heue.”
52 Saw-law-meung ah nin:
“Phan nawh cu lox aux,
hauk kaing nawh pie tix gawng ang yaox tix glaoh;
phan yuh pa caox pa caig,
nawh tag sang yum tete.”
53 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Saw-law-meung ploi pui hu teei Ya-to-ni-ya khaing khu dee tan.
Ya-to-ni-ya hu ka simiang Saw-law-meung,
mai krub ka sivoe simiang.
Simiang ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix ing ka nyiex heue.”
