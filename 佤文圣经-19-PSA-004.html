Lox rawg buan krax teem coi pon bo
4
Siyiex aux,
cao pa krai lox tang aux eue,
yam kok yo aux ka maix,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox aux liak!
Yam ot aux ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
maix teem coi ka aux;
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan liak!
 
2 Pex pui in kix eue,
pex tag sang yuh krax kaig krax tua ka aux hoek yam mawx lie?
Pex muh ka ceu pa giex pa going,
mai leue ka ceu pa pie lhien,
tag sang si dauh yam mawx lie?
 
3 Kieh pex tawng Simiang Siyiex hoik kok aux mawh pui cie nawh;
nawh nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux.
4 aKieh pex si yaung si kua,
paw lai baox yuh phi map;
yam yawg tix ot ka daux lawg it tix,
keud ce ka daux rhawm tix.
5 Tan lu krax tan pa khaw tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
yum rhawm ka nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix heue.
 
6 Koe pui tix blah ah nin:
ex kied simie tix pon buan son tete!
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix teei krax muh tix kia ngien ka piang yix liak!
7 Viang mawh nan,
krax gaux rhawm pa hoik tawx maix ka aux kawn nie
khaing pa pon aux ka nghox koi pa haok huan,
plai pa kreh heue.
 
8 Yam blauh aux tix it,
mai yaox tix nyawm it nyawm tom ka daux ca mao;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
mawh ngie maix pa pon kia ngien aux nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.
