Pui hun doh keud ka phi map tix
9
1-2 Khix in ka daux tix nga pon singaix,
pui Yi-seu-liex khrawm paox tix tau mai tuah priex preem,
laux rhawm kheu phi map kix.
Kix hoik tang khan tix tang ot khaing can pui tang ceu tang cad khawm uik.
Kix ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
phruah nyaox piang kaing tix,
jhi grawng krax pa laux rhawm tix,
khaix an cung tix doh keud ka phi map pa yuh to kaux tix mai phi map pa yuh tax du tix.
3 Been glang loe na rhi,
kix cung tix nghiet ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix;
khaix an loe na rhi,
kix baox doh keud ka phi map tix mai krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix heue.
4 Koe pheen sidiex tix mu hoik mawh pa yuh son pui Li-we,
pa cung ka piang pheen an mawh:
Yie-su-yax,
Pa-ni,
Cia-miiex,
See-pa-ni,
Pu-ni,
See-li-pi,
Pa-ni,
mai Ci-na-ni.
Kix rawg buan ting si num ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix heue.
5 Pui Li-we pa mawh Yie-su-yax,
Cia-miiex,
Pa-ni,
Ha-sa-ni,
See-li-pi,
Heu-ti-ya,
See-pa-ni,
mai Pi-tha-si-ya krai pao:
son sang jauh tix yuh krax krub;
kix ah nin:
Kaoh cung,
yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex;
tag caw tix yaog yaw nawh leeing ju leeing phan!
Ceu kaux nawh lheu lhaong khaing krax pa yaog yaw rhax lai khawm uik;
kieh pui ku kaux yaog yaw ceu kaux pa ting min nawh heue.
Rawg buan krax pa doh keud ka phi map
6 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex rawg buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
mawh ngie maix pa mawh Simiang Siyiex!
Maix hoik phan yuh ka rao ma mai simuing daux rao ma;
maix hoik phan yuh ka haktiex mai pa koe ka daux haktiex khawm uik,
rawm sigrit sigra mai pa koe ka daux;
maix hoik tawx ju ka can pa koe ka daux khawm uik.
Kru naing rao ma hoik krub ka maix khawm uik.
7 aMaix mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pa hoik laug ka Ya-paw-lang;
maix hoik viex nawh lih hu khaing haktiex Wu-eu veng Pa-pi-loeng,
loh ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-paw-la-hang.
8 bMaix tawng grawng pa hoik yum rhawm nawh ka tix;
maix yuh sijax mukheu tau mai nawh.
Maix hoik ap tix tawx haktiex pui Cia-nang,
pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix ka nawh,
haktiex pui Pi-li-si,
pui Yie-pu-see,
pui Keu-cia-sha ka nawh,
Son sang kieh mawh haktiex dee ot doeh ceu cad nawh.
Maix hoik giex lox pa ap tix kaux cong,
kheu hoik mawh maix pa khaw caw.
 
9 cMaix hoik yaox krax pa kha tax du yix rhawm tix ka daux haktiex Ai-cix;
maix hoik mhong lox pa rawg kix tix taoh kix ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
10 dMaix hoik yuh mhai pa daox pa am tieb tie ka simiang Pha-raw,
tieb tie ka pui naing nawh mai pui hun pa ot ka daux meung nawh;
mawh kheu maix hoik tawng kix gut kheem ka pui cie maix.
Mawh kheu nan,
maix hoik pon ao min ceu kaux tom hoek yam in singaix.
11 eMaix hoik guah rawm sigrit sigra tang ot son pui cie tix,
thung kix hu piang tiex kroh.
Maix hoik taing tix tik pui pa leue kix daux rawm sigrit sigra,
awm simaox pa com ka greem rawm sigrit sigra.
12 fPon singaix,
maix teei rong pai awm thung ka kix;
pon sawm,
mai teei rong ngu jhan riang piang krax hu kix.
13 gMaix lih hoek khaing rao ma ka gawng Si-nai,
si kah ka pui cie tix.
Maix hoik tawx lox tat jhawm prawng pa rauh pa see ka kix,
mai lox sidiex gau pa kaux cong mai gum lox ping nyap.
14 Maix hoik nhie kix priex tix hawt ka singaix laux maix,
mai kieh mawh singaix pa singax singeh;
maix hoik tawx siliex maix,
pa mawh Maw-si krai ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap heue.
 
15 hKix cai rhawm,
maix tawx priex preem lih hoek khaing rao ma;
kix simie tix nyaux rawm,
maix kieh rawm buing tix lih khaing rang.
Maix ap tix sang tawx haktiex pa sang tawx ka kix,
Maix tawx lox kieh kix pon ca.
16 iViang mawh nan,
tax du ex nied ot nied tix ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
nied ot nied tix kiien rhawm,
ang cu tix priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap maix.
17 jKix ang cu tix hawt,
mai hoik piawm ka kaing pa yuh maix khawm uik;
kix hoik piawm ka mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh maix khankix.
Kix koe rhawm pa tieh vheen,
mai laug ka simiang tix kaux,
nhie nawh viex kix deeh ing yuh siliex cawng ka haktiex Ai-cix.
Viang mawh nan,
maix hoik mawh Siyiex pa pluat ploi phi map;
maix hoik grum naog ka buan son mai krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi;
maix ang song rhawm yie yie,
mai grum naog ka krax muh.
Maix ang bax tik vud ka kix.
18 kKix raug riang moi tix mu,
krai in mawh siyiex pa thung kix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix!
Kix hoik yuh kaing ting pa ang mhawm ka maix!
19 lViang mawh nan,
maix ang bax tik vud kix ka daux tiag daux tu,
Mawh kheu hoik ting krax pa caox rhawm maix.
Rong pai awm pa thung kix ka krax daux pon ngaix ang bax lih hu khaing kix;
rong ngu pa jhan riang piang kix pon sawm maix kawx ang bax teei hu.
20 Khuan pa mhawm pa tawx maix hoik thung gau ka kix;
Maix tawx kix ih Ma-na,m
mai baox tawx kix nyaux rawm.
21 Yam ot kix ka daux tiag tipon num,
maix hoik tawx can ceu pa sang lo kix khawm uik.
Kraung ceep kix ang sax griah tix bawg kawx;
caong kix kawx ang sax uah.
 
22 nMaix hoik kieh kix pon kuang ka meung haktiex ku meung,
pon tix cie ka haktiex pa ot sivie siveng.
Kix hoik pon tix cie ka haktiex Si-hong,
simiang haktiex Si-seu-peung,
haktiex Pa-sang pa kuang simiang Eux.
23 oMaix hoik tawx kix koe kawn praix,
haoh hun awm simuing daux rao ma.
Maix hoik kieh kix pon kuang mai yaox tix ot daux haktiex in,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik ap maix tix tawx ka tax du kix.
24 pKix hoik pon tix cie ka haktiex Cia-nang;
maix hoik yeen ka pui haktiex an.
Maix tawx riang rao ka pui cie tix,
kieh kix pon teei alo tix yuh ka simiang haktiex Cia-nang khankix mai piang pui hun heue.
25 qPui cie maix hoik teei ka
veng pa kaux cong,
haktiex pa mhawm,
nyiex ma pa naog ka sin ngao,
dung pa hoik kaong tix mhawm,
khaox sime,
khaox plix,
mai si peem plix siiet.
Kix sau riang tix ih,
kaux hak kix kluing mu;
yaox tix ih nyaux ka priex preem pa nyawm pa tawx maix khawm uik heue.
 
26 rViang mawh nan,
pui cie maix tieh vheen ka maix,
ang nghiet lox maix;
Kix ang simi ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
Kix pauh ka cao jhai pa sidiex kix tik krax pa caox mai hu ka daux krax pa rauh pa see.
Kix tix bawg hoik tix bawg map nai ka maix;
27 maix tom ap kix daux tawm taix pui koeng pa gut kheem ka kix.
Yam yaox kix krax kha,
mai khiox khaw maix taoh teem tix,
maix nghiet lox kix khaing rao ma.
Maix grum naog krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi,
tawx kix koe simiang,
taoh kix luat luan khaing tawm taix pui koeng.
28 Khaix hoik pon kix krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai baox yuh phi map ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
maix tom kieh pui koeng baox pon kuang ka kix.
Viang mawh nan,
yam deeh loh kix rhawm tix khiox khaw ka maix,
hoik mhong lox kix ka piang rao ma,
maix hoik koe krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi,
tix bawg hoik tix bawg mai taoh ka kix.
29 sMaix hoik sidiex gau kix,
nhie kix priex tix hawt lox tat jhawm prawng,
viang mawh nan,
kix ca nyhau,
ang cu tix nghiet lox tawx maix.
Viang kix hoik tawng priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix mawh pa thung hu krax pa im ju,
kix ca kawn kieh ngawg tix kiien,
mai ca tieb tie ang nghiet kah.
30 tMaix hoik viex rhawm gun rhawm yeung sidiex gau ka kix ku num.
Maix paoh rhawm cao jhai si kah ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
yhaok pui cie maix hoik lheet,
mawh kheu nan,
maix kieh pui pa ot sivie siveng maix pon kuang ka kix.
31 Viang mawh nan kawx,
krax caox rhawm maix kied ting kied lhaong,
maix ang bax tik vud ka kix,
yuh kix laux leeing;
kheu mawh maix Siyiex pa grum koe buan son mai krax pa caox rhawm!
 
32 uSiyiex yix eue,
maix kied ting kied lhaong,
maix mawh pa kied lhat graung yung yi pui kah,
mawh pa kied ting siawp sibrawm!
Maix hoik giex kaux ka lox pa ap tix.
Jauh tom gut kheem simiang Ya-sux yix hoek tom yam in,
yix hoik kham krax kha rhawm kha rhi!
Simiang yix eue,
pa mawh simiang yix,
cao giex kaing li yix,
cao jhai yix,
tax du yix khawm can pui hun khawm uik,
hoik kham krax kha rhawm kha rhi.
Khiox khaw maix doh keud kheu hoik kham yix krax kha rhawm kha rhi tom nie!
33 Maix ciyang ka yix hoik mawh pa khaw yix tix pon tete;
maix kaux sijax,
mai yix ca kawn yuh phi map.
34 Tax du yix,
simiang meung yix,
simiang yix,
cao giex kaing li yix ang bax nghiet ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix,
ang bax nghiet ka lox tawm jhawm krai maix mai lox sidiex lox gau maix.
35 Pui cie maix hoik sax paung ot ka haktiex mhawm pa vieh va pa hoik tawx maix,
hoik pon buan son pa ting khaing daux meung cie to kaux tix,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang bax yoi tix singai hu khaing phi map,
mai hoek krub ka maix.
36 Yam in yix hoik ot ka daux haktiex pa tawx maix,
yuh siliex cawng ka daux haktiex pa tawx maix yix ih priex nyawm ka daux.
37 To ma haktiex in hoik tawx ka simiang pa ploi maix hoek kuang ka yix khawm uik,
mawh kheu yuh yix phi map.
Kix nieb pon yuh ka yix mai ka khawng eei brie liang yix,
yix hoik yaox krax laux leeing pa ting heue!
Pui hun yuh sijax mukheu
38 Mawh kheu kaing pa lih in kix,
yix pui Yi-seu-liex tom bre rhawm rhi tix yuh ka sijax mukheu.
Simiang yix,
pui Li-we,
mai cao giex kaing li hoik tiiem ceu kaux tix daux lai an khawm uik heue.
