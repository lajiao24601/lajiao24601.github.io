Krax gaux rhawm pui rauh pui see
112
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
 
Pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex kied mawh pa koe buan son tete heue!
Pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap Simiang Siyiex kied mawh pa koe buan son tete heue!
2 Doeh ceu cad pui rauh pui see tag pon buan son tete;
kawn praix nawh tag sang chix pon krax pa haok huan ka piang haktiex.
3 Pui cub nyiex ma nawh tag sang mag mi;
nawh tag sang haok huan tom hoek leeing ju heue.
 
4 Pa riang jhan riang ka daux pa faik son pui rauh pui see,
jhan riang ka pui pa koe krax muh,
krax pa caox rhawm,
krax pa rauh pa see khankix.
5 Pui pa si pieh rhawm tix tawx koe buan son heue;
nawh hoik coh cai lai gee ka daux krax pa mawh pa meh.
6 Pui rauh pui see ang yaox krax laux leeing som nai;
pui ang chix piawm ka nawh leeing ju heue.
 
7 Nawh ang lhat graung kheu pa mhong tix lox caox;
rhawm rhi nawh kaux cong,
kheu hoik yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
8 Nawh ang koe krax khi krax glaoh;
nawh hoik yum rhawm ka grawng sang yaox tix ka pa laux pa leeing pui koeng.
9 aNawh si pieh rhawm tix tawx ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
krax pa caox rhawm nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan;
nawh tag sang ting riang haok huan,
mai pon krax pa nab yam yung yi kawn pui.
10 Pui caox pui caig suk sag rhawm kheu pa yaox tix ka grawng pa ting riang haok huan pui rauh pui see;
kix greeik rang braing mai yum tix;
krax keud pot jun pot kix tag sang mawh pa giex pa going heue.
