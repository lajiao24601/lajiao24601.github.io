Haktiex pa tawx mawh cie Simiang Siyiex
45
Yam guah tix tawx haktiex ka ceu pui ku ceu,
tag caw tix deuh tix nyum tawx ka Simiang Siyiex.
Haktiex in krax lang tag caw tix kieh koe kao ra kongli plak,
krax vieh koe kao konglia;
ka daux haktiex in khawm uik mawh haktiex pa singax.
2 Tag caw tix pawd lih tix dauh kieh mawh haktiex pa siao ka nyiex buan,
ka praok ku daog krax lang koe ra yieh tix phuan kongjhee,
pon praok baox thiem thie haktiex pa yi pa koe krax vieh tix nga phuan kongjhee.
3 Haktiex in tix plak,
krax lang koe kao ra kongli plak,
krax vieh koe phuan kongli,
tag caw tix pawd lih mawh pa siao son pa sum nyiex buan,
mawh haktiex pa kied singax singeh.
4 Ka daux meung haktiex khawm uik,
haktiex in mawh haktiex pa dee singax,
mawh pa pawd lih mawh cie cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing daux nyiex buan;
ka daux haktiex an koe nyiex cie kix mai haktiex pa siao son nyiex buan.
5 Haktiex an tix plak tag caw tix tawx mawh cie pui Li-we pa yuh kaing daux nyiex buan,
ka daux haktiex an koe nyiex ma pa paung ot pui Li-web.
6 Tag caw tix pawd haktiex tix dauh ka haktiex dee singax,
krax lang koe kao ra kongli plak,
krax vieh koe ra kongli plak,
mai kieh mawh pa paung ot pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Haktiex cie simiang
7 Tag caw tix pawd haktiex lih tix dauh tawx mawh cie simiang.
Haktiex in tag caw tix vih tix jauh ka haktiex pa singax plak klip singaix tom hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong,
jauh tix vih ka haktiex pa singax plak lih singaix tom hoek ka mee haktiex meung ka plak lih singaix,
sidu lang mai haktiex pa guah pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pon.
8 Haktiex in mawh sin ngao pa guah simiang pon ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom taug ang lai baox gut kheem ka pui cie aux;
nawh tag sang guah tix tawx haktiex pa deuk ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu heue.
Lox tat jhawm prawng pa caw simiang tix hawt
9 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Simiang Yi-seu-liex eue,
hoik kub heue!
Paw lai baox yuh kaing pa ang khaw gut kheem ka kawn pui;
tag caw tix jhi lih ka pa rauh pa see,
yuh khaw caw.
Pex ang pon tix lai baox khin ka pui cie aux lih hu khaing haktiex kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied laong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
10 c“Kawn pui ku kaux tag caw teei khaox diag pa rauh pa see tiag ka khawm uik heue.
11 “ ‘Yi-fax’ pa tong ka ceu pa koe mu tag caw tix kieh khaw mai ‘Pa-theux’ pa tong ka ceu pa ang chix giex ka mu,
mai kieh ‘Haw-me-euxd’ mawh diag tong yuh nin:
“Tix ‘Haw-me-eux’ mawh kao ‘Yi-fa’,
mai mawh nang kao ‘Pa-theux’ heue.
12 “Pa sawn ka diag tong krax cien mawh:
Tix ‘Seu-kheu-leuxe’ mawh tix nga ‘Ci-la;’
Tix ‘Mi-nax’ mawh tix gliah ‘Seu-kheu-leux’ heue.
13-15 “Pex tag caw tix hawt tix tan lu awm been pa hoik tat plak greem yuh nin:
Nghox ca:
pa pon tix gliah son tix son
nghox ca pa lang mu:
pa pon tix gliah son tix son
bee sime:
pa pon tix yieh son tix son
In kix tag caw tix teei ‘Pa-theux’ tiag ka khawm uik yuh nin:
kao ‘Pa-theux’ mawh tix mu ‘Haw-me-eux’,
mai mawh nang tix mu ‘Kheu-eux’ heue.
“Yung:
yung pa liang ka daux bang liang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
ra yieh mu tag caw tix tan lu tix mu heue.
“Pex tag caw tix tan lu ka Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
yuh nin,
phi map pex tom taug pon krax pa pluat ploi.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
16 “Kawn pui meung haktiex khawm uik tag caw tix viex krax tan in kix hoek ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex dee an.
17 Yam haok khix tix ngaix,
singaix tuah,
khawm singaix poi tang ceu khankix,
simiang tag caw tix mawh cao pa kuang kia ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai Krax-tan Pa-jauh pa tan lu kawn pui meung haktiex khawm uik.
Nawh tag sang rien khre ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map,
ka Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
son sang pluat ploi ka phi map pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
Poi
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 12.1–20; Lai Li-we 23.33–43)
18 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Yam haok khix tix tix ngaix,
maix tag caw tix tan lu kawn moi mhaing pa mhawm loig tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
pa si ghrah nyiex buan singax.
19 Cao giex kaing li tag caw tix teei nham Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map,
kraix ka rong siviex nyiex buan,
ka rung khu khawm pon rung,
mai ka piang rong siviex ka dee laig ka diang siviex ting brawk plak daux.
20 Yam haok khix an aliah ngaix,
maix kawx tag caw tix yuh krax tan son pui pa ang su tix yuh phi map,
phan ang mawh nan pui pa yuh phi map kheu pa daox aw tix khankix.
Yuh nin,
maix tom taug chix pon kieh nyiex buan singax singeh ang jud heue.
21 f“Jauh yam haok khix tix kao pon singaix,
maix tag caw tix yuh Poi Luat-luan aliah singaix.
Ka daux aliah singaix in,
pui ku kaux tag caw tix ih ka khao mun pa ang koe su saix.
22 Yam yuh poi ka daux tix singaix,
simiang tag caw tix tan lu moi mhaing tix mu son to kaux tix mai son pui meung,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map.
23 Ka daux poi aliah singaix,
nawh tag sang tan lu moi mhaing pa mhawm loig aliah mu,
yung mhaing aliah mu ka Simiang Siyiex,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
Nawh kawx tag sang tan lu ka pex mhaing tix ngaix tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map.
24 Moi mhaing ku mu,
yung mhaing ku mu tag sang tan lu tau mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi kao aliah kongsheng plak,
mai bee sime loe kongsheng heue.
25 g“Yam yuh poi nyiex gox bang,
pa mawh yam jauh haok khix aliah kao phuan singaix,
ka daux aliah singaix,
simiang tag sang hawt ka been pa tat Poi Luat-luan,
mai yuh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map,
Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai bee sime ku singaix heue.”
