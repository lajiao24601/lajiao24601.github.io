Krax tuah priex preem pa mawh pa meh
58
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix kok yo lox pa ting si num,
awm luah lox phlu teet tawng yuh nan,
paw vi kieh jud.
Tag caw tix krai pao phi map kix ka pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
2 Kix krub ka aux ku ngaix,
mai ce tix hoik tawng daux krax aux,
ce meung tix mawh pa rauh pa see,
mawh meung pa hawt ka jhawm prawng aux.
Kix tag caw tix khiox khaw aux tawx lox tat jhawm prawng pa khaw caw ka kix,
son sang jhi kix grawng pa muh tix krub ka aux heue.”
3 Pui Yi-seu-liex chawk ah nin:
“Phan Simiang Siyiex ang simi ka ex,
ex kawn tuah yuh pa tix ka priex preem lie?
Phan nawh ang jhak ka ex,
ex kawn yuh tix kha rhawm yuh ka mawx lie?”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tuah nang priex preem,
yuh nang kaing cie to kaux tix,
khaix an gut kheem ka pui pa yuh kaing khankix.
4 Krax pa tuah pex priex preem nie kieh pex cau nyhau,
lawh maw,
yuh simah.
Pex ce tix tuah priex preem yuh nin aux chix nghiet ka lox rawg pex buan lie?
5 Yam tuah pex priex preem,
kha ka to kaux tix,
laux rhawm laux rhi,
awm tai pa dex sang khrum yuh nan.
Pex tawm man keei sum piang tiex,
rhuat ka nyaox nyia,
khaix an it ka piang.
In mawh krax pa tuah ka priex preem lie?
Pex ce aux sang chix khaw rhawm ka krax pa tuah priex preem yuh nin lie?
6 “Krax tuah priex preem pa khaw rhawm aux mawh:
pa tik ka maox ceng krax gut krax kheem,
mai beeh glawm pa ang khaw caw,
mai pa pluat ploi pui pa gut kheem pui khankix heue.
7 aTag caw guah tix tawx priex preem ka pui pa cai rhawm;
tawx pui hot pui sibhawm pa ang koe nyiex koe ma ot ka nyiex pex;
tawx pui pa ot ka kaong ceep ka kraung ceep;
paw vi tieb tie ka pui pa ang koe cao teem cao coi heue.
8 “Yuh nin,
aux tom taug muh ka pex,
awm pa riang pon ngawb pa jhan riang ka piang pex yuh nan;
khaix mat pex tom taug pon pai tix moi dum mawx dum an.
Aux tag sang taoh ka pex ku bawg ku yam.
9 Pex rawg buan,
aux tag nghiet lox rawg pex;
pex khiox khaw,
aux tag cu lox pex heue.
“Phan pex naox grawng pa gut kheem ka pui,
ne raw ka pui,
salao ka pui;
10 phan pex tawx pui pa cai rhawm sawm,
kieh pui pa hot pa sibhawm yaox tix naog rhawm,
pa faik pa koe ka sivie siveng pex tag tieh tix lih mawh pa riang,
pon sawm tag sang lih mawh pon singaix.
11 Aux tag sang nied tix duk thung ka pex,
tawx pex naog rhawm ka kraung khrai,
kieh pex ting riang ting rao,
pon jaux ka daux pa kroh,
awm si peem pa koe dee keet rawm,
awm dee keet rawm pa ang chix hit.
12 Pex tag sang deeh cox sum nyiex ka piang dee riem,
mai pon krax haok huan ka piang riah pa dawg koe sivoe an.
Ceu kaux pex tag sang koe leeing ju leeing phan,
mawh kheu hoik deeh cox sum pex guh veng ot ka dee riem,
deeh cox yuh ka nyiex ma heue.”
Khawng phak ka daux pa priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah
13 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Phan pex priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah mai kieh mawh singaix pa singax singeh,
paw vi yuh kaing cie to kaux tix daux singaix an.
Phan pex yung yi ka singaix pa singax singeh in,
paw vi lih hu khaing nyiex ma tix ka daux singaix an,
paw vi yuh kaing,
krai lox pa ang koe ceu koe cak,
14 pex tom taug chix pon krax pa khaw rhawm aux.
Aux tag sang kieh pon krax pa yung yi ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
mai pex tag sang pon krax gaux rhawm ka daux haktiex pa tawx aux ka tax du pex Ya-keux.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
