Ang sawg krax pa naog rhawm to kaux tix
15
Ex pui pa kaux cong krax yum rhawm,
tag caw tix teem coi ka pui pa iak krax yum rhawm,
guah tix glawm beeh glawm kix,
mai paw vi sawg krax pa naog rhawm to kaux tix.
2 Ex ku kaux tag caw tix keud sawn ceu pa mhawm son kawn Khrit tang kaux,
mai yuh krax pa grum rhawm tau mai paox tix ka daux krax yum rhawm.
3 aMawh kheu Khrit ang sawg krax pa naog rhawm to kaux tix,
nawh tieh tix awm lox pa krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Krax pa jhax lawg pui pa jhax lawg ka maix hoik cawd ka piang to kaux aux khawm uik.”
4 Gum lox ka daux phuk lai sigang khri,
hoik mawh pa tiiem son sang gau ka ex khawm uik,
kaing ting hoik mawh son sang kieh ex pon tix koe krax keud pot jun pot ka daux krax gun yeung,
mai ka daux krax pa joh jhan pa tawx phuk lai sigang khri.
5 Kieh Siyiex kaw pa tawx krax gun yeung an,
mai krax pa joh jhan an teem coi pex koe rhawm keud pa khaw paox tix yuh nan,
mai gau tix ka kaing mhawm pa yuh Ye-sux Khrit,
6 son sang kieh pui hun ot tau,
mai rom paox tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka keeing Siyiex cie simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue.
Sidiex gum lox buan ka pui tang ceu tang cad
7 Mawh kheu nan,
kheu son min pak ngai Siyiex,
pex ra lai rieb ka paox tix,
awm hoik rieb Khrit pex yuh nan.
8 Aux krai ka pex,
Khrit hoik mawh siliex pui Yo-thaix son sang jhi pa kaux sijax Siyiex,
son sang yuh lih ka gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex ka cao giex kaing ceu pui tix khankix,
9 bmai kieh pui tang ceu tang cad pon krax caox rhawm Siyiex nang.
Awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw maix ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad;
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ceu kaux maix heue.
10 cPhuk lai sigang khri baox ah nin:
pui tang ceu tang cad eue!
Tag caw tix rom tix gaux rhawm tau mai pui cie Siyiex heue.
11 dBaox ah nin:
can pui tang ceu tang cad eue!
Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
kawn pui piang haktiex eue!
Pex tag caw tix yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
12 eYi-sai-yax kawx ah nin:
doeh ceu cad Yie-si tag sang lih hoek,
nawh tag sang hoek tix giex kaing piang pui tang ceu tang cad;
pu tang ceu tang cad tag sang een rhawm tix ka nawh khawm uik heue.
13 Kieh Siyiex,
pa mawh riah rawng krax keud pon jun pot an,
kheu krax pa yum rhawm pex ka nawh,
kieh pex naog krax pa gaux rhawm,
krax pa nyawm rhawm ku ceu,
kieh krax keud pot jun pot pex,
baox nie thiem thie tix ka daux riang rao Khuan Singax Singeh ang jud ang jai heue.
Pao-law krai lox sidawng
14 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
to kaux aux hoik tawng sidawng ka grawng pa hoik naog pex ka krax pa mhawm rhawm rhi,
mai grawng pa hoik grum koe pex ka krax tawng krax yung,
hoik ra lai pon sidiex ka paox tix.
15 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux lox lai in,
aux ca kawn viex rhawm ting krai ka pex grawh grawng kaing tix ceu ceu.
Aux krai lox sidawng,
mawh kheu hoik tawx Siyiex siawp sibrawm ka aux,
16 kieh aux lih mawh siliex Ye-sux Khrit,
mai yuh kaing ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad.
Aux hoik awm cao giex kaing li pa krai pao ka gum lox buan Siyiex yuh nan,
son sang kieh pui tang ceu tang cad lih tix mawh khawng phak krax pa tan Khuan Singax Singeh,
mawh pa khaw caw rhawm rhi Siyiex.
17 Yuh nin,
mawh kheu ka daux Ye-sux Khrit,
aux hoik chix tix pon min plak ngai kheu pa yuh tix kaing jhaw Siyiex.
18-19 Pa hoik viex aux rhawm ting krai mawh ngie gum lox in tix gum khaing ah nin:
Khrit teei lox pa krai aux,
kaing pa yuh aux,
mai baox teei mhai pa daox pa am,
riang rao khuan Siyiex kieh pui tang ceu tang cad cu nghiet ka Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
jauh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tom hoek ka haktiex Yi-li-li-kux,
aux hoik tauh sidiex ka gum lox buan pa dex dawt mai Khrit.
20 Pa hoik dawg simie rhawm rhi aux,
mawh sang tawx lox buan Khrit ka haktiex dee ang nyang sax mhong,
son ang kaing yuh aux sang sum tix ka piang riah rawng cie pui tang kaux khankix.
21 fPhuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
pui pa ang sax yaox ka nawh tag sang yaox;
pui pa ang sax mhong ka nawh tag sang doh rhawm heue.
Pao-law keud tix sang hu vhi ka Law-ma
22 gViang mawh nan,
aux hoik si pup krax pa tieb tie tom nie bawg,
mai ang pon yaox tix hu ka pex dee an.
23 Viang mawh nan,
yam in,
kheu hoik si dauh kaing yuh aux ka daux haktiex in,
kheu nan aux hoik dawg simie tix hu vhi ka pex.
24 Aux simie tix hu yam in,
yam hu aux ka daux krax hu plak Si-pang-ya,
mai veh tix jhak ka pex,
khaix hoik gaux rhawm tix ot tau mai pex dee an tix baux bawg yam,
khaix an kieh pex teem coi tix hu krax.
25 hViang mawh nan,
son sang teem coi ka kawn Khrit,
yam in aux tag sang haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
26 Mawh kheu ceu cao Ma-chi-toeng mai Ya-kai-yax gaux rhawm tix sang tawx mau,
teem coi ka pui hot pa mawh kawn Khrit pa koe daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
27 iIn hoik mawh pa gaux rhawm kix tix yuh,
tete te lai,
hoik mawh pa caw kix tix yuh.
Kheu hoik guah pui Yo-thaix pa mawh kawn Khrit tix tawx khawng phak buan son khuan ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
pui tang ceu tang cad kawx tag caw tix teem coi kix ka daux kraung khrai.
28 Krox aux yuh kaing in hoik si dauh,
khaix hoik tawx aux mau pa tan kix sidawng,
aux tag sang hu ka pex dee an,
mai hoek ka Si-pang-ya.
29 Aux hoik tawng,
yam hu aux vhi ka pex,
aux kawx tag sang teei tawx buan son pa naog grum cie Khrit ka pex heue.
30 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux khiox khaw pex ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit,
mai ka daux krax muh Khuan Singax Singeh,
kheu son aux,
pex sau riang tix khiox khaw tau mai aux ka Siyiex.
31 Son ang sang kieh aux yaox krax pa tuk tiak pui Yo-thaix pa ang koe krax yum rhawm khankix,
son sang kieh kaing krax teem coi aux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung pon pa khaw rhawm kawn Khrit.
32 Phan Siyiex cu,
aux tag sang chix gaux rhawm tix hu jhak ka pex,
mai laux lee tix ka daux pex tix baux bawg yam heue.
33 Kieh krax nhim pa tawx Siyiex ot tau mai pex ku kaux heue!
A-meung.
