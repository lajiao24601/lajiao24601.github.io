Gum lox pa tawx ka ceu cao Yi-fu-saw
2
“Maix tag caw tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka kawn cau Siyiex ceu cao Yi-fu-saw ah nin:
“ ‘Kaw an pa giex simuing aliah mu ka daux taix khawn dawm tix,
pa hu lai ling ka si nax jawk kawk ngu khri aliah mu krai ah nin:
2 Aux hoik tawng kaing pa yuh maix.
Hoik taug mao maix yuh ka mawx,
hoik gun yeung maix yuh ka mawx;
aux kawx hoik tawng grawng ang maix va rhawm ka pui caox,
maix hoik sax cheu tix yaox ka pui pa tang kok tix mawh kawn cau khankix,
tete kix ang mawh kawn cau,
mai hoik tawng grawng mawh kix pui pie lhien.
3 Maix hoik koe rhawm gun rhawm yeung,
mai hoik sax cim krax kha rhawm kheu son aux,
ang sax yawn tix deeh tix bawg kawx.
4 Viang mawh nan,
koe kaing tix ceu aux tag sang ne raw ka maix:
pa mawh maix hoik kieh krax muh krax rhawm tix ka aux pa koe yam jauh grai.
5 Maix bre tix keud,
maix hoik koe krax caox caig miex ding heue!
Maix tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
kieh krax muh pa yuh maix tom yam jauh deeh lih tix koe.
Phan maix ang loh rhawm rhi tix,
aux tag sang hoek,
mai teei ka jawk kawk ngu cie maix hu khaing dee ot.
6 Viang mawh nan,
maix koe ceu pa mhawm tix ceu:
maix hoik koeng awm koeng aux ka kaing pa yuh cub caw Ni-keu-la heue.
7 a“ ‘Lox pa krai Khuan Singax Singeh ka ceu cao ku dauh mawh,
pa koe yhaok,
tag caw tix nghiet khawm uik heue!
“ ‘Phan mawh pui pa pex an,
aux tag sang tawx nawh koe siawp sibrawm ih ka plix khaox pa im ju an pa koe ka daux si peem gaux rhawm cie Siyiex heue.’ ”
Gum lox pa tawx ka ceu cao See-me-nax
8 b“Maix tag caw tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka kawn cau Siyiex ceu cao See-me-nax ah nin:
“ ‘Kaw pa jauh an mai pa si dauh,
pa yum an mai pa deeh kaoh im krai ah nin:
9 Aux hoik tawng grawng pa kha maix rhawm tix mai pa hot sibhawm maix;
viang mawh nan,
maix mawh pa mi pa koe heue!
Aux hoik tawng grawng pa kham maix krax pa map nai pui an kix pa tang kok tix mawh pui Yo-thaix;
tete te lai kix ang mawh pui Yo-thaix,
mawh cub caw cie Sa tan heue!
10 Paw vi lhat ka grawng sang si pup tix ka krax kha rhawm.
Jhak heue,
phi caox tag sang cheuk tix yaox ka pex,
mai teei pui tix blah ka daux pex sau laig daux thawng;
pex tag sang kham krax kha rhawm kao singaix.
Maix tag sang kaux sijax tom yum tix,
aux tom taug tawx mhok khri pa im ju ka maix heue.
11 c“ ‘Gum lox pa krai Khuan Singax Singeh ka ceu cao ku dauh mawh,
pa koe yhaok tag caw tix nghiet khawm uik heue!
“ ‘Pui pa puing pex an ang doh lai laux leeing ka daux krax yum bawg khaix heue.’ ”
Gum lox pa tawx ka ceu cao Piie-cia-mawx
12 “Maix tag caw tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka kawn cau ceu cao Piie-cia-mawx ah nin:
“ ‘Kaw pa koe vaig lawm khawm ra plak krai ah nin:
13 Aux hoik tawng dee paung ot maix mawh dee pa kok Sa tan mawh simiang.
Maix hoik kaux sijax ka aux,
mai hoik mawh yam kham aux sakse yam pauh pui Ang-thi-pha yum ka haktiex cie Sa tan,
maix kawx ang bax ploi krax pa yum rhawm ka aux.
14 dViang mawh nan,
koe kaing ceu tix blah aux sang ne raw ka maix:
ka daux pex koe pui hawt ka krax pa gau Pa-lang;
Pa-lang in nyaw glaw ka Pa-leux,
cheeik tix kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map,
ih ka priex preem pa krub ka riang rao,
mai baox yuh krax phit vao.
15 Awm an,
ka daux pex koe pui hawt ka krax pa sidiex gau Ni-keu-la.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
phan ang loh rhawm rhi tix,
aux tag sang khran hoek,
mai pauh pui in kix ka vaig pa lih khaing daux jheeh tix heue.
17 e“ ‘Lox pa krai Khuan Singax Singeh ka ceu cao ku dauh mawh,
pa koe yhaok,
kieh mhong khawm uik heue!
“ ‘Phan mawh ka son pui pa pex khankix,
aux tag sang tawx Ma-nax pa mox tix ot dee glip ka kix.
Aux kawx tag sang tawx simaox paing tix lon ka nawh,
ka piang simaox an tiiem ka ceu kaux pa khraox,
ceu kaux in lheu khaing pui pa rieb kah,
ang koe pui tang kaux pa tawng tix kaux kawx heue.’ ”
Gum lox pa tawx ka ceu cao Thoe-ya-thoe-la
18 “Maix tag caw tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka kawn cau Siyiex ceu cao Thoe-ya-thoe-la ah nin:
“ ‘Kawn simex Siyiex,
kaw pa toh riang tawm ngai awm rao ngu yuh nan,
caong hoik sawng awm tawng pa awt plang yuh nan,
krai yuh nin:
19 aux hoik tawng ceu pa yuh maix.
Aux hoik tawng krax muh krax rhawm maix,
krax yum rhawm maix,
krax krub pa yuh maix,
mai rhawm gun rhawm yeung maix.
Aux hoik tawng ceu pa yuh maix yam in kawn lheu khaing pa yuh maix sivoe an.
20 fViang mawh nan,
koe tix ceu aux tag sang ne raw ka maix:
maix hoik va rhawm ka Yie-si-pix bun an pa tang kok tix mawh cao jhai.
Nawh hoik nyaw glaw mai kieh siliex aux phit vao,
ih ka priex preem pa tan lu pui ka riang rao.
21 Aux hoik sax tawx bawg yam ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh loh rhawm rhi tix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang cu tix tik vud ka krax pa phit vao pa yuh tix.
22 Jhak heue,
aux tag sang kieh nawh saux it ka piang ku;
pui pa yuh krax pa phit vao tau mai nawh khankix,
lheu khaing loh kix rhawm rhi tix,
ang lai baox yuh krax pa phit vao tau mai nawh,
aux tag sang kieh kix kham krax kha rhawm pa ting tau mai nawh.
23 gAux kawx tag sang pauh ka pui pa hawt ka nawh,
ceu cao ku dauh tom taug tawng grawng pa hoik tawng aux sidawng ka rhawm keud kawn pui mai krax keud krax sawn kix.
Aux tag sang hawt tix pawk bix awm kaing pa hoik yuh pex ku kaux yuh nan heue.
24 “ ‘Kheu nan,
pex pa mawh pui pa deuk ka Thoe-ya-thoe-la ang lai hawt ka lox pa ang mawh ang meh,
ang lai gau tix ka ceu pa ah pui mawh pa raux pa mox cie Sa tan.
Aux krai ka pex,
aux ang lai baox teei tix tawx beeh glawm pa ciien piang pex.
25 Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix giex tom kaux cong ka ceu pa hoik koe pex,
tom hoik hoek aux.
26-28 hPhan mawh son pui pa pex an,
pa kawn pon priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx aux tom si dauh khankix,
aux tag sang tawx akhuang ka nawh,
awm hoik tawx keeing aux ka aux yuh nan.
Aux tag sang tawx akhuang ka nawh,
kieh nawh kuang ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik,
teei khaox gix rhiem kuang ka piang kix,
mai lauh kix max phawng awm lauh pui ka aw tiex max phawng yuh nan.
Aux kawx tag sang teei tix tawx simuing ka pui pa pex khankix heue.
29 “ ‘Gum lox pa krai Khuan Singax Singeh ka ceu cao ku dauh mawh,
pa koe yhaok tag caw tix nghiet khawm uik heue!’ ”
