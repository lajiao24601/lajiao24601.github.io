16
Kix grawng teug sijax mukheu laig daux nyiex gox bang pa hoik rien khre Ta-wue,
khaix an yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka Siyiex.
2 Khaix hoik hoik yuh Ta-wue krax tan,
tom nawb buan ka piang kawn pui ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
3 khaix an mai guah tix tawx kix ih priex preem.
Pui Yi-seu-liex,
simex bun ang ah,
hoik pon ka khao mun tix kaux tix lon,
nex tong tix khika,
mai plix siiet kroh tix blah heue.
4 Ta-wue tawx pui Li-we tix blah mawh cao pa thung kawn pui rhax lai ka plak sivoe ngai teug sijax mukheu,
yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
5 Ya-sa mawh cao bung kix,
Sa-cia-li-ya mawh cao bung kix ka daux ra kaux tix kaux,
Ya-shiie,
See-mi-la-maw,
Yie-shiie,
Ma-tha-thi-ya,
Yi-li-ya,
Pi-na-ya,
Eu-piie Yi-tong,
mai Yie-lix mawh cao pa tauh ting.
Ya-sa mawh cao pa tauh chiang.
6 Cao giex kaing li ra kaux,
pa mawh Pi-na-ya mai Ya-ha-si hawt bawg yam mawh cao pa phlu teet tawng ka plak sivoe ngai teug sijax mukheu.
7 In mawh daux bawg tix pa tawx Ta-wue Ya-sa mai paox ceu pui nawh pui Li-we,
mawh cao pa koe beeh glawm tix thung pui hoek rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
(Lai Yaog Yaw 105.1–15; 96.1–13; 106.1, 47–48)
8 Pex caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
krai grawng pa ting ceu nawh;
caw tix krai lao ka kaing yuh nawh ka pui kaing mheen meung.
9 Pex caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw nawh;
krai lao kaing pa daox deng yuh nawh.
10 Pex caw tix tawx min plak ngai ka ceu kaux pa singax singeh nawh;
kieh pui pa sawg tix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
11 Caw tix rawg mhaing Simiang Siyiex teem coi tix;
krub nawh ku si jheeing jheeng.
12-13 Kawn cau Siyiex doeh ceu cad Ya-keux eue,
kawn praix Yi-seu-liex pa hoik laug nawh eue,
pex caw tix doh keud ka kaing pa daox pa deng yuh nawh,
kieh tix tawng krax prawng ca pa yuh nawh.
14 Nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex;
siawp sibrawm nawh hoik tauh hu ka piang haktiex uik.
15 Pex caw tix giex kaux ka sijax mukheu nawh leeing ju;
gum lox pa hoik deuh nawh hoek tom kaing rheng ju.
16 bNawh tag sang giex kaux ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tix tau mai Ya-paw-la-hang,
giex tom kaux cong ka gum lox pa hoik tiex tu tix ka Yi-sha.
17 cSimiang Siyiex mai pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh ka sijax mukheu leeing ju leeing phan,
hoik tat tix deuh jhawm prawng tau mai Ya-keux leeing ju.
18 Nawh ah nin:
aux tag sang tawx haktiex Cia-nang ka maix,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie to kaux maix.
 
19 Kaux pui ceu pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex tux,
ot ka daux haktiex Cia-nang.
20 Kix jauh tix vu vie ka daux haktiex in hoek ka daux haktiex an,
jauh tix vu vie ka meung in hoek ka meung an.
21 dViang mawh nan,
Siyiex ang kieh pui tix kaux kawx nu nai ka kix;
mawh kheu jao kix nawh ne raw ka simiang.
22 Nawh ah nin:
ang pon tix kieh raw ka siliex pa hoik laug aux;
ang pon tix yuh laux ka cao jhai aux.
 
23 Piang haktiex khawm uik tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex ku ngaix!
Tag caw tix krai pao ka lox buan krax taoh nawh.
24 Tag caw tix krai min plak ngai nawh ka meung piang haktiex;
tag caw tix krai lao kaing yuh nawh pa ting pa lhaong.
25 Simiang Siyiex kied ting lhaong,
khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw;
nawh khaw tix lheu pon krax lhat graung yung yi khaing siyiex piang haktiex khawm uik.
26 Siyiex meung ka piang haktiex mawh ngie riang rao krub khaing,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik phan yuh ka rao ma.
27 Siawp sibrawm pa riang min plak ngai vung viang ka nawh;
mai pa ting siawp sibrawm krax gaux rhawm hoik naog ka daux nyiex buan nawh.
 
28 Pui piang haktiex khawm uik tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
tag caw tix yaog yaw ka min plak ngai nawh,
mai pa ting siawp sibrawm nawh.
29 Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka ceu kaux min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex;
tag caw tix viex ka khawng phak lak sawng hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
Yam lih jhi Simiang Siyiex pa singax singeh tix,
tag caw gup tix krub ka nawhe;
30 haktiex khawm uik tag caw tix yaoh si yaung ka plak sivoe ngai nawh!
Haktiex sum tix kaux cong,
mai ang chix yung yang.
31 Gaux rhawm heue,
haktiex mai rao ma!
Tag caw tix krai pao ka meung piang haktiex:
Simiang Siyiex kuang ka piang khawm uik!
32 Kok yo lox pa si num heue,
rawm sigrit sigra mai ceu pa im ju pa ot ka daux;
gaux rhawm heue,
kaung ma mai plix nghox plix koi pa ot kaux daux.
33 Yam an khaox ox pa ot ka daux tiag,
tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoek kuang kia ka piang haktiex.
34 fTag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
kheu mawh nawh pa kied mhawm tete;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan!
35 Tag caw tix ah nin ka nawh:
Siyiex cao pa taoh ka ex eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka yix liak!
Khiox khaw maix deeh viex yix hoek khaing meung tang meung,
son sang yaog yaw yix ka maix,
kieh yix yaog yaw ka ceu kaux pa singax singeh maix.
36 Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex!
Tag caw tix yaog yaw nawh leeing ju leeing phan heue!
 
Khaix an kawn pui rieb tix pawk lox ah nin:
“A-meung!”
Kix hoik yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik heue.
Yuh krax krub ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka Ci-piien
37 Simiang Ta-wue tawx Ya-sa mai paox ceu paox cad nawh pui Li-we,
thung pui yuh kaing krub ka dee deuh teug sijax mukheu ot.
Kix caw tix ot yuh kaing dee an ku singaix.
38 Kawn simex Yie-tu-toeng pa kaux Eu-piie Yi-tong mai pui ceu nawh tix gliah daix kaux pa hoik tawx mawh pui teem coi kix.
Heu-sax mai Eu-piie Yi-tong mawh cao kaing pui pau siviex heue.
39 Viang mawh nan,
Sa-tu cao giex kaing li mai cao giex kaing li tang kaux mawh cao thung pui yuh kaing krub ka nyiex dee krub ka Ci-piien.
40 Ku ngaix pon ngawb pon bo,
kix tag sang hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tiiem Simiang Siyiex tix tawx ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu.
41 Pa ot tau mai kix mawh Si-mang,
Yie-tu-toeng,
mai pui tang kaux khankix;
kix hoik mawh pui pa jawg tix laug lih,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai yaog yaw krax muh Simiang Siyiex leeing ju leeing phan.
42 Si-mang mai Yie-tu-toeng kawx mawh nang cao pa koe beeh glawm tix phlu ka teet,
tauh ka chiang,
mai ka lox ting tang ceu khankix;
ting in kix hoik mawh pa tauh pa phlu yam rhax ka lox rhax.
Kawn saux Yie-tu-toeng mawh cao pa koe beeh glawm tix pau ka siviex heue.
43 gKhaix an can kawn pui khawm uik deeh ing ka nyiex tix;
Ta-wue kawx paung ot tau mai pui nyiex tix tix baux bawg yam heue.
