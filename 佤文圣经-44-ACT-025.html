Pao-law krai ka Vawng Law-ma
25
Yam mawh Fe-see-to simiang ka daux loe ngaix,
nawh kaoh khaing Khai-sha-li-yax haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai simiang pui Yo-thaix krai grawng pa deuh phi map piang Pao-law ka nawh.
Kix khiox khaw ka Fe-see-to,
3 nhie nawh tawx Pao-law hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu krax pa tieh vheen hoik kuan tix pauh ka nawh lawng krax.
4 Fe-see-to pawk lox ah nin:
“Yam in Pao-law hoik laung tix ot ka Khai-sha-li-yax,
ang rheeng tag sang deeh hoek dee an.
5 Simiang pex chix tix hu tau mai aux,
phan pui an yuh kaing ceu pa ang khaw,
chix tix deuh phi map ka nawh dee an heue.”
6 Fe-see-to baox ot mai kix dee an daix dim kao ngaix,
khaix an deeh ing ka Khai-sha-li-yax.
Ka daux ra singaix nawh tom prawng ca,
mai tawx lox nhie kix viex Pao-law hoek.
7 Pao-law hoek,
pui Yo-thaix pa kaoh khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung cung sivie siveng nawh,
mai deuh phi map pa cien piang nawh tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng ceu pa sang teei phi map dee lud nawh.
8 Pao-law krai lox son to kaux tix ah nin:
“Aux ang bax yuh kaing pa phit lud ka lox tat jhawm prawng pui Yo-thaix,
ang bax salao ka nyiex buan,
mai ang bax yuh krax phit ka Vawng Law-ma heue.”
9 Viang mawh nan,
Fe-see-to yuh son sang khaw rhawm pui Yo-thaix,
nawh tom chawk Pao-law ah nin:
“Cu maix tix haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai rieb ka krax prawng aux ka see in dee an lie?”
10 Pao-law ah nin:
“Yam in aux cung ka daux nyiex prawng see Vawng;
dee in hoik mawh dee caw aux tix kham krax prawng.
Maix kawx hoik tawng,
aux ang bax phit ka pui Yo-thaix.
11 Phan aux lud,
koe phi map pa caw tix kieh yum,
aux ang doh chix mox tix khaing krax pa ciyang in.
Phan krax pa deuh kix phi map piang aux ang mawh tete,
ang koe pui pa pon tawx aux ka kix.
Aux tag sang krai ka Vawng heue.”
12 Khaix hoik kum kawng Fe-see-to tau mai pui pa ot greem tix,
nawh ah nin:
“Phan maix sang krai ka Vawng,
maix hu ka nawh dee an heue.”
Fe-see-to krai grawh grawng Pao-law ka simiang Ya-ci-phax
13 Khaix hoik luan ka daux singaix tix blah,
simiang Ya-ci-phax mai Pe-ni-see hoek rieb Fe-see-to ka Khai-sha-li-yax.
14 Khaix hoik ot kix dee an ra lox singaix,
Fe-see-to krai grawh grawng Pao-law ka simiang Ya-ci-phax,
ah nin:
“Dee in koe pui tix kaux,
nawh mawh pui laung pa prah Fe-li-see.
15 Yam haok aux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung bawg sivoe,
simiang cao giex kaing li pui Yo-thaix plak in mai kuad song khankix deuh phi map piang nawh,
mai nhie aux prawng ka phi map nawh.”
16 “Aux krai ka kix,
phan hawt ka been Law-ma,
sivoe sang kieh kiex ra kaux krai lox tau,
sivoe sang krai lox son to kaux tix,
ang pon tix nyang prawng.
17 Khaix hoik krox kix hoek dee in,
aux ang bax gla ka bawg yam,
ka daux ra singaix tag sang prawng ca,
mai tawx lox nie pui an krai lox pa sang pawk tix.
18 Pui pa deuh phi map piang an hoik yaog tix kaoh cung deuh phi map piang nawh khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
pa krai kix ang bax mawh phi map pa keud aux daux rhawm tix.
19 Pa thung thiang kix ka nawh mawh grawh grawng pa dex dawt mai been li,
khawm pui pa kaux Ye-sux tix kaux;
pui in hoik yum,
viang mawh nan,
Pao-law krai kawn im nawh.
20 Son see in ang tawng ka sang yuh tix.
Aux chawk Pao-law cu ang nawh cu tix haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kham tix rieb krax prawng dee an.
21 Viang mawh nan,
Pao-law khiox khaw prah nawh ot,
kieh Vawng prawng.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tawx lox nhie kix prah nawh ot daux thawng,
krox jun tix kah nawh hoek ka Vawng dee an heue.”
22 Ya-ci-phax krai ka Fe-see-to ah nin:
“Aux kawx simie tix nghiet ka lox krai pui in heue.”
Fe-see-to ah nin:
“Pa sax maix yaox tix mhong heue.”
23 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
Ya-ci-phax mai Pe-ni-see yuh tix koe jhawm mai laig daux nyiex ting;
pui pa hoek tau mai nawh koe simiang naing,
mai pui pa koe min ka daux veng.
Fe-see-to tawx lox tix gum,
kix tom viex Pao-law laig.
24 Fe-see-to ah nin:
“Simiang Ya-ci-phax mai pui pa ngawm dee in eue!
Pex jhak ka pui in;
nawh mawh pui pa deuh pui Yo-thaix khawm pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa krai grawng pa deuh phi map piang aux,
khiox khaw tix pauh ka nawh.
25 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang yaox phi map pa yuh nawh pa khaw tix prawng nawh yum;
kheu nan nawh kawn sang krai ka Vawng,
aux tom but tix kah nawh viex nawh hu.
26 Mawh ngie phan mawh son pui in,
aux ang koe pa tiiem sidawng grawng nawh son sang krai ka Vawng,
mawh kheu nan,
aux viex nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai pui pa ot dee in ku kaux,
mai ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Ya-ci-phax,
son sang koe lox pa krai khaix hoik si kaw tix yaox ka see in.
27 Phan aux keud tix yaox,
grawng see pa ang sidawng pa kah tix viex pui laung hu,
hoik mawh kaing pa ang khaw heue.”
