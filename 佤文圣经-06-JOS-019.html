Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Si-miien
19
Bawg cawk lai ra bawg tix bawg,
mawh pa guah haktiex ka ceu pui Si-miien ku nyiex.
Haktiex kix laig ka daux haktiex ceu pui Yo-tax,
2 akoe Piie-see-pa,
See-pa,
Maw-la-tax,
3 Ha-sa Su-yax,
Pa-la,
Yi-seeng,
4 Yi-li-to-la,
Pi-thu-lix,
Heu-eu-ma,
5 Si-keu-la,
Paw Ma-cia-pawx,
Ha-sa Su-sha,
6 Paw Li-pa-wux,
mai Sa-lu-siien,
khawm uik koe kao loe mu veng,
mai yaong pa ot hawt vung viang heue.
7 Lheu khaing an,
kawn koe Ya-ying,
Li-meung,
Yi-thie,
mai Ya-sang pon mu veng mai yaong pa ot hawt vung viang veng heue.
8 Yaong pa ot hawt vung viang veng khawm uik,
tom hoek plak seh Pa-la Pi-eu (pa mawh La-max),
hoik mawh ka khawm uik.
Haktiex in kix plak piang mawh sin ngao pa pon ceu pui Si-miien ku nyiex heue.
9 Mawh kheu haktiex pa pon ceu pui Yo-tax kawn nie khaing pa sang cie kix,
tix blah tag guah ka ceu pui Si-miien heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Si-pu-loeng
10 Pa cawk lai loe bawg tix bawg,
hoik mawh haktiex pa caw tix guah ka ceu pui Si-pu-loeng ku nyiex.
Haktiex kix hu tom Sa-lix heue.
11 Mee haktiex plak klip singaix jauh ka Sa-lix hu plak klip singaix hoek ka Ma-la-lax.
Toe tix ka Ta-pa-seux mai rawm klawng Yaw-nien plak lih singaix,
12 mee plak lih singaix jauh ka Sa-lix hu plak lih singaix hoek ka daux mee Cie-see-lui Tha-pawx,
mai baox hoek ka Ta-pi-la,
mai baox hoek ka Ya-fe-yax,
13 baox jauh ka dee an hu plak lih singaix hoek ka Cia-theux Si-fu mai Yi-theu Cia-shuing,
mai veeh tix hoek ka Ling-meung veng Ni-yax heue.
14 Mee haktiex plak laong jauh ka dee an veeh tix hoek ka Ha-na-toeng,
tom hoek ka daux ngawd Yi-fu-tha Yi-leu.
15 Haktiex kix mawh khawm Cia-tha,
Na-ha-la,
Seung-loeng,
Yi-ta-la,
mai Paw-li-haung kao ra veng mai yaong pa ot hawt vung viang heue.
16 Veng mai yaong in kix mawh sin ngao pa pon cie pui Si-pu-loeng ku nyiex heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Yi-sa-cia
17 Pa cawk lai pon bawg tix bawg mawh haktiex pa guah ka ceu pui Yi-sa-cia ku nyiex.
18 Haktiex kix mawh khawm Yie-see-liex,
Ci-su-luix,
su-niang,
19 Ha-fu-liien,
See-ang,
Ya-na-ha-la,
20 La-pix,
Ci-sang,
Ya-piie,
21 Li-miiex,
Yin Kang-niang,
Yin Ha-ta,
mai Paw Pha-pix.
22 Haktiex kix bleeh hoek tom Tha-paw,
Sa-ha-si-ma,
mai Paw See-mai,
tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
khawm uik koe kao liah mu veng mai yaong pa ot hawt vung viang heue.
23 Veng mai yaong in kix mawh sin ngao pa pon ceu pui Yi-sa-cia ku nyiex heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Ya-seux
24 Pa cawk lai phuan bawg tix bawg mawh haktiex pa guah ka ceu pui Ya-seux ku nyiex.
25 Haktiex kix mawh khawm He-cia,
Ha-lix,
Pi-thiien,
Ya-sa,
26 Ya-la-mi-leux,
Ya-mawx,
mai Mi-sa-leux.
Mee haktiex plak klip singaix bleeh hoek tom Cia-mix mai Si-heu Li-na,
27 plak lih singaix hoek tom Paw Ta-koeng,
toe tix mai Si-pu-loeng mai daux ngawd Yi-fu-tha Yi-leu,
hoek Paw Yi-mawx mai veng Ni-yiex plak laong,
baox hu plak laong hoek tom Cia-pu-leu.
28 Kawn koe,
Yi-paw-loeng,
Li-haw,
Ha-meung,
mai Cia-na,
tom hoek ka Si-toeng heue.
29 Baox jauh dee in veeh tix hoek ka La-max,
hoek ka veng pa kaux pa cong Thai-eux,
khaix an veeh tix hu ka Heu-sax,
tom hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
Haktiex kix mawh khawm Ma-he-la,
Ya-keu-si,
30 Wu-ma,
Ya-fu,
mai Li-haw,
khawm uik koe tix nga ra mu veng mai yaong pa ot hawt vung viang.
31 Veng mai yaong in kix mawh sin ngao pa pon ceu pui Ya-seux ku nyiex heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix
32 Pa cawk lai liah bawg tix bawg mawh haktiex pa pon ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix ku nyiex.
33 Mee haktiex cie kix,
jauh tix ka Si-li-fux mai hoek ka Sa-na-ying dee koe khaox yang yit,
baox hoek ka Ya-ta-mi Ni-cie mai Ya-pi-niiex,
bleeh hoek La-kong,
tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
34 Mee haktiex jauh ka dee in veeh tix hu plak klip singaix,
hoek ka Ya-see-na Tha-paw,
baox hoek ka Hu-keux,
plak seh hoek ka Si-pu-loeng,
plak klip singaix hoek ka Ya-seux,
plak lih singaix hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tangb.
35 Mai veng pa kaux pa cong koe Si-ting,
Cheu-eux,
Ha-mawx,
La-cia,
Ci-ni-liex,
36 Ya-ta-ma,
La-max,
Sha-saw,
37 Ci-ti-see,
Yi-teu-lai,
Yin Sha-saw,
38 Yi-li-veng,
Mi-ta Yi-leux,
Heu-liien,
Paw Ya-na,
mai Paw See-mai,
khawm uik koe kao dim mu veng mai yaong pa ot hawt vung viang.
39 Veng mai yaong in kix hoik mawh sin ngao cie ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix ku nyiex heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Tang
40 Pa cawk lai aliah bawg tix bawg mawh haktiex pa pon ceu pui Tang ku nyiex.
41 Haktiex kix mawh khawm Saw-la,
Yi-seu-thao,
Yi-eu See-mai,
42 Sa-la-ping,
Ya-ya-loeng,
Yi-thi-la,
43 Yi-loeng,
Thing-na,
Yi-keu-loeng,
44 Yi-li-thi-ci,
Ci-pi-toeng,
Pa-la,
45 Yi-hu-teu,
mai Pi-ni Pi-la,
Cia-theux Ling-meung,
46 Me Yie-khuing,
La-khoeng,
mai baoh haktiex Yaw-pha heue.
47 cYam tik pui ceu pui Tang ka haktiex,
kix hu puing ka Li-sang,
mai puing pex kah,
mai pauh ka pui pa ot dee an uik,
cax ka haktiex an.
Kix ot ka dee an,
siao ka ceu kaux tax du kix Tang,
mai loh veng Li-sang kaux Tang.
48 Veng mai yaong in kix mawh sin ngao pa pon ceu pui Tang ku nyiex khawm uik heue.
Haktiex pa nghoik tix guah
49 Khaix hoik guah pui Yi-seu-liex ka haktiex,
kix tawx kawn simex Noeng pa mawh Yaw-su-yax cie haktiex tix dauh,
kieh mawh sin ngao nawh.
50 Kix hawt ka gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
tawx veng pa mhaing nawh ka nawh,
pa mawh Thing-na Si-la pa ot ka gawng Yi-fa-liien.
Yaw-su-yax cox ka veng an,
ot ka dee an heue.
51 Cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Noeng pa mawh Yaw-su-yax,
khawm simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui,
ku nyiex pui ot ka See-law,
cawk lai dee diang sivie nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
chawk ka Simiang Siyiex,
guah ka haktiex.
Yuh nin,
kix hoik hoik ka kaing pa guah tix haktiex heue.
