Lai Ha-kai
Krai dawt
Yam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng ka daux ra num, pa mawh sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num phuan yieh tix nga num, Siyiex kieh cao jhai Ha-kai krai gum lox pa dawt tix blah lih. Lai Ha-kai mawh pa khrawm tix tiiem ka gum lox in kix lih. Yam an, pui Yi-seu-liex kaoh khaing haktiex pa vuk vied vuk viang tix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik kied rheeng, viang mawh nan nyiex buan ca kawn riem. Gum lox pa koe ka daux phuk lai in dhiet simiang ka daux pui hun khankix nied tix cox sum ka nyiex buan, khaix an krai sidawng ka gum lox pa hoik deuh Simiang Siyiex ah nin: tag caw tix kieh pui pa loh tix lih mawh pui khraox, mai pui pa singax khankix pon krax nyawm ot nyawm koe pa haok huan ka daux singaix pa sang hoek heue.
Krai kaing
1. Simiang Siyiex tawx lox cox sum ka nyiex buan (1.1–15)
2. Lox pa khu rhawm mai krax keud pot jun pot (2.1–23)
