Pa-pi-loeng laux leeing
18
Khaix an,
aux yaox kawn cau Siyiex tang kaux lih hoek khaing rao ma.
Nawh giex ka siawp sibrawm pa ting;
pa riang nawh jhan ka piang haktiex.
2 aNawh kok yo ka lox si num ah nin:
“Hoik ghrup heue!
Pa-pi-loeng pa ting hoik ghrup heue!
Nawh lih tix mawh dee ot phi caox,
mai daux dee laux lee khuan pa caox pa caig tix,
mheem sim pa seeing brai.
3 bMawh kheu hoik nyaux meung piang haktiex ka plai khien cie bun pa ting krax phit vao an,
simiang piang haktiex yuh krax pa phit vao tau mai nawh,
pui pa yuh lai gee piang haktiex hoik mag mi khaing krax pa phit vao nawh heue.”
4 cAux baox mhong lox pa lih hoek khaing rao ma ah nin:
Lih heue,
pui cie aux heue!
Lih khaing daux veng heue!
Phan ang mawh nan,
pex taug sang seeing ka phi map sibun an,
mai guah tix glawm krax pa laux leeing nawh heue!
5 dMawh kheu hoik kied ting phi map nawh.
Siyiex tiiem tix deuh ka krax pa caox pa caig yuh nawh.
6 eYuh nawh pex yuh ka mawx,
pex yuh nan nang ka nawh,
mai baox hawt tix thiem thie piang pa yuh nawh ra son.
Hoik yuh nawh pex nyaux plai yuh ka mawx,
pex deeh teei plai ra son prauk naog ka kawk cie nawh heue!
7 fHoik nie luan yuh nawh,
yaw nawh to kaux tix yuh ka mawx,
pex tag map nai nawh,
mai kieh nawh kha tix tom nyawm nyiem yuh nan.
Mawh kheu ca krai nawh ang jud ka to kaux tix ah nin:
Aux mawh moeng simiang pa ngawm ka piang dang ngeung,
aux ang mawh miex mai,
aux ang doh chix laux rhawm heue!
8 Mawh kheu jao in yuh nan,
ka daux tix singaix,
krax pa laux leeing tag sang lih hoek piang nawh:
Koe sibhawm praix,
krax laux rhawm,
krax yum heue!
Nawh tag gleeing ka daux ngu,
mawh kheu kied ting siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex pa prawng ca ka nawh heue!
9 gSimiang piang haktiex pa hoik phit vao tau mai nawh khankix,
yaox ka taox ngu pa gleeing ka nawh,
kix tom laux rhawm yiiem son nawh.
10 Kix lhat ka yaox grawng pa kha nawh rhawm tix,
mai kok cung tix jhak ka dee singai ah nin:
“Hoik laux leeing heue!
Hoik laux leeing heue!
Veng ting in.
Veng Pa-pi-loeng pa mhawm grang eue,
ang lai koe tix taix nari,
maix hoik si pup krax ciyang heue!”
11 hPui pa yuh lai gee ka piang haktiex kawx hoik laux rhawm yiiem son nawh,
mawh kheu ang lai baox koe pui pa hoek tix liag ka kraung coh kix.
12 iAng koe pui pa liag ka khri,
mau,
siang simaox,
mai siang ploi cie kix.
Ang koe pui pa liag man keei sum,
keei luat,
mai man pa raoh mai man pa raoh pliex pa ngawh cie kix,
khaox bhawng ku ceu,
kraung khrai pa yuh ka rhang sang ku ceu,
khaox pa ngawh,
kraung tawng,
kraung rhiem,
mai kraung pa yuh ka simaox pa mhawm plang.
13 Ang lai koe nang pui pa liag ka ro kui,
ceu pa yuh si dauh bhawng,
nyawng bhawng,
rawm sidah bhawm,
sidah bhawng Law-ban,
plai,
rawm bee,
miien gaox,
nghox ca,
moi,
yung,
brung,
ling law,
siliex cawng,
pui khankix.
14 Pui pa yuh lai gee in kix krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ceu pa ghoing maix ka kraung mhawm ku ceu ku cawng ang lai yaox khawm uik;
krax mag mi min plak ngai hoik grai,
ang lai pon sawg hoek heue!”
15 jPui pa yuh lai gee daux veng an,
pa hoik mak mi khankix,
hoik lhat ka yaox tix krax pa kha nawh rhawm tix,
mai kok cung tix jhak dee singai.
Kix laux rhawm yiiem,
16 ah nin:
“Hoik laux leeing heue!
Hoik laux leeing heue!
Veng pa ting in eue,
nawh hoik ceep ka man keei sum,
kraung ceep pa klai pa raoh pa ngawh,
hoik ceep ka kraung khri,
siang simaox,
siang ploi khankix.
17 kAng bax koe tix taix nari,
sin ngao in kix hoik grai khawm uik heue!”
Pui cao kaing phat reu,
nyo si nie,
pui chix loe rawm,
mai can pui pa dex rawm son krax ot krax koe tix,
kok cung jhak ka dee singai.
18 lKix yaox taox ngu pa gleeing ka sibun an,
kok tom kok yo ah nin:
“Veng mawx pa sang pon tiag tix tau mai veng ting in heue!”
19 mKix teei phawng tiex rhuat ka piang kaing tix,
mai yiiem ah nin:
“Hoik laux leeing heue!
Hoik laux leeing heue!
Veng pa ting in eue,
can pui pa koe reu tix phat hu piang rawm hoik mag mi ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka nawh khawm uik.
Ang baox koe tix taix nari,
can pa koe nawh hoik grai khawm uik heue!”
20 nRao ma eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm!
Mawh kheu hoik laux leeing nawh!
Pui cie Siyiex,
kawn cau mai cao jhai khankix eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm heue!
Mawh kheu hoik prawng Siyiex son pex,
mai hoik ciyang ka sibun an heue.
21 oKhaix an,
koe kawn cau Siyiex pa ting riang tix kaux yaog ka simaox pa ting awm simaox laing yuh nan,
tik ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
mai ah nin:
“Veng Pa-pi-loeng pa ting kawx tag sang sau riang tix tik laig yuh nan nang,
mai ang lai baox lih tix koe leeing ju.
22 pqMaix ang lai baox mhong ka lox ting tauh sara ting jung lah,
mai lox pui pa phlu shiao teet tawng.
Pui pa chix yuh ka kraung pa yuh ka taix khankix ang lai yaox kah;
lox krid mhaw kawx ang lai baox mhong kah.
23 Pa riang kawk ngu kawx ang lai riang;
lox su shiep simex pa yuh ka paox mai sibun pa yuh ka paox kawx ang lai baox mhong kah.
Pui pa yuh lai gee cie maix hoik mawh pa blawn ting siawp sibrawm ka piang haktiex;
pui piang haktiex kawx hoik glup ka daux si meed sat pa yuh maix khawm uik heue.”
24 rPa-pi-loeng hoik kham krax ciyang,
mawh kheu hoik yaox nham cao jhai mai nham pui cie Siyiex ka daux veng in;
mawh tete,
nham pui pa hoik pauh pui yum ka piang haktiex,
hoik yaox ka daux veng in khawm uik heue.
