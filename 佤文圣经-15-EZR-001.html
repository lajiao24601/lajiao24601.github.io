Lai Yi-see-la
Simiang Sai-lu-seex tawx lox nhie pui Yo-thaix deeh ing meung tix
1
bYam giex Vawnga Paw-see pa kaux Sai-lu-seex kaing daux tix num,
Simiang Siyiex son sang kieh lox jhai pa tawx nawh ka cao jhai Yie-li-mi lih tix mawh,
tom so rhawm Sai-lu-seex tawx lox son piang meung tix ceu,
mai siao ngai lai tiiem lih,
tawx hu ka piang meung haktiex ku daog.
Gum lox lai in tiiem ot plak greem:
2 c“In mawh gum lox pa tawx Vawng Paw-see pa kaux Sai-lu-seex ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex piang rao ma kieh aux mawh simiang ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
nawh kieh aux mawh cao pa koe beeh glawm tix yuh ka nyiex buan son nawh tix lhang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung haktiex Yo-tax.
3 Kieh Siyiex ot tau mai pex pa mawh pui cie nawh khankix.
Pex tag caw tix hu ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung haktiex Yo-tax,
cox sum nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh mawh Siyiex pa krub pui ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
4 Phan ot ka daux ceu pui pa vuk vied vuk viang khankix,
sang lo krax teem krax coi tag pon yaox tix deeh ing,
paox plawg paox nyawm kix tag caw tix teem coi ka kix,
tawx mau khri,
kraung khrai,
khawng brie ka kix,
mai yuh nang khawm krax tan pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm tix ka son nyiex buan Siyiex daux Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Yo-tax mai cao kaing ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we,
khawm pui pa hoik laig Khuan Singax Singeh ka daux rhawm ku kaux,
hoik koe krax rien khre tix khawm uik,
son sang deeh ing cox sum nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
6 Can paox plawg paox nyawm hoek tix teem coi ka kix khawm uik,
mai tawx kraung mau,
kraung khri,
kraung khrai tang ceu,
khawng brie,
khawm khawng phak lak sawng pa koe ngawh koe jum ka kix,
khaix an kawn koe khawng phak lak sawng tang ceu pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm tix tan lu son nyiex buan heue.
7 Vawng Sai-lu-seex teei kawk nyaux mai klong pa tud simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha sivoe an khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa hoik deuh ot daux nyiex vad krub tix deeh tawx ka kix.
8 Nawh ploi Mi-thi-li-tax kawn cau pa ting daux nyiex simiang mawh cao pa rieb tix tawx kraung khrai in kix ka Seu-pa-sax simiang haktiex Yo-tax,
9-10 Koe miex mu tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Klong khri pa siao yuh krax tankoe tix ngoe mu.
Klong mau pa siao yuh krax tankoe tix rheng mu.
Klong tang ceu pa koe khawm uikkoe tix nga dim mu.
Kawn klong khri iakkoe tix ngoe mu.
Kawn klong mau iakkoe pon yieh kao mu
Kraung khrai tang ceukoe tix rheng ceu.
11 Klong khri,
klong mau,
khawm kraung khrai tang ceu khawm uik,
koe phuan rheng pon yieh ceu.
Yam deeh hoek pui pa vuk vied vuk viang pa kaoh khaing Pa-pi-loeng,
Seu-pa-sax viex kraung in kix hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
