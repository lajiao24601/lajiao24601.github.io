Si doe tix khaw caw mai Siyiex
5
Kheu hoik kok ex mawh pui pa rauh see kheu krax yum rhawm,
ex tom pon si doe tix khaw caw mai Siyiex ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
2 Kheu krax yum rhawm,
Khrit hoik kieh ex pon tix im ka daux buan son Siyiex,a
mawh kheu nan,
ex gaux rhawm gaux rhi tixb keud pot jun pot ka pa pon ka min plak ngai Siyiex heue!
3 Ja lao ang mawh nan,
yam ot ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi kawx,
ex ca kawn gaux rhawm;c
mawh kheu hoik tawng ex krax kha rhawm kha rhi pon kieh krax pa gun yeung lih tix mawh,
4 krax pa gun yeung pon krax pa yaog yaw Siyiex,
krax pa yaog yaw Siyiex viex krax keud pot jun pot hoek.
5 Krax keud pot jun pot ang lai chix giex going;
mawh kheu hoik tawx Siyiex Khuan Singax Singeh ka ex ka daux nawh,
mai hoik prauk krax muh tix laig ka daux rhawm rhi ex heue.
6 Yam iak riang rao ex,
Khrit tag sang hawt ka bawg yam pa tat Siyiex tix deuh,
mai yum son pui phi map.
7 Pa yum son pui rauh see ang hiex yaox;
pa yum son pui mhawm,
phan ang mawh nan,
koe pui pon yuh nan.
8 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik jhi ex yaox krax muh pa ting tix yuh nin:
yam kawn mawh ex pui phi map,
Khrit hoik yum son ex heue!
9 Mawh kheu pa yum nawh,
yam in ex hoik pon tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw;
pa yum nawh nie sang taoh ex luat luan khaing krax pa song rhawm Siyiex.
10 Ex hoik dawg mawh pui koeng Siyiex kah,
viang mawh nan,
hoik kieh ex pon tix mhawm mai nawh ka daux pa yum kawn simex nawh,
kheu hoik pon ex tix mhawm tau mai nawh,
khex ang ex nie pon krax taoh ka daux pa im ju Khrit lie?
11 Ja lao ang mawh nan,
kheu hoik pon ex tix mhawm tau mai Siyiex,
khex ang mawh pa gaux rhawm ex ka Siyiex ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit lie?
Ya-tang mai Khrit
12 dPhi map laig ka daux haktiex kheu pui tix kaux,
kheu phi map,
krax yum tom si doe tix hoek;
mawh kheu nan,
krax yum hoek ka piang kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik yuh kawn pui phi map ku kaux.
13 Sivoe sang tawx ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
haktiex hoik dawg koe ka phi map;
viang mawh nan,
phan ang koe lox tat jhawm prawng,
kawn pui ang hoek krax ciyang greem diag tong lox tat jhawm prawng.
14 Kheu nan,
jauh ka Ya-tang hoek ka Maw-si,
krax yum hoik giex gang ka kawn pui,
viang mawh pui pa ang bax yuh ka phi map pa ang nghiet ka Siyiex awm Ya-tang yuh nan kawx,
ang yaox tix luat luan khaing krax yum heue.
Ya-tang mawh pa grox krai ka kaw pa tag sang lih hoek phaox khaix.
15 Kheu nan,
kiex ra kaux ang awm paox tix,
mawh kheu Siyiex hoik tawx ka khawng phak buan son giex going,
mai ka phi map Ya-tang ang doh khaw paox tix.
Koe pui tom nie rug yum ka phi map pa yuh Ya-tang tix kaux;
viang mawh nan,
buan son Siyiex nie blawn ting,
nawh tawx buan son pa ting giex giex going going ka pui tom nie,
ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux Khrit tix kaux khaing.
16 Khawng phak buan son Siyiex mai krax phit lud pui tix kaux ang doh awm paox tix.
Pui tix kaux yuh phi map,
mai prawng ca nawh “koe phi map”;
viang mawh nan,
pui tom nie yuh phi map,
mai kawn pon ka khawng phak buan son pa ang khaw tix pon,
mai prawng ca “ang koe phi map” heue!
17 Krax yum rug jauh tix lih hoek tix giex gang kawn pui piang haktiex kheu pa lud pui tix kaux,
viang mawh nan,
kaing pa yuh Ye-sux Khrit tix kaux khaing lih nie blawn ting heue!
Can pui pa rieb ka buan son pa ting Siyiex,
mai pa tawx nawh si doe tix ka pui giex giex going going ka daux pa khaw caw,
hoik mawh pa keh pa giex kaing daux Khrit khawm uik heue.
18 Yuh nan,
kheu pui tix kaux yuh phi map,
pui hun tom hoek krax prawng ca khawm uik;
awm an,
kheu pa rauh see pui tix kaux,
pui hun tom pon pa pluat ploi mai ju.
19 Awm pui tix kaux lieh lheu gum lox,
pui hun tom lih mawh pui phi map;
awm an,
pui tix kaux cu nghiet,
pui hun tom lih mawh pui pa rauh see.
20 Pa yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng hoik kieh pui lud tom nie;
viang mawh nan,
phi map nied tix nie,
buan son Siyiex kawx nied tix ting.
21 Awm phi map hoek tix giex gang ka daux krax yum,
buan son Siyiex kawx hoek tix giex kaing ka daux pa rauh see,
mai kieh ex pon ju leeing ju ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue.
