Lox rhax krax pa yaog yaw
145
Siyiex aux eue,
aux sang krai pao grawng pa ting pa lhaong maix;
simiang aux eue,
aux sang yaog yaw maix leeing ju.
2 Aux sang yaog yaw maix ku singaix;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw maix tix ju hoek tix ju.
3 Simiang Siyiex kied ting kied lhaong,
nawh khaw tix pon krax pa yaog yaw;
pa ting pa lhaong nawh ang lai pon chix tiag tix yaox heue.
 
4 Tix ju hoek tix ju tag caw tix krai pao ka kaing pa yuh maix;
kix tag caw tix krai ka mhai sakse pa ting pa lhaong maix khawm uik.
5 Kix tag caw tix krai lao ka min plak ngai maix mai ka siawp sibrawm maix;
aux sang sidiex ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh maix.
6 Kawn pui tag caw tix krai lao ka pa ting riang ting rao maix;
aux sang krai pao ka pa ting pa lhaong maix.
7 Kix sang krai lao ka pa mhawm pa mhiem maix,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka pa khaw caw maix heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex koe krax muh krax rhawm,
mai pa grum naog ka krax pa caox rhawm;
nawh ang chix song rhawm yie yie,
nawh grum naog mai krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh.
9 Nawh chix kia ngien can pa im ju ku ceu;
buan son cie nawh chix hoek ka piang can ceu pa phan yuh khawm uik.
 
10 Simiang Siyiex eue,
can ceu pa phan pa yuh maix khawm uik hoik yaog yaw ka maix;
pui cie maix hoik yaog yaw ka maix khawm uik.
11 Kix sang krai pao ka min plak ngai siawp sibrawm maix,
sang krai lao ka pa ting riang ting rao maix;
12 kix sang kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex tawng ka kaing pa ting riang ting rao maix khawm uik,
tawng ka min plak ngai mai siawp sibrawm meung maix.
13 Siawp sibrawm maix koe leeing ju leeing phan;
maix sang mawh simiang leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
Gum lox pa hoik deuh Simiang Siyiex mawh pa kaux sijax;
can kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik hoik mawh pa mhawm pa mhiema;
14 Nawh joh jhan pui pa si pup ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi;
nawh yaog ka pui pa gut kheem kaoh heue.
 
15 Can ceu pa im ju khawm uik hoik een rhawm tix ka maix;
maix hoik tawx priex preem ka kix awm pa hoik hawt ka bawg yam,
16 Maix hoik tawx ka kix tom haoh tom nie,
hoik kieh can ceu pa sang lo kix kub heue.
 
17 Kaing yuh Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh pa khaw caw khawm uik;
can pa yuh nawh khawm uik hoik mawh ka daux krax pa caox rhawm.
18 Simiang Siyiex greed tix dex ka can pui pa khiox khaw ka nawh.
Nawh rieb ka pui pa khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
19 Nawh sang kieh ceu pa simie pui pa lhat graung yung ka nawh lih tix mawh;
nawh sang nghiet ka lox pa kok pa yo kix,
mai taoh ka kix.
20 Nawh kia ngien ka can pui pa muh ka nawh khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
nawh sang yuh pui pa caox pa caig khankix laux leeing heue.
 
21 Aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex;
kieh ceu pa phan pa yuh khawm uik rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux pa singax nawh heue.
