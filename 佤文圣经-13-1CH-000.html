Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix
Krai dawt
Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix mai phuk ra mawh tix phuk khaing, ka daux lai in khawm ra phuk, hoik krai grawng Lai Sa-mu-eu phuk tix mai phuk ra mai Lai Simiang phuk tix mai lai simiang phuk ra. Lai Grawng Been phuk tix mai phuk ra kaing ting hoik krai grawng kaing daux meung pui Yi-seu-liex ra ceu:
1. Krai yam yaox Yi-seu-liex mai Yo-tax krax kha rawm kha rhi, Siyiex kawn ca hawt ka lox pa hoik ap tix, mai kieh pui pa ot ka daux Yo-tax, yuh kaing pa hoik keud nawh tix sang yuh ka daux pui cie tix. Gawng krax yum rhawm in, ka daux lai in hoik blawn kieh kaing pa yuh Ta-wue mai Saw-law-meung mawh pa kied ting, kaing pa lo Yaw-sa-fa, Si-si-cia, Yaw-si-yax tix yuh, mai kawn pui an kix hoik mawh pa kied yum rhawm ka Siyiex heue.
2. Krai yam jauh tix yuh krax krub ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung, mai nhie blawn krai sidawng grawng been krax krub pa yuh cao giex kaing li, mai been pa hoik yuh ceu pui Li-we tix deuh khankix. Viang nyiex buan hoik mawh pa sum Saw-law-meung, lai in hoik kieh Ta-wue mawh pa jauh tix yuh nyiex buan mai pa jauh tix yuh krax krub heue.
Krai kaing
1. Ceu cad nyiex mai ceu kaux pui (1.1–9.44)
2. Grawng yum Sao-law (10.1–14)
3 Grawng pa giex Ta-wue kaing (11.1–29.30)
(1) Krax kha rhawm kha rhi mai pa sum khaox (11.1–22.1)
(2) Grawng rien khre tix sum ka nyiex buan (22.2–29.30)
