Sao-law yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux
(Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 22.6–16; 26.12–18)
9
Sao-law nied tix teei lox caox ghlat ka pui gau Simiang Ye-sux.
Nawh hu ka cao giex kaing li pa ting,
2 mai khiox khaw ka lai pa tawx hu ka nyiex sidiex pui Yo-thaix ku dauh pa ot haktiex Ta-ma-see-keux,
cu nawh giex ka pui pa hawt ka daux krax nawh,
mawh simex bun ang ah,
tag sang giex kix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik heue.
3 Ka daux krax pa hu plak Ta-ma-see-keux,
yam dex sang hoek ka daux veng,
dum mawx dum an koe pa riang tix mu lih hoek khaing rao ma,
mai jhan riang ka nawh khawm pon daog.
4 Nawh goh piang tiex,
mhong lox pa ah nin ka nawh:
“Sao-law,
Sao-law!
Yuh ka mawx maix lai tuk tiak ka aux lie?”
5 Nawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
mawh maix mawx?”
Gum lox an pawk ah nin:
“Aux mawh Ye-sux pa hoik tuk tiak maix heue.
6 Kaoh,
laig ka daux veng,
koe pui pa sang krai kaing pa caw maix tix yuh ka maix heue.”
7 Pui pa hu tau mai Sao-law kaog khawm uik,
mai ang lai chix krai lox lih;
kix mhong lox pa si kah pui,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox cao.
8 Sao-law yaog tix kaoh khaing piang tiex,
mai blah ngai tix ang yaox ceu pa tid tix;
pui pa hu tau mai nawh bluh taix nawh,
viex nawh laig daux veng Ta-ma-see-keux.
9 Nawh ang chix yaox ceu pa tid tix loe singaix;
mai ang ih ang nyaux heue.
10 Ka daux veng Ta-ma-see-keux,
koe pui gau tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-na-ni-yax.
Nawh hoik pon riang su sai tix ceu;
ka daux riang su sai an Simiang Ye-sux kok nawh ah nin:
“Ya-na-ni-yax!”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux ot dee in heue.”
11 Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix nied tix hu ka plak Cee-cie,
ka daux nyiex ma pui Yo-tax sawg pui Ta-sux tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Sao-law.
Yam kawn rawg nawh buan,
12 tom yaox pui tix kaux ka daux riang su sai,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-na-ni-yax,
laig deuh taix tix piang nawh,
mai kieh ngai nawh deeh chix yaox heue.”
13 Ya-na-ni-yax pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
pui tom nie hoik krai ka aux,
pui in hoik mawh pa kin tuk tiak kawn Khrit ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
14 Yam in nawh hoek ka veng Ta-ma-see-keux,
mai viex akhuang pa tawx simiang cao giex kaing li,
son sang giex ka can pui pa krub ka maix heue.”
15 Simiang Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix tawng hu tix khaing heue!
Mawh kheu hoik laug aux nawh yuh kaing tix,
mai sang kieh nawh krai lao ceu kaux aux ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai ka simiang,
khawm ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
16 Aux tag sang hoek tix krai ka nawh,
nawh tag sang kham krax kha rhawm ku ceu son aux heue.”
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-na-ni-yax hu.
Nawh laig daux nyiex an,
mai deuh taix tix piang to kaux Sao-law ah nin:
“Pux aik Sao-law eue,
Ye-sux pa hoik jhi maix yaox tix ka daux krax pa hoek dee in ploi aux hoek,
sang kieh maix baox deeh chix yaox,
khaix an pon naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh.”
18 Dum mawx dum an,
koe ceu pa awm si ngawb kax lih khaing daux ngai Sao-law,
ngai nawh deeh riang.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh kaoh,
mai rieb tix cub com;
19 khaix hoik ih nawh ka ceu pa ih,
riang rao nawh deeh lih koe heue.
Sao-law sidiex ka Ta-ma-see-keux
Yam paung ot Sao-law tau mai pui gau ka daux veng Ta-ma-see-keux ka daux tix vud.
20 Nawh nied tix hoek sidiex grawng Ye-sux ka daux nyiex sidiex,
mai ah nin:
“Nawh hoik mawh kawn simex Siyiex.”
21 Can pui pa mhong kied daox deng khawm uik,
mai ah nin:
“Pui in ang hoik mawh pui an pa kin tuk tiak ka kawn Khrit pa ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung lie?
Nawh hoek dee in,
ang hoik mawh son sang giex ka kawn Khrit,
mai ap tix tawx kix ka simiang cao giex kaing li lie?”
22 Viang mawh nan,
krax pa sidiex Sao-law tieh tix nie koe riang;
nawh hoik teei lox pa kaux cong kham grawng mawh Ye-sux Khrit,
mai hoik kieh pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka Ta-ma-see-keux ang lai koe lox pa sang pawk ka nawh.
23 aKhaix hoik luan been tix baux bawg yam,
pui Yo-thaix keud tix sang tieh vheen mai pauh ka Sao-law.
24 Koe pui krai Sao-law mhong grawng pa sang tieh vheen kix ka nawh.
Son sang pauh ka Sao-law,
kix pau diang siviex veng leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
25 Viang mawh nan,
koe ka daux tix bo,
pui gau nawh teei khrawng ploi nawh lih piang tiex khaing piang guh veng heue.
Sao-law ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
26 Sao-law hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai keud tix sang hu lai ling ka pui gau.
Viang mawh nan,
kix ang yum rhawm grawng mawh nawh pui gau tete.
Pui hun hoik lhat graung khawm uik,
27 mawh ngie Pa-na-pa pa hoek tix teem coi ka nawh khaing,
mai viex nawh hu ka kawn cau,
krai ka kix grawng pa hoik si pup Sao-law ka Simiang Ye-sux daux krax yuh ka mawx,
Simiang Ye-sux hoik krai lox yuh ka mawx ka nawh.
Nawh baox krai grawng pa koe rhawm kua Sao-law tix sidiex ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux ka veng Ta-ma-see-keux yuh ka mawx ka kix.
28 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law hu lai ling tau mai kawn cau,
mai viex rhawm ting sidiex ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
29 Nawh kawx si kah,
mai thung thiang ka pui Yo-thaix pa si kah ka lox Si-lax khankix,
viang mawh nan,
kix keud tix sang pauh ka nawh.
30 Kawn Khrit khankix hoik tawng ka kaing in kix,
tom viex nawh hu ka Khai-sha-li-yax,
mai tawx nawh ing ka Ta-sux heue.
31 Yam an,
ceu cao ka daux haktiex Yo-thaix,
Cia-li-lix,
Sa-ma-li-yax ku dauh hoik pon krax nyawm ot nyawm koe been tix bawg yam.
Ceu cao hoik pon sum tix kaux cong ka daux krax pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Ye-sux,
mai ka daux krax teem krax coi Khuan Singax Singeh,
kaux pui hoik thiem thie tix haok huan ku singaix heue.
Pi-teux ot ka Lui-tax mai ka Yaw-pha
32 Pi-teux hoik tauh hu ka haktiex ku dauh ku dee;
koe tix bawg,
nawh hu vhi ka kawn Khrit pa ot ka Lui-tax.
33 Nawh si pup pui tix kaux ka dee an,
ceu kaux mawh Yi-ni-ya;
pui in mawh pui pa koe jhawm saux yum taix caong,
nawh ot piang ku it tix daix num.
34 Pi-teux krai ka nawn ah nin:
“Yi-ni-ya,
Ye-sux Khrit hoik pai moi ka maix.
Kaoh teei prux it maix hu heue!”
Yi-ni-ya yaog tix kaoh dum mawx dum an.
35 Can pui pa paung ot ka Lui-tax mai ka Sa-loeng hoik yaox nawh khawm uik,
kix tom yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux khawm uik heue.
36 Ka daux Yaw-pha koe pui gau bun tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ta-pi-tax (ceu kaux si lax mawh To-cia,
deeh bleeh mawh pex rang).
Sibun an hoik yuh ka kaing pa mhawm tom nie ceu,
nawh gaux rhawm tix teem coi ka pui hot pui sibhawm.
37 Koe tix ngaix,
bun an koe jhawm saux,
nawh yum.
Pui hun khoek ka to kaux yum nawh,
mai deuh nawh ot ka daux lawg nyiex plak piang.
38 Yaw-pha ang singai khaing Lui-tax miex glang tix,
pui gau pa ot ka Yaw-pha mhong ot Pi-teux ka Lui-tax,
tom ploi pui hu ka nawh,
mai khiox khaw ka nawh ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix nied tix hoek ka yix dee in.”
39 Pi-teux yaog tix hu tau mai kix dum mawx dum an.
Yam hoek Pi-teux,
koe pui thung nawh haok ka lawg nyiex plak piang.
Can miex mai yiiem ka vung viang Pi-teux,
mai baox tawx nawh jhak ka kraung ceep plak daux mai plak praix pa hoik caing To-cia yam im tix sivoe an.
40 Pi-teux tawx lox ka pui hun nhie kix lih khawm uik,
nawh tom grux ngawng tix rawg buan,
khaix an kling to kaux yum an ah nin:
“Ta-pi-tax,
kaoh heue!”
Nawh blah ngai tix,
mai yaox ka Pi-teux,
nawh tom ngawm.
41 Pi-teux hu yaog nawh kaoh,
mai baox nhie kawn cau mai miex mai khankix laig daux nyiex,
khaix an tawx To-cia pa deeh im an ka kix.
42 Grawng kaing in hoik tauh hu ka Yaw-pha ku dauh,
koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux.
43 Pi-teux hoik paung ot ka nyiex ma Si-meung pa mawh cang mai hak haktiex Yaw-pha tom rheeng heue.
