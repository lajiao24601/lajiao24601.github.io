Ping nyia cuyi mai pa daox aw
9
Ping nyia cuyi yuh ka nyiex ma cie tix,
mai sum ka rong aliah gawng.
2 Nawh jauh tix khan priex preem,
mai pauh ka moi yung,
cia ka plai pa nyawm,
mai khan ot piang pheen.
3 Nawh ploi bun siliex tix hu,
mai kok yo ka dee pa kied lhaong ka daux veng ah nin:
4 “Pui daox pui aw eue,
hoek heue!”
Baox ah nin ka pui daox aw:
5 “Hoek tix sawm ka eep pa kuh aux,
nyaux ka plai nyawm pa cia aux.
6 Tag caw kieh tix im ju mai singai hu khaing pui daox aw khankix;
tag caw tix lawng daux krax ping nyia cuyi heue.”
7 Phan maix cox ka krax lud pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
hoik mawh krax kaig krax tua pa yuh nawh pa deeh hoek ka piang to kaux nawh;
keud tix sang ne raw ka pui pa caox pa caig,
hoik mawh pa deeh yuh to kaux tix laux leeing.
8 Paw vi ah ka krax lud krax loe pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mawh kheu nawh tag chix koeng ka maix.
Phan maix sidiex gau ka pui pa lhak lhiao,
nawh tag chix yung yi ka maix.
9 Sidiex gau ka pui pa lhak lhiao,
tag sang kieh nawh nie baox lhak lhiao;
krax pa sidiex gau ka pui rauh pui see,
tag chix kieh nawh nie baox haok huan krax lhak lhiao heue.
10 aPa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh dee jauh ping nyia cuyi;
pa tawng yung ka cao pa singax singeh hoik mawh pa lhak lhiao.
11 Ping nyia cuyi tag chix kieh maix lang ju lang phan.
12 Phan maix koe ping nyia cuyi,
hoik kieh to kaux maix pon krax pa mhawm;
phan maix tieb tie ka ping nyia cuyi,
hoik kieh to kaux maix som nai nang heue.
13 Pa daox aw awm bun pa riag yo,
pa ang koe krax lhak lhiao,
ang koe krax kaig krax tua tix kaux.
14 Nawh ngawm ka diang siviex nyiex,
ngawm dee blawn lhaong ka daux veng,
15 mai kok yo ka pui an kix pa lawng krax laung laung ah nin:
16 “Hoek heue,
pui daox aw khankix!”
Nawh ah nin ka pui daox aw:
17 “Rawm pa briex blawn tie,
khao mun pa briex blawn nyawm.”
18 Pui pa hoek krax som nai ang tawng krax yum hoik ot tau mai nawh tan,
hu ka plak ot nawh tan,
hoik cawd ka dee pa kied raux meung yum heue.
