Lai Plak Jhi
1
Lai in mawh pa tiiem krax pa plak jhi Ye-sux Khrit.
Siyiex tawx krax pa plak jhi in ka nawh,
mai nhie nawh krai kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka siliex cie Siyiex khankix ka daux bawg yam pa dawt.
Khrit ploi kawn cau Siyiex hoek tix jhi kaing in ka siliex tix pa kaux Yaw-hang,
2 Yaw-hang tom krai ceu pa hoik yaox tix ka pui hun,
kham sakse gum lox Siyiex mai pa mawh pa meh krax pa plak jhi Ye-sux Khrit.
3 Pui pa phat ka lai in kied koe buan son heue!
Pui pa mhong ka lox jhai in mai priex tix hawt ka pa hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai kied koe buan son heue!
Mawh kheu singaix bawg yam lih kaing in tix mawh dex sang hoek heue.
Chawk ceu cao aliah mu mhawm ot
4-5 abAux pa mawh Yaw-hang tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka ceu cao aliah mu pa ot ka daux haktiex Ya-si-yax yuh nin:
kieh Siyiex kaw pa koe tom yam jauh.
Yam in,
singaix pa sang hoek,
khawm khuan aliah mu ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung nawh,
mai kaw pa kham sakse pa kaux sijax an,
pa kaoh im khaing krax yum sivoe paox,
pa mawh Ye-sux Khrit,
cao giex kaing ka piang haktiex,
tawx buan son mai pa nhim ka pex heue!
Nawh muh ka ex,
mai hoik tawx ju tix yum jauh nham son ex,
taoh ex lih khaing daux phi map,
6 ckieh ex lih mawh cao giex kaing li ka daux meung cie tix,
yuh kaing krub cie keeing nawh Siyiex.
Kieh min plak ngai mai siawp sibrawm mawh cie Ye-sux Khrit tix ju hoek tix ju heue!
A-meung.
7 dJhak heue,
nawh brug pai awm hoek heue!
Pui ku kaux tag sang yaox ka nawh,
pie khawm pui pa klauh nawh ka pliah kawx tag sang yaox ka nawh.
Yam hoek nawh,
ceu pui piang haktiex khawm uik tag sang yiiem grai laux rhawm son nawh.
Kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh heue!
A-meung.
8 ePa mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm pa koe yam jauh,
yam in,
singaix pa tag sang hoek ah nin:
“Aux mawh A-eu-fa,
pa mawh pa jauh,
mawh Ya-mi-cia,
pa mawh pa si dauh heue.”
Khrit jhi Yaw-hang yaox tix
9 Aux mawh Yaw-hang pa mawh pux aik pex.
Aux kham krax kha rhawm tau mai pex ka daux Ye-sux,
gun yeung tau mai pex,
tag sang yaox tix ot ka siawp sibrawm cie nawh nang.
Son sang sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex mai ka pa mawh pa meh pa plak jhi Ye-sux,
mai pui hoik sax laung aux ot piang klawn tiex rawm sigrit sigra Pa-mawx.
10 Koe ka daux singaix tuah tix ngaix,
Khuan Singax Singeh laig ka daux aux,
aux mhong ka lox pa ting si num,
pa awm lox teet tawng yuh nan,
ot ka plak khaix aux ah nin ka aux:
11 “Tiiem lih ka ceu pa hoik mhong maix,
khaix an teei tix tawx phuk lai in ka ceu cao Yi-fu-saw,
See-me-nax,
Piie-cia-mawx,
Thoe-ya-thoe-la,
Sa-tix,
Fe-la-thie-fe,
Lao-ti-cia aliah mu heue.”
12 Aux kling to kaux tix jhak mawh pui mawx pa si kah ka aux,
aux yaox jawk kawk ngu aliah mu.
13 fSigreung jawk kawk ngu an koe pui tix kaux pa awm kawn kawn pui yuh nan,
cung ka dee an,
to kaux nawh ceep ka kraung dha pa hoek tom loek caong,
naok nawh fua tix ka maox khri.
14 ghHauk kaing nawh paing awm rawm rha,
mai paing singax awm hauk yung nang;
tawm ngai nawh toh riang awm rao ngu;
15 icaong nawh sawng awm tawng pa hoik ceed lih khaing ngu mai baox awt plang yuh nan;
gum lox nawh awm lox luah lih rawm khaing rang yuh nan.
16 Taix khawn dawm nawh giex ka simuing aliah mu,
daux jheeh nawh plih lih ka vaig pa lawm khawm ra plak;
pak ngai nawh awm pa riang singaix yam greung ngaix.
17 jYam yaox aux ka nawh,
tom kup ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
mai awm pui yum yuh nan.
Nawh teei taix khawm dawm tix deuh piang aux,
mai ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat heue!
Aux mawh pa jauh,
mai pa si dauh.
18 Aux mawh cao pa koe leeing ju leeing phan heue!
Aux hoik sax yum tix bawg,
yam in kawn im,
khaix an tag sang im leeing ju leeing phan.
Aux hoik giex ka jiak saw krax yum mai meung yum.
19 Can ceu pa hoik yaox maix khawm uik,
mawh kaing ceu pa koe yam in ang ah,
mawh pa tag sang hoek ang ah,
maix tag caw tix tiiem lih khawm uik.
20 Can ceu pa hoik yaox maix,
simuing pa ot ka daux tawm taix khawn dawm aux aliah mu,
mai jawk kawk ngu aliah mu pa hoik tiag ka ceu pa raux pa mox mawh yuh nin:
simuing aliah mu mawh ceu cao kawn cau Siyiex aliah mu;
jawk kawk ngu aliah mu mawh ceu cao aliah mu heue.”
