28
Yi-sha kok Ya-keux hoek sivoe tix,
mai nawb buan piang nawh ah nin:
“Maix tag ang pon teei tix moeng ka bun ceu pui Cia-nang.
2 Maix hu ka haktiex Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax nyiex Pi-thu-lix pa mawh keeing ka miex maix,
teei maix moeng kawn bun La-pang prie miex maix tix kaux kaux heue.
3 Kieh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm nawb buan ka nyiex ma maix,
kieh maix pre haoh hun,
mawh tax du ka ceu pui tom nie!
4 aKieh nawh tawx buan son ka maix,
mai ka doe ceu pui maix awm hoik tawx ka Ya-paw-la-hang yuh nan!
Kieh maix giex kaux ka haktiex pa ot maix ka yam in,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka Ya-paw-la-hang.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-sha ploi Ya-keux hu ka Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax,
keeing miex nawh pui Ya-lang ka La-pang kawn simex Pi-thu-lix dee an.
La-pang mawh prie Li-pai-cia,
paox Ya-keux mai Yi-sao heue.
Yi-sao baox teei tix koe moeng tang kaux
6 Yi-sao hoik tawng Yi-sha nawb buan ka Ya-keux,
mai ploi nawh hu ka Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax,
son sang teei nawh tix koe moeng tan,
hoik tawng nang yam nawb Yi-sha buan ka Ya-keux mai ang kieh nawh teei tix moeng ka bun Cia-nang.
7 Ya-keux hoik cu nghiet ka lox miex keeing tix,
mai lih hu ka haktiex Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax.
8 Yi-sao taug tawng ang keeing kix Yi-sha khaw rhawm ka bun Cia-nang.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh lih hu ka Yi-seu-ma-lix kawn Ya-paw-la-hang dee an,
mai teei tix moeng ka kawn bun nawh Ma-ha-la;
pa mawh pux Ni-pai-yaw.
Ca maox Ya-keux ka daux Paw-theu-lix
10 Ya-keux lih hu khaing Piie-see-pa mai hu plak meung Ha-lang heue.
11 Yam klip singaix,
nawh hoek ka haktiex tix dauh,b
mai nawh it tan tix sawm;
nawh teei simaox tix lon gaong kah,
it ka piang tiex,
mai laig it heue.
12 cNawh ca maox bawng tix mu,
jung tix tom cux ka rao ma;
koe kawn cau Siyiex khankix lih lih haok haok ka piang bawng an.
13 eNawh baox yaox cung Siyiex ka praok nawhd,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh Siyiex keeing maix Ya-paw-la-hang mai Siyiex Yi-sha;
haktiex pa it maix in,
aux tag tawx ka maix,
khawm doeh ceu cad maix.
14 gKix tag sang nie awm phawng tiex ka piang haktiex,
mai tauh hu khawm pon caong daog haktiex.
Aux sang tawx buan ka meung piang haktiex ku meung ka daux ceu kaux maixf mai doeh ceu cad maix.
15 Aux sang ot tau mai maix,
maix ot dee mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang kia ngien ka maix,
thung maix hoek ka haktiex in khawm uik.
Aux ang doh tik vud ka maix;
aux tag sang hawt tix yuh ka lox pa hoik ah aux tix deuh ka maix tete heue.”
16 Ya-keux si duad khaing it tix,
mai ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ot ka dee in tete;
aux ang tawng ka heue!”
17 Nawh tom lhat graung,
baox ah nin:
“Dee in mawh dee koe krax lhat krax graung ka mie ding;
dee in mawh nyiex Siyiex,
mai siviex bleeh hoek ka rao ma heue.”
18 Ngaix khaix plak pon ngawb cao,
Ya-keux kaoh,
mai teei simaox gaong tix sum mawh mhai pa doh keud,
groi bee ka piang,
tan ka Siyiex.
19 Nawh kok ceu kaux dee an mawh Paw-theu-lix,h
(ceu kaux veng an hoik dawg mawh Lu-see heue).
20 Kheu nan,
nawh tom mhaing lox ka Siyiex ah nin:
“Phan maix ot tau mai aux,
kia ngien ka piang krax hu aux,
mai tawx aux kraung ceep eep sawm,
21 kieh aux mhawm taix loig caong tom hoek ka nyiex tix,
maix tom mawh Siyiex aux.
22 Simaox sum aux pa mawh mhai pa doh keud,
tag mawh nyiex pa singax pa krub aux;
can ceu pa tawx maix ka aux khawm uik aux tag deeh tan kao son tix son ka maix heue.”
