Khu dee tan
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 38.1–7)
27
“Caw tix yuh khu dee tan ka khaox Cing-heu-huang.
Khu an kieh koe pon pak,
plak lang ra kongjhee ra kongchuing,
plak vieh ra kongjhee ra kongchuing,
plak lhaong tix kongjhee loe kongchuing.
2 Caw tix yuh rung pa bhlien ka piang rung khu dee tan pon rung,
kieh si doe tix tau mai khu dee tan tix mu khaing,
mai om ka tawng.
3 Yuh pan pa sang sau nyaox kah,
mai baox yuh jauk,
klong,
cuan ciie nex,
aw ngu;
kraung pa in kix khawm uik tag raug ka tawng.
4 Caw tix yuh rub tawng tix mu son khu dee tan,
pon jaong koe khaox grawng siao jauk tawng ka pon gawng heue.
5 Nih rub an ot ka vung viang nyoi khu plak greem,
tom hoek ka nyoi khu plak piang.
6 Caw tix yuh khaox grawng khu ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka tawng,
7 mai yam grawng kah,
chup khaox grawng laig ka daux jhawk khawm ra plak.
8 Caw tix yuh khu dee tan ka phia;
kieh daux sigreung khu yi.
Maix caw tix yuh awm pak peung pa hoik jhi aux ka maix ka piang gawng heue.”
Biang brawk nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 38.9–20)
9 “Caw teei man tie pa hoik taing ka man keei sum mai yuh die vung viang nyiex gox buan Simiang Siyiex kah,
kieh mawh brawk.
Man tie plak seh plak lang koe tipon pon kongjhee,
10 kieh mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong tawng tix nga gawng pa giex kaux,
goik pa ot ka piang rong khaox an mai lat mawh pa yuh ka mau.
11 Man tie ka plak laong hoik awm paox,
plak lang koe tipon pon kongjhee,
mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong tawng tix nga gawng pa giex kaux.
Goik pa ot ka piang rong khaox an mai lat mawh pa yuh tix ka mau.
12 Man tie ka plak klip singaix koe vieh tix nga ra kongjhee,
koe rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong kao gawng.
13 Ka plak lih singaix,
ka diang siviex dee laig daux brawk,
man tie plak vieh koe tix nga ra kongjhee.
14-15 “Ka daux laig ka diang siviex khawm ra plak,
man tie plak vieh koe liah kongjhee liah kongchuing,
mawh rong khaox pa koe daux jhup jaong loe gawng pa giex kaux.
16 Siviex brawk caw tix kieh man tie koe plak lang dim kongjhee,
yuh ka man keei sum mai man pa klai rao ma,
man pa lung pliex,
mai man pa raoh rawt pa hoik taing tix ka keei,
ka piang koe lai tai,
mawh rong pa koe chup jaong mau pon gawng pa joh kaux.
17 Rong ot vung viang brawk khawm uik mawh pa lang lad kaux ka rong mau,
goik pa ot ka piang rong khankix,
mawh pa yuh ka mau,
mai daux jhup jaong rong,
tag kieh mawh pa yuh ka tawng.
18 Brawk plak lang tag kieh koe tipon pon kongjhee,
plak vieh kieh koe tix nga ra kongjhee,
plak lhaong kieh koe ra kongjhee ra kongchuing.
Man tie caw tix yuh ka man keei sum,
mai yuh daux jhup jaong ka tawng.
19 Can kraung pa siao daux nyiex gox buan man,
jim pa siao daux nyiex gox buan man mai vung viang biang brawk mawh pa raug ka tawng khawm uik heue.”
Been cap kawk ngu
(Lai Li-we 24.1–4)
20 “Tawx lox ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix viex bee sime pa kied mhawm hoek tawx ka maix,
mai kieh mawh pa siao ka jawk kawk ngu,
son sang kieh kawk ngu riang ang jud ang jai.
21 Ya-loeng mai kawn praix nawh tag tok kawk ngu ka daux nyiex buan man,
sivoe teug sijax mukheu plak praix man tie,
kawk ngu in tag riang sivoe ngai aux jauh tom pon bo hoek pon ngawb.
Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix mai kawn praix kix,
tag caw tix priex tix hawt ka gum lox in leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
