Priex tix nghiet ka gum lox ping nyap pon buan son
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 7.12–24; 28.1–14)
26
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pex ang pon tix yuh riang krub son to kaux tix;
pex ang pon tix yaog jung ka riang pui,
rong simaox,
mai saik riang simaox kieh mawh pa krub tix kah.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex.
2 Pex caw priex tix hawt ka singaix laux,
mai yung yi ka nyiex dee pa singax singeh aux.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.
3 b“Phan pex priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
hawt grawng lawng khaix gum lox ping nyap aux khankix,
4 aux tag tawx rawm lhiex lih ka piang pex,
kieh haktiex lih tawx plix nghox plix koi,
mai khaox plix khankix tag lih tix koe plix.
5 Pex tag sang yaox tix vawg ka nghox koi ang jud,
hoek tom bawg yam hoik teem plix siiet;
pex tag sang yaox tix pieh ka plix siiet ang jud,
hoek tom yam rhuat simie.
Pex tag yaox tix koe priex preem tom nie,
tag sang nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux haktiex tix heue.
6 “Aux tag kieh pa nhim koe daux haktiex pex,
pex tag ang koe krax lhat krax graung,
yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe.
Aux tag teei tix tik ka to tiag pa vao khaing ka daux haktiex pex khankix;
ka daux haktiex pex khankix tag ang lai kieh naing lih koe.
7 Pex tag sang puing pex ka pui koeng.
8 Pex phuan kaux tag pon pex ka pui tix yieh kaux,
tix yieh kaux tag pon pex ka pui tix mheen kaux;
pui koeng khankix tag goh tix yum ka greem lawm vaig pex.
9 Aux tag sang tawx buan ka pex,
kieh kawn praix pex haoh hun.
Aux tag sang giex ka gum lox sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai pex kaux cong.
10 Pex tag sang koe ka priex preem tom haoh tom nie,
leeing num ang yaox tix cud,
mai kawn sang tik ka priex preem pa prim son sang baox deeh deuh ka priex preem pa khraox.
11 Aux tag sang ot tau mai pex ka daux nyiex gox buan man aux,
ang doh tik vud ka pex.
12 cAux tag sang ot tau mai pex,
tag sang mawh Siyiex pex;
pex tag mawh pui cie aux.
13 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Aux hoik viex pex lih khaing daux Ai-cix,
ang lai kieh pex yaox tix mawh siliex cawng.
Aux hoik yuh laux leeing ka sibrawm pui pa tuk tiak ka pex,
mai hoik kieh pex yaog kaing tix mai hu tix krax heue.”
Lieh lheu ka gum lox ping nyap pon pa ciyang
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 28.15–68)
14 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Phan pex ang cu nghiet ka gum lox aux,
mai tik vud ka gum lox ping nyap aux khankix,
pex tag sang pon pa ciyang.
15 Phan pex ang hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux mai been aux,
mai tik vud ka sijax mukheu aux,
16 aux tag sang ciyang ka pex.
Aux tag sang kieh krax laux krax leeing hoek ka piang pex,
mai kieh pex saux ngia ka jhawm saux pa ang pui lai pon pai,
jhawm saux pa si aoh,
jhawm saux pa faik yuh ngai,
jhawm saux pa krawx pa rhao.
Simie pa hoik rhuat pex ang koe ceu,
mawh kheu pui pa koeng khankix tag sang ih ka can pa sum pex khawm uik.
17 Aux vud pak ngai tix khaing pex;
mai pui koeng tag sang puing pex ka pex.
Pui koeng ka pex tag sang hoek tix kuang ka piang pex,
ghlat ka pex,
mai kieh pex to khawm ang pui tie baox leue tix heue.
18 “Phan pex hoik yaox tix kham gee ka krax pa kha rhawm in kix,
pex ca kawn ang baox cu nghiet ka aux,
aux tom tag sang tawx krax ciyang pa nie kheu jao phi map pex aliah son.
19 Aux tag sang lauh ka pa ting rhawm kiien rhi mai ting rhawm lhaong rhi pex.
Aux ang ploi rawm lhiex lih,
mai kieh haktiex pex kroh,
mai lih kiien awm rhiem yuh nan.
20 Pex sau riang rao tix yuh kaing kawx,
mai ang yaox tix vawg,
mawh kheu haktiex ang chix tawx pa kuah,
mai khaox plix ang chix plix plix heue.
21 “Phan pex ca kawn kiien rhawm rhi,
ang nghiet lox aux,
aux tag sang baox thiem thie krax ciyang ka piang pex aliah son.
22 Aux tag sang ploi to tiag pa vao khankix hoek ka si nax pex;
kix tag sang ih ka kawn simex kawn bun pex,
mai bleet ka khawng brie pex,
kieh pex pa deuk ang lai koe miex ding tix,
daux krax khankix hoik nhik hoik riem heue.
23 “Khaix hoik yaox pex tix kham gee ka krax pa ciyang in kix,
phan pex ca kawn ang cu nghiet ka aux,
ca kawn baox tieb tie ka aux,
24 Aux tag sang vud pak ngai tix khaing pex,
mai baox thiem thie krax ciyang piang pex,
lheu khaing sivoe an aliah son.
25 Aux tag sang kieh naing ciyang ka pex,
jao phi map pa lieh lheu pex ka gum lox sijax mukheu.
Phan khrawm paox tix sawg mox tix nyawm ot ka daux veng,
aux tag sang ploi jhawm saux preng hoek ka piang pex;
mai pex tag laux leeing ka daux tawm taix pui pa koeng.
26 Aux tag sang klat ka krax pa tawx pex ih ka priex preem,
mai kieh bun kao kaux rom tix kuh ka aw tix mu khaing.
Kix tag guah tix tawx khao mun son pex,
pex tag ih mai ang chix sak heue.
27 “Khaix hoik kham gee pex ka krax pa ciyang in kix,
phan pex ca kawn tieb tie ka aux,
mai ang nghiet lox aux,
28 aux tag sang yuh pex laux ka daux pa song rhawm song rhi tix,
mai baox thiem thie krax pa ciyang lheu khaing sivoe an aliah son hoek ciyang ka piang pex.
29 Pex tag cai rhawm mai ih ka nex kawn simex kawn bun tix khankix.
30 Aux tag yiex laux nyiex vad ka piang gawng iak pa krub pex laux leeing,
mai yieh tix tid ka khu dee tan pa tok pex nyawng bhawng khankix,
mai taing tix tik ka to kaux yum pex ot ka piang riang pa krub an.
Aux ang rhawm yaox ka pex.
31 Aux tag kieh veng dee ot pex lih tix mawh dee riem;
mai yieh nyiex dee krub pex khankix laux leeing,
tag ang lai rieb ka taox nyawng bhawng krax tan pex.
32 Aux tag kieh haktiex dee ot pex lih tix riem khawm uik,
khawm pui koeng khankix pa hoik pon cax ka haktiex pex,
tag sang koe krax pa daox deng ka heue!
33 Aux tag kieh pex si pup ka naing,
mai kieh pex prung prang hu ka meung haktiex pui.
Haktiex pex tag sang riem.
Veng dee ot pex tag lih tix mawh dee riem.
34-35 Kheu nan,
haktiex tom yaox tix laux lee tix khawm uik,
gaux rhawm kheu yam ot pex dee an,
pex ang kieh kix yaox tix laux lee tix.
Khaix hoik hu vung viang pex hoek ka meung haktiex pui khankix,
haktiex tag yaox tix laux lee tix kheu riem tix heue.
36 “Aux tag kieh pui pa hu vung viang tix ot ka daux haktiex pui pa koeng khankix yaoh yee,
pie lox luah lhax khaox khaing pa phlu bhaung khankix,
tag kieh kix yaoh to.
Kix tag to awm pa leue khaing daux bang naing yuh nan,
mai goh khawm ang pui baox leue khaix tix.
37 Pui ang bax leue kawx,
mai lai goh sideeing ka paox tix;
pex tag ang lai koe riang tix tieb tie ka pui pa koeng.
38 Pex tag yum ka daux pa vung viang tix,
mai haktiex pui pa koeng ka pex,
tag bleet ka pex.
39 Can pui pa kawn deuk ka daux haktiex pui pa koeng khankix,
jao phi map to kaux kix,
mai phi map tax du kix,
kix tag sang grai grup awm tax du kix yuh nan heue.
40 “Viang mawh nan,
doeh ceu cad pex tag sang doh keud ka phi map tix,
mawh kheu phi map pa yuh tax du kix,
kheu hoik tieb tie kix ka aux,
tieh vheen ka aux,
41 mai cheeik aux song rhawm yuh ka kix,
mai ploi kix hu ka haktiex pui pa koeng khankix.
Ca mawh tawng doeh ceu cad pex tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
mai rieb krax pa ciyang son phi map tix,
mai son krax pa tieh vheen tix,
42 daux tag chix doh keud ka sijax mukheu pa yuh tix tau mai Ya-keux,
mai Yi-sha,
mai Ya-paw-la-hang,
mai cox deeh cu tix tawx haktiex ka pui cie aux khankix.
43 “Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix ran grheu ka pui pa ot ka daux haktiex an hu singax pak lawg,
mai kieh haktiex yaox tix laux lee tix khawm uik;
pui pa ot ka daux haktiex khankix,
tag caw tix rieb ka krax pa ciyang pa khaw kix tix pon,
jao pa lheu kix ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai ka gum lox ping nyap.
44 Viang yuh nan kawx,
yam kawn ot kix daux haktiex pui pa koeng,
aux ang doh tik vud ka kix khawm uik,
mai ang yuh kix laux leeing.
Phan yuh nan,
tag sang chix grai yuh ka gum lox sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai kix.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix.
45 Aux tag sang deeh cox yuh ka sijax mukheu mai kix pa hoik yuh aux tau mai tax du kix.
Yam an,
aux hoik plak jhi ka siawp sibrawm pa ting tix ka meung haktiex khawm uik,
mai aux hoik viex pui cie tix lih khaing Ai-cix,
son sang mawh aux Siyiex kix.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex.”
46 Lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap in plak piang hoik mawh pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si piang gawng Si-nai,
mai nhie nawh krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
