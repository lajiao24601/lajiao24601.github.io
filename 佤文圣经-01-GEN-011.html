Gawng mu Pa-piie
11
Yam jauh kawn pui khawm uik,
koe ngie lox tix ceu,
mai ah lox tix ceu khaing.
2 Yam vung viang ot kix plak lih singaix,
kix hoek ka haktiex dee lung Pa-pi-loeng,
mai ot tan heue.
3 Kix kum kawng paox tix ah nin:
“Hoek heue,
ex yuh lon tiex gleeing,
mai gleeing tom kaux.”
Kheu nan,
kix tom yuh lon tiex gleeing,
mai siao peeh khaox gix tang lon tiex gleeing.
4 Kix ah nin:
“Hoek heue!
Kieh ex sum veng tix mu,
veng an kieh koe gawng mu,
tom cux ka pai awm,
son sang krai ka min ex,
son ang ex prung prang tix khaing piang haktiex ku dauh heue.”
5 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex lih hoek,
sang jhak grawng pa sum kawn pui khankix ka veng an mai gawng mu an.
6 Nawh ah nin:
“Kix hoik mawh pui tix ceu,
si kah lox tix ceu;
viang mawh nan,
in mawh ngie yam jauh,
khaix in,
kix kawn sang nieb pon hawt yuh alo tix.
7 Hoek heue!
Kieh ex lih hu suk sag yuh lox si kah kix,
kieh lox si kah kix tang khaing paox tix.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh kix prung prang ka piang haktiex,
mai kix tom naox ka kaing veng pa yuh tix.
9 Mawh kheu veng in kok mawh Pa-piiexa;
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex suk sag yuh lox kawn pui dee an,
mai kieh kix prung prang ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee heue.
Doeh ceu cad Sang
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 1.24, 27)
10 In plak greem mawh doeh ceu cad Sang.
Khaix hoik kheem rawm haktiex ra num,
Sang koe ju tix yieh num mai koe kawn simex Ya-fa-sa.
11 Khaix an,
nawh baox im ju phuan yieh num,
baox koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
12 Ya-fa-sa koe ju tix ngoe phuan num keh Sa-la;
13 Khaix an,
nawh baox im ju pon yieh loe num,
mai baox koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
14 Sa-la koe ju tix ngoe num mai keh Si-paw;
15 khaix an nawh baox im ju pon yieh loe num,
mai baox koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
16 Si-paw koe ju tix ngoe pon num mai nawh koe kawn Fa-leux;
17 khaix an nawh baox im ju pon yieh tix ngoe num,
mai nawh baox koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
18 Fa-leux koe ju tix ngoe num mai koe kawn La-wu;
19 khaix an nawh baox im ju ra yieh dim num,
baox koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
20 La-wu koe ju tix ngoe ra num,
mai koe kawn Si-lux;
21 khaix an nawh baox im ju ra yieh aliah num,
mai koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
22 Si-lux koe ju tix ngoe num mai koe kawn Na-heux;
23 khaix an nawh baox im ju ra yieh num,
mai koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
24 Na-heux koe ju tix nga dim num mai koe kawn Tha-la;
25 khaix an nawh baox im ju tix yieh kao dim num,
mai koe kawn simex kawn bun heue.
26 Yam koe Tha-la atigliah num,
nawh hoik mawh keeing Ya-paw-lang,
Na-heux mai Ha-lang heue.
Doeh ceu cad Tha-la
27 Plak greem mawh doeh ceu cad Tha-la.
Tha-la koe kawn Ya-paw-lang,
Na-heux,
mai Ha-lang.
Ha-lang koe kawn Law-teux.
28 Yam yum Ha-lang daux haktiex paox ceu paox cad tix Wu-eu veng Pa-pi-loeng,
keeing nawh Tha-la kawn im ju.
29 Ya-paw-lang teei tix moeng Sa-lai;
Na-heux teei tix moeng Mi-cia kawn bun Ha-lang;
Ha-lang hoik koe kawn bun tix kaux kaux Yi-cia.
30 Sa-lai mawh bun pa neh,
ang chix koe kawn.
31 Tha-la viex kawn simex tix Ya-paw-lang mai kawn saux nawh Law-teux kawn simex Ha-lang,
mai mun nawh Sa-lai moeng Ya-paw-lang,
lih khaing veng Wu-eu haktiex Pa-pi-loeng,
hu ka meung Cia-nang.
Kix hoek ka Ha-lang,
tom ot tan.
32 Yam koe ju Tha-la ra yieh phuan num nawh yum daux veng Ha-lang heue.
