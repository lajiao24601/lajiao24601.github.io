Ka klawn tiex Ma-eu-tha
28
Khaix hoik pon yix tix luat luan,
yix tom taug tawng klawn tiex an mawh Ma-eu-tha.
2 Pui pa ot ka klawn tiex an kied chix kia ngien ka yix.
Yam an kheu lih lhiex,
praix klawng hoik kuat,
kix tom phlu ngu,
mai rieb ka yix.
3 Pao-law sut khix tix glien,
deuh ka daux ngu.
Koe si uing pa koe jai tix mu ang lai pon kham si aoh,
lih khaing dee ot,
mai vung vhien tix ka taix nawh.
4 Pui pa ot ka klawn tiex an yaox preue si uing an tix ka taix Pao-law,
ra lai krai ah nin:
“Pui in tee rien tix mawh pa pauh ka pui,
viang hoik pon tix luat luan khaing daux rawm sigrit sigra,
been rao ma kawn ang bax cu tix kieh nawh klawd ju heue.”
5 Viang mawh nan,
Pao-law si pluat si uing an cawd daux ngu,
to kaux nawh ang bax mat pie ceeix kawx.
6 Pui hun krox tix jhak grawng sang uah taix nawh,
mai grawng sang goh nawh tix yum dum mawx dum an;
viang mawh nan,
kix hoik krox tom rheeng,
mai ang yaox grawng pa yuh nawh ka mawx tix,
kix tom tieh tix ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Siyiex heue!”
7 Ang khan tix singai khaing dee an miex glang tix,
simiang Pu-pai-liox koe ma tix dauh.
Nawh gaux rhawm tix rieb ka yix,
mai kia ngien ka yix loe singaix.
8 Yam an,
keeing Pu-pai-liox koe jhawm saux si aoh mai jhawm saux vaig,
it piang ku,
Pao-law hoek ka lawg dee it nawh,
mai rawg buan son nawh,
deuh taix tix piang nawh pai nawh moi.
9 Khaix hoik yuh nawh nan,
pui pa koe jhawm saux ka klawn tiex an hoek khawm uik,
mai hoik pon tix moi khawm uik nang.
10 Kix tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka yix tom nie;
yam phat reu hu,
mai baox tawx ceu pa lo yix ka daux krax hoek daux reu heue.
Jauh ka Ma-eu-tha hoek ka Law-ma,
11 Khaix hoik luan loe khix,
yix brug reu pa hoek khaing Ya-li-sang-tax tix lhang;
ceu kaux reu in mawh “na bat mhai brawm”,
reu in kaog ka klawn tiex an ka daux bawg yam kuat.
12 Reu hoek ka Shui-la-kux,
yix tom kaog dee an loe singaix,
13 khaix an deeh phat ka reu,
hoek ka Li-ci-weng.
Ka daux singaix khaix koe bhaung plak seh,
mawh kheu ka daux ra singaix,
yix tom hoek ka Pu-tio-lix,
14 mai si pup kawn Khrit dee an tix blah;
kix ghaok yix kaog dee an aliah singaix.
Yuh nan,
yix tom hoek ka Law-ma.
15 Kawn Khrit Law-ma khankix mhong grawng pa hoek yix,
kix tom hoek tix rieb yix ka Ya-pi-wu bang dee yuh pui lai gee mai ka nyiex nyo.
Yam yaox Pao-law ka kix nawh yaog yaw Siyiex,
mai nie baox kua rhawm heue.
Pao-law ot ka Law-ma
16 Khaix hoik hoek yix ka Law-ma,
Pao-law hoik pon ka krax pa cu pui,
nawh paung ot tau mai lien pa pau nawh an tang dauh heue.
17 Khaix hoik luan ka daux loe singaix,
Pao-law kok simiang pui Yo-thaix hoek ka tix.
Kix hoek,
Pao-law ah nin ka kix:
“Paox ceu paox cad khankix eue,
viang aux ang bax yuh ka krax ceu pa phit lud ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
phan ang mawh nan,
lieh lheu ka been pa deuh tax du,
yuh ka mawx aux lai laung tix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai baox ap tix tawx ka daux tawm taix pui Law-ma.
18 Kix prawng ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox grawng phi map pa khaw aux tix yum,
mai koe rhawm tix sang ploi ka aux.
19 aViang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix tieb tie kah,
ang kieh aux tawng ka sang yuh tix,
aux tom krai ka Vawng,
ang mawh koe dee sang deuh aux phi map piang paox ceu paox cad tix.
20 Mawh kheu son kaing in,
aux sang khiox khaw pui ku kaux hoek tix krai lao tau.
Grawng viex aux ka maox ceng in,
mai hoik dawg mawh kheu son pa simie pui Yi-seu-liex ka kaw an heue.”
21 Kix krai ka Pao-law ah nin:
“Phan mawh son maix,
yix ang bax pon lox tid tix khaing pui Yo-thaix,
mai ang koe nang paox pa yuh kaing tau pa viex ka gum lox ceu tid tix hoek,
phan ang mawh nan,
krai ka grawng pa ang maix mhawm.
22 Viang mawh nan,
yix simie tix nghiet ka rhawm keud maix,
mawh kheu hoik tawng yix ka cub caw cie maix in,
hoik mawh pa koeng pui ka heue.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
kix hoik tat lox tau mai Pao-law son daux singaix pa sang khrawm tix.
Ka daux singaix an pui pa hoek ka dee ot Pao-law kied hun;
jauh pon nawb hoek pon bo,
kix krai ka gum lox meung Siyiex.
Nawh jhi ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si mai ka lai cao jhai,
mai nhie kix yum rhawm ka Ye-sux.
24 Phan mawh son lox pa krai nawh,
koe pui tix blah yum rhawm kah,
tix blah ang yum rhawm kah.
25 Kix ra lai ang khaw rhawm paox tix,
kix tom prung prang tix tang hu.
Sivoe sang prung prang tix tang hu,
Pao-law krai lox tix gum ah nin:
“Khuan Singax Singeh kieh cao jhai Yi-sai-yax si kah ka tax du pex,
hoik mawh pa khaw tete heue!
26 bYi-sai-yax ah nin:
maix hu krai ka pui hun ah nin:
pex hoik nghiet baox nghiet,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng,
hoik jhak baox jhak,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix yaox.
27 Mawh kheu hoik klan pui in kix rhawm rhi tix;
yhaok kix hoik thox tix,
ngai kix hoik nyip tix.
Phan ang mawh nan,
ngai kix tag sang chix yaox,
yhaok kix kawx tag sang chix mhong,
rhawm rhi kix kawx tag sang chix doh,
tag sang chix loh tix,
aux tom pai kix moi heue.”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix tawng,
gum lox krax taoh Siyiex hoik sidiex tix hu tawx ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
Kix tieh tix chix nghiet heue!”c
30 Pao-law hoik ot ka nyiex pa liag tix ra num.
Can pui pa hoek tix vhi ka nawh,
nawh hoik rieb tix khre ka kix khawm uik.
31 Nawh viex rhawm ting sidiex gum lox meung Siyiex,
mai sidiex gau ka kaing ceu pa dex dawt mai Simiang Ye-sux Khrit,
ang bax lai yaox krax pa tieb tie heue.
