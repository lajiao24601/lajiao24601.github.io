Ta-wue to mox tix khaing Sao-law
21
aTa-wue hoek ka Naw-pawx cao giex kaing li pa kaux Ya-si-mi-leux dee an.
Ya-si-mi-leux si yaung mai hoek ka nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai hoek dee in nong nong lie?”
2 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Aux mawh pa ploi simiang hoek dee in.
Simiang krai ka aux,
ang pon tix kieh pui tix kaux kawx tawng ka kaing pa yuh aux.
Phan mawh pui pa ot greem aux,
aux hoik tawx lox nhie kix hoek rieb aux ka haktiex tix dauh dauh.
3 Yam in,
koe maix pa ih ceu pa tix?
Tawx khao mun ka aux phuan lon,
phan ang mawh nan ca mawh ceu pa chix tix ih ang ah heue.”
4 Cao giex kaing li pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux ang koe khao mun pa chix ih pui khankix,
mawh ngie khao mun pa singax singeh pa koe khaing.
Phan pui pa ot greem maix khankix,
ang nyang bax dex ka bun ka daux ra loe singaix,
tag chix tix ih heue.”
5 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Ang bax koe.
Pui pa ot greem aux viang koe kaing pa tix ceu ceu tix yuh kawx,
mai kix kawx hoik hawt ka been pa kieh to kaux tix singax singeh,
daux in singaix,
kix nie koe kaing pa ting ceu tix yuh ang mawh lie?”
6 bMawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li teei khao mun pa singax singeh mai tawx ka Ta-wue;
mawh kheu nawh koe ngie khao mun pa singax singeh pa sang tan ka Siyiex,
taug mawh pa hoik teei lih khaing piang pheen pa singax singeh heue.
(
7 Cao kuang pa liang khawng brie Sao-law,
pui Yi-tong pa kaux To-yix jiet ot tan mai kix,
mawh kheu nawh,
hoek tix yuh kaing been li pa caw nawh tix sang yuh heue.)
8 Ta-wue ah nin ka Ya-si-mi-leux:
“Ka dee ot maix tin koe ang koe pliah mai vaig lie?
Simiang kied ki tawx aux hu krax,
mai ang koe bawg yam tix tien teei ka vaig mai ka kraung naing tang ceu aux heue.”
9 cYa-si-mi-leux pawk lox ah nin:
“Ka dee ot aux tin koe vaig ka tix mag,
mawh pa hoik pauh maix pui Fe-li-see pa kaux Keu-li-yax,
yam ot ka daux ngawd Yi-la.
Vaig gawn an deuh tix ot ka plak khaix teug sijax mukheu,
mai pua tix ka man.
Phan maix sang siao,
hu teei heue.
Vaig gawn an hoik mawh kraung naing pa koe tin tix ceu khaing heue.”
Ta-wue ah nin:
“Tawx aux cie heue,
ang lai koe vaig gawn pa lheu mhawm khaing in heue!”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue lih hu,
mox tix ka Sao-law,
mai to hoek ka Cia-theux simiang Ya-cie dee an.
11 dSiliex simiang Ya-cie ah nin ka simiang tix:
“An ang mawh Ta-wue simiang Yi-seu-liex lie?
Ang nawh hoik mawh Ta-wue pa rhax bun miex khankix yam graoh ah nin:
‘Sao-law hoik pauh pui yum kaing rheng,
Ta-wue hoik pauh pui yum kaing mheen’ an lie?”
12 Lox pa si kah kix Ta-wue hoik mhong kah;
rhawm rhi nawh koe krax lhat graung ka simiang Ya-cie,
13 mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom yuh tix yhot ka plak sivoe kix.
Kix sang tieb tie nawh,
nawh tom ca yuh tix nieb tiiem ku kried ngai lai ka ding venge,
mai rawm mia nawh hoik jauh tom hoek ka piang hauk jheeh.
14 Simiang Ya-cie ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Pex jhak,
in mawh pui yhot!
Yuh ka mawx pex lai viex nawh hoik dex aux lie?
15 Pui yhot pa ot tin kawn ang nyang bax kub nie lie?
Yuh ka mawx pex lai baox viex hoek tix kaux,
mai vung vied vung viang tix ot ka daux nyiex aux,
cheeik aux ang rhawm yaox lie?”
