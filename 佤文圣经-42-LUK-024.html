Grawng kaoh im Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.1–10; Lai Ma-kheu 16.1–8; Lai Yaw-hang 20.1–10)
24
Ka daux singaix tuah,
yam jauh praix tix riang,
bun khankix viex sidah bhawng pa hoik rien khre tix hu ka si meeig.
2 Kix yaox simaox hoik yoi tix hu khaing plak sivoe moing daux si meeig,
3 kix tom laig daux si meeig,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox to kaux pa yum Simiang Ye-sux.
4 Yam kawn keud kix ka yuh ka mawx,
dum mawx dum an,
koe pui ra kaux pa jhan riang kraung ceep cung ka praok kix;
5 kix kied lhat graung tete,
mai gup tix piang tiex.
Pui an kiex ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai sawg pui im ka daux pui yum lie?
6 aNawh ang ot dee in;
nawh hoik kaoh im.
Tag caw tix doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai nawh ka pex yam ot ka haktiex Cia-li-lix;
nawh ah nin:
7 ‘Kawn kawn pui tag sang caw tix tawx ka daux tawm taix pui phi map,
mai tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak,
ka daux loe singaix deeh kaoh im heue.’ ”
8 Kix tom taug doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai Ye-sux,
9 kix tom deeh ing khaing si meeig,
mai krai can kaing pa hoik yaox kix khawm uik ka kawn cau kao tix kaux,
mai ka pui tang kaux khankix.
10 Bun pa hoek tix krai ka can kaing in kix khawm uik ka kawn cau hoik mawh khawm Ma-li-yax pa kaux Maw-ta-la,
Yaw-ya-na,
Ma-li-yax miex Ya-keux,
mai bun miex pa hu tau mai kix.
11 Viang mawh nan,
kawn cau ce bun in kix nieb pon krai tix,
kix ang yum rhawm ka lox pa krai kix.
12 Viang mawh nan,
Pi-teux yaog tix kaoh to hoek ka si meeig,
nawh num to kaux tix jhak ka daux si meeig,
mai yaox ngie man keei sum an khaing,
ang lai koe tang ceu.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh deeh ing,
mai hoik kied daox deng ka kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh heueb.
Ka daux krax Yi-ma-wu-see
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.12–13)
13 Ka daux ngaix an,
daux pui gau koe pui ra kaux sang hu ka yaong tix yaong.
Ceu kaux yaong in mawh Yi-ma-wu-see,
singai Yie-lu-sa-leung been kao tix kongli.
14 Kix krai lao ka kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh in khawm uik leeing lawng krax.
15 Yam kawn krai lao kix kah,
Ye-sux hu dex ka kix,
mai hu tau mai kix;
16 kix yaox ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
ang tawng mawh nawh.
17 Ye-sux chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Hoik krai lao pex ceu pa tix mai hu tix lie?”
Kix tom kaog,
pak ngai kix hoik voi.
18 Ka daux kix koe pui tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Keu-lio-pa,
chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Khex mawh maix nyo nyiex pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa ang tawng ka kaing ceu pa hoik lih tix mawh plak an ka daux ra loe singaix lie?”
19 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh kaing ceu Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux heue!
Nawh hoik mawh cao jhai,
ka plak sivoe Siyiex mai pui hun,
lox pa hoik krai nawh mai kaing ceu pa hoik yuh nawh kied mawh pa koe riang koe rao tete.
20 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai simiang ex nieb pon giex ka nawh,
prawng ca nawh yum,
tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak.
21 Yix hoik dawg simie nawh mawh kaw an pa tag sang hoek tix taoh pui Yi-seu-liex heue!
Ja lao ang mawh nan,
kaing in hoik lih tix mawh loe ngaix.
22 Ka daux yix koe bun miex ra loe kaux kied kieh yix daox deng,
yam pon ngawb cao cao kix hu ka si meeig dee an,
23 mai ang lai yaox to kaux Ye-sux.
Kix tom hoek tix krai ah nin,
yix hoik yaox kawn cau Siyiex,
khaix an kawn cau Siyiex krai hoik kaoh im Ye-sux ka kix.
24 Ka daux yix koe pui hu jhak ka daux si meeig,
pa yaox kix hoik awm pa krai bun miex an kix yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
ang lai yaox to kaux Ye-sux heue.”
25 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex kied daox tete heue!
Lox pa hoik krai cao jhai yuh ka mawx pex lai kawn kieh mawh pa nu tix yum rhawm ka yuh nan lie?
26 Khrit khex ang nawh tag sang kham grawng in kix khawm uik,
nawh tom tag sang laig ka daux min plak ngai lie?”
27 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh hoik hawt ka lox pa tiiem Maw-si mai cao jhai yuh nan,
mai jauh tix kah ka gum lox pa hoik tiiem phuk lai sigang khri ka son to kaux tix khawm uik ka kix heue.
28 Kix hoik dex ka nyiex yaong pa sang hu kix kah,
Ye-sux awm pa kawn sang nied tix hu krax yuh nan,
29 viang mawh nan,
kix ca nhie nawh ot ah nin ka nawh:
“Singaix hoik laig daux gawng,
praix hoik faik,
khiox khaw maix paung tau mai yix heue!”
Ye-sux tom laig daux nyiex,
mai sang paung tau mai kix.
30 Yam ngawm kix tix sawm,
Ye-sux teei khao mun,
yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
khaix an si vit tix tawx khao mun ka kix.
31 Ngai kix riang dum mawx dum an,
kix tom taug tawng ka nawh;
viang mawh nan,
dum mawx dum an,
ang lai yaox ka Ye-sux.
32 Kix ra lai ah nin:
“Nawh hoik si kah tau mai ex ka daux krax,
yam kah nawh phuk lai sigang khri son ex,
rhawm rhi ex ang hoik rup awm ngu yuh nan lie?”
33 Kix nied tix yaog tix hu,
mai deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Ka dee an,
kix yaox kawn cau kao tix kaux mai pui tang kaux khankix,
khrawm paox tix ot tau ka dee an,
34 yam kawn ah kix nin:
“Simiang Ye-sux hoik kaoh im tete;
nawh hoik jhi Si-meung yaox tix heue!”
35 Pui an kiex kawx krai ceu pa hoik yaox tix daux krax,
mai ka grawng pa hoik tawng kix ka nawh yam si vit Simiang Ye-sux tix tawx khao mun yuh ka mawx ka kix heue.
Ye-sux jhi pui gau yaox tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.16–20; Lai Ma-kheu 16.14–18; Lai Yaw-hang 20.19–23; Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 1.6–8)
36 Yam kawn krai lao kix ka kaing ceu in kix,
dum mawx dum an,
Simiang Ye-sux hoek tix cung ka si nax kix,
ah nin ka kix:
“Kieh pex pon krax pa nyawm rhawm heuec!”
37 Kix lhat graung mai si yaung si kua,
mai ce tix yaox ka miang.
38 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai laux rhawm lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai ang yum rhawm ka lie?
39 Jhak tix yaox ka taix caong aux heue!
Mawh aux,
ang mawh pui tang kaux heue!
Pu pi tix yaox ka aux,
mai pex tom taug tawng;
miang ang koe nex koe nham koe si-ang si niag,
pex jhak aux hoik koe heue.”
40 Nawh hoik krai yuh nin,
tom tawx kix jhak ka taix caong tixd.
41 Kix kawn ang yum rhawm kah,
viang mawh nan,
hoik gaux rhawm ka tete.
Nawh chawk kix ah nin:
“Koe pex ceu pa ih pa tix dee in lie?”
42 Kix tom teei kax tong tix bliah tawx ka nawh.
43 Nawh rieb kah,
mai ih nawh ka plak sivoe ngai kix heue.
44 Khaix an Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Kaing in kix khawm uik,
aux hoik krai ka pex yam ot aux tau mai pex ka daux singaix pa hoik luan ah nin:
lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
phuk lai cao jhai,
mai pa krai Lai Yaog Yaw ka kaing ceu pa dex dawt tau mai aux,
tag sang lih tix mawh ku ceu heue.”
45 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh paoh ka rhawm rhi kix,
mai kieh kix tawng sidawng ka gum lox phuk lai sigang khri,
46 mai baox ah nin ka kix:
“Phuk lai sigang khri hoik tiiem ah nin:
Khrit tag sang kham krax yum,
ka daux loe ngaix nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum.
47 Pex tag caw tix yuh kaing ka daux ceu kaux nawh,
mai sidiex ka gum lox pa kieh pui loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai ka gum lox pa pluat ploi phi map,
jauh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung hoek ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
48 Pex hoik mawh pui pa kham sakse kaing ceu in kix.
49 eAux tag sang hoek tix tawx ceu pa hoik ap pong keeing aux ka aux ka pex;
pex tag caw tix krox jun ka daux veng,
tom hoik lih hoek riang rao pa hoek khaing plak piang ka piang pex heue.”
Rieb Ye-sux haok rao ma
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.19–20; Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 1.9–11)
50 fKhaix an,
Ye-sux thung kix lih khaing veng hoek ka Paw-ta-ni.
Nawh yaog taix tix nawb buan piang kix ka dee an.
51 Nawh lih hu khaing kix yam nawb buan piang kix,
khaix an rieb nawh haok piang rao mag.
52 Kix tom krub ka nawh,
mai gaux rhawm gaux rhi mai deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
53 Kix rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex ka daux nyiex buan jheeng jheeng heue.
