Pao-law haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
21
Khaix hoik guah ex tix tang hu khaing kix,
ex tom phat reu hu.
Reu phat tix hu tom hoek klawn tiex daux rawm sigrit sigra Keu-see,
ka daux ngaix khaix hoek ka Law-tix,
jauh tix dee an hu ka Pha-ta-la.
2 Si pup reu iak tix mu ka Pha-ta-la pa sang hu plak Fe-ni-ci,
ex tom haok daux reu phat hu.
3 Phat reu tom hoik kok chix yaox tix ka Sai-phu-lu-see,
reu via hu haktiex plak seh,
mai veeh tix plak Shui-li-yax.
Ex lih hoek praok rawm ka Thai-eux,
mawh kheu sang tauh reu kraung viex tix dee in.
4 Ex yaox pui gau tix blah dee in,
ex tom paung ot tau mai kix aliah ngaix.
Kix pon krax pa duk thung Khuan Singax Singeh,
mai ang cu Pao-law haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
5 Viang mawh nan,
bawg yam laux ex tix dee in hoik hoek,
ex nied tix hu krax pa hu tix.
Kawn Khrit mai moeng kawn kix tawx ex hoek ka plak praix veng khawm uik.
Pui hun hoik grux ngawng tix rawg buan ka daux pak mhaik khawm uik,
6 khaix an ra lai guah paox tix tang hu.
Ex tom haok daux reu,
kix tom deeh ing ka nyiex heue.
7 Ex ca niet tix phat reu hu,
jauh ka Thai-eux hoek ka To-li-mai,
chawk cheng kawn Khrit haktiex an mhawm ot,
mai paung ot tau mai kix tix ngaix.
8 aKa daux ngaix khaix ex lih hu khaing dee an,
hoek ka Khai-sha-li-yax,
ka nyiex ma Fe-lix,
mai paung ot tau mai nawh.
Nawh mawh pui cao teem coi ka daux aliah kaux pa hoik laug ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
9 Nawh hoik koe kawn bun pon kaux pa ang bax koe mex,
kix hoik mawh pa koe khawng phak buan son tix sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex.
10 bKhaix hoik paung ot ex dee an ra loe singaix,
koe cao jhai tix kaux pa kaux Ya-cia-pu,
nawh kaoh khaing Yo-thaix.
11 Nawh hoek tix jhak ka ex,
mai teei maox mad nyoi Pao-law,
phrawk taix caong tix kah,
ah nin:
“Khuan Singax Singeh ah nin:
cao maox mad nyoi in tag sang kham krax pa phrawk pui Yo-thaix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung yuh nin,
khaix an ap tix tawx nawh ka pui tang ceu tang cad heue.”
12 Yam mhong ex ka lox in,
ex tom rom tix rawg buan tau mai pui haktiex an,
ang cu Pao-law haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
13 Viang mawh nan,
nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai yiiem yuh nin,
mai kieh rhawm rhi aux laux lie?
Kheu son Ye-sux,
ja lao pui ang phrawk aux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
viang yum kawx dee an aux hoik cu ka heue.”
14 Ex ang lai tawng ka sang krai ka nawh,
tom thox jheeh tix khaing,
mai ah nin:
“Kieh alo Siyiex yuh lih mawh heue.”
15 Ex ot dee an ra loe ngaix,
tom khre kraung khrai tix,
haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
16 Koe pui gau tix blah pa hoek khaing Khai-sha-li-yax hu tau mai ex,
mai viex ex hoek paung ka nyiex ma Na-soeng pui Sai-phu-lu-see;c
pui in mawh pui gau pa koe sivoe an heue.
Pao-law hoek tix vhi ka Ya-keux
17 Yam hoek ex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kawn Khrit khankix gaux rhawm tix rien khre ka ex.
18 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
Pao-law hu ka Ya-keux tau mai ex;
can kuad song ceu cao kawx ot dee an khawm uik.
19 Pao-law chawk cheng kix mhawm ot,
khaix an krai kix mhong grawng pa siao Siyiex tix yuh kaing jhaw ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad yuh ka mawx ka kix khawm uik.
20 Kix mhong kah,
mai yaog yaw Siyiex,
mai baox krai ka Pao-law ah nin:
“Pux aik eue,
grawh grawng in mawh nin:
ka daux pui Yo-thaix koe kawn Khrit kaing mheen,
kix hoik mawh pui pa sau riang tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si.
21 Yam hoik mhong kix pa sidiex gau maix pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad,
tik vud ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
ang lai cu kawn nyawm Pat-hak tix,
mai ang siao lai priex tix hawt ka been pa deuh pui Yo-thaix khankix.”
22 “Kix tee rien tix mhong grawng pa hoek maix ka haktiex in,
ex sang yuh ka mawx lie?
23 dYam in viex maix hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai yix heue!
Dee in koe pui pon kaux pa hoik ap tix plud ka lox pa hoik deuh tix;
24 maix viex kix hu,
mai yuh been pa singax tau mai kix,
tawx mau son kix.
Yuh nin,
kix tom tag chix tix khlao kaing tix.
Phan maix yuh nin,
pui hun tom taug tawng,
pa hoik mhong kix grawng pa dex dawt tau mai maix ang mawh tete,
khaix an mai hoik tawng to kaux maix kawx hoik priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si.
25 ePhan mawh son kawn Khrit pa ot ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad,
ex hoik tiiem lai tawx lox ka kix ang pon tix ih ka priex preem pa tan lu pui ka riang rao,
ang pon tix ih ka nham mai ka khawng brie pa rhawt pui yum,
ang pon tix yuh krax pa phit vao.”
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law viex pui an kix hu,
mai yuh been pa singax tau mai kix ka daux ngaix khaix.
Nawh baox hu krai pao singaix bawg yam hoik naog been pa singax ka nyiex buan,
krox jun cao giex kaing li tan lu khawng tan son kix ku kaux heue.
Giex Pao-law ka daux nyiex buan
27 Yam hoik dex sang si dauh bawg yam aliah singaix an,
koe pui Yo-thaix tix blah pa hoek khaing haktiex Ya-si-yax,
yaox Pao-law ka daux nyiex buan.
Kix tom kieh pui hun suk sag,
giex ka Pao-law,
28 mai kok yo ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
hoek teem ka aux heue!
Pui in hoik hu tix sidiex ku dauh ku dee,
nawh tieh vheen ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
mai baox tieh vheen ka nyiex buan in,
yam in nawh kawx baox viex pui Si-lax laig daux nyiex buan,
mai hoik kieh dee singax in seeing brai heue!” (
29 fKix ah yuh nin hoik mawh kheu yaox kix Theu-law-fe-maw pui Yi-fu-saw ot tau mai Pao-law ka daux veng,
ce Pao-law hoik sax viex nawh laig daux nyiex buan heue.)
30 Yam an,
daux veng hoik suk sag khawm uik,
pui hun to tix hoek giex ka Pao-law,
mai reu nawh lih khaing daux nyiex buan;
siviex nyiex buan nyip tix dum mawx dum an.
31 Yam ce pui cau nyhau khankix tix sang pauh ka Pao-law,
koe pui krai ka simiang naing pa ot ka Law-ma mhong,
krai hoik suk sag ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik.
32 Simiang naing an viex pui naing mai simiang naing ra loe kaux,
nied hu ka pui hun dee an.
Yam yaox kix ka simiang naing an mai ka pui naing,
kix tom naox lauh tix ka Pao-law.
33 Simiang naing an hoek dex kix mai giex ka Pao-law,
tawx lox nhie kix teei maox ceng ra nyaing phrawk ka Pao-law.
Nawh chawk ah nin:
“Pui in mawh pui mawx?
Yuh nawh kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
34 Ka daux pui hun koe pui tix blah kok pui in,
koe tix blah kok ka pui an,
hoik lih tix lawh mawx khawm uik,
mai ang kieh simiang naing an tawng ka sang si kaw tix yaox,
mawh kheu nan,
tawx lox nhie kix viex Pao-law laig daux nyiex bang.
35 Pao-law jiet taug sang jauh tix haok ka glawk bawng,
mawh kheu kied cau nyhau pui hun,
pui naing khankix tom grawng Pao-law hu.
36 Pui hun hawt khaix kix khawm uik,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Pauh ka nawh heue!”
Pao-law krai lox son to kaux tix
37 Yam hoik dex sang viex nawh laig daux nyiex bang,
Pao-law krai ka simiang naing ah nin:
“Chix ang chix tix kieh aux krai lox ka maix tix gum?”
Simiang naing an pawk ah nin:
“Maix kawx hoik tawng ka lox Si-lax heue!
38 Phan mawh nan,
ang maix hoik mawh pui Ai-cix an pa yuh krax pa suk sag,
pa thung pui cau nyhau pon rheng kaux to hu daux tiag lie?”
39 Pao-law pawk ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui Yo-thaix,
pa keh ka veng Ta-sux haktiex Ci-li-cia,
mawh pui pa ot ka daux veng pa ting min tix mu.
Khiox khaw maix kieh aux krai lox ka pui hun liak heue!”
40 Simiang naing an cu lox nawh.
Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law cung ka piang glawk bawng,
yeeb taix tix ka pui hun.
Pui hun hoik yien yawg tix khawm uik,
Pao-law tom siao lox Si-paw-lai si kah ka kix heue.
