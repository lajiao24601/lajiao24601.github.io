Pi-teux sidiex gum lox buan ka Keu-ni-lio
10
Ka daux Khai-sha-li-yax koe pui tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Keu-ni-lio,
mawh simiang naing pui naing Law-ma ka daux “nyiex bang Yi-ta-lix”.
2 Nawh mawh pui pa yum rhawm tix kaux;
nawh khawm pui nyiex nawh hoik mawh pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex khawm uik,
kix hoik rien si pieh rhawm tix teem coi ka pui Yo-thaix pa hot sibhawm jheeng jheeng,
mai baox koe krax gaux rhawm tix rawg buan ka Siyiex jheeng jheeng.
3 Koe ka daux tix ngaix,
yam greung ngaix loe taix nari,
nawh hoik pon tix yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu;
ka daux riang su sai an nawh hoik yaox sidawng ka kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux laig daux nyiex,
mai kok nawh ah nin:
“Keu-ni-lio heue!”
4 Nawh dong ngai tix jhak ka kawn cau Siyiex,
kied lhat graung tete,
tom ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ceu pa tix lie?”
Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik nghiet ka lox rawg maix buan,
mai hoik yaox ka kaing mhawm pa hoik teem coi maix ka pui.
5 Yam in maix tag caw tix ploi pui hu ka Yaw-pha,
kok pui pa kaux Si-meung Pi-teux hoek.
6 Nawh paung ot ka daux nyiex ma Si-meung pa mawh cang mai hak;
nyiex ma Si-meung ot ka praok rawm sigrit sigra.”
7 Khaix hoik lih kawn cau Siyiex an pa si kah tau mai nawh,
Keu-ni-lio kok siliex daux nyiex ma tix ra kaux,
mai pui naing pa pau pa koe krax yum rhawm tix kaux pa pau ka nawh jheeng jheeng hoek,
8 mai krai kaing pa hoik yaox tix ka kix khawm uik,
khaix an ploi kix hu ka Yaw-pha heue.
9 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
kix hu krax dex sang hoek ka Yaw-pha.
Bawg yam greung ngaix,
Pi-teux haok rawg buan ka piang braux nyiex.
10 Nawh hoik cai rhawm,
mai simie tix ih ceu pa ih.
Yam hoik rien khre pui an ka priex ngawb,
nawh hoik pon tix yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu,
11 hoik yaox pa baoh rao ma,
koe kraung tix ceu lih hoek khaing rao ma,
pa awm man vieh tix plah,
man in khawm pon daog hoik phrawk tix kaux,
mai ploi tix cawd piang tiex.
12 Ka daux koe to sat sim saik ku ceu,
mai baox koe viag via.
13 Koe lox ah nin ka nawh:
“Pi-teux,
kaoh,
pauh tix ih ka heue!”
14 Viang mawh nan,
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ang doh chix!
Aux ang sax ih ka ceu pa seeing pa brai pa ang singax tix bawg kawx heue.”
15 Lox an ka daux bawg ra ah nin ka nawh:
“Ceu pa hoik een Siyiex mawh pa singax singeh,
maix ang pon tix kieh mawh pa seeing pa brai.”
16 Yuh nin,
ka daux loe bawg,
kraung ceu an kix tom deeh yaog tix haok ka rao ma heue.
17 Pi-teux kawn ca keud tix yaox,
ang tawng riang su sai pa hoik yaox tix deeh bleeh mawh ceu pa tix.
Yam an,
pui pa ploi Keu-ni-lio hoik yaox ka nyiex ma Si-meung,
mai cung ka diang siviex plak praix,
18 kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Mawh ang mawh pa kaux Si-meung Pi-teux paung ot dee in lie?”
19 Yam kawn keud Pi-teux ka dee bleeh riang su sai an mawh ceu pa tix,
Khuan Singax Singeh krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Koe pui loe kauxa hoek tix sawg ka maix heue!
20 Maix kaoh lih hu,
mai hu tau mai kix,
ang lai haoh rhawm keud,
kheu hoik mawh aux pa ploi kix hoek heue.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-teux lih hu,
mai krai ka pui an kix ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh pui pa sawg pex.
Pex hoek mawh son ceu pa tix lie?”
22 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh Keu-ni-lio simiang yix pa ploi yix hoek;
nawh hoik mawh pui rauh see pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex tix kaux,
mai pui pa hoik nab yam yung yi pui Yo-thaix ka tix kaux.
Koe kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux krai ka nawh,
nhie nawh kok maix hoek daux nyiex ma nawh,
son sang pon tix rieb ka krax pa sidiex gau maix heue.”
23 Pi-teux tom kok kix laig daux nyiex,
mai rieb kix paung it dee an heue.
Ka daux ngaix khaix,
Pi-teux yaog tix hu tau mai kix;
khawm kawn Khrit ra loe kaux an kix,
pa kaoh khaing Yaw-pha kawx hu tau mai nawh.
24 Baox koe tix ngaix,
kix hoek ka Khai-sha-li-yax.
Keu-ni-lio hoik ghaok paox grawm tix krox jun nawh ka nyiex.
25 Yam jauh Pi-teux tix laig daux nyiex,
Keu-ni-lio hoek dex nawh,
mai gup tix krub ka sivoe caong nawh.
26 Pi-teux nied tix yaog nawh kaoh,
mai ah nin:
“Kaoh,
aux kawx hoik mawh kawn pui.”
27 Pi-teux si kah tau mai Keu-ni-lio mai yam hu tix laig daux nyiex.
Nawh yaox pui tom nie khrawm paox tix ka daux nyiex,
28 nawh tom ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoik tawng,
phan hawt ka been ex,
pui Yo-thaix ang cu tix yuh paox pie lai ling ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik krai ka aux,
ang pon tix kieh pui tix kaux kawx mawh ceu pa ang singax singeh,
phan ang mawh nan,
mawh pa dex mai been haktiex.
29 Mawh kheu nan,
yam ploi pex pui hoek kok aux,
aux hoek mai ang bax tieb tie kah.
Yam in aux chawk pex,
pex kok aux hoek mawh son ceu pa tix lie?”
30 Keu-ni-lio ah nin:
“Singaix pa hoik luan pon ngaix,
yam greung ngaix been loe taix nari,
aux rawg buan ka nyiex.
Dum mawx dum an,
koe pui pa ceep kraung toh riang cung ka plak sivoe ngai aux tix kaux,
31 nawh ah nin:
‘Keu-ni-lio!
Siyiex hoik nghiet ka lox rawg maix buan,
mai hoik doh keud ka kaing mhawm pa teem coi maix ka pui.
32 Maix tag caw tix ploi pui hu ka Yaw-pha,
kok pui pa kaux Si-meung Pi-teux an hoek.
Nawh paung ot ka nyiex ma Si-meung pa mawh cang mai hak;
nyiex ma Si-meung ot ka praok rawm sigrit sigra.’
33 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom ploi pui hu kok maix dum mawx dum an;
maix hoik gaux rhawm tix hoek,
in hoik mawh kaing pa mhawm tete.
Yam in ex hoik ot ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex khawm uik,
mai sang nghiet ka lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Ye-sux maix krai khawm uik heue.”
Pi-teux tawx lox sidiex
34 bPi-teux jauh tix ah nin:
“Yam in aux hoik tawng tete,
Siyiex hoik kia ngien ka can pui khawm uik khaw paox tix.
35 Ca mawh lhat graung yung yi ka nawh,
yuh kaing pa rauh pa see,
mawh pui ceu tix ang ah nawh hoik khaw rhawm ka khawm uik.
36 Pex hoik tawng,
gum lox pa hoik teei nawh tix sidiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
ka daux Simiang Ye-sux Khrit simiang pa ting ka piang khawm uik,
mawh gum lox buan pa nhim.
37 Glang hoik sidiex Yaw-hang ka gum lox buan been krax cub com,
kaing ceu pa ting pa lih tix koe jauh ka haktiex Cia-li-lix tauh hu ka haktiex Yo-thaix khawm uik,
pex hoik tawng kah.
38 Pex kawx hoik tawng ka Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux.
Siyiex hoik teei Khuan Singax Singeh mai siawp sibrawm klup ka piang to kaux nawh yuh ka mawx,
mai kieh nawh tauh hu ka haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
mai yuh kaing pa mhawm,
mai kieh pui pa giex gang phi caox moi khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik ot tau Siyiex mai nawh.
39 Yix hoik mawh pui pa kham sakse kaing in kix pa hoik yuh ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka haktiex Yo-thaix khawm uik.
Kix hoik tawk nawh yum piang khaox lhak.”
40 “Viang mawh nan,
ka daux loe singaix,
Siyiex hoik deeh kieh nawh kaoh im,
khaix an jhi kawn pui yaox;
41 nawh ang jhi pui hun yaox tix,
mai jhi ngie ex in kix pa mawh pui pa kham sakse pa hoik laug Siyiex yaox tix khaing.
Khaix hoik deeh nawh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
ex kawx hoik sax sawm rheep tau mai nawh.
42 Nawh tawx lox nhie ex sidiex gum lox buan ka kawn pui,
kham grawng hoik mawh nawh cao pa sum Siyiex mawh cao prawng ca ka pui im mai pui yum.
43 Can cao jhai khawm uik kawx hoik kham sakse son nawh;
kix hoik ah nin khawm uik,
can pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh,
hoik chix tix pon krax pa pluat ploi phi map ka daux ceu kaux nawh khawm uik heue.”
Pui tang ceu tang cad rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh
44 Yam kawn sidiex Pi-teux,
Khuan Singax Singeh lih hoek ka piang to kaux can pui pa hoik rieb ka gum lox in khawm uik.
45 Kawn Khrit Yo-thaix pa hawt ka Pi-teux khaing haktiex Yaw-pha khankix,
hoik yaox nang grawng pa klup Siyiex tix tawx khawng phak buan son Khuan Singax Singeh ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
kied daox deng ka tete.
46 Mawh kheu hoik mhong kix si kah pui in kix ka lox daux khuan,
khaix an yaog yaw pa ting pa lhaong Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-teux ah nin:
47 “Pui in kix hoik rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh awm ex yuh nan,
pui mawx sang pon tieb tie kix ang rieb ka krax pa cub com lie?”
48 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tawx lox nhie kix cub com ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux Khrit.
Kix baox khiox khaw Pi-teux paung ot mai kix ra loe ngaix heue.
