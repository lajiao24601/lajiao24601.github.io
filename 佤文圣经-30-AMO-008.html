Riang su sai plix khaox
8
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tawx aux yaox riang su sai tix ceu;
aux hoik yaox ka plix khaox teem tix khrawng.
2 Nawh chawk aux ah nin:
“A-maw-see,
yaox maix ceu pa tix lie?”
Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Hoik yaox ka plix khaox teem tix khrawng.”
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Singaix bawg yam si dauha pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex hoik hoek.
Bawg in aux ang doh lai pluat ploi ka phi map kix heue!
3 Ka daux singaix an,
lox rhax ka daux nyiex haw simiang tag sang tieh tix lih mawh lox laux rhawm laux rhi;
to kaux yum hoik koe ku dauh ku dee,
hoik yien yi khawm uik heue.”
Kaing ang mhawm pa hoek piang pui Yi-seu-liex
4 Pex pui in kix gut kheem ka pui hot,
sux ka pui sibhawm,
pex nghiet heue!
5 To kaux pex ah nin:
“Singaix poi pa singax singeh nied luan hu heue!
Son sang yaox yix tix hu coh ka nghox koi.
Singaix tuah nied si dauh heue!
Son sang yaox yix tix hu yuh lai gee.
Yix chix tix yaog ka ngawh jum lhaong,
teei jiang ang rauh see mai mawk tong pie lhien ka nyo si nie khankix.
6 Yix chix tix yaog ngawh jum lhaong mai coh ka nghox ca pa ang mhawm,
yix tag sang sawg ka pui hot pa ang pon plud ka ceuk tix,
teei ngawh jum kiiep tix ku liag nawh mawh siliex cawng ka heue.”
7 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tiex tu ah nin:
“Aux ang doh piawm ka kaing yuh pui Yi-seu-liex pa laux pa leeing.
8 Aux tag sang kieh haktiex yung yang,
kawn pui ku kaux tag sang si pup ka krax kha rhawm.
Haktiex tag sang yung yang khawm uik,
awm yam naog yam hit rawm klawng Ni-law yuh nan.
9 Hoek ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang kieh singaix klip yam greung ngaix,
mai kieh pon ngaix tieh tix lih mawh pa faik.
In mawh lox pa ah aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong heue.
10 Aux tag sang kieh poi yuh pex krax gaux rhawm tieh tix lih mawh been si paung pui ka pui yum,
lox rhax gaux rhawm tieh tix lih mawh lox yiiem ka daux krax laux rhawm.
Aux tag sang kieh pex khlao hauk kaing tix,
mai ceep ka man keei sum.
Pex tag sang laux rhawm awm pa yum kawn tix kaux yuh nan.
An hoik mawh singaix pa laux rhawm leeing lang singaix heue.
11 “Bawg yam hoik hoek,
aux tag sang kieh haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex sibhawm ku dauh.
Kix cai rhawm,
ang mawh kheu ang yaox tix ih ka khao mun;
kix simie tix nyaux rawm,
ang mawh kheu ang yaox tix nyaux rawm.
Kix simie tix nyaux rawm hoik mawh kheu ang kix chix mhong ka lox Simiang Siyiex.
In hoik mawh lox pa krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong heue.
12 Jauh ka plak seh hoek ka plak laong,
jauh ka plak lih hoek ka plak klip singaix,
kix tag sang hu sawg gum lox Simiang Siyiex ku dauh ku dee,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang yaox.
13 Ka daux singaix an,
pie kawn bun nhum ju pa mhawm grang,
mai kawn simex num ju pa ting riang kawx tag sang goh kheu simie tix nyaux rawm.
14 Pui pa hoik krub ka riang rao Sa-ma-li-yax an kix,
hoik ap pong tix tiex tu ka siyiex Tang mai ka siyiex Piie-see-pa tag sang goh khawm uik,
mai ang lai baox pon kaoh cung heue.”
