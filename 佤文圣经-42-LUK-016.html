Phek tiiem ka cao kuang nyiex pa koe rhawm lhak
16
Ye-sux krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Koe cao kuang nyiex cao mi tix kaux;
koe pui deuh phi map ka nawh,
krai mai cao kuang nyiex in ka sin ngao simiang cao nyiex tix,
2 Simiang cao nyiex tom kok cao kuang nyiex in hoek,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Pa hoik mhong aux mawh kaing ceu pa tix lie?
Tawx phuk lai sawn maix mau ka aux heue,
maix ang lai pon tix yuh kaing pa kuang nyiex heue.’
3 Pui cao kuang nyiex an keud daux rhawm tix ah nin:
‘Simiang aux tag sang tik ka aux,
sang yuh aux ka mawx hu sivoe ngai lie?
Sang yuh kaing khaw lie?
Ang koe riang tix yuh;
sang caung tix ih lie?
Lhat tix tik pak ngai tix.
4 Mawh heue,
aux hoik tawng grawng pa sang yuh tix,
yam ang aux yaox kaing yuh tix,
son sang rieb paox grawm aux ka aux hoek paung nyiex tix heue.’
5 “Mawh kheu nan,
nawh kok pui pa cheuk ka simiang tix hoek ku kaux.
Nawh krai ka daux tix kaux ah nin:
‘Ceuk maix ka simiang aux miex ding?’
6 Nawh pawk ah nin:
‘Bee sime tix yieh pung heue.’
Cao kuang nyiex an ah nin:
‘In mawh pa ceuk maix,
nied hoek tix ngawm,
loh mawh tix phuan pung heue.’
7 Nawh baox chawk tang kaux tix kaux ah nin:
‘Maix kawx,
maix ceuk miex ding?’
Nawh pawk ah nin:
‘Nghox ca tix yieh glawm heue.’
Cao kuang nyiex an ah nin:
‘In mawh pa ceuk maix,
loh mawh tix daix glawm heue.’
8 “Simiang cao nyiex an yaw ka krax lhak pa yuh cao kuang nyiex pa ang rauh ang see in.
Mawh kheu kaing pa yuh nawh ka piang haktiex,
pui pa dex mai haktiex nie blawn lhak khaing pui pa ot ka daux pa riang heue.”
9 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex,
tag caw tix teei siao mau pa koe ka haktiex singaix yam in yuh paox grawm kah,
yuh nin yam hoik uik mau an,
maix tag chix tix rieb hoek ka nyiex ma pa koe leeing ju.
10 Pui tix kaux kaux phan nawh hoik kaux sijax ka daux kaing pa iak khankix,
ka daux kaing pa ting kawx hoik kaux sijax kah;
pui tix kaux kaux phan nawh ang rauh see ka daux kaing pa iak khankix,
ka daux kaing pa ting kawx ang rauh see.
11 Phan pex ang kaux sijax ka daux pa yuh kaing priex mau khri ka haktiex singaix yam in,
pui mawx sang tawx pex priex ka sin ngao pa mawh pa meh lie?
12 Phan pex ang kaux sijax ka pa mawh cie pui tang kaux,
pui mawx sang tawx cie tix ka pex lie?
13 a“Ang koe pui pa pon rien khre ka simiang ra kaux.
Phan nawh ang koeng ka in mai tag muh ka an,
phan kieh in ting ceu mai tag kieh an iak ceu.
Pex ang doh pon mawh siliex Siyiex,
mai baox mawh siliex cawng mau khri heue.”
Krax sidiex tang ceu
(Lai Ma-thaix 5.31–32; 11.12–13; Lai Ma-kheu 10.11–12)
14 Pui Fa-li-saix an kix pa muh ka mau khri mhong lox in kix,
kix tom nyiah Ye-sux.
15 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex yuh tix awm pui rauh pui see ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik tawng ka daux rhawm pex sidawng;
mawh kheu pa muh kawn pui tix jhak,
viang mawh nan,
ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex hoik mawh pa seeing pa brai heue.
16 b“Lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si mai phuk lai cao jhai khankix,
koe ceu pa hawt tix yuh die mee Yaw-hang cao cub com khaing;
jauh yam an,
gum lox buan meung Siyiex tag sang tauh hu,
kawn pui ku kaux tag sang ghrang su shiet paox tix laig.
17 cViang mawh nan,
pa grai rao ma haktiex kawn lheu yie khaing pa preet ka ngai lai lox tat jhawm prawng ku grawng heue!
18 d“Pui mawx ang ah pa tik ka moeng tix mai kleuk tix tau mai bun tang kaux,
hoik mawh pa yuh krax pa phit vao;
pui pa moeng ka bun an kawx hoik mawh pa yuh krax pa phit vao heue.”
Cao mi mai La-sa-lux
19 “Sivoe an koe cao mi tix kaux,
nawh hoik ceep ka kraung mhawm ku singaix,
yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe.
20 Yam an koe nang pui caung tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh La-sa-lux,
to kaux nawh hoik muad khawm uik.
Pui hoik viex nawh hoek ka diang siviex nyiex cao mi an jheeng jheeng,
21 nawh simie tix sut tix ih ka priex preem pa cawd khaing piang pheen nyiex cao mi an,
son ang sang cai rhawm;
pie sox kawx hoek tix lied ka dee leem to kaux nawh heue.
22 “Khaix an pui hot an yum,
kawn cau Siyiex viex nawh hoek ka praok Ya-paw-la-hang.
Cao mi an kawx yum nang,
khaix an si paung ka nawh.
23 Cao mi an kied kha rhawm tix ka daux meung yum;
nawh maong kaing tix kok jhak ka Ya-paw-la-hang ka dee singai,
mai yaox La-sa-lux ot ka praok nawh,
24 nawh tom kok yo ah nin:
‘Tax du aux Ya-paw-la-hang eue,
caox rhawm ka aux liak!
Tawx lox nhie La-sa-lux teei giien taix tix cub daux rawm kieh lhong ka dak aux liak,
mawh kheu kied kha aux rhawm tix ka daux rao ngu dee in heue!’
25 “Viang mawh nan,
Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
‘Kawn nyawm eue,
maix khaw tix tawng yam yaox tix krax nyawm ot nyawm koe yam im ju tix sivoe an,
viang mawh nan,
La-sa-lux ang sax yaox ka singaix mhawm tix bawg kawx.
Yam in nawh hoik pon krax pa nyawm ot dee in,
mai maix tieh tix ot ka daux krax pa kha rhawm.
26 Kheu nan,
si nax pex mai yix hoik kied raux khan paox tix tete,
phan pui jauh ka plak in sang hu ka plak an ang doh pon,
phan jauh ka plak an sang hu ka plak in kawx ka ang doh pon.’
27 Cao mi an ah nin:
‘Tax du eue,
phan mawh nan,
aux khiox khaw maix ploi La-sa-lux ing ka nyiex ma keeing aux liak;
28 aux koe pux aik phuan kaux,
kieh nawh ing ne raw ka kix,
son ang kix sang hoek kha rhawm tix dee in nang heue.’
29 “Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
‘Pux aik maix hoik koe Maw-si mai cao jhai khankix pa ne raw ka kix,
kieh pux aik maix hu nghiet ka kix heue!’
30 Cao mi an ah nin:
‘Tax du Ya-paw-la-hang eue,
yuh nan kawn ang nyang kub.
Phan koe pui pa kaoh im khaing krax yum,
mai hu ka kix dee an,
kix tee rien tix loh rhawm rhi tix.’
31 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
‘Phan kix ang nghiet ka lox Maw-si mai cao jhai khankix,
viang hoik koe pui pa kaoh im khaing krax yum kawx,
kix tag ang yum rhawm ka heue.’ ”
