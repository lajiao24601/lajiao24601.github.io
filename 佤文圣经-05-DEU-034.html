Grawng yum Maw-si
34
Maw-si kaoh khaing daux lung Maw-ya,
haok ka piang gawng Ni-paw,
mai hoek ka choek gawng Pi-see-cia,
pa koe plak toi veng Yie-li-keu.
Simiang Siyiex kieh nawh yaox haktiex Cia-nang khawm uik tan,
jauh ka haktiex Ci-liex hoek ka veng Tang plak laong,
2 hoek ka haktiex Na-fu-tha-lix khawm uik,
khawm haktiex Yi-fa-liien mai haktiex Ma-na-si,
haktiex Yo-tax khawm uik.
Khaix an,
mai vih tix ka plak klip singaix tom hoek ka Rawm sigrit Sigra Ti-cong,
3 mai haktiex Yo-tax plak seh,
jauh ka Saw-eux hoek ka daux lung daux ngawd pa koe si nax Yie-li-keu mai veng khaox jhie yaong.
4 aSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Haktiex in khawm uik hoik mawh pa sang ap aux tix tawx ka doeh kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux khankix.
Aux hoik kieh maix yaox ka haktiex in,
viang mawh nan maix tag ang pon tix laig ka heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si pa mawh siliex Simiang Siyiex yum ka daux haktiex Maw-ya,
awn gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex.
6 Simiang Siyiex si paung nawh ka daux ngawd haktiex Maw-ya,
plak toi veng Paw Pi-eux;
tom hoek in singaix,
ang koe pui pa tawng nawh si paung tix ot dee mawx.
7 Yam yum Maw-si ju nawh hoik koe tix yieh tix nga num,
mai kaux hak nawh kawn mhawm naog,
mai ngai nawh kawn mhawm riang.
8 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix yiiem doe son Maw-si ka daux lung Maw-ya tix ngoe singaix heue.
9 Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng naog ka khuan ping nyia cuyi,
mawh kheu hoik deuh Maw-si taix tix ka piang nawh,
son sang tawx nawh mawh cao pa plud ju tix.
Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix cu nghiet ka lox Yaw-su-yax,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa tawm Maw-si ka kix khaing Simiang Siyiex heue.
10 bKa daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex mai ang nyang sax koe cao jhai pa awm Maw-si yuh nan;
Simiang Siyiex hoik si kah tau mai nawh plak ngai tawx plak ngai.
11-12 Ang nyang sax koe cao jhai pa pon yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
awm yam pa hoik ploi Simiang Siyiex Maw-si hu yuh ka daux Ai-cix,
ka plak sivoe simiang Ai-cix,
ka siliex nawh khankix,
mai ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik;
mai ang nyang sax koe nang cao jhai pa pon yuh ka kaing pa kied yaoh yee pa ting pa yuh Maw-si ka sivoe ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
