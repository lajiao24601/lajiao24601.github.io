Grawng pa yum Yaw-hang cao cub com
(Lai Ma-kheu 6.14–29; Lai Lu-cia 9.7–9)
14
Yam an,
simiang Si-luix pa kuang ka Cia-li-lix mhong grawh grawng Ye-sux,
2 tom krai ka kawn cau tix ah nin:
“Pui in rien tix mawh Yaw-hang cao cub com pa kaoh im,
nawh tom tag koe siawp sibrawm tix yuh mhai pa daox pa am in kix heue.”
3 aAng tawng,
Si-luix hoik sax tawx lox giex ka Yaw-hang kheu Si-law-tix moeng pux aik nawh Fe-lix,
phrawk ka nawh,
mai laung nawh ka daux thawng.
4 bMawh kheu hoik ne raw Yaw-hang ka Si-luix tom nie bawg ah nin:
“Maix ang pon tix teei Si-law-tix mawh moeng tix.”
5 Si-luix keud tix sang pauh ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
lhat ka pui hun,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm kix grawng mawh Yaw-hang cao jhai heue.
6 Ka daux singaix pa yuh Si-luix poi keh tix,
kawn bun Si-law-tix graoh ka plak sivoe ngai nyo si nie khankix,
mai kied kieh Si-luix gaux rhawm.
7 Si-luix tom tiex tu ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix sang lo ceu pa tix ang ah,
aux tag cu tix tawx ka maix khawm uik heue.”
8 Kawn bun an nghiet lox pa krai miex tix mai khiox khaw ah nin:
“Khiox khaw simiang kid tix deuh kaing Yaw-hang cao cub com ka daux pan dum mawx dum an,
mai tawx ka aux heue!”
9 Simiang ang tawng ka sang yuh tix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik tiex tu ka plak sivoe ngai nyo si nie,
tom tawx lox nhie pui yuh awm pa khiox khaw bun an.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
Si-luix ploi pui hu tat kaing Yaw-hang ka daux thawng,
11 deuh ka daux pan,
tawx ka kawn bun Si-law-tix;
kawn bun tom tawx ka miex tix.
12 Pui gau Yaw-hang hoek,
mai teei to kaux yum nawh hu si paung kah,
khaix an krai grawng kaing in ka Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux kieh pui phuan rheng kaux ih sak
(Lai Ma-kheu 6.30–44; Lai Lu-cia 9.10–17; Lai Yaw-hang 6.1–13)
13 Ye-sux mhong grawng kaing in,
nawh tom haok ka daux reu hu khaing dee an,
mai hu ka daux tiag daux tu nong nong tix.
Pui hun tawng kah,
mai kaoh khaing ku veng hawt khaix nawh.
14 Ye-sux hu ka praok rawm,
yaox cub pui pa haoh hun caox rhawm caox rhi kah,
mai pai jhawm saux kix moi heue.
15 Yam hoek boi praix,
pui gau hoek ka Ye-sux mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Praix hoik lha,
dee in hoik mawh daux tiag daux tu,
nhie pui hun tang prung prang tix hu,
kieh kix tang hu liag tix ih priex preem ka yaong pa dex baoh an heue.”
16 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Ang siao kieh pui prung prang tix hu;
pex tawx kix ih heue.”
17 Pui gau ah nin:
“Dee in ex koe ngie khao mun phuan lon mai kax ra mu khaing heue.”
18 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Viex hoek tawx ka aux heue.”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tawx lox nhie pui hun ngawm piang tiag rib,
khaix an teei ka khao mun phuan lon mai kax ra mu maong ka rao ma,
yaog yaw Siyiex,
khaix an si vit ka khao mun,
tawx ka pui gau tix,
pui gau tom guah tix tawx ka pui hun
20 Pui hun ih ka khawm uik,
khaix an hoik ih sak khawm uik.
Pui gau khrawm pa max pa deuk khankix,
mai naog kao ra khrawng.
21 Kaux pui pa ih,
lheu khaing bun miex mai kawn nyawm khankix,
koe been phuan rheng kaux heue.
Ye-sux hu ka piang rawm
(Lai Ma-kheu 6.45–52; Lai Yaw-hang 6.15–21)
22 Khaix hoik kaing in,
Ye-sux dhiet pui gau tix laig daux reu dum mawx dum an,
mai ran tiang hu toi rawm,
krox nawh kieh pui hun prung prang heue.
23 Khaix hoik prung prang pui hun tix hu,
nawh haok rawg buan ka piang gawng nong nong;
tom hoek pon bo kawn ot dee an.
24 Yam in,
reu hoik kied singai hu khaing praok rawm,
kix si pup ka bhaung caox,
pung piang ka piang rawm.
25 Yam dex sang riang praix,
Ye-sux hu ka pui gau tix piang rawm.
26 Pui gau yaox hu nawh ka piang rawm,
mai kied lhat kah,
ah nin:
“Mawh miang!”
Kix kied lhat tom riag yo khawm uik.
27 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix dum mawx dum an:
“Paw vi khi,
mawh aux,
paw vi lhat heue!”
28 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
phan mawh maix,
kieh aux hu piang rawm hoek ka dee ot maix tan heue!”
29 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Hoek heue!”
Pi-teux tom lih khaing daux reu,
mai hu ka Ye-sux piang rawm.
30 Viang mawh nan,
yam yaox nawh pa kied ting bhaung,
rhawm rhi nawh kied lhat graung kah,
mai caong nawh jauh tix blawn daux rawm,
nawh tom kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
taoh aux liak!”
31 Ye-sux tawx taix tix bluh ka nawh dum mawx dum an,
mai ah nin:
“Krax yum rhawm maix kied iak,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh rhawm tix kawn suk sag ka lie?”
32 Kix laig daux reu,
bhaung tom naox tix.
33 Pui gau pa ot daux reu krub ka nawh khawm uik,
mai ah nin:
“Maix mawh kawn simex Siyiex tete heue.”
Pai moi ka jhawm saux pui Keu-ni-sa-leux
(Lai Ma-kheu 6.53–56)
34 Kix hoik tiang ka nhawng,
mai lih khaing praok rawm Keu-ni-sa-leux.
35 Yam tawng pui pa ot dee an mawh Ye-sux,
kix tom ploi pui hu,
mai viex pui pa koe jhawm saux baoh haktiex pa dex dee an hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux.
36 Kix khiox khaw Ye-sux kieh pui pa koe jhawm saux pu pi ngie ka jaong sibex dha nawh;
can pui pa pu pi hoik pon tix moi khawm uik heue.
