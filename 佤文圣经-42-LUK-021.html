Krax tan lu miex mai
(Lai Ma-thaix 12.41–44)
21
"Ye-sux yaog kaing tix jhak",
mai yaox pui khankix tix blah sau mau tan laig daux teug tan.
2 Nawh baox yaox miex mai tix kaux pa sau mau tan ra plah mau tawng.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
pa tan lu miex mai in kawn lheu khaing pa tan lu pui tang kaux khankix.
4 Mawh kheu pui tang kaux hoik tan lu khaing ka daux sin ngao pa haoh lheu tix;
viang mawh nan,
miex mai in hoik kied hot sibhawm,
mai nawh hoik tan lu ka can pa koe tix khawm uik heue.”
Grox krai sang laux leeing nyiex buan
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.1–2; Lai Ma-kheu 13.1–2)
5 Koe pui hoik krai lao ka son nyiex buan,
krai grawng sang teei simaox pa mhawm grang,
mai ka khawng phak kraung khrai pa ap pong pui yuh lih yuh ka mawx.
Ye-sux ah nin:
6 “Hoik yaox pex ceu in kix khawm uik lie?
Singaix bawg yam tag sang hoek,
yam an,
ang lai koe simaox pa deuh ot piang paox tix lon kawx,
tag sang yieh tix tik khawm uik.”
Krax pa laux leeing mai krax pa tuk tiak
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.3–14; Lai Ma-kheu 13.3–13)
7 Kix tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh bawg yam mawx lie?
Yam lih kaing in tix mawh tag sang koe mhai sakse ceu pa tix lie?”
8 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex tag caw rien rhawm tix,
paw vi kieh pui pie lhien ka tix;
mawh kheu koe pui tom nie tag sang pie tix hoek ka daux ceu kaux aux,
mai ah nin:
‘Aux mawh Khrit heue!’
Baox ah nin:
‘Bawg yam hoik hoek heue!’
Viang mawh nan,
paw vi hawt ka kix.
9 Yam mhong pex lox naing mai grawng pa yuh pui krax tieh krax vheen,
paw vi lhat.
Kaing in kix tag sang ran lih tix mawh;
viang mawh nan,
in ang mawh pa krai grawng hoik sang si dauh been heue.”
10 Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Pui tix ceu mai tix ceu tag sang yuh naing ka paox tix,
tix meung mai tix meung tag sang puing ka paox tix.
11 Kla reem,
sibhawm praix,
mai jhawm saux preng tag sang lih koe ku dauh ku dee,
mai baox lih koe riang su sai mhai pa daox deng rao ma pa kied lhat graung pui kah.
12 Viang mawh nan,
sivoe sang lih kaing ceu in kix tix lih mawh,
pui tag sang giex ka pex,
mai pex tag sang yaox tix kham krax pa tuk tiak tuk te;
pui tag sang viex tix hoek prawng pex ka daux nyiex sidiex,
mai baox kieh pex ngawm daux thawng.
Mawh kheu son aux,
pui tag sang viex pex hoek ka plak sivoe ngai simiang,
mai ka plak sivoe ngai cao giex kaing khankix.
13 In hoik mawh bawg yam pa kham pex sakse son gum lox buan.”
14 a“Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix keud mhawm mhawm,
paw vi siao khi ka lox pa sang krai son tix;
15 mawh kheu aux tag sang tawx jheeh pa chix krai mai ping nyia cuyi ka pex,
mai kieh pui koeng ka pex ang lai tawng ka sang pawk lox pex.
16 Pie khawm miex keeing pex,
pux aik pex,
paox nyiex pex,
paox grawm pex kawx tag sang coh ka pex;
ka daux pex koe pui tix blah,
pui tag sang pauh kix yum.
17 Mawh kheu son aux,
pui hun tag sang koeng ka pex.
18 Viang mawh nan,
hauk kaing pex pie tix gawng kawx ang khrum.
19 Pex tag caw sau riang tix gun yeung,
mai pex tom taug pon yaox tix im ju heue.”
Grox krai sang laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.15–21; Lai Ma-kheu 13.14–19)
20 “Yam yaox pex grawng pa hoik lai lawm pui naing pa koeng ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai pex tag sang tawng grawng hoik dex sang laux leeing.
21 Yam an,
pui pa paung ot ka Yo-thaix tag caw to mox tix ka gawng;
pui pa paung ot ka haktiex yaong iak khankix paw vi laig ka daux veng.
22 bKheu in hoik mawh ‘singaix bawg yam prawng ca’,
son sang kieh gum lox phuk lai sigang khri lih tix mawh.
23 Ka daux singaix yam an,
bun pa nghe mai bun miex pa tawx kawn tix peux tag sang kha rhawm tix heue!
Krax laux leeing pa kin tag sang lih hoek ka haktiex in,
krax pa song rhawm Siyiex tag sang lih hoek ka piang to kaux pui in kix.
24 Kix tag sang yum greem lawm vaig,
phan ang mawh nan pui giex viex laung kix hoek meung haktiex ku meung.
Pui laig li tang ceu tag sang dug dig ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tom hoik naog bawg yam singaix an heue.”
Pa lih hoek kawn kawn pui
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.29–31; Lai Ma-kheu 13.24–27)
25 c“Yam an,
singaix,
khix,
simuing tag sang tawx riang su sai.
Meung ka piang haktiex tag sang pru praoh yaoh yee kheu pa pu plak rawm sigrit sigra tix.
26 Kawn pui hoik krox jun ka kaing ceu pa sang lih hoek ka piang haktiex,
krax lhat krax graung tag sang kieh pui yum pi,
mawh kheu rao ma tag sang yung yang khawm uik.
27 dBawg yam an,
kawn kawn pui tag sang lih hoek,
mai naog ka riang rao,
mai brug pai awm lih hoek ka daux min plak ngai.
28 Yam lih kaing in kix tix lih mawh,
pex tag sang yaog kaing tix hu,
mawh kheu hoik hoek bawg yam singaix pa taoh ka pex heue!”
Pa sidiex ka khaox ruh
(Lai Ma-thaix 24.32–35; Lai Ma-kheu 13.28–31)
29 Ye-sux baox krai gum lox phek tiiem ka kix tix ceu ah nin:
“Pex jhak tix yaox ka khaox ruh mai ka khaox tang ceu ku ceu khankix.
30 Yam hoik lih glaoh lhax pa khraox kix,
pex tag tawng bawg yam rung praix dex sang hoek.
31 Awm an,
yam yaox pex ka sakse in kix khawm uik,
pex tag sang tawng siawp sibrawm Siyiex hoik dex sang lih hoek heue.
32 “Pex tag caw priex tix tawng yuh nin:
sivoe sang yum pui baux ju in khaing haktiex,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh heue.
33 Rao ma mai haktiex tag sang grai,
viang mawh nan,
gum lox aux ang grai leeing ju heue.”
Tag caw tix rien rhawm tix
34 “To kaux pex caw rien rhawm tix heue!
Paw vi kieh nex plai mai krax khi rhawm khi rhi daux krax ot krax koe mu mi yuh khuan rhawm rhi pex,
mawh kheu singaix bawg yam an tag lih hoek piang pex tix vud khaing.
Mawh kheu singaix bawg yam an
35 tag sang awm rub yuh nan,
lih hoek ka piang kawn pui pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
36 Pex caw rien rhawm tix,
rawg buan ang jud ang jai,
kieh pex koe riang rao tix kham gee,
ka can kaing ceu pa sang lih tix mawh khawm uik,
mai pon tix kaux cong ka plak sivoe ngai kawn kawn pui heue.”
37 eYe-sux hoik sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan yam pon ngaix ku ngaix,
plak pon bo nawh lih khaing daux veng,
mai paung it ka gawng sime;
38 yam pon ngawb pui hun hoik laig daux nyiex buan,
son sang nghiet ka gum lox pa sidiex Ye-sux heue.
