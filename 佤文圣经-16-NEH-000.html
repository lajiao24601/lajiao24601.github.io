Lai Ni-si-mi
Krai dawt
Lai Ni-si-mi chix tix guah koe pon baux:
1. Ni-si-mi kuang kia Yo-tax ka daux gum lox pa tawx Vawng Paw-see, mai tieh loh ka krax ot krax koe kawn pui mai krax been li tom nie heue.
2. Ni-si-mi thung pui cox yuh ka guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
3. Yi-see-la phat ka lai lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex ka plak sivoe ngai pui haoh pui hun; pui haoh pui hun doh keud ka phi map tix heue.
4. Kaing pa yuh Ni-si-mi khaix hoik mawh nawh cao bung kaing pui Yo-tax khankix.
Krax pa yum rhawm Ni-si-mi ka Siyiex mai pa sau nawh riang tix rawg buan, mawh gum lox pa blawn caw yix krai ka daux lai in heue.
Krai kaing
1. Ni-si-mi deeh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung (1.1–2.20).
2. Cox sum ka guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung (3.1–7.73).
3. Phat ka lai lox tat jhawm prawng, cox baox yuh ka Sijax mukheu (8.1–10.39).
4. Kiang yuh Ni-si-mi tang ceu khankix (11.1–13.31).
