Ta-wue si kaw kaux pui
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 21.1–28)
24
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
kheu nan tom kieh Ta-wue ang khaw rhawm ka kix.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Maix hu si kaw kaux pui Yi-seu-liex mai kaux pui Yo-tax.”
2 Ta-wue tom tawx lox ka simiang naing Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Maix viex pui naing cie maix,
hu ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku cub,
jauh ka Tang plak laong haktiex,
tom hoek ka Piie-see-pa plak seh haktiex,
mai simin tix yaox kaux kawn pui.
Aux sang tawng kaux kawn pui koe miex ding heue.”
3 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-ya pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng,
kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix,
kieh kawn pui Yi-seu-liex kawn lheu khaing kaux pui yam in tix yieh son.
Kieh ngai maix yaox ka kaing pa yuh Siyiex.
Viang mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx Vawng lai yuh kaing pa yuh nin lie?”
4 Viang mawh nan,
simiang kawn ca kieh Yaw-ya mai pui naing cie nawh hawt ka lox tawx tix,
kix tom lih hu khaing simiang,
mai simin ka kawn pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
5 Kix tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai sum bang ot ka plak seh veng Ya-law-eu,
ka daux ngawd haktiex Cia-teux,
khaix an mai baox yaog tix khaing tan,
hu ka plak laong hoek ka Ya-shiex,
6 mai baox hoek ka Cia-ti-see haktiex pui Ci-liex mai haktiex pui Heux.a
Khaix an,
baox hoek bleeh ka Tang,
mai baox yaog tix khaing Tang,
hu plak klip hoek ka Si-toeng,
7 mai baox hu plak seh,
hoek ka guh veng Thai-eux,
mai hoik hoek ka can veng yaong pui Si-we mai pui Cia-nang khawm uik,
dee nghoik kix hoek ka Piie-see-pa plak seh Yo-tax.
8 Kix hoik tauh hu ka daux meung haktiex ku dauh,
mai hoik hu dim khix mai tix nga ngaix,
khaix an deeh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
9 Kix krai ka simiang,
pui Yi-seu-liex pa koe riang tix pon viex ka kraung naing khawm uik koe tix daix mheen kaux.
Pui Yo-tax pa koe riang khawm uik koe tix phuan mheen kaux heue.
10 Khaix hoik si kaw Ta-wue kaux pui,
ka daux rhawm nawh ang nyawm,
nawh tom ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Aux yuh kaing in hoik mawh phi map pa ting.
Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka aux liak!
Aux hoik yuh ka kaing pa daox heue.”
11-12 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Cia-teux cao pa chix yaox sivoe cie Ta-wue:
“Hu krai ka Ta-wue,
aux tawx nawh laug loe grawng,
nawh laug ka ceu pa tix,
mai aux tag sang yuh ka ceu an.”
Pon ngawb ngaix khaix,
khaix hoik kaoh Ta-wue khaing it tix,
13 Cia-teux hu ka nawh,
mai krai lox krai Simiang Siyiex ka nawh,
mai chawk Ta-wue ah nin:
“Maix sang laug pa yuh ka mawx?
Ka daux meung maix koe sibhawm praix ka loe numb,
phan ang mawh nan,
maix sang to mox tix ka plak sivoe ngai pui pa koeng loe khix?
Phan ang mawh nan,
ka daux meung maix jhawm saux preng sang lih koe ka loe singaix?
Maix keud tix yaox,
sang but maix tix kieh aux pawk lox Simiang Siyiex ka lox ceu pa tix heue.”
14 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Kied nu ka rhawm aux tete.
Aux ang rhawm kieh pui ciyang ka tix,
lheu khaing kieh aux Simiang Siyiex hoek ciyang ka tix,
mawh kheu hoik grum naog Simiang Siyiex ka krax pa caox rhawm.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ploi jhawm saux preng lih ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
jauh ngawb ngaix an tom hoek bawg yam pa hoik ciak nawh,
jauh ka Tang plak laong tom hoek ka Piie-see-pa plak seh,
pui Yi-seu-liex koe pui yum aliah mheen kaux.
16 Yam sang yuh kawn cau Siyiex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung laux leeing,
Simiang Siyiex dee deeh rhawm pa ciyang tix ka kawn pui,
nawh tom ah nin ka kawn cau tix pa yuh ka krax laux leeing an:
“Ding heue,
hoik kub!”
Bawg yam an,
kawn cau Siyiex ot ka daux glauh pauh nghox ca pui Yie-pu-see pa kaux Ya-lao-na heue.
17 Ta-wue yaox kawn cau Siyiex pa yuh krax laux leeing an,
nawh tom ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Mawh phi map pa yuh aux,
aux hoik mawh pui pa yuh kaing pa phit lud an.
Kawn pui ang koe dee lud,
yuh kix ceu pa tix lie?
Maix caw tix ciyang aux mai ka pui nyiex pui ma aux khaing heue!”
18 Singaix an,
Cia-teux hu ka Ta-wue,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Hu ka glauh pauh nghox ca Ya-lao-na,
mai yuh khu dee tan son Simiang Siyiex dee an tix mu.”
19 Ta-wue hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Cia-teux.
20 Ya-lao-na veeh ngai tix jhak plak jee,
mai yaox simiang tau mai siliex nawh haok ka dee ot nawh plak blaong,
nawh tom hu krub ka plak sivoe ngai Ta-wue,
21 mai chawk ah nin:
“Vawng hoek yuh kaing ceu pa tix dee in?”
Ta-wue ah nin:
“Aux sang liag ka glauh pauh maix nghox ca,
mai yuh khu dee tan tix mu son Simiang Siyiex,
son sang kieh jhawm saux preng naox tix heue.”
22 Ya-lao-na ah nin:
“Vawng,
muh maix ceu tix maix teei tix viex ing tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
Dee in koe moi chix tix kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai baox koe nang khaox glawm pa bluh moi,
mai phia pa pauh nghox ca pa chix tix kieh mawh khix taung.”
23 Ya-lao-na tan lu ceu in kix ka simiang khawm uik,
mai baox ah nin ka simiang:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix rieb ka krax pa tan lu maix heue.”
24 Viang mawh nan,
simiang ah nin:
“Ang chix,
aux tag sang hawt awm jum mai liag kah.
Aux ang tan lu krax tan pa pon tix giex going ka Simiang Siyiex.”
Simiang tom teei mau luah tix phuan plah,
liag glauh pauh nghox ca mai moi,
25 khaix an nawh tom yuh khu dee tan tix mu son Simiang Siyiex,
mai tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim ot ka piang.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw simiang ka son meung,
jhawm saux preng tom grai khaing ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
