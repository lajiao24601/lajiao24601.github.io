Phek tiiem ka poi ka paox
(Lai Lu-cia 14.15–24)
22
Ye-sux baox teei gum lox phek tiiem ah nin ka kix:
2 “Meung rao ma awm simiang tix kaux pa rien khre poi ka paox son kawn simex tix.
3 Nawh ploi siliex tix hu dhiet ka can nyo si nie pa ghaok nawh hoek ih poi ka paox,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang cu tix hoek.
4 Nawh baox ploi siliex tix cub hu,
mai tawx lox nhie kix krai ka nyo si nie ah nin:
‘Aux hoik khan ka priex preem,
mai hoik pauh ka moi mhaing mai khawng brie pa kluing,
ku ceu khawm uik hoik rien mhawm,
khiox khaw pex hoek tix ih heue.’
5 Viang mawh nan,
nyo si nie pa ghaok kix hoek an kix ca kawn ang simi kah,
tang ki kaux mawx kaux an:
tix kaux hu ka daux kaung,
tix kaux hu jhak ka kraung coh tix,
6 pui pa deuk khankix giex ka siliex an kix,
tawk kix ka taix tix,
tiied kix ka caong tix,
mai pauh ka kix.
7 “Simiang Vawng song rhawm,
ploi pui naing hu pauh ka pui pa cau nyhau an kix,
mai gleeing veng ot kix laux leeing.
8 Khaix an nawh krai ka siliex tix ah nin:
‘Aux hoik khan ka priex preem,
viang mawh nan,
can nyo pa ghaok aux hoek sivoe ang keung tix ih.
9 Yam in pex hu ka daux laih,
mai ghaok ka pui pa si pup pex hoek ih poi khawm uik.’
10 Mawh kheu nan,
siliex tom hu daux laih,
mai ghaok ka pui pa hoik yaox tix,
mawh pa mhawm ang ah,
mawh pa caox ang ah kok hoek,
son sang kieh nyo si nie naog ka daux nyiex khawm uik heue.
11 “Yam lih simiang Vawng tix rieb ka nyo si nie,
nawh yaox pui tix kaux ang bax ceep ka kraung poi ka paox,
12 tom chawk ka nawh ah nin:
‘Paox grawm,
maix hoek dee in yuh ka mawx lai ang ceep ka kraung poi ka paox lie?’
Pui an ang nye ang nyux tix gum kawx.
13 aSimiang Vawng tom tawx lox ka pui cau ah nin:
‘Phrawk ka taix caong nawh khawm uik,
mai taing ka daux pa faik plak praix.
Nawh tag sang yiiem riag mai greeik rhang braing tix ka dee an heue.’ ”
14 Dee nghoik,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui pa ghaok hoek kied hun,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa laug kied tux heue!”
Grawng tawx mau koh keen ka Kai-sax
(Lai Ma-kheu 12.13–17; Lai Lu-cia 20.19–26)
15 Pui Fa-li-saix lih hu,
mai ra lai kum kawng paox tix grawng sang pauh ka nawh ka lox pa kaoh khaing nawh.
16 Kix ploi pui gau tix rom tix hu tau mai cub Si-luix hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
yix hoik tawng mawh maix pui pa rauh see tix kaux.
Ang kuang pui keud yuh ka mawx,
maix hoik teei gum lox Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix sidiex gau ka pui,
mawh kheu ang maix jhak ka plak ngai pui.
17 Khiox khaw maix krai yix mhong ka rhawm keud tix:
phan tawx mau koh keen ka Kai-sax Vawng Law-ma,
mawh lud ang lud ka lox tat jhawm prawng ex lie?”
18 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka krax rhawm pa keud kix,
mai nawh tom ah nin:
“Pui pa pie lhien eue,
yuh ka mawx pex lai keud tix sang yuh aux laux?
19 Teei mau luah pa siao ka pa tawx mau koh keen tix plah,
aux sang jhak ka heue!”
Kix tawx mau luah ka nawh tix plah.
20 Ye-sux chawk kix ah nin:
“Riang pa koe ka mau in mai ceu kaux pa koe ka mawh mawx?”
21 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh Kai-sax heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Phan mawh nan,
cie Kai-sax tawx ka Kai-sax,
cie Siyiex tawx ka Siyiex heue.”
22 Kix mhong ka lox in kied daox deng kah,
tom lih hu khaing nawh heue.
Grawng pa kaoh im
(Lai Ma-kheu 12.18–27; Lai Lu-cia 20.27–40)
23 bKa daux singaix an,
koe pui Sa-tu-kai pa ang yum rhawm ka grawng pa kaoh im tix blah hoek ka Ye-sux,
chawk nawh ah nin:
24 c“Sara,
Maw-si sidiex gau ka yix,
koe pui tix kaux yum,
ang bax koe kawn,
pux aik nawh tag caw teei tix moeng o tix,
son sang kieh plud ka doeh ceu cad pux ke tix.
25 Sivoe an,
yix dee in koe pux aik aliah kaux,
pux ke lhong koe moeng,
nawh yum,
ang bax koe kawn,
mai deuh moeng tix tawx pux tix moeng;
26 nyi mai sam kawx yuh nin nang,
hoek tom jiet,
kaing pa yaox kix awm paox tix.
27 Dee si dauh,
bun an kawx yum nang.
28 Khiox khaw,
chawk ka maix,
kix ku kaux hoik moeng ka nawh,
yam hoek singaix pa kaoh im,
bun in sang sawn tix mawh moeng pa kaux tix lie?”
29 Ye-sux pawk ka kix ah nin:
“Pex lud heue!
Pex ang tawng phuk lai sigang khri,
mai ang tawng nang ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex.
30 Yam kaoh im pui khaing krax yum,
kix tag sang awm kawn cau Siyiex daux rao ma yuh nan,
mai ang koe mex ang koe moeng heue.
31 Phan mawh grawng pa kaoh im pui yum,
ang pex bax phat ka gum lox pa krai Siyiex ka pex lie?
32 dSiyiex ah nin:
‘Aux mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Siyiex Yi-sha,
Siyiex Ya-keux.’
Dee bleeh gum lox in krai mawh Siyiex cie pui im ju,
ang mawh Siyiex pui yum heue.”
33 Pui hun mhong lox pa sidiex gau in,
mai kied daox deng ka khawm uik heue.
Gum lox tawm ping nyap pa blawn ting
(Lai Ma-kheu 12.28–34; Lai Lu-cia 10.25–28)
34 Pui Fa-li-saix mhong hoik thox Ye-sux ka jheeh pui Sa-tu-kai,
kix tom khrawm tix ot tau.
35 eKa daux kix koe sara lox tat jhawm prawng tix kaux,
keud tix teei grawng kaing tix ceu sang yuh Ye-sux laux kah.
36 Nawh chawk ah nin:
“Sara,
ka daux lai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
mawh gum lox ping nyap pa yuh ka mawx pa blawn ting ceu lie?”
37 fYe-sux ah nin:
“ ‘Maix tag caw tix muh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi,
khawm ju,
khawm pa chix keud tix khawm uik.’
38 In mawh gum lox ping nyap pa blawn ting ceu khaing paox tix ka daux tix gum.
39 gKa daux ra gum kawx ting ceu yuh nin nang:
‘Maix tag caw tix muh ka paox plawg tix,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan.’
40 Lai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si khawm uik,
mai gum lox pa sidiex gau cao jhai hoik kieh gum lox ping nyap in kiex ra gum mawh riah heue.”
Grawh grawng pa dex mai Khrit
(Lai Ma-kheu 12.35–37; Lai Lu-cia 20.41–44)
41 Yam khrawm pui Fa-li-saix tix ot tau,
Ye-sux chawk ka kix ah nin:
42 “Keud pex grawh grawng Khrit mawh ceu tix?
Mawh nawh kawn praix pui mawx lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Nawh mawh kawn praix Ta-wue.”
43 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx Khuan Singax Singeh lai kieh Ta-wue kok nawh mawh ‘simiang’ lie?
Ta-wue ah nin:
44 hSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka simiang aux:
maix ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm aux,
krox aux kieh pui koeng ka maix kreet tix ot greem caong maix heue.
45 Phan Ta-wue kok nawh mawh ‘Simiang’,
Khrit sang chix mawh kawn praix Ta-wue yuh ka mawx lie?”
46 Ang koe pui pa pon krai lox tix gum pa pawk ka lox Ye-sux.
Jauh singaix an ang lai koe pui pa baox pon krai lox pa yuh nin ka nawh heue.
