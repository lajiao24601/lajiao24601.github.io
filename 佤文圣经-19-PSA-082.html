Siyiex pa giex kaing pa ting pa lhaong
82
Siyiex mawh cao pa paoh ka nyiex rao ma dee khrawm paox tix;
nawh ot tix prawng ca ka daux nyiex pa kum kawng cub siyiex khawm uik,
2 nawh ah nin:
pex tag caw tix naox ka krax prawng pa ang rauh ang see;
pex ang pon tix hu plak pui pa caox pa caig!
3 Tag caw tix teem ka sibrawm pui hot mai kawn doe;
tag caw tix teei krax pa rauh pa see yuh son pui hot pa ang koe cao teem krai.
4 Tag caw tix taoh teem ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
kieh kix luat luan khaing daux pa pie lhien pui pa caox pa caig heue.
 
5 Pex lai daox aw yuh nin,
pheem yuh nin!
Pex lawng ka daux krax pa faik;
pa rauh pa see hoik grai hu khaing haktiex.
6 aAux hoik sax ah nin:
pex mawh Siyiex;
pex hoik mawh kawn simex cao pa kied ting kied lhaong.
7 Kheu nan,
pex tag sang yum awm pui tang ceu yuh nan;
pex tag sang goh tix yum awm kawn simiang yuh nan heue.
 
8 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kaoh prawng ca ka haktiex in,
kheu hoik mawh meung piang haktiex cie maix khawm uik heue.
