Kham krax prawng ka plak sivoe Pi-la-to
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.1–2; Lai Lu-cia 23.1–5; Lai Yaw-hang 18.28–38)
15
Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
simiang cao giex kaing li nied tix phai kum kawng ka kuad song,
sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri,
mai bang kum pui Yo-thaix,
mai hoik tat tix yuh ka krax pa keud kix.
Kix phrawk ka Ye-sux viex nawh hu,
mai tawx nawh ka Pi-la-to.
2 Pi-la-to chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh maix simiang pui Yo-thaix lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“In hoik mawh maix pa krai heue.”
3 Simiang cao giex kaing li deuh phi map piang Ye-sux tom nie,
4 Pi-la-to tom baox chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
kix hoik deuh phi map in piang maix tom nie,
ang maix pawk ka lie?”
5 Ye-sux ca kawn ang pawk pie tix gum;
Pi-la-to kied daox deng ka heue.
Prawng nawh yum
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.15–26; Lai Lu-cia 23.13–25; Lai Yaw-hang 18.39–16.19)
6 Ka daux Poi Luat-luan ku num,
Pi-la-to hoik hawt tix ploi ka pui lud tix kaux awm pa khiox khaw pui hun yuh nan.
7 Yam an jiet koe pui tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Pa-la-pa,
kix laung nawh ot tau mai pui tieh vheen pa hoik pauh ka pui yam suk sag.
8 Yam khrawm pui hun paox tix khiox khaw,
Pi-la-to hawt awm been pa hoik tat tix deuh,
mai yuh kaing in son kix,
9 Pi-la-to chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Pex sang kieh aux ploi ka simiang pui Yo-thaix son pex lie?”
10 Tete te lai,
Pi-la-to hoik tawng,
mawh jao boh rhawm simiang cao giex kaing li,
tom ap tix tawx Ye-sux ka nawh heue.
11 Viang mawh nan,
simiang cao giex kaing li cheeik ka pui hun,
mai khiox khaw Pi-la-to ploi Pa-la-pa son kix.
12 Pi-la-to baox chawk pui hun ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pui an pa hoik kok pex mawh simiang pui Yo-thaix,
sang khaw aux tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
13 Kix baox kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue!”
14 Pi-la-to chawk ah nin:
“Hoik yuh nawh kaing lud ceu pa tix lie?”
Kix nied tix kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue!”
15 Pi-la-to keud grawng sang khaw ka rhawm pui hun,
nawh tom ploi tix tawx Pa-la-pa ka kix,
mai baox tawx lox nhie kix si vied ka Ye-sux,
khaix an tawx pui tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue.
Pui naing map nai ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.27–31; Lai Yaw-hang 19.2–3)
16 Pui naing viex Ye-sux laig daux biang brawk nyiex cao kuang kaing,
kix khrawm pui naing ot tau.
17 Kix tawx Ye-sux ceep ka kraung dha raoh pliex tix pheen,
mai baox teei mhok pa yuh ka kat lawm,
tawx nawh ceep,
18 khaix an yuh krax nab yam ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang pui Yo-thaix lang ju pu rhang heue!”
19 Kix baox teei maox priem si vied ka kaing nawh,
mai baig ka nawh,
grux ngawng tix krub ka nawh.
20 Khaix hoik map nai kix ka nawh,
kix tom peeik ka kraung dha raoh pliex nawh,
mai deeh tawx nawh ceep ka kraung ceep tix,
khaix an viex tix tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak heue.
Tawk Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.32–44; Lai Lu-cia 23.26–43; Lai Yaw-hang 19.17–27)
21 aKix si pup pui tix kaux ka daux krax,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Si-meung,
taug khraox hoek khaing yaong iak laig ka daux veng,
kix khin nawh klawm khaox lhak tang Ye-sux (Si-meung mawh pui Ku-li-naix,
keeing Ya-li-sang-tax mai Lu-fux).
22 Kix viex Ye-sux hoek ka haktiex tix dauh,
ceu kaux mawh Keu-keu-tha,
dee bleeh mawh “Gawng si-ang kaing”.
23 Ka dee an,
kix teei plai pa klao tix mai Muran tawx Ye-sux nyaux,
viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ang nyaux.
24 bMawh kheu nan,
kix tawk Ye-sux ka piang khaox lhak,
mai baox yuh krax pa cawk lai,
guah ka kraung ceep nawh.
25 Bawg yam pon ngawb dim taix nari,
kix tawk Ye-sux piang khaox lhak.
26 Na bat phia an hoik tiiem ah nin:
“Simiang pui Yo-thaix.”
27 dYam an kix baox tawk pui caox tau mai Ye-sux ra kaux ka piang khaox lhak,
tix kaux ot plak khawn dawm nawh,
tix kaux ot plak khawn goix nawh heue.c
29 ePui pa hoek dee an map nai ka Ye-sux,
mai vuk vied kaing tix ah nin:
“Hai,
maix pa keud tix sang yieh ka nyiex buan in,
ka daux loe singaix deeh cox sum ka heue!
30 Yam in,
lih khaing piang khaox lhak,
taoh ka to kaux maix heue!”
31 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri kawx nyiah ka nawh,
mai ra lai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Nawh hoik taoh ka pui tang kaux,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon taoh ka to kaux tix heue!
32 Khrit,
simiang Yi-seu-liex eue,
yam in maix lih khaing khaox lhak,
kieh yix jhak,
yix tom tag yum rhawm ka heue!”
Pui pa tawk tau mai Ye-sux piang khaox lhak hoik map nai ka nawh yuh nan nang heue.
Grawng yum Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.45–56; Lai Lu-cia 23.44–49; Lai Yaw-hang 19.28–30)
33 Yam hoek greung ngaix,
pa faik tawb kheem ka haktiex,
been loe taix nari.
34 fYam hoek pon ngaix loe taix nari,
Ye-sux kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Yi-law-yi,
Yi-law-yi,
La-ma-sha-pa-keu-ta-ni?”
Dee bleeh mawh:
“Siyiex aux,
Siyiex aux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tik vud ka aux lie?”
35 Pui pa cung ka praok nawh khankix tix blah hoik mhong kah,
mai ah nin:
“Maix nghiet,
nawh kok yo ka Yi-li-yax heue!”
36 gKoe pui tix kaux to hu ka nawh,
mai teei tai pa ce ka plai niex pa hoik phrawk ka maox priem,
tawx ka jheeh Ye-sux ah nin:
“Krox ha,
ex sang jhak tix yaox,
sang hoek ang Yi-li-yax hoek tix yah ka nawh heue!”
37 Ye-sux kok yo tom ting tix gum,
mai dut rhawm heue.
38 hMan tie pa vhak ot daux nyiex buan,
jauh ka plak piang hoek ka plak greem hoik griah koe ra plak.
39 Simiang naing pa cung ka sivoe khaox lhak,
yaox grawng pa kok yo Ye-sux maii grawng pa yum nawh,
mai nawh tom ah nin:
“Pui in mawh kawn simex Siyiex tete heue!”
40 jKawn koe bun miex tix blah ot dee singai kok jhak kah;
ka daux bun miex khankix koe Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la,
baox koe Ma-li-yax miex kawn nyawm Ya-keux mai Yaw-seux,
khawm Sa-law-mi.
41 Yam ot Ye-sux ka haktiex Cia-li-lix,
kix hoik hawt ka nawh yuh son nawh.
Kawn koe bun miex tang kaux khankix hoik hoek tau mai Ye-sux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Si paung ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 17.57–61; Lai Lu-cia 23.50–56; Lai Yaw-hang 19.38–42)
42-43 Yam pon bo,
Yaw-seux pa mawh pui Ya-li-ma-thaix hoek;
nawh mawh pui pa nab yam yung yi pui ka tix kaux,
nawh hoik dawg simie siawp sibrawm Siyiex lih tix mawh.
Ka daux singaix an mawh singaix pa rien khre (pa mawh sivoe singaix tuah tix ngaix).
Yaw-seux viex rhawm ting tix hu ka Pi-la-to,
mai khiox khaw tix priex ka to kaux yum Ye-sux.
44 Pi-la-to mhong hoik yum Ye-sux,
nawh kied daox deng kah.
Nawh kok simiang naing pa ot greem tix hoek,
mai chawk Ye-sux hoik yum koe miex glang. k
45 Kheu hoik mhong nawh lox pa krai simiang naing pa ot greem tix an,
Pi-la-to tom tawx to kaux yum Ye-sux ka Yaw-seux.
46 Yaw-seux liag ka man keei sum,
teei ka to kaux yum Ye-sux lih,
teei man keei sum kie mhawm kah,
deuh ot ka daux si meeig pa hoik saik tix lih khaing rang simaox,
mai baox pu plie simaox tix khlawp hoek thox moing si meeig.
47 Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la mai Ma-li-yax miex Yaw-seux pau ka dee deuh kix Ye-sux heue.
