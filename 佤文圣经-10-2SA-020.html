Si-pa tieh vheen
20
aKa daux Cie-cia koe pui pa ang mhawm ka tix kaux pa kaux See-pa,
nawh mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming kawn simex Pi-ci-lix.
Nawh phlu ka teet mai kok yo ah nin:
“Ta-wue ang mawh pui pa dex dawt tau mai ex!
Kawn simex Yie-si ang lai koe krax pa mhawm tau mai ex pie ceeix kawx!
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
deeh ing ka nyiex pex heue!”
2 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex lih hu khaing Ta-wue,
hawt ka See-pa,
viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-tax ca kawn hawt rhawm Ta-wue,
jauh tix hawt nawh ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang tom hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
3 bTa-wue hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung nyiex haw,
mai nawh teei bun an kix kao kaux pa mawh moeng iak nawh,
pa hoik prah nawh pau nyiex haw,
nawh laung kix ang kieh kix lih hu,
mai tawx can ceu pa sang lo kix khawm uik ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang it tau mai kix.
Kix tag ot ka daux nyiex haw awm miex mai yuh nan heue.
4 Simiang ah nin ka Ya-ma-sha:
“Khrawm ka pui Yo-tax,
ka daux loe singaix hoek bleeh tau mai kix tin.”
5 Ya-ma-sha tom hu,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang hoek bawg yam nari pa hoik tat simiang nawh hoek.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang ah nin ka Ya-pi-sai:
“Aux lhat pa yuh See-pa ka aux kawn lheu kin khaing pa yuh Ya-sa-long ka aux.
Maix viex pui naing hu leue ka nawh!
Phan ang mawh nan,
nawh rien tix pon cax ka veng pa kaux cong,
mai grai ka plak sivoe ngai ex.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
pui naing cie Yaw-ya,
pui pa hawt tix pau nyiex haw simiang,
mai pui pa kua ka naing khawm uik lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung tau mai Ya-pi-sai,
leue ka See-pa.
8 Yam leue kix nawh hoek ka Ci-piien dee koe simaox ting dee an,
Ya-ma-sha hoek rieb ka kix.
Yam an,
Yaw-ya ceep ka sibex kraung naing,
ka nyoi nawh kawg ka vaig,
vaig ot ka daux phak vaig.
Yam yaog Yaw-ya tix hu ka plak sivoe ngai,
mai vaig viex nawh cawd khaing daux phak.
9 Yaw-ya ah nin ka Ya-ma-sha:
“Paox grawm,
maix nyawm ot nyawm koe lie?”
Nawh tom teei taix khawn dawm tix jhawp ka hauk jheeh nawh,
mai sang ngheet ka nawh.
10 Ya-ma-sha ang baox tien rien tix ka taix Yaw-ya tang khawn pa jhawp ka vaig,
Yaw-ya klauh tu nawh ka vaig in,
kraung vaig nawh glhua piang tiex.
Yaw-ya ang naox lai baox klauh tix bawg,
Ya-ma-sha tom yum heue.
Yaw-ya mai pux nawh Ya-pi-sai ca nied tix leue ka See-pa.
11 Koe pui pa hawt khaix Yaw-ya tix kaux,
cung ka praok to kaux yum Ya-ma-sha,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Pui pa muh ka Yaw-ya mai Ta-wue caw tix hu tau mai Yaw-ya heue!”
12 To kaux yum Ya-ma-sha hoik naok ka nham,
noi tix it ka daux krax.
Pui pa hawt khaix Yaw-ya yaox kaog cung pui pa hu blawng an,
kix tom gah to kaux yum an hu daux kaung,
mai lhop kraung nawh ka piang.
13 Khaix hoik gah kix to kaux yum an hu,
pui haoh pui hun tom hawt leue See-pa tau mai Yaw-ya heue.
14 See-pa hoik tauh hu ka baoh haktiex dee ot ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
nawh hoek bleeh ka Ya-paw Paw Ma-cia.
Can cub pui Pi-ci-lixc khrawm paox tix,
mai hawt nawh laig daux veng.
15 Pui naing cie Yaw-ya mhong ot See-pa ka Ya-paw Paw Ma-cia,
kix tom lai lawm ka veng an.
Kix sum tab see guh veng an plak praix,
mai kaong daux greem guh veng an,
son sang kieh guh veng ghrup.
16 Ka daux veng koe bun pa lhak lhiao tix kaux,
mai kok yo ka piang guh veng ah nin:
“Nghiet heue,
nghiet heue!
Hu kok Yaw-ya hoek tin.
Aux sang koe lox krai ka nawh.”
17 Yaw-ya hoek.
Bun an chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix mawh Yaw-ya lie?”
Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Mawh heue.”
Bun an ah nin:
“Nghiet lox krai bun siliex.”
Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Aux nghiet kah.”
18 Bun an ah nin:
“Koe lox been pa yie krai lao pui pa ah nin:
‘Hu gau ex khaing veng Ya-paw heue!’
Kix hoik yuh nan tete.
19 Veng ex hoik mawh veng pa ting tix mu,
ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex veng in hoik mawh veng pa kied koe krax pa nhim,
pa kied koe krax pa kaux sijax tete.
Yuh ka mawx maix lai keud tix sang yuh laux lie?
Maix sang yuh laux ka sin ngao Simiang Siyiex lie?”
20 Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Ang,
aux ang doh yuh laux ka veng in!
21 An ang mawh pa sang keud yix tix yuh.
Koe pui tix kaux pa kaux See-pa,
nawh mawh kawn simex Pi-ci-lix,
pa kaoh khaing gawng Yi-fa-liien.
Nawh tieh vheen ka simiang Ta-wue.
Ca mawh tawx pex pui in lih,
mai aux tag sang tawx lox kieh yix deeh heue.”
Bun an ah nin:
“Yix chix taing kaing nawh ka maix khaing piang guh veng!”
22 Mawh kheu nan,
bun an tom tawx krax keud krax sawn tix ka pui pa ot daux veng.
Kix tom kid ka kaing See-pa,
ot piang guh veng mai taing ka Yaw-ya.
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya phlu ka teet,
pui naing cie nawh tom lih hu khaing veng in,
mai deeh ing ka nyiex.
Yaw-ya deeh ing ka simiang Ta-wue ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Kawn cau pa ting cie Ta-wue
23 Yaw-ya hoik mawh cao thung pui naing Yi-seu-liex.
Pi-na-ya kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax,
mawh cao giex kaing lien cie Ta-wue.
24 Ya-to-lang mawh pa kuang ka piang pui pa kieh kix yuh ka kaing pa kha tix khankix kawn simex Yaw-sa-fa pa kaux Ya-si-lui mawh cao kaing been.
25 See-fa mawh cao tiiem lai ka daux nyiex simiang.
Sa-tu mai Ya-pi-ya-tha mawh cao giex kaing li.
26 Yi-la pa ot ka daux veng Ya-eux,
hoik mawh cao giex kaing li Ta-wue tix kaux nang heue.
