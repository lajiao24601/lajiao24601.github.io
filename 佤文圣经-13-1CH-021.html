Ta-wue si kaw ka ca rang pui
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 24.1–25)
21
Sa tan tieb tie ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kieh Ta-wue but rhawm tix si kaw ka ca rang pui.
2 Ta-wue tom tawx lox ka simiang pui naing Yaw-ya mai ka simiang tang kaux khankix ah nin:
“Pex hu ka haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh ku dee,
jauh ka veng Tang pa koe ka daog plak laong hoek ka Piie-see-pa pa koe ka daog plak seh,
simin tix yaox ka kawn pui;
aux sang tawng kaux kawn pui koe miex ding heue.”
3 Yaw-ya pawk lox ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex kieh kaux pui Yi-seu-liex lheu nie khaing yam in tix yieh son.
Vawng,
kix hoik mawh siliex maix khawm uik,
yuh ka mawx maix lai sang yuh kaing pa yuh nin,
mai kieh pui khawm uik yuh phi map lie?”
4 Viang mawh nan,
simiang kawn ca kieh Yaw-ya hawt ka lox tawx tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya lih hu,
mai hoik tauh hu ka meung pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
khaix an deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
5 Yaw-ya krai ka simiang Ta-wue,
can pui Yi-seu-liex pa pon puing ka naing koe tix yieh kao mheen kaux;
pui Yo-tax pa pon puing ka naing koe tipon aliah mheen kaux.
6 Yaw-ya ang bax si kaw ka ceu pui Li-we mai ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
mawh kheu ang nawh khaw rhawm ka lox tawx simiang heue.
7 Siyiex ang khaw rhawm ka kaing in,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom ciyang ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
8 Ta-wue ah nin ka Siyiex:
“Aux yuh kaing in hoik mawh phi map pa ting;
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka aux liak!
Kaing pa yuh aux mawh pa daox heue.”
9 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka cao pa chix yaox sivoe cie Ta-wue pa kaux Cia-teux:
10 “Hu krai ka Ta-wue,
aux tawx nawh laug loe grawng,
nawh laug ceu pa tix,
aux tag sang yuh ka ceu an.”
11 Cia-teux hu ka Ta-wue,
mai krai lox krai Simiang Siyiex ka nawh,
mai chawk Ta-wue ah nin:
“Maix sang laug pa yuh ka mawx?
12 Mawh krax sibhawm praix loe num?
Phan ang mawh nan,
krax pa to maix mox tix khaing pui koeng loe khix?
Phan ang mawh nan,
sang kieh Simiang Siyiex teei vaig viex tix,
yuh jhawm saux preng lih koe ka daux meung loe singaix,
mai ploi kawn cau Siyiex lih yuh krax yum ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh ku dee?
Yam in maix but rhawm tix,
aux sang khaw tix pawk lox Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx heue.”
13 Ta-wue ah nin ka Cia-teux:
“Kied nu ka rhawm aux tete.
Aux ang rhawm kieh pui ciyang ka tix;
lheu khaing kieh aux Simiang Siyiex hoek ciyang ka tix,
mawh kheu hoik grum naog Simiang Siyiex ka krax pa caox rhawm.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ploi jhawm saux preng lih ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
koe pui yum aliah mheen kaux.
15 Nawh baox ploi kawn cau Siyiex hu yuh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung laux leeing,
viang mawh nan,
yam tag sang jauh kawn cau Siyiex tix sang yuh,
nawh deeh loh rhawm keud tix,
mai ah nin ka kawn cau Siyiex:
“Ding heue,
hoik kub!”
Bawg yam an,
kawn cau Siyiex ot ka daux glauh pauh nghox ca pui Yie-pu-see pa kaux Ya-lao-na heue.
16 Ta-wue yaog kaing tix jhak,
mai yaox kawn cau Siyiex an cung ka daux sigreung rao ma,
ka daux tawm taix nawh jhawp ka vaig,
tag sang rien tix yuh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung laux.
Ta-wue mai kuad song khankix ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
mai gup ngai tix krub piang tiex.
17 Ta-wue rawg buan ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
aux hoik mawh pui pa yuh kaing pa phit lud an,
hoik mawh aux pa tawx lox si kaw ka ca rang pui.
Kawn pui ang koe dee lud ang bax yuh kaing pa lud loe.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix ciyang aux mai pui nyiex ma aux,
pluat ploi ka kawn pui cie maix heue!”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex krai ka Cia-teux,
nhie nawh tawx lox,
kieh Ta-wue hu ka glauh pauh nghox ca Ya-lao-na,
mai yuh khu dee tan son Simiang Siyiex dee an tix mu.
19 Ta-wue hawt ka lox pa krai Cia-teux,
mai nghiet ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex lih hu heue.
20 Ya-lao-na mai kawn simex nawh pon kaux pauh nghox ca ot ka daux glauh;
yam yaox kix ka kawn cau Siyiex,
kawn simex an kix pon kaux yaog tix to mox tix.
21 Ya-lao-na yaox ka simiang Ta-wue hu plak ot nawh,
nawh tom lih hu khaing glauh pauh nghox ca,
hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh mai kup tix krub piang tiex.
22 Ta-wue ah nin ka nawh:
“Viex maix coh glauh pauh nghox ca ka aux,
aux sang yuh khu dee tan son Simiang Siyiex tix mu,
son sang kieh jhawm saux preng naox tix,
aux tag hawt tix tawx ngawh awm jum pa mhaing maix heue.”
23 Ya-lao-na ah nin:
“Vawng,
teei hu heue,
maix sang yuh ka mawx tag yuh heue.
Dee in koe moi pa chix tix yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai baox koe nang phia pa pauh nghox ca pa chix tix kieh mawh khix taung,
mai baox kawn koe nghox ca pa chix tix kieh mawh Krax-tan Nghox-koi.
Aux sang tawx in kix khawm uik ka maix heue.”
24 Viang mawh nan,
simiang Ta-wue ah nin:
“Ang chix!
Aux tag sang hawt tix tawx ngawh tom kub ka maix;
aux ang rhawm tan lu pa mawh cie maix ka Simiang Siyiex,
ang rhawm tan lu ka khawng tan pa pon tix giex going.”
25 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue liag glauh pauh nghox ca ka Ya-lao-na liah yieh plah khri.
26 Nawh sum khu dee tan son Simiang Siyiex dee an tix mu,
tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
Ta-wue rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex tom nghiet lox pa khiox khaw nawh,
ploi ngu lih khaing rao ma,
mai gleeing ka khawng tan pa deuh ot piang khu dee tan heue.
27 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie kawn cau Siyiex sau vaig laig daux phak,
kawn cau Siyiex tom hawt tix yuh.
28 Ta-wue tawng Simiang Siyiex hoik nghiet ka lox rawg nawh buan,
mawh kheu nan tom yuh krax tan lu ka piang khu dee tan pa yuh nawh ka daux glauh pauh nghox ca cie Ya-lao-na.
29 Nyiex gox buan singax singeh cie Simiang Siyiex pa yuh Maw-si daux tiag mai khu dee tan pa yuh krax tan khawng brie;
ca kawn ot dee krub ka haktiex Ci-piien yam in heue;
30 Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue ang pon hoek krub Siyiex dee an,
mawh kheu hoik lhat nawh ka vaig viex kawn cau Simiang Siyiex heue.
