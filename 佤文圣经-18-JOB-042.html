42
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-paw pawk lox Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh ah nin:
 
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux tawng maix pon yuh kaing ku ceu;
maix pon yuh lih ka kaing pa keud tix khawm uik.
3 aMaix chawk,
aux pa mawh pui daox yuh ka mawx lai ang yum rhawm ka ping nyia cuyi maix;
aux krai lao ka kaing pa ang to kaux tix pon tawng sidawng,
krax pa daox pa deng,
mai ang pon tix tawng sidawng.
4 bMaix nhie aux bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox maix,
pawk ka lox pa hoik krai maix.
5 Sivoe an aux mhong lox pa krai lao pui tang kaux ka maix;
yam in aux hoik yaox maix ka ngai tix.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
lox pa hoik krai aux kieh aux koe krax kaig kah,
mai klih rhawm tix ngawm piang phawng tiex mai ka daux nyaox glah ngu heue.
Lox nghoik
7 Khaix hoik krai Simiang Siyiex lox in kix ka Yaw-paw,
nawh tom krai ka Yi-li-fa ah nin:
“Aux kied ang naog rhawm ka maix mai ka paox grawm maix an ra kaux,
mawh kheu pex krai lao ka lox aux,
krai ang lheu rauh see khaing lox pa hoik krai siliex aux Yaw-paw.
8 Yam in,
pex tag caw tix teei moi mhaing aliah mu,
yung mhaing aliah mu,
viex hoek ka Yaw-paw dee an,
son sang tan lu mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing son to kaux pex.
Yaw-paw tag sang khiox khaw son pex;
aux tag sang chix rieb ka krax pa khiox khaw nawh,
mai ang hawt tix ciyang pex awm krax pa daox aw pex.
Krax pa salao pex ka aux,
ang baox koe krax awm lox pa hoik krai Yaw-paw heue.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-fa,
Pi-leu-ta,
Saw-fax hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex rieb ka lox pa khiox khaw Yaw-paw.
10 cKhaix hoik rawg Yaw-paw buan son paox grawm tix an loe kaux,
Simiang Siyiex deeh tawx krax pa mhawm ot mhawm koe ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh lheu haok huan khaing singaix pa hoik luan ra son.
11 Pux aik nawh,
pux o nawh,
mai paox grawm nawh sivoe an,
hoek tix kia ngien ka nawh khawm uik,
sawm tau mai nawh ka daux nyiex nawh.
Kix kied koe krax pa caox rhawm ka nawh,
son krax laux leeing pa pon nawh khawm uik khaing Simiang Siyiex dee an,
mai hoek tix ngu ka rhawm nawh.
Kix ku kaux tawx mau mai kreem khri tix mu ka nawh heue.
12 Yam hoik kuad ju Yaw-paw,
Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka nawh,
mai kawn lheu nie khaing pa pon nawh yam nhum ju tix.
Yaw-paw koe yung tix mheen pon rheng mu,
ka-la-uk liah rheng mu,
moi ra rheng mu,
la tix rheng mu.
13 Nawh koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
kawn bun loe kaux.
14 Nawh sum ceu kaux kawn bun yex kaux Yie-mi-ma,
kawn bun i kaux Ci-si-yax,
kawn bun am kaux Ci-liien Ha-phu.d
15 Ka piang haktiex ang koe bun pa kied mhawm grang awm kawn bun Yaw-paw khankix tix kaux kawx.
Keeing kix kieh kix mai prie kix khankix yaox tix ih nyaux gaux rhawm ka daux sin ngao tix heue.
16 Khaix an,
Yaw-paw baox deeh im ju koe tix yieh tipon num,
mai ngai nawh pon tix yaox ka doeh ceu kawn praix tix pon ju.
17 Yuh nin,
Yaw-paw hoik lang ju mai yum yam kuad tix heue.
