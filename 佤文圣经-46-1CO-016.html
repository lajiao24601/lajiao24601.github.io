Teem coi kawn Khrit ka mau khri
16
aPhan mawh son pa khrawm mau khri teem coi ka kawn Khrit,
pex chix tix hawt tix yuh awm hoik tawx aux lox sivoe an ka ceu cao Cia-la-thaix yuh nan.
2 Ku singaix tuah,
pex ku kaux caw tix hawt awm pa pon tix teei mau lih tix blah khrawm tau,
ang siao krox aux hoek taug khrawm.
3 Yam hoek aux,
aux tag sang ploi pui pa hoik yum rhawm pex,
tawx lai pa krai grawh grawng ka kix,
mai kieh kix viex mau pa teem coi pex hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
4 Phan sang lo to kaux aux hu,
kix chix tix hu tau mai aux heue.
Pa keud Pao-law tix sang yuh
5 bYam in aux jiet tag sang hu ka Ma-chi-toeng.
Khaix hoik vhi aux ka Ma-chi-toeng,
aux tag sang hoek ka pex dee an.
6 Aux tee kawn ot mai pex dee an tix baux bawg yam,
tee mawh bawg yam kuat priex klawng,
khaix an ca nied tix hu krax hu tix.
Yuh nin,
aux hu dee mawx ang ah,
pex chix tix hu tawx aux.
7 Aux ang rhawm vhi ka pex lawng krax;
phan Simiang Ye-sux cu,
aux simie tix paung ot mai pex tom rheeng bawg yam heue.
8 cdViang mawh nan,
aux kawn sang ot ka Yi-fu-saw,
tom hoek ka poi tix phuan ngaix;
9 mawh kheu viang hoik koe pui tom nie pa tieb tie ka aux kawx,
siviex dee in hoik paoh tix,
mai aux hoik koe bawg yam pa mhawm tix yuh ka kaing heue.
10 ePhan Thi-maw-thaix hoek ka pex dee an,
aux simie pex rieb tix kia ngien nawh mhawm mhawm,
kieh nawh ot mhawm mhawm,
mawh kheu hoik yuh nawh kaing jhaw son Siyiex awm aux yuh nan.
11 Pui tix kaux kawx ang pon tix map nai ka nawh;
tag caw tix tawx lox mhawm kieh nawh nied tix hu ka krax hu tix,
kieh nawh deeh hoek ka aux dee in,
mawh kheu hoik krox jun aux nawh hoek tau mai kawn Khrit khankix heue.
12 Phan mawh pux aik Ya-paw-law,
aux hoik joh jhan nawh mai kawn Khrit tang kaux hu vhi tau ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ca krai phan hu yam in ang hiex khaw,f
nawh kawn krox ka bawg yam pa khaw tom taug hu heue.
Lox nghoik tix krai
13 Pex tag caw rien rhawm tix,
tag caw tix cung kaux cong ka daux krax yum rhawm,
tag caw tix kua rhawm,
tag caw tix koe riang.
14 Kaing pa yuh khawm uik tag caw tix yuh ka daux krax muh krax rhawm heue.
15 gPux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux koe lox pa krai ka pex.
Pex hoik tawng ka See-thi-fa-na mai pui nyiex ma nawh;
kix mawh pui pa lih mawh kawn Khrit sivoe paox ka Ya-kai-yax,
kheu nan,
kied koe krax gaux rhawm ka daux pa yuh kaing pa khre ka kawn Khrit.
16 Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka pui pa yuh nin,
khawm pui pa yuh kaing pa taug mao tau mai kix heue.
17 See-thi-fa-na,
Fu-thu-na-tu mai Ya-kai-kux pon hoek dee in,
aux kied gaux rhawm ka tete.
Kix hoik blaox ka krax pa ang pex nyawm rhawm ka ang tix ot dee in.
18 Kix hoik kieh aux pon krax pa nyawm rhawm,
pa hoik awm pa kieh pex nyawm rhawm yuh nan.
Pui pa yuh nin,
khaw tix mawh pui pa nab yam yung yi pex ka heue.
19 hCeu cao pa ot ka haktiex Ya-si-yax ku dauh,
chawk cheng pex mhawm ot khawm uik;
Ya-cui-la mai Pai-ci-la,
khawm ceu cao ka daux nyiex ma kix,
chawk cheng pex mhawm ot ka daux Simiang Ye-sux.
20 Pux aik pux o khankix dee in chawk cheng pex mhawm ot khawm uik heue.
Pex tag caw tix ra lai chawk cheng ngheet paox tix ka daux krax pa singax singeh heue.
21 To kaux aux tiiem lai ah nin:
“Pao-law nawb lox piang pex heue!”
22 Phan koe pui pa ang muh ka Simiang Ye-sux,
nawh mawh pa khaw tix pon krax pa jhax lawg heue.
Simiang Ye-sux eue,
kieh maix hoek heue!
23 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son ka pex heue!
24 Ka daux Ye-sux Khrit,i
aux kied muh pex pui ku kaux heue!j
