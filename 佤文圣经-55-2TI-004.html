4
Ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex mai Ye-sux Khrit pa tag sang prawng ca ka pui im mai pui yum an,
ka daux pa lih bleeh mai siawp sibrawm nawh,
aux sau riang tix sidiex gau maix:
2 tag caw tix sidiex ka gum lox buan,
caw bawg yam pa mhawm ang ah,
bawg yam pa ang mhawm ang ah,
tag caw tix teei krax gun yeung pa ting jheeh nyaw,
ne raw,
joh jhan,
sidiex gau.
3 Yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
yam an pui tag sang tieb tie ka been pa gau ceu pa grum mhawm,
hawt ka alo nex nham to kaux tix,
hu sawg pui mawh sara tix ku dauh ku dee,
son sang naog yuh yhaok pa ngiex kix.
4 Kix thox yhaok tix ang nghiet ka lox pa mawh pa meh,
mai tieh tix krai ka lox lao pui pa ang koe ceu koe cak.
5 Phan mawh maix,
tag caw tix rien tix ka daux kaing ku ceu;
tag caw tix kham krax pa kha rhawm,
yuh kaing pa caw cao sidiex tix yuh,
kaux sijax ka daux kaing pa yuh maix heue.
6 Phan mawx aux,
bawg yam pa tawx aux ju tix hoik hoek;
yam in hoik mawh ka daux bawg yam pa lih hu aux khaing haktiex.
7 Pa keung aux tix yuh krax pa lah to ka an,
aux hoik to hoek;
krax pa caw aux tix sang to,
aux hoik si dauh to;
krax yum rhawm pa caw aux tix sang hawt,
aux hoik giex kaux cong.
8 Jauh yam in hu sivoe ngai,
mhok khri pa rauh pa see krox jun ka aux,
pa mawh pa sang tawx Siyiex pa yuh krax prawng krax ca an ka aux,
ka daux singax bawg yam pa lih hoek Ye-sux Khrit,
ang joeng tawx ka aux khaing,
mai tawx ka khawm can pui pa kied simie ka pa lih hoek nawh khankix heue.
Lox tawm ka tang ceu
9 Maix tag caw tix nied hoek ka aux dee in.
10 aTi-ma muh ka haktiex pa koe yam in,
mai hoik lih hu khaing aux hu ka Thie-sa-law-ni-cia.
Keu-leu-see hu ka Cia-la-thaix,
Thi-to hu ka Ta-ma-thaix,
11 bmawh ngie Lu-cia pa ot tau mai aux khaing.
Maix tag caw tix hu sawg ka Ma-kheu,
viex nawh hoek tau mai maix,
mawh kheu nawh taug sang chix teem coi ka kaing pa yuh aux.
12 cAux hoik ploi Thoe-ci-ku hu ka Yi-fu-saw.
13 dYam hoek maix,
tag caw tix viex sibex dha pa deuh aux ot ka daux nyiex ma Cia-pux yam ot aux ka Theu-law-yax hoek;
tag caw tix viex hoek khawm lai an kix,
nie mawh phuk lai yung an ra loe phuk tag caw tix viex hoek nang heue.
14 eYa-li-sang-tax cang mai tawng hoik yuh pa ang khaw ka aux ang lai iak;
Simiang Ye-sux tag sang hawt tix pawk bix ka nawh awm kaing pa yuh nawh.
15 To kaux maix kawx tag caw tix rien tix ka nawh,
mawh kheu kied sau nawh riang tix tieb tie ka gum lox pa sidiex ex heue.
16 Yam krai aux lox son to kaux tix ka daux bawg tix,
pui ang koe praok aux,
pui hun hoik tik vud ka aux.
Paw kieh Siyiex thiem tie phi map piang kix heue!
17 Simiang Ye-sux teem coi ot ka praok aux,
tawx riang rao ka aux,
kieh aux pon sidiex gum lox buan in khawm uik ka can pui tang ceu tang cad;
aux kawx hoik pon pa taoh lih khaing daux jheeh sivai sang si.
18 Simiang Ye-sux tag sang taoh aux luat luang khaing krax pa caox khawm uik,
mai rieb aux hoek ka meung rao ma cie nawh dee yien yawg.
Kieh min plak ngai mawh cie nawh leeing ju leeing phan heue!
Lox nghoik pa chawk mhawm ot
19 fChawk pui nyiex ma Pai-ci-la,
Ya-cui-la,
mai A-ni-seu-fu mhawm ot son aux liak.
20 gYi-la-to hoik paung ot ka Keu-ling-to.
Theu-law-fe-maw koe jhawm saux,
aux nhie nawh ot ka Mi-li-to.
21 Maix tag caw kieh tix khran hoek sivoe bawg yam sang kuat praix heue.
Yo-pu-law,
Pu-thiien,
Li-nu,
Kha-lao-ti-ya,
mai pux aik pux o tang kaux khankix chawk maix mhawm ot khawm uik heue.
22 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux ot tau mai maix heue!
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka pex heue!
