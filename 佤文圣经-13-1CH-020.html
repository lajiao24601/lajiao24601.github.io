Ta-wue pon teei ka veng La-pa
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 12.26–31)
20
aKa daux num khaix yam rung praix,
pa mawh bawg yam siao simiang khankix ka pui naing,
Yaw-ya thung pui naing pon laig cam ka haktiex Ya-meung;
viang mawh nan,
simiang Ta-wue ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Yaw-ya puing teei ka veng La-pa.
2 Koe mhok khri ka piang kaing riang siyiex Mi-leu-kongb pa krub pui Ya-meung tix mu,
been cien koe tix ngoe phuan kongcing,
sau siang simaox ot kah,
Ta-wue tom teei siang simaox an lih,
mai sau ot ka mhok simiang pa ceep tix.
Nawh kawx pon tud kraung tom nie khaing daux veng,
3 mai tawx lox ka pui pa ot daux veng an,
kieh kix teei tix yuh kaing ka jhi,
rhiem,
moe.
Ta-wue kawx yuh nang nan ka pui tang kaux pa ot ka daux veng Ya-meung.
Khaix an Ta-wue mai pui naing cie nawh deeh ing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Puing ka pui ting Fe-li-see
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 21.15–22)
4 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Fe-li-see baox puing paox tix ka haktiex Ci-seux.
Yam an,
pui Hu-sa pa kaux Si-pi-kai pauh ka pui pa ting tix kaux pa mawh Si-phaix,
pui Fe-li-see tom ang lai pex heue.
5 cPui Yi-seu-liex baox puing ka pui Fe-li-see.
Kawn simex Ya-eux pa kaux Yi-leu-ha-nang pauh ka pui Cia-theux pux aik Keu-li-yax pa kaux La-ha-mix yum;
pliah pa viex pui in hoik ting awm kraung cag taing man yuh nan heue.
6 Ang koe miex glang tix,
naing baox lih koe ka Cia-theux.
Haktiex an koe pui ting tix kaux,
giien taix giien caong nawh koe tix plak liah mu,
khawm uik koe tix nga pon giien,
nawh mawh doeh ceu cad pui ting sivoe dix dix.
7 Nawh si lah ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
pux ke Ta-wue kawn simex See-mi-yax,
pa kaux Yaw-na-tang pauh nawh yum heue.
8 Pui in plak piang loe kaux,
hoik mawh doeh ceu cad pui Cia-theux pui ting.
Kix hoik yum daux tawm taix Ta-wue mai pui pa ot greem nawh heue.
