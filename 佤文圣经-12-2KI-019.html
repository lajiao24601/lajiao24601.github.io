Simiang khiox khaw ka cao jhai Yi-sai-yax
(Lai Yi-sai-yax 37.1–7)
19
Yam mhong Simiang Si-si-cia ka lox pa krai kix,
nawh kied song rhawm kah,
mai riah ka kraung ceep tix griah,
fua tix ka kraung man keei sum,
laig ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
2 Nawh ploi cao giex kaing ka piang nyiex haw pa kaux Yi-li-ya-cing,
cao tiiem lai daux nyiex simiang pa kaux Seu-paw-nax,
mai cao giex kaing li pa kied lhaong kaing yuh tix blah hu ka kawn simex Ya-maw-see pa mawh cao jhai Yi-sai-yax;
kix hoik ceep ka kraung man keei sum khawm uik.
3 Simiang Si-si-cia tawx lox nhie kix krai ka Yi-sai-yax ah nin:
“In singaix mawh singaix laux leeing meung;
ex hoik ot ka daux krax pa kaig pa tua,
mai ka daux krax pa ciyang.
Ex hoik awm bun pa sang keh kawn tix yuh nan,
kheu kied krawx hoik nu sang keh.
4 Vawng Ya-sux ploi simiang naing hoek map nai ka Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju.
Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix mhong ka lox pa salao in kix,
mai ciyang ka pui pa krai lox in.
Mawh kheu nan,
khiox khaw maix rawg mhaing ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix son yix pui pa deuk in kix heue.”
5 Siliex simiang Si-si-cia hoek ka Yi-sai-yax,
6 nawh tom nhie kix pawk lox Vawng ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
pex ang siao lhat graung kheu lox pa hoik map nai pui Ya-sux ka aux.
7 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh Vawng Ya-sux mhong ka lox pie lox lhien pui,
nied deeh ing ka meung haktiex ot tix,
mai pui tag sang pauh nawh yum ka haktiex pa ot tix heue.”
Pui Ya-sux baox yuh krax ghlat tix bawg
(Lai Yi-sai-yax 37.8–2o)
8 Simiang naing Ya-sux tawng Vawng hoik lih khaing La-ciex,
mai hu puing ka veng Li-na pa dex kix,
mai nawh tom haok hu dee an krai ka nawh.
9 Ka daux lai been pui Ya-sux pa hoik tiiem tix deuh,
hoik krai thung simiang Ku-seu pa kaux Theu-ha-cia pui naing Ai-cix hoek puing ka kix.
Yam mhong Vawng Ya-sux ka lox in,
tom baox ploi kawn cau hu tawx lai ka simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Si-si-cia,
10 ah nin:
“Siyiex pa hoik yum rhawm maix krai ka maix,
aux ang bax yaox tix puing pex ka maix;
viang mawh nan,
maix paw vi glup ka daux krax pa pie lhien heue.
11 Maix tee rien tix hoik tawng,
can meung haktiex pa sang yuh Vawng Ya-sux laux leeing khankix,
dee si dauh mawh pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Maix keud tix kawn sang luat luan lie?
12 Tax du aux hoik sax puing ka Keu-sang,
Ha-lang,
Li-seux,
mai pauh ka ceu pui Paw Yi-tiiena pa ot haktiex Thi-la-sha yum;
Siyiex kix hoik pon taoh ka kix lie?
13 Yam in,
simiang Ha-ma,
simiang Ya-eu-pa,
simiang Si-fa-wa-ying,
simiang Si-nax,
mai simiang Yi-wa khankix ot dee mawx lie?”
14 Simiang Si-si-cia rieb lai khaing taix pui pa tawx lai,
khaix hoik phat nawh kah,
nawh hu ka nyiex buan,
mai yah lai ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
15 brawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa ngawm ka piang Ci-lu-paw eue,
maix hoik mawh Siyiex pa koe tix kaux khaing.
Maix kuang kia ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik;
maix phan yuh ka rao ma haktiex.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan.
16 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kia ngien ka yix liak!
Nghiet ka lox pa hoik map nai Si-na-ci-lix ka maix,
pa mawh Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju.
17 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yix hoik tawng,
simiang pui Ya-sux tom dix,
kix hoik pon dig ka meung pui tom nie,
mai kieh mawh haktiex pa si dawm,
18 mai hoik gleeing ka Siyiex meung pui ku dauh,
tete te lai,
kix ang mawh Siyiex,
mawh ngie riang pa yuh pui ka khaox ka simaox.
19 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
aux khiox khaw maix taoh yix khaing daux tawm taix Ya-sux;
yuh nin,
meung ka piang haktiex tom taug tawng,
mawh ngie maix pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tix kaux khaing heue.”
Lox tawx Yi-sai-yax ka simiang
(Lai Yi-sai-yax 37.21–38)
20 Kawn simex Ya-maw-see pa kaux Yi-sai-yax tawx lai ka simiang Si-si-cia,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex hoik nghiet ka lox rawg maix buan,
son pa krai maix ka grawng Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Si-na-ci-lix.
21 Nawh ah nin:
‘Si-na-ci-lix eue,
pui Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik nyiah ka maix,
nu nai ka maix,
mai vuk vied kaing tix ka maix.
22 Maix hoik tawng,
pa map nai maix mawh mawx lie?
Pa ne raw maix mawh mawx lie?
Maix een aux,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh,
ang koe ceu cad.
23 Maix hoik ploi kawn cau tix yaw rang di ka aux,
krai hoik pon klawh ling law maix ka gawng lhaong kieh si dawm,
mai hoik pon kuang ka gawng Li-pa-noeng pa blawn lhaong khaing paox tix khawm uik.
Maix yaw rang di,
hoik kid ka khaox si oe bhawng pa blawn lhaong,
mai ka khaox gix pa blawn mhawm dee an,
mai hoik laig ka daux nong khaox pa kied singai khankix.
24 Maix yaw rang di,
maix hoik sax kaong tix nyaux ka rawm dung tang meung,
khaix an pui naing cie maix hoik dig ka rawm klawng Ai-cix kroh khawm uik heue.
25 “ ‘Khex ang maix bax mhong ka kaing pa hoik dawg keud aux tix sang yuh khawm uik lie?
Yam in aux hoek tix yuh lih heue.
Aux tawx riang rao ka maix kieh maix yuh veng an kix lih mawh pum tiex dee riem.
26 Aux hoik kieh pui pa ot dee an ang lai koe krax keud krax sawn,
mai hoik yaoh yee.
Kix hoik awm rib klai pa koe ka daux tiag mai pa koe ka piang braux nyiex,
yam hoek bhaung pa si aoh tix phlu khaing plak lih singaix,
mai hoik kroh vhit khawm uik heue.
27 “ ‘Mai yuh ceu pa tix,
hu dee mawx,
aux hoik tawng ka khawm uik.
Aux kawx hoik tawng maix song rhawm ka tix.
28 Aux hoik dawg mhong grawng pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi maix,
maix hoik song rhawm ka aux.
Yam in aux sang teei goik ciex meeh maix doh kah,
mai teei jhawk jhup jheeh maix kah;
kaoh maix khaing krax mawx,
tom sang deeh reu mai ing ka krax an heue.’ ”
29 Khaix an,
Yi-sai-yax krai ka simiang Si-si-cia ah nin:
yam in aux tawx mhai sakse ka maix.
Ka daux num in mai num in sax,
maix ih ngie ka ceu pa lak kuah daux tiag khaing.
Hoek ka daux an num sax,
maix tom tag chix yaox tix rhuat simie,
khrawm ka priex preem;
khaox plix siiet kawx chix tix sum,
mai chix tix ih ka plix siiet pa kuah khaing daux si peem.
30 Pui pa deuk khaing krax pa tud rhim meung Yo-tax,
mai ca kawn awm khaox ox sum riah tix laig daux tiex,
tawx plix tix koe plak piang yuh nan.
31 Ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ka piang gawng Shi-ang,
ca kawn deuk ka pui pa tud rhim pui pa paung ot kah,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
hoik but rhawm tix yuh kaing in lih heue.
32 “Phan mawh Vawng Ya-sux,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Nawh ang pon laig daux veng in,
mai ang pon puing veng in ka te pie tix taix.
Pui naing pa viex dieb tie naok ang pon hoek dex ka veng in,
mai ang pon sum nang ka tab pa lai lawm veng ka haktiex pa dex dee an.
33 Kaoh nawh khaing krax mawx,
mai sang deeh ing ka krax an;
nawh ang pon laig ka daux veng in.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
sang krai pao yuh nin heue.
34 Kheu son to kaux aux mai son siliex aux Ta-wue,
aux sang priex pau ka veng in,
mai taoh ka veng in heue.’ ”
35 Ka daux bo an,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex kieh pui pa laig daux nyiex bang pui Ya-sux,
pon pauh ka pui naing kix yum kao daix mheen mai phuan rheng kaux.
Yam hoik riang praix ngaix khaix,
haktiex an to kaux yum pui hoik naog ka khawm uik.
36 Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Si-na-ci-lix dak tix deeh,
mai hoek ka Ni-ni-we heue.
37 Koe tix singaix,
yam ot nawh tix krub ka Siyiex Ni-see-lawx daux nyiex vad,
kawn simex nawh ra kaux pa kaux Ya-teu-mi-leux mai Sa-li-seux pauh nawh yum,
mai to hu mox tix ka haktiex Ya-la-lax.
Kawn simex tang kaux pa kaux Yi-sha-ha-toeng plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
