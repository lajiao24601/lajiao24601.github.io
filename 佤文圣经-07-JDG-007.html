Ci-tiien puing pex ka pui Mi-tiien
7
Koe tix singaix,
Ci-tiien mai can pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix kaoh pon ngawb cao,
mai sum nyiex bang ot ka praok dee lih rawm Ha-lui.
Nyiex bang cie pui Mi-tiien ot ka sigreung ngawd,
ka plak laong dee ot kix,
mai dex ka Maw-li-kang heue.
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ci-tiien:
“Pui pa hawt khaix maix kied nie;
phan aux kieh kix pex ka pui Mi-tiien,
mai kix tag een mawh riang rao tix pa puing pex.
3 aMaix caw tix krai ka pui khankix ah nin:
‘Mawx lhat graung chix tix deeh ing ka nyiex;
ex sang ot ka gawng Ci-liex.’ ”
Mawh kheu nan,
tom koe pui ra mheen ra rheng kaux pa deeh ing ka nyiex,
viang mawh nan,
koe ngie pui tix mheen kaux khaing pa kawn ot heue.
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ci-tiien:
“Pui pa hawt khaix maix ca kawn nie.
Viex kix hu ka praok rawm;
aux tag sang guah kix lih dee an tang maix.
Phan aux krai ka maix,
pui in khaw tix hu mai maix,
nawh tom tag chix tix hu;
phan aux krai,
pui in ang khaw tix hu,
nawh tag ang pon tix hu heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien viex pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix hoek ka praok rawm;
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix tang guah pui an kix pa lied rawm ka dak tix awm sox yuh nan,
mai pui an kix pa grux ngawng tix nyaux yuh nan khaing paox.”
6 Koe pui loe yieh kaux pa teei taix tix dawm ka rawm lied ka dak tix,
pa deuk khankix tag grux ngawng tix nyaux uik heue.
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ci-tiien:
“Aux tag sang teei ka pui in kix pa teei taix tix dawm ka rawm lied ka dak tix khankix taoh ka pex,
mai ap pui Mi-tiien ka daux tawm taix pex;
kieh pui pa deuk khankix deeh ing ka nyiex heue.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien deuh pui loe yieh kaux kuang kia ka ceu kraung khrai pa siao mai teet tawng,
nhie pui pa deuk khankix prung prang tang ing ka nyiex tix.
Nyiex bang cie pui Mi-tiien ot ka daux ngawd greem kix heue.
9 Ka daux bo an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Ci-tiien ah nin:
“Kaoh!
Lih puing ka nyiex bang pui Mi-tiien;
aux sang ap kix ka daux tawm taix maix.
10 Phan maix lhat puing,
maix ran viex kawn cau maix Phu-la lih hu ka nyiex bang Mi-tiien.
11 Maix nghiet tix mhong ka lox pa krai kix,
maix tag sang koe riang tix hu puing heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien viex kawn cau tix lih hoek ka daog nyiex bang pui pa koeng khankix.
12 Pui Mi-tiien,
pui Ya-ma-lix,
mai ceu pui pa ot ka daux tiag khankix,
hoik naog ka daux ngawd uik,
mai haoh hun awm phung sido yuh nan;
ka-la-uk kix hoik haoh hun awm mu mhaik ka praok rawm heue.
13 Ci-tiien hoik hoek,
mai mhong grawng krai pui ca maox tix ka paox grawm pui naing nawh.
Pui an ah nin:
“Aux ca maox tix yaox ka lon khao mun nghox ca pa lang mu tix mu,
pa bang lia tix laig ka daux bang ot ex pui Mi-tiien,
mai dheem ka nyiex bang,
nyiex bang laux mai ghrup piang tiex heue.”
14 Paox pui naing nawh an pawk lox ah nin:
“An rien tix mawh vaig kawg Ci-tiien kawn simex Yaw-a-see pui Yi-seu-liex!
Ang lai koe dee bleeh tang ceu;
Siyiex hoik ap Mi-tiien mai pui naing ex khawm uik ka daux tawm taix nawh heue.”
15 Khaix hoik mhong grawng pa ca maox pui an mai grawng pa kah pui ka ca maox an,
Ci-tiien tom grux ngawng tix krub ka Siyiex.
Nawh deeh ing ka dee ot pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
ah nin ka kix:
“Kaoh!
Simiang Siyiex hoik ap pui naing Mi-tiien ka daux tawm taix pex!”
16 Ci-tiien guah pui loe yieh kaux lih koe loe cub,
tix kaux pui tawx teet tawng tix mu,
mai kawng tix mu,
ka daux kawng ngu koe daux.
17 Nawh krai ka kix ah nin:
“Yam hoek aux ka daog bang dee ot pui pa koeng khankix,
pex caw tix jhak ka aux,
aux yuh ka mawx,
pex hawt tix yuh yuh nan.
18 Yam phlu aux mai cub ex in ka teet,
pex kawx phlu ka teet ot ka vung viang dee bang,
tag caw tix phlu ka teet,
mai kok yo ah nin:
‘Yuh son Simiang Siyiex!
Son Ci-tiien!’ ”
19 Ci-tiien mai pui pa viex nawh hu an tix yieh kaux,
sivoe sang hoek greung sawm,
khaix hoik loh pui naing cao pa pau nyiex khankix,
hoek ka daog nyiex bang.
Kix phlu teet,
mai lauh max ka kawng pa ot ka daux tawm taix tix;
20 pui tang cub ra cub kawx hawt tix yuh awm an nang.
Taix khawn goix kix yaog ka ngu proh,
taix khawn dawm kix giex ka teet,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Pauh pui koeng son Simiang Siyiex!
Pauh pui koeng son Ci-tiien!”
21 Kix ot vung viang nyiex bang,
mai tang giex kaux cong ka kaing yuh tix;
pui naing pa koeng khankix,
riag yo mai yaog tix tang to.
22 Yam phlu pui Ci-tiien loe yieh kaux ka teet,
Simiang Siyiex tag kieh pui koeng khankix ra lai mug ka paox tix.
Kix to hu mox tix klawd ka plak Si-li-la,
tom hoek ka Paw See-tha mai ka Ya-paw Mi-heu-la dex ka Tha-pa heue.
23 Na-fu-tha-lix,
Ya-seux,
mai pui haktiex Ma-na-si khawm uik hoik khrawm tix lih leue ka pui Mi-tiien.
24 Ci-tiien ploi kawn cau tix hu ka gawng Yi-fa-liien ku dauh ku dee,
ah nin ka kix:
“Lih puing ka pui Mi-tiien.
Pau kaux cong ka diang rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
tom hoek ka Paw Pa-la,
kuan pui Mi-tiien,
paw kieh tiang ka rawm.”
Pui Yi-fa-liien tom khrawm ka paox tix,
mai pau ka diang rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
tom hoek Paw Pa-la.
25 Kix pon giex ka simiang pui Mi-tiien ra kaux,
pa mawh Eu-lix mai Si-yi-paw.
Kix pauh Eu-lix ka haktiex Eu-li-yiien,
mai pauh Si-yi-paw ka haktiex Si-yi-paw dee greeik plai.
Kix kawn ca leue puing ka pui Mi-tiien,
mai viex ka si-ang kaing Eu-lix mai Si-yi-paw hoek tawx ka Ci-tiien ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix heue.
