Rieb teug sijax mukheu laig daux nyiex buan
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 5.2–6.2)
8
aSon sang viex ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex laig ka daux nyiex buan khaing Shi-ang veng Ta-wue,
simiang Saw-law-meung khrawm ka kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex mai cao kaing ceu pui ku cub khankix,
hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 bKix tom ot ka daux khix Yi-tha-nian,
pa mawh khix aliah,
bawg yam Poi Nyiex-gox-bang,
kix khrawm paox tix hoek ka simiang Saw-law-meung dee an.
3 Yam hoik grum hoek kuad song ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
4 cao giex kaing li khankix viex teug sijax mukheu laig ka daux nyiex buan.
Pui Li-we mai cao giex kaing li kawx viex ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
nyiex gox buan man,
mai kraung pa siao khawm uik laig daux nyiex buan.
5 Simiang Saw-law-meung mai pui haoh hun Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
khrawm paox tix ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu,
mai tan lu ka moi yung kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
ceu pa tan lu in kix kied nie,
mai ang lai dak uik simin.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li khankix viex teug sijax mukheu laig ka daux nyiex buan,
mai deuh ot ka greem Ci-lu-paw ra mu daux nyiex pa kied singax singeh.
7 Preeik Ci-lu-paw ra mu nih tix tawb teug sijax mukheu mai khaox grawng teug sijax mukheu.
8 Khaox grawng kied lang,
pui pa cung see sivoe nyiex dee pa kied singax singeh,
tom tag chix yaox ka khaox grawng khawm ra toh,
phan ot tang dauh mai ang chix yaox.
(Khaox grawng in tom hoek singaix kawn ot dee an.)
9 cKa daux teug sijax mukheu deuh ngie khlawp simaox ka ra mu khaing,
mai ang deuh kraung tang ceu kah;
khlawp simaox in ra mu hoik mawh yam lih kawn pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Ai-cix,
Simiang Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
Maw-si ot ka gawng Si-nai mai deuh khlawp simaox in ot ka daux teug sijax mukheu heue.
10 dYam lih hu cao giex kaing li khaing nyiex buan,
dum mawx dum an koe pai awm naog ka daux nyiex buan,
11 pa riang min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex jhan riang,
tom ang lai pon kieh kix yaox tix laig yuh kaing yuh tix ka daux nyiex.
12 eYam an,
Saw-law-meung rawg buan ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik sau singaix ot ka daux rao maf.
Viang mawh nan,
maix but rhawm tix sang paung ot ka daux pa pu ceep pai awm.
13 Aux hoik sum nyiex buan pa mhawm son maix tix lhang,
son sang kieh mawh nyiex dee ot maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Gum lox sidiex Saw-law-meung ka kawn pui
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 6.3–11)
14 Pui hun Yi-seu-liex cung dee an khawm uik.
Simiang Saw-law-meung veeh tix hu plak in,
ot ka plak sivoe pui hun,
mai rawg buan ka Siyiex son kix.
15 Nawh ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex!
Nawh hoik giex kaux ka lox pa ah tix ka keeing aux Ta-wue;
nawh hoik ah nin ka Ta-wue:
16 g‘Jauh tom viex aux pui cie tix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
aux ang sax laug ka veng Yi-seu-liex tix dauh kawx son sang sum ka nyiex buan dee krub ka aux;
viang mawh nan,
aux laug ka maix,
pa mawh Ta-wue mawh cao pa kuang ka piang pui cie aux heue.’ ”
17 hSaw-law-meung ca nied tix ah nin:
“Keeing aux Ta-wue keud tix sang sum ka nyiex buan,
son sang kieh mawh dee krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
18 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix koe rhawm tix sang sum nyiex buan son aux,
hoik mawh pa mhawm;
19 iviang mawh nan,
maix ang pon tix sum,
mawh ngie kawn simex maix,
pa mawh kawn lih maix taug pon tix sum nyiex buan aux khaing heue.’
20 “Yam in Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh lih ka lox pa ap tix.
Aux plud keeing tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
khaix an mai hoik sum ka nyiex buan dee krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
21 Aux kawx hoik rien khre ka dee deuh teug sijax mukheu ka daux nyiex buan.
Ka daux teug sijax mukheu hoik deuh khlawp simaox gum lox sijax mukheu ot kah;
gum lox sijax mukheu in hoik mawh yam viex Simiang Siyiex tax du ex lih khaing Ai-cix,
mai yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix heue.”
Saw-law-meung khiox khaw rawg buan
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 6.12–42)
22 Khaix an,
Saw-law-meung kieh pui hun Yi-seu-liex yaox tix yam hu cung ka sivoe khu dee tan Simiang Siyiex,
nawh yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn
23 mai rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
greem rao ma piang haktiex ang koe siyiex pa pon tiag tix tau mai maix!
Maix hoik giex kaux ka sijax mukheu,
pa hoik yuh maix tau mai pui cie tix:
yam nghiet kix ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
maix tag caw tix jhi ka krax muh tix lih.
24 Maix hoik giex kaux ka lox pa hoik ah tix ka keeing aux Ta-wue,
hoek yam in singaix,
gum lox in hoik lih tix mawh ku gum heue.
25 jSimiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
yam in aux khiox khaw maix kieh lox pa hoik ah maix tix deuh ka keeing aux tang ceu khankix lih tix mawh:
maix hoik krai ka nawh,
phan doeh ceu cad nawh priex rhawm nghiet ka maix,
awm hoik nghiet maix ka nawh yuh nan,
tag sang koe pui pa yuh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju.
26 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh gum lox pa hoik ah maix tix deuh ka siliex maix,
pa mawh keeing aux Ta-wue lih tix mawh heue.
27 k“Siyiex eue,
maix rug sang chix ot ka piang haktiex lie?
Viang hoik mawh daog rao ma pa ang koe dee die,
mai ang doh kub maix ot,
nyiex buan pa hoik sum aux doh nie sang kub maix ot yuh ka mawx lie?
28 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
aux mawh siliex maix;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox buan rawg aux,
nghiet ka lox khiox khaw aux plak sivoe ngai maix liak.
29 lKhiox khaw maix kia ngien ka nyiex buan in ku singaix ku sawm;
in hoik mawh pa laug maix tix kieh mawh dee krub ka maix.
Yam rawg aux buan ka nyiex buan in,
maix nghiet ka liak.
30 Yam rawg siliex maix mai pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex buan ka dee in,
khiox khaw maix nghiet khaing rao ma,
mai pluat ploi ka phi map yix heue.
31 “Phan koe pui pa deuh phi map phit lud ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
viex nawh hoek ka nyiex buan in,
pa mawh sivoe khu dee tan maix,
tiex tu ang tix koe dee lud,
32 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg yix khaing rao ma,
mai prawng son siliex maix:
pa koe krax phit lud,
tag hawt tix pawk bix awm pa khaw nawh tix kham.
Pui ang koe dee lud,
tag hawt tix prawng awm ang nawh mawh pui ang koe dee lud heue.
33 “Phan pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex kheu phit lud ka maix pui koeng pon pex ka kix,
khaix an kix deeh veeh tix hoek ka maix,
krai doh keud ka maix,
mai viex rhawm tiiem hoek ka nyiex buan in,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi phi map tix,
34 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox kix khaing rao ma.
Khaix an,
baox pluat ploi ka phi map pui cie maix,
viex kix deeh ing ka haktiex pa hoik tawx maix ka kix mai ka tax du kix heue.
35 “Phan koe pui cie maix phit lud ka maix,
mai kieh rao ma ang lai tawx rawm lhiex,
khaix an,
kix deeh loh rhawm tix,
krai doh keud ka maix,
mai viex rhawm tiiem veeh tix hoek ka nyiex buan in,
rawg buan ka maix,
36 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix khaing nyiex rao ma.
Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi phi map siliex maix mai pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex,
sidiex gau kix hu ka daux krax rauh krax see.
Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix tawx rawm lhiex lih ka piang haktiex cie maix,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik tawx maix mawh sin ngao pui cie maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
37 “Phan ka daux meung lih koe sibhawm praix,
jhawm saux preng,
bhaung pa kroh yuh praix klawng,
pa moh laux,
viag vua,
sido,
phan ang mawh nan pui koeng lai lawm pui cie maix,
mai si pup jhawm saux krax laux krax leeing,
38 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg buan kix.
Phan ka daux pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex,
pa koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm nawh tete,
tawx taix tix khawm ra khawn rawg buan ka nyiex buan in,
39 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg buan kix,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix khaing nyiex rao ma,
pluat ploi ka kix,
mai teem coi ka kix.
Mawh ngie maix pa tawng ka daux rhawm pui khaing.
Khiox khaw maix hawt tix kia kix awm pa khaw pui ku kaux tix pon,
40 son sang lhat graung yung yi pui cie maix ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap maix tix tawx ka tax du yix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
41-42 “Phan koe pui tang ceu tang cad pa paung ot dee singai hoik mhong ceu kaux pa ting min maix,
taix pa ting riang maix,
plawng taix pa taoh teem maix ku bawg ku yam,
mai hoek tix rawg buan ka nyiex buan in,
43 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg nawh.
Khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg nawh khaing nyiex rao ma,
mai hawt tix yuh awm lox pa khiox khaw nawh ka maix,
son sang kieh pui khawm bee ma tawng maix awm pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex yuh nan,
lhat graung yung yi ka maix,
tawng ka nyiex buan pa hoik sum aux in mawh dee krub ka maix heue.
44 “Phan koe pui cie maix hawt tix yuh gum lox tawm maix,
hu puing pui koeng ka daux bang naing,
viang kix ot dee mawx ang ah,
mai veeh ngai tix ka plak veng pa hoik laug maix in,
mai rawg buan ka nyiex buan in pa hoik sum aux son maix,
45 khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix liak.
Khiox khaw maix nghiet lox buan rawg kix khaing rao ma,
mai kieh kix puing pex heue.
46 “Phan koe pui cie maix phit lud ka maix,
(ang koe pui pa ang yuh phi map,)
ka daux pa song rhawm maix,
maix kieh pui koeng pon pex ka kix,
mai giex laung kix hu ka haktiex tang dauh,
viang hoik mawh haktiex pa kied singai an kawx,
47 khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg pui cie maix buan.
Phan kix deeh loh rhawm rhi tix ka dee an,
mai khiox khaw ka maix,
krai doh ka phi map tix pa lang teung caox,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg kix buan.
48 Phan kix loh rhawm rhi tix tete ka daux haktiex an,
mai veeh tix rawg buan ka maix,
ka daux haktiex pa hoik tawx maix ka tax du yix in,
veeh tix rawg buan ka veng pa hoik laug maix in,
mai ka nyiex buan in pa hoik sum aux son maix,
49 khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa rawg kix.
Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa rawg kix khaing nyiex dee ot maix rao ma,
caox rhawm ka kix.
50 Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi phi map kix khawm uik,
mai kieh pui caox kawx chix koe krax pa caox rhawm ka kix.
51 Kix hoik mawh pui cie maix,
mawh maix pa hoik viex kix lih khaing Ai-cix ka daux glah ngu ting an heue.
52-53 “Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong eue,
kieh maix tawx buan son kia ngien ka pui cie maix Yi-seu-liex mai ka simiang kix leeing ju,
yam khiox khaw kix maix teem coi tix ku bawg,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg buan kix.
Awm yam viex maix tax du yix lih khaing Ai-cix yuh nan,
maix hoik kieh gum lox pa deuh tix lih mawh ka daux siliex maix Maw-si,
maix laug kix mawh pui cie tix khaing ka daux pui piang haktiex khawm uik heue.”
Gum lox rawg buan pa nghoik tix rawg
54 Saw-law-meung ca krub ka plak sivoe khu dee tan,
mai yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn rawg buan;
khaix hoik si dauh rawg nawh buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tom yaog tix kaoh cung.
55 Nawh ah lox ting tix tom khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka pui hun Yi-seu-liex khawm uik;
nawh ah nin:
56 m“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex!
Nawh hoik yuh lih ka lox pa hoik ap tix deuh,
mai tawx krax pa nhim ka pui cie tix.
Nawh hoik giex kaux ka lox pa ah nawh tix deuh khawm uik ka daux siliex nawh Maw-si.
57 Kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex ot tau mai ex,
awm hoik ot tau nawh mai tax du ex yuh nan.
Kieh nawh paw vi lih hu khaing ex,
paw vi tik vud ka ex leeing ju!
58 Kieh nawh kieh rhawm ex hawt tix,
son sang kieh ex hawt tix yuh alo nawh tom si dauh,
mai priex tix hawt lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx nawh ka tax du ex.
59 Kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex doh keud ka lox pa khiox khaw in mai lox pa khiox khaw aux ka nawh leeing ju.
Kieh nawh hawt awm pa sang lo kix pui Yi-seu-liex mai simiang kix ku ngai caox rhawm ka kix heue.
60 “Yuh nin,
meung piang haktiex khawm uik tag tawng,
mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex khaing,
mai ang lai koe tang kaux.
61 Kieh pex pui hun,
pa mawh pui cie Siyiex,
koe krax cu nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex leeing ju,
priex tix hawt ka can lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap nawh khawm uik,
awm in singaix yuh nin heue.”
Poi tan lu ka nyiex buan
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 7.4–10)
62 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Saw-law-meung mai pui hun Yi-seu-liex tan lu krax tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
63 Nawh tan lu moi ra mheen mai ra rheng mu,
mai yung kao ra mheen mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
Yuh nin,
simiang mai pui hun khawm uik yuh poi krax tan lu son nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex.
64 Ka daux singaix an,
nawh kawx guah dee yi haktiex ka sigreung biang brawk,
pa mawh biang brawk pa koe sivoe nyiex buan mawh pa singax,
mai tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
khawm bee kraung vaig khawng brie pa siao ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim dee an.
Saw-law-meung yuh mawh nin mawh kheu kied iak khu tawng,
ang kub khawng tan ot piang tom nie heue.
65 Saw-law-meung mai pui hun Yi-seu-liex khawm uik yuh Poi Nyiex-gox-bang ot ka nyiex buan aliah singaixn.
Pui hun kaoh khaing diang moing gawng Ha-ma ka plak laong dee singai,
mai baoh mee haktiex Ai-cix ku dauh ka plak seh,
hoek tix yuh poi tau.
66 Ka daux daix singaix,
Saw-law-meung tawx pui haoh pui hun deeh ing ka nyiex;
kix hoik nawb buan ka simiang khawm uik,
mai gaux rhawm gaux rhi deeh ing ka nyiex,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka siliex nawh Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh Yi-seu-liex heue.
