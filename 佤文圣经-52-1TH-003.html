3
aMawh kheu nan,
ex ang lai baox pon krox jun,
mai hoik but tix ca kawn ot ka Ya-tien,
2 kheu nan,
tom ploi pux aik ex Thi-maw-thaix hu ka pex dee an;
nawh mawh paox yuh kaing tau mai ex pa sidiex ka gum lox buan Khrit pa yuh son Siyiex.
Ex ploi nawh hu,
kaing ting mawh son sang joh jhan mai teem coi ka krax yum rhawm pex,
3 son ang sang kieh pui ka daux pex deeh yawn tix kheu pa kham krax tuk tiak tuk te.
Pex hoik tawng,
krax pa tuk tiak tuk te in hoik mawh pa khaw ex tix kham.
4 Yam ot ex tau mai pex,
ex hoik grox krai pex mhong grawng tag sang kham krax tuk tiak tuk te.
Yam in kaing ceu pa lih tix mawh jiet hoik khaw mai lox pa dawg krai ex;
in hoik mawh pa tawng pex.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom ploi Thi-maw-thaix hu;
aux ang lai baox pon krox jun.
Aux ploi nawh hu,
mawh son sang kieh tix tawng krax yum rhawm pex mawh pa yuh ka mawx.
Aux ang yum rhawm ka grawng hoik nyaw glaw phi caox ka pex,
mai kieh kaing yuh ex mawh pa giex going heue!
6 bYam in,
Thi-maw-thaix hoik deeh hoek khaing dee ot pex dee an;
nawh viex grawh grawng pa dex mai krax yum rhawm pex,
mai krax muh krax rhawm pex.
Nawh krai pex kied braong ka ex jheeng jheeng,
kied simie tix yaox ka ex,
awm hoik simie ex tix yaox ka pex yuh nan.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
yam ot ex ka daux krax pa kha rhawm khawm uik,
kheu krax pa yum rhawm pex hoik kieh ex pon krax pa joh jhan.
8 Phan pex koe krax yum rhawm pa kaux pa cong ka Simiang Siyiex,
yam in ex chix tix taug toek bhawm tix tete.
9 Ex chix tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex.
Ex yaog yaw ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik kieh pex ex pon krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
10 Ex khiox khaw rawg buan ka nawh leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
son sang pon yaox ex tix hu jhak ka pex,
mai thiem thie ka ceu pa sang lo pex ka daux krax yum rhawm heue.
11 Kieh Siyiex pa mawh keeing ex Siyiex,
mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit paoh daux krax son ex,
kieh ex pon hu ka pex dee an heue!
12 Kieh Simiang Ye-sux kieh pex koe rhawm pa ra lai muh ka paox tix mai ka kawn pui,
khaix an pon krax pa haok huan ku singaix,
awm hoik kied muh ex ka pex yuh nan.
13 Yuh nin,
nawh tag sang kieh rhawm rhi pex kaux cong,
yam lih hoek Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit mai kawn Khrit cie nawh khankix,
mai pon tix singax singeh,
ang koe dee cud cha ka plak sivoe ngai keeing ex Siyiex heue.
