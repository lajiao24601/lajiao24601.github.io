Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka Ku-seu
18
aKa daog rawm klawng Ku-seu plak an koe haktiex ka tix dauh,
dee an hoik chix mhong ka lox luah preeik.
2 Kawn cau tang daux tang meung hu brug ka reu pa yuh ka gawng priem,
jauh ka dee an mai lawng hawt rawm klawng Ni-law lih hoek.
Nied tix deeh ing ka nyiex ma pex heue,
pex pa mawh kawn cau pa ting daux tang meung eue!
Viex lox in deeh ing ka haktiex pex,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik guah rawm klawng tang ot,
meung pa ting riang an,
pa hoik lhat graung haktiex ka khawm uik,
pui pa ting pa lhaong hoik ot dee an heue.
3 Nghiet heue,
can kawn pui pa koe ka piang haktiex!
Tag caw rien tix kia yam yaog chix ka piang gawng heue!
Bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox luah teet tawng heue!
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Aux tag ang nye ang nyux mai kok jhak ka rao ma,
mai jiet awm rawm ciex ka daux bawg yam khrawm ka to ma,
mai pa jhan riang singaix ka daux bawg yam si aoh praix.
5 Ka daux bawg yam pa ang bax pieh ka plix siiet,
yam taug jiet khrum tai,
plix siiet tag sang jauh tix teem,
pui koeng sang yuh Ku-seu laux leeing,
mai hoik yie awm teei vaig pa lawm pat ka nhu plix siiet yuh nan.
6 To kaux yum pui naing kix hoik bhlien tix lih ot plak praix,
kieh mawh priex preem pa ih sim mai to sat;
bawg yam raux praix koe sim pu tix hoek ih,
bawg yam kuat praix koe to sat hoek tix sai ka heue.”
7 Bawg yam an hoik hoek,
haktiex pa hoik guah rawm klawng kieh tang ot an,
meung pa lhat graung pui,
pa ting pa lhaong,
mai pui pa ting riang ting rao,
tag caw tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing.
Kix tag sang hu krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing ka gawng Shi-ang heue.
