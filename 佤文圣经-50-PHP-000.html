Lai Fe-li-pix
Krai dawt
Lai Fe-li-pix mawh pa tiiem Pao-law tix tawx ka ceu cao tix mu pa sum tix yam ot ka haktiex O-co. Fe-li-pix ot ka haktiex Ma-chi-toeng ka daux pa giex Law-ma kaing. Lox lai in mawh pa tiiem Pao-law yam ot tix ka daux thawng. Yam an, nawh si pup krax pa tuk tiak paox pa yuh kix kaing tau, mai baox nie koe krax pa suk sag kheu pa koe been li pa ang mawh ang meh ka ceu cao Fe-li-pix. Viang mawh nan, lox lai in ca kawn naog krax pa gaux rhawm mai krax yum rhawm pa khaw caw, mai hoik jhi grawng pa kaux cong ang yung yang krax yum rhawm nawh ka Ye-sux Khrit heue.
Pao-law tiiem lox lai in, kaing ting mawh son sang krai lox pa khiox khaw ka kawn Khrit haktiex Fe-li-pix; mawh kheu yam cud cha nawh, kix hoik teem coi ka nawh. Yam in nawh sang jawm ka daux bawg yam in, mai joh jhan ka kix, kieh krax yum rhawm kix kaux cong, kieh kix yaox sidawng, viang nawh hoik si pup krax kha rhawm, mai kix kawx ot ka daux krax pa cud, viang mawh nan, ca kawn koe rhawm pa kua mai krax yum rhawm. Pao-law sidiex gau kix ka grawng pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi Ye-sux Khrit, paw vi kieh ka daux pa tang teei ka alo tix mai ka pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi giex gang tix. Nawh krai ah nin: krax ot krax koe kix ka daux ju Khrit hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing buan son Siyiex; buan son in hoik mawh pa pon kix ka daux krax yum rhawm, ang mawh pa pon khaing pa hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pui Yo-thaix. Nawh kawx hoik krai ka grawng pa tawx Siyiex krax gaux rhawm, mai pa nhim ka pui pa im ju ka daux Khrit heue.
Phuk lai in blawn krai sidawng ka krax gaux rhawm, pa lih mawh tix mu, mai krax yum rhawm pa kaux cong, mai krax ot krax koe khankix heue. Ka daux lai hoik jhi lih krax muh Pao-law ka ceu cao Fe-li-pix ku dauh heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–11)
2. Grawh grawng to kaux Pao-law (1.12–26)
3. Krax ot krax koe ka daux Khrit (1.27–2.18)
4. Thi-maw-thaix mai Yi-pa-fu-thi (2.19–30)
5. Rien tix tieb tie ka pui koeng mai krax pa nu tang ceu (3.1–4.9)
6. Pao-law mai paox grawm Fe-li-pix khankix (4.10–20)
7. Lox nghoik (4.21–23)
