19
Khaix an,
aux mhong lox pa ting si num,
awm cub pui hun ka piang rao ma pa kok yo ah nin:
“Ha le lu ya,
yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex heue!
Krax taoh,
min plak ngai,
mai siawp sibrawm hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex ex khawm uik heue!
2 aKrax prawng ca nawh mawh pa mawh pa meh pa rauh pa see heue!
Mawh kheu prawng ca nawh ka bun pa ting krax phit vao pa kieh haktiex laux leeing ka krax pa phit vao tix,
mai hoik ciyang ka phi map pa hoik pauh nawh ka siliex Siyiex.”
3 bKix baox kok yo tix bawg ah nin:
“Ha le lu ya,
yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex heue!
Taox ngu pa gleeing ka veng ting an chu haok daux rao ma ang jud,
mai ang chix naox tix leeing ju heue!”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
kuad song tix nga pon kaux mai to sat pa im ju pon mu an gup tix krub ka Siyiex pa ngawm ka piang dang ngeung ah nin:
“A-meung!
Ha le lu ya!”
Poi ka paox kawn yung
5 cKhaix an,
koe lox lih hoek khaing dang ngeung ah nin:
“Can siliex Siyiex khawm uik,
pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh khankix,
mawh pui ting min ang ah pui iak min ang ah,
tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex ex khawm uik heue!”
6 dAux baox mhong pa awm lox cub pui hun tix cub,
pa awm lox luah rawm pa lih khaing rang,
mai lox si num praix yuh nan ah nin:
“Ha le lu ya,
yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex heue!
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm ex hoik mawh simiang heue!
7 Ex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
yaog yaw ka pa ting pa lhaong nawh heue!
Mawh kheu hoik hoek bawg yam poi ka paox cie kawn yung;
sibun pa yuh ka paox hoik rien khre tix mhawm.
8 Siyiex hoik tawx kraung ceep man keei sum pa paing sawng singax ka nawh.”
(Kraung ceep man keei sum mawh pa krai ka grawng krax rauh see pa yuh pui cie Siyiex heue.)
9 eMawh kheu nan,
kawn cau Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix tiiem lih ah nin:
pui pa ghaok hoek ih poi ka paox kawn yung ting buan son heue!”
Kawn cau Siyiex baox ah nin:
“In hoik mawh gum lox Siyiex tete te lai heue.”
10 Aux baox gup tix ka plak sivoe caong nawh,
tag sang krub ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Paw doh yuh nin heue!
Aux mai maix,
khawm kawn Khrit tang kaux,
can pa hoik giex kaux cong ka krax pa mawh pa meh pa hoik plak jhi Ye-sux,
tag sang mawh siliex khawm uik.
Maix tag caw tix krub ka Siyiex heue!”
Mawh kheu krax pa mawh pa meh pa hoik plak jhi Ye-sux,
mai hoik mawh nang krax pa mawh pa meh pa duk thung ka cao jhai khankix heue.
Pui pa brug brung paing
11 fKhaix an aux yaox pa hoik baoh rao ma.
Jhak heue,
koe brung paing tix mu,
pui pa brug brung an kok mawh “pa mawh pa meh” mai “pa kaux sijax”;
nawh hoek tix prawng ca mai yuh naing ka daux pa rauh pa see.
12 gTawm ngai nawh awm rao ngu yuh nan,
kaing nawh ceep ka mhok khri tom nie.
To kaux nawh tiiem ka ceu kaux pui tix kaux,
viang mawh nan,
lheu khaing to kaux nawh,
ang koe pui pa tawng ka dee bleeh tix kaux kawx.
13 Kraung dha pa ceep nawh hoik naog ka nham.
Ceu kaux nawh kok mawh “gum lox Siyiex”.
14 Kru naing piang rao ma brug ka brung paing,
ceep ka kraung man keei sum pa paing singax,
mai hawt khaix nawh.
15 hVaig pa lawm tix mag lih khaing daux jheeh nawh;
nawh teei vaig in yuh naing ka meung piang haktiex.
Nawh sang teei khaox gix rhiem kuang ka kix,
khaix an sang kieh plai krax pa song rhawm Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm lih khaing daux dung pa greeik plai khien.
16 Kraung dha pa ceep nawh mai ka piang ba nawh hoik tiiem ka “simiang piang simiang,
Siyiex piang Siyiex” ceu kaux in heue.
17 iAux baox yaox kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux cung ka daux singaix.
Nawh kok yo lox pa ting si num ka can sim pa pu ka rao ma ah nin:
“Hoek heue,
hoek tix ih tau ka priex preem cie Siyiex heue!
18 Hoek tix ih ka nex simiang ting,
caox giex kaing naing pa ting,
mai pui naing khankix heue,
mai ih nang nex brung mai nex pui naing brug brung,
mai baox ih ka nex siliex cawng,
nex pui pa pon tix bheeik luan,
nex pui pa ting min pui pa iak min ku ceu pui heue!”
19 Aux baox yaox ka to sat an,
mai ka simiang piang haktiex khankix,
khawm pui naing cie kix pa khrawm tix tau,
yuh naing ka pui brug brung an mai ka pui naing cie nawh.
20 jPui hoik giex ka to sat an;
khawm cao jhai pa pie lhien pa hoik sax yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka plak sivoe ngai nawh kawx hoik giex laung tau.
(Cao jhai pa pie lhien in hoik sax teei mhai pa daox pa am nyaw glaw ka pui pa koe mhai to sat an kah,
mai pa krub ka riang to sat an khankix heue.)
To sat an mai cao jhai pa pie lhien khankix,
hoik teei taing tix tik kix ka daux glauh ngu pa gleeing ka mhat.
21 Pui naing kix hoik yum ka vaig pa lih khaing daux jheeh pui naing pa brug brung khankix;
can sim pa koe khawm uik,
hoik pu hoek tix ih ka nex kix uik heue.
