Ye-sux mai Sa-kai
19
Ye-sux laig daux veng Yie-li-keu,
tag sang jauh tix laig ka daux veng an.
2 Yam an ka daux haktiex koe cao giex kaing pui koh keen tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Sa-kai,
nawh mawh pui pa koe mau tix kaux.
3 Sa-kai kied simie tix yaox Ye-sux mawh pui yuh ka mawx,
viang mawh nan,
to kaux nawh tiiem iak,
nawh ang chix yaox Ye-sux ka daux pui hun.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh to hu ot ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai haok ka khaox liah,
son sang yaox ka Ye-sux,
mawh kheu tag sang jauh Ye-sux tix hu ka krax an.
5 Ye-sux hoek ka haktiex an,
yaog kaing tix jhak ka Sa-kai,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Sa-kai,
nied lih heue!
Ka daux in singaix aux tag sang paung ot ka daux nyiex ma maix heue.”
6 Sa-kai nied tix lih khaing khaox,
mai kied gaux rhawm ka sang rieb ka Ye-sux.
7 Pui hun yaox kah,
mai hoik salao khawm uik ah nin:
“Pui in lai hu paung ka nyiex ma pui phi map lie!”
8 Sa-kai yaog tix kaoh cung krai ka Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux tag sang teei ka sin ngao tix mai guah tix tawx ka pui hot pui sibhawm tix plak;
phan aux gut kheem ka pui mawx,
aux tag sang deeh plud ka nawh pon son heue.”
9 Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Ka daux in singaix krax taoh hoik hoek ka daux nyiex ma in,
mawh kheu hoik mawh pui in kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang nang.
10 Kawn kawn pui tag sang hoek sawg tix taoh ka pui pa hoik grai heue.”
Phek tiiem ka siliex mai ka mau luah
(Lai Ma-thaix 25.14–30)
11 Yam kawn nghiet pui hun ka lox in,
Ye-sux baox krai gum lox phek tiiem ka kix tix ceu.
Yam an,
Ye-sux hoik dex sang hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kix ce siawp sibrawm Siyiex sang lih tix hoek.
12 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Koe pui pa ting ceu tix kaux hoek ka haktiex dee singai,
tag sang yaog nawh mawh simiang,
khaix an deeh hoek.
13 Sivoe sang yaog nawh tix hu,
nawh kok siliex tix kao kaux hoek,
mai tawx mau luah ka kix tix kaux tix plaha ah nin:
‘Yam ang aux ot,
pex teei mau in viex hu yuh lai gee,
mai jhak tix yaox sang pon ka rao miex ding.’
14 Pui meung haktiex ot nawh hoik koeng ka nawh,
khaix an tawx lox nhie pui hu ah nin:
‘Yix ang kieh pui in mawh simiang yix heue.’
15 “Khaix deeh hoek pui pa ting ceu pa hoik yaog mawh simiang an,
dum mawx dum an,
tawx lox ka siliex pa hoik rieb tix teei mau luah an kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
mai sang kieh tix tawng grawng lai gee pa yuh kix hoik pon rao miex ding.
16 Ka daux tix kaux hoek ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
mau luah pa tawx maix ka aux an,
aux hoik pon rao kao plah heue.’
17 Simiang ah nin:
‘Kied mhawm!
Maix pa mawh siliex pa mhawm;
kheu hoik kaux sijax maix ka daux kaing pa iak,
aux tag sang tawx maix kuang veng kao veng.’
18 Siliex ka daux ra kaux hoek ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
mau pa teei maix tix tawx ka aux an,
aux hoik pon rao phuan plah mau luah heue.’
19 Simiang nawh krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Maix kawx tag sang kuang veng phuan veng.’
20 Siliex baox hoek tix kaux ah nin:
‘Simiang,
mau luah pa tawx maix ot dee in;
aux teei man awt taix kie kah.
21 Aux hoik lhat ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik mawh maix pui pa kied kin jhawm tix kaux.
Dee ang pui bax deuh,
maix kawn sang teei lih khaing;
dee ang pui bax van,
maix kawn sang khrawm tix vawg kah.’
22 “Simiang nawh krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Maix pa mawh siliex pa caox,
aux tag sang teei lox pa krai maix prawng ca ka phi map maix heue!
Kheu hoik tawng maix aux mawh pui pa kin jhawm tix kaux,
hoik teei ka pa ang bax deuh,
khrawm vawg ka dee ang bax sum bax van,
23 phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang teei tix deuh mau aux ka daux nyiex krao mau,
yam deeh hoek aux,
chix tix pon khawm num khawm rao lie?’
24 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka pui pa cung tix pau ka plak goix plak dawm tix:
‘Teei mau luah cie nawh hoek,
tawx ka siliex pa koe mau kao plah an heue.’
25 Kix krai ka nawh ah nin:
‘Simiang,
nawh hoik koe mau luah kao plah heue.’
26 bSimiang nawh ah nin:
‘Aux krai ka pex,
pui pa hoik koe,
tag sang nie baox tawx kah;
pui pa ang koe,
pie pa hoik koe nawh ceeix ceeix kawx tag sang teei hu heue.
27 Phan mawh pui koeng an kix pa ang kieh aux mawh simiang,
teei viex kix hoek,
mai pauh kix yum ka plak sivoe ngai aux heue!’ ”
Viex min plak ngai laig ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.1–11; Lai Ma-kheu 11.1–11; Lai Yaw-hang 12.12–19)
28 Khaix hoik krai ka lox in,
Ye-sux hu sivoe ngai,
mai haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
29 Yam dex sang hoek nawh ka Paw-fa-chix mai Paw-ta-ni,
ka plak gawng sime,
Ye-sux tawx lox kieh pui gau tix ra kaux ran hu sivoe,
30 mai tawx lox ka kiex ah nin:
“Pax hu ka nyiex yaong pa koe plak sivoe.
Yam laig pax daux yaong,
pax tag sang yaox ka kawn la tix mu pa ang pui bax brug phrawk tix dee an.
Pax kah ka kawn la an,
tuk hoek dee in.
31 Phan koe pui pa chawk pax yuh ka mawx lai kah ka kawn la in,
pax tom ah nin:
‘Simiang c sang siao ka heue.’ ”
32 Kiex hu,
ceu pa si pup kiex jiet hoik awm lox pa krai nawh ka kiex yuh nan.
33 Yam kah kiex ka kawn la,
cao la chawk kiex ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pax lai kah ka kawn la in lie?”
34 Kiex pawk ah nin:
“Simiang sang siao lo ka heue.”
35 Khaix an kix tuk kawn la hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux,
teei kraung ceep tix tawm ka piang blawg la,
yaog Ye-sux brug kah.
36 Yam hu Ye-sux ka plak sivoe ngai,
pui hun teei kraung ceep tix tawm ka daux krax.
37 Yam hoek dex nawh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ka greem blaong gawng sime,
pui gau khankix hoik yaog yaw Siyiex kheu pa hoik yaox kix ka mhai pa daox pa am in kix khawm uik,
kix kok yo tom ting ah nin:
38 d“Kieh Siyiex tawx buan ka simiang pa hoek ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Ye-sux heue!
Piang rao ma hoik koe pa nhim,
min plak ngai tawx ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong heue!”
39 Ka daux pui hun koe pui Fa-li-saix krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
tawx lox nhie pui gau maix yien yawg tix heue!”
40 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex,
phan kix ang nye,
simaox in kix kawx tag sang kok yo heue.”
Yiiem son Yie-lu-sa-leung
41 Yam sang dex hoek Ye-sux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
nawh yaox ka veng an,
mai yiiem son veng an,
42 ah nin:
“Kieh maix tawng kaing ceu pa dex dawt mai maix ka daux in singaix,
mai ka daux pa nhim,
viang mawh nan,
yam in maix hoik nieb pon yuh tix yuh nan.
43 Bawg yam sang hoek,
pui koeng ka maix yam an tag sang sum tab lai lawm ka maix,
mai giex gang ka maix khawm pon daog.
44 Kix tag sang yuh maix mai pui pa ot ka daux guh veng maix laux lak pak leeing,
mai ang lai kieh simaox ot piang paox tix pie tix lon,
mawh kheu ang maix tawng ka bawg yam pa taoh Siyiex tix heue.”
Kieh nyiex buan singax singeh
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.12–17; Lai Ma-kheu 11.15–19; Lai Yaw-hang 2.13–22)
45 Ye-sux laig ka daux nyiex buan,
mai grheu ka can pui pa coh cai lai gee lih,
46 emai ah nin ka kix:
“Ka daux phuk lai sigang khri hoik tiiem ka gum lox Siyiex ah nin:
‘Nyiex buan aux tag sang kieh mawh nyiex dee rawg buan’,
viang mawh nan,
pex lai kieh lih mawh dee ot pui briex pui lak heue!”
47 fYe-sux ot tix sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan ku singaix.
Simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri,
mai simiang pui ka daux haktiex sang pauh ka nawh,
48 mawh ngie ang tawng grawng sang tawx taix tix.
Mawh kheu hoik khaw rhawm pui hun ka nawh khawm uik,
mai bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa hoik krai nawh ku gum heue.
