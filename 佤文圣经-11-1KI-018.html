Yi-li-yax mai cao jhai Pa-lix khankix
18
Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
sibhawm kroh praix klawng ka daux loe num,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yi-li-yax:
“Maix hu ka simiang Ya-ha,
aux tag sang tawx rawm lhiex lih.”
2 Yi-li-yax tom yaog tix hu heue.
Yam an,
krax sibhawm praix ka daux Sa-ma-li-yax kied sidaing kin;
3 Ya-ha kok ka Eu-pa-tix cao pa kuang kaing nyiex haw hoek.
(Eu-pa-tix hoik mawh pui pa yum rhawm lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex tix kaux.
4 Yam pauh Yie-si-pix ka cao jhai Simiang Siyiex,
Eu-pa-tix hoik taoh ka cao jhai tix yieh kaux khankix,
guah kix lih koe ra cub,
tix cub tix phuan kaux,
mai mox kix ot ka daux gawng,
khaix an tawx kix ih priex preem mai nyaux rawm.)
5 Ya-ha ah nin ka Eu-pa-tix:
“Exa hu sawg ka can dee keet rawm mai dawng ang koe rawm khawm uik ka daux haktiex ex,
sang yaox ang ex yaox ka dee koe rib klai,
son sang eei ka brung mai law;
ex ang siao pauh ka khawng brie in kix.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
kix cu kah,
mai guah tix hu jiang tix yaox ka ku plak heue.
7 Ka daux krax,
Eu-pa-tix si pup ka Yi-li-yax.
Nawh tawng ka Yi-li-yax,
tom krub piang tiex ah nin:
“Maix rub mawh cao simiang aux Yi-li-yax tete lie?”
8 Yi-li-yax ah nin:
“Mawh heue.
Maix hu krai ka simiang,
pa mawh simiang maix,
krai ot aux dee in heue.”
9 Eu-pa-tix ah nin:
“Aux lud kheu pa tix,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tawx aux ka simiang Ya-ha,
kieh nawh pauh ka aux lie?
10 Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix pa im ju leeing ju,
simiang hoik sawg maix ka meung piang haktiex ku dauh.
Phan simiang meung an krai ang maix ot ka daux meung nawh,
Ya-ha tag sang krai lox pa tiex tu grawng ang maix ot dee an tete.
11 Yam in maix tieh tix nhie aux hu krai ka nawh,
grawng ot maix dee in lie?
12 Phan khuan Simiang Siyiex viex maix hu ka dee glip tix dauh dauh khaix hoik lih hu aux khaing maix,
aux hu krai ot maix dee in ka Ya-ha,
nawh ang yaox ka maix,
nawh rien tix pauh aux yum.
Jauh tom yam iak aux hoik mawh pui pa yum rhawm lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex.
13 Khex ang maix baox tawng,
yam pauh Yie-si-pix ka cao jhai Simiang Siyiex,
aux hoik mox ka cao jhai tix yieh kaux ot daux gawng lie?
Aux guah kix lih koe ra cub mai mox ka kix,
tix cub tix phuan kaux,
tawx kix nyaux rawm mai ih priex preem.
14 Yam in maix yuh ka mawx lai nhie aux hu krai ka simiang,
krai ot maix dee in lie?
Nawh rien tix pauh ka aux heue!”
15 Yi-li-yax ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa krub aux,
pa mawh simiang naing khawm bee ma,
ka daux in singaix,
aux tag sang kieh simiang yaox tix heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Eu-pa-tix hu krai ka simiang Ya-ha dee an,
Ya-ha tom hu ka Yi-li-yax.
17 Yam yaox Ya-ha ka Yi-li-yax,
nawh tom ah nin:
“Khex mawh ot maix dee in,
maix pa kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yaox krax pa laux leeing in heue!”
18 Yi-li-yax ah nin:
“Aux ang bax kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yaox krax pa laux leeing;
maix mai pui nyiex keeing maix pa kieh kix yaox khaing!
Maix ang nghiet ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt ka Pa-lix.
19 Yam in,
maix hu kok ka can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoek yaox aux ka gawng Cia-mix,
khaix an mai viex ka cao jhai Pa-lix pa eei Yie-si-pix miex simiang pon yieh tix phuan kaux,
mai cao jhai bun siyiex Ya-seu-la pon yieh kaux hoek tau heue.”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-ha kok can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik mai cao jhai Pa-lix khankix hoek ka gawng Cia-mix.
21 Yi-li-yax hoek dex sivoe ngai,
mai ah nin ka kawn pui:
“Pex tag sang pon doh keud ka grawng in yam mawx lie?
Phan Simiang Siyiex rub mawh Siyiex tete,
kieh ex krub ka nawh,
phan Pa-lix rub mawh Siyiex tete,
kieh ex hu krub ka nawh heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
kawn pui ang nye tix kaux kawx.
22 Yi-li-yax baox ah nin:
“Cao jhai Simiang Siyiex deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing,
viang mawh nan,
cao jhai Pa-lix koe pon yieh tix phuan kaux.
23 Pex viex moi mhaing hoek ra mu,
tix mu tawx ka cao jhai Pa-lix,
nhie kix pauh moi an,
sit lih mawh khik,
deuh ot piang nawm khix,
paw vi cux ngu kah;
an tix mu tawx ka aux,
aux kawx sang hawt tix yuh mawh nan.
24 Khaix an,
kieh cao jhai Pa-lix rawg buan ka siyiex kix,
aux kawx sang rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex aux;
mai ngu pa lih krax rawg buan an tag mawh Siyiex heue.”
Kawn pui hoik cu ka khawm uik.
25 Yi-li-yax ah nin ka cao jhai Pa-lix:
“Mawh kheu hun pui pex,
pex ran viex moi tix mu hu khre ka heue!
Rawg buan ka siyiex pex,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix cux ka ngu heue.”
26 Kix hoik rien khre ka moi pa viex tix tawx ka kix,
kix tom rawg buan ka Pa-lix,
mai rawg tom hoek greung ngaix.
Kix kok yo lox ah nin:
“Pa-lix eue,
nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw yix liak!”
Kix ca nied tix graoh hawt vung viang khu kix,
viang mawh nan,
ceu pa tid tix ang lih tix hoek heue.
27 Yam hoek greung ngaix,
Yi-li-yax nyiah kix ah nin:
“Rawg buan ah lox pex tom ting heue!
Nawh mawh Siyiex!
Nawh tee kawn keud rhawm rhi tix;
phan ang mawh nan tee kawn hu neem aing tix;
phan ang mawh nan tee kawn hu tang dauh tang dee.
Phan ang mawh nan,
nawh tee kawn laig it,
pex caw tix kok yo nawh heue!”
28 Cao jhai an kix tom nie rawg buan tom ting lox tix,
mai hawt tix yuh awm been krax pa yuh kix,
teei gawn mai lawm pliah pat ka to kaux tix mat,
tom hoek buing lih nham.
29 Kix ca nied tix kok yo,
tom hoek bawg yam yuh krax tan plak pon bo,
mai ca kawn ang bax mhong tid tix,
ang nghiet ka lox kix,
lox luah pie ceeix khaing pon ang koe heue.
30 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-yax ah nin ka pui haoh pui hun:
“Hoek ka dee ot aux dee in khaing!”
Kix tom vung viang ka praok nawh.
Nawh ran cox mhawm ka khu dee tan Simiang Siyiex pa hoik yieh tix laux.
31 bNawh teei ka simaox kao ra lon,
tix lon simaox tang tix ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
ceu kaux pui in kix hoik hawt ka ceu kaux kawn simex Ya-keux kao ra kaux.
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx ceu kaux ka Ya-keux mawh Yi-seu-liex.
32 Yi-li-yax teei simaox in kix,
cox baox yuh khu dee tan pa krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai kaong daux rawng vung viang khu an,
daux rawng an chix tix sau simie ot daux been kao pon kongsheng.
33 Khaix an,
nawh sau khix ot ka piang khu,
mai sit moi lih mawh khik,
deuh ot piang khix.
Nawh tawx lox ah nin:
“Kin rawm hoek pon pung,
hieh ka piang nex tan an mai ka piang khix.”
Kix hawt tix yuh.
34 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Baox yuh tix bawg.”
Kix baox hawt tix yuh.
Nawh baox ah nin:
“Baox yuh tix bawg.”
Kix baox hawt tix yuh.
35 Rawm hoik jauh vung viang khu dee tan,
ka daux rawng hoik naog ka rawm khawm uik heue.
36 Yam tan krax tan plak pon ngaix,
cao jhai Yi-li-yax hu ka sivoe khu dee tan,
mai rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux eue,
khiox khaw maix jhi grawng mawh maix Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux mawh siliex maix,
can pa yuh aux hoik hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawm maix.
37 Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox aux!
Simiang Siyiex eue,
nghiet ka lox aux liak,
son sang kieh pui haoh hun in kix tawng ka maix,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex,
son sang kieh kix deeh loh rhawm loh rhi tix heue.”
38 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ploi ngu lih gleeing ka nex krax tan,
khix,
mai gleeing laux khawm simaox mai ka piang tiex,
mai gleeing ka rawm pa koe daux rawng an kroh.
39 Pui haoh pui hun yaox kah,
mai gup tix krub ka piang tiex,
kok yo lox tom ting ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex tete;
mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing heue!”
40 Yi-li-yax tawx lox ah nin:
“Giex ka cao jhai Pa-lix,
tix kaux kawx paw kieh kix yaox tix luat luan!”
Pui haoh pui hun tom giex ka kix ku kaux.
Yi-li-yax viex kix hoek ka praok rawm klawng Ci-suing,
mai pauh kix yum dee an heue.
Krax pa laux leeing kroh praix klawng hoik uik
41 Yi-li-yax ah nin ka simiang Ya-ha:
“Yam in maix chix tix hu ih ka ceu priex preem.
Aux hoik mhong ka lox luah rawm lhiex heue.”
42 cYam hu ih Ya-ha ka ceu priex preem,
Yi-li-yax haok ka choek gawng Cia-mix,
mai grux ngawng tix piang tiex,
nawh num kaing tix ot si nax deem tix.
43 Nawh ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Maix hu,
mai veeh ngai maix jhak ka rawm sigrit sigra plak an heue.”
Siliex tom hu,
mai deeh hoek ah nin:
“Aux ang bax yaox ka ceu pa tid tix.”
Yi-li-yax hoik kok nawh hu jhak aliah bawg khawm uik.
44 Ka daux aliah bawg siliex hoek mai ah nin:
“Aux yaox ka lon pai awm iak tix lon,
pa ang bax koe pie guah tiag taix pui,
hoek khaing piang rawm sigrit sigra plak an heue.”
Yi-li-yax tawx lox ka siliex tix ah nin:
“Hu ka simiang Ya-ha dee an,
mai krai ka nawh,
nhie nawh nied tix brug ling law ing,
phan ang mawh nan,
lhiex tag ang chix kieh nawh yaox tix ing heue.”
45 Ang rheeng,
pai awm rug ceep ka rao ma,
bhaung kawx phlu tix hoek,
lhiex ting lih hoek.
Ya-ha sau tix brug ling law,
deeh ing ka Yie-see-liex.
46 Siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang Yi-li-yax,
nawh tom nhawt ka kraung ceep tix kaux,
mai to sivoe Ya-ha,
tom hoek ka Yie-see-liex heue.
