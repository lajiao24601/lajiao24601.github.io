Krax sidiex pa grum mhawm
2
Phan mawh maix,
krax pa sidiex gau maix tag caw tix khaw caw mai been pa grum mhawm.
2 Maix tag caw tix sidiex ka kuad song,
nhie kix chix rien tix,
koe rhawm pa mhawm,
chix priex ka to kaux tix,
tag caw tix koe krax yum rhawm pa grum mhawm,
krax muh krax rhawm,
mai rhawm pa gun yeung.
3 Maix kawx tag caw tix sidiex gau ka bun pa riex ju,
tag caw tix chix rien tix ka kaing pa yuh tix.
Ang pon tix nieb pon yuh ka kaing ceu pa ang khaw caw,
ang pon tix mawh siliex cawng ka plai.
Tag caw tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm pa kieh pui gau.
4 Sidiex gau mhawm mhawm ka bun pa awn ju khankix,
pheuk tix gau kix grawng sang muh ka mex tix mai ka kawn praix tix,
5 grawng sang chix priex ka to kaux tix,
tag caw kieh tix singax,
rien yuh ka kaing nyiex,
koe krax caox rhawm tix teem ka pui,
nghiet ka mex tix,
son ang pui sang map nai ka gum lox Siyiex heue.
6 Awm an,
maix kawx tag caw tix sidiex gau ka pui pa nhum ju,
nhie kix priex mhawm ka to kaux tix.
7 Maix lie,
ka daux kaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
tag caw tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
kieh tix mawh pa mhawm pa gau pui tix hawt.
Phan sidiex ka pui tag caw tix kieh mawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
ang mawh ka daux pa klieh,
8 lox si kah tag caw tix kieh khaw caw,
paw kieh pui koe lox pa ne ka tix.
Yuh nin,
pui pa tieb tie ka maix khankix,
tag sang chix kaig tua,
mai ang koe lox pa sang krai kheu ang tix yaox dee lud ex heue.
9 Maix tag sidiex gau ka pa mawh siliex cawng khankix,
nhie kix nghiet ka simiang tix,
yuh krax pa khaw rhawm simiang tix ka daux kaing ku ceu,
ang pon tix lawg ka kix.
10 Ang pon tix briex lak,
tag caw kieh tix kaux sijax ka daux kaing ku ceu,
son sang kieh kaing ceu pa hoik yuh tix khawm uik,
kieh been cie Siyiex cao taoh ex nie pon krax pa nab yam yung yi heue.
11 Mawh kheu buan son krax taoh cie Siyiex kawn pui haktiex hoik jhi tix lih.
12 Buan son in hoik pheuk tix gau ex tik vud ka kaing pa ang koe krax nab yam yung yi mai pa dex dawt mai alo nex nham,
ot ka piang haktiex chix priex ka rhawm tix,
yuh pa rauh pa see,
mai krax pa nab yam yung yi.
13 Krox jun pa sang lih hoek singaix pa pon buan son pa keud pot jun pot ex an.
Bawg yam an,
Siyiex pa ting min ka piang khawm uik,
mai pa lih hoek min plak ngai cao taoh ex Ye-sux Khrita.
14 bCao taoh hoik tan to kaux tix son ex,
taoh ex luat luan khaing pa caox pa caig khawm uik,
kieh ex lih mawh pui pa singax cie tix,
mawh pui cie nawh,
gaux rhawm tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm heue.
15 Maix tag caw tix krai lao ka kaing in kix,
siao ka siawp sibrawm pa koe maix hoek tix sidiex gau ka pui,
phan ang mawh nan,
ne raw ka pui.
Paw vi kieh pui map nai ka maix heue.
