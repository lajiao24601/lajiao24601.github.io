22
Pao-law ah nin:
“Tax yiex miex keeing pux aik pux o khankix eue,
viex pex nghiet lox pa krai lao aux son tix.”
2 Yam mhong kix siao Pao-law lox Si-paw-lai si kah ka kix,
kix tom nie yien yawg tix.
Pao-law nied tix krai ah nin:
3 a“Aux mawh pui Yo-thaix,
keh ka daux veng Ta-sux haktiex Ci-li-cia,
mai ting ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
gau lai ka greem Cia-ma-liex,
hoik gau tix chix ka lox tat jhawm prawng tax du khawm uik,
mai gaux rhawm tix yuh kaing jhaw Siyiex,
awm pex pui pa ot dee in ku kaux yuh nan.
4 bAux hoik sax tuk tiak tuk te ka pui pa hawt ka daux krax in ka singaix pa hoik luan,
mai hoik pauh kix yum.
Aux kawx hoik giex ka kawn Khrit pa mawh simex bun,
mai laung kix daux thawng.
5 Cao giex kaing li pa ting mai pui ka daux bang kum hoik kham sakse grawng pa mawh lox krai aux tete.
Aux hoik pon ka lai pa tawx ka paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka haktiex Ta-ma-see-keux,
mawh kheu nan aux tom hoek dee an,
son sang giex ka pui khankix,
mai phrawk ka kix,
viex ing prawng ca kix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.”
Pao-law krai lao ka grawng pa yum rhawm tix ka Simiang Ye-sux
(Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 9.1–19; 26.12–18)
6 “Yam lawng aux krax dex sang hoek ka Ta-ma-see-keux,
bawg yam greung ngaix,
dum mawx dum an koe pa riang tix mu khaing rao ma jhan ka sivie siveng aux.
7 Aux goh piang tiex,
mhong lox pa ah nin ka aux:
‘Sao-law,
Sao-law!
Yuh ka mawx maix lai tuk tiak ka aux lie?’
8 Aux tom chawk ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
mawh maix mawx?’
Nawh ah nin:
‘Aux mawh Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux pa hoik tuk tiak maix heue.’
9 Pui pa hu tau mai aux yaox ka pa riang an,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang mhong lox pa si kah ka aux an.
10 Aux baox chawk ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
sang khaw aux tix yuh ka mawx?’
Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
‘Kaoh,
laig ka daux veng Ta-ma-see-keux,
dee an,
koe pui tag sang krai ka kaing ceu pa sang kieh Siyiex maix yuh khawm uik heue.’
11 Mawh kheu pa riang ting an,
ngai aux ang lai chix yaox,
pui pa hu tau mai aux tom tuk taix aux,
viex aux laig ka daux veng Ta-ma-see-keux.
12 “Ka dee an koe pui tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-na-ni-yax,
nawh mawh pui pa yum rhawm tix kaux,
pa chix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
nawh hoik pon krax pa nab yam yung yi can pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka daux Ta-ma-see-keux khawm uik.
13 Nawh hoek tix jhak ka aux,
mai cung ka praok aux ah nin:
‘Pux aik Sao-law eue,
kieh ngai maix deeh chix yaox heue!’
Bawg yam an,
ngai aux deeh chix yaox,
aux tom yaox ka nawh.
14 Nawh ah nin:
‘Siyiex tax du yix hoik laug ka maix,
mai kieh maix tawng ka alo nawh,
pon tix yaox ka siliex pa rauh pa see nawh,
mai baox mhong ka lox pa lih khaing daux jheeh nawh.
15 Mawh kheu sang kham maix sakse son nawh,
krai kawn pui piang haktiex mhong ka ceu pa hoik yaox maix,
pa hoik mhong maix.
16 Phan mawh nan,
maix kawn sang gla ka ceu pa tix lie?
Kaoh,
khiox khaw ka ceu kaux nawh,
mai rieb tix cub com,
son sang singax phi map maix heue!’ ”
Kok Pao-law sidiex ka pui tang ceu tang cad
17 “Khaix an,
aux deeh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
yam rawg aux buan ka daux nyiex buan aux pon tix yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu,
18 mai aux yaox Simiang Ye-sux ka daux riang su sai an.
Nawh ah nin ka aux:
‘Nied lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu pui dee in ang rieb ka sakse pa kham maix son aux heue.’
19 Aux ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
kix hoik tawng aux hoek ka nyiex sidiex ku dauh,
kix giex tix lauh ka pui pa yum rhawm ka maix.
20 cYam pauh kix ka See-thi-fang pui pa kham sakse maix,
aux kawx ot dee an,
mai hoik cu kix yuh nan ka nawh,
khaix an kawn priex ka kraung ceep son pui cau nyhau pa pauh ka nawh khankix.’
21 Simiang Ye-sux krai ka aux ah nin:
‘Hu heue!
Aux sang ploi maix hu dee singai,
hu ka pui tang ceu tang cad dee an.’ ”
22 Pui hun mhong lox pa krai lao Pao-law;
yam mhong kix ka gum lox in,
kix kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Pauh ka nawh heue!
Pauh ka nawh heue!
Khaw tix pauh nawh yum heue!”
23 Kix riag yo nang,
peeik tik ka kraung ceep tix nang,
mai rhuat phawng tiex.
24 Simiang lien Law-ma tawx lox nhie kix viex Pao-law laig daux nyiex bang,
tawx lox nhie lien si vied ka nawh,
chawk cheng ka nawh,
si kaw tix yaox yuh ka mawx pui hun lai kok ka nawh yuh nin.
25 Yam phrawk kix tix sang lauh ka nawh,
Pao-law cung ka praok pui naing tix kaux ah nin:
“Pex lai yuh nin ka pui Law-ma,
tawx taix tix si vied sivoe sang prawng kah,
mawh khaw lie?”
26 Pui naing an mhong gum lox in,
nawh tom hu ka simiang naing,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Sang yuh maix ka mawx?
Pui an mawh pui Law-ma heue!”
27 Simiang naing an hoek ka Pao-law,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Krai ka aux,
mawh maix pui Law-ma lie?”
Pao-law pawk ah nin:
“Mawh heue.”
28 Simiang naing an ah nin:
“Aux teei mau tom nie,
tom taug pon tix mawh pui Law-ma.”
Pao-law ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh tom yam keh tix heue.”
29 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa chawk cheng Pao-law khankix tang yoi tix hu.
Simiang naing kawx hoik si yaung si kua;
mawh kheu hoik tawng kix Pao-law mawh pui Law-ma,
mai hoik phrawk ka nawh heue.
Pao-law krai lox ka daux bang kum
30 Simiang naing an sang kieh tix tawng grawng pa deuh pui Yo-thaix phi map piang Pao-law.
Mawh kheu nan ka daux ngaix khaix,
nawh yah maox ceng khaing Pao-law,
mai khrawm simiang cao giex kaing li mai pui ka daux bang kum khankix rom tix tau,
khaix an viex Pao-law lih,
kieh nawh cung ka plak sivoe ngai kix heue.
