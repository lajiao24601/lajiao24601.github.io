Ne raw ka pui pa koe mau khri
5
Phan mawh nan,
pex pa mawh pui pa koe mau nghiet ka lox krai aux.
Pex tag caw tix yiiem grai son krax kha rhawm pa tag sang lih hoek an heue!
2 aSin ngao pex hoik grai,
kraung ceep pex greeing hoik lhawn.
3 Khri mau pex rhaw hoik ih;
rhaw in tag sang mawh sakse pa kham tix deuh phi map piang pex,
mai baox awm ngu pa bleet ka to kaux pex yuh nan.
Yam si dauh haktiex in,
pex tawng ngie tix khrawm tix deuh ka sin ngao khaing.
4 bPex gut kheem ka pui pa yuh kaing son pex daux kaung ma khankix,
mai tieh ka mau ngawh riang kix,
kix hoik deuh phi map piang pex heue!
Lox pa kok yo pui yuh kaing khrawm nghox khankix hoik laig ka daux yhaok Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik.
5 Pex yuh krax gaux rhawm ka piang haktiex,
kieh to kaux tix kluing mu,
krox jun ka daux singaix bawg yam pa pauh.
6 Pex prawng ca ka pui pa ang koe dee lud,
mai pauh kix yum;
kix ang bax tieb tie ka pex heue. c
Krax pa gun yeung mai pa rawg buan
7 Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix gun yeung,
mai krox jun Simiang Siyiex deeh hoek.
Jhak heue,
pui pa yuh kaing haktiex viex rhawm gun rhawm yeung krox jun ka to ma pa kied ngawh pa koe daux kaung,
viex rhawm gun rhawm yeung krox jun ka rawm lhiex cao.
8 Pex kawx tag caw tix viex rhawm gun rhawm yeung.
Pex tag caw tix koe rhawm pa kaux cong ka daux krax keud pot jun pot,
mawh kheu dex sang hoek singaix bawg yam cie Simiang Siyiex heue.
9 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
paw lai salao ka paox tix,
lhat pex chix pon krax prawng ca Siyiex.
Cao Simiang Ye-sux pa prawng ca tag sang dex hoek,
hoik ot ka diang siviex heue.
10 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
tag sang yuh tix tawng cao jhai pa een rhawm tix si kah daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
tag caw tix gau krax pon kham kix krax kha rhawm mai krax pon gun yeung.
11 Mawh kheu pon gun kix tom si dauh,
ex keud kix mawh pa kied koe buan son tete.
Pex hoik sax mhong krax pa pon kham Yaw-paw,
hoik tawng nang krax pa kia ngien Simiang Siyiex nawh khaix an,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik naog krax caox rhawm mai krax muh heue.
12 dPux aik pux o aux khankix eue,
ceu pa blawn ting ceu mawh:
ang pon tix tiex tu,
ang pon tix tiex tu ka rao ma mai haktiex,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka ceu tid tix tix ceu ceu.
Phan mawh krai mawh;
phan ang mawh krai ang mawh.
Yuh nin,
pex tom ang yaox tix kham krax prawng ca Siyiex heue.
13 Ka daux pex koe pui pa si pup krax pa kha rhawm lie?
Nawh tag caw tix rawg buan;
phan koe krax gaux rhawm lie?
Nawh tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw;
14 ephan koe pui pa saux ngia lie?
Nawh tag caw tix kok kuad song ceu cao hoek tix rawg buan son nawh,
mai kraix rawm bee ka nawh ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex.
15 Lox rawg buan in phan mawh pa kaoh khaing krax yum rhawm,
tag sang pon pai ka pui saux moi;
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh nawh pon krax pa brax kua,
phi map pa yuh pui pa koe jhawm saux an tag sang pon krax pa pluat ploi.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix ra lai krai doh ka phi map tix,
ra lai rawg buan ka son paox tix,
mai kieh pex pon krax pa pai moi.
Lox rawg buan pui pa rauh see kied sidaing koe riang.
17 fYi-li-yax hoik mawh pui awm ex yuh nan nang;
nawh sau riang tix rawg buan,
khiox khaw Siyiex paw vi tawx lhiex lih,
lhiex rug ang lih been loe num plak.
18 gNawh baox deeh rawg buan,
rao ma tom tag tawx lhiex lih,
haktiex tom tag lih kuah nghox koi heue.
19 Pux aik pux o aux khankix eue,
ka daux pex phan koe pui pa lud krax,
mai lih hu khaing krax pa mawh pa meh,
koe pui tix kaux kaux sawg nawh deeh hoek,
20 hpex tag caw tix tawng yuh nin:
an pa kieh pui phi map pa lud krax deeh hoek,
tag sang pon taoh ka khuan ju pui phi map an khaing daux krax yum.
Kieh phi map tom nie pon krax pa pluat ploi heue.
