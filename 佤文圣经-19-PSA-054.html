Khiox khaw krax pa kia ngiena
54
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh aux ka siawp sibrawm maix;
khiox khaw maix teei pa ting riang ting rao tix prawng rauh see son aux.
2 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox aux.
3 Pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi hoek tix puing ka aux;
pui pa tud pa rhim keud tix sang yuh aux laux leeing;
ka daux ngai kix ang koe Siyiex heue.
 
4 Aux tawng Siyiex mawh krax teem krax coi ka aux;
Simiang Siyiex mawh cao pa kia ngien ka ju aux.
5 Kieh Siyiex hawt tix pawk bix awm kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui pa koeng ka aux;
nawh tag sang hawt pa kaux sijax tix yuh kix laux leeing heue.
 
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix tan lu krax tan ka maix;
aux tag sang yaog yaw ka maix,
mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem maix.
7 Maix hoik taoh aux luat luan khaing krax kha rhawm kha rhi khawm uik;
aux hoik yaox ka grawng pa laux leeing pui koeng heue.
