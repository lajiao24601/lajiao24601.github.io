Been pa prawng kieh yum
20
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
2 nhie nawh krai ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ah nin:
“Ka daux pex pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu tang cad ang ah,
phan koe pui pa tan kawn simex kawn bun tix ka Siyiex Maw-law pa caox pa caig,
tag sang pauh nawh yum;
pui haoh pui hun tag sang dheem nawh yum ka simaox.
3 Pui mawx ang ah pa tan kawn simex kawn bun tix ka Maw-law,
mai kieh nyiex singax singeh aux seeing brai,
mai kaig tua yuh ceu kaux pa singax singeh aux,
aux tag koeng ka nawh,
mai teei tix tik nawh khaing daux ceu pui cie aux khankix.
4 Viang mawh nan,
phan pui mawx yuh tix ang yaox,
ang pauh pui an yum,
5 aux tag sang koeng ka nawh mai khawm pui daux nyiex nawh khawm uik;
mai khawm can pui pa rom tau mai nawh tix tieh vheen ka aux,
mai hu krub ka Maw-law.
Aux tag sang teei tix tik kix khaing ka daux ceu pui cie aux heue.
6 “Phan koe pui pa tieh vheen ka aux,
kok khuan pui yum,
hu chawk ka cao chix si meed,
aux tag koeng ka nawh,
mai teei tix tik nawh khaing ka daux ceu pui cie aux khankix.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
kieh to kaux pex singax singeh,
tag singax singeh,
kheu mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
8 Pex caw priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
kheu hoik mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa kieh pex singax singeh heue.
9 a“Pui mawx ang ah pa jhax lawg ka miex keeing tix,
tag sang kieh nawh yum;
phi map in tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek ka piang nawh tete.
10 b“Phan koe pui pa phit vao mai moeng paox ceu paox cad pui Yi-seu-liex,
simex pa phit vao mai bun pa phit vao an tag sang kieh yum khawm ra kaux.
11 cPhan koe pui pa phit vao tau mai moeng iak keeing tix tang kaux,
kiex khawm ra kaux tag sang kieh yum,
phi map an tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek piang kiex tete.
12 dPhan koe pui tix kaux pa phit tau mai mun tix,
kiex khawm ra kaux tag sang kieh yum;
kheu mawh kaing pa kaig tua,
phi map an tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek ka piang kiex tete.
13 ePhan koe simex pa it tau mai paox simex tix,
kiex hoik yuh kaing seeing brai pui kah,
tag kieh kiex yum khawm ra kaux tix;
phi map an tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek ka piang kiex.
14 fPhan koe pui pa teei tix koe moeng,
baox teei tix moeng miex moeng tix,
kix khawm loe kaux tag sang gleeing yum ka ngu,
mawh kheu hoik yuh kix kaing seeing brai pui kah;
mai ka daux pex ang pon tix kieh kaing pa yuh nan lih koe.
15 gPhan koe pui pa phit vao tau mai to sat,
pui an khawm to sat tag sang kieh yum.
16 Phan koe bun pa dex dawt mai to sat,
mai phit vao tau mai to sat,
nawh khawm to sat an tag sang kiex yum;
mai phi map an tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek ka piang kiex tete heue.
17 h“Phan koe pui pa teei tix moeng pux o lih tix,
mawh nan pa tang miex ang ah,
mawh pa tang keeing ang ah,
tag caw tix krai lih,
kieh kiex kham krax kaig tua,
mai grheu kiex lih hu khaing ka dee ot kix tan;
kheu hoik yuh nawh kaing pa seeing brai mai pux o tix,
mai phi map an tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek ka piang nawh tete.
18 iPhan koe pui pa it tau mai bun yam kawn lih nham seeing nawh,
kiex khawm ra kaux tag sang grheu lih hu khaing ka dee ot kix tan,
mawh kheu lieh lheu kiex lox tat jhawm prawng pa ang singax khankix heue.
19 j“Phan koe pui pa it tau mai nan keeing tix,
mai pux o miex tix,
kix hoik phit vao yuh kaing pa seeing brai,
mai phi map an tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek ka piang kix tete.
20 Phan koe pui pa yuh pa seeing brai tau mai moeng keeing ting tix,
tau mai moeng keeing iak tix,
hoik mawh pa yuh krax kaig tua ka keeing ting tix,
ka keeing iak tix;
mai kiex khawm ra kaux tag sang tang klawm ka phi map tix,
tag kieh kiex yum ang lai koe bawg yam yaox ka kawn praix tix.
21 kPhan koe pui pa teei tix moeng moeng pux ke tix,
phan ang mawh nan,
moeng moeng pux tix,
hoik mawh pa kaig tua yuh pux aik tix,
kieh mawh pa ang singax;
tag kieh kiex ang lai koe bawg yam yaox ka kawn praix tix tom hoek yum tix heue.
22 “Pex priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux mai lox been pa deuh aux,
son ang pex sang yaox krax pa tieb tie ka daux haktiex Cia-nang an pa sang viex aux pex laig ot ka daux.
23 Pex ang pon tix hawt grawng lawng khaix been pui haktiex pa koe sivoe an.
Aux sang grheu pui tang ceu khankix lih hu,
son sang yaox pex tix laig ka daux.
Grawng pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix hoik kieh aux song rhawm.
24 Aux hoik ap tix tawx pex cie haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia,
mai tawx mawh sin ngao pex;
aux sang tawx ka pex khawm uik.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex;
aux hoik tang guah pex lih khaing pui tang ceu tang meung khankix.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix guah to sat pa singax singeh,
mai to sat pa ang singax singeh sidawng,
ang pon tix ih ka to sat pa ang singax mai sim khankix.
Aux hoik krai pao an kix hoik mawh ceu pa ang singax singeh,
phan ih kah,
tag kieh pex mawh pa ang singax singeh.
26 Pex caw tix singax singeh,
mai mawh cie aux;
kheu mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
mawh pa singax singeh.
Aux hoik tang guah pex khaing pui tang ceu tang meung,
son sang mawh pex pui cie aux heue.
27 “Can simex bun pa hu kok khuan yuh phi,
tawx si pawk chawk jhai khankix tag kieh nawh yum.
Pui haoh pui hun tag sang dheem kix yum ka simaox;
phi map pui an kix tag mawh pa khaw tix hoek ka piang kix tete heue.”
