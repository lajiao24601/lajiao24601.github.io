14
Bun pa kied rien rhawm sum ka nyiex ma;
bun pa daox aw yieh laux ka nyiex ma.
2 Pui pa rauh see lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex;
pui pa yuh kaing pa ang khaw caw map nai ka Simiang Siyiex.
3 Pui pa daox aw ah lox pa ang khaw caw kheu pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tix;
pui pa lhak lhiao chix teei lox kia ngien ka to kaux tix.
4 Phan ang koe khawng brie pa thai kaung thai ma,
sang kaoh nghox koi khaing dee mawx;
phan koe khawng brie pa thai kaung thai ma,
nghox koi tom taug chix haok huan.
5 Pui kaux sijax pa kham mhai sakse krai lox pa mawh pa meh;
pui kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh krai lox pie lox lhien.
6 Pui pa cau nyhau ang pon ka ping nyia cuyi;
pui pa koe krax doh krax riang khiox khaw tix sawg ka krax lhak lhiao mawh pa yie.
7 Tag caw tix to hu khaing pui pa daox aw,
mawh kheu ang kix koe ngai lig ngai lai ka pa sang sidiex.
8 Ping nyia cuyi pui pa lhak lhiao pon kieh nawh yaox krax pa khaw nawh tix lawng;
pui pa daox aw deeh pie lhien ka to kaux tix.
9 Pui pa daox aw yuh phi map mai ang cu tix loh rhawm rhi tix;
pui rauh pui see khiox khaw sawg ka krax pa ra lai chix pluat ploi.
10 Rhawm rhi pa taug pa mao,
pui tang kaux ang pon klawm;
rhawm rhi krax gaux rhawm,
pui tang kaux ang pon yaox tix pon.
11 Nyiex ma pui pa caox pa caig tag sang ghrup;
nyiex ma pui pa rauh pa see tag sang kaux cong.
12 aKoe krax tix blah phan jhak mawh pa rauh see,
viang mawh nan,
hoik mawh pa thung hu krax pa yum.
13 Krax nyiah krax gaux rhawm tee chix tawb kheem ka krax laux rhawm laux rhi;
khaix hoik luan krax gaux rhawm,
krax laux rhawm laux rhi ca kawn koe.
14 Pui caox tang deeh kieh kaing pa caox hoek piang to kaux tix;
kaing mhawm pa yuh pui rauh pui see tag deeh pawk hoek ka piang nawh.
15 Pui pa daox aw yum rhawm ka kaing ku ceu;
pui pa lhak lhiao chix rien chix khre tix ku diang caong.
16 Pui pa lhak lhiao mox tix khaing krax laux krax leeing;
pui pa daox aw viex rhawm cau nyhau tix yuh deeh hoek piang tix.
17 Pui pa dawt rhawm yuh kaing pa daox aw;
pui pa koe krax doh rhawm doh rhi pon viex rhawm gun rhawm yeungb.
18 Pui pa ang koe krax lhak lhiao,
tang deeh klawm ka pa caox pa caig pa yuh kheu daox tix;
pui pa koe krax doh krax riang teei ngai lig ngai lai mawh mhok khri.
19 Pui ang mhawm grux gawng tix krub ka plak sivoe ngai pui rauh pui see;
pui pa caox pa caig caung tix ih ka plak sivoe ngai pui rauh pui see.
20 Pui hot pui sibhawm,
pui haoh pui hun ang rhawm yaox;
Pui pa koe mau koe khri,
haoh paox meed paox tawng.
21 Pa caox rhawm ka pui hot pui sibhawm koe buan son;
Pa map nai ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix koe phi map.
22 Pa yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm tag pon krax pa nab yam yung yi pui;
pa yuh kaing ceu pa caox pa caig hoik lud krax hu tix heue.
23 Pa sau riang tix yuh kaing,
ang yaox tix koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi;
pa krai lox pa ang koe ceu koe cak leeing lang singaix,
tag sang hot sibhawm.
24 Pui pa lhak lhiao teei ping nyia cuyi mawh mhok khri;
pui pa daox aw teei pa daox aw mawh pa yaw rang di.
25 Pa kham mhai sakse pa mawh pa meh tag pon taoh ka ju pui;
pa krai lox pie lox lhien tag laux yuh pui tang kaux khankix.
26 Pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex tag koe dee deuh rhawm rhi tix;
pui daux nyiex ma nawh khankix tag pon krax pa nyawm ot heue.
27 Pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh num dee kaoh ju;
nawh pon kieh pui luat luan khaing daux pa viex hoek krax yum.
28 Min plak ngai simiang ot ka daux pui haoh pui hun;
phan ang koe pui haoh pui hun,
nawh tag ang koe ceu pa tid tix.
29 Pa ang koe krax song rhawm yie yie,
taug sawn nawh mawh pui pa lhak lhiao;
pa hax rhawm hax rhi chix plih ngie krax pa daox aw lih.
30 Yien yawg kieh to kaux tix brax kua;
krax boh rhawm mawh jhawm saux pa ang chix tix pai ka daux si-ang.
31 Pa gut kheem ka pui hot pui sibhawm awm hoik map nai ka cao pa phan pa yuh tix;
pa caox rhawm ka pui hot pui sibhawm hoik mawh kaing nab yam yung yi.
32 Krax tud krax rhim pui pa caox pa caig hoik kieh nawh laux leeing;
pui rauh pui see hoik pon krax kia ngien kheu pa mawh pa meh tixc.
33 Rhawm rhi pui pa koe krax doh krax riang,
hoik koe ka ping nyia cuyi khawm uik;
pui pa daox aw ang tawng ceu pa tid tix.
34 Pa rauh pa see pon kieh meung haok huan ting lhaong;
phi map phi ghram hoik mawh krax kaig krax tua ka piang pui ku ceu.
35 Siliex pui pa lhak lhiao hoik pon buan son pa tawx simiang;
kawn cau simiang pa teei tix tik lih,
tag si pup ka krax ciyang heue.
