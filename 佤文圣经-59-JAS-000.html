Lai Ya-keux
Krai dawt
Lai Ya-keux khrawm tix sawg ka daux krax pa duk thung tete khankix, cao pa rieb ka lox lai mawh “Pui cie Siyiex pa pu prang tix ot ka piang haktiex ku dauh”. Cao tiiem teei lox pa nyawm tix nghiet mai lox pa tiag, krai lox sidiex mai krax pa duk thung ka rhawm rhi mai ka kaing yuh pa caw kawn Khrit tix koe. Nawh krai lao grawng krax pa mag mi, pa nyaw glaw, pa yuh kaing pa mhawm, koe rhawm pa ang khaw, krax yum rhawm mai kaing pa yuh pui, lox krai pui, ping nyia cuyi, pa simah simaing, pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi mai pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi, pa prawng ca ka pui tang kaux, pa yaw rang di tix, pa gun yeung, pa rawg buan khankix kaing ceu pa ang awm paox khaing daux kawn Khrit tom nie heue.
Phuk lai in krai sidawng ka kaing pa yuh pui, mai krax yum rhawm ra ceu pa kied ting ceu awm paox ka daux krax pa yum rhawm ka Khrit heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1)
2. Krax yum rhawm mai ping nyia cuyi (1.2–8)
3. Pa hot sibhawm mai pa mi koe (1.9–11)
4. Pa cheuk cheu mai pa nyaw glaw (1.12–18)
5. Pa nghiet mai pa hawt tix yuh (1.19–27)
6. Pa ne raw mai pa ang pon tix koe rhawm pa ang khaw (2.1–13)
7. Krax yum rhawm mai kaing pa yuh (2.14–26)
8. Pa rien khre ka dak tix (3.1–18)
9. Kawn Khrit mai haktiex (4.1–5.6)
10. Krax pa sidiex tang ceu (5.7–20)
