Kawn Khrit pon krax pa bheeik luan
5
Son sang kieh ex pon krax pa bheeik luan,
Khrit hoik pluat ploi ka ex.
Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw kieh tix kiien geeik,
paw vi lai baox kieh khaox glawm siliex cawng giex gang ka pex heue.
2 Aux pa mawh Pao-law krai ka pex sidawng,
phan pex rieb ka been pa Pat-hak,
Khrit tag ang koe ceu ka pex pie ceeix kawx.
3 Yam in aux baox ne raw ka pex,
pui mawh ang ah pa rieb ka been pa Pat-hak,
tag caw tix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng khawm uik.
4 Pui pa yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
kheu pa een rhawm tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
tag mawh pa ang yuh paox pie tau mai Khrit,
hoik tang deeh klat ka buan son Siyiex.
5 Phan mawh ceu pa simie ex,
hoik mawh Siyiex pa kieh ex yuh paox pie tau mai nawh ka daux pa khaw caw;
in hoik mawh riang rao Khuan Singax Singeh pa yuh lih ka daux krax yum rhawm ex.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
yam ot ex ka daux ju Ye-sux Khrit,
been pa Pat-hak mai pa ang Pat-hak ang sawn mawh tid tix,
mawh ngie krax muh krax yum rhawm pa yuh lih,
tom tag sawn tix mawh ceu pa ting ceu heue.
7 Pa yuh pex hoik dawg mawh pa mhawm heue!
Mawh pui mawx pa tieb tie ka pex,
kieh pex ang lai baox nghiet ka ceu pa mawh pa meh lie?
8 Krax sidiex pa nyaw glaw in ang doh mawh pa kok Siyiex ka pex.
9 aLox been prim ah nin:
“Su saix ceeix ceeix khaing chix kieh khao mun khawm lua lih tix ploh.”
10 Viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn yum rhawm grawng pex;
mawh kheu hoik ot ex tau ka daux ju Simiang Siyiex,
aux hoik yum rhawm grawng ang pex koe lox pa ang khaw tau mai aux.
Mawh ngie pa tuk tiak ka pex,
mawh pui mawx ang ah,
tag sang kham krax ciyang Siyiex heue.
11 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
phan aux ca nied tix sidiex ka been pa Pat-hak,
yuh ka mawx aux lai kawn kham krax pa tuk tiak lie?
Phan aux yuh nin,
viang aux hoik sidiex ka khaox lhak Khrit kawx,
mai ang lih tix mawh krax pa tieb tie ka kix.
12 Aux tieh tix simie pui pa tuk tiak ka pex pat ka to kaux tix heue!
13 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
Siyiex hoik laug ka pex,
mai sang kieh pex lih mawh pui pa bheeik luan.
Mawh ngie ang pon tix teei krax pa bheeik luan kieh mawh pa duh son sang hawt ka alo nex nham,
tag caw tix ra lai yuh kaing jhaw ka daux krax muh krax rhawm.
14 bMawh kheu lox tat jhawm prawng hoik rom tix “muh pui awm muh ka to kaux tix” ka daux gum lox tawx in.
15 Tag caw rien rhawm tix heue,
phan pex awm to sat pa ra lai kiied bleet ka paox tix yuh nan,
pex tag sang rom tix laux leeing khawm uik heue.
Khuan Singax Singeh mai rhawm rhi kawn pui
16 Ceu pa sang krai aux mawh:
Lox ah si kah krai pex tag caw tix hawt ka krax pa duk thung Khuan Singax Singeh,
paw vi sawg krax pa naog rhawm alo nex nham to kaux tix.
17 cKheu alo nex nham mawh pa yuh krax pa tieb tie ka Khuan Singax Singeh,
mai krax pa ang khaw,
kieh pex ang pon yuh ka ceu pa simie pex tix yuh khankix.
18 Viang mawh nan,
phan Khuan Singax Singeh duk thung ka pex,
pex tag ang ot ka daux pa giex gang lox tat jhawm prawng heue.
19 Alo nex nham kawn pui mawh pa yie jhi tix lih.
Chix jhi lih ka krax pa phit vao,
pa seeing brai,
pa caox caig,
20 pa krub riang rao,
pa yuh si meed sat,
pa pawk bix,
pa lauh paox,
pa koeng khoi,
pa song rhawm,
pa brax yuh simah,
pa guah cub caw,
pa guah ceu cad,
21 pa boh rhawm,
pa yoek ka plai,
pa si lao daux poi,
mai kaing tang ceu pa awm in kix.
Sivoe an aux hoik ne raw ka pex,
yam in baox ne raw ka pex:
pui pa yuh kaing ceu in kix,
tag ang pon tix lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex heue.
22 Phan mawh plix Khuan Singax Singeh mawh:
krax muh krax rhawm,
krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
pa nhim pa tiiem,
pa gun pa yeung,
krax pa caox rhawm,
pa mhawm rhawm rhi,
pa kaux sijax,
23 pa awn rhawm awn rhi,
pa chix priex rhawm tix.
Ceu in kix hoik mawh pa ang lox tat jhawm prawng tix ceu kawx chix pon tieb tie kah.
24 Pui an kix pa dex dawt tau mai Ye-sux Khrit,
kix hoik tawk rhawm rhi pa caox caig tix ka daux alo nex nham yum ka piang khaox lhak khawm uik.
25 Kheu hoik tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ju ka ex,
ex tag caw tix kieh nawh duk thung krax ot krax koe tix.
26 Ex ang pon tix ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
ang pon tix ra lai song rhawm ka paox tix,
ra lai boh rhawm ka paox tix heue.
