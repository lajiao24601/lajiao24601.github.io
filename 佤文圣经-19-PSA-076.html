Simiang Siyiex cao pa puing pex
76
Siyiex ot ka daux pui Yo-tax,
pui hoik tawng yung ka ku kaux;
ceu kaux nawh ot ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui hoik yung yi ka ku kaux.
2 Nawh koe dee paung ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung;
nawh koe nyiex ot ka gawng Shi-ang.
3 Ka dee an,
nawh hoik raig pot ka te pa puing pui koeng,
hoik yuh laux ka dieb tie naok kix,
vaig gawn kix,
mai kraung naing kix ku ceu heue.
 
4 Siyiex eue,
maix kied sidaing koe min plak ngai tete!
Maix hoik puing pex ka pui koeng,
kaoh khaing gawng khankix,
mai kied sidaing koe siawp sibrawm tete!
5 Can kraung khrai pa hoik tud pui naing pa kied kua kix,
hoik teei viex hu khawm uik;
pui naing kix it tom rheeng ang kaoh,
mai ang lai koe riang tix tieb tie.
6 Siyiex Ya-keux eue,
yam ne raw maix ka kix,
brung naing brug kix tag yum khawm uik heue.
 
7 Siyiex eue,
maix mawh pa khaw pui tix lhat graung yung yi kah;
yam song rhawm maix,
pui mawx sang pon cung ka plak sivoe ngai maix lie?
8-9 Maix krai pao krax prawng ca tix khaing rao ma;
yam kaoh cung maix tix krai pao krax prawng ca,
son sang taoh ka pui pa gut kheem pui kah,
haktiex khawm uik hoik lhat graung kah,
mai ang nye ang nyux heue.
 
10 Krax pa song rhawm kawn pui tag sang nie kieh maix pon krax pa yaog yaw;
pui pa deuk khaix hoik tud pui hu tag sang priex tix hawt ka poi cie maix heue.
 
11 Tag caw tix plud tix tawx ka lox pa hoik ap pong maix tix deuh ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix;
meung pa ot sivie siveng maix tag caw tix tawx keen ka maix,
mai lhat graung yung yi ka nawh.
12 Nawh yeen ka krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi kawn simiang;
nawh kieh simiang haktiex lhat graung heue.
