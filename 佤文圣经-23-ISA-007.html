Lox pa krai ka simiang Ya-ha-see
7
aYam mawh Ya-ha-see kawn simex Yaw-thang kawn saux Wu-si-ya simiang pui Yo-tax,
naing lih tix taoh heue.
Li-shuing simiang Shui-li-yax mai Pi-cia simiang pui Yi-seu-liex kawn simex Li-ma-lix rom paox tix tau,
mai puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon puing ka veng in heue.
2 Grawng pa paoh pui naing Shui-li-yax tix laig ka daux haktiex Yi-seu-liex hoek ka daux yhaok simiang pui Yo-tax dee an,
simiang mai pui hun khankix kied lhat graung kah,
mai vuk vied tix awm kak khaox pa phlu bhaung yung yang yuh nan heue.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yi-sai-yax:
“Viex kawn simex maix See-ya-ya-sub hu ka simiang Ya-ha-see;
nawh ot ka dee die dung rawm rawng plak laong,
pa phlat hu plak krax ting hoek ka bang dee si daux man.
4 Maix hu krai ka nawh,
nhie nawh giex rhawm rhi tix kaux cong ka daux pa yien yawg,
paw vi lhat graung si yaung rhawm.
Simiang Li-shuing mai pui naing cie nawh Shui-li-yax,
khawm krax pa song rhawm simiang Pi-cia,
awm ngie nge khix gleeing ra gawng pa chu taox ngu tix lih khaing.
5 Shui-li-yax yuh paox pie tau mai simiang Yi-seu-liex,
mai rug hoik koe krax pa sang tieh vheen tete.
6 Kix simie tix yuh naing ka haktiex Yo-tax,
khin pui Yo-tax hawt ka rhawm kix,
khaix an,
yaog kawn simex Tha-pi-leux mawh simiang heue.
7 “Viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai pao yuh nin:
krax pa tieh vheen kix ang doh pon tix lih mawh.
8 Kheu Ta-ma-see-keux mawh ngie veng ting ka Shui-li-yax khaing;
simiang Li-shuing mawh ngie simiang ka Ta-ma-see-keux khaing;
mai ang tie bax sawn mawh tid tix.
Phan mawh Yi-seu-liex,
hoek ka daux tix gliah phuan num,
pui tag sang pon pex kah,
mai meung haktiex kix tag hoek krax laux leeing.
9 Sa-ma-li-yax mawh ngie veng ting ka Yi-seu-liex khaing;
simiang Pi-cia mawh ngie simiang ka Sa-ma-li-yax khaing;
mai ang tiex bax sawn mawh tid tix heue.
“Phan krax yum rhawm maix ang kaux cong,
maix tag ang pon tix cung cong heue.”
Mhai son Yi-ma-noe-lix
10 Simiang Siyiex baox ah nin ka simiang Ya-ha-see:
11 “Maix khaw tix khiox khaw mhai tix ceu ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix;
mhai an tee rien tix hoek khaing meung yum,
phan ang mawh nan hoek khaing rao ma heue.”
12 Ya-ha-see ah nin:
“Ang,
aux ang khiox khaw ka mhai;
aux ang pon tix nyaw glaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
13 Mawh kheu nan Yi-sai-yax ah nin:
“Kawn praix Ta-wue eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet.
Khex sang kieh pex pui ang rhawm yaox tix,
mai baox kieh Siyiex ang rhawm yaox tix lie?
14 eTo kaux Simiang Siyiex tag sang tawx mhai tix ceu ka pex:
koe bun krih pa sang nghe kawn simex tix kaux,
mai sang kok ceu kaux nawhc mawh ‘Yi-ma-noe-lixd’.
15 Yam hoik hoek bawg pa tawng kawn simex an tix guah ka ceu pa mawh mai ka ceu pa ang mawh,
tik ka ceu pa caox yuh ka ceu pa mhawm,
mai nawh tag sang kieh rawm teeh mai rawm hia mawh priex preem pa ih pa nyaux tix.f
16 Viang mawh nan,
sivoe sang pon guah nawh tix tawng ka ceu pa mhawm mai ceu pa caox,
haktiex meung pa caox an kiex ra kaux tag sang lih tix riem khawm uik heue.
17 “Simiang Siyiex tag sang ploi krax laux krax leeing lih piang maix,
lih piang pui cie maix,
khawm ka piang nyiex haw maix khawm uik;
krax laux krax leeing in,
jauh tom yam guah Yi-seu-liex tix lih khaing Yo-tax,
mai ang nyang sax lih koe;
nawh tag sang kieh Vawng Ya-sux tud ka haktiex meung maix heue.
18 “Singaix yam an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang phlu vhit kok yo pui Ai-cix hoek,
mai kix tag sang awm phung roi pa pu hoek dee singai khaing rawm klawng Ni-law.
Nawh tag sang kok yo pui Ya-sux hoek khaing haktiex ot kix,
awm phung awng yuh nan,
19 pung tix hoek,
hoik naog thox ka daux ngawd,
ka daux gawng,
mai ka daux rang;
kix tag sang kheem ka yeem kat ku yeem,
mai ka dee lung pak rib ku dauh heue.
20 “Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang teei tix siao ka cang mai ghrip hauk tix kaux khaing praok rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
pa mawh Vawng Ya-sux.
Nawh tag sang khlao tix tik ka hauk jheeh pex,
mai khlao ka hauk kaing pex ghle,
mai reeig ka hauk haw pa koe ka gaw kaux pex khawm uik heue.
21 “Yam an,
viang hoik mawh pui pa yuh kaing haktiex kawx,
chix ngie tix deuh ka kawn moi tix mu mai pex ra mu khaing;
22 rawm teeh kix hoik kub tix eei ka nawh.
Can pui pa deuk khaing ka daux pa laux leeing meung bawg in,
tag sang yaox tix ih nyaux ka rawm teeh mai rawm hia ku kaux heue.
23 “Yam an,
phan hoik sum khaox plix siiet tix rheng num,
pa mawh si peem pa mhawm plix pa khaw ngawh jum tix rheng mau haok plak piang,
mai yeem kat tag sang kuah naog kah;
24 pui tag sang viex ak viex te laig tix jaw ka daux.
Yam an,
haktiex ku dauh ku dee hoik mawh yeem kat,
25 mai gawng iak khankix pa hoik sum nghox koi ka sivoe an,
yam in hoik lih tix mawh yeem kat,
mai ang lai baox koe pui pa cu tix hu ka dee an,
mai ca liang moi liang yung khaing heue.”
