Rawg buan son grawng laux leeing meung
74
Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tik vud ka yix rheeng yuh nin lie?
Maix sang song rhawm ka kawn yung cie maix piang pak rib leeing ju lie?
2 Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka pui cie maix pa hoik laug maix yam jauh dix dix,
pa hoik deeh taoh maix hoek,
pa mawh ceu pui cie to kaux maix;
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka gawng Shi-ang pa hoik paung maix ot ka heue.
3 Pui pa koeng ka yix hoik yuh ka nyiex buan laux leeing khawm uik;
khiox khaw maix hoek tix kia ka bang dee nhik dee riem in pa koe bliah beung heue.
 
4 Pui pa koeng ka maix ot ka daux nyiex buan mai gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
kix hoik yaog tix sum ka man chix cie tix.
5 Kix awm pui pa gu khaox khankix,
teei lawm moe gu ka khaox.
6 Can rong khaox pa saik yuh lih mawh riang ka daux nyiex buan,
kix hoik teei lawm moe tawk laux kah.
7 Kix ploi ngu gleeing ka nyiex buan maix;
kix salao ka dee krub pui ka maix.
8 Kix but rhawm tix sang yuh laux lak pak leeing ka yix;
kix hoik gleeing laux ka nyiex pa singax singeh pa koe ka haktiex in khawm uik heue.
 
9 Mhai man chix pa singax singeh yix khawm uik ang lai yaox kah;
ang lai baox koe cao jhai heue;
ang lai koe pui pa tawng krax laux krax leeing in kawn sang lih koe miex glang.
10 Siyiex eue,
pui pa koeng ka yix sang nyiah maix tom hoek yam mawx lie?
Khex sang salao kix ka ceu kaux maix tom hoek leeing ju lie?
11 Yuh ka mawx maix lai ang teem coi ka yix?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai ang simi tix jhak ka yix?
 
12 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex eue,
tom yam jauh dix dix maix hoik mawh simiang yix;
maix hoik taoh yix tom nie bawg.
13 aMaix teei riang rao pa ting tix guah ka rawm sigrit sigra;
maix tawk max ka kaing to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui kah.
14 cMaix hoik lauh phawng ka kaing to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigrab,
hoik guah tix tawx to sat pa koe daux tiagd ih ka nex kix.
15 Maix hoik kieh rawm keet buing tix lih;
maix hoik kieh rawm klawng ting kroh.
16 Maix hoik yuh ka pon singaix pon sawm;
maix hoik kieh khix singaix tang ot dee ot tix.
17 Maix hoik yuh ka mee haktiex;
maix hoik tat tix deuh ka bawg yam raux mai bawg yam rung heue.
 
18 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka nyiah pui koeng ka maix;
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka pa map nai pui pa ang koe krax yung yi ka maix.
19 Paw vi tawx pui cie maix pa ang koe cao teem cao coi ka pui koeng pa cau nyhau;
paw vi piawm ka pui cie maix pa kham krax pa gut pa kheem pui heue.
 
20 Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh maix tau mai yix,
mawh kheu pa faik haktiex in ku dauh ku dee hoik naog ka pa tud pa rhim.
21 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh pui pa gut kheem pui pon krax pa kaig tua;
khiox khaw maix kieh pui hot pa ang koe cao teem cao coi pon tix yaog yaw ka maix.
22 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kaoh tix teem ka pa rauh see to kaux tix;
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka pa nyiah pui pa ang koe krax pa yung yi ka maix.
23 Paw vi piawm ka lox caox ah pui koeng ka maix;
paw vi piawm ka lox riag yo pui koeng ka maix ang jud heue.
