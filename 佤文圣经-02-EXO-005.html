Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hoek ka simiang Ai-cix
5
Khaix an,
Maw-si mai Ya-loeng hu ka simiang Ai-cix,
ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
‘Ploi pui cie aux hu,
son sang hu kix ka daux tiag yuh poi,
mai krub ka aux heue.’ ”
2 Simiang Ai-cix ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex mawh mawx?
Aux nghiet yuh tix ka lox nawh?
Aux yuh ka mawh sang ploi pui Yi-seu-liex hu?
Aux ang tawng ka Simiang Siyiex;
pui Yi-seu-liex kawx aux tag ang ploi lih hu heue.”
3 Maw-si mai Ya-loeng pawk lox:
“Siyiex ceu pui Si-paw-lai hoik lih bleeh ka yix.
Yam in,
maix kieh yix hu been loe ngaix krax,
hoek ka daux tiag mai rawg buan tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix.
Phan ang mawh nan,
nawh tag kieh jhawm saux mai naing hoek ka piang yix heue.”
4 Simiang Ai-cix ah nin ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng:
“In mawh grawng ceu pa tix?
Pex lai sang kieh pui in kix gla kaing yuh tix lie?
Nhie pui siliex cawng in kix nied yuh kaing yuh tix khaing!
5 Kaux pui pex Yi-seu-liex hoik nie khaing pui Ai-cix,
pex lai keud tix sang kieh kix ang yuh kaing lie?”
6 Daux tix ngaix,
Vawng Ai-cix tawx lox tix hu ka cao thung cao bung kaing jhaw Ai-cix khankix,
ah nin:
7 “Ang lai pon tix baox tawx brex nghox son sang yuh pui Yi-seu-liex lon tiex gleeing,
kieh kix tang hu sawg,
8 viang mawh nan,
kieh kix yuh awm diag yuh kix sivoe,
tix lon kawx paw doh kieh yawm.
Mawh kheu nieh kix,
kix tom mhaing tix yuh krax tan krub ka Siyiex tix.
9 Kieh kix nie baox yuh kaing pa cien,
kaing pa nu tom nie,
son ang kix sang koe bawg yam tix sang hu nghiet ka lox pa ang mawh ang meh in kix heue.”
10 Pui cao thung cao bung kaing jhaw Ai-cix khankix hu mai krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Vawng tawx lox ang lai tawx brex nghox ka pex.
11 Nawh krai viang singai miex glang,
pex tang hu sawg,
lon tiex gleeing pie tix lon ang pon tix kieh yawm heue.”
12 Kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hu ka haktiex Ai-cix ku dauh,
son sang sawg ka brex nghox.
13 Cao thung cao bung kaing jhaw khankix khin ka kix,
kieh kix tawx lon tiex gleeing awm been singaix pa hoik luan yam tawx kix ih brex nghox.
14 Cao bung kaing Ai-cix si vied cao pa tawx kix thung kaing pui ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pex ang lai pon tawx lon tiex gleeing grum naog awm diag pa yuh glang ka in kawx lie?”
15 Khaix an,
cao thung kaing hu khiox khaw ka Vawng ah nin:
“Vawng,
maix lai yuh nin ka yix lie?
16 Cao bung kaing ang lai tawx brex nghox ka yix,
mai nhie yix tawx lon tiex gleeing awm singaix pa luan!
Yam in,
yix hoik kham krax pa si vied,
in hoik mawh krax pa lud pex pui Ai-cix heue.”
17 Simiang Ai-cix ah nin:
“Pex kied sidaing nieh,
mai simie tix lu lhia tix,
pex pon mhaing tix hu tan lu krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
18 Nied deeh ing hu yuh kaing!
Yix ang tawx brex nghox ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
pex ca kawn tawx lon tiex gleeing awm diag prim heue.”
19 Cao thung kaing khankix mhong grawng kix ca kawn tawx lon tiex gleeing awm been prim ku ngaix,
mai kix tom tawng krax kha rhawm som nai sang hoek piang kix heue.
20 Kix lih plak praix,
mai yaox Maw-si mai Ya-loeng ot ka plak praix krox ka kix.
21 Kix ah nin ka kiex:
“Simiang Siyiex rien tix hoik tawng ka kaing pa yuh pax;
pax hoik kieh Vawng mai simiang ot greem nawh koeng ka yix,
mai kieh kix koe krax tix sang pauh ka yix.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka pax heue!”
Maw-si krai kha tix rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex
22 Kheu nan,
Maw-si deeh hoek ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
yuh ka mawh maix lai yuh krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi ka piang ceu pui cie maix yuh nin?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai ploi aux hoek tin?
23 Tom yam jauh aux tix hoek ka sivoe ngai Vawng,
krai lox son maix,
nawh nie tawx krax kha rhawm kha rhi ka piang pui cie maix,
maix lai ang taoh teem ka pui cie tix heue!”
