Been Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud
7
Plak greem mawh lox tat jhawm prawng Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud;
in mawh pa kied singax singeh.
2 Ka plak laong khu dee tan,
caw tix pauh ka khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud,
pa mawh dee pauh ka khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
mai phruah nham vung viang khu dee tan khawm uik.
3-4 Khlawp bee khawm uik,
mai num si dax pa kied kluing khawm uik mai bee pa kawb ka piang kraung vaig,
sok lax ra mu,
mai bee pa koe ka piang sok lax,
tawm dee blawn mhawm,
tag deuh ot ka piang khu dee tan khawm uik.
5 Cao giex kaing li tag tan tix gleeing bee an khawm uik ka Simiang Siyiex piang khu dee tan;
in hoik mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud.
6 Can simex daux nyiex cao giex kaing li khankix ku kaux,
tag chix tix ih nex krax tan ceu in,
viang mawh nan,
caw tix ih daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
kheu in hoik mawh pa kied singax singeh heue.
7 Koe lox tat jhawm prawng tix ceu ceu mawh pa rom tix yuh tau ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud pa yuh nin:
nex pa tan caw mawh cie cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
8 Hak khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-gleeing an caw mawh cie cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krax tan.
9 Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mawh pa hoik ping sin ka daux glah ngu ang ah,
mawh pa hoik ngaik sin ka daux pan ang ah,
phan ang mawh nan,
pa hoik diag sin ka daux aw ang ah,
tag mawh cie cao giex kaing li pa yuh kaing krax tan.
10 Viang mawh nan,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi pa ang nyang sin,
mawh pa hoik klao tix ka bee ang ah,
mawh pa kroh ang ah,
tag sang tawx mawh cie doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li khankix khawm uik;
kix tag guah khawng tan in sidu awm paox tix heue.
Been Krax-tan Pa-nhim
11 In plak greem mawh grawng lox tat jhawm prawng Krax-tan Pa-nhim pa sang tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex.
12 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux pa yuh Krax-tan Pa-nhim son pa yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
lheu khaing tan khawng brie,
kawn sang baox tan ka khao mun pa ang sau su saix:
khao mun pu khlawp pa yuh tix ka phawng mien pa hoik klao ka bee sime,
mai khao mun rhi khlawp pa kraix ka bee sime,
khawm khao mun phawng mien pa klao tix ka bee sime.
13 Lheu khaing in kix,
nawh kawn sang baox tan ka khao mun pa koe su saix tix blah.
14 Nawh tag sang teei khao mun daux in kix ku nyum,
kieh mawh khawng phak pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Khawng phak in tag mawh cie cao giex kaing li pa phruah nham Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka praok khu dee tan.
15 Nex khawng tan in caw tix ih uik daux ngaix an,
mai ang pon tix deuh hoek ngawb ngaix khaix pie ceeix kawx heue.
16 Phan tan Krax-tan Pa-nhim pa kieh mawh krax tan pa hawt awm lox pa hoik deuh tix mai krax tan pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm,
nex khawng tan an tag ang siao ih uik bawg yam yuh krax tan in,
mai pa kawn deuk khankix tag chix tix ih tom hoek singaix khaix.
17 Phan hoek ka daux loe singaix,
nex pa kawn deuk khankix tag sang gleeing uik.
18 Phan hoek ka daux loe singaix,
pa kawn ih nex pa deuk an,
Simiang Siyiex tag ang rieb ka krax pa tan nawh;
pa tan nawh tag ang sawn tix mawh krax pa tan tete.
Khawng tan in tag mawh pa ang singax singeh;
pui mawx ang ah phan ih ka nex krax tan in,
to kaux nawh tag sang tang klawm kah.
19 Phan nex khawng tan teeh ka been ceu pa yuh ka ceu pa ang singax singeh tix ceu ceu,
tag ang pon tix ih,
tag caw tix gleeing heue.
Ka daux been,
pui pa hoik ciak mawh pui pa singax singeh,
pui mawx ang ah,
tag chix tix ih ka nex Krax-tan Pa-nhim khawm uik.
20 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux been,
pui pa ang singax singeh,
phan ih ka nex in,
tag sang teei tix tik nawh khaing daux ceu pui Siyiex.
21 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux khaix hoik teeh ka been ceu pa ang singax singeh,
ih ka nex krax tan ceu in,
pa teeh pui ang ah,
to tiag ang ah,
khawng brie pa ang singax ang ah,
tag sang teei tix tik lih khaing ka daux pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex heue.
22 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
23 nhie nawh krai grawng lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
Moi,
yung,
mai khlawp bee pex khankix tag ang pon tix ih.
24 Khlawp bee to tiag pa lawk yum,
mai khlawp bee khawng brie pa hoik kiied to tiag khankix kawx,
tag ang pon tix ih,
viang mawh nan,
chix tix siao son tang ceu.
25 Pui mawx ang ah,
pa ih ka khlawp bee khawng brie pa hoik gleeing tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang teei tix tik nawh khaing daux ceu pui Siyiex.
26 aPui Yi-seu-liex ot dee mawx ang ah,
tag ang pon tix ih nex sim tau mai nham to sat khankix.
27 Can pui pa lieh lheu ka been in,
tag sang teei tix tik nawh khaing daux ceu pui Siyiex heue.
28 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
29 nhie nawh krai grawng lox tat jhawm prawng in plak greem ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pui mawx ang ah pa tan ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
nawh tag caw tix teei nex lih tix blah,
tawx kieh mawh khawng phak krax tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
30 To kaux nawh tag sang viex khawng tan in hoek tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag sang viex ka khlawp bee khawng brie tan in mai nex eek hoek,
kieh mawh khawng phak pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
31 Cao giex kaing li tag gleeing khlawp bee in ka piang khu dee tan;
mai nex eek in tag mawh cie Ya-loeng mai kawn praix nawh khankix.
32-33 Ba khawn dawm tag tawx ka cao giex kaing li pa kuang tix yuh ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim,
pa tan ka khlawp bee mai nham,
son pa mawh khawng phak.
34 Nex eek khawng brie tan an mai bax khawn dawm hoik mawh khawng phak pa tang blawk Simiang Siyiex tix teei lih khaing ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx mawh cie cao giex kaing li khankix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex tag caw tix kieh nyum in kix mawh cie cao giex kaing li khankix.
In hoik mawh lox tat jhawm prawng pa ang chix tieh loh tix leeing ju.
35 Nyum nex khawng tan in hoik dawg mawh pa ciak tix tawx mawh cie kix,
jauh singaix yam ap pong Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix mawh cao giex kaing li.
36 Singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nhie kix guah tix tawx nyum nex khawng tan in ka cao giex kaing li khankix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex tag caw tix hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng in leeing ju leeing phan heue.
37 In pa tiiem ot plak piang mawh grawng lox tat jhawm prawng Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phit-lud,
pa ap pong cao giex kaing li,
mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
38 Simiang Siyiex ot ka daux tiag daux tu ka gawng Si-nai,
mai tawx lox tat jhawm prawng in ka Maw-si.
Daux singaix an,
nawh tawx lox nhie pui Yi-seu-liex tan khawng tan ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
