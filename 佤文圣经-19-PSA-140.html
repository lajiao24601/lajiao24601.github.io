Khiox khaw krax pa kia ngien
140
Simiang Siyiex eue,
taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa caox pa caig;
khiox khaw maix kia ngien aux,
son ang aux yaox krax pa tuk tiak tuk te pui pa caox pa caig.
2 Rhawm rhi kix keud sawn ka krax pa caox pa caig khawm uik,
mai yuh krax pa simah leeing lang singaix.
3 aDak kix awm si uing pa koe jai yuh nan;
Siyieb jheeh kix awm si uing tiex teen pa koe jhai yuh nan heue.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
taoh aux luat luan khaing siawp sibrawm pui pa caox pa caig,
tieb tie krax pa keud sawn pui pa caox pa caig tix sang yuh aux goh.
5 Pui pa ca nyhau nih tix taong rub son sang yuh aux laux leeing;
kix hoik taong ka daux jhawk,
hoik taong saw hawt krax son sang pon giex ka aux heue.
 
6 Aux ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
maix mawh Siyiex aux.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox pa kok yo aux maix teem coi tix.
7 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
cao taoh aux pa ting riang ting rao eue,
maix kia ngien aux ka daux bang naing.
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
paw vi kieh pui pa caox pa caig pon yuh awm pa simie kix tix yuh;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh krax keud krax sawn kix lih tix mawh heue.
 
9 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh pui koeng ka aux pon pex;
kieh kaing pa yuh kix deeh hoek ka piang to kaux kix.
10 Kieh gaox ngu pa hax cawd ka piang kaing kix;
kieh pui tik kix ka daux o pa raux,
mai ang pon lih leeing ju.
11 Kieh pui pa krai lox pa caox ka pui tang kaux khankix ang pon cung kaux cong;
kieh krax laux krax leeing yuh pui pa tud pa rhim khankix laux leeing heue.
 
12 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix sang teem coi hu plak pui pa iak riang iak rao;
aux hoik tawng grawng sang teei maix pa khaw caw tix,
prawng son pui hot pui sibhawm heue.
 
13 Pui pa rauh pa see tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix;
kix tag sang paung ot ka plak sivoe ngai maix heue.
