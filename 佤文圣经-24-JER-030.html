Simiang Siyiex tawx gum lox sijax mukheu ka pui cie tix
30
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex
2 krai yuh nin ka aux:
“Maix tag caw tix tiiem gum lox pa hoik krai aux ka maix khawm uik ka daux phuk lai,
3 mawh kheu hoik hoek bawg yam,
aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix Yo-tax,
mai pui Yi-seu-liex deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix,
mai deeh sum ka nyiex ma tix.
Aux tag sang thung kix deeh hoek ka haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka tax du kix;
kix tag sang deeh yaox tix pon ka haktiex in.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
4 Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai ka pui Yo-tax ah nin:
5 aux hoik mhong ka lox naing,
hoik mawh krax lhat krax graung,
ang mawh pa nhim pa tiiem.
6 Cheu keud tix yaox,
simex sang chix keh kawn lie?
Yuh ka mawx aux lai hoik yaox ka simex ku kaux,
pa teei taix tix khawm ra khawn klien ka vaig tix,
awm bun miex yha pa sang keh yuh nan lie?
Yuh ka mawx pak ngai simex ku kaux hoik paing pau yuh nan lie?
7 Hoik laux leeing heue,
singaix pa kied lhat graung pui ka hoik hoek heue!
In mawh singaix pa ang sax koe phaox ka mai pa ang sax koe hu sivoe ngai;
in mawh singaix bawg yam pa kha pui cie aux rhawm rhi tix,
viang mawh nan,
kix ca kawn yaox tix klawd ju heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
“Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang raig pot ka khaox glawm pa jhup tix ot ka ngawg kix,
tat dut ka ceng pa prawk ka kix,
khaix an kix ang lai yaox tix mawh siliex cawng pui tang ceu tang cad.
9 Kix tag sang yuh kaing aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex kix,
tag sang yuh kaing simiang pa hoik laug aux hoek tix plud ka Ta-wue heue.
10 aPui cie aux eue,
paw vi lhat graung;
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
paw vi yaoh yee.
Aux tag sang jauh ka haktiex pa singai ka meung pa vuk vied vuk viang an,
taoh pex lih khaing.
Pex tag sang yaox tix deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix,
yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe,
ang koe krax lhat krax graung.
11 Mawh kheu hoik ot aux tau mai pex,
tag sang taoh ka pex.
Aux tag sang yuh ka meung haktiex pa hoik grheu pex hu khankix laux leeing,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang yuh pex laux leeing,
aux tag sang hawt tix sidiex ka pex awm pa khaw tix yuh ka pex,
ang yuh pex laux leeing uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
 
12 Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui cie tix ah nin:
Rhoi khaix mat pex ang yaox tix pon moi,
dee mat ang chix moi heue.
13 Ang koe pui pa simi ka pex,
mai pa kie dee mat son pex;
ang koe sidah pa chix pai pex moi.
14 Simex paox muh pex hoik piawm ka pex khawm uik;
kix ang baox lai sawg ka pex.
Aux puing ka pex awm puing ka pui koeng yuh nan,
ciyang ka pex tom kha rhawm tix heue.
Mawh kheu hoik kied ting phi map pex;
krax phit krax lud pex hoik koe tom nie.
15 Paw vi yiiem son rhoi khaix mat pex;
dee mat pex ang chix pon pai moi.
Mawh kheu hoik kied ting phi map pex,
krax phit krax lud pex hoik kied nie,
aux tom tag ciyang ka pex.
16 Khaix an,
pui pa bleet tix ih ka pex,
tag sang pon krax pa bleet tix ih;
can pui pa koeng ka pex khawm uik,
tag sang pon krax pa giex laung.
Pui pa gut kheem ka pex,
tag sang pon krax pa gut kheem;
pui pa tud rhim ka pex,
tag sang pon krax pa tud rhim heue.
17 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa koeng ka pex ah nin:
Pui hoik tik vud ka Shi-ang,
ang koe pui pa simi tix jhak kah,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang pai moi ka rhoi khaix mat pex,
kieh pex brax kua.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
18 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
Jhak heue,
aux tag sang kieh pui cie aux deeh hoek ka haktiex ot tix,
mai cox sum ka nyiex ma;
aux tag sang teei krax muh kia ngien ka cub nyiex ma Ya-keux.
Aux tag sang kieh kix cox sum ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
deeh cox sum nyiex haw ot ka dee prim sivoe an.
19 Pui pa ot dee an tag sang rhax lox pa yaog yaw;
kix tag sang gaux rhawm rhax lai heue.
Aux tag sang kieh kix pon krax pa haok huan,
kaux pui kix baox thiem thie tix;
aux tag sang deeh tawx min plak ngai ka kix,
ang lai baox kieh kix pon krax kaig krax tua.
20 Aux tag sang yaog meung kix haok huan,
deeh tawx siawp sibrawm ka kix;
aux tag sang ciyang ka can pui pa gut kheem ka kix,
21-22 ka piang kix,
tag sang mawh pui cie kix pa giex ka kaing;
simiang kix tag sang mawh pa kaoh khaing daux ceu pui kix.
Yam ghaok aux ka kix,
nawh tag sang hoek tix dex ka aux;
phan aux ang bax ghaok ka pui mawx,
mai pui mawx sang pon hoek tix dex ka aux lie?
Kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux;
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
23-24 Jhak heue,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex hoik awm bhaung klia pa ting mai klia tix ka piang kaing pui caox ang jud.
Krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex tag sang nied tix lih,
tom hoik si dauh yuh nawh kaing pa sang yuh tix khawm uik.
Yam an,
pui cie nawh tom taug chix tawng sidawng ka kaing in heue.
