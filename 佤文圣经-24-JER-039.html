Yie-lu-sa-leung laux leeing
39
Yam mawh Si-ti-cia simiang pui Yo-tax ka daux dim num khix kao,
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng thung pui naing tix puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 Yam mawh Si-ti-cia simiang ka daux kao tix num khix pon dim ngaix,
guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik ghrup heue.
3 Yam laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung,a
can simiang naing pa ting ka daux Pa-pi-loeng hoek ka siviex daux greung,
mai prawng see dee an.
Simiang naing pa ting in kix mawh:
Ni-cia Sa-li-shiie,
Sang-cia Ni-paw,
Sha-si-cing,
mai Ni-cia Sa-li-shiieb tang kaux heue.
4 Simiang Si-ti-cia mai pui naing pa pau cie nawh yaox grawng ang kix lai mawh,
mai to lih mox tix ka plak praix veng leeing sawm.
Kix laig ka daux si peem nyiex haw simiang,
lawng ka krax pa phlat hoek ka guh veng khawm ra krax,
mai to hu mox tix ka plak ngawd Yaw-tang.
5 Viang mawh nan,
pui naing Pa-pi-loeng leue ka kix,
mai krub Si-ti-cia ka dee lung dex ka Yie-li-keu.
Kix giex ka nawh,
mai viex nawh hoek ka simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha dee an.
Yam an,
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ot ka haktiex Ha-ma veng Li-pi-la;
nawh tom prawng ca Si-ti-cia dee an,
6 mai pauh can kawn simiang ka plak sivoe ngai Si-ti-cia khawm uik,
mai pui nang pui Yo-tax pa koe min khankix.
7 Khaix an nawh koik tawm ngai Si-ti-cia lih khawm ra khawn,
teei maox ceng phrawk nawh kah,
mai viex nawh hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
8 Pui Pa-pi-loeng ploi ngu gleeing ka nyiex haw simiang mai ka nyiex ot pui haktiex,
mai yieh laux ka guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
9 Dee nghoik,
Ni-pu-sha-la-tang simiang naing,
teei pui pa hoik kreet tix ot greem kix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng.
10 Nawh teei tix deuh pui pa blawn hot,
pui pa ang koe sin ngao khankix ot ka haktiex Yo-tax,
mai guah tix tawx si peem plix siiet mai kaung ma ka kix heue.
Yie-li-mi pon krax pa pluat ploi
11 Simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha tawx lox nhie Ni-pu-sha-la-tang simiang naing ah nin:
12 “Sawg Yie-li-mi hoek,
aux sang kia ngien ka nawh mhawm mhawm,
ang pon tix yuh nawh laux mat.
Phan nawh koe lox mhaing son ceu pa tix,
tag caw tix yuh son nawh heue.”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ni-pu-sha-la-tang mai simiang naing pa ting Ni-pu-sa-see-pang,
Ni-cia Sa-li-shiie,
khawm cao kaing tang kaux
14 teei aux lih khaing daux thawng nyiex haw simiang,
mai tawx ka kawn saux Sa-fang,
kawn simex Ya-si-kang pa kaux Ci-ta-lix,
mai tawx lox nhie nawh khre aux deeh ing ka nyiex.
Aux tom deeh ing,
mai paung ot ka daux paox ceu paox cad tix heue.
Krax keud pot jun pot Yi-paw Mi-leux
15 Yam kawn laung aux tix ot ka daux thawng nyiex haw simiang,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux
16 krai ka pui Ku-seu pa kaux Yi-paw Mi-leux,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang hawt tix gleeing ka veng in awm lox pa hoik krai pao tix yuh nan,
ang kieh haok huan.
Maix tag sang yaox kaing pa lih tix mawh in ka ngai tix.
17 Viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang kia ngien ka maix;
maix ang yaox tix ot daux tawm taix pui pa hoik lhat graung maix kah.
18 Aux tag sang kia ngien ka maix nyawm ot;
maix ang yaox tix yum ka greem pa lawm vaig.
Maix tag sang yaox tix klawd ju,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm maix ka aux.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
