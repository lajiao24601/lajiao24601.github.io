8
Grawng pa pauh ka See-thi-fang in,
Sao-law hoik cu ka heue.
Sao-law tuk tiak tuk te ka ceu cao
Jauh ka daux singaix an,
ceu cao ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung kham krax pa tuk tiak tuk te pa kied kin tete.
Lheu khaing kawn cau khankix,
can kawn Khrit khawm uik prung prang tix hoek ka veng Yo-thaix mai Sa-ma-li-yax ku dauh ku dee.
2 Koe pui pa yum rhawm khankix si paung ka See-thi-fang,
khaix an yiiem son nawh heue.
3 aYam an Sao-law yuh krax pa tuk tiak pa laux leeing ka kaing yuh ceu cao;
nawh sawg tix giex kawn Khrit pa mawh simex bun ku nyiex ku ma,
mai laung kix daux thawng.
Gum lox buan sidiex tix hoek ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax
4 Kawn Khrit khankix prung prang tix hu,
sidiex gum lox buan ka haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
5 Fe-lix hoek ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
mai krai pao grawh grawng Khrit ka pui haktiex an.
6 Pui hun khrawm paox tix nghiet ka lox pa krai Fe-lix.
Kix nghiet nang,
kix jhak nang ka mhai pa daox pa am yuh nawh.
7 Khuan pa seeing brai kok yo lox pa ting,
mai lih khaing daux to kaux pui pa hoik laig daux khankix;
pui pa yum taix caong,
pui pa chawh kawx hoik pon tix moi khawm uik.
8 Veng Sa-ma-li-yax hoik naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
9 Koe pui tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Si-meung,
krax si meed pa yuh nawh hoik sax si num ka pui pa ot daux veng Sa-ma-li-yax.
Nawh kawx hoik een to kaux tix mawh pui vao tix kaux;
10 pui pa ot daux veng ku cub hoik khaw rhawm ka lox khawm uik.
Pui hun ah nin:
“Pui in hoik koe siawp sibrawm Siyiex,
mawh ‘pui pa ting siawp sibrawm’.”
11 Mawh kheu pui in hoik teei krax si meed pa chix tix nyaw glaw ka kix,
mawh kheu nan kix nghiet ka nawh.
12 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoik yum rhawm kix ka gum lox buan siawp sibrawm Siyiex pa sidiex Fe-lix,
mai ka ceu kaux Ye-sux Khrit,
simex bun hoik rieb krax pa cub com khawm uik.
13 Si-meung kawx hoik yum rhawm kah;
khaix hoik cub com tix,
nawh ot tau mai Fe-lix jheeng jheeng.
Nawh hoik yaox ka kaing ting pa daox deng mai mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh Fe-lix,
mai nawh kied daox deng ka heue.
14 Kawn cau pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mhong grawng pa hoik rieb pui Sa-ma-li-yax ka gum lox Siyiex,
kix tom ploi Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang hu ka kix dee an.
15 Yam hoek kiex,
kiex tom rawg buan son kawn Khrit khankix,
son sang kieh kix pon rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh.
16 Mawh kheu yam an Khuan Singax Singeh kawn ang bax lih hoek ka pui tix kaux kawx ka daux kix;
kix rieb ngie krax cub com ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux khaing.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang deuh taix tix piang kix,
kix tom pon rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh heue.
18 Si-meung yaox hoik pon rieb pui pa deuh kawn cau taix tix piang khankix ka Khuan Singax Singeh khawm uik,
nawh tom teei mau tawx ka kawn cau,
19 ah nin:
“Tawx riang rao in ka aux nang,
mai kieh aux deuh taix tix piang pui mawx,
mai pui an tag pon rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh heue.”
20 Viang mawh nan,
Pi-teux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Kieh maix yum tau mai mau khri tix heue!
Maix lai ce tix sang teei mau liag ka khawng phak buan son Siyiex heue!
21 Maix hoik koe beeh glawm ka kaing jhaw pa yuh yix;
mawh kheu ang rhawm maix rauh see ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
22 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
tik vud ka pa caox pa caig,
khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex pluat ploi ka krax keud pa koe daux rhawm maix in.
23 Aux hoik yaox grawng pa khliak maix ka plix song pa boh rhawm,
mai mawh pui laung ka daux phi map heue.”
24 Si-meung krai ka Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang ah nin:
“Khiox khaw son aux ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai paw kieh kaing pa hoik krai pex hoek ka piang to kaux aux heue.”
25 Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang kham sakse ka dee an,
mai krai pao ka gum lox Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Ka daux krax,
kix hoik sidiex ka gum lox buan ka yaong pa koe ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax tom nie heue.
Fe-lix mai Yi-saw-phi-ya pui sieh
26 Koe kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux krai ka Fe-lix ah nin:
“Maix kling to kaux tix hu plak seh,
hoek ka krax an pa hu plak Cia-sa pa jauh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.”
(Krax in hoik riem.b)
27-28 Fe-lix tom yaog tix hu.
Ka daux krax,
nawh si pup ka pui sieh tix kaux pa kaux Yi-saw-phi-ya.
Pui in mawh simiang ting tix kaux,
nawh mawh pui pa giex mau ka greem bun simiang Kang-ta-ci simiang Yi-saw-phi-ya tix kaux.
Nawh haok krub Siyiex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
ka daux krax,
nawh ngawm tix brug ling law mai phat lai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax.
29 Khuan Singax Singeh krai ka Fe-lix ah nin:
“Maix hu dex tau mai ling law.”
30 Fe-lix tom to tix hu,
mai mhong lox phat pui sieh ka lai cao jhai Yi-sai-yax,
nawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Lai pa phat maix,
tawng maix ka lie?”
31 Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Lheu khaing ang koe pui pa dug thung,
aux sang chix tawng yuh ka mawx lie?”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh nhie Fe-lix haok piang ling law,
mai brug tau mai nawh.
32 cBaux gum lox pa phat nawh an mawh:
Nawh awm kawn yung tix mu pa tuk pui tix hu pauh,
awm kawn yung pa ang nye pa kip pui ka hauk;
awm an,
nawh kawx ang nye pie tix gum lox.
33 Nawh hoik kham krax kaig krax tua;
ang koe pui pa teem coi nawh ka daux pa rauh pa see,
ang koe pui pa pon krai ka ju nawh piang haktiex,
mawh kheu ju nawh ka piang haktiex hoik die heue.
34 Pui sieh chawk ka Fe-lix ah nin:
“Krai ka aux liak,
gum lox cao jhai baux in mawh krai ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh hoik krai ka to kaux nawh lie?
Mawh kawn sang krai ka pui tang kaux lie?”
35 Fe-lix tom ang jheeh tix,
mai krai dee bleeh grawh grawng pa dex mai gum lox buan Ye-sux,
jauh ka gum lo baux lai in tix baux.
36 Kiex hoek ka haktiex tix dauh,
rawm koe ka praok krax,
pui sieh an ah nin:
“Dee in koe rawm,
ang aux chix tix cub com dee in lie?”d
38 Pui sieh an tom tawx lox nhie ling law kaog;
Fe-lix laig tau mai nawh daux rawm,
mai cub com ka nawh.
39 Yam lih kiex khaing daux rawm,
khuan Siyiex viex Fe-lix hu;
pui sieh kawx ang lai yaox ka nawh.
Pui sieh an ca nied tix hu krax,
mai naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm.
40 Khaix an koe pui yaox Fe-lix ka Ya-saw-tu;
nawh tauh hu ka haktiex an ku dauh,
mai sidiex ka gum lox buan hu ka ku yaong,
tom hoik hoek nawh ka haktiex Khai-sha-li-yax heue.
