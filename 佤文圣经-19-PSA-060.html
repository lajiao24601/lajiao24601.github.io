Khiox khaw krax pa taoh teem Siyiexa
60
bSiyiex eue,
maix hoik tik vud ka yix,
mai hoik puing pex ka yix;
maix hoik sax song rhawm song rhi ka yix,
yam in khiox khaw maix deeh kieh yix haok huan heuec.
2 Maix hoik sax kieh haktiex yung yang bih;
yam in khiox khaw maix deeh cox,
mawh kheu hoik laux;
3 maix hoik kieh pui cie tix yaox krax kha rhawm kha rhi tom nie;
maix hoik kieh yix goh,
awm pui pa nyaux ka plai khien yuh nan.
4 Maix yaog tix sum man chix son pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix,
kieh kix yaox tix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing.
5 Khiox khaw maix cu nghiet ka lox yix,
mai teei pa ting riang ting rao maix taoh teem ka yix,
kieh pui pa muh maix kah,
pon krax pa taoh heue.
 
6 Siyiex krai lox tix lih khaing daux nyiex pa singax singeh nawh ah nin:d
Aux tag sang puing pex,
tag sang guah ka See-ciien tang ot;
aux tag sang teei ka ngawd Su-keu,
mai guah tix tawx ka pui cie tix.
7 Ci-liex mawh cie aux,
mai Ma-na-si kawx mawh cie aux;
Yi-fa-liien mawh mhok rhiem aux;
Yo-tax mawh khaox gix aux.
8 Aux tag sang teei Maw-ya mawh pan khoek caong,
kieh Yi-tong mawh dee deuh kiiep.
Aux tag sang rhax lai pa puing pex ka pui Fe-li-see heue.
 
9 Siyiex eue,
pui mawx pon viex aux laig daux veng pa kaux pa cong in lie?
Pui mawx pon thung aux hu ka Yi-tong lie?
10 Siyiex eue,
maix sang tik vud ka yix tete lie?
Ang maix hu puing naing tau mai pui naing yix lie?
11 Khiox khaw maix teem coi yix tieb tie ka pui koeng;
krax pa teem coi kawn pui ang koe ceu.
12 Siyiex ot ka plak ex,
mai ex tag sang pex;
nawh tag sang chix pon pex ka pui pa koeng ka ex tete heue.
