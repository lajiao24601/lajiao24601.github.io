7
Kawn pui ot ka piang haktiex,
awm pui pa khin tix yuh pui naing yuh nan,
yaox krax taug krax mao ku singaix,
2 awm siliex cawng simie tix nyaux rawm si kied yuh nan,
awm pui cang pui caing krox jun ka mau riang tix yuh nan.
3 Aux hoik caw tix mawh pui pa kha rhawm kha rhi tete;
hoik mawh ngie krax khi rhawm pa hoek piang aux ku sawm khaing.
4 Aux blauh tix it,
pa faik pon sawm kied lang,
tieh jee tieh blaong tix ang chix laig it,
mai simie praix riang.
5 To kaux aux hoik graik khawm gaw,
mai ngoi hoik naog ka khawm uik;
hak aux hoik leem hoik bih.
6 Krax ot krax koe aux kawn lheu phai khaing keei breeing taing khankix,
hoik luan hu ka daux pa ang koe krax keud pot jun pot heue.
 
7 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud kah,
krax pa im ju aux deuk ngie bhawm tix gum khaing;
aux ang lai baox yaox krax pa mhawm ot mhawm koe.
8 Yam in maix yaox ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
hu sivoe ngai tag ang lai baox yaox;
phan maix sang sawg ka aux,
aux ang lai ot heue.
9 Hoik awm lon pai awm pa grai,
pui pa hoik yum ang lai chix deeh hoek.
10 Nawh ang lai baox deeh ing ka nyiex,
pui pa tawng ka nawh hoik piawm nawh.
11 Ang,
aux ang lai ang krai lox!
Aux tag sang hao krax khi rhawm pa koe ka daux rhawm tix lih;
aux tag sang krai lao grawng krax pa kha pa koe daux rhawm aux heue.
 
12 Yuh ka mawx maix lai pau ka aux?
Khex een maix aux mawh to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui kaha lie?
13 Aux blauh tix it,
mai simie tix laux lee tix,
simie tix tik ka krax pa kha aux rhawm tix;
14 Khaix an,
maix teei ca maox caox ghlat aux kah,
kieh aux kha tix ka daux rhawm keud tix.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
aux kawn cu tix yum khaing,
ang rhawm kha tix ka daux pa im ju tix.
16 Aux ang lai koe krax gaux rhawm ka son sang im tix,
mai seeing ka ju tix.
Paw lai simi ka aux heue,
kheu ju aux ang lai koe dee bleeh heue.
 
17 bSawn kawn pui tix mawh ceu pa tix,
maix lai kawn een nawh mawh pa ting ceu?
Kaing pa yuh nawh koe ngawh jum ceu pa tix?
18 Maix hoik kaoh jhak ka nawh ku ngawb,
hoik cheuk tix yaox ka nawh ku mit nari.
19 Maix ang yoi ngai tix ang jhak ka aux,
kieh aux pon yaox tix bleet ka rawm mia tix tix gum lie?
20 Cao pa lam lia kia ngien ka kawn pui eue,
khex hoik kieh phi map aux maix laux lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai kieh aux mawh mhai tang son te pa puing?
Aux hoik mawh beeh glawm pa cien yuh nin ka maix yuh ka mawx lie?
21 Maix ang pon pluat ploi ka phi map aux lie?
Maix ang pon pluat ploi ka krax lud aux lie?
Ang lai koe miex glang tix aux tag sang laig daux si meeig;
maix tag sang sawg ka aux,
mai aux ang lai ot heue.
