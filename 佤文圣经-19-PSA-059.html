Khiox khaw krax pa kia ngiena
59
bSiyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui koeng;
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa puing ka aux.
2 Khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing pui pa caox pa caig,
kieh aux bheeik luan khaing tawm taix pui pa pauh ka pui heue.
 
3 Jhak heue,
kix kuan mox tix sang puing ka aux;
pui pa cau nyhau khrawm paox tix tieb tie ka aux.
In ang mawh kheu krax pa lud loe aux,
ang mawh nang phi map pa yuh aux.
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kix kok yo mai hoek tix,
ang mawh kheu pa hoik yuh aux krax pa lud loe heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao pa giex kaing kru naing kaing mheen eue,
kaoh maix teem coi ka aux liak!
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
khiox khaw maix hoek tix lam lia heue!
Khiox khaw maix kaoh tix ciyang ka kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik;
Paw vi jhi krax pa caox rhawm maix ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig khankix heue.
 
6 Kix sang deeh hoek yam pon bo,
mai awm raoh sox tiag yuh nan,
lai ling ka daux veng.
7 Nghiet ka lox ghlat pa map nai kix khaing heue.
Dak kix hoik lawm awm vaig,
kawn ce pui ang mhong ka grawng kix lie?
 
8 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix nyiah ka kix;
maix salao ka meung ka piang haktiex.
9 Aux tag sang een rhawm tix ka pa ting riang ting rao maix;
Siyiex eue,
maix mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux.
10 Siyiex aux muh ka aux,
mai nawh tag sang hoek tix teem coi ka aux;
nawh tag sang kieh aux yaox ka grawng pa laux leeing som nai pui koeng heue.
 
11 Siyiex eue,
maix ang pauh ka kix lie?
Yuh nin,
pui hun cie aux tom taug ang chix piawm kah.
Simiang aux eue,
maix mawh caox pa kia ngien ka yix;
khiox khaw maix teei riang rao tix,
mai grheu kix hu,
yuh kix laux leeing.
12 Jheeh kix hoik naog ka phi map,
mai siyieb jheeh kix hoik mawh phi map phi ghram khawm uik;
kieh kix goh sideeing tix ka daux pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tix.
Mawh kheu hoik ah kix lox jhax lox lawg,
mai hoik pie hoik lhien,
13 Khiox khaw maix tawx krax pa song rhawm tix lih piang kix,
mai yuh kix laux leeing;
khiox khaw maix yuh kix laux lak pak leeing.
Yuh nin,
kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik tom taug tawng Siyiex kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Ka daux pa giex nawh kaing,
hoik tauh ka piang haktiex khawm uik heue.
 
14 Pui pa koeng ka aux hoek yam pon bo,
hoik awm pa raoh sox tiag yuh nan,
lai ling ka daux veng,
15 hoik awm sox tiag pa lai ling tix hu ku dauh ku dee,
son sang sawg ka priex preem,
ang yaox tix sawm sak tom graox ang jud heue.
 
16 Viang mawh nan,
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka riang rao maix;
ku ngaix pon ngawb cao cao,
aux tag sang rhax si num ka krax muh maix pa koe leeing ju.
Maix hoik dawg mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux;
maix mawh krax kia ngien ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi aux.
17 Cao pa kia ngien ka aux eue,
aux sang yaog yaw ka maix;
Siyiex mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux,
mawh Siyiex pa muh ka aux heue.
