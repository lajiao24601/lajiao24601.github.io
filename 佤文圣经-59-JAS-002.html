Ne raw ka rhawm rhi pa ang khaw
2
Pux aik pux o aux khankix eue,
kheu hoik mawh pex kawn Khrit min plak ngai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit,
tag ang pon tix jhak ka plak praix pui,
teei krax pa ang khaw yuh ka pui.
2 Phan koe pui pa koe mau pa ceep ka jeep giien khri,
ceep ka kraung mhawm,
hoek ka dee khrawm pex paox tix,
khaix an koe nang pui hot pa ceep ka kraung griah hoek.
3 Maix tag sang krai lox pa nab yam ka pui pa ceep kraung mhawm an ah nin:
“Ngawm piang dang ngeung”,
viang mawh nan,
krai ka pui hot an ah nin:
“Cung ka plak an”,
phan ang mawh nan ah nin:
“Ngawm ka praok ngot ngawm aux”.
4 Maix tag mawh pui pa koe rhawm pa ang khaw,
mawh pui pa su tix map nai pui heue.
5 Pux aik pux o pa muh aux khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet:
Siyiex laug ka pui hot piang haktiex,
mai kieh kix mawh pui mag mi ka plak krax yum rhawm,
mai baox kieh kix pon ka meung pa khraox pa hoik deuh nawh tix tawx ka pui pa muh tix khankix.
6 Pex lai map nai ka pui hot heue!
Tete te lai,
pa gut kheem ka pex,
mai pa viex tix tawx pex ka simiang cao prawng mawh cub pui pa koe mau in kix heue!
7 Pa map nai ka ceu kaux pa ting min pa hoik pon pex an mawh kix heue.
8 aPhan pex priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng meung pa khraox pa tiiem phuk lai sigang khri “muh pui awm muh ka to kaux tix” an,
an tag mawh pa khaw heue.
9 Viang mawh nan,
phan pex jhak ka plak praix pui,
hoik mawh phi map;
lox tag jhawm prawng tag sang prawng ca grawng yuh pex pa lud loe ka been.
10 Pui mawx pa hoik lud loe ka gum lox ping nyap ka daux gum tix,
hoik awm mawh pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng khawm uik.
11 bMawh kheu kaw an hoik tawx lox ah nin “ang pon tix phit vao”,
mai baox ah nin “ang pon tix pauh pui yum”.
Viang maix ang bax phit vao,
maix hoik pauh pui yum,
hoik mawh pa lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
12 Kheu hoik sang pon maix ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa kieh pui pon krax prawng ca,
lox ah si kah krai maix tag caw tix kieh khaw mai diag tong in.
13 Mawh kheu yam prawng ca Siyiex,
nawh ang chix teei krax pa caox rhawm tix kia ngien ka pui pa ang koe krax pa caox rhawm khankix.
Kheu nan krax pa caox rhawm mawh pa pon pex ka krax prawng ca heue.
Krax yum rhawm mai krax pa yuh
14 Pux aik pux o aux khankix eue,
phan koe pui pa krai koe krax yum rhawm tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon jhi lih ka kaing ceu pa yuh tix,
sang chix koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
Krax yum rhawm an sang pon taoh ka nawh lie?
15 Ka daux pex phan koe pux aik,
phan ang mawh nan,
pux o pa ang yaox tix ceep kraung,
ang yaox tix ih,
16 pex krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Hu mhawm mhawm heue!
Hu mhawm mhawm heue!
Kieh pex yaox tix ceep tom si u,
yaox tix sawm tom sak heue!”
Ang tawx ceu pa sang lo ka kix,
an koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
17 Awm an,
krax yum rhawm phan ang koe krax yuh lih taug mawh pa yum heue.
18 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui tag sang ah nin:
“Pui in koe krax yum rhawm,
pui an koe krax pa yuh lih.”
Aux tag sang pawk ah nin:
“Maix tawx aux jhak ka krax yum rhawm pa ang koe krax pa yuh lih;
aux tag sang teei krax pa yuh lih tawx maix jhak ka krax yum rhawm tix heue.”
19 Maix hoik yum rhawm ka koe Siyiex tix kaux khaing,
an kied mhawm heue!
Phi caox hoik yum rhawm ka nang,
kheu nan kied lhat ka nawh tete.
20 Pui pa daox aw eue,
pex tag caw tix tawng krax yum rhawm,
phan ang koe krax pa yuh lih,
tag mawh pa ang koe ceu lie?
21 cTax du ex Ya-paw-la-hang,
mawh hoik si doe nawh tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw yuh ka mawx lie?
Hoik mawh kheu pa tan lu nawh kawn simex tix Yi-sha ka piang khu dee tan ka daux krax pa yuh lih in.
22 Jhak tix yaox,
krax yum rhawm mai krax pa yuh lih ra lai teem paox tix hu tau,
krax yum rhawm mawh ka daux krax pa yuh lih tom tag pon tix grum naog.
23 dPhuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Ya-paw-la-hang yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
kheu krax yum rhawm nawh,
Siyiex tom sawn nawh mawh pui rauh see heue.”
Gum lox in hoik lih tix mawh;
hoik kok nawh mawh paox grawm Siyiex.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui tag pon tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw kheu krax pa yuh lih,
mai ang joeng mawh ngie kheu krax yum rhawm khaing.
25 eGrawh grawng La-heux sibun coh kaux kawx hoik yuh nan nang.
Nawh hoik pon tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa kaw caw kheu krax pa yuh lih;
nawh hoik rieb tix khre ka kawn cau pui Yo-thaix,
mai baox teem kix bheeik luan khaing daux krax tang tix nyaing heue.
26 Mawh kheu nan awm to kaux phan ang koe bhawm tag mawh pa yum yuh nan,
krax yum rhawm kawx phan ang koe krax pa yuh lih tag mawh pa yum nang heue.
