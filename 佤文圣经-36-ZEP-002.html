Kok yo kawn pui loh rhawm rhi tix
2
Meung pa ang tawng tix kaig tua eue,
si duad heue!
2 Sivoe sang khrum maix awm lawm tai yuh nan,
sivoe sang proe ngu krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka maix,
sivoe sang hoek singaix krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
si duad heue!
3 Can pui pa ot piang haktiex pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi pa nghiet ka gum lox tawm nawh khankix eue,
sawg tix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Pex tag caw tix leue ka pa rauh pa see,
mai tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
Ka daux singaix pa tawx Simiang Siyiex krax pa song rhawm tix,
pex tee kawn chix yaox tix luat luan khaing krax ciyang heue.
Meung pa ot sivie siveng pui Yi-seu-liex si pup krax laux leeing
4 aKa daux veng Cia-sa ang lai koe pui pa klawd ju;
Ya-seu-ci-loeng tag sang riem.
Pui tag sang grheu pui Ya-seu-thu hu yam pon ngaix;
pui tag sang grheu nang pui Yi-keu-loeng lih hu khaing daux veng,
5 Pui pa paung ot ka hawt rawm Fe-li-see eue,
pex laux leeing heue!
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao sang prawng ca ka pex;
nawh tag sang yuh pex laux leeing,
mai ang lai deuh koe tix kaux kawx.
6 Pex pa ot haktiex hawt rawm tag sang tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag pa yi,
mai mawh ngie nyiex gox cao liang yung mai kawg yung pa koe ka khaing.
7 Pui Yo-tax pa deuk khaix tud rhim tag sang liang yung dee an,
mai it ka nyiex pa koe ka haktiex Ya-seu-ci-loeng.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix tag sang kia ngien ka kix,
mai kieh kix deeh ing cox ka nyiex ma tix heue.
8 bSimiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
“Aux mhong lox nyiah pui Maw-ya mai lox pa jhax lawg pui Ya-meung;
kix nyiah ka pui cie aux,
mai krai hu grawng sang teei ka haktiex kix.
9 cAux mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa im ju leeing ju;
aux tiex tu ah nin:
Maw-ya tag sang laux leeing awm Saw-to-ma yuh nan,
Ya-meung tag sang laux leeing awm Eu-maw-la yuh nan.
Veng in kix tag sang tieh tix lih mawh haktiex daux kih,
mai riem leeing ju,
rieb riem hoik kuah naog daux.
Pui cie aux pa deuk khankix tag sang tud rhim ka kix,
mai pon ot ka daux haktiex kix heue.”
10 Pui Maw-ya mai pui Ya-meung tag sang yaox krax ciyang yuh nin;
mawh kheu ting rhawm lhaong rhi kix,
mai map nai ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh kru naing kawn pui ka piang khawm uik.
11 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kix pru praoh yaoh yee.
Nawh tag sang yuh laux leeing ka siyiex piang haktiex,
khaix an meung ku meung tag sang krub nawh ka daux haktiex tix heue.
12 dSimiang Siyiex kawx tag sang pauh ka pui Ku-seu heue.
13 eSimiang Siyiex tag sang teei pa ting riang ting rao tix yuh pui Ya-sux laux leeing;
nawh tag sang kieh veng Ni-ni-we lih mawh daux tiag pa kroh pa riem.
14 Dee an tag sang lih mawh dee it phung moi,
phung yung,
khawm to sat pa koe daux tiag ku ceu.
Pang plawh yuh mheem tix ka dee riem an,
sim ah lox lih khaing daux doh;
lakf ah ak ak yuh nan ka sivoe glawk bawng;
khaox si oe bhawng pa sum pui nyiex ka tag sang leed lih.
15 Ceu in kix khawm uik tag sang lih hoek ka veng in khawm uik;
mawh kheu hoik yaw kix rang di riang rao tix,
mai ce tix hoik pon krax pa kaux cong.
Pui pa ot daux veng ce veng in hoik mawh veng pa blawn ting ka piang haktiex.
Viang mawh nan,
veng an tag sang lih mawh haktiex dee riem,
dee laux lee to sat tix.
Pui pa lawng krax ku kaux tag sang nyiah mai jhax lawg ka khawm uik heue.
