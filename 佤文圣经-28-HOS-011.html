Siyiex caox rhawm ka pui pa tieh vheen
11
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
Yam kawn mawh pui Yi-seu-liex kawn nyawm,
aux hoik muh ka nawh;
aux hoik kieh nawh mawh kawn simex tix,
mai viex nawh lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix.b
2 Viang mawh nan,
aux nied tix kok yo ka nawh,
mai nawh nied tix tieh vheen ka aux.c
Kix yuh krax tan lu ka Pa-lix;
kix gleeing tix tok nyawng ka riang rao.
3 Kheu nan,
pa gau pui Yi-seu-liex hu krax ang mawh aux lie?
Auxd teei plawng taix tix khawm ra khawn klien kix ot ka naog tix,
viang mawh nan kix ang doh keud ka buan son pa eei aux ka kix.
4 Aux teei maox krax muh pa caox rhawm tix tuk kix hu.
Aux klien ka kix,
awm klien pui kawn tix yuh nan;
aux gup ka to kaux,
mai eei ka kix heue.
 
5 “Mawh kheu ang pui Yi-seu-liex cu tix hoek ka aux,
kix tag sang deeh ing ka Ai-cix;
pui Ya-sux tag sang giex kaing piang kix.
6 Naing tag sang naog ka daux veng ot kix,
mai kieh siviex veng ot kix laux,
kieh pui cie aux laux leeing;
mawh kheu hoik een kix tix lhat lhiao.
7 Kix ca simie tix tieh vheen ka aux.
Kix tag sang laux rhawm yiiem grai kheu pa hoik sau khaox glawm piang ngawg tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa pon kah son kix heue.
 
8 ePui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux sang tik vud pex yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux sang tieb tie pex yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux sang yuh pex laux leeing awm yuh Ya-ma laux leeing yuh nan lie?
Aux sang yuh pex awm yuh ka Si-piien yuh nan lie?
Aux ang rhawm yuh nin ka pex,
mawh kheu kied muh aux ka pex heue!
9 Aux ang lai ciyang pex ka daux krax pa song rhawm;
aux ang lai baox yuh pui Yi-seu-liex laux leeing.
Mawh kheu mawh aux Siyiex,
ang mawh kawn pui.
Aux mawh cao pa singax pa ot ka daux pex;
aux ang lai baox prawng ca pex ka daux krax pa song rhawm heue.
 
10 “Aux tag sang awm sivai sang si pa graox ka pui pa koeng ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui cie aux tag sang hawt khaix aux.
Kix tag sang kaoh khaing plak klip singaix mai nied tix phai deeh hoek ka aux dee in.
11 Kix tag sang awm kawn sim pa phai pu hoek khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
baox awm si kaux pa pu hoek khaing Ya-sux.
Aux tag sang baox viex pui cie tix deeh hoek ka haktiex tix.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax kham krax pa ne raw
12 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex teei krax pa pie lhien lai lawm ka aux;
pui Yo-tax ca kawn tieh vheen ka aux,
tieh vheen ka Siyiex pa kaux sijax,
pa singax singeh heue.”
