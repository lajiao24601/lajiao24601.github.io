4
Kawn koe,
yam ang pui pa sang plud sin ngao nyang ting,
viang sin ngao pa koe khawm uik hoik mawh cie nawh,
viang nawh nan,
nawh ang tang siyox khaing siliex miex ding tix.
2 Yam kawn iak num nawh,
koe pui kia ngien ka nawh,
yuh kaing son nawh,
mai krox jun singaix bawg yam pa hoik tat keeing nawh lih hoek son nawh.
3 Awm an,
yam jauh tix ot ka daux khuan,
ex kawx kawn ot ka lox sidiex ka daux been haktiex.
4 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
Siyiex tag sang ploi kawn simex tix hu,
mai keh khaing bun,
im ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng,
5 ason sang taoh ka pui pa ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai kieh ex pon tix mawh min ceu kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex heue.
6 Mawh kheu hoik mawh exb kawn sime kawn bun nawh,
Siyiex tom ploi khuan kawn simex tix laig ka daux rhawm rhi ex,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Keeing,
keeing eue!”
7 Yuh nin,
maix tag ang lai mawh siliex cawng,
hoik mawh kawn simex nawh;
kheu hoik mawh maix kawn simex Siyiex,
Siyiex tag sang kieh maix mawh pui pa plud sin ngao heue.
Pao-law chix kia ngien ka pui Cia-la-thaix
8 Ka daux singaix pa hoik luan pex ang tawng ka Siyiex,
mai hoik mawh siliex cawng siyiex tang kaux pa ang mawh siyiex.
9 Yam in pex hoik tawng ka Siyiex (phan ang mawh nan,
ah Siyiex hoik tawng ka pex),
yuh ka mawx lai deeh ing sawg ka lox sidiex ka daux been haktiex pa ang koe ceu koe cak lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai sang deeh cox mawh siliex cawng kix lie?
10 Pex lai hawt tix tuah ka singaix,
khix,
poi,
jhong num heue!
11 Aux kied khi rhawm son pex,
lhat kaing pa hoik yuh tix ka daux pex sivoe an mawh pa giex going khawm uik heue.
12 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux khiox khaw pex cu ka lox pa krai tix,
aux kawx hoik sax cu ka lox pa krai pex sivoe an nang.
Pex ang sax yuh kaing ceu pa ang khaw caw ka aux.
13 Pex hoik tawng,
mawh kheu koe jhawm saux aux,
aux tom koe bawg yam tix sidiex gum lox buan ka pex tix bawg khaing.
14 Viang jhawm saux aux hoik kieh pex nyhap mao,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang bax prah ghram ka aux,
tik vud ka aux.
Deeh tieh tix,
pex tieh tix rieb ka aux,
awm rieb ka kawn cau Siyiex yuh nan,
awm rieb ka Ye-sux Khrit yuh nan.
15 Yam an pex hoik koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
yam in yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux chix tix krai ah nin,
bawg yam an,
pex kawx koik tix tawx tawm ngai tix ka aux hoik chix tix heue!
16 Yam in aux krai lox tete te lai ka pex,
mai aux tieh tix lih mawh pui koeng pex ka lie?
17 Pui an kix koe krax gaux rhawm ka pex,
tete te lai,
daux rhawm rhi kix ang mawh nan.
Kaing ting kix mawh son sang kieh aux tang ot,
son sang kieh pex kawx koe krax gaux rhawm ka kix.
18 Pa gaux rhawm ka kaing ceu pa mhawm hoik mawh ceu pa mhawm,
viang mawh nan,
ang mawh pa taug yuh yam ot aux tau mai pex khaing.
19 Kawn aux khankix,
aux sang baox awm miex pa kham krax kha rhawm son pex yam keh tix yuh nan,
tom hoek lih mawh pak peung Khrit sidawng ka daux krax pa im ju pex.
20 Aux kied simie tix ot tau mai pex yam in,
son sang kia ngien aux pex ka daux krax pa tang siyox tix ceu ceu.
Kheu son pex,
rhawm rhi aux kied ang nyawm ot nyawm koe heue!
Lox tiag ka Sa-cia mai Sa-la
21 Kieh aux chawk lox ka pui pa cu tix ot greem lox tat jhawm prawng khankix yuh nin:
Ang pex bax mhong ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si lie?
22 cLox tat jhawm prawng hoik tiiem tix deuh,
Ya-paw-la-hang koe kawn simex ra kaux,
tix kaux mawh pa keh khaing bun siliex,
tix kaux mawh pa keh khaing bun pa hoik pon krax pa bheeik luan.
23 Pa keh khaing bun siliex,
mawh pa lawk keh awm been pa koe kawn pui yuh nan,
pa keh khaing bun pa hoik pon krax pa bheeik luan,
mawh pa kaoh khaing lox pa ap Siyiex.
24 In chix tix kieh mawh lox leng tix ceu yuh nin:
sibun in kiex mawh pa krai grawng sijax mukheu ra ceu.
Pa mawh Sa-cia,
nawh hoek khaing gawng Si-nai,
mawh kheu pa keh nawh mawh siliex cawng khawm uik.
25 Sa-cia mawh son gawng Si-naid ka daux haktiex A-la-pawx,
mai jhi grawng veng Yie-lu-sa-leung daux in singaix;
nawh mai kawn simex kawn bun nawh ot ka daux pa yuh siliex cawng.
26 Viang mawh nan,
Yie-lu-sa-leung pa koe ka piang rao ma an mawh pa pon tix bheeik luan;
nawh mawh miex ex.
27 eMawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri hoik tiiem ah nin:
bun pa ang chix nghe,
pa ang bax keh kawn eue,
maix tag sang gaux rhawm heue!
Bun pa ang bax cim krax keh kawn pa kha rhawm khankix eue,
maix tag sang ah lox pa ting si num.
Mawh kheu bun pa hoik tik vud pui
kawn lheu hun kawn khaing
bun pa ot tau mai mex tix heue!
28 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
hoik awm Yi-sha yuh nan,
pex hoik lih mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex khaing gum lox pa hoik ap nawh.
29 fYam an,
pa lawk keh awm been pa koe kawn pui,
tag sang tuk tiak ka pa keh khaing Khuan Singax Singeh;
yam in kawx hoik mawh yuh nan.
30 gViang mawh nan,
phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ah ka mawx lie?
Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Grheu bun siliex an mai kawn simex nawh lih hu,
mawh kheu kawn simex bun siliex an ang pon tix rom tix pon sin ngao tau mai kawn simex bun pa pon krax pa bheeik luan heue.”
31 Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
ex ang mawh kawn simex kawn bun siliex,
mai mawh pa keh khaing bun pa pon krax pa bheeik luan heue.
