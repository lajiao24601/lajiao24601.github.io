Pao-law krai lox son to kaux tix ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Ya-ci-phax
26
Ya-ci-phax krai ka Pao-law ah nin:
“Aux cu tix kieh maix krai lox son to kaux heue.”
Pao-law tom nyeu taix tix krai lox son tix ah nin:
2 “Simiang Ya-ci-phax eue,
daux in singaix aux yaox tix cung ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
mai krai lox son kaing pa deuh pui Yo-thaix phi map piang aux khawm uik,
sidaing mawh kaing ceu pa mhawm heue!
3 Kied nie koe buan son ka grawng pa hoik tawng maix sidawng ka been pui Yo-thaix,
mai grawng pa simah kix paox tix.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux khiox khaw maix viex rhawm gun nghiet lox pa krai aux liak.
4 “Jauh tom yam iak aux hoek yam in,
hoik mawh aux pui pa yuh ka mawx,
pui Yo-thaix ang koe pui pa ang tawng tix kaux kawx.
Jauh tom yam lih keh aux ka piang haktiex,
aux hoik dawg ot ka daux meung pui haktiex tix,
mai ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
5 aPhan kix cu tix kham mhai sakse son aux,
kix hoik tawng grawng mawh aux cub ex pui Fa-li-saix pa kied chix priex tix hawt ka been li.
6 Yam in aux cung tix kham krax pa prawng pui dee in,
hoik mawh kheu koe aux krax keud pot jun pot ka gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex tix deuh ka tax du ex.
7 Gum lox in hoik mawh krax pa hoik krub ex,
mai krax pa hoik keud pot jun pot ex paox ceu paox cad kao ra ceu ka Siyiex leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
Simiang eue,
kheu hoik mawh krax keud pot jun pot son ceu in,
pui Yo-thaix tom taug deuh phi map piang aux heue!
8 Viang mawh nan,
pex pui Yo-thaix yuh ka mawx lai ang koe krax yum rhawm ka grawng in pa hoik kieh Siyiex pui yum deeh kaoh im lie?
9 “Sivoe an aux hoik sau riang tix tieb tie ka Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux.
10 Aux tom yuh nin ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Aux hoik pon akhuang khaing simiang cao giex kaing li,
mai giex laung kawn Khrit daux thawng tom nie;
ca lao ang mawh nan,
ciyang kix yum kawx aux hoik cu kah.
11 Lheu khaing an,
aux kawn kha rhawm kix ka daux nyiex sidiex ku dauh,
mai khin kix tik vud ka been li kix.
Aux kied koeng ka kix,
mai map nai kix ka daux veng pui tang meung heue.”
Pao-law krai lao grawng pa yum rhawm tix ka Simiang Ye-sux
(Lai Grawng Pa Yuh Kawn Cau 9.1–19; 22.6–16)
12 “Koe tix bawg,
aux viex akhuang mai lox pa tawx simiang cao giex kaing li,
mai ka gum lox tawm hu ka Ta-ma-see-keux.
13 Simiang eue,
ka daux bawg yam greung ngaix,
aux yaox pa riang tix mu kawn lheu riang khaing pa riang singaix,
lih khaing rao ma jhan ka sivie siveng aux mai ka pui pa hu tau mai aux.
14 Yix tom goh piang tiex.
Aux mhong lox tix gum,
mai siao lox Si-paw-laib ah nin ka aux:
‘Sao-law,
Sao-law!
Yuh ka mawx maix lai tuk tiak ka aux lie?
Maix hoik awm moi pa teei caong tix tied ka khaox lawm cie cao eei tix yuh nan,
mai deeh tieh tix mat yuh to kaux tix.’
15 Aux tom chawk:
‘Simiang eue,
mawh maix mawx?’
Simiang Ye-sux ah nin:
‘Aux mawh Ye-sux pa hoik tuk tiak maix heue.’
16 “Kaoh cung.
Aux sang jhi maix yaox tix,
mai sang ploi maix hu mawh siliex tix.
Maix tag caw tix kham sakse ka pa hoik yaox maix daux in singaix,
mai ka grawng pa dex dawt mai aux,
khawm kaing pa sang jhi aux ka maix ka daux singaix pa sang hoek.
17 Aux tag sang taoh maix lih khaing daux tawm taix pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu tang cad khankix,
ploi maix hu laig ka daux kix.
18 Maix tag caw tix paoh ka ngai kix,
kieh kix lih khaing daux pa faik,
kling tix hu ka pa riang,
lih khaing siawp sibrawm Sa tan,
veeh tix hoek ka Siyiex,
son sang kieh kix pon krax pa pluat ploi phi map kheu hoik yum rhawm kix ka aux,
khaix an koe ceu kaux ka daux pui cie Siyiex khankix heue.”
Pao-law krai lao kaing yuh tix
19 “Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-ci-phax eue,
aux ang bax lieh lheu ka riang su sai pa hoek khaing rao ma.
20 cAux ran ot ka Ta-ma-see-keux mai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
khaix an ot ka daux pui Yo-thaix mai ka daux pui tang ceu tang cad sidiex gau kix loh rhawm rhi tix,
veeh tix hoek ka Siyiex,
ka can kaing pa yuh tix khawm uik,
tag caw tix kieh khaw caw maix krax pa loh kix rhawm rhi tix.
21 Kheu son kaing in,
yam ot aux ka daux nyiex buan,
pui Yo-thaix giex ka aux,
mai keud tix sang pauh ka aux.
22 Viang mawh nan,
tom hoek yam in aux pon krax pa teem coi Siyiex,
mai pon tix cung dee in,
kham sakse ka can pui pa koe min mai pui pa iak min khawm uik.
Pa krai aux kawx hoik mawh kaing pa krai cao jhai mai Maw-si,
pa tag sang lih tix mawh singaix pa sang hoek,
23 dpa mawh grawng pa sang kham Khrit krax kha rhawm,
khaix an ran deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
krai pao hoik lih hoek pa riang krax taoh ka pui Yo-thaix mai ka pui tang ceu tang cad heue.”
24 Pao-law krai lox son to kaux tix yuh nin;
Fe-see-to kok yo tom ting ah nin ka nawh:
“Pao-law,
maix hoik yhot;
krax pa lhaong ngai lai maix hoik kieh maix yhot heue!”
25 Pao-law ah nin:
“Fe-see-to simiang pa ting heue!
Aux ang bax yhot;
lox pa krai aux mawh tete.
26 Simiang kawx hoik tawng grawng in kix,
mawh kheu nan aux viex rhawm ting krai sidawng ka simiang;
kaing in kix ku ceu aux yum rhawm grawng hoik een simiang tix tawng,
mawh kheu kaing in kix hoik mawh kaing pa tawng pui ka ku kaux.
27 Simiang Ya-ci-phax eue,
yum rhawm maix ka cao jhai lie?
Aux tawng grawng hoik yum rhawm maix ka heue!”
28 Ya-ci-phax krai ka Pao-law ah nin:
“Maix ce tix sang teei gum lox ra loe gum kieh aux mawh pui cie Khrit lie?”
29 Pao-law pawk ah nin:
“Lox viang krai nie krai iak ang ah,
pa hoik khiox khaw aux ka Siyiex,
mawh maix mai ka pui pa ot dee in daux singaix pa hoik mhong ka lox krai aux,
tag sang awm aux khawm uik yuh nan,
mawh ngie ang awm viex aux ka maox ceng in kix yuh nin khaing heue!”
30 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang,
cao giex kaing,
Pe-ni-see,
mai pui tang kaux khankix yaog tix kaoh cung khawm uik.
31 Khaix hoik deeh kix plak khaix tix,
mai ra lai ah nin:
“Pui in ang bax yuh phi map pa khaw tix yum,
phan ang mawh nan pa khaw tix laung heue.”
32 Ya-ci-phax krai ka Fe-see-to ah nin:
“Phan pui in ang bax krai ka Vawng,
mai hoik dawg pon tix ploi ka nawh heue.”
