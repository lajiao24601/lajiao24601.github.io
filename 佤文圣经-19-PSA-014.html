Krax pa caox pa caig kawn pui
(Lai Yaog Yaw 53)
14
aPui pa cau nyhau tang ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
Siyiex ang koe.
Kix hoik laux leeing,
mai hoik yuh ka kaing pa seeing pa brai;
pie pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm tix kaux kawx ang lai koe heue.
 
2 Simiang Siyiex kok jhak kawn pui piang haktiex khaing rao ma,
mai jhak tix yaox koe ang koe pui pa lhak lhiao,
koe ang koe pui pa sawg ka nawh.
3 Mawh kheu nan pui ku kaux hoik via hu khaing krax rauh krax see;
ku kaux ku pui hoik laux leeing awm paox tix,
ang koe pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm,
pie tix kaux kawx ang koe heue.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex chawk ah nin:
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig ang tawng lie?
Khex hoik daox aw kix khawm uik lie?
Kix bleet ka pui cie aux;
kix ang tawng tix khiox khaw ka aux heue.
 
5 Kheu nan,
kix tag sang si yaung si kua,
mawh kheu ot Siyiex mai pui rauh pui see.
6 Pui caox pui caig nyiah ka krax keud krax sawn pui hot pui sibhawm;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang mawh nyiex dee mox nawh tix.
7 Kieh pa taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex hoek khaing Shi-ang heue!
Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pui cie tix deeh cox ka daux nyiex ma tix.
Kawn praix Ya-keux tag sang gaux rhawm jang luh tix;
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
