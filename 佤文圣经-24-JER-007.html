Yie-li-mi sidiex ka daux nyiex buan
7
1-3 Simiang Siyiex ploi aux hoek ka diang siviex nyiex buan,
nhie aux cung dee an krai pao gum lox nawh ka pui Yo-tax.
Mawh kheu nan aux hoek ka diang siviex nyiex buan,
mai krai gum lox Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix tik vud ka pa caox pa caig heue!
Aux tom tag kieh pex paung ot ka haktiex in.
4 Paw vi lai baox yum rhawm ka gum lox pa pie lhien pui khankix,
ah nin:
‘Aux kied nyawm ot nyawm koe,
in mawh nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex;
in mawh nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex;
in mawh nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex heue!’
5 “Pex tag caw tix tik vud ka pa caox pa caig,
ra lai kia ngien paox tix ka daux pa rauh pa see.
6 Paw vi lai baox gut kheem ka pui tang ceu tang cad mai miex mai kawn doe khankix.
Naox pauh ka pui pa ang bax lud loe ka daux haktiex in.
Paw vi lai baox krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
tang deeh laux leeing yuh to kaux tix.
7 Phan pex loh rhawm rhi tix,
aux tag sang kieh pex deeh yaox tix paung ot dee in,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik dawg tawx aux ka tax du pex kieh mawh sin ngao kix sivoe an leeing ju heue.
8 “Jhak heue,
phan pex een rhawm tix ka pui pie lhien an mawh pa giex pa going heue!
9 Pex briex lak,
pauh ka pui,
phit vao,
kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh,
tan lu ka Pa-lix,
krub ka siyiex pa ang pex tawng.
10 Pex hoik yuh ka kaing ceu pa koeng aux kah,
khaix an to tix hoek ka daux nyiex buan aux,
cung ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
mai ah nin:
‘Yix kied nyawm ot nyawm koe heue!’
11 aPex ce nyiex buan aux mawh dee mox pui briex tix lie?
Aux hoik tawng sidawng ka krax keud krax sawn pex khawm uik.
12 cPex hu ka haktiex See-law,b
jhak tix yaox ka nyiex dee pa hoik laug aux,
pex tom tag tawng grawng pa yuh aux ka haktiex an kheu phi map pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
13 Pex hoik yuh ka phi map tom nie;
aux ca kawn baox sidiex gau ka pex,
mai pex ca kawn ang cu tix nghiet kah;
aux kok yo ka pex,
pex ca kawn ang cu tix pawk ka lox aux.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
yuh aux ka mawx ka See-law,
aux kawx tag sang yuh nan ka nyiex buan aux pa hoik een pex rhawm tix ka an,
khawm haktiex dee pa hoik tawx aux ka pex,
mai ka tax du pex awm yuh aux ka See-law sivoe an yuh nan.
15 Aux tag sang grheu pex hu khaing plak sivoe ngai tix,
awm hoik grheu aux ka paox ceu paox cad pex pui Yi-seu-liex sivoe an yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Kawn pui lieh lheu gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
paw vi rawg buan tang pui in kix,
mai paw vi khiox khaw son kix.
Maix paw vi khiox khaw ka aux,
mawh kheu aux ang nghiet.
17 Khex ang maix bax yaox ka grawng pa yuh aux ka veng Yo-tax ku dauh,
mai ka daux laih daux cau pa ting pa iak veng Yie-lu-sa-leung lie?
18 dKawn simex kawn bun kix mawh cao pa khrawm khix,
keeing kix mawh cao pa phlu ngu,
miex kix mawh cao pa yuh glua khao mun,
mai tan lu ka siyiex bun kix.
Kix kawx hoik tawx nang siyiex tang kaux nyaux ka plai,
mai cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm.
19 Tete te lai,
kix ang mawh yuh aux laux,
hoik mawh kix pa tang deeh yuh to kaux tix laux,
tang deeh kieh to kaux tix kaig tua.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang hieh krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang nyiex buan in,
mai ka piang kawn pui,
ka piang to sat,
ka piang khaox ox,
mai ka piang to ma to koi.
Krax pa song rhawm aux hoik awm rup ngu ting yuh nan,
ang koe pui pa pon preet yeet heue.
21 “Pui cie aux eue,
pex hoik een tix tawng,
koe Krax-tan Khawng-brie tix blah khankix tag caw tix gleeing singax uik,
koe khawng tan tix blah khankix chix tix ih.
Viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex simiang kru naing ah nin:
pex ih ka nex khawng tan can pa koe khawm uik khaing heue.
22 Yam thung aux tax du pex lih khaing Ai-cix,
aux ang bax tawx lox nhie kix tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
phan ang mawh nan krax tan tang ceu khankix.
23 Aux tawx ngie lox nhie kix cu nghiet ka aux;
yuh nin,
aux tom tag mawh Siyiex kix,
mai kix tom taug mawh pui cie aux.
Aux nhie kix priex tix hawt ka krax cie aux;
yuh nin,
kix tom taug pon yaox tix mhawm ka daux kaing ku ceu.
24 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang nghiet,
mai ang simi,
tieh tix kieh rhawm rhi pa caox pa caig tix,
rhawm rhi pa kiien tix mawh pa thung ka to kaux tix,
nied ot nied caox.
25 Jauh tom yam lih hu tax du pex khaing haktiex Ai-cix ka daux singaix an,
aux hoik ca baox ploi siliex tix,
pa mawh cao jhai khankix tawx lox si kah ka pex ang jud,
26 Viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa simi,
ang koe pui pa nghiet.
Pex kawn lheu thiang lox,
lieh lheu lox khaing tax du pex heue.
27 “Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-li-mi eue,
maix tag caw tix krai lox in kix ka pui cie aux,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang nghiet;
maix tag caw tix kok yo ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang pawk lox maix,
28 Maix tag caw tix ne raw ka kix ah nin:
meung haktiex kix ang rieb ka krax pa ciyang,
ang nghiet ka aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex kix.
Krax cu krax nghiet kix hoik laux leeing grai grup,
pie krai kix ang lai krai heue!”
Phi map pa yuh ka daux ngawd Sing-noeng-cee
29 “Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
yiiem grai heue!
Khlao ka hauk kaing,
mai tik vud ka heue!
Hu ah lox rhax yiiem doe ka piang gawng heue!
Mawh kheu aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik tik ka pa cheeik aux song rhawm pa mawh pui cie tix heue.
30 “Pui Yo-tax hoik yuh ka kaing ceu pa caox pa caig tix ceu.
Kix teei ka riang rao pa koeng aux mai deuh ot daux nyiex buan dee krub aux,
hoik seeing brai yuh nyiex dee ot aux.
31 eKix yuh khu Thaw-fe-theux ot ka daux ngawd Sing-noeng-cee,
mai gleeing tix tan kawn simex kawn bun tix ka piang.
Aux ang bax mhaing kix tan krax tan pa yuh nin,
pie keud kawx ang bax keud.
32 Mawh kheu nan,
koe tix singaix,
dee an ang lai baox kok mawh Thaw-fe-theux,
phan ang mawh nan ngawd Sing-noeng-cee,
tag sang kok mawh ‘ngawd dee pauh’.
Mawh kheu ang si meeig kub,
pui tom tag sang si paung to kaux pui yum ot ka Thaw-fe-theux.
33 Sim mai to sat tag sang ih ka nex to kaux pui yum,
ang koe pui pa grheu hu.
34 fAux tag sang kieh daux haktiex an khawm uik lih tix riem ang lai koe krax gaux rhawm.
Veng haktiex Yo-tax ku mu mai daux laih ting laih iak pa koe ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung ang lai baox koe krax nyiah,
ang lai baox koe lox gaux rhawm poi ka paox.
Haktiex in tag sang riem khawm uik heue.”
