Krai grawng krax yum rhawm
11
Phan mawh nan,
krax yum rhawm mawh ceu pa tix lie?
Krax yum rhawm mawh pa giex tom kaux cong ka kaing ceu pa hoik keud pon jun pot kah,
ka kaing ceu pa hoik een mawh pa ang nyang bax yaox ka ngai tix.
2 Pui dix dix pon krax pa yaog yaw Siyiex,
mai hoik mawh kheu pa yum rhawm kix ka Siyiex heue.
3 aMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
ex hoik tawng rao ma mawh pa phan yuh Siyiex lih khaing lox tix;
yuh nin,
ceu pa chix yaox hoik mawh pa phan yuh lih khaing ceu pa ang chix yaox an heue.
4 bMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
Ya-paw tan lu khawng tan pa lheu mhawm khaing pa tan lu Kai-yin ka Siyiex.
Ka daux krax yum rhawm nawh hoik pon pa yaog yaw Siyiex,
mai pui hoik kok nawh mawh pui rauh pui see,
mawh kheu hoik hoek Siyiex tix rieb ka khawng phak lak sawng cie nawh.
Viang nawh hoik yum kawx,
mai ca kawn si kah ka daux krax yum rhawm heue.
5 cMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
Yi-naw ang yaox tix yum.
Hoik yaog nawh haok ka Siyiex dee an,
ang koe pui pa pon yaox ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik rieb Siyiex nawh hu.
Phuk lai sigang khri krai ah nin,
sivoe sang yaog nawh haok,
nawh hoik dawg pon krax pa khaw rhawm Siyiex.
6 Pui pa ang koe krax yum rhawm tag ang pon krax pa khaw rhawm Siyiex.
Can pui pa hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex tag caw tix yum rhawm grawng pa koe Siyiex,
kheu nan yum rhawm grawng sang vied Siyiex buan son ka pui pa sawg ka nawh heue.
7 dMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
Naw-yax hoik nghiet lox pa ne raw Siyiex ka daux kaing ceu pa ang tix bax yaox ka ngai tix,
yuh ka reu tix mu,
mai hoik pon taoh ka to kaux tix mai pui nyiex tix.
Yuh nin,
nawh prawng ca ka phi map singaix baux ju an,
to kaux nawh kawx hoik pon tix rauh see kheu krax yum rhawm heue.
8 eMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
Ya-paw-la-hang nghiet ka lox pa kok yo Siyiex,
mai hu ka haktiex dee pa tag sang tawx Siyiex gum lox pa deuh tix.
Yam lih hu nawh khaing meung haktiex tix,
nawh ang tawng ka dee sang hoek tix.
9 fKa daux krax yum rhawm,
nawh paung ot ka haktiex dee pa hoik deuh Siyiex gum lox tix,
mawh nyo si nie ka haktiex pui,
mai paung ot tau mai Yi-sha mai Ya-keux ka daux nyiex gox bang pa hoik pon ka gum lox pa deuh Siyiex.
10 Mawh kheu Ya-paw-la-hang keud pot jun pot ka veng pa sum Siyiex,
num jaong pa kaux cong an leeing ju heue.
11 hMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
viang Sa-la ang chix nghe,
hoik luan ka bawg yam num pa keh kawx,
mai nawh ca kawn koe riang rao tix keh,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm nawhg ka pa giex Siyiex kaux cong ka gum lox pa deuh tix.
12 iViang Ya-paw-la-hang awm pui pa hoik yum yuh nan,
kawn praix pa si doe tix lih khaing nawh tag sang haoh hun awm simuing piang rao ma,
mai mu mhaik pa ang uik simin ka praok rhawm sigrit sigra yuh nan heue.
13 Pui in kix hoik koe krax yum rhawm tom hoek yum tix.
Kix ang bax pon ka gum lox pa deuh Siyiex;
viang mawh nan,
kok jhak dee singai,
rhawm rhi gaux rhawm kah,
mai baox doh keud yam ot kix ka piang haktiex,
grawng hoik mawh ngie kix pui tang ceu mai nyo si nie khaing.
14 Pui pa krai lox in hoik jhi sidawng grawng pa sawg kix nyiex ma son to kaux tix.
15 Kix ang braong ka haktiex pa hoik lih hu tix khaing,
phan ang mawh nan,
kix kawn sang koe bawg yam tix deeh ing.
16 Pa kied simie kix mawh nyiex ma pa koe ka piang rao ma pa nie blawn mhawm an;
mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex ang doh kaig tua kheu pa kok kix nawh mawh Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik rien khre nawh veng son kix tix mu heue.
17 jMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
yam cheuk cheu Ya-paw-la-hang ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
tan lu ka kawn simex tix Yi-sha kieh mawh khawng tan.
Ya-paw-la-hang mawh pui pa hoik pon ka gum lox pa deuh Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
nawh cu tix tawx kawn simex tix pa koe tix kaux khaing mawh khawng tan.
18 kSiyiex hoik sax ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix tag sang pon doeh ceu cad khaing Yi-sha awm gum lox pa hoik dauh aux.”
19 Nawh hoik yum rhawm grawng sang pon kieh Siyiex Yi-sha deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum.
Tete te lai,
ex kawx chix tix krai ah nin,
Ya-paw-la-hang rug hoik teei Yi-sha deeh lih khaing krax yum heue.
20 lMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
Yi-sha nawb buan son ka Ya-keux mai ka Yi-sao son singaix pa sang hoek heue.
21 mMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
yam sang yum Ya-keux,
nawh nawb buan ka piang kawn simex Yaw-seux ra kaux;
nawh gix ka khaox gix tix krub ka Siyiex heue.
22 nMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
yam sang yum Yaw-seux,
nawh krai grawng sang lih hu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Ai-cix ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
khaix an tawx lox grawng sang si paung ka to kaux yum tix heue.
23 oMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
khaix hoik keh Maw-si,
miex keeing nawh yaox pa kied mhawm grang nawh,
mai mox nawh loe khix;
kix ang lhat ka lox tawx simiang heue.
24 Mawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
khaix hoik ting Maw-si,
nawh ang cu tix mawh kawn simex kawn bun simiang Ai-cix. p
25 Nawh cu tix kha rhawm tau mai pui cie Siyiex,
ang cu tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix vud tix cu ka daux phi map.
26 Ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
kham krax pa kaig tua son Mi-sai-yax kawn lheu koe ngawh koe jun khaing sin ngao pa koe ka daux haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik,
mawh kheu kied keud nawh ka khawng phak buan son pa tag sang hoek heue.
27 Mawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
Maw-si hoik lih hu khaing Ai-cix,
ang lhat ka krax pa song rhawm simiang;
mawh kheu hoik yaox nawh ka Siyiex pa ang ngai kawn pui chix yaox,
nawh hoik gun yeung tom si dauh,
mai ang cu tix ing.
28 qMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
nawh yuh ka Poi Luat-luan,
mai tawx lox nhie pui phruah nham ka piang siviex,
son ang sang kieh kawn cau Siyiex pa yuh krax pa laux leeing pauh ka kawn ai pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
29 rMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
pui Yi-seu-liex pon tix tiang ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
kix awm pa hu ka piang tiex kroh yuh nan;
pui Ai-cix cheu tix sang hu,
rawm tom kheem ka kix khawm uik heue.
30 sMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
khaix hoik vung viang pui Yi-seu-liex ka veng Yie-li-keu aliah singaix,
guh veng ghrup.
31 tMawh kheu krax yum rhawm,
La-heux sibun coh kaux ang yaox tix yum tau mai pui pa ang cu nghiet ka Siyiex khankix,
mawh kheu chix kia ngien nawh ka pui pa jiang heue.
32 uAux kawn sang baox krai hu lie?
Aux ang lai koe bawg yam tix krai ka Ci-tiien,
Pa-la,
Chang-suing,
Yie-fu-tha,
Ta-wue,
Sa-mu-eu,
mai ka kaing pa yuh cao jhai khankix lie.
33 vKa daux krax yum rhawm,
kix hoik pon pex ka meung pa koe sivie siveng kix.
Kix yuh pa rauh pa see,
mai hoik pon ka gum lox pa deuh Siyiex.
Kix thox ka daux jheeh sivai sang si,
34 wpreet ka ngu ting yeet,
mai hoik bheeik luan khaing pa pauh ka vaig.
Kix kieh pa iak riang iak rao tieh tix lih mawh pa ting riang ting rao,
koe siawp sibrawm ka bang naing,
mai pon pex ka pui naing tang meung.
35 xKa daux krax yum rhawm,
koe bun tix blah rieb ka pui pa deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum khankix heue.
Koe pui tix blah tieb tie ka krax pa pluat ploi,
cu tix yum ka daux krax ciyang pa kin,
son sang pon ka ju pa khraox pa nie blawn mhawm.
36 yBaox koe nang pui pa kham krax pa map nai pui,
pa si vied pui;
mai koe nang pui pa phrawk pui,
pa laung pui.
37 zKawn pui pa tawk pui yum ka simaox,
pa rid pui dut ka jhi,
pa pauh pui yum ka vaig.
Baox koe pui pa ceep ka hak yung mai hak pex,
to tix hu ku dauh ku dee,
kham ka krax pa hot sibhawm,
pa tuk tiak tuk te,
mai krax pa nieb pui pon yuh tix.
38 Haktiex in ang keung kix paung tix ot ka heue!
Kix awm pui pa si pup krax laux pa vu vie tix ot daux tiag mai ka gawng lua,
mai ot ka daux dawh rang mai ka daux tiex heue.
39 Krax yum rhawm pui in kix hoik mawh pa tiiem pa kied tang siyox tete;
viang mawh nan,
kix ang bax pon ka gum lox pa hoik deuh Siyiex,
40 mawh kheu hoik bot Siyiex tix tawx dee pa nie blawn mhawm ka ex.
Alo nawh mawh:
kix tag sang mawh krax tix nyaing tau mai ex,
tom taug pon tix hoek ka diag tong pa grum mhawm heue.
