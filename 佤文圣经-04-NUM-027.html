Kawn bun Si-law-fe-ha khankix
27
Kawn bun Si-law-fe-ha khankix mawh Ma-lax,
Naw-a,
Heu-la,
Mi-cia,
Teu-sa.
Keeing Si-law-fe-ha mawh Si-fu;
keeing Si-fu mawh Ci-liex;
keeing Ci-liex mawh Ma-ciex;
keeing Ma-ciex mawh Ma-na-si;
keeing Ma-na-si mawh Yaw-seux.
2 Kawn bun Si-law-fe-ha phuan kaux,
hoek ka diang siviex nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
mai cung ka plak sivoe Maw-si mai cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa,
mai ka plak sivoe simiang mai ka plak sivoe kawn pui,
mai ah nin ka kix:
3 “Keeing yix yum ka daux tiag,
mai ang koe kawn simex.
Nawh ang laig tau mai cub caw paox Kheu-la pa tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh yum,
hoik mawh kheu phi map tix.
4 Mawh kheu ang keeing yix koe kawn simex,
tom sang teei tix tik ceu kaux nawh khaing ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex lie?
Kieh yix pon sin ngao tix nyum ka daux pui nyiex pui ma keeing yix khankix liak.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Maw-si krai grawng see kix sidawng ka Simiang Siyiex.
6 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
7 a“Lox mhaing kawn bun Si-law-fe-ha khankix hoik khaw.
Maix guah tix tawx kix koe sin ngao khaing daux pui nyiex pui ma keeing kix khankix,
mai kieh kix deeh plud tix pon ka sin ngao keeing tix.
8 Maix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
phan koe pui tix kaux kaux yum,
mai nawh ang koe kawn simex,
kawn bun nawh khankix tag chix tix plud ka sin ngao nawh.
9 Phan nawh ang koe kawn bun,
pux aik nawh khankix tag chix tix plud kah;
10 Phan nawh ang koe pux aik,
pux aik keeing nawh khankix tag plud kah;
11 phan keeing nawh kawn baox ang koe pux aik,
pui nyiex pa dex ka nawh tag chix tix plud ka sin ngao nawh.
In tag lih tix mawh lox tat jhawm prawng ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
aux tawx lox ka maix yuh nin heue.”
Laug Yaw-su-yax mawh pui pa plud ka Maw-si
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 31.1–8)
12 bSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix haok ka piang gawng Ya-pa-ling,
mai jhak ka haktiex pa sang tawx aux ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
13 Khaix hoik yaox maix kah,
maix tag sang yum,
awm pux ke maix Ya-loeng.
14 Mawh kheu pax hoik lieh lheu ka gum lox aux ka daux tiag Shing.
Yam salao pui hun aux ka haktiex Mi-li-pa,
maix ang bax doh keud ka siawp sibrawm aux plak sivoe ngai kix.”
(Mi-li-pa mawh rawm keet ka Cia-ti-see daux tiag Shing heue.)
15 Maw-si rawg buan ah nin:
16 “Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh nhum ju.
Rawg maix tawx pui tix kaux mawh simiang ka piang pui hun,
17 cmai pon thung kix hu puing ka naing,
son ang pui cie maix sang awm yung pa ang koe cao liang heue.”
18 dSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng mawh pui pa koe rhawm keud rhawm sawn;
maix deuh taix tix ka piang kaing nawh.
19 Maix kieh nawh cung sivoe ngai Yi-li-ya-sa cao giex kaing li mai sivoe ngai kawn pui,
mai krai pao nawh mawh pui pa plud ka maix ka daux kix dee an.
20 Maix guah tix tawx akhuang maix ka nawh,
mai kieh pui hun Yi-seu-liex cu nghiet ka nawh.
21 eNawh tag sang cu nghiet ka lox cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa;
mai Yi-li-ya-sa tag sang siao ‘Wu-ling’ kieh chix tawng ka alo aux.
Yuh nin,
Yi-li-ya-sa tag sang mawh cao pa thung ka kaing pa sang yuh Yaw-su-yax mai pui hun Yi-seu-liex khankix khawm uik.”
22 Maw-si yuh awm lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka nawh.
Nawh kok Yaw-su-yax cung sivoe ngai Yi-li-ya-sa cao giex kaing li mai kawn pui,
23 fmai hawt awm gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
nawh deuh taix tix ka piang kaing Yaw-su-yax,
mai krai pao nawh mawh pui pa plud ka to kaux tix heue.
