Ye-sux kieh pui pon rheng kaux sawm sak
(Lai Ma-thaix 15.32–39)
8
Ang koe miex glang tix,
cub pui hun tix cub khrawm paox tix tau.
Kix ang koe ceu pa ih tid tix;
Ye-sux kok pui gau tix hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
2 “Aux kied caox rhawm caox rhi ka cub pui in kix;
mawh kheu kix hoik ot tau mai aux loe singaix,
yam in ang koe ceu pa ih tid tix.
3 Phan aux kieh kix cai rhawm mai ing tix ka nyiex,
kix tee rien tix goh lawng krax,
kheu kix hoik mawh pui pa kaoh khaing haktiex dee singai tix blah heue.”
4 Pui gau nawh tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Ka dee ang pui koe yuh nin,
ex sang hu sawg priex preem tom kub pui in kix ih sak dee mawx lie?”
5 Ye-sux chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Pex koe khao mun miex ding lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Aliah lon heue.”
6 Ye-sux tawx lox nhie pui hun ngawm piang tiex,
khaix an,
teei khao mun an aliah lon yaog yaw Siyiex,
mai si vit kah,
tawx ka pui gau tix,
pui gau nawh tom guah tix tawx ka pui hun.
7 Kix kawn koe kax iak ra loe mu;
Ye-sux yaog yaw Siyiex,
mai tawx lox nhie pui gau tix guah tix tawx nang ka pui hun.
8-9 Pui hun ih kah,
mai hoik ih sak;
kaux pui pa ih koe been pon rheng kaux.
Pui gau khrawm pa max phawng pa deuk khankix,
hoik naog aliah khrawng.
Ye-sux ploi pui hun prung prang tix hu,
10 dum mawx dum an nawh laig daux reu tau mai pui gau tix hu ka plak haktiex Ta-ma-nu-tha heue.
Pui Fa-li-saix khiox khaw mhai pa daox pa am
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.1–4)
11 aKoe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai sang chawk nawh ka lox si kah.
Kix keud tix sang yuh nawh laux,
khiox khaw nawh yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
son sang jhi grawng kaing pa yuh nawh mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex.
12 bYe-sux sau riang tix ngia tix gum ah nin:
“Pui baux ju singaix in yuh ka mawx lai khiox khaw ka mhai pa daox pa am lie?
Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
pui singaix baux ju in ang keung tix yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am heue!”
13 Nawh tom lih hu khaing kix,
mai baox laig daux reu tiang rawm nhawng hu plak an heue.
Su saix pui Fa-li-saix mai Si-luix
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.5–12)
14 Pui gau hoik piawm tix viex ka khao mun,
ka daux reu koe ngie khao mun tix lon khaing,
mai ang lai koe ka priex preem tang ceu.
15 cYe-sux ne raw ka kix ah nin:
“Tag caw rien rhawm tix,
tag caw rien tix ka su saix cie pui Fa-li-saix mai cie Si-luix heue.”
16 Kix ra lai krai lao ah nin:
“Nawh krai lox in mawh kheu ang ex viex ka khao mun heue.”
17 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka ceu pa keud kix ceu pa tix,
nawh tom chawk kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai krai lao grawng ang khao mun koe lie?
Khex kawn ang pex bax tawng bax yung ka lie?
Dieb dong pex lai daox aw yuh nan lie?
18 dPex koe ngai,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix yaox lie?
Koe yhaok viang mawh nan,
ang chix mhong lie?
Ang pex doh keud ka
19 yam si vit aux tix tawx pui phuan rheng kaux ih ka khao mun phuan lon lie?
Pex khrawm ka pa max pa phawng pa deuk khaix ih pui,
naog miex khrawng lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Kao ra khrawng heue.”
20 Ye-sux baox chawk ah nin:
“Yam si vit aux tix tawx pui pon rheng kaux ih ka khao mun aliah lon,
pex baox sau pa max pa phawng naog miex khrawng lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Aliah khrawm heue.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux chawk kix ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pex kawn ang bax tawng lie?”
Pai ka pui dug ngai Paw-sai-tax moi
22 Kix hoek ka Paw-sai-tax,
koe pui viex pui dug ngai hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux tix kaux,
mai khiox khaw Ye-sux pu pi ka nawh.
23 Ye-sux tuk ka taix pui dug an viex nawh hoek plak praix nyiex yaong,
mai ran baig rawm mia tix ka ngai nawh sivoe,
khaix an deuh taix piang to kaux nawh chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yaox maix ceu pa tix lie?”
24 Nawh yaog kaing tix jhak ah nin:
“Aux yaox pui,
kix awm khaox tix gawng gawng pa hu hu hoek hoek yuh nan heue.”
25 Ye-sux baox deuh taix tix piang ngai nawh.
Bawg in pui dug in dong ngai tix jhak,
ngai nawh riang,
mai hoik chix yaox ku ceu tom sidawng.
26 Ye-sux kieh nawh ing ka nyiex,
mai baox tawx lox ka nawh ah nin:
“Paw vi lai baox laig daux yaong eue”.
Pi-teux tawng Ye-sux mawh Khrit
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.13–20; Lai Lu-cia 9.18–21)
27 Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh hu ka plak yaong dee dex haktiex Khai-sha-li-yax Fe-li-pi.
Ka daux krax,
nawh chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Pui ah mawh aux pui mawx lie?”
28 eKix pawk ah nin:
“Koe tix blah ah mawh maix Yaw-hang cao cub com;
koe tix blah ah mawh maix Yi-li-yax;
mai tix blah ah mawh maix cao jhai tix kaux heue.”
29 fYe-sux baox chawk kix ah nin:
“Pex lie?
Pex ah mawh aux mawx lie?”
Pi-teux pawk ah nin:
“Maix mawh Khrit.”
30 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux tawx lox ang cu kix krai to kaux ka pui tix kaux kawx heue.
Ye-sux grox krai grawng sang kha rhawm tix mai grawng sang yum tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.21–28; Lai Lu-cia 9.22–27)
31 Ye-sux jauh tix sidiex gau ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Kawn kawn pui tag sang kham krax kha rhawm tom nie,
mai kuad song,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix tag sang tik vud ka nawh,
pauh ka nawh,
khaix an loe singaix,
nawh tag sang deeh kaoh im heue.”
32 Nawh krai kaing in kix sidawng khawm uik.
Pi-teux tom bluh Ye-sux hu ka tix plak,
son sang tieb tie ka nawh.
33 Ye-sux kling tix jhak ka pui gau tix,
mai ne raw ka Pi-teux ah nin:
“Sa tan,
yoi heue!
Ceu pa keud maix ang mawh pa keud Siyiex,
hoik mawh pa keud kawn pui heue.”
34 gMawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux kok pui hun mai pui gau tix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix khawm uik,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Phan koe pui pa sang hawt khaix aux,
tag caw tix tawx ka to kaux tix,
mai klawm khaox lhak tix hawt khaix aux.
35 hMawh kheu pui pa keud tix sang taoh ka ju to kaux tix,
tag sang tieh tix som ju;
pui pa som ju son aux mai son gum lox buan,
tag sang tieh tix pon ju.
36 Pui tix kaux kaux phan hoik pon ka haktiex khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
plud ka ju to kaux tix,
koe dee mhawm ceu pa tix lie?
Ang koe heue!
37 Nawh tag sang teei ceu pa tix loh ka ju to kaux tix lie?
38 Ka daux baux ju pa phit vao mai pa caox caig in,
phan koe pui pa een aux mai krax pa sidiex gau aux mawh pa kaig tua,
yam lih hoek kawn kawn pui tau mai keeing tix,
tau mai kawn cau Siyiex khankix ka daux min plak ngai,
tag sang kieh nawh kaig tua nang heue.”
