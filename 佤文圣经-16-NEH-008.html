Yi-see-la phat ka lox tat jhawm prawng
8
Yam hoek daux khix aliah,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik tang nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux veng ot tix.
Khix an ka daux tix ngaix,
kix khrawm paox tix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
dee brawk sivoe Siviex Rawm.
Kix nhie pui pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai cao giex kaing li Yi-see-la viex lox tat jhawm prawng hoek;
lox tat jhawm prawng in hoik mawh pa tawx Simiang Siyiex kieh Maw-si krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-see-la tom viex hoek ka dee khrawm pui hun paox tix;
dee an koe simex koe bun,
kawn koe kawn nyawm pa ting num pa hoik tawng praix khankix.
3 Yi-see-la phat kix mhong lox tat jhawm prawng ka dee pak sivoe Siviex Rawm,
jauh tom bax riang praix hoek tom greung ngaix;
kix hoik bre tix nghiet mhawm mhawm heue.
4 Yi-see-la cung ka piang glawk bawng khaox pa hoik bre kix tix yuh son kaing in.
Pa cung plak dawm nawh mawh Ma-tha-thi-ya,
See-ma,
Ya-nai-ya,
Wu-li-yax,
Si-leu-cia,
mai Ma-si-ya,
pa cung plak goix nawh mawh Pi-ta-ya,
Mi-sa-lix,
Ma-ci-ya,
Ha-soeng,
Ha-pa-ta-na,
Sa-cia-li-yax,
mai Mi-su-lang heue.
5 Yi-see-la cung ka piang glawk dee lhaong,
kix hoik dong ngai tix jhak ka nawh.
Yam paoh nawh lai in,
pui hun kaoh cung khawm uik.
6 Yi-see-la ah nin:
“Yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong heue!”
Can pui khawm uik yaog taix tix,
mai pawk ah nin:
“A-meung!
A-meung!”
Khaix an grux ngawng tix,
mai gup ngai tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
7 Khaix an,
pui haoh pui hun kaoh,
mai cung ka dee ngawm kix sivoe an;
koe pui Li-we tix blah tom kah kix mhong lox tat jhawm prawng.
Pui in kix mawh Yie-su-yax,
Pa-ni,
See-li-pi,
Ya-ming,
Ya-kux,
Sa-pi-thaix,
Heu-ti-ya,
Ma-si-ya,
Ci-li-tha,
Ya-sa-li-ya,
Yaw-sa-pa,
Ha-nang,
mai Pi-lai-ya.
8 Kix krai dee bleeh loxa lai lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiexb ka pui hun ka jheeh tix,
mai baox kah,
son sang kieh pui hun pon tawng heue.
9 Khaix hoik mhong pui hun lox tat jhawm prawng pa tat tix deuh,
mai hoik paoh rhawm rhi kix kieh kix yiiem.
Mawh kheu nan,
Ni-si-mi simiang haktiex,
cao giex kaing li mai pui pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai Yi-see-la,
mai pui Li-we pa kah ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
krai pui hun mhong ah nin:
“Ngaix in mawh singaix Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh singaix pa singax singeh cie Siyiex pex,
pex ang pon tix laux rhawm yiiem grai.
10 Yam in,
pex tang ing khre priex ka nyiex tix!
Guah tix tawx priex cie pex mai plai cie pex ka pui pa ang kub khankix.
Ngaix in mawh singaix pa singax singeh cie cao simiang ex;
pex ang pon tix laux rhawm laux rhi,
mawh kheu krax gaux rhawm pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pex,
hoik mawh riang rao pex heue.”
11 Pui Li-we khu rhawm pui hun,
nhie kix yien yawg,
paw vi laux rhawm,
mawh kheu singaix in hoik mawh singaix pa singax singeh.
12 Pui hun tom ing ka nyiex,
mai ih nyaux ka daux krax pa gaux rhawm,
khaix an mai baox yuh krax gaux rhawm tau mai pui tang kaux ka daux krax pa hoik koe tix khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik tawng kix ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik phat kix mhong heue.
Poi Nyiex-gox-bang
13 Ka daux ra ngaix,
cao kaing ceu cad cub nyiex pui ku cub mai cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we hoek ka Yi-see-la dee an,
kum kawng gum lox lai pa sidiex lox tat jhawm prawng.
14 dKix hoik yaox,
grawng lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex Maw-si tawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
yam yuh Poi Nyiex-gox-bang,c
paung ot ka daux nyiex gox bang pa ran kix tix yuh kah.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tawx lox ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka veng tang dauh plak greem ah nin:e
“Pex tag caw tix lih hu,
hu kid khaox sime,
khaox gix,
khaox mak cang,
khaox jhie yaong,
mai khaox tang ceu khankix ka gawng,
mai hawt tix yuh awm pa krai lox tat jhawm prawng,
teei kak khaox sum tix ot nyiex bang heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
pui haoh pui hun kid tix viex kak khaox hoek tom nie,
mai sum nyiex bang ot ka piang dee lung braux nyiex kix,
ka biang brawk nyiex kix,
ka biang brawk nyiex buan,
khawm ka piang pak Siviex Rawm mai ka piang Yi-fa-liien-meung.
17 Can pui pa hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix hoik sum ka nyiex gox bang khawm uik,
mai paung ot daux.
Jauh tom singaix bawg yam Yaw-su-yax kawn simex Noeng,
in taug mawh pa yuh pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux bawg tix.
Pui ku kaux kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
18 Jauh ka daux poi tix ngaix tom hoek ka singaix pa si dauh,
Yi-see-la phat lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex tix baux ku singaix.
Kix yuh poi aliah singaix,
tom hoek daux daix singaix,
mai hawt tix naox yuh krax gaux rhawm awm pa hoik krai lox tat jhawm prawng heue.
