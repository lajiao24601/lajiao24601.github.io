Veng pui Li-we
21
Simiang ceu pui Li-we khankix hoek ka cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa,
kawn simex Noeng pa mawh Yaw-su-yax,
mai simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui,
ku nyiex khankix,
2 amai ah nin ka kix daux See-law ka haktiex Cia-nang:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx lox ka Maw-si,
son sang tawx veng pa sang ot yix,
mai baox tawx pak rib dee liang yix phung moi.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
teei haktiex khaing cie tix tix blah tawx veng mai pak rib dee liang khawng brie ka pui Li-we heue.
4 Ceu pui Li-we cub nyiex Keu-siax pon ka veng sivoe.
Doeh ceu cad cao giex kaing li cub nyiex Ya-loeng pon ka veng khaing Yo-tax,
Si-miien,
mai Piien-ya-ming kao loe mu veng.
5 Cub nyiex pui Keu-siax tang ceu pon ka veng kao mu khaing haktiex ceu pui Yi-fa-liien,
Tang,
mai Ma-na-si.
6 Cub pui Keu-soeng pon veng kao loe veng khaing haktiex ceu pui Yi-sa-cia,
Ya-seux,
Na-fu-tha-lix,
mai Ma-na-si plak lih singaix ka haktiex Pa-sang heue.
7 Cub pui Mi-la-lix pon tix cie veng kao ra veng khaing haktiex ceu pui Lui-piien,
Cia-teux,
mai Si-pu-loeng heue.
8 Pui Yi-seu-liex hawt ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
siao ka cawk lai mai tawx veng mai dee pak rib ka pui Li-we heue.
9 Veng in kix plak greem mawh pa guah Yo-tax mai Si-miien khaing haktiex tix
10 tawx ka doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng.
Ya-loeng mawh cub nyiex Keu-siax pa mawh ceu pui Li-we.
Kix mawh pa pon veng phaox voe heue.
11 Veng pa pon kix koe Ya-pa (Ya-pa mawh keeing Ya-na),
yam in kaux Si-paw-loeng,
pa koe ka gawng Yo-tax;
kix kawn baox pon ka pak rib hawt vung viang Ya-pa.
12 Viang mawh nan,
kaung mai yaong veng in,
hoik guah tix tawx ka Cia-leux kawn simex Yie-fu-ni,
kieh mawh sin ngao nawh heue.
13 Doeh ceu cad cao giex kaing li Ya-loeng,
lheu khaing pon ka Si-paw-loeng (pa mawh veng mox tix veng),
kawn pon ka veng in kix plak greem Li-na,
14 Ya-thi-eu,
Yi-seu-thi-mawx,
15 Heu-loeng,
Ti-pix,
16 Ya-ying,
Yui-tha,
Paw See-mai,
khawm uik koe dim veng pa mawh cie Yo-tax mai ceu pui Si-miien,
mai pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang.
17 Kix baox pon veng pon mu khaing haktiex ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
pa mawh Ci-piien,
Cia-pa,
18 Ya-na-thu,
mai Ya-leu-meung,
khawm pak rib ot hawt vung viang.
19 Doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li khawm uik pon ka veng kao loe veng,
mai pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng heue.
20 Cub nyiex Keu-siax tang nyiex pa mawh ceu pui Li-we,
pon ka veng tix blah khaing haktiex ceu pui Yi-fa-liien,
21 khawm uik koe pon veng:
See-ciien (veng mox tix veng),
pa ot ka gawng Yi-fa-liien,
Ci-seux,
22 Ci-paw-shien,
mai Paw Heu-loeng,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang.
23 Kix pon ka veng pon veng khaing haktiex ceu pui Tang:
Yi-li-thi-ci,
Ci-pi-toeng,
24 Ya-ya,
mai Cia-theux Ling-meung,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
25 Kix pon ka veng ra veng khaing haktiex ceu pui Ma-na-si,
Tha-na,
mai Cia-theux Ling-meung,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
26 Cub nyiex Keu-siax ku nyiex khawm uik pon ka veng kao veng,
mai pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng heue.
27 Cub pui Keu-soeng khaing daux doeh nyiex ma ceu pui Li-we pon ka veng ra veng khaing haktiex ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singaix:
veng Keu-lang (veng mox tix veng),
pa koe ka haktiex Pa-sang mai veng Pi See-thi-la,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang.
28 Kix pon ka veng khaing ceu pui Yi-sa-cia pon veng:
Ci-sang,
Ta-pi-la,
29 Yie-maw,
Yin Kang-niang,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
30 Kix pon ka veng pon veng khaing haktiex ceu pui Ya-seux:
Mi-sa-leux,
Ya-toeng,
31 He-cia,
mai Li-haw,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
32 Kix pon ka veng loe veng khaing haktiex ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix:
Ci-ti-see (veng mox tix veng) ka daux haktiex Cia-li-lix,
Ha-maw To-eux,
mai Cia-eu-thang,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
33 Cub nyiex Keu-soeng ku nyiex khawm uik pon ka veng kao loe veng,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
34 Cub nyiex Mi-la-lix,
ceu pui Li-we pa kawn deuk,
pon ka veng pon mu khaing haktiex ceu pui Si-pu-loeng:
Yaw-nien,
Cia-eu-tha,
35 Ting-nax,
mai Na-ha-la,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
36 Kix pon ka veng pon veng mai khaing ceu pui Lui-piien:
Pi-si,
Ya-cax,
37 Ci-ti-maw,
mai Mi-fa-ya,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
38 Kix pon ka veng pon veng khaing daux haktiex ceu pui Cia-teux:
La-mawx (veng mox tix veng) ka haktiex Ci-liex,
Ma-ha-nian,
39 Si-seu-peung,
mai Ya-shiex,
khawm pak rib pa ot hawt vung viang veng.
40 Cub nyiex Mi-la-lix khawm uik pon ka veng kao ra veng heue.
41-42 Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik guah veng khaing haktiex tix tipon daix veng,
khawm pak rib hawt vung viang veng tawx ka pui Li-we heue.
Haktiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex
43 Simiang Siyiex hawt ka lox pa ap tix ka tax du pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx haktiex khawm uik ka kix.
Yam pon rhim ka haktiex an,
tom ot ka dee an.
44 Simiang Siyiex tawx haktiex kix khawm uik ang koe krax tuk tiak tuk te,
awm pa hoik ap nawh ka tax du kix.
Pui pa koeng ka kix ang koe pui tix kaux pon pex ka kix,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx can pui koeng khawm uik daux tawm taix kix.
45 Simiang Siyiex kieh lox pa ap tix kaux cong ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
