Lox rawg buan ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhia
102
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan;
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa kok yo teem coi ka aux.
2 Singaix yam krax aux rhawm tix,
khiox khaw maix paw vi mox tix ang simi ka aux.
Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa rawg aux;
yam khiox khaw aux ka maix,
mai nied tix pawk ka lox aux heue.
 
3 Ju aux awm taox ngu pa grai hu;
to kaux aux awm mawk ngu pa cap.
4 Rhawm rhi aux awm rib kroh pa hoik dig phawng;
pie priex preem kawx aux ang lai keud tix ih.
5 Aux riag yo mai ngia;
to kaux aux khawm uik deuk ngie hak pa kawb si-ang khaing.
6 Aux awm sim ka daux tiag daux tu;
aux awm pang plawh ka dee riem.
7 Aux ang laig it laig tom;
aux awm saik tix mu ka piang choek nyiex pa ang koe paox koe pie.
8 Pui pa koeng ka aux,
ah lox pa ang khaw ka aux leeing lang singaix;
pui pa nu nai ka aux,
jhax lawg ka daux ceu kaux aux heue.
 
9-10 Kheu krax pa song rhawm song rhi maix,
aux ih ka nyaox mawk ngu,
awm sawm aux yuh nan,
aux nyaux ka rawm tie tau mai rawm ngai tix.
Maix yaog ka aux,
mai si blat tix tik ka aux.
11 Ju aux awm pang peue yam faik boi praix yuh nan;
aux hoik awm rib kroh yuh nan heue.
 
12 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix giex ka kaing leeing ju;
tix ju hoek tix ju tag sang doh keud ka maix.
13 Maix tag caw tix kaoh,
tag caw tix caox rhawm ka Shi-ang;
mawh kheu bawg yam pa caox rhawm maix ka nawh hoik hoek,
yam in hoik mawh bawg yam pa khaw.
14 Viang Shi-ang hoik laux leeing,
siliex maix kawn muh ka nawh;
viang nawh hoik lih mawh dee riem,
mai kix ca kawn caox rhawm ka nawh heue.
 
15 Meung ka piang haktiex tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex;
simiang ka piang haktiex tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka siawp sibrawm nawh.
16 Yam deeh cox sum Simiang Siyiex ka Shi-ang,
tag sang jhi min plak ngai nawh lih.
17 Nawh tag sang nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw pui hot pui sibhawm;
nawh ang chix nieb pon kieh lox rawg kix buan ang koe ceu heue.
 
18 Tag caw tix tiiem ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex lih son doeh ceu cad ju phaox khaix,
kieh pui pa ang nyang bax keh tawng tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
19 Simiang Siyiex kok jhak nyiex pa singax khaing dee kied lhaong;
nawh kok jhak kawn pui khaing rao ma.
20 Nawh nghiet ka lox pa yiiem riag pui pa laung;
nawh pluat ploi ka pui pa prawng ca kieh yum.
21 Yuh nin,
kawn pui tag sang sidiex gau ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex ka Shi-ang;
kix tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw nawh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
22 Singaix yam an,
meung ka piang haktiex tag sang khrawm tix ot tau;
kawn pui ka piang haktiex tag sang rom tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
23 Yam kawn nhum ju koe riang aux,
Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh aux iak riang iak rao;
nawh hoik kieh ju aux dawt.
24 Siyiex aux eue,
paw vi kieh aux yum yam nhum ju heue.
 
Simiang Siyiex eue,
krax pa im ju maix ang chix die chix uik heue.
25 bMaix hoik phan haktiex tom yam jauh dix dix;
maix baox phan ka rao ma heue.
26 Rao ma mai haktiex tag sang grai,
viang mawh nan,
maix tag sang koe leeing ju leeing phan;
rao ma haktiex tag sang griah awm kraung ceep.
Maix tag sang loh ka haktiex mai rao ma,
awm loh ka kraung ceep yuh nan;
rao ma mai haktiex tag sang grai.
27 Viang mawh nan,
maix ca mawh nan tom si dauh;
krax pa im ju maix ang chix die chix uik.
28 Kawn praix yix tag sang pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe;
doeh ceu cad kix tag sang pon krax pa haok huan ka daux pa kia ngien maix heue.
