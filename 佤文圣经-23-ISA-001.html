Lai Yi-sai-yax
1
aYam mawh Wu-si-ya,
Yaw-thang,
Ya-ha-see,
mai Si-si-cia simiang pui Yo-tax,
Siyiex plak jhi grawng kaing pa dex pa dawt mai meung Yo-tax,
mai veng Yie-lu-sa-leung ka Yi-sai-yax kawn simex Ya-maw-see.
Pa hoik tiiem phuk lai in tix deuh mawh gum lox pa hoik pon Yi-sai-yax heue.
Siyiex ne raw ka pui cie tix
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Rao ma eue,
haktiex eue,
hoek tix nghiet khawm uik heue!
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex krai pao yuh nin:
kawn praix pa jawg aux tix eei ting taix lhaong caong yuh ka mawx lai tieh vheen ka aux.
3 Moi tawng cao tix,
la tawng dawng pa tawx cao nawh tix sawm kah,
viang mawh nan,
pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex ang tawng ka aux;
pui cie aux ang tawng tid tix heue.”
4 Meung pa yuh phi map in,
pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig in,
pex tag sang yaox krax laux leeing heue!
Pex hoik tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex,
hoik map nai ka Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh;
yuh ka mawx pex lai tieh vheen ka nawh.
5 Yuh ka mawx pex lai ca baox deeh tieh vheen ka nawh lie?
Krax ciyang pa kham pex kawn ang nyang bax kub lie?
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
kaing pex doh,
nham pex jauh,
rhawm rhi pex mai dieb dong pex hoik koe jhawm saux khawm uik.
6 Jauh ka kaing hoek ka caong,
nex nham ang grum naog,
to kaux uah,
mai rhoi khaix mat hoik leem khawm uik.
Ang koe pui pa teem pex si ghrah,
kie ka man,
mai ang koe pui cao teem pex kraix sidah ka heue.
7 Haktiex ot pex tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag riem;
veng ot pex lih mawh nyaox nyia.
Ngai pex hoik yaox grawng pa hoek pui tang ceu tix puing ka pex,
mai yuh ka sin ngao pex laux leeing.
8 Mawh ngie veng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa deuk khaing,
pie veng in kawx pui hoik lai lawm kah,
awm nyiex gox pa ot pui pa pau si peem plix siiet,
awm nyiex gox ka daux ma ke,
ang koe krax pa kaux cong pie ceeix kawx.
9 bPhan Simiang Siyiex pa mawh kru naing kaing mheen ang deuh tix een ju pui tix blah,
mai veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik dawg laux leeing awm Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la yuh nan heue.
10 Veng Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
simiang pex mai kawn pui khankix hoik awm simiang mai kawn pui veng Saw-to-ma mai veng Eu-maw-la yuh nan.
Pex caw tix nghiet ka lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka pex,
mai priex krax pa sidiex gau Siyiex ka pex.
11 cNawh ah nin:
“Khex ce pex aux sang khaw rhawm ka ceu pa tan lu pex tom nie khankix lie?
Aux ang yuh tid tix ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing pex ka yung mai ka bee khawng brie pa kluing;
aux ang rhawm yaox tan lu pex ka moi,
yung,
mai nham pex khankix.
12 Yam krub pex ka aux,
mawx pa nhie pex viex khawng brie in kix hoek lie?
Mawx pa nhie pex viex ceu in kix hoek seeing brai yuh nyiex buan aux lie?
13 Aux ang lo ka khawng phak pa tan pex in kix,
pa ang koe ceu ceeix kawx;
aux ang lai baox lo tix ngheet ka nyawng pa gleeing pex.
Aux ang lai pon gun yeung grawng pa khrawm pex paox tix yuh krax tan lu ka daux pa haok khix,
ka daux singaix tuah pex,
mai ka daux been li pex;
grawng krax pa krub in kix hoik ang lai koe ceu koe cad kheu phi map pex.
14 Aux kied ang rhawm yaox ka krax tan lu pa yuh pex yam haok khix,
mai ka daux poi been li ku ceu;
in kix hoik lih tix mawh beeh glawm pa cien ka piang aux,
mai aux ang lai rhawm klawm ka heue.
15 “Yam yaog pex taix tix rawg buan,
mai aux ang nghiet kah;
viang pex hoik rawg buan miex ding kawx,
mai aux ang doh nghiet kah;
mawh kheu taix pex khawm ra khawn hoik naog ka nham pui.
16 Pex caw tix si ghrah to kaux tix singax,
mai paw vi kieh aux baox yaox ka grawng pa yuh pex ka phi map,
tag caw tix naox yuh phi map dum mawx dum an heue!
17 Pex caw tix gau ka krax pa khaw caw,
yuh pa rauh pa see,
teem coi ka pui pa gut kheem,
priex mhawm ka kawn doe,
mai teem coi ka miex mai krai lox heue.”
18 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Hoek heue,
ex hoek tix lao lox si kah heue!
Viang phi map pex hoik raoh awm nham,
mai aux sang kieh pex paing awm rhad.
Viang phi map pex hoik raoh awm nham,
mai aux sang kieh pex singax paing awm hauk yung.e
19 Phan pex cu nghiet ka aux,
pex tag sang yaox tix ih ka sin ngao pa blawn mhawm ka piang haktiex.
20 Phan pex tieb tie ka aux,
tieh vheen ka aux,
tag sang laux leeing ka greem lawm vaig.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Veng phi map
21 Veng in hoik mawh veng pa singax singeh singaix pa hoik luan,
yam in mai hoik lih tix mawh awm sibun coh kaux yuh nin!
Singaix pa hoik luan,
pui pa ot ka daux hoik mawh pui rauh pui see,
yam in mawh ngie pui pa pauh pui yum pa koe ka daux khaing.
22 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
maix hoik dawg mawh pa awm mau pa singax,
yam in ang lai keung tix mawh mau pie tix mit;
singaix pa hoik luan maix awm plai pa nyawm,
yam in maix hoik tieh tix lih mawh rawm ceet pa ang koe jhawm nyawm.
23 Simiang cao giex kaing pex mawh pui tieh pui vheen,
pa yuh cub caw tau mai pui briex pui lak,
mai kix ku kaux simie tix cie ka sin ngao pa ang rauh ang see,
mai rieb ka mau chun praok;
ang sax teem coi ka kawn doe,
ang sax prawng rauh see son miex mai heue.
24 Yam in,
pex khaw tix nghiet ka lox simiang pa ting lhaong ka piang khawm uik,
pa mawh gum lox pa hoik krai simiang kru naing kaing mheen,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa ting siawp sibrawm.
Nawh ah nin:
“Krox jun heue,
pui pa koeng ka aux,
aux sang pawk bix;
aux sang pawk bix.
25 Aux sang tieb tie ka pex heue.
Aux sang ceed ka pex,
awm raug pui ka khri yuh nan,
mai sang teei tix tik ka pa seeing pa brai pex hu khawm uik.
26 Aux tag sang baox tawx riang rao ka simiang caox giex kaing pex,
mai ka cao pa teem coi pex.
Yuh nin,
veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tom taug chix kok mawh veng rauh veng see,
mai veng kaux cong heue.”
27 Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh pa khaw caw mai pa rauh pa see,
nawh tag sang taoh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka can pui pa ot daux veng pa hoik loh ka rhawm rhi tix khawm uik;
28 viang mawh nan,
pui pa yuh phi map mai pui pa tieh vheen,
nawh tag sang yuh kix laux leeing;
pui pa tik vud ka nawh,
mai nawh tag sang yuh kix laux leeing heue.
29 Si peem pa paih pex pui in kix,f
pa hoik ah pex mawh si peem pa singax singeh,
mai pui pa baox teei khaox ox kieh mawh Siyiex krub tix,
tag sang pon krax kaig krax tua.
30 Pex tag sang kroh sang rham,
awm khaox pa hoik yum yuh nan,
awm si peem pa ang yaox tix caux ka rawm yuh nan.
31 Khaox kroh khaox rham phan yaox ka ngu tag sang cap,
ka daux pex pui pa koe siawp sibrawm kawx tag sang yuh nan;
kix hoik laux leeing kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa hoik yuh to kaux tix,
mai ang koe pui pa pon taoh ka kix heue.
