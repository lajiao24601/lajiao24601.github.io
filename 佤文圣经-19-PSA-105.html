Siyiex mai pui cie nawh
(Lai Been Phuk Tix 16.8–22)
105
Pex tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai krai pao grawng pa ting pa lhaong nawh;
tag caw tix krai lao kaing pa yuh nawh ka meung piang haktiex.
2 Pex tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
tag caw tix krai lao ka can kaing pa daox pa deng yuh nawh.
3 Pex tag caw tix yaog yaw ka min ceu kaux pa singax nawh;
kieh can pui pa sawg tix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex pon krax gaux rhawm.
4 Tag caw tix khiox khaw krax teem krax coi Simiang Siyiex;
tag caw tix krub ka nawh jheeng jheeng.
5-6 Siliex Siyiex doeh ceu cad Ya-paw-la-hang eue,
kawn praix Ya-keux pa hoik laug nawh ka heue,
pex tag caw tix doh keud ka kaing pa daox pa deng yuh nawh,
tag caw tix doh keud ka krax prawng krax ca pa hoik yuh nawh heue.
 
7 Nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex,
mawh Siyiex ex;
siawp sibrawm nawh hoik tauh ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
8 Nawh tag sang giex kaux cong ka sijax mukheu tix leeing ju;
gum lox pa hoik ap nawh tix deuh tag sang koe kaing rheng ju.
9 aNawh tag sang giex kaux cong ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tix tau mai Ya-paw-la-hang,
giex tom kaux cong ka lox pa hoik tiex tu tix ka Yi-sha.
10 bSimiang Siyiex mai pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh ka sijax mukheu pa koe leeing ju,
hoik tat tix deuh jhawm prawng tau mai Ya-keux leeing ju.
11 Nawh ah nin:
aux tag sang tawx haktiex Cia-nang ka maix,
kieh mawh sin ngao son to kaux maix heue.
 
12 Kaux pui cie Siyiex kied tux,
kix ot ka haktiex Cia-nang.
13 Kix jauh ka haktiex in mai vu vie tix hoek ka haktiex an,
jauh ka meung in vu vie tix hoek ka meung an.
14 cViang mawh nan,
Siyiex ang kieh pui tix kaux kawx map nai ka kix;
mawh kheu son kix mai nawh hoik ne raw ka simiang.
15 Nawh ah nin:
ang pon tix khin ka siliex pa hoik laug aux;
ang pon tix yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka cao jhai cie aux heue.
 
16 dSimiang Siyiex kieh meung haktiex kix si pup ka sibhawm praix,
hoik klat ka priex preem pa koe kix.
17 eViang mawh nan,
nawh ploi Yaw-seux mawh cao pa rien khre ka kix sivoe;
coh Yaw-seux mawh siliex cawng pui.
18 fCaong nawh khawm ra khawn hoik sau gang kah,
ngawg nawh hoik sau viex ka gang rhiem,
19 tom hoek lih lox jhai pa krai nawh tix mawh.
Gum lox Simiang Siyiex hoik kham sakse ka grawng pa mawh pa meh nawh.
20 gMawh kheu nan,
simiang Ai-cix tawx lox kieh pui deeh ploi ka nawh;
simiang pa kuang meung ka piang haktiex hoik ploi ka nawh,
21 htawx nawh mawh cao pa kuang ka kaing nyiex simiang,
kieh nawh mawh cao pa kuang ka piang meung khawm uik,
22 tawx nawh kuang piang simiang pa ot plak greem,
tawx nawh koe akhuang mawh cao pa thung pui kuad ju.
 
23 iKhaix an Ya-keux hoik hoek ka haktiex Ai-cix,
mai paung ot dee an heue.
24 jSimiang Siyiex kieh kaux pui pa mawh pui cie tix haoh hun,
kieh kix kawn lheu ting riang ting rao khaing pui koeng.
25 Nawh kieh pui Ai-cix koeng khoi ka pui cie tix,
yuh krax keud pa pie lhien ka siliex nawh heue.
 
26 kMawh kheu nan,
nawh ploi siliex tix Maw-si hu,
baox ploi ka Ya-loeng pui pa hoik laug nawh.
27 Kix hawt tix yuh ka kaing pa ting pa lhaong cie Siyiex,
jhi mhai pa daox pa am lih ka Ai-cix,
28 lSiyiex kieh pa faik kheem ka piang tiex,
pui Ai-cix ang nghietm ka lox tawx nawh.
29 nNawh kieh rawm klawng pa koe ka daux haktiex kix tieh tix lih mawh nham,
hoik kieh can kax pa koe daux rawm yum khawm uik.
30 oKhiet hoik naog ka daux meung kix ku dauh ku dee,
pie ka daux nyiex haw kawx hoik naog kah.
31 pSiyiex tawx lox tix,
mai phung roi pu tix hoek,
maing hoik naog ka daux haktiex khawm pon daog.
32 qNawh ploi mu pre lih mawh rawm lhiex,
kieh pu plak praix si dauh ka haktiex khawm uik.
33 Nawh hoik yuh laux ka nhu khaox plix siiet mai khaox ruh,
cot goh ka can khaox ox pa koe ka daux haktiex.
34 rNawh ang ka jheeh tix,
mai phung sido pu tix hoek,
lih mawh kaing rheng kaing mheen,
ang lai pon uik simin.
35 Kix hoik ih uik ka taux lhax pa koe ka daux haktiex,
hoik bleet ka can nghox koi pa koe khawm uik.
36 sNawh pauh ka kawn ai pui nyiex ma pa ot daux haktiex ku nyiex,
hoik pauh ka can ceu pa liang tix keh khawm uik heue.
 
37 tKhaix an nawh thung pui Yi-seu-liex viex khri mau lih hu;
kix hoik mhawm nex nham ku kaux.
38 Yam lih hu kix,
pui Ai-cix kied sidaing gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu hoik lhat kix ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
39 uSiyiex kieh pai awm tawb kheem ka pui cie tix,
kieh ngu jhan riang piang kix.
40 wKixv rawg mhaing,
mai nawh tom tawx keed pu tix lih hoek;
nawh ploi priex preem lih khaing rao ma eei kix sak kah.
41 xNawh lauh ka rang simaox baoh,
mai kieh rawm buing tix lih khaing;
nawh kieh daux tiag daux tu pa kroh lih mawh rawm klawng.
42 Mawh kheu nawh hoik doh keud ka lox pa ap tix,
pa mawh gum lox pa hoik ap nawh tix ah ka siliex tix Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
 
43 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh viex pui cie tix lih hu ka daux krax pa gaux rhawm;
can pui cie nawh pa hoik laug nawh hoik gaux rhawm tix rhax lai.
44 yNawh teei tix tawx haktiex cie pui tang ceu ka kix,
kieh kix plud tix yuh ka kaung ma cie pui tang kaux,
45 mawh son sang kieh pui cie tix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng cie tix,
hawt tix yuh ka gum lox ping nyap nawh khawm uik heue.
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
