Lai Ma-kheu
Yaw-hang cao cub com sidiex
(Lai Ma-thaix 3.1–12; Lai Lu-cia 3.1–18; Lai Yaw-hang 1.19–28)
1
Kawn simex Siyiex,
agum lox buan Ye-sux Khrit yam jauh tix mawh nin.
2 bCao jhai Yi-sai-yax hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai nawh ah nin:
Siyiex ah nin:
jhak heue,
aux tag sang ploi kawn cau tix hu;
nawh tag sang mawh pa hu sivoe maix,
paoh daux krax son maix.
3 cKoe pui kok yo ka daux tiag daux tu ah nin:
cox khre daux krax son Simiang Ye-sux,
mai cox rauh see ka krax pa sang lawng nawh heue!
4 Rug mawh,
Yaw-hang lih bleeh ka daux tiag,
mai cub com pui ka rawm,
khaix an krai paod ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
rieb ka krax pa cub com,
mai Siyiex tag sang pluat ploi ka phi map pex.”
5 Pui hun kaoh khaing veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai haktiex Yo-thaix ku dauh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Yaw-hang.
Kix doh keud ka phi map tix,
Yaw-hang tom cub com kix ka daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue.
6 eYaw-hang ceep ka kraung hauk ka-la-uk,
mai mad nyoi tix ka maox hak;
pa ih nawh mawh sido mai rawm hia.
7 Nawh krai pao ah nin:
“Kaw pa sang hoek khaix aux an kawn lheu ting khaing aux,
pie nyah aux tix kah ka maox kiiep nawh kawx aux ang keung kah.
8 Aux teei rawm cub com pex kah,
viang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang teei Khuan Singax Singeh cub com pex ka heue.”
Ye-sux cub com mai kham krax nyaw krax glaw
(Lai Ma-thaix 3.13–14.11; Lai Lu-cia 3.21–22; 4.1–13)
9 Ang koe miex glang tix,
Ye-sux hoek khaing Cia-li-lix haktiex Na-sha-leux,
Yaw-hang tom cub com nawh ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
10 Yam lih Ye-sux khaing daux rawm,
nawh yaox rao ma hoik baoh,
Khuan Singax Singeh awm si kaux pa lih hoek ka piang to kaux nawh.
11 fLox lih hoek khaing rao ma ah nin:
“Maix mawh kawn simex pa muh aux,
aux khaw rhawm ka maix heue.”
12 Khuan Singax Singeh dhiet Ye-sux hu ka daux tiag daux tu dum mawx dum an.
13 Nawh ot dee an tipon singaix,
mai kham krax pa nyaw glaw Sa tan;
nawh ot tau mai to sat,
viang mawh nan,
koe kawn cau Siyiex kia ngien ka nawh heue.
Ye-sux kok ka pui pa rub kax pon kaux
(Lai Ma-thaix 4.12–22; Lai Lu-cia 4.14–15; 5.1–11)
14 Khaix hoik giex laung pui Yaw-hang laig daux thawng,
Ye-sux hoek ka haktiex Cia-li-lix,
mai krai pao ka gum lox buan Siyiex.
15 gNawh ah nin:
“Bawg yam hoik hoek,
meung Siyiex hoik dex sang hoek heue!
Pex tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai yum rhawm hawt ka gum lox buan heue.”
16 Ye-sux hu hawt ka praok rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix,
mai yaox pui rub kax ra kaux pa mawh Si-meung mai pux aik nawh Ang-teu-liex,
rub kax ka piang nhawng.
17 Ye-sux ah nin ka kiex:
“Hawt khaix aux heue!
Aux tag sang kieh pax lih mawh pui rug kax pa pon ka pui heue.”
18 Kix tik rub dum mawx dum an,
mai hawt khaix nawh heue.
19 Ang bax hu singai,
Ye-sux yaox Ya-keux kawn simex Si-pi-thaix mai pux aik nawh Yaw-hang;
kix ngawm tix cox rub ka piang reu.
20 Ye-sux kok ka kix,
kix tom prah keeing tix mai pui cang ot daux reu,
mai hawt khaix Ye-sux heue.
Pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai ka daux tix kaux
(Lai Lu-cia 4.31–37)
21 Kix hoek ka veng Cia-pai-nong;
yam hoek ka daux singaix tuah,
Ye-sux laig sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex sidiex.
22 hPui pa mhong lox pa sidiex gau Ye-sux kied daox deng kah,
mawh kheu krax pa sidiex gau nawh kied koe siawp sibrawm,
mai ang awm pa sidiex gau sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix yuh nan heue.
23 Yam in,
koe pui pa laig khuan pa seeing brai daux tix kaux hoek ka nyiex sidiex,
kok yo ah nin:
24 “Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tuk tiak ka yix lie?
Sang hoek maix tix yuh yix laux leeing lie?
Aux hoik tawng mawh maix pui mawx;
maix mawh pui pa singax cie Siyiex heue!”
25 Ye-sux tawx lox ka khuan pa seeing brai ah nin:
“Paw nye,
nied lih khaing to kaux pui in heue!”
26 Khuan pa seeing brai kieh pui an rak rawn tix,
kok yo tix gum,
khaix an lih hu khaing pui an.
27 Pui hun kied daox deng kah,
mai briex su shiep ah nin:
“In mawh kaing ceu pa tix lie?
Grawng kaing pa yuh nin mawh ceu pa ang ex sax mhong heue!
Nawh hoik koe akhuang tix kuang ka khuan pa seeing brai,
mai khuan pa seeing brai kawx hoik nghiet ka nawh heue!”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu kaux Ye-sux hoik tauh hu ka haktiex Cia-li-lix khawm uik heue.
Ye-sux pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux moi tom nie
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.14–17; Lai Lu-cia 4.38–41)
29 Kix lih hu khaing nyiex sidiex,
mai hoek ka nyiex Si-meung mai ka nyiex Ang-teu-liex;
Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang kawx hu tau mai nawh.
30 Miex moeng Si-meung jiet saux si aoh,
it ka piang ku;
yam hoek Ye-sux,
kix krai Ye-sux mhong grawng jhawm saux nawh.
31 Ye-sux hu jhak ka nawh,
bluh ka taix nawh,
mai yaog nawh kaoh.
Saux si aoh nawh moi,
nawh tom kaoh rien khre ka kix heue.
32 Plak pon bo,
yam laig singaix khaing gawng,
koe pui tix blah viex pui pa koe jhawm saux ku ceu mai pui pa laig phi daux hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux khawm uik;
33 pui pa ot daux veng khawm uik kawx hoek nang,
khrawm paox tix ot ka sivoe diang siviex.
34 Ye-sux pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux ku ceu moi,
mai grheu nang ka phi hu tom nie.
Nawh ang cu phi si kah,
mawh kheu hoik tawng kix mawh nawh pui mawx heue.
Sidiex gau ka Cia-li-lix
(Lai Lu-cia 4.42–44)
35 Ka daux singaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
sivoe sang riang praix,
Ye-sux kaoh lih hu khaing nyiex,
hoek ka haktiex dee riem tix dauh,
mai rawg buan dee an.
36 Si-meung mai paox pa hu tau mai nawh hu sawg ka nawh;
37 yaox ka nawh,
kix tom ah nin:
“Ex hoik sawg ka maix heue!”
38 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Ex hu ka nyiex yaong pa dex dee in heue,
aux kawx tag sang tawx lox buan ka daux haktiex dee an khankix,
kheu in hoik mawh kaing ting grawh grawng pa hoek aux heue.”
39 iMawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux tauh hu ka haktiex Cia-li-lix khawm uik,
mai tawx lox buan ka nyiex sidiex ku dauh,
khaix an grheu ka phi heue.
Ye-sux pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.1–4; Lai Lu-cia 5.12–16)
40 Koe pui pa koe jhawm saux si pawg tix kaux hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux,
grux ngawng tix khiox khaw ka nawh ah nin:
“Ca mawh cu maix,
maix pon kieh aux singax heue.”
41 Ye-sux hoik chu ka rhawm pa mox tix,j
mai tawx taix tix giex ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux cu,
kieh maix singax heue!”
42 Jhawm saux si pawg pa koe ka to kaux nawh lih hu khaing nawh dum mawx dum an,
nawh tom singax singeh.
43 Ye-sux tawx nawh hu dum mawx dum an,
mai krai ka nawh tom sidawng
44 kah nin:
“Paw vi krai ka pui tix kaux kawx.
Hu ka cao giex kaing li,
kieh nawh lam lia ka maix,
khaix an hawt tix tan lu awm been pa hoik tat Maw-si tix deuh yuh nan,
mai jhi grawng pa hoik singax singeh maix ka pui hun khankix heue.”
45 Viang mawh nan,
yam lih hu pui in,
nawh hoik krai grawng kaing in hu ku dauh,
mai kieh Ye-sux ang lai pon kieh pui tawng tix yam laig daux veng,
kheu nan nawh ca paung ot ka plak praix veng dee yien heue.
Pui hun ca kawn hoek tix sawg nawh khaing haktiex ku dauh ku dee heue.
