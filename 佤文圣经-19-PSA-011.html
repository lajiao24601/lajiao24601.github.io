Yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex
11
Aux kieh Simiang Siyiex mawh nyiex dee mox aux tix.
Yuh pa tix pex lai krai lox daox aw ka aux ah nin:
tag sang awm kawn sim pa pu laig ka daux gawnga,
2 mawh kheu pui pa caox pa caig vaik ak sau te,
tag sang pauh pui rauh pui see ka daux pa faik.
3 Phan riah hoik laux leeing,
pui rauh pui see kawn pon yuh ceu pa tix?
 
4 Simiang Siyiex ot ka daux nyiex buan nawh,
dang ngeung nawh sum tix ot ka piang rao ma.
Nawh lam lia ka kawn pui haktiex,
mai hoik jhak ka can pa yuh kix khawm uik.
5 Nawh cheuk tix yaox ka pui rauh pui see mai ka pui pa caox pa caig;
nawh yum koeng ka pui pa rien tud rhim ka pui khankix heue.
 
6 Nawh ploi gaox ngub mai mhat lih ka piang pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig;
nawh teei rao ngu pa hax mai ciyang ka kix.
7 Simiang Siyiex mawh pa khaw caw,
mai nawh muh ka krax pa rauh pa see;
pui pa rauh pa see pon tix yaox ka nawh heue.
