Yaw-su-yax ploi pui jiang laig ka Yie-li-keu
2
aKawn simex Noeng pa mawh Yaw-su-yax ploi pui jiang ra kaux lih hu khaing See-thing,
kieh kix briex hu jiang ka haktiex Cia-nang,
mai jiang krax kaux cong veng Yie-li-keu.
Kix hoek ka daux veng,
mai it ka daux nyiex sibun coh kaux pa kaux La-heux.
2 Simiang Yie-li-keu mhong koe pui Yi-seu-liex hoek tix jiang ka daux veng bo an,
3 tom ploi pui hu ah nin ka La-heux:
“Pui pa ot ka daux nyiex maix mawh pa hoek tix jiang ka meung haktiex,
maix tawx kix lih khaing!”
4-6 La-heux ah nin:
“Mawh tete,
koe pui hoek ka aux dee in,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang tawng kaoh kix khaing dee mawx.
Sivoe sang klip singaix,
ang siviex veng nyang nyip tix kix hoik lih hu.
Aux ang bax chawk kix hu kix ka dee mawx;
phan pex nied tix hu leue kix,
pex tag chix krup ka kix.”
(Tete,
La-heux hoik viex pui jiang an kiex haok piang nyiex,
mai mox kiex ka daux greem nyhee khaox gan.)
7 Yam lih hu pui pa ploi simiang khaing Yie-li-keu,
siviex veng tag nyip tix.
Pui an kix sawg ka pui jiang Yi-seu-liex,
tom hoek ka diang tiang rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue.
8 Sivoe ang pui jiang an kiex nyang laig it,
La-heux haok ka piang braux nyiex,
9 mai ah nin ka kiex:
“Aux tawng Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx haktiex in ka pex;
pui ot ka haktiex in ku kaux lhat graung ka pex.
10 bYix hoik mhong,
yam lih pex khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
Simiang Siyiex kieh yuh ka mawx Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh hit sivoe pex,
mai baox hoik mhong,
pex pauh yuh ka mawx ka simiang Ya-maw-lix ra kaux pa kaux Si-hong mai Eux ka plak lih singaix toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
11 Yam hoik mhong yix ka grawng kaing in kied lhat kah,
yam keud ka pex kied lhat graung kah,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex mawh Siyiex ka piang haktiex rao ma.
12 Yam in,
khiox khaw pax tiex tu ka Siyiex in ka aux,
pax jhi krax caox rhawm caox rhi ka nyiex ma aux,
awm hoik jhi aux pax yuh nan.
Tawx mhai sakse ka aux,
son sang yum rhawm aux ka pax.
13 Khiox khaw pax pluat ploi ka miex keeing aux,
pux aik pux o aux mai can ceu pa koe ka nyiex kix,
ang kieh kix yaox krax pa laux leeing.”
14 Pui an kiex pawk lox:
“Phan maix ang kieh kaing pa mox yiex lih,
yiex cu ka lox maix.
Yam tawx Simiang Siyiex haktiex in ka yix,
yix tag keud krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi ka maix.
Phan yix ang hawt ka lox tix,
kieh Siyiex teei ka juc yiex heue.”
15 Nyiex cie La-heux sum ot ka piang ding veng,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh kieh pui jiang an kiex ra kaux lih ka maox khaing daux doh.
16 Nawh ah nin:
“Pax to mox tix ka piang gawng;
phan ang mawh nan,
pui pa leue tag chix yaox ka pax.
Pax mox tix dee an loe singaix,
krox kix deeh ing ka daux veng,
pax taug hu krax.”
17 Kiex ah nin ka La-heux:
“Yiex tag hawt tix kieh mawh lox pa nhie maix yiex tiex tu,
18 viang mawh nan,
maix tag hawt yuh ka lox pa krai yiex.
Bawg yam pon yix ka haktiex cie pex,
maix tag vien maox pa raoh in ka daux doh pa hoik ploi maix yiex lih.
Maix tag teei miex keeing maix,
pux aik pux o maix,
mai can pui pa koe ka daux nyiex ma miex keeing maix hoek tix ot ka daux nyiex maix heue.
19 Phan koe pui hu lih khaing daux nyiex in,
pui pauh nawh tang mawh krax lud cie nawh,
ang mawh krax lud yix;
phan koe pui yaox krax laux ka daux nyiex ma maix,
phi map tag kieh ot piang yix.
20 Phan maix krai lih grawng kaing pa yuh yiex in,
yiex tag ang siao hawt ka lox pa tiex tu tix ka maix.”
21 La-heux cu ka lox kiex,
khaix an tom tawx kiex ing.
Khaix hoik ing kiex,
La-heux tom vien maox raoh ka daux doh in.
22 Pui jiang an kiex ra kaux mox tix ka piang gawng.
Pui pa ploi simiang hu sawg kiex ka daux tiag ku dauh,
sawg ka loe singaix ang yaox,
tom deeh ing ka veng Yie-li-keu.
23 Mawh kheu nan,
pui jiang an kiex ra kaux deeh lih khaing piang gawng,
mai tiang ka rawm klawng hu yaox ka Yaw-su-yax.
Kiex tom krai kaing in khawm uik ka nawh,
24 khaix an tom ah nin:
“Ex kied yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx haktiex khawm uik ka ex.
Pui ot dee an kied lhat graung ka ex.”
