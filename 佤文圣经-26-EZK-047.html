Rawm keet pa lih khaing nyiex buan
47
aPui an thung aux hoek ka diang siviex nyiex buan.
Koe rawm keet buing tix lih khaing ka greem dee laig ka diang siviex,
mai lih hu ka plak lih singaix,
pa mawh ka plak sivoe ngai nyiex buan.
Rawm an keet lih khaing greem tiex ka plak seh nyiex buan,
mai laig ka khu dee tan plak seh.
2 Khaix an,
pui an thung aux lih khaing siviex plak laong,
mai viex aux via hoek ka siviex pa koe plak praix pa veeh tix plak lih singaix.
Koe rawm lih khaing siviex pa koe plak seh.
3 Pui an teei khaox diag hu tiag kah,
jauh ka siviex hu plak lih singaix,
mai hoik tiag ka phuan yieh kongjhee;
nawh nhie aux laig tiang rawm ka dee an,
rawm an hoek ngie pie ngai liag caong aux khaing.
4 Jauh ka dee an nawh baox tiag tix yaox hu sivoe ngai phuan yieh kongjhee,
rawm an hoek ngie pie ngawng;
mai baox jauh dee an tiag tix yaox hu sivoe ngai phuan siyiex kongjhee,
rawm an hoek pie nyoi.
5 Nawh baox jauh dee an tiag tix yaox hu sivoe ngai phuan siyiex kongjhee,
rawm hoek dee raux ang lai pon tiang ka caong tix,
mai loe tix tom taug pon tiang hu.
6 Nawh krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw bre rhawm rhi tix ka ceu in khawm uik heue!”
Khaix an,
pui an viex aux ing ka praok rawm klawng;
7 aux yaox koe khaox tom nie ka rawm klawng dee an khawm ra plak.
8 Nawh krai ka aux ah nin:
“Rawm klawng in hu plak lih singaix,
laig ka rawm klawng ngawd Yaw-tang,
ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog.
Rawm klawng an laig daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
mai tag kieh rawm laoh lih mawh rawm ceet.
9 Rawm klawng in phan hoek dee mawx ang ah,
tag kieh can ceu pa koe ju ku ceu mai can kax koe kah.
Rawm klawng an kieh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog mhawm si ngaong,
can dee hoek rawm an ceu pa im ju hoik lih koe ka khawm uik.
10 Jauh ka Yin Ci-ti hoek ka Yin Yi-keu-liien,
ka praok rawm sigrit sigra an koe pui pa rub kax hoik hok rub kah.
Daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog an hoik awm ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong koe kax ku ceu yuh nan,
kied sidaing nie.
11 Viang mawh nan,
rawm dung hawt praok klawng mai rawm nhawng khankix ca kawn laoh heue.
12 bKa praok rawm klawng in khawm ra plak hoik koe khaox ku ceu pa kuah kah,
chix tix kieh mawh priex preem pa ih.
Lhax khaox ang chix kroh chix rham leeing ju,
khaox kawx plix ang jud.
Khaox an kix chix plix plix pa mhawm ku khix,
mawh kheu khaox in kix pui hoik teei rawm pa lih khaing nyiex buan caux kah.
Plix khaox chix tix kieh mawh priex preem pa ih,
lhax chix tix yuh sidah ka heue.”
Mee haktiex
13 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Mee haktiex pex tiiem ot plak greem.
Haktiex pa koe tag caw tix guah tix tawx ka ceu pui kao ra ceu;
ceu pui Yaw-seux tag caw tix pon ra nyum.c
14 Aux hoik sax tiex tu tix tawx haktiex in ka tax du pex,
mai kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix.
Yam in pex tag caw tix guah haktiex in khaw caw heue.
15 “Mee haktiex plak laong mawh nin:
jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong hu plak lih singaix,
hoek ka Si-theu-loeng,
mai baox hoek ka diang moing Ha-ma,
Si-ta-ta,
16 Pi-law-tha,
Si-paw-liien (veng in kix ot si nax meung Ta-ma-see-keux mai ka simiang meung Ha-ma),
Ha-sha-ha-thi-kang (ot ka daog simiang meung Hao-lang).
17 Mee haktiex plak laong jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong vih tix hoek ka veng Yi-nang,
mai kieh plak seh Ta-ma-see-keux mai Ha-ma mawh mee haktiex heue.
18 “Mee haktiex plak lih singaix mawh nin:
jauh ka mee dee kleuk haktiex Ta-ma-see-keux mai Hao-lang paox tix hu plak seh,
hawt rawm klawng Yaw-tang (rawm klawng plak klip singaix mawh Yi-seu-liex,
rawm klawng plak lih singaix mawh Ci-liex) nih tix hoek ka Tha-ma Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog heue.
19 “Mee haktiex plak seh mawh nin:
jauh ka Tha-ma hu plak seh hoek ka Cia-ti-see dee klai rib,
mai baox kling tix hu plak klip singaix pa dex ka plak laong,
hawt mee haktiex Ai-cix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong heue.
20 “Mee haktiex plak klip singaix mawh nin:
jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong hoek ka si tieb plak klip singaix ka diang moing Ha-ma heue.
21 “Pex ku ceu pui tag caw tix guah ka haktiex in.
22 Haktiex in tag sang mawh sin ngao cie pex leeing ju.
Pui tang ceu pa paung ot si nax pex,
mai kawn simex kawn bun kix pa keh dee in tag caw tix guah kix pon haktiex khawm uik.
Pex tag caw tix kia ngien kix awm kia ngien ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nan,
kieh kix ot ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu,
mai yuh krax cawk lai guah kix pon ka haktiex.
23 Pui tang ceu paung ot ka daux ceu pui ceu tix,
tag pon haktiex ka daux ceu pui an.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
