62
Aux tag sang krai lox pa joh jhan Yie-lu-sa-leung;
aux ang doh yien yawg tix,
mai krox tom hoek pon nawh krax taoh,
krox tom hoek jhan riang pa rauh see awm pa riang singaix,
krax taoh awm pa cap ngu proh yuh nan heue.
2 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
meung ka piang haktiex hoik yaox grawng pa pon pex maix khawm uik.
Can simiang hoik yaox min plak ngai maix khawm uik,
maix tag sang lih koe ceu kaux pa khraox tix ceu,
Simiang Siyiex sang hoek tix sum ceu kaux tang maix.
3 Maix tag sang lih mawh mhok khri pa mhawm ka daux tawm taix Simiang Siyiex,
lih mawh mhok khri ka daux tawm taix Siyiex.
4 Maix ang lai baox mawh “bun miex pa tik pa vud pui;”
haktiex maix kawx ang lai baox kok mawh “dee riem.”
Ceu kaux pa khraox maix mawh “kawn pa muh Simiang Siyiex”;
haktiex maix tag kok mawh “mex moeng pa muh paox tix.”
Mawh kheu khaw rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka maix;
nawh tag sang mawh mex ka haktiex maix heue.
5 Jiet awm simex pa nhum ju teei tix moeng ka kawn bun krih yuh nan,
pui pa sum ka maix an tag sang teei tix moeng ka maix.
Jiet awm simex pa yuh ka paox hoik muh ka bun pa yuh ka paox yuh nan,
Siyiex maix kawx muh ka maix heue.
 
6 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
ka piang guh veng maix,
aux tag sang tawx cao pa pau koe kah.
Pon singaix pon sawm,
kix tag ang pon tix yien yawg;
kix tag sang kieh Simiang Siyiex doh keud ka gum lox pa hoik deuh tix,
ang kieh nawh chix piawm ka kix.
7 Kix ang kieh Simiang Siyiex laux lee tix,
krox tom deeh cox sum nawh ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kieh veng an lih mawh veng pa yaog yaw meung ka piang haktiex heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx taix khawn dawm tix tiex tu;
nawh hoik but rhawm tix sang yuh lih ka gum lox pa hoik deuh tix ah nin:
Simie khaing daux kaung ma maix ang lai baox lih mawh nghox koi cie pui koeng;
pui tang ceu tang cad ang lai baox nyaux ka plai pa pon maix khaing daux krax pa taug mao tix.
9 Maix sum,
tom taug yaox tix vawg khrawm,
ih ka priex preem cie to kaux tix,
yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex.
Maix sum,
tom taug koe plix siiet,
nyaux plai cie to kaux tix ka brawk nyiex buan pa singax singeh aux heue.
 
10 Pui pa ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
lih ka veng plak praix heue;
paox ceu paox cad ex pa deeh hoek yuh krax ting tix nyaing.
Rien khre ka krax ting tix nyaing,
mai teei tix tik ka ceu pa thox ka daux krax;
sum mhai krax ka son pui piang haktiex heue.
11 aSimiang Siyiex krai pao ka kawn pui piang haktiex ah nin:
tag caw tix krai ka pui pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang hoek tix taoh ka kix;
nawh tag sang viex ka pui cie tix pa hoik taoh nawh ka deeh hoek.
12 Kix tag sang kok mawh “pui cie Siyiex pa singax singeh”,
kok mawh “kawn praix ceu pui pa hoik taoh Simiang Siyiex kah.”
Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang kok mawh “veng pa muh Siyiex”,
kok mawh “veng pa ang Siyiex tik vud” heue.
