Bun pa ting krax phit vao
17
aKawn cau Siyiex aliah kaux pa giex klong aliah mu ka daux kix,
koe tix kaux hoek krai ka aux ah nin:
“Maix hoek,
aux tag sang kieh maix yaox ka pa kham bun pa ting krax phit vao an ka krax ciyang;
nawh hoik mawh veng ting an pa sum ot ka praok rawm klawng.
2 bSimiang piang haktiex khankix hoik yuh krax pa phit vao tau mai bun pa ting krax phit vao an;
kawn pui piang haktiex kawx hoik yoek ka plai pa phit vao cie nawh heue.”
3 cKhuan singax laig ka daux aux;
kawn cau Siyiex viex aux hu daux tiag.
Aux yaox sibun tix kaux brug to sat pa raoh tix mu;
to kaux to sat an hoik tiiem naog ka ceu kaux pa map nai,
kaing koe aliah mu,
rung koe kao mu.
4 dSibun an ceep ka kraung ceep pa raoh rawt mai pa klai,
mai ceep naog ka kraung mau kraung khri,
siang simaox,
mai siang ploi.
Taix nawh giex ka kawk khri,
ka daux kawk khri an hoik sau naog ka ceu pa phit vao pa ang singax cie sibun coh kaux an.
5 Ka dong re nawh hoik tiiem ka ceu kaux pa raux pa mox tix ceu ah nin:
“Pa-pi-loeng pa ting,
pa mawh miex cie bun pa phit vao piang haktiex mai ceu pa seeing brai khawm uik heue!”
6 Aux baox yaox ka pa hoik yoek sibun an ka nham pui cie Siyiex,
mai ka nham pui pa hoik yum ka pa kham sakse son Ye-sux khankix heue.
Yam yaox aux ka nawh,
kied daox deng kah.
7 Kawn cau Siyiex chawk aux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai daox deng ka lie?
Aux tag sang krai ceu pa raux pa mox sibun an,
mai to sat pa koe kaing aliah mu mai rung kao mu ka maix.
8 eTo sat pa hoik yaox maix pa im sivoe an,
yam in ang lai baox im,
ang rheung,
tag sang lih khaing daux kied raux pa ang koe dee die,
hu plak krax laux leeing.
Kawn pui piang haktiex,
can pa ang koe ceu kaux ka daux lai im ju tom yam phan ka haktiex,
yam yaox ka to sat pa hoik sax im,
yam in ang lai baox im,
singaix pa sang hoek tag sang lih hoek,
tag sang kied daox deng ka heue.
9 “Dee in tag caw tix sang lo ka ping nyia cuyi.
Kaing aliah mu mawh pa krai ka gawng aliah mu pa hoik ngawm sibun coh kaux an piang,
mai mawh nang pa krai simiang aliah kaux.
10 Ka daux kix phuan kaux hoik goh,
mai kawn koe tix kaux;
an tix kaux ang bax hoek,
yam hoek nawh mai kaog ngie tix vud khaing.
11 To sat pa hoik im sivoe an,
yam in ang lai baox im,
pa mawh simiang ka daux daix kaux;
nawh kawx hoik mawh simiang ka daux aliah kaux nang,
pa hu krax pa laux leeing heue.
12 f“Rung pa hoik yaox maix kao mu an,
mawh pa krai ka simiang pa ang baox jauh tix giex kaing kao kaux;
viang mawh nan,
kix tag sang pon ka akhuang simiang,
mai rom tix giex kaing tau mai to sat an tix taix nari.
13 Simiang in kix kao kaux,
kaing pa sang yuh kix khaw paox yuh nin:
kix tag sang teei tix tawx akhuang pa koe tix ka to sat an.
14 Kix tag sang yuh naing mai kawn yung;
viang mawh nan,
kawn yung tag sang pon pex ka kix,
mawh kheu nawh mawh Siyiex ka piang siyiex,
simiang piang simiang.
Pui pa hoik laug nawh,
pa kaux sijax pa hawt ka nawh khankix taug sang pex nang heue.”
15 Kawn cau Siyiex baox krai ka aux ah nin:
“Rawm klawng pa hoik yaox maix ngawm bun pa phit vao an piang,
mawh pa krai ka ceu pui ku ceu,
cub pui ku cub,
meung pui ku meung,
mai ka lox si kah pui ku ceu.
16 Rung pa hoik yaox maix kao mu an,
mai to sat an tag sang koeng ka bun pa phit vao an khawm uik,
tag sang teei hu ka can pa koe nawh khawm uik,
mai kieh nawh ot ka kaong;
kix tag sang ih ka nex nawh,
mai gleeing nawh ka ngu.
17 Mawh kheu hoik teei Siyiex alo pa sang yuh tix deuh ot ka daux rhawm simiang kao kaux an kix,
kieh kix yuh kaing pa khaw paox,
teei tix tawx akhuang pa giex kaing ka to sat an,
tom hoek lih mawh gum lox Siyiex tix mawh heue.
18 “Sibun pa hoik yaox maix an mawh veng ting an pa giex simiang an kix kaing ka piang haktiex heue.”
