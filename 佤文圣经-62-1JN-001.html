Lai Yaw-hang Phuk Tix
Gum lox pa im ju
1
aYix tiiem lox lai in ka pex,
krai grawh grawng yam jauh gum lox pa im ju in tix koe.
Gum lox pa im ju in ex hoik mhong kah,
mai hoik yaox kah;
mawh tete ex hoik yaox kah,
mai hoik giex kah.
2 bYam jauh ju in tix lih koe,
ex hoik yaox kah;
mawh kheu nan,
ex hoik kham sakse ka pex,
mai hoik sidiex ka ju leeing ju pa hoik dawg ot tau mai Siyiex,
khaix an pa hoik jhi ex yaox kah.
3 Ceu pa hoik yaox ex,
pa hoik mhong ex kawx sidiex tix tawx ka pex,
son sang kieh pex yuh paox pie tau mai ex;
pa yuh paox pie in tag mawh pa koe tau mai keeing ex Siyiex,
mai kawn simex nawh Ye-sux Khrit.
4 Ex tiiem lox lai in mawh son sang kieh krax gaux rhawm exc pui ku kaux grum naog heue.
Siyiex mawh pa riang
5 Yam in ex sang teei gum lox pa hoik mhong tix khaing kawn simex Siyiex sidiex tix tawx ka pex yuh nin:
Siyiex mawh pa riang,
nawh ang koe pa faik.
6 Kheu nan,
phan ex krai grawng yuh tix paox pie tau mai nawh,
viang mawh nan,
mai ca kawn ot ka daux pa faik,
ex tag mawh pui pa pie lhien,
kheu ang krax pa yuh khaw mai pa mawh pa meh.
7 Viang mawh nan,
phan ex ot ka daux pa riang,
awm ot Siyiex ka daux pa riang yuh nan,
ex tag mawh pa ra lai yuh tix koe paox pie,
khaix an nham kawn simex nawh Ye-sux Khrit hoik si ghrah phi map ex singax khawm uik heue.
8 Phan ex krai ang phi map tix koe,
ex tag pie lhien ka to kaux tix,
pa mawh pa meh tag ang dex dawt mai ex.
9 Phan ex krai doh phi map tix ka Siyiex,
nawh mawh pa kaux sijax pa rauh see,
nawh tag sang pluat ploi ka phi map ex,
mai si ghrah krax pa lud loe pa hoik yuh ex ku ceu singax.
10 Phan ex krai ang tix bax yuh phi map,
awm hoik kieh ex Siyiex mawh pa pie lhien yuh nan,
gum lox nawh tag ang dex dawt mai ex heue.
