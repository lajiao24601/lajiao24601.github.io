Lai Ha-pa-kux
1
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa plak jhi Simiang Siyiex ka Ha-pa-ku heue.
Rhawm rhi Ha-pa-ku ang nyawm
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux tag sang kok yo koe miex glang,
maix tom tag sang nghiet lie?
Maix tom taug taoh yix luat luan khaing krax tud krax rhim lie?
3 Yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh aux yaox ka krax laux pa yuh nin lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai kawn gun yeung ka kaing pa caox in yuh nin lie?
Ka sivie siveng aux hoik mawh pa laux leeing,
pa tud pa rhim khawm uik,
mai ku dauh ku dee hoik mawh pa simah simaing,
pa lawh pa maw khawm uik.
4 Lox tat jhawm prawng ang koe riang koe rao,
ang koe ceu;
pa rauh pa see ang pon yuh tix lih leeing ju leeing phan.
Pui caox pui caig hoik gut kheem ka pui rauh pui see,
pa rauh pa see hoik lih tix vawk heue.
Lox pawk Simiang Siyiex
5 aSimiang Siyiex krai ka pui cie tix ah nin:
“Pex bre rhawm tix lam lia ka meung pa koe ka sivie siveng pex khankix,
mai pex tag sang chix daox deng ka ceu pa hoik yaox tix.
Aux tag sang yuh ka kaing tix ceu,
pex mhong mai tag ang yum rhawm kah.
6 bAux tag sang kieh pui Pa-pi-loeng pa cau nyhau,
pa tud pa rhim haok huan ting lhaong.
Kix tag sang tauh ka piang haktiex,
mai pon kuang ka haktiex pui tang ceu.
7 Kix hoik kieh pui yaoh yee lhat graung ka tix;
kix ang een pui koe ceu,
mai kieh to kaux tix mawh lox tat jhawm prawng heue.
8 “Brung brug kix kawn lheu brax khaing sivai kaoh ku,
kawn lheu kua khaing sox la pa cai rhawm.
Pui naing brug kix kaoh khaing dee singai;
kix bhuih tix hoek,
awm giex klang ting tix ih ka to tiag heue.
9 “Pui naing kix kieh tix awm jhawm pui pa map nai ka pui,
ka can dee hoek kix khawm uik,
mai hoik kieh pui yaoh yee lhat graung.
Pui pa giex laung kix hoik haoh hun awm mu mhaik yuh nan.
10 Kix nyiah ka simiang,
mai map nai ka simiang naing.
Ceu pa pon tieb tie ka kix ang koe;
kix yuh tab tiex puing ka veng.
11 Kix awm phlu bhaung ting yuh nan,
khaix hoik phlu tom lih hu;
pui in kix hoik kieh riang rao to kaux tix mawh siyiex heue.”
Ha-pa-ku baox salao tix bawg
12 Simiang Siyiex eue,
tom yam jauh maix hoik mawh Siyiex.
Maix mawh Siyiex aux,
mawh pa singax singeh,
mawh ju leeing ju leeing phan.
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao pa kia ngien ka aux eue,
maix hoik laug ka pui Pa-pi-loeng,
mai kieh kix ting lhaong haok huan,
son sang hoek kix tix ciyang ka yix.
13 Viang mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kawn gun yeung ka pui pa tieh pa vheen,
pa caox pa caig yuh nin lie?
Tawm ngai maix kied singax singeh;
maix ang doh jhak ka phi map pa caox,
mai ang gun yeung pui cie tix yuh kaing pa caox pa caig.
Phan mawh nan,
yam yuh kix pui pa lheu khaw caw khaing to kaux tix laux leeing,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang nye lie?
14 Yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh pui cie tix awm kax pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra,
phan ang mawh nan mawh viag vua pa ang koe simiang cao bung lie?
15 Pui Pa-pi-loeng teei goik vheuk pui kah,
awm miiet pui kax yuh nan;
kix teei rub rub pui kah,
mai gaux rhawm yam pon rub ka pui.
16 Kix kawx krub ka rub kix,
mai yuh krax tan lu ka rub,
mawh kheu rub khankix pon tawx kix ih ka ceu pa kub tom nie heue.
17 Khex kawn sang siao lo kix ka vaig gawn ang jud ang jai,
mai pauh ka kawn pui piang haktiex lie?
