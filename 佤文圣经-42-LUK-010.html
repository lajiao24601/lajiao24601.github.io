Ye-sux ploi pui hu atigliah ra kaux
10
Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
Simiang Ye-sux baox laug pui atigliah ra kauxa,
mai ploi kix hu tix cub ra kaux ra kaux,
ran hu ka yaong ka veng pa nhie nawh kix hu pa sang hu jhak nawh.
2 bNawh ah nin ka kix:
“Nghox koi kied nie,
pui yuh kaing kied tux,
mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix khiox khaw ka cao si peem ploi pui yuh kaing hoek tix vawg ka to ma cie nawh heue.
3 cHu heue,
aux ploi pex lih hu,
awm hoik ploi kawn yung ka daux phung sox la yuh nan.
4 dPaw vi viex hau mau;
paw vi viex bau kraung;
paw vi viex kiiep;
mai paw vi kaog tix si kah mai pui ka daux krax.
5 Pex hoek ka nyiex mawx ang ah,
tag caw tix ran krai ah nin:
‘Kieh pex nyawm ot nyawm koe khawm nyiex!’
6 Phan dee an koe pui pa muh ka krax pa nhim,
krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe pa khiox khaw pex tag mawh cie nawh;
phan ang mawh nan,
buan pa nawb pex an tag deeh hoek ka piang pex.
7 e“Pex paung ot nyiex mawx ang ah,
mai ih nyaux ka pa tawx nawh,
mawh kheu pui pa yuh kaing pon mau riang tix hoik mawh pa khaw caw.
Paw vi gah tix hu khaing nyiex in hoek ka nyiex an.
8 Pex hoek ka veng mawx ang ah,
phan koe pui pa rieb ka pex,
rien khre ka ceu pa ih pa tix son pex,
mai pex tag caw tix ih ka ceu an,
9 khaix an mai pai ka pui haktiex an pa koe jhawm saux,
ah nin ka kix:
‘Siawp sibrawm Siyiex tag sang lih hoek ka daux pex heue.’
10 fViang mawh nan,
phan pex hoek ka veng mawx ang ah,
pui dee an ang rieb ka pex,
pex tom hu ka daux laih mai krai pao ah nin:
11 ‘Pie phawng tiex pa kawb caong ex pa koe daux veng pex in,
ex kawx tag sang khruah cawd,
mai jhi grawng pa hoik ne raw pex ka kix.
Viang mawh nan,
pex tag yuh tix tawng,
siawp sibrawm Siyiex tag sang lih hoek heue!’
12 gAux krai ka pex,
ka daux singaix pa prawng ca,
krax ciyang pa si pup veng Saw-to-ma kawn lheu chaong khaing pa si pup veng an heue!”
Veng pa ang yum rhawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 11.20–24)
13 h“Keu-la-shuing eue,
maix tag sang si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!
Paw-sai-tax eue,
maix tag sang si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!
Mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik yuh aux ka daux pex tag sang yuh lih ka daux Thai-eux mai Si-toeng,
pui pa ot dee an hoik dawg ngawm ka piang tiex,
mai lhop man keei sum piang tix,
jhi grawng pa hoik tik kix pa caox caig mai hoik loh rhawm rhi tix heue!
14 Ka daux singaix pa prawng ca,
krax ciyang pa si pup Thai-eux mai Si-toeng kawn lheu chaong pa kham pex lie!
15 iPhan mawh maix Cia-pai-nong eue,
maix tag sang yaog to kaux tix haok ka rao ma lie?
Tag sang si blat maix laig daux Maw-na-rhok heue!”
16 jYe-sux baox krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Pui mawx nghiet ka pex hoik mawh nghiet ka aux,
pa tieb tie ka pex hoik mawh pa tieb tie ka aux;
pa tieb tie ka aux kawx hoik mawh pa tieb tie ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an heue.”
Pui atigliah ra kaux deeh hoek
17 Pui atigliah ra kaux an kix gaux rhawm mai deeh hoek tix,
mai krai ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
yix tawx lox ka daux ceu kaux maix,
pie phi kawx hoik nghiet ka yix heue!”
18 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux yaox Sa tan lih hoek khaing rao ma awm tuh sah yuh nan.
19 kPex nghiet heue!
Aux hoik tawx akhuang ka pex,
pex hoik pon dug dig ka si uing mai ka Tam-tim,
hoik pon pex nang ka riang rao pui koeng khawm uik,
ang lai baox koe ceu pa pon yuh pex laux leeing.
20 Viang mawh nan,
pex paw vi gaux rhawm kheu pa hoik cu khuan phi caox ka lox pex,
viang mawh nan,
mai tag caw tix gaux rhawm kheu hoik tiiem ceu kaux pex ot ka piang rao ma heue.”
Pa gaux rhawm Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 11.25–27; 13.16–17)
21 Ka daux bawg yam in,
Ye-sux hoik naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm kheu pa laig Khuan Singax Singehl ka daux nawh ah nin:
“Keeing Siyiex,
simiang haktiex rao ma,
aux yaog yaw ka maix.
Mawh kheu hoik mox maix kaing yuh tix ka pui pa lhak lhiao mai ka pui pa koe ngai lai,
viang mawh nan,
hoik plak jhi ka pui pa ang koe ngai lai heue.
Mawh tete,
keeing Siyiex eue,
grawng pa yuh nin hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo pa mhawm maix heue!
22 m“Keeing aux hoik tawx ku ceu khawm uik ka aux.
Lheu khaing keeing,
ang koe pui pa tawng ka kawn simex mawh mawx;
lheu khaing kawn simex mai pa cu kawn simex tix plak jhi,
ang koe nang pui pa tawng ka keeing mawh mawx heue.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux kling to kaux tix ka plak ot pui gau,
mai briex ah nin ka kix:
“Pex pon yaox ka kaing in kix khawm uik kied koe buan son heue!
24 Aux krai ka pex,
koe cao jhai mai simiang tom nie tag sang jhak ceu pa hoik yaox pex,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix yaox,
tag sang nghiet ceu pa hoik mhong pex,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix mhong nang heue.”
Phek tiiem ka pui mhawm Sa-ma-li-yax
25 nKoe sara lox tat jhawm prawng tix kaux hoek tix nyaw glaw ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
sang khaw tix yuh ka mawx tag sang pon ka ju leeing ju lie?”
26 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phuk lai lox tat jhawm prawng hoik krai ah ka mawx lie?
Hoik kah maix yuh ka mawx ka lie?”
27 oPui an pawk ah nin:
“Maix tag caw tix muh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi,
khawm ju,
khawm riang rao tix khawm uik,
mai baox caw tix muh ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan.”
28 pYe-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix hoik khaw pawk,
hawt tix yuh yuh nin maix tom tag sang pon ka ju leeing ju eue.”
29 Sara lox tat jhawm prawng an,
son sang kieh tix koe krax,
nawh tom chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawx pa mawh paox plawg paox nyawm aux lie?”
30 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Koe pui tix kaux kaoh khaing veng Yie-lu-sa-leung lih ka veng Yie-li-keu,
nawh si pup pui con daux krax.
Kix peeik kraung ceep nawh,
lauh nawh yum pi,
mai tik nawh ot ka dee an.
31 Yam an koe cao giex kaing li lawng lih ka daux krax an;
nawh yaox ka pui an mai via hu plak an.
32 Awm an,
koe pui Li-we hoek dee an;
nawh hu dex jhak ka pui an,
mai baox via hu ka plak an nang.
33 qViang mawh nan,
koe pui Sa-ma-li-yax lawng krax hoek dex ka praok pui an,
nawh yaox pui an,
mai caox rhawm caox rhi ka nawh.
34 Pui Sa-ma-li-yax an teei bee viex tix mai plai klup ka dee mat pui an,
mai teei man kie ka nawh,
khaix an yaog nawh kaoh sau nawh piang khawng brie viex tix,
viex nawh hoek ka nyiex nyo,
mai kia ngien nawh ka dee an.
35 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
nawh viex mau luah ra plah tawx ka cao nyiex paung nyo an,
mai ah nin:
‘Maix kia ngien ka nawh mhawm mhawm,
krox aux hoek dee in,
aux taug sang tawx mau son nawh tom kub heue.’ ”
36 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux chawk ah nin:
“Awm rhawm pa keud maix,
pui in kix loe kaux pui mawx mawh paox plawg paox nyawm pui pa si pup ka pui tud rhim an lie?”
37 Sara lox tat jhawm prawng an pawk ah nin:
“Mawh pui pa kia ngien nawh ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix an heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan mawx nan maix hu,
mai hawt tix yuh yuh nan heue!”
Ye-sux hu jhak ka Ma-tax mai Ma-li-yax
38 rYe-sux mai pui gau nawh ca nied tix hu lawng krax pa hu kix,
mai hoek ka yaong tix mu.
Dee an koe pa kaux Ma-tax bun miex tix kaux,
mai rieb Ye-sux hoek ka nyiex tix.
39 Ma-tax koe pux o tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Ma-li-yax.
Ma-li-yax ngawm ka plak sivoe caong Ye-sux,
mai nghiet ka lox pa sidiex nawh.
40 Viang mawh nan,
kheu kied nie kaing pa sang yuh Ma-tax,
rhawm rhi nawh suk sag,
nawh tom hoek dex ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
pux o aux kieh aux tix kaux khaing yuh kaing tom nie yuh nin,
mawh khaw ka rhawm maix lie?
Nhie nawh hoek tix teem ka aux liak!”
41 Simiang Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Ma-tax eue!
Ma-tax eue!
Maix hoik suk sag rhawm ka yuh tix kaing tom nie,
42 viang mawh nan,
mawh ngie tix ceu khaing pa ang pon tix cud.
Ma-li-yax hoik laug ka ceu pa blawn mhawm;
ang koe pui pa pon teei hu khaing daux tawm taix nawh heue.”
