Ta-wue mawh simiang Yi-seu-liex mai simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 11.1–9;14.1–7)
5
Can ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu,
hoek ka Ta-wue daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Yix hoik mawh nex nham to kaux paox ceu paox cad maix.
2 Singaix pa hoik luan,
yam kawn mawh Sao-law simiang ex,
maix hoik thung ex kawn pui Yi-seu-liex puing ka naing.
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx lox ka maix ah nin,
maix tag sang mawh cao kaing pa liang ka pui cie nawh,
mai mawh simiang ka piang kix.”
3 Can kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka simiang Ta-wue daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng.
Ta-wue yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
kix tom ap nawh mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
4 aYam mawh Ta-wue simiang,
nawh koe tix ngoe num,
mai nawh mawh simiang tipon num.
5 Nawh ot ka haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
mai kuang ka pui Yo-tax aliah num plak.
Nawh ot ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax tix ngoe loe num heue.
6 bTa-wue mai pui naing cie nawh rien tix sang puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Pui haktiex Yie-pu-see keud Ta-wue ang doh sang pon teei ka veng in,
tom ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Maix ang pon laig daux veng in leeing ju,
pie pui dug ngai mai pui chawh caong tag sang pon pex ka maix!”
7 Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue pon teei ka tab Shi-ang kix.
An hoik li tix mawh veng Ta-wue pa hoik tawng pui pa hoek khaix ku kaux heue.
8 Ka daux singaix an,
Ta-wue ah nin ka pui pa ot greem tix:
“Mawh pui mawx pa awm aux pa hoik koeng ka pui Yie-pu-see,
phan keud tix sang pex ka kix,
tag caw tix haok ka daux rawng in,
mai puing ka pui pa dug an kix mai pui pa chawh an kix!”
Mawh kheu nan,
lox prim koe ah nin tix gum:
“Pui dug ngai mai pui chawh caong ang pon tix laig ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex heue.”
9 Khaix hoik pon teei ka tab,
Ta-wue laig ot ka daux,
mai kok ceu kaux mawh veng Ta-wue.
Ta-wue sum veng vung viang tab in,
mai jauh tix sum ka gawng haktiex an plak lih singaix.
10 Ta-wue tix ngaix hoik tix ngaix nied tix ting riang,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik ot tau mai nawh heue.
11 Simiang Thai-eux pa mawh Si-lang ploi pui yuh lai gee tix cub hu ka Ta-wue dee an,
tawx khaox si oe bhawng ka Ta-wue,
mai baox viex cang mai khaox mai cang mai simaox hoek,
son sang mawh cao pa sum nyiex haw tang nawh.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue hoik tawng grawng hoik ap Simiang Siyiex tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kheu pui cie nawh khankix hoik kieh meung nawh haok huan heue.
13 Khaix hoik lih hu Ta-wue khaing Si-paw-loeng hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
nawh baox laug tix koe moeng iak tom nie,
mai hoik keh kawn simex kawn bun khankix tom nie.
14 Pa keh ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung mawh Sa-mu-yax,
Saw-pax,
Na-tang,
Saw-law-meung,
15 Yi-sia,
Yi-li-su-yax,
Ni-fe,
Ya-fe-yax Jonadab,
16 Yi-li-sa-ma,
Yi-li-ya-tax,
Yi-li-fa-liex heue.
Pon pex ka pui Fe-li-see
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 13: 8–1)
17 Pui Fe-li-see hoik mhong grawng pa ap Ta-wue tix mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
tom khrawm pui naing hu,
mai sawg ka Ta-wue.
Ta-wue hoik tawng kah,
tom laig ka daux tab.
18 Pui Fe-li-see hoek ka daux ngawd Li-fa-ying,
mai cax ka dee an.
19 Ta-wue khiox khaw chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang khaw aux tix haok puing ka pui Fe-li-see lie?
Maix tag sang kieh aux puing pex lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix haok,
aux tag sang kieh maix puing pex ka pui Fe-li-see heue!”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue haok ka Pa-lix Pi-la-sing,
mai pon pex ka pui Fe-li-see dee an.
Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pung prang ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
awm rawm ting pa chun die tiex laux yuh nan.”
Mawh kheu nan,
dee an tom kok mawh Pa-lix Pa-la-singc.
21 Yam to pui Fe-li-see,
mai kix tik riang rao pa krub kix dee an,
Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh khankix tom teei tix viex hu heue.
22 Ang koe miex glang tix,
pui Fe-li-see deeh hoek ka daux ngawd Li-fa-ying,
mai cax ka dee an.
23 Ta-wue baox khiox khaw chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex;
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Paw vi puing kix khaing dee in,
caw tix via krax hoek plak khaix kix,
mai puing kix dee dex koe khaox liah plak an.
24 Yam mhong maix luah lox caong pui ka piang lhax khaox,
tom yaog tix puing heue,
mawh kheu aux hoik hu sivoe maix,
mai puing ka pui naing Fe-li-see.”
25 Khaix an,
Ta-wue hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh puing ka pui Fe-li-see deeh,
jauh ka haktiex Cia-pa mai leue tom hoek ka haktiex Ci-seux.
