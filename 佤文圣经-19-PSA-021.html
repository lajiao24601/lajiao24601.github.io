Rhax lai yaog yaw son hoik puing pex
21
Simiang Siyiex eue,
simiang gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu pa tawx maix riang rao;
nawh gaux rhawm gaux rhi kheu pa tawx maix krax pa puing pex.
2 Maix hoik yuh lih pa simie nawh tix yuh;
maix hoik nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw nawh heue.
 
3 Maix hoik tawx krax nyawm ot nyawm koe ka nawh tom ang lai chix uik chix leeing,
mai baox sau mhok khri ka piang kaing nawh.
4 Nawh khiox khaw tix lang ju ka maix,
mai maix hoik tawx ka nawh,
kieh nawh lang ju lang phan heue.
 
5 Nawh hoik pon min plak ngai kheu krax teem coi maix;
maix hoik tawx nawh koe min,
koe ceu.
6 Maix hoik tawx buan son ka nawh ang chix uik leeing ju leeing phan;
krax pa ot maix mai nawh,
hoik kieh nawh grum naog ka krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
 
7 Simiang yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex;
kheu krax muh cao pa ting siawp sibrawm ka piang khawm uik,
siawp sibrawm simiang nawh kaux cong leeing ju.
8 Simiang haktiex sang sawg tix giex ka pui pa koeng ka nawh khawm uik;
nawh sang giex laung ka can pui pa koeng pa khoi ka nawh khawm uik.
9 Yam lih hoek nawh,
tag sang awm ngu ting pa si aoh gleeing ka kix heue.
 
Simiang Siyiex sang bleet kix ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix;
ngu krax pa song rhawm sang gleeing kix laux lak pak leeing.
10 Simiang tag sang yuh laux leeing ka kawn praix kix;
nawh tag sang pauh ka doeh ceu cad kix heue.
 
11 Krax caox pa keud kix tix sang yuh nawh laux,
mai ang pon tix lih mawh.
12 Nawh tag sang vaik ak puh nyeu krox,
kieh kix kling yaog tix to heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kieh maix jhi riang rao tix lih;
yix sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka pa ting riang ting rao maix heue.
