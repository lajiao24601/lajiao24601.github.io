14
Tom yam jauh ex tix keh hoik iak riang iak rao,
mai hoik yaox ka krax pa dawt ju mai krax kha rhawm kha rhi.
2 Ex hoik awm rib riem pa kuah,
pa chix vhit chix khrum yuh nan;
ex chix grai awm riang pang peue yuh nan,
ang chix kaog.
3 Siyiex eue,
awm pa mawh aux in,
maix pon chix jhak ka aux mai viex aux hu kham krax prawng lie?
4 Pui pa seeing brai sang pon teei lih ka ceu pa singax singeh lie?
Ang,
ang doh pon!
5 Ju kawn pui hoik dawg ciak tix deuh;
krax pa im ju nawh ka piang haktiex hoik mawh maix pa tawx.
Krax pa tat maix ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon jao luan.
6 Khiox khaw maix kling ngai jhak tix,
paw lai hu kuang ka nawh,a
kieh nawh awm pui cang pui caing hoik si dauh yuh kaing pa yuh tix tix ngaix.
7 Khaox pa hoik kid tix kawn koe krax keud pot jun pot;
khaox an kawn sang chix deeh im,
mai plaung lih ka kak pa awn.
8 Viang riah hoik kuad ot daux tiex,
kak hoik rham ot daux tiex,
9 yam hoik deeh kub lih rawm khaox an kawn ca chix pon plaung,
awm simie pa lih kuah kak pa khraox yuh nan.
10 Viang mawh nan,
yam yum pui hoik mawh dee si dauh krax lawng nawh;
khaix hoik dut rhawm nawh,
nawh ot dee mawx lie?
 
11 Awm rawm klawng pa ang rawm lai lih khaing,
rawm nhawng hoik kroh,
12 pui hoik yum ang lai chix deeh kaoh im;
viang mawh rao ma pa hoik ghrup kawx,
mai ang lai pon haing,
ang lai pon so kix haing khaing it tix tom rheeng heue.
 
13 Kieh maix mox aux ka daux meung yum,
kieh aux mox tix,
tom hoik grai krax pa song rhawm song rhi maix,
ciak tix deuh ka singaix bawg yam pa doh keud ka aux,
14 phan pui hoik yum,
sang deeh pon chix im lie?
Viang mawh nan,
aux sang krox jun ka singaix pa mhawm an,
krox jun singaix kha rhawm hoik luan.
15 Yam an,
maix tag sang kok yo,
mai aux tag sang pawk;
maix tag chix muh ka aux pa phan yuh tix.
16 Yam an,
maix tag chix jhak simi ka diang caong aux,
viang mawh nan,
maix ang si kaw ka krax pa lud loe aux.
17 Maix tag chix pluat ploi ka krax pa lud loe aux,
teei tix tik ka phi map phi ghram aux khawm uik heue.
 
18 Awm gawng lua pa bih pa max laux leeing,
simaox rang hoik gah tix hu khaing dee ot tix,
19 rawm pa lih jauh hoik klaing simaox laux,
lhiex ting hoik chun tiex hu;
maix kawx sang yuh laux ka krax keud pot jun pot kawn pui.
20 Maix hoik pex ka kawn pui,
mai tawx nawh hu leeing ju;
maix hoik kieh pak ngai nawh tieh loh tix khawm uik,
mai ploi nawh hu.
21 Kawn simex nawh khankix pon ka min plak ngai,
mai nawh ang tawng;
kix pon krax kaig krax tua,
nawh kawx ang tawng.
22 Nawh mhong ngie krax kha rhawm pa koe ka to kaux tix khaing;
nawh ngia ngie son to kaux tix khaing heue.
