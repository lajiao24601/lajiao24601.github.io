Yi-si-ciex khlao hauk kaing tix
5
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag teei vaik tix mad kieh mawh gawn khlao mai khlao hauk jheeh maix,
mai hauk kaing maix,
ciang hauk jheeh maix ot ka piang jiang,
khaix an guah koe loe pum.
2 Khaix hoik naog singaix pa lai lawm ka veng,
maix tag caw tix teei hauk jheeh viex lih loe son tix son gleeing ka daux veng;
an loe son tix son teei lih ot plak praix veng,
mai klauh phawng yam hu tix;
baox teei pa deuk an loe son tix son rhuat ka rao ma kieh bhaung phlu,
mai aux tag sang teei vaig viex tix leue kah.
3 Maix tag caw tix deuh hauk kaing tix ra loe gawng,
mai teei jaong sibex maix kie kah,
4 khaix an teei hauk an lih ra loe gawng,
mai sau ka daux ngu.
Ngu tag sang daw tix hawt ka hauk kaing an ra loe gawng hoek ka meung Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.”
5 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“In hoik mawh Yie-lu-sa-leung heue!
Aux sang deuh nawh ot ka sigreung haktiex,
mai kieh meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik lai lawm ka nawh.
6 Viang mawh nan,
Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik lieh lheu ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
kix kawn lheu caox caig khaing meung ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai kawn lheu lieh lox khaing meung pa koe sivie siveng kix.
Yie-lu-sa-leung ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
mai hoik tik vud ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
maix tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa ting lhaong.
Maix ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
tik vud ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
hawt ka been krax ot krax koe meung piang haktiex,
mai cheeik tix kieh kaing ceu pa nyhap kawn lheu khaing meung pa ot sivie siveng kix.
8 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai pao,
aux tag sang tieb tie ka maix;
aux tag sang prawng ca maix ka sivoe ngai meung ka piang haktiex.
9 Mawh kheu kaing pa yuh maix hoik mawh pa yum koeng aux kah,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang ciyang ka maix.
Aux ang nyang sax ciyang maix yuh nin,
khaix in sax kawx ang lai chix ciyang maix yuh nin.
10 aDee si dauh,
ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
miex keeing tag sang ih ka nex kawn praix tix;
kawn praix kawx tag sang ih ka nex miex keeing tix.
Aux tag sang ciyang ka maix,
tag sang grheu ka pui pa kawn deuk khankix hu ka haktiex ku dauh ku dee heue.
11 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka daux ju tix leeing ju ah nin:
kheu hoik yuh maix kaing pa yum koeng aux kah,
kaing pa ang aux rhawm yaox hoik seeing brai yuh nyiex buan aux,
aux tag sang yuh maix laux leeing ang lai yawn rhawm tix ka pie ceeix.
12 Kawn pui loe son tix son tag sang yum ka jhawm saux,
yum ka cai rhawm daux veng,
an loe son tix son tag sang yum ka pa vaig gawn plak praix veng;
aux tag sang grheu ka pui pa kawn deuk khankix loe son tix son hu ka haktiex khawm pon daog,
mai teei vaig cie aux leue kix ka heue.
13 “Aux tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang to kaux maix khawm uik,
mai klup khawm uik tom taug naog rhawm.
Yuh nin,
maix tom taug tawng,
mawh kheu ang maix nghiet lox tom hoik cheeik tix kieh aux song rhawm;
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik sax krai lox in ka maix.
14 Pui meung pa koe sivie siveng hoek ka dee an,
mai tag nyiah maix,
to hu khaing maix khawm uik heue.
15 “Yam song rhawm aux tix ciyang ka maix tom kin,
meung pa ot sivie siveng maix khankix hoik yaoh yee ka khawm uik.
Yam yaox kix ka kaing pa lhat graung yuh nin,
kix tag koeng ka maix,
mai salao ka maix.
16 Aux tag sang kieh priex preem pa ih maix uik,
kieh maix ang yaox tix sawm,
kieh maix kha rhawm rhi tix ka cai rhawm,
awm te pa lawm pa cieh maix yum yuh nan.
17 bAux tag sang kieh sibhawm praix mai to sat yuh kawn praix maix laux leeing,
mai baox kieh jhawm saux preng,
krax simah,
mai naing pauh ka maix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
