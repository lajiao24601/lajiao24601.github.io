Lox rawg buan krax teem krax coi
(Lai Yaog Yaw 57.7–11; 60.5–12)
108
Siyiex eue,
aux hoik giex rhawm rhi tix kaux cong,
mai ang pon kieh chuh;
aux sang rhax ka lox rhax,
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix.
Rhawm rhi aux eue,
si duad tix kaoh heue!
2 Ting eue,
chiang eue,
si duad tix kaoh heue!
Aux sang so singaix si duad tix kaoh heue!
3 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang yaog yaw maix ka meung ka piang haktiex;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw maix ka daux kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik;
4 Krax muh maix pa koe leeing ju hoik hoek ka rao ma;
pa kaux sijax maix hoik cux ka pai awm heue.
 
5 Siyiex eue,
kieh pa ting pa lhaong maix jhi tix ka piang rao ma;
kieh min plak ngai maix naog ka piang haktiex.
6 Khiox khaw maix cu nghiet ka lox yix,
mai teei pa ting riang ting rao maix taoh ka yix,
kieh pui cie maix pa hoik muh maix ka pon krax pa taoh heue.
 
7 Siyiex krai lox tix lih khaing daux nyiex pa singax ah nin:a
aux tag sang pon pex,
sang guah ka See-ciien tang ot;
aux sang guah tix tawx ngawd Su-keu ka pui cie tix.
8 Ci-liex mawh cie aux,
Ma-na-si kawx mawh cie aux;
Yi-fa-liien mawh mhok rhiem aux;
Yo-tax mawh khaox gix siawp sibrawm aux;
9 aux sang teei Maw-ya mawh pan khoek caong,
mai tik kiiep plak Yi-tong.
Aux sang rhax lai pa pon pex ka pui Fe-li-see heue.
 
10 Siyiex eue,
pui mawx pon viex aux laig daux veng pa kaux cong in lie?
Pui mawx pon thung aux hoek ka Yi-tong lie?
11 Siyiex eue,
sang tik vud maix ka yix tete lie?
Ang maix yuh naing tau mai pui naing cie yix lie?
12 Khiox khaw maix teem coi yix tieb tie ka pui pa koeng;
krax pa teem coi kawn pui ang koe ceu pie ceeix kawx.
13 Siyiex ot mai yix plak in,
yix tom pon krax pa pon pex;
nawh tag sang puing pex ka pui pa koeng ka yix tete heue.
