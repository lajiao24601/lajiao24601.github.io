Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka Ya-meung
49
aPhan mawh grawh grawng Ya-meung,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Simex pui Yi-seu-liex ot dee mawx?
Khex ang koe pui pa kia mhawm ka haktiex kix lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai kieh pui krub ka Mi-leu-kong khankix pon rhim ka haktiex ceu pui Cia-teux,
mai paung ot dee an lie?
2 Kuan jhak tix yaox heue,
yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
aux tag sang kieh pui Ya-meung pa ot ka veng ting La-pa mhong lox naing.
La-pa tag sang lih mawh dee riem,
veng nawh tag sang laux leeing.
Khaix an pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang deeh ing haktiex ot tix khaing daux tawm taix pui pa tud rhim ka kix.
3 Pui Si-seu-peung eue,
yiiem doe heue,
mawh kheu hoik laux leeing veng Ai heue!
Bun miex La-pa eue,
laux rhawm yiiem heue!
Tag caw tix ceep ka kraung man keei sum son krax laux rhawm,
mai kleeing tix ka daux krax pa suk sag heue!
Siyiex tang kaux pex Mi-leu-kong mai cao giex kaing li nawh,
simiang nawh tag sang giex kix tau.
4 Meung pa tieh vheen eue,
yuh ka mawx lai yaw rang di lie?
Riang rao pex hoik dieh hu;
yuh ka mawx lai een rhawm ka riang rao to kaux tix,
mai ce pui ang pon sang hoek tix tud rhim lie?
5 Aux tag sang kieh krax lhat lai lawm ka pex,
mai pex hoik yaog tix to.
Pui ku kaux tang yaog tix to son sang yaox tix klawd ju,
ang lai koe pui pa deeh cox kraix khrawm ka pui naing pa som nai khankix deeh kaoh heue.
6 “Viang mawh nan,
koe tix ngaix sax,
aux tag sang kieh Ya-meung deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma tix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka Yi-tong
7 bPhan mawh grawh grawng Yi-tong,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
“Ang lai baox yaox pui pa koe ping nyia ka Yi-tong lie?
Pui pa koe krax doh riang ang lai baox krai ka kaing pa khaw kix tix sang yuh lie?
Krax ping nyia kix hoik grai khawm uik lie?
8 Pui Ti-tang eue,
kling to kaux tix to heue!
Mox tix heue!
Mawh kheu singaix bawg yam pa ciyang aux ka doeh ceu cad Yi-sao hoik hoek;
aux tag sang yuh kix laux leeing!
9 Yam pieh pui ka plix siiet chix tix deuh koe ka khaox tix blah,
pui briex sawg tix briex yam pon sawm,
mai briex ngie ka ceu pa sang lo tix.
10 Viang mawh nan,
auxc tag sang peeik to kaux doeh ceu cad Yi-sao ghle uik,
mai jhi pui yaox ka dee mox kix tix,
kieh kix ang lai baox pon mox tix.
Pui Yi-tong mai ceu pui kix,
paox plawg paox nyawm kix hoik laux leeing,
mai ang lai deuh koe tix kaux kawx.
11 Tawx kawn doe pex ka aux heue,
aux tag chix tix eei ka kix;
miex mai pex kawx kieh aux mawh cao pa kia ngie heue.
12 “Kuan jhak tix yaox,
phan pui pa ang khaw tix kham krax ciyang khankix nyaux ka kawk krax ciyang,
pex chix tix ang kham krax ciyang lie?
Ang doh mawh nan!
Pex tag sang nyaux ka kawk in.
13 Aux hoik sax tiex tu ka to kaux tix ah nin,
veng Paw-see-la tag sang tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag pa riem,
lih mawh dee pa kieh pui yaoh yee,
map nai,
jhax lawg.
Can nyiex yaong pa dex dee an tag sang mawh dee riem khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
14 Aux ah nin:
“Yi-tong eue,
aux pon gum lox khaing Simiang Siyiex dee an.
Nawh ploi kawm cau hoek ka meung ka piang haktiex,
mai nhie kix khrawm ka pui naing,
rien tix puing ka maix.
15 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh maix lih mawh meung blawn pa iak tix mu;
ang koe pui pa een maix koe ceu.
16 Krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi maix hoik pie lhien ka maix.
Tete te lai,
maix ang tie awm krax keud to kaux tix kieh pui lhat graung yuh nan.
Viang maix hoik paung ot ka daux dawh rang ka piang gawng lhaong,
viang maix hoik awm klang ting pa yuh mheen tix ot ka dee lhaong,
mai Simiang Siyiex tag sang bluh maix lih.
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
17 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yi-tong tag sang si pup ka krax laux leeing pa kied lhat graung pui kah;
yam yaox pui pa lawng krax ku kaux hoik yaoh yee baig ka khawm uik.
18 dSaw-to-ma,
Eu-maw-la,
mai veng pa koe sivie siveng hoik laux leeing yuh ka mawx sivoe an,
Yi-tong kawx tag sang laux leeing yuh nan nang,
ang lai baox koe pui pa paung ot kah.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
19 Kuan jhak tix yaox heue,
jiet awm sivai sang si pa lih hu khaing daux nong khaox pa koe ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang yuh nan,
pa hoek ka pak rib klai,
awm an,
mai aux tag sang kieh pui Yi-tong lih hu khaing haktiex kix dum mawx dum an.
Khaix an aux laug ka simiang lih,
mai nawh tag sang giex kaing ka piang meung haktiex in.
Pui mawx sang pon tiag tix tau mai aux lie?
Pui mawx sang pon tiex ka aux lie?
Simiang mawx sang pon tieh vheen ka aux lie?
20 “Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka kaing pa sang yuh aux yuh nin:
aux tag sang tieb tie ka Yi-tong,
mai lauh ka pui veng Thi-mang.
Pie kawn nyawm kix kawx tag sang reu hu khawm uik,
pui ku kaux hoik lhat kah.
21 Lox luah pa ghrup Yi-tong tag sang yung yang yuh haktiex,
lox pru praoh yaoh yee pui tag sang hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
22 Kuan jhak tix yaox heue,
pui koeng hoik awm klang ting pa nih preeik tix pu,
mai breeng tix lih hoek tuh ka Paw-see-la.
Ka daux singaix an,
pui naing Yi-tong tag sang si yaung awm bun yha yam saux sang keh yuh nan heue.”
Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka Ta-ma-see-keux
23 ePhan mawh grawh grawng Ta-ma-see-keux,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui veng Ha-ma mai Ya-eu-pa ang nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mawh kheu kix hoik mhong ka gum lox pa ang mhawm.
Krax pa khi rhawm kix hoik awm rawm ting puk pak yuh nan,
mai kieh kix ang nyawm ot nyawm koe.
24 Pui Ta-ma-see-keux hoik grai riang rao,
kling to kaux tix to.
Kix awm bun yha yam saux sang keh,
mai kied sidaing lang teung kha rhawm tix tete.
25 Veng in pa hoik sax kieh pui gaux rhawm,f
pa hoik kieh pui koe min,
yam in hoik mawh haktiex dee riem heue.
26 Pui nhum ju pa ot daux veng khankix,
pui tag sang pauh kix yum ka daux laih ting laih iak;
pui hoik yuh can pui naing laux leeing ka daux tix ngai khaing.
27 Aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing ka guh veng Ta-ma-see-keux,
mai yuh laux ka tab simiang Piien Ha-tax.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Prawng ca ka ceu pui Ci-tax mai veng Sha-saw
28 Phan mawh grawh grawng pa hoik pon pex simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka ceu pui Ci-tax,
mai ka ceu pui pa ot sivie siveng Sha-saw khankix,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Kaoh heue!
Haok puing ka pui Ci-tax,
yuh ceu pui pa ot ka plak lih singaix laux leeing heue!
29 Tud rhim ka nyiex gox bang kix mai phung yung kix,
teei ka man siviex daux doh mai ka ceu pa ot daux khawm uik.
Viex ka-la-uk kix hu,
krai ka kix ah nin:
‘Krax lhat graung lai lawm ka pex!’
30 “Pui Sha-saw eue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka pex,
to mox tix heue!
To hu dee singai khankix,
mai mox tix heue!
Simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoik riem tix sang puing ka pex.
Kix krai yuh nin:
31 ‘Hu heue,
ex haok puing ka pui pa ce tix hoik nyawm ot,
mai ang koe krax khi rhawm khi rhi!
Veng kix ang koe siviex,
mai ang koe jhong nang,
ang koe krax pa tieb tie pie ceeix kawx heue.’
32 “Teei hu ka ka-la-uk kix mai ka can moi yung kix heue!
Aux tag sang grheu pui pa hoik kip hauk kaing tix ngaing khankix hu ka piang haktiex khawm pon daog;
aux tag sang ploi krax laux leeing khaing ka praok kix lih piang kix.
33 Veng Sha-saw tag sang riem leeing ju,
mai lih mawh dee yuh sox tiag mheem tix,
dee ang pui paung ot.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai yuh nin heue.”
Simiang Siyiex prawng ca ka Yi-lang
34 Singaix yam jauh Si-ti-cia tix mawh simiang Yo-tax,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai aux mhong grawng kaing pa dex pa dawt mai Yi-lang.
35 Nawh ah nin:
“Aux tag sang raig pot ka kraung naing pa blawn ting ceu cie Yi-lang,
pa mawh ak puh.
36 Aux tag sang kieh bhaung pa kaoh khaing pon praok si daix daog hoek tix phlu ka Yi-lang,
mai kieh pui cie nawh rai rhuat tix hu ka haktiex ku dauh,
ku haktiex ku meung hoik koe ka rhoi khaix caong pui pa si pup krax laux.
37 Aux tag sang kieh pui Yi-lang lhat graung ka pui koeng pa keud tix sang pauh ka kix.
Aux tag sang yuh pui Yi-lang laux leeing ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix,
mai teei krax naing yuh kix laux lak pak leeing.
38 Aux tag sang yuh simiang kix,
mai ka cao kaing laux leeing,
khaix an mai sum dang ngeung tix ot dee an.
39 Viang mawh nan,
koe tix ngaix sax,
aux tag sang kieh pui Yi-lang deeh cox sum tix ot ka nyiex ma,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
