Ca maox Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka daux ra bawg
4
Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha tawx lox ka meung piang haktiex,
ku ceu pui,
mai ka pui pa si kah ka lox ku ceu plak greem ah nin:
“Kieh pex nyawm ot nyawm koe heue!
2 Aux cu tix krai lih ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong pa jhi mhai pa daox pa am ka aux heue.
3 Mhai pa daox pa am Siyiex kied ting kied lhaong heue!
Kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh nawh kied koe riang heue!
Siawp sibrawm nawh ang chix uik leeing ju leeing phan;
Pa giex nawh kaing toe tix tix ju hoek tix ju heue!
4 “Aux hoik nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux nyiex haw simiang tix,
mai koe min plak ngai;
5 viang mawh nan aux ca maox yam laig it tix,
mai yaox ka riang su sai krax pa lhat pa graung.
6 Aux kok can pui pa koe ping nyia ka daux nyiex haw simiang hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
son sang kieh kix kah ca maox son aux.
7 Can pui pa chix si pawk,
cao chix si meed,
mai pa chix sawn singaix hoek khawm uik.
Aux krai ca maox tix ka kix,
mai kix ang pon kah son aux.
8 Khaix an Tang-yi-li hoek,
(nawh baox kaux Paw-thi-sa-sha,
sum nawh hawt ceu kaux siyiex aux.)
Khuan siyiex pa kied singax ot tau mai nawh.
Mawh kheu nan aux krai ca maox tix ka nawh,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
9 ‘simiang pui pa chix si pawk Paw-thi-sa-sha eue,
aux tawng ot tau khuan siyiexa pa kied singax tau mai maix,
maix hoik tawng ka can pa raux pa mox khawm uik.
In mawh ca maox aux,
maix kah son aux liak heue.’
10 “Aux yaox riang su sai yam laig it tix mawh nin:
koe khaox ting ka daux sigreung haktiex,
11 khaox in nied ot nied tix ting lhaong,
mai cux ka rao ma;
can pui ka piang haktiex hoik yaox ka khawm uik.
12 Lhax khaox in kied mhawm,
plix hoik naog ka khaox,
hoik kub kawn pui piang haktiex ih.
To sat laux lee tix ka greem veei khaox in;
sim saik yuh mheem tix ka kak,
can ceu pa im ju ih ka plix khaox in khawm uik heue.
13 “Yam kawn keud aux ka riang su sai in,
aux yaox kawn cau Siyiex tix kaux lih hoek khaing rao ma.
14 Nawh kok yo lox pa ting si num ah nin:
‘Kid khaox in ghrup,
tat kak dut,
yung yang lhax khrum,
lauh plix cawd.
Grheu to sat pa ot ka greem khaox,
grheu ka sim saik pa ot ka kak khaox in pu.
15 Deuh ka kak khaox graik in ot piang tiex,
teei maox tawng maox riem lai lawm kah,
mai deuh ot tau mai rib riem daux tiag heue.
“ ‘Teei tix deuh pui in ot daux tiag;
kieh nawh sawm rib riem tau mai to sat,
kieh nawh kham pa lan rawm ciex.
16 Ka daux aliah num,
nawh ang chix koe rhawm lhak awm kawn pui,
ang lai koe rhawm rhi pa chix keud chix sawn awm kawn pui;
nawh awm ngie to sat yuh nan khaing.
17 In hoik mawh krax pa tat kawn cau Siyiex pa mawh cao pa pau,
son sang kieh kawn pui piang haktiex tawng,
meung ka piang haktiex hoik mawh Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong pa kuang ka piang,
nawh koe sibrawm tix tawx meung ka pui pa hoik laug nawh khankix,
viang mawh pui pa iak pa lied kawx,
hoik chix tix kieh nawh koe sibrawm tix kuang heue.’ ”
18 Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ah nin:
“In hoik mawh ca maox aux.
Paw-thi-sa-sha eue,
khiox khaw maix kah ca maox son aux liak.
Pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi ka daux nyiex haw simiang ang koe pui tix kaux pa pon kah ka ca maox aux;
viang mawh nan,
maix hoik pon kah son aux,
mawh kheu ot khuan siyiexb tau mai maix heue.”
Tang-yi-li kah ka ca maox
19 Tang-yi-li ceu kaux nawh baox kaux Paw-thi-sa-sha,
yam mhong ka lox in,
mai yaoh yee tom ang lai chix krai lox lih.
Simiang ah nin:
“Paw-thi-sa-sha eue,
paw kieh ca maox in mai son lox in kieh maix yaoh yee heue.”
Paw-thi-sa-sha pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng eue,
kieh ca maox in mai dee bleeh mawh son pui pa koeng ka maix,
ang mawh son maix.
20 Khaox ting pa lhaong tom cux rao ma an,
kawn pui ka piang haktiex hoik chix yaox ka khawm uik;
21 lhax khaox an kied mhawm,
plix khaox an hoik kub pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik ih.
To sat laux lee tix ka greem veei,
sim saik yuh mheem tix ka piang heue.
22 “Vawng eue,
maix hoik mawh khaox ting an pa ting pa lhaong.
Siawp sibrawm maix ting lhaong tom cux rao ma;
pa kuang maix kaing hoik tauh ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
23 Yam kawn jhak Vawng kah,
koe kawn cau Siyiex lih hoek khaing rao ma,
mai ah nin:
‘Kid khaox in ghrup,
kieh nawh laux,
deuh ngie kak pot ot piang tiex khaing,
teei maox tawng mai maox riem lai lawm kah,
kieh ot tau mai rieb riem daux tiag,
kieh kham pa lan rawm ciex,
kieh ot tau mai to sat aliah num heue.’
24 “Vawng eue,
in mawh dee bleeh ca maox;
Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao ka kaing pa tag sang lih hoek ka piang to kaux maix;
25 tag sang grheu maix lih khaing ka daux kawn pui khankix,
tag sang ot tau mai to sat.
Ka daux aliah num,
maix sang sawm rib awm moi yuh nan,
mai it ka daux tiag,
kham pa lan rawm ciex.
Khaix an,
maix tag sang doh keud ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
mai giex kaing ka piang meung kawn pui haktiex khawm uik;
nawh koe sibrawm tix tawx meung haktiex ka pui pa hoik laug tix khankix.
26 Kawn cau Siyiex tawx lox nhie pui deuh kak graik ka piang tiex;
an hoik krai sidawng,
khaix hoik doh keud maix ka Siyiex giex kaing ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
maix tag sang baox deeh mawh simiang.
27 Mawh kheu nan,
Vawng eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox krai aux:
ang pon tix lai baox yuh phi map,
tag caw tix prawng ka daux pa rauh pa see,
caox rhawm ka pui hot pui sibhawm;c
yuh nin,
maix tom taug deeh yaox tix pon krax pa haok huan heue.”
28 Kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh ka piang to kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha khawm uik heue.
29 Khaix hoik hoek kao ra khix,
yam lai ling nawh ka piang grah nyiex haw simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
30 nawh ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
veng Pa-pi-loeng pa kied ting lhaong heue!
Aux teei siawp sibrawm tix sum ka veng in kieh mawh veng ting ka piang meung khawm uik,
son sang jhi ka min plak ngai mai siawp sibrawm aux heue.”
31 Nawh ang bax hoik krai ka lox in,
lox lih khaing rao ma ah nin:
“Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha,
maix nghiet heue!
Siawp sibrawm simiang mawh maix yam in hoik teei hu heue.
32 Tag sang grheu maix lih hu khaing daux kawn pui,
maix tag sang ot tau mai to sat,
mai sawm rib riem awm moi yuh nan,
koe been aliah num.
Khaix an maix tag sang deeh doh keud ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
pa koe sibrawm tix kuang ka piang meung kawn pui haktiex,
koe sibrawm tix tawx meung haktiex ka pui pa hoik laug tix khankix heue.”
33 Gum lox in hoik lih tix mawh dum mawx dum an.
Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoik grheu lih hu khaing daux kawn pui,
nawh sawm rib awm moi yuh nan,
mai kham pa lan rawm ciex.
Hauk kaing nawh hoik lang awm hauk klang yuh nan,
nghim nawh awm nghim sim yuh nan heue.
Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha yaog yaw Siyiex
34 Simiang ah nin:
“Khaix hoik luan ka daux aliah num,
aux yaog ngai tix jhak ka rao ma;
krax tawng krax yung aux deeh lih koe.
Aux yaog yaw ka cao pa kied ting kied lhaong,
mai tawx min plak ngai ka Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Krax pa kuang nawh ang chix uik leeing ju;
siawp sibrawm nawh toe tix tix ju hoek tix ju heue.
35 Ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
kawn pui ka piang haktiex ang sawn tix mawh tid tix;
kawn cau Siyiex mai kawn pui piang haktiex mawh nawh pa kuang.
Pui mawx pon tieb tie ka alo nawh lie?
Pui mawx pon ne raw ka kaing pa yuh nawh lie?
36 “Yam hoik deeh pon lih koe krax tawng krax yung aux,
min plak ngai aux,
siawp sibrawm aux,
mai pa jhan riang simiang kawx hoik deeh mawh cie aux.
Siliex aux mai simiang pa ot greem aux,
hoik muh ka aux khawm uik;
siawp sibrawm pa deeh pon aux kawn lheu ting khaing sivoe an heue.
37 “Yam in,
aux pa mawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha yaog yaw,
krub,
mai tawx min plak ngai ka Simiang piang rao ma.
Kaing pa yuh nawh rauh see khaw caw;
nawh pon yeen dip ka pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi heue.”
