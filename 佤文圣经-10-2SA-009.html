Ta-wue mai Mi-fe-paw-seu
9
aKoe tix singaix,
Ta-wue chawk ah nin:
“Cub nyiex Sao-law kawn koe ang kix koe pui pa kawn klawd ju lie?
Phan koe,
aux sang kia ngien nawh mhawm kheu Yaw-na-tang heue.”
2 Ka daux cub nyiex Sao-law koe siliex kix tix kaux pa kaux Si-pa.
Koe pui nhie nawh hu ka Ta-wue.
Simiang Ta-wue chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix mawh Si-pa lie?”
Si-pa pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh,
sang krai maix ceu tix krai heue.”
3 bSimiang Ta-wue baox chawk ah nin:
“Ka daux cub nyiex Sao-law kawn koe ang koe pui pa kawn klawd ju lie?
Aux sang hawt awm lox pa hoik ah Siyiex ka aux,
mai kia ngien nawh ka daux krax muh heue.”
Si-pa ah nin:
“Kawn koe kawn simex Yaw-na-tang tix kaux;
ba nawh khawm ra khawn hoik laux meung.”
4 Simiang Ta-wue chawk ah nin:
“Ot nawh dee mawx?”
Si-pa ah nin:
“Ot Law Ti-pa,
daux nyiex ma kawn simex Ya-mi-lix pa kaux Ma-ciex.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ta-wue tom ploi pui hu rieb nawh hoek heue.
6 Yam hoik hoek kawn saux Sao-law kawn simex Yaw-na-tang pa kaux Mi-fe-paw-seu,
nawh yuh krax pa nab yam yung yi mai grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai Ta-wue.
Ta-wue chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mi-fe-paw-seu!”
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh,
sang krai maix ceu tix krai heue.”
7 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat,
aux sang kia ngien maix mhawm kheu keeing maix Yaw-na-tang.
Aux sang deeh tawx haktiex tax maix Sao-law ka maix khawm uik.
Maix chix tix hoek sawm tau mai aux jheeng jheeng heue.”
8 Mi-fe-paw-seu baox krub Ta-wue tix bawg,
ah nin:
“Pie sox tix mu aux ang keung,
maix lai kawn chix kia ngien ka aux yuh nin!”
9 Simiang Ta-wue kok siliex Sao-law pa kaux Si-pa hoek,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux sang deeh tawx sin ngao Sao-law,
mai sin ngao pui nyiex ma nawh ka kawn saux simiang maix pa kaux Mi-fe-paw-seu.
10 Maix mai kawn simex maix khankix,
mai siliex maix tag sang sum kaung ma son cub nyiex ma simiang maix Sao-law,
nghox pa hoik pon pex tawx kix ih,
viang mawh nan phan mawh Mi-fe-paw-seu,
to kaux nawh tag sang mawh nyo pa ih priex tau mai aux ku jheeng jheeng.”
(Si-pa koe kawn simex kao phuan kaux,
koe siliex tix nga kaux heue.)
11 Si-pa pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux sang hawt tix yuh ka lox tawx Vawng ku gum heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Mi-fe-paw-seu hoek sawm tau mai simiangc jheeng jheeng,
awm kawn simex simiang Ta-wue yuh nan.
12 Mi-fe-paw-seu koe kawn simex tix kaux pa kaux Mi-cia.
Can pui nyiex ma Si-pa hoik lih mawh siliex Mi-fe-paw-seu khawm uik.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Mi-fe-paw-seu pa hoik laux meung ba khawm ra khawn tom paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai sawm tau mai simiang jheeng jheeng heue.
