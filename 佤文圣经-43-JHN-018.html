Giex ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.47–56; Lai Ma-kheu 14.43–50; Lai Lu-cia 22.47–53)
18
Khaix hoik rawg Ye-sux buan,
nawh hu tau mai pui gau tix tiang ka rawm klawng Ci-loeng.
Dee an si peem koe ka tix dauh,
Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh laig daux.
2 Yo-tax kaw pa coh Ye-sux kawx hoik tawng ka dee an,
mawh kheu rien khrawm Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh tix ot ka dee an jheeng jheeng.
3 Yo-tax thung pui naing Law-ma tix cub hoek,
pui pau nyiex buan pa ploi pui pa hu tau mai simiang cao giex kaing li,
mai pui Fa-li-saix khankix laig daux si peem.
Kix viex kraung naing khawm uik,
khawm kawk ngu mai ngu proh.
4 Ye-sux hoik tawng kaing ceu pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka to kaux tix khawm uik,
mawh kheu nan hu dex ka kix,
mai chawk kix ah nin:
“Sawg pex pui mawx?”
5 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Sawg Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh aux heue.”
Yam an,
Yo-tax kaw pa coh ka Ye-sux kawx cung tau mai kix.
6 Yam krai Ye-sux “mawh aux heue”,
kix deeh plak khaix tix khawm uik,
mai goh ka piang tiex.
7 Ye-sux baox chawk ah nin:
“Sawg pex pui mawx?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Sawg Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux heue”.
8 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux hoik krai ka pex,
mawh aux heue.
Phan pex sawg ka aux,
kieh pui in kix hu heue.”
9 Ye-sux krai yuh nin,
jiet hoik kieh lih tix mawh ka gum lox pa hoik krai nawh sivoe an ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
pui pa hoik tawx maix ka aux,
aux ang kieh grai tix kaux kawx heue.”
10 Si-meung Pi-teux viex vaig tix mag;
nawh leed vaig lih,
mai thak ka Ma-leu-kux siliex cao giex kaing li pa ting,
thak yhaok plak dawm pui an.
11 aYe-sux ah nin ka Pi-teux:
“Sau vaig ka daux phak heue!
Pex ce aux ang cu tix nyaux ka kawk pa song pa tawx keeing aux aux nyaux lie?”
Ka plak sivoe ngai Ya-na
12 Cub pui naing Law-ma mai cao kuang pui naing,
khawm pui pau nyiex buan pui Yo-thaix giex ka Ye-sux,
phrawk ka nawh,
13 mai ran viex nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ya-na;
Ya-na mawh keeing moeng Kai-ya-fa cao giex kaing li pa ting yam an.
14 bKai-ya-fa in hoik sax mhaing lox ka pui Yo-thaix,
nhie kix kieh pui tix kaux yum tang pui khawm uik,
mai in hoik mawh kaing mhawm tix ceu son kix heue.
Pi-teux krai ang tawng ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.69–70; Lai Ma-kheu 14.66, 68; Lai Lu-cia 22.55–57)
15 Si-meung Pi-teux mai pui gau tang kaux hawt ka Ye-sux;
pui gau an hoik mawh pa sidawng cao giex kaing li pa ting ka tix kaux,
mawh kheu nan kix laig ka biang brawk nyiex cao giex kaing li pa ting tau mai Ye-sux.
16 Pi-teux kaog ot ka plak praix.
Pui gau an pa hoik tawng cao giex kaing li pa ting baox lih,
krai lox ka kawn bun pui pau siviex tix gum,
khaix an viex Pi-teux laig.
17 Kawn bun pui pau siviex ci ka Pi-teux ah nin:
“Maix kawx ang hoik mawh pui gau nawh lie?”
Pi-teux ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh heue!”
18 Yam an praix kied kuat,
siliex an kix mai pui pau phlu ngu,
kix cung tix co ngu;
Pi-teux kawx hoek dex dee an,
mai cung tix co ngu tau mai kix heue.
Cao giex kaing li pa ting chawk ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.59–66; Lai Ma-kheu 14.55, 64; Lai Lu-cia 22.66–71)
19 Cao giex kaing li pa ting chawk Ye-sux ka grawng pui gau nawh mai grawng pa gau nawh.
20 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux krai lox hoik krai ka sivoe ngai pui.
Aux hoik sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex sidiex,
mai ka dee khrawm pui Yo-thaix tix ot jheeng jheeng,
mai ang sax mox tix krai ceu pa tid tix.
21 Yuh ka mawx pex lai chawk ka aux lie?
Hu chawk pui pa hoik mhong ka lox krai aux khankix heue,
kix hoik tawng krai aux ceu pa tix heue.”
22 Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux ka lox in,
pui pau pa ot praok nawh si mang si bax nawh tix taix ah nin:
“Maix lai pon pawk ka lox cao giex kaing li pa ting yuh nin heue!”
23 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan aux lud krai,
maix ci grawng dee lud aux,
phan khaw yuh ka mawx maix lai si mang ka aux lie?”
24 Yam an kix ca kawn phrawk ka Ye-sux,
Ya-na baox hu tawx nawh ka Kai-ya-fa cao giex kaing li pa ting dee an heue.
Pi-teux baox thiang ang tawng ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.71–75; Lai Ma-kheu 14.69–72; Lai Lu-cia 22.58–62)
25 Yam an,
Si-meung Pi-teux kawn cung tix co ngu.
Koe pui ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix kawx ang hoik mawh pui gau pui an lie?”
Pi-teux thiang ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh heue!”
26 Koe siliex cao giex kaing li pa ting tix kaux,
nawh mawh pui nyiex pa hoik mug Pi-teux yhaok cawd,
ah nin:
“Ang aux hoik yaox yam ot maix mai pui an ka daux si peem lie?”
27 Pi-teux baox ah nin:
“Ang mawh heue!”
Bawg yam in ia tom ux.
Kham krax prawng plak sivoe ngai Pi-la-to
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.1–2; 11–14; Lai Ma-kheu 15.1–5; Lai Lu-cia 23.1–5)
28 Kix viex Ye-sux lih khaing nyiex Kai-ya-fa hoek ka nyiex simiang.
Bawg yam an,
praix hoik riang.
Simiang pui Yo-thaix ang bax laig ka daux nyiex cao kuang kaing,
kix sang kieh tix singax ka daux singaix bawg yam yuh poi,
son sang ih ka Poi Luat-luan.
29 Mawh kheu nan,
Pi-la-to hoek chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Sang teei pex phi map ceu pa tix deuh piang pui in lie?”
30 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Phan nawh ang bax yuh kaing caox,
yix ang doh viex nawh hoek dee in heue.”
31 Pi-la-to ah nin ka kix:
“To kaux pex viex nawh hu,
mai hawt tix yuh awm lox tat jhawm prawng pex prawng ka nawh heue.”
Kix ah nin:
“Viang mawh nan,
yix ang koe akhuang tix ciyang pui yum heue.”
32 cIn hoik kieh gum lox pa krai Ye-sux lih tix mawh,
grawng sang yum to kaux nawh yuh ka mawx heue.
33 Pi-la-to baox laig ka daux nyiex cao kuang kaing,
mai kok Ye-sux hoek chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh maix simiang pui Yo-thaix lie?”
34 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pa chawk maix in mawh pa kaoh khaing maix,
phan ang mawh nan,
mhong grawng pa krai lao pui ka aux lie?”
35 Pi-la-to ah nin:
“Maix ce aux mawh pui Yo-thaix lie?
Maix hoik mawh pui pa tawx pui meung haktiex mai simiang cao giex kaing li khankix ka aux.
Maix hoik yuh kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
36 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Meung cie aux ang mawh pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex in;
phan meung cie aux mawh pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex in,
pui cie aux tee rien tix yuh naing son aux,
mai ang kieh aux cawd ka daux tawm taix pui Yo-thaix.
Ang mawh,
meung cie aux ang mawh pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex in heue!”
37 Pi-la-to ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
maix mawh simiang lie?”
Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux mawh simiang,
in hoik mawh maix pa krai heue.
Gum lox tawm aux hoik mawh pa kham sakse son pa mawh pa meh,
aux hoik keh son in,
hoik hoek piang haktiex son in.
Can pui pa mawh pa dex dawt mai pa mawh pa meh,
tag sang nghiet ka lox aux heue.”
38 Pi-la-to chawk ah nin:
“Pa mawh pa meh mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
Prawng Ye-sux yum
(Lai Ma-thaix 27.15–31; Lai Ma-kheu 15.6–20; Lai Lu-cia 23.13–25)
Pi-la-to baox lih hoek,
mai krai ka pui Yo-thaix ah nin:
“Aux ang yaox phi map dee phit pui in tix ceu kawx.
39 Viang mawh nan,
pex koe been pa tat tix deuh,
pex nhie aux ploi pui lud tix kaux kaux son pex yam yuh tix Poi Luat-luan.
Pex sang kieh aux ploi simiang pui Yo-thaix son pex lie?”
40 Kix baox kok yo ah nin:
“Ang mawh nawh!
Pa mhaing yix mawh Pa-la-pa!”
(Pa-la-pa hoik mawh pui caox tix kaux heue.)
