Simiang Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex daux haktiex Maw-ya
29
Lheu khaing yuh Simiang Siyiex sijax mukheu tau mai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka gawng Heu-liex,
nawh kawn baox yuh tau mai kix ka haktiex Maw-ya.
In kix plak greem mawh grawng sijax mukheu pa tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix.
2 Maw-si khrawm ka can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Ngai pex hoik yaox ka ceu pa hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex ka simiang Ai-cix mai siliex nawh khankix,
khawm can ceu pa hoik yuh nawh ka daux meung khawm uik.
3 Pex hoik yaox ka pa hoik ploi Simiang Siyiex ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi pa kied kin an,
mai ka mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh nawh pa kied ting kied lhaong khankix.
4 Viang mawh nan,
hoek ka daux in singaix,
nawh kawn ang nyang bax kieh pex chix tawng sidawng ka kaing pa hoik yaox pex dee bleeh mawh pa yuh ka mawx.
5 Glang ka daux tipon num,
Simiang Siyiex hoik thung pex hu laig ka daux tiag,
kraung ceep pex mai kiiep ceep pex,
kawn ang nyang bax griah.
6 Pex ang koe khao mun,
ang koe plai khien,
mai plai num,
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx can pa sang lo pex ka pex khawm uik,
son sang kieh pex tawng nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
7 aYam hoek ex ka dee in,
Si-hong simiang haktiex Si-seu-peung,
mai Eux simiang haktiex Pa-sang hoek tix puing ka ex;
mai ex pex ka kix,
8 bmai pon ka haktiex kix,
mai guah tix tawx haktiex ka ceu pui Lui-piien,
mai ceu pui Cia-teux,
ra ceu pui mai ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak ceu.
9 Pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox sijax mukheu pa hoik tat tix deuh in khawm uik,
son sang kieh kaing pa yuh pex khawm uik pon mhawm heue.
10-11 “Daux in singaix,
pex ku ceu pui pa mawh cao kaing,
mai kuad song,
mai simiang,
mai can simex bun kawn nyawm ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
khawm pui tang ceu tang cad pa lug pex taung khix,
kin pex nyaux rawm khankix,
12 tag caw tix cung ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Pex tag caw tix rieb ka grawng pa yuh nawh sijax mukheu,
mai gum lox pa tiex tu tau mai pex.
13 Yuh nin,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang chix hawt awm lox pa ap nawh tix deuh ka pex,
mai ka tax du pex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux khankix,
hoek ka daux bawg yam in,
nawh hoik rieb pex mawh pui cie tix,
mai nawh sang mawh Siyiex pex.
14 Simiang Siyiex lao ang yuh sijax mukheu in tau mai pex,
mai ka lox pa tiex tu in,
15 nawh kawx hoik yuh tau mai can pui pa cung ka plak sivoe nawh daux in singaix,
khawm kawn saux kawn sex kix pa ang nyang bax keh khankix heue.
16 “Pex doh keud ka singaix yam ot pex ka daux Ai-cix mawh pa yuh ka mawx,
yam laig pex ka daux meung haktiex pui tang ceu baox mawh pa yuh ka mawx.
17 Pex hoik yaox ka grawng pa siao kix ka khaox ox,
simaox rang,
mai mau khri khankix,
yuh riang pa koeng pui kah.
18 cPex pa cung dee in singaix,
mawh simex bun ang ah,
ceu pui nyiex ma pui ang ah,
mai ceu pui ang ah,
ang pon tix tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex tix kaux kawx,
ang pon tix hu yuh kaing krub ka siyiex tang kaux pui tang meung khankix;
phan koe,
tag sang awm riah khaox pa lih kuah ka khaox pa plix song pa koe jai.
19 “Pex cung dee in in singaix,
hoik mhong ka pui pa kua pa ah ka pui,
mai ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon krai,
viang nawh kiien rhawm mai yuh alo tix,
khawm uik kawn ca chix mhawm;
phan koe,
pui pa yuh nin tag sang prung prang yuh pex,
mai tag kieh pui mhawm pui caox rom tix laux leeing tau.
20 Simiang Siyiex ang doh pluat ploi ka pui pa yuh nin;
krax song rhawm Siyiex tag sang hoek ka piang nawh.
Gum lox jhax lawg pa hoik tiiem ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng tag sang hoek ka piang to kaux nawh,
tom hoek yuh Siyiex nawh laux lak pak leeing.
21 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh nawh mawh mhai pa sidiex ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ku ceu pui,
tag hawt awm pa hoik tiiem tix deuh ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng,
pa mawh krax jhax lawg ku ceu ka piang sijax mukheu,
mai kieh krax laux hoek ka piang to kaux nawh heue.
22 “Doeh ceu pui kawn praix pex,
mai pui tang ceu pa ot dee singai khankix,
tag sang yaox pa ploi Simiang Siyiex krax laux krax leeing,
mai jhawm saux lih hoek ka piang haktiex ot pex.
23 dKaung ma pex tag sang riem,
haktiex khawm uik hoik mawh mhat mai kih,
ang lai pon sum,
pie rib riem kawx ang lai pon kuah.
Haktiex pex tag sang awm yam hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex ka veng Saw-to-ma mai Eu-maw-la,
mai Ya-ma,
mai Si-piien ka daux pa song rhawm song rhi tix.
24 “Yam an,
can ceu pui ka piang haktiex khankix tag sang chawk ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx,
nawh lai yuh nin ka haktiex in lie?
Nawh yuh ka mawx song rhawm song rhi lie?’
25 Koe pui khankix tag dee pawk ah nin:
‘Mawh kheu pui cie Simiang Siyiex hoik tik vud ka gum lox sijax mukheu pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix tau mai kix;
sijax mukheu in hoik mawh pa yuh Simiang Siyiex tau mai kix,
khaix hoik viex nawh kix lih khaing Ai-cix.
26 Kix hoik tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex mai hu yuh kaing jhaw siyiex tang kaux khankix,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux pa ang kix sax krub sax yi,
pa mawh siyiex tang kaux pa ang Simiang Siyiex cu kix krub kah.
27 Mawh kheu nan,
krax song rhawm song rhi Simiang Siyiex hoek ka piang kix,
mai kieh krax jhax lawg pa hoik tiiem tix deuh daux phuk lai in,
lih hoek ka piang haktiex ot kix.
28 Ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi,
Simiang Siyiex reeig kix khawm riah khaing haktiex ot kix,
mai tik kix ot meung tang meung.
Tom in singaix,
mai kix ca kawn ot dee an heue.’
29 “Ka daux kaing tix blah,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex hoik mox glip ka ex;
viang mawh nan,
nawh plak jhi lox tat jhawm prawng tix ka ex,
ex mai kawn praix ex tag caw priex tix hawt leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
