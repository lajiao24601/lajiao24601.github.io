Lox rhax daux poi
81
Tag caw tix gaux rhawm ka Siyiex pa kia ngien ka ex!
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex Ya-keux!
2 Tag caw tix rhax lai,
tag caw tix tauh kraung;
tag caw tix teei ting jung lah mai ting dia lah tauh lox rhax pa nyawm tix nghiet kah.
3 aKa daux pa haok khix tix kao ngaix phuan,
tag caw tix phlu teet yuh krax gaux rhawm.
4 In mawh lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mawh gum lox tawx Siyiex ka Ya-keux.
5 Yam yaog nawh tix kaoh puing ka pui Ai-cix,
tawx gum lox ping nyap in ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
 
Aux mhong ka lox pa ah pui pa ang tix sax tawng ah nin:
6 aux hoik gah ka beeh glawm pa cien pa koe ka piang blawg maix;
aux hoik kieh maix ploi ka khrawng pa deuh lon tiex gleeing naog daux.
7 bYam si pup maix ka krax pa kha rhawm,
maix khiox khaw ka aux,
mai aux hoik taoh ka maix.
Aux hoik pawk lox maix khaing daux bhaung ting pa glip;
aux hoik cheuk tix yaox maix ka praok rawm keet Mi-li-pa;
8 pui cie aux eue,
nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau aux heue;
pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux kied simie maix nghiet ka lox tix tete heue!
9 cMaix ang doh pon tix yuh kaing jhaw siyiex pui tang kaux tang hak;
maix ang pon tix krub ka siyiex tang kaux lheu khaing aux.
10 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix;
aux viex maix lih khaing Ai-cix.
Ang jheeh maix heue,
aux tag sang tawx maix sawm sak heue.
 
11 Viang mawh nan,
pui cie aux ang nghiet ka lox aux;
pui Yi-seu-liex ang cu tix nghiet ka aux.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ghram kix kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
kieh kix nied tix yuh ka kaing pa daox aw.
13 Aux kied simie pui cie tix cu nghiet ka lox!
Aux kied simie kix cu nghiet ka tix!
14 Aux sang nied tix puing ka pui pa koeng ka kix laung laung,
pon kuang ka can pui pa tieb tie ka kix khawm uik.
15 Pui pa koeng ka aux,
tag sang dip ka plak sivoe ngai aux;
kix tag sang pon krax pa ciyang leeing ju.
16 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang tawx pex ih ka simie nghox ca pa blawn mhawm,
kieh pex yaox tix ih ka rawm hia tiag pa nyawm tie heue.
