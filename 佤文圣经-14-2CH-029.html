Si-si-cia Simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 18.1–3)
29
Yam koe ju Si-si-cia tix nga phuan num nawh mawh simiang Yo-tax,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung tix nga dim num.
Miex nawh Ya-pi-yax mawh kawn bun Sa-cia-li-ya.
2 Si-si-cia hawt ka kaing mhawm pa yuh tax du tix simiang Ta-wue,
mai yuh kaing pa mhawm plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
Kieh nyiex buan singax singeh
3 Yam mawh Si-si-cia simiang num an ka daux khix tix,
nawh deeh cox paoh ka siviex nyiex buan,
mai cox ka nyiex buan.
4 Nawh khrawm ka cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we hoek ka biang brawk nyiex buan plak lih singaix,
5 mai ah nin ka kix dee an:
“Pex pui Li-we tag caw tix kieh to kaux tix singax singeh,
mai kieh nang nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex singax singeh,
teei tix tik ka ceu pa seeing pa brai lih hu khawm uik.
6 Tax du ex ang yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
tik vud ka nawh,
ang kia ngien ka dee ot nawh.
7 Kix nyeep ka siviex nyiex buan,
mai preet ngu yeet,
ang gleeing ka nyawng bhawng,
mai ka nyiex buan Siyiex Yi-seu-liex kawx ang tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom song rhawm ka Yo-tax mai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Grawng kaing pa yuh nawh hoik kieh pui yaoh yee,
lhat graung,
ah lox salao.
In kix khawm uik pex hoik tawng sidawng.
9 Ju keeing ex hoik yum ka bang naing,
moeng kawn simex kawn bun hoik giex viex hu heue.
10 “Yam in,
aux but rhawm tix yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex,
son ang nawh sang baox lai song rhawm ka ex.
11 Kawn aux khankix eue,
ang pon tix lai dha bawg yam,
pex hoik mawh pui pa laug Simiang Siyiex hoek tix gleeing nyawng bhawng ka nawh,
thung kawn pui krub ka nawh heue.”
12-14 Pui Li-we pa ot dee an mawh:
cub nyiex Keu-siax:
Ma-ha kawn simex Ya-ma-saix mai Yaw-eux kawn simex Ya-sa-li-ya.
Cub nyiex Mi-la-lix:
Ci-see kawn simex Ya-paw-ti mai Ya-sa-li-ya kawn simex Yie-ha-li-leux.
Cub nyiex Keu-soeng:
Yaw-yax kawn simex Sing-ma mai Yi-tiien kawn simex Yaw-yax.
Cub nyiex Yi-li-sha-fang:
Sheung-lix mai Yie-lix.
Cub nyiex Ya-sa:
Sa-cia-li-ya mai Ma-thang-ya.
Cub nyiex Si-mang:
Yie-shiie mai See-me.
Cub nyiex Yie-tu-toeng:
See-ma-ya mai Wu-shiie.
15 Pui in kix khrawm pux aik tix ceu pui Li-we,
mai hoik kieh to kaux tix singax singeh ka daux been,
khaix an priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx simiang,
mai hawt tix yuh awm lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
jauh tix kieh nyiex buan singax singeh.
16 Cao giex kaing li laig tix kieh nyiex buan singax singeh.
Kix gah ka can kraung ceu pa ang singax singeh ka biang brawk nyiex buan,
pui Li-we baox deeh gah lih plak praix veng,
mai tik daux rawm klawng iak Ci-loeng heue.
17 Kaing in jauh tix yuh ka daux khix tix singaix tix,
bleeh ka daux daix singaix kaing in hoik hoik khawm uik,
khawm sivoe ngai nyiex buan kawx hoik kieh singax.
Khaix an kix deeh baox yuh kaing daix singaix,
hoek khix an daux kao liah singaix,
mai hoik rien khre ka nyiex buan mhawm,
son sang krub ka Siyiex heue.
Deeh cox tan lu ka nyiex buan
18 Pui Li-we krai ka simiang Si-si-cia ah nin:
“Yix hoik kieh nyiex buan singax singeh khawm uik ka daux been,
khawm khu tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
pheen pa deuh khao mun ot piang,
mai can kraung khrai pa iak pa lied khankix hoik kieh singax khawm uik.
19 Yix kawx hoik deeh viex ka kraung khrai can pa viex simiang Ya-ha-see hu yam tieh vheen tix Siyiex;
yix hoik deeh cox tan lu ka kraung khrai in kix,
yam in hoik deuh ot ka sivoe khu dee tan cie Simiang Siyiex heue.”
20 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
simiang Si-si-cia khrawm simiang pa ot ka daux veng khankix hoek tau ka nyiex buan.
21 Son sang pluat ploi ka phi map nyiex simiang,
phi map kawn pui Yo-tax,
khawm pa kieh nyiex buan singax singeh,
kix viex moi mhaing hoek aliah mu,
yung mhaing aliah mu,
kawn pex mhaing aliah mu,
pex mhaing aliah mu hoek.
Simiang tawx lox ka cao giex kaing li doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng,
kieh to tiag in kix mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa tan ka piang khu dee tan.
22 Cao giex kaing li khankix ran pauh ka moi mhaing sivoe,
khaix an taug pauh ka yung,
mai khaix an pauh ka kawn pex,
mai phruah nham khawng brie an kix ku mu ka piang khu dee tan,
23 dee nghoik,
tom tuk pex hoek ka simiang mai ka plak sivoe ngai pui pa yuh krax krub khankix,
kix tom deuh taix tix piang kaing pex an.
24 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li pauh ka pex an,
mai hieh nham ka piang khu dee tan,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
son sang teei tix tik ka phi map kawn pui,
mawh kheu simiang tawx lox,
tag caw tix tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap son kawn pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
25 Si-si-cia hawt tix yuh awm lox pa nhie Simiang Siyiex cao jhai Cia-teux mai cao jhai Na-tang krai ka simiang Ta-wue,
tawx pui Li-we mawh cao pa yuh kaing ka nyiex buan,
viex ka ting jung lah,
ting aliah lah,
mai tauh ka chiang.
26 Pui Li-we viex ka kraung ting pa siao baux bawg yam singaix Ta-wue,
mai cao giex kaing li khankix kawx viex teet tawng cung dee an.
27 Si-si-cia tawx lox nhie pui tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka piang khu.
Yam jauh tix sang tan lu krax tan,
kawn pui yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
sara pui tauh ting mai pui tauh ting kawx jauh tix phlu ka teet tawng mai tauh ka ting tang ceu khankix.
28 Can pui hun khawm uik hoek tix yuh krax krub,
pui pa kied chix rhax lai rhax ka lai,
pui pa phlu teet tawng yaog tix phlu ka teet tawng,
tom hoik hoik gleeing krax tan uik.
29 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Si-si-cia mai kawn pui khawm uik krub ka Siyiex.
30 Simiang meung mai simiang khankix tawx lox nhie pui Li-we yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex,
rhax lai pa tiiem simiang Ta-wue mai cao pa chix yaox sivoe Ya-sa.
Pui haoh pui hun hoik gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix rhax lai khawm uik,
khaix an krub ka Siyiex heue.
31 Si-si-cia ah nin ka pui hun:
“Yam in pex hoik singax singeh,
viex khawng tan hoek kieh mawh krax tan pa yaog yaw tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex heue!”
Kix tom nghiet ka lox simiang,
ka daux kix koe pui tix blah tang gaux rhawm tix viex khawng tan hoek,
mai pauh kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
32 Kix viex moi mhaing hoek atigliah mu,
yung mhaing tix yieh mu,
kawn yung mhaing ra yieh mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai tan tix tawx ka Simiang Siyiex.
33 Kix kawx viex moi mhaing hoek liah yieh mu mai yung loe rheng mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa tawx pui haoh pui hun ih.
34 Mawh kheu kied nie khawng brie,
cao giex kaing li pa pauh khawng brie ang kub,
paox ceu paox cad kix pui Li-we tom teem coi ka kix,
tom hoik hoik kiang yuh.
Yam an,
koe cao giex kaing li tom nie hoik kieh to kaux tix singax singeh.
Pui Li-we nie kawn lheu chix priex tix hawt been pa singax khaing cao giex kaing li.
35 Lheu khaing tan lu Krax-tan Pa gleeing khawm mu,
cao giex kaing li khankix kawx mawh cao pa gleeing ka bee vaig Krax-tan Pa-nhim pa ih kawn pui khankix,
mai hieh plai pa rom tix tan tau mai Krax-tan Pa gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-jauh lih heue.
Yuh nin,
mai baox deeh yaox tix yuh krax krub ka daux nyiex buan.
36 Simiang Si-si-cia mai kawn pui kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu hoik teem coi Siyiex kix khran hoik yuh ka kaing in heue.
