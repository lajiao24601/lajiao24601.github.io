Simiang Siyiex deuh lox deeh cox sum ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
8
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing tawx gum lox in plak greem ka Sa-cia-li-yax.
Nawh ah nin:
2 “Aux kied simie tix teem coi ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu kied muh aux ka kawn pui pa koe dee an,
mawh kheu nan aux tawx krax pa song rhawm tix ka pui pa koeng ka nawh.
3 Aux tag sang deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung pa mawh veng pa singax cie aux;
aux tag sang paung ot dee an.
Kawn pui tag sang kok Yie-lu-sa-leung mawh ‘veng pa kaux sijax’,
mai yaog yaw gawng Simiang Siyiexa,
pa mawh simiang kru naing mawh gawng pa singax.
4 Simex bun pa hoik riex ju,
pa gix ka khaox gix khankix,
tag sang baox ngawm ka biang brawk ka daux veng.
5 Ka daux laih tag sang baox si nghox ka kawn simex kawn bun pa nyawm klieh heue.
6 “Yam in kawn pui pa kawn deuk khankix kawx tee ce kaing in ang pon lih tix mawh,
viang mawh nan,
phan mawh ka son aux,
kaing in ang doh ang pon lih tix mawh.
7 Aux tag sang taoh pui cie tix lih khaing haktiex pa hoik giex laung kix hu ot kah,
8 mai jauh tix viex kix lih khaing plak lih singaix,
khaing plak klip singaix,
kieh kix paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux;
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix,
mai kuang kix ka daux pa kaux sijax mai ka daux pa rauh see tix heue.
9 “Tag caw tix kaux cong heue!
Lox pa hoik mhong pex yam in,
hoik mawh gum lox pa krai cao jhai yam taug deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan,
mai ka riah nyiex buan.
10 Ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
ang koe pui pa tien kok tix siao pui mai ka khawng brie,
ka daux pa jiang pui koeng ang koe pui pa pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mawh kheu kieh aux kawn pui ra lai yuh naing ka paox tix.
11 Viang mawh nan,
yam in aux ang lai awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan ka kawn pui pa deuk khankix.
12 Kix tag sang sum sang van ka daux krax pa yien pa yawg;
khaox plix siiet tag sang plix plix,
haktiex tag sang lih tawx ka nghox koi;
rawm lhiex hoik kub.
Aux tag sang tawx buan son in kix ka kawn pui pa deuk khankix.
13 Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pui tang ceu tang cad hoik ra lai jhax lawg ah nin:
‘Kieh krax laux leeing pa si pup pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex lih hoek ka piang to kaux maix heue!’
Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang taoh ka pex;
yam an,
pui tang ceu tang cad tag sang ra lai ah nin:
‘Kieh maix pon ka buan son pa hoik pon pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex heue!’
Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix kaux cong,
paw vi lhat graung heue.”
14 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Yam cheeik tax du pex tix kieh aux song rhawm,
aux hoik but rhawm tix sang ploi krax laux leeing lih hoek piang kix,
aux hoik yuh awm pa tat tix deuh,
mai ang lai loh ka rhawm keud tix.
15 Viang mawh nan,
yam in aux but rhawm tix tawx buan ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka pui Yo-tax.
Mawh kheu nan,
pex paw vi lhat graung.
16 bPa khaw pex tix rien rhawm tix ka mawh:
krai lox pa mawh pa meh;
prawng see son pui tag caw tix kieh mawh ka daux krax pa rauh see,
mai ka daux krax pa kieh khaw caw rhawm paox;
17 ang pon tix keud sawn son sang yuh pui tang kaux laux leeing;
ang pon tix kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh.
Kaing in kix khawm uik hoik mawh pa koeng aux ka heue.”
18 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing baox tawx gum lox in plak greem ka Sa-cia-li-yax.
Nawh ah nin:
19 “Yam tuah priex preem ka daux khix pon,
khix phuan,
khix aliah,
khix kao,
tag sang lih mawh poi pa gaux rhawm ka pui Yo-tax.
Pex tag caw tix muh ka pa mawh pa meh mai ka pa nhim pa tiiem heue.”
20 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Bawg yam hoik dex sang hoek,
yam an kawn pui tag sang kaoh khaing veng c ku veng,
mai hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
21 Pui pa ot ka veng in tag sang krai ka pui pa ot ka veng an ah nin:
‘Nied ex hu krub ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
mai khiox khaw ka nawh tawx buan son.
Pex hu tau mai yix heue!’
22 Ceu pui tom nie mai meung haktiex pa ting riang tag sang hoek krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai khiox khaw nawh tawx buan son.
23 Ka daux singaix an,
pui tang ceu kao kaux tag sang hoek ka pui Yo-tax tix kaux dee an,
mai ah nin:
‘Kieh yix hu tau mai pex heue,
mawh kheu hoik mhong yix ot Siyiex tau mai pex heue.’ ”
