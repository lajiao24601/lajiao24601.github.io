Grawng pa tieh pui Yo-thaix
8
Ngaix an,
simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu tawx sin ngao cie Ha-mang pui pa koeng ka Yo-thaix ka Yi-see-thie.
Yi-see-thie krai ka simiang,
Maw-ti-kai mawh paox nyiex dex nawh.
Jauh yam an,
Maw-ti-kai pon tix hoek ka plak ngai simiang.
2 Simiang teei cum giien tai pa teei khaing Ha-mang,
mai tawx ka Maw-ti-kai.
Yi-see-thie kawx tawx Maw-ti-kai mawh cao pa kuang sin ngao Ha-mang.
3 Khaix an,
Yi-see-thie baox krub ka sivoe simiang,
yiiem mai khiox khaw,
rawg simiang tieb tie ka kaing pa yuh Ha-mang pui Ya-cia pa sang kieh pui Yo-thaix laux leeing.
4 Simiang tawx khaox gix khri ka Yi-see-thie,
Yi-see-thie tom yaog tix kaoh cung,
ah nin:
5 “Phan aux pon krax pa caox rhawm Vawng,
Vawng muh ka aux,
keud krax rawg aux mawh pa mhawm,
tom tawx lox tix gum,
ang kieh lox pa tawx Ha-mang lih mawh.
Ha-mang kawn simex Ha-mi-ta-tha pui Ya-cia sang kieh pui Yo-thaix laux leeing ka daux meung khawm uik.
6 Aux chix pon tix yaox ka krax laux leeing paox ceu pui tix lie?
Aux chix pon yaox ka krax pa pauh pui paox nyiex aux lie?”
7 Simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu ah nin ka moeng tix Yi-see-thie mai pui Yo-thaix pa kaux Maw-ti-kai:
“Yuh yuh nin heue!
Kheu grawng sang pauh Ha-mang pui Yo-thaix,
aux hoik teug teue nawh yum,
mai baox tawx sin ngao nawh ka Yi-see-thie.
8 Viang mawh nan,
lox pa tawx ka min simiang,
ka piang hoik koe cum cie simiang,
ang chix kieh ang lih.
Viang mawh nan,
aux chix tix kieh pex hawt ka rhawm keud pex tawx lox ka pui Yo-thaix;
pex kawx chix tix tawx lox ka min aux,
mai tawk ka cum heue.”
9 Kheu nan,
ka daux khix loe,
pa mawh Khix Si-vuang daux tix nga ngaix loe,
Maw-ti-kai tom kok ka cao tiiem lai simiang hoek,
kieh kix tawx lox ka pui Yo-thaix,
khawm simiang haktiex mai cao kaing daux Ying-tux hoek ka Ku-seu khawm uik koe tix yieh tix nga aliah kaux.
Lox pa tawx in mawh pa tiiem ka lai haktiex ku ceu mai lai pui Yo-thaix.
10 Maw-ti-kai siao ka min simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu,
tawk ka cum simiang,
mai tawx ka gum lox in,
mai kieh pui tawx lai brug ka brung pa kied phai ka daux nyiex haw tawx lox in heue.
11 Gum lox in ah,
simiang cu pui Yo-thaix ku haktiex tang rien rhawm tix.
Phan yaox krax naing haktiex mawx,
meung mawx pa sang puing ka kix,
kix chix tix tieb tie,
pauh ka pui,
kieh pui an kix laux leeing,
mai moeng kix,
kawn simex kawn bun kix kawx chix tix yuh laux leeing,
mai teei ka sin ngao cie kix heue.
12 Gum lox in tawx ka meung haktiex khawm uik,
ka daux khix kao ra,
pa mawh khix Ya-ta kao ngaix loe,
kieh lih tix mawh bawg yam singaix sang pauh ka pui Yo-thaix heue.
13 Gum lox in sang tawx lih awm tawx lox tat jhawm prawng yuh nan,
son sang kieh pui ku kaux tawng ka daux haktiex ku dauh.
Yuh nin,
pui Yo-thaix tag rien rhawm tix,
son sang pawk bix ka pui pa koeng ka daux singaix an heue.
14 Kheu nan,
cao pa tawx lai hawt ka lox pa tawx simiang,
brug ka brung phai cie simiang,
nied tix tawx gum lox in;
gum lox in kawx tawx ka daux veng ting ot simiang veng ting Su-sang heue.
15 Maw-ti-kai ceep ka kraung klai paing,
lhop ka man keei sum pa lung pliex piang klip tix,
ceep ka mhok khri lih khaing nyiex haw simiang;
veng ting Su-sang khawm uik hoik naog krax gaux rhawm kah.
16 Son pui Yo-thaix,
in mawh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
krax mhawm rhawm,
kaing pa ting min.
17 Gum lox pa tawx simiang hoik tau hu ka haktiex ku dauh,
pui Yo-thaix hoik phat kah,
kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
yuh poi laux lee gaux rhawm ka heue.
Pui haktiex an tom nie lhat ka pui Yo-thaix tom laig ka cub ca rang pui Yo-thaix heue.
