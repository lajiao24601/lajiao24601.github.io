Lai Sidiex
Krai dawt
Lai Sidiex tiiem tix deuh ka krax keud krax sawn “pui cao sidiex.” Nawh bre tix keud grawng pa dawt ju kawn pui, mai ka kaing pa nu tix tawng khankix ka daux rhawm rhi tix, khawm kaing ceu pa ang chix si dawm khaw paox khankix, mai kaing ceu pa kieh pui hoek krax laux rhawm laux rhi khankix. Gum lox pa pon nawh dee si dauh mawh: “Kawn pui pa giex pa going!” Nawh ang tawng ka daux krax pa hoik giex Siyiex ka ju kawn pui. Viang mawh nan, nawh ca kawn sidiex kawn pui sau riang rao tix yuh kaing jhaw, mai tawng tix ih nyaux ka buan son pa hoik tawx Siyiex heue.
Cao sidiex awm pa krai ka krax laux rhawm tom nie, mai ka rhawm keud pa cien pa nu. Kheu nan, ka daux phuk lai sigang khri hoik koe ka lox lai pa yuh nin tix phuk, mai hoik pon kieh pui tawng ka daux phuk lai sigang khri pa hoik grum krai ka ku ceu, viang mawh khawm lox pa kieh pui laux rhawm, khawm rhawm rhi krax yum rhawm pa ang kaux cong khankix. Pui tom nie hoik yaox sidawng ka pak peung to kaux tix, kheu nan tom pon krax pa doh rhawm mai krax pa nyawm rhawm, khaix an hoik pon yaox ka daux phuk lai sigang khri tix phuk khaing, pa mawh krax yum pa tawx ka Siyiex pa hoik naog ka krax keud pot jun pot ku dauh ku dee; krax yum rhawm in kieh kawn pui blawn ting ceu heue.
Viang mawh nan, dee nghoik lox lai in, cao sidiex kieh mawh gum lox pa kieh pui koe krax gaux rhawm pa ah nin: “Tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex, mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawm jhawm krai nawh, kheu hoik mawh in pa caw pui tix yuh ku kaux heue.”
Krai kaing
1. Krax suk sag ka daux kawn pui (1.1–2.26)
2. Gum lox sidiex pa khaw mai kawn pui (3.1–11.8)
3. Grawng pa sidiex ka pui pa nhum ju (11.9–12.8)
4. Gum lox pa nghoik (12.9–14)
