Been pa deuh tax tu
(Lai Ma-thaix 15.1–20)
7
Pui Fa-li-saix mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri tix blah kaoh khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung rom tix hoek ka Ye-sux.
2 Kix yaox pui tix blah ka daux pui gau nawh sawm ka taix pa ang singax tix,
pa mawh pa ang bax khoek taix tix awm pui Fa-li-saix yuh nan heue.
3 Kheu pui Fa-li-saix mai pui Yo-thaix khankix hoik dawg mawh pui pa hawt ka been tax du,
phan ang hawt ka been pa khoek ka taix tix ang pon tix sawm;
4 ceu pa liag hoek khaing daux laih phan ang ran si ghrah sivoe ang pon tix ih nang.a
Kix kawn hawt ka been tang ceu pa koe tom nie khankix,
awm sang phak ka kawk nyaux,
aw,
kraung tawng mai kub khankix yuh ka mawx heue.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Fa-li-saix mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri chawk ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pui gau maix ang hawt ka been pa deuh tax du,
mai hoik sawm ka taix pa ang singax tix lie?”
6 cYe-sux ah nin:
“Yi-sai-yax krai lox jhai ka pex pa mawh cub pui pa pie lhien in kix hoik khaw tete;
nawh ah nin:
“Siyiex krai yuh nin:
pui in kix teei siyieb jheeh tix nab yam yung yi ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi kix singai khaing aux.
7 Kix kieh been pa tat kawn pui tix deuh mawh gum lox tawx aux;
krax pa krub kix ka aux mawh pa giex going khawm uik heue!”
8 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex pa mawh pa hawt ka been kawn pui,
mai hoik tik vud ka gum lox tawx Siyiex.”
9 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Krax pa chix pex tieb tie ka gum lox tawm Siyiex,
mawh son sang hawt pex ka been kawn pui.
10 dMaw-si tawx lox ka pex ah nin:
‘Tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka miex keeing tix;’
mai baox tat tix deuh ah nin:
‘Pa jhax lawg ka miex keeing tix tag caw tix pauh yum.’
11 Pex ca kawn krai,
phan koe pui pa teei ceu pa tawx miex keeing tix ih kieh mawh ‘Keu-eu-pang’ (dee bleeh mawh khawng tan pa tan lu ka Siyiex),
12 nawh tag ang lai siao eei ka miex keeing tix.
13 Pex yuh nin hoik awm teei pex been pa tawx ka kawn pui kieh mawh pa tieb tie ka gum lox Siyiex.
Pex kawn yuh ka kaing pa awm in tom nie heue.”
Ceu pa kieh pui ang singax singeh
(Lai Ma-thaix 15.10–20)
14 Ye-sux baox khrawm pui hun hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux khawm uik,
tag caw tix tawng heue.
15 Ceu pa laig ka plak daux kawn pui ang chix kieh pui ang singax;
deeh tieh tix,
mawh pa lih khaing plak daux pui pa kieh chix ang singax heue.e”
17 Yam lih hu Ye-sux khaing pui hun laig daux nyiex,
pui gau chawk nawh ka dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem in.
18 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex kawx ang tawng awm kix lie?
Pex ang tawng lie?
Ceu pa laig ka plak daux kawn pui an ang chix kieh nawh ang singax;
19 mawh kheu ceu pa laig ka plak daux ang hoek ka daux rhawm nawh,
mai hoek ka daux vaig nawh,
khaix an aing tix tik lih.”
(Ye-sux krai ka grawng priex preem khawm uik hoik mawh pa singax heue.)
20 Nawh nied tix ah nin:
“Ceu pa ang kieh pui singax an mawh pa kaoh khaing plak daux.
21 Mawh kheu pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm pui mawh pa caox caig ku ceu.
Pa caox in kix kieh nawh yuh phi map pa phit vao mai pui,
briex lak,
pauh pui,
22 phit vao,
ghoing rhawm,
caox caig,
pie lhien,
pa simie tix yuh ceu pa ang khaw ang jiet,
boh rhawm,
map nai,
pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
cau nyhau khankix.
23 Krax pa caox pa caig in khawm uik hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing plak daux kawn pui pa kieh pui ang singax tete heue.”
Krax yum rhawm bun tix kaux
(Lai Ma-thaix 15.21–28)
24 Ye-sux lih khaing haktiex an,
mai hoek ka baoh haktiex Thai-eux.
Nawh paung ot ka nyiex pui tix nyiex mai ang rhawm kieh pui tawng,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon mox tix glip.
25 Dum mawx dum an,
koe bun tix kaux,
khuan pa seeing brai laig daux kawn bun nawh,
nawh mhong grawh grawng Ye-sux,
nawh tom hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
26 Bun in mawh pui meung tang meung,
pa keh ka Fe-ni-ci haktiex Shui-li-yax.
Nawh khiox khaw Ye-sux grheu phi lih khaing to kaux kawn bun tix.
27 Ye-sux ah nin ka nawh:
“Ran kieh kawn simex kawn bun maix sawm sak heue.
Teei tix tawx kawn sox ih ka priex preem cie kawn simex kawn bun ang khaw heue.”
28 Bun an pawk ah nin:
“Mawh tete,
simiang eue,
viang mawh nan,
kawn sox pa ot greem pheen hoik ih pa max pa phawng pa deuk khaix ih kawn nyawm an nang heue!”
29 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Kheu gum lox pa hoik krai maix in,
maix chix tix ing ka nyiex heue;
phi hoik lih khaing ka daux kawn bun maix heue!”
30 Mawh kheu nan,
bun an ing ka nyiex,
mai yaox it kawn bun tix ka piang ku;
phi hoik lih hu khaing nawh heue.
Ye-sux pai ka pui lheet mai pui ang chix nye moi
31 Ye-sux lih hu khaing baoh haktiex dex Thai-eux,
mai laig ka daux haktiex Si-toeng,
jauh ka mee haktiex See-yix deeh hoek ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix.
32 Koe pui viex pui lheet pui kle hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux tix kaux,
mai khiox khaw Ye-sux deuh taix tix piang kaing nawh.
33 Ye-sux teei nawh lih khaing daux pui hun viex ot tix plak,
mai teei giien taix tix chup ka daux yhaok nawh,
mai baox teei rawm mia tix pu phat ka piang dak nawh.
34 Ye-sux maong kaing tix jhak ka rao ma,
mai sau riang tix ngia tix gum ah nin ka pui an:
“Yi-fa-tax heue!”
Dee bleeh mawh “Ang jheeh” heue!
35 Yhaok pui an baoh dum mawx dum an,
dak nawh kawx hoik chix chuh tix,
nawh tom ang jheeh tix si kah,
mai ang lai mawh pa nu.
36 Ye-sux tawx lox ka pui hun ang doh cu kix krai grawng kaing in ka pui;
viang mawh nan,
nawh nied ah nan,
kix nied gaux rhawm tix krai hu.
37 Can pui pa hoik mhong ku kaux hoik daox deng ka khawm uik;
kix ah nin:
“Can kaing pa hoik yuh nawh kied mawh pa mhawm tete heue!
Mawh kheu hoik kieh nawh pui lheet chix mhong,
pui ang chix nye chix si kah heue!”
