Lox rhax yiiem doe
19
Siyiex nhie aux rhax lox rhax yiiem doe in son simiang Yi-seu-liex ra kaux ah nin:
2 Miex maix mawh miex sivai sang si,
pa ngawm tix ot ka daux phung sivai sang si mhaing;
nawh ot ka daux phung sivai sang si mhaing,
mai eei ting ka kawn sivai sang si.
3 Nawh eei kawn sivai sang si ting lih mawh sivai sang si nhum ju pa mhawm kluing;
sivai sang si nhum ju pa mhawm kluing gau tix chix hu sawg kug,
mai lih mawh to sat pa ih nex pui.
4 Meung ka piang haktiex hoik mhong kah,
mai nyaw glaw nawh laig ka daux o pa raux;
kix teei goik viex nawh hu ka Ai-cix.
5 Miex sivai sang si deek rhawm deek rhi;
nawh baox eei ka kawn sivai sang si tix mu lih mawh sivai sang si nhum ju pa mhawm kluing.
6 Sivai sang si nhum ju pa mhawm kluing an hawt lai ling hu ku dauh ku dee tau mai sivai sang si tang mu;
nawh kawx hoik gau tix chix sawg ka kug,
mai lih mawh to sat pa ih pui.
7 Nawh hoik yuh laux ka veng;
kieh veng laux leeing;
yam graox nawh,
pui piang haktiex hoik si yaung khawm uik.
8 Meung ka piang haktiex hoek tix lai lawm ka nawh khawm pon daog;
kix tawm ka rub,
mai nyaw glaw nawh laig ka daux o pa raux.
9 Kix laung nawh ot ka daux gang,
mai viex hu ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng dee an;
kix viex nawh laig daux thawng,
gawng lua Yi-seu-liex khankix kawx ang lai baox mhong ka lox graox nawh heue.
10 Miex maix awm khaox plix siiet tix gawng pa sum ot ka praok rawm klawng iak yuh nan,
mawh kheu hoik haoh dee keet rawm,
lhax kak hoik yeem,
mai plix plix tom nie.
11 Kak nawh kied kiien,
chix tix yuh khaox gix simiang;
kak nawh ting huan hu plak piang,
mai kied mhawm yeem;
kak nawh lhaong cux ka pai awm,
mai kawn pui chix yaox ka ku kaux.
12 Viang mawh nan,
koe taix pa song rhawm laoh nawh khawm riah;
si blat nawh ot ka piang tiex,
mai kieh bhaung plak lih singaix phlu kroh,
kak hoik pot,
plix hoik kroh,
ngu hoik gleeing ka khawm uik.
13 Yam in gah nawh hu ot daux tiag daux tu,
mai sum ot ka dee kroh tiex dee ang rawm koe.
14 Khaox kroh ngu gleeing kah,
mai hoik gleeing kak mai plix khawm uik;
nawh ang lai baox koe kak pa kiien,
pa chix tix yuh khaox gix simiang kah.
In hoik mawh lox rhax yiiem doe tix mu;
lox rhax yiiem doe in hoik toe tix hu ang jud heue.
