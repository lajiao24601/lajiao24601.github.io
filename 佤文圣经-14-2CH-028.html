Ya-ha-see simiang Yo-tax
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 16.1–4)
28
Yam koe ju Ya-ha-see tix nga num nawh mawh simiang,
mai giex kaing ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung kao liah num.
Nawh ang hawt tix yuh ka kaing mhawm pa yuh tax du tix simiang Ta-wue,
mai ang yuh kaing khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
2 Nawh hawt ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh simiang Yi-seu-liex khankix,
raug yuh ka riang Pa-lix,
3 mai gleeing nyawng ka ngawd Sing-noeng-cee,
pie kawn simex to kaux tix kawx hoik kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan lu ka riang krub,
hawt ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh pui haktiex in sivoe an.
Pui an kix hoik mawh pui pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex lih hu yam jauh pui Yi-seu-liex tix laig daux haktiex in.
4 Ya-ha-see yuh krax tan lu gleeing ka nyawng bhawng ka daux nyiex vad,
ka piang gawng iak,
ka greem veei khaox heue.
Puing ka Shui-li-yax mai ka Yi-seu-liex
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 16.5)
5-6 aMawh kheu simiang Ya-ha-see yuh phi map,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex nawh kieh simiang Shui-li-yax pex ka nawh,
mai pon giex pui Yo-tax tom nie viex hu ka Ta-ma-see-keux.
Simiang Siyiex kawx kieh nang kawn simex Li-ma-lix simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Pi-cia pex ka Ya-ha-see,
ka daux tix singaix khaing pon pauh ka pui naing pa kua Yo-tax kao ra mheen.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix cu kaing in lih tix mawh kheu tik vud pui Yo-tax ka nawh.
7 Pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Si-ci-lix tix kaux pauh ka Ma-si-ya kawn simex simiang Ya-ha-see yum,
mai pauh nang Ya-see-li-kang cao kuang nyiex haw simiang mai simiang ting pa kuang meung pa kaux Yi-li-cia-na.
8 Kru naing Yi-seu-liex giex ka paox ceu paox cad kix pui Yo-tax,
khawm bun miex kawn nyawm khawm uik tix nga mheen kaux viex hu ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
mai hoik teei tix viex nang sin ngao hu tom nie heue.
Cao jhai Eu-teux
9 Koe cao jhai Simiang Siyiex tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Eu-teux,
paung ot ka daux veng Sa-ma-li-yax.
Yam viex pui naing Yi-seu-liex pui Yo-tax pa pon giex kix ing ka Sa-ma-li-yax,
yam sang dex laig ka daux veng,
Eu-teux hu rieb kix,
mai ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex,
kheu song rhawm ka pui Yo-tax nawh tom kieh pex puing pex ka kix,
pex lai pauh kix yum ka daux krax pa song rhawm pa pon pex ka kix.
10 Yam in,
pex lai su tix kieh bun simex Yie-lu-sa-leung mai Yo-tax mawh siliex cawng tix.
Khex ang pex tawng,
pex kawx hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex lie?
11 Nghiet lox krai aux!
Pui pa giex pex in kix hoik mawh pux aik pux o pex.
Ploi kix deeh ing heue,
phan ang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang song rhawm ciyang ka pex heue.”
12 Meung plak laong koe simiang pon kaux,
pa mawh Ya-sa-li-ya kawn simex Yaw-ha-nang,
Pi-li-cia kawn simex Mi-seu-li-mawx,
Yie-si-si-cia kawn simex Sa-long,
Ya-ma-sha kawn simex Ha-teu-lai khankix kawx hoik tieh vheen ka kaing yuh pui naing.
13 Kix ah nin:
“Paw vi viex pui pa giex an kix hoek tin!
Ex hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex;
nawh hoik khaw tix dawg ciyang ka ex.
Yam in pex kawn sang baox yuh pa caox nie baox phi map ex ting lie?
Krax pa phit lud ex hoik kied cien tete;
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex hoik lih hoek piang Yi-seu-liex.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
pui naing in kix tawx pui pa giex kix mai kraung pa pon kix daux naing ka kawn pui mai simiang kix.
15 Simiang in pon kaux hoik laug lih mawh pa kia ngien ka pui pa giex.
Kix teei kraung ceep mai kiiep khaing daux kraung pa pon teei kix khaing daux naing,
mai tawx pui pa giex an kix ceep,
tawx kix ih priex preem tom sak,
mai baox teei bee sime kraix ka dee mat kix,
pui pa ang pon hu khankix tang ot piang blawg la,
tawx can pui giex khawm uik ing ka haktiex Yo-tax,
ka veng khaox liah Yie-li-keu.
Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex deeh ing ka Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
Ya-ha-see mhaing pui Ya-sux teem coi tix
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 16.7–9)
16-17 Pui Yi-tong baox deeh puing ka Yo-tax,
mai pon giex ka pui tom nie.
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ya-ha-see nhie Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Thi-keu-la Pi-lie-seux teem coi tix.
18 Yam an,
pui Fe-li-see puing ka Yo-tax plak seh mai veng ot ka caong gawng plak klip singaix.
Kix pon ka Paw See-mai,
Ya-ya-loeng,
Ci-ti-lawx,
Saw-keux,
Thing-na,
Ching-saw,
khawm ka nyiex yaong pa ot sivie siveng tan,
mai kix paung ot dee an.
19 Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ha-see kieh Yo-tax ang lai koe krax pa kaux sijax,
phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh krax laux krax leeing hoek piang Yo-tax.
20 Vawng Ya-sux lao sang teem coi Ya-ha-see,
deeh tieb tie ka nawh,
mai sawg krax nyhap ka nawh.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-ha-see teei tix tawx khri pa koe daux nyiex buan mai nyiex haw simiang,
khawm khri pa koe daux nyiex ma simiang pui haktiex khankix ka Vawng Ya-sux,
viang mawh nan,
ca kawn ang teem coi ka nawh heue.
Phi map Ya-ha-see
22 Yam ot Ya-ha-see ka daux krax pa kied kha rhawm tix,
nawh kawn nie phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex lheu khaing sivoe an.
23 Nawh tawx Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Siyiex pui Shui-li-yax,
mawh kheu hoik pex pui Shui-li-yax ka nawh.
Nawh ah nin:
“Siyiex pui Shui-li-yax teem coi ka simiang Shui-li-yax khankix;
phan aux tawx krax tan ka nawh,
nawh kawx tag sang chix teem coi ka aux.”
Kaing in hoik kieh Ya-ha-see mai meung cie nawh yaox krax laux krax leeing.
24 Ya-ha-see baox lauh ka kraung can pa koe ka daux nyiex buan max khawm uik,
nyeep ka nyiex buan,
mai sum khu ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung ku dauh ku dee.
25 Nawh baox sum nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak koe ka haktiex Yo-tax ku dauh ku dee,
mai gleeing nyawng ka siyiex pui tang ceu tang cad,
hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du nawh song rhawm.
26 Kaing yuh Ya-ha-see tang ceu khankix,
jauh ka kaing hoek ka rao hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux Lai Been Simiang Yo-tax mai Lai Been Simiang Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
27 bYam hoik yum Ya-ha-see,
kix si paung nawh ot ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
viang mawh nan,
ang kieh ot ka daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang.
Kawn simex nawh Si-si-cia plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
