Pui pa yuh kaing tau mai Siyiex
3
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
krai lox sidawng,
lox pa hoik krai aux ka pex,
ang een pex mawh pui dex mai Khuan Singax Singeh,
hoik een pex mawh pa dex mai haktiex,
pa mawh kawn ngie ka daux krax yum rhawm ka Khrit.
2 aAux teei ngie rawm teeh eei pex ka khaing,
pex ang pon sawm eep,
mawh kheu ang pex bax chix sawm eep.
Ka daux yam in kawx,
pex kawn ang bax chix sawm eep,
3 mawh kheu ca kawn hawt pex ka diag tong been krax ot krax koe kawn pui.
Ka daux pex koe krax pa boh rhawm,
pa simah;
khex ang in jhi grawng mawh pex pa dex mai haktiex,
pa hawt ka diag tong been krax ot krax koe kawn pui lie?
4 bKa daux pex koe pui pa ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui cie Pao-law”;
koe tix blah ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui cie Ya-paw-law.”
Yuh nin,
kawn tang siyox pex khaing kawn pui pa dex mai haktiex yuh ka mawx lie?
5 Ya-paw-law sawn tix mawh tix lie?
Pao-law sawn tix mawh tix lie?
Ex hoik mawh ngie siliex Siyiex khaing,
son sang duk thung pex yum rhawm ka Siyiex.
Ex ku kaux hoik hawt tix yuh kaing awm pa hoik guah Simiang Ye-sux tix tawx ka ex yuh nin:
6 cAux mawh cao pa sum,
Ya-paw-law mawh cao pa jaux,
viang mawh nan cao kieh huan mawh Siyiex.
7 Pui pa sum mai pui pa jaux ang sawn mawh tid tix;
pa kieh huan khawm uik mawh Siyiex.
8 Pui pa sum mai pui pa jaux ang tang siyox khaing paox tix miex ding tix,
Siyiex tag sang hawt tix pawk awm kaing pa yuh pui kaux mawx kaux an.
9 Ex mawh pui pa yuh kaing tau mai Siyiex;
pex mawh si peem Siyiex heue.
Pex kawx hoik mawh nyiex ma pa sum Siyiex.
10 Aux siao khawng phak buan son pa tawx Siyiex ka aux,
awm sara pa chix sum nyiex tix kaux pa kaw ka num jaong,
mai pui tang kaux baox sum ka piang.
Viang mawh nan,
pui ku kaux tag caw tix rien tix grawng sang sum ot ka piang num jaong yuh ka mawx.
11 Mawh kheu Siyiex hoik sum Ye-sux Khrit mawh num jaong tix mu khaing,
mai ang lai koe pui pa pon sum num jaong tang mu.
12 Ka piang num jaong in,
koe pui tag sang teei mau khri yuh,
phan ang mawh nan teei siang simaox yuh;
koe nang pui tix blah tag sang teei khaox,
rib riem,
nyhee glauh yuh.
13 Yam hoek ka daux singaix bawg yam prawng ca,
ceu pa mhawm mai ceu pa ang mhawm kaing pa yuh pui ku kaux tag sang plih lih khawm uik;
mawh kheu ngu ting tag sang plih lih mai cheu tix yaox ka kaing pa yuh pui ku kaux,
kieh kraung pa mawh pa meh jhi tix lih.
14 Phan kaing pa yuh nawh hoik ceed tix yaox ka ngu,
nawh tom taug chix pon ka khawng phak buan son.
15 Phan kaing pa yuh nawh laux leeing ka pa gleeing ngu,
nawh taug sang som nai,
viang mawh nan,
to kaux nawh taug sang pon krax taoh,
awm pa pon to tix lih khaing daux ngu yuh nan heue.
16 dPex tag caw tix tawng,
pex hoik mawh nyiex buan Siyiex,
khuan Siyiex ot ka daux pex.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
phan koe pui pa yuh laux ka nyiex buan Siyiex,
Siyiex tag sang yuh nawh laux leeing;
mawh kheu nyiex buan Siyiex mawh pa singax singeh,
to kaux pex hoik mawh nyiex buan Siyiex heue.
18 Pui ang pon tix pie lhien ka to kaux tix.
Phan ka daux pex koe pui pa hawt ka diag tong krax ot krax koe kawn pui haktiex,
mai een tix koe ping nyia cuyi,
nawh tag sang caw tix tieh tix lih mawh pui pa daox aw,
son sang lih mawh pa koe ping nyia cuyi tete.
19 eMawh kheu pa een haktiex in koe ping nyia cuyi,
viang mawh nan,
ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex tag sang mawh pa daox aw.
Awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik kieh pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi krix ka daux krax keud pa pie lhien to kaux tix.”
20 fPhuk lai sigang khri tang dauh baox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik tawng rhawm keud pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi,
hoik mawh ngie ceu pa giex pa going.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
pui mawx ang ah,
ang caw tix krai lox ting mu ka kaing pa pon yuh kawn pui.
Tete te lai,
hoik mawh cie pex khawm uik yuh nin:
22 Pao-law ang ah,
Ya-paw-law ang ah,
Pi-teux ang ah,
haktiex in ang ah,
im ang ah,
yum ang ah,
yam in ang ah,
singaix pa tag sang hoek ang ah,
in kix hoik mawh cie pex khawm uik.
23 Pex hoik mawh cie Khrit,
mai Khrit hoik mawh cie Siyiex heue.
