Pon pex ka haktiex
5
Can pui pa yum rhawm grawng mawh Ye-sux Khrit,
hoik mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex khawm uik.
Phan ex muh pa mawh keeing tix kaux,
tag sang muh ka kawn simex kawn bun nawh nang.
2 Mawh kheu muh ex ka Siyiex,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh,
ex tom tag tawng grawng muh tix ka kawn simex kawn bun nawh.
3 aPa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Siyiex tag mawh pa muh ka Siyiex;
gum lox tawx nawh ang mawh pa nu tix hawt,
4 mawh kheu kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex ku kaux hoik pon pex ka haktiex khawm uik.
Pa kieh ex pon pex ka haktiex mawh krax yum rhawm ex.
5 Pui mawx pon pex ka haktiex lie?
Mawh ngie pa yum rhawm grawng mawh Ye-sux kawn simex Siyiex pa pon tix pex haktiex khaing heue.
Kham sakse grawng mawh Ye-sux Khrit
6 Ye-sux Khrit hoek ka piang haktiex mawh pa cub com ka daux rawm mai ka daux nham pa tawx ju to kaux tix yum;
ang joeng teei ngie rawm khaing,
mai hoik teei khawm rawm khawm nham.
Khuan Singax Singeh kawx hoek tix kham sakse grawng mawh kaing in pa mawh pa meh,
mawh kheu hoik mawh Khuan Singax Singeh pa mawh pa meh.
7-8 Khuan Singax Singeh,
rawm,
mai nham loe ceu hoik mawh pa kham sakse khawm uik,
in kix loe ceu hoik khaw paox tix khawm uik.
9 Kheu hoik rieb ex ka sakse pa kham pui,
sakse pa kham Siyiex nie khaw tix mawh pa koe ceu,
sakse in mawh pa hoik yuh Siyiex son kawn simex tix.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pui mawx yum rhawm ka kawn simex Siyiex,
rhawm rhi pui an tag koe ka sakse in;
pui mawx ang yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
pui an tag kieh Siyiex mawh pa pie lhien,
mawh kheu ang nawh yum rhawm grawng hoik sax yuh Siyiex sakse son kawn simex tix.
11 bSakse in tag mawh:
ju pa tawx Siyiex ka ex leeing ju,
num ju in hoik mawh kawn simex nawh.
12 Pui mawx koe ka kawn simex Siyiex,
pui an tag koe ju;
pui mawx ang koe kawn simex Siyiex,
pui an tag ang koe ju heue.
Ju leeing ju
13 Aux tiiem tix tawx in kix ka pex,
mawh son sang kieh pex tawng,
pex pa yum rhawm ka kawn simex Siyiex khankix tag koe ju leeing ju.
14 Ex ang koe rhawm ring ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex;
mawh kheu hoik tawng ex ka sidawng,
phan ex hawt tix khiox khaw ka daux alo nawh,
nawh tag sang nghiet ka khawm uik.
15 Kheu hoik tawng ex nawh nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw ex khawm uik,
mai ex tag caw tix tawng nang,
phan ex khiox khaw ceu pa tix ka nawh,
mai nawh tag sang tawx ka ex heue.
16 Phan koe pui pa yaox pux aik pux o tix yuh phi map pa ang nyang baox kieh nawh hoek krax yum,
nawh caw tix khiox khaw ka Siyiex,
Siyiex tag kawn chix tawx ju ka kix.
In mawh pa krai ka son pui pa yuh phi map pa ang nyang bax kieh kix hoek krax yum.
Viang mawh nan,
koe phi map tix blah tag chix viex pui hoek krax yum,
aux keud,
pex ang siao lai khiox khaw son phi map pa yuh nin.
17 Pa yuh ka krax pa ang rauh see hoik mawh phi map khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
koe nang phi map pa ang nyang kieh hoek krax yum heue.
18 Ex hoik tawng kawn simex kawn bun cie Siyiex,
ang lai chix baox yuh ka phi map,
mawh kheu hoik kia ngien kawn simex Siyiex ka nawh,
ang kieh kaw pa caox caig pon yuh nawh laux heue.
19 Ex hoik tawng,
viang haktiex khawm uik hoik ot ka daux pa giex gang kaw pa caox pa caig an kawx,
mai ex ca kawn mawh pa dex dawt tau mai Siyiex heue.
20 Ex hoik tawng kawn simex Siyiex hoik hoek,
khaix an hoik tawx riang rao pa tawng dee bleeh ka ex,
son sang kieh tawng yung ka Siyiex pa mawh pa meh.
Ex ot ka daux ju Siyiex pa mawh pa meh,
pa mawh pa ot ka daux ju kawn simex nawh Ye-sux Khrit.
Nawh mawh Siyiex pa mawh pa meh,
pa mawh ju leeing ju heue.
21 Kawn nyawm khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix singai hu khaing Siyiex pa pie lhien heue!
