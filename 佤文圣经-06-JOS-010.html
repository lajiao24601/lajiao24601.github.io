Pon pex ka pui Ya-maw-lix
10
Simiang Yie-lu-sa-leunga pa mawh Ya-to-ni Si-teux mhong ka krax pa hoik rhim Yaw-su-yax ka veng Ai,
mai pauh ka simiang veng Ai,
awm pa yuh nawh ka veng Yie-li-keu mai ka piang simiang kix.
Nawh baox mhong pui Ci-piien hoik yuh sijax mukheu pa nhim tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex,
yam in kix hoik ot tau ka daux si nax kix.
2 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung kied koe krax lhat krax graung sigrien sigre;
mawh kheu veng Ci-piien ang mawh veng pa iak,
nawh ting awm veng pa koe simiang yuh nan,
kawn ting khaing veng Ai.
Pui veng Ci-piien kawx mawh nang pui naing pa ting riang.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-to-ni Si-teux kieh pui tawx lox ka Heu-shiien pa mawh simiang Si-paw-loeng,
Pi-lang pa mawh simiang Yie-maw,
Ya-fe-yax pa mawh simiang La-ciex,
mai Ti-pix pa mawh simiang Yi-ci-loeng,
4 ah nin:
“Khiox khaw pex hoek teem coi ka aux,
puing ka Ci-piien.
Mawh kheu kix hoik yuh sijax mukheu pa nhim tau mai Yaw-su-yax mai ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
5 Kheu nan,
simiang Yie-lu-sa-leung,
simiang Si-paw-loeng,
simiang Yie-maw,
simiang La-ciex,
simiang Yi-ci-loeng phuan kaux simiang Ya-maw-lix khrawm paox tix tau,
lai lawm ka Ci-piien.
6 Pui Ci-piien ploi pui hu tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax ka daux bang Cie-cia,
ah nin:
“Paw lai jhak giex going liak,
rawg pex hoek teem coi ka yix liak!
Taoh teem ka yix!
Can simiang Ya-maw-lix pa ot ka gawng hoik khrawm paox tix sang puing ka yix heue!”
7 Yaw-su-yax viex kru naing tix khawm uik,
mawh khawm pui naing pa kied ting riang,
lih khaing Cie-cia,
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Paw lhat paw graung ka kix;
aux hoik ap tix tawx kix ka daux tawm taix maix!
Ka daux kix ang koe pui pon tieb tie ka maix.”
9 Yaw-su-yax viex pui naing tix leeing sawm,
jauh ka Cie-cia hoek ka Ci-piien,
pheet ka pui Ya-maw-lix.
10 Simiang Siyiex kieh pui Ya-maw-lix yaox ka pui naing Yi-seu-liex kied koe krax lhat krax graung.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pauh kix ka Ci-piien,
pheet kix hoek tom diang moing gawng Paw Heu-loeng,
mai baox pheet plak seh tom hoek ka Ya-si-cia mai Ma-ci-tax heue.
11 Yam to pui Ya-maw-lix sivoe pui Yi-seu-liex ka diang moing gawng,
Simiang Siyiex ploi mu pre ting lauh ka piang kix ka daux krax,
tom hoek ka Ya-si-cia.
Can pui pa yum ka mu pre kawn baox lheu khaing can pa pauh pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
12 Simiang Siyiex kieh pui Yi-seu-liex puing pex ka pui Ya-maw-lix.
Daux ngaix an,
Yaw-su-yax rawg buan sivoe pui Yi-seu-liex:
Singaix eue,
kaog tix ot ka piang Ci-piien;
khix eue,
kaog tix ot ka piang daux ngawd Ya-ya-loeng.
13 bSingaix kaog ot tix dauh,
mai khix kawx kaog ot tix dauh,
tom si dauh hoik pex pui Yi-seu-liex pui pa koeng.
Kaing in tiiem ka daux Lai Ya-sha-eux.
Singaix kaog ka daux sigreung rao ma,
leeing ngaix ang klip ka heue.
14 Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox pui,
sivoe in ang sax awm daux singaix in,
ngaix khaix kawx ang chix koe.
Simiang Siyiex yuh naing son pui Yi-seu-liex!
15 Khaix kaing in,
Yaw-su-yax deeh viex pui naing tix ing ka daux bang Cie-cia heue.
Pon giex im ka simiang Ya-maw-lix phuan kaux
16 Simiang haktiex Ya-maw-lix phuan kaux hoik to,
mox tix ka daux rang Ma-ci-tax heue.
17 Koe pui yaox ka kix,
tom krai ka Yaw-su-yax dee mox kix tix.
18 Yaw-su-yax ah nin:
“Pu plieh simaox pa ting khankix mai thox ka daux moing rang,
mai kieh pui pau dee an heue.
19 Viang mawh nan,
pex paw lai ot dee an,
mai kawn ca pheet ka pui pa koeng,
ang kieh kix deeh to ka daux veng!
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex hoik tawx kix ka daux taix pex.”
20 Yaw-su-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex ang koe krax caox rhawm mai pauh ka kix,
mawh ngie pui pa tux to mox tix ka daux veng,
ang pon tix pauh ka kix.
21 Khaix kaing in,
Yaw-su-yax viex pui naing tix mhawm,
deeh ing ka nyiex bang Ma-ci-tax heue.
Khaix an,
ang koe pui pon ah lox pa tieb tie ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
22 Yaw-su-yax ah nin:
“Cot ka simaox moing rang,
viex simiang an kix phuan kaux hoek ka aux!”
23-24 Kheu nan,
kix paoh ka moing rang an,
mai viex simiang Yie-lu-sa-leung,
simiang Si-paw-loeng,
simiang Yie-maw,
simiang La-ciex,
mai simiang Yi-ci-loeng hoek sivoe Yaw-su-yax.
Yaw-su-yax khrawm can pui Yi-seu-liex hoek,
tawx lox kieh simiang naing pa hu puing naing mai nawh,
dig ka ngawng simiang an kix phuan kaux,
kix tom hawt tix yuh heue.
25 Kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax ah nin ka simiang naing an kix:
“Paw lhat paw graung;
yuh tix kaux cong,
yuh tix kua rhawm,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex sang pauh ka pui pa koeng ka pex khawm uik.”
26 Khaix an,
Yaw-su-yax pauh ka simiang an kix phuan kaux,
mai teuk teue to kaux pa yum kix ka piang khaox phuan gawng,
tom hoek plak pon bo.
27 Yam hoek bawg yam sang klip singaix,
Yaw-su-yax tawx lox teei to kaux yum kix lih,
mai tik ka daux dawh rang pa hoik mox kix tix ka sivoe,
moing rang thox ka simaox pa ting.
Simaox an kawn koe tom in singaix dee an heue.
Yaw-su-yax pon haktiex Ya-maw-lix tom nie
28 Ka daux singaix an,
Yaw-su-yax puing ka Ma-ci-tax.
Nawh pauh ka simiang haktiex an mai can pui pa ot daux veng,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
Nawh yuh ka simiang Ma-ci-tax awm yuh nawh ka simiang Yie-li-keu.
29 Khaix kaing in,
Yaw-su-yax mai pui naing nawh jauh ka haktiex Ma-ci-tax baox hu puing ka Li-na.
30 Simiang Siyiex ap tix tawx veng in mai khawm simiang in ka daux tawm taix pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kix pauh ka pui pa ot daux veng khawm uik,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
Kix yuh ka simiang Li-na awm yuh kix ka simiang Yie-li-keu heue.
31 Khaix kaing in,
Yaw-su-yax thung pui naing tix jauh ka Li-na lai lawm ka La-ciex.
32 Khaix naing daux ra singaix,
Simiang Siyiex tawx La-ciex ka daux tawm taix pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kix yuh ka La-ciex awm yuh kix ka Li-na,
mai pauh ka pui ot daux veng khawm uik,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
33 Simiang Ci-seux Heu-lang hoek tix teem coi ka La-ciex,
Yaw-su-yax pex ka nawh,
kieh pui naing nawh laux khawm uik,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
34 Khaix an,
Yaw-su-yax viex pui naing tix lih khaing La-ciex mai lai lawm ka Yi-ci-loeng,
35 ka daux ngaix an mai pon teei ka veng an,
pauh pui ot dee an uik,
awm pa yuh kix ka La-ciex yuh nan heue.
36 Baox khaix an,
Yaw-su-yax thung pui naing lih khaing Yi-ci-loeng laig ka gawng haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
mai lai lawm ka Si-paw-loeng heue.
37 Kix puing pex ka veng an,
pauh ka simiang haktiex an mai can pui pa ot daux veng khawm uik,
mai pui pa ot vung viang veng an.
Yaw-su-yax puing pex ka veng in,
awm pa yuh nawh ka Yi-ci-loeng,
ang deuh pui tix kaux kawx.
38 Khaix an,
Yaw-su-yax viex pui naing tix deeh ing puing ka Ti-pix,
39 mai puing pex ka veng an mai veng pa ot vung viang an khawm uik,
mai pauh ka simiang haktiex an mai pui khawm uik,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
Yaw-su-yax yuh ka Ti-pix mai simiang nawh,
awm pa yuh nawh ka Si-paw-loeng mai Li-na,
khawm simiang Li-na.
40 Yaw-su-yax pon kuang ka haktiex khawm uik.
Nawh pon kuang ka gawng lua,
mai daux lung plak lih singaix mai daux tiag daux tu plak klip singaix,
kawn koe haktiex pa kroh an kix plak seh.
Nawh pex ka simiang baoh in khawm uik,
mai pauh ka kawn pui khawm uik,
ang deuh tix kaux kawx.
In mawh lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
41 Yaw-su-yax pex ka pui pa koeng,
jauh ka Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax tom hoek ka Cia-sa pa dex mai rawm sigrit sigra,
mawh khawm haktiex Keu-sang khawm uik,
plak laong hoek tom Ci-piien.
42 Yaw-su-yax pon kuang simiang in kix khawm haktiex kix ka daux naing tix bawg,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex yuh naing son kix.
43 Khaix kaing in,
Yaw-su-yax viex pui naing tix deeh ing ka nyiex bang Cie-cia heue.
