Deuh ka kawk ngu aliah mu
8
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix krai ka Ya-loeng,
yam tok nawh tix deuh kawk ngu ka piang jawk kawk ngu,
caw tix kieh pa riang an jhan hu ka plak sivoe.”
3 Ya-loeng hawt yuh awm an,
mai tok tix deuh kawk ngu jhan riang ka sivoe jawk kawk ngu.
4 Pang deuh jawk kawk ngu khawm uik,
jauh plak piang tom hoek plak greem,
hoik mawh pa tawk lih khaing khri khawm uik,
pa yuh lih awm pak peung pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.
Yuh krax pa singax singeh mai krax tan son pui Li-we
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
6 “Maix tang guah ceu pui Li-we lih khaing ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix,
7 siao grawng krax in plak greem kieh kix singax,
teei rawm pa singax singeh mai phruah ka piang to kaux kix,
nhie kix khlao hauk pa koe ka gaw kaux tix singax khawm uik,
mai si daux ka kraung ceep tix singax.
Yuh nin,
kix tom tag sawn tix singax ka daux been.
8 Kix tag caw tix viex kawn moi mhaing tix mu mai krax tan phawng mien,
pa hoik klao tix tau mai bee sime hoek,
maix tom baox viex kawn moi mhaing tang mu tix mu hoek,
son sang kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
9 Khaix an,
maix tom khrawm ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex hoek,
nhie ceu pui Li-we cung ka sivoe diang siviex nyiex gox buan.
10 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang sau taix tix ka piang kaing ceu pui Li-we;
11 Ya-loeng tag sang ap pong ceu pui Li-we ka aux,
son sang kieh mawh khawng phak pa blawk tix tan son ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Yuh nin,
kix tom tag chix pon yuh ka kaing cie aux.
12 Ceu pui Li-we tag sang deuh taix tix ka piang kaing moi mhaing an khawm ra mu;
tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
an tix mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka ngu,
tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
son sang yuh been pa singax singeh son ceu pui Li-we heue.
13 “Maix kieh ceu pui Li-we,
mawh khawng phak pa blawk tix tan ka aux,
kheu nan mai nhie Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix kuang kia ka piang kix.
14 Maix tag caw tix tang guah ceu pui Li-we lih khaing ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix,
kieh kix mawh ceu pui cie aux.
15 Khaix hoik kieh maix ceu pui Li-we singax singeh mai khaix hoik tan ka aux,
kix tom tag chix ot tix yuh kaing ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
16 Aux hoik krai pao,
kix tag sang tang son kawn ai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
kix tag sang mawh cie aux nong nong.
17 bKa daux singaix pa hoik pauh aux ka can pa liang tix keh ka daux haktiex Ai-cix,
aux hoik kieh kawn ai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku nyiex,
khawng brie kix pa liang tix keh,
guah tix mawh pa singax singeh,
mai kieh mawh cie aux.
18 Yam in aux tag sang kieh ceu pui Li-we tang son kawn ai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
19 Aux hoik guah tix tawx ceu pui Li-we ka Ya-loeng mai ka kawn simex nawh khankix,
son sang kieh mawh khawng phak son ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ot yuh kaing ka daux nyiex gox buan man son ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
son ang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex sang laux leeing kheu pa hoek dex kix ka nyiex dee pa singax singeh heue.”
20 Maw-si,
Ya-loeng,
mai cub pui hun ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
mai tan ka ceu pui Li-we khankix.
21 Ceu pui Li-we yuh to kaux tix singax,
mai si daux kraung ceep tix;
Ya-loeng kieh kix mawh khawng phak pa blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
baox yuh been pa singax singeh son kix.
22 Phan mawh grawng son ceu pui Li-we,
ceu pui khawm uik tag sang hawt tix yuh,
awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si ku gum.
Yuh nin,
ceu pui Li-we tom tag chix yaox tix ot yuh kaing ka greem tawm taix Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh,
yuh ka kaing pa dex pa dawt ka daux nyiex gox buan man heue.
23 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
24 “Ceu pui Li-we pa hoik koe ju tix nga phuan num haok plak piang ku kaux,
tag koe beeh glawm tix yuh kaing jhaw ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
25 yam hoek tix phuan num tag sang laux lee tix heue.
26 Khaix an kix kawn chix tix teem coi ka pux aik tix ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang pon tix nieb teei alo tix yuh nong nong.
Maix tag caw tix guah kaing yuh ceu pui Li-we yuh nin heue.”
