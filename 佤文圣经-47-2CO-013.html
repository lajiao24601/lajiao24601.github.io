Gum lox pa ne raw mai pa chawk mhawm ot
13
aIn hoik mawh pa hoek aux tix vhi ka daux loe bawg.
Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pa deuh phi map ku ceu tag caw tix kieh mawh pui ra kaux,
phan ang mawh nan loe kaux pa kham,
tom taug teei tix prawng.”
2 Ka son pui pa yuh phi map sivoe an,
mai ka pui pa deuh khankix,
yam hoek aux tix vhi ka daux ra bawg aux hoik ne raw ka kix,
yam in aux ang ot tau mai pex dee an,
mai baox ne raw ka pex,
yam hoek aux bawg khaix,
aux tag ang lai va rhawm ka pex.
3 Pex hoik koe ka mhai sakse pa sang lo pex,
viang mawh nan,
Khrit hoik si kah ka daux aux.
Yam yuh Khrit ka pex,
nawh ang mawh pa iak riang;
deeh tieh tix,
nawh tieh tix jhi riang rao tix ka daux pex.
4 Viang pui hoik tawk nawh piang khaox lhak ka daux pa iak riang nawh,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ca kawn im ka daux riang rao Siyiex.
Ex hoik kleuk tix tau mai nawh,
mai lih tix mawh pa iak riang,
viang mawh nan,
son sang kieh pex mhawm,
ex tag sang im tau mai nawh ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka Siyiex heue.
5 Pex tag caw cheuk cheu tix yaox ka to kaux tix,
lam lia ka to kaux tix,
jhak tix yaox sang koe ang pex koe krax yum rhawm.
Lheu khaing ang pex pon kham krax pa cheuk tix yaox ka pex,
pex caw tix tawng grawng pa hoik ot Ye-sux Khrit ka daux ju pex.
6 Aux simie pex tawng,
ex ang mawh ang pon kham krax pa cheuk cheu,
7 ex khiox khaw ka Siyiex,
son ang sang kieh pex yuh kaing pa caox.
In ang mawh son sang yaog yaw grawng pa pon kham ex krax pa cheuk cheu,
mai hoik mawh son sang kieh pex yuh krax pa rauh see,
viang hoik kieh ex mawh pa som nai kawx.
8 Ex ang doh yuh kaing ceu pa tieb tie ka ceu pa mawh pa meh tix ceu kawx,
mai yuh ngie ka ceu pa mawh pa meh khaing.
9 Ex iak riang pex ting riang,
in hoik mawh pa khaw ex tix gaux rhawm.
Ceu pa khiox khaw ex,
hoik chix kieh pex lih mawh pa grum naog.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
yam singai ot aux khaing pex,
aux tiiem ka lox lai in,
son ang sang siao ka ara pa tawx Simiang Ye-sux ka aux,
yuh krax pa kin yam yaox tix ka pex.
Simiang Ye-sux tawx ara ka aux,
hoik mawh son sang sum pex kaux cong,
mai ang mawh son sang kieh pex laux leeing heue.
11 Dee nghoik,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
koi yaox bawg khaixb.
Pex sau riang tix mawh pui pa grum naog.
Rieb ka lox pa sidiex aux;
pui ku kaux tag caw tix ih rhawm rhi khri mau paox tix,
yuh krax pa khaw rhawm paox tix tau.
Kieh krax muh mai pa nhim Siyiex ot tau mai pex heue!
12 Pex tag caw tix ra lai ngheet paox tix ka daux krax pa singax singeh heue!
Can kawn Khrit ku kaux hoik chawk pex mhawm ot mhawm koe heue!
13 Kieh buan son Simiang Ye-sux Khrit,
krax muh krax rhawm Siyiex,
krax pa dex dawt mai Khuan Singax Singeh,
ot tau mai pex ku kaux heue!
