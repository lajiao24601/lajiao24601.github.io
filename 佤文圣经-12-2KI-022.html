Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-si-yax
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 34.1–2)
22
aYam koe ju Yaw-si-yax daix num nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai nawh giex kaing ka piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tix ngoe tix num.
Miex nawh mawh Yie-ti-tax,
mawh kawn bun Ya-ta-ya pa ot ka daux veng Paw-see-cia.
2 Yaw-si-yax hoik yuh ka kaing pa mhawm ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
hawt tix lawng khaix caong tax du tix Ta-wue,
mai ang hu plak goix plak dawm heue.
Yaox ka lai lox tat jhawm prawng
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 34.8–28)
3 Yam giex Yaw-si-yax kaing daux kao daix num,
nawh ploi kawn saux Mi-su-lang,
kawn simex Ya-sa-lix pa mawh cao tiiem lai daux nyiex simiang,
pa kaux Sa-fang hu ka daux nyiex buan.
Yaw-si-yax tom tawx lox ka nawh ah nin:
4 “Maix hu ka Si-leu-cia cao giex kaing li pa ting dee an,
mai teei ka lai ca rang pa ci mau,
ka daux lai an hoik tiiem ka mau pa hoik rieb cao giex kaing li khaing kawn pui,
yam pau nawh ka diang siviex nyiex buan.
5 Nhie nawh tawx mau ka cao kaing pa thung pui sum nyiex buan;
kix sang tawx riang
6 cang mai khaox,
pui sum nyiex,
cang mai yuh glaox,
mai kawn sang liag khaox ox mai simaox son nyiex buan.
7 bCao kaing pa thung pui yuh kaing,
hoik mawh pui pa mawh pa meh khawm uik,
ang lai siao simin mau tau mai kix heue.”
8 Sa-fang tawx lox pa hoik krai simiang ka Si-leu-cia.
Si-leu-cia krai ka Sa-fang,
nawh hoik yaox lai lox tat jhawm prawng ka daux nyiex buan.
Si-leu-cia teei tix tawx lai an ka Sa-fang,
Sa-fang phat kah.
9 Khaix an,
nawh deeh ing krai ka simiang dee an ah nin:
“Siliex maix khankix hoik tawx mau nyiex buan ka cao kaing pa thung pui yuh kaing heue.”
10 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Si-leu-cia tawx lai ka aux tix bua.”
Sa-fang tom phat simiang mhong tom ting heue.
11 Simiang mhong ka lox pa tiiem tix ot ka daux lai lox tat jhawm prawng an,
tom riah ka kraung ceep tix griah.
12 Khaix an,
tom tawx lox ka Si-leu-cia cao giex kaing li,
pa mawh kawn simex Sa-fang pa kaux Ya-si-kang,
kawn simex Mi-kai-yax pa kaux Ya-keu-paw,
cao tiiem lai daux nyiex simiang pa kaux Sa-fang,
mai pui cau simiang pa kaux Ya-sha-ya.
13 Ah nin:
“Pex hu khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex son aux mai son kawn pui Yo-tax,
mai si kaw tix yaox dee bleeh lox sidiex ka daux bua lai in mawh ceu pa tix.
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm song rhi ka ex,
mawh kheu jao ang tax du ex bax nghiet ka lox nawh,
ang bax hawt tix yuh awm pa hoik tiiem tix ka daux lai in heue.”
14 Si-leu-cia cao giex kaing li,
Ya-si-kang,
Ya-keu-paw,
Sa-fang,
mai Ya-sha-ya hu chawk ka bun cao jhai pa kaux Hu-leu-tax tix kaux.
Bun cao jhai an ot ka veng haktiex pa khraox Yie-lu-sa-leung.
(Mex nawh Sa-long mawh kawn simex Theu-wa,
kawn saux Ha-eu-ha-see,
pa mawh cao kaing pa kuang ka kraung ceep ka daux nyiex buan.)
Kix krai nawh mhong kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh.
15 Nawh nhie kix deeh ing ka simiang,
16 mai deeh krai lox Simiang Siyiex ka simiang ah nin:
“Aux sang hawt tix yuh ka lox pa mhong simiang pa hoik tiiem tix ot ka daux lai an khawm uik,
mai ciyang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai can pui pa ot ka daux khankix.
17 Kix hoik tik vud ka aux,
mai gleeing nyawng ka siyiex tang kaux,
kieh riang rao pa krub kix song yuh rhawm rhi aux,
mawh kheu nan aux tom song rhawm song rhi ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung ang jud ang jai.
18 “Phan mawh to kaux simiang,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah yuh nin ka nawh:
‘Yam hoik mhong maix ka pa tiiem tix daux lai an,
19 maix tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix mai viex pa awn pa tiiem ka plak sivoe ngai aux.
Yam hoik mhong maix ka pa ne raw aux,
son sang ciyang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai pui pa ot ka daux khankix,
kieh lih mawh dee kroh dee riem,
dee jhax lawg pui kah,
mai maix riah ka kraung ceep tix griah,
yiiem ka aux,
aux tom nghiet ka lox rawg maix buan.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
sivoe sang yum maix,
aux ang nyang ploi krax laux krax leeing lih hoek piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
aux sang kieh maix hu khaing haktiex ka daux pa nhim heue.’ ”
Pui in kix tom viex lox in ing krai ka simiang Yaw-si-yax heue.
