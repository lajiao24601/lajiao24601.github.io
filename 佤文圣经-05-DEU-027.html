Lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex pa tiiem ka piang simaox
27
Maw-si mai kuad song ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tawx lox ka pui khankix ah nin:
“Pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa tawm aux ka pex daux in singaix khawm uik.
2 aYam tiang pex ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
laig ka haktiex pa sang tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex ka pex ka daux singaix an.
Pex tag caw sum tix deuh ka lon simaox ting khankix,
mai kraix simaox ceed ka piang,
3 mai tiiem lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox sidiex lox gau ot ka piang khawm uik.
Yam laig pex ka daux haktiex pa hoik ap Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du pex tix tawx ka pex,
pa mawh haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia an,
4 mai khaix hoik tiang pex tix dig ka toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang ka plak klip singaix,
pex tag caw tix hawt ka lox pa tawm aux pex daux in singaix,
sum simaox ting in kix tix blah ot ka piang gawng Yi-pa-lux,
maix kraix simaox ceed ka piang.
5 b“Pex siao simaox pa ang pui bax saik ka rhiem khankix,
6 mai yuh khu ka dee an;
mawh kheu can pa yuh khu ku ceu khu son Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag caw tix yuh ka simaox pa ang pui bax saik kah.
Pex tag caw tix tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ot ka piang khu dee tan,
7 tag sang ih Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka dee an nang,
mai gaux rhawm ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
8 Pex tag caw tix tiiem lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex sidawng ot ka piang simaox pa ting an heue.”
9 Khaix an,
Maw-si mai cao giex kaing li ceu pui Li-we,
ah nin ka ceu pui haoh hun Yi-seu-liex khankix:
“Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix eue,
yien yawg pex,
bre rhawm pex nghiet ka lox pa krai aux.
Ka daux singaix in,
pex hoik lih mawh ceu pui cie Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pex cu nghiet ka lox nawh,
mai hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap pa tawm aux ka pex daux in singaix khawm uik heue.”
Pa lieh lheu ka lox tawx tag sang pon krax jhax lawg
11 Ka daux singaix an,
Maw-si baox ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix:
12 c“Khaix hoik tiang pex ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
yam nawb cao giex kaing li ceu pui Li-we buan ka piang pui khankix,
ceu pui Si-miien,
Li-we,
Yo-tax,
Yi-sa-cia,
Yaw-seux mai Piien-ya-ming khankix,
tag sang cung ka piang gawng Ci-li-sing.
13 Yam krai pao ka lox jhax lawg,
Lui-piien,
Cia-teux,
Ya-seux,
Si-pu-loeng,
Tang,
mai Na-fu-tha-lix khankix,
tag sang cung ka gawng Yi-pa-lux.
14 Ceu pui Li-we cao giex kaing li khankix,
tag sang krai pao ka lox pa ting si num ka daux pui hun ah nin:
15 d“ ‘Can pui pa yuh riang ka simaox,
ka khaox,
mai ka riang pa raug ka mau ka khri ang ah,
mai briex mox tix krub kah,
tag sang pon krax jhax lawg Siyiex,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik koeng ka pa krub ka riang rao heue.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
16 e“ ‘Pui pa ang yung yi ka miex keeing tix,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
17 f“ ‘Pui pa gah ka mhai mee haktiex paox plawg paox nyhawm tix,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
18 g“ ‘Pui pa su tix thung pui pa dug ngai khankix lud krax,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
19 h“ ‘Pui pa yeen ya ka sibrawm krax pa khaw cie pui tang ceu tang cad,
kawn doe,
mai miex mai,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
20 i“ ‘Pui pa it tau mai moeng iak keeing tix,
kieh keeing tix kaig tua,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
21 j“ ‘Pui pa phit vao tau mai to sat,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
22 k“ ‘Pui pa it tau mai pux o tix,
pa mawh kawn bun pa hawt keeing mai kawn bun pa hawt miex tang keeing,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
23 l“ ‘Pui pa it tau mai miex moeng tix,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
24 “ ‘Pui pa briex mox tix pauh ka pui,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
25 “ ‘Pui pa rieb ka mau chun praok,
son sang pauh ka pui ang koe dee lud,
tag sang pon krax pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’
26 m“ ‘Pa ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex,
mai gum lox pa sidiex gaux nawh,
tag sang pon pa jhax lawg Siyiex.’
“Pui hun tag sang deeh pawk lox ah nin:
‘A-meung!’ ”
