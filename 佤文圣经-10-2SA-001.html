Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra
Ta-wue mhong grawng pa hoik yum Sao-law
1
Khaix hoik yum Sao-law,
Ta-wue kaoh khaing puing tix pui Ya-ma-lix,
mai paung ot ka Si-keu-la ra singaix.
2 Ka daux loe singaix,
koe pui nhum ju tix kaux kaoh khaing daux pui naing cie Sao-law,
son sang jhi ka grawng pa hoik laux rhawm tix,
nawh riah ka sibex tix,
mai rhuat nyaox piang kaing tix.
Nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngaix Ta-wue,
mai yuh krax pa nab yam yung yi gup tix krub ka piang tiex.
3 Ta-wue chawk nawh ah nin:
“Kaoh maix khaing dee mawx?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux to mox tix khaing ka daux pui naing Yi-seu-liex heue.”
4 Ta-wue chawk ah nin:
“Krai aux mhong,
yuh plak an ka mawx?”
Pui nhum ju an ah nin:
“Pui naing yix ang pex to khaing bang naing,
mai pui hoik yum tom nie.
Sao-law mai kawn simex nawh Yaw-na-tang kawx hoik yum heue!”
5 Ta-wue chawk ah nin:
“Maix hoik tawng Sao-law mai Yaw-na-tang yum yuh ka mawx lie?”
6 aPui nhum ju an pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux jiet caw tix ot ka gawng Ci-li-paw,
mai yaox ka Sao-law gup tix piang pliah viex tix,
ling law pui naing pa koeng mai pui naing brug brung dex sang krup ka nawh.
7 Nawh veeh tix mai yaox ka aux,
tom kok ka aux.
Aux ah nin:
‘Aux ot tin!’
8 Nawh chawk aux mawh mawx.
Aux krai ka nawh aux mawh pui Ya-ma-lix.
9 Nawh ah nin:
‘Hoek cung ka praok aux,
mai klauh aux yum!
Aux hoik nie mat mai dex sang yum,
viang mawh nan,
kawn ang nyang yum.’
10 Aux tom hu klauh ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik tawng aux grawng ang nawh sang lai klawd.
Aux teei mhok khri pa ceep nawh piang kaing tix mai ble pa koe ka taix nawh tawx ka maix heue.”
11 Ta-wue laux rhawm mai riah ka sibex tix griah.
Pui naing cie nawh kawx riah ka sibex tix griah nang.
12 Kix laux rhawm yiiem,
mai tuah priex preem tom hoek pon bo son Sao-law,
Yaw-na-tang mai pui cie Simiang Siyiex paox ceu paox cad kix Yi-seu-liex,
mawh kheu hoik yum pui tom nie ka daux bang naing heue.
13 Ta-wue chawk pui nhum ju pa viex lox an hoek krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Mawh maix pui dee mawx?”
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mawh pui Ya-ma-lix,
pa mawh pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot ka daux meung pex heue.”
14 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai pon pauh ka simiang pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex lie?”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka pui pa hawt tix:
“Pauh nawh yum!”
Pui pa hawt nawh an tom klauh pui Ya-ma-lix an yum.
16 Ta-wue ah nin ka pui Ya-ma-lix an:
“Maix hoik khaw tix yum ka daux phi map pa yuh tix!
Maix hoik krai doh ka pa hoik pauh tix ka simiang pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex,
mai an hoik mawh tang prawng maix tix yum ka daux phi map yuh tix heue.”
Ta-wue tiiem lox rhax yiiem doe son Sao-law mai Yaw-na-tang
17 Ta-wue tiiem lox rhax yiiem doe tix ceu son yum Sao-law mai Yaw-na-tang,
18 cmai tawx lox kieh pui Yo-tax gau ka lox rhax inb.
Lox rhax in tiiem tix ot ka daux phuk lai Ya-sa-eu.
 
19 Ka piang gawng Yi-seu-liex,
simiang ex hoik yum!
Pui naing pa ting riang ao min ka daux ex hoik goh!
20 Paw vi krai grawng in ka Cia-theux.
Paw vi pao hu ka daux laih daux cau Ya-seu-ci-loeng.
Paw kieh bun miex pui Fe-li-see khankix gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
Paw kieh bun miex pui tang ceu khankix gaux rhawm.
 
21 Kieh gawng Ci-li-paw khawm uik ang lai pon yaox ka rawm lhiex leeing ju.
Kieh kaung ma khankix riem leeing ju.
Mawh kheu rhaw dieb tie naok cie pui pa ting riang ao min khankix.
Tieb tie naok cie Sao-law ang lai yaox tix awt plang ka bee.
22 Te ak puh cie Yaw-na-tang koe riang tix kieh ju pui yum,
lawm vaig cie Sao-law mug dee mawx ang ah,
ang doh ang krox,
pon mug dut ka pui pa ting riang ting rao,
klauh ka pui pa koeng khankix.
 
23 Sao-law mai Yaw-na-tang mawh pui mhawm pa khaw ex tix yaog yaw yung yi.
Kiex im tau,
hoik yum kawx mai ang guah paox tix.
Kiex kawn brax khaing klang ting,
mai kawn koe riang khaing sivai sang si mhaing.
 
24 Bun miex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag caw tix yiiem son Sao-law!
Nawh pon kieh pex ceep ka kraung pa raoh rawt,
kieh pex ceep ka mau khri siang.
 
25 Pui pa ting riang ao min khankix hoik goh mai yum ka daux bang naing.
Yaw-na-tang yuh ka mawx lai goh yum ka piang gawng.
 
26 Pux aik aux Yaw-na-tang eue,
aux yiiem son maix.
Krax muh maix ka aux kied sidaing ting!
Krax muh krax rhawm maix ka aux kied sidaing mhawm,
kawn lheu ting khaing krax muh bun miex khankix.
 
27 Pui pa ting riang ao min khankix yuh ka mawx lai goh.
Kraung naing cie kix yuh ka mawx lai lih tix mawh pa som nai heue.
