Ye-sux mawh nhu plix shiet pa mawh pa meh
15
“Aux mawh nhu plix shiet pa mawh pa meh;
keeing aux mawh cao si peem.
2 Can kak pa kleuk tix mai aux pa ang chix plix plix,
nawh tag sang kid tix tik;
kak pa pon plix plix nawh tag sang cox pot kah,
mai kieh nawh nie baox plix plix.
3 Gum lox pa krai aux ka pex hoik kieh pex singax.
4 Pex tag caw tix kleuk tix tau mai aux jheeng jheeng,
aux tom taug kleuk tix tau mai pex jheeng jheeng nang.
Phan pex ang kleuk tix tau mai aux,
pex tag ang pon tix chix plix plix,
awm kak pa ang kleuk tix tau mai nhu plix shiet ang pon tix plix plix yuh nan heue.
5 “Aux mawh nhu plix shiet;
pex mawh kak.
Pa hoik kleuk tix tau mai aux,
aux kawx hoik kleuk tix tau mai nawh jheeng jheeng nang,
mai nawh tag sang plix plix tom nie;
mawh kheu phan ang koe aux,
pex tag ang pon chix yuh ceu pa tid tix.
6 Pui pa ang kleuk tix tau mai aux tag sang tik kah,
awm hoik tik kak pa kroh yuh nan,
mai kieh pui hu sut tik ka daux ngu.
7 Phan pex kleuk tix tau mai aux jheeng jheeng,
gum lox aux kawx tag ot ka daux pex jheeng jheeng,
phan pex sang lo ka ceu pa tix,
khiox khaw kah,
tag sang pon.
8 Keeing aux hoik pon min plak ngai kheu pa hoik plix plix pex tom nie,
mai pex kawx tag sang lih mawh pui gau aux kheu in.
9 Aux muh ka pex awm hoik muh keeing ka aux yuh nan;
pex tag caw tix ot ka daux krax muh aux jheeng jheeng.
10 Phan pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx aux,
pex tag sang pon tix ot ka daux krax muh aux jheeng jheeng,
awm hoik priex aux tix hawt ka gum lox tawx keeing aux,
mai hoik ot ka daux krax muh nawh jheeng jheeng yuh nan heue.
11 “Aux krai kaing in kix ka pex,
son sang kieh pex pon ka krax gaux rhawm aux,
mai kieh krax gaux rhawm pex grum naog.
12 aPex tag caw tix ra lai muh ka paox tix,
awm hoik muh aux pex yuh nan;
in hoik mawh gum lox tawx aux.
13 Pui pa hoik tawx ju tix son paox grawm tix,
ka daux krax muh kawn pui ang lai lheu ting khaing in.
14 Phan pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx aux,
pex tag mawh paox grawm aux.
15 Aux ang lai baox kieh pex mawh siliex,
mawh kheu ang siliex tawng ka kaing ceu pa yuh simiang tix.
Aux hoik kieh pex mawh paox grawm tix,
mawh kheu hoik krai aux ceu pa mhong tix ka keeing aux dee an ka pex khawm uik.
16 Ang mawh pex pa laug ka aux,
hoik mawh aux pa laug ka pex,
khaix an tawx pex hu plix plix jheeng jheeng an.
Pex rawg ka daux ceu kaux aux,
rawg ceu pa tix ang ah ka keeing,
nawh tag sang tawx ka pex.
17 Pex tag caw tix ra lai muh ka paox tix;
in hoik mawh gum lox pa tawx aux ka pex heue.”
Krax pa koeng kawn pui piang haktiex
18 “Phan kawn pui piang haktiex koeng ka pex,
pex tag caw tix tawng,
kix hoik koeng ka aux sivoe.
19 Phan pex mawh pa dex dawt mai haktiex in,
kawn pui piang haktiex tag sang muh ka ceu pa mawh pa dex dawt mai cie tix.
Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik laug pex lih khaing haktiex in,
pex ang mawh pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex in;
mawh kheu nan kawn pui piang haktiex tom koeng ka pex.
20 “bPex tag caw tix doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai aux ka pex ah nin:
‘Siliex ang lheu ting khaing simiang tix.’
Phan kix tuk tiak ka aux,
kix kawx tag sang tuk tiak ka pex;
phan kix priex tix hawt ka gum lox aux,
kix kawx taug sang priex tix hawt ka gum lox pex.
21 Mawh kheu jao aux,
kix tag sang yuh kaing ceu in kix ka pex khawm uik,
mawh kheu ang kix tawng yung ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
22 Phan aux ang bax hoek tix krai dee bleeh ka kix,
kix tag ang koe phi map;
yam in phi map kix ang lai koe ceu pa du.
23 Pui pa koeng ka aux,
ka keeing aux kawx nawh koeng kah.
24 Phan aux ang nyang sax yuh kaing ceu pa ang pui sax yuh tix bawg kawx ka daux kix,
kix tag ang koe phi map.
Tete te lai,
kix hoik yaox ka kaing ceu pa yuh aux,
viang mawh nan,
ca kawn koeng ka aux,
mai ka keeing aux.
25 cViang mawh nan,
in tee sang kieh lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tiiem ot ka daux phuk lai kix lih ah nin:
‘Kix hoik koeng ka aux ang koe krax koe grawng heue!’
26 “Viang mawh nan,
cao teem coi tag sang lih hoek khaing keeing aux an;
nawh hoik mawh khuan pa mawh pa meh.
Yam ploi aux lih hoek khaing keeing aux dee an,
nawh tag sang mawh cao pa kham mhai sakse son aux.
27 Awm an,
pex kawx tag sang mawh cao pa kham mhai sakse son aux,
mawh kheu hoik ot pex tau mai aux tom yam jauh heue.”
