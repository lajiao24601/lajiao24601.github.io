Nyo ka plak lih singaix
2
Yam mawh Si-luix simiang,
Ye-sux keh ka daux meung Yo-thaix veng Paw-li-haung.
Koe pui pa chix kah dee bleeh simuing ra loe kaux kaoh khaing plak lih singaix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
2 kix chawk ah nin:
“An pa keh tix sang mawh simiang pui Yo-thaix ot dee mawx lie?
Yix hoik yaox jhan riang simuing nawh,
tom hoek tix krub ka nawh heue.”
3 Simiang Si-luix mhong ka lox in,
kied ang nyawm ot;
khawm pui ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung kawx ang nyawm ot nang.
4 Si-luix tom khrawm can simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix ka daux haktiex hoek,
chawk kix ah nin:
“Khrit keh ka dee mawx?”
5 Kix pawk ah nin:
“Yo-thaix pa ot ka veng Paw-li-haung,
kheu cao jhai hoik sax tiiem yuh nin:
6 a “Paw-li-haung pa ot haktiex Yo-tax eue,
maix ot ka daux veng Yo-tax ang mawh pa blawn iak khaing paox tix.
Kheu koe simiang tix kaux sang lih hoek khaing maix dee an,
nawh tag sang liang ka pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Si-luix briex kok ka pui pa chix kah dee bleeh simuing pa kaoh khaing plak lih singaix hoek,
si kaw tix chawk kix grawng singaix pa lih simuing an yam mawx.
8 Khaix an tawx lox yam hu kix plak Paw-li-haung ah nin:
“Pex hu,
bre tix sawg ka kawn nyawm an mhawm mhawm,
phan yaox deeh hoek krai ka aux,
aux kawx sang hu krub ka nawh heue.”
9 Khaix hoik mhong lox in,
kix tom hu.
Bawg yam in,
simuing pa yaox kix ka plak lih singaix baox lih hoek,
mai duk thung ka sivoe ngai kix,
tom hoek ka piang dee keh kawn nyawm tom taug kaog.
10 Kix yaox ka simuing an,
mai kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi tete.
11 Kix laig daux nyiex,
mai yaox kawn nyawm mai miex nawh Ma-li-yax,
tom krub ka kawn nyawm in,
khaix an paoh ka iiep siang,
teei ka khri,
sidah bhawng Law-ban,
Muran khankix kieh mawh khawng phak tan ka nawh heue.
12 Ka daux ca maox,
Siyiex krai ka kix ang lai cu kix deeh ing ka Si-luix,
kheu nan kix ing ka krax tang nyaing hoek ka haktiex tix heue.
To mox tix ka Ai-cix
13 Khaix hoik hu kix,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka Yaw-seux ka daux ca maox,
ah nin:
“Kaoh heue!
Viex kawn nyawm in mai miex nawh to hu mox tix ka haktiex Ai-cix,
paung ot dee an,
tom hoek tawm aux lox nhie maix hu;
kheu Si-luix sang sawg tix giex ka kawn nyawm in,
mai sang pauh nawh yum heue.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-seux yaog tix hu,
mai viex kawn nyawm an mai miex nawh to hu mox tix haktiex Ai-cix tom pon sawm,
15 bmai paung ot dee an tom hoik yum Si-luix.
Kaing in tag sang kieh gum lox pa krai cao jhai lih tix mawh ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux kok kawn simex tix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix heue.”
Pauh ka kawn ngie
16 Si-luix tawng pa hoik pie lhien pui pa chix kah dee bleeh simuing ka tix,
kied song rhawm,
hawt awm pa mhong nawh pa tat pui pa chix kah dee bleeh simuing ka bawg yam,
tawx pui hu pauh ka kawn nyawm simex pa koe ra num plak greem ka veng Paw-li-haung mai ka baoh haktiex pa dex dee an heue.
17 Kaing in hoik lih tix mawh awm lox pa krai cao jhai Yie-li-mi ah nin:
18 cka daux La-max hoik mhong
lox pa yiiem tom ting heue.
Le-ciiex yiiem son kawn praix khankix,
mai ang cu tix rieb ka lox khu rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik yum kix khawm uik heue.
Hoek khaing Ai-cix
19 Khaix hoik yum Si-luix,
ka daux Ai-cix,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka Yaw-seux ka daux ca maox,
20 ah nin:
“Kaoh heue!
Viex kawn nyawm mai miex nawh deeh ing ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex,
mawh kheu hoik yum pui pa keud tix sang pauh ka kawn nyawm in heue.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-seux yaog tix hu,
mai viex ka kawn nyawm mai miex nawh deeh ing ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex heue.
22 Mawh kheu mhong Yaw-seux grawng pa plud Ya-ci-lao tix mawh simiang Yo-thaix khaix keeing tix Si-luix,
mai nawh ang pon hu dee an.
Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex baox lih bleeh ka Yaw-seux ka daux ca maox,
Yaw-seux tom hu mox ka baoh haktiex Cia-li-lix,
23 dmai paung ot ka pa kok mawh pui Na-sha-leux.
In hoik kieh mawh awm gum lox pa krai cao jhai lih ah nin:
“Tag sang kok nawh mawh pui veng Na-sha-leux heue.”
