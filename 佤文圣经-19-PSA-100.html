Lox rhax pa yaog yaw
100
Ka piang haktiex khawm uik eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
2 Tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi mai krub ka nawh;
tag caw tix rhax lox rhax pa yaog yaw,
mai hoek ka plak sivoe ngai nawh heue.
 
3 Tag caw tix tawng Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex.
Nawh hoik phan yuh ka ex,
mai ex mawh cie nawh;
ex mawh pui cie nawh,
mawh phung yung pa eei liang nawh heue.
 
4 Tag caw tix teei krax pa yaog yaw viex laig ka daux nyiex buan nawh;
tag caw tix teei lox rhax krax yaog yaw viex laig ka daux nyiex pa singax nawh.
Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka nawh,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue!
 
5 aSimiang Siyiex kied mhawm kied mhiem,
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju;
pa kaux sijax nawh koe tom hoek kaing mheen ju heue.
