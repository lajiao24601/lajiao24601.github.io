Pa rauh pa see pa mawh pa meh
3
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux kawn koe lox krai ka pex.
Kieh pex gaux rhawm gaux rhi ka daux Simiang Siyiex heue!
Deeh baox krai ka lox pa hoik krai ka pex sivoe an,
ka son aux ang bax mawh pa nyhap,
viang mawh nan,
ka son pex hoik koe buan son.
2 Pex tag caw rien tix ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig,
cub caw sox la an kix,
pa mawh pui kawn ca hawt ka pa tag sang pat ka to kaux tix khankix.
3 Tete te lai,
pui pa rieb ka been pa Pat-hak,
ang mawh kix,
hoik mawh ex.
Mawh kheu hoik krub ex ka daux khuan Siyiex;
ceu pa yaw ex rang di mawh Ye-sux Khrit.
Aux ang een rhawm tix ka been pa yuh pui plak praix tix ceu kawx.
4 Phan koe pui pa ce tix chix een rhawm ka been pa yuh pui plak praix,
aux nie blawn chix yuh nin.
5 aYam keh aux ka daux tix daix ngaix,
aux hoik Pat-hak tix.
Aux hoik mawh pui Yi-seu-liex tom yam keh tix,
mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
nham pui Si-paw-lai tete.
Phan ex krai ka pa hawt ka been li Yo-thaix,
aux mawh cub caw pui Fa-li-saix;
6 bphan hawt krax gaux rhawm tix krai,
aux hoik sax mawh pui pa tuk tiak ka ceu cao.
Mawh kheu nan,
phan sawn tix rauh see ka daux pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
aux ang koe dee deuh phi map tix ceu kawx.
7 Viang mawh nan,
pa een aux mawh ceu pa mhawm ka tix sivoe an,
yam in kheu son Khrit,
aux hoik kieh in kix mawh ceu pa som nai.
8 Ang joeng mawh ngie nan,
aux nie kieh ceu pa koe khawm uik mawh pa som nai,
mawh kheu hoik een aux krax pa tawng tix ka Simiang Ye-sux Khrit mawh ceu pa ting ngawh ting jum.
Kheu son nawh aux hoik som nai ka ku ceu khawm uik,
mai kieh an kix mawh kram nyiex pa tik pui,
son sang pon ka Khrit,
9 mai kleuk tix tau mai nawh khawm uik.
Aux ang lai koe krax pa rauh see kheu pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
Pa rauh see aux yam in,
hoik mawh kheu pa yum rhawm ka Khrit,
mawh pa tawx Siyiex,
mawh pa kieh mawh ka daux krax yum rhawm.
10 Aux simie ngie tix tawng yung ka Khrit khaing,
mai pon tix yaox ka siawp sibrawm pa kaoh im nawh,
klawm ka krax pa kha rhawm nawh,
yuh ka daux krax pa yum nawh,
11 simie to kaux tix pon tix deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum nang heue.
To hu ka dee koe mhai
12 In ang mawh krai aux hoik pon tix kah,
phan ang mawh nan,
hoik grum mhawm tix.
Aux ca nied tix to hu,
mai sawg ngie tix pon ka khawng phak buan son an;
tete te lai,
son sang kieh hoek ka dee koe mhai,
Ye-sux Khrit hoik pon ka aux.
13 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux ang doh een to kaux tix hoik pon ka khawng phak buan son in;
aux ca viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix tix ceu khaing:
piawm ka ceu pa koe plak khaix tix,
mai sau riang tix leue ka kaing ceu pa koe sivoe ngai tix.
14 Aux to hu ka dee koe mhai,
son sang pon ka khawng phak buan son;
khawng phak buan son in mawh ju pa khraox pa dex dawt tau mai rao ma,
mawh pa tawx Siyiex ka Ye-sux Khrit kok aux hu teei heue.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
ka daux ex pui pa haok huan ka daux khuan,
tag caw tix koe rhawm keud pa yuh nin;
phan pex koe rhawm keud pa ang awm paox tix,
Siyiex tag sang krai ka pex sidawng.
16 Viang yuh ka mawx kawx,
ex tag caw tix hawt awm been pa hoik hawt ex sivoe an hu sivoe heue.
17 cPux aik pux o khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix hawt ka aux.
Yix hoik yuh pex yaox kaing ceu pa mhawm;
pex tag caw gau tix mai pui pa hawt ka yix khankix.
18 Aux hoik sidiex gau ka pex tom nie bawg,
yam in baox sidiex gau ka pex khawm roik rawm ngai tix yuh nin:
koe pui tix blah kaing yuh kix,
kieh kix lih mawh pui pa koeng ka khaox lhak Khrit.
19 Krax lawng kix dee si dauh mawh krax laux leeing,
mawh kheu siyiex cie kix mawh vaig tu kix.
Kix kieh kaing pa kaig tua lih mawh min plak ngai,
mai ang chix piawm ka ceu pa koe ka piang haktiex.
20 Kheu nan ex mawh pui cie Siyiex;
ex viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix krox jun ka cao taoh ex,
pa mawh Simiang Ye-sux Khrit pa lih hoek khaing rao ma.
21 Nawh tag sang kieh can ceu pa koe khawm uik ot greem siawp sibrawm tix,
mai tieh loh ka to kaux ex pa iak riang pa chix yum in,
kieh ex awm nawh yuh nan,
mawh to kaux pa koe min plak ngai heue.
