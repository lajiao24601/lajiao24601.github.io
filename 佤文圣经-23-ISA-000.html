Lai Yi-sai-yax
Krai dawt
Ceu kaux Lai Yi-sai-yax mawh pa kieh hawt ka ceu kaux cao jhai pa ting tix kaux, pa ot ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung ka daux sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num yam sang uik daux daix num. Phuk lai ih khawm uik chix tix guah koe loe grawng:
1. Simin ting tix mu tom hoek simin ting tix ngoe dim mu, mawh pa tiiem lih khaing yam yuh pui meung Ya-sux krax tuk tiak ka meung Yo-tax plak seh. Yi-sai-yax hoik tawng grawng krax laux leeing in ka piang Yo-tax, mai ang joeng mawh ngie kheu krax pa tuk tiak pui meung Ya-sux, mai nie mawh kheu hoik yuh pui piang meung phi map, lieh lheu ka gum lox tawm jhawm krai Siyiex, ang yum rhawm ka nawh. Cao jhai teei lox pa hoek ka rhawm pui, mai ka kaing pa yuh tix sidiex gau pui haoh pui hun, mai simiang khankix tag caw tix yuh kaing pa khaw caw, kaing pa rauh see, khaix an ne raw ka kix ah nin: Phan pui mawx tieb tie ka gum lox tawm jhawm krai Siyiex, tag sang hoek krax laux krax leeing. Yi-sai-yax kawx hoik grox krai singaix pa sang hoek, tag sang lih koe krax pa nhim pa tiiem, khaix an ka daux doeh ceu cad Ta-wue tag sang lih koe simiang pa mhawm rhawm keud tix kaux heue.
2. Simin ting tipon hoek tix phuan phuan, mawh pa tiiem yam yaox pui Yo-tax krax laux rhawm ka daux pa giex laung kix hu ka Pa-pi-loeng. Cao jhai krai pao ah nin: Siyiex tag sang ploi ka pui cie tix, mai viex kix deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung, kieh kix deeh cox jauh ka krax ot krax koe tix. Kaing ting ka daux simin ting in kix, hoik krai sidawng ka Siyiex mawh cao pa yuh ka been pa hoik lih tix mawh khawm uik; ka kaing pa rien Siyiex tix sang yuh ka piang pui cie tix, hoik mawh khawm lox tawm nawh ka meung ka piang haktiex: pa mawh meung ka piang haktiex tag sang pon buan son kheu Yi-seu-liex. Pa krai phuk lai sigang khri “siliex Simiang Siyiex” gum lox in kix, mai hoik mawh gum lox pa sidawng pui ka ku kaux ka daux phuk lai sigang khri pa prim heue.
3. Simin ting tix phuan liah hoek tix gliah liah, mawh gum lox pa krai ka pui hun pa hoik deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix. Yam an, pui haoh pui hun kied ki sang lo ka daux gum lox pa kaux pa cong, pa mawh grawng pa sang kieh Siyiex gum lox pa hoik deuh tix lih tix mawh ka piang meung kix. Cao jhai hoik jhi grawng muh kix ka pa rauh pa see, mai grawng pa hoik yung yi kix tix priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah, mai grawng krax pa tan lu, mai grawng krax pa rawg buan. Yam jauh Ye-sux tix yuh kaing, mai nawh hoik sax teei tix siao ka simin ting tix gliah tix, simin iak tix mu ra mu, mai kieh mawh pa krai sidawng ka gum lox tawx nawh heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pa ne raw mai pa krai tix deuh (1.1–12.6)
2. Pa ciyang ka meung ka piang haktiex (13.1-23.18)
3. Siyiex prawng ca ka haktiex (24.1–27.13)
4. Pa baox ne raw mai pa krai tix deuh (28.1–35.10)
5. Simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Si-si-cia mai pui Ya-sux (36.1–39.8)
6. Gum lox pa hoik ap tix deuh mai gum lox krax keud pot jun pot (40.1–55.13)
7. Ne raw mai pa krai tix deuh (56.1–66.24).
