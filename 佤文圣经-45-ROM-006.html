Yum kheu phi map, im ka daux Khrit
6
Phan mawh nan,
ex sang khaw tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Ex khaw tix ca ot ka daux phi map,
son sang kieh buan son nied tix baox nie lie?
2 Ang doh mawh nan heue!
Phan ex krai tix yaox ka plak phi map in,
ex hoik yum heue.
Ex lai kawn ca ot ka daux phi map yuh ka mawx lie?
3 Pex tee rien tix tawng,
ex hoik cub com tix lih mawh tix mu tau mai Ye-sux Khrit,
mai hoik cub com tix yum tau mai nawh.
4 aKa daux pa cub com,
ex hoik yum tau mai nawh,
si paung tau mai nawh;
awm hoik kieh keeing Siyiex Khrit kaoh im khaing krax yum ka daux min plak ngai tix yuh nan,
ex kawx sang ot ka daux singaix pa khraox yuh nang heue.
5 Phan ex rom tix mawh tix mu tau mai Khrit,
cim ka krax pa yum nawh,
ex kawx tag sang cim ka pa kaoh im nawh yuh nan nang.
6 Ex hoik tawng,
to kaux pa prim ex hoik tawk tix tau mai nawh ka piang khaox lhak,
son sang yuh laux ka to kaux phi map ex,
mai kieh ex ang lai baox mawh siliex cawng phi map.
7 Mawh kheu pui yum hoik bheeik luan khaing siawp sibrawm phi map.
8 Phan ex hawt tix yum tau mai Khrit,
ex yum rhawm,
ex kawx sang im tau mai Khrit.
9 Mawh kheu hoik tawng ex kah,
Khrit hoik kaoh im khaing krax yum,
nawh ang lai baox yum;
krax yum kawx ang lai pon giex gang ka nawh.
10 Krax pa yum nawh hoik kieh phi map dut,
tix taix khaing hoik pon pex khawm uik;
yam in nawh im hoik mawh im son Siyiex.
11 Awm an,
phan krai tix yaox ka plak phi map in,
pex kawx tag caw tix kieh to kaux tix yum nang,
viang mawh nan,
ot ka daux ju Khrit,
pex tom mawh pa im son Siyiex heue.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
ang pon tix kieh phi map giex gang ka to kaux pex pa chix laux chix leeing,
kieh pex hawt ka alo nex nham.
13 Ang pon tix kieh to kaux pak peung pex kreet tix ot greem phi map,
mawh kraung siao pa caox pa caig.
Deeh tieh tix,
tan tix tawx to kaux pex ka Siyiex,
awm pui tix kaux pa taoh lih khaing krax yum,
mai hoik tan tix tawx to kaux tix khawm uik ka nawh,
kieh mawh kraung siao pa rauh pa see.
14 Jauh yam in,
pex ang pon tix lai baox kieh phi map giex gang tix;
mawh kheu pex ang lai ot greem lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai ot greem buan son Siyiex heue.
Siliex pa rauh pa see
15 Krai mawh yuh nin,
mawh kheu ang ex lai ot greem lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai ot greem buan son Siyiex,
ex tom chix tix yuh phi map lie?
Ang doh chix heue!
16 Pex kawx hoik tawng,
yam mawh pex siliex pui,
pex cu nghiet ka nawh,
pex tag mawh siliex nawh.
In tom krai mawh nin,
pex mawh siliex phi map,
dee si dauh mawh krax yum;
pex cu nghiet ka Siyiex,
pex tom pon tix lih mawh pui pa rauh see.
17 Ting buan son Siyiex heue!
Viang pex hoik sax mawh siliex phi map,
yam in pex hoik nghiet ka gum lox pa hoik sidiex aux ka pex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
18 Pex hoik bheeik luan khaing daux phi map,
mai lih mawh siliex pa rauh see.
19 Aux lhat pex ang pon tawng dee bleeh gum lox pa krai tix,
mawh kheu nan aux teei tiag tix yaox ka been pa yie krai ka pex.
Sivoe an pex hawt ka alo nex nham pak peung to kaux tix,
lih mawh pui cao caig,
mawh siliex phi map.
Yam in pex tag caw tix tan lu to kaux tix khawm uik,
lih mawh siliex pa rauh see,
lih mawh kraung siao pa singax singeh heue.
20 Yam mawh pex siliex phi map,
pex ang ot ka daux pa rauh see.
21 Yam in ceu pa een pex mawh kaing pa kaig pa tua,
yam hoik yuh pex kah,
pex hoik pon ceu pa mhawm pa tix lie?
Hoik mawh ngie krax yum heue!
22 Yam in,
pex hoik pheeik luan khaing daux phi map,
mai mawh siliex Siyiex;
pex tan tix tawx ju tix khawm uik ka nawh,
pa pon pex tag sang mawh ju leeing ju.
23 Mawh kheu ngawh riang phi map mawh krax yum;
viang mawh nan,
buan son pa tawx Siyiex giex giex going going ka pex,
hoik kieh ex pon ju leeing ju ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue.
