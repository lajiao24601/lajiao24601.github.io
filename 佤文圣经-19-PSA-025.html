Khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex duk thung kia ngien
25
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang rawg buan ka maix;
2 Siyiex aux eue,
aux yum rhawm ka maix tix kaux khaing.
Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux kaig tua som nai,
paw vi kieh pui koeng ka aux yaw rang di ka aux.
3 Pui pa yum rhawm ka maix ang hoek krax sum krax nai;
pui pa sum pa nai an kix mawh pui pa tieh vheen ka maix heue.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix jhi aux hu daux krax cie maix,
krai aux mhong daux krax cie maix.
5 Khiox khaw maix sidiex gau aux hu daux krax pa mawh pa meh cie maix.
Kheu mawh maix Siyiex pa taoh ka aux;
aux een rhawm tix ka maix leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka krax pa caox rhawm maix,
doeh keud ka krax muh maix an pa hoik jhi maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix tom dix dix.
7 Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi phi map pa yuh aux tom yam mawh kawn nyawm;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kia ngien aux kheu buan son maix,
mai krax muh maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix heue!
 
8 Simiang Siyiex pa naog mai krax pa grum mhawm,
mai mawh pa khaw caw;
nawh jhi pui phi map hu krax pa khaw kix tix hu.
9 Nawh duk thung pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi hu krax rauh krax see,
teei alo tix sidiex gau ka kix.
10 Nawh teei krax muh pa kaux sijax pa ang chix tieh loh tix,
duk thung ka can pui pa priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu nawh mai ka lox tawx nawh heue.
 
11 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix hawt tix pluat ploi ka phi map aux awm gum lox pa hoik deuh maix,
mawh kheu phi map aux hoik kied ting kied lhaong.
12 Can pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex tag chix jhi krax pa khaw nawh tix lawng.
13 Kix tag sang pon krax pa gaux rhawm;
kawn praix kix tag sang nyawm ot nyawm koe ka piang haktiex in.
14 Simiang Siyiex yuh paox grawng mai pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh,
jhi ka sijax mukheu to kaux tix ka kix heue.
 
15 Aux een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex ku bawg ku yam;
nawh taoh aux luat luan khaing krax pa nu.
16 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh veeh kaing tix kia ngien ka aux,
caox rhawm ka aux,
mawh kheu kha aux rhawm tix mai ang koe paox koe pie.
17 Khiox khaw maix teei tix tik krax pa khi rhawm khi rhi aux khawm uik,
taoh aux luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing khawm uik.
18 Khiox khaw maix jhak simi ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi aux tix,
mai pluat ploi ka phi map aux heue.
 
19 Maix jhak,
aux hoik koe pui koeng tom nie yuh nin;
kix hoik koeng khoi aux sigrien sigre yuh nin.
20 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka aux,
taoh teem ka aux;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux som nai,
mawh kheu aux hoik sawg krax pa kia ngien maix.
21 Kieh krax pa mawh pa meh aux mai krax pa rauh see aux pon priex ka aux mhawm,
mawh kheu aux een rhawm tix ka maix heue.
 
22 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
khiox khaw maix kieh kix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing khawm uik heue.
