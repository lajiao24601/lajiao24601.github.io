13
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang kaong daux keet rawm tix mu son doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
mai son pui pa ot haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai si ghrah tix tik ka phi map kix mai ka ceu pa seeing pa brai kix singax.
2 Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang preet tix tik ka ceu kaux riang rao lih khaing haktiex in,
mai ang lai baox kieh pui doh keud ka kix.
Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka pui pa tang kok tix mawh cao jhai khankix,
tag sang preet grai ka rhawm rhi pa keud ka riang rao an.
3 Phan kawn koe pui pa kawn ca krai lox jhai,
miex keeing nawh tag sang krai ka nawh,
tag caw tix pauh nawh yum;
mawh kheu dhua nawh tix krai lox son Simiang Siyiex,
tete te lai nawh pie lhien.
Yam krai nawh lox jhai,
miex keeing keh nawh tag sang klauh nawh yum.
4 Ka daux singaix an,
mai tag ang lai baox koe cao jhai pa yaw rang di son riang su sai to kaux tix,
mai cao jhai pa ceep ka kraung ceep man keei ting mu pa pie lhien ka pui.
5 Nawh tieh tix ah nin:
‘Aux ang mawh cao jhai;
aux mawh pui yuh kaing haktiex,
mai sum kaung sum ma tom si dauh ju tix.’
6 Phan koe pui pa chawk nawh ah nin:
‘Roi khaix mat pa koe ka naok maix kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?’
Nawh pawk ah nin:
‘Mawh khaix mat aux yam ot daux nyiex ma paox grawm tix heue.’ ”
Pauh ka pui liang yung
7 aSimiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Vaig gawn eue,
haing heue,
hu puing ka pui liang yung pa yuh kaing son aux heue!
Phan maix pauh ka pui liang yung,
phung yung taug prung prang tix hu khawm pon daog.
Aux tag sang pauh ka pui cie tix,
8 mai kawn pui piang haktiex loe son ra son tag sang yum.
9 Aux tag sang cheuk cheu tix yaox ka pa deuk an loe son tix son,
mai kieh kix singax,
son sang singax awm mau pa ceed daux glah ngu yuh nan.
Aux tag sang cheuk tix yaox ka kix,
awm cheuk tix yaox ka khri yuh nan,
khaix an kix tom taug rawg buan ka aux,
mai aux tom taug nghiet kah.
Aux tag sang krai ka kix,
kix mawh pui cie aux;
kix tag sang doh keud,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex kix heue.”
