Puing pex ka pui Mi-tiien
8
Pui Yi-fa-liien ah nin ka Ci-tiien:
“Yam hu maix puing ka pui Mi-tiien,
yuh ka mawx lai ang kok ka yix lie?
Maix lai yuh nin ka yix lie?”
Kix ne raw ka Ci-tiien heue.
2 Ci-tiien krai ka kix ah nin:
“Ceu pa pon yuh aux ang lheu khaing ceu pa pon yuh pex;
plix siiet pa sut pex pui Yi-fa-liien kawn lheu mhawm khaing plix siiet pa mhawm cie pui Ya-pi-yi-shiex heue!
3 aSiyiex hoik ap simiang pui Mi-tiien pa kaux Eu-lix mai Si-yi-paw ka daux tawm taix pex;
ceu pa yuh aux tag sang chix tiag tix mai pex yuh ka mawx lie?”
Khaix hoik mhong kix lox pa krai Ci-tiien,
mai kix tag ang lai saux rhawm ka heue.
4 Ci-tiien thung pui an kix loe yieh kaux hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai tiang ka klawng;
viang kix kied taug mao kawx,
mai ca kawn leue ka pui pa koeng khankix.
5 Kix hoek ka haktiex Su-keu,
Ci-tiien ah nin ka pui pa ot ka daux veng:
“Tawx pui naing cie aux ih khao mun ceeix liak,
mawh kheu kied taug mao kix.
Aux kawn leue ka simiang Mi-tiien pa kaux Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na heue.”
6 Viang mawh nan,
simiang pui haktiex Su-keu ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx yix lai tawx pui naing maix ih priex preem lie?
Maix ang nyang pon giex ka Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na heue.”
7 Ci-tiien ah nin:
“Chix tix!
Khaix hoik ap Simiang Siyiex tix tawx Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na ka aux,
aux tag sang teei kat lawm pa koe daux tiag lauh pex ka heue.”
8 Ci-tiien hu sivoe,
hoek ka haktiex Pi-nu-yi-leux,
mai rawg mhaing ka lox pa hoik awm paox ka pui dee an.
Lox pa pawk pui dee an hoik awm lox pui Su-keu yuh nan.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien ah nin ka kix:
“Aux tag chix pon tix hoek mhawm mhawm,
yam an,
aux tag sang yieh ka veng in heue!”
10 Yam an,
Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na ot ka haktiex Cia-keux tau mai pui kix.
Ceu pui kix khawm uik ka daux tiag,
kawn koe ngie pui naing pa hoik mat tix mheen phuan rheng kaux khaing,
mai hoik yum kao ra mheen kaux.
11 Ci-tiien ca nied tix hu ka krax daog daux tiag Naw-pa mai daux tiag Yaw-pi-ha ka plak lih singaix,
mai pheet puing ka pui naing an kix.
12 Simiang pui naing Mi-tiien ra kaux pa mawh Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na yaog tix to;
Ci-tiien leue ka kiex,
pon giex ka kiex,
kieh pui naing kix prung prang tang hu heue.
13 Kawn simex Yaw-a-see pa kaux Ci-tiien kaoh khaing puing tix naing,
yam hoek nawh ka diang moing gawng Si-lie-see,
14 mai pon giex ka pui Su-keu pa nhum ju tix kaux,
mai chawk cheng ka nawh.
Nawh tiiem tix tawx ceu kaux simiang Su-keu mai ceu kaux kuad song khankix ka Ci-tiien,
khawm uik koe atigliah aliah kaux.
15 Ci-tiien hoek ka Su-keu,
mai ah nin ka pui pa ot ka daux veng khankix:
“Kawn doh keud pex yam ang pex cu tix teem coi ka aux lie?
Pex krai ang aux bax pon giex ka Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na,
kheu nan,
ang tawx pui naing aux pa taug pa maox khankix ih ka priex preem.
Yam in pex jhak,
mai kix hoik ot tin heue!”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien tom teei kat lawm pa koe ka daux tiag,
mai si vied simiang pui Su-keu kah;
17 nawh kawx yieh ka veng Pi-nu-yi-leux,
mai pauh ka pui pa ot ka daux veng yum heue.
18 Ci-tiien prawng ka Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na ah nin:
“Pui pa pauh pex yum ka Tha-paw mawh pui pa yuh ka mawx lie?”
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Kix kied awm maix,
hoik awm kawn simiang ku kaux heue.”
19 Ci-tiien ah nin:
“Kix hoik mawh pux aik aux,
hoik mawh pa keh miex tix kaux tau mai aux.
Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex,
phan pex ang pauh ka kix,
aux tag ang pauh ka pex heue.”
20 Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien ah nin ka kawn ai tix pa kaux Yi-thie:
“Hu pauh kix yum heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
Yi-thie lhat,
ang pon leed vaig,
mawh kheu kawn mawh nawh kawn nyawm heue.
21 Si-pa mai Sa-maw-na ah nin ka Ci-tiien:
“Tawx taix maix pauh yiex heue!
Mawh ngie maix pa koe riang tix pauh khaing.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien tom pauh kiex,
mai teei hu ka rhang pa ot ka ngawg ka-la-uk cie kiex heue.
22 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin ka Ci-tiien:
“Khiox khaw maix yuh cao giex kaing yix;
maix mai kawn praix maix khawm uik yuh cao giex kaing yix,
mawh kheu hoik taoh maix yix luat luan khaing pui Mi-tiien heue.”
23 Ci-tiien ah nin:
“Aux ang yuh cao kaing pex;
mai kawn aux kawx ang yuh simiang pex.
Simiang Siyiex tag sang yuh simiang pex.”
24 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Aux koe ngie kaing ceu pa sang rawg ka pex tix ceu khaing,
viex pex tawx kreem pa hoik pon tud pex ka aux ku kaux heue.”
(Kheu hoik mawh pui Mi-tiien pa ih kreem khri awm pui pa ot ka daux tiag khankix heue.)
25 Pui hun pawk lox ah nin:
“Yix gaux rhawm tix tawx ka maix.”
Kix tom tawm sibex tix pheen,
pui ku kaux taing tix tik ka kreem khri pa hoik pon tud tix ka piang.
26 Ci-tiien khrawm ka kreem khri yhaok hoik koe been tix nga kongcing,
khaix an kawn koe ka kraung khrai ble pa ceep simiang Mi-tiien,
mai sibex lung pliex,
khawm ling fua ot ka piang ngawg ka-la-uk.
27 Ci-tiien teei khri in kix yuh riang pa krubb ka tix mu,
deuh ot ka haktiex kix Eu-fu-la.
Khaix an pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik tik vud ka Siyiex,
mai hu krub ka riang dee an;
in tom lih tix mawh daux o pa kieh Ci-tiien,
mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh khankix lud hu heue.
28 Yuh nin,
pui Mi-tiien tom ang pex ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ang lai pon ghlat ka kix.
Singaix yam koe Ci-tiien ka piang haktiex,
ka daux haktiex yien yawg tipon num heue.
Grawng yum Ci-tiien
29 Ci-tiien laux lee tix deeh ing haktiex tix,
mai ot ka nyiex.
30 Nawh koe kawn simex atigliah kaux,
kheu koe moeng iak nawh tom nie.
31 Ka haktiex See-ciien kawx,
nawh koe moeng iak ka tix kaux;
nawh keh kawn simex tix kaux,
Ci-tiien sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-pi-mi-leux.
32 Kawn simex Yaw-a-see pa kaux Ci-tiien,
hoik yum kheu kuad tix,
mai si paung nawh ot tau mai Eu-fu-la pui Ya-pi-yi-shiex,
ka daux si meeig keeing nawh Yaw-a-see heue.
33 Khaix hoik yum Ci-tiien,
pui Yi-seu-liex deeh baox tik vud ka Siyiex,
mai hu krub ka Pa-lix.
Kix kieh Pa-lix.
Pi-li-thux mawh siyiex kix,
34 mai ang lai krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pa hoik taoh kix luat luan khaing pui koeng pa vung viang kix heue.
35 Kix kawx ang lai kia ngien mhawm ka nyiex ma Ci-tiien,
kheu pa hoik yuh nawh kaing pa mhawm son pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
