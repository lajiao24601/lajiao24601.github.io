Siyiex kok ka Maw-si
3
Koe tix ngaix,
Maw-si liang yung paox tix Ye-theu-law pa mawh cao giex kaing li Mi-tiien;
nawh viex yung khankix hu plak daux tiag pak mhaik,
hoek ka gawng Siyiex pa mawh gawng Heu-liex.
2 aKa dee an,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex awm rao ngu,
mai jhi Maw-si yaox tix ka daux pang kat.
Maw-si yaox pa gleeing yeem khaox pang kat,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax proe.
3 Nawh keud:
“Kied daox am kah,
yuh ka mawh yeem khaox pang kat an ang chix proe kheu pa tix lie?
Aux sang haok jhak yaox heue!”
4 Simiang Siyiex yaox hu Maw-si dex ka nawh,
nawh tom kok Maw-si khaing daux sigreung yeem khaox pang kat ah nin:
“Maw-si!
Maw-si!”
Maw-si pawk lox:
“Mawh,
aux ot tin!”
5 Siyiex ah nin:
“Paw hoek dex.
Peeik kiiep maix,
mawh kheu dee cung maix mawh dee singax singeh.
6 Aux mawh Siyiex tax du maix,
pa mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux.”
Maw-si mox pak ngai tix,
lhat jhak ka Siyiex heue.
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux hoik yaox grawng pa gut kheem kix ceu pui aux ka daux Ai-cix;
aux hoik mhong lox pa yiiem kix,
kheu simie kix tix bheeik luan khaing pa yuh tix siliex cawng.
Aux hoik tawng grawng pa kha kix rhawm tix,
8 kheu nan,
tom lih hoek,
son sang taoh aux kix lih khaing daux tawm taix ceu pui Ai-cix,
mai tuk kix hoek ka haktiex pa mhawm,
pa vieh,
haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia.
Dee an mawh dee ot ceu pui Cia-nang,
pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we mai pui Yie-pu-see khankix.
9 Aux hoik mhong ka lox pa yiiem ceu pui aux tete,
baox yaox ka grawng pa yeen pui Ai-cix ka kix.
10 Yam in,
aux sang ploi maix hu ka simiang Ai-cix dee an;
maix hu teei pui cie aux lih khaing Ai-cix heue.”
11 Viang mawh nan,
Maw-si deeh ah nin ka Siyiex:
“Sawn aux tix mawh tix?
Aux doh sang pon hu ka simiang Ai-cix dee an,
mai teei pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih khaing Ai-cix lie?”
12 Siyiex pawk lox:
“Aux sang ot tau mai maix.
Khaix hoik teei maix pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Ai-cix,
caw tix yuh kaing krub aux ka gawng in.
In tag mawh mhai sakse grawng pa cau aux ka maix heue.”
13 bMaw-si ah nin:
“Yam hu aux krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
‘Siyiex keeing pex hoik ploi aux hoek ka pex dee in’,
kix tee chawk aux:
‘Mawh kaux nawh ceu pa tix?’
Yam an,
aux sang pawk yuh ka mawx lie?”
14 dSiyiex ah nin:
“Aux mawh pa koe tom yam jauhc.
Maix krai kix mhong yuh nin:
‘Cao pa koe tom yam jauh an,
hoik ploi aux hoek ka pex tin.’
15 Aux mawh Yie-heu-hua,
mawh Siyiex tax du kix,
mawh Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux heue.
Aux ploi maix hoek ka kix tan;
ceu kaux aux mawh Yie-heu-hua leeing ju,
leeing ju leeing phan caw tix kok aux mawh nin heue.
16 Maix hu khrawm cub simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
krai kix mhong.
Aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex tax du kix,
Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux hoik bleeh ka maix.
Krai ka kix,
aux hoek ka daux kix,
aux hoik yaox can ceu pa yuh pui Ai-cix khankix ka pex.
17 Aux hoik but rhawm tix sang teei kix lih khaing Ai-cix.
Kix hoik kub kham krax kha rhawm;
aux sang viex kix hoek ka haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia dee kied mhawm haktiex,
dee ot ceu pui Cia-nang,
pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we mai pui Yie-pu-see heue.
18 “Pui cie aux tag nghiet lox maix.
Khaix an maix viex simiang pui Yi-seu-liex hu ka simiang Ai-cix,
ah nin ka nawh:
‘Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ceu pui Si-paw-lai,
hoik bleeh ka yix.
Yam in,
viex maix cu yix hu loe singaix krax,
hoek ka daux tiag,
son sang yuh yix krax tan mai krub yix ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix heue.’
19 Aux hoik tawng,
lheu khaing khin aux ka nawh,
simiang Ai-cix ang doh cu tix ploi pex lih hu heue.
20 Viang mawh nan,
aux sang siao siawp sibrawm tix yuh mhai pa daox pa am ku ceu hoek ciyang ka Ai-cix.
Dee si dauh simiang Ai-cix tag ploi pex lih hu heue.
21 e“Aux tag kieh ceu pui Ai-cix yung yi ka pex;
kheu nan,
yam lih hu pui cie aux khaing tan,
kix tag ang yaox tix lih hu mai taix yi tix.
22 Bun Yi-seu-liex ku kaux tag hu ka paox plawg paox nyhawm tix,
mai mhaing kraung mau,
kraung khri mai kraung ceep khaing bun miex Ai-cix khankix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex sang tawx kawn simex kawn bun tix khankix ceep,
yuh nin kix tom pon viex kraung khrai sin ngao pui Ai-cix hu heue.”
