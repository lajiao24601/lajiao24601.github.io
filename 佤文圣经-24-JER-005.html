Phi map Yie-lu-sa-leung
5
Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
hu tauh ka daux laih ting laih iak pex heue!
Hu tix lam lia mhawm mhawm ka heue!
Hu tix sawg ka daux laih daux cau dee yuh pui lai gee ka heue!
Jhak tix yaox sang pon ang pon yaox ka pui pa rauh pa see tix kaux kaux lie?
Phan pex pon tix yaox,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang chix pluat ploi ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 Pex krai lhat graung yung yi tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
tete te lai,
ang tie bax mawh lox pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm rhi pex.
3 Simiang Siyiex rug sawg ka pui pa rauh pa see tete.
Nawh puing ka pex,
mai pex ang simi ka nawh,
ciyang ka pex,
mai pex kawx ang nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau nawh.
Pex hoik kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
mai ang cu tix tik vud ka phi map.
4 Mawh kheu nan aux keud ah nin:
Kix mawh ngie pui hot pa daox aw;
kix ang tawng daux krax cie Simiang Siyiex,
ang tawng lox tat jhawm prawng cie Siyiex.
5 Aux tag sang sawg tix krai lox tau mai simiang kix.
Kix tee rien tix tawng daux krax cie Simiang Siyiex,
tee rien tix tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng cie Siyiex.
Viang mawh nan,
kix hoik thiang ka siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex,
ang cu tix nghiet ka nawh heue.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
sivai sang si tag sang kaoh khaing daux nong khaox,
mai hoek tix bleet ka kix;
sox la tag sang kaoh khaing daux tiag,
mai hoek tix kiied kix phawng vid;
sivai kaoh ku tag sang ot tix kuan jun kix ka hawt siviex veng;
pui mawx lih hu,
tag sang kiied pui an phawng vid.
Mawh kheu ca baox singai hu kix khaing Siyiex ang jud;
phi map kix ang lai chix uik simin.
7 Simiang Siyiex tang deeh chawk to kaux tix ah nin:
Aux sang chix pluat ploi ka phi map pui cie tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Kix hoik tieh vheen ka aux,
mai hu krub ka siyiex pa pie lhien.
Aux kieh kix yaox tix sawm sak,
mai kix tieh tix keud krax phit krax lud kheu pa si u ot tix,
lih tix mawh cub caw pa hu sawg ka bun coh kaux.
8 Kix awm brung tiag pa eei pui kluing pa simie tix si keeing yuh nan,
hawt khaix moeng pui tang kaux khankix.
9 Aux ang khaw tix ciyang ka kix kheu kaing in kix lie?
Aux ang khaw tix pawk bix ka meung haktiex pa yuh nin lie?
10 Aux tag sang kok pui koeng hoek tix yuh ma si peem plix siiet pui cie aux laux leeing.
Ca mawh ang aux baox yuh kix laux leeing khawm uik khaing.
Aux tag sang kieh kix kid ka kak khaox plix siiet,
mawh kheu ang kak khaox in kix lai mawh cie aux heue.
11 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai pui Yo-tax hoik tieh vheen ka aux khawm uik heue.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Simiang Siyiex tik vud ka pui Yi-seu-liex
12 Pui cie Simiang Siyiex thiang ka nawh.
Kix ah nin:
“Tete te lai Simiang Siyiex ang tieb tie ka yixa.
Yix ang yaox tix hoek krax laux krax leeing,
ang yaox tix si pup ka naing mai krax cai rhawm sibhawm praix.”
13-14 Kix baox ah nin:
“Cao jhai krai ngie kix lox giex going khaing;
kix ang baox pon lox khaing Simiang Siyiex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex simiang kru naing ah nin ka aux:
“Yie-li-mi eue,
pui in kix lai krai lox pa yuh nin,
aux sang sau gum lox tix ka daux jheeh maix.
Gum lox aux awm ngu,
kawn pui awm khix;
ngu tag sang gleeing kix singax khawm uik heue.”
15 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh meung tix meung hoek khaing dee singai,
mai puing ka pex.
Meung in hoik dawg mawh meung haktiex prim,
mai kied ting riang ting rao tete;
lox si kah kix pex ang tawng.
16 Pui pa kied chix puing ka ak puh kix hoik mawh pui naing pa kua khawm uik,
ang lai lhat graung mai pauh ka pui.
17 Kix tag sang ih ka nghox koi pex.
Kix tag sang pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun pex,
pauh tix ih ka moi yung pex.
Kix tag sang ih uik ka plix siiet pex mai plix ruh pex.
Pui naing kix tag sang yieh laux ka tab pa hoik een pex rhawm tix ka heue.
18 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Viang aux hoik kieh krax laux krax leeing lih,
aux kawx ang yuh pui cie tix laux leeing khawm uik.
19 Yie-li-mi eue,
phan kix chawk yuh ka mawx aux lai yuh nan ka kix,
maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
mawh kheu hoik tik vud kix ka aux,
mai yuh kaing son siyiex ka daux meung haktiex tix,
mawh kheu nan kix tag sang vuk vied vuk viang hoek ka meung haktiex pui tang meung,
mai yuh kaing son pui meung haktiex tang meung heue.”
Siyiex ne raw ka pui cie tix
20 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Tag caw tix krai hu ka doeh ceu cad Ya-keux,
mai krai pao ka pui Yo-tax ah nin:
21 bPex pa mawh pui daox aw in kix eue,
tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet mhawm mhawm heue!
Viang pex koe ngai ang chix yaox,
koe yhaok ang chix mhong heue!
22 cAux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex chawk pex ah nin:
yuh ka mawx pex lai ang lhat graung yung yi ka aux lie?
Ot ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
yuh ka mawx lai ang si yaung si kua lie?
Aux kieh mhaik mawh mee rawm sigrit sigra,
mai rawm ting ang pon jao ka mee in.
Rawm sigrit sigra pa pu pak kawx ang pon jao luan;
pa pu pak rawm ting kawx ang pon yieh laux.
23 Viang mawh nan,
pex pa mawh pui in kix pa kiien rhawm kiien rhi mai pa lieh lox lheu si kah heue!
Pex tieh vheen ka aux,
tik vud ka aux.
24 Viang aux hoik hawt bawg yam tawx rawm lhiex cao ka pex,
mai kieh to ma to koi pex haok huan,
viang mawh nan,
pex tieh tix ang lhat graung yung yi ka aux.
25 Phi map pa yuh pex hoik kieh bhaung lhiex ang lai khaw paox;
phi map pex ang lai kieh pex yaox tix pon to ma to koi heue.
26 “Pui phi map ot ka daux pui cie aux.
Kix taong jhawk yuh pui laux leeing,
awm pui pa sawg tix giex ka sim pa hoik nih tix taong ka rub yuh nan.
27 Ka daux nyiex ma nawh hoik pum naog ka kraung pa briex pa lak,
mai awm ka daux grau pui jaw pa hoik naog ka to tiag yuh nan.
Mawh kheu nan,
kix koe mau koe khri,
koe siawp sibrawm,
28 mai yaox tix kluing mu raoh ngai baoh.
Kix kied caox kied caig,
ang cu tix prawng rauh see son kawn doe pa ang koe cao kia cao ngiien,
ang cu tix prawng rauh see son pui hot pui sibhawm pa ang koe cao teem cao coi heue.
29 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka kix kheu kaing ceu in kix;
aux tag sang pawk bix ka meung pa yuh nin.
30 Ka daux meung haktiex in lai lih koe ka kaing pa lang teung caox,
pa kieh pui pru praoh yaoh yee ka heue.
31 Cao jhai chix pie lhien ka pui khaing;
cao giex kaing li chix sawg ngie tix pon ka siawp sibrawm son to kaux tix khaing;
pui cie aux lai ang tieb tie ka kix.
Yam hoik hoek singaix bawg yam pa ciyang,
kix tag sang yuh ka mawx lie?”
