Lai A-maw-see
Krai dawt
Ka daux pui pa toe tix krai lox Siyiex ka daux phuk lai sigang khri pa prim, gum lox A-maw-see mawh pa tiiem tix sidawng tix kaux. Viang nawh hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing yaong plak seh pa koe ka daux meung Yo-tax tix yaong, viang mawh nan, nawh hoik sidiex ka meung Yi-seu-liex pa koe plak laong. Yam an mawh sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num been daix bawg yam, jiet mawh yam haok huan meung daux been li kied sidawng, kawn pui hoik pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe. Kawn pui ce in hoik mawh mhai sakse buan son pa tawx Siyiex. Viang mawh nan, A-maw-see hoik tawng kah, pa ah kix krax pa haok huan an mai hoik mawh ngie son pui mag mi khaing, pui hot sibhawm ca kawn kham krax pa ang khaw caw mai krax gut krax kheem; grawng krax haok huan been li hoik mawh pa pieh pa lhien, pa ah mawh krax nyawm ot kawx hoik mawh ngie plak praix khaing heue.
A-maw-see tawx lox pa ne raw ka daux krax muh tix mai ka daux krax pa kua tix, mai grox krai Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka Yi-seu-liex. Nawh khiox khaw mai kieh pa rauh pa see “buing tix lih awm rawm klawng yuh nan heue!” Nawh ah nin: “Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing tee kawn chix tawx krax pa caox rhawm tix ka pui meung in pa kawn deuk khankix heue.” (5.15)
Krai kaing
1. Pa prawng ca ka meung pa ot sivie siveng Yi-seu-liex (5.15)
2. Pa prawng ca ka Yi-seu-liex (2.4–6.14)
3. Riang su sai phuan ceu (7.1–9.15)
