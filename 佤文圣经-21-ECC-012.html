12
Yam kawn nhum ju maix,
tag caw tix doh keud ka cao pa phan pa yuh ka maix.
Yam kawn ang maix nyang kuad,
maix kawn ang nyang bax krai “grawng krax pa im ju ang koe dee bleeh”,
2 kheu nan,
yam ang singaix,
khix,
simuing nyang bax grai pa riang,
yam ang pai awm nyang bax grai khaix hoik lih lhiex,
maix tag caw tix doh keud ka nawh.
3 Bawg yam hoik hoek,
plawng taix maix tag sang si yaung,
ba maix tag sang si kua mai ang lai koe riang.
Rhang maix deuk ngie ra loe plah khaing,
ang lai pon sai tix ih.
Ngai maix hoik pu pi,
ang lai chix yaox sidawng.
4 Yhaok maix hoik lheet,
mai ang lai chix mhong lox pa lawh pui daux laih daux cau;
lox pa krid pui mhaw mai lox pa rhax pui lai maix ang lai chix mhong.
Viang mawh nan,
yam jauh saik tix rhax lai,
mai maix hoik haing.
5 Maix hoik lhat haok ka dee lhaong,
yam lawng maix krax kawx hoik mawh pa nu tete.
Hauk kaing maix hoik paing,
riang rao maix hoik uik,
rhawm si lao maix hoik grai,
mai ang lai chix deeh teei hoek heue.
Ex sang hu ka dee ot tix dee si dauh khawm uik;
yam an,
ka daux laih tag sang koe ka lox pa yiiem.
6 Yam an,
maox mau hoik dut,
jawk kawk ngu khri hoik max,
maox pa vhak daux dung hoik dut,
aw rawm pa ot ka praok dung hoik pauh max.
7 To kaux ex tag sang deeh ing ka phawng tiex;
bhawm ex tag sang deeh ing ka Siyiex cao pa tawx ju ka ex heue.
8 Pa giex pa going,
pa giex pa going,
cao sidiex ah nin,
khawm uik hoik mawh pa giex pa going heue.
Gum lox pa nghoik
9 Kheu nan,
cao sidiex mawh pui pa lhak lhiao;
nawh ca nied tix sidiex gau ceu pa tawng tix ka pui haoh pui hun.
Nawh si kaw tix yaox ka lox pa tawx satix,
mai cheuk tix yaox ka grawng pa mawh pa meh nawh.
10 Cao pa sidiex sawg ka gum lox pa chix haing yuh rhawm rhi kawn pui,
mai tiiem ka gum lox pa mawh pa meh lih.
11 Gum lox pa krai pui pa lhak lhiao chix kia ngien dee lud pui,
awm khaox buat pa koe kat pa viex pui liang yung daux tawm taix tix yuh nan,
mai gum lox satix pa hoik tiiem nawh tix deuh kaux cong ang yung yang,
awm jim rhiem pa hoik tawk tix kaux cong yuh nan,
hoik mawh pa tawx Siyiex cao pa liang ka kawn pui heue.
12 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
kawn koe tix ceu pa caw maix bre rhawm tix kah.
Gum lox lai pa hoik tiiem tix deuh ang chix uik chix leeing;
phan phat lai tom nie mai tag chix kieh to kaux maix taug mao heue.
13 Lox pa koe khawm uik hoik krai uik,
phan ex khrawm tix krai mawh gum lox tix gum:
tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh,
kheu hoik mawh pa khaw pui ku kaux tix yuh.
14 Can kaing pa hoik yuh ex khawm uik,
phan ang mawh nan pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm,
pie khawm kaing ceu pa glip khankix,
Siyiex tag sang prawng ca ka heue.
