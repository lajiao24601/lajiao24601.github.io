Yie-li-mi ne raw ka nyiex ma simiang Yo-tax
22
1-2 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux hu nyiex haw simiang Yo-tax,
mai krai ka simiang ka daux bawg yam pa giex Ta-wue kaing mai ka simiang naing nawh,
mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Khaix an Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
3 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie pex prawng ca ka daux pa khaw caw,
ka daux pa khaw caw,
mai taoh ka pui pa gut kheem pui khaing daux tawm taix cao pa gut kheem ka kix.
Pex ang pon tix yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka nyo si nie,
ka kawn doe,
mai ka miex mai khankix;
ang pon tix kieh nham pui pa ang bax lud loe jauh ka daux haktiex in.
4 Phan pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx aux,
doeh ceu cad Ta-wue tag sang yaox tix plud tix mawh simiang.
Kix khawm simiang naing kix,
mai pui hun tag sang yaox tix ngawm ka piang law ling,
brug ka brung,
mai laig ka daux diang siviex veng,
laig ka daux nyiex haw simiang.
5 aPhan pex ang nghiet ka lox tawx aux,
aux tag sang tiex tu ka pex,
nyiex haw in tag sang lih tix mawh dee riem.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
6 “Ka plak sivoe ngai aux,
nyiex haw nyiex ma simiang awm Ci-liex,
mai baox awm gawng Li-pa-noeng pa mhawm yuh nan;
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang kieh nawh lih mawh daux tiag pa nhik,
ang koe pui pa paung ot kah.
7 Aux tag sang ploi pui yuh nawh laux.
Kix tag sang viex lawm moe hoek,
kid ka rong khaox si oe bhawng pa kied mhawm an kix,
mai taung sau daux ngu heue.
8 “Khaix an,
koe pui tang meung tom nie tag sang laig ka daux haktiex in,
mai ra lai chawk ka paox tix,
Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx lai yuh nin ka veng ting in lie?
9 Khaix an kix baox chawk ka to kaux tix ah nin:
mawh kheu pui veng in hoik lieh lheu ka sijax mukheu pa yuh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix tau mai kix,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux heue.”
Grawng Yaw-ha-see
10 Pui Yo-tax eue,
paw vi yiiem son simiang pa hoik yum;
paw vi laux rhawm son nawh.
Tag caw tix yiiem son kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yaw-ha-see;
pui hoik giex laung ka nawh,
mai nawh ang pon deeh hoek leeing ju,
ang pon yaox ka haktiex tix.
11 bPhan mawh Yaw-ha-see grawng pa plud nawh tix mawh simiang Yo-tax son keeing tix pa mawh Yaw-si-yax,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Pui hoik giex laung nawh hu khaing dee in,
mai nawh ang lai pon hoek leeing ju.
12 Nawh tag sang yum ot ka daux haktiex pa vuk vied vuk viang tix,
ang lai pon yaox ka haktiex in heue.”
Grawng Yaw-ya-cin
13 Hoik laux leeing heue,
pui in hoik hoek krax laux krax leeing heue!
Nawh teei krax pa ang rauh ang see yuh ka nyiex haw son to kaux tix,
teei krax pa ang khaw caw vih vieh ka nyiex haw.
Nawh khin pui hun yuh kaing giex going,
ang tawx mau riang kix heue.
14 Hoik laux leeing heue,
pui in hoik hoek krax laux krax leeing heue!
Nawh ah nin:
aux tag sang sum ka nyiex haw pa ting lhaong,
tag sang yuh ka nyiex lhaong pa vieh pa va.
Nawh sau siviex daux doh,
teei khaox si oe bhawng yuh kawn phia kah,
kraix rawm siang pa raoh rawm kah.
15 Khex sang sum maix ka nyiex haw pa yuh ka khaox si oe bhawng pa lheu mhawm khaing pui tang kaux,
son sang jhi ka grawng mawh tix simiang pa lhak tix kaux lie?
Keeing maix hoik nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux ju tix.
Nawh hoik yuh pa rauh pa see tom si dauh;
kaing pa hoik yuh nawh khawm uik pon yaox tix mhawm.
16 Nawh hoik prawng rauh see son pui hot pa ang koe cao teem khankix;
nawh hoik pon yaox tix mhawm khawm uik.
In hoik mawh pa jhi grawng pa tawng nawh ka Simiang Siyiex.
17 Viang mawh nan,
maix hoik sawg ngie son sang mhawm ka to kaux tix khaing;
maix hoik kieh nham pui pa ang koe dee lud jauh,
hoik gut kheem pui hun ka daux krax pa tud pa rhim.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
18 cMawh kheu nan,
phan mawh Yaw-ya-cing kawn simex Yaw-si-yax simiang Yo-tax,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
ang koe pui pa yiiem son nawh ah nin:
lox pa yiiem pa ngia,
paox grawm aux kied kha rhawm rhi tix heue!
Ang koe pui pa yiiem son nawh ah nin:
simiang aux eue!
Simiang aux eue!
19 Pui tag sang reu nawh hu,
tik nawh ka plak praix veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
awm hoik si paung pui ka la yuh nan.
Grawng kaing pa si pup pui hun Yie-lu-sa-leung
20 Pui pa ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
haok ka gawng Li-pa-noeng kok yo heue!
Hoek yiiem ka haktiex Pa-sang heue!
Jauh ka choek gawng Maw-ya kok yo heue!
Mawh kheu paox grawng pex hoik laux leeing som nai khawm uik.
21 Yam haok huan pex Simiang Siyiex hoik ne raw ka pex,
pex ah nin:
yix ang nghiet ka heue!
Jauh tom yam iak pex,
mai pex ang nghiet lox;
ang sax nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex tix bawg kawx.
22 Bhaung tag sang pua simiang pex hu;
pui tag sang giex laung paox grawm pex hu.
Pex tag sang pon krax kaig krax tua,
mawh kheu hoik yuh pex pa caox pa caig.
23 Pex paung ot ka nyiex haw Li-pa-noeng pa teei pui khaox si oe bhawng yuh kah,
pex ce tix tix kied nyawm ot,
viang mawh nan,
yam lih hoek krax laux krax leeing,
pex kied kha rhawm kha rhi tix heue!
Pex tag sang riag yo awm bun yha yam sang keh tix yuh nan heue.
Siyiex prawng ca ka Yaw-ya-cin
24 dSimiang Siyiex krai ka Yaw-ya-cing kawn simex Yaw-ya-cin simiang Yo-tax ah nin:
“Aux pa mawh Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju tiex tu ah nin:
viang maix hoik mawh cum jeep giien pa ceep aux ot ka taix khawm dawm tix kawx,
aux tag sang peeik tix tik ka maix,
25 mai tawx ka pui pa lhat graung maix mai ka pui pa sang pauh ka maix.
Aux tag sang ap tix tawx maix ka Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
mai ka pui naing cie nawh.
26 Aux tag sang grheu maix mai miex maix hu ka meung haktiex tang meung,
mai hoek ka haktiex pa ang mawh haktiex pa keh pex;
pex tag sang yum ka dee an.
27 Pex kied simie tix deeh hoek,
viang mawh nan,
ang lai pon deeh hoek leeing ju heue.”
28 Aux ah nin:
“Simiang Yaw-ya-cin ang awm kawng pa max tix mu,
pa ang koe pui pa lo tix siao lie?
Phan ang mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx pui lai tik vud ka nawh mai ka kawn simex kawn bun nawh yuh nan,
ploi hu ka haktiex tang dauh lie?”
29 Haktiex eue!
Haktiex eue!
Haktiex eue!
Tag caw tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex.
30 Nawh ah nin:
pui in hoik tat tix sang kieh kawn simex kawn bun tix grai,
lih tix mawh pui pa ang koe tid tix heue!
Nawh ang koe doeh ceu cad son sang plud tix mawh simiang khaix simiang Ta-wue;
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa kuang ka piang pui Yo-tax.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
