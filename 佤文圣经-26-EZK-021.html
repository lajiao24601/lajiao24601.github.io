Vaig cie Simiang Siyiex
21
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix veeh ngai tix ka plak Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ne raw ka nyiex pa singax an kix.
Tag caw tix ne raw ka Yi-seu-liex,
3 aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
aux tag sang tieb tie ka pex;
aux tag sang lawd vaig pauh ka pex,
mawh pui mhawm pui caox ang ah tag sang pauh yum khawm uik.
4 Aux tag sang teei vaig tix pauh ka pui,
jauh ka plak laong hoek ka plak seh tag sang pauh yum khawm uik.
5 Can pui khawm uik tag sang tawng,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik lawd ka vaig tix;
aux ang doh lai leed deeh heue.
6 “Pui pa chix laux chix leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix yiiem ngia awm pui laux rhawm laux rhi yuh nan;
maix tag caw tix yiiem ngia ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun.
7 Phan kix chawk maix yuh ka mawx lai yiiem ngia,
maix tom krai ka kix,
maix hoik yiiem ngia ka mhong tix lox pa ang mhawm.
Yam hoek lox pa ang mhawm an,
rhawm rhi kix tag sang si kua,
taix kix khawm ra khawn ang lai koe riang,
ngawng kix khawm ra khawn si yaung,
rhawm pa kua kix hoik grai.
Bawg yam hoik hoek,
krax laux krax leeing tag sang ot sivoe kix heue!”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
8 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
9 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai lox jhai,
tag caw tix krai ka kawn pui,
aux pa mawh Siyiex krai yuh nin:
Vaig tix kre,
vaig pa sawng riang tix kre,
10 mai hoik klaing lawm son sang pauh ka pui,
hoik awt plang awm pa jhan riang ngu sah.
Pui cie aux ang nghiet ka lox ne raw,
ang lhat ka krax ciyang;
kix sang chix koe krax gaux rhawm yuh ka mawx heue!
11 Vaig hoik awt plang,
mai hoik chix tix siao;
vaig hoik klaing lawm,
mai chix tix ap ka daux tawm taix pui pa pauh pui heue.
 
12 Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
yiiem grai heue!
In hoik mawh son vaig pa klaing pui cie aux lawm;
in hoik mawh vaig pa awt plang simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pui hoik pauh kix yum tau mai pui cie aux pa kawn deuk khankix.
Maix deek rhawm dieh naog tix heue.
13 Yam cheuk aux tix yaox ka pui cie tix;
phan kix ang loh rhawm rhi tix,
krax laux krax leeing tag sang hoek ka piang kix heue.
14 “Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa chix laux chix leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai ka lox jhai.
Maix tag caw tix dieh ka taix tix,
vaig an tom chuh tix mug pauh ang jud.
In hoik mawh vaig pa pauh ka pui,
mawh vaig pa kieh pui si yaung pa pauh ka pui.
15 Vaig in kieh pui cie aux laux rhawm sideeing tix goh.
Aux teei vaig jhan riang awm ngu sah,
pa rien tix sang pauh ka pui ghlat ka veng kix.
16 Vaig pa lawm eue,
pauh ka plak goix plak dawm heue!
Maix ang koe pa ang tix pex.
17 Aux kawx tag sang dieh ka taix tix,
mai krax pa song rhawm aux tag sang grai.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Vaig cie simiang Pa-pi-loeng
18 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
nawh ah nin:
19 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix mhai ka krax pa viex simiang Pa-pi-loeng vaig laig tix tap lih ra nyaing;
krax in kiex ra nyaing hoik mawh krax pa jauh meung haktiex ku meung tix lawng kah.
Maix tag caw tix sum mhai koe ka gah krax in kiex son simiang haktiex,
20 tix mu kieh jhi plak veng La-pa daux haktiex Ya-meung,
tix mu kieh jhi plak Yo-tax,
mai doh hu plak veng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa kieh kaux cong.
21 Yam hoik hoek simiang Pa-pi-loeng ka gah krax,
nawh cung ka sivoe mhai krax,
mai teei ka tea yuh krax pa si pawk,
son sang khiox khaw ka riang rao pa krub an,
mai lam lia tix yaox ka tawm nhao Krax-tan khawng tan an.
22 Taix khawn dawm nawh giex ka te,
mai tiiem ka ‘Yie-lu-sa-leung’ heue!
In mawh krai grawng sang puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Nawh tag sang rien tix yuh ka khaox dung pa yieh ka veng,
mai siao son sang puing ka siviex veng;
tag sang pum ka tiex,
kaong ka daux rawng,
mai kok yo lox pa nhie pui pauh.
23 “Viang mawh nan,
pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ang yum rhawm ka lox jhai in,
mawh kheu hoik yuh kix sijax mukheu tau mai pui Pa-pi-loeng.
Lox jhai in tag sang so ka rhawm kix,
kieh kix doh keud ka phi map pa hoik yuh tix,
mai ne raw ka kix pui tag sang giex kix hu.
24 In hoik mawh gum lox pa krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong.
Phi map pex hoik plih lih.
Pui ku kaux tag sang tawng ka ting phi map pex,
kaing pa yuh ku ceu tag sang kieh phi map lih.
Pex tag sang kham krax ciyang;
aux tag sang ap pex ka pui koeng heue.
25 “Maix pa mawh pa caox pa caig simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa khaw tix yum eue,
krax lawng maix hoik si dauh;
maix tag sang kham krax ciyang pa nghoik tix koe singaix heue.
26 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin.
Peeik mhok simiang mai lawk kaing lih khaing kaing maix heue!
Hoik tieh loh tix khawm uik.
Pui pa ang koe siawp sibrawm tag sang yaog kix lhaong,
simiang tag sang cot kix lih khaing dee lhaong.
27 Hoik laux leeing,
hoik laux leeing,
veng in hoik laux leeing.
Viang mawh nan,
tag sang krox jun aux hoek ciak tix ciyang ka pui veng in,
kaing in tom taug lih tix mawh.
Yam an aux tag sang ap tix tawx veng in ka nawh heue.”
Vaig mai pui Ya-meung
28 b“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai lox jhai;
maix tag caw tix krai pao lox pa map nai aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka pui Ya-meung haktiex Yi-seu-liex.
Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix ah nin:
‘Vaig pa pauh pui hoik rien khre tix;
vaig hoik awt plang,
hoik jhan riang awm ngu sah,
tag sang pauh ka pui heue.
29 Riang su sai pa hoik yaox pex mawh pa giex pa going;
lox jhai pa hoik krai pex mawh lox pie lox lhien.
Pex pa caox caig khaw tix yum,
krax lawng pex hoik si dauh.
Pex tag sang kham krax ciyang pa nghoik tix koe singaix heue.
Vaig tag deuh tix ot piang ngawg pex heue.
30 “ ‘Sau vaig pex laig daux phak vaig heue!
Aux tag sang prawng ca pex ka haktiex ot pex,
pa mawh haktiex keh pex.
31 Ka daux rhawm rhi aux hoik rup awm ngu yuh nan,
tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang pex khawm uik.
Aux tag sang ap tix tawx pex ka daux tawm taix pui pa pauh pui.
32 Ngu tag sang gleeing ka pex khawm uik.
Nham pex tag sang jauh ka daux meung haktiex pex,
mai ang lai baox koe pui pa doh keud ka pex.’ ”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
