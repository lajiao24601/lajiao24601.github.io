Ni-si-mi pon pex ka pui pa tieb tie ka kaing yuh nawh
4
Yam mhong Chang-pa-la pui yix Yo-thaix hoik jauh tix cox sum ka guh veng,
tom kied song rhawm song rhi,
mai nyiah ka yix.
2 Nawh ah nin ka plak sivoe ngai paox pa yuh tix kaing tau mai ka plak sivoe ngai pui naing Sa-ma-li-yax:
“Pui Yo-thaix pa awn riang awn rao in kix yuh ceu pa tix lie?
Kix keud tix sang cox sum ka veng in lie?
Kix ce tix sang hoik yuh kaing in tix ngaix khaing kheu hoik yuh krax tan lu lie?
Kix khex ce tix sang deeh sawg simaox khaing daux pum bliah beung gleeing pa max,
mai viex hoek sum ka lie!”
3 To-pi-ya pui Ya-meung cung ka praok nawh,
mai baox ah nin:
“Kix sang pon sum ka guh veng pa yuh ka mawx lie?
Ex keud pie sox la tix mu khaing rien tix pon dig ghrup heue!”
4 Aux rawg buan ah nin:
“Siyiex yix eue,
yix hoik mhong lox pa nyiah kix ka yix!
Kieh krax pa nyiah kix deeh hoek piang kaing kix heue!
Kieh pui tud sin ngao kix hu;
kieh kix awm pui lud pa laung pui yuh nang,
pui giex kix hu meung tang meung.
5 Khiox khaw maix paw vi pluat ploi ka kaing lud pa yuh kix,
paw vi piawm ka phi map kix pa caox pa caig yuh kix,
mawh kheu kix hoik map nai ka yix pui pa yuh kaing in heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
yix nied tix cox sum ka guh veng,
ang rheeng hoik pon sum tix plak been lhaong,
mawh kheu pui hun hoik gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix yuh kaing in khawm uik heue.
7 Chang-pa-la mai To-pi-ya,
khawm pui A-la-pawx,
Ya-meung,
Ya-seu-thu hoik mhong grawng hoik phai yuh kix kaing pa cox sum guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai dee cud dee cha guh veng hoik cox sum tix mhawm khawm uik,
kix kied song rhawm song rhi kah.
8 Kix rom tix keud krax pa ang mhawm,
sang hoek tix puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
yuh krax pa suk sag.
9 Viang mawh nan,
yix rawg buan ka Siyiex yix,
mai ploi pui hu pau leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
rien ka kix heue.
10 Pui pa ot haktiex Yo-tax koe lai rhax tix ceu pa rhax ah nin:
Riang rao pui pa yuh kaing nied ot nied iak;
koe bliah beung max tom nie sang gah hu!
Yix doh sang pon hoik cox guh veng daux in singaix lie?
11 Pui pa koeng ka yix ce yix ang yaox kix,
ang tawng kaing pa lih tix mawh,
sang krox jun kix hoek dee in,
pauh ka yix,
yam tieb tie ka kaing pa yuh yix,
yix taug tawng kah.
12 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix pa ot daux pui koeng khankix,
tix bawg hoek tix bawg baox hoek tix ne raw ka yix,
pui koeng sang tieh ka rhawm keud yix.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
aux nhie pui hun rien kraung naing tix,
viex ka vaig,
pliah,
ak puh,
te,
kieh ceu cad cub pui mawh tix cub,
tawx kix pau ka guh veng plak khaix dee tiiem haktiex pa ang bax cox sum tix hoik heue.
14 Khaix hoik hu jhak aux tix yaox,
aux tom krai ka pui haoh pui hun mai simiang mai simiang naing kix ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat ka pui koeng!
Caw tix doh keud,
cao Siyiex mawh pa lhat graung yung yi pui kah.
Caw tix yuh naing son paox ceu paox cad pex,
moeng pex,
kawn simex kawn bun pex,
mai son nyiex ma pex.”
15 Pui koeng hoik mhong grawng pa hoik tawng yix krax rhawm keud pa ang mhawm kix,
kix tom hoik sidawng ka Siyiex hoik yuh laux ka krax pa keud sawn kix.
Mawh kheu nan,
yix pui hun deeh hoek ka kaing pa yuh tix sivoe an,
mai deeh cox sum ka guh veng heue.
16 Jauh tom yam an,
pui greem aux tix plak tom yuh ka kaing;
pui an kix tix plak cung tix pau,
viex ka vaig pliah,
dieb tie naok,
ak puh,
ceep ka kraung naing rhiem.
Simiang khankix sau riang tix teem coi
17 ka pui pa sum guh veng.
Pie pui pa gah kraung khrai khankix kawx,
tix khawn taix yuh ka kaing,
tix khawn taix viex ka kraung naing.
18 Pui pa yuh kaing ku kaux,
kix kawx hoik gieb vaig ka nyoi tix.
Pui pa phlu ka teet sang nied pui naing tix khrawm ka paox tix hoik ot ka praok aux.
19 Aux ah nin ka pui hun mai ka simiang naing mai ka simiang kix ah nin:
“Kaing in kied ting,
mai hoik prung prang tix tang ot tom vieh,
yix ku kaux ku pui kawx hoik khan tix kied singai ot khaing paox.
20 Yam mhong pex lox luah teet pui naing,
tag caw nied tix khrawm paox tix hoek ka aux dee in.
Siyiex yix tag sang teem coi ka yix puing naing tete.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
ku singaix jauh yam riang praix hoek yam bo praix simuing lih,
pui yix tix plak ot tix yuh kaing ka piang guh veng,
tix plak viex ka kraung naing pau diang siviex heue.
22 Ka daux bawg yam in,
aux krai ka can pui cao kuang ku cub,
mai ka pui pa teem coi kix,
bo praix tag caw tix paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
yuh nin,
yix tom tag pon yaox tix pau ka veng in yam pon sawm,
pon ngaix yuh ka kaing.
23 Pie yam bo praix aux ang peeik ka kraung ceep tix;
pui pa yuh paox tau mai aux,
siliex,
mai pui naing pa pau khankix,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa peeik ka kraung ceep tix.
Ex hoik jhawp kraung naing ka daux tawm taix tix khawm uik heue.
