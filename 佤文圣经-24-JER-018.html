Yie-li-mi ot nyiex ma cang mai yuh aw
18
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
2 “Maix hu nyiex ma cang mai yuh aw;
ka dee an aux koe lox krai ka maix.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
aux hu ka nyiex ma cang mai yuh aw.
Aux yaox nawh yia ka pan jaong ling,
mai yuh ka aw.
4 Phan tiex glaox pa miien nawh daux tawm taix tix ang mhawm,
nawh tag teei tiex glaox an yuh aw tang mu ka heue.
5 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex baox ah nin ka aux:
6 “Khex ang aux sang pon hawt tix yuh pui Yi-seu-liex awm grawng pa yuh cang mai ka aw yuh nan lie?
Pex ot ka daux tawm taix aux tag awm tiex glaox ka daux tawm taix cang mai yuh nan.
7 Yam mawx ang ah,
phan aux krai pao sang yuh tix krax pa laux leeing,
krax pa tuk tiak,
phan ang mawh nan yuh laux ka meung haktiex tix meung,
8 phan meung haktiex an deeh tik vud ka phi map,
aux tag sang chix deeh veeh rhawm rhi tix ka kix,
ang lai ploi krax laux krax leeing lih.
9 Yam mawx ang ah,
phan aux krai pao sang sum meung tix meung kaux cong,
10 phan meung an ang lai cu nghiet ka aux,
deeh tieh tix yuh kaing pa caox,
aux tag sang deeh loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai deeh kieh kaing pa sang yuh tix grai.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag sang krai ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung yuh nin:
aux hoik keud tix sang puing ka kix,
hoik rien khre tix sang ciyang ka kix.
Maix tag sang sidiex gau kix naox yuh phi map,
tieh loh ka can kaing pa yuh kix.
12 Kix tag sang pawk lox ah nin:
‘Ang!
Ex kawn sang tieh loh tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Ex tag caw tix hawt ka alo to kaux tix,
hawt yuh kaing pa caox pa caig,
awm rhawm rhi pa kiien ex yuh nan heue.’ ”
Pui Yi-seu-liex tik vud ka Simiang Siyiex
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
pex chawk tix yaox ka meung ka piang haktiex,
pui mawx pa hoik mhong kaing pa yuh nin lie?
Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig.
14 Rawm rha pa ron tix ot ka piang rang simaox Li-pa-noeng hoik ceh lie?
Rawm klawng si kied khaing ka piang gawng hoik kroh lie?
15 Mawh kheu nan,
pui cie aux hoik piawm ka aux;
kix gleeing tix tok nyawm ka riang rao.
Kix hoik goh ka daux krax pa caw kix tix sang lawng,
ang lai baox hu ka daux krax prim an;
kix hu ka krax iak pa ang koe mhai.
16 Kix kieh haktiex in tieh tix lih mawh dee lhat graung,
pui baig rawm mia tix ka leeing ju;
pui pa lawng krax hoik yaox kah,
mai yaoh yee vuk vied kaing tix kah.
17 Aux tag sang grheu pui cie tix ka plak sivoe ngai pui koeng,
awm phawng tiex pa phlu bhaung ka plak lih singaix yuh nan.
Yam lih hoek krax laux krax leeing,
aux tag sang veeh to kaux tix ang simi ka kix heue.
Keud tix yuh Yie-li-mi laux leeing
18 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui ah nin:
“Hoek heue!
Ex hoek tix tieb tie ka Yie-li-mi heue!
Ex hoik koe cao giex kaing li pa sidiex gau ka ex ku bawg ku yam,
hoik koe pui pa lhak pa duk thung ka ex,
koe cao jhai pa krai gum lox Siyiex ka ex.
Ex deuh phi map ka piang Yie-li-mi,
ang lai nghiet ka lox nawh heue.”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
aux rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux,
nghiet ka lox pa tieb tie pui koeng ka aux.
20 Ceu pa mhawm sang khaw tix pon ka pa caox pa caig lie?
Kix kaong daux o sang yuh aux laux leeing.
Khiox khaw maix keud tix yaox,
aux hoik yiiem tix khiox khaw son kix ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
aux hoik sax khiox khaw maix paw vi yuh kix ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
21 Viang mawh nan,
yam in lie?
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw maix yuh nin:
kieh kawn praix kix yum ka cai rhawm;
kieh kix yum ka naing;
kieh bun kix grai mex grai kawn;
kieh pui kix pa koe riang yum ka jhawm saux preng,
to kaux yum pui nhum ju naog ka bang naing.
22 Khiox khaw maix ploi pui tud pui rhim hoek dum mawx dum an,
con ka nyiex ma kix,
kieh kix yaoh yee yiiem ngia;
mawh kheu kaong kix daux o sang keud tix yuh aux laux leeing,
mai nih rub sang giex ka aux.
23 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawng ka grawng sang kied tix yuh aux laux leeing.
Paw vi pluat ploi ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix;
paw vi pluat ploi ka phi map kix.
Khiox khaw maix kieh kix goh ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
khiox khaw maix ciyang kix ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix heue.”
