Pa laux leeing simiang pa caox
11
Li-pa-noeng eue,
paoh siviex daux doh maix heue,
son sang kieh ngu gleeing ka khaox si oe bhawnga maix uik heue!
2 Khaox gix eue,
yiiem heue,
khaox si oe bhawng hoik goh;
khaox pa mhawm grang khankix pui hoik kid ka khawm uik heue!
Khaox yang yit Pa-sang eue,
laux rhawm heue,
nong khaox pa mhawm yeem khankix pui hoik yuh laux leeing heue!
3 Nghiet ka lox yiiem simiang heue,
mawh kheu hoik grai min plak ngai kix heue!
Nghiet ka lox graox sivai sang si heue,
mawh kheu hoik riem nong khaox pa koe ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue!”
Cao liang yung ra kaux
4 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux ah nin ka aux:
“Maix hu mawh cao liangb cub yung pa tag sang pauh pui ka khankix.
5 Cao yung an pauh ka yung mai ang pon krax pa ciyang.
Kix coh ka nex yung,
viang mawh nan,
ah nin:
‘Yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
ex hoik koe mau heue!’
Pie cao liang yung cie nawh kawx ang koe krax pa caox rhawm ka yung khankix heue.”
6 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux ang lai baox caox rhawm ka pui haktiex in;
aux sang ap kix ka daux tawm taix simiang.
Simiang in kix tag sang dug dig ka haktiex in;
aux ang taoh haktiex in luat luan khaing riang rao kix heue.”
7 Pui pa coh cai lai gee ka phung yung an kix vai aux;
aux lih tix mawh cao liang phung yung pa tag sang pauh pui khankix.
Aux teei ka khaox gix ra gawng,
tix gawng kok mawh “buan son”,
ang tix gawng kok mawh “kleuk tau”.
Yuh nin,
aux jauh tix liang ka phung yung.
8 Aux ang rhawm yaox ka pui liang yung an kix loe kaux pa koeng ka aux,
mawh kheu nan ka daux tix khix khaing aux pauh kix yum khawm uik.
9 Aux krai ka phung yung ah nin:
“Aux ang lai baox mawh cao liang pex.
Kieh pa khaw tix yum kieh yum heue,
pa khaw tix laux kieh laux heue;
pa deuk khankix tag sang ra lai pauh ka paox tix heue.”
10 Khaix an aux tom raig ka khaox gix pa kok mawh “buan son” an pot,
mai hoik tik ka sijax mukheu pa yuh Simiang Siyiex tau mai meung ka piang haktiex.
11 Ka daux singaix an,
sijax mukheu an tom ang lai koe ceu.
Cub pui pa coh cai lai gee ka phung yung khankix dong ngai tix ka aux,
kix tawng in hoik mawh gum lox Simiang Siyiex.
12 cdAux krai ka kix ah nin:
“Phan pex cu,
pex tawx mau ngawh bix ka aux,
phan ang cu,
pex deuh tix cai heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom tawx mau luah ka aux tix ngoe plak kieh mawh ngawh bix aux heue.
13 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Teei tix sau mau in kix laig daux nyiex krao mau nyiex buane.”
Mawh kheu nan,
aux teei mau luah in tix ngoe plak,
pa een kix mawh pa khaw aux tix pon,
laig daux nyiex krao mau nyiex buan.
14 Khaix an aux raig khaox gix pa kok mawh “kleuk tau” pot,
mai yuh laux ka krax pa rom pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex paox tix tau heue.
15 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Maix baox hu mawh cao liang yung tix bawg heue;
bawg in mawh cao liang yung pa caox.
16 Aux vai cao liang yung tix kaux,
mai nhie nawh kia ngien ka phung yung mhawm mhawm;
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang teem ka pa yaoh khankix,
ang sawg ka pa grai,
ang pai moi ka pa mat,
mai ang eei ka pa kawn klawd khankix;
deeh tieh tix,
nawh ih ka yung pa blawn kluing,
mai riah ka gip yung heue.
17 Pui liang yung pa ang mhawm an pa tik vud ka phung yung hoik laux leeing heue!
Naing tag sang yuh riang nawh laux lak pak leeing.
Plawng taix nawh tag sang sawt tix ngaing;
ngai khawn dawm nawh hoik faik khawm uik heue.”
