Cu nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex pon buan son
128
Pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
Pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh kied sidaing koe buan son heue!
 
2 Pa pon maix khaing daux pa taug pa mao tix hoik kub maix ih;
maix tag yaox tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mai mhawm kaing yuh ku ceu.
3 Moeng maix ot ka daux nyiex ma maix awm khaox plix siiet pa plix plix tom nie;
kawn simex kawn bun maix sivie ka pheen maix awm kawn khaox sime.
4 Pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag pon buan son yuh nin heue.
 
5 Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka maix khaing Shi-ang heue!
Kieh ka daux ju maix yaox grawng pa haok huan Yie-lu-sa-leung ka ngai tix;
6 kieh maix kawn im ju tix yaox ka kawn saux kawn sex tix heue.
 
Kieh meung Yi-seu-liex pon krax yien yawg nhim tiiem heue.
