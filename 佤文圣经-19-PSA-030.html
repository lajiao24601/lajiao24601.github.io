Lox rawg buan pa yaog yawa
30
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik taoh maix ka aux,
ang kieh pui koeng yaw rang di ka aux.
2 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
aux sang khiox khaw krax teem maix,
maix tom pai aux moi.
3 Maix taoh lawd aux lih khaing veei krax yum;
maix kieh aux ang yaox tix hoek meung yumb.
 
4 Kawn praix Simiang Siyiex pa singax singeh eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex;
pex tag caw tix yaog yaw ka kaing pa yuh cao pa kied singax singeh.
5 Krax pa song rhawm nawh hoik grai daux tix khiep ngai khaing;
buan son nawh ang chix naox tix leeing ju leeing phan.
Yam pon sawm lih rawm ngai bai rawm meeh,
yam riang praix pon ngawb cao,
tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
 
6 Aux pon krax pa nyawm rhawm mai ah nin:
Aux ang yaox tix hoek krax sum krax nai leeing ju leeing phan.
7 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik kia ngien aux ka daux buan son tix,
kieh aux pon krax pa kaux cong,
awm tab pa jung ka piang gawng yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
yam mox maix tix ang simi ka aux,
aux tom koe krax lhat krax graung heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw kok yo ka maix;
aux khiox khaw maix teem coi tix ah nin:
9 Phan aux yum sang koe dee mhawm yuh ka mawx?
Phan aux laig daux si meeig sang koe dee mhawm yuh ka mawx?
Pui yum sang chix pon rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix lie?
Kix sang chix pon krai pa kaux sijax maix lie?
10 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox aux,
caox rhawm ka aux liak!
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix teem coi ka aux liak!
11 Maix kieh krax pa laux rhawm aux tieh tix lih mawh krax pa graoh;
maix kieh aux peeik tix tik ka krax pa laux rhawm,
mai ceep ka krax pa gaux rhawm.
12 Mawh kheu nan aux yien yawg tix ang nye,
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix;
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
aux sang yaog yaw ka maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
