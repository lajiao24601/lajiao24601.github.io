Ot ka Keu-ling-to
18
Khaix hoik hoik kaing in,
Pao-law lih hu khaing Ya-tien,
mai hoek ka Keu-ling-to.
2 Nawh si pup pui Yo-thaix pa keh ka daux Peung-to tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Ya-cui-la,
nawh khraox hoek tau mai moeng tix Pai-ci-la khaing Yi-ta-lix,
mawh kheu hoik tawx Vawng Kheu-lao-tix lox nhie pui Yo-thaix khawm uik lih hu khaing Law-ma.
Pao-law hu jhak ka kix,
3 mai ot tix yuh kaing tau mai kix;
mawh kheu Pao-law dawg mawh pui pa eei to kaux tix ka yuh kaing pa sum nyiex gox man,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa awm kaing yuh kix.
4 Yam hoek ka daux ku singaix tuah,
Pao-law hoek ka nyiex sidiex,
thung thiang ka pui hun,
sidiex pui Yo-thaix mai pui Si-lax yum rhawm heue.
5 Yam kaoh Si-la mai Thi-maw-thaix hoek khaing Ma-chi-toeng,
Pao-law teei bawg yam pa koe tix khawm uik sidiex ka gum lox in,
mai kham sakse grawng mawh Ye-sux Khrit ka pui Yo-thaix.
6 Viang mawh nan,
yam map nai salao kix ka nawh,
nawh khruah phawng tiex cawd khaing kraung ceep tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Phan pex laux leeing,
phi map in ang hoek piang aux,
pex tang klawm ka heue!
Jauh yam in,
aux sang hu ka pui tang ceu tang cad dee an heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law lih hu khaing kix,
hu paung ot ka nyiex pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex tix kaux,
pa kaux Thi-to Yo-see-tu,
nyiex nawh dex ka dee ot nyiex sidiex.
8 Ci-li-see-pu cao kuang nyiex sidiex mai pui nyiex ma nawh hoik yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux;
kawn koe pui Keu-ling-to tom nie mhong ka gum lox in nang yum rhawm kah,
khaix an rieb tix cub com heue.
9 Koe ka daux tix bo,
Pao-law pon tix yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu.
Simiang Ye-sux ot ka daux riang su sai an krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix paw vi lhat,
tawng krai ka khaing,
paw vi yien yawg tix ang nye,
10 aux hoik ot tau mai maix,
ang koe pui pa pon yuh maix laux,
kheu nan ka daux veng in pui cie aux koe ka tom nie heue.”
11 Pao-law tom ot dee an tix num plak,
mai sidiex gau kix ka gum lox Siyiex.
12 Yam mawh Cia-liox cao kuang ka Ya-kai-yax,
pui Yo-thaix khrawm paox tix hoek tawk ka Pao-law,
mai reu nawh haok daux nyiex prawng see,
13 deuh phi map piang nawh ah nin:
“Pui in sidiex pui tang kaux siao ka krax pa ang khaw krub ka Siyiex heue!”
14 Pao-law jiet tag sang ang jheeh tix krai lox,
Cia-liox krai ka pui Yo-thaix ah nin:
“Phan in mawh kaing nieb pon ah mai kaing pa lud been,
aux kawx tag sang viex rhawm gun nghiet ka pex.
15 Phan pa hoik thung thiang pex paox mawh lox ngai lai ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng pex,
pex tang hu tix prawng ka heue.
Aux ang rhawm prawng ka kaing pa yuh nin heue!”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
grheu kix lih khaing daux nyiex prawng see.
17 Pui hun tom giex ka Saw-thi-ni cao kuang nyiex sidiex,
mai lauh nawh ka sivoe nyiex prawng see.
Kaing in Cia-liox kawx ang chawk cheng tix yaox heue.
Pao-law deeh ing ka Ang-thi-a
18 aPao-law baox ot mai kawn Khrit ka Keu-ling-to tom rheeng singaix,
khaix an lih hu khaing kix,
brug reu hu tau mai Pai-ci-la mai Ya-cui-la hoek ka Shui-li-yax.
Sivoe sang yaog nawh tix hu,
nawh kip hauk kaing tix ka Cin-keu-li,
mawh kheu hoik sax ap pong nawh tix deuh lox.
19 Kix hoek ka Yi-fu-saw,
ka dee an,
Pao-law guah tix tang hu khaing Pai-ci-la mai Ya-cui-la,
nawh hu ka nyiex sidiex mai thung thiang ka pui Yo-thaix.
20 Pui hun nhie nawh ot tom rheeng singaix,
viang mawh nan,
Pao-law ang cu,
21 Yam guah paox tix tang hu,
nawh ah nin ka kix:
“Phan hoik mawh alo Siyiex,
aux kawn sang deeh hoek ka pex dee in.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law baox jauh tix brug reu ka Yi-fu-saw heue.
22 Yam hoek Pao-law ka Khai-sha-li-yax,
nawh haok jhak ceu cao ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
khaix an hu ka Ang-thi-a,
23 mai ot dee an tom rheeng singaix,
khaix an tom taug hu.
Nawh hoik tauh hu ka haktiex Cia-la-thaix,
mai ka Fu-lui-cia,
mai hoik tawx krax yum rhawm pa kaux cong ka pui gau ku dauh heue.
Ya-paw-law ot ka Yi-fu-saw mai ka Keu-ling-to
24 Koe pui Yo-thaix tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-paw-law,
nawh hoek ka Yi-fu-saw.
Nawh keh ka haktiex Ya-li-sang-tax,
mawh pui pa kied chix krai lox tix kaux,
nawh hoik kied chix tawng ka phuk lai sigang khri.
25 Nawh ot ka piang daux krax pa hoik jhi Siyiex,
mai hoik gau tix tom sidaing,
kheu nan koe krax muh krax rhawm,
mai rien kah pui mhong dee bleeh grawng Ye-sux ka daux pa khaw caw.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh tawng ngie ka krax cub com Yaw-hang khaing.
26 Ya-paw-law jauh tix viex rhawm ting sidiex ka daux nyiex sidiex;
Pai-ci-la mai Ya-cui-la mhong kah,
tom kok nawh hoek ka nyiex tix,
mai nie krai daux krax Siyiex sidawng ka nawh.
27 Khaix an Ya-paw-law but rhawm tix hu ka Ya-kai-yax,
kawn Khrit pa ot ka daux Yi-fu-saw tom teem coi ka nawh,
tiiem lai tawx ka pui gau pa ot daux Ya-kai-yax,
nhie kix rieb tix kia ngien ka nawh.
Yam hoek nawh dee an,
kied koe krax pa teem coi ka pui pa hoik pon buan son yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux tete.
28 Nawh hoik pon goh yuh pui Yo-thaix ka daux pa thung thiang kix paox tix,
ka sivoe ngai pui hun tom nie bawg,
mai hoik teei phuk lai sigang khri kham sakse grawng hoik mawh Ye-sux Khrit heue.
