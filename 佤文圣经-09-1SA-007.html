7
aMawh kheu nan,
pui veng Ci-liex Yie-ling hoek rieb tix teei ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
klawm nawh hoek ka piang gawng iak tix mu,
mai deuh ot ka daux nyiex Ya-pi-na-ta.
Kix bre tix tawx kawn simex Ya-pi-na-ta,
pa kaux Yi-li-ya-sa mawh cao pa pau priex teug sijax mukheu heue.
Sa-mu-eu kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex
2 Teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex hoik ot ka veng Ci-liex Yie-ling tom rheung,
hoik mawh been tix nga num.
Ka daux glang bawg yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex rawg mhaing Simiang Siyiex teem coi tix heue.
3 Sa-mu-eu ah nin ka kawn pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Phan pex veeh rhawm tix hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
pex tag caw tix tik vud ka can siyiex pui tang ceu tang cad mai riang siyiex bun Ya-see-tha-lux khankix.
Pex tag caw tix tan lu to kaux tix khawm uik ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai yuh kaing jhaw nawh khaing.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom tag taoh pex luat luan khaing krax pa giex gang pui Fe-li-see.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex tik ka Pa-lix mai ka riang krub ka Ya-see-tha-lux,
mai yuh ngie ka kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex heue.
5 Sa-mu-eu khrawm ka can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoek ka Mi-see-pa,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Aux sang rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex son pex dee an.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom khrawm tix hoek ka Mi-see-pa.
Kix kin rawm mai hieh ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
son sang kieh mawh krax tan,
khaix an mai tuah priex preem leeing lang singaix.
Kix ah nin:
“Ex hoik phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex.”
(Mi-see-pa hoik mawh haktiex dee prawng Sa-mu-eu son simah pui Yi-seu-liex heue.)
7 Pui Fe-li-see mhong grawng pa khrawm pui Yi-seu-liex tix ot ka Mi-see-pa,
pa mawh simiang pui Fe-li-see an phuan kaux,
tom thung pui naing hoek puing ka kix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex mhong ka lox an mai kix kied lhat graung kah,
8 mai kix tom ah nin ka Sa-mu-eu:
“Nied tix rawg buan son yix ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
khiox khaw nawh taoh ex luat luan khaing pui Fe-li-see.”
9 Sa-mu-eu tom pauh kawn yung tix mu,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan khawm mu ka Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex teem coi ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox buan rawg nawh.
10 Yam tan lu Sa-mu-eu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
pui Fe-li-see hoek tix puing ka kix.
Ka daux bawg yam in,
Simiang Siyiex kieh sah kaoh khaing rao ma lih tuh ka kix,
mai kieh kix suk sag to tix deeh.
11 Pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing Mi-see-pa,
leue si gheet pui pa koeng khankix tom hoek dex ka Paw Cia,
mai hawt tix pauh kix yum lawng krax heue.
12 Khaix an,
Sa-mu-eu sum simaox tix lon si nax Mi-see-pa mai Sang,
mai ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ca teem coi ka ex ang jud ang jai”,
mawh kheu nan,
tom kok simaox an mawh “Yi-ping-yi-shie”,
dee bleeh mawh “Simaox pa teem coi.”
13 Yuh nin,
pui Fe-li-see ang pex.
Glang yam kawn koe ju Sa-mu-eu ka piang haktiex,
Simiang Siyiex ang pon kieh pui Fe-li-see nieb pon chuh ka haktiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex.
14 Jauh ka Yi-keu-loeng hoek ka Cia-theux,
can veng yaong pa teei pui Fe-li-see khankix,
hoik dee deeh ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik pon teei ka haktiex cie kix khawm uik.
Yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Cia-nang lai ling ka paox tix ka daux krax pa nhim heue.
15 Sa-mu-eu yuh simiang cao prawng tom si dauh ju tix,
mai kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex.
16 Nawh lai ling tix hu kia ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix,
haktiex Cie-cia,
mai haktiex Mi-see-pa ku num,
mai hoik prawng see son simah pui ka haktiex in kix.
17 Khaix hoik lai liang nawh tix kia,
nawh ing ka haktiex nyiex max dee ot tix La-max,
mai yuh cao prawng dee an.
Nawh yuh khu dee tan tix mu son Simiang Siyiex ka La-max heue.
