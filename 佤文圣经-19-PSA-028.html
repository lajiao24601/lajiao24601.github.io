Lox rawg buan krax teem krax coi
28
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw kok yo ka maix;
cao pa kia ngien aux eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox aux.
Phan maix ang simi ka aux,
aux tag sang si pup krax pa yaox pui laux pui leeing.
2 Aux khiox khaw maix teem coi tix,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka heue!
Aux sang yaog taix tix khiox khaw ka nyiex buan maix.
3 Paw vi prawng ca phi map aux tau mai pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig,
mai laux leeing tau mai pui pa caox pa caig.
Jheeh pui in kix krai lox pa mhawm,
ka daux rhawm rhi kix hoik naog krax pa koeng pa khoi heue.
 
4 aKhiox khaw maix ne raw ka kaing pa yuh kix,
pawk bix ka krax pa caox pa caig kix;
khiox khaw maix ciyang krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix,
pawk bix awm pa khaw kix tix pon yuh nan.
5 Mawh kheu kix nu nai ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
ang een pa phan pa yuh nawh koe ceu;
mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex sang ciyang ka kix,
yuh kix laux leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
6 Kieh Simiang Siyiex pon krax pa rhax lai yaog yaw,
mawh kheu hoik nghiet nawh lox pa khiox khaw aux nawh taoh tix.
7 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka aux,
teem coi ka aux;
aux yum rhawm ka nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
Nawh teem coi ka aux,
mai kieh aux gaux rhawm;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka pui cie tix;
nawh kia ngien teem coi ka simiang pa hoik laug tix.
9 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka pui cie tix,
tawx buan ka pui pa mawh cie to kaux maix.
Khiox khaw maix eei liang ka kix,
kia ngien kix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
