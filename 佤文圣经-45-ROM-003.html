3
Phan mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix sang koe dee lheu mhawm khaing pui tang ceu tang cad yuh ka mawx lie?
Krax pa Pat-hak sang koe ngawh jum yuh ka mawx lie?
2 Tete te lai,
hoik mawh son ku ceu.
Grawng tix,
Siyiex hoik ap tix tawx gum lox nawh ka pui Yo-thaix.
3 Ka daux kix phan koe pui pa lieh lheu ka gum lox in,
Siyiex tag ang kaux sijax kheu in lie?
4 aAng doh mawh nan heue!
Viang pui ku kaux kawx ang lai mawh tid tix,
Siyiex kawn ca mawh pa mawh pa meh;
awm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Yam krai maix lox ang koe krax pa tieb tie;
yam si pup maix krax pa ne raw,
tag sang pon krax pa pex heue.
5 Phan krax pa ang rauh see ex pon jhi tix ka daux pa rauh see Siyiex,
ex tag sang chix krai ah ka mawx lie?
Siyiex ciyang ka ex,
mawh kheu pa ang nawh rauh see lie?
(Aux hawt tix krai awm rhawm keud kawn pui.)
6 Ang mawh heue!
Phan Siyiex mawh pa ang rauh see,
nawh sang chix pon prawng ca haktiex yuh ka mawx lie?
7 Phan pa giex going aux sang pon kieh pa mawh pa meh Siyiex nie baox jhi tix lih,
nie pon ka min plak ngai,
aux sang krai yuh ka mawx lie?
Yuh ka mawx kawn sang prawng aux mawh pui phi map lie?
8 Yuh ka mawx ang krai ah nin:
“Kieh ex yuh kaing pa caox lih mawh pa mhawm lie?”
Koe pui tix blah hoik deuh phi map aux ka lox pa yuh nin;
prawng ka phi map kix hoik mawh pa khaw heue.
Ang koe pui rauh pui see
9 Phan mawh nan,
ex pui Yo-thaix kawn sang lheu kaux congb khaing pui tang ceu tang cad lie?
Ang doh mawh nan heue!
Aux hoik krai yuh nin,
pui Yo-thaix mai pu tang ceu tang cad hoik ot greem siawp sibrawm phi map awm paox tix yuh nan.
10 cAwm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
pui rauh see ang koe,
pie tix kaux kawx ang koe heue.
11 Pui pa lhak lhiao ang koe,
pui pa sawg ka Siyiex kawx ang koe heue.
12 Kawn pui ku kaux hoik tieh vheen ka Siyiex,
mai hoik rom tix laig tau ka daux krax pa ang khaw caw;
pui pa yuh kaing mhawm ang koe,
pie tix kaux kawx ang koe heue.
13 d Ghok kix hoik awm si meeig pa bang tix yuh nan;
dak kix hoik krai lox pie lox lhien khawm uik;
jai si uing khankix hoik lih khaing siyieb jheeh kix;
14 eka daux jheeh kix hoik naog ka jai pa caox mai pa jhax lawg.
15 fCaong kix kied brax to,
hoik hu pauh pui ku dauh ku dee,
16 can dee hoek kix hoik mawh pa deuh ka rhoi khaix laux leeing mai krax kha rhawm.
17 Kix ang tawng krax pa mhawm koe;
18 gkix kawx ang tawng tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex heue.
19 Ex hoik tawng grawng gum lox tawm jhawm prawng,
mawh pa krai ka pui pa ot ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng,
mawh son sang kieh kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik kreet tix ot greem krax prawng ca Siyiex,
mai ang lai kieh kawn pui koe lox duh tang ceu.
20 hMawh kheu ang koe pui pa krai pao rauh see tix ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
ka daux pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
Dee koe ceu lox tat jhawm prawng mawh ngie son sang kieh kawn pui tawng yung ka phi map tix khaing heue.
Siyiex kieh kawn pui si doe tix tau mai nawh ka daux pa khaw caw
21 Viang mawh nan,
yam in Siyiex hoik jhi grawng sang kieh kawn pui si doe tix tau mai nawh ka daux pa khaw caw yuh ka mawx;
in ang mawh pa koe grawh grawng mai lox tat jhawm prawng.
Lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si mai cao jhai khankix hoik kham sakse yuh nin khawm uik.
22 iSiyiex kieh kix si doe tix tau mai nawh ka daux pa khaw caw,
hoik mawh kheu pa yum rhawm kix ka Ye-sux Khrit.
Siyiex hoik kia nyiex can pa yum rhawm ka Khrit khawm uik,
ang lai tang siyox tix ceu kawx:
23 mawh kheu hoik yuh kawn pui phi map ku kaux,
hoik kieh min plak ngai Siyiex laux.
24 Khaix an,
Siyiex tawx buan son giex giex going going,
taoh kix ka daux Ye-sux Khrit,
mai kieh kix si doe tix tau mai nawh ka daux pa khaw caw.
25 Siyiex kieh Khrit yum,
kieh nawh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap,
kieh kawn pui pon krax pa pluat ploi kheu krax yum rhawm ka daux pa yum nawh.
Siyiex yuh nin hoik mawh son sang jhi pa rauh see to kaux tix.
26 Mawh kheu krax pa gun yeung nawh mai krax pa va rhawm nawh hoik pluat ploi ka phi map kawn pui sivoe an.
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux bawg yam an,
nawh kieh krax pa teei nawh tix tik ka phi map jhi pa rauh see to kaux tix.
Yuh nin,
Siyiex hoik jhi krax pa rauh see tix,
hoik kieh nang can pui pa yum rhawm ka Ye-sux khawm uik si doe tix tau mai nawh ka daux pa khaw caw heue.
27 Phan mawh nan,
ex sang chix ting mu lox yuh ka mawx lie?
Pie ceeix kawx ang chix heue!
Grawh grawng ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh kheu pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng lie?
Ang mawh heue!
Hoik mawh kheu yum rhawm.
28 Lox nghoik ex tix krai mawh nin:
grawng pa pon kawn pui tix si doe tix tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
hoik mawh kheu krax pa yum rhawm,
ang mawh pa priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
29 Khex mawh ngie Siyiex Siyiex pui Yo-thaix khaing lie?
Nawh kawx ang hoik mawh Siyiex pui tang ceu tang cad lie?
Hoik mawh heue!
30 jSiyiex koe ngie tix kaux khaing,
nawh nhie pui Yo-thaix yum rhawm,
pui tang ceu tang cad kawx hoik si doe tix tau mai nawh ka daux pa khaw caw kheu krax yum rhawm.
31 Phan mawh nan,
krax yum rhawm ex sang kieh lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si ang lai koe ceu lie?
Ang doh mawh heue!
Deeh tieh tix,
ex hoik kieh lox tat jhawm prawng nie baox kaux cong heue.
