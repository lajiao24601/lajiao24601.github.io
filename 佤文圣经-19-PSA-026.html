Lox rawg buan pui rauh pui see
26
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix prawng ca aux ang koe phi map,
mawh kheu yuh aux pa rauh pa see,
mai yum rhawm ka maix khawm uik.
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix lam lia tix yaox ka aux,
cheuk tix yaox ka aux;
khiox khaw maix lam lia tix yaox ka rhawm rhi aux mai krax keud krax sawn aux.
3 Krax muh maix pa ang chix tieh loh tix duk thung ka aux;
pa kaux sijax maix hoik thung ka aux tom si dauh sidua.
4 Aux ang lai ling tau mai pui pa pie lhien,
mai ang yuh paox yuh pie tau mai pui pa pie lhien.
5 Aux seeing brai ka pui pa rom tix mai pui phi map,
singai hu khaing pui pa caox pa caig nang heue.
 
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khoek taix tix hoik mawh jhi aux grawng ang koe dee lud,
mai baox via tix hu krub maix ka vung viang khu dee tan.
7 Aux rhax lai pa yaog yaw,
krai lao ka grawng kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh maix khawm uik heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux muh ka nyiex buan dee ot maix;
aux muh ka min plak ngaix dee laux lee maix tix.
9 Khiox khaw maix paw vi rom tix yuh aux laux tau mai pui phi map;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux yum tau mai pui pa caox pa caig.
10 Kix yuh pa caox pa caig ku bawg ku yam,
rieb ka mau chun praok ku dauh ku dee heue.
 
11 Phan mawh aux,
aux yuh kaing pa rauh pa see;
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka aux,
taoh ka aux liak!
 
12 Aux luat luan khaing krax nu krax kha khawm uik;
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex ka daux pa khrawm pui hun tix heue.
