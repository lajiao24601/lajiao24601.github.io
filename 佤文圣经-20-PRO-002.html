Plix ping nyia cuyi
2
Pui pa nhum ju eue,
tag caw gau tix ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux,
ang pon tix piawm ka krax pa thung gau aux ka maix.
2 Tag caw tix nghiet ka gum lox pa lhak lhiao,
mai tawng ka dee bleeh heue.
3 Mawh tete,
tag caw tix sawg ka ngai lig ngai lai;
tag caw sawg tix pon tix tawng sidawng ka kaing pa mhawm.
4 Tag caw tix sawg awm pa viex krax gaux rhawm tix sawg ka mau khri yuh nan,
awm pa bre rhawm tix sawg ka siang simaox yuh nan.
5 Yuh nin,
maix tom taug chix pon tix tawng sidawng grawng sang lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx,
mai tawng sidawng grawng sang tawng ka Siyiex yuh ka mawx.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
cao pa tawx ping nyia cuyi mawh Simiang Siyiex,
ngai lig ngai lai mai pa pon chix kieh rieb tix tawng hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing nawh khawm uik.
7 Nawh teem coi ka pui pa rauh pa see,
mai kia ngien ka pui pa mawh pa meh.
8 Nawh teem coi ka pui pa chix prawng rauh see ka pui,
mai chix priex chix kia ka pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh khankix heue.
9 Phan maix nghiet ka lox aux,
tom taug tawng ceu tix mawh pa mawh pa meh,
pa rauh pa see,
pa khaw caw,
mai tawng ceu pa tix pa khaw pex tix yuh.
10 Maix tag sang pon tix lih mawh pui pa lhak lhiao,
mai ngai lig ngai lai tag sang kieh maix gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
11 Ceu pa chix maix tag sang chix priex chix kia ka maix,
ceu pa pon rieb maix tix tawng tag sang kia ngien ka maix.
12 In ang kieh maix yaox tix yuh kaing lud kaing loe,
tag sang kia ngien ka maix,
kieh maix singai hu khaing pui pa teei dak tix ah lox caox khankix.
13 Singai hu khaing pui pa tik vud ka krax pa rauh see,
pa ot ka daux pa faik,
14 muh tix yuh kaing pa caox pa caig,
kieh pa caox caig mawh krax gaux rhawm,
15 pa hu ka daux krax pa vawk pa kawk,
pa ang pon tix yum rhawm ka heue.
16 Ping nyia cuyi pon kieh maix tieb tie ka bun pa keud tix sang teei lox mhawm jhawm jiet nyaw glaw maix kah.
17 Bun in mawh bun pa seeing brai ka mex tix,
mai hoik piawm ka gum lox sijax mukheu pa tiex tu tix pa singax singeh.
18 Phan maix hoek daux nyiex ma nawh,
in hoik mawh hu maix daux krax yum tete;
krax pa lawng nawh mawh pa thung pui hu meung yum.
19 Can pui pa hu vhi ka nawh ang lai pon deeh hoek tix kaux kawx,
ang lai koe pui pa deeh hoek krax pa im ju heue.
20 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix mawh pui mhawm sivoe ngai pui,
mai yuh kaing pa rauh pa see ka daux krax ot krax koe.
21 Pui pa rauh pa see mai pui pa rauh rhawm mhawm vaig khankix tag pon tix ot ka daux haktiex in;
22 viang mawh nan,
Siyiex tag sang teei tix tik ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig khankix,
mai yuh kix laux leeing awm reeig tix tik rib riem yuh nan heue.
