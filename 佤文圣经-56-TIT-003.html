Kaing pa yuh kawn Khrit
3
Maix tag caw tix krai ka pui ku kaux,
nhie kix nghiet ka simiang pa giex kaing mai pa koe sibrawm khankix,
nghiet ka lox tawx,
yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm ku bawg yam.
2 Sidiex gau kix paw vi map nai ka pui tang kaux,
paw vi yuh krax pa lawh maw;
tag caw tix yuh paox grawm ka daux pa awn rhawm,
viex rhawm pa awn pa tiiem ka can pui pa koe khawm uik.
3 Ex kawx hoik mawh nang pui pa daox aw,
pa lieh lox,
mai pa lud krax sivoe an;
ex hoik mawh siliex cawng ka alo nex nham ku ceu mai krax gaux rhawm,
mai ot ka daux pa caox mai pa boh rhawm,
pa ra lai koeng khoi ka paox tix.
4-5 Viang mawh nan,
cao taoh ex Siyiex hoik jhi krax pa caox rhawm tix mai krax muh tix lih,
mai hoik kieh ex deeh baox keh ka daux khuan singax singeh mai pa cub cum lih mawh pa khraox,
mai hoik taoh ka ex.
6 Ka daux cao taoh ex Ye-sux Khrit,
Siyiex hoik prauk Khuan Singax Singeh pa kub ka piang to kaux ex;
7 mawh kheu buan son nawh,
ex tom pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw,
mai pon ka ju leeing ju pa hoik keud pot jun pot ex an.
8 Gum lox in mawh pa kaux sijax heue.
Aux simie maix nie blawn krai ceu in kix,
son sang kieh pui pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex khankix gaux rhawm tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm;
kaing in kix mawh pa mhawm khawm uik,
mawh pa koe buan son ka pui.
9 Tag caw kieh tix hu singai khaing lox pa thung thiang pa daox aw,
mai pa simah ka grawh grawng pa tiiem ka ceu kaux ceu cad khawm lox tat jhawm prawng;
in kix mawh pa ang koe buan son,
pa ang koe ngawh jum.
10 Ka son pui pa yuh krax pa lawh maw lih khankix,
ah yawm tix ne raw ra bawg,
khaix an taug ang caw tix lai baox lai ling ka nawh.
11 Maix hoik tawng pui pa yuh nin,
mawh pa kieh pui laux leeing;
phi map pa yuh nawh hoik kham grawng pa lud nawh heue.
Lox pa nghoik tix krai
12 aYam ploi aux Ya-thi-ma mai Thoe-ci-ku hoek ka maix dee an,
maix tag caw nied tix hoek ka aux ka Ni-keu-phaw-li,
mawh kheu hoik but aux tix ot dee an bawg yam kuat praix.
13 bTag caw tix nied keud sawn grawng sang kieh Si-nax sara lox tat jhawm prawng mai Ya-paw-law lih hu khaing nyiex,
teem coi kiex son ang sang kieh kiex cud cha ka ceu tid tix.
14 Kieh pui cie ex gau tix sau riang yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
mai tawx pa sang lo ka daux krax ot krax koe;
paw vi kieh tix mawh pui pa ot giex pa ang koe ceu heue.
15 Pui pa ot tau mai aux khankix hoik chawk maix mhawm ot khawm uik.
Chawk ka paox grawm pa yum rhawm tau mhawm ot.
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka pex ku kaux heue!
