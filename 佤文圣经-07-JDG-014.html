Chang-suing mai bun cie Thing-na
14
Koe tix singaix,
Chang-suing hoek ka Thing-na;
mai yaox kawn bun Fe-li-see dee an tix kaux.
2 Nawh ing ka nyiex mai krai miex keeing tix mhong ah nin:
“Aux yaox bun Fe-li-see tix kaux ka Thing-na,
teei nawh hoek kieh mawh moeng aux heue.”
3 Miex keeing nawh chawk ka nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai sang teei tix moeng bun tang ceu pa mawh pui Fe-li-see dee an lie?
Khex ang bun koe ka daux ceu pui cie ex mai ka daux paox ceu paox cad ex lie?”
Viang mawh nan,
Chang-suing ah nin ka keeing tix:
“Aux kied muh nawh;
teei aux moeng nawh heue.”
4 Miex keeing nawh ang tawng hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kieh Chang-suing yuh nan,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tag sang yuh krax pa tieh pa ang khaw ka pui Fe-li-see khankix.
Yam an,
pui Fe-li-see hoik kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
5 Khaix an,
Chang-suing hu tau mai miex keeing tix hoek ka Thing-na.
Yam laig kix ka daux si peem plix siiet dee an,
nawh mhong ka lox pa hoek kawn sivai sang si tix graox ka nawh.
6 Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang nawh dum mawx dum an,
nawh riah sivai sang si an ka taix yi tix khaing,
awm riah ka kawn pex yuh nan.
Nawh ang krai can kaing pa yuh tix ka miex keeing tix heue.
7 Chang-suing hu ka kawn bun an,
si kah tau mai nawh;
Chang-suing kied muh ka nawh.
8 Ang koe miex glang tix,
Chang-suing hoek teei tix moeng nawh.
Ka daux krax,
nawh veeh tix hu kia ka sivai sang si pa hoik pauh tix yum,
ang doh tawng tix keud,
nawh yaox hia pa yuh rawm tix ka daux to kaux pa yum sivai an.
9 Nawh caok rawm hia an ceeix ot ka daux tawm taix tix,
mai ih ka khawm hu tix.
Khaix an nawh hoek ka dee ot miex keeing tix tan,
tawx kix ih ka rawm hia,
mai kix kawx ih kah;
viang mawh nan,
Chang-suing ang krai rawm hia mawh pa teei hoek khaing ka daux to kaux pa yum sivai sang si heue.
10 Keeing nawh hoek ka nyiex kawn bun an;
Chang-suing hawt tix yuh awm been singaix cie pui pa nhum ju khankix,
mai yuh poi ka paox dee an.
11 Pui Fe-li-see yaox ka Chang-suing,
kix tom ploi pui tix ngoe kaux hoek gaux rhawm tau mai nawh.
12 Chang-suing ah nin ka kix:
“Aux si nieb pex ka lox tix gum,
phan pex pon krai dee bleeh lih ka daux poi in aliah singaix,
aux tag tawx pex cie sibex man keei sum tix kaux tix jung;
13 Phan pex ang pon krai lih,
pex tag caw tix tawx aux cie sibex man keei sum tix kaux tix jung heue.”
Kix ah nin:
“Kraix yix mhong lox si nieb in heue!”
14 Chang-suing ah nin:
Priex pa ih kaoh khaing cao priex pa ih;
rawm hia kaoh khaing cao pa ting riang.
Kix hoik keud ka loe singaix,
mai ang pon keud lih.
15 Ka daux pon singaixa,
kix ah nin ka moeng Chang-suing:
“Maix nyaw glaw ka mex maix krai dee bleeh lox si nieb lih,
phan ang mawh nan,
yix tag ploi ngu gleeing ka nyiex ma keeing maix khawm uik,
maix kawx tag sang gleeing kah.
Pax ra kaux kok yix hoek,
mawh son sang tud rhim ka yix lie?”
16 Khaix an,
moeng Chang-suing yuh tix yiiem ka plak sivoe ngai nawh ah nin:
“Maix koeng ka aux,
mai ang muh aux tete!
Maix krai lox si nieb ka paox ceu paox cad aux,
viang mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx lai ang krai aux mhong dee bleeh heue!”
Chang-suing ah nin:
“Pie miex keeing aux kawx,
aux ang krai ka kix,
aux sang chix krai ka maix yuh ka mawx lie?”
17 Glang daux poi ka paox an aliah singaix,
moeng nawh ca yuh tix yiiem son grawng kaing an ang jud.
Ka daux aliah singaix,
Chang-suing ang lai pon kham lox pa mhaing moeng tix,
nawh tom krai dee bleeh lox si nieb an ka moeng tix,
moeng nawh tom hu krai ka pui Fe-li-see.
18 Ka daux aliah singaix,
sivoe sang laig Chang-suing lawg it tixb,
pui veng an hu ka Chang-suing mai ah nin ka nawh:
Kawn koe ceu pa tix pa tie khaing rawm hia lie?
Kawn koe ceu pa tix pa ting riang khaing sivai sang si lie?
Chang-suing pawk ah nin:
Phan ang siao moi miex cie aux thai ma,
pex ang doh pon krai dee bleeh lox si nieb in lih heue!
19 Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang Chang-suing dum mawx dum an,
mai kieh nawh ting riang ting rao,
nawh tom pauh pui an kix tix ngoe kaux yum ka haktiex Ya-seu-ci-loeng,
peeik kraung kix khaing kaux kix,
mai teei kraung kix tawx ka pui an kix pa pon krai dee bleeh lox si nieb lih.
Khaix hoik kaing in,
nawh kied song rhawm,
tom ing ka nyiex keeing tix.
20 Khaix an moeng nawh mex ka paox grawm nawh,
pa yuh paox pie tau mai nawh yam yuh poi ka paox heue.
