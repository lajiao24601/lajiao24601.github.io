Krax muh Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex
11
aPhan mawh nan,
aux tag sang chawk ah nin:
Siyiex hoik tik vud ka pui cie tix lie?
Ang doh mawh nan heue!
To kaux aux kawx hoik mawh pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux,
mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-paw-la-hang,
ceu pui cie Piien-ya-ming.
2 Siyiex ang bax tik vud ka pui cie tix pa hoik laug nawh tom yam jauh dix dix.
Pex hoik tawng,
phuk lai sigang khri hoik tiiem ah nin:
yam krai Yi-li-yax tix deuh phi map piang pui Yi-seu-liex ka Siyiex,
3 bNawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
kix hoik pauh ka cao jhai maix,
mai hoik yieh laux ka khu dee tan maix.
Deuk ngie aux tix kaux khaing,
mai kix kawn keud tix sang pauh ka aux heue.”
4 cSiyiex pawk lox nawh yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux hoik deuh pui aliah rheng kaux son tix,
kix ang bax grux ngawng tix krub ka siyiex pa pie lhien Pa-lix.”
5 Grawh grawng yam in kawx hoik yuh nin:
pui pa hoik laug Siyiex khaing ka daux buan son tix,
deuk ngie pui pa tux khaing.
6 Pa laug Siyiex hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing buan son nawh,
ang mawh pa hawt ka kaing pa yuh kawn pui;
phan mawh pa hawt ka kaing pa yuh kawn pui,
buan son pa krai ex ang sawn tix mawh buan son heue.
7 Dee si dauh yuh ka mawx lie?
Dee si dauh pui Yi-seu-liex ang pon ka ceu pa sawg tix,
mai tieh tix mawh pui pa laug Siyiex pa tux khankix pa pon;
pui pa deuh khankix ang kieh yhaok tix mhong ka lox pa kok Siyiex.
8 dAwm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik kieh khuan rhawm rhi kix mu mi;
tom yam in,
ngai kix ang chix yaox,
yhaok kix ang chix mhong heue.”
9 eTa-wue kawx hoik krai ah nin:
kieh kix krix ka groi,
giex ka kix yam yuh poi ih priex preem;
kieh kix sideeing tix goh,
si pup krax ciyang heue!
10 Kieh ngai kix faik,
mai ang chix yaox;
kieh kix vawk nyoi uah gao,
ang jud ang jai heue!
11 Phan mawh nan,
aux tag sang chawk ah nin:
pui Yo-thaix sideeing cao tix goh,
tag sang laux leeing khawm uik lie?
Ang doh mawh nan heue!
Mawh kheu krax pa lud kix,
krax taoh tom lih hoek piang pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai kieh pui Yo-thaix boh rhawm lih krax pa song rhawm.
12 Krax pa lud pui Yo-thaix viex buan son pa ting lhaong hoek ka kawn pui piang haktiex;
krax pa cud cha kix ka daux khuan,
tieh tix kieh pui tang ceu tang cad kub.
Phan mawh nan,
phan pui Yo-thaix khawm uik,
khawm pui pa ot ka daux krax taoh Siyiex,
khex ang nie blawn viex buan son pa kub hoek ang mawh lie?
Siyiex taoh ka pui tang ceu tang cad
13 Yam in aux krai lox ka pex pui tang ceu tang cad ah nin:
kheu hoik mawh aux kawn cau ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
aux hoik kieh kaing yuh tix mawh pa ting ceu.
14 Phan ang mawh nan,
aux kaw chix cheeik tix kieh paox ceu paox cad tix boh rhawm,
mai taoh pui tix blah ka daux kheu jao in.
15 Phan tik vud ka kix,
mai tieh tix kieh pui piang haktiex lih tix mawh paox grawm mai Siyiex,
phan mawh nan,
yam hoik rieb ka kix,
pui pa hoik yum khankix ang lai chix deeh pon ka ju lie?
16 Phan hoik tan tix tawx glua khao mun pa liang tix yuh ka Siyiex tix lon,
yuh nan,
khawm glua kawx hoik mawh pa tan ka nawh.
Phan tan lu tix tawx riah khaox ka Siyiex,
yuh nan,
khawm kak kawx hoik mawh pa tan lu ka nawh.
17 Koe kak khaox sime tix blah hoik pot,
mai kak khaox sime tiag deeh kleuk tix kah.
Maix pa mawh pui tang ceu tang cad hoik awm khaox sime tiag yuh nan,
yam in hoik yaox tix ot ka daux riang rao pa ting lhaong krax im ju pui Yo-thaix.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
maix ang pon tix iak jhak ka pui pa awm kak khaox pa hoik pot khankix.
Maix mawh ngie kak yuh nan khaing,
sang pon krai ka lox ting mu yuh ka mawx lie?
Ang mawh maix pa joh jhan riah khaox,
hoik mawh riah khaox pa joh jhan maix heue!
19 Maix kawx tee rien tix ah nin:
“Kak hoik pot,
mawh son sang kieh aux kleuk tix ka heue.”
20 Gum lox in khaw.
Kix hoik pot,
mawh kheu ang kix yum rhawm;
maix pon tix cung kaux cong,
mawh kheu yum rhawm maix.
Viang mawh nan,
maix ang pon tix ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
tag caw tix koe rhawm pa rien khre tix.
21 Pui Yo-thaix hoik dawg mawh kak pa koe sivoe;
kheu nan Siyiex ang bax pluat ploi ka kix,
maix ce nawh sang pluat ploi ka tix lie?
22 Ex chix tix yaox khaing dee in,
Siyiex kied koe krax pa caox rhawm nang,
kied kin jhawm nang.
Phan mawh son pui pa hoik sideeing tix goh khankix,
nawh kied kin kah;
phan mawh son maix,
nawh mawh krax muh krax rhawm,
ca mawh ot maix ka daux krax muh nawh jheeng jheeng;
phan ang mawh nan,
maix kawx tag sang pot nang.
23 Phan mawh son pui Yo-thaix,
phan kix ang kiien rhawm ka daux krax yum rhawm tix,
kix kawx chix tix deeh pon tix awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan,
mawh kheu Siyiex pon kieh kix kleuk tix.
24 Maix pa mawh pui tang ceu tang cad hoik awm kak khaox sime tiag pa pot yuh nan,
mai hoik lawk kleuk tix ka kak khaox sime pa mhawm yuh nan.
Pui Yo-thaix hoik awm khaox pa van pui tix sum yuh nan;
Siyiex teei kak pa hoik pot deeh kleuk ka khaox pa hoik dawg koe an,
ang mawh pa nie blawn yie lie?
Krax muh Siyiex tauh hu ka kawn pui piang haktiex
25 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
koe krax pa mawh pa meh pa raux pa mox tix ceu,
aux simie pex tawng,
son ang pex sang keud tix lhak lhiao.
Krax pa mawh pa meh in mawh:
pui Yi-seu-liex tix blah hoik kiien rhawm,
mawh ngie son sang krox jun pui tang ceu tang cad khawm uik deeh hoek ka Siyiex.f
26 gYam hoek daux singaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik tag sang pon krax taoh.
Awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
cao taoh tag sang lih hoek khaing Shi-ang;
nawh tag sang teei tix tik ka phi map kawn praix Ya-keux.
27 hSiyiex ah nin:
in hoik mawh sijax mukheu pa yuh aux tau mai kix;
bawg yam an,
aux tag sang pluat ploi ka phi map kiex heue.
28 Pui Yo-thaix tieb tie ka gum lox buan,
mai lih mawh pui koeng Siyiex,
dee si dauh kieh pex pui tang ceu tang cad pon ceu pa mhawm.
Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu pa laug Siyiex,
mai baox hawt ka gum lox pa hoik deuh Siyiex ka simiang ceu pui pa laug khankix,
pui Yo-thaix ca kawn mawh pao grawm Siyiex.
29 Mawh kheu krax pa kok Siyiex mai buan son nawh ang chix tieh loh tix.
30 Sivoe an pex pui tang ceu tang cad ang cu nghiet ka Siyiex;
yam in kheu pa ang pui Yo-thaix cu nghiet,
pex hoik pon krax muh Siyiex.
31 Awm an,
mawh kheu hoik pon pex krax caox rhawm Siyiex,
yam in pui Yo-thaix ang cu nghiet,
mawh son sang kieh kix pon krax muh Siyiex ka daux bawg yam an.
32 Siyiex hoik kieh kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik,
laung tix ot ka daux krax pa ang cu ang nghiet,
son sang jhi krax caox rhawm tix ka kix heue.
Rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex
33 iBuan son Siyiex kied ting kied lhaong heue!
Ping nyia cuyi nawh,
mai krax tawng krax yung nawh kied raux kied mox tete heue!
Pui mawx sang pon kah ka ceu pa prawng nawh lie?
Pui mawx sang pon si kaw ka daux krax nawh lie?
34 jAwm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
pui mawx sang pon tawng ka rhawm rhi Simiang Siyiex lie?
Pui mawx sang pon keud sawn tang nawh lie?
35 kPui mawx hoik tawx ceu pa tix ka nawh,
kieh nawh ang plud ang chix lie?
36 lMawh kheu can ceu pa koe khawm uik,
hoik kaoh khaing nawh,
yuh ka daux nawh,
mawh cie nawh.
Kieh min plak ngai mawh cie Siyiex,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue!
A-meung.
