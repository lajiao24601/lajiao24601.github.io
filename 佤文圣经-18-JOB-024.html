24
Cao pa ting siawp sibrawm ka piang khawm uik yuh ka mawx lai ang tat ka singaix bawg yam prawng ca tix lie?
Yuh ka mawx lai ang kieh pui pa tawng ka nawh yaox lih hoek pa rauh pa see?
 
2 Koe pui gah ka mee haktiex,
mai tud tix pon ka haktiex tom nie;
kix tud ka phung yung cie pui tang kaux,
to kaux kix hu liang kah.
3 Kix tud ka la cie kawn doe,
teei moi cie miex mai kieh mawh pa tang kah.
4 Kix ang kieh pui hot pui sibhawm yaox tix ih ka daux siawp sibrawm pa khaw kix tix pon,
kix ghlat ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai kieh kix to mox tix heue.
 
5 Pui hot pui sibhawm awm la tiag yuh nan,
mai sawg tix ih priex preem ka daux tiag daux tu;
mai ang koe dee sang sawg kawn simex kawn bun tix ih priex.
6 Kix khrawm vawg ka daux kaung cie pui tang kaux khankix,a
pieh plix siiet ka daux si peem plix siiet cie pui pa caox pa caig.
7 Kix it ka kaong,
ang koe kraung ceep pa lhop ka to kaux kix;
pon bo yam kuat praix,
mai ang koe prux lhop pa kieh si u.
8 Kix caux ka lhiex ting ka piang gawng,
mai mox ngie tix ot ka rang simaox khaing heue.
 
9 Pui caox pui caig kieh kawn doe mawh siliex cawng,
mai teei kawn simex kawn bun pui hot pui sibhawm plud ceuk.
10 Yam lih hu pui hot pui sibhawm,
kraung ceep ang koe ka to kaux kix;
kix cai rhawm khawm teem tix pui klawm khix khaox.
11 Kix krid bee sime ka plak daux guh veng cie pui pa caox pa caig;
kix biiet plix siiet mawh plai,
to kaux kawx hoik kham gee ka kroh ghok tix.
12 Koe pui mat ngia ka daux veng,
koe pui yiiem ka sang yum tix,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex ang simi ka lox pa khiox khaw kix heue.
 
13 Koe pui tix blah tieb tie ka pa riang;
kheu ang kix tawng ka pa riang,
ang hawt khaix pa duk thung pa riang.
14 Sivoe sang riang praix,
pui pa sawg tix pauh ka pui tag yaog tix kaoh;
kix lih hu pauh ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
yam pon sawm kix mawh pui briex.
15 Simex pa phit vao kied simie praix faik;
nawh tawb pak ngai tix,
mai ang kieh pui yaox tix.
16 Yam pon sawm,
pui briex yieh siviex laig ka daux nyiex;
yam pon ngaix,
kix mox tix,
ang rhawm kieh tix yaox pa riang.
17 Kix lhat ka pa riang praix pon ngaix,
krax lhat graung yam pon sawm,
kix hoik tawng ka sidawng heue.
 
[Saw-fax ah lox tix:]b
18 Pui caox pui caig rawm ting hoik chun kix hu.
Haktiex pa koe kix Siyiex hoik jhax lawg kah;
kix ang lai yaox tix deeh baox hoek yuh kaing ka daux si peem plix siiet cie tix.
19 Awm hax koek kroh tiex kieh rha ceh,
krax yum hoik bleet ka pui phi map.
20 Pie miex kix kawx ang lai doh keud ka kix;
kix sang awm khaox rham pa hoik sawm greeing.
21 Mawh kheu hoik nu nai kix ka miex mai,
ang koe krax pa caox rhawm pie ceeix kawx ka bun miex pa ang chix keh kawn.
22 Siyiex teei siawp sibrawm tix yuh laux ka pui tud pa rhim;
yam yaog nawh tix hu,
pui pa caox pa caig tag yum.
23 Siyiex kieh kix yaox krax nyawm ot tix vud tix cu khaing,
mai ang koe krax khi rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
ngai nawh daong tix jhak ka can kaing pa yuh kix khawm uik.
24 Pui caox pui caig yaox tix haok huan tix vud tix cu,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe miex glang tix,
tag sang khrum vhit awm rib riem,
awm si gleem kiak nghox ca pa hoik vawg tix.
25 Pui mawx pa pon thiang ka kaing in ang mawh tete lie?
Pui mawx pa pon kham sakse lox pa krai aux ang koe grawng lie?
