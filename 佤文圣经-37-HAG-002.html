Nyiex buan pa mhawm greeh grang
2
Num an ka daux khix aliah tix nga tix ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex baox si kah ka cao jhai Ha-kai.
2 Nawh nhie Ha-kai deeh krai ka Saw-law-pa-paw simiang haktiex Yo-tax,
mai Yaw-su-yax cao giex kaing li pa ting,
khawm ka kawn pui khankix ah nin:
3 a“Ka daux pex kawn koe ang koe pui pa tawng ka nyiex buan pa kied mhawm grang sivoe an lie?
Yam in yuh ka mawx lie?
Phan jhak tix yaox awm pa ang lai keeing tix mawh mau pie tix piak lie?
4 Viang mawh nan,
Saw-law-pa-paw,
Yaw-su-yax,
mai pui haktiex in eue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pex baox sidiex ka pex,
pex tag caw kieh tix kiien,
tag caw sau riang tix yuh kaing,
mawh kheu aux pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik ot tau mai pex.
5 bYam lih pex khaing Ai-cix,
aux hoik sax yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pex,
mai hoik cu tix sang ot tau maix pex.
Yam in aux ca kawn ot tau mai pex,
mawh kheu nan paw vi lhat heue.
6 c“Ang koe miex glang tix,
aux tag sang yung yang ka rao ma haktiex,
tag sang yung yang ka rawm sigrit sigra mai ka piang haktiex.
7 Aux tag sang puk pliak ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai teei ka sin ngao kix hoek dee in,
kieh nyiex buan in mhawm greeh grang.
8 Can mau khri pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik hoik mawh cie aux.
9 Nyiex buan pa khraox sum in tag sang blawn lheu mhawm greeh grang khaing pa prim;
aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix pon krax pa haok huan nyawm ot nyawm koe ka dee an.”
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Cao jhai mhaing krax pa sidiex gau ka cao giex kaing li
10 Yam mawh Ta-lio-see Vawng ka daux num ra khix dim tix nga pon ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing baox si kah ka cao jhai Ha-kai.
11 Nawh ah nin:
“Maix hu kok cao giex kaing li hoek ka dee bleeh kaing in tix ceu:
12 phan koe pui pa teei nex khaing daux Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa hoik guah mawh pa singax tix khik,
mai teei kraung dha tix kie kah.
Khaix an kraung in theeh ka khao mun,
ka rawm taux,
ka plai,
ka bee sime,
phan ang mawh nan ka priex preem tang ceu,
priex preem in kix sawn ang sawn mawh kraung ceu pa singax lie?”
Cao giex kaing li pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang sawn heue.”
13 dHa-kai baox chawk ah nin:
“Phan koe pa hoik seeing brai kheu theeh ka to kaux yum,
khaix an nawh baox giex ka priex preem in kix,
priex preem in kix kawx hoik singax singeh ang singax singeh lie?”
Cao giex kaing li ah nin:
“Chix tix.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Ha-kai ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai ah nin,
grawng kaing pa yuh nin phan siao ka daux pui meung in,
mai ka daux to ma pa yuh kix ku ceu,
pa mawh ceu pa tan lu ku ceu ka piang khu dee tan hoik mawh pa ang singax singeh khawm uik heue.”
Simiang Siyiex cu tix tawx buan son
15 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Khex ang pex bax yaox ka kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh lie?
Sivoe sang jauh tix sum ka nyiex buan,
16 koe pui hoek dex ka pum nghox,
mai simie tix pon ra yieh kongcing,
viang mawh nan,
nawh pon ngie tix yieh kongcing khaing;
koe pui simie tix pon plai khaing daux pung plai tix yieh kongsheng,
viang mawh nan,
nawh pon ngie tipon kongsheng khaing.
17 Aux hoik kieh bhaung si aoh mai mu pre yuh laux ka ceu pa sum pex,
mai pex ca kawn ang bax loh ka rhawm tix.
18 In singaix mawh khix dim tix nga ngaix pon,
mawh singaix pa sum ka riah nyiex buan.
Pex tag caw bre rhawm rhi tix ka kaing pa tag sang lih singaix pa sang hoek.
19 Viang nghox koi ang koe ka daux nyiex krao,
khaox plix siiet,
khaox ruh,
khaox mak cang,
khaox sime ang bax teem khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
jauh yam in,
aux tag sang tawx buan son ka pex heue.”
Gum lox pa hoik deuh Simiang Siyiex ka Saw-law-pa-paw
20 Ka daux tix ngaix khaing,
pa mawh khix dim tix nga pon ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah mai Ha-kai ka daux bawg ra,
21 nhie nawh deeh krai ka Saw-law-pa-paw simiang haktiex Yo-tax,
ah nin:
“Aux tag sang yung yang ka rao ma haktiex,
22 mai puk piak ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik,
ang lai tawx siawp sibrawm ka kix.
Aux tag sang cot goh ka ling law kix,
mai ka pui naing brug brung kix;
brung hoik yum khawm uik,
mai cao pa brug kawx hoik ra lai pauh paox tix.
23 Siliex aux Saw-law-pa-paw eue,
ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang laug ka maix.
Aux tag sang tawx maix giex kaing ka daux ceu kaux tix.
Maix mawh pa hoik laug aux.”
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
