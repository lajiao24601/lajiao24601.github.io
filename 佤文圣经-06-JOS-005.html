5
Yam mhong simiang Ya-maw-lix pa ot plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang mai simiang haktiex vung viang Cia-nang pa ot hawt Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong,
kieh Simiang Siyiex rawm klawng Yaw-tang hit,
kieh pui Yi-seu-liex tiang kah,
tom lhat graung kah,
awm ang khuan lai ot mai to kaux.
Pat-hak ka haktiex Cie-cia
2 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax:
“Maix yuh ngu simaox lih mawh vaig,
baox yuh krax Pat-hak ka pui Yi-seu-liex.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax tom yuh ka simaox vaig,
mai yuh krax Pat-hak ka pui Yi-seu-liex ka gawng Pat-hak.
4-6 aYam lih hu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
can simex kawm uik hoik Pat-hak tix.
Viang mawh nan,
can kawn simex pa keh yam vung viang kix ka daux tiag tipon num,
ang bax Pat-hak tix.
Khaix an tipon num,
can pui naing yam lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix hoik yum khawm uik,
mawh kheu tieh vheen kix ka Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang Siyiex hoik tiex tu,
ang kieh kix yaox haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia pa hoik ap nawh tix tawx ka tax du kix.
7 Kawn praix pui in kix ang sax Pat-hak tix;
Yaw-su-yax tom teem pui pa khraox in kix Pat-hak tix heue.
8 Khaix hoik Pat-hak tix,
kawn pui kawn ot ka daux bang,
mai krox khaix Pak-hak moi.
9 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“In singaix,
aux hoik teei tix tik krax kaig pa yuh pex siliex cawng khaing Ai-cix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
tom in singaix ceu kaux dee an kawn kok mawh Cie-ciab.
10 cPui Yi-seu-liex sum bang ot ka Cie-cia.
Daux khix an kao pon ngaix pon bo,
kix yuh Poi Luat-luan ka daux lung Yie-li-keu.
11 Ngaix khaix,
mai kix jauh tix ih to ma to koi pa pon tix khaing haktiex Cia-nang:
koe si gleem plix nghox ca pa tong ka ngu mai khao mun pa ang sau su saix.
12 dSingaix an,
Ma-na jauh tix ang lai lih,
mai pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai yaox tix ih Ma-na.
Jauh yam an,
kix tom ih ka to ma to koi pa pon tix khaing haktiex Cia-nang heue.
Yaw-su-yax mai simiang kru naing Simiang Siyiex
13 Yam dex hoek Yaw-su-yax ka veng Yie-li-keu,
dum mawx dum an,
nawh yaox ka pui tix kaux cung ka sivoe ngai nawh,
daux taix nawh giex ka vaig pa lawd tix khaing phak.
Yaw-su-yax haok chawk nawh:
“Maix mawh pa hoek tix teem yix,
mai mawh pa hoek tix teem ka pui pa koeng ka yix lie?”
14 Pui an ah nin:
“Ang mawh tix ceu kawx.
Aux hoek son sang mawh cao kaing kru naing Simiang Siyiex.”
Yaw-su-yax gup tix piang tiex mai krub ka nawh,
ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
aux mawh siliex maix.
Sang tawx maix lox ceu pa tix?”
15 Cao kaing kru naing Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Peeik kiiep maix!
Mawh kheu dee cung maix mawh dee pa singax singeh.”
Yaw-su-yax tom hawt tix yuh awm lox nawh heue.
