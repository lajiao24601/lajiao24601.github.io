Pui Yi-seu-liex tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang
3
Ngaix khaix pon ngawb,
Yaw-su-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik yaog tix kaoh pon ngawb cao cao,
lih khaing See-thing,
mai hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
yuh nyiex bang dee an,
krox tix tiag ka rawm klawng.
2 Khaix loe singaix,
simiang pui hu ka daux bang ku dauh
3 krai ka pui ah nin:
“Yam yaox pex klawm cao giex kaing li pui Li-we teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex hu,
pex tag gah bang tix hawt ka kix heue.
4 Pex ang sax hoek dee an,
kheu nan,
kix tom thung krax hu sivoe.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang pon tix dex ka teug sijax mukheu,
caw tix hawt khaix teug sijax mukheu,
kieh si nax tix koe been tix kongli heue.”
5 Yaw-su-yax krai ah nin ka pui khankix:
“Yuh to kaux pex singax singeh,
mawh kheu pa sax Simiang Siyiex tag yuh pa daox pa am ka daux si nax pex.”
6 Khaix an,
nawh kieh cao giex kaing li yaog tix klawm teug sijax mukheu,
mai hu sivoe pui khankix.
Kix hawt tix yuh uik heue.
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
“In singaix,
aux sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex khankix yaog maix ting min,
kieh maix mawh simiang pa ting.
Kix tag chix yaox ot tau aux mai maix,
awm ot tau aux mai Maw-si yuh nan sivoe.
8 Maix tag tawx lox ka cao giex kaing li pa klawm ka teug sijax mukheu khankix,
yam hoek pex ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
kix tag laig hu ka daux rawm,
cung ka praok rawm.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax ah nin ka pui khankix:
“Hoek heue!
Hoek tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
10-11 Yam preeng pex tix hu sivoe,
Siyiex tag grheu ceu pui Cia-nang,
ceu pui Heux,
ceu pui Si-we,
ceu pui Pi-li-si,
ceu pui Keu-cia-sha,
ceu pui Ya-maw-lix,
mai ceu pui Yie-pu-see khankix.
Simiang Siyiex mawh Siyiex cao haktiex khawm uik,
yam tiang teug sijax mukheu nawh ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
pex tag tawng Siyiex leeing ju ot tau mai pex.
12 Yam in,
pex sawg pui kao ra kaux ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
tix ceu pui tix kaux.
13 Yam yaog cao giex kaing li teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh cie cao haktiex rao ma tiang ka rawm,
rawm klawng Yaw-tang ang chix lih hu;
rawm pa kaoh khaing plak laong tag khrawm tix tau,
mawh guh rawm tix mu heue.”
14-15 Yam an mawh bawg yam vawg nghox koi,
rawm naog ka khawm ra plak.
Kheu nan,
pui khankix gah bang ot tix,
son sang tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang;
cao giex kaing li khankix klawm ka teug sijax mukheu hu sivoe kix.
Yam laig caong cao giex kaing li khankix daux rawm,
16 rawm tom kaog ang lai lih hu,
rawm plak laong mawh guh tix mu ka veng Ya-tang ka praok Sa-la-tang,
rawm pa lih laig ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog khankix,
ang lai chix lih hu,
mawh pa dut heue.
Kheu nan,
kawn pui hu tiang ka praok plak toi veng Yie-li-keu heue.
17 Yam hu pui hun ka klawng ang koe rawm,
cao giex kaing li khankix klawm ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
cung ka daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang dee kroh tiex,
krox pui khankix khawm uik hu heue.
