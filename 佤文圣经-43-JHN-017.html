Ye-sux rawg buan son pui gau tix
17
Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux ka lox in kix,
nawh maong ngai tix jhak ka rao ma ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
bawg yam hoik hoek,
khiox khaw maix yaog min plak ngai kawn simex maix,
son sang yaog kawn simex maix ka min plak ngai maix nang.
2 Maix hoik tawx akhuang pa kuang kawn pui haktiex ka nawh,
son sang kieh nawh tawx ju ka pui pa hoik ap maix tix tawx ka nawh.
3 Pa tawng ka mawh maix Siyiex pa mawh pa meh pa koe tix kaux khaing,
mai pa tawng Ye-sux Khrit kaw pa ploi maix hoek,
in tag mawh ju leeing ju.
4 Aux hoik yaog min plak ngai maix ka piang haktiex;
aux hoik hawt tix yuh si dauh ka gum lox pa hoik tawx maix ka aux.
5 Keeing eue,
yam in khiox khaw maix yaog min plak ngai aux ka sivoe maix,
tawx min plak ngai pa pon aux tau mai maix sivoe sang phan yuh ka haktiex heue!”
6 “Aux hoik jhi maix sidawng ka pui pa hoik laug maix khaing daux haktiex,
pa tawx maix ka aux khankix.
Kix hoik dawg mawh cie maix,
maix hoik tawx kix ka aux;
kix kawx hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox maix.
7 Yam in kix hoik tawng,
pui pa tawx maix ka aux hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing maix dee an khawm uik.
8 Aux hoik tawx gum lox pa hoik tawx maix ka aux ka kix,
kix kawx hoik rieb kah.
Kix hoik tawng aux mawh pa kaoh khaing maix tete,
hoik yum rhawm mawh maix pa ploi aux hoek heue.”
9 “Aux khiox khaw son kix;
aux ang khiox khaw son kawn pui haktiex,
mai hoik khiox khaw son pui pa hoik tawx maix ka aux khankix,
mawh kheu hoik mawh kix pui cie maix.
10 Can pa koe aux khawm uik hoik mawh cie maix;
can pa koe maix khawm uik kawx hoik mawh cie aux.
Min plak ngai aux hoik jhi tix lih ka daux kix.
11 Yam in aux hoek ka maix dee an,
mai ang lai ot ka piang haktiex,
viang mawh nan,
kix kawn ot ka piang haktiex.
Keeing eue!
Khiox khaw maix kia ngiien kix ka daux ceu kaux maix,
pa mawh ceu kaux pa hoik tawx maix ka aux,
a kieh kix kleuk tix lih mawh tix mu khaing,
awm hoik kleuk aux tix tau mai maix yuh nan.
12 cYam ot aux tau mai pex,
aux hoik kia ngiien kix ka daux ceu kaux maix,
pa mawh ceu kaux pa hoik tawx maix ka aux.b
Aux hoik kia ngiien ka kix,
lheu khaing pui an pa hoik tat pon krax pa laux leeing tix kaux khaing,
tang kaux ang lai kieh cawd tix kaux kawx;
in hoik kieh gum lox phuk lai sigang khri lih tix mawh.
13 “Yam in,
aux hoek ka maix dee an,
aux kawn sang krai ka lox in kix yam ot ka piang haktiex,
son sang kieh rhawm rhi kix grum naog ka krax gaux rhawm aux.
14 Aux hoik tawx gum lox maix ka kix;
kawn pui piang haktiex hoik koeng ka kix,
mawh kheu ang kix mawh pa dex dawt mai haktiex in,
awm ang aux mawh pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex in yuh nan.
15 Aux ang khiox khaw maix viex kix hu khaing haktiex,
viang mawh nan,
khiox khaw maix kieh kix bheeik luan khaing kaw pa caox pa caig an.
16 Awm ang aux mawh pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex,
kix kawx ang mawh pa dex dawt tau mai haktiex.
17 Khiox khaw maix kieh kix tan to kaux tix ka maix ka daux ceu pa mawh pa meh;
gum lox maix hoik mawh pa mawh pa meh.
18 Awm hoik ploi maix aux laig ka daux haktiex yuh nan,
aux kawx sang ploi kix laig ka daux haktiex yuh nan nang.
19 Mawh kheu jao kix,
aux hoik tan to kaux tix ka maix,
son sang kieh kix tan to kaux tix ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix nang heue.
20 “Aux ang joeng khiox khaw son kix,
mai ka khawm pui khankix pa hoik yum rhawm ka aux kheu pa rieb ka gum lox pa krai kix.
21 Kieh kix kleuk tix lih mawh tix mu khawm uik.
Keeing cao eue,
kieh kix ot ka daux ju ex;
awm hoik ot maix ka daux ju aux,
ot aux ka daux ju maix yuh nan.
Kieh kix kleuk tix lih mawh tix mu khawm uik,
son sang kieh kawn pui piang haktiex yum rhawm ka mawh maix pa ploi aux hoek.
22 Min plak ngai pa hoik tawx maix ka aux,
aux kawx hoik tawx ka kix nang,
son sang kieh kix kleuk tix lih mawh tix mu khawm uik,
awm hoik kleuk ex tix lih mawh tix mu yuh nan.
23 Aux hoik ot ka daux ju kix,
mai maix hoik ot ka daux ju aux,
son sang kieh kix kleuk tix lih mawh tix mu,
son sang kieh kawn pui haktiex tawng mawh maix pa ploi aux hoek,
mai tawng maix hoik muh ka kix,
awm hoik muh maix ka aux yuh nan heue.
24 “Keeing eue,
maix hoik tawx kix ka aux;
aux ot dee mawx,
kix kawx kieh ot tau maix aux nang,
son sang kieh kix yaox min plak ngai pa tawx maix ka aux;
sivoe sang phan yuh ka haktiex,
maix hoik muh ka aux.
25 Keeing pa rauh pa see eue,
kawn pui haktiex ang tawng ka maix,
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng ka maix.
Pui in kix hoik tawng mawh maix pa ploi aux hoek.
26 Aux hoik jhi maix sidawng ka kix;
aux tag sang nied tix baox yuh nin,
son sang kieh krax muh maix ka aux kuah ot ka daux ju kix,
mai aux kawx hoik ot ka daux ju kix heue.”
