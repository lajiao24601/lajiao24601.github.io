Lu-teux sut si gleem nghox ca ka daux kaung Paw-a-see
2
Na-eu-me koe paox nyiex dex tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh kaux Paw-a-see,
mawh pa kied koe mau koe min,
mawh paox nyiex dex ka mex nawh Yi-li-mi-leux.
2 aKoe tix ngaix,
Lu-teux ah nin ka Na-eu-me:
“Kieh aux hu sut si gleem nghox ca pa cawd yuh pui daux kaung,
aux tag chix yaox ka pui pa chix priex ka aux tix kaux heue.”
Na-eu-me ah nin:
“Maix hu heue,
kawn bun.”
3 Mawh kheu nan,
Lu-teux hu ka daux kaung,
hawt sut si gleem nghox ca pa cawd khaing pui pa vawg.
Kaung pa sut Lu-teux si gleem nghox ca jiet caw tix mawh cie Paw-a-see.
4 Ang rheeng,
Paw-a-see hoek khaing Paw-li-haung;
nawh ah nin ka cao vawg nghox ca khankix:
“Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai pex!”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex nawb buan ka piang maix heue!”
5 Paw-a-see chawk ka cao kaing:
“An mawh kawn bun nyiex mawx?”
6 Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Nawh mawh kawn bun meung tang ceu tang cad pa hawt ka Na-eu-me khaing haktiex Maw-ya.
7 Nawh khiox khaw tix kieh aux cu nawh sut si gleem nghox ca khaix pui pa vawg nghox ca.
Pon ngawb cao cao nawh tom jauh tix yuh kaing,
yam in kawn taug khraox laux tix ka greem nyiex bang heue.”
8 Paw-a-see tom hu ah nin ka Lu-teux:
“Nghiet ka lox aux,
kawn bun eue,
maix ang siao lai hu sut si gleem nghox ca ka daux kaung cie pui,
ot tau dee in mai kawn bun cao kaing.
9 Jhak cao kaing simex vawg ka dee mawx,
maix tom hu sut tau mai cao kaing bun.
Aux hoik tawx lox ka cao kaing simex tix,
ang cu kix nu nai ka maix.
Phan maix simie tix nyaux rawm,
chix tix hu teei tix nyaux ka daux aw pa hoik kin kix hoek heue.”
10 Lu-teux krub ka Paw-a-see,
gup pak ngai tix ka piang tiex,
mai ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai caox rhawm ka aux?
Caox rhawm ka aux pui tang meung tix kaux lie?”
11 Paw-a-see ah nin:
“Khaix hoik yum mex maix,
krax priex maix miex mex tix,
aux hoik mhong ka khawm uik.
Aux hoik tawng lih hu maix yuh ka mawx khaing miex keeing tix mai haktiex tix,
ot yuh ka mawx ka daux ceu pui pa ang tix tawng.
12 Kieh Simiang Siyiex deeh pawk buan son ka kaing jhaw pa yuh maix.
Kieh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa deuh rhawm tix tawx buan son ka maix heue!”
13 Lu-teux pawk lox ah nin:
“Sara,
maix kied mhawm rhawm ka auxb!
Viang aux ang keung tix yuh ka siliex maix kawx,
maix kawn ah lox pa nyawm rhawm yuh nin ka aux.”
14 Yam hoek bawg yam ih priex,
Paw-a-see ah nin ka Lu-teux:
“Hoek tin,
ih khao mun nghox ca,
cux ka daux rawm niex.”
Lu-teux tom ngawm tau mai cao kaing.
Paw-a-see tawx khao mun pa hoik tong tix tix blah ka nawh,
nawh ih tom sak,
kawn koe pa deuk.
15-16 Yam baox kaoh hu sut nawh si gleem nghox ca,
Paw-a-see ah nin ka cao kaing tix khankix:
“Kieh nawh viex rhawm tix hu sut,
phan nawh hu sut ka daux pa hoik mad pex kawx ang pon tix ah ka nawh;
pie pa hoik mat pex kawx teei lih tix blah,
kieh nawh sut,
ang pon tix ne raw ka nawh.”
17 Lu-teux hu sut si gleem nghox ca pa cawd pa khrum ka daux kaung yuh nin,
tom hoek plak bo.
Nawh pauh nghox ca pa sut tix,
pon dex tix khrawng heue.
18 Nawh viex nghox ca pa hoik pauh tix ing ka daux veng mai tawx miex mex tix yaox,
mai baox tawx miex mex tix ih ka khao mun pa kawn deuk.
19 Na-eu-me chawk ka nawh:
“In singaix maix sut si gleem nghox ca dee mawx nie ding?
Yuh maix kaing ka daux kaung nyiex mawx?
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka pui pa priex ka maix!”
Lu-teux krai ah nin ka miex mex tix,
nawh yuh kaing ka daux kaung cie Paw-a-see heue.
20 cNa-eu-me ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka Paw-a-see!
Simiang Siyiex kied koe krax pa caox rhawm ka pui pa im mai pui pa yum.”
Na-eu-me baox kah ah nin:
“Pui an mawh paox nyiex dex ex,
khaw tix priex ka ex heue.”
21 Lu-teux ah nin:
“Nawh kawn kieh aux hu sut si gleem nghox ca khaix cao kaing nawh,
tom hoik uik vawg ka nghox ca.”
22 Na-eu-me ah nin ka Lu-teux:
“Mawh heue,
kawn bun,
nie mhawm maix hu ka kaung Paw-a-see tau mai cao kaing bun;
phan maix hu ka kaung cie pui tang kaux,
lhat kix nu nai ka maix.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
Lu-teux hu sut si gleem nghox ca tau mai cao kaing bun,
tom hoik uik vawg ka nghox ca pa lang mu mai nghox ca.
Lu-teux kawn ca ot tau mai miex mex tix heue.
