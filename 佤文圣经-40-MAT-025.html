Phek tiiem bun krih kao kaux
25
a“Ka daux singaix an,
meung rao ma awm grawng lox been plak greem yuh nin:
koe bun krih kao kaux viex kawk ngu ka daux taix tix,
mai hu rieb ka kaw pa yuh ka paox.
2 Ka daux kix phuan kaux mawh bun daox,
phuan kaux mawh bun lhak.
3 Pa daox teei viex ka kawk ngu,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax rien khre ka bee tom kub;
4 pa lhak teei viex ka kawk ngu,
mai baox teei viex ka bee ra loe kawng.
5 Kaw pa yuh ka paox lha hoek,
bun krih khankix gaing mai laig it heue.
6 “Hoek yam greung sawm,
koe pui kok yo ah nin:
‘Kaw pa yuh ka paox hoek,
pex hu rieb ka nawh heue!’
7 Bun krih an kix kao kaux haing,
mai tok ka kawk ngu kix riang.
8 Yam an,
bun pa daox an kix krai ka bun pa lhak an kix ah nin:
‘Guah tix tawx bee ka yix ceeix,
mawh kheu kawk ngu cie yix hoik dex sang yeet heue.’
9 Bun pa lhak an kix pawk ah nin:
‘Ang chix,
bee cie yix ang kub tix guah ka pex,
pex tang hu liag ka nyiex pa coh ka bee heue.’
10 Yam hu liag bun pa daox ka bee,
kaw pa yuh ka paox hoek.
Pa lhak phuan kaux pa hoik rien khre tix mhawm yaox tix laig tau mai kaw pa yuh ka paox ka daux bang poi,
mai nyeep ka siviex heue.
11 b“Bun pa daox khankix kawx hoek,
mai kok yo ah nin:
‘Sara,
sara,
paoh siviex ka yix liak!’
12 Kaw pa yuh ka paox pawk ah nin:
‘Aux ang tawng ka pex heue.’ ”
13 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag rien rhawm tix,
mawh kheu ang pex tawng ka daux singaix bawg yam an,
nari bawg yam an sang hoek yam mawx heue.”
Phek tiiem siliex loe kaux
(Lai Lu-cia 19.11–27)
14 “Meung rao ma baox awm grawng lox been plak greem yuh nin:
koe pui tix kaux sang lih hu plak praix,
nawh kok siliex tix hoek,
mai ap tix tawx sin ngao tix ka kix.
15 Nawh hawt awm koe riang rao pa koe kix kux kaux,
tix kaux nawh tawx mau luah ka phuan rheng,
tix kaux nawh tawx ka ra rheng,
tix kaux nawh tawx ka tix rheng,
khaix an nawh yaog tix hu.
16 Pa rieb mau luah phuan rheng an hu yuh lai gee dum mawx dum an,
mai baox pon rao phuan rheng.
17 Awm an nang,
pa rieb ra rheng mau luah an,
mai baox pon rao ra rheng.
18 Viang mawh nan,
pa rieb tix rheng mau luah an lih hu,
nawh kaong daux tix mu,
mai si paung ka mau pa tawx simiang heue.
19 “Hoik luan rheeng nyao,
simiang siliex an kix deeh hoek,
sawn mau maix kix.
20 Pa rieb mau luah phuan rheng laig daux nyiex,
mai baox deeh viex rao pa pon tix phuan rheng hoek,
ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
mau luah pa tawx maix ka aux phuan rheng,
maix jhak,
aux deeh baox pon rao phuan rheng.’
21 Simiang ah nin:
‘Kied mhawm,
maix pa mawh siliex pa mhawm mai pa kaux sijax heue!
Maix hoik kaux sijax ka daux kaing pa iak,
aux sang tawx maix kuang ka kaing pa ting.
Laig tix gaux rhawm tau mai simiang maix heue!’
22 Pa rieb mau ra rheng laig daux nyiex ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
mau pa tawx maix ka aux ra rheng,
maix jhak,
aux baox pon rao ra rheng.’
23 Simiang ah nin:
‘Kied mhawm,
maix pa mawh siliex pa mhawm mai pa kaux sijax,
maix hoik kaux sijax ka daux kaing pa iak,
aux sang tawx maix kuang ka kaing pa ting.
Laig tix gaux rhawm tau mai simiang maix heue!’
24 Yam in,
siliex pa rieb mau luah tix rheng kawx laig daux nyiex nang,
mai ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
aux hoik tawng maix mawh pui pa kied kin tete;
dee ang maix bax sum bax van,
maix tag sang vawg kah,
dee ang maix bax sum bax van,
maix kawx tag sang khrawm kah.
25 Aux kied lhat,
aux tom si paung mau maix ot ka greem tiex.
Maix jhak,
mau maix ot dee in.’
26 Simiang nawh ah nin:
‘Maix pa mawh siliex pa caox pa nieh in heue!
Kheu hoik tawng maix aux tag sang vawg ka dee ang bax sum bax van,
tag sang khrawm vawg ka dee ang bax sum bax van,
27 maix khaw tix teei mau aux deuh ka daux nyiex krao mau,
yam deeh hoek aux,
chix tix pon khawm num khawm rao heue.
28 Pex teei mau luah cie nawh hoek,
tawx ka kaw pa koe tix mheen an.
29 cMawh kheu pui pa hoik koe an,
tag sang nie baox tawx kah,
mai kieh nawh kub tom nie;
pui pa ang koe an,
pie pa hoik koe nawh ceeix ceeix kawx tag sang teei hu heue.
30 dPhan mawh siliex pa ang koe ceu in,
grheu nawh lih hu ka pa faik plak praix;
ka dee an,
nawh tag sang yiiem grai mai greeik rhang braing tix heue.’ ”
Krax prawng ca pa nghoik
31 e“Yam mawh kawn kawn pui simiang,
kawn cau Siyiex lih hoek tau mai nawh,
nawh tag sang ngawm ka piang dang ngeung min plak ngai;
32 kawn pui piang haktiex tag sang khrawm tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
Nawh tag sang guah kix lih koe ra cub,
awm hoik guah cao liang yung pex lih khaing yung yuh nan.
33 Nawh tag sang deuh yung ot ka plak taix khawm dawm tix,
pex ot ka plak taix khawm goix tix.
34 “Khaix an,
simiang tag sang krai ka pui pa ot plak taix khawm dawm tix ah nin:
‘Pex pa mawh pui pa pon buan son pa tawx keeing aux eue,
pex hoek heue!
Hoek tix pon ka meung pa hoik rien khre son pex tom yam jauh tix phan yuh haktiex.
35 Mawh kheu yam hoik cai rhawm aux,
pex tawx aux sawm,
yam simie aux tix nyaux rawm,
pex tawx aux nyaux.
Yam vuk vied vuk viang aux ka haktiex tang dauh,
pex priex ka aux;
36 yam ot ka kaong aux,
pex tawx aux ceep kraung;
yam saux aux pex kia ngien ka aux;
yam ot aux ka daux thawng,
pex hoek tix jhak ka aux.’
37 Yam an,
pui pa rauh pa see tag sang pawk ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
yix yaox maix hoik cai rhawm yam mawx tawx maix sawm?
Yam sang nyaux maix rawm,
tawx maix nyaux?
38 Yaox yam vuk vied vuk viang maix,
priex ka maix?
Yaox yam ot ka kaong maix,
tawx maix ceep kraung?
39 Yix yaox yam saux maix yam mawx,
phan ang mawh nan,
yam ot maix ka daux thawng hoek tix jhak ka maix lie?’
40 Simiang pawk ah nin:
‘Aux krai lox tete ka pex,
phan pex hoik yuh ka daux pui pa iak pa hawt khaix aux tix kaux,
an hoik mawh yuh pex ka son aux heue!’
41 “Khaix an,
simiang krai ka pui pa ot ka plak taix khawn goix tix ah nin:
‘Yoi heue!
Pa mawh pui pa pon krax pa jhax lawg Siyiex eue,
pex lih khaing aux heue!
Laig ka daux ngu pa ang chix yeet leeing ju pa hoik rien son Phi-caox mai lien nawh heue!
42 Mawh kheu yam hoik cai rhawm aux,
pex ang tawx aux sawm,
yam simie aux tix nyaux rawm,
pex ang tawx aux nyaux;
43 yam vuk vied vuk viang aux pex ang priex ka aux.
Yam ot ka kaong aux,
pex ang tawx aux ceep kraung;
yam saux aux,
phan ang mawh nan yam ot aux ka daux thawng pex ang kia ngien ka aux.’
44 Yam an kix tag sang ah nin:
‘Simiang eue,
yix yaox maix cai rhawm yam mawx,
phan ang mawh nan yam sang nyaux rawm,
phan ang mawh nan yam vuk vied vuk viang,
phan ang mawh nan yam ot ka kaong,
phan ang mawh nan yam saux,
phan ang mawh nan yam ot ka daux thawng,
ang teem coi ka maix lie?’
45 Simiang tag sang pawk ah nin:
‘Aux krai ka pex tete,
phan pex ang bax yuh ka daux pui pa iak khaing paox tix tix kaux,
in hoik mawh ang yuh ka son aux.’
46 fPui pa yuh nin tag sang khaw tix kham krax ciyang leeing ju.
Phan mawh pui pa rauh pa see khankix,
kix tag sang pon ju leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
