Yuh naing mai pui Ya-ma-lix
15
aSa-mu-eu ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Simiang Siyiex ploi aux hoek groi rawm bee piang maix,
mai kieh maix mawh simiang pui cie nawh Yi-seu-liex.
Mawh kheu nan,
maix caw tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex.
2 bSimiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing tag sang ciyang ka pui Ya-ma-lix,
mawh kheu yam lih pui Yi-seu-liex khaing daux Ai-cix,
tax du kix hoik tieb tie ka kix.
3 Maix caw tix hu puing ka pui Ya-ma-lix,
mai yuh laux ka can pa koe kix khawm uik,
paw lai deuh tix ceu kawx;
tag caw tix pauh simex,
kawn bun,
kawn nyawm,
kawn ngie,
phung moi,
phung yung,
ka-la-uk,
mai la kix yum khawm uik heue.”
4 Sao-law ot ka Thi-liien khrawm ka pui naing tix mai simin kah,
pa hoek khaing Yi-seu-liex koe tix nga mheen kaux,
pa hoek khaing Yo-tax koe tix mheen kaux.
5 Nawh thung pui naing tix hu ka veng Ya-ma-lix,
mai kuan mox tix ot daux ngawd krox jun ka bawg yam hoek.
6 Sao-law ploi pui hu ne raw ka pui Ci-ni ah nin:
“Pex lih hu singai ot tang dauh khaing pui Ya-ma-lix,
son ang aux sang loi tix pauh ka pex.
Mawh kheu yam lih pui Yi-seu-liex khaing daux Ai-cix dix,
tax du pex hoik chix kia ngien ka kix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Ci-ni lih hu heue.
7 Sao-law pex ka pui Ya-ma-lix,
jauh tix puing kix ka Ha-fe-la tom hoek ka Su-eux plak lih singaix Ai-cix,
8 mai giex im ka simiang Ya-ma-lix pa kaux Ya-cia,
mai pauh ka kawn pui khawm uik.
9 Viang mawh nan,
Sao-law mai pui naing nawh deuh ka ju Ya-cia,
ang bax pauh ka yung,
moi mhaing,
kawn moi iak,
mai kawn yung iak pa blawn mhawm khankix,
can kraung tang ceu pa blawn mhawm kawx deuh kah.
Kix laux ngie ka ceu an kix pa ang chix tix siao mai ceu pa ang koe ngawh jum khankix heue.
Simiang Siyiex koeng mawh simiang Sao-law
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Sa-mu-eu:
11 “Aux klih rhawm ka kieh tix Sao-law mawh simiang;
nawh hoik tik vud ka aux,
mai lieh lheu gum lox tawx aux.”
Sa-mu-eu kied ang gaux rhawm mai khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex leeing sawm.
12 Ka daux ra singaix pon ngawb cao,
Sa-mu-eu hu sawg ka Sao-law.
Nawh mhong hoik hoek Sao-law ka Cia-mix,
mai sum mhai pa doh keud son to kaux tix dee an tix mu,
khaix an,
mai hu krax plak Cie-cia heue.
13 Sa-mu-eu hoek ka Sao-law,
Sao-law ah lox nawb ka nawh ah nin:
“Sa-mu-eu,
kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka maix!
Aux hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh heue.”
14 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Mawh lie?
Yuh ka mawx aux lai kawn mhong lox bawx moi bawx yung lie?”
15 Sao-law pawk lox ah nin:
“In mawh pa pon teei pui naing aux khaing pui Ya-ma-lix tan.
Kix deuh ka yung moi pa blawn mhawm,
mawh kheu son sang kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix.
Pa deuk khankix,
yix hoik yuh laux uik heuec.”
16 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Paw lai krai!
Aux sang krai maix mhong lox pa si kah Simiang Siyiex ka aux ka daux bo kawx.”
Sao-law ah nin:
“Krai heue!”
17 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Die maix ang een to kaux tix koe ceu,
viang mawh nan,
maix hoik mawh simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui.
Simiang Siyiex hoik laug maix yuh simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
18 nawh ploi maix lih hu,
mai tawx lox nhie maix yuh laux ka pui pa caox pa caig an kix pui Ya-ma-lix,
pauh kix yum khawm uik.
19 Yuh ka mawx maix lai ang nghiet lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh tix ki ka son sang tud ka kraung pa pon khaing daux naing,
yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka Simiang Siyiex lie?”
20 Sao-law ah nin:
“Aux sang nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex,
hu puing naing awm lox pa hoik krai nawh,
giex ka simiang Ya-cia,
mai pauh ka can pui Ya-ma-lix yum heue.
21 Viang mawh nan,
pui naing cie aux ang bax pauh ka moi yung pa blawn mhawm,
pa mawh pa hoik pon giex hoek khankix;
kix viex moi yung hu ka Cie-cia,
son sang kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix heue.”
22 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex muh ka ceu pa tix lie?
Mawh nghiet ka lox nawh lie?
Phan ang mawh nan,
mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai khawng tan pa tan ka nawh lie?
Nghiet ka lox nawh kawn lheu mhawm khaing khawng tan;
Nghiet lox kawn lheu mhawm khaing tan kawn yung pa blawn mhawm heue.
23 Pa tieh pa vheen ka nawh hoik mawh pa caox pa caig awm pa yuh si meed yuh sat khankix;
pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi hoik koe phi map awm pa krub ka riang rao khankix.
Mawh kheu hoik tik vud maix ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
nawh ang rhawm yaox ka maix,
ang kieh maix yuh simiang heue.
24 Sao-law ah nin:
“Mawh heue,
aux hoik yuh phi map;
aux hoik lieh lheu ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex mai ka lox pa hoik krai maix.
Aux lhat ka kawn pui,
kheu nan aux tom hawt tix yuh awm lox pa mhaing kix.
25 Yam in,
aux khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map aux,
ing tau mai aux ka Cie-cia,
aux sang krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.”
26 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Aux ang ing tau mai maix.
Maix hoik tik vud ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex;
nawh kawx ang rhawm yaox ka maix,
mai ang kieh maix yuh simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
27 dSa-mu-eu sang vud tix hu,
Sao-law bluh ka sibex dha nawh,
mai kieh griah heue.
28 Sa-mu-eu ah nin ka nawh:
“Ka daux singaix in,
Simiang Siyiex deeh teei ka meung pa kuang maix Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx ka pui pa blawn mhawm khaing maix.
29 Cao pa ting riang ting rao ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh Siyiex pa ang krai lox pie lox lhien,
nawh kawx ang lai loh rhawm keud tix;
nawh ang awm pui haktiex,
ang lai loh rhawm keud tix heue.”
30 Sao-law ah nin:
“Aux hoik phit lud,
viang mawh nan,
viex maix ca tawx pak ngai ka aux sivoe kuad song kawn pui mai sivoe ngai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
viex maix ing tau mai aux,
son sang hu krub aux ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix heue.”
31 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu ing tau mai nawh ka haktiex Cie-cia;
Sao-law tom krub ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
32 Sa-mu-eu tawx lox ah nin:
“Viex simiang Ya-cia hoek dee ot aux tin!”
Ya-cia si yaung si kua mai hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Sa-mu-eu,
nawh keud ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
“Krax yum kied mawh pa lhat graunge ex ka heue!”
33 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Lawm vaig maix hoik sax kieh miex keeing pui tom nie som nai ka kawn simex kawn bun tix,
yam in miex maix sang som nai ka kawn simex tix heue.”
Sa-mu-eu tom pauh Ya-cia ka plak sivoe ngai khu dee tan Cie-cia heue.
34 Khaix an,
Sa-mu-eu ing ka La-max;
mai simiang Sao-law kawx ing ka nyiex Ci-pi-yax.
35 Jauh yam an hoek tom yum,
Sa-mu-eu ang lai sax yaox ka Sao-law,
viang mawh nan,
nawh laux rhawm laux rhi son Sao-law.
Simiang Siyiex klih ka sum tix Sao-law mawh simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
