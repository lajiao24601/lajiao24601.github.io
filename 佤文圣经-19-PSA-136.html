Lox rhax pa ah ting buan son
136
aTag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem nawh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
2 Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex pa koe ka piang siyiex;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
3 Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka simiang pa koe ka piang simiang;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
 
4 Mawh ngie nawh pa pon yuh mhai pa daox pa am khaing;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
5 bNawh phan yuh rao ma ka daux ping nyia cuyi to kaux tix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
6 cNawh nih haktiex ot ka piang rawm raux;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
7 dNawh phan yuh singaix mai khix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
8 Singaix kuang ka pon ngaix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
9 Khix mai simuing kuang ka pon sawm;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
 
10 eNawh hoik pauh ka pui Ai-cix pa liang tix keh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
11 fNawh viex pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing daux Ai-cix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
12 Nawh nyeu tawm taix tix mai plawng taix pa ting riang ting rao;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
13 gNawh hoik guah ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh tang ot;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
14 Nawh viex pui cie tix tiang hu;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
15 Nawh hoik kieh rawm kheem ka simiang Ai-cix mai pui naing cie nawh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
 
16 Nawh viex pui cie tix laig ka daux tiag daux tu;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
17 Nawh hoik pauh ka simiang pa ting riang ting rao khankix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
18 Nawh hoik pauh ka simiang pa koe min khankix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
19 hNawh hoik pauh ka Si-hong simiang Ya-maw-lix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan;
20 iNawh hoik pauh ka Eux,
simiang Pa-sang;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
21 Nawh hoik teei tix tawx haktiex cie kix ka pui cie tix;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
22 Nawh hoik teei tix tawx haktiex cie kix ka siliex tix pui Yi-seu-liex;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
 
23 Yam ang ex pex,
mai nawh ang bax tik vud ka ex;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
24 Nawh hoik taoh ex luat luan khaing daux tawm taix pui koeng;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
25 Nawh tawx priex preem ka kawn pui mai to sat;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
26 Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex piang rao ma;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.
