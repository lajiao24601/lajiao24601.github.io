Riang su sai sido
7
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tawx aux yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu:
nawh hoik phan yuh ka phung sido.
Yam an,
jiet mawh bawg yam khrawm ka to ma pui nyiex simiang ka daux tix bawg,
yam jauh simie tix tawx plaung tix ka daux ra bawg.
2 Aux yaox ka sido pu tix hoek,
mai ih ka to ma pa koe piang tiex singax khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka pui cie maix heue!
Kix kied iak lied kied iak riang,
sang pon kham yuh ka mawx lie?”
3 Rhawm rhi Simiang Siyiex hoik deeh awn.
Nawh ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
ka kaing pa hoik yaox maix kieh luan heue.”
Riang su sai ngu
4 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tawx aux yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu:
nawh rien khre ka ngu,
tag sang ciyang ka pui cie tix.
Ngu hoik gleeing dee keet rawm pa koe ka greem tiex kroh khawm uik,
mai baox jauh tix gleeing ka ceu pa koe piang tiex khawm uik.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
naox kaing yuh maix heue!
Pui cie maix kied iak lied kied iak riang,
sang pon kham yuh ka mawx lie?”
6 Rhawm rhi Simiang Siyiex hoik awn.
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
ka kaing pa hoik yaox maix kieh luan heue.”
Riang su sai maox diag
7 Simiang Siyiex tawx aux yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu:
aux yaox Simiang Siyiex cung ka praok guh ding pa khraox tawm haok,
taix nawh giex ka maox diag.
8 Simiang Siyiex chawk aux ah nin:
“A-maw-see yaox maix ceu pa tix lie?”
Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Yaox maox diag heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang vhak ka maox diag,
mai kieh pui cie tix tawng,
kix jiet awm guh ding pa vawk yuh nan.
Bawg in aux ang doh lai pluat ploi ka kix heue!
9 Dee yuh doeh ceu cad Yi-sha krax krub tag sang yieh laux leeing;
nyiex pa singax dee krub pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang yuh laux leeing khawm uik.
Aux tag sang kieh meung pa giex simiang Yie-law-paw-ang laux leeing heue.”
A-maw-see mai Ya-ma-shiex
10 Cao giex kaing li Paw-theu-lix pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex tawx pui hu krai ka simiang Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ah nin:
“A-maw-see cheeik pui cie maix suk sag,
kieh kix tieh vheen ka maix;
gum lox pa krai nawh ang mawh pa mhawm son meung.
11 Nawh ah nin:
‘Yie-law-paw-ang tag sang yum ka daux bang naing;
pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang vuk vied vuk viang hoek ka meung tang meung khaing haktiex tix heue.’ ”
12 Ya-ma-shiex krai ka A-maw-see ah nin:
“Cao jhai eue,
maix hoik kub krai ka heue.
Maix deeh ing sidiex ka haktiex Yo-tax,
kieh kix eei ka maix heue!
13 Paw-theu-lix mawh nyiex buan nyiex haw simiang,
mawh num dee krub pui piang meung,
paw maix sidiex gau dee in heue.”
14 A-maw-see pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh cub cao jhai awm pa krai maix yuh nan,
pa sidiex son mau khri.
Aux mawh cao liang yung,
mai mawh nang cao pa kuang kia khaox liah.
15 Viang mawh nan,
yam liang aux ka yung,
Simiang Siyiex hoek tix kok ka aux,
mai nhie aux hoek tix sidiex gau gum lox nawh ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix nghiet ka gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux.
Maix ang cu aux sidiex gau ka gum lox Siyiex,
ang cu aux ne raw ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Moeng maix tag sang lih mawh bun coh kaux ka daux veng;
kawn simex kawn bun maix tag sang yum ka bang naing khawm uik.
Pui tag sang guah ka haktiex maix;
to kaux maix tag sang yum ka daux haktiex pui tang kaux.
Pui tag sang grheu pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing haktiex kix,
mai vuk vied vuk viang ka meung tang meung heue.’ ”
