Cao jhai ne raw ka Yaw-sa-fa
19
Yaw-sa-fa pa mawh simiang Yo-tax pon yaox tix ing mhawm hoek ka nyiex haw Yie-lu-sa-leung.
2 Cao jhai Yie-hu kawn simex Ha-na-ni hoek ka simiang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix keud,
teem ka pui caox,
mai muh tix koeng ka pui cie Simiang Siyiex mawh khaw lie?
Pa yuh maix hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm.
3 Viang mawh nan kawx,
maix ca kawn koe kaing pa mhawm tix blah.
Maix hoik yieh tix tik ka riang rong siyiex bun Ya-seu-la khaing daux meung khawm uik;
maix hoik but rhawm tix khiox khaw sawg ka Siyiex heue.”
Been pa loh Yaw-sa-fa
4 Simiang Yaw-sa-fa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik tat bawg yam hu lai ling ka kawn pui haktiex,
jauh ka Piie-see-pa plak seh tom hoek ka gawng haktiex Yi-fa-liien plak laong,
mai kok yo kawn pui hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix.
5 Nawh sum veng pa kaux pa cong ot ka Yo-tax ku dauh,
mai tawx simiang cao prawng ot kah,
6 ah nin ka kix:
“Pex rien rhawm tix yam yuh kaing;
pex ang prawng ca ka daux siawp sibrawm kawn pui,
mai prawng ca ka daux siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex.
Yam prawng ca pex,
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai pex.
7 Tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai rien rhawm tix yam prawng ca;
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex ang khaw rhawm ka pa ang rauh ang see,
pa teei alo tix,
pa rieb mau chun praok heue.”
8 Yaw-sa-fa kawx tawx pui Li-we,
cao giex kaing li,
mai simiang tang kaux mawh simiang prawng ca ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai kix prawng ca ka see pa lheu lieh pui ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex mai krax pa simah pui haktiex ka paox tix.
9 Nawh krai ka kix ah nin:
“Pex caw tix koe rhawm pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex yuh kaing,
caw tix koe rhawm pa mawh pa meh yuh kaing.
10 Phan koe paox ceu paox cad pex kaoh khaing veng tang veng,
kheu pa pauh pui,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka see pa hoik lheu lieh ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap ang ah,
mai viex hoek ka plak si oe ngai pex,
pex tag caw tix bre rhawm tix duk thung kix mhawm mhawm ka daux bawg yam chawk cheng kix,
khaw tix sang yuh ka mawx,
son ang sang lud loe ka Simiang Siyiex.
Phan ang mawh nan,
maix mai paox ceu paox cad maix rien tix chix kieh Simiang Siyiex song rhawm.
Phan pex hoik yuh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
pex tom ang lai koe phi map.
11 “Simiang cao giex kaing li Ya-ma-li-ya pa ting hoik koe siawp sibrawm tix tat dut ka piang see been li khawm uik;
simiang haktiex Yo-tax pa mawh Si-pa-ti-ya kawn simex Yi-seu-ma-lix hoik koe siawp sibrawm pa tat dut ka piang see kawn pui haktiex khawm uik.
Pui Li-we tag koe akhuang tix kuang ka krax pa prawng ca nyiex dee prawng.
Pex tag caw tix viex rhawm kua yuh awm lox pa hoik krai plak piang.
Kieh Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai pui pa mhawm khankix heue.”
