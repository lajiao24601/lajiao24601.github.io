Yuh teug sijax mukheu
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 25.10–22)
37
Pi-sha-liex yuh teug sijax mukheu ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
plak lang tix yieh kao kongfeeng,
plak vieh tix gliah liah kongfeeng,
plak lhaong tix gliah liah kongfeeng.
2 Nawh caw tix om khri mhawm ka plak daux khawm plak praix teug,
mai kawb khri hawt praok khawm uik.
3 Nawh baox yuh jeep khri pon mu,
son sang grawng kah,
mai kap ot ka piang jaong teug khawm pon jaong,
tix plak ra mu.
4 Nawh caw tix yuh khaox glawm ra gawng ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka khri,
5 mai chup ka daux jeep khri teug an khawm ra plak.
6 Nawh yuh dawb teug tix mu ka khri mhawm,
plak lang koe tix yieh kao kongfeeng,
plak vieh tix gliah liah kongfeeng,
7 baox tawk riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu ka khri,
8 ka daog dawb koe tix plak tix mu,
mai kleuk tix tau mai dawb,
lih mawh tix mu.
9 Ci-lu-paw an ra mu veeh ngai tix plak paox,
mai di preeik tix tawb teug sijax mukheu heue.
Yuh pheen khan khao mun
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 25.23–30)
10 Pi-sha-liex yuh pheen tix lhang ka khaox Cing-heu-huang;
plak lang tix daix daix kongfeeng,
plak vieh tipon pon kongfeeng,
plak lhaong tix gliah liah kongfeeng.
11 Nawh om ka khri mhawm,
mai om khri ka hawt vung viang.
12 Vung viang pheen baox sau lat pa koe ka praok diag atigliah phuan kongli,
mai praok tag kawb ka khri.
13 Nawh caw tix yuh daux grawng jeep khri pon mu,
mai sau ka jaong pheen khawm pon daog.
14 Jeep khri an kieh dex ka praok,
son sang chix chup ka khaox grawng pheen laig.
15 Nawh yuh khaox glawm ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka khri.
16 Nawh baox yuh kraung siao pa ot piang pheen ka khri mhawm,
pa mawh pan,
cuan,
si ngien mai kawk pa prauk plai.
Yuh jawk kawk ngu
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 25.31–40)
17 Nawh yuh jawk kawk ngu tix mu ka khri mhawm,
jawk kawk ngu,
mai jaong,
rong caw tix tawk ka khri;
kho tai,
tux tai,
lawm tai,
caw tix kieh si doe paox lih mawh tix mu tau.
18 Jawk kawk ngu ra plak kieh lih koe liah kak,
tix plak loe gawng.
19 Tix gawng kieh koe loe mu kho tai,
greeh grang awm tai khaox Batan,
koe tux koe lawm.
20 Rong jawk kawk ngu kieh koe pon mu kho tai,
greeh grang awm tai khaox Batan,
koe tux koe lawm.
21 Kak loe brawm ku brawm ka greem,
koe tix mu lawm tai.
22 Lawm tai,
kak,
mai jawk kawk ngu tag tawk lih mawh ka khri mhawm tix khlawp tau.
23 Nawh yuh jan son jawk kawk ngu aliah jan,
caw tix siao khri mhawm yuh si kiep kip tai kawn ngu mai pan tok ngu.
24 Nawh siao khri mhawm tix ngoe phuan kongcing raug jawk kawk ngu mai kraung pa siao ka khawm uik.
Yuh khu dee tan nyawng bhawng
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 30.1–5)
25 Nawh yuh khu dee tan pa gleeing nyawng bhawng tix mu ka khaox Cing-heu-huang.
Khu an mawh pon pak,
plak lang tipon phuan kongfeeng,
plak vieh tipon phuan kongfeeng,
plak lhaong tix dim kongfeeng.
Rung pa bhlien an pon rung kleuk tix tau mai khu lih mawh tix mu heue.
26 Nawh om khri mhawm ka toh plak piang,
hawt praok,
khawm pon plak khawn pon rung,
mai kawb khri ka praok.
27 Nawh baox yuh daux jhawk khri ra mu son sang chup ka kao grawng khu,
kieh rieb ka greem khri khu khawm ra plak pa ot ka praok,
son sang chix chup khaox grawng khu gleeing nyawng bhawng tix laig.
28 Nawh yuh khaox grawng ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka khri.
Yuh bee pa singax singeh mai nyawng bhawng
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 30.22–38)
29 aNawh baox yuh bee pa singax singeh,
mai nyawng bhawng pa mhawm,
pa jiet nyawm bhawng,
awm pa yuh ka sidah bhawng.
