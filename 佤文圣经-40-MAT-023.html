Ne raw ka sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai pui Fa-li-saix
(Lai Ma-kheu 12.38–39; Lai Lu-cia 11.43, 46; 20.45–46)
23
Ye-sux krai ka pui hun mai pui gau tix ah nin:
2 “Sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai pui Fa-li-saix cung tix kah lox tat jhawm prawng ka piang dee ngawm Maw-si,
3 pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex gau kix,
viang mawh nan,
paw vi gau tix hawt ka kaing pa yuh kix.
Mawh kheu kix chix krai khaing ang chix yuh.
4 Kix phrawk beeh glawm pa cien ot ka piang klip pui tang kaux,
viang mawh nan,
to kaux kix ang cu tix tawx giien taix tix,
pie tix giien teei tix tieh ka beeh glawm kix.
5 aKix yuh kaing ceu pa tix ang ah mawh yuh son sang kieh pui yaox.
Kix viex ka hau phuk lai sigang khri,
mai baox sau tom sibex dha pa lang.
6 Kix muh tix ngawm ka dee ngawm pa koe sivoe yam yuh poi,
mai ka nyiex sidiex pa blawk pui ngawm kah,
7 kix simie pui tawx krax nab yam yung yi ka tix sivoe ngai pui ka daux bang,
pui kok kix mawh ‘sara.’
8 Tete te lai pex ang khaw tix kok mawh ‘sara;’
kheu hoik ra lai mawh pex paox yuh kaing tau,
koe ngie tix kaux khaing pa mawh ‘sara.’
9 Pex paw vi kok pui piang haktiex tix kaux kawx mawh ‘keeing’,
mawh kheu ‘keeing pex’ piang rao ma koe ngie tix kaux khaing heue.
10 “Pex kawx ang khaw tix kok mawh ‘Sara cao thung’,
mawh kheu ‘Sara cao thung’ pex mawh Khrit tix kaux khaing.
11 bKa daux pex pui mawx pa blawn ting,
pui an tag sang mawh siliex pex.
12 cSiyiex tag sang yeen pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tiiem,
mai yaog pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi lhaong heue.”
Ne raw ka pui pa pie lhien
(Lai Ma-kheu 12.40; Lai Lu-cia 11.39–42; 44, 52; 20.47)
13 “Pex pa mawh sara chix kah phuk lai sigang khri pa pie lhien mai pui Fa-li-saix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex hoik nyip siviex meung rao ma ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
viang mawh nan,
to kaux ang laig,
mai ang rhawm kieh nang pui tang kaux laig heue!d
15 “Pex pa mawh sara pa chix kah phuk lai sigang khri pa pie lhien mai pui Fa-li-saix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Son sang kieh pui tix kaux laig been li,
pex sau riang tix hu ku si dauh haktiex,
khaix hoik laig nawh been li,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik yuh pui an lheu caox khaing tix,
lheu pon tix kham krax Maw-na-rhok khaing pex heue.
16 “Pex pa mawh pui dug ngai pa duk thung pui tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex ah nin:
‘Phan koe pui pa tiex tu ka nyiex buan,
chix tix ang sawn;
phan tiex tu ka klong khri pa koe daux nyiex buan,
tag caw hawt tix yuh.’
17 Pex pa mawh pui daox pa dug ngai heue!
Mawh chawk pex khri ting ceu lie?
Mawh khri pa lih mawh kraung nyiex buan pa singax singeh pa ting ceu lie?
18 Pex baox ah nin:
‘Phan koe pui pa tiex tu ka khu dee tan,
chix tix ang sawn;
phan koe pa tiex tu ka khawng tan piang khu dee tan,
tag caw tix hawt tix yuh heue.’
19 Pex pa mawh pui dug heue!
Mawh khawng tan pa ting ceu lie?
Mawh khu dee tan pa singax singeh pa ting ceu lie?
20 Tag caw tix tawng,
pui pa tiex tu ka khu dee tan hoik mawh pa tiex tu ka khu mai krax tan ka piang khu khawm uik.
21 Pa tiex tu ka nyiex buan hoik mawh pa tiex tu ka nyiex buan mai ka Siyiex pa ot ka daux nyiex buan;
22 epa tiex tu ka rao ma hoik mawh pa tiex tu ka dang ngeung Siyiex mai ka Siyiex pa ot piang heue.
23 f“Pex sara pa chix kah phuk lai sigang khri pa pie lhien mai pui Fa-li-saix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pie sidah bhawng lhax si oe,
pa mawh lhax haw,
lhax ki ting,
lhax ki iak khankix hoik tan ka Siyiex kao son tix son,
viang mawh nan,
lox tat jhawm prawng pa sidiex gau pui ting ceu,
awm pa mawh pa rauh pa see,
krax pa caox rhawm,
mai pa kaux sijax,
pex tieh tix ang hawt.
Gum lox sidiex pa ting ceu in kix tag mawh pa caw pex tix hawt,
phan mawh tang ceu ka caw tix yuh nan.
24 Pui duk thung pui dug eue,
pex teei maing tix mu lih khaing daux rawm tie nyaux pui,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik bleet ka-la-uk laig tix mu heue!
25 “Pex sara pa chix kah phuk lai sigang khri pa pie lhien mai pui Fa-li-saix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex phak pan singax plak praix,
viang mawh nan,
hoik sau rhawm going mai alo nex nham naog daux.
26 Pui Fa-li-saix pa dug heue!
Ran phak kawk singax plak daux,
mai plak praix kawx hoik singax heue.
27 g“Pex sara pa chix kah phuk lai sigang khri pa pie lhien mai pui Fa-li-saix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex hoik awm si meeig pa phuk phat pui ka simaox ceed,
plak praix nyawm tix jhak,
plak daux hoik mawh si-ang kaing pui yum mai ceu pa cux pa si um.
28 Awm an,
phan jhak tix yaox plak praix pex kied mawh pa rauh pa see tete,
viang mawh nan,
plak daux hoik sau naog ka pa pie lhien mai pa caox pa caig heue.”
Grox krai grawng sang kham kix krax ciyang
(Lai Lu-cia 11.47–51)
29 “Pex sara pa chix kah phuk lai sigang khri pa pie lhien mai pui Fa-li-saix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Pex hoik yuh si meeig son cao jhai,
yuh si meeig son pui pa koe ping nyia,
30 mai ah nin:
‘Phan ex ot ka daux baux singaix ju tax du,
ex ang doh awm kix yuh nan pauh ka cao jhai yuh nan.’
31 Kheu nan pex hoik krai mawh tix pa pauh ka kawn praix cao jhai khankix heue!
32 Pex tawng sau riang tix yuh kah,
yuh tom si dauh ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh tax du pex heue!
33 hSi uing pa koe jai mai kawn praix si uing pa koe jai heue!
Pex sang chix luat luan khaing krax ciyang Maw-na-rhok lie?
34 Pex nghiet heue,
aux tag sang ploi cao jhai,
pui pa koe ping nyia mai sara hoek ka pex dee an,
koe tix blah pex tag sang pauh kah,
koe tix blah pex tag sang tawk ka piang khaox lhak,
tix blah tag sang kham krax pa si vied ka daux nyiex sidiex,
jauh ka haktiex tix dauh hoek ka tix dauh.”
35 “iYuh nin,
krax ciyang pa kham pui pa ang koe dee lud tag sang hoek ka piang to kaux pex,
pa mawh Ya-paw pa ang koe dee lud,
tom hoek ka pa vied ka nham Sa-cia-li-yax kawn simex Pa-la-cia,
pa hoik pauh pex yum ka si nax nyiex buan mai khu dee tan.
36 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
krax ciyang in kix tag sang hoek piang pui baux singaix ju in khawm uik heue.”
Yiiem son Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Lu-cia 13.34–35)
37 “Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
maix hoik pauh ka cao jhai,
mai baox teei simaox tawk kawn cau pa ploi Siyiex hoek ka maix dee in heue!
Aux hoik sang kia ngien ka kawn praix maix miex bawg mawx,
awm khrawm miex ia kawn tix ot greem preeik tix yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang cu heue!
38 jJhak heue,
nyiex buan pex tag sang lih tix mawh haktiex pa riem.
39 kAux krai ka pex,
jauh yam in pex ang lai baox yaox ka aux,
tom hoek ah pex nin:
‘Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka kaw an pa hoek tix yuh ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Ye-sux heue!’ ”
