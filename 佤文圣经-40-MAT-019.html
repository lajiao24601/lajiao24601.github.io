Pa sidiex son pa tik ka moeng tix
(Lai Ma-kheu 10.1–12)
19
Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux ka lox in kix,
nawh lih hu khaing Cia-li-lix,
mai deeh ing ka haktiex Yo-thaix toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
2 Cub pui hun hawt khaix nawh,
mai nawh pai pui pa koe jhawm saux dee an moi heue.
3 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai keud tix sang yuh nawh laux,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Lox tat jhawm prawng ex cu simex tik moeng tix ka grawng ceu pa tix ang ah,
khaw lie?”
4 aYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Ang pex sax phat ka baux phuk lai sigang khri in lie?
‘Yam jauh,
Simiang Siyiex phan yuh ka simex mai baox phan yuh ka bun.’
Siyiex ah nin:
5 b‘Mawh kheu nan,
pui tag sang lih hu khaing miex keeing tix,
mai kleuk tix tau mai moeng tix,
kiex ra kaux lih mawh tix kaux khaing.’
6 Kheu yuh nin,
mex moeng ang lai baox mawh ra kaux,
mai hoik mawh tix kaux khaing.
Mawh kheu nan,
pa kleuk Siyiex tau,
kawn pui ang pon tix yieh laux heue.”
7 cPui Fa-li-saix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx Maw-si lai tawx gum lox ping nyap ka ex tix grawng,
krai phan mex tawx lai pa tik moeng tix,
tag chix tix tik ka nawh lie?”
8 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Maw-si cu pex tik moeng tix,
hoik mawh kheu kied kiien rhawm rhi pex;
viang mawh nan,
yam jauh dee bleeh gum lox pa krai Siyiex ang doh mawh nan.
9 dAux krai ka pex lheu khaing ang moeng pex singax,
pui mawx ang ah,
phan tik moeng tix,
mai baox hu moeng bun tang kaux,
hoik mawh krax pa phit vao heue.”
10 Pui gau ah nin:
“Phan mex mai moeng koe grawh grawng pa yuh nin,
ang mex moeng paox tix,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing heue.”
11 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Krax pa sidiex yuh nin ang mawh pui ku kaux pa pon hawt,
mawh ngie pui pa pon khawng phak buan son pa blawk Siyiex pa pon rieb khaing.
12 Mawh kheu krax pa ang pui koe mex koe moeng kied nie yuh nin:
koe pui tix blah ang koe mex koe moeng tom keh tix;
koe pui tix blah mawh grawh grawng pa yuh kawn pui pa ang kieh koe mex koe moeng.
Kawn koe tix blah mawh kheu yuh son meung rao ma pa ang kieh koe mex koe moeng.
Pui pa pon rieb ka krax pa sidiex in rieb ka heue!”
Nawb buan son ka kawn nyawm
(Lai Ma-kheu 10.13–16; Lai Lu-cia 18.15–17)
13 Koe pui viex kawn nyawm hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai sang kieh Ye-sux deuh taix tix rawg buan piang kix;
viang mawh nan,
pui gau ne raw ka pui an kix.
14 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kieh kawn nyawm iak hoek ka aux dee in,
paw vi tieb tie ka kix,
mawh kheu pui cie meung Siyiex tag sang awm kix pa yuh nin heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux deuh taix tix piang kix,
khaix an lih hu khaing haktiex an heue.
Pui cao mi pa nhum ju
(Lai Ma-kheu 10.17–31; Lai Lu-cia 18.18–30)
16 Koe tix bawg,
koe pui hoek ka Ye-sux mai ah nin:
“Sara,
sang khaw aux tix yuh ceu pa mhawm tix tag sang pon ju leeing ju lie?”
17 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai chawk aux ka grawng kaing ceu ‘Pa mhawm’ lie?
Koe ngie pa mawh ceu pa mhawm tix kaux khaing.
Phan maix sang kieh tix pon ju leeing ju,
tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap heue.”
18 eNawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Gum lox tawm ping nyap ceu pa tix lie?”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Ang pon tix pauh pui yum;
ang pon tix phit vao;
ang pon tix briex lak;
ang pon tix kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh;
19 ftag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka miex keeing tix;
tag caw tix muh ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
awm muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan heue.”
20 Pui pa nhum ju an pawk ah nin:
“Gum lox ping nyap in kix khawm uik aux hoik priex tix hawt khawm uik,
kawn sang yuh aux ceu pa tix lie?”
21 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Phan maix sang kieh tix hoek glawk pa grum mhawm,
hu coh can sin ngao pa koe maix khawm uik,
mai tan mau ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
maix tom tag koe sin ngao pa deuh ot ka piang meung rao ma;
khaix an hawt khaix aux heue.”
22 Pui pa nhum ju an mhong lox in,
tom laux rhawm laux rhi tix lih hu,
mawh kheu kied mag mi nawh heue.
23 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka pui gau tix:
“Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
pui pa koe mau sang lih tix mawh pui cie meung Siyiex mawh pa kied nu heue!
24 Aux baox krai ka pex,
pui pa koe mau sang lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex,
kawn nu khaing laig ka-la-uk ka daux kre nyex heue!”
25 Pui gau mhong lox in,
kied daox deng kah,
tom chawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pui mawx sang pon hoek krax taoh lie?”
26 Ye-sux dong tix jhak kix ah nin:
“Kawn pui ang pon,
viang mawh nan,
ka Siyiex hoik pon yuh ku ceu heue.”
27 Yam an Pi-teux ang jheeh tix ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
yix hoik prah can pa koe tix khawm uik,
mai hawt ka maix heue,
yix tag sang pon ceu pa tix lie?”
28 gYe-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai ka pex,
ka daux singaix bawg yam pa khraox pa tag sang hoek,
yam ngawm kawn kawn pui ka piang dang ngeung min plak ngai cie nawh,
pex pa mawh pui pa hawt khaix aux tag sang ngawm ka piang dang ngeung kao ra mu,
mai hoek tix prawng ca ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu pui.
29 Mawh pui mawx ang ah,
pa hoik prah nyiex ma,
pux aik,
pux o,
kawn praix,
mai kaung ma tix kheu son aux,
tag sang deeh pawk ka kaing yieh son,
khaix an tag sang pon ju leeing ju.
30 hViang mawh nan,
koe pui tom nie pa hu sivoe tag sang ot khaix,
pa ot khaix tag sang hu sivoe heue.”
