Tang-yi-li rawg buan son paox ceu cad tix
9
Pui Mi-ti-yax kawn simex Ya-ha-sui-lu pa kaux Ta-lio-see giex kaing ka meung Pa-pi-loeng.
2 aYam mawh nawh simiang ka daux tix num,
aux kawn si kaw tix phat ka phuk lai sigang khri,
keud ka gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka cao jhai Yie-li-mi daux rhawm tix,
grawng sang nhik sang riem Yie-lu-sa-leung atigliah num.
3 Aux tuah priex preem,
ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
ngawm ka daux nyaox,
mai veeh plak ngai tix rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex.
4 Aux rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux,
mai krai doh ka phi map son paox ceu paox cad tix.
Aux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
maix mawh Siyiex pa kied ting;
yix nab yam krub ka maix.
Maix hoik giex kaux ka sijax mukheu tix,
mai hoik tawx krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix ka pui pa muh ka maix,
pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx maix khankix heue.
5 “Yix hoik yuh phi map kaing pa caox pa caig,
krax lud loe yix hoik kied ting,
yix hoik lieh lheu ka gum lox ping nyap mai ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix.
6 Siliex maix pa mawh cao jhai khankix,
hoik sax krai lox ka simiang,
ka cao kaing,
ka tax du,
mai ka meung haktiex ka daux ceu kaux maix;
viang mawh nan,
yix ang cu nghiet ka kix.
7 Simiang eue,
kaing pa yuh maix mawh pa khaw caw tom si dauh,
viang mawh nan,
yix ca kawn yuh kaing pa kaig pa tua ang jud ka to kaux tix.
Mawh heue,
yix pa mawh pui Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung in kix,
hoik tieh vheen ka maix,
mai maix hoik ploi yix hu ka meung pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot dee singai mai dee dex ku meung,
hoik mawh kheu ang koe krax yum rhawm ka maix,
tom hoek krax kaig tua.
8 “Simiang Siyiex eue,
yix,
simiang yix,
cao kaing yix,
mai tax du yix hoik phit ka maix,
mai hoik yuh krax pa kaig pa tua.
9 Simiang pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
viang yix hoik tieh vheen ka maix,
maix ca kawn caox rhawm ka yix,
pluat ploi ka yix heue.
10 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
maix kieh siliex tix,
pa mawh cao jhai khankix krai yix mhong lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai nhie yix priex tix hawt,
viang mawh nan,
yix ang nghiet.
11 “Can pui Yi-seu-liex hoik lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix khawm uik,
hoik singai hu khaing maix,
ang cu nghiet ka maix.
Mawh kheu nan,
krax jhax lawg pa hoik tiiem ot ka daux phuk lai lox tat jhawm prawng siliex maix Maw-si,
tom hoek ka piang to kaux yix khawm uik.
12 Maix hoik kieh gum lox tix lih mawh ka piang yix,
mai ka piang to kaux simiang.
Maix ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung kawn lheu kin khaing ciyang maix ka veng pa koe piang haktiex khawm uik;
13 maix hoik ploi krax ciyang pa tiiem ot ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si lih ka piang to kaux yix khawm uik.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
hoek tom yam in,
yix ca kawn ang bax tik vud ka phi map,
ang bax priex tix hawt ka pa mawh pa meh maix,
ang bax kieh khaw ka rhawm maix.
14 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
maix hoik sax rien khre tix sang ciyang ka yix.
Tete te lai,
maix hoik ciyang ka yix;
kheu hoik mawh kaing pa yuh maix pa khaw caw tom si dauh,
viang mawh nan yix ang bax cu nghiet ka maix heue.
15 “Simiang pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
maix hoik sax jhi pa ting siawp sibrawm tix,
thung pui cie tix lih khaing Ai-cix;
ceu kaux maix hoik krai lao tom hoek singaix.
Yix hoik yuh phi map kaing pa caox pa caig.
16 Maix hoik taoh yix ka daux singaix pa hoik luan,
yam in khiox khaw maix paw vi song rhawm ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung pa singax singeh maix.
Mawh kheu phi map pa hoik yuh yix,
mai krax pa caox pa caig pa hoik yuh tax du yix,
pui meung pa ot sivie siveng yix hoik map nai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka pui cie maix.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex yix eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg siliex maix buan,
mai ka lox yiiem nawh liak.
Son sang kieh pui tawng mawh maix Siyiex,
khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka yix,
mai baox kia ngien ka nyiex buan maix pa hoik yuh pui laux leeing an.
18 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa rawg mhaing yix liak!
Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka yix,
khawm ka krax pa laux leeing veng pa ot ka greem ceu kaux maix heue!
Yix khiox khaw ka maix ang mawh kheu pa rauh see to kaux yix,
hoik mawh kheu krax pa ting caox rhawm maix.
19 “Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox yix liak!
Simiang eue,
khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka yix liak!
Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox yix,
mai taoh ka yix liak!
Siyiex aux eue,
son sang kieh pui tawng mawh maix Siyiex,
khiox khaw maix paw vi lai dha ka bawg yam;
kheu hoik mawh veng in veng cie maix,
pui in kix mawh pui cie maix heue.”
Cia-pai-liex kah ka dee bleeh riang su sai
20 Aux ca nied tix rawg buan,
krai doh ka phi map tix mai ka phi map paox ceu paox cad tix Yi-seu-liex,
khaix an kawn baox khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux,
son grawng pa singax cie nawh.
21 bYam kawn rawg aux buan,
Cia-pai-liex pa ran tix lih sivoe ka daux riang su sai pa yaox aux,
pu brax ka dee ot aux tin;
yam an hoik mawh bawg yam pa tan ka krax tan pon bo.
22 Cia-pai-liex hoik krai sidawng ka aux ah nin:
“Tang-yi-li,
aux hoek tix kah dee bleeh ka maix,
mai sang kieh maix koe ping nyia cuyi,
pon tawng sidawng.
23 Yam jauh maix tix khiox khaw ka Siyiex,
nawh hoik nghiet ka lox maix.
Nawh muh ka maix,
mawh kheu nan aux hoek tix krai lox nawh ka maix.
Yam in,
maix bre rhawm tix ka son gum lox in,
yuh tix tawng ka dee bleeh riang su sai in:
24 “Aliah atigliah num hoik mawh pa tat Siyiex tix taoh ka paox ceu paox cad maix,
mai ka veng pa singax luat luan khaing phi map.
Yam an,
nawh tag sang pluat ploi ka phi map,
viex pa rauh pa see pa koe leeing ju hoek,
kieh riang su sai mai lox jhai lih tix mawh,
mai deeh cox tan lu ka nyiex buan.c
25 Maix tag caw tix kieh tix tawng sidawng yuh nin:
jauh yam krai pao ka gum lox tawx son pa sang deeh cox sum ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
hoek ka pa laug Siyiex ka simiang lih,
ka daux glang bawg yam an tag sang koe been aliah aliah num.
Veng Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang deeh cox sum kah,
kieh koe krax daux laih mai veng kaux cong;
veng in tag sang sum kaux cong hoek aliah tix gliah ra num,
viang mawh nan,
an hoik mawh ka daux bawg yam pa kha rhawm.
26 “Khaix hoik luah ka daux bawg yam in,
simiang pa laug Siyiex,
pui tag sang pauh ka khawm ang tie bax lud;
veng in mai nyiex buan,
cub pui naing cie simiang tix kaux tag sang yieh laux.
Dee si dauh awm rawm ting pa preeng tix hoek yuh nan,
mai viex naing mai krax laux leeing pa hoik tat Siyiex hoek.
27 eSimiang an tag sang rom tix yuh paox pie tau mai pui aliah num,
viang mawh nan,
aliah num hoik luan tix plak,
nawh tom ang kieh pui yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai krax tan tang ceu.
Yam an,
an pa mawh ‘to tiag pa yum koeng pa kieh laux leeingd’ tag sang deuh ot ka nyiex buan ka dee blawn lhaong,
mai deuh ot dee an tom hoik si pup pui pa deuh ka to tiag an,
ka pa hoik tat Siyiex krax laux krax leeing son nawh heue.”
