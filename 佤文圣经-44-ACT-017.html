Ot ka Thie-sa-law-ni-cia
17
Pao-law mai Si-la nied tix hu krax hu kix,
kix laig daux haktiex Ang-fe-phaw-li,
Ya-paw-law-ni-yax,
hoek ka Thie-sa-law-ni-cia,
dee an mawh nyiex sidiex pui Yo-thaix.
2 Pao-law laig ka nyiex sidiex awm been deuh kix,
ka daux loe bawg singaix tuah,
nawh hawt ka gum lox phuk lai sigang khri thung thiang ka pui,
3 krai dee bleeh kham sakse grawng caw Khrit tix sang kham krax yum,
khaix an deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum.
Nawh ah nin:
“Ye-sux pa sidiex aux ka pex in,
mawh Khrit heue.”
4 Ka daux kix koe pui tix blah hoik yum rhawm kah,
mai lih mawh paox pa yuh kaing tau mai Pao-law mai Si-la;
koe pui Si-lax tix blah mai bun miex simiang pa krub Siyiex kawx hoik yum rhawm ka nang heue.
5 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix ka daux haktiex an boh rhawm kah,
kix tom khrawm pui cau nyhau pa ot ka daux laih khankix,
lih mawh cub caw,
mai yuh krax suk sag ka daux veng.
Kix preeng tix laig ka nyiex Yie-soeng,
sang sawg tix tawx Pao-law mai Si-la ka pui pa cau nyhau an kix.
6 Viang mawh nan,
ang yaox,
kix tom reu tix tawx Yie-soeng mai kawn Khrit tang kaux ra loe kaux hoek ka simiang haktiex,
deuh phi map ah nin:
“Pui in kix hoik suk sag yuh kawn pui piang haktiex,
yam in kix hoek ka veng ot ex,
7 Yie-soeng lai rieb kix ot daux nyiex tix heue!
Kix hoik lieh lheu ka gum lox tawm Vawng,
krai kawn koe simiang tang kaux tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ye-sux.”
8 Gum lox in hoik kieh pui hun mai simiang haktiex lawh maw.
9 Simiang haktiex tawx lox nhie Yie-soeng mai pui tang kaux khankix tawx mau sux,
khaix an tom taug ploi kix lih heue.
Ot ka Pi-li-yax
10 Hoek ka daux tix bo,
kawn Khrit khankix tawx Pao-law mai Si-la hu plak Pi-li-yax dum mawx dum an.
Kix hoek dee an,
kix tom laig daux nyiex sidiex Yo-thaix.
11 Pui dee in kawn lheu lhak khaing pui Thie-sa-law-ni-cia.
Kix gaux rhawm tix nghiet ka gum lox pa sidiex,
mai si kaw tix jhak ka phuk lai sigang khri ku ngaix,
son sang kieh tix tawng ka lox pa krai Pao-law mawh ang mawh.
12 Ka daux kix koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm kah;
koe bun miex Si-lax pa koe min ka daux haktiex tom nie,
mai simex Si-lax kawx hoik yum rhawm kah.
13 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yo-thaix pa ot ka haktiex Thie-sa-law-ni-cia,
mhong nang grawng pa ot Pao-law tix sidiex gum lox Siyiex ka Pi-li-yax,
kix tom hoek tix yuh krax pa suk sag ka pui hun dee an.
14 Kawn Khrit khankix tawx Pao-law hu plak hawt rawm sigrit sigra dum mawx dum an;
Si-la mai Thi-maw-thaix kawn ot ka Pi-li-yax.
15 Pui pa hu tawx Pao-law,
kix tawx tom hoek nawh ka Ya-tien,
khaix an deeh ing ka Pi-li-yax,
mai viex gum lox pa krai Pao-law ka kix hoek,
nhie Si-la maix Thi-maw-thaix nied tix si pup nawh dee an heue.
Ot ka Ya-tien
16 Yam ot Pao-law tix krox jun Si-la mai Thi-maw-thaix ka haktiex Ya-tien,
nawh yaox riang rao pa krub pui hoik naog ka daux veng khawm uik,
nawh kied laux rhawm rhi ka tete.
17 Nawh thung thiang ka pui Yo-thaix mai ka pui tang ceu tang cad pa krub ka Siyiex khankix ka daux nyiex sidiex,
mai baox thung thiang ka pui pa yaox tix pa ot ka daux bang ku ngaix.
18 Koe cub caw pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi Yi-pi-cio-lu mai cub caw See-to-yax,
hoek tix thung thiang ka nawh.
Koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Pui pa vu vie in krai lox pie ceu pa tix?”
Koe tix blah ah nin:
“Nawh awm pa krai ka si ngie siyiex cie meung tang meung.”
Kix krai yuh nin hoik mawh kheu pa sidiex Pao-law ka Ye-sux mai ka gum lox buan pa deeh kaoh im nawh.
19 Kix tom viex Pao-law hoek ka bang kum pui Yo-thaix Ya-liie Pa-kux,
mai ah nin:
“Yix chix tix tawng ka gum lox khraox pa hoik krai maix tix blah lie?
20 Koe kaing tix blah yix kied daox deng ka mhong tix,
mai kied simie tawng grawh grawng ceu pa tix.” (
21 Pui Ya-tien mai pui tang meung pa paung ot dee an khawm uik,
hoik muh tix nai bawg yam ka pa krai lao ka kaing ceu pa khraox,
hawt nghiet tix mhong ka gum lox pa krai plak piang heue.)
22 Pao-law cung ka bang kum pui Ya-liie Pa-kux,
mai ah nin:
“Pui Ya-tien khankix eue!
Aux hoik tawng grawng pa kied koe krax gaux rhawm pex ku plak ku vang ka daux been li.
23 Aux hoik lai ling tix hu ka daux veng ku dauh,
mai jhak ka nyiex,
pa krub pex,
yaox khu dee tan ka tix mu,
ka piang hoik tiiem ‘tan tix tawx ka siyiex pa ang tix tawng’.
Yam in pa sang krai aux ka pex,
mawh in pa ang pex tawng kah,
viang mawh nan,
pex krub nawh mawh siyiex
24 aSiyiex cao pa phan pa yuh rao ma haktiex,
mai can ceu pa koe ka daux khawm uik,
mawh cao rao ma haktiex.
Nawh ang paung ot ka daux nyiex buan pa sum pui,
25 mai ang lo ka ceu pa tan lu pui tix ceu kawx;
mawh kheu hoik tawx nawh ju,
bhawm,
mai can ceu pa koe khawm uik ka kawn pui.
26 Nawh phan yuh pui khawm uik khaing pui tix kaux,
mai kieh kix prung prang tix ot ka piang haktiex,
khaix an tat tix deuh jhong khix mee num son kix,
mai paung ot ka mee haktiex.
27 Nawh yuh nin mawh son sang kieh kix sawg ka Siyiex,
phan ang mawh nan,
pon tix yaox nawh ka daux pa pu pi tix sawg.
Tete te lai,
Siyiex ang singai ot khaing ex ku kaux.
28 Koe pui ah nin:
‘Krax ot krax koe ex,
pa chix chuh ex,
pa koe ex hoik mawh ka daux nawh khawm uik.’
Baox awm pui pa chix rhax lai ka daux pex tix kaux kaux ah nin:
‘Ex kawx hoik mawh kawn simex kawn bun nawh.’
29 Kheu hoik mawh ex kawn simex kawn bun nawh,
ex tom caw tix ang keud to kaux Siyiex mawh pa giex pa going,
mawh pa chix teei pui krax chix krax tawng tix yuh kah,
mawh riang rao pa teei mau khri mai simaox saik lih tiag kah.
30 Yam ot kawn pui ka daux pa aw,
Siyiex ang si kaw kah,
viang mawh nan,
yam in nawh tawx lox,
nhie kawn pui piang haktiex tik vud ka krax pa caox pa caig.
31 Mawh kheu hoik tat nawh tix deuh ka singaix sawm,
mai sang laug pui tix kaux lih khaing ka daux nawh,
son sang hoek tix prawng ca piang haktiex ka daux krax pa rauh pa see.
Mawh kheu hoik kieh pui in deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
nawh hoik teei tix tawx sakse in ka kawn pui piang haktiex heue.”
32 Yam hoik mhong kix grawng pa krai Pao-law ka pui yum deeh kaoh im,
koe pui tom nyiah ka nawh;
koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Ex simie tix baox nghiet ka pa krai maix grawng in.”
33 Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law lih hu khaing bang kum.
34 Koe pui tix blah lih mawh pui pa yuh kaing tau mai nawh,
mawh kawn Khrit;
ka daux kix koe pui bang kum Ya-liie Pa-kux pa kaux Du-ni-sio,
baox koe ceu kaux tang ceu mawh bun miex Ta-ma-li,
mai pui tang kaux khankix tix blah heue.
