Ye-sux ploi kawn cau kao ra kaux hu
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.5–15; Lai Ma-kheu 6.7–13)
9
Ye-sux khrawm kawn cau tix kao ra kaux,
mai tawx akhuang pa grheu phi mai pa pai jhawm saux moi ka kix,
2 khaix an ploi kix hu sidiex gum lox buan meung Siyiex,
mai baox pai ka pui pa koe jhawm saux moi.
3 aNawh krai ka pui gau ah nin:
“Yam lawng pex krax ang siao viex ceu tid tix:
paw viex khaox gix,
paw viex bau,
paw viex priex preem,
phan ang mawh nan mau,
paw viex nang ka kraung ceep plak daux ra pheen.
4 Yam hoek pex haktiex tix dauh dauh,
nyiex pui mawx cu tix rieb ka pex,
pex tom paung ot nyiex an,
tom hoek lih hu pex khaing haktiex an.
5 Phan si pup ka pui pa ang rieb ka pex,
yam lih hu pex khaing veng khaing yaong an,
phawng tiex pa kawb kiiep pex kawx khruah tix tik,
in mawh jhi grawng pa hoik ne raw ka kix heue.”
6 Pui gau lih hu,
mai sidiex lox buan,
pai jhawm saux pui tauh hu ku nyiex ku yaong.
Si-luix suk sag rhawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.1–12; Lai Ma-kheu 6.14–29)
7 bSimiang Si-luix haktiex Cia-li-lix mhong grawng kaing pa lih tix mawh khawm uik,
daux rhawm nawh kied suk sag kah,
mawh kheu koe pui ah nin:
“Yaw-hang cao cub com pa kaoh im heue.”
8 Koe nang pui pa ah mawh Yi-li-yax pa lih hoek;
mai baox koe nang pui pa ah mawh cao jhai dix dix tix kaux pa kaoh im heue.
9 Si-luix ah nin:
“Aux hoik kid kaing Yaw-hang,
viang mawh nan,
pui in mawh pui mawx lie?
Aux lai mhong tom nie ka grawng kaing pa dex dawt tau mai nawh yuh ka mawx.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh simie tix yaox ka Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux kieh pui phuan rheng kaux ih sak
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.13–21; Lai Ma-kheu 6.30–44; Lai Yaw-hang 6.1–13)
10 Kawn cau khankix deeh hoek,
mai krai can kaing pa hoik yuh tix ka Ye-sux khawm uik.
Ye-sux viex kix hu,
mai briex hu ka veng pa kok mawh Paw-sai-tax.
11 Yam tawng pui kah,
mai kix tom hawt ka nawh.
Ye-sux gaux rhawm tix rieb ka kix,
mai kah kix mhong ka grawng kaing siawp sibrawm pa dex mai Siyiex,
mai baox pai ka pui pa sang simie tix moi jhawm saux khankix heue.
12 Yam hoik laig singaix khaing gawng,
kawn cau kao ra kaux hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Nhie pui hun tang prung prang tix hu,
kieh kix tang hu sawg tix ih priex preem ka yaong pa dex dee an,
mai paung ot dee an,
mawh kheu dee in hoik mawh dee kied kroh kram heue.”
13 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tawx kix ih heue!”
Kix ah nin:
“Ex koe ngie khao mun phuan lon mai kax ra mu khaing heue.
Khex sang nhie maix ex hu liag ka priex preem tawx pui hun in kix ih lie?” (
14 Kaux pui simex koe been phuan rheng kaux.)
Ye-sux ah nin ka pui gau tix:
“Nhie pui hun ngawm cub mawx cub an,
tix cub tix phuan kaux.”
15 Pui gau hawt tix yuh awm lox nawh,
mai kieh pui hun ngawm khawm uik.
16 Ye-sux teei ka khao mun phuan lon mai kax ra mu,
maong ka rao ma,
yaog yaw Siyiex,
khaix an si vit ka khao mun,
tawx ka pui gau tix,
pui gau tom guah tix tawx ka pui hun.
17 Pui hun ih ka khawm uik,
khaix an hoik ih sak khawm uik.
Pui gau khrawm pa max pa deuk khankix,
mai naog kao ra khrawng.
Pi-teux tawng Ye-sux mawh Khrit
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.13–19; Lai Ma-kheu 8.27–29)
18 Koe tix bawg,
Ye-sux rawg buan nong nong tix,
pui gau nawh khankix hoek ka nawh.
Nawh chawk ka kix ah nin:
“Pui ah mawh aux pui mawx lie?”
19 cKix pawk ah nin:
“Tix blah ah mawh maix Yaw-hang cao cub com;
tix blah ah mawh maix Yi-li-yax;
mai tix blah ah mawh maix cao jhai tix kaux dix dix pa kaoh im heue.”
20 dYe-sux chawk kix ah nin:
“Pex lie?
Pex ah mawh aux mawx lie?”
Pi-teux pawk ah nin:
“Maix mawh Khrit pa ap Siyiex heue.”
Ye-sux grox krai grawng sang kham to kaux tix krax kha rhawm mai krax yum
(Lai Ma-thaix 16.20–28; Lai Ma-kheu 8.30—9.1)
21 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux bre tix tawx lox ang cu kix krai kaing in ka pui tix kaux kawx.
22 Khaix an nawh baox ah nin:
“Kawn kawn pui tag sang kham krax kha rhawm tom nie,
mai kuad song,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri tag sang tik vud ka nawh,
pui tag sang pauh ka nawh,
ka daux loe singaix,
nawh tag sang deeh kaoh im heue.”
23 eYe-sux baox ah nin ka pui hun:
“Phan koe pui sang hawt khaix aux,
tag caw tix tawx ka to kaux tix,
mai klawm ka khaox lhak tix hawt khaix aux.
24 fMawh kheu pui pa keud tix sang taoh ka ju tix,
tag sang tieh tix som ju;
pui pa som ju son aux,
tag sang tieh tix pon ju.
25 Pui tix kaux kaux phan hoik pon ka haktiex khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
hoik tik ka to kaux tix,
phan ang mawh nan,
plud ka ju to kaux tix,
koe dee mhawm ceu pa tix lie?
Ang koe heue!
26 Phan koe pui pa een aux mai krax pa sidiex gau aux mawh pa kaig tua,
yam lih hoek kawn kawn pui tau mai keeing tix,
tau mai kawn cau Siyiex khankix ka daux min plak ngai,
tag sang kieh nawh kaig tua nang heue.
27 Aux bre tix krai ka pex,
pui pa cung dee in,
koe tix blah sivoe sang yum,
kix tag sang yaox pa lih siawp sibrawm Siyiex tix mawh heue.”
Tieh loh ka greeh grang pak peung tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 17.1–8; Lai Ma-kheu 9.2–8)
28 gKhaix hoik krai ka lox in kix been daix singaix,
Ye-sux viex Pi-teux mai Yaw-hang mai Ya-keux haok rawg buan ka gawng.
29 Yam rawg Ye-sux buan,
pak peung nawh hoik tieh loh tix;
kraung ceep nawh kawx hoik tieh tix lih paing toh riang.
30 Dum mawx dum an,
koe pui ra kaux lih hoek tix si kah tau mai nawh,
pui in kiex mawh Maw-si mai Yi-li-yax.
31 Kiex lih hoek ka daux min plak ngai,
mai si kah tau mai Ye-sux ka Yie-lu-sa-leung grawng tag sang teei nawh yum,
son sang pon yuh gum lox tawx Siyiex si dauh.
32 Pi-teux mai paox nawh khankix hoik laig it khawm uik;
yam haing kix,
kix yaox min plak ngai Ye-sux khawm pui pa cung tau mai nawh ra kaux.
33 Yam sang lih hu pui an kix khaing Ye-sux,
Pi-teux krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
ex ot dee in kied mhawm heue!
Kieh ex sum nyiex gox bang loe lhang,
tix lhang mawh cie maix,
tix lhang mawh cie Maw-si,
tix lhang mawh cie Yi-li-yax heue.”
(Nawh ang tawng pa krai nawh ceu pa tix heue.)
34 Yam kawn si kah nawh,
koe lon pai awm tix lon lih hoek,
tawb kheem ka kix,
yam gah pai awm an tix lih dex,
pui gau hoik kied lhat.
35 hDum mawx dum an,
koe lox lih hoek khaing pai awm ah nin:
“In mawh kawn simex aux,
mawh pa hoik laug aux.
Pex tag caw tix nghiet ka nawh heue!”
36 Khaix hoik naox lox an tix,
mawh ngie Ye-sux pa ot dee an tix kaux khaing.
Ka daux singaix yam an,
pui gau ang krai lih ka grawng kaing an,
mai ang krai ka pui tix kaux kawx ka grawng kaing pa hoik yaox kix heue.
Pai moi ka kawn nyawm pa laig phi daux
(Lai Ma-thaix 17.14–18; Lai Ma-kheu 9.14–27)
37 Ka daux singaix khaix,
yam lih hoek kix khaing piang gawng,
koe cub pui hun hoek rieb ka Ye-sux.
38 Ka daux pui hun koe pui kok yo ah nin:
“Sara,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka kawn simex aux liak,
nawh mawh kawn simex aux pa koe tix kaux khaing heue!
39 Khuan phi caox hoek tix yuh ka nawh jheeng jheeng,
mai kieh nawh riag yo dum mawx dum an,
mai baox kieh nawh rak rawn,
kieh bai lih khaing daux jheeh nawh;
khuan phi caox an hoik yuh ka nawh ang jud,
mai ang cu tix naox yuh ka nawh heue!
40 Aux khiox khaw ka pui gau maix grheu ka khuan phi in,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang pon grheu ka heue.”
41 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex pa mawh pui baux ju in kied ang koe krax yum rhawm,
kied caox caig heue!
Aux kawn sang ot mai pex dee in miex glang lie?
Kawn sang gun yeung ka pex miex glang lie?”
Khaix an nawh krai ka pui an ah nin:
“Viex kawn simex maix hoek dee in heue.”
42 Yam hoik hoek kawm simex an dee an,
phi an kieh nawh goh piang tiex,
mai kieh nawh rak rawn tix.
Ye-sux ne raw ka khuan pa seeing brai an,
pai ka kawn nyawm an moi,
mai tawx nawh ka keeing nawh.
43 Pui hun kied daox deng ka siawp sibrawm pa ting Siyiex heue.
Ye-sux baox grox krai ka grawng pa sang yum tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 17.22, 23; Lai Ma-kheu 9.30–32)
Yam kawn daox deng pui hun ka grawng kaing pa hoik yuh Ye-sux khawm uik, Ye-sux baox krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
44 “Paw vi piawm ka gum lox pa krai aux ka pex:
kawn kawn pui tag sang ap tix tawx ka daux tawm taix kawn pui heue.”
45 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox in;
mawh kheu dee bleeh kied raux blawn ang sidawng,
mai kix ang pon tawng.
Kix kawx ang pon chawk nawh grawh grawng kaing in mawh ceu pa tix heue.
Mawh pui mawx pa ting
(Lai Ma-thaix 18.1–5; Lai Ma-kheu 9.33–37)
46 iPui gau thung thiang paox tix ka daux kix mawh mawx pa blawn ting.
47 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka ceu pa keud kix ceu pa tix,
nawh tom kok kawn nyawm tix kaux hoek,
mai kieh nawh cung ka praok tix,
48 jkhaix an ah nin ka kix:
“Pui pa rieb ka kawn nyawm in son ceu kaux aux,
mai hoik mawh pa rieb ka aux.
Pui pa rieb ka aux,
hoik mawh pui pa rieb ka kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
Ka daux pex pui mawx mawh pa blawn iak khaing paox tix,
pui an tag sang mawh pa blawn ting khaing paox tix heue.”
Pa tieb tie mai pa khaw rhawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 9.38)
49 Yaw-hang ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Sara,
yix yaox pui pa grheu phi lih khaing ka daux ceu kaux maix,
yix tom tieb tie ka nawh,
mawh kheu ang nawh mawh paox pie mai ex pa hawt ka maix heue.”
50 Ye-sux tom ah nin ka nawh mai ka pui gau tang kaux:
“Paw vi tieb tie ka nawh,
mawh kheu pui pa ang tieb tie ka pex hoik mawh pa khaw rhawm ka pex heue.”
Nyiex yaong pa ang rieb ka Ye-sux
51 Bawg yam pa rieb Ye-sux haok ka rao ma hoik dex sang hoek,
nawh but rhawm tix veeh tix hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
52 mawh kheu nan nawh ploi pui ran hu sivoe.
Kix hoek ka nyiex yaong Sa-ma-li-yax,
son sang rien khre ka son Ye-sux khawm uik.
53 Viang mawh nan,
pui haktiex dee an ang rieb ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik veeh nawh tix hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
54 lPui gau nawh Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang yaox grawng in,
tom ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
maix sang nhie yix kok yo k ngu khaing rao ma hoek tix gleeing ka kix lie?”
55 Ye-sux kling to kaux tix hoek,
mai ne raw ka kixm.
56 Kix tom hu ka nyiex yaong tang dauh heue.
Pui pa sang hawt ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 8.9–22)
57 Ka daux krax hu kix koe pui tix kaux krai ka Ye-sux ah nin “Maix hu dee mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang hawt ka maix heue.”
58 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Sox praix koe daux,
sim saik koe mheem,
viang mawh nan,
pie dee deuh kawn kawn pui kaing tix piang ang koe heue.”
59 Nawh tom krai ka pui an tix kaux ah nin:
“Hawt ka aux heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
pui an ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
kieh aux ran ing si paung ka keeing tix heue.”
60 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kieh pui yum tang hu si paung ka pui yum heue!
Phan mawx maix,
tag caw tix hu sidiex ka gum lox buan meung Siyiex heue.”
61 nBaox koe tix kaux pa krai ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux sang hawt ka maix,
viang mawh nan,
kieh aux ran deeh ing krai pui nyiex pui ma tix mhong ka ha!”
62 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa thai ma pa jhak ka plak khaix tix ang jud,
ang koe ceu ka meung Siyiex heue!”
