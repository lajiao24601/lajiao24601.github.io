Lox rhax Maw-si
15
aMaw-si mai pui Yi-seu-liex ah lai rhax ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Aux tag ah lox rhax ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik puing pex nawh ka daux pa ting min plak ngai;
nawh hoik tik brung naing mai pui naing brug brung ka daux rawm sigrit sigra.
2 bSimiang Siyiex mawh riang rao pa kia ngien ka aux;
nawh taoh ka aux.
Nawh mawh Siyiex aux,
mai aux tag yaog yaw ka nawh;
nawh mawh Siyiex keeing aux,
mai aux tag yaog nawh.
3 Simiang Siyiex mawh pui naing;
Yie-heu-hua mawh ceu kaux Nawh.
 
4 Nawh hoik kieh pui naing Ai-cix mai ling law naing kix com greem rawm sigrit sigra,
hoik kieh simiang naing pa vao yum ka daux Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
5 Rawm raux hoik kheem ka kix;
kix hoik com awm lon simaox greem rawm.
 
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
taix khawn dawm maix hoik mawh pa ting riang rao;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik pon yuh pui pa koeng laux leeing.
7 Pa kied ting sibrawm maix hoik pex pon ka pui pa koeng;
krax pa song rhawm maix hoik yuh kix laux,
awm ngu pa gleeing ka khlawp nghox ca heue.
8 Maix phlu rawm sigrit sigra ka bhawm tix,
rawm khankix tom khrawm paox tix tau,
mai jung tix rauh see awm ding;
greem rawm tieh tix lih mawh tiex kroh.
9 Pui koeng ah nin:
aux tag sang hawt leue ka kix,
mai giex ka kix;
aux tag sang guah ka sin ngao kix,
mai ca nieb tud rhim kah.
Aux tag teei vaig gawn tix yuh kix laux leeing.
10 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix kok phlu ngie bhawm tix tix gum khaing,
mai kieh rawm sigrit sigra hoik kheem ka pui Ai-cix khankix yum.
Kix com greem rawm awm si vun.
 
11 Simiang Siyiex eue!
Ka daux siyiex khankix mawh mawx pa awm maix lie?
Mawh mawx pa awm maix koe siawp sibrawm pa kied singax singeh?
Mawh mawx pa pon yuh mhai pa daox pa am awm maix?
12 Maix nyeu taix khawn dawm tix,
haktiex hoik bleet ka pui pa koeng.
13 Maix teei krax caox rhawm tix,
pa ang chix grai grup duk thung ka pui pa hoik taoh maix ka khankix;
maix teei sibrawm pa ting riang tix,
tok kix hu ka haktiex pa singax singeh.
14 Meung khawm uik tag mhong,
mai lhat graung kah;
krax lhat graung tag kheem ka piang pui Fe-li-see.
15 Simiang pui Yi-tong lhat graung;
pui Maw-ya pa ting riang ting rao yaoh si yaung;
pui pa ot haktiex Cia-nang laux rhawm.
16 Krax lhat mai krax yaoh hoek piang kix.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
kix yaox ka pa ting riang maix;
kix hoik lhat tom ang lai pon chuh,
krox tom hoek yam taoh maix pui cie tix lih khaing yuh kaing siliex,
mai hu ka plak sivoe ngai kix.
17 Maix viex kix laig ka gawng maix,
mai gau kix dee an heue;
dee an mawh dee hoik laug maix,
Simiang Siyiex tix ot,
hoik sum nyiex pa singax singeh ka heue.
18 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix tag yuh simiang leeing ju leeing phan!
Lox rhax Me-li-ang
19 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hu ka piang tiex kroh,
viang mawh nan,
yam laig ling law naing,
brung naing,
mai pui naing brug brung Ai-cix khankix ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
Simiang Siyiex deeh kieh rawm sigrit sigra kleuk paox,
mai kheem kix yum heue.
20 Pux ke bun Ya-loeng pa mawh Me-li-ang cao jhai,
giex kraung daux taix tix,
mai can bun khawm uik lih hu hawt khaix nawh,
tauh ka kraung,
mai graoh.
21 Me-li-ang rhax ah nin tau mai kix:
Rhax lai ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik puing pex nawh ka daux krax pa koe min plak ngai;
nawh hoik teei tik brung mai pui naing brug brung ka daux rawm sigrit sigra heue.
Rawm song
22 Khaix an,
Maw-si viex pui Yi-seu-liex hu khaing Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
hoek ka daux tiag Su-eux,
loe ngaix ka daux tiag,
ang yaox tix nyaux ka rawm.
23 Kix hoek ka Ma-lax;
rawm pa koe tan song,
kix ang pon nyaux.
Mawh kheu nan,
dee an tom tag kok mawh Ma-laxc.
24 Pui khankix salao ka Maw-si,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Ex sang nyaux pa tix lie?”
25 Maw-si khiox khaw ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex;
mai Simiang Siyiex jhi nawh yaox khaox tix gawng.
Nawh tik khaox an ka daux rawm,
rawm tom tag chix tix nyaux ka heue.
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox tat jhawm prawng ka kix tan,
mai cheuk tix yaox ka kix tan.
26 Nawh ah nin:
“Phan pex cu nghiet ka lox aux,
mai yuh pa rauh see sivoe ngai aux,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tawm jhawm krai aux,
aux tag ang lai kieh jhawm saux pa ciyang ka piang pui Ai-cix ciyang ka pex.
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex cao pa pai jhawm saux pex moi heue.”
27 Khaix an kix hoek ka Yi-ling;
dee an koe rawm maw kao ra daux mai khaox jhie yaong atigliah num.
Kix tom sum bang ot ka praok rawm tan heue.
