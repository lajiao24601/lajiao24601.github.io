Lai Ma-kheu
Krai dawt
Lai Ma-kheu tiiem gum lox grawng pa dex mai Ye-sux Khrit kawn simex Siyiex. Krai ex mhong, Ye-sux hoik yuh pui yaox lih mai kied koe siawp sibrawm. Siawp sibrawm nawh hoik pon tix yaox ka daux pa kaoh khaing krax pa sidiex gau nawh, pa hoik dig ka riang rao khuan phi caox mai ka akhuang pa pluat ploi ka phi map. Ye-sux tang kok tix mawh “Kawn kawn pui”; kaing ting grawng hoek nawh ka piang haktiex mawh son sang yuh son kawn pui, khaix an tawx ju to kaux tix son sang taoh ka kawn pui heue.
Lai Ma-kheu teei gawng meug pa singax tiiem lao ka grawng kaing pa yuh Ye-sux, ang kieh mawh lox pa krai mai krax pa sidiex mawh pa ting ceu, mai hoik kieh kaing yuh tix mawh pa ting ceu. Cao pa tiiem ran teei lox pa dawt krai lao grawng pa sidiex gau Yaw-hang cao cub com, khawm grawng pa com Ye-sux rawm mai pa kham krax nyaw krax glaw, khaix an krai grawng pa pai Ye-sux jhawm saux moi mai pa sidiex gau nawh, mai baox krai lao ka grawng pa hoik tawng yung pui gau Ye-sux ka nawh yuh ka mawx hu si nyek si nyek, viang mawh nan, riang rao pa tieb tie ka nawh kawx hoik ting hu yuh ka mawx. Dee nghoik simin ting ra loe mu, hoik tiiem ka grawng kaing pa nghoik Ye-sux tix yuh ka piang haktiex ka daux tix vang tuah, nie mawh grawh grawng pa tawk nawh ka piang khaox lhak mai pa kaoh im nawh heue.
Koe cao tiiem lai tix blah een mawh nin, phuk lai in gum lox pa nghoik ra baux [teei mhai in krai sidawng] ang mawh pa kaoh khaing khaix taix Ma-kheu, mai hoik mawh cao pa tiiem tang kaux pa baox piang heue.
Krai kaing
1. Yam jauh gum lox buan tix (1.1–13)
2. Kaing pa yuh Ye-sux yam ot ka haktiex Cia-li-lix (1.14–9.50)
3. Jauh ka Cia-li-lix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung (10.1–52)
4. Pa nghoik tix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ka daux tix vang tuah mai ka baoh haktiex pa dex tan (11.1–15.47)
5. Pa kaoh im Ye-sux (16.1–8)
6. Pa kaoh im Simiang Ye-sux pa jhi tix lih mai pa haok rao ma (16.9–20)
