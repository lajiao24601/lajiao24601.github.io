Nyaox moi siang raoh
19
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng,
2 nhie pui Yi-seu-liex hawt been in plak greem.
Caw tix viex moi siang raoh pa loig,
pa ang nyang yuh kaing tix mu hoek,
3 tawx ka cao giex kaing li Yi-li-ya-sa,
khaix an tuk moi siang raoh an hu plak praix bang,
pauh sivoe nawh.
4 Yi-li-ya-sa cux nham moi in ka giien taix tix,
mai phruah plak nyiex gox buan man aliah bawg.
5 Moi siang an khawm mu,
khawm hak,
nex,
nham,
kraung vaig,
tag gleeing sivoe cao giex kaing li.
6 Khaix an,
cao giex kaing li teei khaox si oe bhawng,
rao rib im krim tix gawng,
mai man raoh rawt tix nyaing tik daux ngu.
7 Khaix an,
nawh tag si daux kraung tix,
mai heem tix ka rawm,
tom tag chix tix laig ka daux bang;
viang mawh nan,
tom hoek leeing bo,
nawh ang singax ka daux been.
8 Pui pa gleeing moi siang an tag caw tix si daux kraung tix,
heem tix ka rawm;
awm paox,
tom hoek pon bo,
nawh ang singax ka daux been.
9 aKhaix an pui pa singax singeh ka daux been tag teei ka nyaox moi siang an,
mai deuh ka plak praix bang dee singax singeh.
Nyaox in deuh ot dee an,
pui Yi-seu-liex siao son sang kieh rawm seeing an singax.
Yuh been in mawh son sang tik ka phi map.
10 Pui pa khrawm nyaox moi siang an,
tag si daux kraung tix,
tom hoek plak bo,
nawh ang singax.
Been in tag mawh son pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui tang ceu pa ot daux kix leeing ju heue.
Teeh ka to kaux pa yum
11 Pui pa teeh to kaux pa yum tag ang singax ka daux been aliah singaix.
12 Nawh tag si ghrah to kaux tix ka rawm singax daux loe singaix mai daux aliah singaix.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom singax.
Phan nawh ang si ghrah to kaux tix ka daux loe singaix mai ka daux aliah singaix,
nawh tag ang singax singeh.
13 Pui pa teeh ka to kaux yum mai ang si ghrah to kaux tix,
tag mawh pa ang singax singeh,
mawh kheu ang nawh baox phruah rawm pa kieh singax ka to kaux tix.
Nawh seeing brai yuh nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang teei tix tik nawh khaing ka daux ceu pui Siyiex.
14 Koe pui yum daux nyiex bang,
pui pa ot ka daux bang yam an mai pui pa laig ka daux nyiex bang tag ang singax aliah singaix.
15 Kraung siao pa ang koe dawb mai aw khankix ka daux nyiex bang tag ang singax singeh nang.
16 Koe pui teeh ka to kaux yum plak praix mai pui pa lawk yum,
phan ang mawh nan,
teeh ka si-ang kaing pui yum mai si meeig,
nawh ang singax singeh aliah singaix heue.
17 Pex caw tix teei nyaox moi siang raoh,
pa mawh pa hoik gleeing son sang siao ka pa tik phi map,
deuh ka daux aw,
sau rawm singax daux,
son sang tik pa seeing brai kix.
18 Yam jauh,
caw tix mawh pui pa singax singeh tix kaux,
pa teei rao rib im krim tix gawng,
cub ka daux rawm,
phruah ka piang nyiex gox bang,
ka piang kraung khrai pa ot daux bang khawm uik,
mai ka piang pui pa ot tan.
Khaix an,
pui pa singax singeh an tag phruah rawm ka piang pui pa teeh ka si-ang kaing pui yum,
to kaux yum mai si meeig.
19 Ka daux loe singaix mai daux aliah singaix,
pui pa singax singeh an tag phruah rawm piang pui pa ang singax singeh.
Daux aliah ngaix,
nawh tag singax yuh pui an;
pui an tag si daux kraung tix,
heem tix ka rawm,
khaix hoik yuh nin,
krox singaix klip,
nawh tag singax singeh heue.
20 Koe pui ang singax singeh daux been mai ang si ghrah to kaux tix,
nawh kawn ang nyang singax singeh,
mawh kheu ang nawh nyang phruah rawm pa kieh singax singeh an ka to kaux tix.
Nawh seeing brai yuh nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang teei tix tik nawh khaing pui cie tix.
21 Pex tix ju hoek tix ju priex tix hawt ka been in.
Pui pa phruah rawm pa kieh singax in kawx tag si daux kraung tix nang;
can pui pa theeh ka rawm an ang singax tom hoek pon bo.
22 Pui pa ang singax singeh,
kraung pa theeh nawh kawx mawh pa ang singax;
pui pa teeh ka kraung in kix,
ang singax tom hoek pon bo heue.
