Lai A-maw-see
1
aYam mawh Wu-si-ya simiang Yo-tax,
kawn simex Yaw-hua-see pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang simiang Yi-seu-liex ka daux ra baux bawg yam,
sivoe sang tok kla reem ting ra num,
Siyiex plak ji kaing pa dex pa dawt mai Yi-seu-liex ka A-maw-see,
pa mawh pui liang yung ka yaong Thi-keu-yax.
Plak greem mawh pa tiiem ka lox A-maw-see heue.
2 bA-maw-see ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex ploi lox song rhawm pa ting lih khaing Shi-ang,
ploi lox si num tuh sah lih khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Rib riem ka daux pak rib hoik kroh;
rib klai pa koe ka gawng Cia-mix hoik tieh tix lheung heue.
Siyiex prawng ca meung pa ot sivie siveng Yi-seu-liex
Grawng Shui-li-yax
3 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Ta-ma-see-keux ca baox yuh phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
Kix hoik teei krax ciyang pa kin yuh ka pui Ci-liex.
4 Mawh kheu nan aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing ka nyiex haw simiang Ha-shiiex,
mai yuh laux leeing ka nyiex haw simiang Piien Ha-tax.
5 Aux tag sang yuh laux leeing ka siviex veng Ta-ma-see-keux,
mai pauh ka pui pa ot ka dee lung Ya-weung mai ka simiang Paw Yi-tiien.
Tag sang giex viex pui Shui-li-yax hu ka haktiex Cie-eux.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Grawng pui Fe-li-see
6 dSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Cia-sa ca baox yuh ka phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix tete.
Kix hoik giex laung ka pui meung pa ot sivie siveng kix,
mai hoik coh pui Yi-tong mawh siliex cawng.
7 Mawh kheu nan aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing ka guh veng Cia-sa,
mai yuh laux leeing ka nyiex haw nawh.
8 Aux tag sang pauh ka pui pa ot haktiex Ya-seu-thu mai ka simiang pui Ya-seu-ci-loeng.
Aux tag sang ciyang ka veng Yi-keu-loeng;
can pui Fe-li-see pa ot daux veng tag sang yum khawm uik.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Grawng Thai-eux
9 eSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Thai-eux ca kawn baox yuh phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix tete.
Kix ang priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tau mai paox grawm tix,
mai tieh tix grheu pui meung pa ot sivie siveng kix hu mawh siliex cawng pui Yi-tong.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing guh veng Thai-eux,
mai yuh laux leeing ka nyiex haw nawh heue.”
Grawng Yi-tong
11 fSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-tong ca kawn baox yuh phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
Kix ang koe krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi,
mai teei vaig gawn pauh ka pui nyiex kix Yi-seu-liex.
Krax pa caox caig kix ang chix naox tix leeing ju;
krax pa song rhawm kix ang chix grai leeing ju.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing veng Thi-mang,
mai yuh laux leeing ka nyiex haw simiang Paw-see-la heue.”
Grawng Ya-meung
13 gSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Ya-meung ca kawn baox yuh ka phi map,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix.
Son sang nih vieh ka haktiex,
mai kix hoik pih ka vaig bun nghe haktiex Ci-liex.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang kieh ngu gleeing ka guh veng La-pa,
mai yuh laux leeing ka nyiex haw simiang kix.
Yam taoh naing,
tag sang mhong ka lox pauh pui paox tix,
lox lauh pui paox tix awm lox bhaung ting lhiex ting yuh nan.
15 Vawng mai cao kaing kix tag sang vuk vied vuk viang khawm uik.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
