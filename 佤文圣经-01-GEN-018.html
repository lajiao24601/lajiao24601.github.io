Ap tix kieh Ya-paw-la-hang koe kawn simex
18
Khaix an Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka Ya-paw-la-hang ka daux tiag khaox yang yit pa singax singeh Mang-lix,
yam ngawm nawh ka diang siviex nyiex gox man,
bawg yam hax koek ka daux singaix an heue.
2 aNawh yaog ngai tix mai yaox pui loe kaux pa cung sivoe ngai nawh tan.
Yam yaox nawh kix,
nawh to hu khaing diang siviex nyiex gox man,
mai hu rieb ka kix,
mai gup ka piang tiex,
3 ah nin ka kix:
“Simiang aux eue,
khiox khaw laux lee maix tin tix vud,
kieh aux khre ka maix ha,
koi hu khaix.
4 Aux viex rawm mai khoek caong pex,
khiox khaw laux lee greem khaox ha.
5 Aux hu rien khre pex ih khao mu ceeix,
khaix ih pex kah,
tom koe riang nied hu krax.
Pex hoek ka nyiex ma aux,
khiox khaw rieb krax pa rien khre aux.”
Kix ah nin:
“Ting buan son heue!”
6 Ya-paw-la-hang nied tix laig daux nyiex gox man,
ka Sa-la mai ah nin:
“Nied maix teei mien phawng nghox ca pa blawn mhawm,
yuh khao mun kah.”
7 Khaix an,
nawh to hu ka cub khawng moi,
mai laug kawn moi iak pa kluing tix mu mai tawx ka siliex tix,
son sang nied tix khre priex.
8 Nawh teei bee mai rawm teeh tau mai nex kawn moi pa hoik khre nawh hoek ka sivoe kix.
Nawh cung greem khaox dex kix,
kix tom ih kah.
9 Kix ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Moeng maix Sa-la ot dee mawx?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Ot daux nyiex gox man heue.”
10 bKa daux kix ah nin tix kaux “Hoek num khaix bawg yam in,
aux tag sang deeh hoek,
moeng maix Sa-la tag keh kawn simex tix kaux heue.”
Sa-la nghiet lox kix ka diang siviex nyiex gox man plak khaix kix.
11 Yam an,
Ya-paw-la-hang mai Sa-la hoik kuad,
Sa-la hoik hit nham seeing tom daux yam an.
12 cMawh kheu nan,
Sa-la briex nyiah ka daux rhawm tix mai ah nin:
“Kheu hoik riex ju aux,
mex aux kawx hoik kuad;
aux tag sang pon krax pa nyawm rhawm chix keh kawn yuh ka mawx?”
13 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Sa-la briex nyiah jao pa tix,
ah nin daux rhawm tix:
‘Aux hoik kuad yuh nin,
mai sang chix koe kawn lie?’
14 dPa ang Simiang Siyiex pon yuh ceu pa nu khankix khex kawn koe tix ceu ceu lie?
Hoek num khaix bawg yam in,
aux tag sang deeh hoek;
Sa-la tag keh kawn simex tix kaux heue.”
15 Sa-la hoik lhat graung,
thiang ah nin:
“Aux ang nyiah heue.”
Nawh ah nin:
“Mawh,
maix hoik nyiah tete heue.”
Ya-paw-la-hang khiox khaw son veng Saw-to-ma
16 Khaix an,
pui in kix yaog tix kaoh khaing tan,
mai hu ka plak dee chix yaox ka Saw-to-ma.
Ya-paw-la-hang kawx lih hu tau mai kix,
mai nawh hu tawx kix tom hoek tix baux krax heue.
17 Simiang Siyiex keud daux rhawm tix:
“Aux tag sang mox kaing pa yuh tix ka Ya-paw-la-hang yuh ka mawx?
18 Doeh ceu cad nawh tag sang mawh meung pa ting siawp sibrawm;
aux sang tawx buan son ka kawn pui ku ceu ka daux maix.e
19 Aux laug ka nawh,
mawh son sang nhie aux nawh sidiex gau ka kawn praix tix,
mai can doeh ceu cad tix,
hawt grawng lawng khaix aux,
mai yuh pa khaw caw,
pa rauh pa see heue.
Yuh nin,
tom tag lih mawh awm lox pa ap aux tix deuh ka Ya-paw-la-hang heue.”
20 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ya-paw-la-hang:
“Mawh kheu hoik mhong aux lox deuh pui phi map ka piang Saw-to-ma,
Eu-maw-la;
kix kied ting phi map.”
21 Aux sang lih jhak ka pa mhong aux deuh phi map mawh ang mawh tete heue.
22 Khaix an pui an kiex gah tix khaing tan,
mai hu plak Saw-to-ma!
Viang mawh nan,
Ya-paw-la-hang kawn ca ot sivoe Simiang Siyiex tan.
23 Ya-paw-la-hang tom hoek dex,
mai ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Maix tag yuh pui ang koe dee lud laux tau mai pui phit lud khankix lie?
24 Phan pui ang koe dee lud koe daux veng an tix phuan kaux,
khex sang yuh maix veng an laux lie?
Ang taoh ka pui rauh see khankix tix phuan kaux pa ot tan mai pluat ploi ka veng an lie?
25 Maix ang doh rom tix pauh pui ang koe dee lud tau mai pui phit lud!
Ang doh chix yuh!
Maix yuh nan,
pui ang koe dee lud tag yaox tix kham krax ciyang tau mai pui phit lud.
Ang doh chix yuh!
Cao pa prawng ca ka haktiex mawh pa khaw caw heue.”
26 Simiang Siyiex pawk ah nin:
“Phan aux yaox pui ang koe dee lud daux veng Saw-to-ma tix phuan kaux,
aux tag sang pluat ploi veng an jao kix heue.”
27 Ya-paw-la-hang deeh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue!
Aux mawh ngie pui pa iak pa lied ka piang haktiex,
rawg maix cu aux viex rhawm ting tix khiox khaw ka maix liak.
28 Ka daux veng,
phan koe tipon phuan kaux pui ang koe dee lud,
kheu kix yawm phuan kaux maix kawn sang yuh veng an laux khawm uik lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox:
“Phan aux kawn yaox pui ang koe dee lud tipon phuan kaux,
aux ka ang doh yuh veng in laux heue.”
29 Ya-paw-la-hang baox ah nin:
“Phan kawn koe ngie tipon kaux lie?”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Phan kawn yaox tipon kaux aux ka ang doh yuh veng in laux heue.”
30 Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex aux eue!
Khiox khaw maix paw vi song rhawm,
aux sang baox chawk ka maix.
Phan kawn koe ngie tix ngoe kaux lie?”
Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan aux kawn yaox tix ngoe kaux,
aux ka ang doh yuh veng in laux heue.”
31 Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue!
Khiox khaw maix cu aux viex rhawm ting baox chawk ka maix liak.
Phan kawn yaox tix nga kaux lie?”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Phan kawn yaox tix nga kaux,
aux ka ang doh yuh veng in laux heue.”
32 Dee nghoik,
Ya-paw-la-hang ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue!
Khiox khaw maix paw vi song rhawm,
cu aux baox rawg tix bawg ha!
Phan kawn yaox kao kaux lie?”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Kao kaux kawx ngie,
aux ka ang doh yuh veng in laux heue.”
33 Khaix hoik si kah Simiang Siyiex tau mai Ya-paw-la-hang nawh tom hu,
mai Ya-paw-la-hang kawx ing ka nyiex tix heue.
