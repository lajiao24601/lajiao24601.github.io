Pai jhawm saux Nai-mang moi
5
aVawng kied muh ka simiang naing Shui-li-yax pa kaux Nai-mang,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh pui naing Shui-li-yax pon tix pex jao nawh.
Nawh hoik mawh pui naing pa kua tix kaux,
viang mawh nan,
nawh pon jhawm saux si pawg.
2 Koe tix bawg,
yam jawm pui Shui-li-yax tix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pon giex ka kawn bun iak pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux;
khaix an kawn bun iak in pon tix mawh bun cau moeng Nai-mang.
3 Koe tix singaix,
nawh ah nin ka bun simiang tix:
“Kieh simiang aux hu kia ka cao jhai an pa paung ot ka Sa-ma-li-yax liak!
Cao jhai an rien tix pon pai moi ka jhawm saux si pawg nawh heue.”
4 Nai-mang hoik tawng kah,
tom hu ka simiang,
mai krai lox pa hoik krai kawn bun an ka simiang.
5 Simiang ah nin:
“Maix hu ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx lai in ka nawh heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Nai-mang yaog tix hu,
viex mau luah loe mheen plah,
khri liah rheng plah mai kraung kao jung.
6 Lai pa viex nawh an ah nin:
“Lai in hoik krai gawng kawn cau pa ting aux Nai-mang.
Aux sang kieh maix pai jhawm saux si pawg nawh moi heue.”
7 Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex phat ka lai in,
yaoh yee riah kraung ceep tix griah,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang pui Shui-li-yax keud aux sang chix pai pui in moi yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh khex een aux mawh Siyiex pa chix yuh pui yum yuh pui im lie?
Nawh yuh nin,
mawh son sang sawg nawh gleeh ka aux heue!”
8 Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex mhong grawng pa hoik riah simiang pui Yi-seu-liex ka kraung ceep tix griah,
nawh tom ploi pui hu ah nin ka simiang:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai laux rhawm yuh nin?
Nhie pui an hoek ka aux dee in,
aux sang kieh nawh tawng koe cao jhai ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex tix kaux heue!”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Nai-mang viex brung,
mai ling brung hoek ka diang siviex nyiex Yi-li-sa.
10 Yi-li-sa nhie siliex tix tix kaux hu krai ka Nai-mang,
mai nhie nawh hu khoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang aliah bawg,
yuh nin,
hak nex nawh tom tag pon moi,
khaix an mai singax singeh heue.
11 Nai-mang deeh hu mai song rhawm tix.
Nawh ah nin:
“Aux ce nawh sang ca lih rieb tix mai rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh,
teei taix tix pu pi tix yaox ka dee saux aux,
mai pai jhawm saux si pawg aux moi heue.
12 Khaix an,
phan ah tete,
rawm klawng Ya-pa-na mai rawm klawng Fa-eu,
ka daux haktiex Ta-ma-see-keux,
khex ang kawn lheu mhawm khaing rawm klawng pa koe daux haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex ku klawng lie?
Khex ang aux chix tix khoek tix dee an,
mai pon tix singax singeh ang mawh lie?”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh song rhawm mai hu phai phai heue.
13 Siliex Nai-mang hoek dex ka nawh,
mai ah nin:
“Sara,
phan cao jhai nhie maix yuh kaing pa nu tix yuh khankix,
maix tag caw hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai nawh;
kheu nan,
yam in nawh nhie ngie maix hu khoek tix khaing,
mai maix tag pon tix singax singeh ang mawh lie?”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Nai-mang hu ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang mai hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai Yi-li-sa,
khoek heem tix daux klawng aliah bawg,
nawh tom pon tix singax singeh heue.
Hak nex nawh deeh mhawm awm kawn nyawm yuh nan.
15 Nawh viex can pui pa hawt khaix tix deeh ing ka Yi-li-sa dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Yam in aux hoik tawng,
ka piang haktiex khawm uik mawh ngie pui Yi-seu-liex pa koe Siyiex khaing,
tang dauh ang koe.
Khiox khaw maix rieb ka khawng phak aux heue.”
16 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa krub aux,
pa im ju leeing ju,
aux ang rieb tix ceu kawx heue.”
Nai-mang kawn ca kieh nawh rieb,
nawh kawn ca ang cu tix rieb kah.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
Nai-mang ah nin:
“Phan maix ang rieb ka khawng phak aux,
phan mawh nan,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux sau law ra mu tang ka tiex glaox haktiex in deeh ing ka nyiexb;
mawh kheu jauh yam in,
lheu khaing Simiang Siyiex,
aux ang lai tan lu Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka siyiex tang kaux.
18 Yam hawt aux khaix simiang tix hoek tix krub ka nyiex vad Ling-meung Siyiex pui Shui-li-yax,
khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex pluat ploi ka phi map aux.
Aux yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex tag chix pluat ploi ka phi map aux heue!”
19 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Maix ing mhawm heue!”
Nai-mang tom ing heue.
Yam taug hu Nai-mang tix baux krax.
20 Siliex Yi-li-sa pa kaux Ci-ha-si,
keud ah nin ka daux rhawm tix:
“Simiang aux ang rieb ka khawng phak Nai-mang tix ceu kawx,
mai hoik ploi nawh hu!
Nawh khaw tix rieb ka khawng phak pui Shui-li-yax tom khaw.
Aux sang tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
aux sang leue ka nawh,
mai mhaing nawh deeh tawx tix ih tix blah heue.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh yaog tix leue ka Nai-mang.
Nai-mang yaox pui leue ka kix,
tom lih khaing ling brung brug tix,
mai rieb nawh ah nin:
“Koe kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
22 Ci-ha-si ah nin:
“Ang mawh tid tix.
Mawh ngie simiang aux pa ploi aux hoek krai ka maix,
koe cao jhai ra kaux pa taug khraox hoek khaing gawng Yi-fa-liien,
nawh simie maix tawx kix cie mau luah loe rheng mai kraung ra jung heue.”
23 Nai-mang ah nin:
“Teei viex mau luah liah rheng ing heue.”
Nawh baox kawn ca kieh nawh rieb,
khaix an mai sau mau ka daux chee ra mu,
khawm kraung ceep ra jung,
tawx ka siliex tix,
nhie kix yaog tix vie hu sivoe Ci-ha-si.
24 Yam hoik hoek kix ka gawng iak ot Yi-li-sa,
Ci-ha-si viex ka chee an khawm ra mu laig daux nyiex,
khaix an nhie kawn cau Nai-mang deeh ing.
25 Ci-ha-si laig daux nyiex;
Yi-li-sa chawk nawh ah nin:
“Hu maix dee mawx?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Aux ang bax hu dee mawx tix heue.”
26 Viang mawh nan,
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Yam lih pui an khaing ling brung brug tix rieb maix,
rhawm aux kawx khex ang ot dee an lie?
In ang mawh bawg yam rieb ka mau,
kraung ceep,
si peem sime,
si peem plix siiet,
phung yung,
phung moi,
mai siliex pui heue!
27 Yam in,
jhawm saux si pawg Nai-mang tag sang lih koe ka to kaux maix;
maix mai doeh ceu cad maix tag sang pon ka jhawm saux in leeing ju heue.”
Yam lih hu Ci-ha-si,
nawh pon ka jhawm saux si pawg,
mai hak nex nawh hoik paing awm rhax yuh nan heue.
