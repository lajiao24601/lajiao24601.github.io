13
“Ka daux pex phan koe pui pa krai mawh tix cao jhai,
phan ang mawh nan,
pa chix kah dee bleeh ca maox tix kaux kaux,
mai yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
2 pie lhien pex,
kieh pex hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux pa ang tix sax krub,
viang mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh nawh khankix hoik lih tix mawh kawx,
3 pex kawx ang pon tix nghiet ka lox nawh.
Kheu mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
siao nawh cheuk tix yaox ka pex,
son sang tawng muh ang pex muh ka nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
4 Pex tag caw tix hawt grawng lawng khaix Simiang Siyiex,
mai lhat graung yung yi ka nawh;
pex caw tix nghiet ka lox nawh,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap nawh;
pex yuh kaing jhaw nawh,
mai hawt nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
5 Phan mawh cao jhai mai cao pa chix krai dee bleeh ca maox an,
tag caw tix pauh yum;
mawh kheu hoik cheeik nawh pex lieh lheu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex pa hoik viex pex lih khaing daux Ai-cix,
pa hoik taoh pex khaing daux yuh pex siliex.
Pui in kix hoik mawh pui pa caox pa caig;
nawh sang nyaw glaw pex lud loe khaing daux krax pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Pex tag caw tix pauh nawh yum,
mai teei tix tik kaing ceu pa caox pa caig ceu in khaing daux si nax pex heue.
6 “Pie pux aik pex,
kawn simex kawn bun pex,
moeng pa kied muh pex,
paox grawm pa kied khaw pex rhawm paox khankix,
rien tix chix cheeik pex hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
pa ang pex mai tax du pex sax yuh kaing krub ka khankix.
7 Ka daux kix,
rien tix koe pui pa sang nyaw glaw pex hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux pa krub ceu pui pa ot dex pex,
mai pui pa ot dee singai khankix.
8 Viang mawh nan,
pex ang pon tix nghiet ka lox nawh,
mai ang pon tix cu lox nawh.
Pex ang pon tix koe krax pa caox rhawm ka pui pa yuh nin,
mai ang pon tix kia ngien ka nawh.
9 Tag caw tix pauh nawh yum!
Pex ran tawx taix tix ka nawh sivoe,
khaix an kieh pui tang kaux hawt tix dheem nawh ka simaox.
10 Tag caw tix dheem nawh yum ka simaox,
kheu jao nyaw glaw nawh pex hu singai khaing Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pex,
pa hoik viex pex lih khaing daux Ai-cix,
pa taoh pex khaing dee yuh pex siliex.
11 Yuh nin,
pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag mhong ka grawng in,
tom koe krax lhat krax graung kah,
mai tag ang lai koe pui pa baox deeh yuh kaing ceu pa caox pa caig ceu in heue.
12-13 “Phan pex mhong koe pui tix kaux kaux pa krai,
ka daux veng pa ot pex ka daux,
pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex ka pex tix veng veng,
koe pui caox pa lih kaing,
mai nyaw glaw ka pui pa ot ka daux veng khankix hu yuh kaing krub ka siyiex pa ang pex sax krub kah,
14 pex tag caw tix si kaw tom die.
Phan kham sakse hoik tawng sidawng grawng kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh nan,
mawh tete,
15 mai pex tag caw tix pauh ka can pui pa ot ka daux veng an khankix,
khawm can khawng brie khankix,
mai yuh laux lak pak leeing ka veng an khawm uik.
16 Pex tag caw tix khrawm ka sin ngao pa ot ka daux veng khawm uik,
mai deuh pum ot ka daux pak brawk,
khaix an ploi ngu gleeing ka veng,
mai ka khawm sin ngao pa ot ka daux veng khankix,
mai kieh mawh krax tan pa tan pex tix tawx ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex.
Veng an tag sang mawh dee riem leeing ju leeing phan,
ang lai baox deeh cox sum heue.
17-18 “Can ceu pa caw tix gleeing laux khawm uik,
pex tag ang lai pon tix deuh kah.
Yuh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom tag chix veeh krax pa song rhawm tix grai,
mai jhi krax pa caox rhawm tix ka pex.
Phan pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa rauh pa see Simiang Siyiex khawm uik pa hoik tawm aux ka pex daux in singaix,
mai yuh pa khaw caw pa nhie nawh pex yuh,
nawh tom tag chix jhi krax pa caox rhawm tix ka pex,
mai hawt awm lox pa hoik ap nawh tix deuh ka tax du pex khankix,
kieh pui pex pre haok huan heue.”
