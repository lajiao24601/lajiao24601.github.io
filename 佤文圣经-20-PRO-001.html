Lai Lox kab
Ngawh jum lai Lox kab
1
aKawn simex Ta-wue,
lai lox kab simiang Yi-seu-liex pa mawh Saw-law-meung.
2 Lai lox kab in kix hoik chix kieh maix tawng ka ping nyia cuyi mai gum lox pa sidiex,
tawng sidawng ka dee bleeh gum lox kab pa raux pa mox.
3 Kix chix sidiex gau maix yuh ka kaing pa lhak lhiao yuh ka mawx,
mawh pui pa khaw caw yuh ka mawx,
yuh kaing pa rauh pa see yuh ka mawx.
4 Kix chix kieh pui pa daox aw lhak lhiao tawng been,
chix sidiex gau pui nhum ju koe rhawm keud son sang mhawm yuh kaing.
5 Lai lox kab in kix kawx chix pon kieh pui lhak pui lhiao chix pon krax haok huan ka ngai lig ngai lai,
kieh pui pa lhak lhiao pon krax pa sidiex,
6 mai tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox phek tiiem lai lox kab,
khawm grawng pa chawk cheng pui pa koe krax lhak lhiao heue.
Sidiex gau ka pui pa nhum ju khankix
7 cPa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex mawh dee jauh ping nyia cuyib.
Pui pa daox aw nu nai ka ping nyia cuyi,
mai ang cu tix gau heue.
8 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau keeing maix,
mai ang pon tix piawm ka gum lox pa sidiex gau miex maix.
9 Gum lox pa sidiex gau kix,
hoik awm pa ceep ka mhok khri,
mai nie mawh pa hoik jhi sidawng ka krax keud krax sawn maix;
hoik awm maox soi ka ngawg maix,
pa nie kieh maix mhawm grang heue.
10 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
phan pui caox hoek tix nyaw glaw ka maix,
paw vi nieb nghiet ka kix.
11 Phan kix ah nin:
“Hoek heue,
hu ex pauh pui,
mai sawg tix lauh ka pui pa ang koe krax lud krax loe,
kieh mawh pa vhah yuh rhawm rhi ex;
12 ex sang awm meung yum pa bleet im ka kix,
mai kieh pui pa mhawm kaux hak goh sideeing tix laig ka daux o pa raux;
13 ex tag sang chix pon ka kraung khrai ku ceu,
ka daux nyiex ma ex hoik deuh naog ka kraung ceu pa tud rhim ex.
14 Hoek ex yuh tau heue,
kieh ex guah tix cie ka kraung khrai pa tud ex hoek heue.”
15 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
paw vi hawt khaix pui pa yuh nin,
tag caw tix singai hu khaing kix.
16 Kix hoik ki sang yuh ka kaing ceu pa caox pa caig,
hoik keud tix sang pauh pui yum ku bawg ku yam.
17 Yam rien kawn sim tix,
phan maix keud tix sang giex ka nawh,
hoik mawh pa giex pa going;
18 viang mawh nan,
pui caox jiet hoik nih tix taong rub son to kaux tix,
mai sang yuh to kaux tix laux leeing.
19 Pui pa tud pa rhim hoik mawh krax pa sum ju son nawh;
krax pa si pupd pui briex pui lak son pa ih pa nyaux to kaux tix,
tag sang awm in yuh nin heue.
Lox pa kok yo ping nyia cuyi
20 eNghiet heue,
ping nyia cuyi kok yo ka daux laih mai ka brawk,
21 kok yo tom ting ka praok siviex veng mai dee si ghox pui haoh pui hun ah nin:
22 “Pui pa daox aw eue,
pex sang daox aw yuh nin hoek yam mawx lie?
Pex map nai ka ngai lig ngai lai hoek yam mawx lie?
Pex ang chix si duad tom si dauh lie?
23 Tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux;
aux sang sidiex gau ka pex,
mai krai pex mhong ceu pa hoik tawng tix.
24 Aux kok yo,
pex ang nghiet;
aux ghaok pex hoek,
pex ang simi.
25 Pex map nai ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux khawm uik,
mai ang rieb krax pa cox gau aux pex.
26-27 Mawh kheu nan,
krax laux krax leeing sang lih hoek piang pex,
aux sang nyiah ka pex;
pex si pup kaing ceu pa ang mhawm,
krax lhat krax graung tag sang awm bhaung ting pa hoek tix klauh ka pex,
viex krax laux krax leeing hoek,
kieh pex laux rhawm laux rhi,
aux tag sang nyiah pex heue.
28 “Yam an,
pex tag sang khiox khaw kok yo ka aux,
mai aux tag ang simi ka pex;
sang sawg ka aux,
mai tag ang yaox ka aux.
29 Pex ang sax siao ka ngai lig ngai lai,
mai ang lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex.
30 Pex ang sax nab yam yung yi ka gun lox pa sidiex gau aux tix bawg kawx,
mai map nai ka krax pa ne raw aux.
31 Mawh kheu nan,
kaing pa yuh pex tag sang deeh hoek ka piang pex,
mai kaing pa yuh pex deeh laux leeing yuh to kaux pex.
32 Pui daox pui aw som ju kheu tieb tie tix ping nyia cuyi.
Pui pa daox aw laux leeing kheu pa ting rhawm ting rhi tix.
33 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa cu nghiet ka aux tag pon krax pa nyawm ot;
nawh tag sang pon yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai ang lhat graung ka krax pa laux pa leeing heue.”
