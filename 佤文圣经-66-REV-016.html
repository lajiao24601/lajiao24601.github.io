Klong krax pa song rhawm Siyiex
16
Aux mhong lox pa ting khaing daux nyiex buan pa krai ka kawn cau Siyiex aliah kaux ah nin:
“Pex hu,
mai teei klong an pa hoik prauk naog ka krax pa song rhawm Siyiex klup piang haktiex heue!”
2 aKawn cau Siyiex ka daux tix kaux hu,
mai teei klong an klup piang haktiex;
mawh kheu nan koe muad cux pa lang teung koe ka to kaux pui pa koe cum to sat an mai ka pui pa krub ka riang to sat an heue.
3 Kawn cau Siyiex ka daux ra kaux tix kaux teei klong an klup ka piang rawm sigrit sigra;
rawm sigrit sigra tieh tix lih awm nham pui yum yuh nan,
ceu pa im ju pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra hoik yum khawm uik heue.
4 bKawn cau Siyiex ka daux loe kaux tix kaux teei klong an klup ka daux klawng mai ka dee keet rawm,
rawm tom tieh tix lih mawh nham.
5 Aux mhong kawn cau Siyiex pa kuang rawm an ah nin:
“Kaw pa singax pa koe tom yam jauh,
mai yam in eue,
maix mawh kaw pa prawng ca pa rauh pa see.
6 Kix hoik sax kieh nham pui cie Siyiex mai nham cao jhai khankix jauh,
yam in maix tawx kix nyaux nham;
in hoik mawh pa khaw kix tix pon heue!”
7 Aux baox mhong lox pa lih hoek khaing khu dee tan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm eue,
krax prawng ca maix hoik mawh pa rauh pa see tete te lai heue!”
8 Kawn cau Siyiex ka daux pon kaux tix kaux teei klong an klup ka piang singaix,
mai kieh singaix chix teei pa hax tix gleed yuh kawn pui.
9 Kawn pui hoik gleed ka pa hax singaix,
kix tom map nai ka Siyiex kaw pa koe akhuang tix tawx ka krax pa laux leeing in kix.
Kix ca kawn ang bax loh rhawm rhi tix,
ang cu tix yaog yaw ka pa ting pa lhaong Siyiex heue.
10 cKawn cau Siyiex ka daux phuan kaux tix kaux teei klong an klup ka piang dang ngeung simiang to sat an.
Mawh kheu nan,
pa faik tawb kheem ka meung cie to sat an,
kawn pui hoik kiied dak tix kheu kha rhawm tix.
11 Kix hoik map nai Siyiex rao ma kheu kham tix krax kha rhawm,
mai pa muad to kaux tix,
mai ca kawn ang tik vud ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix heue.
12 dKawn cau Siyiex ka daux liah kaux tix kaux teei klong an klup piang klawng ting Yo-fa-la-ti,
rawm klawng hit,
son sang rien khre krax tix nyaing son simiang pa kaoh khaing plak lih singaix khankix.
13 Khaix an aux yaox khuan pa seeing brai loe kaux,
pa awm khiet yuh nan,
pa guah tix lih khaing daux jheeh si yawng,
daux jheeh to sat,
mai daux jheeh cao jhai pa pie lhien khankix.
14 Kix mawh khuan phi caox,
pa chix yuh mhai pa daox pa am.
Khuan in kix hu sawg ka simiang piang haktiex,
mai khrawm kix ot tau,
rien tix sang yuh naing ka daux singaix pa ting pa lhaong cie Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm heue.
15 e“Bre rhawm rhi tix heue,
aux tag sang lih hoek awm pui briex dum mawx dum an yuh nan heue!
Pui pa si duad,
pa hoik ceep ka kraung ceep tix kied ting buan son heue!
Nawh ang yaox tix hu ka kaong,
ang yaox tix pon krax pa kaig tua ka plak sivoe ngai pui heue.”
16 fMawh kheu nan,
khuan pa caox pa caig an kix khrawm simiang an kix ot tix dauh;
dee an lox Si-paw-lai kok mawh Ha-mi-cie-to-toeng heue.
17 Kawn cau Siyiex ka daux aliah kaux tix kaux teei klong an klup ka daux rao ma.
Koe lox pa lih hoek khaing dang ngeung nyiex buan ah nin:
“Hoik mawh heue!”
18 gMawh kheu nan lih koe pu plak praix,
lox luah,
si num praix,
mai kla reem pa kied lhat pui kah.
Tom yam jauh kawn pui tix koe ka piang haktiex,
ang nyang sax koe kla reem pa ting yuh nan tix bawg kawx.
19 hVeng ting Pa-pi-loeng an bih tix koe loe baux;
veng ku meung haktiex kawx hoik ghrup khawm uik.
Siyiex een tix tawng ka phi map Pa-pi-loeng pa ting,
mai kieh kix nyaux ka plai khien krax pa song rhawm pa koe daux kawk Siyiex.
20 iMawh kheu nan,
can klawn tiex ang lai yaox tix mu kawx;
can gawng lua pa koe khawm uik kawx hoik grai.
21 jMu pre ting khaing rao ma cawd ka piang to kaux kawn pui,
tix mu been ciien koe tipon kongcing.
Kheu son pa laux leeing ka mu pre in,
kawn pui tom map nai ka Siyiex heue.
