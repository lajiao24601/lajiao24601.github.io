Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ma-na-si
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 33.1–20)
21
Yam koe ju Ma-na-si kao ra num nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
nawh hoik giex ka kaing piang pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tix phuan phuan num.
Miex nawh mawh Shie-si-pa.
2 aMa-na-si hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt ka kaing pa caox pa caig pui haktiex pa koe sivoe an pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex lih hu yam kieh pui cie tix preeng tix laig.
3 Nawh deeh cox sum ka nyiex vad pa hoik yieh keeing nawh Si-si-cia laux pa ot dee lung ka piang gawng,
mai baox sum khu krub Pa-lix,
yuh ka rong riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la,
mai simuing kawx krub kah.
4 bNawh yuh khu been li pui tang ceu ot ka daux nyiex buan pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex lox nhie pui caw tix krub simiang ka daux,
5 mai baox yuh khu pa krub ka simuing,
ot ka brawk nyiex buan khawm ra mu.
6 Nawh gleeing tix tan lu ka kawn simex tix,
mai baox yuh cao si pawk,
chix si meed yuh sat,
sawg tix chawk ka cao chix kia lai tiag taix,
cao kok khuan.
Nawh hoik yuh phi map pa ting,
tieb tie ka Simiang Siyiex,
kieh Simiang Siyiex song rhawm song rhi.
7 cNawh teei tix deuh rong riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la ot ka daux nyiex buan.
Phan mawh nyiex buan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik sax krai ah nin ka Ta-wue mai kawn simex nawh Saw-law-meung:
“Nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik mawh pa laug aux lih khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu,
son sang kieh mawh dee krub ka aux.
8 “Phan pui Yi-seu-liex nghiet ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tawx siliex aux Maw-si ka kix,
aux tag ang doh grheu kix lih hu khaing haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka tax du kix in heue.”
9 Viang mawh nan,
kawn pui Yo-tax ang nghiet ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex.
Ma-na-si thung kix yuh ka phi map,
mai phi map pa yuh kix kawn lheu ting khaing phi map pa yuh pui haktiex pa koe sivoe an,
pa hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex lih hu khaing plak sivoe ngai pui cie tix heue.
10 Simiang Siyiex tawx siliex tix pa mawh cao jhai khankix ah nin:
11 “Simiang Ma-na-si hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig in kix,
mai kawn lheu caox khaing pa yuh pui Cia-nang;
nawh kieh riang rao nyaw glaw ka kawn pui Yo-tax yuh phi map.
12 Mawh kheu nin,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang ploi krax laux leeing hoek piang pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai piang pui Yo-tax,
mai kieh pui pa mhong kaing in yaoh yee kah.
13 Aux tag sang ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
awm hoik ciyang aux ka Sa-ma-li-yax yuh nan,
awm hoik ciyang aux ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Ya-ha mai doeh ceu cad nawh yuh nan.
Aux tag sang pih tix tik ka pui veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
awm hoik phak pui ka pan singax yuh nan,
mai pu pliak gup kah.
14 Aux tag sang tik vud ka kawn pui pa deuk khankix,
mai ap tix tawx kix ka pui koeng;
pui koeng tag sang pon kuang ka kix,
mai tud ka haktiex kix.
15 Aux tag sang yuh nin ka pui cie tix;
mawh kheu hoik phit lud kix ka aux,
mai kieh aux song rhawm song rhi.
Jauh ka yam lih tax du kix khaing Ai-cix tom hoek yam in,
kix ca kawn yuh nin heue.”
16 Lheu khaing an,
Ma-na-si kawx hoik pauh nang pui ang koe dee lud tom nie,
mai hoik kieh nham naog ka daux laih daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
nawh baox thung pui Yo-tax krub ka riang rao,
mai hoik kieh kix yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex heue.
17 Kaing yuh Ma-na-si tang ceu khankix,
khawm grawng pa hoik yuh nawh ka phi map,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
18 Ma-na-si hoik yum,
kix si paung nawh ka daux si peem nyiex haw simiang,
pa mawh biang brawk nyiex Wu-sha;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Ya-meung plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-meung
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 33.21–25)
19 Yam koe ju Ya-meung tix nga ra num,
nawh mawh simiang pui Yo-tax,
mai nawh giex kaing ka piang veng Yie-lu-sa-leung ra num.
Miex nawh mawh Mi-su-li-mix,
kawn bun Ha-lu-see ka daux veng Yaw-thi-pa.
20 Ya-meung awm keeing tix Ma-na-si yuh nan,
hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
21 Nawh hawt ka kaing pa yuh keeing tix,
mai krub ka riang rao pa hoik krub keeing nawh khankix.
22 Nawh tieb tie ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du nawh,
mai ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap Simiang Siyiex heue.
23 Siliex Ya-meung tieh vheen ka nawh,
mai klauh nawh yum ka daux nyiex haw simiang.
24 Pui Yo-tax hoik pauh ka pui pa yuh Ya-meung laux,
mai ap kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yaw-si-yax plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
25 Kaing yuh Ya-meung tang ceu khankix hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
26 Ya-meung si paung tix ot ka daux biang brawk nyiex Wu-sha,
ka gawng si meeig cie nawh;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yaw-si-yax plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
