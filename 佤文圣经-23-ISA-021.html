Pa-pi-loeng laux leeing
21
Plak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Pa-pi-loeng.
Awm bhaung klia mhaik yuh nan,
mai krax laux krax leeing tag sang hoek khaing dee pa koe krax lhat krax graung.
2 Aux hoik yaox ka riang su sai krax pa khi rhawm pa gawp paox tix,
pa mawh pa pie lhien ca nied tix pie lhien;
pa yuh krax pa laux pa leeing ca nied tix yuh krax pa laux pa leeing heue.
Pui naing Yi-lang eue,
preeng tix hu heue!
Pui naing Mi-ti-yax eue,
puing heue!
Siyiex tag sang kieh krax kha rhawm pa hoik tawx Pa-pi-loeng ka pui naox tix heue.
3 Yam yaox aux ka riang su sai in,
mhong ka lox in,
aux ang lai pon kham krax kha rhawm in,
hoik awm saux yam sang keh bun miex yuh nan.
Ngai aux hoik faik kheu si yaung tix,
mai ang lai chix mhong lox ceu pa tid tix kheu lhat graung tix.
4 Kaing aux hoik vung ving;
aux hoik yaoh yee mai si yaung si kua.
Aux simie praix nied faik,
viang mawh nan,
pa faik praix hoik mawh pa viex ka krax lhat krax graung heue.
5 Ka daux riang su sai,
aux hoik yaox priex preem pa hoik rien khre tix mhawm,
khawm man pa tawm ot piang tiex kawx hoik rien khre mhawm son nyo si nie;
yam jiet taug ih nyaux kix,
mai dum mawx dum nan,
koe pui tawx lox hoek ah nin:
“Simiang naing khankix,
rien khre pex heue!”
6 Yam an,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Maix hu,
ploi pui pau hu,
tawx lox nhie nawh deeh krai ceu pa hoik yaox tix ku bawg ku yam.
7 Phan nawh hoik yaox ka pui naing pa brug brung ra kaux ra kaux hoek ka plak in,
khaix an mai kawn yaox ka pui pa brug la mai ka-la-uk,
tag caw rien rhawm tix,
mai kuan jhak tix yaox mhawm mhawm heue.”
8 Pui cao pau an kok yo ah nin:
“Simiang aux eue,
mai aux hoik cung tix pau ka dee in leeing ngaix leeing sawm heue.”
9 aDum mawx dum an,
koe pui naing pa brug brung ra kaux ra kaux hoek;
mai pui cao pau an krai ah nin:
“Pa-pi-loeng hoek laux heue!
Pui hoik lauh max riang rao pa krub kix piang tiex khawm uik heue.”
10 Paox ceu paox cad aux Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex hoik dawg awm nghox ca pa hoik grheeng ot daux grheeng yuh nan;
viang mawh nan aux sang krai gum lox pa gaux rhawm ka pex tix gum,
in hoik mawh gum lox pa mhong aux khaing Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex dee an heue.
Gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Yi-tong
11 Plak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Yi-tong heue.
Koe pui hoek khaing Yi-tong mai chawk ka aux ah nin:
“Cao pau eue,
pa faik praix hoik dex sang luan lie?
Pa faik praix hoik dex sang luan lie?”
12 Aux pawk ah nin:
“Praix dex sang riang,
viang mawh nan,
pa faik tag sang baox deeh hoek.
Phan maix kawn sang baox chawk,
deeh chawk bawg khaix heue.”
Gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai A-la-pawx
13 Plak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai A-la-pawx heue.
Pui Ti-tang eue,
pui yuh lai gee pa sum nyiex bang tix ot ka daux nong khaox A-la-pawx eue!
14 Pui pa sang nyaux rawm khankix hoek ka dee ot pex,
mai pex taug caw tix teei rawm tawx kix nyaux heue!
Pui pa ot ka Thi-max eue,
koe pui pa si pup krax laux hoek,
tawx kix sawm heue!
15 Pui in kix hoik mawh pui pa to tix klawd khaing pa lawm vaig,
khaing pa puing te,
mai pui pa to tix klawd khaing daux naing heue.
16 Mawh kheu nan Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
“Ka daux tix num,
awm hoik naog singaix bawg yam pa hoik tat tix deuh pui cang yuh nan,
krax pa haok huan pui Ci-tax tag sang uik.
17 Ka daux pui Ci-tax ceu pa blawn kua kix mawh pa kied chix puing ka ak;
kix kawx tag sang dieh hu,
ang lai koe miex kaux tix.
Aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
