Lai Keu-law-si
Krai dawt
Lai Keu-law-si mawh lox lai pa tiiem Pao-law tix tawx ka ceu cao Keu-law-si. Keu-law-si mawh veng iak pa ot ka haktiex Yi-fu-saw, haktiex Ya-si-yax pa iak plak lih singaix tix mu. Ceu cao dee in ang mawh pa sum Pao-law, viang mawh nan, nawh awm pa kied koe beeh glawm ka ceu cao haktiex in kix yuh nan, mawh kheu hoik sax ploi nawh ka paox pa yuh kix kaing tau hu yuh kaing ka Yi-fu-saw (nyiex dee ot simiang ka daux haktiex Ya-si-yax pa giex Law-ma kaing piang). Pao-law mhong lih koe sara pa pie lhien ka ceu cao Keu-law-si; kix sau riang tix, phan pui sang tawng ka Siyiex, sang pon krax taoh pa grum naog, tag caw tix krub ka an kix “pa mawh simiang haktiex mai cao giex kaing”. Kix baox krai ah nin: kawn pui tag caw priex tix hawt ka been pa hoik tat tix deuh, awm pa mawh been pa Pat-hak, mai tag caw priex tix hawt ka been pa tuah ka priex preem, mai ka been tang ceu pa hoik tat tix deuh khankix heue.
Pao-law hawt awm pa krai gum lox buan tiiem lox lai tieb tie ka krax pa sidiex yuh nin. Nawh een Ye-sux Khrit mawh krax taoh pa grum naog, lox tang ceu mai grawng krax tang ceu mawh pa kieh pui lud krax, singai hu khaing Khrit. Siyiex phan haktiex ka daux Khrit, mai baox haktiex mawh cie nawh ka daux Khrit. Mawh ngie pa ot ka daux ju Khrit, haktiex tom taug koe krax keud pot jun pot grawng sang pon krax taoh. Dee nghoik, Pao-law krai sidawng ka dee bleeh gum lox sidiex pa ting ceu in ka daux krax ot krax koe kawn Khrit heue.
Pa khaw ex tix deuh koe ka daux rhawm tix mawh, pa teem Pao-law viex lox lai in hu tawx ka Thoe-ci-ku ceu cao Keu-law-si, mawh A-ni-si-mo mai pui pa hu tau mai nawh. A-ni-si-mo mawh siliex cawng tix kaux; Pao-law hoik sax tiiem tix tawx lox lai son nawh ka Fe-li-meung heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–8)
2. To kaux Khrit mai kaing yuh nawh (1.9–2.19)
3. Ju pa khraox ka daux Khrit (2.20–4.6)
4. Lox pa nghoik (4.7–18)
