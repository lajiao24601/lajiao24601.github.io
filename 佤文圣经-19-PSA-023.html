Simiang Siyiex mawh cao pa liang aux
23
Simiang Siyiex mawh cao pa liang aux;
aux ang koe ceu pa cud cha tix ceu kawx.
2 aNawh kieh aux blauh tix it ka piang tiex dee koe rib klai,
thung aux hoek ka praok rawm klawng iak dee yien dee yawg.
3 Nawh kieh rhawm rhi aux haing.
Nawh hawt awm lox pa deuh tix mai thung aux hu krax rauh krax see heue.
 
4 Viang aux hoik hu preeng daux nong daux ngawd,
aux kawx ang lhat graung;
mawh kheu maix hoik ot tau mai aux,
maix teei khaox gix tix thung ka aux,
mai teei khaox nguh tix kia ngien ka aux heue.
 
5 Ka plak sivoe ngai pui koeng,
maix hoik khan aux ih priex preem,
kieh aux awm nyo si nie pa ting ceu khankix,
mai hoik prauk ka kawk aux naog.
6 Buan son krax muh maix ang lih hu khaing aux leeing ju leeing phan;
aux sang ot ka daux nyiex buan maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
