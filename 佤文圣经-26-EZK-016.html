Yie-lu-sa-leung pa ang nghiet ka lox
16
Simiang Siyiex baox si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai lih ka kaing ceu pa hoik yuh Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa hoik kieh pui kied koeng kah.
3 Tag caw tix krai ka nawh,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Cia-nang mawh haktiex dee ot maix;
maix hoik keh dee an.
Keeing maix mawh pui Ya-maw-lix,
miex maix mawh pui Heux.a
4 Yam keh maix,
ang koe pui pa pat ka sidaing maix,
heem ka maix,
teei kih pu phat ka to kaux maix,
teei man pua kah.
5 Yam keh kawn ngie pa khaw pui tix kia,
maix ang yaox pie ceeix kawx.
Yam keh maix ang koe pui pa caox rhawm tix kia ka maix;
pui hoik tik maix ka daux tiag daux tu heue.
6 “Khaix an,
aux hoek ka dee an,
mai aux yaox pa lu lieh maix tix ka daux nham;
nham hoik naog ka to kaux maix,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang rhawm yaox grawng sang yum maix.
7 Aux sum maix haok huan,
awm hoik kia ngien aux ka rib riem daux kaung ma.
Maix hoik haok huan hu si nyek si nyek,
mai lih tix mawh kawn bun krih,
teeh maix hoik pung,
hauk kaing maix hoik mhawm lang,
viang mawh nan maix ca kawn ot ka kaong heue.
8 “Aux baox hoek dee an,
mai yaox grawng pa hoik lhak maix,
pa hoik chix yuh maix paox muh.
Aux teei kraung ceep tawb dee kaig tua maix kah,
mai ah lox pa tiex tu tau mai maix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong yuh sijax mukheu tau mai maix,
mawh kheu nan maix tom lih mawh moeng aux heue.
9 “Khaix an,
aux teei rawm khoek nham pa krat ka to kaux maix,
mai baox teei bee sime awt ka hak maix.
10 Aux tawx maix ceep ka kraung pa breeh mhawm ka tai,
kiiep hak pa blawn mhawm,
mai hoik tawx nang maox phrawk hauk pa yuh ka man keei sum mai kraung man luat.
11 Aux jawg tix bre maix mhawm,
mai tawx maix ceep siang pa ngawh,
pa mawh ble,
maox soi,
12 kreem meeh,
kreem yhaok,
mai ceep mhok khri ka kaing maix nang.
13 Maix ceep kraung khri kraung mau,
mai kraung man keei sum pa hoik breeh mhawm ka tai,
mai kraung man luat.
Maix yaox tix ih khao mun pa hoik yuh ka phawng nghox ca,
rawm hia,
mai bee sime pa mhawm.
Maix hoik mhawm greeh mhawm grang,
mai lih mawh bun simiang.
14 Mawh kheu aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik kieh maix mhawm grang khaing pui khawm uik,
grawng pa ao min mhawm grang maix hoik tauh ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik heue.
15 “Viang mawh nan,
maix lai teei grawng pa ao min mhawm grang tix yuh krax pa phit vao kah,
mai kieh pui ang lai pon tieb tie.
16 Maix teei kraung ceep tix jawg ka dee krub ka siyiex,
mai hoik awm yuh bun coh kaux ka daux nyiex vad krax pa phit vao tau mai pui dee an yuh nan.
17 Maix hoik teei mau khri mai siang pa ngawh pa hoik tawx aux ka maix yuh riang simex kah,
khaix an phit vao tau mai kix.
18 Maix hoik tawx riang rao in kix ceep ka kraung mhawm tai pa tawx aux ka maix,
mai baox teei bee sime mai sidah bhawng pa tawx aux ka maix tan lu ka kix.
19 Priex preem pa tawx aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka maix,
pa mawh phawng nghox ca mhawm,
bee sime,
rawm hia,
maix hoik teei hu kieh mawh krax tan lu ka riang rao krub pui an,
son sang pon krax pa khaw rhawm kix heue.
20 “Khaix an,
maix hoik teei kawn simex kawn bun pa keh maix mai aux kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka riang rao.
Lao maix sang kieh tix ang singax son aux,
21 maix kawn pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun tix kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka riang rao.
22 Maix yuh bun coh kaux,
glang yam yuh maix kaing pa seeing brai yuh nan,
maix ang sax doh keud yam ka kaong yam mawh tix kawn nyawm,
yam lu lieh maix tix ka daux nham yuh nan heue.”
Yie-lu-sa-leung awm bun pa phit vao
23 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong baox ah nin:
“Maix hoik laux leeing heue!
Maix hoik si pup krax laux krax leeing heue!
Maix hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig khawm uik,
24 mai baox sum nyiex dee krub ka riang rao krub pui an ka kre laih,
mai coh to kaux tix dee an.
25 Pa ao min mhawm grang maix hoik grai ka piang haktiex,
pui chix tix mawh mex maix khawm uik,
mai ang lai koe pui pa pon tieb tieh kah,
nied ot nied kin.
26 Maix hoik it tix phit vao tau mai paox plawg paox nyhawm tix pui Ai-cix;
maix hoik kieh krax pa phi vao cheeik aux song rhawm ang jud heue.
27 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux sang nyeu taix tix hoek ciyang ka maix,
mai deeh teei ka buan son pa hoik tawx aux ka maix.
Aux sang tawx maix ka pui Fe-li-see pa koeng ka maix;
kix ang rhawm yaox ka kaing pa seeing pa brai yuh maix heue.
28 “Viang mawh nan,
maix kawn ca ang bax naog rhawm,
baox briex to hu mai Ya-sux,
mai mawh moeng iak nawh;
viang mawh nan,
nawh kawx kawn ca ang bax naog rhawm ka pa phit vao tau mai maix.
29 Mawh kheu nan,
briex phit vao mai Pa-pi-loeng meung haktiex pa yuh lai gee,
viang mawh nan,
ca kawn ang bax naog rhawm ka heue.”
30 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Maix hoik awm sibun coh kaux pa ang koe krax kaig krax tua pa hoik yuh ka kaing in khawm uik.
31 Maix hoik sum nyiex dee krub riang rao ot ka kre laih,
mai yuh krax pa phit vao dee an.
32 Viang mawh nan,
maix ang awm sibun coh kaux pa sawg ngie son mau khri khaing;
maix mawh bun pa phit vao,
pa cu tix briex to hu mai pui tang kaux,
mai ang muh ka mex tix.
33 Sibun coh kaux teei ngawh phit vao tix ka pui,
viang mawh nan,
maix tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka simex paox muh tix;
maix hoik nyaw ka kix,
mai nyaw kix hoek phit vao tau maix tix khaing haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
34 Maix ang awm sibun coh kaux tang kaux yuh nan,
ang koe pui pa khin maix coh tix.
Maix ang rieb mau,
mai tieh tix tawx simex paox muh tix cai mau.
Maix ang awm pui tang kaux tete heue!”
Simiang Siyiex prawng ca Yie-lu-sa-leung
35 Mawh kheu nan,
maix pa mawh bun pa phit vao Yie-lu-sa-leung in eue,
maix tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex heue.
36 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Maix awm sibun coh kaux,
pa hoik peeik kraung tix ghle yuh nan,
mai tawx to kaux tix ka simex paox muh tix mai ka riang rao.
Maix hoik pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun tix,
mai kieh kix mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka riang rao.
37 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang khrawm ka simex paox muh maix sivoe an,
mawh pa hoik muh maix ang ah,
pa hoik koeng maix ang ah,
tag sang khrawm hoek khawm uik.
Aux tag sang nhie kix hoek khaing haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
mai lai lawm ka maix,
khaix an mai peeik kraung maix ghle ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
kieh kix yaox pa ot ka kaong maix.
38 Aux tag sang ciyang ka phi map pa phit vao maix,
mai ka phi map pa pauh maix pui yum,
tag sang pauh maix yum ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
39 Aux tag sang ap maix ka daux tawm taix kix;
kix tag sang yieh laux ka riang rao pa krub maix,
ka nyiex dee phit vao maix,
mai teei ka kraung ceep maix mai ka kraung pa sau maix ot ka to kaux tix,
mai kieh maix ot ka kaong ang lai deuh tid tix ka piang maix heue.
40 “Kix tag sang cheeik pui hun tap maix ka simaox,
mai teei vaig gaing maix phawng kah.
41 Kix tag sang ploi ngu gleeing ka nyiex ma maix,
mai kieh cub bun miex khankix yaox grawng pa kham maix krax ciyang.
Aux tag ang lai kieh maix baox mawh sibun coh kaux,
ang lai baox kieh maix dex ka simex paox muh tix.
42 Yuh nin,
krax song rhawm aux tom taug chix grai,
rhawm rhi aux tom taug nyawm,
mai ang lai baox song rhawm,
ang lai baox boh rhawm.
43 Mawh kheu hoik piawm maix ka krax pa kia ngien aux yam iak tix,
mai tieh tix yuh kaing in kix khawm uik hoik cheeik aux song rhawm,
mawh kheu nan aux tom pawk bix ka maix yuh nin.
Maix hoik yuh ka kaing pa caox pa caig tom nie,
yuh ka mawx lai kawn baox yuh ka krax pa phit vao lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Yuh miex ka mawx kawn bun kawx yuh nan nang
44 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
pui tag sang teei gum lox pa prim krai ka maix ah nin:
‘Yuh miex ka mawx kawn bun kawx yuh nan nang.’
45 Maix hoik mawh kawn sibun miex tix tete,
hoik awm nawh yuh nan.
Nawh hoik koeng ka mex tix mai ka kawn simex kawn bun tix heue.
Maix kawx hoik awm pux o tix yuh nan,
hoik koeng ka mex tix mai ka kawn simex kawn bun tix.
Maix mai veng pux o maix khankix hoik mawh pa keh miex maix pui Heux tau mai keeing maix pui Ya-maw-lix heue.
46 “Pux o maix mawh Sa-ma-li-yax;
nawh mai yaong pa ot sivie siveng maix paung ot ka plak laong maix.
Pux o maix mawh Saw-to-ma;
nawh mai yaong pa ot sivie siveng maix paung ot ka plak seh maix.
47 Maix hoik hawt ka khaix caong kix,
hoik gau tix ka krax pa caox pa caig yuh kix;
pa gau maix hoik kub lie?
Ang!
Tix khiep ngai khaing maix kawn lheu caox khaing kix ku plak ku vang heue.
48 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka daux krax pa im ju tix leeing ju yuh nin:
Pux o maix Saw-to-ma,
mai yaong pa ot sivie siveng maix ang bax yuh phi map pa yuh maix,
mai pa yuh yaong pa ot sivie siveng maix khankix.
49 Nawh yaox tix sawm sak tau mai kawn bun tix,
mai yaox tix mhawm ot mhawm koe,
nawh tom ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
ang kia ngien ka pui hot pui sibhawm khankix.
50 Kix ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai hoik yuh ka kaing pa seeing brai pui kah,
mawh kheu nan aux hoik yuh kix laux leeing.
In hoik mawh pa tawng maix ka heue.
51 “Phi map pa yuh Sa-ma-li-yax kawn ang bax mawh pa yuh maix tix plak.
Pa yuh maix kawn lheu caox khaing pa yuh nawh.
Krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh maix phan tiag tau mai nawh,
nawh kawn sawn tix mawh pa rauh see khaing maix.
52 Yam in maix tag sang kham krax kaig krax tua son phi map tix.
Phi map pa yuh maix kawn lheu caox khaing pa yuh pux o maix,
nawh tieh sawn tix mawh pa rauh see khaing maix.
Maix khaw tix kaig ngai,
mawh kheu pux o maix hoik sawn tix mawh pa kawn singax khaing maix heue.”
Saw-to-ma mai Sa-ma-li-yax tag sang haok huan
53 Simiang Siyiex krai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh Saw-to-ma mai Sa-ma-li-yax khawm yaong pa ot sivie siveng khankix deeh cox haok huan.
Aux kawx tag sang kieh maix haok huan.
54 Maix tag sang kaig tua;
krax pa kaig tua maix hoik kieh pux o maix kawn lheu mhawm khaing maix.
55 Kix tag sang deeh pon krax haok huan;
maix mai yaong pa ot sivie siveng maix kawx tag sang haok huan.
56-57 Phi map maix ang bax jhi tix lih,
ka daux bawg yam pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi maix an,
ang maix hoik sax nyiah ka Saw-to-ma lie?
Yam in maix hoik awm kix yuh nan;
pui Yi-tong,
pui Fe-li-see,
mai pui pa koeng pa ot sivie siveng maix khankix hoik nyiah ka maix.
58 Maix tag sang kha rhawm tix ka son pa phit vao tix,
mai ka son kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix tete.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Sijax mukheu leeing ju
59 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang hawt tix pawk bix ka maix awm kaing pa yuh maix,
mawh kheu maix hoik lieh lheu ka gum lox sijax mukheu.
60 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang doh keud ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tix tau mai maix yam nhum ju tix,
mai deeh cox yuh sijax mukheu mai maix leeing ju.
61 Khaix hoik rieb maix pux o tix deeh hoek,
maix chix tix keud ka kaing pa hoik yuh tix khawm uik,
mai koe krax kaig tua kah.
Viang kaing in ang mawh pa koe daux sijax mukheu pa yuh aux tau mai maix sivoe an,
aux tag sang ap tix tawx kix ka maix,
mai kieh kix mawh kawn bun maix.
62 Aux tag sang deeh cox yuh sijax mukheu tau mai maix,
pex tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
63 Aux tag sang pluat ploi ka krax lud loe pa hoik yuh maix khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
maix ca kawn chix tix doh keud ka kaing in kix,
mawh kheu hoik kaig tua aux mai ang pon ang jheeh tix si kah.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
