Yaw-paw jhax lawg to kaux tix
3
aYaw-paw hoik naox yien yawg tix ang nye,
mai ang jheeh tix jhax lawg ka daux singaix keh tix.
2-3 Siyiex eue,
kieh maix jhax lawg ka daux singaix pa hoik keh aux an;
kieh maix jhax lawg yam nghe aux tix lih mawh kawn simex daux bo an.
4 Siyiex eue,
kieh maix tieh loh daux bo an lih mawh pa faik.
Kieh maix ang lai baox deeh doh keud ka singaix an,
ang lai baox kieh pa riang jhan ka an.
5 Kieh an lih mawh ka daux singaix pa faik veei krax yum.
Kieh pai awm kheem ka piang an,
pa faik tie ka pa riang singaix.
6 Kieh daux bo an preet tix grai,
ang lai baox dia tix ot tau mai khix num.
7 Kieh an lih mawh pa ang nyawm ot pa ang koe paox pie,
ang koe bo pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
8 Kieh pui an kix pa pon kok ka to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui kah,b
cao chix si meed khankix hoek jhax lawg ka daux singaix an.
9 Paw lai kieh simuing jhan riang;
paw kieh pa faik simie tix koe ka pa riang.
10 Tag caw tix jhax lawg ka daux bo pa keh aux an,
mawh kheu hoik kieh aux si pup ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi tom nie ceu heue.
 
11 Yuh ka mawx aux lai ang yum yam nghe daux vaig miex tix?
Phan ang mawh nan,
dut rhawm yam lih tix khaing daux vaig miex tix?
12 Yuh ka mawx miex aux lai teem aux ka piang ngawng tix?
Yuh ka mawx nawh lai tawx aux peux teeh tix?
13 Phan kieh aux yum yam an,
daux in singaix mai tag yaox tix laux lee tix,
14 tag yaox tix it tau mai simiang pa deeh cox sum ka nyiex haw simiang,
mai cao kaing,
15 mai it tom rheeng tau mai kawn simiang pa hoik naog daux nyiex ka mau khri,
16 mai it tau mai kawn ngie pa laux ka daux vaig miex tix,
pa ang bax yaox ka pa riang praix khankix.
17 Ka daux si meeig,
pui caox hoik ang lai yuh pa caox,
pui pa taug mao ka yuh kaing hoik pon krax pa laux lee.
18 Pie pui pa hoik laung kawx hoik yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe,
ang lai baox yaox krax pa jhax lawg cao kuang kaing khankix;
19 ang guah pui hot pui mi pui ting pui iak,
hoik ot dee an khawm uik,
khawm siliex cawng kawx hoik pon krax pa luat luan heue.
 
20 Yuh ka mawx lai kieh pui pa laux rhawm laux rhi kawn klawd ju?
Yuh ka mawx lai kieh pui pa khi rhawm khi rhi ca kawn yaox ka pa riang?
21 cKix khiox khaw tix yum ang pon;
kix kawn cu tix laig ka daux si meeig,
mai ang cu tix pon ka sin ngao.
22 Kix tag sang krox tix yum,
krox pui si paung ka kix,
taug sawn mawh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
23 Siyiex hoik kieh krax pa lawng kix iak hu,
mai giex gang kix ka sivie siveng.
24 Aux kieh lox ngia mawh priex preem tang kix;
lox yiiem aux awm rawm pa lih ang jud.
25 Kaing ceu pa lhat graung aux,
tix ceu hoik tix ceu lih koe;
kaing ceu pa yaoh yee aux,
ca nied tix lih hoek.
26 Aux ang koe pa nhim,
mai ang yaox tix laux lee tix;
krax pa suk sag dieb dong aux ang chix uik chix leeing heue.
