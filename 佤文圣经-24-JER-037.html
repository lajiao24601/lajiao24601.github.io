Si-ti-cia khiox khaw Yie-li-mi rawg buan
37
aNi-pu-cia-ni-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng yaog Si-ti-cia kawn simex Yaw-si-yax mawh simiang Yo-tax tang Yaw-ya-cin pa mawh kawn simex Yaw-ya-cing.
2 Viang mawh nan,
Si-ti-cia mai simiang pa ot greem nawh khawm pui hun ang nghiet ka lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex aux krai heue.
3 Simiang Si-ti-cia ploi Yo-cia kawn simex See-li-mi-ya mai Si-fang-ya cao giex kaing li kawn simex Ma-si-ya hoek ka aux,
mai nhie aux rawg buan son meung ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex ex.
4 Yam an,
ang nyang bax laung aux laig daux thawng,
aux chix tix lai ling hu daux pui haktiex.
5 Yam an pui naing Pa-pi-loeng lai lawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
viang mawh nan,
yam mhong kix pui naing Ai-cix hoik lih khaing daux meung haktiex,
kix tom dak tix deeh heue.
6 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex tawx lox nhie aux
7 krai ka Si-ti-cia simiang Yo-tax ah nin:
“Pui naing pa kaoh khaing Ai-cix pa sang hoek tix joh jhan maix tag sang kling tix deeh ing.
8 Pui naing Pa-pi-loeng tag sang hoek tix puing ka veng;
kix tag sang pon ka veng in,
mai ploi ngu gleeing heue.
9 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka maix,
paw vi pie lhien ka to kaux tix,
mai ce pui Pa-pi-loeng ang hoek;
tete te lai kix tag sang hoek.
10 Viang maix hoik pex kawx pui naing Pa-pi-loeng,
mai deuk ngie pui naing pa mat pa it daux bang khaing,
kix kawn sang deeh kaoh,
mai ploi ngu gleeing veng in laux leeing heue.”
Giex Yie-li-mi laig daux thawng
11 Mawh kheu hoik dex pui naing Ai-cix ka kix,
pui naing Pa-pi-loeng tom lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
12 mawh kheu nan aux rien tix sang lih khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai hu ka daux haktiex Piien-ya-ming dee ot ceu pui pa dex ka aux dee an,
son sang pon ka sin ngao pa guah aux pon.
13 Viang mawh nan,
yam hoek aux ka siviex Piien-ya-ming,
kawn saux Ha-na-ni-yax,
kawn simex See-li-mi-ya,
cao kaing pui naing pa pau pa kaux Yi-li-ya giex ka aux.
Nawh deuh phi map ka aux ah nin:
“Maix keud tix sang kreet tix ot greem pui Pa-pi-loeng heue!”
14 Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Ang,
aux ang mawh hu son sang kreet tix ot greem kix heue!”
Viang mawh nan,
Yi-li-ya ang nghiet,
nawh giex ka aux,
mai tawx aux ka cao kaing.
15 Kix briah ka aux,
nhie pui lauh aux,
mai laung aux ot daux lawg nyiex Yaw-na-tang cao tiiem lai nyiex haw simiang;
lawg nyiex in hoik loh lih mawh daux thawng.
16 Laung aux ot daux thawng tiex,
hoik laung tom rheeng heue.
17 Khaix an,
simiang Si-ti-cia ploi pui hu teei aux hoek ka tix dee an.
Nawh briex chawk aux ka daux nyiex haw simiang ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik koe ang koe gum lox pa krai lie?”
Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Koe heue!
Tag sang ap maix ka daux tawm taix simiang Pa-pi-loeng.”
18 Khaix an,
aux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh lud aux maix dee mawx,
lud aux simiang pa ot greem maix mai pui hun yam mawx,
pex lai laung aux laig daux thawng yuh nin.
19 Cao jhai pa krai ang simiang Pa-pi-loeng doh sang hoek tix puing ka pex,
ang hoek rhim tix teei ka meung in,
yam in ot kix dee mawx lie?
20 Vawng eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg mhaing aux,
paw vi lai tawx aux deeh ing ka daux thawng nyiex ma Yaw-na-tang,
son ang aux sang yum dee an heue.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Si-ti-cia tawx lox nhie pui laung aux ot ka daux thawng nyiex haw simiang.
Aux ot dee an,
kix guah tix tawx aux ih khao mun tix ngaix tix lon khaing daux laih,
tom hoik uik ih pui ka khao mun pa koe daux veng heue.
