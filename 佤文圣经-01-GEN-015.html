Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu mai Ya-paw-lang
15
Khaix hoik ceu in kix,
Ya-paw-lang mhong lox Simiang Siyiex ka daux riang su sai mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Ya-paw-lang,
paw lhat graung.
Aux sang kia ngien ka maix,
tawx khawng phak pa ting ka maix heue.”
2 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-paw-lang pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix tag sang tawx ceu pa tix ka aux lie?
Aux ang koe kawn simex,
maix tawx khawng phak ka aux kawx ang koe ceu?
Pui pa sang plud ju aux mawh ngie Yi-li-yi-shiex pui Ta-ma-see-keux.
3 Maix ang bax tawx aux koe kawn simex,
kheu nan,
mawh siliex pa keh daux nyiex aux tix kaux pa sang plud ka ju aux heue.”
4 Khaix an,
nawh deeh mhong lox Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Siliex Yi-li-yi-shiex ang chix mawh pui pa plud ka ju maix;
mawh ngie kawn simex lih maix pa tag chix plud ju maix heue.”
5 aKhaix an,
Siyiex viex nawh lih plak praix,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix maong tix jhak ka rao ma,
simin tix yaox ka simuing;
doeh ceu cad maix kawn sang haoh hun awm simuing heue.”
6 bYa-paw-lang yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai Simiang Siyiex tom sawn nawh mawh pui rauh see heue.
7 Simiang Siyiex deeh ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa viex maix lih khaing haktiex Pa-pi-loeng veng Wu-eu.
Aux sang tawx haktiex in khawm uik ka maix,
son sang kieh mawh sin ngao cie tix heue.”
8 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-paw-lang chawk ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong eue,
aux tag sang tawng tix pon haktiex in khawm uik yuh ka mawx lie?”
9 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix viex kawn moi siang pa koe loe num tix mu,
mai kawn pex siang pa koe loe num,
kawn yung mhaing pa koe loe num tix mu,
si kaux krie tix mu hoek ka aux tin heue.”
10 Ya-paw-lang tom viex to sat in kix hoek ka Simiang Siyiex dee an,
mai pih vaig mu mawx mu an,
guah nawh koe ra plak,
mai dia veeh ngai tix plak paox tix ra dia;
viang mawh nan,
ang bax pih kawn sim.
11 Koe klang pu tix hoek,
mai tom ka piang khik nex an,
Ya-paw-lang tom grheu pu heue.
12 cYam hoik klip singaix,
Ya-paw-lang hoik kied sidaing laig it;
koe ceu pa lhat graung pui ka awm pa lih hoek piang nawh.
13 dSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix caw tix tawng,
doeh ceu cad maix tag sang vung viang ka haktiex pui tang ceu,
yuh siliex cawng,
mai kham krax nu nai pui pon yieh num.
14 eViang mawh nan,
aux tag sang ciyang ka meung an pa kieh kix mawh siliex;
yam hu kix khaing tan,
kix kawn sang viex sin ngao lih tom nie.
15 Phan mawh to kaux maix,
kawn sang pon krax lang ju,
mai pon pa nhim tom yum tix laig ka daux tiex.
16 Khaix hoik luan pon ju,
kawn praix maix tom tag sang pon tix deeh hoek tin,
mawh kheu kawn sang jun aux phi map pui Ya-maw-lix naog,
hoek yam ciyang aux ka kix,
tag sang grheu kix to heue.”
17 Yam klip singaix,
hoik faik praix,
dum mawx dum an koe glah ngu mai mawk taox ngu pa lih gleeing.
Hu ka si nax khik nex an khawm ra plak.
18 fSimiang Siyiex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai Ya-paw-lang yam an dee an,
nawh ah nin:
“Aux tawx haktiex in ka simie ceu pui maix.
Haktiex in jauh ka daog haktiex Ai-cix hoek ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti,
19 khawm haktiex pui Ci-ni,
pui Ci-ni-si,
pui Cia-maw-ni,
20 pui Heux,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Li-fa-ying,
21 pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Cia-nang;
pui Keu-cia-sha,
mai pui Yie-pu-see khankix heue.”
