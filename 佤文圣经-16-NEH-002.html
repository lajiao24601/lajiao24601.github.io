Ni-si-mi hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
2
Khaix hoik luan pon khix,
koe tix ngaix,
Vawng Ya-ta-shiie-si sawm bo,
aux prauk tawx plai ka nawh.
Nawh ang nyang sax yaox yam voe pak ngai aux tix bawg kawx,
2 mawh kheu nan,
nawh chawk aux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx jhak tix yaox pak ngai maix kied laux rhawm?
Maix ang tie koe jhawm saux,
tee rien tix koe kaing tix ceu ceu pa ang kieh maix gaux rhawm!”
Aux hoik yaoh yee kah,
3 amai pawk ah nin:
“Kieh Vawng lang ju pu rhang!
Veng pa hoik si paung tax du aux,
hoik si pup ka krax pa laux pa leeing,
siviex veng hoik gleeing,
aux doh ang laux rhawm laux rhi lie?”
4 Vawng chawk aux ah nin:
“Sang koe maix lox pa rawg ceu pa tix?”
Aux rawg buan ka Siyiex piang rao ma,
5 khaix an krai ka Vawng ah nin:
“Phan maix Vawng paoh krax pa caox rhawm tix,
mai cu ka lox pa khiox khaw aux,
kieh aux deeh ing ka haktiex Yo-tax,
mai hoek ka veng pa hoik si paung tax du aux ot ka an,
kieh aux yaox tix pon deeh cox sum ka veng an heue.”
6 Vawng nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux;
yam an,
moeng simiang jiet ngawm ka praok nawh.
Vawng chawk aux sang hu miex greeng?
Sang deeh hoek yam mawx?
Aux krai ka nawh,
nawh tom cu aux hu heue.
7 Aux baox khiox khaw Vawng paoh krax khawng phak buan son tix tawx lai akhuang ka aux,
tawx lox ka simiang haktiex Heu-sib khankix cu aux tiag,
hoek ka haktiex Yo-tax.
8 Aux kawx khiox khaw tawx nang lai akhuang ka Ya-sa cao kuang kia ka nong khaox nyiex ma simiang tix blah,
nhie nawh tawx khaox ox khankix,
son sang sum ka siviex tab nyiex buan,
guh veng,
mai pa sang siao son nyiex ot aux.
Mawh kheu Siyiex ot tau mai aux,
can pa rawg mhaing aux,
Vawng hoik tawx ka aux khawm uik heue.
9 Vawng ploi pui simiang naing tix blah mai pui naing brug brung khankix hu tawx ka nawh,
aux tom hu ka haktiex Heu-si.
Ka dee an,
aux tom tawx lai pa tawx Vawng ka simiang haktiex.
10 To-pi-ya simiang veng Chang-pa-la mai haktiex Ya-meung haktiex Heu-loeng mhong hoek pui son kaing pa mhawm son pui Yi-seu-liex,
tom kied song rhawm ka heue.
11 Aux hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
mai ot dee an loe singaix,
12 aux ang bax krai kaing pa nhie Siyiex aux yuh son Yie-lu-sa-leung ka pui tix kaux kawx.
Ka daux loe singaix yam greung sawm,
aux kaoh khaing it,
mai viex paox pie tix lih hu.
Khawng brie pa viex yix tix mu khaing mawh la pa brug aux.
13 Aux jawm yam faik praix lih hu khaing veng,
mai tiang ka Ku-meung plak klip singaix,
hu ka plak dee lih rawm si yawng haktiex plak laong,
hoek ka La-ci-meung.
Aux hu nang,
jhak nang ka guh veng pa hoik laux mai ka siviex veng pa hoik gleeing ngu laux.
14 Khaix an,
ka veng plak lih singaix,
mai veeh tix hu plak laong,
hoek ka Chuan-meung mai Wang-chee.
Ka dee an,
mawh kheu la pa brug aux ang yaox krax iak pa phlat hu laig ka dee koe pum lon simaox,
15 aux tom lih ka klawng iak Ci-loeng,
mai lawng hawt ngawd klawng iak an,
hawt jhak tix yaox ka guh veng,
khaix an deeh veeh tix ing krax prim,
tiang laig ka Ku-meung hoek ka daux veng heue.
16 Simiang pui haktiex an tix kaux kawx,
ang tawng dee hoik hu aux,
kaing pa yuh aux.
Tom hoek yam an,
aux ang bax krai ka paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yo-thaix tix kaux kawx;
cao giex kaing li ang ah,
simiang ang ah,
simiang naing ang ah,
mai pui pa chix yuh ka kaing in ku kaux ang ah,
aux ang bax krai tid tix ka kix.
17 Viang mawh nan,
yam in aux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoik tawng ka kaing pa nu pa si pup yix khawm uik;
mawh kheu Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik riem,
siviex veng hoik laux.
Ex hoek tix deeh cox sum guh veng heue,
son ang yix sang baox yaox krax kaig krax tua!”
18 Aux krai ka kix,
Siyiex ot tau mai aux yuh ka mawx,
teem coi aux yuh ka mawx,
mai baox krai kix mhong grawng lox pa hoik krai Vawng ka aux.
Kix ah nin:
“Ex jauh tix deeh cox sum phaox heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix hoik rien khre tix mhawm mhawm,
mai jauh tix yuh kaing in heue.
19 Chang-pa-la,
To-pi-ya,
mai pa kaux Ci-sang pui A-la-pawx tix kaux,
mhong ka kaing pa sang keud yix tix yuh,
tom nyiah ka yix.
Kix ah nin:
“Yuh pex ceu pa tix?
Pex sang tieh vheen ka Vawng lie?”
20 Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Siyiex piang rao ma tag sang kieh yix pon sum.
Yix hoik mawh kawn cau nawh;
yix sang jauh tix deeh cox sum kah.
Pex ang mawh pui ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung;
pex ang koe akhuang sin ngao ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.”
