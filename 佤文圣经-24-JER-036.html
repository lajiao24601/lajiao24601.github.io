Pa-lux phat phuk lai ka daux nyiex buan
36
aYam mawh Yaw-ya-cing kawn simex Yaw-si-yax simiang meung Yo-tax ka daux pon num,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
2 “Maix teei phuk lai hoek,
krai kaing pa dex pa dawt mai pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui Yo-tax,
mai meung in kix pa hoik krai aux ka maix ku ceu tiiem lih.
Jauh ka singaix bawg yam simiang Yaw-si-yax hoek singaix yam in,
gum lox pa hoik krai aux ka maix,
tiiem lih khawm uik.
3 Pui Yo-tax tee rien tix mhong ka grawng pa sang ploi aux krax laux krax leeing lih hoek ka piang kix,
kix tee chix deeh tik vud ka krax pa caox pa caig,
aux tom tag pluat ploi ka phi map krax pa lud loe kix heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
aux kok Pa-lux kawn simex Ni-li-yax hoek,
mai krai gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux,
krai ka nawh ku gum,
nawh tom taug tiiem ot ka daux phuk lai.
5 Aux tawx lox ka Pa-lux ah nin:
“Pui ang kieh aux haok nyiex buan,
6 mawh kheu nan aux tag sang hoek nyiex buan ka daux singaix yam tuah maix ka priex preem bawg khaix,
mai phat gum lox pa hoik tiiem maix ot daux phuk lai in ka pui hun,
kieh kix mhong ka gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux,
pa nhie aux teei jheeh tix krai ka maix.
Maix tag caw tix phat ka dee chix mhong pui hun ka khawm uik,
son sang chix mhong pui Yo-tax pa kaoh khaing haktiex ku dauh kah.
7 Kix tee rien tix chix deeh khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai tik vud ka krax pa caox pa caig,
mawh kheu nan Simiang Siyiex hoik ne raw kix ka daux krax pa kied song rhawm song rhi tix.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Pa-lux kawn simex Ni-li-yax hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai aux,
mai phat gum lox Simiang Siyiex ka daux nyiex buan heue.
9 Yam mawh Yaw-ya-cing simiang Yo-tax ka daux phuan num khix dim,
kawn pui yuh son sang pon krax pa khaw rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
mai krai pao nhie pui tuah priex preem.
Can pui Yie-lu-sa-leung mai pui Yo-tax pa hoek khaing haktiex ku dauh,
hoik priex tix hawt ka singaix bawg yam pa tuah ka priex preem.
10 Pa-lux krai pao ka gum lox pa hoik krai aux,
pa hoik tiiem aux ot ka daux phuk lai an ka daux pui hun;
dee ot kix mawh ka daux lawg nyiex cao tiiem lai Ci-ma-li-ya,
kawn simex Sa-fang ka daux nyiex buan.
Lawg nyiex in ot ka piang brawk nyiex tix jan,
mai dex ka diang siviex dee laig ka “siviex khraox” nyiex buan heue.
Phat phuk lai in ka simiang
11 Kawn saux Sa-fang,
kawn simex Ci-ma-li-ya pa kaux Mi-kai-yax mhong lox phat Pa-lux ka gum lox Simiang Siyiex pa hoik tiiem ot daux phuk lai in,
12 nawh tom hoek ka daux lawg nyiex pui cao tiiem lai nyiex haw simiang.
Yam an,
can cao kaing hoik kum kawng dee an khawm uik.
Yi-li-sa-ma pui cao tiiem lai nyiex haw simiang,
Ti-lai-ya kawn simex See-ma-ya,
Yi-li-na-tang kawn simex Ya-keu-paw,
Ci-ma-li-ya kawn simex Sa-fang,
Si-ti-cia kawn simex Ha-nang,
mai cao kaing tang kaux khankix hoik ot dee an khawm uik.
13 Mi-kai-yax krai ka kix sidawng ka gum lox pa hoik mhong tix,
pa phat Pa-lux ka daux pui hun.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
kix ploi kawn sex Ku-see,
kawn saux See-li-mi-ya,
kawn simex Ni-thang-ya pa kaux Yo-ti hu kok yo ka Pa-lux,
mai nhie nawh viex phuk lai pa phat nawh ka daux pui hun hoek.
Pa-lux tom viex phuk lai an hoek ka dee ot kix dee an.
15 Kix ah nin ka nawh:
“Ngawm,
phat yix mhong heue!”
Pa-lux tom phat.
16 Khaix hoik mhong kix kah,
kix si yaung mai lai jhak ka paox tix,
kix tom ah nin ka Pa-lux:
“Yix tag sang krai kaing in ka Vawng.”
17 Kix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix krai ka yix,
hoik tiiem maix gum lox in yuh ka mawx?
Mawh pa teei Yie-li-mi jheeh tix krai ka maix lie?”
18 Pa-lux pawk ah nin:
“Mawh pa teei Yie-li-mi jheeh tix krai sidawng ku gum;
aux tom teei rawm meug tiiem ot daux phuk lai in heue.”
19 Kix krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix mai Yie-li-mi tag caw tix to mox tix,
paw vi kieh pui tawng dee mox pax tix heue.”
Simiang gleeing phuk lai an
20 Simiang khankix deuh phuk lai an ot daux lawg nyiex Yi-li-sa-ma pui cao tiiem lai nyiex haw simiang,
khaix an kix hu ka nyiex haw simiang,
mai krai grawng kaing in tom jauh tom si dauh ka simiang.
21 Simiang ploi Yo-ti hu teei ka phuk lai an.
Yo-ti tom hu ka daux lawg nyiex Yi-li-sa-ma pui cao tiiem lai nyiex haw simiang,
mai phat simiang mai cao kaing pa ot ka plak goix plak dawm simiang khankix mhong.
22 Yam an mawh bawg yam kuat praix,
simiang ot ka nyiex haw ot tix yam kuat praix,
mai caw ngu ka plak sivoe glah ngu.
23 Yam hoik phat Yo-ti ka phuk lai an hoek daux baux loe,
baux pon,
simiang tom teei kawn gawn pat phuk lai an griah,
mai tik daux glah ngu.
Nawh hoik pat tix gleeing phuk lai an uik khawm phuk heue.
24 Simiang mai cao kaing pa ot greem nawh mhong ka gum lox pa tiiem ot daux phuk lai an,
mai kix ang koe krax lhat pie ceeix kawx,
mai ang griah nang kraung ceep tix jhi grawng hoik laux rhawm tix.
25 Viang Yi-li-na-tang,
Ti-lai-ya,
mai Ci-ma-li-ya khiox khaw simiang paw vi gleeing ka phuk lai an,
simiang ang nghiet ka lox kix,
26 mai tieh tix tawx lox ka Si-lai-ya kawn simex Ya-see-liex mai Yie-la-mawx,
See-li-mi-ya kawn simex Ya-paw-tiie hoek giex aux mai Pa-lux cao pa teem aux tiiem lai.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex mox ka yiex heue.
Yie-li-mi baox deeh tiiem ka phuk lai an
27 Khaix hoik gleeing simiang Yaw-ya-cing ka phuk lai an pa hoik teei aux jheeh tix krai,
mai kieh Pa-lux tiiem,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux
28 mai nhie aux deeh baox tiiem ka gum lox pa koe ka daux phuk lai pa hoik gleeing simiang Yaw-ya-cing ot ka daux phuk lai tang mu.
29 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox nhie aux krai ka simiang Yaw-ya-cing ah nin:
“Maix hoik gleeing ka phuk lai an,
mai baox si kaw tix chawk ka Yie-li-mi,
yuh ka mawx nawh lai tiiem Pa-pi-loeng tag sang hoek tix yuh haktiex in laux leeing,
yuh ka pui pa ot daux laux leeing,
mai gum lox pa brai yuh nan heue.
30 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Yaw-ya-cing eue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex krai ka maix,
maix ang lai chix koe doeh ceu cad son sang plud tix mawh simiang khaix Ta-wue.
To kaux yum maix pui hoik tik daux tiag,
yam pon ngaix hoik kham pa hax koek hax pied,
yam pon sawm hoik kham pa kuat praix pa si duad rawm ciex.
31 Mawh kheu phi map pa yuh maix khawm uik,
aux tag sang ciyang ka maix,
ka kawn praix maix,
ka simiang pa koe greem maix.
Maix mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai pui Yo-tax ang simi ka lox pa ne raw aux,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang hawt awm lox pa hoik krai tix,
ploi krax laux krax leeing lih hoek piang to kaux pex heue.”
32 Mawh kheu nan,
aux baox teei phuk lai an tawx ka Pa-lux,
nawh tom tiiem gum lox pa teei aux jheeh tix krai lih ku gum.
Nawh deeh baox tiiem ka gum lox pa koe ka daux phuk lai pa hoik gleeing simiang Yaw-ya-cing khawm uik lih,
ma baox tiiem ka gum lox pa awm paox tix yuh nan heue.
