Grawng puing pex Ta-wue ka daux naing
(Lai Sa-mu-eu Phuk Ra 8.1–18)
18
Khaix an,
simiang Ta-wue baox hu puing ka pui Fe-li-see,
hoik pon pex ka kix,
mai pon teei ka veng Cia-theux mai nyiex yaong pa ot dex tan.
2 Nawh kawx pon pex ka pui Maw-ya,
kieh pui Maw-ya hawt rhawm tix,
mai tawx keen ka nawh heue.
3 Khaix an,
Ta-wue puing simiang pui Saw-pa pa kaux Ha-ta-ti-shiex haktiex Shui-li-yax pa dex ka haktiex Ha-ma,
mawh kheu Ha-ta-ti-shiex keud tix sang giex kaux cong ka siawp sibrawm pa kuang ka plak toh rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
4 Ta-wue pon giex ka ling law naing nawh tix rheng lhang,
pui naing brug brung aliah rheng kaux,
pui naing hu ka caong tix ra mheen kaux.
Ta-wue hoik deuh ka brung tom kub tix bluh ling law naing tix yieh lhang,
mai mug dut ka gip si niag brung pa kawn deuk khankix heue.
5 Yam ploi pui Shui-li-yax pui naing hu khaing veng Ta-ma-see-keux teem ka simiang Ha-ta-ti-shiex,
Ta-wue puing ka kix,
mai pon pauh kix yum ra mheen ra rheng kaux.
6 Khaix an,
Ta-wue kieh pui naing cie tix ot ka daux haktiex kix;
kix hawt rhawm Ta-wue khawm uik,
mai tawx keen ka nawh.
Simiang Siyiex kieh Ta-wue ang lai koe pui pa koeng pa ang nawh pex,
mai hoik puing pex ku dauh ku dee heue.
7 Ta-wue hoik pon teei ka dieb tie naok khri pa viex simiang naing pui Ha-ta-ti-shiex khankix,
mai viex hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
8 amai baox pon teei tawng tom nie ka veng pa kuang Ha-ta-ti-shiex,
pa mawh veng Thi-pa mai veng Cuing heue.
Khaix an,
Saw-law-meung teei tawng in kix raug yuh dung tawng,
rong tawng,
mai kraung tawng khankix ka daux nyiex buan heue.
9 Simiang Ha-ma pa kaux Thaw-yi mhong grawng pa hoik pon pex Ta-wue ka simiang pui naing Ha-ta-ti-shiex khawm uik,
10 tom ploi kawn simex tix pa kaux Yaw-lang hu chawk simiang Ta-wue mhawm ot,
mai ah lox pa yaog yaw grawng hoik puing pex nawh (sivoe an,
Thaw-yi mai Ha-ta-ti-shiex hoik yie puing ka paox tix).
Yaw-lang viex khawng phak lak sawng pa hoik yuh ka khri,
mau,
tawng khankix mai tawx ka Ta-wue.
11 Ta-wue tom rieb ka khawng phak lak sawng in kix mai ka khawm khri pa hoik pon tud tix khaing meung tang meung.
Pa mawh kraung pa teei khaing meung Yi-tong,
Maw-ya,
Ya-meung,
Fe-li-see,
Ya-ma-lix,
mai tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex khawm uik heue.
12 Ya-pi-sai (miex nawh mawh Si-lu-ya) pauh pui Yi-tong yum ka ngawd kih tix mheen daix rheng kaux.
13 Nawh tawx pui naing cie tix ot ka daux haktiex Yi-tong ku dauh;
pui haktiex an hoik hawt rhawm simiang Ta-wue khawm uik.
Simiang Siyiex kieh Ta-wue ang lai koe pui pa koeng pa ang nawh pex,
mai hoik puing pex ku dauh ku dee heue.
14 Ta-wue kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai nawh prawng rauh see son kawn pui.
15 Kawn simex Si-lu-ya pa kaux Yaw-ya mawh simiang naing.
Kawn simex Ya-si-lui pa kaux Yaw-sa-fa mawh cao kaing been.
16 Kawn simex Ya-si-thu pa kaux Sa-tu,
mai kawn simex Ya-pi-ya-tha pa kaux Ya-si-mi-leux mawh cao giex kaing li;
Si-lai-yab mawh cao tiiem lai ka daux nyiex simiang;
17 kawn simex Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya mawh cao giex kaing lien cie Ta-wue.
Kawn simex Ta-wue khankix yuh kaing ka plak goix plak dawm mai simiang heue.
