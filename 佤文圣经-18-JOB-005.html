5
Yaw-paw eue,
maix kok yo heue,
jhak tix yaox koe pui mawx pa sang pawk;
maix sang chix tix khiox khaw kawn cau Siyiex pa yuh ka mawx teem coi tix?
2 Song rhawm pauh pui pa daox aw yum;
boh rhawm kieh pui pa tiiem rhawm laux ju.
3 Aux hoik yaox ka pui pa daox aw,
awm pa hoik pon krax pa kaux cong yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
dum mawx dum an krax laux krax leeing lih hoek piang daux nyiex kix;
4 kawn simex kawn bun kix ang koe krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe,
ka daux nyiex pa prawng see,
mai ang koe pui pa krai lox tang kix.
5 To ma cie pui pa pheem khankix,
pui pa cai rhawm hoik ih kah,
khawm pa kuah ka daux pang kat khankix kawx hoik ih ka uik,
sin ngao cie nawh pui cai rhawm hoik bleet kah;
6 Pa caox pa caig ang chix kuah lih khaing daux tiex.
Krax kha rhawm kha rhi kawx ang chix lih khaing greem tiex.
7 Kawn pui keh lih piang haktiex,
kieh krax laux krax leeing lih koe,
awm tai ngu pa pu tix haok.a
8 Phan mawh aux,
aux sang sawg ka Siyiex,
krai grawng to kaux tix ka nawh.
9 Ex hoik pon tawng ka kaing ting pa yuh nawh;
krax pa daox pa am pa yuh nawh ang chix uik chix leeing.
10 Nawh tawx rawm lhiex lih ka piang haktiex;
nawh jaux ka kaung ma.
11 Mawh tete,
Siyiex yaog ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
tawx krax gaux rhawm ka pui pa khi rhawm khi rhi.
12 bNawh yuh laux ka krax keud krax sawn pui pa lhak khoik,
kieh kaing yuh kix lih tix ang mawh.
13 Nawh kieh pui pa lhak lhiao krix ka daux krax keud pa pie lhien tix,
kieh krax keud krax sawn kix mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik.
14 Plak pon ngaix kix si pup ka pa faik,
greung ngawb pu pi tix hu,
awm yam pon sawm.
15 Siyiex taoh ka pui hot pui sibhawm luat luan khaing krax yum;
nawh taoh ka pui cud pui cha luat luan khaing krax gut krax kheem.
16 Nawh kieh pui hot pui sibhawm koe krax keud pot jun pot,
kieh pui pa caox pa caig nyeep jheeh tix ang nye heue.
 
17 cPui pa sidiex gau Siyiex ka hoik pon buan son;
krax pa ne raw cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik ang pon tix iak jhak.
18 dSiyiex kie khaix mat pa yuh tix ka maix;
taix nawh lauh maix mat,
taix nawh kawx deeh pai maix moi.
19 Nawh tag sang taoh ka maix ku bawg ku yam;
nawh tag sang baox taoh maix luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing tix bawg hoik tix bawg.
20 Yam lih sibhawm praix ka maix,
nawh tag sang eei maix im;
yam puing ka naing,
nawh tag sang taoh maix luat luan khaing vaig gawn.
21 Nawh kieh maix ang yaox tix hoek krax pa map nai pui,
ang kieh maix yaox krax laux krax leeing.
22 Ka daux krax sibhawm praix mai ka daux krax pa suk sag yuh naing,
maix nyiah jheeng jheeng;
maix kawx ang lhat ka pa chuh to sat pui mat.
23 Kaung ma pa sum maix ang koe simaox;
to sat ka daux kaung ma kawx ang chuh maix mat.
24 Yam an,
maix tag sang paung ot ka daux nyiex gox bang;
maix lai ling tix jhak ka phung yung maix,
yung hoik mhawm ot khawm uik.
25 Kawn praix maix kied haoh hun,
awm rib klai ka daux lung pak rib.
26 Maix tag sang lang ju pu rhang tom laig tix daux si meeig,
awm nghox ca pa kaux,
krox jun pui khrawm vawg.
27 Yaw-paw eue,
in hoik mawh daux krax pa mawh pa meh pa hoik jhak yix tix yaox;
son sang mhawm son maix,
maix caw rieb tix teei heue.
