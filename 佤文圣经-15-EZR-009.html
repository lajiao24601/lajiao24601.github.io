Ne raw ka pui pa mex moeng pui tang ceu
9
Khaix hoik luan kaing in,
koe simiang pui Yi-seu-liex tix blah hoek krai ka aux,
mai krai ang pui Yi-seu-liex,
cao giex kaing li,
mai pui Li-we bax tang tia tix lih hu khaing meung pui Cia-nang,
pui Heux,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Yie-pu-see,
pui Ya-meung,
pui Maw-ya,
pui Ai-cix,
mai pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot sivie siveng kix.
Kix hawt ka pui an kix,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig awm kix.
2 Kix teei to kaux tix mai kawn praix tix moeng ka kawn bun tang meung,
mai kieh ceu pui pa singax singeh cie Siyiex seeing brai;
simiang mai simiang plak greem hoik mawh num phi map.
3 Yam mhong aux ka kaing in,
aux kied song rhawm mai riah kraung ceep tix griah,
mai baox reeig ka hauk kaing tix mai hauk jheeh tix,
ngawm mai laux rhawm laux rhi tix.
4 Aux ngawm tix jeung,
tom hoek yam caw tix tan lu krax tan plak bo.
Mawh kheu Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai,
nawh tag sang yuh pui vuk vied vuk viang pa yuh phi map,
can pui pa mhong ka lox in mai yaoh yee lhat graung kah,
mai hoek tix lai lawm ka aux khawm uik heue.
5 Yam tan lu krax tan plak bo,
aux kaoh khaing daux pa laux rhawm laux rhi tix,
mai ca ceep ka kraung pa hoik griah.
Aux grux ngawng tix,
mai yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux.
6 Aux ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
aux hoik koe krax kaig krax tua tete,
mai ang pon yaog kaing tix ka plak sivoe maix.
Phi map ex hoik kieh kin,
krax pa phit lud hoik naog ka rao ma.
7 Jauh ka tax du yix tom hoek singaix,
phi map yix kied ting kied lhaong.
Mawh kheu krax pa phit lud yix,
yix mai simiang yix khawm cao giex kaing li khankix,
hoik cawd ka daux tawm taix simiang meung tang meung.
Pui pauh ka yix,
tud rhim ka yix,
awm hoik giex laung pui ka pui lud yuh nan,
mai hoik pon krax map tom nie,
tom hoek yam in singaix ca kawn mawh nan.
8 “Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
maix hoik sax tawx nang buan son ka yix,
mai kieh yix pa mawh pui pa deuk in kix,
ran yaox tix luat luan khaing daux pa mawh tix siliex cawng,
mai yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe ka dee pa singax singeh.
Maix kieh yix deeh cox yaox ka pa riang,
luat luan khaing daux pa phrawk pa nhawt,
mai tawx yix pon krax pa im ju.
9 Yix hoik mawh nang siliex cawng sivoe an,
viang mawh nan maix ang bax lai kieh yix yaox tix ca mawh siliex cawng.
Maix hoik kieh Vawng Paw-see chix kia ngien ka yix mhawm mhawm,
hoik cu tix kieh yix pon krax pa im ju,
mai baox pon deeh cox sum ka nyiex buan pa hoik laux an,
mai hoik kieh haktiex Yo-tax mai veng Yie-lu-sa-leung yaox krax nyawm ot heue.
10 “Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex eue,
yam in kaing in rug hoik lih tix mawh,
yix kawn sang chix krai ah ka mawx lie?
Yix hoik lie lheu
11 gum lox pa hoik tawx maix ka kawn cau tix,
pa mawh cao jhai khankix krai ka yix.
Kix hoik sax krai ka yix,
krai dee pa sang hu pon yix tix cie,
mawh haktiex pa seeing brai;
mawh kheu kaing yum koeng pa yuh pui haktiex an hoik seeing brai yuh haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
12 aKix krai ka yix:
phan yix haoh huan ting lhaong,
pon yaox tix cie ka sin ngao pa koe ka daux haktiex in,
leeing ju pon deeh yaox tix tawx kawn praix tix plud,
yix ang doh pon tix mex moeng ka pui an kix,
ang pon tix teem coi kieh kix haoh huan ting lhaong.
13 “Siyiex yix eue,
kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh ka piang yix ku ceu,
hoik mawh kheu jao phi map pa yuh yix,
mai yix hoik tawng,
krax pa ciyang maix ka yix hoik mawh pa iak khaing pa khaw yix tix pon;
maix ca kawn kieh yix yaox tix im ju.
14 Yix ang sang baox lai simi ka gum lox tawm maix,
mai mex moeng ka pui pa caox pa caig an kix yuh ka mawx lie?
Phan yix yuh nin,
maix rien tix song rhawm song rhi,
yuh yix laux leeing,
mai ang lai kieh yix yaox tix koe pie tix kaux,
deuk pie tix kaux.
15 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix mawh pa khaw caw;
viang mawh nan,
maix kawn kieh yix pa mawh pui pa deuk in kix yaox tix im ju.
Yix doh keud ka krax pa lud tix ka maix;
yix ang koe akhuang tix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai maix heue.”
