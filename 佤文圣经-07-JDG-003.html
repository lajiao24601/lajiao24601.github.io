Ceu pui pa kawn ot haktiex Cia-nang
3
Simiang Siyiex prah ceu pui in kix ot ka daux haktiex in,
mawh son sang cheuk tix yaox ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa ang bax yaox tix laig daux naing Cia-nang.
2 Kaing ting mawh son sang gau ka pui Yi-seu-liex ku ju chix puing ka naing,
nie mawh pui pa ang sax yaox tix laig ka daux naing tix bawg kawx khankix.
3 Pui pa hoik prah ot khankix,
mawh simiang veng Fe-li-see phuan kaux,
pui Cia-nang,
pui Si-toeng,
pa ot ka piang gawng Li-pa-noeng si nax gawng Pa-lix He-meung mai diang moing gawng Ha-ma,
hoek ka gah krax pui Si-we.
4 Simiang Siyiex prah ka ceu pui in kix,
mawh son sang cheuk tix yaox ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai kuan tix jhak ka kix,
sang hawt ang kix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex Maw-si tawx ka tax du kix.
5 Kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka daux si nax pui Cia-nang,
pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we,
mai pui Yie-pu-see;
6 kix mex moeng ka ceu pui an kix,
krub ka siyiex kix heue.
Eu-thaw-niiex
7 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hoik piawm ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix;
kix hoik phit ka nawh,
hu krub ka Pa-lix mai Ya-seu-la.
8 Kheu nan,
krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex hoek ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ap kix ka daux tawm taix simiang Ku-sang Li-sa-thiien haktiex Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax.
Kix yuh kaing jhaw simiang Ku-sang Li-sa-thiien daix num.
9 Khaix an kix khiox khaw kok yo ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex tom tawx pui tix kaux hoek tix taoh ka kix.
Pui in mawh Eu-thaw-niiex;
nawh mawh kawn simex Ci-na-see pux Cia-leux.
10 Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang nawh,
nawh tom yuh cao pa prawng see pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
Eu-thaw-niiex hu puing ka naing;
Simiang Siyiex ap simiang Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax ka daux tawm taix nawh,
kieh nawh pex.
11 Kheu nan,
haktiex an hoik pon pa yien yawg tipon num,
tom hoik yum Eu-thaw-niiex heue.
Yi-hux
12 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix deeh phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex kieh Yi-ci-loeng pa mawh simiang Maw-ya ting riang ting rao khaing pui Yi-seu-liex.
13 Yi-ci-loeng yuh paox tau mai pui Ya-meung mai pui Ya-ma-lix,
hoek puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pon ka veng khaox jhie yaong Yie-li-keu.
14 Pui Yi-seu-liex yuh kaing jhaw Yi-ci-loeng kao daix num heue.
15 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex khiox khaw kok yo ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex tom tawx pui tix kaux lih taoh ka kix.
Pui in mawh Yi-hux;
nawh mawh kawn simex Ci-la ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
pa meed siao ka plak taix khawn goix.
Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ploi Yi-hux hu tawx keen ka Yi-ci-loeng simiang Maw-ya.
16 Yi-hux yuh gawn pa lawm khawm ra plak,
plak lang koe tix plak kongjhee,
mai mad ot ka piang caong khawn dawm tix,
mox ot ka daux kraung ceep tix,
17 khaix an,
nawh viex khawng phak an hoek ka Yi-ci-loeng;
nawh mawh pui pa kied kluing.
18 Khaix hoik tawx Yi-hux khawng phak ka nawh,
nawh nhie pui pa klawm khawng phak khankix deeh ing ka nyiex uik.
19 To kaux nawh deeh veeh tix ing plak dee koe riang rong simaox dex Cie-cia,
mai hoek ka Yi-ci-loeng dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Vawng,
aux koe lox grawng pa mox tix ceu,
sang krai ka maix heue.”
Kheu nan,
simiang tawx lox ka pui ot plak goix plak dawm tix:
“Pex lih hu heue!
Kix tom lih hu khaing nawh heue.”
20 Yam an,
simiang Yi-ci-loeng ngawm ka piang choek nyiex lawg dee si kied nong nong;
Yi-hux hu dex ka nawh,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux koe lox pa kaoh khaing Siyiex sang krai ka maix.”
Simiang yaog tix kaoh cung.
21 Yi-hux teei taix khawn goix mai lawd ka gawn pa mox ot ka caong khawn dawm tix lih,
mai blung vaig simiang an kah.
22 Khawm si dux gawn an kawx hoik blung laig,
mai bee hoik kawb lawm gawn an,
phlat hoek tom piang blawg.
Yi-hux prah gawn ot ka daux vaig simiang an.
23 Khaix an lih hu,
mai nyeep siviex,
sau ka saw,
24 lih hu khaing tan.
Pui cau hoek,
mai yaox hoik sau siviex saw tix,
mai keud simiang aing tix daux nyiex.
25 Kix hoik lu lax rhawm khawm pau tix nawh tom rheung,
simiang kawn ca ang bax paoh ka siviex,
kix tom teei jiak saw mai paoh ka siviex,
yaox ka it simiang tix piang tiex,
mai hoik yum heue.
26 Yi-hux hoik ghlua tix hu glang yam kawn krox pui cau simiang ka nawh.
Nawh hu preeng ka dee koe rong riang simaox,
mai to hoek ka yaong Si-yi-la.
27 Khaix hoik bleeh nawh ka yaong Si-yi-la,
piang gawng Yi-fa-liien,
nawh phlu teet tawng,
mai khrawm pui Yi-seu-liex khankix lih hu puing ka naing,
nawh thung pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing gawng,
28 ah nin ka kix:
“Hawt khaix aux!
Simiang Siyiex hoik ap pui Maw-ya pa koeng pex ka daux tawm taix pex.”
Kix tom lih khaing gawng tau mai Yi-hux,
pon cax ka dee tiang rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai kuan giex ka pui Maw-ya,
ang kieh pui tix kaux kawx tiang ka rawm.
29 Ka daux singaix an,
kix pon pauh ka pui Maw-ya tix mheen kaux,
pie tix kaux kawx,
kix ang pon kieh yaox tix luat luan.
30 Daux ngaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex khankix pex ka pui Maw-ya.
Kheu nan,
haktiex in hoik yien yawg daux tix daix num heue.
Sang-cia
31 Khaix Yi-hux,
Sang-cia kawn simex Ya-na,
baox yuh cao prawng see.
Nawh hoik pauh pui Fe-li-see yum liah yieh kaux ka gawng khaox lawm pa grheu moi,
mai taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
