Krax prawng ca Siyiex
2
aPaox grawm eue,
phan maix prawng ca ka pui tang kaux,
mawh pui mawx ang ah,
taug ang pon tix pluat ploi ka maix.
Maix prawng ca ka pui tang kaux,
viang mawh nan,
kaing pa yuh maix hoik awm pa yuh kix yuh nan,
in hoik mawh prawng ca maix ka phi map tix.
2 Ex hoik tawng,
phan mawh Siyiex pa prawng ca pui pa yuh nin ang lud.
3 Viang mawh nan,
paox grawm eue,
ceu pa teei maix hoek tix prawng ca ka pui tang kaux,
jiet mawh kaing pa yuh maix heue!
Maix ce tix sang pon luat luan khaing krax prawng ca Siyiex lie?
4 Mawh ang maix mawh pa map nai ka krax pa caox rhawm nawh,
krax pa va rhawm nawh,
mai krax pa gun yeung nawh lie?
Maix khaw tix tawng krax pa caox rhawm Siyiex,
mawh kheu nhie nawh maix loh rhawm rhi tix heue!
5 Viang mawh nan,
rhawm rhi maix kiien,
mai kieh krax pa song rhawm hoek piang to kaux tix tom nie,
tom hoek yaox tix kham krax ciyang pa cien ka daux krax pa song rhawm Siyiex,
mai ka daux singaix pa prawng ca pa rauh see tag sang hoek.
6 bMawh kheu Siyiex tag sang hawt tix pawk bix awm kaing pa yuh pui kaux mawx kaux an.
7 Koe pui tix blah sau riang tix yuh kaing pa mhawm,
leue ka ceu pa ting ceu,
pa koe min,
mai ka ju pa ang chix laux chix leeing pa kaoh khaing Siyiex;
pui pa yuh nin,
Siyiex tag sang tawx ju leeing ju ka kix.
8 Phan mawh pui pa keud son to kaux tix,
pa tieb tie ka pa mawh pa meh,
pa tieh tix hawt ka pui pa ang rauh ang see,
krax pa song rhawm Siyiex mai krax ciyang tag sang lih hoek piang kix.
9 Can pui pa yuh pa caox caig khawm uik,
tag ang pon tix luat luan khaing krax khi rhawm mai krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
ran mawh pui Yo-thaix sivoe,
khaix hoek ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
10 Viang mawh nan,
can pui pa yuh kaing pa mhawm khawm uik,
nawh tag sang tawx ceu pa ting ceu,
pa koe min,
mai krax pa nyawm ot ka kix,
ran mawh pui Yo-thaix sivoe,
khaix hoek ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
11 cMawh kheu Siyiex kia ngien kawn pui awm paox tix heue.
12 Pui tang ceu tang cad ang koe lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
phan kix yuh phi map,
kix ang laux leeing ka greem lox tat jhawm prawng;
pui Yo-thaix koe lox tat jhawm prawng,
phan kix yuh phi map,
kix tag sang kham krax prawng ca lox tat jhawm prawng.
13 Mawh kheu pui pa prawng ca Siyiex ang koe phi map,
ang mawh ngie pui pa nghiet lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai mawh pui pa hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng khankix.
14 Pui tang ceu tang cad ang koe lox tat jhawm prawng;
viang mawh nan,
yam hoik yuh kix kaing pa khaw mai lox tat jhawm prawng khaing ka daux to kaux tix,
viang kix ang koe lox tat jhawm prawng,
to kaux kix hoik mawh lox tat jhawm prawng ka kix.
15 Kaing pa yuh kix hoik jhi ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa nhie pui yuh,
hoik tiiem ot ka daux rhawm rhi kix.
Rhawm keud rhawm sawn kix kawx hoik jhi grawng mawh ceu in pa khaw;
mawh kheu rhawm rhi kix,
bawg tix blah ne raw ka to kaux kix,
bawg tix blah krai lox son to kaux tix.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
hawt awm gum lox buan pa sidiex aux,
Siyiex tag sang kieh Ye-sux Khrit yuh krax prawng ca ka ceu pa glip ot ka daux rhawm rhi kawn pui heue.
Pui Yo-thaix mai lox tat jhawm prawng
17 Phan mawh nan,
maix sang yuh ka mawx lie?
Maix tang kok tix mawh pui Yo-thaix,
mai een rhawm tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai baox krai lox ting mu grawng pa si doe tix tau mai Siyiex.
18 Maix hoik gau tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
mai hoik tawng ka alo Siyiex,
hoik pon guah tix tawng ka ceu pa khaw mai ceu pa ang khaw.
19 Maix hoik kieh to kaux tix mawh pa thung krax ka pui dug,
mawh pa riang ka pui pa ot ka daux pa faik,
20 mawh pa gau ka pui pa daox aw,
mai baox mawh sara ka pui pa nhum ju.
Maix baox een tix tawng,
mawh kheu hoik koe maix lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si,
maix tom taug pon tawng ka ngai lai mai ka pa mawh pa meh.
21 Maix sidiex gau ka pui tang kaux,
yuh ka mawx maix lai ang sidiex gau ka to kaux tix lie?
Maix sidiex gau pui ang pon tix briex lak,
to kaux maix lai briex lak lie?
22 Maix krai ang pon tix phit vao,
to kaux maix lai phit vao lie?
Maix koeng ka riang rao,
to kaux maix lai briex ka kraung ceu pa ot daux nyiex vad lie?
23 Maix krai lox ting mu grawng hoik koe tix ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex,
hoik map nai ang maix map nai nawh ka pa hoik yuh laux ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex lie?
24 dPhuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Kheu jao pex pui Yo-thaix,
pui tang ceu tang cad hoik map nai ceu kaux Siyiex heue.”
25 Phan maix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
krax pa Pat-hak maix tom tag koe ngawh jum;
phan maix lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
krax pa Pat-hak maix ang koe ceu pie ceeix kawx.
26 Phan jhak tix yaox yuh nin,
pui pa ang bax Pat-hak tix,
phan nawh priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx lox tat jhawm prawng,
khex ang Siyiex kieh nawh mawh pui pa Pat-hak tix lie?
27 Pex pa mawh pui Yo-thaix,
pui tang ceu tang cad tag sang prawng ca ka phi map pex.
Mawh kheu viang pex hoik koe lox tat jhawm prawng kawx,
hoik Pat-hak tix kawx,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik yuh laux ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tiiem tix deuh,
viang to kaux kix ang bax Pat-hak tix,
mai kix hoik priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
28 Tete te lai,
mawh pui mawx pa sawn tix mawh pui Yo-thaix,
mawh pui mawx pa sawn tix Pat-hak lie?
Ang mawh yuh pui Yo-thaix ka plak praix,
rieb krax Pat-hak ka to kaux tix khaing.
29 ePa mawh pui Yo-thaix tete te lai mawh pa kaoh khaing daux rhawm;
phan loh tix krai,
daux rhawm rhi nawh hoik Pat-hak tix,
in hoik mawh kaing pa yuh khuan Siyiex,
ang mawh pa kaoh khaing lox tat jhawm prawng pa tiiem tix deuh.
Pui pa yuh nin krax pa yaog yaw pui ka nawh,
ang mawh pa kaoh khaing kawn pui,
mai hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex heue.
