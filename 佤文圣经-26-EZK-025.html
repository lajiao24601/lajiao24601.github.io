Lox jhai pa krai krax pa ciyang ka Ya-meung
25
aSimiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka pui Ya-meung.
3 Tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
pex simie tix yaox grawng pa seeing brai nyiex buan aux,
simie tix yaox grawng pa riem haktiex Yi-seu-liex mai grawng pa vu vie pui Yo-tax khankix.
4 Mawh kheu pex gaux rhawm ka laux leeing pui,
aux tag sang kieh ceu pui pa ot daux tiag daux tu plak lih singaix hoek tix pon kuang ka pex.
Kix tag sang sum bang,
mai paung ot ka haktiex pex.
Kix tag sang ih ka to ma to koi pex,
mai nyaux ka rawm teeh pex.
5 Aux tag sang kieh veng La-pa tieh tix lih mawh bang dee liang ka-la-uk,
kieh meung Ya-meung lih tix mawh pak liang dee it phung yung.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
6 “Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting lhaong krai yuh nin:
mawh kheu dieh pex taix tix,
gaux rhawm ka laux leeing pui,
ang een haktiex Yi-seu-liex koe ceu,
7 mai aux tag sang ap tix tawx pex ka pui meung tang meung;
kix tag sang hoek tix tud rhim ka pex.
Aux tag sang yuh pex laux leeing,
kieh pex ang pon lih tix mawh meung,
mai ang lai baox koe haktiex cie.
Yuh nin,
pex tom tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Ne raw ka Maw-ya
8 cSimiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Mawh kheu Maw-yab krai ang meung Yo-tax lai tang siyox khaing meung tang meung pie ceeix kawx,
9 aux tag sang kieh pui puing ka veng iak Maw-ya pa ot ka mee haktiex,
khawm veng pa hoik yaw kix rang di,
pa mawh Paw Yie-si-mawx,
Pa-lix Mien,
Ci-lie-thing khankix khawm uik.
10 Aux tag sang kieh ceu pui pa ot daux tiag daux tu plak lih singaix pon kuang ka Maw-yaxd,
khawm Ya-meung kawx hoik pon kuang tau,
mai kieh meung ka piang haktiex piawm ka Maw-yax.
11 Aux tag sang ciyang ka Maw-ya tete.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Ne raw ka Yi-tong
12 eSimiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Pui Yi-tong sau riang tix pawk bix ka pui Yo-tax,
phi map kix hoik kied ting kied lhaong.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai pao yuh nin:
aux tag sang ciyang ka Yi-tong,
mai yuh pui khawm khawng eei brie liang laux leeing.
Jauh ka Thi-mang tom hoek ka haktiex Ti-tang tag sang riem khawm uik;
can pui tag sang pauh tix yum khawm uik.
14 Pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex tag sang pawk bix ka Yi-tong son aux.
Kix tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm aux ka piang pui Yi-tong.
Yuh nin,
Yi-tong tom taug tawng mawh aux pa pawk bix ka nawh.”
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Ne raw ka Fe-li-see
15 fSimiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Pui Fe-li-see sau riang tix pawk bix ka pui pa koeng ka kix,
mai hoik pauh pui koeng yum ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
16 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai pao yuh nin:
aux tag sang puing ka pui Fe-li-see,
mai yuh kix laux leeing,
pie khawm pui pa ot ka haktiex dee lung Fe-li-see khankix tag sang yuh laux leeing khawm uik.
17 Aux tag sang ciyang ka kix tom kin,
mai klup krax pa song rhawm tix piang kix,
pawk bix ka kix.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
