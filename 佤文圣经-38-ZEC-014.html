Yie-lu-sa-leung mai meung piang haktiex
14
Singaix bawg yam ngawm Simiang Siyiex tix prawng ca hoik dex sang hoek,
yam an,
pui tag sang tud rhim laung ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
kawn pui tag sang guah tix ih ka sin ngao pa ang rauh ang see ka sivoe ngai pex.
2 Simiang Siyiex tag sang khrawm ka can meung pa koe khawm uik yuh naing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Pui tag sang teei ka veng,
pui tag sang tud rhim ka nyiex ma,
pui tag sang phit vao ka bun miex,
pui tag sang giex laung ka pui haktiex tix blah,
pui pa deuk khankix ot ka daux veng.
3 Khaix an Simiang Siyiex tag sang lih hu yuh naing ka meung haktiex an kix,
awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan.
4 Ka daux singaix an,
nawh tag sang cung ka piang gawng sime pa koe ka praok Yie-lu-sa-leung plak lih singaix.
Gawng sime koe ngawd ting tix mu tag sang khan si nax lih koe ra plak,
jauh ka plak lih hoek ka plak klip,
gawng pa koe ka daog gah tix hu plak laong,
an tix plak gah tix hu plak seh.
5 Pex tag caw yaog tix to,
mai tiang ka ngawd in;
ngawd in phlat luan ka gawng,
mai nih tix hoek ka daog an tang plak.
Pex tag sang yaog tix to,
awm ka daux singaix bawg yam ot tax du pex tau mai Wu-si-ya simiang pui Yo-tax,
yam to mox tix khaing kla reem yuh nan.
Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux tag sang hoek,
mai viex can kawn cau Siyiex hoek tau heue.
6 Ka daux ngaix an,
ang lai baox koe kuat,
koe rha,
7 mai ang lai baox koe pa faik;
leeing ju leeing phan hoik mawh pon ngaix,
viang mawh pon sawm kawx hoik riang awm pon ngaix yuh nan.
Kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh yam mawx,
mai mawh ngie Simiang Siyiex pa tawng tix kaux khaing heue.
8 aKa daux ngaix an,
rawm klawng im ju tag sang jauh lih khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tix plak jauh lih hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
tix plak jauh lih hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong.
Rawm in tag sang jauh lih hu ang jud ang jai,
yam raux yam rung hoik awm paox.
9 Simiang Siyiex tag sang mawh simiang piang haktiex khawm uik;
kawn pui ku kaux tag sang doh keud ka grawng pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa koe tix kaux khaing;
ceu kaux nawh mawh tix kaux khaing heue.
10 Jauh ka Cia-pa pa koe ka plak laong,
hoek ka siviex Ling pa koe plak seh Yie-lu-sa-leung,
can haktiex khawm pui tag sang yuh kieh si dawm.
Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang lhaong khaing haktiex pa koe ka sivie siveng tix;
veng tag sang jauh ka siviex Piien-ya-ming nih tix hoek ka Rung Siviex (dee in sivoe an siviex prim koe kah),
jauh ka gawng mu Ha-nang-yiex hoek ka dee greeik plai nyiex ma simiang.
11 bKawn pui tag sang nyawm ot nyawm koe ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ang lai baox koe krax pa laux leeing heue.
12 Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh pui meung haktiex pa yuh naing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung an kix pon jhawm saux pa lhat graung pui kah.
Yam kawn im kix,
nex nham kix tag sang laux,
ngai baoh kix mai dak kix kawx tag sang laux heue.
13 Ka daux ngaix an,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kix suk sag,
yaoh yee;
pui hoik giex tix lauh ka pui pa ot ka praok tix ku kaux.
14 Pui Yo-tax tag sang yuh naing son Yie-lu-sa-leung;
kix tag sang tud rhim ka can sin ngao meung khawm uik,
pa mawh khri,
mau,
mai kraung ceep tom nie heue.
15 Brung,
law,
ka-la-uk,
la,
khawm to sat pa koe ka bang dee ot pui koeng khawm uik,
jhawm saux pa lhat pui ka in hoik ot ka khawm uik heue.
16 cKhaix an,
pui meung haktiex pa puing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung pa kawn klawd khankix,
tag sang hoek krub ka simiang ting,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing ku num ka dee an,
khaix an yuh poi nyiex gox bang.
17 Phan koe meung haktiex mawx pa ang hu krub ka simiang ting,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing,
mai rawm lhiex tag ang lih ka haktiex cie kix.
18 Phan pui Ai-cix ang yuh ka poi nyiex gox bang,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh kix pon jhawm saux preng;
meung haktiex pa ang haok yuh ka poi in kawx,
tag sang pon jhawm saux pa awm in nang.
19 Phan pui Ai-cix mai pui meung tang meung ang yuh ka Poi Nyiex-gox-bang,
tag sang pon krax ciyang pa yuh nin heue.
20 Ka daux ngaix an,
pie hing pa koe ka piang brung kawx tag sang tiiem ka gum lox “pa singax mawh cie Simiang Siyiex” pa ah nin.
Aw pa koe daux nyiex buan kawx tag sang singax awm klong pa koe sivoe khu dee tan yuh nan.
21 Aw pa koe daux Yie-lu-sa-leung mai ka Yo-tax khawm uik,
tag sang kieh singax mawh cie Simiang Siyiex khawm uik,
son sang kieh mawh pa siao ka daux pa krub ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing.
Pui pa tan lu krax tan tag sang siao kraung in kix mawh pa ngaik ka nex khawng tan.
Ka daux ngaix an,
daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
ang lai baox koe pui pa coh cai lai gee heue.
