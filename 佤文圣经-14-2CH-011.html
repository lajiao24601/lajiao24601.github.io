Lox jhai krai See-ma-ya pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 12.21–24)
11
Yam hoik hoek Law-paw-ang ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tom khrawm ka pui naing pa kied vao ceu pui Yo-tax mai ceu pui Piien-ya-ming kao daix mheen,
mai keud tix sang kieh kix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
son sang deeh pon tix giex gang ka ceu pui pa ot plak laong.
2 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai See-ma-ya pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex mhong,
3 nhie nawh hu krai ka Law-paw-ang mai ka ceu pui Yo-tax,
ceu pui Piien-ya-ming khawm uik ah nin:
4 “Paw vi hu puing ka pux aik pex pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pex deeh ing ka nyiex tix heue!
Kaing pa lih tix mawh bawg in,
hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing aux.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom nghiet ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
ang bax hu puing ka Yie-law-paw-ang heue.
Law-paw-ang sum ka tab
5 Law-paw-ang paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
nawh sum tab son veng pui Yo-tax mai son veng pui Piien-ya-ming khankix tix blah.
6 Veng in kix mawh Paw-li-haung,
Yi-thang,
Thi-keu-yax,
7 Paw Su,
Saw-keux,
Ya-tu-lang,
8 Cia-theux,
Ma-li-sa,
Si-fu,
9 Ya-to-lai-ying,
La-ciex,
Ya-si-cia,
10 Saw-la,
Ya-ya,
mai Si-paw-loeng.
11 Nawh sum tab pa kaux cong son veng in kix,
ku mu veng hoik tawx simiang naing ot kah,
mai tawx kix ih priex preem,
bee sime,
mai plai.
12 Nawh hoik rien tix deuh dieb tie naok mai pliah son can veng khawm uik,
son sang kieh veng kaux cong.
Yuh nin,
Law-paw-ang tom pon giex gang ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Piien-ya-ming heue.
Cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we gah tix hu ka Yo-tax
13 Cao giex kaing li mai pui Li-we ka daux haktiex Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoek ka haktiex Yo-tax plak seh.
14 Pui Li-we tik ka dee lung pak rib cie kix mai ka haktiex tang dauh,
mai gah tix hu ka haktiex Yo-tax mai Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang mai pui pa plud nawh ang cu kix yuh cao giex kaing li Simiang Siyiex.
15 aYie-law-paw-ang tawx pui cie tix yuh cao giex kaing li,
mai kieh kix yuh kaing krub ka nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak,
mai krub ka siyiex pex mai ka riang kawn moi pa raug to kaux tix.
16 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pa viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix sawg Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hawt pui Li-we hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix.
17 Kaing in hoik kieh meung Yo-tax kaux cong,
glang ka daux loe num,
kawn pui hawt rhawm kawn simex Saw-law-meung pa kaux Law-paw-ang,
mawh kheu krax ot krax koe kix hoik awm singaix bawg yam giex simiang Ta-wue mai simiang Saw-law-meung kaing yuh nan heue.
Nyiex ma Law-paw-ang
18 Law-paw-ang teei Ma-ha-la mawh moeng tix;
keeing Ma-ha-la mawh kawn simex Ta-wue pa kaux Yie-li-maw,
miex nawh mawh kawn saux bun Yie-si,
kawn bun Yi-li-ya pa kaux Ya-pi-hai.
19 Nawh koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Yie-wu-see,
See-ma-li-ya,
Sha-hang.
20 Khaix an Law-paw-ang baox teei tix moeng kawn bun Ya-sa-long pa kaux Ma-cia;
nawh koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ya-pi-yax,
Ya-thaix,
Si-sha,
See-law-mix.
21 Law-paw-ang khawm uik koe moeng kao daix kaux,
moeng iak tix gliah kaux,
kawn simex tix nga daix kaux,
mai kawn bun tix gliah kaux.
Can ka daux moeng mai moeng iak nawh khawm uik,
nawh blawn muh Ma-cia,
22 mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom blawn muh nang ka kawn simex Ma-cia pa kaux Ya-pi-yax,
mai sang ap nawh plud tix mawh simiang khaix tix.
23 Law-paw-ang hoik lhak lhiao tix guah tawx beeh glawm ka kawn simex tix khawm uik,
mai tawx kix ot ka veng Yo-tax mai ka veng Piien-ya-ming pa koe tab ku dauh,
mai hoik tawx kix ih can pa sang lo kix khawm uik,
mai hoik teei kix koe moeng iak tom nie nang heue.
