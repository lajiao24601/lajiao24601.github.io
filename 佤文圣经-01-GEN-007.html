Kheem rawm
7
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Naw-yax:
“Maix mai nyiex ma maix khawm uik laig ka daux reu,
mawh kheu ka daux singaix yam in aux hoik yaox mawh maix pui rauh see sivoe tix.
2 Maix tag viex to sat laig daux reu;
can pa sawn mawh pa singax singeh daux been,
tix ceu mhaing mai siang aliah ku,
pa ang singax singeh,
tix ceu mhaing mai siang tix ku.
3 Sim khankix tix ceu aliah ku khawm mhaing mai siang.
Yuh nin,
to sat ku ceu,
sim saik tag pon tix im ju,
mai rhuat simie tix ka piang haktiex.
4 Khaix hoik naog aliah ngaix,
aux sang kieh lhiex ting lih ka piang haktiex tix pon ngaix tipon sawm,
kieh can ceu pa im ju pa phan yuh aux khankix laux leeing ka piang haktiex.”
5 Naw-yax yuh ku ceu awm gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex heue.
6 Yam koe Naw-yax liah yieh num,
rawm ting kheem ka piang haktiex.
7 aNawh mai kawn simex nawh,
moeng nawh,
mai mun nawh laig daux reu khawm uik,
son sang luat luan khaing pa kheem rawm.
8 Hawt been to sat pa singax singeh mai pa ang singax singeh,
sim saik mai can ceu viag vua,
9 awm gum lox tawx Siyiex ku mawx ku an,
mhaing mai siang laig daux reu tau mai Naw-yax.
10 Khaix aliah singaix,
rawm ting lih kheem ka piang haktiex.
11 bYam koe ju Naw-yax liah yieh num,
daux khix ra kao aliah singaix,
dee keet rawm khaing dee raux khawm uik hoik bih,
siviex rao ma hoik baoh.
12 Lhiex lih ka piang haktiex tipon ngaix tipon sawm.
13 Ka daux ngaix an,
Naw-yax mai kawn simex nawh loe kaux pa mawh Sang,
Hang,
mai Ya-fu,
mai moeng nawh,
mai mun nawh laig daux reu.
14 To sat,
khawng brie,
viag vua mai sim ceu mawx ceu an.
15 Brawm mawx brawm an laig tau mai Naw-yax ka daux reu.
16 Can to sat ku ceu pa mawh mhaing mai siang laig ka daux reu awm gum lox pa tawx Siyiex.
Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex nyeep siviex reu ka kix heue.
17 Rawm ca nied tix kheem ka piang haktiex tipon singaix;
rawm haok huan,
mai yaog reu haok lhaong khaing haktiex.
18 Rawm haok huan si nyek si nyek,
mai reu jauh tix si bloe ka piang rawm.
19 Rawm ting kheem ka haktiex,
pie gawng lua pa koe greem rao ma hoik glip khawm uik.
20 Viang mawh nan,
rawm ca haok huan,
hoik kheem lhaong khaing gawng lua aliah kongjhee.
21 Can to sat piang tiex:
pa mawh khawng brie,
sim,
viag vua mai kawn pui hoik yum uik heue.
22 Can ceu pa im ju piang haktiex kawx yum uik.
23 Simiang Siyiex yuh laux ka can pa im ju piang haktiex:
pa mawh kawn pui,
khawng brie,
to sat viag vua;
mai sim saik;
mawh ngie Naw-yax mai can pa ot tau mai nawh daux reu pa klawd ju.
24 Rawm kheem ka piang haktiex tix yieh tix phuan ngaix heue.
