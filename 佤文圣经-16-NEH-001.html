Lai Ni-si-mi
1
Plak greem mawh pa tiiem ka kaing pa yuh Ni-si-mi kawn simex Ha-cia-li-yax.
Ni-si-mi khi rhawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
Yam mawh Ya-ta-shiie-si Vawng Paw-see ka daux tix nga num khix Ci-see-liox, a aux pa mawh Ni-si-mi paung ot ka veng ting Su-sang haktiex Paw-see.
2 Pux aik aux Ha-na-ni kaoh khaing Yo-tax mai pui tix cub hoek ka veng Su-sang.
Aux chawk paox ceu cad tix pui Yo-thaix pa deeh hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix ka Pa-pi-loengb,
mai grawh grawng Yie-lu-sa-leung ka kix.
3 Kix krai ka aux:
pui an kix pa hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tixc,
hoik si pup ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
hoik kham ka krax pa kaig pa tua.
Kix baox deeh krai ka aux,
guh veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik laux,
siviex veng hoik gleeing.
4 Aux mhong grawng kaing in kix,
tom nyah tix ngawm,
mai laux rhawm tom lih rawm ngai heue.
Aux hoik laux rhawm tom nie singaix,
mai ang ih ang nyaux.
Aux rawg buan ka Siyiex ah nin:
5 “Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex piang rao ma eue,
maix mawh pa kied ting pa lhat graung pui kah;
maix hoik yuh sijax mukheu kaux cong tau mai pui pa hoik muh ka maix mai pa hoik priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap maix khankix.
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw son siliex maix pui hun Yi-seu-liex leeing ngaix leeing sawm,
maix kia ngien ka aux,
nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan liak!
Aux krai doh yix pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh ka phi map;
tax du aux mai aux hoik yuh phi map khawm uik.
7 Yix viex rhawm pa caox pa caig tieb tie ka maix,
ang bax hawt ka gum lox ping nyap maix,
mai ang bax pon giex kaux cong ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx maix Maw-si krai ka yix.
8 dKhiox khaw maix doh keud ka pa hoik krai maix ka Maw-si ah nin:
‘Phan pex pui Yi-seu-liex ang nghiet ka lox aux,
aux tag sang kieh pex prung prang tix hoek ka meung tang meung khankix.
9 eViang mawh nan,
phan pex deeh veeh tix hoek ka aux dee in,
mai priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx aux,
aux tag sang viex pex deeh hoek;
viang pex hoik vung viang tix hu ku dauh ku dee,
aux kawx kawn chix viex pex deeh hoek ka haktiex pa hoik laug aux pex krub tix dee in heue.’
10 “Simiang eue,
in kix hoik mawh siliex maix khawm uik,
pui cie maix,
pa mawh pui pa hoik teei maix siawp sibrawm pa ting tix taoh kah.
11 Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg aux buan,
khawm ka can lox rawg buan siliex pa lhat graung yung yi ka maix khankix.
Khiox khaw maix teem coi aux pon krax pa nhim ot,
kieh Vawng paoh krax pa caox rhawm son aux.”
Ka daux baux bawg yam an,
aux hoik mawh cao kaing pa tawx Vawng nyaux plai heue.
