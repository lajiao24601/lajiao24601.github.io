Ni-si-mi prawng ka kaing ceu pa nu khankix
5
Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
pui haoh pui hun,
simex bun hoik koe ku kaux,
mai jauh tix si briem ka paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yo-thaix.
2 Tix blah ah nin:
“Dee in yix hoik koe pui tix nyiex tom haoh tom hun yuh nin,
mai tag caw tix lo ka nghox koi ex tom taug yaox tix im ju heue!”
3 Tix blah ah nin:
“Ex tag caw tix tang ka ma kaung tix,
si peem plix siiet tix,
nyiex ma tix,
ex tom taug yaox tix kub ih nghox koi heue.”
4 Pui tix blah kawx ah nin:
“Ex tag caw tix yaog mau tawx keen kaung ma mai keen si peem plix siiet ka Vawng.
5 Ex mai paox ceu paox cad ex pui Yo-thaix,
hoik mawh pui tix ceu khaing.
Kawn simex kawn bun ex mai kawn simex kawn bun kix ang lai tang siyox.
Yuh ka mawx lai khin kawn simex kawn bun ex hu yuh siliex cawng.
Kawn bun ex tix blah hoik coh mawh siliex cawng,
ex ang lai tawng pa sang yuh tix,
mawh kheu kaung ma ex mai si peem plix siiet ex pui hoik tud hu heue.”
6 Aux mhong lox ngia kix mai lox si briem kix,
aux hoik kied song rhawm,
7 amai but rhawm tix prawng ka kaing in.
Aux ne raw ka simiang pui hun mai ka simiang naing khankix ah nin:
“Pex lai nieb gut ka paox ceu paox cad tix yuh nin!”
Aux khrawm pui hun hoek prawng ka kaing in,
8 mai ah nin ka kix:
“Yix hoik sau riang sau rao tix yuh,
son sang deeh taoh ka pux aik Yo-thaix pa hoik khin tix coh to kaux tix ka pui tang ceu tang cad khankix.
Yam in pex lai kawn nieb khin pux aik tix coh to kaux tix ka pex,
pa mawh paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yo-thaix yuh nin!”
Cao kaing khankix ang nye ang nyux,
ang koe lox pa sang kraix heue.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
aux baox ah nin:
“Pex yuh kaing in hoik mawh pa ang khaw tete!
Pex khaw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
mai hu yuh kaing pa khaw tix yuh ang mawh lie?
Paw kieh pui tang ceu pa koeng ka ex,
koe krax nyiah ka ex.
10 Aux hoik sax tawx paox ceu paox cad ex yaog ka nghox koi;
paox grawm aux mai pui yuh kaing aux kawx hoik tawx kix yaog yeung.
Yam in,
ex tag caw tix pluat ka can ceuk pa koe khawm uik,
11 mai preet tix tik ka can ceuk kix khawm uik,
mawh mau mawh nghox koi ang ah,
mawh plai mawh bee ang ah,
paw lai kieh kix plud.
Nied tix deeh tawx kaung ma,
si peem plix siiet,
si peem sime,
mai nyiex ma kix ka kix heue.”
12 Cao kaing pawk lox ah nin:
“Yix cu tix hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai maix,
deeh tawx sin ngao cie kix,
mai ang lai teei ceuk khaing kix heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
aux kok ka cao giex kaing li hoek,
mai nhie simiang naing khankix tiex tu ka plak sivoe ngaix kix,
son sang priex tix hawt ka lox pa ap aux nu.
13 Khaix an,
aux yah ka maox rhawtb pa fua ot ka nyoi tix,
mai khruah ah nin:
“Siyiex tag sang khruah tix tik ka pui pa ang hawt ka lox tiex tu in,
mai teei hu ka nyiex ma nawh,
khawm can pa koe nawh khawm uik,
mai kieh nawh ang lai koe ceu tid tix heue.”
Can pui pa ot dee an khawm uik ah nin:
“A-meung!”
Khaix an yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex.
Pui haoh pui hun hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik ah kix tix deuh heue.
Ni-si-mi yuh son pui haoh pui hun ang yuh son to kaux tix
14 Yam mawh aux simiang haktiex Yo-tax ka daux kao ra num,
pa mawh yam jauh Ya-ta-shiie-si tix mawh Vawng ka daux tix nga num hoek tix ngoe ra num,
aux mai pui nyiex pui ma aux ang bax ih ka priex preem pa khaw simiang haktiex tix sang pon.
15 Pa mawh simiang haktiex ka sivoe aux,
hoik mawh pa klawm beeh glawm pa cien son pui haoh pui hun;
kix nhie pui haoh pui hun tawx priex preem,
mai plai mau luah tipon plah.
Pie sliex kix kawx hoik gut ka pui haoh pui hu.
Viang mawh nan,
aux ang yuh nin,
mawh kheu aux lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex.
16 Aux hoik teei riang rao tix khawm uik,
yuh ka kaing pa deeh cox sum ka guh veng in;
aux ang bax deuh ka sin ngao cie tix.
Can siliex aux khawm uik kawx,
hoik yuh ka kaing in.
17 Pui Yo-thaix mai simiang pa sawm tau mai aux,
hoik mawh pui been tix yieh tix phuan kaux,
khaix an kawn koe pui meung pa ot sivie siveng ex khankix hoek ka aux.
18 Ku singax,
aux hoik rien khre ka moi mhaing tix mu,
yung pa kluing liah mu,
mai ia tom nie.
Yam hoek kao singaix,
aux tawx ka plai khraox tix bawg.
Viang mawh nan kawx,
aux ang bax mhaing ngawh riang pa khaw simiang haktiex tix sang pon,
mawh kheu aux hoik tawng,
pui haoh pui hun hoik cien beeh glawm heue.
19 Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix hawt tix yuh awm alo pa mhawm maix,
mai doh keud ka can kaing pa yuh aux son pui haoh pui hun heue.
