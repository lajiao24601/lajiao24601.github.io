Ceu pa mhawm ping nyia cuyi
4
Pui pa nhum ju eue,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa sidiex keeing tix;
maix bre rhawm rhi tix rieb mhawm mhawm,
tom tag chix tawng sidawng.
2 Grawng pa sidiex gau aux ka maix hoik mawh pa mhawm khawm uik,
maix tag caw priex tix tawng khawm uik.
3 Yam kawn mawh aux kawn nyawm,
aux hoik mawh kawn pa kied muh miex keeing tix tix kaux khaing,
4 keeing aux tom sidiex gau aux ah nin:
“Tag caw priex ka lox sidiex lox aux,
ang pon tix piawm.
Priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawm aux,
maix tom taug koe ju pa kaux cong.
5 Tag caw tix sawg ka ping nyia cuyi mai krax keud krax sawn;
ang pon tix piawm mai ang pon tix nieh jhak ka gum lox aux.
6 Paw vi tik vud ka ping nyia cuyi,
nawh tom taug chix kia ngien ka maix;
phan maix muh ka nawh,
nawh tom tag chix kia ngien ka maix.
7 Pa sawg ka ping nyia cuyi hoik mawh kaing pa kied ting ceu tete,
tag caw tix teei can pa koe maix khawm uik loh ka krax keud krax sawn.
8 Pa muh ka ping nyia cuyi,
ping nyia cuyi tag sang kieh nawh pon mhawm kaing yuh;
pa muh ka ping nyia cuyi,
ping nyia cuyi tag sang kieh nawh pon min plak ngai heue.
9 Ping nyia cuyi tag sang mawh mhok khri min plak ngai ka piang kaing maix heue.”
10 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
nghiet lox krai aux,
phan maix priex tix hawt ka lox aux,
maix tag yaox tix lang ju lang phan.
11 Aux hoik sidiex gau maix ka daux krax ping nyia cuyi,
mai thung maix lawng krax pa rauh see kawn pui.
12 Phan maix chix priex tix hawt krax pa lhak lhiao mhawm mhawm,
yam hu krax maix tag ang yaox krax pa sideeing tix goh.
13 Tag caw priex tix deuh kaux cong ka can pa hoik gau maix khankix;
krax pa hoik gau maix hoik awm mawh krax pa im ju maix.
14 Krax lawng pui caox,
maix paw vi lawng;
krax chix krax tawng pui pa caox pa caig,
maix paw vi gau tix ka heue.
15 Tag caw mox tix khaing pa caox pa caig,
paw vi hawt;
lawng ka krax pa lawng maix khaing,
tieb tie yuh kaing ceu pa caox mai kix.
16 Pui caox phan ang yuh kaing ceu pa caox,
kix ang chix laig it laig tom;
phan ang yaox tix yuh pui laux leeing,
kix ang chix laig it laig tom.
17 Pa caox pa caig mawh priex preem ka kix,
krax cau nyhau mawh plai nyaux kix heue.
18 Krax lawng pui rauh pui see awm pa riang praix pon ngawb,
nied jhan nied riang,
tom hoik hoek pon ngaix.
19 Krax lawng pui caox hoik awm yam faik praix pon sawm;
kix hoik sideeing tix goh,
mai kawn ang tawng mawh ceu pa tix pa kieh kix sideeing tix goh heue.
20 Pui pa nhum ju eue,
tag caw tix bre rhawm tix ka lox pa krai aux,
nghiet ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux.
21 Paw vi kieh kix ghlua tix hu,
tag caw priex tix deuh,
mai tiiem ot daux rhawm tix.
22 Mawh kheu phan hoik pon ka kix,
hoik mawh pon krax pa im ju,
pon krax pa brax kua.
23 Rien rhawm tix ka ceu pa keud pa sawn tix,
mawh kheu ju hoik kaoh khaing rhawm keud rhawm sawn.
24 Lox pa ang mawh ang meh ang pon tix kraix pie tix gum;
lox pie lox lhien kawx ang pon tix krai pie tix gum.
25 Tag caw tix teei rhawm pa rauh pa see jhak ka plak sivoe ngai,
paw vi laux rhawm laux rhi.
26 aKaing pa keud maix tix sang yuh,
tag caw tix giex kaux cong,
kaing yuh maix tom taug ang chix lud loe.
27 Tag caw teei tix tik ka ceu pa caox pa caig,
mai hu rauh ka plak sivoe ngai,
paw vi singai hu khaing krax rauh krax see pie tix diang caong heue.
