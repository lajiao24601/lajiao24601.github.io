Tang-yi-li ot ka daux laung sivai sang si
6
Simiang Ta-lio-see tawx simiang haktiex tix yieh tix nga kaux kuang ka piang meung haktiex tix.
2 Khaix an,
nawh baox tawx Tang-yi-li mai pui tang kaux ra kaux kuang ka cub simiang haktiex in kix,
son sang giex siawp sibrawm simiang kaux cong.
3 Tang-yi-li ang koe miex glang tix,
nawh kawn lheu mhawm krax keud krax sawn khaing cao kuang kaing tang kaux,
mai pui kawn lheu muh nawh khaing simiang haktiex.
Mawh kheu kied lhak lhiao chix yuh Tang-yi-li ka kaing jaw,
simiang tom tawx nawh kuang ka piang meung tix khawm uik.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
cao kuang kaing tang kaux mai simiang haktiex sau riang tix sawg dee lud nawh yam yuh nawh kaing meung,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang yaox dee lud nawh,
kheu hoik mawh Tang-yi-li pui pa rauh rhawm tix kaux,
mai ang koe dee lud pie ceeix kawx.
5 Kix tom kum kawng paox tix ah nin:
“Lheu khaing yuh ex Tang-yi-li ka daux been li krax yum rhawm nawh,
ex ang lai yaox grawng sang deuh phi map piang nawh heue.”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
kix khrawm paox tix hu ka simiang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Ta-lio-see lang ju lang phan heue!
7 Yix cao kuang kaing,
simiang haktiex,
simiang haktiex na bat ra,
mai can simiang khawm uik yuh kaing ka daux meung cie Vawng,
mai hoik khaw rhawm rhi ka kieh maix Vawng tawx lox pa tieb tie tix ceu hu.
Pui mawx ang ah,
ka daux tix ngoe singaix ang pon tix rawg buan ka siyiex tix kaux kawx,
mai ang pon tix khiox khaw ka pui tix kaux kawx,
mai kieh pui khiox khaw ka Vawng tix kaux khaing.
Pui mawx thiang ka lox in,
tag sang tik nawh ka daux laung sivai sang si.
8 Khiox khaw Vawng tawx gum lox in hu,
mai tiiem ceu kaux tix ot daux,
son sang koe ceu.
Phan hawt lox tat jhawm prawng Mi-ti-yax mai lox tat jhawm prawng Paw-see,
lox tawx pa yuh nin ang lai pon tix tieh loh kah.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang Ta-lio-see tiiem ceu kaux tix ot ka daux lai in.
10 Yam mhong Tang-yi-li grawng hoik tiiem Vawng ceu kaux tix ot daux lai in,
nawh tom ing ka nyiex.
Ka piang braux nyiex nawh daux lawg koe ka tix mu,
daux doh siviex an veeh tix hu plak Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Tang-yi-li tom grux ngawng tix ka sivoe ngai daux doh siviex pa hoik paoh tix,
mai hawt tix yuh awm pa hoik yuh nawh jheeng jheeng,
tix ngaix loe bawg yaog yaw mai rawg buan ka Siyiex heue.
11 Pui pa koeng ka Tang-yi-li yaog grawng pa rawg nawh buan ka Siyiex,
12 tom hu deuh phi map piang nawh ka simiang dee an.
Kix ah nin ka simiang:
“Vawng eue,
maix hoik tawx lox pa tieb tie tix ceu,
mai hoik tat,
pui mawx ang ah,
ka daux tix ngoe singaix ang pon tix rawg buan ka siyiex tix kaux kawx,
ang pon tix khiox khaw ka pui tix kaux kawx,
tag caw tix khiox khaw ka Vawng tix kaux khaing.
Pui mawx lieh lheu ka gum lox in,
tag sang taing ka daux laung sivai sang si heue.”
Simiang ah nin:
“Mawh,
in hoik mawh gum lox pa tawx aux;
hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Mi-ti-yax mai lox tat jhawm prawng Paw-see,
ang pon tix tieh loh ka heue.”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
kix ah nin ka simiang:
“Tang-yi-li pa giex hoek khaing ka daux pui Yo-tax ang yung yi ka maix Vawng,
ang hawt ka gum lox lai pa tawx maix Vawng hu,
viang mawh nan,
nawh kawn hawt tix rawg buan tix ngaix loe bawg yuh nan heue.”
14 Simiang mhong lox in kied song rhawm rhi kah,
rhawm nawh simie tix taoh ka Tang-yi-li,
tom hoek faik bo praix nawh hoik ca keud ka grawng kaing in.
15 Khaix an pui an baox khrawm paox tix hoek ka simiang ah nin:
“Simiang,
maix hoik tawng,
phan hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Mi-ti-yax mai lox tat jhawm prawng Paw-see,
lox lai pa hoik tawx simiang hu ang pon tix tieh loh ka heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang tawx lox nhie pui giex ka Tang-yi-li,
mai taing nawh ka daux laung sivai sang si.
Simiang ah nin ka Tang-yi-li:
“Kieh Siyiex pa hoik yum rhawm maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix taoh ka maix heue.”
17 Ka moing daux an hoik teei simaox tawb si nyeep kah;
simiang teei cum tix mai cum kawn cau pa ting tix khankix jhawp kah,
son ang lai sang kieh pui pon taoh Tang-yi-li lih.
18 Simiang deeh ing ka nyiex haw tix,
nawh ang laig it laig tom leeing lang sawm,
ang ih ka priex preem,
mai ang lo krax gaux rhawm tix ceu kawx heue.
19 Yam hoik riang praix,
simiang tom kaoh,
mai nied tix hu ka daux laung sivai sang si.
20 Yam hoek simiang ka praok daux laung,
nawh kok yo khawm hax rhawm tix ah nin:
“Tang-yi-li,
siliex Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju eue!
Siyiex pa yum rhawm maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
hoik ang hoik taoh maix luat luan khaing daux jheeh sivai sang si lie?”
21 Tang-yi-li pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng lang ju lang phan heue!
22 Siyiex ploi kawn cau tix hoek thox daux jheeh sivai sang si,
mai kieh nawh ang pon yuh aux laux mat.
Siyiex yuh nin,
mawh kheu hoik tawng nawh aux ang bax koe dee lud loe,
aux ang sax yuh kaing pa phit ka Vawng heue.”
23 Simiang kied gaux rhawm,
nawh tom tawx lox nhie pui cau tix taoh Tang-yi-li lih.
Mawh kheu nan kix tom bluh Tang-yi-li lih khaing daux,
mai hoik yaox grawng ang nawh bax mat pie ceeix kawx,
mawh kheu yum rhawm nawh ka Siyiex.
24 Khaix an,
simiang tawx lox nhie pui giex ka can pui pa deuh phi map ka piang Tang-yi-li khawm uik,
mai taing sau kix mai khawm moeng kix kawn simex kawn bun kix ka daux laung sivai sang si khawm uik.
Sivoe sang cawd kix ka daux kawg piang tiex,
sivai sang si hoik rieb tix si ngam ka kix,
mai kiied si-ang kix phawng vid heue.
25 Khaix an,
simiang Ta-lio-see krai ka meung piang haktiex ku meung,
ka pui ku ceu,
ka pui pa si kah ka lox pui ku ceu khankix ah nin:
“Kieh pex nyawm ot nyawm koe heue!
26 Yam in aux tawx lox ka meung ka kawn pui pa hoik giex aux kaing ka piang,
tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex Tang-yi-li heue!
Nawh mawh Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju;
nawh tag sang giex kaing leeing ju.
Sibrawm nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan;
siawp sibrawm cie nawh ang chix uik chix leeing.
27 Nawh taoh teem,
nawh teem coi,
mai yuh kaing mhai pa daox pa am ka piang rao ma mai ka greem haktiex heue.
Nawh taoh Tang-yi-li luat luang khaing daux jheeh sivai sang si heue.”
28 Jauh yam an,
ka daux bawg yam pa giex simiang Ta-lio-see mai Sai-lu-seex simiang Paw-see ka kaing,
kaing yuh Tang-yi-li pon yaox tix mhawm khawm uik heue.
