Lai Yo-tax
1
aAux mawh siliex Ye-sux Khrit,
Yo-tax pux aik Ya-keux.
Aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka pui pa hoik laug Siyiex,
pa ot ka daux krax muh Siyiex,
mai pa hoik kia ngien Ye-sux Khrit ka heue.
2 Kieh Siyiex tawx krax caox rhawm,
pa nhim,
mai krax muh krax rhawm tom nie ka pex heue.
Cao gau pa pie
3 Paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
aux hoik dawg simie tix tiiem lox lai krai lao tau mai pex grawng krax taoh pa dex dawt tau mai ex.
Viang mawh nan,
aux keud tix yaox,
tag khaw tix krai ka pex dum mawx dum an,
simie pex niied tix ca yuh son Siyiex tix bawg khaing,
tag sang pon yaox tix grum tawx ka kawn Khrit ka daux naing krax yum rhawm heue.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
koe pui pa ang koe krax yung yi tix blah,
briex tix laig ka daux ex,
kix teei krax pa kah ka gum lox buan son Siyiex kieh mawh pa mox ka kaing ang mhawm pa yuh tix.
Kix tieb tie ka simiang pa koe tix kaux,
pa mawh Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
Ka daux singaix pa hoik luan tom rheeng,
phuk lai sigang khri hoik grox krai sang pon kix krax ciyang heue.
5 cViang pex hoik tawng khawm uik,
aux ca kawn sang krai ka pex yuh nin:
Simiang Siyiexb hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex bheeik luan khaing Ai-cix yuh ka mawx,
khaix an yuh laux leeing ka pui pa ang yum rhawm khankix.
6 Paw vi piawm grawng pa ang kawn cau Siyiex an kix cu tix yuh kaing pa yuh tix,
mai lih hu khaing dee ot tix,
kheu nan tag kieh kix ot ka dee pa kied raux pa faik,
pa phrawk ka maox ceng pa ang lai chix yah ka leeing ju.
Siyiex laung kix ot ka dee an,
mai krox jun ka daux singaix pa prawng ca.
7 dKawn koe veng Saw-to-ma,
Eu-maw-la,
mai pui pa ot dex ka baoh veng an,
kix awm kawn cau Siyiex an kix yuh nan,
kix yuh krax phit vao,
mai hawt ka alo nex nham pa tieh ka been pa tawx koe ka daux haktiex,
kheu nan tom kham krax ciyang pa mawh ngu pa ang chix yeet leeing ju.
Kaing in hoik chix tix kieh mawh pa sidiex ka pui ku kaux heue.
8 Pui pa ang koe krax pa yung yi in kix kawx yuh nan nang.
Rhawm keud kix pa seeing brai hoik kieh kix yuh phi map,
seeing yuh to kaux tix;
kix nunai ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex,
map nai ka cao pa koe min plak ngai ka piang rao ma.
9 ePie cao kaing kawn cau Siyiex pa mawh Mi-cia-leux kawx,
yam thung thiang nawh tau mai phi caox son to kaux yum Maw-si,
mai phi caox an ang pon teei lox pa map nai ne raw ka nawh,
ah ngie nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ne raw ka maix heue!”
10 Viang mawh nan,
pui in kix lai map nai ka kaing ceu pa ang kix tawng,
hoik awm to sat yuh nan,
yuh kaing ceu pa kieh to kaux tix laux leeing khankix.
11 fPui in kix tag sang laux leeing heue!
Kix hoik lawng krax pa lawng Kai-yin.
Kheu son mau khri,
kix hoik cawd ka daux krax pa lud loe yuh Pa-lang,
hoik tieh vheen laux leeing awm Kheu-la yuh nan.
12 Pui in kix yuh krax gaux rhawm ang koe krax pa kaig tua ka daux pa sawm tau mai pex,
kieh to kaux tix seeing brai,
awm cao liang yung pa keud son sang sak vaig tix yuh nan khaing.
Kix awm lon pai awm pa phlu bhaung hu,
pa ang tawx rawm lhiex lih,
mai baox awm nang khaox plix pa ang chix plix yam hoek bawg yam caw tix plix yuh nan,
pa hoik laoh tix tik khawm riah,
pa kroh yum khawm uik.
13 Kaing yuh kix pa kaig pa tua awm bai puk pak rawm sigrit sigra yuh nan.
Kix awm simuing pa ang lai hu ka daux rhoi tix,
mai cawd ka dee pa kied raux pa faik pa hoik deuh Siyiex son kix leeing ju heue.
14 gYi-naw pa mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-tang ka daux aliah ju hoik dawg grox krai ka son pui in kix,
Nawh ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
Simiang Siyiex viex kawn cau Siyiex pa singax singeh hoek tau mai tix kaing rheng mheen.
15 Nawh tag sang prawng ca ka pui khawm uik,
mai ciyang ka can pui phi map pa ang koe krax yung yi;
mawh kheu kaing pa yuh kix ang koe krax pa yung yi,
mai baox krai lox pa ang yung yi lud loe ka Siyiex heue.”
16 Pui in kix salao ka pui tang kaux jheeng jheeng,
mai brax si briem ka pui tang kaux;
kix hawt ka alo nex nham pa caox tix,
mai yie krai lox ting lhaong,
son sang kieh tix pon buan son ka daux pa nyaw pui tang kaux heue.
Pa ne raw mai pa sidiex gau
17 Viang mawh nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
paw vi piawm ka gum lox pa hoik krai kawn cau Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit khankix.
18 hKix ah nin ka pex:
“Ka daux singax pa si dauh been koe pui tag sang lih hoek,
hawt ka alo pa caox caig to kaux tix,
mai ka alo nex nham pa ang koe krax yung yi nyiah ka pex.”
19 Pui in kix yuh simah lih,
hawt ka daux alo to kaux tix,
ang koe Khuan Singax Singeh.
20 Phan mawh pex,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
pex tag caw kieh tix kaux cong ka daux krax yum rhawm pa kied singax tom si dauh,
rawg buan ka daux riang rao Khuan Singax Singeh,
21 ot ka daux krax muh Siyiex jheeng jheeng,
een rhawm tix ka krax pa caox rhawm Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit ka pex,
mai tawx ju ka pex leeing ju heue.
22 Ka son pui pa haoh rhawm khankix,
pex tag caw tix caox rhawm ka kix.
23 Koe pui tix blah,
pex tag caw tix taoh kix lih khaing daux ngu;
baox koe pui tix blah,
pex tag caw tix caox rhawm ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
tag caw tix rien khre tix,
pie kraung ceep pa hoik preeh kix ka daux alo nex nham an tag caw tix seeing ka heue.
Lox rawg buan pa yaog yaw
24 Siyiex pon kia ngien pex ang kieh pex sideeing tix goh,
mai kieh pex ang koe dee cud,
mai pon kieh pex gaux rhawm gaux rhi hoek ka sivoe min plak ngai nawh.
25 Siyiex pa koe tix kaux khaing,
hoik taoh ex ka daux Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit.
Kieh nawh pon min plak ngai,
pa ting sibrawm,
riang rao,
mai siawp sibrawm,
jauh dix dix kaing mheen ju tom hoek yam in,
mai leeing ju leeing phan heue.
A-meung!
