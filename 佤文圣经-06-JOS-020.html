Veng mox
20
aSimiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax
2 kieh nawh ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex:
“Pex tag hawt ka lox pa kieh aux Maw-si krai ka pex,
laug ka veng tix blah kieh mawh veng mox.
3 Can pa ang su tix pauh pui yum,
chix tix hu dee an,
mox tix ka pui pa pawk bix.
4 Pui in kix chix tix mox tix ka daux veng tix veng,
hoek diang siviex veng dee prawng ca ka pui,
mai krai kuad song khankix mhong sidawng grawng kaing.
Kuad song khankix chix tix kieh nawh laig,
tawx dee ot nawh,
yuh nin,
nawh chix tix ot dee an.
5 Phan cao pawk bix hawt leue dee an,
pui veng an ang pon tix tawx pui an lih,
mai tag caw tix mox ka nawh;
mawh kheu nawh ang koe dee phit lud ka pui yum an,
ang mawh su tix pauh nawh yum.
6 Nawh chix tix ot daux veng an,
tom hoek deeh prawng ca sidawng,
krox khaix yum cao giex kaing li pa ting,
yam an taug chix tix ing ka daux nyiex nawh heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak klip singaix haktiex Na-fu-tha-lix Cia-li-lix,
mai laug ka Ci-ti-see,
ka haktiex Yi-fa-liien laug ka See-ciien,
ka haktiex Yo-tax laug ka veng Ya-pa (Pa mawh veng Si-paw-loeng).
8 Ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
ka piang gawng daux tiag plak lih singaix Yie-li-keu,
ka haktiex Lui-piien laug ka veng Pi-si,
ka Ci-liex haktiex Cia-teux laug ka La-mawx,
ka Pa-sang haktiex Ma-na-si laug ka veng Keu-lang,
kieh mawh veng mox.
9 Veng mox in kix sum son can pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui pa ot ka daux kix.
Can pui pa ang su tix pauh ka pui,
chix tix mox tix ka daux veng in,
mox tix ka pui pa pawk bix;
pui pa pawk bix ang pon tix pauh ka nawh,
lheu khaing nawh hoik prawng ca tix sidawng heue.
