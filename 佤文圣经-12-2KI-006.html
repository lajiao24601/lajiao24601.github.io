Sawg ka lawm moe
6
Koe tix singaix,
cub cao jhai pa thung Yi-li-sa khankix salao ah nin:
“Dee paung ot ex kied iak.
2 Khiox khaw maix kieh ex hu kid khaox khankix ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai baox sum nyiex tang mu khankix heue.”
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Chix tix heue.”
3 Koe pui ka daux kix tix kaux,
nhie Yi-li-sa hu tau mai kix,
Yi-li-sa cu ka lox nawh.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
kix hu tau,
hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai kix jauh tix yuh kaing.
5 Koe pui tix kaux yam kid khaox,
lawm moe cie nawh cawd daux rawm.
Nawh kok yo Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Sara,
sang yuh aux ka mawx lie?
Moe an mawh pa vai aux heue.”
6 Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex chawk nawh ah nin:
“Cawd dee mawx lie?”
Pui an ci nawh yaox.
Yi-li-sa kid kak khaox tix gawng,
tik ka daux rawm,
lawm moe an tom si bloe haok plak piang.
7 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Teei haok heue.”
Pui an tom tawx taix tix sut ka lawm moe heue.
Pui naing Shui-li-yax ang pex daux naing
8 Simiang pui Shui-li-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex puing paox tix.
Nawh kum kawng tau mai siliex,
mai laug tix sum nyiex bang ka haktiex tix dauh.
9 Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex tawx lai ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
ang cu nawh hu dex ka baoh dee an,
mawh kheu pui Shui-li-yax hoik mox tix kuan kix ka dee an.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex krai lox pa hoik krai pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex ka pui pa paung ot haktiex an khankix,
kix tom rien khre tix.
Kaing pa awm paox hoik lih tix mawh miex bawg mawx heue.
11 Simiang Shui-li-yax kied suk sag rhawm kheu jao kaing in;
nawh khrawm ka siliex,
mai chawk kix ah nin:
“Ka daux pex mawh mawx pa kieh pui Yi-seu-liex tawng ka grawng ex?”
12 Ka daux kix koe pui tix kaux ah nin:
“Vawng,
ang mawh yix.
Mawh cao jhai Yi-li-sa pa krai ka simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh hoik krai lox pa mox maix tix krai daux nyiex it tix ka nawh heue.”
13 Simiang tawx lox ah nin:
“Hu sawg si kaw ot nawh dee mawx;
aux sang giex ka nawh heue.”
Koe pui krai simiang mhong,
Yi-li-sa ot ka To-thang.
14 Simiang tom ploi pui tix cub mai ling brung hu dee an.
Kix hoek dee an yam hoik bo praix,
dum mawx dum an lai awm ka veng an.
15 Ka daux ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
siliex Yi-li-sa kaoh khaing it,
mai lih hu plak praix nyiex,
yaox ka pui naing Shui-li-yax mai brung cie nawh,
ling brung cie nawh,
lai lawm ka veng.
Nawh kok yo ah nin:
“Sara,
laux leeing ex phaox.
Sang yuh ex ka mawx lie?”
16 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Paw vi lhat!
Pui ex plak in kawn nie hun khaing pui kix plak an heue.”
17 Khaix an,
nawh rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paoh ngai nawh,
mai kieh nawh yaox.”
Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox khiox khaw Yi-li-sa.
Siliex Yi-li-sa yaog kaing tix jhak,
nawh yaox ka praok caong gawng hoik naog ka ling ngu mai brung ngu,
ot vung viang Yi-li-sa heue.
18 Yam puing pui Shui-li-yax tix hoek plak in,
Yi-li-sa rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh ngai pui in kix dug!”
Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox rawg buan Yi-li-sa,
mai kieh ngai kix dug.
19 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-sa hoek ka dee ot kix ah nin:
“Pex lud krax hu tix;
in ang mawh veng pa sang sawg pex.
Hawt khaix aux,
aux viex pex hu ka pui pa sang sawg pex dee an.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-sa viex kix hoek ka Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
20 Yam hoik laig kix daux veng,
Yi-li-sa tom rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paoh ngai kix,
mai kieh kix yaox.”
Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox khiox khaw Yi-li-sa mai deeh kieh ngai kix riang.
Kix taug yaox ot to kaux tix ka daux veng Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
21 Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex yaox pui Shui-li-yax,
tom chawk Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Sang khaw aux tix pauh kix yum lie?
Sang khaw aux tix pauh kix yum lie?”
22 Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Ang chix!
Pie pui naing pa pon giex maix daux naing khankix kawx,
ang pon tix pauh.
Tawx kix ih priex preem nyaux rawm,
kieh kix yaox tix ih,
yaox tix nyaux;
mai deeh ploi kix ing ka simiang kix dee an.”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khan kheu kix ih priex preem,
khaix hoik sak ih sak nyaux kix,
mai nhie kix deeh ing ka simiang pui Shui-li-yax dee an.
Jauh yam an,
pui Shui-li-yax ang baox lai puing ka haktiex cie pui Yi-seu-liex khaix heue.
Lai lawm ka Sa-ma-li-yax
24 Khaix hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
simiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Piien Ha-tax,
khrawm ka pui naing tix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai lai lawm ka veng Sa-ma-li-yax.
25 Dee si dauh,
nghox koi ka daux veng hoik cud,
la tix mu khaw tix daix plah mau luah khaing,
lhax mo tiaga ra yieh kongkheu khaw phuan plah mau luah khaing heue.
26 Koe tix ngaix,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex lih hoek khaing guh veng,
koe bun tix kaux kok yo ah nin:
“Vawng,
khiox khaw maix teem coi aux liak!”
27 Simiang ah nin:
“Phan Simiang Siyiex ang teem coi maix,
aux sang chix teem coi maix yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux koe nghox ca mai plai lie?
28 Pa nu ka maix mawh ceu tix?”
Bun miex an pawk lox ah nin:
“Bun in hoik sax ah nin ka aux,
ran teei tix ih ka kawn aux sivoe,
ngaix khaix ih ka kawn nawh.
29 bMawh kheu nan,
yix tom ngaik tix ih ka kawn simex aux.
Ngaix khaix,
aux krai ka nawh,
khaw tix ih ka kawn simex nawh,
viang mawh nan,
nawh mox kawn simex tix heue!”
30 Simiang mhong kaing in,
riah kraung ceep tix griah;
pui pa ot dex guh veng khankix hoik yaox pa ceep to kaux simiang ka sibex man keei sum.
31 Simiang ah nin:
“Ka daux in singaix,
phan aux ang tat ka kaing Yi-li-sa,
kieh Siyiex pauh ka aux!”
32 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang tom ploi pui hu giex ka Yi-li-sa heue.
Yam an,
Yi-li-sa kawn ot tau mai kuad song pa hoek tix kia ka nawh khankix.
Sivoe sang hoek pui pa ploi simiang,
Yi-li-sa ah nin ka kuad song khankix:
“Pui pa caox pa caig an ploi pui sang hoek tix tat ka kaing aux.
Yam hoek nawh dee in,
pex nyeep ka siviex,
mai paw kieh nawh laig.
To kaux simiang tag sang hawt plak khaix nawh.”
33 Yi-li-sa ang bax uik krai lox krai tix,
simiangc tom hoek.
Simiang ah nin:
“Krax laux krax leeing in mawh pa tawx Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang ex.
Aux yuh pa tix kawn sang krox jun krax teem krax coi Simiang Siyiex lie?”
