Yaw-seux kah ca maox simiang
41
Khaix hoik luan ra num,
simiang ca maox tix ceu:
nawh cung hawt rawm klawng Ni-law,
2 yaox moi miex pa mhawm kluing aliah mu lih khaing daux rawm klawng Ni-law,
sawm rib pa kuah hawt klawng tan heue.
3 Khaix an,
koe moi miex pa ang mhawm grang pa krawx rhao aliah mu,
baox lih khaing rawm klawng,
cung hawt praok moi miex pa kluing an aliah mu ka praok rawm klawng;
4 moi miex pa ang mhawm grang pa krawx pa rhao aliah mu khankix,
ih ka moi miex pa mhawm kluing an aliah mu.
Yam an,
simiang si duad khaing it tix.
5 Nawh baox laig it,
mai baox ca maox tix yaox nghox ca tix pang pa jiet mhawm sok aliah si gleem,
6 khaix an baox koe nghox ca pa ghrip aliah si gleem,
pa laux jao bhaung lih khaing plak lih singaix;
7 si gleem nghox ca pa ghrip aliah si gleem an,
bleet ka si gleem nghox pa ting mu pa jiet mhawm sok an.
Khaix si duad simiang,
tom taug tawng mawh ca maox tix.
8 aNgaix khaix pon ngawb,
simiang kied koe krax khi rhawm ang nyawm ot,
ploi pui khrawm ka cao chix si meed mai pui koe ping nyia cao jhak ka simuing khankix pa ot ka daux Ai-cix hoek sivoe nawh,
krai ca maox nawh ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa pon kah son nawh heue.
9 Yam an,
cao kaing pa tawx kawk nyaux ah nin ka simiang:
“Aux doh keud ka pa lud tix singaix.
10 Koe tix bawg,
Vawng song rhawm,
tawx lox kieh pui cao kuang pa tawx priex ih mai aux laig ka daux thawng cie cao giex kaing pui naing.
11 Koe daux tix sawm,
yiex ra kaux ca maox,
ca maox yiex dee bleeh ang khaw paox.
12 Yam an koe pui Si-paw-lai pa awn ju tix kaux,
laung tix tau mai yiex,
nawh mawh siliex cao giex kaing lien.
Yiex tang krai ca maox tix ka nawh,
nawh tom kah son yiex.
13 Dee si dauh,
kaing an hoik lih tix awm pa kah nawh,
Vawng deeh tawx kaing pa yuh aux,
mai teuk teue ka cao kaing pa tawx priex ih an yum.”
14 Simiang ploi pui hu kok ka Yaw-seux hoek.
Kix nied tix teei nawh lih khaing daux thawng;
nawh cox khre to kaux tix singax,
mai loh kraung ceep tix,
hoek ka sivoe ngai simiang.
15 Simiang ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux hoik ca maox tix ceu,
ang koe pui pa pon kah dee bleeh;
aux mhong chix kah maix kah.”
16 Yaw-seux pawk lox:
“Vawng,
to kaux aux ang chix kah,
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex chix kah mhawm son maix heue.”
17 Simiang ah nin:
“Aux ca maox tix cung praok rawm klawng Ni-law.
18 Koe moi miex pa mhawm kluing aliah mu kaoh khaing daux rawm klawng,
mai sawm rib ka praok klawng tan.
19 Khaix an,
koe moi miex pa ang mhawm grang pa krawx pa rhao aliah mu baox lih,
moi krawx an mawh pa ang aux sax yaox ka daux haktiex Ai-cix tix bawg kawx.
20 Moi miex pa ang mhawm grang pa krawx pa rhao an ih ka moi miex pa kluing pa lih sivoe aliah mu,
21 kawn ca krawx yuh nan,
awm pa ang yaox grawng pa bleet nawh moi pa kluing.
Yam an,
aux si duad.
22 Aux baox ca maox tix ceu,
aux ca maox tix yaox nghox ca tix pang pa jiet mhawm sok si gleem aliah mu,
23 khaix an baox yaox nghox pa kroh pa vhit aliah si gleem,
pa laux ka bhaung pa hoek plak lih singaix.
24 Nghox ca pa kroh pa vhit an aliah si gleem bleet ka nghox ca pa mhawm an aliah si gleem.
Aux krai ca maox in ka cao chix si meed khankix,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pon kah aux mhong dee bleeh tix kaux kawx heue.”
25 Yaw-seux ah nin ka simiang:
“Grawng ca maox in ra ceu chix tix kah mawh tix ceu khaing,
dee bleeh awm paox.
Siyiex hoik jhi Vawng yaox ka kaing pa sang yuh tix.
26 Moi miex pa kluing an aliah mu,
mawh aliah num;
mai si gleem nghox ca pa mhawm an kawx mawh aliah num;
ca maox in ra ceu mawh tix ceu khaing heue.
27 Khaix an moi miex pa krawx pa rhao mai nghox ca pa ghrip an aliah si gleem,
pa hoik laux ka bhaung pa kaoh khaing plak lih singaix,
mawh sibhawm praix aliah num heue.
28 Awm pa taug krai aux ka Vawng,
Siyiex hoik jhi kaing pa sang yuh tix ka Vawng.
29 Ka daux haktiex Ai-cix tag sang lih koe num pa haok huan pa ang sax yaox sivoe an,
30 khaix an deeh lih koe sibhawm praix aliah num.
Haktiex meung khawm uik sang laux ka sibhawm praix,
tom ang pui lai doh keud ka num pa haok huan singaix pa hoik luan;
31 Sibhawm praix kied kin ang lai koe pui pa doh keud ka krax pa haok huan num pa hoik luan.
32 Vawng baox ca maox ra bawg,
in mawh pa hoik tat Siyiex tix deuh,
ang rheeng kaing pa sang yuh nawh sang lih tix mawh heue.
33 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux simie maix Vawng laug pui pa naog ping nyia cuyi mai pui pa koe krax tawng,
kieh nawh kuang ka piang haktiex,
34 mai kieh simiang tang kaux,
ka daux aliah num pa haok huan,
khrawm nghox koi ka piang meung phuan son tix son.
35 Tawx lox kieh kix khrawm nghox koi ka daux num pa mhawm an aliah num,
ka ku veng khrawm ka nghox koi,
kieh pui koe cao kuang.
36 Ka daux num pa hoek sibhawm praix an,
nghox koi in kix chix tix kub kawn pui meung haktiex ih,
mai ang kieh pui yaox tix yum ka cai rhawm tix heue.”
Yaw-seux pon siawp sibrawm tix kuang Ai-cix
37 Simiang mai siliex nawh khankix khaw rhawm ka lox pa krai Yaw-seux.
38 Khaix an,
simiang ah nin ka siliex tix khankix:
“Ang ex doh yaox pui pa kawn lheu mhawm khaing Yaw-seux,
mawh kheu koe khuan Siyiex tau mai nawh!”
39 Simiang ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Mawh kheu Siyiex hoik jhi maix yaox grawng in khawm uik,
maix hoik koe ping nyia cuyi rhawm keud pa mhawm khaing pui ku kaux.
40 bAux sang tawx maix kuang ka piang meung aux;
pui cie aux tag sang nghiet ka lox maix uik.
Ka piang meung,
mawh ngie aux pa lhaong siawp sibrawm khaing maix.
41 Yam in aux tawx maix mawh simiang Ai-cix.”
42 cSimiang peeik jeep giien cum khaing taix tix,
mai ceep ka giien taix Yaw-seux,
mai baox ceep sibex dha man keei sum pa mhawm ka piang nawh,
vhak maox khri ka ngawg nawh.
43 Nawh baox kieh Yaw-seux ngawm ka ling law brung daux ra mu,
koe pui pau hu sivoe,
kok yo daux krax ah nin:
“Yoi!
Yoi!”
Yuh nin,
Yaw-seux hoik mawh simiang,
kuang ka piang Ai-cix khawm uik.
44 Simiang ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Aux mawh simiang,
viang mawh nan,
haktiex Ai-cix khawm uik,
phan ang kaoh khaing lox maix,
pui tix kaux kawx ang pon tix teei alo tix yuh.”
45-46 Simiang sum Yaw-seux koe ceu kaux Ai-cix tix ceu,
mawh Sa-fa-na-theu,
Pa-noe-yax mai baox tawx Yaw-seux moeng kawn bun cao giex kaing li Paw-thi-fe-la pui veng Si-li-phaw-li pa kaux Ya-si-na heue.
Yaw-seux koe ju tix ngoe num,
jauh tix yuh kaing son simiang Ai-cix;
nawh lih hu khaing nyiex haw simiang,
hu jhak ka Ai-cix ku dauh ku dee.
47 Ka daux num pa haoh priex preem,
nghox koi haok huan.
48 Yaw-seux khrawm nghox koi,
dee ot ka veng ku dauh ku dee,
khrawm nghox koi ka vung viang haktiex ku dauh.
49 Nawh khrawm nghox koi tom nie,
nie awm mhai ka praok rawm,
ang lai uik simin heue.
50 Sivoe ang num sibhawm praix nyang hoek,
moeng Yaw-seux Ya-si-na keh kawn simex son nawh ra kaux.
51 Nawh ah nin:
“Siyiex hoik kieh aux piawm pa kha rhawm kha rhi mai krax pa laux rhawm daux nyiex.”
Mawh kheu nan,
tom sum ceu kaux kawn ai mawh Ma-na-sid.
52 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Siyiex kieh aux haok huan ka daux haktiex pa kha.”
Kheu nan tom sum ceu kaux daux ra kaux mawh Yi-fa-liiene heue.
53 Num haoh priex preem pui Ai-cix aliah num hoik si dauh;
54 fawm lox jhai pa krai Yaw-seux,
num sibhawm praix aliah num hoik rieb tix hoek.
Viang sibhawm praix koe ka vung viang haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
Ai-cix koe nghox koi ku dauh ku dee.
55 gHoek bawg yam cai rhawm pui Ai-cix,
kix khiox khaw rawg mhai nghox koi ka simiang.
Simiang tawx lox kieh kix hu ka Yaw-seux,
mai nghiet ka lox pa ah nawh ku ceu.
56 Sibhawm praix nied tix kin si pie si pie,
si dauh ka meung khawm uik.
Kheu nan,
Yaw-seux paoh ka krao khawm uik,
tawx ka pui Ai-cix liag nghox priex tix.
57 Pui meung vung viang kix ku meung hoek tix liag nghox ka Yaw-seux,
kheu koe sibhawm praix ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
sibhawm praix kied kin tete heue.
