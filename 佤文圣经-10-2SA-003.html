3
Pui naing pa hawt rhawm cub nyiex Sao-law mai pui naing cie Ta-wue hoik puing ka paox tix tom rheeng.
Ta-wue tix ngaix hoik tix ngaix nied tix ting riang,
cub nyiex Sao-law tix ngaix hoik tix ngaix hoik iak riang heue.
Kawn simex Ta-wue khankix
2 Ta-wue koe kawn simex liah kaux ka daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng.
Hawt awm pa hoik keh kix,
mai dia tix hawt paox yuh nin:
Ang-noeng,
hoik mawh pa keh khaing pui Yie-see-liex pa kaux Ya-si-nuang.
3 Ci-li-ya hoik mawh pa keh khaing pui Cia-mix moeng Na-pax pa kaux Ai-pi-kai.
Ya-sa-long hoik mawh pa keh khaing simiang Ci-sux kawn bun Ta-mai pa kaux Ma-cia.
4 Ya-to-ni-ya mawh pa keh khaing Ha-ci.
See-fa-thi-ya mawh pa keh khaing Ya-pi-tha.
5 Yi-theu-nian mawh pa keh khaing Yi-keu-la.
Kawn simex in kix liah kaux mawh pa keh ka daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng heue.
Ya-ni-eu hawt rhawm Ta-wue
6 Pui naing cie Ta-wue mai pui naing pa hawt rhawm cub nyiex Sao-law ca nied tix yuh naing ka paox tix ang jud.
Ka daux pui naing cie Sao-law,
riang rao Ya-ni-eu simiang naing nied ot nied tix ting lhaong heue.
7 Sao-law koe moeng iak tix kaux kaux Li-see-pa,
nawh mawh kawn bun Ai-yax.
Koe tix singaix,
kawn simex Sao-law pa kaux Yi-see-paw-seux chawk Ya-ni-eu ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai it tau mai moeng iak keeing aux lie?”
8 In hoik kieh Ya-ni-eu song rhawm tete.
Nawh ah nin:
“Maix keud aux sang chix coh ka Sao-law lie?
Maix khex ce aux sang hawt rhawm yuh kaing son pui Yo-tax lie?
Tom yam jauh aux hoik cu lox nghiet si kah keeing maix Sao-law mai pux aik nawh mai paox grawm nawh khankix.
Aux kawx hoik teem ka maix,
mai ang bax kieh Ta-wue puing maix laux.
In singaix,
maix lai yuh tix simah ka aux jao bun tix kaux khaing.
9-10 aSimiang Siyiex hoik ap tix deuh,
Ta-wue tag sang teei siawp sibrawm meung haktiex khaing daux taix Sao-law mai doeh ceu cad nawh khankix,
mai kieh Ta-wue mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex mai simiang pui Yo-tax,
jauh ka Tang pa ot ka haktiex plak laong,
tom hoek ka Piie-see-pa pa ot ka haktiex plak seh.
Yam in,
phan ang mawh aux pa hoik yuh kaing in,
kieh Siyiex pauh ka aux!”
11 Yi-see-paw-seux kied lhat ka Ya-ni-eu,
mai ang lai pon krai lox pie tix gum kawx heue.
12 Ya-ni-eu ploi kawn cau tix hu ka Ta-wue dee an,
ah nin:
“Mawh mawx pa keud tix sang kuang ka meung in lie?
Phan maix yuh sijax mukheu tau mai aux,
mai aux tag sang teem maix kuang ka meung pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.”
13 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Chix tix,
aux sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai maix;
viang mawh nan,
aux kawn koe lox rawg tix ceu:
yam hoek yaox maix ka aux,
maix viex kawn bun Sao-law pa kaux Mi-cia hoek tau mai tix.”
14 bTa-wue kawx ploi kawn cau tix hu ka Yi-see-paw-seux,
ah nin:
“Deeh tawx moeng aux Mi-cia ka aux heue!
Nawh hoik mawh pa loh aux ka hak klix pui Fe-li-see tix yieh kaux.”
15 Yi-see-paw-seux tom ploi pui hu teei Mi-cia khaing mex nawh,
pa mawh kawn simex La-yix pa kaux Pha-thiex dee an.
16 Pha-thiex hawt Mi-cia hu khawm yiiem tix,
tom hoek ka Pa-hu-ling.
Ya-ni-eu ah nin ka mex nawh:
“Maix deeh ing ka nyiex heue!”
Pha-thiex tom deeh ing heue.
17 Ya-ni-eu hoek ka kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex khankix dee an,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Ka sivoe an tom rheeng,
pex hoik kied simie Ta-wue mawh simiang tix,
18 Yam in,
bawg yam hoik hoek.
Pex khaw tix kawn doh keud ka lox Simiang Siyiex pa hoik ah nin:
‘Aux sang kieh siliex aux Ta-wue hoek taoh ka pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing tawm taix pui Fe-li-see mai pui pa koeng khawm uik heue.’ ”
19 Ya-ni-eu hoik ah nin ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming awm lox in nang,
khaix an nawh hu ka Si-paw-loeng,
mai krai Ta-wue mhong grawng pa hoik cu ceu pui Piien-ya-ming mai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka lox tix heue.
20 Yam viex Ya-ni-eu pui tix nga kaux hoek ka Ta-wue haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
Ta-wue khan kheu ka priex preem kix.
21 Ya-ni-eu ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Yam in,
aux sang khrawm pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoek hawt rhawm Vawng,
mai kieh maix mawh simiang,
son sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai maix,
awm pa hoik simie rhawm maix tix yuh,
yaox tix pon kuang ka haktiex khawm uik.”
Ta-wue hoik kham sidawng grawng sang priex ka Ya-ni-eu nyawm ot,
khaix an,
mai deeh tawx nawh hu krax heue.
Briex mox tix pauh ka Ya-ni-eu
22 Ang koe miex glang tix,
Yaw-ya mai siliex Ta-wue kaoh khaing puing tix pui koeng,
mai viex kraung pa pon tix khaing daux naing tom nie.
Viang mawh nan,
yam an,
Ya-ni-eu ang lai ot ka Si-paw-loeng,
mawh kheu hoik kham Ta-wue sidawng grawng sang priex nawh nyawm ot,
mai nhie nawh ing.
23 Yam hoek Yaw-ya mai pui naing cie nawh ka Si-paw,
koe pui krai ka nawh grawng pa hu Ya-ni-eu ka simiang Ta-wue,
mai grawng pa hoik kham Ta-wue sidawng son sang priex nawh nyawm ot,
deeh tawx nawh ing.
24 Yaw-ya tom hu ka Ta-wue mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Yuh maix kaing ceu pa tix eue?
Ya-ni-eu hoek ka maix,
yuh pa tix maix lai kawn deeh kieh nawh hu lie?
25 Maix caw tix tawng,
kawn simex Ni-eu pa kaux Ya-ni-eu hoek dee in mawh son sang pie lhien ka maix,
nawh hoek jiang ka dee laig,
dee lih maix mai kaing can pa hoik yuh maix khankix heue.”
26 Khaix hoik lih hu Yaw-ya khaing Ta-wue,
nawh ploi kawn cau hawt leue ka Ya-ni-eu,
mai deeh viex Ya-ni-eu hoek khaing dung Si-la.
Viang mawh nan,
grawng kaing in Ta-wue ang tawng.
27 Yam hoik deeh hoek Ya-ni-eu khaing Si-paw-loeng,
Yaw-ya reu nawh hoek ka praok diang siviex veng,
mai lhien lox ah sang krai lox pa klip ka nawh,
mai klauh ka tu nawh dee an.
Yuh nin,
hoik briex tix pauh ka Ya-ni-eu,
mawh kheu hoik pauh nawh ka pux Yaw-ya pa kaux Ya-sha-he heue.
28 Khaix hoik mhong Ta-wue ka grawng in,
nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik tawng aux mai kawn pui cie aux hoik een grawng pa yum Ya-ni-eu mawh pui ang koe dee lud.
29 Kieh krax pa pawk bix hoek ka piang Yaw-ya mai piang cub nyiex ma nawh!
Kieh nyiex ma nawh ku ju pon jhawm saux ka lang mai jhawm saux si pawg,
phan ang mawh nan,
pon ngie tix yuh kaing pa yuh bun khankix,
phan ang mawh nan,
yum ka daux naing,
phan ang mawh nan,
ang yaox tix ih ceu pa ih tom sak.”
30 Yuh nin,
Yaw-ya mai pux aik nawh Ya-pi-sai hoik pauh ka Ya-ni-eu,
mawh son sang pawk bix ka son pux tix pa kaux Ya-sha-he yam yum nawh daux naing ka haktiex Ci-piien heue.
Si paung Ya-ni-eu
31 Ta-wue tawx lox nhie Yaw-ya mai pui cie nawh riah ka kraung tix griah,
ceep ka sibex man keei sum,
mai yiiem son Ya-ni-eu.
Yam khu kix si paung yum nawh,
simiang Ta-wue hawt plak khaix lawng nawh.
32 Kix si paung Ya-ni-eu ot ka Si-paw-loeng.
Simiang Ta-wue ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting ka plak sivoe si meeig;
can kawn pui kawx kied koe krax laux rhawm.
33 Ta-wue yuh lox rhax yiiem doe son Ya-ni-eu tix mu ah nin:
Ya-ni-eu khaw tix yum awm mawh tix pui pa daox yuh nin lie?
34 Taix maix khawm ra khawn ang phrawk tix.
Caong maix khawm ra khawn ang mad tix.
Maix lai yum awm hoik mawh pui pa caox pui caig pa pauh pui heue!
Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui baox yiiem son Ya-ni-eu.
35 Pui haoh pui hun glaw Ta-wue ih ceu pa ih leeing lang singaix,
viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue tiex tu ah nin:
“Phan aux ih ceu pa ih sivoe sang klip singaix,
kieh Siyiex pauh aux!”
36 Pui haoh pui hun tawng grawng in,
mai kied gaux rhawm ka yuh simiang nin.
Tete te lai,
can kaing pa hoik yuh simiang ku ceu,
kix hoik kied khaw rhawm kah.
37 Ka daux singaix an,
can pui pa hawt khaix Ta-wue mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoik tawng grawng ang simiang bax laig yuh kaing pa briex tix pauh Ya-ni-eu yum.
38 Simiang ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Khex ang pex bax tawng ka daux in singaix,
daux pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yum cao kaing pa ting tix kaux lie?
39 Ka daux in singaix,
viang aux hoik mawh simiang pa hoik laug Siyiex,
mai aux ang koe riang rao.
Kawn simex Si-lu-ya in kiex khawm ra kaux kied cau nyhau,
mai aux ang tawng grawng sang yuh ka kiex.
Kieh Simiang Siyiex hawt tix ciyang kiex,
awm phi map pa hoik yuh kieh heue!”
