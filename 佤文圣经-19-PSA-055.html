Lox rawg buan krax pa teem coi
55
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet lox rawg aux buan;
paw vi mox tix ang simi ka krax pa khiox khaw aux.
2 Khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox aux,
pawk ka lox aux;
aux hoik taug mao kheu khi rhawm tix.
3 Krax pa ghlat pui koeng ka aux,
mai pa tuk tiak pui caox ka aux,
hoik kieh aux suk sag rhawm khawm uik.
Kix thiem thie tix tawx krax laux krax leeing ka piang aux;
kix song rhawm ka aux,
koeng ka aux heue.
 
4 Rhawm rhi aux hoik naog ka krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi;
pa lhat krax yum hoik toing max yuh aux.
5 Krax yaoh yee hoik pon giex ka aux;
pa lhat hoik tawb kheem ka aux.
6 Aux simie tix koe preeik,
awm si kaux yuh nan,
pon pu tix hu sawg dee sang laux lee tix.
7 Aux sang pu hu ka dee singai,
paung ot ka daux tiag daux tu.
8 Aux sang nied tix sawg ka nyiex dee mox son to kaux tix,
son sang mox tix ka bhaung ting lhiex ting.
9 Simiang aux eue,
khiox khaw maix tieh loh ka gum lox pui pa koeng khankix heue.
 
Aux yaox ka pa suk sag daux veng;
10 Kix lai ling tix hu daux veng leeing ngaix leeing sawm;
ku dauh ku dee hoik naog ka phi map krax laux krax leeing.
11 Hoik koe krax pa tuk tiak ku dauh ku dee;
ka daux laih hoik mawh krax gut kheem mai pa pie lhien khawm uik heue.
 
12 Phan pui koeng map nai ka aux,
aux kawn pon gun yeung kah;
phan pui pa tieb tie yaw rang di ka aux,
aux kawn chix tix to mox tix.
13 Viang mawh nan,
ang doh tawng mawh maix pa mawh paox pie tau mai aux,
pa mawh pui pa tawng ka rhawm rhi aux,
mai paox grawm ting aux!
14 Yix hoik ra lai krai lox pa dex pa dawt tau mai paox tix;
yix hoik sax hoek krub ka nyiex buan tau.
15 Kieh krax yum lih hoek ka piang pui pa koeng ka aux dum mawx dum an;
kieh kix cawd tix yum im ka daux meung yum.
Mawh kheu nyiex ma kix,
ka daux rhawm rhi kix hoik naog ka krax pa caox pa caig heue.
 
16 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang khiox khaw krax teem krax coi Siyiex;
Simiang Siyiex tag sang chix taoh ka aux tete.
17 Yam pon ngawb ang ah,
greung ngaix ang ah,
pon bo ang ah,
aux tag sang krai nawh mhong khawm ngia tix;
nawh tag sang chix nghiet ka lox aux tete.
18 Aux si pup pui koeng ka daux naing;
nawh tag sang thung aux pon krax pa nhim lih hoek khaing bang naing.
19 Siyiex pa koe sibrawm tom yam jauh dix dix tag sang chix nghiet ka lox aux tete,
mai yuh laux ka kix;
mawh kheu ang kix cu tix loh rhawm loh rhi tix,
mai ang lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex.
 
20 Pui pa yuh paox pie tau mai aux sivoe an,
puing ka paox grawm tix;
nawh hoik tieh vheen ka lox pa ap tix.
21 Siyieb jheeh nawh kawn lheu nyo khaing bee rawm teeh,
rhawm rhi nawh hoik naog ka pa koeng khoi.
Lox krai nawh kawn lheu awn nyu khaing bee.
Tete te lai,
mai hoik awm vaig pa lawd lih khaing daux phak.
22 Teei tix tawx khaox glawm pa cien ka Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag sang chix teem coi ka maix tete.
Nawh ang doh kieh pui rauh pui see som nai heue.
 
23 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex eue,
maix tag caw tix cot pui pa caox pa caig cawd daux o krax yum;
maix tag caw tix kieh pui pie pui lhien yum yam nhum ju tix.
Phan mawh aux,
aux tag sang yum rhawm ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix heue.
