Lox pa nghoik Yaw-paw tix krai
29
Yaw-paw baox krai lox ah nin:
2 Aux kied simie baux singaix bawg yam pa hoik luan baox lih tix koe,
awm singaix pa hoik luan yam hoik kia ngien Siyiex ka aux yuh nan.
3 Yam an,
kawk ngu nawh jhan riang ka piang kaing aux jheeng jheeng;
pa riang nawh hu tau mai aux ka daux pa faik.
4 Yam an,
aux kawn nhum ju koe riang;
krax muh krax rhawm Siyiex tawb kheem ka piang aux.
5 Cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik ot tau mai aux;
kawn simex kawn bun aux khankix hoik ot vung viang aux.
6 Ka dee pa hoik hoek aux,
mai ang uik nyaux ka rawm teeh,
pie rang simaox kawx hoik buing lih ka bee sime.
7 Kuad riex ju ka daux veng hoik khrawm paox tix ku bawg,
aux tom ngawm ka piang dang ngeung pa hoik deuh son aux,
8 pui nhum ju yaox ka aux mai mox tix;
pui riex ju yaox ka aux mai kaoh cung nab yam khawm uik;
9 simiang khankix naox ka lox pa krai tix,
teei taix tix thox jheeh tix kah.
10 Pie ceu pui pa koe min khankix kawx ang pon nye,
hoik yien yawg tix ang koe lox krai khawm uik heue.
 
11 Can pui pa mhong ka aux hoik nawb buan piang aux khawm uik;
can pui pa yaox ka aux hoik tawx jheeh tix yaog yaw ka khawm uik.
12 Pui hot pui sibhawm khiox khaw krax teem coi,
mai aux gaux rhawm tix teem coi ka kix;
kawn doe khiox khaw krax teem coi,
mai aux tom tawx taix tix teem.
13 Pui pa ot ka daux dung raux daux nu nawb buan piang aux;
aux kawx hoik kieh rhawm rhi miex mai pon krax pa nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi.
14 Aux kieh pa rauh pa see mawh kraung ceep ka to kaux;
pa khaw caw mawh sibex dha ka to kaux aux,
mawh mhok khri ka aux.
15 Aux mawh ngai pui pa dug ngai;
aux mawh caong ka pui chawh.
16 Aux mawh keeing pui hot pui sibhawm;
aux prawng rauh see son pui tang pui praw jheeng jheeng.
17 Aux yuh laux ka riang rao pui tud pui rhim,
taoh ka piang pui pa gut kheem kix heue.
 
18 Aux hoik een tix pon krax pa nyawm it nyawm tom lang ju lang phan;
ju aux tag sang haoh hun awm mhaik yuh nan.
19 Aux awm num khaox pa tawx tix hoek praok rawm,
yam pon sawm koe rawm ciex pa hoik caux ka kak khaox aux.
20 Pui ku kaux hoik yaog yaw ka aux;
riang rao aux ang bax dieh.
21 Pui ku kaux krox jun ka krax pa sidiex gaux aux,
bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux.
22 Khaix hoik uik lox pa krai aux,
pui khankix ang lai koe lox pa sang krai;
lox pa krai aux hoik awm rawm ciex jauh ka piang to kaux kix.
23 Kix hoik simie tix krox jun ka krax pa sidiex gaux aux khawm uik,
awm pui pa yuh kaing haktiex simie ka rawm lhiex yuh nan.
24 Yam hoik grai krax pa cong rhawm kix,
aux nyiah mai kix;
krax pa mhawm nyiah aux mai kix hoik joh jhan ka kix.
25 Aux mawh simiang kix,
mai tat ka krax pa khaw kix tix lawng;
aux viex kix lih hu,
awm simiang naing thung pui naing yuh nan,
yam laux rhawm laux rhi kix,
mai tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka kix heue.
