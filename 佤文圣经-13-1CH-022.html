22
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ah nin:
“Nyiex buan cie Simiang Siyiex tag sang sum tix ot dee in;
dee in mawh dee yuh kawn pui Yi-seu-liex ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing heue.”
Rien khre tix sang sum ka nyiex buan
2 Simiang Ta-wue tawx lox ka can nyo si nie pa ot tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex,
tawx lox kieh kix saik simaox,
mai rien khre ka simaox pa sang siao son nyiex buan.
3 Ta-wue tawx rhiem tom nie,
son sang yuh jim rhiem mai goik siviex kah,
mai baox tawx tawng tom nie,
tom ang lai dak uik ciang.
4 Kawn pui Thai-eux mai kawn pui Si-toeng baox reu tix hoek tawx khaox si oe bhawng tom nie ka Ta-wue.
5 Ta-wue keud:
“Nyiex buan pa sang sum kawn simex aux Saw-law-meung,
tag sang mawh pa mhawm grang,
mai ao min ka piang haktiex.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh kawn awn ju,
ang bax kub rhawm keud,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang rien khre ku ceu khawm uik mhawm mhawm.”
Mawh kheu nan,
sivoe sang yum Ta-wue,
nawh hoik rien khre khaox ox simaox rang tom nie heue.
6 Ta-wue kok kawn simex tix Saw-law-meung hoek,
mai tawx lox ka nawh grawng sang sum nawh nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex.
7 aTa-wue ah nin ka nawh:
“Kawn simex aux eue,
to kaux aux hoik keud tix sang sum nyiex buan son Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux tix lhang,
8 viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux,
mawh kheu hoik pauh aux ka pui tom nie,
hoik puing naing tom nie,
hoik kieh nham pui jauh tom nie,
nawh ang kieh aux sum nyiex buan son nawh.
9 Viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik deuh lox ka aux tix gum.
Nawh ah nin:
‘Maix tag sang koe kawn simex tix kaux.
Ka daux bawg yam pa giex nawh kaing,
meung haktiex yaox tix ot ka daux pa nhim,
mawh kheu aux tag sang tawx singaix pa nhim ka nawh,
mai nawh ang yaox krax pa tuk tiak tuk te pui pa koeng khankix.
Ceu kaux nawh tag sang kok mawh Saw-law-meungb;
mawh kheu daux bawg yam mawh nawh simiang,
aux tag sang tawx pa nhim mai pa nyawm ot ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
10 Nawh tag sang sum nyiex buan son aux tix lhang.
Nawh tag sang mawh kawn simex aux;
aux tag sang mawh keeing nawh.
Aux tag sang joh jhan ka meung pa giex nawh kaing kaux cong,
mai kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex leeing ju leeing phan heue.’ ”
11 Ta-wue nied tix ah nin:
“Kawn simex aux eue,
kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix ot tau mai maix;
kieh nawh giex kaux ka lox pa ap tix,
mai kieh maix pon sum nyiex buan mhawm mhawm son nawh.
12 Kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix tawx ping nyia chix keud maix,
mai pon hawt lox tat jhawm prawng nawh kia ngien ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
13 cPhan maix priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap,
pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex Maw-si krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mai maix tag pon yaox tix mhawm ot mhawm koe.
Maix tag caw tix kaux cong;
paw vi lhat,
mai paw vi khi rhawm khi rhi heue.
14 Phan mawh kraung pa sang sum ka son nyiex buan,
aux hoik sau riang tix khrawm khri loe rheng pon yieh kongtoeng mai mau loe mheen pon rheng kongtoeng in kix chix tix siao son pa sum nyiex buan,
khaix an kawn koe tawng mai rhiem tom nie.
Aux kawx hoik rien khre ka khaox ox mai simaox rang khankix,
viang mawh nan,
maix kawn sang baox rien khre ka tom nie.
15 Maix hoik koe pui pa yuh kaing tom nie,
koe cang mai simaox,
cang mai bid,
mai cang mai khaox,
kawn koe cang mai pa blawn chix taix,
16 pa pon raug kraung ku ceu lih khaing khri,
mau,
tawng,
rhiem khankix.
Yam in jauh maix yuh heue,
kieh Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai maix heue.”
17 Ta-wue tawx lox nhie simiang pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik teem coi Saw-law-meung.
18 Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng,
tawx krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ka pex khawm pon daog.
Nawh hoik kieh aux pon kuang ka can pui ku ceu pa ot ka haktiex in sivoe an.
Pui ceu in kix yam in hoik hawt rhawm pex mai Simiang Siyiex khawm uik.
19 Pex caw tix yuh kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
jauh tix sum ka nyiex buan.
Yuh nin,
pex tom tag pon yaox tix deuh teug sijax mukheu,
mai can kraung pa siao son krax krub khankix ot ka daux nyiex buan heue.”
