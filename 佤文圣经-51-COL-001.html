Lai Keu-law-si
1
Aux mawh Pao-law;
aux mawh kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit ka daux alo Siyiex.
Aux mai Thi-maw-thaix pux aik
2 tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka ceu cao kawn Khrit Keu-law-si,
pa mawh pux aik pux o pa kaux sijax ka daux Khrit khankix heue.
Kieh keeing ex Siyiex tawx buan son,
mai pa nhim ka pex heue!
Lox rawg buan pa ting buan son
3 Yam rawg aux buan son pex,
yix ah lox pa ting buan son ka Siyiex jheeng jheeng,
pa mawh keeing Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit;
4 mawh kheu hoik mhong yix grawng krax yum rhawm pex ka Ye-sux Khrit,
grawng krax muh pex ka kawn Khrit ku kaux.
5 Yam jauh,
yam sidiex gum lox buan pa mawh pa meh tix hoek ka pex dee an,
pex tag mhong ka grawng pa viex gum lox buan krax keud pot jun pot.
Yuh nin,
krax yum rhawm pex mai krax muh krax rhawm pex,
hoik kieh krax keud pot jun pot pa deuh ot ka rao ma son pex,
tag sang mawh riah rawng.
6 Gum lox buan in ang joeng sidiex tix hoek ka pex dee an khaing,
tag sang tauh ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai haok huan plix plix ang jud,
awm yam jauh pex tix mhong mai tawng tete ka buan son Siyiex,
mai pon haok huan plix plix yuh nan.
7 bPex hoik pon gau ka gum lox buan in khaing paox pa muh yuh kaing tau mai aux Yi-pa-fu;
nawh hoik mawh siliex pa kaux sijax ka Khrit son ex.a
8 Phan mawh son grawh grawng krax muh pa tawx Khuan Singax Singeh ka pex,
mai hoik mawh nawh pa krai ex mhong heue.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
jauh yam mhong aux ka grawh grawng pex,
yix hoik rawg buan son pex ang jud,
khiox khaw Siyiex kieh pex pon ping nyia ku ceu,
mai riang rao krax tawng krax yung khaing Khuan Singax Singeh,
pon grum tawng sidawng ka alo nawh.
10 Yuh nin,
krax ot krax koe pex tom tag khaw caw mai alo Simiang Siyiex,
mai kieh nawh khaw rhawm ka can kaing ku ceu.
Awm an,
pex tag chix plix ka plix pa mhawm ku ceu ka daux krax ot krax koe tix,
krax tawng ka Siyiex kawx chix tix haok huan.
11 Kieh pex pon riang rao pa kaux cong khaing daux siawp sibrawm min plak ngai nawh,
mai pon gun yeung ka khawm uik.
Tag caw tix teei krax gaux rhawm
12 ah lox pa ting buan son ka keeing Siyiex,c
mawh kheu hoik kieh nawh pexd kub diag tix pon ka buan son pa hoik deuh nawh son kawn Khrit ka daux meung pa riang an.
13 Nawh hoik taoh ex luat luan khaing siawp sibrawm pa faik,
mai kieh ex ot ka greem sibrawm kawn pa muh nawh.
14 eKa daux kawn pa muh nawh,
ex tom pon tix luat luan,
pa mawh phi map ex hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi.
Khrit mai kaing yuh nawh
15 Khrit hoik mawh pak peung Siyiex pa ang ex chix yaox an,
mawh kawn ai pa ting ka piang khawm uik.
16 Ka daux nawh,
Siyiex hoik phan yuh ka haktiex rao ma mawh:
ceu pa chix yaox mai ceu pa ang chix yaox,
khawm pa ot ka daux haktiex khuan cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik,
simiang,
mai cao giex kaing.
Ka daux nawh,
mai ka son nawh nang,
Siyiex hoik phan yuh ka rao ma khawm uik.
17 Khrit hoik dawg koe sivoe sang lih koe can ceu pa koe khawm uik;
can ceu pa koe khawm uik kawx hoik lih tix mawh ka daux nawh.
18 fNawh mawh kaing ka ceu cao,
pa mawh kaing ka to kaux yuh nan;
nawh mawh num riah ju pa khraox.
Nawh mawh kawn ai,
mai jauh tix deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum sivoe,
kaing ting mawh son sang mawh nawh simiang ka piang khawm uik.
19 Siyiex hoik but tix kieh kawn simex tix lih koe awm pa koe tix yuh nan khawm uik.
20 gKa daux kawn simex nawh,
Siyiex hoik but tix kieh rao ma khawm uik deeh mhawm tau mai to kaux tix.
Siyiex kieh pa yum kawn simex tix ka khaox lhak pon yuh lih ka pa nhim,
mai kieh rao ma haktiex ceu pa koe khawm uik mawh cie tix heue.
21 Singaix pa hoik luan,
kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh pex,
mai rhawm rhi pex hoik singai hu khaing Siyiex,
lih mawh pa koeng ka Siyiex.
22 Yam in,
ka daux pa yum nex nham kawn simex nawh,
Siyiex hoik kieh pex lih mawh paox grawm nawh,
son sang kieh pex singax singeh ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
ang koe dee cud,
ang koe dee ne raw.
23 Pex tag caw tix giex kaux ka krax yum rhawm,
mai sum ka piang riah pa kaux cong;
paw vi tik ka krax keud pot jun pot pa hoik pon pex yam jauh tix rieb ka gum lox buan.
Yam in,
gum lox buan in hoik tauh hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
aux Pao-law pa mawh siliex gum lox buan in heue.
Kaing yuh Pao-law
24 Yam in aux een krax pa kha rhawm tix son pex mawh kaing ceu pa gaux rhawm tix ceu;
mawh kheu krax pa kha aux rhawm tix ka daux nex nham,
hoik awm mawh pa ca kawn kham aux son to kaux Khrit,
pa mawh krax kha rhawm pa kham ceu cao nawh pa ang bax yuh hoik.
25 Son sang koe dee mhawm son pex,
Siyiex tawx kaing in ka aux,
mai kieh aux mawh siliex ceu cao.
Kaing in mawh son sang kieh aux sau riang tix sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex,
26 pa mawh ceu pa raux pa mox pa hoik mox nawh glip ka kawn pui tix ju hoek tix ju;
pa raux pa mox in,
yam in hoik jhi tix lih sidawng ka pui cie nawh.
27 Krax pa keud Siyiex tix sang yuh mawh son sang kieh pui cie tix tawng,
pa raux pa mox pa grum naog mai min plak ngai in,
mawh son sang jhi tix tawx ka kawn pui piang haktiex.
Pa raux pa mox in mawh:
Khrit ot ka daux ju pex,
mawh yuh nin nang,
pex tag sang pon ka min plak ngai Siyiex.
28 Mawh kheu nan,
yix sidiex Khrit ka can pui pa koe khawm uik,
teei ping nyia ku ceu sidiex gau ka pui hun,
son sang kieh pui ku kaux lih mawh kawn Khrit pa haok huan,
mai viex kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
29 Kheu son kaing in,
aux siao lo ka siawp sibrawm pa tawx Khrit ka tix,
ang lhat ka taug mao,
mai sau riang tix yuh kaing heue.
