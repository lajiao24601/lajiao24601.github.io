Mi-cia mai ceu pui Tang
18
Bawg yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang koe simiang.
Ceu pui Tang hu sawg ka haktiex pa sang chix kix tix ot paung kah,
mawh kheu ang kix koe haktiex nyum cie khaing ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Tang laug pui pa ting riang pa kua lih khaing ka daux ceu pui kix phuan kaux,
ploi kix lih hu khaing Saw-la mai Yi-seu-thao,
son sang jiang ka haktiex.
Kix hoek ka gawng Yi-fa-liien,
mai paung ot ka nyiex ma Mi-cia.
3 Kix mhong lox pa si kah pui nhum ju Li-we an,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh maix tix dee in?
Mawh mawx pa viex maix hoek dee in?”
4 Nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh Mi-cia pa tawx aux hoek;
nawh tawx aux yuh cao giex kaing li cie tix heue.”
5 Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix chawk ka Siyiex,
ka daux pa jiang yix bawg in mhawm ang mhawm.”
6 Cao giex kaing li pawk lox ah nin:
“Hu mhawm mhawm heue,
Simiang Siyiex chix kia ngien ka piang krax hu pex heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
pui in kix phuan kaux tom hu.
Kix hoek ka veng La-yix,
yaox ka pui pa ot haktiex an nyawm ot nyawm koe,
awm pui Si-toeng yuh nan,
yien yawg,
nhim tiiem,
ang koe krax pa simah simaing;
to ma haktiex kied mhawm.
Kix kied singai hu khaing pui Si-toeng,
ang yaox tix lai ling ka pui tang kaux khankix.
8 Khaix hoik deeh ing pui in kix phuan kaux ka Saw-la mai Yi-seu-thao,
paox ceu paox cad kix chawk kix yaox ceu pa tix,
9 kix ah nin:
“Hoek heue,
hu puing ex ka La-yix!
Yix hoik jiang ka haktiex an,
kied mhawm tete.
Nied phai phai,
paw lai kieh ex kaog ang koe ceu pa yuh tin,
hu ex kuang ot ka heue!
10 Yam hoek pex ka dee an,
pex tag yaox pui haktiex an,
ang koe krax rien tid tix tieb tie ka ex;
ka dee an kheu hoik mawh haktiex pa kied vieh va,
to ma to koi kied mhawm kah,
mai koe ka ku ceu.
Siyiex hoik ap ka pex heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
ka daux ceu pui Tang koe liah yieh kaux pui pa hoik rien kraung naing,
lih hu khaing Saw-la mai Yi-seu-thao.
12 Kix lih hu,
mai sum bang ot ka Ci-liex Yie-ling ka plak klip singaix haktiex Yo-tax,
mawh kheu nan,
haktiex in tom hoek singaix kawn kok mawh Tang-ying.
13 Kix ca nied hu sivoe,
mai hoek ka gawng Yi-fa-liien nyiex ma Mi-cia heue.
14 Pui phuan kaux an kix pa hoik jiang La-yix ah nin ka paox tix:
“Hoik tawng pex ka lie?
Koe nyiex dee in tix lhang,
ka daux koe riang krub ka tix mu,
ka plak praix hoik om tix ka mau luah,
kawn koe riang krub tang ceu khankix mai Yi-fu-teu tix pheen.
Yam in pex bre tix keud mhawm mhawm,
sang caw ex tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
15 Pui jiang an kix phuan kaux laig ka daux nyiex ma Mi-cia,
mai chawk pui Li-we an mhawm ot.
16 Yam an,
ceu pui Tang liah yieh kaux pui naing pa hoik rien kraung naing an kix,
cung ka diang siviex.
17 Yam kawn ot cao giex kaing li tau mai pui naing pa hoik rien kraung naing an kix liah yieh kaux ka diang siviex,
pui jiang an kix phuan kaux laig daux nyiex,
mai teei ka riang krub pa hoik om ka mau luah mai riang krub tang ceu khankix,
khawm Yi-fu-teu heue.
18 Yam laig kix daux nyiex ma Mi-cia teei hu ka riang siyiex in kix,
cao giex kaing li chawk kix ah nin:
“Yuh pex ceu pa tix lie?”
19 Kix ah nin:
“Si,
paw nye!
Hawt khaix yix,
yuh cao giex kaing li cie yix mai cao pa teem coi yix khaing.
Yuh cao giex kaing li pui Yi-seu-liex ceu pui mawx ceu pui an,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing yuh cao giex kaing li pui tix lhang nyiex khaing heue!”
20 Cao giex kaing li mhong lox in kied sidaing gaux rhawm kah,
nawh tom viex Yi-fu-teu mai riang krub pa hoik om tix ka mau luah,
khawm riang krub tang ceu khankix hu tau mai kix heue.
21 Kix yaog tix hu,
mai kieh kawn simex kawn bun tix,
khawng brie tix,
khawm sin ngao tix hu sivoe.
22 Khaix hoik hu kix khaing nyiex ma Mi-cia been tix baux krax,
Mi-cia khrawm paox plawg paox nyhawm tix hawt tix leue ka kix.
Nawh krup ka ceu pui Tang,
23 mai kok yo ka kix.
Ceu pui Tang veeh to kaux tix chawk Mi-cia ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx?
Cub pui in kix yuh ka ceu pa tix lie?”
24 Mi-cia pawk lox ah nin:
“Pex kawn chawk ceu pa tix lie?
Pex hoik tud riang siyiex cie aux mai cao giex kaing li aux hu;
mai aux hoik laux ka kaing ku ceu heue!”
25 Ceu pui Tang ah nin ka nawh:
“Hoik mhawm ka ang maix lai krai nie,
phan ang mawh nan,
maix cheeik pui in kix song rhawm,
mai kix tag sang lauh ka maix,
maix khawm pui nyiex pui ma maix,
tag sang yaox tix laux leeing khawm uik.”
26 Ceu pui Tang ca nied tix hu plak sivoe;
Mi-cia yaox mawh kix pui pa ting riang,
mai to kaux nawh ang doh pex ka kix,
kheu nan nawh deeh ing ka nyiex heue.
27-28 Khaix hoik tud ceu pui Tang ka cao giex kaing li mai riang siyiex cie Mi-cia,
kix hu puing ka La-yix.
La-yix mai Paw Li-haw hoik ot ka daux ngawd gawng tau,
pa mawh veng pa nhim pa tiiem,
pa yien pa yawg.
Ceu pui Tang pauh ka pui pa ot ka daux veng,
mai ploi ngu gleeing veng.
Ang koe pui pa hoek taoh ka kix,
kheu kied singai ot La-yix khaing Si-toeng,
kix kawx ang nyang sax hoek hu ka pui.
Ceu pui Tang cox sum ka veng,
mai ot paung tan.
29 Kix hawt ceu kaux tax du kix kawn simex Ya-keux pa kaux Tang sum,
mai loh ceu kaux veng La-yix in mawh Tang.
30 Ceu pui Tang priex ka riang krub an mhawm,
mai tawx kawn saux Maw-si pa mawh Yaw-na-tang kawn simex Keu-soeng,
yuh cao giex kaing li ceu pui Tang.
Kawn saux Yaw-na-tang,
yuh cao giex kaing li dee an tom hoek pon giex pui ceu pui Tang hu.
31 Ka daux baux bawg yam ot nyiex buan man Siyiex ka See-law,
riang krub Mi-cia ot ka Tang heue.
