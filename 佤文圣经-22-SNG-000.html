Lai Rhax Pa Kied Mhawm
Krai dawt
Lai Rhax Pa Kied Mhawm, ceu kaux sivoe an mawh “lox rhax khaing daux lox rhax”, mai mawh nang “lox rhax pa kied mhawm”. In hoik mawh lox rhax grawng krax muh, teei ka plak lox rhax mai tiiem ka simex mai bun ra lai krai lao ka grawng krax muh paox tix. Koe phuk lai pa thut tix blah kok phuk lai in mawh “lox rhax Saw-law-meung”, mawh kheu ka daux lai prim Si-paw-lai hoik krai mawh lox rhax in pa yuh Saw-law-meung heue.
Pui Yo-thaix hoik ah mawh phuk lai in pa tiiem ka grawng pa yuh Siyiex paox pie tau mai pui cie tix; viang mawh nan, kawn Khrit ah mawh pa tiiem ka grawng pa yuh Khrit paox pie tau mai ceu cao heue.
Krai kaing
Lox rhax tix mu mawh (1.1–2.7)
Lox rhax ra ceu mawh (2.8–3.5)
Lox rhax loe ceu mawh (3.6–5.1)
Lox rhax pon ceu mawh (5.2–6.3)
Lox rhax phuan ceu mawh (6.4–8.4)
Lox rhax liah ceu mawh (8.5–14)
