Pum simaox krax pa doh keud
4
Khaix hoik tiang pui meung khawm uik ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yaw-su-yax:
2 “Maix laug pui kao ra kaux ka daux pui khankix,
tix ceu pui tix kaux,
3 kieh kix hu ka daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
dee cung cao giex kaing li,
teei simaox kao ra lon,
viex hu,
deuh ot dee sum pex bang bo in.”
4 Kheu nan,
Yaw-su-yax khrawm ka pui kao ra kaux pa hoik laug nawh,
5 ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hu ka daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
hu sivoe teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tix kaux pui teei simaox tix mu ka daux greem rawm,
klawm ka piang klip tix.
Simaox tix lon tang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tix ceu.
6 Pex tag grux simaox in kix kieh mawh mhai sakse.
Khaix in sax,
kawn saux kawn sex pex chawk ka pex,
simaox in kix mawh mhai son ceu pa tix.
7 Pex tag krai ka kix,
yam tiang teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
rawm klawng hoik ang lai lih hu.
Simaox in kix sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex tawng kaing pa hoik lih dee in leeing ju.”
8 Pui kao ra kaux hawt ka lox pa krai Yaw-su-yax.
Kix hawt awm lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Yaw-su-yax,
teei simaox daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang kao ra lon,
simaox tix lon tang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tix ceu,
viex hoek ka daux bang,
deuh ot dee an.
9 Yaw-su-yax kawx ot ka daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
dee klawm cao giex kaing li teug sijax mukheu,
baox grux ka simaox kao ra lon.
(Simaox an kix kawn koe tom in singaix.)
10 Cao giex kaing li khankix pa klawm ka teug sijax mukheu cung ka daux greung rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
tom hoik hoik kaing yuh pui khankix ka lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Yaw-su-yax.
In mawh pa hawt lox pa tawx Maw-si heue.
Pui khankix nied tix hu tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
11 Khaix hoik tiang kix ka plak toi rawm,
cao giex kaing li khankix klawm ka teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
hu plak sivoe ngai kawn pui,
hu ka sivoe.
12 Ceu pui Lui-piien,
ceu pui Cia-teux,
ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak hoik rien kru naing tix,
hawt ka lox Maw-si,
mai hu nang sivoe ngai pui hun.
13 Koe pui kru naing pa hoik khre rien tix pon mheen kaux,
hoik tiang ka rawm klawng sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
hoek ka daux lung veng Yie-li-keu,
son sang yuh naing heue.
14 Ka daux ngaix an,
Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex khankix yaog Yaw-su-yax ting min plak ngai,
mai kieh nawh yuh simiang pa ting.
Jauh yam an,
Yaw-su-yax awm Maw-si,
leeing ju pon krax pa nab yam yung yi.
15 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax
16 tawx lox ka cao giex kaing li khankix pa klawm ka teug sijax mukheu haok khaing daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
17 Yaw-su-yax hawt tix yuh heue.
18 Yam taug lih cao giex kaing li khankix khaing daux rawm,
rawm klawng baox jauh tix lih hu,
awm sivoe an naog khawm ra plak.
19 Khix tix kao ngaix,
pui khankix hoik tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai sum bang ot ka Cie-cia plak lih singaix veng Yie-li-keu heue.
20 Yaw-su-yax teei simaox kao ra lon pa teei khaing daux dawng rawm klawng Yaw-tang sum ot dee an.
21 Nawh ah nin ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix:
“Tix ngaix sax,
phan kawn praix pex chawk,
simaox in mawh mhai pa tix?
22 Pex tag krai ka kix,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik hu ka dawng ang koe rawm ka daux rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
23 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex hoik kieh rawm klawng Yaw-tang sivoe pex kroh,
tom hoik hu ka khawm uik,
awm yam kieh nawh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh lih kroh num sivoe,
kieh ex lih hu yuh nan.
24 Mawh kheu kaing in,
kawn pui piang haktiex hoik tawng Simiang Siyiex kied ting riang ting rao,
phan mawh pex tag lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex leeing ju.”
