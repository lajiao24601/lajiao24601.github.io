Simiang Siyiex jhi to kaux tix ka Sa-mu-eu
3
Sa-mu-eu kawn nyawm iak an,
yuh kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex ka daux krax pa sidiex gau Yi-lix.
Ka daux singaix bawg yam an,
gum lox pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex ang lai lih koe jheeng jheeng,
mai riang su sai kawx hoik kied yawm koe.
2 Koe ka daux tix bo,
Yi-lix kuad pa hoik vhak ngai it ka daux nyiex tix;
3 Sa-mu-eu it ka dee singax pa deuh teug sijax mukheu Siyiex.
Yam ang praix nyang bax riang,
kawk ngu ca kawn riang,
4 Simiang Siyiex kok ka Sa-mu-eu.
Sa-mu-eu pawk ah nin:
“Mawh,
aux ot tin!”
5 Nawh tom to hu ka Yi-lix dee an,
ah nin:
“Maix kok ka aux lie?
Aux ot tin.”
Yi-lix ah nin:
“Aux ang bax kok ka maix,
deeh ing it heue!”
Sa-mu-eu tom deeh ing it.
6-7 Simiang Siyiex baox kok ka sha-mu-eux.
Yam an,
Sa-mu-eu ang nyang bax tawng ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu ang Simiang Siyiex nyang sax jhi nawh yaox tix.
Nawh baox deeh kaoh,
hu ka Yi-lix dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Maix kok ka aux lie?
Aux ot tin heue.”
Yi-lix ah nin:
“Kawn aux eue,
aux ang bax kok ka maix,
deeh ing it heue!”
8 Simiang Siyiex kok ka Sa-mu-eu ka daux loe bawg.
Nawh baox kaoh,
hu ka Yi-lix dee an,
mai ah nin:
“Maix kok ka aux lie?
Aux ot tin heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-lix hoik tawng mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kok ka kawn nyawm in,
9 mai nawh tom ah nin ka Sa-mu-eu:
“Deeh ing it heue;
phan nawh baox kok ka maix,
maix tom ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex eue,
krai heue,
siliex nghiet ka heue!’ ”
Sa-mu-eu tom deeh ing it.
10 Simiang Siyiex hoek,
mai cung dee an,
baox kok yo awm bawg sivoe yuh nan:
“Sa-mu-eu!
Sa-mu-eu!”
Sa-mu-eu pawk ah nin:
“Krai heue,
siliex nghiet ka heue!”
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Ang lai rheeng,
aux tag sang yuh kaing pa ting tix ceu ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kieh pui pa mhong ka ku kaux yaoh yee kah.
12 Singaix an,
aux tag sang yuh lih khawm uik ka lox pa hoik krai aux ka nyiex ma Yi-lix.
13 Aux hoik krai ka nawha,
aux tag sang ciyang ka nyiex ma nawh,
kheu lox pa caox pa caig pa ah kawn simex nawh khankix ka aux.
Yi-lix hoik tawng yuh kix kaing pa caox,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang tieb tie ka kix khaing.
14 Kheu nan,
aux tom bre tix krai ka nyiex ma Yi-lix ah nin:
khawng phak lak sawng,
Krax-tan Khawng-brie ceu pa tix ang ah,
tag ang pon tix taoh ka phi map pa cien in heue.”
15 Sa-mu-eu it tom hoek riang praix,
nawh kaoh paoh siviex nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex;
nawh lhat krai riang su sai pa yaox tix ka Yi-lix.
16 Yi-lix kok nawh ah nin:
“Kawn aux Sa-mu-eu eue!”
Sa-mu-eu pawk ah nin:
“Mawh,
aux ot tin!”
17 Yi-lix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex krai lox ceu pa tix ka maix lie?
Ang pon tix mox ka aux.
Phan maix ang krai lih khawm uik,
kieh Siyiex ciyang ka maix tom cien!”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu krai lih sidawng khawm uik,
ang mox pie ceeix kawx.
Yi-lix ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Simiang Siyiex;
kieh nawh yuh awm pa khaw caw rhawm nawh heue.”
19 Sa-mu-eu ting hu si nyek si nyek;
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai nawh,
mai kieh lox pa krai nawh lih tix mawh ku gum.
20 Kawn pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
jauh ka haktiex Tang tom hoek ka haktiex Piie-see-pa,
hoik tawng Sa-mu-eu mawh cao jhai pa laug Simiang Siyiex tete.
21 Simiang Siyiex ca kawn ot ka haktiex See-law,
pa mawh dee jhi Sa-mu-eu yaox tix yam si kah tau mai nawh heue.
