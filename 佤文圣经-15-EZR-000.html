Lai Yi-see-la
Krai dawt
Lai Yi-see-la mawh pa toe tix tiiem hawt Lai Grawng Been, mai krai grawng pa deeh hoek pui Yo-thaix pa hoik giex laung pui tix blah khankix khaing Pa-pi-loeng, kieh kix deeh yaox tix ot mai yuh kaing pa krub ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung. Kaing in kix guah tix koe loe baux plak greem:
1. Pui Yo-thaix pa giex laung pui bawg tix, deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix ka greem gum lox pa hoik tawx Vawng Paw-see pa kaux Sai-lu-seex khaing haktiex Pa-pi-loeng heue.
2. Cox sum ka nyiex buan, cox yuh ka krax tan pa khraox. Pui Yo-thaix deeh yaox tix yuh ka krax pa krub kix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
3. Khaix hoik koe ra loe num, pui Yo-thaix pa hoik giex laung pui tang bawg khankix, deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ka daux krax pa thung Yi-see-la. Yi-see-la mawh pui pa hoik pon awng ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex tix kaux; nawh teem coi kawn pui cox been li mai krax ot krax koe pui sidawng sidu, yuh been pa kieh meung pui Yi-seu-liex kaux cong heue.
Krai kaing
1. Pui pa hoik giex laung pui bawg tix deeh ing ka meung tix (1.1–2.70).
2. Cox sum ka nyiex buan, cox yuh ka krax tan pa khraox (3.1–6.22).
3. Yi-see-la thung pui haoh hun pa hoik giex laung pui tang cub deeh ing ka meung tix (7.1–10.44).
