Simiang Ya-sa pon pex ka pui Ku-seu
14
Khaix hoik yum simiang Ya-pi-yax,
kix si paung nawh ka veng Ta-wue daux tiag si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang;
kawn simex nawh Ya-sa plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh.
Glang yam kawn giex Ya-sa kaing,
meung haktiex hoik pon krax pa nhim kao num.
2 Ya-sa yuh kaing pa mhawm,
nawh koe rhawm pa rauh see,
mai yuh kaing pa khaw ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh.
3 Nawh yieh tix tik khu pui tang ceu mai nyiex vad pa ot piang gawng iak,
hoik lauh ka rong simaox max,
kid ka rong riang bun siyiex Ya-seu-la goh.
4 Nawh tawx lox nhie pui Yo-tax sawg ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix,
mai cu nghiet ka lox sidiex gau nawh mai gum lox ping nyap nawh.
5 Simiang Ya-sa hoik yieh tix tik ka can nyiex vad mai khu gleeing nyawng bhawng pa koe ka veng Yo-tax khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
bawg yam giex nawh kaing,
meung Yo-tax pon pa nhim pa tiiem.
6 Kax daux baux bawg yam an,
nawh sum veng pa kieh kaux cong son pui Yo-tax,
meung haktiex ang lai koe krax naing tom nie num,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx krax pa nhim ka nawh.
7 Nawh ah nin ka kawn pui Yo-tax:
“Ex sang sum guh veng,
nyiex lhaong veng,
mai siviex veng pa chix nyeep chix paoh tix son sang kieh dung veng kaux cong.
Mawh kheu priex ex tix hawt ka alo Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
ex tom pon yaox tix giex gang ka meung haktiex in.
Nawh hoik kia ngien ka ex jheeng jheeng,
mai hoik tawx krax pa nhim ka ex khawm pon daog.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix jauh tix ting lhaong haok huan,
mai kaing yuh kix pon mhawm ku ceu.
8 Pui naing cie simiang Ya-sa khawm pui Yo-tax pa hoik rien khre ka dieb tie naok mai pliah tix ngoe mheen kaux,
mai baox mawh khawm pui Piien-ya-ming pa hoik rien khre dieb tie naok mai ak tix nga daix mheen kaux.
Kix hoik mawh pui naing pa kied kua khawm uik heue.
9 Simiang Ku-seu pa kaux Shie-la thung pui naing tix yieh mheen kaux mai ling law loe yieh lhang hu puing ka pui Yo-tax;
kix hoek ka Ma-li-sa.
10 Ya-sa lih rieb tix puing kix.
Kix khawm ra plak rien tix sang puing paox ka dee ngawd Si-fa dex Ma-li-sa.
11 Ya-sa rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix chix teem coi ka pui naing pa iak riang iak rao,
ka pui naing pa ting riang ting rao kawx maix chix teem coi kah.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
yam in khiox khaw maix teem coi ka yix,
mawh kheu yix een rhawm tix ka maix.
Yix lih yuh naing ka pui naing pa ting riang ting rao in kix khaing ka daux ceu kaux maix.
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh Siyiex yix,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon pex ka maix heue.”
12 Yam puing Ya-sa mai pui naing Yo-tax ka pui naing Ku-seu,
Simiang Siyiex puing ka pui naing Ku-seu.
Kix yaog tix to;
13 Ya-sa thung pui naing tix leue ka kix,
mai leue kix hoek tom Ci-la-eux.
Pui naing Ku-seu kied nie yum,
mai ang lai pon yuh naing.a
Simiang Siyiex mai pui naing cie nawh hoik pex ka kix;
pui naing Yo-tax hoik teei ka kraung pon khaing naing tom nie.
14 Kix hoik yuh laux ka nyiex yaong pa ot sivie siveng Ci-la-eux,
mawh kheu kawn pui haktiex an hoik lhat graung ka Simiang Siyiex.
Pui naing Yo-tax pon tud ka veng an kix,
mai teei ka kraung pa pon khaing naing tom nie.
15 Kix kawx hoik yieh ka nyiex gox pui pa liang khawng brie khankix,
teei yung mai ka-la-uk hu tom nie,
khaix an deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
