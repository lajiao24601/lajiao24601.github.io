Kawn cau pa hoek khaing Pa-pi-loeng
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 20.12–19)
39
Ka daux bawg yam in,
kawn simex Pa-la-tang simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Mi-law-ta Pa-la-tang hoik mhong grawng pa saux simiang Si-si-cia,
mai deeh moi,
tom tawx lai pa chawk nawh mhawm ot mai khawng phak tix ceu ka nawh.
2 Si-si-cia rieb ka kawn cau pa ting,
mai tawx kix jhak ka sin ngao tix,
pa mawh khri,
mau,
sidah bhawng,
rawm bhawng,
mai kraung naing khawm uik;
nawh tawx kix jhak ka nyiex krao meung haktiex nawh ku dauh,
mai can pa koe nawh khawm uik.
3 Mawh kheu nan cao jhai Yi-sai-yax hoek ka simiang Si-si-cia,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Pui in kix kaoh khaing dee mawx?
Krai kix lox ceu pa tix ka maix?”
Si-si-cia pawk lox ah nin:
“Kix kaoh khaing meung Pa-pi-loeng pa kied singai heue.”
4 Yi-sai-yax chawk ah nin:
“Yaox kix ceu pa tix ka daux nyiex haw simiang lie?”
Si-si-cia pawk lox ah nin:
“Kix hoik yaox ku ceu khawm uik;
aux hoik tawx kix jhak kraung ceu pa koe ka daux nyiex krao ku ceu heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-sai-yax ah nin ka simiang:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
6 ‘Bawg yam hoik dex hoek,
kraung ka daux nyiex haw simiang ku ceu,
pa mawh kraung pa hoik deuh tax du maix ku ceu,
tag caw tix gah hu ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh koe tix ceu kawx heue.
7 aPie doeh ceu cad maix kawx tag sang giex kix hu,
kieh kix yuh pui sieh tix ka daux nyiex haw simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
mai yuh kaing son simiang kix heue.’ ”
8 Si-si-cia keud gum lox in mawh pa krai grawng sang pon krax pa nhim mai krax pa nyawm ot yam kawn im ju tix ka piang haktiex,
mawh kheu nan nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Gum lox pa krai maix pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex kied mhawm heue.”
