Riang su sai pa yaox Yi-si-ciex bawg ra
(8.1–10.22)
Krub riang rao daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung
8
Yam vuk vied vuk viang ex ka daux liah num haok khix liah daux phuan ngaix,
kuad song pa hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix ka Yo-tax khankix hoek tix kia aux ka nyiex aux.
Riang rao Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong lih hoek piang aux dum mawx dum an.
2 aAux yaog ngai tix jhak,
mai yaox ka pa awm riang pak peung pui yuh nan;
plak greem nyoi nawh awm ngu,
plak piang nyoi nawh awm tawng pa hoik awt plang yuh nan.
3 Nawh nyeu ceu pa awm taix tix khawn yuh nan,
mai giex ka hauk kaing aux.
Khaix an,
ka daux riang su sai an,
khuan Siyiex yaog aux haok daux rao ma,
mai viex aux hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Nawh thung aux hu ka dee laig ka biang brawk siviex nyiex buan plak laong.
Dee an koe ka riang rao krub pui pa cheeik tix kieh Siyiex song rhawm tix mu heue.
4 bKa dee an,
aux yaox ka min plak ngai Siyiex Yi-seu-liex,
mai awm pa hoik yaox aux ka praok rawm klawng Cia-pa-lu yuh nan nang.
5 Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix jhak ka plak laong heue!”
Aux tom jhak ka plak laong,
mai yaox ka riang rao krub pui tix mu pa cheeik tix kieh Siyiex song rhawm pa ot ka praok khu dee tan ka diang siviex dee laig ka grah heue.
6 Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix hoik yaox kix yuh ceu pa tix lie?
Maix jhak,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik yuh kaing pa kied caox caig dee in,
mai khin aux hu singai khaing nyiex buan aux.
Viang mawh nan,
maix tag sang yaox ka kaing pa kawn lheu caox caig khaing in heue.”
7 Nawh thung aux hu ka diang siviex dee laig ka biang brawk plak praix,
mai kieh aux jhak ka daux doh pa koe ka piang guh ding.
8 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix kaong daux ting tix mu ka piang guh ding in.”
Aux tom kaong ka daux ting tix mu,
mai yaox ka siviex tix mu.
9 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Laig,
jhak tix yaox ka kaing pa caox pa caig pa hoik yum koeng pui pa yuh kix dee an.”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom laig hu jhak tix yaox.
Aux hoik yaox ka riang si uing pa pawd pui naog ka ding,
ka to sat tang ceu pa ang singax singeh,
mai kawn koe nang riang rao pa krub pui Yi-seu-liex.
11 Kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex ot ka dee an atigliah kaux,
mawh khawm kawn simex Sa-fang pa kaux Ya-sha-ni-yax.
Pui ku kaux hoik tang giex ka mawk ngu daux taix tix,
tix kaux tix mu,
mai taox bhawng hoik haok ka rao ma.
12 Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix hoik yaox ka kuad song pui Yi-seu-liex hoik briex tix yuh ceu pa tix lie?
Kix yuh krax krub ka daux nyiex pa naog riang rao an.
Kix kieh tix koe krax ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex ang yaox ka ex;
nawh hoik tik vud ka haktiex in heue.’ ”
13 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Maix tag sang yaox ka kaing pa yuh kix kawn lheu caox caig khaing in heue.”
14 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh thung aux hu ka siviex nyiex buan plak laong,
mai kieh aux yaox ka cub bun miex khankix;
kix hoik yiiem son pa yum siyiex Ta-maw-see c heue.
15 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix hoik yaox ka kaing in lie?
Maix tag sang yaox ka kaing pa yuh kix kawn lheu caox caig khaing in heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh thung aux hu ka dee laig ka diang siviex biang brawk nyiex buan plak daux,
ka si nax khu dee tan mai grah nyiex,
mai koe pui been tix nga phuan kaux,
pa ot plak khaix biang brawk,
pa veeh ngai tix plak lih singaix,
mai krub riang singaix pa jauh tix lih heue.
17 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix hoik yaox ka kaing in lie?
Maix jhak,
pui Yo-tax in kix hoik yuh kaing pa caox caig,
mai yuh krax pa tud rhim ka piang meung khawm uik,
kawn ang bax naog rhawm kah,
kawn baox to hu yuh kaing pa caox caig ka nyiex buan,
mai cheeik aux song rhawm.
Jhak heue,
kix hoik teei krax pa ang nab yamd map nai ka aux.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang klup krax pa song rhawm tix ka piang kix.
Aux ang pluat ploi ka kix,
mai ang caox rhawm ka kix.
Viang kix hoik kok yo lox pa ting si num kawx,
hoik khiox khaw ka aux kawx,
aux kawx ang nghiet ka heue.”
