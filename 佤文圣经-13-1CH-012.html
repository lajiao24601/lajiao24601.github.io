Pui Piien-ya-ming pa jauh tix hawt grawng Ta-wue
12
Ta-wue paung ot ka haktiex Si-keu-la,
yam mox nawh tix ka simiang Sao-law,
pui naing pa kied kua tom nie khankix hoek tix peung ka rhawm nawh.
2 Kix pon teei taix khawm guah tix,
mai taix khawn dawm tix pon puing ka ak puh mai pon tap simaox.
Kix mawh ceu pui paox Sao-law,
pa mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming.
3-7 Pui Ci-pi-yax kawn simex See-ma pa kaux Ya-si-yi-shiex mai Yaw-a-see mawh simiang kix heue.
Plak greem mawh ceu kaux pui naing in kix:
Kawn simex Ya-see-ma-we pa mawh Yie-shiie mai Pi-lix;
pui Ya-na-thu pa mawh Pi-la-cia mai Yie-hu;
pui Ci-piien pa mawh Yi-seu-mai-ya (pui naing pa koe min,
simiang ka daux “pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux” tix kaux).
Kawn koe,
pui Ci-teu-la pa mawh Yie-li-mi,
Ya-ha-si,
Yaw-ha-nang mai Yaw-sa-pa;
pui Ha-lui-fux pa mawh Yi-li-wu-sai,
Yie-li-maw.
Pi-ya-li-ya,
See-ma-li-ya mai See-fa-thi-ya;
pui Kheu-la pa mawh Yi-li-cia-na,
Yie-si-yax,
Ya-sa-liex,
Yaw-yi-shiex mai Ya-saw-pang;
pui Ci-to pa mawh kawn simex Yie-law-hang,
pa kaux Yo-la mai Si-pa-ti-ya heue.
Pui Cia-teux pa hawt grawng Ta-wue
8 Yam ot Ta-wue ka gawng yaong daux tiag,
ceu pui Cia-teux pa kied kua ka naing khankix tix blah,
laig yuh paox tau daux bang naing cie Ta-wue.
Pui naing in kix hoik mawh pui pa kied chix siao dieb tie naok mai pliah khawm uik;
kix hoik awm sivai sang si pa kied vao yuh nan,
awm cak gawng pa kied phai yuh nan.
9-13 Ceu kaux kix hawt awm glawk mawh kix cao bung naing mai dia ot plak greem:
Yi-shiie,
Eu-pa-ti-ya,
Yi-li-ya,
Mi-see-ma-na,
Yie-li-mi,
Ya-thaix,
Yi-li-yiex,
Yaw-ha-nang,
Yi-li-sa-pa,
Yie-li-mi,
Maw-pa-nai heue.
14 Cao bung kaing naing pui Cia-teux in kix,
tix blah thung pui naing tix rheng kaux,
tix blah thung tix yieh kaux.
15 Ka daux num an khix tix,
rawm klawng Yaw-tang haok huan khawm ra plak,
cub pui in kix tiang ka rawm klawng,
mai grheu ka pui pa ot praok klawng plak lih singaix mai plak klip singaix khawm ra plak heue.
Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming mai ceu pui Yo-tax pa hawt grawng Ta-wue
16 Koe tix bawg,
ka daux ceu pui Piien-ya-ming mai ceu pui Yo-tax,
koe pui tix cub hu ka gawng yaong Ta-wue mai peung rhawm tix ka nawh.
17 Ta-wue hu rieb kix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Phan pex yuh paox grawm hoek teem ka aux,
aux gaux rhawm pex hoek laig daux bang naing yix;
aux ang koe rhawm pa sang yuh pex laux.
Phan pex keud tix sang coh aux ka pui pa koeng ka aux,
tax du Siyiex yix hoik tawng kah,
mai nawh tag sang ciyang ka pex heue.”
18 Khuan Siyiex laig ka daux pa mawh Ya-ma-sha cao kaing ka daux (“Simiang pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux”) kix tix kaux,
mai nawh kok yo lox ah nin:
Ta-wue eue,
yix hoik mawh pui cie maix heue!
Kawn simex Yie-si eue,
yix hoik cung ka plak maix in heue!
Kieh maix mai paox grawng maix khankix pon pex heue!
Siyiex maix hoik mawh cao pa teem coi ka maix.
Ta-wue gaux rhawm ka hoek kix,
mai tawx kix mawh cao bung kaing naing ka daux pui naing tix heue.
Ceu pui Ma-na-si pa hawt grawng Ta-wue
19 Yam hu puing Ta-wue ka simiang Sao-law tau mai pui Fe-li-see,
koe pui naing ceu pui Ma-na-si tix blah hoek tix peung ka rhawm nawh.
Tete te lai,
yam an Ta-wue ang tie bax teem ka pui Fe-li-see;
mawh kheu cao kaing pui Fe-li-see khankix lhat Ta-wue coh kix ka simiang nawh Sao-law sivoe an,
mawh kheu nan kix tawx lox nhie Ta-wue deeh ing ka Si-keu-la.
20 Yam deeh ing Ta-wue ka daux krax ing ka Si-keu-la,
pui naing Ma-na-si pa hoek peung ka rhawm nawh mawh:
Ya-na,
Yaw-sa-pa,
Yie-tiie,
Mi-cia-leux,
Yaw-sa-pa,
Yi-li-hux,
Si-leu-thaix.
Pui in kix yam ot kix ka daux pui naing Ma-na-si,
kix hoik mawh cao bung kaing naing piang pui kaing rheng ku kaux.
21 Kix hoik mawh cao bung kaing naing ka daux pui naing cie Ta-wue,a
mawh kheu hoik mawh kix pui naing pa kied kua khankix.
Khaix an,
kix kawx mawh nang cao bung kaing naing ka daux pui naing Yi-seu-liex.
22 Dex sang koe pui ku ngaix pa hoek tix peung ka rhawm Ta-wue,
mawh kheu nin,
pui naing cie Ta-wue ang koe miex glang tix tom lih tix mawh pui naing pa kied ting haoh hun heue.
Pui naing cie Ta-wue
23-37 Yam ot Ta-wue ka haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
pui naing pa hoik gau tix,
hoek neuex tix laig tau mai pui naing cie nawh,
teem coi nawh giex simiang tang simiang Sao-law,
awm gum lox pa hoik dawg ap Simiang Siyiex.
Plak greem mawh kaux pui naing in kix:
ceu pui Yo-tax:
liah rheng daix yieh kaux pui naing pa hoik rien khre kraung naing tix mhawm,
mai hoik viex ka dieb tie naok mai pliah.
Ceu pui Si-miien:
aliah rheng tix yieh kaux pui naing pa kied kua khankix.
Ceu pui Li-we:
pon rheng liah yieh kaux.
Doeh ceu pui Ya-loeng,
pa mawh pui pa hawt grawng Yie-heu-yie-tax koe loe rheng atigliah yieh kaux.
Pui naing pa kied kua pa awn ju khankix,
pa mawh pui ka daux paox nyiex Sa-tu koe simiang tix nga ra kaux.
Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming (ceu pui Sao-law an):
loe rheng kaux (ceu pui Piien-ya-ming plak nie ca kawn hawt ka rhawm Sao-law).
Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien:
ra mheen mai daix yieh kaux ka daux ceu pui kix pa mawh pui naing pa kied kua pa koe min.
Ceu pui Ma-na-si plak klip singaix:
tix mheen daix rheng kaux,
laug lih yaog Ta-wue mawh simiang.
Ceu pui Yi-sa-cia:
ra yieh kaux pa mawh simiang khawm pui pa ot greem kix (pui simiang in kix hoik tawng kaing tix,
pui Yi-seu-liex mawh sang khaw tix yuh kaing ceu pa tix bawg yam mawx,
kix hoik tawng kah).
Ceu pui Si-pu-loeng:
phuan mheen kaux pa mawh pui pa kaux sijax,
mai mawh pui naing pa hoik pon siao ka kraung naing ku ceu,
bawg yam mawx ang ah kix chix tix hu puing naing.
Ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix:
tix rheng kaux pui simiang naing khawm pui naing loe mheen aliah rheng kaux pa pon siao ka dieb tie naok mai pliah.
Ceu pui Tang:
ra mheen daix rheng liah yieh kaux pa mawh pui pa hoik gau tix chix.
Ceu pui Ya-seux:
pon mheen kaux,
mai yam mawx ang ah,
kix chix tix hu pui naing.
Ceu pui Lui-piien,
ceu pui Cia-teux,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singaix pa ot praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix:
kao ra mheen kaux pa mawh pui naing pa hoik gau tix chix,
mai hoik pon siao ka kraung naing ku ceu heue.
38 Pui naing in kix plak piang,
yam mawx ang ah hoik chix tix hu puing naing.
Kix hu ka haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
mai hoik but rhawm tix yaog Ta-wue mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix kawx hoik khrawm paox tix yaog Ta-wue mawh simiang.
39 Kix ot dee an loe singaix,
mai ih priex tau mai Ta-wue;
priex preem in kix hoik mawh pa khre paox ceu paox cad nawh son kix.
40 Kawn pui kaoh khaing ceu pui Yi-sa-cia,
Si-pu-loeng,
Na-fu-tha-lix khankix,
pa ot dee singai ka caong ma laong,
kix kieh la,
ka-la-uk,
law,
mai moi tang miien gaox,
mai plix ruh,
plix siiet kroh,
plai,
mai bee sime priex ih khankix.
Kix kawx viex nang moi yung hoek tom nie,
son sang pauh tix ih,
mawh kheu pui piang meung hoik naog gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
