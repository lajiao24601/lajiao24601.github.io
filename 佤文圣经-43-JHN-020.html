Si meeig yi
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.1–8; Lai Ma-kheu 16.1–8; Lai Lu-cia 24.1–12)
20
Ka daux singaix tuah pon ngawb cao,
yam ang praix nyang bax riang,
Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la hu ka si meeig,
yaox hoik gah simaox tix hu khaing moing si meeig.
2 Nawh tom to tix hu sawg ka Si-meung mai pui gau pa muh nawh tang kaux,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pui hoik teei Simiang Ye-sux lih hu khaing daux si meeig,
yix ang tawng een kix nawh dee mawx heue!”
3 Pi-teux mai pui gau an tom hu ka si meeig.
4 Kiex ra kaux to tix hu tau,
viang mawh nan,
pui gau an brax to khaing Pi-teux,
nawh hoek ka daux si meeig sivoe.
5 Nawh num to kaux tix jhak daux si meeig,
mai yaox man keei sum kawn ot dee an,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax laig ka daux si meeig.
6 Si-meung Pi-teux kawx hoek nang;
nawh hu luan daux si meeig,
mai yaox man keei sum kawn ot dee an,
7 mai baox yaox ka man khoek pa kie ka Ye-sux,
ang bax ot tau mai man keei sum,
kawn pua tix tang ot tix dauh.
8 Pui gau pa hoek sivoe an kawx laig hawt Pi-teux ka daux si meeig nang;
yam yaox nawh kah,
nawh hoik yum rhawm ka heue. (
9 Kiex kawn ang bax tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox phuk lai sigang khri,
pa hoik krai grawng tag sang kaoh im khaing krax yum heue.)
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pui gau an kiex ra kaux deeh ing ka nyiex heue.
Ye-sux jhi Ma-li-yax pa mawh haktiex pui Maw-ta-la yaox tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.9–10; Lai Ma-kheu 16.9–11)
11 Ma-li-yax kawn cung yiiem ka plak praix si meeig.
Nawh yiiem mai num kaing tix kok jhak ka daux si meeig,
12 mai yaox kawn cau Siyiex ra kaux pa ceep kraung paing,
pa ngawm ka dee deuh kix to kaux Ye-sux sivoe an,
tix kaux ot ka plak kaing,
tix kaux ot ka plak caong heue.
13 Kiex chawk Ma-li-yax ah nin:
“Bun,
yuh ka mawx maix lai yiiem lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Kix hoik teei simiang aux hu,
aux ang tawng mawh deuh kix nawh ot dee mawx heue!”
14 Khaix hoik krai nawh lox in,
Ma-li-yax kling to kaux tix,
mai yaox Ye-sux cung ka dee an,
viang mawh nan,
kawn ang bax tawng mawh Ye-sux.
15 Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Bun,
yuh ka mawx maix lai yiiem lie?
Sawg maix pui mawx lie?”
Ma-li-yax ce nawh mawh pui pa pau si peem,
mawh kheu nan,
krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
phan mawh maix pa teei hu,
krai ka aux mawh deuh maix nawh ot dee mawx,
son sang hu aux teei nawh hoek heue.”
16 Ye-sux kok nawh ah nin:
“Ma-li-yax!”
Ma-li-yax kling to kaux tix,
mai si kah ka lox Si-paw-lai ah nin:
“La-paw-ni” (dee bleeh mawh “Sara” heue.)
17 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix paw vi bluh ka aux,
mawh kheu ang aux bax haok ka keeing aux dee an.
Maix hu ka pux aik aux dee an,
mai ah nin ka kix:
‘Aux tag sang haok ka keeing aux,
mai hoik mawh keeing pex nang;
haok ka Siyiex aux,
mai hoik mawh Siyiex pex nang heue.’ ”
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Ma-li-yax pa mawh pui haktiex Maw-ta-la hu krai ka pui gau nawh,
mai krai hoik yaox ka Simiang Ye-sux,
mai baox krai lox pa nhie Simiang Ye-sux nawh krai heue.
Ye-sux jhi tix ka pui gau
(Lai Ma-thaix 28.16–20; Lai Ma-kheu 16.14–18; Lai Lu-cia 24.36–49)
19 Ka daux singaix tuah pon bo,
pui gau Ye-sux khrawm tix ot tau,
mai nyip siviex kaux,
mawh kheu lhat kix ka simiang pui Yo-thaix.
Bawg yam an,
Ye-sux jhi ka tix,
mai cung ka si nax kix,
ah nin:
“Kieh pex pon krax pa nyawm rhawm heue!”
20 Khaix hoik krai nawh ka lox in,
nawh tawx kix yaox ka taix tix mai ka paw tix.
Pui gau yaox ka Simiang Ye-sux,
mai kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
21 Ye-sux baox ah nin ka kix:
“Kieh pex pon krax pa nyawm rhawm heue!
Awm hoik ploi keeing aux hoek,
aux kawx hawt tix ploi pex hu yuh nan nang.”
22 Khaix hoik krai nawh lox in,
nawh phlu bhawm tix ka kix tix gum ah nin:
“Rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh heue!
23 aPhan pex pluat ploi ka phi map pui mawx,
pui an tag pon krax pa pluat ploi;
phan pex ang pluat ploi ka phi map pui mawx,
pui an tag ang pon krax pa pluat ploi heue.”
Ye-sux mai To-ma
24 Yam jhi Ye-sux kix yaox tix,
ka daux kawn cau kao ra kaux pa mawh To-ma (pa mawh ceu kaux kawn brawm) ang bax ot tau mai kix.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
pui gau tang kaux hoik krai grawng pa yaox tix Ye-sux ka To-ma heue.
To-ma ah nin ka kix:
“Lheu khaing teei aux ngai tix yaox ka rhoi khaix tawk jim rhiem ka tawm taix nawh,
khaix an teei giien taix tix pu pi ka rhoi khaix tawk jim rhiem,
teei giien taix tix pu pi ka paw nawh,
aux ang doh yum rhawm ka heue.”
26 Khaix hoik luan tix vang tuah,
pui gau baox khrawm tix ot daux nyiex;
To-ma kawx ot tau mai kix.
Siviex nyip tix,
viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux jhi tix lih dum mawx dum an,
mai cung ka si nax kix ah nin:
“Kieh pex pon krax pa nyawm rhawm heue!”
27 Khaix an nawh ah nin ka To-ma:
“Teei giien taix maix deuh ot dee in,
jhak ka tawm taix aux heue;
baox tawx taix tix,
pu pi ka paw aux.
Paw vi ang koe krax yum rhawm,
yum rhawm khaing heue!”
28 To-ma ah nin:
“Simiang aux,
Siyiex aux heue!”
29 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix taug yum rhawm ka yaox tix aux lie?
Pui pa ang bax yaox ka aux mai hoik yum rhawm kah,
kied koe buan son heue!”
Kaing ting phuk lai in
30 Ye-sux ot ka plak sivoe ngai pui gau tix hoik yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax tiiem ot ka daux phuk lai in.
31 Phuk lai in kaing ting mawh son sang nhie pex yum rhawmb ka Ye-sux mawh Khrit,
mawh kawn simex Siyiex,
khaix an tag sang kieh pex pon ka ju kheu yum rhawm ka nawh heue.
