Khawng phak pa tan lu son pa sum nyiex buan
29
aSimiang Ta-wue krai pao ka pui hun khawm uik ah nin:
“Kawn simex aux Saw-law-meung hoik mawh pa laug Siyiex;
viang mawh nan,
nawh kawn nhum ju,
rhawm keud nawh kawn cud cha.
Kaing pa sang yuh nawh lih hoik kied mawh pa nu tete;
mawh kheu nyiex buan in ang mawh pa sum kawn pui ot daux,
mai hoik mawh nyiex buan pa sum son Simiang Siyiex.
2 Aux sau riang rao tix rien khre kraung khrai son nyiex buan in,
pa mawh khri mau rhiem tawng,
khaox ox,
simaox pa ngawh,
siang simaox,
simaox pa hoik breeh,
mai simaox pa mhawm plang.
3 Lheu khaing hoik rien khre aux ka in kix,
aux kawn baox teei mau khri lih khaing ka daux sin ngao cie to kaux tix,
mawh kheu muh aux ka nyiex buan Siyiex.
4 Aux hoik teei ka khri pa blawn mhawm tix yieh kongtoeng mai mau pa blawn mhawm been ra yieh tipon kongtoeng yuh ding nyiex buan mhawm kah,
5 khawm pa tawx cang mai ku kaux lox tix raug yuh kraung tang ceu ka khankix.
Yam in,
pui mawx sang si pieh rhawm tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex lie?”
6 Mawh kheu nan,
simiang ceu cub nyiex pui ku ceu,
simiang naing ceu pui ku ceu,
simiang naing kaing rheng kaux mai kaing yieh kaux,
mai cao pa kuang sin ngao daux nyiex simiang khankix,
hoik gaux rhawm
7 tix tan lu khawng phak son pa yuh kaing nyiex buan.
Hoik koe khri tix yieh atigliah kongtoeng,
mau loe yieh tipon kongtoeng,
tawng been liah yieh tix nga kongtoeng,
mai rhiem loe rheng pon yieh kongtoeng.
8 Pui pa koe siang simaox khankix hoik tan lu lih,
mai deuh daux nyiex krao nyiex buan,
mai kieh ceu pui Keu-soeng pui Li-we pa kaux Yie-shiie mawh cao pa kuang kia.
9 Kawn pui hoik gaux rhawm tix tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex;
pui ku kaux hoik gaux rhawm kheu hoik pon tan lu kix kraung khrai khankix tom nie lih.
Simiang Ta-wue kawx hoik kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
Ta-wue yaog yaw Siyiex
10 Simiang Ta-wue yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex ka plak sivoe ngai pui haoh pui hun khawm uik.
Nawh ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex tax du ex Ya-keux eue,
kieh maix pon krax pa yaog yaw leeing ju leeing phan!
11 bMaix kied ting lhaong;
maix koe riang pa ting,
min plak ngai pa jhan riang,
siawp sibrawm.
Piang haktiex greem rao ma khawm uik hoik mawh cie maix;
maix mawh simiang,
mawh cao pa phan yuh ku ceu.
12 Can krax pa mag mi,
sin ngao hoik hoek khaing maix dee an;
maix teei pa ting riang rao akhuang tix giex gang ka can pa koe khawm uik.
Maix pon kieh pui ting riang ting rao,
mhawm kaux mhawm hak.
13 Siyiex yix eue,
yix ah lox pa ting buan son ka maix,
yaog yaw ka ceu kaux min plak ngai maix heue!
14 “Pui cie aux mai aux ang lai koe ceu pa tawx ka maix;
mawh kheu can pa koe khawm uik hoik mawh maix pa tawx,
yix hoik tawx ngie ka maix pa mawh cie maix.
15 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawng yix mawh pui pa vung viang mai pui pa ot ka piang haktiex,
awm tax du yix yuh nan.
Krax ot krax koe yix awm riang pang peue pa kied phai luan,
ang chix mox tix khaing krax yum.
16 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
yix viex sin ngao in hoek,
son sang sum ka nyiex buan tix lhang,
son sang yaog yaw ka min ceu kaux maix.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing maix dee an,
mai mawh nang cie maix.
17 Aux tawng maix cheuk tix yaox ka rhawm kawn pui;
maix muh ka pui rauh see.
Aux teei krax pa yum rhawm gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix tan ka maix.
Aux hoik yaox ka pui cie maix gaux rhawm gaux rhi khawm viex tix khawng tan mai tan ka maix.
18 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh pui cie maix yung yi ka maix leeing ju,
mai kieh kix yum rhawm ka maix leeing ju.
19 Khiox khaw maix tawx kawn simex aux Saw-law-meung rhawm pa kaux cong tix mu,
kieh nawh priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng maix khawm uik,
siao ka kraung pa rien aux sum ka nyiex buan cie maix heue.”
20 Khaix an,
Ta-wue tawx lox ka pui khawm uik:
“Tag yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex!”
Kheu nan,
pui khawm uik yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix.
Kawn pui krub ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai nab yam nang simiang heue.
21 Ngaix khaix,
kawn pui pauh ka khawng brie mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an tag guah ka pui khawm uik ih.
Lheu khaing an,
kix baox pauh ka moi mhaing tix rheng mu,
yung mhaing tix rheng mu,
kawn pex tix rheng mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.
Kix kawx tan ka Krax-tan Pa-jauh.
22 Ngaix an,
kix sawm nyaux ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
kied gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
Kawn pui bawg ra krai pao Saw-law-meung mawh simiang.
Kix teei ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex ap nawh mawh simiang piang kix,
mai ap Sa-tu mawh cao giex kaing li.
23 cKheu nan,
Saw-law-meung plud ka keeing tix Ta-wue ngawm ka dee ngawm simiang;
sibrawm in mawh pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex.
Saw-law-meung mawh pa mhawm yuh simiang tix kaux;
pui Yi-seu-liex khawm meung tag hawt ka lox nawh.
24 Cao kaing mai pui naing khawm uik khawm kawn simex tang kaux Ta-wue,
tag nghiet ka lox simiang Saw-law-meung.
25 Simiang Siyiex kieh pui khawm meung yung yi nghiet ka lox Saw-law-meung,
mai kieh sibrawm Saw-law-meung lheu ting khaing can simiang pa kuang ka Yi-seu-liex heue.
Krai grawng pa giex Ta-wue kaing
26 Kawn simex Yie-si pa kaux Ta-wue kuang ka Yi-seu-liex
27 dkhawm uik koe tipon num:
Yuh simiang ka Si-paw-loeng aliah num,
yuh simiang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung tix ngoe loe num.
28 Ta-wue kied mag mi nyawm ot,
yum mai kuad ju tix;
kawn simex nawh Saw-law-meung plud tix yuh simiang khaix nawh.
29 Grawng kaing yuh simiang Ta-wue leeing ju hoik tiiem ka daux lai cao pa chix yaox sivoe Sa-mu-eu,
cao jhai Na-tang,
cao pa chix yaox sivoe Cia-teux.
30 Lai in kix hoik krai gawng kuang Ta-wue mai krax kua nawh,
mai hoik tiiem ka grawng kaing yuh nawh mai grawng meung Yi-seu-liex khawm meung hawt vung viang khawm uik heue.
