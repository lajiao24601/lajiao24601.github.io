Nyiex haw simiang Saw-law-meung
7
Saw-law-meung sum nyiex haw tix lhang son tix,
kao loe num taug hoik sum tix.
2-3 Nyiex haw Li-pa-nuing-linga krax lang koe tipon pon kongjhee,
krax vieh koe tix nga ra kongjhee,
krax lhaong koe kao loe kongjhee plak;
koe rong khaox si oe bhawng ka loe tiab,
tix tia kao phuan gawng,
ka piang kap ka lat gi khaox si oe bhawng.
Phia pa tawm ot plak piang hoik yuh tix ka khaox si oe bhawng,
mai lang lad tix tawm hoek ka nyiex krao;
nyiex krao mawh rong pa giex kaux.
4 Ding khawm ra plak hoik kieh tang koe daux doh tix plak loe mu.
5 Pang siviex mai daux dohc hoik mawh pa lang krax gi.
Daux doh plak in loe mu mai si tieb daux doh plak an loe mu see paox tix heue.
6 Nawh baox yuh ka lawm le rong nyiex ting,
krax lang koe tix nga ra kongjhee,
krax viex koe kao loe kongjhee plak,
baox yuh grah koe ka plak praix greem nyiex mai kieh rong joh kaux heue.
7 Dee ngawm nyiex ting,
mai baox kok mawh nyiex dee prawng,
mawh dee prawng Saw-law-meung see cawng khankix.
Ka daux nyiex,
jauh piang tiex tom hoek ka plak piang,
hoik tawm tix ka khaox si oe bhawng khawm uik heue.
8 dBiang brawk plak khaix nyiex prawng an,
mawh nyiex it Saw-law-meung;
nyiex haw in hoik sum tix awm pak peung pa sum nyiex tang mu yuh nan.
Saw-law-meung kawx hoik sum moeng tix pa mawh kawn bun simiang Ai-cix ot ka nyiex haw pa awm an yuh nan nang heue.
9 Can nyiex haw pa sum in kix,
mai biang brawk,
jauh ka jaong tom hoek ka kaing nyiex,
hoik siao simaox pa ngawh yuh kah,
mai simaox in kix hoik mawh pa hawt tix teei ka diag,
saik lih khaing dee teei simaox,
pa hoik rid mhawm plak daux khawm plak praix.
10 Jaong hoik mawh yuh kaux ka simaox ting pa ngawh,
tix blah krax lang koe loe kongjhee plak,
tix blah koe lang pon kongjhee.
11 Ka piang hoik hawt tix sau simaox pa hoik saik hawt diag mai lat khaox si oe bhawng.
12 Mawh nyiex haw ang ah,
mawh biang brawk nyiex buan ang ah,
phan ang mawh nan,
grah nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex ang ah,
hoik mawh simaox pa saik tix mhawm loe tia,
pa si khap ka lat khaox si oe bhawng tix tia ka daux ding heue.
Kaing yuh Hu-lang
13 Simiang Saw-law-meung ploi pui hu kok pa kaux Hu-lang hoek khaing veng Thai-eux.
14 Nawh hoik mawh cang mai pa raug tawng pa ao min tix kaux.
Keeing nawh mawh pui Thai-eux,
mawh cang mai pa raug tawng nang,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik yum;
miex nawh mawh ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix.
Hu-lang mawh cang mai pa lhak lhiao pa kub rhawm keud pa ao min tix kaux;
nawh rieb lox pa tawx simiang Saw-law-meung,
mai mawh cao pa kuang kia ka piang pui pa yuh kaing tawng khawm uik heue.
Rong tawng ra gawng
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 3.15–17)
15 Hu-lang raug ka rong tawng ra gawng,
tix gawng krax lhaong koe daix kongjhee,
rong lawm le phuan kongjhee loe kongchuinge,
mai sum ot ka dee laig ka diang siviex nyiex buan.
16 Nawh kawx baox yuh ka toh kaing rong tawng ra mu,
tix mu krax lhaong koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing,
mai sau ot piang toh kaing rong tawng.
17 Ka piang toh kaing rong tawng,
hoik sau riang hing pa duing tix tau kah,
18 mai riang plix mak cang tawng ra tia ot ka heue.
19 Toh rong grah an krax lhaong koe tix kongjhee daix kongchuing,
greeh grang pak peung awm tai siyag,
20 mai sau ot ka piang riang hing an.
Koe ra yieh riang plix mak cang pa guah tix koe ra tia mai vung vhien ka toh rong an ku gawngf heue.
21 Hu-lang sum rong tawng in kiex ot ka diang siviex nyiex buan:
gawng pa ot plak seh kok mawh Ya-cingg;
gawng pa ot plak laong kok mawh Paw-a-seeh.
22 Greeh grang riang tai siyag pa ot ka piang toh rong sau ot ka piang toh rong tawng.
Yuh nin,
kaing pa yuh ka rong tawng tom hoik si dauh heue.
Dung rawm tawng
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 4.2–5)
23 Hu-lang raug ka dung rawm tawng pa lawm le tix mu,
krax raux koe ra kongjhee ra kongchuing,
been vieh moeng koe pon kongjhee pon kongchuing,
vung viang moeng koe kao loe kongjhee ra kongchuing.
24 Ka daog plak praix dung rawm tawng an mawh ra tia riang si ngien,
si ngien in kix mai dung rawm tawng hoik mawh pa raug khaing tawng tix mu khaing.
25 Dung rawm tawng deuh tix ot ka piang blawg moi tawng kao ra mu;
moi tawng veeh ngai tix plak praix,
kaing loe mu veeh tix ka plak lih singaix,
loe mu veeh tix ka plak klip singaix,
loe mu veeh ka plak seh,
loe mu veeh ka plak laong.
26 Pu dung rawm tawng koe aliah kongfeeng plak,
ka daog awm daog kawk nyaux yuh nan,
mai riang lawm tai siyag hoik pawd tix koe kah.
Ka daux dung rawm tawng pon uik sau rawm pon mheen kongsheng heue.
Jaong tawng
27 Hu-lang baox raug ka jhup jaong dang dee deuh pan tawng pa chix gah tix hu kao mu,
tix mu krax lang koe tix kongjhee daix kongchuing,
krax vieh koe tix kongjhee mai daix kongchuing,
krax lhaong koe tix kongjhee loe kongchuing.
28 Dee deuh pan tawng hoik yuh ka phia pa koe daux pak lang;
plak praix phia pa koe daux an hoik kieh koe ngam,
29 ka piang koe riang sivai sang si,
riang moi mhaing,
mai riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu khankix kah.
Ka piang ngam an,
hoik mawh riang sivai sang si mai riang moi mhaing pa koe ka toh plak piang mai plak greem,
mai breeh riang tai bhlien tix lih khaing.
30 Ka jhup jaong pan tawng ku mu koe pon mu jaong ling tawng,
gawng pa kieh yia hoik mawh tawng nang.
Ka greem jhup jaong koe jaong tawng pon mu hoik joh kaux,
ka piang jaong an,
breeh riang bhlien tix lih khaing.
31 Ka piang pan hoik kieh koe ngam pa lawm le tix mu,
jauh ka toh jhup jaong pan jhup tix haok plak piang tipon phuan kongfeeng,
jhup tix lih plak greem kao daix kongfeeng,
vung viang hoik pawd riang koe ka khawm uik.
32 Jaong ling jhup jaong krax lhaong koe tix gliah liah kongfeeng,
ka greem phia pa koe daux.
Gawng pa kieh yia mai to kaux jhup jaong hoik mawh pa raug lih mawh tix neh.
33 Pak peung jaong ling hoik awm jaong ling law yuh nan;
gawng pa kieh yia,
lat pa yia piang tiex,
gawng jung pa giex kaux,
mai daux pa kieh vid tix sigreung hoik mawh pa raug tix khawm uik.
34 Ka jhup jaong plak greem ku mu hoik mawh jaong pon mu pa joh kaux;
jaong in kix mai to kaux jhup jaong hoik mawh pa raug tix lih mawh tix neh khaing.
35 Ka piang toh jhup jaong ku mu,
krax lhaong daog pa hoik vung vhien tix koe tix nga ra kongfeeng.
Jaong jhup,
phia pa koe daux mai to kaux jhup jaong hoik mawh pa raug tix lih mawh.
36 Jaong mai phia pa koe daux hoik pawd riang sivai sang si,
riang khaox jhie yaong,
mai riang Ci-lu-paw ra mu ot kah,
ka si nax hoik baox breeh riang vung viang kah.
37 Dee deuh pan tawng hoik raug tix mawh nin.
Ku mu hoik kieh greeh grang awm paox,
been ting iak awm paox heue.
38 iHu-lang baox raug pan tawng son jhup jaong kao mu;
pan tawng ku mu been vieh moeng koe tix kongjhee daix kongsheng,
pon sau rawm ka daux daix yieh kongsheng.
39 Nawh yuh dee deuh pan tawng ot ka plak seh nyiex buan phuan mu,
an phuan mu deuh ot ka plak laong,
mai deuh dung rawm tawng ot ka plak lih singaix ka dee kawk nyiex buan heue.
Lai ca rang can kraung pa siao ka nyiex buan
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 4.11–5.1)
40-45 Hu-lang baox raug yuh aw,
jauk,
mai klong.
Nawh hoik hoik kaing pa caw nawh tix sang yuh ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex son simiang Saw-law-meung.
Kraung pa hoik raug nawh tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Rong tawng ra gawng
toh kaing rong riang klong ra mu pa sau ot ka rong tawng
riang maox ceng pa duing tix ot ka piang toh kaing rong.
Riang plix mak cang tawng pon yieh mu,
piang riang toh rong ku gawng an,
riang plix mak cang hoik vung vhien tix ot ka ra tia,
tix tia tix yieh mu
dee deuh pan tawng kao mu
pan tawng kao mu
dung rawm tawng
riang moi mhaing tawng kao mu ra pa joh dung rawm tawng kaux
aw,
jauk,
klong.
Kraung nyiex buan in kix pa hoik yuh Hu-lang son simiang Saw-law-meung khawm uik,
hoik mawh pa yuh ka tawng pa klaing mhawm sawng.
46 Kraung in kix khawm uik hoik hawt awm lox tawx simiang,
mai raug yuh ka bang dee yuh kaing,
ka si nax ngawd Yaw-tang,
Su-keu,
mai Sa-la-tang.
47 Simiang Saw-law-meung ang bax kieh pui ciang ka kraung tawng in kix;
mawh kheu kied nie tete,
mawh kheu nan,
been cien kraung in kix mai ang sax ku tix tawng ka heue.
48 jSaw-law-meung kawx kieh pui yuh kraung khri son nyiex buan:
koe khu khri,
pheen pa deuh khao mun,
49 kjawk kawk ngu kao mu pa deuh ka plak sivoe dee pa kied singax singeh (Phuan mu ot plak seh,
phuan mu ot plak laong),
tai,
ngu,
si kiep ngu,
50 pan,
si kiep kip tai ngu,
klong,
pan pa deuh nyawng ot piang,
pan pa sau nyaox daux,
jim siviex nyiex dee pa kied singax singeh,
mai siviex plak praix nyiex buan khankix.
Kraung in kix hoik raug tix ka khri khawm uik heue.
51 lSimiang Saw-law-meung hoik hoik kaing nyiex buan khawm uik,
nawh tom teei khri mau pa tan lu keeing tix Ta-wue ka Simiang Siyiex mai kraung khrai tang ceu khankix,
deuh ot ka nyiex krao nyiex buan heue.
