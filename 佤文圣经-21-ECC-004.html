4
Aux baox lam lia ka kaing pa ang khaw caw pa koe ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
Pui pa kham krax pa gut kheem yiiem grai lih rawm ngai,
ang koe pui pa teem coi kix.
Kix ang pon krax teem krax coi,
mawh kheu pui pa gut kheem ka kix mawh pui pa koe siawp sibhawm.
2 Aux kied ghoing ka pui pa hoik yum khankix,
kix kawn lheu nyawm ot nyawm koe khaing pui pa im ju khankix.
3 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa ang bax keh tix koe,
pa ang bax yaox ka kaing pa ang khaw caw pa koe ka piang haktiex khankix,
kawn lheu koe son khaing pui in kiex pa krai plak piang heue.
4 Aux baox yaox:
kawn pui sau riang tix yuh kaing,
sawg tix koe min koe ceu,
hoik mawh kheu kied ghoing ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix.
In kawx hoik mawh nang pa giex pa going mai pa ang koe ceu.
5 Koe pui krai ah nin:
“Pui pa daox aw ang tawng tix yuh kaing jhaw,
mai kieh to kaux tix yum ka cai rhawm tix heue.”
6 Viang mawh nan,
teei tix jhawp iak chix pon krax pa nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi,
lheu khaing giex naog ka taix tix khawm ra khawn,
in mawh pa taug mao mai pa ang koe ceu heue.
7 Aux baox yaox ka kaing pa giex pa going tix blah pa koe ka greem singaix.
8 Koe pui pa ot nong nong tix,
ang koe kawn simex,
ang koe pux aik,
viang mawh nan,
nawh yuh kaing taug mao leeing lang singaix,
ang sax naog rhawm ka can sin ngao pa hoik koe tix.
Nawh kied taug kied mao,
mai ang pon ih pon nyaux,
nawh sang yuh son pui mawx lie?
In hoik mawh nang pa giex pa going mai kaing pa ang mhawm heue.
9 Pui ra kaux ca kawn mhawn khaing pui tix kaux,
mawh kheu kaing pa rom pui ra kaux tix yuh nie blawn mhawm.
10 Phan pui tix kaux goh,
pui an tix kaux chix yaog nawh kaoh.
Phan mawh nong nong khaing ang koe paox pie,
phan nawh goh ang koe pui pa yaog kaoh,
mai nawh tag hoek krax som nai heue.
11 Pui ra kaux it tau yaox tix ra lai si u,
pui tix kaux it nong nong sang chix yaox tix si u yuh ka mawx lie?
12 Pui ra kaux rom riang tix mai pon tieb tie ka pui tix kaux pa hoek tix con,
phan mawh pui tix kaux mai ang pon tieb tie mhawm mhawm.
Maox loe nyaing pa brawm tix tau,
ang doh chix dut yie yie heue.
13-14 Pui tix kaux kaux viang hoik keh khaing daux nyiex ma pui pa hot pa sibhawm,
mai tee kawn pon tix mawh simiang,
viang mawh pa hoik kaoh khaing daux thawng kawx,
mai tee kawn chix pon tix mawh simiang pa ngawm piang dang ngeung.
Viang mawh nan,
phan hoik daox aw yam kuad ju tix,
mai ang cu tix nghiet ka lox pa khaw caw,
nawh tag ang lai lheu khaing pui hot pa nhum ju pa lhak lhiao tix kaux.
15 Aux doh keud ka can pui pa koe ka piang haktiex,
mai hoik tawng koe pui pa nhum ju tix kaux ka daux kix,
sang yaog tix giex kaing tang simiang prim.
16 Simiang tix kaux kaux tee rien tix giex kaing ka piang pui haoh pui hun;
viang mawh nan khaix hoik yum nawh,
ang koe pui pa doh keud ka kaing mhawm pa hoik yuh nawh.
In kawx hoik mawh nang pa giex pa going mai pa ang koe ceu heue.
