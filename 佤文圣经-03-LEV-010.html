Phi map pa yuh Na-ta mai Ya-pi-hux
10
Kawn simex Ya-loeng pa mawh Na-ta mai Ya-pi-hux tang viex pan ngu hoek tix kaux tix mu,
mai sau ngu ka daux,
gleeing ka nyawng bhawng,
mai tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
Viang mawh nan,
in mawh ngu gleeing pa bhawng pa yie yuh kawn pui khankix,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex ang bax tawm kix tan ngu gleeing pa bhawng an.
2 Dum mawx dum an,
Simiang Siyiex ploi ngu lih ka sivoe ngaix tix,
mai gleeing kix yum ka piang tiex.
3 Kheu nan,
Maw-si krai ka Ya-loeng:
“Simiang Siyiex hoik ah nin:
‘Pui pa krub ka aux tag caw tix yung yi ka pa singax singeh aux;
aux tag sang jhi min plak ngai tix ka pui cie tixa.’
Khaing ka daux kaing in,
maix tom tag tawng dee bleeh gum lox in.”
Viang mawh nan,
Ya-loeng yawg tix ang nye ang nyux heue.
4 Maw-si kok ka keeing iak Ya-loeng kawn simex Wu-shiie pa kaux Mi-sa-lix mai Yi-li-sha-fang hoek,
krai ka kiex ah nin:
“Grawng to kaux yum pui nyiex ma pex lih khaing ka daux nyiex buan pa singax singeh,
mai viex hu ka plak praix nyiex bang.”
5 Kix hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Maw-si,
giex ka sibex to kaux pui yum an,
grawng to kaux yum an lih plak praix nyiex bang heue.
6 Maw-si krai ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yi-tha-ma ah nin:
“Pex ang pon tix bhla hauk kaing tix,
mai baox ang pon tix riah ka kraung ceep tix,
son krax pa laux rhawm.
Phan ang mawh nan,
pex tag yum tete;
Simiang Siyiex kawx rien tix tawx krax pa song rhawm song rhi ka piang kawn pui.
Viang mawh nan,
pex paox ceu pui ex pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
chix tix yiiem grai mai khi rhawm son ngu pa hoik ploi Simiang Siyiex lih gleeing pui yum.
7 Pex tag ang pon tix lih hu khaing siviex nyiex gox buan man,
phan ang mawh nan,
pex tag yum tete,
mawh kheu bee pa singax singeh Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh pex singax.”
Kheu nan,
kix hawt yuh awm gum lox pa hoik krai Maw-si heue.
Been cao giex kaing li
8 Simiang Siyiex krai ka Ya-loeng ah nin:
9 “Maix khawm kawn simex maix khankix,
khaix hoik nyaux ka plai num mai plai khien,
ang pon tix laig ka daux nyiex gox buan man aux,
phan ang mawh nan,
pex tag yum tete.
Doeh ceu pui pex tag caw tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng in leeing ju leeing phan.
10 Pex tag caw tix chix guah ka ceu pa singax singeh,
mai ceu pa singax singeh mai pa ang singax singeh khaing ka daux been.
11 Pex caw tix sidiex gau pui Yi-seu-liex ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik krai aux ka Maw-si khawm uik heue.”
12 bMaw-si krai ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh pa kawn deuk ra kaux pa mawh Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yi-tha-ma ah nin:
“Viex pex teei Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
pa deuk khaing Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai yuh mawh khao mun pa ang sau su saix,
mai ih ka praok khu dee tan,
mawh kheu khawng tan ceu in hoik mawh pa kied singax singeh.
13 Pex tag ih ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh.
In hoik mawh khawng tan pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
hoik mawh nyum son cie pex mai kawn praix pex.
In hoik mawh gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka aux.
14 cPex mai pui nyiex pui ma pex,
tag chix tix ih ka khawng phak pa hoik blawk tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh nex naok mai khawn ba caong plak kreue.
Pex tag chix tix ih nex ceu in ka dee pa yuh pex kaing pa singax singeh.
In hoik mawh pa guah tix tawx pex mai kawn praix pex ih,
khaing Krax-tan Pa-nhim pa tan pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
15 Yam gleeing kix tix tan ka khlawp bee ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang viex khawm khawn ba caong plak kreue mai nex naok hoek.
Hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex,
nyum in tag sang mawh cie pex mai kawn praix pex leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
16 Maw-si si kaw tix chawk ka pex mhaing son Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap an,
nawh yaox ka hoik gleeing.
Nawh song rhawm ka Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yi-tha-ma,
ah ka kiex ah nin:
17 d“Yuh ka mawx pax lai ang ih Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh lie?
An hoik mawh pa kied singax singeh.
Simiang Siyiex tawx ka pax,
mawh son sang teei tix tik ka phi map cub kawn pui.
18 Kheu ang pex nyang bax viex nham khawng brie krax tan laig ka daux nyiex pa singax singeh,
pex caw tix hawt ka gum lox tawm aux,
mai ih nex khawng brie tan in ka dee an heue.”
19 Ya-loeng deeh ah nin:
“Phan aux ih ka khawng tan Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap in singaix,
chix sang yuh ka mawx lie?
Simiang Siyiex kawn sang chix rieb ka lie?
Daux in singaix,
kawn pui tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
viang mawh nan kawx,
aux ca kawn si pup ka krax laux krax leeing ceu in.”
20 Khaix hoik mhong Maw-si ka lox in,
nawh naog rhawm ka heue.
