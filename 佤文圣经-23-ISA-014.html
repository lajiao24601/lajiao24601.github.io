Deeh hoek khaing vuk vied vung viang tix
14
Simiang Siyiex tag sang baox tawx krax muh ka pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex,
laug kix mawh cie to kaux tix,
mai kieh kix paung ot ka daux haktiex cie to kaux tix;
koe pui tang ceu tom nie tag sang ot tau maix kix,
mai kleuk tix tau mai kix.
2 Koe meung pui tom nie hoek tix teem coi ka kix,
mai viex kix deeh hoek ka haktiex pa hoik tawx Siyiex,
kheu nan tag sang kieh kix mawh siliex,
mai pa yuh kaing jhaw son kix.
Pui pa giex laung pui Yi-seu-liex sivoe an,
yam in hoik tieh tix lih mawh siliex cawng pui Yi-seu-liex;
pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang giex kaing ka piang pui pa yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka kix sivoe an heue.
Simiang Pa-pi-loeng ot daux meung yum
3 Simiang Siyiex tag sang teei tix tik ka krax laux rhawm mai krax kha rhawm pa koe ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex;
ang lai baox koe pui pa khin kix yuh kaing ceu pa nu.
4 Khaix hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex kaing son kix,
kix tag sang si briem ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng ah nin:
“Simiang pa caox hoik goh heue!
Nawh ang lai baox pon gut kheem ka pui heue!
5 Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh sibrawm simiang pui caox naox tix.
6 Pui in kix tawng ngie tix chix kha ka pui hun,
gun kheem ka meung pa pon kuang kix ang jud ang jai.
7 Haktiex in hoik pon pa yien pa yawg mai pa nhim pa tiiem yam in;
pui ku kaux ku pui hoik gaux rhawm rhax lai.
8 Khaox gix mai khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noeng hoik gaux rhawm kheu laux leeing Vawng,
mawh kheu nawh hoik laux,
mai ang lai baox koe pui pa hoek kid ka khaox an kix heue.
9 “Meung yum rien khre tix sang hoek rieb ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
Khuan pui an kix pa hoik sax ting min tix vud tix cu hoik lih tix chuh khawm uik;
khuan pui an kix pa hoik sax mawh simiang ka piang haktiex hoik lih hoek khaing dang ngeung.
10 Kix ah nin ka nawh:
‘Yam in maix hoik iak riang iak rao awm yix,
mai hoik lih tix mawh pa ot ka daux yix tix kaux heue!
11 Sivoe an pui hoik tauh ting rhax lai yaog yaw grawng pa mhawm kaing yuh maix,
yam in maix kawx hoik hoek ka daux meung yum heue!
Ka piang ku dee it maix hoik tawm tix naog ka viag vua;
prux it maix hoik mawh graik heue.’
12 a“Simiang Pa-pi-loeng eue,
maix pa mawh simuing pa riang,
maix hoik cawd hoek khaing rao ma.
Maix hoik pon kuang ka meung ka piang haktiex,
yam in pui si blat maix piang tiex.
13 bMaix hoik ca keud tix sang haok ka rao ma,
son sang deuh dang ngeung tix ot ka piang simuing pa haoh hun cie Siyiex;
maix hoik keud tix sang ot ka piang gawng dee die haktiex plak laong,
ka dee khrawm siyiex paox tix khawm uik.
14 Maix hoik krai,
maix tag sang haok ka piang pai awm,
ka dee sang lah cha cao pa ting siawp sibrawm paox tix dee an.
15 Mawx sang tawng,
maix hoik goh laig ka daux meung yum,
mai cawd laig daux pa kied raux heue.
16 “Pui yum tag sang blin ngai tix jhak ka maix;
kix chawk ah nin:
‘In hoik mawh pui pa kieh haktiex yung yang,
kieh meung ka piang haktiex si yaung si kua lie?
17 In hoik mawh pui pa kieh veng tom nie laux leeing,
kieh haktiex tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag lie?
In hoik mawh pui pa ang cu tix ploi ka pui pa giex laung,
pa ang cu tix kieh kix deeh ing ka nyiex tix lie?’
18 Simiang ka piang haktiex khawm uik hoik it ka daux si meeig nyiex haw simiang kix.
19 Viang mawh nan,
maix ang koe si meeig,
pui hoik tik maix daux tiag daux tu;
to kaux yum maix pui hoik pum si-ang pui naing pa yum ot ka piang,
hoik cawd ka daux tiex tau mai si-ang pui yum an kix,
pui hoik dig ka caong tix.
20 Maix ang awm simiang tang kaux,
yam yum kix koe pui si paung ka kix;
mawh kheu hoik yuh maix meung cie to kaux tix laux leeing,
hoik pauh ka pui cie to kaux tix.
Cub nyiex ma maix pa yuh phi map in tag sang yuh laux leeing,
tix kaux kawx mai ang lai deuh heue.
21 “Pex rien khre tix hu pauh ka kawn praix nawh heue!
In hoik mawh phi map pa deuh tax du kix;
ka daux kix ang lai baox koe pui pa pon giex kaing ka haktiex in,
mai ang lai pon sum veng ka daux haktiex ih heue.”
Siyiex sang yuh Pa-pi-loeng laux leeing
22 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang puing ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai yuh kieh laux leeing heue.
Aux tag ang lai deuh rib riem koe dee an;
ang lai kieh kawn nyawm koe,
ang lai kieh doeh ceu cad kix koe.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
23 Aux tag sang kieh Pa-pi-loeng tieh tix lih mawh daux dung bing,
lih mawh mheem pang plawh.
Aux tag sang teei bih mai bih tix tik ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai pih tix tik khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Siyiex sang yuh Ya-sux laux leeing
24 cSimiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing tiex tu ah nin:
“Krax keud krax sawn aux tag sang lih tix mawh tete;
kaing pa sang yuh aux,
mai aux tag sang yuh lih tete.
25 Aux tag sang yuh pui Ya-sux laux leeing ka piang haktiex cie tix,
mai dig kix ka piang gawng cie tix.
Aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix pon krax pa luat luan khaing daux pa giex gang Ya-sux,
mai pluat ploi kix bheeik luan khaing beeh glawm pa cien.
26 In hoik mawh krax keud aux son ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
aux tag sang tawx taix tix lauh meung ka piang haktiex heue.”
27 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik but rhawm tix yuh yuh nin;
nawh hoik tawx taix tix ciyang;
ang lai koe pui pa pon tieb tie ka heue.
Siyiex sang yuh Fe-li-see laux leeing
28 dYam yum simiang Ya-ha-see ka daux num an,
Siyiex krai pao ka gum lox in plak greem heue.
29 ePui Fe-li-see eue,
khaox nguh pa lauh ka maix hoik pot,
viang mawh nan,
maix paw vi gaux rhawm.
Si uing tix mu hoik yum,
mai an tang mu pa blawn ting jai kawn krox jun ka plak khaix;
tawm si uing pa koe jai an hoik guah lih mawh si yawng pa koe preeik heue!
30 Simiang Siyiex tag sang mawh cao pa liang ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
mai kieh pui pa ang koe cao teem coi yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh tag sang kieh pui Fe-li-see yaox krax pa cai rhawm;
pui pa kawn deuk khankix kawx tag sang nyao yum heue.
31 Veng Fe-li-see eue,
pex yiiem riag yiiem yo heue!
Pui Fe-li-see eue,
pex khaw tix si yaung heue!
Taox ngu pa ting chu kaoh hoek khaing plak laong;
an hoik mawh cub pui koeng pa kied kua son naing heue.
32 Ex sang pawk lox kawn cau pa ploi pui Fe-li-see hoek khiox khaw tix taoh kix yuh ka mawx lie?
Ex tag sang krai ka kix,
mai krai hoik sum Simiang Siyiex ka Shi-ang;
pui cie nawh pa hoik kha rhawm rhi tix tag sang pon krax pa kia ngien dee an heue.
