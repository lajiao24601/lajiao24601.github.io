Siyiex sang kia ngien ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
31
Pui pa hu sawg krax taoh teem ka Ai-cix tag sang yaox krax laux krax leeing heue!
Kix hoik een rhawm tix ka pui naing Ai-cix pa haoh hun,
pa mawh brung naing,
ling law naing,
mai pui naing pa hu ka caong tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang een rhawm tix ka Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh,
mai ang khiox khaw krax teem krax coi Simiang Siyiex.
2 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex hoik koe ping nyia cuyi pa blawn ting,
mai nawh tag sang ciyang ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig,
mai pui pa teem ka kix.
Nawh hoik but rhawm tix sang ploi krax laux krax leeing lih,
mai ang doh chix tieh loh.
3 Pui Ai-cix ang mawh Siyiex;
kix hoik mawh ngie kawn pui khaing.
Brung kix kawx ang mawh brung Siyiex.
Phan Simiang Siyiex tawx taix tix,
meung pa ting riang kawx tag sang laux,
mai meung pa iak riang pa hoik joh jhan nawh kawx tag sang laux leeing;
meung in kiex khawm ra tag sang rom tix laux leeing tau heue.
4 Simiang Siyiex hoik krai ah nin ka aux:
“Jiet awm sivai sang si tix mu,
phan ang mawh nan kawn sivai sang si tix mu pa pon giex ka to tiag tix mu,
viang cao liang yung hoik kok yo yuh ka mawx kawx,
to tiag an ang lhat ang graung.
Awm an,
aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing tag sang kia ngien ka gawng Shi-ang;
mai ang koe pui pa pon tieb tie tix kaux kawx.
5 Hoik awm sim pa kia ngien ka kawn tix pa koe ka daux mheen,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing,
tag sang kia ngien ka Yie-lu-sa-leung yuh nan nang,
mai priex pau kah,
taoh teem kah,
ang kieh nawh yaox krax laux heue.”
6 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex hoik sax tieh vheen ka aux.
Yam in deeh hoek heue!
7 Bawg yam hoik hoek,
pex khaw tix tik ka riang rao krub pui pa caox pa caig an kix,
pa hoik teei pex taix tix raug lih khaing mau khri.
8 Ya-sux tag sang laux leeing ka daux naing,
viang mawh nan,
in ang mawh pa kaoh khaing riang rao kawn pui.
Pui Ya-sux tag sang tang yaog tix to khaing bang naing;
pui tag sang giex laung pui pa nhum ju kix yuh siliex cawng.
9 Vawng kix tag sang yaoh yee yaog tix to;
simiang cao bung kaing naing khankix si yaung si kua mai to deeh tix,
pie man chix naing kawx kix hoik tik kah.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Nawh tag sang yuh glah ngu koe ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai gleeing pui koeng ka Shi-ang heue.
