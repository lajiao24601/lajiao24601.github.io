Krax ot krax koe pa yuh kawn pui ka Siyiex
12
Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik pux o khankix eue,
kheu hoik caox rhawm Siyiex yuh nin ka ex,
aux nhie pex tan to kaux tix mawh krax tan lu pa im ju ka Siyiex,
yuh kaing jhaw nawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
yuh krax pa khaw caw ka rhawm rhi nawh.
In mawh krax krub pa caw pex tix tan lu tete.
2 Paw vi kieh haktiex in grai yuh pex,
tag caw tix kieh Siyiex tieh lox ka pex,
loh ka rhawm rhi krax keud krax sawn pex,
son sang sidawng mawh alo nawh ceu pa tix,
ceu pa tix mawh pa mhawm pa mhiem,
ceu pa tix mawh pa grum naog,
mai ceu pa khaw rhawm Siyiex heue.
3 Kheu buan son pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka aux,
aux tag sang krai ka pex ku kaux ah nin:
paw vi een to kaux tix mawh pui pa luan lhak,
tag caw tieh tix koe rhawm awn rhawm tiiem,
kaux mawx kaux an tang tiag tix yaox ka krax yum rhawm pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka ex.
4 aTo kaux tix mu hoik mawh pa yuh lih ka pak peung tom nie,
pak peung ku mu hoik tang koe dee siao.
5 Awm an,
viang ex hoik mawh pui tom nie,
ex lih mawh tix mu khaing ka daux Khrit,
ra lai si doe paox tix,
mai lih pak peung pa ang awm paox tix ka to kaux.
6 bMawh kheu nan,
ex tag sang hawt awm buan son pa tawx Siyiex ka ex,
mai siao ka khawng phak buan son pa ang awm paox tix,
yuh kaing pa khaw tix yuh.
Phan khawng phak buan son pa tawx ka ex mawh pa krai ka gum lox buan,
caw hawt tix sidiex awm pa koe krax yum rhawm tix yuh nan.
7 Phan mawh pa khre ka pui,
tag caw tix khre ka pui,
phan mawh pa sidiex gau,
tag caw tix sidiex gau.
8 Phan mawh pa jheeh nyaw,
tag caw tix jheeh nyaw;
phan mawh pa teei tix tawx ka pui;
tag caw tix si pieh rhawm;
phan mawh simiang,
tag caw tix rien rhawm;
phan mawh pa caox rhawm ka pui,
tag caw tix koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue.
9 Muh pui tag caw tix kieh mawh khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
Tag caw tix tik pa caox giex pa mhawm.
10 Tag caw muh paox tix awm pux aik pux o yuh nan;
tag caw tix ra lai nab yam yung yi ka paox tix.
11 Paw vi nieh,
tag caw rien rhawm tix yuh kaing,
tag gaux rhawm ka daux pa yuh kaing jhaw Siyiex.
12 Tag caw tix gaux rhawm ka daux krax keud pot jun pot,
tag caw tix gun yeung ka daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
rawg buan khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
13 Tag caw tix kieh pux aik pa hot yaox tix ih nyaux ka pa koe maix;
tag caw rieb tix khre ka nyo pui tang ceu heue.
14 cTag caw tix nawb buan ka piang pui pa tuk tiak ka maix;
mawh tete,
tag caw tix nawb buan kah,
paw vi jhax lawg.
15 dTag caw tix gaux rhawm tau mai pui pa gaux rhawm,
yiiem grai tau mai pui pa yiiem grai.
16 ePui mawx ang ah,
tag caw ra lai kia ngien ka paox tix.
Paw vi ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
tag caw tieh tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi;
paw vi een to kaux tix lhak lhiao heue.
17 Paw vi pawk pa caox son pa caox;
kaing ceu pa een pui mawh pa mhawm,
tag caw sau riang tix yuh.
18 Maix tag caw sau riang tix,
kieh tix khaw rhawm paox ka pui hun.
19 fPaox grawm khankix,
ang pon tix pawk bix son to kaux tix,
kieh krax pa song rhawm Siyiex teem maix prawng rauh see,
mawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri hoik krai ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
krax pa prawng rauh see mawh cie aux;
aux tag sang vied ka heue.”
20 gPhuk lai sigang khri baox ah nin:
“Phan pui koeng ka maix cai rhawm,
tag caw tix tawx nawh ih,
phan simie tix nyaux rawm,
tag caw tix tawx nawh nyaux;
phan maix yuh nin,
tag sang kieh nawh nie kaig tua heue.”
21 Mawh kheu nan,
paw vi kieh krax pa caox pon pex ka pex,
tag caw tix kieh pa mhawm pex pa caox heue.
