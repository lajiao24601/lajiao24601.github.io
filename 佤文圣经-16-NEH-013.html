Kieh pui tang ceu tang cad tang ot
13
aKa daux singaix an,
koe pui phat pui hun mhong lai lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si;
kix phat lox pa ah nin tix baux:
ang doh kieh pui Ya-meung mai pui Maw-ya laig daux bang kum cie Siyiex.
2 bMawh kheu yam to pui Yi-seu-liex lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix daux krax,
pui Ya-meung mai pui Maw-ya lao sang tawx pui Yi-seu-liex ih priex preem nyaux rawm,
khaix an kawn teei tix siao Pa-lang hoek jhax lawg kix;
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex ex kieh krax pa jhax lawg tieh tix mawh krax nawb buan.
3 Pui Yi-seu-liex mhong lox tat jhawm prawng in,
kix tom kieh tix tang ot khaing cub pui tang ceu khawm uik heue.
Krax pa tieh loh Ni-si-mi
4 Yi-li-ya-seu cao giex kaing li pa kuang kia krao nyiex buan hoik dawg yuh paox grawm tau mai To-pi-ya.
5 Nawh tawx To-pi-ya siao nyiex ting tix lhang.
Nyiex an hoik mawh nyiex pa deuh pui nghox koi mai sidah bhawng son krax tan,
kraung pa siao daux nyiex buan,
khawng tan pa tawx ka cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan,
pa tan lu kao son tix son ka pui pa pau nyiex buan (pa mawh nghox koi,
plai,
bee sime pa pon kao son tix son).
6 Yam lih kaing in tix mawh,
aux ang ot ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung;
mawh kheu Ya-ta-shiie-sic mawh simiang Pa-pi-loeng ka daux tix ngoe ra num,
aux ing krai ka nawh.
Ang koe miex glang tix,
aux pon lox cu simiang
7 mai deeh ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Aux tom taug tawng Yi-li-ya-seu tawx To-pi-ya siao nyiex ting tix lhang khaing daux nyiex buan hoik mawh kaing pa ang mhawm.
8 Aux kied song rhawm song rhi,
mai teei tix tik can kraung khrai To-pi-ya plak praix.
9 Aux tawx lox kieh pui yuh nyiex an singax singeh,
mai deeh deuh kraung khrai nyiex buan,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai sidah bhawng khankix ka dee tan heue.
10 dAux baox mhong pui pa kied chix rhax lai mai pui Li-we tang kaux khankix hoik hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
ing ka nyiex yaong haktiex ot kix,
mawh kheu pui hun ang bax tawx priex preem kub son sang im ju kix.
11 Kheu kieh simiang khankix nyiex buan riem,
aux tom ne raw ka kix.
Aux kok pui Li-we mai pui pa kied chix rhax lai deeh ing ka nyiex buan,
kieh kix deeh cox yuh kaing pa khraox.
12 eMawh kheu nan,
can pui Yi-seu-liex jauh tix teei nghox koi,
plai,
bee sime kao son tix son viex hoek nyiex krao nyiex buan.
13 Aux ploi pui tix blah mawh cao pa kuang kia nyiex krao;
kix mawh:
cao giex kaing li See-li-mi-ya,
Sa-tu pui pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai,
Pi-ta-ya pui Li-we.
Pui pa teem coi kix Ha-nang kawn simex Sa-kheux,
kawn saux Ma-tha-ni.
Aux yum rhawm grawng pui in kix;
kix koe rhawm pa mawh pa meh tix guah kraung khrai tawx ka paox pa yuh kaing tau mai kix heue.
14 Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka kaing pa yuh aux son nyiex buan maix mai kaing pa krub ka maix heue.
15 fSingaix yam an,
aux ot ka daux pui Yo-tax mai yaox pui krid plix siiet ka daux singaix tuah,
mai baox tang nghox koi,
plai,
plix siiet,
plix ruh,
mai kraung tang ceu ot ka piang blawg la,
viex hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Aux ne raw kix ang pon tix yuh lai gee ka daux singaix tuah.
16 Koe pui pa hoek khaing veng Thai-eux tix blah paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung;
kix viex kax mai kraung khrai ku ceu hoek daux veng,
mai coh kraung in kix ka pui ex daux singaix tuah.
17 Aux ne raw ka simiang pui Yo-thaix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai pon yuh kaing pa caox pa caig in lud ka daux singaix tuah lie?
18 In ang hoik mawh jao pa ciyang Siyiex tax du pex,
yuh veng in laux leeing lie?
Yam in pex lud ka daux singaix tuah,
mai kieh krax pa song rhawm Siyiex nied tix lih hoek piang pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
19 Mawh kheu nan aux tawx lox,
yam jauh singaix tuah tix hoek ku bawg,
pa mawh yam jauh praix tix boi faik,g
mai nyeep ka siviex veng,
tom hoek luan daux singaix tuah mai taug paoh kah.
Aux ploi siliex ra loe kaux pau ka diang siviex veng,
mai ang cu pui viex kraung khrai laig daux veng singaix tuah,
20 Koe tix ra bawg,
ka daux bo singaix phuan,
pui yuh lai gee an kix paung ot ka plak praix veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
21 Aux ne raw ka kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai kaog ot plak praix krox praix riang lie?
Phan pex ca kawn baox yuh nin,
aux tag sang yuh pex laux heue.”
Jauh yam an,
kix ang baox lai hoek daux veng ka daux singaix tuah.
22 Aux tawx lox nhie pui Li-we kieh to kaux tix singax singeh,
khaix an hu pau ka siviex veng,
son sang kieh pui hun priex tix hawt singaix tuah,
mai kieh mawh singaix pa singax singeh heue.
Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka aux son kaing in liak,
khaix an mai caox rhawm ka aux kheu krax muh pa ting maix heue.
23 hKa daux singaix yam an,
aux kawx hoik yaox pui Yo-thaix tom nie teei tix moeng bun Ya-seu-thu,
Ya-meung,
Maw-ya.
24 Ka daux kawn praix kix tix blak ah lox pui Ya-seu-thu,
mai lox pui tang ceu,
mai ang chix si kah ka lox cao ex.
25 Aux tom ne raw ka kix,
jhax lawg ka kix,
lauh kix,
reeig hauk kaing kix.
Aux nhie kix tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex,
ang lai cu to kaux kix mai kawn simex kawn bun kix baox mex moeng pui tang ceu tang cad.
26 iAux ah nin ka kix:
“Hoik mawh bun pui tang ceu tang cad pa kieh simiang Saw-law-meung yuh phi map.
Viang simiang Saw-law-meung hoik ting lhaong khaing simiang meung tang meung,
Siyiex hoik muh ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh kuang ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex,
nawh kawx kawn yuh ka phi map ceu in.
27 Khex sang gau ex tix hawt kaing pa ang mhawm pa yuh pex,
kieh kawn praix tix mex moeng pui tang ceu mai lieh lheu gum lox Siyiex lie?”
28 jYie-heu-yie-tax mawh kawn simex Yi-li-ya-seu cao giex kaing li pa ting.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh koe kawn simex tix kaux teei tix moeng kawn bun Chang-pa-la pui Heu-loeng,
mawh kheu nan,
aux nhie Yie-heu-yie-tax lih hu khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
29 Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka pui in kix,
grawng pa hoik kieh kix kaing yuh cao giex kaing li,
mai sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh maix tau mai cao giex kaing li,
khawm pui Li-we seeing brai heue.
30 Mawh kheu nan,
aux kieh pui hun singax singeh,
mai hoik kieh kix lih hu singai khaing kraung cie pui tang ceu tang cad khawm uik.
Aux yuh been son cao giex kaing li mai son pui Li-we,
mai hoik kieh pui tang sidawng ka kaing pa yuh tix ku kaux.
31 Aux kawx hoik rien khre mhawm mhawm,
mai nhie pui hun hawt bawg yam viex khix pa sang siao son krax tan,
khawm nghox koi mai plix khaox plix ox liang tix pon hoek heue.
Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix hawt awm alo pa mhawm maix,
mai doh keud ka can kaing pa yuh aux khawm uik heue.
