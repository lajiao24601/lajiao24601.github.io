Lai Fe-li-meung
1
Aux mawh Pao-law;
aux lih tix mawh pui laung kheu Ye-sux Khrit.
Aux mai Thi-maw-thaix pux aik aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka maix,
pa mawh Fe-li-meung paox muh pa yuh kaing tau mai ex,
2 amai ceu cao pa khrawm tix yuh krax krub ka daux nyiex ma maix,
tawx nang Ya-fe-yax pux o khawm Ya-ci-pux pa yuh ex pui naing tau.
3 Kieh keeing ex Siyiex mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son mai pa nhim ka pex heue!
Krax muh krax rhawm Fe-li-meung mai krax yum rhawm nawh
4 Pux aik Fe-li-meung eue,
yam rawg aux buan ku bawg aux hoik si muad ka maix,
hoik yaog yawg ka Siyiex aux jheeng jheeng son maix;
5 mawh kheu hoik mhong aux koe krax muh krax rhawm maix ka kawn Khrit khawm uik,
mai krax yum rhawm pa koe maix ka Simiang Ye-sux.
6 Aux khiox khaw Siyiex kieh ex pon krax muh ka daux krax yum rhawm,
mai nie pon tawng sidawng ka grawng ceu pa mhawm pa kleuk ex tix tau mai Khrit.
7 Pux aik pa muh aux eue,
krax muh krax rhawm maix hoik kieh aux koe krax gaux rhawm pa ting mai krax pa joh jhan;
can kawn Khrit khawm uik kawx hoik pon krax gaux rhawm kheu maix heue.
Khiox khaw son A-ni-si-mo
8 Kheu hoik mawh aux pux aik maix ka daux Khrit,
aux hoik dawg chix tix viex rhawm ting tawx lox nhie maix yuh kaing pa caw maix tix sang yuh;
9 viang mawh nan,
krax muh krax rhawm dhiet aux khiox khaw ka maix.
Viang aux pa mawh Pao-law,
mawh kawn cau pa ting cie Khrit,
yam in deeh lih tix mawh pui laung kheu nawhb,
mai kawn cu tix yuh nin.
10 cPa khiox khaw aux ka maix mawh son A-ni-si-mo.
Nawh mawh kawn simex aux ka daux Khrit;
yam ot aux ka daux thawng,
aux mawh keeing nawh ka daux khuan.
11 eKa daux singaix pa hoik luan,
nawh ang koe ceu ka maix,
yam in nawh koe ceud ka maix mai ka aux khawm uik heue.
12 Yam in aux tawx nawh ing ka maix dee an,
mai hoik awm tawx aux rhawm rhi tix yuh nan.
13 Aux hoik dawg keud tix sang kieh nawh ot tau mai tix,
son sang kieh nawh mawh pui pa khre aux son maix yam ot aux daux thawng son gum lox buan;
14 viang mawh nan,
aux simie in kix khawm uik mawh pa kaoh khaing krax gaux rhawm maix,
mai ang mawh ka daux pa khin.
Phan ang mawh khaing lox maix aux ang rhawm yuh heue.
15 A-ni-si-mo ran lih hu khaing maix tix vud,
tee mawh nang son sang kieh maix giex nawh ot leeing ju.
16 Yam in nawh ang lai baox mawh siliex cawng,
mai hoik singai hu khaing siliex cawng;
kheu hoik mawh nawh pux aik pa muh ka daux Khrit.
Phan mawh ka son aux,
nawh mawh nan tete,
ka son maix nie mawh yuh nan heue!
Kheu hoik mawh pex simiang mai siliex si nax paox tix,
mai baox mawh pux aik ka daux Simiang Ye-sux heue.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
phan maix kieh aux mawh paox grawm pa ting tix,
maix tag caw tix rieb nawh ing,
awm hoik rieb ka aux yuh nan.
18 Phan nawh yuh kaing ceu pa ang khaw ka maix,
mai lud loe ka maix tix ceu ceu,
chix tix deuh ot piang kaing aux.
19 Aux tiiem dee in ah nin:
aux pa mawh Pao-law cu tix vied.
(Kheu nan,
aux ang siao krai ka maix,
pa ceuk maix ka aux ang hoik mawh ju to kaux maix lie!)
20 Mawh kheu nan,
pux aik eue,
kieh aux khiox khaw maix ka kaing in son Simiang Ye-sux;
son sang pon teei tix tik ka beeh glawm pa koe piang to kaux aux heue!
Ex hoik mawh pux aik ka daux Khrit khawm uik heue.
21 Aux hoik yum rhawm grawng sang nghiet maix ka aux,
aux tom tiiem ka lox lai in.
Tete te lai aux hoik tawng,
ceu pa hoik yuh maix kawn lheu khaing ceu pa khiox khaw aux.
22 Yam an,
rien khre lawg nyiex tix lawg son aux;
aux simie Siyiex chix nghiet ka lox rawg buan pui ku kaux,
kieh aux pon hoek ka pex dee an heue.
Lox pa nghoik tix chawk mhawm ot
23 fYi-pa-fu pa ot daux thawng tau mai aux son Ye-sux Khrit chawk maix mhawm ot.
24 gMa-kheu,
Ya-li-ta-ku,
Ti-ma,
Lu-cia pa yuh kaing tau mai aux hoik chawk maix mhawm ot khawm uik heue.
25 hKieh Simiang Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son ka pex heue!
