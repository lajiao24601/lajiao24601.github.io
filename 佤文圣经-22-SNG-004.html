[Bun pa sang koe mex]
4
Paox muh aux,
maix kied sidaing mhawm grang tete!
Tawm ngai maix awm tawm ngai si kaux,
ka plak khaix man tie hoik jhan ka pa riang krax muh.
Hauk kaing maix hoik awm cub phung pex yuh nan,
jang luh tix hoek khaing gawng Ci-liex.
2 Rhang maix hoik awm hauk pa taug khraox kip tix yuh nan,
hoik paing awm hauk yung pa taug si ghrah singax yuh nan,
dia tix tau hawt paox,
tix plah kawx ang cud heue.
3 Siyieb jheeh maix hoik awm maox keei pa raoh rawt tix nyaing;
yam ang maix jheeh tix krai lox kied sidaing nyawm tix jhak.
Si bax maix khawm ra plak ka plak khaix man tie hoik mhawm raoh,
hoik awm plix mak cang pa guah tix max koe ra plak yuh nan.
4 Ngawg maix hoik awm nyiex gawng mu lhaong Ta-wue,
pa lawm le tix rauh kaux cong,
pa hoik vhak maox soi tix ka awm dieb tie naok pui kua kaing rheng.
5 Teeh maix khawm ra plak awm kiah tix brawm,
awm kawn cak brawm pa sawm rib ka daux khaox tai siyag yuh nan.
6 Aux sang hu ka gawng Muran,
hoek ka piang gawng iak sidah bhawng Law-ban,
krox jun ka bhaung pa phlu yam pon ngawb,
pa faik hoik grai hu si nyek si nyek.
7 Paox muh aux,
maix kied sidaing mhawm grang tete!
Maix kied sidaing grum mhawm tete!
 
8 Moeng pa muh aux,
viex maix hu khaing gawng Li-pa-noeng tau mai aux;
lih hu khaing Li-pa-noeng tau mai aux.
Viex maix lih khaing piang gawng Ya-ma-nax,
khaing piang gawng See-ni-eu mai piang gawng He-meung,
dee an hoik mawh dee mox sivai sang si mai sivai kaoh ku tix ot.
9 Pui pa muh aux,
moeng pa muh aux,
tawm ngai maix pa jhak,
maox soi maix pa chuh,
mai hoik teei khuan ngao aux hu.
10 Pui pa muh aux,
moeng pa muh aux,
krax muh maix kied sidaing tie tete!
Krax muh maix kawn lheu tie khaing plai pa tie;
taox bhawng pa si oe maix kawn lheu bhawng khaing sidah bhawng ku ceu.
11 Moeng pa muh aux,
siyieb jheeh maix hoik tie awm rawm hia yuh nan;
rawm hia mai rawm teeh hoik koe ka dak maix.
Kraung ceep maix jiet nyawm bhawng awm pa bhawng Li-pa-noeng yuh nan heue.
 
12 Pui pa muh aux,
moeng pa muh aux,
maix hoik mawh si peem tai pa nyip tix,
guh ding pa hoik sivie,
rawm keet pa hoik tawb.
13 Khaox plix hoik raug ka daux si peem,
hoik koe pa mawh si peem khaox plix mak cang,
plix khaox pa nyawm tix ih;
tai Feung-sing mai khaox Na-tax,
14 tai Fang-hong,
chang phu,
ro kui,
khaox sidah bhawng Law-ban ku ceu,
Muran,
Acaw mai sidah bhawng pa kied ngawh khawm uik.
15 Maix hoik mawh rawm keet ka daux si peem,
rawm keet pa ang chix hit,
rawm klawng iak pa buing tix lih khaing gawng Li-pa-noeng heue.
[Simie pa sang koe moeng]
16 Bhaung ka plak laong eue,
haing heue.
Bhaung ka plak seh eue,
phlu hu ka plak si peem tai cie aux,
kieh bhawm lih naog ka pa si oe bhawng.
Kieh pui pa muh aux laig ka daux si peem cie nawh,
ih ka plix khaox pa blawn nyawm ka daux si peem heue.
