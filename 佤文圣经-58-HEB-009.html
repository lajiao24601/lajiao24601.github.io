Krax krub ka rao ma mai ka haktiex
9
Sijax mukheu pa jauh tix koe been krax krub pa tat tix deuh mai ka nyiex dee krub pa yuh kawn pui.
2 aNyiex gox buan man pa yuh tix lih an,
plak praix kok mawh nyiex pa singax singeh,
plak daux jawk kawk ngu,
pheen,
mai khao mun koe kah.
3 bNyiex gox buan man plak khaix man tie ka jan ra kok mawh nyiex dee kied singax singeh,
4 cplak daux khu dee tan khri nyawng bhawng pa siao ka yam tok ka nyawng.
Teug sijax mukheu pa om ka khri khawm uik koe kah,
ka daux teug deuh ngoi khri pa sau Ma-nax ot daux,
mai baox mawh khaox gix Ya-loeng pa lih plaung an,
mai khlawp simaox ra khlawp pa tiiem gum lox ping nyap ot kah.
5 dKa piang teug sijax mukheu riang Ci-lu-paw koe kah,
in mawh pa jhi grawng pa lih hoek Siyiex;
preeik riang Ci-lu-paw tawb ka piang dang ngeung pa pluat ploi phi map.
Phan mawh son grawng in,
yam in ang pon krai tom sidawng heue.
6 eKraung in kix hoik siao yuh nin.
Cao giex kaing li khankix hoik yuh been kaing pa krub ka plak praix nyiex gox buan man ku ngaix.
7 fMawh ngie cao giex kaing li pa ting pa pon laig hoek ka jan ka daux nyiex gox buan man khaing,
viang mawh nan,
mawh ngie tix num tix bawg khaing,
yam laig ku bawg tag caw tix viex nham laig,
son sang tan lu ka Siyiex son phi map to kaux tix mai ka phi map pa loi pui hun tix yuh.
8 Khuan singax singeh krai sidawng ka daux kaing pa yuh in kix,
ca mawh yam kawn koe nyiex gox buan man jan plak praix,
krax pa phlat hoek ka nyiex pa singax tag ang paoh tix.
9 Nyiex gox buan man jan plak praix mawh pa jhi ka singaix bawg yam in.
In hoik mawh krai ka khawng phak pa tan lu ka Siyiex,
mai krax tan lu ang pon kieh rhawm rhi pui pa krub grum naog.
10 In kix hoik mawh ngie ka son pa ih pa nyaux mai ka son been pa kieh singax khaing,
mawh pa dex dawt mai been plak praix;
dee koe ceu in kix hoik die ngie yam loh Siyiex ka been heue.
11 Viang mawh nan,
Khrit hoik hoek;
nawh mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting,
mai hoik yuh lih ka g kaing ceu pa mhawm khankix.
Nyiex gox buan man cie nawh nie blawn ting blawn grum mhawm,
ang mawh pa yuh kawn pui ka taix tix;
pa krai ang mawh pa yuh ka daux haktiex in.
12 Yam laig Khrit ka daux nyiex gox buan man in,
yam laig tix bawg khaing hoik mawh son jheeng jheeng ka daux nyiex dee pa kied singax,
nawh ang bax siao ka nham kawn pex mai nham kawn moi,
viang mawh nan siao ka nham to kaux tix son sang pon teei ka krax taoh pa koe leeing ju son ex.
13 hPhan teei nham kawn pex mai nham moi mhaing,
mai nyaox pa hoik gleeing ka moi miex,
phruah ka piang to kaux pui pa ang singax singeh ka daux been khankix,
pon teei tix tik ka ceu pa seeing pa brai kix,
pon kieh kix singax.
14 Phan mawh nan,
nham Khrit pa pon kieh grum naog khex ang nie blawn kieh singax lie?
Kheu ka daux khuan pa koe leeing ju an,
nawh hoik teei to kaux tix kieh mawh krax pa tan lu pa grum mhawm tan lu ka Siyiex.
Nham nawh tag sang kieh rhawm rhi ex i singax,
mai teei tix tik ka kaing yuh ex pa seeing pa brai,
kieh ex pon yuh kaing krub ka Siyiex pa im leeing ju heue.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Khrit lih mawh pui si nax ka sijax mukheu pa khraox in,
son sang kieh pui pa hoik kok Siyiex pon ka buan son pa hoik deuh nawh leeing ju.
Grawng pa lih kaing in tix mawh hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing ka daux krax yum;
krax yum in hoik pluat ploi ka phi map pa yuh kix ka sijax mukheu pa jauh an heue.
16 Can pa yuh son lai pa guah sin ngao,
tag caw tix kham sidawng grawng pa hoik yum pui pa yuh son lai pa guah sin ngao.
17 Mawh kheu yam kawn im pui pa yuh son lai pa guah sin ngao,
lai pa yuh son pa guah son sin ngao tag ang koe ceu,
khaix hoik yum nawh lai pa yuh son pa guah sin ngao tag chix koe ceu.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
pie sijax mukheu pa jauh an kawx hoik mawh pa yuh ka nham tom taug chix koe ceu.
19 jYam jauh,
Maw-si hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai ran tix sidiex gau ka can gum lox ping nyap khawm uik ka kawn pui,
khaix an teei nham kawn moi mai nham kawn pex,
klao tau mai rawm,
teei man luat pa raoh mai rao rib im krim cub daux,
phruah ka piang phuk lai lox tat jhawm prawng mai ka piang to kaux kawn pui khawm uik.
20 Nawh ah nin:
“Nham in hoik jhi sidawng ka grawng pa tawx Siyiex pex priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu.”
21 kAwm an,
Maw-si kawx phruah nham ka nyiex gox buan man mai ka piang kraung khrai pa siao ku ceu yam yuh krax krub.
22 lHawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
kraung khrai ku ceu hoik teei nham si ghrah singax khawm uik;
phan ang koe grawng pa jauh nham,
tag ang koe krax pa pluat ploi phi map heue.
Pa yum Khrit hoik si ghrah singax ka phi map
23 Kraung khrai pa yuh hawt awm pak peung rao ma,
tag caw tix siao grawng in si ghrah singax.
Viang mawh nan,
kraung khrai piang rao ma tag caw tix siao krax tan lu pa blawn mhawm si ghrah singax.
24 Khrit ang bax laig ka daux nyiex pa singax pa yuh pui ka taix tix;
an hoik mawh ngie pak peung nyiex buan tete khaing.
Nawh haok ka rao ma,
mai cung ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex ka son ex.
25 Cao giex kaing li pa ting pui Yo-thaix viex nham khawng brie hoek laig ka daux nyiex buan dee kied singax ku num.
Viang mawh nan,
Khrit ang siao lai tan lu ka to kaux tix tom nie bawg,
26 phan ang mawh nan,
jauh tom phan yuh ka haktiex,
nawh tag sang caw tix pon gun yeung ka krax kha rhawm tom nie bawg.
Khrit tan lu tix bawg khaing mai hoik lih tix mawh jheeng jheeng,
yam si dauh bawg yam haktiex in,
nawh teei to kaux tix mawh krax pa tan lu,
son sang si ghrah singax ka phi map.
27 Yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
kawn pui tag sang yum ku kaux,
khaix hoik yum tag sang lih koe krax pa prawng ca Siyiex.
28 mAwm an,
Khrit kawx tan lu tix bawg khaing,
hoik teei tix tik ka phi map pui tom nie.
Nawh tag sang baox lih hoek,
ang mawh ka daux pa yuh son phi map,
hoik mawh pa hoek tix taoh ka pui pa viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix krox jun ka nawh khankix heue.
