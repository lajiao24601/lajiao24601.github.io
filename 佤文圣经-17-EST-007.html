7
1-2 Ngaix khaix,
simiang mai Ha-mang baox hu ih priex pa khre Yi-see-thie.
Yam nyaux kiex ka plai,
simiang baox chawk ka Yi-see-thie:
“Moeng aux Yi-see-thie eue,
yam in krai ka aux,
rawg maix ceu pa tix,
aux tag chix tawx ka maix.
Mawh khawm meung aux tix plak kawx,
aux kawx tawx ka maix heue.”
3 Yi-see-thie pawk lox:
“Phan aux pon krax muh Vawng,
khiox khaw maix cu pa rawg aux tix ceu.
Aux khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka ju aux,
mai pluat ploi nang ju paox ceu aux liak.
4 Aux mai paox ceu aux hoik coh tix ka daux tawm taix pui pa sang pauh ka pui.
Phan pui coh ngie yix mawh siliex cawng,
aux tag ang chix yien yawg tix ang nye,
ang doh ah lox suk sag ka Vawng;
viang mawh nan,
yix sang grai uik,
sang laux leeing uik heue!”
5 Simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu chawk ka moeng tix Yi-see-thie:
“Pui mawx pa pon yuh kaing yuh nin?
Pui in ot dee mawx?”
6 Yi-see-thie pawk lo:
“Pui pa sang kieh yix laux mawh pui pa caox in Ha-mang heue!”
Ha-mang kied si yaung daux rhawm sivoe ngai simiang mai moeng simiang dum mawx dum an.
7 Simiang kied song rhawm tix kaoh cung,
mai lih khaing dee nyaux plai hu ka si peem tai plak praix.
Ha-mang jhak tix yaox simiang sang but rhawm tix ciyang nawh son kaing in,
tom ot tix khiox khaw ka moeng simiang Yi-see-thie pluat ploi ka ju nawh.
8 Yam deeh hoek simiang khaing si peem tai ka dee sawm,
Ha-mang kawn krub ka khaox ngawm Yi-see-thie son sang pon krax pluat ploi.
Simiang yaox grawng in,
tom ah lox si num,
ah nin:
“Pui in pon yuh krax pa ang khaw ka moeng aux sivoe aux ka daux nyiex haw lie?”
Yam ah simiang lox in,
pui sieh tom lhop ka kaing Ha-mang.
9 Pui sieh an tix kaux pa kaux Ha-paw-na haok sivoe ah nin:
“Ha-mang kawn yuh khaox ciyang tix mu pa koe tix nga ra kongjhee ka daux nyiex tix,
son sang teuk teue ka Maw-ti-kai pa taoh ka ju Vawng heue!”
Simiang tawx lox:
“Teuk teue Ha-mang ka piang heue!”
10 Kheu nan,
kix teuk teue Ha-mang ka khaox ciyang pa sang teuk teue Maw-ti-kai ka piang.
Krax song rhawm simiang taug grai heue.
