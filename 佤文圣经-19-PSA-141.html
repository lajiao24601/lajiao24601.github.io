Lox rawg buan plak pon bo
141
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux khiox khaw rawg mhaing ka maix,
khiox khaw maix nied tix teem coi ka aux;
yam khiox khaw kok yo aux ka maix,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka liak.
2 aKieh lox rawg buan aux awm taox pa gleeing nyawng bhawng haok ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
kieh aux yaog taix tix khawm ra khawn lih mawh krax tan plak pon bo heue.
 
3 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kuang ka jheeh aux;
khiox khaw maix pau ka siyieb jheeh aux.
4 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux hu ka daux krax pa caox pa caig,
paw vi kieh aux rom tix seeing brai tau mai pui pa caox pa caig;
khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux hoek tix ih ka priex preem pa ghaok kix heue.
 
5 Aux kawn cu tix rieb ka krax muh pa lauh pui rauh pui see ka aux,
ang cu tix rieb ka krax pa nab yam yung yi pui caox ka aux,
mawh kheu rawg aux buan tieb tie ka krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix jheeng jheeng.
6 Yam si blat pui simiang kix ka greem jaong rang,
pui haoh pui hun tom krai doh grawng lox pa krai aux mawh tete.
7 Pui hoik tik si-ang kix ka praok si meeig,
awm gaw khaox pa hoik lug pui phawng yuh nan.
8 Kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex aux pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
aux ca kawn yum rhawm ka maix;
aux sang sawg krax pa kia ngien maix,
khiox khaw maix paw vi tik vud ka aux.
9 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux krix ka rub pa hoik taong kix,
taoh aux luat luan khaing daux jhawk pa taong pui pa caox pa caig khankix.
10 Kieh pui pa caox pa caig khankix krix ka rub pa taong tix.
Viang mawh nan,
aux pon yaox tix luat luan mhawm mhawm heue.
