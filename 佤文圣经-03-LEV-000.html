Lai Li-we
Krai dawt
Lai Li-we mawh pa tiiem yam jauh cub pui Yi-seu-liex tix yuh kaing krub mai grawng krax been li pa hoik tat tix yuh khankix, khawm kaing pa khaw cao giex kaing li tix yuh yam yuh kix kaing krub khankix heue.
Phuk lai in kaing ting krai Siyiex mawh pa singax singeh, mai krax pa yuh paox yuh pie tau mai Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh, mai lox tat jhawm prawng krax krub pa khaw kawn praix Siyiex tix yuh khankix.
Simin ting kao dim simin iak kao daix “muh pui awm muh to kaux tix” mawh gum lox sigang khri pa blawn koe min ka daux phuk lai in. Ye-sux hoik siao ka gum lox in, mai ah mawh gum lox ping nyap pa blawn ting khaing paox tix ka daux grawng ra heue.
Krai kaing
1. Lox tat jhawm prawng krax tan mai grawng been Krax-tan Pa-gleeing (1.1–7.38)
2. Pa kieh Ya-loeng mai doeh ceu cad nawh yuh cao giex kaing li (8.1–10.20)
3. Lox tat jhawm prawng pa yuh krax pa singax singeh mai pa ang singax singeh (11.1–15.33)
4. Singaix Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap (16.1–34)
5. Krax ot krax koe pa singax singeh mai grawng lox tat jhawm prawng yuh kaing krub (17.1–27.34)
