Cao jhai yuh tix mawh pui pa si pup krax laux
12
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
2 aNawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix hoik paung ot ka daux pui pa lieh lox khankix.
Kix koe ngai,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix yaox,
kix koe yhaok,
viang mawh nan,
ang chix mhong,
mawh kheu kix hoik mawh pui pa lieh lox heue.
3 “Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix rien khre bau pa sang lo tix yam vuk vied vuk viang tix,
mai lih hu sivoe sang faik praix.
Maix tag caw kieh ngai pui hun yaox tix yam lih hu tix,
mai to hoek ka haktiex tix dauh dauh,
phan ang mawh nan pui pa lieh lox khankix tag sang kuan tix jhak grawng pa yuh maix.b
4 Maix tag caw nied tix mad mhawm bau pa sang lo tix yam pon ngaix sivoe sang to mox tix,
son sang kieh kix yaox ka kaing pa yuh maix.
Yam dex sang faik praix,
maix tag caw tix lih khaing nyiex yam kawn jhak kix ka maix,
awm pui pa sang vuk vied vuk viang yuh nan.
5 Yam kawn jhak kix ka maix,
maix tag caw tix kaong daux tix mu ka piang ding nyiex maix,
mai teei bau an lih khaing daux an.
6 Maix tag caw tix kawg bau an ka klip tix sivoe ngai kix,
mai tawb ngai tix lih hu.
Pa yuh maix in hoik mawh pa tag sang ne raw aux ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.”
7 Aux hoik hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka aux.
Singaix an,
aux hoik mad mhawm ka bau pa sang lo aux yam to mox tix,
yam dex sang faik praix,
aux teei taix tix kaong daux ka piang ding tix mu,
khaix an kawg bau piang klip tix ka sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai lih hu heue.
8 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux;
9 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
yam in pui Yi-seu-liex pa lieh lox khankix tag sang chawk maix,
mai simie tix tawng grawng pa yuh maix ceu pa tix.
10 Maix tag sang krai lox pa sang krai aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka kix.
Gum lox in mawh pa krai son simiang pa kuang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka pui haktiex khankix.
11 Tag caw tix krai ka kix,
kaing pa yuh maix mawh mhai sakse tix ceu,
mai jhi grawng kaing pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka piang to kaux kix yuh nan.
Kix tag sang lih mawh pui pa si pup krax laux,
mai mawh pui pa giex pui viex hu heue.
12 Simiang pa ot daux kix tag sang teei bau an yam faik praix,
mai to hu mox tix lih khaing daux pa hoik grox kix kaong piang ding son nawh.
Nawh tag sang tawb ngai tix,
mai ang yaox ka dee sang hu tix.
13 cViang mawh nan,
aux tag sang nih tix taong ka rub cie tix,
mai giex ka nawh,
viex nawh hoek ka veng Pa-pi-loeng.
Sivoe sang yaox nawh veng an nawh tag sang yum.
14 Aux tag sang grheu ka can pui daux nyiex haw simiang khawm uik,
khawm pui cao teem mai pui naing pa pau khankix prung prang tix hu ka meung tang meung.
Pui tag sang leue giex kix ku dauh ku dee,
mai pauh kix yum heue.
15 “Khaix hoik grheu aux kix prung prang tix hu ka meung ku meung,
ploi kix hu ka meung tang meung khankix,
kix tag tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
16 Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang deuh pui pa tux ka daux kix luat luan khaing naing,
kaing cai rhawm,
khaing jhawm saux preng.
Yuh nin,
pui pa kawn deuk khankix tag sang krai lao kaing yuh tix pa caox pa caig singaix pa hoik luan,
yam vuk vied vuk viang tix ka meung ku meung khankix,
khaix an mai krai doh grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Mhai sakse krax pa yaoh yee si yaung
17 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
18 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag sang si yaung yam sawm yam rheep tix;
si kua mai khi rhawm yam ih yam nyaux tix.
19 Maix tag caw tix krai ka pui meung khawm uik,
in hoik mawh gum lox pa sang krai Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa kawn deuk khankix ka haktiex Yi-seu-liex.
Kix tag sang si yaung yam sawm yam rheep tix;
mai khi rhawm si kua rhi yam ih yam nyaux tix.
Haktiex kix tag sang riem,
mawh kheu hoik kied lieh pui haktiex lox.
20 Veng pa naog pui daux tag sang yuh laux leeing,
nyiex yaong khankix tang tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag daux tu.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Gum lox pa khaw mai gum lox pa tang siyox
21 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux;
22 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
yuh ka mawx pui Yi-seu-liex lai krai lox pa yuh nin hu ‘bawg yam hoik luan,
riang su sai kied singaix’ gum lox been prim in lie?
23 Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai yuh nin:
aux tag ang lai kieh gum lox been prim in krai lao tix hu.
Ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex tag ang lai krai lao lox in.
Maix tieh tix krai ka kix yuh nin:
bawg yam pa khaw hoik hoek,
riang su sai hoik dex sang lih tix mawh heue!
24 “Ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex tag ang lai baox koe ka riang su sai pa pie lhien,
mai ka lox jhai pa nyaw glaw ka pui.
25 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang hoek tix si kah ka kix;
gum lox pa krai aux tag sang lih tix mawh tete,
mai ang lai baox dha bawg yam.
Pui pa lieh lox eue,
aux sang tawx lox pa ne raw ka pex,
tag sang yuh kaing in lih yam kawn im ju pex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
26 Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
27 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
pui Yi-seu-liex ce riang su sai pa hoik yaox maix,
mai lox jhai pa hoik krai maix mawh son singaix pa kawn singai.
28 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai yuh nin:
aux ang doh lai baox dha bawg yam;
gum lox pa krai aux tag sang lih tix mawh tete;
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
