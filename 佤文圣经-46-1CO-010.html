Grawng pa ang cu krub ka riang rao
10
aPux aik pux o khankix eue,
aux tag sang nhie pex doh keud ka grawng pa hawt tax du ex Maw-si hoek ka daux tiag daux tu.
2 Kix hoik yaox tix tiang Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh mhawm mhawm ka daux pa kia ngien lon pai awm.
Kix hoik cub com tix mawh pui cie Maw-si ka daux pai awm,
ka daux rawm.
3 bKix hoik ih priex ka daux khuan pa awm paox khawm uik,
4 choik nyaux rawm keet ka daux khuan pa awm paox.
Rawm keet in hoik mawh pa jauh lih khaing rang simaox pa dex dawt mai khuan pa hu tau mai kix;
rang simaox an mawh Khrit.
5 dViang mawh nan,
ka daux kix koe pui tom nie ang mawh pa khaw rhawm Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan hoik goh tix yum daux tiag daux tu heue.
6 eKaing in kix khawm uik hoik mawh pa ang ex cu pui yuh,
ne raw ex ang pon tix ghoing rhawm ka kaing ceu pa caox pa caig awm kix yuh nan,
7 fang pon tix hu krub riang rao awm pui tix blah ka daux kix yuh nan.
Awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pui hun in kix ngawm tix ih nyaux,
kaoh cung tix graoh heue.”
8 gEx ang pon tix phit vao yuh kaing pa kaig tua awm pui tix blah ka daux kix yuh nin:
mawh kheu phi map pa yuh kix krax pa phit vao,
ka daux tix ngaix khaing,
kix goh tix yum ra mheen loe rheng kaux.
9 iEx ang pon tix nyaw glaw ka Khrith awm hoik nyaw glaw pui tix blah ka daux kix yuh nin:
si uing hoik tuh kix yum kheu pa nyaw glaw tix ka nawh.
10 jEx ang pon tix kieh lox pa salao lih khaing daux jheeh tix awm pui tix blah yuh nin:
kawn cau Siyiex hoik yuh kix laux leeing kheu pa kieh kix lox pa salao lih khaing daux jheeh tix heue.
11 Kaing ceu in kix pa hoik si pup kix,
hoik lih mawh krax pa sidiex ka pui tang kaux khawm uik,
mai baox tiiem lih mawh krax pa sidiex ka ex khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik ot ex ka daux singaix bawg yam pa si dauh heue.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
pui mawx pa ce tix pon cung kaux cong,
pui an tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
son ang sang sideeing caong tix goh.
13 Krax nyaw glaw pa si pup pex ku ceu,
ang mawh pa hoik pon kham pui ku kaux.
Siyiex mawh pa kaux sijax;
nawh ang doh kieh pex si pup ka krax nyaw krax glaw pa ang pex pon tieb tie.
Yam hoek krax nyaw krax glaw,
nawh tag sang tawx riang rao ka pex,
kieh pex pon kham,
mai pon daux krax tix nyaing son pex heue.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
pex tag caw tix to singai hu khaing kaing ceu pa krub riang rao khankix.
15 Aux mawh pa krai ka pex pa mawh pui pa hoik tawng sidawng ka grawh kaing ceu in kix;
pex chix tix tang guah tix tawng ka lox pa krai aux khaw ang khaw.
16 kEx hoik nyaux ka kawk pa nawb buan,
khex ang hoik mawh pa guah ex tix pon ka nham Khrit lie?
Ex hoik ih ka khao mun pa hoik si vit tix,
khex ang hoik mawh pa guah ex tix pon ka to kaux Khrit lie?
17 Mawh kheu koe ex khao mun tix lon khaing,
mai hoik baox guah ex tix pon ka khao mun tix lon;
mawh kheu nan,
viang kaux pui ex hun,
mai ca kawn mawh to kaux tix mu khaing heue.
18 lJhak ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue,
pui pa hoik ih ka khawng tan khankix,
tag sang si doe tix tau mai khu dee tan.
19 Aux krai yuh nin dee bleeh mawh ceu tix lie?
Mawh ce pex riang rao an mai khawng tan an kied mawh pa ting ceu lie?
20 mAng mawh heue!
Pa krai aux mawh yuh nin,
khawng tan pa tan lu ot ka piang khu dee tan been li tang ceu,
mawh pa tan lu ka phi,
ang mawh pa tan lu ka Siyiex.
Aux ang simie pex yuh paox pie tau mai phi.
21 Pex ang pon tix nyaux ka kawk cie Simiang Ye-sux,
mai baox nyaux ka kawk cie phi,
ang pon tix ih ka priex preem cie Simiang Ye-sux,
mai baox ih ka priex preem cie phi.
22 nEx tag sang cheeik tix kieh Simiang Ye-sux song rhawm lie?
Ex ting riang khaing nawh lie?
23 oKoe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Ex chix tix tang teei alo tix yuh kaing ku ceu heue.”
Lox in ang lud.
Kheu nan,
ang mawh kaing ku ceu pa koe dee mhawm khawm uik.
“Ex chix tix tang teei alo tix yuh kaing ku ceu heue.”
Kheu nan,
ang mawh kaing ku ceu pa chix teem ka pui khawm uik.
24 Pui ku kaux ang caw tix keud son sang mhawm to kaux tix nong nong,
tag caw tix kia ngien son sang mhawm ka pui tang kaux heue.
25 Nex pa coh pui daux laih,
pex tag chix tix ih khawm uik,
paw vi si kaw tix chawk cheng son rhawm rhi tix.
26 pMawh kheu hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Haktiex mai can ceu pa koe ka daux haktiex hoik mawh cie Simiang Siyiex khawm uik heue.”
27 Phan koe pui pa ang yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux ghaok pex sawm,
pex kawx chix tix hu,
kheu nan,
pex tawng ih ka priex preem pa hoik khan ot piang pheen,
ang siao yuh tix haoh rhawm rhi.
28 Viang mawh nan,
phan koe pui pa krai pex mhong,
krai mawh priex preem an kix pa tan lu pui ka riang rao,
pex tag caw tix paw vi ih ka priex preem in son pui pa krai pex mhong an,
mai ka son rhawm rhi tix,
29 aux ang krai ka son rhawm rhi pex,
mai hoik krai ka rhawm rhi pui an heue.
Tee koe pui pa chawk ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
yuh ka mawx krax pa bheeik luan aux,
lai kawn sang kham pa phrawk rhawm rhi pui tang kaux lie?
30 Phan aux rieb tix ih priex preem ka daux krax pa yaog yaw buan son,
pui tang kaux kawn sang chix ne raw ka priex preem ih aux,
pa hoik nawb buan son piang yuh ka mawx lie?”
31 Phan mawh nan,
pex yuh ceu pa tix ang ah,
ih nyaux ceu pa tix ang ah,
tag caw tix kieh mawh pa yuh son min plak ngai Siyiex.
32 Viang mawh ka daux pui Yo-thaix ang ah,
pui Si-lax ang ah,
phan ang mawh nan ceu cao Siyiex ang ah,
tag ang pon tix kieh pui sideeing tix goh.
33 Pex tag caw hawt tix yuh awm kaing pa hoik yuh aux;
aux tag sang kieh pui hun gaux rhawm,
mai ang sax keud ceu pa mhawm son to kaux tix nong nong,
mai hoik keud ceu pa mhawm son pui hun khawm uik,
son sang kieh kix pon krax taoh heue.
