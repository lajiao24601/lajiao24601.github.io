Siyiex mai pui cie nawh
78
Pui cie aux eue,
tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa sidiex gau aux;
pex tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet ka lox aux.
2 aAux sang teei gum lox phek tiiem krai sidawng;
aux tag sang siao lox si nieb paoh ka been prim pa raux pa mox ah nin:
3 Pa mawh pa hoik yaox pex mai pa hoik mhong pex,
kaing pa hoik krai tax du yix ka yix.
4 Yix ang mox kaing in ka kawn praix tix,
krai kix mhong sidawng ah nin:
siawp sibrawm Simiang Siyiex mai kaing pa daox pa deng yuh nawh khawm uik heue.
 
5 Nawh tawx lox tat jhawm prawng ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh tawx gum lox ping nyap ka doeh ceu cad Ya-keux.
Nawh tawx lox ka tax du yix,
sang sidiex gau lox tat jhawm prawng tix ka kawn praix tix,
6 Son sang kieh ju phaox khaix gau kah,
baox deeh sidiex gau kawn praix kix kah.
7 Yuh nin,
kix kawx tag sang chix yum rhawm ka Siyiex,
ang piawm ka kaing pa yuh nawh,
priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap nawh leeing ju.
8 Kix tom ang yaox tix awm tax du tix yuh nan,
lih tix mawh pui pa tieh vheen ka lox tawm jhawm krai,
ang koe krax yum rhawm pa kaux pa cong ka Siyiex,
ang koe krax cu nghiet pa ang chix tieh loh ka nawh heue.
 
9 Kawn praix Yi-fa-liien viang siao ak puh puing naing,
yam hoek singaix puing naing kix tang yaog tix to khawm uik.
10 Kix ang cu nghiet ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh kix tau mai Siyiex;
kix ang nghiet ka lox tat jhawm prawng nawh.
11 Kix piawm ka kaing pa yuh nawh,
piawm ka mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik jhi nawh kix yaox.
12 bSiyiex ot ka daux tiag kaung Saw-ang,
mai ka daux Ai-cix,
hoik yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka plak sivoe ngai tax du kix.
13 cNawh kieh rawm sigrit sigra tang guah paox tix,
mai thung kix hu;
nawh kieh rawm jung tix awm ding yuh nan.
14 dYam pon ngaix nawh teei pai awm thung ka kix;
yam pon sawm nawh teei ngu jhan riang ka kix.
15 eNawh kieh rang simaox bih ka daux tiag,
kieh kix yaox tix nyaux rawm pa koe dee kied rauh tom sak.
16 Nawh kieh rang simaox buing tix lih ka rawm keet,
kieh rawm lih awm rawm klawng yuh nan heue.
 
17 Viang mawh nan,
kix ca kawn phit lud ka Siyiex,
tieh vheen ka cao pa ting siawp sibrawm ka daux tiag.
18 fKix su tix nyaw glaw ka Siyiex,
teei ka priex preem pa sang lo tix.
19 Kix phit lud ka Siyiex ah nin:
Siyiex sang chix pon khran priex preem ka daux tiag yuh ka mawx lie?
20 Nawh hoik lauh ka rang simaox,
kieh rawm keet buing tix lih khaing;
viang mawh nan,
nawh kawx chix pon tawx ex ih ka priex preem,
rien khre ka nex son kawn pui cie tix lie?
 
21 Simiang Siyiex kheu mhong ka lox in tom kied song rhawm.
Nawh teei ngu mai gleeing ka pui cie tix Ya-keux,
krax song rhawm ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai pon tieb tie kah;
22 mawh kheu ang kix yum rhawm ka nawh,
ang een rhawm tix ka krax pa taoh nawh.
23 Viang mawh nan,
nawh kawn tawx lox ka rao ma,
tawx lox kieh siviex rao ma baoh;
24 gNawh tawx kix ih priex preem lih khaing rao ma,
tawx man na lih eei kix.
25 Mawh kheu nan,
kix hoik ih ka priex preem cie kawn cau Siyiex;
Siyiex kieh kix sak vaig.
26 Nawh kieh bhaung plak lih singaix hoek tix phlu;
nawh baox siao siawp sibrawm bhaung plak laong lih.
27 Nawh kieh sim lih cawd awm phawng tiex yuh nan,
tawx kix ih nex tom nie awm mhaik pa koe ka praok rawm yuh nan;
28 Sim cawd ka daux bang,
mai ka sivie siveng nyiex gox bang khankix.
29 Kix hoik yaox tix ih khawm uik,
mai hoik sak heue;
Siyiex hoik tawx kix ih awm pa sang lo kix yuh nan.
30 Viang mawh nan,
kix kied ghoing ka priex tete,
Yam kawn sai jheeh kix ka priex preem,
31 Mai krax pa song rhawm Siyiex lih hoek piang kix,
pauh ka pui pa nhum ju kix,
yuh laux ka pui nhum ju cie Yi-seu-liex pa blawn mhawm heue.
 
32 Viang hoik koe ka mhai pa daox pa am in kix khawm uik,
mai kix ca kawn nied tix yuh phi map;
viang hoik koe mhai pa daox pa deng in kix khawm uik,
mai kix ca kawn ang cu tix yum rhawm.
33 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh kieh singaix bawg yam cie kix si dauh tix vud khaing,
kieh ju kix si pup ka krax laux krax leeing dum mawx dum an.
34 Yam pauh nawh ka kix,
mai kix tom kling tix deeh ka nawh;
kix loh rhawm rhi tix,
mai deeh baox sawg tix khiox khaw ka nawh.
35 Kix doh keud ka Siyiex mawh cao pa kia ngien ka kix,
Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong mawh cao pa taoh ka kix.
36 Viang mawh nan,
jheeh kix naog ka pa pie lhien;
lox pa krai kix hoik mawh pa pie lhien khawm uik.
37 Kix ang yum rhawm ka nawh;
kix ang nghiet ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tix tau mai nawh heue.
 
38 Kheu nan,
Siyiex caox rhawm ka pui cie tix:
nawh pluat ploi ka phi map kix,
mai ang yuh kix laux leeing;
nawh hoik gut krax pa song rhawm tix tom nie bawg,
mai ang ploi lih hoek piang kix.
39 Nawh hoik doh keud grawng mawh kix pui iak riang pa khaw tix yum,
hoik awm bhaung pa phlu,
pa ang lai deeh hoek heue.
 
40 Kix ot daux tiag daux tu,
mai baox tieh vheen ka Siyiex ang jud;
kix ca kieh nawh laux rhawm ang jud ang jai.
41 Kix ca baox deeh nyaw ka Siyiex,
cheeik tix kieh cao pa kied singax pui Yi-seu-liex chu rhawm.
42 Kix hoik piawm ka pa ting riang ting rao nawh,
ang lai doh keud ka singaix yam taoh nawh kix luat luan khaing pui koeng,
43 kix hoik piawm ka mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik yuh nawh ka daux haktiex Ai-cix,
ang lai doh keud ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh ka daux lung Saw-ang heue.
44 hNawh hoik kieh rawm klawng ting tieh tix lih mawh nham,
kieh pui Ai-cix ang lai chix nyaux,
45 iNawh hoik kieh phung roi hoek tix yuh kix laux,
kieh khiet yuh kaung ma kix laux leeing.
46 jNawh hoik kieh kawng ih ka to ma kix uik,
kieh sido laux yuh ma si peem kix.
47 kNawh hoik ploi mu pre lih laux yuh nhu khaox plix siiet kix,
ploi lon si dap lih laux yuh khaox ruh kix.
48 Nawh hoik kieh mu pre lih lauh ka phung moi kix yum,
kieh sahl tuh ka phung yung kix yum.
49 Nawh hoik kieh kix si pup ka krax laux krax leeing pa ting,
kieh krax song rhawm song rhi mawh kawn cau pa yum pa lauh ka kix.
50 Nawh ang lai gut ka krax pa song rhawm to kaux tix;
nawh ang lai va rhawm ka ju kix,
kieh jhawm saux preng pauh kix yum.
51 mNawh hoik pauh ka kawn ai pui nyiex Ai-cix ku nyiex heue.
Hoik pauh pa mhawm kluing pa liang tix keh ka daux nyiex gox bang cie Hang.
52 nKhaix an,
nawh awm cao liang pa thung ka pui cie to kaux tix lih hu,
duk thung kix laig ka daux tiag daux tu.
53 oNawh thung kix nyawm ot,
ang koe krax pa lhat graung khi rhawm;
mai hoik kieh rawm sigrit sigra kheem ka pui koeng ka kix.
54 pNawh viex kix hoek ka daux haktiex pa singax cie tix,
Hoek ka gawng pa hoik pon kuang nawh.
55 qNawh hoik grheu pui tang ceu tang cad lih hu khaing plak sivoe ngai pui cie tix,
guah tix tawx haktiex ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku cub,
hoik tawx nyiex ma cie pui tang ceu ka pui cie tix heue.
 
56 rViang mawh nan,
kix ca kawn nyaw,
mai tieh vheen ka Siyiex cao pa ting pa lhaong ka piang khawm uik;
kix ang cu ang nghiet ka ping nyap nawh.
57 Kix ang cu lox mai tieh vheen awm tax du kix yuh nan;
kix awm ak puh pa deen lah,
mai ang pon tix een rhawm tix kah.
58 Kix sum khu dee tan been li pui tang ceu,
mai cheeik tix kieh nawh song rhawm;
kix teei riang rao pa krub pui,
mai kieh nawh song rhawm.
59 Yam yaox Siyiex kah,
mai nawh tom song rhawm,
tik vud ka pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
60 tNawh tik ghram ka nyiex gox bang pa ot ka See-laws,
pa mawh nyiex yam ot nawh tau mai yix,
61 uNawh hoik cu tix kieh pui koeng tud ka teug sijax mukheu hu,
pa mawh pa jhi ka siawp sibrawm nawh,
mai ka min plak ngai nawh an,
62 Nawh song rhawm ka pui cie tix,
kieh kix yum ka daux tawm taix pui koeng.
63 Ngu naing hoik bleet pui pa nhum ju kix;
kawn bun krih kix ang yaox tix koe mex.
64 Cao giex kaing li kix pui hoik pauh yum ka vaig gawn.
Miex mai khankix,
mai ang pon tix yiiem grai heue.
 
65 Simiang Siyiex awm pa hoik haing khaix it tix;
nawh mhong koe riang tix awm pui pa ting riang khaix hoik yoek tix.
66 Nawh grheu pui koeng dak tix deeh,
kieh kix pon krax pa som nai kaig tua.
67 Viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik tik ka doeh ceu cad Yaw-seux;
nawh ang baox laug ka ceu pui Yi-fa-liien.
68 Nawh hoik laug ka ceu pui Yo-tax,
hoik khaw rhawm ka gawng Shi-ang pa muh nawh.
69 Ka dee an,
nawh sum ka nyiex buan,
mai kieh krax lhaong sidu mai rao ma,
yaog jung awm haktiex,
kaux cong leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
70 vNawh laug ka siliex tix Ta-wue,
kok nawh lih khaing daux pa yuh kaing pa liang yung,
71 ang lai kieh nawh deeh baox kia ngien ka phung yung,
ap nawh mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
mawh cao pa liang pui cie Siyiex.
72 Ta-wue eei liang kix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
duk thung kix ka daux krax pa lhak tix heue.
