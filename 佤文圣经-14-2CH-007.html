Been krax tan ka nyiex buan
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 8.62–66)
7
aSimiang Saw-law-meung hoik rawg buan,
koe ngu lih khaing rao ma,
mai gleeing ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing khawm uik mai khawng tan tang ceu;
pa riang min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex naog ka daux nyiex buan.
2 Mawh kheu naog pa riang in ka daux nyiex buan,
cao giex kaing li ang pon laig.
3 bYam yaox pui Yi-seu-liex lih ngu khaing rao ma,
mai baox yaox pa riang Simiang Siyiex naog ka daux nyiex buan,
kix tom gup tix krub Simiang Siyiex ka piang simaox pa tawm tix piang tiex,
yaog yaw nawh ah nin:
“Nawh kied mhawm kied mhiem;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.”
4 Khaix an,
Saw-law-meung mai pui haoh pui hun tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Simiang Siyiex.
5 Nawh tan lu moi ra mheen mai ra rheng mu,
yung kao ra mheen mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
Yuh nin,
simiang mai pui hun khawm uik yuh poi krax tan son nyiex buan.
6 Cao giex kaing li khankix cung ka dee cia kah,
pui Li-we cung ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
mai siao kraung pa tawx Ta-wue yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an mai rhax lai yaog yaw awm been pa hoik tat Ta-wue ah nin:
“Krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan.”
Yam in,
ka plak sivoe ngai pui Li-we,
cao giex kaing li khankix phlu ka teet tawng;
mai pui haoh pui hun khankix hoik kaoh cung khawm uik heue.
7 Saw-law-meung guah dee yi haktiex ka sigreung biang brawk,
pa mawh biang brawk pa koe sivoe nyiex buan mawh pa singax,
mai tan lu ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
khawm bee kraung vaig khawng brie pa siao ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim dee an.
Saw-law-meung yuh mawh nin mawh kheu kied iak khu tawng,
ang kub khawng tan ot piang tom nie heue.
8 Saw-law-meung mai pui hun Yi-seu-liex khawm uik yuh Poi Nyiex-gox-bang ot ka nyiex buan aliah singaix.
Pui hun kaoh khaing diang moing gawng Ha-ma ka plak laong dee singai,
mai baoh mee haktiex Ai-cix ku dauh ka plak seh,
hoek tix yuh poi tau.
9 Kix teei bawg yam aliah singaix tan lu ka khu,
mai baox yuh poi krax gaux rhawm aliah singaix;
ka daux singaix nghoik,
kix tom yuh krax pa nyeep ka bang poi.
10 Baox hoek singaix khaix,
pa mawh khix aliah tix nga loe singaix,
Saw-law-meung tawx pui haoh pui hun deeh ing ka nyiex.
Kix hoik gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka pui cie nawh Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx buan ka Ta-wue mai Saw-law-meung nang heue.
Siyiex baox lih bleeh ka Saw-law-meung
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 9.1–9)
11 Khaix hoik sum simiang Saw-law-meung ka nyiex buan mai nyiex haw simiang,
hoik grum yuh kaing can pa keud tix khawm uik,
12 Simiang Siyiex lih bleeh ka nawh yam pon sawm,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux hoik mhong ka lox rawg maix buan;
aux laug nyiex buan in mai kieh mawh dee tan lu krax tan ka aux.
13 Yam ang aux tawx rawm lhiex lih,
phan ang mawh nan kieh sido ih ka to ma laux uik,
phan ang mawh nan tawx jhawm saux preng lih hoek ka piang to kaux pui cie aux,
14 ca mawh rawg kix buan ka aux,
khaix an mai deeh loh rhawm rhi tix tete,
mai tik vud ka kaing pa caox pa yuh tix.
Aux tag chix deeh nghiet ka kix khaing rao ma,
pluat ploi ka phi map kix,
mai deeh cox kieh haktiex kix haok huan heue.
15 Aux chix kia ngien ka nyiex buan in,
mai nghiet ka krax rawg buan pa tawx kix khaing dee in;
16 mawh kheu aux hoik laug ka nyiex buan in,
hoik kieh guah tix lih mawh pa singax,
son sang mawh dee krub ka aux leeing ju leeing phan,
aux tag jhak ka nawh jheeng jheeng,
kia ngien ka nawh.
17 “Phan maix awm keeing tix Ta-wue yum rhawm krub ka aux yuh nan,
mai priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap aux,
18 caux tag sang giex tom kaux ka lox pa ap aux tix deuh ka keeing maix Ta-wue,
pa mawh doeh ceu cad nawh tag sang kuang ka piang Yi-seu-liex leeing ju.
19 Phan maix mai pui cie maix ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx aux ka pex,
mai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux,
20 aux tag grheu pex li hu khaing haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka pex,
khaix an mai singai hu khaing nyiex buan in pa hoik guah aux mawh pa singax,
pa mawh dee krub ka aux.
Pui ku dauh ku dee tag sang nyiah ka nyiex buan in,
mai map nai ka heue.
21 “Yam in pui ku kaux hoik yung yi ka nyiex buan in,
viang mawh nan,
hoek singaix yam an,
pui pa lawng krax ku kaux tag sang daox deng mai chawk ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex yuh ka mawx lai yuh nin ka meung in mai ka nyiex buan in lie?’
22 Pui tag sang pawk ah nin:
‘Mawh kheu hoik tik vud kix Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix.
Simiang Siyiex hoik viex tax du kix lih khaing haktiex Ai-cix,
mai kix lai hu krub ka siyiex tang kaux yuh nin,
mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tom ploi krax laux krax leeing in lih piang kix khawm uik heue.’ ”
