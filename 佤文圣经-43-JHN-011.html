Grawng yum La-sa-lux
11
aKoe pui pa koe jhawm saux tix kaux ceu kaux mawh La-sa-lux,
paung ot ka Paw-ta-ni;
Ma-li-yax mai pux o nawh Ma-tax kawx ot ka yaong in nang. (
2 bMa-li-yax in hoik mawh pui pa sax kraix rawm bee ka caong Simiang Ye-sux,
mai teei hauk kaing tix awt kroh kah.
La-sa-lux pa koe jhawm saux an hoik mawh pux aik nawh.)
3 Kiex pux o ra kaux ploi pui hu ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
paox grawm pa muh maix hoik koe jhawm saux heue.”
4 Ye-sux mhong lox in nawh tom ah nin:
“Jhawm saux La-sa-lux ang pon kieh nawh yum,
mai hoik mawh son sang kieh Siyiex koe min plak ngai,
khaix an kieh kawn simex Siyiex pon min plak ngai kheu in heue.”
5 Ye-sux muh ka Ma-tax mai pux o nawh,
mai ka La-sa-lux kawx hoik muh ka nang.
6 Khaix hoik tawng nawh koe jhawm saux La-sa-lux,
nawh ca kawn paung ot ka haktiex dee paung tix ra singaix.
7 Khaix an nawh krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Baox hu ex hoek ka haktiex Yo-thaix heue.”
8 Pui gau nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
singaix pa hoik luan khankix,
pui Yo-thaix sang teei simaox tawk maix kah,
maix lai kawn sang baox hu dee an lie?”
9 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yam pon ngaix ang mawh kao ra taix nari lie?
Pui hu krax yam pon ngaix,
mai ang chix sideeing tix goh,
mawh kheu hoik chix yaox nawh ka pa riang haktiex in.
10 Pui phan hu krax yam pon sawm,
nawh tag sang sideeing tix goh,
mawh kheu ang nawh koe pa riang heue.”
11 Khaix hoik krai Ye-sux ka lox in kix,
nawh baox ah nin:
“Paox grawm aux La-sa-lux hoik laig it,
aux tag sang hu so ka nawh heue.”
12 Pui gau ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
phan nawh hoik laig it,
nawh kawn sang chix moi heue.”
13 Tete te lai,
dee bleeh lox krai Ye-sux mawh krai hoik yum La-sa-lux;
viang mawh nan,
kix ce nawh krai laig it nawh tete.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux krai ka kix sidawng ah nin:
“La-sa-lux hoik yum;
15 son sang kieh pex yum rhawm,
aux ang ot dee ot nawh tan tieh tix mawh pa mhawm.
Yam in ex hu jhak ka nawh heue.”
16 To-ma (ceu kaux kawn brawm) krai ka pui gau tang kaux ah nin:
“Hu ex tau mai sara,
yum ex tau mai nawh heue!”
Pa kaoh im mai simiang ju
17 Ye-sux hoek ka Paw-ta-ni,
nawh tawng pui hoik si paung ka La-sa-lux koe pon singaix.
18 Paw-ta-ni singai Yie-lu-sa-leung ang lai koe loe kongli;
19 koe pui Yo-thaix tix blah hoek tix jhak ka Ma-tax mai Ma-li-yax,
mai hoek tix khu rhawm kiex son pa hoik yum pux aik kiex heue.
20 Ma-tax mhong hoek Ye-sux,
nawh tom lih rieb ka Ye-sux;
mai Ma-li-yax kawn ot ka nyiex.
21 Ma-tax krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
phan maix ot dee in,
pux aik aux tag ang yum heue!
22 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawng,
viang mawh yam in ang ah,
phan maix khiox khaw ceu pa tix ka Siyiex,
nawh tag sang tawx ka maix heue.”
23 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Pux aik maix tag sang deeh kaoh im heue.”
24 Ma-tax ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawng grawng tag sang deeh kaoh im nawh ka daux singaix pa si dauh heue.”
25 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh pa deeh kaoh im,
hoik mawh ju.
Pui pa yum rhawm ka aux,
viang nawh hoik yum,
nawh kawn sang im;
26 pui pa im pa yum rhawm ka aux khankix tag ang lai yum leeing ju.
Yum rhawm maix ka in khawm uik lie?”
27 Ma-tax pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
mawh tete!
Aux hoik yum rhawm ka mawh maix Khrit kaw pa tag sang hoek ka piang haktiex an,
pa mawh kawn simex Siyiex heue.”
Ye-sux yiiem
28 Ma-tax krai ka lox in tom ing ka nyiex,
mai ah lox iak krai ka pux o tix Ma-li-yax ah nin:
“Sara hoik hoek,
nawh kok ka maix.”
29 Ma-li-yax mhong lox in,
mai yaog tix kaoh hu ka Ye-sux dum mawx dum an. (
30 Yam an Ye-sux ang bax laig ka daux yaong,
mai ca kawn ot dee rieb Ma-tax ka nawh.)
31 Pui Yo-thaix pa hoek tix khu khrawm Ma-li-yax daux nyiex yaox ka nawh,
kix tom nied tix yaog tix hu hawt ka nawh,
mai kix ce nawh sang hu yiiem ka si meeig heue.
32 Ma-li-yax hoek ka Ye-sux dee an,
yaox ka nawh,
mai gup tix krub ka plak sivoe ngai nawh ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
phan maix ot dee in,
pux aik aux tag ang yaox tix yum heue!”
33 Ye-sux yaox yiiem Ma-li-yax,
mai yaox yiiem pui Yo-thaix pa hoek tau mai nawh nang,
nawh kied laux rhawm kah,
mai ngia ka daux rhawm tix tete,
34 nawh tom chawk kix ah nin:
“Si paung pex nawh dee mawx?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
hoek tix jhak heue.”
35 Ye-sux yiiem.
36 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yo-thaix ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
nawh kied muh pui ih tete heue!”
37 Koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Nawh hoik paoh ka ngai pui dug,
khex ang nawh pon kieh La-sa-lux ang yum lie?”
Kieh La-sa-lux deeh kaoh im
38 Ye-sux hoik ngia ka daux rhawm tix tete.
Nawh hoek ka sivoe si meeig;
si meeig an mawh daux rang,
moing daux si meeig hoik thox ka simaox.
39 Ye-sux tawx lox ah nin:
“Teei simaox in hu heue!”
Pux o pui yum an ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
nawh hoik yum koe pon singaix,
to kaux yum nawh hoik cux heue!”
40 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Ang aux hoik krai ka maix,
phan maix yum rhawm kah,
maix tag sang yaox ka min plak ngai Siyiex lie?”
41 Mawh kheu nan,
kix teei simaox hu.
Ye-sux maong tix jhak ka rao ma ah nin:
“Keeing eue,
aux yaog yaw ka maix,
mawh kheu hoik nghiet maix ka lox aux.
42 Aux hoik tawng grawng pa nghiet maix ka aux jheeng jheeng;
viang mawh nan,
aux krai ka lox in hoik mawh son pui pa ot sivie siveng in kix,
son sang kieh kix yum rhawm ka mawh maix pa ploi aux hoek.”
43 Khaix hoik krai nawh lox in,
nawh kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“La-sa-lux,
kaoh lih heue!”
44 Pui yum an tom kaoh lih;
taix caong nawh kie tix ka man,
pak ngai nawh kawx kie tix ka man.
Ye-sux tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Yah man khaing nawh,
kieh nawh hu heue!”
Krax keud tix pauh ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.1–5; Lai Ma-kheu 14.1–2; Lai Lu-cia 22.1–1)
45 Pui Yo-thaix pa hoek tix jhak ka Ma-li-yax hoik yaox ka can kaing yuh Ye-sux,
kix tom yum rhawm ka nawh.
46 Viang mawh nan kawx,
koe pui tix blah ing ka pui Fa-li-saix,
mai krai ka can kaing pa yuh Ye-sux ka kix.
47 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Fa-li-saix mai simiang cao giex kaing li khrawm paox tix bang kum pui Yo-thaix,
mai krai ka daux bang kum ah nin:
“Pui in hoik yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am,
sang yuh ex ka mawx lie?
48 Phan ex kieh nawh ca yuh nin,
pui hun yum rhawm ka nawh khawm uik,
pui Law-ma tag sang hoek tix tud rhim ka nyiex buan ex mai ka ceu pui ex heue!”
49 Ka daux kix koe pui tix kaux ceu kaux mawh Kai-ya-fa,
pa mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting yam an.
Nawh si kah ah nin:
“Ang pex tawng tid tix heue!
50 Kieh pui tix kaux yum tang pui hun,
son ang ceu pui khawm uik sang laux leeing.
Khex ang pex tawng kaing in hoik mawh pa mhawm ka pex lie?”
51 Tete te lai,
gum lox in ang kaoh khaing to kaux nawh.
Kheu hoik mawh nawh cao giex kaing li pa ting singaix yam an khaing,
nawh hoik grox krai grawng sang yum Ye-sux son pui Yo-thaix,
52 mai nawh ang joeng yum son kix,
pie kawn praix Siyiex kawx pa hoik prung prang tix ka piang haktiex ku dauh,
tag sang khrawm ot tau khawm uik,
mai kieh lih mawh pui tix cub heue.
53 Jauh bawg yam an,
simiang pui Yo-thaix keud tix sang pauh ka Ye-sux.
54 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ang kieh pui tawng kaing pa yuh tix ka haktiex Yo-thaix.
Nawh hoek ka haktiex pa dex ka daux tiag tix dauh,
pa kok mawh yaong Yi-fa-liien,
mai nawh ot dee an tau mai pui gau tix heue.
55 Bawg yam Poi Luat-luan cie pui Yo-thaix dex sang hoek.
Sivoe sang yuh poi,
koe pui tom nie kaoh khaing haktiex ot tix haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
son sang yuh ka been pa singax.
56 Kix hu sawg ka Ye-sux ku dauh ku dee;
yam khrawm kix tix ot ka daux nyiex buan,
kix ra lai chawk paox tix ah nin:
“Maix hoik keud mawh nin,
sang hoek ang nawh hoek yuh poi lie?”
57 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai pui Fa-li-saix hoik dawg tawx lox ah nin:
phan koe pui pa tawng Ye-sux ot dee mawx,
tag caw tix krai,
son sang kieh kix hu giex ka nawh heue.
