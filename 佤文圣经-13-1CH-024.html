Kaing yuh cao giex kaing li
24
Doeh ceu pui Ya-loeng guah tix lih koe miex cub mawx.
Ya-loeng koe kawn simex pon kaux pa mawh Na-ta,
Ya-pi-hux,
Yi-li-ya-sa,
Yi-tha-ma.
2 aNa-ta mai Ya-pi-hux yum sivoe keeing tix,
mai ang koe doeh ceu cad,
mawh kheu nan pux kix pa kaux Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yi-tha-ma mawh cao giex kaing li.
3 Simiang Ta-wue hawt kaing yuh doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng,
mai guah kix lih mawh cub in plak greem:
kieh doeh ceu cad Yi-li-ya-sa pa kaux Sa-tu mai doeh ceu cad Yi-tha-ma pa kaux Ya-si-mi-leux mawh cao teem Ta-wue yuh kaing in.
4 Doeh ceu pui Yi-li-ya-sa pa mawh simex cao kaing ceu pui kix kawn nie khaing Yi-tha-ma,
mawh kheu nan doeh ceu cad Yi-li-ya-sa tom guah tix lih mawh kao liah cub;
doeh ceu cad Yi-tha-ma guah tix lih mawh daix cub.
5 Viang mawh nan,
kaing pa yuh kix hoik guah lih khaing daux pa cawk lai,
mawh kheu doeh ceu cad Yi-li-ya-sa mai doeh ceu cad Yi-tha-ma koe pui pa kieh mawh cao kaing daux nyiex pa singax singeh mai krax ot krax koe ka daux khuan.
6 Doeh ceu pui Yi-li-ya-sa mai doeh ceu cad Yi-tha-ma lai loh tix cawk lai.
Yam an,
pui Li-we kawn simex Na-thang-yiex pa kaux See-ma-ya cao tiiem lai mawh cao pa teem coi tiiem rang ceu kaux kix;
simiang mai siliex simiang,
Sa-tu cao giex kaing li,
kawn simex Ya-pi-ya-tha pa kaux Ya-si-mi-leux,
ceu pui nyiex cao giex kaing li mai cao kaing ceu pui nyiex Li-we mawh pui cao kham sakse heue.

19 Pui in kix hoik hawt tix tiiem ka ca rang kix awm kaing pa yuh kix ka daux nyiex buan;
kaing in kix hoik mawh tax du kix Ya-loeng pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx pa ciak ci Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
Ca rang ceu kaux pui Li-we
20 Ca rang ceu kaux cao kaing ceu pui Li-we tang nyiex khankix tiiem ot plak greem:
Kawn praix Ang-lang doeh ceu cad Su-pa-yiex pa kaux Yie-si-ti-yax.
21 Doeh ceu pui Li-ha-pi-ya pa kaux Yi-see-ya.
22 Kawn praix Yi-see-ha doeh ceu cad See-law-maw pa kaux Ya-ha.
23 Kawn simex Si-paw-loeng pon kaux,
jauh ka pa ting hoek ka pa iak dia paox tix:
Yie-li-ya,
Ya-ma-li-yax,
Ya-ha-si,
Yie-cia-miien.
24 Kawn praix Wu-shiie doeh ceu cad Mi-cia pa kaux Sa-mi.
25 Kawn praix Wu-shiie doeh ceu cad Yi-see-ya (pux aik Mi-cia) pa kaux Sa-cia-li-ya heue.
26 Doeh ceu pui Mi-la-lix pa kaux Maw-lix,
Mu-seex,
mai Ya-si-ya.
27 Ya-si-ya koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Saw-hang,
Sa-kheux,
Yi-pi-lix.
28-29 Maw-lix koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Yi-li-ya-sa mai Ci-see.
Yi-li-ya-sa ang koe kawn simex;
Ci-see koe kawn simex tix kaux pa mawh Yie-la-miiex.
30 Mu-seex koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Maw-lix,
Yi-teu,
Yie-li-maw heue.
Plak piang mawh cub nyiex ceu pui Li-we heue.
31 Cub nyiex cao kaing ku kaux mai ka daux ceu pui kix pa mawh pui pa nhum ju tix kaux,
kix mawh cao cawk lai son kaing pa sang yuh kix,
awm kaing pa hoik yuh doeh ceu cad paox nyiex kix Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li yuh nan.
Simiang Ta-wue,
Sa-tu,
Ya-si-mi-leux,
cao giex kaing li mai cub nyiex cao kaing pui Li-we khankix hoik mawh pui cao pa kham sakse heue.
