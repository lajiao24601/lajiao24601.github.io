Ka daux phuk loe
(Lai Yaog Yaw 73–89)
Pa khaw pa caw cie Siyiex
73
Siyiex hoik chix kia chix ngien ka pui Yi-seu-liex tete,
mai ka pui pa singax singeh daux rhawm daux rhi khankix.
2 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik dex sang khluk khlaik caong tix goh,
mai caong aux hoik dex sang cang klad;
3 mawh kheu boh rhawm aux ka grawng pa haok huan pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
yam yaox pa haok huan pui pa caox pa caig,
rhawm aux ang nyawm ot nyawm koe ka heue.
 
4 Kix ang bax si pup ka krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi;
mai mhawm kaux hak mai koe riang koe rao.
5 Kix ang yaox tix kha rhawm kha rhi awm pui tang kaux;
mai ang si pup krax laux krax leeing awm pui tang kaux.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
kix kieh pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tix mawh soi pa tauk ka ngawg tix;
kieh krax pa tud pa rhim tix mawh kraung dha pa ceep.
7 Rhawm rhi kix lih ka krax pa caox pa caig;
mai krax keud krax sawn kix hoik naog ka pa pie lhien.
8 Kix nyiah ka pui tang kaux khankix,
mai krai lox caox lox caig;
kix ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai keud sawn tix sang gut kheem ka pui tang kaux.
9 Kix salao ka Siyiex piang rao ma,
mai nieb pon yuh ka paox kawn pui tix piang haktiex,
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pie pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex kawx hoik veeh tix hu ka kix,
mai yum rhawm ka lox pa krai kix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix khawm uik.
11 Kix ah nin:
Siyiex ang doh tawng;
cao pa ting siawp sibrawm ang doh chix yaox.
12 Pui caox pui caig hoik mawh yuh nin;
kix hoik mag mi,
mai ca kawn koe rhawm ghoing ang jud heue.
 
13 Phan mawh nan,
aux priex rhawm rhi tix,
ang pon yuh phi map,
hoik mawh pa giex pa going lie?
14 Siyiex eue,
maix hoik kieh aux kha rhawm rhi tix leeing lang singaix;
pon ngawb cao ku singaix,
maix hoik ciyang ka aux heue.
 
15 Phan aux kraix lox pa yuh nin,
aux hoik mawh pui cie maix pa ang kaux sijax.
16 Aux sau riang tix si kaw tix tawng ka kaing in,
mai ang lai tawng ka sang yuh tix;
17 Hoek yam laig aux ka daux nyiex buan maix,
aux tom taug tawng ka krax pa si dauh cie pui pa caox pa caig heue.
 
18 Maix tag sang deuh kix ot ka dee plang dee nyu;
maix tag sang kieh kix si blat tix yum.
19 Kix hoik laux leeing ka daux tix khiep ngai khaing;
kix hoik krix ka daux dee pa si dauh pa lhat pa graung.
20 Kix hoik awm yam ca maox pui yuh nan,
mai grai hu yam hoik riang praix pon ngawb;
simiang eue,
yam hoik haing maix,
mai kix hoik yeet hu heue.
 
21 Rhawm rhi aux hoik dawg ang nyawm ot nyawm koe,
mai rhawm rhi aux hoik laux,
22 Aux hoik daox aw awm to sat yuh nan;
aux ang pon tawng ka maix sidawng.
23 Kheu nan,
aux ca kawn ot tau mai maix;
maix teei tawm taix tix joh rien ka aux ang jud.
24 Maix teei krax pa sidiex gau tix thung ka aux,
dee si dauh kawn sang rieb ka aux,
mai kieh aux pon pa riang min plak ngai.
25 Lheu khaing maix,
ka piang rao ma aux kawn sang koe pui mawx lie?
Kheu pa hoik koe maix,
ka piang haktiex aux kawn sang lo pui mawx lie?
26 Rhawm rhi to kaux aux hoik chix iak riang iak rao;
viang mawh nan,
Siyiex mawh riang rao ka aux,
hoik mawh pa lo aux leeing ju leeing phan khawm uik heue.
 
27 Pui pa tik vud ka maix khankix,
tag sang yum tete;
can pui pa ang nghiet ka lox maix khawm uik,
maix tag sang yuh kix laux leeing.
28 Phan mawh aux,
aux hoik pon yaox tix dex tau mai Siyiex kied sidaing mhawm heue!
Aux hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong mawh nyiex dee mox ka aux;
aux tag sang krai pao ka kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik heue.
