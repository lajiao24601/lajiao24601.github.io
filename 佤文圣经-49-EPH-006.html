Grawh grawng miex keeing mai kawn praix
6
bPa mawh kawn simex kawn bun khankix,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox miex keeing tix;
in hoik mawh pa khaw kawn Khrita tix yuh.
2-3 c“Tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka miex keeing tix,
son sang kieh kaing yuh maix mhawm ku ceu,
yaox tix lang ju ka piang haktiex.”
In mawh grawng tix pa koe ka daux gum lox ping nyap heue.
4 dPa mawh keeing khankix,
pex paw vi cheeik tix kieh kawn tix song rhawm,
tag caw tix teei gum lox cie Simiang Siyiex sidiex gau kix mhawm mhawm heue.
Grawh grawng simiang mai siliex
5 ePa mawh siliex khankix,
pex tag caw tix koe krax lhat krax graung,
mai viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh son simiang ka piang haktiex,
awm hoik yuh ka son Khrit yuh nan.
6 Ang joeng yuh ngie nin yam yaox kix ka pex khaing,
son sang kieh tix pon krax pa khaw rhawm nawh,
kheu nan yam mawh pex siliex Khrit,
tag caw priex tix hawt ka alo Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
7 Pex tag caw gaux rhawm tix sau riang yuh ka ceu pa khaw siliex tix yuh,
awm hoik yuh ka son Simiang Siyiex yuh nan,
ang mawh pa yuh ka son kawn pui.
8 Tag caw tix tawng,
mawh siliex ang ah,
mawh pui pa pon tix bheeik luan ang ah,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang hawt tix pawk bix awm kaing pa mhawm pa yuh kaux pui mawx kaux pui an heue.
9 fPa mawh simiang khankix,
pex kawx tag caw tix chix kia ngien ka siliex tix mhawm nang,
paw vi ghlat ka kix.
Tag caw tix tawng,
pex mai siliex pex hoik rom tix koe simiang tix kaux ka piang rao ma;
nawh tag sang siao ka diag tong pa awm paox tix,
hoek tix yuh ka pui ku kaux heue.
Kraung naing pa tawx Siyiex
10 Dee nghoik,
pex tag caw tix een rhawm tix ka siawp sibrawm pa ting cie Simiang Siyiex,
mai mawh pui pa kua khankix.
11 gPex tag caw tix ceep ka kraung naing pa tawx Siyiex,
son sang pon cung pex kaux cong,
mai pon tieb tie ka krax keud pa pie lhien cie phi caox.
12 Mawh kheu ang ex tieb tie ka pui pa koe nex koe nham khankix,
mai hoik mawh pa tieb tie ka khuan phi caox pa koe ka rao ma,
pa mawh pa yuh naing ka simiang,
cao giex kaing ka daux singaix bawg yam pa faik in,
mai ka sibrawm pa caox caig ka daux rao ma.
13 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix rien khre to kaux tix ka kraung naing pa tawx Siyiex,
son sang pon tieb tie pex ka pa puing pui koeng pex ka daux singaix pa caox caig tom si dauh,
kheu nan mai pon pau kaux cong ka bang naing tom si dauh heue.
14 hMawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix rien khre tix mhawm.
Tag caw tix teei pa mawh pa meh kieh mawh maox nyoi,
teei pa rauh pa see kieh mawh dieb tie naok,
15 iteei pa sidiex ka gum lox buan ku bawg yam mai pa nhim kieh mawh kiiep pa ceep.
16 Tag caw teei tix viex ka dieb tie naok krax yum rhawm jheeng jheeng,
son sang pon tieb tie ka te pa puing pui caox an khawm uik.
17 jPex tag caw tix teei krax taoh kieh mawh mhok rhiem,
teei gum lox Siyiex kieh mawh vaig lang pa tawx Khuan Singax Singeh.
18 Pex tag caw tix khiox khaw krax teem coi Siyiex ka daux krax rawg buan,
mai rawg buan ot ka daux krax pa duk thung Khuan Singax Singeh jheeng jheeng.
Tag caw tix si duad ka daux kaing ku ceu,
ang pon bhla rhawm tix;
tag caw tix rawg buan son kawn Khrit khankix ang jud.
19 Tag caw tix rawg buan son aux nang,
khiox khaw Siyiex tawx lox pa chix si kah ka aux liak,
yam khaw aux tix krai,
kieh aux pon viex rhawm ting ang jheeh tix sidiex ka gum lox buan pa raux pa mox.
20 Viang aux hoik viex ka maox ceng,
aux hoik mawh kawn cau pa ting pa yuh son gum lox buan.
Pex tag caw tix khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex tawx aux koe rhawm kua tix pon krai ka lox pa khaw aux tix krai heue.
Gum lox pa chawk mhawm ot mai pa nawb buan
21 klThoe-ci-ku tag sang krai pex mhong grawng kaing ceu pa dex mai aux khawm uik,
kieh pex tawng grawh grawng aux.
Nawh mawh pux aik pa muh ex,
pa kaux sijax ka daux kaing jhaw cie Simiang Siyiex tix kaux.
22 Aux ploi nawh hu ka pex dee an,
kaing ting mawh son sang kieh pex tawng grawng pa ot yix dee in,
son sang kieh pex pon krax pa joh jhan.
23 Kieh keeing Siyiex Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit tawx pa nhim,
krax muh krax rhawm,
mai krax yum rhawm ka can kawn Khrit pa koe khawm uik.
24 Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son ka can pui pa viex khawm rhawm tix muh ka Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue!
