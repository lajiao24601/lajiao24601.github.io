Been veng mox
(Lai Simin-pui 35.9–28; Lai Yaw-su-yax 20.1–9)
19
“Khaix hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
ka ceu pui haktiex pa sang tawx nawh ka pex an laux leeing,
son sang kieh pex cax ka haktiex kix,
nyiex ma kix,
mai paung ot ka dee an.
2-3 Pex tag caw tix guah ka haktiex khawm uik koe loe dauh,
tix dauh sum veng son pui lai ling ot ka tix mu,
son sang mawh dee to pui pa lud loe tix pauh ka pui,
khiox khaw mox tix ot ka dee an.
4 Phan koe pui pa lud loe tix pauh ka pui,
mai nawh ang tie sax phit lud ka pui an,
nawh chix tix to hoek mox tix ka veng mox an kix ku veng,
son sang yaox tix im ju.
5 Tiag ex tix yaox yuh nin,
koe pui tix kaux kaux,
hu gu khaox tau mai paox plawg paox nyawm tix ka daux tiag,
yam gu ka khaox,
moe cie nawh goh khaing blax,
mai phlu cawd ka piang to kaux paox plawg paox nyawm nawh,
mai kieh nawh yum,
pui in chix tix to hoek mox tix ka veng mox an ku veng.
6 Phan koe ngie tix mu veng mox khaing,
mai dee ot veng an kied singai,
cao pui nyiex pa sawg tix pawk bix an,
tag leue krub ka nawh,
mai pauh nawh ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
Phan ex ah tete,
pui an ang tie su tix pauh ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix,
pa ang tie sax koe see pa tid tix tau mai nawh,
ang khaw tix nyang kieh nawh yum.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tawx lox ka pex,
tag caw tix sum veng mox koe loe veng heue.
8 “Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
hoik hawt awm lox pa ap ka tax du pex,
mai vih ka mee haktiex pex khankix vieh va,
mai khaix hoik tawx nawh haktiex pa hoik yuh nawh sijax mukheu son sang tawx ka pex khankix,
9 pex tag caw tix baox thiem thie tix yuh ka veng mox loe mu.
(Phan pex priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap pa tawm aux ka pex daux in singaix khawm uik,
mai muh ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
priex tix hawt ka daux krax pa gau nawh,
nawh tom tawx haktiex in ka pex heue.)
10 Pex tag caw tix yuh yuh nin,
son ang pui pa ang koe dee lud sang yaox tix yum;
mai pex kawx ang lai siao klawm ka nham pa jauh pui ang koe dee lud ka daux haktiex pa sang tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pex heue.
11 “Viang mawh nan,
phan koe pui hoik dawg koe rhawm koeng,
mai su tix pauh ka paox plawg paox nyhawm tix,
mai to hu mox tix ka daux veng mox an tix veng,
12 pa mawh kuad song pa ot ka daux veng ot pui an,
tag caw tix hu giex nawh hoek,
mai tawx ka pui nyiex pui ma pa pawk bix ka nham pui an,
mai pauh nawh yum.
13 Pex tag ang pon tix koe krax pa caox rhawm ka pui pa yuh nan,
tag caw tix teei tix tik pui pa pauh pui yum ka daux krax pa yuh nan khaing ka daux Yi-seu-liex,
mai kaing pa yuh pex tom tag yaox tix pon mhawm ku ceu heue.”
Been mee haktiex pa prim
14 a“Pex ot ka daux haktiex pa sang tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex ka pex,
ang pon tix gah ka mee haktiex paox plawg paox nyhawm tix;
kheu an hoik mawh mee pa mhai pui ju sivoe an dix dix heue.”
Been pui pa kham sakse
15 b“Pa kham sakse pui tix kaux,
ang nyang kub tix pon ciyang ka phi map pui;
ah yawm tix koe ra kaux haok plak piang,
tom tag chix tix pon ciyang ka phi map pui.
16 Phan koe pui tix kaux kaux,
pa kham sakse pa pie lhien,
mai kieh pui tang kaux laux,
17 pui in kiex khawm ra kaux tag caw tix hoek ka dee pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex pui krub,
mai khiox khaw ka cao giex kaing li pa yuh ka kaing,
mai cao prawng see khankix prawng.
18 Cao prawng see tag sang jawg tix si kaw ka grawng see an,
phan tawng sakse pa kham pui an mawh pa pie lhien,
mai kieh paox ceu paox cad tix pui Yi-seu-liex laux,
19 Pui an tag caw tix kham krax pa ciyang ka son pa yuh nawh ka pux aik tix.
Yuh nin,
pex tom tag pon teei tix tik ka kaing pa caox pa caig an.
20 Pui tang kaux mhong,
tag sang chix lhat kah,
mai tag ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon yuh kaing pa caox pa caig an.
21 cPhan mawh son pui pa yuh nan,
pex tag ang khaw tix lai koe krax pa caox rhawm caox rhi kah,
tag caw tix teei ju plud ju,
ngai son ngai,
rhang son rhang,
taix son taix,
caong son caong.”
