Jhawm saux simiang Si-si-cia mai grawng hoik moi
(Lai Yi-sai-yax 38.1–8, 21–11; Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 32.24–26)
20
Ka daux bawg yam in,
simiang Si-si-cia saux,
mai dex sang yum.
Yi-sai-yax kawn simex Ya-maw-see hoek ka simiang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Tag caw tix yuh kaing daux nyiex ma maix mhawm,
mawh kheu ang jhawm saux maix lai chix moi;
rien khre maix sivoe sang yum tix heue.’ ”
2 Si-si-cia veeh pak ngai tix plak guh ding,
mai rawg buan ah nin:
3 “Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka grawng pa hoik mawh aux pui rauh pui see tom si dauh,
aux hoik viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh kaing jhaw maix,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa khaw caw ka plak sivoe ngai maix.”
Nawh ang pon gun mai ploi lox yiiem tix heue.
4 Yi-sai-yax taug khraox lih hoek khaing dee ot simiang tan,
sivoe sang hu nawh ka daux sigreung nyiex haw simiang,
Simiang Siyiex nhie nawh
5 deeh ing ka simiang kawn pui cie tix pa mawh Si-si-cia dee an.
Khaix an,
tom ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex tax du maix Ta-wue hoik mhong ka lox rawg maix buan,
mai hoik yaox nang ka rawm ngai maix.
Aux sang pai maix moi;
ka daux loe singaix,
maix tag pon yaox tix haok ka nyiex buan.
6 Aux kawn sang kieh maix baox im ju kao phuan num,
mai sang taoh maix mai veng Yie-lu-sa-leung luat luan khaing tawm taix Vawng Ya-sux.
Kheu hoik mawh son aux,
mai son siliex aux Ta-wue,
aux sang hoek tix priex pau ka veng in heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-sai-yax krai ka siliex simiang,
tag caw tix teei bee plix ruh kraix ka dee muad simiang,
nawh tom taug moi ka heue.
8 Si-si-cia chawk ah nin:
“Koe mhai sakse ceu pa tix son sang pai Simiang Siyiex aux moi,
khaix an ka daux loe singaix,
mai aux tag pon yaox tix haok nyiex buan lie?”
9 Yi-sai-yax ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex sang tawx mhai sakse ka maix,
son sang kham sakse lih mawh lox pa ap nawh tix deuh.
Mawh sang kieh maix veei pang peue riang singaix hu plak sivoe kao diang,
phan ang mawh nan deeh plak khaix kao diang lie?”
10 Si-si-cia ah nin:
“Veei pang peue riang singaix hu plak sivoe kao diang mawh pa yie,
aux sang kieh nawh deeh plak khaix tix kao diang heue!”
11 Yi-sai-yax rawg buan ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex tom kieh veei pang peue riang singaix deeh khaing piang glawk bawng simiang Ya-ha-see kao diang heue.
Kawn cau pa hoek khaing Pa-pi-loeng
(Lai Yi-sai-yax 39.1–8)
12 Ka daux bawg yam in,
kawn simex Pa-la-tang simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Mi-law-ta Pa-la-tang hoik mhong grawng pa saux simiang Si-si-cia,
tom tawx lai pa chawk nawh mhawm ot mai khawng phak tix ceu ka nawh.
13 Si-si-cia rieb ka kawn cau pa ting,
mai tawx kix jhak ka sin ngao tix,
pa mawh khri,
mau,
sidah bhawng,
rawm bhawng,
mai kraung naing khawm uik;
nawh tawx kix jhak ka nyiex krao meung haktiex nawh ku dauh,
mai can pa koe nawh khawm uik.
14 Mawh kheu nan,
cao jhai Yi-sai-yax hoek ka simiang Si-si-cia,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Pui in kix kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?
Krai kix lox ceu pa tix ka maix lie?”
Si-si-cia pawk lox ah nin:
“Kix kaoh khaing meung Pa-pi-loeng pa kied singai heue.”
15 Yi-sai-yax chawk ah nin:
“Yaox kix ceu pa tix ka daux nyiex haw simiang lie?”
Si-si-cia pawk lox ah nin:
“Kix hoik yaox ku ceu khawm uik;
aux hoik tawx kix jhak kraung ceu pa koe ka daux nyiex krao ku ceu heue.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
Yi-sai-yax ah nin ka simiang:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
17 a‘Bawg yam hoik dex hoek,
kraung ka daux nyiex haw simiang ku ceu,
pa mawh kraung pa hoik deuh tax du maix ku ceu,
tag caw tix gah hu ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh koe tix ceu kawx heue.
18 bPie doeh ceu cad maix kawx tag sang giex kix hu,
kieh kix yuh pui sieh ka daux nyiex haw simiang Pa-pi-loeng,
mai yuh kaing son simiang kix heue.’ ”
19 Si-si-cia keud gum lox in mawh pa krai grawng sang pon krax pa nhim mai krax pa nyawm ot,
yam kawn im ju tix ka piang haktiex,
mawh kheu nan nawh pawk lox ah nin:
“Gum lox pa krai maix pa kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex kied mhawm heue.”
Dee si dauh yam giex Si-si-cia kaing
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Ra 32.32–33)
20 Kaing yuh simiang Si-si-cia tang ceu khankix,
grawng pa kied kua nawh mai pa sum nawh dung rawm,
kaong nawh ka rawng pa viex rawm laig daux veng khankix,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yo-tax khawm uik.
21 Si-si-cia hoik yum,
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Ma-na-si plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
