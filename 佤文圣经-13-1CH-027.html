Cub caw kru naing mai kaing son pui haktiex khankix
27
Plak greem mawh cub pui nyiex cao kaing pui Yi-seu-liex pa thung pui kaing rheng mai simiang naing kaing yieh kaux,
khawm ca rang cao kaing pa yuh kaing son piang meung haktiex khankix.
Kix ku num lai loh tix pau nyiex ku khix,
tix cub hoik kieh cao bung kaing koe tix kaux;
tix cub koe ra mheen pon rheng kaux heue.
2-15 Ca rang ceu kaux simiang pa pau nyiex ku khix tiiem ot plak greem:
Khix tix:
mawh kawn simex Sa-pa-ti-yiex pa kaux Ya-saw-pang (nawh mawh ceu cub pui Fa-leu-see khaing ka daux ceu pui Yo-tax tix kaux).
Khix ra:
mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-ha-si pa kaux Taw-taix (Mi-ci-lawx mawh cao pa teem coi nawh).
Khix loe:
mawh kawn simex cao giex kaing li Yie-heu-yie-tax pa kaux Pi-na-ya,
nawh mawh cao kaing ka daux “Pui pa kied kua tix ngoe kaux” (kawn simex nawh pa kaux Ang-mi-sa-pa plud tix mawh cao kaing cub caw in).
Khix pon:
mawh pux Yaw-ya pa kaux Ya-sa-he (kawn simex nawh pa kaux Si-pa-ti-ya plud tix mawh cao kaing cub caw in).
Khix phuan:
mawh doeh ceu cad Yi-see-la pa kaux Sang-heux.
Khix liah:
mawh pui Thi-keu-yax kawn simex Yi-ci pa kaux Yi-la.
Khix aliah:
mawh ceu pui Yi-fa-liien pui Pi-loeng pa kaux Si-li-see.
Khix daix:
mawh pui Hu-sa pa kaux Si-pi-kai (nawh mawh ceu pui cub nyiex Shie-la khaing ka daux ceu pui Yo-tax tix kaux).
Khix dim:
mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming pui Ya-na-thu pa kaux Ya-pi-yi-shiex.
Khix kao:
mawh pui Ni-thaw-fax pa kaux Ma-ha-lai (nawh mawh ceu pui cub nyiex Shie-la tix kaux).
Khix kao tix:
mawh ceu pui Yi-fa-liien pui Pi-la-toeng pa kaux Pi-na-ya.
Khix kao ra:
mawh pui Ni-thaw-fax pa kaux He-taix (nawh mawh doeh ceu cad Eu-thaw-niiex).
Cao kaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku cub
16-22 Plak greem mawh ca rang simiang naing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku cub:
ceu pui Lui-piien:
mawh kawn simex Si-ci-lix pa kaux Yi-li-yi-shiex
ceu pui Si-miien:
mawh kawn simex Ma-cia pa kaux See-fa-thi-ya
ceu pui Li-we:
mawh kawn simex Ci-mu-lix pa kaux Ha-sa-pi-ya
ceu pui Ya-loeng:
mawh Sa-tu.
Ceu pui Yo-tax:
mawh pux ke simiang Ta-wue pa kaux Yi-li-hux
ceu pui Yi-sa-cia:
mawh kawn simex Mi-cia-leux pa kaux Ang-lix
ceu pui Si-pu-loeng:
mawh kawn simex Eu-pa-ti-ya pa kaux Yi-see-ma-ya.
Ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix:
mawh kawn simex Ya-see-liex pa kaux Yie-li-maw
ceu pui Yi-fa-liien:
mawh kawn simex A-sha-si-ya pa kaux Heu-si-yax
ceu pui Ma-na-si plak klip singaix:
mawh kawn simex Pi-ta-ya pa kaux Yaw-eux.
Ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singax:
mawh kawn simex Sa-cia-li-yax pa kaux Yi-to
ceu pui Piien-ya-ming:
kawn simex Ya-ni-eu pa kaux Ya-si-yiex
ceu pui Tang:
mawh kawn simex Yie-law-hang pa kaux Ya-sa-liex.
23 aSimiang Ta-wue ang bax si kaw tix yaox ka pui pa koe tix nga num lih plak greem,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik dawg ap tix deuh,
tag sang kieh kaux pui Yi-seu-liex haoh hun awm simuing ka daux rao ma yuh nan.
24 bYaw-ya jauh (miex nawh mawh Si-lu-ya) tix si kaw ka ca rang pui khankix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax si kaw grum uik.
Siyiex tom ciyang pui Yi-seu-liex,
kheu pa si kaw nawh ka ca rang in,
mawh kheu nan,
kaux pui khawm uik ang bax tiiem tix ot ka daux lai ca rang cie cao kaing pa ot plak simiang Ta-wue heue.
Cao kaing pa kuang sin ngao daux nyiex simiang
25-31 Plak greem mawh ca rang cao kaing pa kuang sin ngao daux nyiex simiang:
Cao kuang daux nyiex krao simiang:
mawh kawn simex Ya-tiie pa kaux Ya-see-we.
Cao kuang daux nyiex krao haktiex tang dauh:
mawh kawn simex Wu-si-ya pa kaux Yaw-na-tang.
Cao kuang pui yuh kaing daux kaung ma:
mawh kawn simex Ci-luix pa kaux Yi-see-lix.
Cao kuang si peem plix siiet:
mawh pui La-max pa kaux See-me.
Cao kuang nghaung plai mawh pui Seu-fu-mi pa kaux Sa-pa-ti.
Cao kuang khaox sime mai khaox liah (pa ot ka caong gawng plak klip singaix):
mawh pui Ci-ti-lix pa kaux Pa-leu Ha-nang.
Cao kuang krao bee sime:
mawh Yaw-a-see.
Cao kuang phung moi ka dee lung Sa-loeng:
mawh pui Sa-long pa kaux See-thi-laix.
Cao kuang phung moi ka daux ngawd:
mawh kawn simex Ya-ti-lai pa kaux Sa-fa.
Cao kuang phung ka-la-uk:
mawh pui Yi-seu-ma-lix pa kaux A-pi-leu.
Cao kuang phung la:
mawh pui Mi-loeng pa kaux Yie-si-ti-yax.
Cao kuang phung yung:
mawh pui Sa-cia pa kaux Ya-si.
Cao pa teem coi lawng kaux simiang Ta-wue
32 Keeing iak simiang Ta-wue pa kaux Yaw-na-tang mawh pui teem coi pa lhak tix kaux,
nawh mawh nang pui pa lhaong ngai lai.
Kawn simex Ha-maw-ni pa kaux Yie-shiie mawh sara kawn simiang khankix.
33 Ya-si-to-fu mawh cao pa teem coi simiang.
Pui Ya-ci pa kaux Hu-sai mawh paox grawm simiang mai pui pa kied yum rhawm nawh.
34 Khaix hoik yum Ya-si-to-fu,
Ya-pi-ya-tha mai kawn simex Pi-na-ya pa kaux Yie-heu-yie-tax mawh cao teem.
Yaw-ya mawh simiang naing cie nyiex simiang heue.
