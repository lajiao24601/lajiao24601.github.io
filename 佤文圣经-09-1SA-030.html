Ta-wue puing ka pui Ya-ma-lix
30
Khaix hoik luan ra singaix,
Ta-wue viex ka pui cie tix deeh ing ka Si-keu-la.
Pui Ya-ma-lix hoik pon laig cam ka daog plak seh Yo-tax,
mai pon ka Si-keu-la.
Kix gleeing ka veng laux,
2 mai giex ka can bun miex,
mawh pa iak min pa ting min ang ah hoik giex hu khawm uik,
viang mawh nan,
ang pauh yum tix kaux kawx.
Yam hu kix,
mai hoik viex pui khawm uik hu.
3 Yam hoik hoek Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh khankix,
kix yaox grawng pa hoik gleeing veng,
moeng kix kawn simex kawn bun kix hoik viex hu khawm uik,
4 mai kix ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting,
mai yiiem tom hoik uik riang rao tix.
5 aMoeng Ta-wue khawm ra kaux pa kaux Ya-si-nuang mai Ai-pi-kai kawx hoik giex hu heue.
6 Yam in Ta-wue kied koe krax pa khi rhawm khi rhi.
Mawh kheu hoik grai kawn simex kawn bun pui pa ot greem nawh khankix,
mai rhawm kix kied laux tete,
kix tom ghlat Ta-wue,
mai teei simaox sang tap nawh yum kah.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex nawh tawx nawh koe riang rao.
7 bTa-wue ah nin ka kawn simex Ya-si-mi-leux cao giex kaing li pa kaux Ya-pi-ya-tha:
“Viex Yi-fu-teu hoek tin!”
Ya-pi-ya-tha tom hawt lox pa tawm nawh heue.
8 Ta-wue chawk ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang khaw ang aux khaw tix hu leue ka pui pa rhim an kix?
Aux kawn sang pon krup ka kix lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Maix hu leue ka kix heue.
Maix kawn sang pon krup ka kix,
mai deeh pon taoh ka pui pa hoik giex kix hu heue.”
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh liah yieh kaux leue kah,
hoek ka klawng iak Pi-saw.
Koe pui tix blah kaog ot dee an.
10 Ta-wue viex pui pon yieh kaux ca nied tix hu krax,
kheu pui pa deuk khankix ra yieh kaux hoik uik riang,
mai ang lai pon tiang ka rawm klawng iak Pi-saw,
kix tom kaog ot plak khaix.
11 Pui cie Ta-wue yaox ka kawn nyawm pui Ai-cix tix kaux pa ot ka daux tiag daux tu,
tom viex nawh ing ka Ta-wue.
Kix tawx nawh ih priex mai tawx nawh nyaux rawm,
12 mai tawx nawh ih nang khao mun plix ruh mai plix siiet kroh ra jieh.
Khaix hoik ih nawh kah,
mai riang nawh deeh lih koe.
Nawh ang yaox tix ih ang yaox tix nyaux loe singaix loe sawm.
13 Ta-wue chawk nawh ah nin:
“Simiang maix mawh mawx?
Kaoh maix khaing dee mawx?”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Aux mawh pui Ai-cix,
pa mawh siliex cawng pui Ya-ma-lix tix kaux.
Simiang aux sivoe an loe singaix hoik tik aux dee in,
mawh kheu hoik koe jhawm saux aux.
14 Yix hoik rhim tix teei ka haktiex Yo-tax plak seh,
pa mawh haktiex Ci-li-thix mai haktiex ceu pui Cia-leux,
mai hoik gleeing ka veng Si-keu-la heue.”
15 Ta-wue chawk nawh ah nin:
“Cu ang maix cu tix thung yix hu leue ka pui pa tud rhim an kix?”
Nawh ah nin:
“Phan maix tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Siyiex ang pauh ka aux,
mai ang deeh tawx aux ka simiang aux,
aux cu tix thung pex hu.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom thung Ta-wue leue ka kix heue.
Pui tud pui rhim an kix prung prang tix tauh hu ku dauh ku dee,
mai ih nyaux yuh krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
mawh kheu hoik pon tud kix kraung tom nie khaing daux pui Fe-li-see mai pui Yo-tax.
17 Ngawb ngaix khaix cao cao,
Ta-wue puing ka kix hoek tom pon bo.
Lheu khaing pui nhum ju pa brug ka-la-uk pon yieh kaux pa pon to,
mai ang lai koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tix luat luan.
18 Ta-wue pon deeh teei ka can pa tud pui Ya-ma-lix hu khawm uik,
khawm moeng nawh ra kaux.
19 Ang koe tix ceu kawx pa hoik tik grai.
Ta-wue hoik pon taoh ka can bun pui cie tix khawm uik,
mai deeh pon teei ka sin ngao pa hoik tud pui Ya-ma-lix,
20 mai khawm can phung yung phung moi kawx hoik deeh pon teei kah.
Pui cie nawh khankix grheu ka khawng brie hu plak sivoe,
mai ah nin:
“In kix hoik mawh cie Ta-wue khawm uik heue!”
21 Ang rheeng,
Ta-wue deeh hoek ka dee ot pui an kix ra yieh kaux tan.
Pui in kix hoik taug mao mai ang lai pon hu tau mai nawh,
mai ot ka praok rawm klawng iak Pi-saw.
Kix haok rieb ka Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh.
Ta-wue kawx gaux rhawm mai kuan rieb tix chawk kix mhawm ot.
22 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui tix blah pa hu tau mai Ta-wue,
mawh pui pa cau nyhau mai pui vu vie.
Kix ah nin:
“Pui in kix ang bax hu tau mai ex,
mai ex ang pon tix guah kix cie sin ngao pa hoik pon teei ex hoek.
Nhie kix viex moeng tix,
kawn simex kawn bun tix hu heue!”
23 Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue ah nin:
“Pux aik eue,
ex ang pon tix yuh nin ka pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka ex.
Nawh kia ngien ka ex,
mai hoik kieh ex pon pex ka pui pa koeng khankix.
24 Pui tix kaux kawx ang chix cu ka lox pex!
Can ceu pa koe khawm uik,
pui ku kaux tag sang koe nyum cie,
pui pa pau bang mai pui pa hu puing naing tag caw tix guah khaw paox.”
25 Ta-wue tat tix deuh ka been in plak greem;
been in ot ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex hoik hawt ka tom si dauh heue.
26 Khaix hoik deeh ing Ta-wue ka Si-keu-la,
mai guah tix tawx sin ngao pa pon teei tix tix blah ka paox grawm tix,
pa mawh simiang ceu pui Yo-tax khankix,
mai kawn baox tawx lox ah nin:
“Khawng phak lak sawng in ceeix,
hoik mawh pa pon tud yix khaing pui pa koeng Simiang Siyiex ka khankix dee an.”
27 Ta-wue tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka pui Paw-theu-lix,
pui La-mawx plak seh Yo-tax,
ka pui Ya-thi-eu,
28 ka pui Ya-law-eu,
ka pui Si-mawx,
ka pui Yi-seu-thi-mawx,
29 ka pui La-ha-leux ku veng,
mai tawx nang ka ceu pui Yie-la-miiex mai ceu pui Ci-ni ku veng khankix,
30 mai baox tawx nang ka pa ot ka Heu-eu-ma,
Keu-la-sang,
Ya-tax,
31 mai Si-paw-loeng ku veng khankix.
Can haktiex dee pa hoik laig Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh ku dauh,
nawh hoik guah tix tawx khawng phak lak sawng ka kix heue.
