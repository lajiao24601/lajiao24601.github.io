Lai Cao-prawng
Ceu pui Yo-tax mai ceu pui Si-miien pon giex ka Ya-to-ni Pi-seux
1
Khaix hoik yum Yaw-su-yax,
pui Yi-seu-liex khiox khaw chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ka daux yix mawh ceu pui mawx pa sang caw tix ran haok puing ka ceu pui Cia-nang sivoe lie?”
2 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Ceu pui Yo-tax pa ran hu sivoe.
Aux tag sang ap haktiex an ka daux tawm taix kix.”
3 Ceu pui Yo-tax ah nin ka pux aik kix ceu pui Si-miien:
“Hu tau mai yix ka daux haktiex pa hoik guah tix tawx ka ex,
son sang pon kuang tau ex ka pui Cia-nang,
khaix an,
ex tom hu tau mai pex ka daux haktiex pa hoik guah tix tawx ka pex.”
Khaix an,
ceu pui Si-miien mai
4 ceu pui Yo-tax tom rom tix hu puing naing tau;
Simiang Siyiex ap tix tawx pui Cia-nang,
khawm pui Pi-li-si ka daux tawm taix kix.
Kix pauh pui tix mheen ka daux veng Pi-seux,
5 kix yaox Ya-to-ni Pi-seux dee an,
tom yuh naing ka nawh.
6 Ya-to-ni Pi-seux hoik to;
kix leue ka nawh,
mai pon giex ka nawh,
tom tat ka giien ting taix caong nawh.
7 Ya-to-ni Pi-seux ah nin:
“Aux hoik tat giien ting taix caong simiang atigliah kaux;
kix hawt sut ka priex preem pa ot greem pheen aux khankix.
Yam in Siyiex hoik deeh pawk bix ka aux awm pa hoik yuh aux.”
Kix viex nawh hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai nawh yum tan heue.
Ceu pui Yo-tax pon kuang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung mai Si-paw-loeng
8 Ceu pui Yo-tax puing ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Kix pon ka veng an,
pauh ka pui pa ot daux veng,
mai gleeing veng ka ngu.
9 Khaix an,
kix baox hu puing ka pui pa ot ka piang gawng mai caong gawng,
khawm pui Cia-nang pa ot plak seh dee kroh haktiex.
10 Kix kawx puing ka pui Cia-nang pa ot ka daux veng Si-paw-loeng (ceu kaux Si-paw-loeng sivoe an mawh Ci-lie Ya-pa),
kix pex ka ceu pui See-sai,
ceu pui Ya-si-mang,
mai ceu pui Ta-mai.
Eu-thaw-niiex pon kuang ka veng Ti-pix
(Lai Yaw-su-yax 15.13–19)
11 Ceu pui Yo-tax jauh ka dee an mai puing ka veng Ti-pix;
yam an,
Ti-pix kok mawh Ci-lie Si-fu;
12 Ceu pui Yo-tax Cia-leux ah nin:
“Pui mawx pa pon puing ka veng Ci-lie Si-fu,
aux tag tawx nawh moeng kawn bun tix Ya-sa.”
13 Eu-thaw-niiex kawn simex Ci-na-see,
pux Cia-leux puing pex ka veng an,
Cia-leux tom tawx nawh moeng kawn bun tix Ya-sa.
14 Singaix yuh ka paox,
Eu-thaw-niiex cheeika Ya-sa hu chawk kaung ka keeing nawh tix dauh.
Ya-sa lih khaing la,
Cia-leux tom chawk nawh sang cie ceu pa tix;
15 nawh ah nin:
“Khiox khaw tawx khawng phak ka aux tix ceu;
aux sang cie dee lih rawm tix blah,
mawh kheu haktiex pa tawx maix ka aux mawh dee kroh.”
Kheu nan,
Cia-leux tom tawx dee lih rawm plak piang khawm plak greem ka nawh heue.
Grawng pon pex ceu pui Yo-tax mai ceu pui Piien-ya-ming
16 Doeh ceu pui keeing moeng Maw-si mawh pui Ci-ni;
mai kix lih hu khaing veng khaox jhie yaong,
pa mawh pui Yo-tax ka veng Yie-li-keu hoek tau ka daux tiag Yo-tax ka plak seh veng Ya-la-teu,
mai ot ka daux si nax pui Ya-ma-lix.b
17 Ceu pui Yo-tax haok tau mai pux aik tix ceu pui Si-miien,
mai rom tix puing pex tau ka pui Cia-nang pa ot ka daux veng Si-fa,
mai yuh veng an laux lak pak leeing,
kok ceu kaux mawh Heu-eu-mac heue.
18-19 Simiang Siyiex teem coi ka ceu pui Yo-tax,
mai kix pon teei ka haktiex piang gawng,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax pon teei kad Cia-sa,
Ya-seu-ci-loeng,
Yi-keu-loeng,
mai haktiex vung viang veng in kix.
Pui pa ot hawt baoh praok rawm sigrit sigra in kix hoik koe ling law rhiem,
kheu nan,
pui Yo-tax ang pon grheu kix hu.
20 Hawt gum lox pa tawm Maw-si,
Cia-leux pon ka Si-paw-loeng;
nawh grheu doeh ceu cad Ya-na loe ceu lih khaing veng.
21 eCeu pui Piien-ya-ming ang bax grheu pui Yie-pu-see pa ot ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hu,
mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Yie-pu-see tom ot tau mai pui Piien-ya-ming dee an,
tom hoek in singaix heue.
Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien mai ceu pui Ma-na-si pon kuang ka haktiex Paw-theu-lix
22-23 Ceu pui Yi-fa-liien mai ceu pui Ma-na-si khankix hu puing ka veng Paw-theu-lix;
yam an veng in kok mawh veng Lu-see.
Simiang Siyiex teem coi ka kix.
Kix ploi pui hu jiang ka veng an;
24 pui jiang an yaox ka pui tix kaux pa kaoh khaing daux veng,
tom chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh buan son krai yix mhong grawng sang laig daux veng yuh ka mawx,
mai yix ang yuh tid tix ka maix.”
25 Pui an krai ka kix.
Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Yi-fa-liien mai ceu pui Ma-na-si pauh pui pa ot daux veng yum khawm uik,
deuh ngie pui an mai khawm nyiex ma nawh khankix.
26 Khaix an,
pui an hu ka haktiex pui Heux,
sum veng tix mu ka dee an,
mai kok ceu kaux veng an mawh Lu-see;
veng in tom hoek in singaix kawn ca kok mawh ceu kaux in heue.
Kawn pui pa ang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex bax grheu hu
27 fCeu pui Ma-na-si ang bax grheu pui pa ot ka Paw Sang,
Tha-na,
To-eux,
Yi-paw-liien,
Mi-cie-to mai pui nyiex yaong pa ot dex tan khankix lih hu;
pui Cia-nang ca kawn ot dee an,
mai ang bax yoi tix hu.
28 Khaix hoik ting riang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
kix khin ka ceu pui Cia-nang yuh kaing pa taug pa mao son kix,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax grheu kix lih hu khawm uik heue.
29 gCeu pui Yi-fa-liien ang bax grheu pui Cia-nang pa ot daux veng Ci-seux lih hu,
mawh kheu nan,
pui Cia-nang ca kawn ot tau mai kix heue.
30 Ceu pui Si-pu-loeng ang bax grheu ceu pui pa ot ka daux veng Ci-loeng mai pa ot daux veng Na-ha-la khankix lih hu,
mawh kheu nan,
pui Cia-nang ca kawn ot dee an,
viang mawh nan,
kix yuh siliex ceu pui Si-pu-loeng heue.
31 Ceu pui Ya-seux ang bax grheu ceu pui pa ot ka daux Ya-kheu,
Si-toeng,
Ya-he-la,
Ya-keu-si,
He-pa,
Ya-fu-keu mai pui pa ot ka daux veng Li-haw khankix lih hu.
32 Kix ot tau mai ceu pui Cia-nang haktiex an,
ang bax grheu kix lih hu heue.
33 Ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix ang bax grheu ceu pui pa ot daux Paw See-mai mai veng Paw Ya-na lih hu.
Kix ot tau mai ceu pui Cia-nang haktiex an,
viang mawh nan,
mai khin kix yuh kaing pa taug pa mao son tix heue.
34 Pui Ya-maw-lix khin ka ceu pui Tang haok ka piang gawng,
ang kieh kix lih hoek ot ka dee lung haktiex.
35 Pui Ya-maw-lix ca kawn ot ka daux Ya-ya-loeng,
Sa-ping,
mai ka gawng Si-liex;
viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-fa-liien mai pui Ma-na-si hoik pon kuang ka kix,
khin kix yuh kaing pa taug pa mao heue.
36 Mee haktiex pui Yi-tongh jauh ka diang moing gawng Ya-kheu-la-ping,
ka baoh Si-la plak laong heue.
