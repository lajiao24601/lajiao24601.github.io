Rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex
111
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex!
 
Aux sang yaog yaw Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
yaog yaw nawh ka dee khrawm pui pa rauh see cie nawh paox tix ot.
2 Kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex kied mawh pa daox pa deng heue!
Pui pa muh ka nawh hoik cu tix tawng khawm uik.
3 Kaing pa yuh nawh hoik naog ka min plak ngai mai siawp sibrawm;
pa rauh see nawh hoik koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex ang kieh ex piawm ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa hoik yuh nawh;
nawh koe buan son mai krax muh krax rhawm,
grum naog mai krax pa caox rhawm.
5 Nawh tawx priex preem ka pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka nawh;
nawh doh keud ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh tix.
6 Nawh jhi pa ting riang ting rao ka pui cie tix,
tawx haktiex pui tang ceu ka kix heue.
 
7 Kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik hoik mawh pa kaux sijax mai pa rauh see;
gum lox ping nyap nawh khaw tix een rhawm ka tete te lai.
8 Gum lox tawx nawh koe leeing ju,
hoik hawt tix sum kaux cong ka daux krax pa kaux sijax mai pa rauh see;
9 nawh taoh ka pui cie tix,
yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix leeing ju.
Nawh mawh pa singax mai pa lhat graung pui ka heue!
10 bPa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex mawh num ping nyia cuyia.
Nawh tawx krax pa lhak lhiao ka pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh.
Nawh hoik mawh pa khaw tix pon ka krax pa yaog yaw leeing ju leeing phan heue.
