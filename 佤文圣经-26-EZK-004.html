Jhi grawng pa lai lawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
4
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
teei lon tiex gleeing tix lon deuh ot ka sivoe ngai maix,
mai yuh riang veng tix mu son sang tang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
2 mai baox kaong rawng ka hawt siviex veng,
sum tab,
sum nyiex bang,
sau ka khaox dung pa yieh veng laux,
mai kieh mawh pa jhi grawng pa hoik lai lawm ka veng.
3 Khaix an,
maix teei rhiem tix bliah sum ot ka si nax maix mai veng kieh mawh guh veng.
Maix tag caw tix veeh ngai tix ka plak veng in,
mai jhi ka grawng pa hoik lai lawm ka veng,
pa lai lawm ka veng in mawh maix.
In hoik mawh mhai pa tawx aux ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
4-5 “Khaix an,
maix tag caw veeh tix it plak goix;
aux tag sang gut phi map pui meung Yi-seu-liex ka piang to kaux maix.
Maix tag caw tix it dee an loe yieh tix dim singaix,
mai kham krax kha rhawm son phi map kix.
Ka daux singaix glang yam nhie aux maix it hoik mawh been glang singaix pa prawng ca aux ka kix,
tix ngaix hoik awm tix num.
6 Khaix hoik naog bawg yam singaix pa prawng ca in,
maix tag caw veeh tix it plak dawm tipon singaix,
kham krax kha rhawm son phi map pui Yo-tax,
tix ngaix hoik awm tix num heue.
7 “Maix tag caw tix veeh ngai tix plak Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai yeeb taix tix ka veng pa lai lawm pui ka an,
krai lox jhai pa puing ka veng in heue.
8 Aux tag sang phrawk ka maix,
mai kieh maix ang lai pon pu pliak ka to kaux tix,
tom hoik naog singaix bawg yam pa lai lawm ka veng in heue.
9 “Khaix an,
maix tag caw tix teei nghox ca,
nghox ca pa lang mu,
ceem,
teu teu,
khawx,
mai nghox ca pa lang mu khankix lu le lih mawh lon,
yuh khao mun kah.
In hoik mawh priex preem pa ih maix yam it ka plak goix ka daux loe yieh tix dim singaix an,
10 tix ngaix caw tix ih ngie ra yieh tix ngoe kongkheu khaing,
ih hawt awm diag tong pa hoik tat tix deuh.
11 Maix tag caw tix pon tix nyaux rawm tix ngaix ra kawk khaing.
12 Maix tag caw tix ot ka sivoe ngai pui hun,
teei aing pui yuh khix pa tong tix ih ka khao mun ceu in heue.”
13 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“In hoik mawh yam grheu aux pui Yi-seu-liex hu ka meung tang meung,
kix tag sang ih ceu pa ang singax singeh pa ang cu pui ih ka daux lox tat jhawm prawng heue.a”
14 Viang mawh nan,
aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
aux ang sax kieh to kaux tix seeing brai,
jauh tom yam iak aux ang sax ih ka ceu pa lawk yum,
phan ang mawh nan khawng brie pa kiied to sat phawng.
Aux ang sax ih ka priex preem pa ang singax singeh khankix heue.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex ah nin:
“Chix tix,
maix chix tix teei aing moi tang aing pui kieh mawh khix taung,
mai tong khao mun cie maix ka piang heue.”
16 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
aux tag sang kieh priex preem ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung uik.
Yam ih kawn pui ka khao mun,
mai nyaux ka rawm pa hoik hawt tix tawx kix ih nyaux awm diag tong pa tat tix deuh,
kix tag chix koe krax pa khi rhawm mai ngia.
17 Kix tag sang cud cha ka priex preem rawm nyaux;
kix tag sang deek rhawm,
mai yum krawx kheu phi map tix si nyek si nyek heue.”
