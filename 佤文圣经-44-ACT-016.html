Thi-maw-thaix yuh kaing tau mai Pao-law mai Si-la
16
Pao-law hoek ka Theu-pix mai Lu-see-teux.
Ka Lu-see-teux koe pui gau tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Thi-maw-thaix;
miex nawh mawh pui Yo-thaix,
mawh kawn Khrit nang,
keeing nawh mawh pui Si-lax.
2 Kawn Khrit pa ot ka Lu-see-teux mai ka Yi-keu-nien khankix,
hoik yaw ka Thi-maw-thaix khawm uik.
3 Pao-law sang viex nawh hu tau mai tix,
nawh tom Pat-hak tix,
mawh kheu hoik tawng pui Yo-thaix pa ot haktiex an khawm uik mawh keeing nawh pui Si-lax.
4 Kix laig ka yaong an kix,
mai ap tix tawx been pa hoik tat kawn cau ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung mai kuad song khankix ka kix,
tawx lox nhie kix priex tix hawt.
5 Mawh kheu nan,
krax yum rhawm ceu cao ku dauh hoik pon krax pa kaux cong,
kaux pui kix kawx hoik hun khaing tix ngaix hoik tix ngaix heue.
Lox kok yo ka daux Ma-chi-toeng
6 Kix hu ka krax haktiex Fu-lui-cia mai ka Cia-la-thaix,
mawh kheu ang Khuan Singax Singeh cu kix sidiex gum lox in ka haktiex Ya-si-yax.
7 Yam hoek kix ka praok mee haktiex Me-si-yax,
kix keud tix sang laig daux haktiex Pi-thoe-ni,
viang mawh nan,
khuan Ye-sux tieb tie ka kix.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom viaa haktiex Me-si-yax,
hoek ka Theu-law-yax.
9 Ka daux bo an,
Pao-law pon tix yaox ka riang su sai tix ceu,
ka daux riang su sai nawh yaox pui Ma-chi-toeng tix kaux,
cung tix khiox khaw ka nawh ah nin:
“Hoek tix teem coi yix ka Ma-chi-toeng heue!”
10 Pao-law hoik yaox ka riang su sai an,
ex nied khre tix hu plak Ma-chi-toeng,
mawh kheu hoik tawng ex Siyiex kok ex hu sidiex gum lox buan ka pui haktiex an heue.
Lui-ti-yax yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux
11 Jauh ex brug reu lih hu khaing Theu-law-yax,
tom hoek ka Sha-maw-theu-la,
ka daux ngaix khaix hoek ka Ni-ya-phaw-li.
12 Jauh dee an ex hu plak daux haktiex,
hoek ka veng Fe-li-pix pa koe ka daux haktiex Ma-chi-toeng tix mu;b
veng in kawx hoik mawh haktiex dee ot pui pa yuh siliex cawng Law-ma.
Ex paung ot dee in ra loe singaix.
13 Ka daux singaix tuah,
ex lih hu khaing veng hoek ka praok rawm klawng,
mai rhawm rhi ex keud,
dee an tee koe dee rawg pui Yo-thaix buan ka tix dauh dauh.
Ex ngawm dee an,
mai sidiex ka dee khrawm bun miex tix dee an.
14 Ka daux pui hun koe bun miex tix kaux pa kaoh khaing veng Thoe-ya-thoe-la,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Lui-ti-yax,
nawh mawh pui pa coh ka man raoh klai pliex tix kaux.
Nawh dawg mawh pui pa yum rhawm ka Siyiex;
Simiang Ye-sux paoh ka rhawm rhi nawh,
mai kieh nawh bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa krai Pao-law.
15 Nawh mai pui nyiex ma nawh cub com tix khawm uik.
Khaix an,
nawh khiox khaw ex ah nin:
“Phan pex een aux mawh pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux tete,
viex pex hoek paung ka nyiex ma aux heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh but rhawm tix deuh ex ot heue.
Ka daux thawng Fe-li-pix
16 Koe tix ngaix,
ex hu ka dee rawg tix buan an,
bun siliex tix kaux kuan rieb ka ex.
Bun siliex in,
khuan phi caox hoik laig daux nawh,
nawh hoik pon sawn ka kaing pa sang hoek,
mawh kheu nan,
hoik pon mau tom nie son simiang tix.
17 Bun an hawt ka Pao-law mai ex,
mai kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Pui in kix hoik mawh siliex Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang krai pao ka daux krax pa taoh ka pex heue!”
18 Bun an jiiem tix kok yo ra loe singaix yuh nin;
Pao-law ang lai rhawm mhong,
nawh tom kling tix krai ka khuan phi caox ah nin:
“Aux tawx lox ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux Khrit,
nhie mai lih khaing to kaux bun in heue!”
Khuan phi caox an lih hu dum mawx dum an heue.
19 Simiang bun siliex an tawng hoik dut krax dee hoek sin ngao kix,
nawh tom giex ka Pao-law mai Si-la,
mai viex kiex hu ka bang hoek ka simiang,
20 viex kiex hoek ka plak sivoe ngai simiang Law-ma,
mai deuh phi map ah nin:
“Pui ih kix mawh pui Yo-thaix,
kix lai pon hoek tix suk sag yuh veng ex.
21 Kiex hoik mawh pui pa lieh lheu ka been,
mawh pa ang pui Law-ma pon rieb kah,
ang pon hawt tix yuh ka heue!”
22 Pui hun kawx hawt tix ah ka kiex,
simiang tom tawx lox nhie kix peeik ka kraung ceep kiex,
si vied ka kiex.
23 Khaix hoik sau lien khankix riang tix lauh Pao-law mai ka Si-la,
kix tom laung kiex daux thawng,
mai tawx lox nhie pui pau jhak ka mhawm mhawm.
24 Pui pau tawx lox nhie kix laung kiex daux nyiex daux thawng plak daux,
mai sau gang caong kiex khawm ra khawn heue.
25 Hoek bawg yam greung sawm,
Pao-law mai Si-la rawg buan,
rhax lai yaog yaw Siyiex,
pui tang kaux pa laung ot daux thawng daong yhaok tix nghiet ka khawm uik.
26 Dum mawx dum an,
kla reem ting lih,
pie khawm jaong nyiex daux thawng kawx hoik yung yang;
tix vud khaing siviex nyiex daux thawng hoik baoh khawm uik,
maox ceng pa phrawk ot ka pui laung kawx hoik cawd khawm uik.
27 Yam hoik si duad pui pau,
kix yaox hoik bhang siviex tix khawm uik,
mai ce pui pa laung kix ka hoik to hu,
lawd vaig lih,
ce tix sang plaux tix.
28 Pao-law yaox kah,
kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Paw vi yuh to kaux tix laux,
yix kawn ot dee in heue!”
29 Pui pa pau teei kawk ngu,
preeng tix laig,
mai si yaung si kua krub ka plak sivoe ngai Pao-law mai Si-la,
30 khaix an thung kix lih,
mai chawk ah nin:
“Sara ra kaux,
sang khaw aux tix yuh ka mawx tom taug pon krax taoh lie?”
31 Kiex ah nin:
“Yum rhawm ka Simiang Ye-sux,
maix mai khawm pui nyiex ma maix tom taug pon krax taoh heue.”
32 Kiex khawm ra kaux tom baox krai nawh mai pui nyiex ma nawh mhong ka gum lox cie Simiang Ye-sux.
33 Bo an ka daux yam an,
pui pa pau an viex kiex hu si ghrah ka rhoi khaix mat;
nawh mai pui nyiex ma nawh cub com dum mawx dum an.
34 Nawh kok Pao-law mai Si-la hoek ka nyiex tix,
mai tawx kiex ih ceu pa ih.
Nawh mai pui nyiex ma nawh hoik naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm kix ka Siyiex heue.
35 Ka daux singaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
simiang Law-ma ploi simiang naing hu ah nin:
“Ploi ka pui an kiex lih heue!”
36 Pui pa pau an tom krai ka Pao-law ah nin:
“Simiang naing tawx lox nhie kix ploi ka maix mai Si-la,
Yam in pax chix tix lih khaing daux thawng,
mai deeh ing mhawm mhawm heue.”
37 Viang mawh nan,
Pao-law krai ka pui naing an ah nin:
“Yiex mawh pui Law-ma,
yam ang bax prawng ka phi map yiex,
viang mawh nan,
kix si vied yiex ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai baox laung yiex ka daux thawng.
Yam in kix keud tix sang briex mox tix tawx yiex lih hu;
in ang chix heue!
Nhie simiang naing hoek tix thung yiex lih heue.”
38 Pui naing an krai lox in ka simiang naing.
Yam mhong kix mawh Sao-law mai Si-la pui Law-ma,
kix hoik lhat rhawm,
39 kix tom hu krai lox mhawm ka kiex,
mai thung kiex lih,
nhie kiex lih hu khaing veng an.
40 Pao-law mai Si-la lih khaing siviex daux thawng,
kiex tom hu plak nyiex ma Lui-ti-yax,
mai yaox kawn Khrit khankix dee an,
mai tawx krax nyawm rhawm ka kix,
khaix an lih hu heue.
