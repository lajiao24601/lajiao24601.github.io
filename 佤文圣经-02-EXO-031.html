Cang mai pa sum ka nyiex gox buan man
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 35.30–36.1)
31
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Aux hoik laug ka Pi-sha-liex kawn simex Wu-lix,
doeh doe ceu pui Hu-eux ka daux ceu pui Yo-tax.
3 Khuana aux hoik naog ka nawh;
aux tawx nawh koe krax lhak lhiao,
ping nyia cuyi,
krax keud krax sawn,
pon chix yuh kaing ku ceu,
4 awm pa yuh ka riang pak peung,
mai raug kraung ku ceu ka khri,
mau,
tawng;
5 Pon chix pat chix kleuk ka siang simaox;
pon chix saik ka khaox riang pak peung;
mai baox pon yuh ka kaing taix pa mhawm ku ceu.
6 “Aux hoik yaog Ya-si-sha-maw kawn simex Ya-heu-li-ya-pawx,
ceu pui Tang,
mawh cao pa teem coi nawh.
Ka pui pa koe rhawm keud pa chix yuh kaing taix khankix,
aux kawx sang tawx ping nyia ka kix,
mai kieh kix pon yuh ka kaing can ceu pa tawm aux ku ceu.
7 Awm pa mawh nyiex gox buan man aux,
mai teug sijax mukheu,
mai dawb teug sijax mukheu,
mai kraung pa siao ka nyiex gox buan man khawm uik.
8 Pheen khawm kraung pa siao ka khawm uik,
mai jawk kawk ngu khri khawm kraung pa siao ka khawm uik,
mai khu dee tan nyawng bhawng,
9 mai khu dee tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing khawm kraung pa siao ka khawm uik,
pan khoek khawm jaong pan.
10 Sibex mhawm cao giex kaing li pa singax singeh,
pa ceep Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix yam yuh kix kaing yuh cao giex kaing li,
11 bee pa singax singeh,
khawm pa si oe bhawng can pa siao ka nyiex buan pa singax singeh.
In khawm uik kix tag sang hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawm aux ku gum heue.”
Pa priex tix hawt ka singaix tuah
12-13 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si,
nhie nawh krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
ah nin:
“Pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka singaix laux aux;
kheu in mawh mhai si nax pex mai aux leeing ju leeing phan,
krai grawng aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik laug pex mawh pui cie tix.
14 Pex tag caw priex tix hawt ka singaix laux,
mawh kheu singaix in mawh singaix pa singax singeh.
Can pui pa ang priex tix hawt ka singaix in,
mai ca kawn yuh ka kaing,
tag sang kieh yum.
15 bPex koe liah singaix,
chix tix yuh kaing,
viang mawh nan,
ka daux aliah singaix,
mawh singaix laux lee pa ting,
pa tan ka aux.
Pui pa yuh kaing ka daux singaix laux lee an,
tag kieh nawh yum.
16 Pui Yi-seu-liex khankix tag caw priex tix hawt ka singaix laux an leeing ju leeing phan,
kieh mawh mhai sakse pa yuh aux sijax mukheu.
17 cIn mawh mhai ka pui Yi-seu-liex mai si nax aux leeing ju leeing phan;
mawh kheu aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh rao ma mai haktiex ka daux liah singaix,
ka daux aliah singaix naox kaing pa yuh tix mai laux lee tix heue.”
18 Khaix hoik krai Siyiex lox in kix ka Maw-si piang gawng Si-nai,
nawh tawx khlawp simaox ra khlawp ka Maw-si;
Siyiex tiiem gum lox ping nyap ot piang khlawp simaox an heue.
