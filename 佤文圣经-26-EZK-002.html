Siyiex laug Yi-si-ciex mawh cao jhai
2
Yam yaox aux ka riang su sai in,
mai aux gup tix krub ka piang tiex.
Khaix an,
aux mhong lox pa si kah pui;
lox in ah nin ka aux:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
kaoh cung heue!
Aux tag sang si kah ka maix.”
2 Yam si kah lox in tix,
khuan Siyiex laig ka daux aux,
mai yaog aux kaoh cung.
Aux mhong ka lox in ca ah nin ka aux:
3 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
aux tag sang ploi maix hu ka pui Yi-seu-liex dee an.
Kix hoik tieh vheen mai tik vud ka aux awm tax du tix yuh nan,
hoek yam in mai ca kawn tieh vheen ka aux heue.
4 Kix kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
mai ang yung yi ka aux,
mawh kheu nan aux tag sang ploi maix hu,
mai krai gum lox pa sang krai aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ka kix.
5 Kix pa mawh pui pa tieh vheen in kix nghiet ang nghiet,
mai kix tag caw tix tawng ka daux kix hoik sax koe cao jhai tix kaux heue.
6 “Viang mawh nan,
pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix paw vi lhat ka kix,
paw vi lhat ka lox pa krai kix nang.
Kix tag sang tieb tie ka maix,
ang een maix koe ceu;
maix tag sang awm pa ot ka si nax Tam-tim yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
maix paw vi yaoh yee kheu pui pa tieh vheen in kix,
mai paw vi lhat ka lox pa krai kix.
7 Ang kuang kix nghiet ang nghiet,
maix tag caw tix krai lox aux ka kix.
Tag caw tix doh keud ka cub kix pa mawh pui pa tieh vheen heue.
8 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet ka lox pa krai aux ka maix;
ang pon tix mawh pui pa tieh vheen awm kix yuh nan.
Maix tag caw tix ang jheeh tix,
mai ih ka ceu pa tawx aux ka maix.”
9 aMawh kheu nan,
aux yaox taix tix khawn pa nyeu tix ka aux,
ka daux taix an giex ka phuk lai tix bua.
10 Nawh yah ka phuk lai an;
aux yaox ka phuk lai an,
khawm plak praix khawm plak daux hoik tiiem ka ngai lai,
pa tiiem ka piang hoik mawh lox yiiem,
lox ngia,
mai lox laux rhawm khankix heue.
