Rhax lai yaog yaw cao pa phan pa yuh
104
Khuan aux eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex!
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
maix kied ting kied lhaong heue!
Maix ceep ka siawp sibrawm pa riang min plak ngai;
2 maix lhop pa riang mawh kraung dha ka to kaux tix.
Maix tawm nih ka rao ma,
awm sum ka nyiex gox man;
3 maix sum nyiex dee ot koe ka piang rawm daux rao ma.
Maix teei pai awm yuh ling law kah,
hu lai ling ka daux preeik bhaung.
4 aMaix kieh bhaung mawh kawn cau,
kieh pu plak praix mawh siliex heue.
 
5 Maix sum haktiex ot ka piang riah rawng pa kaux cong,
kieh haktiex ang lai chix yung yang leeing ju.
6 Maix kieh rawm sigrit sigra tawb kheem ka piang haktiex awm kraung dha yuh nan;
maix kieh rawm ting kheem ka can gawng lua.
7 Yam tawx maix lox pa ne raw tix,
mai rawm tom lih hu;
yam mhong rawm ting ka lox pa tawx maix,
tom to hu khawm uik.
8 Rawm ting kheem ka piang gawng,
mai laig ka daux ngawd,
khrawm tix hoek ka dee pa hoik mhai maix.
9 Maix hoik tat tix deuh ka mee haktiex,
mai ang kieh kix jao luan,
ang lai baox kieh kix kheem ka haktiex heue.
 
10 Maix kieh rawm keet buing lih khaing daux ngawd,
kawk tix laig ka si nax gawng khankix;
11 to sat yaox tix nyaux ka rawm,
la tiag ang lai kha tix ka rawm nyaux.
12 Ka piang khaox pa ot ka praok rawm,
koe sim pa pu ka daux rao ma,
rhax lai mai laux lee tix ka piang heue.
 
13 Maix tawx lhiex lih ka si nax gawng khaing rao ma;
haktiex hoik naog ka buan son pa tawx maix.
14 Maix kieh rib klai kuah,
mai eei ka khawng brie,
kieh taux lhax lih koe,
kawn pui yaox tix ih,
kieh kawn pui sum nghox koi ka piang haktiex:
15 Kieh plai lih mawh pa nyawm yuh rhawm rhi kawn pui,
yuh bee lih mawh pa kieh pui mhawm grang,
kieh priex preem lih mawh pa eei riang rao kawn pui heue.
 
16 Simiang Siyiex sum ka khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noeng,
kieh khaox an pon ka rawm lhiex tom kub.
17 Kawn sim yuh mheem tix ot ka dee an;
ka piang khaox gix an klang kax yuh nyiex tix ot kah;
18 pex tiag ot ka piang gawng lhaong;
ghot mox tix ot ka daux dawh rang heue.
 
19 Maix phan yuh riang khix lih mawh pa tat ka singaix sawm;
riang singaix hoik tawng ka bawg yam pa sang klip tix.
20 Maix phan yuh ka pa faik pon sawm,
mai kieh yien yawg,
to tiag tom lih khaing daux nong khaox.
21 Sivai sang si pa nhum ju graox mai sawg tix ih kug,
sawg tix ih priex preem pa hoik tawx Siyiex.
22 Yam lih singaix kix tom to mox tix,
blauh tix it ka daux mheem tix,
23 Kawn pui hu yuh kaing,
taug mao tom hoek pon bo heue.
 
24 Simiang Siyiex eue,
pa phan pa yuh maix kied nie.
In kix khawm uik hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing ping nyia cuyi maix;
ceu pa phan pa yuh maix khawm uik hoik naog ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee.
25 Ka daux rawm sigrit sigra pa vieh va,
to sat pa iak pa ting ang dak uik simin hoik koe ka daux,
26 breu lai ling tix hu ka daux,
to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui ka pa phan pa yuh maix klieh ka daux heue.
 
27 Kix hoik een rhawm tix ka maix,
maix tawx kix ih priex preem yam hoek bawg yam.
28 Maix hoik tawx ka kix,
mai kix tom ih;
maix tawx kix ih priex preem,
mai kix tom yaox tix sak;
29 maix hu khaing kix,
mai kix tom si yaung si kua;
phan maix deeh teei bhawm khaing kix,
mai kix taug yum,
deeh ka phawng tiex.
30 Viang mawh nan,
maix phlu bhawm ka kix,
mai kix tom yaox tix im ju;
maix kieh haktiex deeh cox lih mawh pa khraox heue.
 
31 Kieh min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex koe leeing ju heue!
Kieh Simiang Siyiex muh ka ceu pa phan pa yuh tix heue!
32 Nawh jhak simi ka haktiex,
mai haktiex si yaung si kua;
nawh giex chuh ka gawng lua,
mai taox ngu tom lih khaing heue.
 
33 Aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex ka daux ju tix tom si dauh;
singaix yam kawn im ju aux,
tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka Siyiex aux.
34 Kieh nawh khaw rhawm ka lox rhax aux,
mawh kheu krax pa gaux rhawm aux hoik kaoh khaing nawh.
35 Kieh pui phi map grai hu khaing haktiex;
kieh pui pa caox pa caig ang lai baox lih tix koe heue.
 
Khuan aux eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
