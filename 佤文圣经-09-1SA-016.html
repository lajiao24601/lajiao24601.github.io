Yaog Ta-wue mawh simiang
16
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Sa-mu-eu:
“Kawn sang laux rhawm laux rhi maix son Sao-law miex greeng lie?
Aux hoik tik ka nawh,
mai ang lai kieh nawh mawh simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
Maix viex bee sime tix blah hoek ka Paw-li-haung,
hu ka pui pa kaux Yie-si dee an,
mawh kheu hoik laug aux kawn simex nawh tix kaux mawh simiang heue.”
2 Sa-mu-eu chawk ah nin:
“Aux sang hu yuh ka mawx lie?
Phan Sao-law tawng,
nawh rien tix pauh ka aux!”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Maix viex kawn moi iak hu tix mu,
mai krai sang hu ka dee an son sang yuh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Simiang Siyiex.
3 Maix nhie Yie-si hoek yuh krax tan tau mai maix;
yam an,
aux tag sang krai ceu pa caw maix tix sang yuh.
Pui pa hoik krai aux ka maix an,
maix caw tix kraix rawm bee ka nawh mai kieh nawh mawh simiang heue.”
4 Sa-mu-eu hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh hoek ka Paw-li-haung,
kuad song pa ot ka daux veng khankix kied lhat graung mai hoek ka nawh ah nin:
“Pa hoek maix mawh viex maix pa nhim lie?”
5 Sa-mu-eu pawk lox ah nin:
“Hoik viex pa nhim.
Aux hoek mawh son sang tan Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Simiang Siyiex.
Pex kieh to kaux tix singax,
khaix an mai hawt aux heue.”
Sa-mu-eu kawx nhie Yie-si khawm kawn simex nawh khankix kieh to kaux tix singax,
mai ghaok kix hoek yuh krax tan mai tix heue.
6 Yam hoik hoek kix,
Sa-mu-eu yaox ka kawn simex Yie-si pa kaux Yi-li-ya,
nawh keud daux rhawm tix ah nin:
“Pui in pa cung sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
rien tix mawh pa hoik laug nawh heue.”
7 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Sa-mu-eu:
“Paw vi jhak plak praix nawh mai pa ting lhaong to kaux nawh.
Aux ang bax laug ka nawh,
mawh kheu grawng pa jhak aux pui,
ang awm pui haktiex;
pui jhak ka plak praix,
aux jhak ka daux rhawm heue.”
8 Khaix an,
Yie-si kok ka kawn simex tix Ya-pi-na-ta hoek ka sivoe ngai Sa-mu-eu.
Viang mawh nan,
Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Ang,
Simiang Siyiex ang bax laug ka nawh heue.”
9 Yie-si baox kok ka Sa-ma hoek;
Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Ang,
Simiang Siyiex ang bax laug ka nawh heue.”
10 Yuh nin,
Yie-si tom kok kawn simex tix khawm aliah kaux hoek dex ka sivoe Sa-mu-eu.
Sa-mu-eu ah nin ka nawh:
“Ang,
Simiang Siyiex ang bax laug ka daux kix tix kaux kawx heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu chawk Yie-si ah nin:
“Maix kawn koe kawn simex tang kaux lie?”
Yie-si ah nin:
“Kawn koe pa blawn iak khaing paox tix tix kaux;
nawh liang yung plak praix heue.”
Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Kok nawh hoek tin.
Phan nawh ang hoek,
ex ang chix yuh krax tan heue.”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-si tom ploi pui hu kok nawh hoek.
Nawh mawh pui nhum ju pa kied mhawm grang,
pak ngai nawh raoh,
tawm ngai nawh kied mhawm riang.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Sa-mu-eu:
“Mawh nawh,
ap ka nawh heue!”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Sa-mu-eu tom teei bee sime lih,
mai kraix ka Ta-wue plak sivoe ngai pux ke nawh khankix.
Khuan Simiang Siyiex dum mawx dum an laig daux Ta-wue,
jauh tom yam an mai ot tau mai nawh.
Khaix an,
Sa-mu-eu deeh ing ka La-max heue.
Ta-wue ot daux nyiex haw Sao-law
14 Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih khaing Sao-law;
Simiang Siyiex ploi khuan phi caox hoek tuk tiak ka nawh.
15 Siliex nawh ah nin ka nawh:
“Ex hoik tawng Siyiex ploi khuan phi caox hoek tuk tiak maix.
16 Kheu nan,
mhaing Vawng tawx lox,
kieh ex sawg ka pui pa chix tauh ting jung hoek.
Yam hoek khuan phi caox tix tuk tiak ka maix,
pui an tauh ka ting mai maix tag nyawm ot heue.”
17 Sao-law tom tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Hu sawg ka pui pa chix tauh ting hoek heue!”
18 Pui cau Sao-law tix kaux ah nin:
“Ka daux veng Paw-li-haung koe kawn simex Yie-si tix kaux;
nawh mawh pui pa kied chix tauh ting.
Nawh kied kua ka naing,
mai kied mhawm grang,
kied chix krai lox;
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai nawh heue.”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law ploi kawn cau tix hu ka Yie-si dee an,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix kok kawn simex maix Ta-wue pa liang ka yung an hoek ka aux tin heue.”
20 Yie-si tom nhie Ta-wue viex ka plai pa hoik sau naog ka si ngien hak tix mu,
mai kawn pex iak tix mu,
mai la tix mu pa tang naog ka khao mun hu ka Sao-law heue.
21 Ta-wue hoek ka Sao-law dee an,
mai khre ka nawh.
Sao-law kied khaw rhawm ka Ta-wue,
mai laug nawh mawh cao pa hawt pau tix,
pa viex ka kraung naing.
22 Sao-law ploi pui hu tawx lox ka Yie-si ah nin:
“Aux kied khaw rhawm ka Ta-wue,
kieh nawh ot dee ot aux tin,
mai khre ka aux heue!”
23 Jauh tom yam an,
yam ploi Siyiex khuan phi caox laig daux to kaux Sao-law,
Ta-wue tom teei ka ting jung cie tix mai tauh kah,
khuan phi caox an lih hu khaing Sao-law dum mawx dum an,
mai Sao-law mhong nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi tix heue.
