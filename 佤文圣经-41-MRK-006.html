Pui Na-sha-leux koeng ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 13.53–58; Lai Lu-cia 4.16–30)
6
Ye-sux lih hu khaing haktiex an,
mai deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix;
pui gau nawh kawx hoek tau mai nawh.
2 Yam hoek ka daux singaix tuah,
nawh sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex sidiex.
Koe pui tom nie mhong ka lox nawh kied daox deng kah,
mai ah nin:
“Pui in hoik pon krax pa lhak khaing dee mawx lie?
Mawh pui mawx pa tawx ping nyia cuyi ka nawh lie?
Nawh kawx hoik pon yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am heue!
3 Ang nawh mawh cang mai khaox tix kaux lie?
Ang nawh mawh kawn simex Ma-li-yax,
pux ke Ya-keux,
Yaw-seux,
Yo-tax mai Si-meung khankix lie?
Nan nawh khankix ang hoik ot dee in khawm uik lie?”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom koeng ka nawh.
4 aYe-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Cao jhai pon krax yung yi pui plak praix yaong,
plak praix ceu pui tix,
plak praix nyiex ma ot tix heue.”
5 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh ot ka daux haktiex tix ang yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
deuh ngie taix tix ka piang pui pa koe jhawm saux tix blah khaing,
mai pai kix moi.
6 Phan mawh son pui pa ang yum rhawm khankix,
nawh kied daox deng ka heue.
Ye-sux ploi kawn cau tix kao ra kaux hu
(Lai Ma-thaix 10.5–15; Lai Lu-cia 9.1–6)
Ye-sux nied tix sidiex gau pui ka baoh nyiex yaong pa dex dee an.
7 Nawh khrawm kawn cau tix kao ra kaux,
mai ploi kix lih hu ra kaux ra kaux.
Nawh tawx akhuang pa grheu khuan pa seeing brai ka kix,
8 bkhaix an tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Yam lawng pex krax lheu khaing khaox gix pa koe ka taix pex tix gawng khaing,
mai ang pon tix viex ka ceu pa tid tix.
Ang pon tix viex ka priex preem,
ang pon tix viex ka bau kraung,
daux hau pex kawx ang pon viex ka mau,
9 ceep ngie kiiep tix ku khaing,
mai ang siao kraung ceep plak daux ra pheen.”
10 Nawh baox ah nin ka kix:
“Yam hoek pex ka haktiex tix dauh dauh,
nyiex pui mawx cu tix rieb ka pex,
pex tom caw tix paung ot dee an,
tom hoek lih hu pex khaing haktiex an.
11 cYam hoek pex ka haktiex dee mawx ang ah,
phan pui haktiex an ang rieb ka pex,
mai ang cu tix nghiet ka lox pex,
pex tom lih hu khaing haktiex an,
mai khruah phawng tiex pa koe ka caong pex cawd,
jhi grawng pa hoik ne raw ka kix heue.”
12 Kix tom lih hu sidiex,
mai nhie pui loh rhawm rhi tix.
13 dKix baox grheu phi hu tom nie,
teei bee kraix ka pui pa koe jhawm saux tom nie,
mai pai ka jhawm saux kix moi heue.
Grawng yum Yaw-hang cao cub com
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.1–12; Lai Lu-cia 9.7–9)
14 ePhan mawh grawng kaing pa yuh Ye-sux khawm uik simiang Si-luix hoik mhong kah,
mawh kheu hoik tauh hu ceu kaux Ye-sux ka haktiex ku dauh.
Koe pui ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Yaw-hang cao cub com pa kaoh im,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom koe riang rao tix yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am heue.”
15 Koe pui ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Yi-li-yax heue.”
Koe nang pui pa ah nin:
“Nawh mawh cao jhai,
pa awm cao jhai dix dix tix kaux heue.”
16 Si-luix mhong ka lox in,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ah nin:
“Nawh mawh Yaw-hang cao cub com kaw pa hoik kid aux kaing an,
nawh hoik kaoh im heue!”
17 fSi-luix hoik sax tawx lox nhie pui giex ka Yaw-hang phrawk ka nawh,
mai laung nawh ka daux thawng.
Si-luix yuh nin mawh son sang yuh pa khaw rhawm Si-law-tix,
mawh kheu hoik teei Si-luix tix moeng ka Si-law-tix pa mawh moeng pux aik nawh Fe-lix.
18 Yaw-hang ne raw ka nawh tom nie bawg ah nin:
“Maix hoik teei tix moeng ka moeng pux aik tix ang khaw heue!”
19 Mawh kheu nan,
rhawm rhi Si-law-tix koeng ka Yaw-hang,
mai keud tix sang pauh ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon yuh awm rhawm pa keud tix.
20 Si-luix lhat ka Yaw-hang,
nawh hoik tawng Yaw-hang mawh pui pa rauh pa see pa singax singeh tix kaux,
sang kia ngie ka nawh mhawm mhawm.
Si-luix mhong ka lox pa krai Yaw-hang miex bawg,
nawh kied ang nyawm ot kah,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ca kawn muh tix nghiet ka lox pa krai nawh heue.
21 Bawg yam pa krox jun Si-law-tix hoik hoek.
Ka daux singaix pa yuh Si-luix poi keh tix,
nawh ghaok simiang pa ot greem tix,
simiang pa chix tiiem lai pa chix puing naing,
mai simiang ka daux pui haktiex Cia-li-lix.
22 Yam sawm kix,
kawn bun Si-law-tixg lih graoh;
Si-luix mai nyo si nie kied khaw rhawm kah.
Mawh kheu nan,
simiang krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix mhaing ceu pa tix ka aux ang ah,
aux tag sang tawx ka maix khawm uik.”
23 Khaix an nawh baox tiex tu ah nin:
“Maix mhaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
viang hoik mawh haktiex pa kuang aux tix plak,
mai aux kawx tag sang tawx ka maix heue!”
24 Kawn bun an lih chawk ka miex tix ah nin:
“Aux sang khaw tix mhaing ka ceu pa tix lie?”
Miex nawh pawk ah nin:
“Kaing Yaw-hang cao cub com heue.”
25 Kawn bun an deeh hoek ka simiang dum mawx dum an,
mai khiox khaw ah nin:
“Khiox khaw simiang kid tix deuh kaing Yaw-hang cao cub com ka daux pan dum mawx dum an,
mai tawx ka aux heue!”
26 Simiang mhong ka lox pa khiox khaw in,
simiang kied suk sag rhawm kah;
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik tiex tu ka plak sivoe ngai nyo si nie,
ang rhawm tieb tie ka lox pa khiox khaw kawn bun an.
27 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tawx lox ka pui naing pa pau nhie kix hu teei kaing Yaw-hang hoek dum mawx dum an.
Pui naing pa pau lih hu ka daux thawng,
mai kid ka kaing Yaw-hang,
28 deuh ka daux pan,
viex tix tawx ka kawn bun Si-law-tix;
kawn bun tom tawx ka miex tix.
29 Pui gau Yaw-hang mhong ka grawng in,
kix tom teei ka to kaux yum Yaw-hang hu,
mai si paung ka daux si meeig heue.
Ye-sux kieh pui phuan rheng kaux sawm sak
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.13–21; Lai Lu-cia 9.10–17; Lai Yaw-hang 6.1–13)
30 Kawn cau khankix deeh hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai krai can kaing pa hoik yuh tix pa hoik sidiex tix ka nawh khawm uik.
31 Mawh kheu kied hun pui pa hoek tix lai ling,
pie bawg yam sawm Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh kawx ang koe,
mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoek,
mai mox tix hawt aux hu laux lee tix tix vud ka haktiex dee yien yi heue.”
32 Mawh kheu nan,
kix brug reu hu,
mai briex mox tix hoek ka haktiex dee yien yi heue.
33 Viang mawh nan,
koe pui tix blah yaox yam lih kix,
mai tawng ka kix dum mawx dum an.
Pui hun tom lih khaing veng ku veng,
nied tix hu krax,
kawn hoek sivoe Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh ka haktiex an.
34 hYe-sux hoek ka praok rawm,
yaox ka cub pui hun,
mai caox rhawm caox rhi ka kix,
mawh kheu hoik awm kix cub yung pa ang koe cao liang yuh nan.
Nawh jauh tix sidiex gau kix ka grawh grawng kaing tom nie ceu.
35 Yam hoek bo praix,
pui gau hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Praix hoik lha,
dee in hoik mawh daux tiag daux tu,
36 nhie pui hun tang prung prang tix hu,
kieh kix tang hu liag tix ih priex preem ka yaong pa dex baoh an heue.”
37 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex tawx kix ih heue.”
Kix chawk ah nin:
“Maix sang nhie yix hu liag khao mun tawx kix ih ka mau luah ra yieh lie?”
38 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Hu jhak pex kawn koe khao mun miex ding lie?”
Khaix hoik jhak kix tom ah nin:
“Khao mun phuan lon mai kax ra mu khaing heue.”
39 Ye-sux tawx lox ka pui gau tix nhie pui hun cub mawx cub an ngawm piang tiag rib.
40 Pui hun tom ngawm,
tix cub koe kaing yieh kaux,
koe kaing tix phuan kaux.
41 Ye-sux teei ka khao mun phuan lon mai kax ra mu,
maong ka rao ma yaog yaw Siyiex,
khaix an si vit ka khao mun,
tawx ka pui gau tix,
pui gau tom guah tix tawx ka pui hun.
Awm an,
nawh kawx guah nang kax heue.
42 Pui hun ih ka khawm uik,
khaix an hoik ih sak khawm uik.
43 Pui gau khrawm khao mun mai kax pa deuk mai naog kao ra khrawng.
44 Kaux pui pa ih sak,
simex koe phuan rheng kaux heue.
Ye-sux hu ka caong tix piang rawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.22–33; Lai Yaw-hang 6.16–21)
45 Khaix hoik kaing in,
Ye-sux dhiet pui gau tix laig daux reu dum mawx dum an,
mai ran tiang hu ka Paw-sai-tax plak an,
krox nawh kieh pui hun prung prang heue.
46 Nawh hoik tawx kix hu,
mai tom haok rawg buan ka piang gawng,
47 nari plak pon bo,
reu hoik phat tix hoek ka sigreung nhawng,
to kaux Ye-sux kawn ot ka praok nhawng.
48 Nawh yaox kied taug mao pui gau tix ka vai reu,
mawh kheu hu reu plak phlu bhaung.
Yam dex sang riang praix,
Ye-sux hu ka kix piang rawm,
mai keud tix sang luan hu ka praok kix.i
49 Kix yaox hu nawh ka piang rawm,
ce mawh miang,
kix kied lhat,
tom riag yo khawm uik;
50 mawh kheu yaox kix ka nawh,
mai kied yaoh yee ka heue.
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix dum mawx dum an:
“Paw vi khi,
mawh aux,
paw vi lhat heue!”
51 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh laig daux reu,
mai ot tau mai kix,
bhaung tom yien heue.
Kix daox deng nang suk sag rhawm nang,
52 mawh kheu ang kix bax tawng ka dee bleeh grawng khao mun pa hoik si vit tix tawx in;
rhawm rhi kix lha doh ka heue.
Pai pui Keu-ni-sa-leux pa koe jhawm saux moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.34–36)
53 Kix hoik tiang ka nhawng,
mai hoek ka praok nhawng Keu-ni-sa-leux,
kix phrawk ka reu.
54 Yam lih kix khaing daux reu,
cub pui hun tawng ka Ye-sux dum mawx dum an.
55 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tauh hu ka haktiex an,
mhong kix ot Ye-sux dee mawx,
kix tom grawng ku it pui pa koe jhawm saux hoek dee an.
56 Ye-sux hoek daux yaong ang ah,
daux veng ang ah,
phan ang mawh nan hoek ka nyiex yaong pui ang ah,
pui hun hoik viex tix deuh pui pa koe jhawm saux ot daux laih,
mai khiox khaw Ye-sux kieh pui pa koe jhawm saux pu pi ngie ka jaong sibex dha nawh.
Can pui pa pu pi hoik pon tix moi khawm uik heue.
