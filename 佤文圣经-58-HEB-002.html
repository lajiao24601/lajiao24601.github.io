Krax taoh pa ting pa lhaong
2
Mawh kheu nan,
ex tag caw tix nie giex kaux cong ka ceu pa mawh pa meh pa hoik mhong ex,
son ang sang kieh baux bawg yam chun tix hu.
2 Gum lox pa hoik sidiex kawn cau Siyiex ka ex,
hoik jhi grawng mawh pa koe ceu khawm uik,
can pui pa ang nghiet ka gum lox in hoik kham krax ciyang pa khaw tix pon.
3 Kheu hoik mawh nin,
phan ex kawn prah ka krax taoh pa ting yuh nin,
sang chix pon tix luat luan khaing krax ciyang yuh ka mawx lie?
Simiang Ye-sux hoik krai pao ka krax taoh in sivoe;
pui pa hoik mhong khankix kawx hoik jhi sidawng ka ex.
4 Yam an,
Siyiex tom teei mhai sakse pa tang siyox,
pa daox deng,
mai mhai pa daox pa am ku ceu sau riang tix kham sakse kix.
Nawh baox hawt ka alo tix,
mai guah tix tawx buan son Khuan Singax Singeh ka ex heue.
Paoh daux krax taoh son kawn pui haktiex
5 Siyiex ang doh bax teei haktiex pa tag sang phan yuh tix,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik krai ex an,
deuh ot ka daux pa kuang kawn cau tix.
6 aDeeh tieh tix,
nawh tieh tix yuh awm pa koe daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Siyiex eue,
kawn pui sawn mawh ceu pa tix,
maix lai kawn chix kia ngien ka nawh;
kawn pui haktiex sawn mawh ceu pa tix,
maix lai kawn chix muh ka nawh.
7 Maix hoik kieh nawh tiiem ceeix ceeix khaing kawn cau Siyiex tix vud;
maix teei min plak ngai,
pa koe min mawh mhok khri nawh;
8 maix hoik kieh nawh kuang ka piang khawm uik heue.
Dee in ah nin:
Siyiex kieh kawn pui “kuang ka piang khawm uik”,
in hoik jhi sidawng mawh ka son khawm uik.
Viang mawh nan,
yam in aux ang sax yaox grawng pa kuang pui ka piang ku ceu khawm uik.
9 Ex tieh tix hoik yaox ka Ye-sux,
hoik deuh nawh iak ceeix ceeix khaing kawn cau Siyiex,
son sang yum son pui kaing mheen ka daux buan son Siyiex.
Daux in singaix,
ex hoik yaox ka grawng pa pon nawh mhok khri pa koe min plak ngai,
pa koe min ceu kaux ka daux pa cim nawh krax kha rhawm pa yum.
10 Siyiex cao pa phan yuh mai pa kieh ku ceu khawm uik lih tix mawh,
kieh Ye-sux cim krax kha rhawm,
lih mawh pa grum naog,
son sang kieh kawn simex tom nie rom tix pon ka min plak ngai nawh;
Siyiex yuh nin hoik mawh pa khaw caw.
Mawh kheu hoik dawg mawh Ye-sux pa thung pui laig daux krax taoh sivoe heue.
11 Nawh si ghrah ka phi map kawn pui;
nawh mai pui pa hoik pon tix singax singeh,
rom tix koe keeing tix kaux khaing.
Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ang een pa kieh kix tix mawh nyiex tix lhang mawh krax pa kaig tua.
12 bNawh ah nin:
Siyiex eue,
aux tag sang krai pao ceu kaux maix hu ka pux aik pux o aux khankix;
aux tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw maix ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun heue.
13 cNawh kawx ah nin:
aux tag sang een rhawm tix ka Siyiex;
Baox ah nin:
jhak heue,
aux mai kawn simex kawn bun pa tawx Siyiex ka aux hoik ot dee in khawm uik heue!
14 Kheu hoik mawh kawn praix in kix pui pa koe nex nham khawm uik,
to kaux Ye-sux kawx hoik mawh awm to kaux kawn pui yuh nan.
Yuh nin,
mawh kheu krax yum nawh,
nawh hoik pon yuh laux leeing ka siawp sibrawm krax yum pa kuang phi caox,
15 mai hoik pluat ploi ka pui pa yuh siliex cawng kheu pa lhat graung ka daux pa im ju tix.
16 dIn hoik kied sidawng tete,
nawh ang teem coi ka kawn cau Siyiex,
mai hoik teem coi ka kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tag caw tix awm pux aik pux o tix khankix ka daux kaing ku ceu,
mawh cao giex kaing li kix,
pa koe krax caox rhawm pa kaux sijax plak sivoe Siyiex,
son sang kieh phi map kawn pui pon pa pluat ploi.
18 Mawh kheu nawh hoik cim ka ceu pa cheuk tix yaox,
mai krax pa kied kha rhawm;
yam in nawh hoik pon teem coi ka pui pa hoik cheuk cheu tix yaox ka heue.
