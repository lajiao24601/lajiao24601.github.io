Maw-si hoik pex ka simiang khankix
12
aSivoe in pui Yi-seu-liex pon kuang mai pon ka haktiex baoh rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
jauh ka ngawd Ya-noeng plak laong tom hoek ka rawm klawng ngawd Yaw-tang,
baox vih tix hoek ka gawng He-meung.
Kix pex ka simiang ra kaux.
2 An tix kaux mawh Si-hong pa mawh simiang pui Ya-maw-lix veng Si-seu-peung.
Meung haktiex nawh mawh khawm cie Ci-liex tix plak:
pa mawh Ya-law-eu praok ngawd Ya-noeng,
mai veng pa ot ka daux ngawd,
hoek ka rawm klawng Ya-paw ka mee haktiex Ya-meung.
3 Baox mawh khawm rawm klawng ngawd Yaw-tang:
jauh ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix,
tom hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
plak lih singaix hoek ka Paw Yie-si-mawx,
tom vih tix hoek ka gawng Pi-see-cia heue.
4 Kix kawx pex ka Eux simiang Pa-sang.
Nawh nghoik tix mawh pui Li-fa-ying tix kaux;
nawh kuang ka Ya-see-tha-lux mai Yi-teu-lai.
5 Meung nawh mawh khawm gawng He-meung,
Sa-cia,
mai Pa-sang khawm uik,
tom hoek ka mee haktiex Ci-sux mai Ma-cia;
lheu khaing an kawn koe haktiex Ci-liex tix plak,
tom vih tix hoek ka hawt mee haktiex Si-hong simiang Si-seu-peung heue.
6 bMaw-si mai pui Yi-seu-liex puing pex ka simiang in kiex ra kaux.
Siliex Simiang Siyiex Maw-si guah haktiex in kix ka ceu pui Lui-piien,
ceu pui Cia-teux,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak,
kieh mawh sin ngao kix heue.
Yaw-su-yax pex ka simiang khankix
7 Yaw-su-yax mai pui Yi-seu-liex puing pex ka simiang baoh haktiex plak klip singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
jauh ka Pa-lix Cia-teux daux ngawd Li-pa-noeng hoek ka dex gawng Ha-la plak seh Yi-tong.
Yaw-su-yax guah haktiex in ka ceu pui ku ceu,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix leeing ju heue.
8 Haktiex in mawh khawm gawng lua,
daux lung plak klip singaix,
rawm klawng ngawd Yaw-tang mai daux lung nawh,
dee caong gawng plak lih singaix,
khawm haktiex dee kroh plak seh.
Haktiex in yam jauh mawh dee ot pui Heux,
pui Ya-maw-lix,
pui Cia-nang,
pui Pi-li-si,
pui Si-we,
mai dee ot pui Yie-pu-see.
9 Pui Yi-seu-liex pex ka simiang veng in ku ceu plak greem:
Yie-li-keu,
Ai (ot dex ka Paw-theu-lix),
10 Yie-lu-sa-leung,
Si-paw-loeng,
11 Yie-maw,
La-ciex,
12 Yi-ci-loeng,
Ci-seux,
13 Ti-pix,
Ci-teux,
14 Heu-eu-ma,
Ya-la-teu,
15 Li-na,
Ya-tu-lang,
16 Ma-ci-tax,
Paw-theu-lix,
17 Tha-pu-yax,
Si-fu,
18 Ya-fu,
La-sa-loeng.
19 Ma-toeng,
Sha-saw,
20 Seung-loeng Mi-loeng,
Ya-sa,
21 Tha-na,
Mi-cie-to,
22 Ci-ti-see,
Yaw-nien (ot ka daux Cia-mix),
23 To-eux (pa ot praok rawm sigrit sigra),
Keu-ying (pa ot ka Cia-li-lixc),
24 mai Teu-sa,
khawm uik koe tix ngoe tix kaux heue.
