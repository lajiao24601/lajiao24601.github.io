Pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan
25
Simiang Ta-wue mai simiang pui Li-we tawx ceu cub pui Ya-sa,
Si-mang,
Yie-tu-toeng loe ceu pui Li-we mawh cao pa thung pui yuh kaing krub khankix.
Kix tag sang rhax ka gum lox Siyiex,
teei ting jung lah,
ting aliah lah mai chiang tauh tau mai lox rhax.
Plak greem mawh ca rang pui pa hoik laug mawh cao thung pui yuh kaing krub khankix,
mai beeh glawm kaing yuh cub caw mawx cub caw an:
2 Kawn simex Ya-sa pon kaux pa mawh Sa-kheux,
Yaw-seux,
Ni-thang-ya,
Ya-sa-li-la,
mawh Ya-sa pa thung kix.
Ya-sa priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa hoik tawx simiang,
mai rhax ka gum lox Siyiex lih heue.
3 Kawn simex Yie-tu-toeng liah kaux pa mawh Ci-ta-lix,
Si-lix,
Yie-sai-yax,
See-me,
Ha-sa-pi-ya,
Ma-tha-thi-ya,
kix rhax gum lox Siyiex ot ka greem pa sidiex gau keeing tix,
mai tauh ting jung lah,
rhax lox lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
4 Kawn simex Si-mang kao pon kaux pa mawh Pu-ci-ya,
Ma-thang-ya,
Wu-shiie,
Si-pu-yiex,
Yie-li-maw,
Ha-na-ni-ya,
Ha-na-ni,
Yi-li-ya-tha,
Ci-ta-li-thix,
Law-mang-thi Yi-shiex,
Yaw-see-pi-cia-sa,
Ma-law-thix,
Heu-thix,
Ma-ha-shiox.
5 Siyiex hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik deuh nawh,
tawx kawn simex in kix kao pon kaux ka cao jhai cie simiang pa kaux Si-mang,
mai baox koe kawn bun loe kaux,
kieh Si-mang koe siawp sibrawm.
6 Kawn simex Si-mang khankix,
ot ka greem pa sidiex gau keeing tix tauh ka ting jung lah,
ting aliah lah mai tauh ka chiang,
son krax pa yuh kaing krub ka daux nyiex buan.
Ya-sa,
Yie-tu-toeng,
mai Si-mang hoik nghiet ka lox tawx simiang heue.
7 Pui in kix tix nga pon kaux hoik mawh pui pa koe ping nyia,
mai paox ceu paox cad kix pui Li-we kawx hoik mawh pui pa gau tix chix yuh lox rhax krax klieh,
kix khawm uik koe pui ra yieh tix daix daix kaux heue.
8 Pui in kix mawh pui pa nhum ju ang ah,
pui pa riex ju ang ah,
pui pa koe ping nyia ang ah,
mai pui pa jauh tix gau tix ang ah,
mai hoik kieh mawh krax pa cawk lai pa ciak ci ka kaing pa sang yuh kix heue.
9-31 Pui ra yieh tix daix daix kaux in kix,
mai tang hawt ka cub nyiex tix guah kix koe tix nga pon cub,
tix cub kao ra kaux,
kieh cao kuang kix koe tix cub tix kaux.
