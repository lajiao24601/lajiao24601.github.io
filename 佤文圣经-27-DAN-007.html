Riang su sai pa yaox Tang-yi-li
(7.1–12.13)
Yaox ka to sat pa ting pon mu
7
1-2 Yam mawh Paw-sa-sha simiang Pa-pi-loeng ka daux tix num,
koe ka daux tix bo,
aux pa mawh Tang-yi-li ca maox,
mai yaox ka riang su sai.
Aux tom tiiem pa hoik yaox tix ka daux bo an lih mawh nin:
Koe bhaung pa phlu hoek khaing pon praok si daix daog,
mai klauh ka piang rawm sigrit sigra;
3 amai baox lih koe to sat pa ting pon mu pa ang greeh grang pak peung awm paox,
mai lih hoek khaing daux rawm.
4 bTo sat ka daux tix mu awm sivai sang si,
viang mawh nan,
koe preeik awm klang.
Yam kawn jhak aux kah,
preeik to sat an griah.
Pui yaog nawh kaoh cung,
caong nawh khawm ra khawn cung piang tiex,
awm pui yuh nan;
nawh koe kaing mawh kawn pui heue.
5 To sat ka daux ra mu awm krih,
koe ba ra khawn pa cung ka piang tiex;
rhang nawh khliak ka si-ang loe nyaing.
Koe lox ah nin ka nawh:
“Ih heue,
sau riang tix ih heue!”
6 Yam kawn jhak aux kah,
to sat tang mu baox lih koe.
To sat an awm sivai ngu,
ka piang blawg preeik koe pon mu,
awm preeik sim yuh nan,
mai baox koe kaing pon mu,
phan ex jhak tix yaox kied sidaing koe riang heue.
7 cYam kawn jhak aux kah,
to sat ka daux pon mu lih hoek.
To sat an kied ting riang pui lhat kah,
mai hoik kieh pui lhat graung ka tete.
To sat an teei rhang rhiem tix kiied ka to tiag phawng,
khaix an teei caong tix dug dig kah.
To sat an ang awm to sat an kix sivoe loe mu,
koe rung kao mu.
8 dYam kawn jhak aux tix yaox ka rung in kix,
rung pa koe sigreung kuah ka kawn rung iak tix mu;
kawn rung iak in laoh rung pa koe sivoe an kao mu glaoh loe mu.
Kawn rung iak in koe tawm ngai pui mai jheeh pa krai lox pa ting tix mu heue.
Yaox ka cao pa koe leeing ju
9 eYam kawn jhak aux kah,
aux yaox ka dang ngeung pa deuh tix ot dee an ra loe mu.
Cao pa koe leeing ju an ngawm ka piang dang ngeung ka daux tix mu.
Kraung ceep nawh hoik paing awm rawm rha yuh nan,
hauk kaing nawh hoik paing singax awm hauk yung yuh nan.
Dang ngeung pa ngawm nawh mawh rao ngu,
mai deuh ot ka piang jaong ling ngu pa hax.
10 fDang ngeung an ngu cap tix lih khaing,
awm rawm klawng pa hu yuh nan.
Pui kaing rheng kaing mheen krub nawh ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung an;
pui kaing shien mheeng cung ka plak sivoe ngai nawh.
Nawh krai pao paoh ka siviex nyiex prawng,
mai plak ka phuk lai heue.
11 Yam kawn jhak aux kah,
aux kawn mhong ka lox pa ting pa krai kawn rung iak an.
Yam dong aux ngai tix jhak,
pui pauh ka to sat ka daux pon mu,
to kaux yum to sat an pui taing tix gleeing ka daux rao ngu.
12 Phan mawh to sat pa kawn deuk an kix loe mu,
pui hoik teei hu ka akhuang kix,
viang mawh nan,
ca kawn kieh kix im ju tix baux bawg yam heue.
13 gKa daux riang su sai yam pon bo,
aux yaox ka pa awm greeh grang kawn kawn pui tix kaux,
mai brug ka pai awm hoek.
Pui thung nawh hu ka plak sivoe ngai cao pa koe leeing ju leeing phan an,
14 hrieb ka siawp sibrawm,
min plak ngai,
mai siawp sibrawm simiang,
son sang kieh meung ku meung,
pui ku ceu,
pui pa si kah ka lox ku ceu cu nghiet ka nawh khawm uik.
Nawh tag sang giex ka kaing leeing ju leeing phan;
siawp sibrawm nawh ang chix uik leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Kah ka dee bleeh riang su sai
15 Riang su sai pa hoik yaox aux kieh aux khi rhawm khi rhi,
mai kied ang nyawm ot nyawm koe.
16 Aux hu ka plak sivoe ngai pui cau tix kaux,
mai khiox khaw nawh kah dee bleeh in kix khawm uik son aux.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh kah dee bleeh riang su sai in son aux.
17 Nawh ah nin:
“To sat in pon mu mawh pa krai grawng ka piang haktiex tag sang lih koe meung ting pa haok huan pon mu.
18 iViang mawh nan,
pui cie Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang rieb ka siawp sibrawm simiang,
mai pon ka siawp sibrawm simiang,
tom hoek leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
19 Aux keud tix sang nie blawn tawng ka grawng to sat ka daux pon mu,
mawh kheu to sat pa lhat pui in ang awm to sat tang mu loe mu khankix.
To sat an teei gip tawng tix,
mai rhang rhiem tix kiied ka to tiag phawng,
khaix an teei caong tix dug dig kah.
20 Aux kawx hoik keud tix sang tawng ka dee bleeh rung to sat an kao mu,
mai khaix an rung iak pa baox lih kuah pa laoh ka rung tang mu an kix loe mu glaoh.
Rung iak an koe tawm ngai kawn pui mai jheeh pa krai lox ting tix mu,
kieh pui kawn lheu lhat ka tix khaing rung tang mu heue.
21 jYam kawn jhak aux kah,
rung iak an yuh naing ka pui cie Siyiex,
mai hoik pon kuang ka kix.
22 lKhaix an,
cao pa koe leeing ju leeing phan hoek bleeh,
mai prawng rauh see son pui cie Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong.k
Bawg yam rieb pui cie Siyiex ka siawp sibrawm simiang hoik hoek heue.
23 Pui cau an baox kah dee bleeh ka aux ah nin:
“To sat ka daux pon mu mawh pa krai grawng ka piang haktiex tag sang lih koe meung ting pa haok huan pon mu.
Meung an kix ang awm meung tang mu yuh nan,
nawh tag sang bleet ka haktiex khawm uik,
dug dig kah,
mai yuh haktiex laux leeing.
24 mRung an kao mu mawh pa krai grawng simiang sang toe tix giex kaing meung ting an kao kaux.
Khaix an koe simiang tang kaux tix kaux yaog tix kaoh,
mai ang awm simiang pa koe sivoe an;
nawh tag sang pon pex ka simiang loe kaux.
25 nNawh tag sang krai lox ting lox lhaong lih khaing jheeh tix,
mai tieb tie ka Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
khaix an yuh krax pa tuk tiak tuk te ka pui cie Siyiex.
Nawh keud sawn tix sang tieh loh ka lox tat jhawm prawng kix,
mai ka poi.
Pui cie Siyiex tag sang rieb ka pa giex nawh kaing loe num plak.
26 “Khaix an,
cao pa prawng ca rao ma tag sang paoh ka nyiex prawng,
mai prawng ca kah,
mai teei ka siawp sibrawm pa giex nawh kaing,
mai yuh nawh laux lak pak leeing.
27 oPui cie Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang rieb ka siawp sibrawm pa ting meung piang haktiex ku meung;
siawp sibrawm simiang kix ang chix uik leeing ju leeing phan,
can simiang ka piang haktiex tag sang cu nghiet ka kix khawm uik heue.”
28 Kaing in hoik die dee in.
Phan mawh aux pa mawh Tang-yi-li aux kied sidaing lhat graung kah,
mai pak ngai aux hoik tieh tix paing.
Aux priex kaing in ot ka daux rhawm rhi tix khawm uik heue.
