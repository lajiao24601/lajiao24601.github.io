Teug sijax mukheu ot haktiex Fe-li-see
5
Khaix hoik pon teei pui Fe-li-see ka teug sijax mukheu Siyiex,
kix jauh ka haktiex Yi-piien-yi-shiex grawng hoek ka veng Ya-seu-thu,
2 grawng laig ka daux nyiex vad Ta-koeng kix,
deuh ot ka praok riang siyiex Ta-koeng.
3 Ka daux ra singaix pon ngawb cao cao,
pui Ya-seu-thu yaox ka riang siyiex Ta-koeng,
pak ngaix tawx tix piang tiex goh piang tiex ka sivoe ngai teug sijax mukheu Siyiex.
Kix yaog nawh cung,
mai deuh nawh ka dee ot nawh.
4 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
yam kaoh kix khaing it,
mai baox yaox riang an goh ot ka sivoe ngai teug sijax mukheu.
Bawg in,
kaing riang an mai plawng taix khawm ra khawn hoik pot,
goh ot ka diang siviex,
mai deuk ngie to kaux khaing heue.
5 Kheu nan,
tom hoek in singaix,
cao giex kaing li riang siyiex Ta-koeng mai can pui pa ot Ya-seu-thu khawm uik,
hoik diang ka pang siviex nyiex vad Ta-koeng,
mai ang pon diang ka heue.
6 Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka Ya-seu-thu mai kawn pui pa ot vung viang haktiex an khawm pon daog,
mai kieh kix ot ka daux krax pa lhat pa graung,
hoik kieh muad yiaa naog ka khawm uik.
7 Kix yaox grawng pa yuh nin,
tom ah nin:
“Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik ciyang ka ex mai ka siyiex Ta-koeng ex.
Ex ang pon tix lai deuh teug sijax mukheu ot tin heue.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom ploi pui hu kok simiang pui Fe-li-see phuan kaux hoek,
chawk kix ah nin:
“Ex sang yuh ka mawx ka teug sijax mukheu Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex lie?”
Kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Viex hu ka haktiex Cia-theux heue!”
Mawh kheu nan pui haoh pui hun tom viex teug sijax mukheu hu ka dee an.
9 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik hoek teug sijax mukheu dee an,
Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka veng an nang,
mai kieh krax lhat krax graung lih tix koe.
Siyiex kieh can pui pa ot ka daux veng khawm uik muad yia,
khawm pui ting pui iak awm paox.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
kix deeh viex teug sijax mukheu hu tawx ka haktiex Fe-li-see veng tang mu tix veng,
pa mawh veng Yi-keu-loeng.
Yam hoik viex kix teug sijax mukheu hoek,
pui haktiex an hoik riag yo khawm uik ah nin:
“Kix viex teug sijax mukheu Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoek,
tag sang kieh ex laux lak pak leeing heue!”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
kix baox deeh hu kok ka simiang pui Fe-li-see hoek,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Deeh viex ing tawx teug sijax mukheu Yi-seu-liex ka dee ot sivoe an heue!”
Yuh nin,
nawh kawx ang chix kieh ex mai kawn pui ex laux leeing.
Ka daux veng hoik suk sag khawm uik,
kheu hoik mawh Siyiex pa ciyang ka kix.
12 Viang mawh pui pa ang bax yum khankix kawx,
to kaux kix hoik naog ka muad yia khawm uik.
Ex ku kaux kok yo siyiex kix taoh tix heue.
