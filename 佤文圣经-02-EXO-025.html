Tan lu son nyiex pa singax singeh
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 35.4–9)
25
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
nhie kix tan lu ka aux.
Can pui pa gaux rhawm tix tan khawng phak ka aux,
pex rieb ka khawm uik.
3 Khawng phak pa caw pex tix rieb ka mawh:
khri,
mau,
tawng;
4 man keei sum;
hauk yung pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt;
man pa taing ka hauk pex.
5 Hauk yung mhaing pa siang tix raoh;
hak pa mhawm;
khaox Cing-heu-huang;
6 bee kawk ngu;
nyawng bhawng pa yuh ka bee pa singax singeh mai ceu sidah bhawng,
7 siao kraix ka sibex Yi-fu-teu cao giex kaing li pa tinga,
mai na bat ka riang naok siang simaox bawd raoh mai siang simaox tang ceu heue.
8 Pui haoh pui hun caw tix sum nyiex pa singax singeh son aux tix lhang,
son sang ot aux ka daux kix.
9 Phan mawh kraung ka daux nyiex gox buan man in,
pex caw tix yuh hawt awm pak peung pa nhie aux pex yuh heue.”
Teug sijax mukheu
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 37.1–9)
10 “Caw tix yuh teug sijax mukheu ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
plak lang tix yieh kao kongfeeng,
plak vieh tix gliah liah kongfeeng,
plak lhaong tix gliah liah kongfeeng.
11 Caw tix om khri mhawm ka plak daux khawm plak praix teug,
mai kawb khri hawt praok khawm uik.
12 Caw tix raug jeep khri pon mu son sang grawng kah,
mai kab ot ka piang jaong teug khawm pon jaong,
tix plak ra mu.
13 Caw tix yuh khaox glawm ra gawng ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka khri,
14 mai chup ka daux jeep khri teug an khawm ra plak.
15 Khaox glawm in ra gawng,
caw tix kieh ot ka daux jeep khri an jheeng jheeng,
mai ang pon tix lawd lih.
16 Sau khlawp simaox an ra khlawp pa tawx aux ka maix pa hoik tiiem gum lox ping nyap ot daux.
17 b“Caw tix yuh dawb teug tix mu ka khri mhawm,
plak lang koe tix yieh kao kongfeeng,
plak vieh tix gliah liah kongfeeng.
18 Baox tawk riang Ci-lu-paw c ra mu ka khri,
19 ka daog dawb koe tix plak tix mu,
mai kleuk tix tau mai dawb,
lih mawh tix mu.
20 Ci-lu-paw an ra mu veeh ngai tix plak paox,
mai di preeik tix tawb teug sijax mukheu heue.
21 Caw tix sau khlawp simaox an ra khlawp ka daux teug,
sau dawb ot ka piang teug.
22 Aux tag sang si pup maix tan,
mai ot ka piang dawb teug si nax Ci-lu-paw khawm ra mu,
tawx lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tiiem aux son pui Yi-seu-liex khankix ka maix heue.”
Pheen deuh khao mun
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 37.10–16)
23 “Caw tix yuh pheen tix lhang ka khaox Cing-heu-huang;
plak lang tix daix daix kongfeeng,
plak vieh tipon pon kongfeeng,
plak lhaong tix gliah kongfeeng.
24 Om ka khri mhawm,
mai om khri ka hawt vung viang.
25 Vung viang pheen baox sau lat pa koe ka praok diag atigliah phuan kongli,
mai praok tag kawb ka khri.
26 Caw tix yuh daux grawng jeep khri pon mu,
mai sau ka jaong pheen khawm pon daog.
27 Jeep khri an kieh dex ka praok,
son sang chix chup ka khaox grawng pheen laig.
28 Caw tix yuh khaox glawm ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
mai om ka khri.
29 Caw tix yuh pan,
cuan,
si ngien mai kawk pa prauk plai,
pa ot piang pheen ka khri mhawm.
30 dPheen caw tix deuh ot ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu,
ka piang pheen an,
deuh khao mun pa tan lu ka aux ot piang jheeng jheeng heue.”
Jawk kawk ngu
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 37.17–24)
31 “Caw tix yuh jawk kawk ngu tix mu ka khri mhawm,
jawk kawk ngu,
mai jaong,
num pang caw tix tawk ka khri;
kho tai,
tux tai,
lawm tai,
caw tix kieh si doe paox lih mawh tix mu tau.
32 Jawk kawk ngu ra plak kieh lih koe liah kak,
tix plak loe gawng.
33 Tix gawng kieh koe loe mu kho tai,
greeh grang awm tai khaox Batan,
koe tux koe lawm.
34 Rong jawk kawk ngu kieh koe pon mu kho tai,
greeh grang awm tai khaox Batan,
koe tux,
koe lawm.
35 Kak loe brawm ku brawm ka greem,
koe tix mu lawm tai.
36 Lawm tai,
kak,
mai jawk kawk ngu tag tawk lih mawh ka khri mhawm tix khlawp tau.
37 Caw tix yuh jan son jawk kawk ngu aliah jan,
kawk ngu tag tok jhan riang plak sivoe.
38 Caw tix siao khri mhawm yuh si kiep kip tai kawn kawk ngu mai pan tok ngu.
39 Caw tix siao khri mhawm tix ngoe phuan kongcing yuh jawk kawk ngu mai kraung pa siao ka khawm uik.
40 eCaw priex rhawm tix,
hawt awm pak peung pa krai aux ka maix piang gawng yuh nan.”
