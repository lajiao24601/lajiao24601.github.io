Kawn simex kawn bun simiang Ta-wue
3
1-3 Can kawn simex pa koe Ta-wue ka daux Si-paw-loeng,
jauh ka pa ting hoik ka pa iak,
ceu kaux kix tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Ang-noeng:
miex nawh mawh pui Yie-see-liex pa kaux Ya-si-nuang.
Tang-yi-lix:
miex nawh mawh pui Cia-mix pa kaux Ai-pi-kai
Ya-sa-long:
miex nawh mawh Ma-cia,
kawn bun simiang Ci-sux pa kaux Ta-mai
Ya-to-ni-ya:
miex nawh mawh Ha-ci.
See-fa-thi-ya:
miex nawh mawh Ya-pi-tha.
Yi-theu-nian:
miex nawh mawh Yi-keu-la.
4 aKawn simex in kix liah kaux plak piang,
hoik mawh pa keh Ta-wue yam mawh tix simiang ka haktiex Si-paw-loeng ka daux aliah num plak.
Ta-wue mawh simiang tix ngoe loe num ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung:
5 bKawn simex nawh tom nie hoik keh baux yam an ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
Moeng nawh Pa-see-pa mawh kawn bun Ya-mi-lix,
mai keh nawh cie kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh See-mi-yax,
Saw-pax,
Na-tang,
Saw-law-meung.
6 Nawh koe kawn simex tang kaux dim kaux,
pa mawh Yi-sia,
Yi-li-su-yax,
Yi-fa-liex,
7 Naw-cia,
Ni-fe,
Ya-fe-yax,
8 Yi-li-sa-ma,
Yi-li-ya-tax,
Yi-li-fa-liex.
9 Lheu kaing kawn simex in kix,
Ta-wue kawn baox keh kawn simex tang kaux khaing moeng iak tix pa ot daux nyiex haw simiang.
Nawh kawx koe kawn bun tix kaux,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Tha-ma heue.
Doeh ceu pui simiang Saw-law-meung
10 Doeh ceu pui simiang Saw-law-meung,
jauh ka nyiex keeing kix toe tix hu tiiem ot plak greem:
mawh Saw-law-meung,
Law-paw-ang,
Ya-pi-yax,
Ya-sa,
Yaw-sa-fa,
11 Yaw-lang,
Ya-ha-shiex,
Yaw-a-see,
12 Ya-ma-shiex,
Ya-sa-li-ya,
Yaw-thang,
13 Ya-ha-see,
Si-si-cia,
Ma-na-si,
14 Ya-meung,
Yaw-si-yax.
15 Yaw-si-yax koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Yaw-ha-nang,
Yaw-ya-cing,
Si-ti-cia,
Sa-long.
16 Yaw-ya-cing koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Yaw-ya-cin mai Si-ti-cia heue.
Doeh ceu pui Yaw-ya-cin
17 Plak greem mawh doeh ceu cad simiang Yaw-ya-cin pa hoik giex pui Pa-pi-loeng hu.
Yaw-ya-cin koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
pa mawh Sa-la-thiie,
18 Ma-ci-lang,
Pi-ta-ya,
See-na-sax,
Yie-cia-mi,
Heu-sa-max,
Ni-ta-pi-ya.
19 Pi-ta-ya koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Saw-law-pa-paw mai See-me.
Saw-law-pa-paw koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Mi-su-lang mai Ha-na-ni-ya,
mai koe kawn bun tix kaux pa kaux See-law-mix.
20 Nawh kawn koe kawn simex tang kaux phuan kaux,
pa mawh Ha-su-pa,
A-he,
Pi-li-cia,
Ha-sha-tix,
Yui-sa Si-si heue.
21 Ha-na-ni-ya koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Pi-la-thi mai Yie-sai-yax.
Yie-sai-yax keh Li-fa-ya;
Li-fa-ya keh Ya-eu-nang;
Ya-eu-nang keh Eu-pa-ti-yax;
Eu-pa-ti-yax keh See-cia-ni.
22 See-cia-ni koe kawn simex tix kaux pa kaux See-ma-ya,
koe kawn saux phuan kaux,
pa mawh Ha-thux,
Yi-cia,
Pa-li-yax,
Ni-li-ya,
Sa-fa.
23 Ni-li-ya koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Yi-li-yaw-nai,
Si-si-cia,
Ya-see-li-kang.
24 Yi-li-yaw-nai koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
pa mawh Heu-ta-yax,
Yi-li-ya-seu,
Pi-lai-ya,
A-kux,
Yaw-ha-nang,
Ti-lai-ya,
A-na-ni heue.
