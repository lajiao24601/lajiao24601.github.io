Lai Cia-la-thaix
Krai dawt
Yam jauh gum lox buan tix sidiex hoek ka daux pa ang mawh pui Yo-thaix, mai pui hoik kied gaux rhawm tix rieb, ka daux ceu cao jauh tix kum kawng grawng sang hawt ang hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si, mai tag pon tix mawh kawn Khrit tete. Pao-law een grawng in ang mawh kaing ting. Nawh krai ah nin: phan sang kieh tix pon ju ka daux Khrit, kaing ting mawh ngie ka daux krax yum rhawm tix ceu khaing; mawh kheu krax yum rhawm, kawn pui tag pon tix yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw. Viang mawh nan, yam giex Law-ma kaing ka piang haktiex Cia-la-thaix, haktiex Ya-si-yax pa iak, ceu cao pa ot daux haktiex ra loe mu, koe pui tieb tie Pao-law ka daux haktiex Cia-la-thaix, krai pui tag caw tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si tom taug pon yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw heue.
Pao-law tiiem Lai Cia-la-thaix kaing ting mawh son sang viex kawn Khrit pa rieb ka krax sidiex gau pa ang khaw khankix, deeh hoek ka daux been pa khaw mai krax ot krax koe. Pao-law ran krai lox son pa mawh tix kawn cau sivoe. Nawh krai mawh tix kawn cau hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Siyiex, ang mawh pa kaoh khaing akhuang cie kawn pui, kheu nan nawh hoik koe beeh glawm son pa ang mawh pui Yo-thaix. Kheu nan, nawh krai rhawm keud cao tix, mawh ngie ka daux krax yum rhawm khaing, pui tom taug pon yuh paox pie tau mai Siyiex ka daux pa khaw caw. Dee nghoik, Pao-law krai ah nin: mawh ngie ka daux krax yum rhawm tix ceu kaing, kawn Khrit tom taug pon krax pa bheeik luan tete, krax bheeik luan in caw tix mawh ka daux krax muh krax rhawm pa jhi lih ka krax ot krax koe heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa jauh tix krai (1.1–10)
2. Akhuang cie mawh Pao-law kawn cau (1.11–2.21)
3. Lox buan cie buan son Siyiex (3.1–4.31)
4. Krax luat luan mai beeh glawm kawn Khrit (5.1–6.10)
5. Lox ghoik (6.11–18)
