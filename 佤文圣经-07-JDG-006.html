Ci-tiien
6
Pui Yi-seu-liex phit lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan Simiang Siyiex ap kix ka daux tawm taix Mi-tiien aliah num.
2 Pui Mi-tiien ting riang khaing pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui Yi-seu-liex tom mox tix ka daux rang,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka dee glip piang gawng khankix.
3 Yam sau pui Yi-seu-liex simie,
pui Mi-tiien tom rom tix mai pui Ya-ma-lix,
khawm pui pa liang khawng brie daux tiag khankix,
mai hoek tix puing ka kix.
4 Pui pa koeng in kix sum nyiex bang si tiep kix,
mai yuh to ma kix laux leeing,
tom hoek ka Cia-sa plak seh haktiex.
Kix tud rhim can yung,
moi,
mai la khankix khawm uik hu,
mai ang deuh tid tix ceeix kawx,
son sang kieh mawh ceu priex preem son pui Yi-seu-liex.
5 Kix hoik haoh hun awm sido khankix,
mai viex phung moi,
nyiex gox bang preeng tix hoek;
mai ka-la-uk kix kawx hoik haoh hun tom ang lai dak uik simin.
Kix laig ka daux meung haktiex,
kieh laux leeing khawm uik,
6 pui Yi-seu-liex ang pon tieb tie,
kix tom khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
7 Yam khiox khaw pui Yi-seu-liex Simiang Siyiex teem coi kix tieb tie ka pui Mi-tiien,
8 Simiang Siyiex tawx cao jhai lih hoek ka kix dee an tix kaux,
mai sidiex gum lox Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ka kix,
ah nin:
“Aux hoik teei pex luat luan khaing Ai-cix,
luat luan khaing dee pa yuh siliex.
9 Aux hoik taoh pex lih khaing daux tawm taix pui Ai-cix;
aux kawx hoik taoh pex luat luan khaing daux tawm taix pui Cia-nang,
pa mawh pui haktiex sivoe an,
yam preeng pex tix laig,
aux hoik grheu kix hu,
mai tawx haktiex kix ka pex.
10 Aux hoik krai ka pex,
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex;
pex ang pon tix krub ka siyiex pui Ya-maw-lix,
pa mawh pui haktiex sivoe an.
Viang mawh nan,
pex ang nghiet ka aux heue.”
11 Khaix nan,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex hoek bleeh ka yaong Eu-fu-la,
ngawm ka greem khaox yang yit nyiex ma Yaw-a-see;
Yaw-a-see mawh cub nyiex ma Ya-pi-yi-shiex,
kawn simex nawh Ci-tiien briex mox tix pauh nghox ca dee yuh ka plai,
ang kieh pui Mi-tiien tawng.
12 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex jhi nawh yaox tix dee an,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Pui pa ting riang ting rao khankix eue,
Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai maix heue!”
13 Ci-tiien ah nin:
“Sara,
phan Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai yix,
yuh ka mawx yix lai kawn si pup ka kaing pa ang mhawm in kix yuh nin lie?
Tax du yix hoik krai ka yix,
Simiang Siyiex hoik teei yix lih khaing daux Ai-cix yuh ka mawx,
daux in singaix,
mhai sakse pa daox pa am an kix ot dee mawx lie?
Simiang Siyiex hoik tik vud ka yix,
mai hoik ap yix ka daux tawm taix pui Mi-tiien heue.”
14 Simiang Siyiex veeh tix plak Ci-tiien,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix sau riang rao tix taoh pui Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing pui Mi-tiien.
Aux sang ploi to kaux maix hu heue.”
15 Ci-tiien pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
aux sang pon taoh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux hoik mawh ceu pui Ma-na-si pa iak riang khaing paox;
mai mawh pui pa ang ting ceu ka daux nyiex ma tix heue.”
16 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang ot tau mai maix.
Maix tag sang pex ka ceu pui Mi-tiien yie yie nan khaing,
awm hoik lauh ka pui tix kaux yuh heue.”
17 Ci-tiien pawk lox ah nin:
“Phan aux khaw rhawm maix,
viex maix tawx mhai sakse ka aux,
son sang kham sakse Simiang Siyiex hoik si kah ka aux.
18 Paw vi hu khaing aux,
krox aux viex priex preem tix blah ka maix.”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux krox jun maix hoek heue.”
19 Kheu nan,
Ci-tiien ing ka nyiex,
mai ngaik kawn pex tix mu,
mai baox yuh khao mun pa ang koe su saix ka mien kao kongcing.
Nawh deuh nex ot ka daux khrawng,
sau rawm rheep ka daux aw,
viex hu tawx ka kawn cau Siyiex pa ot greem khaox yang yit.
20 Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Teei nex mai khao mun pa ang koe su saix,
deuh ot ka piang khlawp simaox in,
mai hieh rawm rheep ka piang.”
Ci-tiien hawt tix yuh awm an.
21 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex lawd taix tix mai teei ka khaox gix,
toh khaox gix theeh ka nex mai khao mun.
Koe ngu buing tix lih khaing simaox an,
gleeing ka nex mai khao mun uik;
khaix an mai ang lai yaox ka kawn cau Siyiex heue.
22 Ci-tiien hoik tawng pa yaox nawh mawh kawn cau Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tom lhat graung mai ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
aux khex plak ngai tawx plak ngai mai yaox ka kawn cau Siyiex heue.”
23 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Ci-tiien:
“Kieh koe pa nhim!
Paw vi lhat graung.
Maix ang doh yaox tix yum.”
24 Ci-tiien tom sum khu dee tan dee an tix mu son Simiang Siyiex,
mai kok nawh mawh “Yie-heu-hua Sa-long” a Khu in ca kawn ot ka daux ceu pui Ya-pi-yi-shiex pa mawh Eu-fu-la heue.
25 Ka daux bo an,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka Ci-tiien ah nin:
“Maix viex kawn moi mhaing cie keeing maix mai kawn moi mhaing pa koe aliah num tix mu bhu,
yuh laux ka khu pa yuh nawh son Pa-lix,
mai kid goh ka rong khaox Ya-seu-la pa ot ka praok khu.
26 Maix tag caw tix yuh khu pa mhawm sidu tix mu son Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex maix ot ka gawng iak in,
mai teei khaox Ya-seu-la pa hoik kid maix goh yuh mawh khix,
teei kawn moi mhaing ka daux ra muc deuh ot ka piang,
son sang gleeing mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing.”
27 Kheu nan,
Ci-tiien viex pui cau tix kao kaux,
mai hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka nawh.
Mawh kheu lhat nawh ka pui nyiex tix mai pui pa ot ka daux veng,
nawh ang pon yuh yam riang praix,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom yuh pon bo heue.
28 Ka daux singaix khaix pon ngawb cao cao,
pui veng khankix kaoh,
mai yaox ka khu dee tan Pa-lix mai ka khaox Ya-seu-la pa ot ka praok dee an hoik yuh tix laux,
kawn moi mhaing an daux ra mu,
hoik gleeing tix ot ka piang khu pa khraox sum an.
29 Kix ra lai chawk ah nin:
“Mawh mawx pa yuh ceu in lie?”
Kix si kaw tix yaox mai tawng mawh Ci-tiien kawn simex Yaw-a-see pa yuh.
30 Kix tom ah nin ka Yaw-a-see:
“Teei kawn simex maix lih,
yix sang pauh nawh yum!
Nawh hoik yuh ka khu dee tan Pa-lix laux,
mai hoik kid ka khaox Ya-seu-la pa ot ka praok goh heue.”
31 Yaw-a-see pawk lox kawn pui ah nin:
“Pex sang ah lox tang Pa-lix,
mai keud tix sang teem nawh lie?
Mawx pa ah lox tang nawh,
sivoe sang riang praix,
tag sang pauh nawh yum.
Phan Pa-lix mawh Siyiex,
mai tang kieh nawh ah lox tang cao tix!
Kheu hoik mawh khu nawh pa yieh tix laux!”
32 Jauh singaix yam an,
pui tag kok Yaw-a-see mawh Yie-lu.
Pa-lixd,
mawh kheu Yaw-a-see ah nin:
“Kieh Pa-lix tang ah lox tang cao tix heue!
Kheu hoik mawh khu nawh pa yieh tix laux heue!”
33 Yam an,
pui Mi-tiien,
pui Ya-ma-lix,
mai ceu pui pa ot ka daux tiag rom paox tix,
tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai sum bang ot ka ngawd Yie-see-liex.
34 Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka Ci-tiien,
nawh tom phlu teet,
mai khrawm ka ceu pui Ya-pi-yi-shiex hawt khaix nawh.
35 Nawh ploi kawn cau tix hu ka haktiex Ma-na-si ku dauh ku dee,
mai khrawm kix hoek hawt khaix tix.
Nawh kawx baox ploi pui cau tix hu ka ceu pui Ya-seux,
ceu pui Si-pu-loeng,
mai ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix khankix;
kix kawx hoek khrawm tix tau mai nawh khawm uik heue.
36 Mawh kheu nan,
Ci-tiien ah nin ka Siyiex:
“Maix hoik krai tix sang teei aux hoek taoh ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.
37 Phan mawh nan,
aux sang deuh hauk yung ot ka glauh dee pauh yix nghox ca,
hoek pon ngawb cao,
phan mawh ngie hauk yung pa koe rhawm ciex ka khaing,
mai piang tiex hoik kroh,
aux tom tawng mawh maix pa sang teei aux taoh ka pui Yi-seu-liex.”
38 Khawm uik hoik lih tix mawh awm lox pa krai nawh.
Ci-tiien kaoh cao cao,
nawh biiet ka hauk yung an,
mai biiet rawm ciex lih khaing tix klong.
39 Ci-tiien baox ah nin ka Siyiex:
“Paw vi saux rhawm ka aux,
kieh aux baox rawg tix bawg ha,
mai baox teei hauk yung cheu tix yaox tix bawg.
Ka daux bawg in maix kieh hauk yung kroh,
mai kieh ka piang tiex koe ka rawm ciex.”
40 Ka daux bo an,
Siyiex hawt tix yuh awm an.
Ka daux singaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
hauk yung rug kroh,
mai ka piang tiex hoik naog ka rawm ciex khawm uik heue.
