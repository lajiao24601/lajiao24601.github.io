Pui Yi-seu-liex ang cu nghiet ka Simiang Siyiex
9
“Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix eue,
bre tix nghiet!
In singaix pex sang tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai cax ka haktiex cie pui pa ting lhaong khaing pex.
Veng kix kied ting lhaong,
ding veng lhaong hoek tom cux pai awm.
2 Pui pa ot ka daux haktiex an kied ting kied lhaong,
kix mawh doeh ceu cad pa ting;
pex kawx hoik mhong krai pui kah,
mai ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon tieb tie ka kix.
3 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix,
pex sang yaox Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
awm rao ngu pa kied hax hu plak sivoe pex.
Yam greed pex tix hu sivoe ngai,
nawh sang yuh kix laux leeing som nai,
awm pa hoik ap nawh pex laig ang lai koe pa tieb tie,
mai grheu pui koeng lih hu,
yuh kix laux leeing heue.
4 “Khaix hoik grheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex pui koeng lih hu son pex,
pex ang pon tix krai ah nin,
Simiang Siyiex hoik viex pex cax ka haktiex an hoik mawh buan son pa yuh pex,
hoik mawh pa khaw pex tix pon kah.
Ang doh mawh!
In hoik mawh kheu jao krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh ceu pui pa ot ka daux haktiex an,
Simiang Siyiex tom grheu kix lih hu;
5 ang mawh kheu jao pa mhawm rhawm pex,
mai mawh pui rauh see mhawm kaing yuh,
Simiang Siyiex tom kieh pex cax ka haktiex kix.
Simiang Siyiex grheu kix lih hu,
mawh kheu jao pui in kix hoik mawh pui pa caox pa caig,
mai baox mawh kheu jao pa hawt nawh tix yuh ka lox pa ap nawh ka tax du pex,
pa mawh Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
in ang doh mawh kheu jao pa mhawm rhawm pex,
Simiang Siyiex tom tawx haktiex pa mhawm in ka pex,
tete te lai,
pex hoik mawh ceu pui pa kiien rhawm heue.
7 “Ang pon tix piawm ka yam cheeik pex Simiang Siyiex song rhawm song rhi ka daux tiag.
Jauh ka singaix lih pex khaing daux Ai-cix tom hoek yam in,
mai pex hoik tieh vheen ka nawh ang jud ang jai.
8 Viang mawh yam ot ka gawng Heu-liex kawx,
pex kawx hoik cheeik nawh song rhawm,
mai nawh dex sang yuh pex laux leeing heue.
9 aYam an aux haok ka piang gawng,
son sang rieb aux tix teei khlawp simaox sijax mukheu,
pa mawh piang khlawp simaox hoik tiiem gum lox sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex tau mai pex,
yam an aux ot ka piang gawng tipon ngaix tipon sawm,
aux ang ih,
mai ang nyaux.
10 Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex ap khlawp simaox in ra khlawp ka aux.
Khlawp simaox in ra khlawp mawh gum lox pa hoik tiiem ka giien taix nawh,
mawh singaix khrawm pex paox tix ka piang gawng,
gum lox pa si kah nawh ka pex khawm uik khaing sigreung rao ngu.
11 Khaix hoik luan tipon singaix tipon sawm,
Simiang Siyiex ap tix tawx khlawp simaox sijax mukheu an ra khlawp ka aux heue.
12 “Khaix an,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
‘Maix caw tix nied lih khaing gawng,
mawh kheu pui pa viex maix lih khaing Ai-cix khankix hoik yuh krax pa laux pa leeing,
kaing pa caox pa caig.
Kix hoik veeh tix hu khaing gum lox pa tawm aux ka kix,
mai hoik raug riang son to kaux tix heue.’
13 “Simiang Siyiex baox krai ka aux ah nin:
‘Aux hoik tawng ka ceu pui in khankix,
mawh ceu pui pa kied kiien rhawm kiien rhi.
14 Aux sang yuh kix laux leeing,
mai ang lai kieh pui tix kaux kawx lai doh keud ka kix;
maix paw vi tieb tie ka aux.
Aux tag kieh maix mawh ceu keeing pui tix meung;
meung cie maix tag lheu ting riang ting rao khaing kix heue.’
15 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom teei ka khlawp simaox pa hoik tiiem ka gum lox sijax mukheu an ra khlawp,
mai veeh tix lih khaing piang gawng;
ka piang gawng rao ngu ca kawn cap kah.
16 Aux yaox pex hoik phit hoik lud ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
mai pex hoik raug riang kawn moi son to kaux tix.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom si vun tix tik khlawp simaox an ra khlawp max piang tiex sivoe pex,
mai kieh max bhawng vid.
18 Aux baox deeh gup tix krub ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
tipon singaix tipon sawm mai ang yaox tix ih,
ang yaox tix nyaux.
In hoik mawh kheu jao pa phit pa lud pex ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai cheeik nawh song rhawm song rhi.
19 bAux lhat graung ka krax pa song rhawm song rhi Siyiex,
mawh kheu krax pa song rhawm song rhi nawh hoik kub tix yuh pex laux leeing.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex hoik baox deeh nghiet ka lox pa rawg aux buan.
20 Ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi Simiang Siyiex hoik ca dex sang pauh ka Ya-loeng,
viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn rawg buan son nawh.
21 Aux teei tix tik gleeing ka riang kawn moi khri pa hoik raug pex tix yuh,
pa hoik kieh pex phit lud an ka daux ngu,
mai lauh phawng vid,
mai krid lih phawng,
mai rhuat ka daux rawm klawng pa kaoh khaing gawng heue.
22 c“Yam ot pex hawt baoh Tha-pe-la,
Ma-sha,
mai Ci-paw-law Ha-tha-wa khankix,
pex kawx hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm song rhi.
23 dYam ploi nawh pex lih khaing haktiex Cia-ti-see Pa-ni-yax,
mai tawx lox nhie pex haok cax tix cie haktiex pa hoik tawx nawh ka pex,
pex lai baox tieh vheen ka nawh,
mai ang yum rhawm ka nawh,
mai ang cu nghiet ka nawh.
24 Tom yam jauh aux tix tawng yung ka pex,
pex hoik tieh vheen ka Simiang Siyiex jheeng jheeng heue.
25 “Aux gup tix krub ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex tipon singaix tipon sawm,
kheu hoik tawng aux nawh sang but rhawm tix yuh pex laux leeing.
26 Mawh kheu nan,
aux rawg buan ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong eue,
rawg maix paw yuh pui cie tix khankix laux leeing liak.
Kix hoik mawh pa teei maix ka taix pa ting riang ting rao taoh lih khaing Ai-cix.
27 Rawg maix doh keud doh sawn ka siliex maix Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux liak,
paw nyang jhak ka pa kiien rhawm pui cie maix,
mai pa caox pa caig kix,
mai phi map phi ghram kix.
28 Phan ang mawh nan,
pui Ai-cix khankix tag sang ah,
maix ang pon viex kix hu ka daux haktiex pa hoik tawx maix ka kix.
Kix tag sang ah nin,
maix koeng ka pui cie to kaux tix,
mai viex kix hu ka daux tiag daux tu,
song sang pauh ka kix.
29 Pui in kix viang yuh ka mawx kawx,
kix hoik mawh nan pui cie maix,
pa hoik teei maix pa ting riang rao tix,
viex kix lih khaing Ai-cix heue.’ ”
