Siyiex sang tawx krax pa caox rhawm
55
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
Pui pa sang nyaux rawm khankix eue,
hoek heue,
dee in hoik koe ka rawm nyaux heue!
Pui pa ang koe mau eue,
hoek heue,
hoek liag tix ih ka priex preem heue,
ang lai siao lo ka mau khri,
ang lai tawx ka ceu pa tid tix,
hoek liag ka plai mai ka rawm teeh heue!
2 Yuh ka mawx lai hu liag ka ceu pa ang pui chix sak ka lie?
Yuh ka mawh lai nai ka ngawh riang tix,
mai ca kawn cai rhawm cai rhi lie?
Nghiet ka lox aux,
mai hawt tix yuh awm lox pa krai aux,
maix tom taug pon ka priex preem pa blawn nyawm,
kieh rhawm rhi maix yaox tix sak heue.
 
3 bPui cie aux eue,
nghiet ka lox aux,
mai hoek ka aux dee in.
Bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet ka lox aux,
mai pex tom taug pon ju heue!
Aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pex leeing ju,
tawx krax muh ka pex,
pa hoik ah tix deuh ka Ta-wue.
4 Aux kieh nawh mawh simiang mai simiang ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik,
jhi siawp sibrawm tix ka kix ka daux nawh,
5 yam in maix tag caw tix khrawm meung ka piang haktiex;
kix ang tawng ka maix sivoe an,
yam in kix tag sang hoek tix deuh rhawm ka maix.
Aux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex tag sang yuh kaing in lih;
Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa singax singeh tag sang tawx min plak ngai ka maix heue.
 
6 Ka daux bawg yam pa sawg Simiang Siyiex,
mai caw tix nied sawg ka nawh;
ka daux bawg yam pa dex Simiang Siyiex ka ex,
mai caw tix nied rawg mhaing ka nawh.
7 Pui pa caox pa caig caw tix lih hu khaing daux krax pa caox pa caig;
pui pa yuh phi map khankix tag caw tix loh ka rhawm rhi tix.
Kix tag sang veeh ka hoek ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai pon ka krax pa caox rhawm nawh;
kix tag sang hoek ka Siyiex,
mai pon krax pa pluat ploi nawh.
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
krax keud krax sawn aux ang mawh krax keud krax sawn pex;
ka daux krax lawng aux ang awm ka daux krax lawng pex.
9 Awm lhaong rao ma khaing haktiex yuh nan,
ka daux krax lawng aux lhaong khaing ka daux krax lawng pex;
krax keud krax sawn aux lhaong khaing krax keud krax sawn pex heue.
 
10 cGum lox aux awm rha mai lhiex pa lih khaing rao ma,
ang lai chix deeh,
mai jaux ka haktiex leet,
kieh nghox koi kuah,
kieh pui pa van ka simie koe simie,
kieh pui pa chix ih chix nyaux koe ka priex preem.
11 Gum lox aux tag mawh nin heue.
Gum lox aux ang sax mawh pa giex pa going tix bawg kawx,
tag sang yuh lih krax keud krax sawn aux,
tag sang hawt yuh alo aux heue.
 
12 Pex tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi mai lih hu khaing Pa-pi-loeng,
deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix ka daux krax pa nhim pa tiiem.
Gawng ting gawng iak tag sang rhax lox lai pa ting pa si num;
khaox ox tag sang dieh taix tix gaux rhawm.
13 Haktiex dee kuah pang kat tag sang lih koe khaox gix;
haktiex dee kuah yeem yaw tag sang lih koe khaox plix mak cang.
In mawh mhai leeing ju,
tag caw tix doh keud ka aux,
pa mawh kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex heue.
