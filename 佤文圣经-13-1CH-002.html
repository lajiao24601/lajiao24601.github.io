Doeh ceu pui Yo-tax
2
Ya-keux koe kawn simex kao ra kaux,
pa mawh Lui-piien,
Si-miien,
Li-we,
Yo-tax,
Yi-sa-cia,
Si-pu-loeng,
2 Tang,
Yaw-seux,
Piien-ya-ming,
Na-fu-tha-lix,
Cia-teux,
Ya-seux heue.
3 Yo-tax koe kawn simex phuan kaux.
Moeng nawh pui Cia-nang pa kaux Pa-su-yax keh nawh cie kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Eux,
Eu-nang,
See-la.
Kawn ai kied mawh pa caox pa caig,
Simiang Siyiex hoik pauh nawh yum.
4 Moeng Yo-tax pa kaux Tha-ma keh nawh cie kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Fa-leu-see mai Shie-la heue.
5 Fa-leu-see koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Si-see-loeng mai Ha-mu-leux.
6 Pux nawh pa kaux Shie-la koe kawn simex phuan kaux,
pa mawh Sing-lix,
Yi-thang,
Si-mang,
Cia-keux,
Ta-la.
7 bDoeh ceu pui Shie-la tix ceu mawh kawn simex Cia-mi pa kaux Ya-kanga,
mawh kheu briex mox nawh ka khawng phak pa pon daux naing pa caw tix sang tan lu ka Siyiex,
mai hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yaox krax laux leeing heue.
8 Yi-thang koe kawn simex tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Ya-sa-li-ya heue.
Doeh ceu pui nyiex ma simiang Ta-wue
9 Si-see-loeng koe kawn simex loe kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Yie-la-miiex,
Lang,
Cia-leux heue.
10 Jauh ka doeh ceu cad nyiex ma Lang hoek ka doeh ceu cad nyiex ma Yie-si hoik tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Lang,
Ya-mi-na-ta,
Na-suing (hoik mawh pui pa kied lhak ka daux ceu pui Yo-tax tix kaux),
11 Sa-meung,
Paw-a-see,
12 Eu-pe-teux,
Yie-si heue.
13 Yie-si koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
jauh ka pux ke hoek ka pa pux mawh:
Yi-li-ya,
Ya-pi-na-ta,
See-mi-yax,
14 Na-thang-yiex,
La-taix,
15 A-shiien,
Ta-wue.
16 Yie-si koe kawn bun ra kaux,
pa mawh Si-lu-ya mai Ai-pi-kai heue.
Kawn bun Yie-si pa kaux Si-lu-ya koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Ya-pi-sai,
Yaw-ya,
Ya-sha-he.
17 Kawn bun Yie-si tang kaux tix kaux pa kaux Ai-pi-kai mex ka pui Yi-seu-ma-lix pa kaux Yi-thie,
mai keh kawn simex tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Ya-ma-sha heue.
Doeh ceu pui Si-see-loeng
18 Kawn simex Si-see-loeng pa kaux Cia-leux moeng ka A-su-pa,
keh kawn bun tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Yie-liiex.
Nawh koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Yie-seux,
Saw-pax,
Ya-toe.
19 Khaix hoik yum A-su-pa,
Cia-leux baox moeng ka Yi-fa-tha,
mai keh kawn simex tix kaux pa kaux Hu-eux.
20 Kawn simex Hu-eux mawh Wu-lix,
kawn saux nawh mawh Pi-sha-liex heue.
21 Yam koe ju Si-see-loeng tix gliah num,
nawh moeng ka kawn bun Ma-ciex,
pa mawh pux o Ci-liex.
Kiex koe kawn simex tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Si-keux;
22 Si-keux koe kawn simex tix kaux mawh Ya-eux.
Ya-eux hoik dawg mawh pa kuangc ka piang veng tix nga loe mu ka daux haktiex Ci-liex.
23 Kheu nan,
meung Ci-sux mai meung Ya-lang hoik pon kuang ka veng dee an tix gliah mu,
khawm yaong Ya-eux mai yaong Ci-na,
khawm veng pa dex baoh an.
Can pui pa ot haktiex an khawm uik,
hoik mawh doeh ceu cad keeing Ci-liex pa kaux Ma-ciex.
24 Khaix hoik yum Si-see-loeng,
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Cia-leux moeng ka moeng iak keeing tix;
kiex keh kawn simex tix kaux,
pa mawh Ya-see-hux.
Ya-see-hux pang ka veng Thi-keu-yax heue.
Doeh ceu pui Yie-la-miiex
25 Kawn ai Si-see-loeng pa kaux Yie-la-miiex,
koe kawn simex phuan kaux,
pa mawh Lang (kawn ai),
Pu-na,
A-liien,
A-shiien,
Ya-si-ya.
26-27 Lang koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Ma-see,
Ya-ming,
Yi-cie.
Moeng Yie-la-miiex tang kaux tix kaux mawh Ya-tha-la,
nawh keh kawn simex tix kaux,
pa mawh A-nang.
28 A-nang koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Sa-mai mai Ya-tax.
Sa-mai kawx koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Na-ta mai Ya-pi-sux heue.
29 Ya-pi-sux moeng ka Ya-pi-hai,
mai keh kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Ya-pang mai Maw-lix.
30 Pux ke Ya-pi-sux pa kaux Na-ta koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Si-liex mai Ya-piien.
Khaix hoik yum Si-liex,
ang koe kawn simex.
31 Ya-piien keh Yi-seex;
Yi-seex keh See-sang;
See-sang keh Ya-lai heue.
32 Pux Sa-mai pa kaux Ya-tax koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Yi-thie mai Yaw-na-tang.
Khaix hoik yum Yi-thie ang koe kawn simex.
33 Yaw-na-tang koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Pi-leux mai Sha-sax.
In plak piang mawh doeh ceu cad Yie-la-miiex khawm uik heue.
34 See-sang ang koe kawn simex,
mawh ngie kawn bun pa koe khaing.
Nawh koe siliex pui Ai-cix tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Yie-ha.
35 See-sang tawx kawn bun tix tix kaux mex ka nawh,
kiex keh kawn simex tix kaux pa mawh Ya-thaix.
36 Jauh ka ceu pui nyiex ma Ya-thaix hoek ka ceu pui nyiex ma Yi-li-sa-ma hoik tiiem tix ot plak greem:
mawh Ya-thaix,
Na-tang,
Sa-pa,
37 Yi-fu-la,
Eu-pe-teux,
38 Yie-hu,
Ya-sa-li-ya,
39 Si-li-see,
Yi-li-ya-sa,
40 Si-see-mai,
Sa-long,
41 Yie-cia-mi-ya,
Yi-li-sa-ma heue.
Doeh ceu pui Cia-leux tang ceu
42 Ceu kaux kawn ai Cia-leux (mawh pux Yie-la-miiex),
mawh Mi-sa.
Mi-sa keh Si-fu;
Si-fu keh Ma-li-sa;
Mai-li-sa keh Si-paw-loeng;
43 Si-paw-loeng koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Kheu-la,
Tha-pu-yax,
Li-kheung,
See-ma.
44 See-ma mawh keeing La-hang,
tax du Yaw-kang.
Pux ke See-ma pa kaux Li-kheung keh Sa-mai;
45 Sa-mai keh Ma-yuing;
Ma-yuing keh Paw Su heue.
46 Cia-leux koe moeng iak tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Yi-fa,
keh nawh cie kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Ha-lang,
Maw-sha,
Cia-shiex.
Ha-lang koe kawn simex tix kaux pa kaux Cia-shiex heue.
(
47 Koe pui tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-taix;
nawh koe kawn simex liah kaux,
pa mawh Li-ciien,
Yaw-thang,
Ci-sang,
Pi-lix,
Yi-fa,
Sa-ya-fu heue.)
48 Moeng iak Cia-leux tang kaux pa kaux Ma-cia keh nawh cie kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh See-piiex mai Theu-ha-na.
49 Khaix an nawh baox keh kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Sa-ya-fu mai See-fa.
Sa-ya-fu pang ka veng Me-ma-na;
See-fa pang ka veng Maw-pi-na mai veng Ci-pi-yax heue.
Lheu khaing an,
Cia-leux kawn koe kawn bun tix kaux kaux Ya-sa.
50 Cia-leux kawn koe doeh ceu cad tang tix blah,
hoik tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Hu-leux mawh kawn ai Cia-leux tau mai moeng nawh Yi-fa-tha.
Kawn ai Hu-eux pa kaux Saw-pa pang ka Ci-liex Yie-ling;
51 kawn nyi Sa-ma pang ka Paw-li-haung;
kawn sam Ha-leux pang ka Paw Cia-teux heue.
52 Pui pa pang ka Ci-liex Yie-ling pa kaux Saw-pa keh Ha-law-yi;
Ha-law-yi mawh tax du haktiex Mi-nu-ha pa ot dee an tix plak,
53 mai hoik mawh nang tax du ceu pui ku ceu pa ot ka Ci-liex Yie-ling,
pa mawh pui Yi-thie,
pui Pu-theux,
pui Su-ma,
pui Mi-laix khankix.
(Pui pa ot daux veng Saw-la mai daux veng Yi-seu-thao khankix hoik mawh ceu pui in kix khawm uik heue.)
54 Pui cao pa pang Paw-li-haung pa kaux Sa-ma mawh tax du pui Ni-thaw-fax mai pui Ya-tha-lui Paw Yaw-ya,
mai hoik mawh nang tax du Saw-lix pa ot ka haktiex Ma-na-ha heue.
55 Ceu pui Theu-la,
ceu pui See-mi-ya,
mai ceu pui Su-cia mawh cao pa meung tix tiiem ka lai been haktiex,
ka veng Ya-pi-see.
Kix mawh doeh ceu cad pui Ci-ni mai pui Li-cia pa mex moeng ka paox tix heue.
