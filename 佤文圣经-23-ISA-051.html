Lox pa tawx krax nyawm rhawm ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
51
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Pui pa simie tix pon krax taoh teem,
mai pa sawg aux teem coi tix khankix eue,
pex caw tix nghiet ka lox aux khawm uik.
Keud tix yaox ka num dee kaoh pex;
keud tix yaox ka grawng krax ot krax koe pex.
2 Keud tix yaox ka tax du pex Ya-paw-la-hang;
keud tix yaox ka Sa-la pa keh pex.
Yam kok aux ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
nawh ang koe kawn simex;
viang mawh nan,
aux hoik tawx buan son ka nawh,
kieh nawh koe kawn praix tom nie heue.
 
3 Aux tag sang tawx krax pa caox rhawm tix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tag sang tawx krax nyawm rhawm ka pui pa ot ka dee riem khankix.
Aux tag sang kieh haktiex dee riem awm si peem Yi-tiien;
aux tag sang kieh dee riem lih mawh si peem tai Simiang Siyiex.
Dee an koe krax gaux rhawm,
koe krax gaux rhi,
koe lox rhax krax pa yaog yaw heue.
 
4 Pui cie aux khankix eue,
nghiet heue;
meung cie aux khankix eue,
tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet:
aux tag sang tawx lox tat jhawm prawng aux ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik;
krax pa khaw caw aux tag sang viex pa riang hoek tawx ka kix.
5 Aux tag sang taoh teem ka kix dum mawx dum an;
bawg yam pa pon pex aux hoik hoek heue.
Aux tag sang hoek giex kaing ka piang meung haktiex khawm uik;
klawn tiex pa koe dee singai khankix krox jun ka aux,
mai kied simie aux hu taoh teem ka kix heue.
6 Pex tag caw maong tix jhak ka rao ma,
mai simi tix yaox ka piang haktiex.
Rao ma tag sang awm taox ngu pa grai yuh nan,
haktiex tag sang awm kraung ceep pa hoik griah yuh nan;
pui pa ot ka piang haktiex kawx tag sang yum awm roi yuh nan.
Viang mawh nan,
krax taoh aux ang chix jud leeing ju leeing phan;
krax pa pex aux tag koe ku ju leeing ju heue.
 
7 Hoik tawng ceu pa tix mawh pa khaw caw,
pui pa hoik tiiem lox sidiex gau aux koe ka daux rhawm rhi tix,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux.
Paw vi lhat ka krax pa yuh pui pa kaig pa tua mai krax pa map nai pui ka maix;
8 Kix tag sang awm kraung ceep pa griah pa hoik ih greeing ka heue.
Viang mawh nan,
krax taoh aux ang chix jud chix jai leeing ju;
krax pa puing pex aux koe ku ju leeing ju heue.
 
9 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kaoh haing tix teem coi ka aux heue!
Awm singaix pa hoik luan dix dix yuh nan,
maix hoik teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix taoh aux heue!
Mawh maix pa hoik lauh ka La-ha-pawa to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat pui ka max phawng vid,
mawh maix pa hoik klauh ka si yawng yum.
10 Mawh maix pa hoik kieh rawm sigrit sigra kroh hit,
hoik kieh rawm sigrit sigra pa raux tieh tix lih mawh haktiex pa kroh.
Maix hoik paih krax ting koe ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
hoik kieh pui pa pon krax pa taoh maix khankix tiang hu.
11 Pui pa hoik pon krax pa taoh maix khankix tag sang deeh hoek,
gaux rhawm gaux rhi hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
gaux rhawm gaux rhi rhax ka lox rhax,
mai kok yo ka lox pa ting si num.
Kix tag sang gaux rhawm gaux rhi leeing ju,
ang lai baox koe krax laux rhawm laux rhi heue.
 
12 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
mawh aux,
mawh aux pa tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka maix.
Yuh ka mawx maix lai lhat graung ka pui pa sang yum lie?
Kix ang tie yaox tix lheu lang ju khaing tai rib khankix.
13 Maix hoik piawm ka Simiang Siyiex cao pa phan pa yuh maix lie?
Mawh mawx pa nih ka rao ma lie?
Mawh mawx pa sum riah haktiex kaux cong lie?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai ca kawn lhat graung ka pui pa tuk tiak tuk te maix khankix,
maix lhat graung ka krax pa song rhawm pui pa keud tix sang yuh maix laux lie?
Krax pa song rhawm kix ang tie pon chix kieh maix laux leeing.
14 Pa hoik prawk pui khankix tag sang yaox tix bheeik luan heue;
kix tag sang yaox tix lang ju,
mai pon yaox tix sak heue.
 
15 Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
mawh Siyiex maix;
aux klu klao ka rawm sigrit sigra,
mai kieh rawm puk pak.
Ceu kaux aux mawh Yie-heu-hua pa mawh simiang kru naing.
16 Aux nihb ka rao ma,
sum riah haktiex kaux cong.
Aux ah nin ka Yie-lu-sa-leung:
maix mawh pui cie aux heue!
Aux tawx lox sidiex gau ka maix;
aux teei taix tix kia ngiien ka maix heue.
Krax kha Yie-lu-sa-leung rhawm tix hoik si dauh
17 cYie-lu-sa-leung eue,
kaoh haing heue!
Kaoh heue,
cung rauh see heue!
Yam tawx Simiang Siyiex krax ciyang,
kieh maix nyaux ka kawk krax pa song rhawm song rhi,
khaix hoik nyaux maix,
mai maix hoik pung piang.
18 Ka daux paox ceu paox cad maix,
ang koe pui pa thung ka maix;
ka daux paox ceu paox cad maix,
ang koe nang pui pa tuk ka maix heue.
 
19 Maix hoik yaox ka krax laux leeing pa mawh ra ceu in:
haktiex maix hoik gleeing laux ka naing;
pui cie maix hoik laux leeing ka daux pa cai rhawm mai vaig gawn.
Ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa hoek tix tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka maix.
20 Paox ceu paox cad maix hoik goh ka daux laih daux cau;
Kix hoik awm cak pa cawd ka daux rub pa taong pui jaw yuh nan.
Kix hoik mhong ka krax pa song rhawm song rhi Simiang Siyiex,
hoik pon krax jhax lawg Siyiex heue.
 
21 Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pa kha rhawm kha rhi tix eue,
pex hoik yoek heue,
viang mawh nan,
ang tie mawh pa yoek kheu plai.
22 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
pa mawh Siyiex pex hoik teem pex krai lox,
mai ah nin:
aux tag sang teei kawk pa kieh pex pung piang hu,
an hoik mawh kawk pa tawx aux ka pex yam song rhawm tix;
pex ang lai baox deeh yaox tix nyaux ka heue.
23 Aux tag sang teei kawk in tawx ka pui pa gut kheem ka pex,
tawx ka pui pa kieh pex goh ka daux laih daux cau,
pa hoik dig ka piang to kaux pex khankix heue.
