Sara pa pie lhien
4
Khuan Singax Singeh hoik krai sidawng,
singaix bawg yam si dauh,
koe pui tix blah tik ka krax yum rhawm,
mai hawt ka khuan pa pie lhien mai ka daux krax cie phi caox.
2 Daux krax in mawh pa hoek khaing pui pie lhien;
rhawm rhi kix awm khaix hoik su si yum ka rhiem hax yuh nan.
3 Kix ang teei tix koe mex koe moeng;
kix tuah ka priex preem khankix,
viang mawh nan,
phan yuh ka priex preem in kix,
mawh son sang tawx pui pa tawng yung ka pa mawh pa meh yaog yaw khaix tom ih.
4 Ceu pa phan pa yuh Siyiex mawh pa mhawm khawm uik,
tag caw tix ih ka daux krax pa yaog yaw,
ang pon tix tieb tie,
5 mawh kheu gum lox Siyiex mai krax pa rawg buan hoik kieh priex preem in kix lih mawh pa singax singeh heue.
Siliex pa mhawm cie Ye-sux Khrit
6 Phan maix sidiex ceu in kix ka pux aik pux o tix khankix,
maix tag mawh siliex pa mhawm cie Ye-sux Khrit;
krax yum rhawm an kawx mai krax pa khaw caw pa hoik dawg hawt maix,
tag sang teem coi to kaux maix haok huan ka daux khuan.
7 Phan mawh lox lao pui pa ang mawh ang meh pa ang keung tix krai khankix,
tag caw to mox tix khaing;
tag caw gau tix pheuk to kaux tix ot ka daux krax pa rauh see.
8 Pa pheuk ka to kaux tix koe dee mhawm,
pa pheuk ka daux khuan nie koe dee mhawm ka maix khawm uik,
mawh kheu krax pheuk ka daux khuan viex krax keud pot jun pot pa koe yam in mai singaix pa sang hoek.
9 Gum lox in mawh pa kaux sijax,
chix rieb tix yum rhawm ka khawm uik.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
ex sau riang tix yuh kaing pa taug maoa,
mai deuh krax keud pot jun pot ka Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju;
nawh mawh cao taoh ka kawn pui khawm uik,
mai nie mawh cao taoh ka pui pa yum rhawm ka nawh heue.
11 Maix tag caw tix krai kaing in ka kix,
mai sidiex gau ka kix.
12 Paw vi kieh pui ang een maix koe ceu ka pa awn ju maix.
Mawh ka lox si kah ang ah,
kaing yuh ang ah,
krax muh krax rhawm ang ah,
krax yum rhawm ang ah,
mai pa singax singeh ku ceu ang ah,
tag caw tix kieh mawh pa gau kawn Khrit tix yuh.
13 Maix tag caw tix phat ka phuk lai sigang Khrit,
sidiex ka gum lox Siyiex,
mai ka kaing pa sidiex gau khankix tag caw tix sau riang rao tix yuh,
tom hoik hoek aux.
14 Paw vi dah rhawm ka khawng phak buan son cie maix;
khawng phak buan son in mawh pa tawx ka maix,
ka daux lox cao jhai mai ka daux pa deuh kuad song khankix taix tix piang.
15 Kaing in kix maix tag caw tix viex rhawm rhi tix yuh,
kieh pui ku kaux yaox grawng pa haok huan maix.
16 Maix tag caw tix rien ka to kaux tix,
lox pa sidiex gau ka maix kawx tag caw rien tix.
Tag caw tix sau riang tix yuh kaing in;
yuh nin,
maix ang joeng taoh ka to kaux tix,
hoik taoh ka khawm pui pa mhong khankix heue.
