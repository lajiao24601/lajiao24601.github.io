Pauh ka cao giex kaing li
22
Ta-wue jauh to hu mox tix ka veng Cia-theux hoek ka daux dawh rang pa dex ka veng Ya-tu-lang.
Pux aik nawh mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh,
mhong ot nawh dee an mai lih kia ka nawh.
2 Pui pa gut kheem kix,
pui pa koe ceuk ka kix,
mai pui pa ang naog rhawm ka kaing pa yuh kix,
hoik peung rhawm tix ka nawh khawm uik;
kaux pui kix koe been pon yieh kaux.
Ta-wue lih tix mawh simiang ka piang kix heue.
3 Ta-wue yaog tix hu plak Mi-see-pa haktiex Maw-ya khaing dee an,
mai nawh ah nin ka simiang Maw-ya:
“Rawg maix kieh miex keeing aux ot ka maix dee in liak,
krox jun aux tawng lox pa tawx Siyiex ka aux,
grawng sang yuh ka mawx.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue viex miex keeing tix ot ka dee ot simiang Maw-ya.
Glang yam kawn mox nawh tix ot ka daux dawh rang dee an,
miex keeing nawh hoik ot paung dee an heue.
5 Khaix an,
cao jhai Cia-teux hoek ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Paw lai paung ot tin;
nied hu ka Yo-tax.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue yaog tix hu plak daux tiag khaox Ha-liex heue.
6 Koe tix singaix,
Sao-law ot ka Ci-pi-yax mai ngawm ka greem khaox si glai pa ot ka blaong tix gawng,
taix nawh giex ka pliah.
Siliex nawh cung vung viang nawh.
Koe pui krai simiang mhong ka dee mox Ta-wue mai pui cie nawh tix.
7 Simiang ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Pui Piien-ya-ming eue,
nghiet kah!
Pex ce Ta-wue sang pon tawx kaung ma mai si peem plix siiet ka tix,
mai sang tawx tix mawh simiang naing ka daux pui naing lie?
8 Khex hoik mawh in grawng pa tieh vheen pex ka aux lie?
Kawn simex aux hoik rom tix yuh paox pie tau mai Ta-wue,
pex tix kaux kawx ang krai ka aux.
Ang koe pui pa khaw rhawm ka aux;
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa krai ka aux,
pa ot greem aux Ta-wue tag sang sawg bawg yam pa khaw caw tix pauh ka aux,
pie kawn simex aux kawx kawn hawt ka rhawm nawh heue!”
9 aPui Yi-tong pa kaux To-yix cung tau mai Sao-law khawm siliex nawh dee an;
nawh ah nin:
“Yam hoek Ta-wue ka haktiex Naw-pawx dee ot kawn simex Ya-si-thu pa kaux Ya-si-mi-leux dee an,
aux hoik yaox ka nawh.
10 Ya-si-mi-leux khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex son Ta-wue,
khaix an tawx priex preem mai vaig Keu-li-yax pui Fe-li-see an ka nawh heue.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law tom ploi pui hu kok ka Ya-si-mi-leux cao giex kaing li,
khawm can pui nyiex pui ma pa yuh cao giex kaing li pa ot ka Naw-pawx;
kix hoek khawm uik.
12 Sao-law ah nin ka Ya-si-mi-leux:
“Ya-si-mi-leux,
maix nghiet!”
Ya-si-mi-leux pawk lox ah nin:
“Vawng,
maix krai heue!”
13 Sao-law ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai hawt tix tieh vheen ka aux tau mai Ta-wue?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai tawx kix ih priex preem mai vaig,
mai khiox khaw ka Siyiex son nawh?
Nawh hoik tieh vheen ka aux yam in,
mai sawg bawg yam pa khaw caw tix pauh ka aux!”
14 Ya-si-mi-leux ah nin:
“Ta-wue hoik mawh siliex pa kaux sijax ka Vawng tix kaux,
mawh kheue Vawng,
mawh cao giex kaing lien Vawng,
khaix an nawh mawh pui pa kied yung yi pui ka daux nyiex haw simiang.
15 Aux hoik khiox khaw ka Siyiex son nawh tete,
viang mawh nan,
in ang lai mawh tix bawg khaingb.
Phan mawh grawng pa sang tieh vheen ka maix simiang,
khiox khaw maix Vawng paw deuh phi map ka piang aux mai ka piang pui nyiex pui ma aux tix kaux kawx,
mawh kheu ang aux tawng kaing in pie ceeix kawx heue!”
16 Simiang ah nin:
“Ya-si-mi-leux,
maix mai pui nyiex pui ma maix khawm uik tag khaw tix yum.”
17 Khaix an nawh ah nin ka pui pau tix pa cung ka praok mawh:
“Pauh cao giex kaing li Simiang Siyiex in kix yum!
Kix yuh paox pie tau Ta-wue;
mai kix hoik dawg tawng Ta-wue to,
mai ang krai ka aux.”
Viang mawh nan,
cao pa pau an ang pon tawx taix tix pauh ka cao giex kaing li Simiang Siyiex.
18 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law ah nin ka To-yix:
“Maix hu pauh kix yum heue!”
To-yix tom pauh kix yum khawm uik.
Ka daux singaix an,
To-yix pauh ka cao giex kaing li pa hoik koe ara tix ceep sibex dha Yi-fu-teu tix daix phuan kaux.
19 Sao-law baox pauh ka can pui pa ot ka daux Naw-pawx veng cao giex kaing li yum,
simex mai bun ang ah,
kawn nyawm mai kawn ngie,
moi,
yung,
la hoik pauh ka khawm uik.
20 Viang Mawh nan,
kawn simex Ya-si-mi-leux tix kaux pa kaux Ya-pi-ya-tha pon yaox tix luat luan,
mai to hoek peung rhawm tix ka Ta-wue.
21 Nawh krai ka Ta-wue grawng pa hoik pauh Sao-law ka cao giex kaing li Simiang Siyiex.
22 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Singaix an,
aux yaox ka To-yix ot dee an,
mai hoik tawng nawh rien tix hu krai ka Sao-law.
Grawng pa yum pui nyiex pui ma maix,
aux hoik koe beeh glawm kah;
23 Viex maix ot dee ot aux tin,
paw vi lhat.
Sao-law keud tix sang pauh maix mai aux,
viang mawh nan,
maix ot tau mai aux,
maix tag yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.”
