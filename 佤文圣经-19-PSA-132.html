Rhax lai yaog yaw ka nyiex buan
132
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi piawm ka Ta-wue;
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka krax pa kham gee nawh pa taug pa mao khawm uik.
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka pa hoik ap pong nawh tix tiex tu ka maix yuh ka mawx,
hoik tiex tu yuh ka mawx ka Siyiex Ya-keux pa ting siawp sibrawm:
3-5 Sivoe sang yaox aux dee ot son Simiang Siyiex,
sang yaox dee paung ot Siyiex Ya-keux pa ting siawp sibrawm,
aux ang doh laig daux nyiex tix.
Aux ang doh laig it,
ang doh gaing heue.
 
6 aEx mhong koe teug sijax mukheu ka Paw-li-haung;
ex hoik yaox nawh ka daux kaung Ya-eux.
7 Ex ah nin:
kieh ex laig daux nyiex ot Simiang Siyiex heue;
kieh ex hoek krub ka plak sivoe dang ngeung nawh heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex eue,
kaoh heue,
laig daux nyiex dee laux lee maix tix,
awm siawp sibrawm teug sijax mukheu pa ting maix laig tau ka daux nyiex gox buan man.
9 Kieh cao giex kaing li maix ceep ka pa rauh pa see;
kieh pui cie maix ah lox pa ting si num heue.
 
10 Maix hoik ap tix deuh lox ka siliex maix Ta-wue;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix paw vi tik vud ka simiang pa hoik laug maix.
11 bMaix hoik tiex tu ka Ta-wue,
maix ang doh lai deeh teei.
Maix ah nin:
aux sang sum kawn simex maix tix kaux mawh simiang;
nawh tag sang plud ka simiang pa mawh maix.
12 Kawn praix maix khankix phan priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu aux,
hawt tix yuh ka gum lox pa tawx aux ka kix,
kawn praix kix kawx tag caw tix plud ka simiang pa mawh maix leeing ju heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex hoik laug ka Shi-ang;
nawh kieh haktiex an mawh nyiex dee ot tix.
14 Nawh ah nin:
aux but rhawm tix paung ot ka haktiex in leeing ju;
dee in mawh dee sang koe siawp sibrawm aux tix kuang kah.
15 Aux sang tawx ceu pa sang siao sang lo ka Shi-ang khawm uik;
aux sang kieh pui hot pui sibhawm pa koe daux veng yaox tix sawm sak.
16 Aux sang kieh cao giex kaing li krai pao ka krax taoh aux;
pui pa singax singeh ka daux Shi-ang tag sang ah lox gaux rhawm pa ting si num heue.
17 cAux sang yaog doeh ceu cad Ta-wue tix kaux mawh simiang ka dee in;
aux sang kieh simiang pa hoik laug tix mawh simiang ang jud dee in.
18 Aux sang kieh pui koeng ka nawh laux leeing som nai,
viang mawh nan,
meung cie nawh tag sang haok huan ting lhaong heue.
