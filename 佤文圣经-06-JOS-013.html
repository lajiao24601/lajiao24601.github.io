Haktiex pa ang bax nyang pon
13
Yam in,
Yaw-su-yax hoik kuad.
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hoik kuad ju,
viang mawh nan,
kawn koe haktiex tom nie ang bax nyang pon kuang,
2 pa mawh haktiex Fe-li-see mai haktiex Ci-sux khawm uik.
3 Pa mawh can haktiex plak seh cie pui Ya-wue.
(Haktiex in jauh ka plak seh mee Ai-cix tom hoek ka rawm klawng Si-heu,
plak laong hoek tom mee Yi-keu-loeng,
sivoe in mawh cie pui Cia-nang,
pa mawh pa ot ka Cia-sa,
Ya-seu-thu,
Ya-seu-ci-loeng,
Cia-theux,
mai Yi-keu-loeng cie simiang pui Fe-li-see phuan kaux.)
4 Kawn koe haktiex cie pui Cia-nang khawm uik,
mai mawh cie pui Si-toeng mai pui Mi-ya-la,
tom hoek ka Ya-fu mee haktiex pui Ya-maw-lix;
5 kawn koe haktiex cie pui Cia-pa-leux,
Li-pa-noeng plak lih singaix khawm uik:
jauh ka gawng He-meung plak seh tom hoek ka Pa-lix Cia-teux,
tom hoek ka diang moing gawng Ha-ma.
6 aHaktiex in khawm uik mawh khawm cie pui Si-toeng pa ot ka si nax gawng Li-pa-noeng mai Mi-see-li-fu Ma-ying.
Yam laig pui Yi-seu-liex,
aux sang grheu pui in kix lih.
Maix sang hawt ka lox pa tawx aux ka maix guah haktiex in ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
7 Yam in,
maix guah haktiex in ka si dim ceu pui mai Ma-na-si tix plak,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix heue.”
Guah ka haktiex pa koe plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang
8 bCeu pui Lui-piien,
ceu pui Cia-teux,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak hoik pon ka haktiex pa guah siliex Simiang Siyiex Maw-si ka kix,
haktiex plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
9 Haktiex kix jauh ka Ya-law-eu pa ot ka daux ngawd Ya-noeng,
tom hoek ka veng pa ot daux lung,
mawh khawm ka gawng Mi-ti-pa tom hoek ka Ti-peung can gawng lua khawm uik;
10 mai hoek tom daux mee Ya-meung,
mawh khawm veng pa kuang Si-hong simiang Ya-maw-lix pa ot ka Si-seu-peung.
11 Haktiex in mawh khawm Ci-liex,
Ci-sux,
Ma-cia,
khawm gawng He-meung khawm uik,
mai Pa-sang khawm uik,
tom hoek ka Sa-cia,
12 mai mawh khawm haktiex pa kuang simiang Eux pui Li-fa-ying pa kawn koe tix kaux,
pa mawh Ya-see-tha-lux mai Yi-teu-lai.
Maw-si hoik pex ka pui in kix,
mai grheu kix lih heue.
13 Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex ang lai grheu pui Ci-sux,
mai pui Ma-cia;
tom in singaix kix kawn ot ka daux haktiex pui Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.
14 cMaw-si ang guah haktiex ka pui Li-we.
Simiang Siyiex hoik ah nin ka Maw-si,
kix sang pon pa gleeing tix tan ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh khawng tan Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
in hoik mawh sin ngao cie kix heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Lui-piien
15 Maw-si guah haktiex tix blah ka ceu pui Lui-piien ku nyiex,
kieh mawh sin ngao kix.
16 Mee haktiex kix hoek tom Ya-law-eu praok ngawd Ya-noeng,
mai veng pa koe daux sigreung daux ngawd,
khawm gawng lua hawt vung viang Mi-ti-pa.
17 Baox mawh khawm Si-seu-peung mai veng pa koe piang gawng lua khawm uik,
pa mawh Ti-peung,
Pa-maw Pa-lix,
Paw Pa-lix Miien,
18 Ya-cax,
Ci-ti-maw,
Mi-fa-ya,
19 Ci-lie-thing,
Si-pi-ma,
mai Si-lie-ha Sa-sia,
pa koe ka ngawd piang gawng iak,
20 Paw Pi-eux,
mai haktiex caong gawng Pi-see-cia,
mai Paw Yie-si-mawx.
21 Haktiex kix mawh khawm can veng pa ot piang gawng lua khawm uik mai meung haktiex simiang pui Ya-maw-lix Si-hong ka daux Si-seu-peung.
Maw-si hoik pex ka simiang Si-hong,
mai baox pex nang simiang Mi-tiien khankix:
Yi-wex,
Li-cing,
Su-eux,
Hu-eux,
mai Li-pa.
Simiang in kix hoik teem simiang Si-hong kuang ka haktiex in kix.
22 Ka daux pui pa pauh pui Yi-seu-liex,
koe kawn simex Pi-eux cao si pawk Pa-lang.
23 Plak klip singaix ceu pui Lui-piien kieh rawm klawng Yaw-tang mawh mee.
Veng in kix plak piang mawh pa guah ka ceu pui Lui-piien ku nyiex,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Cia-teux
24 Maw-si kawx tawx haktiex tix blah ka ceu pui Cia-teux,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix.
25 Mee haktiex kix mawh khawm veng ka daux Ya-shiex mai Ci-liex khawm uik,
mawh khawm haktiex pui Ya-meung tix plak,
tom hoek ka La-pa plak lih singaix Ya-law-eu;
26 mai baox jauh ka Si-seu-peung tom hoek ka La-maw Mi-see-pa mai Pi-to-ling,
jauh ka Ma-ha-nian tom hoek ka mee haktiex Law-ti-pa.
27 Daux ngawd Yaw-tang mawh khawm Paw Ha-lang,
Paw Ling-la,
Su-keu,
mai Sa-feung,
pa mawh haktiex cie simiang Si-seu-peung Si-hong tang dauh,
mee haktiex plak klip singaix mawh rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
plak laong hoek tom rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix.
28 Veng in kix plak piang mawh pa guah ka ceu pui Cia-teux ku nyiex,
kieh mawh sin ngao kix heue.
Haktiex pa pon ceu pui Ma-na-si pa ot plak lih singaix
29 Maw-si guah haktiex tix blah ka ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak ku nyiex,
kieh mawh sin ngao cie kix heue.
30 Mee haktiex kix hoek tom Ma-ha-nian,
mawh khawm Pa-sang khawm uik,
pa mawh haktiex simiang Pa-sang Eux khawm uik,
khawm yaong pa ot ka Pa-sang,
Ya-eux tix gliah yaong.
31 Baox mawh khawm veng ting Ci-liex tix plak mai simiang Pa-sang Eux,
pa mawh Ya-see-tha-lux mai Yi-teu-lai.
Haktiex in plak piang mawh pa guah ka ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak;
ceu pui tix plak in mawh kawn simex Ma-na-si doeh ceu cad Ma-ciex heue.
32 Pa tiiem in plak piang mawh grawng yam ot Maw-si ka daux lung Maw-ya mai guah mee Yie-li-keu mai rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix.
33 dNawh ang guah haktiex ka ceu pui Li-we;
nawh ah nin ka kix,
khawng tan pa tawx ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex mawh sin ngao cie kix heue.
