Si-ang pa rham deeh im
37
Siawp sibrawm pa ting Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka aux;
khuan nawh viex aux hoek ka daux ngawd.
Ka daux ngawd an hoik mawh si-ang pui khawm uik.
2 Nawh viex aux tauh hu ka daux ngawd;
aux hoik yaox si-ang pa kroh hoik pum tix naog ka daux ngawd an.
3 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
si-ang in kix sang pon im lie?”
Aux pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting lhaong eue,
mawh ngie maix pa tawng khaing heue!”
4 Nawh ah nin:
“Tag caw tix krai lox jhai ka si-ang in kix,
krai ka si-ang pa kroh tag caw tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex.
5 Tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai ka kix ah nin:
aux tag sang phlu bhawm tix gum laig ka daux pex,
mai kieh pex deeh im.
6 Aux tag sang kieh pex kuah si niag kuah nex,
mai kieh hak kawb nex tix jan.
Aux tag sang phlu bhawm tix gum laig ka daux pex,
kieh pex deeh im.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
aux hawt ka gum lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex krai ka lox jhai.
Yam jiet taug krai,
aux hoik mhong ka lox pa luah si-ang,
khaix hoik chu tix vud,
si-ang an kix ra lai kleuk paox tix.
8 Yam jhak aux kah,
si-ang an jauh tix kuah si niag kuah nex,
hak kawb nex tix jan,
viang mawh nan,
ghlaok to kaux ang bax koe bhawm heue.
9 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai lox jhai ka bhaung,a
krai ka nawh,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
bhaung pa phlu tix hoek khaing pon praok si daix daog,
phlu laig ka ghlaok to kaux in kix,
mai kieh kix deeh im heue.”
10 bMawh kheu nan,
aux hawt tix krai lox jhai awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex,
bhawm laig daux ghlaok to kaux,
ghlaok to kaux tom deeh im,
mai kaoh cung.
Kaux pui kix hoik kub tix mawh pui naing heue.
11 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
pui Yi-seu-liex jiet awm to kaux yum in kix.
Kix ce to kaux tix kied kroh,
ang lai koe krax keud pot jun pot,
ang lai koe krax haok huan sivoe ngai.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix krai lox jhai ka pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex,
krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
aux tag sang paoh ka si meeig pex,
mai teei pex lih,
thung pex deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix Yi-seu-liex.
13 Khaix hoik paoh aux si meeig pex,
mai thung pex lih hu,
pex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
14 Aux tag sang phlu bhawm tix laig ka daux pex,
kieh pex deeh im,
mai pex taug yaox tix paung ot ka haktiex tix.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
Aux hoik krai,
tag sang kieh lih mawh tete.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex rom tix lih mawh meung simiang tix mu
15 Simiang Siyiex baox si kah ka aux;
16 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix teei khaox nguh tix gawng hoek,
ka piang khaox nguh an tiiem ‘meung Yo-tax’ ot kah,
mai baox teei khaox nguh tix gawng hoek,
ka piang khaox nguh an tiiem ‘meung Yi-seu-liex’ ot kah.
17 Khaix an,
jhawp khaox nguh an khawm ra gawng ot ka daux tawm taix tix,
mai kieh toh kleuk paox tix lih mawh khaox nguh tix gawng.
18 Phan paox ceu paox cad maix chawk in mawh grawng ceu pa tix,
19 maix tom krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
tag sang mawh pa tang ka son khaox nguh cie pui Yi-seu-liex an pa kleuk tix ka piang khaox nguh cie pui Yo-tax an,
mai kieh khaox nguh an kiex ra gawng lih mawh tix gawng ka daux tawm taix aux heue.
20 “Maix tag caw tix jhawp khaox nguh an kiex khawm ra gawng ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
mai kieh kix yaox.
21 Khaix an,
maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang thung pui cie tix lih khaing meung pa vuk vied vuk viang kix tix ot ka ku meung,
khrawm kix ot tau,
khaix an viex kix deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix.
22 Aux tag sang ot dee an,
ot ka piang gawng pui Yi-seu-liex khankix,
mai khrawm kix ot tau lih mawh meung tix meung.
Kix tag sang caw tix lih koe simiang tix kaux pa giex kaing ka piang kix,
ang lai baox kieh kix guah tix lih mawh meung ra meung.
23 “Kix ang lai baox krub ka riang rao pa yum koeng kieh to kaux tix seeing brai,
mai ang lai baox lud loe yuh phi map.
Aux tag sang taoh kix luat luan khaing pa yuh phi map mai krax ot krax koe pa caox pa caig.
Aux tag sang si ghrah kix singax singeh.
Kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux;
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix.
24 cTag sang lih koe pui tix kaux pa awm siliex aux simiang Ta-wue;
nawh tag sang mawh simiang kix;
mawh cao liang kix,
tag sang rom kix tau.
Kix tag sang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
25 “Kix tag sang paung ot ka haktiex an pa tawx aux ka siliex tix Ya-keux,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik paung ot tax du kix.
Kix tag sang paung ot ka dee an leeing ju,
doeh ceu cad kix kawx tag sang paung ot ka dee an.
Koe pui tix kaux pa awm siliex aux simiang Ta-wue,
tag sang giex kaing piang kix leeing ju.
26 Aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix,
mai kieh kix kaux cong son sang pon kix krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe leeing ju.
Aux tag sang kieh kix pon krax pa haok huan dee an,
kaux pui kix haok huan,
khaix an tag sang kieh nyiex buan aux jung tix ot ka haktiex kix leeing ju.
27 dAux tag sang ot tau mai kix dee an.
Aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix;
kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux.
28 Yam jung aux nyiex buan tix ot si nax kix,
meung ka piang haktiex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
pa hoik laug ka pui Yi-seu-liex mawh pui cie tix heue.”
