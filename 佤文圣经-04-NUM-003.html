Kawn simex Ya-loeng
3
Ka daux bawg yam si kah Simiang Siyiex tau mai Maw-si ka piang gawng Si-nai,
doeh ceu cad Ya-loeng mai Maw-si mawh in kix pa koe plak greem:
2 aYa-loeng koe kawn simex pon kaux:
kawn ai mawh Na-ta,
pa hawt khaix nawh mawh Ya-pi-hux,
Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yi-tha-ma.
3 Kix mawh pa hoik groi rawm bee ka piang pa mawh cao giex kaing li.
4 bViang mawh nan,
Na-ta mai Ya-pi-hux yum daux tiag Si-nai,
yam tan kiex ka ngu pa ang singax ka Simiang Siyiex.
Kiex ang koe kawn simex kawn bun,
mawh kheu nan,
Yi-li-ya-sa mai Yi-tha-ma yuh kaing jhaw cao giex kaing li singaix yam kawn im ju keeing kiex Ya-loeng heue.
Tawx ceu pui Li-we yuh kaing jhaw cao giex kaing li
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
6 “Maix viex ceu pui Li-we khankix hoek tin,
mai tawx kix yuh siliex Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li.
7 Kix tag sang yuh kaing ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
yuh son cao giex kaing li mai son cub pui hun khawm uik,
8 kix tag sang kuang kia rien khre ka kraung khrai pa koe ka daux nyiex gox buan man khawm uik,
mai yuh son ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux khankix.
9 Kaing ceu pa sang caw pui Li-we khankix tix yuh,
mawh ngie pa hawt tix kia ngien ka Ya-loeng mai ka kawn simex nawh khankix.
10 Maix tag tawx Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix yuh cao giex kaing li,
pui mawx ang ah phan yuh pa ang khaw ka kaing in,
tag sang kieh yum heue.”
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
12-13 c“Yam in ceu pui Li-we tag sang mawh cie aux.
Ka daux singaix yam pauh aux ka ceu pui pa liang tix keh daux Ai-cix khawm uik,
aux hoik guah ka kawn nyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa liang tix keh ku nyiex,
mai ka khawng brie kix pa liang tix keh ku mu lih mawh pa singax singeh son aux.
Mawh kheu nan,
yam in ceu pui Li-we khankix tag sang tang ka son dee ngawm kawn praix pa liang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
kix tag mawh cie aux,
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Si kaw ca rang pui Li-we
14 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si ka daux tiag Si-nai,
15 tag caw tix tiiem ca rang pui Li-we khankix,
hawt awm koe ceu pui kix mai nyiex ma kix,
can kawn simex pa hoik naog khix haok plak piang,
tag caw tix t iiem ka ca rang kix lih khawm uik.
16 Maw-si hawt tix yuh heue.
17-20 Li-we koe kawn simex loe kaux:
pa mawh Keu-soeng,
Keu-siax mai Mi-la-lix;
kix lih mawh tax du ceu pui Keu-soeng,
ceu pui Keu-siax,
ceu pui Mi-la-lix.
Keu-soeng koe kawn simex ra kaux:
pa mawh Li-ni mai See-me.
Keu-siax koe kawn simex pon kaux:
pa mawh Ang-lang,
Yi-see-ha,
Si-paw-loeng,
mai Wu-shiie.
Mi-la-lix koe kawn simex ra kaux:
pa mawh Maw-lix mai Mu-seex.
Kix hoik mawh tax du pa kaoh khaing ceu pui Li-we heue.
21 Ceu pui Keu-soeng koe ceu nyiex Li-ni mai ceu nyiex See-me ra ceu.
22 Can kawn simex pa hoik tiiem ka ca rang,
pa hoik naog tix khix haok plak piang,
khawm uik koe aliah rheng phuan yieh kaux.
23 Ceu pui in khankix,
tag sang sum bang tix ot ka plak klip singaix khaix nyiex gox buan man.
24 Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex La-yi-leux mawh cao kaing pa kuang ka piang ceu pui an khankix.
25 Kix tag sang kuang kia ka nyiex gox buan man,
mai braux nyiex gox buan man pa ot ka daux,
mai dawb braux nyiex plak praix,
man tie son diang siviex khankix,
26 man tie son pa vung viang biang brawk nyiex gox buan man mai biang brawk khu dee tan,
khawm can man pa vheuk hawt vung viang siviex biang brawk khankix.
Kix tag sang kuang ka kaing ceu pa dex pa dawt mai ceu in khawm uik heue.
27 Ceu pui Keu-siax mawh Ang-lang,
Yi-see-ha,
Si-paw-loeng,
Wu-shiie pon ceu pui.
28 Can kawn simex khawm uik pa koe ju tix khix haok plak piang,
mawh daix rheng liah yieh kaux.
29 Can ceu pui in tag sang sum bang tix ot ka plak seh nyiex gox buan man.
30 Yi-li-sha-fang pa mawh kawn simex Wu-shiie mawh cao kaing pa kuang ka piang ceu pui in kix.
31 Beeh glawm pa sang kuang kia kix mawh teug sijax mukheu,
pheen,
jawk kawk ngu mai khu dee tan,
mai kraung pa siao cao giex kaing li ka nyiex dee pa singax singeh,
khawm man tie diang siviex nyiex dee pa singax singeh.
Beeh glawm kaing pa yuh kix,
hoik mawh pa dex pa dawt mai kraung ceu in kix heue.
32 Simiang ceu pui Li-we mawh Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Ya-loeng cao giex kaing li;
beeh glawm kaing pa yuh nawh,
mawh pa kuang ka can pui pa yuh kaing ka nyiex dee pa singax singeh khankix heue.
33 Ceu pui Mi-la-lix lih mawh Maw-lix mai Mu-seex ra nyiex ceu pui;
34 can kawn simex pa hoik simin tix yaox khawm uik,
pa koe ju tix khix haok plak piang,
koe liah rheng ra yieh kaux.
35 Ceu pui in kix tag sang sum bang ot ka plak laong nyiex gox buan man.
Su-liex pa mawh kawn simex Ya-pi-haix,
mawh cao kaing ka piang ceu pui in kix.
36 Beeh glawm kaing pa yuh kix,
mawh pa kia ngien ka kraung pa ot ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
pa mawh pang joh phia jung,
lat,
rong,
daux jhup jaong,
khawm kraung pa iak pa lied khawm uik.
Beeh glawm kaing pa sang yuh kix,
mawh kaing pa dex pa dawt mai kraung ceu an kix khawm uik heue.
37 Kix kawx kawn sang yuh ka khawm kaing pa kuang kia ka biang brawk plak praix,
pa mawh rong,
daux jhup jaong,
jim,
mai maox khankix heue.
38 Maw-si,
Ya-loeng,
mai kawn simex Ya-loeng khankix,
tag sang sum bang ot ka plak lih singaix,
sivoe nyiex gox buan man.
Kix mawh cao pa yuh kaing son ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka nyiex dee pa singax singeh.
Pui tang kaux phan yuh pa ang khaw ka kaing ceu in kix,
tag sang kieh nawh yum.
39 Maw-si hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex,
can kawn simex pa hoik simin khaing ceu pui Li-we,
pa koe ju tix khix haok plak piang khawm uik,
koe ra mheen ra rheng kaux heue.
Pui Li-we tang son kawn ai pa liang tix keh
40-41 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Can kawn simex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa liang tix keh khawm uik,
tag mawh cie aux,
mawh kheu nan,
tag caw tix tiiem ka ca rang kawn ai pa liang tix keh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku kaux,
pa koe ju tix khix haok plak piang.
Kheu nan,
aux tom krai pao ceu pui Li-we tag sang ap pong tix tang son kix,
mai mawh cie aux!
Aux mawh Simiang Siyiex.
Aux kawx baox sang krai pao moi cie pui Li-we,
tag sang mawh pa ap pong tix tang son moi pa liang tix keh khawm uik.”
42-43 Maw-si hawt ka gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
mai tiiem ka ca rang kawn ai pa liang tix keh pa hoik keh koe tix khix haok plak piang lih,
khawm uik koe ra mheen ra rheng ra yieh atigliah loe kaux heue.
44 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
45 “Yam in maix teei tix tan lu ceu pui Li-we ka aux,
son sang tang kawn ai pa liang tix keh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai baox tan moi cie ceu pui Li-we khankix ka aux,
son sang mawh tang son moi ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa liang tix keh khankix.
46 Mawh kheu nie kawn ai pa liang tix keh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing ceu pui Li-we ra yieh atigliah loe kaux,
maix tag caw tix deeh taoh ka kawn ai pa liang tix keh in kix.
47 Hawt ka been diag tong pa hoik deuh nyiex dee pa singax singeh,
tix kaux pui tag caw tix tawx phuan plah mau luah.
48 Maix tag caw tix tawx mau in ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix.”
49 Maw-si hawt tix yuh ka gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex,
50 nawh tom teei tix rheng loe yieh tix gliah phuan mau luah,
51 tawx ka Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix heue.
