40
Simiang Siyiex ca nied tix krai ka Yaw-paw ah nin:
2 Yaw-paw eue,
maix hoik tieb tie lox cao pa ting ka piang khawm uik;
maix thung thiang ka Siyiex,
yam in maix pawk lox heue.
 
3 Yaw-paw pawk lox ah nin:
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux hoik krai lox pa daox aw,
mai aux kawn sang krai ceu pa tix lie?
Aux ang lai pon baox krai heue.
5 Aux hoik krai kah;
aux ang lai baox krai.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka Yaw-paw khaing daux bhaung klia tix ah nin:
 
7 Maix kaoh cung,
kieh tix awm pui pa koe riang yuh nan;
maix tag caw tix pawk lox pa chawk aux ka maix.
8 Phan maix keud tix sang kham sakse grawng ang aux rauh see;
maix ce tix koe krax mai ne raw krai ang cie aux khaw lie?
9 Maix ting riang awm aux lie?
Maix pon yuh lox tuh sah lih luah awm aux yuh nan lie?
10 Phan maix pon yuh,
tag sang kieh pa koe min mawh kraung ceep pa jawg ka maix,
kieh pa koe min plak ngai mawh kraung ceep ka maix.
11 Jhak ka pui pa cau nyhau khankix;
song rhawm ka kix,
mai kieh kix ang koe ceu koe cak.
12 Jhak pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi khankix,
mai bluh kix lih;
mai yuh laux ka pui pa caox pa caig.
13 Si paung ka kix khawm uik;
phrawk kix ot ka daux meung yum.
14 Yuh nin,
aux tom tag sang chix yaog yaw ka maix,
krai doh maix hoik pon pex ka daux pa yuh naing son to kaux cao tix heue.
 
15 Jhak brung rawma to sat pa ting an heue;
aux phan yuh ka nawh,
mai phan nang ka maix.
Nawh sawm rib awm moi yuh nan,
16 viang mawh nan,
riang rao pa koe ka to kaux nawh kied ting tete!
Nex nham nawh kied mhawm sied tete!
17 Num si dax nawh jung tix awm khaox si oe bhawng yuh nan;
nex nham pa koe ka ba nawh kied mhawm kluing tete!
18 Si-ang nawh awm gawng tawng yuh nan;
ba nawh awm gawng rhiem yuh nan.
19 Ka daux pa phan yuh aux to sat kaing yieh ceu,
nawh mawh ceu pa blawn tang siyox;
mawh ngie cao pa phan pa yuh nawh pa pon pex ka nawh khaing.
20 Ka daux nong khaox dee laig dee lih to sat khankix,
hoik kuah ka rib riem ceu pa chix tix ih son nawh.
21 Nawh blauh tix it ka greem veei yeem khaox pang kat,
lu lieh tix ka daux glaox dee koe pang priem khankix.
22 Khaox si glai ka praok klawng vung viang ka nawh;
yeem khaox kat tawb kheem ka nawh.
23 Yam haok huan rawm nawh ang lhat ang graung;
yam huan rawm klawng Yaw-tang hoek ka moing klawng,
nawh kawx ang lhat.
24 Pui mawx pon tawb ngai nawh mai pon giex ka nawh?
Pui mawx pon teei goik mai cieh meeh nawh doh?
