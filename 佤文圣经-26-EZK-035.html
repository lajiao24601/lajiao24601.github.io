Siyiex ciyang ka Yi-tong
35
aSimiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw Yi-tong ka plak sivoe ngai pui,
3 krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
Gawng Yi-tong eue,
aux tag sang tieb tie ka maix heue!
Aux tag sang nyeu taix tix lauh ka maix,
mai kieh maix riem.
4 Aux tag sang kieh veng ot maix riem;
aux tag sang kieh haktiex maix nhik;
yuh nin,
maix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
5 “Maix mawh pui koeng ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Yam yaox pui Yi-seu-liex krax laux krax leeing kheu phi map tix,
mai yam nghoik kix tix kham krax prawng ca,
maix hoik ap tix tawx pui koeng pauh ka kix.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka krax pa im ju tix leeing ju yuh nin:
hoik dawg tat maix yum tete,
ang yaox tix luat luan.
Maix hoik yuh ka phi map pa pauh pui yum,
nham tag sang teei nham vied kah.
7 Aux tag sang kieh gawng Yi-tong lih tix riem khawm uik,
mai pauh ka can pui pa hoek dee an khawm uik.
8 Aux tag sang kieh gawng Yi-tong khankix naog ka to kaux pui yum,
kieh gawng pa koe ka dee lung,
ngawd,
mai klawng iak thom naog ka pui yum ka naing.
9 Aux tag sang kieh maix riem leeing ju,
ang lai koe pui pa sang paung ot ka veng maix.
Yuh nin,
maix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
10 “Viang aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh Siyiex pui Yo-tax mai Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ra meung,
maix lai pon krai mawh kix cie tix,
tag sang bleet ka haktiex kix.
11 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tiex tu ka krax pa im ju tix leeing ju yuh nin:
maix hoik koeng,
hoik boh rhawm,
hoik seeing brai ka pui cie aux yuh ka mawx,
aux kawx tag sang hawt tix yuh nan ka maix nang.
Kix tag sang tawng grawng ciyang aux ka maix hoik mawh pa prawng rauh see aux son kix.
12 Yam an maix tom taug tawng,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik sax mhong lox pa map nai maix ah nin,
gawng Yi-seu-liex khankix hoik riem khawm uik,
tag sang mawh maix pa bleet uik.
13 Maix ting rhawm cau nyhau,
mai krai lox pa salao ka aux,
aux hoik mhong ka khawm uik heue.”
14-15 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh maix riem.
Yam yaox pui cie aux ka krax laux krax leeing,
hoik gaux rhawm maix ka yuh ka mawx,
yam in pui piang haktiex khawm uik kawx tag sang hawt tix gaux rhawm kheu laux leeing maix yuh nan.
Gawng haktiex Si-eux,
mawh heue,
haktiex Yi-tong khawm uik,
tag sang tieh tix lih mawh dee riem khawm uik.
Yam an,
kawn pui tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
