Rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex
150
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw nawh ka daux nyiex pa singax nawh;
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw nawh ka daux rao ma,
pa hoik jhi nawh siawp sibrawm tix kah.
2 Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing yuh nawh pa ting siawp sibrawm;
tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw pa kied ting kied lhaong nawh heue.
 
3 Tag caw tix phlu teet rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
tag caw tix tauh ting rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
4 Tag caw tix tauh kraung graoh mai rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
tag caw tix tauh ting phlu pi rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
5 Tag caw tix teei lox chiang pa mhawm luah rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh;
tag caw tix teei chiang pa mhawm luah rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh.
6 Can ceu pa koe ju pa hoik phan hoik yuh tix tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
