Pui Yi-seu-liex deeh ing ka haktiex tix
31
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Bawg yam tag sang hoek,
aux tag sang mawh Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu;
kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux.
2 Ka daux tiag daux tu,
aux hoik caox rhawm ka pui pa deuk khaix tud pui hu khankix.
Yam simie pui Yi-seu-liex tix laux lee tix,
3 aux kok jhi kixa yaox tix khaing dee singai.
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux hoik dawg muh ka pex,
aux ca kawn teei pa kaux sijax pa ang chix tieh chix loh muh ka pex.
4 Aux tag sang cox sum pex kaux cong.
Pex tag sang baox deeh tauh ka kraung,
gaux rhawm gaux rhi graoh.
5 Pex tag sang cox deeh sum ka si peem plix siiet ka piang gawng Sa-ma-li-yax;
pui pa sum tag sang yaox tix ih ka to ma to koi pa pon khaing daux si peem.
6 Koe tix ngaix,
pui pa pau tag sang kok yo ka gawng Yi-fa-liien ah nin:
‘Hoek,
ex haok krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex ka Shi-ang heue.’ ”
7 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
Pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm rhax lai son Yi-seu-liex,
kok lox pa ting si num son meung pa ting pa lhaong,
pex tag caw tix rhax lox lai pa yaog yaw ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh ka pui cie tix;
nawh hoik teem coi ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa deuk khankix.
8 Jhak heue,
aux tag sang viex kix hoek khaing haktiex plak laong,
khrawm kix hoek khaing haktiex khawm pon daog.
Pui pa dug ngai,
pa chawh caong,
bun nghe,
bun keh,
kix hoik lih mawh cub caw pa hoek tau;
9 jhak heue!
Pui cie aux tag sang yiiem leeing gawng krax yam hoek tix;
kix hu tau mai aux,
mai rawg buan leeing gawng krax.
Aux tag sang viex kix tiang ka praok rawm klawng iak,
thung kix hu daux krax pa si dawm,
ang kieh kix yaox tix sideeing caong tix.
Aux mawh keeing Yi-seu-liex;
Yi-fa-liien mawh kawn ai aux heue.
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Meung ka piang haktiex eue,
pex tag caw bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox aux,
pex tag caw tix ot ka klawn tiex pa singai krai lao ka gum lox aux hu.
Aux hoik grheu ka pui cie tix hu,
viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang baox khrawm ka kix;
aux tag sang kia ngien ka kix,
awm kia ngien cao liang yung ka phung yung yuh nan.
11 Aux hoik pluat ploi ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
hoik taoh kix lih khaing daux tawm taix meung pa ting riang.
12 Kix tag sang deeh hoek,
mai rhax lai gaux rhawm ka gawng Shi-ang;
kix tag sang preeng tix hoek,
mai pon yaox tix ih ka khawng phak lak sawng aux pa mawh:
nghox koi,
plai khraox,
bee sime,
moi yung.
Kix tag sang awm kaung ma pa haoh dee keet rawm daux yuh nan;
kix ang baox lai cud cha ka ceu pa tid tix heue.
13 Yam an,
bun krih tag sang gaux rhawm graoh;
kuad song mai pui nhum ju tag sang gaux rhawm tau.
Aux tag sang kieh krax laux rhawm kix tieh tix lih mawh krax gaux rhawm,
kieh krax khi rhawm kix lih mawh krax gaux rhawm.
14 Aux tag sang kieh cao giex kaing li khankix haoh ka priex preem;
aux tag sang kieh pui cie tix ang koe ceu pa cud cha.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Simiang Siyiex caox rhawm ka Yi-seu-liex
15 bSimiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
ka daux La-max hoik mhong lox pa yiiem tom ting heue;
Le-ciiex yiiem son kawn praix tix khankix,
mai ang cu tix rieb ka lox khu rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik yum kix khawm uik heue.
16 Paw vi lai baox yiiem!
Awt ka rawm ngai maix heue!
Taug mao son kawn simex kawn bun tix ang sawn mawh pa giex pa going;
kix tag sang deeh hoek khaing meung haktiex pui koeng.
17 Krax pa hu maix hoik naog ka krax keud pot jun pot;
kawn simex kawn bun maix tag sang deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
18 Aux mhong lox laux rhawm pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex eue,
yix hoik awm kawn moi tiag yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
maix sidiex gau ka yix,
mai kieh yix cu lox.
Khiox khaw maix viex yix deeh hoek,
yix tag sang mawh cie maix;
mawh kheu hoik mawh maix Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix.
19 Viang yix hoik tieh vheen ka maix,
yix hoik deeh loh rhawm rhi tix dum mawx dum an.
Krax pa kuang maix kieh yix kied klih rhawm;
kheu phi map pa yuh yix yam iak tix hoik kieh yix kaig tua heue.
 
20 Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix mawh kawn simex pa muh aux;
maix mawh kawn pa blawn muh aux.
Yam krai lao aux ka ceu kaux maix,
aux tag sang keud maix ot ka daux rhawm tix.c
Aux kied muh maix tete;
aux tag caox rhawm ka maix tete.
21 Maix tag caw tix sau mhai krax,
mai sum ka khlawp na bat krax;
maix tag caw tix sawg ka krax ting pa lawng tix an.
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
deeh hoek heue!
Deeh hoek ka haktiex ot pex heue!
22 Pui pa lud krax eue,
kawn sang haoh rhawm miex greeng lie?
Aux yuh kaing khraox tix ceu ka piang haktiex mawh:
bun tag sang kia ngien ka simex heue.
Krax lawng pa riang pui cie Siyiex
23 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex krai ah nin:
“Yam kieh aux pui cie tix deeh ing cox sum ka nyiex ma tix,
kix tag sang baox ah nin ka veng pa ot ka daux Yo-tax ku mu:
nyiex pa khaw caw,
gawng Shi-ang pa singax singeh eue,
kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka maix heue!
24 Haktiex Yo-tax mai veng yaong pa ot ka daux haktiex ku mu,
kawn pui tag sang naog daux;
pui yuh kaing haktiex,
cao liang khankix,
mai phung yung kix,
tag sang paung ot tau dee an.
25 Aux tag sang kieh pui pa taug mao deeh koe riang,
mai kieh pui pa cai rhawm yaox tix sawm sak.
26 Mawh kheu nan,
kawn pui tag sang ah nin:
‘Aux hoik laig it,
yam haing aux khaing it daux rhawm aux kied nyawm heue.’
27 “Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ah nin,
yam hoik hoek bawg yam,
aux tag sang kieh pui naog ka daux haktiex Yi-seu-liex mai ka daux haktiex Yo-tax,
kieh khawng brie pre haoh hun.
28 Aux hoik sax rien khre tix yuh kaing pa laoh tix tik,
kaing pa yieh laux,
kaing pa klup tix tik,
kaing pa yuh laux,
mai kaing pa yuh laux leeing khankix ka kix;
aux kawx tag sang hawt tix rien khre tix yuh kaing pa sum ka kix,
pa kieh kix kaux cong.
29 dYam an,
kawn pui ang lai baox ah nin:
keeing ih ka plix siiet pa niex,
rhang kawn simex kawn bun hoik kied si ki ka heue.
30 Deeh tieh tix,
pui mawx hoik ih ka plix siiet pa niex in,
rhang pui an tag sang si ki kah;
pui ku kaux tag sang yum kheu phi map tix heue.”
31 efSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Bawg yam sang hoek,
aux tag sang yuh sijax mukheu pa khraox tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax.
32 Khaix hoik teei aux taix tix thung tax du kix lih khaing Ai-cix,
hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kix.
Aux mawh mex kix,
viang mawh nan kix hoik yuh laux ka sijax mukheu in.
Sijax mukheu pa khraox mai sijax mukheu pa prim in ang awm paox tix.
33 gSijax mukheu pa khraox pa sang yuh aux tau mai pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang mawh nin:
aux tag sang deuh lox tat jhawm prawng tix ka daux kix,
mai tiiem ot ka piang sok rhawm kix.
Aux tag sang mawh Siyiex kix,
kix tag sang mawh pui cie aux heue.
34 hKix ang lai baox sidiex gau ka paox ceu paox cad tix ah nin:
‘Tag caw tix tawng yung ka Simiang Siyiex’,
mawh kheu hoik tawng yung meung haktiex ka aux khawm uik.
Aux tag sang pluat ploi ka phi map kix,
mai ang lai tiiem tix deuh ka krax pa lud loe kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
 
35 Simiang Siyiex kieh singaix jhan riang pon ngaix,
kieh khix simuing jhan riang ka daux pa faik.
Nawh su shiah ka rawm sigrit sigra,
mai kieh rawm puk pak.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua,
pa mawh simiang kru naing heue.
36 Nawh ap tix deuh:
ca mawh kawn mhawm ot grawh grawng krax haktiex,
krax si pup meung Yi-seu-liex kawx chix haok huan.
37 Phan pui chix pon tiag tix yaox ka mee haktiex die rao ma,
tiag tix yaox ka dee raux riah haktiex,
Simiang Siyiex tom taug chix tik vud ka pui Yi-seu-liex kheu phi map pa yuh kix.
Simiang Siyiex tom krai pao yuh nin heue.
 
38 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
bawg yam hoik hoek,
veng Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik,
jauh ka nyiex lhaong Ha-nang-yiex hoek ka Rung Siviex,
tag sang cox sum tix mawh cie aux.
39 Mee veng tag sang vih tix plak klip singaix hoek ka gawng Cia-li,
tiag tix hoek ka Keu-yax.
40 Can gawng pa pum ka to kaux pa yum mai gawng pa tik ka kraung pa seeing,
khawm klawng Ci-loeng hoek ka plak lih singaix Siviex Brung pa mawh haktiex pa riem,
tag sang laig ka haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung,
kieh mawh veng pa singax singeh cie aux.
Veng in ang doh lai yieh laux,
yuh laux.”
