Jhi tix ka pui gau aliah kaux
21
Khaix hoik kaing in kix,
Ye-sux baox jhi pui gau yaox tix ka praok rawm nhawng Thi-pi-li-yax.
Grawng pa jhi tix bawg in mawh nin:
2 Yam an Si-meung Pi-teux,
To-ma pa mawh ceu kaux kawn brawm,
Na-tang-yiex pui Cia-nax veng Cia-li-lix,
kawn simex Si-pi-thaix ra kaux,
mai pui gau tang kaux ra kaux,
hoik ot tau mai nawh khawm uik.
3 aSi-meung Pi-teux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux hu rub kax heue.”
Kix ah nin:
“Yix sang hu tau mai maix heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix hu laig daux reu;
viang mawh nan,
leeing sawm ang pon tid tix.
4 Yam jauh singaix tix lih,
Ye-sux cung ka praok rawm,
viang mawh nan,
pui gau ang tawng nawh mawh Ye-sux.
5 Ye-sux krai ka kix ah nin:
“Paox grawm,
hoik pon pex kax lie?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Ang pon heue.”
6 bYe-sux ah nin:
“Teei rub sau ka plak dawm reu,
plak an kax koe ka heue.”
Kix tom sau rub,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon bluh haok,
mawh hoik pon rub kax tom nie heue.
7 Pui gau pa muh Ye-sux krai ka Pi-teux ah nin:
“Mawh simiang heue!”
Si-meung Pi-teux mhong mawh simiang,
nawh tom teei kraung dha ceep tix tix pheen vhak ka piang to kaux tix (yam an nawh ot ka kaong),
bhuih tix laig daux rawm.
8 Pui gau khankix vai reu iak hu dex ka praok rawm,
mai bluh kax pa pon rub kix lih khawm rub.
Yam an kix ang lai singai khaing praok rawm,
koe been tix yieh kongjhee.
9 Kix hoik lih khaing ka praok rawm,
mai yaox pum ngu tix pum,
kax mai khao mun koe ka piang.
10 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Teei kax pa taug khraox pon pex ra loe mu heue.”
11 Si-meung Pi-teux haok ka piang reu,
mai bluh rub lih hoek ka praok rawm;
ka daux rub hoik mawh kax ting khawm uik,
khawm uik koe tix yieh tix phuan loe mu.
Viang hoik koe kax tom nie yuh nin,
rub ang bax griah.
12 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoek tix ih priex ngawb heue.”
Ang koe pui gau tix kaux kawx pa pon chawk ka nawh “mawh maix pui mawx”,
mawh kheu hoik tawng kix nawh mawh Simiang Ye-sux.
13 Ye-sux tom hu ka kix,
teei khao mun guah tix tawx ka kix,
khawm kax kawx hawt tix guah ka nang heue.
14 In hoik mawh khaix hoik kaoh im Ye-sux khaing krax yum,
pa jhi pui gau yaox tix ka daux loe bawg heue.
Ye-sux mai Pi-teux
15 Khaix hoik sawm kix,
Ye-sux chawk Si-meung Pi-teux ah nin:
“Si-meung kawn simex Yaw-hang,
muh maix aux lheu khaing ceu in kix lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
mawh tete,
maix hoik tawng aux muh tix heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix tawx kawn yung cie aux sawm heue.”
16 Ye-sux chawk nawh ka daux ra bawg ah nin:
“Si-meung kawn simex Yaw-hang,
muh maix aux lie?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
mawh tete,
maix hoik tawng aux muh tix heue.”
Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix liang ka yung cie aux heue.”
17 Ye-sux chawk ka daux loe bawg ah nin:
“Si-meung kawn simex Yaw-hang,
muh maix aux lie?”
Pi-teux tom laux rhawm ka jiiem Ye-sux tix chawk nawh loe bawg “muh maix aux lie”,
nawh krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
ceu pa ang maix tawng ang koe tix ceu kawx,
maix hoik tawng aux muh tix heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix tawx yung cie aux sawm.
18 Aux krai ka maix tom sidawng,
yam nhum ju maix,
maix mad ka maox nyoi tix,
mai lai ling awm pa simie tix lai ling;
viang mawh nan,
yam hoik kuad maix,
maix tag sang nyeu taix tix,
pui tang kaux tag sang phrawk ka maix,
viex maix hu dee ang maix rhawm hu heue.”
19 (Ye-sux krai ka lox in mawh pa krai ka grawng pa tag sang yum Pi-teux yuh ka mawx,
ka daux pa yaog nawh min plak ngai Siyiex.)
Khaix an Ye-sux baox krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix hawt ka aux heue!”
Ye-sux mai pui gau tang kaux
20 cPi-teux kling to kaux tix,
mai yaox pui gau pa blawn muh Ye-sux an ot plak khaix (pui gau an hoik sax greed tix dex ka Ye-sux yam sawm kix priex bo,
chawk ka nawh “Simiang eue,
pui pa sang coh maix mawh mawx.”)
21 Pi-teux yaox ka nawh,
tom chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
pui in tag sang yuh ka mawx sax lie?”
22 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Phan aux kieh nawh im ju tix krox jum yam hoek aux,
ang koe grawh grawng mai maix.
Maix tawng hawt ngie ka aux khaing heue!”
23 Mawh kheu nan,
gum lox hoik tauh hu ka daux pui pa hawt ka Ye-sux,
krai ang pui gau an sang yum.
Tete te lai,
Ye-sux ang doh baox krai ang nawh sang yum,
mai krai “phan aux kieh nawh im ju tix krox jum yam hoek aux,
ang koe grawh grawng mai maix” heue.
24 Pui in hoik mawh pa kham sakse pui gau an ka kaing ceu in;
nawh hoik tiiem kaing in kix lih.
Ex hoik tawng sakse pa kham nawh hoik mawh tete khawm uik heue.
Lox nghoik
25 Ye-sux kawn yuh ka kaing tang ceu tom nie,
phan tiiem lih khawm uik,
aux keud ka piang haktiex kawx ang pon uik deuh ka phuk lai tom nie yuh nin heue.
