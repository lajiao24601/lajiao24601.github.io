Na-tang tawx lox ka Ta-wue
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 17.1–15)
7
Simiang Ta-wue ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang.
Simiang Siyiex kieh nawh pon pa nhim pa tiiem,
mai ang yaox krax pa tuk tiak tuk te pui koeng.
2 Simiang ah nin ka cao jhai Na-tang:
“Maix jhak,
aux ot ka daux nyiex haw simiang pa hoik sum ka khaox si oe bhawng,
teug sijax mukheu Siyiex hoik tieh tix ot ka daux nyiex gox bang heue!”
3 Na-tang ah nin:
“Maix simie tix yuh ceu tix yuh heue!
Mawh kheu ot Simiang Siyiex tau mai maix.”
4 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux bo an Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Na-tang:
5 “Hu krai ka siliex aux Ta-wue,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
‘Maix ang mawh pui pa sum nyiex buan tawx aux ot daux.
6 Jauh yam taoh aux pui Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing Ai-cix hoek yam in,
aux ang sax ot ka nyiex buan;
aux ot ka daux nyiex gox bang ang jud ang jhai,
tom gah tix ot ku dauh.
7 Singaix num hoik vung vied vung viang aux tau mai kawn pui Yi-seu-liex sivoe an,
aux ang sax chawk cheng ka simianga mawx tix pa hoik kok aux liang ka pui cie tix,
yuh ka mawx lai ang sum nyiex buan pa yuh ka khaox si oe bhawng tang aux?’
8 “Mawh kheu nan,
maix caw tix krai ka Ta-wue,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex simiang kru naing ah mawh nin:
‘Maix liang yung ka daux tiag,
mai aux hoik kok ka maix hoek,
mai tawx maix mawh simiang pui cie aux Yi-seu-liex.
9 Maix hu dee mawx ang ah,
aux tag sang ot tau mai maix.
Aux hoik pon pex ka pui pa koeng maix ka khawm uik.
Aux tag sang kieh maix sidu min mai pui pa kied ting min ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
10-11 “ ‘Aux hoik laug haktiex tix dauh son pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex,
mai tawx kix ot dee an,
ang lai kieh kix yaox krax pa tuk tiak tuk te.
Jauh tom yam laig kix ka daux haktiex in,
pui tud pui rhim hoik puing ka kix ang jud ang jai,
viang mawh nan,
hu sivoe ngaix,
kaing in kix ang lai baox lih tix koe.
Aux sang kieh maix pon pa nhim pa tiiem,
mai ang yaox krax pa tuk tiak tuk te pui koeng tix kaux kawx,
doeh ceu cad maix kawx tag sang tawx pa nhim kah.
12 b Yam hoik yum maix,
tag sang si paung maix ot tau mai tax du tix,
mai aux tag yaog kawn simex maix mawh simiang,
mai kieh meung nawh haok huan ting lhaong.
13 Nawh tag sang mawh pui pa sum nyiex buan son aux;
aux tag sang kieh meung pa giex nawh kaing yaox tix koe leeing ju leeing phan.
14 cAux tag sang mawh keeing nawh;
nawh tag sang mawh kawn simex aux.
Phan nawh yuh kaing pa ang khaw,
aux tag sang ciyang ka nawh,
awm ciyang keeing ka kawn tix yuh nan.
15 Viang mawh nan,
aux ang chix deeh teei ka krax pa muh ka nawh,
awm hoik deeh teei aux krax muh tix ka Sao-law yuh nan.
Aux hoik teei tix tik nawh,
kieh maix mawh simiang.
16 dMaix tag koe doeh ceu cad leeing ju leeing phan.
Meung cie maix tag sang kaux cong leeing ju;
meung pa giex maix kaing tag sang koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.’ ”
17 Na-tang hoik krai kaing pa jhi Siyiex ka nawh ku ceu ka Ta-wue heue.
Lox rawg buan yaog yaw Ta-wue
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 17.16–27)
18 Simiang Ta-wue laig ka daux nyiex buan man,
hoek ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai ngawm tix rawg buan ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
can pa hoik yuh maix son aux khawm uik,
aux ang keung tix pon,
mai ceu pui nyiex ma aux kawx ang keung.
19 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
maix hoik tawx ka aux tom nie,
pie ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
maix hoik ap tix tawx buan ka kawn praix aux.
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
hoik mawh in kaing pa hoik yie si pup pui lie?
20 Aux kawn sang koe lox krai ceu pa tix lie?
Maix tawng ka siliex maix.
21 Maix yuh nin hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing alo maix.
Maix yuh ka kaing pa ting in kix khawm uik,
mawh son sang sidiex gau ka aux.
22 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
maix kied ting kied lhaong tete!
Ang koe pui pa pon tiag tix tau mai maix.
Yix hoik dawg tawng,
lheu khaing maix,
mai ang lai koe siyiex tang kaux.
23 f“Ka piang haktiex ang koe meung tix meung kawx pa awm Yi-seu-liex.
Maix hoik taoh pui Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing yuh tix siliex cawng,
mai kieh kix mawh pui cie tix.
Maix hoik yuh kaing pa ting daox deng son kixe,
mai kieh ceu kaux tix tauh hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
Maix hoik pluat ploi pui cie tix lih khaing Ai-cix,
yam hoek kix dee mawx ang ah,
maix hoik grheu ka pui tang ceu mai siyiex pa krub kix hu.
24 Maix hoik kieh pui Yi-seu-liex mawh pui cie tix leeing ju,
maix pa mawh Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix mawh Siyiex kix heue.
25 “Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh gum lox pa hoik ap maix tix deuh pa dex dawt mai aux,
khawm doeh ceu cad aux lih tix mawh.
Khiox khaw maix hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik krai maix leeing ju heue.
26 Kieh pui yung yi ka ceu kaux maix leeing ju.
Kieh kawn pui yaog yaw leeing ju:
‘Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
hoik mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex!’
Maix giex kaux cong ka meung pa hoik giex aux kaing ku ju leeing ju.
27 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
aux viex rhawm ting rawg buan yuh nin ka plak sivoe ngai maix;
mawh kheu hoik jhi maix ku ceu khawm uik ka aux,
pa mawh siliex maix,
mai krai aux mhong grawng sang yaog maix doeh ceu cad aux mawh simiang.
28 “Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
maix mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing.
Maix hoik giex kaux ka lox pa ap tix leeing ju leeing phan.
Maix hoik tawx gum lox pa daox deng pa hoik ap tix ka aux.
29 Yam in khiox khaw maix cu tix tawx buan ka doeh ceu cad aux,
mai kieh kix ca pon buan son maix ang jud.
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong eue,
maix hoik ap tix tawx,
kieh maix kia ngien ka doeh ceu cad aux leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
