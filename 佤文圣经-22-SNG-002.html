2
Aux mawh tai kat nghim tiag ka daux pak mhaik,
mawh tai siyag ka daux ngawd heue.
[Simie pa sang koe moeng]
2 Pui pa muh aux ot ka daux nyawm bun khankix,
hoik awm tai siyag tix gawng ka daux pang kat yuh nan heue.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
3 Pui pa muh aux ot ka daux nyawm simex khankix,
hoik awm khaox mak kawk tie tix gawng ka daux nong khaox yuh nan,
aux muh tix ngawm ka greem veei nawh,
plix nawh kied tie,
kied nyawm ka rhawm aux.
4 Nawh viex aux laig daux nyiex pa khan kheu nawh pui,
man chix krax muh hoik vuk vied tix chuh ka piang kaing aux.
5 Nawh teei tix tawx plix siiet kroh eei ka aux,
teei mak kawk tie deeh kieh riang rao aux koe,
mawh kheu hoik saux aux ka jhawm saux braong.
6 Nawh teei taix khawn goix tix giex ka kaing aux,
teei taix khawn dawm tix kawm ka aux.
7 Nyawm bun Yie-lu-sa-leung khankix heue,
nghiet ka aux liak,
viex pex tiex tu ka kiah mai cak miex khankix,
pex paw vi so yiex haing,
paw vi tuk tiak ka krax muh yiex heue.
Lox rhax ka daux ra ceu
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
8 Aux hoik mhong ka lox pui pa muh aux;
nawh haok ka piang gawng piang lua son sang hoek ka aux.
9 Pui pa muh aux hoik awm kiah,
awm kawn cak yuh nan.
Nawh cung ka praok guh ding nyiex aux;
nawh kok jiang aux ka daux doh nyiex,
kok jiang daux doh man tie ka daux nyiex.
10 Pui pa muh aux si kah tau mai aux heue.
[Simex pa sang koe moeng]
Yaog maix kaoh heue,
paox muh aux,
bun mhawm paox muh aux,
hu tau mai aux heue!
11 Maix jhak,
bawg yam kuat praix hoik luan;
bawg yam lih lhiex hoik luan.
12 Tai ku ceu pa koe daux tiag hoik pruh;
bawg yam pa rhax kawn sim ka lai hoik hoek,
hoik mhong ah si kaux krie lox tix ka daux kaung daux ma.
13 Plix ruh hoik jauh tix teem;
tai khaox plix siiet kawx hoik pruh bhawng.
Yaog maix kaoh,
paox muh aux,
bun mhawm paox muh aux,
hu tau mai aux heue!
14 Si kaux cie aux eue,
maix mox tix ot ka daux dawh rang.
Kieh aux jhak tix yaox ka greeh grang maix;
kieh aux nghiet tix mhong ka gum lox maix;
mawh kheu kied mhawm grang maix,
gum lox maix kied sidaing mhawm tete heue.
 
15 Tag caw tix giex ka sox la,
kawn sox la an hoik dig laux ka si peem plix siiet,
mawh kheu hoik caw bawg yam pruh tai khaox plix siiet.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
16 Pui pa muh aux hoik mawh cie aux,
mai aux hoik mawh cie nawh.
Nawh liang yung ka daux tiag tai siyag,
17 krox jun bhaung awn pa phlu yam pon ngawb,
pon sawm hoik grai hu si nyek si nyek.
Pui pa muh aux eue,
deeh hoek heue,
awm kiah tix mu,
awm kawn cak ka gawng Pi-theua pa to tix hoek yuh nan heue.
