Grawng pa loh Ya-sa been
15
Khuan Siyiex lih hoek piang kawn simex Eu-teux pa kaux Ya-sa-li-ya,
2 nawh tom hu ka simiang Ya-sa,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Ya-sa mai can pui Yo-tax,
pui Piien-ya-ming eue,
nghiet ka lox pa krai aux.
Ca mawh cu nghiet pex ka Simiang Siyiex,
mai nawh tag ot tau mai pex tete.
Pex sawg ka nawh,
nawh tom kieh pex yaox ka tix;
phan pex singai hu khaing nawh,
mai nawh tag tik vud ka pex.
3 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik ang lai koe Siyiex pa mawh pa meh tom rheeng bawg yam,
ang lai koe cao giex kaing li pa sidiex gau ka kix,
ang lai koe lox tat jhawm prawng.
4 Viang mawh nan,
yam lih hoek krax laux krax leeing,
kix deeh veeh tix plak Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex;
kix sawg ka nawh,
mai nawh tom kieh kix yaox tix.
5 Singaix bawg yam an,
pui lih lih laig laig mai ang koe krax nyawm ot,
mawh kheu hoik suk sag haktiex ku dauh.
6 Meung in puing ka meung an,
veng in puing ka veng an,
mawh kheu Siyiex hoik kieh krax laux krax leeing lih hoek ka piang kix.
7 Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix kaux cong,
ang pon tix laux rhawm;
kaing yuh pex tag pon krax pawk bix pa mhawm tete heue.”
8 Yam mhong Ya-sa ka lox jhai pa krai kawn simex Eu-teux pa kaux Ya-sa-li-ya,a
nawh tom pon krax pa kaux pa cong.
Nawh yieh tix tik ka can riang rao pa koe ka daux haktiex Yo-tax mai ka haktiex Piien-ya-ming,
khawm can riang rao pa koe ka veng ot haktiex gawng Yi-fa-liien ku dauh.
Nawh deeh cox sum ka khu Simiang Siyiex pa ot ka biang brawk nyiex buan heue.
9 Pui tom nie kaoh khaing Yi-fa-liien,
Ma-na-si,
Si-miien,
hoek tix hawt rhawm Ya-sa,
mai ot ka daux meung nawh,
mawh kheu hoik yaox kix ot tau Simiang Siyiex mai nawh.
Ya-sa khrawm pui in kix mai pui Yo-tax mai khawm kawn pui Piien-ya-ming;
10 kix tom khrawm tix ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung yam giex Ya-sa ka kaing daux kao phuan num mai loe khix.
11 Singaix an,
kix teei moi aliah yieh mu,
yung aliah rheng mu pa pon tix khaing daux naing,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie pa tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
12 Kix yuh sijax mukheu son sang krub Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du kix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
13 Mawh simex bun kawn nyawm kuad song ang ah,
pui mawx ang sawg ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang pauh pui an yum.
14 Kix hoik ah lox pa tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
mai sang priex tix hawt ka gum lox sijax in;
khaix an kix tom kok yo ah lox tix,
mai phlu ka teet tawng.
15 Can kawn pui Yo-tax khawm uik hoik gaux rhawm gaux rhi;
mawh kheu kix hoik grum rhawm ka daux lox sijax mukheu in,
mai sawg Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
nawh tom taug kieh kix yaox tix,
khaix an mai tawx krax pa nhim ka haktiex kix khawm pon daog heue.
16 Simiang Ya-sa tik ka miex simiang pa mawh yiex tix Ma-cia,
mawh kheu hoik yuh nawh riang rong Ya-seu-la bun siyiex pa phit vao ka daux pa seeing pa brai.
Ya-sa kid ka riang bun siyiex an goh,
mai lug phawng,
mai gleeing ka daux rawm klawng iak Ci-loeng.
17 Viang Ya-sa ang bax yuh laux khawm uik ka can nyiex vad pa ot ka piang gawng iak khankix,
mawh kheu nan,
ka daux ju nawh,
nawh hoik hawt Simiang Siyiex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
18 Nawh hoik teei ka kraung pa tan lu keeing tix Ya-pi-yax ka Siyiex,
khawm khri mau pa tan lu tix khawm uik,
mai deuh ot ka daux nyiex buan.
19 Tom hoek yam giex nawh kaing ka daux tix ngoe phuan num,
ka daux baux bawg yam in ang lai koe naing heue.
