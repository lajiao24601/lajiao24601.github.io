Kraung nyiex buan
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 7.23–51)
4
aSimiang Saw-law-meung kieh pui raug yuh khu tawng lih tix mu,
krax lang dim kongjhee,
krax vieh dim kongjhee,
krax lhaong pon kongjhee plak.
2 Nawh raug yuh nang rawm dung tawng tix mu,
krax raux koe ra kongjhee mai ra kongchuing,
been vieh moing koe pon kongjhee pon kongchuing,
vung viang moing koe kao loe kongjhee ra kongchuing.
3 Toh moing rawm dung tawng in plak praix,
riang koe ka ra tia,
tix tia ot plak piang,
tix tia ot plak greem,
hoik kieh awm greeh grang moi mhaing yuh nan.
Moi tawng in kix mai rawm dung tawng in hoik yuh tix lih ka tawng khawm uik.
4 Rawm dung tawng deuh tix ot ka piang blawg moi tawng kao ra mu,
moi tawng veeh ngai tix plak praix,
kaing loe mu veeh tix ka plak lih singaix,
loe mu veeh ka plak klip singaix,
loe mu veeh ka plak seh,
loe mu veeh ka plak laong.
5 Pu dung rawm tawng koe aliah kongfeeng plak,
ka daog awm daog kawk nyaux yuh nan,
mai riang lawm tai siyag hoik pawd tix koe kah.
Ka daux dung rawm tawng pon uik sau rawm pon mheen kongsheng heue.
6 bKix baox raug yuh pan tawng kao mu,
phuan mu deuh ot plak seh nyiex buan,
an phuan mu deuh ot plak laong.
Pan tawng in kix mawh pa siao yam si ghrah ka nex khawng brie Krax-tan Pa-gleeing lih mawh khik.
Rawm pa ot daux rawng dung tawng mawh pa tawx cao giex kaing li khoek tix ka heue.
7-8 cdKix hawt awm been pa hoik tat tix deuh,
mai yuh ka jhawk kawk ngu khri kao mu,
pheen kao mu,
mai deuh ot daux nyiex ting nyiex buan,
tix plak phuan mu jhawk kawk ngu khri mai phuan mu pheen.
Kix baox yuh klong khri tix yieh mu.
9 Kix yuh biang brawk plak daux son cao giex kaing li,
mai yuh nang biang brawk plak praix.
Siviex si nax biang brawk hoik om ka tawng uik.
10 Rawm dung tawng deuh ot dee kawk nyiex buan ka plak lih singaix plak seh heue.
11-16 Hu-lang baox raug yuh aw,
jauk,
mai klong.
Nawh hoik hoik kaing pa caw nawh tix sang yuh ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex son simiang Saw-law-meung.
Kraung pa hoik raug nawh tix yuh mawh in kix plak greem:
Rong tawng ra gawng.
Riang klong toh choeng rong ra mu pa sau ka piang rong tawng.
Riang maox ceng ra mu pa duing tix ot ka piang toh choeng rong.
Plix mak cang tawng pon yieh mu,
riang pa ot ka piang toh choek rong ku gawng vung vhien ka riang plix mak cang ra tia.
Daux jhup jaong tawng kao mu.e
Pan tawng kao mu.
Rawm dung tawng.
Moi tawng kao ra mu pa joh kaux ka rawm dung tawng.
Aw,
jauk,
khaox cieh.
Cang mai Hu-lang hawt tix yuh ka gum lox tawx Saw-law-meung,
mai teei tawng pa hoik klaing tix mhawm plang yuh kraung ku ceu pa koe plak piang pa siao son nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex heue.
17 Kraung in kix khawm uik hoik hawt awm lox tawx simiang,
mai raug yuh ka bang dee yuh kaing,
ka si nax ngawd Yaw-tang,
Su-keu,
mai Sa-la-tang.
18 Mawh kheu kied nie ceu pa raug tix yuh,
mai ang koe pui pa pon tawng grawng pa hoik siao tawng miex ding heue.
19 Simiang Saw-law-meung baox raug yuh kraung khri ku ceu son nyiex buan:
koe khu khri,
pheen pa deuh khao mun;
20 kawk ngu khri pa tok riang jung ot ka plak sivoe dee pa kied singax singeh,
mai jawk kawk ngu;
21 tai,
kawk ngu,
si kiep ngu;
22 si kiep kip tai ngu,
klong,
pan pa deuh nyawng ot piang,
pan pa sau nyaox ot daux.
Kraung in kix hoik yuh tix ka khri pa kied mhawm.
Siviex nyiex buan plak praix mai siviex nyiex dee pa kied singax hoik om khri ka heue.
