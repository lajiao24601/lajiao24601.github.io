Caing ka kraung ceep cao giex kaing li
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 28.1–14)
39
Kix caing kraung ceep pa kied mhawm son cao giex kaing li Siyiex khankix ka keei klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
mai keei raoh rawt yam yuh kaing krub.
Kix caing kraung pa singax singeh son Ya-loeng.
In mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.
2 Kix yuh sibex singax singeh Yi-fu-teu ka keei khri,
keei pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt mai man keei sum.
3 Kix tawk khri lih mawh khlawp rhi,
kip lih mawh gawng keei,
awm man keei sum mai keei pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
keei pa raoh rawt pa hoik taing tix tau.
4 Kix baox yuh maox klip ra nyaing son Yi-fu-teu,
caing khawm ra plak,
kieh chix mad tix kaux.
5 Maox an caing kleuk tix ka Yi-fu-teu,
son sang mawh maox rhawt ka to kaux,
mai mawh pa yuh ka man pa awm paox,
pa mawh keei khri,
keei klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt,
mai man keei sum.
In mawh pa hawt yuh awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si.
6 Kix baox teei siang simaox bawd raoh ra mu sau ot ka daux jhawk khri an,
mai saik ceu kaux pui Yi-seu-liex kao ceu ra ot ka piang.
7 Siang in ra mu caing ot ka piang maox klip Yi-fu-teu khawm ra nyaing,
son sang krai grawng ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ceu ra.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.
Yuh dieb naok
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 28.15–30)
8 Kix baox yuh na bat naok ka keei khri,
pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt,
mai man keei sum,
mai kieh koe lai tai,
awm caing Yi-fu-teu yuh nan khawm uik.
9 Pak peung na bat naok an mawh pon pak,
mai niep tix koe ra jan,
plak lang tix nga ra kongfeeng,
plak vieh tix nga ra kongfeeng.
10 Kix sau piang na bat naok,
siang simaox ka piang pon rhoi:
rhoi tix sau siang simaox raoh,
siang simaox lheung,
mai siang simaox pa raoh;
11 rhoi ra sau siang simaox pa klai,
siang simaox pa klai rao ma mai siang simaox pa toh riang.
12 Rhoi loe sau siang simaox pa klai si ngi,
siang simaox bawd paing mai siang simaox pa raoh pliex;
13 rhoi pon sau siang simaox pa klai,
siang simaox pa raoh mai siang simaox pa paing pau.
Siang in kix plak piang tag sau ka piang jaong khri khawm uik.
14 Ka piang siang simaox kao mu ra an kix,
tag saik ceu kaux kawn simex Ya-keux ot ka tix kaux tix khlawp,
son sang mawh pa tang min ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ceu ra.
15 Kix yuh maox ceng na bat naok ka khri mhawm,
greeh grang kieh awm maox pa duing tix tau.
16 Kix baox yuh jhawk khri ra mu,
mai daux jhawk khri ra mu,
jhawk khri kieh toe kleuk paox tix ka piang na bat naok khawm ra plak.
17 Kix phrawk maox khri pa hoik duing tix tau ra nyaing kaux ka daux jhawk toh dawb tie naok khawm ra toh.
18 Toh maox khri an khawm ra toh plak an,
phrawk tix kaux ka piang daux jhawk khri an ra mu,
mai sau ot ka piang maox rhawt klip pa koe plak sivoe ngai Yi-fu-teu.
19 Kix baox yuh daux jhawk khri ra mu,
mai kleuk ot ka greem toh na bat naok khawm ra toh,
ot ka daog plak daux Yi-fu-teu,
20 baox yuh daux jhawk ra mu,
mai kleuk tix ka piang maox rawd klip ra nyaing ka plak greem sivoe ngai Yi-fu-teu,
dex ka gawng caing,
ka piang maox pa hoik jawg pre tix taing mhawm.
21 Kix teei maox pa klai rao ma,
phrawk daux jhawk na bat naok mai daux jhawk Yi-fu-teu ot tau,
kieh na bat naok ot ka piang maox Yi-fu-teu,
son ang sang chix ghah.
In hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.
Caing kraung ceep pa singax singeh tang ceu
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 28.31–43)
22 Sibex dha pa ceep piang Yi-fu-teu,
hoik mawh pa caing tix ka keei pa klai rao ma khawm uik.
23 Vung viang ngawg kraung dha an baox caing ka daog,
son ang sang griah.
24-26 Vung viang jaong sibex dha caing tix ka keei pa klai rao ma,
keei pa lung pliex,
keei pa raoh rawt,
mai man keei sum yuh ka riang plix mak cang,
mai sau kawn hing pa yuh ka khri mhawm khan ka si nax.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.
27 Kix caing Ya-loeng mai kawn simex nawh khankix ceep sibex dha ka man keei sum plak daux,
28 mai baox yuh lawk kaing ka man keei sum,
mai mhok,
mai khlax,
29 mai baox yuh maox rawd nyoi tix nyaing ka keei klai rao ma,
keei lung pliex,
keei raoh rawt,
ka piang koe lai tai.
In khawm uik hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh awm gum lox pa tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si.
30 Kix baox yuh khlawp na bat mhai pa tan lu ka khri mhawm tix khlawp,
mai saik ka piang mawh nin:
“Kieh pa singax singeh mawh cie Simiang Siyiex.”
31 Kix phrawk maox pa klai rao ma ka piang lawk kaing plak sivoe ngai.
In hoik mawh pa hawt awm gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si heue.
Hoik sum ka nyiex gox buan man
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 35.10–19)
32 Can kaing nyiex gox buan man hoik hoik khawm uik.
Lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik hawt tix yuh ku ceu.
33 Kix viex nyiex gox buan man mai kraung nyiex gox buan man khawm uik hoek ka sivoe ngai Maw-si,
pa mawh:
goik,
pang joh phia jung,
lat gi,
rong,
khawm daux jhup jaong;
34 dawb hak yung pa hoik siang tix raoh,
dawb hak pa mhawm plang,
man tie pa tieb.
35 Teug sijax mukheu pa deuh khlawp lox tat jhawm prawng,
khaox grawng teug sijax mukheu mai dawb teug;
36 pheen tan,
kraung pa siao piang pheen,
mai khao mun pa tan lu ka Siyiex.
37 Jawk kawk ngu khri mhawm,
kawk ngu,
mai kraung pa siao tau,
mai bee kawk ngu;
38 khu dee tan khri,
bee pa singax singeh,
nyawng bhawng,
man tie ka dee laig daux nyiex gox buan man;
39 khu dee tan tawng,
mai rub tawng piang khu dee tan,
khaox grawng khu mai kraung pa siao,
pan tawng mai jaong pan.
40 Man tie vung viang biang brawk,
rong man tie mai daux jhup jaong;
man tie ka dee laig ka biang brawk mai maox;
jim nyiex gox buan man.
41 Kraung ceep pa kied mhawm pa ceep cao giex kaing li yam laig tix yuh kaing krub daux nyiex pa singax singeh khankix.
42 Gum lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex ka Maw-si,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hoik yuh ka ku ceu.
43 Maw-si jhak ka kaing jhaw pa yuh kix khawm uik,
mai kix hoik yuh awm lox pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex khawm uik,
mai Maw-si tom nawb buan ka piang kix heue.
