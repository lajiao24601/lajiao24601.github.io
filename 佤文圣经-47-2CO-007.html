7
Pao grawm pa muh khankix eue,
kheu hoik pon ex gum lox pa ap in,
ex caw tix kieh to kaux tix singax,
teei tix tik ka ceu pa seeing brai pa koe ka to kaux tix mai ka khuan rhawm rhi tix khawm uik,
mai kieh tix hoek diag tong pa singax singeh ka daux krax pa lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex heue.
Krax gaux rhawm Pao-law
2 Khiox khaw pex viex rhawm pa vieh va rieb ka yix liak.
Yix ang sax yuh kaing ceu pa lud ka pui tix kaux kawx;
ang sax yuh pui mawx tix laux,
mai ang sax teei tix cie ka ceu pa yie pui mawx tix.
3 Aux krai lox yuh nin,
ang mawh ne raw aux ka pex.
Aux hoik sax krai,
yix kied muh ka pex tete;
yiex cu tix yum yum tau mai pex,
im im tau mai pex.
4 Aux hoik yum rhawm ka pex tete,
yaw rang di ka pex tete.
Yam yaox aux krax laux leeing,
aux hoik pon krax pa nyawm rhawm,
grum naog mai krax pa gaux rhawm heue.
5 aKhaix hoik hoek aux ka Ma-chi-toeng,
yix kawn ca ang bax yaox tix nyawm ot.
Yix hoik si pup krax pa kha rhawm kha rhi,
krax pa simah simaing,
mai krax pa lhat graung ka daux rhawm rhi.
6 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex pa joh jhan ka pui pa laux rhawm laux rhi khankix,
hoik tawx Thi-to joh jhan ka yix.
7 Ja lao nawh ang hoek dee in,
nawh kawn krai pao yix mhong grawng pa hoik pon tix krax pa joh jhan khaing pex,
khawm pa hoik simie pex tix yaox ka aux yuh ka mawx,
laux rhawm yuh ka mawx,
khaix an sau riang tix krai lox son aux.
In kix hoik mawh pa nie blawn kieh aux nyawm rhawm heue.
8 Phan mawh lox lai pa tawx aux ka pex an,
viang hoik kieh pex laux rhawm,
aux kawx ang kieh tix klih rhawm.
Aux tee chix klih rhawm tix ceeix ceeix kheu hoik tawng lox lai an tee chix kieh krax khi rhawm lih tix koe ka daux pex tix vud tix cu.
9 Viang mawh nan,
yam in aux kied gaux rhawm,
ang mawh kheu pa kieh aux pex khi rhawm,
mai hoik mawh kheu krax khix rhawm pa hoik tieh loh rhawm rhi pex.
Siyiex siao ka krax pa khi rhawm pex,
mawh kheu nan ceu pa yuh yix ang bax laux yuh pex.
10 Siyiex siao ka krax pa khi rhawm tieh loh ka rhawm rhi pui,
mai kieh pui pon krax taoh;
in ang caw tix klih rhawm tix ka heue!
Viang mawh nan,
krax khi rhawm pa dex dawt mai haktiex chix kieh pui yum.
11 Pex jhak tix yaox,
Siyiex hoik siao yuh nin ka krax pa khi rhawm pex,
mai kieh pex nied tix krai lox son to kaux tix,
khaix an jhi krax pa song rhawm ka daux pa rauh see,
mai baox rien rhawm,
baox koe krax gaux rhawm,
baox kaux sijax,
baox ciyang pa ang rauh see heue!
Ka daux kaing ku ceu pex hoik jhi grawng pa singax singeh tix heue.
12 Mawh kheu nan,
viang aux hoik tiiem lox lai an tix pheen,
viang mawh nan,
ang mawh pa tiiem son pui yuh krax pa lud loe,
mai ang mawh nang pa tiiem son pui pa hoek krax pa som nai khankix.
Deeh tieh tix,
kaing ting aux tiiem ka lox lai an,
hoik mawh son sang jhi krax pa rien rhawm pex ka yix,
ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex mawh pa kied raux tete.
13 Yix hoik pon krax pa joh jhan kheu in heue.
Ja lao ang mawh nan,
krax gaux rhawm Thi-to nie thiien thie ka krax pa gaux rhawm yix;
mawh kheu hoik teem coi pex ka nawh khawm uik,
mai kieh nawh gaux rhawm.
14 Aux hoik sax yaw pex ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
pex ang bax kieh aux kaig tua.
Yix hoik ca krai lox tete te lai ka pex;
yuh nang nan,
gum lox pa yaw yix pex ka Thi-to kawx,
hoik krai mawh tete.
15 Mawh kheu nan,
yam keud ce Thi-to koe krax pa cu nghiet pex yuh ka mawx,
hoik rieb pex nawh ka daux rhawm pa lhat graung yuh ka mawx,
krax muh nawh ka pex taug nie baox thiem thie tix.
16 Yam in aux kied gaux rhawm tete,
mawh kheu hoik chix aux tix yum rhawm ka pex khawm uik heue.
