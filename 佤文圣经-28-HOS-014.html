Heu-si-ax kok yo pui Yi-seu-liex deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex
14
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
phi map pex hoik kieh pex goh;
pex tag caw tix deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex.
2 Pex tag caw tix deeh hoek ka Simiang Siyiex dee in,
mai rawg buan ka nawh ah nin:
“Khiox khaw maix pluat ploi ka phi map yix khawm uik,
mai nghiet ka lox rawg yix buan,
kieh yix pon hawt tix yaog yaw maix awm lox pa hoik ap tix.
3 Pui Ya-sux ang pon taoh ka yix;
brung naing kawx ang pon kia ngien ka yix.
Yix ang lai baox krub ka riang rao ah nin:
‘maix mawh Siyiex yix.’
Simiang Siyiex eue,
yix hoik tawng grawng pa caox rhawm maix ka kawn doe heue.”
Simiang Siyiex ap tix tawx krax im ju pa khraox ka Yi-seu-liex
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Aux tag sang pai khaix mat pui cie tix moi,
mai muh kix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix;
aux ang lai baox song rhawm ka kix.
5 Aux mawh rawm lhiex ka daux haktiex kroh pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kix tag sang pruh awm tai siyag,
awm riah khaox Li-pa-noeng pa raux riah yuh nan.
6 Kix tag sang lih kuah plaung pa khraox,
mhawm awm khaox sime yuh nan;
kix tag sang lih taox bhawng,
awm khaox si oe bhawng Li-pa-noeng.
7 Kix tag sang baox pon ka krax pa kia ngien aux.
Kix tag sang awm si peem tai pa yeem yuh nan,
awm si peem plix siiet pa kied nie plix.
Kix tag sang awm plai pa nyawm Li-pa-noeng.
8 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,a
pex tag caw tix hu singai khaing riang rao;
aux tag sang nghiet ka lox pa rawg kix buan,
mai kia ngien ka kix.
Aux tag sang kieh kix ot greem veei tix awm khaox klai lhax yuh nan;
Aux mawh num dee bleeh buan son kix khawm uik heue.
Lox pa nghoik tix krai
9 Kieh pui pa lhak lhiao koe ping nyia cuyi tawng sidawng pa tiiem tix deuh ka daux phuk lai in;
kieh kix priex daux rhawm rhi tix.
Daux krax cie Simiang Siyiex mawh pa rauh pa see;
pui rauh pui see lawng ka daux krax in,
viang mawh nan,
pui phi map hoik sideeing tix goh kheu een daux krax in ang koe ceu heue.
