Grawng pa saux simiang Si-si-cia mai grawng pa deeh moi nawh
(Lai Simiang Phuk Ra 20.1–11; lai Been Phuk Ra 32.24–26)
38
Ka daux bawg yam in,
simiang Si-si-cia saux,
mai dex sang yum.
Yi-sai-yax kawn simex Ya-maw-see hoek ka simiang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
‘Tag caw tix yuh kaing daux nyiex ma maix mhawm,
mawh kheu ang jhawm saux maix lai chix moi;
rien khre maix sivoe sang yum tix heue.’ ”
2 Si-si-cia veeh pak ngai tix plak guh ding,
mai rawg buan ah nin:
3 “Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka grawng pa hoik mawh aux pui rauh see tom si dauh,
aux hoik viex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix yuh kaing jhaw maix,
hoik yuh kaing pa khaw caw ka plak sivoe ngai maix.”
Nawh ang pon gun mai ploi lox yiiem tix heue.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Yi-sai-yax
5 mai nhie nawh deeh ing ka Si-si-cia dee an,
krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex tax du maix Ta-wue krai yuh nin:
aux hoik mhong ka lox rawg maix buan,
mai hoik yaox nang ka rawm ngai maix;
aux tag sang kieh maix baox im ju kao phuan num heue.
6 Aux tag sang taoh maix mai Yie-lu-sa-leung khaing daux tawm taix Vawng Ya-sux,
mai ca kawn kia ngien ka veng in heue.”
21 Yi-sai-yax krai ka siliex simiang,
tag caw tix teei bee plix ruh kraix ka dee muad simiang,
nawh tom taug moi ka heue.
22 Simiang Si-si-cia chawk ah nin:
“Koe mhai sakse ceu pa tix pa sang kham grawng kawn sang pon aux tix hoek krub ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex lie?”
7 Yi-sai-yax pawk lox ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex tag sang tawx mhai sakse ka maix tix ceu,
mai kham sakse grawng lox pa ap nawh tix deuh kaux cong.
8 Ka piang glawk bawng pa yuh simiang Ya-ha-see,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh veei pa riang singaix tieh tix deeh kao diang.”
Khaix an,
veei pa riang singaix tieh tix deeh kao diang heue.
9 Khaix hoik moi saux Si-si-cia,
nawh tiiem ka lox rhax tix ceu ah nin:
10 Aux khi rhawm ka daux singaix pa num ju tix,
aux chix hu meung yum ka yum tix,
mai ang yaox im ka daux singaix num.
11 Aux lhat tix ot ka piang haktiex in,
ang pon tix lai baox yaox ka Simiang Siyiex,
ka pui pa kawn im ju ku kaux heue.
12 Riah ju aux hoik dut,
awm yieh ka nyiex gox laux,
awm man pa pat khaing daux cag taing man yuh nan.
Aux keud Simiang Siyiex sang kieh ju tix si dauh.
13 Aux hoik laux rhawm yiiem grai tom riang praix,
hoik awm kiied sivai sang si si-ang aux pot.
Aux keud Simiang Siyiex hoik sang kieh ju aux si dauh.
14 Lox si kah aux iak mai ang lai koe riang,
awm lox riag yo si kaux yuh nan.
Tawm ngai aux hoik taug mao kheu jhak tix ka rao ma.
Simiang aux eue,
khiox khaw maix teei tix tik ka krax kha rhawm kha rhi aux.
15 In hoik mawh kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex;
aux kawn sang krai ah ka mawx lie?
Rhawm rhi aux laux,
mai ang chix laig it laig tom heue.
 
16 Simiang aux eue,
aux sang im ju son maix,
mai im ju ngie son maix khaing;
khiox khaw maix pai aux moi,
kieh aux yaox tix im ju heue!
17 Krax laux rhawm aux tieh tix lih mawh pa nyawm rhawm.
Maix taoha ka ju aux,
luat luan khaing krax khi rhawm khawm uik;
maix pluat ploi ka phi map aux khawm uik.
18 Ka daux meung yum ang koe pui pa yaog yaw ka maix;
pui pa hoik yum ang chix yaog yaw ka maix;
Pui pa hoek meung yum ang chix een rhawm tix ka pa kaux sijax maix.
19 Mawh ngie pui pa im ju pa chix yaog yaw ka maix,
awm yaog yaw aux ka maix yam in.
Keeing tag sang krai ka kawn simex kawn bun tix,
maix kied sidaing kaux sijax heue!
20 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik pai aux moi heue.
Yix tag sang tauh ting,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix;
ka daux singaix yam kawn im ju,
yix sang yaog yaw maix ka daux nyiex buan heueb.
