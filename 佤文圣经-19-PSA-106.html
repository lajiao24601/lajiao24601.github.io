Siyiex kia ngien pui cie tix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix
106
aTag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!
Tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem nawh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju.
2 Pui mawx sang pon uik krai lao ka kaing ting pa yuh Simiang Siyiex lie?
Pui mawx sang pon uik krai lao ka lox pa yaog yaw nawh lie?
3 Pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx nawh kied koe buan son heue!
Pui pa yuh pa rauh pa see tom si dauh kied koe buan son heue!
4 Simiang Siyiex eue,
yam kia ngien maix ka pui cie tix,
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka aux,
taoh ka aux.
5 Khiox khaw maix kieh aux yaox ka grawng pa haok huan pui cie maix,
kieh aux pon krax gaux rhawm pui cie maix,
rom tix yaw rang di tau mai pa mawh pui cie maix heue.
 
6 Yix hoik yuh krax pa phit lud awm tax du tix yuh nan;
yix hoik yuh phi map mai kaing pa caox pa caig khawm uik.
7 bYam ot tax du yix ka Ai-cix,
mai kix ang tawng ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh Siyiex;
kix hoik piawm ka krax muh pa hoik jhi Siyiex ka kix tom nie bawg;
kix nieb pon tieh vheen ka cao pa ting riang ting raoc ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
8 Kheu nan,
Siyiex yuh son sang jhi ka pa ting riang ting rao tix,
ca kawn hawt tix taoh ka kix awm gum lox pa hoik ap tix deuh.
9 dNawh ne raw ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh,
mai rawm lih tix kroh dum mawx dum an;
nawh thung pui cie tix tiang hu,
mai awm hoik hu ka piang tiex kroh yuh nan;
10 nawh taoh kix luat luan khaing pui pa koeng ka kix;
nawh taoh kix lih khaing daux tawm taix pui koeng.
11 Viang mawh nan,
rawm hoik kheem ka pui koeng khawm uik;
pie tix kaux kawx ang yaox tix luat luan.
12 eMawh kheu nan,
pui cie nawh yum rhawm ka gum lox pa hoik ap nawh tix deuh;
kix rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
 
13 Viang mawh nan,
ang koe miex glang tix,
kix hoik piawm ka kaing pa yuh nawh;
kix ang cu tix krox jun ka alo nawh.
14 fKa daux tiag daux tu,
kix nieb pon hawt alo tix;
ka daux pak mhaik,
kix cheuk ka Siyiex tom nie bawg.
15 Nawh hawt awm pa khiox khaw kix mai tawx ka kix,
viang mawh nan kawx,
mai ploi nang ka jhawm saux lih hoek ka daux kix heue.
 
16 gKa daux nyiex bang,
kix boh rhawm ka Maw-si,
keud tix sang boh rhawm ka Ya-loeng pa mawh kawn cau Simiang Siyiex pa singax.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
haktiex bih,
mai bleet ka Ta-thang;
haktiex hoik si paung ka Ya-pi-lang mai ka pui nyiex ma nawh.
18 Koe ngu lih ka piang to kaux cub caw pui pa hawt rhawm kix,
hoik gleeing ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig yum khawm uik.
 
19 hKix raug tix yuh ka riang kawn moi ka gawng Heu-liex,
krub ka riang rao pa yuh ka taix.
20 Kix teei riang kawn moi pa sawm rib an,
kieh mawh pa tang son min plak ngai Siyiex.
21 Kix hoik piawm ka Siyiex pa taoh ka kix,
hoik piawm ka kaing ting pa yuh nawh ka haktiex Ai-cix,
22 yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka Hang,
yuh kaing pa kieh pui lhat graung ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh.
23 Yam krai Siyiex sang yuh laux ka pui cie tix,
pa mawh Maw-si siliex pa hoik laug nawh sau riang tix si kah ka nawh,
pon kieh nawh deeh teei ka krax pa song rhawm tix,
hoik teei tix tik ka krax pa sang yuh laux leeing ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
 
24 iViang mawh nan,
kix een haktiex pa mhawm an ang koe ceu;
kix ang yum rhawm ka gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex tix deuh.
25 Kix salao ka daux nyiex gox bang,
ang cu tix nghiet ka lox Simiang Siyiex.
26 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh bre tix ne raw ka kix ah nin:
Tag sang kieh kix goh tix yum ka daux tiag daux tu,
27 jmai kieh kawn praix kix prung prang tix hoek ka meung ku meung,
kieh kix vuk vied vung viang ka meung haktiex pui tang meung heue.
 
28 kKhaix an pui cie Siyiex nieb pon krub Pa-lix ka haktiex Phi-eu,
mai ih ka nex khawng tan pa hoik tan lu pui ka siyiex pa yum.
29 Kaing pa yuh kix cheeik Siyiex song rhawm song rhi,
jhawm saux preng pa lhat pui ka lih hoek ka daux kix.
30 Viang mawh nan,
Fe-ni-ha kaoh tix ciyang ka phi map pa yuh kix,
hoik pon tieb tie ka jhawm saux preng pa tauh hu.
31 Kaing pa mhawm in hoik kieh kawn pui doh keud kah,
jauh singaix yam an,
tom hoek tix ju hoek tix ju heue.
 
32 lKix kawx hoik cheeik Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka praok rawm keet Mi-li-pa,
khaix an mai kawn kieh kaing in hoek ka piang Maw-si.
33 Kix kieh Maw-si hoik kied sidaing suk sag rhawm,
kawn baox krai ka lox pa ang khaw tix krai.
34 mKix lheu ka gum lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
ang bax pauh ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
35 tieh tix mex moeng ka kix,
hawt tix gau ka been krax ot krax koe kix.
36 Pui cie Siyiex nieb pon hu krub ka riang rao,
kieh krax laux krax leeing hoek ka piang to kaux tix.
37 nKix hoik pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun pa keh tix,
kieh mawh krax tan pa tan lu ka phi ka daux haktiex Cia-nang.
38 oKix hoik pauh ka pui pa ang koe krax lud krax loe,
hoik kieh nham kawn simex kawn bun tix jauh,
hu krub ka riang rao pa koe ka haktiex Cia-nang;
grawng pa pauh kix pui yum hoik kieh haktiex an seeing brai.
39 Kaing pa yuh kix hoik kieh to kaux tix seeing brai,
hoik mawh krax pa lieh lheu mai pa tieh vheen ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex heue.
 
40 pMawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui cie tix;
nawh koe rhawm pa kied yum koeng ka kix.
41 Nawh kieh kix laux leeing ka daux tawm taix pui tang ceu tang cad,
kieh kix ot ka daux pa giex gang pui koeng.
42 Pui koeng gut kheem ka kix,
ang kieh kix yaox krax nyawm ot nyawm koe.
43 Simiang Siyiex hoik taoh ka pui cie tix tom nie bawg,
kix kawn gaux rhawm tix lawng daux krax pa tieh vheen,
nie baox nied tix laig ka daux krax phi map.
44 Viang mawh nan,
yam deeh koe krax pa khiox khaw kix,
Siyiex tom nghiet kah;
nawh kia ngien krax pa kha kix rhawm tix.
45 Mawh kheu jao son kix,
Simiang Siyiex doh keud ka sijax mukheu tix;
kheu krax muh nawh,
nawh deeh rhawm rhi tix.
46 Nawh kieh pui pa giex laung ka kix koe krax pa caox rhawm ka kix heue.
 
47 qSimiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
khiox khaw maix taoh ka yix.
Khiox khaw maix thung yix lih khaing meung haktiex ku meung,
kieh yix pon tix yaog yaw ka maix,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka ceu kaux pa singax maix heue.
 
48 Kieh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pon krax pa yaog yaw;
jauh tom dix dix tom hoek leeing ju,
rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix.
Kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex rom tau tix ah nin:
A-meung!
 
Tag caw tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue.
