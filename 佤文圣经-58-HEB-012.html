Keeing ex Siyiex
12
Kheu hoik koe ex sakse tom nie,
awm lai lawm pai awm ex yuh nan,
ex tag caw teei tix tik ka ceu pa tieb tie ka ex khawm uik,
mai ka phi map pa vung vhien ka ex ang jud,
viex rhawm gun sau riang tix to ka daux krax pa koe sivoe ex.
2 Ex tag caw dong ngai tix jhak ka Ye-sux,
kheu hoik mawh nawh cao pa phan pa yuh ka krax yum rhawm ex,
mai ka cao pa pon yuh grum naog.
Nawh ang yawn tix deeh ka plak sivoe khaox lhak;
deeh tieh tix,
kheu son pui pa krox jun krax gaux rhawm nawh,
nawh ang een krax pa kaig tua pa yum piang khaox lhak mawh pa tid tix.
Yam in nawh hoik ngawm ka piang dang ngeung plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex heue.
3 Keud tix yaox ka krax pa si pup nawh.
Hoik gun yeung nawh pa koeng khoi pui phi map yuh ka mawx heue!
Mawh kheu nan,
pex paw vi laux rhawm.
4 Pex yuh naing ka phi map,
mai ang bax tieb tie tom hoik lih nham tix.
5 aKhex hoik piawm pex grawng pa teei Siyiex gum lox pa sidiex gau ka kawn simex tix sidiex gau pex ka yuh ka mawx lie?
Nawh ah nin:
kawn simex aux eue,
tag caw bre rhawm tix ka krax pa sidiex gau Simiang Siyiex,
paw vi laux rhawm kheu pa ne raw nawh;
6 mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex sidiex gau ka can pui pa muh tix khankix,
si vied ka kawn simex pa hoik rieb tix ku kaux heue.
7 Pex tag caw tix gun yeung ka krax pa sidiex gau,
mawh kheu krax pa sidiex gau ka pex hoik jhi grawng pa kia ngien Siyiex pex awm kawn simex tix yuh nan.
Khex koe kawn simex mawx pa ang keeing sidiex gau lie?
8 Phan pex ang rieb krax pa sidiex gau awm kawn simex khankix,
pex tag ang mawh kawn simex tete,
mai mawh kawn nghe nga.
9 Keeing ka daux nex nham,
ex rieb krax pa sidiex gau,
mai ca kawn nab yang yung yi ka kix.
Phan mawh nan,
keeing ka daux khuan,
khex ang caw tix nie nghiet,
son sang khiox khaw tix pon ka ju lie?
10 Keeing ka daux nex nham hawt awm alo tix,
ran sidiex tix gau ka ex;
viang mawh nan,
krax pa sidiex gau Siyiex mawh son sang mhawm son ex,
son sang kieh ex koe nyum ka daux pa singax cie nawh.
11 Yam sidiex gau ka ex,
ex ang koe krax pa gaux rhawm;
viang mawh nan,
khaix an,
pui pa sidiex gau mai pa hoik cim krax pa pheuk taug chix plix plix pa nhim,
mai ot ka daux krax pa rauh pa see tete heue.
Pa sidiex mai pa jheeh nyaw
12 bMawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix yaog taix pa iak riang iak rao,
mai nyat caong pa si yaung si kua rauh heue!
13 cTag caw tix lawng ka daux krax pa rauh pa see jheeng jheeng,
son ang sang kieh caong pa chawh yaox tix laux meung khawm uik,
mai kieh tieh tix deeh pon riang rao pa khraox.
14 Tag caw sau riang tix khaw caw ka rhawm pui,
ot ka daux krax pa singax singeh;
pui pa ang ot ka daux krax pa singax singeh tag ang pon yaox ka Simiang Siyiex.
15 dTag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
son ang sang koe pui pa kieh grai ka buan son Siyiex.
Tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
son ang sang koe khaox ox pa koe jai yuh nan tix gawng,
ting haok huan,
mai kieh pui hun laux.
16 eTag caw rien rhawm tix,
son ang sang koe pui pa phit vao,
phan ang mawh nan,
ang rauh see awm Yi-sao,
kheu son priex preem tix ceeix ceeix mai coh ceu kawn ai tix.
17 fKhaix an pex hoik tawng,
nawh simie tix pon ka buan son pa nawb keeing tix,
viang mawh nan,
pui tieb tie kah;
viang nawh hoik roik rawm ngai mai sawg tix grawng sang deeh pon,
viang mawh nan,
ang lai koe rhawm keud ka grawng kaing pa hoik yuh tix heue.
18 gPex ang bax hoek ka gawng Si-nai pa chix tix theeh kah,
awm hoik hoek pui Yi-seu-liex ka yuh nan;
dee an koe rao ngu,
lon pai awm,
pa faik,
mai bhaung ting,
19 koe lox teet tawng mai lox si kah pui.
Yam mhong kawn pui lox in,
tag ang kieh kix lai baox nghiet ka lox tang ceu;
20 hmawh kheu ang kix pon klawm ka gum lox tawm an ah nin:
“Pa theeh ka gawng in,
viang hoik mawh khawng brie kawx tag sang teei simaox dheem nawh yum ka heue.”
21 iGrawng kaing an kied mawh pa lhat graung tete,
Maw-si tom ah nin:
“Aux si yaung si kua heue!”
22 Viang mawh nan,
pex hoek ka grawng Shi-ang,
mai hoik mawh hoek ka veng Siyiex pa koe leeing ju,
mawh Yie-lu-sa-leung pa koe ka piang rao ma,
koe kawn cau Siyiex ka kaing mheen kaing rheng.
23 Pex mawh pa hoek ka nyiex dee khrawm kawn ai Siyiex paox tix gaux rhawm,
ceu kaux kawn ai in kix hoik tiiem tix ot ka piang rao ma.
Pex mawh pa hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex pa prawng ca pui kaing mheen,
mawh pa hoek ka dee ot khuan pui pa rauh see pa hoik kub diag tong pa grum naog dee an.
24 jPex mawh pa hoek ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux pui pa ot si nax sijax mukheu pa khraox,
mawh pa hoek ka dee phruah nham dee an;
grawng pa jhi ka nham in kawn lheu mhawm khaing nham Ya-paw heue.
25 kPhan mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
paw vi thiang ka lox pa si kah ka pex an.
Pa thiang ka gum lox singax singeh pa sidiex pui tix tawx ka kix an piang haktiex,
tag ang pon tix luat luan khaing phi map,
phan ex thiang ka gum lox pa krai kaw an ka ex,
tag nie ang pon tix luat luan khaing phi map heue!
26 lYam an,
gum lox nawh hoik yung yang yuh haktiex,
viang mawh nan,
yam in nawh pawk ah nin:
“Baox tix bawg,
ja lao aux ang yung yang yuh haktiex,
rao ma kawx yung yang yuh heue.”
27 “Baox tix bawg” gum lox in hoik jhi sidawng grawng pa hoik yung yang ceu pa phan pa yuh,
grawng pa hoik gah tix hu,
son sang kieh ceu pa ang bax yung yang tix khankix deuh tix ot heue.
28 Mawh kheu nan,
ex tag caw tix yaog yaw ka meung an pa ang pon kieh yung yang tix.
Ex tag caw tix hawt awm pa khaw caw rhawm Siyiex,
viex rhawm pa rauh see pa lhat graung yung yi yuh kaing pa krub ka nawh,
29 mMawh kheu Siyiex ex mawh ngu pa yuh laux leeing heue.
