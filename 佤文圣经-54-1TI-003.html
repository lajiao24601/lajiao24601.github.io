Simiang ceu cao
3
Gum lox in mawh pa kaux sijax.a
Koe pui krai ah nin:
pui mawx koe beeh glawm tix mawh simiang ceu cao,b
pui an tag mawh pa ghoing ka kaing ceu pa mhawm.
2 dSimiang ceu cao tag caw tix mawh pui pa ang koe dee ne raw.
Nawh tag caw tix mawh pa koe moeng tix kaux khaingc;
nawh tag caw tix mawh pa nab yam pui,
pa pon priex ka to kaux tix,
krax ot krax koe nawh hoik chix tat tix deuh,
mai chix kia ngien ka pui tang praix tang klawng.
Nawh tag caw tix mawh pui pa chix sidiex,
3 ang yoek ka plai,
ang rhawm yuh simah,
rhawm rhi nawh awn tiiem rauh see,
ang muh ka mau.
4 Nawh tag caw tix chix yuh ka kaing daux nyiex tix mhawm,
mai kieh kawn simex kawn bun tix cu lox nab yam ka kaing ku ceu.
5 Pui pa ang chix yuh ka kaing daux nyiex tix,
sang chix kia ngien ka ceu cao Siyiex yuh ka mawx lie?
6 Simiang ceu cao kawx ang caw tix mawh pui pa taug khraox laig ka daux ceu cao,
son ang pui in sang lhaong rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai cud ka daux pa prawng ca phi caox.
7 Nawh kawx caw tix mawh pui pa koe min ka daux pui pa ang laig li,
son ang pui sang salao,
mai glup ka daux kie cie phi caox heue.
Pui cao teem coi ka simiang ceu cao
8 Pui cao teem coie simiang ceu cao kawx tag caw tix mawh pa mhawm jhawm.
Kix tag caw tix krai lox pa mawh pa meh,
paw vi ghoing ka kawk plai,
paw vi ghoing ka sin ngao;
9 caw tix teei rhawm rhi pa chix tawng yung pa singax giex kaux cong ka pa raux pa mox krax yum rhawm.
10 Kix tag caw tix ran ceed tix sivoe,
mai jhi grawng ang tix koe dee ne raw son sang pon yuh ka kaing in.
11 Moeng kixf kawx tag caw tix mhawm jhawm,
ang krai lox pa ang koe ceu,
tag caw tix koe krax pa nab yam,
mai kaux sijax rauh see ka daux kaing ku ceu.
12 Pui cao teem coi ka simiang ceu cao,
tag caw tix mawh pa koe moeng tix kaux khaing,
gkhaix an tag caw tix mawh pa chix sidiex gau ka kawn praix tix,
mai pa chix priex ka nyiex ma tix.
13 Pui cao teem coi pa mhawm yuh kaing khankix,
tag sang pon min ceu kaux pa mhawm,
mai ang ring krai lao grawng pa yum rhawm kix ka Ye-sux Khrit heue.
Pa raux pa mox pa kied ting
14 Yam tiiem aux ka lox lai in,
mai aux kied simie tix nied yaox ka pex;
15 phan hoik gla bawg yam,
lox lai in tag sang krai ka pex,
grawng krax ot krax koe ex sang caw tix yuh ka mawx ka daux nyiex Siyiex.
Nyiex in hoik mawh ceu cao Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
mawh rong simaox mai num jaong pa mawh pa meh.
16 Pui ku kaux hoik tawng,
pa raux pa mox krax yum rhawm ex mawh pa kied raux tete yuh nin:
nawh jhi tix lih ka daux pak peung kawn pui,
mawh Khuan Singax Singeh pa kham grawng pa rauh see nawh,
kawn cau Siyiex khankix yaox kah.
Kawn pui haktiex khawm uik sidiex nawh,
mai kieh kawn pui piang haktiex yum rhawm kah,
mai rieb nawh haok piang rao ma heue.
