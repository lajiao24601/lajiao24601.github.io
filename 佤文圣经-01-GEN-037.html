Yaw-seux mai pux aik nawh
37
Ya-keux kawn ca ot ka haktiex Cia-nang pa hoik ot keeing nawh sivoe.
2 In plak greem mawh pa tiiem grawng nyiex ma Ya-keux khankix.
Yam koe ju Yaw-seux kao aliah num,
nawh rien hu liang yung mai pux ke tix khankix.
Nawh teem kawn simex moeng iak keeing tix Pi-la mai Si-phax liang yung;
nawh rien krai kaing caox pa yuh pux ke tix khankix ka keeing tix.
3 Ya-keux kied muh ka Yaw-seux,
lheu khaing kawn simex nawh tang kaux,
kheu mawh nawh kawn simex pa pon Yaw-seux yam kuad ju tix;
Ya-keux caing Yaw-seux ceep kraung sibex pa mhawm grang pa koe rawm sianga.
4 Pux ke Yaw-seux khankix yaog blawn muh keeing kix Yaw-seux,
tom koeng ka pux aik tix,
ang yuh paox pie khaw rhawm mai nawh heue.
5 Koe tix bawg,
Yaw-seux ca maox.
Nawh krai ca maox in ka pux ke tix khankix,
kix nie baox koeng ka nawh.
6 Nawh ah nin:
“Kieh aux krai ca maox tix ka pex:
7 Ex mad lhiem nghox ca ka daux kaung,
pa mad aux yaog tix kao cung rauh;
pa mad cie pex vung viang tix krub ka mad cie aux.”
8 Pux ke nawh khankix chawk nawh:
“Maix sang mawh simiang pa kuang ka piang yix lie?”
Mawh kheu krai Yaw-seux ca maox tix ka pux ke tix mhong,
kix nied tix koeng ka nawh lheu khaing singaix pa luan.
9 Khaix an,
Yaw-seux baox deeh ca maox.
Nawh baox krai ka pux ke tix khankix:
“Aux baox ca maox;
aux yaox ka singaix,
khix,
mai simuing kao mu tix krub ka aux heue.”
10 Yaw-seux kawx krai ca maox in ka keeing tix.
Keeing nawh ne raw ka nawh ah nin:
“Sawn maix ca maox in mawh pa tix?
Maix keud sang kieh miex maix,
pux ke maix,
mai aux kawx krub ka maix lie?”
11 bPux ke Yaw-seux khankix kied boh rhawm ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
keeing nawh een tix tawng grawng in daux rhawm tix heue.
Coh Yaw-seux hoek ka haktiex Ai-cix
12 Koe tix ngaix,
pux ke Yaw-seux khankix hu liang yung cie keeing kix ka veng See-ciien.
13 Ya-keux ah nin ka Yaw-seux:
“Pux ke maix khankix hu liang yung ka See-ciien,
maix hu jhak ka kix heue.”
Yaw-seux pawk lox:
“Aux lawk hu!”
14 Keeing ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix hu jhak ka pux ke maix khankix nyawm ot ang nyawm ot,
phung yung mhawm ang mhawm ot,
deeh hoek krai ka aux.”
Kheu nan keeing nawh tawx nawh hoek ka daux ngawd gawng Si-paw-loeng.
Yaw-seux hoek ka See-ciien,
15 Mai lud krax daux tiag.
Koe pui tix kaux yaox ka nawh,
chawk nawh:
“Sawg maix pa tix lie?”
16 Nawh pawk:
“Aux sawg pux ke tix khankix.
Krai aux mhong liak,
liang kix yung dee mawx?”
17 Pui an ah nin:
“Kix hoik hu.
Aux hoik mhong sang hu kix ka haktiex To-thang.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-seux deeh hawt khaix pux ke tix khankix,
mai yaox kix ka To-thang heue.
18 Kix kok yaox ka Yaw-seux.
Sivoe sang hoek dex nawh ka kix,
kix kum paox tix,
sang pauh ka nawh.
19 Kix ah nin ka paox tix:
“Pui ca maox an hoek,
20 hoek heue!
Pauh ex ka nawh,
mai tik to kaux yum nawh ka daux rawm maw kroh,
krai kiied to tiag tix ih nawh.
Kieh ex jhak ca maox nawh sang pon ang pon tix lih heue.”
21 Lui-piien mhong ka lox pa kum kix,
mai keud tix sang taoh ka Yaw-seux;
nawh ah nin:
“Ex ang pon tix pauh ka nawh,
22 tik nawh laig ka daux rawm maw kroh daux tiag,
paw lai yuh nawh laux.”
Nawh ah yuh nin,
daux rhawm nawh keud tix sang taoh Yaw-seux khaing daux tawm taix kix,
mai deeh tawx ing ka keeing dee an.
23 Yaw-seux hoek ka pux ke tix dee an,
kix tom peeik ka sibex pa mhawm rawm siang nawh,
24 Mai giex ka nawh,
tik nawh laig ka daux rawm maw kroh heue.
25 Yam ngawm kix tix sawm,
yaog kaing tix jhak,
kix yaox ka pui Yi-seu-ma-lix pa yuh lai gee hawt ka ka-la-uk kaoh khaing Ci-liex,
sang hu ka haktiex Ai-cix;
ka-la-uk kix tang naog ka ceu nyawng bhawng,
sidah bhawng Law-ban,
Muran khankix.
26 Yo-tax ah nin ka pux tix khankix:
“Ex pauh ka pux aik tix,
kieh phi map mox tix koe dee mhawm yuh ka mawx lie?
27 Ang lheu khaing coh ex nawh ka pui Yi-seu-ma-lix in kix.
Yuh nin,
ex ang siao yuh nawh laux;
nawh hoik mawh nang pux ex,
nex nham ex.”
Pux aik nawh khankix cu kah.
28 dYam an koe pui Mi-tiien khankix pa yuh lai gee hu ka krax an,
pux ke nawh khankix c tom leed Yaw-seux lih khaing daux rawm maw krox,
coh nawh ka mau tix nga plah ka pui Yi-seu-ma-lix;
pui in kix tom viex Yaw-seux ing ka haktiex Ai-cix heue.
29 Lui-piien deeh hoek ka praok rawm maw krox,
ang yaox ka Yaw-seux.
Nawh laux rhawm mai riah ka sibex tix,
30 deeh hoek ka dee ot pux aik tix tan,
ah nin:
“Ang lai yaox kawn nyawm!
Sang yuh ka mawx lie?”
31 Khaix an,
kix pauh ka kawn pex tix mu,
mai kraix nham ka sibex Yaw-seux,
32 viex hoek ka dee ot keeing kix dee an,
ah nin:
“Yix yaox ka sibex in,
mawh cie kawn simex maix lie?”
33 Ya-keux tawng ka sibex in,
tom ah nin:
“In mawh cie kawn simex aux!
Rien tix mawh to tiag pa ih ka nawh.
Kawn simex aux Yaw-seux,
to tiag hoik kiied nawh phawng!”
34 Ya-keux kied laux rhawm mai riah sibex tix,
lhop ka man keei sum ka piang tix,
yiiem son kawn simex tix tom rheung singaix.
35 Kawn simex kawn bun nawh hoek tix kieh nawh nyawm rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang cu tix nyawm rhawm.
Nawh ah nin:
“Tom hoek aux meung yum,
kawn sang laux rhawm son kawn simex aux!”
Nawh kawn ca yiiem son kawn simex tix heue.
36 Yam an,
pui Mi-tiien khankix coh Yaw-seux ka Paw-thi-fa pa mawh simiang Ai-cix cao giex kaing pui naing ka haktiex Ai-cix heue.
