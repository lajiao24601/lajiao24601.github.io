Lox rhax krax pa laux leeing son meung
89
aSimiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka krax muh maix leeing ju;
aux sang krai pao ka pa kaux sijax maix tix ju hoek tix ju.
2 Aux hoik tawng,
krax muh maix hoik koe leeing ju leeing phan;
pa kaux sijax maix hoik kaux cong awm rao ma leeing ju.
3 Maix ah nin:
aux hoik sax yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pui pa hoik laug aux;
aux hoik sax ap lox ka siliex tix Ta-wue ah nin:
4 bAux sang kieh doeh ceu cad maix mawh Simiang leeing ju;
aux sang joh jhan ka Simiang pa mawh nawh tom hoek kaing mheen ju heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex eue,
rao ma khawm uik tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka kaing pa daox pa deng pa yuh maix;
cao pa singax tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka pa kaux sijax maix ka rao ma.
6 Simiang Siyiex eue,
piang rao ma ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa lheu khaing maix;
ang koe siyiex tix kaux kawx pa awm maix.
7 Maix blawn pon krax pa yung pa yi ka daux pa khrawm cao pa singax ka paox tix;
kix ot hawt sivie siveng maix,
mai hoik lhat graung yung yi ka maix heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao kru naing kaing mheen eue,
pa ting riang ting rao maix pui mawx pa pon tiag tix tau mai lie?
Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik kaux sijax ka piang kaing ku ceu khawm uik.
9 Maix kuang ka rawm sigrit sigra;
maix kieh rawm ting pa puk pak yien yawg.
10 Maix tawk bhliet ka La-ha-pawc to sat pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra pa lhat graung pui kah,
mai pauh kix yum heue.
Maix teei ka siawp sibrawm tix,
mai pauh ka pui pa koeng ka maix.
11 Rao ma mawh cie maix,
haktiex kawx mawh cie maix;
maix hoik phan yuh ka haktiex mai ka to sat pa koe ka daux haktiex khawm uik.
12 Jauh ka daog haktiex plak seh tom hoek plak laong hoik mawh pa phan pa yuh maix khawm uik;
gawng Tha-paw,
gawng He-meung hoik gaux rhawm rhax lox pa si num khawm uik.
13 Plawng taix maix kied sidaing koe riang tete!
Riang rao maix kied sidaing ting tete!
14 Meung cie maix kieh pa rauh pa see mawh riah;
can pa yuh maix khawm uik hoik mawh pa kaux sijax mai krax muh.
15 Pui pa teei lox rhax krub ka maix kied ting buan son tete heue!
Krax ot krax koe kix hoik ot ka daux pa riang krax muh maix.
16 Mawh kheu maix,
kix hoik gaux rhawm leeing lang singaix;
kix rhax lai yaog yaw ka pa mhawm pa mhiem maix.
17 Min plak ngai pa ting riang ting rao maix hoik kieh yix brax kua;
buan son maix hoik kieh yix pon rhax lox si num.
18 Mawh kheu maix pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh cao pa kia ngien ka yix;
maix pa mawh cao pa singax Yi-seu-liex,
mai hoik mawh Simiang ka yix heue.
Gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex ka Ta-wue
19 Maix hoik sax ot ka daux riang su sai,
mai ah nin ka pui cie tix pa singax singeh:
Aux hoik thiem tie tix tawx mhok khri ka pui naing pa mhawm rhawm keud;
aux hoik tawx pui pa hoik laug aux an khaing daux pui hun mawh simiang.
20 dAux hoik laug ka siliex aux Ta-wue;
aux hoik ap nawh mawh simiang pex.
21 Aux sang tawx riang rao ka nawh;
Riang rao aux tag sang kieh nawh kaux cong.
22 Pui koeng ka nawh ang chix pon pex nawh leeing ju;
pui caox ang pon puing pex ka nawh.
23 Aux sang tawk laux ka pui pa tieb tie ka nawh;
aux sang pauh ka can pui pa koeng khoi ka nawh yum.
24 Aux sang teei pa kaux sijax tix mai krax muh tix kia ngien ka nawh;
aux sang kieh nawh pon pex jheeng jheeng.
25 Aux sang vih ka meung haktiex cie nawh vieh va,
jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
26 Nawh sang ah nin ka aux:
maix mawh keeing aux,
mawh Siyiex aux;
maix mawh cao pa kia ngien aux,
cao pa taoh ka aux.
27 eAux sang ap nawh mawh kawn ai,
kawn lheu lhaong khaing simiang pa blawn ting ka piang haktiex.
28 Aux sang giex tom kaux cong ka krax muh pa ang chix tieh loh tix ka nawh;
sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai nawh hoik koe ceu leeing ju leeing phan.
29 Ka daux doeh ceu cad nawh koe pui tag sang giex ka kaing leeing ju;
dang ngeung nawh hoik awm rao ma koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
30 Viang mawh nan,
doeh ceu cad nawh,
phan lieh lheu ka lox tat jhawm prawng aux,
ang priex tix hawt ka jhawm prawng aux,
31 phan kix tik vud ka gum lox pa sidiex gau aux,
ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox ping nyap aux,
32 aux tom tag sang ciyang kix kheu phi map kix;
aux tag sang si vied kix kheu krax pa lud loe kix.
33 Kheu nan,
aux sang ca nied tix tawx krax muh tix ka Ta-wue;
aux sang kaux sijax ka nawh leeing ju.
34 Aux ang tik ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai nawh,
ang lai deeh teei nang ka lox pa hoik ap tix ka nawh heue.
 
35 Aux hoik tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux pa singax singeh tix ah nin:
Aux ang doh krai lox pie ka Ta-wue.
36 Doeh ceu cad nawh tag sang toe tix ang jud;
aux tag sang kia ngien ka meung cie nawh awm hoik jhan riang singaix leeing ju yuh nan.
37 Dang ngeung ngawm nawh tag sang koe awm riang khix leeing ju
pa mawh mhai sakse pa kaux sijax ka piang rao ma an heue.
Pa yiiem son ang simiang pex
38 Viang mawh nan,
maix hoik song rhawm ka simiang pa hoik laug tix;
maix hoik tieb tie ka nawh,
tik vud ka nawh.
39 Maix hoik teei tix tik ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh maix tau mai siliex tix;
maix hoik tik ka mhok khri nawh piang tiex.
40 Maix hoik yieh laux ka guh ding veng cie nawh;
maix hoik kieh tab cie nawh lih mawh dee riem.
41 Pui pa lai ling hoik tud rhim ka nawh;
meung pa koe sivie siveng nawh hoik map nai ka nawh.
42 Maix hoik kieh pui pa koeng ka nawh hoek krax pa pon pex;
hoik kieh can pui koeng khawm uik pon krax pa gaux rhawm ka daux pa ang khaw.
43 Maix hoik kieh kraung naing cie nawh ang lai chix tix siao,
ang lai kieh nawh pex ka daux naing.
44 Maix hoik teei ka khaox gix pa koe sibrawmf cie nawh hu,
hoik cot ka dang ngeung nawh klup tix piang tiex.
45 Maix hoik kieh nawh iak riang tom ang nyang bax kuad ju;
maix hoik kieh nawh kham krax kaig krax tua khawm uik heue.
Khiox khaw krax taoh Siyiex
46 Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix ang simi ka yix sang hoek yam mawx lie?
Krax pa song rhawm maix awm ngu pa cap tom hoek leeing ju lie?
47 Khiox khaw maix doh keud ka ju aux kied mawh pa dawt tete;
khiox khaw maix doh keud ka kawn pui pa phan yuh maix tag sang chix laux chix si um.
48 Pui mawx pa ang chix yum lie?
Pui mawx pon yaox tix luat luan khaing sibrawm si meeig lie?
49 Simiang eue,
krax muh maix sivoe an ot dee mawx lie?
Pa kaux sijax maix ka Ta-wue ot dee mawx lie?
50 Khiox khaw maix paw vi piawm ka krax pa kaig pa tua pa kham siliex maix;
khiox khaw maix paw vi piawm pa kham aux krax pa map naig pui tang ceu tang cad.
51 Simiang Siyiex eue,
pui koeng ka maix map nai ka simiang pa hoik laug maix;
viang nawh hoek dee mawx ang ah,
kix hoik map nai ka nawh heue.
 
52 Kieh Simiang Siyiex pon krax pa yaog yaw leeing ju heue!
 
A-meung!
A-meung!
