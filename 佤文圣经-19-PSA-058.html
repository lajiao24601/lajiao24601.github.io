Khiox khaw Siyiex ciyang ka pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig
58
Simianga eue,
pex hoik hawt ka lox pa krai tix tete lie?
Pex hoik teei pa rauh see prawng ca ka kawn pui lie?
2 Ang!
Ka daux rhawm pex hoik keud ngie tix yuh ka pa caox pa caig,
ka phi map krax pa tud rhim piang haktiex heue.
 
3 Pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig,
yam jauh tix keh,
mai hoik lud ka krax hu tix;
yam jauh kix tix lih khaing daux vaig miex tix,
mai hoik chix krai lox pie lhien.
4 To kaux kix hoik naog ka jai pa caox pa caig,
awm si uing pa koe jai yuh nan;
kix thox yhaok tix,
awm si uing tiex teen pa lheet yhaok,
5 ang mhong lox lak leh pui pa sawg tix klieh mai si uing,
ang mhong nang lox jhax lawg pui pa chix si meed heue.
 
6 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix raig ka rhang pa koe daux jheeh kix pot;
Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix laoh tix tik ka rhang kab sivai sang si pa nhum ju an kix.
7 Kieh kix awm rawm klawng iak pa kroh,
awm gawng rib pa dig pui laux greem caong tix.
8 Kieh kix awm laox pa mo laux,
awm kawn ngie pa hoik laux ka daux vaig ang yaox ka pa riang singaix.
9 Kix tag ang bax tien keud tien sawn ka daux tix vud khaing,
awm lhax khaox kroh pa phlu Siyiex hu ka daux pa song rhawm tix heue.
 
10 Pui rauh pui see gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
kheu yaox tix grawng pa kham pui phi map krax ciyang.
Kix tag sang hu dig khaing ka daux nham rawm klawng pui pa caox pa caig,
11 Yuh nin,
kawn pui tag sang ah nin:
pui rauh pui see rub hoik yaox krax pa mhawm,
kheu pa hoik yuh tix kaing pa mhawm;
rub hoik koe ka Siyiex cao pa prawng ca ka haktiex tix kaux heue.
