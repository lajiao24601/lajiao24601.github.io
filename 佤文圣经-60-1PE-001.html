Lai Pi-teux Phuk Tix
1
Aux pa mawh Pi-teux mawh kawn cau Ye-sux Khrit.
Aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka pui ceu pa hoik laug Siyiex ka yuh nin:
kawn Khrit pa prung prang tix ot ka haktiex Peung-to,
Cia-la-thaix,
Cia-pha-to-cia,
Ya-si-yax,
Pi-thoe-ni ku dauh khankix kheu krax kha rhawm.
2 Pex pon pa laug Siyiex hoik mawh pa hawt ka alo pa grox ciak ci keeing Siyiex,
mawh pa lih tix singax singeh ka daux Khuan Singax Singeh,
son sang cu nghiet ka Ye-sux Khrit,
khaix an pon tix singax ka daux nham nawh heue.
Kieh Siyiex tawx buan son pa kub mai krax pa nhim ka pex heue!
Krax keud pot jun pot pa im
3 Ex tag yaog yaw ka keeing Siyiex Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue!
Mawh kheu hoik koe nawh krax caox rhawm pa ang chix uik chix leeing,
mai ka daux Ye-sux Khrit pa deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
pa teei tix tawx ju pa khraox ka pex,
kieh ex koe krax keud pot jun pot pa im,
4 hoek tix pon ka buan son pa hoik deuh Siyiex piang rao ma son pui cie tix.
Buan son in mawh pa ang chix laux leeing,
ang chix seeing brai,
ang chix grai grup,
5 mawh pa tag sang tawx ka pui in kix kheu hoik yum rhawm ka Siyiex.
Tag pon krax kia ngien nawh pa ting siawp sibrawm,
son sang kieh pex pon krax taoh pa hoik rien khre mhawm mhawm,
pa tag sang lih tix mawh ka daux pa si dauh been.
6 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
yam in viang pex hoik kham krax pa cheuk cheu tom nie ceu,
mai krax kha rhawm pa caw pex tix ran sang kham tix vud tix cu.
7 Krax pa kham ceu in mai hoik mawh nang son sang jhi sidawng ka grawng pa koe pex krax yum rhawm pa kaux cong.
Viang mawh nan,
gawng khri pa chix laux khankix kawx hoik pon kham ka pa ceed ngu.
Krax yum rhawm pex kawn lheu ngawh khaing gawng khri tom nie,
pex tag caw tix nie blawn pon kham ka krax pa cheuk cheu,
son sang kieh pex pon krax pa yaog yaw,
ka min plak ngai,
mai ka ceu pa koe min ceu kaux ka daux singaix yam lih hoek Ye-sux Khrit.
8 Viang pex ang sax yaox ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik muh ka nawh;
yam in viang pex ang chix yaox ka nawh,
pex hoik yum rhawm ka nawh.
Pex hoik koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi pa ting pa lhaong an,
pa ang pui pon teei lox tix krai lih;
9 mawh kheu pex hoik rieb ka ceu pa yuh ka daux krax yum rhawm,
pa mawh krax taoh ka ju khuan pex heue.
10 Phan krai lao ka grawng krax taoh in,
cao jhai khankix hoik sawg tix khiox khaw mai si kaw ka sidawng,
khaix an hoik grox krai grawng sang tawx Siyiex buan son in ka pex.
11 Kix si kaw kah,
son sang kieh tix tawng khuan Khrit pa hoik jhi ka daux kix,
tag sang lih yam mawx,
sang lih tix mawh ka daux kaing ceu pa yuh ka mawxa.
Kaing in mawh gum lox jhai grawng sang kham Khrit krax kha rhawm,
khaix an tag sang pon min plak ngai.
12 Siyiex kieh cao jhai khankix tawng,
kaing pa yuh kix ang mawh pa yuh son to kaux tix,
hoik mawh pa yuh son pex.
Kaing pa hoik krai kix in,
yam in hoik sidiex tix tawx ka pex ka daux kawn cau pa sidiex gum lox buan khankix,
mai ka daux pa een rhawm tix ka riang rao Khuan Singax Singeh pa kaoh khaing rao ma;
kaing in kix pie kawn cau Siyiex kawx hoik simie pex tawng sidawng heue.
Krax ot krax koe pa singax singeh
13 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix rien khre rhawm rhi tix mhawm mhawm,
si duad tix haing ku bawg ku yam,
mai teei tix deuh krax keud pot jun pot yam lih hoek Ye-sux Khrit tix sang tawx buan son ka piang pex.
14 Tag caw tix nghiet ka lox Siyiex,
ang pon tix kieh alo nex nham giex gang ka pex,
awm yam daox aw pex ka daux singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan.
15 Siyiex kaw pa kok pex an mawh pa singax singeh;
pex kawx tag caw kieh tix singaix ka daux kaing pa yuh tix khawm uik.
16 Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix singax singeh,
mawh kheu aux mawh pa singax singeh heue.”
17 Yam rawg pex buan,
Siyiex kaw pa hawt tix yuh awm kaing pa yuh pui,
pa teei diag tong pa awm paox hoek tix prawng ca kawn pui mawh keeing;
phan mawh nan,
singaix yam kawn ot pex piang haktiex tag caw tix lhat graung yung yi ka nawh.
18 Pex hoik tawng,
pex hoik pon krax pa pluat ploi khaing daux pa laux leeing seeing brai tax du tix,
in hoik mawh pa tawx ka ngawh pa yuh ka mawx.
Ang doh mawh ngawh jum ceu pa chix grai,
awm mau khri yuh nan,
19 hoik mawh ka daux pa jauh nham pa ting ngawh Khrit,
awm nham kawn yung ang koe dee ne yuh nan.
20 Khrit mawh pa grox laug Siyiex sivoe sang phan yuh tix ka rao ma haktiex,
mai lih bleeh son pex ka daux singaix yam si dauh been in.
21 Ka daux nawh,
pex hoik yum rhawm ka Siyiex kaw pa kieh nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
pa tawx min plak ngai ka nawh;
mawh kheu nan,
krax yum rhawm pex mai krax keud pot jun pot hoik mawh pa rom tix ka daux Siyiex khawm uik heue.
22 Yam in,
mawh kheu hoik nghiet pex ka pa mawh pa meh,
pex hoik kieh to kaux tix singax,
mai si doe rhawm rhi tix tau mai kawn Khrit tang kaux,
pex hoik ra lai muh paox tix khawm rhawm khawm rhi.
23 Pex hoik deeh baox keh,
ang mawh ka daux pa keh khaing simie pa chix laux chix leeing,
hoik mawh pa keh ka daux simie pa ang chix laux chix leeing,
pa mawh ka daux gum lox pa im ju cie Siyiex leeing ju.
24 Awm krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Kawn pui awm rib riem daux tiag yuh nan,
min plak ngai khawm uik awm tai rib tai riem;
rieb riem chix kroh,
tai chix khrum,
25 viang mawh nan,
gum lox cie Simiang Siyiex koe leeing ju heue!
Gum lox in mawh gum lox buan pa hoik sidiex tix tawx ka pex heue.
