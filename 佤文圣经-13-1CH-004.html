Doeh ceu pui Yo-tax
4
Doeh ceu pui Yo-tax mawh khawm Fa-leu-see,
Si-see-loeng,
Cia-mi,
Hu-eux mai Saw-pa.
2 Saw-pa keh Li-ya-ya;
Li-ya-ya keh Ya-ha;
Ya-ha keh Ya-hu-mai mai La-ha.
Kix mawh tax du pui pa ot haktiex Saw-la heue.
3-4 Hu-eux mawh kawn simex pa keh Cia-leux mai Yi-fa-tha;
doeh ceu cad nawh hoik pang ka veng Paw-li-haung.
Hu-eux koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Yi-thang,
Pi-nu-yi-leux,
Yi-shie-eu.
Yi-thang koe kawn simexa loe kaux,
pa mawh Yie-see-liex,
Yi-see-max,
Yi-teu-pa,
kawn koe kawn bun tix kaux pa mawh Ha-si-leu-paw-ni.
Pi-nu-yi-leux pang ka veng Ci-to;
Yi-shie-eu pang ka veng Hu-sa heue.
5 Pui pa pang ka veng Thi-keu-yax pa mawh Ya-see-hux koe moeng ra kaux,
mawh Si-la mai Na-la.
6 Na-la keh nawh cie kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ya-hu-sha,
Si-fu,
Thi-mi-ni,
Ha-sia-see-tha-lix.
7 Si-la keh nawh cie kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Si-liex,
Saw-sia,
Yi-thi-nang heue.
8 Keu-see mawh keeing Ya-naw mai Saw-pi-pa,
kawn simex Ha-loeng,
tax du ceu pui Ya-ha-he heue.
9 Koe pui tix kaux ceu kaux mawh Ya-pi-see,
nawh hoik mawh pui pa ting ceu tix kaux ka daux nyiex kix.
Miex nawh sum ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-pi-seeb,
mawh kheu yam keh nawh kied sidaing kha rhawm tix.
10 Ya-pi-see khiox khaw ka Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix tawx buan ka aux,
mai tawx haktiex ka aux tom nie.
Khiox khaw maix ot tau mai aux,
kia ngien ka aux,
kieh aux luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing khawm uik.”
Siyiex tom hawt tix yuh awm pa khiox khaw nawh heue.
Ceu kaux cub nyiex pui tang ceu khankix
11 Pux aik Su-ha pa kaux Ci-luix koe kawn simex tix kaux,
ceu kaux mawh Mi-hex.
Mi-hex keh Yi-see-thuing;
12 Yi-see-thuing koe kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh Paw La-pa,
Pa-si-yax,
Thi-sing-na.
Thi-sing-na mawh pui pa pang ka veng Na-sia.
Doeh ceu pui in kix ot ka Li-cia khawm uik heue.
13 Ci-na-see koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Eu-thaw-niiex mai Si-lai-ya.
Eu-thaw-niiex kawx hoik koe kawn simex ra kaux nang,
pa mawh Ha-thax mai Ming-naw-thaix.c
14 Ming-naw-thaix mawh keeing Eu-fu-la heue.
Si-lai-ya keh Yaw-ya;
Yaw-ya mawh cao pa pang ngawd pui cang mai;
pui pa ot ka ngawd pui cang mai mawh pui pa chix kaing taix tix.
15 Kawn simex Yie-fu-ni pa mawh Cia-leux koe kawn simex loe kaux,
mawh Yi-lux,
Yi-la,
Na-ang.
Yi-la keh Ci-na-see heue.
16 Yie-ha-li-leux koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Si-fu,
Si-fa,
Thi-lix,
Ya-sha-liex heue.
17-18 Yi-see-la koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Yi-thie,
Mi-liex,
Yi-fu,
Ya-loeng.
Mi-liex moeng kawn bun simiang Ai-cix pa kaux Pi-thi-ya;
kiex koe kawn bun tix kaux pa mawh Mi-li-ang,
kawn simex ra kaux pa mawh Sa-mai mai Yi-pa.
Yi-pa sum ka veng Yi-seu-thi-maw.
Mi-liex kawx moeng ka kawn bun ceu pui Yo-tax tix kaux;
kiex keh ka kawn simex loe kaux,
pa mawh pui pa pang ka veng Ci-to pa kaux Ya-liex,
pui pa pang ka veng Saw-keux pa kaux Si-paw,
mai pui pa pang ka veng Sa-naw-yax pa kaux Yie-ku-thiie heue.
19 Heu-ti-ya moeng ka nan Na-hang;
doeh ceu cad kix mawh tax du ceu pui Cia-mi pa ot ka veng Ci-yi-la mai ceu pui Ma-cia pa ot daux veng Yi-seu-thi-maw heue.
20 See-meung koe kawn simex pon kaux,
pa mawh Ang-noeng,
Ling-na,
Piien Ha-nang,
Thi-loeng heue.
Yi-seex koe kawn simex ra kaux,
pa mawh Saw-hex mai Piien Saw-hex heue.
Doeh ceu pui See-la
21 Ka daux kawn simex Yo-tax,
koe pui tix kaux ceu kaux mawh See-la.
Doeh ceu pui See-la hoik mawh khawm pui pa hoik pang ka veng Li-ciax pa kaux Eux,
pui pa hoik pang ka veng Ma-li-sa pa kaux La-tax,
ceu pui pa ot tix taing man keei sum ka veng Paw Ya-seu-pi,
22 mai khawm Yaw-cing,
pui pa ot daux veng Keu-si-pa,
Yaw-a-see mai pui pa hoik teei tix moeng ka bun Maw-ya an,
pa ot ka daux veng Paw-li-haung pa kaux Sa-la.
(In kix mawh pa hoik tiiem tix deuh tom dix dix.)
23 Kix hoik mawh cang mai pa yuh aw simiang,
pa ot daux Ni-tha-ying mai Ci-ti-la heue.
Doeh ceu pui Si-miien
24 Si-miien koe kawn simex phuan kaux,
pa mawh Ni-mu-lix,
Ya-ming,
Ya-lix,
Shie-la,
Sao-law.
25 Kawn simex Sao-law mawh Sa-long,
kawn saux nawh mawh Mi-pi-sang,
kawn sex nawh mawh Mi-see-max.
26 Kawn simex Mi-see-max mawh Ha-mu-lix,
kawn saux nawh mawh Sa-kheux,
kawn sex nawh mawh See-me.
27 See-me koe kawn simex kao liah kaux,
kawn bun liah kaux,
viang mawh nan,
kawn praix pux aik nawh kied tux,
mawh kheu nin,
ceu pui Si-miien ang haok huan awm ceu pui Yo-tax yuh nan heue.
28 dDoeh ceu pui Si-miien,
tom hoek ka baux bawg yam singaix simiang Ta-wue,
hoik ot ka daux veng in kix pa tiiem tix ot plak greem:
pa mawh Piie-see-pa,
Maw-la-tax,
Ha-sa Su-yax,
29 Pi-la,
Yi-seeng,
Thaw-lax,
30 Pi-thu-lix,
Heu-eu-ma,
Si-keu-la,
31 Paw Ma-cia-pawx,
Ha-sa Su-sha,
Paw Pi-lix,
Sa-la-ying.
32 Kix kawx hoik ot ka veng Yi-thang,
Ya-ying,
Ling-meung,
Thaw-ciien,
Ya-sang khankix phuan veng,
33 mai khawm yaong pa dex dee an khankix,
tom hoek ka veng Pa-lix pa ot plak klip singaix ka plak seh.
In plak piang mawh pa tiiem cub nyiex ma kix mai ka dee ot kix heue.
34-38 Ceu kaux cao kaing ceu pui nyiex ma kix,
tiiem tix ot plak greem:
Mi-saw-pa,
Ya-mi-leu,
kawn simex Ya-ma-shiex pa kaux Yaw-sa,
Yaw-eux,
kawn simex Yaw-see-pi pa kaux Yie-hu (Yaw-see-pi mawh kawn simex Si-lai-ya,
kawn saux Ya-shiie).
Yi-li-yaw-nai,
Ya-keu-pa,
Yaw-saw-hai,
Ya-soi-ya,
Ya-di-yiex,
Yie-si-miie,
Pi-na-ya,
kawn simex See-fe pa mawh Si-sha (See-fe mawh kawn simex Ya-long,
mawh doeh ceu cad Yie-ta-ya,
Sheung-lix,
See-ma-ya heue.)
Mawh kheu ceu pui nyiex ma kix nied tix haok huan,
39 mai kix vih tix vieh va plak klip singaix,
tom sang dex hoek ka Ci-la-euxe,
kix liang yung ka ngawd Ci-la-eux plak lih singaix.
40 Kix yaox pak rib pa kied ting vieh mhawm dee an;
pak rib pa vieh an kied sidaing yien yawg.
Sivoe an dix,
ceu pui Hang hoik paung ot ka heue.
41 Ka daux baux bawg yam singaix simiang Yo-tax pa mawh Si-si-cia,
cao kaing ceu pui in kix plak piang hoek bleeh ka Ci-la-eux,
yuh laux ka nyiex gox bang ot pui haktiex an mai ka nyiex aing kixf,
mai grheu kix hu,
son sang ot tix dee an,
mawh kheu dee an hoik koe pak rib son sang liang ka phung yung.
42 Ceu pui Si-miien tang kaux,
kawn koe phuan yieh kaux pa hu plak lih singaix hoek bleeh ka Yi-tong.
Simiang kix mawh kawn simex Yi-seex pon kaux,
pa mawh Pi-la-thi,
Ni-li-ya,
Li-fa-ya,
Wu-shiie.
43 Kix pauh ka pui Ya-ma-lix pa kawn koe ka Yi-tong,
jauh tom yam an,
mai kix paung ot dee an heue.
