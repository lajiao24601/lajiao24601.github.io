Khawng phak buan son Khuan Singax Singeh
12
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
phan mawh son khawng phak buan son Khuan Singax Singeh,
aux simie pex tawng khawm uik.
2 Sivoe an yam yum rhawm pex ka been li tang ceu,
pex hoik lud krax lawng tix,
riang rao pa ang koe ju an kix hoik giex gang ka pex.
3 Aux krai ka pex,
can pui pa hoik laig khuan Siyiex ka daux khankix,
ang doh ah nin:
“Ye-sux mawh pa khaw tix jhax lawg ka heue”.
Awm an,
lheu khaing pa duk thung Khuan Singax Singeh,
ang koe pui pa pon doh keud ah mawh “Ye-sux mawh Siyiex” heue.
4 aKhawng phak buan son hoik koe tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
mawh simiang tix kaux khaing pa tawx.
5 Krax krub hoik koe tom nie ceu,
viang mawh nan,
mawh Khuan Singax Singeh tix kaux khaing pa tawx;
6 kaing pa yuh hoik koe tom nie ceu,
viang mawh nan,
mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing pa tawx riang rao pa yuh kaing ka pui ku kaux.
7 Son sang kieh pui hun pon ceu pa mhawm,
ceu pa jhi Khuan Singax Singeh ka to kaux pui mawx pui an kawx ang awm paox.
8 Khuan Singax Singeh tawx gum lox ping nyia ka pui in tix kaux,
Khuan Singax Singeh tix kaux khaing tawx gum lox ngai lai ka pui an tix kaux.
9 Khuan Singax Singeh tix kaux khaing tawx krax yum rhawm ka pui in tix kaux,
mai tawx riang rao pa pai jhawm saux ka pui an tix kaux.
10 Khuan Singax Singeh tawx riang rao pa yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka pui in,
mai tawx khawng phak buan son pa sidiex gum lox Siyiex ka pui an,
tawx riang rao pa pon guah tix tawng ka khuan ka pui tix kaux kaux,
tawx riang rao pa krai lox ka daux khuan ka pui tang kaux tix kaux kaux.
Baox tawx riang rao pa kah dee bleeh lox ka daux khuan ka pui tix kaux kaux.
11 Viang mawh nan,
in kix khawm uik hoik mawh pa kaoh khaing Khuan Singax Singeh tix kaux khaing;
nawh hawt tix yuh awm alo tix,
mai tawx khawng phak buan son pa ang awm paox tix ka kaux pui mawx kaux pui an heue.
To kaux tix mu khaing koe pak peung tom nie
12 bKhrit tag awm to kaux tix mu khaing,
hoik koe pak peung tom nie;
viang pak peung hoik koe tom nie,
to kaux ca kawn mawh tix mu khaing.
13 Awm an,
ex mawh pui Yo-thaix ang ah,
pui Si-lax ang ah,
mawh siliex cawng ang ah,
pui pa bheeik luan ang ah,
hoik cub com ka Khuan Singax Singeh tix kaux khaing,
hoik lih mawh to kaux tix mu khaing,
khaix an ot ka daux Khuan Singax Singeh in tix kaux khaing heue.
14 To kaux ang koe ngie pak peung tix mu khaing,
mai hoik mawh pak peung tom nie pa kieh lih tix mawh.
15 Phan caong ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh taix,
mawh kheu nan aux ang mawh cie to kaux”,
caong an ang doh ang mawh cie to kaux tix blah kheu in.
16 Phan yhaok ah nin:
“Aux ang mawh ngai,
mawh kheu nan,
aux ang mawh cie to kaux”,
ngai an ang doh ang mawh cie to kaux tix blah kheu in.
17 Phan to kaux mawh ngai khawm uik,
sang nghiet yuh ka mawx lie?
Phan to kaux mawh yhaok khawm uik,
sang ngheut yuh ka mawx lie?
18 Kheu nan,
Siyiex hoik hawt tix yuh awm alo tix,
mai hoik sau tix deuh pak peung an kix pa ang awm paox ka to kaux pui.
19 Phan to kaux mawh ngie pak peung tix mu khaing,
sang chix sawn mawh to kaux yuh ka mawx lie?
20 Tete te lai,
pak peung hoik koe tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
to kaux mawh ngie tix mu khaing heue.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
ngai ang pon krai ah nin ka taix:
“Aux ang lo ka maix heue!”
Kaing kawx ang pon krai ah nin ka caong:
“Aux ang lo ka maix heue!”
22 Deeh tieh tix,
pak peung pa blawn iak riang khaing paox tix ka to kaux khankix,
nie blawn mawh ceu pa ang pon tix cud cha ka ex.
23 Pa koe ka to kaux ex,
pa een ex mawh ceu pa ang hiex ting ceu khankix,
hoik nie mawh ceu pa blawn chix kia ngien ex;
pa een ex mawh ceu pa ang hiex mhawm tix jhak khankix,
nie blawn mawh ceu pa simi ex,
24 pa hoik mhawm khankix,
ex taug ang lai lo tix jawg.
Siyiex hoik yuh tix deuh to kaux ex khankix mawh nin,
mai guah tix tawx pa blawn ting min plak ngai ka pak peung ceu pa ang mhawm khankix,
25 son ang sang kieh to kaux tang guah paox tix,
pak peung pa ang awm paox ra lai chix kia ngien ka paox tix.
26 Phan pak peung tix mu kha rhawm tix,
can pak peung khawm uik,
tag sang rom tix kha rhawm tix tau nang;
phan pak peung tix mu pon min plak ngai,
can pak peung khawm uik,
tag sang rom tix gaux rhawm nang heue.
27 Pex hoik mawh to kaux Khrit,
mai pui ku kaux hoik mawh pak peung tix mu khaing.
28 cKa daux ceu cao,
Siyiex hoik tang kieh pui ku kaux koe ceu pa chix.
Ceu pa hoik ciak nawh tix deuh mawh:
na bat tix mawh kawn cau,
na bat ra mawh cao jhai,
na bat loe mawh sara.
Khaix an mawh pa yuh mhai pa daox pa am,
mai baox mawh pa koe riang rao tix pai ka jhawm saux.
Khaix an kawn koe pa teem coi ka pui tang kaux,
pa pon khre ka kaing jhaw,
pa pon si kah ka lox daux khuan.
29 Kix ang dak mawh kawn cau khawm uik,
ang dak mawh cao jhai khawm uik,
ang dak mawh sara khawm uik.
Ang dak mawh nang pa pon yuh mhai pa daox pa am khawm uik,
30 ang dak pon pai jhawm saux,
ang dak si kah ka lox daux khuan,
ang dak pon kah dee bleeh lox daux khuan.
31 Viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix leue ka khawng phak buan son pa nie ting ceu an heue.
Yam in aux tag sang jhi daux krax pa mhawm an ka pex heue.
