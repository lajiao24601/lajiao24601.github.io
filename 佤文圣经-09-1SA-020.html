Yaw-na-tang teem coi ka Ta-wue
20
Ta-wue to tix hu khaing nyiex Na-yaw haktiex La-max,
mai hoek ka Yaw-na-tang,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Mawh yuh aux ceu pa tix?
Lud aux ka ceu pa tix?
Yuh aux kaing ceu pa ang khaw ceu pa tix ka keeing maix,
nawh lai sang pauh ka aux lie?”
2 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Ang,
maix ang doh khaw tix yum!
Keeing aux yuh kaing ceu pa tix,
mawh kaing ceu pa ting ceu mai pa ang ting ceu ang ah,
nawh tag krai ka aux.
Kaing in nawh ang doh mox ka aux,
mai ang doh mox heue!”
3 Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue baox tiex tu tix ah nin:
“Keeing maix hoik tawng khaw rhawm maix ka aux,
nawh ang doh krai kaing pa sang keud tix yuh ka maix,
mawh kheu lhat nawh maix laux rhawm.
Aux tiex tu ka Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju:
krax yum hoik ot ka plak sivoe ngai aux heue!”
4 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Maix sang kieh aux yuh ceu pa tix,
aux ang doh tieb tie heue.”
5 aTa-wue pawk lox ah nin:
“Pa sax mawh singaix haok khix khraox,
aux caw tix sang ih priex preem tau mai simiang,
phan maix cu kah,
maix kieh aux hu mox tix ka daux kaung,
mai jun tom hoek bo ngaix khaix an.
6 Phan keeing maix hoik yaox grawng ang aux ot mai pheen,
maix krai aux nawh:
aux sang mhaing tix ing ka Paw-li-haung dee ot nyiex ma aux ka maix,
mawh kheu in jiet mawh bawg yam yuh nyiex yix krax tan tix num tix bawg ka dee an heue.
7 Phan nawh ah nin:
‘Chix tix!’
Aux tag yaox tix nyawm ot;
phan nawh song rhawm,
maix tag sang tawng,
nawh hoik but rhawm tix sang pauh ka aux.
8 Viex maix giex tom kaux ka lox sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh maix mai aux sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
Phan aux rug koe dee phit,
cao maix chix tix pauh ka aux;
yuh pa tix ex lai kawn sang kieh keeing maix pauh ka aux lie?”
9 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Paw keud yuh nin!
Phan aux tawng keeing tix sang pauh ka maix tete,
aux ang doh ang krai ka maix lie?”
10 Ta-wue baox chawk ah nin:
“Phan keeing maix pawk lox maix mai song rhawm tix,
mawx sang hoek krai ka aux lie?”
11 Yaw-na-tang pawk ah nin:
“Hoek,
hu ax ka daux tiag ma kaung heue!”
Kiex tom hu tau.
12 Yaw-na-tang ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex mawh cao pa kham sakseb son ax!
Pa sax mai ngaix khaix ka daux bawg yam in,
aux tag sang jiang tix yaox ka krax keud keeing aux.
Phan nawh ang lai koe rhawm pa caox ka maix,
aux tag sang tawx lox ka maix;
13 Phan nawh rug koe rhawm tix sang yuh maix laux,
aux ang lai krai ka maix,
kieh maix lih hu mhawm mhawm,
kieh Simiang Siyiex pauh ka aux.”
Yaw-na-tang baox ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex ot tau mai maix,
awm hoik ot tau mai keeing maix sivoe yuh nan!
14 Phan aux kawn yaox tix im ju,
maix tag caw tix kia ngien aux mhawm mhawm;
phan aux yumc,
15 dmaix tag caw tix kia ngien ka pui nyiex pui ma aux mhawm mhawm nang.
Khaix hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex ka pui pa koeng laux lak pak leeing,
16 kieh lox sijax mukheu pa yuh maix mai aux ca kawn kaux cong.
Phan maix kieh sijax mukheu in laux,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka maix heue.”
17 Yaw-na-tang baox nhie Ta-wue ah lox tiex tu grawng muh ka nawh,
mawh kheu kied muh nawh ka Ta-wue,
awm hoik muh ka to kaux tix yuh nan.
18 Yaw-na-tang ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Pa sax mawh singaix haok khix tix ngaix,
bawg yam sawm rheep,
phan maix ang ot,
pui rien tix tawng kah;
19 ngaix khaix maix ang ot,
pui nie tawng kahe.
Mawh kheu nan,
maix tag caw tix hoek ka dee mox maix tix sivoe an,
mox tix ot ka plak khaix pum simaoxf.
20 Aux tag sang kieh pum simaox an mawh mhai te pa puing tix,
mai puing ka loe taix,
21 khaix an tawx lox ka siliex hu sawg ka te.
Phan aux ah nin ka siliex:
‘Te ot ka plak khaix maix,
hu sawg heue!’
An hoik mawh maix ang lai yuh ka mawx tix,
maix tag chix tix lih.
Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
maix ang doh yaox tix hoek krax laux krax leeing.
22 Phan aux krai ka siliex ah nin:
‘Te ot sivoe maix’,
maix caw tix to heue!
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tag sang kieh maix to.
23 Phan mawh sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh ax,
Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh cao pa kham mhai sakse ka daux si nax maix mai aux leeing ju heue.”
24 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom hu mox tix ka daux kaung.
Yam haok khix tix ngaix singaix an,
simiang Sao-law hoek tix sawm,
25 nawh ngawm dex ding dee yie ngawm nawh jheeng jheeng.
Ya-ni-eu ngawm praok nawh,
Yaw-na-tang ngawm sivoe nawhg,
mai dee ngawm Ta-wue hoik yi.
26 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux singaix an Sao-law ang ah tid tix,
mawh kheu nawh keud Ta-wue hoik si pup ka kaing ceu pa ang kieh nawh singax singeh.
27 Ka daux ngaix khaix,
pa mawh singaix haok khix ra ngaix,
dee ngawm Ta-wue ca kawn yi;
Sao-law tom chawk ka Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx Ta-wue lai ang hoek sawm daux ngaix mai kawx lie?”
28 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Nawh hoik mhaing tix laux lee tix ka aux,
nhie aux kieh tix deeh ing ka Paw-li-haung.
29 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
‘Kieh aux ing liak,
mawh kheu nyiex yix sang yuh krax tan ka daux veng;
mai pux ke aux yuh ka mawx nhie aux ing.
Phan maix mawh paox grawm aux,
kieh aux ing kia ka pui nyiex tix heue!’
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom ang yaox tix hoek ih ka priex in heue.”
30 Sao-law song rhawm ka Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Maix pui brai in!
Yam in aux tawng hoik ot maix plak Ta-wue,
hoik tik pak ngai tix mai pak ngai miex keeing tix!
31 Khex ang maix baox tawng,
Ta-wue im ju tix ngaix,
maix ang pon tix keud grawng sang yaox tix mawh simiang meung lie?
Maix hu sawg ka nawh,
mai viex nawh hoek.
Nawh ang yum ang chix heue!”
32 Yaw-na-tang ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx nawh khaw tix yum lie?
Mawh hoik yuh nawh krax lud ceu pa tix lie?”
33 Tix vud khaing,
Sao-law yaog ka pliah tix,
mai puah hu ka Yaw-na-tang,
sang pauh ka nawh.
Yaw-na-tang tom taug tawng grawng keeing tix sang pauh ka Ta-wue tete.
34 Yaw-na-tang song rhawm yaog tix cung khaing dee sawm,
ang ih priex leeing lang singaix,
singaix an mawh ngaix haok khix ra singaix.
Yaw-na-tang laux rhawm son Ta-wue tete,
kheu hoik yuh Sao-law kaing pa ang khaw ka nawh.
35 Singaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Yaw-na-tang hawt bawg yam nari pa hoik tat tix deuh awm sivoe an,
mai hu ka Ta-wue daux kaung.
Nawh viex ka kawn nyawm iak tix kaux,
36 ah nin ka nawh:
“Maix to hu sawg ka te pa puing aux.”
Kawn nyawm iak an tom to hu.
Yaw-na-tang tom puing ka te tix taix luan khaing nawh.
37 Yam sang hoek kawn nyawm iak an ka dee cawd te,
Yaw-na-tang ah nin ka nawh:
38 “Te kawn ot sivoe maix!
Paw vi cung tan,
nied to!”
Kawn nyawm iak an sut ka te,
mai tawx ka cao simiang tix,
39 ang tawng dee bleeh lox an kix pie ceeix kawx,
mawh ngie Yaw-na-tang mai Ta-wue pa tawng khaing.
40 Yaw-na-tang tom tawx ak puh ka kawn nyawm iak an,
mai nhie nawh viex ing ka veng sivoe heue.
41 Khaix hoik hu kawn nyawm iak an,
Ta-wue lih khaing plak khaix pum simaoxh an,
grux ngawng tix piang tiex,
mai krub nawh loe bawg.
Nawh ngheet ka Yaw-na-tang khawm yiiem tix,
Ta-wue nie laux rhawm khaing Yaw-na-tang.
42 Yaw-na-tang ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Hu mhawm mhawm heue!
Simiang Siyiex tag chix kieh maix mai aux mai doeh ceu cad maix mai aux,
priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh ax tau leeing ju heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom yaog tix hu;
Yaw-na-tang kawx deeh ing ka veng heue.
