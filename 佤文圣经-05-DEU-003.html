Pui Yi-seu-liex pon kuang ka simiang Pa-sang
(Lai Simin-pui 21.31–35)
3
“Khaix an,
ex baox deeh hu ka plak laong,
ka haktiex Pa-sang.
Eux simiang Pa-sang thung pui naing cie tix khawm uik,
kuan rieb tix yuh naing dex ka veng Yi-teu-lai.
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
‘Pex paw lhat.
Aux sang ap nawh mai pui cie nawh,
mai haktiex nawh ka daux tawm taix pex.
Sivoe an pex yuh yuh ka mawx ka Si-hong simiang pui Ya-maw-lix pa ot ka daux Si-seu-peung,
yam in kawx yuh nang nan ka simiang Pa-sang heue.’
3 “Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex hoik ap tix tawx Eux simiang Pa-sang,
mai pui cie nawh khawm uik ka daux tawm taix ex.
Ex hoik pauh kix,
mai ang lai deuk tix kaux kawx.
4 Yam an,
ex pon cax veng nawh khawm uik,
mai veng pa ang ex cax ang koe tix dauh kawx.
Ex kawx hoik pon teei ka veng pa kuang Eux simiang Pa-sang,
ka daux haktiex Ya-eu-keu-paw ku dauh,
khawm uik mawh veng tix gliah mu;
5 veng in kix mawh veng pa kaux pa cong pa lhaong guh,
pa koe siviex,
mai pa koe jhong.
Ex kawn baox pon cax ka yaong pa ang koe guh veng khankix tix blah.
6 Ex hoik yuh veng kix laux lak pak leeing,
pui pa ot ka daux veng,
simex bun ang ah,
kuad ang ah,
kawn nyawm ang ah,
hoik pauh yum khawm uik,
awm hoik yuh yix ka Si-hong simiang Si-seu-peung,
mai veng ku veng laux leeing yuh nan sivoe.
7 Ex tud ka khawng brie kix hu,
mai yieh ka veng ot kix heue.
8 “Yam an,
ex hoik pon teei ka haktiex rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
khaing daux tawm taix simiang pui Ya-maw-lix ra kaux,
jauh ka plak seh daux ngawd Ya-noeng tom hoek ka plak laong gawng He-meung.
9 Pui Si-toeng kok ceu kaux gawng He-meung mawh Si-liien;
mai ceu pui Ya-maw-lix kok mawh See-ni-eux.
10 Ex hoik pon cax ka veng Eux simiang Pa-sang pa koe khawm uik,
khawm can veng pa koe ka daux lung ku dauh ku dee,
mai haktiex Ci-liex,
mai haktiex Pa-sang khawm uik,
plak lih singaix hoek ka veng Sa-cia mai veng Yi-teu-lai heue.”
11 Simiang Eux mawh ceu pui Li-fa-ying tix kaux ngie.
Lawnga cie nawh mawh pa yuh ka simaoxb,
hawt ka khaox diag pa yie sax siao pui,
plak lang koe pon kongjhee,
plak vieh koe ra kongjhee.
Pui Ya-meung pa ot ka veng La-pa,
kawn pon yaox tix jhak ka lawng an heue.
Cub ceu pui ku cub pa ot ka hawt rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix
(Lai Simin-pui 32.1–42)
12 “Khaix hoik pon cax ex ka haktiex an,
aux guah tix tawx haktiex pa koe plak laong veng Ya-law-eu ka dex ka ngawd Ya-noeng,
mai ka haktiex gawng Ci-liex tix blah,
khawm haktiex veng an khankix ka cub ceu pui Lui-piien mai ceu pui Cia-teux.
13 Aux dee baox guah tix tawx haktiex Ci-liex pa deuk khankix,
mai haktiex Eux simiang Pa-sang khawm uik ka Ma-na-si tix plak cub ceu pui heue.”
(Haktiex Pa-sang baox kok mawh haktiex Li-fa-ying.
14 Ya-eux cub ceu pui Ma-na-si tix plak pon cax tix cie haktiex meung Ya-eu-keu-paw khawm uik,
pa mawh haktiex Pa-sang,
jauh dee an vih tix tom hoek dee kleuk Ci-sux mai Ma-cia ka paox tix.
Ya-eux kok veng an kix hawt ceu kaux tix;
hoek tom in singaix,
veng in kix ca kawn kok mawh ceu kaux Ya-eux heue.)
15 “Aux guah tix tawx veng Ci-liex ka ceu pui Ma-na-si,
pa mawh cub nyiex pui Ma-ciex,
16 mai guah tix tawx haktiex Ci-liex,
hoek haktiex ngawd Ya-noeng tix dauh ka ceu pui Lui-piien mai ceu pui Cia-teux khankix.
Ka mee plak seh kix,
ot ka si nax ngawd Ya-meung,
mai mee plak laong mawh rawm klawng Ya-paw,
mee kix tix blah kleuk tix tau mai mee haktiex pui Ya-meung.
17 Mee haktiex kix ka plak klip singaix,
nih tix tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
jauh ka plak laong rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix,
hoek ka plak seh Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-kaog,
ka plak lih singaix hoek ka caong gawng Pi-see-cia heue.
18 c“Yam an,
aux tawx lox in plak greem ka kix ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ex,
tawx haktiex hawt rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix ka pex,
mai kieh pex pon ka haktiex an.
Yam in,
pex tag caw tix kieh pui naing rien khre tix,
mai tawx kix mawh pui naing pa hu sivoe ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
teem ka ceu pui ku ceu pon cax ka haktiex pa tawx Siyiex ka kix.
19 Mawh ngie moeng pex,
kawn simex kawn bun pex,
mai khawng brie pex khankix (aux tawng ka hoik nie khawng brie pex) chix tix deuh ot ka daux veng pa hoik tawx aux ka pex.
20 Teem coi ka paox ceu paox cad pex pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai joh jhan kix cax ka haktiex rawm klawng Yaw-tang daog plak klip singaix,
pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka kix,
tom hoik kieh Siyiex kix nyawm ot dee an,
awm hoik kieh nawh pex ot dee an yuh nan.
Yam an,
pex tom yaox tix deeh hoek ka haktiex pa hoik guah aux tix tawx ka pex heue.’
21 “Khaix an,
aux hoik tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax ah nin:
‘Ngai maix hoik yaox ka kaing pa hoik yuh Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex khawm uik ka simiang Si-hong mai Eux ra kaux;
nawh kawx tag sang yuh nang nan ka simiang tang meung,
pa tag sang pon cax pex kah.
22 Pex paw lhat graung ka kix,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pex,
tag sang yuh naing son pex heue.’ ”
Maw-si ang pon tix laig ka Cia-nang
23 d“Yam an,
aux khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
24 ‘Simiang Siyiex pa ting ka piang khawm uik eue,
aux tawng grawng pa hoik jhi maix tix ka aux,
hoik mawh ngie pa ting pa lhaong maix khaing,
mai kaing pa daox deng pa jauh maix tix yuh lih.
Ka piang haktiex ang koe siyiex tang kaux,
pa pon yuh ka kaing ting pa yuh maix!
25 Simiang Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh aux tiang rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai hu jhak ka haktiex mhawm plak an,
gawng pa jiet mhawm,
mai gawng Li-pa-noeng heue.’
26 “Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu jao pex,
Simiang Siyiex hoik song rhawm ka aux,
mai ang lai nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw aux.
Nawh ah nin ka aux:
‘Ding heue,
ding heue,
paw lai krai grawng kaing in!
27 Maix haok ka piang choek gawng Pi-see-cia,
mai jhak ka plak lih,
plak klip,
plak laong,
ka plak seh khawm pon plak ka dee an,
maix jawg bre tix jhak mhawm mhawm,
mawh kheu ang maix pon yaox tix tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
28 Maix tawx lox ka Yaw-su-yax,
mai kieh nawh kaux rhawm,
mai kieh nawh koe riang,
mawh kheu nawh tag thung pui haoh hun khankix tiang ka rawm klawng,
mai pon tix cax ka haktiex pa hoik yaox maix heue.’
29 “Mawh kheu nan,
ex hoik ot ka daux ngawd gawng si tieb veng Paw Pi-eux heue.”
