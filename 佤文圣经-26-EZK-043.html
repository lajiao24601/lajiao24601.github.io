Simiang Siyiex deeh hoek ka nyiex buan
43
Pui an viex aux hu ka siviex pa veeh tix ka plak lih singaix.
2 aAux yaox ka min plak ngai Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa hoek khaing plak lih singaix.
Lox nawh puk pak awm rawm sigrit sigra;
haktiex jhan riang kheu min plak ngai nawh.
3 Riang su sai in hoik awm riang su sai pa hoik yaox aux pa yuh Siyiex ka Yie-lu-sa-leung laux leeing,
mai awm riang su sai pa hoik yaox aux ka praok rawm klawng Cia-pa-lu yuh nan.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom kup tix ka piang tiex.
4 Min plak ngai Siyiex laig daux nyiex buan ka siviex plak lih singaix heue.
5 Khuan Simiang Siyiex yaog ka aux,
mai thung aux laig ka plak daux nyiex buan.
Ka dee an aux yaox ka nyiex buan hoik naog ka min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex.
6 Pui an cung ka praok aux;
aux mhong lox pa si kah Simiang Siyiex ka aux khaing daux nyiex buan.
7 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
dee in mawh dang ngeung aux;
aux tag sang paung ot ka si nax pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai kuang kia kix leeing ju.
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai simiang kix tag ang lai baox deeh krub ka riang rao pa krub pui,
phan ang mawh nan ang lai baox deeh sum mhai son simiang pa hoik yum,
mai map nai ka ceu kaux pa singax aux.
8 Ka diang siviex nyiex haw simiang mai ka rong siviex pa dex ka sivoe ngai nyiex buan,
ka si nax ex khan ngie paox tix tix dieb guh ding khaing.
Kix hoik sax map nai ka aux son kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix,
mawh kheu nan aux tom yuh kix laux leeing ka daux krax pa song rhawm song rhi tix.
9 Yam in kix tag caw tix naox krub tix riang rao,
yieh laux ka mhai pa hoik sum kix son simiang pa koe sivoe an,
yuh nin,
aux tom taug chix paung ot ka si nax kix leeing ju heue.
10 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix krai grawng pa sum nyiex buan ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
kieh kix si kaw tix yaox.
Tag caw tix kieh tawng tix kaig tua son phi map tix.
11 Phan kix tawng kaing pa hoik yuh tix mawh pa kaig pa tua,
maix tom taug chix krai grawng pa sum tix nyiex buan in ka kix sidawng mawh nin:
khawm pak peung nyiex buan,
ka diang siviex dee laig lih,
pak peung plak praix,
pa cox khre ka plak daux,
mai can lox tat jhawm prawng khawm uik.
Maix tag caw tix tiiem in kix lih khawm uik,
kieh kix tawng grawng sang cox khre kah,
khaix an hawt tix yuh awm lox tat jhawm prawng.
12 Been nyiex buan pa hoik tat tix deuh mawh nin:
nyiex buan tag caw tix sum ot ka piang gawng,
vung viang nyiex buan hoik mawh haktiex pa singax khawm uik heue.”
Khu dee tan
13 bKhu dee tan been ting iak tiiem ot plak greem mawh nin:
khaox diag pa tiag ka khu dee tan hoik awm pa tiag ka nyiex buan yuh nan.
Dang ngeung khu dee tan khawm pon daog koe ka daux dawng,
krax raux koe tix phuan kongfeeng,
krax vieh koe tix phuan kongfeeng,
ka daog plak praix krax lhaong koe tix nga phuan kongfeeng.
14 Jan khu dee tan plak greem jauh tix sawn ka daux dawng plak greem koe plak lhaong tix kongjhee;
jan daux greung jauh ka praok daux jan greem laig plak daux tix phuan kongfeeng,
plak lhaong koe ra kongfeeng.
Jan plak piang kawx jauh ka daog plak praix ka jan sigreung,
laig plak daux tix phuan kongfeeng nang,
15 Jan plak piang,
mawh glawk pa tan lu ka Krax-tan Khawng-brie,
krax lhaong koe ra kongjhee nang,
rung pa koe ka praok khawm pon daog hoik tang bhlien tix lih ku rung.
16 Jan khu dee tan plak piang mawh pon pak,
ka praok lang koe liah kongjhee.
17 Jan sigreung kawx mawh pon pak,
ka praok lang koe aliah kongjhee,
ka daog plak praix koe praok,
krax lhaong koe tix nga phuan kongfeeng.
Krax vieh dawng koe tix phuan kongfeeng.
Glawk bawng pa haok ka khu dee tan koe ka plak lih singaix heue.
Tan lu khu dee tan
18 Simiang Siyiex krai ka aux ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
tag caw bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet heue!
Plak greem mawh been jhawm prawng pa dex mai krax tan krax lu.
Khaix hoik yuh khu dee tan,
maix tag caw tix tan ka Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
phruah nham khawng brie kah,
mai tan ka aux.
19 Mawh ngie ceu pui Li-we,
pa mawh cao giex kaing li doeh ceu cad Sa-tu pa chix tix pon hoek tan ka plak sivoe ngai aux.
In hoik mawh gum lox pa tawx aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong.
Maix tag caw tix tawx kawn moi mhaing tix mu ka kix,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map.
20 Maix tag caw tix kraix nham khawng brie in ka jan plak piang ka rung pa bhlien tix lih an khawm pon rung,
ka jan sigreung ka rung khawm pon rung,
khawm ka daog an plak praix.
Maix tag caw tix si ghrah singax ka khu dee tan,
mai guah lih mawh pa singax.
21 Maix tag caw tix viex kawn moi mhaing Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map hoek plak praix nyiex buan,
mai gleeing ka dee pa hoik ciak ci tix gleeing.
22 “Ka daux singaix khaix,
maix tag caw tix teei pex mhaing pa mhawm loig kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map.
Maix tag caw tix teei nham yung si ghrah singax ka khu dee tan,
awm hoik teei nham kawn moi mhaing si ghrah singax ka khu dee tan yuh nan.
23 Khaix hoik singax khu dee tan,
maix tag caw tix viex kawn moi mhaing mai yung mhaing pa mhawm loig
24 hoek ka plak sivoe ngai aux.
Cao giex kaing li tag sang phruah kih ka piang to kaux khawng brie an kix,
mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing tan ka aux.
25 Ka daux aliah singaix,
maix tag caw tix tan kawn moi mhaing,
pex mhaing,
yung mhaing tix ngaix tix mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phi-map.
Khawng brie in kix tag caw tix mawh pa mhawm loig khankix.
26 Ka daux aliah singaix,
cao giex kaing li tag caw tix si ghrah singax ka khu dee tan,
mai guah lih mawh pa singax singeh.
27 c“Ka daux daix singaix,
cao giex kaing li tag caw tix yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka piang khu dee tan son pui hun khankix.
Yuh nin,
aux tom taug rieb ka pex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
