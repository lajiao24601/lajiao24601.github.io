7
Yam in guh veng hoik cox sum tix mhawm,
mai siviex veng kawx hoik sau tix;
pui pau siviex nyiex buan,
cub caw pa rhax lai,
mai kaing yuh pui Li-we khankix hoik ciak ci sidawng mhawm mhawm.
2 Mawh kheu nan,
aux ploi pui ra kaux pa kuang kia ka piang kaing son veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
kix hoik mawh pux aik aux Ha-na-ni mai Ha-na-ni-ya simiang ting veng.
Ha-na-ni-ya hoik mawh pui pa kaux sijax pa yum rhawm lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex tix kaux,
mai ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa lheu mhawm khaing nawh.
3 Aux tawx lox ka kix,
sivoe sang riang praix pon ngawb cao cao,
ang pon tix paoh siviex veng Yie-lu-sa-leung;
pon bo yam hoik laig singaix daux gawng,
sivoe sang lih hu cao pau siviex khankix,
caw tix nyeep siviex sau jhong.
Aux kawx tawx lox nhie kix ciak ci pui pa pau siviex lih khaing daux pui haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung,
tix blah kieh pau ka dee pa hoik ciak ci kix,
pui pa kawn deuk khankix,
ot tix lai ling ka siviex veng nyiex ma tix heue.
Ca rang pui pa deeh hoek khankix
(Lai Ma-la-ci 2.1–70)
4 Veng Yie-lu-sa-leung kied ting kied vieh,
viang mawh nan,
pui pa ot daux veng ang hun,
nyiex ma pa hoik sum tix mhawm kawx ang nie.
5 Siyiex paoh rhawm rhi aux khrawm ka pui hun,
mai simiang kix,
mai simiang naing khankix,
si kaw tix yaox ka ru cub nyiex kix.
Aux hoik yaox ka lai tiiem pui pa deeh hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix ka daux bawg tix.
In plak greem mawh lai pa yaox aux ka grawng kix:
6 Pui pa deeh hoek khaing vuk vied vuk viang tix lih hu khaing haktiex Pa-pi-loeng,
deeh hoek ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai haktiex Yo-tax,
kix tang deeh ing ka haktiex tix.
Cub caw pui in kix mai pui nyiex pui ma kix,
jauh tom yam giex simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha kix hu,
tom vuk vied vuk viang hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng.
