Paw vi yuh paox grawm tau mai haktiex
4
Krax pa simah simaing ka daux pex mawh pa kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?
Mawh pa kaoh khaing alo nex nham krax naing pa koe ka daux pex heue!
2 Pex tag sang giex sang teei,
viang mawh nan,
phan ang pon tag sang pauh pui yum;
pex tag sang ghoing rhawm ghoing rhi,
viang mawh nan,
phan ang pon tag sang lauh sang tawk pui.
Pex ang pon ka ceu pa sang lo tix,
hoik mawh kheu ang pex bax khiox khaw ka Siyiex.
3 Pex ca kawn ang bax pon ka ceu pa khiox khaw tix,
mai hoik mawh kheu ceu pa khiox khaw pex ang khaw;
ceu pa khiox khaw pex hoik mawh ngie krax gaux rhawm son to kaux tix khaing heue!
4 Pex pa mawh cub pui pa ang kaux sijax eue,
khex ang pex tawng pa yuh paox pie tau mai haktiex tag mawh pa tieb tie ka Siyiex lie?
Pui mawx yuh paox grawm tau mai haktiex,
pui an tag mawh pui pa tieb tie ka Siyiex heue!
5 Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Khuan pa deuh mai Siyiex ot ka daux ex kied mawh pa ting ka daux alo nex nhama.”
Pex ce gum lox in ang koe dee bleeh lie?
6 bGrawh grawng nawh,
buan son pa tawx Siyiex nie mawh pa blawn kub,
awm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Siyiex tieb tie ka pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai tawx buan son ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi heue.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka Siyiex;
tag caw tix tieb tie ka phi caox,
phi caox tom taug yaog tix to.
8 Pex dex mai Siyiex,
Siyiex tag dex mai pex.
Pui pa koe phi map eue,
pex tag caw tix kieh taix tix singax heue!
Pui pa pie lhien eue,
pex tag caw tix kieh rhawm rhi tix singax heue!
9 Pex tag caw tix laux rhawm,
khi rhi,
yiiem grai;
tag caw tix kieh krax pa nyiah tieh tix lih mawh krax pa yiiem,
krax gaux rhawm tieh tix lih mawh krax pa laux rhawm.
10 Tag caw tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
nawh tag sang yaog pex lhaong heue.
Paw vi prawng ca ka pui
11 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
paw vi ra lai si briem ka paox tix.
Pui pa si briem mai pa prawng ca ka pux aik pux o tix,
tag mawh pa si briem mai pa prawng ca ka lox tat jhawm prawng Maw-si.
Phan maix prawng ca ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
maix tag mawh pui pa ang nghiet ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
pa prawng ca ka lox tat jhawm prawng.
12 Mawh ngie Siyiex tix kaux khaing pa mawh cao pa yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng,
pa mawh cao pa prawng ca;
mawh ngie nawh tix kaux khaing pa pon taoh,
pa pon yuh laux leeing.
Mawh maix pui mawx,
lai pon prawng ca ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix heue!
Paw vi tang deeh yaw rang di ka to kaux tix
13 Koe pui ah nin:
“Daux in singaix ang ah,
pa sax ang ah,
ex tag sang hu ka veng tix mu mu,
mai paung ot dee an tix num,
yuh lai gee pon mau tom nie.”
Viang mawh nan,
nghiet ka lox krai aux,
14 pie pex pa kawn im ju pa sax khankix ang koe pui pa ang pon tawng ka heue!
Pex awm ngie taox si mawg yuh nan khaing,
lih koe tix vud tix cu khaing,
khaix an tag ang lai yaox.
15 Pex tag caw tix krai ah nin:
“Phan Simiang Siyiex cu,
ex tag yaox tix pon krax im ju,
mai yuh kaing ceu in kaing ceu an heue.”
16 Viang mawh nan,
pex lai pon yuh tix ting rhawm lhaong rhi;
pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi yuh nin mawh pa caox pa caig khawm uik heue.
17 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa tawng pa caw tix sang yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang yuh tag mawh phi map heue.
