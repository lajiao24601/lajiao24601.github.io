Kieh Khrit mawh pa gau ka ceu pa mhawm
2
Ju Khrit ka daux pex hoik kieh ang kieh pex pon krax pa kaux cong lie?
Krax muh nawh ang hoik joh jhan ka pex lie?
Pex mai Khuan Singax Singeh ang hoik yuh kaing tau lie?
Ka daux pex ang hoik ra lai koe krax pa caox rhawm ka paox tix lie?
2 Phan koe,
aux khiox khaw ka pex,
tag caw tix grum rhawm tau ka daux kaing tix ceu ceu,
krax muh krax rhawm pa awm paox tix,
krax rhawm rhi pa khaw paox tix,
mai krax keud krax sawn pa khaw tau,
son sang kieh aux naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm.
3 Paw vi tang keud son to kaux tix nong nong,
paw vi ghoing ka min ceu kaux pa giex going,
tag caw tix ra lai va rhawm ka paox tix,
mai een pui tang kaux kawn lheu lhak khaing tix.
4 Paw vi jhak ngie ka to kaux tix khaing,
ka pui tang kaux kawx tag caw tix jhak kah,
son sang koe buan son.
5 Pex tag caw tix kieh rhawm pa koe ka daux Ye-sux Khrit koe ka daux tix yuh nin:
6 nawh hoik mawh pa dawg koe awm to kaux Siyiex,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang bax nieb siaoa ka siawp sibrawm pa sidu mai Siyiex.
7 Deeh tieh tix,
nawh cu tix ploi ka ceu pa koe tix khawm uik,
mai teei tix awm to kaux siliex.
Nawh lih mawh kawn pui,
mai teei tix lih mawh awm pak peung kawn pui.
8 Nawh kieh to kaux tix tiiem,
mai cu nghiet tom yum tix,
khaix an yum ka piang khaox lhak.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex yaog nawh lhaong,
tom hoek ka dee lhaong,
tawx nawh ting min ceu kaux pa ting lhaong khaing ku ceu khawm uik.
10 bSon sang nab yam yung yi ka ceu kaux Ye-sux,
can ceu pa im ju ka piang rao ma,
si nax kawn pui,
mai ka greem haktiex
tag sang krub ka nawh khawm uik,
11 jheeh khawm uik tag sang krai doh ah nin:
Ye-sux Khrit mawh simiang,
mai rhax lai yaog yaw min plak ngai keeing Siyiex heue.
Pa riang haktiex
12 Phan mawh nan,
paox grawm pa muh khankix eue,
yam ot aux tau mai pex,
pex hoik nghiet ka aux jheeng jheeng;
yam in aux ang ot mai pex dee an,
pex nie caw tix nghiet ka aux.
Tag caw tix lhat graung,
mai sau riang tix yuh hoik ka krax taoh cie to kaux tix ang jud;
13 mawh kheu hoik yuh Siyiex kaing jhaw ka daux rhawm rhi pex jheeng jheeng,
mai kieh pex cu mai baox hawt tix yuh ka alo pa mhawm nawh heue.
14 Pex yuh kaing ceu pa tix ang ah,
tag caw tix paw vi kieh mawh pa salao mai pa thung thiang,
15 cson sang pon tix singax singeh,
mai ang koe krax pa caox ka daux singaix baux ju in pa laux leeing,
pa kawk pa vawk,
mawh kawn praix Siyiex pa ang koe dee cud cha.
Pex tag caw kieh tix jhan riang ka daux kawn pui,
awm simuing pa jhan riang ka rao ma yuh nan,
16 giex tom kaux cong ka gum lox pa im jud.
Phan pex yuh nin,
ka daux singaix yam lih hoek Khrit,
aux tag sang koe krax pa yaw rang di,
mawh kheu in hoik chix tix kham sakse ka daux pa taug mao aux khawm uik,
ang mawh pa giex going heue.
17 Viang aux hoik teei nham to kaux tix kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-jauh,
mai jauh ka piang khawng tan pa teei pex krax yum rhawm tix tan lu ka Siyiex,
aux kawx hoik gaux rhawm kah,
kheu nan,
aux tag sang rom tix gaux rhawm tau mai pex,
ka daux krax gaux rhawm tix.
18 Awm an,
pex tag sang gaux rhawm,
mai kieh pex rom tix gaux rhawm tau mai aux,
ka daux krax pa gaux rhawm pex nang heue.
Thi-maw-thaix mai Yi-pa-fu-thi
19 Aux deuh rhawm tix ka Simiang Ye-sux,
mai simie ang rheung tag ploi Thi-maw-thaix hu ka pex dee an,
son sang pon kieh aux tix mhong ka grawh grawng pex mai pon krax pa nyawm rhawm.
20 Nawh hoik mawh pui pa khaw rhawm aux tix kaux khaing,
mai hoik chix kia ngien ka pex tete.
21 Pui tang kaux sawg ngie son to kaux tix nong nong,
mai ang chix kia ngien ka kaing cie Ye-sux Khrit.
22 Viang mawh nan phan mawh Thi-maw-thaix,
pex hoik tawng sidawng,
nawh mai aux hoik awm keeing kawn yuh nan,
mai hoik taug mao son pa yuh kaing gum lox buan.
23 Aux simie,
yam mhong aux ka grawh grawng pex mawh yuh ka mawx dee si dauh,
aux tag sang ploi nawh hu jhak pex dum mawx dum an.
24 Aux kawx hoik yum rhawm ka grawng sang kieh Simiang Siyiex aux hoek tix jhak pex,
ka daux bawg yam pa ang lai rheeng heue.
25 Aux keud,
aux khaw tix tawx pux aik tix Yi-pa-fu-thi deeh hoek ka pex dee an.
Pex laug tix tawx nawh hoek teem coi ka aux,
yuh kaing tau mai aux,
yuh naing tau mai aux.
26 Nawh kied braong ka pex ku kaux,
khaix an mai hoik tawng mhong pex grawng pa saux nawh,
mai nawh kied laux rhawm kah.
27 Nawh rub hoik saux tete,
mai dex sang yum.
Viang mawh nan Siyiex caox rhawm ka nawh,
ang joeng caox rhawm ka nawh,
ka aux kawx hoik caox rhawm kah,
mai ang kieh aux si pup ka krax khi rhawm pa blawn ting khankix.
28 Mawh kheu nan,
aux nie simie tix sang hu tawx nawh ing,
son sang kieh pex koe krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi tix ot tau mai nawh;
in tee chix kieh aux grai krax pa khi rhawm.
29 Aux simie pex gaux rhawm tix rien khre nawh ka daux Simiang Siyiex.
Pex tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka pui pa awm nawh yuh nin;
30 mawh kheu nawh hoik tawx ju tix kham krax pa nu son kaing cie Khrit,
mai dex sang yum,
son sang cox naog ka kaing ceu pa ang pex bax yuh ka aux heue.
