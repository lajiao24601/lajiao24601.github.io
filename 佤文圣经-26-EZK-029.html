Ne raw ka Ai-cix
29
aYam vuk vied vuk viang yix ka daux kao num khix kao kao ra singaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
2 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka simiang Ai-cix;
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka nawh,
mai ka Ai-cix.
3 Tag caw tix krai ka nawh,
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
simiang Ai-cix eue,
aux tag sang tieb tie ka maix heue!
Maix awm goi rawm ting pa mox tix ot ka daux rawm klawng.
Maix ah mawh rawm klawng Ni-law cie tix,
mai ah mawh pa phan pa yuh tix.
4 Aux tag sang teei goik vhak kap maix kah,
mai kieh kax pa koe daux rawm klawng khankix kawb ka khlawp si ngawb maix,
khaix an tag sang bluh maix lih khaing daux rawm klawng Ni-law,
mai bluh lih khawm kax pa kawb ka maix khankix.
5 Aux tag sang tik maix mai kax ka daux tiag daux tu.
To kaux yum maix tag sang naog ka daux tiag daux tu,
mai ang koe pui pa khrawm tix si paung.
Aux tag sang teei to kaux yum maix tawx sim mai to sat ih.
6 bYuh nin,
can pui Ai-cix khawm uik tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Ai-cix eue,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik een rhawm tix ka krax pa joh jhan pex;
tete te lai,
pex mawh ngie khaox gix pa awn yuh nan.
7 Yam gix pui ka pex,
pex tom pot heue,
khaix an klauh ka daux klaik kix mat,
mai kieh kix ku khlaik blawg tix.
8 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
aux tag sang kieh pex si pup krax pa laux leeing ka naing,
kawn pui mai khawng brie hoik pauh yum khawm uik.
9 Haktiex Ai-cix tag sang riem.
Yuh nin,
pex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.
“Mawh kheu nan,
maix ah mawh rawm klawng Ni-law cie tix,
mawh pa phan pa yuh maix,
10 aux sang tieb tie ka maix mai rawm klawng Ni-law maix.
Aux tag sang kieh haktiex Ai-cix,
jauh ka veng Mi-tawx pa koe plak laong,
hoek ka veng Seu-fu-ni pa koe plak seh,
tom hoek ka mee haktiex Ku-seu hoik riem khawm uik,
11 ang koe pui,
mai ang koe nang khaix caong to tiag.
Ka daux tipon num,
haktiex Ai-cix ang koe pui pa paung ot.
12 Aux tag sang kieh Ai-cix lih mawh dee blawn riem ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
ka daux tipon num,
veng Ai-cix tag sang nie nhik khaing veng pa koe khawm uik.
Aux tag sang kieh pui Ai-cix lih mawh pui pa si pup krax laux,
mai to tix hu ka meung ku meung,
mai paung ot ka si nax pui meung tang meung khankix heue.”
13 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Khaix hoik luan tipon num,
aux tag sang deeh khrawm pui Ai-cix hoek khaing meung pa ploi aux kix ot ka ku meung,
14 mai thung kix deeh hoek ka haktiex kix pa koe ka plak seh Ai-cix.
Ka dee an,
mai kix tag sang khrawm tix lih mawh meung iak tix mu,
15 pa mawh meung pa blawn iak riang ka piang haktiex khawm uik;
kix ang lai pon baox giex kaing ka piang meung tang meung.
Aux tag sang kieh kix lih mawh meung iak pa ang koe min pex ceeix kawx,
ang lai nieb pon giex kaing ka piang meung tang meung.
16 Pui Yi-seu-liex ang doh lai een rhawm tix ka kix.
Grawng pa laux leeing Ai-cix kieh pui Yi-seu-liex doh keud ka pa een rhawm tix ka pui hoik mawh kaing pa ting lud.
Yuh nin,
pui Yi-seu-liex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong heue.”
Simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha sang pon kuang ka Ai-cix
17 Yam vuk vied vuk viang ex ka daux tix nga aliah num haok khix tix ngaix,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
18 Nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha puing ka Thai-eux.
Nawh tawx khaox glawm pa cien ka piang pui naing,
tom hoik kieh hauk kaing kix ghle,
klip kix gleed,
viang mawh nan,
simiang mai pui naing ang bax pon ka ngawh riang.
19 Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
aux tag sang ap tix tawx haktiex Ai-cix ka simiang Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha.
Nawh tag sang tud rhim ka Ai-cix,
mai teei ka sin ngao kix;
kraung in kix pa pon khaing naing tag sang kieh mawh ngawh riang nawh.
20 Aux ap tix tawx Ai-cix ka nawh,
mai kieh mawh mau riang nawh,
mawh kheu pui naing cie nawh yuh kaing son aux.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
21 “Yam an,
aux tag sang kieh pui Yi-seu-liex ting riang;
aux tag sang kieh maix pa mawh Yi-si-ciex krai lox ka dee chix mhong pui ka ku kaux.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
