Ye-sux mai bun Sa-ma-li-yax
4
Pui Fa-li-saix mhong krai pui sawg Ye-sux tix khrawm ka pui gau,
mai krax pa cub com nawh pui ka rawm kawn nie khaing Yaw-hang. (
2 Tete te lai,
Ye-sux ang sax tawx pui tix kaux kawx cub com,
mai hoik mawh pui gau nawh pa cub com.)
3 Ye-sux tawng ka kaing in,
nawh tom lih hu khaing pui Yo-thaix,
mai deeh ing ka meung Cia-li-lix;
4 nawh tag caw tix sang laig ka veng Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
5 aNawh hoek ka yaong Shui-cia pa koe ka veng Sa-ma-li-yax,
pa ang lai singai khaing haktiex pa tawx Ya-keux ka kawn simex tix Yaw-seux;
6 dung rawm maw Ya-keux hoik koe dee an.
Ye-sux hoik taug mao ka hu tix krax,
nawh tom ngawm ka praok dung rawm maw;
yam an mawh bawg yam greung ngaix heue.
7 Koe bun Sa-ma-li-yax tix kaux hoek tix kin rawm;
Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Tawx aux nyaux rawm ceeix liak.” (
8 Pui gau nawh hoik hu liag priex preem ka yaong khankix heue.)
9 bBun an pawk ah nin:
“Maix hoik mawh pui Yo-thaix,
mai aux hoik mawh bun Sa-ma-li-yax,
yuh ka mawx maix lai nhie aux tawx tix nyaux rawm lie?”
(Yam an pui Yo-thaix mai pui Sa-ma-li-yax ang lai ling ka paox tix heue.)
10 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan maix tawng khawng phak buan son Siyiex mai pa nhie maix tawx tix nyaux rawm mawh pui mawx yam in,
maix tag sang khiox khaw ka nawh,
mai nawh tag sang tawx rawm pa im ju ka maix heue.”
11 Bun an ah nin:
“Sara,
maix ang koe kraung pa kin ka rawm,
dung rawm maw in kied raux,
sang hu teei maix rawm pa im ju dee mawx lie?
12 Tax du yix Ya-keux hoik tawx dung rawm maw in ka yix;
nawh mai kawn praix nawh,
mai khawng briex nawh hoik nyaux ka dung rawm maw in khawm uik.
Khex ce maix tix kawn lheu ting khaing nawh lie?”
13 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Pui pa nyaux ka rawm in kawn sang baox simie tix nyaux;
14 viang mawh nan,
pui pa nyaux ka rawm pa tawx aux,
tag ang lai baox simie tix nyaux.
Rawm pa tawx aux tag sang lih tix mawh rawm keet ka daux nawh,
mai hoik buing tix lih mawh rawm pa im ju ang jud,
mai hoik kieh nawh pon ju leeing ju leeing phan heue.”
15 Bun an ah nin:
“Sara,
tawx rawm in ka aux liak,
son ang aux sang lai baox simie tix nyaux,
mai ang siao lai baox hoek tix kin rawm dee in heue.”
16 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix hu kok mex maix,
khaix an deeh baox hoek ka dee in heue.”
17 Bun an ah nin:
“Aux ang koe mex heue.”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Maix krai ang tix koe mex,
hoik mawh tete.
18 Maix hoik sax koe mex sivoe an phuan kaux,
yam in pa ot tau mai maix ang mawh mex maix.
Lox pa krai maix mawh tete heue.”
19 Bun an ah nin:
“Sara,
aux hoik tawng mawh maix cao jhai tix kaux.
20 Tax du yix pui Sa-ma-li-yax hoik krub Siyiex ka gawng in,
viang mawh nan,
pex pui Yo-thaix hoik krai mawh Yie-lu-sa-leung dee krub pui ka Siyiex heue.”
21 Ye-sux krai ka bun an ah nin:
“Bun,
maix tag caw tix yum rhawm ka aux heue!
Bawg yam sang hoek,
pui ang lai baox krub keeing Siyiex ka gawng in,
phan ang mawh nan,
ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
22 Pex pui Sa-ma-li-yax,
ang tawng pa krub tix mawh mawx,
yix pui Yo-thaix hoik tawng pa krub tix mawh mawx,
mawh kheu hoik lih hoek krax taoh khaing pui Yo-thaix.
23 Viang mawh nan,
bawg yam sang hoek,
hoik mawh yam in,
pui pa krub ka keeing Siyiex tete,
tag sang krub ka daux khuan mai ka daux pa rauh pa see.
Krax krub pa yuh nin hoik mawh pa khaw caw ka rhawm keeing Siyiex meung rao ma.
24 Siyiex mawh khuan,
pui pa krub nawh tag sang krub nawh ka daux khuan rhawm rhi mai ka daux pa rauh pa see heue.”
25 Bun an krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux hoik tawng pa kok mawh Mi-sai-yax Khrit an tag sang hoek,
nawh tag sang hoek tix krai kaing in kix khawm uik ka ex heue.”
26 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux pa krai lox ka maix yam in mawh nawh heue!”
27 Yam an,
pui gau Ye-sux hoek.
Kix yaox si kah nawh tau mai bun tix kaux,
mai kix kied daox deng kah,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui pa chawk ka bun an ah nin:
“Sang lo maix ceu pa tix?”
Phan ang mawh nan chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai si kah tau mai nawh lie?”
28 Bun an tauh ka ngoi rawm viex tix,
hu ka plak ot yaong,
mai krai ka pui hun ah nin:
29 “Pex hoek tix jhak!
Koe pui tix kaux pon krai lih ka can kaing pa yuh aux khawm uik;
pui in tee mawh Khrit heue?”
30 Pui hun tom lih khaing veng hu jhak ka Ye-sux heue.
31 Yam in,
pui gau ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Sara,
ih ka ceu pa ih ceeix heue.”
32 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Ceu pa ih aux,
hoik mawh ceu pa ang pex tawng heue.”
33 Pui gau ra lai krai lao ah nin:
“Khex koe pui pa sang tawx nawh ih ka ceu pa ih lie?”
34 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Priex preem pa ih aux hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh ka alo kaw pa ploi aux hoek an,
khaix an yuh hoik ka kaing pa hoik ap nawh tix tawx ka aux.
35 Pex ah nin:
‘Baox pon khix taug mawh bawg yam pa vawg.’
Aux krai ka pex jhak tix yaox ka haktiex an heue,
to ma hoik kaux,
mai hoik chix tix vawg heue!
36 Pui pa vawg pon ka ngawh riang tix,
mai khrawm plix nghox son ju leeing ju leeing phan,
mai hoik kieh pui pa sum mai pa vawg rom tix gaux rhawm tau.
37 ‘Tix kaux mawh cao pa sum,
tix kaux mawh cao khrawm’.
Gum lox in mawh tete.
38 Aux ploi pex hu vawg ka kaung ma dee ang pex bax sum;
pui tang kaux hoik taug mao,
viang mawh nan pex hoik pon yaox tix ih nyaux ka daux pa taug mao kix heue.”
39 Ka daux yaong koe pui Sa-ma-li-yax tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka Ye-sux,
mawh kheu bun an ah nin:
“Nawh hoik krai ka kaing ceu pa hoik yuh aux khawm uik lih.”
40 Pui Sa-ma-li-yax an kix hoek ka Ye-sux,
kix mhaing tix ot tau mai Ye-sux,
mawh kheu hoik paung ot Ye-sux dee an ra singaix heue.
41 Koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka Ye-sux kheu gum lox pa krai nawh.
42 Kix krai ka bun an ah nin:
“Yam in yix hoik yum rhawm,
ang mawh kheu gum lox maix,
hoik mawh kheu mhong yix ka lox pa krai nawh,
tawng grawng hoik mawh nawh cao taoh haktiex heue.”
Ye-sux pai ka kawn simex simiang moi
43 Ye-sux paung ot dee an ra singaix,
khaix an nawh hoek ka Cia-li-lix.
44 cNawh hoik sax krai ah nin:
“Cao jhai ang pon krax pa yung yi ka haktiex ot tix heue.”
45 dYam hoek Ye-sux ka Cia-li-lix,
pui haktiex an hoik khaw rhawm ka nawh khawm uik;
mawh kheu yam haok nawh yuh poi ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pui hoik yaox ka can kaing pa yuh nawh khawm uik heue.
46 eYe-sux baox deeh hoek ka veng Cia-nax haktiex Cia-li-lix,
ka dee tieh nawh rawm lih mawh plai sivoe an.
Dee an koe simiang tix kaux,
kawn simex nawh koe jhawm saux ka Cia-pai-nong.
47 Yam mhong nawh kaoh Ye-sux khaing Cia-li-lix haktiex Yo-thaix,
tom hu yaox ka nawh,
mai khiox khaw nawh hu pai kawn simex tix pa nie saux ka daux Cia-pai-nong.
48 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Phan ang mawh grawng pa yaox pex ka mhai pa daox pa am,
pex ang tau tix yum rhawm ka heue.”
49 Simiang an pawk ah nin:
“Sara,
khiox khaw maix hu tau mai aux sivoe sang yum kawn simex aux heue.”
50 Ye-sux krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Hu heue,
kawn simex maix tag sang im heue!”
Pui an yum rhawm ka lox pa krai Ye-sux,
nawh tom ing.
51 Yam kawn ot nawh daux krax,
siliex nawh rieb ka nawh,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Kawn simex maix hoik im heue!”
52 Nawh chawk kix,
kawn simex aux hoik moi saux yam mawx.
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Jhawm saux si aoh nawh hoik dieh kawx yam greung ngaix.”
53 Keeing nawh an doh keud,
mai hoik mawh bawg yam nari an,
yam krai Ye-sux ah nin “Kawn simex maix tag sang im heue”.
Mawh kheu nan,
nawh mai pui nyiex nawh khawm uik hoik yum rhawm ka heue.
54 In hoik mawh mhai pa daox pa am pa yuh Ye-sux ka daux ra bawg yam kaoh nawh khaing haktiex Yo-thaix hoek ka Cia-li-lix heue.
