Veng pa guah tix tawx ka pui Li-we
35
aSimiang Siyiex si kah ka Maw-si daux lung Maw-ya,
praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
ka plak toi veng Yie-li-keu ah nin:
2 “Maix tawx lox ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
kieh kix teei sin ngao ka daux pa pon kix,
guah tix tawx veng tix blah mai vung viang pak rib ka pui Li-we.
3 Veng in kix tag tawx mawh cie pui Li-we,
kieh kix ot dee an;
pak rib mawh son sang kieh kix liang phung moi mai khawng brie tang ceu.
4 Pak rib in jauh ka vung viang ding veng plak praix vih tix koe pon yieh tix phuan kongjhee,
5 kieh veng mawh daux greung,
ku plak khaw paox khawm pon daog,
koe dim yieh kongjhee.
6 Pex tag caw tix tawx pui Li-we cie veng mox liah mu,
kieh pui pa lud tix pauh pui chix tix to mox tix kah.
Khaix an,
tawx veng ka kix tipon ra mu,
7 mai khawm pak rib vung viang veng,
khawm uik koe tipon daix mu.
8 Tix ceu pui sang tawx veng mie ding ka pui Li-we,
caw tix tat awm been vieh va haktiex kix.”
Veng mox
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 19.1–13; Lai Yaw-su-yax 20.1–9)
9 Simiang Siyiex tawx lox ka Maw-si
10 krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
“Khaix hoik tiang pex ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
laig ka daux haktiex Cia-nang,
11 tag caw tix laug ka veng mox,
son sang kieh pa lud tix pauh pui chix tix mox tix ka daux,
12 mox tix ka pui nyiex ma pa sang sawg tix pawk bix.
Pui tix kaux prawng nawh lud loe ka pa pauh pui,
phan ang bax yaox tix prawng ca sidawng,
ang pon tix prawng kieh yum.
13 Pex caw tix laug ka veng liah mu,
14 loe mu ot ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix,
loe mu ot ka haktiex Cia-nang.
15 Veng in kix tag tawx mawh son pa mox pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai son pui tang ceu tang cad pa ot si nax pex tix vud tix cu mai ka tom rheeng,
can pa lud tix pauh pui yum,
chix tix mox ka daux veng mox an heue.
16-18 “Viang mawh nan,
phan koe pui pa pauh pui ka kraung naing,
mawh rhiem ang ah,
simaox ang ah,
khaox ang ah,
hoik mawh krax phit lud pa pauh pui yum,
tag kieh nawh yum.
19 Cao nyiex pui yum an tag caw tix tang pauh ka pui an;
yam yaox nawh pui pa pauh an,
tag pauh nawh yum heue.
20 “Phan koe pui pa koeng ka pui tix kaux kaux,
mai cot nawh goh,
kieh nawh yum,
phan ang mawh nan,
tik kraung khrai lauh nawh yum,
21 phan ang mawh nan,
tawk nawh yum ka taix tix,
pui an tag phit lud ka pauh tix pui,
tag kieh nawh yum.
Cao nyiex pui yum an tang pauh pui pa phit lud an;
yam yaox nawh pui pa phit lud an,
tag pauh nawh yum heue.
22 “Viang mawh nan,
phan koe pui pa ang su tix pauh ka pui pa ang tix koeng kah,
viang mawh pa cot pui goh,
mawh pa tik ka kraung khrai pa lauh pui yum,
23 phan ang mawh nan,
ang yaox ka pui yam khuang tix simaox,
mai krox ka pui pa ang nawh su tix sang kieh yum,
pui an ang tie mawh pui pa koeng ka nawh.
24 Ka daux grawng kaing in,
pui hun tag khaw tix kia ngien ka pa lud tix pauh an,
mai ang khaw tix hu plak pui nyiex pa hoek sawg tix pawk bix son pui yum an.
25 Pui hun tag sang taoh pui pa lud an luat luan khaing tawm taix pui nyiex pa yum an,
mai hu tawx nawh hoek ka daux veng mox,
nawh tag sang ot tan,
tom hoik yum cao giex kaing li pa ting pa hoik laug kix.
26 Viang mawh nan,
phan pa lud tix pauh an,
sang hu khaing daux veng mox,
27 pui nyiex yum an yaox ka nawh,
mai pauh nawh yum,
ang sawn tix koe phi map.
28 Pui pa lud tix pauh pui yum an,
tag caw tix paung ot ka daux veng mox,
mai krox tom hoik yum cao giex kaing li pa ting,
tom tag chix tix deeh ing ka nyiex.
29 Pex mai ceu pui pex,
ot dee mawx ang ah,
tag caw tix hawt ka been in heue.
30 b“Phan koe pui krai phit lud grawng pa pauh tix pui,
tag caw tix koe pui ra kaux plak piang mawh cao pa kham sakse,
tag chix tix prawng nawh yum;
phan mawh ngie pui tix kaux khaing,
ang kub tix ciyang kieh yum.
31 Pui pa pauh ka pui tag sang plud ju son ju,
ang pon tix plud ka mau mai pon tix luat luan khaing krax ciyang.
32 Phan koe pui pa to mox tix ka daux veng mox,
ang pon tix teei mau taoh ka ju nawh,
cu nawh ing ka nyiex tix sivoe sang yum cao giex kaing li pa ting.
33 Phan cu nawh yuh nin,
tag seeing brai yuh haktiex dee ot pex.
Phan pa pauh pui yum hoik mawh seeing brai yuh haktiex;
lheu khaing pui pa pauh an plud ju son ju,
mai ang koe grawng tang ceu son sang kieh dee pauh pui yum an singax.
34 Pex ang pon tix seeing brai yuh haktiex dee ot tix;
mawh kheu aux mawh Simiang Siyiex,
aux ot ka daux si nax pui Yi-seu-liex khankix heue.”
