Rawg buan son sang deeh sum meung
80
aCao liang pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox rawg yix liak;
simiang cub phung yung eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet.
Cao pa ngawm ka piang dang ngeung Ci-lu-paw ra mu eue,
2 ka plak sivoe ngai Yi-fa-liien,
Piien-ya-ming,
Ma-na-si,
khiox khaw maix jhi pa ting riang ting rao maix;
khiox khaw maix hoek tix taoh ka yix liak!
 
3 Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh kieh yix haok huan;
khiox khaw maix jhi krax muh maix,
mai yix tom taug pon krax taoh heue.
 
4 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao pa bung kru naing kaing mheen eue,
maix tag ang yuh tix tawng pa rawg pui cie tix buan sang hoek yam mawx lie?
5 Maix hoik kieh yix teei rawm ngai tix mawh priex pa ih,
tawx yix nyaux ka rawm ngai tix kawk ting.
6 Maix hoik kieh meung pa koe siviex veng yix,
yuh naing ka daux haktiex yix;
pui pa koeng ka yix,
mai nieb pon map nai ka yix heue.
 
7 Siyiex pa mawh cao bung kru naing kaing mheen eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh kieh yix haok huan;
khiox khaw maix jhi krax muh tix,
yix tom taug pon krax pa taoh heue.
 
8 Maix viex nhu khaox plix siiet tix gawng hoek khaing Ai-cix;
maix grheu ka pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai sum ka khaox in.
9 Maix hoik paih haktiex tix dauh son khaox in,
riah khaox in kied rauh laig,
mai tauh hu ka haktiex in.
10 Veei khaox in hoik tawb kheem ka gawng lua khawm uik;
kak khaox in hoik tawb kheem ka khaox si oe bhawng pa ting pa lhaong.
11 Kak khaox in tawx tix hoek ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong;
riah khaox in tauh tom hoek ka rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
12 Yuh ka mawx maix lai yieh laux ka die,
ghram pui pa lawng krax briex tix pieh ka plix siiet khaox in lie?
13 Kieh prieh pa koe ka daux tiag dug dig ka khaox in lie?
Kieh to sat pa koe daux tiag hoek tix bleet ka khaox in lie?
 
14 Siyiex pa mawh cao bung kru naing kaing mheen eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh loh ka rhawm rhi tix,
kok jhak yix khaing daux rao ma,
taoh ka pui cie maix.
15 Khiox khaw maix hoek tix kia ngien ka nhu khaox plix siiet pa hoik sum maix,
pa mawh kawn khaox pa mhawm grang pa bre maix tix sum.
 
16 Pui koeng ka yix kid ka khaox in,
mai gleeing kah;
khiox khaw maix tawx krax pa song rhawm piang kix,
mai yuh kix laux leeing!
17 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien ka meung pa hoik sum maix,
kia ngien ka piang ceu pui pa hoik laug maix.
18 Yix ang doh lai baox tieh vheen ka maix;
kieh yix yaox tix im ju,
yix tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix heue.
 
19 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh cao kru naing kaing mheen eue,
khiox khaw maix deeh kieh yix haok huan;
khiox khaw maix jhi krax muh tix,
yix tom taug pon krax pa taoh heue.
