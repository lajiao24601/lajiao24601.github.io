Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix
Krai dawt
Ceu kaux Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix mawh pa kaoh khaing phuk lai sigang khri pa prim pa thut lih khaing lai Si-lax, dee bleeh mawh: “Lai lih hu krax”, mawh been pa ting ceu ka son ceu pui Yi-seu-liex, pa mawh ceu kawn pui Yi-seu-liex pa lih hu krax hoik bheeik luan khaing yuh tix siliex cawng ka daux haktiex Ai-cix. Phuk lai in guah tix koe pon grawng:
1. Grawng pa bheeik luan ceu pui Si-paw-lai khaing pa yuh tix siliex cawng ka daux haktiex Ai-cix.
2. Grawng pa hu daux krax plak gawng Si-nai.
3. Grawng pa yuh Siyiex sijax mukheu tau mai kawn praix nawh ka piang gawng Si-nai, mai tawx kix chix tawng daux krax, been kawn pui, mai lox tat jhawm prawng krax been li.
4. Grawng pa yuh dee krub son pui Yi-seu-liex; mai tat tix deuh son kaing yuh krax krub cao giex kaing li mai been krub.
Kaing ting phuk lai in tiiem grawng kaing pa yuh Siyiex. Nawh hoik pluat ploi ka kawn praix tix pa yuh siliex cawng yuh ka mawh, mai kieh kix koe krax yum rhawm, son sang yuh meung tix mhawm ka daux singaix pa sang hoek heue.
Pui pa ting ceu ka daux phuk lai in mawh Maw-si, nawh mawh pui pa hoik laug Siyiex lih taoh pui cie tix luat luan khaing daux tawm taix simiang Ai-cix. Phuk lai in khawm uik, dee blawn ting ceu mawh simin ting (20) grawng pa tiiem gum lox ping nyap pa tawx Siyiex khankix heue.
Krai kaing
1. Kawn pui Yi-seu-liex bheeik luan khaing krax kha pa yuh pui Ai-cix (1.1–15.21).
(1) Pa yuh siliex ka daux Ai-cix (1.1–22).
(2) Pa keh Maw-si mai krax ot krax koe nawh yam jauh (2.1–4.31).
(3) Pa yuh Maw-si mai Ya-loeng naing ka simiang Ai-cix.
(4) Poi Luat-luan mai pa lih hu khaing Ai-cix (12.1–15.21).
2. Jauh ka Rawm Sigrit Sigra Pa-raoh hoek ka gawng Si-nai (15.22–18.27).
3. Lox tat jhawm prawng mai sijax mukheu (19.1–24.18).
4. Lox tat jhawm prawng nyiex gox buan man mai grawng yam yuh kaing krub (25.1–40.38).
