Ta-wue baox ang pauh ka Sao-law
26
Koe pui kaoh khaing Si-fu hoek ka Sao-law ka Ci-pi-yax,
mai krai mox Ta-wue tix ka gawng Ha-ci-la ka daog daux tiag Yo-tax.
2 Sao-law nied viex pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa kua loe rheng kaux hu sawg Ta-wue ka daux tiag Si-fu.
3 Kix sum bang ot ka praok gawng Ha-ci-la.
Ta-wue kawn ot ka daux tiag,
yam hoik tawng nawh Sao-law viex pui naing tix hoek,
4 mai nawh ploi pui jiang hu jhak,
Sao-law hoik hoek tete.
5 Ta-wue yaog tix hu ka dee bang Sao-law dum mawx dum an,
mai yaox ka dee it Sao-law mai simiang naing nawh kawn simex Ni-eu pa kaux Ya-ni-eu.
Sao-law it ka bang ot pui naing;
pui naing nawh sum bang ot hawt vung viang nawh heue.
6 Ta-wue chawk pui Heux pa kaux Ya-si-mi-leux mai kawn simex Si-lu-ya pux Yaw-ya,
pa kaux Ya-pi-sai ah nin:
“Pax ra kaux mawx sang hu tau mai aux ka dee bang ot Sao-law lie?”
Ya-pi-sai ah nin:
“Aux sang hu heue!”
7 Ka daux bo an,
Ta-wue mai Ya-pi-sai laig ka daux bang ot Sao-law,
mai yaox Sao-law hoik laig it ka si nax bang,
pliah viex nawh cum tix ot ka praok kaing nawh piang tiex;
Ya-ni-eu mai pui naing khankix lai lawm tix it ka vung viang nawh.
8 Ya-pi-sai ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Ka daux bo in singaix,
Siyiex hoik ap pui pa koeng ka maix ka daux tawm taix maix.
Yam in,
kieh aux teei pliah viex nawh deeh klauh nawh kah,
pie tix vud khaing tag sang pon tix klauh nawh ot piang tiex,
mai ang siao klauh ra taix heue!”
9 Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue ah nin:
“Maix ang pon tix yuh nawh laux!
Pui mawx yuh simiang pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex laux,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka nawh heue.”
10 Ta-wue baox ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
aux hoik tawng Simiang Siyiex koi tang chix pauh ka Sao-law,
mai koi kieh nawh yum yam khaw tix sang yum,
phan ang mawh nan,
yum ka daux bang puing pui naing.
11 aSimiang Siyiex ang cu aux yuh simiang pa hoik laug nawh laux,
ex teei tix viex ka pliah cie nawh mai si ngien rawm pa viex nawh hu heue!”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tom teei tix viex hu ka pliah cie Sao-law mai si ngien rawm khaing praok kaing nawh dee an,
khaix an deeh hu tau mai Ya-pi-sai heue.
Ang koe pui pa yaox,
mai ang koe pui pa tawng ka kaing pa hoik lih tix mawh yuh nin,
pie pui tix kaux kawx ang haing.
Kix hoik laig it khawm uik,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh kix laig it heue.
13 Ta-wue tiang ka ngawd plak an,
hoek ka piang choek gawng,
mai hoik kub singai,
14 khaix an mai kok yo ka pui naing cie Sao-law mai ka Ya-ni-eu ah nin:
“Ya-ni-eu!
Hoik mhong maix ka aux lie?”
Ya-ni-eu chawk ah nin:
“Mawh pui mawx pa nieb pon kok yo,
mai kieh simiang haing lie?”
15 Ta-wue pawk ah nin:
“Ya-ni-eu,
ang maix hoik mawh pui Yi-seu-liex pa blawn ting riang khaing paox tix tix kaux lie?
Maix yuh ka mawx lai ang kia ngien ka Vawng pa mawh cao simiang maix mhawm mhawm lie?
Koe pui hoik taug khraox laig ka daux bang,
mai sang pauh ka cao simiang maix yum!
16 Maix ang pon giex ka kaing yuh tix mhawm mhawm,
Ya-ni-eu!
Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
pex khaw tix yum heue!
Mawh kheu ang pex bax chix kia ngien ka simiang tix,
pa mawh simiang pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex kah.
Hu jhak kah!
Pliah cie simiang ot dee mawx?
Si ngien rawm pa ot praok kaing nawh ot dee mawx?”
17 Sao-law hoik tawng mawh lox si kah Ta-wue,
nawh tom chawk ah nin:
“Ta-wue kawn aux eue!
Mawh maix lie?”
Ta-wue pawk ah nin:
“Simiang aux,
Vawng,
mawh aux!”
18 Khaix an baox ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kawn leue ka siliex tix lie?
Lud yuh aux ceu pa tix lie?
Yuh aux ka phi map ceu pa tix lie?
19 Vawng,
maix nghiet ka lox krai aux.
Phan Simiang Siyiex kieh maix yuh krax pa tieb tie ka aux,
mai aux cu tix tawx krax tan ka nawh,
son sang kieh rhawm rhi nawh deeh;
phan kaoh khaing rhawm keud kawn pui pa sang tieh vheen ka paox tix,
kieh Siyiex jhax lawg ka kix.
Mawh kheu hoik grheu kix aux lih khaing daux haktiex pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pui cie tix,
mai kieh aux vung vied vung viang ka meung tang meung khankix,
krub ka siyiex pa ot tan.
20 Khiox khaw maix paw vi kieh aux yum ka haktiex meung tang meung,
mai singai hu khaing Simiang Siyiex.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex yuh pa tix lai sang hoek pauh ka aux,
awn pauh ka diiep tix mu khaing lie?
Yuh ka mawx sang puing ka aux,
awm puing ka sim tiag tix mu lie?”
21 Sao-law ah nin:
“Aux hoik lud.
Deeh hoek heue,
kawn aux Ta-wue!
Aux ang lai baox sang yuh maix laux,
mawh kheu daux in singaix maix hoik deuh ka ju aux.
Aux hoik kied mawh pui daox tete;
mai aux hoik ting lud heue!”
22 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Vawng,
in mawh pliah maix.
Maix tawx lox kieh pui hu teei plak in.
23 Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka pui pa kaux sijax rauh see.
Ka daux in singaix nawh hoik ap maix ka daux tawm taix aux,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang bax yuh krax laux ka simiang pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex.
24 Kieh Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka ju aux,
awm hoik kia ngien aux ka ju maix daux in singaix,
mai kieh aux yaox tix luat luan khaing krax laux heue.”
25 Sao-law ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Kawn aux eue,
Siyiex tawx buan ka maix!
Kaing pa yuh maix ku ceu tag pon tix mhawm heue!”
Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue lih khaing dee an;
Sao-law kawx deeh ing ka nyiex tix heue.
