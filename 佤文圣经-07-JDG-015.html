15
Ang koe miex glang tix,
hoek ka daux bawg yam pa vawg nghox ca,
Chang-suing viex kawn pex tix mu hu kia moeng tix.
Chang-suing ah nin ka keeing moeng tix:
“Aux sang laig ka daux lawg it mai sawg moeng tix.”
Keeing moeng nawh ang kieh nawh laig,
2 ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux tawng hoik koeng maix ka nawh,
mawh kheu nan,
aux tom tawx nawh mex paox grawm maix pa yuh paox pie yam yuh poi ka paox.
Pux nawh kawn nie mhawm khaing nawh,
maix chix tix teei tix moeng nawh heue.”
3 Chang-suing ah nin:
“Bawg in aux yuh krax pa ang khaw ka pui Fe-li-see,
aux chix tix ang koe beeh glawm kah!”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom giex sox la loe yieh mu,
mai vien si dax ra mu tau,
mai chup mad ngu proh kah,
5 khaix an cux ngu,
ploi sox la an kix laig ka daux ma nghox ca cie pui Fe-li-see.
Yuh nan,
nawh hoik kieh nghox ca pa hoik vawg khawm pa ang bax vawg gleeing pak lawg,
pie khawm si peem sime kawx hoik gleeing.
6 Pui Fe-li-see si kaw pui pa ploi ngu,
mai tawng mawh Chang-suing pa yuh,
mawh kheu jao tawx keeing moeng nawh (pui Thing-na),
moeng nawh mex ka paox grawm nawh pa yuh paox pie tau mai nawh yam yuh poi ka paox.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui Fe-li-see tom hu ka nyiex ma keeing moeng nawh,
gleeing bun an khawm nyiex ma keeing moeng nawh heuea.
7 Chang-suing ah nin ka kix:
“Pex lai nieb pon yuh ka kaing pa yuh nin,
aux tag sang tiex tu,
ang pawk bix son kaing in ang chix!”
8 Chang-suing pauh ka pui kix yum tom nie.
Khaix an nawh tom hoek ot haktiex Yi-thang,
mai ot ka daux dawh rang tan heue.
Chang-suing pon pex ka pui Fe-li-see
9 Pui Fe-li-see hoek ka Yo-tax,
mai sum bang ot tan,
kix puing ka veng Li-si.
10 Pui Yo-tax chawk kix ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx pex lai haok puing ka yix lie?”
Kix ah nin:
“Yix hoek giex ka Chang-suing;
nawh yuh ka mawx ka yix,
mai yix kawx sang yuh ka mawx ka nawh.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
koe pui Yo-tax loe rheng kaux,
hoek ka daux dawh rang Yi-thang mai sawg ka Chang-suing,
ah nin ka nawh:
“Khex ang maix tawng pui Fe-li-see mawh simiang ka piang yix lie?
Maix hoik kieh yix kha rhawm tix heue!”
Chang-suing pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik hawt tix yuh ngie awm kaing pa hoik yuh kix ka piang aux!”
12 Kix krai nawh mhong ah nin:
“Yix hoek dee in,
mawh son sang phrawk ka maix,
mai tawx maix ka pui Fe-li-see heue.”
Chang-suing ah nin:
“Pex tiex tu ka aux,
pex ang pon tix tawx taix pauh ka aux heue.”
13 Kix ah nin:
“Chix tix,
yix sang phrawk ngie maix,
mai tawx ka kix;
yix ang pauh maix.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom teei maox khraox ra nyaing phrawk Chang-suing kah,
mai viex nawh lih khaing daux dawh rang heue.
14 Chang-suing hoek ka Li-si,
pui Fe-li-see to tix hoek rieb ka nawh,
mai kok yo lox tix tom ting ka nawh.
Khuan Simiang Siyiex lih hoek ka piang nawh dum mawx dum an,
kieh nawh ting riang ting rao;
yam chuh nawh to kaux tix,
maox pa phrawk kix ka plawng taix nawh,
hoik dut awm maox gan pa gleeing ngu yuh nan.
15 Nawh yaox si-ang kap la pa ang bax kroh tix khlawp,
mai sut kah,
kieh mawh kraung pa siao tix pauh ka pui an kix tix rheng kaux.
16 Chang-suing thut ka lai leng ah nin:
Aux teei si-ang kap la tix nyaing pauh ka pui tix rheng kaux;
aux teei ka si-ang kap la tix nyaing kieh to kaux yum pui pumb tix piang paox.
17 Khaix hoik ah nawh lox tix uik,
nawh tom tik ka si-ang kap la.
Dee an tom kok mawh La-maw Li-sic heue.
18 Khaix an,
Chang-suing kied sang nyaux rawm;
nawh tom khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Maix hoik kieh siliex tix pon pex ka pui pa koeng khankix;
yam in maix sang kieh aux yum ka pa simie tix nyaux rawm,
mai cawd ka daux tawm taix pui tang ceu tang cad Fe-li-see lie?”
19 Siyiex kieh haktiex Li-si bih,
mai kieh rawm keet buing lih khaing.
Khaix hoik nyaux nawh rawm,
riang rao nawh deeh lih koe awm phaox ka tom ting.
Mawh kheu nan,
rawm keet an tom kok mawh Yin Ha-keu-lixd,
tom hoek singaix,
kawn ot ka haktiex Li-si.
20 Yam kawn kuang pui Fe-li-see ka meung,
Chang-suing yuh cao prawng ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex tix nga num heue.
