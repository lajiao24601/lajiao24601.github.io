Prawng ca ka meung pa koe siviex veng
9
abIn mawh gum lox pa plak jhi Simiang Siyiex yuh nin:
nawh hoik krai pao grawng sang ciyang tix ka haktiex Ha-teu-la,
mai ka veng Ta-ma-see-keux.
Ang joeng mawh ngie ceu pui Yi-seu-liex pa mawh pui cie Simiang Siyiex,
veng ting Shui-li-yax kawx hoik mawh cie Simiang Siyiex nang.
2 Ha-ma daog haktiex Ha-teu-la kawx mawh cie nawh,
pui veng Thai-eux,
Si-toeng ra veng mai krax tawng krax yung kix kawx mawh cie Simiang Siyiex.
3 Pui Thai-eux sum tab son to kaux tix,
deuh mau khri hoik nie awm phawng tiex pa koe ka daux laih yuh nan.
4 Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang teei can ceu pa koe nawh khawn uik hu,
mai tik sin ngao pa koe nawh ka daux rawm sigrit sigra,
veng in tag sang gleeing laux leeing heue.
5 cVeng Ya-seu-ci-loeng tag sang lhat graung kheu yaox grawng kaing in;
veng Cia-sa hoik yaox ka grawng pa tag sang kha kix rhawm tix;
Yi-keu-loeng kawx awm in nang,
krax keud pot jun pot nawh hoik grai.
Haktiex Cia-sa tag sang grai ka simiang;
pui ang lai koe ka Ya-seu-ci-loeng tix kaux kawx.
6 Ceu pui pa hoik lu laig tix tag sang naog ot ka haktiex Ya-seu-thu.
Simiang Siyiex an nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh pui Fe-li-see pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi tiiem rhawm.
7 Kix ang lai baox ih nex pa koe nham daux,
phan ang mawh nan priex preem pa yum koeng tang ceu.
Can pui pa deuk khankix tag sang lih mawh pui cie aux khawm uik,
awm ceu pui Yo-tax ka daux tix ceu pui.
Pui Yi-keu-loeng tag sang lih mawh pui cie aux,
awm ceu pui Yie-pu-seed yuh nan,
8 Aux tag sang kia ngien ka nyiex ma tix,
mai ang kieh pui naing dug dig.
Aux kawx ang kieh simiang pa caox baox gut kheem ka pui cie tix.
Aux hoik yaox krax pa kha pui cie tix rhawm rhi tix heue.”
Simiang pa sang hoek
9 ePui haktiex Shi-ang eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm gaux rhi heue!
Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm kok yo heue!
Jhak,
simiang pex hoik hoek heue!
Nawh hoik pon pex,
mai gaux rhawm yam hoek tix,
viang mawh nan,
nawh brug la tix mu ka daux pa awn rhawm rhi tix,
pa mawh
brug ka kawn la tix mu.
10 fSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
aux tag sang teei hu ka ling law naing pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai yuh laux leeing ka ling law brung naing pui Yie-lu-sa-leung;
Ak puh pa siao ka daux naing kawx tag sang raig pot.
Simiang pex tag sang yuh pa nhim pa khaw caw ka meung piang haktiex;
nawh tag sang giex kaing piang rawm sigrit sigra khawm pon daog,
jauh ka rawm klawn Yo-fa-la-ti hoek ka dee uik haktiex heue.
Pa haok huan pui cie Siyiex
11 gSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
Mawh kheu hoik yuh aux sijax mukheu tau mai pex,
pa mawh sijax mukheu pa teei nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie tawk cum kah,
aux tag sang pluat ploi ka pex pa mawh pui pa vuk vied vuk viang in kix,
mai taoh pex lih khaing ka daux kroh tiex.
12 Pui vuk vied vuk viang pa koe krax keud pot jun pot eue,
deeh hoek heue!
Deeh hoek ka tab pex.
Yam in aux krai ka pex,
aux tag sang baox thiem thie ka piang ceu pa hoik koe pex.
13 Aux tag sang siao ka Pui Yo-tax awm siao tix ka ak puh yuh nan;
aux tag sang siao ka pui Yi-seu-liex awm siao ka te yuh nan;
aux tag sang siao ka kawn pui Shi-ang awm siao ka vaig gawn yuh nan,
aux hu puing ka pui Si-lax heue.
 
14 Simiang Siyiex tag sang jhi tix lih ka piang kaing pui cie tix;
nawh puing te awm pu plak praix.
Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong tag sang phlu teet;
nawh tag sang hoek mai bhaung ting khaing haktiex plak seh.
15 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing tag sang kia ngien ka pui cie tix;
kix tag sang yuh pui koeng laux leeing.
Kix tag sang kok yo ka daux naing,
awm pui pa yoek yuh nan;
kix tag sang kieh nham pui koeng jauh tix lih,
awm jauh nham Krax-tan Khawng-brie yuh nan,
mai jauh lih ka khu dee tan khawm pon daog.
16 Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex kix tag sang taoh ka kix,
awm taoh cao liang yung ka yung lih khaing daux pa laux leeing yuh nan.
Kix tag sang jhan riang ka daux haktiex ot tix,
awm siang pa koe ka mhok khri yuh nan.
17 Haktiex in kied sidaing mhawm,
kied sidaing haok huan heue!
Koe nghox koi pa kieh simex nhum ju ting riang;
koe plai khraox pa kieh bun nhum ju mhawm grang heue.
