Siyiex tawx lox ka Ya-paw-la-hang tan ka Yi-sha
22
aKhaix an ang lai koe miex greeng tix,
Siyiex cheuk tix yaox ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Siyiex kok yo ka nawh:
“Ya-paw-la-hang!”
Mai nawh pawk lox:
“Aux ot tin heue.”
2 bSiyiex ah nin:
“Teei kawn simex maix,
Yi-sha pa kied muh maix tix kaux,
viex hu ka haktiex Maw-li-yax,
ka piang gawng pa sang jhi aux maix yaox,
yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing ka ngu son aux heue.”
3 Ka daux ra singaix plak pon ngawb cao cao,
Ya-paw-la-hang lug khix son pa sang gleeing nawh tix tan,
mai sau ka piang la,
viex Yi-sha mai siliex nawh ra kaux hu dee jhi Siyiex nawh yaox.
4 Daux loe ngaix,
Ya-paw-la-hang kok yaox ka dee an dee singai.
5 Kheu nan,
nawh ah nin ka siliex tix:
“Pax ot tin tau mai la;
aux viex kawn nyawm hu krub plak an,
khaix an,
aux tag deeh hoek tin heue.”
6 Ya-paw-la-hang kieh Yi-sha klawm khix pa sang gleeing tix tan,
nawh viex ngu mai vaig tix mag ka daux taix tix.
Keeing mai kawn hu,
7 Yi-sha ah nin:
“Keeing eue!”
Ya-paw-la-hang pawk lox:
“Kawn aux,
ceu pa tix?”
Yi-sha chawk:
“Ngu mai khix hoik koe,
kawn yung pa sang gleeing tix tan lu koe dee mawx?”
8 Ya-paw-la-hang pawk lox ah nin:
“Kawn eue,
Siyiex tag tawx kawn yung son pa sang gleeing ax tix tan son nawh”.
Kiex ra kaux nied tix hu heue.
9 cKiex hoek ka dee pa hoik jhi Siyiex ka Ya-paw-la-hang,
Ya-paw-la-hang jauh tix yuh khu dee tan,
deuh khix ka piang,
mai phrawk kawn tix,
deuh nawh ka piang khix ka khu dee tan,
10 Khaix an yaog vaig sang pauh kawn simex tix kah.
11 Viang mawh nan,
kawn cau Simiang Siyiex kok nawh khaing daux rao ma mai ah nin:
“Ya-paw-la-hang!
Ya-paw-la-hang!”
Nawh pawk lox:
“Aux ot tin!”
12 Kawn cau Siyiex ah nin:
“Paw vi sau taix tix,
ang pon tix yuh kawn nyawm in mat!
Yam in aux hoik tawng maix lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex;
mawh kheu maix ang bax thiang tix ang tawx kawn simex tix,
pa mawh kawn tix kaux ka nawh heue.”
13 Ya-paw-la-hang jhak khawm pon daog,
mai yaox ka kawn yung mhaing tix mu,
pa vung vhien rung tix ka yeem khaox.
Nawh tom hu yah,
mai teei nawh kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing son kawn simex tix.
14 Kheu nan,
Ya-paw-la-hang kok ceu kaux dee an mawh,
“Yie-heu-hua Yi-leu;”d
tom in singaix pui ah mawh “gawng pa hoik cox khree Simiang Siyiex ka heue.”
15 Kawn cau Simiang Siyiex deeh kok ka Ya-paw-la-hang ra bawg tix bawg.
16 fSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kheu hoik yuh maix kaing in,
mai ang baox thiang tix ang tawx kawn simex tix pa koe tix kaux ka aux,
kheu nan,
aux sang tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux tix.
17 gAux tag tawx buan son ka maix,
tag kieh kawn praix maix haoh hun awm simuing daux rao ma,
mhaik ka daux rawm ting;
doeh ceu cad maix tag sang pon kuang ka pui pa koeng.
18 hMeung ka piang haktiex tag sang pon buan son khaing ka daux doeh ceu cad maix,
kheu hoik hawt maix ka lox tawm jhawm krai aux.”
19 Ya-paw-la-hang deeh hoek ka dee ot siliex tix tan,
mai kix tom ing tau deeh hoek ka haktiex Piie-see-pa,
pa mawh dee ot Ya-paw-la-hang heue.
Doeh ceu cad Na-heux
20 Ang koe miex glang tix,
Ya-paw-la-hang mhong grawng pa keh Mi-cia pux nawh Na-heux cie kawn daix kaux:
21 Kawn ai mawh Wu-see kawn nyi mawh Pu-see,
pux nawh mawh Ci-mu-lix pa mawh keeing Ya-lang,
22 Ci-shiiex,
Ha-sawx,
Pi-tax,
Yi-la,
23 mai Pi-thu-lix pa mawh keeing Li-pai-cia.
Mi-cia keh pux Ya-paw-la-hang pa mawh Na-heux cie kawn simex daix kaux.
24 Lio-ma moeng iak Na-heux keh Thi-pa,
Cia-hang,
Tha-sia,
Ma-cia heue.
