Ceu pui pa ot plak laong tieh vheen
(Lai Simiang Phuk Tix 12.1–20)
10
Law-paw-ang hu ka See-ciien;
pui Yi-seu-liex khrawm tix dee an khawm uik,
mai ap nawh mawh simiang.
2 Kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang,
kheu mox tix ka simiang Saw-law-meung mai hu ka haktiex Ai-cix;
yam hoik mhong nawh mawh Law-paw-ang simiang,
nawh tom khran nied hoek khaing haktiex Ai-cix.
3 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui plak laong ploi pui hu kok nawh hoek,
khaix an hu tau mai nawh hoek ka Law-paw-ang,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
4 “Keeing maix Saw-law-meung kieh yix peeh beeh glawm pa cien.
Phan maix tieh ka beeh glawm yix,
mai kieh krax ot krax koe yix nyawm,
yix tag sang hawt khaix maix heue.”
5 Law-paw-ang ah nin:
“Khaix in loe singaix pex deeh hoek.”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix tom ing heue.
6 Simiang Law-paw-ang chawk tix yaox rhawm keud kuad song ra loe kaux;
kix hoik sax mawh pui pa teem ka keeing nawh simiang Saw-law-meung khankix.
Nawh chawk kix ah nin:
“Pex keud aux sang khaw tix pawk ka lox pui in kix yuh ka mawx lie?”
7 Kix ah nin:
“Maix caw tix kia ngien ka kix mhawm mhawm,
son sang kieh kix gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
yam pawk maix ka lox kix,
caw tix riex krai lox mhawm,
kix tom tag yuh kaing jhaw maix leeing ju heue.”
8 Viang mawh nan,
Law-paw-ang ang simi ka lox pa krai kuad song in kix,
mai tieh tix hu sawg ka pui awn ju tix blah;
pui awn ju in kix hoik mawh paox pa ting tau mai nawh,
yam in kieh kix mawh cao pa teem tix.
9 Law-paw-ang chawk kix ah nin:
“Pex koe rhawm keud ceu pa tix lie?
Pui an kix khiox khaw aux tieh beeh glawm kix,
aux sang khaw tix pawk lox kix yuh ka mawx lie?”
10 Kix ah nin:
“Maix chix tix krai kix mhong ah nin:
‘Giien taix iak aux kawn lheu ting khaing nyoi keeing aux!
11 Keeing aux tawx beeh glawm pa cien ka pex,
aux tag sang baox tawx beeh glawm pa blawn cien ka pex;
nawh si vied pex ka maox,
aux tag sang lauh pex ka khaox pa koe kat heue!’ ”
12 Khaix an loe singaix,
Yie-law-paw-ang mai can kawn pui hawt ka lox pa hoik krai simiang Law-paw-ang,
mai baox hoek ka plak sivoe ngaix nawh.
13-14 Simiang ang nghiet ka lox pa krai kuad song khankix,
mai tieh tix hawt ka lox pa krai pui nhum ju,
briah lox pa caox ka kawn pui.
Nawh ah nin:
“Keeing aux tawx beeh glawm pa cien ka pex,
aux tag sang baox tawx beeh glawm pa blawn cien ka pex;
nawh si vied pex ka maox,
aux tag sang lauh pex ka khaox pa koe kat heue!”
15 Simiang ang simi ka lox pa khiox khaw kawn pui;
nawh ang tawng in hoik mawh alo Simiang Siyiex,
son sang lih tix mawh awm gum lox pa hoik tawx nawh ka pui See-law cao jhai Ya-si-ya,
krai ka kawn simex Ni-pa pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang heue.
16 aKawn pui Yi-seu-liex yaox ang simiang rieb ka lox pa khiox khaw kix,
tom kok yo ah nin:
Ta-wue hoik mawh pa tix ka ex lie?
Kawn simex Yie-si ang koe dee mhawm son ex pie ceeix kawx heue!
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
ex tang ing ka nyiex tix khaing!
Kieh nyiex Ta-wue tang jhak cao tix heue!
Kheu nan,
kawn pui Yi-seu-liex tieh vheen mai tang ing ka nyiex ma tix heue;
17 Law-paw-ang mawh ngie simiang kawn pui ka daux haktiex Yo-tax khaing heue.
18 Simiang Law-paw-ang ploi Ya-to-lang cao kuang pui yuh kaing hu ka pui Yi-seu-liex dee an,
pui Yi-seu-liex teei simaox dheem nawh yum kah.
Yam in,
Law-paw-ang si yaung mai ngawm ka piang ling law,
mai to ing ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
19 Jauh yam an,
meung pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot plak laong tom tieh vheen khaing meung Ta-wue heue.
