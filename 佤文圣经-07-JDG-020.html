Pui Yi-seu-liex rien khre tix yuh naing
20
Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
jauh ka Tang haktiex plak laong tom hoek ka Piie-see-pa haktiex plak seh,
Ci-liex ka plak lih singaix,
tag kok paox tix hoek khawm uik.
Pui khawm uik mawh rhawm tix mu,
khrawm paox tix ka Mi-see-pa ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex.
2 Simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu,
tag hoek ka daux pa khrawm pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex tix ka daux bawg in.
Pui naing hu ka caong tix koe tipon mheen kaux.
3 Ka daux bawg yam an,
pui Piien-ya-ming kawx mhong nang can ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui khawm uik,
hoik khrawm paox tix ot ka Mi-see-pa heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex chawk ah nin:
“Krai yix mhong,
kaing pa caox in lih koe yuh ka mawx?”
4 Pui Li-we an pa mawh mex bun pa kieh kix yum an pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux mai moeng iak aux hoik it ka Ci-pi-yax,
ka daux haktiex Piien-ya-ming;
5 ka daux bo an pui Ci-pi-yax hoek tix lai lawm ka nyiex ot yiex,
sang giex tix pauh ka aux,
khaix an,
kix rhim tix phit tau mai moeng iak aux tom hoik kieh nawh yum.
6 Aux tom khik to kaux yum nawh lih mawh khik mawx khik an,
mai guah tix tawx ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu,
tix ceu pui tix khik.
Pui in kix yuh kaing pa caox pa ang mhawm ka daux ex.
7 Pex hoik mawh ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik;
pex krai tix yaox,
pex sang kieh ex yuh ka mawx ka kaing in?”
8 Can pui khawm uik yaog tix kaoh cung,
rom tix ah nin:
“Ex ang lai kuang paung ot daux nyiex gox bang ang ah,
paung ot ka daux nyiex tix ang ah,
ang pon tix lai deeh ing.
9 Yam in ex sang yuh mawh nin:
ex cawk lai laug ka pui pa sang hu puing ka Ci-pi-yaxa khankix.
10 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux ku ceu pui,
kao kaux tix kaux tag sang caw tix tawx priex sawm pui naing,
can pa deuk khankix,
tag sang hu pawk bix ka Ci-pi-yaxb,
mawh kheu yuh kix kaing pa ang mhawm ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.”
11 Mawh kheu nan,
can ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik mawh rhawm tix mu khaing,
mai khrawm paox tix puing ka veng an heue.
12 Kawn cau ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku kaux,
hu ka daux haktiex ceu pui Piien-ya-ming ku dauh ku dee,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoik yuh ka phi map pa ting pa lhaong!
13 Yam in teei lih ka pui pa cau nyhau Ci-pi-yax an kix,
yix sang pauh kix yum,
son sang teei tix tik pa caox pa caig khaing daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex.”
Viang mawh nan,
pui Piien-ya-ming ang simi ka paox ceu paox cad tix ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
14 kix kaoh khaing veng Piien-ya-ming ku veng,
mai hoek ka Ci-pi-yax,
son sang yuh naing ka ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix.
15-16 Singaix an,
kix kaoh khaing veng ku veng mai khawm ka pui naing pa viex vaig ra mheen liah rheng kaux.
Lheu khaing an,
pui pa ot ka daux veng Ci-pi-yax kawx,
khrawm ka puic pa bre kix tix laug kah,
pa koe riang ka taix plak khawn goix aliah yieh kaux.
Kix ku kaux hoik mawh pui pa pon nyiak simaox,
pa ang kieh khluk khlaik pie ceeix kawx.
17 Ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
lheu khaing ceu pui Piien-ya-ming tix ceu khaing,
mai kix khrawm ka pui naing pa hoik gau tix chix tipon mheen kaux heue.
Puing ka pui Piien-ya-ming
18 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoek ka nyiex pa singax singeh ka Paw-theu-lix,
mai rawg mhaing chawk cheng ka Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang mawh ceu pui pa yuh ka mawx pa sang ran hu puing ka pui Piien-ya-ming lie?”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ceu pui Yo-tax.”
19 Ka daux ra singaix pon ngawb cao,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex yaog tix hu,
mai sum bang ot dex ka Ci-pi-yax.
20 Kix dia tix ot si tiep blawng sang hu ka veng,
son sang puing ka pui naing Piien-ya-ming.
21 Pui naing Piien-ya-ming lih khaing daux veng,
mai pauh ceu pui naing Yi-seu-liex yum ka daux singaix an ra mheen ra rheng kaux.
22-23 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex deeh ing ka nyiex pa singax singeh,
mai yiiem ka plak sivoe Simiang Siyiex,
tom hoek plak pon bo.
Kix rawg mhaing chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ex khaw ang khaw tix hu puing ka paox ceu paox cad tix ceu pui Piien-ya-ming lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Khaw tix hu heue.”
Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex deeh pon krax pa yawm rhawm,
mai baox deeh dia tix ka dee pa hoik dia kix tix awm singaix phaox kah.
24 Kix puing pui naing Piien-ya-ming ka daux ra bawg.
25 Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming baox deeh lih khaing Ci-pi-yax,
mai pauh ka pui naing Yi-seu-liex pa hoik gau tix tix mheen mai daix rheng kaux.
26 Can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
hoek tix yiiem ka Paw-theu-lix,
mai ngawm ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
tom hoek pon bo ang sawm ang rheep.
Kix tan Krax-tan Pa-nhim mai Krax-tan Pa-gleeing sivoe Simiang Siyiex.
27-28 Yam an,
teug sijax mukheu Siyiex ot ka Paw-theu-lix;
kawn saux Ya-loeng pa mawh kawn simex Yi-li-ya-sa pa kaux Fe-ni-ha cung tix ot ka sivoe teug sijax mukheu.
Pui haoh pui hun rawg mhaing chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Ex khaw ang khaw tix sang hu puing ka paox ceu paox cad ex ceu pui Piien-ya-ming lie?
Phan ang mawh nan ghram ex ka lie?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Hu puing kah!
Singaix pa sax aux sang ap kix ka daux tawm taix pex heue.”
29 Mawh kheu nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex kieh pui khankix tix blah kuan mox tix ot vung viang Ci-pi-yax.
30 Ka daux loe singaix,
kix puing ka pui naing Piien-ya-ming,
awm sivoe an ra bawg yuh nan,
mai dia tix ot si tiep plak hu ka veng.
31 Pui Piien-ya-ming lih tix rieb puing ka kix,
pui Yi-seu-liex pon nyaw glaw kix hu singai khaing veng.
Piien-ya-ming hoik awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan,
ka daux krax hu plak Paw-theu-lix mai Ci-pi-yax pauh ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
koe been tix ngoe kaux.
32 Pui Piien-ya-ming ah nin:
“Ex hoik pex ka kix awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex hoik keud kaing pa sang yuh,
mai yuh tix dak,
son sang nyaw kix lih hu singai khaing veng,
hoek ka daux krax ting.
33 Mawh kheu nan,
yam hoik deeh khrawm pui naing hun Yi-seu-liex tix hoek ka Pa-lix Tha-ma,
son sang deeh baox kieh pui naing tix mawh pui pa kuan mox tix ot Ci-pi-yaxd khawm pon daog,
dum mawx dum an,
preeng tix hoek khaing sivie siveng dee koe simaox.
34 Koe pui pa jawg tix laug lih khaing ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tix mheen kaux,
puing ka Ci-pi-yax,
ka daux pa yuh kix naing in kied ting.
Pui Piien-ya-ming ang nyang bax tawng kix dex hoik laux leeing.
35 Simiang Siyiex ap pui naing Piien-ya-ming ka daux tawm taix ceu pui Yi-seu-liex;
ka daux singaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex pon pauh ka pui pa koeng yum ra mheen phuan rheng tix yieh kaux.
36 Khaix an,
pui Piien-ya-ming tag tawng ang tix pex heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex pon pex
Pui naing hun Yi-seu-liex yuh tix deeh khaing plak sivoe ngai pui Piien-ya-ming,
mawh kheu yum rhawm kix ka pui pa hoik kuan mox ot ka vung viang Ci-pi-yax khankix.
37 Pui in kix kied phai preeng tix hoek ka Ci-pi-yax,
mai prung prang tix tauh ot ka daux veng,
pauh pui pa ot ka daux veng yum khawm uik.
38 Pui haoh hun Yi-seu-liex mai pui pa kuan mox tix khankix,
hoik tawx lox ka paox tix,
yam yaox kix hoik chu taox ngu lih khaing daux veng,
39 pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka daux bang naing tag caw tix deeh hoek.
Bawg yam in,
pui Piien-ya-ming hoik pauh pui Yi-seu-liex yum tix ngoe kaux;
mai kix tang ah nin:
“Ex hoik pex ka kix awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan.”
40 Khaix an,
pui Yi-seu-liex plih lox pa hoik tawx kix ka paox tix lih,
taox ngu jauh tix lih khaing daux veng,
pui Piien-ya-ming yaog kaing tix jhak,
mai yaoh ka yaox tix taox ngu khaing daux veng khawm uik,
pa klia tix haok daux rao ma.
41 Pui Yi-seu-liex deeh kling tix hoek,
pui Piien-ya-ming hoik pru praoh yaoh yee,
mawh kheu hoik tawng kix tix sang laux lak pak leeing.
42 Kix to mox tix plak daux tiag khaing pui Yi-seu-liex,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon tix luat luan;
kix su shiet tix yum si nax pui naing haoh hun Yi-seu-liex,
mai pui pa lih khaing daux venge khankix.
43 Pui Yi-seu-liex kieh pui koeng laig ka daux jhawk,
mai leue ka kix ang jud,
tom hoek ka Ci-pi-yax plak lih singaix,
pui Yi-seu-liex pauh kix yum hawt krax.
44 Pui naing Piien-ya-ming hoik yum been tix mheen daix rheng kaux;
45 pui pa deuk khankix,
deeh kling tix to mox tix hu daux tiag Ling-meung-yiien,
yum hawt krax phuan rheng kaux.
Pui Yi-seu-liex ca nied tix leue ka pui pa deuk khankix,
tom hoek ka Ci-toeng,
baox pon pauh ka ra rheng kaux.
46 Singaix an,
pui Piien-ya-ming pui kua pa yum khankix,
khawm uik koe ra mheen phuan rheng kaux heue.
47 Koe pui liah yieh kaux pa to tix pon luat luan hu plak daux tiag Ling-meung-yiien;
kix paung ot tan pon khix.
48 Pui Yi-seu-liex kling tix deeh ing puing ka pui Piien-ya-ming pa deuk khankix,
pauh ka can kawn nyawm simex kawn nyawm bun,
khawm khawng brie yum khawm uik,
can veng pa koe baoh haktiex an khawm uik kawx,
hoik gleeing laux heue.
