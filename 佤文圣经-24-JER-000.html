Lai Yie-li-mi
Krai dawt
Cao jhai Yie-li-mi mawh pui ka daux sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num aliah bawg yam hoek liah yieh num haok plak piang. Ka daux pa kied rheeng mawh nawh pa yuh kaing cao jhai, nawh ne raw ka pui cie Siyiex, kheu krub kix riang rao yuh ka phi map, meung ot kix tag sang yaox ka krax laux krax leeing pa ting. Yam kawn im ju nawh ka piang haktiex, nawh hoik yaox ka grawng pa lih lox jhai tix mawh yuh nin: Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik ot ka daux tawm taix simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha; veng mai nyiex buan hoik laux leeing; simiang Yo-tax hoik ploi hu tau mai pui haoh pui hun tom nie hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng. Nawh kawx hoik krai lox jhai grawng sang deeh hoek pui hun ka daux haktiex ot tix, mai deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma tix mhawm heue.
Lai Yie-li-mi chix tix guah lih koe phuan baux:
1. Mawh grawng pa kok ka Yie-li-mi.
2. Mawh bawg yam singaix pa giex simiang Yaw-si-yax, simiang Yaw-ya-cing, simiang Yaw-ya-cin, simiang Si-ti-cia khankix kaing, mai gum lox pa hoik krai Siyiex ka Yo-tax mai simiang khankix heue.
3. Mawh lox lai pa doh keud cao tiiem lai tang Yie-li-mi pa kaux Pa-lux, khawm lox dha Yie-li-mi tix krai mai grawng kaing pa yuh nawh pa ting ceu yam im ju tix ka piang haktiex heue.
4. Mawh gum lox pa krai Simiang Siyiex ka meung ka piang haktiex heue.
5. Mawh lox pa kawb tix tiiem: tiiem grawng pa laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung, mai grawng pa giex laung pui hun hu ka Pa-pi-loeng heue.
Yie-li-mi mawh pui pa koe rhawm keud dong sawn tix kaux, mai pa kied muh ka paox ceu paox cad tix khankix. Nawh ang rhawm prawng ca ka phi map pui hun khankix. Ka daux phuk lai sigang khri tom nie dee, nawh hoik plih lih ka lox pa koe ka daux rhawm rhi tix khawm uik, mai tiiem ka grawng pa kha rhawm tix ku ceu ka daux pa hoik kok Siyiex tix mawh cao jhai. Ka daux rhawm rhi nawh, gum lox Siyiex hoik awm ngu pa cap yuh nan, nawh ang krai ang chix heue.
Gum lox pa ting ceu tix blah ka daux phuk in, mai nie kawn lheu khaing ka daux singaix bawg yam Yie-li-mi, mai krai ka singaix bawg yam sijax mukheu pa khraox an. Singaix yam an, pui cie Siyiex tag sang priex tix hawt ka sijax mukheu an, mai ang lai baox lo ka lox sidiex sara, mawh kheu sijax mukheu an tag sang tiiem ot ka daux rhawm rhi kix heue (31.31–34).
Krai kaing
1. Mawh grawng kok ka Yie-li-mi (1.1–19).
2. Lox dha tix krai ka daux singaix bawg yam simiang Yaw-si-yax, simiang Yaw-ya-cing, simiang Yaw-ya-cin, simiang Si-ti-cia khankix (2.1–25.38).
3. Mawh grawng kaing pa yuh Yie-li-mi ka daux ju nawh khankix (26.1–45.5).
4. Lox dha tix krai pa ne raw meung ka piang haktiex (46.1–51.64).
5. Mawh grawng pa laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung heue (52.1–34).
