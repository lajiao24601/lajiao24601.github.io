Phung yung cie Siyiex
5
Yam in aux khiox khaw tix krai lox ka ex kuad song ku kaux ka daux pa mawh tix kuad song tix kaux nang.
Aux mawh pa kham sakse,
grawng pa kham Khrit krax kha rhawm,
tag sang pon min plak ngai ka daux singaix pa sang hoek.
Aux khiox khaw ka pex,
2 atag sang liang cub phung yung pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka pex,
koe krax gaux rhawm hawt tix kia ngien ka kix ka daux pa hawt tix yuh awm alo Siyiex,
ang mawh ka daux krax pa khin.
Ang mawh son sang pon ngawh riang tix tom yuh kaing,
hoik mawh pa yuh kaing ka daux pa tang kaoh khaing rhawm rhi tix.
3 Paw vi giex gang ka cub phung yung pa hoik liang pex,
viang mawh nan,
tag caw tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm son sang kieh cub phung yung hawt.
4 Yuh nin,
yam hoek cao liang pa ting,
pex tag sang pon ka mhok khri pa ang chix grai pa pruh riang an heue.
5 bAwm an,
pex pa mawh pui nhum ju tag caw tix cu nghiet ka pui pa riex ju khankix.
Pui ku kaux tag caw tix rhawt nyoi tix ka man dai ka daux krax pa awn rhawm awn rhi,
mai ra lai yuh kaing son paox tix;
mawh kheu hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Siyiex tieb tie ka pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi,
mai tawx buan ka pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi heue.”
6 cMawh kheu nan,
pex tag caw tix tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi ka greem tawm taix Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm,
yuh nan,
nawh tag sang yaog pex lhaong ka daux bawg yam pa khaw caw.
7 Tag caw teei tix tawx krax pa khi rhawm ka nawh khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik muh nawh ka pex heue.
8 Tag caw rien rhawm rien khre tix mhawm heue!
Pui pa koeng ka pex pa mawh phi caox jiet awm sivai sang si pa lai ling tix graox yuh nan,
sawg tix bleet ka pui.
9 Pex tag caw tix kieh krax yum rhawm tix kaux cong,
tieb tie kah,
mawh kheu hoik tawng pex ka pux aik pux o pui pa yum rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex khankix,
kix kawx hoik kham krax kha rhawm pa yuh nin nang.
10 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik kham pex krax kha rhawm tix vud tix cu,
Siyiex kaw pa tawx buan son khawm uik,
pa mawh Siyiex pa kieh pex si doe tix pon min plak nawh leeing ju,
pa kok pex ka daux Ye-sux Khrit.
Tag sang hoek tix cox khre ka pex,
kieh pex kaux cong,
tawx riang rao mai krax joh jhan riah rawng ka pex.
11 Kieh siawp sibrawm mawh cie nawh khawm uik,
tix ju hoek tix ju heue!
A-meung.
Gum lox pa nghoik tix chawk mhawm ot
12 dKa daux krax pa teem coi Si-la pux aik pa kaux sijax,
aux tiiem lox lai dawt in ka pex.
Aux tag sang sidiex gau ka pex,
mai kham sakse grawng mawh in buan son pa mawh pa meh Siyiex.
Pex tag caw kieh tix kaux cong ka daux buan son in heue.
13 fCeu cao Pa-pi-loenge pa hoik pon tix laug tau mai pex,
chawk pex mhawm ot;
kawn simex aux Ma-kheu kawx chawk pex mhawm ot.
14 Pex tag caw tix teei krax muh krax rhawm ra lai chawk paox tix mhawm ot heue.
Kieh pex pa mawh cie Khrit ku kaux pon krax pa nhim heue!
