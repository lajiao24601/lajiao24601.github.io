Yi-see-thie mawh moeng simiang
2
Khaix an,
krax song rhawm simiang hoik krai,
tom braong rhawm ka Hua-seu-tix,
mai baox keud ka ang nawh nghiet lox tawx mai grawng hoik tik ka nawh.
2 Pui cau pa khaw rhawm simiang tawx rhawm keud ka simiang ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx ang sawg ka bun krih pa awn ju mai pa mhawm grang hoek lie?
3 Khiox khaw Vawng kieh pui hu ka meung haktiex ku dauh,
kieh kix viex bun pa mhawm grang hoek ka daux veng ting ot simiang Su-sang daux nyiex haw simiang plak khaing,
tawx ka cao pa kuang bun Si-kai pa hoik sieh tix,
tawx kix siao ka sidah bhawng.
4 Khaix an,
Vawng chix tix laug ka bun pa muh tix,
kieh nawh mawh moeng simiang plud ka Hua-seu-tix.”
Simiang muh ka lox in,
tom hawt tix yuh ka heue.
5 Ka daux veng Su-sang,
koe pui Yo-thaix tix kaux kaux Maw-ti-kai.
Nawh mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
mawh doeh ceu cad Ci-see mai See-me,
kawn simex Ya-eux.
6 aYam pon simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa kaux Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha ka simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Yie-keu-ni-ya khaing Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai viex nang pui tix phung,
Maw-ti-kai kawx ot ka daux.
7 Maw-ti-kai koe nan tix kaux kaux Yi-see-thie,
ceu kaux Si-paw-lai nawh kaux Ha-ta-sa;
nawh mawh bun pa kied mhawm grang,
koe grang kaux nawh.
Khaix hoik yum miex keeing nawh,
Maw-ti-kai teei tix eei nawh mai kieh mawh bun pa ot mai tix,
eei nawh ting lhaong.
8 Yam hoek lox pa tawx simiang,
koe bun mhawm grang tom nie viex hoek ka veng Su-sang,
Yi-see-thie kawx viex hoek ka nyiex haw simiang,
mai tawx ka pui sieh cao pa kuang bun pa kaux Si-kai.
9 Si-kai kied muh ka nawh,
kied mhawm priex ka nawh,
mai tawx nawh siao ka sidah bhawng mai priex pa kied nyawm,
mai baox laug bun ka daux nyiex haw aliah kaux hawt priex ka nawh,
mai tawx nang nyiex haw pa kied mhawm ka nawh.
10 Yi-see-thie nghiet ka lox pa tawx Maw-ti-kai,
ang bax krai ceu cad tix mai paox nyiex tix ka pui tix kaux kawx.
11 Maw-ti-kai lai ling ka sivoe brawk daux nyiex haw ku ngaix,
sang tawng ka grawng Yi-see-thie,
mai jhak ka krax haok huan kaing.
12 Hawt ka been,
bun an kix sang bre tix tix num tom taug hoek ka sivoe simiang:
sivoe an liah khix siao ka bee Muran,
khaix an liah khix siao ka pa si oe bhawng mai sidah ceu pa kieh pui mhawm.
Khaix luan bawg yam in,
bun ku kaux tag lai loh tix hoek ka simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu.
13 Yam laig bun an kix ka nyiex haw simiang,
chix tix tang ceep ka kraung pa muh tix hoek.
14 Nawh tag it ka nyiex haw simiang tix sawm,
ngaix khaix pon ngawb tag viex hu ka nyiex haw tang dauh,
mai tawx ka cao kuang moeng iak pa kaux Sa-cia pui sieh tix;
lheu khaing blawn muh simiang ka nawh,
kok ka ceu kaux nawh,
nawh kawx ang koe bawg yam tix baox hoek ka simiang dee an.
15 Singaix yaox Yi-see-thie simiang hoik hoek.
Nawh mawh kawn bun Ya-pi-haix,
nan Maw-ti-kai,
khaix an hoik yuh kawn bun pa ot mai Maw-ti-kai.
Can pui pa yaox ka Yi-see-thie ku kaux tag muh ka nawh.
Yam hoek bawg yam hu yaox nawh simiang,
lheu khaing ceep nawh kraung pa kieh pui sieh cao kuang bun Si-kai nawh ceep,
ang koe krax bre tang ceu.
16 Yam yuh Ya-ha-sui-lu simiang ka daux aliah num mai kao khix,
pa mawh Khix Thi-pie,
kix viex Yi-see-thie hoek ka nyiex haw simiang.
17 Simiang kied muh ka nawh lheu khaing muh nawh bun tang kaux;
nawh lheu pon krax pa muh simiang khaing bun tang kaux.
Kheu nan,
simiang tom ceep mhok moeng simiang ka piang kaing nawh,
kieh nawh mawh moeng simiang,
tang ka Hua-seu-tix.
18 Khaix an,
simiang yuh poi ting son Yi-see-thie,
mai kok ka can pa koe min mai can simiang greem nawh hoek.
Ngaix an,
nawh tawx lox kieh meung khawm uik laux lee tixb,
mai si pieh rhawm tix tawx ka khawng phak.
Maw-ti-kai taoh ka ju simiang
19 Yam an,
Maw-ti-kai pon tix mawh cao kaing ka daux nyiex haw tix ceu.
20 Yi-see-thie nghiet ka lox Maw-ti-kai,
mai ang krai pui mhong tix kaux grawng ceu pui nawh mai paox nyiex nawh.
Yi-see-thie nghiet ka lox Maw-ti-kai,
awm yam iak nawh nghiet ka lox nawh yuh nan.
21 Bawg yam yuh Maw-ti-kai cao kaing ka nyiex haw simiang,
koe pui sieh tix ra kaux cao pa pau siviex nyiex haw,
pa mawh Pi-thang mai Thi-liex,
kied koeng ka simiang Ya-ha-sui-lu,
keud tix pauh ka nawh.
22 Maw-ti-kai yaox ka grawng kaing in,
tom krai ka moeng simiang Yi-see-thie,
Yi-see-thie tom krai ka kaing yaox Maw-ti-kai ka simiang.
23 Khaix hoik si kaw ka kaing in,
mawh tete;
pui in kiex ra kaux tom teuk teue tix yum ka piang khaox.
Simiang tom tawx lox mai tiiem grawng in khawm uik ka daux lai simiang.
