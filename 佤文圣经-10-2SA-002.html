Ta-wue mawh simiang pui Yo-tax
2
Khaix hoik kaing in,
Ta-wue khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Sang chix ang aux chix tix haok hu ka veng pui Yo-tax tix dauh?”
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
“Chix tix haok.”
Ta-wue chawk ah nin:
“Mawh veng yuh ka mawx lie?”
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Si-paw-loeng.”
2 aMawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue haok ka Si-paw.
Nawh viex moeng tix khawm ra kaux pa mawh pui Yie-see-liex pa kaux Ya-si-nuang mai pui Cia-mix moeng Na-pax pa kaux Ai-pi-kai,
3 mai nawh viex pui cie tix mai pui nyiex pui ma kix hu tau mai tix.
Kix paung ka veng pa ot sivie siveng Si-paw-loeng.
4 bPui Yo-tax tom hoek ka Si-paw-loeng,
mai kieh Ta-wue mawh simiang pui Yo-tax heue.
Ta-wue hoik mhong grawng pa si paung Ci-liex pui Ya-pi ka Sao-law,
5 nawh tom ploi kawn cau hu dee an,
ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx buan son ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm cu nghiet pex ka Sao-law,
mai si paung ka nawh.
6 Kieh Simiang Siyiex tawx krax pa kaux sijax ka pex,
mai kia ngien ka pex leeing ju.
Aux kawx sang chix kia ngien ka pex kheu kaing pa hoik yuh pex.
7 Pex tag caw tix kaux cong kua rhawm!
Simiang pex Sao-law hoik yum,
viang mawh nan,
kawn pui Yo-tax hoik tawx aux mawh simiang kix heue.”
Yi-see-paw-seux mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex
8 Simiang pui naing Sao-law kawn simex Ni-eu pa kaux Ya-ni-eu mai kawn simex Sao-law pa kaux Yi-see-paw-seux to hu tiang rawm klawng Yaw-tang hoek ka Ma-ha-nian.
9 Ka dee an,
Ya-ni-eu tawx Yi-see-paw-seux mawh simiang,
kuang ka Ci-liex,
Ya-suc,
Yie-see-liex,
Yi-fa-liien,
Piien-ya-ming,
tete te lai hoik mawh pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
10 Yam mawh Yi-see-paw-seux simiang pui Yi-seu-liex,
ju nawh koe tipon num.
Nawh giex kaing ka piang pui Yi-seu-liex ra num.
Viang mawh nan,
ceu pui Yo-tax hawt rhawm Ta-wue.
11 Ta-wue ot ka Si-paw-loeng giex kaing piang pui Yo-tax aliah num plak heue.
Nyiex ma Sao-law mai nyiex ma Ta-wue yuh naing ka paox tix
12 Ya-ni-eu mai kawn cau kawn simex Sao-law pa kaux Yi-see-paw-seux,
kaoh khaing Ma-ha-nian hu ka Ci-piien.
13 Kawn simex Si-lu-ya pa kaux Yaw-ya mai siliex Ta-wue hu si pup paox tix ka praok rawm dung Ci-piien.
Kix khawm uik hoik tang ngawm mhawm mhawm,
tix cub ot praok dung plak in,
tix cub ot praok dung plak an.
14 Ya-ni-eu ah nin ka Yaw-ya:
“Ex tang ploi pui pa nhum ju tix plak ra loe kaux lih lah cha paox tix tap ka ceng,
chix ang chix lie?”
Yaw-ya pawk lox ah nin:
“Chix tix.”
15 Mawh kheu nan,
ceu pui Yi-see-paw-seux mai ceu pui Piien-ya-ming laug pui pa chix blok kao ra kaux lih lauh pui cie Ta-wue plak in kao ra kaux.
16 Kix khawm ra plak lai giex ka kaing paox tix,
mai lai klauh paw paox tix ka lawm vaig.
Yuh nin,
kix khawm tix nga pon kaux hoik goh,
mai yum tau.
Mawh kheu nin,
Ci-piien dee an tom kok mawh “Bang lawm vaig.”
17 Khaix an kix khawm ra plak tom sau riang tix lauh paox.
Ya-ni-eu mai pui Yi-seu-liex ang pex pui Ta-wue plak in heue.
18 Kawn simex Si-lu-ya khawm loe kaux ot tan,
kix loe kaux mawh Yaw-ya,
Ya-pi-sai,
mai Ya-sha-he.
Ya-sha-he kied brax to awm cak tiag yuh nan,
19 mai nawh leue ka Ya-ni-eu,
ang via plak goix plak dawm,
hoik leue hu ka sivoe ngai.
20 Ya-ni-eu vud kaing tix jhak plak khaix,
ah nin:
“Ya-sha-he,
mawh maix lie?”
Ya-sha-he ah nin:
“Mawh aux.”
21 Ya-ni-eu ah nin:
“Paw vi leue ka aux,
hu leue ka pui naing tang kaux,
mai tud ka kraung khrai kix heue.”
Viang mawh nan,
Ya-sha-he kawn ca leue ka nawh.
22 Ya-ni-eu baox ah nin ka nawh:
“Paw vi leue ka aux!
Maix sang kieh aux pauh tix lie?
Phan aux pauh ka maix,
aux doh sang yuh kaing pa ang khaw ka pux ke maix Yaw-ya lie?”
23 Viang mawh nan,
Ya-sha-he kawn ca ang cu tix deeh.
Mawh kheu nan,
Ya-ni-eu tom si vun pliah viex tix ka plak khaix tix,
mai klauh ka vaig tu Ya-sha-he phlat.
Lawm pliah an phlat lih ka blawg nawh,
mai nawh tom goh yum heue.
Can pui pa hoek dee goh Ya-sha-he ku kaux tag kaog tix cung tan heue.
24 Viang mawh nan,
Yaw-ya mai Ya-pi-sai yaog tix hawt leue ka Ya-ni-eu.
Yam hoik laig Singaix ka daux gawng,
kix hoek ka gawng Ya-ma,
pa mawh plak lih singaix ka Ci-ya,
ka krax hu plak daux tiag Ci-piien.
25 Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming baox khrawm ka pui pa deuk khankix,
mai hawt ka Ya-ni-eu,
pau ka gawng tix mu.
26 Ya-ni-eu kok yo ka Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Ex sang hawt tix puing naing yuh nin leeing ju lie?
Khex ang maix tawng,
dee si dauh krax simah khawm ra plak,
khex ang nied ot nied raux lie?
Yix hoik mawh paox ceu paox cad maix,
maix kawn sang krox jun hoek miex greeng,
tag sang tawx lox kieh pui naox tix ang lai leue ka yix lie?”
27 Yaw-ya ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju,
phan maix ang taug krai lox,
pui naing aux tag sang nied tix leue ka pex,
mai leue tom hoek pa sax pon ngawb.”
28 Mawh kheu nan,
Yaw-ya phlu ka teet tawng,
mai tawx lox ka pui naing tix,
ang lai cu kix leue ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Naing kawx hoik naox tix heue.
29 Ya-ni-eu mai pui naing nawh khankix hu leeing sawm,
mai laig ka daux ngawd Yaw-tang,
tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
ngaix khaix kix hu leeing ngawb mhawm mhawm tag hoek ka Ma-ha-nian heue.
30 Khaix hoik naox Yaw-ya leue tix ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
tom khrawm can pui naing tix,
lheu khaing yaox Ya-sha-he khaing,
ang lai yaox kao kaux dim.
31 Viang mawh nan,
Ta-wue plak in hoik pon pauh ka pui Piien-ya-ming pa hawt khaix Ya-ni-eu loe yieh tix gliah kaux.
32 Yaw-ya mai pui naing teei to kaux yum Ya-sha-he viex hoek ka Paw-li-haung,
mai si paung nawh ot daux haktiex si meeig pui nyiex kix.
Khaix an,
kix baox hu ka daux tix sawm,
tom hoik riang praix tag deeh hoek ka Si-paw-loeng heue.
