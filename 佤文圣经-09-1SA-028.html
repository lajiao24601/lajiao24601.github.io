28
Ang koe miex glang tix,
pui Fe-li-see khrawm ka pui naing mai puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
Simiang Ya-cie ah nin ka Ta-wue:
“Maix khaw tix tawng,
maix mai pui cie maix tag sang rom tix puing naing tau mai aux.”
2 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Chix tix,
aux hoik mawh siliex maix.
Maix hoik tawng aux chix yuh pa tix.”
Simiang Ya-cie ah nin:
“Kied mhawm!
Aux sang kieh maix mawh cao pa hawt pau tix tom rheeng heue.”
Sao-law chawk ka yiex kuad chix si meed
3 aSa-mu-eu hoik yum;
pui Yi-seu-liex yiiem son nawh,
mai si paung nawh ka daux haktiex keh nawh La-max.
Sao-law hoik khin ka can pui pa chix si pawk mai ka pui pa chix si meed khankix lih khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
4 Pui naing Fe-li-see khrawm paox tix sum bang ot ka praok veng Su-niang;
Sao-law khrawm pui Yi-seu-liex sum bang ot ka gawng Ci-li-paw.
5 Sao-law yaox ka pui naing Fe-li-see,
mai kied lhat kah,
6 bnawh tom khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex son sang yuh ka mawx.
Viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex ang tawx ca maox,
mai ang tawx nang wu-ling mai cao jhai pawk lox nawh.
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law tawx lox ka siliex tix ah nin:
“Sawg bun miex pa chix yuh si meed son aux tix kaux,
aux sang hu chawk ka nawh heue.”
Kix ah nin:
“Ka daux Yin To-eux koe tix kaux heue.”
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Sao-law loh kraung ceep tix,
khaix hoik faik praix,
nawh hawt khaix pui cau an kiex hu ka bun an.
Sao-law ah nin ka nawh:
“Khiox khaw maix kok khuan pui yum son aux,
mai krai aux mhong grawng kaing pa sang lih tix mawh.
Aux ci ka pui tix kaux kaux,
maix kok ka khuan nawh hoek.”
9 Bun an ah nin:
“Maix rien tix hoik tawng ka kaing pa yuh simiang Sao-law klang ka an,
mai nawh hoik khin ka pui pa chix si pawk mai pui pa chix si meed lih khaing daux pui Yi-seu-liexc.
Yuh ka mawx maix lai keud tix sang yuh aux laux,
mai kieh aux yum lie?”
10 Sao-law ah lox pa tiex tu ka nawh ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa im ju mai cu ka lox maix:
maix ang doh chix yaox tix hoek krax ciyang son kaing in.”
11 Bun an chawk ah nin:
“Maix sang kieh aux kok pui mawx hoek lie?”
Sao-law ah nin:
“Sa-mu-eu heue.”
12 Yam yaox bun an ka Sa-mu-eu,
nawh yaoh yee riag yo,
mai ah nin ka Sao-law:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai pie lhien ka aux?
Maix hoik mawh simiang Sao-law heue!”
13 Simiang Sao-law ah nin ka nawh:
“Paw vi lhat!
Hoik yaox maix ceu pa tix?”
Bun an pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik yaox pui pa awm siyiex tix kaux lih hoek khaing daux tiex heue.”
14 Sao-law chawk ah nin:
“Nawh awm ceu pa tix?”
Bun an pawk lox ah nin:
“Mawh kuad tix kaux pa hoek;
mai nawh ceep ka kraung dha.”
Sao-law hoik tawng mawh an Sa-mu-eu,
nawh tom gup tix krub ka piang tiex.
15 Sa-mu-eu chawk Sao-law ah nin:
“Maix yuh ka mawx lai tuk tiak ka aux?
Yuh ka mawx lai kok aux hoek?”
Sao-law ah nin:
“Krax laux leeing sang hoek piang aux!
Pui Fe-li-see sang puing ka aux,
mai Siyiex hoik tik vud ka aux.
Nawh ang lai tawx cao jhai hoek,
mai ang lai pawk lox aux ka daux ca maox.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tom tag kok maix hoek krai tix mhong sang caw aux tix yuh ka mawx.”
16 Sa-mu-eu ah nin:
“Phan Simiang Siyiex hoik tik vud ka maix,
mai hoik lih mawh pa koeng maix kah,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kawn kok aux hoek lie?
17 dPa yuh Simiang Siyiex ka piang maix,
hoik toe khaing daux jheeh aux krai ka maix;
nawh hoik teei ka meung cie maix,
mai tawx ka Ta-wue;
18 emawh kheu lieh lheu maix ka lox tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai ang bax yuh laux ka pui Ya-ma-lix mai ka pa koe kix khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan,
daux in singaix,
Simiang Siyiex yuh nin ka maix.
19 Nawh tag sang ap maix mai pui Yi-seu-liex ka pui Fe-li-see.
Pa sax,
maix mai kawn simex maix khankix tag sang hoek ot tau mai aux;
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ap pui naing Yi-seu-liex ka pui Fe-li-see heue.”
20 Sao-law yaoh yee ka lox pa krai Sa-mu-eu,
mai goh it piang tiex dum mawx dum an,
to kaux nawh khawm mu hoik si kied,
noi tix it piang tiex,
mawh kheu ang nawh bax ih priex tix ngaix tix sawm,
mai ang lai koe riang rao pie ceeix kawx.
21 Bun an hoek dex ka sivoe Sao-law,
yaox yaoh yee nawh,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Aux hoik teei ju tix yuh ka kaing pa nhie maix aux yuh.
22 Yam in maix yuh awm pa nhie aux maix yuh.
Aux ngaik maix ih ka ceu pa ih ceeix,
maix ih ka tom taug koe riang rao tix hu krax.”
23 Sao-law ang cu tix ih;
nawh krai ang tix ih.
Pui pa hawt nawh kawx hoik nhie nawh ih ceeix.
Dee si dauh,
nawh nghiet kah,
mai yaog tix kaoh khaing piang tiex,
ngawm piang ku.
24 Bun an tom nied tix pauh ka kawn moi iak pa eei tix kluing ka nyiex tix mu,
mai baox teei glua mien gaox,
tong ka khao mun pa ang sau su saix tix blah.
25 Nawh tom viex priex preem an hoek ka sivoe ngai Sao-law mai ka sivoe ngai pui pa hawt khaix nawh khankix,
mai kix tom ih.
Ka daux bo an,
kix tom taug hu heue.
