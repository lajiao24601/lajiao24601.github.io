Siyiex pa mawh simiang
93
Simiang Siyiex giex ka kaing,
mai nawh teei pa ting siawp sibrawm mawh kraung ceep;
Simiang Siyiex teei pa ting riang ting rao mawh maox rawd nyoi.
Haktiex sum tix kaux cong,
mai ang pon tix yung yang.
2 Simiang Siyiex eue,
dang ngeung maix hoik sum tix kaux cong tom yam jauh dix dix;
tom yam jauh dix dix maix hoik dawg koe heue.
 
3 Simiang Siyiex eue,
lox luah lih khaing dee kied raux;
lox luah rawm ting chun tix lih.
4 Simiang Siyiex giex kaing ka piang rao ma,
gut ka lox luah rawm ting,
hoik pon pex ka puk pak rawm sigrit sigra heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex eue,
lox tat jhawm prawng maix koe leeing ju,
nyiex buan maix pa singax,
ang chix grai grup leeing ju leeing phan heue.
