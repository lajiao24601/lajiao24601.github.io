Hu tawx teug sijax mukheu hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
(Lai Grawng Been Phuk Tix 13.1–14; 15.25–16.6,43)
6
Ta-wue baox khrawm pui naing pa blawn mhawm khaing ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex loe mheen kaux,
2 bmai thung kix hu ka Pa-laa haktiex Yo-tax,
kaing ting mawh son sang teei teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex hoek khaing dee an.
Teug sijax mukheu in hoik hawt ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing,
dee ngawm Simiang Siyiex koe ka piang Ci-lu-paw ra mu.
3 cKix deuh teug sijax mukheu ot ka piang ling law khraox tix lhang,
jauh tix grawng lih khaing ka piang gawng nyiex Ya-pi-na-ta.
Kawn simex Ya-pi-na-ta ra kaux pa kaux Wu-sha mai Ya-si-yaw mawh cao pa thung kix hu.
4 Ya-si-yaw hu sivoe.
5 Ta-wue mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik hoik rhax ka laid,
yaog yaw ka min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex.
Kix tauh ting ku ceu,
koe ting jung,
ting pawd aliah lah,
kraung,
pi biang,
mai chiang heue.
6 Yam hoek kix ka Na-khang dee pauh nghox ca,
moi pa bluh ling law dex sang goh,
mai Wu-sha tom tawx taix tix giex ka teug sijax mukheu Siyiex.
7 Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka jao kaing pa lud loe yuh Wu-sha,
mai pauh ka nawh.
Nawh tom yum ka praok teug sijax mukheu Siyiex.
Dee an tom kok mawh Pi-lie-see Wu-shae,
tom hoek yam in singaix.
8 Kheu jao song rhawm Simiang Siyiex ka Wu-sha,
mai ciyang ka nawh,
Ta-wue kied ang gaux rhawm ka heue.
9 Singaix an,
Ta-wue lhat ka Simiang Siyiex,
tom ah nin:
“Yam in aux sang pon teei teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex hoek yuh ka mawx lie?”
10 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom but rhawm tix ang viex teug sijax mukheu laig daux Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai viex hu krax tang nyaing,
hoek ka veng Cia-theux nyiex ma Eu-piie Yi-tong.
11 fTeug sijax mukheu ot ka daux nyiex ma Eu-piie Yi-tong loe khix.
Simiang Siyiex tawx buan ka nawh mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh khawm uik heue.
12 Ta-wue mhong grawng pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex buan ka nyiex Eu-piie Yi-tong mai pui nyiex pui ma nawh mai kraung khrai nawh khawm uik kheu jao teug sijax mukheu,
nawh tom yuh krax gaux rhawm teei teug sijax mukheu khaing nyiex Eu-piie Yi-tong,
mai viex hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
13 Pui pa grawng teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang yaog tix hu liah diang,
Ta-wue tom nhie kix kaog.
Nawh teei moi mhaing mai kawn moi pa mhawm kluing mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie ka Simiang Siyiex.
14 Ta-wue kok fua ngie tix ka man keei sum Yi-fu-teu tix plah khaing,
mai sau riang tix graoh,
yaog yaw min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex.
15 Nawh mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik gaux rhawm phlu ka teet tawng,
mai viex teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung heue.
16 Yam viex kix teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex laig ka daux veng Ta-wue,
kawn bun Sao-law pa kaux Mi-cia kok jhak ka daux doh,
mai yaox ka graoh simiang Ta-wue,
mai ang rhawm yaox nawh.
17 Kix grawng teug sijax mukheu Simiang Siyiex laig daux nyiex gox bang pa hoik rien khre Ta-wue.
Khaix an,
Ta-wue yuh krax Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka Simiang Siyiex.
18 Khaix hoik hoik krax tan,
Ta-wue nawb buan ka kawn pui ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh kru naing pa ting ka piang khawm uik,
19 h khaix an mai guah tix tawx kix ih priex preem.
Can pui ka daux Yi-seu-liex ku kaux,
simex bun ang ah,
hoik pon ka khao mun tix kaux tix lon,
nex tix kaux tix khikg,
mai plix siiet kroh tix blah heue.
Pui haoh pui hun deeh ing ka nyiex heue.
20 Khaix hoik ing ka nyiex,
Ta-wue sang nawb buan son ka pui nyiex pui ma tix.
Mi-cia lih kuan rieb ka nawh,
ah nin:
“Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex kied sidaing koe pak ngai daux in singai!
Nawh hoik awm pui pa daox mai jhi to kaux tix lih ka plak sivoe bun siliex tix khankix heue!”
21 Ta-wue ah nin:
“Aux graoh mawh son sang yaog yaw ka min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex.
Nawh hoik laug aux tang keeing maix mai tang cub nyiex keeing maix,
son sang kieh aux mawh simiang pui cie nawh Yi-seu-liex.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux tag sang nied tix graoh,
yaog yaw ka min plak ngaix nawh,
22 son sang nie kieh aux to kaux tix tiiem.
Maixi tee een aux mawh pui pa ang koe ceu,
viang mawh nan,
bun siliex an kix hoik een aux mawh pui pa ting min tete lie!”
23 Kawn bun Sao-law pa kaux Mi-cia,
mai ang koe kawn simex kawn bun ka daux ju tix heue.
