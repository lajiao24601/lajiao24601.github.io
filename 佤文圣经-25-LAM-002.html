Simiang Siyiex ciyang ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
2
Ka daux krax pa song rhawm Simiang Siyiex,
mai kieh pa faik tawb kheem ka Shi-ang.
Nawh teei tix tik ka pa mhawm grang Yi-seu-liex;
ka daux singaix pa song rhawm nawh,
pie nyiex buan cie to kaux tix kawx hoik tik ka heue.
 
2 Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh laux leeing ka nyiex yaong Yo-tax ku yaong,
pie ceeix kawx ang lai koe krax pa caox rhawm kah.
Ka daux krax pa song rhawm nawh mai hoik yieh laux ka tab pa priex ka haktiex kix.
Nawh kieh meung haktiex kix mai simiang khankix pon krax kaig krax tua heue.
 
3 Ka daux krax pa song rhawm nawh,
mai hoik yuh laux leeing ka riang rao pui Yi-seu-liex;
yam hoek puing pui koeng ka kix,
nawh ang taoh teem ka kix.
Nawh awm ngu pa bleet ka ex yuh nan,
mai hoik gleeing laux leeing khawm uik heue.
 
4 Nawh hoik vaik ak sau te ka ex,
awm puing ka puing koeng yuh nan.
Nawh hoik pauh ka pui pa muh ex khankix;
Nawh ploi krax pa song rhawm tix hoek ka piang Yie-lu-sa-leung khawm uik heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex awm pui koeng yuh nan,
mai yuh laux leeing ka pui Yi-seu-liex;
Nawh kieh tab pui Yi-seu-liex mai nyiex haw simiang lih mawh dee riem;
nawh baox thiem thie krax khi rhawm yiiem grai ka piang pui Yo-tax.
 
6 Nawh hoik yuh laux leeing ka nyiex buan dee khrawm ex paox tix krub;
Simiang Siyiex hoik naox yuh tix poi mai singaix tuah ka Shi-ang.
Nawh hoik tieb tie simiang mai cao giex kaing li ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
 
7 Simiang Siyiex tik ka khu dee tan mai nyiex pa singax singeh cie to kaux tix;
nawh hoik cu pui koeng yieh laux ka nyiex haw simiang mai ka guh ding veng.
Ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
kix hoik gaux rhawm ah lox awm pui pa yuh poi yuh nan heue.
 
8 Simiang Siyiex but rhawm tix sang yieh ka guh veng Shi-ang;
nawh teei maox diag tiag kah,
tag sang but tix yuh laux.
Nawh kieh nyiex lhaong veng mai guh veng ghrup tau heue.
 
9 Siviex veng hoik si paung tix ot ka daux pum bliah beung;
jhong siviex hoik laux leeing.
Simiang mai ceu pui pa ting min khankix hoik grheu kix hu khawm uik.
Ang koe pui pa krai ka lox tat jhawm prawng;
cao jhai ang pon riang su sai pa hoek khaing Simiang Siyiex heue.
 
10 Tax yieh miex keeing ka daux Shi-ang ngawm ka piang tiex,
mai yien yawg tix ang nye ang nyux.
Kix hoik ceep ka kraung man keei sum,
mai sau nyaox piang kaing tix;
bun krih ka daux Yie-lu-sa-leung laux rhawm laux rhi tix mai krub ka piang tiex heue.
 
11 Ngai aux hoik dug kheu yiiem aux;
rhawm rhi aux kied sidaing laux rhawm heue!
Aux hoik yiiem doe kheu laux leeing paox ceu paox cad tix;
kawn nyawm mai kawn ngie hoik goh ka daux laih heue.
 
12 Kix khiox khaw ka miex tix ah nin:
koe pa ih pa un dee mawx lie?
Koe pa nyaux dee mawx?
Kix awm pui mat pa goh ka daux krax yuh nan,
mai koi yum ka daux deem miex tix heue.
 
13 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
aux sang pon krai ceu pa tix lie?
Aux sang pon kieh maix nyawm rhawm yuh ka mawx lie?
Koe pui mawx pa si pup krax laux leeing yuh nin awm maix lie?
Krax pa laux leeing maix awm rawm sigrit sigra pa ang koe dee die yuh nan;
pui mawx pon tawx krax keud pot jun pot ka maix lie?
 
14 Cao jhai maix chix pie lhien ngie khaing;
riang su sai kix mawh pa giex pa going.
Krax pa sidiex gau kix ang sax plih lih ka phi map maix,
can lox pa krai kix mawh pa pie lhien khawm uik;
Kix cheeik maix ang siao loh rhawm rhi tix heue.
 
15 Pui pa lawng krax hoik teei ngai pa map nai jhak ka maix;
kix vuk vied kaing tix nyiah ka laux leeing Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
in hoik mawh veng pa kied grum mhawm lie?
In hoik mawh veng pa hoik rhax lai yaog yaw haktiex khawm bee ma ka lie?
 
16 Can pui koeng hoik nu nai ka maix khawm uik;
kix greeik rhang braing tix mai yum koeng ka maix.
Kix nyiah ah nin:
yix hoik yuh nawh laux leeing heue!
Ka daux singaix pa kroh jun yix ka hoik hoek heue!
 
17 Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh lih ka kaing pa sang yuh tix;
nawh hoik hawt tix yuh awm pa ne raw ka yix singaix pa hoik luan,
mai ang lai koe krax pa caox rhawm yuh yix laux leeing.
Nawh kieh pui koeng ka yix puing pex,
mai kieh kix gaux rhawm kheu laux leeing yix heue.
 
18 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
kieh guh veng maix khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex heue!a
Kieh rawm ngai maix leeing ngaix leeing sawm awm rawm klawng pa buing tix lih hu heue!
Kieh maix laux rhawm yiiem grai ang laig it laig tom heue!
 
19 Kaoh heue!
Yam pon sawm maix tag caw tix kaoh khaing it tix,
kaoh miex bawg ploi lox pa kok yo ka Simiang Siyiex,
viex rhawm awn rhawm tiiem khiox khaw nawh caox rhawm ka kawn praix maix khankix;
kawn praix maix hoik goh ka kre laih kheu cai rhawm tix heue.
 
20 Jhak heue,
Simiang Siyiex eue,
jhak tix yaox ka pui pa hoik kieh maix kha rhawm tix heue!
Bun hoik ih ka nex kawn ngie pa muh tix heue!
Pui hoik pauh cao giex kaing li mai cao jhai khankix ka daux nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex heue!
 
21 Pui riex ju mai pui pa nhum ju hoik yum awm paox ka daux laih yuh nan;
nyawm si naw nyawm bun krih hoik yum ka greem vaig gawn pui koeng.
Maix hoik pauh kix yum ka daux singaix pa song rhawm song rhi tix,
mai ang lai koe krax pa caox rhawm ka pie ceeix kawx heue.
 
22 Maix kok pui koeng hoek tix gaux rhawm ka praok aux,
kieh krax lhat krax graung lai lawm ka aux khawm pon daog;
Ka daux singaix pa song rhawm maix,
ang koe pui pa pon tix luat luan.
Kix pauh ka kawn simex kawn bun pa muh pa hoik eei aux heue.
