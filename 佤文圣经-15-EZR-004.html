Tieb tie cox sum ka nyiex buan
4
Yam mhong pui pa koeng ka pui Yo-tax mai pui Piien-ya-ming grawng cox sum pui pa vuk vied vuk viang nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh nyiex buan Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
2 akix tom hoek ka Saw-law-pa-paw mai cao kaing ceu pui ku ceu khankix ah nin:
“Kieh yix yuh kiang pa cox sum pex nyiex buan liak.
Pa krub yix mai pex hoik mawh Siyiex tix kaux khaing.
Jauh tom viex Vawng Ya-sux pa kaux Yi-sha-ha-toeng yix hoek dee in,
yix kawn ca tan lu krax tan ka Siyiex ang jud heue.”
3 Saw-law-pa-paw,
Yie-su-yax,
mai cao kaing ceu pui ku ceu khankix krai ka kix ah nin:
“Yix ang siao lo krax pa teem coi pex hoek sum ka nyiex buan Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh nyiex buan Siyiex yix.
To kaux yix sang hawt tix sum awm lox pa hoik tawx Sai-lu-seex Vawng Paw-see heue.”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
pui haktiex keud tix sang tieb tie mai ghlat ka pui Yo-thaix,
mai ang kieh kix sum.
5 Pui an kix baox tawx mau chun praok ka simiang Paw-see,
hu tieb tie ka kix,
jauh yam giex Vawng Sai-lu-seex ka cao kaing tom hoek yam giex Vawng Ta-lio-seeb ka kaing,
hoik tuk tiak yuh nin ang jud heue.
Tieb tie cox sum ka veng Yie-lu-sa-leung
6 cYam jauh Vawng Ya-ha-sui-lu tix giex kaing,
pui pa koeng ka pui haktiex Yo-tax mai pui Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik viex lai hu deuh phi map ka kix heue.
7 Yam an,
mawh yam giex Ya-ta-shiie-si Vawng Paw-see kaing,
Pi-see-lang,
Mi-theu-li-ta,
Tha-piiex,
mai paox cub caw nawh hoik tawx lai ka Vawng;
lox lai in hoik tiiem ka ngai lai Ya-lang,d
yam phat ka taug dee thut lih mawh lox Paw-seee heue.
8 Simiang haktiex pa kaux Li-hong mai simiang haktiex pa tiiem lai pa kaux Seung-soix kawx tiiem lai pa deuh phi map pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tix pheen plak greem,
mai tawx ka Vawng Ya-ta-shiie-si ah nin:
9 “Simiang haktiex pa kaux Li-hong,
simiang haktiex pa tiiem lai pa kaux Seung-soix,
mai paox pa yuh yix simiang khankix,
khawm pui tang kaux pa gah tix hoek khaing Yi-lix,
Pa-pi-loeng,
mai Su-sang haktiex Yi-lang.
10 Kawn koe pa mawh,
pui haoh pui hun pa kaoh khaing haktiex Ya-see-na-pa an kix pa koe min mai gah tix hu ot ka veng Sa-ma-li-yax mai haktiex Heu-si,f
rom tix tawx lai in heue.”
11 Gum lox ka daux lai mawh nin:
“Kawn pui ka daux haktiex Heu-si tawx lai ka Vawng Ya-ta-shiie-si heue.
12 Yix simie tix kieh Vawng tawng:
ka daux haktiex pa kuang Vawng tang dauh khankix,
pui Yo-thaix pa gah tix hoek ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung cox sum ka veng pa caox pa caig pa tieh vheen an.
Kix hoik jauh tix sum ka guh veng,
kaing yuh kix hoik dex sang si dauh heue.
13 Vawng eue,
phan veng in hoik sum tix mhawm,
guh veng hoik hoik,
pui an kix tag ang cu tix tawx mau koh keen ku ceu;
krao Vawng rien tix yawm hu.
14 Mawh kheu mawh ex pui yuh kaing been,
mai khaw tix yuh kaing pa khaw tix yuh ka Vawng,
ang rhawm kieh kaing in lih tix mawh,
kheu nan ex tom krai ka Vawng,
15 khiox khaw Vawng tawx lox nhie pui si kaw tix yaox ka lai tiiem simiang sivoe.
Yuh nin,
maix tag sang tawng grawng krax pa tieh vheen veng in ang jud,
jauh tom dix dix,
hoik laux yuh simiang mai cao kaing haktiex ku kaux.
Pui pa ot ka daux veng in hoik mawh pa nu tix priex tete.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui tom yieh ka veng in laux.
16 Ex hoik tawng,
phan veng in deeh cox sum tix mhawm,
guh veng hoik hoik,
Vawng tag ang lai pon baox giex gang ka haktiex Heu-si heue.”
17 Lox pawk Vawng tiiem plak greem ah nin:
“Li-hong simiang haktiex,
Seung-soix simiang haktiex pa tiiem lai,
mai simiang pa ot ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax khawm haktiex Heu-si,
kieh pex mhawm ot mhawm koe:
18 Lai pa tawx pex hoik koe pui pa thut lih,
mai hoik phat aux mhong.
19 Aux hoik tawx lox nhie pui si kaw,
rug yaox grawng pa hoik tieh vheen Yie-lu-sa-leung ka lox simiang tom dix dix;
veng an hoik naog ka pui pa yuh krax pa tieh vheen mai krax pa suk sag.
20 Simiang pa ting siawp sibrawm khankix hoik sax kuang ka dee an mai ka haktiex Heu-si khawm uik,
mai koh keen ku ceu.
21 Mawh kheu nan,
pex caw tix tawx lox hu,
tieb tie ka pa sum kix veng an,
krox tom hoik tawx aux lox.
22 Pex caw nied tix yuh ka kaing in,
ang pon tix dha bawg yam,
son ang aux sang baox yaox krax pa som nai tom nie heue.”
23 Li-hong,
Seung-soix,
mai paox pa yuh kix kaing simiang taug hoik mhong pa phat Vawng Ya-ta-shiie-si ka lox lai pa pawk,
tom hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung dum mawx dum an,
mai viex kraung naing tuk tiak ka pui Yo-thaix naox kaing yuh tix heue.
Baox jauh tix cox sum nyiex buan
24 gKaing pa cox sum ka nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik naox tix,
ang lai pon yuh,
tom hoek ka daux bawg yam pa kuang Ta-lio-see Vawng Paw-see ka daux ra num heue.
