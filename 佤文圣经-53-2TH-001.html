Lai Thie-sa-law-ni-cia Phuk Ra
1
aAux mawh Pao-law;
aux mai Si-la mai Thi-maw-thaix tiiem tix tawx lox lai ka ceu cao Thie-sa-law-ni-cia,
pa mawh kawn Khrit khankix pa dex dawt tau mai keeing Siyiex mai Simiang Ye-sux Khrit heue.
2 Kieh keeing ex Siyiex mai Simiang Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son mai pa nhim ka pex heue!
Krax prawng ca yam sang deeh hoek Khrit
3 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
ex caw tix yaog yaw ka Siyiex jheeng jheeng.
In mawh pa khaw tix yuh;
mawh kheu krax yum rhawm pex hoik haok huan,
krax pa ra lai muh pex paox tix kawx hoik thiem thie tix.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
ex yaw rang di pex ka ceu cao Siyiex ku dauh ku dee.
Yam kham pex krax pa tuk tiak tuk te,
krax kha rhawm khawm uik,
pex ca kawn koe krax pa yum rhawm,
ca kawn pon gun yeung;
in hoik mawh ceu pa yaw ex rang di ka heue.
5 In hoik mawh mhai sakse krax prawng ca pa rauh pa see Siyiex,
mai krai grawng pa hoik keung pex tix pon ka buan son meung Siyiex.
Pex hoik ca nied tix kham krax pa kha rhawm son meung in.
6 Siyiex tag sang yuh pa rauh pa see tix lih.
Nawh tag sang kieh pui pa tuk tiak tuk te ka pex khankix si pup krax pa kha rhawm;
7 nawh tag sang kieh pex pa kham krax kha rhawm in kix pon krax pa pluat ploi tau mai ex.
Yam lih jhi Simiang Ye-sux mai kawn cau Siyiex pa ting siawp sibrawm tix ka daux rao ngu,
Siyiex tag sang yuh kaing in lih.
8 Nawh tag sang ciyang ka pui an kix pa ang doh keud ka nawh,
pa ang nghiet ka gum lox buan pa dex dawt tau mai Simiang ex Ye-sux.
9 bSingaix yam deeh hoek Simiang Ye-sux,
pui an kix tag sang kham krax ciyang,
tag sang khan tix tang ot khaing plak sivoe ngai Simiang Ye-sux leeing ju leeing phan,
mai lih hu khaing min plak ngai pa ting nawh.
10 Ka daux singaix an,
nawh tag sang pon min plak ngai khaing daux kawm Khrit khankix,
mai pon krax pa yaog yaw kix.
Pex kawx tag sang dia tix ot ka daux kix,
mawh kheu hoik yum rhawm pex ka gum lox buan pa sidiex ex heue.
11 Son kaing ting in aux rawg buan son pex jheeng jheeng,
khiox khaw Siyiex ex ang kieh pex thiang ka lox pa kok nawh.
Kieh nawh teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix hoek teem coi ka pex,
yuh lih ka ceu pa mhawm pa hoik dawg simie rhawm pex tix yuh,
yuh lih ka kaing ceu pa mawh ka daux krax yum rhawm pex.
12 Yuh nin,
ceu kaux Simiang ex Ye-sux tom taug pon min plak ngai ka daux pex,
mai pex kawx tag sang pon min plak ngai khaing nawh;
in hoik mawh buan son pa hoek khaing Siyiex mai Simiang ex Ye-sux Khrit heue.
