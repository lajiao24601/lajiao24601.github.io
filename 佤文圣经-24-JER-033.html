Deeh baox krai gum lox son sang sum meung
33
Yam kawn ot aux ka daux thawng nyiex haw simiang,
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux.
2 Cao pa phan pa yuh pa kieh haktiex lih tix mawh,
pa kieh haktiex lih tix kaux cong an si kah ka aux.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua.
Nawh ah nin:
3 “Maix khiox khaw kok yo ka aux,
aux tag sang pawk kah;
aux tag sang krai kaing ceu pa ang maix tawng pa kied raux mox tix ka maix.
4 Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
nyiex ma pa koe daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai nyiex haw pui Yo-tax,
pui tag sang lai lawm tix puing,
tag sang yieh laux leeing khawm uik.
5 Koe pui tix blah lih tieb tie pui Pa-pi-loeng ka plak praix veng;
pui Pa-pi-loeng tag sang thom ka to kaux yum pui pa pauh aux yum ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix naog daux veng in.
Mawh kheu krax pa caox pa yuh pui pa ot ka daux veng in,
mai aux tom tik vud ka veng in heue.
6 “Viang mawh nan,
aux tag sang pai ka pui pa ot daux veng in,
mai kieh kix deeh pon yaox tix moi.
Aux tag sang kieh kix pon naog ka daux krax pa nhim pa tiiem,
mai ka daux krax pa nyawm ot.
7 Aux tag sang kieh pui Yo-tax mai pui Yi-seu-liex nyaox tix deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix,
mai deeh cox sum tix ot ka nyiex ma.
8 Aux tag sang si ghrah ka phi map krax pa lud loe kix ka aux khawm uik,
mai pluat ploi ka phi map krax pa tieh vheen kix ka aux khawm uik.
9 Yie-lu-sa-leung tag caw tix kieh aux gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
pon krax pa yung yi,
pon min plak ngai.
Yam mhong pui haktiex khawm uik ka pa tawx aux buan son ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
hoik kieh veng in deeh cox tix haok huan,
kix hoik si yaung si kua khawm uik heue.”
10 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Pui ku kaux hoik krai sang lih haktiex in tix mawh daux tiag pa riem,
ang koe pui,
mai to tiag kawx ang koe kah.
Mawh tete,
meung Yo-tax ku dauh,
mai ka daux laih ting laih iak pa koe daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung hoik riem khawm uik,
to tiag ang koe kah,
pui kawx ang koe kah.
Viang mawh nan,
pex tag sang baox mhong ka lox nyiah pui dee in
11 amai ka lox yuh pui krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi daux poi ka paox.
Pex kawx tag sang mhong ka lox rhax pa yuh pui Krax-tan Pa-yaog-yaw ka daux nyiex buan;
kix tag sang rhax ah nin:
tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing heue!
Mawh kheu kied mhawm kied mhiem nawh;
krax muh nawh koe leeing ju leeing phan heue.
Aux tag sang kieh pex deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix,
mai deeh cox sum tix ot ka nyiex ma.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
12 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing krai yuh nin:
“Haktiex pa riem in,
pa mawh haktiex pa ang pui mai to tiag koe kah,
tag sang deeh lih tix mawh dee liang pui khawm brie,
pui liang khawng brie liang yung dee an.
13 Veng iak pa koe ka piang gawng piang lua,
ka yaong pa koe piang gawng,
ka baoh haktiex Yo-tax plak seh,
mai baoh haktiex Piien-ya-ming ku mu,
ka nyiex yaong pa koe ka sivie siveng Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mai ka veng Yo-tax ku mu,
pui liang khawng brie tag sang baox simin ka yung tix bawg.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
14 bSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Yam sang hoek bawg yam,
aux tag sang yuh lih ka lox pa ap aux tix krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai ka pui Yo-tax.
15 Yam an,
aux tag sang yaog ka ‘kak khaox’ pa rauh pa see son Ta-wue;
nawh tag sang teei pa khaw caw,
mai pa rauh pa see priex meung mhawm ka daux haktiex in.
16 Ka daux pa giex nawh kaing,
pui Yo-tax tag sang nyawm ot nyawm koe,
pui Yie-lu-sa-leung pon ka krax pa nhim pa tiiem.
Veng in tag sang kok mawh ‘Simiang Siyiex pa mawh pa rauh pa see ex’.
17 cAux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ap tix deuh yuh nin:
doeh ceu cad Ta-wue tag sang giex kaing meung Yi-seu-liex leeing ju leeing phan;
18 dceu pui Li-we tag sang mawh cao giex kaing li leeing ju,
yuh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing,
Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
mai pa viex Krax-tan Khawng-brie hoik tan lu ka aux heue.”
19 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
20 “Aux hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pon ngaix pon sawm,
mai kieh nawh lai ling tix hu awm bawg yam;
sijax mukheu in ang doh pon tix tik kah.
21 Awm an,
sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai siliex aux Ta-wue,
hoik kieh meung pa giex nawh kaing piang koe leeing ju.
Aux kawx hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pui Li-we,
mai kieh kix mawh cao giex kaing li yuh kaing jhaw aux leeing ju;
sijax mukheu in kawx ang doh pon tix tik vud.
22 Aux tag sang kieh doeh ceu cad siliex tix Ta-wue,
mai kaux pui cao giex kaing li Li-we thiem thie tix haoh hun,
awm simuing daux rao ma,
mu mhaik ka praok rawm sigrit sigra yuh nan,
mai ang lai uik simin ka heue.”
23 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
24 “Hoik tawng maix ka lie?
Pui hoik krai tik vud aux ka meung haktiex pa hoik laug aux ra mu,
pa mawh meung Yi-seu-liex mai meung Yo-tax.
Kix hoik map nai ka pui cie aux,
ang een kix mawh meung haktiex tix mu mu.
25 Khaix an,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pon ngaix pon sawm;
aux hoik yuh ku ceu ku cawng pa koe khankix lih koe ka daux rao ma haktiex.
26 Sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh aux tau mai ku ceu ku cawng pa koe khankix,
ang pon tix tik vud;
aux kawx hoik giex kaux cong ka sijax mukheu pa yuh aux tau mai doeh ceu cad Ya-keux,
mai siliex tix Ta-wue.
Aux tag sang laug simiang tix kaux lih khaing daux doeh ceu cad Ta-wue,
mai kieh nawh hoek tix giex kaing ka piang kawn praix Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
mai Ya-keux.
Aux tag sang kia ngien kix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm tix,
kieh kix deeh ing ka haktiex ot tix,
mai deeh cox sum tix ot ka nyiex ma heue.”
