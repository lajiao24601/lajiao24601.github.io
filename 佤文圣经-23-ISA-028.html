Ne raw ka meung pa ot plak laong
28
Meung pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang laux leeing heue!
Min plak ngai nawh hoik grai,
hoik awm mhok tai pa ceep simiang pui pa yoek ka plai yuh nan.
Kix hoik phruah rawm sidah bhawng ka piang kaing pui pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi an,
viang mawh nan,
kix hoik yoek,
mai hoik goh it piang tiex.
2 Simiang Siyiex hoik rien khre pui pa ting riang ting rao an sang hoek tix puing ka kix.
Yam hoek nawh,
hoik awm bhaung ting,
lhiex ting,
pa kheem rawm ting chun ka haktiex uik.
3 Simiang pui pa hoik yoek ka plai khankix,
tag sang dig tix ot greem caong pui;
4 min plak ngai pa pruh riang kix hoik grai,
mai hoik awm khaox plix ruh pa taug jauh tix teem yam jauh praix tix rung,
yam jauh tix teem mai pui hoik pieh tix ih ka heue.
5 Ka daux singaix an,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
tag sang awm mhok tai pa kied mhawm yuh nan,
mai ceep ka kaing kawn pui pa kawn deuk khankix.
6 Nawh tag sang tawx rhawm pa khaw caw ka simiang cao prawng see,
tawx rhawm pa kua ka cao pa pau ka diang siviex veng,
son sang pon tieb tie ka pui pa koeng heue.
Yi-sai-yax mai cao jhai yoek pui Yo-tax
7 Cao jhai mai cao giex kaing li kawx hoik yoek mai vawk jee vawk blaong;
kix hoik kied nie nyaux ka plai,
mai hoik awm pui daox aw yuh nan.
Cao jhai khankix hoik kied sidaing yoek,
mai ang lai tawng ka riang su sai pa tawx Siyiex ka kix,
cao giex kaing li kawx hoik nyaux ka plai tom ang lai tawng praix.
8 Kawk plai pa ot ka piang pheen,
hoik mawh hao ceu pa seeing pa brai khawm uik heue.
9 Pui haoh pui hun salao ka aux ah nin:
“Cao jhai in hoik gau ceu pa tix lie?
Pui mawx sang nghiet ka lox nawh lie?
Nawh tee ca krai ngie ka kawn ngie pa khraox pluat peux khaing heue!
10 Nawh keud tix kawn sang gau ka yix ceu mawx ceu an,
grawng mawx grawng an,
ngai mawx ngai an lie!”
11 aPhan pex rub ang nghiet ka lox aux,
Siyiex tag sang tawx pui meung tang meung hoek,
mai kix tag sang teei gum lox pa daox pa deng sidiex gau ka pex heue.
12 Nawh hoik tawx krax pa yien pa yawg pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
pex hoik tieb tie ka nawh.
13 Yam in Simiang Siyiex tag sang sidiex gau ka pex ceu mawx ceu an,
grawng mawx grawng an,
ngai mawx ngai an.
Pex tag sang hu tix diang,
mai jang klad tix goh;
pex tag sang sideeing tix goh mat,
mai lih mawh pui pa giex laung pui heue.
Jaong simaox Shi-ang
14 Yam in,
pex pa mawh simiang pa kuang ka piang pui Yie-lu-sa-leung,
pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi khankix eue,
nghiet ka lox pa hoik krai Simiang Siyiex ka pex heue!
15 Pex ce tix hoik yuh lox sijax mukheu tau mai krax yum,
tau mai meung yum,
mawh kheu nan ce tix hoik mawh pui pa luan khaing daux krax yum;
pex ce tix sang chix pon krax pa luat luan kheu lox pie lox lhien tix.
16 bYam in Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong sang ah nin ka pex:
“Aux tag sang sum num jaong pa kaux cong ot ka Shi-ang,
mai deuh jaong simaox pa ngawh dee an,
mai tiiem ngai lai ot piang ah nin:
‘Pui pa koe krax yum rhawm tag sang kaux cong ang yung yang’.
17 Ka piang num jaong in,
aux tag sang kieh pa rauh pa see mawh mhai diag,
kieh pa mawh pa meh mawh diag heue.”
Mu pre tag sang lauh max ka lox pie lox lhien pa hoik een pex rhawm tix kah;
pa huan rawm ting tag sang kheem ka krax pa nyawm ot pex.
18 Lox sijax mukheu pa hoik yuh pex tau mai krax yum hoik nai,
pa hoik yuh tau mai meung yum ang lai koe ceu.
Yam hoek krax laux leeing,
pex tom yum heue.
19 Krax laux krax leeing in tix bawg hoik tix bawg lauh ka pex,
pex tag sang kham gee krax kha rhawm leeing ngaix leeing sawm.
Gum lox khraox pa hoik tawx Siyiex ka pex ku bawg,
mai hoik viex krax lhat krax graung pa khraox hoek ku bawg heue.
20 Awm gum lox kab pa hoik tiiem ah nin:
“Phan ku it ngaing,
mai ang chix nyat ka caong tix;
phan prux iak pak,
mai ang pon lhop ka to kaux khawm uik heue.”
21 cViang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang awm yam ot tix yuh naing ka gawng Pi-la-sing mai ka ngawd Ci-piien yuh nan,
mai nawh hoik yaog tix kaoh yuh kaing yuh tix lih;
in hoik mawh kaing pa ang pui bax doh keud kah.
Nawh tag sang yuh kaing cie tix pa raux pa mox an lih tix mawh heue.
22 Mawh kheu nan,
yam ne raw aux ka pex,
mai pex ang pon tix nyiah ang koe ceu,
phan ang mawh nan,
pex tag nie nu sang luat luan khaing krax laux krax leeing in.
Aux hoik mhong kah,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing,
tag sang yuh ka meung haktiex laux leeing khawm uik heue.
Ping nyia cuyi cie Siyiex
23 Pex tag caw tix bre rhawm rhi tix nghiet ka lox aux,
mai priex rhawm tix ka lox pa hoik krai aux ka pex.
24 Yam yuh pui kaing haktiex ka ma kaung,
khex sang thai kix tiex bhaw ngie khaing lie?
25 Yam hoik thai nawh ma,
tom taug rhuat ka simie,
sum ka lhax ki ting mai lhax ki iak.
Nawh tag sang sum ka nghox ca mai nghox ca pa lang mu tia mawx tia an,
khaix an mai sum nghox tang ceu khankix ot ka daog kaung heue.
26 Pui pa yuh kaing haktiex hoik tawng ka grawng sang sum tix kaung ma,
mawh kheu hoik gau Siyiex ka nawh.
27 Nawh ang siao khaox bauh pa ting,
mai pauh ka lhax ki ting mai lhax ki iak;
khaox bauh pa siao nawh mawh pa iak pa hoik khaw mai diag.
28 Nawh kawx ang siao ka ling pa klia bhaung ang jud,
pa kieh nghox ca laux phawng.
Nawh hoik tawng grawng sang grheu ka ling,
mai briex koi kieh pum nghox ca lu lie tix hu,
kieh bliah kam nghox ca mai mu nghox ca tang hu khaing paox tix,
mai ang yaox tix kieh nghox ca laux.
29 Ping nyia cuyi in hoik mawh pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing ka nawh.
Krax keud krax sawn Siyiex kied mawh pa daox pa deng tete;
ping nyia cuyi nawh kied mawh pa ting pa lhaong heue!
