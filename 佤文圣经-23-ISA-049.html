Pa riang min plak ngai meung haktiex
49
aPui pa ot klawn tiex khankix eue,
pex hoek tix nghiet heue!
Pui pa ot dee singai eue,
pex nghiet ka aux khawm uik eue!
Sivoe sang keh aux,
Simiang Siyiex hoik laug ka aux;
sivoe ang aux nyang bax lih khaing daux vaig miex tix,
nawh hoik kok yo aux ka daux ceu kaux tix.
2 bNawh hoik kieh lox aux lih lawm awm vaig;
nawh teei taix tix kia ngien ka aux.c
Nawh hoik phan aux lawm awm te yuh nan,
chix tix puing ku bawg ku yam.
3 Nawh ah nin ka aux:
Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix mawh siliex aux;
pui ku kaux tag sang yaog yaw aux kheu maix heue.
 
4 Aux ah nin:
aux sau riang rao tix yuh kaing,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe ceu pie ceeix kawx;
aux hoik sau riang rao tix khawm uik,
mai ca kawn ang tie chix mawh tix ceu kawx.
Viang mawh nan,
aux chix tix een rhawm ka Simiang Siyiex son sang prawng rauh see son aux;
Siyiex chix hawt tix tawx khawng phak ka aux awm kaing pa yuh aux heue.
 
5 Sivoe sang keh aux,
Simiang Siyiex hoik tawx aux mawh siliex tix;
aux sang thung Ya-keux pui cie aux deeh hoek,
thung pui Yi-seu-liex pa rai rhuat tix ot ku dauh ku dee deeh hoek.
Simiang Siyiex tawx pa riang min plak ngai ka aux;
Siyiex mawh num dee kaoh riang rao aux heue.
 
6 dSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka aux:
siliex aux eue,
aux tawx kaing pa ting ceu ka maix tix ceu:
aux tag sang kieh maix yaog ceu pui Ya-keux haok huan,
thung kawn praix Yi-seu-liex deeh hoek.
Aux kawn sang kieh maix lih mawh pa riang min plak ngai meung piang haktiex khawm uik,
kieh krax taoh aux tauh hu ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
7 Cao pa taoh Yi-seu-liex,
Siyiex pa singax singeh ah nin:
pui pa kham krax map nai,
pa koeng pui meung ka piang haktiex,
pui pa yuh siliex pa giex pui kaing khankix eue,
simiang tag sang cung tix yuh krax pa nab yam yung yi ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
siawp sibrawm kawx tag sang krub ka maix heue.
 
Kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh tete,
mawh kheu hoik laug Siyiex pa singax singeh Yi-seu-liex ka maix;
Simiang Siyiex tag sang giex kaux cong ka lox pa hoik deuh tix heue.
Pa deeh cox sum ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
8 eSimiang Siyiex an nin ka pui cie tix:
bawg yam pa rieb ka maix hoik hoek,
aux tag sang nghiet ka lox maix;
bawg yam pa taoh ka maix hoik hoek,
aux tag sang teem coi ka maix.
Aux tag sang kia ngiien ka maix,
simi ka maix,
yuh sijax mukheu tau mai kawn pui piang haktiex ka daux maix.
Aux tag sang kieh maix deeh hoek ka haktiex pa riem an,
kieh maix deeh cox sum ka nyiex ma tix
9 Aux tag sang ah nin ka pui pa giex pui tix laung khankix:
pex hoik pon krax pa pheeik luan heue!
Ah nin ka pui pa ot ka daux pa faik:
pex deeh yaox pa riang heue!
Kix hoik awm kawn yung pa sawm rib ka daux pak rib yuh nan;
gawng lhaong taug mawh pak rib kix heue.
10 fKix ang chix cai rhawm ang simie tix nyaux rawm.
Riang singaix pa kied hax,
ang pon yuh kix laux;
mawh kheu pui pa muh ka kix tag sang thung ka kix;
nawh tag sang duk thung kix hoek ka dee koe rawm keet heue.
 
11 Aux tag sang paoh daux krax koe ka gawng lhaong,
yuh daux krax ting tix nyaing tawx ka pui cie tix.
12 Pui cie aux tag sang hoek khaing dee singai;
jauh tix hoek khaing plak klip singaix,
khaing plak laong,
mai baox jauh khaing plak seh haktiex Si-nig heue.
 
13 Rao ma eue,
tag caw tix rhax lai heue!
Haktiex eue,
tag caw tix gaux rhawm heue!
Gawng lua khankix eue,
tag caw tix ah lox pa ting si num hu heue!
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tag sang tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka pui cie tix,
tag sang caox rhawm ka pui cie tix pa kham krax kha rhawm heue.
 
14 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa ot haktiex Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex hoik tik vud ka ex heue!
Simiang Siyiex hoik piawm ka ex heue!
15 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
pa mawh miex khankix sang chix piawm tix tawx kawn tix peux lie?
Nawh sang chix ang muh ka kawn simex tix lie?
Viang pa mawh miex khankix hoik piawm ka kawn tix kawx,
aux ang doh piawm ka maix heue!
16 Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
aux ang doh piawm ka maix;
aux hoik tiiem ceu kaux maix ka daux rhawm tix heue.
 
17 Pui pa sang deeh cox sum ka maix tag sang khran hoek;
pui pa sang yuh maix laux leeing hoik sang khran hu.
18 Blah ngai maix jhak tix yaox ka khawm pon daog heue!
Paox ceu paox cad maix hoik khrawm paox tix;
kix tag sang deeh hoek ka nyiex ma tix heue!
Aux tiex tu ka daux ju tix ah nin:
maix tag sang pon min plak ngai ka paox ceu paox cad tix,
hoik awm yaog yaw bun pa sang yuh ka paox ka siang cie tix heue.
 
19 Meung haktiex maix hoik sax yaox krax pa yuh pui laux leeing;
yam in nawh ang lai kub kieh pui pa deeh hoek laig ot ka daux.
Pui pa tuk tiak tuk te ka maix sivoe tag sang singai hu khaing maix.
20 Koe tix singaix,
pui pa keh khaing daux pui pa vuk vied vuk viang khankix tag sang ah nin ka maix:
haktiex in kied iak tete;
ex kawn sang lo ka haktiex tom nie heue!
21 Yam an maix tag sang ah nin ka to kaux tix:
pui mawx sang pon keh kawn in kix tom nie tang aux lie?
Kawn aux hoik yum yam keh tix khawm uik,
aux hoik dut krax pa im ju;
aux hoik to mox tix ot plak praix,
mai vu vie ku dauh ku dee,
mawh pui mawx pa priex kawn nyawm in kix ting lie?
Aux ot nong nong tix mai ang koe paox koe pie;
kawn nyawm in kix kaoh khaing dee mawx lie?
22 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong ah nin ka pui cie tix:
aux tag sang kieh meung ka piang haktiex tawng,
tawx lox ka kawn pui piang haktiex khawm uik;
kix tag sang klien ka kawn simex maix,
pux ka kawn bun maix hoek.
23 Simiang meung ka piang haktiex tag sang mawh keeing pa eei ka kawn simex kawn bun maix;
moeng simiang tag sang mawh miex kawn maix.
Kix tag sang krub ka plak sivoe ngai maix,
lied ka phawng tiex pa kawb caong maix.
Yam an maix taug tawng aux mawh Simiang Siyiex;
pui pa krox jun aux teem coi tix ang koe krax laux rhawm laux rhi heue.
 
24 Maix pon teei kraung naing khaing daux tawm taix pui naing pa kua lie?
Maix pon taoh pui pa hoik giex laung khaing daux tawm taix pui pon pex lie?
 
25 Simiang Siyiex pawk lox ah nin:
Kaing pa yuh nin hoik dex sang lih tix mawh.
Pui pa hoik giex pui tix laung ot ka daux tawm taix pui naing pa kua tag sang pon tix taoh;
kraung naing pa koe ka daux tawm taix simiang pa caox tag sang pon teei kah.
Aux tag sang tieb tie ka pui pa koeng ka maix;
aux tag sang taoh ka kawn simex kawn bun maix.
26 Aux tag sang kieh pui pa tuk tiak tuk te ka maix ra lai pauh ka paox tix;
kix hoik yoek goh kheu nyaux tix nham to kaux tix,
Yam an ka piang haktiex hoik tawng khawm uik,
aux mawh Simiang Siyiex pa taoh ka maix;
aux mawh cao taoh maix,
mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex pa ting riang ting rao heue.
