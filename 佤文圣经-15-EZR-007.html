Yi-see-la hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
7
Khaix hoik luan daux ra loe num,
Ya-ta-shiie-si yuh Vawng Paw-see,
Yi-see-la hoek khaing Pa-pi-loeng.
Tax du nawh chix tix si kaw tom hoek ka Ya-loeng simiang cao giex kaing li pa ting;
ru ceu cad cub nyiex ma nawh tiiem ot plak greem:
Yi-see-la mawh kawn simex Si-lai-ya,
Si-lai-ya mawh kawn simex Ya-sa-li-ya,
Ya-sa-li-ya mawh kawn simex Si-leu-cia.
2 Si-leu-cia mawh kawn simex Sa-long,
Sa-long mawh kawn simex Sa-tu,
Sa-tu mawh kawn simex Ya-si-thu,
3 Ya-si-thu mawh kawn simex Ya-ma-li-ya,
Ya-ma-li-ya mawh kawn simex Ya-sa-li-ya,
Ya-sa-li-ya mawh kawn simex Mi-la-yaw,
4 Mi-la-yaw mawh kawn simex Si-la-si-ya,
Si-la-si-ya mawh kawn simex Wu-si,
Wu-si mawh kawn simex Pu-ci.
5 Pu-ci mawh kawn simex Ya-pi-su,
Ya-pi-su mawh simex Fe-ni-ha,
Fe-ni-ha mawh kawn simex Yi-li-ya-sa,
Yi-li-ya-sa mawh kawn simex Ya-loeng heue.
6 Yi-see-la mawh pui pa kied koe ping nyia tix kaux,
mai hoik grum tawng ka lox tat jhawm prawng pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ka Maw-si.
Mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex Yi-seu-liex hoik tawx buan son ka Yi-see-la,
Yi-see-la rawg mhaing kaing ceu pa tix ang ah ka Vawng,
Vawng hoik cu ka lox nawh.
7 Yam giex Ya-ta-shiie-si cao kaing ka daux aliah num,
Yi-see-la yaog tix hu tau mai cub pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Pa-pi-loeng,
deeh hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Cub pui in kix khawm cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
pui pa kied chix rhax lai ka daux nyiex buan,
pui pa pau ka nyiex buan,
mai pui yuh kaing daux nyiex buan khawm uik.
8-9 Yam lih hu kix khaing Pa-pi-loeng mawh haok khix tix tix ngaix;
kheu pon kix krax pa teem coi Siyiex,
kix tom yaox tix hoek ka Yie-lu-sa-leung ka haok khix phuan tix ngaix.
10 Ka daux ju Yi-see-la nawh meung tix keud sawn mai hawt tix yuh ka lox tat jhawm prawng Simiang Siyiex,
mai baox sidiex gau lox tat jhawm prawng ka pui hun Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.
Vawng Ya-ta-shiie-si tawx lai ka Yi-see-la
11 Vawng Ya-ta-shiie-si tawx lai in plak greem ka Yi-see-la;
Yi-see-la mawh cao giex kaing li mai pui pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai,
hoik grum tawng gum lox ping nyap mai lox tat jhawm prawng pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex heue.
12 Aux,
pa mawh Ya-ta-shiie-si,
simiang piang simiang,
tawx lai ka Yi-see-la cao giex kaing li pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai pa hoik tawx Siyiex piang rao ma grum koe krax lhak lhiao. a
13 Aux tawx lox nhie pui Yi-seu-liex,
cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
pa ot ka daux meung,
can pui pa gaux rhawm tix sang deeh hoek,
chix tix ing tau mai maix ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
14 Aux,
khawm pui cau pa ot greem aux aliah kaux,
tawx maix hu si kaw ka grawh grawng Yie-lu-sa-leung mai grawh grawng Yo-tax,
jhak tix yaox ka pui hun priex ang priex tix hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex pa hoik tawx ka maix.
15 Maix caw tix viex mau khri pa tan lu aux mai pui cau pa ot greem aux gaux rhawm tix tawx ka Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka daux nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung an.
16 Maix kawx caw tix viex nang ka mau khri pa hoik koh maix khaing haktiex Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik,
khawm khawng phak lak sawng pa tang tan lu pui Yi-seu-liex mai cao giex kaing li khankix ka nyiex buan Siyiex Yie-lu-sa-leung.
17 Maix caw priex rhawm tix siao mau in kix liag moi mhaing,
yung mhaing,
kawn yung,
nghox koi,
mai plai,
mai tan lu ka piang khu dee tan nyiex buan Yie-lu-sa-leung.
18 Mau khri pa kawn deuk,
maix chix tix hawt tix yuh awm alo Siyiex,
siao ka daux pa keud maix khaw mai ka paox ceu paox cad maix.
19 Maix caw tix tan lu can kraung khrai pa siao ka nyiex buan pa hoik tawx ka maix ka Siyiex pa ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
20 Lheu khaing an,
kraung khrai tang ceu pa sang lo nyiex buan khankix,
mai chix teei tix siao lih khaing daux krao heue.
21 Aux tawx lox nhie can simiang cao giex mau ka daux haktiex Heu-si,
nied tix tawx kraung ku ceu pa sang lo pui pa koe ping nyia tix kah lai,
pa grum tawng lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex piang rao ma,
pa mawh cao giex kaing li Yi-see-la.
22 Diag dong been sang caw tix tawx hoek mawh:
mau luah loe rheng pon yieh kongcing,
nghox ca tix mheen kongcing,
plai ra rheng kongsheng;
bee sime ra rheng kongsheng;
phan mawh kih ang siao krai.
23 Can pa nhie Siyiex piang rao ma pex yuh ka son nyiex buan nawh,
pex caw gaux rhawm tix yuh.
Yuh nin,
nawh tom taug ang lai song rhawm song rhi ka aux,
mai ka pui pa sang plud tix mawh simiang khaix aux.
24 Pex ang pon tix koh keen ka cao giex kaing li,
pui Li-we,
pui pa kied chix rhax lai,
pui pa pau nyiex buan,
pui yuh kaing,
mai pui pa koe beeh glawm ka nyiex buan in ku kaux heue.
25 “Yi-see-la,
maix caw tix siao ping nyia cuyi pa tawx Siyiex ka maix,
tawx simiang pa yuh kaing been mai simiang cao prawng see,
hu kuang kia ka pui hun haktiex Heu-si khawm uik,
pa mawh pui hun pa hoik priex tix hawt lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex maix.
Maix tag caw tix sidiex gaux lox tat jhawm prawng an ka pui pa ang tawng khankix.
26 Phan koe pui pa ang hawt ka lox tat jhawm prawng Siyiex,
mai lox tat jhawm prawng piang meung in,
tag caw tix kieh hoek krax ciyang tom kin dum mawx dum an:
pauh yum,
phan ang mawh nan,
grheu hu,
phan ang mawh nan,
teei ka sin ngao,
phan ang mawh nan,
laung daux thawng heue.”
Yi-see-la Yaog yaw Siyiex
27 Yi-see-la ah nin:
“Kieh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex tax du ex pon krax pa yaog yaw!
Nawh kieh Vawng tang viex rhawm tix siao grawng krax in,
kieh nyiex buan Siyiex daux Yie-lu-sa-leung pon min plak ngai.
28 Buan son Siyiex hoik kieh aux pon krax pa teem coi siawp sibrawm khaing Vawng,
mai pui cau pa ot greem nawh khankix,
khawm can simiang pa ot greem nawh khankix.
Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux tawx aux koe riang,
mai kieh aux pon khrawm ka simiang cub ceu pui Yi-seu-liex deeh hoek tau mai aux tom nie heue.”
