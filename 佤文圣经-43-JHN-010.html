Phek tiiem ka kawg yung
10
Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex sidawng,
pa ang laig ka daux siviex kawg yung,
viang mawh nan,
preed tix laig ka tang dauh,
mawh pui briex mawh pui con.
2 Pa laig ka daux siviex yung,
tag mawh cao liang yung.
3 Pui pau siviex paoh siviex son nawh;
yung cie nawh tawng yung ka lox nawh.
Nawh hawt tix kok ceu kaux yung cie tix,
mai thung kix lih hoek.
4 Nawh thung yung cie tix lih hoek,
mai hu ka plak sivoe ngai kix;
yung cie nawh hawt ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik tawng yung kix ka lox nawh.
5 Yung cie nawh khankix ang hawt ka pui pa ang kix tawng yung,
tag sang tieh tix to hu,
mawh kheu ang kix tawng yung ka lox pui pa ang kix tawng heue.”
6 Ye-sux krai gum lox phek tiiem in ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng dee bleeh lox pa krai nawh mawh ceu pa tix heue.
Ye-sux cao liang yung pa mhawm
7 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux baox ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai ka pex sidawng,
aux mawh siviex yung.
8 Can pui pa hoek sivoe aux hoik mawh pui briex pui con khawm uik;
yung ang nghiet ka lox kix.
9 Aux mawh siviex yung;
pui pa laig ka daux aux,
tag sang pon tix kaux cong,
khaix an tag chix tix lih lih laig laig,
tag sang baox yaox ka pak rib.
10 Pui briex phan laig mawh son sang briex lak,
son sang pauh yung,
son sang yuh laux leeing.
Kaing ting aux hoek mawh son sang kieh kix pon krax pa im ju,
kheu nan son sang nie pon krax pa im ju tom nie heue.
11 “Aux mawh cao liang pa mhawm;
cao liang pa mhawm cu tix tawx ju tix son yung khankix.
12 Pui cang pui caing ang mawh cao liang,
yung kawx ang mawh cie to kaux nawh.
Yam yaox nawh hoek sox la,
nawh tag sang ghram yung mai yaog tix to;
sox la tag sang giex ka yung,
mai kieh yung khankix prung prang tix hu.
13 Pui cang pui caing hoik to,
mawh kheu hoik mawh kix pui cang pui caing khaing,
kix ang keud ka yung khankix.
14-15 aAux mawh cao liang pa mhawm.
Awm hoik tawng yung keeing ka aux,
aux hoik tawng yung ka keeing.
Awm an,
aux hoik tawng yung ka yung cie tix;
kix kawx hoik tawng yung ka aux nang.
Aux cu tix tawx ju tix son kix.
16 Aux kawn koe yung tang mu pa ang ot ka daux kawg yung,
aux kawx tag sang thung kix lih hoek;
kix tag sang nghiet ka lox aux.
Kix ra cub tag sang lih mawh phung yung tix cub,
cao liang tix kaux heue.
17 “Keeing cao muh ka aux;
mawh kheu hoik cu aux tix tawx ka ju to kaux tix,
mai son sang baox pon ka ju.
18 Ang koe pui pa pon teei hu ka ju aux,
mai hoik mawh to kaux aux pa cu tix tawx hu;
aux koe akhuang tix tawx ju tix hu,
mai koe akhuang tix deeh teei hoek.
In hoik mawh gum lox pa tawm keeing aux yuh heue.”
19 Pui Yo-thaix baox simah paox tix ka son lox in kix.
20 Ka daux kix koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Nawh mawh phi pa laig daux heue!
Nawh hoik yhot heue!
Nghiet yuh tix ka nawh lie?”
21 Baox koe pui tix blah ah nin:
“Pui pa laig phi daux ang pon krai lox pa yuh nin lih heue!
Phi sang pon paoh ka ngai pui dug riang lie?”
Pui Yo-thaix tik vud ka Ye-sux
22 Bawg yam pa yuh poi pa ap pong ka nyiex buan hoik hoek;
bawg yam an,
mawh bawg yam kuat.
23 Ye-sux lai ling hu ka greem grah Saw-law-meung ka daux nyiex buan;
24 pui Yo-thaix lai lawm ka nawh,
mai krai ka nawh ah nin:
“Sang kieh maix yix ang tawng ka tix hoek yam mawx lie?
Krai ka yix sidawng,
mawh ang maix mawh Khrit lie?”
25 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Aux hoik krai ka pex,
viang mawh nan,
pex ang yum rhawm kah.
Kaing pa hoik yuh aux ka daux ceu kaux keeing aux hoik mawh pa kham sakse aux.
26 Viang mawh nan,
pex ang mawh yung cie aux,
mawh kheu nan pex tom ang yum rhawm.
27 Yung cie aux mhong ka lox aux,
aux tawng yung ka kix;
kix hawt ka aux.
28 Aux tawx ju leeing ju ka kix,
kix tag ang lai hoek krax laux krax leeing;
pui mawx ang ah ang pon teei kix hu khaing daux tawm taix aux.
29 Can ceu pa tawx keeing aux ka aux kawn lheu ting khaing ku ceu khawm uikb,
ang koe pui pa pon teei kix hu khaing daux tawm taix keeing aux.
30 Keeing cao mai aux hoik dawg mawh kaux tix mu heue.”
31 Yam an,
pui Yo-thaix baox teei simaox sang tawk nawh kah.
32 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux hoik yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm tom nie ka plak sivoe ngai pex,
pa hoik nhie keeing aux yuh;
pex sang teei simaox tawk aux kah,
kheu son kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
33 cKix pawk ah nin:
“Yix ang doh sang teei simaox tawk maix ka son pa hoik yuh maix kaing ceu pa mhawm;
hoik mawh kheu pa map nai maix ka Siyiex heue!
Maix mawh ngie pui tix kaux khaing,
maix lai kieh to kaux tix mawh Siyiex heue!”
34 dYe-sux ah nin:
“Lox tat jhawm prawng pex ang hoik sax tiiem ah nin ‘pex mawh Siyiex’ lie?
35 Ex hoik tawng gum lox phuk lai sigang khri mawh pa ang chix tieh loh tix leeing ju leeing phan;
ka pui pa rieb ka gum lox Siyiex khankix,
Siyiex hoik kok kix mawh Siyiex.
36 Phan mawh son aux,
aux hoik mawh kaw pa laug keeing,
mai hoik ploi aux hoek ka piang haktiex.
Aux krai mawh tix kawn simex Siyiex,
yuh ka mawx pex lai ah aux map nai Siyiex lie?
37 Phan aux ang yuh kaing ceu pa nhie keeing aux yuh,
pex tag ang siao yum rhawm ka aux.
38 Phan aux krai yuh tix kah,
pex kawn ang yum rhawm ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix yum rhawm ka kaing ceu pa yuh aux,
son sang kieh pex tawng grawng hoik ot Siyiex ka daux ju aux tete,
aux kawx hoik ot ka daux ju keeing heue.”
39 Mawh kheu nan,
kix baox sang keud tix giex ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
nawh hoik pheeik luan khaing daux tawm taix kix heue.
40 eYe-sux baox deeh ing ka toi rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
mai hoek ka dee cub com Yaw-hang rawm sivoe an,
nawh paung ot dee an.
41 Koe pui tom nie hoek tix sawg ka nawh,
mai ah nin:
“Yaw-hang ang sax yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am,
viang mawh nan,
gum lox pa krai nawh son pui in khawm uik,
hoik mawh tete te lai heue.”
42 Ka dee an koe pui tom nie hoik yum rhawm ka Ye-sux heue.
