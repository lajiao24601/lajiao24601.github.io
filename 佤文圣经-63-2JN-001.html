Lai Yaw-hang Phuk Ra
1
Aux mawh kuad song;
aux tiiem tix tawx lox lai in ka maix,
pa mawh bun pa hoik laug kah,
mai baox tawx nang ka kawn simex kawn bun maix yuh nin:
Pex hoik mawh pui pa muh aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
Ja lao aux ang muh ka pex,
khawm can pui pa tawng yung ka pa mawh pa meh kawx hoik muh ka pex;
2 mawh kheu pa mawh pa meh ot ka daux rhawm ex,
mai tag sang ot tau mai ex leeing ju heue.
3 Kieh keeing ex Siyiex mai kawn simex nawh Ye-sux Khrit tawx buan son,
krax pa caox rhawm,
mai pa nhim ka ex,
kieh ex ot ka daux krax pa mawh pa meh nawh mai krax muh nawh tom si dauh heue!
Pa mawh pa meh mai krax muh
4 Aux kied gaux rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik tawng aux koe pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx keeing Siyiex,
pa ot ka daux krax pa mawh pa meh khaing ka daux kawn simex kawn bun maix.
5 aMawh kheu nan,
bun pa muh aux eue,
aux khiox khaw maix,
ex tag caw tix ra lai muh paox tix.
Pa krai aux ka maix,
ang mawh gum lox tawx pa khraox tix grawng,
mai mawh gum lox pa hoik rieb ex tom yam jauh.
6 Krax muh pa krai aux mawh:
ex tag caw priex tix hawt ka gum lox tawx Siyiex.
Gum lox tawx pa hoik mhong pex tom yum jauh mawh:
pex tag caw tix teei gum lox tawx in,
pex kieh mawh kaing ting ka daux krax ot krax koe tix heue.
7 Pui pie lhien tom nie hoik hu ka piang haktiex ku dauh ku dee,
kix thiang ka grawng pa lih Ye-sux Khrit tete son kawn pui ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
Pui pa yuh nin mawh pui pie lhien,
mawh kaw pa tieb tie ka Khrit.
8 Pex tag caw tix rien rhawm tix,
pex tom tag ang chix tik ka ceu pa hoik pon pex,
tag sang pon khawng phak buan son khawm uik heue.
9 Phan koe pui pa ang priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex Khrit,
mai tieh tix singai hu khaing,
tag mawh pui pa ang kieh Siyiex koe ka daux rhawm tix;
pui pa priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa sidiex an,
tag mawh pa hoik pon ka keeing cao mai kawn cao.
10 Phan koe pui pa hoek ka pex dee an,
mai ang sidiex gau ka gum lox pa sidiex in,
paw vi rieb nawh hoek ka daux nyiex ma pex,
paw vi chawk nawh mhawm ot;
11 mawh kheu phan chawk nawh mhawm ot,
hoik awm yuh paox pie tau mai nawh ka daux kaing ceu pa caox yuh nan heue.
Lox pa nghoik
12 Aux kawn koe lox pa sang krai ka pex tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang rhawm tawx hu ka daux rawm meug.
Aux simie tix hu jhak ka pex,
krai lao ka plak sivoe ngai pex,
son sang kieh krax gaux rhawm pex grum naog heue.
13 Pa mawh kawn simex kawn bun pux o pa hoik laug maix kahb,
chawk maix mhawm ot heue.
