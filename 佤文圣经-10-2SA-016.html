Ta-wue mai Si-pa
16
aTa-wue jiet taug luan khaing choek gawng,
dum mawx dum an nawh yaox ka Si-pa siliex Mi-fe-paw-seu tuk ka la ra mu,
ka piang blawg la tang ka khao mun ra yieh lon,
jieh plix siiet kroh tix yieh gawng,
khlawp plix khaox pa hoik pieh yam rung praix tix yieh jieh,
mai hau hak plai tix mu rieb ka nawh.
2 Simiang Ta-wue chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix viex ceu in kix hoek sang yuh pa tix ka lie?”
Si-pa ah nin:
“La sang tawx pui nyiex maix Vawng brug.
Khao mun mai khlawp plix khaox ruh pa hoik pieh yam rung praix,
sang tawx pui pa hawt khaix maix ih.
Plai sang tawx kix nyaux yam taug maox kix ka daux tiag heue.”
3 bSimiang ah nin ka nawh:
“Ot kawn saux Sao-law pa kaux Mi-fe-paw-seu dee mawx?”
Si-pa ah nin:
“Nawh ot Yie-lu-sa-leung,
mawh kheu nawh keud yam in hoik keud pui Yi-seu-liex sang deeh tawx meung cie Sao-law ka nawh heue.”
4 Simiang ah nin ka Si-pa:
“Sin ngao pa hoik mawh cie Mi-fe-paw-seu khawm uik,
yam in tag sang tawx ka maix heue.”
Si-pa pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh siliex simiang,
kieh aux pon buan son ka greem maix Vawng leeing ju heue!”
Ta-wue mai See-me
5 Yam hoek simiang Ta-wue ka Pa-hu-ling,
pui nyiex Sao-law pa mawh See-me kawn simex Ci-la lih rieb ka Ta-wue.
Nawh jhax lawg Ta-wue khawm hu tix.
6 See-me ang mi lox Ta-wue ka pa ot Ta-wue ka daux pui pa hawt khaix nawh mai kawn cau pa kia ngien ka nawh,
kix khuang simaox ka Ta-wue mai siliex nawh khankix.
7 See-me jhax lawg Ta-wue ah nin:
“Yoi!
Maix pa mawh pui caox pa hoik pauh ka pui!
8 Maix hoik tuh ka meung cie Sao-law,
yam in Simiang Siyiex sang ciyang maix jao hoik pauh maix ceu pui nyiex Sao-law yum.
Simiang Siyiex hoik ap meung in ka kawn simex maix Ya-sa-long.
Maix pa mawh pui caox in,
maix hoik yaox tix laux leeing heue!”
9 Ya-pi-sai (miex nawh mawh Si-lu-ya) ah nin ka simiang:
“Vawng eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai kieh sox brai in jhax lawg tix lie?
Aux sang hu kid ka kaing nawh!”
10 Simiang ah nin ka Ya-pi-sai mai pux ke nawh Yaw-ya:
“In ang mawh kaing pa dex dawt mai pax.
Phan nawh jhax lawg ka aux,
hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kieh nawh yuh nin,
pui mawx sang koe akhuang tix sang chawk nawh,
yuh ka mawx nawh lai yuh nin lie?”
11 Ta-wue baox ah nin ka Ya-pi-sai mai siliex khawm uik:
“Pie kawn aux deeh sang pauh ka aux,
grawng kaing pa yuh pui Piien-ya-ming in sawn mawh tix lie?
In hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kieh nawh jhax lawg.
Ghram pa yuh nawh heue!
12 Simiang Siyiex koi chix deeh doh keud ka krax kha rhawmc pa kham aux,
mai deeh tawx buan ka aux,
tang krax pa jhax lawg ka daux in singaix.”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue ca nied tix hu sivoe tau mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh.
See-me mai hawt khaix kix,
hoek ka blaong ca jhax lawg khawm hu tix krax,
mai khuang simaox mai tiex glaox ka kix.
14 Yam hoek simiang mai pui pa hawt khaix nawh khawm uik ka rawm klawng Yaw-tangd kix kied taug maox,
mai tom laux lee tix dee an heue.
Ya-sa-long ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung
15 Ya-sa-long mai pui Yi-seu-liex pa hawt khaix nawh khankix,
laig ka daux veng Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Ya-si-to-fu kawx ot tau mai kix.
16 Paox grawm pa hawt rhawm Ta-wue pa kaux Hu-sai rieb ka Ya-sa-long,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Vawng lang ju lang phan!
Vawng lang ju lang phan!”
17 Ya-sa-long chawk ah nin ka nawh:
“Krax pa hawt maix ka rhawm paox grawm tix Ta-wue hu dee mawx?
Yuh ka mawx maix lai ang hu tau mai nawh lie?”
18 Hu-sai pawk lox ah nin:
“Aux sang chix hu yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux hoik mawh pa hawt rhawm pui pa hoik laug Simiang Siyiex,
mai kawn pui mai pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
mawh kheu nan,
aux tom sang ot tau mai maix tin.
19 Lheu khaing an,
phan aux ang yuh kaing jhaw son kawn simex simiang tix,
aux sang yuh son mawx lie?
Aux yuh kaing son keeing maix yuh ka mawx,
mai aux tag sang hawt tix yuh son maix yuh nan heue.”
20 Ya-sa-long veeh to kaux tix ah nin ka Ya-si-to-fu:
“Maix koe rhawm keud yuh ka mawx?
Aux sang khaw tix yuh ka mawx?”
21 Ya-si-to-fu ah nin:
“Maix hu it tau mai moeng iak keeing maix pa pau nyiex haw.
Yuh nin,
pui Yi-seu-liex ku kaux tom tawng maix ang lai khaw rhawm keeing tix;
pui pa hawt khaix maix tom tag nie koe riang.”
22 eMawh kheu nan,
kix tom sum nyiex gox ka choek piang nyiex haw tix lhang mai tawx mawh son cie Ya-sa-long.
Ka daux pa kok yaox pui ka ngai tix khawm uik,
Ya-sa-long laig ka daux nyiex gox,
mai it tau mai moeng iak keeing tix heue.
23 Ka daux singaix bawg yam an,
pui haoh pui hun hoik een lox pa krai Ya-si-to-fu awm pa hoik mawh lox Siyiex.
Ta-wue mai Ya-sa-long khawm ra kaux kied yung yi ka lox krai nawh heue.
