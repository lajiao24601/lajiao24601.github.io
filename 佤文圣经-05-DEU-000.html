Lai Been-ra-bawg
Krai dawt
Lai Been-ra-bawg mawh pa kaoh khaing phuk lai “lox tat jhawm prawng ra ceu tix ceu” lai Si-lax, ang mawh pa baox tiiem lox tat jhawm prawng an tix ceu, mai mawh pa baox krai dee bleeh an, mai baox krai lox pa tawx ka gawng Si-nai, tiiem grawng pa yuh Maw-si ka pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux haktiex Maw-ya. Yam an, pui Yi-seu-liex hoik si dauh krax hu tix, mai son sang hu ot ka haktiex Cia-nang.
Phuk lai in kaing ting tiiem tix deuh grawng in plak greem:
1. Maw-si hawt tix krai grawng ka daux tipon num can kaing pa hoik yuh kix ku ceu, mai kieh kawn pui tawng Siyiex, Siyiex thung kix yuh ka mawx, laig preeng ka daux tiag, mai kok yo kix hawt ka Siyiex, mai yung yi ka nawh heue.
2. Maw-si baox krai grawng lo ping nyap kao gum, mai kieh kix tawng gum lox ping nyap son grawng tix, nhie kawn pui krub ka Siyiex. Khaix an, baox krai yam laig pui Yi-seu-liex ka daux haktiex pa ap Siyiex tix tawx ka kix, mai been pa khaw kix tix yuh heue.
3. Maw-si krai ka kawn pui, dee bleeh sijax mukheu pa yuh Siyiex tau mai kix, mai sidiex kix baox deeh cox rhawm rhi tix, mai priex tix hawt ka lox sijax mukheu an heue.
4. Yaw-su-yax caw tix laug mawh cao pa plud Maw-si, pa mawh simiang cub pui Yi-seu-liex. Khaix hoik phat Maw-si ka lox leng tix ceu, mai yaog yaw pa kaux sijax Siyiex, mai nawb buan ka piang cub pui Yi-seu-liex. Nawh yum ka daux haktiex Maw-ya plak lih singaix rawm klawng Yaw-tang heue.
Phuk lai in kaing ting tiiem grawng krax pa taoh Siyiex mai pa tawx nawh buan ka kawn pa laug tix yuh ka mawx; kheu nan, kawn praix nawh tag sang priex kaux cong ot daux rhawm tix, mai muh nawh, nghiet lox nawh, son sang yaox tix klawd ju, mai pon buan son ang jud ang jai heue.
Simin ting 6 simin iak 5, “pex tag muh ka Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pex khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix, tau mai khawm ju tix, khawm riang khawm rao tix khawm uik” in hoik mawh dee ting ceu gum lox phuk lai in. Ye-sux ah in mawh gum lox ping nyap tix grawng, pa blawn ting ceu khaing paox tix khawm uik heue.
Krai kaing
1. Gum lox pa krai Maw-si ka bawg tix (1.1–4.49)
2. Gum lox pa krai Maw-si ka bawg ra (5.1–26.19)
(1) Gum lox ping nyap kao gum (5.1–10.22)
(2) Gum lox sijax mukheu mai gum lox pa sidiex gau khankix (11.1–26.19)
3. Gum lox pa krai sivoe sang laig ka daux haktiex Cia-nang (27.1–28.68)
4. Baox yuh gum lox sijax mukheu (29.1–30.20)
5. Lox ah Maw-si sivoe dex sang yum tix (31.1–33.29)
6. Grawng pa yum Maw-si (34.1–12)
