Lox kab pa tawx Saw-law-meung
10
Plak greem mawh lox kab pa tawx Saw-law-meung:
kawn simex pa lhak lhiao kieh keeing tix pon krax pa nyawm rhawm nyawm rhi;
kawn simex pa daox aw kieh miex tix koe krax pa khi rhawm khi rhi.
2 Sin ngao pa ang rauh ang see ang koe dee mhawm son kawn pui;
pa mawh pa meh pon taoh ka ju.
3 Simiang Siyiex ang kieh pui rauh pui see cai rhawm;
nawh ang kieh pui pa yuh pa caox pa caig hawt tix yuh awm alo to kaux tix.
4 Pa nieh kieh pui hot sibhawm;
rhawm rien kieh pui chix mi chix koe.
5 Pui pa lhak lhiao chix khrawm tix deuh ka priex preem awm pa khaw mai bawg yam;
pa gaing yam vawg tix nghox kied mawh pa kaig pa tua tete.
6 Pui rauh pui see tag sang pon krax pa nawb buan son;
lox pui pa cau nyhau mox ka krax pa caox.
7 Pui rauh pui see pui ang chix piawm kah;
pui pa caox pa caig ceu kaux kix laux leeing som nai.
8 Pui pa lhak lhiao yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm tom nie;
pa krai lox pa daox aw,
tang deeh yuh to kaux tix laux leeing som nai.
9 Pa mawh pa meh kaux cong ka dee ot dee koe tix;
pui pa pie lhien laux leeing som nai dee si dauh.
10 Pui pa mox ka kaing pa tawng tix,
mawh pa cheeik ka simah simaing;
pa sidiex gau pui ka daux krax pa sidawng,
mawh pa kieh haktiex nhima.
11 Jheeh pui pa rauh pa see mawh num dee keet ju;
lox pui pa caox pa caig mox ka krax pa cau nyhau.
12 bKrax pa koeng pa khoi viex simah simaing lih;
krax muh krax rhawm pon tawb kheem ka krax lud krax loe khawm uik.
13 Jheeh pui pa lhak lhiao koe ping nyia cuyi;
pui pa daox aw ka piang blawg kix yie yaox tix kham krax pa si vied.
14 Pui pa lhak lhiao chix rieb tix teei ka ngai lig ngai lai;
pui pa daox aw hoik yuh kaing caox ku dauh ku dee.
15 Sin ngao mawh pa kaux cong yuh pui pa koe mau koe khri;
krax hot krax sibhawm yuh pui hot pui sibhawm laux leeing.
16 Pa yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm khankix pon krax im ju;
phi map phi ghram chix kieh krax lud krax loe lih koe tom nie.
17 Pui pa nghiet ka lox sidiex lox gau tag yaox tix im ju;
pui pa ang doh keud ka krax lud krax loe tix,
to kaux nawh sang hoek krax sum krax nai.
18 Pui pa koe rhawm koeng rhawm boh mawh pui pie pui lhien;
pui pa krai lox pie lox lhien hu,
hoik mawh pui pa daox tete.
19 Pui haoh lox haoh moing yie hoek krax lud krax loe;
pui pa chix rien jheeh jhai tix mawh pa koe ping nyia cuyi.
20 Lox pui pa rauh pa see awm mau luah pa mhawm singax;
rhawm keud pui pa caox pa caig ang koe ngawh jum pie ceeix kawx.
21 Lox pui pa rauh pa see chix kieh pui haoh pui hun nyawm ot nyawm koe;
pui pa daox aw yum kheu ang tix koe krax lhak lhiao.
22 Simiang Siyiex ploi buan son lih,
mai kieh kawn pui mag mi;
pa yuh kaing ka daux riang rao tix giex going,
ang chix hoek krax mi krax koec.
23 Pa daox pa aw kieh kaing pa caox pa caig pa yuh tix lih mawh krax klieh;
pui pa koe krax doh krax riang kieh ping nyia cuyi mawh krax gaux rhawm.
24 Kaing pa simie pui rauh pui see tix yuh pon lih tix mawh;
ceu pa lhat pa graung pui caox ca lih tix koe ang jud ang jai.
25 Yam hoek bhaung ting,
mai phlu ka pui pa caox pa caig hu,
pui rauh pui see tag kaux cong tom si dauh sidu.
26 Paw vi sawg pui nieh yuh kaing tang maix,
nawh awm rawm niex pa si ki yuh rhang,
mai taox ngu pa meet ka ngai kieh maix lu lax ang nyawm ot.
27 Pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex tag yaox tix lang ju lang phan;
pui pa caox pa caig kied dawt ju.
28 Krax keud pot jun pot pui pa rauh pa see tag chix viex krax gaux rhawm gaux rhi hoek;
krax keud pot jun pot pui pa caox pa caig tag awm riang pang peue yuh nan.
29 Simiang Siyiex kia ngien ka pui pa mawh pa meh;
nawh yuh laux leeing ka pui pa caox pa caig.
30 Pui rauh pui see ang chix yaox tix vu vie;
pui pa caox pa caig ang pon yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe ka daux kaing pa yuh tix.
31 Ka daux jheeh pui mhawm ping nyia cuyi hoik koe ka jheeng jheeng;
dak pui pa caox pa caig tag sang pat tix tik.
32 Pui rauh see krai lox khaw yuh rhawm rhi pui;
lox lih khaing jheeh pui pa caox caig kieh pui laux rhawm laux rhi heue.
