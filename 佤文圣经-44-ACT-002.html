Khuan Singax Singeh lih hoek
2
aKa daux singaix poi tix phuan ngaix,
kawn Khrit khrawm paox tix ot tau tix dauh.
2 Dum mawx dum an,
koe lox lih hoek khaing rao ma,
awm lox bhaung ting pa phlu tix hoek yuh nan,
pa naog ka nyiex pa ngawm kix tix ot.
3 Kix baox yaox ka greeh grang pak peung dak pui pa awm rao ngu pa prung prang tix hu yuh nan,
mai tom ka piang to kaux kix ku kaux.
4 Kix hoik naog ka Khuan Singax Singeh khawm uik,
hawt awm krax chix krax tawng pa tawx Khuan Singax Singeh,
kix jauh tix krai lox tang ceu tang cad heue.
5 Bawg yam an,
koe pui Yo-thaix pa koe krax yum rhawm kaoh hoek khaing piang haktiex ku meung,
kix paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
6 Yam mhong kix luah lox in,
cub pui hun tom khrawm paox tix ot tau tix dauh khaing.
Pui hun kied gaux rhawm,
mawh kheu hoik mhong pui si kah kawn Khrit ka lox pui haktiex in ku kaux.
7 Ka daux krax pa daox deng an,
kix ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
pui in kix ang hoik si kah ka lox pui Cia-li-lix khawm uik lie?
8 Yuh ka mawx ex mhong lox pa si kah kix ka lox ex khawm uik lie?
9 Ka daux ex koe pui Pha-thi-yax,
Mi-ti-yax,
Yi-lang;
kawn koe pui pa kaoh khaing Me-saw-pu-ta-mi-yax,
Yo-thaix,
Cia-pha-to-cia,
peung-to,
Ya-si-yax.
10 Fu-lui-cia,
Phang-fe-li-yax,
Ai-cix,
mai pui pa dex ka veng Li-pi-yax haktiex Ku-li-naix,
mai pui pa kaoh khaing Law-ma,
11 khawm pui Yo-thaix mai pui tang ceu tang cad pa laig been li Yo-thaix khankix.
Lheu khaing an,
kawn koe pui Kheu-li-theux mai pui A-la-pawx.
Ex lai mhong si kah kix ka lox haktiex ex,
mai krai lao kaing ting pa yuh Siyiex heue!”
12 Kix baox daox deng kah,
mai ra lai chawk paox tix,
aux chawk maix ah nin:
“In hoik mawh kaing ceu pa tix lie?”
13 Koe pui tix blah kawn nyiah kawn Khrit ah nin:
“Pui in kix ang hoik mawh yoek ka plai heue!”
Gum lox krai Pi-teux
14 Yam an Pi-teux mai kawn cau tang kaux kao tix kaux kaoh cung.
Nawh krai lox tom ting ka pui hun ah nin:
“Paox ceu paox cad pui Yo-thaix mai can pui pa paung ot ka Yie-lu-sa-leung eue,
pex tag caw tix tawng,
tag caw tix bre rhawm tix nghiet ka lox aux.
15 Pex ce pui in kix mawh pa yoek ka plai lie?
Ang mawh,
mawh kheu yam in taug mawh pon ngawb dim taix nari khaing.
16 Grawng kaing in hoik mawh awm lox ah cao jhai Yaw-eux ah nin:
17 bSiyiex ah nin:
In hoik mawh pa sang yuh aux ka daux singaix bawg yam si dauh haktiex ot aux:
aux tag sang klup tix tawx khuan tix ka pui ku kaux.
Kawn simex kawn bun pex tag sang krai pao ka gum lox aux;
pui nhum ju pex tag sang yaox ka riang su sai;
pui riex ju pex tag sang ca maox ka daux riang su sai.
18 Ka daux singaix an,
aux tag sang klup khuan tix lih,
tag sang tawx ka siliex mai ka bun siliex aux;
kix tag sang krai pao ka gum lox aux.
19 Aux tag sang jhi mhai pa daox pa am ka rao ma,
mai yuh kaing pa daox pa am ka piang haktiex;
koe nham,
koe ngu,
koe taox ngu;
20 Singaix tag sang faik ang koe pa riang.
Khix hoik raoh awm nham yuh nan;
sivoe sang hoek singaix min plak ngai Simiang Siyiex pa ting pa lhaong,
kaing in kix tag sang lih tix mawh khawm uik.
21 Yam an,
can pui pa kok tix khiox khaw ka ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex tag sang pon krax taoh heue.
22 “Paox ceu paox cad Yi-seu-liex eue,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka lox aux.
Gum lox tawx Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux pa singax singeh,
hoik sidawng grawng pa yuh nawh mhai pa daox pa am,
riang su sai,
kaing pa daox deng khaing Siyiex,
pa hoik jhi pex yaox lih.
Kaing in to kaux pex hoik tawng khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik lih tix mawh ka daux pex
23 cSiyiex hoik hawt ka alo tix mai ka pa grox yaox tix,
hoik dawg bot tix ap Ye-sux ka pex;
kheu nan pex teei pui pa caox tawk nawh piang khaox lhak,
mai pauh nawh yum.
24 dViang mawh nan,
Siyiex kieh nawh deeh kaoh im khaing krax yum,
mai hoik teei tix tik ka pa kha rhawm krax yum,
mawh kheu krax yum ang pon laung ka nawh.
25 eTa-wue hoik sax krai ka nawh ah nin:
aux hoik yaox ka Simiang Ye-sux ot plak sivoe ngai aux jheeng jheeng.
Nawh ot ka plak dawm aux,
mai ang kieh aux yung yang
26 Mawh kheu nan,
aux gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
gum lox aux hoik naog ka krax gaux rhawm;
kheu nan ghlaok to kaux aux ca kawn laux lee tix ka daux krax keud pot jun pot.
27 Mawh kheu maix ang kieh aux laig daux meung yum,
ang kieh siliex pa kaux sijax maix yaox tix laux si um.
28 Maix hoik jhi krax pa im ju aux;
krax ot tau maix hoik kieh aux naog krax pa gaux rhawm heue.
29 “Paox ceu paox cad khankix,
phan mawh son tax du pex Ta-wue,
aux tag sang krai pex mhong sidawng.
Ja lao nawh ang hoik yum,
mai hoik si paung ka nawh;
si meeig nawh tom yam in kawn ot ka ex dee in.
30 fNawh mawh cao jhai,
nawh hoik tawng ka gum lox pa ap Siyiex tix deuh ka nawh yuh nin:
Siyiex hoik sax tiex tu tix sang yaog simiang tix kaux lih khaing ka daux kawn praix nawh,
mai hoek tix ngawm ka piang dang ngeung cie nawh.
31 Ta-wue hoik grox tawng ka krax pa yuh Siyiex,
mawh kheu nan,
yam krai nawh ka grawh grawng pa kaoh im Khrit,
nawh ah nin:
ang bax prah ghram nawh ot daux meung yum.
Nex nham nawh kawx ang bax laux si um.
32 Ye-sux kaw in,
Siyiex hoik kieh nawh deeh kaoh im;
yix hoik mawh pui pa kham sakse kaing in.
33 Hoik yaog nawh lhaong ot ka plak taix khawn dawm Siyiex,
khaix an mai hoik pon tix rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh pa hoik ap tix deuh khaing keeing nawh.
Yam in pa hoik yaox pex mai pa hoik mhong pex,
pa mawh khawng phak buan son pa hoik klup Siyiex tix tawx ka ex.
34 gTo kaux Ta-wue ang bax haok ka rao ma,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka simiang aux:
maix ngawm ka plak khawn dawm aux,
35 krox aux kieh pui koeng ka maix
kreet tix ot greem caong maix heue.
36 “Mawh kheu nan,
paox ceu paox cad Yi-seu-liex khawm uik eue,
pex tag caw kieh tix tawng sidawng,
Ye-sux kaw pa tawk pex piang khaox lhak in,
Siyiex hoik ap nawh mawh simiang,
mawh Khrit heue!”
37 Pui hun hoik mhong lox in,
mai hoik dung ka daux rhawm kix,
kix krai ka Pi-teux mai ka kawn cau tang kaux ah nin:
“Pux aik khankix,
sang khaw ex tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
38 Pi-teux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex ku kaux tag caw tix loh rhawm rhi tix,
khaix an tag caw cub com tix ka daux ceu kaux Ye-sux Khrit,
son sang pon pluat ploi ka phi map pex,
pex tom taug chix pon tix rieb ka Khuan Singax Singeh pa hoik tawx Siyiex.
39 Mawh kheu gum lox pa hoik ap Siyiex,
hoik mawh pa tawx ka pui pa hoik kok simiang ex Simiang Siyiex kah,
pa mawh pex,
kawn praix pex,
mai pui pa ot dee singai khawm uik heue.”
40 Pi-teux baox teei lox tom nie kham sakse ka kix,
sidiex gau ka kix ah nin:
“Pex tag caw tix taoh to kaux tix luat luan khaing krax ciyang pa si pup pui caox pui caig in kix heue!”
41 Pui tom nie hoik rieb ka gum lox nawh,
khaix an rieb tix cub com;
singaix an kawn Khrit baox thiem thie tix been loe rheng kaux.
42 Kix ca tawng nghiet ngie ka krax pa sidiex gau kawn cau khankix,
mai yuh krax cub caw pa khrawm tix ot tau,
ih nyaux priex preem tau,
rawg buan tau heue.
Krax ot krax koe cub caw
43 Kawn cau khankix hoik yuh ka mhai pa daox pa am tom nie;
pui hoik koe krax lhat graung yung yi kheu in ku kaux.
44 hPui tom nie nied tix yuh krax pa khrawm tix ot tau,
ku ceu khawm uik pui hun hoik rom tix siao tau.
45 Kix baox coh ka kaung ma tix,
hawt tix guah awm pa sang lo kaux pui mawx kaux pui an heue.
46 Kix ih rhawm rhi khri mau paox tix,
khrawm paox tix ot ka daux nyiex buan ku singaix,
mai baox lai loh tix ih priex preem ka daux nyiex ma paox tix ku kaux,
mai ih nyaux tau ka daux krax gaux rhawm pa singax,
47 yaog yaw Siyiex,
yuh krax pa khaw caw rhawm rhi paox tix.
Simiang Siyiex thiem thie kaux pui pa pon krax taoh ku singaix ka kix heue.
