Akhuang cie Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.23–27; Lai Ma-kheu 11.27–33)
20
Koe ka daux tix ngaix,
Ye-sux ot tix sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan,
mai krai pao ka gum lox buan.
Koe simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri,
mai kuad song hoek ka nawh,
2 chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh maix kaing in kix ka daux akhuang cie pui mawx?
Mawh mawx pa tawx akhuang in ka maix lie?
Krai ka yix heue!”
3 Ye-sux pawk lox kix ah nin:
“Aux ran chawk pex ka lox tix gum,
krai ka aux,
4 akhuang cie Yaw-hang cao cub com mawh kaoh khaing Siyiex,
mawh kaoh khaing kawn pui lie?”
5 Kix jauh tix ra lai thung thiang ka paox tix:
“Ex sang pawk ah ka mawx lie?
Phan ex krai ‘Kaoh khaing Siyiex dee an’,
nawh tee ah nin:
‘Phan mawh nan,
pex lai ang yum rhawm ka Yaw-hang lie?’
6 Phan ex krai ‘kaoh khaing kawn pui dee an’,
pui hun tee rien tix teei simaox tawk ex kah,
mawh kheu kix hoik yum rhawm grawng mawh Yaw-hang cao jhai.”
7 Mawh kheu nan,
kix pawk lox ah nin:
“Yix ang tawng mawh kaoh khaing dee mawx heue.”
8 Ye-sux tom ah nin ka kix:
“Phan mawh nan,
aux kawx ang krai ka pex,
yuh aux kaing in ka daux akhuang cie pui mawx heue.”
Phek tiiem ka pui caox vai si peem
(Lai Ma-thaix 21.33–46; Lai Ma-kheu 12.1–12)
9 aKhaix an,
Ye-sux krai gum lox phek tiiem in ka kix ah nin:
“Koe pui tix kaux paih ka si peem plix shiet tix dauh,
mai tawx pui vai tix yuh si peem,
nawh hu ka haktiex dee singai,
mai paung ot dee an been tix baux bawg yam.
10 Hoek bawg yam pieh plix shiet,
nawh ploi siliex tix tix kaux hu teei ka pa khaw nawh tix pon ka pui pa vai tix yuh si peem.
Viang mawh nan,
kix lauh ka siliex an,
mai kieh nawh ing khawm taix yi tix.
11 Cao nyiex si peem baox ploi siliex tang kaux hu;
kix baox lauh ka nawh,
map nai ka nawh,
mai baox kieh nawh viex taix yi tix ing nang.
12 Ka daux loe bawg cao nyiex si peem baox ploi siliex hu tix kaux;
pui pa vai si peem an baox lauh ka nawh,
mai grheu nawh lih khaing daux si peem.
13 Cao nyiex si peem plix shiet an ah nin:
‘Sang khaw aux tix yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux sang ploi kawn simex pa muh aux tix kaux hu,
kix tee rien tix nab yam yung yi ka nawh.’
14 Viang mawh nan,
cao vai si peem an yaox ka kawn simex cao nyiex si peem,
mai ra lai ah nin ka paox tix:
‘In mawh pui pa sang plud ka cao nyiex si peem;
ex pauh ka nawh,
sin ngao cie nawh tag mawh cie ex khaix heue!’
15 Mawh kheu nan,
kix cot nawh lih plak praix si peem plix shiet,
mai pauh nawh yum heue.
“Yuh nin,
cao nyiex si peem plix shiet tag sang yuh ka mawx ka pui pa vai si peem lie?
16 Nawh tee rien tix sang hoek pauh kix yum,
mai deeh teei si peem plix shiet tawx pui tang kaux vai heue.”
Pui hun mhong ka lox in,
mai hoik krai ah nin khawm uik:
“Ang doh pon tix kieh kaing pa yuh nin koe heue!”
17 bYe-sux dong ngai tix ka kix,
mai chawk ah nin:
“Phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
Simaox pa hoik tik cang mai yuh nyiex
hoik lih mawh simaox jaong nyiex pa blawn ting ceu.
Dee bleeh gum lox in mawh ceu tix lie?
18 Pui mawx goh piang simaox an,
pui an tag sang laux leeing;
simaox an cawd ka piang to kaux pui mawx,
tag sang lauh nang pui an max phawng vid heue.”
Grawng pa tawx mau koh keen ka Kai-sax
(Lai Ma-thaix 22.15–22; Lai Ma-kheu 12.13–17)
19 Sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri mai simiang cao giex kaing li hoik tawng ka gum lox pa krai Ye-sux hoik mawh son kix,
kix tom ce tix sang giex Ye-sux ka dee an;
viang mawh nan,
kix lhat ka pui hun,
20 mai ca baox krox jun ka bawg yam.
Kix liag tix pon ka rhawm pui tix blah,
pie tix yuh kaing ceu pa mhawm,
mai chawk grawng kaing ka Ye-sux,
son sang pon jhawp ka lox nawh,
son sang tawx nawh ka cao kaing prawng ca ka nawh.
21 Pui pa jiang in kix krai ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sara,
yix hoik tawng pa sidiex gau maix hoik khaw tete,
hoik tawng maix ang jhak ka plak ngai pui,
mai maix hoik ca viex rhawm rauh see sidiex gau pui ka gum lox Siyiex.
22 Khiox khaw krai ka yix,
phan tawx mau koh keen ka Kai-sax Vawng Law-ma,
mawh lud ang lud ka lox tat jhawm prawng ex lie?”
23 Ye-sux hoik tawng ka krax keud pa pie lhien kix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
24 “Teei mau luah tix plah tawx aux jhak!
Riang pa koe ka mau in mai ceu kaux pa koe ka mawh mawx?”
Kix pawk ah nin:
“Mawh Kai-sax heue.”
25 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Phan mawh nan,
cie Kai-sax tawx ka Kai-sax,
cie Siyiex tawx ka Siyiex heue.”
26 Kix ang tawng ka sang jhawp ka lox pa hoik krai Ye-sux ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun;
lox pa pawk Ye-sux hoik kieh kix kied daox deng kah,
tom ang lai koe lox krai heue.
Grawh grawng pa kaoh im
(Lai Ma-thaix 22.23–33; Lai Ma-kheu 12.18–27)
27 cKoe pui Sa-tu-kai pa ang yum rhawm ka grawng pa kaoh im tix blah hoek ka Ye-sux.
28 dChawk nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
Maw-si hoik yuh lox tat jhawm prawng son ex ah nin:
‘Phan pui tix kaux yum,
prah ka moeng tix,
viang mawh nan,
ang bax koe kawn,
pux aik nawh tag caw tix teei tix moeng ka o tix,
son sang kieh plud ka doeh ceu cad pux ke tix.’
29 Sivoe an koe pux aik aliah kaux yuh nin:
pux ke lhong koe moeng,
nawh yum,
ang bax koe kawn.
30 Nyi teei tix moeng o tix,
31 khaix an,
sam hoik moeng nawh nang,
tom hoek ka jiet hoik moeng ka nawh nang,
ang bax koe kawn,
tom yum heue.
32 Dee si dauh,
bun an kawx hoik yum nang.
33 Khiox khaw,
chawk ka maix,
ka daux singaix kaoh im,
nawh sang sawn tix mawh moeng pa kaux tix lie?
Mawh kheu kix pux aik khawm aliah kaux hoik moeng ka nawh khawm uik heue.”
34 Ye-sux pawk lox kix ah nin:
“Ka daux singaix yam in,
simex sibun koe mex koe moeng;
35 viang mawh nan,
an kix pa keung tix pon kaoh im khaing krax yum,
khaix an im ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
mai kix ang lai koe mex koe moeng.
36 Kix ang lai chix yum awm kawn cau Siyiex yuh nan.
Kix mawh kawn simex kawn bun Siyiex,
mawh kheu hoik kaoh im kix khaing krax yum.
37 eMaw-si hoik jhi sidawng grawng pa kaoh im pui yum,
yam tiiem nawh tix deuh pang kat pa gleeing,
nawh ah nin:
Simiang Siyiex mawh ‘Siyiex Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha mai Ya-keux heue.’
38 Dee bleeh gum lox in krai mawh Siyiex cie pui im ju,
ang mawh Siyiex pui yum heue.
Mawh kheu ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex,
kawn pui hoik im khawm uik heue.”
39 Koe sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
“Sara,
maix hoik khaw krai heue!”
40 Jauh yam an,
kix ang lai pon chawk Ye-sux ka lox heue.
Grawh grawng Khrit
(Lai Ma-thaix 22.41–46; Lai Ma-kheu 12.35–37)
41 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Yuh ka mawx pui lai ah Khrit mawh kawn praix Ta-wue lie?
42 fTa-wue hoik ah nin ka daux Lai Yaog Yaw:
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka simiang aux:
maix ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm aux,
43 krox aux kieh pui koeng kreet tix ot greem caong maix heue.
44 Ta-wue hoik kok nawh mawh ‘simiang’,
Khrit sang chix mawh kawn praix Ta-wue yuh ka mawx lie?”
Ye-sux ne raw ka sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri
(Lai Ma-thaix 23.1–36; Lai Ma-kheu 12.38–40)
45 Yam kawn nghiet pui hun kah,
Ye-sux krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
46 “Tag caw rien tix ka sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri.
Kix muh tix ceep ka kraung dha hu daux laih,
simie pui tawx krax nab yam yung yi ka tix sivoe ngai pui ka daux bang,
mai baox muh tix ngawm ka nyiex sidiex pa blawk pui ngawm,
mai ka dee ngawm pa koe sivoe yam yuh poi.
47 Kix hoik bleet ka sin ngao cie miex mai,
khaix an rawg buan tom rheeng ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
Kix tag sang kham krax ciyang pa lang teung kin heue!”
