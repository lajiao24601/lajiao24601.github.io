Ne raw ka simiang Thai-eux
28
Simiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
2 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
tag caw tix krai ka simiang Thai-eux,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
maix tang deeh ah nin,
maix mawh Siyiex,
maix awm siyiex pa ngawm ka piang dang ngeung ka sigreung rawm sigrit sigra.
Viang maix hoik een to kaux tix mawh siyiex,
tete te lai,
maix tag sang laux leeing,
ang mawh Siyiex.
3 Maix ce tix lhak lhiao khaing Ta-ni-eux,
mai tawng ka can kaing pa raux pa mox khawm uik.
4 Maix teei krax pa lhak lhiao mai krax chix krax tawng kieh tix mag mi,
ka daux nyiex krao maix hoik khrawm naog ka mau khri tom nie.
5 Maix hoik tawng grawng sang yuh tix ka lai gee,
mai sin ngao maix thiem thie tix ang jud ang jai.
Maix hoik ting rhawm lhaong rhi son sin ngao pa koe tix heue!
6 “Mawh kheu nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
mawh kheu hoik een maix tix lhak lhiao awm siyiex yuh nan,
7 Aux tag sang viex pui koeng pa caox hoek tix puing ka maix.
Sin ngao pa pon maix khaing pa teei krax pa lhat lhiao tix,
mai krax pa chix pa tawng tix,
kix tag sang yuh laux khawm uik.
8 Kix tag sang pauh ka maix,
mai kieh maix com greem rawm sigrit sigra.
9 Yam hoek kix tix pauh maix,
maix kawn pon kok tix mawh siyiex lie?
Yam ot maix ka sivoe ngai pui pa pauh ka maix,
maix tom taug tawng mawh tix pa tag sang laux leeing,
ang mawh Siyiex.
10 Maix tag sang yum ka daux tawm taix pui meung tang meung pa ang koe krax nab yam yung yi.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Dee si dauh krax lawng simiang Thai-eux
11 Simiang Siyiex baox si kah ka aux;
12 nawh ah nin:
“Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix rhax lox yiiem doe son simiang Thai-eux.
Tag caw tix krai ka nawh,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
maix hoik sax mawh pui pa grum mhawm tix kaux,
hoik grum naog ka ping nyia cuyi,
hoik kied sidaing mhawm grang.
13 Maix paung ot ka daux si peem Siyiex,
pa mawh ka daux si peem Yi-tiien,
maix hoik teei siang simaox pa raoh,
siang simaox kiien,
siang simaox pa lheung,
siang simaox pa klai,
siang simaox bawd raoh,
siang simaox pa toh riang,
siang simaox pa klai rao ma,
siang simaox pa klai bre ka to kaux tix,
mai teei nang kraung khri jawg to kaux tix kah.
Yam phan maix ka daux singaix an,
aux hoik rien in kix khawm uik son maix.
14 Aux ploi Ci-lu-paw pa lhat graung pui ka kia ngien ka maix.
Maix paung ot ka gawng pa singax aux,
mai hu ka si nax pa jhan riang siang pa ting ngawh.
15 “Jauh yam phan ka maix hoek yam jauh maix tix laux leeing singaix an,
kaing pa yuh maix hoik mawh pa grum mhawm.
16 Yam hoek haok huan lai gee pa yuh maix,
maix tag cau nyhau yuh phi map.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux khin maix lih hu khaing gawng pa singax aux;
kawn cau Siyiex pa kia ngien ka maix an,
grheu maix lih hu khaing siang dee pa jhan riang an.
17 Kheu pa mhawm grang maix tang yaw to kaux tix;
ceu kaux pa ao min maix kieh maix cau nyhau.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux si blat maix piang tiex,
mai kieh kaing in ne raw ka simiang haktiex.
18 Maix hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig tom nie,
mai yuh lai gee ka daux krax pa ang rauh ang see,
hoik seeing brai yuh nyiex pa singax.
Mawh kheu nan,
aux ploi ngu gleeing ka maix,
kieh maix tieh tix lih mawh nyaox nyia,
kieh pui jhak kah.
19 Maix hoik laux leeing,
hoik laux leeing ju heue!
Meung pa tawng yung ka maix hoik lhat graung khawm uik,
mai lhat tix si pup ka krax laux leeing awm maix yuh nan heue.”
Ne raw ka Si-toeng
20 aSimiang Siyiex si kah ka aux;
nawh ah nin:
21 “Pui pa tag sang laux leeing eue,
maix tag caw tix ne raw ka veng Si-toeng.
22 Tag caw tix krai ka kix,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
Si-toeng eue,
aux tag sang tieb tie ka maix heue!
Kawn pui tag sang yaog yaw aux kheu pa ciyang aux ka maix.
Yam jhi aux pa singax singeh tix,
yuh krax pa ciyang,
kix tom taug tawng grawng pa mawh aux Simiang Siyiex.
23 Aux tag sang kieh ka daux veng khawm uik lih koe ka jhawm saux preng pa ting,
ka daux laih ting laih iak ku dauh hoik naog ka nham.
Pui koeng tag sang hoek tix puing ka maix khaing haktiex khawm pon praok si daix daog,
mai pauh ka pui cie maix yum.
Yuh nin,
maix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang pon buan son
24 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Veng pa ot sivie siveng Yi-seu-liex pa map nai ka kix,
ang lai baox deeh awm pang kat pa klauh ka pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nan,
pui Yi-seu-liex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong heue.”
25 Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong krai yuh nin:
“Aux tag sang thung pui Yi-seu-liex deeh hoek khaing meung pa ploi aux kix hu ot daux ku meung;
yuh nin,
meung ka piang haktiex tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux pa singax singeh.
Pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang paung ot ka haktiex cie tix,
pa mawh haktiex pa hoik tawx aux ka siliex tix Ya-keux,
26 Kix tag sang paung ot dee an ka daux krax pa nhim pa tiiem,
mai sum ka nyiex ma,
paih ka si peem plix siiet.
Aux tag sang ciyang ka meung pa ot ka sivie siveng Yi-seu-liex pa map nai ka kix,
pui Yi-seu-liex tag sang yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe.
Yuh nin,
kix tom taug tawng grawng mawh aux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex kix heue.”
