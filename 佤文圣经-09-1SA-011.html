Sao-law puing pex ka pui Ya-meung
11
Khaix hoik luan been tix khix,
simiang pui Ya-meung pa kaux Na-sia lai lawm puing ka veng Ya-pi ka daux haktiex Ci-liex.
Pui Ya-pi ah nin ka Na-sia:
“Pex yuh sijax mukheu tau mai yix,
mai yix tom cu nghiet ka maix heue.”
2 Na-sia ah nin:
“Phan sang kieh aux yuh sijax mukheu tau mai pex,
aux koe lox mhaing ka tix grawng,
kieh aux koik tix tik tawm ngai pex plak khawn dawm ku kaux lih,
mai kieh in mawh pa kaig tua yuh pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik heue.”
3 Kuad song Ya-pi khankix ah nin:
“Tawx bawg yam ka yix aliah singaix ha,
kieh yix ploi kawn cau hu ka haktiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh ku dee.
Phan ang koe pui pa teem coi ka yix,
yix tom ap tix ang pex ka maix heue.”
4 Kawn cau hoek ka dee ot Sao-law Ci-pi-yax,
mai krai lox in hu,
kawn pui kied deek rhawm deek rhi tix yiiem heue.
5 Yam an,
Sao-law jiet tag hawt moi hoek khaing daux kaung,
nawh chawk ah nin:
“Kaing ceu pa tix heue?
Yuh ka mawx pui lai yiiem ku kaux lie?”
Kix tom krai Sao-law mhong lox pa viex pui Ya-pi hoek.
6 Sao-law mhong ka lox an,
dum mawx dum an,
khuan Siyiex lih ot tau mai nawh,
mai kied song rhawm ka tete.
7 Nawh tuk moi hoek ra mu,
mai kid moi lih mawh khik,
mai tawx lox ka kawn cau hu tawx khik nex an hoek ka haktiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku dauh ku dee,
mai ne raw ka kawn pui ah nin:
“Pui mawx pa ang hu puing naing tau mai Sao-law,
tau mai Sa-mu-eu,
moi nyiex nawh tag sang kid yuh nin heue!”
Krax pa song rhawm song rhi Simiang Siyiex kieh kawn pui Yi-seu-liex lhat graung,
can pui khawm uik ih rhawm paox tix hu puing ka naing.
8 Sao-law khrawm kix ot ka Pi-seux,
koe pui tix ngoe mheen kaux pa lih khaing daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa lih khaing daux Yo-tax loe mheen kaux.
9 Kix ah nin ka kawn cau pa hoek khaing Ya-pi:
“Hu krai ka pex pui Ya-pi,
pa sax sivoe sang hoek greung ngaix,
kix tag pon krax taoh teem.”
Pui Ya-pi mhong ka lox an,
kied gaux rhawm ka tete,
10 mai ah nin ka Na-sia:
“Pa sax yix ap tix ang pex,
maix chix tix yuh ka yix awm pa simie maix tix yuh heue.”
11 Ngaix khaix pon ngawb cao,
Sao-law guah pui naing koe loe cub;
yam hoik riang praix,
kix preeng tix laug puing ka daux bang pui koeng,
puing ka pui Ya-meung,
tom hoek greung ngaix,
mai hoik pauh ka pui tom nie;
pui naing pa kawn klawd khankix,
hoik rai rhuat tix hu khawm pon daog,
mai yaog tix tang to klawd ju heue.
12 Pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin ka Sa-mu-eu:
“Mawh mawx pa krai Sao-law ang khaw tix mawh simiang?
Teei pui in kix lih,
mai ex sang pauh kix yum heue!”
13 Viang mawh nan,
Sao-law ah nin:
“Ka daux in singaix,
ang pon tix pau pui yum,
mawh kheu hoik taoh Simiang Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex.”
14 Sa-mu-eu ah nin ka kix:
“Ex hu ka Cie-cia,
mai deeh cox sum Sao-law mawh simiang dee an.”
15 Mawh kheu nan kix tom hu ka Cie-cia,
mai sum Sao-law mawh simiang ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
khaix an mai tan ka Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
Sao-law mai can pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
yuh krax gaux rhawm son kaing ting an heue.
