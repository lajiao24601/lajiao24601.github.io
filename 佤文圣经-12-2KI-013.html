Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-ha-see
13
Yam giex simiang Yo-tax kawn simex Ya-ha-shiex pa kaux Yaw-a-see ka kaing daux tix nga loe num,
kawn simex Yie-hu pa kaux Yaw-ha-see mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh giex kaing ka piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax kao aliah num.
2 Nawh hoik awm simiang Yie-law-paw-ang pa koe sivoe nawh yuh nan,
mai hoik yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
kieh pui Yi-seu-liex yuh phi map,
mai ang ploi yuh ka tom si dauh.
3 Mawh kheu nin,
Simiang Siyiex song rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
mai cu tix kieh simiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Ha-shiiex khawm kawn simex nawh pa kaux Piien Ha-tax ca baox pon pex ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
4 Khaix an,
Yaw-ha-see khiox khaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
Simiang Siyiex yaox grawng krax pa kied cau nyhau simiang pui Shui-li-yax ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
nawh tom nghiet ka lox rawg buan Yaw-ha-see.
5 Simiang Siyiex tawx cao pa taoh tix kaux ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
cao pa taoh in kieh pui Yi-seu-liex luat luan khaing krax pa kha pui Shui-li-yax ka kix.
Mawh kheu nin,
pui Yi-seu-liex deeh yaox tix pon krax pa yien yawg awm singaix pa hoik luan yuh nan.
6 Viang mawh nan,
kix ca kawn yuh phi map pa yuh simiang Yie-law-paw-ang,
pa kieh pui Yi-seu-liex krix ka daux phi map,
ang bax yoi tix singai khaing pa krub riang krub rao;
mai ca kawn deuh ka riang siyiex bun Ya-seu-la ot ka daux haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax heue.
7 Simiang Shui-li-yax pauh ka pui naing Yaw-ha-see yum,
mai dig kix awm phawng tiex yuh nan,
deuk ngie pui naing brug brung kix tix phuan kaux,
ling law kao lhang,
pui naing hu ka caong tix tix mheen kaux khaing heue.
8 Kaing yuh Yaw-ha-see tang ceu khankix,
mai grawng pa kied kua nawh hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka daux Lai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
9 Yam hoik yum nawh,
kix si paung nawh ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yaw-hua-see plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see
10 Yam giex simiang Yo-tax pa kaux Yaw-a-see kaing daux tix ngoe aliah num,
kawn simex Yaw-ha-see pa kaux Yaw-hua-see mawh simiang pui Yi-seu-liex;
nawh giex kaing ka piang pui Sa-ma-li-yax kao liah num.
11 Nawh yuh kaing pa caox pa caig ka plak sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex,
mai hawt phi map pa thung simiang Yie-law-paw-ang pui Yi-seu-liex yuh nan.
12 Kaing yuh Yaw-hua-see tang ceu khankix,
khawm grawng pa kied kua nawh yuh naing tieb tie ka simiang pui Yo-tax pa kaux Ya-ma-shiex,
hoik tiiem tix sidawng ka dauxLai Been Simiang Pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik.
13 Yam hoik yum Yaw-hua-see,
kix si paung nawh ka daux si meeig pui nyiex ma simiang ka haktiex Sa-ma-li-yax;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Yie-law-paw-ang ka daux ra kaux plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh heue.
Yi-li-sa yum
14 aCao jhai Yi-li-sa pon jhawm saux pa nu tix pai;
yam hoik dex sang yum nawh,
simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see hu jhak ka nawh,
yiiem mai kok yo ah nin:
“Keeing aux eue,
keeing aux eue!
Cao kia ngien pa ting riang pui Yi-seu-liex eue!”
15 Yi-li-sa tawx lox ka simiang ah nin:
“Maix hu teei ak puh mai te hoek.”
Yaw-hua-see tom hu teei hoek.
16 Yi-li-sa nhie nawh rien tix vaik ka ak puh,
simiang hawt tix yuh.
Yi-li-sa teei taix tix deuh ka piang taix simiang,
17 simiang tom hawt tix yuh awm lox pa hoik nhie cao jhai nawh yuh,
mai paoh ka daux doh pa veeh tix plak Shui-li-yax.
Yi-li-sa ah nin:
“Puing ak heue!”
Simiang puing kah,
cao jhai tom kok yo ah nin:
“Maix hoik mawh te cie Simiang Siyiex;
nawh sang kieh te in pon pex ka pui Shui-li-yax.
Maix tag sang puing pui Shui-li-yax ka haktiex Ya-fu,
tom hoik laux leeing kix heue.”
18 Khaix an,
Yi-li-sa nhie simiang teei ka te pa deuk puing piang tiex,
simiang puing ka loe taix,
tom naox puing kah.
19 Yi-li-sa pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex kied ang gaux rhawm kah,
mai ah nin ka simiang:
“Maix khaw tix puing phuan liah taix,
yuh nan,
maix tom taug pon pauh ka pui Shui-li-yax yum uik;
viang mawh nan,
yam in maix pon pex ngie kix loe bawg khaing heue.”
20 Yi-li-sa hoik yum;
kix si paung ka nawh heue.
Ku num pui Maw-ya kaing phung hoek tix puing ka pui Yi-seu-liex.
21 Koe tix bawg,
yam kawn si paung pui ka pui yum,
dum mawx dum an,
kix yaox pui tix cub lih hoek,
kix tom tik pui yum an laig ka daux si meeig Yi-li-sa,
yaog tix to.
Yam theeh pui yum an ka si-ang Yi-li-sa,
pui yum an tom kaoh im,
mai yaog tix kaoh cung heue.
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Shui-li-yax puing paox tix
22 Yam giex Yaw-ha-see ka kaing,
simiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Ha-shiiex ca kawn kha ka pui Yi-seu-liex ang jud.
23 Viang mawh nan,
mawh kheu hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex sijax mukheu tau mai Ya-paw-la-hang,
Yi-sha,
Ya-keux,
nawh tom caox rhawm ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
teem coi ka kix,
ang simie tix yuh kix laux leeing.
Nawh ang sax chix piawm ka pui cie tix heue.
24 Simiang pui Shui-li-yax pa kaux Ha-shiiex hoik yum;
kawn simex nawh pa kaux Piien Ha-tax plud tix mawh simiang khaix nawh.
25 Simiang pui Yi-seu-liex pa kaux Yaw-hua-see,
puing naing pex ka Piien Ha-tax loe bawg,
mai deeh pon teei ka veng pa hoik tud Piien Ha-tax,
ka daux bawg yam singaix ju keeing nawh pa kaux Yaw-ha-see heue.
