Pao-law brug reu hu haktiex Law-ma
27
Kheu hoik but rhawm tix sang kieh ex hu krax piang rawm ka haktiex Yi-ta-lix,
kix tom ap tix tawx Pao-law mai pui laung tang kaux ka Yo-liox pui naing cao kuang “nyiex bang” Law-ma.
2 Ex haok laig daux reu pa kaoh khaing Ya-ta-mi-thiien tix lhang;
reu in tag sang yaog tix hu,
mai lawng ka diang hawt rawm haktiex Ya-si-yax ra loe dauh.
Koe pui Thie-sa-law-ni-cia haktiex Ma-chi-toeng,
ceu kaux nawh mawh Ya-li-ta-ku,
pa hu tau mai ex.
3 Ka daux singaix khaix hoek ka Si-toeng,
Yo-liox kia ngien ka Pao-law,
mai cu nawh hu sawg paox grawm tix kia ngien ka tix.
4 Ex baox brug reu hu khaing dee an,
mawh kheu si pup ex ka bhaung ting,
reu tom hu dex Sai-phu-lu-see plak khaix bhaung.
5 Ex tiang hu ka plak Ci-li-cia,
Phang-fe-li-yax,
hoek ka Me-la haktiex Lui-cia.
6 Ka dee an,
Yo-liox yaox ka reu tix lhang pa hoek khaing Ya-li-sang-tax,
pa sang phat hu plak haktiex Yi-ta-lix,
kix tom nhie ex haok laig daux reu heue.
7 Tix neh ra loe singaix,
reu kied koi,
kix tom taug hoek dex ka baoh haktiex Keu-ni-thux.
Mawh kheu krax pa tie bhaung,
ex ang pon nied tix hu awm hu sivoe an yuh nan,
ex tom hu dex ka plak khaix klawn tiex Kheu-li-theu,
mai luan hu khaing dee kawk Sa-maw-ni.
8 Ex hu ka praok rawm sigrit sigra,
sau riang tix hu tom taug hoek ka Cia-ao-kang tix dauh,
pa ang lai khan tix singai khaing veng La-si-yax heue.
9 Ex hoik gla bawg yam tom nie,
hoik yuh ka poi pa tuah ka priex preem,
mai ang lai khaw tix nied tix brug reu hu.
Mawh kheu nan,
Pao-law krai ka pui hun,
10 mai ah nin:
“Ex pui ku kaux,
jhak tix yaox phan ex nied tix brug reu hu khaing dee in,
hoik mawh krax pa nu tete;
ja lao kraung viex ex mai reu ang laux leeing,
pie ju ex kawx hoik kied nu tix ah heue.”
11 Viang mawh nan,
pui naing yum rhawm ka lox cao kuang reu mai cao reu,
ang yum rhawm ka lox Pao-law.
12 Diang rawm in kawx ang mawh pa mhawm tix hu ka bawg yam kuat,
mawh kheu nin,
pui hun hoik cu kix phat reu,
son sang pon hoek ka Fe-ni-ci yam kuat.
Fe-ni-ci mawh diang dee klawn tiex Kheu-li-theu,
veeh tix haktiex plak klip plak seh mai haktiex plak klip plak laong heue. a
Bhaung ting piang rawm
13 Yam an,
koe bhaung awn pa phlu tix hoek khaing plak seh,
pui hun ce nawh mawh hawt tix phat hu awm been prim yuh nan,
kix tom yaog goik phat reu hu,
mai dex ka klawn tiex Kheu-li-theu.
14 Ang koe miex glang tix,
koe ceu pa kok mawh “bhaung plak laong plak lih singaix” bhaung ting phlu tix hoek khaing piang klawn tiex.
15 Bhaung klauh ka reu,
ang lai pon tie kah,
ex ang lai tawng ka sang yuh tix,
mai ghram bhaung phlu reu hu.
16 Yam dex reu ka piang klawn tiex iak pa kok mawh Kao-tax,
ex tom taug yaox tix kieh reu in ang hoek krax laux leeing.
17 Pui pa chix rawm khankix bluh reu an haok piang reu ting,
teei maox phrawk kah.
Kix baox lhat reu kleeing tix ka piang pak mhaik Sai-eu-ti,
kix tom pluat man vhak reu,
kieh reu si bloe hawt bhaung.
18 Bhaung ting ca nied tix klauh kix,
ka daux singaix khaix,
kix jauh tix tik kraung viex tix daux rawm,
19 baox koe tix singaix,
khawm kraung pa ot daux reu kawx hoik tik khawm uik.
20 Khaix hoik luan tom rheeng bawg yam,
ex ang lai yaox singaix mai simuing;
bhaung ting nied tix phlu,
dee si dauh ex hoik ploi ka grawng sang simie tix pon krax pa klawd ju.
21 Pui ka daux reu hoik ang lai yaox tix ih ka ceu pa ih miex ngaix mawx,
Pao-law cung ka plak sivoe ngai kix ah nin:
“Pui ku kaux,
phan pex dawg cu ka lox aux,
paw vi phat reu hu ka klawn tiex Kheu-li-theu,
ex taug ang yaox tix som nai yuh nin khawm uik.
22 Yam in aux ang cu pex khi rhawm,
ka daux pex ang lai koe pui pa som ju,
mawh ngie som ka reu in khaing.
23 Ka daux bo kawx,
Siyiex pa tawng pa krub aux ploi kawn cau Siyiex tix ah nin:
24 ‘Pao-law,
paw vi lhat heue!
Maix tag caw tix cung ka plak sivoe ngai Vawng.
Kheu nan,
mawh kheu krax muh Siyiex,
nawh hoik but rhawm tix kia ngien ka ju pui pa ot daux reu tau mai maix khawm uik.’
25 Mawh kheu nan,
pui ku kaux paw vi lai khi rhawm;
aux hoik yum rhawm ka Siyiex grawng gum lox pa hoik krai nawh ka aux tag sang lih tix mawh.
26 Viang mawh nan,
ex kawn sang si bloe hoek ka piang klawn tiex tix dauh heue.”
27 Yam hoik hoek ka daux bo kao pon singaix,
reu cie ex ca kawn si bloe tix ot piang Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ya-teu-li-yax.
Yam hoek greung sawm,
pui pa chix rawm ce ex hoik dex hoek piang tiex,
28 kix tom si vun maox diag,
mai tiag tix yaox ka been raux rawm,
koe tipon kongjhee;
thiem baox tiag tix yaox ka hu plak sivoe ngai,
koe tix ngoe kongjhee.
29 Kix lhat reu si gleeing ka simaox,
kix tom si vun goik vhak pon mu ka kre reu,
mai kied simie praix riang.
30 Pui pa chix rawm khankix keud tix sang to lih khaing daux reu in,
kix ploi reu taoh pui laig daux rawm,
lhien tix sang si vun goik vhak khaing kaing reu.
31 Pao-law krai ka simiang naing mai pui naing khankix ah nin:
“Pui in kix ang lai ot daux reu,
pex tag ang pon tix luat luan heue.”
32 Pui naing tom tat ka maox reu taoh pui,
mai kieh reu si bloe tix hu.
33 Yam hoik dex riang praix,
Pao-law nhie pui hun ih ka ceu pa ih.
Nawh ah nin:
“Pex khi rhawm khi rhi,
ang ih ang nyaux,
hoik koe kao pon singaix.
34 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai ka pex,
ih ka ceu pa ih tom taug pon koe riang,
mawh kheu pex ku kaux ang yaox tix som nai pie tix gawng hauk kaing heue.”
35 Khaix hoik krai lox in,
Pao-law teei lon khao mun,
yaog yaw Siyiex ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
khaix an si vit ih kah.
36 Pui hun tom taug nyawm rhawm,
mai ih ka heue.
37 Ex pa brug reu tau khawm uik koe ra yieh atigliah liah kaux.b
38 Khaix hoik sak ih pui hun,
son sang tieh ka pa cien reu,
kix baox si vun tix tik nghox ca ka daux rawm heue.
Reu teeh
39 Yam hoik riang praix,
pui pa chix rawm ang tawng dee an,
viang mawh nan,
kix yaox dee ku kak rawm tix dauh,
kheu nan dee an mawh pak mhaik pa koe kah,
kix tom but rhawm tix sau riang tix bluh reu hu.
40 Kix ran mug ka gawng rhiem vhak an,
mai tik goik vhak reu ka daux rawm,
khaix an ploi maox reu,
bluh man kaing reu yaog tix,
kieh bhaung phlu reu hu ka praok rawm.
41 Viang mawh nan,
reu chun ka pak mhaik,
kaing reu tuh ang lai pon chuh,
puk pak rawm sau riang tix klauh ka kre reu,
reu laux heue.
42 Lien keud tix sang pauh ka pui laung khankix,
lhat kix loe tix to hu daux rawm.
43 Viang mawh nan,
simiang naing sang taoh ka Pao-law,
ang cu kix nieb pon yuh,
mawh kheu nan,
tawx lox nhie pui pa chix rawm ran taing tix laig daux rawm,
mai loe tix hu praok rawm.
44 Pui pa deuk khankix teei phia mai baux khaox pa koe daux reu loe tix hu ka praok rawm.
Yuh nin,
pui hun tom tag pon tix luat luan khawm uik heue.
