Yie-li-mi chawk cheng ka Simiang Siyiex
12
Simiang Siyiex eue,
aux sang krai maix mhong ka see pa koe tix,
ku bawg ku yam hoik mawh cie maix pa khaw.
Viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn sang chawk ka maix ah nin:
ceu tix pa mawh pa khaw caw lie?
Yuh ka mawx pui caox lai pon tix mhawm kaing yuh lie?
Yuh ka mawx pui pie pui lhien lai pon tix yaox krax nyawm ot nyawm koe lie?
2 Maix sum ka kix,
kix tom tawx riah tix,
Khaix an haok huan,
mai plix plix.
Jheeh kix si muad ka maix jheeng jheeng,
ka daux rhawm rhi kix ang koe maix.
3 Viang mawh nan Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix hoik tawng ka aux;
maix hoik tawng ka kaing pa yuh aux,
hoik tawng grawng pa muh aux ka maix khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix.
Khiox khaw maix bluh pui caox hu,
awm bluh ka yung pa viex laig daux bang pauh yuh nan,
laung kix tom hoek ka daux singaix yam pauh kix yum.
4 Haktiex yix kawn sang kroh tom hoek miex greeng lie?
Rib pa koe ka daux si peem sang kroh tom hoek yam mawx lie?
Sim mai to tiag khankix hoik yum khawm uik,
hoik mawh kheu phi map paox ceu paox cad aux;
Kix ah nin:
Siyiex ang chix yaox ka kaing pa yuh ex khankix heue.a
 
5 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Yie-li-mi eue,
phan maix lah to mai pui tag sang chix uik riang rao,
maix ce tix sang pon yuh lah to mai brung lie?
Phan maix ot ka daux tiag pa yi tag ang pon tix cung kaux cong,
maix ce tix sang pon cung kaux cong daux nong khaox ka praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang lie?
6 Pux aik maix pui nyiex pui ma maix hoik tieh vheen ka maix khawm uik;
kix khrawm tix yuh naing ka maix.
Viang kix hoik krai kawx lox mhawm jhawm jiet,
maix ang pon tix yum rhawm ka kix heue.
Simiang Siyiex laux rhawm son pui cie tix
7 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Aux hoik tik vud ka pui Yi-seu-liex,
tik vud ka meung pa hoik laug aux.
Aux hoik ap tix tawx pui cie tix pa muh aux ka pui koeng khankix.
8 Pui pa laug aux hoik tieh vheen ka aux;
kix awm sivai sang si ka daux nong khaox pa hoek tix graox ka aux,
mawh kheu nan aux yum koeng ka kix.
9 Pui pa laug aux hoik awm kawn sim pa hoek klang tix sang teei yuh nan;
miex klang kok yo to tiag hoek tix ih tau.
10 Simiang ka meung ka piang haktiex hoik yuh ka si peem plix siiet aux laux;
kix hoik dug dig ka ma kaung aux,
kieh ma kaung pa muh aux lih tix rien.
11 Kix kieh kaung ma lih tix mawh daux tiag daux tu heue.
 
Ka plak sivoe ngai aux hoik mawh dee yiien dee ang tid tix koe.
Gawng lua klawng ngawd hoik nhik khawm uik,
ang yaox pui pa teem coi ka tix.
12 Cao pa yuh laux leeing haok gawng tiang ngawd.
Aux kieh naing tauh hu ka haktiex khawm uik;
ang koe pui pa pon yaox tix nyawm ot nyawm koe.
13 Pa sum pui cie aux mawh nghox ca,
mai pa khrawm vawg kix mawh pang kak;
kix taug mao ka yuh tix kaing,
viang mawh nan,
hoik mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik.
Yam tawx aux krax pa song rhawm song rhi,
kix tag ang yaox tix khrawm vawg tid tix heue.
Lox deuh Simiang Siyiex ka pui Yi-seu-liex
14 Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Phan mawh meung pa ot sivie siveng an kix,
mawh kheu hoik tud rhim kix ka haktiex pa tawx aux ka pui cie tix Yi-seu-liex,
aux tag sang laoh tix tik kix khawm riah khaing haktiex in;
aux tag sang taoh pui Yo-tax lih khaing ka daux kix.
15 Viang mawh nan,
khaix hoik laoh tix tik ka kix,
tag sang deeh caox rhawm ka kix,
kieh kix deeh hoek ka haktiex tix heue.
16 Phan kix rieb ka been li pui cie aux khawm rhawm khawm rhi tix,
tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa im ju leeing ju,
awm hoik nhie kix pui cie aux tiex tu ka daux ceu kaux Pa-lix yuh nan,
kix tom tag yaox tix ot tau mai pui cie aux,
haok huan tau mai pui cie aux.
17 Phan kix ang nghiet ka aux,
aux tag sang laoh tix tik kix khawm riah,
yuh kix laux lak pak leeing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
