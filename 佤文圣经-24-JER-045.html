Gum lox pa tat Simiang Siyiex tix tawx ka Pa-lux
45
aYam mawh kawn simex Yaw-si-yax pa kaux Yaw-ya-cing simiang Yo-tax ka daux pon num,
Pa-lux tiiem gum lox pa krai aux ka nawh.
Khaix an,
aux krai ka nawh,
2 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik sax krai yuh nin:
“Pa-lux eue,
3 to kaux maix ah nin:
‘Aux hoik laux leeing heue!
Simiang Siyiex hoik baox thiem thie krax kha rhawm kha rhi ka piang aux.
Aux hoik kroh krawx kheu riag yo tix,
mai ang pon krax nyawm ot nyawm koe heue.’
4 “Viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang yieh laux ka nyiex pa hoik sum tix,
mai laoh tix tik ka ceu pa hoik sum tix.
Aux tag sang yuh yuh nin ka haktiex.
5 Maix ce tix sang yuh kaing ting lih son to kaux tix lie?
Paw cawm keud heue!
Aux tag sang ploi krax laux leeing lih hoek ka piang kawn pui khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
maix hu dee mawx ang ah,
ca kawn koe bawg yam to tix klawd ju.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
