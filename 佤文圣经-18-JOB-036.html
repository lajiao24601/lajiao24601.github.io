36
Yi-li-hux ca nied tix krai ah nin:
2 Viex maix gun rhawm tix nghiet ka lox pa kah aux;
aux kawn sang krai lox ceeix ceeix tang Siyiex.
3 Ka daux pa tawng aux kied nie;
aux sang krai sidawng ka krax pa khaw caw cao pa phan pa yuh aux.
4 Gum lox pa krai aux mawh tete;
ang koe pa pie lhien;
maix tag sang chix yaox,
ka plak sivoe ngai maix koe pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi ka tix kaux tete heue.
 
5 Siyiex kied ting riang ting rao,
viang mawh nan,
nawh ang map nai ka kawn pui;
nawh ang koe kaing ceu pa ang tawng.
6 Nawh ang kieh pui phi map im ju;
nawh teem pui hot pui bhawm yuh krax rauh see.
7 Nawh kia ngien ka pui rauh pui see;
nawh kieh kix awm simiang pa giex kaing ka piang pui,
mai pon krax pa yung yi.
8 Viang mawh nan,
phan kham krax pa prawk ka ceng,
mawh kheu yaox kix krax kha rhawm son kaing pa yuh tix,
9 Siyiex sang jhi lih ka phi map kix,
kieh kix tawng to kaux tix ting rhawm cau nyhau.
10 Nawh sang kieh kix gaux rhawm tix rieb ka krax pa ne raw ka kix,
kieh kix lih hu khaing daux krax pa caox pa caig.
11 Phan kix nghiet ka Siyiex,
mai yuh kaing jhaw nawh,
kix tom tag pon tix yaox ka singaix pa nyawm ot nyawm koe ka piang haktiex,
kied nyawm ot mai krax pa im ju kix.
12 Phan ang mawh nan,
kix tag sang yum ka daux pa daox aw pa ang tix tawng,
tiang ka klawng yum laig daux meung yum heue.
 
13 Pui pa ang lhat graung yung yi ka Siyiex,
krax song rhawm kix ang chix naox tix;
kix kham krax ciyang,
mai kawn ca ang khiox khaw Siyiex teem coi tix.
14 Ju kix dawt yum yam nhum ju tix,
ju hoik uik ka daux krax pa kaig pa tua.
15 Kheu nan,
Siyiex teei krax kha rhawm sidiex gau ka kawn pui,
teei krax laux krax leeing paoh ka ngai pui heue.
 
16 Siyiex hoik taoh maix khaing daux krax kha rhawm kha rhi,
kieh maix pon krax pa nyawm ot nyawm koe;
ka piang pheen maix hoik khan naog ka priex nyawm rawm lhong.
17 Viang mawh nan,
yam in maix kham krax ciyang pa khaw maix tix kham;
maix ang pon to luan khaing krax prawng krax ca pa khaw caw.
18 Priex rhawm tix heue,
paw vi kieh mau chun praok pon nyaw glaw ka maix;
paw vi kieh krax mag mi duk thung maix guah khaing krax.
19 Krax mag mi maix koe dee mhawm son maix lie?
Siawp sibrawm pa koe maix chix pon teem ka maix lie?
20 Paw vi simie pa faik pon sawm lih hoek;
paw vi simie bawg yam laux leeing meung ka piang haktiex lih hoek.
21 Priex rhawm tix heue,
paw vi hu plak krax pa hu pui pa caox pa caig;
krax pa kha maix rhawm tix ne raw maix hu singai khaing nawh heue.
 
22 Tag caw tix doh keud ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex kied ting kied lhaong;
nawh mawh sara pa ting pa lhaong tix kaux.
23 Pui mawx pon ciak ci nawh yuh kaing pa khaw nawh tix yuh?
Pui mawx pon ne raw ka kaing pa yuh nawh ang khaw?
24 Kaing pa yuh nawh tag pon krax pa yaog yaw leeing ju;
maix kawx tag caw tix yaog yaw ka nawh.
25 Kawn pui piang haktiex yaog kaing tix jhak ka kaing pa yuh nawh;
ex kok jhak ngie ka dee singai khaing.a
26 Siyiex kied ting kied lhaong,
ex ang koe riang tix tawng;
ex ang pon simin ka num heue.
 
27 Siyiex tawt tix teei rawm lih khaing haktiex,
kieh tieh lih mawh rawm ciex.
28 Nawh kieh lhiex lih cawd khaing daux pai awm,
caux ka piang kawn pui khawm uik.
29 Pui mawx pon tawng Siyiex kieh pai awm yaog tix yuh ka mawx?
Pui mawx pon tawng nawh kieh sah lih tuh khaing nyiex dee ot tix mai lih ka rao ma yuh ka mawx?
30 Nawh kieh pa riang tuh sah jhan riang daux rao ma,
kieh dee kied raux rawm sigrit sigra kawn ca faik.
31 Nawh eeib ka pui khawm bee ma yuh nin,
tawx kix ih priex preem tom kub.
32 Nawh teei taix tix jhawp ka sah,
tawx lox kieh nawh krox ka ceu pa tuh tix.
33 Sah hoik krai sivoe sang lih hoek bhaung ting,
pie khawng brie kawx hoik tawng tix to mox tix heue.
