Pa lih mawh ceu cao tix mu
4
Mawh kheu nan,
aux sidiex gau ka pex,
kheu pa yuh aux kaing jhaw Simiang Siyiex mai lih mawh pui laung,
pa mawh diag tong pa yuh pex son pui,
pa khaw mai yam kok Siyiex ka pex.
2 aPex tag caw tix tiiem rhawm,
awn rhawm,
gun yeung,
kieh krax muh ra lai va rhawm ka paox tix,
3 kieh pa nhim ra lai si doe ka paox tix,
sau riang tix giex tom cong ka rhawm rhi pa hoik tawx Khuan Singax Singeh tix mu.
4 To kaux koe ngie tix mu khaing,
Khuan Singax Singeh koe ngie tix mu khaing,
awm hoik kok Siyiex pex hoek pon ka krax keud pon pot jun pot pa koe tix ceu khaing.
5 Simiang Siyiex koe ngie tix kaux khaing,
krax yum rhawm tix ceu,
krax cub com tix ceu.
6 Siyiex koe ngie tix kaux khaing,
pa mawh keeing kawn pui;
nawh ot ka daux ceu pa koe khawm uik,
mai giex kaing kuang ka piang ceu pa koe khawm uik heue.
7 Ex pui ku kaux tag sang hawt ka pa hoik guah Khrit,
mai rieb tix pon ka buan son pa blawn.
8 bAwm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
yam haok nawh hoek ka dee pa kied blawn lhaong,
mai viex ka pui pa laung hu tom hun;
nawh tawx buan son ka pui heue.
9 “Nawh haok” gum lox in dee bleeh mawh ceu tix lie?
In mawh krai ah nin:
nawh hoik sax ran lih hoek sivoe,
mai hoek ka dee pa kied blawn raux.
10 Pa “lih hoek” an tag mawh “haok” hoek ka kaw an piang rao ma;
nawh yuh nin mawh son sang kieh naog ka ceu pa koe khawm uik.
11 Nawh kawx hoik mawh kaw “pa tawx buan son ka pui”;
nawh ciak ci pui tix blah mawh kawn cau,
tix blah mawh cao jhai,
tix blah mawh pui pa sidiex gum lox buan,
tix blah mawh sara ting mai sara.
12 Kaing ting mawh son sang rien khre pui cie Siyiex yuh kaing son nawh,
pa sum ka ceu cao pa mawh to kaux Khrit.
13 Dee nghoik,
ex tag sang koe krax yum rhawm mai tawng yung ka kawn simex Siyiex;
ex tag sang lih mawh pui ting,
mai hoek ka diag tong pa grum naog Khrit.
14 Yuh nin,
ex taug sang ang lai baox awm kawn nyawm,
glup ka daux krax keud pa pie lhien pui,
mai yung yang ka daux pa hawt ka been li cie pui tang ceu.
15 Deeh tieh tix,
kieh ex krai lox pa rauh see ka daux krax muh,
mai haok huan ang jud ka kaing ku ceu plak Khrit.
Nawh mawh kaing;
16 cto kaux khawm mu tag een rhawm tix ka nawh khawm uik,
ra lai yuh kaing tau ka daux pa koe jhong si-ang si niag,
ra lai kleuk ka paox tix.
Yuh nin,
yam lih pak peung to kaux tix tawx riang,
to kaux taug sang chix ting haok huan si nyek si nyek ka daux krax muh,
mai sum tix kaux cong heue.
Ju pa khraox ka daux Khrit
17 Yam in aux krai sidawng ka pex ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex yuh nin:
paw lai ot ka daux krax ot krax koe pa awm pui tang ceu tang cad yuh nan.
Krax keud krax sawn kix mawh pa ang koe ceu,
18 rhawm rhi faik,
ang koe ju pa tawx Siyiex;
mawh kheu hoik daox aw kix,
mai hoik kiien rhawm kiien rhi kix.
19 Kix ang lai koe rhawm rhi krax pa kaig tua;
hawt ka alo nex nham,
ang lai koe krax pa rien khre to kaux tix ka kaing ceu pa caox pa caig ku ceu heue.
20 Viang mawh nan,
pex pa hoik gau ka Khrit ang mawh nan heue!
21 Ang pex sax mhong ka grawh grawng nawh,
yuh ka pui gau nawh,
gau ka pa mawh pa meh tom taug pon ka daux Ye-sux.
22 dPhan mawh nan,
pex taug caw tix peeik ka to kaux “pa prim tix” pa kieh pex laux leeing;
to kaux pa prim an mawh pa laux leeing,
pa kaoh khaing krax nyaw alo nex nham.
23 Rhawm rhi krax keud krax sawn pex tag caw tix kieh loh tix mawh pa khraox,
24 etag caw tix ceep ka “pa khraox tix”;
pa khraox in hoik mawh pa hawt tix yuh ka pak peung Siyiex,
tag sang pon lih tix mawh ka ceu pa rauh see mai pa singax singeh khaing daux pa mawh pa meh heue.
25 fMawh kheu nan,
pex ang pon tix lai baox pie lhien heue!
Pui ku kaux tag caw tix krai lox pa mawh pa meh ka pux aik tix,
kheu hoik mawh ex pak peung to kaux Khrit khawm uik.
26 gPhan pex koe krax pa song rhawm,
ang pon tix yuh phi map kheu krax pa song rhawm in;
mai ang pon tix song rhawm leeing lang singaix,
27 ang pon tix kieh phi caox koe bawg yam tix laig.
28 Pa briex sivoe an,
ang lai pon tix baox briex;
tag caw tix yuh kaing khawm ra khawn taix tom taug chix pon teem coi ka pui hot pui sibhawm.
29 Paw vi krai lox pa kieh pui laux rhawm,
tag caw tix krai lox pa teem coi ka pui,
lox mhawm ka pui,
mai kieh pui pa mhong pon ka buan son.
30 Paw vi kieh Khuan Singax Singeh Siyiex khi rhawm;
mawh kheu Khuan Singax Singeh hoik mawh mhai cum pa tawx Siyiex ka pex,
son sang giex kaux cong ka singaix pa tag sang lih hoek Siyiex tix pluat ploi ka pex.
31 Pex tag caw tix tik ka ceu pa koeng,
ceu pa caox,
ceu pa song rhawm khawm uik.
Ang pon tix lai baox lawh maw,
map nai;
ang pon tix lai baox koe krax pa pawk bix khawm uik.
32 hTag caw tix ra lai kia ngien paox tix ka daux krax pa caox rhawm,
ra lai pluat ploi ka paox tix,
awm hoik pluat ploi Siyiex pex ka daux Khrit yuh nan heue.
