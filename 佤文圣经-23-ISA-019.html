Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka Ai-cix
19
aPlak greem mawh gum lox pa koe grawh grawng mai Ai-cix heue.
Simiang Siyiex brug lon pai awm pa brax hoek ka Ai-cix.
Riang rao pa krub pui Ai-cix hoik si yaung ka plak sivoe ngai nawh;
pui Ai-cix hoik grai rhawm rhi pa kua.
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Aux tag sang kieh naing lih koe ka daux Ai-cix,
mai kieh pux aik deeh puing pux aik,
paox plawg deeh puing paox plawg,
veng deeh puing paox veng,
simiang ra lai ghrang siawp sibrawm.
3 Aux tag sang kieh krax keud krax sawn pui Ai-cix mawh pa giex pa going,
rhawm rhi pa kua kawn pui ka naing hoik grai.
Kix tom khiox khaw riang rao teem coi tix,
khiox khaw ka cao pa chix si meed,
cao pa chix jhak ka lai tiag taix,
mai kok khuan pui yum teem coi tix.
4 Aux tag sang ap pui Ai-cix ka daux tawm taix simiang pa caox pa caig tix kaux,
simiang pa caox tix kaux,
mai kieh nawh giex kaing ka piang kix.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pa kied ting kied lhaong,
pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
5 Rawm klawng Ni-law tag sang dieh,
tag sang hit sang kroh.
6 Taox cux hoik lih kheu pa kroh rawm klawng an,
gawng priem mai nhu oi tag sang kroh sang vhit;
7 To ma pa koe ka praok rawm klawng Ni-law khawm ra plak hoik yum kroh,
mai bhaung hoik phlu heue.
8 Pui eei tix ka rub kax pa paung ot ka praok rawm klawng Ni-law hoik yiiem khawm uik;
gawng miiet kix kax mai rub kix ang lai koe ceu pie ceeix kawx.
9 Pui pa caing man keei sum mai pa caing man keei pa mhawm hoik laux rhawm khawm uik;
10 Pui taing man mai cang mai tang kaux hoik laux leeing som nai.
11 Simiang ka daux veng Saw-ang kied sidaing mawh pa daox aw tete.
Pui Ai-cix pa blawn koe krax lhak lhiao khaing paox tix kawx ang lai koe rhawm pa chix keud tid tix.
Kix lai kawn sang pon krai mawh tix pui prim dix dix pa koe ping nyia,
mai pui pa sang plud tix mawh simiang ka plak sivoe ngai Vawng lie?
12 Simiang Ai-cix eue,
cao pa teem coi maix pa lhak lhiao an hu dee mawx lie?
Kix kawx tee chix krai ka maix,
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik koe krax keud krax sawn ceu pa tix ka Ai-cix.
13 Simiang Saw-ang mai simiang Meung-fe-see hoik kied daox aw,
mai kix khaw tix duk thung ka meung tix mhawm mhawm,
viang mawh nan,
krax duk thung kix hoik lud.
14 Simiang Siyiex hoik kieh kix tawx krax keud krax sawn pa daox aw,
tom kieh can pa yuh pui Ai-cix lih mawh pa lud khawm uik;
meung kix ang lai kaux lai cong,
mai hoik awm pui pa yoek ka plai yuh nan,
deeh cang klad ka piang ceu pa hoik hao tix lih.
15 Ka daux Ai-cix,
mawh pui mag mi ang ah,
pui hot sibhawm ang ah,
pui koe min ang ah,
pui iak min ang ah,
mai ang lai koe ceu tix kaux kawx heue.
Pui Ai-cix tag sang krub ka Simiang Siyiex
16 Hoek bawg yam an,
simex pui Ai-cix khankix tag sang iak rhawm awm kawn bun yuh nan.
Kix yaox grawng pa yaog Simiang Siyiex taix tix ciyang ka kix,
mai kix tom taug si yaung si kua kah.
17 Yam doh keud pui Ai-cix ka krax pa hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing ka krax pa im ju kix yuh ka mawx,
kix tom taug lhat graung ka meung Yo-tax heue.
18 Yam hoik hoek singaix an,
veng ting Ai-cix phuan mu hoik krai ka lox pui Si-paw-lai khawm uik.
Pui hun tag sang tiex tu tix krub ka daux ceu kaux Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing.
Veng an tix mu tag sang kok mawh “veng singaix” heue.
19 Yam hoek singaix an,
Ai-cix tag sang koe veng tix mu pa sum khu dee tan son Simiang Siyiex;
ka daog mee haktiex Ai-cix tag sang sum rong simaox jung tix son Simiang Siyiex.
20 In kix hoik mawh pa jhi grawng pa hoik ot Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing ka daux pui Ai-cix.
Yam yaox kix krax pa gut kheem,
mai khiox khaw Simiang Siyiex teem coi tix,
Simiang Siyiex tag sang ploi cao taoh,
pa mawh cao pa kia ngien ka kix hoek tix taoh ka kix.
21 Simiang Siyiex tag sang jhi to kaux tix ka pui Ai-cix,
mai kieh pui Ai-cix doh keud ka nawh,
krub ka nawh,
tan lu Krax-tan Khawng-brie mai priex preem tang ceu ka nawh.
Kix tag sang yuh krax pa hoik ap pong tix tiex tu ka plak sivoe ngaix nawh heue.
22 Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka kix,
tag sang deeh pai kix moi nang.
Kix tag sang deeh kling tix deeh,
mai mawh cie nawh.
Nawh tag sang nghiet ka lox buan pa rawg kix,
mai pai kix moi heue.
23 Yam hoek singaix an,
ka si nax Ai-cix mai Ya-sux tag sang paih krax koe ka tix nyaing.
Pui in kix khawm ra meung tag sang lai ling ka paox tix;
kix tag sang rom tix krub ka Simiang Siyiex.
24 Yam hoek singaix an,
Yi-seu-liex mai Ai-cix tag sang rom tix tau mai Ya-sux;
meung ting in kix loe mu tag sang kieh kawn pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik pon buan son.
25 Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh simiang kru naing tag sang tawx buan son ka kix,
mai ah nin:
“Ai-cix,
pa mawh pui cie aux;
Ya-sux,
pa mawh ceu pa phan pa yuh aux;
Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh pui pa hoik laug aux;
aux sang tawx buan ka kix heue!”
