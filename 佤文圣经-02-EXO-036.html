36
“Pi-sha-liex,
Ya-heu-li-ya-pawx mai pa chix yuh ka kaing taix tang ceu,
pui pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex koe krax ping nyia cuyi,
krax chix,
tawng grawng sang sum nyiex gox buan man yuh ka mawx,
mai kix tag hawt awm lox tawm Simiang Siyiex,
yuh ka kaing jhaw an heue.”
Kawn pui tan lu ka khawng phak
2 Maw-si khrawm ka Pi-sha-liex,
Ya-heu-li-ya-pawx mai can pui pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ping nyia kah,
pa viex rhawm rhi tix yuh kaing khankix,
tawx lox nhie kix yuh kaing.
3 Kix rieb tix teei khawng phak pa tan pa lu ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khaing Maw-si,
son sang sum ka nyiex gox buan man.
Kheu nan,
ku ngaix pon ngawb,
pui khankix mai ca baox viex khawng tan hoek,
tawx ka Maw-si.
4 Mawh kheu nan,
pui pa kuang ka pa yuh kaing taix khankix,
naox ka kaing jhaw pa yuh kix,
5 mai hoek ka Maw-si,
krai ah nin:
“Khawng phak pa tan pui khankix hoik nie khaing pa sang siao son kaing jhaw pa hoik tawm Simiang Siyiex heue.”
6 Kheu nan,
Maw-si tawx lox hu ka nyiex bang khawm uik,
krai ka pui khankix,
ang cu kix lai baox tan lu tix ka son nyiex gox buan man.
7 Mawh kheu khawng phak pa hoik rieb kix,
hoik kub hoik haoh,
son kaing jhaw pa sang yuh khawm uik heue.
Sum nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex
(Lai Lih-khaing Ai-cix 26.1–37)
8 Can pui pa yuh kaing ka daux kix,
pa mawh pui pa koe ping nyia khankix,
yuh ka nyiex gox buan man Simiang Siyiex.
Kix yuh man tie plak daux nyiex gox buan man ka man kao bua,
man tie in caw tix taing ka man keei sum mai pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
keei pa raoh rawt,
mai taing tix sau riang Ci-lu-paw ka piang ra mu,
9 man tie ku bua kieh khaw paox tix,
plak lang kao ra kongjhee,
plak vieh ra kongjhee.
10 Kix caing man phuan bua kleuk paox lih mawh tix dauh,
an phuan bua kawx yuh awm an heue.
11 Kix caing daux jhawk maox ka man pa klai rao ma ka praok ka daog ku bua.
12 Kix caing daux jhawk maox tix phuan daux,
ot ka kaing man tie tix dauh,
an tix phuan daux caing ot ka daog dee die man tie ka daux ra dauh.
13 Kix baox yuh goik khri tix phuan mu,
mai kieh man tie an khawm ra plak kleuk paox tix tau heue.
14 Kix baox siao hauk pex taing man ka kao bua tix,
mai yuh man tawp braux nyiex gox buan man ka heue.
15 Man tie ku plah khaw paox tix:
plak lang kao loe kongjhee,
plak vieh ra kongjhee.
16 Kix caing man tie an phuan plah kleuk paox,
lih mawh tix dauh,
an liah plah kieh mawh tix dauh.
17 Kix caing man tie an tix dauh ka praok daux jhawk koe tix phuan mu,
mai ka daog an tang dauh kawx caw tix caing koe tix phuan mu.
18 Kix yuh goik tawng tix phuan mu,
mai kieh man khawm ra plak kleuk paox tix tau,
lih mawh braux nyiex gox buan man tix lhang.
19 Kix baox yuh braux nyiex gox buan man ka hak yung pa hoik siang tix raoh mai hak pa mhawm ra mu,
kieh mawh braux nyiex gox buan man jan plak praix heue.
20 Kix yuh pang joh phia jung nyiex gox buan man ka khaox Cing-heu-huang.
21 Pang joh phia jung an tix blah plak lhaong koe pon kongjhee,
plak viex koe tix gliah kongfeeng,
22 tang koe toh jhup ra mu,
son sang kieh pang joh phia jung kleuk ka paox tix.
Can pang joh phia jung khawm uik tag koe toh jhup pa yuh nin.
23 Kix yuh pang joh phia jung ot ka plak seh nyiex gox buan man tix nga plah.
24 Ka greem plah phia koe jaong mau tipon mu,
ku plah pang joh phia jung hoik mawh jaong mau pa kieh kaux ra mu heue.
25 Kix baox sum pang joh phia jung plak laong nyiex gox buan man tix nga plah,
26 mai daux jhup jaong mau tipon mu,
pang joh phia jung ku plah ka jaong plak greem tag koe ka daux jhup jaong mau ra mu.
27 Plak khaix nyiex gox buan man,
pa mawh plak klip singaix nyiex gox buan man,
kix jung ka pang joh phia jung liah plah;
28 ka dee kawk koe pang joh phia jung ra plah.
29 Pang joh phia jung ra plah kieh toh kleuk paox tix,
tom hoek ka toh plak piang,
lih mawh tix neh heue.
Pang joh phia ra plah ka daux kawk,
hoik yuh tix awm an heue.
30 Kheu nan khawm uik caw tix koe pang joh phia jung daix plah mai jhup jaong mau kao liah mu,
ka jaong pang joh phia jung plak greem ku plah tang koe jhup jaong mau ra mu heue.
31 Kix baox yuh lat ka khaox Cing-heu-huang kao phuan gawng;
ka praok pang joh phia jung nyiex gox buan plak in phuan gawng,
32 plak an phuan gawng;
plak khaix nyiex gox buan man,
pa mawh plak klip ka nyiex gox buan man,
mai kieh koe nang phuan gawng.
33 Lat pa koe sigreung,
sau ot ka nyoi pang joh phia jung,
jauh ka toh nyiex gox buan plak in tom phlat hoek ka toh plak an.
34 Kix om pang joh phia jung ka khri,
mai yuh daux jhawk ka khri,
son sang chix chup lat tix laig;
mai lat kawx om ka khri.
35 Kix yuh man tie ka man keei sum mai keei klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
keei pa raoh rawt tix plah,
mai taing riang Ci-lu-paw ka piang ra mu.
36 Kix baox yuh rong khaox pa sang vhak man tie pon rong ka khaox Cing-heu-huang,
rong an om ka khri;
mai ka piang rong an goik ka khri.
Kix baox yuh jaong mau pon mu son rong an.
37 Kix baox yuh man tie diang siviex laig daux nyiex gox buan man tix plah ka man keei sum mai keei pa klai rao ma,
pa lung pliex,
pa raoh rawt,
ka piang koe lai tai.
38 Kix yuh rong phuan gawng son man tie diang siviex in,
ka piang rong kieh koe goik.
Khaox grawng ka piang rong mai ka piang toh rong hoik om ka khri khawm uik,
mai baox yuh jaong tawng phuan mu son rong heue.
