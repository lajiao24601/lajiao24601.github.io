Rien ka dak tix
3
Pux aik pux o aux khankix eue,
pex ang caw tix simie tix mawh sara cao gau ku kaux,
mawh kheu hoik tawng pex pa mawh sara cao gau kawn lheu pon krax prawng ca pa lheu kin khaing pui pa gau pui ka khankix.
2 Ex yie yuh krax pa phit lud jheeng jheeng.
Pui pa ang lud loe ka lox ah si kah krai tix tag mawh pui pa grum mhawm;
nawh hoik pon rien ka to kaux tix khawm uik.
3 Ex sau keu ka jheeh brung,
tag pon kieh brung cu lox,
hu ka dee sang nhie ex nawh hu.
4 Baox jhak tix yaox ka reu tix lhang viang hoik mawh pa ting kawx,
yam phlu bhaung ting,
tag siao ngie ka goik vhak reu pa iak tix gawng khaing,
tag sang chix hawt khaix cao pa vai reu yuh nan,
mai kieh reu hu ka plak dee sang hu tix.
5 Awm an,
dak viang mawh pa iak kawx,
mai pon krai ka lox pa ting heue.
Keud tix yaox ka ngu iak pa koe ceeix khaing taug chix kieh ngu ting gleeing ang mawh lie?
6 Dak jiet awm ngu yuh nan,
mawh haktiex pa caox pa caig ka daux pak peung to kaux ex,
chix seeing brai yuh to kaux khawm uik;
nawh pon yuh laux leeing ka krax lawng kawn pui ka daux ngu maw-na-rhok.
7 Kawn pui pon kieh to sat,
sim saik,
viag vua,
mai ceu pa koe daux rawm khankix ngie;
tete te lai kawn pui hoik pon kieh to tiag ku ceu pon ngie.
8 Viang mawh nan,
kawn pui ang pon rien ka dak tix;
dak mawh ceu pa caox pa caig pa ang pui pon kieh ngie,
mawh jai ceu naog ka bhawm pa awp pa chix kieh pui yum.
9 aEx teei dak tix rhax lai yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex ex,
keeing Siyiex ex,
mai baox teei dak tix jhax lawg ka pui pa hoik phan Siyiex lih awm riang pak peung tix yuh nan.
10 Pa rhax lai yaog yaw mai pa jhax lawg mawh pa lih khaing daux jheeh tix mu khaing heue!
Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
in ang caw tix yuh nin heue!
11 Rawm keet tix daux chix buing tix lih ka rawm song mai rawm tie ra ceu lie?
12 Pux aik pux o khankix eue,
khaox plix ruh ang chix plix lih mawh plix khaox sime;
nhu plix shiet ang chix plix lih mawh plix khaox ruh;
daux rawm keet pa song krin kawx ang chix jauh lih mawh rawm pa tie heue.
Ping nyia cuyi pa kaoh khaing rao ma
13 Ka daux pex koe pui pa koe ping nyia cuyi lie?
Phan koe,
nawh tag caw tix koe krax pa yuh lih ka pa naog ka pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi mai ping nyia cuyi,
kieh mawh pa jhi grawng pa koe nawh krax ot krax koe pa mhawm.
14 Kheu hoik koe krax boh rhawm boh rhi pa caox caig mai pa yuh son to kaux tix ka daux rhawm rhi pex,
pex tag ang pon tix yaw rang di to kaux tix,
ang pon tix pie lhien tieb tie ka pa mawh pa meh.
15 Ping nyia cuyi pa hoik krai pex ang mawh pa kaoh khaing rao ma,
mawh pa dex dawt mai haktiex,
mawh alo nex nham,
mawh cie phi caox.
16 Can pui pa koe krax boh rhawm boh rhi mai ceu pa yuh son to kaux tix,
tag mawh pa yuh lih ka ceu pa caox pa caig ku ceu mai krax suk sag.
17 Viang mawh nan,
ping nyia cuyi pa kaoh khaing plak piang koe grawng pa yuh nin ra loe ceu:
tix ceu mawh pa singax,
khaix an mawh pa nhim,
pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi,
pa chix yuh paox grawm tix mhawm tau mai pui,
pa naog ka krax pa caox rhawm,
pa chix grum plix plix ka plix ceu pa mhawm,
pa ang koe rhawm pa ang khaw pa jhak ka plak ngai pui,
pa ang koe krax pie krax lhien.
18 Plix pa rauh see khawm uik hoik sum tix ka daux pui pa nhim,
mai pui pa sau riang tix yuh lih ka daux pa nhim.
