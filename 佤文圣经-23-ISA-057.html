Ne raw pui Yi-seu-liex kheu krub kix riang rao
57
Pui mhawm yum,
ang koe pui pa priex ot ka daux rhawm tix;
pui pa rauh see hu khaing haktiex,
ang koe nang pui pa kieh koe daux rhawm tix.
Kix hoik yum,
chix yaox tix luat luan khaing krax laux leeing singaix pa sang hoek.
2 Pui pa yuh kaing pa rauh pa see,
tag pon krax pa laux lee kheu pa yum tix heue.
3 Pex pa mawh pui phi map in kix,
khaw tix kham krax prawng krax ca heue!
Pex ang pon yaox tix lheu mhawm khaing cao chix si meed,
khaing simex pa yuh krax phit mai khaing sibun coh kaux heue.
4 Pex hoik map nai ka pui mawx lie?
Pex hoik ang jheeh tix salao ka pui mawx lie?
Pex hoik mawh pui phi map,
pui pie pui lhien.
5 Pex hoik phit lud ka greem khaox klai,
viang mawh nan,
krai krub tix ka siyiex pa keh kawn haoh hun heue!
Pex ot ka daux dawh rang,
mai teei kawn tix kieh mawh Krax-tan Khawng-brie.
6 Pex hoik teei simaox pa plang dee an kieh mawh siyiex pa krub tix,
mai hieh plai ka piang kieh mawh krax tan,
mai baox tan lu Krax-tan Nghox-koi ka kix.
Pex ce aux hoik khaw rhawm ka kaing ceu in kix lie?
7 Pex hu yuh krax tan ka piang gawng dee lhaong,
mai baox yuh krax phit krax lud dee an heue.
8 Pex hoik tan lu ka siyiex pa caox pa caig ot ka plak khaix diang siviex ting nyiex tix.
Pex hoik tik vud ka aux.
Pex hoik peeik ka kraung ceep tix,
haok ka piang ku,
it tau mai paox muh pa hoik teei mau khri kok hoek kah,
son sang kieh tix naog ka krax gaux rhawm alo nex nham heue.
9 Pex hoik kraix rawm sidah bhawng mai bee sidah bhawng ka tix,
mai hu krub ka siyiex Maw-law,
mai ploi kawn cau tix hu sawg siyiex ka dee singai,
phan ang mawh nan hu sawg tom daux meung yum.
10 Pex hu sawg ka siyiex tom hoik uik riang rao tix,
mai kawn ang bax chix vhah rhawm kah.
Pex ce siyiex pa caox pa caig in kix sang chix tawx riang rao ka tix,
son ang sang chix kieh tix iak riang iak rao heue.
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Siyiex an kix pa kieh pex lhat graung mawh mawx,
yuh ka mawx lai kieh pex pie lhien ka aux,
mai piawm ka aux khawm uik lie?
Pex ang lai baox yung yi ka aux,
mawh ang mawh kheu aux yien yawg tix ang nye mai pex tom rheeng lie?
12 Pex ce tix hoik khaw pa yuh.
Aux tag sang plih lih ka phi map pex,
riang rao pa krub pex ang pon teem coi ka pex.
13 Yam khiox khaw pex krax teem coi,
kieh riang rao pa sut pex hoek tix taoh ka pex heue!
Tete te lai,
bhaung ting tag sang phlu kix hu heue.
Viang mawh nan,
pui pa deuh rhawm tix ka aux khankix,
tag sang yaox tix im ju ka daux haktiex in,
mai krub aux ka gawng pa singax singeh heue.”
Siyiex cu tix teem coi mai pai moi
14 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kieh pui cie aux deeh hoek ka aux.
Tag caw tix yieh tix tik ka ceu pa thox ka daux krax heue!
Tag caw tix cox ka daux krax,
mai rien khre tix khawm uik heue!
15 “Aux mawh Siyiex pa kied singax singeh;
aux koe leeing ju leeing phan.
Aux paung ot ka dee singax dee kied lhaong,
viang mawh nan ot nang ka daux pui pa koe krax laux rhawm mai pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi.
Aux kieh pui pa tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi deeh koe krax yum rhawm,
mai kieh pui pa koe krax laux rhawm pon krax gaux rhawm.
16 Aux tawx ju ka pui cie tix,
mai ang lai baox sawg tix deuh phi map ka krax pa iak riang kix,
mai ang lai baox song rhawm ka kix leeing ju nang heue.a
17 Aux song rhawm ka kix,
hoik mawh kheu koe rhawm ghoing kix tix yuh ka phi map,
mawh kheu nan aux tom ciyang ka kix,
tik vud ka kix.
Kix ca kawn kiien rhawm kiien rhi,
mai ca nied tix lawng krax lawng tix heue.
18 “Aux hoik yaox ka kaing pa yuh kix,
viang mawh nan,
aux ca kawn sang pai kix moi.
Aux tag sang thung ka kix,
mai teem coi ka kix;
aux tag sang tawx krax pa nyawm rhawm ka pui pa laux rhawm.”
19 bSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pa nhim!
Pa nhim!
Viang mawh pui pa ot dee singai dee dex ang ah,
aux tag sang tawx pa nhim ka kix;
aux tag sang pai kix moi.
20 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa caox pa caig awm rawm sigrit sigra pa ang chix yien yawg tix,
pa kied ting puk pak yuh nan;
krax ot krax koe kix hoik naog ka ceu pa seeing pa brai khawm uik.”
21 cSimiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui phi map ang yaox krax pa nhim heue!”
