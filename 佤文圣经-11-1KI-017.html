Yi-li-yax mai krax pa laux leeing ka kroh praix klawng
17
Koe cao jhai tix kaux ceu kaux nawh mawh Yi-li-yax,
nawh mawh pui Thi-see-pi pa ot haktiex Ci-liex.
Nawh ah nin ka simiang Ya-ha:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa krub aux ka pa im ju leeing ju:
ka daux ra loe num hu sivoe ngai,
lheu khaing khiox khaw aux kah,
lhiex ang lai lih,
mai rawm ciex kawx ang lih heue.”
2 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yi-li-yax:
3 “Maix lih hu khaing dee in,
lih hu ka plak lih singaix,
hoek mox maix dex ka rawm klawng iak Ci-li plak lih singaix ka rawm klawng ting Yaw-tang.
4 Maix chix tix nyaux ka rawm klawng iak an;
aux hoik kieh lak hoek tix tawx maix ih priex dee an heue.”
5 Yi-li-yax nghiet ka lox pa tawx Simiang Siyiex,
mai hu paung ot ka praok rawm klawng iak Ci-li;
6 nawh nyaux ka rawm klawng an;
ku ngai pon ngawb cao mai pon bo,
lak hoek tawx nawh ih khao mun mai nex.
7 Hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
mawh kheu ang lhiex lih,
rawm klawng an hoik kroh heue.
Yi-li-yax mai miex mai Sa-leu-fax
8 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Yi-li-yax:
9 a“Maix hu ka Sa-leu-fax dex ka Si-toeng,
mai paung ot dee an.
Aux hoik tawx lox ka miex mai pa ot dee an tix kaux eei ka maix.”
10 Yi-li-yax tom hu ka Sa-leu-fax.
Yam hoek nawh ka diang siviex veng,
nawh yaox ka miex mai tix kaux sut ka khix.
Nawh ah nin ka miex mai an:
“Tawx aux nyaux ka rawm ceeix liak.”
11 Yam sang hu miex mai an teei ka rawm,
Yi-li-yax kok yo ah nin:
“Viex aux ih khao mun nang ceeix liak!”
12 Miex mai an ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex maix pa im ju leeing ju,
aux ang koe khao mun,
mawh ngie phawng mien nghox ca tix jhawp ka daux ngoi,
mai bee sime ceeix khaing ka daux kawng.
Aux hoek sut khix dee in sang viex ing mai rien khre ka priex preem pa koe ngie ceeix khaing,
son sang ih yiex miex kawn kah;
an hoik mawh priex preem pa sang nghoik yiex tix ih tix brie khaing,
hu sivoe ngai deuk ngie krox tix yum ka cai rhawm tix heue.”
13 Yi-li-yax ah nin ka nawh:
“Paw vi khi rhawm,
hu yuh priex pa sang yuh maix khaing heue!
Viang mawh nan,
caw tix ran yuh aux ih khao mun iak tix lon,
khaix an pa deuh khankix,
yuh maix mai kawn simex maix ih,
14 mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex ah nin:
‘Tom hoik ploi aux,
pa mawh Simiang Siyiex ka rawm lhiex lih ka daux singaix an,
phawng mien nghox ca pa koe ka daux ngoi an ang uik ih,
bee pa koe ka daux kawng an ang chix uik heue.’ ”
15 Miex mai an hawt tix yuh awm lox pa tawx Yi-li-yax.
Kix hoik yaox tix ih priex preem tom kub,
khaix an mai ih tom rheeng bawg yam heue.
16 Awm gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex Yi-li-yax krai yuh nan,
phawng mien nghox ca ka daux ngoi ang uik ih,
bee ka daux kawng ang chix uik heue.
17 Yam hoik luan tix baux bawg yam,
kawn simex miex mai an saux,
nied ot nied nie,
dee si dauh kawn an yum.
18 Miex mai an ah nin ka Yi-li-yax:
“Pui cie Siyiex eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai yuh nin ka aux?
Maix hoek dee in,
mawh son sang kieh maix Siyiex tawng ka phi map aux,
mai kieh kawn simex aux yum lie?”
19 Yi-li-yax ah nin:
“Tawx kawn nyawm ka aux.”
Nawh tom teei tix klien kawn nyawm khaing daux deem miex mai an,
viex nawh haok ka lawg dee it tix,
mai deuh ot piang ku.
20 Khaix an,
Yi-li-yax rawg buan tom ting lox tix ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
yuh ka mawx maix lai tawx krax laux leeing lih hoek piang miex mai in lie?
Aux vai tix paung it daux nyiex nawh,
maix lai kieh kawn simex nawh yum!”
21 bYi-li-yax tom gup tix ka piang kawn nyawm in loe bawg,
mai khiox khaw ah nin:
“Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex aux eue,
khiox khaw maix kieh khuan ju kawn nyawm in deeh hoek liak!”
22 Simiang Siyiex nghiet ka lox rawg buan Yi-li-yax,
kieh khuan kawn nyawm an deeh hoek,
kawn nyawm an deeh im heue.
23 Yi-li-yax deeh klien kawn nyawm an lih hoek daux nyiex jan plak greem,
tawx ka miex nawh,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“Jhak heue,
kawn maix deeh im!”
24 Miex mai an ah nin:
“Yam in aux hoik tawng sidawng maix mawh pui pa kaoh khaing Siyiex;
gum lox pa hoik tawx Simiang Siyiex ka maix mawh tete heue!”
