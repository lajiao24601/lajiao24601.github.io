Lox rhax yuh ka paox simiang
45
Daux rhawm aux ah lox pa nyawm tix nghiet lih,
phat lox yaog yaw son simiang;
dak aux hoik sug awm rawm meug pui pa chix tiiem lai tix kaux heue.
 
2 Maix kied sidaing mhawm ka daux pui kaing mheen;
daux jheeh maix hoik chix krai ka lox awn lox tiiem;
Siyiex tawx buan son ka maix leeing ju.
3 Simiang pa ting pa lhaong,
kawg ka vaig pa viex maix heue;
maix hoik naog ka min plak ngai mai siawp sibrawm heue.
 
4 Son sang priex mhawm ka pa mawh pa meh maix,
mai pa rauh pa seea maix,
son sang hu plak sivoe ngai ka daux pa koe siawp sibrawm heue!
Riang rao maix tag sang kieh maix pon pex khawm uik heue!
5 Te puing maix kied lawm,
mai pon phlat ka daux rhawm pui koeng;
meung ka piang haktiex tag sang dip tix ot greem tiag caong maix khawm uik heue.
 
6 cDang ngeung pa tawx Siyiex ka maixb koe ku ju leeing ju;
maix teei pa rauh pa see tix kuang ka meung tix.
7 Maix muh ka pa rauh pa see,
mai koeng ka pa caox pa caig;
mawh kheu nan,
Siyiex maix hoik laug ka maix,
kieh maix pon krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi,
lheu khaing pui tang kaux.
8 Kraung ceep maix hoik phruah sidah bhawng,
Muran,
Acaw,
ro kui ot kah;
ka daux nyiex haw simiang,
koe pui cao pa tauh ting son maix pa nyawm tix nghiet.
9 Ka daux moeng simiang mawh maix,
hoik koe ka kawn bun simiang khaing ku meung;
miex simiang viex ka ble khri,
mai cung ka praok dawm dang ngeung heue.
 
10 Moeng khraox simiang eue,
nghiet ka lox krai aux pa ah nin:
piawm ka ceu pui maix mai paox nyiex paox ma maix heue!
11 Pa mhawm grang maix hoik kieh simiang ghoing rhawm kah;
nawh mawh simiang maix,
maix khaw tix nghiet ka lox nawh.
12 Pui Thai-eux sang hoek tix tawx keen ka maix;
pui mi pui koe sang sawg krax pa khaw rhawm maix heue.
 
13 Ka daux nyiex haw,
kawn bun simiang kied sidaing mhawm heue!
Kraung ceep nawh hoik caing tix ka keei khri.
14 Nawh ceep ka kraung pa mhawm grang,
mai pui viex nawh hoek ka plak sivoe ngai simiang heue!
Bun pa hu tau mai nawh khankix kawx hoik hu yaox simiang tau mai nawh khawm uik.
15 Kix hoik gaux rhawm gaux rhi khawm uik,
hoik viex kix laig tau daux nyiex haw heue.
 
16 Simiang aux tag sang koe kawn praix tom nie pa plud ka simiang mawh tax du kix;
maix tag sang ap kix giex kaing ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
17 Lox yaog yaw aux tag sang kieh min ceu kaux maix ang chix laux leeing ju;
Kawn pui ka piang haktiex tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka maix leeing ju leeing phan heue.
