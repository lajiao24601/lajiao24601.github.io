Pauh ka Yi-seu-paw-seux
4
Kawn simex Sao-law pa kaux Yi-see-paw-seux mhong grawng pa hoik pauh pui Ya-ni-eu ka haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
tom lhat graung kah.
Can kawn pui Yi-seu-liex kawx hoik si yaung khawm uik.
2 Yi-see-paw-seux koe simiang naing ra kaux,
pa mawh Pa-na mai Li-cia.
Kiex hoik mawh ceu pui Piien-ya-ming pui Pi-lux kawn simex Ling-meung.
(Pi-lux mawh pa ot baoh haktiex Piien-ya-ming tix dauh.
3 Pui pa ot ka haktiex Pi-lux sivoe an,
hoik to hu ka haktiex Ci-tha-ying.
Jauh tom yam an kix hoik paung ot ka haktiex Ci-tha-ying.)
4 aDoeh ceu pui Sao-law tang kaux tix kaux mawh kawn simex Yaw-na-tang pa kaux Mi-fe-paw-seu.
Yam pauh pui Sao-law mai Yaw-na-tang yum,
nawh taug koe phuan num.
Yam krai pui grawng pa hoik yum kiex hoek khaing Yie-see-liex,
mai miex eei nawh klien nawh to hu mox tix.
Ka daux krax pa yaoh yee,
miex eei nawh viex nawh goh piang tiex,
jauh yam an nawh tom lih mawh pui pa chawh heue.
5 Li-cia mai Pa-na hoek ka dee ot Yi-see-paw-seux,
yam hoek kix dee an caw tix mawh greung ngaix bawg yam si aoh praix,
Yi-see-paw-seux kawn it.
6 Bun miex pa pau siviex veng kheu kied taug maox ka grheeng tix nghox ca,
nawh gaing.
Li-cia mai Pa-na briex lhua tix laig daux vengb.
7 Khaix hoik laig kiex,
mai kiex tom hoek ka nyiex it Yi-see-paw-seux,
Yi-see-paw-seux kawn nyawm it,
mai kiex tom pauh nawh yum,
khaix an pat ka kaing nawh,
baox hu krax been tix sawm,
mai laig ka daux ngawd Yaw-tang.
8 Kiex viex kaing an hoek ka simiang Ta-wue daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
mai ah nin ka nawh:
“In mawh kaing pui pa koeng ka maix,
pa mawh kawn simex Sao-law pa kaux Yi-see-paw-seux.
Sao-law hoik dawg simie tix sang pauh ka Vawng,
in singaix Simiang Siyiex hoik pawk bix ka Sao-law mai doeh ceu cad nawh son Vawng heue.”
9 Ta-wue pawk lox kiex ah nin:
“Aux tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex pa hoik kieh aux luat luan khaing kraix sum krax nai khawm uik.
10 cPui pa krai grawng pa hoik yum Sao-law ka aux ka daux haktiex Si-keu-la,
nawh ce tix hoik viex ka lox pa mhawm hoek.
Aux giex ka nawh,
mai pauh nawh yum.
In hoik mawh pa vied aux tix tawx ka pui pa viex lox an hoek!
11 Kheu nan pax nie mawh pui pa kied ting phi map!
Pax hoik jawm yam laig it pui ang koe dee lud,
mai nieb pon pauh nawh yum ka daux nyiex to kaux nawh.
Yam in aux sang pawk bix kheu pa hoik briex pax tix pauh nawh yum,
mai yuh pax laux leeing khaing ka piang haktiex!”
12 Mawh kheu nan,
Ta-wue tawx lox nhie pui cau pa ot plak goix plak dawm tix pauh ka Li-cia mai Pa-na yum,
kid taix caong kiex dut,
mai vhak to kaux yum ot ka praok rawm dung Si-paw-loeng,
khaix an teei si paung kaing Yi-see-paw-seux ot daux haktiex Si-paw-loeng,
ka daux si meeig Ya-ni-eu heue.
