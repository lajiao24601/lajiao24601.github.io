[Simie pa sang koe moeng]
7
Nyawm bun pa mhawm grang khaing pui khawm uik eue,
caong maix pa ceep ka kiiep kied sidaing mhawm heue!
Ba maix hoik mawh ceu pa yuh pui pa koe min pa chix yuh riang heue!
2 Si taing maix hoik lawm le awm kawk plai yuh nan,
hoik sau naog ka plai pa nyawm.
Nyoi maix hoik awm nghox ca tix mat,
tai siyag hoik vung viang ka khawm pon daog.
3 Teeh maix khawm ra khawn hoik awm kawn cak brawm tix ku,
awm kiah tix ku.
4 Ngawg maix hoik awm nyiex gawng mu pa yuh tix ka rhang sang;
tawm ngai maix hoik awm rawm dung veng Si-seu-peung,
pa dex ka praok diang siviex veng pa ao min an.
Meeh maix hoik nyawm tix jhak awm gawng mu Li-pa-noeng yuh nan,
pa hoik sum jung vud tix ka plak Ta-ma-see-keux.
5 Kaing maix hoik yaog tix cong si jong awm gawng Cia-mix yuh nan.
Hauk kaing maix hoik sawng awm man luat yuh nan,
hoik pon giex gang ka rhawm rhi simiang heue.
6 Maix kied sidaing mhawm,
kied sidaing nyawm tix jhak heue!
Paox muh aux,
maix hoik kied sidaing kieh pui yoek ka tix heue!
7 Grang maix hoik mhawm grang awm khaox jhie yaong;
teeh maix khawm ra khawn hoik awm si gleem plix pa preue tix.
8 Aux sang haok khaox jhie yaong mai pieh ka plix.
Ka daux ngai jhak aux,
teeh maix khawm ra khawn hoik awm si gleem plix siiet pa preue tix;
bhawm maix hoik bhawng awm mak kawk tie yuh nan;
9 jheeh pa ngheet maix hoik nyawm awm plai pa nyawm yuh nan heue.
[Bun pa sang koe mex]
Kieh plai pa nyawm jauh tix laig ka daux jheeh pui pa muh aux,
kieh jauh tix luan ka si nax jheeh nawh mai num rhang nawha.
10 Aux mawh cie pui pa muh aux;
pui pa muh aux braong rhawm ka aux.
11 Paox muh aux,
hoek heue,
hu ax ka daux tiag daux tu,
paung ot ka dee koe nyiex yaong khankixb.
12 Ex caw tix kaoh cao,
mai hu ka si peem plix siiet,
jhak tix yaox ka plix siiet hoik glaoh ang glaoh,
jhak tix yaox ka plix siiet hoik tai ang tai,
jhak tix yaox ka khaox plix mak cang hoik tawx ang tawx tai tix;
ka dee an,
aux sang tawx rhawm muh dut aux ka maix.
13 Maix tag sang nghiet tix mhong jhawm pa bhawng plix pa kieh pui haing rhawm;
plix pa nyawm ku ceu hoik pum tix ot ka ding siviex nyiex ax.
Paox muh aux,
ceu pa khraox ang ah,
pa prim ang ah,
aux hoik deuh tix mox son maix khawm uik heue.
