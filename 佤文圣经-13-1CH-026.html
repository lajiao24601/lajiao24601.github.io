Pui pa pau nyiex buan
26
Kaing pa yuh pui Li-we pa pau nyiex buan tiiem ot plak greem:
ceu pui Kheu-la koe kawn simex Kheu-lix pa kaux Mi-see-li-mi-ya mawh pui daux nyiex Ya-sa.
2 Nawh koe kawn simex aliah kaux,
jauh ka pa ting hoek ka pa iak mawh Sa-cia-li-yax,
Yie-tiie,
Si-pa-ti-ya,
Yie-thi-niie,
3 Yi-lang,
Yaw-ha-nang,
Yi-li-yaw-nai heue.
4 aKawn koe tix kaux ceu kaux mawh Eu-piie Yi-tong.
Siyiex tawx buan ka nawh,
nawh koe kawn simex daix kaux,
jauh ka pa ting hoek ka pa iak mawh See-ma-ya,
Yaw-sa-pa,
Yaw-yax,
Sa-cia,
Na-thang-yiex,
5 Ya-mi-lix,
Yi-sa-cia,
Pi-wu-li-thaix heue.
6-7 Kawn ai Eu-piie Yi-tong pa kaux See-ma-ya koe kawn simex liah kaux,
pa mawh Eu-teu-ni,
Li-fa-yix,
Eu-pe-teux,
Yi-li-sa-pa,
Yi-li-hux,
Si-ma-cia.
Kix hoik mawh pui pa ting ceu ka daux ceu pui kix,
kheu mawh kix pui pa kied chix yuh,
nie mawh pa nghoik khaix paox ra kaux nie chix yuh tete heue.
8 Cub nyiex Eu-piie Yi-tong khawm uik koe tix gliah ra kaux pa kied chix koe riang rao pon tix yuh kaing in heue.
9 Cub nyiex Mi-see-li-mi-ya koe kao daix kaux pa pon tix yuh ka kaing in heue.
10 Ceu pui Mi-la-lix mawh Heu-sax;
Heu-sax koe kawn simex pon kaux pa mawh Sheung-lix (nawh ang mawh kawn ai,
viang mawh nan,
keeing nawh ap nawh mawh simiang),
11 Cub nyiex Si-leu-cia,
Ti-pa-li-ya,
Sa-cia-li-yax nyiex Heu-sax khawm uik koe kao loe kaux mawh cao pa pau nyiex buan heue.
12 Hawt awm cub nyiex,
pa pau nyiex buan guah tix lih koe miex cub mawx.
Tawx kix mawh pui pa yuh kaing daux nyiex buan,
awm pui Li-we yuh nan.
13 Ku ceu nyiex pui,
ting iak ang ah,
yuh pa cawk lai koe beeh glawm mawh cao pa pau siviex tix mu mu.
14 See-li-mi-ya pon cawk ka siviex plak lih singaix;
kawn simex nawh Sa-cia-li-ya mawh pui pa kied lhak lhiao pa mhawm krax keud krax sawn tix kaux,
nawh pon cawk ka siviex plak laong.
15 Eu-piie Yi-tong pon cawk ka siviex plak seh;
kawn simex nawh khankix pon cawk ka nyiex pa deuh kraung khrai.
16 Su-phing mai Heu-sax pon cawk ka siviex pa ot plak klip singaix mai pa ot dex ka siviex Sa-li-ci.
Kaing yuh pui pa pau khankix guah cub hawt bawg yam,
tix cub rieb tix cub.
17 Siviex pa ot plak lih singaix,
ku singaix pui pau ka tix ngaix liah kaux,
siviex pa ot plak laong pui pau ka pon kaux,
siviex pa ot plak seh pui pau ka pon kaux.
Pa pau ka nyiex pa deuh kraung kawx,
tix ngaix pui pau ka pon kaux nang;
tix lhang nyiex pa deuh kraung cao pau ra kaux.
18 Pa dex ka grah nyiex plak klip singaix,
pui cao pau ot tix pau ka praok krax pon kaux,
pau ka piang grah ra kaux.
19 Plak piang mawh ceu pui cub nyiex Kheu-la mai ceu pui cub nyiex Mi-la-lix pa guah tix lih yuh kaing pa pau heue.
Kaing tang ceu son nyiex buan
20 Pui Li-we tang kaux khankixb kuang ka krao nyiex buan mai nyiex krao pa deuh kraung pa tan lu pui ka Siyiex.
21 Kawn simex Keu-soeng pa kaux La-tang mawh tax du ceu pui ra loe ceu,
ka daux kix mawh khawm kawn simex nawh ceu pui Yie-si-yi-li.
22 Kawn simex La-tang tang kaux ra kaux pa mawh Si-thang mai Yaw-eux kuang ka nyiex krao pa ngawh mai nyiex krao tang ceu heue.
23 Doeh ceu pui Ang-lang,
Yi-see-ha,
Si-paw-loeng,
mai Wu-shiie kawx hoik guah kix koe kaing yuh ku kaux heue.
24 Kawn simex Maw-si pa kaux Keu-soeng ceu cub pui Si-pu-yiex mawh simiang pa kuang ka nyiex krao ngawh nyiex buan.
25 Nawh mawh paox nyiex paox ma pui nyiex See-law-mix,
kheu tax du nawh Yi-li-yi-shiex mawh pux ka Keu-soeng.
Yi-li-yi-shiex keh Li-ha-pi-ya;
Li-ha-pi-ya keh Yie-sai-yax;
Yie-sai-yax keh Yaw-lang;
Yaw-lang keh Si-ci-lix;
Si-ci-lix keh See-law-mix.
26 See-law-mix mai pui nyiex nawh kuang ka can khawng phak pa tan lu pui ka Siyiex khawm uik,
khawm pa tan lu simiang Ta-wue,
mai pa tan lu simiang ceu pui kix,
kawn koe khawm pa tan lu cao kaing ceu cub pui ku ceu mai simiang naing khankix.
27 Kix teei tix viex ka kraung pa pon khaing daux naing mai tan lu ka tix blah,
son sang kieh mawh pa lo tix siao ka pa cox nyiex buan.
28 Can kraung ceu pa tan lu tix siao ka nyiex buan khawm uik hoik kieh mawh See-law-mix mai pui nyiex ma nawh pa kuang,
khawm khawng phak pa hoik tan lu cao pa chix yaox sivoe Sa-mu-eu,
khawng phak pa hoik tan lu simiang Sao-law,
khawng phak pa hoik tan lu kawn simex Ni-eu pa kaux Ya-ni-eu mai kawn simex Si-lu-ya pa kaux Yaw-ya khankix heue.
Kaing yuh pui Li-we tang kaux khankix
29 Ka daux doeh ceu cad Yi-see-ha,
Ci-na-ni-ya mai kawn simex nawh khankix,
hoik mawh cao pa koe beeh glawm tix yuh ka kaing plak simiang haktiex:
pa kuang ka ca rang pui mai pa prawng son krax pa simah pui Yi-seu-liex paox tix heue.
30 Ka daux doeh ceu cad Si-paw-loeng,
Ha-sa-pi-ya mai pui nyiex ma nawh pa chix yuh ka kaing khankix tix rheng atigliah yieh kaux kuang ka been li pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak klip singaix khawm uik mai ka kaing pa yuh son kaing pui haktiex khankix.
31 Yie-li-ya mawh simiang doeh ceu cad pui Si-paw-loeng.
Yam mawh Ta-wue simiang ka daux tipon num,
nawh si kaw tix yaox ka doeh ceu cad Si-pu-loeng in tix bawg,
mai nawh yaox grawng pa kied kua pui naing doeh ceu cad pui nyiex in hoik ot ka veng Ya-shiex haktiex Ci-liex.
32 Simiang Ta-wue laug cao kaing pa kied chix yuh khankix lih khaing pui nyiex ma Yie-li-ya ra rheng aliah yieh kaux,
mai tawx kix kuang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang plak lih singaix pa mawh been li pui Yi-seu-liex mai kaing pa yuh son pui haktiex khankix khawm uik ka daux haktiex ceu pui Lui-piien,
ceu pui Cia-teux,
mai ceu pui Ma-na-si plak lih singaix heue.
