Baox prawng ca ka Pa-pi-loeng
51
Simiang Siyiex krai yuh nin:
“Kuan tix jhak heue,
aux tag sang kieh bhaung ting pa laux yuh puia phlu ka Pa-pi-loeng mai ka pui cie nawh.
2 Aux tag sang kieh pui meung tang meung yuh Pa-pi-loeng laux leeing,
awm phlu bhaung ting brex nghox hu yuh nan.
Yam hoik hoek singaix pa yuh laux leeing,
kix tag sang puing ka Pa-pi-loeng khawm pon praok si daix daog,
mai kieh haktiex cie nawh riem.
3 Paw vi kieh pui naing cie nawh koe bawg yam tix puing ka ak,
mai paw vi kieh kix koe bawg yam tix puing naing.
Ang pluat ploi ka ju pui pa nhum ju!
Tag sang yuh pui naing laux leeing khawm uik!
4 Kix tag sang ot ka daux veng cie to kaux tix,
mai pauh kix yum ka daux laih ting laih iak cie to kaux tix.
5 “Viang pui Yi-seu-liex mai pui Yo-tax hoik lud loe ka aux,
pa mawh cao pa singax singeh Yi-seu-liex,
viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
kru naing Siyiex ka piang khawm uik,
ang bax tik vud ka kix.
6 To tix klawd ju heue,
pex nied tix to lih khaing Pa-pi-loeng heue!
Paw vi kieh tix yum tau mai phi map nawh.
Mawh kheu aux yam in hoik hawt tix pawk bix ka Pa-pi-loeng,
awm pa khaw nawh tix pon yuh nan.
7 bPa-pi-loeng hoik ca awm kawk khri ka daux tawm taix aux,
mai hoik kieh pui piang haktiex yoek khawm uik.
Meung ka piang haktiex hoik yhot ka nyaux tix plai pa tawx nawh.
8 Yam in Pa-pi-loeng hoik laux leeing dum mawx dum an,
hoik laux leeing heue!
Pex tag sang yiiem son nawh heue!
Sawg sidah kraix viex hoek kraix ka dee mat nawh,
mai tee kawn chix pon pai nawh moi.
9 cPui tang ceu pa paung ot ka Pa-pi-loeng ah nin:
‘Yix hoik dawg ce tix sang pai ka maix moi,
viang mawh nan,
bawg yam hoik lha.
Yam in yix hoik lih hu,
hoik tang deeh ing haktiex ot tix!
Mawh kheu Siyiex hoik sau riang tix ciyang ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai hoik yuh kix laux leeing heue.’ ”
10 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui cie aux hoik gaux rhawm ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex hoik prawng rauh see son yix.
Yix hu ka Shi-ang,
mai hoik krai pao ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex pa mawh Siyiex yix lih heue.’ ”
11 Simiang Siyiex so ka rawm simiang Mi-ti-yax khankix,
tag sang teei kix hoek yuh laux leeing ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai son sang pawk bix son to kaux tix,
mai ka son pa yuh nyiex buan laux leeing heue.
Simiang cao bung kaing ah nin:
“Klaing toh te lawm!
Teei ka dieb tie naok!
12 Tawx lox nhie pui puing ka guh veng Pa-pi-loeng!
Sau riang tix son sang rien tix puing ka naing!
Ploi pui naing cao phlu vhit hu!
Kieh pui naing kuan tix puing heue.”
Simiang Siyiex hoik hawt tix puing pui Pa-pi-loeng awm lox pa hoik krai tix yuh nan.
13 dHaktiex Pa-pi-loeng hoik koe rawm klawng tom nie,
hoik koe sin ngao tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
krax pa lawng nawh hoik si dauh,
krax pa im ju nawh hoik tat tix.
14 Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing hoik tiex tu ka ju to kaux tix,
nawh khrawm ka pui naing tix khawm uik,
awm phung sido pa preeng tix hoek yuh nan,
mai laig tix yuh naing ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Kix tag sang ploi lox krax gaux rhawm pa pex lih heue.
Lox rhax yaog yaw ka Siyiex
15 Simiang Siyiex teei pa ting siawp sibrawm tix phan yuh ka haktiex.
Nawh teei ping nyia cuyi tix sum haktiex kaux cong,
mai teei krax pa lhak lhiao tix nih ka rao ma.
16 Yam tawx nawh gum lox tix,
rawm ka piang rao mae tag sang puk pak;
nawh kieh lon pai awm lih khaing mee haktiex pa si dawm.
Nawh kieh ngu sah jhan riang ka daux rawm lhiex;
nawh kieh bhaung lih phlu khaing nyiex krao tix.
17 Ceu in kix hoik kieh pui daox aw kah;
cang mai yuh khri hoik laux rhawm son riang rao pa krub yuh tix,
mawh kheu siyiex pa yuh nawh,
hoik mawh pa pie lhien,
ang koe ju.
18 Riang rao krub ang koe ngawh jum pie ceeix kawx,
mai mawh pa pie lhien pa nyiah pui kah.
Yam puing Simiang Siyiex ka kix,
kix tag sang laux leeing.
19 Siyiex Ya-keux ang awm riang rao krub in kix;
nawh mawh cao pa phan pa yuh ku ceu khawm uik,
nawh kok pui Yi-seu-liex mawh pui cie tix.
Ceu kaux nawh mawh Yie-heu-hua,
pa mawh simiang kru naing heue.
Daix rhiem cie Simiang Siyiex
20 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
Pa-pi-loeng eue,
maix mawh daix rhiem pa ting cie aux,
kraung naing cie aux.
Aux teei maix lauh max ka meung ka piang haktiex,
21 Aux teei maix yuh laux leeing ka brung naing mai ka pui brug khankix,
hoik yuh laux leeing ka ling law naing mai pui naing khankix
22 Aux teei maix pauh ka simex bun,
ka pui ting pui iak,
pauh ka si naw bun krih.
23 Aux teei maix pauh ka cao liang mai phung yung khankix,
hoik pauh ka pui yuh kaing haktiex,
mai ka moi pa thai kaung thai ma.
Aux teei maix yuh laux leeing ka simiang haktiex mai ka simiang khankix heue.
Ciyang ka Pa-pi-loeng
24 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yo-tax eue,
maix tag sang yaox pawk aux bix ka Pa-pi-loeng,
mai ka pui cie nawh ka ngai tix,
mawh kheu hoik yuh kix kaing pa caox laux yuh ka Shi-ang.
25 Pa-pi-loeng eue,
maix mawh gawng lua pa yuh haktiex laux leeing;
viang mawh nan,
aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik mawh cao pa tieb tie ka maix.
Aux tag sang bluh maix lih,
tawk si dawm,
mai gleeing kieh lih mawh nyaox.
26 Simaox pa koe dee riem khankix,
ang lai baox deeh chix tix siao mawh simaox ka jaong nyiex.
Maix tag sang riem leeing ju.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
27 “Tawx lox puing ka naing heue!
Phlu teet tawng ka daux meung ka piang haktiex heue!
Khrawm meung piang haktiex puing ka Pa-pi-loeng heue!
Khrawm Ya-la-lax,
Mi-ni,
mai meung Ya-seu-ci-na khankix hoek tix puing.
Ciak ci ka simiang ting puing naing,
krai grawng sang puing ka naing,
ploi pui naing brung hu puing naing,
kieh haoh hun awm sido yuh nan.
28 Khrawm meung ka piang haktiex,
puing ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Khrawm simiang Mi-ti-yax khankix,
pa mawh simiang haktiex,
cao kaing,
mai pui naing pa kuang kix hoek tix puing.
29 Haktiex yung yang si yaung,
mawh kheu hoik yuh Simiang Siyiex ka kaing pa yuh tix,
mai kieh Pa-pi-loeng lih mawh haktiex pa riem,
dee ang pui paung ot kah.
30 “Pui naing pa kua Pa-pi-loeng ploi ka kraung naing tix,
mai mox tix ka daux tab.
Rhawm vao rhawm kua kix hoik grai,
ang lai koe riang awm bun yuh nan.
Siviex veng hoik laux!
Ngu hoik gleeing ka nyiex!
31 Pui hoik tawx lox,
tix kaux lai tix kaux krai grawng pa hoik pon teei pui ka veng ting khawm pon daog ka simiang Pa-pi-loeng.
32 Pui koeng pon tix cie ka diang rawm,
mai ploi ngu gleeing ka tab.
Pui naing Pa-pi-loeng hoik yaoh yee si kua!
33 Ang koe miex glang tix,
pui koeng tag sang pauh ka kix;
Pa-pi-loeng tag sang awm bang glauh nghox,
pa hoik duk dig ka yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing,
Siyiex pui Yi-seu-liex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
34 Simiang Pa-pi-loeng pa mawh Ni-pu-cia-ni-sha hoik bleet ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Nawh hoik tud rhim veng singax awm klup pui kawng yi yuh nan,
hoik awm bleet si yawng ting ka sin ngao pa koe daux veng yuh nan.
Nawh hoik teei ka ceu pa sang lo tix,
mai tik ka ceu pa deuk khankix.
35 Pui haktiex Shi-ang tag sang ah nin:
Pa-pi-loeng hoik nieb pon yuh ka ex;
ex khaw tix pawk bix heue!
Pui Yie-lu-sa-leung tag sang ah nin:
Pa-pi-loeng hoik kieh nham ex jauh;
ex khaw tix sawg ka ngawh nham tix heue!
Simiang Siyiex sang teem coi pui Yi-seu-liex
36 Mawh kheu nan,
Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui Yie-lu-sa-leung ah nin:
“Aux sang prawng rauh see son maix,
mai pawk bix son maix.
Aux tag sang kieh num dee keet rawm Pa-pi-loeng kroh,
rawm klawng hit.
37 Pa-pi-loeng tag sang lih mawh dee riem,
mawh dee yuh sox tiag mheem tix,
lih mawh pa yaoh yee pui kah,
dee ghak baig pui kah,
dee ang pui paung ot ka heue.
38 Pui Pa-pi-loeng tag sang awm phung sivai sang si pa graox,
awm kawn sivai sang si pa dia tix graox.
39 Kix kied brax ka ceu pa ih lie?
Chix tix,
aux rien tix sang khan priex preem son kix,
mai kieh yoek ka plai,
kied gaux rhawm,
laig it,
mai ang haing leeing ju.
40 Aux tag sang viex tix pauh kix,
awm grheu kawn yung,
yung mhaing,
pex mhaing laig bang dee pauh pui kix yuh nan.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Krax pa si pup Pa-pi-loeng
41 Phan mawh grawng Pa-pi-loeng,
Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Veng ting pa yaog yaw pui ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
pui hoik pon teei hu heue!
Pa-pi-loeng yuh ka mawx lai lih mawh pa yaoh yee meung ka piang haktiex kah.
42 Rawm sigrit sigra hoik naog,
pak rawm hoik kheem ka Pa-pi-loeng.
43 Veng hoik tieh tix lih mawh daux tiag pa riem,
haktiex dee kroh,
pak mhaik,
dee ang pui paung ot kah,
mai ang koe pui pa lawng krax dee an.
44 Aux tag sang ciyang ka siyiex tang kaux Pa-pi-loeng pa mawh Pi-leux,
mai khin nawh deeh tawx ka sin ngao pa tud tix.
Meung ka piang haktiex ang lai baox krub ka nawh heue.
“Guh veng Pa-pi-loeng hoik ghrup heue!
45 Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
nied to lih khaing haktiex an,
to tix klawd ju,
to mox tix khaing krax pa song rhawm aux heue!
46 Paw vi iak rhawm,
yaoh yee kheu mhong tix lox pie lhien haktiex in.
Ku num hoik koe lox pa krai pui hoek,
pa mawh pa krai lox pie lhien sang suk sag ka daux haktiex,
simiang pa ang mhawm ra lai puing paox tix.
47 Kuan jhak tix yaox heue,
yam tag sang hoek bawg yam,
aux sang tieb tie ka riang rao krub pui Pa-pi-loeng.
Meung kix khawm uik tag sang hoek krax kaig krax tua,
haktiex ku dauh hoik mawh to kaux pui yum pa noi tix it kah.
48 fCao pa yuh laux leeing khaing haktiex plak laong,
yam yuh nawh Pa-pi-loeng laux leeing,
ka daux haktiex khawm uik hoik gaux rhawm rhax lai pa si num kah.
49 gPa kieh Pa-pi-loeng yum ka piang haktiex ku dauh,
yam in nawh tag sang laux leeing kheu pa hoik pauh tix ka pui Yi-seu-liex yum tom niex.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Gum lox pa tawx ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa ot ka Pa-pi-loeng
50 Simiang Siyiex krai ka pui Yi-seu-liex pa paung ot ka Pa-pi-loeng ah nin:
“Pex hoik luat luan khaing krax yum,
yam in nied tix to hu,
paw vi lai baox krox jun!
Viang pex hoik singai ot khaing haktiex tix,
viang mawh nan,
pex tag caw tix braong ka aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex pex,
mai ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
51 Pex ah nin:
‘Yix kied kaig tua kheu mhong tix ka lox pa jhax lawg,
pak ngai yix rang ca kah,
mawh kheu hoik yieh pui tang meung ka nyiex buan pa singax.’
52 Mawh kheu nan,
aux krai,
yam tag sang hoek bawg yam,
aux tag sang ciyang ka riang rao krub pui Pa-pi-loeng;
ka daux haktiex ku dauh,
tag sang mhong ka lox riag yo pui mat.
53 Viang Pa-pi-loeng hoik pon mo tix haok ka rao ma kawx,
pon sum ka tab pa kaux cong kawx,
aux kawx tag sang ploi cao pa yuh laux leeing hu yuh nawh laux leeing.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.”
Pa laux leeing Pa-pi-loeng
54 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
nghiet heue,
koe lox pa yiiem doe pui khaing Pa-pi-loeng;
koe lox pa ting laux leeing hoek.
55 Aux jiet kawn yuh Pa-pi-loeng laux leeing,
mai kieh naox ka lox pa riag yo daux veng.
Pui naing pui koeng awm rawm puk pak pa preeng tix hoek;
kix tawx lox si num pa yung yang yuh rao ma.
56 Kix hoek tix yuh laux leeing ka Pa-pi-loeng.
Pui hoik pon giex ka pui naing pa kua cie nawh;
ak cie kix hoik pot.
Aux mawh Siyiex pa ciyang ka phi map;
aux tag sang pawk bix ka Pa-pi-loeng.
57 Aux tag sang kieh simiang ting cie nawh,
pui pa koe ping nyia,
simiang haktiex,
cao kaing,
pui naing pa kua khankix yoek ka plai khawm uik;
kix tag sang laig it,
mai ang lai haing leeing ju.
Aux mawh simiang;
aux hoik krai pao yuh nin heue;
ceu kaux aux mawh Yie-heu-hua,
pa mawh simiang kru naing.
58 Guh veng Pa-pi-loeng pa vieh va,
tag sang kieh si dawm mai haktiex;
siviex veng pa ting lhaong hoik gleeing laux leeing.
Krax pa sau meung ka piang haktiex riang rao tix hoik mawh pa giex pa going khawm uik;
krax pa taug mao kix hoik tawx ngu gleeing ka khawm uik.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
simiang kru naing hoik krai pao yuh nin heue.
Gum lox Yie-li-mi sidiex hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng
59 Kawn saux Ma-si-yax,
kawn simex Ni-li-yax pa kaux Si-lai-ya mawh pui cau simiang Si-ti-cia.
Yam mawh Si-ti-cia simiang Yo-tax ka daux pon num,
Si-lai-ya hu tau mai simiang hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng,
yam an,
aux tawx lox pa sidiex gau ka nawh tix blah.
60 Aux tiiem krax laux leeing pa tag sang si pup Pa-pi-loeng,
mai kaing pa dex dawt mai Pa-pi-loeng khawm uik ka daux phuk lai.
61 Aux krai ka Si-lai-ya ah nin:
“Khaix hoik hoek ka Pa-pi-loeng,
maix tag caw tix phat tom ting ka lox pa hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai in ka pui hun.
62 Khaix an maix tag caw tix rawg buan ah nin:
‘Simiang Siyiex eue,
mai hoik krai pao tag sang yuh laux leeing ka haktiex in,
mai kieh nawh riem leeing ju,
pui ang koe kah,
mai to sat kawx ang koe ka heue.’
63 hSi-lai-ya eue,
khaix hoik phat maix ka phuk lai in,
maix tag caw tix mad phuk lai in tau mai simaox,
mai tik ka daux rawm klawng Yo-fa-la-ti.
64 Khaix an maix ah nin:
‘Pa-pi-loeng tag sang com daux rawm awm phuk lai in yuh nan,
ang lai baox si bloe haok plak piang,
mawh kheu Simiang Siyiex tag sang ploi krax laux leeing lih ka piang nawh heue.i
’ ”
Gum lox Yie-li-mi hoik ding heue.
