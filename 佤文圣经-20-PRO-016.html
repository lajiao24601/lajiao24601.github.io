16
Krax pa chix keud chix sawn ot ka daux kawn pui;
krax pa prawng dut mawh Simiang Siyiexa.
2 Kawn pui ce kaing pa yuh tix mawh pa khaw khawm uik;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex chawk cheng tix yaox ka krax keud nawh tix sang yuh.
3 Teei tix tawx kaing pa keud pa sawn tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
maix tom tag pon mhawm kaing yuh.
4 Can pa phan pa yuh Simiang Siyiex khawm uik hoik mawh pa koe ka piang ceu mawx ceu an;
dee si dauh krax lawng pui pa caox pa caig mawh krax yum.
5 Simiang Siyiex koeng ka pui pa ca nyhau;
nawh ang doh kieh kix luat luan khaing krax ciyang.
6 Kaux sijax mai rauh see,
phi map pon krax pa pluat ploi;
lhat graung yung yi ka Simiang Siyiex tag yaox tix bheeik luan khaing krax laux leeing.
7 Kaing pa yuh nawh,
phan Simiang Siyiex khaw rhawm kah,
pie pui koeng kawx tag chix tieh tix lih mawh paox grawm nawh.
8 Cu tix pon ceeix ceeix khaing ka daux krax pa kaux sijax,
ang rhawm kieh tix pon nie ka daux krax pa tieh pa vheen.
9 Krax pa chix keud chix sawn ot ka daux kawn pui;
viang mawh nan,
Simiang Siyiex duk thung ka diang caong maix.
10 Lox simiang koe siawp sibrawm cie Siyiex;
krax pa prawng pa ca nawh ang chix lud chix loe.
11 Jiang pa khaw caw mawh cie Simiang Siyiex;
khaox jiang pa rauh pa see mawh pa tat nawh.
12 Simiang ang lai pon gun yeung ka pa caox pa caigb,
kheu siawp sibrawm mawh pa kaux cong ka daux krax pa rauh pa see.
13 Simiang tag caw tix nghiet ka lox pa rauh pa see;
nawh muh ka pui pa krai lox pa mawh pa meh;
14 pui pa lhak lhiao chix kieh simiang pon krax pa nyawm rhawm;
krax pa song rhawm song rhi nawh chix kieh pui hoek krax yum.
15 Buan son simiang hoik awm rawm lhiex yam rung praix pa lih hoek;
pa mhawm pak ngai nawh chix kieh pui koe ju.
16 Ping nyia cuyi kawn lheu ting khaing khri;
ngai lig ngai lai hoik mhawm singax awm mau.
17 Krax cie pui rauh see hoik singai khaing pa caox pa caig;
pa priex rhawm tix hu daux krax rauh krax see,
ju nawh pon yaox tix kaux cong.
18 Ting rhawm lhaong rhi chix viex krax laux leeing;
ting rhawm lhaong rhi tag hoek krax sum nai.
19 Pa viex rhawm awn rhawm tiiem lai ling tau mai pui hot pui sibhawm,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing yaox tix guah kraung khrai tau mai pui cau nyhau.
20 Bre rhawm tix nghiet gum lox sidiex,
yaox tix pon mhawm tom nie;
pa een rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag pon buan son krax gaux rhawm leeing ju.
21 Pui pa lhak lhiao tag kok mawh pui pa koe krax doh krax riang;
Lox si kah nied tix mhawm mai nied tix koe riang.
22 Ping nyia cuyi kieh krax lhak lhiao mawh num dee keet ju;
Sidiex gau ka pui pa daox aw,
hoik mawh pa giex pa going.
23 Pui pa lhak lhiao chix keud mhawm sivoe ha,
khaix an tom taug chix krai lox;
lox pa krai nawh hoik kied koe riang.
24 Lox pa chix khiox khaw hoik awm rawm hia pa tie,
pon kieh rhawm rhi gaux rhawm,
to kaux koe riang.
25 cKoe krax tix blah,
phan jhak mawh pa rauh see,
viang mawh nan,
mawh daux krax pa viex pui hu krax yum.
26 Son sang naog krax ih krax nyaux,
pui yuh kaing tag caw tix yuh kaing;
mawh kheu jao vaig tu,
pui yuh kaing ang chix tix nieh.
27 Pui pa cau nyhau keud tix sang yuh pui laux leeing,
lox pa krai nawh hoik awm ngu pa cap.
28 Lox pa caox pa caig chix kieh simah simaing lih koe;
krai lox pa ang koe ceu,
chix kieh paox grawm ang khaw rhawm paox.
29 Pui pa cau nyhau pie lhien ka paox plawg paox nyawm tix;
nawh thung pui hu daux krax pa caox.
30 Pui pa khiep ngai tix ka paox,
rhawm rhi kix ang rauh ang see;
pui pa mip jheeh tix,
mai keud sawn tix sang yuh kaing ceu pa ang mhawm.
31 Hauk kaing pa paing hoik mawh mhok khri pa koe min plak ngai,
hoik mawh krax pawk bix pa mhawm son pui rauh pui see.
32 Krax gun yeung lheu khaing ting riang ting rao;
krax priex rhawm rhi tix hoik ting awm pa pon tud veng.
33 Viang kawn pui hoik yuh krax pa cawk lai chawk cao si pawk kawx,
cao pa prawng dut hoik mawh Simiang Siyiex heue.
