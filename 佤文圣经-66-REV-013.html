To sat ra mu
13
aKhaix an,
aux yaox to sat tix mu lih khaing daux rawm sigrit sigra.
To sat an rung koe kao mu,
kaing koe aliah mu,
ka piang rung ku mu ceep ka mhok khri simiang,
ka piang kaing ku mu tiiem ka ceu kaux pa map nai ka Siyiex.
2 bTo sat pa yaox aux awm sivai preeh yuh nan;
caong awm caong krih yuh nan,
jheeh awm jheeh sivai sang si yuh nan.
Si yawng teei tix tawx riang rao tix,
simiang mawh tix,
mai akhuang pa ting tix ka to sat an khawm uik.
3 Kaing to sat an tix mu awm pa koe khaix mat pa kieh dex yum yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
khaix mat an hoik moi.
Haktiex khawm uik hoik kied daox deng kah,
kix hawt khaix to sat an.
4 Pui hun hoik krub ka si yawng an khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik ap si yawng tix tawx akhuang ka to sat an.
Kix kawx krub ka to sat an ah nin:
“Pui mawx sang pon tiag tix tau mai to sat in lie?
Pui mawx sang pon pex ka nawh lie?”
5 cTo sat an hoik pon ka jheeh pa ting map nai tix mu,
mai baox tawx akhuang pa kieh nawh teei alo tix yuh kaing tipon ra khix.
6 To sat an ang jheeh tix map nai ka Siyiex,
salao ka ceu kaux nawh,
ka nyiex nawh,
ka can pa paung ot ka piang rao ma khawm uik.
7 dTo sat an hoik pon pa kieh nawh yuh krax pa tieb tie ka pui cie Siyiex,
hoik pex ka kix;
mai baox tawx akhuang pa kieh nawh giex gang ka piang pui ku cub,
pui ku ceu,
meung ku meung,
mai pui lox si kah ku ceu.
8 eCan pui pa ot ka piang haktiex,
pa ang ceu kaux bax tiiem tix ot ka daux phuk lai pa im ju cie kawn yung pa hoik pauh yum an sivoe sang phan yuh haktiex,
hoik krub ka to sat an khawm uik heue.
9 “Pa koe yhaok tag caw tix nghiet khawm uik heue!
10 fPa khaw tix laung khankix,
mai tag sang laung kah;
pa khaw tix yum greem pa lawm vaig khankix,
mai tag sang yum.
Mawh kheu nan,
pui cie Siyiex tag caw tix koe rhawm gun rhawm yeung,
mai krax yum rhawm heue.”
11 Aux baox yaox to sat tang mu tix mu pa lih hoek khaing haktiex.
To sat an koe rung ra mu awm rung kawn yung yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
yam si kah awm si yawng ting yuh nan.
12 To sat an ot ka plak sivoe ngai to sat pa lih koe sivoe an tix mu,
mai yuh lih ka akhuang pa ting to sat pa lih koe sivoe an tix mu.
To sat an khin haktiex mai can pa paung ot ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
hu krub ka to sat pa lih koe sivoe pa hoik sax mat dex sang yum mai dee moi an.
13 To sat ka daux ra mu tix mu an baox yuh mhai pa daox pa am;
to sat an kieh ngu lih ka piang haktiex khaing rao ma ka plak sivoe ngai pui.
14 To sat an hoik pon ka akhuang,
mai yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka plak sivoe ngai to sat pa lih koe sivoe,
kheu nan hoik pon nyaw glaw ka kawn pui pa paung ot ka piang haktiex khawm uik.
To sat an tawx lo ka kawn pui piang haktiex,
yuh riang tix mu son to sat pa mat ka vaig pa kawn im an.
15 To sat ka daux ra mu tix mu baox pon ka akhuang,
mai chix tix phlu bhawm laig ka daux riang to sat pa lih koe sivoe an,
kieh riang rao an chix si kah,
khaix an pauh ka can pui pa ang krub ka riang to sat an yum khawm uik.
16 To sat in baox khin can pui khawm uik,
mawh pui ting pui iak ang ah,
mawh pui hot pui mi ang ah,
mawh siliex cawng mai pui pa hoik luat luan ang ah,
tawk cum ka taix khawm dawm kix mai ka piang dong re kix.
17 Cum in mawh ceu kaux to sat an mai ngai lai pa krai grawh grawng ceu kaux;
pa ang koe cum in,
taug ang pon tix yuh krax pa coh cai lai gee heue.
18 Dee in tag caw tix lo ka ping nyia cuyi.
Can pui pa lhak lhiao hoik pon sawn tix yaox ka ngai lai to sat in khawm uik,
mawh kheu hoik krai ngai lai in grawh grawng pui tix kaux.
Ngai lai in mawh liah siyiex tix gliah liah heue.
