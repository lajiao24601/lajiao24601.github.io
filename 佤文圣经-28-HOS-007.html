7
“Yam yaog aux pui Yi-seu-liex haok huan,
pai aux pui cie tix moi,
aux hoik ca yaox ka phi map kix yuh nin:
kix pie lhien,
briex lak,
mai con pui ka daux laih;
2 kix ang tawng aux hoik tiiem tix deuh ka krax pa caox pa caig in kix khawm uik;
kix hoik naog krax pa caox pa caig,
phi map pa yuh kix hoik jhi tix lih ka plak sivoe ngai aux heue.”
Daux nyiex haw simiang koe krax simah simaing
3 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Kawn pui pie lhien ka Vawng,
ka simiang naing;
4 kix tieh vheen ang nghiet lox cu si kah.
Krax song rhawm kix hoik awm ngu pa eet tix ka daux glah ngu yuh nan,
cao pa yuh khao mun kawn krox jun lon khao mun ploh,
ang nyang pon chuh ka ngu pa kawn ting cap.
5 Yam yuh Vawng poi krax gaux rhawm,
kix teei plai khien pleeng Vawng mai simiang naing yoek kah,
mai yuh krax pa ang khaw ka kix tom nie ceu.
6 Krax pa tieh vheen kix hoik awm ngu pa eet tix ka daux glah ngu yuh nan,
taox ngu chu leeing lang singaix,
yam hoik riang praix tag jhi tix lih heue.
7 “Kix hoik briex pauh ka simiang ka daux krax pa song rhawm tix.
Pui briex tix pauh ka simiang,
viang mawh nan,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa khiox khaw aux taoh teem tix heue.”
Pui Yi-seu-liex mai meung ka piang haktiex
8 Simiang Siyiex ah nin:
“Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik awm lon khao mun pa ang nyang sin tix plak yuh nan.
9 Kix hoik een rhawm tix ka meung pa ot sivie siveng kix,
mai ang tawng riang rao pa hoik een kix rhawm tix kah deeh tieh tix bleet kix laux leeing.
Kix ang tawng singaix bawg yam sang yum tix hoik hoek.
10 Krax pa ting rhawm lhaong rhi pui Yi-seu-liex hoik lih tix mawh pa kham phi map to kaux tix.
Kaing in kix hoik lih tix mawh,
kix ca kawn ang hoek ka aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex,
Siyiex kix.
11 Pui Yi-seu-liex hoik awm si kaux pa aw tix mu,
mai pu hoek pu hu;
kix ran khiox khaw Ai-cix teem coi tix,
mai baox khiox khaw Ya-sux teem coi tix.
12 Aux tag sang nih rub ka daux krax,
mai giex kix awm giex ka kawn sim yuh nan.
Mawh kheu krax pa caox pa caig pa yuh kix,
aux tag sang ciyang ka kix heue.
13 “Hoik laux leeing heue!
Kix hoik yaox krax laux leeing heue!
Mawh kheu hoik tik vud kix ka aux.
Kix tag sang yaox krax laux leeing,
mawh kheu hoik tieh vheen kix ka aux.
Aux ce tix sang taoh ka kix,
viang mawh nan,
kix hoik pie lhien ka aux.
14 Kix ang bax viex rhawm tix khiox khaw ka aux,
viang mawh nan,
kix ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting,
mai lu lieh tix piang tiex awm pui ih li tang ceu yuh nan;
kix mug ka to kaux tix mat,
hoek tix khiox khaw nghox koi plai nyawm ka aux.
Kix hoik mawh pui tieh vheen tete heue!
15 Hoik mawh aux pa eei ka kix,
kieh kix ting riang ting rao,
viang mawh nan,
kix yuh krax tieh vheen ka aux.
16 Kix hoik hawt khaix siyiex pa ang mawh tid tix;
kix hoik awm ak puh pa vawk yuh nan,
mai ang pon tix een rhawm tix kah.
Simiang kix tag sang noi tix yum ka daux bang naing,
mawh kheu hoik ah kix lox pa cau nyhau.
Kaing in tag sang lih tix mawh krax pa nyia pui Ai-cix heue.”
