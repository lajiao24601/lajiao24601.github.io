Ka daux phuk pon
(Lai Yaog Yaw 90–106)
Siyiex mai kawn pui
90
Simiang eue,
tix ju hoek tix ju,
maix mawh nyiex dee paung yix.
2 Sivoe sang phan yuh ka gawng lua,
sivoe sang lih haktiex mai pa koe ka daux haktiex tix mawh,
jauh tom dix dix hoek tom leeing ju,
maix hoik mawh Siyiex heue.
 
3 Maix kieh kawn pui deeh ka num dee kaoh tix;
maix kieh kix deeh ing ka phawng tiex.
4 aKa plak sivoe ngai maix,
ka daux tix rheng num awm ngie tix ngaix khaing;
awm singaix pa hoik luan sivoe an;
awm tix bawg yam ka daux tix sawm khaing.
5 Maix awm rawm ting pa chun yix hu;
ju yix hoik dawt awm ca maox.
Yix awm rib riem pa plaung kuah yam pon ngawb,
6 yam pon ngawb kuah haok huan,
yam pon sawm kroh vhit heue.
 
7 Yix hoik laux leeing kheu krax pa song rhawm maix;
yix hoik si yaung kheu krax pa hax rhawm maix.
8 Maix hoik deuh phi map yix ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
hoik plih ka phi map pa glip yix lih heue.
 
9 Krax pa im yix hoik dawt kheu ot ka daux pa song rhawm maix;
ju yix hoik grai awm tix toek bhawm khaing.
10 Ju yix ka piang haktiex kok mawh ngie atigliah num khaing,
phan mhawm kaux hak ca koe tix daix num;
viang mawh nan,
ceu pa hoik pon mawh ngie krax pa taug pa mao khaing;
ju hoik grai ngie tix vud tix cu khaing,
yix kawx tag sang lih mawh pa luan hu heue.
 
11 Pui mawx pon tawng ka siawp sibrawm pa song rhawm maix lie?
Pui mawx pon sidawng ka krax lhat krax graung pa song rhawm maix lix?
12 Khiox khaw maix sidiex gau yix simin ka krax pa im ju tix,
mai son sang kieh rhawm rhi yix koe ping nyia cuyi heue.
 
13 Simiang Siyiex eue,
krax pa song rhawm maix sang naox tix yam mawx lie?
Khiox khaw maix caox rhawm ka siliex maix.
14 Pon ngawb ku ngaix,
khiox khaw maix kieh yix yaox tix ih tom sak ka krax muh maix,
kieh ka daux ju yix naog ka krax pa gaux rhawm gaux rhi.
15 Maix hoik kieh yix si pup ka singaix pa kha rhawm miex ding,
khiox khaw maix kieh nang yix yaox singaix pa gaux rhawm miex ding.
16 Khiox khaw maix kieh siliex maix yaox ka kaing ting pa yuh maix,
kieh kawn praix yix yaox siawp sibrawm min plak ngaix maix.
17 Cao pa mawh Siyiex yix eue,
khiox khaw maix tawx buan son ka yix liak!
Khiox khaw maix kieh kaing pa yuh yix pon mhawm khawm uik;
khiox khaw maix kieh can pa yuh yix khawm uik lih tix mawh heue.
