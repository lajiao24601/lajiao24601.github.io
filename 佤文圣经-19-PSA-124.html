Siyiex cao pa taoh
124
Pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
maix krai heue;
phan ang mawh Simiang Siyiex pa teem coi ka ex,
sang yuh ka mawx lie?
 
2 Phan ang mawh Simiang Siyiex pa teem coi ka ex,
kheu nan,
yam puing pui koeng ka ex,
3 yam song rhawm kix ka ex,
kix hoik dawg pon bleet im ka ex.
4 Rawm ting hoik dawg pon si bloe ex hu,
puk pak rawm hoik dawg kheem ka ex,
5 rawm ting hoik dawg pon chun ex hu heue.
 
6 Ex tag caw tix yaog yaw ka Simiang Siyiex,
mawh kheu ang nawh bax kieh pui koeng yuh ex laux leeing.
7 Ex awm kawn sim pa simie tix luat luan khaing rub pa taong pui jaw;
rub laux,
ex pon krax pa luat luan.
8 Krax pa teem coi ex kaoh khaing Simiang Siyiex;
nawh mawh cao pa phan pa yuh rao ma haktiex heue.
