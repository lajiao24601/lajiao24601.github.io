Yie-fu-tha mai pui Yi-fa-liien
12
Pui Yi-fa-liien rien tix yuh naing;
kix tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang,
hoek ka haktiex Sa-feung,
mai ah nin ka Yie-fu-tha:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai jao mee haktiex,
hu puing ka pui Ya-meung,
mai ang kok yix hu tau mai pex lie?
Yix tag sang gleeing ka maix,
mai ka nyiex ma maix heue!”
2 Yie-fu-tha ah nin ka kix:
“Aux mai pui cie aux puing ka pui Ya-meung;
aux hoik khiox khaw pex teem coi tix,
mai pex ang cu tix tawx taix tix taoh ka yix.
3 Aux yaox ang pex hoek,
yix tom ang lai kuang ka ju tix mai jao ka mee haktiex,
hu puing ka pui Ya-meung.
Simiang Siyiex kieh aux pex.
Yam in yuh ka mawx pex lai haok puing ka aux lie?”
4 Mawh kheu nan,
Yie-fu-tha tom khrawm ka can pui Ci-liex khawm uik,
mai puing ka pui Yi-fa-liien,
mai pex ka kix.
(Pui Yi-fa-liien hoik krai ah nin:
“Pex pui Ci-liex pa ot ka daux haktiex Yi-fa-liien,
mai ka daux haktiex Ma-na-si khankix,
mawh ngie pui naing pa hoik to khaing Yi-fa-liien.”)
5 Pui Ci-liex hoik pon tix ot ka diang rawm klawng Yaw-tang ra loe dauh,
son sang kuan ka pui Yi-fa-liien ang kieh luan.
Phan koe pui naing Yi-fa-liien pa to,
sang tiang ka rawm klawng,
pui Ci-liex tag sang chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix mawh pui Yi-fa-liien lie?”
Phan pui an pawk ah nin:
“Ang mawh!”
6 Kix tom kieh nawh ah:
“See-paw-liiex”,
mawh kheu lox si kah pui Yi-fa-liien ang rauh,
tom ah mawh “Si-paw-liiex;”
yuh nin,
pui Ci-liex tom giex ka nawh,
mai pauh nawh yum ka diang rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Yam an,
hoik pauh pui Yi-fa-liien yum pon mheen ra rheng kaux heue.
7 Yie-fu-tha yuh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex liah num.
Nawh hoik yum,
mai si paung ka daux haktiex yaonga ot nawh Ci-liex.
Yi-pi-cang, Yi-loeng, Ya-toeng
8 Khaix hoik yum Yie-fu-tha,
koe pui Paw-li-haung pa kaux Yi-pi-cang yuh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex.
9 Nawh koe kawn simex tix ngoe kaux,
kawn bun tix ngoe kaux.
Nawh tawx kawn bun tix mex pui tang ceu tang cad,
mai teei kawn simex tix moeng kawn bun tang ceu tang cad tix ngoe kaux,
Yi-pi-cang yuh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex aliah num.
10 Nawh yum,
mai si paung ot ka haktiex Paw-li-haung heue.
11 Khaix hoik yum Yi-pi-cang,
koe pui Si-pu-loeng pa kaux Yi-loeng yuh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex kao num.
12 Nawh yum,
mai si paung ot ka Ya-ya baoh haktiex Si-pu-loeng heue.
13 Khaix hoik yum Yi-loeng,
koe pui Pi-la-toeng kawn simex Si-liex,
pa kaux Ya-toeng yuh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex.
14 Nawh koe kawn simex tipon kaux,
koe kawn saux tix ngoe kaux;
mai kix brug la atigliah mu.
Ya-toeng yuh cao prawng pui Yi-seu-liex daix num.
15 Ya-toeng yum,
mai si paung ot ka gawng haktiex pui Ya-ma-lix mai ka gawng haktiex Yi-fa-liien,
pa mawh Pi-la-toeng heue.
