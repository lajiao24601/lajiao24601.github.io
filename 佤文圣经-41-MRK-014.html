Krax pa keud sawn tix sang pauh ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.1–5; Lai Lu-cia 22.1–2; Lai Yaw-hang 11.45–53)
14
aSivoe Poi Luat-luan mai Poi Tik-su-saix ra ngaix,
simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix keud sawn tix sang briex mox tix giex ka Ye-sux,
mai pauh nawh yum.
2 Kix ah nin:
“Ex paw vi tawx taix tix ka nawh ka daux yam poi,
son ang sang kieh pui hun suk sag heue.”
Ye-sux pon pa groi rawm bee ka Paw-ta-ni
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.6–13; Lai Yaw-hang 12.1–8)
3 bYe-sux ot ka daux nyiex ma Si-meung kaw pa koe jhawm saux si pawg ka haktiex Paw-ta-ni;
bawg yam sawm kix,
koe bun tix kaux viex kawng hoek tix mu,
ka daux hoik sau naog ka rawm bee bhawng na tax pa ngawh.
Nawh pauh kawng an max,
mai hieh bee bhawng an ka piang kaing Ye-sux.
4 Koe pui pa ngawm dee an tix blah kied ang naog rhawm kah,
mai ra lai ah nin ka paox tix:
“Kieh rawm bee nai yuh nin koe ceu yuh ka mawx lie?
5 Rawm bee in chix tix coh mawh loe yieh mau luah,
mai teei mau in teem pui hot pui sibhawm mawh pa kied mhawm!”
Mawh kheu nan,
kix kied song rhawm ka bun an heue.
6 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Ghram nawh yuh heue!
Yuh pa tix lai tieb tie ka nawh lie?
Nawh hoik yuh kaing pa mhawm son aux tix ceu.
7 cMawh kheu hoik ot pui hot pui sibhawm tau mai pex jheeng jheeng,
phan pex cu,
pex hoik chix tix teem coi kix ku jheeng jheeng,
viang mawh nan,
aux ang yaox tix ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng.
8 Nawh hoik yuh awm pa koe riang rao tix;
nawh hieh rawm bee pa bhawng in ka to kaux aux,
mawh son sang rien khre tix sang si paung ka aux.
9 Aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
ka piang haktiex khawm uik,
gum lox buan in sidiex tix hoek dee mawx ang ah,
pui ku kaux tag caw tix krai lao ka kaing pa hoik yuh nawh khawm uik,
son sang doh keud ka nawh heue.”
Yo-tax cu tix coh ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.14–16; Lai Lu-cia 22.3–6)
10 Ka daux kawn cau Ye-sux kao ra kaux,
koe Yo-tax pa mawh pui Cia-liiex tix kaux;
nawh hu ka simiang cao giex kaing li,
son sang coh Ye-sux ka kix.
11 Kix mhong lox pa krai Yo-tax yuh nin,
mai kied gaux rhawm,
cu tix tawx mau ka nawh.
Jauh yam an,
Yo-tax sawg bawg yam pa sang coh ka Ye-sux heue.
Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh ih priex bo Poi Luat-luan
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.17–25; Lai Lu-cia 22.7–14; 21–23; Lai Yaw-hang 13.21–30)
12 Poi Tik-su-saix ka daux tix ngaix,
pa mawh singaix pauh kawn yung Poi Luat-luan,
pui gau hoek tix chawk ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Sang kieh maix ex rien khre priex bo Poi Luat-luan son maix dee mawx lie?”
13 Mawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux ploi pui gau tix ra kaux hu,
mai tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Pex laig ka daux veng,
tag sang yaox pui tix kaux pa viex ka rawm tix kawng,
pex tom hawt ka nawh.
14 Laig nawh daux nyiex pui mawx,
pex tom chawk ka cao nyiex pui an ah nin:
‘Sara ah nin:
lawg nyiex pa tag sang ih aux mai pui gau aux priex bo Poi Luat-luan koe dee mawx lie?’
15 Nawh tag sang viex pex jhak ka lawg nyiex ting pa hoik khre mhawm ka jan plak piang;
pex tom rien khre ka son ex dee an heue.”
16 Pui gau ra kaux lih hu,
laig ka daux veng,
can kaing pa yaox kix ku ceu hoik awm lox pa krai Ye-sux ka kix yuh nan;
kix tom rien khre ka priex bo Poi Luat-luan dee an heue.
17 Pon bo,
Ye-sux mai kawn cau nawh kao ra kaux hoek.
18 dYam ngawm kix tix sawm,
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex,
ka daux pex,
pa sawm tau mai aux,
koe pui tix kaux tag sang coh ka aux heue.”
19 Kix kied laux rhawm tete;
tix kaux lai tix kaux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh aux lie!”
20 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Mawh ka daux pex kao ra kaux tix kaux;
mawh pui an pa cux khao mun tau mai aux ka daux pan.
21 Awm pa hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri,
kawn kawn pui tag sang kham krax pa kha rhawm;
viang mawh nan,
pui pa coh kawn kawn pui an hoek krax laux krax leeing heue!
Pui in phan ang lih keh,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing heue!”
Priex bo cie Simiang Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.26–30; Lai Lu-cia 22.14–20; Lai Keu-ling-to Phuk tix 11.23–25)
22 Yam sawm kix,
Ye-sux teei ka khao mun,
ran rawg buan ka daux krax pa yaog yaw,
khaix an si vit kah,
mai guah tix tawx ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Pex ih;
in mawh to kaux aux heue.”
23 Nawh baox teei ka kawk,
khaix hoik yaog yaw ka Siyiex,
mai tawx ka kix;
kix nyaux ka khawm uik heue.
24 eYe-sux ah nin:
“In mawh nham aux,
pa jhi grawng pa yuh Siyiex sijax mukheu mai kawn pui,
pa jauh son pui khawm uik.
25 Aux krai ka pex,
aux ang doh lai baox nyaux ka plai in,
tom hoek ka daux meung Siyiex,
nyaux ka plai khraox ka daux singaix an heue.”
26 Kix rhax ka lai tix ceu,
tom lih,
mai hu ka gawng sime heue.
Ye-sux grox krai ang Pi-teux tawng ka tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.31–35; Lai Lu-cia 22.31–34; Lai Yaw-hang 13.36–38)
27 fYe-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex tag sang tik vud ka aux khawm uik,
mawh kheu phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Siyiex tag sang pauh ka cao liang yung,
cub yung tag sang prung prang tix tang hu heue.’
28 gViang mawh nan,
khaix hoik kaoh im aux,
aux tag sang ran hoek sivoe pex ka haktiex Cia-li-lix heue.”
29 Pi-teux ah nin:
“Viang pui tang kaux hoik tik vud ka maix,
aux ang doh tik vud ka maix heue!”
30 Ye-sux krai ka Pi-teux ah nin:
“Aux krai ka maix tete te lai,
ka daux bo in sivoe sang ux ia ra bawg,
maix tag sang thiang tix ang tawng ka aux loe bawg heue.”
31 Pi-teux krai lox ting mu ah nin:
“Viang aux caw tix yum tau mai maix,
mai aux ang doh thiang tix ang tawng ka maix heue.”
Pui gau tang kaux kawx hoik ah nin khawm uik heue.
Rawg buan ka daux si peem Kheu-si-ma-ni
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.36–46; Lai Lu-cia 22.39–46)
32 Kix hoek ka haktiex tix dauh,
ceu kaux mawh Kheu-si-ma-ni;
Ye-sux ah nin ka pui gau:
“Pex ngawm dee in,
krox aux hu rawg buan.”
33 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh viex Pi-teux,
Ya-keux,
Yaw-hang hu tau mai tix.
Nawh jauh tix laux rhawm laux rhi,
34 mai ah nin ka kix:
“Aux kied laux rhawm rhi tete,
hoik dex sang yum.
Pex ot dee in,
rien rhawm tix heue!”
35 Nawh hu sivoe ra loe diang,
mai gup tix piang tiex,
rawg buan ka Siyiex ah nin,
phan chix,
paw kieh nawh kham krax pa kha rhawm in.
36 Nawh khiox khaw ah nin:
“Keeing,
Keeing aux eue,
maix hoik chix yuh khawm uik.
Khiox khaw maix teei kawk pa song in hu;
viang mawh nan,
paw kieh mawh alo aux,
mai kieh mawh alo maix heue.”
37 Nawh deeh hoek,
mai yaox pui gau tix loe kaux hoik laig it;
nawh tom ah nin ka Pi-teux:
“Si-meung,
maix kawn it lie?
Ang maix pon rien rhawm tix been tix nari lie?”
38 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Tag rien rhawm tix rawg buan,
son ang pui sang pon nyaw glaw ka pex,
rhawm rhi pex hoik cu tix yuh nan,
viang mawh nan,
nex nham ca mawh pa iak riang heue.”
39 Nawh baox hu,
mai ca rawg buan awm bawg sivoe yuh nan.
40 Khaix an nawh baox deeh hoek ka pui gau tix dee an,
mai yaox kix hoik laig it.
Kix ang pon blah ka ngai tix,
mai ang tawng lox sang krai tix ka nawh heue.
41 Ye-sux deeh hoek ka daux loe bawg,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex ca kawn laig it,
ca kawn laux lee tix lie?
Hoik kub,
bawg yam hoik hoek,
kawn kawn pui tag sang coh ka daux tawm taix pui phi map.
42 Kaoh,
hu ex heue!
Jhak heue,
pui pa coh aux hoik hoek heue.”
Pui giex ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.47, 56; Lai Lu-cia 22.47–53; Lai Yaw-hang 18.2–12)
43 Yam kawn si kah Ye-sux,
Yo-tax pa mawh kawn cau ka daux kao ra kaux tix kaux hoek.
Koe cub pui hun viex vaig khaox hoek tau mai nawh;
kix mawh simiang cao giex kaing li mai sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri,
mai kuad song khankix pa tawx kix hoek.
44 Pui pa coh ka Ye-sux an ran yuh mhai klip ka kix,
ah nin:
“Hu ngheet aux pui mawx,
pui an tag mawh pui pa sang giex pex.
Pex giex ka nawh,
pau ka nawh tom nyawm nyiem,
mai viex nawh hu heue.”
45 Yam hoek Yo-tax,
mai nawh hu ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux dum mawx dum an,
kok yo ah nin:
“Sara!”
Baox ngheet ka nawh.
46 Mawh kheu nan,
kix tawx taix giex ka Ye-sux.
47 Ka daux pui pa cung ka praok kix tix kaux,
leed vaig lih mug ka siliex cao giex kaing li pa ting,
vaig thak ka yhaok nawh cawd tix khawn.
48 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex viex vaig khaox hoek giex ka aux,
mai kieh aux awm pui tud pui rhim lie?
49 hAux ot tix sidiex gau pui ka daux nyiex buan ku singaix,
mai ot tau mai pex jheeng jheeng,
pex ang bax giex ka aux.
Kheu nan gum lox phuk lai sigang khri tag sang lih tix mawh heue.”
50 Bawg yam in,
can pui gau khawm uik hoik tik vud ka nawh,
mai hoik yaog tix to heue.
51 Koe pui pa nhum ju tix kaux,
to kaux nawh fua ngie tix ka man keei sum tix plah khaing,
nawh hawt khaix Ye-sux.
Kix giex ka nawh,
52 viang mawh nan,
nawh tik ka man an,
mai yaog tix to ot ka kaong heue.
Kham krax prawng ka bang kum pui Yo-thaix
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.57–68; Lai Lu-cia 22.54–55; 63–71; Lai Yaw-hang 18.13–14, 19–24)
53 Kix viex Ye-sux hu ka daux nyiex cao giex kaing li pa ting;
can simiang cao giex kaing li mai kuad song,
sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khrawm paox tix dee an.
54 Pi-teux ot dee singai hawt khaix Ye-sux,
tom hoek ka daux biang brawk nyiex cao giex kaing li pa ting,
nawh pu plaik tix laig ngawm ka daux pui pau,
mai co ka ngu.
55 Simiang cao giex kaing li mai bang kum pui Yo-thaix,
sau riang tix sawg son sang deuh phi map ka Ye-sux,
mai son sang pauh nawh yum,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang yaox.
56 Koe pui tom nie lih kham sakse pa ang mawh ang meh ka nawh,
viang mawh nan,
pa kham kix ang khaw khawm uik heue.
57 Khaix an koe pui ra loe kaux kaoh cung,
kham sakse pa ang mawh ang meh pa deuh phi map ka Ye-sux ah nin:
58 i“Yix hoik mhong lox ah nawh nin:
‘Aux tag sang yieh ka nyiex buan pa sum pui ka taix tix in,
khaix an ka daux loe singaix sang deeh sum ka nyiex buan pa ang mawh taix kawn pui pa sum heue.’ ”
59 Pie krax pa kham kix tix deuh phi map in,
hoik mawh pa ra lai ang khaw paox tix heue.
60 Mawh kheu nan,
cao giex kaing li pa ting kaoh cung ka plak sivoe ngai pui hun,
chawk ka Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kix deuh phi map piang maix,
maix ang koe lox sang pawk lie?”
61 Ye-sux yawg tix,
ang nye tix gum kawx.
Cao giex kaing li pa ting baox chawk nawh ah nin:
“Mawh ang maix mawh Khrit,
mawh kawn simex Siyiex pa khaw pui tix yaog yaw ka lie?”
62 jYe-sux pawk ah nin:
“Mawh aux heue!
Pex tag sang yaox kawn kawn pui ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm cao pa ting siawp sibrawm,
mai brug pai awm lih hoek khaing rao ma heue!”
63 Cao giex kaing li pa ting riah ka kraung ceep tix ah nin:
“Ex ang lai lo ka pui pa kham sakse heue!
64 kPex hoik mhong ka lox pa map nai nawh ka Siyiex.
Pex krai sang khaw tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
Kix hoik prawng ca nawh koe phi map khawm uik,
mai khaw tix pauh yum heue.
65 Koe pui tix blah baig ka nawh,
mai baox tawb ngai nawh,
lua taix tix tawk ka nawh,
chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix krai yaox,
mawh mawx pa tawk ka maix lie?”
Pui pau an kix kawx bluh ka nawh,
mai si mang ka nawh heue.
Pi-teux ang tawng ka Ye-sux
(Lai Ma-thaix 26.69–75; Lai Lu-cia 22.56–62; Lai Yaw-hang 18.15–18, 25–27)
66 Yam an Pi-teux kawn ot ka biang brawk plak praix.
Koe bun siliex cao giex kaing li pa ting tix kaux hoek,
67 yaox Pi-teux co ngu,
mai dong ngai tix ka nawh ah nin:
“Maix hoik mawh nang paox Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux heue.”
68 Pi-teux thiang ka ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng,
mai ang tawng nang ka lox pa krai maix heue.”
Khaix an,
nawh tom mox tix ka brawk pa ot sivoe.
Tom hoek bawg yam in,
ia tom ux heue.
69 Baox koe tix vud,
bun siliex an baox yaox ka nawh,
baox krai ka pui pa cung praok tix ah nin:
“Pui in mawh paox kix heue!”
70 Pi-teux baox thiang ka heue.
Baox koe tix vud,
pui pa cung ka praok kix baox ci Pi-teux ah nin:
“Maix ang lai tawng ka sang thiang tix mawh paox kix,
mawh kheu maix hoik mawh nang pui Cia-li-lix heue!”
71 Pi-teux tom tiex tu ah nin:
“Aux ang tawng ka pui pa krai pex an heue!
Phan lox pa krai aux ang mawh tete,
Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka aux heue!”
72 Ka daux bawg yam in,
ia hoik ux ra bawg;
Pi-teux tom taug doh keud ka lox pa hoik krai Ye-sux ka nawh ah nin:
“Sivoe sang ux ia ra bawg maix tag ang tawng ka aux loe bawg.”
Pi-teux tom yiiem ang lai pon gu ka heue.
