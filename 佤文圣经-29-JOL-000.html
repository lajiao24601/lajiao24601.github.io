Lai Yaw-eux
Krai dawt
Phan mawh pa tiiem grawng kaing pa yuh cao jhai Yaw-eux ang nie, krax ot krax koe nawh mawh bawg yam mawx ang sidawng kah. Phuk lai in tee rien tix mawh sivoe sang koe jhong khix mee num phuan num, phan ang mawh nan, daux baux pon bawg yam, pa tiiem yam giex meung Paw-a-see kaing ka piang pui Yo-tax. Yaw-eux tiiem grawng pa laux leeing Pa-leu-see-thang ka sido, mai sibhawm praix kroh praix klawng. Kheu pa lih kaing in kix, mai hoik kieh nawh yaox grawng mhai sakse pa lih hoek singaix bawg yam cie Simiang Siyiex; yam an Simiang Siyiex tag sang ciyang ka pui pa tieb tie ka alo nawh khankix. Yaw-eux sidiex gau ka lox in, mai krai nhie Simiang Siyiex kawn pui loh rhawm rhi tix, khaix an nawh tag sang tawx krax pa haok huan ka pui cie tix, tawx buan son ka kix. Ceu pa nie caw ex tix tawng mawh, Siyiex tag sang tawx khuan tix lih hoek ka piang to kaux pui cie tix, ang guah simex bun, ang guah pui ting iak heue.
Krai kaing
1. Krax laux leeing ka sido (1.1–2.17)
2. Cu tix kieh haok huan (2.18–27)
3. Singaix cie Simiang Siyiex (2.28–3.21)
