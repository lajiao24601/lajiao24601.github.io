Khawng phak krax tan lu cao kaing ku ceu pui
7
Ka daux singaix pa hoik si dauh yuh Maw-si ka nyiex gox buan Simiang Siyiex,
nawh kraix bee pa singax singeh ka nyiex gox buan mai khawm kraung pa siao khankix,
khu dee tan,
mai kraung siao pa iak pa lied khawm uik,
mai tan ka Simiang Siyiex.
2 Simiang ceu pui Yi-seu-liex,
pa mawh simiang doeh ceu cad kix,
pa thung kix si kaw ka ca rang pui,
3 mai viex krax tan kix hoek tan lu ka Simiang Siyiex:
pa mawh ling law koe braux liah lhang,
moi mhaing kao ra mu.
Ra kaux simiang tix lhang ling law koe braux,
tix kaux simiang tan moi mhaing tix mu.
Khaix hoik tan lu ka kraung ceu in kix,
4 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
5 “Rieb maix teei khawng phak ceu in kix son sang siao ka daux pa yuh kaing jhaw nyiex gox buan,
hawt awm kaing jhaw pa caw ceu pui Li-we tix sang yuh,
mai tawx kix yuh heue.”
6 Khaix an,
Maw-si teei tix tawx ling law an mai moi ka pui Li-we.
7 Nawh teei tix tawx ling law an ra lhang mai moi pon mu ka kawn praix Keu-soeng,
8 tawx ka kawn praix Mi-la-lix ling law pon lhang,
mai moi daix mu.
Kix tag sang yuh kaing jhaw ka greem pa thung Yi-tha-ma pa mawh kawn simex Ya-loeng.
9 Viang mawh nan,
Maw-si ang bax tawx ling law mai moi ka kawn praix Keu-siax,
mawh kheu kaing jhaw pa kuang kia kix,
mawh pa klawm ka kraung pa singax singeh ka piang klip tix heue.
10 Simiang ku kaux viex nang khawng phak krax tan pa ap pong son khu dee tan.
Yam hoik rien khre kix khawng phak krax tan ot ka piang khu dee tan,
11 Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
“Maix tag caw tix krai ka kix,
ka daux kao ra singaix,
ku singaix tag caw tix koe simiang pa yuh khawng phak krax tan pa ap pong son khu dee tan heue.”
12-83 Kix hawt awm pa dia tix in plak greem.
Ka daux tix singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Yo-tax,
pa mawh Na-suing kawn simex Ya-mi-na-ta pa tan.
Ka daux ra singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Yi-sa-cia,
pa mawh Na-thang-yiex kawn simex Su-yax pa tan.
Ka daux loe singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Si-pu-loeng,
pa mawh Yi-li-ya kawn simex Si-loeng pa tan.
Ka daux pon singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Lui-piien,
pa mawh Yi-li-su kawn simex See-tio-eux pa tan.
Ka daux phuan singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Si-miien,
pa mawh See-lu-mi kawn simex Su-li-sa-taix pa tan.
Ka daux liah singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Cia-teux,
pa mawh Yi-li-ya-sa kawn simex Tiu-eu pa tan.
Ka daux aliah singaix,
mawh cao kaing ceu pui Yi-fa-liien,
pa mawh Yi-li-sa-ma kawn simex Ya-mi-fu pa tan.
Ka daux daix singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Ma-na-si,
pa mawh Cia-ma-liex kawn simex Pi-ta-su pa tan.
Ka daux dim singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Piien-ya-ming,
pa mawh Ya-pi-tang kawn simex Ci-to-ni pa tan.
Ka daux kao singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Tang,
pa mawh Ya-si-yi-shiex kawn simex Ya-mi-sa-tai pa tan.
Ka daux kao tix singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Ya-seux,
pa mawh Pha-ciex kawn simex Eu-lang pa tan.
Ka daux kao ra singaix,
mawh simiang ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix,
pa mawh Ya-si-la kawn simex Yi-nang pa tan.
Can khawng phak pa tan kix simiang hoik awm paox ku kaux,
pan mau tix mu,
been cien koe tix kongcing plak,
klong mau tix mu,
been cien koe daix yieh kongkheu,
hoik hawt yuh tix awm diag tong pa hoik ciak ci nyiex buan,
hoik sau phawng mien pa hoik klao tix tau mai bee naog ka daux,
son sang kieh mawh Krax-tan Nghox-koi.
Dieb khri tix mu,
been cien koe tix yieh mai kao kongkheu,
pa hoik sau nyawng bhawng naog kah.
Kawn moi mhaing tix mu,
yung mhaing tix mu,
mai kawn pex mhaing tix mu pa koe tix num,
son sang kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing;
pex mhaing tix mu mai kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap;
moi mhaing ra mu,
yung mhaing phuan mu,
pex mhaing phuan mu,
mai yung mhaing pa koe tix num phuan mu kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim heue.
84-88 Khawng phak krax tan pa yuh cao kaing kao ra kaux,
son pa ap khu dee tan khawm uik,
hoik tiiem ot plak greem:
Pan mau kao ra mu,
mai klong mau kao ra mu,
khawm ra mu been cien koe tix nga aliah kongcing mai liah rawng.
Dieb khri kao ra mu,
been cien koe tix kongcing mai loe ra rawng,
hoik sau nyawng bhawng naog kah khawm uik.
Moi mhaing kao ra mu,
yung mhaing kao ra mu,
kawn yung mhaing pa koe tix num kao ra mu,
mai Krax-tan Nghox-koi,
hoik kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-gleeing heue.
Pex gawng mhaing kao ra mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-pluat-ploi-phimap.
Moi mhaing tix nga pon mu,
yung mhaing tix gliah mu,
pex mhaing pa koe tix num tix gliah mu,
kieh mawh Krax-tan Pa-nhim.
89 Maw-si laig ka daux nyiex gox buan man,
yam sang si kah tau mai Simiang Siyiex,
nawh mhong lox pa si kah Simiang Siyiex khaing ka piang dawb teug sijax mukheu,
ka sigreung Ci-lu-paw ra mu,
si kah tau mai nawh heue.
