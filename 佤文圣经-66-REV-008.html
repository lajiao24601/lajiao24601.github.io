Cum ka daux aliah mu
8
Yam paoh kawn yung cum ka daux aliah mu,
piang rao ma hoik yien yawg ang koe lox ceu pa tid tix,
been plak taix nari.
2 Khaix an,
aux yaox cung kawn cau Siyiex aliah kaux ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex;
kix rieb ka teet tawng aliah mu heue.
3 aKoe kawn cau Siyiex tang kaux tix kaux lih hoek,
viex ka mawk ngu khri bhawng,
mai cung ka sivoe ngai khu dee tan.
Nawh rieb ka nyawng bhawm tom nie,
khawm krax rawg buan kawn Khrit khankix,
tan lu ot tau ka piang khu dee tan ka plak sivoe ngai dang ngeung.
4 Taox nyawng bhawng pa chu tix lih,
mai krax rawg buan kawn Khrit khankix,
kaoh khaing daux tawm taix kawn cau Siyiex haok ka plak sivoe ngai Siyiex.
5 bKhaix an,
kawn cau Siyiex teei ka mawk ngu an,
mai tik ngu piang khu dee tan pa sau naog ka mawk ngu piang tiex.
Mawh kheu nan tom lih koe si num praix,
lox luah,
pu plak praix,
mai kla reem heue.
Teet tawng
6 Khaix an,
kawn cau Siyiex aliah kaux pa giex teet tawng aliah mu rien khre tix sang phlu heue.
7 cKawn cau Siyiex tix kaux phlu teet tawng,
koe mu pre mai ngu pa klao tix tau mai nham,
klup tix ka piang haktiex.
Mawh kheu nan,
haktiex loe son tix son,
khaox ox loe son tix son,
hoik gleeing khawm uik;
can rib riem kawx hoik gleeing khawm uik heue.
8 Kawn cau Siyiex ka daux ra kaux tix kaux phlu teet tawng,
koe gawng ting tix mu awm pa proe ka ngu yuh nan,
mai cawd ka daux rawm sigrit sigra.
Rawm sigrit sigra loe son tix son tieh tix lih mawh nham,
9 to sat ka daux rawm sigrit sigra loe son tix son hoik yum,
reu kawx loe son tix son hoik laux nang heue.
10 dKawn cau Siyiex ka daux loe kaux tix kaux phlu teet tawng,
koe simuing ting tix mu,
hoik cap awm proh yuh nan,
khrum khaing piang rao ma,
cawd ka daux rawm klawng loe son tix son,
mai ka piang dee keet rawm khawm uik.
11 e(Simuing in ceu kaux mawh “pa song krin.”)
Mawh kheu nan rawm loe son tix son tieh tix lih song.
Mawh kheu tieh rawm tix lih song,
pui pa nyaux ka rawm in hoik yum khawm uik heue.
12 fKawn cau Siyiex ka daux pon kaux tix kaux phlu ka teet tawng,
singaix loe son tix son,
khix loe son tix son,
mai simuing loe son tix son hoik laux khawm uik.
Mawh kheu nan singaix,
khix,
simuing loe son tix son ang lai koe pa riang;
pon ngaix loe son tix son ang lai koe pa riang,
pon sawm loe son tix son kawx ang lai koe pa riang heue.
13 Aux jhak kah,
mai baox mhong lox si num pu klang tix mu ka daux rao ma ah nin:
“Hoik laux leeing heue!
Laux leeing heue!
Kawn pui ka piang haktiex hoik laux leeing khawm uik heue!
Kawn cau Siyiex tang kaux loe kaux taug sang phlu teet tawng heue!”
