Ye-sux kieh pui phuan rheng kaux ih sak
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.13–21; Lai Ma-kheu 6.30–44; Lai Lu-cia 9.10–17)
6
Yam hoik luan ka daux tix baux bawg yam,
Ye-sux tiang ka rawm nhawng Cia-li-lix (baox kok mawh rawm nhawng Thi-pi-li-yax).
2 Koe cub pui hun hawt ka nawh,
mawh kheu hoik yaox kix mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik pai nawh pui moi.
3 Ye-sux haok ka gawng,
mai ngawm tau mai pui gau tix dee an.
4 Yam an Poi Luat-luan cie pui Yo-thaix hoik dex sang hoek.
5 Ye-sux yaog kaing tix,
mai yaox hoek cub pui hun ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
nawh tom krai ka Fe-lix ah nin:
“Sang hu ex sawg tix liag priex preem ka dee mawx,
son sang kieh pui in kix yaox tix ih sak lie?”
6 Nawh krai lox in hoik mawh son sang cheu tix yaox ka Fe-lix;
mawh kheu hoik tawng nawh ka grawng ceu pa sang yuh tix heue.
7 Fe-lix pawk ah nin:
“Viang ex teei mau luah ra yieh plah kawx liag khao mun ang kub pui ih tix kaux tix lon heue!”
8 Kawn koe pui gau tang kaux tix kaux,
pa mawh Ang-teu-liex pux aik Si-meung Pi-teux,
hoek tix ah nin:
9 “Dee in koe kawn nyawm tix kaux viex khao mun nghox ca phuan lon mai kax ra mu,
viang mawh nan,
sang kub tix guah pui hun in kix ih yuh ka mawx lie?”
10 Ye-sux tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Nhie pui hun ngawm.”
(Dee an hoik mawh dee kied nie rib.)
Pui hun ngawm,
simex nong nong khaing,
koe been phuan rheng kaux.
11 Ye-sux teei ka khao mun,
nawb ka buan,
khaix an guah tix tawx ka pui pa ngawm dee an,
kax kawx hoik guah tix tawx yuh nan nang;
kix hoik ih sak khawm uik.
12 Khaix hoik sak kix,
Ye-sux tawx lox ka pui gau ah nin:
“Khrawm tix sut ka pa max pa phawng khawm uik,
mai ang pon tix dug dig.”
13 Kix tom khrawm tix sut ka khao mun phuan lon pa max pa phawng khankix,
pa mawh pa deuk khaix ih pui hun,
khawm uik kawn koe kao ra khrawng heue.
14 Pui hun yaox ka mhai pa daox pa am pa hoik yuh Ye-sux,
tom ah nin:
“Pui in rien tix mawh cao jhai kaw pa tag sang hoek ka piang haktiex heue!”
15 Ye-sux hoik tawng kix sang bluh tix,
khin tix mawh simiang,
mai nawh tom baox hu ka gawng nong nong tix heue.
Hu ka piang rawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 14.22–33; Lai Ma-kheu 6.45–52)
16 Yam pon bo,
pui gau Ye-sux hoek ka praok rawm nhawng,
17 laig daux reu,
hu ka Cia-pai-nong plak toi rawm.
Yam an praix hoik faik,
Ye-sux ang nyang bax hoek ka kix dee an.
18 Dum mawx dum an,
bhaung ting hoek tix phlu,
mai kieh rawm puk pak.
19 Pui gau vai reu hu been phuan liah kongli,
kix yaox Ye-sux veeh tix hu plak ot reu ka piang rawm,
mai kix kied lhat kah.
20 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Mawh aux,
paw vi lhat heue!”
21 Kix tom taug gaux rhawm tix rieb nawh laig daux reu;
reu tom hoek ka dee sang hu tix dum mawx dum an heue.
Pui hun sawg ka Ye-sux
22 Ka daux singaix khaix,
cub pui pa kawn ot ka toi rawm nhawng khankix,
kix yaox ngie reu iak tix mu khaing pa ot dee an;
kix hoik tawng Ye-sux ang bax laig daux reu tau mai pui gau tix,
mai hoik mawh pui gau pa hu khaing.
23 Koe reu iak ra loe mu pa kaoh khaing Thi-pi-li-yax kaog ot ka praok rawm,
pa mawh haktiex pa dex ka dee hoik tawx Simiang Ye-sux pui hun ih ka khao mun.
24 Cub pui hun in kix hoik tawng ang Ye-sux ot tau mai pui gau tix ka dee an,
kix tom laig daux reu hu sawg nawh ka plak haktiex Cia-pai-nong heue.
Ye-sux mawh priex preem pa im ju
25 Kix hoik yaox Ye-sux ka plak toi rawm,
kix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sara,
hoik hoek maix dee in bawg yam mawx?”
26 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Tete te lai,
pex sawg ka aux,
ang mawh kheu hoik yaox pex ka mhai pa daox pa am,
mai hoik mawh kheu hoik yaox pex tix sak vaig naog tu.
27 Paw vi taug mao son priex preem pa chix laux chix leeing,
tag caw tix sau riang tix yuh son priex preem pa koe leeing ju leeing phan.
Priex preem in hoik mawh pa tag sang tawx kawn kawn pui ka pex,
mawh kheu hoik tawk keeing Siyiex cum ka piang to kaux kawn kawn pui heue.”
28 Kix tom chawk ah nin:
“Sang yuh ex ka mawx tom taug sawn tix yuh kaing Siyiex lie?”
29 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yum rhawm ka kaw pa hoik ploi nawh lih hoek,
mai in hoik mawh kaing pa tag sang kieh Siyiex pex yuh heue.”
30 Kix baox ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
maix taug sang chix yuh mhai pa daox pa am ceu pa tix,
son sang kieh yix yaox mai yum rhawm ka maix lie?
Kaing yuh maix hoik mawh pa yuh ka mawx lie?
31 aTax du yix hoik ih Ma-nax ka daux tiag daux du heue,
awm hoik tiiem ka daux phuk lai sigang khri ah nin:
‘Nawh hoik tawx kix ih ka priex preem pa kaoh khaing rao ma heue.’ ”
32 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux krai ka pex sidawng,
Maw-si ang bax tawx pex ih ka priex preem pa kaoh khaing rao mab;
priex preem pa mawh pa meh pa kaoh khaing rao ma hoik mawh pa tawx keeing aux ka pex.
33 Mawh kheu priex preem pa tawx Siyiex hoik mawh pa lih hoek khaing rao ma,
khaing kaw pa hoik tawx ju ka haktiex heue.”
34 Kix ah nin:
“Sara,
tawx priex preem in ka yix jheeng jheeng liak heue!”
35 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux hoik mawh priex preem pa im ju;
pui pa hoek ka aux dee in,
ang lai cai rhawm leeing ju;
pui pa yum rhawm ka aux,
ang lai simie tix nyaux rawm.
36 Viang mawh nan,
aux hoik krai ka pex,
pex hoik yaox ka aux,
mai ca kawn ang yum rhawm kah.
37 Can pui pa hoik tawx keeing ka aux,
tag sang hoek ka aux dee in khawm uik.
Pui pa hoek ka aux dee in,
aux ang doh tik vud ka nawh;
38 mawh kheu aux lih hoek khaing rao ma,
mai ang mawh kheu pa hawt aux alo tix,
mai hoik mawh kheu pa hawt tix yuh ka alo kaw pa ploi aux hoek an.
39 Alo kaw pa ploi aux hoek an mawh:
pui pa hoik tawx nawh ka aux,
tix kaux kawx ang grai,
khaix an yam hoik si dauh bawg yam,
tag sang kieh kix deeh kaoh im.
40 Mawh kheu alo keeing aux tag sang kieh can pui pa hoik yaox ka kawn tix,
mai pa yum rhawm ka nawh pon ju leeing ju leeing phan;
yam hoik si dauh bawg yam,
aux tag sang kieh kix deeh kaoh im heue.”
41 Simiang pui Yo-thaix mawh kheu hoik krai Ye-sux ah nin:
“Aux hoik mawh priex preem pa lih hoek khaing rao ma”,
kix tom briex mox tix kum kawng paox tix ah nin:
42 “Pui in ang nawh hoik mawh Ye-sux kawn simex Yaw-seux lie?
Ex hoik tawng yung ka miex keeing nawh.
Yam in nawh lai krai mawh tix pa lih hoek khaing rao ma heue!”
43 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pex ang siao lai mox tix kum kawng paox tix.
44 Phan ang mawh keeing aux an pa ploi aux hoek,
mai kieh pui hoek,
ang koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon hoek ka aux dee in;
pui pa hoek ka aux dee in,
ka daux yam si dauh bawg yam,
aux tag sang kieh nawh deeh kaoh im.
45 cKa daux phuk lai cao jhai hoik krai ah nin:
‘Kawn pui tag sang pon krax pa sidiex gau Siyiex khawm uik.’
Can pui pa nghiet ka lox keeing mai pa rieb ka krax pa sidiex gau nawh,
tag sang hoek ka aux dee in khawm uik.
46 In ang mawh pa krai hoik mawh pui mawx pa yaox ka keeing,
mawh ngie kaw pa lih hoek khaing Siyiex dee an tix kaux khaing pa yaox ka keeing.
47 Aux krai sidawng ka pex,
pui pa yum rhawm tag sang koe ju leeing ju leeing phan.”
48 “Aux hoik mawh priex preem pa im ju.
49 Tax du pex hoik ih Ma-na ka daux tiag daux tu,
mai ca kawn chix yum;
50 viang mawh nan,
priex preem pa lih hoek khaing rao ma an,
mai hoik kieh pui pa ih ka ang lai yaox tix yum.
51 Aux hoik mawh priex preem pa im ju pa tawx lih hoek khaing rao ma;
pui pa ih ka priex preem in ang lai yaox tix yum leeing ju.
Priex preem pa tag sang tawx aux ka kawn pui hoik mawh nex aux,
pa hoik tan lu son sang kieh kawn pui piang haktiex pon ju heue.”
52 Gum lox in hoik kieh krax simah lih koe ka daux pui Yo-thaix,
kix ah nin:
“Pui in sang tawx ex ih nex tix yuh ka mawx lie?”
53 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Aux krai ka pex sidawng,
phan pex ang ih ka nex kawn kawn pui,
nyaux ka nham nawh,
pex tag ang koe ju pa mawh pa meh.
54 Pa ih ka nex aux,
mai pa nyaux ka nham aux,
tag koe ju leeing ju leeing phan;
ka daux yam si dauh bawg yam,
aux tag sang kieh nawh deeh kaoh im.
55 Nex aux hoik mawh priex preem pa ih tete,
nham aux hoik mawh rawm tie pa nyaux tete.
56 Pui pa ih ka nex aux,
mai pa nyaux ka nham aux,
hoik ot ka daux ju aux jheeng jheeng,
mai aux kawx hoik ot ka daux ju nawh nang.
57 Keeing cao pa im leeing ju hoik ploi aux hoek,
aux kawx hoik im kheu nawh.
Awm an,
pui pa ih ka nex aux kawx tag sang im kheu aux.
58 In hoik mawh priex preem pa lih hoek khaing rao ma;
pui pa ih ka priex preem in,
tag sang im leeing ju leeing phan.
Priex preem in ang awm pa hoik ran ih tax du pex yuh nan,
kix ih kah,
mai ca kawn chix yum heue.”
59 Gum lox in hoik mawh pa krai Ye-sux yam sidiex gau nawh pui ka daux nyiex sidiex haktiex Cia-pai-nong heue.
Gum lox krax pa im ju leeing ju
60 Pui gau tix blah khankix hoik mhong ka lox in,
kix tom ah nin:
“Krax pa sidiex gau in kied nu tete,
pui mawx sang pon nghiet ka lie?”
61 Ye-sux hoik tawng pui gau tix mox tix krai lao ka kaing in,
tom ah nin ka kix:
“Gum lox in hoik kieh krax yum rhawm pex yung yang lie?
62 Phan pex yaox grawng pa haok kawn kawn pui tix deeh ka haktiex dee ot tix,
sang yuh ka mawx lie?
63 Pa tawx ju ka kawn pui hoik mawh Khuan Singax Singeh,
nex nham ang pon yuh ceu pa tid tix;
gum lox pa krai aux ka pex,
hoik mawh khuan pa tawx ju.
64 Viang mawh nan,
ka daux pex koe pui ang yum rhawm ka heue.”
(Ye-sux hoik dawg tawng mawh pui mawx pa ang yum rhawm,
mawh pui mawx pa tag sang coh ka nawh.)
65 Nawh baox ah nin:
“Mawh kheu nan,
aux hoik dawg sax krai ka pex,
phan ang mawh khawng phak buan son pa kaoh khaing keeing aux,
ang koe pui pa pon hoek ka aux dee in heue.”
66 Jauh yam an,
ka daux pui pa hawt ka nawh,
koe pui tom nie hoik deeh ing,
ang lai hawt tix hu tau mai nawh.
67 Ye-sux tom chawk kawn cau tix kao ra kaux ah nin:
“Pex lie?
Pex kawx tag sang deeh ing nang lie?”
68 dSi-meung Pi-teux pawk ah nin:
“Simiang eue,
maix hoik koe gum lox pa tawx ka ju leeing ju,
yix kawn sang hawt ka pui mawx lie?
69 Yix hoik yum rhawm,
khaix an mai hoik tawng mawh maix cao pa singax pa lih hoek khaing Siyiex dee an heue.”
70 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Khex ang aux hoik laug ka pex kao ra kaux lie?
Viang mawh nan,
ka daux pex tix kaux hoik mawh Phi-caox heue!”
71 Gum lox Ye-sux in hoik mawh pa krai ka son Yo-tax kawn simex Si-meung pui Cia-liiex.
Yo-tax hoik mawh kawn cau tix kaux ka daux kao ra kaux,
pa mawh pui pa tag sang coh ka Ye-sux khaix an heue.
