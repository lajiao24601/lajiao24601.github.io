Lox pa dex pa dawt mai pui Fe-li-see
47
aSivoe sang laig simiang Ai-cix tix puing ka Cia-sa,
Simiang Siyiex krai kaing pa dex pa dawt mai pui Fe-li-see ka aux.
2 Nawh ah nin:
jhak heue.
Rawm klawng naog ka haktiex plak laong,
mai lih mawh rawm ting pa haok huan.
Rawm ting tag sang kheem ka haktiex mai ka ceu pa ot daux khawm uik,
mai bleet ka veng mai ka pui pa ot daux veng khankix.
Kawn pui tag sang kok yo lox pa ting si num;
can pui haktiex tag sang mhong lox laux rhawm yiiem grai pui.
3 Kix tag sang mhong ka lox luah caong brung naing,
mai lox luah ling law,
lox luah jaong ling law!
Taix keeing khawm ra khawn hoik si klua ang lai koe riang,
ang lai pon kia ngien ka kawn praix tix heue.
4 Singaix bawg yam yuh laux leeing ka Fe-li-see hoik hoek;
Bawg yam yuh laux leeing ka pui pa taoh teem Thai-eux mai Si-toeng hoik hoek.
Aux pa mawh Simiang Siyiex tag sang yuh laux leeing ka pui Fe-li-see,
mai yuh laux leeing ka pui deuk pa kaoh khaing hawt klawn tiex Cia-fe-thaw pa koe daux rawm sigrit sigra.
5 Pui Cia-sa hoik naog ka krax laux rhawm laux rhi;
pui Ya-seu-ci-loeng yum ang koe lox.
Pui Fe-li-see pa yaox tix klawd ju khankix kawn sang riag yo hoek yam mawx lie?
6 Pex kok yo ah nin:
vaig cie Simiang Siyiex eue,
maix tag sang chuh tix miex glang tom tag sang naox lie?
Chup vaig laig daux phak vaig,
mai kieh laux lee tix heue!
7 Viang mawh nan,
aux nhie nawh chuh tix mug pui pauh pui,
nawh sang chix naox tix ang chuh yuh ka mawx lie?
Aux tawx lox nhie nawh mug pui Ya-seu-ci-loeng yum,
mai pauh ka pui pa ot hawt rawm sigrit sigra khankix yum heue.
