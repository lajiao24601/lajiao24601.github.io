Pui jiang
(Lai Been-ra-bawg 1.19–33)
13
Simiang Siyiex ah nin ka Maw-si:
2 “Maix laug simiang ka daux kao ra ceu pui,
tix ceu tix kaux,
ploi kix hu jiang ka haktiex Cia-nang,
pa mawh haktiex sang tawx aux pui Yi-seu-liex cie.”
3-15 Maw-si hawt ka lox an,
mai laug simiang kao ra kaux ka daux tiag Pa-lang.
Ceu kaux kix tiiem ot plak greem:
Ceu pui Lui-piien:
mawh Sa-mu-yax kawn simex Sa-kheux;
ceu pui Si-miien:
mawh Sa-fa kawn simex Heu-lix;
ceu pui Yo-tax:
mawh Cia-leux kawn simex Yie-fu-ni;
ceu pui Yi-sa-cia:
mawh Yi-cia kawn simex Yaw-seux;
ceu pui Yi-fa-liien:
mawh Heu-si-ax kawn simex Noeng.
Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming:
mawh Pha-thix kawn simex La-fux;
ceu pui Si-pu-loeng:
mawh Cia-tiiex kawn simex Saw-tix;
ceu pui Ma-na-si:
mawh Cia-tix kawn simex Su-si.
Ceu pui Tang:
mawh Ya-mi-lix kawn simex Ci-ma-lix;
ceu pui Ya-seux:
mawh Si-tie kawn simex Mi-cia-leux;
ceu pui Na-fu-tha-lix:
mawh Na-pi kawn simex Fu-si;
ceu pui Cia-teux:
mawh Cio-lix kawn simex Ma-ci.
16 Pui in kix plak piang mawh pui jiang pa ploi Maw-si hu.
Maw-si loh ceu kaux kawn simex Noeng pa mawh Heu-si-ax kaux Yaw-su-yax.
17 Yam ploi Maw-si kix hu,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Jauh dee in hu plak laong,
hoek ka plak seh haktiex Cia-nang,
khaix an laig ka piang gawng.
18 Jhak tix yaox ka haktiex an mawh pa yuh ka mawx,
koe pui ot dee an mie ding,
pui khankix mawh iak riang ting riang.
19 Jhak haktiex an mawh mhawm mawh caox,
pui pa ot daux veng mai daux bang rien ang rien tix.
20 Jhak ka haktiex an mhawm ang mhawm,
koe ang koe khaox ox.
Pex viex plix khaox pa koe dee an hoek.”
(Yam an jiet mawh yam teem plix siiet.)
21 Khaix an,
kix lih hu plak laong,
jauh ka daux tiag Shing plak seh,
tom hoek jiang ka haktiex plak laong ka diang moing gawng Ha-ma dex ka Li-haw.
22 Kix ran laig ka plak seh haktiex an,
khaix an hoek ka Si-paw-loeng.
Pui pa ot tan mawh ceu pui Ya-si-mang,
ceu pui See-sai,
mai ceu pui Ta-mai,
kix mawh doeh ceu cad Ya-na pui pa ting lhaong.
(Si-paw-loeng hoik sum tix sivoe Saw-ang daux Ai-cix aliah num.)
23 Kix hoek ka ngawd Yi-seu-keux,
mai kid nhu plix siiet tix kak dee an,
piang kak an koe si gleem plix siiet kah,
caw tix mawh pui ra kaux pa pon klawm ka khaox glawm;
kix kawx viex ka plix mak cang mai plix ruh hoek tix blah.
24 Dee an kok mawh ngawd Yi-seu-keuxa,
mawh kheu pui Yi-seu-liex hoik kid si gleem plix siiet dee an tix maox heue.
25 Khaix hoik jiang pui jiang ka haktiex an tipon singaix,
mai deeh hoek
26 ka haktiex Cia-ti-see daux tiag Pa-lang,
krai grawng pa yaox tix ka Maw-si mai Ya-loeng,
mai ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik,
khaix an mai baox tawx kix yaox plix pa viex tix hoek.
27 Kix krai ka Maw-si ah nin:
“Yix hoik jiang ka haktiex an,
mai yaox grawng pa mhawm haktiex dee lih rawm teeh mai rawm hia an;
in mawh plix pa lih khaing haktiex an.
28 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa ot daux haktiex an,
mawh pa ting riang;
mai veng kix kawx kied ting kied kaux.
Kawn koe,
yix yaox doeh ceu cad pui Ya-na pa ting lhaong dee an.
29 Pui Ya-ma-lix ot ka daux haktiex an plak seh;
pui Heux,
pui Yie-pu-see mai pui Ya-maw-lix ot ka piang gawng;
pui Cia-nang ot praok Rawm Sigrit Sigra Ti-cong mai praok rawm klawng Yaw-tang.”
30 Cia-leux ah lox pa nyawm rhawm ka pui pa salao ka Maw-si ah nin:
“Yam in ex khaw tix haok pon cax ka haktiex dee vieh an;
ex koe riang rao tix pon kuang kah.”
31 Viang mawh nan,
pui pa hu tau mai Cia-leux ah nin:
“Ang,
ex ang koe riang rao tix pex ka kix;
pui ot dee an kied ting lhaong khaing ex.”
32 Kix pa hu jiang ka haktiex an,
krai lox pa ang mawh tete ka daux pui Yi-seu-liex.
Kix ah nin:
“Nghox koi dee an ang kup eei ka pui pa ot dee an.
Pui pa yaox yix dee an ku kaux kied ting lhaong.
33 bYix yaox doeh ceu cad Ya-na pui ting lhaong pa ot dee an;
ex awm ngie sido pa iak ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
ex kawx mawh nan heue.”
