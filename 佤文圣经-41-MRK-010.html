Pa sidiex grawng pa tik moeng tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 19.1–12; Lai Lu-cia 16.18)
10
Ye-sux lih hu khaing dee an hoek ka baoh haktiex Yo-thaix,
mai tiang ka rawm klawng Yaw-tang.
Pui hun baox khrawm paox hoek ka nawh dee an,
nawh tom sidiex gau ka kix heue.
2 Koe pui Fa-li-saix tix blah hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai keud tix sang yuh nawh laux,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Maix krai ka ex,
lox tat jhawm prawng ex cu simex tik moeng tix lie?”
3 Ye-sux tieh tix chawk ah nin:
“Gum lox tawx Maw-si hoik krai ah ka mawx lie?”
4 aKix pawk ah nin:
“Maw-si cu phan mex tawx lai pa tik moeng tix,
tag chix tix tik ka nawh lie.”
5 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Maw-si tawx pex tiiem ka gum lox ping nyap in tix grawng hoik mawh kheu kiien rhawm rhi pex.
6 bViang mawh nan,
yam jauh tix phan yuh haktiex,
Siyiex hoik phan yuh kawn pui,
koe simex koe bun;
7 cMawh kheu nan,
pui tag sang lih hu khaing miex keeing tix,
mai kleuk tix tau mai moeng tix,
8 kiex ra kaux lih mawh tix kaux khaing.
Kheu yuh nin,
mex moeng ang lai baox mawh ra kaux,
mai hoik mawh tix kaux khaing.
9 Mawh kheu nan,
pa kleuk Siyiex tau,
kawn pui ang pon tix yieh laux heue.”
10 Yam deeh hoek nawh ka daux nyiex,
pui gau baox chawk ka grawng kaing in.
11 dYe-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Simex mawx ang ah pa tik ka moeng tix,
mai baox hu moeng ka bun tang kaux,
hoik mawh krax pa phit vao,
laux yuh min moeng tix;
12 moeng khankix phan tik ka mex tix,
mai baox hu mex tang kaux,
hoik mawh krax pa phit vao heue.”
Ye-sux nawb buan ka kawn nyawm
(Lai Ma-thaix 19.13–15; Lai Lu-cia 18.15–17)
13 Koe pui viex kawn nyawm hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai kieh Ye-sux pu pi ka kix;
viang mawh nan,
pui gau ne raw ka pui an kix.
14 Ye-sux yaox kah,
tom song rhawm,
mai krai ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Kieh kawn nyawm iak hoek ka aux dee in,
paw vi tieb tie ka kix,
mawh kheu pui meung Siyiex tag sang awm kix pa yuh nin heue.
15 ePex tag yuh tix tawng,
can pui pa ang rieb ka siawp sibrawm Siyiex awm kawn nyawm yuh nin,
ang doh pon lih mawh pui cie nawh.”
16 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh klien ka kawn nyawm,
mai pu pi kix tix kaux lai tix kaux,
nawb buan ka kix heue.
Ceu pa nu tix yuh ka cao mi
(Lai Ma-thaix 19.16–30; Lai Lu-cia 18.18–30)
17 Ye-sux jiet taug hu krax,
koe pui tix kaux to tix hoek,
grux ngawng tix ka plak sivoe ngai nawh,
mai chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sara pa mhawm,
sang khaw aux tix yuh ka mawx tag pon ka ju leeing ju lie?”
18 Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx maix lai kok aux mawh pa mhawm lie?
Lheu khaing Siyiex tix kaux khaing,
ang lai baox koe pa mhawm.
19 fMaix tee hoik tawng gum lox ping nyap in kix pa ah nin:
‘Ang pon tix pauh ka pui;
ang pon tix phit vao;
ang pon tix briex lak;
ang pon tix kham mhai sakse pa ang mawh ang meh;
ang pon tix pie lhien;
tag caw tix nab yam yung yi ka miex keeing tix heue.’ ”
20 Pui an pawk ah nin:
“Sara,
gum lox ping nyap in kix khawm uik aux hoik priex tix hawt yuh tom yam iak tix heue.”
21 Ye-sux dong ngai tix jhak ka nawh,
daux rhawm nawh kied muh ka nawh,
Ye-sux tom ah nin:
“Maix kawn cud tix ceu.
Hu coh can sin ngao pa koe maix khawm uik,
mai tan mau ka pui hot pui sibhawm,
maix tom tag koe sin ngao pa deuh ot ka piang meung rao ma;
khaix an hawt khaix aux heue.”
22 Pui an mhong ka lox in,
pak ngai nawh hoik tieh loh tix,
tom laux rhawm laux rhi tix lih hu,
mawh kheu kied mag mi nawh heue.
23 Ye-sux kling kaing jhak ka pui gau plak goix plak dawm tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“pui pa koe mau sang lih tix mawh pui cie meung Siyiex mawh pa kied nu heue!”
24 Pui gau hoik daox deng ka lox nawh in;
viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux baox ah nin:
“Kawn nyawm eue,
sang lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex kied mawh pa nu tete heue!
25 Pui pa koe mau sang lih mawh pui cie meung Siyiex,
kawn nu khaing laig ka-la-uk ka daux kre nyex heue!”
26 Yam in,
pui gau nie kied daox deng kah,
ra lai chawk paox tix ah nin:
“Phan mawh nan,
pui mawx sang pon hoek krax taoh lie?”
27 Ye-sux dong ngai tix jhak kix ah nin:
“Kawn pui ang pon,
ka Siyiex ang sawn mawh tid tix;
mawh kheu ka Siyiex,
hoik pon yuh ku ceu heue.”
28 Pi-teux rieb tix krai lox ah nin:
“Maix jhak,
yix hoik prah can pa koe tix khawm uik,
mai hawt ka maix heue.”
29 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Mawh tete,
aux krai ka pex tete te lai,
can pui pa hoik prah ka nyiex ma tix,
pux aik tix,
pux o tix,
miex keeing tix,
kawn praix tix,
mai ka kuang ma nhu ke tix son aux mai gum lox buan,
30 tag sang nie pon tom nie ka daux singaix.
Nawh tag sang pon ka nyiex ma,
pux aik,
pux o,
miex keeing,
kawn simex kawn bun,
gawng ma kaing yieh son,
khaix an tag sang si pup ka krax pa tuk tiak tuk te;
mai ka daux singaix pa sang hoek,
nawh tag sang pon ka ju leeing ju.
31 gViang mawh nan,
koe pui tom nie pa hu sivoe tag sang ot khaix,
pa ot khaix tag sang hu sivoe heue.”
Ye-sux grox krai sang yum tix ka daux loe bawg
(Lai Ma-thaix 20.17–19; Lai Lu-cia 18.31–34)
32 Kix lawng krax hu ka Yie-lu-sa-leung,
Ye-sux hu ka sivoe kix.
Rhawm rhi pui gau kied lhat;
pui tang kaux pa hawt khaix kix kawx hoik kied lhat khawm uik.
Ye-sux baox viex kawn cau tix kao ra kaux ot tix plak,
mai krai kix mhong grawng kaing pa sang lih tix mawh ka to kaux tix.
33 Nawh ah nin:
“Jhak heue,
yam in ex haok ka Yie-lu-sa-leung.
Kawn kawn pui tag sang coh tix tawx ka simiang cao giex kaing li mai ka sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri khankix;
kix tag sang prawng ca nawh yum,
khaix an ap tix tawx nawh ka pui tang ceu tang cad.
34 Kix tag sang map nai ka nawh,
baig rawm mia ka nawh,
si vied ka nawh,
mai pauh ka nawh;
khaix an loe singaix,
nawh tag sang deeh kaoh im heue.”
Pa khiox khaw Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang
(Lai Ma-thaix 20.20–28)
35 Ya-keux kawn simex Si-pi-thaix mai Yaw-hang hoek ka Ye-sux,
mai ah nin:
“Sara,
yiex koe lox tix gum sang khiox khaw ka maix,
simie maix cu ka heue.”
36 Ye-sux chawk ah nin:
“Sang mhaing pax aux yuh ceu pa tix?”
37 Kiex pawk ah nin:
“Yam ngawm maix ka piang dang ngeung min plak ngai,
khiox khaw maix kieh yiex ngawm tau mai maix,
tix kaux ngawm ka plak dawm maix,
tix kaux ngawm ka plak goix maix heue.”
38 hYe-sux ah nin ka kiex:
“Pex ang tawng ka ceu pa khiox khaw tix.
Kawk song pa sang nyaux aux,
sang pon nyaux pax ka lie?
Krax pa sang cub com aux,
sang pon rieb pax lie?”
39 Kiex pawk ah nin:
“Yiex pon heue!”
Ye-sux ah nin:
“Kawk pa tag sang nyaux aux,
pax rug sang nyaux,
krax pa sang cub com aux,
pax rug sang rieb,
40 viang mawh nan,
pui mawx pa sang ngawm ka plak goix plak dawm aux,
mai ang mawh aux pa pon tat.
Dang ngeung in kix,
Siyiex hoik rien khre son pui mawx,
mai nawh tag sang tawx ka pui an heue.”
41 Pui gau tang kaux kao kaux mhong grawng in,
mai kied ang naog rhawm ka Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang.
42 iMawh kheu nan,
Ye-sux khrawm kix hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix khawm uik,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pex hoik tawng,
ka piang haktiex pa ce simiang koe akhuang tix kuang ka kawn pui,
simiang kawx koe akhuang tix yuh piang kawn pui.
43 jViang mawh nan,
pex ang pon yuh nin.
Ka daux pex pui mawx sang mawh pui pa ting ceu,
pui an tag caw tix yuh siliex pex;
44 pui mawx sang mawh cao kaing,
pui an tag caw tix yuh siliex pui hun.
45 Mawh kheu kawn kawn pui ang hoek son sang kieh pui kia ngien ka tix,
mawh hoek son sang kia ngien ka pui,
khaix an tan ju tix son sang taoh ka pui hun heue.”
Pai ka pui dug ngai Pa-ti-mai moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 20.29–34; Lai Lu-cia 18.35–43)
46 Kix hoek ka Yie-li-keu.
Yam lih hu Ye-sux mai pui gau nawh khawm cub pui hun khaing Yie-li-keu,
koe pui dug ngai tix kaux pa mawh Pa-ti-mai kawn simex Ti-mai,
ngawm caung ot ka praok krax.
47 Nawh mhong hoek Ye-sux pui Na-sha-leux,
mai kok yo ah nin:
“Ye-sux kawn praix Ta-wue eue,
caox rhawm ka aux liak!”
48 Pui tom nie ne raw ka nawh ang cu nawh nye.
Viang mawh nan,
nawh nied tix kok yo tom ting ah nin:
“Kawn praix Ta-wue eue,
caox rhawm ka aux liak!”
49 Ye-sux kaog,
mai ah nin:
“Nhie nawh hoek tin.”
Kix tom krai ka pui dug ngai an ah nin:
“Maix paw khi,
kaoh,
nawh kok ka maix!”
50 Pui dug ngai an tik kraung ceep tix dum mawx dum an,
jang luh tix,
mai hu ka plak sivoe ngai Ye-sux heue.
51 Ye-sux chawk nawh ah nin:
“Sang simie maix aux yuh ceu pa tix son maix lie?”
Pui dug ngai an pawk ah nin:
“Sara,
aux simie tix chix yaox heue!”
52 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Hu heue,
krax yum rhawm maix hoik pai ka maix heue.”
Pui dug ngai an chix yaox dum mawx dum an,
nawh tom hawt khaix Ye-sux heue.
