5
Cao giex kaing li pa ting ku kaux hoik mawh pa laug lih khaing daux kawn pui khawm uik,
mai ciak ci mawh pa yuh kaing jhaw Siyiex,
son sang tan lu ka khawng phak mai krax tan son phi map kix.
2 Cao giex kaing li pa ting hoik mawh pa iak riang ka to kaux tom nie,
mawh kheu nan,
nawh tom chix kia ngien pui pa daox aw,
mai ka pui pa lud loe ka daux krax awn rhawm awn rhi.
3 aKheu nan,
mawh kheu iak riang to kaux nawh,
nawh ang joeng caw tix yuh krax pa tan lu son phi map kawn pui,
mai tag caw tix yuh krax pa tan lu khawm son to kaux tix.
4 bAng koe pui tix kaux kawx pa pon teei tix koe min ceu kaux cao giex kaing li pa ting,
mawh ngie pui pa hoik laug Siyiex pa taug pon tix mawh cao giex kaing li pa ting,
awm Ya-loeng yuh nan heue.
5 cAwm an,
Khrit ang bax teei to kaux tix koe min ceu kaux cao giex kaing li pa ting;
deeh tieh tix,
Siyiex tieh tix krai ka nawh ah nin:
maix mawh kawn simex aux;
in singaix aux mawh keeing maix.
6 dNawh baox krai tix dauh ah nin:
maix tag caw tix hawt ka doeh cub nyiex Mai-ci-si-teux,
mawh cao giex kaing li leeing ju leeing phan heue.
7 eYam ot Ye-sux ka piang haktiex,
nawh hoik sax rawg buan,
mai roik rawm ngai khiox khaw ka Siyiex kaw pa taoh nawh bheeik luan khaing krax yum an.
Mawh kheu hoik tiiem rhawm tiiem rhi nawh,
Siyiex tom nghiet ka lox pa khiox khaw nawh.
8 Viang nawh hoik mawh kawn simex Siyiex kawx,
ex ca kawn gau tix hawt ka krax kha rhawm pa hoik kham nawh.
9 Kheu Siyiex hoik kieh nawh grum mhawm ka daux kaing pa yuh tix,
nawh tom lih mawh riah rawng krax taoh leeing ju ka can pui pa nghiet ka nawh khawm uik.
10 Siyiex krai pao hawt nawh tix mawh cub nyiex Mai-ci-si-teux cao giex kaing li pa ting heue.
Ne raw ka pui pa tieh vheen
11 Grawh grawng son kaing in,
kawn koe lox krai tom nie,
viang mawh nan,
ang kah dee bleeh ka pex yie yie,
kheu hoik mawh pex pui pa ang hiex chix mhong khankix.
12 fPex hoik dawg caw tix mawh pui pa thung ka pui,
viang mawh nan,
pex kawn siao pui tang kaux teei gum lox Siyiex pa jauh tix gau hoek tix gau ka pex.
Pex kawn sang lo tix peux ka rawm teeh,
mai ang pon tix sawm eep.
13 Can pa kawn peux ka rawm teeh hoik mawh kawn ngie khawm uik,
mai ang bax chix pon guah ka ceu pa mawh mai pa ang mawh.
14 Teei grawng tang ceu krai,
mawh ngie pui ting pa taug pon tix ih ka eep.
Kix hoik kub rhawm keud ka daux kaing pa yuh tix,
hoik pon guah ka ceu pa mhawm mai pa ang mhawm khankix heue.
