Sawg ceu pui Piien-ya-ming koe moeng
21
Yam khrawm ceu pui Yi-seu-liex tix ot Mi-see-pa,
mai kix hoik tiex tu tix ah nin ka Simiang Siyiex:
“Yix ang doh kieh kawn bun tix mex ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming.”
2 Yam in,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex khankix hoek ka Paw-theu-lix,
ngawm ka sivoe ngai Simiang Siyiex tom hoek pon bo,
mai ploi lox yiiem tix tom ting ah nin:
3 “Simiang Siyiex,
pa mawh Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex eue,
yuh ka mawx lai lih koe kaing pa yuh nin lie?
Ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex yuh ka mawx lai cud cha ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming lie?”
4 Ka daux ra singaix pon ngawb cao,
pui haoh pui hun yaog tix kaoh,
sum khu dee tan tix mu ka dee an,
mai tan Krax-tan Pa-gleeing mai Krax-tan Pa-nhim ka piang.
5 Kix chawk:
“Ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui,
kawn koe pui pa ang khrawm tix hoek ka sivoe Simiang Siyiex ka Mi-see-pa?”
(Kix hoik tiex tu ah nin:
pui ang hoek ka Mi-see-pa tag sang pauh yum heue.)
6 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex son sang kieh paox ceu paox cad tix Piien-ya-ming klih rhawm tix ka daux pa dut ju,
ah nin:
“Ka daux singaix,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex hoik cud tix ceu pui,
7 ex sang sawg ceu pui Piien-ya-ming pa deuk khankix koe moeng yuh ka mawx lie?
Ex hoik tiex tu ka Simiang Siyiex,
ang kieh kawn bun tix mex ka kix heue.”
8 Kix si kaw tix chawk ka daux ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ku ceu pui,
mawh pui mawx pa ang bax khrawm tix hoek ka Mi-see-pa,
khaix an kix tom tawng mawh Ci-liex ceu pui Ya-pi,
pa ang bax hoek dee khrawm kix paox tix tan tix kaux kawx;
9 yam simin ka kaux pui naing,
ang yaox pui Ya-pi tix kaux kawx tan.
10 Mawh kheu nan,
pui haoh pui hun tom ploi pui naing hu tix mheen ra rheng kaux,
mai tawx lox ka kix:
“Hu ka Ya-pi,
mai pauh pui pa ot tan yum,
khawm bun miex khawm kawn nyawm pauh yum khawm uik.
11 Tang pauh simex yum khawm uik,
mai pauh nang bun pa hoik koe mex yum khawm uik.”
12 Kix yaox bun krih pa nhum ju ka daux ceu pui Ya-pi pon yieh kaux,
mai viex kix hoek ka dee bang See-law haktiex Cia-nang heue.
13 Khaix an,
pui haoh pui hun ploi pui hu ka pui Piien-ya-ming pa ot ka Ling-meung-yiien,
son sang kieh kix deeh khaw rhawm paox.
14 Khaix hoik hoek pui Piien-ya-ming,
pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux khankix deeh tawx kawn bun pui Ya-pi pa hoik pon tix luat luan khankix ka kix,
viang mawh nan kawn ang nyang bax kub heue.
15 Pui haoh pui hun laux rhawm son pui Piien-ya-ming,
mawh kheu hoik kieh Simiang Siyiex ceu pui Yi-seu-liex cud tix ceu.
16 Kuad song ka daux pui haoh pui hun ah nin:
“Ceu pui Piien-ya-ming ang lai koe bun khankix,
ex sang sawg can pui pa deuk khankix koe moeng yuh ka mawx lie?
17 Ceu pui Yi-seu-liex kao ra ceu ang doh pon tix cud tix ceu;
ex yuh ka mawx khaw tix keud,
son sang deuh ka ceu pui Piien-ya-ming koe.
18 Viang mawh nan,
ex ang tie kieh kix teei tix moeng ka kawn bun ex,
mawh kheu hoik tiex tu jhax lawg ex ka pui pa tawx kawn bun tix mex ka pui Piien-ya-ming heue.”
19 Khaix an,
kix keud ah nin daux rhawm tix:
“Tix num tix bawg koe poi Simiang Siyiex ka daux See-law;
num in dex sang hoek.”
(See-law ot ka Paw-theu-lix plak caong ma laong,
ka Li-paw-na plak seh,
ka plak lih singaix krax ting si nax Paw-theu-lix mai See-ciien.)
20 Kix ah nin ka pui Piien-ya-ming:
“Pex hu mox tix ka si peem plix siiet,
21 mai kuan tix jhak.
Yam yaox pex lih kawn bun See-law yuh graoh,
pex tom lih khaing daux si peem plix siiet,
tix kaux tud tix kaux mawh moeng tix,
mai viex kix ing haktiex Piien-ya-ming.
22 Phan miex keeing kix mai prie kix hoik sawg tix thung thiang mai pexa,
pexb chix tix ah nin ka kix:
‘Tawx yix moeng ka kix liak!
Mawh kheu ang yix tie bax rhim kix mawh moeng tix khaing daux bang naing;
mai ang bax mawh pex pa tawx kix mex yix.
Kheu nan,
phi map in ang deuh piang pex heue.’ ”
23 Pui Piien-ya-ming tom hawt tix yuh.
Kix sawg kawn bun See-law pa yuh graoh,
tix kaux teei tix kaux tang mawh moeng tix,
mai viex kix hu.
Kix ing ka haktiex tix,
deeh cox sum ka veng ka yaong,
paung ot dee an.
24 Singaix yam an,
pui Yi-seu-liex tang kaux khankix kawx tang ing nang,
tix kaux lai tix kaux tang ing ka ceu pui tix,
ceu pui nyiex pui ma tix,
mai ka haktiex tix heue.
25 cBaux singaix bawg yam an,
ceu pui Yi-seu-liex ang nyang bax koe simiang;
pui ku kaux tang hawt alo tix yuh heue.
