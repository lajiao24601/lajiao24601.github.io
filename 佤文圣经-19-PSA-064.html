Khiox khaw krax pa kia ngien
64
Siyiex eue,
khiox khaw maix nghiet ka lox pa yiiem ngia aux;
khiox khaw maix taoh aux luat luan khaing krax pa cau nyhau pui koeng.
2 Khiox khaw maix kia ngien aux,
paw kieh aux yaox krax pa con pui pa caox pa caig,
ang yaox tix laux leeing ka daux pa tuk tiak pui con pui cam.
3 Kix klaing ka dak tix,
hoik lawm awm vaig gawn yuh nan,
ploi lox caox pa koe jai tix awm te yuh nan.
4 Kix kuan mox tix sang puing pauh ka pui pa ang koe krax lud loe;
yam puing kix ka te dum mawx dum an,
mai ang lai kuang pa yuh tix.
5 Kix ra lai cheeik ka paox tix,
mai yuh krax pa caox pa caig;
kix hoik keud tix yuh kaing pa mox pa glip,
mai nih ka rub.
Kix ah nin:
pui mawx yaox ka ex lie?
6 Kix chix keud chix sawn ka kaing pa caox pa caig,
mai baox ah nin:
kaing pa yuh ex hoik pon glip tete;
rhawm rhi kawn pui kied sidaing nu tix tawng heue.
 
7 Viang mawh nan,
Siyiex teei te puing ka kix,
mai kix mat dum mawx dum.
8 Nawh tag sang kieh lox pa krai kix deeh yuh kix laux leeinga;
pui pa yaox ka kix tag vung vied kaing tix ka kix.
9 Pui haoh pui hun hoik lhat graung khawm uik;
kix tag sang krai lao ka kaing pa yuh Simiang Siyiex,
keud ka kaing pa yuh nawh.
10 Pui rauh pui see deuh rhawm tix ka Simiang Siyiex,
tag sang gaux rhawm kheu nawh;
pui pa yuh kaing pa khaw caw,
tag sang rhax lai yaog yaw ka nawh heue.
