Simiang Siyiex mai simiang pa hoik laug nawh
110
aSimiang Siyiex ah nin ka simiang aux:
Maix ngawm ka plak taix khawn dawm aux,
krox aux kieh pui koeng ka maix kreet tix ot greem caong maix heue.
2 Simiang Siyiex tag sang yah tix tawx siawp sibrawm simiang ka maix khaing Shi-ang.
Nawh ah nin:
maix tag sang kuang ka piang pui pa koeng ka maix.
3 Yam puing maix ka pui koeng,
pui cie maix tag sang gaux rhawm tix yuh ka pui naing cie maix;
pui pa nhum ju cie maix tag sang awm rawm ciex pon ngawb,
tag sang hoek ka maix ka gawng pa singax singeh heue.
 
4 bSimiang Siyiex tiex tu,
mai ang chix tieh loh ka rhawm rhi tix:
maix tag caw tix hawt ka doeh cub nyiex Mai-ci-si-teux,
mawh cao giex kaing li leeing ju leeing phan heue.
 
5 Simiang Siyiex ot ka plak dawm maix;
yam song rhawm nawh,
nawh tag sang lauh ka simiang piang haktiex khawm uik.
6 Nawh tag sang prawng ca ka meung piang haktiex khawm uik,
mai kieh to kaux pui yum naog ka bang naing;
nawh tag sang puing pex ka simiang meung piang haktiex ku meung.
7 Simiang aux tag sang nyaux ka rawm keet pa koe ka praok krax,
riang rao nawh hoik naog hoik kub,
mai pon giex ka pui koeng khankix heue.
