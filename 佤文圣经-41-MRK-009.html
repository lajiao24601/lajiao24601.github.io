9
Ye-sux baox ah nin ka kix:
“Aux bre tix krai ka pex,
pui pa cung dee in,
koe tix blah sivoe sang yum,
kix tag sang yaox pa lih riang rao siawp sibrawm Siyiex tix mawh heue.”
Tieh loh ka pak peung tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 17.1–13; Lai Lu-cia 9.28–36)
2 aKhaix hoik luan liah singaix,
Ye-sux viex Pi-teux mai Ya-keux mai Yaw-hang pux aik ra kaux haok ka gawng lhaong tix mu.
Ka plak sivoe ngai kix,
Ye-sux tom tieh loh pak peung tix,
3 kraung ceep nawh hoik paing riang awm rawm rha yuh nan;
ka piang haktiex ang koe pui pa pon si daux kraung kieh paing singax yuh nan.
4 Pui gau loe kaux yaox Yi-li-yax mai Maw-si si kah tau mai Ye-sux dum mawx dum an.
5 Pi-teux ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Sara,
ex ot dee in kied mhawm heue!
Kieh ex sum nyiex gox bang loe lhang,
tix lhang mawh cie maix,
tix lhang mawh cie Maw-si,
tix lhang mawh cie Yi-li-yax heue.”
6 Pi-teux mai pui tang kaux kied lhat kah,
ang tawng ka lox pa sang krai tix heue.
7 bKoe lon pai awm tix lon tawb kheem ka kix.
Dum mawx dum an,
lox lih khaing pai awm ah nin:
“In mawh kawn simex pa muh aux,
pex tag caw tix nghiet ka nawh heue!”
8 Kix nied tix jhak ka sivie siveng tix khawm pon daog,
viang mawh nan,
ang yaox pui tix kaux kawx,
mawh ngie Ye-sux pa ot tau mai kix khaing heue.
9 Yam lih kix khaing piang gawng,
Ye-sux tawx lox ka kix ah nin:
“Sivoe sang kaoh im kawn kawn pui khaing krax yum,
ang pon tix krai ceu pa hoik yaox pex ka pui tix kaux kawx heue.”
10 Kix priex tix hawt ka gum lox pa tawx Ye-sux,
mawh ngie ra lai krai lao ah nin:
“Pa kaoh im khaing krax yum dee bleeh mawh ceu pa tix lie?”
11 cKix baox chawk Ye-sux ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx sara pa kah phuk lai sigang khri ah Yi-li-yax tag sang ran hoek sivoe lie?”
12 Ye-sux pawk ah nin:
“Yi-li-yax rug sang ran hoek tix cox yuh khawm uik;
khaix an,
phuk lai sigang khri yuh ka mawx lai baox krai kawn kawn pui tag sang kham krax pa kha rhawm mai pa tik vud pui lie?
13 Aux krai ka pex,
Yi-li-yax hoik hoek,
viang mawh nan,
kix ca nieb pon yuh ka nawh,
awm hoik krai phuk lai sigang khri gum lox pa dex dawt mai nawh heue.”
Pai ka kawn nyawm pa laig phi daux moi
(Lai Ma-thaix 17.14–21; Lai Lu-cia 9.37–43)
14 Yam deeh hoek kix ka dee ot pui gau tang kaux khankix dee an,
kix yaox cub pui hun pa lai lawm ka kix,
koe sara pa chix ku ceu hoek tix krai lox mai kix.
15 Yam yaox cub pui hun ka Ye-sux kix kied daox deng kah;
pui hun to tix rieb ka nawh.
16 Ye-sux chawk pui gau tix ah nin:
“Hoik lai krai pex lox ceu pa tix lie?”
17 Koe pui tix kaux ka daux pui hun ah nin:
“Sara,
aux hoik viex kawn simex tix hoek jhak ka maix;
mawh kheu hoik laig phi ang chix nye ka daux to kaux nawh,
mai ang pon chix si kah.
18 Yam yuh phi ka nawh,
mai kieh nawh si blat tix goh piang tiex,
kieh bai lih khaing daux jheeh nawh,
mai greeik rhang braing tix,
to kaux nawh hoik cong khawm uik.
Aux khiox khaw pui gau maix grheu ka phi in,
viang mawh nan,
kix ang pon grheu ka heue.”
19 Ye-sux ah nin ka kix:
“Pex pa mawh baux ju in kied ang koe krax yum rhawm!
Aux kawn sang ot mai pex dee in miex glang lie?
Kawn sang gun yeung ka pex miex glang lie?
Viex kawn nyawm hoek ka aux dee in heue!”
20 Kix tom viex kawn nyawm an hoek heue.
Yam yaox phi an ka Ye-sux,
tom kieh kawn nyawm an rak rawn dum mawx dum an,
mai lu lieh jee lu lieh blaong tix piang tiex,
bai lih khaing daux jheeh.
21 Ye-sux chawk keeing nawh ah nin:
“Saux nawh in koe miex glang?”
Nawh pawk ah nin:
“Hoik dawg koe tom yam iak tix.
22 Phi hoik sang pauh ka nawh tom miex bawg,
sang taung nawh laig daux ngu,
cot nawh laig daux rawm.
Viang mawh nan,
phan maix pon yuh tix ceu ceu,
khiox khaw maix caox rhawm caox rhi ka yix,
teem coi ka yix liak!”
23 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Mawh tete,
phan maix pon yuh!
Pui pa koe krax yum rhawm hoik pon yuh kaing ku ceu heue.”
24 Keeing kawn nyawm an kok yo dum mawx dum an ah nin:
“Aux yum rhawm,
viang mawh nan,
krax yum rhawm aux ang kub,
khiox khaw maix teem coi ka aux liak!”
25 Ye-sux yaox pui hun hoek tix lai lawm ka nawh,
nawh tom khin tix tawx lox ka khuan pa seeing brai an ah nin:
“Maix pa mawh phi lheet pa kle in,
aux tawx lox nhie maix lih hu khaing to kaux kawn nyawm in,
ang kieh maix lai baox laig ka daux nawh heue!”
26 Phi an kok yo tom ting tix gum,
mai kieh kawn nyawm an biak tix,
khaix an tom lih.
Kawn nyawm an hoik awm pui yum yuh nan,
pui hun hoik ah nin khawm uik:
“Nawh hoik yum heue!”
27 Viang mawh nan,
Ye-sux bluh ka taix nawh,
yaog nawh kaoh,
nawh tom kaoh cung heue.
28 Ye-sux laig ka daux lawg nyiex,
pui gau nawh khankix tom briex mox tix chawk nawh ah nin:
“Yuh ka mawx yix lai ang pon grheu ka phi an lih lie?”
29 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Caw tix een ngie rhawm ka daux krax pa rawg buan khaing,
tom tag pon grheu ka phi ceu in lih,
lheu khaing in ang lai koe krax tang ceu heue.”
Ye-sux baox grox krai grawng pa sang yum tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 17.22–23; Lai Lu-cia 9.43–45)
30 Kix hoik lih hu khaing haktiex an,
mai laig ka daux meung Cia-li-lix.
Ye-sux ang rhawm kieh pui tix kaux kawx tawng ka krax pa hu tix,
31 mawh kheu kawn sidiex gau nawh ka pui gau tix.
Nawh ah nin:
“Kawn kawn pui tag ap tix tawx ka daux tawm taix pui;
kix tag sang pauh ka nawh,
khaix hoik yum nawh loe singaix,
nawh tag sang kaoh im heue.”
32 Viang mawh nan,
kix ang tawng ka dee bleeh gum lox in,
viang mawh nan,
ang pon chawk nawh heue.
Mawh pui mawx pa blawn ting khaing paox tix
(Lai Ma-thaix 18.1–5; Lai Lu-cia 9.46–48)
33 Kix hoek ka Cia-pai-nong,
khaix hoik laig kix daux lawg nyiex Ye-sux chawk ka pui gau tix ah nin:
“Thung thiang pex ceu pa tix lawng krax lie?”
34 dKix ang nye tix kaux kawx,
mawh kheu hoik lai thung thiang kix paox tix ka daux krax,
mawh mawx pa blawn ting.
35 eYe-sux ngawm,
kok kawn cau kao ra kaux hoek ka plak sivoe ngai tix,
mai ah nin ka kix:
“Pui mawx sang mawh cao kaing,
pui an tag caw tix ot khaix,
mai yuh siliex pui hun.”
36 Mawh kheu nan,
nawh sawg kawn nyawm tix kaux hoek,
mai kieh nawh cung ka si nax kix,
mai baox klien ka nawh,
mai ah nin ka kix:
37 f“Pa rieb ka kawn nyawm in tix kaux son aux,
hoik mawh rieb ka aux.
Pa rieb ka aux ang joeng mawh ngie rieb ka aux,
hoik mawh pa rieb ka kaw an pa ploi aux hoek nang heue.”
Pa tieb tie mai pa khaw rhawm kah
(Lai Lu-cia 9.49–50)
38 Yaw-hang ah nin ka Ye-sux:
“Sara,
yix yaox pui pa grheu phi lih khaing ka daux ceu kaux maix,
yix tom tieb tie ka nawh,
mawh kheu ang nawh mawh paox pie mai ex heue.”
39 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Paw vi tieb tie ka nawh;
mawh kheu ang koe pui pa yuh mhai pa daox pa am ka daux ceu kaux aux,
mai kling tix krai lox caox ah ka aux dum mawx dum an.
40 gMawh kheu pui pa ang tieb tie ka ex hoik mawh pui pa khaw rhawm ka ex heue.
41 hAux krai tete te lai ka pex,
pui mawx ang ah,
pa tawx pex nyaux rawm tix kawk khaing kheu hoik mawh pex pui cie Khrit,
nawh tag sang pon khawng phak buan son heue.”
Pa nyaw pui yuh phi map
(Lai Ma-thaix 18.6–9; Lai Lu-cia 17.1–2)
42 Ye-sux ah nin:
“Pui mawx ang ah pa kieh kawn Khrit pa iak khaing paox tix tix kaux tik vud ka aux,
ang lai lheu khaing teei nawh simaox laing pa ting phrawk ot ka ngawg nawh,
mai kieh nawh com greem rawm sigrit sigra.
43 jPhan taix maix tix khawn kieh maix yuh phi map,
kid tix tik heue!
Cud taix maix tix khawn mai pon ka ju leeing ju,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing viex maix taix mhawm tix khawm ra khawn laig daux Maw-na-rhok,
cawd maix daux rup ngu ting pa ang chix yeet leeing ju heue.i
45 Phan caong maix tix khawn kieh maix yuh phi map,
kid tix tik heue!
Cud caong maix tix khawn mai pon ka ju leeing ju,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing sang viex maix caong mhawm tix khawm ra khawn laig daux Maw-na-rhok heue.k
47 lPhan ngai maix tix khawn kieh maix yuh phi map,
koik lih heue!
Cud ngai maix tix khawn mai pon tix laig daux meung Siyiex,
kawn lheu mhawm khaing grum ngai maix khaing sang laig ka daux Maw-na-rhok heue.
48 mKa dee an,
viag vua ang yum,
pa rup ngu ang yeet leeing ju heue.
49 “Pui ku kaux tag pheuk tix ka ngu khawm uik,
awm hoik teei kih si ma ka yuh nan.
50 nKih hoik dawg mawh ceu pa mhawm,
viang mawh nan,
phan pa laoh hoik grai,
nawh kawn sang baox chix laoh yuh ka mawx lie?
Pex tag caw tix koe pa siao lo pui awm kih yuh nan,
ra lai kieh tix khaw rhawm paox tix heue.”
