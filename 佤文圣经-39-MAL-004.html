Singaix bawg yam cie Simiang Siyiex dex hoek
4
Simiang Siyiex pa mawh simiang kru naing ah nin:
“Singaix an hoik dex sang hoek,
can pui pa cau nyhau pa caox pa caig tag sang gleeing awm nyhee glauh nghox ca yuh nan.
Ka daux singaix an,
kix tag sang gleeing khawm uik,
mai ang lai deuh koe.
2 Viang mawh nan,
ka pex pa mawh pui pa lhat graung yung yi ka aux,
tag sang lih koe singaix jhan riang pa taoh ka pex,
pai pex moi.
Pex tag sang jang luh tix awm kawn moi pa ploi lih khaing daux kawg yuh nan,
mai pon krax pa gaux rhawm.
3 Ka daux ngaix pa yuh aux,
pex tag sang dug dig ka pui caox;
kix tag sang awm phawng tiex ka greem caong pex yuh nan.
4 “Pex tag caw tix doh keud ka gum lox pa sidiex gau siliex aux Maw-si,
pa mawh lox tat jhawm prawng mai gum lox ping nyap pa hoik tawx aux ka nawh ka gawng Heu-liex,
mai kieh pui Yi-seu-liex khawm uik priex tix hawt heue.
5 a“Viang mawh nan,
Sivoe sang lih hoek singaix cie Simiang Siyiex pa ting lhat graung pui kah,
aux tag sang ploi cao jhai Yi-li-yax hoek ka pex dee an.
6 Nawh tag sang kieh keeing mai kawn simex kawn bun tix baox deeh khaw caw paox,
son ang aux sang hoek tix yuh haktiex in laux leeing heue.”
